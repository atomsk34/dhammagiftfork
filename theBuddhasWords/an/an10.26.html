The Chinese character corresponds to PńĀli ńĀdi, not to assńĀda, thus supporting Ce and Ee against Be, which may have normalized the reading. By seeing the beginning, the danger, and the escape, and by seeing the knowledge and vision of the path and the non-path, he knew the attainment of the goal, the peace of the heart.
