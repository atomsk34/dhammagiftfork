# Project Guide: The Buddha's Words (EBT Reader)

## What it is and why
Provide an easy-to-use, offline-capable reader for Early Buddhist Texts (EBTs) with Pāḷi-English dictionary lookup.

## Who it's for
Students, practitioners, and scholars of Early Buddhism who want to study Suttas offline.

## One-off or ongoing
Ongoing maintenance and feature expansion.

## What it will produce
A self-contained directory of HTML/CSS/JS files that can be packaged into a Kiwix ZIM file or hosted online.

## How you'll know it worked
The website runs correctly in browser/Kiwix, lookup works smoothly, and translations load without errors.
