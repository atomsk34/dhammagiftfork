# Tech Notes: The Buddha's Words (EBT Reader)

## Tools & Platforms
Vanilla HTML, CSS, client-side JS (jQuery, Mousetrap), and w3.css. No backend database.

## Who This Is For
Students, practitioners, and scholars of Early Buddhism who want to study Suttas offline.

## Constraints
Clients running Kiwix, standalone web browsers (Chrome/Safari), offline first. Must work entirely client-side without a back-end.

## Resources
Digital Pāli Dictionary (DPD) JS lookup files, localized Sutta HTML files.

## What the output looks like
A fully static, offline-first website packaged as a Kiwix ZIM archive.
