DĀ 21

Dīrghāgama 长阿含经

Chapter 3 第三分

21 （二一）梵动经

Parallel to: DN 1

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Magadha with a great assembly of 1,250 bhikṣus. He was wandering among the people and arrived at a bamboo grove, where he stayed overnight in the king's hall. At that time, there was a brāhman named Sudatta, and Sudatta's disciple was named Brahmadatta. The master and disciple always followed the Buddha, but Sudatta the brāhman used countless skillful means to slander the Buddha, the Dharma, and the bhikṣu saṅgha, while his disciple Brahmadatta used countless skillful means to praise the Buddha, the Dharma, and the bhikṣu saṅgha. The two masters and disciples each had different minds and were in disagreement. Why was this so? It was because of different habits, different views, and different close associations.

一时，佛游摩竭国，与大比丘众千二百五十人俱，游行人间，诣竹林，止宿在王堂上。时，有梵志名曰善念，善念弟子名梵摩达，师徒常共随佛后行，而善念梵志以无数方便毁谤佛、法及比丘僧，其弟子梵摩达以无数方便称赞佛、法及比丘僧，师徒二人各怀异心，共相违背。所以者何？斯由异习、异见、异亲近故。

At that time, many bhikṣus gathered in the Dharma hall after their alms round and discussed: "How amazing! How extraordinary! The World-Honored One has great spiritual power and perfect majestic virtue. He knows the intentions of all beings. This Sudatta brāhman and his disciple Brahmadatta follow the Tathāgata and the bhikṣu saṅgha; but Sudatta the brāhman uses countless skillful means to slander the Buddha, the Dharma, and the saṅgha, while his disciple Brahmadatta uses countless skillful means to praise the Tathāgata, the Dharma, and the saṅgha. The two masters and disciples each have different minds, different views, different habits, and different close associations."

尔时，众多比丘于乞食后集会讲堂，作如是论：「甚奇！甚特！世尊有大神力，威德具足，尽知众生志意所趣。而此善念梵志及其弟子梵摩达随逐如来及比丘僧；而善念梵志以无数方便毁谤佛、法及与众僧，弟子梵摩达以无数方便称赞如来及法、众僧；师徒二人各怀异心，异见、异习、异亲近故。」

At that time, the World-Honored One was in a quiet room. With his pure divine ear surpassing that of human beings, he heard the discussion among the bhikṣus. The World-Honored One rose from the quiet room and went to the Dharma hall. He sat before the great assembly and knowingly asked, "Bhikṣus! For what reason did you gather in this Dharma hall? What were you discussing?"

尔时，世尊于静室中以天净耳过于人耳，闻诸比丘有如是论，世尊于净室起诣讲堂所，大众前坐，知而故问：「诸比丘！汝等以何因缘集此讲堂？何所论说？」

At that time, the bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "After our alms round, we gathered in this Dharma hall and discussed: 'How amazing! How extraordinary! The Tathāgata has great spiritual power and perfect majestic virtue. He knows the intentions of all beings. Now Sudatta the brāhman and his disciple Brahmadatta always follow the Tathāgata and the saṅgha. Sudatta the brāhman uses countless skillful means to slander the Tathāgata, the Dharma, and the saṅgha, while his disciple Brahmadatta uses countless skillful means to praise the Tathāgata, the Dharma, and the saṅgha. Why is this? Because they have different views, different habits, and different close associations.' We gathered in the Dharma hall to discuss these matters."

时，诸比丘白佛言：「我等于乞食后集此讲堂，众共议言：『甚奇！甚特！如来有大神力，威德具足，尽知众生心志所趣。而今善念梵志及弟子梵摩达常随如来及与众僧，以无数方便毁谤如来及法、众僧，弟子梵摩达以无数方便称赞如来及法、众僧。所以者何？以其异见、异习、异亲近故。』向集讲堂议如是事。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "If there are skillful means to slander the Tathāgata, the Dharma, and the saṅgha, you should not harbor resentment, ill-will, or harmful intentions towards them. Why is this? If you harbor resentment, ill-will, or harmful intentions towards those who slander me, the Dharma, and the bhikṣu saṅgha, you will sink into self-destruction. Therefore, you should not harbor resentment, ill-will, or harmful intentions towards them. Bhikṣus, if someone praises the Buddha, the Dharma, and the bhikṣu saṅgha, you should not be overjoyed and delighted. Why is this? If you give rise to joy, you will sink into self-destruction. Therefore, you should not give rise to joy. Why is this? This is a minor matter of the precepts of deportment. Ordinary people with little learning do not understand the profound meaning. They simply praise what they see as it is.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「若有方便毁谤如来及法、众僧者，汝等不得怀忿结心，害意于彼，所以者何？若诽谤我、法及比丘僧，汝等怀忿结心，起害意者，则自陷溺，是故汝等不得怀忿结心，害意于彼。比丘若称誉佛及法、众僧者，汝等于中亦不足以为欢喜庆幸，所以者何？若汝等生欢喜心，即为陷溺，是故汝等不应生喜。所以者何？此是小缘威仪戒行，凡夫寡闻，不达深义，直以所见如实赞叹。

What is meant by the minor matter of the precepts of deportment, where ordinary people with little learning simply praise what they see as it is? They praise, 'The śramaṇa Gautama has eliminated killing, removed killing, abandoned weapons, has a heart of shame and remorse, and has compassion for all.' This is a minor matter of the precepts of deportment. Those ordinary people with little learning praise the Buddha for this. They also praise, 'The śramaṇa Gautama has abandoned taking what is not given, eliminated taking what is not given, and has no mind of theft.' They also praise, 'The śramaṇa Gautama has abandoned sexual desire, purified his practice of celibacy, guarded his precepts in one way, and does not indulge in sexual misconduct. His conduct is pure.' They also praise, 'The śramaṇa Gautama has abandoned and eliminated false speech. His words are sincere, his speech is true, and he does not deceive the world. The śramaṇa Gautama has abandoned and eliminated divisive speech. He does not use these words to create discord between others, or use those words to create discord between these. He is able to reconcile those who are in dispute, and increases the joy of those who are already reconciled. His words are not divisive. They are sincere and enter the heart. His words are timely. The śramaṇa Gautama has abandoned and eliminated harsh speech. If there are harsh words that harm others and increase their resentment and enmity, he does not do such harsh words. He always speaks kindly, pleasing to the hearts of people, loved by the many, and delightful to listen to. He only speaks such words. The śramaṇa Gautama has abandoned and eliminated frivolous speech. He speaks words that are timely, true, beneficial, Dharma-like, Vinaya-like, and words that stop non-Dharma. He only speaks such words.

「云何小缘威仪戒行，凡夫寡闻，直以所见如实称赞？彼赞叹言：『沙门瞿昙灭杀、除杀，舍于刀杖，怀慙愧心，慈愍一切。』此是小缘威仪戒行，彼寡闻凡夫以此叹佛。又叹：『沙门瞿昙舍不与取，灭不与取，无有盗心。』又叹：『沙门瞿昙舍于婬欲，净修梵行，一向护戒，不习婬逸，所行清洁。』又叹：『沙门瞿昙舍灭妄语，所言至诚，所说真实，不诳世人。沙门瞿昙舍灭两舌，不以此言坏乱于彼，不以彼言坏乱于此；有诤讼者能令和合，已和合者增其欢喜，有所言说不离和合，诚实入心，所言知时。沙门瞿昙舍灭恶口，若有麤言伤损于人，增彼结恨长怨憎者，如此麤言尽皆不为；常以善言悦可人心，众所爱乐，听无厌足，但说此言。沙门瞿昙舍灭绮语，知时之语、实语、利语、法语、律语、止非之语，但说是言。

The śramaṇa Gautama has abandoned drinking alcohol, is not attached to fragrant flowers, does not watch singing and dancing, does not sit on high beds, does not eat at improper times, does not hold gold and silver, does not keep wives, children, servants, and maids, does not keep elephants, horses, pigs, sheep, chickens, dogs, and other birds and animals, does not keep elephant troops, horse troops, chariot troops, and infantry, does not keep fields, houses, and cultivate the five grains, does not use fists to strike others, does not deceive others with measures and scales, also does not sell and take promissory notes, also does not take interest and give rise to unreasonable things, also does not secretly scheme with different faces and backs, does not go at improper times; for the sake of nourishing the body, he eats in moderation. Wherever he goes, his robes and alms bowl follow him, like a flying bird with wings and body together.' This is a minor matter of precepts. Those ordinary people with little learning praise the Buddha for this.

「『沙门瞿昙舍离饮酒，不著香华，不观歌舞，不坐高床，非时不食，不执金银，不畜妻息、僮仆、婢使，不畜象马、猪羊、鸡犬及诸鸟兽，不畜象兵、马兵、车兵、步兵，不畜田宅种殖五谷，不以手拳与人相加，不以斗秤欺诳于人，亦不贩卖券要断当，亦不取受觝债横生无端，亦不阴谋面背有异，非时不行；为身养寿，量腹而食，其所至处，衣钵随身，譬如飞鸟，羽翮身俱。』此是持戒小小因缘，彼寡闻凡夫以此叹佛。

The śramaṇa Gautama, having received the offerings of others, does not seek to accumulate more. He is never satisfied with clothing and food. Other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, having received the offerings of others, engage in occupations to support themselves. They plant trees that are relied upon by ghosts and spirits. The śramaṇa Gautama does not do such things. Other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, having received the offerings of others, seek further means to obtain various benefits, such as ivory, various treasures, high and wide large beds, various embroidered woolen blankets, and soft and thick bedding. The śramaṇa Gautama does not do such things. Other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, having received the offerings of others, seek further means to adorn themselves. They anoint their bodies with butter and oil, bathe with fragrant water, apply fragrant powder, comb their hair with fragrant oil, wear fine flower garlands, dye their eyes a dark blue color, wipe their faces to adorn them, keep their fingernails clean, look at themselves in a mirror, wear jeweled leather shoes, wear pure white upper garments, hold a parasol and a whisk, and adorn themselves with banners and flags. The śramaṇa Gautama does not do such things.

「如余沙门、婆罗门受他信施，更求储积，衣服饮食无有厌足，沙门瞿昙无有如此事。如余沙门、婆罗门食他信施，自营生业，种殖树木，鬼神所依，沙门瞿昙无如此事。如余沙门、婆罗门食他信施，更作方便，求诸利养，象牙、杂宝、高广大床、种种文绣、氍氀［毯－炎＋荅］［毯－炎＋登］、綩𫄧被褥，沙门瞿昙无如此事。如余沙门、婆罗门食他信施，更作方便，求自庄严，酥油摩身，香水洗浴，香末自涂，香泽梳头，著好华鬘，染目绀色，拭面庄饰，镮纽澡洁，以镜自照，著宝革屣，上服纯白，戴盖执拂，幢麾庄饰，沙门瞿昙无如此事。

The śramaṇa Gautama, having received the offerings of others, does not seek to accumulate more. He is never satisfied with clothing and food. Other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, having received the offerings of others, engage in occupations to support themselves. They plant trees that are relied upon by ghosts and spirits. The śramaṇa Gautama does not do such things. Other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, having received the offerings of others, seek further means to obtain various benefits, such as ivory, various treasures, high and wide large beds, various embroidered woolen blankets, and soft and thick bedding. The śramaṇa Gautama does not do such things. Other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, having received the offerings of others, seek further means to adorn themselves. They anoint their bodies with butter and oil, bathe with fragrant water, apply fragrant powder, comb their hair with fragrant oil, wear fine flower garlands, dye their eyes a dark blue color, wipe their faces to adorn them, keep their fingernails clean, look at themselves in a mirror, wear jeweled leather shoes, wear pure white upper garments, hold a parasol and a whisk, and adorn themselves with banners and flags. The śramaṇa Gautama does not do such things.

「如余沙门、婆罗门专为嬉戯，碁局博奕，八道、十道，至百千道，种种戯法以自娱乐，沙门瞿昙无如是事。如余沙门、婆罗门食他信施，但说遮道无益之言，王者、战鬪、军马之事，群僚、大臣、骑乘出入、游戯园观，及论卧起、行步、女人之事，衣服、饮食、亲里之事，又说入海采宝之事，沙门瞿昙无如此事。如余沙门、婆罗门食他信施，无数方便，但作邪命，谄谀美辞，现相毁呰，以利求利，沙门瞿昙无如此事。如余沙门、婆罗门食他信施，但共诤讼，或于园观，或在浴池，或于堂上，互相是非，言：『我知经律，汝无所知；我趣正道，汝趣邪径；以前著后，以后著前；我能忍，汝不能忍；汝所言说，皆不真正；若有所疑，当来问我，我尽能答。』沙门瞿昙无如是事。

The śramaṇa Gautama, having received the offerings of others, does not seek to accumulate more. He is never satisfied with clothing and food. Other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, having received the offerings of others, engage in occupations to support themselves. They plant trees that are relied upon by ghosts and spirits. The śramaṇa Gautama does not do such things. Other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, having received the offerings of others, seek further means to obtain various benefits, such as ivory, various treasures, high and wide large beds, various embroidered woolen blankets, and soft and thick bedding. The śramaṇa Gautama does not do such things. Other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, having received the offerings of others, seek further means to adorn themselves. They anoint their bodies with butter and oil, bathe with fragrant water, apply fragrant powder, comb their hair with fragrant oil, wear fine flower garlands, dye their eyes a dark blue color, wipe their faces to adorn them, keep their fingernails clean, look at themselves in a mirror, wear jeweled leather shoes, wear pure white upper garments, hold a parasol and a whisk, and adorn themselves with banners and flags. The śramaṇa Gautama does not do such things.

「如余沙门、婆罗门食他信施，更作方便，求为使命，若为王、王大臣、婆罗门、居士通信使，从此诣彼，从彼至此，持此信授彼，持彼信授此，或自为，或教他为，沙门瞿昙无如是事。如余沙门、婆罗门食他信施，但习战阵鬪诤之事，或习刀杖、弓矢之事，或鬪鸡犬、猪羊、象马、牛驼诸兽，或鬪男女，或作众声，吹声、鼓声、歌声、舞声，缘幢倒绝，种种伎戯，无不玩习，沙门瞿昙无如是事。如余沙门、婆罗门食他信施，行遮道法，邪命自活，瞻相男女，吉凶好丑，及相畜生，以求利养，沙门瞿昙无如是事。

The śramaṇa Gautama, having received the offerings of others, does not seek to accumulate more. He is never satisfied with clothing and food. Other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, having received the offerings of others, engage in occupations to support themselves. They plant trees that are relied upon by ghosts and spirits. The śramaṇa Gautama does not do such things. Other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, having received the offerings of others, seek further means to obtain various benefits, such as ivory, various treasures, high and wide large beds, various embroidered woolen blankets, and soft and thick bedding. The śramaṇa Gautama does not do such things. Other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, having received the offerings of others, seek further means to adorn themselves. They anoint their bodies with butter and oil, bathe with fragrant water, apply fragrant powder, comb their hair with fragrant oil, wear fine flower garlands, dye their eyes a dark blue color, wipe their faces to adorn them, keep their fingernails clean, look at themselves in a mirror, wear jeweled leather shoes, wear pure white upper garments, hold a parasol and a whisk, and adorn themselves with banners and flags. The śramaṇa Gautama does not do such things.

「如余沙门、婆罗门食他信施，行遮道法，邪命自活，召唤鬼神，或复驱遣，种种［袖－由＋厌］祷，无数方道，恐热于人，能聚能散，能苦能乐，又能为人安胎出衣，亦能呪人使作驴马，亦能使人聋盲瘖痖，现诸技术，叉手向日月，作诸苦行以求利养，沙门瞿昙无如是事。如余沙门、婆罗门食他信施，行遮道法，邪命自活，或为人呪病，或诵恶呪，或诵善呪，或为医方、针炙、药石，疗治众病，沙门瞿昙无如此事。如余沙门、婆罗门食他信施，行遮道法，邪命自活，或呪水火，或为鬼呪，或诵刹利呪，或诵象呪，或支节呪，或安宅符呪，或火烧、鼠囓能为解呪，或诵知死生书，或诵梦书，或相手面，或诵天文书，或诵一切音书，沙门瞿昙无如此事。如余沙门、婆罗门食他信施，行遮道法，邪命自活，瞻相天时，言雨不雨，谷贵谷贱，多病少病，恐怖安隐，或说地动、彗星、月蚀、日蚀，或言星蚀，或言不蚀，方面所在，皆能记之，沙门瞿昙无如此事。如余沙门、婆罗门食他信施，行遮道法，邪命自活，或言此国当胜，彼国不如，或言彼国当胜，此国不如，瞻相吉凶，说其盛衰，沙门瞿昙无如是事。诸比丘！此是持戒小小因缘，彼寡闻凡夫以此叹佛。」

"The Buddha told the monks, "There are other profound and subtle great Dharma lights, which only the wise and holy disciples can use these words to praise the Tathāgata. What are these profound and subtle great Dharma lights, which the wise and holy disciples can use these Dharma to praise the Tathāgata? All the śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who have views about the beginning and end of the eon, with various countless opinions, all fall into the sixty-two views. The views about the beginning and end of the eon, with various countless opinions, cannot go beyond the sixty-two views. What are the reasons for the śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas to have various countless opinions about the beginning and end of the eon, all falling into the sixty-two views, not going beyond them? All the śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who have views about the beginning of the eon, with various countless opinions, all fall into the eighteen views. The views about the beginning of the eon, with various countless opinions, cannot go beyond the eighteen views. What are the reasons for the śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas to have various countless opinions about the beginning of the eon, all falling into the eighteen views, not going beyond them? All the śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who have views about the beginning of the eon, hold the view of permanence, saying, 'I and the world are permanent.' This all falls into the four views. The views about the beginning of the eon, saying 'I and the world are permanent,' all fall into the four views, not going beyond them.

佛告诸比丘：「更有余法，甚深微妙大法光明，唯有贤圣弟子能以此言赞叹如来。何等是甚深微妙大光明法，贤圣弟子能以此法赞叹如来？诸有沙门、婆罗门于本劫本见、末劫末见，种种无数，随意所说，尽入六十二见中；本劫本见、末劫末见，种种无数，随意所说，尽不能出过六十二见中。彼沙门、婆罗门以何等缘，于本劫本见、末劫末见，种种无数，各随意说，尽入此六十二见中，齐是不过？诸沙门、婆罗门于本劫本见，种种无数，各随意说，尽入十八见中；本劫本见，种种无数，各随意说，尽不能过十八见中。彼沙门、婆罗门以何等缘，于本劫本见，种种无数，各随意说，尽入十八见中，齐此不过？诸沙门婆罗门于本劫本见，起常论，言：『我及世间常存。』此尽入四见中；于本劫本见言：『我及世间常存。』尽入四见，齐是不过。

What are the reasons for the śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas to have various countless opinions about the beginning of the eon, all falling into the eighteen views, not going beyond them? 「诸有沙门、婆罗门于末劫末见，无数种种，随意所说；于四十四见中，齐是不过，唯佛能知此诸见处，亦复如是。诸有沙门、婆罗门于本劫本见、末劫末见，无数种种，随意所说，尽入此六十二见中；于本劫本见、末劫末见，无数种种，随意所说；于六十二见中，齐此不过，唯如来知此见处，亦复如是。诸有沙门、婆罗门于本劫本见，生常论，说：『我、世间是常。』彼沙门、婆罗门于此生智，谓异信、异欲、异闻、异缘、异觉、异见、异定、异忍，因此生智，彼以希现则名为受，——乃至现在泥洹，亦复如是。诸有沙门、婆罗门生常论，言：『世间是常。』彼因受缘，起爱生爱而不自觉知，染著于爱，为爱所伏，——乃至现在泥洹，亦复如是。诸有沙门、婆罗门于本劫本见，生常论，言：『世间是常。』彼因触缘故，若离触缘而立论者，无有是处，——乃至现在泥洹，亦复如是。诸有沙门、婆罗门于本劫本见、末劫末见，各随所见说，彼尽入六十二见中，各随所见说，尽依中在中，齐是不过。犹如巧捕鱼师，以细目网覆小池上，当知池中水性之类，皆入网内，无逃避处，齐是不过。诸沙门、婆罗门亦复如是，于本劫本见、末劫末见，种种所说，尽入六十二见中，齐是不过。

"If a bhikṣu truly knows the origin, cessation, taste, fault, and escape of the six contacts, he will be supreme and transcend those views. The Tathāgata himself knows that birth and death are exhausted. The reason he has a body is to benefit and liberate devas and humans. If he had no body, devas and humans would have nothing to rely on. It is like a tāla tree whose head is cut off and will not grow again. The Buddha is also like this. Having already cut off birth and death, he will never be reborn again."

「若比丘于六触集、灭、味、过、出要，如实而知，则为最胜，出彼诸见。如来自知生死已尽，所以有身，为欲福度诸天、人故，若其无身，则诸天、世人无所恃怙，犹如多罗树断其头者，则不复生。佛亦如是，已断生死，永不复生。」

When the Buddha spoke this Dharma, the great trichiliocosm shook in six ways three times. At that time, Ānanda was behind the Buddha, holding a fan to fan him. He bared his right arm, knelt on his right knee, joined his palms, and said to the Buddha, "This Dharma is very profound. What should it be named? How should it be upheld?"

当佛说此法时，大千世界三返六种震动。尔时，阿难在佛后执扇扇佛，偏露右臂，长跪叉手，白佛言：「此法甚深，当以何名？云何奉持？」

The Buddha told Ānanda, "This sūtra should be named 'Shaking of Meaning,' 'Shaking of Dharma,' 'Shaking of Views,' 'Shaking of Māra,' and 'Shaking of Brahmā.'"

佛告阿难：「当名此经为义动、法动、见动、魔动、梵动。」

At that time, Ānanda heard what the Buddha said and joyfully upheld it.

尔时，阿难闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
