DĀ 22

Dīrghāgama 长阿含经

Chapter 3 第三分

22 （二二）种德经

Parallel to: DN 4 / MN 36 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Anga with a great assembly of 1,250 bhikṣus. He stopped to spend the night by the Gagga Pond near the city of Campā.

一时，佛在鸯伽国，与大比丘众千二百五十人俱。游行人间，止宿瞻婆城伽伽池侧。

At that time, there was a brāhman named Varṣākāra who lived in the city of Campā. The people of that city were numerous, prosperous, and abundant. King Prasenajit granted this city to the brāhman Varṣākāra as a share of the brahmanical portion. This brāhman had been pure in his seven generations of parents, not despised by others. He was proficient in the three sections of the non-Buddhist teachings and was able to distinguish various scriptures. He was well-versed in the subtle and obscure points of worldly literature and was also skilled in the methods of discerning the marks of great men, observing auspicious and inauspicious omens, and performing sacrificial rites. He had five hundred disciples whom he taught without ceasing.

时，有婆罗门，名曰种德，住瞻婆城，其城人民众多，炽盛丰乐，波斯匿王即封此城与种德婆罗门，以为梵分。此婆罗门七世以来父母真正，不为他人之所轻毁，异学三部讽诵通利，种种经书尽能分别，世典幽微靡不综练，又能善于大人相法、瞻候吉凶、祭祀仪礼，有五百弟子，教授不废。

At that time, the brāhmanas, elders, and householders in the city of Campā heard that the śramaṇa Gautama, the son of the Śākya clan, had left home, attained the Way, and was traveling through the human world from the country of Aṅga to the Gagga Pond near the city of Campā. His great name was widely known throughout the world. He was a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, endowed with the ten titles. Among the devas, humans, Māra, or Māra's followers, śramaṇas, and brāhmaṇas, he personally attained realization and taught the Dharma to others. His words, whether superior, middling, or inferior, were all truly genuine, endowed with the meaning and flavor, and his pure practice was complete. "Such a true person should be visited and seen. Now, I should go to meet him." Having said this, they immediately gathered together and left the city of Campā, following each other in groups, wishing to go to where the Buddha was.

时，瞻婆城内诸婆罗门、长者、居士闻沙门瞿昙释种子出家成道，从鸯伽国游行人间，至] cf.【丽】瞻婆城伽伽池侧，有大名称，流闻天下，如来、至真、等正觉，十号具足，于诸天、世人、魔、若魔、天、沙门、婆罗门中，自身作证，为他说法，上中下言，皆悉真正，义味具足，梵行清净。「如此真人应往觐现，今我宁可往与相见。」作此言已，即共相率，出瞻婆城，队队相随，欲往诣佛。

At that time, the brāhman Varṣākāra was on a high terrace. He saw from afar the people following each other in groups. He asked his attendant, "For what reason are these people following each other in groups? Where are they going?"

时，种德婆罗门在高台上，遥见众人队队相随，故问侍者：「彼诸人等以何因缘队队相随？欲何所至？」

The attendant replied, "I heard that the śramaṇa Gautama, the son of the Śākya clan, left home, attained the Way, and was traveling through the human world from the country of Aṅga to the Gagga Pond near the city of Campā. His great name was widely known throughout the world. He was a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, endowed with the ten titles. Among the devas, humans, Māra, or Māra's followers, śramaṇas, and brāhmaṇas, he personally attained realization and taught the Dharma to others. His words, whether superior, middling, or inferior, were all truly genuine, endowed with the meaning and flavor, and his pure practice was complete. The brāhmanas, elders, and householders of this city of Campā have gathered together and are following each other in groups, wishing to go and pay respects to the śramaṇa Gautama.

侍者白言：「我闻沙门瞿昙释种子出家成道，于鸯伽国游行人间，至瞻婆城伽伽池侧，有大名称，流闻天下，如来、至真、等正觉，十号具足，于诸天、世人、魔、若魔、天、沙门、婆罗门中自身作证，为他人说，上中下言，皆悉真正，义味具足，梵行清净；此瞻婆城诸婆罗门、长者、居士众聚相随，欲往问讯瞿昙沙门耳。」

At that time, the brāhman Varṣākāra immediately ordered his attendant, "Quickly go and tell the people in my voice, 'You all wait a moment. Wait until I go there. I will go together with you to where that Gautama is.'"

时，种德婆罗门即勅侍者：「汝速持我声，往语诸人：『卿等小住，须我往至，当共俱诣彼瞿昙所。』」

At that time, the attendant immediately used the voice of Varṣākāra to tell the people, "All of you wait a moment. Wait until I go there. I will go together with you to where that Gautama is."

时，彼侍者即以种德声，往语诸人言：「诸人且住，须我往到，当共俱诣彼瞿昙所。」

At that time, the people replied to the attendant, "Quickly go back and tell the brāhman, 'Now is the right time. It is appropriate to go together.'"

时，诸人报侍者言：「汝速还白婆罗门言：『今正是时，宜共行也。』」

The attendant returned and reported, "The people are already waiting. They say, 'Now is the right time. It is appropriate to go together.'" At that time, the brāhman Vṛṣākārin immediately descended from the platform and stood at the middle gate.

侍者还白：「诸人已住，言：『今正是时，宜共行也。』」时，种德婆罗门即便下台，至中门立。

At that time, there were five hundred other brāhmanas who had gathered at the gate for some minor reason. Seeing the brāhman Vṛṣākārin coming, they all stood up to welcome him and asked, "Great brāhman! Where are you going?"

时，有余婆罗门五百人，以少因缘，先集门下，见种德婆罗门来，皆悉起迎问言：「大婆罗门！欲何所至？」

Vṛṣākārin replied, "There is a śramaṇa Gautama, a son of the Śākya clan who has renounced the householder life and attained enlightenment. He is traveling through the human world in the country of Aṅga, staying by the Gagga Pond in the city of Campā. He has a great reputation that is heard throughout the world. He is a Tathāgata, an Arhat, a Perfectly Enlightened One, endowed with the ten titles. Among devas, humans, Māra, or Māra's followers, śramaṇas, and brāhmaṇas, he has personally realized and teaches the Dharma. His words, whether superior, middling, or inferior, are all truly genuine, endowed with the meaning and flavor of the Dharma, and his pure practice is complete. Such a true person should be visited and seen. I now wish to go and see him."

种德报言：「有沙门瞿昙释种子出家成道，于鸯伽国游行人间，至瞻婆城伽伽池侧，有大名称，流闻天下，如来、至真、等正觉，十号具足，于诸天、世人、魔、若魔、天、沙门、婆罗门中，自身作证，为他说法，上中下言，皆悉真正，义味具足，梵行清净。如是真人宜往觐现，我今欲往至彼相见。」

At that time, the five hundred brāhmaṇas immediately said to Vṛṣākārin, "Do not go to see him. Why is that? He should come to us, not us to him. Now, the great brāhmaṇa's parents for seven generations have been genuine and not despised by others. If one accomplishes this teaching, he should come to us, not us to him. Moreover, the great brāhmaṇa is well-versed in the three collections of the non-Buddhist teachings, is able to discern various scriptures, is well-versed in the subtle and obscure worldly texts, and is also skilled in the methods of discerning the marks of great men, auspicious and inauspicious omens, and sacrificial rituals. If one accomplishes this teaching, he should come to us, not us to him. Moreover, the great brāhmaṇa has an upright and handsome appearance, and has attained the appearance of Brahmā. If one accomplishes this teaching, he should come to us, not us to him. Moreover, the great brāhmaṇa has increased virtuous precepts and accomplished wisdom. If one accomplishes this teaching, he should come to us, not us to him.

时，五百婆罗门即白种德言：「勿往相见，所以者何？彼应诣此，此不应往。今大婆罗门七世以来父母真正，不为他人之所轻毁；若成就此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。又大婆罗门异学三部讽诵通利，种种经书皆能分别，世典幽微靡不综练，又能善于大人相法、瞻相吉凶、祭祀仪礼；成就此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。又大婆罗门颜貌端正，得梵色像；成就此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。又大婆罗门戒德增上，智慧成就；成就此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。

"Moreover, the great brāhmaṇa speaks softly and has eloquence and pure meaning and flavor. If one accomplishes this teaching, he should come to us, not us to him. Moreover, the great brāhmaṇa is a great teacher with many disciples. If one accomplishes this teaching, he should come to us, not us to him. Moreover, the great brāhmaṇa always teaches five hundred brāhmaṇas. If one accomplishes this teaching, he should come to us, not us to him. Moreover, students from all directions come to request and receive instruction on various skills and sacrificial methods, and he is able to answer them all. If one accomplishes this teaching, he should come to us, not us to him. Moreover, the great brāhmaṇa is respected and offered by King Prasenajit and King Bimbisāra. If one accomplishes this teaching, he should come to us, not us to him. Moreover, the great brāhmaṇa is wealthy with abundant treasures and overflowing storehouses. If one accomplishes this teaching, he should come to us, not us to him. Moreover, the great brāhmaṇa is wise and discerning, eloquent, and without timidity. If one accomplishes this teaching, he should come to us, not us to him."

「又大婆罗门所言柔和，辩才具足，义味清净；成就此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。又大婆罗门为大师，弟子众多；成就此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。又大婆罗门常教授五百婆罗门；成就此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。又大婆罗门四方学者皆来请受，问诸技术祭祀之法，皆能具答；成就此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。又大婆罗门为波斯匿王及瓶沙王恭敬供养；成就此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。又大婆罗门富有财宝，库藏盈溢；成就此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。又大婆罗门智慧明达，所言通利，无有怯弱；成就此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。」

At that time, Vṛṣākārin said to the brāhmaṇas, "Indeed! Indeed! As you have said, I possess these virtues, not without them. You should listen to me speak. The virtues of the śramaṇa Gautama, we should go to him, not him to us. The śramaṇa Gautama's parents for seven generations have been genuine and not despised by others. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, the śramaṇa Gautama has an upright and handsome appearance, born into a kṣatriya clan. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, the śramaṇa Gautama was born into a noble family and left home to practice the path. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, the śramaṇa Gautama has a complete radiant complexion, a genuine clan, and left home to practice the path. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, the śramaṇa Gautama was born into a wealthy family with great power, and left home to practice the path. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us.

尔时，种德告诸婆罗门曰：「如是！如是！如汝所言，我具有此德，非不有也。汝当听我说，沙门瞿昙所有功德，我等应往彼，彼不应来此。沙门瞿昙七世已来父母真正，不为他人之所轻毁；彼成就此法者，我等应往彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙颜貌端正，出刹利种；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙生尊贵处，出家为道；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙光色具足，种姓真正，出家修道；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙生财富家，有大威力，出家为道；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。

Moreover, the śramaṇa Gautama is endowed with the precepts of the noble ones and has accomplished wisdom. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, the śramaṇa Gautama is skilled in speech, gentle and soft. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, the śramaṇa Gautama is a guide for the multitude, with many disciples. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, the śramaṇa Gautama has forever extinguished desire and love, is without sudden outbursts, has removed worries and fears, his body hair does not stand on end, he is joyful and harmonious, when seen by people he is praised as good, he speaks well of the rewards of practice, and does not disparage other paths. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, the śramaṇa Gautama is always honored and offered by King Prasenajit and King Bimbisāra. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, the śramaṇa Gautama is honored and offered by the brāhmaṇa Vṛṣākārin, as well as by the brāhmaṇas Brahmadatta, Tārāpīḍa, and Śāṇḍilya, and by the young brāhmaṇa Śāriputra. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us.

「又沙门瞿昙具贤圣戒，智慧成就；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙，善于言语柔软和雅；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙，为众导师，弟子众多；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙，永灭欲爱，无有卒暴，忧畏已除，衣毛不竖，欢喜和悦，见人称善，善说行报，不毁余道；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙，恒为波斯匿王及瓶沙王礼敬供养；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙，为沸伽罗娑罗婆罗门礼敬供养，亦为梵婆罗门、多利遮婆罗门、锯齿婆罗门、首迦摩纳都耶子所见供养；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。

Moreover, the śramaṇa Gautama is revered and offered by his śrāvaka disciples, and is also respected by the devas and other ghost-spirits. The Śākyas, Kurus, Pañcālas, Vṛjis, Mallas, and Somas all revere him. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, the śramaṇa Gautama has caused King Prasenajit and King Bimbisāra to take the three refuges and five precepts. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, the śramaṇa Gautama has caused the brāhmaṇa Vṛṣākārin and others to take the three refuges and five precepts. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, the śramaṇa Gautama's disciples have taken the three refuges and five precepts, and the devas, Śākyas, Kurus, and others have also taken the three refuges and five precepts. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us.

「又沙门瞿昙为诸声闻弟子之所宗奉，礼敬供养，亦为诸天、余鬼神众之所恭敬，释种、俱利、冥宁、跋祇、末罗、酥摩皆悉宗奉；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙授波斯匿王及瓶沙王受三归五戒；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙授沸伽罗娑罗婆罗门等三归五戒；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙弟子受三自归五戒，诸天、释种、俱利等，皆受三归五戒；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。

Moreover, when the śramaṇa Gautama travels, he is revered and offered by all people. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, wherever the śramaṇa Gautama goes, he is offered by the people. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, wherever the śramaṇa Gautama goes, non-humans and ghosts dare not disturb him. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, wherever the śramaṇa Gautama goes, the people there see light and hear heavenly music. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, wherever the śramaṇa Gautama goes, when he is about to leave, the people are full of longing and weep as they see him off. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us.

「又沙门瞿昙游行之时，为一切人恭敬供养；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙所至城郭聚落，为人供养；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙所至之处，非人、鬼神不敢触娆；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙所至之处，其处人民皆见光明，闻天乐音；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙所至之处，若欲去时，众人恋慕，涕泣而送；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。

Moreover, when the śramaṇa Gautama first left home, his parents wept, loved, cherished, and lamented. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, the śramaṇa Gautama left home in his youth, abandoning all adornments, elephants, horses, jeweled carriages, the five desires, and necklaces. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, the śramaṇa Gautama renounced the position of a wheel-turning king to leave home and practice the path. If he had stayed at home, he would have ruled the four continents and all the people and wealth. We would all belong to him. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us.

「又沙门瞿昙初出家时，父母涕泣，爱惜恋恨；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙少壮出家，舍诸饰好、象马、宝车、五欲、璎珞；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙舍转轮王位，出家为道，若其在家，当居四天下，统领民物，我等皆属；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。

"Moreover, the śramaṇa Gautama is well-versed in the Dharma of the Brahmas and can explain it to others. He also converses with the Brahma Heaven. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, the śramaṇa Gautama is fully endowed with the thirty-two marks. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. Moreover, the śramaṇa Gautama has penetrating wisdom and is not timid. If he accomplishes this teaching, we should go to him, not him to us. That Gautama has now come to this city of Campā and is staying by the Gaggarā Lake. He is superior to us and is also a distinguished guest. We should go to pay our respects."

「又沙门瞿昙明解梵法，能为人说，亦与梵天往返言语；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙三十二相皆悉具足；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙智慧通达，无有怯弱；成就此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。彼瞿昙今来至此瞻婆城伽伽池侧，于我为尊，又是贵客，宜往亲觐。」

At that time, the five hundred brāhmanas said to Vṛddhimat: "How wonderful! How extraordinary! Are his virtues really like that? If he could accomplish just one of those virtues, he would not come. How much less so now that he has them all! We should all go together to pay our respects."

时，五百婆罗门白种德言：「甚奇！甚特！彼之功德乃如是耶？若彼于诸德中能成一者尚不应来，况今尽具！宜尽相率，共往问讯。」

Vṛddhimat replied, "If you wish to go, it is time to do so."

种德答言：「汝欲行者，宜知是时。」

At that time, Vṛddhimat immediately prepared a jeweled carriage, surrounded by five hundred brāhmanas, brāhmanas, elders, and householders from the city of Campā, and went to the Gaggarā Pond. Not far from the pond, he thought to himself: "If I ask Gautama, he may not be pleased. That śramaṇa Gautama will rebuke me, saying, 'You should ask like this, not like that.' If the people hear, they will think I am ignorant and damage my reputation. If that śramaṇa Gautama asks me about the meaning, my answer may not please him. That śramaṇa will rebuke me, saying, 'You should answer like this, not like that.' If the people hear, they will think I am ignorant and damage my reputation. If I remain silent and return from here, the people will say, 'He knows nothing.' He cannot even go to the place of the śramaṇa Gautama, damaging his reputation. If the śramaṇa Gautama asks me about the brāhmanic Dharma, I will answer Gautama to his satisfaction."

时，种德即严驾宝车，与五百婆罗门及瞻婆城诸婆罗门长者、居士，前后围遶，诣伽伽池。去池不远，自思惟言：「我设问瞿昙，或不可彼意，彼沙门瞿昙当呵我言：『应如是问，不应如是问。』众人闻者，谓我无智，损我名称。设沙门瞿昙问我义者，我答或不称彼意，彼沙门当呵我言：『应如是答，不应如是答。』众人闻者，谓我无智，损我名称。设我默然于此还者，众人当言：『此无所知。』竟不能至沙门瞿昙所，损我名称。若沙门瞿昙问我婆罗门法者，我答瞿昙足合其意耳。」

At that time, Vṛddhimat, having thought this by the Gaggarā Pond, then proceeded on foot to where the World-Honored One was. After paying respects, he sat to one side. At that time, the brāhmanas, elders, and householders from the city of Campā, some paid homage to the Buddha and sat, some inquired after his well-being and sat, some addressed him by name and sat, some joined their palms towards the Buddha and sat, some sat in silence. When the assembly was seated, the Buddha, knowing the thoughts in the mind of the brāhmaṇa Vṛddhimat, said to him, "What you are thinking, you may follow your wish!" The Buddha asked Vṛddhimat, "How many dharmas have you brāhmanas accomplished? Your words are true and not false.

时，种德于伽伽池侧作是念已，即便前行下车步进，至世尊所，问讯已，一面坐。时，瞻婆城诸婆罗门、长者、居士，或有礼佛而坐者，或有问讯而坐者，或有称名而坐者，或叉手向佛而坐者，或有默然而坐者。众坐既定，佛知种德婆罗门心中所念，而告之曰：「汝所念者，当随汝愿！」佛问种德：「汝婆罗门成就几法？所言诚实，能不虚妄。」

At that time, Vṛddhimat silently thought to himself, "How amazing! How extraordinary! The śramaṇa Gautama has great spiritual power, seeing into people's minds, asking me about the meaning as I was thinking."

尔时，种德默自念言：「甚奇！甚特！沙门瞿昙有大神力，乃见人心，如我所念而问我义。」

At that time, the brāhmaṇa Vṛddhimat sat upright, looked around the assembly, smiled broadly, and then replied to the Buddha, "I, a brāhmaṇa, have accomplished five dharmas, and what I say is sincere and not false. What are the five? First, a brāhmaṇa's parents for seven generations have been genuine and not despised by others. Second, he is proficient in reciting the three sections of the heterodox teachings, is able to distinguish various scriptures, is well-versed in the subtle and obscure points of worldly literature, and is also skilled in the methods of discerning the marks of great men, discerning auspicious and inauspicious omens, and the rites of sacrifice. Third, he has an upright and handsome appearance. Fourth, he is fully endowed with precepts. Fifth, he has penetrating wisdom. These are the five. Gautama! A brāhmaṇa who has accomplished these five dharmas speaks sincerely and without falsehood."

时，种德婆罗门端身正坐，四顾大众，熙怡而笑，方答佛言：「我婆罗门成就五法，所言至诚，无有虚妄。云何为五？一者婆罗门七世已来父母真正，不为他人之所轻毁。二者异学三部讽诵通利，种种经书尽能分别，世典幽微靡不综练，又能善于大人相法、明察吉凶、祭祀仪礼。三者颜貌端正。四者持戒具足。五者智慧通达。是为五。瞿昙！婆罗门成就此五法，所言诚实，无有虚妄。」

The Buddha said, "Excellent! Vṛddhimat! Is there a brāhmaṇa who abandons one of the five dharmas and accomplishes four, and still speaks sincerely and without falsehood, and is called a brāhmaṇa?"

佛言：「善哉！种德！颇有婆罗门于五法中舍一成四，亦所言诚实，无有虚妄，得名婆罗门耶？」

Vṛddhimat said to the Buddha, "There is. Why is that? Gautama! What is the use of birth? If a brāhmaṇa is proficient in reciting the three sections of the heterodox teachings, is able to distinguish various scriptures, is well-versed in the subtle and obscure points of worldly literature, and is also skilled in the methods of discerning the marks of great men, discerning auspicious and inauspicious omens, and the rites of sacrifice, has an upright and handsome appearance, is fully endowed with precepts, and has penetrating wisdom; if he has these four dharmas, then what he says is sincere and without falsehood, and is called a brāhmaṇa."

种德白佛言：「有。所以者何？瞿昙！何用生为？若婆罗门异学三部讽诵通利，种种经书尽能分别，世典幽微靡不综练，又能善于大人相法、明察吉凶、祭祀仪礼，颜貌端正，持戒具足，智慧通达；有此四法，则所言诚实，无有虚妄，名婆罗门。」

The Buddha said to Vṛddhimat, "Excellent! Excellent! If one abandons one of these four dharmas and accomplishes three, is what one says still sincere and without falsehood, and is one called a brāhmaṇa?"

佛告种德：「善哉！善哉！若于此四法中舍一成三者，亦所言诚实，无有虚妄，名婆罗门耶？」

Vṛddhimat replied, "There is. Why is that? What is the use of birth and recitation? If a brāhmaṇa has an upright and handsome appearance, is fully endowed with precepts, and has penetrating wisdom; if one accomplishes these three, what one says is sincere and without falsehood, and is called a brāhmaṇa."

种德报言：「有。所以者何？何用生、诵为？若婆罗门颜貌端正，持戒具足，智慧通达；成此三者，所言真诚，无有虚妄，名婆罗门。」

The Buddha said, "Excellent! Excellent! How is it? If one abandons one of the three dharmas and accomplishes two, is what one says still sincere and without falsehood, and is one called a brāhmaṇa?"

佛言：「善哉！善哉！云何？若于三法中舍一成二，彼亦所言至诚，无有虚妄，名婆罗门耶？」

He replied, "There is. Why is that? What is the use of birth, recitation, and upright appearance?"

答曰：「有。所以者何？何用生、诵及端正为？」

At that time, the five hundred brāhmaṇas each raised their voices and said to the brāhmaṇa Sūrata, "Why do you criticize birth, recitation, and upright appearance, saying they are useless?"

尔时，五百婆罗门各各举声，语种德婆罗门言：「何故呵止生、诵及与端正，谓为无用？」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the five hundred brāhmaṇas, "If the brāhmaṇa Sūrata has an ugly appearance, no lineage, is not proficient in recitation, lacks eloquence, wisdom, and good answers, and cannot speak with me, then you may speak. If Sūrata has a handsome appearance, a complete lineage, is proficient in recitation, has wisdom and eloquence, is good at answering questions, and is able to discuss the Dharma with me, then you should all be silent and listen to this person's words."

尔时，世尊告五百婆罗门曰：「若种德婆罗门容貌丑陋，无有种姓，讽诵不利，无有辩才、智慧、善答，不能与我言者，汝等可语；若种德颜貌端正，种姓具足，讽诵通利，智慧辩才，善于问答，足堪与我共论义者，汝等且默，听此人语。」

At that time, the brāhmaṇa Sūrata said to the Buddha, "May the Gautama please wait a moment! I will myself use the Dharma to instruct this person."

尔时，种德婆罗门白佛言：「唯愿瞿昙且小停止！我自以法往训此人。」

At that time, Sūrata said to the five hundred brāhmaṇas, "Here in this assembly is my nephew, the brāhmaṇa Aṅga. Do you see him? Now all the great assembly is gathered here. Except for the Gautama, who has a handsome appearance, none is equal to this young man. Yet this young man kills, steals, indulges in sex, is disrespectful, lies, deceives, burns people with fire, and obstructs the path to do evil. O brāhmaṇas! This Aṅga is fully endowed with all kinds of evil. Then what use is his recitation and handsome appearance?"

尔时，种德寻告五百婆罗门曰：「鸯伽摩纳今在此众中，是我外甥，汝等见不？今诸大众普共集此，唯除瞿昙颜貌端正，其余无及此摩纳者；而此摩纳杀生、偷盗、淫逸、无礼、虚妄、欺诳，以火烧人，断道为恶。诸婆罗门！此鸯伽摩纳众恶悉备，然则讽诵、端正，竟何用为？」

At that time, the five hundred brāhmaṇas remained silent and did not respond. Sūrata said to the Buddha, "If one upholds the precepts fully and has wisdom and insight, then what one says is sincere and without falsehood. One can be called a brāhmaṇa."

时，五百婆罗门默然不对。种德白佛言：「若持戒具足，智慧通达，则所言至诚，无有虚妄，得名婆罗门也。」

The Buddha said, "Excellent! Excellent! How is it? Sūrata! If one abandons one of the two dharmas and accomplishes the other, is what one says also sincere and without falsehood, and can one be called a brāhmaṇa?"

佛言：「善哉！善哉！云何？种德！若于二法中舍一成一，亦所言诚实，无有虚妄，名婆罗门耶？」

He replied, "No. Why? Precepts are wisdom, wisdom is precepts; only when there are precepts and wisdom can what one says be sincere and without falsehood. I say this is a brāhmaṇa."

答曰：「不得。所以者何？戒即智慧，智慧即戒；有戒有智，然后所言诚实，无有虚妄，我说名婆罗门。」

The Buddha said, "Excellent! Excellent! As you have said, when there are precepts, there is wisdom; when there is wisdom, there are precepts. Precepts can purify wisdom, wisdom can purify precepts. Sūrata! It is like washing one's hands, the left and right hands need each other. The left can purify the right, the right can purify the left. It is the same here. When there is wisdom, there are precepts; when there are precepts, there is wisdom. Precepts can purify wisdom, wisdom can purify precepts. Brāhmaṇa! One who is endowed with precepts and wisdom, I say is a bhikṣu."

佛言：「善哉！善哉！如汝所说，有戒则有慧，有慧则有戒；戒能净慧，慧能净戒。种德！如人洗手，左右相须，左能净右，右能净左。此亦如是，有慧则有戒，有戒则有慧，戒能净慧，慧能净戒。婆罗门！戒、慧具者，我说名比丘。」

At that time, the brāhmaṇa Sūrata said to the Buddha, "What are the precepts?"

尔时，种德婆罗门白佛言：「云何为戒？」

The Buddha said, "Listen carefully! Listen carefully! Consider it well, and I shall explain it to you one by one."

佛言：「谛听！谛听！善思念之，吾当为汝一一分别。」

He replied, "Yes, I wish to hear it."

对曰：「唯然！愿乐欲闻。」

At that time, the World Honored One told the brahmin, "When a Tathāgata appears in the world, an Arhat, a Perfectly Enlightened One, with perfect knowledge and conduct, a Sugata, a knower of the world, an unsurpassed guide for those who can be trained, a teacher of gods and humans, a Buddha, a Blessed One, he personally realizes and teaches others the Dharma that is good in the beginning, good in the middle, and good in the end, with the right meaning and phrasing, revealing a holy life that is perfectly complete and purified. If a householder or householder's son hears this Dharma, their faith becomes pure. With their faith pure, they contemplate thus: 'Living at home is difficult, like being in shackles; if one wishes to practice the holy life, one is not free. Now I should shave off my hair and beard, put on the three Dharma robes, leave home to practice the path.' At another time, they abandon their family wealth and property, give up their relatives, put on the three Dharma robes, remove their ornaments, recite the Vinaya, and fully observe the precepts. They abandon killing and do not kill, - - up to the four dhyānas of the mind, which they directly experience and delight in. Why is this? It is because of their diligent practice, single-minded mindfulness without forgetting, and delight in solitary seclusion, which they have attained. Brahmin! This is called 'fully observing the precepts.'"

尔时，世尊告婆罗门曰：「若如来出现于世，应供、正遍知、明行成、善逝、世间解、无上士、调御丈夫、天人师、佛、世尊，于诸天、世人、沙门、婆罗门中，自身作证，为他人说，上中下言，皆悉真正，义味具足，梵行清净。若长者、长者子闻此法者，信心清净；信心清净已，作如是观：『在家为难，譬如桎梏；欲修梵行，不得自在。今我宁可剃除须发，服三法衣，出家修道。』彼于异时舍家财业，弃捐亲族，服三法衣，去诸饰好，讽诵毘尼，具足戒律，舍杀不杀，——乃至心法四禅现得欢乐。所以者何？斯由精勤，专念不忘，乐独闲居之所得也。婆罗门！是为具戒。」

He asked again, "What is wisdom?"

又问：「云何为慧？」

The Buddha said, "If a bhikṣu, with a mind of samādhi, is pure and undefiled, gentle and subdued, abiding in the immovable state, - - up to attaining the three insights, removing ignorance, giving rise to the light of wisdom, extinguishing darkness, producing the great light of the Dharma, and attaining the knowledge of the exhaustion of taints. Why is this? It is because of their diligent practice, single-minded mindfulness without forgetting, and delight in solitary seclusion, which they have attained. Brahmin! This is called 'the perfection of wisdom.'"

佛言：「若比丘以三昧心清净无秽，柔软调伏，住不动处，——乃至得三明，除去无明，生于慧明，灭于暗冥，生大法光，出漏尽智。所以者何？斯由精勤，专念不忘，乐独闲居之所得也。婆罗门！是为智慧具足。」

At that time, the brāhman Vṛddhimatra said to the Buddha, "Now I take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṅgha. Please allow me to be an upāsaka in the true Dharma! From now on, for as long as I live, I will not kill, steal, engage in sexual misconduct, deceive, or drink alcohol."

时，种德婆罗门白佛言：「今我归依佛、法、圣众，唯愿听我于正法中为优婆塞！自今已后，尽形寿不杀、不盗、不婬、不欺、不饮酒。」

At that time, the brāhman Vṛddhimatra, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

时，种德婆罗门闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
