DĀ 23

Dīrghāgama 长阿含经

Chapter 3 第三分

23 （二三）究罗檀头经

Parallel to: DN 5

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Kusumapura with a great assembly of 1,250 bhikṣus. He arrived at the north of the village of Kusumapura, a brāhmaṇa village, and stayed overnight in the Śiśiravana forest.

一时，佛在俱萨罗国，与大比丘众千二百五十人俱，游行人间，至俱萨罗佉［少／兔］婆提婆罗门村北，止宿尸舍婆林中。

At that time, there was a brāhmaṇa named Jāṇussoṇi who lived in the village of Kusumapura. The village was prosperous and the people were thriving. There were gardens, ponds, and trees with cool shade. King Prasenajit granted this village to the brāhmaṇa Jāṇussoṇi as a brahmin's share. This brāhmaṇa had been born into a family of seven generations of pure ancestry, not despised by others. He was well-versed in the three collections of the non-Buddhist teachings and was able to discern various scriptures. He was well-versed in the subtle and obscure points of worldly literature and was also skilled in the methods of divining the characteristics of great men, predicting auspiciousness and inauspiciousness, and performing sacrificial rituals. He had five hundred disciples whom he taught without ceasing. At that time, the brāhmaṇa wished to perform a great sacrifice and had prepared five hundred bulls, five hundred cows, five hundred bull calves, five hundred heifer calves, five hundred ewes, and five hundred rams to be used as offerings.

时，有婆罗门名究罗檀头，止佉［少／兔］婆提村，其村丰乐，人民炽盛，园观浴池，树木清凉，波斯匿王即封此村与究罗檀头婆罗门，以为梵分。此婆罗门七世已来父母真正，不为他人之所轻毁，异学三部讽诵通利，种种经书尽能分别，世典幽微靡不综练，又能善于大人相法、瞻候吉凶、祭祀仪礼，有五百弟子，教授不废。时，婆罗门欲设大祀，办五百特牛、五百牸牛、五百特犊、五百牸犊、五百羖羊、五百羯羊，欲以供祀。

At that time, the brāhmaṇas, elders, and householders of the village of Kusumapura heard that the śramaṇa Gautama, the son of the Śākya clan, had renounced the householder's life, attained the path, and was traveling through the human realm from the country of Kusumapura. He had arrived at the north of the village of Kusumapura, at the Śiśiravana forest, and had attained great fame, renowned throughout the world. He was a Tathāgata, an Arhat, a Perfectly Enlightened One, endowed with the ten titles. Among the devas, humans, Māra, or Māra's followers, śramaṇas, and brāhmaṇas, he had personally realized and taught the Dharma. His words, whether superior, middling, or inferior, were all truly genuine, endowed with the meaning and taste of the Dharma, and pure in the practice of the Brahma-way. "Such a true person should be visited and seen. Now, we should go together to see him." Having said this, they immediately led each other out of the village of Kusumapura, following each other in groups, wishing to go to where the Buddha was.

时，佉［少／兔］婆提村诸婆罗门、长者、居士闻沙门瞿昙释种子出家成道，从俱萨罗国人间游行，至佉［少／兔］婆提村北尸舍林止，有大名称，流闻天下，如来、至真、等正觉，十号具足，于诸天、世人、魔、若魔、天、沙门、婆罗门中，自身作证，为他说法，上中下言，皆悉真正，义味具足，梵行清净。「如此真人，应往觐现，今我等宁可往共相见。」作此语已，即便相率，出佉［少／兔］婆提村，队队相随，欲诣佛所。

At that time, the brāhmaṇa Jāṇussoṇi was on a high tower. He saw the people following each other in groups from afar. He asked his attendant, "For what reason are these people following each other in groups? Where are they going?"

时，究罗檀头婆罗门在高楼上，遥见众人队队相随，顾问侍者：「彼诸人等，以何因缘队队相随？欲何所至？」

The attendant replied, "I heard that the śramaṇa Gautama, the son of the Śākya clan, renounced the householder's life, attained the path, and is traveling through the human realm from the country of Kusumapura. He has arrived at the north of the village of Kusumapura, at the Śiśiravana forest, and has attained great fame, renowned throughout the world. He is a Tathāgata, an Arhat, a Perfectly Enlightened One, endowed with the ten titles. Among the devas, humans, Māra, or Māra's followers, śramaṇas, and brāhmaṇas, he has personally realized and teaches the Dharma. His words, whether superior, middling, or inferior, are all truly genuine, endowed with the meaning and taste of the Dharma, and pure in the practice of the Brahma-way. The brāhmaṇas, elders, and householders of this village have gathered together and are following each other, wishing to go and pay their respects to the śramaṇa Gautama."

侍者白言：「我闻沙门瞿昙释种子出家成道，于俱萨罗国游行人间，诣佉［少／兔］婆提村北尸舍婆林中止，有大名称，流闻天下，如来、至真、等正觉，十号具足，于诸天、世人、魔、若魔、天、沙门、婆罗门中，自身作证，为他说法，上中下言，皆悉真正，义味具足，梵行清净。此村诸婆罗门、长者、居士众聚相随，欲往问讯沙门瞿昙耳。」

At that time, the brāhmaṇa Jāṇussoṇi ordered his attendant, "Quickly go and tell the people in my voice, 'You all wait a moment. Wait for me to go. I will go together with you to see the śramaṇa Gautama.'"

时，究罗檀头婆罗门即勅侍者：「汝速持我声，往语诸人言：『卿等小住，须待我往，当共俱诣沙门瞿昙。』」

At that time, the attendant immediately accepted the order and went to tell the people, "Wait! Wait for me to go. I will go together with you to see the śramaṇa Gautama."

时，彼侍者即承教命，往语诸人言：「且住！须我往到，当共俱诣沙门瞿昙所。」

The people replied to the messenger, "Quickly return and tell the brāhmaṇa, 'Now is the right time. Let us go together.'"

诸人报使者言：「汝速还白婆罗门：『今正是时，宜共行也。』」

The attendant returned and reported that the people were already waiting, saying, "Now is the right time. Let us go together." At that time, the brāhmaṇa then descended from the building and stood at the central gate.

侍者还白，诸人已住，言：「今正是时，宜共行也。」时，婆罗门即便下楼，出中门立。

At that time, there were five hundred other brāhmaṇas sitting outside the central gate, assisting Jūlakadāṇḍa in preparing for the great sacrifice. Seeing Jūlakadāṇḍa, they all stood up to welcome him and asked, "Great brāhmaṇa! Where are you going?"

时，有余婆罗门五百人在中门外坐，助究罗檀头施设大祀，见究罗檀头，皆悉起迎问言：「大婆罗门！欲何所至？」

They replied, "We have heard that the śramaṇa Gautama, the son of the Śākya clan, has renounced the householder's life and attained enlightenment. He is traveling in the human realm of the country of Kuru and has arrived at the Śiśupāvana grove north of the village of Kāsī. He has a great reputation that is well-known throughout the world. He is a Tathāgata, an Arhat, a Perfectly Enlightened One, endowed with the ten titles. Among devas, humans, śramaṇas, and brāhmaṇas, he has personally realized and teaches the Dharma to others. His words, whether superior, middling, or inferior, are all truly genuine, endowed with the meaning and taste of the Dharma, and his pure practice is complete. Such a true person is worthy of being visited and seen. O brāhmaṇas! I have also heard that Gautama knows the three kinds of sacrifices and the sixteen sacrificial implements. What our elders in the assembly cannot know, I now wish to perform a great sacrifice. The cattle and sheep are already prepared. I wish to go to Gautama to ask about the three kinds of sacrifices and the sixteen sacrificial implements. Once we obtain this method of sacrifice, our merits will be complete and our fame will spread far and wide."

报言：「我闻有沙门瞿昙释种子出家成道，于俱萨罗国人间游行，诣佉［少／兔］婆提村北尸舍婆林，有大名称，流闻天下，如来、至真、等正觉，十号具足，于诸天、世人、沙门、婆罗门中，自身作证，为人说法，上中下言，皆悉真正，义味具足，梵行清净。如此真人，宜往觐现。诸婆罗门！我又闻瞿昙知三种祭祀、十六祀具，今我众中先学旧识所不能知，我今欲大祭祀，牛羊已备，欲诣瞿昙问三种祭祀、十六祀具，我等得此祭祀法已，功德具足，名称远闻。」

At that time, the five hundred brāhmaṇas said to Jūlakadāṇḍa, "Master, do not go. Why is that? They should come here, not us going there. The master's parents for seven generations have been genuine and not disparaged by others. If one accomplishes this Dharma, they should come here, not us going to them."

时，五百婆罗门白究罗檀头言：「大师勿往，所以者何？彼应来此，此不应往。大师七世已来父母真正，不为他人之所轻毁，若成此法者，彼应来此，此不应诣彼。」

They also said, "Master, you are well-versed in the three collections of scriptures, proficient in various texts, and well-versed in the subtle and profound worldly classics. You are also skilled in the Dharma of the marks of a great person, in divining auspiciousness and inauspiciousness, and in sacrificial rites and ceremonies. If one accomplishes this Dharma, they should come here, not us going to them. Moreover, the master has an upright and handsome appearance, endowed with the marks of a Brahmā. If one accomplishes this Dharma, they should come here, not us going to them. Moreover, the master's precepts and virtues are superior, and he is accomplished in wisdom. If one accomplishes this Dharma, they should come here, not us going to them. Moreover, the master's words are gentle, and he is fully endowed with eloquence, with pure meaning and flavor. If one accomplishes this Dharma, they should come here, not us going to them."

又言：「大师异学三部讽诵通利，种种经书尽能分别，世典幽微无不综练，又能善于大人相法、瞻相吉凶、祭祀仪礼，成此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。又大师颜貌端正，得梵色像，成此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼，又大师戒德增上，智慧成就，成就此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。又大师所言柔和，辩才具足，义味清净，成此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。」

"Moreover, the master is the leader of the assembly, with many disciples. If one accomplishes this Dharma, they should come here, not us going to them. Moreover, the master always teaches five hundred brāhmaṇas. If one accomplishes this Dharma, they should come here, not us going to them. Moreover, students from the four directions all come to request and receive teachings, asking about various skills and sacrificial rites, and the master is able to answer them all. If one accomplishes this Dharma, they should come here, not us going to them. Moreover, the master is respected and offered to by King Prasenajit and King Bimbisāra. If one accomplishes this Dharma, they should come here, not us going to them. Moreover, the master is wealthy with treasures, and his treasury is overflowing. If one accomplishes this Dharma, they should come here, not us going to them. Moreover, the master is wise and discerning, and his words are eloquent and without timidity. If one accomplishes this Dharma, they should come here, not us going to them. If the master is fully endowed with these eleven Dharmas, they should come here, not us going to them."

「又大师为众导首，弟子众多，成此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。又大师常教授五百婆罗门，成此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。又大师四方学者皆来请受，问诸技术祭祀之法，皆能具答，成此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。又大师为波斯匿王及瓶沙王恭敬供养，成此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。又大师富有财宝，库藏盈溢，成此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。又大师智慧明达，所言通利，无有怯弱，成此法者，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。大师若具足此十一法，彼应诣此，此不应诣彼。」

Then Jāṇusṣuṇi said, "Yes, yes, as you say. I indeed have these virtues, not without them. You should listen to me speak further about the virtues accomplished by Śramaṇa Gautama. We should go to him, not him coming here. Śramaṇa Gautama's parents for seven generations have been genuine, not despised by others. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama has an upright and handsome appearance, born into a kṣatriya family. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama was born into a noble family and left home to practice the path. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama is fully endowed with radiance and has a genuine family lineage, leaving home to cultivate the path. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama was born into a wealthy family with great power, leaving home to cultivate the path. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here."

时，究罗檀头言：「如是，如是，如汝等言。我实有此德，非不有也，汝当复听我说，沙门瞿昙所成功德，我等应诣彼，彼不应来此。沙门瞿昙七世已来父母真正，不为他人之所轻毁，彼成此法者，我等应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙颜貌端正，出刹利种，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙生尊贵家，出家为道，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来此。又沙门瞿昙光明具足，种姓真正，出家修道，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙生财富家，有大威力，出家修道，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。」

"Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama is fully endowed with the precepts of the noble ones and has accomplished wisdom. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama is skilled in speech, gentle and harmonious. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama is the leader of the assembly, with many disciples. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama has forever extinguished desire and love, without any sudden outbursts. Worries and fears are already removed, and the hairs on his body do not stand on end. He is joyful and harmonious, praised by others as good. He speaks well of the rewards of practice and does not disparage other paths. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama is always respectfully offered to by King Prasenajit and King Bimbisāra. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama is respectfully offered to by the brāhmaṇa Vṛṣākāyava, as well as by the brāhmaṇas Brahmadatta, Tārāpīḍa, Vṛṣadhvaja, and the young brāhmaṇa Sañjayin. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here."

「又沙门瞿昙具贤圣戒，智慧成就，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙善于言语，柔软和雅，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙为众导师，弟子众多，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙永灭欲爱，无有卒暴，忧畏已除，衣毛不竖，欢喜和悦，见人称善，善说行报，不毁余道，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙常为波斯匿王及瓶沙王礼敬供养，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙为沸伽罗娑罗婆罗门礼敬供养，亦为梵婆罗门、多利遮婆罗门、种德婆罗门、首伽摩纳兜耶子恭敬供养，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。」

"Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama is revered and offered to by his śrāvaka disciples. He is also respected by the devas and spirits. The Śākyas, Kurus, Pañcālas, Vṛjis, Mallas, and Somas all revere him. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama has King Prasenajit and King Bimbisāra accept the three refuges and five precepts. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama has the brāhmaṇa Vṛṣākāyava and others accept the three refuges and five precepts. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama's disciples accept the three refuges and five precepts. The devas, Śākyas, Kurus, and others accept the three refuges and five precepts. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here."

「又沙门瞿昙为诸声闻弟子之所宗奉礼敬供养，亦为诸天及诸鬼神之所恭敬，释种、俱梨、冥宁、跋祇、末罗、苏摩皆悉宗奉，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙波斯匿王及瓶沙王受三归五戒，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙沸伽罗娑罗婆罗门等受三归五戒，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙弟子受三归五戒，诸天、释种、俱梨等，受三归五戒，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。」

"Moreover, wherever Śramaṇa Gautama goes, he is revered and offered to by all people. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, wherever Śramaṇa Gautama goes, whether to a city, town, or village, he is not without being moved by respect and offerings. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, wherever Śramaṇa Gautama goes, non-humans and spirits dare not disturb him. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, wherever Śramaṇa Gautama goes, the people there all see light and hear the sounds of heavenly music. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, wherever Śramaṇa Gautama goes, when he is about to leave, the people there all long for him, weep, and see him off. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here."

「又沙门瞿昙所游行处，为一切人恭敬供养，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙所至城郭村邑，无不倾动恭敬供养，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙所至之处，非人、鬼神不敢触娆，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙所至之处，其处人民皆见光明，闻天乐音，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙所至之处，若欲去时，众人恋慕，涕泣而送，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。」

"Moreover, when Śramaṇa Gautama first left home, his parents and relatives wept with longing. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama left home in his youth, abandoning all adornments, elephants, horses, jeweled carriages, the five desires, and necklaces. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama renounced the position of a wheel-turning king and left home to cultivate the path. If he had stayed at home, he would have been king of the four continents, ruling over the people and resources. We would all belong to him. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here."

「又沙门瞿昙初出家时，父母宗亲涕泣恋恨，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙少壮出家，舍诸饰好、象马、宝车、五欲、璎珞，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙舍转轮王位，出家修道，若其在家，王四天下，统领民物，我等皆属，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。」

"Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama is well-versed in the Brahma-dharma and can explain it to others. He also converses with Brahma. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama is well-versed in the three kinds of sacrifices and the sixteen sacrificial implements, which we elders do not know. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama is endowed with the thirty-two marks. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. Moreover, Śramaṇa Gautama is wise and penetrating, without timidity. If one accomplishes this Dharma, we should go to him, not him coming here. That Gautama has come to this village of Kūṭadanta. He is our superior and a distinguished guest. We should go to pay our respects."

「又沙门瞿昙明解梵法，能为人说，亦与梵天往返语言，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙明解三种祭祀、十六祀具，我等宿旧所不能知，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙三十二相具足，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。又沙门瞿昙智慧通达，无有怯弱，成此法者，我应诣彼，彼不应来。彼瞿昙来至此佉［少／兔］婆提村，于我为尊，又是贵客，宜往觐现。」

At that time, the five hundred brāhmanas said to Jūlakaṇṭha, "How wonderful! How extraordinary! Are his merits really like that? If Gautama had accomplished just one of those merits, he still should not have come. How much less so now that he has them all? We should all go together to pay our respects."

时，五百婆罗门白究罗檀头言：「甚奇！甚特！彼之功德乃如是耶？若使瞿昙于诸德中成就一者，尚不应来，况今尽具？宜尽相率，共往问讯。」

Jūlakaṇṭha said, "Those who wish to go should know the time has come."

究罗檀头言：「欲行者，宜知是时。」

At that time, the brāhman immediately prepared his jeweled carriage, surrounded by five hundred brāhmanas, and the brāhman householders and elders of Kūṭadanta, and went to the Śiśupāvana forest. Having arrived, he got off the carriage, walked to where the World-Honored One was, greeted him, and sat to one side. At that time, the brāhmanas, elders, and householders, some bowed to the Buddha and sat, some greeted him and sat, some called his name and sat, some joined their palms towards the Buddha and sat, and some sat in silence. When the assembly had all sat down, Jūlakaṇṭha said to the Buddha, "I wish to ask something. If you have the leisure, and if I may be allowed to ask, I will dare to ask."

时，婆罗门即严驾宝车，与五百婆罗门及佉［少／兔］婆提诸婆罗门长者、居士，前后围遶，诣尸舍婆林，到已下车，步进至世尊所，问讯已，一面坐。时，诸婆罗门、长者，居士，或有礼佛而坐者，或问讯而坐者，或有称名而坐者，或有叉手向佛而坐者，或有默然而坐者。众坐已定，究罗檀头白佛言：「欲有所问，若有闲暇得见听者，乃敢请问。」

The Buddha said, "Ask as you wish."

佛言：「随意所问。」

At that time, the brāhman said to the Buddha, "I have heard that Gautama is well-versed in the three kinds of sacrifices and the sixteen kinds of sacrificial implements, which our elders and ancestors were unable to know. We now wish to perform a great sacrifice. We have already prepared five hundred bulls, five hundred cows, five hundred bull calves, five hundred heifer calves, five hundred ewes, and five hundred rams. We wish to perform a sacrifice. Today, we have come to ask about the three sacrificial methods and the sixteen sacrificial implements. If we can accomplish this sacrifice, we will obtain great rewards, our fame will be widely known, and we will be respected by gods and humans."

时，婆罗门白佛言：「我闻瞿昙明解三种祭祀及十六种祭具，我等先宿耆旧所不能知，我等今者欲为大祭祀，已办五百特牛、五百牸牛、五百特犊、五百牸犊、五百羖羊、五百羯羊，欲以祭祀，今日顾来，问三祭法及十六祭具，若得成此祀者，得大果报，名称远闻，天人所敬。」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the brāhman Jūla-t'an-tou, "Listen attentively now, listen attentively, and think about it well. I shall explain it for you."

尔时，世尊告究罗檀头婆罗门曰：「汝今谛听，谛听，善思念之，当为汝说。」

The brāhman said, "Yes, Gautama! I wish to hear it with great joy."

婆罗门言：「唯然。瞿昙！愿乐欲闻。」

At that time, the Buddha said to Jūla-t'an-tou, "Long ago, in the distant past, there was a king of the kṣatriya caste who was of the water-sprinkling lineage. He wanted to perform a great sacrifice, so he gathered the brāhman ministers and said to them, 'I now have abundant wealth and treasures, and I indulge in the five desires. I am already old, but my troops are strong and fearless. My treasury is overflowing. I now wish to perform a great sacrifice. What do you ministers say is necessary for the sacrifice?' At that time, the ministers said to the king, 'Yes, Your Majesty! As the king says, the country is rich and the troops are strong, and the treasury is overflowing. But the people have many evil thoughts and practice many unwholesome things. If a sacrifice is performed at this time, it will not be in accordance with the Dharma of sacrifice. It is like sending a thief to chase a thief, which will not succeed. Your Majesty! Do not think that these are my people who can conquer, kill, rebuke, and stop. The neighboring kings should provide what they need, those who engage in commerce should provide their wealth and treasures, and those who cultivate fields should provide their oxen, calves, and seeds, so that they can each take care of themselves. If the king does not oppress the people, the people will be at ease, raise their children and grandchildren, and enjoy themselves together.'"

尔时，佛告究罗檀头曰：「乃往过去久远世时，有刹利王，水浇头种，欲设大祀，集婆罗门大臣而告之曰：『我今大有财宝具足，五欲自恣，年已朽迈，士众强盛，无有怯弱，库藏盈溢。今欲设大祀，汝等说祀法斯何所须？』时，彼大臣即白王言：『如是。大王！如王所言，国富兵强，库藏盈溢。但诸民物多怀恶心，习诸非法，若于此时而为祀者，不成祀法，如遣盗逐盗，则不成使。大王！勿作是念言，此是我民，能伐能杀，能呵能止。诸近王者当给其所须，诸治生者当给其财宝，诸修田业者当给其牛犊、种子，使彼各各自营。王不逼迫于民，则民人安隐，养育子孙，共相娱乐。』」

The Buddha said to Jūla-t'an-tou, "At that time, when the king heard the words of the ministers, he provided clothing and food for those close to him, wealth and treasures for those engaged in commerce, and oxen and seeds for those who cultivated fields. At that time, the people each took care of themselves, did not harm each other, raised their children and grandchildren, and enjoyed themselves together."

佛告究罗檀头：「时，王闻诸臣语已，诸亲近者给其衣食，诸有商贾给其财宝，修农田者给牛、种子。是时人民各各自营，不相侵恼，养育子孙，共相娱乐。」

The Buddha said, "At that time, the king again summoned the ministers and said to them, 'My country is rich and the troops are strong, and the treasury is overflowing. I provide for the people so that they have nothing lacking, and they raise their children and grandchildren and enjoy themselves together. I now wish to perform a great sacrifice. What do you ministers say is necessary for the sacrifice?' The ministers said to the king, 'Yes, yes, as the king says. The country is rich and the troops are strong, and the treasury is overflowing. I provide for the people so that they have nothing lacking, and they raise their children and grandchildren and enjoy themselves together. If the king wishes to perform a sacrifice, he should inform the inner palace.' At that time, the king entered and informed the inner palace, 'My country is rich and the troops are strong, and the treasury is overflowing. I have abundant wealth and treasures and wish to perform a great sacrifice.' At that time, the consorts immediately said to the king, 'Yes, yes, as the great king says. The country is rich and the troops are strong, and the treasury is overflowing. I have abundant treasures and wish to perform a great sacrifice. Now is the right time.' The king came out and reported to the ministers, 'My country is rich and the troops are strong, and the treasury is overflowing. I provide for the people so that they have nothing lacking, and they raise their children and grandchildren and enjoy themselves together. I now wish to perform a great sacrifice. I have already informed the inner palace. You should tell me what is needed in a moment.'"

佛言：「时，王复召诸臣语言：『我国富兵强，库藏盈溢，给诸人民，使无所乏，养育子孙，共相娱乐。我今欲设大祀，汝说祀法悉何所须？』诸臣白王：『如是，如是，如王所说。国富兵强，库藏盈溢，给诸人民，使其无乏，养育子孙，共相娱乐。王欲祀者，可语宫内使知。』时，王即如臣言，入语宫内：『我国富兵强，库藏盈溢，多有财宝，欲设大祀。』时，诸夫人寻白王言：『如是，如是，如大王言。国富兵强，库藏盈溢，多有珍宝，欲设大祀，今正是时。』王出报诸臣言：『我国富兵强，库藏盈溢，给诸人民，使其无乏，养育子孙，共相娱乐。今欲大祀，已语宫内，汝尽语我，斯须何物？』」

"At that time, the ministers immediately said to the king, 'Yes, yes, as the king says. If you wish to perform a great sacrifice, you should inform the crown prince, princes, ministers, and generals.' At that time, when the king heard the words of the ministers, he immediately informed the crown prince, princes, ministers, and generals, saying, 'My country is rich and the troops are strong, and the treasury is overflowing. I wish to perform a great sacrifice.' At that time, the crown prince, princes, and all the ministers and generals immediately said to the king, 'Yes, yes, great king! Now the country is rich and the troops are strong, and the treasury is overflowing. If you wish to perform a sacrifice, now is the right time.' At that time, the king again told the ministers, 'My country is rich and the troops are strong, and I have abundant wealth and treasures. I wish to perform a great sacrifice. I have already informed the inner palace, the crown prince, princes, and even the generals. Now I wish to perform a great sacrifice. What is needed?' The ministers said to the king, 'As the great king says, if you wish to perform a sacrifice, now is the right time.' Having heard these words, the king immediately built a new hall to the east of the city. The king entered the new hall, wore a deerskin robe, anointed his body with fragrant butter oil, and placed a deer horn on his head. He smeared cow dung on the ground and sat and lay on it, together with the first consort, brahmins, and ministers. They selected a yellow cow, and the king ate one of its teats, the consort ate one, the ministers ate one, and one was offered to the great assembly, while the rest was given to the calf. At that time, the king accomplished eight principles, and the ministers accomplished four principles."

「时，诸大臣即白王言：『如是，如是，如王所说。欲设大祀，已语宫内，而未语太子、皇子、大臣、将士，王当语之。』时，王闻诸臣语已，即语太子、皇子、群臣、将士言：『我国富兵强，库藏盈溢，欲设大祀。』时，太子、皇子及诸群臣、将士即白王言：『如是，如是，大王！今国富兵强，库藏盈溢，欲设祀者，今正是时。』时，王复告大臣曰：『我国富兵强，多有财宝，欲设大祀。已语宫内、太子、皇子，乃至将士，今欲大祀，斯何所须？』诸臣白王：『如大王言，欲设祀者，今正是时。』王闻语已，即于城东起新堂舍，王入新舍，被鹿皮衣，以香酥油涂摩其身，又以鹿角戴之头上，牛屎涂地，坐卧其上，及第一夫人、婆罗门、大臣，选一黄牸牛，一乳王食，一乳夫人食，一乳大臣食，一乳供养大众，余与犊子。时，王成就八法，大臣成就四法。」

"What are the eight principles accomplished by the king? For seven generations, the parents of that kṣatriya king were genuine and not despised by others. This is the first principle accomplished. That king has an upright appearance and is of the kṣatriya caste. This is the second principle. That king has increased virtuous precepts and is endowed with wisdom. This is the third principle. That king is skilled in various arts, such as riding elephants, horses, and chariots, and in the methods of wielding swords, spears, and bows and arrows, and he knows them all. This is the fourth principle. That king has great power and subdues all minor kings, who all submit. This is the fifth principle. That king is good at speaking and his words are gentle, with complete meaning and flavor. This is the sixth principle. That king has abundant wealth and treasures, and his treasury is overflowing. This is the seventh principle. That king is wise, courageous, and resolute, without any cowardice. This is the eighth principle. The kṣatriya king accomplishes these eight principles."

「云何王成就八法？彼刹利王七世以来父母真正，不为他人所见轻毁，是为成就初法。彼王颜貌端正，刹利种族，是为二法。彼王戒德增盛，智慧具足，是为三法。彼王习种种技术，乘象、马车、刀牟、弓矢、战鬪之法，无不具知，是为四法。彼王有大威力，摄诸小王，无不靡伏，是为五法。彼王善于言语，所说柔软，义味具足，是为六法。彼王多有财宝，库藏盈溢，是为七法。彼王智谋勇果，无复怯弱，是为八法。彼刹利种王，成此八法。」

"What are the four principles accomplished by the brahmin ministers? For seven generations, the parents of those brahmin ministers were genuine and not despised by others. This is the first principle. Furthermore, those ministers are well-versed in the three divisions of the Vedas, are able to discern various scriptures, are well-versed in the subtle and obscure worldly classics, and are also skilled in the methods of discerning the marks of great men, observing auspiciousness and inauspiciousness, and performing sacrificial rites. This is the second principle. Furthermore, those ministers are good at speaking and their words are gentle, with complete meaning and flavor. This is the third principle. Furthermore, those ministers are wise, courageous, and resolute, without any cowardice, and are well-versed in all sacrificial rites. This is the fourth principle. At that time, the king accomplished eight principles, and the brahmin ministers accomplished four principles. That king had four kinds of assistance, three sacrificial rites, and sixteen sacrificial implements."

「云何大臣成就四法？彼婆罗门大臣七世以来父母真正，不为他人所见轻毁，是为初法。复次，彼大臣异学三部讽诵通利，种种经书皆能分别，世典幽微靡不综练，又能善于大人相法、瞻察吉凶、祭祀仪礼，是为二法。复次，大臣善于言语，所说柔和，义味具足，是为三法。复次，大臣智谋勇果，无有怯弱，凡祭祀法无不解知，是为四法。时，彼王成就八法，婆罗门大臣成就四法，彼王有四援助、三祭祀法、十六祀具。」

"At that time, the brahmin ministers explained the king's intention in sixteen ways in the new palace, dispelling the king's doubts. What are the sixteen? The ministers said to the king, 'Some people say: "Now the kṣatriya king wants to perform a great sacrifice, but for seven generations his parents were not genuine and were always despised by others." Even if there are such words, they cannot defile the king. Why is that? The king's parents for seven generations were genuine and not despised by others.' 'Some people say: "Now the kṣatriya king wants to perform a great sacrifice, but his appearance is ugly and he is not of the kṣatriya caste." Even if there are such words, they cannot defile the king. Why is that? The king has an upright appearance and is of the kṣatriya caste.' 'Some people say: "Now the kṣatriya king wants to perform a great sacrifice, but he does not have superior precepts and is not endowed with wisdom." Even if there are such words, they cannot defile the king. Why is that? The king has increased virtuous precepts and is endowed with wisdom.' 'Some people say: "Now the kṣatriya king wants to perform a great sacrifice, but he is not skilled in various arts, such as riding elephants, horses, and chariots, and cannot understand the methods of various troops." Even if there are such words, they cannot defile the king. Why is that? The king is skilled in various arts, such as riding elephants, horses, and chariots, and in the methods of various troops, and he understands them all.' 'Some people say: "The king wants to perform a great sacrifice, but he does not have great power to subdue the minor kings." Even if there are such words, they cannot defile the king. Why is that? The king has great power to subdue the minor kings."

「时，婆罗门大臣于彼新舍，以十六事开解王意，除王疑想。云何十六？大臣白王，或有人言：『今刹利王欲为大祀，而七世以来父母不正，常为他人所见轻毁。』设有此言，不能污王。所以者何？王七世以来父母真正，不为他人之所轻毁。或有人言：『今刹利王欲为大祀，而颜貌丑陋，非刹利种。』设有此言，不能污王，所以者何？王颜貌端正，刹利种族。或有人言：『今刹利王欲为大祀，而无增上戒，智慧不具。』设有此言，不能污王，所以者何？王戒德增上，智慧具足。或有人言：『今刹利王欲为大祀，而不善诸术，乘象、马车、种种兵法不能解知。』设有此言，不能污王，所以者何？王善诸技术，战阵兵法，无不解知。或有人言：『王欲为大祀，而无大威力摄诸小王。』设有是言，不能污王，所以者何？王有大威力，摄诸小王。」

'Some people say: "The king wants to perform a great sacrifice, but he is not good at speaking, and his words are coarse and do not have complete meaning and flavor." Even if there are such words, they cannot defile the king. Why is that? The king is good at speaking, and his words are gentle, with complete meaning and flavor.' 'Some people say: "The king wants to perform a great sacrifice, but he does not have much wealth and treasure." Even if there are such words, they cannot defile the king. Why is that? The king's treasury is full and overflowing with wealth and treasure.' 'Some people say: "The king wants to perform a great sacrifice, but he is not wise, and his mind is timid and weak." Even if there are such words, they cannot defile the king. Why is that? The king is wise, courageous, and resolute, without any cowardice.' 'Some people say: "The king wants to perform a great sacrifice, but he does not speak to the inner palace." Even if there are such words, they cannot defile the king. Why is that? The king wants to perform a sacrifice, and he first speaks to the inner palace.' 'Some people say: "The king wants to perform a great sacrifice, but he does not speak to the crown prince and princes." Even if there are such words, they cannot defile the king. Why is that? The king wants to perform a sacrifice, and he first speaks to the crown prince and princes.' 'Some people say: "The king wants to perform a great sacrifice, but he does not speak to the ministers." Even if there are such words, they cannot defile the king. Why is that? The king wants to perform a great sacrifice, and he first speaks to the ministers.' 'Some people say: "The king wants to perform a great sacrifice, but he does not speak to the generals." Even if there are such words, they cannot defile the king. Why is that? The king wants to perform a sacrifice, and he first speaks to the generals.

「或有人言：『王欲大祀，而不善于言语，所说麤犷，义味不具。』设有此言，不能污王，所以者何？王善于言语，所说柔软，义味具足。或有人言：『王欲大祀，而无多财宝。』设有是言，不能污王，所以者何？王库藏盈溢，多有财宝。或有人言：『王欲大祀，而无智谋，志意怯弱。』设有是言，不能污王，所以者何？王智谋勇果，无有怯弱。或有人言：『王欲大祀，不语宫内。』设有是语，不能污王，所以者何？王欲祭祀，先语宫内。或有人言：『王欲大祀，而不语太子、皇子。』设有此言，不能污王，所以者何？王欲祭祀，先语太子、皇子。或有人言：『王欲大祀，不语群臣。』设有此言，不能污王，所以者何？王欲大祀，先语群臣。或有人言：『王欲大祀，不语将士。』设有此言不能污王，所以者何？王欲祭祀，先语将士。」

'Some people say: "The king wants to perform a great sacrifice, but the brahmin ministers for seven generations have parents who are not genuine and are always despised by others." Even if there are such words, they cannot defile the king. Why is that? My parents for seven generations are genuine and are not despised by others.' 'Some people say: "The king wants to perform a great sacrifice, but the ministers are not proficient in reciting the three divisions of the non-Buddhist teachings, cannot distinguish various scriptures, are not well-versed in the subtle and obscure of worldly texts, and are not good at the methods of discerning the marks of great men, observing auspicious and inauspicious omens, and performing sacrificial rites." Even if there are such words, they cannot defile the king. Why is that? I am proficient in reciting the three divisions of the non-Buddhist teachings, can distinguish various scriptures, am well-versed in the subtle and obscure of worldly texts, and am also good at the methods of discerning the marks of great men, observing auspicious and inauspicious omens, and performing sacrificial rites.' 'Some people say: "The king wants to perform a great sacrifice, but the ministers are not good at speaking, and their words are coarse and do not have complete meaning and flavor." Even if there are such words, they cannot defile the king. Why is that? I am good at speaking, and my words are gentle, with complete meaning and flavor.' 'Some people say: "The king wants to perform a great sacrifice, but the ministers are not endowed with wisdom, their minds are timid and weak, and they do not understand the methods of sacrifice." Even if there are such words, they cannot defile the king. Why is that? I am wise, courageous, and resolute, without any cowardice, and I understand all the methods of sacrifice.

「或有人言：『王欲大祀，而婆罗门大臣七世以来父母不正，常为他人之所轻毁。』设有是语，不能污王，所以者何？我七世以来父母真正，不为他人所见轻毁。或有人言：『王欲大祀，而大臣于异学三部讽诵不利，种种经书不能分别，世典幽微亦不综练，不能善于大人相法、瞻察吉凶、祭祀仪礼。』设有此言，不能污王，所以者何？我于三部异典讽诵通利，种种经书皆能分别，世典幽微靡不综练，又能善于大人相法、瞻察吉凶、祭祀仪礼。或有人言：『王欲大祀，而大臣不善言语，所说麤犷，义味不具。』设有此言，不能污王，所以者何？我善言语，所说柔和，义味具足。或有人言：『王欲大祀，而大臣智谋不具，志意怯弱，不解祀法。』设有是言，不能污王，所以者何？我智谋勇果，无有怯弱，凡祭祀法，无不解知。」

The Buddha told Jūla-t'an-t'ou: "The king had doubts about sixteen matters, but the ministers resolved the king's mind with sixteen matters."

佛告究罗檀头：「彼王于十六处有疑，而彼大臣以十六事开解王意。」

The Buddha said: "At that time, the great minister, in the new palace, instructed and benefited the king with ten practices. What are the ten? The great minister said: 'When the king performs a sacrifice, all those who come to the assembly, whether they have killed or not killed, are equally given. If those who have killed come, they are also given, and they themselves will know. If those who have not killed come, they are also given. This is why they are given, and they are given with such a mind. If those who come to the assembly have stolen, engaged in sexual misconduct, slandered, spoken harshly, lied, engaged in frivolous talk, been greedy, jealous, or had wrong views, they are also given. They themselves will know. If those who have not stolen, up to those with right views come, they are also given. This is why they are given, and they are given with such a mind.'"

佛言：「时，大臣于彼新舍，以十事行示教利喜于王。云何为十？大臣言：『王祭祀时，诸有杀生、不杀生来集会者，平等施与，若有杀生而来者，亦施与，彼自当知，不杀而来者，亦施与，为是故施，如是心施。若复有偷盗、邪婬、两舌、恶口、妄言、绮语、贪取、嫉妬、邪见来在会者，亦施与，彼自当知，若有不盗，乃至正见来者，亦施与，为是故施，如是心施。』」

The Buddha told the brāhmaṇa: "The great minister instructed and benefited the king with these ten practices."

佛告婆罗门：「彼大臣以此十行示教利喜。」

He further told the brāhmaṇa: "At that time, the kṣatriya king had three regretful thoughts in the new palace, which were eliminated by the great minister. What were the three? The king had the regretful thought: 'I have already performed a great sacrifice, will perform a great sacrifice, and am now performing a great sacrifice. This has caused great loss of wealth and treasures. I have these three thoughts and feel regret.' The great minister said: 'The king has already performed a great sacrifice, has already given, will give, and is now giving. It is not appropriate to feel regret about this meritorious sacrifice.' These were the three regretful thoughts the king had when entering the new palace, which were eliminated by the great minister."

又告婆罗门：「时，彼刹利王于彼新舍生三悔心，大臣灭之。云何为三？王生悔言：『我今大祀，已为大祀、当为大祀、今为大祀，多损财宝，起此三心，而怀悔恨。』大臣语言：『王已为大祀，已施、当施、今施，于此福祀不宜生悔。』是为王入新舍生三悔心，大臣灭之。」

The Buddha told the brāhmaṇa: "At that time, the kṣatriya king, the water-sprinkled head-anointed one, on the full moon day of the fifteenth, emerged from the new palace and lit a large fire pile in front of the palace. Holding an oil bottle in his hand, he poured oil onto the fire, chanting: 'Give, give.' At that time, the king's queen heard that the king, on the full moon day of the fifteenth, had emerged from the new palace and lit a large fire pile in front of the palace. Holding an oil bottle in his hand, he was pouring oil onto the fire, chanting: 'Give, give.' The queen and her attendants, bringing much wealth and treasures, came to the king and said: 'These various treasures will assist the king in performing the sacrifice.' Brāhmaṇa! The king then told the queen and attendants: 'Stop, stop! You have already made offerings. I have plenty of wealth and treasures to perform the sacrifice.' The queens and attendants thought to themselves: 'We should not take these treasures back to the palace. If the king performs a great sacrifice in the east, we will use them to assist.' Brāhmaṇa! Later, when the king performed a great sacrifice in the east, the queens and attendants used these treasures to assist in performing the great sacrifice."

佛告婆罗门：「尔时，刹利王水浇头种，以十五日月满时出彼新舍，于舍前露地然大火［卄／积］，手执油瓶注于火上，唱言：『与，与。』时，彼王夫人闻王以十五日月满时出新舍，于舍前然大火［卄／积］，手执油瓶注于火上，唱言：『与，与。』彼夫人、婇女多持财宝，来诣王所，而白王言：『此诸杂宝，助王为祀。』婆罗门！彼王寻告夫人、婇女言：『止！止！汝便为供养已，我自大有财宝，足以祭祀。』诸夫人、婇女自生念言：『我等不宜将此宝物还于宫中，若王于东方设大祀时，当用佐助。』婆罗门！其后王于东方设大祀时，夫人、婇女即以此宝物助设大祀。」

"At that time, the crown prince and princes heard that the king, on the full moon day of the fifteenth, had emerged from the new palace and lit a large fire pile in front of the palace. Holding an oil bottle in his hand, he was pouring oil onto the fire, chanting: 'Give, give.' The crown prince and princes, bringing much wealth and treasures, came to the king and said: 'Use these treasures to assist the king in performing the great sacrifice.' The king said: 'Stop, stop! You have already made offerings. I have plenty of wealth and treasures to perform the sacrifice.' The crown princes and princes thought to themselves: 'We should not take these treasures back. If the king performs a great sacrifice in the south, we will use them to assist.' In this way, the great ministers brought treasures, saying: 'We have already assisted the king in performing the great sacrifice in the west.' The generals brought treasures, saying: 'We have already assisted the king in performing the great sacrifice in the north.'"

「时，太子、皇子闻王十五日月满时出新舍，于舍前然大火［卄／积］，手执油瓶注于火上，唱言：『与，与。』彼太子、皇子多持财宝，来诣王所，白王言：『以此宝物，助王大祀。』王言：『止！止！汝便为供养已，我自大有财宝，足已祭祀。』诸太子、皇子自生念言：『我等不宜持此宝物还也，王若于南方设大祀者，当以佐助。』如是大臣持宝物来：『愿已助王祭祀西方。』将士持宝物来：『愿已助王祭祀北方。』」

The Buddha told the brāhmaṇa: "When that king performed the great sacrifice, he did not kill cows, sheep, or any other beings. He only used butter, milk, sesame oil, honey, molasses, and rock candy for the sacrifice."

佛告婆罗门：「彼王大祭祀时，不杀牛、羊及诸众生，唯用酥、乳、麻油、蜜、黑蜜、石蜜，以为祭祀。」

The Buddha told the brāhmaṇa: "When that kṣatriya king performed the great sacrifice, he was joyful at the beginning, joyful in the middle, and joyful at the end. This is the method for accomplishing the sacrifice."

佛告婆罗门：「彼刹利王为大祀时，初喜、中喜，后亦喜，此为成办祭祀之法。」

The Buddha told the brāhmaṇa: "After that kṣatriya king performed the great sacrifice, he shaved his beard and hair, put on the three Dharma robes, left home to practice the path, cultivated the four immeasurable minds, and when his body perished and his life ended, he was reborn in the Brahma Heaven. At that time, the king's wife, after performing the great giving, also shaved her hair, put on the three Dharma robes, left home to practice the path, practiced the four Brahma practices, and when her body perished and her life ended, she was reborn in the Brahma Heaven. The brāhmaṇa minister, after teaching the king to perform the sacrifice in the four directions, also performed the great giving, and then shaved his beard and hair, put on the three Dharma robes, left home to practice the path, practiced the four Brahma practices, and when his body perished and his life ended, he was reborn in the Brahma Heaven."

佛告婆罗门：「彼刹利王为大祀已，剃除须发，服三法衣，出家为道，修四无量心，身坏命终，生梵天上。时，王夫人为大施已，亦复除发，服三法衣，出家修道，行四梵行，身坏命终，生梵天上。婆罗门大臣教王四方祭祀已，亦为大施，然后剃除须发，服三法衣，出家修道，行四梵行，身坏命终，生梵天上。」

The Buddha told the brāhmaṇa: "At that time, the king performed the three sacrificial rites and the sixteen sacrificial implements, and accomplished the great sacrifice. What do you think about this?"

佛告婆罗门：「时，王为三祭祀法、十六祀具，而成大祀，于汝意云何？」

At that time, when Jāṇussoṇi heard the Buddha's words, he remained silent and did not reply. Then the five hundred brāhmaṇas said to Jāṇussoṇi: "The words of the Śramaṇa Gautama are subtle. Why does the master remain silent and not answer?"

时，究罗檀头闻佛言已，默然不对。时，五百婆罗门语究罗檀头言：「沙门瞿昙所言微妙，大师何故默然不答？」

Jāṇussoṇi replied: "The words of the Śramaṇa Gautama are subtle. I do not disagree with them. The reason I remain silent is because I am thinking to myself. The Śramaṇa Gautama speaks of this matter as if he has not heard it from others. I am silently thinking: 'Could the Śramaṇa Gautama be that kṣatriya king? Or is he that brāhmaṇa minister?'"

究罗檀头答言：「沙门瞿昙所说微妙，我非不然可，所以默然者。自思惟耳，沙门瞿昙说此事，不言从他闻，我默思惟：『沙门瞿昙将无是彼刹利王耶？或是彼婆罗门大臣耶？』」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to Jāṇussoṇi: "Excellent! Excellent! Your observation of the Tathāgata is indeed appropriate. At that time, the kṣatriya king who performed the great sacrifice, was he not someone else? Do not think so. It was I myself. At that time, I made great offerings and gave wisdom."

尔时，世尊告究罗檀头曰：「善哉！善哉！汝观如来，正得其宜。是时，刹利王为大祀者，岂异人乎？勿造斯观，即吾身是也，我于尔时极大施慧。」

Jāṇussoṇi said to the Buddha: "Are there greater rewards than these three sacrifices and the sixteen sacrificial implements?"

究罗檀头白佛言：「齐此三祭祀及十六祀具得大果报，复有胜者耶？」

The Buddha said: "There are."

佛言：「有。」

Jāṇussoṇi asked: "What are they?"

问曰：「何者是？」

The Buddha said: "If one can always make offerings to the Sangha, ensuring that it is not cut off, the merit is superior to those."

佛言：「于此三祭祀及十六祀具，若能常供养众僧，使不断者，功德胜彼。」

He further asked: "If one can always make offerings to the Sangha, ensuring that it is not cut off, and this merit is the most supreme, is there anything superior to this?"

又问：「于三祭祀及十六祀具，若能常供养众僧使不断者，为此功德最胜，复有胜者耶？」

The Buddha said: "There is."

佛言：「有。」

He further asked: "What is it?"

又问：「何者是？」

The Buddha said: "If one were to perform the three sacrifices and the sixteen offerings, and make offerings to the Sangha continuously, it would not be as good as building a monastery for the Sangha. This offering is the most supreme."

佛言：「若以三祭祀及十六祀具并供养众僧使不断者，不如为招提僧起僧房堂阁，此施最胜。」

He further asked: "Is there anything more supreme than performing the three sacrifices and the sixteen offerings, and making offerings to the Sangha continuously, as well as building a monastery for the Sangha?"

又问：「为三祭祀及十六祀具，并供养众僧使不断绝，及为招提僧起僧房堂阁，为此福最胜，复有胜者耶？」

The Buddha said: "There is."

佛言：「有。」

He further asked: "What is it?"

又问：「何者是？」

The Buddha said: "If one performs three kinds of sacrifices and sixteen sacrificial implements, and makes offerings to the Sangha continuously, and also builds monasteries and halls for the Sangha, it is not as good as giving rise to a joyful mind and speaking with one's own mouth: 'I take refuge in the Buddha, I take refuge in the Dharma, I take refuge in the Sangha.' This merit is the most supreme."

佛言：「若为三种祭祀、十六祀具，供养众僧使不断绝，及为招提僧起僧房堂阁，不如起欢喜心，口自发言：『我归依佛，归依法，归依僧。』此福最胜。」

He further asked: "Is this the limit of the three refuges to attain great rewards? Is there anything superior to this?"

又问：「齐此三归，得大果报耶？复有胜者？」

The Buddha said: "There is."

佛言：「有。」

He further asked: "What is it?"

又问：「何者是？」

The Buddha said: "If one joyfully accepts and practices the five precepts, not killing, not stealing, not engaging in sexual misconduct, not deceiving, and not drinking alcohol for the duration of one's life, this merit is supreme."

佛言：「若以欢喜心受、行五戒，尽形寿不杀、不盗、不婬、不欺、不饮酒，此福最胜。」

He further asked: "Up to these three sacrifices and the five precepts, can one attain great rewards? Is there anything superior to this?"

又问：「齐此三祀，至于五戒，得大果报耶？复有胜者？」

The Buddha said: "There is."

佛言：「有。」

He further asked: "What is it?"

又问：「何者是？」

The Buddha said: "If one can think of all sentient beings with a compassionate mind, even for the time it takes to milk a cow, that merit is supreme."

佛言：「若能以慈心念一切众生，如搆牛乳顷，其福最胜。」

He further asked: "Is the great fruit of compassion attained by these three sacrifices? Is there anything superior to this?"

又问：「齐此三祀，至于慈心，得大果报耶？复有胜者？」

The Buddha said: "There is."

佛言：「有。」

He further asked: "What is it?"

又问：「何者是？」

The Buddha said: "If a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One appears in the world, someone who leaves home to practice the path in the Buddha's teachings, who is fully endowed with all virtues, and who attains the three knowledges, eliminates all ignorance, and is fully endowed with wisdom, why is this? Because of not being negligent and enjoying solitude, this merit is supreme."

佛言：「若如来、至真、等正觉出现于世，有人于佛法中出家修道，众德悉备，乃至具足三明，灭诸痴冥，具足慧明，所以者何？以不放逸、乐闲静故，此福最胜。」

Jūtarandhu further said to the Buddha: "Gautama! I have prepared five hundred cows and sheep each for sacrifice, but now I release them all to roam freely following water and grass. I now take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha. Please allow me to be an upāsaka in the true Dharma. From now on, for as long as I live, I will not kill, steal, engage in sexual misconduct, deceive, or drink alcohol. I only wish that the World-Honored One and the great assembly would accept my invitation." At that time, the World-Honored One silently accepted.

究罗檀头又白佛言：「瞿昙！我为祭祀，具诸牛羊各五百头，今尽放舍，任其自游随逐水草。我今归依佛，归依法，归依僧，听我于正法中为优婆塞，自今以后，尽形寿不杀、不盗、不婬、不欺、不饮酒，唯愿世尊及诸大众时受我请。」尔时，世尊默然受之。

At that time, the brāhmaṇa saw that the Buddha silently accepted the invitation. He then rose, paid homage to the Buddha, circumambulated him three times, and left. Returning home, he prepared various delicacies. The next day, when the time came, the World-Honored One put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and together with a great assembly of 1,250 bhikṣus, went to the brāhmaṇa's house, took his seat, and sat down. At that time, the brāhmaṇa personally served and offered food to the Buddha and the saṃgha. After they had finished eating and put away their bowls, the Buddha spoke a verse for the brāhmaṇa:

时，婆罗门见佛默然受请已，即起礼佛，绕三匝而去，还家供办种种肴饍。明日时到，尔时，世尊著衣持钵，与大比丘众千二百五十人俱，诣婆罗门舍，就座而坐。时婆罗门手自斟酌，供佛及僧，食讫去钵，行澡水毕，佛为婆罗门而作颂曰：

Sacrificing to fire is supreme, chanting the hymns is supreme, the
king among men is supreme, the ocean among rivers is supreme
the moon among stars is supreme, the sun among lights is
supreme, above, below, and in the four directions, among all living
beings, among the gods and people of the world, only the
Buddha is supreme. Those who seek great merit should make offerings
to the Three Jewels.

「祭祀火为上，　　讽诵诗为上，人中王为上，　　众流海为上，星中月为上，　　光明日为上，上下及四方，　　诸有所生物，天及世间人，　　唯佛为最上，欲求大福者，　　当供养三宝。」

At that time, the brāhmaṇa Jāṇussoṇi took a small seat and sat in front of the Buddha. At that time, the World-Honored One gradually taught the Dharma for him, showing, teaching, benefiting, and delighting him, speaking about giving, precepts, and being born in heaven, showing the great suffering of desire, the hindrance of the upper defilements, and the supreme way of transcendence, distributing and revealing various holy life. At that time, the World-Honored One saw that the brāhmaṇa's mind was soft and pliable, with light and minimal afflictions, easily to be tamed. According to the constant practice of all Buddhas, he spoke about the truth of suffering, distinguishing and revealing it, speaking about the truth of the origin of suffering, the truth of the cessation of suffering, and the truth of the path of transcendence. At that time, the brāhmaṇa Jāṇussoṇi immediately removed dust and defilements on that seat, and attained the purity of the Dharma eye. Just as a clean white cloth is easy to be dyed, so was the brāhmaṇa Jāṇussoṇi. He saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, attained the fruit, and abided in certainty, not relying on others' faith, and attained fearlessness. He said to the Buddha, "I now take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṅgha for the third time. Please allow me to be an upāsaka in the true Dharma. From now on, for as long as I live, I will not kill, steal, engage in sexual misconduct, deceive, or drink alcohol."

尔时，究罗檀头婆罗门即取一小座于佛前坐。尔时世尊渐为说法，示教利喜，施论、戒论、生天之论，欲为大患，上漏为碍，出要为上，分布显示诸清净行。尔时，世尊观彼婆罗门志意柔软，阴盖轻微，易可调伏，如诸佛常法，为说苦谛，分别显示，说集圣谛、集灭圣谛、出要圣谛。时，究罗檀头婆罗门即于座上远尘离垢，得法眼净。犹如净洁白叠，易为受染，檀头婆罗门亦复如是，见法得法，获果定住，不由他信，得无所畏，而白佛言：「我今重再三归依佛、法、圣众，愿佛听我于正法中为优婆塞，自今已后，尽形寿不杀、不盗、不婬、不欺、不饮酒。」

He again said to the Buddha, "I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept my invitation for seven more days." At that time, the World-Honored One silently accepted. At that time, the brāhmaṇa personally served and offered to the Buddha and the Saṅgha for seven days. After seven days, the World-Honored One traveled through the human world.

重白佛言：「唯愿世尊更受我七日请。」尔时，世尊默然受之。时，婆罗门即于七日中，手自斟酌，供佛及僧，过七日已，世尊游行人间。

Not long after the Buddha left, the brāhmaṇa Jāṇussoṇi became ill and passed away. At that time, many bhikṣus heard that Jāṇussoṇi had offered to the Buddha for seven days, and not long after the Buddha left, he became ill and passed away. They thought to themselves, "Where will that person be reborn after death?" At that time, the bhikṣus went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed their heads at his feet, and sat to one side. "Where will Jāṇussoṇi be reborn after his death? "

佛去未久，时，究罗檀头婆罗门得病命终。时，众多比丘闻究罗檀头供养佛七日，佛去未久，得病命终，即自念言：「彼人命终，当何所趣？」时，诸比丘诣世尊所，头面礼足，于一面坐。「彼究罗檀头今者命终，当生何所？」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "That person purified the practice of the brahmacarya, accomplished the Dharma, and was not disturbed by the Dharma. Having severed the five lower fetters, he attained parinirvāṇa in that very life and will not return to this world."

佛告诸比丘：「彼人净修梵行，法法成就，亦不于法有所触娆，以断五下分结，于彼现般涅槃，不来此世。」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
