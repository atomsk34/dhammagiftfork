DĀ 25

Dīrghāgama 长阿含经

Chapter 3 第三分

25 （二五）倮形梵志经

Parallel to: DN 8

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying in the Golden Deer Park in the country of Vārāṇasī, together with a great assembly of 1,250 bhikṣus.

一时，佛在委若国金槃鹿野林中，与大比丘众千二百五十人俱。

At that time, there was a naked ascetic named Kāśyapa. He went to where the World-Honored One was, greeted him, and sat to one side. The naked Kāśyapa said to the Buddha, "I have heard that the śramaṇa Gautama criticizes all sacrificial rites and reviles those who practice austerities as filthy. Gautama, if someone says, 'The śramaṇa Gautama criticizes all sacrificial rites and reviles those who practice austerities as filthy,' is this person speaking the truth, speaking in accordance with the Dharma, and not slandering the śramaṇa Gautama?"

时，有倮形梵志姓迦叶，诣世尊所，问讯已，一面坐。倮形迦叶白佛言：「我闻沙门瞿昙呵责一切诸祭祀法，骂诸苦行人以为弊秽。瞿昙！若有言：『沙门瞿昙呵责一切诸祭祀法，骂苦行人以为弊秽。』作此言者，是为法语，法法成就，不诽谤沙门瞿昙耶？」

The Buddha said, "Kāśyapa! If they say, 'The śramaṇa Gautama criticizes all sacrificial rites and reviles those who practice austerities as filthy,' that is an incorrect statement, not in accordance with the Dharma. It is slandering me, not a truthful statement. Why is that? Kāśyapa! I see that some of those who practice austerities, when their bodies perish and their lives end, fall into hell. I also see that some who practice austerities, when their bodies perish and their lives end, are reborn in a good place in the heavens. I see that some who practice austerities enjoy practicing austerities, and when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in hell. I see that some who practice austerities enjoy practicing austerities, and when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in a good place in the heavens. Kāśyapa! I know and see the results of these two destinies. Should I then criticize those who practice austerities as filthy? I am speaking correctly, but they say it is incorrect. I am speaking correctly, but they say it is incorrect. Kāśyapa! There are some dharmas that śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas have in common, and there are some dharmas that śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas do not have in common. Kāśyapa! Those that they do not have in common, I set aside. Because those dharmas are not shared by śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas.

佛言：「迦叶！彼若言：『沙门瞿昙呵责一切诸祭祀法，骂苦行人以为弊秽。』者，彼非法言，非法法成就，为诽谤我，非诚实言。所以者何？迦叶！我见彼等苦行人，有身坏命终，堕地狱中者；又见苦行人身坏命终，生天善处者；或见苦行人乐为苦行，身坏命终，生地狱中者；或见苦行人乐为苦行，身坏命终，生天善处者。迦叶！我于此二趣所受报处，尽知尽见，我宁可呵责诸苦行者以为弊秽耶？我正说是，彼则言非，我正说非，彼则言是。迦叶！有法沙门、婆罗门同，有法沙门、婆罗门不同。迦叶！彼不同者，我则舍置，以此法不与沙门、婆罗门同故。

Kāśyapa! Those who are wise should contemplate thus: 'Among the unwholesome dharmas, the heavy defilements, the darkness, and the unwholesome dharmas, who among the teachers of other groups is able to extinguish these dharmas?' Kāśyapa! When those who are wise contemplate in this way, they know and see thus: 'Only the śramaṇa Gautama is able to extinguish these dharmas.' Kāśyapa! When those who are wise contemplate in this way, seek in this way, and discuss in this way, I then have a reputation.

「迦叶！彼有智者作如是观，沙门瞿昙于不善法、重浊、黑冥、非贤圣法，彼异众师于不善法、重浊、黑冥、非贤圣法，谁能堪任灭此法者？迦叶！彼有智者作是观时，如是知见，唯沙门瞿昙能灭是法。迦叶！彼有智者作如是观，如是推求，如是论时，我于此中则有名称。

Furthermore, Kāśyapa! Those who are wise should contemplate thus: 'Among the unwholesome dharmas, the heavy defilements, the darkness, and the unwholesome dharmas, who among the disciples of the teachers of other groups is able to extinguish these dharmas?' Kāśyapa! When those who are wise contemplate in this way, they know and see thus: 'Only the disciples of the śramaṇa Gautama are able to extinguish these dharmas.' Kāśyapa! When those who are wise contemplate in this way, seek in this way, and discuss in this way, my disciples then gain a reputation.

「复次，迦叶！彼有智者作如是观，沙门瞿昙弟子于不善法、重浊、黑冥、非贤圣法，彼异众师弟子于不善法、重浊、黑冥、非贤圣法，谁能堪任灭此法者？迦叶！彼有智者作如是观，如是知见，唯沙门瞿昙弟子能灭是法。迦叶！彼有智者作如是观，如是推求，如是论时，我弟子则得名称。

Furthermore, Kāśyapa! Those who are wise should contemplate thus: 'Among the wholesome dharmas, the pure, the subtle, and the noble dharmas, who among the teachers of other groups is able to increase and cultivate these dharmas?' Kāśyapa! When those who are wise contemplate in this way, they know and see thus: 'Only the śramaṇa Gautama is able to increase and cultivate these dharmas.' Kāśyapa! When those who are wise contemplate in this way, seek in this way, and discuss in this way, I then have a reputation.

「复次，迦叶！彼有智者作如是观，沙门瞿昙于诸善法、清白、微妙及贤圣法，彼异众师于诸善法、清白、微妙及贤圣法，谁能堪任增广修行者？迦叶！彼有智者作如是观，如是知见，唯有沙门瞿昙堪任增长修行是法。迦叶！彼有智者作如是观，如是推求，如是论时，我于此中则有名称。

Kāśyapa! Those who are wise should contemplate thus: 'Among the wholesome dharmas, the pure, the subtle, and the noble dharmas, who among the disciples of the teachers of other groups is able to increase and cultivate these dharmas?' Kāśyapa! When those who are wise contemplate in this way, they know and see thus: 'Only the disciples of the śramaṇa Gautama are able to increase and cultivate these dharmas.' Kāśyapa! When those who are wise contemplate in this way, seek in this way, and discuss in this way, my disciples then gain a reputation.

「迦叶！彼有智者作如是观，沙门瞿昙弟子于诸善法、清白、微妙及贤圣法，彼异众师弟子于诸善法、清白、微妙及贤圣法，谁能堪任增长修行者？迦叶！彼有智者作如是观，如是知见，唯有沙门瞿昙弟子能堪任增长修行是法。迦叶！彼有智者作如是观，如是推求，如是论时，于我弟子则有名称。迦叶！有道有迹，比丘于中修行，则自知自见，沙门瞿昙时说、实说、义说、法说、律说。

Kāśyapa! What is the path? What is the trace? When a bhikṣu practices in it, he knows and sees for himself that the śramaṇa Gautama speaks in a timely manner, speaks the truth, speaks the meaning, speaks the Dharma, and speaks the Vinaya.

「迦叶！何等是道？何等是迹？比丘于中修行，自知自见，沙门瞿昙时说、实说、义说、法说、律说。迦叶！于是比丘修念觉意，依止息，依无欲，依出要；修法、精进、喜、猗、定、舍觉意，依止息，依无欲，依出要。迦叶！是为道，是为迹，比丘于中修行，自知自见，沙门瞿昙时说、实说、义说、法说、律说。」

Kāśyapa said: "Gautama! There is only this path and trace, in which a bhikṣu practices and knows and sees for himself that the śramaṇa Gautama speaks in a timely manner, speaks the truth, speaks the meaning, speaks the Dharma, and speaks the Vinaya.

迦叶言：「瞿昙！唯有是道、是迹，比丘于中修行，自知自见。沙门瞿昙时说、实说、义说、法说、律说。但苦行秽污，有得婆罗门名，有得沙门名。何等是苦行秽污，有得婆罗门名，有得沙门名？瞿昙！离服倮形，以手自障蔽，不受夜食，不受朽食，不受两壁中间食，不受二] cf.【丽】人中间食，不受两刀中间食，不受两杇中间食，不受共食家食，不受怀姙家食，狗在门前不食其食，不受有蝇家食，不受请食。他言先识则不受其飡，不食鱼，不食肉，不饮酒，不两器食，一飡一咽，至七飡止，受人益食，不过七益，或一日一食，或二日、三日、四日、五日、六日、七日一食。或复食果，或复食莠，或食饭汁，或食麻米，或食［禾＊咸］稻，或食牛粪，或食鹿粪，或食树根枝叶花实，或食自落果；或披衣，或披莎衣，或衣树皮，或草襜身，或衣鹿皮，或留发，或被毛编，或著冢间衣；或有常举手者，或不坐床席，或有常蹲者，或有剃发留髭须者，或有卧荆棘上者，或有卧果蓏上者，或有倮形卧牛粪上者；或一日三浴，或一夜三浴，以无数苦，苦役此身。瞿昙！是为苦行秽污，或得沙门名，或得婆罗门名。」

The Buddha said: "Kāśyapa! Those who practice nakedness subject their bodies to countless hardships and difficulties, but their precepts are incomplete, their views are incomplete, and they are unable to practice diligently and extensively.

佛言：「迦叶！离服倮形者，以无数方便苦役此身，彼戒不具足，见不具足，不能勤修，亦不广普。」

Kāśyapa said to the Buddha: "What is meant by complete precepts? What is meant by complete views? What is the most subtle and supreme way beyond all ascetic practices?"

迦叶白佛言：「云何为戒具足？云何为见具足？过诸苦行，微妙第一。」

The Buddha told Kāśyapa: "Listen carefully and think about it well, and I will explain it to you."

佛告迦叶：「谛听，善思念之，当为汝说。」

Kāśyapa said: "Yes, Gautama! I wish to hear it with great joy."

迦叶言：「唯然。瞿昙！愿乐欲闻。」

The Buddha told Kāśyapa: "When a Tathāgata, Arhat, and Perfectly Enlightened One appears in the world, up to the fourth dhyāna, one attains happiness in the present life. Why is this? It is because of diligent practice, single-minded concentration, delighting in solitude, and being undistracted. Kāśyapa! This is the perfection of precepts, the perfection of view, surpassing all ascetic practices, and the most subtle."

佛告迦叶：「若如来、至真出现于世，乃至四禅，于现法中而得快乐。所以者何？斯由精勤，专念一心，乐于闲静，不放逸故。迦叶！是为戒具足，见具足，胜诸苦行，微妙第一。」

Kāśyapa said: "Gautama! Although you say that the perfection of precepts and the perfection of view surpass all ascetic practices and are the most subtle, but the Dharma of śramaṇas is difficult, and the Dharma of brāhmaṇas is difficult."

迦叶言：「瞿昙！虽曰戒具足，见具足，过诸苦行，微妙第一，但沙门法难，婆罗门法难。」

The Buddha said: "Kāśyapa! This is a worldly uncommon Dharma. Namely, the Dharma of śramaṇas and the Dharma of brāhmaṇas are difficult. Kāśyapa! Even up to the female lay followers can know this Dharma. Abandoning clothing and going naked, up to countless skillful means of tormenting the body, but they do not know the mind: whether it has an angry mind or not, a resentful mind or not, a harmful mind or not. If one knows this mind, one is not called a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa. It is because one does not know it that the śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa are difficult."

佛言：「迦叶！此是世间不共法。所谓沙门法、婆罗门法难，迦叶！乃至优婆夷亦能知此法。离服倮形，乃至无数方便苦役此身，但不知其心：为有恚心、为无恚心，有恨心、无恨心，有害心、无害心？若知此心者，不名沙门、婆罗门，为已不知故，沙门、婆罗门为难。」

At that time, Kāśyapa addressed the Buddha, saying: "What is a śramaṇa? What is a brāhmaṇa? Who has the perfection of precepts and the perfection of view, is supreme, excellent, and most subtle?"

尔时，迦叶白佛言：「何等是沙门、何等是婆罗门，戒具足、见具足，为上为胜，微妙第一？」

The Buddha told Kāśyapa: "Listen attentively, listen attentively, and consider it well. I shall explain it for you."

佛告迦叶：「谛听，谛听，善思念之，当为汝说。」

Kāśyapa said: "Yes, Gautama! I wish to hear it with great joy."

迦叶言：「唯然。瞿昙！愿乐欲闻。」

The Buddha said: "Kāśyapa! That bhikṣu, with a samādhi mind, attains the three insights, extinguishes all ignorance and darkness, and gives rise to the light of wisdom, namely, the knowledge of the exhaustion of taints. Why is this? It is because of diligent effort, single-minded mindfulness without forgetting, delighting in solitude and tranquility, and not being negligent. Kāśyapa! This is called a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who is fully endowed with precepts and views, the most supreme, the most excellent, the most subtle, and the foremost."

佛言：「迦叶！彼比丘以三昧心，乃至得三明，灭诸痴冥，生智慧明，所谓漏尽智生。所以者何？斯由精勤，专念不忘，乐独闲静，不放逸故。迦叶！此名沙门、婆罗门，戒具足、见具足，最胜最上，微妙第一。」

Kāśyapa said: "Gautama! Although you say that this is a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who is fully endowed with views and precepts, the most supreme, the most excellent, the most subtle, and the foremost, but the Dharma of śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas is very difficult! Very difficult! It is also difficult to know what a śramaṇa is, and also difficult to know what a brāhmaṇa is."

迦叶言：「瞿昙！虽言是沙门、婆罗门，见具足、戒具足，为上为胜，微妙第一。但沙门、婆罗门法，甚难！甚难！沙门亦难知，婆罗门亦难知。」

The Buddha told Kāśyapa: "Upāsakas can also practice this Dharma." He said: "From today onwards, I can give up wearing robes and nakedness, and even use countless means to torment this body. This practice cannot be called śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa. If this practice were called śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa, it would not be said that śramaṇa is very difficult and brāhmaṇa is very difficult. Because this practice is not śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa, it is said that śramaṇa is very difficult and brāhmaṇa is very difficult."

佛告迦叶：「优婆塞亦能修行此法。」白言：「我从今日能离服倮形，乃至以无数方便苦役此身，不可以此行名为沙门、婆罗门，若当以此行名为沙门、婆罗门者，不得言沙门甚难！婆罗门甚难！不以此行为沙门、婆罗门故，言沙门甚难！婆罗门甚难！」

The Buddha told Kāśyapa: "Once, I was in Rājagṛha, in the seven-leaf cave on a high mountain, and I spoke to the brāhmaṇa Nārada about pure ascetic practices. At that time, the brāhmaṇa was filled with joy and attained pure faith. He made offerings to me and praised me, making the foremost offerings and praises to me."

佛告迦叶：「我昔一时在罗阅祇，于高山七叶窟中，曾为尼俱陀梵志说清净苦行。时梵志生欢喜心，得清净信，供养我、称赞我，第一供养称赞于我。」

Kāśyapa said: "Gautama! Who would not give rise to the foremost joy, pure faith, offerings, and praises towards Gautama? I now also give rise to the foremost joy towards Gautama, attain pure faith, make offerings and praises, and take refuge in Gautama."

迦叶言：「瞿昙！谁于瞿昙不生第一欢喜、净信、供养、称赞者？我今于瞿昙亦生第一欢喜，得清净信，供养、称赞，归依瞿昙。」

The Buddha told Kāśyapa: "Among all the precepts in the world, there is none that is equal to this superior precept, let alone surpassing it. Among all the samādhis, wisdom, liberation, and liberating insight, there is none that is equal to this superior samādhi, wisdom, liberation, and liberating insight, let alone surpassing it. Kāśyapa! The so-called lion refers to the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One. When the Tathāgata expounds the Dharma extensively in the great assembly, he is self-assured and fearless, and thus is called a lion. What do you think, Kāśyapa? Do you think that when the Tathāgata roars like a lion, he is not courageous and valiant? Do not think so! The Tathāgata roars like a lion with courage and fearlessness. Kāśyapa! Do you think that when the Tathāgata roars like a courageous lion, he is not in the great assembly? Do not think so! The Tathāgata is in the great assembly, roaring like a courageous lion. Kāśyapa! Do you think that when the Tathāgata is in the great assembly, roaring like a courageous lion, he cannot expound the Dharma? Do not think so. Why? Because the Tathāgata is in the great assembly, roaring like a courageous and fearless lion, and is able to expound the Dharma well.

佛告迦叶：「诸世间诸所有戒，无有与此增上戒等者，况欲出其上？诸有三昧、智慧、解脱、见解脱慧，无有与此增上三昧、智慧、解脱、见解脱慧等者，况欲出其上？迦叶！所谓师子者，是如来、至真、等正觉，如来于大众中广说法时，自在无畏，故号师子。云何？迦叶！汝谓如来师子吼时不勇捍耶？勿造斯观，如来师子吼勇捍无畏。迦叶！汝谓如来勇捍师子吼时不在大众中耶？勿造斯观，如来在大众中勇捍师子吼。迦叶！汝谓如来在大众中作师子吼不能说法耶？勿造斯观。所以者何？如来在大众中勇捍无畏，作师子吼，善能说法。

What do you think, Kāśyapa? Do you think that when the Tathāgata is in the great assembly, courageous and fearless, roaring like a lion, skillfully expounding the Dharma, the assembly listening is not single-minded? Do not think so. Why? Because the Tathāgata is in the great assembly, courageous and fearless, roaring like a lion, skillfully expounding the Dharma, and the assembly that comes is all single-mindedly listening. What do you think, Kāśyapa? Do you think that when the Tathāgata is in the great assembly, courageous and fearless, roaring like a lion, skillfully expounding the Dharma, the assembly that comes is all single-mindedly listening, but does not joyfully believe and practice? Do not think so. Why? Because the Tathāgata is in the great assembly, courageous and powerful, roaring like a lion, skillfully expounding the Dharma, and the assembly that comes is all single-mindedly listening, joyfully believing and practicing. Kāśyapa! Do you think that when the Tathāgata is in the great assembly, courageous and fearless, roaring like a lion, skillfully expounding the Dharma, the assembly that comes joyfully believes and practices, but does not make offerings? Do not think so. Because the Tathāgata is in the great assembly, courageous and fearless, roaring like a lion, skillfully expounding the Dharma, the assembly that comes is all single-mindedly listening, joyfully believing and practicing, and making offerings.

「云何？迦叶！汝谓如来于大众中勇捍无畏，为师子吼，善能说法，众会听者不一心耶？勿造斯观。所以者何？如来在大众中勇捍无畏，为师子吼，善能说法，诸来会者皆一心听。云何？迦叶！汝谓如来在大众中勇捍无畏，为师子吼，善能说法，诸来会者皆一心听，而不欢喜信受行耶？勿造斯观。所以者何？如来在大众中勇捍多力，能师子吼，善能说法，诸来会者皆一心听，欢喜信受。迦叶！汝谓如来在大众中勇捍无畏，为师子吼，善能说法，诸来会者欢喜信受，而不供养耶？勿造斯观，如来在大众中勇捍无畏，为师子吼，善能说法，诸来会者皆一心听，欢喜信受，而设供养。

"Kāśyapa! Do you think that when the Tathāgata is in the great assembly, courageous and fearless, roaring like a lion, up to making offerings with faith and respect, he does not shave his beard and hair, put on the three Dharma robes, and leave home to cultivate the path? Do not think so. Why? Because the Tathāgata is in the great assembly, courageous and fearless, roaring like a lion, up to making offerings with faith and respect, shaving his beard and hair, putting on the three Dharma robes, and leaving home to cultivate the path. Kāśyapa! Do you think that when the Tathāgata is in the great assembly, courageous and fearless, up to leaving home to cultivate the path, he does not ultimately practice the Brahma way, reach the place of peace and tranquility, and attain nirvāṇa without remainder? Do not think so. Why? Because the Tathāgata is in the great assembly, courageous and fearless, up to leaving home to cultivate the path, ultimately practicing the Brahma way, reaching the place of peace and tranquility, and attaining nirvāṇa without remainder."

「迦叶！汝谓如来在大众中勇捍无畏，为师子吼，乃至信敬供养，而不剃除须发，服三法衣，出家修道耶？勿造斯观。所以者何？如来在大众中勇捍无畏，乃至信敬供养，剃除须发，服三法衣，出家修道。迦叶！汝谓如来在大众中勇捍无畏，乃至出家修道，而不究竟梵行，至安隐处，无余泥洹耶？勿造斯观。所以者何？如来于大众中勇捍无畏，乃至出家修道，究竟梵行，至安隐处，无余泥洹。」

At that time, Kāśyapa said to the Buddha, "How can I, Gautama, leave home and receive full ordination in this Dharma?"

时，迦叶白佛言：「云何？瞿昙！我得于此法中出家受具戒不？」

The Buddha told Kāśyapa, "If a non-Buddhist wishes to come to my Dharma to leave home and cultivate the path, they should be kept for four months to observe and be approved by the assembly, then they can leave home and receive the precepts. Kāśyapa! Although there is this rule, it is also to observe the person."

佛告迦叶：「若异学欲来入我法中出家修道者，当留四月观察，称可众意，然后当得出家受戒。迦叶！虽有是法，亦观其人耳。」

Kāśyapa said, "If a non-Buddhist wishes to come to the Buddha's Dharma to cultivate the brahma-conduct, they should be kept for four months to observe and be approved by the assembly, then they can leave home and receive the precepts. I can now observe for four years in the Buddha's Dharma, and be approved by the assembly, then I will leave home and receive the precepts."

迦叶言：「若有异学欲来入佛法中修梵行者，当留四月观察，称可众意，然后当得出家受戒。我今能于佛法中四岁观察，称可众意，然后乃出家受戒。」

The Buddha told Kāśyapa, "I have already said that it is only to observe the person."

佛告迦叶：「我已有言，但观其人耳。」

At that time, Kāśyapa immediately left home and received full ordination in the Buddha's Dharma. Not long after Kāśyapa received the precepts, with pure faith he cultivated the unsurpassed brahma-conduct, personally realized in this very life, with the end of birth and death, the establishment of the brahma-conduct, the accomplishment of what was to be done, and not accepting future existence, he then attained arahantship.

尔时，迦叶即于佛法中出家受具足戒。时，迦叶受戒未久，以净信心修无上梵行，现法中自身作证，生死已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不受后有，即成阿罗汉。

At that time, Kāśyapa, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，迦叶闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
