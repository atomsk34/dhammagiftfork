DĀ 26

Dīrghāgama 长阿含经

Chapter 3 第三分

26 （二六）三明经

Parallel to: DN 13

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Kusumapura, together with a great assembly of 1,250 bhikṣus. He arrived at the village of Icchaṅgalakuśāvatī, a brahmin village, and stayed in the Icchaṅgala Grove.

一时，佛在俱萨罗国人间游行，与大比丘众千二百五十人俱，诣伊车能伽罗俱萨罗婆罗门村，止宿伊车林中。

At that time, there were two brahmins named Vṛṣākapi and Tārukkha who were going to the village of Icchaṅgalakuśāvatī on some minor matter. This brahmin Vṛṣākapi had parents who were pure for seven generations, not despised by others. He was well-versed in the three Vedas and was proficient in various scriptures. He was also skilled in the methods of divining the characteristics of great men, observing auspicious and inauspicious omens, and performing sacrificial rites. He had five hundred disciples whom he taught without interruption. His disciple named Vāṣpa had parents who were pure for seven generations, not despised by others. He was well-versed in the three Vedas and was proficient in various scriptures. He was also skilled in the methods of divining the characteristics of great men, observing auspicious and inauspicious omens, and performing sacrificial rites. He also had five hundred disciples whom he taught without interruption.

时，有婆罗门名沸伽罗娑罗、婆罗门名多梨车，以小缘诣伊车能伽罗村。此沸伽罗娑罗婆罗门，七世以来父母真正，不为他人之所轻毁，异典三部讽诵通利，种种经书善能分别。又能善于大人相法、观察吉凶、祭祀仪礼，有五百弟子，教授不废。其一弟子名婆悉咤，七世以来父母真正，不为他人之所轻毁，异学三部讽诵通利，种种经书尽能分别，亦能善于大人相法，观察吉凶，祭祀仪礼，亦有五百弟子，教授不废。

The brahmin Tārukkha also had parents who were pure for seven generations, not despised by others. He was well-versed in the three Vedas and was proficient in various scriptures. He was also skilled in the methods of divining the characteristics of great men, observing auspicious and inauspicious omens, and performing sacrificial rites. He had five hundred disciples whom he taught without interruption. His disciple named Pāṭikāputra had parents who were pure for seven generations, not despised by others. He was well-versed in the three Vedas and was proficient in various scriptures. He was also skilled in the methods of divining the characteristics of great men, observing auspicious and inauspicious omens, and performing sacrificial rites. He also had five hundred disciples whom he taught without interruption.

多梨车婆罗门，亦七世已来父母真正，不为他人之所轻毁，异学三部讽诵通利，种种经书尽能分别，亦能善于大人相法、观察吉凶、祭祀仪礼，亦有五百弟子，教授不废。其一弟子名颇罗堕，七世已来父母真正，不为他人之所轻毁，异学三部讽诵通利，种种经书尽能分别，亦能善于大人相法、观察吉凶、祭祀仪礼，亦有五百弟子，教授不废。

At that time, the two, Vāṣpa and Pāṭikāputra, went to the garden in the morning and discussed the Dharma, criticizing each other. Vāṣpa said to Pāṭikāputra, "My path is true and can lead to liberation and reach the Brahma Heaven. This is what the great teacher Vṛṣākapi, the brahmin, says."

时，婆悉咤、颇罗堕二人于清旦至园中，遂共论义，更相是非。时，婆悉咤语颇罗堕：「我道真正，能得出要，至于梵天，此是大师沸伽罗娑罗婆罗门所说。」

Pāṭikāputra also said, "My path is true and can lead to liberation and reach the Brahma Heaven. This is what the great teacher Tārukkha, the brahmin, says." In this way, Vāṣpa asserted three times that his path was true, and Pāṭikāputra also asserted three times that his path was true. The two discussed together but could not reach a conclusion.

颇罗堕又言：「我道真正，能得出要，至于梵天，此是大师多梨车婆罗门所说。」如是，婆悉咤再三自称己道真正，颇罗堕亦再三自称己道真正，二人共论，各不能决。

At that time, Vāṣpa said to Pāṭikāputra, "I have heard that the śramaṇa Gautama, the son of the Śākya clan, renounced the householder life and attained the Way. He is traveling in the country of Kośala and is now in the Icchānaṅkala forest. He has a great reputation that is heard throughout the world. He is a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, endowed with the ten titles. Among devas, humans, Māra, or Māra's followers, śramaṇas, and brahmins, he has personally realized and teaches the Dharma to others. His words, whether superior, middling, or inferior, are all true and genuine, complete in meaning and flavor, and pure in the practice of the Brahma-way. Such a true person is worthy to be seen. I have heard that Gautama knows the path to the Brahma Heaven and can teach it to others. He always converses with Brahma. We should go together to Gautama and resolve this matter. If the śramaṇa Gautama speaks, we should accept it." At that time, Vāṣpa and Pāṭikāputra followed each other to the Icchānaṅkala forest, went to where the World-Honored One was, greeted him, and sat to one side.

时，婆悉咤语颇罗堕曰：「我闻沙门瞿昙释种子出家成道，于拘萨罗国游行人间，今在伊车能伽罗林中，有大名称，流闻天下，如来、至真、等正觉，十号具足。于诸天、世人、魔、若魔天、沙门、婆罗门中，自身作证，为他说法，上中下言，皆悉真正，义味具足，梵行清净。如是真人，宜往觐现。我闻彼瞿昙知梵天道，能为人说，常与梵天往返言语，我等当共诣彼瞿昙，共决此义。若沙门瞿昙有所言说，当共奉持。」尔时，婆悉咤、颇罗堕二人相随到伊车林中，诣世尊所，问讯已，一面坐。

At that time, the World-Honored One knew what the two of them were thinking in their minds, and he immediately said to Vāṣpa, "You two came to the garden in the morning and had this discussion, criticizing each other. One of you said, 'My Dharma is true and can lead to liberation and reach the Brahma Heaven. This is what the great teacher Vāsiṣṭha says.' The other said, 'My Dharma is true and can lead to liberation and reach the Brahma Heaven. This is what the great teacher Tārukkha says.' Did you have such a discussion three times, criticizing each other like this?"

尔时，世尊知彼二人心中所念，即告婆悉咤曰：「汝等二人清旦至园中，作如是论，共相是非。汝一人言：『我法真正，能得出要，至于梵天，此是大师沸伽罗娑罗所说。』彼一人言：『我法真正，能得出要，至于梵天，此是大师多梨车所说。』如是再三，更相是非，有如此事耶？」

At that time, Vāṣpa and Pāṭikāputra, hearing the Buddha's words, were all startled and their hair stood on end. They thought to themselves, "The śramaṇa Gautama has great divine power and knows the minds of others. What we wanted to discuss, the śramaṇa Gautama has already spoken about first." At that time, Vāṣpa said to the Buddha, "This path and that path both claim to be true and can lead to liberation and reach the Brahma Heaven. Is it what the brāhman Vāsiṣṭha says or is it what the brāhman Tārukkha says?"

时，婆悉咤、颇罗堕闻佛此言，皆悉惊愕，衣毛为竖，心自念言：「沙门瞿昙有大神德，先知人心，我等所欲论者，沙门瞿昙已先说讫。」时，婆悉咤白佛言：「此道、彼道皆称真正，皆得出要，至于梵天，为沸伽罗娑罗婆罗门所说为是？为多梨车婆罗门所说为是耶？」

The Buddha said, "Even if, Vāṣpa, this path and that path are true and can lead to liberation and reach the Brahma Heaven, why did you discuss and criticize each other in the garden in the morning, up to three times?"

佛言：「正使，婆悉咤！此道、彼道真正出要，得至梵天，汝等何为清旦园中共相是非，乃至再三耶？」

At that time, Vāṣpa said to the Buddha, "The brāhmaṇas with the three knowledges speak of various paths, such as the path of self-mastery, the path of self-creation, and the path of Brahma. These three paths all lead to the Brahma Heaven. Gautama, it is like a village, all the paths lead to the city. Although the brāhmaṇas speak of various paths, they all lead to the Brahma Heaven."

时，婆悉咤白佛言：「诸有三明婆罗门说种种道，自在欲道、自作道、梵天道，此三道者尽向梵天。瞿昙！譬如村营，所有诸道皆向于城，诸婆罗门虽说种种诸道，皆向梵天。」

The Buddha said to Vāṣpa, "Do all those paths lead to the Brahma Heaven?"

佛告婆悉咤：「彼诸道为尽趣梵天不？」

He replied, "They all lead there."

答曰：「尽趣。」

The Buddha asked a second and a third time, "Do all the various paths lead to the Brahma Heaven?"

佛复再三重问：「种种诸道尽趣梵天不？」

He replied, "They all lead there."

答曰：「尽趣。」

At that time, the World Honored One, having determined his words, said to Vasistha, "Among the three knowledges of the Brahmins, is there anyone who has seen the Brahma Heaven?"

尔时，世尊定其语已，告婆悉咤曰：「云何三明婆罗门中，颇有一人得见梵天者不？」

He replied, "There is no one who has seen it."

答曰：「无有见者。」

"Then, Vasistha, did your former teachers of the three knowledges of the Brahmins ever see the Brahma Heaven?"

「云何？婆悉咤！三明婆罗门先师，颇有得见梵天者不？」

He replied, "There is no one who has seen it."

答曰：「无有见者。」

"Then, Vasistha, did the former Brahmins of the three knowledges, who were well-versed in reciting and could teach others the old praises and recitations, the songs and books, such as the Brahmins Atthama, Bhamavati, Vibhasha, Iniratha, Shebhatika, Pubbasila, Kasyapa, Aruna, Gautama, Shuddhita, and Brahmadanda, did they also see the Brahma Heaven?"

「云何？婆悉咤！乃往三明仙人旧婆罗门，讽诵通利，能为人说旧诸赞诵，歌咏诗书，其名阿咤摩婆罗门、婆摩提婆婆罗门、毘婆审婆罗门、伊尼罗斯婆罗门、蛇婆提伽婆罗门、婆婆悉婆罗门、迦叶婆罗门、阿楼那婆罗门、瞿昙摩婆罗门、首脂婆罗门、婆罗损陀婆罗门，彼亦得见梵天不耶？」

He replied, "There is no one who has seen it."

答曰：「无有见者。」

The Buddha said, "If none of the Brahmins of the three knowledges have seen the Brahma Heaven, if none of the former teachers of the Brahmins of the three knowledges have seen the Brahma Heaven, and if none of the old great sages, the Brahmins of the three knowledges, such as Atthama and others, have seen the Brahma Heaven, then we should know that what the Brahmins of the three knowledges say is not true."

佛言：「若彼三明婆罗门无有一见梵天者，若三明婆罗门先师无有见梵天者，又诸旧大仙三明婆罗门阿咤摩等亦不见梵天者，当知三明婆罗门所说非实。」

He further said to Vasistha, "Suppose there is a lustful person who says, 'I have had sexual intercourse with that beautiful woman and praise the act of sex.' Others ask him, 'Do you know where she is? Is she in the east, west, south, or north?' He replies, 'I don't know.' They further ask, 'Do you know the land, city, or village where she lives?' He replies, 'I don't know.' They further ask, 'Do you know her parents and their family name?' He replies, 'I don't know.' They further ask, 'Do you know if she is a kshatriya, brahmin, vaishya, or shudra woman?' He replies, 'I don't know.' They further ask, 'Do you know if she is tall or short, fat or thin, black or white, beautiful or ugly?' He replies, 'I don't know.' What do you think, Vasistha? Is that person's praise true or not?"

又告婆悉咤：「如有] cf.【丽】婬人言：『我与彼端正女人交通，称叹婬法。』余人语言：『汝识彼女不？为在何处？东方、西方、南方、北方耶？』答曰：『不知。』又问：『汝知彼女所止土地城邑村落不？』答曰：『不知。』又问：『汝识彼女父母及其姓字不？』答曰：『不知。』又问：『汝知彼女为是刹利女，为是婆罗门、居士、首陀罗女耶？』答曰：『不知。』又问：『汝知彼女为长短、麤细、黑白、好丑耶？』答曰：『不知。』云何？婆悉咤！彼人赞叹为是实不？」

He replied, "It is not true."

答曰：「不实。」

"It is the same, Vasistha. The words of the three-versed brahmin are also like this, they are not true. What do you think, Vasistha? Do the three-versed brahmins see the sun and moon moving and rising and setting, and worship them with joined palms, saying, 'This path is truly correct, one can attain liberation and reach the sun and moon'?"

「如是。婆悉咤！三明婆罗门所说亦尔，无有实也。云何？婆悉咤！汝三明婆罗门见日月游行出没处所，叉手供养，能作是说：『此道真正，当得出要，至日月所。』不？」

He replied, "It is so. The three-versed brahmins see the sun and moon moving and rising and setting, and worship them with joined palms, but they cannot say, 'This path is truly correct, one can attain liberation and reach the sun and moon.'"

报曰：「如是。三明婆罗门见日月游行出没处所，叉手供养，而不能言：『此道真正，当得出要，至日月所。』也。」

"It is so, Vasistha. The three-versed brahmins see the sun and moon moving and rising and setting, and worship them with joined palms, but they cannot say, 'This path is truly correct, one can attain liberation and reach the sun and moon.' They always worship and respect with joined palms. Is this not false?"

「如是。婆悉咤！三明婆罗门见日月游行出没之处，叉手供养，而不能说：『此道真正，当得出要，至日月所。』而常叉手供养恭敬，岂非虚妄耶？」

He replied, "It is so, Gautama. They are indeed false."

答曰：「如是。瞿昙！彼实虚妄。」

The Buddha said, "It is like a person building a ladder on empty ground. Others ask, 'Why are you building a ladder?' They reply, 'I want to climb to the hall.' They are then asked, 'Where is the hall? Is it east, west, south, or north?' They say, 'I don't know.' What do you think, Vasistha? Is this person building a ladder to climb to a hall not false?"

佛言：「譬如有人立梯空地，余人问言：『立梯用为。』答曰：『我欲上堂。』又问：『堂何所在？东、西、南、北耶？』答云：『不知。』云何？婆悉咤！此人立梯欲上堂者，岂非虚妄耶？」

He replied, "It is so, it is indeed false."

答曰：「如是，彼实虚妄。」

The Buddha said, "The three knowledges of the Brahmins are also like this, false and unreal. Vasistha! The five desires are pure and very delightful. What are the five? The eyes see forms, which are very delightful; the ears hear sounds, the nose smells fragrances, the tongue tastes flavors, and the body feels tactile sensations, which are very delightful. In my wise and holy Dharma, they are attachments, bonds, and fetters. Those three-knowledge Brahmins are stained by the five desires, attached to them firmly, not seeing their faults, not knowing how to escape them. They are bound by the five desires. Even if they serve the sun, moon, water, and fire, and say, 'Please help me go to be reborn in the Brahma Heaven,' it is impossible. It is like the Airavana River, whose water level is even with the banks, allowing birds to drink. If a person on this bank is heavily bound and calls out to the other bank, 'Come and ferry me across,' would the other bank come to ferry that person across?"

佛言：「三明婆罗门亦复如是，虚诳无实。婆悉咤！五欲洁净，甚可爱乐。云何为五？眼见色，甚可爱乐；耳声、鼻香、舌味、身触，甚可爱乐。于我贤圣法中，为著、为缚，为是拘锁。彼三明婆罗门为五欲所染，爱著坚固，不见过失，不知出要，彼为五欲之所系缚。正使奉事日月水火，唱言：『扶接我去生梵天者。』无有是处。譬如阿夷罗河，其水平岸，乌鸟得饮，有人在此岸身被重系，空唤彼岸言：『来渡我去。』彼岸宁来渡此人不？」

He replied, "No, it would not."

答曰：「不也。」

Vasistha! The five desires are pure and very delightful. In the wise and holy Dharma, they are like fetters. Those three-knowledge Brahmins are stained by the five desires, attached to them firmly, not seeing their faults, not knowing how to escape them. They are bound by the five desires. Even if they serve the sun, moon, water, and fire, and say, 'Please help me go to be reborn in the Brahma Heaven,' it is also like this, ultimately impossible. Vasistha! It is like the Airavana River, whose water level is even with the banks, allowing birds to drink. If a person wants to cross, but does not use their hands, feet, or body strength, and does not rely on a boat or raft, can they cross?

「婆悉咤！五欲洁净，甚可爱乐，于贤圣法中犹如拘锁。彼三明婆罗门为五欲所染，爱著坚固，不见过失，不知出要。彼为五欲之所系缚，正使奉事日月水火，唱言：『扶接我去生梵天上。』亦复如是，终无是处。婆悉咤！譬如阿夷罗河，其水平岸，乌鸟得饮，有人欲渡，不以手足身力，不因船栰，能得渡不？」

He replied, "No, they cannot."

答曰：「不能。」

Vasistha! The three-knowledge Brahmins are also like this. If they do not cultivate the pure and holy practices of a monk, but cultivate other impure practices, and wish to be reborn in the Brahma Heaven, it is impossible. Vasistha! It is like a mountain stream suddenly rising, carrying away many people, and there are no boats or rafts, nor any bridges. If a traveler comes and wants to cross to the other shore, seeing the mountain stream suddenly rising, carrying away many people, and there are no boats or rafts, nor any bridges, that person thinks, 'I should now gather a lot of grass and wood, tightly bind a raft, and use my own strength to cross to the other shore.' Then they quickly bind a raft and use their own strength to safely cross. Vasistha! It is also like this. If a monk abandons impure practices that are not of a monk, and practices the pure and holy practices of a monk, and wishes to be reborn in the Brahma Heaven, it is possible. What do you think, Vasistha? Does the Brahma Heaven have an angry mind or not?

「婆悉咤！三明婆罗门亦复如是，不修沙门清净梵行，更修余道不清净行，欲求生梵天者，无有是处。婆悉咤！犹如山水暴起，多漂人民，亦无船栰，又无桥梁，有行人来，欲渡彼岸，见山水暴起，多漂人民，亦无船栰，又无桥梁，彼人自念：『我今宁可多集草木，牢坚缚栰，自以身力渡彼岸耶？』即寻缚栰，自以身力安隐得渡。婆悉咤！此亦如是，若比丘舍非沙门不清净行，行于沙门清净梵行，欲生梵天者，则有是处。云何？婆悉咤！梵天有恚心耶？无恚心耶？」

He replied, "It has no angry mind."

答曰：「无恚心也。」

He further asked, "Do the three-knowledge Brahmins have an angry mind or not?"

又问：「三明婆罗门有恚心、无恚心耶？」

He replied, "They have an angry mind."

答曰：「有恚心。」

"Brahma has no angry mind, while the three-knowledge Brahmins have an angry mind. Those with angry minds and those without angry minds do not share the same path, do not share liberation, and do not head in the same direction. Therefore, Brahma and the Brahmins do not share the same path. How is it, Vasistha? Does Brahma have an angry mind or not?"

「婆悉咤！梵天无恚心，三明婆罗门有恚心，有恚心、无恚心不共同，不俱解脱，不相趣向，是故梵天、婆罗门不共同也。云何？婆悉咤！梵天有瞋心、无瞋心耶？」

He replied, "He has no angry mind."

答曰：「无瞋心。」

He further asked, "Do the three-knowledge Brahmins have an angry mind or not?"

又问：「三明婆罗门有瞋心、无瞋心耶？」

He replied, "They have an angry mind."

答曰：「有瞋心。」

The Buddha said, "Brahma has no angry mind, but the three-knowledge Brahmins have an angry mind. Those with angry minds and those without angry minds have different paths and different liberations. Therefore, Brahma and the Brahmins are not the same. What do you think, Vasistha? Does Brahma have a mind of resentment or not?"

佛言：「梵天无瞋心，三明婆罗门有瞋心，有瞋心、无瞋心不同趣，不同解脱，是故梵天、婆罗门不共同也。云何？婆悉咤！梵天有恨心、无恨心耶？」

He replied, "He has no mind of resentment."

答曰：「无恨心。」

He asked again, "Do the three-knowledge Brahmins have a mind of resentment or not?"

又问：「三明婆罗门有恨心、无恨心耶？」

He replied, "They have a mind of resentment."

答曰：「有恨心。」

The Buddha said, "Brahma has a mind without resentment, while the three-knowledge Brahmins have a mind of resentment. Those with a mind of resentment and those without a mind of resentment have different destinations and different liberations. Therefore, Brahma and the Brahmins are not the same. What do you think, Vasistha? Does Brahma have a family, property, and occupation?"

佛言：「梵天无恨心，三明婆罗门有恨心，有恨心、无恨心不同趣，不同解脱，是故梵天、婆罗门不共同也。云何？婆悉咤！梵天有家属产业不？」

He replied, "No."

答曰：「无。」

He further asked, "Do the three-knowledge Brahmins have a family, property, and occupation?"

又问：「三明婆罗门有家属产业不？」

He replied, "Yes."

答曰：「有。」

The Buddha said, "Brahma Heaven has no family, property, or occupation, while the three-knowledge Brahmins have family, property, and occupation. Those with and without family, property, and occupation have different destinations and different liberations. Therefore, Brahma Heaven and Brahmins are not the same. What do you think, Vasita? Does Brahma Heaven attain self-mastery or not?"

佛言：「梵天无家属产业，三明婆罗门有家属产业，有家属产业、无家属产业不同趣，不同解脱，是故梵天、婆罗门不共同也。云何？婆悉咤！梵天得自在、不得自在耶？」

He replied, "It attains self-mastery."

答曰：「得自在。」

He further asked, "Do the three-knowledge Brahmins attain self-mastery or not?"

又问：「三明婆罗门得自在、不得自在耶？」

He replied, "They do not attain self-mastery."

答曰：「不得自在。」

The Buddha said, "Brahma Heaven attains self-mastery, while the three-knowledge Brahmins do not attain self-mastery. Those who do not attain self-mastery and those who do attain self-mastery have different paths and different liberations. Therefore, Brahma Heaven and Brahmins are not the same."

佛言：「梵天得自在，三明婆罗门不得自在，不得自在、得自在不同趣，不同解脱，是故梵天、婆罗门不共同也。」

The Buddha said, "If someone were to come and ask the three-knowledge Brahmins about profound meanings, would they be able to answer fully? Is this true?"

佛言：「彼三明婆罗门，设有人来问难深义，不能具答。实如是不？」

He replied, "Yes, it is so."

答曰：「如是。」

At that time, both Vasistha and Prashnaka said to the Buddha, "Let us set aside other discussions. I have heard that the Shakyamuni Gautama is clear about the path of Brahma and can teach it to others. He has also seen and spoken with Brahma. I only wish that the Shakyamuni Gautama, out of compassion, would explain the path of Brahma and reveal it in detail."

时，婆悉咤、颇罗堕二人俱白佛言：「且置余论，我闻沙门瞿昙明识梵道，能为人说，又与梵天相见往来言语，唯愿沙门瞿昙以慈愍故，说梵天道，开示演布。」

The Buddha said to Vasistha, "I will ask you a question. Please answer as you wish. Vasistha, how far is the country of Mindful from here?"

佛告婆悉咤：「我今问汝，随意报我。云何？婆悉咤！彼心念国，去此远近？」

He replied, "It is close."

答：「近。」

The Buddha said, "If someone is born and raised in that country, and someone else asks them about the road to that country, Vasistha, would that person who was born and raised there have any doubts about answering the road?"

「若使有人生长彼国，有余人问彼国道径，云何？婆悉咤！彼人生长彼国答彼道径，宁有疑不？」

He replied, "There would be no doubt. Why? Because they were born and raised there."

答曰：「无疑。所以者何？彼国生长故。」

The Buddha said, "Even if that person was born and raised in that country, they might still have doubts. But if someone comes and asks me about the Brahma path, there will be no doubt. Why? Because I have spoken about that Brahma path many times."

佛言：「正使彼人生长彼国，或可有疑；若有人来问我梵道，无疑也。所以者何？我常数数说彼梵道故。」

At that time, Vatsa and Pūraṇa both said to the Buddha, "Let us put that aside for now. I have heard that the śramaṇa Gautama clearly knows the Brahma path and can teach it to others. He has also seen and spoken with the Brahma Heaven. I only wish that the śramaṇa Gautama, out of compassion, would speak about the Brahma path and reveal and explain it."

时，婆悉咤、颇罗堕俱白佛言：「且置此论，我闻沙门瞿昙明识梵道，能为人说，又与梵天相见往来言语，唯愿沙门瞿昙以慈愍故，说于梵道，开示演布。」

The Buddha said, "Listen carefully and think well, and I will explain it to you."

佛言：「谛听，善思，当为汝说。」

He replied, "Yes, I wish to hear it."

答言：「唯然，愿乐欲闻。」

The Buddha said, "When a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One appears in the world, endowed with the ten epithets, up to the four dhyānas, he delights in the present life. Why is that? It is because of diligent practice, single-minded mindfulness, delighting in solitude and tranquility, and being undistracted. He fills one direction with loving-kindness, and so with the other directions, spreading it without limit, without duality, immeasurable, without resentment, and without harm. He plays with this mind and delights in it. He fills one direction with compassion, joy, and equanimity, and so with the other directions, spreading it without limit, without duality, immeasurable, without resentment, and without the intention to harm. He plays with this mind and delights in it. What do you think, Vaśiṣṭha? Does Brahma have an angry mind or a non-angry mind?"

佛言：「若如来、至真、等正觉出现于世，十号具足，乃至四禅，于现法中而自娱乐。所以者何？斯由精勤，专念不忘，乐独闲静，不放逸故。彼以慈心遍满一方，余方亦尔，广布无际，无二、无量，无恨、无害，游戏此心而自娱乐：悲、喜、舍心遍满一方，余方亦尔，广布无际，无二、无量，无有结恨，无恼害意，游戏此心以自娱乐。云何？婆悉咤！梵天有恚心、无恚心耶？」

He replied, "It is a non-angry mind."

答曰：「无恚心也。」

He further asked, "Does a monk who practices loving-kindness have an angry mind or a non-angry mind?"

又问：「行慈比丘有恚心、无恚心耶？」

He replied, "It is a non-angry mind."

答曰：「无恚心。」

The Buddha said, "Brahma has a non-angry mind, and a monk who practices loving-kindness has a non-angry mind. The non-angry mind and the non-angry mind have the same destination and the same liberation. Therefore, Brahma and the monk are the same. What do you think, Vasistha? Does Brahma have an angry mind or a non-angry mind?"

佛言：「梵天无恚心，行慈比丘无恚心，无恚心、无恚心同趣，同解脱，是故梵天、比丘俱共同也。云何？婆悉咤！梵天有瞋心耶？无瞋心耶？」

He replied, "No."

答曰：「无也。」

He further asked, "Does a monk who practices loving-kindness have an angry mind or a non-angry mind?"

又问：「行慈比丘有瞋心、无瞋心耶？」

He replied, "No."

答曰：「无。」

The Buddha said, "Brahma has a non-angry mind, and a monk who practices loving-kindness has a non-angry mind. The non-angry mind and the non-angry mind are the same in destination and liberation. Therefore, Brahma and the monk are the same. What do you think, Vasita? Does Brahma have a mind of resentment or a mind without resentment?"

佛言：「梵天无瞋心，行慈比丘无瞋心，无瞋心、无瞋心同趣，同解脱，是故梵天、比丘俱共同也。云何？婆悉咤！梵天有恨心、无恨心耶？」

He replied, "No."

答曰：「无。」

He further asked, "Does a monk who practices loving-kindness have a mind of resentment or a mind without resentment?"

又问：「行慈比丘有恨心、无恨心耶？」

He replied, "No."

答曰：「无。」

The Buddha said, "Brahma Heaven has no mind of resentment, and a monk who practices loving-kindness has no mind of resentment. The same goes for those who have no mind of resentment. Therefore, monks and Brahma Heaven are the same. What do you think, Vasistha? Does Brahma Heaven have family and property?"

佛言：「梵天无恨心，行慈比丘无恨心，无恨心、无恨心同趣，同解脱，是故比丘、梵天俱共同也。云何？婆悉咤！梵天有家属产业不？」

He replied, "No."

答曰：「无也。」

He asked again, "Does a monk who practices loving-kindness have family and property?"

又问：「行慈比丘有家属产业不耶？」

He replied, "No."

答曰：「无也。」

The Buddha said, "Brahma has no family or property. A monk who practices loving-kindness also has no family or property. Those who have no family or property and those who have no family or property are the same in their destination and liberation. Therefore, Brahma and the monk are the same. What do you think, Vasita? Does Brahma have mastery?"

佛言：「梵天无家属产业，行慈比丘亦无家属产业，无家属产业、无家属产业同趣，同解脱，是故梵天、比丘俱共同也。云何？婆悉咤！梵天得自在不耶？」

He replied, "He has mastery."

答曰：「得自在。」

He asked again, "Does a monk who practices loving-kindness have mastery?"

又问：「行慈比丘得自在耶？」

He replied, "He has mastery."

答曰：「得自在。」

The Buddha said, "Brahma has mastery, a monk who practices loving-kindness has mastery. Mastery and mastery are the same destination, the same liberation. Therefore, Brahma and the monk are the same."

佛言：「梵天得自在，行慈比丘得自在，得自在、得自在同趣，同解脱，是故梵天、比丘俱共同也。」

The Buddha told Vasistha, "You should know that when a monk who practices loving-kindness's body perishes and life ends, like the time it takes to shoot an arrow, he is reborn in the Brahma Heaven." When the Buddha taught this Dharma, Vasistha and Prajapatya immediately removed dust and defilements from their seats, and the Dharma eye was born.

佛告婆悉咤：「当知行慈比丘身坏命终，如发箭之顷，生梵天上。」佛说是法时，婆悉咤、颇罗堕即于座上远尘离垢，诸法法眼生。

At that time, Vasistha and Prajapatya heard what the Buddha said and joyfully accepted it.

尔时，婆悉咤、颇罗堕闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
