DĀ 27

Dīrghāgama 长阿含经

Chapter 3 第三分

27 （二七）沙门果经

Parallel to: DN 2 / EĀ 43.7

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying in the old children's mango grove in Rājagṛha, together with a great assembly of 1,250 bhikṣus.

一时，佛在罗阅祇耆旧童子庵婆园中，与大比丘众千二百五十人俱。

At that time, King Ajātaśatru, son of Vaidehī, on the full moon night of the fifteenth day, said to a queen, "Tonight is clear and bright, no different from daytime. What should we do?"

尔时，王阿阇世韦提希子以十五日月满时，命一夫人而告之曰：「今夜清明，与昼无异，当何所为作？」

The queen said to the king, "Tonight is the full moon night of the fifteenth day, as bright as daytime. It is suitable to wash the hair and bathe, and enjoy the five desires with the court ladies."

夫人白王言：「今十五日夜月满时，与昼无异，宜沐发澡浴，与诸婇女五欲自娱。」

At that time, the king also ordered the eldest prince, Udayana, saying, "Tonight is the full moon night of the fifteenth day, as bright as daytime. What should we do?"

时，王又命第一太子优耶婆陀而告之曰：「今夜月十五日月满时，与昼无异，当何所施作？」

The prince said to the king, "Tonight is the full moon night of the fifteenth day, as bright as daytime. It is suitable to assemble the four divisions of troops and discuss attacking the rebellious borderlands, and then return here to enjoy ourselves together."

太子白王言：「今夜十五日月满时，与昼无异，宜集四兵，与共谋议伐于边逆，然后还此共相娱乐。」

At that time, the king also ordered the brave and strong general, saying, "Tonight is the full moon night of the fifteenth day, as bright as daytime. What should we do?"

时，王又命勇健大将而告之曰：「今十五日月满时，其夜清明，与昼无异，当何所为作？」

The general said, "Tonight is as bright as daytime. It is suitable to assemble the four divisions of troops and inspect the world to know what is contrary and what is in accordance."

大将白言：「今夜清明，与昼无异，宜集四兵，案行] cf.【丽】天下，知有逆顺。」

At that time, the king also ordered the brahmin Uśīra and said to him, "Tonight is the full moon night of the fifteenth day, as bright as daytime. Which śramaṇas or brahmins can enlighten my mind?"

时，王又命雨舍婆罗门而告之曰：「今十五日月满时，其夜清明，与昼无异，当诣何等沙门、婆罗门所能开悟我心？」

At that time, Uśīra said, "Tonight is as bright as daytime. There is Uruvilvā Kāśyapa, who is the leader among the great assembly, with many followers and a far-reaching reputation, like the great ocean that can accommodate much. He is widely venerated. Great King! You should go to him to pay respects. If the king sees him, his mind may be enlightened."

时，雨舍白言：「今夜清明，与昼无异。有不兰迦叶，于大众中而为导首，多有知识，名称远闻，犹如大海多所容受，众所供养。大王！宜往诣彼问讯，王若见者，心或开悟。」

The king also ordered Uśīra's younger brother Sunītha and said to him, "Tonight is as bright as daytime. Which śramaṇas or brahmins can enlighten my mind?"

王又命雨舍弟须尼陀而告之曰：「今夜清明，与昼无异，宜诣何等沙门、婆罗门所能开悟我心？」

Sunītha said, "Tonight is as bright as daytime. There is Maskarin Gośālīputra, who is the leader among the great assembly, with many followers and a far-reaching reputation, like the great ocean that can accommodate much. He is widely venerated. Great King! You should go to him to pay respects. If the king sees him, his mind may be enlightened."

须尼陀白言：「今夜清明，与昼无异。有末伽梨瞿舍利，于大众中而为导首，多有知识，名称远闻，犹如大海无不容受，众所供养。大王！宜往诣彼问讯，王若见者，心或开悟。」

The king also ordered the chief minister of works and said to him, "Tonight is as bright as daytime. Which śramaṇas or brahmins can enlighten my mind?"

王又命典作大臣而告之曰：「今夜清明，与昼无异，当诣何等沙门、婆罗门所能开悟我心？」

The chief minister of works said, "There is Ajita Keśakambalin, who is the leader among the great assembly, with many followers and a far-reaching reputation, like the great ocean that can accommodate much. He is widely venerated. Great King! You should go to him to pay respects. If the king sees him, his mind may be enlightened."

典作大臣白言：「有阿耆多翅舍钦婆罗，于大众中而为导首，多有知识，名称远闻，犹如大海无不容受，众所供养。大王！宜往诣彼问讯，王若见者，心或开悟。」

The king also ordered the gatekeeper Kālā to him and said, "Tonight is as bright as daytime. Which śramaṇas or brahmins can enlighten my mind?"

王又命伽罗守门将而告之曰：「今夜清明，与昼无异，当诣何等沙门、婆罗门所能开悟我心？」

The gatekeeper Kālā said, "There is Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, who is the leader among the great assembly, with many followers and a far-reaching reputation, like the great ocean that can accommodate much. He is widely venerated. Great King! You should go to him to pay respects. If the king sees him, his mind may be enlightened."

伽罗守门将白言：「有婆浮陀伽旃那，于大众中而为导首，多有知识，名称远闻，犹如大海无不容受，众所供养。大王！宜往诣彼问讯，王若见者，心或开悟。」

The king also ordered Udayin Māṇava to him and said, "Tonight is as bright as daytime. Which śramaṇas or brahmins can enlighten my mind?"

王又命优陀夷漫提子而告之曰：「今夜清明，与昼无异，当诣何等沙门、婆罗门所能开悟我心？」

Udayin said, "There is Sañjayin Vairaṭiputra, who is the leader among the great assembly, with many followers and a far-reaching reputation, like the great ocean that can accommodate much. He is widely venerated. Great King! You should go to him to pay respects. If the king sees him, his mind may be enlightened."

优陀夷白言：「有散若夷毘罗梨沸，于大众中而为导首，多所知识，名称远闻，犹如大海无不容受，众所供养。大王！宜往诣彼问讯，王若见者，心或开悟。」

The king also ordered his brother Abhaya and said, "Tonight is as bright as daytime. Which śramaṇas or brahmins can enlighten my mind?"

王又命弟无畏而告之曰：「今夜清明，与昼无异，当诣何等沙门、婆罗门所能开悟我心？」

Brother Abhaya said, "There are Nirgranthas, who are the leaders among the great assembly, with many followers and a far-reaching reputation, like the great ocean that can accommodate much. They are widely venerated. Great King! You should go to them to pay respects. If the king sees them, his mind may be enlightened."

弟无畏白言：「有尼乾子，于大众中而为导首，多所知识，名称远闻，犹如大海无不容受，众所供养。大王！宜往诣彼问讯，王若见者，心或开悟。」

The king also ordered the youth Jīvaka and said, "Tonight is as bright as daytime. Which śramaṇas or brahmins can enlighten my mind?"

王又命寿命童子而告之曰：「今夜清明，与昼无异，当诣何等沙门、婆罗门所开悟我心？」

The youth Jīvaka said, "There is a Buddha, the World-Honored One, now in my Aṅgulimāla Garden. Great King! You should go to pay respects. If the king sees him, his mind will certainly be enlightened."

寿命童子白言：「有佛、世尊今在我庵婆园中。大王！宜往诣彼问讯，王若见者，心必开悟。」

The king ordered Jīvaka, "Prepare my mount, the jeweled elephant, and five hundred other white elephants."

王𠡠寿命言：「严我所乘宝象及余五百白象。」

The elder received the order and immediately prepared the king's elephant and five hundred elephants. He then said to the king, "The carriages are ready. Please know the time."

耆旧受教，即严王象及五百象讫，白王言：「严驾已备，唯愿知时。」

King Ajātaśatru himself rode on the jeweled elephant, and the five hundred consorts rode on the five hundred female elephants. Each held a torch, displaying the king's majestic authority. He left Rājagṛha and wished to go to where the Buddha was. After walking a short distance, he said to Jīvaka, "You are deceiving me now, putting me in a difficult position, leading me and the great assembly to our enemies."

阿阇世王自乘宝象，使五百夫人乘五百牝象，手各执炬，现王威严，出罗阅祇，欲诣佛所。小行进路，告寿命曰：「汝今诳我，陷固于我，引我大众欲与冤家。」

Jīvaka said, "Great King! I dare not deceive the king, nor dare I put the king in a difficult position, leading the king and the great assembly to our enemies. The king should just proceed forward. He will surely obtain blessings and merits."

寿命白言：「大王！我不敢欺王，不敢陷固引王大众以与冤家。王但前进，必获福庆。」

At that time, the king proceeded a little further and said to Jīvaka, "You are deceiving me, putting me in a difficult position, wanting to lead my assembly to our enemies." This happened twice. Why is that? There is a large assembly of 1,250 people, silent and without sound. They must be plotting something.

时，王小复前进，告寿命言：「汝欺诳我，陷固于我，欲引我众持与冤家。如是再三。所以者何？彼有大众千二百五十人，寂然无声，将有谋也。」

Jīvaka again said three times, "Great King! I dare not deceive or put you in a difficult position, leading your great assembly to our enemies. The king should just proceed forward. He will surely obtain blessings and merits. Why is that? The śramaṇa's Dharma always delights in solitude and tranquility. That is why there is no sound. The king should just proceed forward. The garden and forest are already visible."

寿命复再三白言：「大王！我不敢欺诳陷固，引王大众持与冤家。王但前进，必获福庆。所以者何？彼沙门法常乐闲静，是以无声。王但前进，园林已现。」

King Ajātaśatru arrived at the garden gate, dismounted from the elephant, unfastened his sword, removed his canopy, put away his five insignia of royalty, and walked into the garden gate. He asked Jīvaka, "Where is the Buddha, the World-Honored One, now?"

阿阇世王到园门，下象、解剑、退盖，去五威仪，步入园门，告寿命曰：「今佛、世尊为在何所？」

Jīvaka replied, "Great King! The Buddha is now on the high hall. There is a bright lamp in front. The World-Honored One is seated on the lion seat, facing south. The king should proceed a little further and he will see the World-Honored One himself."

寿命报言：「大王！今佛在高堂上，前有明灯，世尊处师子座，南面而坐，王小前进，自见世尊。」

At that time, King Ajātaśatru went to the lecture hall. He washed his feet outside and then went up to the hall. He silently looked around and gave rise to a joyful mind. He spoke, saying, "Now the śramaṇas are silent and still, with complete tranquility and contemplation. May my crown prince Udayi also accomplish tranquility and contemplation, no different from this."

尔时，阿阇世王往诣讲堂所，于外洗足，然后上堂，默然四顾，生欢喜心，口自发言：「今诸沙门寂然静默，止观具足，愿使我太子优婆耶亦止观成就，与此无异。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told King Ajātaśatru, "Because you think of your son, you spoke, saying, 'May Crown Prince Udayi also accomplish tranquility and contemplation, no different from this.' You may take a seat."

尔时，世尊告阿阇世王曰：「汝念子故，口自发言：『愿使太子优婆耶亦止观成就，与此无异。』汝可前坐。」

At that time, King Ajātaśatru immediately went forward, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, "I now wish to ask something. If you have the leisure, I dare to ask."

时，阿阇世王即前头面礼佛足，于一面坐，而白佛言：「今欲有所问，若有闲暇，乃敢请问。」

The Buddha said, "Great King! If you wish to ask, you may ask."

佛言：「大王！欲有问者，便可问也。」

King Ajātaśatru said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Nowadays people ride elephants, horses, and chariots, practice with swords, spears, bows, arrows, weapons, and fighting techniques. Princes, strongmen, powerful men, servants, leather workers, barbers, garland makers, cartwrights, potters, bamboo workers, reed workers, all make a living through various skills and arts, enjoying themselves as they please. Parents, wives, children, slaves, and servants enjoy each other. This is how they make a living, and there are visible results. Now, what the śramaṇas practice in the present, do they attain visible results?"

阿阇世王白佛言：「世尊！如今人乘象、马车，习刀、牟、剑、弓矢、兵仗、战鬪之法，王子、力士、大力士、僮使、皮师、剃发师、织鬘师、车师、瓦师、竹师、苇师，皆以种种伎术以自存生，自恣娱乐，父母、妻子、奴仆、僮使共相娱乐，如此营生，现有果报；今诸沙门现在所修，现得果报不？」

The Buddha said to the king, "Have you ever gone to the śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas to ask about this meaning?"

佛告王曰：「汝颇曾诣诸沙门、婆罗门所问如此义不？」

The king said to the Buddha, "I have indeed gone to the śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas to ask about this meaning. I remember once going to Nirgrantha Jñātiputra and asking, 'Just as people ride elephants, horses, and chariots, practice military arts, and make a living in various ways, with visible results, do the śramaṇas now practicing the path attain visible results?' Nirgrantha Jñātiputra replied to me, 'O King! If you yourself do it, or instruct others to do it, to cut, chop, and harm, to cook and slice, to torment and disturb sentient beings, to cause them sorrow and crying, to kill, steal, engage in sexual misconduct, lie, jump over walls, rob, set fires, and commit evil on the roads. O King! To do such things is not evil. O King! If you were to cut all sentient beings with a sharp sword and make a pile of their flesh, filling the world, this would not be evil, nor would there be any karmic retribution. On the south bank of the Ganges, to cut sentient beings would not be evil either. On the north bank of the Ganges, to hold a great giving assembly, giving to all sentient beings, benefiting them equally, would also not have any merit or retribution.'"

王白佛言：「我曾诣沙门、婆罗门所问如是义。我念一时至不兰迦叶所，问言：『如人乘象、马车，习于兵法，乃至种种营生，现有果报；今此众现在修道，现得果报不？』彼不兰迦叶报我言：『王若自作，若教人作，斫] cf.【丽】伐残害，煮炙] cf.【丽】切割，恼乱众生，愁忧啼哭，杀生偷盗，婬逸妄语，逾墙劫夺，放火焚烧，断道为恶。大王！行如此事，非为恶也。大王！若以利剑脔割一切众生，以为肉聚，弥满世间，此非为恶，亦无罪报。于恒水南，脔割众生，亦无有恶报。于恒水北岸，为大施会，施一切众，利人等利，亦无福报。』」

The king said to the Buddha, "It is like asking about a melon and being told about a plum, or asking about a plum and being told about a melon. He was like that too. I asked if there was immediate retribution, but he told me there was no retribution of merit or demerit. I then thought to myself, 'I am a kṣatriya king, a water-sprinkled one, without the means to kill monastics or bind and expel them.' At that time, I was filled with resentment and anger, and after thinking this, I left.

王白佛言：「犹如有人问瓜报李，问李报瓜。彼亦如是，我问现得报不？而彼答我无罪福报。我即自念言：『我是刹利王，水浇头种，无缘杀出家人，系缚驱遣。』时，我怀忿结心，作此念已，即便舍去。」

He also said to the Buddha, "Once I went to Makkhali Gosala and asked, 'Just as people ride elephants, horses, and chariots, practice military arts, and make a living in various ways, with visible results, do the śramaṇas now practicing the path attain visible results?' He replied to me, 'O King! There is no giving, no receiving, no ritual offerings, no good or evil, no retribution of good or evil, no present world, no future world, no father, no mother, no gods, no transformation, no sentient beings, no śramaṇas or brāhmaṇas who practice equally in the world, no present world or future world, no personal realization and teaching others. All those who say there is, are all false.' O World-Honored One! It is like asking about a melon and being told about a plum, or asking about a plum and being told about a melon. He was like that too. I asked if there was immediate retribution, but he answered with meaningless words. I then thought to myself, 'I am a kṣatriya king, a water-sprinkled one, without the means to kill monastics or bind and expel them.' At that time, I was filled with resentment and anger, and after thinking this, I left.

又白佛言：「我于一时至末伽梨拘舍梨所，问言：『如今人乘象、马车，习于兵法，乃至种种营生，皆现有果报；今者此众现在修道，现得报不？』彼报我言：『大王！无施、无与，无祭祀法，亦无善恶，无善恶报，无有今世，亦无后世，无父、无母，无天、无化、无众生，世无沙门、婆罗门平等行者，亦无今世、后世，自身作证，布现他人。诸言有者，皆是虚妄。』世尊！犹如有人问瓜报李，问李报瓜。彼亦如是，我问现得报不？彼乃以无义答。我即自念言：『我是刹利王，水浇头种，无缘杀出家人，系缚驱遣。』时，我怀忿结心，作此念已，即便舍去。」

He also said to the Buddha, "Once I went to Ajita Kesakambala and asked, 'Venerable One! Just as people ride elephants, horses, and chariots, practice military arts, and make a living in various ways, with visible results, do the śramaṇas now practicing the path attain visible results?' He replied to me, 'Those who accept the four great elements and take rebirth, when their life ends, the earth element returns to earth, water returns to water, fire returns to fire, and wind returns to wind, all decaying and disintegrating. The senses return to emptiness. When a person dies, their body is placed in a charnel ground on a bier. Fire burns their bones to the color of a pigeon, or they turn to ash and soil. Whether wise or foolish, when life ends, all decay and disintegrate, becoming a law of cessation.' O World-Honored One! It is like asking about a plum and being told about a melon. He was like that too. I asked if there was immediate retribution, but he answered with annihilation. I then thought to myself, 'I am a kṣatriya king, a water-sprinkled one, without the means to kill monastics or bind and expel them.' At that time, I was filled with resentment and anger, and after thinking this, I left.

又白佛言：「我于一时至阿夷陀翅舍钦婆罗所，问言：『大德！如人乘象、马车，习于兵法，乃至种种营生，皆现有果报；今者此众现在修道，现得报不？』彼报我言：『受四大人取命终者，地大还归地，水还归水，火还归火，风还归风，皆悉坏败，诸根归空。若人死时，床舆举身置于冢间，火烧其骨如鸽色，或变为灰土，若愚、若智取命终者，皆悉坏败，为断灭法。』世尊！犹如有人问李瓜报，彼亦如是，我问现得报不？而彼答我以断灭。我即念言：『我是刹利王，水浇头种，无缘杀出家人，系缚驱遣。』时，我怀忿结心，作此念已，即便舍去。」

He also said to the Buddha, "Once I went to Pūraṇa Kāśyapa and asked, 'Venerable One! Just as people ride elephants, horses, and chariots, practice military arts, and make a living in various ways, with visible results, do the śramaṇas now practicing the path attain visible results?' He replied to me, 'O King! There is no power, no diligence. People have no power, no skillful means. There are no causes or conditions for sentient beings to become attached. There are no causes or conditions for sentient beings to become pure. All sentient beings with life are all powerless, without self-control. There are no enemies or retribution determined by fate. Within these six births, one experiences various sufferings and pleasures.' It is like asking about a plum and being told about a melon, or asking about a melon and being told about a plum. He was like that too. I asked if there was immediate retribution, but he was already powerless to answer me. I then thought to myself, 'I am a kṣatriya king, a water-sprinkled one, without the means to kill monastics or bind and expel them.' At that time, I was filled with resentment and anger, and after thinking this, I left.

又白佛言：「我昔一时至彼浮陀伽旃延所，问言：『大德！如人乘象、马车，习于兵法，乃至种种营生，皆现有果报；今者此众现在修道，得报不？』彼答我言：『大王！无力、无精进，人无力、无方便，无因无缘众生染著，无因无缘众生清净，一切众生有命之类，皆悉无力，不得自在，无有冤讐定在数中，于此六生中受诸苦乐。』犹如问李瓜报，问瓜李报。彼亦如是，我问现得报不？彼已无力答我。我即自念言：『我是刹利王，水浇头种，无缘杀出家人，系缚驱遣。』时，我怀忿结心，作此念已，即便舍去。」

He also said to the Buddha, "Once I went to Sañjayin Vairaṭiputra and asked, 'Venerable One! Just as people ride elephants, horses, and chariots, practice military arts, and make a living in various ways, with visible results, do the śramaṇas now practicing the path attain visible results?' He replied to me, 'O King! There is immediate retribution for śramaṇas. If asked like this, the answer is like this. This is true. This is different. This is neither different nor not different. O King! There is no immediate retribution for śramaṇas. If asked like this, the answer is like this. This is true. This is different. This is neither different nor not different. O King! There is both immediate retribution and no immediate retribution for śramaṇas. If asked like this, the answer is like this. This is true. This is different. This is neither different nor not different. O King! There is neither immediate retribution nor no immediate retribution for śramaṇas. If asked like this, the answer is like this. This is true. This is different. This is neither different nor not different.' O World-Honored One! It is like asking about a plum and being told about a melon, or asking about a melon and being told about a plum. He was like that too. I asked if there was immediate retribution, but he gave me a different answer. I then thought to myself, 'I am a kṣatriya king, a water-sprinkled one, without the means to kill monastics or bind and expel them.' At that time, I was filled with resentment and anger, and after thinking this, I left.

又白佛言：「我昔一时至散若毘罗梨子所，问言：『大德！如人乘象、马车，习于兵法，乃至种种营生，皆现有果报；今者此众现在修道，现得报不？』彼答我言：『大王！现有沙门果报，问如是，答此事如是，此事实，此事异，此事非异非不异。大王！现无沙门果报，问如是，答此事如是，此事实，此事异，此事非异非不异。大王！现有无沙门果报，问如是，答此事如是，此事实，此事异，此事非异非不异。大王！现非有非无沙门果报，问如是，答此事如是，此事实，此事异，此事非异非不异。』世尊！犹如人问李瓜报，问瓜李报。彼亦如是，我问现得报不？而彼异论答我。我即自念言：『我是刹利王，水浇头种，无缘杀出家人，系缚驱遣。』时，我怀忿结心，作是念已，即便舍去。」

He also said to the Buddha, "Once I went to the Nirgrantha Jñātiputra and asked, 'Venerable One! Just as people ride elephants, horses, and chariots, practice military arts, and make a living in various ways, with visible results, do the śramaṇas now practicing the path attain visible results?' He replied to me, 'O King! I am a person of omniscience and all-seeing, knowing all without exception. Whether walking, standing, sitting, lying down, or waking up, I know all without exception. Wisdom is always present before me.' O World-Honored One! It is like asking about a plum and being told about a melon, or asking about a melon and being told about a plum. He was like that too. I asked if there was immediate retribution, but he answered me with omniscience. I then thought to myself, 'I am a kṣatriya king, a water-sprinkled one, without the means to kill monastics or bind and expel them.' At that time, I was filled with resentment and anger, and after thinking this, I left.

又白佛言：「我昔一时至尼乾子所，问言：『大德！犹如人乘象、马车，乃至种种营生，现有果报；今者此众现在修道，现得报不？』彼报我言：『大王！我是一切智、一切见人，尽知无余，若行，若住、坐、卧，觉悟无余，智常现在前。』世尊！犹如人问李瓜报，问瓜李报。彼亦如是，我问现得报不？而彼答我以一切智。我即自念言：『我是刹利王，水浇头种，无缘杀出家人，系缚驱遣。』时，我怀忿结心，作此念已，即便舍去。

"Therefore, World-Honored One! I have come here now to ask about this meaning. Just as people ride elephants, horses, and chariots, practice military arts, and make a living in various ways, with visible results, do the śramaṇas now practicing the path attain visible results?"

「是故，世尊！今我来此问如是义，如人乘象、马车，习于兵法，乃至种种营生，皆现有果报；今者沙门现在修道，现得报不？」

The Buddha said to King Ajātaśatru, "I will now ask you a question. Please answer as you wish. What do you think, great king? When the servants and workers in the king's household see the king bathing and washing his hair on the full moon day of the fifteenth, sitting on a high terrace enjoying himself with the palace ladies, they think, 'Oh! The results of actions are indeed like this! This King Ajātaśatru, on the full moon day of the fifteenth, bathes and washes his hair, sits on a high terrace enjoying himself with the palace ladies with the five desires. Who can know that this is the result of actions?' Later, they shave their beards and hair, put on the three Dharma robes, leave home to practice the path, and practice the equal Dharma. What do you think, great king? When you see this person coming from afar, would you still think, 'This is my servant'?"

佛告阿阇世王曰：「我今还问王，随意所答。云何？大王！王家僮使、内外作人，皆见王于十五日月满时，沐发澡浴，在高殿上与诸婇女共相娱乐，作此念言：『咄哉！行之果报乃至是乎？此王阿阇世以十五日月满时，沐发澡浴，于高殿上与诸婇女五欲自娱。谁能知此乃是行报者？』彼于后时，剃除须发，服三法衣，出家修道，行平等法。云何？大王！大王遥见此人来，宁复起念言：『是我仆使不耶？』」

The king said to the Buddha, "No, World-Honored One! If I see him coming, I will rise to welcome him and invite him to sit down."

王白佛言：「不也。世尊！若见彼来，当起迎请坐。」

The Buddha said, "Isn't this the present result of being a śramaṇa?"

佛言：「此岂非沙门现得报耶？」

The king said, "Yes, World-Honored One! This is the present result of being a śramaṇa."

王言：「如是。世尊！此是现得沙门报也。」

"Furthermore, great king! If a guest staying in the king's realm eats the king's provisions and sees the king bathing and washing his hair on the full moon day of the fifteenth, enjoying the five desires with the palace ladies on a high terrace, he may think, 'Alas! Is this the result of his actions? Who can know that this is the result of his actions?' Later, he shaves his beard and hair, dons the three Dharma robes, leaves home to cultivate the path, and practices the equal Dharma. What do you think, great king? If the king sees this person coming from afar, would he still think, 'This is my guest who eats my provisions'?"

「复次，大王！若王界内寄居客人食王廪赐，见王于十五日月满时，沐发澡浴，于高殿上与诸婇女五欲自娱。彼作是念：『咄哉！彼行之报乃如是耶？谁能知此乃是行报者？』彼于后时，剃除须发，服三法衣，出家修道，行平等法。云何？大王！大王若遥见此人来，宁复起念言：『是我客民食我廪赐。』耶？」

The king said, "No. If I see him coming from afar, I will rise to greet him, pay respect, ask about his well-being, and invite him to sit down."

王言：「不也。若我见其远来，当起迎礼敬，问讯请坐。」

"What do you think, great king! Is this not the present result of the monk's practice?"

「云何？大王！此非沙门现得果报耶？」

The king said, "Yes, it is the present result of the monk's practice."

王言：「如是，现得沙门报也。」

"Furthermore, great king! When the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One appears in the world, those who enter my Dharma attain the three insights, destroy all darkness, and give rise to great wisdom and illumination, namely the knowledge and realization of the exhaustion of taints. Why is this? It is because of diligent effort, single-minded mindfulness without forgetfulness, delighting in solitude and tranquility, and being undistracted. What do you think, great king! Is this not the present result of the monk's practice?"

「复次，大王！如来、至真、等正觉出现于世，入我法者，乃至三明，灭诸暗冥，生大智明，所谓漏尽智证。所以者何？斯由精勤，专念不忘，乐独闲静，不放逸故。云何？大王！此非沙门现在果报也。」

The king replied, "Yes, World-Honored One! It truly is the present result of the monk's practice."

王报言：「如是，世尊！实是沙门现在果报。」

At that time, King Ajātaśatru immediately rose from his seat, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and said to the Buddha, "I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept my repentance. I was deluded, foolish, ignorant, and unperceptive. My father, King Bimbisāra of Magadha, ruled righteously without partiality or injustice. Yet I, deluded by the five desires, actually harmed my father, the king. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept my repentance with compassion and kindness."

尔时，阿阇世王即从坐起，头面礼佛足，白佛言：「唯愿世尊受我悔过，我为狂愚痴冥无识，我父摩竭瓶沙王以法治化，无有偏枉，而我迷惑五欲，实害父王，唯愿世尊加哀慈愍，受我悔过。」

The Buddha told the king, "You are ignorant and unperceptive. You should only repent yourself. You were deluded by the five desires and harmed your father, the king. Now, in the wise and holy Dharma, you are able to repent. This is beneficial to yourself. I have compassion for you and accept your repentance."

佛告王曰：「汝愚冥无识，但自悔过，汝迷于五欲乃害父王，今于贤圣法中能悔过者，即自饶益。吾愍汝故，受汝悔过。」

At that time, King Ajātaśatru bowed at the World-Honored One's feet and sat to one side. The Buddha taught him the Dharma, showing, teaching, benefiting, and delighting him. Having heard the Buddha's teachings, the king said to the Buddha, "I now take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha. Please allow me to be an upāsaka in the true Dharma. From now on, for as long as I live, I will not kill, steal, engage in sexual misconduct, deceive, or drink alcohol. I only wish that the World-Honored One and the great assembly would accept my invitation tomorrow."

尔时，阿阇世王礼世尊足已，还一面坐。佛为说法，示教利喜。王闻佛教已，即白佛言：「我今归依佛，归依法，归依僧，听我于正法中为优婆塞，自今已后，尽形寿不杀、不盗、不婬、不欺、不饮酒，唯愿世尊及诸大众明受我请。」

At that time, the World-Honored One silently accepted. When the king saw that the Buddha silently accepted the invitation, he immediately rose, bowed to the Buddha, circumambulated him three times, and returned.

尔时，世尊默然许可。时，王见佛默然受请已，即起礼佛，遶三匝而还。

Not long after he left, the Buddha told the bhikṣus, "The fault of King Ajātaśatru has been reduced and he has removed his heavy guilt. If King Ajātaśatru had not killed his father, he would have attained the purity of the Dharma-eye right here on this seat; but now King Ajātaśatru repents and his fault has been reduced, he has removed his heavy guilt."

其去未久，佛告诸比丘言：「此阿阇世王过罪损减，已拔重咎。若阿阇世王不杀父者，即当于此坐上得法眼净；而阿阇世王今自悔过，罪咎损减，已拔重咎。」

At that time, King Ajātaśatru, on the way, told the youth Jīvaka, "Excellent! Excellent! You have greatly benefited me now. You first praised and explained the Tathāgata's instructions and guidance, and then led me to where the World-Honored One was. I have received his enlightenment and deeply recognize your kindness, which I will never forget."

时，阿阇世王至于中路，告寿命童子言：「善哉！善哉！汝今于我多所饶益。汝先称说如来指授开发，然后将我诣世尊所，得蒙开悟，深识汝恩，终不遗忘。」

At that time, the king returned to the palace and prepared various delicacies and foods. The next day, when the time came, he said, "The sages know the time."

时，王还宫办诸肴饍种种饮食，明日时到，唯圣知时。

At that time, the World-Honored One put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and together with the assembly of disciples, numbering one thousand two hundred and fifty, went to the king's palace, took his seat, and sat down. At that time, the king personally served and offered food to the Buddha and the Sangha. After the meal was finished, the bowls were put away, and the water for washing was served. He bowed at the feet of the World-Honored One and said, "I now repent again and again. I was deluded, foolish, ignorant, and did not recognize that my father, King Bimbisāra of Magadha, ruled righteously without partiality or injustice. Yet I was blinded by the five desires and actually harmed my father, the king. I only pray that the World-Honored One will add compassion and mercy and accept my repentance."

尔时，世尊著衣持钵，与众弟子千二百五十人俱，往诣王宫，就座而坐。时，王手自斟酌，供佛及僧，食讫去钵，行澡水毕，礼世尊足，白言：「我今再三悔过，我为狂愚痴冥无识，我父摩竭瓶沙王以法治化，无有偏抂，而我迷于五欲，实害父王，唯愿世尊加哀慈愍，受我悔过。」

The Buddha told the king, "You were ignorant and deluded by the five desires, and you harmed your father, the king. Now, in the Dharma of the sages, you are able to repent. This is beneficial to yourself. I now have compassion for you and accept your repentance."

佛告王曰：「汝愚冥无识，迷于五欲，乃害父王，今于贤圣法中能悔过者，即自饶益。吾今愍汝，受汝悔过。」

At that time, the king bowed at the Buddha's feet, took a small seat, and sat in front of the Buddha. The Buddha taught him the Dharma, showing, teaching, benefiting, and delighting him. Having heard the Buddha's teachings, the king again said to the Buddha, "I now take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha for the third time. I only pray that you allow me to be an upāsaka in the true Dharma. From now on, for as long as I live, I will not kill, steal, engage in sexual misconduct, deceive, or drink alcohol."

时，王礼佛足已，取一小座于佛前坐，佛为说法，示教利喜。王闻佛教已，又白佛言：「我今再三归依佛，归依法，归依僧，唯愿听我于正法中为优婆塞，自今已后，尽形寿不杀、不盗、不婬、不欺、不饮酒。」

At that time, the World-Honored One taught the Dharma to King Ajātaśatru, showing, teaching, benefiting, and delighting him. Having finished, he rose from his seat and departed.

尔时，世尊为阿阇世王说法，示教利喜已，从坐起而去。

At that time, King Ajātaśatru and the youth Jīvaka heard what the Buddha said, and they joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，阿阇世王及寿命童子闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
