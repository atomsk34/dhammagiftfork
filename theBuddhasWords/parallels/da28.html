DĀ 28

Dīrghāgama 长阿含经

Chapter 3 第三分

28 （二八）布咤婆楼经

Parallel to: DN 9

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī, together with a great assembly of 1,250 bhikṣus.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园，与大比丘众千二百五十人俱。

At that time, the World-Honored One, in the morning, put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and entered the city of Śrāvastī to beg for food. The World-Honored One thought, "Today, begging for food is too early. I should now go to the Brāhmaṇa forest of Puṣkarāvatī to take a look. When the time comes, I will beg for food." At that time, the World-Honored One went to the Brāhmaṇa forest.

尔时，世尊清旦著衣持钵，入舍卫城乞食。时，世尊念言：「今日乞食，于时为早，今我宁可往至布咤婆楼梵志林中观看，须时至当乞食。」尔时，世尊即诣梵志林中。

At that time, the Brāhmaṇa Puṣkarāvatī saw the Buddha coming from afar, and immediately got up to welcome him, saying, "Welcome, Śramaṇa Gautama! You haven't come here for a long time. What is the reason for your visit now? Please come and take a seat."

时，布咤婆楼梵志遥见佛来，即起迎言：「善来，沙门瞿昙！久不来此，今以何缘而能屈顾？可前就座。」

At that time, the World-Honored One took his seat and said to Puṣkarāvatī, "What are you all gathered here for? What are you discussing?"

尔时，世尊即就其座，告布咤婆楼曰：「汝等集此，何所作为？为何讲说？」

The Brāhmaṇa said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Yesterday, many Brāhmaṇas, Śramaṇas, and Brāhmaṇas gathered in this Brāhmaṇa hall, discussing such matters, contradicting each other. Gautama! Some Brāhmaṇas say, 'A person's thoughts arise without cause or condition, and thoughts cease without cause or condition. Thoughts come and go, coming is the arising of thoughts, going is the cessation of thoughts.' Gautama! Some Brāhmaṇas say, 'Due to life, thoughts arise, and due to life, thoughts cease. Those thoughts come and go, coming is the arising of thoughts, going is the cessation of thoughts.' Gautama! Some Brāhmaṇas say, 'What was said before is not the case. There are great ghosts and spirits with great power. They hold thoughts and take them away. They hold thoughts and bring them back. They hold thoughts and take them away, then thoughts cease. They hold thoughts and bring them back, then thoughts arise.' Because of this, I thought, 'The Śramaṇa Gautama knows this meaning first. He will surely be able to know thoughts and the cessation of thoughts well.'"

梵志白佛言：「世尊！昨日多有梵志、沙门、婆罗门集此婆罗门堂，说如是事，相违逆论。瞿昙！或有梵志作是说言：『人无因无缘而想生，无因无缘而想灭，想有去来，来则想生，去则想灭。』瞿昙！或有梵志作是说：『由命有想生，由命有想灭，彼想有去来，来则想生，去则想灭。』瞿昙！或有梵志作是说：『如先所言，无有是处。有大鬼神，有大威力，彼持想去，彼持想来，彼持想去则想灭，彼持想来则想生。』我因是故生念，念沙门瞿昙先知此义，必能善知想知灭定。」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the Brāhmaṇa, "Those who discuss have all made mistakes. They say that thoughts arise without cause or condition, and thoughts cease without cause or condition. Thoughts come and go, coming is the arising of thoughts, going is the cessation of thoughts. Some say that thoughts arise due to life, and thoughts cease due to life. Thoughts come and go, coming is the arising of thoughts, going is the cessation of thoughts. Some say, 'It is not the case. There are great ghosts and spirits. They hold thoughts and bring them back. They hold thoughts and take them away. Holding them and bringing them back, thoughts arise. Holding them and taking them away, thoughts cease.' Those who speak like this all make mistakes. Why is that? Brāhmaṇa! Thoughts arise due to causes and conditions, and thoughts cease due to causes and conditions.

尔时，世尊告梵志曰：「彼诸论者皆有过咎。言无因无缘而有想生，无因无缘而有想灭，想有去来，来则想生，去则想灭。或言因命想生，因命想灭，想有去来，来则想生，去则想灭。或有言：『无有是处，有大鬼神，彼持想来，彼持想去，持来则想生，持去则想灭。』如此言者，皆有过咎。所以者何？梵志！有因缘而想生，有因缘而想灭。

If the Tathāgata appears in the world, the Perfectly Enlightened One, endowed with the ten titles, and someone leaves home in the Buddha's teachings to practice the path, up to extinguishing the five hindrances that obscure the mind, removing desire and evil unwholesome states, with initial and sustained thought, joy and happiness, entering the first dhyāna, first extinguishing desire and giving rise to the thought of joy and happiness. Brāhmaṇa! For this reason, it is known that thoughts arise due to causes and conditions, and thoughts cease due to causes and conditions. Extinguishing initial and sustained thought, internally joyful, single-minded, without initial or sustained thought, giving rise to joy and happiness, entering the second dhyāna. Brāhmaṇa! The first dhyāna thought ceases, and the second dhyāna thought arises. For this reason, it is known that thoughts cease due to causes and conditions, and thoughts arise due to causes and conditions. Abandoning joy, cultivating protection, single-minded mindfulness, knowing bodily happiness, the pursuit of the noble ones, pure protection and mindfulness, entering the third dhyāna. Brāhmaṇa! The second dhyāna thought ceases, and the third dhyāna thought arises. For this reason, it is known that thoughts cease due to causes and conditions, and thoughts arise due to causes and conditions. Abandoning suffering and happiness, first extinguishing joy and sorrow, protecting and purifying mindfulness, entering the fourth dhyāna. Brāhmaṇa! The third dhyāna thought ceases, and the fourth dhyāna thought arises. For this reason, it is known that thoughts cease due to causes and conditions, and thoughts arise due to causes and conditions. Abandoning all perceptions of form, extinguishing anger, not thinking of other perceptions, entering the sphere of space. Brāhmaṇa! All perceptions of form cease, and the perception of the sphere of space arises. For this reason, it is known that thoughts cease due to causes and conditions, and thoughts arise due to causes and conditions. Transcending the entire sphere of space, entering the sphere of consciousness. Brāhmaṇa! The perception of the sphere of space ceases, and the perception of the sphere of consciousness arises. For this reason, it is known that thoughts cease due to causes and conditions, and thoughts arise due to causes and conditions. Transcending the entire sphere of consciousness, entering the sphere of nothingness. Brāhmaṇa! The perception of the sphere of consciousness ceases, and the perception of the sphere of nothingness arises. For this reason, it is known that thoughts cease due to causes and conditions, and thoughts arise due to causes and conditions. Abandoning the sphere of nothingness, entering the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception. Brāhmaṇa! The perception of the sphere of nothingness ceases, and the perception of the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception arises. For this reason, it

「若如来出现于世，至真、等正觉，十号具足，有人于佛法中出家为道，乃至灭五盖覆蔽心者，除去欲、恶不善法，有觉、有观，离生喜、乐，入初禅，先灭欲想，生喜、乐想。梵志！以此故知有因缘想生，有因缘想灭。灭有觉、观，内喜、一心，无觉、无观，定生喜、乐，入第二禅。梵志！彼初禅想灭，二禅想生，以是故知有因缘想灭，有因缘想生。舍喜修护，专念一心，自知身乐，贤圣所求，护念清净，入三禅。梵志！彼二禅想灭，三禅想生，以是故知有因缘想灭，有因缘想生。舍苦舍乐，先灭忧喜，护念清净，入第四禅。梵志！彼三禅想灭，四禅想生，以是故知有因缘想灭，有因缘想生。舍一切色想，灭恚，不念异想，入空处。梵志！一切色想灭，空处想生，以是故知有因缘想灭，有因缘想生。越一切空处，入识处。梵志！彼空处想灭，识处想生，故知有因缘想灭，有因缘想生。越一切识处，入不用处。梵志！彼识处想灭，不用处想生，以是故知有因缘想灭，有因缘想生。舍不用处，入有想无想处。梵志！彼不用处想灭，有想无想处想生，以是故知有因缘想灭，有因缘想生。彼舍有想无想处，入想知灭定。梵志！彼有想无想处想灭，入想知灭定，以是故知有因缘想生，有因缘想灭。彼得此想已，作是念：『有念为恶，无念为善。』彼作是念时，彼微妙想不灭，更麤想生。彼复念言：『我今宁可不为念行，不起思惟。』彼不为念行，不起思惟已，微妙想灭，麤想不生。彼不为念行，不起思惟，微妙想灭，麤想不生时，即入想知灭定。云何？梵志！汝从本已来，颇曾闻此次第灭想因缘不？」

The Brāhmaṇa said to the Buddha, "From the beginning, I have never heard of such a gradual cessation of thoughts and conditions." He further said to the Buddha, "I now give rise to the thought, 'This is with thought, this is without thought.' Or, having thought this, they think, 'Having thoughts is bad, not having thoughts is good.' When they have this thought, the subtle thought does not cease, and the coarse thought arises again. They further think, 'I should not engage in thought, and not give rise to contemplation.' When they do not engage in thought, and do not give rise to contemplation, the subtle thought ceases, and the coarse thought does not arise. When they do not engage in thought, and do not give rise to contemplation, the subtle thought ceases, and the coarse thought does not arise, they immediately enter the concentration of cessation of perception and thought.

梵志白佛言：「从本已来信自不闻如是次第灭想因缘。」又白佛言：「我今生念，谓此有想、此无想，或复有想此想已，彼作是念：『有念为恶，无念为善。』彼作是念时，微妙想不灭，麤想更生，彼复念言：『我今宁可不为念行，不起思惟。』彼不为念行，不起思惟已，微妙想灭，麤想不生。彼不为念行，不起思惟，微妙想灭，麤想不生时，即入想知灭定。」

The Buddha told the Brāhmaṇa, "Excellent! Excellent! This is the gradual cessation of thoughts and conditions in the noble teachings."

佛告梵志言：「善哉！善哉！此是贤圣法中次第想灭想定。」

The Brāhmaṇa again said to the Buddha, "Among these thoughts, which one is the supreme thought?"

梵志复白佛言：「此诸想中，何者为无上想？」

The Buddha told the Brāhmaṇa, "The thought of the realm of nothingness is supreme."

佛告梵志：「不用处想为无上。」

The Brāhmaṇa again said to the Buddha, "Among all thoughts, which one is the supreme thought?"

梵志又白佛言：「诸想中，何者为第一无上想？」

The Buddha said, "Among all thoughts and words, the thought of the cessation of thought and perception is the supreme thought."

佛言：「诸想、诸言无想，于其中间能次第得想知灭定者，是为第一无上想。」

The Brāhmaṇa asked again, "Is it one thought or many thoughts?"

梵志又问：「为一想，为多想？」

The Buddha said, "There is one thought, not many thoughts."

佛言：「有一想，无多想。」

The Brāhmaṇa asked again, "Does thought arise first and then wisdom? Does wisdom arise first and then thought? Or do thought and wisdom arise simultaneously?"

梵志又问：「先有想生然后智？先有智生然后想？为想、智一时俱生耶？」

The Buddha said, "Thought arises first and then wisdom, because of thought there is wisdom."

佛言：「先有想生然后智，由想有智。」

The Brāhmaṇa asked again, "Is thought the self?"

梵志又问：「想即是我耶？」

The Buddha told the Brāhmaṇa, "Who do you say is the self?"

佛告梵志：「汝说何等人是我？」

The Brāhmaṇa said to the Buddha, "I do not say that a person is the self. I myself say that the physical body is composed of the four great elements and six sense bases, nurtured and raised by parents, clothed and adorned, impermanent and subject to decay. I say that this person is the self."

梵志白佛言：「我不说人是我，我自说色身四大、六入，父母生育，乳𫗦成长，衣服庄严，无常、磨灭法，我说此人是我。」

The Buddha told the Brāhmaṇa, "You say that the physical body is composed of the four great elements and six sense bases, nurtured and raised by parents, clothed and adorned, impermanent and subject to decay. You say that this person is the self. Brāhmaṇa! Leave aside this self for now. Just the thought of a person arises and ceases."

佛告梵志：「汝言色身四大、六入，父母生育，乳𫗦长成，衣服庄严，无常、磨灭法，说此人是我。梵志！且置此我，但人想生、人想灭。」

The Brāhmaṇa said, "I do not say that a person is the self. I say that the gods of the desire realm are the self."

梵志言：「我不说人是我，我说欲界天是我。」

The Buddha said, "Leave aside the gods of the desire realm as the self for now. Just the thought of a person arises and ceases."

佛言：「且置欲界天是我，但人想生、人想灭。」

The Brāhmaṇa said, "I do not say that a person is the self. I say that the gods of the form realm are the self."

梵志言：「我不说人是我，我自说色界天是我。」

The Buddha said, "Leave aside the gods of the form realm as the self for now. Just the thought of a person arises and ceases."

佛言：「且置色界天是我，但人想生、人想灭。」

The Brāhmaṇa said, "I do not say that a person is the self. I say that the formless heavens of the sphere of empty space, the sphere of consciousness, the sphere of nothingness, the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception, and the heavens of the formless realm are the self."

梵志言：「我不说人是我，我自说空处、识处、不用处、有想无想处、无色天是我。」

The Buddha said, "Leave aside the formless heavens of the sphere of empty space, the sphere of consciousness, the sphere of nothingness, the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception, and the heavens of the formless realm as the self for now. Just the thought of a person arises and ceases."

佛言：「且置空处、识处、无所有处、有想无想处、无色天是我，但人想生、人想灭。」

The Brāhmaṇa said to the Buddha, "How can I know the arising and ceasing of the thought of a person?"

梵志白佛言：「云何？瞿昙！我宁可得知人想生、人想灭不？」

The Buddha told the Brāhmaṇa, "If you want to know the arising and ceasing of the thought of a person, it is very difficult! Very difficult! Why is that? Because you have different views, different practices, different tolerance, and different acceptance, and you rely on different teachings."

佛告梵志：「汝欲知人想生、人想灭者，甚难！甚难！所以者何？汝异见、异习、异忍、异受，依异法故。」

The Brāhmaṇa said to the Buddha, "Indeed, Gautama! Because I have different views, different practices, different tolerance, and different acceptance, and I rely on different teachings, it is very difficult to know the arising and ceasing of the thought of a person. Why is that? Because I say that the self and the world are permanent, this is true and the rest is false; I say that the self and the world are impermanent, this is true and the rest is false; I say that the self and the world are both permanent and impermanent, this is true and the rest is false; I say that the self and the world are neither permanent nor impermanent, this is true and the rest is false. I say that the self and the world have an end, this is true and the rest is false; I say that the self and the world have no end, this is true and the rest is false; I say that the self and the world both have an end and have no end, this is true and the rest is false; I say that the self and the world are neither with an end nor without an end, this is true and the rest is false. I say that the life and the body are the same, this is true and the rest is false; I say that the life and the body are different, this is true and the rest is false; I say that the body and life are neither the same nor different, this is true and the rest is false; I say that there is no life and no body, this is true and the rest is false. I say that the Tathāgata perishes, this is true and the rest is false; I say that the Tathāgata does not perish, this is true and the rest is false; I say that the Tathāgata both perishes and does not perish, this is true and the rest is false; I say that the Tathāgata is neither perishing nor not perishing, this is true and the rest is false."

梵志白佛言：「如是。瞿昙！我异见、异习、异忍、异受，依异法故，欲知人想生、人想灭者，甚难！甚难！所以者何？我、世间有常，此实余虚；我、世间无常，此实余虚；我、世间有常无常，此实余虚；我、世间非有常非无常，此实余虚。我、世间有边，此实余虚；我、世间无边，此实余虚；我、世间有边无边，此实余虚；我、世间非有边非无边，此实余虚。是命是身，此实余虚；命异身异，此实余虚；身命非异非不异，此实余虚；无命无身，此实余虚。如来终，此实余虚；如来不终，此实余虚；如来终不终，此实余虚；如来非终非不终，此实余虚。」

The Buddha told the Brāhmaṇa, "I do not make any statement about whether the world is permanent, up to whether the Tathāgata is neither perishing nor not perishing."

佛告梵志：「世间有常，乃至如来非终非不终，我所不记。」

The Brāhmaṇa said to the Buddha, "Gautama! Why do you not make any statement about whether the self and the world are permanent, up to whether the Tathāgata is neither perishing nor not perishing?"

梵志白佛言：「瞿昙！何故不记，我、世间有常，乃至如来非终非不终，尽不记耶？」

The Buddha said, "This does not accord with the meaning, does not accord with the Dharma, is not the holy life, is not without desire, is not unconditioned, is not cessation, is not tranquility, is not right awakening, is not the śramaṇa, is not nirvāṇa. Therefore, I do not make any statement about it."

佛言：「此不与义合，不与法合，非梵行，非无欲，非无为，非寂灭，非止息，非正觉，非沙门，非泥洹，是故不记。」

The Brāhmaṇa again asked, "What is meant by accord with meaning and accord with the Dharma? What is the beginning of the holy life? What is unconditioned? What is without desire? What is cessation? What is tranquility? What is right awakening? What is the śramaṇa? What is nirvāṇa? What is meant by making a statement?"

梵志又问：「云何为义合、法合？云何为梵行初？云何无为？云何无欲？云何寂灭？云何止息？云何正觉？云何沙门？云何泥洹？云何名记？」

The Buddha told the Brāhmaṇa, "I make statements about the truth of suffering, the origin of suffering, the cessation of suffering, and the path leading to the cessation of suffering. Why is that? Because this accords with meaning and accords with the Dharma, is the beginning of the holy life, is without desire, is unconditioned, is cessation, is tranquility, is right awakening, is the śramaṇa, is nirvāṇa. Therefore, I make statements about it."

佛告梵志：「我记苦谛、苦集、苦灭、苦出要谛。所以者何？此是义合、法合，梵行初首、无欲、无为、寂灭、止息、正觉、沙门、泥洹，是故我记。」

At that time, the World-Honored One taught the Dharma to the Brāhmaṇa, showing, teaching, benefiting, and delighting him. Having done so, he then left his seat and departed.

尔时，世尊为梵志说法，示教利喜已，即从坐而去。

Not long after the Buddha left, the other Brāhmaṇas said to Puṣkarasvādi, "Why did you agree with what the Śramaṇa Gautama said? He said, 'I do not make statements about the permanence of the self and the world, up to the Tathāgata not being one who has ended or not ended, because it does not accord with meaning.' Why did you agree with his words? We cannot accept what the Śramaṇa Gautama said.

佛去未久，其后诸余梵志语布咤婆楼梵志曰：「汝何故听瞿昙沙门所说，语语印可？瞿昙言：『我及世间有常，乃至如来非终非不终，不与义合，故我不记。』汝何故印可是言？我等不可沙门瞿昙如是所说。」

Puṣkarasvādi replied to the Brāhmaṇas, "The Śramaṇa Gautama said, 'I do not make statements about the permanence of the self and the world, up to the Tathāgata not being one who has ended or not ended, because it does not accord with meaning.' I also do not agree with this statement. But the Śramaṇa Gautama abides in the Dharma, speaks the Dharma, and attains liberation through the Dharma. How can I go against his wise words? The Śramaṇa Gautama's subtle Dharma words cannot be contradicted.

布咤婆楼报诸梵志言：「沙门瞿昙所说：『我、世间有常，乃至如来非终非不终，不与义合，故我不记。』我亦不印可此言。但彼沙门瞿昙依法住，以法而言，以法出离，我当何由违此智言？沙门瞿昙如此微妙法言，不可违也。」

At another time, the Brāhmaṇa Puṣkarasvādi went with Śāriputra to see the World-Honored One. After exchanging greetings, he sat to one side. Śāriputra bowed to the Buddha and sat down. The Brāhmaṇa said to the Buddha, "Not long after the Buddha left my place, the other Brāhmaṇas said to me, 'Why did you agree with what the Śramaṇa Gautama said? He said, "I do not make statements about the permanence of the self and the world, up to the Tathāgata not being one who has ended or not ended, because it does not accord with meaning." Why did you agree with his words? We cannot accept what the Śramaṇa Gautama said.' I replied to them, 'The Śramaṇa Gautama said, "I do not make statements about the permanence of the self and the world, up to the Tathāgata not being one who has ended or not ended, because it does not accord with meaning." I also do not agree with this statement. But the Śramaṇa Gautama abides in the Dharma, speaks the Dharma, and attains liberation through the Dharma. How can I go against his wise words? The Śramaṇa Gautama's subtle Dharma words cannot be contradicted.'"

时，布咤婆楼梵志又于异时，共象首舍利弗诣世尊所，问讯已，一面坐。象首舍利弗礼佛而坐，梵志白佛言：「佛先在我所，时，去未久，其后诸余梵志语我言：『汝何故听沙门瞿昙所说，语语印可？瞿昙言：「我、世间常，乃至如来非终非不终，不合义，故不记。」汝何故印可是言？我等不可沙门瞿昙如是所说。』我报彼言：『沙门瞿昙所说：「我、世间有常，乃至如来非终非不终，不与义合，故我不记。」我亦不印可此言。但彼沙门瞿昙依法住法，以法而言，以法出离，我等何由违此智言？沙门瞿昙微妙法言，不可违也。』」

The Buddha told the Brāhmaṇa, "The Brāhmaṇas said, 'Why did you agree with what the Śramaṇa Gautama said?' This statement is wrong. Why is that? The Dharma I teach has definitive and non-definitive statements. What are the non-definitive statements? I also say that the self and the world are permanent, up to the Tathāgata not being one who has ended or not ended, but I do not make definitive statements about them. Why is that? This does not accord with meaning, does not accord with the Dharma, is not the beginning of the holy life, is not desireless, is not unconditioned, is not peace, is not cessation, is not true awakening, is not the śramaṇa, is not nirvāṇa. Therefore, Brāhmaṇa, although I say these words, I do not make definitive statements about them. What are the definitive statements? I make definitive statements about the truth of suffering, the origin of suffering, the cessation of suffering, and the path to the cessation of suffering. Why is that? This accords with the Dharma, accords with meaning, is the beginning of the holy life, is without attachment, is without conditioning, is peace, is cessation, is true awakening, is the śramaṇa, is nirvāṇa. Therefore, Brāhmaṇa, I say that there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who, in one place, are desireless, unconditioned, peaceful, ceased, truly awakened, śramaṇas, and nirvāṇa. Therefore, I make definitive statements about them.

佛告梵志曰：「诸梵志言：『汝何故听沙门瞿昙所说，语语印可？』此言有咎。所以者何？我所说法，有决定记、不决定记。云何名不决定记？我、世间有常，乃至如来非终非不终，我亦说此言而不决定记。所以然者？此不与义合，不与法合，非梵行初，非无欲，非无为，非寂灭，非止息，非正觉，非沙门，非泥洹。是故，梵志！我虽说此言而不决定记。云何名为决定记？我记苦谛、苦集、苦灭、苦出要谛。所以者何？此与法合、义合，是梵行初首，无定无记。梵志！或有沙门、婆罗门于一处世间，无欲，无为，寂灭，止息，正觉，沙门，泥洹，是故我说决定记。

"Brāhmaṇa, there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who, in one place, speak of the world as being entirely blissful. I ask them, 'Do you truly say that one place in the world is entirely blissful?' They reply, 'Yes.' I then ask them, 'Do you know and see that one place in the world is entirely blissful?' They answer, 'We do not know and see.' I further ask them, 'Have you ever seen the gods in that one place in the world who are entirely blissful?' They reply, 'We do not know and see.' I then ask them, 'Have you ever sat, stood, spoken, or practiced concentration with those gods in that one place in the world?' They answer, 'No.' I further ask them, 'Have those gods in that one place in the world who are entirely blissful ever come to you and said, "You should practice straightforwardly, and you will be reborn in that entirely blissful heaven. I have been reborn there because of practicing straightforwardly, and I enjoy the bliss there together with you."?' They answer, 'No.' I further ask them, 'Can you generate the mind to transform your own four great elements into the body of another, complete with all faculties, without deficiency?' They answer, 'No.' How is it, Brāhmaṇa? Is what those śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas say true and in accordance with the Dharma?"

「梵志！或有沙门、婆罗门于一处世间，一向说乐，我语彼言：『汝等审说，一处世间一向乐耶？』彼报我言：『如是。』我又语] cf.【丽】彼言：『汝知见一处世间一向乐耶？』彼答我言：『不知不见。』我复语彼言：『一处世间诸天一向乐，汝曾见不？』彼报我言：『不知不见。』又问彼言：『彼一处世间诸天，汝颇共坐起言语，精进修定不耶？』答我言：『不。』我又问彼言：『彼一处世间诸天一向乐者，颇曾来语汝言：「汝所行质直，当生彼一向乐天。我以所行质直，故得生彼共受乐。」耶？』彼答我言：『不也。』我又问彼言：『汝能于己身起心化作他四大身，身体具足，诸根无阙不？』彼答我言：『不能。』云何？梵志！彼沙门、婆罗门所言为是诚实，为应法不？」

The Brāhmaṇa said to the Buddha, "This is not true, it is not in accordance with the Dharma."

梵志白佛言：「此非诚实，为非法言。」

The Buddha said to the Brāhmaṇa, "It is like someone saying, 'I have had sexual intercourse with that beautiful woman.' Praising the prostitute. Others ask, 'Do you know that woman? Where is she? In the east, west, south, or north?' They answer, 'I don't know.' They further ask, 'Do you know the land, city, or village where she lives?' They answer, 'I don't know.' They further ask, 'Do you know her parents and their family name?' They answer, 'I don't know.' They further ask, 'Do you know if she is a kṣatriya woman, a brāhmaṇa, a householder, or a śūdra woman?' They answer, 'I don't know.' They further ask, 'Do you know if she is tall or short, coarse or fine, black or white, good-looking or ugly?' They answer, 'I don't know.' How is it, Brāhmaṇa? Is what this person says true?"

佛告梵志：「如有人言：『我与彼端正女人交通。』称赞婬女。余人问言：『汝识彼女不？为在何处？东方、西方、南方、北方耶？』答曰：『不知。』又问：『汝知彼女所止土地、城邑、村落不？』答曰：『不知。』又问：『汝识彼女父母及其姓字不？』答曰：『不知。』又问：『汝知彼女为刹利女，为是婆罗门、居士、首陀罗女耶？』答曰：『不知。』又问：『汝知彼女为长短、麤细、黑白、好丑耶？』答曰：『不知。』云何？梵志！此人所说为诚实不？」

He replied, "No."

答曰：「不也。」

"Brahmin, it is the same with those śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas. They are not true. Brahmin, it is like someone building a ladder on empty ground. Others ask, 'Why are you building a ladder?' They answer, 'I want to climb to the hall.' They further ask, 'Where is the hall?' They answer, 'I don't know.' How is it, Brahmin? Isn't the one building the ladder being deceptive?"

「梵志！彼沙门、婆罗门亦复如是，无有真实。梵志！犹如有人立梯空地，余人问言：『立梯用为？』答曰：『我欲上堂。』又问：『堂何所在？』答曰：『不知。』云何？梵志！彼立梯者岂非虚妄耶？」

He replied, "Yes, they are truly deceptive."

答曰：「如是，彼实虚妄。」

The Buddha said, "It is the same with those śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas. They are false and unreal."

佛言：「诸沙门、婆罗门亦复如是，虚妄无实。」

The Buddha told Puṣkarasārin, "You say that my body is composed of the four great elements and six sense bases, nurtured by my parents, grown through breast milk, adorned with clothing, impermanent and subject to decay. You consider this to be me. I say that this is defiled, pure, and can be liberated. What do you think? You may think that defiled dharmas cannot be extinguished, pure dharmas cannot be produced, and one always abides in suffering. Do not think this way. Why? Defiled dharmas can be extinguished, pure dharmas can be produced, abiding in the ground of bliss, joyfully loving, single-mindedly mindful, and wisdom greatly increases. Brahmins! I say that the desire realm gods, the gods of the empty realm, the gods of the consciousness realm, the gods of the non-perception realm, and the gods of the neither-perception-nor-non-perception realm are defiled, pure, and can be liberated. You may think that defiled dharmas cannot be extinguished, pure dharmas cannot be produced, and one always abides in suffering. Do not think this way. Why? Defiled dharmas can be extinguished, pure dharmas can be produced, abiding in the ground of bliss, joyfully loving, single-mindedly mindful, and wisdom greatly increases."

佛告布咤婆楼：「汝言我身色四大、六入，父母生育，乳𫗦成长，衣服庄严，无常、磨灭，以此为我者。我说此为染污，为清净，为得解？汝意或谓染污法不可灭，清净法不可生，常在苦中。勿作是念。何以故？染污法可灭尽，清净法可出生，处安乐地，欢喜爱乐，专念一心，智慧增广。梵志！我于欲界天、空处、识处、不用处、有想无想处天，说为染污，亦说清净，亦说得解。汝意或谓染污法不可灭，清净法不可生，常在苦中。勿作是念。所以者何？染污可灭，净法可生，处安乐地，欢喜爱乐，专念一心，智慧增广。」

At that time, Śāriputra, the chief of the elephants, asked, "World-Honored One! When there is a human body in the desire realm with the four great elements and the faculties, are there also the bodies of the desire realm gods, the form realm gods, the gods of the empty realm, the gods of the consciousness realm, the gods of the non-perception realm, and the gods of the neither-perception-nor-non-perception realm at the same time? World-Honored One! When there is a body of the desire realm gods, are there also the four great elements and faculties of a human body in the desire realm, and the bodies of the form realm gods, the gods of the empty realm, the gods of the consciousness realm, the gods of the non-perception realm, and the gods of the neither-perception-nor-non-perception realm at the same time? World-Honored One! When there is a body of the form realm gods, are there also the four great elements and faculties of a human body in the desire realm, and the bodies of the form realm gods, the gods of the empty realm, the gods of the consciousness realm, the gods of the non-perception realm, and the gods of the neither-perception-nor-non-perception realm at the same time? In this way, up to the bodies of the gods of the neither-perception-nor-non-perception realm, are there also the four great elements and faculties of a human body in the desire realm, and the bodies of the desire realm gods, the form realm gods, the gods of the empty realm, the gods of the consciousness realm, and the gods of the non-perception realm at the same time? "

尔时，象首舍利弗白言：「世尊！当有欲界人身四大诸根时，复有欲界天身、色界天身，空处、识处、不用处、有想无想处天身，一时有不？世尊！当有欲界天身时，复有欲界人身四大诸根，及色界天身，空处、识处、无所有处、有想无想处天身，一时有不？世尊！当有色界天身时，复有欲界人身四大诸根，及色界天身，空处、识处、无所有处、有想无想处天身，一时有不？如是至有想无想处天身时，有欲界人身四大诸根，及欲界天身，色界天身，空处、识处、无所有处天身，一时有不？」

The Buddha told Śāriputra, the chief of the elephants, "If there is a human body in the desire realm with the four great elements and the faculties, at that time there is only a human body in the desire realm with the four great elements and the faculties, not the bodies of the desire realm gods, the form realm gods, the gods of the empty realm, the gods of the consciousness realm, the gods of the non-perception realm, and the gods of the neither-perception-nor-non-perception realm. In this way, up to the bodies of the gods of the neither-perception-nor-non-perception realm, at that time there is only the body of the gods of the neither-perception-nor-non-perception realm, not the four great elements and faculties of a human body in the desire realm, and the bodies of the desire realm gods, the form realm gods, the gods of the empty realm, the gods of the consciousness realm, and the gods of the non-perception realm. Elephant Chief! It is like milk, which turns into curd, curd into fresh butter, fresh butter into clarified butter, and clarified butter into ghee, which is the best. Elephant Chief! When there is milk, it is only called milk, not curd, butter, or ghee. In this way, up to when it becomes ghee, it is only called ghee, not milk, curd, or butter. Elephant Chief! It is the same here. If there is a human body in the desire realm with the four great elements and the faculties, there are no bodies of the desire realm gods, the form realm gods, up to the gods of the neither-perception-nor-non-perception realm. In this way, up to when there are bodies of the gods of the neither-perception-nor-non-perception realm, there are only bodies of the gods of the neither-perception-nor-non-perception realm, not the four great elements and faculties of a human body in the desire realm, and the bodies of the desire realm gods, the form realm gods, up to the gods of the non-perception realm.

佛告象首舍利弗：「若有欲界人身四大诸根，尔时正有欲界人身四大诸根，非欲界天身，色界天身，空处、识处、无所有处、有想无想处天身。如是乃至有有想无想处天身时，尔时正有想无想处天身，无有欲界人身四大诸根，及欲界天身，色界天身，空处、识处、无所有处天身。象首！譬如牛乳，乳变为酪，酪为生酥，生酥为熟酥，熟酥为醍醐，醍醐为第一。象首！当有乳时，唯名为乳，不名为酪、酥、醍醐，如是展转，至醍醐时，唯名醍醐，不名为乳，不名酪、酥。象首！此亦如是，若有欲界人身四大诸根时，无有欲界天身，色界天身，乃至有想无想处天身；如是展转，有有想无想处天身时，唯有有想无想处天身，无有欲界人身四大诸根，及欲界天身，色界天身，乃至无所有天身。

"Elephant Chief! What do you think? If someone were to ask you, 'If there is a past body, are there also future and present bodies at the same time? If there is a future body, are there also past and present bodies at the same time? If there is a present body, are there also past and future bodies at the same time?' If asked this, how would you answer? "

「象首！于汝意云何？若有人问汝言：『若有过去身时，有未来、现在身，一时有不？有未来身时，有过去、现在身，一时有不？有现在身时，有过去、未来身，一时有不？』设有此问者，汝云何报？」

Śāriputra, the chief of the elephants, said, "If asked this, I would answer, 'If there is a past body, it is only a past body, not future or present. If there is a future body, it is only a future body, not past or present. If there is a present body, it is only a present body, not past or future.'"

象首言：「设有如是问者，我当报言：『有过去身时，唯是过去身，无未来、现在。有未来身时，唯是未来身，无过去、现在。有现在身时，唯是现在身，无过去、未来身。』」

Elephant Chief! It is the same here. When there is a human body in the desire realm with the four great elements and faculties, there is no heavenly body in the desire realm, no heavenly body in the form realm, up to no heavenly body in the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception. In this way, when there is a heavenly body in the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception, there is no human body in the desire realm with the four great elements and faculties, no heavenly body in the desire realm, no heavenly body in the form realm, up to no heavenly body in the sphere of nothingness.

「象首！此亦如是，有欲界人身四大诸根时，无欲界天身，色界天身，乃至有想无想处天身；如是展转，至有想无想处天身时，无有欲界人身四大诸根，及欲界天身，色界天身，至不用处天身。

"Furthermore, Elephant Chief! If someone asks you, 'Have you ever had a past that has already ceased? Will you have a future that will arise? Do you have a present that exists now?' If asked this, how would you answer?"

「复次，象首！若有人问汝言：『汝曾有过去已灭不？未来当生不？现在今有不？』设有是问者，汝当云何答？」

Elephant Chief said to the Buddha, "If asked this, I would answer them, 'I have had a past that has already ceased, it is not nonexistent. I will have a future that will arise, it is not nonexistent. I have a present that exists now, it is not nonexistent.'"

象首白佛言：「若有是问者，当答彼言：『我曾有过去已灭，非不有也。有未来当生，非不有也。现在今有，非不有也。』」

The Buddha said, "Elephant Chief! It is the same here. When there is a human body in the desire realm with the four great elements and faculties, there is no heavenly body in the desire realm, up to the body of the heaven of neither perception nor non-perception. In this way, when there is a body in the heaven of neither perception nor non-perception, there is no human body in the desire realm with the four great elements and faculties, nor a heavenly body in the desire realm, up to a heavenly body in the sphere of nothingness."

佛言：「象首！此亦如是，有欲界人身四大诸根时，无欲界天身，乃至有想无想天身；如是展转，至有想无想天身时，无有欲界人身四大诸根，及欲界天身，乃至无所有处天身。」

At that time, Elephant Chief said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, take refuge in the Dharma, take refuge in the Sangha. Please allow me to be an upāsaka in the true Dharma. From now on, for as long as I live, I will not kill, steal, engage in sexual misconduct, deceive, or drink alcohol."

尔时，象首白佛言：「世尊！我今归依佛，归依法，归依僧，听我于正法中为优婆塞，自今已后，尽形寿不杀、不盗、不婬、不欺、不饮酒。」

At that time, the brāhman Puṣkarasvādi said to the Buddha, "May I be allowed to go forth and receive the precepts in the Buddha's teachings?"

时，布咤婆楼梵志白佛言：「我得于佛法中出家受戒不？」

The Buddha told the brāhman, "If a non-Buddhist wishes to go forth and practice the path in my teachings, they must first observe for four months to see if they meet the approval of the assembly. Only then can they go forth and receive the precepts. Although there is this rule, it also depends on the individual."

佛告梵志：「若有异学欲于我法中出家为道者，先四月观察，称众人意，然后乃得出家受戒。虽有是法，亦观人] cf.【丽】耳。」

The brāhman said to the Buddha, "Those who wish to go forth and receive the precepts in the Buddha's teachings must first observe for four months to see if they meet the approval of the assembly. Then they can go forth and receive the precepts. As for me now, I am able to observe for four years to see if I meet the approval of the assembly. Only then do I hope to go forth and receive the precepts."

梵志白佛言：「诸有异学欲于佛法中出家受戒者，先当四月观察，称众人意，然后乃得出家受戒。如我今者，乃能于佛法中四岁观察，称众人意，然后乃望出家受戒。」

The Buddha told the brāhman, "I told you before that although there is this rule, it also depends on the individual."

佛告梵志：「我先语汝，虽有是法，当观其人。」

At that time, the brāhman was able to go forth and receive the precepts in the true Dharma. In this way, not long after, with firm faith, he practiced pure conduct. In this very life, he attained realization himself, exhausted birth and death, accomplished what was to be done, and would not be reborn again. He then became an arhat.

时，彼梵志即于正法中得出家受戒。如是不久以信坚固，净修梵行，于现法中自身作证，生死已尽，所作已办，不受后有，即成阿罗汉。

At that time, Puṣkarasārin heard what the Buddha said, and joyfully accepted it.

尔时，布咤婆楼闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
