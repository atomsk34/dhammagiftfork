DĀ 29

Dīrghāgama 长阿含经

Chapter 3 第三分

29 （二九）露遮经

Parallel to: DN 12

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in the human realm of Kosala, together with a great assembly of 1,250 bhikṣus, and arrived at the Śiśiravana forest north of the brāhmaṇa village of Brāhmaṇabaṭu, where he stayed.

一时，佛在拘萨罗人间游行，与大比丘众千二百五十人俱，往诣婆罗婆提婆罗门村北尸舍婆林中止宿。

At that time, there was a brāhmaṇa named Rucīka who lived in the Brāhmaṇabaṭu village. The village was prosperous and the people were flourishing. King Prasenajit granted this village to the brāhmaṇa as a share of the merit. This brāhmaṇa's parents for seven generations were genuine and not disparaged by others. He was proficient in reciting the three Vedas and was able to distinguish various scriptures. He was also skilled in the methods of divining auspicious and inauspicious omens, and in the ritual procedures of sacrifice. He heard that the śramaṇa Gautama, the son of the Śākya clan, had renounced the householder's life, attained enlightenment, and was traveling in the human realm of Kosala, arriving at the Śiśiravana forest. He had a great reputation that spread throughout the world. He was a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, endowed with the ten titles. Among the assemblies of gods, humans, Māra, Māra's followers, śramaṇas, and brāhmaṇas, he personally attained realization and taught the Dharma to others, with the good in the beginning, middle, and end, complete in meaning and taste, and pure in the practice of the holy life. "Such a true person should be visited and seen. I should now go and meet him."

时，有婆罗门，名曰露遮，住婆罗林中，其村丰乐，人民炽盛，波斯匿王即封此村，与婆罗门以为梵分。此婆罗门七世已来父母真正，不为他人之所轻毁，异典三部讽诵通利，种种经书尽能分别，又能善于大人相法、瞻候] cf.【丽】吉凶、祭祀仪礼。闻沙门瞿昙释种子出家成道，于拘萨罗国人间游行，至尸舍婆林中，有大名称，流闻天下，如来、至真、等正觉，十号具足，于诸天、世人、魔、若魔天、沙门、婆罗门众中自身作证，与他说法，上中下善，义味具足，梵行清净。「如此真人，宜往觐现，我今宁可往共相见。」

At that time, the brāhmaṇa left the village and went to the Śiśiravana forest, where he approached the World-Honored One. After exchanging greetings, he sat to one side. The Buddha taught him the Dharma, showing, teaching, benefiting, and delighting him. Having heard the Dharma, the brāhmaṇa said to the Buddha, "I only wish that the World-Honored One and the great assembly would accept my invitation for tomorrow." At that time, the World-Honored One silently accepted the invitation.

时，婆罗门即出彼村，诣尸舍婆林中，至世尊所，问讯已，一面坐。佛为说法，示教利喜，婆罗门闻法已，白佛言：「唯愿世尊及诸大众明受我请。」尔时，世尊默然受请。

Seeing the Buddha's silent acceptance, the brāhmaṇa rose from his seat, circumambulated the Buddha, and left. Not far from the Buddha, he gave rise to evil views, saying, "The śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas know many good dharmas and have attained many realizations. They should not teach others. They should just know for themselves and not teach others. It is like someone who destroys an old prison and builds a new one. This is greed for evil and unwholesome dharmas.

彼婆罗门见佛默然，知已许可，即从坐起，遶佛而去。去佛不远，便起恶见言：「诸沙门、婆罗门多知善法，多所证成，不应为他人说，但自知，休与他说为。譬如有人坏故狱已，更造新狱，斯是贪恶不善法耳。」

At that time, the brāhmaṇa returned to the Brāhmaṇa Forest and prepared various kinds of delicious food and drink that very night. When the time came, he said to the barber, "Please go to the Śiśiravana forest and tell the śramaṇa Gautama, 'The time has come. Please know it is time.'"

时，婆罗门还至婆罗林已，即于其夜具办种种肴饍饮食。时到，语剃头师言：「汝持我声，诣尸舍婆林中，白沙门瞿昙：『日时已到，宜知是时。』」

The barber accepted the instruction and went to where the Buddha was. He bowed at the Buddha's feet and said, "The time has come. Please know it is time."

剃头师受教即行，往到佛所，礼世尊足白：「时已到，宜知是时。」

At that time, the World-Honored One put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and together with his disciples of 1,250 people, went to the Brāhmaṇa Forest.

尔时，世尊即著衣持钵，从诸弟子千二百五十人俱，诣婆罗林。

The barber attended the World-Honored One, bared his right arm, knelt on his right knee, joined his palms, and said to the Buddha, "The Brāhmaṇa Rucīka, not far from the Buddha, gave rise to evil views, saying, 'Among the śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, those who know many good dharmas and have many realizations should not explain them to others. They should just know for themselves and not explain them to others. It is like a person who destroys an old prison and builds a new one. This is greed, evil, and unwholesome.' I only wish that the World-Honored One would remove his evil views."

剃头师侍从世尊，偏露右臂，长跪叉手，白佛言：「彼露遮婆罗门去佛不远，生恶见言：『诸有沙门、婆罗门多知善法，多所证者，不应为他人说，但自知，休与他说为。譬如有人坏故狱已，更造新狱，斯是贪恶不善法耳。』唯愿世尊除其恶见。」

The Buddha told the barber, "This is a small matter, easy to transform."

佛告剃头师曰：「此是小事，易开化耳。」

At that time, the World-Honored One arrived at the Brāhmaṇa's house, took his seat, and the Brāhmaṇa personally served various delicious foods to the Buddha and the Sangha. After the meal, the Buddha and the Sangha put away their bowls, washed their hands, and the Brāhmaṇa took a small bench and sat in front of the Buddha. The Buddha said to Rucīka, "Yesterday, not far from me, you gave rise to evil views, saying, 'Among the śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, those who know many good dharmas and have many realizations should not explain them to others. They should just know for themselves and not explain them to others. It is like a person who destroys an old prison and builds a new one. This is greed, evil, and unwholesome.' Did you really say this?"

尔时，世尊至婆罗门舍，就座而坐。时，婆罗门以种种甘饍，手自斟酌，供佛及僧，食讫去钵，行澡水毕，取一小床于佛前坐。佛告露遮：「汝昨去我不远，生恶见言：『诸沙门、婆罗门多知善法，多所证者，不应为他人说，乃至贪恶不善法。』实有是言耶？」

Rucīka said, "Yes, it is true."

露遮言：「尔，实有此事。」

The Buddha told Rucīka, "Do not give rise to such evil views again. Why is that? There are three teachers in the world who can admonish themselves. What are the three? First, shaving the beard and hair, wearing the three Dharma robes, leaving home to cultivate the path, and in this present life being able to eliminate afflictions, and also being able to increase and attain the Dharma of the superior person. But in this present life not being able to eliminate afflictions, and not attaining the Dharma of the superior person, one's own practice not yet accomplished, yet explaining the Dharma to disciples. Those disciples do not respectfully serve and support. Moreover, relying on and dwelling together. Rucīka! Those disciples say to the teacher, 'The teacher now shaves the beard and hair, wears the three Dharma robes, leaves home to cultivate the path, and in this present life can eliminate various afflictions, and attain the superior person's supreme Dharma. But now in this present life cannot eliminate afflictions, and does not attain the superior person's supreme Dharma. One's own practice not yet accomplished, yet explaining the Dharma to disciples, causing the disciples to no longer respectfully serve and support, but only relying on and dwelling together.'"

佛告露遮：「汝勿复尔生此恶见。所以者何？世有三师可以自诫。云何为三？一者剃除须发，服三法衣，出家修道，于现法中可以除烦恼，又可增益得上人法。而于现法中不除烦恼，不得上人法，己业未成而为弟子说法，其诸弟子不恭敬承事，由复依止与共同住。露遮！彼诸弟子语师言：『师今剃除须发，服三法衣，出家修道，于现法中可得除众烦恼，得上人胜法。而今于现法中不能除烦恼，不得上人胜法。己业未成而为弟子说法，使诸弟子不复恭敬承事供养，但共依止同住而已。』」

The Buddha said, "Rucīka! It is like a person who destroys an old prison and builds a new one. This is called greedily indulging in evil dharmas. This is the first teacher who can admonish himself. This is the wise and holy precepts, the precepts of the Vinaya, the precepts of propriety, and the precepts of time."

佛言：「露遮！犹如有人坏故狱已，更造新狱，斯则名为贪浊恶法。是为一师可以自诫。是为贤圣戒、律戒、仪戒、时戒。」

Again, he told Rucīka, "The second teacher is one who shaves the beard and hair, wears the three Dharma robes, leaves home to cultivate the path, and in this present life can eliminate various afflictions, but cannot increase and attain the Dharma of the superior person. But in this present life cannot eliminate various afflictions, although having attained a little of the superior person's supreme Dharma, one's own practice not yet accomplished, yet explaining the Dharma to disciples. Those disciples do not respectfully serve and support. Moreover, relying on and dwelling together. Rucīka! Those disciples say to the teacher, 'The teacher now shaves the beard and hair, wears the three Dharma robes, leaves home to cultivate the path, and in this present life can eliminate various afflictions, and attain the superior person's supreme Dharma. But now in this present life cannot eliminate various afflictions, although having attained a little of the superior person's supreme Dharma, one's own benefit not yet accomplished, yet explaining the Dharma to disciples, causing the disciples to no longer respectfully serve and support, but only relying on and dwelling together.'"

又告露遮：「第二师者，剃除须发，服三法衣，出家修道，于现法中可得除众烦恼，不可增益得上人法，而于现法中不能除众烦恼，虽复少多得上人胜法，己业未成而为弟子说法，其诸弟子不恭敬承事，由复依止与共同住。露遮！彼诸弟子语师言：『师今剃除须发，服三法衣，出家修道，于现法中得除众烦恼，得上人法。而今于现法中不能除众烦恼，虽复少多得上人法，己利未成而为弟子说法，使诸弟子不复恭敬承事供养，但共依止同住而已。』」

The Buddha said, "Rucīka! It is like a person walking behind someone, touching their back with their hand. This is called greedily indulging in evil dharmas. This is the second teacher who can admonish himself. This is the wise and holy precepts, the precepts of the Vinaya, the precepts of propriety, and the precepts of time."

佛言：「露遮！犹如有人在他后行，手摩他背，此则名为贪浊恶法。是为二师可以自诫。是为贤圣戒、律戒、仪戒、时戒。」

Again, he told Rucīka, "The third teacher is one who shaves the beard and hair, wears the three Dharma robes, leaves home to cultivate the path, and in this present life can eliminate afflictions, and can also increase and attain the Dharma of the superior person. But in this present life cannot eliminate various afflictions, although having attained a little of the superior person's supreme Dharma, one's own benefit not yet accomplished, yet explaining the Dharma to disciples. Those disciples respectfully serve and support. Relying on and dwelling together. Rucīka! Those disciples say to the teacher, 'The teacher now shaves the beard and hair, wears the three Dharma robes, leaves home to cultivate the path, and in this present life can eliminate various afflictions, and attain a little of the superior person's supreme Dharma. But now in this present life cannot eliminate various afflictions, although having attained a little of the superior person's supreme Dharma, one's own benefit not yet accomplished, yet explaining the Dharma to disciples, causing the disciples to respectfully serve and support, dwelling together.'"

又告露遮：「第三师者，剃除须发，服三法衣，出家修道，于现法中可除烦恼，又可增益得上人法，而于现法中不能除众烦恼，虽复少多得上人法，己利未成而为弟子说法，其诸弟子恭敬承事，依止同住。露遮！彼诸弟子语师言：『师今剃除须发，服三法衣，出家修道，于现法中可得除众烦恼，少多得上人法，而今于现法中不能除众烦恼，虽复少多得上人法，己利未成而为弟子说法，诸弟子恭敬承事，共止同住。』」

The Buddha said, "Rucīka! It is like a person abandoning their own crops and weeding someone else's field. This is called greedily indulging in evil dharmas. This is the third teacher who can admonish himself. This is the wise and holy precepts, the precepts of the Vinaya, the precepts of propriety, and the precepts of time. Rucīka! There is one World-Honored One who does not exist in the world and cannot be shaken. What is that one? If the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One appears in the world, and attains the three insights, eliminates ignorance, gives rise to the light of wisdom, removes all darkness, and emits the great light of the Dharma, namely the knowledge and vision of the exhaustion of taints. Why is this? It is because of diligent effort, single-minded mindfulness, the attainment of dwelling alone in seclusion. Rucīka! This is the first World-Honored One who does not exist in the world and cannot be shaken. Rucīka! There are four fruits of the śramaṇa. What are the four? They are the fruit of stream-entry, the fruit of once-returning, the fruit of non-returning, and the fruit of arahantship. Rucīka! How is it? If someone hears the Dharma and should attain these four fruits of the śramaṇa, if someone were to stop them, saying, 'Do not explain the Dharma,' and they followed that person's words, would that person hear the Dharma and attain the fruits?"

佛言：「露遮！犹如有人舍己禾稼，锄他田苗，此则名为贪浊恶法。是为三师可以自诫。是为贤圣戒、律戒、仪戒、时戒。露遮！有一世尊不在世间，不可倾动，云何为一？若如来、至真、等正觉出现于世，乃至得三明，除灭无明，生智慧明，去诸暗冥，出大法光，所谓漏尽智证。所以者何？斯由精勤，专念不忘，乐独闲居之所得也。露遮！是为第一世尊不在世间，不可倾动。露遮！有四沙门果。何者四？谓须陀洹果、斯陀含果、阿那含果、阿罗汉果。云何？露遮！有人闻法应得此四沙门果。若有人遮言：『勿为说法。』设用其言者，彼人闻法得果以不？」

He replied, "They would not attain it."

答曰：「不得。」

He further asked, "If they do not attain the fruits, can they be reborn in the heavens?"

又问：「若不得果，得生天不？」

He replied, "They would not attain it."

答曰：「不得。」

He further asked, "Is it a good or bad mind to obstruct their Dharma teaching, causing them not to attain the fruits and not to be reborn in the heavens?"

又问：「遮他说法，使不得果，不得生天。为是善心，为不善心耶？」

He replied, "It is not good."

答曰：「不善。」

He further asked, "Does an unwholesome mind lead to a good or an evil destination?"

又问：「不善心者，为生善趣，为堕恶趣？」

He replied, "It leads to an evil destination."

答曰：「生恶趣。」

"Ruciga, suppose someone tells King Prasenajit, 'The king should use all the wealth in his kingdom for himself and not give it to others.' What do you think, Ruciga? If one follows that person's words, will it cut off the provisions for others?"

「露遮！犹如有人语波斯匿王言：『王所有国土，其中财物王尽自用，勿给余人。』云何？露遮！若用彼人言者，当断余人供不？」

He replied, "It will cut off."

答曰：「当断。」

He asked again, "Cutting off others' provisions, is it a good or bad mind?"

又问：「断他供者，为是善心，为不善心？」

He replied, "It is a bad mind."

答曰：「不善心。」

He asked again, "With a bad mind, will one be reborn in a good realm or fall into an evil path?"

又问：「不善心者，为生善趣，为堕恶道耶？」

He replied, "One will fall into an evil path."

答曰：「堕恶道。」

"Rucā, it is the same. If someone hears the Dharma and should attain the four fruits of the śramaṇa, if someone says, 'Do not teach the Dharma,' and if one follows their words, will that person hear the Dharma and attain the fruits?"

「露遮！彼亦如是，有人闻法，应得四沙门果。若有人言：『勿为说法。』设用其言者，彼人闻法得果不？」

He replied, "They will not attain it."

答曰：「不得。」

He further asked, "If they do not attain the fruits, will they be reborn in the heavens?"

又问：「若不得果，得生天不？」

He replied, "They will not be reborn in the heavens."

答曰：「不得。」

He further asked, "When one obstructs others from teaching the Dharma, causing them not to attain the fruits of the path and not to be reborn in the heavens, is that a good or an evil mind?"

又问：「遮他说法，使不得道果，不得生天。彼为是善心，为不善心耶？」

He replied, "It is not good."

答曰：「不善。」

He further asked, "Those with an unwholesome mind, will they be reborn in a good realm or fall into an evil path?"

又问：「不善心者，当生善趣，为堕恶道耶？」

He replied, "They will fall into an evil path."

答曰：「堕恶道。」

"Luocha, if someone tells you, 'The wealth and property of the village of Paravati belongs to you, Luocha. Use it yourself, do not give it to others. Why give it to others?' What do you think, Luocha? If you follow their words, will you cut off the offerings to others?"

「露遮！若有人语汝言：『彼波罗婆提村封所有财物。露遮！自用勿给人，物当自用，与他何为？』云何？露遮！设用彼言者，当断余人供不？」

He replied, "It will be cut off."

答曰：「当断。」

He asked again, "Teaching others to cut off the offerings of others, is this a good or a bad mind?"

又问：「教人断他供者，为是善心，为不善心耶？」

He replied, "It is not good."

答曰：「不善。」

He asked again, "Those with an unwholesome mind, do they go to a good realm or fall into an evil path?"

又问：「不善心者，为生善趣，为堕恶道耶？」

He replied, "They fall into an evil path."

答曰：「堕恶道。」

"Rucā, it is the same. If someone hears the Dharma and should attain the four fruits of the śramaṇa, if someone says, 'Do not teach the Dharma,' and if one follows their words, will that person hear the Dharma and attain the fruits?"

「露遮！彼亦如是，有人闻法应得四沙门果，若有人言：『勿为说法。』设用其言者，彼人闻法得果不？」

He replied, "They will not attain it."

答曰：「不得。」

He further asked, "If they do not attain the fruits, will they be reborn in the heavens?"

又问：「若不得果，得生天不？」

He replied, "They will not be reborn in the heavens."

答曰：「不得。」

He further asked, "Is it a good or bad mind to obstruct their Dharma teaching, causing them not to attain the fruits and not to be reborn in the heavens?"

又问：「遮他说法，使不得果，不得生天。为是善心，为不善心耶？」

He replied, "It is not good."

答曰：「不善。」

He further asked, "Does an unwholesome mind lead to a good realm or a bad realm?"

又问：「不善心者，为生善趣，为堕恶道耶？」

He replied, "It leads to a bad realm."

答曰：「堕恶道。」

At that time, the brāhman Rucā said to the Buddha, "I take refuge in the Buddha, I take refuge in the Dharma, I take refuge in the Saṃgha. Please allow me to be an upāsaka in the true Dharma. From now on, for as long as I live, I will not kill, steal, engage in sexual misconduct, deceive, or drink alcohol."

尔时，露遮婆罗门白佛言：「我归依佛，归依法，归依僧，愿听我于正法中为优婆塞，自今已后，尽形寿不杀、不盗、不婬、不欺、不饮酒。」

After the Buddha had spoken the Dharma, the brāhman Rucā, having heard what the Buddha had said, joyfully received and practiced it.

佛说法已，时，露遮婆罗门闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
