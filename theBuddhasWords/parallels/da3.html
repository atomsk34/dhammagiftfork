DĀ 3

Dīrghāgama 长阿含经

Chapter 1 第一分

3 （三）典尊经

Parallel to: DN 19

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was at Vulture Peak in Rājagṛha, together with a great assembly of 1,250 bhikṣus.

一时，佛在罗阅祇耆阇崛山，与大比丘众千二百五十人俱。

At that time, the Gandharva general Pañcaśikha, in the still and silent night when there were no people, emitted a great light, illuminating Vulture Peak, and came to where the Buddha was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and stood to one side. Then Pañcaśikha said to the World-Honored One, "Yesterday, the Brahma King came to the Heaven of the Thirty-three and discussed with Śakra. I personally heard it from him. Should I now tell it to the World-Honored One?"

尔时，执乐天般遮翼子，于夜静寂无人之时，放大光明，照耆阇崛山来至佛所，头面礼佛足已，在一面立。时，般遮翼白世尊言：「昨梵天王至忉利天，与帝释共议。我亲从彼闻，今者宁可向世尊说不？」

The Buddha said, "If you wish to speak, then speak."

佛言：「汝欲说者，便可说之。」

Pañcaśikha said, "Once, the gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three gathered in the Dharma lecture hall to discuss. At that time, the Four Heavenly Kings each sat in their respective positions according to their directions. The Heavenly King Dhṛtarāṣṭra sat in the east, facing west, with Śakra in front of him; the Heavenly King Virūḍhaka sat in the south, facing north, with Śakra in front of him; the Heavenly King Virūpākṣa sat in the west, facing east, with Śakra in front of him; the Heavenly King Vaiśravaṇa sat in the north, facing south, with Śakra in front of him. When the Four Heavenly Kings had all sat down first, then I sat down. There were also other great gods who had previously practiced pure and chaste conduct in the presence of the Buddha. After their lives ended here, they were reborn in the Heaven of the Thirty-three, causing those gods to increase their five blessings: first, the lifespan of the gods; second, the appearance of the gods; third, the fame of the gods; fourth, the happiness of the gods; fifth, the majestic virtue of the gods. At that time, all the gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three joyfully exclaimed, 'The number of gods is increasing, and the number of asuras is decreasing.' Then Śakra, Lord of the Gods, knowing that the minds of the gods were filled with joy, spoke a verse for the gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three, saying:

般遮翼言：「一时，忉利诸天集法讲堂，有所讲论。时，四天王随其方面，各当位坐，提帝赖咤天王在东方坐，其面西向，帝释在前；毘楼勒天王在南方坐，其面北向，帝释在前；毘楼博叉天王在西方坐，其面东向，帝释在前；毘沙门天王在北方坐，其面南向，帝释在前。时，四天王皆先坐已，然后我坐，复有余大神天，皆先于佛所，净修梵行，于此命终，生忉利天，使彼诸天，增益五福：一者天寿，二者天色，三者天名称，四者天乐，五者天威德。时，诸忉利天皆踊跃欢喜言：『增益诸天众，减损阿须伦众。』尔时，释提桓因知诸天人有欢喜心，即为忉利诸天而作颂曰：

The gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three enjoy themselves with Śakra
paying homage to the Tathāgata, the supreme king of the Dharma. The
gods receive the blessings of the shadow, with lifespan, appearance, fame, happiness
and majestic virtue. Having practiced chaste conduct in the presence of the
Buddha, they are reborn here. There are also other gods, with extremely
majestic radiance and color. The disciples of the Buddha's wisdom are reborn
here, even more distinguished. The Heaven of the Thirty-three and the Heaven
of Yāma contemplate this self-enjoyment, paying homage to the Tathāgata, the supreme
king of the Dharma.

「『忉利诸天人，　　帝释相娱乐；礼敬于如来，　　最上法之王。诸天受影福，　　寿、色、名、乐、威；于佛修梵行，　　故来生此间。复有诸天人，　　光色甚巍巍；佛智慧弟子，　　生此复殊胜。忉利及因提，　　思惟此自乐；礼敬于如来，　　最上法之王。』

At that time, the gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three, having heard this verse, were even more joyful and elated, unable to contain themselves. They increased the number of gods and reduced the number of asuras. Śakra, Lord of the Gods, seeing that the gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three were joyful and delighted, said to them, 'Virtuous ones! Do you wish to hear the Tathāgata's eight unequaled dharmas?' The gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three said, 'We wish to hear them with great joy!'

「尔时，忉利诸天闻此偈已，倍复欢喜，不能自胜，增益诸天众，减损阿须伦众。释提桓因见忉利天欢喜悦豫，即告之曰：『诸贤！汝等颇欲闻如来八无等法不？』时，忉利诸天言：『愿乐欲闻！』

Śakra replied, 'Listen attentively! Listen attentively! Consider it well! Virtuous ones! The Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, endowed with the ten titles, is not seen in the past, future, or present to be like the Buddha, endowed with the ten titles. The Buddha's Dharma is subtle and wonderful, well worth expounding. It is practiced by the wise. In the past, future, or present, there is not seen to be a subtle Dharma like the Buddha's. The Buddha, by means of this Dharma, awakens himself, penetrates without obstruction, and delights in himself. In the past, future, or present, there is not seen to be one who can awaken himself by means of this Dharma, penetrate without obstruction, and delight in himself like the Buddha. Virtuous ones! Having awakened himself by means of this Dharma, the Buddha is also able to reveal the path to nirvāṇa, approaching it gradually and entering into tranquility. It is like the waters of the Ganges and the Yamunā flowing together into the great ocean. The Buddha is also like this, skillfully revealing the path to nirvāṇa, approaching it gradually and entering into tranquility. In the past, future, or present, there is not seen to be one who can reveal the path to nirvāṇa like the Buddha. Virtuous ones! The Tathāgata's retinue is accomplished. Kṣatriyas, brāhmaṇas, householders, śramaṇas, and those with wisdom are all accomplished retinues of the Tathāgata. In the past, future, or present, there is not seen to be an accomplished retinue like the Buddha. Virtuous ones! The Tathāgata's great assembly is accomplished, namely bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, and upāsikās. In the past, future, or present, there is not seen to be an accomplished great assembly like the Buddha. Virtuous ones! The Tathāgata's words and actions are in accord. What is said is as practiced, and what is practiced is as said. This is the accomplishment of the Dharma by the Dharma. In the past, future, or present, there is not seen to be one whose words and actions are in accord, accomplishing the Dharma by the Dharma like the Buddha. Virtuous ones! The Tathāgata benefits many and brings peace to many, benefiting devas and humans with a mind of loving-kindness and compassion. In the past, future, or present, there is not seen to be one who benefits many and brings peace to many like the Buddha. Virtuous ones! These are the Tathāgata's eight unequaled dharmas.'

「帝释报言：『谛听！谛听！善思念之。诸贤！如来、至真、等正觉，十号具足，不见过去、未来、现在有如来、至真，十号具足，如佛者也。佛法微妙，善可讲说，智者所行，不见过去、未来、现在有微妙法，如佛者也。佛由此法，而自觉悟，通达无碍，以自娱乐，不见过去、未来、现在能于此法而自觉悟，通达无碍，以自娱乐，如佛者也。诸贤！佛以此法自觉悟已，亦能开示涅槃径路，亲近渐至，入于寂灭。譬如恒河水、炎摩水，二水竝流，入于大海。佛亦如是，善能开示涅槃径路，亲近渐至，入于寂灭，不见过去、未来、现在有能开示涅槃径路，如佛者也。诸贤！如来眷属成就，刹利、婆罗门、居士、沙门、有智慧者，皆是如来成就眷属，不见过去、未来、现在眷属成就，如佛者也。诸贤！如来大众成就，所谓比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆夷，不见过去、未来、现在大众成就，如佛者也。诸贤！如来言行相应，所言如行，所行如言，如是则为法法成就，不见过去、未来、现在言行相应，法法成就，如佛者也。诸贤！如来多所饶益，多所安乐，以慈愍心利益天人，不见过去、未来、现在多所饶益，多所安乐，如佛者也。诸贤！是为如来八无等法。』

"At that time, the gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three said, 'If there were eight Buddhas in the world, it would greatly increase the number of gods and decrease the number of asuras.' The gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three said, 'Forget about eight Buddhas. Even if there were seven, six, or even two Buddhas appearing in the world, it would greatly increase the number of gods and decrease the number of asuras, let alone eight.' Śakra, Lord of the Gods, told the gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three, 'I have heard from the Buddha and received it directly from the Buddha. I wish for two Buddhas to appear in the world at the same time, but it is impossible. It is only because the Tathāgata abides in the world for a long time, out of great compassion and for the benefit of many, that devas and humans find peace. This greatly increases the number of gods and decreases the number of asuras.'"

「时，忉利天作是说言：『若使世间有八佛出者，当大增益诸天众，减损阿须伦众。』时，忉利天言：『且置八佛，正使七佛、六佛，乃至二佛出世者，亦大增益诸天众，减损阿须伦众，何况八佛？』时，释提桓因告忉利天言：『我从佛闻，亲从佛受，欲使一时二佛出世，无有是处。但使如来久存于世，多所慈愍，多所饶益，天人获安，则大增益诸天，减损阿须伦众。』」

At that time, Pañcaśikha addressed the Buddha, saying, "World-Honored One! The reason the gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three gather in the Dharma assembly hall is to discuss and contemplate, to weigh and observe, to give instructions, and then to order the Four Heavenly Kings. After the Four Heavenly Kings receive the instructions, they each take their seats. Before long, there is a great strange light illuminating the four directions. When the gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three see this light, they are all greatly startled, thinking, 'What strange phenomenon is this strange light?' The great gods with majestic virtue are also frightened, thinking, 'What strange phenomenon is this strange light?' At that time, the Great Brahma King transformed himself into a youth with a five-peaked topknot, standing in the sky above the great assembly. His appearance was upright and handsome, surpassing the others. His body was purple-golden in color, outshining the light of the gods. The gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three did not rise to welcome him, nor did they pay respect to him, nor did they invite him to sit. At that time, the Brahma youth took his seat wherever he went. Sitting there, he was joyful, just like when a kṣatriya anointed with water ascends the throne, he is elated with joy. Before long, he again transformed his body, taking on the appearance of a youth with a five-peaked topknot, sitting in the sky above the great assembly. He sat upright and unmoving, just like a strong man sitting in a comfortable seat. He then spoke a verse, saying:

时，般遮翼白佛言：「世尊！忉利诸天所以集法讲堂上者，共议思惟，称量观察，有所教令，然后敕四天王。四天王受教已，各当位而坐，其坐未久，有大异光照于四方。时，忉利天见此光已，皆大惊愕：『今此异光，将有何怪？』诸大神天有威德者，亦皆惊怖：『今此异光，将有何怪？』时，大梵王即化为童子，头五角髻，在大众上虚空中立，颜貌端正，与众超绝，身紫金色，蔽诸天光。时，忉利天亦不起迎，亦不恭敬，又不请坐。时，梵童子随所诣坐，坐生欣悦，譬如刹利水浇头种，登王位时，踊跃欢喜。来坐未久，复自变身，作童子像，头五角髻，在大众上虚空中坐，譬如力士坐于安座，嶷然不动。而作颂曰：

The gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three, Śakra, and the others
enjoy themselves together. They pay respect to the Tathāgata, the supreme king
of the Dharma. The gods receive the merit of the shadow, with
lifespan, appearance, fame, happiness, and majesty. They cultivate the holy life under
the Buddha, and therefore are reborn here. There are also gods with
extremely majestic radiance and color. The disciples of the Buddha's wisdom are
reborn here, even more distinguished. The Heaven of the Thirty-three and the
Heaven of Yāma contemplate this self-enjoyment. They pay respect to the Tathāgata
the supreme king of the Dharma.

「『忉利诸天人，　　帝释相娱乐；礼敬于如来，　　最上法之王。诸天受影福，　　寿、色、名、乐、威；于佛修梵行，　　故来生此间。复有诸天人，　　光色甚巍巍；佛智慧弟子，　　生此复殊胜。忉利及因提，　　思惟此自乐；礼敬于如来，　　最上法之王。』

At that time, the gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three said to the youth, 'We have heard Śakra Devānām-Indra praise the Tathāgata's eight unequaled dharmas, and we are overjoyed, unable to contain ourselves.' At that time, the Brahma youth said to the gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three, 'What are the Tathāgata's eight unequaled dharmas? I also wish to hear them.' At that time, Śakra Devānām-Indra then spoke to the youth about the Tathāgata's eight unequaled dharmas. When the gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three and the youth heard this, they were doubly joyful, unable to contain themselves. The ranks of the gods increased, while the ranks of the asuras decreased. At that time, the youth saw the gods rejoicing, and he was even more delighted. He then said to the gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three, 'Do you wish to hear one unequaled dharma?' The gods said, 'Excellent! We wish to hear it with joy.'

「时，诸忉利天语童子曰：『吾等闻天帝释称说如来八无等法，欢喜踊跃，不能自胜。』时，梵童子语忉利天言：『何等如来八无等法？吾亦乐闻。』时，天帝释即为童子说如来八无等法，忉利诸天、童子闻说已，倍复欢喜，不能自胜，增益诸天众，减损阿须伦众。是时，童子见天欢喜，复增欣跃，即告忉利天曰：『汝等欲闻一无等法不？』天曰：『善哉！愿乐欲闻。』

The youth said, 'If you wish to hear, listen attentively and accept it well! I shall explain it to you.' He said to the gods, 'When the Tathāgata was a bodhisattva in the past, wherever he was born, he was intelligent and wise. Virtuous ones! You should know that in the distant past, there was a king named Dhṛtarāṣṭra. His eldest prince was named Maitrī. The king had a chief minister named Bhava, and the minister had a son named Jyotiṣkeśa. Prince Maitrī had a friend, and that friend was also friends with six chief ministers of the kṣatriyas. When King Dhṛtarāṣṭra wanted to enter the inner palace for amusement and entertainment, he entrusted the affairs of the country to the chief minister Bhava, and then entered the palace to enjoy himself with music and the five desires. When the chief minister Bhava wanted to handle the affairs of the country, he first asked his son, and then made decisions. When giving orders, he also asked his son.'

「童子告曰：『汝乐闻者，谛听！谛受！当为汝说。』告诸天曰：『如来往昔为菩萨时，在所生处聪明多智。诸贤！当知过去久远时，世有王名曰地主，第一太子名曰慈悲。王有大臣名曰典尊，大臣有子名曰焰鬘。太子慈悲有朋友，其朋亦与六刹利大臣而为朋友。地主大王欲入深宫游戏娱乐时，即以国事委付典尊大臣，然后入宫作倡伎乐，五欲自娱。时，典尊大臣欲理国事，先问其子，然后决断；有所处分，亦问其子。』

After some time, Bhava suddenly passed away. When King Dhṛtarāṣṭra heard of his passing, he was saddened and lamented, 'Alas! How unfortunate to lose a capable minister of the country!' Prince Maitrī silently thought to himself, 'The king is distressed by the loss of Bhava. Now I should go and advise the great king not to be distressed by his death. The reason is that Bhava has a son named Jyotiṣkeśa, who is even more intelligent and wise than his father. Now he can be summoned to handle the affairs of the country.' At that time, Prince Maitrī went to the king and fully reported the above matter to his father, the king. Having heard the prince's words, he immediately summoned Jyotiṣkeśa and said to him, 'I now appoint you to replace your father's position and give you the seal of office.' At that time, Jyotiṣkeśa, having received the seal of office, when the king wanted to enter the palace, he again entrusted the affairs to him.

「其后典尊忽然命终，时地主王闻其命终，愍念哀伤，抚膺而曰：『咄哉！何辜失国良干？』太子慈悲默自念言：『王失典尊以为忧苦，今我宜往谏于大王，无以彼丧而生忧苦。所以然者？典尊有子名曰焰鬘，聪明多智乃过其父，今可征召以理国事。』时，慈悲太子即诣王所，具以上事白其父王，闻太子语已，即召焰鬘而告之曰：『吾今以汝补卿父处，授汝相印。彼时焰鬘受相印已，王欲入宫，复付后事。』

At that time, Jyotiṣkeśa was clear in governance. What his father had done, Jyotiṣkeśa also knew. What his father had not done, Jyotiṣkeśa also knew. Later, his reputation spread throughout the country, and everyone in the world called him the Great Bhava. Later, the Great Bhava had this thought: 'Now King Dhṛtarāṣṭra is already old and his remaining life is short. If the prince succeeds to the throne, it will not be difficult. I should now go and tell the six kṣatriya chief ministers, "Now King Dhṛtarāṣṭra is already old and his remaining life is short. If the prince succeeds to the throne, it will not be difficult. You should also be granted your own kingdoms. When you ascend the throne, do not forget each other."'

「时，相焰鬘明于治理，父先所为焰鬘亦知，父所不及焰鬘亦知，其后名称流闻海内，天下咸称为大典尊。时，大典尊后作是念：『今王地主年已朽迈，余寿未几，若以太子绍王位者，未为难也，我今宁可先往语彼六刹利大臣，今王地主年已朽迈，余寿未几，若以太子绍王位者，未为难也。君等亦当别封王土，居位之日，勿相忘也。』

At that time, the Great Bhava went to the six kṣatriya chief ministers and said to them, 'You should know that now King Dhṛtarāṣṭra is already old and his remaining life is short. If the prince succeeds to the throne, it will not be difficult. You should go and tell the prince this meaning. We have known each other since we were young. When you are in pain, I am in pain. When you are happy, I am happy. Now the king is old and his remaining life is short. If the prince succeeds to the throne, it will not be difficult. When you ascend the throne, you should grant us fiefs.' At that time, the six kṣatriya chief ministers, having heard his words, went to the prince and told him the above matter. The prince replied, 'If I ascend the throne and grant fiefs, to whom should I grant them?'

「时，大典尊即往诣六刹利大臣，而告之曰：『诸君！当知今王地主年已朽迈，余寿未几，若以太子绍王位者，未为难也。汝等可往白太子此意，我等与尊生小知旧，尊苦我苦，尊乐我乐。今王衰老，年已朽迈，余寿未几，今者太子绍王位者，未为难也，尊设登位，当与我封。』时，六刹利大臣闻其语已，即诣太子，说如上事。太子报言：『设吾登位，列土封国，当更与谁？』

At that time, the king suddenly passed away after a short while. The ministers of the country immediately enthroned the prince as the rightful king. After the king ascended the throne, he silently thought to himself, 'Now appointing a prime minister should follow the previous king.' He further thought, 'Who is suitable for this position? It should be the Great Bhava.' At that time, the king, out of compassion, told the Great Bhava, 'I now appoint you to the position of prime minister and give you the seal of office. You should diligently worry about and manage state affairs.' At that time, the Great Bhava, having heard the king's instruction, immediately accepted the seal of office. Whenever the king entered the palace, he would entrust the later affairs to the Great Bhava.

「时，王未久忽然而崩，国中大臣寻拜太子补王正位。王居位已，默自思念：『今立宰相，宜准先王。』复自思念：『谁堪此举？正当即任大典尊位。』时，王慈悲即告大典尊：『我今使汝即于相位，授以印信，汝当勤忧，综理国事。』时，大典尊闻王教已，即受印信，王每入宫，輙以后事付大典尊。

The Great Bhava further thought to himself, 'I should now go to the six kṣatriyas and ask them if they remember what was said before.' He then went to them and said, 'Do you now remember what was said before? Now the prince has ascended the throne and is secluded in the deep palace, enjoying the five desires. You should now go and ask the king, who is in the heavenly position, enjoying the five desires, if he still remembers what was said before?' At that time, the six kṣatriyas, having heard these words, went to the king and said to the great king, 'The king is in the heavenly position, enjoying the five desires. Does he still remember what was said before? Who should occupy the fiefs and territories?' The king said, 'I do not forget the words of the past. Who else but you should occupy the fiefs and territories?' The king further thought to himself, 'This Jambudvīpa land is wide inside and narrow outside. Who can divide it into seven parts?' He further thought to himself, 'Only the Great Bhava can divide it.' He then told him, 'You can divide this Jambudvīpa land into seven parts.'

「大典尊复自念言：『吾今宜往六刹利所，问其宁忆昔所言不？』即寻往诣语刹利曰：『汝今宁忆昔所言不？今者太子以登王位，隐处深宫，五欲自娱，汝等今者可往问王，王居天位，五欲自娱，宁复能忆昔所言不？』时，六刹利闻是语已，即诣王所，白大王言：『王居天位，五欲自娱，宁复能忆昔所言不？列土封邑，谁应居之？』王曰：『不忘昔言，列土封邑，非卿而谁？』王复自念：『此阎浮提地，内广外狭，谁能分此以为七分？』复自念言：『唯有大典尊乃能分尔。』即告之曰：『汝可分此阎浮提地，使作七分。』

At that time, the Great Bhava immediately divided it. The king's capital city, villages, counties, and states were all divided. The six kṣatriya countries were also given their shares. The king rejoiced, saying, 'My wish has been fulfilled!' At that time, the six kṣatriyas also rejoiced, saying, 'Our wish has been fulfilled. We have accomplished this deed, thanks to the power of the Great Bhava.' The six kṣatriya kings further thought to themselves, 'When our country is first established, we need ministers. Who is capable? Like the Great Bhava, we should appoint him to manage state affairs.' At that time, the six kṣatriya kings then appointed the Great Bhava and told him, 'Our country needs a prime minister. You should manage state affairs for us.' Thereupon, the six countries each gave the seal of office to the prime minister.

「时，大典尊即寻分之，王所治城，村邑郡国，皆悉部分，六刹利国亦与分部。王自庆言：『我愿已果！』时，六刹利复自庆幸：『我愿已果，得成此业，大典尊力也。』六刹利王复自思念：『吾国初建，当须宰辅，谁能堪任？如大典尊，即当使之，通领国事。』尔时，六刹利王即命典尊，而告之曰：『吾国须相，卿当为吾通领国事。』于是，六国各授相印。

At that time, the Great Bhava, having received the seal of office, and the six kings entered the palace to tour and enjoy themselves. At that time, they all entrusted state affairs to the Great Bhava. The Great Bhava managed the affairs of the seven countries, and nothing was not accomplished. At that time, there were seven great householders in the country. The Great Bhava also managed their household affairs. He was also able to instruct seven hundred brāhmaṇas in reciting the scriptures. The seven kings respected the Great Bhava as a prime minister, just like a divine being. The seven great householders regarded him as a great king, and the seven hundred brāhmaṇas regarded him as a brahmā. At that time, the seven kings, seven great householders, and seven hundred brāhmaṇas all thought to themselves, 'The Great Bhava prime minister often meets with Brahmā, speaks, sits, and rises with him, and is close to him.'

「时，大典尊受相印已，六王入宫游观娱乐，时，皆以国事付大典尊，大典尊理七国事，无不成办。时，国内有七大居士，典尊亦为处分家事，又能教授七百梵志讽诵经典，七王敬视大典尊相，犹如神明，国七居士视如大王，七百梵志视如梵天。时，七国王、七大居士、七百梵志皆自念言：『大典尊相，常与梵天相见，言语坐起亲善。』

At that time, the Great Bhava silently understood the intentions of the seven kings, householders, and brāhmaṇas, thinking, 'I always meet with Brahmā, speak, sit, and rise with him. But I have never actually seen Brahmā, spoken with him, or eaten with him. It is not right to accept this title in vain. I have also heard the elders say: "Those who dwell in seclusion and cultivate the four immeasurables for four months in the summer will be visited by Brahmā." Now, should I cultivate the four immeasurables so that Brahmā will come down and meet with me?' Thereupon, the Great Bhava went to the seven kings and said to them, 'I only wish that the great kings would attend to state affairs. I wish to cultivate the four immeasurables for four months in the summer.' The seven kings said, 'You should know the right time.' The Great Bhava prime minister also told the seven householders, 'You should each diligently attend to your own affairs. I wish to cultivate the four immeasurables for four months in the summer.' The householders said, 'Yes! You should know the right time.' He also told the seven hundred brāhmaṇas, 'You should diligently recite and teach each other. I wish to cultivate the four immeasurables for four months in the summer.' The brāhmaṇas said, 'Yes! Now, Master, you should know the right time.'

「时，大典尊默识七王、居士、梵志意，谓：『我常与梵天相见，言语坐起；然我实不见梵天，不与言语，不可餐默，虚受此称。我亦曾闻诸先宿言：「于夏四月闲居静处，修四无量者，梵天则下，与共相见。」今我宁可修四无量，使梵天下，共相见不？』于是，典尊至七王所而白王言：『唯愿大王顾临国事，我欲于夏四月修四无量。』七王告曰：『宜知是时。』大典尊相又告七居士：『汝等各勤己务，吾欲夏四月修四无量。』居士曰：『诺！宜知是时。』又告七百梵志：『卿等当勤讽诵，转相教授，我欲于夏四月修四无量。』梵志曰：『诺！今者大师宜知是时。』

At that time, the Great Bhava built a quiet room to the east of the city. He stayed there for four months in the summer to cultivate the four immeasurables. However, Brahmā still did not come down. The Great Bhava thought to himself, 'I have heard the elders say that those who cultivate the four immeasurables for four months in the summer will be visited by Brahmā. Now it is quiet and there is no sign of it.' At that time, on the full moon day of the fifteenth, the Great Bhava came out of his quiet room and sat in the open air. Not long after sitting, a great light appeared. The Great Bhava silently thought, 'This strange light, will it be a sign that Brahmā is about to descend?'

「时，大典尊于彼城东造闲静室，于夏四月，即于彼止，修四无量，然彼梵天犹不来下，典尊自念：『我闻先宿旧言，于夏四月，修四无量，梵天下现。今者寂然，聊无髣髴。』时，大典尊以十五日月满时，出其静室，于露地坐，坐未久顷，有大光现，典尊默念：『今此异光，将无是梵欲下瑞耶？』

At that time, Brahmā Heaven immediately transformed into a youth with a five-pointed topknot, sitting in the air above the Great Bhava. Having seen this, the Great Bhava spoke a verse, saying:

「时，梵天王即化为童子，头] cf.【丽】五角髻，在典尊上虚空中坐，典尊见已，即说颂曰：

What kind of heavenly being is this, sitting in the sky
illuminating the four directions like a great burning pile of firewood?

「『此是何天像？　　在于虚空中，光照于四方，　　如大火［卄／积］燃』。

At that time, the Brahmā youth replied in verse:

「时，梵童子以偈报曰：

Only the gods of the Brahmā world know me as
a Brahmā youth. Others call me a great divine worshipper.

「『唯梵世诸天，　　知我梵童子，其余人谓我，　　祀祠于大神。』

At that time, the great recorder replied in verse:

「时，大典尊以偈报曰：

Now I shall consult and follow your instructions with respect. I
will prepare various exquisite flavors. May the gods know my mind.

「『今我当咨承，　　奉诲致恭敬，设种种上味，　　愿天知我心。』

At that time, the Brahma youth replied in verse:

「时，梵童子以偈报曰：

O Venerable One, what do you seek to accomplish by
your practice? Now I shall accept this offering for you.

「『典尊汝所修，　　为欲何志求？今设此供养，　　当为汝受之。』

He also said to the Great Venerable: "If you have any questions, feel free to ask them, and I shall explain them for you." At that time, the Great Venerable thought to himself: "Should I ask about present matters or future matters?" He further thought: "What is the use of asking about present matters? I shall ask about the profound matters of the future." He then addressed the Brahma youth in verse, asking:

「又告大典尊：『汝若有所问，自恣问之，当为汝说。』时，大典尊即自念言：『我今当问现在事耶？问未然事耶？』复自念言：『今世现事，用复问为？当问未然幽冥之事。』即向梵童子以偈问曰：

Now I ask the Brahma youth, who can resolve doubts and uncertainties. What
do you study and abide by to be reborn in the Brahma Heaven?

「『今我问梵童，　　能决疑无疑，学何住何法，　　得生于梵天？』

At that time, the Brahma youth replied in verse:

「时，梵童子以偈报曰：

Abandon the notion of self and others, dwell alone and cultivate loving-kindness. Remove
desire, be free from defilement, and you will be reborn in the Brahma Heaven.

「『当舍我人想，　　独处修慈心，除欲无臭秽，　　乃得生梵天。』

At that time, the great recorder, having heard these verses, thought to himself: "The Brahma youth's verses suggest removing defilement. I do not understand this. I should ask again." The great recorder then asked in verse:

「时，大典尊闻是偈已，即自念言：『梵童子说偈，宜除臭秽，我不解此，今宜更问。』时，大典尊即以偈问曰：

The Brahma verses speak of defilement. Please explain it to me now. Who opens
the door to the world, falling into evil and not being reborn in heaven?

「『梵偈言臭秽，　　愿今为我说，谁开世间门，　　堕恶不生天？』

At that time, the Brahma youth replied in verse:

「时，梵童子以偈报曰：

Deceit, jealousy, arrogance, and conceit, greed, anger, and delusion, indulging in
the mind and concealing it. This is the defilement of the
world. Now I tell you to know it. This opens the
door to the world, falling into evil and not being reborn in heaven.

「『欺妄怀嫉妬，　　习慢增上慢，贪欲瞋恚痴，　　自恣藏于心。此世间臭秽，　　今说令汝知，此开世间门，　　堕恶不生天。』

At that time, the great recorder, having heard this verse, further thought to himself: 'The meaning of defilement spoken by the Brahma youth I have now understood. But for those at home, there is no way to eliminate it. Now should I abandon the world, go forth, shave my beard and hair, wear the Dharma robes, and cultivate the path?'

「时，大典尊闻此偈已，复自念言：『梵童子所说臭秽之义我今已解，但在家者无由得除，今我宁可舍世出家，剃除须发，法服修道耶！』

At that time, the Brahma youth knew his intention and spoke in verse:

「时，梵童子知其志念，以偈告曰：

You are able to be courageous, this intention is excellent and wonderful; what
the wise do, upon death they will surely be born in the Brahma Heaven.

「『汝能有勇猛，　　此志为胜妙；智者之所为，　　死必生梵天。』

Then the Brahma youth suddenly disappeared.

「于是，梵童子忽然不现。

Then the great minister returned to the seven kings and said, 'Great kings! Please rule the country well with your divine wisdom. Now I wish to leave the world and practice the path in the Dharma robes. Why is this? I personally heard the Brahma youth speak of filth, and my mind is greatly disgusted by it. If I were to stay at home, there would be no way to eliminate it.' At that time, the seven kings thought to themselves, 'Brahmins generally crave wealth and treasures. I should now open the treasury widely and give him whatever he needs, so that he will not leave the household.' The seven kings then summoned the great minister and said to him, 'If there is anything you need, I will give it all to you. You should not leave the household.' The great minister then said to the king, 'Now that I have received the king's gifts, I also have a great deal of wealth and treasures. I will leave them all to the great king. Please allow me to leave the household and fulfill my aspiration.

「时，大典尊还诣七王白言：『大王！唯愿垂神善理国事，今我意欲出家离世，法服修道。所以者何？我亲于梵童子闻说臭秽，心甚恶之。若在家者，无由得除。』彼时，七王即自念言：『凡婆罗门多贪财宝，我今宁可大开库藏，恣其所须，使不出家。』时，七国王即命典尊，而告之曰：『设有所须，吾尽相与，不足出家。』时，大典尊寻白王曰：『我今以为蒙王赐已，我亦大有财宝，今者尽留以上大王，愿听出家，遂我志愿。』

At that time, the seven kings again thought, 'Brahmins generally crave beautiful women. I should now release the palace concubines to fulfill his wishes, so that he will not leave the household.' The king then summoned the great minister and said to him, 'If you need concubines, I will give them all to you. You should not leave the household.' The great minister replied, 'Now that I have received the king's gifts, there are already many concubines in my household. I will release them all, seeking to leave affection and practice the path. Why is this? I personally heard the Brahma youth speak of filth, and my mind is greatly disgusted by it. If I were to stay at home, there would be no way to eliminate it.'

「时，七国王复作是念：『凡婆罗门多贪美色，今我宁可出宫婇女，以满其意，使不出家。』王即命典尊而告之曰：『若须婇女，吾尽与汝，不足出家。』典尊报曰：『我今已为蒙王赐已，家内自有婇女众多，今尽放遣，求离恩爱，出家修道。所以然者？我亲从梵童子闻说臭秽，心甚恶之。若在家者，无由得除。』

Then the great minister addressed King Compassionate with a verse, saying:

「时，大典尊向慈悲王，以偈颂曰：

May the king listen to my words. The king is the most honored
among men. Granting wealth, treasures, and concubines, these treasures are not what I desire.

「『王当听我言，　　王为人中尊，赐财宝婇女，　　此宝非所乐。』

Then King Compassionate replied in verse:

「时，慈悲王以偈报曰：

The city of Dantaka, the city of A婆布和, the city of A槃大天, the
city of Anga, the city of Campa, the city of Sumitra, the city
of Sata, the city of Xita, the city of Lou, the city of
Bhara, the city of Kasi, all built by you, the honored one. If
there is anything lacking in the five desires, I will give it all
to you. It is appropriate to govern the country together. There is no
need to leave home.

「『檀特伽陵城，　　阿婆布和城，阿槃大天城，　　鸯伽瞻婆城，数弥萨罗城，　　西陀路楼城，婆罗伽尸城，　　尽汝典尊造，五欲有所少，　　吾尽当相与；宜共理国事，　　不足出家去。』

Then the great minister replied in verse:

「时，大典尊以偈报曰：

I have no lack of the five desires, and I do not enjoy the
world. Having heard the words of the gods, I have no intention of returning home.

「『我五欲不少，　　自不乐世间；已闻天所语，　　无心复在家。』

Then King Compassionate replied in verse:

「时，慈悲王以偈报曰：

What great sage said this? From which god did you hear
it? Abandoning the five desires, I now ask you to answer me.

「『大典尊所言，　　为从何天闻，舍离于五欲，　　今问当答我。』

Then the great sage replied in verse:

「时，大典尊以偈答曰：

In the past, when I was alone in a quiet
place, contemplating, the Brahma King came and emitted great light
I heard it from him and became dispassionate towards the world.

「『昔我于静处，　　独坐自思惟；时梵天王来，　　普放大光明；我从彼闻已，　　不乐于世间。』

Then King Compassionate replied in verse:

「时，慈悲王以偈告曰：

Please stay a while, great teacher, and together promote the
Dharma. Then we will both leave home, and you will
be my teacher. Just as the sky is filled with
pure lapis lazuli, now my pure faith fills the Buddha's teachings.

「『小住大典尊，　　共弘善法化；然后俱出家，　　汝即为我师。譬如虚空中，　　清净琉璃满；今我清净信，　　充徧佛法中。』

Then the great teacher also spoke in verse:

「时，大典尊复作颂曰：

All gods and worldly people should abandon the five desires
eliminate all defilements, and purify the practice of the Brahma-way.

「『诸天及世人，　　皆应舍五欲，蠲除诸秽污，　　净修于梵行。』

At that time, the seven kings said to the great teacher: 'You can stay for seven years, enjoy the five desires of the world together, and then abandon the country, entrust it to your children, and leave the household life together. Wouldn't that be good? We will also do the same as you.' At that time, the great teacher replied to the seven kings: 'The world is impermanent, human life passes quickly, and even a breath is difficult to guarantee. How far is it to seven years?' The seven kings said again: 'If seven years is far, then six years, five years, and even one year. Stay in the quiet palace, enjoy the five desires of the world together, and then abandon the country, entrust it to your children, and leave the household life together. Wouldn't that be good? We will also do the same as you.' At that time, the great teacher again replied to the king: 'The world is impermanent, human life passes quickly, and even a breath is difficult to guarantee. How far is it to even one year? Even seven months, or one month, is still too long.' The king said again: 'Stay in the deep palace for seven days, enjoy the five desires of the world together, and then abandon the country, entrust it to your children, and leave the household life together. Wouldn't that be good?' The great teacher replied: 'Seven days is not far, I can stay that long. I only hope that the great king will not go back on his word. After seven days, if the king does not leave, I will leave the household life myself.'

「尔时，七国王语大典尊曰：『汝可留住七岁之中，极世五欲，共相娱乐，然后舍国，各付子弟，俱共出家，不亦善耶？如汝所获，我亦当同。』时，大典尊报七王曰：『世间无常，人命逝速，喘息之间，犹亦难保，乃至七岁，不亦远耶？』七王又言：『七岁远者，六岁、五岁，乃至一岁，留住静宫，极世五欲，共相娱乐，然后舍国，各付子弟，俱共出家，不亦善耶？如汝所得，我亦宜同。』时，大典尊复报王曰：『此世间无常，人命逝速，喘息之间，犹亦难保，乃至一岁尚亦久尔，如是七月，至于一月，犹复不可。』王又语言：『可至七日，留住深宫，极世五欲，共相娱乐，然后舍国，各付子弟，俱共出家，不亦善耶？』大典尊答曰：『七日不远，自可留尔，唯愿大王勿违信誓，过七日已，王若不去，我自出家。』

At that time, the great teacher went to the seven householders and said: 'You should each manage your own affairs. I wish to leave the household life to cultivate the unconditioned path. The reason is that I personally heard from Brahma Heaven about the foul stench, and my mind is greatly disgusted by it. If I stay at home, there is no way to eliminate it.' At that time, the seven householders replied to the great teacher: 'Excellent! This aspiration! It is time to know. We also wish to leave the household life together. We will do the same as you.'

「时，大典尊又至七居士所语言：『汝等各理己务，吾欲出家，修无为道。所以然者？我亲从梵天闻说臭秽，心甚恶之。若在家者，无由得除。』时，七居士报典尊曰：『善哉！斯志！宜知是时，我等亦欲俱共出家，如汝所得，我亦宜同。』

At that time, the great teacher again went to the seven hundred Brahmins and said to them: 'You should diligently recite and extensively explore the meaning of the path, teaching each other. I wish to leave the household life to cultivate the unconditioned path. The reason is that I personally heard from Brahma Heaven about the foul stench, and my mind is greatly disgusted by it. If I stay at home, there is no way to eliminate it.' At that time, the seven hundred Brahmins said to the great teacher: 'Master! Do not leave the household life. The householder's life is comfortable, with the five desires for self-entertainment, with many attendants, and no worries or suffering. The one who leaves the household life is alone in the wilderness, with nothing desired, nothing to be greedy for.' The great teacher replied: 'If I consider the householder's life to be happiness and leaving the household life to be suffering, I will never leave the household life; if I consider the householder's life to be suffering and leaving the household life to be happiness, then I leave the household life.' The Brahmins replied: 'If the Master leaves the household life, we will also leave the household life; whatever the Master practices, we will all practice.'

「时，大典尊复诣七百梵志所，而告之曰：『卿等当勤讽诵，广探道义，转相教授，吾欲出家修无为道。所以然者？我亲从梵天闻说臭秽，心甚恶之，若在家者，无由得除。』时，七百梵志白典尊曰：『大师！勿出家也。夫在家安乐，五欲自娱，多人侍从，心无忧苦。出家之人独在空野，所欲悉无，无可贪取。』典尊报曰：『吾若以在家为乐，出家为苦，终不出家；吾以在家为苦，出家为乐，故出家尔。』梵志答曰：『大师出家，我亦出家；大师所行，我亦尽当行。』

At that time, the great teacher went to his wives and said to them: 'You should stay as you wish, or return as you wish. I wish to leave the household life to seek the unconditioned path. I will explain the above matters in detail to express my intention to leave the household life.' At that time, the wives replied: 'The great teacher is like our husband, like our father. If he now leaves the household life, we will also follow him. Whatever the great teacher practices, we should also practice.'

「时，大典尊至诸妻所，而告之曰：『卿等随宜欲住者住，欲归者归，吾欲出家，求无为道，具论上事，明出家意。』时，诸妇答曰：『大典尊在，一如我夫，一如我父，设今出家，亦当随从，典尊所行，我亦宜行。』

After seven days, the great teacher then shaved his beard and hair, put on the three Dharma robes, and left the household life. At that time, the seven kings of the seven countries, the seven great householders, the seven hundred Brahmins, and the forty wives, in this way, there were eighty-four thousand people who simultaneously left the household life and followed the great teacher. At that time, the great teacher traveled to various countries with the great assembly, widely promoting the Dharma and benefiting many.

「过七日已，时，大典尊即剃除须发，服三法衣，舍家而去。时，七国王、七大居士、七百梵志及四十夫人，如是展转，有八万四千人同时出家，从大典尊。时，大典尊与诸大众游行诸国，广弘道化，多所饶益。

At that time, the Brahma King told the heavenly beings: 'The great teacher minister at that time, was he not someone else? Do not have such a view. The present Śākyamuni Buddha is his body. The World-Honored One, after seven days, left the household life to cultivate the path, leading the great assembly, traveling to various countries, widely promoting the Dharma and benefiting many. If you have any remaining doubts about my words, the World-Honored One is now on Vulture Peak. You may go and ask. As the Buddha says, you should uphold it.'

「尔时，梵王告诸天众曰：『时，典尊大臣岂异人乎？莫造斯观，今释迦文佛即其身也。世尊尔时过七日已，出家修道，将诸大众，游行诸国，广弘道化，多所饶益。汝等若于我言有余疑者，世尊今在耆阇崛山，可往问也，如佛所言，当受持之。』」

Panthaka said: "I came here for this reason. Yes, World-Honored One! Is the great teacher the World-Honored One? After seven days, did the World-Honored One leave the household life to cultivate the path, together with the seven kings of the seven countries, and eighty-four thousand people, simultaneously leaving the household life, traveling to various countries, widely promoting the Dharma and benefiting many?"

般遮翼言：「我以是缘，故来诣此。唯然，世尊！彼大典尊即世尊是耶？世尊尔时过七日已，出家修道，与七国王乃至八万四千人同时出家，游行诸国，广弘道化，多所饶益耶？」

The Buddha told Panthaka: "Is the great teacher someone else at that time? Do not have such a view. It is my body. At that time, the men and women of the whole country, when walking or moving, if something was damaged, all raised their voices and said: 'Homage to the great teacher, the great appearance of the seven kings! Homage to the great teacher, the great appearance of the seven kings!' This was repeated three times. Panthaka! At that time, the great teacher had great virtue and power, but he was unable to teach his disciples the ultimate path, unable to make them attain the ultimate pure practice, unable to lead them to the place of peace and tranquility. The Dharma he taught, when the disciples practiced and followed it, after their bodies perished and their lives ended, they were reborn in the Brahma Heaven; those with shallower practice were reborn in the Heaven of Others' Emanations; next, in the Heaven of Emanations, the Heaven of Satisfaction, the Heaven of Brightness, the Heaven of the Thirty-three Gods, the Four Heavenly Kings, the kṣatriyas, brāhmaṇas, and wealthy householders, with freedom in what they desired.

佛告般遮翼曰：「尔时大典尊岂异人乎？莫造斯观，即我身是也。尔时，举国男女行来举动，有所破损，皆寻举声曰：『南无大典尊七王大相！南无大典尊七王大相！』如是至三。般遮翼！时，大典尊有大德力，然不能为弟子说究竟道，不能使得究竟梵行，不能使至安隐之处。其所说法，弟子受行，身坏命终，得生梵天；其次，行浅者生他化自在天；次生化自在天、兜率陀天、焰天、忉利天、四天王、刹利、婆罗门、居士大家，所欲自在。

"Panthaka! The disciples of the great teacher all left the household life without doubt, with results, and with instructions, but it was not the ultimate path, unable to make them attain the ultimate pure practice, unable to lead them to the place of peace and tranquility. The best path among them only reached the Brahma Heaven. Now I teach the Dharma for my disciples, enabling them to attain the ultimate path, the ultimate pure practice, the ultimate peace and tranquility, and ultimately return to nirvana. The disciples who practice and follow my teachings abandon the defiled and accomplish the undefiled, with liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom. In this present life, they personally realize: birth and death are exhausted, the pure practice is established, what is to be done is accomplished, and they will not accept existence again. Next, those with shallower practice sever the five lower fetters and attain parinirvāṇa in the heavens, not returning here again. Next, those who have exhausted the three fetters and have weakened desire, anger, and delusion, come to the world once more and attain parinirvāṇa; next, those who have severed the three fetters become srota-āpannas, do not fall into evil paths, go back and forth seven times at most, and will definitely attain parinirvāṇa. Panthaka! My disciples leave the household life without doubt, with results, with instructions, with the ultimate path, the ultimate pure practice, the ultimate peace and tranquility, and ultimately attain extinction."

「般遮翼！彼大典尊弟子，皆无疑出家，有果报，有教诫，然非究竟道，不能使得究竟梵行，不能使至安隐之处。其道胜者，极至梵天尔。今我为弟子说法，则能使其得究竟道、究竟梵行、究竟安隐，终归涅槃。我所说法弟子受行者，舍有漏成无漏，心解脱、慧解脱。于现法中，自身作证：生死已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，更不受有。其次，行浅者断五下结，即于天上而般涅槃，不复还此。其次，三结尽，薄婬、怒、痴，一来世间而般涅槃；其次，断三结，得须陀洹，不堕恶道，极七往返，必得涅槃。般遮翼！我诸弟子不疑出家，有果报，有教诫，究竟道法，究竟梵行，究竟安隐，终归灭度。」

At that time, Panthaka heard what the Buddha said and joyfully accepted it.

尔时，般遮翼闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
