DĀ 5

Dīrghāgama 长阿含经

Chapter 2 第二分

5 （五）初小缘经

Parallel to: DN 27 / MĀ 154

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was at the Deer Mother's Lecture Hall in the Pure Faith Garden in the country of Shravasti, together with a great assembly of 1,250 bhikṣus.

一时，佛在舍卫国清信园林鹿母讲堂，与大比丘众千二百五十人俱。

At that time, there were two brahmins who went to where the Buddha was with firm faith, left home to practice the path, one named Vashita and the other Varadatta. At that time, the World-Honored One came out of the quiet room and was walking back and forth on the lecture hall. Vashita saw the Buddha walking back and forth, and immediately quickly went to Varadatta and said, "Do you know? The Tathāgata has now come out of the quiet room and is walking back and forth on the hall. We should go together to where the World-Honored One is. Perhaps we will hear the Tathāgata speak." Varadatta, having heard his words, immediately went together to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at his feet, and followed the Buddha in walking back and forth.

尔时，有二婆罗门以坚固信往诣佛所，出家为道，一名婆悉咤，二名婆罗堕。尔时，世尊于静室出，在讲堂上彷徉经行。时，婆悉咤见佛经行，即寻速疾诣婆罗堕，而语之言：「汝知不耶？如来今者出于静室，堂上经行，我等可共诣世尊所，傥闻如来有所言说。」时，婆罗堕闻其语已，即共诣世尊所，头面礼足，随佛经行。

At that time, the World-Honored One said to Vashita, "You two have left the brahmin caste and practiced the path in my Dharma out of firm faith, is that correct?"

尔时，世尊告婆悉咤曰：「汝等二人出婆罗门种，以信坚固于我法中出家修道耶？」

He replied, "Yes!"

答曰：「如是！」

The Buddha said, "Brahmin! Now that you have left home to practice the path in my Dharma, do the brahmins not reproach you?"

佛言：「婆罗门！今在我法中出家为道，诸婆罗门得无嫌责汝耶？」

He replied, "Yes! I have received the great kindness of the Buddha in leaving home to cultivate the path. Indeed, I am seen as objectionable by those brahmins."

答曰：「唯然！蒙佛大恩，出家修道，实自为彼诸婆罗门所见嫌责。」

The Buddha said, "What things do they find objectionable about you?"

佛言：「彼以何事而嫌责汝？」

He then said to the Buddha, "They say, 'Our brahmin caste is the most supreme, the others are inferior. Our caste is pure, the others are dark. Our brahmin caste is born from Brahma, born from Brahma's mouth, and in this present life attains pure understanding, and in the future is also pure. Why do you abandon your pure caste and enter that different Dharma of Gautama?' World Honored One! They see me leaving home to cultivate the path in the Buddha's Dharma and rebuke me with such words."

寻白佛言：「彼言：『我婆罗门种最为第一，余者卑劣；我种清白，余者黑冥；我婆罗门种出自梵天，从梵口生，于现法中得清净解，后亦清净；汝等何故舍清净种，入彼瞿昙异法中耶？』世尊！彼见我于佛法中出家修道，以如此言而呵责我。」

The Buddha said to Vasistha, "You see, these people are ignorant and foolish, like animals. They falsely claim, 'The brahmin caste is the most supreme, the others are inferior. Our caste is pure, the others are dark. Our brahmin caste is born from Brahma, born from Brahma's mouth, and in this present life attains purity, and in the future is also pure.' Vasistha! In my unsurpassed true path, I do not need caste, I do not rely on the arrogant mind of 'I'. The worldly Dharma needs this, but my Dharma is not like that. If there are śramaṇas or brahmins who rely on their caste and have an arrogant mind, they will never attain the unsurpassed realization in my Dharma. If they can abandon their caste and remove their arrogant mind, they will be able to attain the realization of the Way in my Dharma and be worthy of receiving the true Dharma. The evil and inferior people, my Dharma is not like that."

佛告婆悉咤：「汝观诸人愚冥无识犹如禽兽，虚假自称：『婆罗门种最为第一，余者卑劣；我种清白，余者黑冥；我婆罗门种出自梵天，从梵口生，现得清净，后亦清净。』婆悉咤！今我无上正真道中不须种姓，不恃吾我憍慢之心，俗法须此，我法不尔。若有沙门、婆罗门，自恃种姓，怀憍慢心，于我法中终不得成无上证也。若能舍离种姓，除憍慢心，则于我法中得成道证，堪受正法。人恶下流，我法不尔。」

The Buddha said to Vasistha, "There are four castes, in which good and evil dwell. The wise praise them, and the wise blame them. What are the four? First, the kṣatriya caste, second, the brahmin caste, third, the householder caste, and fourth, the śūdra caste. Vasistha! Listen, among the kṣatriya caste there are those who kill, steal, engage in sexual misconduct, lie, slander, speak harshly, engage in idle talk, are stingy, jealous, and have wrong views; the brahmin caste, householder caste, and śūdra caste are also like this, mixed with the ten unwholesome actions. Vasistha! Unwholesome actions have unwholesome results, and dark actions have dark results. If these results were only in the kṣatriya, householder, and śūdra castes, and not in the brahmin caste, then the brahmin caste would be able to say, 'Our brahmin caste is the most supreme, the others are inferior. Our caste is pure, the others are dark. Our brahmin caste is born from Brahma, born from Brahma's mouth, and in this present life attains purity, and in the future is also pure.' If unwholesome actions have unwholesome results, and dark actions have dark results, and they must be in the brahmin, kṣatriya, householder, and śūdra castes, then the brahmins cannot claim alone, 'Our caste is pure and most supreme.'

佛告婆悉咤：「有四姓种，善恶居之，智者所举，智者所责。何谓为四？一者刹利种，二者婆罗门种，三者居士种，四者首陀罗种。婆悉咤！汝听刹利种中有杀生者，有盗窃者，有婬乱者，有欺妄者，有两舌者，有恶口者，有绮语者，有悭贪者，有嫉妬者，有邪见者；婆罗门种、居士种、首陀罗种亦皆如是，杂十恶行。婆悉咤！夫不善行有不善报，为黑冥行则有黑冥报，若使此报独在刹利、居士、首陀罗种，不在婆罗门种者，则婆罗门种应得自言：『我婆罗门种最为第一，余者卑劣；我种清白，余者黑冥；我婆罗门种出自梵天，从梵口生，现得清净，后亦清净。』若使行不善行有不善报，为黑冥行有黑冥报，必在婆罗门种、刹利、居士、首陀罗种者，则婆罗门不得独称：『我种清净，最为第一。』

"Vasistha! If among the kṣatriya caste there are those who do not kill, do not steal, do not engage in sexual misconduct, do not lie, do not slander, do not speak harshly, do not engage in idle talk, are not stingy, jealous, or have wrong views; the brahmin caste, householder caste, and śūdra caste are also like this, practicing the ten wholesome actions together. The practice of wholesome dharmas must have wholesome results, and the practice of pure dharmas must have pure results. If these results were only in the brahmin caste, and not in the kṣatriya, householder, and śūdra castes, then the brahmin caste would be able to say, 'Our caste is pure and most supreme.' If the four castes all have these results, then the brahmins cannot claim alone, 'Our caste is pure and most supreme.'"

「婆悉咤！若刹利种中有不杀者，有不盗、不婬、不妄语、不两舌、不恶口、不绮语、不悭贪、不嫉妬、不邪见；婆罗门种、居士、首陀罗种亦皆如是，同修十善。夫行善法必有善报，行清白行必有白报，若使此报独在婆罗门，不在刹利、居士、首陀罗者，则婆罗门种应得自言：『我种清净，最为第一。』若使四姓同有此报者，则婆罗门不得独称：『我种清净，最为第一。』」

The Buddha said to Vasistha, "Now we can see that the brahmin caste marries and gives birth, no different from the world, yet they falsely claim, 'We are of Brahma's caste, born from Brahma's mouth, attaining purity in this life, and also in the future.' Vasistha! You should know that now my disciples, of different castes and origins, have left home to practice the path in my Dharma. If someone asks, 'What is your caste?' you should answer, 'I am a śramaṇa of the Śākya caste.' You can also claim, 'I am of the brahmin caste, born from the mouth, born from the Dharma, attaining purity in this life, and also in the future.' Why is this? The name of Mahābrahmā is the title of the Tathāgata. The Tathāgata is the eye of the world, the Dharma is the wisdom of the world, the Dharma of the world, the Brahma of the world, the Dharma wheel of the world, the ambrosia of the world, the lord of the Dharma of the world.

佛告婆悉咤：「今者现见婆罗门种，嫁娶产生，与世无异，而作诈称：『我是梵种，从梵口生，现得清净，后亦清净。』婆悉咤！汝今当知，今我弟子，种姓不同，所出各异，于我法中出家修道，若有人问：『汝谁种姓？』当答彼言：『我是沙门释种子也。』亦可自称：『我是婆罗门种，亲从口生，从法化生，现得清净，后亦清净。』所以者何？大梵名者即如来号，如来为世间眼，法为世间智，为世间法，为世间梵，为世间法轮，为世间甘露，为世间法主。

"Vasistha! If among the kṣatriya caste there are those who have faith in the Buddha, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, endowed with the ten titles, who have faith in the Dharma, who have faith in the Tathāgata's Dharma, which is subtle and pure, can be practiced in the present, is taught without time, shows the essential path to nirvāṇa, is known by the wise, and is not within the reach of ordinary fools; who have faith in the Saṃgha, whose nature is good and straightforward, who have accomplished the fruits of the path, who have accomplished the retinue, who are true disciples of the Buddha, who have accomplished the Dharma and the Dharma-accomplishments; namely, the Saṃgha, which has accomplished the Saṃgha of precepts, the Saṃgha of concentration, the Saṃgha of wisdom, the Saṃgha of liberation, and the Saṃgha of liberation and insight. Those who are heading towards the stream-enterer, who have attained the stream-enterer; those who are heading towards the once-returner, who have attained the once-returner; those who are heading towards the non-returner, who have attained the non-returner; those who are heading towards the arhat, who have attained the arhat; the four pairs and eight individuals - these are the Saṃgha of the Tathāgata's disciples, who are worthy of respect and veneration, who are the field of merit for the world, who should receive offerings from others; who have faith in the precepts, who are fully endowed with the holy precepts, without any gaps or flaws, without any stains, praised by the wise, fully endowed with peace and tranquility. Vasistha! The brahmin caste, householder caste, and śūdra caste should also have such faith in the Buddha, faith in the Dharma, and faith in the Saṃgha, and be fully endowed with the holy precepts. Vasistha! There are also among the kṣatriya caste those who make offerings to arhats, who pay respect and make obeisance to them; the brahmins, householders, and śūdras also all make offerings to arhats, and pay respect and make obeisance to them."

「婆悉咤！若刹利种中有笃信于佛、如来、至真、等正觉，十号具足，笃信于法，信如来法，微妙清净，现可修行，说无时节，示泥洹要，智者所知，非是凡愚所能及教；笃信于僧，性善质直，道果成就，眷属成就，佛真弟子法法成就；所谓众者，戒众成就，定众、慧众、解脱众、解脱知见众成就。向须陀洹、得须陀洹；向斯陀含，得斯陀含；向阿那含，得阿那含；向阿罗汉，得阿罗汉；四双八辈，是为如来弟子众也。可敬可尊，为世福田，应受人供；笃信于戒，圣戒具足，无有缺漏，无诸瑕隙，亦无点污，智者所称，具足善寂。婆悉咤！诸婆罗门种、居士、首陀罗种亦应如是笃信于佛、信法、信众，成就圣戒。婆悉咤！刹利种中亦有供养罗汉，恭敬礼拜者；婆罗门、居士、首陀罗亦皆供养罗汉，恭敬礼拜。」

The Buddha said to Vasistha, "Now my relatives, the Śākya clan, also serve and pay respect to King Prasenajit, and King Prasenajit also comes to make offerings and pay respect to me. They do not think, 'The śramaṇa Gautama comes from a noble clan, while our clan is lowly; the śramaṇa Gautama comes from a family of great wealth and great prestige, while we are born into a humble, poor, and lowly family. Therefore, we make offerings and pay respect to the Tathāgata.' King Prasenajit observes the Dharma and discerns the true and false. Therefore, he gives rise to pure faith and pays respect to the Tathāgata.

佛告婆悉咤：「今我亲族释种亦奉波斯匿王，宗事礼敬，波斯匿王复来供养礼敬于我，彼不念言：『沙门瞿昙出于豪族，我姓卑下；沙门瞿昙出大财富、大威德家，我生下穷鄙陋小家故，致供养礼敬如来也。』波斯匿王于法观法，明识真伪，故生净信，致敬如来耳。

Vasistha! Now I will tell you the origin of the four castes. When the world was first formed, at the end of a kalpa, when it was destroyed, all living beings ended their lives and were reborn in the Ābhāsvara Heaven, born spontaneously, subsisting on thought, self-illuminated by their own light, flying through the air with supernatural powers. At that time, this earth completely turned into water, covering everywhere. At that time, there were no sun, moon, stars, or day and night, no months or years. There was only great darkness. Later, this water turned into solid ground, and the Ābhāsvara gods, whose merits and life spans were exhausted, were reborn here. Although reborn here, they still subsisted on thought, flew through the air with supernatural powers, and were self-illuminated. After dwelling here for a long time, they each called out, 'Beings! Beings!' Later, sweet springs gushed forth, resembling butter and honey. The gods who were born here first, being of a light and easy nature, saw these springs and thought to themselves, 'What is this? Let us try it.' They dipped their fingers into the springs and tasted them, doing so again and again, finding them increasingly delicious. They then scooped up the water with their hands and ate to their fill. Enjoying it so much, they became insatiable. The other beings followed suit and ate it, doing so again and again, also finding it increasingly delicious. As they ate it endlessly, their bodies became coarser, their flesh and muscles hardened, they lost their heavenly beauty, and they no longer had supernatural powers. They walked on the ground, and their bodies lost their light, and the world became dark.

「婆悉咤！今当为汝说四姓本缘。天地始终，劫尽坏时，众生命终皆生光音天，自然化生，以念为食，光明自照，神足飞空，其后此地尽变为水，无不周遍。当于尔时，无复日月星辰，亦无昼夜年月岁数，唯有大冥。其后此水变成大地，光音诸天福尽命终，来生此间。虽来生此，犹以念食，神足飞空，身光自照，于此住久，各自称言：『众生！众生！』其后此地甘泉涌出，状如酥蜜。彼初来天性轻易者，见此泉已，默自念言：『此为何物？可试甞之。』即内指泉中，而试甞之，如是再三，转觉其美，便以手抄自恣食之，如是乐著，遂无厌足，其余众生复效食之，如是再三，复觉其美，食之不已，其身转麤，肌肉坚［革＊卬］，失天妙色，无复神足，履地而行，身光转灭，天地大冥。

Vasistha! You should know that it is the constant law of heaven and earth that after great darkness, the sun, moon, and stars will appear in the sky, and then there will be day and night, the waxing and waning of the moon, and the passage of years. At that time, beings only ate the earth's essence. Living in the world for a long time, those who ate a lot had coarse and ugly complexions, while those who ate little still had pleasant and lustrous complexions. This is where beauty and ugliness first arose. The beautiful ones gave rise to arrogant minds, despising the ugly ones. The ugly ones gave rise to jealous and evil minds, hating the beautiful ones. The beings then quarreled with each other. At that time, the sweet springs naturally dried up. Later, natural earth fat grew on this ground, with color, taste, and fragrance, edible and fragrant. At that time, beings again took it to eat, living in the world for a long time. Those who ate a lot had coarse and ugly complexions, while those who ate little still had pleasant and lustrous complexions. The beautiful ones gave rise to arrogant minds, despising the ugly ones. The ugly ones gave rise to jealous and evil minds, hating the beautiful ones. The beings then quarreled with each other. At that time, the earth fat no longer grew.

「婆悉咤！当知天地常法，大冥之后，必有日月星像现于虚空，然后方有昼夜晦明、日月岁数。尔时，众生但食地味，久住世间，其食多者，颜色麤丑；其食少者，色犹悦泽，好丑端正，于是始有；其端正者，生憍慢心，轻丑陋者；其丑陋者，生嫉恶心，憎端正者。众生于是各共忿诤，是时甘泉自然枯涸，其后此地生自然地肥，色味具足，香洁可食，是时众生复取食之，久住世间。其食多者，颜色麤丑；其食少者，色犹悦泽。其端正者，生憍慢心，轻丑陋者；其丑陋者，生嫉恶心，憎端正者，众生于是各共诤讼，是时地肥遂不复生。

Later, coarse and thick earth fat grew on this ground, also fragrant and delicious, but not as good as before. At that time, beings again took it to eat, living in the world for a long time. Those who ate a lot had increasingly coarse and ugly complexions, while those who ate little still had pleasant and lustrous complexions. The beautiful and ugly ones argued with each other, and the earth fat then no longer grew. Later, natural glutinous rice grew on this ground, without husks, with color, taste, and fragrance, edible and fragrant. At that time, beings again took it to eat, living in the world for a long time. Then men and women looked at each other, gradually giving rise to desire, and they became intimate with each other. The other beings saw this and said, 'What you are doing is wrong! What you are doing is wrong!' They then expelled them outside of human society. After three months, they returned.

「其后此地复生麤厚地肥，亦香美可食，不如前者，是时众生复取食之，久住世间。其食多者，色转麤丑，其食少者，色犹悦泽，端正丑陋，迭相是非，遂生诤讼，地肥于是遂不复生；其后此地生自然粳米，无有糠糩，色味具足，香洁可食；是时众生复取食之，久住于世，便有男女，互共相视，渐有情欲，转相亲近，其余众生见已，语言：『汝所为非！汝所为非！』即排摈驱遣出于人外，过三月已，然后还归。」

The Buddha told Vasistha, "What was considered wrong in the past is now considered right. At that time, those beings were accustomed to unrighteousness, indulging their desires without restraint. Out of shame, they began to build houses. This is where houses first appeared in the world. Indulging in unrighteousness, their sexual desires increased, and they began to have children through the womb, born from impurity. This is where the womb first appeared in the world. At that time, those beings ate natural glutinous rice, which grew whenever they took it, never running out. At that time, there were lazy beings who silently thought to themselves, 'I take it in the morning for breakfast and in the evening for dinner. It is too much work. Now I want to take it all at once to last for a day.' They then took it all at once. Later, their companions called them to take rice together, but that person replied, 'I have already taken it all at once to last for a day. If you want to take it, you can do so as you please.' That person then thought to themselves, 'This person is wise. They can store it in advance. Now I also want to store it to last for three days.' That person then stored enough rice for three days. Other beings came and said, 'Let's take some rice together.' The person replied, 'I have already stored enough rice for three days in advance. If you want to take it, you can go and take it yourself.' That person then thought, 'This person is wise. They can store rice in advance to last for three days. I will emulate them and store enough rice to last for five days.' They then went to take it.

佛告婆悉咤：「昔所非者，今以为是。时，彼众生习于非法，极情恣欲，无有时节，以慙愧故，遂造屋舍，世间于是始有房舍。玩习非法，婬欲转增，便有胞胎，因不净生，世间胞胎始于是也。时，彼众生食自然粳米，随取随生，无可穷尽。时，彼众生有懈惰者，默自念言：『朝食朝取，暮食暮取，于我劳勤，今欲并取，以终一日。』即寻并取；于后等侣唤共取米，其人答曰：『我已并取，以供一日，汝欲取者，自可随意。』彼人复自念言：『此人黠慧，能先储积，我今亦欲积粮，以供三日。』其人即储三日余粮。有余众生复来语言：『可共取米。』答言：『吾已先积三日余粮，汝欲取者可往自取。』彼人复念：『此人黠慧，先积余粮，以供三日，吾当效彼，积粮以供五日。』即便往取。

At that time, those beings competed to store it, and the glutinous rice became coarse and produced husks. When harvested, it did not grow again. At that time, those beings saw this and became unhappy, and they became confused. They each thought to themselves, 'When I was first born, I ate thoughts, had divine feet, flew through the air, and my body was self-illuminating. I lived in the world for a long time. Later, a sweet spring gushed forth from this ground, resembling ghee and honey, fragrant and delicious. We all ate it together at that time. As we ate it for a long time, those who ate a lot had coarse and ugly complexions, while those who ate little still had pleasant and lustrous complexions. Because of this food, our complexions became different. Beings then had different opinions about each other and hated each other. At that time, the sweet spring naturally dried up. Later, natural earth fat grew on this ground, with color, taste, and fragrance, edible and fragrant. At that time, we again took it to eat, living in the world for a long time. Those who ate a lot had coarse and ugly complexions, while those who ate little still had pleasant and lustrous complexions. Beings then again had different opinions about each other and hated each other. At that time, the earth fat then no longer grew. Later, coarse and thick earth fat grew again, also fragrant and delicious, edible. At that time, we again took it to eat. Those who ate a lot had coarse complexions, while those who ate little had pleasant complexions. Again, different opinions arose, and we hated each other. At that time, the earth fat then no longer appeared. Then natural glutinous rice grew again, without husks. At that time, we again took it to eat, living in the world for a long time. The lazy ones competed to store it. Because of this, the glutinous rice became coarse and produced husks. When harvested, it did not grow again. Now what should we do?' They then said to each other, 'We should divide the land and set up boundaries.' They then divided the land and set up boundaries.

「时，彼众生竞储积已，粳米荒秽，转生糠糩，刈已不生。时，彼众生见此不悦，遂成忧迷，各自念言：『我本初生，以念为食，神足飞空，身光自照，于世久住，其后此地甘泉涌出，状如酥蜜，香美可食，我等时共食之；食之转久，其食多者，颜色麤丑；其食少者，色犹悦泽。由是食故，使我等颜色有异，众生于是各怀是非，迭相憎嫉，是时甘泉自然枯竭，其后此地生自然地肥，色味具足，香美可食，时我曹等复取食之。其食多者，颜色麤丑，其食少者，颜色悦泽，众生于是复怀是非，迭相憎嫉，是时地肥遂不复生。其后复生麤厚地肥，亦香美可食，时，我曹等复取食之；多食色麤，少食色悦，复生是非，共相憎嫉，是时地肥遂不复现。更生自然粳米，无有糠糩，时，我曹等复取食之，久住于世，其懈怠者，竞共储积，由是粳米荒秽，转生糠糩，刈已不生，今当如何？』复自相谓言：『当共分地，别立幖帜。』即寻分地，别立幖帜。

Vasistha! It is because of this cause and condition that the name of farmland first appeared. At that time, those beings divided the farmland and set up boundaries. Gradually, they gave rise to the mind of stealing and stole others' crops. The other beings saw this and said, 'What you have done is wrong! What you have done is wrong! You have your own farmland, yet you take others' things. From now on, do not do this again!' Those beings still did not stop stealing. The other beings reprimanded them again, but they still did not stop. They then struck them with their hands and told the people, 'This person has their own farmland and crops, yet they steal others' things.' That person then said, 'This person hit me.' At that time, those people saw the two disputing and became worried and unhappy. They lamented, 'Beings are becoming more and more evil. The world now has this unwholesomeness, giving rise to impurity and filth. This is the source of birth, old age, sickness, and death. The suffering of afflictions and retribution leads to falling into the three evil paths. It is because of farmland that this dispute arises. Now, should we appoint one person as the ruler to govern it? Protect those who should be protected, reprimand those who should be reprimanded. Let the people reduce their rice and provide for him to handle disputes.'

「婆悉咤！犹此因缘，始有田地名生。彼时众生别封田地，各立疆畔，渐生盗心，窃他禾稼，其余众生见已，语言：『汝所为非！汝所为非！自有田地，而取他物，自今已后，勿复尔也。』其彼众生犹盗不已，其余众生复重呵责而犹不已，便以手加之，告诸人言：『此人自有田稼，而盗他物。』其人复告：『此人打我。』时，彼众人见二人诤已，愁忧不悦，懊恼而言：『众生转恶，世间乃有此不善，生秽恶不净，此是生、老、病、死之原，烦恼苦报堕三恶道，由有田地致此诤讼。今者宁可立一人为主以治理之，可护者护，可责者责，众共减米，以供给之，使理诤讼。』

At that time, among the people, they themselves selected one person with a tall and handsome body, dignified appearance, and majestic virtue, and said to him, 'You should now be our impartial ruler. Protect those who should be protected, reprimand those who should be reprimanded, and banish those who should be banished. We will all gather rice to provide for you.' At that time, that person heard the people's words and immediately became the ruler, adjudicating disputes. The people then all gathered rice to provide for him. At that time, that person further comforted the people with kind words. The people, upon hearing it, were all greatly delighted and all exclaimed, 'Excellent! Great King! Excellent! Great King!' Thus, the world then had the name of king. Because he ruled the people with the right Dharma, he was called a kṣatriya. Thus, the world first had the name of kṣatriya.

「时，彼众中自选一人，形体长大，颜貌端正，有威德者，而语之言：『汝今为我等作平等主，应护者护，应责者责，应遣者遣，当共集米，以相供给。』时，彼一人闻众人言，即与为主，断理诤讼，众人即共集米供给。时，彼一人复以善言慰劳众人，众人闻已，皆大欢喜，皆共称言：『善哉！大王！善哉！大王！』于是，世间便有王名，以正法治民，故名刹利，于是世间始有刹利名生。

At that time, among the people, there was one person who had this thought: 'The household is a great affliction, the household is a poisonous thorn. I should now abandon this household and live alone in the mountains and forests, practicing the path in tranquility.' He then abandoned his household and entered the mountains and forests, contemplating in silence. At mealtime, he entered the village with his alms bowl to beg for food. The people, upon seeing him, all joyfully made offerings and praised him, saying, 'Excellent! This person is able to abandon the household and live alone in the mountains and forests, contemplating in silence and practicing the path, abandoning various evils.' Thus, the world first had the name of brāhmaṇa. Among those brāhmaṇas, there were some who did not enjoy sitting in meditation and contemplation in solitude. They then entered the human realm and made reciting and studying their profession. They also called themselves 'non-meditators.' Thus, the worldly people called them 'non-meditating brāhmaṇas.' Because they entered the human realm, they were called 'human brāhmaṇas.' Thus, the world had the brāhmaṇa caste. Among those beings, there were some who enjoyed managing household affairs and accumulating wealth and treasures. Because of this, the people called them 'householders.' Among those beings, there were some who were skillful and accomplished many things. Thus, the world first had the name of śūdra craftsmen.

「时，彼众中独有一人作如是念：『家为大患，家为毒刺，我今宁可舍此居家，独在山林，闲静修道。』即舍居家，入于山林，寂默思惟，至时持器入村乞食，众人见已，皆乐供养，欢喜称赞：『善哉！此人能舍家居，独处山林，静默修道，舍离众恶。』于是，世间始有婆罗门名生。彼婆罗门中有不乐闲静坐禅思惟者，便入人间，诵习为业，又自称言：『我是不禅人！』于是，世人称不禅婆罗门。由入人间故，名为人间婆罗门，于是，世间有婆罗门种；彼众生中有人好营居业，多积财宝，因是众人名为居士；彼众生中有多机巧，多所造作，于是世间始有首陀罗工巧之名。

Vāseṭṭha, now in this world there are four kinds of names, and the fifth is the name of the assembly of śramaṇas. Why is this so? Vāseṭṭha, among the kṣatriya class, sometimes there are people who are disgusted with their own Dharma, shave their beards and hair, and don the Dharma robes. Thus, the name of śramaṇa first arises. Among the brāhmaṇa, householder, and śūdra classes, sometimes there are people who are disgusted with their own Dharma, shave their beards and hair, and practice the path in Dharma robes, called śramaṇas. Vāseṭṭha, among the kṣatriya class, those who engage in unwholesome bodily, verbal, and mental actions will definitely experience suffering upon the disintegration of the body and the end of life. Among the brāhmaṇa, householder, and śūdra classes, those who engage in unwholesome bodily, verbal, and mental actions will definitely experience suffering upon the disintegration of the body and the end of life. Vāseṭṭha, among the kṣatriya class, those who engage in wholesome bodily, verbal, and mental actions will definitely experience happiness upon the disintegration of the body and the end of life. Among the brāhmaṇa, householder, and śūdra classes, those who engage in wholesome bodily, verbal, and mental actions will definitely experience happiness upon the disintegration of the body and the end of life. Vāseṭṭha, among the kṣatriya class, those who engage in two kinds of bodily, verbal, and mental actions will experience both suffering and happiness upon the disintegration of the body and the end of life. Among the brāhmaṇa, householder, and śūdra classes, those who engage in two kinds of bodily, verbal, and mental actions will experience both suffering and happiness upon the disintegration of the body and the end of life.

「婆悉咤！今此世间有四种名，第五有沙门众名。所以然者？婆悉咤！刹利众中，或时有人自厌己法，剃除须发，而披法服，于是始有沙门名生。婆罗门种、居士种、首陀罗种，或时有人自厌己法，剃除须发，法服修道，名为沙门。婆悉咤！刹利种中，身行不善，口行不善，意行不善身坏命终，必受苦报。婆罗门种、居士种、首陀罗种，身行不善，口行不善，意行不善，身坏命终，必受苦报。婆悉咤！刹利种中，有身行善，口、意行善，身坏命终，必受乐报。婆罗门、居士、首陀罗种中，身行善，口、意行善，身坏命终，必受乐报。婆悉咤！刹利众中，身行二种，口、意行二种，身坏命终，受苦乐报。婆罗门种、居士种、首陀罗种，身行二种，口、意行二种，身坏命终，受苦乐报。

Vāseṭṭha, among the kṣatriya class, there are those who shave their beards and hair, don the Dharma robes, and practice the seven factors of awakening, soon attaining the path. Why is this so? Because those noble sons, having renounced the householder life, practice the unsurpassed holy life, and in this very life they personally realize: 'Birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, there is no more of this mundane state.' Among the brāhmaṇa, householder, and śūdra classes, there are those who shave their beards and hair, don the Dharma robes, and practice the seven factors of awakening, soon attaining the path. Why is this so? Because those noble sons, having renounced the householder life, practice the unsurpassed holy life, and in this very life they personally realize: 'Birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, there is no more of this mundane state.' Vāseṭṭha, among these four classes, all those who have attained the perfection of wisdom and arahantship are the best and foremost among the five classes.

「婆悉咤！刹利种中，有剃除须发，法服修道，修七觉意，道成不久。所以者何？彼族姓子法服出家，修无上梵行，于现法中自身作证：『生死已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不复受有。』婆罗门、居士、首陀罗种中，有剃除须发，法服修道，修七觉意，道成不久。所以者何？彼族姓子法服出家，修无上梵行，于现法中自身作证：『生死已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不复受有。』婆悉咤！此四种中皆出明行成就罗汉，于五种中为最第一。」

The Buddha told Vāseṭṭha: "The Brahma King has a verse that goes:"

佛告婆悉咤：「梵天王颂曰：

Among the born, the kṣatriyas are supreme, able to abandon their caste and leave; those
who have attained the perfection of wisdom and arahantship are the foremost in the world.

「『生中刹利胜，　　能舍种姓去；明行成就者，　　世间最第一。』」

The Buddha told Vāseṭṭha: "This Brahma spoke well, not unskillfully; this Brahma received well, not unskillfully. I then approved his words. Why is that? Now I, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, also speak of this meaning:

佛告婆悉咤：「此梵善说，非不善说；此梵善受，非不善受。我时即印可其言，所以者何？今我如来、至真亦说是义：

Among the born, the kṣatriyas are supreme, able to abandon their caste and leave; those
who have attained the perfection of wisdom and arahantship are the foremost in the world.

「『生中刹利胜，　　能舍种姓去；明行成就者，　　世间最第一。』」

At that time, after the World-Honored One had spoken this Dharma, Vāseṭṭha and Brāhmaṇadatta attained the liberation of the mind free from taints. Having heard the Buddha's teaching, they joyfully accepted and practiced it.

尔时，世尊说此法已，婆悉咤、婆罗堕无漏心解脱，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
