DĀ 6

Dīrghāgama 长阿含经

Chapter 2 第二分

6 （六）转轮圣王修行经

Parallel to: DN 26 / MĀ 70

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in the human realm of Māraheṣu, gradually arriving in the country of Mālu with a group of 1,250 bhikṣus.

一时，佛在摩罗醯搜人间游行，与千二百五十比丘渐至摩楼国。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "You should burn yourselves, burn the Dharma, and not be burned by others; you should take refuge in yourselves, take refuge in the Dharma, and not take refuge in others. How should bhikṣus burn themselves, burn the Dharma, and not be burned by others; take refuge in themselves, take refuge in the Dharma, and not take refuge in others? Here, bhikṣus contemplate the body internally, diligently and tirelessly, with mindfulness and non-forgetfulness, eliminating worldly greed and sorrow; contemplating the body externally, contemplating the body internally and externally, diligently and tirelessly, with mindfulness and non-forgetfulness, eliminating worldly greed and sorrow. Contemplating feelings, mind, and dharmas are also like this. This is called bhikṣus burning themselves, burning the Dharma, and not being burned by others; taking refuge in themselves, taking refuge in the Dharma, and not taking refuge in others.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「汝等当自炽燃，炽燃于法，勿他炽燃；当自归依，归依于法，勿他归依。云何比丘当自炽燃，炽燃于法，勿他炽燃？当自归依，归依于法，勿他归依？于是，比丘内身身观，精勤无懈，忆念不忘，除世贪忧；外身身观、内外身身观，精勤无懈，识念不忘，除世贪忧。受、意、法观，亦复如是，是为比丘自炽燃，炽燃于] cf.【丽】法，不他炽燃；自归依，归依于法，不他归依。

Those who practice like this cannot be disturbed by Māra, and their merits increase day by day. Why is this? Long ago, in the distant past, there was a king named Firm Mindfulness, of the kṣatriya water-sprinkled head class, who became a wheel-turning sage king, ruling the four continents. At that time, the king ruled freely by the Dharma, outstanding among humans, with the seven treasures complete: first, the golden wheel treasure; second, the white elephant treasure; third, the dark horse treasure; fourth, the divine jewel treasure; fifth, the jade woman treasure; sixth, the householder treasure; seventh, the military commander treasure. He had a thousand sons, brave and valiant, able to subdue enemies without resorting to weapons, naturally bringing peace. After a long reign, the king's golden wheel treasure suddenly left its original place in the sky. The wheel keeper quickly went to inform the king: 'Great King! You should know that now the wheel treasure has left its original place.' When King Firm Mindfulness heard this, he thought: 'I have heard from the elders of the past that if the wheel treasure of a wheel-turning sage king moves, the king's life is short. I have already enjoyed human blessings and happiness. I should make further arrangements to enjoy heavenly blessings and happiness. I shall appoint a crown prince to rule the four continents, and separately grant a fief to the tonsure master. I shall shave my beard and hair, wear the three Dharma robes, and leave home to cultivate the path.'

「如是行者，魔不能娆，功德日增，所以者何？乃往过去久远世时，有王名坚固念，刹利水浇头种，为转轮圣王，领四天下。时，王自在以法治化，人中殊特，七宝具足，一者金轮宝，二者白象宝，三者绀马宝，四者神珠宝，五者玉女宝，六者居士宝，七者主兵宝。千子具足，勇健雄猛，能伏怨敌，不用兵杖，自然太平。坚固念王久治世已，时，金轮宝即于虚空忽离本处，时，典轮者速往白王：『大王！当知今者轮宝离于本处。』时，坚固王闻已念言：『我曾于先宿耆旧所闻，若转轮圣王轮宝移者，王寿未几。我今已受人中福乐，宜更方便受天福乐，当立太子领四天下，别封一邑与下发师，命下须发，服三法衣，出家修道。』

At that time, King Firm Mindfulness immediately ordered the crown prince, saying: 'Do you know? I have heard from the elders of the past that if the golden wheel of a wheel-turning sage king leaves its original place, the king's life is short. I have already enjoyed human blessings and happiness. I should make further arrangements to enjoy heavenly blessings and happiness. I now wish to shave my beard and hair, wear the three Dharma robes, leave home to cultivate the path, and entrust the four continents to you. You should be diligent and care for the people and things.' At that time, the crown prince accepted the king's instructions. Then, King Firm Mindfulness immediately shaved his beard and hair, wore the three Dharma robes, left home to cultivate the path.

「时，坚固念王即命太子而告之曰：『卿为知不？吾曾从先宿耆旧所闻，若转轮圣王金轮离本处者，王寿未几。吾今已受人中福乐，当更方便迁受天福，今欲剃除须发，服三法衣，出家为道，以四天下委付于汝，宜自勉力，存恤民物。』是时，太子受王教已，时，坚固念王即剃除须发，服三法衣，出家修道。

After the king left home for seven days, the golden wheel treasure suddenly disappeared. The wheel keeper went to inform the king: 'Great King! You should know that now the wheel treasure has suddenly disappeared.' The king was displeased and went to King Firm Mindfulness. Upon arrival, he said to the king: 'Father King! You should know that now the wheel treasure has suddenly disappeared.' At that time, King Firm Mindfulness replied to his son: 'Do not be worried and displeased. This golden wheel treasure is not your father's possession. You should diligently practice the righteous Dharma of a sage king. After practicing the righteous Dharma, on the fifteenth day of the month, when the moon is full, bathe in fragrant water, surrounded by court ladies, ascend to the Dharma Hall, and the divine golden wheel will naturally appear. The wheel has a thousand spokes, with complete radiant colors, crafted by celestial artisans, not existing in the world.'

「时，王出家过七日已，彼金轮宝忽然不现，其典轮者往白王言：『大王！当知今者轮宝忽然不现。』时王不悦，即往诣坚固念王所，到已白王：『父王！当知今者轮宝忽然不现。』时，坚固念王报其子曰：『汝勿怀忧以为不悦，此金轮宝者非汝父产，汝但勤行圣王正法，行正法已，于十五日月满时，沐浴香汤，婇女围遶，升正法殿上，金轮神宝自然当现，轮有千辐，光色具足，天匠所造，非世所有。』

The son said to the father king: 'What is the righteous Dharma of a wheel-turning sage king? How should it be practiced?' The king told his son: 'One should rely on the Dharma, establish the Dharma, respect and honor the Dharma, observe the Dharma, take the Dharma as the foremost, and protect the righteous Dharma; one should also teach the Dharma to the court ladies, and protect and instruct the princes, ministers, officials, and all the people, including śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, down to birds and beasts, all of whom should be protected and instructed.'

「子白父王：『转轮圣王正法云何？当云何行？』王告子曰：『当依于法，立法具法，恭敬尊重，观察于法，以法为首，守护正法；又当以法诲诸婇女，又当以法护视教诫诸王子、大臣、群寮、百官及诸人民、沙门、婆罗门，下至禽兽，皆当护视。』

"He further told his son: 'Also, in your territory, there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who practice purity, are endowed with merit, are diligent and tireless, are free from arrogance, are patient and kind, practice in solitude, rest in solitude, attain nirvāṇa in solitude, eliminate greed themselves and teach others to eliminate greed; eliminate anger themselves and teach others to eliminate anger; eliminate delusion themselves and teach others to eliminate delusion. They are not defiled by defilements, not evil in evil, not foolish in foolishness, are attached to what is attachable, dwell in what is dwellable, and live in what is livable. Their physical actions are straightforward, their verbal expressions are straightforward, their mental thoughts are straightforward; their physical actions are pure, their verbal expressions are pure, their mental thoughts are pure, their mindfulness is pure, their kindness and wisdom are inexhaustible, they are content with their robes and food, they beg for alms with their bowls, and they bring blessings to sentient beings. If there are such people, you should frequently visit them, ask them about their practices at any time, what is good and what is bad, what is an offense and what is not an offense, who is worthy of friendship and who is not, what should be done and what should not be done, and what Dharma should be practiced to experience happiness for a long time. After you have asked, observe with your mind, and do what is appropriate. In the country, there are orphans and the elderly who should be supported; if the poor and destitute come to take [from you], do not be disobedient. There are old customs in the country that you should not change. This is the Dharma practiced by the wheel-turning sage kings. You should follow it.'"

「又告子曰：『又汝土境所有沙门、婆罗门履行清真，功德具足，精进不懈，去离憍慢，忍辱仁爱，闲独自修，独自止息，独到涅槃，自除贪欲，化彼除贪；自除瞋恚，化彼除瞋；自除愚痴，化彼除痴。于染不染，于恶不恶，于愚不愚，可著不著，可住不住，可居不居。身行质直，口言质直，意念质直；身行清净，口言清净，意念清净，正念清净，仁慧无厌，衣食知足，持钵乞食，以福众生。有如是人者，汝当数诣，随时咨问，凡所修行，何善何恶？云何为犯？云何非犯？何者可亲？何者不可亲？何者可作？何者不可作？施行何法，长夜受乐？汝咨问已，以意观察，宜行则行，宜舍则舍。国有孤老，当拯给之；贫穷困劣，有来取者，慎勿违逆。国有旧法，汝勿改易，此是转轮圣王所修行法，汝当奉行。』」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus: "At that time, the wheel-turning sage king, having received his father's instructions, practiced accordingly. Later, on the full moon day of the fifteenth, he bathed in fragrant water, ascended to the high palace, surrounded by court ladies, and the naturally-occurring wheel treasure suddenly appeared before him. The wheel had a thousand spokes, complete with radiant colors, crafted by celestial artisans, not of this world, made of pure gold, with a diameter of four zhang. At that time, the wheel-turning sage king silently thought to himself: 'I have heard from the elders of the past that if a kṣatriya king of the water-sprinkled lineage, on the full moon day of the fifteenth, bathes in fragrant water, ascends to the jeweled palace, surrounded by court ladies, and a naturally-occurring golden wheel suddenly appears before him, with a thousand spokes, complete with radiant colors, crafted by celestial artisans, not of this world, made of pure gold, with a diameter of four zhang, then he is called a wheel-turning sage king. Now that this wheel has appeared, could it be so? Now I should test this wheel treasure.'"

佛告诸比丘：「时，转轮圣王受父教已，如说修行，后于十五日月满时，沐浴香汤，升高殿上，婇女围遶，自然轮宝忽然在前，轮有千辐，光色具足，天匠所造，非世所有，真金所成，轮径丈四。时，转轮王默自念言：『我曾从先宿耆旧所闻，若刹利王水浇头种，以十五日月满时，沐浴香汤，升宝殿上，婇女围遶，自然金轮忽现在前，轮有千辐，光色具足，天匠所造，非世所有，真金所成，轮径丈四，是则名为转轮圣王。今此轮现，将无是耶？今我宁可试此轮宝。』

"At that time, the wheel-turning sage king immediately summoned the four divisions of troops, faced the golden wheel treasure, bared his right arm, touched the ground with his right knee, and again stroked the golden wheel with his right hand, saying: 'Turn towards the east according to the Dharma, do not violate the usual principles.' The wheel then turned east. At that time, the king immediately led the four divisions of troops to follow behind. There were four divine guides in front of the golden wheel treasure. Wherever the wheel stopped, the king would halt his carriage. At that time, the kings of the small countries in the east saw the great king arrive. They came to the king's place with a golden bowl filled with silver grains and a silver bowl filled with golden grains, bowed their heads, and said: 'Welcome, great king! This land in the east is rich and prosperous, with a flourishing population. The people are kind and gentle, filial, loyal, and obedient. We only wish that the sage king would rule here. We will provide servants and attendants, and accept what is due.' At that time, the wheel-turning sage king said to the small kings: 'Stop! Stop! All of you are already making offerings to me. You should only rule with the right Dharma, do not be biased or unjust. Do not let there be any unrighteous conduct in the country. This is called my rule.'"

「时，转轮王即召四兵，向金轮宝偏露右臂，右膝著地，复以右手摩扪金轮，语言：『汝向东方，如法而转，勿违常则。』轮即东转。时，王即将四兵随从其后，金轮宝前有四神导，轮所住处，王即止驾。尔时，东方诸小国王见大王至，以金钵盛银粟，银钵盛金粟，来趣王所，拜首白言：『善来，大王！今此东方土地丰乐，人民炽盛，志性仁和，慈孝忠顺，唯愿圣王于此治正，我等当给使左右，承受所当。』时，转轮大王语小王言：『止！止！诸贤！汝等则为供养我已，但当以正法治，勿使偏枉，无令国内有非法行，此即名曰我之所治。』

"At that time, the small kings, having heard this instruction, followed the great king on a tour of the countries, reaching the eastern seacoast. Then they proceeded to the south, west, and north, wherever the wheel went. The kings of those countries each offered their lands, just like the small countries in the east. At that time, the wheel-turning sage king, having followed the golden wheel on a tour of the four seas, enlightened and comforted the people with the Dharma, and returned to his own country. At that time, the golden wheel treasure hovered in the sky above the palace gate. The wheel-turning sage king joyfully said: 'This golden wheel treasure is truly a good omen for me. I am now truly a wheel-turning sage king. This is the accomplishment of the golden wheel treasure.'"

「时，诸小王闻此教已，即从大王巡行诸国，至东海表，次行南方、西方、北方，随轮所至，其诸国王各献国土，亦如东方诸小国比。时，转轮王既随金轮，周行四海，以道开化，安慰民庶，已还本国。时，金轮宝在宫门上虚空中住，时，转轮王踊跃而言：『此金轮宝真为我瑞，我今真为转轮圣王，是为金轮宝成就。』

"After the king had ruled for a long time, the golden wheel treasure suddenly left its original place in the sky. The one in charge of the wheel quickly went to inform the king: 'Great King! You should know that now the wheel treasure has left its original place.' When the king heard this, he thought to himself: 'I have heard from the elders of the past that if the wheel treasure of a wheel-turning sage king moves, the king's life is not long. I have already enjoyed the blessings of humans. I should make further arrangements to enjoy the blessings of the heavens. I shall appoint a crown prince to rule the four continents, and separately grant a fief to the tonsure master, allowing him to shave my beard and hair, wear the three Dharma robes, and leave home to practice the Way.'"

「其王久治世已，时，金轮宝即于虚空忽离本处，其典轮者速往白王：『大王！当知今者轮宝离于本处。』时，王闻已即自念言：『我曾于先宿耆旧所闻，若转轮圣王轮宝移者，王寿未几。我今已受人中福乐，宜更方便受天福乐，当立太子领四天下，别封一邑与下发师，令下须发，服三法衣，出家修道。』

At that time, the king immediately ordered the crown prince and said to him: 'Do you know? I have heard from the elders of the past that if the golden wheel treasure of a wheel-turning sage king leaves its original place, the king's life is not long. I have already enjoyed the blessings of humans. I shall make further arrangements to enjoy the blessings of the heavens. Now I wish to shave my beard and hair, wear the three Dharma robes, leave home to practice the Way, and entrust the four continents to you. You should strive to care for the people and the land.' At that time, the crown prince having received the king's instructions, the king then shaved his beard and hair, wore the three Dharma robes, and left home to practice the Way. After seven days had passed since the king left home, the golden wheel treasure suddenly disappeared. The one in charge of the golden wheel went to inform the king, saying: 'Great King! You should know that now the wheel treasure has suddenly disappeared.' When the king heard this, he did not worry about it, and also did not go to ask the father king's intention. At that time, the father king suddenly passed away.

「时，王即命太子而告之曰：『卿为知不？吾曾从先宿耆旧所闻，若转轮圣王金轮宝离本处者，王寿未几。吾今已受人中福乐，当设方便迁受天乐，今欲剃除须发，服三法衣，出家修道，以四天下委付于汝，宜自勉力，存恤民物。』尔时，太子受王教已，王即剃除须发，服三法衣，出家修道。时，王出家过七日已，其金轮宝忽然不现，典金轮者往白王言：『大王！当知今者轮宝忽然不现。』时王闻已，不以为忧，亦复不往问父王意。时，彼父王忽然命终。

From this point on, six wheel-turning sage kings all succeeded each other, ruling with the right Dharma. Only this one king ruled the country on his own, not following the old laws. His rule was not just, and the people complained. The territory declined, and the people withered. At that time, a brāhman minister went to inform the king, saying: 'Great King! You should know that now the territory is declining, and the people are withering, unlike before. The king now has many knowledgeable people in the country, who are wise and well-versed, clear about the past and present, and know the methods of governance of the former kings. Why not summon them and ask what they know? They will surely answer.' At that time, the king immediately summoned the ministers and asked about the way of governance of the former kings. At that time, the wise ministers fully answered with the matter. The king, having heard their words, immediately implemented the old governance, protecting the world with the Dharma. However, he was unable to rescue the orphaned and elderly, and provide for the destitute.

「自此以前，六转轮王皆展转相承，以正法治，唯此一王自用治国，不承旧法，其政不平，天下怨诉，国土损减，人民凋落。时，有一婆罗门大臣往白王言：『大王！当知今者国土损减，人民凋落，转不如常。王今国内多有知识，聪慧博达，明于古今，备知先王治政之法，何不命集问其所知，彼自当答？』时，王即召群臣，问其先王治政之道。时，诸智臣具以事答，王闻其言，即行旧政，以法护世，而由不能拯济孤老，施及下穷。

At that time, the people of the country became increasingly poor and destitute, and they began to encroach upon and rob each other. Thieves became rampant. Those who were caught were brought before the king and said: 'This person is a thief. Please punish him.' The king then asked: 'Did you really commit theft?' They replied: 'It is true! We are poor and hungry, unable to support ourselves, so we became thieves.' At that time, the king immediately took out treasury goods to supply them, and told them: 'Use these goods to support your parents and take care of your relatives. From now on, do not steal again.' Other people heard that those who became thieves were given wealth by the king, so they also went out to rob others' belongings. Again, they were caught by the watchmen and brought before the king, who said: 'This person is a thief. Please punish him.' The king again asked: 'Did you really commit theft?' They replied: 'It is true! We are poor and hungry, unable to support ourselves, so we became thieves.' At that time, the king again took out treasury goods to supply them, and again told them: 'Use these goods to support your parents and take care of your relatives. From now on, do not steal again.'

「时，国人民转至贫困，遂相侵夺，盗贼滋甚，伺察所得，将诣王所白言：『此人为贼，愿王治之。』王即问言：『汝实为贼耶？』答曰：『实尔！我贫穷饥饿，不能自存，故为贼耳。』时，王即出库物以供给之，而告之曰：『汝以此物供养父母，并恤亲族，自今已后，勿复为贼。』余人转闻有作贼者，王给财宝，于是复行劫盗他物，复为伺察所得，将诣王所白言：『此人为贼，愿王治之。』王复问言：『汝实为贼耶？』答曰：『实尔！我贫穷饥饿，不能自存，故为贼耳。』时，王复出库财以供给之，复告之曰：『汝以此物供养父母，并恤亲族，自今已后，勿复为贼。』

Again, some people heard that those who became thieves were given wealth by the king, so they also went out to rob others' belongings. Again, they were caught by the watchmen and brought before the king, who said: 'This person is a thief. Please punish him.' The king again asked: 'Did you really commit theft?' They replied: 'It is true! We are poor and hungry, unable to support ourselves, so we became thieves.' At that time, the king thought: 'Those who became thieves before, I saw them as poor and gave them wealth, thinking it would stop them. But other people heard about it and followed suit, and thieves became more and more rampant, like this without end. Should I now rather shackle these people, parade them through the streets, and then take them out of the city to be executed in the wilderness, to warn later people?'

「复有人闻有作贼者，王给财宝，于是复行劫盗他物，复为伺察所得，将诣王所白言：『此人为贼，愿王治之。』王复问言：『汝实为贼耶？』答曰：『实尔！我贫穷饥饿，不能自存，故为贼耳。』时王念言：『先为贼者，吾见贫穷，给其财宝，谓当止息，而余人闻，转更相効，盗贼日滋，如是无已，我今宁可杻械其人，令于街巷，然后载之出城，刑于旷野，以诫后人耶！』

At that time, the king ordered his attendants to arrest and shackle them, beat the drum and proclaim it throughout the streets and alleys, and then take them out of the city to be executed in the wilderness. The people of the country all knew that those who became thieves were arrested and shackled by the king, paraded through the streets, and executed in the wilderness. At that time, the people spoke to each other, saying: 'If we become thieves, we will also be like this, no different from them.' Then, the people of the country, in order to protect themselves, began to make weapons, swords, bows and arrows, and attacked and robbed each other. From this time on, the king began to have poverty, and with poverty came robbery, with robbery came weapons, with weapons came killing, and with killing came a haggard complexion and a short lifespan. At that time, people lived for exactly forty thousand years, but later it decreased to twenty thousand years. However, among those beings, some had a lifespan, some had premature death, some had suffering, and some had happiness. Those who had suffering gave rise to thoughts of sexual misconduct and greed, and devised many means to scheme for others' possessions. At that time, the beings became poor and robbed, and the use of weapons and killing became more and more rampant. The lifespan of people decreased further, to ten thousand years.

「时，王即勅左右：『使收系之，声鼓唱令，遍诸街巷，讫已载之出城，刑于旷野。』国人尽知彼为贼者，王所收系，令于街巷，刑之旷野。时，人展转自相谓言：『我等设为贼者，亦当如是，与彼无异。』于是，国人为自防护，遂造兵杖、刀剑、弓矢，迭相残害，攻劫掠夺。自此王来始有贫穷，有贫穷已始有劫盗，有劫盗已始有兵杖，有兵杖已始有杀害，有杀害已则颜色憔悴，寿命短促。时，人正寿四万岁，其后转少，寿二万岁，然其众生有寿、有夭、有苦、有乐。彼有苦者，便生邪婬、贪取之心，多设方便，图谋他物。是时，众生贫穷劫盗，兵杖杀害，转转滋甚，人命转减，寿一万岁。

At the age of ten thousand years, beings again robbed each other. They were caught by the watchmen and brought before the king, who said: 'This person is a thief. Please punish him.' The king asked: 'Did you really commit theft?' They replied: 'I did not!' Then they deliberately told lies in the assembly. At that time, those beings, due to poverty, engaged in robbery. Because of robbery, there were weapons. Because of weapons, there was killing. Because of killing, there was greed and sexual misconduct. Because of greed and sexual misconduct, there was lying. Because of lying, their lifespan decreased further, to one thousand years. At the age of one thousand years, the three evil verbal actions first arose in the world: one, divisive speech; two, harsh speech; three, frivolous speech. These three evil actions became more and more rampant, and the lifespan of people decreased further to five hundred years. At the age of five hundred years, beings again gave rise to three evil actions: one, unlawful sexual desire; two, unlawful greed; three, wrong views. These three evil actions became more and more rampant, and the lifespan of people decreased further to three hundred and two hundred years. Now, the lifespan of people in my time is up to one hundred years, with few living longer and many dying younger.

「一万岁时，众生复相劫盗，为伺察所得，将诣王所白言：『此人为贼，愿王治之。』王问言：『汝实作贼耶？』答曰：『我不作！』便于众中故作妄语。时，彼众生以贫穷故便行劫盗，以劫盗故便有刀兵，以刀兵故便有杀害，以杀害故便有贪取、邪婬，以贪取、邪婬故便有妄语，有妄语故其寿转减，至于千岁；千岁之时，便有口三恶行始出于世，一者两舌，二者恶口，三者绮语，此三恶业展转炽盛，人寿稍减至五百岁；五百岁时，众生复有三恶行起，一者非法婬，二者非法贪，三者邪见，此三恶业展转炽盛，人寿稍减，三百、二百，我今时人，乃至百岁，少出多减。

In this way, the evil deeds continue, and the lifespan decreases further. It will be reduced to ten years. At the age of ten years, girls will be married at the age of five. At that time, in the world, the names of butter, oil, rock candy, and black rock candy, and all delicious flavors will no longer be heard. Rice and wheat will turn into weeds. The famous garments of silk, brocade, satin, and white wool will no longer be seen. Coarse woolen thread will be used as the best clothing. At that time, this land will give rise to many thorns, mosquitoes, flies, lice, snakes, scorpions, bees, and poisonous insects. Gold, silver, lapis lazuli, pearls, and precious gems will all disappear into the ground, and pottery, stones, sand, and gravel will appear on the ground.

「如是展转，为恶不已，其寿稍减，当至十岁，十岁时人，女生五月便行嫁。是时世间酥油、石蜜、黑石蜜，诸甘美味不复闻名，粳粮、禾稻变成草莠，缯、绢、锦、绫、劫贝、白［叠＊毛］，今世名服，时悉不现，织麤毛缕以为上衣。是时，此地多生荆棘，蚊、虻、蝇、虱、蛇、蚖、蜂、蛆，毒虫众多，金、银、琉璃、珠玑、名宝，尽没于地，遂有瓦石砂砾出于地上。

At that time, beings will never again hear the name of the ten wholesome deeds, but only the ten unwholesome deeds will fill the world. At that time, there will be no name for wholesome dharmas. How can those people cultivate wholesome practices? At that time, beings will be able to do extreme evil, not filial to their parents, not respecting their teachers and elders, not loyal and righteous, but rather rebellious and unrighteous. They will then be respected. Now, those who can cultivate wholesome practices, be filial to their parents, respect their teachers and elders, be loyal and righteous, and practice in accordance with the Way are respected. At that time, beings will cultivate the ten unwholesome deeds a lot, and fall into evil paths a lot. When beings see each other, they always want to kill each other, just like hunters seeing a herd of deer. At that time, this land will have many ditches, ravines, deep valleys, and vast areas with few people. Traveling will be fearful. At that time, there will be an uprising of weapons and warfare. Holding grass and wood, they will all become spears and swords. For seven days, they will turn against each other and kill.

「当于尔时，众生之类永不复闻十善之名，但有十恶充满世间。是时，乃无善法之名，其人何由得修善行？是时，众生能为极恶，不孝父母，不敬师长，不忠不义，返逆无道者便得尊敬。如今能修善行，孝养父母，敬顺师长，忠信怀义，顺道修行者便得尊敬。尔时，众生多修十恶，多堕恶道，众生相见，常欲相杀，犹如猎师见于群鹿。时，此土地多有沟坑，溪涧深谷，土旷人希，行来恐惧。尔时，当有刀兵劫起，手执草木，皆成戈𫓴，于七日中，展转相害。

At that time, the wise ones will flee far into the forests and rely on ditches and ravines. For seven days, they will be fearful and speak kind words, 'You do not harm me, I do not harm you.' Eating the seeds of grass and trees to sustain their lives, after seven days they will emerge from the mountains and forests. At that time, those who survive will be able to see each other and rejoice, saying, 'You are not dead? You are not dead?' It is like parents having only one child, seeing each other after a long separation, rejoicing immensely. Those people will also rejoice like this, rejoicing in each other, and then inquiring about their families. The dead will be many. They will grieve and wail for seven days, crying and facing each other. After seven days, they will again rejoice and entertain each other for seven days. They will then think to themselves, 'We have accumulated evil extensively, thus experiencing this difficulty. Our relatives have died and our families have been destroyed. Now we should cultivate some wholesome deeds. What wholesome deeds should we cultivate? We should not kill living beings.'

「时，有智者远逃丛林，依倚坑坎，于七日中怀怖畏心，发慈善言：『汝不害我，我不害汝。』食草木子以存性命，过七日已，从山林出。时有存者，得共相见，欢喜庆贺言：『汝不死耶？汝不死耶？』犹如父母唯有一子，久别相见，欢喜无量。彼人如是各怀欢喜，迭相庆贺，然后推问其家，其家亲属死亡者众，复于七日中悲泣号咷，啼哭相向。过七日已，复于七日中共相庆贺，娱乐欢喜，寻自念言：『吾等积恶弥广，故遭此难，亲族死亡，家属覆没，今者宜当少共修善，宜修何善？当不杀生。』

At that time, all beings will have compassionate minds and will not harm each other. Then the lifespan and appearance of beings will increase. Those who are ten years old will live to be twenty years old. The people of that time will think, 'Because we have cultivated wholesome deeds and not harmed each other, our lifespan has been extended to twenty years. Now we should cultivate even more wholesome deeds. What wholesome deeds should we cultivate? We should not steal.' Having cultivated not stealing, their lifespan will be extended to forty years. The people of that time will think, 'Because we have cultivated wholesome deeds, our lifespan has been extended. Now we should cultivate even more wholesome deeds. What wholesome deeds should we cultivate? We should not engage in sexual misconduct.' Then those people will all not engage in sexual misconduct, and their lifespan will be extended to eighty years.

「尔时，众生尽怀慈心，不相残害，于是众生色寿转增，其十岁者寿二十岁。二十时人复作是念：『我等由少修善行，不相残害故，寿命延长至二十岁，今者宁可更增少善，当修何善？已不杀生，当不窃盗。』已修不盗，则寿命延长至四十岁。四十时人复作是念：『我等由少修善，寿命延长，今者宁可更增少善，何善可修？当不邪婬。』于是，其人尽不邪婬，寿命延长至八十岁。

The people of eighty years will think, 'Because we have cultivated wholesome deeds, our lifespan has been extended. Now we should cultivate even more wholesome deeds. What wholesome deeds should we cultivate? We should not speak falsely.' Then those people will all not speak falsely, and their lifespan will be extended to one hundred and sixty years. The people of one hundred and sixty years will think, 'Because we have cultivated wholesome deeds, our lifespan has been extended. Now we should cultivate even more wholesome deeds. What wholesome deeds should we cultivate? We should not engage in divisive speech.' Then those people will all not engage in divisive speech, and their lifespan will be extended to three hundred and twenty years. The people of three hundred and twenty years will think, 'Because we have cultivated wholesome deeds, our lifespan has been extended. Now we should cultivate even more wholesome deeds. What wholesome deeds should we cultivate? We should not speak harshly.' Then those people will all not speak harshly, and their lifespan will be extended to six hundred and forty years.

「八十岁人复作是念：『我等由少修善，寿命延长，今者宁可更增少善，何善可修？当不妄语。』于是，其人尽不妄语，寿命延长至百六十。百六十时人复作是念：『我等由少修善，寿命延长，我今宁可更增小善，何善可修？当不两舌。』于是，其人尽不两舌，寿命延长至三百二十岁。三百二十岁时人复作是念：『我等由少修善故，寿命延长，今者宁可更增少善，何善可修？当不恶口。』于是，其人尽不恶口，寿命延长至六百四十。

The people of six hundred and forty years will think, 'Because we have cultivated wholesome deeds, our lifespan has been extended. Now we should cultivate even more wholesome deeds. What wholesome deeds should we cultivate? We should not engage in frivolous speech.' Then those people will all not engage in frivolous speech, and their lifespan will be extended to two thousand years. The people of two thousand years will think, 'Because we have cultivated wholesome deeds, our lifespan has been extended. Now we should cultivate even more wholesome deeds. What wholesome deeds should we cultivate? We should not be stingy and greedy.' Then those people will all not be stingy and greedy, but practice giving, and their lifespan will be extended to five thousand years. The people of five thousand years will think, 'Because we have cultivated wholesome deeds, our lifespan has been extended. Now we should cultivate even more wholesome deeds. What wholesome deeds should we cultivate? We should not be jealous and cultivate wholesome deeds with a compassionate mind.' Then those people will all not be jealous, cultivate wholesome deeds with a compassionate mind, and their lifespan will be extended to ten thousand years.

「六百四十时人复作是念：『我等由修善故，寿命延长，今者宁可更增少善，何善可修？当不绮语。』于是，其人尽不绮语，寿命延长至二千岁。二千岁时人复作是念：『我等由修善故，寿命延长，今者宁可更增少善，何善可修？当不悭贪。』于是，其人尽不悭贪而行布施，寿命延长至五千岁。五千岁时人复作是念：『我等由修善故，寿命延长，今者宁可更增少善，何善可修？当不嫉妬，慈心修善。』于是，其人尽不嫉妬，慈心修善，寿命延长至于万岁。

The people of ten thousand years will think, 'Because we have cultivated wholesome deeds, our lifespan has been extended. Now we should cultivate even more wholesome deeds. What wholesome deeds should we cultivate? We should practice right views and not give rise to inverted views.' Then those people will all practice right views and not give rise to inverted views, and their lifespan will be extended to twenty thousand years. The people of twenty thousand years will think, 'Because we have cultivated wholesome deeds, our lifespan has been extended. Now we should cultivate even more wholesome deeds. What wholesome deeds should we cultivate? We should eliminate the three unwholesome dharmas: namely, unwholesome desire, unwholesome greed, and wrong views.' Then those people will all eliminate the three unwholesome dharmas, and their lifespan will be extended to forty thousand years. The people of forty thousand years will think, 'Because we have cultivated wholesome deeds, our lifespan has been extended. Now we should cultivate even more wholesome deeds. What wholesome deeds should we cultivate? We should be filial to our parents and respect our elders.' Then those people will be filial to their parents and respect their elders, and their lifespan will be extended to eighty thousand years.

「万岁时人复作是念：『我等由修善故，寿命延长，今者宁可更增少善，何善可修？当行正见，不生颠倒。』于是，其人尽行正见，不起颠倒，寿命延长至二万岁。二万岁时人复作是念：『我等由修善故，寿命延长，今者宁可更增少善，何善可修？当灭三不善法，一者非法婬，二者非法贪，三者邪见。』于是，其人尽灭三不善法，寿命延长至四万岁。四万岁时人复作是念：『我等由修善故，寿命延长，今者宁可更增少善，何善可修？当孝养父母，敬事师长。』于是，其人即孝养父母，敬事师长，寿命延长至八万岁。

The people of eighty thousand years will have girls who are five hundred years old before they are married. At that time, people will have nine kinds of illnesses: cold, heat, hunger, thirst, defecation, urination, desire, gluttony, and old age. At that time, this great earth will be level and smooth, without ditches, pits, thorns, or brambles, and there will be no mosquitoes, flies, snakes, or poisonous insects. The stones and sand will turn into crystal, and the people will be prosperous, with abundant and cheap grains, and boundless happiness. At that time, there will be eighty thousand great cities, with villages and towns close to each other, where the crowing of roosters can be heard. At that time, there will be a Buddha who appears in the world, named Maitreya Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, perfect in all ten titles, just as the present Tathāgata is perfect in all ten titles. He will personally realize and attain in this world, among the gods, Śakra, Brahmā, Māra, or Māra's followers, monks, brahmins, gods, and humans, just as I have personally realized and attained in this world, among the gods, Śakra, Brahmā, Māra, or Māra's followers, monks, brahmins, gods, and humans. He will teach the Dharma, with good beginning, middle, and end, complete in meaning and taste, and pure in the practice of the holy life, just as I teach the Dharma today, with good beginning, middle, and end, true in meaning and taste, and pure in the practice of the holy life. He will have countless millions of disciples, just as I have hundreds of disciples today. At that time, the people will call his disciples 'Sons of Compassion,' just as my disciples are called 'Sons of Śākya.'

「八万岁时人，女年五百岁始出行嫁。时，人当有九种病，一者寒，二者热，三者饥，四者渴，五者大便，六者小便，七者欲，八者饕餮，九者老。时，此大地坦然平整，无有沟坑、丘墟、荆棘，亦无蚊、虻、蛇、蚖、毒虫，瓦石、沙砾变成琉] cf.【丽】璃，人民炽盛，五谷平贱，丰乐无极。是时，当起八万大城，村城隣比，鸡鸣相闻。当于尔时，有佛出世，名为弥勒如来、至真、等正觉，十号具足，如今如来十号具足，彼于诸天、释、梵、魔、若魔、天、诸沙门、婆罗门、诸天、世人中，自身作证，亦如我今于诸天、释、梵、魔、若魔、天、沙门、婆罗门、诸天、世人中，自身作证。彼当说法，初言亦善，中下亦善，义味具足，净修梵行，如我今日说法，上中下言，皆悉真正，义味具足，梵行清净。彼众弟子有无数千万，如我今日弟子数百。彼时，人民称其弟子号曰慈子，如我弟子号曰释子。

"At that time, there will be a king named Śikhin, a kṣatriya anointed on the head, a wheel-turning sage king, ruling the four continents, governing with the right Dharma, with all submitting without resistance. He will be endowed with the seven treasures: the golden wheel treasure, the white elephant treasure, the dark horse treasure, the divine jewel treasure, the jade woman treasure, the householder treasure, and the military commander treasure. The king will have a thousand sons, brave and heroic, able to repel foreign enemies. The four directions will respect and obey, without the need for military force, naturally enjoying peace. At that time, the sage king will erect a great jeweled banner, sixteen yojanas in circumference, a thousand yojanas in height, adorned with a thousand kinds of colors. The banner will have a hundred branches, each branch having a hundred twigs, woven with jeweled threads, interspersed with various treasures. Then, the sage king will destroy this banner and offer it to śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, and the poor in the country. Only then will he shave his beard and hair, put on the three Dharma robes, leave home to cultivate the path, cultivate the unsurpassed practice, personally realize and attain in this present life, with the end of birth and death, the establishment of the holy life, the accomplishment of what is to be done, and not accepting future existence."

「彼时，有王名曰儴伽，刹利水浇头种转轮圣王，典四天下，以正法治，莫不靡伏，七宝具足，一金轮宝、二白象宝、三绀马宝、四神珠宝，五玉女宝、六居士宝、七主兵宝。王有千子，勇猛雄烈，能却外敌，四方敬顺，不加兵杖，自然太平。尔时，圣王建大宝幢，围十六寻，上高千寻，千种杂色严饰其幢；幢有百觚，觚有百枝，宝缕织成，众宝间厕。于是，圣王坏此幢已，以施沙门、婆罗门、国中贫者，然后剃除须发，服三法衣，出家修道，修无上行，于现法中自身作证，生死已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不受后有。」

"The Buddha told the monks, "You should diligently cultivate good practices. By cultivating good practices, your lifespan will be extended, your complexion will improve, you will be peaceful and happy, your wealth and treasures will be abundant, and your power and influence will be complete. It is like the kings who follow the old Dharma of the wheel-turning sage king, and their lifespan is extended, their complexion improves, they are peaceful and happy, their wealth and treasures are abundant, and their power and influence are complete. The monks are also like this. They should cultivate good dharmas, and their lifespan will be extended, their complexion will improve, they will be peaceful and happy, their wealth and treasures will be abundant, and their power and influence will be complete.

佛告诸比丘：「汝等当勤修善行，以修善行，则寿命延长，颜色增益，安隐快乐，财宝丰饶，威力具足，犹如诸王顺行转轮圣王旧法，则寿命延长，颜色增益，安隐快乐，财宝丰饶，威力具足。比丘亦如是，当修善法，寿命延长，颜色增益，安隐快乐，财宝丰饶，威力具足。

How do monks extend their lifespan? Such monks cultivate the concentration of desire, diligently and tirelessly, extinguishing the practice and accomplishing the divine feet; cultivating the concentration of vigor, intention, and contemplation, diligently and tirelessly, extinguishing the practice and accomplishing the divine feet. This is called extending one's lifespan. What is meant by monks improving their complexion? Here, monks are fully endowed with precepts and discipline, and accomplish dignified conduct. Seeing even minor faults, they give rise to great fear and equally learn all precepts, fully and completely. This is called improving the complexion of monks. What is meant by monks being peaceful and happy? Here, monks cut off desire and remove unwholesome dharmas, with perception and contemplation, joy and happiness arising from separation, practicing the first dhyāna; eliminating perception and contemplation, internally joyful and delighted, concentrating the mind, without perception or contemplation, joy and happiness arising from concentration, practicing the second dhyāna; abandoning joy and guarding the mind, not being distracted, knowing the happiness of the body, sought by the sages, protecting and practicing with joy, practicing the third dhyāna; abandoning suffering and happiness, first eliminating joy and sorrow, neither suffering nor happy, protecting and contemplating purity, practicing the fourth dhyāna. This is called the peaceful and happy state of monks.

「云何比丘寿命延长？如是比丘修习欲定，精勤不懈，灭行成就，以修神足；修精进定、意定、思惟定，精勤不懈，灭行成就，以修神足，是为寿命延长。何谓比丘颜色增益？于是比丘戒律具足，成就威仪，见有小罪，生大怖畏，等学诸戒，周满备悉，是为比丘颜色增益。何谓比丘安隐快乐？于是比丘断除婬欲，去不善法，有觉、有观，离生喜、乐，行第一禅；除灭觉、观，内信欢悦，捡心专一，无觉、无观，定生喜、乐，行第二禅；舍喜守护，专心不乱，自知身乐，贤圣所求，护念、乐行，行第三禅；舍灭苦乐，先除忧喜，不苦不乐，护念清净，行第四禅，是为比丘安隐快乐。

"What is meant by monks being rich in wealth and treasures? Here, monks cultivate the mind of loving-kindness, filling one direction, and the same for the other directions, pervading everywhere, boundless, removing all grudges and hatred, with no ill-will in their minds, peacefully and gently enjoying themselves. The same is true for the minds of compassion, joy, and equanimity. This is called being rich in wealth and treasures of monks. What is meant by monks being fully endowed with power and influence? Here, monks truly know the noble truth of suffering, and the same for the truths of origin, cessation, and the path. This is called being fully endowed with power and influence of monks."

「何谓比丘财宝丰饶？于是比丘修习慈心，遍满一方，余方亦尔，周遍广普，无二无量，除众结恨，心无嫉恶，静默慈柔，以自娱乐，悲、喜、舍心亦复如是，是为比丘财宝丰饶。何谓比丘威力具足？于是比丘如实知苦圣谛，习、尽、道谛亦如实知，是为比丘威力具足。」

The Buddha told the monks, "I now look everywhere and see that there is no one more powerful than the power of Mara. However, a monk who has exhausted the taints can overcome him." At that time, the monks heard what the Buddha said and joyfully practiced it.

佛告比丘：「我今遍观诸有力者无过魔力，然漏尽比丘力能胜彼。」尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
