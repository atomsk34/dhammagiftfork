DĀ 8

Dīrghāgama 长阿含经

Chapter 2 第二分

8 （八）散陀那经

Parallel to: DN 25 / MĀ 104

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was at the seven-leaf tree cave in the Vihāra Mountain in Rājagṛha, together with a great assembly of 1,250 bhikṣus.

一时，佛在罗阅祇毗诃罗山七叶树窟，与大比丘众千二百五十人俱。

At that time, there was a householder in Rājagṛha named Santāna, who liked to go sightseeing. He would leave the city every day to visit the World-Honored One. As the householder looked up at the time, he silently thought to himself, "Now is not the right time to go see the Buddha. The World-Honored One must be in a quiet room in samādhi contemplation. The assembly of bhikṣus must also be in meditative stillness. I should now go to the forest of the brāhman woman Upavāṇā. When the time comes, I will go to the World-Honored One to pay respects and ask about his well-being, and also go to the bhikṣus to pay respects and ask about their well-being."

时，王舍城有一居士，名散陀那，好行游观，日日出城，至世尊所。时，彼居士仰观日时，默自念言：「今往觐佛，非是时也，今者世尊必在静室三昧思惟，诸比丘众亦当禅静，我今宁可往诣乌暂婆利梵志女林中，须日时到，当诣世尊，礼敬问讯，并诣诸比丘所，致敬问讯。」

At that time, in the forest of the brāhman woman, there was a brāhman named Nigrodha, who was staying in the forest with five hundred brāhman youths. At that time, the assembly of brāhmans gathered in one place, loudly discussing various things that obstructed the path and caused confusion. They spent the whole day discussing matters of the country, battles and weapons, the affairs of the state, ministers and commoners, chariots, horses, and outings to parks, seats, clothing, food and drink, women, mountains, seas, turtles and tortoises. They only spoke such discussions that obstructed the path, and spent the whole day like this.

时，梵志女林中有一梵志，名尼俱陀，与五百梵志子俱止彼林。时，诸梵志众聚一处，高声大论，俱说遮道浊乱之言，以此终日。或论国事，或论战鬪兵杖之事，或论国家义和之事，或论大臣及庶民事，或论车马游园林事，或论坐席、衣服、饮食、妇女之事，或论山海龟鼈之事，但说如是遮道之论，以此终日。

At that time, the brāhman saw the householder Santāna coming from afar, and immediately ordered his assembly to be silent, saying, "The reason is that a disciple of the śramaṇa Gautama is coming from outside. Among the śramaṇa Gautama's lay disciples, he is the foremost. He will definitely come here. You should be silent." At that time, the brāhmans all fell silent.

时，彼梵志遥见散陀那居士来，即勅其众，令皆静默：「所以然者？彼沙门瞿昙弟子今从外来，沙门瞿昙白衣弟子中，此为最上，彼必来此，汝宜静默。」时，诸梵志各自默然。

The householder Santāna went to where the brāhman was, exchanged greetings, and sat to one side. He said to the brāhman, "My teacher, the World-Honored One, always enjoys tranquility and does not like busyness. It is not like you and your disciples staying among people, loudly discussing various things that obstruct the path and are of no benefit.

散陀那居士至梵志所，问讯已，一面坐，语梵志曰：「我师世尊常乐闲静，不好愦閙，不如汝等与诸弟子处在人中，高声大论，但说遮道无益之言。」

The brāhman again said to the householder, "Has the śramaṇa Gautama ever had a discussion with others? How can people know that the śramaṇa has great wisdom? Your teacher always enjoys solitude in the borderlands, like a blind cow eating grass, following what it sees. Your teacher Gautama is also like this, preferring solitude and enjoying being alone. If your teacher comes, we will call him a blind cow. He always claims to have great wisdom. I will exhaust him with one word, making him silent like a turtle hiding its six limbs. I will say, 'It is fine,' and shoot him with one arrow, leaving no place to escape."

梵志又语居士言：「沙门瞿昙颇曾与人共言论不？众人何由得知沙门有大智慧。汝师常好独处边地，犹如瞎牛食草，偏逐所见，汝师瞿昙亦复如是，偏好独见，乐无人处；汝师若来，吾等当称以为瞎牛。彼常自言有大智慧，我以一言穷彼，能使默然如龟藏六，谓可无患，以一箭射，使无逃处。」

At that time, the World-Honored One was in a quiet room. He heard with his divine ear the discussion between the brāhman and the householder. He then left the Saptaparṇa Cave and went to the grove of the brāhman Upavāna's daughter. When the brāhman saw the Buddha coming from afar, he instructed his disciples, "You all must be silent. The śramaṇa Gautama wants to come here. You must not rise to welcome him, pay respect and make obeisance, or invite him to sit. Take a separate seat and give it to him to sit on. After he sits, you should ask him, 'Śramaṇa Gautama! From the beginning, what method have you used to teach and instruct your disciples, to attain peaceful and tranquil samādhi, and to cultivate holy life?'"

尔时，世尊在闲静室，以天耳闻梵志居士有如是论，即出七叶树窟，诣乌暂婆利梵志女林。时，彼梵志遥见佛来，勅诸弟子：「汝等皆默，瞿昙沙门欲来至此，汝等慎勿起迎、恭敬礼拜，亦勿请坐，取一别座，与之令坐；彼既坐已，卿等当问：『沙门瞿昙！汝从本来，以何法教训于弟子，得安隐定，净修梵行？』」

At that time, the World-Honored One gradually arrived at the garden. The brāhman did not realize he had risen and gradually welcomed the World-Honored One, saying, "Welcome, Gautama! Welcome, śramaṇa! We have not seen each other for a long time. What is the reason for your coming here now? Please sit down for a moment." At that time, the World-Honored One then sat on the seat. He smiled happily and silently thought to himself, "These foolish people cannot control themselves. They first made a vow but cannot fulfill it. The reason is that it is the Buddha's spiritual power that causes their evil minds to naturally decay and perish."

尔时，世尊渐至彼园，时，彼梵志不觉自起，渐迎世尊，而作是言：「善来，瞿昙！善来，沙门！久不相见，今以何缘而来至此？可前小坐。」尔时，世尊即就其座，嬉怡而笑，默自念言：「此诸愚人不能自专，先立要令，竟不能全。所以然者？是佛神力令彼恶心自然败坏。」

At that time, the householder Saṃdhāna paid respects at the feet of the World-Honored One and sat to one side. The brāhman Nigrodha, after greeting the Buddha, also sat to one side and said to the Buddha, "Śramaṇa Gautama! From the beginning, what method have you used to teach and instruct your disciples, to attain peaceful and tranquil samādhi, and to cultivate holy life?"

时，散陀那居士礼世尊足，于一面坐。尼俱陀梵志问讯佛已，亦一面坐，而白佛言：「沙门瞿昙！从本以来，以何法教训诲弟子，得安隐定，净修梵行？」

The World-Honored One said, "Stop, brāhman! My Dharma is profound and vast. From the beginning, I have taught my disciples to attain peaceful and tranquil states, and to cultivate holy life. It is not something you can reach."

世尊告曰：「且止！梵志！吾法深广，从本以来，诲诸弟子，得安隐处，净修梵行，非汝所及。」

He further said to the brāhman, "Even if your teacher and your disciples practice the Dharma, whether it is pure or impure, I can explain it all."

又告梵志：「正使汝师及汝弟子所行道法，有净不净，我尽能说。」

At that time, the five hundred brāhman disciples each raised their voices and said to each other, "The Śramaṇa Gautama has great power and great spiritual power. When asked about his own meaning, he explains the meaning of others."

时，五百梵志弟子各各举声，自相谓言：「 瞿昙沙门有大威势，有大神力，他问己义，乃开他义。」

At that time, the brāhman Nigrodha said to the Buddha, "Excellent! Gautama! Please explain it in detail."

时，尼俱陀梵志白佛言：「善哉！瞿昙！愿分别之。」

The Buddha told the brāhman, "Listen carefully! I shall explain it for you."

佛告梵志：「谛听！谛听！当为汝说。」

The brāhman replied, "I wish to hear it!"

梵志答言：「愿乐欲闻！」

The Buddha told the brāhman, "What you practice is all lowly and vulgar. You go naked, cover yourself with your hands, do not accept food from a bowl, do not accept food from a dish, do not accept food between two walls, do not accept food between two people, do not accept food between two knives, do not accept food between two dishes, do not accept food from a household where they eat together, do not accept food from a pregnant woman's household, do not accept food if a dog is at the door, do not accept food from a household with many flies, do not accept invited food, do not accept food if someone says they know you first, do not eat fish, do not eat meat, do not drink alcohol, do not eat from two vessels, eat one meal and swallow once, up to seven meals, accept people's additional food, no more than seven additions; or eat once a day, or every two, three, four, five, six, or seven days; or eat fruit, or eat weeds, or eat rice water, or eat sesame and rice, or eat millet, or eat cow dung, or eat deer dung, or eat tree roots, branches, leaves, and fruits, or eat fallen fruit;

佛告梵志：「汝所行者皆为卑陋，离服裸形，以手障蔽，不受瓨食，不受盂食，不受两壁中间食，不受二人中间食，不受两刀中间食，不受两盂中间食，不受共食家食，不受怀姙家食，见狗在门则不受其食，不受多蝇家食，不受请食，他言先识则不受其食；不食鱼，不食肉，不饮酒，不两器食，一餐一咽，至七餐止，受人益食，不过七益；或一日一食，或二日、三日、四日、五日、六日、七日一食；或复食果，或复食莠，或食饭汁，或食麻米，或食稴稻，或食牛粪，或食鹿粪，或食树根、枝叶、果实，或食自落果。

"Or wear clothes, or wear grass robes, or wear tree bark, or cover the body with grass, or wear deer skin, or let the hair grow, or wear woven hair, or wear clothes from a grave, or always raise the hand, or not sit on a bed or mat, or always squat, or shave the head and leave the beard and mustache, or lie on thorns, or lie on fruits and vegetables, or lie naked on cow dung; or bathe three times a day, or bathe three times a night, with countless hardships, tormenting this body. What do you think, Nigrodha? Can such practice be called pure Dharma?"

「或被衣，或披莎衣，或衣树皮，或草襜身，或衣鹿皮，或留头发，或被毛编，或著塜间衣，或有常举手者，或不坐床席，或有常蹲者，或有剃发留髦须者，或有卧荆棘者，或有卧果蓏上者，或有裸形卧牛粪上者；或一日三浴，或有一夜三浴，以无数众苦，苦役此身。云何？尼俱陀！如此行者，可名净法不？」

The brāhman replied, "This Dharma is pure, not impure."

梵志答曰：「此法净，非不净也。」

The Buddha told the brāhman, "You say it is pure, but I will tell you that there is impurity in your pure Dharma."

佛告梵志：「汝谓为净，吾当于汝净法中说有垢秽。」

The brāhman said, "Excellent! Gautama! Please speak of it. I wish to hear it."

梵志曰：「善哉！瞿昙！便可说之，愿乐欲闻。」

The Buddha told the brāhman, "The ascetic always thinks to himself, 'If I practice like this, I will receive offerings, respect, and reverence.' This is impurity. When the ascetic receives offerings, he is attached to them and cannot let go. He does not understand detachment and does not know the way out. This is impurity. When the ascetic sees people coming from afar, they all sit in meditation. When there are no people, they sit or lie down as they please. This is impurity.

佛告梵志：「彼苦行者，常自计念：『我行如此，当得供养恭敬礼事。』是即垢秽。彼苦行者，得供养已，乐著坚固，爱染不舍，不晓远离，不知出要，是为垢秽；彼苦行者，遥见人来，尽共坐禅，若无人时，随意坐卧，是为垢秽。

The ascetic hears others' right views but is unwilling to affirm them. This is impurity. When the ascetic is asked a right question, he is stingy and does not answer. This is impurity. When the ascetic sees someone making offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, he scolds and stops them. This is impurity. When the ascetic sees śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas eating leftovers, he goes to scold them. This is impurity. The ascetic has impure food but is unwilling to give it to others. If he has pure food, he greedily eats it himself, does not see his own faults, and does not know the way out. This is impurity. The ascetic praises himself as good and disparages others. This is impurity. The ascetic commits killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, divisive speech, harsh speech, false speech, idle talk, greed, jealousy, wrong views, and perversion. This is impurity.

「彼苦行者，闻他正义，不肯印可，是为垢秽。彼苦行者，他有正问，恡而不答，是为垢秽；彼苦行者，设见有人供养沙门、婆罗门，则诃止之，是为垢秽；彼苦行者，若见沙门、婆罗门食更生物，就呵责之，是为垢秽；彼苦行者，有不净食，不肯施人，若有净食，贪著自食，不见己过，不知出要，是为垢秽；彼苦行者，自称己善，毁訾他人，是为垢秽；彼苦行者，为杀、盗、婬、两舌、恶口、妄言、绮语、贪取、嫉妬、邪见、颠倒，是为垢秽。

"The ascetic is lazy and forgetful, does not practice meditation, and lacks wisdom, like an animal. This is impurity. The ascetic is arrogant, conceited, and has a sense of superiority. This is impurity. The ascetic has no faith or loyalty, no sincerity, does not uphold pure precepts, cannot diligently accept others' instructions, always associates with bad people, and does not stop doing evil. This is impurity. The ascetic harbors much anger and resentment, likes to be crafty and deceitful, relies on his own views, seeks others' faults, always harbors wrong views, and is together with extreme views. This is impurity. What do you think, Nigantha? Can such a practitioner be said to be pure?"

「彼苦行者，懈堕憙忘，不习禅定，无有智慧，犹如禽兽，是为垢秽；彼苦行者，贵高，憍慢、增上慢，是为垢秽；彼苦行者，无有信义，亦无反复，不持净戒，不能精勤受人训诲，常与恶人以为伴党，为恶不已，是为垢秽；彼苦行者，多怀瞋恨，好为巧伪，自怙己见，求人长短，恒怀邪见，与边见俱，是为垢秽。云何？尼俱陀！如此行者可言净不邪？」

He replied, "It is impure, not pure."

答曰：「是不净，非是净也。」

The Buddha said, "Now I will further explain the pure and undefiled Dharma for you in the midst of your defiled Dharma."

佛言：「今当于汝垢秽法中，更说清净无垢秽法。」

The brahman said, "Please explain it!"

梵志言：「唯愿说之！」

The Buddha said, "The ascetic does not think, 'By practicing like this, I will receive offerings, respect, and reverence.' This is the undefiled Dharma of ascetic practice. When the ascetic receives offerings, his mind is not attached, he understands detachment, and knows the Dharma of liberation. This is the undefiled Dharma of ascetic practice. The ascetic's meditation has a constant method, whether there are people or not, he does not consider it different. This is the undefiled Dharma of ascetic practice. The ascetic joyfully affirms the correct principles of others when he hears them. This is the undefiled Dharma of ascetic practice. The ascetic joyfully explains when others ask him the correct questions. This is the undefiled Dharma of ascetic practice.

佛言：「彼苦行者，不自计念：『我行如是，当得供养恭敬礼事。』是为苦行无垢法也。彼苦行者，得供养已，心不贪著，晓了远离，知出要法，是为苦行无垢法也。彼苦行者，禅有常法，有人、无人，不以为异，是为苦行无垢法也；彼苦行者，闻他正义，欢喜印可，是为苦行无垢法也；彼苦行者，他有正问，欢喜解说，是为苦行离垢法也。

The ascetic, seeing someone making offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, rejoices for them and does not rebuke them. This is the undefiled Dharma of ascetic practice. The ascetic, seeing śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas eating things that are not pure, does not reproach them. This is the undefiled Dharma of ascetic practice. The ascetic, having impure food, does not begrudge it, and if he has pure food, he is not attached to it. He is able to see his own faults and knows the Dharma of liberation. This is the undefiled Dharma of ascetic practice. The ascetic does not praise himself or disparage others. This is the undefiled Dharma of ascetic practice. The ascetic does not kill, steal, engage in sexual misconduct, speak divisively, speak harshly, lie, engage in idle talk, be greedy, jealous, or have wrong views. This is the undefiled Dharma of ascetic practice.

「彼苦行者，设见有人供养沙门、婆罗门，代其欢喜而不呵止，是为苦行离垢法也；彼苦行者，若见沙门、婆罗门食更生之物，不呵责之，是为苦行离垢法也；彼苦行者，有不净食，心不恡惜，若有净食，则不染著，能见己过，知出要法，是为苦行离垢法也；彼苦行者，不自称誉，不毁他人，是为苦行离垢法也；彼苦行者，不杀、盗、婬、两舌、恶口、妄言、绮语、贪取、嫉妬、邪见，是为苦行离垢法也。

"The ascetic diligently practices without forgetting, delights in practicing meditation, cultivates wisdom, and is not foolish like an animal. This is the undefiled Dharma of ascetic practice. The ascetic is not arrogant, haughty, or conceited. This is the undefiled Dharma of ascetic practice. The ascetic always has faith and righteousness, cultivates the practice of returning favors, is able to uphold pure precepts, diligently accepts instruction, always associates with good people, and accumulates goodness without ceasing. This is the undefiled Dharma of ascetic practice. The ascetic does not harbor anger or resentment, is not deceitful, does not rely on his own views, does not seek the faults of others, does not harbor wrong views, and has no extreme views. This is the undefiled Dharma of ascetic practice. What do you think, brāhmaṇa? Are these ascetic practices pure and undefiled dharmas?"

「彼苦行者，精勤不忘，好习禅行，多修智慧，不愚如兽，是为苦行离垢法也；彼苦行者，不为高贵、憍慢、自大，是为苦行离垢法也；彼苦行者，常怀信义，修反复行，能持净戒，勤受训诲，常与善人而为伴党，积善不已，是为苦行离垢法也；彼苦行者，不怀瞋恨，不为巧伪，不恃己见，不求人短，不怀邪见，亦无边见，是为苦行离垢法也。云何？梵志！如是苦行，为是清净离垢法耶？」

He replied, "Yes, they are indeed pure and undefiled dharmas."

答曰：「如是！实是清净离垢法也。」

The brāhmaṇa said to the Buddha, "Is this the highest and most steadfast practice?"

梵志白佛言：「齐有此苦行，名为第一坚固行耶？」

The Buddha said, "No, it is only the skin."

佛言：「未也！始是皮耳。」

The brāhmaṇa said, "Please explain the joints of the tree!"

梵志言：「愿说树节！」

The Buddha told the brāhmaṇa, "You should listen well! I will now explain."

佛告梵志：「汝当善听！吾今当说。」

The brāhmaṇa said, "Yes! I wish to hear it."

梵志言：「唯然！愿乐欲闻。」

The Buddha said, "Brahmin, that ascetic does not kill himself, does not teach others to kill, does not steal himself, does not teach others to steal, does not commit sexual misconduct himself, does not teach others to commit sexual misconduct, does not lie himself, and does not teach others to lie. He fills one direction with loving-kindness, and the other directions are also like this. His loving-kindness is vast, without duality, immeasurable, and without resentment. It fills the world completely. His compassion, joy, and equanimity are also like this. This ascetic practice is called the tree-knot."

「梵志！彼苦行者，自不杀生，不教人杀，自不偷盗，不教人盗，自不邪婬，不教人婬，自不妄语，亦不教人为；彼以慈心遍满一方，余方亦尔，慈心广大，无二无量，无有结恨，遍满世间，悲、喜、舍心，亦复如是，齐此苦行，名为树节。」

The brāhmaṇa said to the Buddha, "Please explain the meaning of the firm ascetic practices!"

梵志白佛言：「愿说苦行坚固之义！」

The Buddha said to the brāhmaṇa, "Listen carefully! I shall explain it to you."

佛告梵志：「谛听！谛听！吾当说之。」

The brāhmaṇa said, "Yes, World-Honored One! I wish to hear it."

梵志曰：「唯然！世尊！愿乐欲闻。」

The Buddha said, "That ascetic practitioner does not kill himself, nor does he teach others to kill. He does not steal himself, nor does he teach others to steal. He does not engage in sexual misconduct himself, nor does he teach others to engage in sexual misconduct. He does not lie himself, nor does he teach others to lie. He fills one direction with a mind of loving-kindness, and the other directions are also like this. His mind of loving-kindness is vast, without duality, immeasurable, and without resentment. It fills the world completely. His mind of compassion, joy, and equanimity is also like this. That ascetic practitioner can recall his past lives in countless eons, one life, two lives, up to countless lives. He sees and knows the formation and destruction of countries and the beginning and end of eons. He also sees and knows, 'I was born in such and such a family name, with such and such a name, eating such and such food, with such and such a lifespan, experiencing such and such suffering and happiness. I was born there and came here, and from here I was born there.' He remembers all these things from countless eons. This is called the firm and indestructible practice of that ascetic practitioner.

佛言：「彼苦行者，自不杀生，教人不杀，自不偷盗，教人不盗，自不邪婬，教人不婬，自不妄语，教人不妄语；彼以慈心遍满一方，余方亦尔，慈心广大，无二无量，无有结恨，遍满世间，悲、喜、舍心，亦复如是。彼苦行者，自识往昔无数劫事，一生、二生，至无数生，国土成败，劫数终始，尽见尽知；又自见知，我曾生彼种姓，如是名字，如是饮食，如是寿命，如是所受苦乐，从彼生此，从此生彼，如是尽忆无数劫事，是为梵志彼苦行者牢固无坏。」

The brāhmaṇa said to the Buddha, "What is the first?"

梵志白佛言：「云何为第一？」

The Buddha said, "Brāhmaṇa, listen carefully! Listen carefully! I shall explain it to you."

佛言：「梵志！谛听！谛听！吾当说之。」

The brāhmaṇa said, "Yes, World-Honored One! I wish to hear it."

梵志言：「唯然！世尊！愿乐欲闻。」

The Buddha said, "That ascetic practitioner does not kill himself, nor does he teach others to kill. He does not steal himself, nor does he teach others to steal. He does not engage in sexual misconduct himself, nor does he teach others to engage in sexual misconduct. He does not lie himself, nor does he deceive others. He fills one direction with a mind of loving-kindness, and the other directions are also like this. His mind of loving-kindness is vast, without duality, immeasurable, and without resentment. It fills the world completely. His mind of compassion, joy, and equanimity is also like this. That ascetic practitioner himself recognizes events from countless eons in the past, from one life, two lives, up to countless lives. He sees and knows the formation and destruction of countries and the beginning and end of eons. He also knows and sees himself, 'I was born in such and such a family name, with such and such a name, eating such and such food, with such and such a lifespan, experiencing such and such suffering and happiness. I was born from there to here, and from here to there.' In this way, he remembers events from countless eons. With his pure divine eye, he observes the various kinds of beings, dying here and being born there, with good or bad complexion, going to good or evil places, falling into the realms of existence according to their actions. He sees and knows all of this. He also knows that sentient beings who engage in unwholesome physical, verbal, and mental actions, who slander the sages, and who have wrong views and inverted views, fall into the three evil paths after their bodies perish and their lives end. He also knows that sentient beings who engage in wholesome physical, verbal, and mental actions, who do not slander the sages, and who see the truth and practice accordingly, are born in the heavens or among humans after their bodies perish and their lives end. The practitioner, with his pure divine eye, sees and knows sentient beings, up to their falling into the realms of existence according to their actions. This is the first supreme excellence of ascetic practice."

佛言：「彼苦行者，自不杀生，教人不杀，自不偷盗，教人不盗，自不邪婬，教人不婬，自不妄语，教人不欺；彼以慈心遍满一方，余方亦尔，慈心广大，无二无量，无有结恨，遍满世间，悲、喜、舍心，亦复如是。彼苦行者，自识往昔无数劫事，一生、二生，至无数生，国土成败，劫数终始，尽见尽知；又自知见，我曾生彼种姓，如是名字、饮食、寿命，如是所经苦乐，从彼生此，从此生彼。如是尽忆无数劫事，彼天眼净观众生类，死此生彼，颜色好丑、善恶所趣，随行所堕，尽见尽知。又知众生身行不善，口行不善，意行不善，诽谤贤圣，信邪倒见，身坏命终，堕三恶道。或有众生身行善，口、意亦善，不谤贤圣，见正信行，身坏命终，生天、人中。行者天眼清净，观见众生，乃至随行所堕，无不见知，是为苦行第一胜也。」

The Buddha said to the brāhmaṇa, "In this Dharma, there is also a superior one. I always use this Dharma to teach the śrāvakas, and they use this Dharma to cultivate the holy life."

佛告梵志：「于此法中复有胜者，我常以此法化诸声闻，彼以此法得修梵行。」

At that time, the five hundred brāhmaṇa disciples all raised their voices and said to each other, "Now we see that the World-Honored One is the most venerable and supreme. Our teacher is not equal to him."

时，五百梵志弟子各大举声，自相谓言：「今观世尊为最尊上，我师不及。」

At that time, the householder Saṃdhāna said to the brāhmaṇa, "You said earlier, 'If Gautama comes, we should call him a blind ox.' Now that the World-Honored One has come, why don't you call him that? Also, you said earlier, 'We should use one word to exhaust Gautama, causing him to be speechless, like a turtle hiding its six limbs, so that there is no worry, and with one arrow, there is no place to escape.' Why don't you use your one word to exhaust the Tathāgata now?"

时，彼散陀那居士语梵志曰：「汝向自言：『瞿昙若来，吾等当称以为瞎牛。』世尊今来，汝何不称？又汝向言：『当以一言穷彼瞿昙，能使默然，如龟藏六，谓可无患，以一箭射，使无逃处。』汝今何不以汝一言穷如来耶？」

The Buddha asked the brāhmaṇa, "Do you remember having said that before?"

佛问梵志：「汝忆先时有是言不？」

He replied, "Indeed!"

答曰：「实有！」

The Buddha said to the brāhmaṇa, "Did you not hear from the elder brāhmaṇas that the Buddhas and Tathāgatas enjoy solitude in the mountains and forests, like my enjoyment of solitude today? Unlike your teachings, which enjoy busyness and speak of meaningless things all day long?"

佛告梵志：「汝岂不从先宿梵志闻诸佛、如来独处山林，乐闲静处，如我今日乐于闲居；不如汝法，乐于愦閙，说无益事，以终日耶？」

The brāhmaṇa said, "I have heard that the Buddhas of the past enjoyed solitude and dwelled alone in the mountains and forests, like the World-Honored One today; unlike my teachings, which enjoy busyness and speak of meaningless things all day long!"

梵志曰：「闻过去诸佛乐于闲静，独处山林，如今世尊；不如我法，乐于愦閙，说无益事，以终日耶！」

The Buddha said to the brāhmaṇa, "Do you not think, 'The Śramaṇa Gautama can speak of Bodhi. He can tame himself and tame others; he can attain peace and make others attain peace; he can reach the other shore and make others reach the other shore; he can attain liberation and liberate others; he can attain parinirvāṇa and cause others to attain parinirvāṇa'?"

佛告梵志：「汝岂不念：『瞿昙沙门能说菩提。自能调伏，能调伏人；自得止息，能止息人；自度彼岸，能使人度；自得解脱，能解脱人；自得灭度，能灭度人？』」

At that time, the brāhmaṇa immediately rose from his seat, bowed his head to the Buddha's feet, and called his own name, saying, "I am the brāhmaṇa Nigrodha! I am the brāhmaṇa Nigrodha! Now I take refuge and pay homage at the feet of the World-Honored One."

时，彼梵志即从座起，头面作礼，手扪佛足，自称己名曰：「我是尼俱陀梵志！我是尼俱陀梵志！今者自归，礼世尊足。」

The Buddha said to the brāhmaṇa, "Stop! Stop! Just stay! If you understand, it is already paying homage."

佛告梵志：「止！止！且住！使汝心解，便为礼敬。」

At that time, the brāhmaṇa again bowed at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side.

时，彼梵志重礼佛足，在一面坐。

The Buddha said to the brāhmaṇa, "Do you think that the Buddha teaches the Dharma for the sake of material gain? Do not think like that! If there is material gain, I will give it all to you. The Dharma I teach is the most subtle, for the sake of eliminating unwholesome and increasing wholesome dharmas."

佛告梵志：「汝将无谓佛为利养而说法耶？勿起是心！若有利养，尽以施汝。吾所说法，微妙第一，为灭不善，增益善法。」

He further said to the brāhmaṇa, "Do you think that the Buddha teaches the Dharma for the sake of fame, respect, leadership, followers, or the assembly? Do not think like that! Now your followers all belong to you. The Dharma I teach is for the sake of eliminating unwholesome and increasing wholesome dharmas."

又告梵志：「汝将无谓佛为名称，为尊重故，为导首故，为眷属故，为大众故，而说法耶？勿起此心！今汝眷属尽属于汝。我所说法，为灭不善，增长善法。」

He further said to the brāhmaṇa, "Do you think that the Buddha puts you in the group of unwholesome and dark dharmas? Do not think like that! You should abandon all unwholesome and dark dharmas, and I will teach you the pure and wholesome dharmas for you."

又告梵志：「汝将无谓佛以汝置不善聚、黑冥聚中耶？勿生是心！诸不善聚及黑冥聚汝但舍去，吾自为汝说善净法。」

He further said to the brāhmaṇa, "Do you think that the Buddha expels you from the group of wholesome and pure dharmas? Do not think like that! You should diligently practice within the group of wholesome and pure dharmas. I will teach you the pure and wholesome dharmas for you, to eliminate unwholesome practices and increase wholesome dharmas."

又告梵志：「汝将无谓佛黜汝于善法聚、清白聚耶？勿起是心！汝但于善法聚、清白聚中精勤修行，吾自为汝说善净法，灭不善行，增益善法。」

At that time, the five hundred brāhmaṇa disciples all focused their minds and listened attentively to the Buddha's teachings. Then Māra Pāpīyas thought, "These five hundred brāhmaṇa disciples are focusing their minds and listening to the Dharma from the Buddha. I should go now and disturb their minds." At that time, the evil Māra immediately used his own power to disturb their minds. At that time, the World-Honored One said to Saṃdhāna, "These five hundred brāhmaṇa disciples are focusing their minds and listening to the Dharma from me. Māra Pāpīyas is disturbing their minds. Now I wish to return. You may go together." At that time, the World-Honored One took the householder Saṃdhāna with his right hand and placed him in his palm, and returned through the air.

尔时，五百梵志弟子皆端心正意，听佛所说。时，魔波旬作此念言：「此五百梵志弟子端心正意，从佛听法，我今宁可往坏其意。」尔时，恶魔即以己力坏乱其意。尔时，世尊告散陀那曰：「此五百梵志子端心正意，从我听法，天魔波旬坏乱其意，今吾欲还，汝可俱去。」尔时，世尊以右手接散陀那居士置掌中，乘虚而归。

At that time, the householder Saṃdhāna, the brāhmaṇa Nigaṇṭha, and the five hundred brāhmaṇa disciples, having heard the Buddha's teachings, joyfully received and practiced them.

时，散陀那居士、尼俱陀梵志及五百梵志子闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
