EĀ² 2.11

Ekottarikāgama 2nd佛说七处三观经

EA2 11（一一）四正断

Parallel to: AN 4.14 / SĀ 879

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

Once, the Buddha was in the country of Shravasti, staying at Jetavana Anathapindika's Park. The Buddha then addressed the bhikshus, saying, "There are four kinds of shelters. What are the four? First, the shelter of the shelter; second, the shelter of guarding; third, the shelter of protection; fourth, the shelter of practice. What is the shelter of the shelter? It means to be mindful of the coming and not to accept or listen to the shelter of ignorance and delusion, or to be mindful of anger and not to accept or listen to the shelter of ignorance and delusion. This is called the shelter of the shelter. What is the shelter of guarding? It means that when the eyes have seen forms, one does not accept characteristics or observe characteristics. If one sees evil arising from causes and conditions, or if one sees delusion, unwholesomeness, or unpleasantness arising from causes and conditions, one should then guard and practice merit, guarding the eyes, ears, nose, tongue, body, and mind. It means to not accept characteristics according to the Dharma, as explained above. This is called the shelter of guarding. What is the shelter of protection? It means that if a bhikshu has already given rise to various good characteristics here, such as redness, bloating, being half-eaten by foxes and dogs, blood flowing red, bluish-black decay, white bones, or a skull, one should carefully observe them and well protect the mind so that it does not lose the good characteristics. This is called the shelter of protection. What is the shelter of practice? It means that if a bhikshu practices the awakening mind, it is because of separation, distinction, and distance. In this way, one reaches the contemplation of the awakening mind. This is called the shelter of practice." The following is a summary:

一时，佛在舍卫国，行在祇树给孤独园。佛便告比丘：「有四舍。何等为四？一者为舍舍，二为守舍，三为护舍，四为行舍。何等为舍舍者？念来不受不声舍晓离远，若以瞋恚亦从欺不声舍晓离远，是名为舍舍。何等为守舍者？眼已见色。不受相不观相，若从因缘见恶生。若从因缘见痴，若从因缘见不可意，若从因缘见弊恶意起。便自守受行福，守眼耳鼻口身意。为如法不受相，如上说，是名为守舍。何等为护舍？是间比丘比丘已生所非一善相，若红汁膖胀，若狐犬半食，若血流赤，若青黑腐，若骨白，若髑髅，熟谛视视，善护令意莫失善相，是名为护舍。何等为行舍？是间比丘比丘觉意行离故，别分故，分别远故，如是到至观觉意，是名为行舍。」从后说绝：

"The shelter of guarding and the shelter of protection and the shelter of practice, these are called the four shelters. The true teaching is like this. The wise practice this without stopping, in order to end suffering and attain the path."

「守舍亦护亦行，是名为四舍，谛说如是，贤者行是不中止，为尽苦得道。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The disciples arose, paid homage to the Buddha, and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是，弟子起礼佛受行。
