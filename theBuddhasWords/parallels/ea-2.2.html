EĀ² 2.2

Ekottarikāgama 2nd佛说七处三观经

EA2 2（二）

Parallel to: AN 3.109

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

Once, the Buddha was in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove. At that time, the Buddha was dwelling there. When the Buddha arrived, he bowed to the Buddha and then sat down. Having sat down, he asked the Buddha, "What is it to not guard the body? What is it to not guard the voice? What is it to not guard the mind?"

一时，佛在舍卫国，行在祇树给孤独园。是时处到佛已，到佛礼便坐，已坐问佛：「何等不守不守身？何等不守不守口声？何等不守不守意？」

The Buddha then said, "Anāthapiṇḍada, if one does not guard the body, one will not be able to guard the voice, and one will not be able to guard the mind. If one does not guard oneself, one will not be able to guard the body, one will not be able to guard the voice, and one will not be able to guard the mind. If one does not guard the body, voice, and mind, one will become corrupt. If one becomes corrupt in body, voice, and mind, one will not be good. One's death will not be good, one's rebirth will not be good, and one's dwelling will not be good. It is like, householder, if a house or hall is not covered, and if rain comes, the rafters will be soaked, the beams will be soaked, and the walls will be soaked. If they become soaked, the walls will rot, the beams will rot, and the rafters will rot. It is like this. Householder, if one does not guard one's mind, one will not be able to guard the body, one will not be able to guard the voice, and one will not be able to guard the mind. If one does not guard the body, voice, and mind, one will not be good. One's death will not be good, one's rebirth will not be good, and one's dwelling will not be good."

佛便说：「给孤独家意不守身行，亦不得守口声行，亦不得守心行，亦不守己行，不守身己行，不守口声己行，不守心身行便腐，声说便腐，心行亦腐；已腐身行声行心行便不善，死亦不善受亦不善处。譬喻迦罗越！若楼若堂屋不覆，若使雨来，柞亦渍椽亦渍壁亦渍，已渍壁亦腐椽亦腐柞亦腐，譬如是。迦罗越！己意不守，身行亦不守，口行亦不守，念行亦不守，己意声身不守便不善，死便不善受便不善处。」

The householder then asked the Buddha, "What is it to guard the body? What is it to guard the voice? What is it to guard the mind?"

迦罗越便问佛：「何等为守令得身守？何等为守令得口守？何等为守令得念守？」

The Buddha told the householder, "If one guards the mind, one will be able to guard the body, voice, and mind. If one guards the body, voice, and mind, one will not become corrupt. If one does not become corrupt in body, voice, and mind, one will be good. One's death will be good, one's rebirth will be good, and one's dwelling will be good. It is like, householder, if a house or hall is covered, and if rain comes, the rafters will not be soaked, the beams will not be soaked, and the walls will not be soaked. If they do not become soaked, the walls will not rot, the beams will not rot, and the rafters will not rot. It is like this. If one guards the mind, one will be able to guard the body, voice, and mind. If one guards the body, voice, and mind, one will be good. One's death will be good, one's rebirth will be good, and one's dwelling will be good."

佛告迦罗越：「意已守，身口念索守，己身守己口守己念守，便身不腐便声不腐便念不腐，已不腐身行，已不腐声行，已不腐念行，死时得善死得善受得善处。譬喻，迦罗越！若楼若堂屋上覆盖，若便雨来，柞亦不渍椽亦不渍壁亦不渍，已不渍壁亦不腐椽亦不腐柞亦不腐，如是譬喻。意以守，身亦守口亦守，己意身口守，便死时善死便善受持便得善处。」

The Buddha then spoke the following verses:

佛从后说绝：

"Those who do not guard their minds are deluded and sleep, and thus the demons gain control. Therefore, one should guard the mind. If one wishes to practice the truth, one should practice seeing the truth. One should know how to emerge from within, not fall into sleep, and thus be able to cut off the root of suffering."

「不守意者，邪疑故，亦睡眠故，魔便得自在。如是但当守意，若欲谛行但当见谛行，亦当知内出已，不堕睡眠，便得断苦本。」

The Buddha spoke thus.

佛说如是。
