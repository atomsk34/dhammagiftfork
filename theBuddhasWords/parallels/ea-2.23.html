EĀ² 2.23

Ekottarikāgama 2nd佛说七处三观经

EA2 23（二三）

Parallel to: AN 5.250

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

Once, the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. The Buddha then addressed the bhikṣus, saying, "There are five afflictions that arise from relying on others. What are the five? If a bhikṣu relies on others, sometimes he may have faults that the bhikṣu saṃgha does not want to see. If he goes out to rely on others, he may think of the one he relies on, and the bhikṣu saṃgha may then go out and not want to see him. He may then become attached and not want to go to the bhikṣu saṃgha, thinking, 'Why should I go to the bhikṣu saṃgha again?' He may then stop going. Having stopped going, he may no longer see the bhikṣu saṃgha. Having no longer seen the bhikṣu saṃgha, he may no longer hear the Dharma. Having no longer heard the Dharma, he may fall away from the Dharma. Having fallen away from the Dharma, he may no longer be in the Dharma. This is the first affliction that arises from relying on others. Second, there may be a bhikṣu who is loved by others, but the one who is loved may also have faults. If he goes to the bhikṣu saṃgha, he may be given the lowest seat. The one who loves him may think, 'The one I love is given the lowest seat by the bhikṣu saṃgha. I will not go to the bhikṣu saṃgha again.' The bhikṣu saṃgha may then give him the lowest seat. He may then not go to the bhikṣu saṃgha again. The middle part is also explained as above. Third, he may go to another country with his alms bowl and robe. Fourth, he may abandon the precepts and become a layperson. Fifth, he may sit alone and be sorrowful, also as explained above."

一时，佛在舍卫国，行在祇树给孤独园。佛便告比丘：「有五恼，人人相依可。何等为五？若比丘人人相依可已，有时依有过，便比丘僧不欲见；出便相依者，念所我相依者，比丘僧便出不欲见。便爱著意不欲至比丘聚，我何以当复至比丘聚，便不复行；已聚不复行，便不复见比丘聚；已不复见比丘聚，便不闻法；已不闻法便不堕法，便离法便不在法，是比丘第一恼人人相依。二者亦有比丘若人所爱者，所爱者人亦有犯过便比丘聚，便有过者最著下坐，便爱者意计我所爱者，为比丘聚最著下坐，我不复为至比丘聚，为比丘聚最著下坐，我不复为至比丘聚，中亦如上说。三者持钵袈裟至他国。四者弃戒受白衣。五者自坐愁失名，亦如上说。」

Thus have I heard:

佛说如是。
