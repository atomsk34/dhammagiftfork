EĀ² 2.42

Ekottarikāgama 2nd佛说七处三观经

EA2 42（四二）

Parallel to: AN 3.76

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

Once, the Buddha was staying at Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī. At that time, Venerable Ānanda went to where the Buddha was, bowed to the Buddha, and then asked the Buddha, "What is the world? What is meant by the world?"

一时，佛在舍卫国，行在祇树给孤独园。时贤者阿难行至佛，已到礼佛，便白问佛：「世间世何等为世？说是何等为世？」

"Ānanda, the world is threefold: the world of desire, the world of form, and the formless world. If one commits offenses in this world, it is called the world. Ānanda, if there were no offenses to be committed in the world of desire, there would be no world of desire. Ānanda, the Buddha's teaching is not apart from this."

「是世，阿难！为三，一为欲世，二为色世，三为不色世。亦若人所致罪令复得，是名为世。若阿难！欲致罪无有，欲世亦无有。阿难！报佛不离是。」

The Buddha then said to Ānanda, "Ānanda, offenses are the basis of consciousness, the cause of desire, craving, ignorance, and darkness. Those who are ignorant and without eyes engage in evil conduct. Due to evil, consciousness is established in evil, and one falls into the world of desire. Ānanda, if one who practices form does not attain the world of form, there is no world of form."

佛便告阿难：「是阿难！罪为地识，为种欲，为爱痴，为冥，已痴人无有眼，便恶行，已恶便识在恶，堕欲世。若阿难！色行人不致色，世亦无有。」

The Buddha again asked Ānanda, "Is there non-attainment?"

佛复重告阿难：「不致是有不？」

Ānanda then said to the Buddha, "It is not apart from this."

阿难便白佛：「不离是。」

The Buddha again told Ānanda, "The consciousness of the stage of practice is the cause of desire, love, delusion, and darkness. Delusion and darkness are the middle. Consciousness of practice is then in the middle. This is the existence of the form world. Ānanda! Because there is merit of formless practice, there is the formless world. If there were no formless practice, there would also be no formless world."

佛复告阿难：「是行地识，为种欲为爱痴为冥，痴冥为中，行识便在中止，是为色世有。阿难！不色行福故有不色世，若无有不色行，亦无有不色。」

Ānanda said to the Buddha, "It is not apart from this."

阿难白佛：「不离是。」

"From this practice, Ānanda! It becomes a field of merit. When consciousness, desire, love, delusion, and darkness are already present, a person has delusion and then no eyes. Since the eyes are not fully developed, there is no form. The upper consciousness is then called the formless world."

「从是行，阿难！便为福地，识种欲爱痴冥已，人有痴便无有眼，已不大了眼故，为无有色，上识便名为无有色世。」

The Buddha spoke thus.

佛说如是。
