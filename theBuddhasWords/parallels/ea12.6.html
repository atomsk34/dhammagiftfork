EĀ 12.6

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

12.6 （六）

Parallel to: SN 16.5 / SĀ 1141 / SĀ² 2.116 / EĀ 41.5

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at the Karanda Bamboo Grove in the city of Rajagriha, together with a great assembly of five hundred bhikshus.

一时，佛在罗阅城迦兰陀竹园所，与大比丘众五百人俱。

At that time, Venerable Mahakasyapa was dwelling in a forest hermitage. He begged for alms at mealtime, without discriminating between rich and poor. He ate in one place and did not move. He sat under a tree or in the open, wore rag robes, or carried the three robes. He stayed in charnel grounds, ate only one meal a day, or ate at noon. He practiced austerities and was old and tall. At that time, after Venerable Mahakasyapa had eaten, he went to sit in meditation under a tree. After meditating, he rose from his seat, adjusted his robes, and went to where the World-Honored One was.

尔时，尊者大迦叶住阿练若，到时乞食，不择贫富，一处一坐，终不移易，树下，露坐，或空闲处，著五纳衣，或持三衣，或在冢间，或时一食，或正中食，或行头陀，年高长大。尔时，尊者大迦叶食后，便诣一树下禅定。禅定已，从坐起，整衣服，往至世尊所。

At that time, the World-Honored One saw Kasyapa coming from afar and said, "Welcome, Kasyapa!"

是时，世尊遥见迦叶来，世尊告曰：「善来，迦叶！」

Then Kasyapa went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side.

时，迦叶便至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。

The World-Honored One said, "Kasyapa! You are now old and your aspirations are declining. You should now give up begging for food and all the ascetic practices. You can also accept invitations from wealthy householders and receive their robes."

世尊告曰：「迦叶！汝今年高长大，志衰朽弊。汝今可舍乞食，乃至诸头陀行，亦可受诸长者请，并受衣裳。」

Kasyapa replied, "I do not follow the Tathāgata's instruction. The reason is that if the Tathāgata does not attain supreme, perfect enlightenment, I will become a pratyekabuddha. Those pratyekabuddhas all practice araṇya, beg for food at the right time, not discriminating between the poor and the wealthy, sit in one place without moving, under a tree, in the open, or in an empty and quiet place, wearing five-piece robes, or carrying three robes, in a charnel ground, eating once a day, or eating at noon, or practicing austerities. I now dare not abandon my original practices and learn other practices."

迦叶对曰：「我今不从如来教。所以然者，若当如来不成无上正真道者，我则成辟支佛。然彼辟支佛尽行阿练若，到时乞食，不择贫富，一处一坐，终不移易，树下，露坐，或空闲处，著五纳衣，或持三衣，或在冢间，或时一食，或正中食，或行头陀。如今不敢舍本所习，更学余行。」

The World-Honored One said, "Excellent! Excellent! Kasyapa! You will benefit many, liberate countless people, and widely liberate all, including gods and humans. The reason is that, Kasyapa! If these ascetic practices exist in the world, my Dharma will also remain in the world for a long time. If the Dharma remains in the world, it will increase the heavenly path and reduce the three evil paths. It will also establish the three vehicles of stream-enterer, once-returner, and non-returner, all existing in the world. All bhikṣus should learn like Kasyapa's practice. Thus, bhikṣus! You should train like this."

世尊告曰：「善哉！善哉！迦叶！多所饶益，度人无量，广及一切，天、人得度。所以然者，若，迦叶！此头陀行在世者，我法亦当久在于世。设法在世，益增天道，三恶道便减，亦成须陀洹、斯陀含、阿那含三乘之道，皆存于世。诸比丘！所学皆当如迦叶所习。如是，诸比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
