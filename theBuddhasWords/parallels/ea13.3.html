EĀ 13.3

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

13.3 （三）竹膊

Parallel to: MN 87 / MĀ 216

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, in the city of Śrāvastī, there was an elder whose only son had just died. He loved and cherished him so much that he could never let go. Seeing his son dead, he became insane, wandering around without stopping in one place. When he saw people, he would ask, "Has anyone seen my child?"

尔时，于舍卫城内，有一长者，适丧一子，甚爱敬念，未曾能舍。彼见子死，便生狂惑，周旋往来，不停一处。若见人时，便作是语：「颇有见我儿乎？」

Gradually, he went to the Jetavana Monastery and came to where the World-Honored One was. He stood to one side and said to the World-Honored One, "Śramaṇa Gautama! Have you seen my child?"

尔时，彼人渐渐往至祇洹精舍，到世尊所，在一面住。尔时，彼人白世尊曰：「瞿昙沙门！颇见我儿乎？」

The World-Honored One said to the elder, "Why do you have an unhappy countenance and confused faculties?"

世尊告长者曰：「何故颜貌不悦，诸根错乱？」

At that time, the elder replied to Gautama, "How could it not be so? The reason is that I now have only one child, who has left me and passed away. I deeply loved and cherished him, never leaving him out of sight; out of compassion for my child, it has caused me to go mad. I now ask the śramaṇa, have you seen my child?"

尔时，长者报瞿昙曰：「焉得不尔。所以然者，我今唯有一子，舍我无常。甚爱敬念，未曾离目前；哀愍彼子，故令我生狂。我今问沙门，见我儿耶？」

The World-Honored One said, "Indeed, elder! As you ask, birth, old age, sickness, and death are the common laws of the world; the suffering of separation from loved ones and the suffering of encountering those we dislike - your child has left you and passed away, how could you not think of him?"

世尊告曰：「如是，长者！如汝所问，生、老、病、死，世之常法；恩爱离苦、怨憎会苦，子舍汝无常，岂得不念乎。」

At that time, that person, upon hearing what the World-Honored One said, did not accept it and left. Proceeding further, he saw another person and again asked, "The śramaṇa Gautama said, 'Through separation from loved ones, there is happiness.' Is what the śramaṇa said true?"

尔时，彼人闻世尊所说，不入其怀，便舍而退去。前行见人，复作是语：「沙门瞿昙说言曰：『恩爱分别，便有快乐。』如沙门所说，为审尔不？」

The other person replied, "How can there be happiness in separation from loved ones?"

前人对曰：「恩爱别离，有何乐哉？」

At that time, not far from Śrāvastī, there were many people playing dice. The person then thought, "These men are wise and knowledgeable, there is nothing they do not know. I will now ask them about this meaning." He then went to where they were playing dice and asked the people, "The śramaṇa Gautama told me, 'The suffering of separation from loved ones and the suffering of encountering enemies, this is happiness.' What do you people think about this?"

当于尔时，去舍卫城不远，有众多人而共博戏。尔时，彼人便作是念：「此诸男子聪明智慧，无事不知，我今当以此义问彼诸人。」尔时，即诣博戏所，问众人曰：「沙门瞿昙向我说曰：『恩爱别离苦、怨憎会苦，此者快乐。』诸人等今于意云何？」

At that time, the dice players replied to the person, "How can there be happiness in separation from loved ones? The meaning of happiness is not so."

是时，诸博戏者报斯人曰：「恩爱别离，有何乐哉？言快乐者，此义不然。」

At that time, the person then thought, "If the Tathāgata's words are true, they cannot be false. How can there be happiness in separation from loved ones? This meaning is not so."

是时，彼人便作是念：「审如来言终不虚妄。云何恩爱别离，当有乐耶？此义不然。」

At that time, the person entered the city of Śrāvastī and, at the palace gate, proclaimed, "The śramaṇa Gautama teaches that separation from loved ones and encountering hatred is happiness." At that time, the news spread throughout the city of Śrāvastī and the inner palace, reaching everywhere.

尔时，彼人入舍卫城，至宫门外称：「沙门瞿昙而作是教：『恩爱别离、怨憎之会，此者快乐。』」尔时，舍卫城及中宫内，普传此语，靡不周遍。

At that time, King Prasenajit and Queen Mallikā were together on a high pavilion, enjoying themselves and playing. King Prasenajit then said to Queen Mallikā, "The śramaṇa Gautama truly said, 'Separation from loved ones and encountering hatred are both happiness.'"

当于尔时，大王波斯匿及摩利夫人，共在高楼之上相娱乐戏。尔时，王波斯匿告摩利夫人曰：「沙门瞿昙审有斯语：『恩爱离别、怨憎之会，此皆快乐。』」

The queen replied, "I have not heard such teachings from the Tathāgata. If the Tathāgata did teach this, it would not be false.

夫人报曰：「吾不从如来闻此言教，设当如来有此教者，事亦不虚。」

King Prasenajit said, "It is like a teacher instructing a disciple, 'Do this, abandon that.' The disciple replies, 'Yes, teacher!' Now you, Mallikā, are also like this. Although the śramaṇa Gautama says this, you should say, 'It is so, not different, not false.' But go quickly, do not stand before me.

王波斯匿告曰：「犹如师教弟子：『为是、舍是。』弟子报言：『如是，大师！』汝今摩利亦复如是，彼瞿昙沙门虽作是说，汝应作是言：『如是不异，无有虚妄。』然卿速去，不须在吾前立。」

At that time, Queen Mallikā said to the bamboo-legged brahmin, "Now you go to the Jetavana Monastery, go to the Tathāgata, and in my name kneel at the Tathāgata's feet. Also, with this meaning, fully inform the World-Honored One, saying, 'In the city of Śrāvastī and in the inner palace, there is this discussion. The śramaṇa Gautama says that separation from loved ones and meeting with those one dislikes are all happiness. I do not know if the World-Honored One has taught this.' If the World-Honored One has taught it, you should receive it well and return to tell me.

尔时，摩利夫人语竹膊婆罗门曰：「汝今往诣祇洹精舍，到如来所，持我名字，跪如来足，复以此义具白世尊云：『舍卫城内及中宫人有此言论，沙门瞿昙言恩爱别离、怨憎合会，此皆快乐。不审世尊有此教耶？』若世尊所有说者，汝善承受，还向我说。」

At that time, the bamboo-legged brahmin received the queen's instructions and went to the Jetavana Monastery. Arriving at the place of the World-Honored One, they exchanged greetings. After exchanging greetings, he sat to one side.

是时，竹膊婆罗门受夫人教勅，寻往至祇洹精舍。到世尊所，共相问讯。共相问讯已，在一面坐。

At that time, the brahmin said to the World-Honored One, "Queen Mallikā pays homage at the feet of the World-Honored One, inquiring about the Tathāgata's well-being, ease of rising, and strength of walking. In guiding the blind and ignorant, is there no weariness?" He also said, "In the city of Śrāvastī, there is a widespread saying: 'The śramaṇa Gautama teaches that separation from loved ones and meeting with those one dislikes is joyful and pleasant.' I do not know if the World-Honored One has taught this.

时，彼梵志白世尊曰：「摩利夫人礼世尊足，问讯如来兴居轻利，游步康强乎？训化盲冥，得无劳耶？」复作是语：「此舍卫城内普传此言：『沙门瞿昙而作是教：「恩爱别离、怨憎之会，此乐快哉！」』不审世尊有是言教耶？」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bamboo-legged brahmin, "In this city of Śrāvastī, there was an elder who lost a son. He was insane and lost his mind, running around east and west, asking anyone he saw, 'Who has seen my son?' Brahmins! Separation from loved ones and meeting with those one dislikes are all without joy. In the past, in this city of Śrāvastī, there was an old mother who passed away. She too became insane and did not recognize east and west. There was also an old father who passed away. There were also brothers and sisters who all passed away. Seeing these changes due to impermanence, they became insane and lost their minds, not recognizing east and west. Brahmins! In the past, in this city of Śrāvastī, there was a man who newly married a wife who was exceptionally beautiful. After some time, he became poor. The wife's parents saw that he was poor and thought, 'We should take back our daughter and marry her to someone else.' The man overheard that the wife's family wanted to take back his wife and marry her to someone else. At that time, the man wore a sharp knife under his clothes and went to the wife's family. At that time, the wife was spinning in the shade outside the wall. The man went to the wife's parents' house and asked, 'Where is my wife now?' The wife's mother replied, 'Your wife is spinning in the shade outside the wall.' The man then went to the wife. Arriving, he asked the wife, 'Do your parents want to take you back and marry you to someone else?' The wife replied, 'It is true, but I do not like to hear these words.' At that time, the man immediately drew his sharp sword, stabbed his wife to death, and then took the sharp sword to stab his own abdomen, saying, 'The two of us will die together.' Brahmins! By this method, know that separation from loved ones and meeting with those one dislikes are all sorrowful and cannot be described.

尔时，世尊告竹膊婆罗门曰：「于此舍卫城内，有一长者丧失一子，彼念此子，狂惑失性，东西驰走，见人便问：『谁见我子？』然，婆罗门！恩爱别离苦、怨憎会苦，此皆无有欢乐。昔日此舍卫城中，复有一老母无常，亦复狂惑不识东西；复有一老父无常；亦复有兄弟姊妹皆悉无常。彼见此无常之变，生狂失性不识东西。婆罗门！昔日此舍卫城中有一人，新迎妇，端正无双。尔时，彼人未经几时，便自贫穷。时，彼妇父母见此人贫，便生此念：『吾当夺女更嫁与余人。』彼人窃闻妇家父母欲夺吾妇，更嫁与余家。尔时，彼人衣里带利刀，便往至妇家，当于尔时，彼妇在墙外纺作。是时，彼人往至妇父母家问曰] cf.【丽】：『我妇今为所在？』妇母报言：『卿妇在墙外阴中纺作。』尔时，彼人便往至妇所。到已，问妇曰：『云卿父母欲夺汝更余嫁耶？』妇报言：『信有此语，然我不乐闻此言耶。』尔时，彼人即拔利剑，取妇刺杀，复取利剑，自刺其腹，并复作是语：『我二人俱取死。』婆罗门！当以此方便，知恩爱别离、怨憎会苦，此皆愁忧，实不可言。」

At that time, the bamboo-legged brahmin said to the World-Honored One, "Indeed, World-Honored One! These afflictions are truly suffering and not joyful. The reason is that I once had a son who passed away. Day and night, I miss him and cannot let go of him in my heart. When I think of my son, my mind is insane and confused, running around east and west, asking anyone I see, 'Who has seen my son?' What the śramaṇa Gautama has just said is indeed true. I have many affairs of state and wish to return to my residence.

尔时，竹膊婆罗门白世尊曰：「如是。世尊！有此诸恼，实苦不乐。所以然者，昔我有一子，舍我无常。昼夜追忆，不离心怀。时我念儿，心意狂惑，驰走东西，见人便问：『谁见我儿？』沙门瞿昙今所说者，诚如所言。国事烦多，欲还所止。」

The World-Honored One said, "Now is the right time."

世尊告曰：「今正是时。」

The bamboo-legged brahmin immediately rose from his seat, circumambulated the Buddha three times, and left. He went to Queen Māyā and told her the details of the matter.

竹膊婆罗门即从坐起，遶佛三匝而去。往来至摩利夫人所，以此因缘具白夫人。

At that time, Queen Māyā went to King Prasenajit and said to the great king, "I have something to ask now. I only wish that the great king would answer all my questions. How is it, great king? Do you think of Prince Vidūdabha?"

时，摩利夫人复至波斯匿王所，到已，白大王曰：「今欲有所问，唯愿大王事事见报！云何，大王！为念琉璃王子不？」

The king replied, "I think of him very much. I love and pity him, and he is always on my mind."

王报言：「甚念！爱愍不去心首。」

The queen asked, "If there is any change in the prince, will the great king be worried?"

夫人问曰：「若当王子有迁变者，大王！为有忧也？」

The king replied, "Yes, my queen, as you say."

王复报言：「如是，夫人！如汝所言。」

The queen asked, "Great king, you should know that separation from loved ones always gives rise to sorrow. How is it, great king, do you think of Prince I-lo?"

夫人问曰：「大王当知，恩爱别离，皆兴愁想。云何，大王！为念伊罗王子乎？」

The king replied, "I love and respect him very much."

王报言：「我甚爱敬。」

The queen asked, "Great king, if Prince I-lo were to change, would you be sorrowful?"

夫人问曰：「大王！若当王子有迁变者，有愁忧耶？」

The king replied, "I would be very sorrowful."

王报言：「甚有愁忧。」

The queen said, "This is the way to know that there is no joy in separation from loved ones. Great king, do you remember the kshatriya Sarata?"

夫人报言：「当以此方便知，恩爱别离，无有欢乐。云何，大王！念萨罗陀刹利种不？」

The king replied, "I loved and respected him very much."

王报言：「甚爱敬念。」

The queen said, "Great king, if anything were to happen to Queen Sarata, would you be sad?"

夫人言：「云何，大王！若使萨罗陀夫人有变易者，大王为有忧耶？」

The king replied, "I would be sad."

王报言：「吾有愁忧。」

The queen said, "Great king, you should know that separation from loved ones is suffering."

夫人言：「大王！当知恩爱别离，此皆是苦。」

The queen said, "Does the king think of me?"

夫人言：「王念我不？」

The king said, "I love and think of you."

王言：「我爱念汝。」

The queen said, "If my body were to change, would the great king be sorrowful?"

夫人言：「设当我身有变易者，大王有愁忧乎？」

The king said, "If your body were to change, I would be sorrowful."

王言：「设汝身有变易，便有愁忧。」

"Great king! You should know that this is the way of separation from loved ones and reunion with those we dislike, without any joy."

「大王！当以此方便知，恩爱别离、怨憎合会，无欢乐心。」

The queen said, "Great king! Do you think of the people of Kasi and Kosala?"

夫人言：「云何，大王！念迦尸、拘萨罗人民乎？」

The king said, "I think very fondly of the people of Kasi and Kosala."

王言：「我甚爱念迦尸、拘萨罗人民。」

The queen said, "If the people of Kasi and Kosala were to change, would the great king be sorrowful?"

夫人言：「迦尸、拘萨罗人民设当变易者，大王有愁忧乎？」

The king said, "If the people of Kasi and Kosala were to change, I would not be able to live. How could I speak of sorrow? The reason is this: I am able to live because of the strength of the people of Kasi and Kosala. By this means, I know that my life is not secure. How could I not give rise to sorrow?"

王言：「迦尸、拘萨罗人民当有变易者，我命不存，况言愁忧乎？所以然者，我因迦尸、拘萨罗国人民力，当得自存。以此方便，知命尚不存，何况不生愁忧乎？」

The queen said, "From this we know that all separation from loved ones is painful and has no joy."

夫人言：「以此知之，恩爱别离，皆有此苦，无有欢乐。」

At that time, King Prasenajit knelt on his right knee, joined his palms towards the World-Honored One, and said, "How wonderful! How wonderful! The World-Honored One speaks of this Dharma. If that śramaṇa Gautama comes, then we can discuss it." He also said to the queen, "From now on, I will look at you even more than usual. The clothes you wear will be no different from mine."

尔时，王波斯匿右膝著地，叉手合掌而向世尊！作是说：「甚奇！甚奇！彼世尊而说此法。若当彼沙门瞿昙来者，尔乃可得共言论。」复语夫人：「自今以后，当更看汝胜于常日，所著服饰与吾无异。」

At that time, the World-Honored One heard that Queen Māyā had made this statement to the king. He told the bhikṣus, "Queen Māyā is very wise. If King Prasenajit asks me about this, I will also explain it to him in the same way as the queen explained it to the king." He also told the bhikṣus, "Among my śrāvakas, the foremost in attaining the state of upāsaka with firm and solid faith is none other than Queen Māyā."

尔时，世尊闻摩利夫人与大王立此论本，告诸比丘：「摩利夫人甚大聪明，设当王波斯匿问我此语者，我亦当以此义向彼王说之，如夫人向王所说而无有异。」又告诸比丘：「我声闻中第一得证优婆斯，笃信牢固，所谓摩利夫人是。」

At that time, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
