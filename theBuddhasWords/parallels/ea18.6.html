EĀ 18.6

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

18.6 （六）

Parallel to: SN 21.8 / SĀ 1067 / SĀ² 2.5

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Nanda was wearing extremely fine clothes that dazzled the eyes, wearing golden-embroidered sandals, and further applying makeup to his eyes. Holding a bowl in his hand, he wanted to enter the city of Śrāvastī. At that time, many bhikṣus saw from afar Venerable Nanda wearing extremely fine clothes, entering the city of Śrāvastī to beg for food. Then, many bhikṣus went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed their heads at his feet, sat to one side, and after a while, withdrew their seats and said to the World-Honored One, "Just now, bhikṣu Nanda was wearing extremely fine clothes that dazzled the eyes, and entered the city of Śrāvastī to beg for food."

尔时，尊者难陀著极妙之衣，色曜人目，著金厕履屣，复抆饰两目，手执钵器，欲入舍卫城。尔时，众多比丘遥见尊者难陀著极妙之衣，入舍卫城乞食。尔时，众多比丘便往至世尊所，头面礼足，在于一面坐，须臾退坐，白世尊曰：「向者，难陀比丘著极妙之衣，色曜人目，入舍卫城乞食。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told a bhikṣu, "Go quickly to where bhikṣu Nanda is and say, 'The Tathāgata calls you!'"

尔时，世尊告一比丘：「汝速往至难陀比丘所：『如来呼卿！』」

He replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!" At that time, the bhikṣu accepted the World-Honored One's instruction, bowed his head at his feet, and left. He went to where bhikṣu Nanda was and, having arrived, said to Nanda, "The World-Honored One calls you."

对曰：「如是。世尊！」时，彼比丘受世尊教，头面礼足而去。往至难陀比丘所，到已，语难陀曰：「世尊呼卿。」

At that time, Nanda heard the bhikṣu's words and immediately came to where the World-Honored One was. Having arrived, he bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side. At that time, the World-Honored One said to Nanda, "Why are you wearing such fine clothes and shoes to beg for alms in Śrāvastī?" At that time, Venerable Nanda remained silent and did not speak.

是时，难陀闻比丘语，即来至世尊所，到已，头面礼足，在一面坐。是时，世尊告难陀曰：「汝今何故著此极妙之衣，又则著履屣，入舍卫城乞食？」时，尊者难陀默然不语。

The World-Honored One again said, "How is it, Nanda? Did you not leave home to study the path because of your firm faith?"

世尊复重告曰：「云何，难陀！汝岂不以信牢固出家学道乎？」

Nanda replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

难陀对曰：「如是。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "You, son of a noble family, should not practice the precepts with firm faith when you have left home to study the path. How can you now wear the finest clothes, groom your appearance, and want to enter the city of Shravasti to beg for food? What is the difference between you and the lay people?"

世尊告曰：「汝今族姓子不应律行，以信牢固出家学道，何由复著极妙之衣，摩治形服，欲入舍卫城乞食？与彼白衣有何差别？」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

When will I see Nandi, who can practice the ascetic practices, whose mind delights
in the Dharma of the śramaṇa, and who has attained the perfection of austerity?

「何日见难陀，　　能治阿练行；心乐沙门法，　　头陀度无极。

"Nandi, you should not engage in such conduct again."

「汝今，难陀！更莫造此如是之行。」

At that time, the Venerable Nandi and the fourfold assembly, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，尊者难陀及四部众闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
