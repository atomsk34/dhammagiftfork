EĀ 18.7

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

18.7 （七）

Parallel to: Ud 3.2 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Nanda was unable to practice the holy life and wanted to remove his Dharma robes and return to lay life.

尔时，尊者难陀不堪行梵行，欲脱法衣，习白衣行。

At that time, many bhikṣus went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed their heads at his feet, and sat to one side. Then the many bhikṣus said to the World-Honored One, "Bhikṣu Nanda is unable to practice the holy life and wants to remove his Dharma robes and return to lay life."

尔时，众多比丘往至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。尔时，众多比丘白世尊曰：「难陀比丘不堪行梵行，欲脱法服，习居家行。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told a bhikṣu, "Go to Nanda and say, 'The Tathāgata summons you.'"

尔时，世尊告一比丘：「汝往至难陀所，云：『如来唤卿。』」

He replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!" At that time, the bhikṣu accepted the World-Honored One's instruction, rose from his seat, bowed his head at the World-Honored One's feet, and then left. He went to where the bhikṣu Nanda was and said, "The World-Honored One summons you."

对曰：「如是。世尊！」时，彼比丘受世尊教，即从坐起，礼世尊足，便退而去。至彼难陀比丘所云：「世尊唤。」

Nanda replied, "Yes." At that time, the bhikṣu Nanda followed this bhikṣu to where the World-Honored One was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side.

难陀对曰：「如是。」尔时难陀比丘寻随此比丘至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。

At that time, the World-Honored One said to Nanda, "What is it, Nanda? Do you not enjoy practicing the holy life, and wish to remove your Dharma robes and practice as a layperson?"

是时，世尊告难陀曰：「云何，难陀！不乐修梵行，欲脱法衣，修白衣行乎？」

Nanda replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

难陀对曰：「如是。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "Why is it so, Nanda?"

世尊告曰：「何以故？难陀！」

Nanda replied, "The mind of desire is burning, unable to restrain itself."

难陀对曰：「欲心炽然，不能自禁。」

The World-Honored One said, "How is it, Nanda! Are you not a clansman who has left home to study the Way?"

世尊告曰：「云何，难陀！汝非族姓子出家学道乎？」

Nanda replied, "Yes, World-Honored One! I am a clansman who has left home with firm faith to study the Way."

难陀对曰：「如是。世尊！我是族姓子，以信牢固出家学道。」

The World-Honored One said, "You clansman! This is not appropriate, to leave home to study the Way and cultivate holy life, yet to abandon the true Dharma and practice defilement. Nanda, you should know, there are two things that are insatiable. If a person practices these two things, they will never be satisfied. What are the two things? They are sexual desire and drinking alcohol. These are the two things that are insatiable. If a person practices these two things, they will never be satisfied. Because of these practices, they will not be able to attain the unconditioned state. Therefore, Nanda! You should remember to abandon these two things, and later you will definitely attain the unconditioned result. Now, Nanda! You should cultivate pure conduct well, and the fruits of the path will all come from it."

世尊告曰：「汝族姓子！此非其宜，以舍家学道修清净行。云何舍于正法欲习秽污？难陀当知，有二法无厌足，若有人习此法者终无厌足。云何为二法？所谓婬欲及饮酒。是谓二法无厌足。若有人习此二法，终无厌足，缘此行果，亦不能得无为之处。是故，难陀！当念舍此二法，后必成无漏之报。汝今，难陀！善修梵行，趣道之果，靡不由之。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

If a house is not well sealed, rain will leak in
If a person does not practice well, the leaks of desire
anger, and ignorance will leak in. If a house is well
sealed, rain will not leak in. If a person can practice
well, the leaks of desire, anger, and ignorance will not leak in.

「盖屋不密，　　天雨则漏；　　人不惟行，漏婬怒痴。　　盖屋善密，　　天雨不漏；人能惟行，　　无婬怒痴。」

At that time, the World-Honored One again thought: "This noble son has excessive desire. I should now extinguish fire with fire." At that time, the World-Honored One used his spiritual power to hold Nanda with his hand. In the time it takes a strong person to bend and stretch an arm, he brought Nanda to the top of a fragrant mountain. At that time, on the mountain top there was a cave, and there was also a blind monkey living there. At that time, the World-Honored One held Nanda with his right hand and said to him: "Do you see this blind monkey, Nanda?"

尔时，世尊复作是念：「此族姓子欲意极多，我今宜可以火灭火。」是时，世尊即以神力手执难陀，犹如力人屈伸臂顷，将难陀至香山上。尔时，山上有一岩穴，复有一瞎猕猴在彼住止。是时，世尊右手执难陀而告之曰：「汝，难陀！颇见此瞎猕猴不？」

He replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

对曰：「如是。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "Which is more wonderful, the Śākya woman Sundarī or this blind monkey?"

世尊告曰：「何者为妙？为孙陀利释种妙耶？为此瞎猕猴妙乎？」

Nanda replied, "It is like someone who injures a vicious dog's nose and then applies poison to it. The dog becomes even more vicious. It is like this. The Śākya woman Sundarī cannot be compared to this blind monkey. It is like a great fire burning a mountain forest, adding dry firewood, the fire blazes even more. I think of that Śākya woman, and she does not leave my mind."

难陀对曰：「犹如有人伤极恶犬鼻，复加毒涂，彼犬倍恶。此亦如是。孙陀利释女，今以此瞎猕猴相比，不可为喻，犹如大火［卄／积］焚烧山野，加益以干薪，火转炽然，此亦如是。我念彼释女，不去心怀。」

At that time, the World-Honored One, in the time it takes to bend and stretch an arm, disappeared from that mountain and arrived in the Heaven of the Thirty-three Gods. At that time, the gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three Gods were all gathered in the Dharma Hall of Good Dharma. Not far from the Dharma Hall of Good Dharma, there was another palace where five hundred celestial maidens amused themselves together, all women, without any men. At that time, Nanda saw from afar the five hundred celestial maidens performing music and amusing themselves together. Having seen this, he asked the World-Honored One, "What is this? Five hundred celestial maidens performing music and amusing themselves together?"

尔时，世尊如屈伸臂顷，从彼山不现，便至三十三天。尔时，三十三天上诸天普集善法讲堂，去善法讲堂不远，复有宫殿，五百玉女自相娱乐，纯有女人，无有男子。尔时，难陀遥见五百天女，作倡伎乐，自相娱乐，见已，问世尊曰：「此是何等，五百天女作倡伎乐，自相娱乐？」

The World-Honored One said, "Nanda, you go and ask them yourself."

世尊告曰：「汝难陀自往问之。」

At that time, Venerable Nanda went to the place of the five hundred celestial maidens. He saw that their palace was furnished with various hundreds of fine seats, all women, without any men. At that time, Venerable Nanda asked those celestial maidens, "What celestial maidens are you, each amusing yourselves together, so happily?"

是时，尊者难陀便往至五百天女所，见彼宫舍，敷好坐具若干百种，纯是女人，无有男子。是时，尊者难陀问彼天女曰：「汝等是何天女，各相娱乐，快乐如是？」

The celestial maidens replied, "We are five hundred people, all pure, without husbands. We heard that there is a disciple of the World-Honored One named Nanda, the son of the Buddha's aunt. He has practiced pure and chaste conduct under the Tathāgata. After his life ends, he will be reborn here to be our husband and amuse himself with us."

天女报曰：「我等有五百人，悉皆清净，无有夫主。我等闻有世尊弟子，名曰难陀，是佛姨母儿，彼于如来所，清净修梵行，命终之后当生此间，与我等作夫主，共相娱乐。」

At that time, Venerable Nanda was very joyful and could not contain himself. He thought, "I am now a disciple of the World-Honored One, and also the son of the Buddha's aunt. These celestial maidens will all be my wives." At that time, Nanda then withdrew and went to where the World-Honored One was.

是时，尊者难陀甚怀喜悦，不能自胜，便作是念：「我今是世尊弟子，且又复是姨母儿，此诸天女皆当为我作妇。」是时，难陀便退而去，至世尊所。

The World-Honored One said, "What did those jade maidens say?"

世尊告曰：「云何，难陀！彼玉女何所言说？」

Nanda replied, "Those jade maidens each said, 'We each have no husbands. We have heard that the disciples of the World-Honored One who practice pure conduct will be reborn here after their lives end.'"

难陀报曰：「彼玉女各作是说：『我等各无夫主，闻有世尊弟子善修梵行，命终之后，当来生此。』」

The World-Honored One said, "Nanda, what do you think? Nanda, what do you think?"

世尊告曰：「难陀，汝意云何？难陀！汝意云何？」

Nanda replied, "At that time, I immediately thought, 'I am a disciple of the World-Honored One, and I am also the son of the Buddha's aunt. These heavenly maidens will all be my wives.'"

难陀报曰：「尔时，即自生念：『我是世尊弟子，又且复是佛姨母儿，此诸天女尽当与我作妻。』」

The World-Honored One said, "Excellent, Nanda! If you cultivate the holy life well, I will give you testimony and make these five hundred women your servants."

世尊告曰：「快哉，难陀！善修梵行，我当与汝作证，使此五百女人皆为给使。」

The World-Honored One further said, "What do you think, Nanda? Is the Śākya girl Sundarī beautiful, or are the five hundred heavenly maidens beautiful?"

世尊复告：「云何，难陀！孙陀利释女妙耶？为是五百天女妙乎？」

Nanda replied, "It is like a blind monkey on the mountain peak in front of Sundarī. It has no luster or color. It is the same here. Sundarī in front of the other heavenly maidens is also like this, without luster."

难陀报曰：「犹如山顶瞎猕猴在孙陀利前，无有光泽，亦无有色。此亦如是。孙陀利在他天女前，亦复如是，无有光泽。」

The World-Honored One said, "If you practice pure conduct well, I will certify that you will obtain these five hundred heavenly beings."

世尊告曰：「汝善修梵行，我当证汝得此五百天人。」

At that time, the World-Honored One had this thought: "I will now use fire to extinguish Nanda's fire." Just like a strong person bending and stretching an arm, the World-Honored One took Nanda's arm with his right hand and brought him to the hells. At that time, the beings in the hells were experiencing various kinds of suffering. In that hell, there was a large cauldron that was empty and had no one in it. Seeing it, Nanda became fearful and his hair stood on end. He went before the World-Honored One and said, "All these beings are experiencing suffering, but only this cauldron is empty and has no one in it."

尔时，世尊便作是念：「我今当以火灭难陀火。」犹如力人屈伸臂顷，世尊右手执难陀臂将至地狱中。尔时，地狱众生受若干苦恼。尔时，彼地狱中有一大镬，空无有人。见已，便生恐惧，衣毛皆竖，前白世尊曰：「此诸众生皆受苦痛，唯有此釜而独空无人。」

The World-Honored One said, "This is called the Avīci Hell."

世尊告曰：「此者名为阿毘地狱。」

At that time, Nanda became even more terrified, his hair standing on end. He said to the World-Honored One, "This is the Avīci Hell, but it is empty and has no one who has committed offenses."

尔时，难陀倍复恐怖，衣毛皆竖，白世尊曰：「此是阿毘地狱，而独自空，亦无罪人？」

The World-Honored One said, "Nanda, you go and ask them yourself."

世尊告曰：「汝难陀自往问之。」

At that time, Venerable Nanda himself asked, "What is this, jailer? Why is this hell empty and without anyone?"

是时，尊者难陀便自问曰：「云何，狱卒！此是何狱？此是何狱空无有人？」

The jailer replied, "Bhikṣu, you should know that the Tathāgata's disciple named Nanda, having practiced pure conduct under the Tathāgata, upon the dissolution of his body and the end of his life, was reborn in a good place, in the heavens, where he enjoyed himself for a thousand years. Having passed away from there, he was reborn in this Avīci hell. This empty cauldron is his abode."

狱卒报曰：「比丘当知，释迦文佛弟子名曰难陀，彼于如来所，净修梵行，身坏命终，生善处天上，于彼寿千岁，快自娱乐。复于彼终，生此阿毘地狱中，此空镬者即是其室。」

At that time, Venerable Nanda, having heard these words, was frightened and his hair stood on end. He thought, "This empty cauldron is indeed for me." He went to the World-Honored One, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and said to the World-Honored One, "Please accept my repentance. It is my own fault that I did not practice pure conduct and disturbed the Tathāgata."

时，尊者难陀闻此语已，便怀怖懅，衣毛皆竖，即生此念：「此之空釜，正为我耳。」来至世尊所，头面礼足，白世尊曰：「愿受忏悔，我自罪缘，不修梵行，触娆如来。」

At that time, Venerable Nanda spoke this verse:

尔时，尊者难陀便说此偈：

Human life is not to be valued, for even heavenly life ends in death
The pain and suffering of hell - only the bliss of nirvana is real.

「人生不足贵，　　天寿尽亦丧；地狱痛酸苦，　　唯有涅槃乐。」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to Nanda, "Excellent! Excellent! As you have said, nirvana is the greatest bliss. Nanda, I allow you to repent. You are foolish and ignorant, and you know that you have erred before the Tathāgata. I now accept your repentance, and you should not commit it again in the future."

尔时，世尊告难陀曰：「善哉！善哉！如汝所言，涅槃者最是快乐。难陀！听汝忏悔，汝愚、汝痴，自知有咎于如来所。今受汝悔过，后更莫犯。」

At that time, the World-Honored One, in the time it takes to bend and stretch an arm, took Nanda by the hand from hell and appeared in the Jeta Grove in Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in Śrāvastī. At that time, the World-Honored One said to Nanda, "Now, Nanda, you should cultivate two dharmas. What are the two dharmas? They are tranquility and insight. You should also cultivate two more dharmas. What are the two dharmas? They are knowing that saṃsāra is not blissful and knowing that nirvāṇa is blissful. These are the two dharmas. You should also cultivate two more dharmas. What are the two dharmas? They are wisdom and eloquence." At that time, the World-Honored One spoke these various dharmas to Nanda.

尔时，世尊屈伸臂顷，手执难陀，从地狱不现，便至舍卫城祇树给孤独园。尔时，世尊告难陀曰：「汝今，难陀！当修二法。云何为二法？所谓止与观也。复当更修二法。云何为二法？生死不可乐，知涅槃为乐，是谓二法。复当更修二法。云何为二法？所谓智与辩也。」尔时，世尊以此种种法向难陀说。

At that time, Venerable Nanda, having received the instruction from the World-Honored One, rose from his seat, bowed at the feet of the World-Honored One, and then departed, going to the Ānanda Grove. Having arrived, he sat cross-legged under a tree, with body and mind upright, focusing his attention in front, contemplating the teachings of the Tathāgata. At that time, Venerable Nanda, in a secluded place, constantly contemplated the teachings of the Tathāgata, not letting go for a moment. Because the clansmen, with firm faith, left home to study the path, practiced the unsurpassed pure practice, exhausted birth and death, established the pure practice, accomplished what was to be done, and would not again experience existence, knowing it as it is. At that time, Venerable Nanda then attained arahantship.

是时，尊者难陀从世尊受教已，从坐起，礼世尊足，便退而去，至安陀园。到已，在一树下结加趺坐，正身正意，系念在前，思惟如来如此言教。是时，尊者在闲静处，恒思惟如来教，不去须臾。所以族姓子，以信牢固出家学道，修无上梵行，生死已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，更不复受有，如实知之。是时，尊者难陀便成阿罗汉。

Having attained arahantship, he immediately rose from his seat, adjusted his robes, went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed at his feet, and sat to one side. At that time, Venerable Nanda said to the World-Honored One, "The World-Honored One previously promised to give the five hundred heavenly maidens to the disciples who attained realization. Now I have given them all up."

已成阿罗汉，即从坐起，整衣服至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。是时，尊者难陀白世尊曰：「世尊前许证弟子五百天女者，今尽舍之。」

The World-Honored One said, "Now that your birth and death have been exhausted, and your pure practice has been established, I will give them up for you."

世尊告曰：「汝今生死已尽，梵行已立，吾即舍之。」

At that time, he spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，便说偈曰：

I now see Nanda practicing the Dharma of a śramaṇa; all evils
have ceased, and he is not lacking in the practice of austerity.

「我今见难陀，　　修行沙门法；诸恶皆以息，　　头陀无有失。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "The one who attained arahantship is now the bhikṣu Nanda. The one who is free from desire, anger, and ignorance is also the bhikṣu Nanda."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘言：「得阿罗汉者，今难陀比丘是。无婬、怒、痴，亦是难陀比丘。」

At that time, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
