EĀ 19.1

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

Chapter 19 劝请品第十九

19.1 （一）

Parallel to: DN 14 / MN 26 / MN 85 / SN 6.1 / MĀ 204 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at the Bodhi Tree in the country of Magadha.

一时，佛在摩竭国道场树下。

At that time, not long after the World-Honored One attained enlightenment, he had this thought: "My profound Dharma is difficult to understand, difficult to comprehend, difficult to realize, inconceivable, subtle, and realized by the wise. Those who can discern the meaning and practice it without weariness will attain joy. If I were to teach the wonderful Dharma to others, but they do not believe and accept it, nor practice it, then my efforts would be in vain and there would be harm. I should now remain silent. Why speak the Dharma?"

尔时，世尊得道未久，便生是念：「我今甚深之法难晓难了，难可觉知，不可思惟，休息微妙，智者所觉知，能分别义理，习之不厌，即得欢喜。设吾与人说妙法者，人不信受，亦不奉行者，唐有其劳，则有所损。我今宜可默然，何须说法！」

At that time, Brahma Heaven, being far away in the Brahma Heaven, knew the Tathāgata's thoughts. In the time it takes for a strong man to bend and stretch his arm, he disappeared from the Brahma Heaven and appeared before the World-Honored One. He bowed his head to the Buddha's feet and stood to one side. Then Brahma Heaven said to the World-Honored One: "This Jambudvīpa will surely perish and the three realms will lose their eyes. The Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One has appeared in the world and should expound the Dharma treasure. But now he does not proclaim the taste of the Dharma. I only pray that the Tathāgata will widely explain the profound Dharma for all beings! Moreover, the roots of these beings are easy to liberate. If they do not hear it, they will forever lose the Dharma eye. These are the remaining sons of the Dharma, like the lotus, jasmine, and blue lotus flowers. Although they emerge from the ground, they have not yet emerged from the water and have not yet bloomed. At that time, those flowers gradually want to grow, so they have not yet emerged from the water. Sometimes those flowers emerge from the water, and sometimes those flowers are not attached to the water. These beings are also like this, being oppressed by birth, old age, sickness, and death. Their faculties are ripe, but if they do not hear the Dharma and perish, would it not be painful? Now is the right time. I only pray that the World-Honored One will explain the Dharma for them."

尔时，梵天在梵天上，遥知如来所念，犹如士夫屈伸臂顷，从梵天上没不现，来至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面住。尔时，梵天白世尊曰：「此阎浮提必当坏败，三界丧目。如来、至真、等正觉出现于世，应演法宝，然今复不畅演法味，唯愿如来普为众生广说深法！又此众生根原易度。若不闻者，永失法眼。此应为法之遗子，犹如优钵莲华、拘牟头华、分陀利华，虽出于地，未出水上，亦未开敷。是时，彼华渐渐欲生，故未出水，或时此华以出水上，或时此华不为水所著。此众生类亦复如是，为生、老、病、死所见逼促，诸根应熟，然不闻法而便丧者，不亦苦哉！今正是时，唯愿世尊当为说法。」

At that time, the World-Honored One knew the thoughts in Brahma Heaven's mind, and out of compassion for all beings, he spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊知梵天心中所念，又慈愍一切众生故，说此偈曰：

Brahma Heaven has now come to encourage the Tathāgata to open the Dharma gate
those who hear will gain firm faith and discern the profound essentials of the
Dharma. It is like being on a high mountain peak, looking down on all
beings. I now have this Dharma, ascending the hall to reveal the Dharma eye.

「梵天今来劝，　　如来开法门；闻者得笃信，　　分别深法要。犹在高山顶，　　普观众生类；我今有此法，　　升堂现法眼。」

At that time, Brahma Heaven immediately thought, "The Tathāgata will surely speak the profound and wonderful Dharma for the sake of beings." Joyfully leaping, unable to contain himself, he bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and then returned to the heavens.

尔时，梵天便作是念：「如来必为众生说深妙法。」欢喜踊跃，不能自胜，头面礼足已，即还天上。

At that time, Brahma Heaven, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully accepted and practiced it.

尔时，梵天闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
