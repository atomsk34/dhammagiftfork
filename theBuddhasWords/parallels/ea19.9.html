EĀ 19.9

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

19.9 （九）迦旃

Parallel to: AN 2.38 / SĀ 547

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Venerable Mahākātyāyana was staying by a deep pool in the country of Vana, together with a great assembly of five hundred bhikṣus.

一时，尊者摩诃迦遮延游婆那国深池水侧，与大比丘众五百人俱。

At that time, the Venerable Kātyāyana had this reputation, which spread far and wide. The Venerable Elder Kānthaka was wandering and teaching there. At that time, the brāhman heard that the Venerable Kātyāyana was wandering and teaching by that pool, together with five hundred bhikṣus, and that the Venerable Elder was endowed with virtues. He thought, "I should now go to pay respects to that person." At that time, the well-favored brāhman, together with five hundred disciples, went to where the Venerable Kātyāyana was, exchanged greetings, and sat to one side. At that time, the brāhman asked the Venerable Kātyāyana, "As for what Kātyāyana practices, this is not the Dharma and Vinaya. The young bhikṣus do not pay respects to us, the virtuous brāhmanas.

尔时，尊者迦遮延有此名闻，流闻四远。尊者长老奸茶婆罗门在此游化。尔时，婆罗门闻尊者迦遮延在此池侧游化，将五百比丘，尊者长老功德具足，「我今可往问讯彼人。」是时，上色婆罗门将五百弟子，往至尊者迦遮延所，共相问讯，在一面坐。尔时，彼婆罗门问尊者迦遮延曰：「如迦遮延所行，此非法、律，年少比丘不向我等诸高德婆罗门作礼。」

Kātyāyana said, "Brāhman, you should know that the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, speaks of these two grounds. What are the two grounds? One is called the ground of old age, and the other is called the ground of youth.

迦遮延曰：「婆罗门当知，彼如来、至真、等正觉，说此二地。云何为二地？一名老地，二名壮地。」

The brāhman asked, "What is the ground of old age? What is the ground of youth?"

婆罗门问曰：「何者为老地？何者为壮地？」

Kātyāyana said, "Even if a brāhman is eighty or ninety years old, if that person does not stop sexual desire and commits evil deeds, that is called a brāhman who may be said to be old, but is now on the ground of youth."

迦遮延曰：「正使婆罗门年在八十、九十，彼人不止婬欲，作诸恶行，是谓婆罗门虽可言老，今在壮地。」

The brāhman said, "Who is young in years and dwells on the ground of old age?"

婆罗门曰：「何者年壮住在老地？」

Kātyāyana said, "Brāhman! If there is a bhikṣu who is twenty, or thirty, forty, or fifty years old, and he does not practice sexual desire, nor does he commit evil deeds, that is called a brāhman who is young in years and dwells on the ground of old age."

迦遮延曰：「婆罗门！若有比丘年在二十，或三十、四十、五十，彼亦不习婬欲，亦不作恶行，是谓婆罗门年壮在老地。」

The brāhman said, "Is there a bhikṣu in this great assembly who does not practice sexual desire and does not commit evil deeds?"

婆罗门曰：「此大众中颇有一比丘不行婬法，不作恶行乎？」

Kātyāyana said, "In my great assembly, there is no bhikṣu who practices desire and commits evil deeds."

迦遮延曰：「我大众中无有一比丘习欲作恶者。」

At that time, the brāhman immediately rose from his seat, bowed at the feet of the bhikṣus, and said, "You are young and dwell in the place of the old, I am old and dwell in the place of the young." At that time, the brāhman went to Kātyāyana, bowed his head at his feet, and confessed, "I now take refuge in Kātyāyana and the bhikṣu saṃgha, and vow to refrain from killing for the rest of my life."

时，婆罗门即从坐起，礼诸比丘足，并作是语：「汝今年少住于老地，我今年老住于少地。」尔时，彼婆罗门复往至迦遮延所，头面礼足，而自陈说：「我今自归迦遮延及比丘僧，尽形寿不杀。」

Kātyāyana said, "Do not take refuge in me now. You should go to the one I take refuge in."

迦遮延曰：「汝今莫自归我，我所自归者汝可趣向之。」

The brāhman said, "Venerable Kātyāyana, to whom do you take refuge?"

婆罗门曰：「尊者迦遮延，为自归谁？」

Then Venerable Kātyāyana knelt and pointed to the place where the Tathāgata had entered parinirvāṇa, saying, "The one who has renounced the householder's life and practices the path is the one I always take refuge in. He is my teacher."

时，尊者迦遮延便长跪向如来所般涅槃处：「有释种子出家学道，我恒自归彼，然彼人即是我师。」

The brāhman said, "Where is this śramaṇa Gautama? I wish to see him."

婆罗门曰：「此沙门瞿昙为在何处？我今欲见之。」

Kātyāyana said, "The Tathāgata has already entered parinirvāṇa."

迦遮延曰：「彼如来已取涅槃。」

The brāhman said, "If the Tathāgata were still in the world, I would travel hundreds of thousands of yojanas to pay my respects. Although the Tathāgata has entered parinirvāṇa, I now take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṃgha. For as long as I live, I will no longer take life."

婆罗门言：「若如来在世者，我乃可百千由旬往问讯之。彼如来虽取涅槃，我今重自归作礼及佛、法、众，尽其形寿，不复杀生。」

At that time, the brāhman of superior appearance, having heard what was said by Venerable Kātyāyana, joyfully accepted and practiced it.

尔时，上色婆罗门闻尊者迦遮延所说，欢喜奉行。
