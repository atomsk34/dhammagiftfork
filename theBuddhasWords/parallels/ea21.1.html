EĀ 21.1

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

Chapter 21 三宝品第二十一

21.1 （一）第一德

Parallel to: SN 45.139 (~) / AN 4.34 / AN 5.32 / Iti

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "There are three virtues of taking refuge. What are the three? They are taking refuge in the Buddha as the first virtue, taking refuge in the Dharma as the second virtue, and taking refuge in the Saṃgha as the third virtue.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「有三自归之德。云何为三？所谓归佛第一之德，归法第二之德，归僧第三之德。

What is meant by the virtue of taking refuge in the Buddha? Among all beings, whether two-legged, four-legged, or many-legged, with form or formless, with perception or without perception, up to the heaven of the Paranirmitavaśavartin, the Tathāgata is the most honored and supreme, without equal. Just as from a cow comes milk, from milk comes curd, from curd comes butter, and from butter comes ghee; and yet ghee is the most honored and supreme among them, without equal. It is the same here. Among all beings, whether two-legged, four-legged, or many-legged, with form or formless, with perception or without perception, up to the heaven of the Paranirmitavaśavartin, the Tathāgata is the most honored and supreme, without equal. Those who serve the Buddha are said to serve the most honored and supreme virtue. By attaining the most honored and supreme virtue, one will receive the blessings of heaven and the human realm. This is called the most honored and supreme virtue.

「彼云何名为归佛之德？诸有众生，二足、四足、众多足者，有色、无色，有想、无想，至尼维先天上，如来于中，最尊、最上，无能及者。由牛得乳，由乳得酪，由酪得酥，由酥得醍醐；然复醍醐于中，最尊、最上，无能及者。此亦如是，诸有众生，二足、四足、众多足者，有色、无色，有想、无想，至尼维先天上，如来于中，最尊、最上，无能及者。诸有众生承事佛者，是谓承事第一之德。以获第一之德，便受天上、人中之福，此名第一之德。

What is meant by taking refuge in the Dharma? It refers to all dharmas, whether with taints or without taints, conditioned or unconditioned, free from desire and defilements, and the cessation of nirvana. The Dharma of nirvana is the most honored and supreme among all dharmas, without equal. Just as from a cow comes milk, from milk comes curd, from curd comes butter, and from butter comes ghee; and yet ghee is the most honored and supreme among them, without equal. It is the same here. It refers to all dharmas, whether with taints or without taints, conditioned or unconditioned, free from desire and defilements, and the cessation of nirvana. The Dharma of nirvana is the most honored and supreme among all dharmas, without equal. Those who serve the Dharma are said to serve the most honored and supreme virtue. By attaining the most honored and supreme virtue, one will receive the blessings of heaven and the human realm. This is called the most honored and supreme virtue.

「云何名为自归法者？所谓诸法：有漏、无漏，有为、无为，无欲、无染，灭尽、涅槃；然涅槃法于诸法中，最尊、最上，无能及者。由牛得乳，由乳得酪，由酪得酥，由酥得醍醐；然复醍醐于中，最尊、最上，无能及者。此亦如是，所谓诸法：有漏、无漏，有为、无为，无欲、无染，灭尽、涅槃；然涅槃法，于诸法中，最尊、最上，无能及者。诸有众生承事法者，是谓承事第一之德，以获第一之德，便受天上、人中之福，此名第一之德。

What is meant by taking refuge in the Sangha? It refers to the Sangha, the great assembly, the great gathering of all beings with form. Among all beings, the Sangha of the Tathāgata is the most honored and supreme, without equal. Just as from a cow comes milk, from milk comes curd, from curd comes butter, and from butter comes ghee; and yet ghee is the most honored and supreme among them, without equal. It is the same here. It refers to the Sangha, the great assembly, the great gathering of all beings with form. Among all beings, the Sangha of the Tathāgata is the most honored and supreme, without equal. This is said to be serving the most honored and supreme virtue. By attaining the most honored and supreme virtue, one will receive the blessings of heaven and the human realm. This is called the most honored and supreme virtue.

「云何名为自归圣众？所谓圣众者，大众大聚有形之类。众生之中，如来众僧于此众中，最尊、最上，无能及者。由牛得乳，由乳得酪，由酪得酥，由酥得醍醐；然复醍醐于中，最尊、最上，无能及者。此亦如是，所谓圣众者，大众大聚者，有形之类众生之中，如来众僧于此众中，最尊、最上，无能及者。是谓承事第一之德，以获第一之德，便受天上、人中之福，此名第一之德。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

First, serving the Buddha is the most honored and
supreme; next, serving the Dharma is without desire and
attachment; respectfully serving the Sangha is the best field
of merit. That person's first wisdom receives blessings first
If in the heavens or among humans, leading the
assembly is the right guide; also obtaining the most
wonderful seat, naturally eating sweet dew. Wearing the seven-jeweled
robe, respected by others; precepts complete and perfect, faculties
without deficiency. Also attaining the ocean of wisdom, gradually
reaching the realm of nirvāṇa; having these three refuges
entering the path is not difficult.

「第一承事佛，　　最尊无有上；次复承事法，　　无欲无所著；敬奉贤圣众，　　最是良福田；彼人第一智，　　受福最在前。若在天人中，　　处众为正导；亦得最妙座，　　自然食甘露。身著七宝衣，　　为人之所敬；戒具最完全，　　诸根不缺漏。亦获智慧海，　　渐至涅槃界；有此三归者，　　趣道亦不难。」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
