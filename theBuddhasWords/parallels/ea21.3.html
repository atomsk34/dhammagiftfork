EĀ 21.3

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

21.3 （三）

Parallel to: MN 38 (~) / MĀ 201 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "There are three causes and conditions for consciousness to come and conceive. What are the three? Bhikṣus! If the mother has lustful thoughts, the parents gather together in one place and spend the night together, but the external consciousness is not yet ready to come, then conception will not take place. If the consciousness is ready to come, but the parents do not gather together, then conception will not take place. If the mother is without lust, and the parents gather together in one place, then if the father has strong lustful thoughts, but the mother is not very earnest, then conception will not take place. If the parents gather together in one place, and the mother has strong lust, but the father is not very earnest, then conception will not take place.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「有三因缘，识来受胎。云何为三？于是，比丘！母有欲意，父母共集一处，与共止宿，然复外识未应来趣，便不成胎。若复欲识来趣，父母不集，则非成胎。若复母人无欲，父母共集一处，尔时父欲意盛，母不大慇懃，则非成胎。若复父母集在一处，母欲炽盛，父不大慇懃，则非成胎。

If the parents gather together in one place, and the father has a wind illness, and the mother has a cold illness, then conception will not take place. If the parents gather together in one place, and the mother has a wind illness, and the father has a cold illness, then conception will not take place. If at some time the parents gather together in one place, and the father's body has excessive moisture, and the mother does not have this affliction, then conception will not take place.

「若复父母集在一处，父有风病，母有冷病，则不成胎。若复父母集在一处，母有风病，父有冷病，则非成胎。若复有时父母集在一处，父身水气偏多，母无此患，则非成胎。

If at some time the parents gather together in one place, and the father has the signs of having a son, and the mother has the signs of not having a son, then conception will not take place. If at some time the parents gather together in one place, and the mother has the signs of having a son, and the father has the signs of not having a son, then conception will not take place. If at some time the parents both have the signs of not having a son, then conception will not take place.

「若复有时父母集在一处，父相有子，母相无子，则不成胎。若有时父母集在一处，母相有子，父相无子，则不成胎。若复有时父母俱相无子，则非成胎。

If at some time the consciousness-spirit approaches the womb, and the father is not present, then conception will not take place. If at some time the parents should gather together in one place, but the mother is away and not present, then conception will not take place. If at some time the parents should gather together in one place, and the father's body encounters a serious illness, and the consciousness-spirit comes to approach, then conception will not take place. If at some time the parents should gather together in one place, and the consciousness-spirit comes to approach, but the mother's body obtains a serious illness, then conception will not take place. If at some time the parents should gather together in one place, and the consciousness-spirit comes to approach, but the parents' bodies both obtain illness, then conception will not take place.

「若复有时识神趣胎，父行不在，则非成胎。若复有时父母应集一处，然母远行不在，则不成胎。若复有时父母应集一处，然父身遇重患，时识神来趣，则非成胎。若复有时父母应集一处，识神来趣，然母身得重患，则非成胎。若复有时父母应集一处，识神来趣，然复父母身俱得疾病，则非成胎。

If at some time, monks! the parents gather together in one place, the parents are without illness, and the consciousness-spirit comes to approach, and the parents both have the signs of having a son, then conception will take place. This is said to be the three causes and conditions for coming to receive conception. Therefore, monks! you should seek means to cut off the three causes and conditions. Thus, monks! you should train in this.

「若复，比丘！父母集在一处，父母无患，识神来趣，然复父母俱相有儿，此则成胎。是谓有此三因缘而来受胎。是故，比丘，当求方便，断三因缘。如是，诸比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said, and joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
