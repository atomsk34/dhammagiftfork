EĀ 21.5

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

21.5 （五）瞿波离

Parallel to: SN 6.9 / SN 6.10 / AN 4.3 (~) / AN 10.89 / Snp 3.10 / SĀ 1278 / SĀ² 2.276

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the bhikṣu Kauṇḍinya went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. Then the bhikṣu said to the World-Honored One, "The bhikṣus Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana are very evil in their conduct and have committed many evil deeds."

尔时，瞿波离比丘至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。尔时，彼比丘白世尊曰：「此舍利弗、目揵连比丘所行甚恶，造诸恶行。」

The World-Honored One said, "Do not speak like that. You should give rise to a joyful mind towards the Tathāgata. The bhikṣus Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana are pure in their conduct and have no evil deeds."

世尊告曰：「勿作是语。汝发欢喜心于如来所，舍利弗、目揵连比丘所行纯善，无有诸恶。」

At that time, the bhikṣu Kauṇḍinya repeated three times to the World-Honored One, "What the Tathāgata says is certainly not false. However, the bhikṣus Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana are very evil in their conduct and have no roots of goodness."

是时，瞿波离比丘再三白世尊曰：「如来所说诚无虚妄；然舍利弗、目揵连比丘所行甚恶，无有善本。」

The World-Honored One said, "You are a foolish person, do you not believe what the Tathāgata says? You say that the bhikṣus Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana are very evil in their conduct. You will now create these evil deeds, and the retribution you receive will not be long in coming."

世尊告曰：「汝是愚人，不信如来之所说乎？方言：『舍利弗、目揵连比丘所行甚恶。』汝今造此恶行，后受报不久。」

At that time, the bhikṣu immediately developed a malignant tumor on his body, the size of a mustard seed, then the size of a soybean, gradually the size of an āmalaka fruit, then the size of a walnut, and finally the size of a cupped hand. Pus and blood flowed out, his body disintegrated, and he died, taking rebirth in the Lotus Hell.

尔时，彼比丘即于坐上，身生恶疮，大如芥子，转如大豆，渐如阿摩勒果，稍如胡桃，遂如合掌，脓血流逸，身坏命终，生莲华地狱中。

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana heard that Bāhu had passed away, and he went to where the World-Honored One was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side. After a while, he withdrew and said to the World-Honored One, "Where has Bāhu the bhikṣu been reborn?"

是时，尊者大目揵连闻瞿波离命终，便至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。斯须退坐，白世尊曰：「瞿波离比丘为生何处？」

The World-Honored One said, "He has been reborn in the Lotus Hell."

世尊告曰：「彼命终者生莲华地狱中。」

At that time, Maudgalyāyana said to the World-Honored One, "I now wish to go to that hell to teach and transform those people."

是时，目连白世尊曰：「我今欲往至彼地狱，教化彼人。」

The World-Honored One said, "Maudgalyāyana! There is no need to go there."

世尊告曰：「目连！不须往彼。」

Maudgalyāyana again said to the World-Honored One, "I wish to go to that hell to teach and transform those people."

目连复重白世尊曰：「欲往至彼地狱中，教化彼人。」

At that time, the World-Honored One also remained silent and did not respond.

尔时，世尊亦默然不对。

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, in the time it takes for a strong man to bend and stretch his arm, disappeared from Śrāvastī and appeared in the Lotus Great Hell. At that time, the monk Guṇaprabha was burning with fire and had a hundred oxen plowing his tongue.

是时，尊者大目揵连如力士屈申臂顷，从舍卫没不现，便至莲华大地狱中。当尔时，瞿波离比丘身体火然，又有百头牛，以犁其舌。

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana was sitting cross-legged in the sky and snapped his fingers to address the monk.

尔时，尊者大目揵连在虚空中结跏趺坐，弹指告彼比丘。

The monk looked up and asked, "Who are you?"

彼比丘即仰问曰：「汝是何人？」

Maudgalyāyana replied, "Gautama, I am a disciple of Śākyamuni Buddha, named Maudgalyāyana, with the surname Kūrita."

目揵连报曰：「瞿波离！我是释迦文佛弟子，字目揵连，姓拘利陀。」

At that time, the monk, seeing Maudgalyāyana, uttered these evil words: "Even though I have fallen into this evil state, I still cannot escape you!" Having said this, at that very moment, a thousand oxen were yoked to plow his tongue.

是时，比丘见目连已，吐此恶言：「我今堕此恶趣，犹不免汝前乎？」说此语讫，即其时以有千头牛以犁其舌。

Maudgalyāyana, seeing this, was even more sorrowful and remorseful. He then disappeared from there and returned to Śrāvastī. He went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and stood to one side. At that time, Maudgalyāyana related this cause and condition in detail to the World-Honored One.

目连见已，倍增愁悒，生变悔心，即于彼没，还至舍卫国至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面住。尔时，目连以此因缘具白世尊。

The World-Honored One said, "I told you before not to go there to see that evil person."

世尊告曰：「我前语汝，不须至彼见此恶人。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

The axe of a person's life is
in their mouth, which cuts their body
due to evil speech. Their breath and
my breath, these two are both good
Having already committed evil deeds, one falls
into evil destinies. This is the most
evil, with an end and no end
Slandering the Tathāgata is the heaviest. Ten
thousand three hundred and sixty-one ash hells
slandering the sages falls into those, created
by body and speech.

「夫士之生，　　斧在口中，　　所以斩身，由其恶言；　　彼息我息，　　此二俱善。已造恶行，　　斯堕恶趣，　　此为最恶，有尽无尽，　　向如来恶，　　此者最重。一万三千，　　六一灰狱，　　谤圣堕彼，身口所造。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "You should learn three dharmas and accomplish their practice. What are the three? Good bodily actions, good verbal actions, and good mental actions. Thus, bhikṣus! You should learn in this way."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「当学三法，成就其行。云何为三？身行善、口行善、意行善。如是，比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
