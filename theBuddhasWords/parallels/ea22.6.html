EĀ 22.6

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

22.6 （六）法

Parallel to: AN 3.2–9

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "There are three characteristics and three dharmas of fools that cannot be relied upon. What are the three? Here, fools contemplate what should not be contemplated; they discuss what should not be discussed; they practice what should not be practiced.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「愚人有三相三法，不可恃怙。云何为三？于是，愚者不可思惟而思惟之；不可论说而论说之；不可行者而修习之。

How do fools contemplate what should not be contemplated? Here, fools contemplate three kinds of thoughts. What are the three? Here, fools give rise to jealous thoughts towards others' wealth and women, and give rise to evil thoughts and words, thinking, 'May what belongs to them belong to me.' Thus, fools contemplate what should not be contemplated.

「云何愚者不可思惟而思念之？于是，愚者意三行便思忆之。云何为三？于是，愚者起嫉心，于他财物及于女色，心念恶言悉兴嫉心：『彼之所有，愿是我许。』如是，愚者不可思惟而思惟之。

How do fools discuss what should not be discussed? Here, fools commit four verbal offenses. What are the four? Here, fools always delight in false speech, frivolous speech, harsh speech, and causing division between others. Thus, fools commit four verbal offenses.

「云何愚者不可论说而论说之？于是，愚者造口四过。云何为四？于是，愚者恒喜妄言、绮语、恶口、鬪乱彼此。如是，愚者造口四过。

How do fools commit evil deeds? Here, fools commit evil deeds with the body, always thinking of killing, stealing, and sexual misconduct. Thus, fools commit evil deeds. Thus, monks! Fools have these three practices, and foolish people practice these three things.

「云何愚者造于恶行？于是，愚者造身恶行，常念杀生、窃盗、淫泆。如是，愚者造于恶行。如是，比丘！愚者有此三行，愚痴之人习此三事。

Furthermore, monks! The wise have three things to practice. What are the three? Here, the wise should contemplate what should be contemplated, discuss what should be discussed, and practice what is good.

「复次，比丘！智者有三事，当念修行。云何为三？于是，智者应思惟者，便思惟之；应论说者，便论说之；应行善者，便修行善。

How do the wise contemplate what should be contemplated? Here, the wise contemplate three mental practices. What are the three? Here, the wise are not jealous, angry, or deluded, always practicing right view. Seeing others' wealth, they do not give rise to thoughts. Thus, the wise contemplate what should be contemplated.

「彼云何智者应思惟事，便思惟之？于是，智者思惟意三行。云何为三？于是，智者不嫉妬、恚、痴，常行正见，见他财货，不生想念。如是，智者应思惟者，便思惟之。

How do the wise discuss what should be discussed? Here, the wise accomplish four verbal practices. What are the four? Here, the wise do not speak falsehoods, do not teach others to speak falsehoods, and do not rejoice when they see others speaking falsehoods. This is called the wise guarding their speech.

「云何智者应论说之？于是，智者成就口四行。云何为四？于是，智者不行妄语，亦不教人妄语，见人妄语者意不喜乐，是谓智者而护其口。复次，智者不行绮语、恶口、鬪乱彼此，亦不教人使行绮语、恶口、鬪乱。如是，智者成就口四行。

How do the wise accomplish three bodily practices? Here, the wise contemplate bodily practices without transgression. Furthermore, the wise do not kill, do not teach others to kill, and do not rejoice when they see others killing. They do not steal, do not teach others to steal, and do not rejoice when they see others stealing. They do not engage in sexual misconduct, and when they see the appearance of another's woman, their mind does not give rise to thoughts. They do not teach others to engage in sexual misconduct. Even when they see an old woman, they regard her as their own relative, a middle-aged woman as their sister, and a young woman as their younger sister, without any sense of superiority or inferiority. Thus, the wise accomplish these three bodily practices. This is called the practice of the wise.

「云何智者成就身三行？于是，智者思惟身行，无所触犯。然复智者自不杀生，亦不教人杀生，见人杀者心不喜乐。自不偷窃，不教人盗，见人盗者心不喜乐。亦不淫泆，见他女人色，心不起想，亦不教人使行淫泆。设见老母，视之如己亲，中者如姊，小者如妹，意无高下。如是，智者身成就三行，是谓智者所行。如是，比丘！有此三有为之相。是故，诸比丘！愚者三相常当舍离，此三智者所行，不发斯须。如是，诸比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha taught and joyfully practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
