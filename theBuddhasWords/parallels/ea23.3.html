EĀ 23.3

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

23.3 （三）耳

Parallel to: AN 6.55 / MĀ 123 / SĀ 254

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at the Thunder Sound Pond in the country of Campā.

一时，佛在占波国雷声池侧。

At that time, Venerable Vīradatta was in a quiet place, cultivating the Dharma on his own, not abandoning the twelve practices of austerity, walking back and forth day and night, not apart from the teachings of the thirty-seven factors of enlightenment. Whether sitting or walking, he always practiced the true Dharma. In the early, middle, and late watches of the night, he constantly encouraged himself, not letting up for a moment. However, he was still unable to attain liberation from the defilements of desire. At that time, the place where Venerable Vīradatta walked back and forth was damaged by his feet, and blood flowed out, filling the sides of the road, like a place where cattle were slaughtered. Crows and magpies ate the blood, but he was still unable to attain liberation from the defilements of desire. At that time, Venerable Vīradatta thought: "Among the disciples of Śākyamuni Buddha who practiced austerities and diligently advanced, I am the foremost. However, my mind is still not liberated from the defilements. Moreover, my family business has much wealth and treasures. I should give it up and return to being a layperson, and use the wealth to widely give alms. But now, being a renunciant is very difficult and not easy!"

是时，尊者二十亿耳在一静处，自修法本，不舍头陀十二法行，昼夜经行，不离三十七道品之教，若坐、若行，常修正法，初夜、中夜、竟夜，恒自克励，不舍斯须，然复不能于欲漏法心得解脱。是时，尊者二十亿耳所经行处，脚坏血流，盈满路侧，犹如屠牛之处，乌鹊食血，然复不能于欲漏心得解脱。是时，尊者二十亿耳便作是念：「释迦文佛苦行精进弟子中，我为第一，然我今日漏心不得解脱；又我家业，多财饶宝，宜可舍服，还作白衣，持财物广惠施。然今作沙门，甚难不易！」

At that time, the World-Honored One, knowing from afar the thoughts in Vīradatta's mind, immediately soared through the sky to the place where he was walking back and forth, spread out his sitting mat, and sat down. At that time, Venerable Vīradatta went before the Buddha, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side.

尔时，世尊遥知二十亿耳心之所念，便腾游虚空，至彼经行处，敷坐具而坐。是时，尊者二十亿耳前至佛所，头面礼足，在一面坐。

At that time, the World-Honored One asked Vīradatta, "Why did you have such thoughts just now? 'Among the disciples of Śākyamuni Buddha who practiced austerities and diligently advanced, I am the foremost. However, my mind is still not liberated from the defilements. Moreover, my family business has much wealth and treasures. I should give it up and return to being a layperson, and use the wealth to widely give alms. But now, being a renunciant is very difficult and not easy.'"

尔时，世尊问二十亿耳曰：「汝向何故作是念：『释迦文尼佛精进苦行弟子中，我为第一，然我今日漏心不得解脱；又我家业，饶财多宝，宜可舍服，还作白衣，持财物广施。然今作沙门，甚难不易。』」

Vīradatta replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

二十亿耳对曰：「如是。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "I will now ask you a question. Answer me as you see fit. Vīradatta, when you were at home, were you good at playing the zither?"

世尊告曰：「我今还问汝，随汝报我。云何，二十亿耳！汝本在家时，善弹琴乎？」

Vīradatta replied, "Yes, World-Honored One! When I was at home, I was good at playing the zither."

二十亿耳对曰：「如是。世尊！我本在家时，善能弹琴。」

The World-Honored One said, "Vīradatta, if the strings are too tight, will the sound of the zither be harmonious and pleasant?"

世尊告曰：「云何，二十亿耳！若弹弦极急，向不齐等，尔时琴音可听采不？」

Vīradatta replied, "No, World-Honored One!"

二十亿耳对曰：「不也。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "Vīradatta, if the strings are too loose, will the sound of the zither be harmonious and pleasant?"

世尊告曰：「云何，二十亿耳！若琴弦复缓，尔时琴音可听采不？」

Vīradatta replied, "No, World-Honored One!"

二十亿耳对曰：「不也，世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "Vīradatta, if the strings are neither too tight nor too loose, will the sound of the zither be harmonious and pleasant?"

世尊告曰：「云何，二十亿耳！若琴弦不急不缓，尔时琴音可听采不？」

Vīradatta replied, "Yes, World-Honored One! If the strings are neither too loose nor too tight, the sound of the zither will be harmonious and pleasant."

二十亿耳对曰：「如是。世尊！若琴弦不缓不急，尔时琴音便可听采。」

The World-Honored One said, "It is the same here. Those who are too diligent are like those who play too much; those who are lazy fall into wrong views; those who are able to be in the middle are those who progress. In this way, they will soon become those who are free from taints."

世尊告曰：「此亦如是。极精进者，犹如调戏；若懈怠者，此堕邪见；若能在中者，此则上行。如是不久，当成无漏人。」

At that time, after the World-Honored One had spoken the subtle Dharma to Bhikṣu Vīradatta, he returned to the side of the Thunder Sound Pond.

尔时，世尊与二十亿耳比丘说微妙法已，还雷音池侧。

At that time, Venerable Vīradatta contemplated the World-Honored One's instructions, not letting go for a moment, and practiced the Dharma in a secluded place. The reason for the noble sons leaving home to study the path, shaving their beards and hair, and practicing the unsurpassed pure conduct is that they have exhausted birth and death, established the pure conduct, accomplished what was to be done, and will no longer accept existence, knowing it as it is. Venerable Vīradatta then attained arahantship.

尔时，尊者二十亿耳思惟世尊教勅，不舍须臾，在闲静处修行其法。所以族姓子出家学道，剃除须发，修无上梵行：生死已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，更不复受有，如实知之。尊者二十亿耳便成阿罗汉。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Among my śrāvakas, the foremost disciple in diligent practice and austerity is none other than Bhikṣu Vīradatta."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「我声闻中第一弟子精懃苦行，所谓二十亿耳比丘是。」

At that time, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and upheld it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
