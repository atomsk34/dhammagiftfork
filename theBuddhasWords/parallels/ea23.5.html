EĀ 23.5

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

23.5 （五）逆顺香

Parallel to: AN 3.79 / SĀ 1073 / SĀ² 2.12

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Ānanda, in a secluded place, gave rise to this thought: "In the world, is there any fragrance that can be smelled both against the wind and with the wind, and both against and with the wind?" At that time, Venerable Ānanda rose from his seat, went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side.

尔时，尊者阿难在闲静处，便生此念：「世间颇有此香，亦逆风香，亦顺风香，亦逆顺风香乎？」尔时，尊者阿难便从坐起，往诣世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。

At that time, Venerable Ānanda addressed the World-Honored One, saying: "In a secluded place, I gave rise to this thought: 'In the world, is there any fragrance that can be smelled both against the wind and with the wind, and both against and with the wind?'"

尔时，尊者阿难白世尊曰：「我于闲静之处，便生此念：『世间颇有此香，亦逆风香，亦顺风香，亦逆顺风香乎？』」

At that time, the World-Honored One told Ānanda: "There is such a wonderful fragrance that can be smelled both against the wind and with the wind, and both against and with the wind."

尔时，世尊告阿难曰：「有此妙香，亦逆风香，亦顺风香，亦逆顺风香。」

Then, Ānanda addressed the World-Honored One, saying: "What is this fragrance that can be smelled both against the wind and with the wind, and both against and with the wind?"

是时，阿难白世尊曰：「此是何者香？亦逆风香，亦顺风香，亦逆顺风香。」

The World-Honored One said: "There is this fragrance, and the power of this fragrance can be smelled both against the wind and with the wind, and both against and with the wind."

世尊告曰：「有此之香，然此香气力，亦逆风香，亦顺风香，亦逆顺风香。」

Ānanda addressed the Buddha, saying: "What is this fragrance that can be smelled both against the wind and with the wind, and both against and with the wind?"

阿难白佛言：「此何等香，亦逆风香，亦顺风香，亦逆顺风香。」

The World-Honored One said: "These three kinds of fragrances can be smelled both against the wind and with the wind, and both against and with the wind."

世尊告曰：「此三种香，亦逆风香，亦顺风香，亦逆顺风香。」

Ānanda said: "What are the three?"

阿难言：「何等为三？」

The World-Honored One said: "The fragrance of precepts, the fragrance of hearing, and the fragrance of giving. Ānanda, these are the three kinds of fragrances. Moreover, these fragrances can be smelled both against the wind and with the wind, and both against and with the wind. Among all the fragrances in the world, these three kinds of fragrances are the most supreme, the most excellent, incomparable, and unsurpassed. Just as from cows comes milk, from milk comes butter, and from butter comes ghee, and this ghee is the most supreme, the most excellent, incomparable, and unsurpassed. It is the same with all the fragrances in the world. These three kinds of fragrances are the most supreme, the most excellent, incomparable, and unsurpassed."

世尊告曰：「戒香、闻香、施香。是谓，阿难！有此香种，然复此逆风香，亦顺风香，亦逆顺风香。诸世间所有之香，此三种香最胜、最上，无与等者，无能及者。犹如由牛有酪，由酪有酥，由酥有醍醐，然此醍醐最胜、最上，无与等者，亦不能及。此亦如是，诸所有世间诸香，此三种香] cf.【丽】最胜、最上，无能及者。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

The fragrance of wood honey and sandalwood, the fragrance of blue lotus
and all fragrances, and also various kinds of fragrances, the fragrance of
precepts is the most supreme. With this accomplishment of precepts, there is
no desire and no defilement, equal wisdom and liberation, where even Māra
does not know. Although this fragrance is wonderful, and the fragrance of
sandalwood and honey, the fragrance of precepts is the most wonderful, and
it is smelled throughout the ten directions. Although sandalwood has fragrance, and
blue lotus and other fragrances, among all these various fragrances, the fragrance
of precepts is the most supreme. Although sandalwood has fragrance, and blue
lotus and other fragrances, among all these various fragrances, the fragrance of
giving is the most supreme.

「木蜜及栴檀，　　优钵及诸香，亦诸种种香，　　戒香最为胜。此戒以成就，　　无欲无所染，等智而解脱，　　逝处魔不知。此香虽为妙，　　及诸檀蜜香，戒香之为妙，　　十方悉闻之。栴檀虽有香，　　优钵及余香，此诸众香中，　　闻香最第一。栴檀虽有香，　　优钵及余香，此诸众香中，　　施香最第一。

"These are called the three kinds of fragrances, which are fragrant against the wind, fragrant with the wind, and fragrant both against and with the wind. Therefore, Ānanda! You should seek the means to accomplish these three fragrances. Thus, Ānanda! You should train in this way."

「是谓此三种香，亦逆风香，亦顺风香，亦逆顺风香。是故，阿难！当求方便，成此三香。如是，阿难！当作是学。」

At that time, Ānanda heard what the Buddha said and joyfully upheld it.

尔时，阿难闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
