EĀ 24.5

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

24.5 （五）神足化

Parallel to: MN 26 (~) / MĀ 204 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at the Bodhi tree in the country of Magadha, having just attained Buddhahood.

一时，佛在摩竭国道场树下，初始得佛。

At that time, the World-Honored One had this thought: "I have now attained this profound Dharma, which is difficult to understand, difficult to comprehend, difficult to realize, and difficult to know. It is extremely subtle and can only be realized by the wise. Whom should I teach this Dharma to first? Who can understand my Dharma?" At that time, the World-Honored One had this thought: "The faculties of Rahula are mature and he should be the first to be liberated. Moreover, he is waiting for me to have the Dharma." Having had this thought, a god in the sky said to the World-Honored One: "Rahula died seven days ago."

尔时，世尊便作是念：「我今以得此甚深之法，难解、难了、难晓、难知，极微极妙智所觉知，我今当先与谁说法？使解吾法者是谁？」尔时，世尊便作是念：「罗勒迦蓝诸根纯熟，应先得度，又且待我有法。」作此念已，虚空中有天白世尊曰：「罗勒迦蓝死已七日。」

At that time, the World-Honored One had this thought: "How painful! He did not hear my Dharma before passing away. If he had heard my Dharma, he would have been liberated." At that time, the World-Honored One had this thought: "Whom should I teach the Dharma to first, so that they may be liberated? Now Udraka Ramaputra should be the first to be liberated. I shall teach him. Having heard my Dharma, he will be liberated first." The World-Honored One had this thought, and a god in the sky said: "He passed away last night at midnight." At that time, the World-Honored One had this thought: "How painful for Udraka Ramaputra! He did not hear my Dharma before passing away. If he had heard my Dharma, he would have been liberated.

是时，世尊复作念曰：「何其苦哉，不闻吾法，而取命终；设当闻吾法者，即得解脱。」是时，世尊复作是念：「我今先与谁说法，使得解脱？今欝头蓝弗先应得度，当与说之，闻吾法已，先得解脱。」世尊作是念，虚空中有天语言：「昨日夜半，以取命终。」是时，世尊便作是念：「欝头蓝弗何其苦哉！不闻吾法，而取命过；设得闻吾法者，即得解脱。」

At that time, the World-Honored One had this thought: "Who will hear the Dharma first and be liberated?" At that time, the World-Honored One further contemplated: "The five bhikshus have been of great benefit to me. They followed me when I was born." At that time, the World-Honored One had this thought: "Where are the five bhikshus now?" He then used his divine eye to see that the five bhikshus were staying in the deer park of the sage Udraka Ramaputra in Varanasi. "I shall now go to first teach the Dharma to the five bhikshus. Having heard my Dharma, they will be liberated.

尔时，世尊复作是念：「谁先闻法而得解脱？」是时，世尊重更思惟：「五比丘多所饶益，我初生时，追随吾后。」是时，世尊复作是念：「今五比丘竟为所在？」即以天眼观五比丘，乃在波罗［木＊奈］仙人鹿园所止之处。「我今当往先与五比丘说法，闻吾法已，当得解脱。」

At that time, the World-Honored One gazed at the Bodhi tree for seven days without blinking. At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊七日之中熟视道树，目未曾眴。尔时，世尊便说此偈：

Here I sat, experiencing the sufferings of birth and death, wielding
the axe of wisdom to forever sever the root of rebirth
The heavenly kings came here, as well as the demons and
their followers, and I further subdued them with skillful means, causing
them to wear the crown of liberation. Now, under this tree
sitting on the vajra seat, I have attained omniscience and attained
unobstructed wisdom. I sat under this tree, seeing the sufferings of
birth and death, and have already removed the root of death
with old age and sickness forever gone.

「我今此坐处，　　经历生死苦，执御智慧斧，　　永断根元栽。天王来至此，　　及诸魔怨属，复以方便降，　　令著解脱冠。今于此树下，　　坐于金刚床，以获一切智，　　逮无所碍慧。我坐此树下，　　见生死之苦，已却死元本，　　老病永无余。」

At that time, after the World-Honored One spoke this verse, he immediately rose from his seat and left, wishing to go to the country of Varanasi.

尔时，世尊说此偈已，便从坐起而去，欲向波罗［木＊奈］国。

At that time, the brahmin Upagupta saw from afar the World-Honored One's radiant appearance, eclipsing the brightness of the sun and moon. Having seen this, he said to the World-Honored One, "Gautama! Where is your teacher now? From whom did you go forth to study the path? What teachings do you always delight in speaking? Where do you come from? Where are you going?"

是时，优毘伽梵志遥见世尊光色炳然，翳日月明，见已，白世尊曰：「瞿昙！师主今为所在？为依何人出家学道？恒喜说何法教？为从何来？为欲所至？」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse to the brahmin:

尔时，世尊向彼梵志，而说此偈：

I have attained arahantship, unsurpassed in the world. Among gods and humans
I am now the foremost. I have no teacher or protector, and
none equal to me. I am the sole honored one, without surpasser
cool and no longer warm. Now I shall turn the wheel of
the Dharma, going to the land of Kashi. Now with the medicine
of ambrosia, I shall open the eyes of the blind and ignorant
In the land of Varanasi, the territory of King Kashi, where the
five bhikshus reside, I wish to speak the subtle Dharma. May they
quickly attain the path and attain the supernormal knowledge of the exhaustion
of taints, eliminating the fundamental evil dharmas. Therefore, I am the most excellent one.

「我成阿罗汉，　　世间最无比，天及世间人，　　我今最为上。我亦无师保，　　亦复无与等，独尊无过者，　　冷而无复温。今当转法轮，　　往诣加尸邦，今以甘露药，　　开彼盲冥者。波罗［木＊奈］国界，　　加尸国王土，五比丘住处，　　欲说微妙法。使彼早成道，　　及得漏尽通，以除恶法元，　　是故最为胜。」

At that time, the brahmin sighed in admiration, bowed his head with palms joined, snapped his fingers and smiled, leading the way as they departed.

时，彼梵志叹咤，俨头叉手，弹指含笑，引道而去。

At that time, the World-Honored One went to Varanasi. At that time, the five bhikshus saw the World-Honored One coming from afar. Having seen him, they discussed among themselves, "This is the Shakyamuni Gautama coming from afar, with a confused nature and an undistracted mind. We should not speak to him, nor should we rise to greet him, nor should we invite him to sit down."

时，世尊往诣波罗［木＊奈］。是时，五比丘遥见世尊来，见已各共论议：「此是沙门瞿昙从远而来，情性错乱，心不专精。我等勿复共语，亦莫起迎，亦莫请坐。」

At that time, the five people spoke this verse:

尔时，五人便说此偈：

This person is not worthy of respect, nor should we look at him with
affection. Do not call him 'welcome,' nor should we invite him to sit down.

「此人不应敬，　　亦莫共亲视，勿复称善来，　　亦莫请使坐。」

At that time, after the five people spoke this verse, they all remained silent. Then the World-Honored One gradually approached the five bhikshus. At that time, the five bhikshus gradually got up to welcome him, some offering seats, some offering water. Then the World-Honored One went forward and sat down, thinking: "These are foolish people, they cannot keep their original promise." At that time, the five bhikshus addressed the World-Honored One as "you." Then the World-Honored One said to the five bhikshus: "You should not address the unsurpassed, truly awakened, and perfectly enlightened one as 'you.' Because I have now attained unsurpassed, true, perfect enlightenment, and have obtained the nectar of the good. Listen attentively to my Dharma words.

尔时，五人说此偈已，皆共默然。尔时，世尊至五比丘所，渐渐欲至。时，五比丘渐起来迎，或与敷座] cf.【丽】者，或与取水者。尔时，世尊即前就坐，作是思惟：「此是愚痴之人，竟不能全其本限。」尔时，五比丘称世尊为卿，是时，世尊告五比丘曰：「汝等莫卿无上至真、等正觉。所以然者，我今已成无上至真、等正觉，已获甘露善。自专念，听吾法语。」

At that time, the five bhikshus said to the World-Honored One: "Gautama! When you were practicing austerities before, you still could not attain the Dharma of the superior person. How much less so now, with your mind confused, do you claim to have attained the Way?"

尔时，五比丘白世尊曰：「瞿昙！本苦行时，尚不能得上人之法，况复今日意情错乱，言得道乎？」

The World-Honored One said: "How is it, five people! Have you ever heard me speak falsely?"

世尊告曰：「云何，五人！汝等曾闻吾妄语乎？」

The five bhikshus said: "No, Gautama!"

五比丘曰：「不也。瞿昙！」

The World-Honored One said: "The Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One has already attained the nectar. You all should concentrate your minds and listen to my teaching." At that time, the World-Honored One again had this thought: "I am now able to subdue these five people."

世尊告曰：「如来、等正觉已得甘露，汝等悉共专心，听吾说法。」是时，世尊便复作是念：「我今堪任降此五人。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the five bhikshus: "You should know that there are these four truths. What are the four? The truth of suffering, the truth of the origin of suffering, the truth of the cessation of suffering, and the truth of the path leading to the cessation of suffering. What is meant by the truth of suffering? Namely, the suffering of birth, the suffering of old age, the suffering of sickness, the suffering of death, the suffering of sorrow, grief, and distress, the suffering of worries and anxieties, the suffering of not being able to obtain what one desires, in short, the suffering of the five aggregates. This is called the truth of suffering.

是时，世尊告五比丘：「汝等当知，有此四谛。云何为四？苦谛、苦习谛、苦尽谛、苦出要谛。彼云何名为苦谛？所谓生苦、老苦、病苦、死苦、忧悲恼苦、愁忧苦痛，不可称记。怨憎会苦、恩爱别苦、所欲不得，亦复是苦，取要言之，五盛阴苦，是谓苦谛。

What is meant by the truth of the origin of suffering? Namely, the part of craving that is experienced, the constant practice of it, and the constant attachment to it. This is called the truth of the origin of suffering.

「云何苦习谛？所谓受爱之分，习之不惓，意常贪著，是谓苦习谛。

What is meant by the truth of the cessation of suffering? Namely, the complete cessation of craving without remainder, and not giving rise to it again. This is called the truth of the cessation of suffering.

「彼云何苦尽谛？能使彼爱灭尽无余，亦不更生，是谓苦尽谛。

What is meant by the truth of the path leading to the cessation of suffering? Namely, the eightfold noble path, namely, right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right concentration. This is called the Dharma of the Four Noble Truths.

「彼云何名为苦出要谛？所谓贤圣八品道。所谓等见、等治、等语、等业、等命、等方便、等念、等定。是谓名为四谛之法。

Moreover, O five bhikṣus! As for these four noble truths, the truth of suffering gives rise to the eye, knowledge, insight, awakening, light, and wisdom, which have not been heard before. Furthermore, the truth of suffering is real, certain, not false or deluded, and will never be different. Because it is spoken by the World-Honored One, it is called the truth of suffering. The truth of the origin of suffering gives rise to the eye, knowledge, insight, awakening, light, and wisdom, which have not been heard before. Furthermore, the truth of the origin of suffering is real, certain, not false or deluded, and will never be different. Because it is spoken by the World-Honored One, it is called the truth of the origin of suffering. The truth of the cessation of suffering gives rise to the eye, knowledge, insight, awakening, light, and wisdom, which have not been heard before. Furthermore, the truth of the cessation of suffering is real, certain, not false or deluded, and will never be different. Because it is spoken by the World-Honored One, it is called the truth of the cessation of suffering. The truth of the path leading to the cessation of suffering gives rise to the eye, knowledge, insight, awakening, light, and wisdom, which have not been heard before. Furthermore, the truth of the path leading to the cessation of suffering is real, certain, not false or deluded, and will never be different. Because it is spoken by the World-Honored One, it is called the truth of the path leading to the cessation of suffering.

「然复，五比丘！此四谛之法，苦谛者眼生、智生、明生、觉生、光生、慧生，本未闻法。复次，苦谛者，实、定，不虚不妄，终不有异；世尊之所说，故名为苦谛。苦习谛者，本未闻法，眼生、智生、明生、觉生、光生、慧生。复次，苦习谛者，实、定，不虚不妄，终不有异；世尊之所说，故名为苦习谛，苦尽谛者，本未闻法，眼生、智生、明生、觉生、慧生、光生。复次，苦尽谛者，实、定，不虚不妄，终不有异；世尊之所说，故名为苦尽谛。苦出要谛者，本未闻法，眼生、智生、明生、觉生、光生、慧生。复次，苦出要谛者，实、定，不虚不妄，终不有异；世尊之所说，故名为苦出要谛。

O five bhikṣus, you should know that these four noble truths, with their three turns and twelve aspects, if not truly understood, one cannot attain unsurpassed perfect enlightenment. Because I have discerned these four noble truths with their three turns and twelve aspects, and truly understood them, therefore I have attained unsurpassed perfect enlightenment.

「五比丘当知，此四谛者，三转十二行，如实不知者，则不成无上正真、等正觉。以我分别此四谛三转十二行，如实知之、是故成无上至真、等正觉。」

At that time, when this Dharma was spoken, Ajnatakauṇḍinya's defilements were completely eliminated and he attained the purity of the Dharma eye.

尔时，说此法时，阿若拘隣诸尘垢尽，得法眼净。

"At that time, the World-Honored One said to Kauṇḍinya, "Have you now attained the Dharma and realized the Dharma?

是时，世尊告拘隣曰：「汝今以逮法、得法？」

Kauṇḍinya replied, "Yes, World-Honored One! I have attained the Dharma and realized the Dharma."

拘隣报曰：「如是。世尊！以得法、逮法。」

At that time, the Earth Deity, having heard these words, proclaimed, "Now the Tathāgata, in the country of Vārāṇasī, turns the wheel of the Dharma. What cannot be turned by devas, humans, Māra, or Māra's followers, or by humans or non-humans, today the Tathāgata turns this wheel of the Dharma. Ājñātakauṇḍinya has attained the Dharma of immortality."

是时，地神闻此语已，作是唱：「今如来在波罗［木＊奈］国转法轮。诸天、世人、魔、若魔天、人及非人所不能转者，今日如来转此法轮，阿若拘隣已得甘露之法。」

At that time, the Four Heavenly Kings, having heard the proclamation from the Earth Deity, further proclaimed, "Ājñātakauṇḍinya has attained the Dharma of immortality."

是时，四天王从地神闻唱令声，复传告曰：「阿若拘隣以得甘露之法。」

At that time, the Thirty-three Heavens further heard from the Four Heavenly Kings, the Yāma Heavens heard from the Thirty-three Heavens, and so on up to the Tuṣita Heavens, the sound was passed on. Even up to the Brahmā Heaven, the sound was also heard: "The Tathāgata, in Vārāṇasī, turns the wheel of the Dharma. What cannot be turned by devas, humans, Māra, or Māra's followers, or by humans or non-humans, today the Tathāgata turns this wheel of the Dharma." At that time, it was then called Ājñātakauṇḍinya.

是时，三十三天复从四天王闻，艶天从三十三天闻，乃至兜术天展转闻声，乃至梵天亦复闻声：「如来在波罗［木＊奈］转法轮，诸天、世人、魔、若魔天、人及非人所不转者，今日如来转此法轮。」尔时，便名为阿若拘隣。

At that time, the World Honored One said to the five bhikṣus: "You two should stay and receive instruction, while the three go to beg for food. The food obtained by the three should be shared by the six. When the three stay and receive instruction, the two go to beg for food. The food obtained by the two should be taken by the six." At that time, the instruction was the Dharma of non-arising nirvāṇa, and also the non-arising, non-illness, non-aging, and non-death. At that time, the five bhikṣus all attained arahantship. At that time, in the trichiliocosm, there were five arhats, with the Buddha as the sixth.

尔时，世尊告五比丘：「汝等二人住受教诲，三人乞食，三人所得食者，六人当共食之；三人住受教诲，二人往乞食，二人所得食者，六人当取食之。」尔时教诲，此时成无生涅槃法，亦成无生、无病、无老、无死。是时，五比丘尽成阿罗汉。是时，三千大千刹土有五阿罗汉，佛为第六。

At that time, the World Honored One said to the five bhikṣus: "You should all go together to beg for food in the human realm, and do not go alone. However, for those sentient beings whose faculties are ripe and can be liberated, I will now go to the village of Uruvilvā and teach the Dharma there."

尔时，世尊告五比丘：「汝等尽共人间乞食，慎莫独行。然复众生之类，诸根纯熟，应得度者，我今当往优留毘村聚，在彼说法。」

At that time, the World Honored One went to the village of Uruvilvā. At that time, by the side of the Nairañjanā River, there was a Kāśyapa who lived there. He was well-versed in astronomy and geography, and was well-versed in all calculations and counting of tree leaves. He had five hundred disciples whom he taught daily. Not far from Kāśyapa, there was a stone chamber where a poisonous dragon lived.

尔时，世尊便往至优留毘村聚所。尔时，连若河侧有迦叶在彼止住，知天文、地理，靡不贯博，算数树叶皆悉了知，将五百弟子，日日教化。去迦叶不远有石室，于石室中，有毒龙在彼止住。

At that time, the World Honored One arrived at Kāśyapa's place. Having arrived, he said to Kāśyapa: "I wish to stay in the stone chamber for one night. If you allow it, I will go and stay there."

尔时，世尊至迦叶所。到已，语迦叶言：「吾欲寄在石室中一宿；若见听者，当往止住。」

Kāśyapa replied: "I do not begrudge it, but there is a poisonous dragon there who may harm you."

迦叶报曰：「我不爱惜，但彼有毒龙，恐相伤害耳。」

The World Honored One said: "Kāśyapa! Do not worry! The dragon will not harm me. But if you allow it, I will stay for one night."

世尊告曰：「迦叶！无苦！龙不害吾，但见听许，止住一宿。」

Kāśyapa replied: "If you wish to stay, go ahead and stay."

迦叶报曰：「若欲住者，随意往住。」

At that time, the World Honored One went to the stone chamber, spread out his seat, and stayed overnight. He sat cross-legged, with his body and mind upright, and focused his attention in front of him. At that time, the poisonous dragon saw the World Honored One sitting and immediately spewed fire and poison. At that time, the World Honored One entered the samadhi of loving-kindness. From the samadhi of loving-kindness, he entered the samadhi of flaming light. At that time, the dragon's fire and the Buddha's light were both produced at the same time.

尔时，世尊即往石室，敷座而宿，结跏趺坐，正身正意，系念在前。是时，毒龙见世尊坐，便吐火毒。尔时，世尊入慈三昧，从慈三昧起，入焰光三昧。尔时，龙火、佛光一时俱作。

At that time, Kāśyapa got up at night to observe the stars. He saw a great firelight in the stone chamber. Having seen it, he immediately told his disciples: "This Śramaṇa Gautama has a dignified appearance. Now he is harmed by a dragon. It is very pitiful! I also said before: 'There is an evil dragon there. It is not suitable to stay overnight.'" At that time, Kāśyapa told his five hundred disciples: "You take water bottles and a high ladder to save that fire and let that Śramaṇa escape this danger."

尔时，迦叶夜起，瞻视星宿，见石室中，有大火光。见已，便告弟子曰：「此瞿昙沙门容貌端政，今为龙所害，甚可怜慜！我先亦有此言：『彼有恶龙，不可止宿。』」是时，迦叶告五百弟子：「汝持水瓶，及舆高梯，往救彼火，使彼沙门得济此难。」

At that time, Kāśyapa, together with his five hundred disciples, went to the stone chamber to save the fire. Some sprinkled water, some provided ladders, but they could not make the fire go out immediately. This was all due to the Tathāgata's majestic spiritual power. At that time, the World Honored One entered the samadhi of loving-kindness, gradually causing the dragon to become free from anger. At that time, the evil dragon was terrified and ran around, wanting to get out of the stone chamber, but it could not get out of the stone chamber. At that time, the evil dragon came towards the Tathāgata and entered the World Honored One's alms bowl to stay.

The next morning, Kāśyapa went to where the World-Honored One was and asked the World-Honored One: "There was no pond here before, but now there is a pond; there was no tree here before, but now there is a tree; there was no stone here before, but now there is a stone. What are the causes and conditions for these changes?"

明日清旦，迦叶至世尊所，问世尊曰：「本无此池，今有此池；本无此树，今有此树；本无此石，今有此石。有何因缘，而有此变？」

The World-Honored One said: "This pond was made last night by the Heavenly King Śakra, knowing that I wanted to wash my robe. I then thought: 'Where should I beat and wash this robe?' The Four Heavenly Kings, knowing what I was thinking in my mind, brought this stone. I then thought: 'Where should I dry this robe?' The tree spirit knew what I was thinking in my mind and bent down this tree branch."

世尊告曰：「此是昨夜天帝释知吾欲浣衣，故作此浴池。吾复作是念：『当于何处蹋浣此衣？』时四天王知吾心中所念，便持此石来。吾复作是念：『当于何处而曝此衣？』时树神知我心中所念，便垂此树枝耳。」

At that time, Kāśyapa again thought: "Although this Śramaṇa Gautama is divine, he is still not as true as my attainment of the Way."

是时，迦叶复作是念：「此沙门瞿昙虽神，故不如我得道真。」

At that time, the World-Honored One, having eaten, returned to stay there. At that time, in the middle of the night, a great black cloud arose and a heavy rain fell, flooding the river like a great river. At that time, Kāśyapa again thought: "This river is flooded. The śramaṇa will surely be swept away by the water. I will now look at him." At that time, Kāśyapa and his five hundred disciples went to the river. At that time, the World-Honored One was walking on the water, his feet not being soaked by the water. At that time, Kāśyapa saw from afar the World-Honored One walking on the water. At that time, Kāśyapa then thought: "How wonderful! How extraordinary! The śramaṇa Gautama is able to walk on the water. I am also able to walk on the water, but I cannot make my feet unstained. Although this śramaṇa is divine, he is still not as true as my attainment of the Way."

是时，世尊食已，还于彼宿。是时，夜半有大黑云起而作大雨，连若大河，极为瀑溢。是时，迦叶复作是念：「此河瀑溢，沙门必当为水所漂，我今看之。」是时，迦叶及五百弟子往至河所，尔时世尊在水上行，脚不为水所渍。是时，迦叶遥见世尊在水上行。是时，迦叶便作是念：「甚奇！甚特！沙门瞿昙乃能在水上行，我亦能在水上行，但不能使脚不污耳。此沙门虽神，故不如我得道真。」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to Kāśyapa: "You are not an arhat either, and you do not know the path of an arhat. You do not even know the name of an arhat, let alone attaining the Way. You are blind, seeing nothing. The Tathāgata has manifested so many transformations, yet you say: 'I am true in attaining the Way.' You now say: 'I can walk on water.' Now is the right time. Can we walk on water together? You should now abandon your wrong views, so as not to suffer for a long time."

是时，世尊语迦叶言：「汝亦非阿罗汉，复不知阿罗汉道，汝尚不识阿罗汉名，况得道乎？汝是盲人，目无所覩，如来现尔许变化，故言：『不如我得道真。』汝方作是语：『吾能在水上行。』今正是时，可共在水上行耶？汝今可舍邪见之心，无令长夜受此苦恼。」

At that time, having heard the words of the World-Honored One, Kāśyapa immediately went forward, bowed his head at the feet, and said: "I now repent and deeply know that it is not the Dharma. I have touched the Tathāgata. I only pray that you accept my repentance." He said this three times.

是时，迦叶闻世尊语已，便前头面礼足：「我今悔过，深知非法，乃触如来，唯愿受悔。」如是再三。

The World-Honored One said: "I allow you to repent of your mistake. You are able to know for yourself that you have disturbed the Tathāgata."

世尊告曰：「听汝改过，乃能自知触扰如来。」

At that time, Kāśyapa told his five hundred disciples: "Each of you should do as you see fit. I now take refuge in the śramaṇa Gautama."

是时，迦叶告五百弟子曰：「汝等各随所宜，我今自归沙门瞿昙。」

At that time, the five hundred disciples said to Kāśyapa: "We also had the intention to go to the śramaṇa Gautama. When he subdued the nāga, we wanted to take refuge. If the teacher takes refuge in Gautama, then all of us five hundred disciples will also take refuge in Gautama."

是时，五百弟子白迦叶言：「我等先亦有心于沙门瞿昙，当降龙时，寻欲归命。若师自归瞿昙者，我等五百弟子尽自归于瞿昙所。」

Kāśyapa replied: "Now is the right time. However, my mind is still attached to this ignorance. Although I have seen so many transformations, my mind is still not enlightened. Therefore, I claim that my path is truly correct."

迦叶报言：「今正是时，然复我心执此愚痴，见尔许变化，意由不解，故自称言：『我道真正。』」

At that time, Kāśyapa, together with his five hundred disciples, surrounded the World-Honored One in front and behind. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and stood to one side. He said to the World-Honored One: "I only wish that the World-Honored One would allow us to become śramaṇas and cultivate holy life."

是时，迦叶将五百弟子，前后围绕，至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面立，白世尊曰：「唯愿世尊听我等得作沙门！修清净行。」

The constant Dharma of the Buddhas is that if one is called "Welcome, bhikṣu!" one becomes a śramaṇa. At that time, the World-Honored One said to Kāśyapa: "Welcome, bhikṣu! This Dharma is subtle and good for cultivating the holy life." At that time, the robes worn by Kāśyapa and his five hundred disciples all transformed into monastic robes, and their hair fell out by itself, as if shaved. After seven days, Kāśyapa's learning tools and spells were all thrown into the water.

诸佛常法。若称：「善来，比丘！」便成沙门。是时，世尊告迦叶曰：「善来，比丘！此法微妙，善修梵行。」是时，迦叶及五百弟子所著衣裳，尽变作袈裟，头发自落，如似剃发，以经七日。是时，迦叶学术之具及于呪术，尽投水中。

At that time, the five hundred disciples said to the World-Honored One: "We only wish that the World-Honored One would allow us to become śramaṇas."

时，五百弟子白世尊曰：「唯愿世尊听我等得作沙门。」

The World-Honored One said: "Welcome, bhikṣus!" At that time, the five hundred disciples immediately became śramaṇas, with their robes on and their hair falling out by itself.

世尊告曰：「善来，比丘！」时，五百弟子即成沙门，袈裟著身，头发自落。

At that time, flowing downstream, there was a brāhman named Nāḍīkāśyapa, staying by the water. At that time, Nāḍīkāśyapa saw the magical implements all being carried away by the water, and he thought: "Alas! My elder brother is being drowned by the water!" At that time, Nāḍīkāśyapa, together with his three hundred disciples, went upstream to search for his elder brother's corpse. From afar, he saw the World-Honored One sitting under a tree, surrounded by Mahākāśyapa and his five hundred disciples, expounding the Dharma. Having seen this, he went forward to Kāśyapa and said: "Is this good? You were originally a teacher of others, but now you are a disciple. Why did your elder brother become a disciple of a śramaṇa?"

尔时，顺水下流，有梵志，名江迦叶，在水侧住。是时，江迦叶见呪术之具，尽为水所漂，便作是念：「咄哉！我大兄为水所溺？」是时，江迦叶将三百弟子，顺水上流，求兄尸骸。遥见世尊在一树下坐，及大迦叶五百弟子前后围绕而为说法。见已，便前至迦叶所，而作是语：「此事为好耶？本为人师，今为弟子。大兄何故与沙门作弟子乎？」

Kāśyapa replied: "This place is wonderful, there is nothing better than this place."

迦叶对曰：「此处为妙，无过此处。」

At that time, Upagu Kāśyapa spoke this verse to Nāḍīkāśyapa:

是时，优毘迦叶向江迦叶而说此偈：

This teacher is honored by humans and gods. I now serve him
The Buddhas appear in the world, which is very difficult to encounter.

「此师人天贵，　　我今师事之，诸佛兴出世，　　甚为难得遇。」

At that time, Nāḍīkāśyapa heard the Buddha's name and was very joyful, leaping with delight. He went before the World-Honored One and said, "I wish to be allowed to practice the path."

是时，江迦叶闻佛名号，甚怀欢喜，踊跃不能自胜，前白世尊：「愿听为道。」

The World-Honored One said, "Welcome, bhikṣu! Cultivate the holy life and end the cycle of suffering."

世尊告曰：「善来，比丘！善修梵行，尽于苦际。」

At that time, Nāḍīkāśyapa and his three hundred disciples became śramaṇas. The robes covered their bodies and their hair fell out by itself. At that time, Nāḍīkāśyapa and his three hundred disciples threw all their magical implements into the water.

是时，江迦叶及三百弟子即成沙门，袈裟著身，头发自落。是时，江迦叶及三百弟子呪术之具，尽投水中。

At that time, there was a brāhman named Geya Kāśyapa who was living by the river. Seeing the magical implements being carried away by the water, he thought, "My two elder brothers are practicing the Way upstream. Now the magical implements are being carried away by the water. The two great Kāśyapa brothers must have been harmed by the water." He then led two hundred disciples upstream to the place of practice. Seeing his two elder brothers had become śramaṇas, he said, "Is this place good? You were once honored by people, but now you are disciples of a śramaṇa.

尔时，顺水下头有梵志，名伽夷迦叶，在水侧住。遥见呪术之具，为水所漂，便作是念：「我有二兄，在上学道。今呪术之具，尽为水所漂，二大迦叶必为水所害。」即将二百弟子，顺水上流，乃至学术之处，遥见二兄而作沙门，便作是语：「此处好耶？本为人尊，今为沙门弟子。」

Kāśyapa replied, "This place is the most wonderful, there is nothing better than this place."

迦叶报曰：「此处最妙，无过此处。」

At that time, Geya Kāśyapa thought, "Now my two elder brothers are knowledgeable and learned. This place must be a good place for them to practice the Way. I can also practice the Way here." Then Geya Kāśyapa said to the World-Honored One, "May the World-Honored One allow me to become a śramaṇa."

是时，伽夷迦叶便作是念：「今我二兄多知博学，此处必是善地，使我二兄在中学道，我今亦可在中学道。」是时，伽夷迦叶前白世尊：「唯愿世尊听作沙门。」

The World-Honored One said, "Welcome, bhikṣu! Practice the holy life and end suffering."

世尊告曰：「善来，比丘！善修梵行，尽于苦际。」

At that time, Geya Kāśyapa immediately became a śramaṇa. The kāṣāya robe was on his body, and his hair fell out by itself, as if shaved. It had been seven days.

是时，伽夷迦叶即成沙门，袈裟著身，头发自落，如似剃头，以经七日。

At that time, the World-Honored One was staying by the river, under a nyagrodha tree, not long after attaining Buddhahood, with a thousand disciples, all of whom were senior elders. At that time, the World-Honored One taught and transformed them in three ways. What were the three? Namely, teaching and transforming through supernatural powers, teaching and transforming through verbal teachings, and teaching and transforming through instruction.

是时，世尊在彼河侧住，尼拘类树下，成佛未久，将千弟子，是皆耆旧宿长。是时，世尊以三事教化。云何为三？所谓神足教化，言教教化，训诲教化。

What is meant by teaching and transforming through supernatural powers? At that time, the World-Honored One sometimes took on various forms and then combined them into one, sometimes disappearing and then appearing. He passed through stone walls without obstruction, sometimes emerging from the ground or entering the ground, like flowing water, without any obstruction. He sometimes sat cross-legged in the sky, like a bird flying through the air without obstruction. He was also like a great volcano, emitting immeasurable smoke. The sun and moon have great divine power and cannot be measured. He could reach up to the Brahma Heaven with his hand. Thus the World-Honored One manifested supernatural powers.

彼云何名为神足教化？尔时，世尊或作若干形，还合为一，或不现，或现。石壁皆过，无所罣碍，或出地，或入地，犹如流水，无所触碍，或结跏趺坐，满虚空中，如鸟飞空，无有罣碍。亦如大火山，烟出无量。此日月有大神力，不可限量，以手往捉，身乃至梵天。如是，世尊现神足。

What is meant by teaching and transforming through verbal teachings? At that time, the World-Honored One taught the bhikṣus: "Abandon this, put aside this. Approach this, distance yourself from this. Remember this, forget this. Observe this, do not observe this." What should be cultivated and what should not be cultivated? Cultivate the seven factors of awakening and eliminate the three fetters. What should be observed and what should not be observed? Observe the three fetters and the good of the śramaṇa: namely, the joy of renunciation, the joy of non-anger, and the joy of non-hatred. What should not be observed? Namely, the three kinds of suffering of the śramaṇa. What are the three? Namely, the observation of desire, the observation of anger, and the observation of hatred. What should be remembered and what should not be remembered? At that time, remember the truth of suffering, remember the truth of the origin, remember the truth of cessation, remember the truth of the path; do not remember the false truths: the view of eternalism, the view of nihilism, the view of having an end, the view of having no end, the view of having a self, the view of having no self, the view of the Tathāgata's having an end, the view of the Tathāgata's not having an end, the view of having an end and not having an end, the view of neither having an end nor not having an end. Do not have these thoughts.

彼云何名为言教教化？尔时，世尊教诸比丘：「当舍是、置是。当近是、远是。当念是、去是。当观是、不观是。」彼云何当修是、不修是者，当修七觉意，灭三结。彼云何当观、当不观者？当观三结、沙门善：所谓出要乐、无恚乐、无怒乐。彼云何不观？所谓三沙门苦。云何为三？所谓欲观、恚观、怒观。彼云何念、云何不念？尔时，当念苦谛，当念习谛，当念尽谛，当念道谛；莫念邪谛：有常见、无常见，有边见、无边见，彼命、彼身，非命、非身，如来命终、如来不命终，有终、有不终，亦不有终、亦不无终。莫作是念。

What is meant by teaching and transforming through instruction? Furthermore, one should go like this and not go like this, come like this and not come like this, be silent and speak like this: "One should wear such robes and not wear such robes, one should enter the village like this and not enter the village like this." This is called teaching and transforming through instruction.

彼云何名为训诲教化？复次，当作是去、不应作是去，作是来、不应作是来，默然、作是言说：「当持如是衣、不应持如是衣，应如是入村、不应如是入村。」是谓名为训诲教化。

At that time, the World-Honored One used these three methods to teach and transform the thousand bhikṣus. At that time, the bhikṣus accepted the Buddha's teachings, and the thousand bhikṣus all attained arahantship. At that time, the World-Honored One saw that the thousand bhikṣus had attained arahantship. At that time, there were a thousand arhats and five bhikṣus in Jambudvīpa. The Buddha was the teacher of the six teachers, and he turned to face Kapilavastu.

是时，世尊以此三事，教化千比丘。是时，彼比丘受佛教已，千比丘尽成阿罗汉。是时，世尊以见千比丘得罗汉，尔时阎浮里地有千阿罗汉及五比丘，佛为六师，回坐向迦毘罗卫。

At that time, Upagupta thought, "Why is the World-Honored One facing Kapilavastu?" At that time, Upagupta went forward, knelt for a long time, and said to the World-Honored One, "I do not know why the Tathāgata is facing Kapilavastu."

是时，优毘迦叶便作是念：「世尊何故向迦毘罗卫坐？」是时，优毘迦叶即前长跪，白世尊曰：「不审如来，何故向迦毘罗卫坐耶？」

The World-Honored One said, "When the Tathāgata is in the world, there are five things to be done. What are the five? First, one should turn the wheel of the Dharma. Second, one should teach the Dharma to one's father. Third, one should teach the Dharma to one's mother. Fourth, one should guide ordinary people to establish the practice of bodhisattvas. Fifth, one should bestow the bodhisattva precepts. These, Kāśyapa, are the five dharmas that the Tathāgata should practice when appearing in the world."

世尊告曰：「如来在世间，应行五事。云何为五？一者当转法轮，二者当与父说法，三者当与母说法，四者当导凡夫人立菩萨行，五者当授菩萨别。是谓，迦叶！如来出世当行此五法。」

At that time, Upagupta again thought, "The Tathāgata is thinking of his relatives and hometown, so he is facing that direction."

是时，优毘迦叶复作是念：「如来故念亲族本邦，故向彼坐耳。」

At that time, the five bhikṣus gradually came to the side of the Nairañjanā River, went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed their heads to his feet, and sat to one side.

是时，五比丘渐来至尼连水侧，到世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。

At that time, Venerable Udayin saw from afar that the World-Honored One was facing Kapilavastu and sitting. Having seen this, he thought, "The World-Honored One must wish to go to Kapilavastu to see his relatives." At that time, Udayin then knelt for a long time and said to the World-Honored One, "I am now able to ask about something. I only wish for you to explain it."

是时，尊者优陀耶遥见世尊向迦毘罗卫坐，见已，便作是念：「世尊必当欲往至迦毘罗卫见诸亲里。」是时，优陀耶即前长跪，白世尊曰：「我今堪任欲有所问，唯愿敷演。」

The World-Honored One said, "Ask what you wish to ask."

世尊告曰：「欲所问者便问之。」

Udayin said to the World-Honored One, "Judging from the Tathāgata's intention, you wish to go to Kapilavastu."

优陀耶白世尊曰：「观如来意，欲向迦毘罗卫。」

The World-Honored One said, "So it is, as you say. Udayin should know, go first to King Śuddhodana. I will go later. The reason is, the kṣatriya class should first send a messenger to inform, then the Tathāgata will go. When you arrive, tell the king, 'After seven days, the Tathāgata will come to see the king.'"

世尊告曰：「如是，如汝所言。优陀耶当知，先至白净王所，吾比后当往。所以然者，刹利之种先当遣使令知，然后如来当往。汝到，语王：『却后七日，如来当来见王。』」

Udayin replied, "So it is, World-Honored One!" At that time, Udayin immediately rose from his seat, adjusted his robes, bowed at the feet of the World-Honored One, and disappeared before the World-Honored One. He went to Kapilavastu and arrived at King Śuddhodana's place. Having arrived, he stood before the king.

优陀耶对曰：「如是。世尊！」是时，优陀耶即从坐起，整衣服，礼世尊足，于世尊前不现，往至迦毘罗卫，到真净王所。到已，在王前立。

At that time, King Śuddhodana was sitting in the great hall with his concubines. At that time, Udayin flew in the sky. When King Śuddhodana saw Udayin holding a bowl and staff standing in front, he became terrified and said, "Who is this? Is it a human or non-human? A god or ghost? A yakṣa, rākṣasa, god, dragon, or spirit?"

尔时，真净王在大殿上坐，及诸婇女。是时，优陀耶飞在空中。时真净王见优陀耶手执钵，持杖而在前立，见已便怀恐怖，而作是说：「此是何人？人耶？非人耶？天耶？鬼耶？阅叉、罗刹、天、龙、鬼神耶？」

At that time, King Śuddhodana asked Udayin, "Who are you?" He also spoke this verse to Udayin:

时，真净王问优陀耶曰：「汝是何人？」又以此偈向优陀耶说：

Are you a god or a ghost? A gandharva or
other? What is your name now? I wish to know it.

「为天为是鬼？　　干沓惒等乎？汝今名为谁？　　我今欲知之？」

At that time, Udayin again replied to the king with this verse:

是时，优陀耶复以此偈报王曰：

I am not a god, nor a gandharva. I am
a person from the kingdom of Kapilavastu. In the past
I destroyed the eighteen koṭis of the evil Māra Pāpīyas
My teacher is Śākyamuni, the true disciple of the Buddha.

「我亦非是天，　　非是干沓惒，于是迦毘国，　　大王邦土人。昔坏十八亿，　　弊魔波旬众，我师释迦文，　　是彼真弟子。」

At that time, King Śuddhodana again spoke to Udayin with this verse:

时，真净王复以此偈，向优陀耶说：

Who destroyed the eighteen koṭis of the evil Māra Pāpīyas? Who
is called Śākyamuni? You should now praise and speak of him.

「谁坏十八亿，　　弊魔波旬众？谁字释迦文？　　汝今叹说之。」

At that time, Udayin again spoke this verse:

是时，优陀耶复说此偈：

When the Tathāgata was first born, the earth shook
greatly. His vows were all accomplished, and today he
is called Siddhārtha! He subdued the eighteen koṭis of
the evil Māra Pāpīyas, and today he has attained
buddhahood! He is the lion among men, the disciple
of Gautama. Today he has become a śramaṇa, and
his original name was Udayin!

「如来初生时，　　天地普大动，誓愿悉成办，　　今曰号悉达！彼降十八亿，　　弊魔波旬众，彼名释迦文，　　今日成佛道！彼人释师子，　　瞿昙次弟子，今日作沙门，　　本字优陀耶！」

At that time, King Śuddhodana, having heard these words, was filled with joy and could not contain himself. He said to Udayin, "Is it true, Udayin, that Prince Siddhārtha is still alive?"

是时，真净王闻此语已，便怀欢喜，不能自胜，语优陀耶曰：「云何，优陀耶！悉达太子今故在耶？」

Udayin replied, "The Buddha Śākyamuni is present today."

优陀耶报言：「释迦文佛今日现在。」

The king asked, "Has he already attained Buddhahood?"

时王问言：「今已成佛耶？」

Udayin replied, "He has already attained Buddhahood."

优陀耶报言：「今已成佛。」

The king further asked, "Where is the Tathāgata now?"

王复问言：「今日如来竟为所在？」

Udayin replied, "The Tathāgata is now under a niṣadha tree in the Magadha country."

优陀耶报言：「如来今在摩竭国界尼拘类树下。」

The king then asked, "Who are the disciples who accompany him?"

时王报言：「翼从弟子斯是何人？」

Udayin replied, "There are billions of devas and a thousand bhikṣus, as well as the Four Heavenly Kings, who are always at his side."

优陀耶报曰：「诸天亿数及千比丘、四天王，恒在左右。」

The king then asked, "What kind of clothes does he wear?"

时王问言：「所著衣服，为像何类？」

Udayin replied, "The Tathāgata's clothes are called kāsāya."

优陀耶报言：「如来所著衣裳，名曰袈裟。」

The king then asked, "What kind of food does he eat?"

时王问言：「食何等食？」

Udayin replied, "The Tathāgata's body is nourished by the Dharma."

优陀耶报言：「如来身者，以法为食。」

The king then asked, "Can the Tathāgata be seen?"

王复问曰：「云何，优陀耶！如来可得见不？」

Udayin replied, "Do not be anxious, Your Majesty. In seven days, the Tathāgata will come to enter the city."

优陀耶报言：「王勿愁悒，却后七日，如来当来入城。」

At that time, the king was extremely joyful and could not contain himself. He personally served and offered to Udayin.

是时，王极欢喜，不能自胜，手自斟酌，供养优陀耶。

At that time, King Śuddhodana beat a great drum and ordered the people of the country to level the roads, remove impurities, sprinkle the ground with fragrant water, hang silk banners and canopies, and perform countless forms of music and entertainment. He also ordered that all the deaf, blind, mute, and dumb in the country be hidden from view. After seven days, Siddhattha would come to enter the city. When King Śuddhodana heard that the Buddha would come to enter the city, he did not sleep for seven days.

是时，真净王击大鸣鼓，勅国界人民，平治道路，除去不净，以香汁洒地，悬缯幡盖，作倡伎乐，不可称计。复勅国中诸有聋、盲、瘖、痖者，尽使不现，却后七日，悉达当来入城。是时，真净王闻佛当来入城，七日之中，亦不睡眠。

At that time, on the seventh day, the World-Honored One thought, "I should now go to the country of Kapilavastu by means of my supernatural powers." At that time, the World-Honored One, together with the bhikṣus surrounding him in front and behind, went to the country of Kapilavastu. Having arrived, he went to the Sālu Garden to the north of the city.

是时，世尊以至七日，便作是念：「我今宜可以神足力，往诣迦毘罗卫国。」是时，世尊即将诸比丘，前后围遶，往诣迦毘罗卫国。到已，便诣城北萨卢园中。

At that time, King Śuddhodana heard that the World-Honored One had already arrived at the Sālu Garden to the north of the city of Kapilavastu. At that time, King Śuddhodana, together with the Śākya assembly, went to where the World-Honored One was.

是时，真净王闻世尊已达迦毘罗卫城北萨卢园中。是时，真净王将诸释众，往诣世尊所。

At that time, the World-Honored One again thought, "If King Śuddhodana comes in person, it is not appropriate for me. I should now go to meet him. The reason is that the kindness of parents is heavy, and their nurturing affection is very deep." At that time, the World-Honored One, together with the assembly of bhikṣus, went to the city gate and flew in the sky, seven fathoms above the ground.

是时，世尊复作是念：「若真净王躬自来者，此非我宜，我今当往与共相见。所以然者，父母恩重，育养情甚。」是时，世尊将诸比丘众，往诣城门，飞在虚空，去地七仞。

At that time, King Śuddhodana saw that the World-Honored One was incomparably handsome, a rarity in the world, with tranquil faculties and few thoughts, adorned with the thirty-two marks and eighty minor marks, and his body was adorned with them. He gave rise to a joyful mind and immediately bowed his head to the feet, saying, "I am a king of the kṣatriya caste, named King Śuddhodana."

是时，真净王见世尊端政无比，世之希有，诸根寂静，无众多念，身有三十二相、八十种好，而自庄身，发欢喜之心，即便头面礼足，而作是说：「我是刹利王种，名曰真净王。」

The World-Honored One said, "May the great king enjoy endless longevity. Therefore, great king! Rule with the right Dharma, not with the wrong Dharma. Great king, you should know that those who rule with the right Dharma, when their bodies perish and their lives end, will be reborn in the good realms of heaven."

世尊告曰：「令大王享寿无穷。是故，大王！当以正法治化，勿用邪法。大王当知，诸有用正法治化者，身坏命终，生善处天上。」

At that time, the World-Honored One then walked through the air and arrived at King Śuddhodana's palace. Having arrived, he took his seat.

是时，世尊即空中行，至真净王宫中。到已，就座而坐。

At that time, the king saw that the World-Honored One had taken his seat. He personally served various kinds of food and drink. Seeing that the World-Honored One had finished eating, he served clean water and then took a small seat to listen to the sūtra.

时，王见世尊坐定，手自斟酌，行种种饮食，见世尊食竟，行净水，更取一小座而听经。

At that time, the World-Honored One gradually spoke the wonderful meaning with King Śuddhodana. The discussion was about giving, precepts, and the discourse on rebirth in heaven. He spoke about the impurity of desire and the bliss of liberation. At that time, the World-Honored One saw that the king's mind was open and his intentions were resolved. He spoke to the king about the teachings that all Buddha-World-Honored Ones always speak about: suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path.

尔时，世尊与真净王渐说妙义，所谓论者：施论、戒论、生天之论，欲不净行，出要为乐。尔时，世尊见王心开意解，诸佛世尊常所说法，苦、习、尽、道，尽向王说。

At that time, King Śuddhodana immediately attained the purity of the Dharma-eye on that seat, with all defilements exhausted.

是时，真净王即于座上，诸尘垢尽，得法眼净。

At that time, the World-Honored One, having spoken the Dharma to the king, immediately rose from his seat and departed.

是时，世尊与王说法已，即从座起而去。

At that time, King Śuddhodana gathered the Śākya assembly and said, "The śramaṇas have extremely ugly appearances. The Śākya clan should gather with the brāhmaṇa assembly. This is not appropriate; the Śākya clan should gather with the Śākya assembly. This is excellent."

是时，真净王普集释众，而作是说：「诸沙门等，颜貌极丑，刹利之种，将诸梵志众，此非其宜；刹利释种还得刹利众，此乃为妙。」

The Śākyas replied, "Yes, great king! As the great king commands, the Śākya clan should gather with the Śākya assembly. This is excellent."

诸释报言：「如是，大王！如大王教，刹利还得刹利众，此乃为妙。」

At that time, the king announced to the country, "If there are brothers who have two sons, one should be made to practice the path. Those who do not comply will be severely punished."

是时，王告国中：「诸有兄弟二人，当取一人作道，其不尔者，当重谪罚。」

At that time, the Śākya assembly heard the king's order: "If there are brothers who have two sons, one should be made to practice the path. Those who do not comply will be severely punished." At that time, Devadatta Śākya said to Ānanda Śākya, "Today, King Śuddhodana has ordered: 'If there are brothers who have two sons, one should be made to practice the path.' Now you should go forth and practice the path. I will stay at home and manage the household affairs." At that time, Ānanda Śākya joyfully leaped up and replied, "As my elder brother commands."

时，诸释众闻王教令：「诸有兄弟二人，当取一人为道，其不从教，当重谪罚。」是时，提婆达兜释种语阿难释言：「真净王今日有教：『诸有兄弟二人，当分一人作道。』汝今出家学道，我当在家修治家业。」是时，阿难释欢喜踊跃，报言：「如兄来教。」

At that time, Nanda Śākya said to Aniruddha Śākya, "King Śuddhodana has ordered: 'If there are brothers who have two sons, one should be made to practice the path. Those who do not comply will be severely punished.' Now you should go forth and practice the path. I will stay at home." At that time, Aniruddha Śākya, upon hearing these words, was overjoyed and could not contain himself. He replied, "Yes, as my elder brother commands."

是时，难陀释语阿那律释言：「真净王有教：『其有兄弟二人者，当分一人作道，其不尔者，当重谪罚。』汝今出家，我当在家。」是时，阿那律释闻此语已，欢喜踊跃，不能自胜，报曰：「如是。如兄来教。」

At that time, King Śuddhodana brought Śuddhodana Śākya, Śuddhodana Śākya, and Śuddhodana Śākya to where the World-Honored One was. At that time, he rode in a chariot drawn by four horses, with a white chariot, white canopy, and white horses. The second Śākya rode in a chariot with a blue canopy and blue horses. The third Śākya rode in a chariot with a yellow canopy and yellow horses. The fourth Śākya rode in a chariot with a red canopy and red horses. At that time, some Śākyas rode elephants and some rode horses, all coming to assemble.

是时，真净王将斛净、叔净释、甘露释，至世尊所。尔时，驾四马之车，白车、白盖，白马驾之；第二释乘青车、青盖，青马驾之；第三释乘黄车、黄盖，黄马驾之；第四释乘赤车、赤盖，赤马驾之。是时，诸释有乘象者，有乘马者，皆悉来集。

At that time, the World-Honored One saw from afar that King Śuddhodana was leading the Śākya assembly and said to the bhikṣus, "You should all look at this Śākya assembly and King Śuddhodana's assembly. Bhikṣus should know that when the Thirty-three Gods go out to the garden, it is also like this, without any difference."

是时，世尊遥见真净王将诸释众而来，告诸比丘：「汝等观此释众，并观真净王众。比丘当知，三十三天出园观时，亦如此法，无有异。」

At that time, Ānanda rode a large white elephant, wearing white clothes and a white canopy. Having seen this, he said to the bhikṣus, "Do you all see this Śākya Ānanda riding a white elephant and wearing white clothes?"

是时，阿难乘大白象、白衣、白盖。见已，告诸比丘：「汝等见此阿难释乘白象、白衣不乎？」

The bhikṣus replied, "Yes, World-Honored One! We see it."

诸比丘对曰：「唯然，世尊！我等见之。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "This person will leave home to study the path, be the foremost in learning, and be able to serve as an attendant. Do you all see this Aniruddha?"

佛告比丘：「此人当出家学道，第一多闻，堪侍左右。汝等见此阿那律不乎？」

The bhikṣus replied, "Yes, we see him."

诸比丘对曰：「唯然，见之。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "This person will leave home to study the path, and have the foremost divine eye."

佛告比丘：「此人当出家学道，天眼第一。」

At that time, King Śuddhodana and his four brothers, Nanda and Ānanda, all walked forward, removed their five adornments, went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed their heads at his feet, and sat to one side. Then King Śuddhodana said to the Buddha, "Last night I had this thought: 'It is not appropriate for the kṣatriya assembly to follow the brāhmaṇa assembly, but to follow the kṣatriya assembly is appropriate.' I then issued an order throughout the country: 'For those with two brothers, take one to leave home and study the path.' I only wish that the World-Honored One would allow them to leave home and study the path."

是时，真净王及兄弟四人，并难陀、阿难，皆步进前，除去五好，至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。尔时，真净王白佛言：「昨日夜生此念：『刹利之众不应将梵志众，还将刹利众，此是其宜。』我便告令国中：『诸有兄弟二人者，便取一人，使出家学道。』唯愿世尊听出家学道。」

The World-Honored One said, "Excellent! Great King! You will benefit many, and gods and humans will find peace. The reason is this: these good friends are a field of blessings and merit. I too, because of good friends, have escaped this birth, old age, sickness, and death." At that time, the Śākya assembly was able to enter the path.

世尊告曰：「善哉！大王！多所饶益，天、人得安。所以然者，此善知识良祐福田，我亦缘善知识，得脱此生、老、病、死。」是时，诸释众便得为道。

At that time, King Śuddhodana said to the World-Honored One, "I only wish that the World-Honored One would instruct these new bhikṣus as you instruct Udayin. The reason is this: this bhikṣu Udayin has great spiritual power. I wish that bhikṣu Udayin would always be in the palace to teach and transform, causing sentient beings to attain peace for a long time. The reason is this: this bhikṣu has great spiritual power. When I first saw bhikṣu Udayin, I immediately gave rise to a joyful mind. I then had this thought: 'Even the disciple has spiritual power, how could the Tathāgata not have this spiritual power?'"

是时，真净王白世尊曰：「唯愿世尊教诲此新比丘，当如教诲优陀耶！所以然者，此优陀耶比丘极有神力。愿优陀耶比丘恒在宫中教化，使众生之类，长夜获安隐。所以然者，此比丘极有神力，我初见优陀耶比丘，便发欢喜之心。我便作此念：『弟子尚有神力，况彼如来而无此神力乎？』」

The World-Honored One said, "So it is, Great King! As the Great King teaches, this bhikṣu Udayin has great spiritual power and great majestic virtue."

世尊告曰：「如是，大王！如大王教，此优陀耶比丘极有神力，有大威德。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "The foremost disciple with extensive knowledge and great learning, who is remembered by the king, is the bhikṣu Ājñātakauṇḍinya. The one who is able to inspire and transform the people is the bhikṣu Udayin. The one with swift wisdom is the bhikṣu Mahānāman. The one who always enjoys flying is the bhikṣu Subhūti. The one who comes and goes in the sky is the bhikṣu Bābhrava. The one with many disciples is the bhikṣu Uruvilvā-Kāśyapa. The one who attains the contemplation of emptiness is the bhikṣu Nadī-Kāśyapa. The one who attains the contemplation of cessation is the bhikṣu Hārita-Kāśyapa."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「第一弟子，博识多知，国王所念，所谓阿若拘邻比丘是。能劝化人民，优陀耶比丘是。有速疾智，所谓摩诃男比丘是。恒喜飞行，所谓须婆休比丘是。空中往来，所谓婆破比丘是。多诸弟子，所谓优毘迦叶比丘是。意得观空，所谓江迦叶比丘是。意得止观。所谓象迦叶比丘是。」

At that time, the World-Honored One extensively spoke the subtle Dharma to King Śuddhodana. Having heard the Dharma, the king rose from his seat, bowed his head at the feet, and then departed.

尔时，世尊广与真净王说微妙之法。尔时，王闻法已，即从坐起，头面礼足，便退而去。

At that time, the bhikṣus and King Śuddhodana, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘及真净王闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
