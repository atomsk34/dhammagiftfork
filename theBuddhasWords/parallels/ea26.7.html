EĀ 26.7

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

26.7 （七）阿夷

Parallel to: SN 3.22 / SĀ 1227 / SĀ² 2.54

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, King Prasenajit immediately ordered his ministers to prepare a jeweled carriage with feathered wheels, wishing to leave the city of Śrāvastī to view the lecture hall. At that time, King Prasenajit's mother had passed away. She was extremely old, approaching a hundred years. The king respected her deeply, never leaving her side. At that time, there was a great minister by the name of Bheṣaka, who was highly talented and respected by the world. He then thought, "King Prasenajit's mother is approaching a hundred years old. If he hears of her death today, he will be greatly saddened, unable to eat or drink, and will become seriously ill. I shall now devise a means to prevent the king from being sorrowful and ill."

尔时，王波斯匿即勅臣佐，严宝羽之车，欲出舍卫城观地讲堂。当于尔时，波斯匿王母命过，年极衰老，垂向百岁，王甚尊敬，念未曾离目。是时，波斯匿王边有大臣，名不奢蜜，高才盖世，世人尊重。时，大臣便作是念：「此波斯匿王母年向百岁，今日命终；设当闻者，王甚愁忧，不能饮食而得重病。我今当设方便，使王不愁忧，亦使不病。」

At that time, the great minister immediately prepared five hundred white elephants, five hundred horses, five hundred infantry, five hundred courtesans, five hundred old women, five hundred brahmins, five hundred śramaṇas, five hundred garments, and five hundred precious treasures. He made a large coffin for the deceased, painting it to the utmost perfection, hanging silk banners and canopies, and performing music and entertainment beyond measure, leaving the city of Śrāvastī.

是时，大臣即严驾五百白象，亦严驾五百疋马，复严五百步兵，复严驾五百妓女，复严驾五百老母，复严驾五百婆罗门，复有五百沙门，复严驾五百衣裳，复严驾五百珍宝，与亡者作好大棺，彩画极令使妙，悬缯幡盖，作倡妓乐，不可称计，出舍卫城。

At that time, King Prasenajit returned to the city. At that time, King Prasenajit had a minor matter. From afar, he saw the deceased and asked those around him, "Who is this person being honored in this way?"

是时，波斯匿王还来入城。是时，王波斯匿有少事，是时王遥见亡者，问左右曰：「此是何人，供养乃至于斯？」

At that time, Unsurpassed said, "In this city of Śrāvastī, there is a mother of an elder who has passed away. This is her funeral."

时不奢蜜曰：「此舍卫城中有长者母无常，是彼之具。」

The king then asked again, "What are these elephants, horses, and carriages used for?"

时王复告曰：「此诸象马车乘复用为？」

The minister replied, "These five hundred old mothers are offered to King Yama to redeem their lives."

大臣报曰：「此五百老母者，用奉上阎罗王，持用赎命。」

The king then laughed and said, "This is the method of fools. Life is hard to guarantee. What can be fixed? It is like a person falling into the mouth of a makaras fish, wanting to escape, but it is really difficult to get out. It is the same here, falling into the side of King Yama, wanting to escape, it is really hard to get out."

时，王便笑而作是说：「此是愚人之法，命也难保，有何可克？如有人堕摩竭鱼口，欲求出者，实复难得。此亦如是，堕阎罗王边，欲求出，实难可得。」

"These five hundred courtesans are also used to redeem their lives."

「此五百妓女亦用赎命。」

The king replied, "This is also difficult to obtain."

王报曰：「此亦难得。」

The minister said, "If these courtesans cannot be obtained, then use others to redeem them."

时大臣曰：「若此妓女不可得者，当用余者赎之。」

The king said, "This is also difficult to obtain."

王曰：「此亦难得。」

The minister said, "If this cannot be obtained, then use five hundred precious treasures to redeem them."

大臣曰：「若此不可得者，当用五百珍宝赎之。」

The king replied, "This is also difficult to obtain."

王报曰：「此亦难得。」

The minister said, "If this cannot be obtained, then use five hundred garments to redeem them."

大臣曰：「此不可得者，用五百衣裳赎之。」

The king said, "This is also difficult to obtain."

王曰：「此亦难得。」

The minister said, "If these garments cannot be obtained, then use these five hundred Brahmins' spells to obtain them through spells."

臣曰：「若此衣裳不可得者，当用此五百梵志呪术，呪术取之。」

The king said, "This is also difficult to obtain."

王曰：「此亦难得。」

The minister said, "If these five hundred Brahmins cannot be obtained, then use this monk's eloquence in teaching the Dharma to redeem them."

大臣曰：「若此五百梵志不可得者，复当持此沙门高才说法，持用赎之。」

The king said, "This cannot be obtained."

王曰：「此不可得。」

The minister said, "If the Dharma cannot be obtained, then gather the troops and fight a great battle to take it."

大臣曰：「若说法不可得者，当集兵众，共大战鬪而取之。」

At that time, King Prasenajit laughed and said, "This is the method of fools. Falling into the mouth of a makaras fish, one will never be able to escape."

时，波斯匿王大笑而曰：「此是愚人之法，以堕摩竭鱼口，终不得出。」

The king said, "You should know, is there anyone who is born and does not die?"

时王曰：「汝当知之，颇有生而不死乎？」

The great minister said, "This is truly impossible."

时大臣曰：「此实不可得也。」

The great king replied, "It is truly impossible. The Buddhas also say this. For those who are born, there is death. Life is also difficult to obtain."

时大王报曰：「实不可得，诸佛亦作是说，夫生有死，命亦难得。」

At that time, Unsurpassed Honey knelt and said to the king, "Therefore, great king! Do not be so worried. All sentient beings return to death."

是时，不奢蜜跪白王曰：「是故，大王！甚莫愁忧，一切众生皆归于死。」

The king asked, "Why should I be worried?"

时王问曰：「我何故愁忧？」

The minister said to the king, "You should know, great king, that your mother has died today."

时臣白王：「王当知之，大王母者，今日已死。」

When King Prasenajit heard these words, he sighed eight or nine times and said to the great minister, "Excellent! As you have said, you are able to know skillful means and expedient methods."

是时] cf.【丽】，波斯匿王闻此语已，八九叹息而语大臣曰：「善哉！如汝所言，乃能知善权方便。」

At that time, King Prasenajit returned to the city and prepared various incense and flowers to make offerings to his deceased mother. After making offerings to his deceased mother, he returned to where the World-Honored One was. Upon arrival, he bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side.

是时，王波斯匿还入城，办种种香华，供养亡母。供养亡母已，便还驾乘至世尊所。到已，头面礼足，在一面坐。

At that time, the World-Honored One asked, "Great King! Why is your body covered in dust and dirt?"

是时，世尊问曰：「大王！何故尘土坌身？」

The king said to the World-Honored One, "My heavenly mother passed away and was sent outside the city. I have now come to the World-Honored One to inquire about the reason. However, my heavenly mother, while alive, upheld the precepts, diligently practiced, and constantly cultivated good dharmas. She was nearly a hundred years old, but now she has passed away. That is why I have come to the World-Honored One. If I can redeem her life with elephants, I will use elephants to redeem it; if I can redeem her life with horses, I will use horses to redeem it; if I can redeem her life with carriages, I will use carriages to redeem it; if I can redeem her life with gold, silver, and precious treasures, I will use gold, silver, and precious treasures to redeem it; if I can redeem her life with slaves, servants, cities, or kingdoms, I will use cities, kingdoms, or slaves and servants to redeem her life; if I can redeem her life with the people of the kingdom of Kashi, I will use the people of Kashi to redeem her life. I do not want my heavenly mother to pass away."

王白世尊：「天母命终，向送至城外。今来诣世尊所，问其所由。然天母在时，持戒精进，恒修善法，年向百岁，今日已命终，故来至世尊所耳。若当我持象赎命可得者，亦当用象赎之；若当马赎命可得者，当用马赎之；若当车乘赎命可得者，便当用车乘赎之；若当金银珍宝赎命可得者，当用金银珍宝赎之；若当以奴婢、仆从、城廓、国界，赎命可得者，当以城廓、国界赎命；若以加尸国界人民赎命可得者，当以加尸人民赎之，莫令我天母命终。」

The World-Honored One said, "Therefore, Great King! Do not be so sorrowful. All sentient beings return to death. All changing dharmas, if one wishes for them not to change, it will never happen. Great King! You should know that the human body is like a snow ball, which will eventually disintegrate; it is also like a clay pot, which will also disintegrate and cannot be preserved; it is also like a mirage, illusory and unreal; it is also like an empty fist, deceiving a child. Therefore, Great King! Do not be sorrowful and rely on this body. Great King! You should know that there are these four great terrors that come to this body, which cannot be guarded against, and cannot be removed by words, mantras, medicine, or talismans. What are the four? First, it is called old age, which destroys youth and makes it lose its complexion; second, it is called sickness, which destroys health; third, it is called death, which destroys the life force; fourth, things that are permanent return to impermanence. These are called, Great King! The four dharmas that cannot be guarded against, which cannot be subdued by force.

世尊告曰：「是故，大王！甚莫愁忧，一切众生皆归于死。一切变易之法，欲令不变易者，终不有此事。大王当知，人身之法犹如雪揣，要当归坏；亦如土坏，同亦归坏不可久保；亦如野马幻化，虚伪不真；亦如空拳，以诳小儿。是故，大王！莫怀愁忧，恃怙此身。大王当知，有此四大恐怖，来至此身，不可障护，亦不可以言语、呪术、药草、符书，所可除去。云何为四？一者名为老，坏败少壮，使无颜色；二者名病尽，坏败无病；三者名为死尽，坏败命根；四者有常之物归于无常。是谓，大王！有此四法不可障护，非力所能伏也。

"Great King! You should know that there are four great mountains from the four directions that come to crush sentient beings, which cannot be repelled by force. Therefore, Great King! Inconstant things cannot be relied upon. Therefore, Great King! You should govern with the Dharma, not with unrighteousness. The king will also not live long before reaching the ocean of birth and death. The king should also know that those who govern with the Dharma, when their bodies perish and their lives end, will be reborn in the heavens; but if they govern with unrighteousness, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will be reborn in hell. Therefore, Great King! You should govern with the Dharma, not with unrighteousness. Thus, Great King! You should train in this way."

「大王当知，犹如四方有四大山，从四方来，使压众生，非力所却。是故，大王！非牢固物，不可恃怙。是故，大王！当以法治化，莫以非法。王亦不久，当至生死之海。王亦当知，诸以法治化者，身坏命终，生善处天上；若复以非法治化者，身坏命终，生地狱中。是故，大王！当以法治化，莫以非法。如是，大王！当作是学。」

At that time, King Prasenajit addressed the World-Honored One, saying, "What is the name of this Dharma? How should it be practiced?"

尔时，波斯匿王白世尊曰：「此法名何等？当云何奉行？」

The World-Honored One said, "This Dharma is called 'Removing the Thorn of Sorrow and Worry.'"

世尊告曰：「此法名除愁忧之刺。」

The king said to the Buddha, "Indeed, World-Honored One! The reason is that after hearing this Dharma, all the thorns of sorrow and worry have been removed today. However, World-Honored One, there are many affairs in the kingdom, and I wish to return to my place."

王白佛言：「实尔，世尊！所以然者，我闻此法已，所有愁忧之刺，今日已除。然，世尊！国界事猥，欲还所在。」

The World-Honored One said, "It is time for you to know." King Prasenajit immediately rose from his seat, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and then left.

世尊告曰：「宜知是时。」波斯匿王即从坐起，头面礼足，便退而去。

At that time, King Prasenajit, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，波斯匿王闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
