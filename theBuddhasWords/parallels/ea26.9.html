EĀ 26.9

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

26.9 （九）舍利

Parallel to: SN 47.13 (~) / SĀ 638 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One was with a great assembly of five hundred bhikṣus. At that time, the World-Honored One wished to go to Rājagṛha for the summer retreat. Śāriputra also wished to go to Rājagṛha for the summer retreat. The twelve hundred and fifty disciples all wished to go to Rājagṛha for the summer retreat. However, after Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana had completed the summer retreat, they would enter parinirvāṇa.

尔时，世尊与大比丘众五百人俱。尔时，世尊欲诣罗阅城夏坐，舍利弗亦欲诣罗阅城夏坐，千二百五十弟子皆欲诣罗阅城夏坐，然舍利弗、目揵连夏坐竟，当取涅槃。

At that time, the World-Honored One, together with the bhikṣus, Śāriputra, Maudgalyāyana, and others, traveled to the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha and spent the summer retreat there. At that time, the World-Honored One said to Śāriputra, "Now the twelve hundred and fifty disciples are spending the summer retreat here with you. However, Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana bhikṣus will enter parinirvāṇa. What do you think, Śāriputra! Are you able to teach the wonderful Dharma to the bhikṣus? I now have a pain in my back and wish to rest a little."

尔时，世尊将诸比丘、舍利弗、目揵连等，游罗阅城迦兰陀竹园，受夏坐已。尔时，世尊告舍利弗：「今千二百五十弟子，为汝等在此夏坐，然舍利弗、目揵连比丘当取灭度。云何，舍利弗！堪任与诸比丘说妙法乎？我今脊痛，欲小止息。」

Śāriputra replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

舍利弗对曰：「如是。世尊！」

At that time, the World-Honored One personally folded his saṃghāṭī robe, placed his right side on the ground, and crossed his legs. He focused his mind on the dawn.

尔时，世尊躬襞僧迦利，右脇著地，脚脚相累，计意在明。

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra said to the bhikṣus, "When I first received the precepts, after half a month, I attained the four kinds of eloquence and realized them fully. I will now explain them, distinguishing their meanings, so that you may understand and clearly distinguish them. Listen carefully and contemplate them well!"

尔时，尊者舍利弗告诸比丘：「我初受戒时，以经半月，得四辩才而作证，义理具足。我今当说之，分别其义，使汝等知，布现分别之。谛听！善思念之。」

The bhikṣus replied, "Yes." At that time, the bhikṣus received the teachings from Śāriputra.

诸比丘对曰：「如是。」是时，诸比丘从舍利弗受教。

Śāriputra said, "What are the four kinds of eloquence? The eloquence of meaning that I have realized. The eloquence of the Dharma that I have realized through this. The eloquence of suitability that I have realized through this. The eloquence of self that I have realized through this. I will now extensively distinguish their meanings. If there are any doubts among the four assemblies, you may ask about their meanings while I am present. If there are any doubts about the four dhyānas, or the four immeasurables, you may ask me, and I will explain them. If there are any doubts about the four right efforts, you may ask about their meanings, and I will explain them. The four bases of supernormal power, the four foundations of mindfulness, and the four noble truths - if there are any doubts, come and ask me about their meanings, and I will explain them. If you do not ask now, it will be of no benefit to regret later! I now possess the profound Dharma and the practices of the World-Honored One, the One Without Attachment, the Perfectly Enlightened One. If you have any questions about their meanings, I will explain them, so that you do not have regrets later."

舍利弗告曰：「何等是四辩才？我得证者所谓义辩；我由此得证所谓法辩；我由此得证所谓应辩；我由此得证所谓自辩。我今当广分别其义。若当四部之众有狐疑者，我今现在，可问其义。若复，诸贤！于四禅有狐疑者，若复，诸贤！于四等心有狐疑者，可问我，今当说之。设复，诸贤！于四意断有狐疑者，可问我义，我今当说。四神足、四意止、四谛，有狐疑者，便来问我义，我今当说之。今不问者，后悔无益！我今唯有世尊、无所著、等正觉所有深法，所行众事，亦问我义，我当说之，后勿有悔。」

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, having finished his meal, put on his robe and took his bowl, and was about to enter Rājagṛha to beg for food. At that time, the staff-bearing brāhmaṇas saw Maudgalyāyana coming from afar, and each went to each other, saying, "This is the foremost disciple among the śramaṇas of Gautama. We should all surround him and beat him to death."

是时，尊者大目揵连到时，著衣持钵，欲入罗阅城乞食。是时，执杖梵志遥见目连来，各各相诣谓曰：「此是沙门瞿昙弟子中，无有出此人上，我等尽共围已，而取打杀。」

At that time, those brāhmaṇas surrounded and seized him, beating him to death with tiles and stones, and then left. His body was covered everywhere, with bones and flesh rotten and exhausted, suffering unbearably.

是时，彼梵志便共围捉，各以瓦石打杀而便舍去，身体无处不遍，骨肉烂尽，酷痛苦恼，不可称计。

At that time, Mahāmaudgalyāyana thought, "These brāhmaṇas surrounded and beat me, causing my bones and flesh to rot and fall off before leaving. Now my body is in pain everywhere, suffering unbearably, and I have no strength to return to the garden. I can now return to the monastery by supernatural power." At that time, Maudgalyāyana immediately returned to the monastery by supernatural power and went to where Śāriputra was, sitting to one side.

是时，大目揵连而作是念：「此诸梵志围我取打，骨肉烂尽，舍我而去。我今身体无处不痛，极患疼痛，又无气力可还至园，我今可以神足还至精舍。」是时，目连即以神足还至精舍，到舍利弗所，在一面坐。

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana said to Śāriputra, "This brāhmaṇa with a staff surrounded and beat me, causing my bones and flesh to rot and fall off. My body is in unbearable pain. I now wish to attain parinirvāṇa and have come to bid you farewell.

是时，尊者大目揵连语舍利弗言：「此执杖梵志围我取打，骨肉烂尽，身体疼痛，实不可堪，我今欲取般涅槃，故来辞汝。」

At that time, Śāriputra said, "Among the disciples of the World-Honored One, who is foremost in supernatural powers and has great spiritual power, why did he not avoid it by supernatural power?"

时舍利弗言：「世尊弟子之中，神足第一，有大威力，何故不以神足而避乎？」

Maudgalyāyana replied, "The actions I have previously committed are extremely deep and heavy. I must seek to receive retribution. It is impossible to avoid it. It is not received in the sky. However, my body is extremely painful today. Therefore, I have come to bid you farewell and attain parinirvāṇa."

目连报言：「我本所造行极为深重，要索受报，终不可避，非是空中而受此报。然我今日身极患疼痛，故来辞汝，取般涅槃。」

Śāriputra said, "Among the bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs, those who cultivate the four bases of supernormal power extensively expound their meaning. If those people wish to dwell for an eon or beyond an eon, and even not attain parinirvāṇa, why do they not dwell and attain parinirvāṇa?"

舍利弗言：「诸有比丘、比丘尼修四神足，多广演其义，若彼人意中欲住劫、过劫，乃至不灭度，何以不住而灭度乎？」

Maudgalyāyana replied, "So it is, Śāriputra! The Tathāgata said, 'If bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs cultivate the four bases of supernormal power and wish to dwell for an eon, it is also possible.' But the Tathāgata dwells for an eon, and I also dwell. But today the World-Honored One will soon attain parinirvāṇa. The lifespan of sentient beings is extremely short. Moreover, I cannot bear to see the World-Honored One attain parinirvāṇa. However, my body is extremely painful and I wish to attain parinirvāṇa."

目连报言：「如是，舍利弗！如来言：『若比丘、比丘尼修四神足，欲住寿经劫者，亦可得耳。』但如来住劫住者，我亦住耳。但今日世尊不久当取般涅槃，众生之类寿命极短，又我不忍见世尊取般涅槃。然我身体极为疼痛，欲取般涅槃。」

At that time, Śāriputra said to Maudgalyāyana, "You should wait a moment. I will first attain parinirvāṇa." At that time, Maudgalyāyana remained silent and did not respond.

尔时，舍利弗语目连言：「汝今小停，我当先取灭度。」是时，目连默然不对。

At that time, Śāriputra went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. Śāriputra then said to the World-Honored One, "I now wish to attain parinirvāṇa. I only request your permission."

是时，舍利弗往至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。时，舍利弗白世尊言：「我今欲取灭度，唯愿听许。」

At that time, the World-Honored One remained silent and did not respond. Śāriputra then repeated his request three times, saying, "Now is the right time for me to attain parinirvāṇa."

是时，世尊默然不对。时舍利弗再三白世尊言：「我今正是时，欲取般涅槃。」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to Śāriputra, "Why do you not wish to remain for one eon, let alone beyond one eon?"

是时，世尊告舍利弗：「汝今何故不住一劫，乃过一劫？」

Śāriputra said to the World-Honored One, "I have personally heard and received from the World-Honored One that the lifespan of sentient beings is extremely short, with a maximum of only one hundred years. Because the lifespan of sentient beings is short, the lifespan of the Tathāgata is also short. If the Tathāgata were to live for one eon, I would also wish to live for one eon."

舍利弗白世尊言：「我躬从世尊闻，躬自承受，众生之类受命极短，极寿不过百岁，以众生命短，故如来寿亦短。若当如来住寿一劫者，我当亦住寿一劫。」

The World-Honored One said, "As Śāriputra has said, because the lifespan of sentient beings is short, the lifespan of the Tathāgata is also short. However, this matter cannot be discussed. Because in the distant past, incalculable eons ago, there was a Buddha named Sudhana-pranidhāna Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, who appeared in the world. At that time, the lifespan of humans was eighty thousand years, and there were none who died prematurely. When Sudhana-pranidhāna Tathāgata attained Buddhahood, he immediately manifested countless Buddhas, established countless sentient beings in the practices of the Three Vehicles, and established some in the stage of non-retrogression. He also established countless sentient beings in the four castes, and established countless sentient beings in the palaces of the Four Heavenly Kings, the Heaven of the Thirty-three Gods, the Heaven of Yāma, the Heaven of Tuṣita, the Heaven of Nirmāṇarati, the Heaven of Paranirmitavaśavartin, the Heaven of Brahmakāyika, the Heaven of Desire, the Heaven of Form, and the Formless Heaven. On that day, he entered parinirvāṇa in the realm of nirvāṇa without remainder. Now Śāriputra says, 'Because the lifespan of sentient beings is short, the lifespan of the Tathāgata is also short.' How can Śāriputra say, 'The Tathāgata will abide for one eon, up to one eon, and I will also abide for one eon, up to one eon'? However, sentient beings cannot know the long or short lifespan of the Tathāgata. Śāriputra should know that the Tathāgata has four inconceivable matters that cannot be known by the Lesser Vehicle. What are the four? The world is inconceivable, sentient beings are inconceivable, dragons are inconceivable, and the realm of the Buddha's land is inconceivable. These are called, Śāriputra, the four inconceivables."

世尊告曰：「如舍利弗言，以众生命短，故如来寿亦短，然复此事亦不可论。所以然者，过去久远阿僧祇劫，有佛名善念誓愿如来、至真、等正觉，出现于世。当于尔时，人寿八万岁，无有中夭者。彼善念誓愿如来当成佛时，即其日便化作无量佛，立无量众生在三乘行，有在不退转地住者；复立无量众生在四姓家；复立无量众生在四天王宫、艶天、兜术天、化自在天、他化自在天、梵迦夷天、欲天、色天、无色天，亦于其日，于无余涅槃界而般涅槃。而今舍利弗言：『以众生寿短，故如来寿命亦短。』云何，舍利弗！而作是说：『如来当住一劫，至一劫，我亦当住一劫，至一劫。』然复众生，不能知如来寿命长短。舍利弗当知，如来有四不可思议事，非小乘所能知。云何为四？世不可思议，众生不可思议，龙不可思议，佛土境界不可思议。是谓，舍利弗！有四不可思议。」

Śāriputra said, "Yes, World-Honored One! There are four inconceivables: the world, sentient beings, dragon palaces, and the Buddha's land are truly inconceivable. However, I have always had this thought for a long time: 'Śākyamuni Buddha will not abide for one eon.' Moreover, the gods came to me and said, 'Śākyamuni Buddha will not be in the world for long, he is about eighty years old, and now the World-Honored One will soon attain parinirvāṇa.' I cannot bear to see the World-Honored One attain parinirvāṇa. Moreover, I have heard this from the Tathāgata: 'All the past, future, and present Buddhas, the foremost disciples first attain parinirvāṇa, and then the Buddha attains parinirvāṇa; and the last disciple also first attains parinirvāṇa, and then the World-Honored One will soon attain parinirvāṇa.' I only wish that the World-Honored One would allow me to attain parinirvāṇa."

舍利弗言：「如是，世尊！有四不可思议，世界、众生、龙宫、佛土实不可思议。然长夜恒有此念：『释迦文佛终不住一劫。』又复诸天来至我所，而语我言：『释迦文佛不久在世，年向八十，然今世尊不久当取涅槃。』我今不堪见世尊取般涅槃。又我躬从如来闻此语：『诸过去、当来、今现在，诸佛上足弟子先取般涅槃，然后佛取般涅槃；又最后弟子亦先取般涅槃，然后世尊不久当取灭度。』唯愿世尊听取灭度。」

The World-Honored One said, "Now is the right time."

世尊告曰：「今正是时。」

Śāriputra then sat in front of the Tathāgata, straightened his body and mind, focused his attention in front, and entered the first dhyāna. From the first dhyāna, he arose and entered the second dhyāna. From the second dhyāna, he arose and again entered the third dhyāna. From the third dhyāna, he arose and again entered the fourth dhyāna. From the fourth dhyāna, he arose and again entered the sphere of space, the sphere of consciousness, the sphere of nothingness, and the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception. From the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception, he arose and entered the concentration of cessation. From the concentration of cessation, he arose and entered the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception. From the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception, he arose and entered the sphere of nothingness, the sphere of consciousness, and the sphere of space. From the sphere of space, he arose and entered the fourth dhyāna. From the fourth dhyāna, he arose and entered the third dhyāna. From the third dhyāna, he arose and entered the second dhyāna. From the second dhyāna, he arose and entered the first dhyāna. From the first dhyāna, he arose and entered the second dhyāna. From the second dhyāna, he arose and entered the third dhyāna. From the third dhyāna, he arose and entered the fourth dhyāna. Then Venerable Śāriputra arose from the fourth dhyāna and said to the bhikṣus, "This is called the lion's heroic concentration.

舍利弗即住如来前坐，正身正意，系念在前，而入初禅；从初禅起，入二禅；从二禅起，复入三禅；从三禅起，复入四禅；从四禅起，复入空处、识处、不用处、有想无想处，从有想无想起，入灭尽定；从灭尽定起，入有想无想处；从有想无想起，入不用处、识处、空处；从空处起，入第四禅；从第四禅起，入三禅；从第三禅起，入第二禅；从第二禅起，入初禅；从初禅起，入第二禅；从第二禅起，入第三禅；从第三禅起，入第四禅。时，尊者舍利弗从四禅起已，告诸比丘：「此名师子奋迅三昧。」

At that time, the bhikṣus exclaimed in wonder, "How amazing! How extraordinary! Venerable Śāriputra's entry into samādhi is so swift!"

是时，诸比丘叹未曾有：「甚奇！甚特！尊者舍利弗入三昧，速疾乃尔。」

At that time, Śāriputra immediately rose from his seat, bowed his head to the World-Honored One's feet, and then withdrew.

尔时，舍利弗即从坐起，头面礼世尊足，便退而去。

At that time, many bhikṣus followed behind Śāriputra. Śāriputra then turned around and said, "Virtuous ones! Where do you wish to go?"

当于尔时，众多比丘从舍利弗后。时舍利弗还顾语：「诸贤！各欲所至？」

The many bhikṣus replied, "We wish to make offerings to Venerable Śāriputra."

众多比丘报曰：「我等欲得供养尊者舍利。」

Śāriputra said, "Stop! Stop! Virtuous ones! This is already an offering. I already have a śrāmaṇera who can make offerings to me. You should each return to your places, contemplate the Dharma, cultivate pure conduct, and end suffering. The Tathāgata's appearance in the world is extremely rare, appearing only occasionally, like the udumbara flower that appears only occasionally. The Tathāgata is also like this, appearing only after many eons. It is also difficult to attain a human body, and it is also difficult to attain the accomplishment of faith. Desiring to renounce the householder life and study the Tathāgata's teachings is also difficult to attain. Wishing for all conditioned phenomena not to cease is also difficult to attain; extinguishing desire and attachment completely without remainder, extinguishing and attaining nirvāṇa. Now there are four principles taught by the Tathāgata. What are the four? All conditioned phenomena are impermanent - this is the first principle taught by the Tathāgata. All conditioned phenomena are suffering - this is the second principle taught by the Tathāgata. All conditioned phenomena are selfless - this is the third principle taught by the Tathāgata. Nirvāṇa is ultimate peace - this is the fourth principle taught by the Tathāgata. These are called, virtuous ones, the four principles taught by the Tathāgata."

舍利弗言：「止！止！诸贤！此则为供养已。吾自有沙弥，足得供养我耳。汝等各还所在，思惟道化，善修梵行，尽于苦际。如来出世，甚难可遇，时时乃出，犹优昙钵华时时乃出。如来亦复如是，亿劫乃出。人身亦复难克，有信成就亦复难得，欲求出家学如来法亦复难得。一切诸行欲使不灭尽，此亦难得；灭于爱欲永尽无余，灭尽涅槃。今有四法本末，如来之所说。云何为四？一切诸行无常，是谓初法本末，如来之所说。一切诸行苦，是谓第二法本末，如来之所说。一切诸行无我，是谓第三法本末，如来之所说。涅槃为永寂，是谓第四法本末，如来之所说。是谓，诸贤！四法本末，如来之所说。」

At that time, the bhikṣus all shed tears together, saying, "Now Śāriputra has entered parinirvāṇa, how could it be so fast?"

尔时，诸比丘咸共堕泪：「今舍利弗灭度，何速乃尔。」

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra said to the bhikṣus, "Stop! Stop! Virtuous ones! Do not grieve. The law of change cannot be avoided. The king of Mount Sumeru is still subject to impermanence, let alone the body of a mustard seed. How can Śāriputra bhikṣu escape this affliction? The Tathāgata's vajra body will also soon enter parinirvāṇa, let alone my body. Therefore, you should each cultivate the Dharma practice and attain the end of suffering."

尔时，尊者舍利弗告诸比丘：「止！止！诸贤！慎莫愁忧，变易之法，欲使不变易者，此事不然。须弥山王尚有无常之变，况复芥子之体，舍利弗比丘而免此患乎？如来金刚之身，不久亦当取般涅槃，何况我身？然汝等各修其法行，得尽苦际。」

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra went to the monastery. Having arrived, he put away his robes and alms bowl, left the bamboo grove, and went to his birthplace. At that time, Venerable Śāriputra gradually begged for alms and arrived in the country of Māṣaka. At that time, Venerable Śāriputra wandered in his birthplace in Māṣaka, and his body fell ill, suffering greatly. At that time, there was only the novice monk Upāli who made offerings, removed the impurities from below his eyes, and provided him with purity.

是时，尊者舍利弗往诣精舍。到已，收摄衣钵，出于竹园，往诣本生住处。是时，尊者舍利弗渐渐乞食至摩瘦国。尔时，尊者舍利弗游于摩瘦本生之处，身遇疾病，极为苦痛。时，唯有均头沙弥供养，目下除去不净，供给清净。

At that time, Śakra, Lord of the Devas, knew what Śāriputra was thinking in his mind. In the time it takes a strong man to bend and stretch his arm, he disappeared from the Heaven of the Thirty-three and came to Śāriputra's monastery. Having arrived, he bowed his head to Śāriputra's feet and rubbed his feet with both hands, proclaiming his name, saying, "I am Śakra, Lord of the Devas."

是时，释提桓因知舍利弗心中所念，譬如力士屈申臂顷，从三十三天没不现，来至舍利弗精舍中。至已，头面礼足，复以两手摩舍利弗足，自称姓名，而作是说：「我是天王帝释。」

Śāriputra said, "Excellent, Lord of the Devas! May your life be endless!"

舍利弗言：「快哉！天帝！受命无穷。」

Śakra, Lord of the Devas, replied, "I now wish to make offerings to Venerable Śāriputra."

释提桓因报言：「我今欲供养尊者舍利。」

At that time, Śāriputra replied, "Stop, stop, Lord of the Devas! This is already an offering, the purity of the devas, the asuras, dragons, ghosts, and the various heavenly beings. I now have my own novice monk who is sufficient to be my attendant."

时舍利弗报言：「止！止！天帝！此则为供养已，诸天清净，阿须轮、龙、鬼及诸天之众。我今自有沙弥，足堪使令。」

At that time, Śakra, Lord of the Devas, spoke to Śāriputra three times, saying, "I now wish to create meritorious deeds. Do not go against my wish. I now wish to make offerings to Venerable Śāriputra." At that time, Śāriputra remained silent and did not respond. At that time, Śakra, Lord of the Devas, personally removed the excrement, not shrinking from the hardship.

时，释提桓因再三白舍利弗言：「我今欲作福业，莫见违愿，今欲供养尊者舍利。」是时，舍利弗默然不对。时，释提桓因躬自除粪，不辞谦苦。

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra entered parinirvāṇa that very night. At that time, the earth shook in six ways, there was a great sound, heavenly flowers rained down, music was played, devas filled the sky, and the wondrous devas also scattered kaṇṭaka flowers and sandalwood powder on top. At that time, when Śāriputra had attained parinirvāṇa, the devas all wailed and cried in the sky, unable to control themselves. In the sky, the desire realm devas, form realm devas, and formless realm devas all shed tears, just like the fine rain of spring. At that time, it was also like this. "Now Venerable Śāriputra has entered parinirvāṇa. How quickly!"

是时，尊者舍利弗即以其夜而般涅槃。是时，此地六变震动，有大音声，雨诸天华，作倡伎乐，诸天侧塞虚空，神妙诸天亦散拘牟头华，或以栴檀杂碎之香而散其上。时，舍利弗已取灭度，诸天皆在空中，悲号啼哭，不能自胜，虚空之中，欲天、色天、无色天，悉共堕泪，亦如春月细雨和畅。尔时，亦复如是。「今尊者舍利弗取般涅槃，何其速哉。」

At that time, Śakra, Lord of the Devas, collected all kinds of incense and perfumed the body of Venerable Śāriputra. After making various offerings, he collected the relics and robes and handed them to the novice monk Mahāmaudgalyāyana, saying, "This is your teacher's relics and robes. Go and offer them to the World-Honored One. When you arrive, tell the World-Honored One about this matter in detail. If he says anything, then follow it."

是时，释提桓因集一切众香，而耶维尊者舍利弗身，种种供养已，而收舍利及衣钵，而付均头沙弥，又告之曰：「此是汝师舍利及衣钵，往奉世尊。到已，以此因缘，具白世尊，若有所说者，便奉行之。」

At that time, Mahāmaudgalyāyana replied, "Yes, Kauśika!"

是时，均头报言：「如是，拘翼！」

At that time, the novice monk Mahāmaudgalyāyana took the robes, alms bowl, and relics, went to where Ānanda was, and said to Ānanda, "My teacher has already attained parinirvāṇa. I now bring his relics and robes to offer to the World-Honored One!"

是时，均头沙弥捉衣、持钵及舍利，往至阿难所，白阿难曰：「我师已取灭度，今持舍利、衣钵来，用上世尊！」

When Ānanda saw this, he immediately shed tears and said, "You should also come with me to where the World-Honored One is. Let us together inform the World-Honored One of this matter. If the World-Honored One has something to say, we will always uphold it."

时阿难见已，即堕泪而作是语：「汝亦来共至世尊所，以此因缘，共白世尊。若世尊有所说，我等常奉行之。」

Mahāmaudgalyāyana replied, "Yes, Venerable One!"

均头报言：「如是，尊者！」

At that time, Ānanda brought the novice monk Mahāmaudgalyāyana to where the World-Honored One was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and said to the World-Honored One, "This novice monk Mahāmaudgalyāyana came to me and said, 'My teacher has attained parinirvāṇa. I now bring his robes and alms bowl to offer to the Tathāgata.' Today my mind is troubled and my disposition is confused. I do not know east from west. I heard that Venerable Śāriputra has attained parinirvāṇa, and I am deeply saddened."

是时，阿难将均头沙弥至世尊所，头面礼足，白世尊曰：「此均头沙弥来至我所，白我言：『我师已灭度，今持衣钵来奉上如来。』我今日心意烦恼，志性迷惑，莫知东西，闻尊者舍利弗取般涅槃，怅然伤心。」

The World-Honored One said, "Ānanda, did the bhikṣu Śāriputra attain parinirvāṇa through the body of precepts?"

世尊告曰：「云何，阿难！舍利弗比丘用戒身般涅槃乎？」

Ānanda replied, "No, World-Honored One!"

阿难对曰：「非也。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "Ānanda, did he attain parinirvāṇa through the body of concentration, the body of wisdom, the body of liberation, or the body of the view of liberation?"

世尊告曰：「云何，阿难！用定身、慧身、解脱所见身，而取灭度乎？」

Ānanda said to the Buddha, "Śāriputra Bhikṣu did not attain parinirvāṇa through the body of precepts, the body of concentration, the body of wisdom, the body of liberation, or the body of the view of liberation. He was always delighted to teach and preach the Dharma without ever tiring of it. He instructed the bhikṣus without ever tiring of it. I now remember Śāriputra's deep kindness as being too much, and that is why I am sorrowful."

阿难白佛言：「舍利弗比丘不用戒身、定身、慧身、解脱身、解脱所见身，而取灭度，但舍利弗比丘恒喜教化，说法无厌足，与诸比丘教诫，亦无厌足。我今忆此舍利弗深恩过多，是以愁悒耳。」

The World-Honored One said, "Stop! Stop! Ānanda, do not be sorrowful. Impermanent things cannot be made permanent. All that is born must die. Ānanda, did not all the past buddhas attain parinirvāṇa? It is like a lamp wick that goes out when the oil is used up. From the time of Dīpaṃkara Buddha up to the present seven buddhas and their disciples, did they not all attain parinirvāṇa? The pratyekabuddhas Śāntadeva, Mahāpraṇāda, and the pratyekabuddhas up to Nīla and Nīlaparvata, did they not all attain parinirvāṇa? At the beginning of the Bhadrakalpa, there was a great universal monarch named Mahādeva. Where is that universal monarch now? Did he not attain parinirvāṇa?"

世尊告曰：「止！止！阿难！莫怀愁忧，不常之物，欲使恒在者，此事不然。夫生有死。云何，阿难！过去诸佛尽非灭度乎？譬如灯炷，油尽即灭。如从宝藏、定光至今七佛及弟子众，尽非般涅槃乎？如是辟支佛审谛、高称、远闻、尼嗟优尼般嗟伽罗，优般伽罗，尔许辟支佛尽非灭度乎？贤劫之初，大国圣王名曰善悦摩诃提婆，如是转轮圣王今为所在，岂非尽非般涅槃乎？」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

All conditioned things are impermanent, and those who are born will die
Not being born, there is no more cessation. This cessation is the best.

「一切行无常，　　生者当有死，不生不复灭，　　此灭最第一。」

The World-Honored One said to Ānanda, "Now give me Śāriputra's relics."

世尊告阿难曰：「汝今授舍利弗舍利来。」

Ānanda replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!" At that time, Ānanda then gave the relics to the World-Honored One.

阿难对曰：「如是。世尊！」是时，阿难即授舍利在世尊手。

At that time, the World-Honored One, having taken the relics in his hand, addressed the bhikṣus, saying, "Now these are the relics of the bhikṣu Śāriputra, who was wise, intelligent, and had great talent. He had various kinds of wisdom, wisdom that was inexhaustible, wisdom that had no limits, wisdom that was swift, wisdom that was light and nimble, wisdom that was sharp, wisdom that was profound, wisdom that was discerning, he had few desires and was content, he delighted in solitary places, he had a courageous mind, he was not confused in what he did, he had no timid or weak mind, he was able to endure, he removed evil dharmas, his nature was gentle, he did not like disputes, he always practiced diligence, he practiced samādhi, he cultivated wisdom, he practiced the knowledge and vision of liberation. Bhikṣus should know, it is like a great tree without branches. Now the bhikṣu saṃgha is like that great tree, and the bhikṣu Śāriputra has attained parinirvāṇa, like a tree without branches. If Śāriputra had gone to any place, it would have been a great fortune for those who encountered him there, thinking, 'Śāriputra is staying in that place.' Because the bhikṣu Śāriputra was able to engage in discussions with non-Buddhist practitioners and defeat them all."

尔时，世尊手执舍利已，告诸比丘：「今此是舍利弗比丘舍利，智慧聪明高才之智。若干种智，智不可穷，智无涯底，智有速疾之智，有轻便之智，有利机之智，有甚深之智，有审谛之智，少欲知足，乐闲静之处，有猛勇意，所为不乱，无怯弱心，能有所忍，除去恶法，体性柔和，不好鬪讼，恒修精进，行三昧，习智慧，念解脱，修行解脱所知见身。比丘当知，犹如大树而无其枝，然今日比丘僧，如来是大树，舍利弗比丘而取灭度，似树无枝。若舍利弗所游之方，彼方便遇大幸云：『舍利弗在彼方止。』所以然者，舍利弗比丘能与外道异学共议论，无不降伏者。」

At that time, Mahāmaudgalyāyana heard that Śāriputra had attained parinirvāṇa. He then used his supernatural powers to go to where the World-Honored One was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and stood to one side. At that time, Mahāmaudgalyāyana addressed the World-Honored One, saying, "The bhikṣu Śāriputra has now attained parinirvāṇa. I now take my leave of the World-Honored One and wish to attain parinirvāṇa."

是时，大目揵连闻舍利弗灭度，即以神足，至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面住。尔时，大目揵连白世尊曰：「舍利弗比丘今已灭度，我今辞世尊，欲取灭度。」

At that time, the World-Honored One remained silent and did not respond. In this way, he asked the World-Honored One a second and a third time, saying, "I wish to attain parinirvāṇa." At that time, the World-Honored One also remained silent and did not reply.

尔时，世尊默然不对。如是再三白世尊曰：「我欲取灭度。」尔时，世尊亦复默然不报。

At that time, Maudgalyāyana saw that the World-Honored One remained silent and did not respond. He then bowed at the feet of the World-Honored One and left. He returned to his dwelling place, put away his robes and alms bowl, left the city of Rājagṛha, and went to his birthplace. At that time, many bhikṣus followed behind Venerable Maudgalyāyana. At that time, the many bhikṣus went with Maudgalyāyana to the village of Māṣaka, where they wandered and taught the Dharma. Their bodies were afflicted with serious illnesses.

尔时，目连以见世尊默然不报，即礼世尊足，便退而去。还诣精舍，收摄衣钵，出罗阅城，自往本生处。尔时，有众多比丘从尊者目连后。是时，众多比丘共目连到摩瘦村，在彼游化，身抱重患。

At that time, Maudgalyāyana himself spread out a seat in the open air and sat down. He entered the first dhyāna, arose from the first dhyāna, entered the second dhyāna, arose from the second dhyāna, entered the third dhyāna, arose from the third dhyāna, entered the fourth dhyāna, arose from the fourth dhyāna, entered the sphere of space, arose from the sphere of space, entered the sphere of consciousness, arose from the sphere of consciousness, entered the sphere of nothingness, arose from the sphere of nothingness, entered the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception, arose from the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception, entered the samādhi of fire light, arose from the samādhi of fire light, entered the samādhi of water light, arose from the samādhi of water light, entered the samādhi of cessation, arose from the samādhi of cessation, entered the samādhi of water light, arose from the samādhi of water light, entered the samādhi of fire light, arose from the samādhi of fire light, entered the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception, arose from the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception, entered the sphere of nothingness, the sphere of consciousness, the four dhyānas, the third dhyāna, the second dhyāna, and the first dhyāna. Arising from the first dhyāna, he flew in the air, sitting, lying down, and walking. Fire came out from his body, water came out from his body, or water came out from his body, fire came out from his body. He performed the eighteen transformations and manifested supernatural powers.

是时，目连躬自露地敷座而坐，而入初禅；从初禅起，入第二禅；从第二禅起，入第三禅；从第三禅起，入第四禅；从第四禅起，入空处；从空处起，入识处；从识处起，入不用处；从不用处起，入有想无想处；从有想无想处起，入火光三昧；从火光三昧起，入水光三昧；从水光三昧起，入灭尽定；从灭尽定起，入水光三昧；从水光三昧起，入火光三昧；从火光三昧起，入有想无想定；从有想无想定起，入不用处；从不用处起，入识处、空处、四禅、三禅、二禅、初禅。从初禅起，飞在空中，坐卧经行，身上出火，身下出水，或身下出火，身上出水，作十八变，神足变化。

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana descended and sat down, crossed his legs, straightened his body and mind, and focused his thoughts in front of him. He again entered the first dhyāna, arose from the first dhyāna, entered the second dhyāna, arose from the second dhyāna, entered the third dhyāna, arose from the third dhyāna, entered the fourth dhyāna, arose from the fourth dhyāna, entered the sphere of space, arose from the sphere of space, entered the sphere of consciousness, arose from the sphere of consciousness, entered the sphere of nothingness, arose from the sphere of nothingness, entered the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception, arose from the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception, entered the samādhi of fire light, arose from the samādhi of fire light, entered the samādhi of water light, arose from the samādhi of water light, entered the samādhi of cessation, arose from the samādhi of cessation, again entered the samādhi of water light, the samādhi of fire light, the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception, the sphere of nothingness, the sphere of consciousness, the four dhyānas, the third dhyāna, the second dhyāna, and the first dhyāna. Again arising from the first dhyāna, he entered the second dhyāna, arose from the second dhyāna, entered the third dhyāna, arose from the third dhyāna, entered the fourth dhyāna, arose from the fourth dhyāna, and immediately attained parinirvāṇa. At that time, Mahāmaudgalyāyana had attained parinirvāṇa.

是时，尊者大目揵连还下就座，结跏趺坐，正身正意，系念在前，复入初禅；从初禅起，入第二禅；从第二禅起，入第三禅；从第三禅起，入第四禅；从第四禅起，入空处；从空处起，入识处；从识处起，入不用处，从不用处起，入有想无想处；从有想无想处起，入火光三昧；从火光三昧起，入水光三昧；从水光三昧起，入灭尽定；从灭尽定起，还入水光、火光、有想无想处、不用处、识处、空处、四禅、三禅、二禅、初禅。复从初禅起，入第二禅；从第二禅起，入第三禅；从第三禅起，入第四禅；从第四禅起，寻时取灭度。尔时，大目揵连已取灭度。

At that time, the earth shook greatly. The devas each informed each other and came down to visit and pay homage to Mahāmaudgalyāyana, to make offerings to his venerable virtue. Some offered various incense and flowers. The devas in the sky played music, plucked stringed instruments, and danced and sang to make offerings to Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana.

是时，此地极大震动，诸天各各相告来下，省觐大目揵连，持用供养尊德，或以种种香华来供养者，诸天在空中作倡伎乐，弹琴、歌舞用供养尊者目揵连上。

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana had already attained parinirvāṇa. At that time, the area within one yojana of the village of Nālaka was filled with devas. At that time, many bhikṣus also held various incense and flowers to scatter on the body of Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana.

尔时，尊者大目揵连已取灭度。是时，那罗陀村中一由旬内，诸天侧满其中。尔时，复有众多比丘持种种香华，散尊者目揵连尸上。

At that time, the World-Honored One gradually begged for alms from the city of Rājagṛha, leading five hundred bhikṣus, wandering and teaching among people, and went to the village of Nālaka, together with five hundred bhikṣus. At that time, Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana had not long passed away. At that time, the World-Honored One was sitting in the open air, silently observing the bhikṣus. Having silently observed the bhikṣus, he told the bhikṣus: "I now see that this assembly of people has greatly diminished. The reason is that in this assembly there are no bhikṣus like Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana. Wherever Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana go, their skillful means are not in vain. It is heard that Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana are now in this direction. The reason is that the bhikṣus Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana are capable of subduing these non-Buddhist paths."

尔时，世尊从罗阅城渐渐乞食，将五百比丘，人中游化，往诣那罗陀村，五百比丘俱。尔时，舍利弗、目连取灭度未久。尔时，世尊在露地而坐，默然察诸比丘已，默然观诸比丘已，告诸比丘：「我今观此众人中，大有损减。所以然者，今此众中无有舍利弗、目揵连比丘。若舍利弗、目揵连所游之方，彼方便为不空。闻舍利弗、目揵连今在此一方。所以然者，舍利弗、目揵连比丘堪任降此外道。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus: "The Buddhas' creations are very wonderful and extraordinary! These two disciples with wisdom and supernatural powers have attained parinirvāṇa, yet the Tathāgata is not sorrowful. Even the past tathāgatas as numerous as the sands of the Ganges also had disciples with wisdom and supernatural powers. The future buddhas who will appear in the world will also have disciples with wisdom and supernatural powers. Bhikṣus should know that there are two kinds of giving in the world. What are the two? They are giving of material things and giving of the Dharma. Bhikṣus should know that if one speaks of giving of material things, one should seek it from the bhikṣus Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana; if one wishes to seek giving of the Dharma, one should seek it from me. The reason is that I, the Tathāgata, have no giving of material things. You should make offerings to the relics of the bhikṣus Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana today."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「诸佛所造甚奇！甚特！有此二智慧、神足弟子取般涅槃，然如来无有愁忧。正使过去恒沙如来，亦复有此智慧、神足弟子，正使当来诸佛出世，亦当有此智慧、神足弟子。比丘当知，世间有二施业。云何为二？所谓财施、法施。比丘当知，若论财施者，当从舍利弗、目连比丘求；若欲求法施者，当从我求之。所以然者，我今如来无有财施，汝等今日可供养舍利弗、目揵连比丘舍利。」

At that time, Ānanda addressed the Buddha, saying: "How can we make offerings to the relics of Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana?"

尔时，阿难白佛言：「云何得供养舍利弗、目揵连舍利？」

The World-Honored One said: "You should gather various incense and flowers, and at the crossroads, build four temple stupas. The reason is that if one builds a temple, one should build four stupas. What are the four? A wheel-turning sage king should build a stupa, an arhat who has exhausted the taints should build a stupa, a pratyekabuddha should build a stupa, and a Tathāgata should build a stupa."

世尊告曰：「当集种种香华，于四衢道头，起四寺偷婆。所以然者，若有起寺，此人有四种应起偷婆。云何为四？转轮圣王应起偷婆，漏尽阿罗汉应起偷婆，辟支佛应起偷婆，如来应起偷婆。」

At that time, Ānanda addressed the World-Honored One, saying: "What are the causes and conditions for the Tathāgata to build a stupa? What are the causes and conditions for pratyekabuddhas, arhats who have exhausted the taints, and wheel-turning sage kings to build stupas?"

是时，阿难白世尊曰：「有何因缘如来应起偷婆？复有何因缘辟支佛、漏尽阿罗汉、转轮圣王应起偷婆？」

The World-Honored One said: "You should know that a wheel-turning sage king practices the ten wholesome deeds and cultivates the ten merits, and also teaches others to practice the ten wholesome merits. What are the ten? He himself does not kill living beings, and also teaches others to refrain from killing. He himself does not steal, and also teaches others to refrain from stealing. He himself does not engage in sexual misconduct, and also teaches others to refrain from sexual misconduct. He himself does not speak falsehoods, and also teaches others to refrain from speaking falsehoods. He himself does not engage in frivolous speech, and also teaches others to refrain from frivolous speech. He himself is not jealous, and also teaches others to refrain from jealousy. He himself does not quarrel, and also teaches others to refrain from quarreling. He himself has a correct mind, and also teaches others to refrain from a confused mind. He himself has right views, and also teaches others to practice right views. Bhikṣus should know that a wheel-turning sage king has these ten merits, and therefore he should be given a stupa."

世尊告曰：「汝今当知，转轮圣王加行十善，修十功德，亦复教人行十善功德。云何为十？己身不杀生，复教他人使不杀生。己身不盗，复教他人使不盗。己身不婬，复教他人使不婬。己身不妄语，复教他人使不妄语。己身不绮语，复教他人使不绮语。己身不嫉妬，复教他人使不嫉妬。己身不鬪讼，复教他人使不鬪讼。己身意正，复教他人使不乱意。身自正见，复教他人使行正见。比丘当知，转轮圣王有此十功德，是故应与起偷婆。」

At that time, Ānanda addressed the World-Honored One, saying: "What are the causes and conditions for the Tathāgata's disciples to build stupas?"

是时，阿难白世尊曰：「复以何因缘如来弟子应与起偷婆？」

The World-Honored One said: "Ānanda should know that an arhat who has exhausted the taints, because he will no longer be reborn, is pure like heavenly gold, and the three poisons and five hindrances will never appear again. For these causes and conditions, the Tathāgata's disciples should build stupas."

世尊告曰：「阿难当知，漏尽阿罗汉以更不复受有，净如天金，三毒五使永不复现，以此因缘如来弟子应与起偷婆。」

Ānanda addressed the Buddha, saying: "What are the causes and conditions for pratyekabuddhas to build stupas?"

阿难白佛：「以何因缘辟支佛应与起偷婆？」

The World-Honored One said: "There are pratyekabuddhas who awaken on their own without a teacher, eliminate the fetters, and will no longer be reborn. Therefore, they should build stupas."

世尊告曰：「有辟支佛，无师自悟，去诸结使，更不受胎，是故应与起偷婆。」

At that time, Ānanda addressed the World-Honored One, saying: "What are the causes and conditions for the Tathāgata to build stupas?"

是时，阿难白世尊曰：「复以何因缘如来应与起偷婆？」

The World-Honored One said: "Ānanda! The Tathāgata has the ten powers and four fearlessnesses. He subdues the unyielding, liberates the unredeemed, causes those who have not attained the path to attain it, and causes those who have not attained parinirvāṇa to attain it. When people see this, they are extremely joyful. This, Ānanda, is called the Tathāgata building stupas. This is called the Tathāgata building stupas."

世尊告曰：「于是，阿难！如来有十力、四无所畏，不降者降，不度者度，不得道者令得道，不般涅槃者令般涅槃。众人见已，极怀欢喜。是谓，阿难！如来应与起偷婆。是谓如来应与起偷婆。」

At that time, Ānanda heard what the World-Honored One had said and joyfully upheld it.

尔时，阿难闻世尊所说，欢喜奉行。
