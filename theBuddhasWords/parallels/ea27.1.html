EĀ 27.1

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

Chapter 27 增壹阿含经等趣四谛品第二十七

27.1 （一）

Parallel to: DN 22 (~) / MN 10 (~) / MN 141 / MĀ 31 / MĀ 98 (~) / EĀ 12.1 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "This is what we always teach, namely the Four Noble Truths. We contemplate these teachings with countless skillful means, analyze their meanings, and extensively explain them to others. What are the four? Namely, the truth of suffering. We contemplate this teaching with countless skillful means, analyze its meaning, and extensively explain it to others. We explain the truths of the origin, cessation, and path with countless skillful means, contemplate these teachings, analyze their meanings, and extensively explain them to others.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「是谓，比丘！我等常所说法，所谓四谛，以无数方便而观察此法，分别其义，广与人演。云何为四？所谓苦谛之法，以无数方便而观察此法，分别其义，广与人演；以无数方便说习、尽、道谛，而观察此法，分别其义，广与人演。

You bhikṣus should be close to the bhikṣu Śāriputra, serve and make offerings to him. The reason is that the bhikṣu Śāriputra explains these Four Noble Truths with countless skillful means and extensively explains them to others. When the bhikṣu Śāriputra analyzes their meanings and extensively explains them to the four groups of followers and sentient beings, countless sentient beings will have their defilements exhausted and attain the purity of the Dharma eye.

「汝等比丘，当亲近舍利弗比丘，承事供养。所以然者，彼舍利弗比丘以无数方便，说此四谛，广与人演。当舍利弗比丘与诸众生及四部众，分别其义，广与人演，时不可计众生诸尘垢尽，得法眼净。

"You bhikṣus should be close to the bhikṣus Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana, serve and make offerings to them. The reason is that the bhikṣu Śāriputra is the parent of sentient beings, who gives birth and nurtures them to maturity. The bhikṣu Maudgalyāyana is the reason. The reason is that the bhikṣu Śāriputra explains the essential teachings to people, accomplishing the Four Noble Truths; the bhikṣu Maudgalyāyana explains the essential teachings to people, accomplishing the ultimate meaning and accomplishing the practice of the unconditioned. You should be close to the bhikṣus Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana." Having spoken thus, the World-Honored One returned to the quiet room.

「汝等比丘，当亲近舍利弗、目犍连比丘，承事供养。所以然者，舍利弗比丘！众生之父母，以生已长养令大者，目犍连比丘！所以然者，舍利弗比丘与人说法要，成四谛；目犍连比丘与人说法要，成第一义，成无漏行。汝等当亲近舍利弗、目犍连比丘。」世尊作是语已，还入静室。

Not long after the World-Honored One left, at that time Śāriputra addressed the bhikṣus, saying: "Those who can attain the Dharma of the Four Noble Truths quickly attain great benefits. What are the four? Namely, the truth of suffering, which is extensively explained with countless skillful means. What is the truth of suffering? Namely, the suffering of birth, the suffering of old age, the suffering of sickness, the suffering of death, the suffering of sorrow, grief, and distress, the suffering of being with what is disliked, the suffering of separation from what is loved, the suffering of not obtaining what one seeks. In brief, the suffering of the five aggregates of clinging. This is called the truth of suffering. What is the truth of the origin of suffering? Namely, the fetters of craving. What is the truth of cessation? Namely, the truth of cessation is the complete and utter extinction of the fetters of craving and desire. This is called the truth of cessation. What is the truth of the path? Namely, the eightfold noble path, namely, right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right concentration. This is called the truth of the path. Those sentient beings quickly attain great benefits, being able to hear this Dharma of the Four Noble Truths."

世尊去未久，尔时舍利弗告诸比丘：「其有能得四谛法者，彼人快得善利。云何为四？所谓苦谛，以无数方便广演其义。云何为苦谛？所谓生苦、老苦、病苦、死苦、忧悲恼苦、怨憎会苦、恩爱别苦、所求不得苦，取要言之，五盛阴苦，是谓苦谛。云何苦习谛？所谓爱结是也。云何为尽谛？所谓尽谛者，爱欲结永尽无余，是谓尽谛。云何为道谛？所谓贤圣八品道是。正见、正治、正语、正方便、正命、正业、正念、正定，是谓道谛也。彼众生快得善利，乃能闻此四谛之法。」

At that time, when the Venerable Śāriputra was expounding this Dharma, immeasurable and innumerable sentient beings, upon hearing this Dharma, their defilements were completely extinguished and they attained the purity of the Dharma eye.

尔时，尊者舍利弗当说此法，无量不可计众生闻此法时，诸尘垢尽，得法眼净。

"We have also quickly attained great benefits. The World-Honored One has taught us the Dharma and established us in the ground of merit."

「我等亦快得善利，世尊与我说法，安处福地。」

"Therefore, the fourfold assembly should seek the means and practice these four truths. Thus, bhikṣus, you should train in this way."

「是故，四部之众，求于方便，行此四谛。如是，诸比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
