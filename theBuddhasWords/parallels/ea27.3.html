EĀ 27.3

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

27.3 （三）

Parallel to: AN 9.20 / MĀ 155

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the elder Anāthapiṇḍada went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. At that time, the World-Honored One asked the elder, "How is it, elder! Do you always give alms in your home?"

尔时，阿那邠邸长者往至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。尔时，世尊问长者曰：「云何，长者！汝家中恒布施耶？」

The elder said to the Buddha, "My poor family always practices giving, and the food and drink is coarse and inferior, not the same as usual."

长者白佛：「贫家恒行布施，又饮食麁弊，不与常同。」

The World-Honored One said, "When giving alms, whether good or bad, much or little, if one does not use the mind, make vows, or have faith, one will not obtain good food in the place where one is reborn. The mind will not be greedy or attached to good clothing, nor will it be attached to good fields and occupations. The mind will not be attached to the five desires. Even if one has servants and slaves, one will not accept their instructions. The reason is that one does not use the mind in the middle. Therefore, one receives this retribution. If an elder gives alms, whether good or bad, much or little, one should use the mind sincerely without increasing or decreasing, abandoning the bridge for future lives. If one is reborn in a place where food and drink are natural, with the seven treasures complete, the mind will always enjoy the five desires. Even if one has servants and slaves, one will always accept their instructions. The reason is that one gives rise to a joyful mind in the middle.

世尊告曰：「若布施之时，若好、若丑，若多、若少，然不用心意，复不发愿，复无信心，由此行报，所生之处不得好食。意不贪乐，意亦复不乐著于好衣裳，亦复不乐著好田业，心亦不著五欲之中，正使有仆从奴婢，亦复不受其教。所以然者，正由其中不用心故，故受其报。若长者布施之时，若好、若丑，若多、若少，当至诚用心，勿有增损，废后世桥梁。彼若所生之处，饮食自然，七财具足，心恒乐五欲之中，正使有奴婢使人，恒受其教。所以然者，由于中发欢喜心故。

Elder, you should know that in the distant past there was a brahman named Vimalamitra, who was wealthy and had many treasures, such as pearls, tiger-eye gems, agate, coral, crystal, and lapis lazuli. He liked to give alms. At that time, when giving alms, he used 84,000 silver bowls filled with gold dust, and also 84,000 golden bowls filled with silver dust, giving like this. He also gave 84,000 golden and silver washbasins. He also gave 84,000 cows, all with golden and silver-covered horns, giving like this. He also gave 84,000 jade maidens, with clothing covering themselves. He also gave 84,000 bedding, all with woolen brocade and woolen blankets covering themselves. He also gave 84,000 garments. He also gave 84,000 dragon elephants, all decorated with gold and silver. He also gave 84,000 horses, all with golden and silver saddles and bridles. He also gave 84,000 chariots, giving such great alms. He also gave 84,000 houses, giving at the four city gates. Those who needed food were given food, those who needed clothing were given clothing, clothing, food, bedding, and medicine for the sick were all given.

「长者当知，过去久远有梵志名毘罗摩，饶财多宝，真珠、虎珀、砗璩、玛瑙、水精、琉璃，好喜布施。尔时，布施之时，用八万四千银钵盛满碎金，复有八万四千金钵盛满碎银，作如是施。复以八万四千金银澡罐施。复以八万四千牛，皆以金银覆角，皆作如是布施。复以八万四千玉女布施，衣裳自覆。复以八万四千卧具，皆用氍氀文绣毾［登＊毛］自覆。复以八万四千衣裳布施。复以八万四千龙象布施，皆用金银挍饰。复以八万四千匹马布施，皆用金银鞍勒自副。复以八万四千车布施，作如是大施。复以八万四千房舍布施，于四城门中布施，须食与食，须衣与衣，衣被、饮食、床卧具、病瘦医药，皆悉与之。

Elder, you should know that although Vimalamitra made these alms, it is not as good as building a house and giving it to the Sangha of the four directions. This merit is immeasurable. Even if he made such alms and built houses to give to the Sangha of the four directions, it is not as good as accepting the three self-refuges in the Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha. This merit is immeasurable. Even if that person made such alms, built houses, and accepted the three self-refuges, although there is this merit, it is still not as good as upholding the five precepts. Even if that person made such alms, built houses, accepted the three self-refuges, and upheld the five precepts, although there is this merit, it is still not as good as a moment of compassion for sentient beings. This merit and virtue is immeasurable.

「长者当知，彼毘罗摩虽作是布施，不如作一房舍，持用布施招提僧，此福不可计量。正使彼作如是施，及作房舍持用施招提僧，不如受三自归佛、法、圣众，此福不可称计。正使彼人作如是施，及作房舍，又受三自归，虽有此福，犹不如受持五戒。正使彼人作如是施，及作房舍，受三自归，受持五戒，虽有此福，故不如弹指之顷慈愍众生，此福功德不可称计。

Even if that person made such alms, built houses for the Sangha, accepted the three self-refuges, upheld the five precepts, and had compassion for sentient beings for a moment, although there is this merit, it is still not as good as a moment of contemplating the impermanence of the world. This merit and virtue is immeasurable. However, I fully certify the merit that person made. I also know the merit of building houses for the Sangha, accepting the three self-refuges, upholding the five precepts, having compassion for sentient beings for a moment, and contemplating the impermanence of the world for a moment.

「正使彼人作如是施，作僧房舍，受三自归，奉持五戒，及弹指之顷慈愍众生，虽有此福，故不如须臾之间，起于世间不可乐想，此福功德不可称量。然彼所作功德，我尽证明，作僧房舍，我亦知此福；受三自归，我亦知此福；受持五戒，我亦知此福；弹指之顷慈愍众生，我亦知此福；须臾之间，起于世间不可乐想，我亦知此福。

"At that time, the one who made such great offerings, was he not someone else? Do not view it like that. Because the one who made the offerings then is none other than myself. Elder, you should know that the merit made in the distant past was not cut off by faith, and did not give rise to attachment. Therefore, elder, when you wish to make offerings, whether many or few, good or bad, give joyfully without giving rise to attachment. Give with your own hands, do not let others do it. Make vows seeking recompense, and later seek to receive blessings. The elder will obtain inexhaustible blessings. Thus, elder, you should train like this."

「尔时，彼婆罗门作如是大施者，岂是异人乎？莫作是观也。所以然者，尔时施主者，即我身是也。长者当知，过去久远所作功德，信心不断，不起著想。是故，长者！若欲布施之时，若多、若少，若好、若丑，欢喜惠施，勿起想著，手自布施，莫使他人，发愿求报，后求受福，长者当获无穷之福。如是，长者！当作是学。」

At that time, the elder, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully accepted and practiced it.

尔时，长者闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
