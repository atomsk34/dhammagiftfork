EĀ 28.5

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

28.5 （五）广演义

Parallel to: DN 16 (~) / AN 4.180 / DĀ 2 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Now there are four great teachings that are widely expounded. What are the four? Namely, the sūtras, the vinaya, the abhidharma, and the precepts - these are called the four. Bhikṣus should know, if there is a bhikṣu who comes from the east, reciting the sūtras, upholding the Dharma, and practicing the precepts, he may say, 'I can recite the sūtras, uphold the Dharma, and practice the precepts, being well-versed and learned.' Even if that bhikṣu speaks, one should not accept it, one should not trust it deeply, but should take that bhikṣu and discuss it together, discussing it according to the Dharma.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「今有四大广演之义。云何为四？所谓契经、律、阿毘昙、戒，是谓为四。比丘当知，若有比丘从东方来，诵经、持法，奉行禁戒，彼便作是语：『我能诵经、持法，奉行禁戒，博学多闻。』正使彼比丘有所说者，不应承受，不足笃信，当取彼比丘而共论议，案法共论。

What is meant by discussing according to the Dharma? Discussing according to the Dharma means discussing these four great teachings, namely the sūtras, the vinaya, the abhidharma, and the precepts. One should explain the sūtras to that bhikṣu, clarify the vinaya, and analyze the Dharma. Even when explaining the sūtras, clarifying the vinaya, and analyzing the Dharma, if he clarifies them, namely in accordance with the sūtras, the vinaya, and the Dharma, then one should accept and uphold them. If they are not in accordance with the sūtras, the vinaya, and the abhidharma, one should tell that person, 'You should know that this is not spoken by the Tathāgata. What you have said is not the original sūtra. The reason is that when I now explain the sūtras, the vinaya, and the abhidharma, they are not in accordance with it.' Because they are not in accordance, one should ask about the precepts. If they are not in accordance with the precepts, one should tell that person, 'This is not the treasure of the Tathāgata.' One should then dismiss them. This is called the first explanation of the basis of the great meaning.

「云何案法共论？所谓案法论者，此四大广演之论，是谓契经、律、阿毘昙、戒，当向彼比丘说契经、布现律、分别法。正使说契经时，布现律、分别法时，若彼布现，所谓与契经相应，律、法相应者，便受持之。设不与契经、律、阿毘昙相应者，当报彼人作是语：『卿当知之，此非如来所说。然卿所说者，非正经之本。所以然者，我今说契经、律、阿毘昙都不与相应。』以不相应，当问戒行，设不与戒行相应者，当语彼人：『此非如来之藏也。』即当发遣使去，此名初演大义之本。

Furthermore, bhikṣus! If there is a bhikṣu who comes from the south and says, 'I can recite the sūtras, uphold the Dharma, practice the precepts, and am well-versed and learned,' even if the bhikṣu speaks, one should not accept it, one should not trust it deeply, but should take that bhikṣu and discuss it together. If what the bhikṣu says is not in accordance with the meaning, one should dismiss him. If it is in accordance with the meaning, one should tell that person, 'This is a meaningful explanation, not the original sūtra.' At that time, one should take that meaning and not accept the original sūtra. The reason is that the meaning is the source of understanding the sūtras. This is called the second explanation of the basis of the great meaning.

「复次，比丘！若有比丘从南方来，而作是语：『我能诵经、持法，奉行禁戒，博学多闻。』正使比丘有所说，不应承受，不足笃信，当取彼比丘而共论议。正使比丘有所说不与义相应者，当发遣之。设与义相应者，当报彼人曰：『此是义说，非正经本。』尔时，当取彼义勿受经本。所以然者，义者解经之源，是谓第二演大义之本。

Furthermore, bhikṣus! If there is a bhikṣu who comes from the west, reciting the sūtras, upholding the Dharma, practicing the precepts, and is well-versed and learned. One should explain the sūtras, the vinaya, and the abhidharma to that bhikṣu. However, that bhikṣu understands the flavor but not the meaning. One should tell that bhikṣu, 'We do not understand this statement. Is it spoken by the Tathāgata or not?' Even when explaining the sūtras, the vinaya, and the abhidharma, if one understands the flavor but not the meaning, although one hears what that bhikṣu says, it is not enough to praise it as good, nor is it enough to say it is bad. One should further ask about the precepts. If they are in accordance, one should remember and accept them. The reason is that the precepts are in accordance with the flavor, but the meaning cannot be clarified. This is called the third explanation of the meaning.

「复次，比丘！若有比丘从西方来，诵经、持法，奉行禁戒，博学多闻。当向彼比丘说契经、律、阿毘昙，然彼比丘正解味，不解义，当语彼比丘作是语：『我等不明此语，为是如来所说也？为非也？』正使说契经、律、阿毘昙时，解味不解义，虽闻彼比丘所说，亦不足誉善，亦不足言恶。复以戒行而问之，设与相应者念承受之。所以然者，戒行与味相应，义不可明故，是谓第三演义也。

Furthermore, bhikṣus! If there is a bhikṣu who comes from the north, reciting the sūtras, upholding the Dharma, and practicing the precepts: 'Virtuous ones, if there are doubts or difficulties, come and ask about the meaning. I will explain it to you.' Even if that bhikṣu speaks, it is not enough to accept it, it is not enough to recite it. One should ask that bhikṣu about the sūtras, the vinaya, the abhidharma, and the precepts. If they are in accordance, one should ask about the meaning. If they are also in accordance with the meaning, one should praise that bhikṣu, 'Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous one! This is truly spoken by the Tathāgata. The meaning is not confused, and it is completely in accordance with the sūtras, the vinaya, the abhidharma, and the precepts.' One should make offerings to that bhikṣu with the Dharma. The reason is that the Tathāgata respects the Dharma. Those who make offerings to the Dharma have already respected me. Those who observe the Dharma have already observed me. Where there is the Dharma, there is me. Where there is the Dharma, there is the community of bhikṣus. Where there is the Dharma, there are the four groups of followers. Where there is the Dharma, the four castes exist in the world. The reason is that because the Dharma exists in the world, there is the appearance of Mahāvikrama in the Fortunate Eon. From then on, the four castes exist in the world: kṣatriyas, brāhmaṇas, artisans, and householders. If the Dharma exists in the world, then the position of the wheel-turning sage kings is uninterrupted. If the Dharma exists in the world, then the four heavenly kings, the Tuṣita Heaven, the Yāma Heaven, the Tuṣita Heaven, the Nirmāṇarati Heaven, and the Paranirmitavaśavartin Heaven exist in the world. If the Dharma exists in the world, then the desire realm gods, form realm gods, and formless realm gods exist in the world. If the Dharma exists in the world, then the fruits of stream-entry, once-returning, non-returning, arahantship, pratyekabuddhahood, and buddhahood appear in the world. Therefore, bhikṣus! One should respect the Dharma well. That bhikṣu should make offerings according to the time and provide for their needs. One should tell that bhikṣu, 'Excellent! Excellent! As you say, what was spoken today is truly spoken by the Tathāgata.' This is called, bhikṣus! the existence of these four great explanations of the meaning.

「复次，比丘！若有比丘从北方来，诵经、持法，奉行禁戒：『诸贤，有疑难者，便来问义，我当与汝说之。』设彼比丘有所说者，不足承受，不足讽诵，然当向彼比丘问契经、律、阿毘昙、戒，共相应者，便当问义。若复与义相应，便当叹誉彼比丘：『善哉！善哉！贤士！此真是如来所说。义不错乱，尽与契经、律、阿毘昙、戒共相应。』当以法供养得彼比丘。所以然者，如来恭敬法故，其有供养法者，则恭敬我已。其观法者，则观我已。有法则有我已，有法则有比丘僧，有法则有四部之众，有法则有四姓在世。所以然者，由法在世，则贤劫中有大威王出世，从是已来便有四姓在世。若法在世，便有四姓在世：刹利、婆罗门、工师、居士种。若法在世者，便有转轮圣王位不绝。若法在世者，便有四天王种、兜术天、艶天、化自在天、他化自在天便在于世。若法在世者，便有欲界天、色界天、无色界天在于世间。若法在世者，便有须陀洹果、斯陀含果、阿那含果、阿罗汉果、辟支佛果、佛乘便现于世。是故，比丘！当善恭敬于法，彼比丘随时供养，给其所须，当语彼比丘作是语：『善哉！善哉！如汝所言，今日所说者，真是如来所说。』是谓，比丘！有此四大广演说之义。

"Therefore, bhikṣus! You should uphold this mind and intention and practice these four things, without letting them slip away. Thus, bhikṣus! You should train in this way."

「是故，诸比丘！当持心执意行此四事，勿有漏脱。如是，诸比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said, and joyfully practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
