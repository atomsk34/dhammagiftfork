EĀ 28.7

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

28.7 （七）柔软

Parallel to: AN 4.88

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "There are four kinds of people who appear in the world. What are the four? There are śramaṇas who resemble the kanerika flower; there are śramaṇas who resemble the bīndurī flower; there are śramaṇas who resemble softness; and among the soft, there are the very soft śramaṇas.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「有四种之人出现于世。云何为四？有似黄蓝花沙门；有似邠陀利华沙门；有似柔软沙门；于柔软中柔软沙门。

What is meant by a śramaṇa who resembles the kanerika flower? There is someone who has severed the three fetters and attained the stage of a stream-enterer, irreversible from the Dharma, destined for nirvāṇa, who at most will be reborn seven times. Or they may be a once-returner or a non-returner, like the kanerika flower that grows from morning to evening. This bhikṣu is also like this. With the three fetters exhausted, he becomes a stream-enterer, irreversible from the Dharma, destined for nirvāṇa, who at most will be reborn seven times. If he seeks skillful means and has a courageous mind, he will become a once-returner or a non-returner. This is called a śramaṇa who resembles the kanerika flower.

「彼云何名为似黄蓝花沙门？或有一人，断三结使，成须陀洹不退转法，必至涅槃，极迟经七死七生；或复家家、一种，犹如黄蓝之花朝取暮长。此比丘亦复如是，三结使尽，成须陀洹，不退转法必至涅槃，极迟至七死七生，若求方便勇猛意者，家家、一种便成道迹，是谓名为黄蓝花沙门。

What is meant by a śramaṇa who resembles the bīndurī flower? There is someone who has exhausted the three fetters, with weakened lust, anger, and delusion, who becomes a once-returner, coming to this world to end suffering. If they are a bit slower, they will come to this world to end suffering. If they are courageous, they will end suffering right here. It is like the bīndurī flower, which opens in the morning and withers in the evening. This is called a śramaṇa who resembles the bīndurī flower.

「彼云何名为邠陀利花沙门？或有一人，三结使尽，婬、怒、痴薄，成斯陀含，来至此世尽于苦际。若小迟者，来至此世尽于苦际；若勇猛者，即于此间尽于苦际。犹如邠陀利花，晨朝剖花，向暮萎死，是谓邠陀利花沙门。

What is meant by a śramaṇa who is gentle? There is someone who has severed the five lower fetters and becomes a non-returner, who attains parinirvāṇa right there and does not return to this world. This is called a gentle śramaṇa.

「彼云何柔软沙门？或有一人断五下分结，成阿那含，即于彼般涅槃，不来此世，是谓柔软沙门。

What is meant by a śramaṇa who is very gentle? There is someone who has exhausted the taints, who attains the liberation of the mind free from taints and the liberation of wisdom, who realizes it for themselves in this very life and plays in it: the cycle of birth and death is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and they will not take rebirth again, knowing it as it really is. This is called a very gentle śramaṇa.

「彼云何柔软中柔软沙门？或有一人有漏尽，成无漏心解脱、智慧解脱，于现法中自身作证而自游戏：生死已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，更不复受胎，如实知之。是谓柔软中柔软沙门。

This is called the appearance of these four people in the world. Therefore, monks! You should seek means to become a gentle śramaṇa among the gentle. Thus, monks! You should train in this way.

「是谓，比丘！有此四人出现于世。是故，诸比丘！当求方便，于柔软中作柔软沙门。如是，诸比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

Suddha, Sumana, Pinda, Rāśi, and Hastī
Lokamuni, and later, the Soft Sutra.

修陀、修摩均　　宾头、尘、翳、手鹿头、广演义　　后乐、柔软经
