EĀ 29.3

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

29.3 （三）

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Now I will speak of the four kinds of merit of the brahman. What are the four? If there are faithful good men and good women who can build a stūpa where one has never been built before, this is called the first kind of merit of the brahman. Furthermore, if faithful good men and good women repair an old temple, this is called the second kind of merit of the brahman. Furthermore, if faithful good men and good women unite the holy assembly, this is called the third kind of merit of the brahman. Furthermore, when the Tathāgata first turned the wheel of the Dharma, the devas and humans in the world requested him to turn the wheel of the Dharma. This is called the fourth kind of merit of the brahman. These are called the four kinds of merit of the brahman."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「今当说四梵之福。云何为四？若有信善男子、善女人，未曾起偷婆处，于中能起偷婆者，是谓初梵之福也。复次，信善男子、善女人，补治故寺者，是谓第二受梵之福也。复次，信善男子、善女人，和合圣众者，是谓第三受梵之福。复次，若多萨阿竭初转法轮时，诸天、世人劝请转法轮，是谓第四受梵之福，是谓四受梵之福。」

At that time, a certain bhikṣu addressed the World-Honored One, saying, "How much merit is there in the merit of the brahman?"

尔时，有异比丘白世尊言：「梵天之福竟为多少？」

The World-Honored One said, "Listen carefully! Listen carefully! Consider it well, and I will now explain it for you."

世尊告曰：「谛听！谛听！善思念之，吾今当说。」

The bhikṣus replied, "Yes."

诸比丘对曰：「如是。」

The World-Honored One said, "The Jambudvīpa continent is seven thousand yojanas from east to west and twenty-one thousand yojanas from south to north. Its shape resembles a chariot. The merit of all the beings within it is equal to the merit of a wheel-turning king.

世尊告曰：「阎浮里地东西七千由旬，南北二万一千由旬，地形像车，其中众生所有功德，正可与一轮王功德等。

The continent of Godānīya is three hundred and twenty thousand yojanas long and wide, and its shape resembles a half-moon. Bhikṣus should know that the merit of the people of Jambudvīpa and a wheel-turning king is equal to the merit of a single person there.

「瞿耶尼纵广三十二万里，地形如半月。比丘当知，阎浮地人民，及一轮王之德，比彼人者，与彼一人德等。

Furthermore, bhikṣus! The continent of Videha is three hundred and sixty thousand yojanas long and wide, and its shape is square. The merit of the two lands of Jambudvīpa and Godānīya is not as good as the merit of a single person in Videha.

「复次，比丘！弗于逮里地纵广三十六万里，地形方正，计阎浮里地及瞿耶尼二方之福，故不如彼弗于逮一人之福。

Bhikṣus should know that the continent of Uttarakuru is four hundred thousand yojanas long and wide, and its shape resembles a full moon. The merit of the people of the three lands is not as good as the merit of a single person in Uttarakuru.

「比丘当知，欝单曰纵广四十万里，地形如月满，计三方人民之福，故不如欝单曰一人之福。

Bhikṣus should know that the merit of the people of the four continents is not as good as the virtue of the Four Heavenly Kings; the merit of the people of the four continents and the Four Heavenly Kings is not as good as the merit of the Thirty-three Heavens; the merit of the people of the four continents, the Four Heavenly Kings, and the Thirty-three Heavens is not as good as the merit of a single Śakra Devānām-Indra; the merit of the people of the four continents, the Four Heavenly Kings, the Thirty-three Heavens, and Śakra Devānām-Indra is not as good as the merit of a single Yāma Heaven; the merit of the people of the four continents, the Four Heavenly Kings, the Thirty-three Heavens, Śakra Devānām-Indra, and the Yāma Heaven is not as good as the merit of a single Tuṣita Heaven; the merit from the four continents up to the Tuṣita Heaven is not as good as the merit of a single Nirmāṇarati Heaven; the merit from the four continents up to the Nirmāṇarati Heaven is not as good as the merit of a single Paranirmitavaśavartin Heaven; the merit from the four continents up to the Paranirmitavaśavartin Heaven is not as good as the merit of a single Brahmā King.

「比丘当知，计四天下人民之福，故不如四天王之德；计四天下人民之福及四天王，故不如三十三天之福；计四天下及四天王、三十三天，故不如释提桓因一人之福；计四天下及四天王及三十三天及释提桓因，故不如一艳天之福；计四天下及四天王、三十三天、释提桓因及艳天，故不如一兜术天福；计从四天下至兜术天之福，故不如一化自在天之福；计从四天下至化自在天之福，故不如一他化自在天之福；计从四天下至他化自在天之福，故不如一梵天王之福。

"Bhikṣus should know that this is the merit of the Brahmā Heaven. If there are good sons and good daughters who seek its merit, this is its measure. Therefore, bhikṣus! Those who wish to seek the merit of the Brahmā Heaven should seek skillful means to accomplish its merit. Thus, bhikṣus! You should train in this way."

「比丘当知，此是梵天之福。若有善男子、善女人，求其福者，此是其量也。是故，比丘！欲求梵天福者，当求方便，成其功德。如是，比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
