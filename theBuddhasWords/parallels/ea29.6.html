EĀ 29.6

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

29.6 （六）

Parallel to: AN 4.77 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "There are four things that should never be contemplated. What are the four? Sentient beings are inconceivable; the world is inconceivable; the realm of nāgas is inconceivable; the realm of the Buddha is inconceivable. The reason is that one cannot attain the nirvāṇa of the cessation of all by relying on these.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「有四事终不可思惟。云何为四？众生不可思议；世界不可思议；龙国不可思议；佛国境界不可思议。所以然者，不由此处得至灭尽涅槃。

How are sentient beings inconceivable? Where do these sentient beings come from? Where do they go? From where do they arise? From here, where will they be born next? In this way, sentient beings are inconceivable.

「云何众生不可思议？此众生为从何来？为从何去？复从何起，从此终当从何生？如是，众生不可思议。

How is the world inconceivable? Those with wrong views say: the world is annihilated, the world is not annihilated, the world has boundaries, the world is boundless, this is the life, this is the body, not life, not body, created by Brahmā, created by the great ghosts and spirits.

「云何世界不可思议？诸有邪见之人：世界断灭、世界不断灭，世界有边、世界无边，是命、是身，非命、非身，梵天之所造，诸大鬼神作此世界耶] cf.【丽】？」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

Brahmā created the people, the world was created by ghosts
or perhaps the ghosts created it - who can determine
this? Bound by desire and anger, the three are equal
together, the mind is not free, and there are calamities
in the world.

「梵天造人民，　　世间鬼所造，或能诸鬼作，　　此语谁当定？欲恚之所缠，　　三者俱共等，心不得自在，　　世俗有灾变。

Thus, bhikkhus! The world is inconceivable.

「如是，比丘！世间不可思议。

How is the dragon realm inconceivable? How does this rain come from the dragon's mouth? Because the raindrops do not come from the dragon's mouth. Does it come from the eyes, ears, or nose? This is also inconceivable. Because the raindrops do not come from the eyes, ears, or nose, but only from the dragon's intention. If it thinks evil, it also rains, if it thinks good, it also rains, and it is also caused by the basis of action. Because now there are gods in the belly of Mount Sumeru, named Mahendra, who know the thoughts of sentient beings and can also cause rain. However, the rain does not come from the mouth, eyes, ears, or nose of those gods. It is all because those gods have supernatural power that they can cause rain. Thus, bhikkhus! The dragon realm is inconceivable.

「云何龙界不可思议？云何此雨为从龙口出耶？所以然者，雨渧不从龙口出也。为从眼、耳、鼻出耶，此亦不可思议。所以然者，雨渧不从眼、耳、鼻出，但龙意之所念，若念恶亦雨，若念善亦雨，亦由行本而作此雨。所以然者，今须弥山腹有天，名曰大力，知众生心之所念，亦能作雨，然雨不从彼天口出，眼、耳、鼻出也。皆由彼天有神力故，而能作雨。如是，比丘！龙境界不可思议。

How is the Buddha realm inconceivable? Is the Tathāgata's body created by parents? This is also inconceivable. Because the Tathāgata's body is pure and undefiled, receiving the weather of all heavens. Is it created by humans? This is also inconceivable. Because it surpasses human practices. The Tathāgata's body is a great body, this is also inconceivable. Because the Tathāgata's body is uncreated, not accessible to the gods. Is the Tathāgata's lifespan short? This is also inconceivable. Because the Tathāgata has the four bases of supernormal power. Is the Tathāgata long-lived? This is also inconceivable. Because the Tathāgata arises in the world, revolving with skillful means. The Tathāgata's body cannot be measured, cannot be said to be long or short. The sound is also immeasurable. The Tathāgata's Brahma voice, the Tathāgata's wisdom and eloquence are inconceivable, not accessible to worldly people. Thus, the Buddha realm is inconceivable.

「云何佛国境界不可思议？如来身者，为是父母所造耶？此亦不可思议。所以然者，如来身者，清净无秽受诸天气。为是人所造耶？此亦不可思议。所以然者，以过人行。如来身者，为是大身，此亦不可思议。所以然者，如来身者，不可造作，非诸天所及。如来寿为短耶？此亦不可思议。所以然者，如来有四神足。如来为长寿耶？此亦不可思议。所以然者，然复如来故兴世间周旋，与善权方便相应。如来身者，不可摸则，不可言长、言短。音声亦不可法则，如来梵音，如来智慧、辩才不可思议，非世间人民之所能及。如是佛境界不可思议。

Thus, bhikkhus! There are these four inconceivable things, not to be conceived by ordinary people. However, these four things have no wholesome basis, and one cannot attain the practice of the holy life by them, nor reach the place of rest, and even not reach the place of nirvana, but only make people confused, with deluded minds, giving rise to various doubts and bonds.

「如是。比丘！有此四处不可思议，非是常人之所思议。然此四事无善根本，亦不由此得修梵行，不至休息之处，乃至不到涅槃之处，但令人狂惑，心意错乱，起诸疑结。

Because, bhikkhus, you should know that in the past, long ago, in this city of Sāvatthī, there was an ordinary person who thought, 'I shall now conceive of the world.' At that time, that person left the city of Sāvatthī and sat cross-legged by a lotus pond, contemplating the world, thinking, 'How is this world formed? How does it decay? Who created this world? Where do these beings come from? Where do they emerge from? When do they arise?' At that time, as that person was contemplating, he saw four kinds of troops entering and leaving the pond. At that time, that person further thought, 'I am now confused, with a deluded mind. What does not exist in the world, I now see.' At that time, that person returned to the city of Sāvatthī and said in the streets, 'Virtuous ones, you should know that what does not exist in the world, I now see.'

「所以然者，比丘当知，过去久远，此舍卫城中有一凡人，便作是念：『我今当思议世界。』是时，彼人出舍卫城，在一华池水侧，结跏趺坐，思惟世界：『此世界云何成？云何败？谁造此世界？此众生类为从何来？为从何出？为何时生？』是时，彼人思议，此时便见池水中有四种兵出入。是时，彼人复作是念：『我今狂惑，心意错乱，世间无者，我今见之。』时，彼人还入舍卫城，在里巷之中作是说：『诸贤当知，世界无者，我今见之。』

At that time, many people said to that person, 'How can you say that what does not exist in the world, you now see?' At that time, that person replied to the many people, 'I was just thinking, "Where does the world come from?" I then left the city of Sāvatthī and sat by a lotus pond, contemplating like this: "Where does the world come from? Who created this world? Where do these beings come from? Who gave birth to them? When they die, where will they be reborn?" I was contemplating, and at that time, I saw four kinds of troops entering and leaving the pond. What does not exist in the world, I now see.' At that time, the many people said to that person, 'You are truly insane. How can there be four kinds of troops in a pond? Among all the insane people in the world, you are the foremost!'

「是时，众多人报彼人曰：『云何世间无者，汝今见之？』时，此人报众多人曰：『我向者作是思惟：「世界为从何生？」便出舍卫城，在华池侧，作是思议：「世界为从何来？谁造此世界？此众生类从何而来？为谁所生？若命终者当生何处？」我当思议，此时，便见池水中有四种兵出入，世界无者，我今见之。』是时，众多人报彼人曰：『如汝实狂愚，池水之中那得四种兵？诸世界狂愚之中，汝最为上！』

Therefore, bhikkhus, I have seen this meaning and have told you. Because this is not a wholesome root of merit, one cannot cultivate the holy life, nor can one attain the place of nirvāṇa. Contemplating this will make one insane and confused. Bhikkhus, you should know that that person really saw the four kinds of troops. Because in the past, the devas fought with the asuras. When they fought, the devas were victorious and the asuras were defeated. At that time, the asuras were terrified and transformed their bodies to be extremely small, passing through the holes in the lotus roots. The Buddha's eyes can see what others cannot reach.

「是故，比丘！我观此义已，故告汝等耳。所以然者，此非善本功德，不得修梵行，亦复不得至涅槃处。然思议此者，则令人狂，心意错乱。然比丘当知，彼人实见四种之兵。所以然者，昔日诸天与阿须伦共鬪，当共鬪时，诸天得胜，阿须伦不如。是时，阿须伦便怀恐怖，化形极使小，从藕根孔中过。佛眼之所见非余者所及。

"Therefore, bhikkhus, you should contemplate the Four Noble Truths. Because these Four Noble Truths have meaning and principle, one can cultivate the holy life, practice the way of the renunciant, and attain nirvāṇa. Therefore, bhikkhus, abandon the worldly dharmas and seek the means to contemplate the Four Noble Truths. Knowing this, bhikkhus, you should train in this way."

「是故，诸比丘！当思议四谛。所以然者，此四谛者，有义、有理，得修梵行，行沙门法，得至涅槃。是故，诸比丘！舍离此世界之法，当求方便，思议四谛。知是，诸比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
