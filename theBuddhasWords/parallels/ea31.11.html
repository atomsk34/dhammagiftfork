EĀ 31.11

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

31.11 （一一）无诤讼

Parallel to: SN 16.6 (~) / SĀ 1138 (~) / SĀ² 2.113 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the disciples of Maudgalyāyana and the disciples of Ānanda had a discussion: "The two of us chant the sūtras together. Who is better?"

尔时，目连弟子、阿难弟子二人共谈：「我等二人同声经呗，谁者为胜？」

At that time, many bhikṣus heard the discussion between the two. Having heard it, they went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed their heads at his feet, and sat to one side. Then the many bhikṣus said to the World-Honored One: "Now there are two people discussing: 'The two of us chant the sūtras together. Which is better?'"

是时，众多比丘闻此二人各各共论，闻已，便往至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。尔时，众多比丘白世尊言：「今有二人共论：『我等二人共诵经呗，何者为妙？』」

At that time, the World-Honored One told a bhikṣu: "Go and call these two bhikṣus to come."

尔时，世尊告一比丘：「汝往呼此二比丘使来。」

The bhikṣu replied: "Yes, World-Honored One!" The bhikṣu received the Buddha's instruction and went to where the two were. He said to the two: "The World-Honored One calls you."

比丘对曰：「如是。世尊！」比丘从佛受教。即往至彼二人所，语彼二人曰：「世尊唤卿。」

At that time, when the two heard the bhikṣu's words, they went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed their heads at his feet, and stood to one side.

是时，二人闻比丘语已，即至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面住。

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the two: "You foolish ones, did you really say, 'We recite sutras together, which one is better?'"

尔时，世尊告二人曰：「汝等愚人实有此语：『我等共诵经呗，何者为妙？』」

The two replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

二人对曰：「如是。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "Have you ever heard me say this Dharma and compete with each other? How is this Dharma different from the Brahmins?"

世尊告曰：「汝等颇闻我说此法共竞诤乎？如此之法，何异梵志？」

The bhikṣus replied, "We have not heard the Tathāgata say this Dharma."

诸比丘对曰：「不闻如来而说此法。」

The World-Honored One said, "Have I ever taught this Dharma to the bhikṣus, saying to compete for victory? Rather, the Dharma I teach today is to subdue and guide. When a bhikṣu receives the Dharma, he should contemplate the four conditions and see if his mind accords with the sūtras, abhidharma, and vinaya. If it does accord, he should remember to practice accordingly."

世尊告曰：「我由来不与诸比丘而说此法，当诤胜负耶？然我今日所说法，欲有降伏，有所教化。若有比丘受法之时，当念思惟四缘之法，意与契经、阿毘昙、律共相应不？设共相应者，当念奉行。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

Reciting much without benefit is not the wonderful Dharma. It
is like counting the horns of an ox, not the
essential of a śramaṇa. If one recites little or much
but practices the Dharma, this Dharma is supreme, and can
be called the Dharma of a śramaṇa. Although one recites
a thousand verses, if they are not meaningful, what benefit
is there? It is better to hear one verse that
can lead to the path. Although one recites a thousand
words, if they are not meaningful, what benefit is there?
It is better to hear one meaning that can lead
to the path. A thousand enemies can be defeated by
one man, but it is better to conquer oneself. The
one who can endure is supreme.

「多诵无益事，　　此法非为妙，犹算牛头数，　　非此沙门要。若少多诵习，　　于法而行法，此法极为上，　　可谓沙门法。虽诵千章，　　不义何益？　　不如一句，闻可得道。　　虽诵千言，　　不义何益？不如一义，　　闻可得道。　　千千为敌，一夫胜之，　　未若自胜，　　已忍者上。

"Therefore, bhikṣus! From now on, do not dispute with each other, having a mind of victory and defeat. The reason is that we should subdue all people. If there are bhikṣus who have a mind of victory and defeat, dispute with each other, and compete, then we should use the Dharma and Vinaya to correct them. Bhikṣus! For this reason, we should practice on our own."

「是故，诸比丘！自今以后，未复诤讼有胜负心。所以然者，念当降伏一切人民。若复比丘有胜负心，共诤讼心而共竞者，即以法、律治彼。比丘！以是之故，当自修行。」

"Therefore, when the two bhikṣus heard these words of the Buddha, they immediately rose from their seats, bowed at the feet of the World-Honored One, and sought to repent, saying: "From now on, we will never do it again. We only pray that the World-Honored One will accept our repentance.

是故，二比丘闻佛此语已，即从坐起，礼世尊足，而求悔过：「自今已后，更不复为，唯愿世尊受其悔过。」

The World-Honored One said: "In the great Dharma, it is excellent to be able to correct one's mistakes. If you know that you have a mind of dispute and competition, I allow you to repent. Bhikṣus! Do not do it again like this. Thus, bhikṣus! You should train like this."

世尊告曰：「大法之中快得改过，自知有诤竞之心，听汝悔过。诸比丘！更莫复尔。如是，诸比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

superior, sitting, traces of practice, impermanence, garden, pond
without taints, without cessation, meditation, four joys, without disputes

增上、坐、行迹　　无常、园观池无漏、无息、禅　　四乐、无诤讼
