EĀ 32.5

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

32.5 （五）岁

Parallel to: SN 8.7 / MĀ 121 / SĀ 1212 / SĀ² 2.228

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at the Deer Park in the Eastern Garden of the Jeta Grove in the country of Śrāvastī, together with a great assembly of five hundred bhikṣus.

一时，佛在舍卫国东苑鹿母园中，与大比丘众五百人俱。

At that time, on the fifteenth day of the seventh month, the World-Honored One was sitting on a seat in the open air, surrounded by the assembly of bhikṣus. The Buddha said to Ānanda, "Now, quickly strike the gong in the open air. Because today is the fifteenth day of the seventh month, the day for receiving the summer retreat."

是时，世尊七月十五日于露野地敷座，比丘僧前后围遶，佛告阿难曰：「汝今于露地速击揵椎。所以然者，今七月十五日是受岁之日。」

At that time, Venerable Ānanda knelt on his right knee, joined his palms, and spoke this verse:

是时，尊者阿难右膝著地，长跪叉手，便说此偈：

Pure eyes are without equal, nothing is not practiced, wisdom
is without attachment, what is called receiving the summer retreat?

「净眼无与等，　　无事而不练，智慧无染著，　　何等名受岁？」

At that time, the World-Honored One again replied to Ānanda in verse:

尔时，世尊复以偈报阿难曰：

Receiving the summer retreat, the three karmas are pure, the body, speech, and
mind are what is done. Two bhikshus face each other and confess their
shortcomings. They themselves proclaim their names, today the assembly receives the summer retreat
I also receive with a pure mind, only wishing to forgive the faults.

「受岁三业净，　　身口意所作，两两比丘对，　　自陈所作短。还自称名字，　　今日众受岁，我亦净意受，　　唯愿原其过。」

At that time, Ānanda again asked the meaning in verse:

尔时，阿难复以偈问其义曰：

The past Buddhas, pratyekabuddhas, and śrāvakas, are all
the Dharma of the Buddhas? Is it only Śākyamuni?

「过去恒沙佛，　　辟支及声闻，尽是诸佛法？　　独是释迦文？」

At that time, the Buddha again replied to Ānanda in verse:

尔时，佛复以偈报阿难曰：

The disciples of the past Buddhas, with pure minds, all practiced the
Dharma of the Buddhas, not just Śākyamuni. The pratyekabuddhas did not have
this Dharma, no years, no disciples. They passed away alone, without companions
and did not teach the Dharma to others. The future Buddha-World-Honored Ones
as numerous as the sands of the Ganges, cannot be calculated. They
will also receive these years, just like the Dharma of Gautama now.

「恒沙过去佛，　　弟子清净心，皆是诸佛法，　　非今释迦文。辟支无此法，　　无岁无弟子，独逝无伴侣，　　不与他说法。当来佛世尊，　　恒沙不可计，彼亦受此岁，　　如今瞿昙法。」

At that time, Venerable Ānanda, having heard these words, was overjoyed and could not contain himself. He immediately ascended the lecture hall, took hold of the bell, and said, "I now strike this drum of faith in the Tathāgata. All the disciples of the Tathāgata should gather here."

是时，尊者阿难闻此语已，欢喜踊跃，不能自胜，即升讲堂，手执揵椎，并作是说：「我今击此如来信鼓，诸有如来弟子众者，尽当普集。」

At that time, he also spoke this verse:

尔时，复说此偈：

Subduing the power of Mara's enemies, eliminating all afflictions without remainder, striking
the bell in the open air, the bhikṣus who hear it should
gather. Those who wish to hear the Dharma, to cross the ocean
of birth and death, hearing this wonderful sound, should all gather here.

「降伏魔力怨，　　除结无有余，露地击揵椎，　　比丘闻当集。诸欲闻法人，　　度流生死海，闻此妙响音，　　尽当运集此。」

At that time, Venerable Ānanda had struck the bell and went to where the World-Honored One was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said to the World-Honored One, "Now is the right time. Please command me, World-Honored One, what should I do?"

尔时，尊者阿难已击揵椎，至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面住，白世尊言：「今正是时，唯愿世尊何所勅使。」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to Ānanda, "You may sit in order. The Tathāgata will know the time himself." At that time, the World-Honored One sat on a grass seat and said to the bhikṣus, "You should all sit on grass seats."

是时，世尊告阿难曰：「汝随次坐，如来自当知时。」是时，世尊坐于草座告诸比丘：「汝等尽当坐于草座。」

The bhikṣus replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!" At that time, the bhikṣus each sat on grass seats.

诸比丘对曰：「如是。世尊！」时，诸比丘各坐草座。

At that time, the World-Honored One silently observed the bhikṣus and then instructed the bhikṣus, "I now wish to receive the annual confession. Do I have any faults among the assembly? Have I not committed any offenses with body, speech, or mind?" Having spoken these words, the bhikṣus remained silent and did not respond. At that time, he again addressed the bhikṣus three times, "I now wish to receive the annual confession. Do I have any faults among the assembly?"

是时，世尊默然观诸比丘已，便勅诸比丘：「我今欲受岁。我无过咎于众人乎？又不犯身、口、意？」如来说此语已，诸比丘默然不对。是时，复再三告诸比丘：「我今欲受岁。然我无过于众人乎？」

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra rose from his seat, knelt with palms joined, and addressed the World-Honored One, saying, "The bhikṣu assembly observes the Tathāgata to be without any faults of body, speech, or mind. The reason is that the World-Honored One today liberates those who are not liberated, frees those who are not freed, causes those who have not attained parinirvāṇa to attain parinirvāṇa, provides refuge for those who have no refuge, becomes the eyes for the blind, becomes the great physician-king for the sick, is the sole honored one in the three realms without equal, the most honored and supreme one, causes those who have not yet aroused the intention for enlightenment to arouse the intention for enlightenment, awakens those who are not yet awakened among the people, causes those who have not heard the Dharma to hear it, becomes the path for the confused, and always uses the true Dharma. For these reasons, the Tathāgata has no faults among the assembly, and also has no faults of body, speech, or mind."

是时，尊者舍利弗即从坐起，长跪叉手，白世尊言：「诸比丘众观察如来，无身、口、意过。所以然者，世尊今日不度者度，不脱者脱，不般涅槃者令般涅槃，无救者为作救护，盲者作眼目，为病者作大医王，三界独尊无能及者，最尊、最上，未起道意者使发道意，众人未寤尊令寤之，未闻法者使令闻之，为迷者作径路，恒以正法；以此事缘，如来无咎于众人，亦无身、口、意过。」

At that time, Śāriputra addressed the World-Honored One, saying, "I now confess to the Tathāgata. Do I have any faults with the Tathāgata and the assembly of bhikṣus?"

是时，舍利弗白世尊言：「我今向如来自陈，然无咎于如来及比丘僧乎？」

The World-Honored One said, "Śāriputra, you now have no faults of body, speech, or mind. The reason is that your wisdom is unsurpassed. You have various wisdoms, immeasurable wisdoms, boundless wisdoms, incomparable wisdoms, swift wisdom, sharp wisdom, profound wisdom, equal wisdom. You have few desires and are content, delight in quietude, have many skillful means, your mindfulness is not confused, you have the dhāraṇī samādhi, and your faculties are complete. You have accomplished precepts, accomplished samādhi, accomplished wisdom, accomplished liberation, and accomplished the wisdom of liberation. You are courageous and patient, your speech is without fault, you do not engage in unwholesome activities, your mind is orderly, and you do not act rashly. You are like the eldest son of a wheel-turning sage king, who will succeed to the throne and turn the wheel of Dharma. Śāriputra is also like this, turning the unsurpassed wheel of Dharma. The devas, humans, nāgas, yakṣas, māras, and their devas have not turned it before, but what you have spoken now is always in accordance with the meaning of the Dharma and has never contradicted the principles."

世尊告曰：「汝今，舍利弗！都无身、口、意所作非行。所以然者，汝今智慧无能及者，种种智慧、无量智慧、无边之智、无与等智、疾智、捷智、甚深之智、平等之智，少欲知足，乐静之处，多诸方便，念不错乱，总持三昧，根原具足：戒成就、三昧成就、智慧成就、解脱成就、解脱见慧成就，勇悍能忍，所说无恶，不为非法，心性庠序，不行卒暴。犹如转轮圣王最大太子，当绍王位，转于法轮。舍利弗亦如是，转于无上法轮，诸天、世人及龙、鬼、魔、若魔天，本所不转，汝今所说，常如法义，未曾违理。」

At that time, Śāriputra addressed the Buddha, saying, "These five hundred bhikṣus will all receive the full ordination. Are there no faults with these five hundred people with regard to the Tathāgata?"

是时，舍利弗白佛言：「此五百比丘尽当受岁。此五百人尽无咎于如来乎？」

The World-Honored One said, "I do not blame these five hundred bhikṣus for their physical, verbal, or mental actions. The reason is that Śāriputra is extremely pure in this great assembly, without any faults. Now the lowest-ranking person in this assembly has attained the path of stream-entry and will definitely progress to the irreversible Dharma. For this reason, I do not blame this assembly."

世尊告曰：「亦不责此五百比丘身、口、意行。所以然者，此舍利弗大众之中极为清净，无有瑕秽，今此众中最小下坐，得须陀洹道，必当上及不退转法；以是之故，我不怨责此众。」

At that time, Tagarasikhin was in this assembly. He immediately rose from his seat, went before the World-Honored One, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and addressed the World-Honored One, saying, "I am now able and willing to discuss something."

尔时，多耆奢在此众中即从坐起，前至世尊所，头面礼足，白世尊言：「我今堪任欲有所论。」

The World-Honored One said, "If you wish to speak, now is the right time."

世尊告曰：「欲有所说，今正是时。」

Tagarasikhin then praised the Buddha and the Sangha of monks in front of the Buddha, speaking this verse:

多耆奢即于佛前叹佛及比丘僧，而说此偈：

On the fifteenth day of purity, five hundred monks gathered, all bonds
and fetters were released, and there was no more craving. The great
sage king of the wheel was surrounded by ministers, universally throughout the
worlds, in the heavens and the world. The great general, the most
honored among men, became a guide for people, with disciples joyfully following
with the three knowledges and the six supernormal powers. They were all
true sons of the Buddha, without any defilements, able to cut off
the thorns of desire and craving. Today I take refuge in myself.

「十五清净日，　　五百比丘集，诸结缚悉解，　　无爱更不生。转轮大圣王，　　群臣所围遶，普遍诸世界，　　天上及世间。大将人中尊，　　为人作导师，弟子乐徒从，　　三达六通彻。皆是真佛子，　　无有尘垢者，能断欲爱刺，　　今日自归命。」

At that time, the World-Honored One approved of what Tagarasikhin had said. Then Tagarasikhin thought: "The Tathāgata has approved of what I have said today." Joyfully leaping and dancing, unable to contain himself, he immediately rose from his seat, bowed to the Buddha, and withdrew to his original position.

尔时，世尊可多耆奢所说。是时，多耆奢作是念：「如来今日可我所说。」欢喜踊跃，不能自胜，即从坐起，礼佛却退，还就本位。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus: "Among my śrāvakas, the foremost disciple in composing verses is none other than Bhikṣu Tagarasikhin. The one who speaks without hesitation is also none other than Bhikṣu Tagarasikhin."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「我声闻中第一造偈弟子，所谓多耆奢比丘是。所说无疑难，亦是多耆奢比丘是也。」

At that time, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and upheld it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
