EĀ 34.8

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

34.8 （八）

Parallel to: SN 11.4 / SĀ 1110 / SĀ² 2.39

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "In the past, Śakra, Lord of the Devas, told the Thirty-three Heavens, 'If you, the virtuous ones, fight with the asuras and the asuras are defeated and the devas are victorious, you should capture Vimalacitra, the asura king, and bring him here, binding him with five ropes.' At that time, Vimalacitra, the asura king, also told the asuras, 'You should fight with the devas today. If you are victorious, capture Śakra, Lord of the Devas, and bring him here, bound.' Bhikṣus, you should know that at that time, the two sides fought, and the devas were victorious, while the asuras were defeated. Then the Thirty-three Heavens personally captured Vimalacitra, the asura king, bound his body, and brought him to where Śakra, Lord of the Devas, was. They placed him outside the central gate, observing his five bonds.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「昔者，释提桓因告三十三天曰：『若诸贤与阿须伦共鬪时，设阿须伦不如，诸天得胜者，汝等捉毘摩质多罗阿须伦，将来至此，身为五系。』是时，毘摩质多罗阿须伦，复告诸阿须伦曰：『卿等！今日与诸天共鬪，设得胜者，便捉释提桓因，缚送此间。』比丘当知，尔时，二家共鬪，诸天得胜，阿须伦不如。是时，三十三天躬捉毘摩质多罗阿须伦王，束缚其身，将诣释提桓因所，著中门外，自观彼五系。

At that time, Vimalacitra, the asura king, thought, 'The ways of the devas are proper, while the ways of the asuras are improper. I do not enjoy the asuras now. I should stay in this deva palace.' At that moment, he spoke these words, 'The ways of the devas are proper, while the ways of the asuras are improper. I wish to stay here.' As soon as he had this thought, Vimalacitra, the asura king, immediately became aware that his body was no longer bound, and he enjoyed the five desires. If Vimalacitra, the asura king, had this thought, 'The ways of the devas are improper, while the ways of the asuras are proper. I do not need this Thirty-three Heaven. I wish to return to the asura palace,' then the asura king's body would be bound with five ropes, and the enjoyment of the five desires would naturally disappear.

「是时，毘摩质多罗阿须伦王便作是念：『此诸天法整，阿须伦所行非法，我今不乐阿须伦，便当即住此诸天宫。』是时，以生此念言：『诸天法整，阿须伦非法，我欲住此间。』作此念已。是时，毘摩质多罗阿须伦王便自觉知身无缚系，五欲而自娱乐。设毘摩质多罗阿须伦王生此念已，言：『诸天非法，阿须伦法整，我不用此三十三天，还欲诣阿须伦宫。』是时，阿须伦王身被五系，五欲娱乐自然消灭。

"Bhikṣus, you should know that the urgency of being bound is nothing more than this; the binding by Māra is even worse than this. If bound by the Māra of afflictions, one is bound by the moving Māra, but not bound by the unmoving Māra. Therefore, bhikṣus, you should seek means to ensure that your mind is not bound, and delight in secluded places. The reason is that these afflictions are the realm of Māra. If a bhikṣu is in the realm of Māra, he will never escape birth, old age, sickness, and death, and will not escape sorrow, grief, suffering, and distress. I now speak of this as the limit of suffering. If a bhikṣu's mind is unmoved and not attached to afflictions, he will be free from birth, old age, sickness, death, sorrow, grief, suffering, and distress. I now speak of this as the limit of suffering. Therefore, bhikṣus, you should train in this way, without afflictions, transcending the realm of Māra. Thus, bhikṣus, you should train in this way."

「比丘当知，缠缚之急，莫过此事；魔之所缚，复甚于斯。设与结使魔以被缚，动魔被缚，不动魔不被缚。是故，诸比丘！当求方便，使心不被缚，乐闲静之处。所以然者，此诸结使是魔境界。若有比丘在魔境界者，终不脱生、老、病、死，不脱愁、忧、苦、恼。我今说此苦际。若复比丘心不移动，不著结使，便脱生、老、病、死、愁、忧、苦、恼，我今说此苦际。是故，诸比丘！当作是学，无有结使，越出魔界。如是，比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said, and joyfully practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
