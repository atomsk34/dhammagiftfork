EĀ 35.9

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

35.9 （九）

Parallel to: SN 8.4 / SĀ 1214 / SĀ² 2.230

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at the Karanda Bamboo Grove in the city of Rajagriha, together with a great assembly of five hundred bhikshus.

一时，佛在罗阅城迦兰陀竹园所，与大比丘众五百人俱。

At that time, Ānanda and Taggasa, having put on their robes and taken their alms bowls, entered the city to beg for food. At that time, Taggasa saw a woman in an alley who was extremely beautiful and extraordinary in the world. Having seen her, his mind became confused and was not as usual.

尔时，阿难、多耆奢时到，著衣持钵，入城乞食。是时，多耆奢在一巷中见一女人，极为端正，与世奇特；见已，心意错乱，不与常同。

At that time, Taggasa immediately spoke a verse to Ānanda:

是时，多耆奢即以偈向阿难说：

The fire of desire burns, the mind is extremely ablaze. Please
speak to extinguish this meaning, for the sake of benefiting many.

「欲火之所烧，　　心意极炽然，愿说灭此义，　　多有所饶益。」

At that time, Ānanda also replied with this verse:

是时，阿难复以此偈报曰：

Knowing the inverted nature of desire, the mind is extremely ablaze. One
should eliminate conceptual thoughts, and the intention of desire will naturally cease.

「知欲颠倒法，　　心意极炽然，当除想像念，　　欲意便自休。」

At that time, Mahakasyapa also replied with this verse:

是时，多耆奢复以偈报曰：

The mind is the basis of form, the eyes are the source of perception
When sleeping, one sees being supported and assisted, the body is like wilted grass.

「心为形之本，　　眼为候之原，睡卧见扶接，　　形如乱草萎。」

At that time, Venerable Ānanda immediately stepped forward and stroked Mahakasyapa's head with his right hand. Then he spoke this verse:

是时，尊者阿难即前进，以右手摩多耆奢头。尔时，即说此偈：

Mindful of the Buddha, free from desire, liberating Nandaka from the difficulties of desire
seeing the heavens and the hells, controlling the mind to leave the five destinies.

「念佛无贪欲，　　度彼欲难陀，覩天现地狱，　　制意离五趣。」

At that time, Mahakasyapa, having heard Venerable Ānanda's words, said, "Stop! Stop! Ānanda!" After begging for alms together, they returned to where the World-Honored One was.

是时，多耆奢闻尊者阿难语已，便作是说：「止！止！阿难！」俱乞食讫，还至世尊所。

At that time, the woman saw Mahakasyapa from afar and smiled. When Mahakasyapa saw the woman smiling from afar, he had this thought: "Your body now is like a painted bottle, filled with impurities inside, deceiving the world and causing people to have disordered thoughts." At that time, Venerable Mahakasyapa observed the woman from head to toe, thinking, "What is there to be greedy about in this body? The thirty-six substances are all impure. Where do these things come from?" At that time, Venerable Mahakasyapa further thought, "I now observe others' bodies, but it is no different from observing my own body. Where does this desire come from? Does it come from the earth element, the water element, the fire element, or the wind element? If it comes from the earth element, the earth element is solid and indestructible; if it comes from the water element, the water element is extremely fluid and cannot be grasped; if it comes from the fire element, the fire element cannot be grasped; if it comes from the wind element, the wind element is formless and cannot be grasped." At that time, Venerable Mahakasyapa thought, "This desire only arises from thought.

是时，彼女人遥见多耆奢便笑。时，多耆奢遥见女人笑，便生此想念：「汝今形体骨立皮缠，亦如画瓶，内盛不净，诳惑世人，令发乱想。」尔时，尊者多耆奢观彼女人，从头至足，此形体中有何可贪？三十六物皆悉不净。今此诸物为从何生？是时，尊者多耆奢复作是念：「我今观他形，为不如自观身中，此欲为从何生？为从地种生耶？水、火、风种生耶？设从地种生，地种坚强不可沮坏；设从水种生，水种极濡不可获持；设从火种生，火种不可获持；设从风种生，风种无形而不可获持。」是时，尊者便作是念：「此欲者，但从思想生。」

At that time, he spoke this verse:

尔时，便说此偈：

I know your origin, desire, you only arise from thought. If
I do not think of you, then you do not exist.

「欲我知汝本，　　但以思想生，非我思想汝，　　则汝而不有。」

At that time, Venerable Tagarasikhi also spoke this verse, contemplating the thought of impurity, and at that place, the mind with taints was liberated.

尔时，尊者多耆奢又说此偈，如思惟不净之想，即于彼处有漏心得解脱。

At that time, Ānanda and Tagarasikhi left Rājagṛha and went to where the World-Honored One was. They bowed their heads at his feet and sat to one side. Then Tagarasikhi said to the World-Honored One, "I have now attained great benefits and have gained realization."

时，阿难及多耆奢出罗阅城至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。是时，多耆奢白世尊言：「我今快得善利，以有所觉。」

The World-Honored One said, "How have you realized it yourself?"

世尊告曰：「汝今云何自觉？」

Tagarasikhi said to the Buddha, "Form is impermanent, not solid, not real, illusory and false. Feeling is impermanent, not solid, like bubbles on water, illusory and false. Perception is impermanent, not solid, illusory and false, like a mirage. Formation is impermanent, not solid, like a plantain tree with no substance. Consciousness is impermanent, not solid, illusory and false." He repeated, "These five aggregates of clinging are impermanent, not solid, illusory and false."

多耆奢白佛言：「色者无牢，亦不坚固，不可覩见，幻伪不真；痛者无牢，亦不坚固，亦如水上泡，幻伪不真；想者无牢，亦不坚固，幻伪不真，亦如野马；行亦无牢，亦不坚固，亦如芭蕉之树，而无有实；识者无牢，亦不坚固，幻伪不真。」重白佛言：「此五盛阴无牢，亦不坚固，幻伪不真。」

At that time, Venerable Tagarasikhi spoke this verse:

是时，尊者多耆奢便说此偈：

Form is like foam, feeling is like bubbles
perception is like a mirage, formation is like
a plantain tree, and consciousness is like an
illusory phenomenon, as the Supreme One has said
Having contemplated this, I have thoroughly observed all
conditioned phenomena, which are all empty and tranquil
without true reality. This is all due to
this body, as the Well-Gone One has said
I should eliminate the three dharmas, seeing form
as impure. This body is like this, illusory
and false. This is called the dharma of
harm. The five aggregates are impermanent and have
already been understood to be unreal. Now I
return to the traces of the past.

「色如聚沫，　　痛如浮泡，　　想如野马，行如芭蕉，　　识为幻法，　　最胜所说。思惟此已，　　尽观诸行，　　皆悉空寂，无有真正，　　皆由此身，　　善逝所说。当灭三法，　　见色不净，　　此身如是，幻伪不真，　　此名害法，　　五阴不牢，已解不真，　　今还上迹。

"So it is, World-Honored One! What I have realized now is precisely this."

「如是。世尊！我今所觉正谓此耳。」

The World-Honored One said, "Excellent! Manyeśa! You are able to observe the origin of these five aggregates. You should know that a practitioner should observe the origin of these five aggregates, which are all impermanent. The reason is that when observing these five aggregates, under the Bodhi tree, I attained supreme perfect enlightenment, just as you observe today." At that time, when this teaching was spoken, sixty bhikṣus on the seat attained the exhaustion of defilements and liberation.

世尊告曰：「善哉！多耆奢！善能观察此五盛阴本。汝今当知，夫为行人当观察此五阴之本，皆不牢固。所以然者，当观此五盛阴时，在道树下成无上等正觉，亦如卿今日所观。」尔时，说此法时，坐上六十比丘漏尽意解。

At that time, Venerable Manyeśa, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，尊者多耆奢闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
