EĀ 36.5

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

36.5 （五）

Parallel to: SN 40.10 (~) / SĀ 506 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī, together with a great assembly of five hundred bhikṣus.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时世尊与大比丘众五百人俱。

At that time, Śakra, lord of the devas, in the time it takes to bend and stretch out an arm, came to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head to the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. At that time, Śakra, lord of the devas, said to the World-Honored One, "The Tathāgata also said, 'When the Tathāgata appears in the world, he must do five things. What are the five? He must turn the wheel of the Dharma; he must liberate his parents; he must establish those without faith on the ground of faith; he must cause those who have not given rise to the bodhi mind to give rise to the bodhi mind; and he must receive the prediction of Buddhahood in between. These five causes and conditions are what the Tathāgata appears in the world for.' Now the Tathāgata's mother is in the Heaven of the Thirty-three, wishing to hear the Dharma. Now the Tathāgata is in Jambudvīpa, surrounded by the four assemblies, with kings and people all coming to gather. Excellent, World-Honored One! Please go to the Heaven of the Thirty-three and teach the Dharma to your mother." At that time, the World-Honored One silently accepted.

尔时，释提桓因如屈申臂顷，来至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。尔时，释提桓因白世尊言：「如来亦说：『夫如来出世必当为五事。云何为五？当转法轮；当度父母；无信之人立于信地；未发菩萨心令发菩萨意；于其中间当受佛决。此五因缘如来出现必当为之。』今如来母在三十三天，欲得闻法，今如来在阎浮里内，四部围遶，国王人民皆来运集。善哉！世尊！可至三十三天与母说法。」是时，世尊默然受之。

At that time, the dragon kings Nanda and Upananda thought, "These bald-headed śramaṇas are flying above us. We should find a way to make them suffer." Then the dragon kings became angry and released a great wind and fire, causing the entire Jambudvīpa to be engulfed in flames.

尔时，难陀、优槃难陀龙王便作是念：「此诸秃沙门在我上飞，当作方便，使不陵易。」是时，龙王便兴瞋恚，放大火风，使阎浮里内洞然火燃。

At that time, Ānanda said to the Buddha, "Why is there this smoke and fire in Jambudvīpa?"

是时，阿难白佛言：「此阎浮里内，何故有此烟火？」

The World-Honored One said, "These two dragon kings had this thought: 'These bald-headed śramaṇas always fly above us. We should restrain them so they cannot fly in the sky.' They then became angry and released this smoke and fire. For this reason, this transformation occurred."

世尊告曰：「此二龙王便生此念：『秃头沙门恒在我上飞，我等当共制之，令不陵虚。』便兴瞋恚，放此烟火，由此因缘，故致此变。」

At that time, Mahākāśyapa immediately rose from his seat and said to the World-Honored One, "I now wish to go and fight with them."

是时，大迦叶即从坐起，白世尊言：「我今欲往，与彼共战。」

The World-Honored One said, "These two dragon kings are extremely fierce and difficult to be tamed. You should return to your seat."

世尊告曰：「此二龙王极为凶恶，难可受化，卿还就坐。」

At that time, Venerable Aniruddha immediately rose from his seat and said to the World-Honored One, "I now wish to go and subdue that evil dragon."

是时，尊者阿那律即从坐起，白世尊言：「我今欲往降彼恶龙。」

The World-Honored One said, "These two evil dragons are extremely fierce and difficult to be tamed. You should return to your seat."

世尊告曰：「此二恶龙极为凶暴，难可受化，卿还就坐。」

At that time, Revata, Venerable Katyayana, Venerable Subhuti, Venerable Udayi, and Venerable Baka each rose from their seats and said to the World-Honored One, "We now wish to go and subdue the evil dragons."

是时，离越、尊者迦旃延、尊者须菩提、尊者优陀夷、尊者婆竭，各从坐起，白世尊言：「我今欲往降伏恶龙。」

The World-Honored One said, "These two dragon kings are extremely fierce and difficult to be tamed. You should return to your seats."

世尊告曰：「此二龙王极为凶恶，难可受化，卿还就坐。」

At that time, Venerable Maha Maudgalyayana immediately rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, knelt on his right knee, joined his palms, and said to the Buddha, "I wish to go there and subdue the evil dragons."

尔时，尊者大目揵连即从坐起，偏露右肩，长跪叉手，白佛言：「欲往诣彼，降伏恶龙。」

The World-Honored One said, "These two dragon kings are extremely fierce and difficult to be tamed. How can you tame those dragon kings now?"

世尊告曰：「此二龙王极为凶恶，难可降化，卿今云何化彼龙王？」

Maudgalyayana said to the Buddha, "I will first go there and transform my form to be extremely large, fearing that it will frighten the dragons. Then I will transform my form to be extremely small, and then subdue them with the usual method."

目连白佛言：「我先至彼，化形极大，恐怯彼龙，后复化形极为微小，然后以常法则而降伏之。」

The World-Honored One said, "Excellent! Maudgalyayana! You are able to endure and subdue the evil dragons. However, Maudgalyayana! Uphold your mind and do not give rise to chaotic thoughts. The reason is that those dragons are fierce and will disturb you."

世尊告曰：「善哉！目连！汝能堪任降伏恶龙。然今，目连！坚持心意，勿兴乱想。所以然者，彼龙凶恶备触娆汝。」

At that time, Maudgalyayana immediately bowed at the Buddha's feet. In the time it takes to bend and stretch an arm, he disappeared from there and went to the top of Mount Sumeru. At that time, the dragon kings Nanda and Upananda circled Mount Sumeru seven times, greatly enraged, releasing great smoke and fire.

是时，目连即礼佛足，屈申臂顷，于彼没不现，往至须弥山上。尔时，难陀、优槃难陀龙王遶须弥山七匝，极兴瞋恚，放大烟火。

At that time, Maudgalyayana himself concealed his original form and transformed into a great dragon king with fourteen heads, circling Mount Sumeru fourteen times, releasing great smoke and fire, and staying above the two dragon kings.

是时，目连自隐本形，化作大龙王，有十四头，遶须弥山十四匝，放大火烟，当在二龙王上住。

At that time, the dragon kings Nanda and Upananda, seeing the great dragon king with fourteen heads, became terrified and said to each other, "We should test the power of this dragon king today to see if he is truly superior to us.

是时，难陀、优槃难陀龙王见大龙王有十四头，便怀恐怖，自相谓言：「我等今日当试此龙王威力，为审胜吾不乎？」

At that time, the dragon kings Nanda and Upananda used their tails to strike the great ocean, sprinkling water on the Heaven of the Thirty-three Gods, but it did not reach Venerable Maudgalyayana's body. At that time, Venerable Maudgalyayana also used his tail to strike the water in the great ocean, the water reaching up to the Heaven of the Pure Abode, and also sprinkling the bodies of the two dragon kings.

尔时，难陀、优槃难陀龙王以尾掷大海中，以水洒三十三天，亦不著目连身。是时，尊者大目连复以尾著大海水中，水乃至到梵迦夷天，并复洒二龙王身上。

At that time, the two dragon kings said to each other, "We have used all our strength to sprinkle water on the Heaven of the Thirty-three Gods, but this great dragon king surpasses us. We have seven heads, but this dragon king has fourteen heads. We encircle Mount Sumeru seven times, but this dragon king encircles Mount Sumeru fourteen times. We, the two dragon kings, should now join forces to fight with him."

是时，二龙王自相谓言：「我等尽其力势，以水洒三十三天；然此大龙王复过我上去，我等正有七头，今此龙王十四头；我等遶须弥山七匝，今此龙王遶须弥山十四匝；我今二龙王当共并力与共战鬪。」

At that time, the two dragon kings were extremely angry, thundering, lightning, and releasing great flames. At that time, Venerable Maudgalyayana thought, "When dragons fight, they use fire and lightning. If I fight with fire and lightning, the people in Jambudvipa and the Heaven of the Thirty-three Gods will all be harmed. I should now transform my form to be extremely small and fight with them." At that time, Maudgalyayana immediately transformed his form to be small, entered the dragon's mouth, and came out through its nose; or entered through the nose and came out through the ears; or entered the ears and came out through the eyes; coming out of the eyes, he walked on the eyebrows.

是时，二龙王极怀瞋恚，雷电霹雳放大火炎。是时，尊者大目连便作是念：「凡龙战鬪以火霹雳，设我以火霹雳共战鬪者，阎浮里内人民之类，及三十三天皆当被害。我今化形极小，当与战鬪。」是时，目连即化形使小，便入龙口中，从鼻中出；或从鼻入，从耳中出；或入耳中，从眼中出；以出眼中，在眉上行。

At that time, the two dragon kings were extremely frightened and thought, "This great dragon king has great power, able to enter through the mouth and come out through the nose; enter through the nose and come out through the eyes. We are indeed inferior today. Our dragon species have four kinds of birth: egg-born, womb-born, moisture-born, and transformation-born. There is none who can surpass us. Now this dragon king has such great power that we cannot fight him. Our lives will be lost in an instant." They were all frightened and their hair stood on end.

尔时，二龙王极怀恐惧，即作是念：「此大龙王极有威力，乃能从口中入，鼻中出；从鼻入，眼中出。我等今日实为不如。我等龙种今有四生，卵生、胎生、湿生、化生，然无有出我等者，今此龙王威力乃尔，不堪共鬪，我等性命死在斯须。」皆怀恐惧，衣毛皆竖。

At that time, Maudgalyayana saw that the dragon kings were frightened and returned to his normal form, walking on the eyelashes. At that time, the two dragon kings saw Maudgalyayana and said to each other, "This is the monk Maudgalyayana! He is not a dragon king. It is amazing! It is extraordinary! He has great power, able to fight with us." At that time, the two dragon kings said to Maudgalyayana, "Venerable One, why did you disturb us so much? What instructions do you have for us?"

是时，目连以见龙王心怀恐惧，还隐其形，作常形容，在眼睫上行。是时，二龙王见大目连，自相谓言：「此是目连沙门！亦非龙王。甚奇！甚特！有大威力，乃能与我等共鬪。」是时，二龙王白目连言：「尊者何为触娆我乃尔，欲何所诫勅？」

Maudgalyayana replied, "You all had this thought yesterday: 'How can a bald-headed monk always fly above us? We should control him now.'"

目连报曰：「汝等昨日而作是念：『云何秃头沙门恒在我上飞，今当制御之。』」

The dragon kings replied, "Yes, Maudgalyayana!"

龙王报曰：「如是，目连！」

Maudgalyayana said, "Dragon kings, you should know that this Mount Sumeru is the road for the gods, not the place where you live."

目连告曰：「龙王当知，此须弥山者是诸天道路，非汝所居之处。」

The dragon kings replied, "Please forgive us and do not blame us again. From now on, we will not dare to disturb you again and give rise to evil and confused thoughts. Please allow us to be your disciples."

龙王报曰：「唯愿恕之，不见重责，自今以后更不敢触娆，兴恶乱想，唯愿听为弟子。」

Maudgalyayana said, "Do not take refuge in me. Take refuge in what I take refuge in."

目连报曰：「汝等莫自归我身，我所自归者，汝等便自归之。」

The dragon kings said to Maudgalyayana, "We take refuge in the Tathāgata today."

龙王白目连：「我等今日自归如来。」

Maudgalyayana said, "You cannot take refuge in the World Honored One by relying on this Mount Sumeru. Now you should come with me to the city of Sravasti to take refuge."

目连告曰：「汝等不可依此须弥山，自归世尊；今可共我至舍卫城，乃得自归。」

At that time, Maudgalyayana took the two dragon kings and in the time it takes to bend and stretch an arm, they went from Mount Sumeru to the city of Sravasti. At that time, the World Honored One was expounding the Dharma to an innumerable assembly. Maudgalyayana said to the two dragon kings, "You should know that today the World Honored One is expounding the Dharma to an innumerable assembly. You cannot go to where the World Honored One is in your own forms."

是时，目连将二龙王，如屈申臂顷，从须弥山上至舍卫城。尔时，世尊与无央数之众而为说法。是时，目连告二龙王曰：「汝等当知，今日世尊与无央数之众而为说法，不可作汝形至世尊所。」

The dragon kings replied, "Yes, Maudgalyayana!"

龙王报曰：「如是，目连！」

At that time, the dragon kings returned to their human forms, transforming into human bodies that were neither too tall nor too short, with dignified and upright features, the color of peach blossoms.

是时，龙王还隐龙形，化作人形，不长不短，容貌端正，如桃华色。

At that time, Maudgalyayana went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. At that time, Maudgalyayana said to the dragon kings, "Now is the right time, you may proceed forward."

是时，目连至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。是时，目连语龙王曰：「今正是时，宜可前进。」

At that time, the dragon kings heard Maudgalyayana's words, immediately rose from their seats, knelt on one knee with palms joined, and addressed the World-Honored One, saying, "We two clansmen, named Nanda and Upananda, take refuge in the Tathāgata and uphold the five precepts. We only wish that the World-Honored One would allow us to become upasakas, and for the rest of our lives, we will no longer take life." At that time, the World-Honored One snapped his fingers in assent. The two dragon kings then returned to their seats, wishing to hear the Dharma.

是时，龙王闻目连语，即从坐起，长跪叉手，白世尊言：「我等二族姓子，一名难陀，二名优槃难陀，自归如来，受持五戒，唯愿世尊听为优婆塞，尽形寿不复杀生。」尔时，世尊弹指可之。时，二龙王还复故坐，欲得闻法。

At that time, King Prasenajit thought, "What is the cause and condition for the smoke and fire in this Jambudvipa?" At that time, King Prasenajit rode in a chariot with jeweled feathers and left the city of Sravasti to go to where the World-Honored One was. At that time, the people saw the king coming from afar and all came forward to welcome him, saying, "Welcome, great king! Please take a seat here."

尔时，波斯匿王便作是念：「有何因缘，使此阎浮利内烟火乃尔？」是时，王波斯匿乘宝羽之车出舍卫城，至世尊所。尔时，人民之类遥见王来，咸共起迎：「善来，大王！可就此坐。」

At that time, the two dragon kings remained silent and did not rise. At that time, King Prasenajit bowed at the feet of the World-Honored One and sat to one side. At that time, the great king addressed the World-Honored One, saying, "I now wish to ask something. I only wish that the World-Honored One will explain it in detail."

时，二龙王默然不起。是时，波斯匿王礼世尊足，在一面坐。是时，大王白世尊言：「我今欲有所问，唯愿世尊事事敷演。」

The World-Honored One said, "If you wish to ask something, now is the right time."

世尊告曰：「欲有所问，今正是时。」

King Prasenajit addressed the Buddha, saying, "What are the causes and conditions that cause the smoke and fire in this Jambudvīpa to be like this?"

波斯匿王白佛言：「有何因缘，令此阎浮里内烟火乃尔？」

The World-Honored One said, "It was created by the dragon kings Nanda and Upananda. However, Your Majesty, do not be afraid. There will be no more changes of smoke and fire today."

世尊告曰：「难陀、优槃难陀龙王之所造。然今，大王！勿怀恐惧，今日更无烟火之变。」

At that time, King Prasenajit thought, "I am now the great king of this country! The people respect me and my fame is known far and wide. Where did these two come from? Seeing me here, they still do not come to greet me. If they stay within my territory, I will capture and imprison them; if they come from another realm, I will capture and kill them."

是时，波斯匿王便作是念：「我今是国之大王！人民宗敬，名闻四远。今此二人为从何来？见吾至此，亦不起迎。设住吾境界者当取闭之；设他界来者当取杀之。」

At that time, the dragon king knew what King Prasenajit was thinking in his mind and became angry. The dragon king then thought, "We have done nothing wrong to this king, yet he wants to harm us in return. I must take this king and the people of the country of Kasi and kill them all." At that time, the dragon king immediately rose from his seat, paid homage at the feet of the World-Honored One, and left. Not far from Jetavana, he disappeared.

是时，龙王知波斯匿心中所念，便兴瞋恚。尔时，龙王便作是念：「我等无过于此王所，更欲反害吾身；要当取此国王及迦夷国人，尽取杀之。」是时，龙王即从坐起，礼世尊足即便而去。离祇洹不远，便不复现。

At that time, King Prasenajit saw the man leave and, before long, said to the World-Honored One, "There are many affairs of state. I wish to return to the palace."

是时，波斯匿王见此人去，未久，白世尊言：「国事猥多，欲还宫中。」

The World-Honored One said, "It is time for you to know."

世尊告曰：「宜知是时。」

At that time, King Prasenajit immediately rose from his seat and left. He told his ministers, "Which way did those two men go? Quickly capture them!" At that time, the ministers heard the king's order and ran to search for them but did not know where they were. They then returned to the palace.

是时，波斯匿王即从坐起，便退而去。告群臣曰：「向者二人为从何道去？速捕取之。」是时，诸臣闻王教令，即驰走求之而不知处，便还宫中。

At that time, the dragon kings Nanda and Upananda each had this thought: "We have no faults with that king, yet he wants to harm us. We should destroy the people, leaving none remaining." The dragon kings then had this thought: "What faults do the people in the country have? We should destroy the people of Śrāvastī." They further thought: "What faults do the people of Śrāvastī have with us? We should take the officials in the king's palace and kill them all."

是时，难陀、优槃难陀龙王各生此念：「我等无过于彼王所，方欲取我等害之。我等当共害彼人民，使无遗余。」是时，龙王复作是念：「国中人民有何过失？当取舍卫城人民害之。」复重作是念：「舍卫国人有何过失于我等？当取王宫官属尽取杀之。」

At that time, the World Honored One, knowing the thoughts in the dragon king's mind, told Maudgalyāyana, "You should now save King Prasenajit, so that he is not harmed by the dragon kings Nanda and Upananda."

尔时，世尊以知龙王心中所念，告目连曰：「汝今当救波斯匿王，无令为难陀、优槃难陀龙王所害。」

Maudgalyāyana replied, "Yes, World Honored One!" At that time, Maudgalyāyana accepted the Buddha's instructions, bowed at the World Honored One's feet, and then left. He sat cross-legged above the king's palace, making his body invisible. At that time, the two dragon kings roared with thunder and lightning, with violent winds and heavy rain, above the king's palace, either raining tiles and stones or raining swords and knives. Before they fell to the ground, they transformed into blue lotus flowers in the sky. At that time, the dragon kings became even more angry and rained down large mountains on the palace. At that time, Maudgalyāyana further transformed them into various kinds of food and drink. At that time, the dragon kings became even more furiously angry and rained down swords and knives. At that time, Maudgalyāyana further transformed them into extremely fine garments. At that time, the dragon kings became even more furiously angry and rained down large sand and gravel stones on King Prasenajit's palace. Before they fell to the ground, they transformed into the seven treasures.

目连对曰：「如是。世尊！」是时，目连受佛教诫，礼世尊足，便退而去；在王宫上，结加趺坐，令身不现。是时，二龙王雷吼霹雳，暴风疾雨，在王宫上，或雨瓦石，或雨刀剑，未堕地之顷，便为优钵莲华在虚空中。是时，龙王倍复瞋恚，雨大高山于宫殿上。是时，目连复化使作种种饮食。是时，龙王倍复瞋恚炽盛，雨诸刀剑。是时，目连复化使作极好衣裳。是时，龙王倍复瞋恚，复雨大沙砾石，在波斯匿宫上，未堕地之顷，便化作七宝。

At that time, King Prasenajit saw various kinds of seven treasures raining down in the palace. He was overjoyed and could not contain himself. He thought, "In Jambudvīpa, there is no one more virtuous than me, except for the Tathāgata. The reason is that in my house, a single root of rice produced one bushel of rice, which was cooked with sugarcane juice and was extremely fragrant and delicious. Now, treasures are raining down on the palace. Can I become a wheel-turning sage king?" At that time, King Prasenajit, together with his concubines, collected the seven treasures.

是时，波斯匿王见宫殿中雨种种七宝，欢喜踊跃，不能自胜，便作是念：「阎浮里内有德之人，无复过我，唯除如来。所以然者，我家中种粳米一根上生，收拾得一斛米，饭以甘蔗之浆，极为香美，今复于宫殿上雨七宝，我便能作转轮圣王乎！」是时，波斯匿王领诸婇女收摄七宝。

At that time, the two dragon kings said to each other, "What is the meaning of this now? When we came, we wanted to harm King Prasenajit. Today, the transformations have reached this point. All our power and strength are now fully displayed. Yet we still cannot move King Prasenajit even a little bit."

是时，二龙王自相谓言：「今将有何意？我等来时欲害波斯匿王，今日变化乃至于斯。所有力势今日尽现。犹不能动波斯匿王毫牦之分。」

At that time, the dragon king saw Mahāmaudgalyāyana sitting cross-legged in the palace, with his body upright and focused, not leaning to one side. Seeing this, he thought, "This must be the doing of Mahāmaudgalyāyana." At that time, the two dragon kings, having seen Maudgalyāyana, withdrew and left. At that time, Maudgalyāyana, seeing the dragons leave, returned to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side.

是时，龙王见大目揵连在宫殿上结加趺坐，正身正意，形不倾斜。见已，便作是念：「此必是大目连之所为也。」是时，二龙王以见目连便退而去。是时，目连见龙去，还舍神足至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。

At that time, King Prasenajit thought, "This variety of food and drink should not be eaten first. I should first offer it to the Tathāgata, and then eat it myself." At that time, King Prasenajit loaded precious treasures and various food and drink into a cart and went to where the World-Honored One was, saying, "Yesterday, heavenly rain of the seven treasures and this food and drink fell. I only wish you would accept it."

时，波斯匿王便作是念：「今此种种饮食不应先食，当先奉上如来，然后自食。」是时，波斯匿王即车载珍宝，及种种饮食，往至世尊所：「昨日天雨七宝及此饮食，唯愿纳受。」

At that time, Mahāmaudgalyāyana was not far from the Tathāgata. The Buddha told the king, "You should now give the seven treasures and food and drink to Mahāmaudgalyāyana. The reason is that because of Mahāmaudgalyāyana's kindness, you have been reborn in the land of sages and worthies."

尔时，大目揵连去如来不远，佛告王曰：「汝今可持七宝饮食之具，与大目连。所以然者，蒙目连恩，得更生圣贤之地。」

King Prasenajit said to the Buddha, "What are the causes and conditions for saying that I have been reborn?"

波斯匿王白佛言：「有何因缘，言我更生？」

The World-Honored One said, "Did you not come to me this morning to listen to the Dharma? At that time, there were two other people who also came to listen to the Dharma. The king had this thought: 'In this country, I am the most noble and respected by the people. But where did these two people come from? They do not stand up to greet me when they see me.'"

世尊告曰：「汝朝不至我所，欲得听法乎？尔时，有二人亦来听法。王生此念：『我于此国界，最为豪尊，众人所敬，然此二人为从何来？见我不起承迎。』」

At that time, the king said to the Buddha, "It is true, World-Honored One!"

时王白佛：「实然，世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "This is also not a human being, but the dragon kings Nanda and Upananda. They knew the king's intention and said to each other, 'We have no fault with this king. Why would he come to harm us? We must find a way to destroy this country.' We immediately knew what the dragon kings were thinking in their hearts, and I ordered Maudgalyāyana, 'Now save King Prasenajit, so that he is not harmed by the dragons.' He then accepted my instruction, and in the palace, he concealed his form and manifested this transformation. At that time, the dragon kings were extremely angry and rained sand, gravel, and stones on the palace. Before they fell to the ground, they were transformed into the seven treasures, clothing, and provisions for food and drink. For this reason, great king! Today you have been reborn again."

世尊告曰：「此亦非人，乃是难陀、优槃难陀龙王。彼知王意，自相谓言：『我等无过于此人王，何故反来害我？要当方宜灭此国界。』我等寻知龙王心中所念，即勅目连：『今可救波斯匿王，无令为龙所害也。』即受我教，在宫殿上，隐形不现，作此变化。是时，龙王极怀瞋恚，雨沙砾石于宫殿上，未堕地之顷，化作七宝、衣裳、饮食之具。由此因缘，大王！今日便为更生。」

At that time, King Prasenajit was filled with fear, his hair standing on end. He knelt on his knees and walked forward to the Tathāgata, and said to the Buddha, "I only wish that the World-Honored One would extend his great kindness and save my life." He also bowed at Maudgalyāyana's feet, paying homage with his head and face, saying, "I am saved by your kindness and my life is saved."

是时，波斯匿王便怀恐怖，衣毛皆竖，前跪膝行至如来前，而白佛言：「唯愿世尊恩垂过厚，得济生命。」复礼目连足，头面礼敬：「蒙尊之恩，得济生命。」

At that time, the king spoke this verse:

尔时，国王便说此偈：

May the Venerable One live forever, protecting my life for a long time
liberating me from suffering and distress, and allowing me to escape from this difficulty.

「唯尊寿无穷，　　长夜护其命，度脱苦穷厄，　　蒙尊得脱难。」

At that time, King Prasenajit scattered heavenly fragrant flowers on the Tathāgata's body and said, "I now offer these seven treasures to the Three Jewels. I only wish that you will accept them." He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated the Buddha three times, and then departed.

是时，波斯匿王以天香华散如来身，便作是说：「我今持此七宝奉上三尊，唯愿纳受。」头面礼足，遶佛三匝，便退而去。

At that time, the World-Honored One thought, "This assembly of the four groups is mostly lazy and does not listen to the Dharma. They also do not seek means to realize it with their bodies, nor do they seek to attain what they have not yet attained. I should now make the assembly of the four groups yearn for the Dharma." At that time, the World-Honored One did not inform the assembly of the four groups, nor did he take any attendants. In the time it takes to bend and stretch an arm, he disappeared from Jetavana and went to the Heaven of the Thirty-three Gods.

是时，世尊便作是念：「此四部之众多有懈怠，替不听法；亦不求方便，使身作证，亦不复求未获者获，未得者得；我今宜可使四部之众渴仰于法。」尔时，世尊不告四部之众，复不将侍者，如屈申臂顷，从祇桓不现，往至三十三天。

At that time, Śakra Devānām-Indra saw from afar that the World-Honored One was coming. He led the heavenly assembly to welcome the World-Honored One, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, invited him to take a seat, and said, "Welcome, World-Honored One! It has been a long time since we last saw you."

尔时，释提桓因遥见世尊来，将诸天众，前迎世尊，头面礼足，请令就坐，并作是说：「善来，世尊！久违觐省。」

At that time, the World-Honored One thought, "I should now use the power of my spiritual feet to hide my physical form so that people cannot see where I am." At that time, the World-Honored One further thought, "I should now transform my body in the Heaven of the Thirty-three Gods to be extremely vast."

是时，世尊便作是念：「我今当以神足之力自隐形体，使众人不见我为所在。」尔时，世尊复作是念：「我今于三十三天，化身极使广大。」

At that time, in the Good Dharma Lecture Hall in the heavens, there was a golden stone one yojana long and wide. At that time, the World-Honored One sat cross-legged on the stone, filling the entire stone. At that time, the Tathāgata's mother Māyā, together with the heavenly maidens, came to where the World-Honored One was, bowed her head at his feet, and sat to one side. She said, "It has been a long time since we last saw each other. Now that I have come here, I am truly fortunate. I have been longing to see you. The Buddha has finally come today." At that time, Māyā bowed her head at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side. Śakra Devānām-Indra also bowed at the Tathāgata's feet and sat to one side. The gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three bowed at the Tathāgata's feet and sat to one side. At that time, the assembly of gods saw the Tathāgata increasing the heavenly assembly and diminishing the asuras.

尔时，天上善法讲堂有金石纵广一由旬。尔时，世尊石上结加趺坐，遍满石上。尔时，如来母摩耶将诸天女至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。并作是说：「违奉甚久，今来至此，实蒙大幸，渴仰思见，佛今日方来。」是时，母摩耶头面礼足已，在一面坐；释提桓因亦礼如来足，在一面坐。三十三天礼如来足，在一面坐。是时，诸天之众见如来在彼增益天众，减损阿须伦。

At that time, the World-Honored One gradually spoke to the assembly of gods about the wonderful teachings, namely, the teachings on giving, precepts, and rebirth in the heavens, the impure thoughts about desire, the filth and evil of sexual desire, and the joy of liberation. At that time, the World-Honored One saw that the minds and thoughts of the great assembly that had come and the gods were open and enlightened. The teachings that the Buddhas of the past always spoke about - suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path - he spoke them to the gods. While sitting, all their defilements were exhausted and they attained the purity of the Dharma eye. There were also eighteen koṭis of heavenly maidens who saw the path, and thirty-six thousand heavenly beings attained the purity of the Dharma eye. At that time, the Tathāgata's mother rose from her seat, bowed at the Tathāgata's feet, and returned to the palace.

尔时，世尊渐与彼诸天之众说于妙论，所谓论者：施论、戒论、生天之论，欲不净想，婬为秽恶，出要为乐。尔时，世尊以见诸来大众及诸天人心开意解，诸佛世尊常所说法：苦、习、尽、道，普与诸天说之。各于坐上，诸尘垢尽，得法眼净。复有十八亿天女之众而见道迹，三万六千天众得法眼净。是时，如来母即从坐起，礼如来足，还入宫中。

At that time, Śakra Devānām-Indra said to the Buddha, "What kind of food should I offer to the Tathāgata? Should it be human food or naturally-occurring heavenly food?"

尔时，释提桓因白佛言：「我今当以何食饭如来乎？为用人间之食，为用自然天食？」

The World-Honored One said, "Use human food to feed the Tathāgata. The reason is that I was born in the human realm, grew up in the human realm, and attained Buddhahood in the human realm."

世尊告曰：「可用人间之食用食如来。所以然者，我身生于人间，长于人间，于人间得佛。」

Śakra Devānām-Indra said to the Buddha, "Yes, World-Honored One!" At that time, Śakra Devānām-Indra further said to the Buddha, "Should it be the time of the heavens or the time of the human realm?"

释提桓因白佛言：「如是，世尊！」是时，释提桓因复白佛言：「为用天上时节？为用人间时节？」

The World-Honored One said, "Use the time of the human realm."

世尊告曰：「用人间时节。」

He replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

对曰：「如是。世尊！」

At that time, Śakra, Lord of the Devas, immediately used the food of the human realm and the time of the human realm to offer food to the Tathāgata.

是时，释提桓因即以人间之食，复以人间时节饭食如来。

At that time, the thirty-three devas each said to each other, "We now see the Tathāgata eating all day long."

尔时，三十三天各各自相谓言：「我等今见如来竟日饭食。」

At that time, the World-Honored One had this thought: "I should now enter such a samādhi that I can make the devas advance when they want to advance, and make them retreat when they want to retreat." At that time, the World-Honored One entered this samādhi, advancing and retreating the devas according to the appropriate time.

是时，世尊便作是念：「我今当入如是三昧，欲使诸天进便进，欲使诸天退便退。」是时，世尊以入此三昧，进却诸天，随其时宜。

At that time, the four groups of people in the human realm had not seen the Tathāgata for a long time. They went to Ānanda and said, "Where is the Tathāgata now? We are thirsty to see him."

是时，人间四部之众不见如来久，往至阿难所，白阿难言：「如来今为所在？渴仰欲见。」

Ānanda replied, "We do not know where the Tathāgata is either."

阿难报曰：「我等亦复不知如来所在。」

At that time, King Prasenajit and King Udayana went to Ānanda and asked, "Where is the Tathāgata today?"

是时，波斯匿王、优填王至阿难所，问阿难曰：「如来今日竟为所在？」

Ānanda replied, "Great King! I do not know where the Tathāgata is either."

阿难报曰：「大王！我亦不知如来所在。」

At that time, the two kings, thinking of seeing the Tathāgata, became afflicted with illness. Then the ministers went to King Udayana and said, "What is your illness now?"

是时，二王思覩如来，遂得苦患。尔时，群臣至优填王所，白优填王曰：「今为所患？」

The king replied, "I am now afflicted with sorrow."

时王报曰：「我今以愁忧成患。」

The ministers said to the king, "How can sorrow become an affliction?"

群臣白王：「云何以愁忧成患？」

The king replied, "It is because I do not see the Tathāgata. If I do not see the Tathāgata, I will die."

其王报曰：「由不见如来故也。设我不见如来者，便当命终。」

At that time, the ministers thought, "What expedient means can we use to prevent King Udayana from dying? We should make an image of the Tathāgata." At that time, the ministers said to the king, "We wish to make an image, and also pay homage and make offerings to it."

是时，群臣便作是念：「当以何方便，使优填王不令命终？我等宜作如来形像。」是时，群臣白王言：「我等欲作形像，亦可恭敬承事作礼。」

When the king heard these words, he was overjoyed and could not contain himself. He told the ministers, "Excellent! Your words are most wonderful."

时，王闻此语已，欢喜踊跃，不能自胜，告群臣曰：「善哉！卿等所说至妙。」

The ministers said to the king, "With what kind of treasure should we make an image of the Tathāgata?"

群臣白王：「当以何宝作如来形像？」

The king then ordered the skilled craftsmen within the kingdom, saying, "I now wish to make an image."

是时，王即勅国界之内诸奇巧师匠，而告之曰：「我今欲作形像。」

The craftsmen replied, "Yes, Your Majesty!"

巧匠对曰：「如是。大王！」

At that time, King Udayana used sandalwood from the head of an ox to make an image of the Tathāgata, five feet tall.

是时，优填王即以牛头栴檀作如来形像高五尺。

At that time, King Prasenajit heard that King Udayana had made a five-foot-tall image of the Tathāgata and worshipped it. King Prasenajit then summoned the craftsmen in his country and said to them, "I now wish to make an image of the Tathāgata. You should prepare it." At that time, King Prasenajit had this thought: "What kind of treasure should I use to make an image of the Tathāgata?" He then thought again: "The Tathāgata's body is yellow like heavenly gold. I should now use gold to make an image of the Tathāgata." At that time, King Prasenajit used pure purple gold to make a five-foot-tall image of the Tathāgata. At that time, there were two such images of the Tathāgata in Jambudvīpa.

是时，波斯匿王闻优填王作如来形像高五尺而供养。是时，波斯匿王复召国中巧匠，而告之曰：「我今欲造如来形像，汝等当时办之。」时，波斯匿王而生此念：「当用何宝，作如来形像耶？」斯须复作是念：「如来形体，黄如天金，今当以金作如来形像。」是时，波斯匿王纯以紫磨金作如来像高五尺。尔时，阎浮里内始有此二如来形像。

At that time, the four groups of disciples went to Ānanda and said to him, "We are longing for the Tathāgata. We wish to see the Venerable One. Where is the Tathāgata now?"

是时，四部之众往至阿难所，白阿难曰：「我等渴仰于如来，所思欲觐尊，如来今日竟为所在？」

Ānanda replied, "We do not know where the Tathāgata is either. But now let us go to Aniruddha and ask about this matter. The reason is that Venerable Aniruddha has the foremost divine eye, pure and flawless. With his divine eye, he can see a thousand worlds, two thousand worlds, and three thousand great thousand worlds. He can know and see them."

阿难报曰：「我等亦复不知如来所在。但今共至阿那律所而问此义。所以然者，尊者阿那律天眼第一，清净无瑕秽，彼以天眼见千世界、二千世界、三千大千世界，彼能知见。」

At that time, the four groups of disciples went with Ānanda to Aniruddha and said to Aniruddha, "Now the four groups of disciples have come to me and asked me, 'Where is the Tathāgata today?' I only wish that the Venerable One would use his divine eye to observe where the Tathāgata is now."

是时，四部之众共阿难往至阿那律所，白阿那律曰：「今此四部之众来至我所，而问我曰：『今日如来竟为所在？』唯愿尊者以天眼观如来今为所在！」

At that time, the Venerable Aniruddha replied, "You all wait! I now wish to observe where the Tathāgata is."

是时，尊者阿那律报曰：「汝等且止！吾今欲观如来竟为所在。」

At that time, Aniruddha straightened his body and mind, focused his attention in front, and used his divine eye to observe within Jambudvīpa but did not see him. He further used his divine eye to observe Kuru, Godānīya, and Uttarakuru but did not see him. He further observed the Four Heavenly Kings, the Thirty-three Heavens, the Yāma Heaven, the Tuṣita Heaven, the Nirmāṇarati Heaven, the Paranirmitavaśavartin Heaven, up to the Brahmā Heaven but did not see him. He further observed the thousand Jambudvīpas, the thousand Kuru, the thousand Uttarakuru, the thousand Godānīya, the thousand Four Heavenly Kings, the thousand Yāma Heavens, the thousand Tuṣita Heavens, the thousand Nirmāṇarati Heavens, the thousand Paranirmitavaśavartin Heavens, the thousand Brahmā Heavens, but still did not see the Tathāgata. He further observed the trichiliocosm but still did not see him. He then rose from his seat and said to Ānanda, "I have now observed the trichiliocosm but still do not see him."

是时，阿那律正身正意，系念在前，以天眼观阎浮里内而不见之，复以天眼观拘耶尼、弗于逮、欝单曰而不见之，复观四天王、三十三天、艳天、兜术天、化自在天、他化自在天，乃至观梵天而不见之。复观千阎浮地、千瞿耶尼、千欝单曰、千弗于逮、千四天王、千艳天、千兜术天、千化自在天、千他化自在天、千梵天，而不见如来。复观三千大千刹土而复不见。即从坐起语阿难曰：「我今已观三千大千刹土而不见之。」

At that time, Ānanda and the fourfold assembly were silent. Ānanda thought, "Will the Tathāgata not enter parinirvāṇa?"

是时，阿难及四部之众默然而止。阿难作是念：「如来将不般涅槃乎？」

At that time, the thirty-three heavens each said to each other, "We have obtained great benefits. We only wish that the seven buddhas would always appear in the world, benefiting the gods and the people." Some gods said, "Forget about the seven buddhas. It would be very good if there were only six buddhas." Some gods said, "Only five buddhas." Some said, "Four buddhas." Some said, "Three buddhas." Some said, "If two buddhas appear in the world, it will benefit many."

是时，三十三天各各自相谓言：「我等快得善利，唯愿七佛常现于世，天及世人多所润益。」或有天而作是语：「且置七佛，但使有六佛者，此亦甚善。」或有天子言：「但使有五佛。」或言：「四佛。」或言：「三佛。」或言：「二佛出现世者，多所润益。」

At that time, Śakra, lord of the devas, told the gods, "Forget about the seven buddhas, up to two buddhas. If only the Śākyamuni Buddha of today could remain in the world for a long time, it would benefit many."

时，释提桓因告诸天曰：「且置七佛，乃至二佛，但使今日释迦文佛久住世者，则多所饶益。」

At that time, the Tathāgata wished for the gods to come, and the gods came. He wished for the gods to leave, and the gods left. At that time, the thirty-three heavens each said to each other, "Why does the Tathāgata eat all day long?"

尔时，如来意欲使诸天来，诸天便来，意欲使诸天去，诸天便去。是时，三十三天各各自相谓言：「如来何故竟日而食？」

At that time, Śakra, lord of the devas, told the thirty-three heavens, "The Tathāgata eats today according to the time of the human realm, not according to the time of the heavenly realm." At that time, the World-Honored One, after three months, had this thought: "The four groups of the people of Jambudvīpa have not seen me for a long time and have a strong longing. I should now abandon my supernatural powers and let the śrāvakas know that the Tathāgata is in the Heaven of the Thirty-three." At that time, the World-Honored One immediately abandoned his supernatural powers.

是时，释提桓因告三十三天曰：「如来今日食，以人间时节，不用天上时节。」是时，世尊以经三月，便作是念：「阎浮里人四部之众不见吾久，甚有虚渴之想。我今当舍神足，使诸声闻知如来在三十三天。」是时，世尊即舍神足。

At that time, Ānanda went to Aniruddha and said to him, "Now the four groups of the assembly are very longing and wish to see the Tathāgata. But will the Tathāgata not attain parinirvāṇa?"

时，阿难往阿那律所，白阿那律言：「今四部之众甚有虚渴，欲见如来。然今如来不取灭度乎？」

At that time, Aniruddha said to Ānanda, "Last night a god came to me and said, 'The Tathāgata is in the Good Dharma Hall of the Heaven of the Thirty-three.' You should wait! I now wish to see where the Tathāgata is." At that time, the Venerable Aniruddha immediately sat cross-legged, with body and mind upright, his mind unmoving, and with his divine eye he looked at the Heaven of the Thirty-three and saw the World-Honored One sitting on a rock the size of a wall in the Heaven of the Thirty-three. At that time, Aniruddha immediately arose from samādhi and said to Ānanda, "The Tathāgata is now in the Heaven of the Thirty-three teaching the Dharma to his mother."

是时，阿那律语阿难曰：「昨夜有天来至我所，云：『如来在三十三天善法讲堂。』汝今且止！吾今欲观如来所在。」是时，尊者阿那律即结加趺坐，正身正意，心不移动，以天眼观三十三天，见世尊在壁方一由旬石上坐。是时，阿那律即从三昧起，语阿难曰：「如来今在三十三天与母说法。」

At that time, Ānanda and the four groups of the assembly were overjoyed and could not contain themselves. At that time, Ānanda asked the four groups of the assembly, "Who is able to go to the Heaven of the Thirty-three to inquire about the Tathāgata?"

是时，阿难及四部之众欢喜踊跃，不能自胜。是时，阿难问四部众曰：「谁能堪任至三十三天问讯如来？」

Aniruddha said, "Now the Venerable Maudgalyāyana is foremost in supernatural powers. I wish he would lower his divine power and go to inquire about the Buddha."

阿那律曰：「今尊者目连神足第一，愿屈神力往问讯佛。」

At that time, the four groups of the assembly said to Maudgalyāyana, "Today the Tathāgata is in the Heaven of the Thirty-three. We only wish that the Venerable one would take the names of the four groups and inquire about the Tathāgata! And also convey this meaning to the Tathāgata: 'The World-Honored One attained the Way in the world of Jambudvīpa. We only wish that you would lower your majestic power and return to the world!'"

是时，四部之众白目连曰：「今日如来在三十三天，唯愿尊者持四部姓名，问讯如来！又持此义往白如来：『世尊在阎浮里内世间得道，唯屈威神还至世间！』」

Maudgalyāyana replied, "Very good, virtuous ones!"

目连报曰：「甚善！诸贤！」

At that time, Maudgalyāyana accepted the instructions of the four groups, and in the time it takes to bend and stretch one's arm, he went to the Heaven of the Thirty-three and arrived where the Tathāgata was. At that time, Śakra Devānām-Indra and the gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three saw Maudgalyāyana coming from afar. The gods each had this thought: "Is he a messenger of the Sangha? Or is he a messenger of the kings?" At that time, the gods all arose to welcome him, saying, "Welcome, Venerable One!"

是时，目连受四部之教，屈申臂顷，往至三十三天，到如来所。是时，释提桓因及三十三天遥见目连来，诸天各生此念：「正是僧使？若当是诸王之使？」是时，诸天皆起往迎：「善来，尊者！」

At that time, Maudgalyāyana saw from afar the World-Honored One expounding the Dharma to an innumerable assembly. Having seen it, he had this thought: "The World-Honored One is here in this heaven, and it is also noisy." Maudgalyāyana went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and stood to one side.

是时，目连遥见世尊与无央数之众而为说法。见已，生此念：「世尊在此天中，亦复烦闹。」目连往至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面立。

At that time, Maudgalyāyana addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! The four groups of disciples inquire after the Tathāgata's well-being; are you at ease and your walking strong?" He also reported this matter: "The Tathāgata was born and raised in Jambudvīpa, and attained the Way in the world. I only pray that the World-Honored One will return to the world. The four groups are thirsty and wish to see the World-Honored One!"

尔时，目连白佛言：「世尊！四部之众问讯如来；起居轻利，游步康强。」又白此事：「如来生长阎浮里内，于世间得道，唯愿世尊还来至世间，四部虚渴，欲见世尊！」

The World-Honored One said: "Cause the four groups of disciples to diligently practice without weariness. How is it, Maudgalyāyana! Are the four groups of disciples wandering and teaching without weariness? Are there no disputes? Are the non-Buddhist heretics not causing trouble?"

世尊告曰：「使四部之众进业无惓。云何，目连！四部之众游化劳乎？无鬪讼耶？外道异学无触娆乎？」

Maudgalyāyana replied: "The four groups of disciples practice the Way without weariness."

目连报曰：「四部之众行道无惓。」

"But, Maudgalyāyana! You just thought, 'The Tathāgata is here, it is also noisy.' This is not so. Because when I teach the Dharma, it is not for a long time. If I have this thought, 'I want the devas to come, they come; I want the devas not to come, the devas do not come.' Maudgalyāyana! You return to the world. After seven days, the Tathāgata will go to the side of the great pond in the country of Saṃkāśya."

「但，目连！汝向者作是念言：『如来在此亦烦閙。』此事不然。所以然者，我说法时亦不经久，设我作是念：『欲使诸天来，便来；欲使诸天不来，诸天则不来。』目连！汝还世间，却后七日，如来当往僧迦尸国大池水侧。」

At that time, Maudgalyāyana, in the time it takes to bend and stretch an arm, returned to the Jeta Grove in Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in Śrāvastī. He went to the four groups of disciples and said, "Virtuous ones, you should know that after seven days, the Tathāgata will come down to the great pond in Saṃkāśya on the ground of Jambudvīpa."

是时，目连屈申臂顷，还诣舍卫城祇树给孤独园，往诣四部众，而告之曰：「诸贤当知，却后七日，如来当来下至阎浮里地僧迦尸大池水侧。」

At that time, when the four groups of disciples heard these words, they were overjoyed and could not contain themselves. At that time, King Prasenajit, King Udayana, King Virūḍhaka, King Udayana, and King Bimbisāra heard that the Tathāgata would come to the great pond in Saṃkāśya after seven days. They were extremely joyful and could not contain themselves. At that time, the people of Vaiśālī, the Śākya clan of Kapilavastu, and the people of Kuśinagara heard that the Tathāgata would come to the ground of Jambudvīpa. Having heard this, they were overjoyed and could not contain themselves.

尔时，四部众闻此语已，欢喜踊跃，不能自胜。是时，波斯匿王、优填王、恶生王、优陀延王、频毘娑罗王，闻如来却后七日，当至僧迦尸国大池水侧，极怀欢喜，不能自胜。是时，毘舍离人民之众，迦毘罗越释种，拘夷罗越人民之众，闻如来当来至阎浮里地，闻已，欢喜踊跃，不能自胜。

At that time, King Prasenajit assembled the four divisions of his army and went to the pond to see the World-Honored One. At that time, the five kings all assembled their troops and went to where the World-Honored One was, wishing to pay homage to the Tathāgata and the people. The Śākyas of Kapilavastu all went to where the World-Honored One was, and the four groups of disciples all went to where the World-Honored One was, wishing to see the Tathāgata.

尔时，波斯匿集四种之兵，诣池水侧，欲见世尊。是时，五王皆集兵众往世尊所，欲得觐省如来及人民之众。迦毘罗越释皆悉往世尊所，及四部之众皆悉往世尊所，欲得见如来。

At that time, on the eve of the seventh day, Śakra, Lord of the Devas, said to the god Vaśavartin, "You should now make three roads from the summit of Mount Sumeru to the pond in Saṃkāśya, to see the Tathāgata coming to Jambudvīpa without using his supernatural powers.

尔时，临七日头，释提桓因告自在天子曰：「汝今从须弥山顶至僧迦尸池水作三道路，观如来不用神足至阎浮地。」

The god Vaśavartin replied, "This is excellent. I will do it right now." At that time, the god Vaśavartin immediately created three roads of gold, silver, and crystal. At that time, the golden road was in the center, flanked by the crystal and silver roads, with golden trees created on both sides. At that time, the gods and devas all came to listen to the Dharma for seven days.

自在天子报曰：「此事甚佳，正尔时办。」尔时，自在天子即化作三道金、银、水精。是时，金道当在中央，侠水精道侧、银道侧，化作金树。当于尔时，诸神妙尊天，七日之中皆来听法。

At that time, the World-Honored One, surrounded by tens of millions of people in front and behind, was teaching the Dharma, saying, "The five aggregates are suffering. What are the five? Namely, form, feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness. What is the aggregate of form? Namely, this body composed of the four great elements, and the form created by the four great elements. This is called the aggregate of form.

尔时，世尊与数千万众前后围遶，而为说法，说：「五盛阴苦。云何为五？所谓色、痛、想、行、识。云何为色阴？所谓此四大身，是四大所造色，是谓名为色阴也。

What is called the aggregate of feeling? Namely, painful feeling, pleasant feeling, and neither painful nor pleasant feeling. This is called the aggregate of feeling.

「彼云何名为痛阴？所谓苦痛、乐痛、不苦不乐痛，是谓名为痛阴。

What is called the aggregate of perception? Namely, the coming together of the three times. This is called the aggregate of perception.

「彼云何名想阴？所谓三世共会，是谓名为想阴。

What is called the aggregate of formations? Namely, bodily formations, verbal formations, and mental formations. This is called the aggregate of formations.

「彼云何名为行阴？所谓身行、口行、意行，此名行阴。

What is called the aggregate of consciousness? Namely, the eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind. This is called the aggregate of consciousness.

「彼云何名为识阴？所谓眼、耳、鼻、口、身、意，此名识阴。

What is called form? Namely, cold is also form, heat is also form, hunger is also form, thirst is also form.

「彼云何名为色？所谓色者，寒亦是色，热亦是色，饥亦是色，渴亦是色。

What is called feeling? Namely, feeling is called perception. What is perceived? It is perceived as suffering, perceived as happiness, and perceived as neither suffering nor happiness. Therefore, it is called perception.

「云何名为痛？所谓痛者，痛者名觉。为觉何物？觉苦、觉乐、觉不苦不乐，故名为觉也。

What is called conception? Namely, conception is also knowledge. Knowing blue, yellow, white, and black, knowing suffering and happiness, is called knowledge.

「云何名为想？所谓想者，想亦是知。知青、黄、白、黑，知苦乐，故名为知。

What is called formation? Namely, formation is that which can be accomplished. Therefore, it is called formation. What is accomplished? Either unwholesome formations or wholesome formations. Therefore, it is called formation.

「云何名为行？所谓行者，能有所成，故名为行。为成何等？或成恶行，或成善行，故名为行。

What is called consciousness? Namely, consciousness distinguishes right from wrong, and also recognizes various tastes. This is called consciousness.

「云何名为识？所谓识，识别是非，亦识诸味，此名为识也。

"All heavenly sons should know that these five aggregates of clinging can know the three evil destinies, the heavenly path, and the human path; when these five aggregates of clinging cease, one can know the path to nirvāṇa." At that time, when this teaching was spoken, sixty thousand heavenly beings attained the purity of the Dharma eye.

「诸天子当知，此五盛阴，知三恶道、天道、人道；此五盛阴灭，便知有涅槃之道。」尔时，说此法时，有六万天人得法眼净。

At that time, the World-Honored One, having taught the Dharma to the heavenly beings and humans, rose from his seat and went to the summit of Mount Sumeru, speaking this verse:

尔时，世尊与诸天人说法已，即从坐起，诣须弥山顶，说此偈：

You should diligently study the Buddha's teachings and the noble assembly, and
extinguish the path of death, like a person taming an elephant with
a hook. If you can be diligent in this teaching, you will
end birth and death, and there will be no original source of suffering.

「汝等当勤学，　　于佛法圣众，当灭死迳路，　　如人钩调象。若能于此法，　　而无懈怠者，便当尽生死，　　无有苦原本。」

At that time, after the World-Honored One spoke this verse, he proceeded along the middle path. At that time, Brahma Heaven was on the right side of the Tathāgata, on the side of the silver path, and Śakra Devānām-Indra was on the side of the crystal path, and the heavenly beings and humans in the sky scattered flowers, burned incense, played music, and entertained the Tathāgata.

尔时，世尊说此偈已，便诣中道。是时，梵天在如来右处银道侧，释提桓因在水精道侧，及诸天人在虚空中散华烧香，作倡伎乐，娱乐如来。

At that time, the bhikṣuṇī Upacālā heard that the Tathāgata would arrive at the Sangkhasi Pond in Jambudvīpa today. Having heard this, she thought: "The four groups of disciples, kings, ministers, and people of the country will all go. If I were to go in my usual way, it would not be appropriate. I should now take on the appearance of a wheel-turning sage king and go to see the World-Honored One." At that time, the bhikṣuṇī Upacālā returned to her original form and took on the appearance of a wheel-turning sage king, complete with the seven treasures. The so-called seven treasures are: the wheel treasure, elephant treasure, horse treasure, jewel treasure, jade woman treasure, treasure of military command, and treasure of treasury command. These are called the seven treasures.

是时，优钵华色比丘尼闻如来今日当至阎浮提僧迦尸池水侧，闻已，便生此念：「四部之众、国王、大臣、国中人民，靡不往者。设我当以常法往者，此非其宜。我今当作转轮圣王形容，往见世尊。」是时，优钵华色比丘尼还隐其形，作转轮圣王形，七宝具足。所谓七宝者，轮宝、象宝、马宝、珠宝、玉女宝、典兵宝、典藏宝，是谓七宝。

At that time, Venerable Subhūti was on Vulture Peak in Rājagṛha, sewing his robes on the side of a mountain. At that time, Subhūti heard that the World-Honored One would arrive at Jambudvīpa today, and that the four groups of disciples would all see him. He thought: "I should now go in time to pay respects and make offerings to the Tathāgata." At that time, Venerable Subhūti then abandoned his work of sewing robes, rose from his seat, and placed his right foot on the ground. At that time, he further thought: "What is the form of the Tathāgata? Is it the eyes, ears, nose, mouth, body, or mind? Is it the earth, water, fire, or wind elements? All dharmas are empty and still, without creation or action. As the World-Honored One said in a verse:

尔时，尊者须菩提在罗阅城耆阇崛山中，在一山侧缝衣裳。是时，须菩提闻世尊今日当来至阎浮里地，四部之众靡不见者，我今者宜可时往问讯礼拜如来。尔时，尊者须菩提便舍缝衣之业，从坐起，右脚著地。是时，彼复作是念：「此如来形，何者是世尊？为是眼、耳、鼻、口、身、意乎？往见者复是地、水、火、风种乎？一切诸法皆悉空寂，无造、无作，如世尊所说偈言：

If one wishes to pay homage to the Buddha, the most
supreme one, one should contemplate the impermanence of the aggregates, sense
bases, and elements. The Buddhas of the past and future, and
the present Buddha, are all impermanent. If one wishes to pay
homage to the Buddhas of the past and future, and speak
of the present, one should contemplate the empty dharmas. If one
wishes to pay homage to the Buddhas of the past and
future, and the present Buddhas, one should consider the absence of self.

「『若欲礼佛者，　　及诸最胜者，阴持入诸种，　　皆悉观无常。曩昔过去佛，　　及以当来者，如今现在佛，　　此皆悉无常。若欲礼佛者，　　过去及当来，说于现在中，　　当观于空法。若欲礼佛者，　　过去及当来，现在及诸佛，　　当计于无我。』

"Here there is no self, no life, no person, no creator, no form, no teacher, no giver. All dharmas are empty and still. Who is the self? The self is without a master. I now take refuge in the true Dharma-aggregate." At that time, Venerable Subhūti returned to his seat to sew his robes.

「此中无我、无命、无人、无造作，亦无形容，有教、有授者，诸法皆悉空寂。何者是我？我者无主。我今归命真法之聚。」尔时，尊者须菩提还坐缝衣。

At that time, the bhikṣuṇī Upacālā took on the form of a wheel-turning sage king, with the seven treasures leading the way, and went to where the World-Honored One was. At that time, the five kings saw from afar the wheel-turning sage king coming, and were overjoyed, unable to contain themselves. They said to each other: "How wonderful! How extraordinary! Two precious treasures appear in the world: the Tathāgata and the wheel-turning sage king."

是时，优钵华色比丘尼作转轮圣王形，七宝导从至世尊所。是时，五国王遥见转轮圣王来，欢喜踊跃，不能自胜，自相谓言：「甚奇！甚特！世间出二珍宝，如来、转轮圣王。」

At that time, the World-Honored One, together with tens of thousands of devas and humans, came from the summit of Mount Sumeru to the side of the pond. At that time, when the World-Honored One raised his foot to step on the ground, this trichiliocosm shook in six ways. At that time, the transformed wheel-turning sage king gradually approached where the World-Honored One was. The kings of the small countries and the people all avoided him. At that time, the transformed sage king, realizing he was near the World-Honored One, reverted to his original form and bowed at the feet of the World-Honored One as a bhikṣuṇī. The five kings, seeing this, each lamented and said to each other: "We have suffered a great loss today. We should have seen the Tathāgata first, but now this bhikṣuṇī has seen him first."

尔时，世尊将数万天人从须弥山顶来，至池水侧。是时，世尊举足蹈地，此三千大千世界六变震动。是时，化转轮圣王渐渐至世尊所，诸小国王及人民之类各各避之。是时，化圣王觉知以近世尊，还复本形，作比丘尼礼世尊足。五王见已，各自称怨，自相谓言：「我等今日极有所失，我等先应见如来，然今此比丘尼先见之。」

At that time, the bhikṣuṇī came to where the World-Honored One was, bowed her head at his feet, and said to the Buddha: "I now pay homage to the most honored one. I was the first to see him today. I, the bhikṣuṇī Upacālā, am a disciple of the Tathāgata."

是时，比丘尼至世尊所，头面礼足，而白佛言：「我今礼最胜尊，今日先得觐省，我优钵花色比丘尼是如来弟子。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse to the bhikṣuṇī:

尔时，世尊与彼比丘尼而说偈言：

Good deeds are first to pay homage, the foremost without fault. The gate of emptiness
and liberation, this is the meaning of paying homage to the Buddha. If one wishes
to pay homage to the Buddha, whether in the future or past, one should contemplate
emptiness and the absence of dharmas. This is called the meaning of paying homage to the Buddha.

「善业以先礼，　　最初无过者，空无解脱门，　　此是礼佛义。若欲礼佛者，　　当来及过去，当观空无法，　　此名礼佛义。」

At that time, the five kings and an immeasurable assembly of people went to where the World-Honored One was. Each one proclaimed their name. "I am King Prasenajit of the country of Kashi." "I am King Udayana of the country of Vatsa." "I am King Ajatasatru, the lord of the people of the five cities." "I am King Udayana, the lord of the Southern Sea." "I am King Bimbisara of the country of Magadha." At that time, an assembly of eleven kotis of people gathered, as well as the fourfold assembly. The most venerable elder, with 1,250 people, went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed their heads at his feet, and stood to one side.

是时，五王及人民之众不可称计，往至世尊所，各自称名。「我是迦尸国王波斯匿。」「我是拔嗟国王，名曰优填。」「我是五都人民之主，名曰恶生。」「我是南海之主，名优陀延。」「我是摩竭国频毘娑罗王。」尔时，十一那术人民运集，及四部之众，最尊长者，千二百五十人往至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面立。

At that time, King Udayana held a sandalwood image in his hand and spoke a verse to the Buddha:

尔时，优填王手执牛头栴檀像，并以偈向如来说：

I now wish to ask, with compassion and protection for all
what kind of merit is attained by making a Buddha image?

「我今欲所问，　　慈悲护一切，作佛形像者，　　为得何等福？」

At that time, the World-Honored One again replied in verse:

尔时，世尊复以偈报曰：

Great King, now listen, I will briefly explain the meaning. Making a
Buddha image, I will now roughly explain it. The eye faculty is
initially not damaged, later attaining the heavenly eye to see, white and
black clearly and distinctly, is the merit of making a Buddha image
The physical form should be complete, the mind upright and not deluded
the power is twice that of ordinary people, for making a Buddha
image. One will never fall into evil destinies, one will always be
reborn in the heavens, becoming a heavenly king there, the merit of
making a Buddha image. The remaining merits are incalculable, the merit is
inconceivable, the fame spreads far and wide, the merit of making a Buddha image.

「大王今听之，　　少多演其义，作佛形像者，　　今当粗说之。眼根初不坏，　　后得天眼视，白黑而分明，　　作佛形像德。形体当完具，　　意正不迷惑，势力倍常人，　　造佛形像者。终不堕恶趣，　　终辄生天上，于彼作天王，　　造佛形像福。余福不可计，　　其福不思议，名闻遍四远，　　造佛形像福。

"Excellent, excellent! Great King, you will benefit many, and gods and humans will be blessed."

「善哉！善哉！大王！多所饶益，天、人蒙祐。」

At that time, King Udayana was extremely joyful and could not contain himself.

尔时，优填王极怀欢悦，不能自胜。

At that time, the World-Honored One expounded the wonderful teachings to the four groups of disciples and the five kings. The teachings were about giving, precepts, rebirth in heaven, the impure nature of desires, the great suffering of afflictions, and the wonderfulness of liberation. At that time, the World-Honored One knew that the minds of the four groups of disciples were open and enlightened. The teachings of the Buddhas always include the teachings on suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path. At that time, more than sixty thousand gods and people in the assembly had their defilements completely eliminated and attained the purity of the Dharma eye.

尔时，世尊与四部众及与五王演说妙论，所谓论者：施论、戒论、生天之论，欲不净想，漏为大患，出要为妙。尔时，世尊以知四部之众心开意解，诸佛世尊常所说法：苦、习、尽、道，尽与彼说之。尔时，坐上天及人民六万余人，诸尘垢尽，得法眼净。

At that time, the five kings said to the World-Honored One, "This place is most blessed and wonderful, the most spiritual ground. The Tathāgata first descended from Tuṣita Heaven to this place to teach the Dharma. Now we wish to establish this place so that it will endure forever."

尔时，五王白世尊言：「此处福妙最是神地，如来始从兜术天来下至此说法，今欲建立此处，使永存不朽。」

The World-Honored One said, "You five kings, by building a divine temple here, you will receive blessings for a long time and they will not perish."

世尊告曰：「汝等五王，于此处造立神寺，长夜受福，终不朽败。」

The kings replied, "How should we build a divine temple?"

诸王报曰：「当云何造立神寺？」

At that time, the World-Honored One extended his right hand and brought forth the temple of Kāśyapa Tathāgata from the ground. Looking at the five kings, he said, "If you wish to build a divine temple, you should follow this as the standard."

尔时，世尊申右手，从地中出迦叶如来寺，视五王而告之曰：「欲作神寺者，当以此为法。」

At that time, the five kings immediately built a great divine temple at that place.

尔时，五王即于彼处起大神寺。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "The number of attendants of the past tathāgatas was also like today, without any difference. The number of attendants of the future buddhas will also be like today, without any difference. This sūtra is called 'The Fundamental Dharma of Wandering in the Heavens.' Thus, bhikṣus, you should train in this way."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「诸过去恒沙如来翼从多少，亦如今日而无有异。正使当来诸佛世尊翼从多少，亦如今日而无有异。今此经名游天法本。如是，诸比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the fourfold assembly and the five kings of the countries, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，四部之众及五国王闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
