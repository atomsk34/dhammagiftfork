EĀ 37.3

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

37.3 （三）

Parallel to: MN 32 / MĀ 184

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at the Lion's Grove in the country of Vārāṇasī.

一时，佛在拔耆国师子园中。

The venerable Śāriputra, the venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, the venerable Mahākāśyapa, the venerable Revata, the venerable Ānanda, and others, a total of five hundred people. At that time, Mahāmaudgalyāyana, Mahākāśyapa, and Aniruddha went to where Śāriputra in the morning. Ānanda saw from afar the three great śrāvakas going to where Śāriputra and said to Revata, "The three great śrāvakas are going to where Śāriputra. The two of us should also go to where Śāriputra. The reason is that we have heard that Śāriputra speaks wonderful Dharma."

诸神足高德比丘，贤者舍利弗、贤者大目干连、贤者迦叶、贤者离越、贤者阿难等五百人俱。是时，大目干连、大迦叶、阿那律晨旦至舍利弗所，然阿难遥见三大声闻诣舍利弗所，语离越言：「三大声闻往至舍利弗，我等二人亦可往至舍利弗所。所以然者，备闻舍利弗说奇妙之法。」

Revata replied, "This is possible."

离越报曰：「此事可然。」

At that time, Revata and Ānanda went to where Śāriputra. At that time, Śāriputra said, "Welcome, all virtuous ones! Take a seat here." At that time, Śāriputra said to Ānanda, "I now wish to ask: 'This Vṛṣabhadhvaja Park is extremely pleasant, with natural heavenly fragrances spreading far and wide. How can this park be made pleasant?'"

是时，离越、阿难往至舍利弗所。是时，舍利弗言：「善来，诸贤！就此处坐。」是时，舍利弗语阿难曰：「我今欲有所问：『此牛师子园极为快乐，自然天香流布四远，云何当使此园快乐？』」

Ānanda replied, "If a bhikṣu has heard much and does not forget, comprehending the meaning and flavor of the Dharma, fully practicing the brahmacarya, possessing all these dharmas without leaking or losing them, and expounding the Dharma to the four assemblies without losing the sequence, without being abrupt, without disordered thoughts. In this way, a bhikṣu is happy in this Vṛṣabhadhvaja Park."

阿难报曰：「若有比丘多有所闻不忘，总持诸法义味，具足修行梵行，如此诸法皆悉具足，亦不漏脱，与四部之众而为说法，不失次第，亦不卒暴，无有乱想。如是，比丘在此牛师子园快乐。」

At that time, Śāriputra said to Revata, "Ānanda has just now explained it. I now wish to ask you about the meaning. The Vṛṣabhadhvaja Park is as pleasant as this. Now you should explain next, what is the meaning?"

是时，舍利弗语离越言：「阿难今日以演说之，我今复欲问汝义。牛师子园快乐如是。汝今次说，义复云何？」

Revata replied, "In this way, a bhikṣu delights in a secluded place, contemplating and sitting in meditation, in accord with right contemplation. In this way, a bhikṣu delights in the Vṛṣabhadhvaja Park."

离越报曰：「于是，比丘乐闲静之处，思惟坐禅，与正观相应。如是，比丘乐牛师子园中。」

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra said to Aniruddha, "You should now explain the meaning of delight."

是时，尊者舍利弗语阿那律曰：「汝今当说悦乐之义。」

Aniruddha replied, "If a bhikṣu has the divine eye and can see the classes of beings, those who die and are born, the good and bad forms, the good and bad destinies, the beautiful and ugly, he knows them all. There are sentient beings whose body, speech, and mind do evil, who slander the sages, and when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are born in hell. There are also sentient beings whose body, speech, and mind do good, who do not slander the sages. It is like a man looking at the sky, seeing everything without exception. A bhikṣu with the divine eye is also like this, seeing the world without any doubt. In this way, a bhikṣu in the Vṛṣabhadhvaja Park is happy in this way."

阿那律报曰：「若有比丘天眼彻视，观众生类，死者、生者，善色、恶色，善趣、恶趣，若好、若丑，皆悉知之。或有众生身、口、意行恶，诽谤贤圣，身坏命终，生地狱中。或复有众生身、口、意行善，不诽谤贤圣，犹如士夫观空中，靡不备悉。有天眼比丘亦复如是，观世界无有疑难。如是，比丘在牛师子园中，快乐如是也。」

At that time, Śāriputra said to Kāśyapa, "I now tell you. These wise ones have explained the meaning of happiness. You should now explain it next."

是时，舍利弗语迦叶曰：「我今语汝。如是诸贤以说快乐之义，汝今次应说之。」

Kāśyapa replied, "If a bhikṣu practices the practice of araṇya, and also teaches others to practice araṇya, praising the virtues of tranquility; himself wearing patched robes, and also teaching others to practice dhuta; himself content, dwelling in a secluded place, and also teaching others to cultivate that practice; himself with complete precepts, samadhi, wisdom, liberation, and liberation-wisdom, and also teaching others to practice that Dharma; praising the Dharma and being able to inspire others, and also teaching others to practice that Dharma; teaching without ever being satisfied. In this way, a bhikṣu is incomparably happy in the Vṛṣabhadhvaja Park."

迦叶报曰：「若有比丘行阿练若行，复教他人使行阿练若，叹说闲静之德；己身著补纳之衣，复教他使行头陀；身自知足在闲居之处，复教他人使修其行；己身戒德具足，三昧成就、智慧成就、解脱成就、解脱见慧成就，复教他人使行其法，叹说其法已能劝化，复教他人使行其法；教训无厌足。如是，比丘在牛师子园中快乐无比。」

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra said to Mahāmaudgalyāyana, "The wise ones have explained the meaning of happiness. You should now explain the meaning of happiness. The happiness in the Vṛṣabhadhvaja Park is incomparable. How do you wish to explain it?"

尔时，尊者舍利弗语大目连曰：「诸贤圣以说快乐之义，汝今次说快乐之义。牛师子园中快乐无双，汝今欲云何说之？」

Maudgalyāyana replied, "In this way, a bhikṣu has great supernatural powers and is free in supernatural powers. He can transform countless thousands of things without difficulty, and can also divide one body into countless bodies, or re-combine them into one. He can pass through stone walls and mountains, and rise and sink freely. He is like a swift river, like a flying bird leaving no trace in the sky. He is like a raging fire burning mountains and fields, and is like the sun and moon illuminating everywhere. He can also raise his hand to touch the sun and moon, and can transform his body to reach the Brahma heavens. Such a bhikṣu should be in the Vṛṣabhadhvaja Park."

目连报曰：「于是，比丘有大神足，于神足而得自在，彼能变化无数千事而无疑难，亦能分一身作无数身，或复还合为一，石壁皆过，踊没自在。亦如驶河，犹如飞鸟在空中无迹，譬如暴火燌烧山野，亦如日月靡所不照，亦能举手摩抆日月，亦能化身至梵天上。如此，比丘宜牛师子园中。」

At that time, Maudgalyāyana said to Śāriputra, "We each speak according to our eloquence. We now ask Śāriputra about the meaning. The Vṛṣabhadhvaja Park is extremely happy. What kind of bhikṣu should be in it?"

是时，目连语舍利弗曰：「我等各随其辩而说之，我等今问舍利弗义，牛师子园极为快乐，何等比丘宜在其中？」

Śāriputra said, "If a bhikṣu can subdue the mind, then the mind cannot subdue the bhikṣu. If that bhikṣu wishes to attain samādhi, then that bhikṣu can attain samādhi. He can accomplish it according to his wishes, whether near or far. It is like a householder's house with fine clothes stored in a box. Then that householder can take whatever clothes he wants without difficulty. He can also enter samādhi according to his wishes. It is like this. The mind can control the bhikṣu, not the bhikṣu controlling the mind. Entering samādhi according to one's wishes is also like this. Such a bhikṣu can control the mind, not the mind controlling the bhikṣu. Such a person should be in the Vṛṣabhadhvaja Park."

舍利弗言：「若有比丘能降心，然彼心不能降比丘。设彼比丘欲得三昧，即时彼比丘能得三昧，随意远近成三昧者，即能成办之。犹如长者家有好衣，盛著箱箧，尔时彼长者随意欲取何等衣，随意取之而无疑难；亦能随意入三昧中，此亦如是，心能使比丘，非比丘能使心，随意入三昧亦无疑难。如是，比丘能使心，非心使比丘，如是之人宜在牛师子园中。」

At that time, Śāriputra said to the wise ones, "We each speak according to our eloquence. We each speak well according to our abilities. Now we should all go together to ask the World-Honored One, 'How can a bhikṣu enjoy this Vṛṣabhadhvaja Park?' If the World-Honored One says something, we should all practice it."

是时，舍利弗告诸贤曰：「我等随其辩而说，各随方宜，善说此义；今各相将往问世尊，云何比丘得乐此牛师子园？若世尊有所说，我等当奉行。」

The bhikṣus replied, "Yes, Śāriputra!"

诸比丘报曰：「如是。舍利弗！」

At that time, the great śrāvakas went together to where the Tathāgata was. Having arrived, they bowed their heads at his feet and sat to one side. At that time, the great śrāvakas fully informed the Buddha of this cause and condition. At that time, the World-Honored One said, "Excellent! As Ānanda has said. The reason is this: Bhikṣu Ānanda hears the Dharma and is able to uphold it, comprehensively embracing all dharmas and fully practicing the holy life. Such a Dharma is well-heard and not forgotten, without wrong views, and he expounds the Dharma to the four assemblies, his words are not mistaken or confused, nor are they abrupt.

是时，大声闻等各各相将往至如来所。到已，头面礼足，在一面坐。尔时，大声闻以此因缘具白佛。尔时，世尊告曰：「善哉！如阿难所说。所以然者，阿难比丘闻法能持，总摄诸法，具足修行梵行。如此之法，善闻不忘，亦无邪见，与四部之众而说法，言不错乱，亦不卒暴。

The words spoken by Bhikṣu Liuyue are also excellent! The reason is this: He delights in solitary places, not dwelling among people, always contemplating sitting in meditation, without disputes or arguments, in accord with tranquility and insight, dwelling in solitude and quietude.

「离越比丘所说亦复快哉！所以然者，乐闲静之处，不处人间，常念坐禅，无有诤讼，与止观相应，闲居寂寞。

Bhikṣu Aniruddha is also excellent! The reason is this: Bhikṣu Aniruddha has the foremost divine eye. With his divine eye, he observes the trichiliocosm as if a person with eyes were looking at a pearl in the palm of their hand. Bhikṣu Aniruddha is also like this. With his divine eye, he observes this trichiliocosm without any difficulty.

「阿那律比丘亦复快哉！所以然者，阿那律比丘天眼第一。彼以天眼观三千世界，犹如有眼之人，掌中观珠。阿那律比丘亦复如是，彼以天眼观此三千大千世界而无疑难。

Now Bhikṣu Kāśyapa is also excellent! The reason is this: Bhikṣu Kāśyapa himself practices the conduct of an araṇya ascetic and is also able to praise the conduct of solitary dwelling; he himself begs for alms and is also able to praise the virtue of begging for alms; he himself wears patched robes and is also able to praise the virtue of patched robes; he himself is content and is also able to praise the virtue of contentment; he himself dwells in caves and also praises the virtue of caves; he himself has accomplished the precepts, samadhi, wisdom, liberation, and the vision and wisdom of liberation, and is also able to teach others to accomplish these five parts of the Dharma body; he himself is able to teach and transform, and is also able to teach others to practice the Dharma.

「今迦叶比丘亦复快哉！所以然者，迦叶比丘己身是阿练若行，复能叹说闲居之行；身能乞食，复能叹誉乞食之德；身著补纳衣，复能叹说补纳衣之德；己身知足，复能叹说知足之德；己身处岩穴，复叹说岩穴之德，己身戒成就、三昧成就、智慧成就、解脱成就、解脱见慧成就，复能教人成此五分法身，身能教化，复能教人使行其法。

Excellent! Excellent! As what Maudgalyāyana has said. The reason is this: Bhikṣu Maudgalyāyana has great power and the foremost supernatural powers. His mind is free and he can accomplish whatever he wishes. He can transform one body into myriads of billions, or combine them back into one. He can pass through stone walls without obstruction, rising and sinking freely. He is like a swift current, without any obstruction. Like a bird in the sky, he leaves no footprints. Like the sun and moon, he illuminates everywhere. He can transform his body up to the Brahma Heaven.

「善哉！善哉！如目连所说。所以然者，目连比丘有大威力，神足第一，心得自在，彼意欲所为，则能办之，或化一身，分为万亿，或还合为一，石壁皆过，无有罣碍，踊没自在；亦如驶水亦无触碍；如空中之鸟亦无足迹，犹如日月靡所不照，能化身乃至梵天。

Excellent! As what Śāriputra has said. The reason is this: Śāriputra is able to subdue the mind, and the mind cannot subdue Śāriputra. When he wishes to enter samadhi, he can accomplish it without any difficulty, just as a householder can take whatever fine clothes he wants without any difficulty. Bhikṣu Śāriputra is also like this. He is able to subdue the mind, and the mind cannot subdue Śāriputra. He can enter samadhi according to his wishes, all before him.

「善哉！如舍利弗之所说。所以然者，舍利弗能降伏心，非心能降伏舍利弗。若欲入三昧时，则能成办无有疑难，犹如长者好衣裳，随意取之而无疑难。舍利弗比丘亦复如是，能降伏心，非心能降伏舍利弗，随意入三昧，皆悉在前。

"Excellent! Excellent! Bhikṣus! What you have said is each according to your expedient means. But now, listen to what I have to say. How do bhikṣus enjoy the Ox-Lion Park? If a bhikṣu lives near a village, he goes there at the right time, puts on his robe, takes his alms bowl, enters the village to beg for food, and returns to his place after begging for food. He washes his hands and face, sits upright with his legs crossed under a tree, and focuses his mind in front of him. The bhikṣu then thinks: 'I will not break this sitting until I have exhausted all defilements and attained purity.' At that time, the bhikṣu's defiled mind is liberated. Thus, bhikṣus should be in the Ox-Lion Park. Thus, bhikṣus should always be diligent and not lazy. Wherever they are, they will be respected. Thus, bhikṣus! You should train like this."

「善哉！善哉！诸比丘！汝等所说各随方便，但今复听我所说。云何比丘乐牛师子园中？若有比丘依村落住，彼到时，著衣持钵，入村乞食，彼已乞食已，还归所在，洗手面，在一树下正身正意，结加趺坐，系念在前。彼比丘便作是念：『我今不坏于坐，要当尽有漏成无漏。』尔时，彼比丘即有漏心得解脱。如是，比丘宜在牛师子园中。如是，比丘恒勤精进，莫有懈怠，所在之处靡不宗奉者。如是，比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said, and joyfully practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
