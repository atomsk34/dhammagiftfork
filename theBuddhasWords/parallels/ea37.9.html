EĀ 37.9

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

37.9 （九）

Parallel to: AN 7.50

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the Brahmācarin Brāhmaṇa went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. The Brāhmaṇa then said to the World-Honored One, "Is there a bhikṣu here who can practice the holy life without any leaks, and practice the holy life in purity?"

尔时，生漏梵志往至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。尔时，梵志白世尊言：「此中颇有比丘，云何得修梵行无有缺漏，清净修梵行？」

The World-Honored One said, "If someone is fully endowed with the precepts and does not violate them, this is called practicing the holy life in purity. Furthermore, Brāhmaṇa! If one sees forms with the eyes, does not give rise to attachment, does not give rise to thoughts, eliminates evil thoughts, and removes unwholesome dharmas, and fully obtains the eye faculty, this is called practicing the holy life in purity. If one hears sounds with the ears, smells fragrances with the nose, tastes flavors with the tongue, feels tactile objects with the body, and knows dharmas with the mind, without any conceptualization, not giving rise to thoughts, practicing the holy life in purity, and fully obtaining the mind faculty, such a person can practice the holy life without any leaks."

世尊告曰：「若有人戒律具足而无所犯，此名清净修得梵行。复次，梵志！若有眼见色，不起想著，不起识念，除恶想，去不善法，得全眼根，是谓此人清净修梵行。若耳闻声、鼻嗅香、舌知味、身知细滑、意知法，都无识想，不起想念，清净得修梵行，全其意根，如此之人得修梵行，无有缺漏。」

The Brāhmaṇa said to the Buddha, "What kind of person does not practice the holy life and does not fully practice the pure conduct?"

婆罗门白佛言：「何等之人不修梵行，不具足清净行？」

The World-Honored One said, "If someone is together with others, this is called not practicing the holy life."

世尊告曰：「若有人俱会者，此名非梵行。」

The Brāhmaṇa said to the Buddha, "What kind of person does not fully possess the leaks?"

婆罗门白佛言：「何等之人漏不具足？」

The World-Honored One said, "If a person has physical contact with a woman, touching hands or feet, and is unable to let go of the thought, this is called not fully practicing, leaking out lust, and being associated with lust, anger, and delusion.

世尊告曰：「若有人与女人交接，或手足相触，擑在心怀而不忘失。是谓，梵志！行不具足，漏诸婬泆，与婬、怒、痴共相应。

Furthermore, Brāhmaṇa! If one jokes and teases with a woman, speaking to each other, this is called not fully practicing, leaking out lust, anger, and delusion, and not fully practicing the pure practice of the Brahma-way.

「复次，梵志！或与女人共相调戏，言语相加。是谓，梵志！此人行不全具，漏婬、怒、痴，梵行不具足修清净行。

Furthermore, Brāhmaṇa! If a woman looks at one with evil eyes and does not turn away, one then gives rise to thoughts of lust, anger, and delusion, and gives rise to various confused thoughts. This is called not practicing the Brahma-way, not practicing the pure practice of the Brahma-way.

「复次，梵志！若有女人恶眼相视而不移转，于中便起婬、怒、痴想，生诸乱念。是谓，梵志！此人梵行不净，不修梵行。

Furthermore, Brāhmaṇa! If one hears a crying or laughing voice from afar, one gives rise to thoughts of lust, anger, and delusion, and gives rise to various confused thoughts. This is called not practicing the pure practice of the Brahma-way, not practicing the Brahma-way, and not fully practicing.

「复次，梵志！若复有人远闻或闻哭声，或闻笑声，于中起婬、怒、痴，起诸乱想。是谓，梵志！此人不清净修梵行，与婬、怒、痴共相应，行不全具。

Furthermore, Brāhmaṇa! If a person has seen a woman before and later gives rise to thoughts, remembering her head and eyes, and gives rise to thoughts in a secluded place, giving rise to lust, anger, and delusion, and associating with evil deeds. This is called not practicing the Brahma-way.

「复次，梵志！若有人曾见女人，后更生想，忆其头目，于中生想；在屏闲之处，生婬、怒、痴，与恶行相应。是谓，梵志！此人不修梵行。」

At that time, the Brāhmaṇa of the Birth-Louse clan said to the World-Honored One: "How wonderful! How extraordinary! This Śramaṇa Gautama also knows the practice of the Brahma-way and also knows the non-practice of the Brahma-way; he also knows the practice of the birth-louse and also knows the non-practice of the birth-louse. Because I also have this thought: If people join their hands and give rise to various confused thoughts, I then think: 'This person's practice is impure and associated with lust, anger, and delusion.' The first pleasure is a woman. The first desire is called the eyes looking at each other. That woman may speak or laugh, binding a man, or speaking with him to bind a man. At that time, I then think: 'These six people all practice impure conduct.' The Tathāgata's words today are even more so, like a blind person obtaining eyes, a lost person seeing the road, a foolish person hearing the Way, a person with eyes seeing forms. The Tathāgata's teaching the Dharma is also like this. I now take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṅgha. From now on, I will no longer take life. I only wish to be accepted as an upāsaka."

是时，生漏梵志白世尊言：「甚奇！甚特！此沙门瞿昙亦知梵行，亦知不梵行；亦知漏行，亦知不漏行。所以然者，我今亦生此念，诸有人民，女人手足相加，起诸乱想。我时便生此念：『此人行不清净，与婬、怒、痴共相应。』第一更乐者，女人是也。第一可欲者，所谓眼眼相视，然彼女人或语、或笑，系缀男子，或共言语而系缀男子。是时，我便生此念：『此六人尽行不清净行。』如来今日所说甚过，犹如盲者得目，迷者见路，愚者闻道，有目之人见色，如来说法亦复如是。我今自归佛、法、众，自今之后不复杀生，唯愿受为优婆塞。」

At that time, the Brāhmaṇa of the Birth-Louse clan, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，生漏梵志闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
