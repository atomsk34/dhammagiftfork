EĀ 38.6

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

38.6 （六）

Parallel to: MN 86 / SĀ 1077 / SĀ² 2.16

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, many bhikṣus entered Śrāvastī to beg for alms. They heard outside the palace gate of King Prasenajit that many people were crying out, all complaining, "There is a bandit named Aṅgulimāla in the kingdom, who is extremely cruel and has killed countless living beings. He has no compassion for any sentient beings. The people of the kingdom all detest him. He takes people's lives every day and makes a garland of their fingers. Therefore, he is called 'Finger Garland'. We only wish that the great king would go and fight him."

尔时，有众多比丘入舍卫城乞食，闻王波斯匿宫门外有众多人民，于中举手唤呼皆称怨：「国界有贼名鸯掘魔，极为凶暴，杀害生类不可称计，无慈悲于一切众生，国界人民无不厌患，日取人杀以指为鬘，故名为指鬘。唯愿大王当往共战。」

At that time, the many bhikṣus, having begged for alms, returned to the Jetavana Monastery. They put away their robes and bowls, took their sitting mats on their shoulders, went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed their heads at his feet, and sat to one side. Then the many bhikṣus said to the World-Honored One, "We, the many bhikṣus, entered Śrāvastī to beg for alms. We saw many people crying out at the palace gate of the king, saying, 'Now there is a bandit named Aṅgulimāla in the king's kingdom. He is cruel to people, has no compassionate mind, kills all sentient beings, and causes the people to die and the country to be empty because of him. He also takes people's fingers to make a flower garland.'"

是时，众多比丘乞食已，还诣祇洹精舍。收摄衣钵，以尼师檀著肩上，往至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。尔时，众多比丘白世尊言：「我等众多比丘入舍卫城乞食，见众多人民在王宫门外，称怨诉辞：『今王国界有贼名鸯掘魔，为人凶暴，无有慈心，杀于一切众生，人亡国虚皆由此人，又取人指以为华鬘。』」

At that time, the World-Honored One, having heard the bhikṣu's words, immediately rose from his seat and walked away in silence.

尔时，世尊闻彼比丘语已，即从座起，默然而行。

At that time, the World-Honored One went to where he was. Those who were gathering firewood, carrying grass, plowing, and tending cattle and sheep, saw the World-Honored One approaching on the road and said to the Buddha, "Ascetic! Ascetic! Do not go that way. Because there is a bandit named Aṅgulimāla who stays on that road. Whenever people want to take that road, they must gather in groups of ten, twenty, thirty, forty, or fifty, and still cannot pass by. They are all captured by Aṅgulimāla. The śramaṇa Gautama is alone without companions. If he is touched and disturbed by Aṅgulimāla, he will not be able to handle it." Although the World-Honored One heard these words, he still advanced and did not stop.

是时，世尊寻到彼所，诸有取薪、负草、犁作之人，及牧牛羊者，见世尊诣彼道，各白佛言：「沙门！沙门！勿从彼道。所以然者，此路侧有贼名鸯掘魔，于中止住。诸有人民，欲就此道者，要集十人、或二十人、或三十、四十、五十人，犹不得过，尽为鸯掘魔所擒获。然沙门瞿昙独无有侣，为鸯掘魔所触娆者，于事不省。」世尊虽闻此语，故进不住。

At that time, Aṅgulimāla's mother brought food to where Aṅgulimāla was. At that time, Aṅgulimāla had this thought: "Is my garland of fingers complete?" At that time, he counted the fingers, but it was not complete. He counted them again, but there was only one person's finger missing. At that time, Aṅgulimāla looked around to the left and right, seeking a living person to kill in order to obtain the finger. But looking in all directions, he still did not see anyone. He then thought: "My teacher has taught that if one can kill one's mother, one will definitely be reborn in heaven. My mother has come here in person now. I can kill her, obtain the finger to complete the number, and be reborn in heaven."

尔时，鸯掘魔母，持食诣鸯掘魔所。是时，鸯掘魔便作是念：「吾指鬘为充数不乎？」是时，即数指，未充数，复更重数，唯少一人指。是时，鸯掘魔左右顾视，求觅生人，欲取杀之；然四远顾望，亦不见人，便作是念：「我师有教：『若能害母者，必当生天。』我今母躬来在此，即可取杀之，得指充数，生于天上。」

At that time, Aṅgulimāla held his mother's head with his left hand, drew his sword with his right hand, and said to his mother, "Wait a moment, mother." At that time, the World-Honored One had this thought: "This Aṅgulimāla will commit the five heinous crimes." He then emitted a light from the mark between his eyebrows, illuminating the forest on the mountain. When Aṅgulimāla saw the light, he again said to his mother, "What is this light that illuminates this mountain forest? Is it not the king gathering his troops to attack me?"

是时，鸯掘魔左手捉母头，右手拔剑而语母言：「小住，阿母。」是时，世尊便作是念：「此鸯掘魔当为五逆。」即放眉间相光明，普照彼山林。是时，鸯掘魔见光明已，复语母言：「此是何光明照此山林？将非国王集诸兵众，攻伐我身乎？」

At that time, the mother said, "You should know now that this is not the light of the sun, moon, or fire, nor is it the light of Śakra or Brahmā."

是时母告曰：「汝今当知，此非日月火光，亦非释、梵天王光明。」

At that time, the mother spoke this verse:

尔时，其母便说此偈：

This is not the light of fire, nor the sun, moon, Śakra, or
Brahmā. The birds and beasts are not startled, their harmonious calls are extraordinary
This light is extremely pure, bringing immeasurable joy to people. It must be
the most honored and supreme one, the Ten-Powered One, who has come here
Among the gods and humans, with the divine eye, he sees the world
Therefore, he wishes to liberate your body. The World-Honored One has come here.

「此非火光明，　　非日月释梵，鸟狩不惊怖，　　和鸣殊于常。此光极清净，　　使人悦无量，必是尊最胜，　　十力至此间。于天世人中，　　天眼覩世界，故欲度汝身，　　世尊来至此。」

At that time, Aṅgulimāla heard the Buddha's voice and was overjoyed, unable to contain himself. He said, "My teacher also instructed me, saying, 'If you can kill your mother and also kill the śramaṇa Gautama, you will definitely be reborn in the Heaven of Brahmā.'"

是时，鸯掘魔闻佛音响，欢喜踊跃，不能自胜，便作是语：「我师亦有教诫而勅我曰：『设汝能害母，并杀沙门瞿昙者，必生梵天上。』」

At that time, Aṅgulimāla said to his mother, "Mother, please wait. I will first take the śramaṇa Gautama and kill him, then I will eat him."

是时，鸯掘魔语母曰：「母！今且住，我先取沙门瞿昙杀，然后当食。」

At that time, Aṅgulimāla immediately released his mother and went after the World-Honored One. Seeing the World-Honored One coming from afar, he was like a mass of gold, illuminating everywhere. Seeing this, he laughed and said, "This śramaṇa is certainly in my hands now. I will definitely kill him, there is no doubt. If any people want to take this path, they all gather in a large crowd and take this path; but this śramaṇa has no companions. I will now take him and kill him."

是时，鸯掘魔即放母而往逐世尊。遥见世尊来，亦如金聚，靡所不照，见已，并笑而说是语：「今此沙门定在我手，必杀不疑。其有人民欲行此道者，皆集大众而行此道；然此沙门独无伴侣，我今当取杀之。」

At that time, Aṅgulimāla immediately drew his sword from his waist and went against the World-Honored One. At that time, the World-Honored One then returned along the same path, walking slowly. Aṅgulimāla ran after him but still could not catch up with the Tathāgata. At that time, Aṅgulimāla said to the World-Honored One, "Stop, stop, Śramaṇa!"

是时，鸯掘魔即拔腰剑，往逆世尊。是时，世尊寻还复道，徐而行步，而鸯掘魔奔驰而逐，亦不能及如来。是时，鸯掘魔白世尊言：「住，住，沙门！」

The World-Honored One said, "I am indeed stopping, but you are not stopping."

世尊告曰：「我自住耳，汝自不住。」

At that time, Aṅgulimāla ran and spoke this verse from afar:

是时，彼鸯掘魔并走，遥说此偈：

Going and then saying to stop, speaking to me of not stopping
please explain this meaning to me, that stopping and my not stopping.

「去而复言住，　　语我言不住，与我说此义，　　彼住我不住。」

At that time, the World-Honored One replied in verse:

尔时，世尊以偈报曰：

The World-Honored One has said to stop, not harming anything at all
You now have a mind to kill, not free from the root
of evil. I abide in the mind of loving-kindness, compassionately protecting all
people. You plant the suffering of hell, not free from the root of evil.

「世尊言已住，　　不害于一切，汝今有杀心，　　不离于恶原。我住慈心地，　　愍护一切人，汝种地狱苦，　　不离于恶原。」

At that time, when Aṅgulimāla heard these verses, he thought: "Am I really doing evil? But my teacher told me, 'This is a great sacrifice, with great rewards. If you can kill a thousand people and make a garland of their fingers, you will fulfill your wishes. Such a person, after death, will be reborn in a good place in heaven. If you kill your mother and the śramaṇa Gautama, you will be reborn in the Brahma heaven.' At that time, the Buddha used his spiritual power, and my consciousness was awakened. The books of the brāhmaṇas also say, 'The Tathāgata's appearance in the world is very rare, appearing only once in many eons. When he appears in the world, he liberates those who are not liberated and frees those who are not free. He teaches the Dharma to destroy the six views. What are the six? For those who say there is a self, he teaches the Dharma to destroy the six views; for those who say there is no self, he also teaches the Dharma to destroy the view of no self; for those who say there is a self and no self, he also teaches the Dharma of the view of self and no self; he also observes and teaches the Dharma of observation; he also teaches the Dharma of no self, and also the Dharma of neither self nor not self. If the Tathāgata appears in the world, he teaches the Dharma to destroy the six views.' Also, when I was running, I could catch up with elephants, horses, and carriages, and also people. But this śramaṇa walks slowly, yet today I cannot catch up with him. He must be the Tathāgata."

是时，鸯掘魔闻此偈已，便作是念：「我今审为恶耶？又师语我言：『此是大祠，获大果报。能取千人杀，以指作鬘者，果其所愿；如此之人，命终之后，生善处天上。设取所生母及沙门瞿昙杀者，当生梵天上。』是时，佛作威神，神识［怡－台＋霍］寤，诸梵志书籍亦有此言：『如来出世甚为难遇，时时亿劫乃出。彼出世时，不度者令度，不解脱者令得解脱。彼说灭六见之法。云何为六？言有我见者，即说灭六见之法；无有我者，亦与说灭无有我见之法；言有我见、无有我见，亦与说有我见、无我见之法，复自观察、说观察之法；自说无我之法，亦非我说、亦非我不说之法。若如来出世，说此灭六见之法。』又我奔走之时，能及象、马、车乘，亦及人民。然此沙门行不暴疾，然今日不能及此，必当是如来。」

At that time, Aṅgulimāla spoke this verse:

是时，鸯掘魔便说此偈：

You have now spoken these subtle verses for my sake. The evil
one now recognizes the truth, all due to your spiritual power. I
immediately discarded my sword and threw it into a deep pit. Now
I pay homage to the śramaṇa's footprints and seek to become a śramaṇa.

「尊今为我故，　　而说微妙偈，恶者今识真，　　皆由尊威神。即时舍利剑，　　投于深坑中，今礼沙门迹，　　即求作沙门。」

At that time, Aṅgulimāla immediately went before the Buddha and said, "World-Honored One! Please allow me to become a śramaṇa."

是时，鸯掘魔即前白佛言：「世尊！唯愿听作沙门。」

The World-Honored One said, "Welcome, bhikṣu!" At that moment, Aṅgulimāla became a śramaṇa and put on the three Dharma robes.

世尊告曰：「善来，比丘！」即时鸯掘魔便成沙门，著三法衣。

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

Now that you have shaved your head, you should also remove your afflictions
By extinguishing afflictions, you will attain great fruits, and no more worries or suffering.

「汝今以剃头，　　除结亦当尔，结灭成大果，　　无复愁苦恼。」

When Angulimala heard these words, his defilements were instantly extinguished, and he attained the pure Dharma eye. At that time, the World-Honored One led the bhiksu Angulimala back to the Jetavana Monastery in the city of Shravasti.

是时，鸯掘魔闻此语已，即时诸尘垢尽，得法眼净。尔时，世尊将鸯掘魔比丘还诣舍卫城祇洹精舍。

At that time, King Prasenajit gathered the four divisions of his troops, intending to attack the bandit Angulimala. The king then thought, "I should go to the World-Honored One and fully inform him of this matter. If the World-Honored One has anything to say, I shall follow it." At that time, King Prasenajit immediately gathered the four divisions of his army and went to where the World-Honored One was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side. At that time, the World-Honored One asked the king, "Great King! Where are you going today? Your body is covered in dust and dirt.

是时，王波斯匿集四部之众，欲往攻伐贼鸯掘魔。是时，王便作是念：「我今可往至世尊所，以此因缘，具白世尊。若世尊有所说者，当奉行之。」尔时，王波斯匿即集四部之兵，往世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。尔时，世尊问王曰：「大王！今日欲何所至？尘污身体，乃至于斯。」

King Prasenajit said to the Buddha, "In my country, there is a bandit named Angulimala who is extremely cruel and has no compassion for any sentient beings. He has caused the countryside to be desolate and the people to scatter. It is all because of this bandit. He now kills people and takes their fingers as a garland. He is a wicked ghost, not a human being. I now wish to exterminate this person.

波斯匿王白佛言：「我今国界有贼名鸯掘魔，极为凶暴，无有慈心于一切众生，使国丘荒，人民流迸，皆由此贼。彼今取人杀之，以指为鬘，此是恶鬼，非为人也。我今欲诛伐此人。」

The World-Honored One said, "If the Great King sees Angulimala with a firm mind of faith, leaving home to study the path, what will the king do about it?"

世尊告曰：「若当大王见鸯掘魔信心坚固，出家学道者，王当奈之何？」

The king said to the Buddha, "What should be done? I will only serve and make offerings, paying homage according to the time. However, World-Honored One! He is an evil person, without the slightest bit of goodness. He always kills. How could he have the mind to leave home and study the path? It is impossible!"

王白佛言：「知复如何？但当承事供养，随时礼拜。然复，世尊！彼是恶人，无毫厘之善，恒杀害，能有此心出家学道乎？终无此理。」

At that time, Angulimala was sitting cross-legged not far from the World-Honored One, with his body and mind upright, focusing his thoughts in front of him. The World-Honored One then extended his right hand and pointed to the king, saying, "This is the thief Angulimala."

是时，鸯掘魔去世尊不远，结跏趺坐，正身正意，系念在前。尔时，世尊伸右手指示王曰：「此是贼鸯掘魔。」

Hearing these words, the king became fearful and his hair stood on end. The World-Honored One said to the king, "Do not be afraid. You can go forward and he will surely enlighten your mind."

王闻此语，便怀恐怖，衣毛皆竖。世尊告王：「勿怀恐怖，可往至前，自当悟王意耳。」

At that time, the king heard the Buddha's words and immediately went before Angulimala. He said to Angulimala, "What is your surname now?"

是时王闻佛语，即至鸯掘魔前，语鸯掘魔曰：「汝今姓谁？」

Angulimala said, "My surname is Gaga, and my mother's name is Satisfaction."

鸯掘魔曰：「我姓伽伽，母名满足。」

At that time, the king bowed at his feet and sat to one side. The king then asked, "May you delight in this true Dharma, without laziness, practicing pure and clean holy conduct to attain the end of suffering. I will provide for you for as long as I live, with robes, food and drink, bedding and furnishings, and medicine for illness and weakness."

是时王礼足已，在一面坐。尔时王问曰：「善乐此正法之中，勿有懈怠，修清净梵行，得尽苦际，我当尽形寿供养衣被、饮食、床卧具、病瘦医药。」

At that time, Angulimala remained silent and did not reply. The king then rose from his seat, bowed his head at his feet, and returned to where the World-Honored One was. He bowed his head at the feet, sat to one side,

是时，鸯掘魔默然不对。王即从座起，头面礼足，还诣世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。

At that time, the king again said to the Buddha, "To subdue the unyielding and tame the untamed is truly amazing and extraordinary! Never before has there been such a thing as being able to subdue an extremely evil person. I only wish that the Great Honored One would accept the command and live forever, nurturing the people. By the grace of the World-Honored One, we have escaped this calamity. The affairs of the country are many, and I wish to return to the city."

是时，王复白佛言：「不降者使降，不伏者使伏，甚奇！甚特！曾所不有，乃能降伏极恶之人。唯愿大尊受命无穷，长养生民，蒙世尊恩，得免此难。国事猥多，欲还城池。」

The World-Honored One said, "The king knows the time." At that time, the king rose from his seat, bowed his head at the feet, and then left.

世尊告曰：「王知是时。」尔时，国王即从座起，头面礼足，便退而去。

At that time, Angulimala practiced as an ascetic, wearing a robe made of rags, begging for alms from house to house at mealtimes, going in a circle. He wore a coarse and ugly robe made of patched rags, and also sat in the open, not covering his body. At that time, Angulimala practiced in a quiet place. The reason why a noble son leaves home to study the Way is to cultivate the unsurpassed pure practice: to end birth and death, establish the pure practice, accomplish what is to be done, and no longer accept rebirth, knowing it as it is. At that time, Angulimala then became an arhat, with the six supernatural powers and clear insight, free from defilements, having already become an arhat.

尔时，鸯掘魔作阿练若，著五纳衣，到时持钵，家家乞食，周而复始，著补纳弊坏之衣，极为麁丑；亦复露坐，不覆形体。是时，鸯掘魔在闲静之处，自修其行，所以族姓子，出家学道者，欲修无上梵行：生死已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，更不复受胎，如实知之。时，鸯掘魔便成罗汉，六通清彻，无有尘垢，已成阿罗汉。

At mealtime, he put on his robe, took his bowl, and entered the city of Shravasti to beg for alms. At that time, there was a woman in labor with great difficulty. Seeing this, he thought, "The suffering of sentient beings is indeed great, and the suffering of bearing a fetus is endless." At that time, after Angulimala had eaten, he put away his robe and bowl, put his sitting mat on his shoulder, went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at the feet, and sat to one side. At that time, Angulimala said to the World-Honored One, "Just now, I put on my robe, took my bowl, and entered the city of Shravasti to beg for alms. I saw a woman with a heavy pregnancy. At that time, I thought, 'The suffering of sentient beings is indeed great.'"

到时，著衣持钵，入舍卫城乞食。是时，有妇女临产甚难，见已，便作是念：「众生类极为苦痛，受胎无限。」是时，鸯掘魔食后，收摄衣钵，以尼师檀著肩上，往至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。尔时，鸯掘魔白世尊言：「我向著衣持钵，入舍卫城乞食，见一妇人身体重妊。是时，我便作是念：『众生受苦何至于斯？』」

The World-Honored One said, "Now go to that woman and say, 'Since I was born from the sages, I have never killed any living being.' With this sincere statement, may the fetus in the mother be unharmed."

世尊告曰：「汝今往彼妇人所，而作是说：『我从贤圣生已来，未曾杀生。』持此至诚之言，使此母人胎得无他。」

Angulimala replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

鸯掘魔对曰：「如是。世尊！」

At that time, Angulimala put on his robe, took his alms bowl, entered the city of Shravasti, went to the woman, and said to her, "Since I was born from the sages, I have never killed any living being. With this sincere statement, may the fetus be freed." At that time, the fetus in the woman's womb was freed.

是时，鸯掘魔即其日，著衣持钵，入舍卫城，往至彼母人所，语彼母人曰：「我从贤圣生已来，更不杀生。持此至诚之言，使胎得解脱。」是时，母人胎即得解脱。

At that time, Angulimala was begging for alms in the city. The men, women, and children saw him and said to each other, "This is Angulimala, who has killed countless beings. Now he is begging for alms in the city.

是时，鸯掘魔城中乞食，诸男女大小见之，各各自相谓言：「此名鸯掘魔，杀害众生不可称计，今复在城中乞食。」

At that time, the people in the city each threw tiles and stones at him, or cut him with knives. His head and eyes were injured, his clothes were torn, and blood flowed and stained his body. He then left the city of Shravasti and went to where the Tathāgata was. At that time, the World Honored One saw from afar that Angulimala's head and eyes were injured, and his clothes were stained with blood. Having seen this, he said, "You should endure this now. The reason is that this sin should be endured for a long time.

是时，城中人民，各各以瓦石打者，或有以刀斫者，伤坏头目，衣裳裂尽，流血污体，即出舍卫城至如来所。是时，世尊遥见鸯掘魔头目伤破，流血污衣而来，见已，便作是说：「汝今忍之。所以然者，此罪乃应永劫受之。」

At that time, Angulimala came to where the World Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. At that time, Angulimala spoke this verse in front of the Tathāgata:

是时，鸯掘魔至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。尔时，鸯掘魔在如来前，便说此偈：

Firmly listen to the Dharma, firmly practice the Buddha's teachings, and be
firm in befriending good friends, then one will attain the state of
extinction. I was once a great bandit named Angulimala, drifting in the
current, but was rescued by the Venerable One. Now I observe the
practice of taking refuge, and also observe the basis of the Dharma
Now I have attained the three insights and accomplished the Buddha's practice
I was originally named Harmless, but I killed countless beings. Now I
am truly called Truthful, not harming anyone. Even if one's body, speech
and mind are without any thought of harming, this is called non-harming
let alone giving rise to thoughts. An archer can tune his bow
a waterman can tame water, a skilled craftsman can tame wood, and
a wise person can tame his own body. Some are subdued by
whips and sticks, some are subdued by words, but I have never
used weapons. Now I have subdued myself. If one commits evil in
the past but stops and does not commit it again, this illuminates
the world, like the moon emerging from the clouds. If one commits
evil in the past but stops and does not commit it again
this illuminates the world, like the sun emerging from the clouds. Whether
a monk is old, young, or strong, practicing the Buddha's teachings, this
illuminates the world, like the moon emerging from the clouds. Whether a
monk is old, young, or strong, practicing the Buddha's teachings, this illuminates
this world, like the sun emerging from the clouds. Now I experience
little pain, and know contentment in eating and drinking. I have completely
escaped all suffering, and the past causes have now ended. I will
no longer experience the traces of death, nor do I delight in
life. Now I am just waiting for the right time, joyfully and without confusion.

「坚固听法句，　　坚固行佛法，坚固亲善友，　　便成灭尽处。我本为大贼，　　名曰鸯掘魔，为流之所漂，　　蒙尊拔济之。今观自归业，　　亦当观法本，今以逮三明，　　成就佛行业。我本名无害，　　杀害不可计，今名真谛实，　　不害于一切。设复身口意，　　都无害心识，此名无杀害，　　何况起思想。弓师能调角，　　水人能调水，巧匠调其木，　　智者自调身。或以鞭杖伏，　　或以言语屈，竟不加刀杖，　　今我自降伏。人前为过恶，　　后止不复犯，是照于世间，　　如云消月现。人前为过恶，　　后止不复犯，是照于世间，　　如云消日现。比丘老少壮，　　修行佛法行，是照于世间，　　如彼月云消。比丘老少壮，　　修行佛法者，是照此世间，　　如彼日云消。我今受痛少，　　饮食自知足，尽脱一切苦，　　本缘今已尽。更不受死迹，　　亦复不乐生，今正待时节，　　欢喜而不乱。」

At that time, the Tathāgata approved of what Angulimala said. Seeing that the Tathāgata had approved, Angulimala immediately rose from his seat, bowed at the feet of the World Honored One, and then withdrew.

是时，如来可鸯掘魔所说。是时，鸯掘魔以见如来然可之，即从座起，礼世尊足，便退而去。

At that time, the bhikṣus addressed the World-Honored One, saying, "What meritorious deeds did Angulimāla perform in the past, that he is now intelligent and wise, with a handsome face, rare in the world? What unwholesome actions did he commit, that he has now killed countless beings? What meritorious deeds did he perform, that he has now encountered the Tathāgata and attained the path of arahantship?"

是时，诸比丘白世尊言：「鸯掘魔本作何功德，今日聪明智慧，面目端政，世之希有？复作何不善行，于今身上，杀害生类不可称计？复作何功德，于今值如来，得阿罗汉道？」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Long ago, in the past, in this Bhadrakalpa, there was a Buddha named Kāśyapa Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, who appeared in the world. After Kāśyapa Tathāgata passed away, there was a king named Mahāphala who ruled over the kingdom and governed Jambudvīpa. At that time, that king had eighty-four thousand palace ladies and concubines, each without any children. At that time, King Mahāphala made offerings to the tree spirits, mountain spirits, sun, moon, stars, and all around, wishing for sons and daughters. At that time, the king's chief queen became pregnant. After eight or nine months, she gave birth to a son who was handsome and rare in the world. At that time, the king had this thought: 'I have been without any children for so long, and now I have finally given birth to a son. I should name him and let him enjoy the five desires.'

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「昔者，过去久远于此贤劫之中，有佛名迦叶如来、至真、等正觉，出现于世。迦叶如来去世之后，有王名大果，统领国界，典阎浮提。尔时，彼王有八万四千宫人婇女，各无儿息。尔时，大果王向诸树神、山神、日月、星宿，靡所不周，欲求男女。尔时，王第一夫人身即怀妊，经八、九月便生男儿，颜貌端政，世之希有。是时，彼王便生是念：『我本无有儿息，经尔许时，今方生儿，宜当立字，于五欲之中，而自娱乐。』

At that time, the king summoned all the ministers who were able to read physiognomy and said to them, 'Now that I have given birth to this child, I will name him.' At that time, the ministers, having heard the king's command, said to the king, 'This prince is extremely wonderful, handsome and incomparable, with a face like the color of a peach blossom. He will certainly have great power and strength. We should name him Mahābala.' At that time, the physiognomists had named the prince and each rose from their seats and left.

「是时，王召诸群臣能瞻相者，而告之曰：『我今以生此儿，各与立字。』是时，群臣闻王教已，即白王言：『今此太子极为奇妙，端政无比，面如桃华色，必当有大力势，今当立字名曰大力。』是时，相师与太子立字已，各从座起而去。

At that time, the king loved and pitied this prince, never leaving his presence. When the prince was about eight years old, he went with his ministers to his father's place to pay respects and ask about his well-being. The father king again thought, 'This prince is extremely extraordinary.' He then said to him, 'Now, how about I arrange a marriage for you?' The prince said to the king, 'I am still young, why get married?' At that time, the father king temporarily stopped and did not arrange a marriage for him. After another twenty years, the king again said, 'I want to arrange a marriage for you.' The prince said to the king, 'No need to arrange a marriage.' At that time, the father king told the ministers and people, 'I have been without any children for a long time, and only now have I given birth to a son. Now he is unwilling to take a wife, pure and flawless.' At that time, the prince's name was changed to Vimalakīrti.

「是时，国王爱慜此太子，未曾去目前。是时，太子年向八岁，将诸臣佐往父所，朝贺问讯。父王复作是念：『今此太子极自奇特。』即告之曰：『吾今与汝取妇何如乎？』太子白王：『子今年幼何须娉娶？』是时，父王权停不与取妇。复经二十岁，王复告曰：『吾欲与汝取妇。』太子白王：『不须取妇。』是时，父王告群臣人民曰：『我本无儿息，经历久远，方生一子，今不肯取妇，清净无瑕。』尔时，王太子转字名曰清净。

At that time, Prince Vimalakīrti was about thirty years old. The king again ordered the ministers, 'I am now old and weak, and have no other children. Now I only have Prince Vimalakīrti. Now the high position of the king should be passed on to the prince. But the prince does not enjoy the five desires. How can he govern the affairs of the country?' The ministers replied, 'We should use skillful means to make him enjoy the five desires.' At that time, the father king immediately struck the bell and drum, and ordered the people in the country, 'Whoever can make Prince Vimalakīrti enjoy the five desires, I will bestow upon them a thousand gold coins and various treasures.'

「是时，清净大子年向三十，王复勅群臣曰：『吾今年已衰微，更无儿息；今唯有清净太子，今王高位应授与太子。然太子不乐五欲之中，当云何理国事？』群臣报曰：『当为方便，使乐五欲。』是时，父王即椎钟鸣鼓，勅国中人：『其能使清净太子乐五欲者，吾当赐与千金及诸宝物。』

At that time, there was a woman named Rati, who was well-versed in the sixty-four arts of seduction. When that woman heard the king's order, 'Whoever can make the prince practice the five desires will be bestowed with a thousand gold coins and various treasures,' she went to the father king and said, 'If I can make the prince practice the five desires, please give me a thousand gold coins and various treasures.' The father king replied, 'If you can indeed do so, I will greatly reward you and not go back on my word.' The woman then asked the king, 'Where does the prince sleep?' The king replied, 'In the eastern hall. There are no women there, only a male attendant.' The woman said, 'I only wish that the great king would order the inner palace not to restrict me, and allow me to come and go as I please!'

「尔时，有女人名曰婬种，尽明六十四变。彼女人闻王有教令：『其能使王太子习五欲者，当赐与金千斤及诸宝物。』即往至父王所，而告之曰：『见与千金及诸宝物，能使王太子习于五欲。』父王报曰：『审能尔者，当重相赐，不负言信。』时婬女白王：『太子为寝宿何处？』王报曰：『在东堂上，无有女人，唯有一男儿，在彼侍卫。』女人白曰：『惟愿大王勅内宫中，勿见限遮，随意出入！』

At that time, the seductive woman immediately cried out twice during the night, by the side of the prince's door. When the prince heard the woman's crying, he ordered his attendant, 'Who is this crying here?' The attendant replied, 'It is a woman crying by the door.' The prince said, 'Go and ask why she is crying.' The attendant went and asked why she was crying. The seductive woman replied, 'My husband has abandoned me, that is why I am crying.' The attendant returned and reported to the prince, 'This woman is abandoned by her husband and fears thieves, that is why she is crying.' The prince said, 'Take this woman to the elephant stable.' When she arrived there, she cried again. He then took her to the horse stable, and she cried again. The prince again told his attendant, 'Bring her here.' He then took her into the hall, and she cried again. The prince himself asked, 'Why are you crying again?' The seductive woman replied, 'Prince! Women are single and weak, and are extremely fearful, that is why I am crying.' The prince said, 'Come up on my bed, you will be safe.' At that time, the woman remained silent, and did not cry either. Then the woman immediately took off her clothes, took the prince's hand, and placed it on her chest. She immediately startled him, and gradually aroused his desire. With the arising of desire, she then approached him.

「是时，婬女即其夜鼓二时，在太子门侧，佯举声哭。是时，太子闻女人哭声，便勅侍人曰：『此是何人于斯而哭？』侍人报曰：『此是女人在门侧哭。』太子告曰：『汝速往问所由哭耶？』时，彼侍臣往而问之所由哭耶？婬女报曰：『夫主见弃，是故哭耳。』侍臣还白太子：『此女人为夫主所弃，又畏盗贼，是故哭耳。』太子告曰：『将此女人著象廐中。』到彼复哭；复将至马廐中，复哭。太子复语侍臣：『将来在此。』即将入堂，复于中哭。太子躬自问曰：『何为复哭？』婬女报曰：『太子！女人单弱极怀恐怖，是故哭耳。』太子告曰：『上吾床上，可得无畏。』时，女人默然不语，亦复不哭。是时，女人即脱衣裳，前捉太子手，举著己胸上，即时惊觉，渐渐起欲想，以起欲心，便身就之。

At that time, Prince Vimalakīrti went to his father the king the next morning. When the king saw the prince's countenance, which was different from usual, he said, 'What you desire has been accomplished?' The prince replied, 'As the great king has said.' At that time, the father king was overjoyed and could not contain himself. He said, 'What wish do you seek? I shall grant it to you.' The prince replied, 'The wish granted, do not regret it later, I shall make my wish.' The king said, 'As you say, I will not regret it. What wish do you seek?' The prince said to the king, 'Great king! Today you rule over the entire Jambudvīpa, all freely. All the unmarried girls within the Jambudvīpa, first come to my house, and then be married.' The king said, 'As you say.' The king then ordered the people of the country, 'All the girls who have not left the house, first let them go to Prince Vimalakīrti, and then marry them.'

「是时，清净太子明日清旦，往父王所。是时，父王遥见太子颜色，殊于常日，见已，便作是说：『汝今所欲者，事果乎？』太子报曰：『如大王所言。』是时，父王欢喜踊跃，不能自胜，并作是说：『欲求何愿，吾当与之。』太子报曰：『所赐愿者，勿复中悔，当求其愿。』时王报曰：『如汝所言，终不中悔，欲求何愿？』太子白王：『大王！今日统领阎浮提内，皆悉自由；阎浮提里内诸未嫁女者，先适我家，然后使嫁。』是时王曰：『随汝所言。』王即勅国内人民之类曰：『诸有女未出门者，先使诣清净太子，然后嫁之。』

At that time, there was a girl named Sumana in the city, who was next to go to the king. At that time, the daughter of the Sumana householder walked naked and barefoot among the people, without any shame. When the people saw her, they talked to each other, 'This is the daughter of the householder, whose reputation is widely known. How can she walk naked among people, no different from a donkey?' The girl said to the people, 'I am not a donkey, you people are donkeys. Have you ever seen a woman who is ashamed of another woman? All the beings in the city are women, only Prince Vimalakīrti is a man! If I go to Prince Vimalakīrti's door, I will put on clothes.' At that time, the people in the city said to each other, 'What this girl says really enters our minds. We are indeed women, not men. Only Prince Vimalakīrti is a man. We should now practice the ways of men.'

「尔时，彼城中有女名须蛮，次应至王所。是时，须蛮长者女露形倮跣在众人中行，亦无羞耻。众人见已，各相对谈：『此是长者女，名称远闻。云何露形在人中行？如驴何异？』女报众人曰：『我非为驴，汝等众人斯是驴耳。汝等颇见女人还见女人有相耻乎？城中生类尽是女人，唯有清净太子是男子矣！若我至清净太子门者，当著衣裳。』是时，城中人民自相谓言：『此女所说诚入我意，我等实是女，非男也。唯有清净太子乃是男也。我等今日当行男子之法。』

At that time, the people in the city each prepared their weapons, put on armor and held staffs, went to the father king, and said to the father king, 'We seek two wishes, please allow them.' The king said, 'What are the two wishes?' The people said to the king, 'If the king wants to live, he should kill Prince Vimalakīrti; if the son wants to live, he should now kill the king. We cannot bear to serve Prince Vimalakīrti and disgrace the country's laws.'

「是时，城中人民各办战具，著铠持杖，往至父王所，白父王曰：『欲求二愿，唯见听许。』王报之曰：『何等二愿？』人民白王：『王欲存者当杀清净太子；子欲存者今当杀王。我等不堪任承事清净太子辱国常法。』

At that time, the father king spoke this verse:

「是时，父王便说此偈：

For the sake of the family, one forgets one person; for the sake of the village, one forgets one family
for the sake of the country, one forgets one village; for the sake of the body, one forgets the world.

「为家忘一人，　　为村忘一家，为国忘一村，　　为身忘世间。

At that time, the father king, having spoken this verse, said to the people: 'Now is the right time, according to your wishes.' At that time, the people took Prince Vimalakīrti and bound his hands, taking him to outside the city, each saying to each other: 'We should all together strike and kill him with tiles and stones. Why should one person kill him?'

「是时，父王说此偈已，告人民曰：『今正是时，随汝等意。』是时，诸人将清净太子取两手缚之，将诣城外，各相谓言：『我等咸共以瓦石打杀，何须一人杀乎？』

At that time, when Prince Vimalakīrti was about to die, he said: 'I also make a vow. The people who unjustly kill me, but my father king granted me a wish. I now accept death without daring to refuse. May I repay this enmity in the future. May I also meet a true arhat and quickly attain liberation.' At that time, the people took the prince and killed him, then each went their separate ways.

「是时，清净太子临欲死时，而作是说：『又作誓愿，诸人民取吾抂杀，然父王自与我愿，我今受死亦不敢辞。使我将来之世，当报此怨。又使值真人罗汉，速得解脱。』是时，人民取太子杀已，各自散去。

"Bhikṣus! Do not view it thus. The great fruit king of that time is none other than Aṅgulimāla. The courtesan of that time is now his wife. The people of that time are now the eighty thousand people who died. The pure prince of that time is now the bhikṣu Aṅgulimāla. At the time of death, he made this vow. Now he has repaid the enmity, and none can escape his hand. Due to this cause and condition, he has killed countless people. Later, he made a vow to meet the Buddha, and now he has attained liberation and become an arhat. This is the meaning. You should remember and practice it."

「诸比丘！莫作是观，尔时大果王者，岂异人乎？今鸯掘魔师是也。尔时婬女者，今师妇是也。尔时人民者，今八万人民死者是也。尔时清净太子，今鸯掘魔比丘是也。临欲死时作是誓愿，今还报怨无免手者。缘此因缘，杀害无限，后作誓愿，愿欲值佛，今得解脱，成阿罗汉。此是其义，当念奉行。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus: "Among my disciples, the one with the most intelligent and swift wisdom is none other than the bhikṣu Aṅgulimāla."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「我弟子中，第一聪明捷疾智者，所谓鸯掘魔比丘是也。」

At that time, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
