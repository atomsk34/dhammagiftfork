EĀ 39.1

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

Chapter 39 等法品第三十九

39.1 （一）等法

Parallel to: AN 7.68 / MĀ 1

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "If a bhikṣu accomplishes seven dharmas, he will experience endless happiness in this life, and if he wishes to exhaust the taints, he will be able to attain them. What are the seven dharmas? Here, a bhikṣu knows the Dharma, knows the meaning, knows the time, is also able to know himself, is also able to know contentment, is also able to know how to enter the assembly, and observes the people in the assembly. These are called the seven dharmas.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「若有比丘成就七法者，于现法中受乐无穷，欲得尽漏，便能获之。云何为七法？于是，比丘知法、知义、知时，又能自知，复能知足，亦复知入众中，观察众人，是谓七法。

What is it that a bhikṣu knows about the Dharma? Here, a bhikṣu knows the Dharma, namely the sūtras, geya, gāthās, nidāna, upamā, abhuta, vaipulya, adbhuta, vaipulya, vyākaraṇa, and jātaka. If a bhikṣu does not know the Dharma, does not know the twelve divisions of the canon, he is not a bhikṣu. Because a bhikṣu is able to understand the Dharma, it is called knowing the Dharma. Thus, a bhikṣu understands the Dharma.

「云何比丘知法？于是，比丘知法，所谓契经、祇夜、偈、因缘、譬喻、本末、广演、方等、未曾有、广普、授决、生经。若有比丘不知法者，不知十二部经，此非比丘也。以其比丘能解了法故，名为知法。如是，比丘解了于法。

What is it that a bhikṣu understands about the meaning? Here, a bhikṣu knows the Tathāgata's intention and understands the profound meaning without any doubts. If a bhikṣu does not understand the meaning, he is not a bhikṣu. Because a bhikṣu is able to know the profound meaning, it is called understanding the meaning. Thus, a bhikṣu is able to discern the meaning.

「云何比丘解了于义？于是，比丘知如来机趣，解了深义，无所疑难。若有比丘不解了义者，此非比丘也。以其比丘能知深义故，名为解义也。如是，比丘能分别义。

What is it that a bhikṣu knows about the appropriate time? Here, a bhikṣu knows the appropriate time, knowing when to cultivate insight and when to cultivate tranquility, knowing when to be silent and when to act, knowing when to recite and when to teach others. If a bhikṣu does not know this, does not know the appropriateness of tranquility, insight, progress, and rest, he is not a bhikṣu. If a bhikṣu knows the appropriate time and does not miss the appropriate time, this is called following the appropriate time. Thus, a bhikṣu knows the appropriate time.

「云何比丘知其时宜？于是，比丘知其时节，可修观时便修观，可修止时便修止，可默知默，可行知行，可诵知诵，可授前人便授前人，可语知语。若有比丘不知此者，不知止观进止之宜，此非比丘。若复比丘知其时节，不失时宜，此名为随其方宜。如是，比丘知其时宜。

What is it that a bhikṣu is able to cultivate himself? Here, a bhikṣu is able to know himself: 'I now have this seeing, hearing, thinking, and knowing, and have such wisdom. My walking and moving always follow the true Dharma.' If a bhikṣu is unable to know the appropriateness of wisdom, coming and going, and walking, he is not a bhikṣu. Because a bhikṣu is able to cultivate himself and the appropriateness of his progress and rest, this is called cultivating his own practice. This is called a bhikṣu being able to know himself.

「云何比丘自能修己？于是，比丘能自知己：『我今有此见闻念知，有如是智慧，行步进止，恒随正法。』若有比丘不能自知智慧之宜，出入行来，此非比丘也。以其比丘能自修己进止之宜，此名为自修己行，是谓比丘能自知己。

What is it that a bhikṣu knows about contentment? Here, a bhikṣu is able to measure and know the appropriateness of sleep, wakefulness, sitting, lying down, walking, and progress. He is able to know contentment. If a bhikṣu is unable to know this, he is not a bhikṣu. Because a bhikṣu is able to understand this, it is called contentment. Thus, a bhikṣu is called content.

「云何比丘自知止足？于是，比丘能自筹量睡眠、觉寤、坐卧、经行、进止之宜，皆能知止足。若有比丘不能知是者，则非比丘也。以其比丘能解了此故，名为知足。如是，比丘名为知足。

What is it that a bhikṣu knows about entering a great assembly? Here, a bhikṣu distinguishes the great assembly: 'This is the kṣatriya class, this is the brāhmaṇa class, this is the householder class, this is the śramaṇa class. I should use this Dharma to suit that assembly, whether speaking or silent, I know it all.' If a bhikṣu does not know how to enter the assembly, he is not a bhikṣu. Because a bhikṣu knows how to enter the great assembly, it is called knowing how to enter the assembly. This is called a bhikṣu knowing how to enter the great assembly.

「云何比丘知入大众？于是，比丘分别大众，此是刹利种，此是婆罗门众，此是长者众，此是沙门众，我当以此法宜则适彼众中，可语可默，皆悉知之。若有比丘不知入众，此非比丘。以其比丘知入大众故，名为知入众也。是谓比丘知入大众也。

What is it that a bhikṣu knows about the origins of the people? A bhikṣu should know that there are two kinds of people. What are the two? There is one person who wishes to go to the garden to visit the bhikṣus, while the other person does not like to go there to see the bhikṣus. The person who wishes to go to the garden to visit the bhikṣus is the superior one.

「云何比丘知众人根元？比丘当知，有二人。云何为二？彼或有一人，欲往至园中亲觐比丘；彼第二人不喜至彼观见比丘。彼人欲至园中亲觐比丘者，此人最为上。

Bhikṣus! There are also two kinds of people. What are the two? One person may go to where the bhikṣus are, but does not ask about what is appropriate. The other person also does not go to the temple to see the bhikṣus. The person who goes to the temple is the best.

「比丘！复有二人。云何为二？彼一人虽至比丘所，然不问其宜；彼第二人亦不往至寺中见比丘。彼至寺人最为第一。

Bhikṣus! There are also two kinds of people. What are the two? One person goes to where the bhikṣus are and asks about what is appropriate. The other person does not go to where the bhikṣus are to ask about what is appropriate. The person who goes to the temple is the most honored and foremost, surpassing the other person.

「比丘！复有二人。云何为二？彼一人至比丘所问讯时宜；彼第二人不至比丘所问讯时宜。彼人至寺者，最尊第一，出彼人上。

Bhikṣus! There are also two kinds of people. What are the two? One person goes to where the bhikṣus are and listens to the Dharma with utmost sincerity. The other person does not go to where the bhikṣus are and does not listen to the Dharma with utmost sincerity. The person who listens to the Dharma with utmost sincerity is the best among those people.

「比丘！复有二人。云何为二？彼一人至比丘所，至心听法；彼第二人不至比丘所，不至心听法。彼至心听法者，于彼人最为第一。

Bhikṣus! There are also two kinds of people. What are the two? One person is able to contemplate the Dharma and uphold and recite it. The other person is unable to uphold and recite it. The person who upholds and recites the Dharma is the best among those people.

「比丘！复有二人。云何为二？彼有一人能观察法，受持讽诵；彼第二人，不能受持讽诵。彼人受持讽诵者，于此人上最为第一。

Bhikṣus! There are also two kinds of people. What are the two? One person hears the Dharma and understands its meaning. The other person hears the Dharma but does not understand its meaning. The person who hears the Dharma and understands its meaning is the most honored and foremost among those people.

「比丘！复有二人。云何为二？彼有一人，闻法解其义；彼第二人，闻法不解其义。彼人闻法解义者，于此人最尊第一。

Bhikṣus! There are also two kinds of people. What are the two? One person hears the Dharma and attains the Dharma. The other person does not hear the Dharma and does not attain the Dharma. The person who hears the Dharma and attains the Dharma is the foremost among those people.

「比丘！复有二人。云何为二？彼有一人闻法，法成就；彼第二人不闻法，法不成就。彼人法法成就者，于此人第一。

"Bhikṣus! There are also two kinds of people. What are the two? One person hears the Dharma and is able to practice with endurance, to uphold and protect the true Dharma. The other person is unable to practice with endurance. The person who is able to practice the Dharma is the most honored and foremost among those people. It is like how milk has curds, curds have butter, and butter has ghee, which is the best and unsurpassed. It is the same here. If there is a person who is able to practice, that person is the best and unsurpassed. This is called a bhikṣu observing the faculties of people. If there is a person who does not understand this, they are not a bhikṣu. Because a bhikṣu who hears the Dharma and discerns its meaning is the best. Thus a bhikṣu observes the faculties of people. If there is a bhikṣu who accomplishes seven dharmas, he will be happy and without afflictions in this present life, and will have no doubt in cutting off afflictions. Therefore, bhikṣus! You should seek the means to accomplish these seven dharmas. Thus, bhikṣus! You should train in this way."

「比丘！复有二人。云何为二？彼一人闻法能堪忍修行，分别护持正法；第二人不能堪忍修行其法。彼能修行法者，于此诸人最尊第一。犹如牛有酪，由酪有酥，由酥有醍醐，最为第一，无能及者。此亦如是，若有人能修行者，此人最为第一，无能及者，是谓比丘观察人根。若有人不了此者，则非比丘也。以其比丘闻法分别其义者，此为最上。如是比丘观察人根。若有比丘成就七法者，于现法中快乐无为，意欲断漏亦无有疑。是故，比丘！当求方便，成此七法。如是，比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said, and joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
