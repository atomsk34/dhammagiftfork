EĀ 39.2

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

39.2 （二）昼度

Parallel to: AN 7.69 / MĀ 2

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "The Cāpāla tree of the Thirty-three Heavens is fifty yojanas long and wide, and a hundred yojanas high. It provides shade for fifty yojanas to the east, west, south, and north. The gods of the Thirty-three Heavens enjoy themselves there for four months. Bhikṣus, you should know that there may come a time when the flowers and leaves of that Cāpāla tree wither and fall to the ground. When the gods see this auspicious sign, they all rejoice and feel joy within, thinking, 'This tree will soon grow flowers and fruits again.'

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「三十三天昼度树，本纵广五十由旬，高百由旬，东、西、南、北荫覆五十由旬，三十三天在彼四月自相娱乐。比丘当知，或有是时，彼昼度树华叶凋落，萎黄在地。尔时，诸天见此瑞应，普怀欢喜，欣情内发：『此树不久当更生华实。』

Bhikṣus, you should know that there may come a time when the flowers and fruits of that tree all wither and fall to the ground. At that time, the gods of the Thirty-three Heavens will be even more joyful, and they will say to each other, 'This tree will soon turn gray.'

「比丘当知，或有是时，彼树华实皆悉凋落，捐弃在地。是时，三十三天倍复欢喜，自相谓言：『此树不久当作灰色。』

Bhikṣus, you should know that after some time, that tree will turn gray. At that time, the gods of the Thirty-three Heavens will see that the tree has turned gray, and they will be very joyful, saying to each other, 'This tree has now turned gray. It will soon grow a net.' At that time, the gods of the Thirty-three Heavens will see that the Cāpāla tree has already grown a net, and it will soon grow hail nodes. At that time, the gods of the Thirty-three Heavens will see this and again feel joyful, thinking, 'This tree has now grown hail nodes. It will soon bloom again.'

「比丘当知，复经历数时，彼树便作灰色。是时，三十三天已见此树而作灰色，甚怀喜悦，自相谓言：『而今此树已作灰色，不久当生罗网。』是时，三十三天见此昼度树已生罗网，不久当生雹节。尔时，三十三天见已，复怀欢喜：『此树今日已生雹节，不久当复开敷。』

Bhikṣus, you should know that the gods of the Thirty-three Heavens will see that this tree gradually blooms, and they will each be joyful, thinking, 'This tree will soon be fully in bloom.'

「比丘当知，三十三天已见此树渐渐开敷，各怀欢喜：『此树不久渐渐开敷，不久当尽著华。』

Bhikṣus, you should know that there will be a time when that tree is fully in bloom. They will all be joyful, thinking, 'This tree is now fully in bloom.' At that time, the fragrance of that tree will be smelled within a hundred yojanas against the wind. At that time, the gods will enjoy themselves for four months, their happiness beyond measure.

「比丘当知，或有是时，彼树普悉开敷，皆怀欢喜：『此树今日皆悉著华。』尔时，此香逆风，百由旬内无不闻香者。尔时，诸天四月之中于彼自相娱乐，乐不可计。

It is the same with this. When a noble disciple wishes to leave home to study the path, it is like that tree beginning to wither.

「此亦如是，若贤圣弟子意欲出家学道时，如似彼树始欲凋落。

Furthermore, when a noble disciple renounces wife and wealth, with firm faith, leaves home to study the path, shaves their beard and hair, it is like the leaves falling to the ground.

「复次，贤圣弟子捐弃妻财，以信坚固，出家学道，剃除须发，如似彼树叶落在地。

Bhikkhus, you should know that when a noble disciple has no thoughts of desire, eliminates unwholesome dharmas, joyfully upholds mindfulness, and abides in the first dhyāna, it is like the Dhava tree turning gray.

「比丘当知，若贤圣弟子无贪欲想，除不善法，念持欢喜，游志一禅，似彼昼度树而作灰色。

Furthermore, when a noble disciple has initial and sustained thought, inner joy, and one-pointedness of mind, without initial and sustained thought, abiding in the second dhyāna, it is like the tree growing a net.

「复次，贤圣弟子有觉、有观息，内有欢喜，专其一心，无觉、无观、游心二禅，如似彼树而生罗网。

Furthermore, when a noble disciple is mindful and has protection, experiences physical bliss, is protected by the noble ones, and fully abides in the third dhyāna, it is like the tree growing hail.

「复次，贤圣弟子念而有护，自觉身有乐，诸贤圣所救，护念具足，游在三禅，如似彼树而生雹节。

Furthermore, when a noble disciple has exhausted suffering and happiness, is free from sorrow, without suffering or happiness, and abides in the fourth dhyāna with pure mindfulness, it is like the tree gradually blooming.

「复次，贤圣弟子苦乐已尽，先无愁忧，无苦无乐，护念清净，游志四禅，如似彼树渐渐开敷。

Furthermore, when a noble disciple has exhausted the taints, attained the liberation of the mind and liberation through wisdom, and delights in the present life, knowing that birth and death are exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done is done, and one will not again experience birth, it is like the tree fully blooming.

「复次，贤圣弟子尽有漏，成无漏心解脱、智慧解脱，现法中而自娱乐：生死已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，更不复受胎，如实知之。如似彼树皆悉敷花。

At that time, the noble disciple's fragrance of virtuous qualities was widely known in the four directions, and none praised it. For four months, he delighted in himself, his mind wandering in the four dhyānas, fully practicing the basis. Therefore, bhikṣus! One should seek means to accomplish the fragrance of virtuous qualities. Thus, bhikṣus! One should practice in this way.

「是时，贤圣弟子戒德之香，遍闻四远，无不称誉者，四月之中而自娱乐，游心四禅，具足行本。是故，诸比丘！当求方便，成戒德之香。如是，比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
