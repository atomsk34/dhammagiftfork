EĀ 39.6

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

39.6 （六）均头

Parallel to: SN 46.16 (~) / SĀ 727 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Upavāna was lying in bed with a serious illness, unable to get up on his own. He thought, "The Tathāgata World-Honored One does not show compassion today. I have a serious illness and my life is not long. Medicine and treatment do not help. I have also heard the World-Honored One say, 'I will not abandon until one person is liberated.' But now I am seen as abandoned. How can I bear this suffering?"

当于尔时，尊者均头身抱重患，卧在床褥，不能自起居。是时，均头便念：「如来世尊今日不见垂愍，又遭重患，命在不久，医药不接。又闻世尊言：『一人不度，吾终不舍。』然今独见遗弃，将何苦哉！」

At that time, the World-Honored One heard Venerable Upavāna's lament with his divine ear. The World-Honored One then told the bhikṣus, "All of you gather together and go to Venerable Upavāna to inquire about his illness."

尔时，世尊以天耳闻均头比丘作是称怨。是时，世尊告诸比丘：「汝等皆集至均头比丘所，问其所疾。」

The bhikṣus replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

诸比丘对曰：「如是。世尊！」

The World-Honored One gradually approached Venerable Upavāna's room with many bhikṣus. At that time, Upavāna saw the Tathāgata coming from afar and immediately threw himself to the ground. The World-Honored One then said to Upavāna, "Your illness is very serious now. You don't need to get off the bed. I have a seat myself."

世尊将众多比丘渐渐至均头比丘房。是时，均头遥见如来来，即自投地。尔时，世尊告均头曰：「汝今抱患极为笃重，不须下床，吾自有坐。」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to Upavāna, "Does your illness increase or decrease? Is it not increasing or decreasing? Can you bear to receive my teaching?"

尔时，世尊告均头曰：「汝所患为增为损，不增损乎？有能堪任受吾教也？」

At that time, the bhikṣu Upavāna said to the Buddha, "The illness of this disciple today is extremely serious. It only increases and does not decrease. I have tried all kinds of medicinal herbs."

是时，均头比丘白佛言：「弟子今日所患极笃，但有增无损也。所服药草，靡不周遍。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Who is attending to the sick?"

世尊问曰：「视瞻病者竟为是谁？」

Upavāna said, "The practitioners of the Brahma-way come to see and attend to him."

均头白言：「诸梵行来见瞻视。」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to Upavāna, "Can you now speak to me about the seven factors of awakening?"

尔时，世尊告均头曰：「汝今堪与吾说七觉意乎？」

At that time, Upavāna thrice proclaimed the names of the seven factors of awakening, saying, "I am now able to speak about the Dharma of the seven factors of awakening in the presence of the Tathāgata."

均头是时，三自称说七觉意名：「我今堪任于如来前说七觉意法。」

The World-Honored One said, "If you are able to speak to the Tathāgata, then speak now."

世尊告曰：「若能堪任向如来说，今便说之。」

At that time, Upavāna addressed the Buddha, saying, "As for the seven factors of awakening, what are the seven? Namely, the factor of awakening of mindfulness, as taught by the Tathāgata, the factor of awakening of the Dharma, the factor of awakening of diligence, the factor of awakening of joy, the factor of awakening of tranquility, the factor of awakening of concentration, and the factor of awakening of equanimity. These, World-Honored One, are said to be the seven factors of awakening, and they are indeed so."

是时，均头白佛言：「七觉意者，何等为七？所谓念觉意，如来之所说，法觉意、精进觉意、喜觉意、猗觉意、定觉意、护觉意。是谓，世尊！有此七觉意者，正谓此耳。」

At that time, after the Venerable Upavāna had spoken these words, all his illnesses were completely cured and he was free from afflictions. Then Upavāna addressed the World-Honored One, saying, "Among medicines, the most excellent is the Dharma of the seven factors of awakening. If one speaks of the most excellent among medicines, it is none other than these seven factors of awakening. Now, contemplating these seven factors of awakening, all illnesses are completely cured."

尔时，尊者均头说此语已，所有疾患，皆悉除愈，无有众恼。是时，均头白世尊言：「药中之盛，所谓此七觉意之法是也。欲言药中之盛者，不过此七觉意，今思惟此七觉意，所有众病皆悉除愈。」

At that time, the World-Honored One addressed the bhikṣus, saying, "You should uphold this Dharma of the seven factors of awakening, recite it well, and have no doubts about the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṃgha. The illnesses of those sentient beings will all be cured. The reason is that these seven factors of awakening are very difficult to understand. They comprehend all dharmas, illuminate all dharmas, and are like good medicine to cure all illnesses. They are like nectar, inexhaustible when eaten. If one does not attain these seven factors of awakening, sentient beings will wander in birth and death. Bhikṣus! One should seek skillful means to cultivate the seven factors of awakening. Thus, bhikṣus! You should train in this way."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「汝等受持此七觉意法，善念讽诵，勿有狐疑于佛、法、众者，彼众生类所有疾患皆悉除愈。所以然者，此七觉意甚难晓了，一切诸法皆悉了知，照明一切诸法，亦如良药疗治一切众病，犹如甘露食无厌足。若不得此七觉意者，众生之类流转生死。诸比丘！当求方便，修七觉意。如是，诸比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and upheld it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
