EĀ 40.3

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

40.3 （三）

Parallel to: AN 7.11–12 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "I will now explain the seven fetters. You should all contemplate them well."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「我今当说七使，汝等善思念之。」

The bhikṣus replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!" At that time, the bhikṣus received the Buddha's instruction.

诸比丘对曰：「如是。世尊！」是时，诸比丘从佛受教。

The World-Honored One said, "What are the seven? First, the fetter of desire, second, the fetter of anger, third, the fetter of pride, fourth, the fetter of delusion, fifth, the fetter of doubt, sixth, the fetter of views, and seventh, the fetter of the desire realm. These are called, bhikṣus, the seven fetters that bind sentient beings, causing them to dwell in darkness for a long time, entangled and bound, wandering in the world without rest, unable to know the root of birth and death. It is like two oxen, one black and one white, yoked together, pulling each other, unable to be apart. Sentient beings are also like this, bound by the fetters of desire and ignorance, unable to be separated. The remaining five fetters also follow. When the five fetters follow, the seven fetters are also so. If ordinary people are bound by these seven fetters, they wander in birth and death, unable to be liberated, unable to know the origin of suffering.

世尊告曰：「云何为七？一者贪欲使，二者瞋恚使，三者憍慢使，四者痴使，五者疑使，六者见使，七者欲世间使。是谓，比丘！有此七使，使众生之类，永处幽暗，缠结其身，流转世间，无有休息，亦不能知生死根原。犹如彼二牛，一黑一白，共同一轭，共相牵引，不得相远。此众生类，亦复如是，为此贪欲使、无明使所缠结，不得相离，其余五使，亦复追从，五使适从，七使亦然。若凡夫之人，为此七使所缚，流转生死，不得解脱，不能知苦之元本。

Bhikṣus, you should know that because of these seven fetters, there are three evil destinies: hell, animal realm, and hungry ghosts. Because of these seven fetters, one cannot transcend the realm of Māra. However, there are also seven remedies for these seven fetters. What are the seven? For the fetter of desire, the remedy is the mindfulness factor of awakening; for the fetter of anger, the remedy is the Dharma discernment factor of awakening; for the fetter of wrong views, the remedy is the vigor factor of awakening; for the fetter of the desire realm, the remedy is the joy factor of awakening; for the fetter of pride, the remedy is the tranquility factor of awakening; for the fetter of doubt, the remedy is the concentration factor of awakening; for the fetter of ignorance, the remedy is the equanimity factor of awakening. These are called, bhikṣus, the seven remedies for the seven fetters using the seven factors of awakening.

「比丘当知，由此七使，便有三恶趣：地狱、畜生、饿鬼；由此七使，不能得度弊魔境界。然此七使之法复有七药。云何为七？贪欲使者，念觉意治之；瞋恚使者，法觉意治之；邪见使者，精进觉意治之；欲世间使者，喜觉意治之；憍慢使者，猗觉意治之；疑使者，定觉意治之；无明使者，护觉意治之。是谓，比丘！此七使用七觉意治之。

"Bhikṣus, you should know that when I had not yet attained Buddhahood, as a bodhisattva, sitting under the bodhi tree, I gave rise to this thought: 'What are the sentient beings of the desire realm bound to?' I further thought: 'These sentient beings are bound by the seven fetters, wandering in birth and death, never attaining liberation. I am also bound by these seven fetters, unable to be liberated.' At that time, I further thought: 'What should be used to treat these seven fetters?' I further contemplated: 'These seven fetters should be treated with the seven factors of awakening. I should contemplate the seven factors of awakening.' When I contemplated the seven factors of awakening, my defiled mind was exhausted, and I attained liberation. Later, I attained the unsurpassed perfect true path. For seven days, I sat cross-legged and repeatedly contemplated these seven factors of awakening. Therefore, bhikṣus, if you wish to abandon the seven fetters, you should contemplate and practice the seven factors of awakening. Thus, bhikṣus, you should train in this way."

「比丘当知，我本未成佛道为菩萨行，坐道树下，便生斯念：『欲界众生为何等所系？』复作是念：『此众生类为七使流转生死，永不得解；我今亦为此七使所系，不得解脱。』尔时，复作是念：『此七使为用何治之？』复重思惟：『此七使者当用七觉意治之，我当思惟七觉意。』思惟七觉意时，有漏心尽，便得解脱，后成无上正真之道，七日之中结跏趺坐，重思惟此七觉意。是故，诸比丘！若欲舍七使者，当念修行七觉意法。如是，诸比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
