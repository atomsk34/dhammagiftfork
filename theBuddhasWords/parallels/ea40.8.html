EĀ 40.8

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

40.8 （八）

Parallel to: AN 6.19 / AN 8.73 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "You should practice the thought of death and contemplate the thought of death."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「汝等当修行死想，思惟死想。」

At that time, a bhikṣu on the seat said to the World-Honored One, "I always practice and contemplate the thought of death."

时，彼座上有一比丘白世尊言：「我常修行、思惟死想。」

The World-Honored One said, "How do you contemplate and practice the thought of death?"

世尊告曰：「汝云何思惟、修行死想？」

The bhikṣu said to the Buddha, "When contemplating the thought of death, I wish to live for seven days, contemplating the seven factors of awakening, which will greatly benefit the Tathāgata's teachings, and have no regrets after death. Thus, World-Honored One, I contemplate the thought of death."

比丘白佛言：「思惟死想时，意欲存七日，思惟七觉意，于如来法中多所饶益，死后无恨。如是，世尊！我思惟死想。」

The World-Honored One said, "Stop! Stop! Bhikṣu! This is not the practice of contemplating death, this is called the Dharma of negligence."

世尊告曰：「止！止！比丘！此非行死想之行，此名为放逸之法。」

Another bhikṣu said to the World-Honored One, "I am able to endure and practice the thought of death."

复有一比丘白世尊言：「我能堪任修行死想。」

The World-Honored One said, "How do you practice and contemplate the thought of death?"

世尊告曰：「汝云何修行、思惟死想？」

The bhikṣu said to the Buddha, "I now think like this: 'I wish to exist for six days, contemplate the Tathāgata's true Dharma, and then take my life. This will be of great benefit.' This is how I contemplate the thought of death."

比丘白佛言：「我今作是念：『意欲存在六日，思惟如来正法已，便取命终，此则有所增益。』如是思惟死想。」

The World-Honored One said, "Stop! Stop! Bhikṣu! That is also a Dharma of indulgence, not contemplating the thought of death."

世尊告曰：「止！止！比丘！汝亦是放逸之法，非思惟死想也。」

Another bhikṣu said to the Buddha, "I wish to exist for five days." Some said four days, some said three days, two days, or one day.

复有比丘白佛言：「欲存在五日。」或言四日，或言三日、二日、一日者。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Stop! Stop! Bhikṣus! This is also a Dharma of indulgence, not contemplating the thought of death."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「止！止！比丘！此亦是放逸之法，非为思惟死想。」

At that time, another bhikṣu said to the World-Honored One, "I am able to endure and practice the thought of death." The bhikṣu said to the Buddha, "When the time comes, I will put on my robe, take my alms bowl, enter the city of Śrāvastī to beg for food, return from the city of Śrāvastī, go to my place, enter a quiet room, contemplate the seven factors of awakening, and take my life. This is contemplating the thought of death."

尔时，复有一比丘白世尊言：「我能堪忍修行死想。」比丘白佛言：「我到时，著衣持钵，入舍卫城乞食已，还出舍卫城，归所在，入静室中，思惟七觉意而取命终，此则思惟死想。」

The World-Honored One said, "Stop! Stop! Bhikṣu! This is also not contemplating and practicing the thought of death. What you bhikṣus have said are all practices of indulgence, not the Dharma of practicing the thought of death."

世尊告曰：「止！止！比丘！此亦非思惟、修行死想。汝等诸比丘所说者，皆是放逸之行，非是修行死想之法。」

At that time, the World-Honored One further told the bhikṣus, "One who can be like the bhikṣu Pokkharasāti is called contemplating the thought of death. That bhikṣu is good at contemplating the thought of death, and is disgusted with this body's impurity. If a bhikṣu contemplates the thought of death, fixes his mind in front, and does not move, he should contemplate the number of inhalations and exhalations, and in the meantime contemplate the seven factors of awakening. This will greatly benefit the Tathāgata's Dharma. Because all conditioned phenomena are empty and still, arising and ceasing are all illusory and unreal. Therefore, bhikṣus! You should contemplate the thought of death in the inhalations and exhalations, and then be liberated from birth, old age, sickness, death, sorrow, anxiety, suffering, and distress. Thus, bhikṣus! You should know and practice accordingly."

是时，世尊重告比丘：「其能如婆迦利比丘者，此则名为思惟死想。彼比丘者，善能思惟死想，厌患此身恶露不净。若比丘思惟死想，系意在前，心不移动，念出入息往还之数，于其中间思惟七觉意，则于如来法多所饶益。所以然者，一切诸行皆空、皆寂，起者、灭者皆是幻化，无有真实。是故，比丘！当于出入息中思惟死想，便脱生、老、病、死、愁、忧、苦、恼。如是，比丘！当知作如是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teachings, joyfully received and practiced them.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
