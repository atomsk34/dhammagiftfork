EĀ 41.4

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

41.4 （四）

Parallel to: SN 22.2 (~) / SĀ 108 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at the Niagrodha Garden in Kapilavastu, together with a great assembly of five hundred bhikṣus.

一时，佛在释翅迦毘罗越城尼拘屡园，与大比丘众五百人俱。

At that time, many bhikṣus went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed their heads at his feet, and sat to one side. Then the many bhikṣus said to the World-Honored One, "We wish to go north to teach and transform."

是时，众多比丘往至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。尔时，众多比丘白世尊言：「我等欲诣北方游化。」

The World-Honored One said, "It is time for you to know." The World-Honored One further said to the bhikṣus, "Have you taken leave of the bhikṣu Śāriputra?"

世尊告曰：「宜知是时。」世尊复告比丘曰：「汝等为辞舍利弗比丘乎？」

The bhikṣus replied, "No, World-Honored One!"

诸比丘对曰：「不也。世尊！」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Go and take leave of the bhikṣu Śāriputra. The reason is that the bhikṣu Śāriputra has always instructed the Dharma to the practitioners of the holy life, speaking the Dharma without ever being satisfied."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「汝等往辞舍利弗比丘。所以然者，舍利弗比丘恒与诸梵行人教诫其法，说法无厌足。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke the subtle Dharma to the bhikṣus. Having heard the Dharma, the bhikṣus rose from their seats, bowed at the feet of the World-Honored One, circumambulated the Buddha three times, and then departed.

尔时，世尊与诸比丘说微妙之法，诸比丘闻法已，即从座起，礼世尊足，遶佛三匝，便退而去。

At that time, Śāriputra was staying at the Śākya Shrine Temple. At that time, many bhikṣus went to where Śāriputra was, exchanged greetings, and sat to one side. Then the many bhikṣus said to Śāriputra, "We wish to travel to the northern human realm to teach. We now take leave of the World-Honored One."

尔时，舍利弗在释翅神寺中游。尔时，众多比丘往至舍利弗所，共相问讯，在一面坐。是时，众多比丘白舍利弗言：「我等欲诣北方人间游化，今以辞世尊。」

Śāriputra said, "You should know that the people, śramaṇas, and brāhmaṇas in the northern region are all very intelligent and difficult to match in wisdom. Moreover, the people like to come and test each other. If they were to ask you, 'Virtuous ones, what kind of discussions do your teachers have?' If asked this, how would you answer?"

舍利弗言：「卿等当知，北方人民、沙门、婆罗门皆悉聪明，智慧难及，复有人民憙来相试。若当来问卿：『诸贤师，作何等论？』设当作是问者，欲云何报之？」

The bhikṣus replied, "If someone were to come and ask, we would answer with this meaning: 'Form is impermanent, and what is impermanent is suffering; what is suffering is selfless, and what is selfless is empty. The empty and selfless is what the wise observe. Feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness are also impermanent, suffering, empty, and selfless. What is truly empty is selfless and empty. This is what the wise learn. These five aggregates of clinging are all empty and still, and all come together through causes and conditions, all returning to destruction, not lasting long. The eightfold path, following the seven, is what my teacher says is correct.' If kṣatriyas, brāhmaṇas, or the people come to ask us about the meaning, we will answer with this meaning."

诸比丘报曰：「设当有人来问者，我当以此义报之：『色者无常，其无常者即是苦也；苦者无我，无我者空，以空无我、彼空，如是智者之所观也。痛、想、行、识亦复无常、苦、空、无我，其实空者彼无我、空，如是智者之所学也。此五盛阴皆空、皆寂，因缘合会皆归于磨灭，不得久住。八种之道，将从有七，我师所说正谓此耳。』若刹利、婆罗门、人民之类，来问我义者，我等当以此义报之。」

At that time, Śāriputra said to the many bhikṣus, "You should firmly hold your minds and not be rash."

是时，舍利弗语众多比丘曰：「汝等坚持心意，勿为轻举。」

At that time, Śāriputra fully spoke the subtle Dharma with the bhikṣus, then rose from his seat and left.

是时，舍利弗具足与诸比丘说微妙之法，即从座起而去。

At that time, not far from where the many bhikṣus had left, Śāriputra said to the bhikṣus, "How should one practice the eightfold path and the sevenfold Dharma?"

是时，众多比丘去不远，舍利弗告比丘：「当云何行八种之道及七种之法？」

At that time, the many bhikṣus said to Śāriputra, "We have come from afar, wishing to hear the meaning. Please explain it."

是时，众多比丘白舍利弗言：「我等乃从远来，欲闻其义，唯愿说之。」

Śāriputra replied, "Listen attentively! Listen attentively and consider it well. I will now explain it." At that time, the bhikṣus accepted his instruction.

舍利弗报曰：「汝等谛听！谛听，善思念之，吾今当说。」是时，比丘而受其教。

Śāriputra said, "If one single-mindedly contemplates right view, it is the contemplation of the awakening factor of mindfulness, which is not confused. Equanimity is the contemplation of the awakening factor of the Dharma, contemplating all dharmas with a single mind. Equanimity in speech is the awakening factor of vigor, with body and mind diligently practicing. Equanimity in action is the awakening factor of joy, attaining the arising of all dharmas. Equanimity in livelihood is the awakening factor of tranquility, being content with the wealth of the sages, abandoning worldly wealth, and resting the body. Equanimity in skillful means is the awakening factor of concentration, attaining the four noble truths of the sages and completely removing all afflictions. Equanimity in mindfulness is the awakening factor of wisdom, contemplating the four foundations of mindfulness, seeing the body as impermanent, empty, and without self. Equanimity in samādhi is attaining what has not been attained, liberating what has not been liberated, and attaining realization of what has not been realized. If someone were to come and ask about the meaning of cultivating the eightfold path and the seven factors, you should answer like this. Because for a bhikṣu who cultivates the eightfold path and the seven factors, the mind with taints will be liberated.

舍利弗告曰：「若一心念正见者，念觉意不乱也；等治者，念一心一切诸法，法觉意也；等语者，身意精进，精进觉意也；等业者，一切诸法得生，喜觉意也；等命者，知足于贤圣之财，悉舍家财，安其形体，猗觉意也；等方便者，得贤圣四谛，尽除去诸结，定觉意也；等念者，观四意止，身无牢固，皆空无我，护觉意也；等三昧者，不获者获，不度者度，不得证者使得证也。设当有人来问此义：『云何修八种道及七法？』汝等当如是报之。所以然者，八种道及七法，其有比丘修此者，有漏心便得解脱。

I now further tell you, if a bhikṣu practices and contemplates the eightfold path and the seven factors, that bhikṣu will attain the two fruits without doubt, and become an arhat. Let us set aside this matter. If one cannot do much, practicing the eightfold path and the seven factors for one day will have immeasurable merit, and one will attain the state of an anāgāmin or an arhat. Therefore, all of you should seek skillful means to practice the eightfold path and the seven factors, and have no doubt in attaining the path.

「我今重告汝，其有比丘修行思惟八种道及七法者，彼比丘便成二果而无狐疑，得阿罗汉。且舍此事。若不能多，一日之中行此八种道及七法者，其福不可称计，得阿那含、若阿罗汉。是故，诸贤，当求方便，行此八种道及七法者，于取道无有狐疑。」

At that time, the bhikṣus, having heard Śāriputra's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻舍利弗所说，欢喜奉行。
