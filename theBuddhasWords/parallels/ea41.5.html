EĀ 41.5

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

41.5 （五）

Parallel to: SN 16.5 / SĀ 1141 / SĀ² 2.116 / EĀ 12.6

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One said to Kāśyapa, "You are now old and decrepit, without the intention of youth. You should accept the clothing and food of the elders."

尔时，世尊语迦叶曰：「汝今年已朽迈，无少壮之意，宜可受诸长者衣裳及其饮食。」

Mahākāśyapa said to the Buddha, "I am not capable of accepting their clothing and food. Now, this rag robe and alms-begging food are incomparable in their happiness. The reason is that in the future there will be bhikṣus whose bodies are soft and who crave fine clothing and food. They will then regress from meditation and no longer be able to practice the hard practices. They will also say, 'In the past, when the Buddhas were here, the bhikṣus also accepted invitations and received people's clothing and food. Why should we not follow the sages of the past?' Because of their attachment to clothing and food, they will then abandon the monastic robes and become laypeople. This will cause the sages and worthies to lose their spiritual power, and the four groups of followers will gradually decrease. When the holy assembly has decreased, the Tathāgata's divine temples will also be destroyed. Because the Tathāgata's divine temples have been destroyed, the Dharma of the sūtras will also decline. At that time, sentient beings will no longer have any spiritual light. Because of the lack of spiritual light, their lifespans will become short. At that time, when those sentient beings have ended their lives, they will all fall into the three evil destinies. It is like the sentient beings of today, those with much merit are all reborn in the heavens. In the future, those with much wrongdoing will all enter hell."

大迦叶白佛言：「我不堪任受彼衣食，今此纳衣随时乞食，快乐无比。所以然者，将来当有比丘！形体柔软，心贪好衣食，便于禅退转，不复能行苦业，又当作是语：『过去佛时，诸比丘等亦受人请、受人衣食，我等何为不法古时圣人乎？』坐贪著衣食故，便当舍服为白衣，使诸圣贤无复威神，四部之众渐渐减少；圣众已减少，如来神寺复当毁坏；如来神寺已毁坏故，经法复当凋落。是时，众生无复精光，以无精光，寿命遂短。是时，彼众生命终已，皆堕三恶趣。犹如今日众生之类，为福多者皆生天上，当来之世为罪多者，尽入地狱。」

The World-Honored One said, "Excellent! Excellent! Kāśyapa benefits many, acting as a good friend and field of merit for the people of the world. Kāśyapa should know, after my parinirvāṇa, for more than a thousand years, there will be bhikṣus who regress from meditation and no longer practice the dhuta practices. They will not beg for alms or wear patched robes, but greedily accept the invitations and clothing and food of the elders. They will also not dwell in secluded places under trees, but will enjoy decorating their dwellings. They will not use urine and feces as medicine, but only use other medicinal herbs that are extremely sweet. They may also be attached to wealth and stingy with their dwellings, always quarreling. At that time, the donors and benefactors who have faith in the Buddha's teachings, who enjoy giving and are not stingy with their wealth, will be reborn in the heavens after their lives end. The lazy bhikṣus will die and enter hell. Thus, Kāśyapa! All conditioned things are impermanent and cannot last.

世尊告曰：「善哉！善哉！迦叶多所饶益，为世人民作良友福田。迦叶当知，吾般涅槃后千岁余，当有比丘于禅退转，不复行头陀之法；亦无乞食、著补纳衣，贪受长者请其衣食；亦复不在树下闲居之处，好喜庄饰房舍，亦不用大小便为药，但著余药草，极甘美者；或于其中贪著财货，悋惜房舍，恒共鬪诤。尔时，檀越施主笃信佛法，好喜惠施，不惜财物。是时，檀越施主命终之后尽生天上，比丘懈怠者，死入地狱中。如是，迦叶！一切诸行皆悉无常，不得久保。

Moreover, Kāśyapa should know, in the future there will be bhikṣus who shave their beards and hair but engage in household affairs, holding a boy in the left arm and a girl in the right arm, and carrying a zither and flute, begging for food in the streets and alleys. At that time, the donors and benefactors will receive endless blessings, let alone those who sincerely beg for alms today. Thus, Kāśyapa! All conditioned things are impermanent and cannot last.

「又迦叶当知，将来之世，当有比丘剃须发而习家业，左抱男，右抱女，又执筝箫在街巷乞食。尔时，檀越施主受福无穷，况复今日至诚乞食者。如是，迦叶！一切行无常，不可久停。

Kāśyapa should know, in the future there will be śramaṇa bhikṣus who will abandon the eightfold path and the seven kinds of Dharma that I have accumulated over three asaṃkhyeya kalpas. They will sing songs about them and beg for food in the midst of the people to sustain their lives. Then the donors and benefactors who offer food to those bhikṣus will still receive blessings, let alone those who do not receive blessings today.

「迦叶当知，将来之世，若有沙门比丘当舍八种道及七种之法，如我今日于三阿僧祇劫所集法宝。将来诸比丘以为歌曲，在众人中乞食以自济命，然后檀越施主饭彼比丘众，犹获其福，况复今日而不得其福乎！

I now entrust this Dharma to Kāśyapa and the bhikṣu Ānanda. The reason is that I am now eighty years old, and the Tathāgata will soon attain parinirvāṇa. I now entrust the Dharma treasure to these two, to recite and uphold it well, so that it is not cut off and spreads throughout the world. Whoever obstructs the teachings of the sages will fall into the extreme. Therefore, today I entrust the sūtras to you, so that they are not lost.

「我今持此法付授迦叶及阿难比丘。所以然者，吾今年老以向八十，然如来不久当取灭度，今持法宝付嘱二人，善念诵持，使不断绝，流布世间。其有遏绝圣人言教者，便为堕边际。是故，今日嘱累汝经法，无令脱失。」

At that time, Mahākāśyapa and Ānanda rose from their seats, knelt with joined palms, and addressed the World-Honored One, saying, "For what reason do you entrust this sūtra to these two and not to the others? Moreover, the Tathāgata's assembly has countless immeasurable supernatural powers, yet you do not entrust it to them?"

是时，大迦叶及阿难即从座起，长跪叉手，白世尊言：「以何等故，以此经法付授二人，不嘱累余人乎？又复如来众中，神通第一不可称计，然不嘱累？」

The World-Honored One said to Kāśyapa, "I have never seen in the heavens or among humans anyone who can uphold this Dharma treasure like Kāśyapa and Ānanda. Among the śrāvakas, there are also none who surpass these two. The past buddhas also had these two to uphold the sūtras, just like the bhikṣus Kāśyapa and Ānanda now, who are extremely wonderful. The reason is that the past buddhas' bhikṣus who practiced austerities existed when the Dharma existed and disappeared when the Dharma disappeared. Now, Kāśyapa bhikṣu remains in the world until Maitreya Buddha appears in the world, then he will attain parinirvāṇa. For this reason, Kāśyapa bhikṣu is superior to the bhikṣu assembly of the past. How is Ānanda bhikṣu superior to the past attendants? The past buddhas' attendants understood only after hearing others speak. Now, Ānanda bhikṣu understands before the Tathāgata speaks. The Tathāgata does not speak again, and he knows it all. For this reason, Ānanda bhikṣu is superior to the past buddhas' attendants. Therefore, Kāśyapa! Ānanda! I now entrust and entrust this Dharma treasure to you, so that it is not diminished."

世尊告迦叶曰：「我于天上、人中，终不见此人，能受持此法宝，如迦叶、阿难之比。然声闻中亦复不出二人上者，过去诸佛亦复有此二人受持经法，如今迦叶、阿难比丘之比，极为殊妙。所以然者，过去诸佛头陀行比丘，法存则存，法没则没。然我今日迦叶比丘留住在世，弥勒佛出世然后取灭度。由此因缘，今迦叶比丘胜过去时比丘之众。又阿难比丘云何得胜过去侍者？过去时诸佛侍者，闻他所说，然后乃解。然今日阿难比丘，如来未发语便解，如来不复语是，皆悉知之。由此因缘，阿难比丘胜过去时诸佛侍者。是故，迦叶！阿难！吾今付授汝、嘱累汝此法宝，无令缺减。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊便说偈言：

All conditioned phenomena are impermanent, arising, they must cease. Without
birth, there is no death; this cessation is the greatest bliss.

「一切行无常，　　起者必有灭，无生则无死，　　此灭最为乐。」

At that time, Mahākāśyapa and Ānanda, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

是时，大迦叶及阿难闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
