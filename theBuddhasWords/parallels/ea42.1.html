EĀ 42.1

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

Chapter 42 八难品第四十二之一

42.1 （一）非时

Parallel to: AN 8.29 / MĀ 124

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Ordinary people do not hear or know the time for teaching the Dharma. Bhikṣus should know that there are eight times when one cannot hear and cannot practice. What are the eight? If the Tathāgata appears in the world, expounds the Dharma teachings, and attains nirvāṇa, the Tathāgata's practice, but these sentient beings are in hell, not hearing or seeing it, this is called the first difficulty.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「凡夫之人不闻不知说法时节。比丘当知，有八不闻时节，人不得修行。云何为八？若如来出现世时，广演法教，得至涅槃、如来之所行，然此众生在地狱中，不闻不覩，是谓初一难也。

If the Tathāgata appears in the world and expounds the Dharma teachings, but these sentient beings are among animals, not hearing or seeing it, this is called the second difficulty.

「若复如来出现世时，广演法教，然此众生在畜生中，不闻不覩，是谓第二之难。

Furthermore, if the Tathāgata appears in the world and expounds the Dharma teachings, but these sentient beings are among hungry ghosts, not hearing or seeing it, this is called the third difficulty.

「复次，如来出现世时，广说法教，然此众生在饿鬼中，不闻不覩，是谓此第三之难也。

Furthermore, if the Tathāgata appears in the world and expounds the Dharma teachings, but these sentient beings are in the long-lived heavens, not hearing or seeing it, this is called the fourth difficulty.

「复次，如来出现世时，广演法教，然此众生在长寿天上，不闻不覩，是谓第四之难也。

Furthermore, if the Tathāgata appears in the world and expounds the Dharma teachings, but these sentient beings are born in borderlands, slandering the wise and holy, and creating various wrong deeds, this is called the fifth difficulty.

「复次，如来出现世时，广演法教，然此众生在边地生，诽谤贤圣，造诸邪业，是谓第五之难。

Furthermore, if the Tathāgata appears in the world and expounds the Dharma teachings, one may attain nirvāṇa, but these sentient beings are born in the central lands, and their six sense faculties are incomplete, and they also do not distinguish between good and evil dharmas, this is called the sixth difficulty.

「复次，如来出现世时，广演法教，得至涅槃，然此众生生于中国，又且六情不完具，亦复不别善恶之法，是谓第六之难也。

If the Tathāgata appears in the world and expounds the Dharma teachings, one may attain nirvāṇa, but these sentient beings are in the central lands, although their six sense faculties are complete and without deficiency, yet those sentient beings have wrong views in their minds: there is no person, no giving, no receiver, no retribution for good and evil, no present world or future world, no parents, no śramaṇas or brāhmaṇas in the world who attain arahantship, and they themselves realize and enjoy themselves, this is called the seventh difficulty.

「若复如来出现世时，广演法教，得至涅槃，然此众生在于中国，虽复六情完具，无所缺漏，然彼众生心识邪见：无人、无施、亦无受者，亦无善恶之报，无今世、后世，亦无父母，世无沙门、婆罗门等成就得阿罗汉者，自身作证而自游乐，是谓第七之难也。

Furthermore, if the Tathāgata does not appear in the world and does not speak the Dharma to lead to nirvāṇa, and these sentient beings are born in the central lands, their six sense faculties are complete and capable of receiving the Dharma, they are intelligent and talented, and upon hearing the Dharma they understand and practice right views: there are things, giving, receivers, retribution for good and evil, present world and future world, in the world there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who practice right views, attain realization, and become arhats, this is called the eighth difficulty. This is not the practice of the holy life. This is called, bhikṣus! These are the eight difficulties, which are not the practice of the holy life.

「复次，如来不出现世，亦复不说法使至涅槃者，又此众生生在中国，六情完具，堪任受法，聪明高才，闻法则解，修行正见：便有物、有施、有受者，有善恶之报，有今世、后世，世有沙门、婆罗门等修正见，取证得阿罗汉者，是谓第八之难。非梵行所修行，是谓，比丘！有此八难，非梵行所修行。

"Then, bhikṣus! There is a time when the holy life is practiced. What is one? Then, when the Tathāgata appears in the world and expounds the Dharma teachings, one may attain nirvāṇa. This person is born in the central lands, with worldly wisdom and eloquence, understanding all things, practicing right views, and also able to distinguish good and evil dharmas, with present world and future world, in the world there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who practice right views, attain realization, and become arhats. This is called the holy life practiced by one dharma, leading to nirvāṇa."

「于是，比丘！有一时节法，梵行人所修行。云何为一？于是，如来出现世时，广演法教，得至涅槃，然此人生在中国，世智辩聪，触物皆明，修行正见，亦能分别善恶之法，有今世、后世，世有沙门、婆罗门等修正见，取证得阿罗汉者。是谓梵行人修行一法，得至涅槃。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

The eight difficulties are not of one kind, causing people to not
attain the Way. Now, in the present, they are not to be
encountered in the world. One should also study the true Dharma, and
not miss this opportunity. Reflecting on the past, one will be reborn
in hell. Thus, cutting off desire, contemplating the true Dharma, will last
long in the world, without ever ceasing. Thus, cutting off desire, contemplating
the true Dharma, will permanently sever the root of birth and death
and last long in the world. Having obtained a human body, discerning
the true Dharma, those who do not attain the fruits will certainly
wander in the eight difficult places. Now, the eight difficulties are explained
the essential practice of the Buddha's Dharma. One difficulty is still more
severe, like a plank floating in the great ocean. Although one may
escape one difficulty, there may be this principle. If one escapes one
of the four truths, one will be forever separated from the true
path. Therefore, one should concentrate the mind, contemplating the wonderful principle, listening
to the true Dharma with utmost sincerity, and one will attain the unconditioned state.

「八难非一类，　　令人不得道，如今现在前，　　世间不可遇。亦当学正法，　　亦莫失是处，追忆过去等，　　便生地狱中。于是断无欲，　　思惟于正法，久存于世间，　　而无断灭时。于是断无欲，　　思惟于正法，永断生死原，　　久存于世间。以得于人身，　　分别正真法，诸不得果者，　　必游八难处。今说有八难，　　佛法之要行，一难犹尚剧，　　如板浮大海。虽当离一难，　　然可有此理，设离一四谛，　　永离于正道。是故当专心，　　思惟于妙理，至诚听正法，　　便得无为处。

"Therefore, bhikṣus! You should seek means to distance yourselves from the eight difficult places and not wish to be among them. Thus, bhikṣus! You should train in this way."

「是故，比丘！当求方便，远离八难之处，莫愿其中。如是，诸比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully upheld it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
