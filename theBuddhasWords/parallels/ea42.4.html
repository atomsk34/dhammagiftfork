EĀ 42.4

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

42.4 （四）须伦

Parallel to: AN 8.19 / MĀ 35

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at the Deer Park in the country of Śrāvastī, together with a great assembly of five hundred bhikṣus.

一时，佛在舍卫国鹿野苑中，与大比丘众五百人俱。

At that time, the asura king Pāpīyān and the heavenly king of the Trayastriṃśa Heaven came to where the World-Honored One was, bowed their heads at his feet, and sat to one side.

是时，波呵罗阿须伦及牟提轮天子，非时至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。

At that time, the Tathāgata asked the asuras, "Do you all greatly enjoy the great ocean?"

是时，如来问阿须伦曰：「汝等甚乐大海中乎？」

The asuras said to the Buddha, "It is truly enjoyable, not not enjoyable."

阿须伦白佛言：「实乐，非为不乐。」

The World-Honored One said, "What extraordinary phenomena are there in the great ocean that you see and enjoy?"

世尊告曰：「大海之中有何奇特之法，汝等见已，于中娱乐？」

The asuras said to the Buddha, "There are eight extraordinary phenomena in the great ocean that the asuras enjoy. What are the eight? The great ocean is extremely deep and vast, this is called the first extraordinary phenomenon.

阿须伦白佛言：「大海之中有八未曾有之法，诸阿须伦娱乐其中。云何为八？于是，大海之中极深且广，是谓初未曾有之法。

Furthermore, the great ocean has this spiritual power: the four great rivers, each with five hundred tributaries, enter the great ocean and lose their original names, this is called the second phenomenon.

「复次，大海有此神德，四大江河，一一河者将从五百以投大海，便失本名字，是谓第二之法也。

Furthermore, the great ocean has a single taste, this is called the third phenomenon.

「复次，大海皆同一味，是谓第三之法也。

Furthermore, the great ocean has a regular tide, never missing the time, this is called the fourth unprecedented phenomenon.

「复次，大海以时朝贺，不失时节，是谓第四未曾有之法。

Furthermore, the great ocean is inhabited by ghosts and spirits, all kinds of creatures are in the great ocean, this is called the fifth unprecedented phenomenon.

「复次，大海鬼神所居，有形之类无不在大海中者，是谓第五未曾有之法。

Furthermore, the great ocean can accommodate extremely large forms, forms of a hundred yojanas, up to seven thousand yojanas, without being cramped, this is called the sixth unprecedented phenomenon.

「复次，大海之中皆容极大之形，百由旬形，乃至七千由旬形，亦不逼迮，是谓第六未曾有之法。

Furthermore, the great ocean produces various kinds of precious treasures, such as agate, coral, pearls, amber, crystal, and lapis lazuli, this is called the seventh unprecedented phenomenon.

「复次，大海之中出若干种珍宝，砗璩、玛瑙、真珠、琥珀、水精、琉璃，是谓第七未曾有之法。

Furthermore, the great ocean has golden sand at the bottom, and also Mount Sumeru, composed of the four treasures, this is called the eighth unprecedented phenomenon. These are called the eight unprecedented phenomena, which delight the asuras.

「复次，大海之中下有金沙，又有须弥山，四宝所成，是谓第八未曾有之法。此名八未曾有之法，使诸阿须伦娱乐其中。」

At that time, the asura addressed the World-Honored One, saying: "In the Tathāgata's Dharma, what is extraordinary that causes the bhikṣus to see and delight in it?"

是时，阿须伦白世尊：「如来法中有何奇特，使诸比丘见已，娱乐其中？」

The Buddha told the asura: "There are eight unprecedented phenomena that cause the bhikṣus to delight in them. What are the eight? Moreover, in my Dharma, the precepts and discipline are complete, and there is no careless conduct. This is called the first unprecedented phenomenon. The bhikṣus see it and delight in it, like the great ocean, which is extremely deep and vast.

佛告阿须伦曰：「有八未曾有之法，使诸比丘娱乐其中。云何为八？又我法中戒律具足，无放逸行，是谓初未曾有之法。诸比丘见已，娱乐其中，如彼大海极深且广。

Furthermore, in my Dharma, there are four castes. Those who become śramaṇas in my Dharma are not recorded by their previous names but are given other names. It is like the great ocean, into which the four great rivers all flow and become of one taste, without any other names. This is called the second unprecedented phenomenon.

「复次，我法中有四种姓，于我法中作沙门，不录前名，更作余字，犹如彼海，四大江河皆投于海而同一味，更无余名，是谓第二未曾有之法。

Furthermore, in my Dharma, the precepts are established, and they are followed without transgressing the sequence. This is called the third unprecedented phenomenon.

「复次，我法中施设禁戒，相随亦不越叙，是谓第三未曾有之法。

Furthermore, in my Dharma, there is only one taste, namely the taste of the eightfold noble path. This is called the fourth unprecedented phenomenon, just as the great ocean has only one taste.

「复次，我法中皆同一味，所谓贤圣八品道味，是谓第四未曾有之法也，如彼大海悉同一味。

Furthermore, in my Dharma, various teachings are fully contained within it, namely the four right efforts, four bases of supernormal power, five faculties, five powers, seven factors of awakening, and eightfold noble path. When the bhikṣus see this, they delight in it, just as the various spirits dwell in the great ocean. This is called the fifth unprecedented phenomenon.

「复次，我法中种种法充满其中，所谓四意断、四神足、五根、五力、七觉意、八真直行，诸比丘见已，娱乐其中，如彼大海诸神居其中，是谓第五未曾有之法。

Furthermore, in my Dharma, there are various precious treasures. Namely, the precious treasure of mindfulness, the precious treasure of the Dharma, the precious treasure of diligence, the precious treasure of joy, the precious treasure of tranquility, the precious treasure of concentration, and the precious treasure of equanimity. This is called the sixth unprecedented phenomenon. When the bhikṣus see this, they delight in it, just as the great ocean produces various precious treasures.

「复次，我法中有种种珍宝。所谓念觉意宝、法觉意宝、精进觉意宝、喜觉意宝、猗觉意宝、定觉意宝、护觉意宝，是谓第六未曾有之法。诸比丘见已，娱乐其中，如彼大海出种种珍宝。

Furthermore, in my Dharma, all sentient beings who shave their heads and beards, put on the three Dharma robes, leave home to study the path, and attain parinirvāṇa in the realm of nirvāṇa without remainder, yet my Dharma does not increase or decrease. Just as the great ocean receives the flow of all rivers without increasing or decreasing, this is called the seventh unprecedented phenomenon. When the bhikṣus see this, they delight in it.

「复次，我法中诸有众生类，剃除须发，著三法衣，出家学道，于无余涅槃界而取灭度。然我法中无有增减，如彼大海，诸河投之无有增减，是谓第七未曾有之法，诸比丘见已，娱乐其中。

Furthermore, in my Dharma, there is the vajra samādhi, the samādhi of cessation, the samādhi of all light, the samādhi of non-arising, and various samādhis beyond measure. When the bhikṣus see this, they delight in it, just as the great ocean has golden sand below. This is called the eighth unprecedented phenomenon. When the bhikṣus see this, they delight in it.

「复次，我法中有金刚三昧，有灭尽三昧，一切光明三昧，得不起三昧，种种三昧不可称计，诸比丘见已娱乐，如彼大海下有金沙，是谓第八未曾有之法。诸比丘见已，娱乐其中。于我法中有此八未曾有之法，诸比丘甚自娱乐。」

At that time, the asura addressed the World-Honored One, saying: "If there is one unprecedented phenomenon in the Tathāgata's Dharma, it surpasses the eight unprecedented phenomena in the ocean by a hundred or a thousand times. Namely, the eightfold noble path. Excellent, World-Honored One! You have spoken these words well."

是时，阿须伦白世尊曰：「如来法中使有一未曾有法者，胜彼海中八未曾有之法，百倍、千倍不可为比，所谓贤圣八道是也。善哉，世尊！快说斯言。」

At that time, the World-Honored One briefly taught the Dharma to them, namely, the discourse on giving, the discourse on precepts, the discourse on rebirth in the heavens, the impure perception of desire, the great affliction of defilements, and the wonderful way of liberation. At that time, seeing that their minds were opened and their intentions were resolved, the World-Honored One, as is the constant practice of the Buddhas, taught them the Dharma of suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path. He taught them completely.

尔时，世尊暂与说法，所谓施论、戒论、生天之论，欲不净想，漏为大患，出要为妙。尔时，以见彼心开意解，诸佛世尊常所说法：苦、习、尽、道，尽与说之。

At that time, the asura thought: "There should be five truths. Now the World-Honored One only speaks of four truths, but he speaks of five truths with the gods." At that time, the heavenly being attained the pure Dharma eye right where he was sitting. The asura addressed the World-Honored One, saying: "Excellent! World-Honored One! You have spoken these words well. Now I wish to return to my place."

尔时，阿须伦便作是念：「应有五谛，今世尊但说四谛，与诸天说五谛。」是时，天子即于座上得法眼净，阿须伦白世尊言：「善哉！世尊！快说斯言，今欲还所在。」

The World-Honored One said: "It is time for you to know." He then rose from his seat, bowed his head at the feet, and returned the way he came.

世尊告曰：「宜知是时。」即从座起，头面礼足，复道而去。

At that time, the heavenly being said to the asura: "What you are thinking now is extremely unwholesome. You say: 'The Tathāgata speaks of five truths with the gods, but speaks of four truths with me.' The reason is this: The World-Honored Buddhas never speak two different things. The Buddhas never abandon sentient beings. Their teaching of the Dharma is also without weariness. Their teaching of the Dharma is also inexhaustible. They also do not choose to teach the Dharma to people. They teach the Dharma with an equal mind. There are four truths: suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path. You should not think like this and disparage the Tathāgata's words of five truths."

时，天子语阿须伦曰：「汝今所念极为不善，云：『如来与诸天说五谛，与我说四谛。』所以然者，诸佛世尊终无二言，诸佛终不舍众生，说法亦无懈倦，说法亦复无尽，亦复不选择人与说法，平等心而说法，有四谛，苦、习、尽、道。汝今莫作是念，而呰如来言有五谛。」

At that time, the asura replied: "The unwholesome I have created, I will now confess! I must go to the Tathāgata and ask about this meaning."

是时，阿须伦报曰：「我今所造不善，自当忏悔！要当至如来所便问此义。」

At that time, the asura and the heavenly being, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，阿须伦及天子闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
