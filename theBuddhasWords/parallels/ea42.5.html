EĀ 42.5

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

42.5 （五）地动

Parallel to: DN 16 / SN 51.10 (~) / AN 8.70 / Ud 6.1 (~) / MĀ 36

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "There are eight causes and conditions for the great earth to shake. What are the eight? Bhikṣus should know that this Jambudvīpa land is 20,100 yojanas from north to south and 7,000 yojanas from east to west. It is 68,000 yojanas thick, the water is 84,000 yojanas thick, the fire is 84,000 yojanas thick, below the fire is wind 68,000 yojanas thick, and below the wind is a vajra wheel. The relics of past buddhas are all there. Bhikṣus should know that sometimes when the great wind moves, the fire also moves; when the fire moves, the water moves; when the water moves, the earth moves. This is called the first cause and condition for the great earth to shake.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「天地大动有八因缘。云何为八？比丘当知，此阎浮里地，南北二万一千由旬，东西七千由旬，厚六万八千由旬，水厚八万四千由旬，火厚八万四千由旬，火下有风厚六万八千由旬，风下际有金刚轮，过去诸佛世尊舍利尽在彼间。比丘当知，或有是时，大风正动，火亦动；火已动，水便动；水已动，地便动，是谓第一因缘使地大动。

Furthermore, when a bodhisattva descends from Tuṣita Heaven into his mother's womb, at that time the earth also greatly shakes. This is called the second cause and condition for the great earth to shake.

「复次，菩萨从兜术天降神来下，在母胎中，是时地亦大动，是谓第二因缘使地大动。

Furthermore, when a bodhisattva descends from his mother's womb, the earth greatly shakes. This is called the third cause and condition for the great earth to shake.

「复次，菩萨降神出母胎时，天地大动，是谓第三因缘使地大动。

Furthermore, when a bodhisattva leaves home to study the path and attains unsurpassed, perfect enlightenment, at that time the earth greatly shakes. This is called the fourth cause and condition for the great earth to shake.

「复次，菩萨出家学道，成无上正真等正觉，是时天地大动，是谓第四因缘使地大动。

Furthermore, when a Tathāgata enters the realm of nirvāṇa without remainder and attains parinirvāṇa, at that time the earth greatly shakes. This is called the fifth cause and condition for the great earth to shake.

「复次，若如来入无余涅槃界而取灭度，是时天地大动，是谓第五因缘使地大动。

Furthermore, there are bhikṣus with great supernatural powers who have mastery of the mind and can perform countless transformations as they wish. They can divide their bodies into hundreds of thousands and then return to one. They can fly through the air, pass through stone walls, and rise and sink freely. They perceive the earth as if it were not earth, and understand it to be empty and nothing. At that time, the earth greatly shakes. This is called the sixth cause and condition for the great earth to shake.

「复次，有大神足比丘心得自在，随意欲行无数变化，或分身为百千之数，复还为一，飞行虚空，石壁皆过，踊没自由，观地无地想，了悉空无，是时地为大动，是谓第六因缘地为大动。

Furthermore, there are devas with great supernatural powers and immeasurable spiritual virtues. When they pass away from there, they are reborn in that realm. Due to their past meritorious practices, they are fully endowed with all virtues. They abandon their original heavenly forms and become Śakra or the Heavenly King Brahmā. At that time, the earth greatly shakes. This is called the seventh cause and condition for the great earth to shake.

「复次，诸天大神足，神德无量，从彼命终还生彼间，由宿福行，具足诸德，舍本天形，得作帝释、若梵天王。时地为大动，是谓第七因缘地为大动。

"Furthermore, when the lifespan and merit of sentient beings come to an end, at that time the kings of various countries do not enjoy their own lands and attack each other. Some die of hunger and famine, some die by the sword. At that time, heaven and earth greatly shake. This is called the eighth cause and condition for the great earth to shake. Thus, bhikṣus, there are eight causes and conditions for heaven and earth to greatly shake."

「复次，若众生命终福尽，是时诸国王不乐本邦，各各相攻伐，或饥俭死者，或刀刃死者，是时天地大动，是谓第八因缘使地大动。如是，比丘！有八因缘使天地大动。」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said, and joyfully upheld it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
