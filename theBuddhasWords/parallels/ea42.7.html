EĀ 42.7

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

42.7 （七）众

Parallel to: DN 16 (~) / AN 8.69

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "There are eight groups of beings. You should know them. What are the eight? They are the kṣatriya group, the brāhmaṇa group, the householder group, the śramaṇa group, the four heavenly kings group, the thirty-three heavens group, the māra group, and the brahmā group. Bhikṣus should know that in the past, I have gone among the kṣatriya group, exchanged greetings, and discussed matters. No one is equal to me. I walk alone without a companion or match. I am content with few desires, my mindfulness is undisturbed, I have accomplished the precepts, samādhi, wisdom, liberation, and learning, and I am diligent.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「有八部之众，汝等当知。云何为八？所谓刹利众、婆罗门众、长者众、沙门众、四天王众、三十三天众、魔众、梵天众。比丘当知，我曩昔已来至刹利众中，共相问讯，言谈讲论，亦复无人与我等者，独步无侣，亦无俦匹；少欲知足，念不错乱，戒成就、三昧成就、智慧成就、解脱成就、多闻成就，精进成就。

"I also remember going among the brāhmaṇa group, householder group, śramaṇa group, four heavenly kings group, thirty-three heavens group, māra group, and brahmā group, exchanging greetings, and discussing matters. I walked alone without a companion or match. I was the most honored among them, and there was no one equal to me. I was content with few desires, my mind was undisturbed, I had accomplished the precepts, samādhi, wisdom, liberation, learning, and diligence. At that time, I walked alone among the eight groups of beings without a companion or match, providing great shelter for so many beings. At that time, the eight groups of beings could not see the top of my head, and they did not dare to look at my face, let alone discuss matters with me. Because I also did not see anyone in the heavens, among humans, māras, or gods, śramaṇas, or brāhmaṇas who had accomplished these eight dharmas, except for the Tathāgata. Therefore, bhikṣus! You should seek means to practice these eight dharmas. Thus, bhikṣus! You should train in this way."

「复自忆念，至婆罗门众中、长者众中、沙门众中、四天王众中、三十三天众中、魔王众中、梵天王众中，共相问讯，言谈讲论，独步无侣，亦无俦匹，于中最尊，亦无等伦；少欲知足，意不错乱，戒成就、三昧成就，智慧成就、解脱成就、多闻成就、精进成就。我当尔时，在八部众中独步无侣，与尔许众生作大覆盖。是时，八部之众无能见顶，亦不敢瞻颜，何况当共论议乎。所以然者，我亦不见天上、人中、魔、若魔天、沙门、婆罗门众中，有能成就此八法者，除如来存不论之。是故，比丘！当求方便，行此八法。如是，诸比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
