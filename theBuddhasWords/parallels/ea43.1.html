EĀ 43.1

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

Chapter 43 马血天子问八政品第四十三

43.1 （一）马血

Parallel to: SN 2.26 (~) / AN 4.45 (~) / AN 4.46 (~) / SĀ 1307 (~) / SĀ² 2.306 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

When the heavenly being Horse Blood was not yet a human, he went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and stood to one side. At that time, the heavenly being said to the World-Honored One, "Just now, I had this thought: 'Can one walk across this world?' I now ask the World-Honored One: Can one walk across the world?"

马血天子非人之时，至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面立。尔时，天子白世尊言：「向者生此念：『在地步度，可尽此世界不乎？』我今问世尊！可以步尽世界不耶？」

The World-Honored One said, "What meaning do you have in asking this?"

世尊告曰：「汝今以何义理而作此问？」

The heavenly being said to the Buddha, "Once, I went to where the Brahma Heaven was. At that time, the Brahma Heaven saw me coming from afar and said to me, 'Welcome, heavenly being Horse Blood! Here is the realm of non-action, without birth, aging, sickness, death, end, beginning, sorrow, worry, suffering, and distress.' At that time, I also thought, 'Is this the path to nirvana? Because in nirvana there is no birth, aging, sickness, death, sorrow, worry, suffering, and distress. Is this the farthest edge of the world? If it is the farthest edge of the world, is this world something that can be walked across?'"

天子白佛言：「我昔日一时至婆伽梵天所。是时，梵天遥见我来，而语我言：『善来，马血天子！此处无为之境，无生、无老、无病、无死、无终、无始，亦无愁、忧、苦、恼。』当我尔时，复作是念：『此是涅槃道耶？何以故？涅槃之中无生、老、病、死、愁、忧、苦、恼。此是世界之极边耶？设当是世界边际者，是为世间可步度耶？』」

The World-Honored One said, "What kind of spiritual powers do you have now?"

世尊告曰：「汝今神足为何等类？」

The heavenly being said to the Buddha, "It is like a strong man skilled in archery, whose arrow goes unimpeded. My spiritual powers are like this now, unobstructed in any way."

天子白佛言：「犹如力士善于射术，箭去无碍；我今神足其德如是，无所罣碍。」

The World-Honored One said, "I will ask you now, and you can answer as you wish. Suppose there are four men skilled in archery, and each of them shoots in the four directions. If someone comes and wants to catch all the arrows from the four directions and prevent them from falling to the ground, what do you think, heavenly being? Would this person be extremely quick and able to prevent the arrows from falling to the ground?

世尊告曰：「我今问汝，随所乐报之。犹如有四男子善于射术，然彼四人各向四方射，设有人来，意欲尽摄四面之箭，使不堕地。云何，天子！此人极为捷疾不耶？乃能使箭不堕于地。

Heavenly being, you should know that there is a heavenly being named Swift-footed in front of the sun and moon, whose speed in coming and going surpasses that of these men. The sun and moon palaces move even faster than this. Compared to the speed of those men, heavenly beings, and the sun and moon palaces, the speed of the Thirty-three Heavens is even faster; compared to the speed of the Thirty-three Heavens, the speed of the Lustrous Heavens is even faster. In this way, the spiritual powers of the various heavens are not mutually comparable. Even if you now have these spiritual powers like those of the various heavens, from one eon to a hundred eons, you still cannot exhaust the boundaries of the world. The reason is that the extent of the earth's regions is immeasurable.

「天子当知，上日月前有捷步天子，行来进止复逾斯人之捷疾，然日月宫殿行甚于斯。计彼人天子及日月宫殿之疾，故不如三十三天之速疾也；计三十三天之疾，不如艶天之疾。如是，诸天所有神足，各各不相及。假使汝今有此神德，如彼诸天，从劫至劫，乃至百劫，犹不能尽世境界。所以然者，地界方域不可称计。

Heavenly being, you should know that in the past, long ago, I was once a sage named Horse Blood, with the same name as you. My desire and love were already exhausted, and I could fly through the sky without obstruction. At that time, my spiritual powers were different from those of humans. In the time it takes to snap one's fingers, I could gather all the arrows from the four directions and prevent them from falling. At that time, with these spiritual powers, I thought, 'Can I now use these spiritual powers to reach the boundaries of the world?' I then traveled the world but could not reach its limits. After my life ended, I advanced in virtue, cultivated practices, and attained buddhahood. Sitting under the king of trees, I contemplated the events of my past experiences as a sage, thinking, 'With these spiritual powers, I still could not reach the limits of the world. With what spiritual power can I reach the limits?' At that time, I further thought, 'I must follow the eightfold path of sages in order to reach the limits of birth and death.'

「天子当知，我过去久远世时，曾作仙人，名为马血，与汝同字，欲爱已尽，飞行虚空，无所触碍。我尔时，神足与人有异，弹指之顷，以能摄此四方箭，使不堕落。时，我以有此神足，便作是念：『我今能以此神足，可尽境地边际乎？』即涉世界，而不能尽其方域。命终之后，进德修业而成佛道，坐树王下，端坐思惟往昔经历所施为事：『本为仙人，以此神德犹不能尽其方面，当以何神力而得] cf.【丽】究其边际乎？』时，我复作是念：『要当乘圣贤八品之径路，然后乃得尽生死边际。』

What is meant by riding the eightfold path of sages? Namely, right view, right intention, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right concentration. Heavenly being, you should know that this is called the eightfold path of sages, which can reach the limits of the world. All the past buddhas as numerous as the sands of the Ganges who reached the limits of the world used this eightfold path of sages to reach the limits of the world; even the future buddhas who will appear in the world will use this eightfold path of sages to reach the limits.

「彼云何名为乘贤圣八品之径路？所谓正见、正治、正语、正业、正命、正方便、正念、正三昧。天子！又知斯名贤圣八品道，得尽世界之边际。诸过去恒沙诸佛得尽世界者，尽用此贤圣八品道而究世界；正使将来诸佛世尊出现世者，当以此贤圣之道得尽边际。」

At that time, the World Honored One then spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

Walking without reaching the end, one can reach the limits
of the world. The earth element is immeasurable, not within
the reach of supernatural powers. Ordinary people's conceptual minds give
rise to confusion within it, not distinguishing the true Dharma
they wander in the five paths. The eightfold path of
sages is used as a boat and ship, practiced by
all buddhas to reach the limits of the world. Even
the future buddhas, such as Maitreya, will also use the
eightfold path to reach the limits of the world. Therefore
the wise should cultivate this noble path, practicing it day
and night, they will reach the state of non-conditioning.

「步涉无究竟，　　得尽世界者，地种不可称，　　非神足所及。凡夫施设意，　　于中起迷惑，不别真正法，　　流转五道中。贤圣八品道，　　以此为舟船，诸佛之所行，　　而究世界边。正使当来佛，　　弥勒之等类，亦用八种道，　　得尽于世界。是故有智士，　　修此圣贤道，昼夜习行之，　　便至无为处。」

At that time, the Heavenly Son of Horse Blood heard the Tathāgata speak of the eightfold noble path and, right there on his seat, all his defilements were exhausted and he attained the purity of the Dharma eye. At that time, the Heavenly Son immediately bowed his head to the Buddha's feet, circumambulated the Buddha three times, and then departed. At that time, the Heavenly Son immediately scattered various fine heavenly flowers over the Tathāgata on that very day and then spoke this verse:

是时，马血天子从如来闻说贤圣八品道，即于座上，诸尘垢尽，得法眼净。尔时，天子即以头面礼足，遶佛三匝，便退而去。是时，彼天子即其日，以天种种好华散如来上，即时便说斯偈：

Wandering in birth and death for a long time, wanting to cross
the world, the eightfold noble path of sages, not knowing and not
seeing. Now I have seen the truth and also heard the eightfold
path, I can reach the limits and the place where all buddhas have gone.

「流转生死久，　　欲涉度世界，贤圣八品道，　　不知又不见。今我以见谛，　　又闻八品道，便得尽边际，　　诸佛所到处。」

At that time, the World-Honored One approved what the Heavenly Son had said. The Heavenly Son, seeing that the Buddha had approved it, immediately bowed at the feet of the World-Honored One and then withdrew.

尔时，世尊可彼天子所说。时，彼天子以见佛可之，即礼世尊足，便退而去。

At that time, the Heavenly Son, having heard what the Buddha said, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，彼天子闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
