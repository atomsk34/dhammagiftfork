EĀ 43.2

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

43.2 （二）斋

Parallel to: AN 8.41 / AN 9.18 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "I will now explain the eight precepts of the noble ones. You should all think about them well and joyfully practice them." At that time, the bhikṣus accepted the Buddha's instruction.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「我今当说贤圣八关斋法，汝等善思念之，随喜奉行！」尔时，诸比丘从佛受教。

The World-Honored One said, "What are the eight precepts of the noble ones? First, not killing living beings. Second, not taking what is not given. Third, not engaging in sexual misconduct. Fourth, not speaking falsely. Fifth, not drinking alcohol. Sixth, not eating at improper times. Seventh, not using high and wide beds. Eighth, staying away from singing, dancing, music, and applying fragrant flowers and ointments to the body. These are called, bhikṣus, the eight precepts of the noble ones."

世尊告曰：「彼云何名为八关斋法？一者不杀生，二者不与不取，三者不婬，四者不妄语，五者不饮酒，六者不过时食，七者不处高广之床，八者远离作倡伎乐、香华涂身。是谓，比丘！名为贤圣八关斋法。」

At that time, Upāli addressed the Buddha, saying, "How does one practice the eight precepts of the noble ones?"

是时，彼优波离白佛言：「云何修行八关斋法？」

The World-Honored One said, "In this way, Upāli! If a good man or good woman, on the eighth, fourteenth, and fifteenth days, goes to the place of a śramaṇa or an elder bhikṣu, proclaims their name, and from morning to evening, upholds the precepts like an arhat, with a mind that does not waver or move, does not harm living beings with weapons, and universally has compassion for all, saying, 'I now accept the precepts of the fast, without violating any, do not give rise to the intention to kill, and practice the teachings of the true person. Do not steal, do not engage in sexual misconduct, do not speak falsely, do not drink alcohol, do not eat at improper times, do not sit on high and wide seats, and do not engage in singing, dancing, music, applying fragrant flowers and ointments to the body.' If there are wise ones, they should speak thus; if there are those without wisdom, they should teach them in this way. Moreover, that bhikṣu should point out each one, without missing the sequence, and not transgress. He should also teach them to make vows."

世尊告曰：「于是，优波离！若善男子、善女人，于八日、十四日、十五日，往诣沙门若长老比丘所，自称名字，从朝至暮如罗汉，持心不移不动，刀杖不加群生，普慈于一切：『我今受斋法，一无所犯，不起杀心，习彼真人之教；不盗，不婬，不妄语，不饮酒，不过时食，不在高广之座，不习作倡伎乐、香华涂身。』设有智慧者，当作是说；假令无智者，当教彼如此之教。又彼比丘当一一指授，无令失次，亦莫超越，复当教使发誓愿。」

Upāli addressed the Buddha, saying, "How should one make vows?"

优波离白佛言：「云何当发愿？」

The World-Honored One said, "When making vows, one should vow, 'With this eight-precept fast, may I not fall into the hells, hungry ghosts, or animal realms, nor into the eight difficult circumstances. May I not be born in borderlands, nor in places of violence and corruption. May I not associate with bad friends, and may my parents be upright, without practicing wrong views. May I be born in the central lands, hear the good Dharma, and discern and contemplate it, accomplishing the Dharma. May I take the merit of upholding this fast and gather the goodness of all beings. With this merit, I give to others, enabling them to attain the unsurpassed true and correct path. May the merit of making this vow be used to accomplish the three vehicles, without regressing. May I also use this eight-precept fast to study the Buddha's path, the pratyekabuddha path, and the arhat path. May all who study the true Dharma in the worlds also practice this. When Maitreya Buddha appears in the world, may I encounter that assembly and attain timely liberation. When Maitreya appears in the world, his three assemblies will be as follows: the first assembly will have 96 koṭis of bhikṣus, the second assembly 94 koṭis of bhikṣus, and the third assembly 92 koṭis of bhikṣus, all of whom will be arhats with all taints exhausted. May I also encounter that king and his teacher in the land.' Teach in this way, without any omissions."

世尊告曰：「彼发愿时：『我今以此八关斋法，莫堕地狱，饿鬼、畜生，亦莫堕八难之处，莫处边境，莫堕凶弊之处，莫与恶知识从事，父母专正，无习邪见，生中国中，闻其善法，分别思惟，法法成就，持此斋法功德，摄取一切众生之善，以此功德，惠施彼人，使成无上正真之道，持此誓愿之福，施成三乘，使不中退。复持此八关斋法，用学佛道、辟支佛道、阿罗汉道，诸世界学正法者亦习此业，正使将来弥勒佛出现世时，如来、至真、等正觉值遇彼会，使得时度。弥勒出现世时，声闻三会，初会之时九十六亿比丘之众，第二之会九十四亿比丘之众，第三会九十二亿比丘之众，皆是阿罗汉，诸漏已尽，亦值彼王及国土教授师。』作如是之教，无令缺漏。」

At that time, Upāli addressed the World-Honored One, saying, "If those good sons and good daughters uphold the eight precepts but do not make vows, will they still attain great merit?"

是时，优波离白世尊言：「设彼善男子、善女人虽持八关斋，于中不发誓愿者，岂不得大功德乎？」

The World-Honored One said, "Although they attain merit, the merit is not enough to speak of. The reason is this. I will now explain.

世尊告曰：「虽获其福，福不足言。所以然者，我今当说。

In the past, there was a king named Bao Yue who ruled this Jambudvīpa realm with the Dharma, without any crookedness. At that time, there was a Buddha named Baozang Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, Perfect in Knowledge and Conduct, Well-Gone, Knower of the Secular World, Unsurpassed Person, Tamer of Men, Teacher of Gods and Men, Buddha, World-Honored One, who appeared in the world. That king had a daughter named Mouni, with an extraordinary appearance, her face the color of peach blossoms, all due to her past offerings to the Buddhas. At that time, that Buddha also had three assemblies. The first assembly had a congregation of 168,000 śrāvakas, the second assembly had a congregation of 160,000, and the third assembly had a congregation of 130,000, all of whom were arhats with their taints exhausted.

「过去世时，有王名宝岳，以法治化，无有阿曲，领此阎浮提境界。尔时，有佛名曰宝藏如来、至真、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐，出现于世。彼王有女名曰牟尼，颜貌殊特，面如桃华色，皆由前世供养诸佛之所致也。尔时，彼佛亦复三会，声闻初会之时一亿六万八千之众，第二之会一亿六万之众，第三之会一亿三万之众，皆是阿罗汉，诸漏已尽。

At that time, that Buddha spoke the Dharma to his disciples like this: 'Bhikṣus! You should remember to meditate and not be lazy. Also seek skillful means to recite and practice the precepts.' That Buddha's attendant was named Man Yuan, foremost in learning, like my present bhikṣu Ānanda who is foremost in learning. At that time, the bhikṣu Man Yuan addressed Buddha Baozang, saying: 'There are bhikṣus whose faculties are dull and not diligent in the Dharma of meditation, and also do not recite and practice. Today, World-Honored One, where do you wish to place these people?' Buddha Baozang said: 'If there are bhikṣus whose faculties are dull and incapable of practicing the Dharma of meditation, they should cultivate the three practices of superior people. What are the three? Namely, meditation, recitation of the sūtras, and assisting in various affairs.' Thus, that Buddha spoke such subtle Dharma to his disciples.

「是时，彼佛与诸弟子说如此之法：『诸比丘！当念坐禅，勿有懈怠；复求方便，诵习经戒。』彼佛侍者名曰满愿，多闻第一，如我今日阿难比丘多闻最胜。时，彼满愿比丘白宝藏佛言：『诸有比丘诸根暗钝，亦不精进于禅定法，又不诵习，今日世尊欲安此人著何聚中？』宝藏佛告曰：『设有比丘诸根暗钝，不堪任行禅法者，当修三上人法业。云何为三？所谓坐禅、诵经、佐劝众事。』如是彼佛与诸弟子说如此微妙之法。

At that time, there was an elder bhikṣu who was also incapable of practicing the Dharma of meditation. At that time, that bhikṣu thought: 'I am now old and cannot practice the Dharma of meditation. Now I should seek to practice the Dharma of encouragement and assistance.' At that time, that elder bhikṣu entered the city of Yema and sought candles and sesame oil to come and make offerings to Buddha Baozang every day, so that the light would not be interrupted.

「尔时，有长老比丘，亦不堪任修行禅法。时，彼比丘便作是念：『我今年衰长大，亦不能修其禅法，今当求愿行劝助之法。』是时，彼长老比丘入野马城中，求烛火、麻油，日来供养宝藏如来，使明不断。

At that time, the princess Mouni saw this elder bhikṣu begging in the streets and alleys. She asked the bhikṣu: 'Bhikṣu! What are you seeking today?' The bhikṣu replied: 'Noble girl, you should know that I am now old and cannot practice the Dharma of meditation. Therefore, I seek to beg for oil to make offerings to the Buddha, to continue the honored light.' At that time, when she heard the name of the Buddha, she was overjoyed and could not contain herself. She said to the elder bhikṣu: 'You, bhikṣu! Do not beg elsewhere. I will provide you with sesame oil and lamp wicks.

「是时，王女牟尼见此长老比丘里巷乞求，即问彼比丘曰：『比丘！今日为何所求？』比丘报曰：『圣女当知，我年衰迈，不堪行禅法，故求乞脂油，用供养佛，续尊光明。』是时，彼女闻佛名号，欢喜踊跃，不能自胜，白彼长老比丘曰：『汝今，比丘！勿在余处乞求，我自相供给，麻油灯炷尽相惠施。』

At that time, the elder bhikṣu accepted the offerings from the princess. He came every day to take oil to make offerings to Buddha Baozang. He dedicated this merit and virtue to the unsurpassed true and correct path, and spoke himself: 'As I am now old and also dull-witted, without wisdom, I cannot practice the Dharma of meditation. I dedicate this merit and virtue so that in my future lives I may not fall into evil destinies, and in the future I may encounter holy and honored ones, no different from Buddha Baozang now; I may also encounter the holy assembly, no different from the present holy assembly; and my teaching will also be no different from now.' At that time, Buddha Baozang knew what the bhikṣu was thinking in his mind. He immediately smiled, emitting five-colored light from his mouth, and said: 'You, bhikṣu! In the future, after countless asaṃkhyeya kalpas, you will become a Buddha, named Dīpaṃkara Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One.' At that time, the elder bhikṣu was overjoyed and could not contain himself. His body and mind were firm, his resolve was unshakable, and his complexion was especially radiant, unlike his usual.

「是时，长老比丘受彼女施，日来取油供养宝藏如来，持此功德福业，施与无上正真之道，口自演说：『年既衰大，又复钝根，无有智慧得行禅法，持此功德之业，所生之处莫堕恶趣，使将来之世值遇圣尊，如今宝藏如来无异；亦遇圣众如今圣众而无有异；说法亦当如今无异。』是时，宝藏如来知彼比丘心中所念，即时便笑，口出五色光，而告之曰：『汝今，比丘！将来无数阿僧祇劫当作佛，号曰灯光如来、至真、等正觉。』是时，长老比丘欢喜踊跃，不能自胜，身心坚固，意不退转，颜色特胜，不与常同。

At that time, the princess Mouni saw that the bhikṣu's complexion was extraordinary and asked him: 'Bhikṣu! Today your complexion is extremely wonderful, unlike your usual. What is the reason?' The bhikṣu replied: 'Princess, you should know that just now the Tathāgata bestowed the vision of sweet dew upon me.' The princess asked: 'How did the Tathāgata bestow the vision of sweet dew?' The bhikṣu replied: 'The Tathāgata Baozang bestowed a prediction upon me, saying that in the future, after countless asaṃkhyeya kalpas, I will become a Buddha named Dīpaṃkara Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One. My body and mind are firm, my resolve is unshakable. Princess, this is the prediction bestowed upon me by that Tathāgata.' The princess asked: 'Did that Buddha also bestow a prediction upon me?' The elder bhikṣu replied: 'I do not know whether he bestowed a prediction upon you.

「时，彼牟尼女人见彼比丘颜色殊常，即前问曰：『比丘！今日颜色极为殊妙，不与常同，得何意故？』比丘报曰：『王女当知，向者，如来以甘露见灌。』牟尼女问曰：『云何如来以甘露见灌？』比丘报曰：『我为宝藏如来所授决，言将来无数阿僧祇劫当得作佛，号曰灯光如来、至真、等正觉，身心牢固，意不退转。如是，王女！为彼如来之所授决也。』王女问曰：『彼佛颇授我决乎？』长老比丘报曰：『我亦不知为授汝莂不？』

At that time, the princess, having heard the bhikṣu's words, immediately rode a chariot adorned with precious feathers and went to where Buddha Baozang was. She bowed her head at his feet and sat to one side. At that time, the princess addressed the Buddha, saying: 'I now see the donors and patrons who constantly provide the necessary oil. However, the World-Honored One bestowed a prediction upon that bhikṣu, but did not bestow one upon me.' Buddha Baozang said: 'The merit of making a vow is immeasurable, let alone giving wealth.' The princess Mouni replied: 'If the Tathāgata does not bestow a prediction upon me, I will end my life.' Buddha Baozang said: 'As a woman, seeking to become a wheel-turning sage king, one will never attain it; seeking to become Śakra, one will also not attain it; seeking to become a Brahma king, one will also not attain it; seeking to become a Māra king, one will also not attain it; seeking to become a Tathāgata, one will also not attain it.' The princess said: 'Can I definitely not attain the unsurpassed path?' Buddha Baozang replied: 'Yes. Princess Mouni, you will attain the unsurpassed true and correct path. However, princess, you should know that in the future, after countless asaṃkhyeya kalpas, there will be a Buddha who appears in the world. He will be your good friend. That Buddha will bestow a prediction upon you.'

「是时，王女闻比丘说已，即乘羽宝之车，往至宝藏如来所，头面礼足，在一面坐。尔时，王女白佛言：『我今见檀越施主，所须脂油恒相供给，然今世尊授彼比丘决，独不见授莂。』宝藏如来告曰：『发心求愿，其福难量，何况以财惠施乎？』牟尼女报曰：『设当如来不授我莂者，当自断其命根。』宝藏如来报曰：『夫处女人之身，求作转轮圣王者，终不获也；求作帝释者，亦不可获也；求作梵天王者，亦不可得也；求作魔王者，亦不可得也；求作如来者，亦不可得也。』女曰：『我定不能得成无上道乎？』宝藏佛报曰：『能也。牟尼女，成无上正真道也。然王女当知，将来无数阿僧祇劫有佛出世，是汝善知识，彼佛当授汝决。』

At that time, the princess addressed the Buddha, saying: 'Is the recipient pure, while the donor is defiled?' Buddha Baozang said: 'What I am saying now is that the mind is pure and the vow is firm.' At that time, the princess, having spoken, immediately rose from her seat, bowed her head at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated the Buddha three times, and then left.

「是时，王女白彼佛言：『受者清净，施主秽浊乎？』宝藏佛告曰：『吾今所说者，心意清净，发愿牢固。』是时，王女语已，即从座起，头面礼足，遶佛三匝，便退而去。

Upali, you should know that after countless asaṃkhyeya kalpas, the Buddha Dīpaṃkara appeared in the world. He ruled in the great country of Paduma, together with a retinue of sixteen thousand eight hundred great bhikṣus. The rulers and people all came to serve him. At that time, there was a king named Tivenna in that country, who ruled by the Dharma and governed this Jambudvīpa realm. At that time, that king invited the Buddha and the bhikṣu saṃgha to a meal. At that time, the Tathāgata Dīpaṃkara, in the morning, put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and entered the city with the assembly of bhikṣus.

「优波离当知，无数阿僧祇劫，灯光佛乃出现于世，治在钵头摩大国，与大比丘众十六万八千众俱，国主、人民悉来承事。是时，彼国有王名提波延那，以法治化，领此阎浮境界。是时，彼王请佛及比丘僧而饭食之。是时，灯光如来清旦著衣持钵，将诸比丘众入城。

At that time, there was a brāhman youth named Maitreya, with an upright and handsome appearance, who stood out from the crowd, resembling a Brahmā god. He was proficient in all the scriptures and treatises, and was well-versed in all the spells and incantations. He was well-versed in astronomy and geography. When he saw from afar the Buddha Dīpaṃkara coming, with an extraordinary appearance, unique in the world, with tranquil faculties, adorned with the thirty-two marks and eighty minor marks, he immediately gave rise to a joyful mind and a good heart, thinking: 'The scriptures say that the appearance of a Tathāgata is extremely rare. It only appears from time to time, like the appearance of an utpala flower. I should now go and test him.' At that time, the brāhman youth held five flowers in his hand and went to where the World-Honored One was. He further thought: 'The one with the thirty-two marks is called a Buddha.' He then scattered the five-colored flowers over the Tathāgata and sought the thirty-two marks, but only saw thirty marks and did not see two marks. He then gave rise to doubt, thinking: 'Now, when I observe the World-Honored One, I do not see the broad and long tongue or the hidden male organ.' He then spoke this verse:

「尔时，有梵志子名曰弥勒，颜貌端政，众中独出，像如梵天，通诸经藏，靡不贯练，诸书呪术，皆悉明了，天文地理，靡不了知。是时，彼梵志遥见灯光佛来，颜貌殊特，世之奇异，诸根寂定，三十二相、八十种好，庄严其身，见已，便发喜豫之意，善心生焉：『书籍所载，如来出现，甚为难遇。时时乃出，犹如优钵华时乃出耳。我今当往试之。』是时，梵志手执五华往至世尊所，复作是念：『其有三十二相者，名曰成佛。』即以五根华散如来上，又求三十二相，唯见三十相，而不见二相，即兴狐疑：『今观世尊不见广长舌及阴马藏。』即时说此偈：

He heard that there are thirty-two marks of a great person. Now
I do not see two marks. Are the marks complete or not?
Is there a hidden male organ, chaste and not lustful? Is there
a broad and long tongue that licks the ears and covers the
face? Please show me the marks and sever all doubts and bonds
I only wish to see the hidden male organ and the tongue.

「『闻有三十二，　　大人之相貌，今不见二相，　　相好为具不？颇有阴马藏，　　贞洁不婬乎？岂有广长舌，　　舐耳覆面乎？为我现其相，　　断诸狐疑结，阴马及舌相，　　唯愿欲见之。』

"At that time, the Buddha Dīpaṃkara immediately entered samādhi, causing the brāhman youth to see the two marks. At that time, the Buddha Dīpaṃkara again extended his broad and long tongue, licked his ears to the left and right, emitted a great light, and then re-entered through the crown of his head. At that time, the brāhman youth saw that the Tathāgata was fully endowed with the thirty-two marks. Having seen this, he was overjoyed and could not contain himself. He said: 'May the World-Honored One please observe me. I now offer these five flowers to the Tathāgata and also offer my body to the Holy One.' When he made this vow, the five flowers in the sky transformed into a jeweled pavilion, extremely wonderful, with four pillars and four gates. At that time, having seen the jeweled pavilion, he was overjoyed and could not contain himself. He made this vow: 'May I become a Buddha in the future, just like the Buddha Dīpaṃkara, with disciples and followers all like this.'"

「是时，灯光佛即入三昧定，使彼梵志见其二相。是时，灯光佛复出广长舌，左右舐耳放大光明，还从顶上入。是时，梵志见如来有三十二相具足，见已，欢喜踊跃，不能自胜，普作是说：『唯愿世尊当见观察，我今持五华奉上如来，又持此身供养圣尊。』发此誓愿时，彼五华在空中化成宝台，极为殊妙，四柱四门。彼时，见交露台已，欢喜踊跃，不能自胜，发此誓愿：『使我将来之世作佛，当如灯光佛，弟子翼从，悉皆如是。』

"At that time, Dīpaṃkara knew what the brāhman youth was thinking in his mind, and immediately smiled. It is the constant practice of the Buddha World-Honored One that when bestowing predictions, the World-Honored One smiles, and five-colored light emerges from his mouth, illuminating the trichiliocosm. At that time, the light had already illuminated the trichiliocosm, and the sun and moon had no further light. It then re-entered through the crown of his head. If the Tathāgata bestows predictions, the light enters through the crown of his head; if he bestows predictions for pratyekabuddhas, the light emerges from his mouth and re-enters his ears; if he bestows predictions for śrāvakas, the light enters through his shoulders; if he bestows predictions for rebirth in the heavens, the light then enters through his arms; if he bestows predictions for rebirth among humans, the light then enters through his sides; if he bestows predictions for rebirth as a hungry ghost, the light then enters through his armpits; if he bestows predictions for rebirth as an animal, the light enters through his knees; if he bestows predictions for rebirth in hell, the light then enters through the soles of his feet."

「是时，灯光知彼梵志心中所念，即时便笑。佛世尊常法：若授决时世尊笑者，口出五色光明，遍照三千大千世界。是时，光明已照三千大千世界，日月无复光明，还从顶上入。设如来授决之时，光从顶上入；设授辟支佛决时，光从口出还入耳中；若授声闻莂者，光从肩上入；若授生天之决者，是时光明从臂中入；若莂生人中者，是时光明从两脇入；若授生饿鬼决者，是时光明从腋入；若授生畜生决者，光明从膝入；若授生地狱决者，是时光明从脚底入。

At that time, the brāhman youth saw the light re-enter through the crown of his head, and was overjoyed and could not contain himself. He immediately spread his hair on the ground and said: 'If the Tathāgata does not bestow predictions on me, I will immediately destroy myself here and not complete the faculties.' At that time, the Buddha Dīpaṃkara knew what the brāhman youth was thinking in his mind, and immediately told him: 'You should quickly get up. In the future, you will become a Buddha, named Śākyamuni Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One.' At that time, the brāhman youth, having heard the Buddha's prediction, was overjoyed and could not contain himself. He immediately attained the samādhi of universal manifestation at that place, rose into the sky seven palm trees above the ground, and joined his palms towards the Tathāgata Dīpaṃkara.

「是时，梵志见光从顶上入，欢喜踊跃，不能自胜，即布发在地，并作是说：『设如来不授我决者，即于此处自断坏，不成诸根。』是时，灯光佛知梵志心中所念，即告之曰：『汝速还起，将来之世，当成作佛，号释迦文如来、至真、等正觉。』是时，摩纳闻佛授决已，心怀踊跃，不能自胜，即于彼处，得遍现三昧，踊在虚空，去地七刃，叉手向灯光如来。

"Upāli, do not think that the elder monk of that time was someone else. He was none other than the Tathāgata Dīpaṃkara. The princess of that time was none other than myself. The Tathāgata Ratnasaṃbhava gave me the name of Śākyamuni. Because of this cause and condition, I now expound this eight-precept observance. One should make vows, for nothing will not be accomplished. If that woman made this vow, she would have accomplished her wishes in that eon. If the elder monk had not made a vow, he would not have attained Buddhahood. The merit of making vows is immeasurable and can lead to the place of the cessation of immortality. Thus, Upāli, one should train in this way."

「汝优波离莫作异观，尔时宝藏如来时长老比丘，岂是异人乎？尔时灯光如来是也。尔时王女牟尼，今我是也。时宝藏如来立我名号字释迦文，我今以此因缘故，说此八关斋法，当发誓愿，无愿不果。所以然者。若彼女人作是誓愿，即于彼劫成其所愿也。若长老比丘不发誓愿者，终不成佛道。誓愿之福不可称记，得至甘露灭尽之处。如是，优波离！当作是学。」

At that time, Upāli, having heard the Buddha's words, joyfully accepted and practiced them.

尔时，优波离闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
