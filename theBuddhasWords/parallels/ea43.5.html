EĀ 43.5

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

43.5 （五）船筏

Parallel to: MN 22 (~) / MĀ 200 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "I will now explain the analogy of a boat. You should all think about it carefully and keep it in your minds."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「我今当说船筏譬喻，汝等善思念之，戢在心怀。」

The bhikṣus replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!" The bhikṣus received the Buddha's instruction.

诸比丘对曰：「如是。世尊！」诸比丘从佛受教。

The World-Honored One said, "What is meant by the analogy of a boat? If you are captured by bandits on the road, you should hold your mind and not give rise to evil thoughts. You should give rise to a protective mind, pervading all directions, immeasurable, limitless, and incalculable. Hold your mind like the earth. Just as this earth can accept both purity and impurity, feces, urine, and filth, yet the earth does not give rise to a mind of increase or decrease, saying this is good or this is ugly. Your practice should also be like this. Even if you are captured by bandits, do not give rise to evil thoughts or a mind of increase or decrease. It is also like earth, water, fire, and wind, which can accept both evil and good, without any mind of increase or decrease. Give rise to a mind of loving-kindness, compassion, joy, and equanimity towards all sentient beings. The reason is that the practice of good can still be abandoned, let alone evil that should be indulged in. It is like someone who encounters a dangerous place and wants to cross the dangerous place to a place of peace and safety. They run around as they please, seeking a place of peace. They see a great river that is extremely deep and wide, with no boat or bridge to cross to the other shore. Yet the place they are standing is extremely dangerous, while the other shore is without any afflictions.

世尊告曰：「彼云何名为船筏譬喻？若汝等行路为贼所擒，当执心意，无起恶情；当起护心，遍满诸方所，无量无限不可称计，持心当如地，犹如此地亦受于净，亦受于不净，屎尿秽恶皆悉受之，然地不起增减之心，不言此好、此丑。汝今所行亦当如是，设为贼所擒获，莫生恶念，起增减心。亦如地、水、火、风，亦受于恶，亦受于好，都无增减之心；起慈、悲、喜、护之心，向一切众生。所以然者，行善之法犹可舍之，何况恶法而可玩习。如有人遭恐难之处，欲度难处至安隐之处，随意驰走，求其安处。彼见大河极为深广，亦无船、桥而可得渡至彼岸者；然所立之处极为恐难，彼岸无为。

"At that time, the person thought of a plan: 'This river is extremely deep and wide. Now I can gather wood, grass, and leaves, tie them together to make a raft, and use it to cross. Relying on this raft, I can cross from this shore to the other shore.' At that time, the person gathered wood, grass, and leaves, tied them together to make a raft, and crossed. They crossed from this shore to the other shore. After crossing the shore, they thought: 'This raft has benefited me greatly. Because of this raft, I have been saved from danger and crossed from a place of fear to a place without afflictions. I will not abandon this raft now, but carry it with me.' What do you think, bhikṣus? Can that person carry the raft with them wherever they go? Or not?"

「尔时，彼人思惟方计：『此河水极深且广，今可收拾材木草叶，缚筏求渡，依此筏已，从此岸得至彼岸。』尔时，彼人即收拾材木草叶，缚筏而渡，从此岸至彼岸。彼人已渡岸，复作是念：『此筏于我，多所饶益，由此筏得济厄难，从有恐之地，得至无为之处。我今不舍此筏，持用自随。』云何，比丘！彼人所至到处，能用此筏自随乎？为不能耶？」

The bhikṣus replied, "No, World-Honored One! The person's wish has now been fulfilled. What use is the raft to them?"

诸比丘对曰：「不也。世尊！彼人所愿，今已果获，复用筏自随乎？」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "Even good dharmas can be abandoned, let alone bad dharmas."

佛告比丘：「善法犹可舍，何况非法。」

At that time, a bhikṣu addressed the World-Honored One, saying, "How can one abandon the Dharma, let alone non-Dharma? Are we not studying the path through the Dharma?"

尔时，有一比丘白世尊言：「云何当舍于法，而况非法？我等岂非由法学道乎？」

The World-Honored One said, "Based on the extinction of arrogance, one can extinguish arrogance, pride, conceit, self-conceit, wrong views, and the arrogance of being arrogant. Using non-arrogance to extinguish pride, extinguishing non-arrogance and right arrogance, extinguishing wrong arrogance and the arrogance of superiority, completely extinguishing the four kinds of arrogance. When I was not yet enlightened, sitting under the Bodhi tree, I had this thought: 'Who is the most powerful and noble in the desire realm? I shall subdue them.' All the gods and people in the desire realm were subdued. Then I had this thought again: 'I have heard of the evil Mara Pāpīyas. Now I shall fight with him.' In order to subdue Mara Pāpīyas, all the arrogant and noble gods were subdued. At that time, bhikṣus, I laughed on the seat, causing the realm of Mara Pāpīyas to shake."

世尊告曰：「依憍慢灭憍慢、慢慢、增上慢、自慢、邪见慢、慢中慢、增上慢，以无慢灭慢慢，灭无慢、正慢，灭邪慢、增上之慢，尽灭四慢。我昔未成佛道，坐树王下时，便生此念：『欲界之中谁最豪贵，我当降伏？』此欲界之中，天及人民，皆悉靡伏。时，我复重作是念：『闻有弊魔波旬，今当与彼战。』以降波旬，一切憍慢豪贵之天，一切靡伏。时我，比丘！于座上笑，使魔波旬境界皆悉震动。」

In the sky, a verse was heard being spoken:

虚空之中闻说偈声：

Abandoning the true and pure royal Dharma, he left home to study the sweet dew
If he fulfills his great vow, he will empty these three evil destinies. I will
now gather my troops to look at that śramaṇa's face. If he does not use
my plan, I will grab his feet and throw him to the edge of the ocean.

「『舍真净王法，　　出家学甘露，设克广愿者，　　空此三恶趣。我今集兵众，　　瞻彼沙门颜，设不用我计，　　执脚掷海表。』

At that time, the evil Mara Pāpīyas was filled with intense anger and immediately told the great general Siṃha, 'Quickly assemble the four divisions of troops and go to attack the śramaṇa! Also, observe what kind of power and strength he has, capable of fighting with me.' I then further contemplated, 'Even if I were to fight with ordinary people, I would not remain silent, let alone those of noble status in the desire realm! I must compete with them, whether little or much.' At that time, bhikṣus, I donned the armor of kindness and compassion, held the bow of samādhi and the arrow of wisdom, and awaited their great assembly. At that time, the evil Mara Pāpīyas and his army of eighteen koṭis, with various appearances, such as monkeys and lions, came to me.

「是时，弊魔波旬瞋恚炽盛，即告师子大将曰：『速集四部之众，欲往攻伐沙门！又当观察为有何力势，堪任与我共战鬪耶？』我尔时复更思惟：『与凡人交战犹不默然，何况欲界豪贵者乎？要当与彼少多争竞。』时我，比丘！著仁慈之铠，手执三昧之弓、智慧之箭，俟彼大众。是时，弊魔、大将兵众十八亿数，颜貌各异，猿猴、师子来至我所。

At that time, the rākṣasa hordes, some with several heads on one body, some with many tens of bodies sharing one head, some with three necks on both shoulders, a mouth on the chest, some with one hand; some with two hands; some with four hands; some holding heads in both hands, mouths biting dead snakes, some with fire burning on the head, mouth emitting firelight; some with both hands tearing open the mouth, wanting to eat it; some with open bellies facing each other, hands holding swords and spears; some holding pestles; some carrying mountains, carrying stones, and carrying large trees; some with both feet above and head below; some riding elephants, lions, tigers, wolves, and poisonous insects; some coming on foot; some flying in the air. At that time, the evil Mara Pāpīyas, with such a multitude, surrounded the Bodhi tree.

「尔时，罗刹之众，或一身若干头，或有数十身而共一头，或两肩有三颈，当心有口，或有一手；或有两手者；或复四手；或两手擎头，口衔死蛇，或头上火然，口出火光；或两手擘口，欲前噉之；或披腹相向，手执刀剑，担持戈矛；或执舂杵；或担山、负石、担持大树者；或两脚在上，头在下；或乘象、师子、虎、狼、毒虫；或步来者；或空中飞。是时，弊魔将尔许之众，围遶道树。

At that time, Mara Pāpīyas spoke to me on my left side, saying, 'Śramaṇa, quickly get up!' At that time, bhikṣus, I remained silent and did not respond. This happened three times. Mara said to me, 'Śramaṇa, are you afraid of me?' I told him, 'I now hold the mind without fear.' At that time, Pāpīyas said, 'Śramaṇa! Do you see my four divisions of troops? Yet you alone, without any weapons or armor, with a shaven head and naked, wearing these three robes, still say, "I am fearless."'

「时，魔波旬在我左侧而语我曰：『沙门速起。』时我，比丘！默然不对。如是再三。魔语我曰：『沙门畏我不乎？』我告之曰：『我今执心无所畏惧。』时波旬曰：『沙门！颇见我四部之众耶？然汝一己，无有器杖兵刃，秃头露形，著此三衣，复言：「吾无所畏。」』

"At that time, I spoke this verse to Pāpīyas:"

「尔时，我向波旬，便说此偈：

The armor of kindness, the bow of samādhi, the arrow of wisdom in
hand, the army of meritorious deeds - now I shall destroy your army.

「『仁铠三昧弓，　　手执智慧箭，福业为兵众，　　今当坏汝军。』

Then the demon Pāpīyas said to me again, 'I have benefited the śramaṇa greatly. If you do not heed my words, I will take you and reduce you to ashes. Moreover, śramaṇa! Your appearance is upright and handsome, and you are young and beautiful. You are of the kṣatriya caste, the lineage of a wheel-turning sage king. Quickly rise from here and enjoy the five pleasures. I will make you a wheel-turning sage king.' At that time, I replied to Pāpīyas, 'What you say is impermanent and changeable, and cannot last long. It will also be abandoned, and is not what I desire.' Then the evil demon Pāpīyas said to me again, 'Śramaṇa! What do you seek today? What do you aspire to?' I replied, 'What I aspire to is the place of no worries or fears, the peaceful and tranquil city of nirvāṇa; to guide those sentient beings who wander in saṃsāra, immersed in suffering and distress, along the right path.'

「时，魔波旬复语我曰：『我于沙门多所饶益。设不从我语者，正尔取汝，灰灭其形。又复沙门！颜貌端政，年壮可美，出处刹利转轮王种，速起此处，习于五乐，我当将和使汝得作转轮圣王。』时，我复报波旬曰：『汝所说者，无常变易，不得久住。亦当舍离，非吾所贪。』时，弊魔波旬复语我曰：『沙门！今日为何所求？志愿何物？』时我报曰：『吾所愿者，无忧畏处，安隐恬泊，涅槃城中；使此众生流浪生死，沈翳苦恼者，导引正路。』

The demon replied to me, 'If the śramaṇa does not quickly rise from his seat, I will grab your feet and throw you to the other side of the ocean.' At that time, I replied to Pāpīyas, 'I observe that in heaven and among humans, among demons, or among gods, humans, or non-humans, or among your fourfold assembly, none can move a single hair of mine.' The demon said to me, 'Śramaṇa! Do you wish to fight with me today?' I replied, 'I wish to engage in battle.' The demon said to me, 'Who is your enemy?' I replied, 'Arrogance is. Arrogance, excessive arrogance, wrong arrogance, arrogance among the arrogant, and excessive arrogance.' The demon said to me, 'By what means do you destroy these arrogances?' At that time, I replied, 'Pāpīyas should know that there are the samādhis of kindness, compassion, joy, and equanimity, the samādhis of emptiness, wishlessness, and signlessness. By the samādhi of kindness, one accomplishes the samādhi of compassion; by the samādhi of compassion, one attains the samādhi of joy; by the samādhi of joy, one attains the samādhi of equanimity. By the samādhi of emptiness, one attains the samādhi of wishlessness; by the samādhi of wishlessness, one attains the samādhi of signlessness. By the power of these three samādhis, I will fight with you. When action is exhausted, suffering is exhausted; when suffering is exhausted, afflictions are exhausted; when afflictions are exhausted, one attains nirvāṇa.'

「魔报我曰：『设今沙门不速起乎坐者，当执汝脚，掷著海表。』时我报波旬曰：『我自观察天上、人中，魔、若魔天、人、若非人，及汝四部之众，不能使吾一毛动。』魔报我曰：『沙门！今日欲与吾战乎？』我报之曰：『思得交战。』魔报我曰：『汝怨是谁？』我复报曰：『憍慢者是。增上慢、自慢、邪慢、慢中慢、增上慢。』魔语我曰：『汝以何义灭此诸慢？』时我报曰：『波旬当知，有慈仁三昧、悲三昧、憙三昧、护三昧、空三昧、无愿三昧、无相三昧，由慈三昧，办悲三昧；缘悲三昧，得喜三昧；缘喜三昧，得护三昧。由空三昧，得无愿三昧；因无愿三昧，得无相三昧。以此三三昧之力，与汝共战。行尽则苦尽，苦尽则结尽，结尽则至涅槃。』

The demon said to me, 'Śramaṇa! Can you destroy the Dharma with the Dharma?' At that time, I replied, 'One can destroy the Dharma with the Dharma.' The demon asked me, 'How can one destroy the Dharma with the Dharma?' At that time, I told him, 'One can destroy wrong views with right views, and right views with wrong views; wrong conduct with right conduct, and right conduct with wrong conduct; wrong speech with right speech, and right speech with wrong speech; wrong action with right action, and right action with wrong action; wrong livelihood with right livelihood, and right livelihood with wrong livelihood; wrong effort with right effort, and right effort with wrong effort; wrong mindfulness with right mindfulness, and right mindfulness with wrong mindfulness; wrong concentration with right concentration, and right concentration with wrong concentration.' The demon said to me, 'Śramaṇa! Although you have these words today, this place is difficult to conquer. You should quickly rise now, lest I throw you to the other side of the ocean.'

「魔语我曰：『沙门！颇以法灭法乎？』时我报曰：『可以法灭法。』魔问我言：『云何以法灭法？』时我告曰：『以正见灭邪见，以邪见灭正见；正治灭邪治，邪治灭正治；正语灭邪语，邪语灭正语；正业灭邪业，邪业灭正业；正命灭邪命，邪命灭正命；正方便灭邪方便，邪方便灭正方便；正念灭邪念，邪念灭正念；正定灭邪定，邪定灭正定。』魔语我曰：『沙门！今日虽有斯语，此处难克也。汝今速起，无令吾掷著海表。』

At that time, I further said to Pāpīyas, 'You have only one act of giving, and now you have become the demon king of the desire realm. The merits I have created in the past cannot be measured. What you say now is very difficult.' Pāpīyas replied, 'The merits you have created now, you can attest to yourself. You claim to have created countless merits, but who can attest to that?' At that time, bhikṣus, I extended my right hand and touched the ground with my finger, saying to Pāpīyas, 'The merits I have created, the earth attests to.' When I said these words, the earth deity emerged from the ground and said with joined palms, 'World-Honored One! I will attest to it.' As soon as the earth deity finished speaking, the demon Pāpīyas became distressed and miserable, and immediately retreated and disappeared.

「时，我复语波旬曰：『汝作福，唯有一施，今得作欲界魔王；我昔所造功德，无能称计。汝今所说，方言甚难耶！』波旬报曰：『今所作福，汝今证知；汝自称说造无数福，谁为证知？』时我，比丘！即伸右手以指案地，语波旬：『我所造功德，地证知之。』我当说此语，是时地神从地涌出，叉手白言：『世尊！我当证知。』地神语适讫，时魔波旬愁忧苦恼，即退不现。

"Bhikṣus, you should know this by this method. Even the Dharma is still extinguished, let alone the non-Dharma. For a long time, I have told you about the Sutra of the Simile of One Awakening, but I have not recorded its text, let alone explained its meaning. The reason is that this Dharma is profound. Those who cultivate this Dharma among the śrāvakas and pratyekabuddhas attain great merits and attain the place of the deathless non-conditioned state. What is meant by the simile of the raft? It means relying on the extinction of pride, with pride completely extinguished, there are no more thoughts of disturbance. It is like a wild cat's skin that is extremely well-treated, when struck with a fist, it makes no sound and has no hard leather. It is the same for this. If a bhikṣu's pride is extinguished, there is no increase or decrease. Therefore, I now tell you: 'If you are captured by thieves, do not give rise to evil thoughts, but fill all directions with loving-kindness. It is like that extremely soft skin, and you will attain the place of the deathless non-conditioned state for a long time.' Thus, bhikṣus, you should think like this."

「比丘！当以此方便知之，法犹尚灭，何况非法。我长夜与汝说一觉喻经，不录其文，况解其义。所以然者，此法玄邃，声闻、辟支佛所修此法者，获大功德，得甘露无为之处。彼云何名为乘筏之喻？所谓依慢灭慢，慢已灭尽，无复诸恼乱想之念。犹如野狸之皮极修治之，以手拳加之，亦无声响，无坚［革＊卬］处。此亦如是，若比丘慢尽，都无增减。是故，我今告汝等曰：『设为贼所擒获者，勿兴恶念，当以慈心遍满诸方，犹如彼极柔之皮，长夜便获无为之处。』如是，比丘！当作是念。」

When this Dharma was being spoken, among those seated, the dust and defilements of three thousand gods were completely eliminated, and they attained the purity of the Dharma eye. More than sixty bhikṣus returned to lay life, and more than sixty bhikṣus eliminated their afflictions and attained the purity of the Dharma eye.

当说此法时，于彼坐上，三千天子诸尘垢尽，得法眼净；六十余比丘还舍法服，习白衣行；六十余比丘漏尽意解，得法眼净。

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said, and joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
