EĀ 45.5

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

45.5 （五）

Parallel to: SN 11.22

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World Honored One told the bhikṣus: "You should practice loving-kindness, widely spread loving-kindness; by practicing loving-kindness, any hateful mind will naturally be eliminated. Why is this so? Bhikṣus should know; in the past, there was a ghost who was extremely vicious and violent, who came and sat on the seat of Śakra Devānām-Indra. At that time, the thirty-three celestials were extremely angry: 'How can this ghost sit on our lord's seat?' At that time, as soon as the celestials gave rise to an angry mind, the ghost suddenly became handsome, with an extraordinary appearance. At that time, Śakra Devānām-Indra was sitting in the assembly hall, enjoying himself with the celestial maidens. At that time, a celestial being went to where Śakra Devānām-Indra was and said to Śakra: 'You should know, now there is an evil ghost sitting on your seat. Now the thirty-three celestials are extremely angry. As soon as the celestials give rise to anger, the ghost suddenly becomes handsome, with an appearance surpassing the ordinary.' At that time, Śakra Devānām-Indra then thought: 'This ghost must be a divine and wondrous ghost.'"

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「当行慈心，广布慈心；以行慈心，所有瞋恚之心，自当消除。所以然者，比丘当知；昔日有鬼极为弊暴，来在释提桓因座上而坐。是时，三十三天极为瞋恚：『云何此鬼在我主床上坐乎？』是时，诸天适兴恚心，彼鬼遂转端正，颜貌殊常。尔时，释提桓因在普集讲堂上坐，与玉女共相娱乐。是时，有天子往至释提桓因所，白帝释言：『瞿翼当知，今有恶鬼在尊座上坐，今三十三天极怀恚怒，诸天适兴恚怒，彼鬼遂转端正 ，颜貌胜常。』是时，释提桓因便作是念：『此鬼必是神妙之鬼。』

"At that time, Śakra, Lord of the Devas, went to where the ghost was, not far away, and proclaimed his name, saying, 'I am Śakra, Lord of the Devas.' When Śakra proclaimed his name, the evil ghost became even more ugly and repulsive in appearance. The evil ghost then immediately disappeared. Bhikṣus! You should know that by this method, the practice of loving-kindness is not abandoned, and its virtues are like this.

「是时，释提桓因往至彼鬼所，相去不远，自称姓名：『吾是释提桓因，诸天之主。』时，释提桓因自称姓名时，彼恶鬼转成丑形，颜貌可恶，是彼恶鬼即时消灭。比丘！当以此方便，知其行慈心而不舍离，其德如是。

"Moreover, bhikṣus! When I was seven years old, I constantly cultivated the mind of loving-kindness for seven eons of formation and destruction. I did not transmigrate in birth and death. When the eon was about to be destroyed, I was reborn in the Ābhāsvara Heaven. When the eon was about to be formed, I was reborn in the Heaven of Non-conception. I became Brahmā, the ruler of the heavens, and presided over ten thousand worlds. I also became Śakra Devānām-Indra thirty-seven times, and I became a wheel-turning sage king countless times. Bhikṣus! Through these means, know that the practice of loving-kindness has such merits.

「又且，比丘！吾昔日时，七岁之中恒修慈心，经历七成劫、败劫，不往来生死，劫欲坏时，便生光音天，劫欲成时，便生无想天上，或作梵天，统领诸天，领十千世界，又复三十七变为释提桓因，又无数变为转轮圣王。比丘！以此方便，知其行慈心，其德如是。

"Furthermore, one who practices loving-kindness, when the body perishes and life ends, is reborn in the Brahma heavens, free from the three evil paths, and avoids the eight difficulties. Furthermore, one who practices loving-kindness is reborn in a central and proper country. Furthermore, one who practices loving-kindness has a dignified appearance, with complete faculties and a perfect body. Furthermore, one who practices loving-kindness personally sees the Tathāgata, serves the Buddhas, does not enjoy being at home, and wishes to leave home to study the path. Wearing the three Dharma robes, shaving the beard and hair, practicing the Dharma of the śramaṇa, practicing the unsurpassed Brahma practice.

「复次，行慈心者，身坏命终，生梵天上，离三恶道，去离八难。复次，其行慈者，生中正之国。复次，行慈者，颜貌端正，诸根不缺，形体完具。复次，其行慈心者，躬自见如来，承事诸佛，不乐在家，欲得出家学道者，著三法衣，剃除须发，修沙门之法，修无上梵行。

"Bhikṣus, you should know that it is like a diamond. If a person takes it and eats it, it will never be digested and must pass through. The person who practices loving-kindness is also like this. If the Tathāgata appears in the world, he will definitely attain the Way and cultivate the unsurpassed Brahma conduct; the cycle of birth and death will be exhausted, the Brahma conduct will be established, what is to be done will be accomplished, and he will no longer accept existence, knowing it as it really is."

「比丘当知，犹如金刚，人取食之，终不消化，要当下过。其行慈心之人，亦复如是，若如来出世，要当作道，修无上梵行；生死已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，更不复受有，如实知之。」

At that time, Venerable Ānanda addressed the Buddha, saying, "World-Honored One! If the Tathāgata does not appear in the world, where will those good sons who do not delight in household life go?"

是时，尊者阿难白佛言：「世尊！设如来不出世时，彼善男子不乐在家，当何所趣向？」

The Buddha told Ānanda, "If the Tathāgata did not appear in the world, then good sons who do not enjoy household life, who shave their own beards and hair, and who practice self-cultivation in secluded places, will be able to exhaust all outflows and accomplish the practice of being free from outflows right there.

佛告阿难曰：「若如来不出时，然善男子不乐在家，自剃须发，在闲静之处，克己自修，即于彼处，尽诸有漏，成无漏行。」

At that time, Ānanda addressed the Buddha, saying, "World-Honored One! How does that person cultivate the practices of the three vehicles on their own? Where will that person go?"

是时，阿难白佛言：「云何，世尊！彼人自修梵行、三乘之行，彼人何所趣向？」

The Buddha told Ānanda: "As you have said, I have always taught the practice of the Three Vehicles. The Buddhas of the past, present, and future will all teach the Dharma of the Three Vehicles. Ānanda should know that there will be a time when the appearance and lifespan of sentient beings will gradually decrease, their physical forms will become weak and without any spiritual power, they will have much anger, jealousy, hatred, deceit, and deception, and their conduct will not be genuine. Some will have sharp faculties and be quick-witted, but they will compete with each other and quarrel, using their fists, tiles, stones, knives, and sticks to harm each other. At that time, sentient beings will take up grass and it will turn into swords and knives, cutting off their life force. Among those sentient beings, those who practice loving-kindness will not be angry. Seeing these strange changes, they will all be frightened and flee to the mountains and wilderness, naturally shaving their beards and hair, wearing the three Dharma robes, cultivating the unsurpassed Brahma conduct, restraining themselves and cultivating, exhausting their defiled minds and attaining liberation, then entering the undefiled realm. They will each say to each other: 'We have defeated our enemies.' Ānanda should know that this is called the supreme victory."

佛告阿难：「如汝所言，吾恒说三乘之行。过去、将来三世诸佛，尽当说三乘之法。阿难当知，或有是时，众生之类颜貌寿命，转转减少，形器瘦弱，无复威神，多诸瞋怒、嫉妬、恚痴、奸伪、幻惑，所行不真。或复有利根捷疾，展转诤竞，共相鬪讼；或以手拳、瓦石、刀杖，共相伤害。是时，众生之类执草便成刀剑，断斯命根。其中众生，行慈心者无有瞋怒，见此变怪，皆怀恐惧，悉共驰走，离此恶处，在山野之中，自然剃除须发，著三法衣，修无上梵行，克己自修，尽有漏心而得解脱，便入无漏境，各各自相谓言：『我等已胜怨家。』阿难当知，彼名为最胜。」

At that time, Ānanda again addressed the Buddha, saying, "In which group is that person? The Śrāvaka group, the Pratyekabuddha group, or the Buddha group?"

是时，阿难复白佛言：「彼人为在何部？声闻部，辟支部，为佛部耶？」

The Buddha told Ānanda: "That person will be called one who is in the position of the branch of enlightenment. The reason is that this person is all due to creating various merits, practicing various roots of goodness, cultivating the pure four truths, and discerning all dharmas. The practice of good dharmas is precisely the mind of kindness. The reason is that practicing kindness and compassion, this virtue is vast. I once donned this armor of kindness and compassion, subdued the demon officials, sat under the king of trees, and attained supreme enlightenment. With this skillful means, I know that kindness is the foremost. Kindness is the supreme Dharma. Ānanda should know, therefore it is called the supreme. The virtue of practicing the mind of kindness is immeasurable. One should seek skillful means to cultivate the mind of kindness. Thus, Ānanda! One should practice accordingly."

佛告阿难：「彼人当名正在辟支部。所以然者，此人皆由造诸功德，行众善本，修清净四谛，分别诸法。夫行善法者，即慈心是也。所以然者，履仁行慈，此德广大。吾昔著此慈仁之铠，降伏魔官属，坐树王下，成无上道，以此方便，知慈最第一，慈者最胜之法也。阿难当知，故名为最胜。行慈心者，其德如是，不可称计。当求方便，修行慈心。如是，阿难！当作是学。」

At that time, Ānanda, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully accepted and upheld it.

尔时，阿难闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
