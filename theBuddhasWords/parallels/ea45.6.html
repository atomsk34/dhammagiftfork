45.6 （六）

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

45.6 （六）

Parallel to: MN 151

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra rose from his quiet room in the morning and went to where the World-Honored One was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side. The Buddha then said to Śāriputra, "Your faculties are now pure, and your appearance is different from others. In which samādhi are you now dwelling?"

尔时，尊者舍利弗清旦从静室起至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。尔时，佛告舍利弗曰：「汝今诸根清净，颜貌与人有异，汝今游何三昧？」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha, "Yes, World-Honored One! I always roam in the emptiness samādhi."

舍利弗白佛言：「唯然，世尊！我恒游空三昧。」

The Buddha said to Śāriputra, "Excellent! Excellent! Śāriputra! You are able to roam in the emptiness samādhi. The reason is this: the emptiness samādhi is the foremost. If a bhikṣu roams in the emptiness samādhi, he does not calculate a self, a person, or a lifespan, and he also does not see any sentient beings; he also does not see the beginning and end of all conditioned phenomena, and having not seen, he does not create the beginning of conditioned phenomena; having no conditioned phenomena, he does not again experience existence; having no experience of existence, he does not again experience the retribution of suffering and happiness.

佛告舍利弗言：「善哉！善哉！舍利弗！乃能游于空三昧。所以然者，诸虚空三昧者最为第一，其有比丘游虚空三昧，计无吾我、人、寿命，亦不见有众生；亦复不见诸行本末，已不见，亦不造行本；已无行，更不受有；已无受有，不复受苦乐之报。

"Śāriputra should know, when I was not yet enlightened, sitting under the Bodhi tree, I thought like this: 'Why do these sentient beings not attain liberation from birth and death?' At that time, I further thought like this: 'Those who do not have the emptiness samādhi wander in birth and death and do not attain ultimate liberation. Although there is this emptiness samādhi, sentient beings have not yet attained it. It causes sentient beings to give rise to the thought of attachment, and to give rise to the thought of the world, thus receiving the division of birth and death. If one attains this emptiness samādhi, there is no wish, and one attains the wishless samādhi; by attaining the wishless samādhi, one does not seek to die here and be born there, and when there is no thought at all, then the practitioner can attain the formless samādhi. These sentient beings all wander in birth and death because they do not attain the samādhi.' Having observed all dharmas, I then attained the emptiness samādhi, and having attained the emptiness samādhi, I attained anuttara-samyak-saṃbodhi. At that time, having attained the emptiness samādhi, I gazed at the Bodhi tree for seven days and seven nights without blinking. Śāriputra, by this method, know that the emptiness samādhi is the foremost among all samādhis, and the king of samādhis is the emptiness samādhi. Therefore, Śāriputra! You should seek the means to attain the emptiness samādhi. Thus, Śāriputra! You should train like this."

「舍利弗当知，我昔未成佛道，坐树王下，便作是念：『此众生类为不克获何法，流转生死不得解脱？』时，我复作是念：『无有空三昧者，便流浪生死，不得至竟解脱。有此空三昧，但众生未克，使众生起想著之念，以起世间之想，便受生死之分。若得是空三昧，亦无所愿，便得无愿三昧；以得无愿三昧，不求死此生彼，都无想念时，彼行者复有无想三昧可得娱乐。此众生类皆由不得三昧故，流浪生死。』观察诸法已，便得空三昧，已得空三昧，便成阿耨多罗三藐三菩提。当我尔时，以得空三昧，七日七夜观视道树，目未曾眴。舍利弗，以此方便，知空三昧者，于诸三昧最为第一三昧，王三昧者，空三昧是也。是故，舍利弗！当求方便，办空三昧。如是，舍利弗！当作是学。」

At that time, Śāriputra, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully accepted and practiced it.

尔时，舍利弗闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
