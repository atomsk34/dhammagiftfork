EĀ 46.2

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

46.2 （二）圣贤居

Parallel to: SN 22.105 (~) / AN 10.20 / SĀ 71 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "There are ten things that the sages of the three times always dwell in. What are the ten? Here, bhikṣus! Having eliminated five things, accomplishing six things, always guarding one thing, protecting the four assemblies, observing the inferior and weak, equal and close, rightly facing the undefiled, relying on bodily practices, mind well liberated, wisdom liberated.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「圣所居之处有十事，三世诸圣常处其中。云何为十？于是，比丘！五事已除，成就六事，恒护一事，将护四部众，观诸劣弱，平等亲近，正向无漏，依倚身行，心善解脱，智慧解脱。

What are the five things that the bhikṣu has eliminated? Here, the bhikṣu has severed the five fetters. Thus, the five things have been eliminated.

「云何比丘五事已除？于是，比丘五结已断。如是五事已除。

What are the six things that the bhikṣu has accomplished? Here, the bhikṣu upholds the six weighty dharmas. Thus, the bhikṣu has accomplished the six things.

「云何比丘成就六事？于是，比丘承六重之法。如是比丘成就六事。

What is the one thing that the bhikṣu always guards? Here, the bhikṣu always guards the mind, with taints or without taints, conditioned or unconditioned, leading to the gate of nirvāṇa. Thus, the bhikṣu always guards the one thing.

「云何比丘恒护一事？于是，比丘恒护于心，有漏、无漏、有为、无为，至涅槃门。如是比丘恒护一事。

What is the bhikṣu's protection of the four assemblies? Here, the bhikṣu accomplishes the four bases of supernormal power. Thus, he protects the four assemblies.

「云何比丘将护四部之众？于是，比丘成就四神足。如是便为将护四部之众。

What is the bhikṣu's contemplation of the inferior and weak? Here, the bhikṣu has exhausted the various practices of birth and death. Thus, the bhikṣu is equal and close to this. The bhikṣu has exhausted the three fetters. This is called the bhikṣu's equality and closeness.

「云何比丘观于劣弱？于是，比丘生死众行已尽。如是比丘，平等亲近，于是，比丘三结已尽，是谓比丘平等亲近。

What is the bhikṣu's correct orientation towards the undefiled? Here, the bhikṣu removes arrogance. Thus, the bhikṣu is correctly oriented towards the undefiled.

「云何比丘正向无漏？于是，比丘除去憍慢。如是比丘正向无漏。

What is the bhikṣu's reliance on physical practices? Here, the bhikṣu has removed ignorance. Thus, the bhikṣu relies on physical practices.

「云何比丘依倚身行？于是，比丘无明已除。如是比丘依倚身行。

What is the bhikṣu's mind well liberated? Here, the bhikṣu has completely removed desire. Thus, the bhikṣu's mind is well liberated.

「云何比丘心善得解脱？于是，比丘爱已除尽。如是比丘心善得解脱。

What is the bhikṣu's wisdom liberation? Here, the bhikṣu contemplates the truth of suffering, the truth of the origin, the truth of cessation, and the truth of the path, and knows them as they really are. Thus, the bhikṣu is wisdom liberated.

「云何比丘智慧解脱？于是，比丘观苦谛，习、尽、道谛，如实知之。如是比丘智慧解脱。

"This is called, bhikṣus, the ten places where the sages dwell. In the past, the sages also dwelt in these places, dwelling in them. Therefore, bhikṣus, remember to eliminate the five things, accomplish the six dharmas, guard the one dharma, protect the four assemblies, observe the weak, befriend equally, be rightly inclined towards the undefiled, rely on bodily practices, attain mind liberation, and wisdom liberation. Thus, bhikṣus, you should train in this." At that time, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and practiced it.

「是谓，比丘！圣贤十事所居之处。昔日贤圣亦居此处，以居方居。是故，比丘！念除五事，成就六法，守护一法，将护四部之众，观察劣弱，平等亲近，正向无漏，依猗身行，心得解脱，智慧解脱。如是，比丘！当作是学。」尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
