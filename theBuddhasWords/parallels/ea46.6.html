EĀ 46.6

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

46.6 （六）十念

Parallel to: AN 10.45

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "There are ten unwholesome things about associating with a country. What are the ten? In this country, there is a plot to kill the king. Because of this plot, the king dies. The people then think, 'This śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa often comes and goes. This must be the work of the śramaṇa.' This is called the first unwholesome difficulty of associating with a country.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「亲近国家有十非法。云何为十？于是国家起谋害心欲杀国王，缘此阴谋，王致命终。彼人民类便作是念：『此沙门、道士数来往返，此必是沙门所为。』是谓初非法亲国之难。

Furthermore, a minister rebels and is captured by the king, all of whom are killed. At that time, the people then think, 'This śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa often comes and goes. This must be the work of the śramaṇa.' This is called the second unwholesome difficulty of entering a country.

「复次，大臣叛逆为王所收，皆取害之。是时，人民便作是念：『此沙门、道士数来往返，此必是沙门所为。』是谓第二非法入国之难。

Furthermore, the country loses its wealth and treasures. At that time, the person in charge of storage also thinks, 'Now these treasures I always guard, and no one else comes in here. It must be the śramaṇa who took them.' This is called the third unwholesome difficulty of the śramaṇa entering the country.

「复次，国家亡失财宝。时，收藏人复生此念：『今此宝物我恒守护，更无余人来入此者，必沙门取之。』是谓沙门第三非法入国之难。

Furthermore, the king's daughter is in the prime of her youth, but has not yet been married, and she becomes pregnant. At that time, the people think, 'There is no one else who comes and goes here. It must be the work of the śramaṇa.' This is called the fourth unwholesome difficulty of entering a country.

「复次，国王女年在盛时，犹未出适，身便怀妊。是时，人民作是念：『此中更无余人往返，必沙门所为。』是谓第四非法亲国之难。

Furthermore, the king's body is afflicted with a serious illness, and he takes medicine from others. At that time, the people also think, 'There is no one else here. This must be the work of the śramaṇa.' This is called the fifth unwholesome difficulty of entering a country.

「复次，国王身抱重患，中他人药。是时，人民复作是念：『其中更无余人，此必是沙门所为。』是谓第五非法亲国之难。

Furthermore, the king and his ministers compete and harm each other. At that time, the people think, 'These ministers were originally in harmony, but now they compete and harm each other. This is not the work of others. It must be the śramaṇas and Daoists.' This is called the sixth unwholesome difficulty of entering a country.

「复次，国王、大臣各共竞诤，共相伤害。是时，人民便作是念：『此诸大臣本共和合，今共竞诤，此非余人所为，必是沙门、道士。』是谓第六非法亲国之难。

Furthermore, two countries fight each other, each vying for victory over the other. The people then think, 'These śramaṇas and Daoists often come inside. It must be the work of the śramaṇas.' This is called the seventh unwholesome difficulty of entering a country.

「复次，二国共鬪，各争胜余，人民便作是念：『此沙门、道士数来在内，必是沙门所为。』是谓第七非法亲国之难。

Furthermore, the king was originally generous and shared wealth with the people, but later he became stingy and unwilling to be generous. At that time, the people each had this thought, 'Our king was originally fond of generosity, but now he is stingy and has no mind for generosity. This must be the work of the śramaṇas.' This is called the eighth unwholesome difficulty of entering a country.

「复次，国王本好惠施，与民分财，后便悋悔，不肯惠施。是时，人民各生斯念：『我等国主本喜惠施，今复悭贪无惠施心，此必沙门所为。』是谓第八非法亲国之难。

Furthermore, the king always took the people's wealth and treasures with the proper Dharma, but later he took the people's wealth and treasures with improper means. At that time, the people each had this thought, 'Our king originally took the people's wealth and treasures with the Dharma, but now he takes the people's wealth and treasures with improper means. This must be the work of the śramaṇas.' This is called the ninth unwholesome difficulty of entering a country.

「复次，国王恒以正法，取民财物，后复非法取民财宝。是时，人民各生斯意：『我等国主本以法取民财宝，今复以非法取民财宝，此必沙门所为。』是谓第九非法亲国之难。

Furthermore, the people of the country all became afflicted with epidemics, all due to past causes and conditions. At that time, the people each had this thought, 'We used to be free from illness, but now we all become afflicted. The dead fill the roads. This must be caused by the śramaṇas' spells and incantations.' This is called the tenth unwholesome difficulty of entering a country.

「复次，国土人民普得疫病，皆由宿缘。是时，人民各生斯念：『我等昔日无复疾病，今各得患，死者盈路，必是沙门呪术所致。』是谓第十非法亲国之难。

"This is called, bhikṣus! the ten unwholesome difficulties of entering a country. Therefore, bhikṣus! do not give rise to the mind of associating with the state. Thus, bhikṣus! should train in this way."

「是谓，比丘！十非法入国之难。是故，比丘！莫复生心亲近国家。如是，比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said, and joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
