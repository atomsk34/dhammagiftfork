EĀ 47.4

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

47.4 （四）

Parallel to: AN 10.69 / AN 10.70 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, many bhikṣus gathered in the assembly hall after the meal, and all discussed this matter. The discussion was about clothing, food and drink, neighboring countries, thieves and battles, drinking, licentiousness, and the five pleasures, singing, dancing, acting, laughing, and music. Such things are not necessary and cannot be counted.

尔时，众多比丘食后皆集普会讲堂，咸共论说此义，所谓论者，衣裳、服饰、饮食之论，邻国、贼寇、战鬪之论，饮酒、淫泆、五乐之论，歌舞、戏笑、妓乐之论。如此非要，不可称计。

At that time, the World-Honored One listened with his divine ear to the various discussions of the bhikṣus, and then went to the assembly hall, asking the bhikṣus: "Why have you gathered here to discuss this matter?"

尔时，世尊以天耳听闻诸比丘各作是论，即往至普会讲堂所，问诸比丘：「汝等集此欲何所论说？」

At that time, the bhikṣus said to the World-Honored One: "We gathered here to discuss these unnecessary matters."

是时，诸比丘白世尊言：「我等集此共论此不要事。」

At that time, the Buddha told the bhikṣus: "Stop! Stop! Bhikṣus! Do not discuss this. Because this discussion is not meaningful, nor does it lead to good dharmas. It does not lead to the practice of the holy life, nor to the state of complete extinction and nirvāṇa, nor to the path of equality of the śramaṇa. These are all worldly discussions, not discussions of the right path. You have already left the world to cultivate the path, and should not contemplate discussions that lead to failure. If you wish to discuss, you should discuss the ten merits. What are the ten? If a diligent bhikṣu has few desires, is content, has a courageous mind, is learned and can teach others the Dharma, is fearless and unafraid, is fully endowed with precepts, has accomplished samādhi, has accomplished wisdom, has accomplished liberation, and has accomplished the wisdom of liberation. If you wish to discuss, you should discuss these ten things. Because they benefit all, bring much benefit, lead to the practice of the holy life, and lead to the state of complete extinction and nirvāṇa, which is the essential path to nirvāṇa. You, sons of noble families, have now left the household life to study the path, and should contemplate these ten things. This discussion is a discussion of the true Dharma, which leads away from evil destinies. Thus, bhikṣus! You should train in this way."

是时，佛告诸比丘曰：「止！止！比丘！勿作此论。所以然者，此论非义，亦无善法之趣，不由此论得修梵行，不得灭尽涅槃之处，不得沙门平等之道。此皆俗论，非正趣之论。汝等已离俗修道，不应思惟败行之论。汝等设欲论者，当论十事功德之论。云何为十？若精勤比丘，少欲、知足、有勇猛心、多闻能与人说法、无畏无恐、戒律具足、三昧成就、智慧成就、解脱成就、解脱见慧成就。汝等设欲论者，当论此十事。所以然者，润及一切，多所饶益，得修梵行，得至灭尽无为之处，涅槃之要也。汝今族姓子已出家学道，应当思惟此十事。此论者，正法之论，去离恶趣。如是，比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
