EĀ 48.1

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

Chapter 48 十不善品第四十八

48.1 （一）

Parallel to: AN 10.217–219 (~) / MĀ 15 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Those sentient beings who practice killing, spread killing, and plant the causes for hell, hungry ghosts, and animals, if they are born among humans, their lifespan will be extremely short. The reason is that they harm the lives of others.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「其有众生，修行杀生，广布杀生，种地狱罪，饿鬼、畜生行；若生人中，寿命极短。所以然者，由害他命。

If there are sentient beings who steal others' possessions, they plant the causes for the three evil paths. If they are born among humans, they will always suffer from poverty, with food not filling their mouths and clothes not covering their bodies. All this is due to stealing. Stealing is like cutting off the life root of others.

「若有众生盗他物者，种三恶道之罪；若生人中，恒遭贫匮，食不充口，衣不盖形。皆由盗故，劫夺物者，即断他命根。

If there are sentient beings who like to indulge in lust, they plant the causes for the three evil paths. If they are born among humans, their families will not be chaste, and they will engage in theft and adultery.

「若有众生，好喜贪泆，种三恶道；若生人中，门不贞良，窃盗淫泆。

If there are sentient beings who speak falsely, they plant the causes for hell. If they are born among humans, they will be looked down upon by others, and their words will not be trusted or accepted. They will be despised by others. The reason for this is all due to false speech in past lives.

「若有众生妄语者，种地狱罪；若生人中，为人所轻，言不信受，为人所贱。所以然者，皆由前世妄语所致。

If there are sentient beings who engage in divisive speech, they plant the causes for the three evil paths. Even if they are born among humans, their minds will always be unsettled and they will constantly harbor worries. The reason for this is because those people spread false words between two parties.

「若有众生两舌者，种三恶道之罪；设生人中，心恒不定，常怀愁忧。所以然者，由彼人两头传虚言故。

If there are sentient beings who speak harshly, they plant the causes for the three evil paths. Even if they are born among humans, they will be ugly and rude, and they will always enjoy scolding. The reason for this is because those people's speech is not focused and correct.

「若有众生麁言者，种三恶道之罪；若生人中，为人丑弊，常喜骂呼。所以然者，由彼人言不专正之所致也。

If there are sentient beings who create disputes between others, they plant the causes for the three evil paths. Even if they are born among humans, they will have many enemies and their close relatives will be separated. The reason for this is because in their past lives they caused disputes.

「若有众生鬪乱彼此，种三恶道之罪；设生人中，多诸怨憎，亲亲离散。所以然者，皆由前世鬪乱之所致也。

If there are sentient beings who are jealous, they plant the causes for the three evil paths. Even if they are born among humans, they will lack clothing. The reason for this is because those people give rise to greed and jealousy.

「若有众生嫉妬者，种三恶道；若生人中，乏诸衣裳。所以然者，由彼人起贪嫉故。

If there are sentient beings who give rise to harmful intentions, they plant the causes for the three evil paths. Even if they are born among humans, they will always be full of falsehood and not understand the ultimate truth, with minds that are confused and unstable. The reason for this is because in their past lives they were angry and resentful, without any kindness or compassion.

「若有众生起害意，种三恶道；设生人中，恒多虚妄，不解至理，心乱不定。所以然者，皆由前世恚怒所致也，无有慈仁。

If there are sentient beings who practice wrong views, they plant the causes for the three evil paths. Even if they are born among humans, they will be born in borderlands, not in the central lands, and will not see the meaning of the Three Jewels and the Dharma. They may also be deaf, blind, or mute, with an irregular physical form, not understanding the nature of good and evil. The reason for this is because in their past lives they lacked the root of faith, and also did not believe in śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, parents, or siblings.

「若有众生，行邪见者，种三恶道；若生人中，乃在边地，不生中国，不覩三尊道法之义，或复聋盲瘖痖，身形不正，不解善法、恶法之趣。所以然者，皆由前世无信根故，亦不信沙门、婆罗门、父母、兄弟。

"Bhikṣus, know that these are the retributions for the ten unwholesome actions. Therefore, bhikṣus! You should abandon the ten unwholesome actions and cultivate right views. Thus, bhikṣus! You should train in this way."

「比丘知之，由此十恶之报，致此殃舋。是故，比丘！当离十恶，修行正见。如是，比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said, and joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
