EĀ 48.2

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

48.2 （二）

Parallel to: AN 8.20 / MĀ 37

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, on the fifteenth day when the Buddha was giving the precepts, he was surrounded by the bhikṣus and went to the assembly hall. At that time, the World-Honored One silently observed the assembly of sages, who were silent and did not speak. Then Ānanda said to the Buddha, "Today the assembly of sages has gathered in the hall. May the World-Honored One please speak the precepts to the bhikṣus." At that time, the World-Honored One also remained silent and did not speak.

尔时，世尊十五日说戒时，将诸比丘，前后围遶，往诣普会讲堂。尔时，世尊默然观察，诸圣众寂寞不语。是时，阿难白佛言：「今日圣众尽集讲堂，唯然，世尊！当与诸比丘说禁戒。」尔时，世尊亦复默然不语。

Then Ānanda said to the Buddha after a while, "Now is the time to speak the precepts. The first watch of the night is almost over." At that time, the World-Honored One also remained silent and did not speak.

是时，阿难须臾复白佛言：「今正是时，宜说禁戒，初夜欲尽。」尔时，世尊复默不语。

Then Ānanda said to the Buddha after a while, "The middle watch of the night is almost over. The assembly is tired. May the World-Honored One please speak the precepts in a timely manner." At that time, the World-Honored One also remained silent and did not speak.

尔时，阿难须臾复白佛言：「中夜欲竟，众僧劳顿，唯愿世尊以时说戒。」尔时，世尊复默然不语。

Then Ānanda said to the Buddha after a while, "The last watch of the night is almost over. May the World-Honored One please speak the precepts in a timely manner."

是时，阿难须臾复白佛言：「后夜欲尽，唯愿世尊以时说戒。」

The Buddha told Ānanda, "Because the assembly is impure, I do not speak the precepts. Now I allow the senior monk to speak the precepts. If the senior monk in the assembly is not capable of speaking the precepts, I allow the one who upholds the Vinaya to speak the precepts. If there is no one who upholds the Vinaya, the one who can recite the precepts fluently should recite them. From now on, the Tathāgata will no longer speak the precepts. If the Tathāgata speaks the precepts in an impure assembly, that person's head will break into seven pieces, no different from a pomegranate fruit."

佛告阿难：「众中不净者，故不说戒。今听上座，使说禁戒。若僧上座不堪任说戒者，听持律说禁戒。若无持律者，其能诵戒通利者，当唱之使说戒。自今已后，如来更不说戒。众中不净，如来于中说戒，彼人头破为七分，如彼酬罗果无异。」

At that time, Ānanda wept and said, "The holy assembly is now orphaned and destitute. How quickly the Tathāgata's true Dharma is disappearing! How quickly impure people are appearing!"

是时，阿难悲泣交集，并作是说：「圣众今日便为孤穷。如来正法去何速疾？不净之人出何速疾？」

At that time, Mahāmaudgalyāyana thought, "What kind of person who has destroyed the Dharma is in this assembly that causes the Tathāgata not to speak the precepts?" At that time, Mahāmaudgalyāyana entered samādhi and surveyed the flaws in the minds of the holy assembly. At that time, Maudgalyāyana saw the two bhikṣus Aśvajit and Vāseṭṭha in the assembly. At that time, Maudgalyāyana rose from his seat, went to those bhikṣus, and said, "You should quickly rise and leave this seat. The Tathāgata sees you as a disgrace. Because of you, the Tathāgata does not speak the precepts."

是时，大目干连便作是念：「此众中何等毁法之人，在此众中，乃令如来不说禁戒？」是时，大目干连入三昧定，遍观圣众心中瑕秽。尔时，目连见马师、满宿二比丘在众会中，是时目连即从座起，至彼比丘所，而告之曰：「汝等速起，离此座中。如来见讥，由卿等故，如来不说禁戒。」

At that time, the two bhikṣus remained silent and did not speak. At that time, Maudgalyāyana again said to them three times, "You should quickly rise and not stay here." At that time, those bhikṣus remained silent and did not respond. At that time, Maudgalyāyana then went forward, took their hands, and led them outside the door. He then returned to close the door and said to the Buddha, "The impure bhikṣus have already been led outside. May the World-Honored One now speak the precepts."

尔时，二比丘默然不语。是时，目连复再三告曰：「汝等速起，不须住此。」是时，彼比丘默然不对。是时，目连即前捉手将至门外，还取门闭，前白佛言：「不净比丘已将在外，唯然世尊时说禁戒。」

The Buddha told Maudgalyāyana, "Stop! Stop! Maudgalyāyana! The Tathāgata will not speak the precepts with the bhikṣus again. What the Tathāgata says is not twofold. Return to your seat."

佛告目连：「止！止！目连！如来更不与比丘说戒。如来所说言不有二，还诣座所。」

At that time, Maudgalyāyana again said to the Buddha, "Now there is already impurity in this assembly. I am not capable of performing the duties of the head monk. I only wish that the World-Honored One would appoint someone else." At that time, the World-Honored One silently consented. At that time, Maudgalyāyana bowed his head to the feet of the World-Honored One and returned to his original seat.

是时，目连复白佛言：「今此众中已生瑕秽，我不堪任行维那法，唯愿世尊更差余人。」尔时，世尊默然可之。是时，目连头面礼世尊足，还就本座。

At that time, Ānanda said to the World-Honored One, "When the Tathāgata Vipaśyin appeared in the world, how many were in the noble assembly? How long did it take before impurity arose? Up to the disciples of Kāśyapa, how did they speak the precepts?"

是时，阿难白世尊言：「毘婆尸如来出现世时，圣众多少？为经几时，乃生瑕秽？乃至迦叶弟子多少？云何说戒？」

The Buddha told Ānanda, "Ninety-one eons ago, a Buddha appeared in the world, named Vipaśyin Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, who appeared in the world. At that time, there were three assemblies of the noble assembly. In the first assembly, there were 168,000 noble monks; in the second assembly, 160,000 noble monks; in the third assembly, 100,000 noble monks, all of whom were arhats. That Buddha lived for 84,000 years. Within a hundred years, the noble assembly was pure. That Buddha always used one verse as the precepts:

佛告阿难：「九十一劫有佛出世，名毘婆尸如来、至真、等正觉，出现世间。尔时，三会圣众，初一会时比丘有百千六万八千圣众；第二会时十六万圣众；第三会时十万圣众，皆是阿罗汉。彼佛寿八万四千岁，百岁之中圣众清净，彼佛恒以一偈为禁戒：

Patience is the foremost, the Buddha said non-action is supreme, not
by shaving the beard and hair, harming others is a śramaṇa.

「『忍辱为第一，　　佛说无为最，不以剃须发，　　害他为沙门。』

At that time, that Buddha used this one verse as the precepts for a hundred years. When faults arose, he established the precepts.

「是时，彼佛以此一偈，百岁之中而为禁戒，已生瑕秽，便立禁戒。

Again, in the thirty-one eons, there was a Buddha named Tryakṣa Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, who appeared in the world. At that time, he also had three assemblies of holy ones. In the first assembly, there were one hundred and sixty thousand holy ones; in the second assembly, one hundred and forty thousand holy ones; in the third assembly, one hundred thousand holy ones. At that time, for eighty years, he was pure and without faults. He also spoke one verse:

「复于三十一劫中，有佛名试诘如来、至真、等正觉，出现于世。尔时，亦复三会圣众，初一会时有十六万圣众；第二会时十四万圣众；第三会时十万圣众。彼佛尔时，八十年中清净无瑕秽，亦说一偈：

If the eyes see what is not wrong, the wise protect and
do not cling. Abandoning all evil, one is wise in the world.

「『若眼见非邪，　　慧者护不著，弃捐于众恶，　　在世为黠慧。』

At that time, the Buddha spoke this verse for eighty years, and later there were faults. He then established precepts. At that time, the Buddha Tryakṣa lived for seventy thousand years.

「尔时，彼佛八十年中说此一偈，后有瑕秽，更立禁戒。尔时，试诘佛寿七万岁。

In that eon, there was another Buddha who appeared in the world, named Viśvabhū. He also had three assemblies of saints. At the first assembly, there were one hundred thousand saints, all arhats. At the second assembly, there were eighty thousand arhats. At the third assembly, there were seventy thousand arhats, all with afflictions exhausted. The Tathāgata Viśvabhū was without faults for seventy years. At that time, he again used a half-verse as a precept:

「于彼劫中，复有佛出现世间，名曰毘舍罗婆，亦三会圣众，初会之时十万圣众，尽是罗汉；第二会时八万罗汉；第三会时七万罗汉，诸漏已尽。毘舍罗婆如来七十年中无瑕秽。尔时，复以一偈半为禁戒：

Do not harm or do wrong, uphold the great precepts
be content with food and bedding, and be single-minded in
one's resolve - this is the teaching of all Buddhas.

「『不害亦不非，　　奉行于大戒，于食知止足，　　床座亦复然，执志为专一，　　是则诸佛教。』

"For seventy years, this half-verse was used as a precept. Later, when there were faults, another precept was established. The lifespan of the Tathāgata Viśvabhū was seventy thousand years."

「七十年中以此一偈为禁戒，后有瑕秽，更立禁戒。毘舍罗婆如来寿七万岁。

In this Bhadrakalpa, a Buddha appeared in the world, named Krakucchanda Tathāgata. At that time, there were two assemblies of noble ones. In the first assembly, there were seventy thousand noble ones, all Arhats. In the second assembly, there were sixty thousand Arhats. At that time, for sixty years, there were no faults. At that time, the Buddha used two verses as precepts.

「于此贤劫中，有佛出世，名曰拘楼孙如来，出现世间。尔时，二会圣众，初会之时七万圣众，皆是阿罗汉；第二会时六万阿罗汉。彼佛尔时，六十年中无有瑕秽。彼佛尔时，以二偈以为禁戒。

Just as a bee gathers flowers, their colors are very fragrant
and pure, and they give their taste to others, a Daoist
wandering through a village. Not slandering others, not observing right or
wrong, but only observing one's own actions, carefully observing what is
right and wrong.

「『譬如蜂采华，　　其色甚香洁，以味惠施他，　　道士游聚落。不诽谤于人，　　亦不观是非，但自观身行，　　谛观正不正。』

For sixty years, he spoke these two verses as precepts. From then on, because there were faults, precepts were established. That Buddha lived for sixty thousand years.

「六十年中说此二偈，以为禁戒，自此已来，以有瑕秽，便立禁戒。彼佛寿六万岁。

In this Bhadrakalpa, there was a Buddha who appeared in the world, named Konāgamana Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One. At that time, there were two assemblies of holy ones. In the first assembly, there were six hundred thousand holy ones, all Arhats. In the second assembly, there were four hundred thousand holy ones, all Arhats. At that time, that Buddha was without fault for forty years, and he took one verse as a precept:

「于此贤劫中有佛出世，名曰拘那含牟尼如来、至真、等正觉。尔时，二会圣众，初会之时六十万圣众，皆是阿罗汉；第二会时四十万圣众，皆是阿罗汉。尔时，彼佛四十年中无有瑕秽，以一偈为禁戒：

Hold your resolve, do not be frivolous, learn the noble path of tranquility
The wise have no worries or sorrows, always extinguishing what the mind thinks.

「『执志莫轻戏，　　当学尊寂道，贤者无愁忧，　　常灭志所念。』

For forty years, he taught this one verse as a precept. From then on, there were faults, and further precepts were established. That Buddha lived for forty thousand years.

「四十年中说此一偈，以为禁戒，自此已来，便有瑕秽，更立禁戒。彼佛寿四万岁。

In this fortunate eon, there was a Buddha named Kāśyapa who appeared in the world. At that time, that Buddha also had two assemblies of holy ones. At the first assembly, there were four hundred thousand; at the second assembly, there were three hundred thousand, all of whom were arhats. For twenty years, there were no faults, and he always used one verse as a precept:

「于此贤劫有佛，名为迦叶，出现世间。尔时，彼佛亦二会圣众，初会之时四十万众；第二会时三十万众，皆是阿罗汉。二十年中无有瑕秽，恒以一偈，以为禁戒：

Do not do any evil, practice what is good, purify
one's own mind - this is the teaching of all Buddhas.

「『一切恶莫作，　　当奉行其善，自净其志意，　　是则诸佛教。』

For twenty years, he taught this one verse as a precept. After violating the precepts, he established further restrictions. At that time, Kāśyapa Buddha lived for twenty thousand years.

「二十年中说此一偈，以为禁戒，犯禁之后，更立制限。尔时，迦叶佛寿二万岁。

Now, I, the Tathāgata, have appeared in the world. The assembly of sages has one thousand two hundred and fifty people. For twelve years, there has been no blemish. I also use one verse as a precept:

「我今如来出现于世，一会圣众千二百五十人，十二年中无有瑕秽，亦以一偈为禁戒：

Guard the purity of speech and mind, and the purity of bodily actions
Purify these three traces of conduct and practice the path of the sages.

「『护口意清净，　　身行亦清净，净此三行迹，　　修行仙人道。』

For twelve years, I taught this one verse as a precept. For those who violate the precepts, there are two hundred and fifty precepts. From now on, when the Sangha gathers, they should announce according to the precepts: 'All of you, listen! Today is the fifteenth day for reciting the precepts. If the Sangha agrees, the Sangha is united.' After announcing the precepts, if any bhikṣu speaks, the precepts should not be recited. All should remain silent. If there is no speech, the precepts should be recited. Up to the end of the recitation of the precepts, it should be asked again: 'All of you, who are not pure?' In this way, twice or thrice, 'Who is not pure?' Those who are pure should remain silent and uphold it. Now, the lifespan of humans is extremely short, not exceeding a hundred years. Therefore, Ānanda, uphold it well.'

「十二年中说此一偈，以为禁戒，以生犯律之人，转有二百五十戒，自今已后众僧集会，启白如律：『诸贤！咸听！今十五日说戒，今僧忍者，众僧和合。』说禁戒以启此已。设有比丘有所说者，不应说戒，各共默然。若无语者，应为说戒。乃至说戒序后，复当问：『诸贤，谁不清净？』如是再三，『谁不清净？』清净者，默然持之。然今人寿命极短，尽寿不过百年。是故，阿难！善受持之。」

At that time, Ānanda addressed the World-Honored One, saying: "In the past, the lifespan of the Buddhas was extremely long, and those who violated the precepts were few, without any faults. Now, the lifespan of people is short, not exceeding ten years. After the parinirvāṇa of the past Buddhas, how long did their Dharma remain in the world?"

尔时，阿难白世尊言：「过去久远诸佛世尊，寿命极长，犯律者少，无有瑕秽，然今人民寿命为短少，不过十十，过去诸佛灭度之后，有遗法住世，为经几时？」

The Buddha told Ānanda: "After the parinirvāṇa of the past Buddhas, the Dharma did not remain for long."

佛告阿难：「过去诸佛灭度之后，法不久存。」

Ānanda addressed the Buddha, saying: "After the Tathāgata's parinirvāṇa, how long will the true Dharma remain in the world?"

阿难白佛言：「设如来灭度之后，正法存世当经几时？」

The Buddha told Ānanda: "After my parinirvāṇa, the Dharma will remain for a long time. After Kāśyapa Buddha's parinirvāṇa, his remaining Dharma remained for seven days. Now, Ānanda! The Tathāgata's disciples are few. Do not view it like this. In the east, there are countless billions of disciples. In the south, there are countless billions of disciples. Therefore, Ānanda! You should have this intention. My Śākyamuni Buddha has an extremely long lifespan. The reason is this: although the physical body attains parinirvāṇa, the Dharma body remains. This is the meaning. You should remember and practice it."

佛告阿难曰：「我灭度之后，法当久存。迦叶佛灭度后，遗法住七日中。汝今，阿难！如来弟子为少。莫作是观。东方弟子无数亿千，南方弟子无数亿千。是故，阿难！当建此意，我释迦文佛寿命极长。所以然者，肉身虽取灭度，法身存在，此是其义。当念奉行。」

At that time, Ānanda and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，阿难及诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
