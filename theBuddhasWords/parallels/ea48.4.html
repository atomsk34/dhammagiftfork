EĀ 48.4

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

48.4 （四）

Parallel to: DN 14 / SN 12.65 (~) / DĀ 1

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, many bhikṣus gathered in the assembly hall and each had this thought: "Now the Tathāgata is very wonderful and extraordinary! He also knows the names, clans, precepts, and retinues of those who attained parinirvāṇa in the past, all clearly. The samādhi, wisdom, liberation, liberation-wisdom, and lifespan of the body, whether long or short, he knows all. How is it, venerable ones? Is this the Tathāgata's place of distinguishing the Dharma, extremely pure, knowing the names and origins of those Buddhas? Or is it that the devas have come to the Buddha and told him this?"

尔时，众多比丘集普会讲堂，各生此念：「今如来甚奇！甚特！过去取般涅槃者，亦复知彼姓名、种族、持戒、翼从，皆悉分明，三昧，智慧、解脱、解脱见慧，身寿有长短，皆悉知之。云何，诸贤！为是如来分别法处，极为清净，知彼诸佛姓字所出之处乎？为是诸天来至佛所而告此耶？」

At that time, the World-Honored One, with his divine ear, clearly heard the discussion of the many bhikṣus. He then went to where the bhikṣus were and sat in the center. The World-Honored One then said to the bhikṣus, "What kind of discussion have you gathered here for? What Dharma do you wish to speak about?"

尔时，世尊以天耳彻闻众多比丘各兴此论，便往至诸比丘所，在中央坐。尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「汝等集此为何等论？欲说何法？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "We gathered here to discuss the essentials of the true Dharma. Everyone had this discussion: 'The Tathāgata is very wonderful and extraordinary! He is able to know the names and clans of the past Buddha-Bhagavāns, their wisdom, and how much they have, all of which he thoroughly understands. It is very wonderful. How is it, venerable ones? Is this the Tathāgata's place of distinguishing the Dharma realm, extremely pure, knowing the names and origins of those Buddhas? Or is it that the devas have come to the Buddha and told him this?'"

诸比丘白佛言：「我等集此，论正法之要。诸人各兴此论议：『如来甚奇！甚特！乃能知过去诸佛世尊名字姓号，智慧多少，靡不贯博，甚可奇雅。云何，诸贤！为是如来分别法界，极为清净，知彼诸佛姓字所出之处乎？为是诸天来至佛所而告此耶？』」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the bhikṣus, "Do you wish to hear about the spiritual power of the past Buddhas, their names, clans, and the length of their lifespans?"

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「汝等欲得闻过去诸佛神智之力乎？姓字名号、寿命长短耶？」

The bhikṣus replied, "Now is the right time. We only wish that the World-Honored One would explain the meaning."

诸比丘对曰：「今正是时，唯愿世尊敷演其义。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "You should think about it well. I will explain the meaning to you in detail." At that time, the many bhikṣus received the Buddha's instruction.

佛告诸比丘：「汝等善思念之，吾当与汝广演其义。」尔时，众多比丘从佛受教。

The World-Honored One said, "Bhikṣus, you should know that in the past, in the ninety-first eon, there was a Buddha who appeared in the world, named Vipaśyin Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One. Furthermore, in the thirty-first eon, there was a Buddha who appeared in the world, named Śikhin Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One. Furthermore, within that thirty-first eon, there was a Buddha named Viśvabhū Tathāgata who appeared in the world. In this Bhadrakalpa, there was a Buddha who appeared in the world, named Krakucchanda Tathāgata. Furthermore, in this Bhadrakalpa, there was a Buddha who appeared in the world, named Kanakamuni Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One. Furthermore, in this Bhadrakalpa, there was a Buddha who appeared in the world, named Kāśyapa. Furthermore, in this Bhadrakalpa, I have appeared in the world, named Śākyamuni Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One."

世尊告曰：「比丘当知，过去九十一劫有佛出世，号毘婆尸如来、至真、等正觉。复次，三十一劫有佛出世，名式诘如来、至真、等正觉。复于彼三十一劫内有佛，名毘舍罗婆如来出世。于此贤劫中有佛出世，名拘屡孙如来。复于贤劫中有佛出世，名拘那含牟尼如来、至真、等正觉。复于贤劫中有佛出世，名曰迦叶。复于贤劫中，我出现世，释迦文如来、至真、等正觉。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

In the ninety-first eon, there was the Buddha Vipaśyin
In the thirty-first eon, the Tathāgata Śikhin appeared. Again
in that eon, the Tathāgata Viśvabhū appeared. In this
Bhadrakalpa, four Buddhas have appeared in the world: Krakucchanda
Kanakamuni, Kāśyapa, and the Sun-like Śākyamuni. If you wish
to know their names, they are all like this.

「九十一劫中，　　有佛毘婆尸，三十一劫中，　　式诘如来出。复于彼劫中，　　毘舍如来现，今日贤劫中，　　四佛复出世。拘孙那迦叶，　　如日照世间，欲知名字者，　　其号悉如是。

The Tathāgata Vipaśyin was born into the kṣatriya caste, the Tathāgata Śikhin was also born into the kṣatriya caste, the Tathāgata Viśvabhū was also born into the kṣatriya caste, the Tathāgata Krakucchanda was born into the brahmin caste, the Tathāgata Kanakamuni was born into the brahmin caste, the Tathāgata Kāśyapa was born into the brahmin caste, and I am now born into the kṣatriya caste.

「毘婆尸如来者出刹利种，式诘如来亦出刹利种，毘舍罗婆如来亦出刹利种，拘屡孙如来出婆罗门种，拘那含牟尼如来出婆罗门种，迦叶如来出婆罗门种，如我今出刹利种。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

The previous Buddhas who appeared were all born into the kṣatriya
caste, from Krakucchanda to Kāśyapa were born into the brahmin caste
The most honored one, incomparable, I, the teacher of gods and
humans today, with tranquil faculties, was born into the kṣatriya caste.

「前佛有现者，　　皆出刹利种，拘孙至迦叶，　　出于婆罗门。最尊无能及，　　我今天人师，诸根而淡泊，　　出于刹利姓。

"The Tathāgata Vipaśyin had the surname Gautama, the Tathāgata Śikhin also came from the Gautama clan, Viśvabhū also came from the Gautama clan, the Tathāgata Kāśyapa came from the Kāśyapa clan, Krakucchanda and Kanakamuni also came from the Kāśyapa clan, the same as before, without difference. I, the Tathāgata now, have the surname Gautama."

「毘婆尸如来姓瞿昙，式诘如来亦出瞿昙，比舍罗婆亦出瞿昙，迦叶如来出迦叶姓，拘楼孙、拘那含牟尼亦出迦叶姓，同上而无异，我今如来姓瞿昙。」

At that time, the World-Honored One then spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

Just as the first three Buddhas came from the Gautama clan
the last three came from the Kāśyapa clan, and were of
the Kāśyapa surname. Just as I am now in the present
revered by gods and humans, with tranquil faculties, I come from
the Gautama clan.

「如初诸三佛，　　出于瞿昙种，后三至迦叶，　　出于迦叶姓。如我今现在，　　天人所奉敬，诸根而淡泊，　　出于瞿昙姓。

Bhikṣus, you should know that Vipaśyin Tathāgata was of the Kṣatriya clan, Śikhin Tathāgata was also of the Kṣatriya clan, Viśvabhū Tathāgata was also of the Kṣatriya clan, Krakucchanda Tathāgata was of the Brāhmaṇa clan, Kanakamuni Tathāgata was also of the Brāhmaṇa clan, Kāśyapa Tathāgata was also of the Brāhmaṇa clan, and I, the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, am now of the Kṣatriya clan.

「比丘当知，毘婆尸如来姓拘邻若，式诘如来亦出拘邻若，毘舍罗婆如来亦出拘邻若，拘屡孙如来出婆罗堕，拘那含牟尼如来亦出婆罗堕，迦叶如来亦出婆罗堕，如我今如来、至真、等正觉出于拘邻若。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

Just as the first three Buddhas appeared in the Kṣatriya clan
the last three up to Kāśyapa appeared in the Brāhmaṇa clan
As I am now in the present, revered by gods and
humans, with tranquil faculties, I have appeared in the Kṣatriya clan.

「如初诸三佛，　　出于拘邻若，后三至迦叶，　　出于婆罗堕。如我今现在，　　天人所奉敬，诸根而淡泊，　　出于拘邻若。

"Vipaśyin Tathāgata attained Buddhahood sitting under a pāruṣaka tree, Śikhin Tathāgata attained Buddhahood sitting under a puṇḍarīka tree, Viśvabhū Tathāgata attained Buddhahood sitting under a pāruṣaka tree, Krakucchanda Tathāgata attained Buddhahood sitting under a śirīṣa tree, Kanakamuni Tathāgata attained Buddhahood sitting under a udumbara tree, Kāśyapa Tathāgata attained Buddhahood sitting under a nyagrodha tree, and as for me today, the Tathāgata attains Buddhahood sitting under a śāla tree."

「毘婆尸如来坐波罗利华树下而成佛道，式诘如来坐分陀利树下而成佛道，毘舍罗婆如来坐波罗树下而成佛道，拘屡孙如来坐尸利沙树下而成佛道，拘那含牟尼如来坐优头跋罗树下而成佛道，迦叶如来坐尼拘留树下而成道果，如我今日如来坐吉祥树下而成佛道。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

The first Buddha attained the Way under a pāruṣaka tree, Śikhin sat
under a puṇḍarīka tree, Viśvabhū sat under a pāruṣaka tree, Krakucchanda sat
under a śirīṣa tree, Kanakamuni sat under an udumbara tree, Kāśyapa sat
under a nyagrodha tree, and under the auspicious tree, I attain Buddhahood
today. The seven Buddhas, the Heavenly Honored Ones, illuminate the world. For
various reasons, they sit under various trees and each attains the fruit
of the Way.

「初一成佛道，　　波罗利树下，式坐分陀利，　　毘舍坐波罗。拘孙坐尸利，　　拘那跋罗下，迦叶拘留树，　　吉祥我成道。七佛天中天，　　照明于世间，因缘坐诸树，　　各成其道果。

"The disciples of Vipaśyin Tathāgata numbered one hundred and sixty-eight thousand, the disciples of Śikhin Tathāgata numbered one hundred and sixty thousand, the disciples of Viśvabhū Tathāgata numbered one hundred thousand, the disciples of Krakucchanda Tathāgata numbered eighty thousand, the disciples of Kanakamuni Tathāgata numbered seventy thousand, the disciples of Kāśyapa Tathāgata numbered sixty thousand, and like my disciples today, there are one thousand two hundred and fifty, all of whom are arhats, with all taints exhausted, and no further bonds."

「毘婆尸如来弟子有十六万八千之众，式诘如来弟子之众有十六万，毘舍罗婆如来弟子之众十万，拘屡孙如来弟子之众有八万人，拘那含牟尼如来弟子之众有七万人，迦叶如来弟子之众有六万众，如我今日弟子之众有千二百五十人，皆是阿罗汉，诸漏永尽，无复诸缚。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

One hundred and sixty-eight thousand were the disciples of Vipaśyin
one hundred and sixty thousand were the disciples of Śikhin
one hundred thousand bhikṣus were the disciples of Viśvabhū, eighty
thousand were the disciples of Krakucchanda, seventy thousand were the
disciples of Kanakamuni, and sixty thousand were the disciples of
Kāśyapa. All of them were arhats. Now, I, Śākyamuni, have
one thousand two hundred and fifty disciples. All of them
are true practitioners, manifesting the Dharma teachings. The remaining disciples
of the Dharma left behind are innumerable.

「百千六万八，　　毘婆尸弟子，百千及六万，　　式诘弟子众。百千比丘众，　　毘舍婆弟子，拘孙八万众，　　拘那含七万。迦叶六万众，　　皆是阿罗汉，我今释迦文，　　千二百五十。皆是真人行，　　布现于法教，遗法余弟子，　　其数不可计。

"The attendant of Vipaśyin Tathāgata was named Mahānavakara; the attendant of Śikhin Tathāgata was named Sudarśana; the attendant of Viśvabhū Tathāgata was named Jina; the attendant of Krakucchanda Tathāgata was named Kṣema; the attendant of Kanakamuni Tathāgata was named Virūḍhaka; the attendant of Kāśyapa Tathāgata was named Nāyaka; now my attendant is named Ānanda."

「毘婆尸如来侍者，名曰大导师；式诘如来侍者，名曰善觉；毘舍罗婆如来侍者，名曰胜众；拘屡孙如来侍者，名曰吉祥；拘那含牟尼如来侍者，名曰毘罗先；迦叶如来侍者，名曰导师；我今侍者，名曰阿难。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

Great Path, and Sudarśana, Jina, and Kṣema, Virūḍhaka
Nāyaka, and Ānanda as the seventh attendant. These
people make offerings to the sages, without missing
the right time, reciting and upholding, not losing their meaning.

「大道及善觉，　　胜众与吉祥，毘罗先导师，　　阿难第七侍。此人供养圣，　　无有不得时，讽诵又受持，　　不失其义理。

"The lifespan of Vipaśyin Tathāgata was eighty-four thousand years, Śikhin Tathāgata's lifespan was seventy thousand years, Viśvabhū Tathāgata's lifespan was sixty thousand years, Krakucchanda Tathāgata's lifespan was fifty thousand years, Kanakamuni Tathāgata's lifespan was forty thousand years, Kāśyapa Tathāgata's lifespan was twenty thousand years, and my lifespan today is extremely short, not exceeding one hundred years."

「毘婆尸如来寿八万四千岁，式诘如来寿七万岁，毘舍罗婆如来寿六万岁，拘屡孙如来寿五万岁，拘那含如来寿四万岁，迦叶如来寿二万岁，如我今日寿极减少，极寿不过百岁。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说斯偈：

The first Buddha had a lifespan of eighty-four thousand years, the next Buddha had a
lifespan of seventy thousand years, Viśvabhū had a lifespan of sixty thousand years, Krakucchanda had
a lifespan of fifty thousand years, Kanakamuni had a lifespan of forty thousand years, Kāśyapa
had a lifespan of twenty thousand years, and my lifespan today is only one hundred years.

「初佛八万四，　　次佛七万岁，毘舍婆六万，　　拘留寿五万、一万二万年，　　是拘那含寿，迦叶寿二万，　　唯我寿百年。

"Thus, bhikṣus! The Tathāgata knows and understands the names and titles of all the Buddhas, all clearly and distinctly, the kinds and origins of all, and he knows their upholding of precepts, wisdom, meditation, and liberation."

「如是，诸比丘！如来观知诸佛姓名号字，皆悉分明，种类出处靡不贯练，持戒、智慧、禅定、解脱皆悉了知。」

At that time, Ānanda addressed the World-Honored One, saying, "The Tathāgata also says that the past Buddhas who have attained parinirvāṇa, the Tathāgata also knows; the future Buddhas who will come, the Tathāgata also knows. Why does the Tathāgata not record the deeds of those Buddhas? Now he only speaks of the origins and ends of the seven Buddhas."

尔时，阿难白世尊言：「如来亦说，过去恒沙诸佛取灭度者，如来亦知；当来恒沙诸佛方当来者，如来亦知。如来何故不记尔许佛所造？今但说七佛本末。」

The Buddha told Ānanda, "There are all causes and conditions, origins and ends. The Tathāgata speaks of the origins and ends of the seven Buddhas; the past Buddhas of the Ganges River sands also spoke of the origins and ends of the seven Buddhas; when Maitreya appears in the world in the future, he will also record the origins and ends of the seven Buddhas; when the Tathāgata Siṃha appears, he will also record the origins and ends of the seven Buddhas; when the Buddha Anuvyañjana appears in the world, he will also record the origins and ends of the seven Buddhas; when the Buddha Prabhāsa appears in the world, he will also record the names and titles of the seven Buddhas; when the Buddha Vimaloṣṇīṣa appears in the world, he will also record the origins and ends of Kāśyapa; when the Buddha Ratnaprabhāsa appears in the world, he will also record the origins and ends of Śākyamuni."

佛告阿难：「皆有因缘本末故，如来说七佛之本末；过去恒沙诸佛，亦说七佛本末；将来弥勒出现世时，亦当记七佛之本末；若师子应如来出时，亦当记七佛之本末；若承柔顺佛出世时，亦当记七佛之本末；若光焰佛出现世时，亦当记七佛之名号；若无垢佛出现世时，亦当记迦叶之本末；若宝光佛出现世时，亦当记释迦文之本末。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

Siṃha, Anuvyañjana, Prabhāsa, Vimaloṣṇīṣa, Ratnaprabhāsa, Maitreya, in succession, will
all attain Buddhahood. Maitreya will record Śikhin Buddha, Siṃha
will record Vipaśyin, Anuvyañjana will record Krakucchanda, Prabhāsa will
record Kanakamuni, Vimaloṣṇīṣa will record Kāśyapa, all speaking of
their past causes and conditions. Ratnaprabhāsa will become the
third Buddha and will also record my name. The
past three Buddhas and those of the future will
all record the origins and ends of the seven Buddhas.

「师子柔顺光，　　无垢及宝光，弥勒之次第，　　皆当成佛道。弥勒记式佛，　　师子记毘舍，柔顺记拘孙，　　光焰记牟尼，无垢记迦叶，　　皆说曩所缘，宝光成三佛，　　亦当记我号。过去诸三佛，　　及以将来者，皆当记七佛，　　曩所之本末。

"For this reason, the Tathāgata only records the names of the seven Buddhas."

「由此因缘故，如来记七佛名号耳。」

At that time, Ānanda addressed the World-Honored One, saying, "What is the name of this sūtra? How should we uphold it?"

尔时，阿难白世尊言：「此经名何等？当云何奉行？」

The Buddha told Ānanda, "This sūtra is called 'The Record of the Names of the Buddhas.' You should remember and uphold it."

佛告阿难：「此经名曰记佛名号，当念奉行。」

At that time, Ānanda and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，阿难及诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
