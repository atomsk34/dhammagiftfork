EĀ 48.6

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

48.6 （六）

Parallel to: Ud 4.4 (~) / SĀ 1330 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at the Karanda Bamboo Grove in the city of Rajagriha, together with a great assembly of five hundred bhikshus.

一时，佛在罗阅城迦兰陀竹园所，与大比丘众五百人俱。

At that time, Venerable Sariputra was mending his old robe in a secluded place on Vulture Peak Mountain. At that time, ten thousand Brahma-like gods from the Brahma Heaven descended and came to where Sariputra was. They bowed their heads at his feet, each circumambulating and attending him, and also praised him with this verse:

尔时，尊者舍利弗在耆阇崛山中屏猥之处，补纳故衣。尔时，有十千梵迦夷天从梵天没，来至舍利弗所，头面礼足，各围遶侍焉，又以此偈而叹颂曰：

We take refuge in the supreme among humans, we take refuge in the
most honored among humans. Now we cannot rely on any kind of meditation.

「归命人中上，　　归命人中尊，我等今不得，　　为依何等禅？」

At that time, after the ten thousand Brahma-like gods had spoken these words, Sariputra silently consented. Then the gods, seeing that Sariputra had silently consented, bowed at his feet and departed. Not long after the gods had left, Sariputra immediately entered the Diamond Samadhi.

是时，十千梵迦夷天说此语已，舍利弗默然可之。尔时，诸天以见舍利弗默然可已，即礼足退去。诸天去未远，舍利弗即入金刚三昧。

At that time, there were two ghosts, one named Garala and the other Uppaka. They were sent by the King of the North to the King of the East to discuss matters concerning humans and gods. As the two ghosts passed through the sky, they saw Sariputra sitting cross-legged, with his mind focused in front, peacefully and still. The ghost Garala said to the other ghost, "I can now punch this śramaṇa on the head with my fist."

是时有二鬼，一名伽罗，二名优婆伽罗。毘沙门天王使遣至毘留勒天王所，欲论人、天之事。是时，二鬼从彼虚空而过，遥见舍利弗结加趺坐，系念在前，意寂然定，伽罗鬼谓彼鬼言：「我今堪任以拳打此沙门头。」

The ghost Uppaka said to the second ghost, "Do not have this intention to punch the śramaṇa on the head. Because this śramaṇa has great spiritual power and great authority. This Venerable One is named Sariputra, the most intelligent and talented among the Buddha's disciples. He is the foremost among the disciples of wisdom. He has endured immeasurable suffering for a long time."

优波伽罗鬼语第二鬼曰：「汝勿兴此意打沙门头。所以然者，此沙门极有神德，有大威力，此尊名舍利弗，世尊弟子中聪明高才无复过是，智慧弟子中最为第一。备于长夜，受苦无量。」

The ghost repeated three times, "I can endure punching this śramaṇa on the head."

是时，彼鬼再三曰：「我能堪任打此沙门头。」

The ghost Uppaka said, "If you do not follow my words now, you can stay here. I want to leave you and go."

优波伽罗鬼报曰：「汝今不随我语者，汝便住此，吾欲舍汝去此。」

The evil ghost said, "Are you afraid of this śramaṇa?"

恶鬼曰：「汝畏此沙门乎？」

The ghost Uppaka said, "I am indeed afraid of him. If you hit this śramaṇa with your hand, the ground will split into two parts. Then there will be violent wind and rain, and the ground will also shake. The gods will be alarmed, and the ground will shake. The Four Heavenly Kings will also be frightened. The Four Heavenly Kings already know about us and are not at ease."

优波伽罗鬼曰：「我实畏之，设汝以手打此沙门者，此地当分为二分，正尔，当暴风疾雨，地亦振动，诸天惊动，地已振动，四天王亦当惊怖，四天王已知于我等，不安其所。」

At that time, the evil ghost said, "I can now endure insulting this śramaṇa." The good ghost, having heard this, then left.

是时恶鬼曰：「我今堪任辱此沙门。」善鬼闻已，便舍而去。

At that time, the evil ghost immediately struck Śāriputra's head with his hand. Then the earth shook violently, and violent winds and rain came from all four directions. The ground immediately split into two parts, and the evil ghost fell into hell with his whole body. At that time, Venerable Śāriputra arose from samādhi, adjusted his robes, descended from Vulture Peak, went to the Bamboo Grove, approached the World-Honored One, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side.

时，彼恶鬼即以手打舍利弗头。是时，天地大动，四面有暴风疾雨，寻时来至，地即分为二分，此恶鬼即以全身堕地狱中。尔时，尊者舍利弗即从三昧起，整衣服，下耆阇崛山，往诣竹园至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。

At that time, the Buddha said to Śāriputra, "Are you now free from illness in your body?"

尔时，佛告舍利弗曰：「汝今身体无有疾病乎？」

Śāriputra said, "My body is generally free from illness, but I am suffering from a headache."

舍利弗言：「体素无患，唯苦头痛。」

The World-Honored One said, "The ghost named Garala struck your head with his hand. If that ghost were to strike Mount Sumeru with his hand, Mount Sumeru would immediately split into two parts. The reason is that that ghost has great power. Now this ghost is experiencing the retribution for his sins, and his entire body has entered the Avīci Hell."

世尊告曰：「伽罗鬼以手打汝头。若当彼鬼以手打须弥山者，即时须弥山便为二分。所以然者，彼鬼有大力故。今此鬼受其罪报故，全身入阿鼻地狱中。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "It is truly amazing! The power of the vajra samādhi is indeed like this. Because of the power of this samādhi, there is no harm. Even if Mount Sumeru were to strike its head, it would not be able to move a single hair. The reason is this. Listen, bhikṣus! In this Bhadrakalpa, there was a Buddha named Krakucchanda Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One. That Buddha had two great śrāvakas, one named Samantabhadra and the other named Mahāprajñā. Bhikṣu Samantabhadra was foremost in supernatural powers, and bhikṣu Mahāprajñā was foremost in wisdom, just as Śāriputra is foremost in wisdom and Maudgalyāyana is foremost in supernatural powers in my present time. At that time, the two bhikṣus Samantabhadra and Mahāprajñā both attained the vajra samādhi. Once, bhikṣu Samantabhadra was in a secluded place, entering the vajra samādhi. At that time, cowherds, shepherds, and firewood gatherers saw this bhikṣu sitting in meditation and each said to each other, 'This śramaṇa has attained the state of impermanence today.' Then the cowherds and firewood gatherers collected various grasses and wood, piled them on top of the bhikṣu, set them on fire, and left.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「甚奇！甚特！金刚三昧力乃至于斯。由此三昧力故无所伤害，正使须弥山打其头者，终不能动其毫毛。所以然者，比丘听之。于此贤劫中有佛，名拘屡孙如来、至真、等正觉。彼佛有二大声闻，一名等寿，二名大智。比丘等寿，神足第一；比丘大智，智慧第一，如我今日舍利弗智慧第一，目干连神足第一。尔时，等寿、大智二比丘，俱得金刚三昧。当于一时，等寿比丘在闲静之处，入金刚三昧。时，诸牧牛人、牧羊人、取薪草人，见此比丘坐禅，各各自相谓言：『此沙门今日以取无常。』是时，牧牛人及取薪人集诸草木，［卄／积］比丘身上，以火烧已，而舍之去。

At that time, bhikṣu Samantabhadra arose from samādhi, adjusted his robes, and left. At that time, the bhikṣu put on his robes, took his alms bowl, and entered the village to beg for food. At that time, the firewood gatherers saw this bhikṣu begging for food in the village and each said to each other, 'This bhikṣu died yesterday and we burned him with fire. Today he has come back to life. We should give him a name, and call him "Resurrected"." If a bhikṣu attains the vajra samādhi, fire cannot burn him, a sword cannot penetrate him, water cannot float him away, and he cannot be harmed by others. Thus, bhikṣus! The vajra samādhi has such majestic virtue. Now Śāriputra has attained this samādhi. Śāriputra bhikṣu often dwells in two places: the empty samādhi and the vajra samādhi. Therefore, bhikṣus! You should seek skillful means to practice the vajra samādhi. Thus, bhikṣus! You should train in this way."

「是时，等寿比丘即从三昧起，正衣服，便退而去。是时，比丘即以其日，著衣持钵，入村乞食。时，诸取薪草人见此比丘村中乞食，各各自相谓言：『此比丘昨日以取命终，我等以火焚烧，今日复还活，今当立字，字曰还活。』若有比丘得金刚三昧者，火所不烧，刀斫不入，水所不漂，不为他所中伤。如是，比丘！金刚三昧威德如是。今舍利弗得此三昧，舍利弗比丘多游二处：空三昧、金刚三昧。是故，诸比丘！当求方便，行金刚三昧。如是，比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "I will teach you about bhikṣu Śāriputra's wisdom, great wisdom, discerning wisdom, boundless wisdom, swift wisdom, all-pervading wisdom, sharp wisdom, profound wisdom, liberating wisdom, contentment with few desires, solitude, diligence, mindfulness without distraction, accomplished precepts, accomplished samādhi, accomplished wisdom, liberated view, accomplished liberation, gentleness, non-contentiousness, elimination of evil, clear eloquence, tolerance of all words, praise of leaving evil, always mindful of leaving, compassion for the arising of life, blazing the true Dharma, and teaching others the Dharma without weariness."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「我当教汝，如舍利弗比丘，比丘智慧、大智、分别广智、无边智、捷疾之智、普游智、利智、甚深智、断智，少欲知足、闲静勇猛、念不分散、戒成就、三昧成就、智慧、解脱见慧成就、柔和无争、去恶辩了、忍诸言语、叹说离恶、常念去离、愍念生萠、然炽正法、与人说法无有厌足。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

Ten thousand heavenly beings, all of them Brahmakāyika, take refuge in
Śāriputra on Vulture Peak Mountain, saying: 'We take refuge in the
supreme among humans, we take refuge in the most honored among
humans. We do not know now on what meditation to rely.'
Thus, the disciples adorn the Bodhi tree of the Buddha like
the Nandana Garden of the heavens, with incomparable happiness and joy.

「十千诸天人，　　尽是梵迦夷，自归舍利弗，　　于灵鹫山顶：『归命人中上，　　归命人中尊，我今不能知，　　为依何等禅？』如是弟子花，　　庄严佛道树，如天昼度园，　　快乐无有比。」

"The disciples' flowers refer to Śāriputra the bhikṣu. The reason is that this person can adorn the Bodhi tree. The Bodhi tree refers to the Tathāgata, who can provide shelter for all sentient beings. Therefore, bhikṣus! You should be mindful and diligently apply your courage and vigor, just like Śāriputra the bhikṣu. Thus, bhikṣus! You should train in this way."

「弟子华者，即是舍利弗比丘是。所以然者，此人则能庄严佛树。道树者，即如来是也；如来能覆盖一切众生。是故，比丘！当念勤加勇猛精进，如舍利弗比丘。如是，比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully upheld it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
