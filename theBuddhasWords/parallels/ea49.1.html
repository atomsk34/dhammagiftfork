EĀ 49.1

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

Chapter 49 放牛品第四十九第四分别诵

49.1 （一）

Parallel to: MN 33 / AN 11.17 (~) / SĀ 1249

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "If a cowherd possesses eleven qualities, the herd will never increase and he will not be able to protect the cows. What are the eleven? Here, the cowherd does not distinguish their colors, does not understand their characteristics, does not groom them when they should be groomed, does not cover their wounds, does not smoke them in a timely manner, does not know where the good grass is, does not know where it is safe, does not know where to cross the cows, does not know the right time, when the cows are to be milked he takes all the milk without leaving any for the calves, and he does not protect the big cows that are useful in a timely manner. These are called, bhikṣus, if a cowherd possesses these eleven qualities, he will not be able to raise the cows and protect their bodies.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「若放牛儿成就十一法，牛群终不长益，亦复不能将护其牛。云何为十一？于是，放牛人亦不别其色，不解其相，应摩刷而不摩刷，不覆护疮痍，不随时放烟，不知良田茂草处，不知安隐之处，亦复不知渡牛处所，不知时宜，若［声－耳＋牛］牛时不留遗余尽取［声－耳＋牛］之，是时诸大牛可任用者不随时将护。是谓，比丘！若牧牛人成就此十一法，终不能长养其牛，将护其身。

Now, the bhikṣus in this assembly are also like this, and will not be able to grow and benefit. What are the eleven? Here, the bhikṣus do not distinguish their colors, do not understand their characteristics, do not groom them when they should be groomed, do not cover their wounds, do not smoke them in a timely manner, do not know where the good grass is, do not know where to cross, do not know where it is safe, do not know the right time, when eating they do not leave any for others, and do not respect the elder bhikṣus.

「今此众中比丘亦复如是，终不能有所长益。云何为十一？于是，比丘不别其色，不晓其相，应摩刷而不摩刷，不覆护疮痍，不随时放烟，不知良田茂草处，不知渡处，亦复不知安隐之处，不知时宜，食不知留遗余，诸长老比丘亦不敬待。

What is a bhikṣu's ignorance of color? Here, a bhikṣu does not know the four great elements and the form derived from the four great elements. Thus, a bhikṣu does not distinguish their colors.

「云何比丘不知色？于是，比丘有四大及四大所造色，皆悉不知。如是比丘不别其色。

What is a bhikṣu's failure to distinguish their characteristics? Here, a bhikṣu does not know foolish conduct, nor does he know wise conduct, and does not know them as they really are. Thus, a bhikṣu does not distinguish their characteristics.

「云何比丘不别其相？于是，比丘不知行愚，亦不知行智，如实而不知。如是比丘不别其相。

What is a bhikṣu's failure to brush and polish? Here, a bhikṣu, when seeing forms with the eye, gives rise to thoughts of forms and various distracting thoughts, and does not guard the eye faculty. With unwholesome attention, he creates various calamities and does not guard the eye faculty. Thus, a bhikṣu, when hearing sounds with the ear, smelling aromas with the nose, tasting flavors with the tongue, touching tangibles with the body, and cognizing dharmas with the mind, gives rise to various distracting thoughts, and also does not guard the mind faculty. He does not change his conduct. Thus, a bhikṣu should brush and polish but does not.

「云何比丘应摩刷而不摩刷？于是，比丘若眼见色便起色想，有诸乱念，又且不守护眼根，以不善摄念，造众殃舋，不守护眼根；如是，比丘若耳闻声，鼻嗅香，舌知味，身知细滑，意知法，起诸乱想，亦不守护意根，不改其行。如是比丘应摩刷而不摩刷。

What is a bhikṣu's failure to cover and protect wounds? Here, a bhikṣu gives rise to thoughts of desire and does not abandon them, nor does he remove those thoughts. If he gives rise to thoughts of anger, thoughts of killing, and various evil and unwholesome thoughts, he does not abandon them. Thus, a bhikṣu fails to cover and protect wounds.

「云何比丘不覆护疮？于是，比丘起欲想而不舍离，亦不除去其念；若起瞋想、杀害想，起诸恶不善想，终不舍之。如是比丘不覆护疮。

What is a bhikṣu's failure to raise smoke in a timely manner? Here, a bhikṣu, with regard to the Dharma he recites, does not teach it to others in a timely manner. Thus, a bhikṣu fails to raise smoke in a timely manner.

「云何比丘不随时起烟？于是，比丘所讽诵法，不随时向人说。如是比丘不随时放烟。

What is a bhikṣu's failure to know good fields and fertile grass? Here, a bhikṣu does not know the four foundations of mindfulness as they really are. Thus, a bhikṣu fails to know good fields and fertile grass.

「云何比丘不知良田茂草？于是，比丘不知四意止，如实而不知。如是比丘不知良田茂草处。

What is a bhikṣu's failure to know the ford? Here, a bhikṣu does not distinguish the eightfold noble path. Thus, a bhikṣu fails to know the ford.

「云何比丘不知渡处？于是，比丘不别贤圣八品道，如是比丘不知渡处。

What is a bhikṣu's failure to know what is dear? Here, a bhikṣu does not know the twelve divisions of the sūtras, the gāthās, the prophecies, the verses, the causes and conditions, the origins, the vaipulya, the parables, the birth stories, the teachings, the extensive, and the unprecedented teachings. Thus, a bhikṣu fails to know what is dear.

「云何比丘不知所爱？于是，比丘于十二部，契经、祇夜、授决、偈、因缘、本末、方等、譬喻、生经、说、广普、未曾有法。如是比丘不知所爱。

What is a bhikṣu's failure to know the right time? Here, a bhikṣu goes to the homes of the lowly and gamblers. Thus, a bhikṣu fails to know the right time.

「云何比丘不知时宜？于是，比丘便往轻贱家、博戏家。如是比丘不知时宜。

What is a bhikṣu's failure to leave a remainder? Here, when a faithful brāhman or upāsaka comes to invite him, the bhikṣus greedily cling to food and drink without knowing moderation. Thus, a bhikṣu fails to leave a remainder.

「云何比丘不留遗余？于是，比丘有信梵志、优婆塞往而请之，然诸比丘贪著饮食不知止足。如是比丘不留遗余。

What is a bhikṣu's failure to respect senior bhikṣus of high virtue? Here, a bhikṣu fails to give rise to a respectful mind towards those of virtue. Thus, a bhikṣu commits many offenses. This is called a bhikṣu's failure to respect the elders. If a bhikṣu possesses these eleven qualities, he will not be of much benefit in this Dharma.

「云何比丘不敬长老诸高德比丘？于是，比丘不起恭敬之心向诸有德人，如是比丘多有所犯。是谓比丘不敬长老。若有比丘成就十一法，终不能于此法中多所饶益。

If a cowherd possesses eleven qualities, he can protect his cows, never missing the right time, and bring them benefit. What are the eleven? Here, a cowherd knows their colors, distinguishes their characteristics, grooms them when necessary, protects their wounds, sets up smoke at the right times, knows where the good grass is, knows the fordable places, loves his cows, distinguishes the right times, also knows their natures and behavior, and when the cows are calling, knows to leave a remainder. He also knows how to protect those that are useful. Thus, a cowherd protects his cows. Thus, bhikṣus! If a cowherd possesses these eleven qualities, he will never miss the right time, and will never be defeated.

「若复牧牛人成就十一法者，能拥护其牛，终不失时，有所饶益。云何为十一？于是，牧牛人知其色，别其相，应摩刷而摩刷，覆护疮痍，随时而起烟，知良田茂草处，知渡要处，爱其牛，分别时宜，亦知性行，若［声－耳＋牛］牛时知留遗余，亦复知随时将护可任用者。如是牧牛人将护牛。如是，比丘！若牧牛人成就此十一法，不失时节者，终不可沮坏。

Thus, bhikṣus! If a bhikṣu possesses these eleven qualities, he will be of much benefit in this present life. What are the eleven qualities? Here, a bhikṣu knows colors, knows characteristics, knows grooming, knows protecting wounds, knows setting up smoke, knows where the good grass is, knows what is loved, knows distinguishing the path, knows the fordable places, knows eating in moderation, and knows respecting and serving senior bhikṣus, paying homage at the right times.

「如是，比丘若成就十一法者，于此现法中多所饶益。云何十一法？于是，比丘知色，知相，知摩刷，知覆护疮，知起烟，知良田茂草处，知所爱，知择道行，知渡处，知食止足，知敬奉长老比丘，随时礼拜。

How does a bhikṣu know colors? Here, a bhikṣu knows the four great elements and the colors derived from the four great elements. This is called a bhikṣu knowing colors.

「云何比丘而知色？于是，比丘知四大色，亦知四大所造色。是谓比丘知色。

How does a bhikṣu know characteristics? Here, a bhikṣu knows the characteristics of fools and the characteristics of the wise, and knows them as they really are. This is how a bhikṣu knows characteristics.

「云何比丘知相？于是，比丘知愚相，知智相，如实而知之。如是比丘知相。

How does a bhikṣu know polishing? Here, a bhikṣu, when thoughts of desire arise, knows them and abandons them, and does not eagerly pursue them, so that there are no more thoughts of desire. When thoughts of anger, malice, and other evil and unwholesome things arise, he knows them and abandons them, and does not eagerly pursue them, so that there are no more thoughts of anger. This is how a bhikṣu knows polishing.

「云何比丘知摩刷？于是，比丘若欲想起，念知舍离，亦不殷勤，永无欲想；若恚想、害想，及诸恶不善想起，念知舍离，亦不殷勤，永无恚想。如是比丘为知摩刷。

How does a bhikṣu know covering wounds? Here, a bhikṣu, when seeing forms with the eye, does not give rise to thoughts of forms, is not attached to them, and purifies the eye faculty. He removes sorrow and evil and unwholesome dharmas, and his mind is not delighted. He protects the eye faculty in this way. Similarly, a bhikṣu, when hearing sounds with the ear, smelling aromas with the nose, tasting flavors with the tongue, touching tangibles with the body, and cognizing dharmas with the mind, does not give rise to thoughts of cognition, is not attached to them, and purifies the mind faculty. This is how a bhikṣu knows covering wounds.

「云何比丘知覆护疮？于是，比丘若眼见色不起色想，亦不染著而净眼根，除去愁忧恶不善法，心不贪乐，于中而护眼根；如是，比丘若耳闻声，鼻嗅香，舌知味，身知细滑，意知法，不起识想，亦不染著而净意根。如是比丘知覆护疮。

How does a bhikṣu know producing smoke? Here, a bhikṣu widely teaches others the Dharma he has heard. This is how a bhikṣu knows producing smoke.

「云何比丘知起烟？于是，比丘所从闻法广与人说。如是比丘为知起烟。

How does a bhikṣu know the place of good fields and lush grass? Here, a bhikṣu truly knows the eightfold noble path. This is called a bhikṣu knowing the place of good fields and lush grass.

「云何比丘知良田茂草处？于是，比丘贤圣八品道如实知之。是谓比丘知良田茂草处。

How does a bhikṣu know what is loved? Here, if a bhikṣu hears the Dharma treasure spoken by the Tathāgata, his mind will be delighted. This is how a bhikṣu knows what is loved.

「云何比丘知所爱？于是，比丘若闻如来所说法宝，心便爱乐。如是比丘为知所爱。

How does a bhikṣu choose the path to practice? Here, a bhikṣu chooses to practice the twelve divisions of the sūtras, namely: sūtras, geya, vyākaraṇa, gāthā, nidāna, avadāna, vaipulya, upamā, itivṛttaka, jātaka, adbhuta, and avadāna. This is how a bhikṣu knows to choose the path to practice.

「云何比丘择道行？于是，比丘于十二部经择而行之，所谓契经、祇夜、授决、偈、因缘、本末、方等、譬喻、生经、说、广普、未曾有法。如是比丘知择道行。

How does a bhikṣu know the place to cross over? Here, a bhikṣu knows the four foundations of mindfulness. This is called a bhikṣu knowing the place to cross over.

「云何比丘知渡处？于是，比丘知四意止。是谓比丘知渡处。

How does a bhikṣu know to be content with food? Here, when a faithful brāhman or upāsaka comes to invite a bhikṣu, he is not greedy for food and drink, and is able to be content. This is how a bhikṣu knows to be content.

「云何比丘知食止足？于是，比丘有信梵志、优婆塞来请者，不贪饮食，能自止足。如是比丘为知止足。

How does a bhikṣu respectfully serve the elder bhikṣus in a timely manner? Here, a bhikṣu always uses good conduct of body, speech, and mind towards the elder bhikṣus. This is how a bhikṣu respectfully serves the elder bhikṣus in a timely manner. Thus, if one accomplishes these eleven dharmas, one will greatly benefit in this present life.

「云何比丘随时恭奉长老比丘？于是，比丘恒以身、口、意善行，向诸长老比丘。如是比丘随时恭奉长老比丘。如是。若成就十一法者，于现法中多所饶益。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

The cowherd who is not negligent, the owner obtains his blessings
In six years, the six cows will be multiplied to sixty
The bhikṣu who accomplishes the precepts, attains mastery in meditation, with
the six sense faculties tranquil, in six years attains the six supernormal powers.

「牧牛不放逸，　　其主获其福，六牛六年中，　　展转六十牛。比丘戒成就，　　于禅得自在，六根而寂然，　　六年成六通。

"So it is, bhikṣu! If a person can abandon these evil dharmas and accomplish the eleven dharmas that follow, they will greatly benefit in this present life. Thus, bhikṣu! You should train in this way."

「如是。比丘！若有人能离此恶法，成后十一法者，于现法中多所饶益。如是，比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said, and joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
