EĀ 49.6

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

49.6 （六）

Parallel to: MN 92 / Snp 3.7

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at the Karanda Bamboo Grove in the city of Rajagriha, together with a great assembly of five hundred bhikshus.

一时，佛在罗阅城迦兰陀竹园所，与大比丘众五百人俱。

At that time, in the city of Rajagriha, there was a brahman named Shila who was proficient in all arts and the records of the scriptures of non-Buddhist teachings. He was well-versed in astronomy and geography and also taught five hundred brahman youths. There was also a non-Buddhist scholar named Shini in that city who was widely knowledgeable and was loved and respected by King Bimbisara, who provided him with offerings and necessities according to the season.

尔时，罗阅城中有梵志，名曰施罗，备知诸术，外道异学经籍所记，天文、地理靡不贯练，又复教授五百梵志童子。又彼城中有异学之士，名曰翅宁，多有所知，为频毘娑罗王所见爱敬，随时供养给与梵志所须之施。

At that time, the Tathāgata's fame spread far and wide. He was the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, Perfect in Knowledge and Conduct, Well-Gone, Knower of the Secular World, Unsurpassed Person, Tamer of Men, Teacher of Gods and Men, Buddha, Blessed One, who had liberated countless beings and appeared in the world. At that time, the brahman Shini had this thought: "The Tathāgata's name is very difficult to hear. Now I wish to go and inquire, pay respects, and make offerings." At that time, the brahman Shini went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. At that time, the brahman said to the World-Honored One, "Shakyamuni Gautama! What is your family name? "

尔时，如来名称远布，如来、至真、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐，度人无量，出现世间。是时，翅宁梵志兴此念：「如来名号甚为难闻，今我欲往问讯，亲近礼敬。」是时，翅宁梵志便往佛所，头面礼足，在一面坐。尔时，梵志白世尊言：「沙门瞿昙！为姓何等？」

The Buddha told the brahman, "My family name is kshatriya."

佛告梵志：「吾姓刹利。」

The brahman asked, "All Brahmins have this discussion: 'Our family name is the most noble, there is none greater.' Some say, 'The family name is white.' Some say, 'The family name is black.' Brahmins claim to be born from Brahma. Now, Shakyamuni Gautama! What do you wish to discuss and say? "

梵志问曰：「诸婆罗门各有此论：『吾姓最豪，无有出者。』或言：『姓白。』或言：『姓黑。』婆罗门自称言：『梵天所生。』今，沙门瞿昙！欲何等论说？」

The Buddha told him, "Brahmin, you should know that in marriage, one should seek a noble and distinguished family. However, in my true Dharma, there are no names of high and low, right and wrong."

佛告之曰：「梵志当知，其有婚姻嫁娶，便当求豪贵之姓。然我正法之中，无有高下、是非之名姓也。」

The brahman again said, "How is it, Gautama! Only when one is born in a pure place can the Dharma be purified?"

梵志复白言：「云何，瞿昙！生处清净，然后法得清净？」

The Buddha told the brahman, "Do you use the Dharma to purify the place of birth?"

佛告梵志：「汝用法清净，生处清净为乎？」

The brahman said again, "The various Brahmins each have this discussion: 'Our surname is the most noble, there is none who surpasses.' Some say, 'The surname is white.' Some say, 'The surname is black.' The Brahmins themselves claim to be born from Brahma."

梵志又曰：「诸婆罗门各兴此论：『吾姓最豪，无有出者。』或言：『姓白。』或言：『姓黑。』婆罗门自称言：『梵天所生。』」

The Buddha told the brahman, "If a Kshatriya woman marries into a Brahmin family and gives birth to a son, from which surname would he be?"

佛告梵志：「若当刹利女适婆罗门家，设生男儿者，当从何姓？」

The brahman replied, "He would be called a Brahmin. Because of the father's form, this child is born."

梵志报曰：「彼当言婆罗门种。所以然者，由父形故，得有此儿。」

The Buddha told the brahman, "If a Brahmin woman marries into a Kshatriya family and gives birth to a son, from which surname would he be?"

佛告梵志：「若复婆罗门女出适刹利家，生男儿者，彼当从何姓？」

The brahman replied, "That person would be of the Kshatriya caste. Because of the father's remains, this child is born."

梵志报曰：「彼人当是刹利种。所以然者，由父遗形故，得有此儿。」

The Buddha told the brahman, "Think carefully before answering me. What you said before and after are not consistent. How is it, brahman! If a donkey gives birth to a foal after a horse, would you call it a horse or a donkey?"

佛告梵志：「熟自思惟，然后报吾。汝今所说前与后皆不相应。云何，梵志！设驴从马后生驹者，当言是马？为是驴也？」

The brahman replied, "Such a creature would be called a donkey-horse. Because of the donkey's remains, this foal is born."

梵志报曰：「如此之类当言驴马。所以然者，由驴遗形故，得此驹也。」

The Buddha told the brahman, "Think carefully before answering me. What you said before and after are not consistent. What you said before was that if a kshatriya girl marries into a brahman family and gives birth to a child, it is called a brahman caste. Now you say that if a donkey follows a horse and gives birth to a foal, it is called a donkey-horse. Isn't this contradicting your previous statement? Moreover, brahman! If a horse follows a donkey and gives birth to a foal, what would you call it?"

佛告梵志：「汝熟思惟，然后报吾。汝今所说前后不相应。汝前所说刹利女出适婆罗门家，若生儿者，便言婆罗门种；今驴逐马生驹者，便言驴马。将不违前语乎？设复，梵志！若马逐驴生驹者，名之云何？」

The brahman replied, "It would be called a horse-donkey."

梵志报曰：「当名为马驴。」

The Buddha said to him, "How is it, brahman! Is there any difference between a horse-donkey and a donkey-horse? If someone says a bushel of treasure and someone else says a bushel of treasure, are these two meanings different?"

佛告之曰：「云何，梵志！马驴、驴马岂复有异乎？若复有人言宝一斛，复有人言一斛宝，此二义岂有异乎？」

The brahman replied, "This is one meaning. The reason is that treasure one and one treasure have the same meaning."

梵志报曰：「此是一义。所以然者，宝一、一宝此义不异也。」

The Buddha said to the brahman, "How is it that horse-donkey and donkey-horse are not one meaning?"

佛告梵志：「云何马驴、驴马此非一义乎？」

The brahman replied, "Now, Shakyamuni! Although you have these words, the Brahmins still claim, 'Our family is the most noble, there is none who surpasses us.'"

梵志报言：「今，沙门瞿昙！虽有斯言，然婆罗门自称言：『吾姓最豪，无有出者。』」

The Buddha said to the brahman, "You first praised its mother and then again praised its father. If both the father and mother are of the Brahmin caste, and later give birth to two sons, then among them, one son has many skills and is knowledgeable in all matters, while the second son knows nothing. At that time, which one would the parents respect? Would they respect the wise one or the ignorant one?"

佛告梵志：「汝先称誉其母，后复叹说其父。若复父亦是婆罗门种，母亦是婆罗门种，后生二儿，彼时其中一儿，多诸技术，无事不览，第二子者了无所知。是时，父母为敬待何者？为当敬待有智者？为当敬待无所知者？」

The brahman replied, "The parents should respect the one with high virtue and wisdom, not the one without wisdom. The reason is that this son now knows nothing that is not understood and nothing that is not familiar. It is right to respect this son, not the son without wisdom."

梵志报曰：「其父母应当敬待高德聪明者，不应敬待无有智者。所以然者，今此一子无事不了，无事不闲，正应敬待此子，不应敬待无智之子。」

The Buddha said to the brahman, "If those two sons, the wise one, then again gives rise to the intention to commit the ten unwholesome dharmas of killing, stealing, and sexual misconduct; while the unintelligent son guards the body, speech, and mind, and does not violate any of the ten wholesome dharmas. Which one would their parents respect?"

佛告梵志：「若彼二子，一聪明者，便复兴意作杀、盗、淫泆十恶之法；彼一子不聪明者，守护身、口、意行，十善之法一无所犯。彼父母应当敬待何者？」

The brahman replied, "The parents should respect the one who practices the ten wholesome dharmas. Should they also respect the one who practices unwholesome dharmas?"

梵志报曰：「彼父母应当敬待行十善之子，彼行不善之人复敬待为？」

The Buddha said to the brahman, "You first praised his extensive learning, and later praised his precepts. How is it, brahman? If there are two sons, one son's father is upright, but the mother is not upright; the other son's father is not upright, but the mother is upright. If the son's mother is upright, but the father is not upright, he is well-versed in all matters and has extensive knowledge of the scriptures and arts. The second son's father is upright, but the mother is not upright, he is not well-versed, but upholds the ten wholesome dharmas. Which one would their parents respect? Should they respect the one whose mother is pure, but the father is impure? Or should they respect the one whose father is pure, but the mother is impure?"

佛告梵志：「汝先叹其多闻，后叹其戒。云何，梵志！若复有二子，一子父专正，母不专正；一子父不专正，母专正。彼子若母正、父不正者，无事不闲，博知经术。第二子父正、母不正者，既不博学，但持十善。然其父母应敬待何者？为当敬待母净父不净者？为当敬待父净母不净者？」

The brahman replied, "They should respect the son whose mother is pure. The reason is that he knows the scriptures and is well-versed in various arts. As for the second son whose father is pure, but the mother is impure, although he upholds the precepts, he lacks wisdom. Where will he end up? Where there is hearing, there are precepts."

梵志报曰：「应当敬待母净之子。所以然者，由知经书，博诸伎术故。所谓第二子父净母不净，虽复持戒而无智慧，竟何所至？有闻则有戒。」

The Buddha said to the brahman, "You previously praised the father's purity, not the mother's purity; now you again praise the mother's purity, not the father's purity. You first praised the virtue of hearing, then the precepts; you again praise the precepts, then the hearing. How is it, brahman? If those two brahman sons, one son is well-versed and extensively learned, and upholds the ten wholesome dharmas; the other son has wisdom and practices the ten unwholesome dharmas. Which one should their parents respect?"

佛告梵志：「汝前叹说父净，不叹说母净；今复叹说母净，不叹说父净。先叹闻德，后叹禁戒；复叹说戒，后方说闻。云何，梵志！若彼二梵志子，其中一子多闻博学，兼持十善；其第二子既有智慧，兼行十恶。彼父母应当敬待何者？」

The brahman replied, "They should respect the son whose father is pure, but the mother is impure. The reason is that he has extensively studied the scriptures, understands various skills, and is born from a pure father. He practices the ten wholesome dharmas without violating them. He is fully endowed with all the virtues."

梵志报曰：「应当敬父净、母不净之子。所以然者，由其博览诸经，晓诸技术，由父净生得此子，兼行十善，无所触犯，一切具足诸德本故。」

The Buddha said to him, "You originally spoke of his surname, then his learning, not his surname; then you again spoke of his precepts, not his learning, then you again spoke of his learning, not his precepts. Now you praise your parents' learning and precepts. Isn't this contradictory to your previous words?"

佛告之曰：「汝本说其姓，后说其闻，不说其姓；后复说戒，不说闻，后复说其闻，不说其戒。汝今叹说父母闻、戒，岂不违前言乎？」

The brahman said to the Buddha, "Shakyamuni! Although you have these words, the Brahmins themselves claim, 'Our surname is the most noble and there is none greater than it.'"

梵志白佛言：「沙门瞿昙！虽有斯言，然婆罗门自称言：『我姓最豪贵，无有出者。』」

The World Honored One said, "In marriages, one discusses surnames. But in my Dharma, there is no such meaning. Have you ever heard of people from border countries, distant lands, or other border regions?"

世尊告曰：「诸有嫁娶之处则论姓，然我法中无有此义。汝颇闻边国远邦及余边地人乎？」

The brahman replied, "Yes, I have heard of these people."

梵志报曰：「唯然，闻之，有此诸人。」

The World Honored One said, "The people in those lands have two surnames. What are the two? One is human, the other is slave. These two surnames are also not fixed."

世尊告曰：「彼土人民有二种之姓。云何为二？一者人，二者奴，此二姓亦复不定。」

He asked again, "How are they not fixed?"

又问：「云何不定？」

The World Honored One said, "Sometimes they are humans, later becoming slaves; sometimes they are slaves, later becoming humans. However, all sentient beings are of the same kind, without any differences. Moreover, Brahmin! When the world is destroyed, everything is empty. At that time, mountains, rivers, stone walls, and plants are all burned up, and people also die. When the world is about to be formed again, there are no sun, moon, or years. At that time, the gods from the Heaven of Light Sound come here. At that time, the merit and virtue of the gods from the Heaven of Light Sound gradually ends, and they lose their radiance. They look at each other and give rise to lustful thoughts. Those with excessive lustful thoughts become women, and those with less lustful thoughts become men. They engage in sexual intercourse and form embryos. For this reason, there are people for the first time, and the four castes are born and spread throughout the world. You should know this by this method, that all people come from the kshatriya caste."

世尊告曰：「或作人，后作奴；或作奴，后作人。然众生之类，尽同一类而无若干。若复，梵志！天地败毁，世间皆空。是时，山河石壁草木之徒，皆悉烧尽，人亦命终。若天地还欲成时，未有日月年岁之限。尔时，光音天来至此间。是时，光音天福德稍尽，无复精光，展转相视，兴起欲想。欲意偏多者便成女人，欲意少者成男子。展转交接，便成胞胎。由此因缘，故最初有人，转生四姓，流布天下。当以此方便知，人民尽出于刹利种。」

At that time, the Brahmin said to the World Honored One, "Stop, Gautama! It is like a hunchback finding relief, a blind person finding eyesight, and a dark person finding light. The Shakyamuni Gautama is also like this, speaking the Dharma to me with countless skillful means. I now take refuge in the Shakyamuni Gautama. Please teach me the Dharma and allow me to become a lay devotee."

尔时，梵志白世尊言：「止，瞿昙！如偻者得申，盲者得眼目，冥者得见明。沙门瞿昙亦复如是，无数方便与我说法。我今自归沙门瞿昙，唯愿与我说法，听为优婆塞。」

At that time, the Brahmin again said to the World Honored One, "I only wish that the Tathāgata would accept my invitation and come to my house with the assembly of bhikshus."

尔时，梵志复白世尊：「唯愿如来当受我请，将诸比丘众当至我家。」

At that time, the World Honored One silently accepted the invitation. The Brahmin, seeing the Buddha silently accept the invitation, immediately rose from his seat, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and then left. Returning home, he prepared food and drink, arranged seats, sprinkled scented water on the ground, and said aloud, "The Tathāgata will sit here."

尔时，世尊默然受请。是时，梵志见佛默然受请，即从坐起，头面礼足，便退而去。还至家中，办具饮食，敷诸坐具，香汁洒地，普自吐言：「如来当于此坐。」

At that time, the Brahmin Sela, together with five hundred disciples, went to the house of the Brahmin Sini. Seeing the fine seats arranged in the house, he asked the Brahmin Sini, "Are you now going to marry off your son or daughter? Or are you inviting King Bimbisara of Magadha?"

尔时，施罗梵志将五百弟子，至翅宁梵志家，遥见彼家敷好坐具，见已，问翅宁梵志：「汝今欲与男女嫁娶？为欲请摩竭国频毘娑罗王乎？」

The Brahmin Sini replied, "I am not inviting King Bimbisara, nor is there any marriage. I now wish to establish great merit and virtue."

翅宁梵志报曰：「我亦不请频毘娑罗王，亦无嫁娶之事。我今欲施设大福业。」

The Brahmin Sela asked, "I wish to hear your intention. What merit and virtue do you wish to give?"

施罗梵志问曰：「愿闻其意，欲施何福业？」

At that time, the Brahmin Sini bared his right shoulder, knelt on his right knee, joined his palms, and addressed the World-Honored One, stating his name. "Sela, you should know that a son of the Sakya clan has left home to study the path and has attained supreme true and equal enlightenment. I now invite the Buddha and the Sangha of monks, and that is why I have prepared various seats."

尔时，梵志偏露右肩，长跪叉手，白世尊自陈姓名。「施罗当知，有释种子出家学道，成无上至真、等正觉。我今请佛及比丘僧，是故办具种种坐具耳。」

At that time, the Brahmin Sela said to the Brahmin Sini, "Do you now speak of the Buddha?"

是时，施罗梵志语翅宁梵志：「汝今言佛乎？」

He replied, "I now speak of the Buddha."

报曰：「吾今言佛。」

He asked again, "How wonderful! How extraordinary! Now I hear the sound of the Buddha. Where is the Tathāgata? I wish to see him."

又问：「甚奇！甚特！今乃闻佛音响，如来竟为所在？吾欲见之。」

Śrīmitra replied, "He is now staying in the Bamboo Grove outside Rājagṛha, together with five hundred disciples, enjoying each other's company. If you wish to go see him, it is time."

翅宁报曰：「今在罗阅城外竹园中住，将五百弟子自相娱乐，欲往见者，宜知是时。」

The brāhmaṇa then led his five hundred disciples to where the Buddha was. Upon arriving, they greeted each other and sat to one side. At that time, the brāhmaṇa Śrīmitra had this thought: "The śramaṇa Gautama is handsome, with a golden-colored body. In our scriptures, it is also said: 'When a Tathāgata appears in the world, it is truly unfathomable, like the udumbara flower appearing from time to time. If one achieves the thirty-two marks and eighty minor marks, one will go to two places: if one stays at home, one will become a wheel-turning sage king, endowed with the seven treasures; if one leaves home to study the path, one will surely attain the unsurpassed path and become the benefactor of the three realms.' I now wish to observe the Buddha's thirty-two marks." At that time, the brāhmaṇa only saw thirty marks, but did not see two marks, giving rise to doubt and hesitation, not seeing the broad and long tongue or the hidden male organ.

此梵志即将五百弟子，往至佛所。到已，共相问讯，在一面坐。尔时，施罗梵志便生此念：「沙门瞿昙为端正，身作黄金色。我等经籍亦有斯言：『如来出世之时，实不可遇，犹如优昙钵花时时乃现。若成就三十二相、八十种好，当趣二处：若在家者当作转轮圣王，七宝具足；若出家学道者，必成无上道，为三界世祐。』我今欲观佛三十二相。」尔时，梵志唯见三十相，而不覩二相，起狐疑犹豫，不见广长舌、阴马藏。

At that time, the brāhmaṇa Śrīmitra then asked in verse:

尔时，施罗梵志即以偈问曰：

I have heard of the thirty-two marks of a great person, but
now I do not see two of the marks. Where are they?
The hidden male organ is very difficult to describe. Does one have
a broad and long tongue that can lick the ears and cover
the face? Please extend your broad and long tongue to remove my
doubts and let me see it, forever free from the net of
doubts and bonds.

「吾闻三十二，　　大人之相好，今不见二相，　　竟为在何所？贞洁阴马藏，　　其相甚难喻，颇有广长舌，　　舐耳覆面不？愿出广长舌，　　使我无狐疑，又使我见之，　　永无疑结网。」

Chapter 49: Herding Cattle

放牛品第四十九今分品

At that time, the World-Honored One extended his tongue, licked his ears left and right, and then retracted it. At that time, the World-Honored One entered samadhi, causing the brahman to see the hidden male organ.

尔时，世尊即吐舌，左右舐耳，还复缩之。尔时，世尊即入三昧，使彼梵志见阴马藏。

At that time, the brahman saw the Buddha's thirty-two marks and eighty minor marks, and was overjoyed, unable to contain himself. Then the brahman Shrāmaṇya said to the Buddha, "I am now a brahman, a śramaṇa, and a kṣatriya. However, śramaṇas and brahmans all follow the same path, seeking the same liberation. May the śramaṇa listen. Can we attain the same path?"

时，梵志见佛三十二相、八十种好，欢喜踊跃，不能自胜。尔时，施罗梵志白佛言：「我今婆罗门，沙门刹利种；然沙门、婆罗门皆同一道，求一解脱。唯沙门听，我等有得同一道乎？」

The Buddha told the brahman, "You have this view."

佛告梵志：「汝有此见。」

The brahman replied, "I have this view."

梵志报曰：「我有斯见。」

The Buddha told the brahman, "You should arouse your mind towards one liberation, namely right view."

佛告梵志：「汝当兴意向一解脱，所谓正见是也。」

The brahman said to the Buddha, "Right view is one liberation, are there any other liberations?"

梵志白佛言：「正见即是一解脱，复更有解脱乎？」

The World-Honored One said, "Brahman! There are other liberations that can attain the realm of nirvana. There are eight of them, namely right view, right intention, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right concentration. These, brahman, are the eightfold path that leads to nirvana."

世尊告曰：「梵志！更有解脱得涅槃界，其事有八，所谓正见、正治、正语、正业、正命、正方便、正念、正定。是谓，梵志！八种之道，得至涅槃。」

At that time, the brahman said to the Buddha, "Are there any sentient beings who know these eightfold paths?"

尔时，梵志白佛言：「颇有此众生知此八种道乎？」

The World-Honored One said, "Not just a hundred thousand. Brahman, you should know that countless hundreds of thousands of sentient beings know these eightfold paths."

世尊告曰：「非一百千。梵志当知，无数百千众生知此八种之道。」

The brahman said to the Buddha, "Are there any sentient beings who do not understand these eightfold paths?"

梵志白佛言：「颇复有此众生不解此八种之道乎？」

The World-Honored One said, "There are sentient beings who do not understand these, not just one person."

世尊告曰：「有此众生其不解者，非一人也。」

The brahman said to the Buddha, "Are there any sentient beings who do not attain this Dharma?"

梵志白佛言：「颇复有众生不得此法乎？」

The Buddha told him, "There are sentient beings who do not attain the path. There are eleven such people. What are the eleven? Namely, deceitfulness, harsh speech, being difficult to admonish, being unfaithful, having a nature of hatred, harming parents, killing arhats, severing wholesome roots and wholesome deeds, turning to evil, conceiving of a self, and giving rise to evil thoughts towards the Tathāgata. These, brahman, are the eleven people who cannot attain these eight kinds of paths."

佛告之曰：「有此众生不得道，如此之人十一种。云何为十一？所谓奸伪、恶语、难谏、无反复、好憎性、害父母、杀阿罗汉、断善根善事、反为恶、计有我、起恶念向如来，是谓，梵志！十一之人不能得此八种之道。」

When the Buddha spoke of these eight kinds of paths, at that time the brahman's defilements were all exhausted and he attained the purity of the Dharma eye.

当说此八种道时，时彼梵志诸尘垢尽，得法眼净。

At that time, the brahman Śrāmaṇya told his five hundred disciples, "Each of you should recite and practice what you like. I wish to cultivate the holy life under the Tathāgata."

尔时，施罗梵志告五百弟子曰：「汝等各所好者，各自诵习，吾欲于如来所，善修梵行。」

The disciples said, "We also wish to leave home and study the path."

诸弟子白曰：「我等亦复欲出家学道。」

At that time, the brahman and his five hundred disciples each knelt for a long time and said to the World-Honored One, "We only wish that the World-Honored One would allow us to leave home and study the path."

尔时，梵志及五百弟子各各长跪，白世尊言：「唯愿世尊听出家学道。」

The Buddha said to the brahman, "Welcome, bhikṣus! Cultivate pure conduct in the Tathāgata's presence, gradually ending the root of suffering."

佛告诸梵志：「善来，比丘！于如来所善修梵行，渐尽苦原。」

When the Tathāgata spoke these words, the five hundred brāhmanas immediately became śramaṇas. At that time, the World-Honored One gradually spoke subtle discussions with the five hundred, namely: discussions on giving, discussions on precepts, discussions on rebirth in heaven, the impure thoughts of desire, and the joy of liberation. Just as the Buddhas of the past always taught the Dharma: suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path. At that time, the World-Honored One extensively spoke to the people. At that time, the five hundred people completely extinguished their afflictions and attained the Dharma of the noble ones.

如来说此语时，五百梵志即成沙门。尔时，世尊渐与五百说微妙之论，所谓论者：施论、戒论、生天之论，欲不净想，出要为乐，如诸佛世尊常所说法：苦、习、尽、道，尔时，世尊广与诸人说之。时五百人诸漏永尽，得上人法。

At that time, the brahman Śībi again said, "The time has come. May the World-Honored One please deign to come."

尔时，翅宁梵志又白：「时到，唯愿屈神。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the five hundred bhikṣus, including Śrāmaṇya, "Each of you should put on your robes and take your alms bowls."

尔时，世尊告施罗等五百比丘：「汝等各著衣持钵。」

Surrounded by a thousand bhikṣus, he went to the city where the brāhmaṇa Śībi was. He took his seat. At that time, the brāhmaṇa Śībi saw that the five hundred brāhmaṇas had all become śramaṇas. He said to them, "Excellent! The essential path is nothing more than this."

千比丘围绕，至城中梵志所，就座而坐。尔时，翅宁梵志见五百婆罗门皆作沙门，即语之曰：「善哉！诸人，趣道之要，莫复是过。」

At that time, the bhikṣu Śrāmaṇya spoke this verse to Śībi:

尔时，施罗比丘为翅宁说此偈曰：

There is no other Dharma that can surpass this essential one
Such a comparison is excellent, and there is nothing better than this.

「此外更无法，　　能胜此要者，如此之比像，　　善者无过是。」

At that time, the brāhman Śībi addressed the World-Honored One, saying: "May the World-Honored One please wait a moment! I will prepare food and drink right away."

尔时，翅宁梵志白世尊言：「唯愿世尊小留神待时！正尔，更办饮食。」

The World-Honored One said: "The food and drink you have prepared, just offer it in a timely manner. Do not fear it will not be enough."

世尊告曰：「所办饮食，但时贡之，勿惧不足。」

At that time, the brāhman Śībi was overjoyed beyond measure. He personally served the food and made offerings to the Buddha and the bhikṣus. At that time, after the World-Honored One had finished his meal and removed the dishes, he scattered various kinds of flowers over the Buddha and the bhikṣus. He then addressed the Buddha, saying: "May the World-Honored One, together with the men, women, and children, all seek to become upāsakas."

是时，翅宁梵志欢喜无量，躬自行食，供养佛及比丘僧。尔时，世尊饭食已讫，除去食器，以若干种华，散佛、比丘僧上，前白佛言：「唯愿世尊，男女大小尽求作优婆塞。」

At that time, the brāhman's wife was pregnant. The woman addressed the Buddha, saying: "I am pregnant. I do not know whether it is a boy or a girl. I also take refuge in the Tathāgata and wish to become an upāsikā."

尔时，梵志妇怀妊，妇人白佛言：「我有娠，亦不知是男，是女耶？亦复自归如来，听为优婆夷。」

At that time, the Tathāgata expounded the subtle Dharma to the great assembly. He then spoke this verse while seated:

尔时，如来与诸大众说微妙之法，即于座上而说此偈：

Excellent indeed is this karmic retribution, the wishes will surely be fulfilled
Gradually reaching the place of peace and tranquility, forever free from worries
and troubles. After death, one will be reborn in the heavens. Even
if the Māra gods try to make those who accumulate merit fall
into sin, they will also seek the wisdom of the sages and
saints, to end the root of suffering and be forever free from
the eight difficulties.

「快哉斯福报，　　所愿必得果，渐至安隐处，　　永无忧厄患。死得生天上，　　设使诸魔天，亦复不能使，　　为福者堕罪。彼亦求方便，　　贤圣之智慧，当尽于苦本，　　长离去八难。」

At that time, the World-Honored One, having spoken this verse, immediately rose from his seat and departed.

尔时，世尊说此偈已，便从坐起而去。

At that time, the brahman Śībi heard what the Buddha said and joyfully accepted it.

尔时，翅宁梵志闻佛所说，欢喜奉行！
