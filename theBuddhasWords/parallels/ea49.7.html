EĀ 49.7

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

49.7 （七）

Parallel to: MN 65 (~) / MN 66 (~) / MĀ 192 (~) / MĀ 194 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "I always eat in one sitting. My body is light and my strength is strong. You bhikṣus should also eat in one sitting. Your bodies will be light and your strength will be strong, and you will be able to cultivate the holy life."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「我恒一坐而食，身体轻便，气力强盛；汝等比丘，亦当一食，身体轻便，气力强盛，得修梵行。」

At that time, Vāḍi Brahmin said to the World-Honored One, "I am not able to eat in one sitting. The reason is that my strength is weak."

尔时，跋提婆罗白世尊言：「我不堪任而一食。所以然者，气力弱劣。」

The Buddha told him, "If you go to a donor's house, eat one part and bring one part back home."

佛告之曰：「若汝至檀越家，一分食之，一分持还家。」

Vāḍi Brahmin said to the Buddha, "I am also unable to practice this method."

跋提婆罗白佛言：「我亦不堪行此法。」

The World-Honored One said, "I allow you to break the fast and eat throughout the day."

世尊告曰：「听汝坏斋，通日而食。」

Vāḍi Brahmin said to the Buddha, "I am also unable to practice this method." At that time, the World-Honored One remained silent and did not reply.

跋提婆罗白佛言：「我亦不堪任施行此法。」尔时，世尊默然不报。

At that time, Kālodāyin entered the city to beg for food in the evening, wearing his robe and carrying his alms bowl. It was very dark at that time. Then Udayin gradually arrived at the elder's house. The elder's wife was pregnant. Hearing that a śramaṇa was begging for food outside, she personally brought rice to give to him. However, Kālodāyin's complexion was extremely dark, and at that time the sky was about to rain, with lightning flashing everywhere. At that time, the elder's wife went out the door and saw the śramaṇa with an extremely dark complexion. She was immediately frightened and called out, "It's a ghost!" She then exclaimed, "Oh no! I see a ghost!" She immediately miscarried, and the child died. At that time, Kālodāyin returned to the monastery, feeling sad and unhappy, sitting and contemplating in regret.

尔时，迦留陀夷向暮日入，著衣持钵，入城乞食。尔时极为暗冥。时优陀夷渐渐至长者家，又彼长者妇怀妊，闻沙门在外乞食，即自持饭出惠施之。然优陀夷颜色极黑，又彼时天欲降雨，处处抴电。尔时，长者妇出门见沙门颜色极黑，即时惊怖乃呼：「是鬼。」自便称唤：「咄哉！见鬼。」即时伤胎，儿寻命终。是时，迦留陀夷寻还精舍，愁忧不欢，坐自思惟，悔无所及。

At that time, there was such a bad reputation in Śrāvastī: "The śramaṇas, sons of the Śākya clan, curse others' children to miscarry." The men and women there said to each other, "Now the śramaṇas! They act without restraint, eat without knowing the time. They are no different from lay householders.

尔时，舍卫城中有如此之恶声：「沙门释种子呪堕他子。」其中男女各相谓言：「今诸沙门！行无节度，食不知时，如在家白衣，有何等异？」

At that time, many bhikṣus heard the people discussing this matter: "The śramaṇas, sons of the Śākya clan, do not know restraint, coming and going without hesitation." Among them, the bhikṣus who upheld the precepts and were fully equipped also blamed themselves, saying, "It is indeed not proper for us to eat without restraint and act without timing. It is indeed our fault." They all went together to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at his feet, and explained this matter in detail to the World-Honored One.

尔时，众多比丘闻诸人民各论此理：「沙门释种子不知节度，行来无忌。」其中，持戒比丘戒完具者，亦自怨责：「实非我等之宜，食无禁限，行无时节，实是我等之非也。」各共相将至佛所，头面礼足，以此因缘，具白世尊。

At that time, the Buddha told a bhikṣu, "Go and call Kālodāyin to come."

尔时，佛告一比丘：「汝往唤迦留陀夷使来。」

At that time, that bhikṣu, having received the Buddha's instruction, went to call Udayin. When Udayin heard that the Buddha wanted to see him, he came to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. At that time, the World-Honored One asked Udayin, "Did you really enter the city to beg for alms yesterday evening, and cause the pregnant wife of the elder to miscarry when you arrived at the elder's house?"

是时，彼比丘受佛教已，即往唤优陀夷。时优陀夷闻佛见呼，即来至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。尔时，世尊问优陀夷曰：「汝审昨日暮入城乞食，至长者家，使长者妇胎堕乎？」

Udayin said to the Buddha, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

优陀夷白佛言：「唯然，世尊！」

The Buddha told Udayin, "Why didn't you distinguish the time? And why did you enter the city to beg for alms when it was about to rain? This is not suitable for you. As a noble son who has left home to study the path, you are greedy for food."

佛告优陀夷：「汝何故不别时节，又复欲雨而入城乞食；此非汝宜，然是族姓子出家学道而贪著于食。」

At that time, Udayin immediately rose from his seat and said to the World-Honored One, "From now on, I dare not commit it again. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept my repentance."

尔时，优陀夷即从坐起，白世尊言：「自今之后，不敢复犯，唯愿世尊听受忏悔。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told Ānanda, "Quickly strike the gong and gather all the bhikṣus in the assembly hall."

尔时，世尊告阿难曰：「速打揵稚，集诸比丘在普会讲堂。」

Ānanda received the Buddha's instruction and immediately gathered all the bhikṣus in the assembly hall. He then addressed the Buddha, saying, "The bhikṣus have already gathered, World-Honored One! Please know the time."

阿难受佛教已。即集诸比丘集在讲堂，前白佛言：「诸比丘已集，世尊！宜知是时。」

At that time, the World-Honored One went to the assembly hall and sat in the center. He told the bhikṣus, "In the past, the Buddhas and World-Honored Ones all ate in one sitting, and the śrāvakas also ate in one sitting. Even in the future, the Buddhas and their disciples will also eat in one sitting. The reason for this is that this is the essential method for practicing the path. One should eat in one sitting. If one can eat in one sitting, the body becomes light and the mind becomes enlightened. Having attained enlightenment, one attains the wholesome roots. Having attained the wholesome roots, one attains samādhi. Having attained samādhi, one truly knows. How does one truly know? One truly knows the truth of suffering, the truth of the origin of suffering, the truth of the cessation of suffering, and the truth of the path to the cessation of suffering. You, sons of noble families, have left home to study the path, abandoning the eight worldly occupations. But you do not know the proper time. What is the difference between you and those who are greedy and lustful? Brahmins have their own brahmin practices, and non-Buddhists have their own non-Buddhist practices."

尔时，世尊即往讲堂，在中央坐，告诸比丘：「过去久远诸佛世尊皆一坐而食，诸声闻等亦一坐而食，正使将来诸佛及弟子众，亦当一坐而食。所以然者，此是行道之要法，应当一坐而食。若能一坐而食，身体轻便，心得开解；心已得解，得诸善根；已得善根，便得三昧；已得三昧，如实而知之。云何如实而知之？所谓苦谛如实而知之；苦习谛如实而知之；苦尽谛如实而知之；苦出要谛如实而知之。汝等族姓子已出家学道，舍世八业，而不知时节，如彼贪欲之人有何差别？梵志别有梵志之法，外道别有外道之法。」

At that time, Upāli addressed the World-Honored One, saying, "The Tathāgatas of the past and the Buddhas of the future all ate in one sitting. I only wish that the World-Honored One would set a time limit for the bhikṣus to eat."

是时，优波离白世尊言：「过去如来、将来诸佛，皆一坐而食，唯愿世尊当与诸比丘限时而食。」

The World-Honored One said, "The Tathāgata also has this knowledge, but as long as there is no offense, we should not set limits."

世尊告曰：「如来亦有此智，但未犯者，要眼前有罪，乃当制限耳。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "I eat in one sitting, and you should also eat in one sitting. Now you eat at noon, and you should not go beyond the time. You should also learn the method of begging for food. How do bhikṣus learn the method of begging for food? Here, bhikṣus! Seek to sustain life. If you obtain it, do not be happy, and if you do not obtain it, do not be sorrowful. When you obtain food, contemplate and eat it, without a mind of attachment. Just wish to make this body continue to exist, remove old pain, and not create new pain, so that the strength is sufficient. Thus, bhikṣus! This is called begging for food. You bhikṣus should eat in one sitting.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「我专一坐而食，汝等亦当一坐而食。今汝日中而食，不得过时。汝等亦当学乞食之法。云何比丘学乞食之法？于是，比丘！趣以支命，得亦不喜，不得亦不忧；设得食时，思惟而食，无有贪著之心，但欲使此身趣得存形，除去旧痛，更不造新，使气力充足。如是，比丘！名为乞食。汝等比丘，应当一坐而食。

"How do bhikṣus eat in one sitting? If they get up, they have committed an offense and should not eat again. Thus, bhikṣus! This is called eating in one sitting. You bhikṣus should also obtain food and eat it. How do bhikṣus obtain food and eat it? Here, bhikṣus! Having obtained food, if there is more, it should be shared with others. If one obtains food again, one should not eat it again. Thus, bhikṣus! This is called obtaining food and eating it. You bhikṣus should also wear the three robes, sit under trees, sit in quiet places, sit in the open air, practice austerities, wear patched robes, stay in charnel grounds, and wear tattered clothes. The reason is to praise those of few desires. I now teach you to be like the bhikṣu Kāśyapa. The reason is that the bhikṣu Kāśyapa himself practiced the eleven dhuta ascetic practices and also taught others to practice these essential methods. I now instruct you to be like the bhikṣu Mūgapakkha. The reason is that the bhikṣu Mūgapakkha wore tattered clothes and did not wear ornaments. This is what I instruct you, bhikṣus! You should remember to cultivate it. Thus, bhikṣus! You should train like this."

「云何比丘一坐而食？起则犯食，更不应食。如是，比丘！名为一坐而食。汝等比丘亦当应得食而食之。云何比丘得而食之？于是，比丘！以得食已，更复有为齐此于？以食更得者不应复食。如是，比丘！得食而食之。汝等比丘，亦当应著三衣，应坐树下，坐闲静处，应露坐苦行，应著补纳衣，应在冢间，应著弊恶之衣。所以然者，叹说少欲之人。我今教汝等，当如迦叶比丘。所以然者，迦叶比丘自行头陀十一法，亦复教人行此要法。我今教诫汝等，当如面王比丘。所以然者，面王比丘著弊坏之衣，不著校饰。是谓，比丘！我之教诫，当念修习。如是，比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, Vāḍitapaṇḍita did not come to see the World-Honored One for three months. At that time, Ānanda, at the beginning of the three months, went to where the bhikṣu Vāḍitapaṇḍita was and said to him, "Now all the bhikṣus are wearing patched robes. Thus, the Tathāgata will travel among humans. If you do not go now, it will be of no benefit to regret later."

尔时，跋提波罗及经三月不至世尊所。尔时，阿难临三月初，至跋提婆罗比丘所，而告之曰：「今诸众僧皆补纳衣裳。如是如来当人间游行，今不往者，后悔无益。」

At that time, Ānanda brought Vāḍitapaṇḍita to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and said to the Buddha, "Yes, World-Honored One! Please allow me to repent. From now on, I will not commit offenses again. The Tathāgata has established the precepts, but I did not accept them. I only pray for your forgiveness." He said this three times.

是时，阿难将跋提婆罗至世尊所，头面礼足，并复白佛言：「唯然，世尊！听我忏悔，自今已后，更不犯之。如来制禁戒，然我不受之，唯愿垂恕。」如是再三。

At that time, the Buddha said, "I allow you to repent and not commit offenses again. The reason is that I remember countless lifetimes in saṃsāra, sometimes as donkeys, mules, camels, elephants, horses, pigs, or sheep, feeding these four great forms with grass; sometimes in hell, eating hot iron balls; sometimes as hungry ghosts, always eating pus and blood; sometimes as human forms, eating these five grains; sometimes as heavenly forms, eating natural nectar. For countless eons, forms and lives compete together, never being satisfied. Upāli should know that like fire obtaining fuel, it is never satisfied; like the ocean water, it swallows streams without being satisfied. Now ordinary people are also like this, greedily eating without being satisfied."

是时，佛告曰：「听汝悔过，后莫复犯。所以然者，我自念生死无数，或作驴、骡、骆驼、象、马、猪、羊，以草养此四大形；或在地狱中，以热铁丸噉之；或作饿鬼，恒食脓血；或作人形，食此五谷；或作天形，食自然甘露。无数劫中，形命共竞，初无厌足。优波离当知，如火获薪，初无厌足，如大海水，吞流无足。今凡夫之人亦复如是，贪食无厌足。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

尔时，世尊便说此偈：

The cycle of birth and death is endless, all due to greed
and desire. Hatred and resentment increase evil, which is practiced by the foolish.

「生死不断绝，　　皆由贪欲故，怨憎长其恶，　　愚者之所习。

"Therefore, Vartipula, you should remember to have few desires and be content, not giving rise to greedy thoughts and creating various chaotic thoughts. Thus, Upali, you should train in this way."

「是故，跋提婆罗，当念少欲知足，无起贪想，兴诸乱念。如是，优波离！当作是学。」

At that time, Vartipula, having heard the Tathāgata's teachings, in a secluded place, reproached himself. The reason why sons of noble families leave home to study the Way is to cultivate the unsurpassed pure practice: to end birth and death, establish the pure practice, accomplish what is to be done, and no longer take rebirth, knowing it as it is. At that time, Vartipula immediately attained arahantship. At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Among my disciples, the foremost śrāvaka who eats a lot of food is none other than the bhikṣu Jīvaka."

尔时，跋提婆罗闻如来教诫已，在闲静之处，而自克责，所以族姓子，出家学道者，修无上梵行：生死已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，更不复受有，如实而知。尔时，跋提婆罗即成阿罗汉。尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「我弟子中第一声闻多饮食者，所谓吉护比丘是也。」

At that time, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teachings, joyfully received and practiced them.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
