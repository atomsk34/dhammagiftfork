EĀ 51.4

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

51.4 （四）

Parallel to: DN 33 / DN 34 / MN 16 / AN 5.205 / AN 5.206 / AN 9.71 / AN 9.72 / AN 9.74–82 / AN 9.84–92 / AN 10.14 / DĀ 10 / MĀ 206

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "If bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs do not cut off the five hindrances in their minds and do not eliminate the five fetters in their minds, then day and night they will diminish in wholesome dharmas and not increase. What are the five hindrances in the mind that are not cut off? Here, bhikṣus, if there is a mind of doubt towards the Tathāgata, one is not liberated, and does not enter the true Dharma. If one's mind is not focused on recitation, this is called a bhikṣu's mind being hindered and not cut off.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「若比丘、比丘尼心五弊而不断，不除心五结，彼比丘、比丘尼日夜于善法减而无增益。云何心五弊而不断？于是，比丘！有狐疑心于如来所，亦不解脱，亦不入其正法，彼人心不在讽诵，是谓斯比丘心弊不断。

Furthermore, bhikṣus, if there is a mind of doubt towards the true Dharma, one is not liberated, and does not enter the true Dharma. If one does not recite, this is called a bhikṣu's mind being hindered and not cut off.

「复次，比丘！有疑心于正法，亦不解脱，亦不入其正法，彼人亦不讽诵，是谓斯人心弊不断。

Furthermore, bhikṣus, if there is a mind of doubt towards the noble assembly, one is not liberated, and does not direct one's mind towards the harmonious assembly. If one is not in the dharmas of the path, this is called a bhikṣu's mind being hindered and not cut off.

「复次，比丘！有疑心于圣众，亦不解脱，亦不施意向和合众，亦复不在道品法中，是谓斯比丘心弊不断。

Furthermore, bhikṣus, if one violates the precepts and does not repent, that bhikṣu, having violated the precepts, does not repent, and does not direct one's mind towards the dharmas of the path. This is called a bhikṣu's mind being hindered and not cut off.

「复次，比丘！犯于禁戒，不自悔过，彼比丘已犯禁戒，不自改悔，亦不施心在道品之中，是谓斯比丘心弊不断。

Furthermore, bhikṣus, if one's mind is not concentrated while practicing the holy life, thinking, 'By the merit of this holy life, I will be reborn in the heavens or among the gods,' then that bhikṣu, with this mental practice, practices the holy life, but the mind is not focused on the dharmas of the path. If the mind is not in the dharmas of the path, this is called a hindered mind that is not cut off. Thus, a bhikṣu's mind is hindered and not cut off in five ways.

「复次，比丘！心意不定而修梵行：『我以此梵行之德，生于天上，若诸神祇。』然彼比丘以此心行修于梵行，心不专在道品之中；心已不在道品之中，是谓心弊不断。如是比丘心五弊不断。

What are the five bonds that are not cut off for a bhikṣu? Here, bhikṣus, if one is lazy and does not seek skillful means, that bhikṣu, having already been lazy, does not seek skillful means. This is called a bhikṣu's mind being bound and not cut off.

「云何比丘五结不断？于是，比丘！懈怠不求方便，彼比丘已有懈怠，不求方便，是谓斯比丘心结不断。

Furthermore, bhikṣus, if one is always fond of much falsehood and greedily indulges in sleep, then that bhikṣu, being fond of much falsehood and greedily indulging in sleep, this is called a bhikṣu's second mental bond that is not cut off.

「复次，比丘！恒喜多妄，贪在眠寐；彼比丘以喜多妄，贪在眠寐，是谓斯比丘第二心结不断。

Furthermore, bhikṣus, if one's mind is not concentrated and one is always fond of much distraction, then that bhikṣu's mind is already distracted and not concentrated. This is called a bhikṣu's third mental bond that is not cut off.

「复次，比丘！意不定，恒喜多乱；彼比丘心已乱不定，是谓比丘第三心结不断。

Furthermore, bhikṣus, if one's sense faculties are not well guarded, then that bhikṣu's sense faculties are already not well guarded. This is called a bhikṣu's fourth mental bond that is not cut off.

「复次，比丘！根门不定；彼比丘已根门不定，是谓比丘第四心结不断。

Furthermore, bhikṣus, if one always likes to be in the marketplace and not in a quiet place, then that bhikṣu's mind is already not in a quiet place. This is called a bhikṣu's fifth mental bond that is not cut off.

「复次，比丘！恒喜在市，不在静处，是谓比丘第五心结不断。

If bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs have these five mental afflictions and five mental bonds that are not cut off, then day and night their wholesome dharmas will be cut off and there will be no increase. It is like eight or twelve chicken eggs that are not covered and protected in a timely manner. Even though the hen thinks, 'May my eggs be complete and unharmed,' still these eggs will not be safe. The reason is that they are not protected in a timely manner, and later they will be broken and not become chicks. It is the same for bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs. If the five mental bonds and five mental afflictions are not cut off and removed, then day and night their wholesome dharmas will diminish and there will be no increase.

「若比丘、比丘尼，有此五心弊、五心结不断，彼比丘、比丘尼昼夜之中善法断绝，无有增益。犹如鸡子若八、若十二，不随时覆荫，不随菢，不随时将护，彼鸡虽生此念：『使我鸡子得全无他。』然此鸡子终不安隐。所以然者，皆由不随时将护之所致，后复断坏不成其子。此亦如是，若比丘、比丘尼、五心结不断、五心弊不除，昼夜之中于善法减，无有增益。

If bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs have cut off the five mental bonds and removed the five mental afflictions, then day and night their wholesome dharmas will increase and there will be no loss. It is like eight or twelve chicken eggs that are protected, nurtured, and covered in a timely manner. Even though the hen thinks, 'May my eggs not be complete and accomplished,' still those eggs will naturally be accomplished and safe and without harm. The reason is that they are nurtured in a timely manner, causing them to be without harm, and then the eggs will eventually hatch. It is the same for bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs. If the five mental afflictions are cut off and the five mental bonds are removed, then those bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs will increase their wholesome dharmas for a long time and there will be no loss.

「若复比丘、比丘尼，五心结断、五心弊除，昼夜之中善法增益，无有损减。犹如鸡子若八、若十二，随时将护，随时育养，随时荫覆，彼鸡虽生斯念：『使我鸡子全不成就。』然彼鸡子自然成就，安隐无为。所以然者，随时长养，令得无为，时诸鸡子寻得出外。此亦如是，若比丘、比丘尼，五心弊断、五心结除，彼比丘、比丘尼于长夜之中善法增益，无有损减。

Therefore, bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs should establish their minds without any doubt or hesitation towards the Buddha or the Saṃgha. They should be fully endowed with the precepts and their minds should be focused and upright, without confusion. They should not give rise to the hope of other dharmas, nor should they expect to practice the holy life, thinking, 'I shall attain the body of a god or human through this practice.'

「是故，比丘、若比丘尼、当施设心无有犹豫狐疑于佛、犹豫狐疑于众。具足于戒律，心意专正，无有错乱，亦不兴意希望余法，亦不侥幸修梵行：『我当以此行法作天、人身，神妙尊豪。』

"If there are bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs who have no doubt or hesitation towards the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṃgha, and who do not violate the precepts or have any leakage, I now tell you, I entrust this to you. Those bhikṣus will go to two places: either to be born in the heavens or to be among humans. It is like a person who is extremely hot and thirsty in a hot place, but then encounters a cool and shady place and cold spring water to drink. That person may think, 'Although I have encountered the cool shade and cold water to drink, I am still not free from hunger and thirst,' but still that person's heat and thirst will be eliminated. It is the same for bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs. If they have no doubt or hesitation towards the Tathāgata, then those bhikṣus will go to two places: either to be born in the heavens or to be among humans. Bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs should seek means to eliminate the five mental afflictions and remove the five mental bonds. Thus, bhikṣus, you should train in this way."

「若复有比丘、比丘尼无有狐疑犹豫于佛、法、圣众，亦无犯戒，无所漏失。我今告汝，重嘱累汝，彼比丘当趣二处：若生天上、若在人中。犹如人处极热之中，兼复饥渴，遇得阴凉之处，得冷泉水饮。彼人虽生斯念：『我虽遇阴凉冷水饮之，犹不断饥渴。』但彼人暑热已尽，饥渴已除。此亦如是，若比丘、比丘尼无狐疑犹豫于如来所者，彼比丘便趣二处：若生天上、若处人中。若比丘、比丘尼，当求方便，断心五弊，除心五结。如是，诸比丘！当作是学。」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said, and joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
