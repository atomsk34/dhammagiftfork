EĀ 51.9

Ekottarāgama 增壹阿含经

51.9 （九）

Parallel to: AN 2.33 (~) / AN 7.63 / EĀ 20.11 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the elder Anāthapiṇḍada had a daughter-in-law named Subhadrā. She was beautiful, with a face like a peach blossom. She was the daughter of a minister of King Prasenajit. Relying on her family background and noble status, she was also disrespectful to her mother-in-law and her husband. She did not serve the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha of monks, nor did she respect and honor the Three Jewels.

尔时，阿那邠祁长者有儿妇名曰善生，颜貌端正，面如桃华色，王波斯匿大臣之女，凭其姓望，恃其豪族，亦不恭敬姑嫜及其夫聟，亦不事佛、法及比丘僧，亦不敬奉三尊。

At that time, the elder Anāthapiṇḍada went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. Then the elder said to the World-Honored One, "Recently I took a wife for my son, the daughter of King Prasenajit's foremost minister. Relying on her family background, she does not serve the Three Jewels and the elders and superiors. I only wish that the World-Honored One would teach her the Dharma, causing her to give rise to joy, and open her mind and understand."

是时，阿那邠祁长者便往至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。尔时，长者白世尊言：「近与儿取妇，是波斯匿王第一大臣之女，自恃其姓望，不承事三尊、长老尊卑，唯愿世尊当与说法，使发欢喜，心开意解。」

At that time, the Tathāgata silently accepted what the elder said.

尔时，如来默然许可长者所说。

At that time, the elder again said to the Buddha, "I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept my invitation, together with the bhikṣus."

时，长者复白佛言：「唯愿世尊当受我请及比丘僧。」

At that time, the elder, seeing that the Tathāgata silently accepted the invitation, immediately rose from his seat, circumambulated the Buddha three times, and departed. Returning to his home, he prepared various foods and laid out fine seats. He then announced, "The time has come. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept my invitation. The food and drink are ready."

尔时，长者以见如来默然受请，即从座起，礼佛三匝而去。还至家中，办具种种饮食，敷好坐具，寻白：「时至，唯愿世尊当受我请，饮食已具。」

At that time, the World-Honored One, together with the assembly of bhikṣus, surrounded by them in front and behind, went to the elder's house and took his seat. At that time, the elder took a small seat and sat in front of the Tathāgata.

尔时，世尊将诸比丘众，前后围绕，至长者家，就座而坐。尔时，长者更取小座，在如来前坐。

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the woman Sudinna, "Elderly woman, you should know that there are four kinds of wives for a husband. What are the four? There are wives who are like mothers, wives who are like relatives, wives who are like thieves, and wives who are like servants. You should know that a wife who is like a mother is one who attends to her husband at all times, not letting him be in want, and serves and offers to him. At that time, the gods will then protect him, and humans and non-humans will not be able to take advantage of him. When he dies, he will be reborn in heaven. This is called, elderly woman, a wife who is like a mother.

尔时，世尊告善生女人曰：「长者女当知，夫为妇人有四事。云何为四？有妇如似母，有妇似亲，有妇似贼，有妇似婢。汝今当知，妇似母者，随时瞻视夫主，不令有乏，承事供养。尔时，诸天便复将护，若人、非人不得其便，死便生天。是谓，长者！此名妇人似母者也。

What is a wife who is like a relative? Here, the elderly woman sees her husband and has no thoughts of increase or decrease, sharing his suffering and joy. This is called a person who is like a relative.

「彼云何有妇似亲亲？于是，长者妇见夫已，无有增减之心，同其苦乐，是谓其人似亲亲者也。

What is a wife who is like a thief? Here, a woman, when she sees her husband, gives rise to anger and resentment towards her husband, does not serve and respect him, and when she sees him, she wants to harm him. The husband is not close to the wife, and the wife is not close to the husband. They are not loved and respected by others, the gods do not protect them, evil ghosts harm them, and when their bodies perish and their lives end, they enter hell. This is called a person who is like a thief.

「彼云何名为妇如似贼？于是，女人若见夫已，便怀瞋恚，憎疾夫主，亦不承事恭敬礼拜，见辄欲害，心在他所。夫不亲妇，妇不亲夫，不为人所爱敬，诸天不拥护，恶鬼侵害，身坏命终，入地狱中，是谓斯人如似贼也。

"What is a wife who is like a servant? Here, a virtuous wife sees her husband and attends to him in a timely manner, tolerates his words and never responds in kind, tolerates his cold and suffering, and always has a compassionate mind. She also thinks of the Three Jewels: 'If he exists, I exist; if he declines, I decline.' Because of this, the gods protect her, and humans and non-humans all love and care for her. When her body perishes and her life ends, she is reborn in a good place in heaven. This is called, young lady, the four kinds of wives. Which one are you now?"

「彼云何名妇人如似婢也？于是，贤良之妇见夫主随时瞻视，忍其言语，终不还报；忍其寒苦，恒有慈心，于三尊所，亦生斯念：『此存我在，此衰我耗。』以此之事，诸天拥护，若人、非人皆悉爱念，身坏命终，生善处天上。是谓，长者女！有此四妇，汝今为在何条？」

When the woman heard the World-Honored One speak these words, she went forward, bowed at the Buddha's feet, and said to the World-Honored One, "I only wish that the World-Honored One would allow me to change my past and cultivate the future. I dare not do it again. From now on, I will always practice the Dharma of respect, like a servant."

时，彼女人闻世尊说此语已，前礼佛足，白世尊言：「唯愿世尊，我今改往修来，更不敢为，自今已后，常行礼法，如似婢也。」

At that time, the young lady Sudinna returned to her husband and bowed at his feet, saying, "I only wish that you would look upon me as a servant."

是时，善生女人还至夫所，头面礼足：「唯愿瞻视，当如婢也。」

At that time, the young lady Sudinna again went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed her head at his feet, and sat to one side. At that time, the World-Honored One gradually spoke the Dharma for her, namely, the discourses on giving, morality, and rebirth in heaven, the view of impurity in desire, and the great defilement of sexual misconduct. At that time, the World-Honored One, having already known that the woman's mind was open and her intentions were resolved, spoke the Dharma that the World-Honored Ones of the Buddhas always speak: suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path. At that time, the World-Honored One spoke it all to the woman. She immediately attained the purity of the Dharma eye while seated, just as new cloth is easily dyed. It was also like this. She distinguished the various dharmas and well understood the profound and wonderful meanings. She took refuge in the Three Jewels and received the five precepts.

是时，善生女人复至世尊所，头面礼足，在一面坐。尔时，世尊渐与说法，所谓论者：施论、戒论、生天之论，欲为不净想，婬为大秽。是时，世尊已知女人心开意解，诸佛世尊常所说法：苦、习、尽、道，尔时世尊尽与女人说之，即于座上得法眼净，犹如新衣易染为色。此亦如是，分别诸法，善解深妙之义，自归三尊，而受五戒。

At that time, the young lady Sudinna, having heard the Buddha's teachings, joyfully accepted and practiced them.

尔时，善生女人闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
