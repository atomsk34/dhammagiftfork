MĀ 10

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 1 七法品

10. The Cessation of the Taints （一〇）漏尽经（初一日诵）

Parallel to: MN 2 / AN 6.58 / EĀ 40.6

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the country of Kuru, staying at the city of Kumbhahattha.

一时，佛游拘楼瘦，在劒磨瑟昙拘楼都邑。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "It is through knowing and seeing that the contaminants are exhausted, not through not knowing and not seeing. How is it that through knowing and seeing the contaminants are exhausted? There is right contemplation and wrong contemplation. If it is wrong contemplation, then the contaminants of desire that have not yet arisen arise, and those that have already arisen increase and spread. The contaminants of existence and ignorance that have not yet arisen arise, and those that have already arisen increase and spread. If it is right contemplation, then the contaminants of desire that have not yet arisen do not arise, and those that have already arisen are extinguished. The contaminants of existence and ignorance that have not yet arisen do not arise, and those that have already arisen are extinguished."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「以知、以见故诸漏得尽，非不知、非不见也。云何以知、以见故诸漏得尽耶？有正思惟、不正思惟，若不正思惟者，未生欲漏而生，已生便增广，未生有漏、无明漏而生，已生便增广；若正思惟者，未生欲漏而不生，已生便灭，未生有漏、无明漏而不生，已生便灭。」

However, ordinary foolish people do not hear the true Dharma, do not encounter true spiritual friends, do not know the holy Dharma, do not tame the holy Dharma, and do not know the true Dharma. Those with wrong contemplation have the contaminants of desire that have not yet arisen arise, and those that have already arisen increase and spread. The contaminants of existence and ignorance that have not yet arisen arise, and those that have already arisen increase and spread. Those with right contemplation have the contaminants of desire that have not yet arisen not arise, and those that have already arisen are extinguished. The contaminants of existence and ignorance that have not yet arisen do not arise, and those that have already arisen are extinguished. Because they do not know the true Dharma, they contemplate what should not be contemplated and do not contemplate what should be contemplated. Because they contemplate what should not be contemplated and do not contemplate what should be contemplated, the contaminants of desire that have not yet arisen arise, and those that have already arisen increase and spread. The contaminants of existence and ignorance that have not yet arisen arise, and those that have already arisen increase and spread.

「然凡夫愚人不得闻正法，不值真知识，不知圣法，不调御圣法，不知如真法。不正思惟者，未生欲漏而生，已生便增广，未生有漏、无明漏而生，已生便增广。正思惟者，未生欲漏而不生，已生便灭，未生有漏、无明漏而不生，已生便灭。不知如真法故，不应念法而念，应念法而不念，以不应念法而念，应念法而不念故，未生欲漏而生，已生便增广，未生有漏、无明漏而生，已生便增广。

The learned noble disciples hear the true Dharma, encounter true spiritual friends, tame the holy Dharma, and know the true Dharma. Those with wrong contemplation have the contaminants of desire that have not yet arisen arise, and those that have already arisen increase and spread. The contaminants of existence and ignorance that have not yet arisen arise, and those that have already arisen increase and spread. Those with right contemplation have the contaminants of desire that have not yet arisen not arise, and those that have already arisen are extinguished. The contaminants of existence and ignorance that have not yet arisen do not arise, and those that have already arisen are extinguished. Having known the true Dharma, they do not contemplate what should not be contemplated and contemplate what should be contemplated. Because they do not contemplate what should not be contemplated and contemplate what should be contemplated, the contaminants of desire that have not yet arisen do not arise, and those that have already arisen are extinguished. The contaminants of existence and ignorance that have not yet arisen do not arise, and those that have already arisen are extinguished.

「多闻圣弟子得闻正法，值真知识，调御圣法，知如真法。不正思惟者，未生欲漏而生，已生便增广，未生有漏、无明漏而生，已生便增广。正思惟者，未生欲漏而不生，已生便灭，未生有漏、无明漏而不生，已生便灭。知如真法已，不应念法不念，应念法便念，以不应念法不念，应念法便念故，未生欲漏而不生，已生便灭，未生有漏、无明漏而不生，已生便灭也。

There are seven ways to eliminate the contaminants, afflictions, and sorrows. What are the seven? Some contaminants are eliminated through seeing. Some contaminants are eliminated through guarding. Some contaminants are eliminated through separation. Some contaminants are eliminated through use. Some contaminants are eliminated through tolerance. Some contaminants are eliminated through removal. Some contaminants are eliminated through contemplation.

「有七断漏、烦恼、忧戚法。云何为七？有漏从见断，有漏从护断，有漏从离断，有漏从用断，有漏从忍断，有漏从除断，有漏从思惟断。

How are contaminants eliminated through seeing? Ordinary foolish people do not hear the true Dharma, do not encounter true spiritual friends, do not know the holy Dharma, do not tame the holy Dharma, do not know the true Dharma, and do not contemplate correctly. They then think: 'I have a past life, I do not have a past life, why do I have a past life, how do I have a past life? I have a future life, I do not have a future life, why do I have a future life, how do I have a future life? I doubt myself, what is this, how is this? Where do these beings come from, where will they go? What is the cause of their existence, what will be the cause of their existence?' They contemplate incorrectly in this way. Among the six views, they give rise to the view that there truly is a self, the view that there truly is no self, the view that the self sees the self, the view that the self sees what is not the self, the view that what is not the self sees the self, and the view that this is the self, which can speak, know, act, teach, arise, and teach arising, experiencing good and bad results in various places, definitely not coming from anywhere, definitely not existing, and definitely not going to exist. This is called the defilement of views, being moved by views, and being bound by the fetters of views. Because of this, ordinary foolish people experience the suffering of birth, old age, sickness, and death.

「云何有漏从见断耶？凡夫愚人不得闻正法，不值真知识，不知圣法，不调御圣法，不知如真法，不正思惟故，便作是念：『我有过去世，我无过去世，我何因过去世，我云何过去世耶？我有未来世，我无未来世，我何因未来世，我云何未来世耶？自疑己身何谓是，云何是耶？今此众生从何所来，当至何所？本何因有，当何因有？』彼作如是不正思惟，于六见中随其见生而生真有神，此见生而生真无神，此见生而生神见神，此见生而生神见非神，此见生而生非神见神，此见生而生此是神，能语、能知、能作、教、作起、教起，生彼彼处，受善恶报，定无所从来，定不有、定不当有，是谓见之弊，为见所动，见结所系。凡夫愚人以是之故，便受生、老、病、死苦也。

The well-versed noble disciples hear the true Dharma, encounter true spiritual friends, tame the holy Dharma, know the true Dharma, know suffering as it truly is, know the origin of suffering, know the cessation of suffering, and know the path to the cessation of suffering as it truly is. Having known these as they truly are, they eliminate the three fetters: the view of a self, adherence to rules and rituals, and doubt. Having eliminated these three fetters, they become stream-enterers, not falling into evil ways, definitely progressing towards right awakening, experiencing at most seven existences, going back and forth between the heavens and the human realm seven times, and then attaining the end of suffering. If they do not know and see, then afflictions and sorrows arise. If they know and see, then afflictions and sorrows do not arise. This is called eliminating contaminants through seeing.

「多闻圣弟子得闻正法，值真知识，调御圣法，知如真法，知苦如真，知苦习、知苦灭、知苦灭道如真。如是知如真已，则三结尽，身见、戒取、疑三结尽已，得须陀洹，不堕恶法，定趣正觉，极受七有，天上人间七往来已，便得苦际；若不知见者，则生烦恼、忧戚，知见则不生烦恼、忧戚，是谓有漏从见断也。

How are contaminants eliminated through guarding? Bhikṣus! When the eyes see forms, guarding the eye faculty means contemplating impurity with correct contemplation, while not guarding the eye faculty means contemplating purity with incorrect contemplation. If one does not guard, then afflictions and sorrows arise. If one guards, then afflictions and sorrows do not arise. Similarly, the ears, nose, tongue, body, and mind know dharmas. Guarding the mind faculty means contemplating impurity with correct contemplation, while not guarding the mind faculty means contemplating purity with incorrect contemplation. If one does not guard, then afflictions and sorrows arise. If one guards, then afflictions and sorrows do not arise. This is called eliminating contaminants through guarding.

「云何有漏从护断耶？比丘！眼见色护眼根者，以正思惟不净观也，不护眼根者，不正思惟以净观也。若不护者，则生烦恼、忧戚，护则不生烦恼、忧戚。如是耳、鼻、舌、身、意知法，护意根者，以正思惟不净观也，不护意根者，不正思惟以净观也。若不护者，则生烦恼、忧戚，护则不生烦恼、忧戚，是谓有漏从护断也。

How are contaminants eliminated through separation? Bhikṣus! When seeing a bad elephant, one should avoid it. Also bad horses, bad oxen, bad dogs, poisonous snakes, bad roads, ditches, latrines, rivers, deep springs, mountains, cliffs, bad friends, bad companions, bad non-Buddhists, bad neighborhoods, and bad abodes. If one dwells with good practitioners, but one's doubts are not resolved, then a bhikṣu should avoid them. Bad friends, bad companions, bad non-Buddhists, bad neighborhoods, and bad abodes. If one dwells with good practitioners, but one's doubts are not resolved, one should completely avoid them. If one does not avoid them, then afflictions and sorrows arise. If one separates, then afflictions and sorrows do not arise. This is called eliminating contaminants through separation.

「云何有漏从离断耶？比丘！见恶象则当远离，恶马、恶牛、恶狗、毒蛇、恶道、沟坑、屏厕、江河、深泉、山岩。恶知识、恶朋友、恶异道、恶闾里、恶居止，若诸梵行与其同处，人无疑者而使有疑，比丘者应当离。恶知识、恶朋友、恶异道、恶闾里、恶居止，若诸梵行与其同处，人无疑者而使有疑，尽当远离。若不离者，则生烦恼、忧戚，离则不生烦恼、忧戚，是谓有漏从离断也。

How are contaminants eliminated through use? Bhikṣus! When using clothing, it is not for profit, not for arrogance, not for adornment, but only to ward off mosquitoes, flies, wind, rain, cold, and heat, and for the sake of shame and modesty. When using food and drink, it is not for profit, not for arrogance, not for fattening and pleasing, but only to sustain the body for a long time, to eliminate afflictions and sorrows, to practice the brahmacarya, to eliminate old illnesses, to prevent new illnesses, and to dwell in peace and health for a long time. When using dwellings, rooms, beds, bedding, and mats, it is not for profit, not for arrogance, not for adornment, but only to rest from fatigue and to sit in tranquility. When using medicine and tonics, it is not for profit, not for arrogance, not for fattening and pleasing, but only to eliminate illness and distress, to control the life faculty, and to dwell in peace and health. If one does not use them, then afflictions and sorrows arise. If one uses them, then afflictions and sorrows do not arise. This is called eliminating contaminants through use.

「云何有漏从用断耶？比丘！若用衣服，非为利故，非以贡高故，非为严饰故，但为蚊虻、风雨、寒热故，以惭愧故也。若用饮食，非为利故，非以贡高故，非为肥悦故，但为令身久住，除烦恼、忧戚故，以行梵行故，欲令故病断，新病不生故，久住安隐无病故也。若用居止房舍、床褥、卧具，非为利故，非以贡高故，非为严饰故，但为疲惓得止息故，得静坐故也。若用汤药，非为利故，非以贡高故，非为肥悦故，但为除病恼故，摄御命根故，安隐无病故。若不用者，则生烦恼、忧戚，用则不生烦恼、忧戚，是谓有漏从用断也。

How are contaminants eliminated through endurance? Bhikṣus! When diligently cutting off evil and unwholesome, and cultivating wholesome dharmas, one always has the thought of arising, single-mindedly striving diligently, causing the body, skin, flesh, sinews, bones, blood, and marrow to all dry up, not abandoning diligence, only seeking what is to be attained, then abandoning diligence. Bhikṣus! One should also be able to endure hunger, thirst, cold, heat, mosquitoes, flies, fleas, lice, wind, sun, harassment, bad sounds, beatings, and also be able to endure bodily illnesses, extreme suffering, and even death. All that is unpleasant, one is able to endure. If one is not able to endure, then afflictions and sorrows arise. If one is able to endure, then afflictions and sorrows do not arise. This is called eliminating contaminants through endurance.

「云何有漏从忍断耶？比丘！精进断恶不善，修善法故，常有起想，专心精勤，身体、皮肉、筋骨、血髓皆令干竭，不舍精进，要得所求，乃舍精进。比丘！复当堪忍饥渴、寒热、蚊虻蝇蚤虱，风日所逼，恶声捶杖，亦能忍之，身遇诸病，极为苦痛，至命欲绝，诸不可乐，皆能堪忍。若不忍者，则生烦恼、忧戚，忍则不生烦恼、忧戚，是谓有漏从忍断也。

How are contaminants eliminated through elimination? Bhikṣus! When lustful thoughts arise, one does not eliminate, cut off, or abandon them. When hateful thoughts and harmful thoughts arise, one does not eliminate, cut off, or abandon them. If one does not eliminate them, then afflictions and sorrows arise. If one eliminates them, then afflictions and sorrows do not arise. This is called eliminating contaminants through elimination.

「云何有漏从除断耶？比丘！生欲念不除断舍离，生恚念、害念不除断舍离，若不除者，则生烦恼、忧戚，除则不生烦恼、忧戚，是谓有漏从除断也。

How are contaminants eliminated through contemplation? Bhikṣus! Contemplating the first mindfulness factor of awakening, based on detachment, based on non-desire, based on cessation, arising to reach the ultimate, the dharmas of diligence, joy, tranquility, and concentration, contemplating the seventh equanimity factor of awakening, based on detachment, based on non-desire, based on cessation, reaching the ultimate. If one does not contemplate, then afflictions and sorrows arise. If one contemplates, then afflictions and sorrows do not arise. This is called eliminating contaminants through contemplation.

「云何有漏从思惟断耶？比丘！思惟初念觉支，依离、依无欲、依于灭尽，起至出要，法精进喜息定，思惟第七舍觉支，依离、依无欲、依于灭尽，趣至出要。若不思惟者，则生烦恼、忧戚，思惟则不生烦恼、忧戚，是谓有漏从思惟断也。

If bhikṣus eliminate contaminants through seeing, they eliminate them through seeing. If contaminants are eliminated through guarding, they are eliminated through guarding. If contaminants are eliminated through separation, they are eliminated through separation. If contaminants are eliminated through use, they are eliminated through use. If contaminants are eliminated through patience, they are eliminated through patience. If contaminants are eliminated through elimination, they are eliminated through elimination. If contaminants are eliminated through contemplation, they are eliminated through contemplation. This is called bhikṣus exhausting all contaminants, severing all bonds, and attaining the end of suffering through right knowledge.

「若使比丘有漏从见断则以见断，有漏从护断则以护断，有漏从离断则以离断，有漏从用断则以用断，有漏从忍断则以忍断，有漏从除断则以除断，有漏从思惟断则以思惟断，是谓比丘一切漏尽诸结已解，能以正智而得苦际。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

The tenth sūtra on the exhaustion of contaminants is completed.
