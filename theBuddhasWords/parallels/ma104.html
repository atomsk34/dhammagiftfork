MĀ 104

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 9 因品

104. （一〇四）优昙婆逻经

Parallel to: DN 25 / DĀ 8

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was dwelling in the Bamboo Grove Kalanda-nivāpa near Rājagṛha.

一时，佛游王舍城，在竹林伽兰哆园。

At that time, there was a householder named Sthavira. In the morning, he left Rājagṛha and went to where the Buddha was to make offerings and pay respects. Then, the householder Sthavira thought, "Let me put aside going to see the Buddha. The World-Honored One may be in meditation or with the venerable bhikṣus. I should go to the Utpala Grove and visit the park of the non-Buddhist practitioners." Then, the householder Sthavira went to the Utpala Grove and visited the park of the non-Buddhist practitioners.

尔时，有一居士名曰实意，彼于平旦从王舍城出，欲往诣佛供养礼事。于是，实意居士作如是念：「且置诣佛，世尊或能宴坐及诸尊比丘，我宁可往优昙婆逻林诣异学园。」于是，实意居士即往优昙婆逻林诣异学园。

At that time, in the park of the non-Buddhist practitioners in the Utpala Grove, there was a non-Buddhist practitioner named Amita. He was the teacher of the non-Buddhist practitioners there, respected by many, and had subdued many. He was the leader of five hundred non-Buddhist practitioners. He was causing a disturbance in the assembly, speaking loudly, discussing various topics such as birds, language, kings, thieves, fighting, food and drink, clothing, women, girls, prostitutes, worldly matters, non-Dharma, the ocean, and countries. He was discussing various topics such as birds. They were all gathered there and seated. Then, the non-Buddhist practitioner Amita saw from afar the householder Sthavira coming and ordered his assembly to be silent, saying, "Virtuous ones! Do not speak. Be silent. Enjoy silence. Collect yourselves. Why is that? The householder Sthavira is coming. He is a disciple of the śramaṇa Gautama. If there are disciples of the śramaṇa Gautama who are renowned for their virtue and are respected, and who live as householders in Rājagṛha, he is the foremost. He does not speak. He enjoys silence. He collects himself. If he knows that this assembly is silent, he may come." Then, the non-Buddhist practitioner Amita made the assembly silent and also became silent himself.

彼时，优昙婆逻林异学园中，有一异学名曰无恚，在彼中尊为异学师，众人所敬，多所降伏，为五百异学之所推宗，在众调乱，音声高大，说种种鸟论、语论、王论、贼论、鬪诤论、饮食论、衣被论、妇女论、童女论、婬女论、世俗论、非道论、海论、国论，如是比说种种鸟论，皆集在彼坐。于是，异学无恚遥见实意居士来，即勅己众，皆令嘿然：「诸贤！汝等莫语嘿然，乐嘿然，各自敛摄。所以者何？实意居士来，是沙门瞿昙弟子。若有沙门瞿昙弟子名德高远，所可宗重，在家住止，居王舍城者，彼为第一，彼不语，乐嘿然，自收敛。若彼知此众嘿然住者，彼或能来。」于是，异学无恚令众嘿然，自亦嘿然。

Then, the householder Sthavira went to where the non-Buddhist practitioner Amita was and exchanged greetings with him. He then sat to one side and said to Amita, "Amita! Our Buddha World-Honored One, when in an uninhabited place, in a mountain forest under a tree, or dwelling in a high cliff, in a quiet place with no sound, far away, without evil, and without people, follows the practice of sitting in meditation. The Buddha World-Honored One, in such places as an uninhabited place, in a mountain forest under a tree, or dwelling in a high cliff, in a quiet place with no sound, far away, without evil, and without people, follows the practice of sitting in meditation. He always enjoys sitting in meditation in a secluded place, at ease and happy. The Buddha World-Honored One does not gather with you and your retinue for even one day and night, as you do today.

于是，实意居士往诣异学无恚所，共相问讯，却坐一面，实意居士语曰：「无恚！我佛世尊若在无事处山林树下，或住高岩，寂无音声，远离，无恶，无有人民，随顺宴坐。是佛世尊如斯之比，在无事处山林树下，或住高岩，寂无音声，远离，无恶，无有人民，随顺宴坐。彼在远离处常乐宴坐，安隐快乐，彼佛世尊初不一日一夜共聚集会，如汝今日及眷属也。」

Then, the non-Buddhist practitioner Amita said, "Householder! Stop! Stop! How do you know that the śramaṇa Gautama has the liberation of emptiness? This is not worth discussing. It may or may not be appropriate, or it may or may not be suitable. The śramaṇa Gautama practices the extreme, delights in the extreme, and abides in the extreme. It is like a blind cow eating in a borderland, practicing the extreme, delighting in the extreme, and abiding in the extreme. The śramaṇa Gautama is also like this. Householder! If the śramaṇa Gautama comes to this assembly, I will refute him with one argument, like playing with an empty pot. I will also use the metaphor of the blind cow for him."

于是，异学无恚语曰：「居士！止！止！汝何由得知，沙门瞿昙空慧解脱？此不足说，或相应或不相应，或顺或不顺，彼沙门瞿昙行边至边，乐边至边，住边至边。犹如瞎牛在边地食，行边至边，乐边至边，住边至边，彼沙门瞿昙亦复如是。居士！若彼沙门瞿昙来此众者，我以一论灭彼，如弄空瓶，亦当为彼说瞎牛喻。」

Then, the non-Buddhist practitioner Amita told his own assembly, "Virtuous ones! If the śramaṇa Gautama comes to this assembly, if he definitely comes, you should not respect him by rising from your seats, joining your palms towards him, or inviting him to sit. Prepare a seat in advance. When he arrives here, he will say, 'Gautama! There is a seat. If you wish to sit, you may do so as you please.'"

于是，异学无恚告己众曰：「诸贤！沙门瞿昙傥至此众，若必来者，汝等莫敬，从坐而起，叉手向彼，莫请令坐，豫留一座，彼到此已，作如是语：『瞿昙！有座，欲坐随意。』」

At that time, the World-Honored One was sitting in meditation. With his pure divine ear surpassing that of humans, he heard householder Richiya and the non-Buddhist practitioner Amita discussing this matter. Then, in the afternoon, he rose from his meditation and went to the non-Buddhist practitioner Amita's garden in the Uddālaka forest. The non-Buddhist practitioner Amita saw the World-Honored One coming from afar. He immediately rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and praised, "Welcome, Śramaṇa Gautama! You have not come here for a long time. Please sit on this seat."

尔时，世尊在于宴坐，以净天耳出过于人，闻实意居士与异学无恚共论如是，则于晡时从宴坐起，往诣优昙婆逻林异学园中。异学无恚遥见世尊来，即从坐起偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，赞曰：「善来，沙门瞿昙！久不来此，愿坐此座。」

At that time, the World-Honored One thought, "This foolish person is going against his own promise." Knowing this, the World-Honored One then sat on the bed. The non-Buddhist practitioner Amita then exchanged greetings with the World-Honored One and sat to one side. The World-Honored One asked, "Amita! What were you discussing with householder Richiya? Why are you gathered here to sit?"

彼时，世尊作如是念：「此愚痴人，自违其要。」世尊知已，即坐其床，异学无恚便与世尊共相问讯，却坐一面。世尊问曰：「无恚！向与实意居士共论何事？以何等故集在此坐？」

The non-Buddhist practitioner Amita replied, "Gautama! We thought, 'What are the teachings of Śramaṇa Gautama that he instructs his disciples? Having received the teachings, how does he bring them peace and enable them to practice pure conduct for the rest of their lives, and also teach others?' Gautama! This is what I was discussing with householder Richiya. This is why we are gathered here to sit."

异学无恚答曰：「瞿昙！我等作是念：『沙门瞿昙有何等法，谓教训弟子？弟子受教训已，令得安隐，尽其形寿，净修梵行，及为他说。』瞿昙！向与实意居士共论如是，以是之故，集在此坐。」

Having heard their words, householder Richiya thought, "This non-Buddhist practitioner Amita is indeed a liar. Why is that? He is deceiving the World-Honored One in front of the Buddha."

实意居士闻彼语已，便作是念：「此异学无恚异哉妄语。所以者何？在佛面前欺诳世尊。」

The World-Honored One, knowing this, said, "Amita! My teachings are profound, wonderful, and extraordinary. They are difficult to realize, know, see, and attain. I instruct my disciples. Having received my instructions, they practice pure conduct for the rest of their lives, and also teach others. Amita! If your teacher's school cannot understand and dislikes the practice, you can ask me. I will definitely be able to answer and satisfy you."

世尊知已，语曰：「无恚！我法甚深！甚奇！甚特！难觉难知，难见难得，谓我教训弟子。弟子受教训已，尽其形寿，净修梵行，亦为他说。无恚！若汝师宗所可不了憎恶行者，汝以问我，我必能答，令可汝意。」

Then the disorderly non-Buddhist practitioners all spoke in unison, saying in a loud voice, "The śramaṇa Gautama is indeed wonderful and extraordinary! He has great supernatural powers, great authority, great merit, and great spiritual power. Why is that? He is able to abandon his own school and follow the questions of others." Then the non-Buddhist practitioner Amita admonished his own followers to be silent, and asked, "Gautama! How does one attain the complete practice of what is not understood and disliked? How does one not attain the complete practice of what is not understood and disliked?"

于是，调乱异学众等同音共唱，高大声曰：「沙门瞿昙甚奇！甚特！有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神。所以者何？乃能自舍己宗，而以他宗随人所问。」于是异学无恚自勅己众，令嘿然已，问曰：「瞿昙！不了可憎行。云何得具足？云何不得具足？」

Then the World-Honored One replied, "Amita! There are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who are naked without clothes, or use hands as clothes, or use leaves as clothes, or use beads as clothes. They do not take water with a pot, or take water with a ladle. They do not eat food obtained by violence, or food obtained by deception. They do not go themselves or send messages. They do not seek the respect of others, or the respect of the good, or the respect of the virtuous. If two people are eating, they do not eat in between. They do not eat in a household with pregnant women, or in a household with dogs. Even if flies come flying from a household with excrement, they do not eat. They do not eat fish, meat, or drink alcohol. They do not drink bad water, or drink nothing at all. They practice not drinking. They eat one mouthful, and are content with one mouthful. Or two, three, four, up to seven mouthfuls, and are content with seven mouthfuls. They eat one meal, and are content with one meal. Or two, three, four, five, six, seven days, half a month, or one month for one meal, and are content with one meal.

于是，世尊答曰：「无恚！或有沙门、梵志倮形无衣，或以手为衣，或以叶为衣，或以珠为衣，或不以瓶取水，或不以櫆取水，不食刀杖劫抄之食，不食欺妄食，不自往、不遣信，不求来尊，不善尊，不住尊。若有二人食，不在中食，不怀姙家食，不畜狗家食，设使家有粪蝇飞来而不食，不噉鱼，不食肉，不饮酒，不饮恶水，或都无所饮，学无饮行，或噉一口，以一口为足，或二、三、四，乃至七口，以七口为足，或食一得，以一得为足，或二、三、四乃至七得，以七得为足，或日一食，以一食为足，或二、三、四、五、六、七日、半月、一月一食，以一食为足。

"They eat vegetables, or eat millet, or eat rice from the wild, or eat coarse flour, or eat food from the wild, or eat coarse food. They go to a place without affairs and rely on being without affairs. They eat roots, or eat fruits, or eat fruits that have fallen by themselves. They wear a robe made of rags, or wear a robe made of hair, or wear a robe made of bark, or wear a robe made of hair and bark. They wear a whole hide, or wear a pierced hide, or wear a whole and pierced hide. They let their hair loose, or braid their hair, or let their hair loose and braided. Some shave their hair, some shave their beards, some shave their hair and beards. Some pull out their hair, some pull out their beards, some pull out their hair and beards. Some stand and sit cross-legged, some practice squatting, some lie on thorns, using thorns as a bed, some lie on fruits, using fruits as a bed. Some serve water, stirring it with their hands day and night. Some serve fire, burning it all night. Some serve the sun and moon, the honored ones of great virtue, with their hands folded towards them. In this way, they undergo immeasurable suffering, practicing the path of affliction. Amita! What do you think? Is the practice of what is not understood and disliked like this complete or incomplete?"

「或食菜茹，或食稗子，或食［木＊祭］米，或食杂［麸－夫＋黄］，或食头头逻食，或食麤食，或至无事处，依于无事，或食根，或食果，或食自落果，或持连合衣，或持毛衣，或持头舍衣，或持毛头舍衣，或持全皮，或持穿皮，或持全穿皮，或持散发，或持编发，或持散编发，或有剃发，或有剃须，或剃须发，或有拔发，或有拔须，或拔须发，或住立断坐，或修蹲行，或有卧刺，以刺为床，或有卧果，以果为床，或有事水，昼夜手抒，或有事火，竟昔然之，或事日月尊祐大德，叉手向彼。如此之比，受无量苦，学烦热行。无恚！于意云何？不了可憎行如是，为具足？为不具足？」

The non-Buddhist Amita replied, "Gautama! This practice of what is not understood and disliked is complete, not incomplete."

异学无恚答曰：「瞿昙！如是不了可憎行为具足，非不具足。」

The World-Honored One said again, "Amita! I will tell you that this practice of what is not understood and disliked is defiled by immeasurable impurities."

世尊复语曰：「无恚！我为汝说此不了可憎具足行为无量秽所污。」

The non-Buddhist Amita asked, "Gautama! How will you tell me about this practice of what is not understood and disliked being defiled by immeasurable impurities?"

异学无恚问曰：「瞿昙云何为我说此不了可憎具足行为无量秽所污耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Amita! There is one who practices asceticism and austerities, and because of practicing asceticism and austerities, they have evil desires and thoughts of desire. Amita! If there is one who practices asceticism and austerities, and because of practicing asceticism and austerities, they have evil desires and thoughts of desire, this is called, Amita! the defilement of practicing asceticism and austerities. Furthermore, Amita! There is one who practices asceticism and austerities, and because of practicing asceticism and austerities, they look up at the sunlight and inhale the sunlight. Amita! If there is one who practices asceticism and austerities, and because of practicing asceticism and austerities, they look up at the sunlight and inhale the sunlight, this is called, Amita! the defilement of practicing asceticism and austerities. Furthermore, Amita! There is one who practices asceticism and austerities, and because of practicing asceticism and austerities, they become arrogant. Having attained the practice of asceticism and austerities, their mind becomes attached. Amita! If there is one who practices asceticism and austerities, and because of practicing asceticism and austerities, they become arrogant. Having attained the practice of asceticism and austerities, their mind becomes attached, this is called, Amita! the defilement of practicing asceticism and austerities.

世尊答曰：「无恚！或有一清苦行苦行，因此清苦行苦行，恶欲、念欲。无恚！若有一清苦行苦行，因此清苦行苦行，恶欲、念欲者，是谓，无恚！行苦行者秽。复次，无恚！或有一清苦行苦行，因此清苦行苦行，仰视日光，吸服日气。无恚！若有一清苦行苦行，因此清苦行苦行，仰视日光，吸服日气者，是谓，无恚！行苦行者秽。复次，无恚！或有一清苦行苦行，因此清苦行苦行而自贡高，得清苦行苦行已，心便系著。无恚！若有一清苦行苦行，因此清苦行苦行而自贡高，得清苦行苦行已，心便系著者，是谓，无恚！行苦行者秽。

Furthermore, Amita! There is one who practices asceticism and austerities, and because of practicing asceticism and austerities, they esteem themselves and despise others. Amita! If there is one who practices asceticism and austerities, and because of practicing asceticism and austerities, they esteem themselves and despise others, this is called, Amita! the defilement of practicing asceticism and austerities. Furthermore, Amita! There is one who practices asceticism and austerities, and because of practicing asceticism and austerities, they go from house to house and proclaim, 'I practice asceticism, I practice great difficulty.' Amita! If there is one who practices asceticism and austerities, and because of practicing asceticism and austerities, they go from house to house and proclaim, 'I practice asceticism, I practice great difficulty,' this is called, Amita! the defilement of practicing asceticism and austerities.

「复次，无恚！或有一清苦行苦行，因此清苦行苦行，自贵贱他。无恚！若有一清苦行苦行，因此清苦行苦行，自贵贱他者，是谓，无恚！行苦行者秽。复次，无恚！或有一清苦行苦行，因此清苦行苦行，往至家家而自称说：『我行清苦，我行甚难。』无恚！若有一清苦行苦行，因此清苦行苦行，往至家家而自称说：『我行清苦，我行甚难。』者，是谓，无恚！行苦行者秽。

Furthermore, Amita! There is one who practices asceticism and austerities, and because of practicing asceticism and austerities, if they see a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa being respected, venerated, and served by others, they give rise to jealousy and say, 'Why respect, venerate, and serve those śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas? You should respect, venerate, and serve me. Why is that? I practice asceticism.' Amita! If there is one who practices asceticism and austerities, and because of practicing asceticism and austerities, if they see a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa being respected, venerated, and served by others, they give rise to jealousy and say, 'Why respect, venerate, and serve those śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas? You should respect, venerate, and serve me. Why is that? I practice asceticism.' This is called, Amita! the defilement of practicing asceticism and austerities.

「复次，无恚！或有一清苦行苦行，因此清苦行苦行，若见沙门、梵志为他所敬重、供养、礼事者，便起嫉妬言：『何为敬重、供养、礼事彼沙门、梵志？应敬重、供养、礼事于我。所以者何？我行苦行。』无恚！若有一清苦行苦行，因此清苦行苦行，若见沙门、梵志为他所敬重、供养、礼事者，便起嫉妬言：『何为敬重、供养、礼事彼沙门、梵志？应敬重、供养、礼事于我。所以者何？我行苦行。』者，是谓，无恚！行苦行者秽。

Furthermore, Amita! There is one who practices asceticism and austerities, and because of practicing asceticism and austerities, if they see a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa being respected, venerated, and served by others, they openly rebuke those śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, saying, 'Why respect, venerate, and serve them? You are greedy, seeking much, always eating, eating the seeds of roots, tree seeds, fruit seeds, node seeds, and the five kinds of seeds. It is like a heavy rain, damaging many kinds of grain seeds, disturbing livestock and people.' In this way, those śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas often enter others' homes in the same way. Amita! If there is one who practices asceticism and austerities, and because of practicing asceticism and austerities, if they see a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa being respected, venerated, and served by others, they openly rebuke those śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, saying, 'Why respect, venerate, and serve them? You are greedy, seeking much, always eating, eating the seeds of roots, tree seeds, fruit seeds, node seeds, and the five kinds of seeds. It is like a heavy rain, damaging many kinds of grain seeds, disturbing livestock and people.' In this way, those śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas often enter others' homes in the same way, this is called, Amita! the defilement of practicing asceticism and austerities.

Amita, what do you think? Does this practice of the complete lack of what is undesirable attain the joints?"

世尊答曰：「无恚！或有一沙门梵志行四行，不杀生、不教杀、不同杀，不偷、不教偷、不同偷，不取他女、不教取他女、不同取他女，不妄言、不教妄言、不同妄言。彼行此四行，乐而不进，彼有行有相貌，忆本无量昔所经历，或一生、二生、百生、千生、成劫、败劫、无量成败劫。彼众生名某，彼昔更历，我曾生彼，如是姓、如是字、如是生、如是饮食、如是受苦乐、如是长寿、如是久住、如是寿命讫，此死生彼，彼死生此，我生在此如是姓、如是字、如是生、如是饮食、如是受苦乐、如是长寿、如是久住、如是寿命讫。无恚！于意云何？如是此不了可憎行得节耶？」

Amita replied, "Gautama, this practice of the complete lack of what is undesirable attains the joints. Gautama, how does this practice of the complete lack of what is undesirable attain the first, attain the true?"

无恚答曰：「瞿昙！如是此不了可憎行得节也。瞿昙！云何此不了可憎行得第一、得真实耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Amita, it is like this: there is a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who practices four practices: not killing living beings, not teaching others to kill, not rejoicing in killing; not stealing, not teaching others to steal, not rejoicing in stealing; not taking others' wives, not teaching others to take others' wives, not rejoicing in taking others' wives; not speaking falsely, not teaching others to speak falsely, not rejoicing in speaking falsely. They practice these four practices, delighting in them but not progressing. They attain pure divine eyes that surpass human eyes, and see the death and rebirth of beings, their good and bad appearances, their good and bad rebirths, and their going to good and bad places. They see the true karma of these beings. If these beings have practiced bad conduct with the body, speech, and mind, have slandered the sages, and have attained wrong views and wrong view karma, then because of this, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. If these beings have practiced good conduct with the body, speech, and mind, have not slandered the sages, and have attained right views and right view karma, then because of this, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely ascend to a good place and be reborn in heaven. Amita, what do you think? Does this practice of the complete lack of what is undesirable attain the first, attain the true?"

世尊答曰：「无恚！或有一沙门梵志行四行，不杀生、不教杀、不同杀，不偷、不教偷、不同偷，不取他女，不教取他女、不同取他女，不妄言、不教妄言、不同妄言。彼行此四行，乐而不进。彼以清净天眼出过于人，见此众生死时生时，好色恶色，妙与不妙，往来善处及不善处，随此众生之所作业，见其如真。若此众生成就身恶行，口、意恶行，诽谤圣人，邪见成就邪见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。若此众生成就身妙行，口、意妙行，不诽谤圣人，正见成就正见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必升善处，乃生天上。无恚！于意云何？如是此不了可憎行得第一、得真实耶？」

Amita replied, "Gautama, this practice of the complete lack of what is undesirable attains the first, attains the true. Gautama, how does this practice of the complete lack of what is undesirable attain the realization, so that the disciples of the śramaṇa Gautama practice the holy life based on the practice of the śramaṇa?"

无恚答曰：「瞿昙！如是此不了可憎行得第一、得真实也。瞿昙！云何此不了可憎行作证故，沙门瞿昙弟子依沙门行梵行耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Amita, it is not because of this practice of the complete lack of what is undesirable that my disciples practice the holy life based on the practice of my disciples. Amita, there is another, supreme, most excellent, and most sublime, for the realization of which my disciples practice the holy life based on the practice of my disciples."

世尊答曰：「无恚！非因此不了可憎行作证故，我弟子依我行梵行也。无恚！更有异，最上、最妙、最胜，为彼证故，我弟子依我行梵行。」

Then the group of wandering ascetics, who were agitated and confused, exclaimed in a loud voice, "So it is, so it is! It is for the realization of that that the disciples of the śramaṇa Gautama practice the holy life based on the practice of the śramaṇa Gautama."

于是，调乱异学众等发高大声：「如是，如是，为彼证故，沙门瞿昙弟子依沙门瞿昙行梵行。」

Then the wandering ascetic Amita admonished his own group to be silent, and said, "Gautama, what is that other, supreme, most excellent, and most sublime, for the realization of which the disciples of the śramaṇa Gautama practice the holy life based on the practice of the śramaṇa Gautama?"

于是，异学无恚自勅己众，令默然已，白曰：「瞿昙！何者更有异，最上、最妙、最胜，为彼证故，沙门瞿昙弟子依沙门瞿昙行梵行耶？」

Then the World-Honored One replied, "Amita, when a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, Perfect in Knowledge and Conduct, Well-Gone, Knower of the World, Unsurpassed, Tamer of Men, Teacher of Gods and Men, Buddha, Bhagavān appears in the world, he abandons the five hindrances, which are impurities of the mind that weaken wisdom, and attains the fourth dhyāna, which is free from desire and evil, unwholesome dhyāna. With his mind thus concentrated, purified, and cleansed, free from defilements, supple, wieldy, steady, and imperturbable, he directs it to the knowledge of the destruction of the taints. He knows as it really is: 'This is suffering.' He knows as it really is: 'This is the arising of suffering.' He knows as it really is: 'This is the cessation of suffering.' He knows as it really is: 'This is the path to the cessation of suffering.' He knows as it really is: 'These are the taints.' He knows as it really is: 'This is the arising of the taints.' He knows as it really is: 'This is the cessation of the taints.' He knows as it really is: 'This is the path to the cessation of the taints.' Knowing and seeing thus, his mind is liberated from the taint of sensual desire, from the taint of existence, and from the taint of ignorance. When it is liberated, he knows it is liberated. He understands: 'Birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, there is no more of this to come.' Amita, this is the other, supreme, most excellent, and most sublime, for the realization of which my disciples practice the holy life based on my practice."

于是，世尊答曰：「无恚！若如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐，出于世间，彼舍五盖心秽慧羸，离欲、离恶不善之法，至得第四禅成就游。彼已如是定心清净，无秽无烦，柔软善住，得不动心，趣向漏尽智通作证。彼知此苦如真，知此苦习、知此苦灭、知此苦灭道如真，亦知此漏、知此漏习、知此漏灭、知此漏灭道如真。彼如是知、如是见，欲漏心解脱，有漏、无明漏心解脱，解脱已，便知解脱，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。无恚！是谓更有异，最上、最妙、最胜，为彼证故，我弟子依我行梵行。」

Then the householder Anathapindika said, "Amita, the World-Honored One is here. You can now refute him with a single argument, like throwing an empty pot, saying that a blind bull is eating in the borderlands."

于是，实意居士语曰：「无恚！世尊在此，汝今可以一论灭，如挊空瓶，说如瞎牛在边地食。」

Hearing this, the World-Honored One said to the non-Buddhist Amita, "Is it true that you said this?"

世尊闻已，语异学无恚曰：「汝实如是说耶？」

The non-Buddhist Amita replied, "It is true, Gautama!"

异学无恚答曰：「实如是，瞿昙！」

The World-Honored One further asked, "Amita, have you ever heard from the elders that the past Tathāgatas, Arhats, Perfectly Enlightened Ones, when they were in secluded places such as mountains, forests, or under trees, or in high caves, quiet and soundless, far away, without evil, and without people, they practiced meditation in accordance with their inclinations. The Buddha-World-Honored Ones, when in secluded places such as mountains, forests, or under trees, or in high caves, quiet and soundless, far away, without evil, and without people, practiced meditation in accordance with their inclinations. They always enjoyed practicing meditation in secluded places, and were at ease and happy. Did they ever gather together for a day and night like you and your followers today?"

世尊复问曰：「无恚！汝颇曾从长老旧学所闻如是，过去如来、无所著、等正觉，若有无事处山林树下，或有高岩，寂无音声，远离、无恶，无有人民，随顺宴坐。诸佛世尊在无事处山林树下，或住高岩，寂无音声，远离，无恶，无有人民，随顺宴坐。彼在远离处常乐宴坐，安隐快乐，彼初不一日一夜共聚集会，如汝今日及眷属耶？」

The non-Buddhist Amita replied, "Gautama, I have heard from the elders that the past Tathāgatas, Arhats, Perfectly Enlightened Ones, when they were in secluded places such as mountains, forests, or under trees, or in high caves, quiet and soundless, far away, without evil, and without people, they practiced meditation in accordance with their inclinations. The Buddha-World-Honored Ones, when in secluded places such as mountains, forests, or under trees, or in high caves, quiet and soundless, far away, without evil, and without people, practiced meditation in accordance with their inclinations. They always enjoyed practicing meditation in secluded places, and were at ease and happy. They never gathered together for a day and night like me and my followers today."

异学无恚答曰：「瞿昙！我曾从长老旧学所闻如是，过去如来、无所著、等正觉，若有无事处山林树下，或有高岩，寂无音声，远离，无恶，无有人民，随顺宴坐。诸佛世尊在无事处山林树下，或住高岩，寂无音声，远离，无恶，无有人民，随顺宴坐。彼在远离处常乐宴坐，安隐快乐，初不一日一夜共聚集会，如我今日及眷属也。」

"Amita, do you not think that the World-Honored One, when in secluded places such as mountains, forests, or under trees, or in high caves, quiet and soundless, far away, without evil, and without people, practiced meditation in accordance with his inclinations. He always enjoyed practicing meditation in secluded places, and was at ease and happy. Does the Śramaṇa Gautama practice the path of right enlightenment?"

「无恚！汝不作是念，如彼世尊在无事处山林树下，或住高岩，寂无音声，远离，无恶，无有人民，随顺宴坐。彼在远离处常乐宴坐，安隐快乐，彼沙门瞿昙学正觉道耶？」

The non-Buddhist Amita replied, "Gautama, if I knew, how could I say such things? One argument would be enough to refute me, like a jug of empty air. How can a blind cow eat in a borderland?"

异学无恚答曰：「瞿昙！我若知者，何由当复作如是说，一论便灭，如弄空瓶，说瞎牛在边地食耶？」

The World-Honored One said, "Amita, I have a Dharma that is well-aligned with goodness. I can realize the phrases of liberation one by one. The Tathāgata proclaims this fearlessness. My disciples come to me without flattery, deceit, or falsehood. They are straightforward and sincere. When I instruct them and they follow my teachings, they will definitely attain ultimate wisdom. Amita, if you think, 'The Śramaṇa Gautama teaches the Dharma out of greed for a teacher,' do not think that way. I return the teacher to you. I will teach the Dharma for you. Amita, if you think, 'The Śramaṇa Gautama teaches the Dharma out of greed for disciples,' do not think that way. I return the disciples to you. I will teach the Dharma for you. Amita, if you think, 'The Śramaṇa Gautama teaches the Dharma out of greed for offerings,' do not think that way. I return the offerings to you. I will teach the Dharma for you. Amita, if you think, 'The Śramaṇa Gautama teaches the Dharma out of greed for praise,' do not think that way. I return the praise to you. I will teach the Dharma for you. Amita, if you think, 'If I have a Dharma that is well-aligned with goodness, I can realize the phrases of liberation one by one, and the Śramaṇa Gautama will deprive me of it and destroy it,' do not think that way. I return the Dharma to you. I will teach the Dharma for you."

世尊语曰：「无恚！我今有法善善相应，彼彼解脱句能以作证，如来以此自称无畏。诸比丘我弟子来，无谀谄，不欺诳，质直无虚，我训随教已，必得究竟智。无恚！若汝作是念：『沙门瞿昙贪师故说法。』汝莫作是念，以师还汝，我其为汝说法。无恚！若汝作是念：『沙门瞿昙贪弟子故说法。』汝莫作是念，弟子还汝，我其为汝说法。无恚！若汝作是念：『沙门瞿昙贪供养故说法。』汝莫作是念，供养还汝，我其为汝说法。无恚！若汝作是念：『沙门瞿昙贪称誉故说法。』汝莫作是念，称誉还汝，我其为汝说法。无恚！若汝作是念：『我若有法善善相应，彼彼解脱句能以作证，彼沙门瞿昙，夺我灭我者。』汝莫作是念，以法还汝，我其为汝说法。」

Then the great assembly fell silent. Why was that? Because they were held by the demon king. At that time, the World-Honored One said to the householder Real Intention, "Look at this great assembly falling silent. Why is that? Because they are held by the demon king. He prevents any non-Buddhist from thinking, 'I will practice the holy life under the Śramaṇa Gautama.'"

于是，大众默然而住。所以者何？彼为魔王所制持故。彼时，世尊告实意居士曰：「汝看此大众默然而住。所以者何？彼为魔王所制持故。彼令异学众无有一异学作是念：『我试于沙门瞿昙所修行梵行。』」

The World-Honored One, knowing this, taught the Dharma to the householder Real Intention, inspiring him with yearning and bringing him to fulfillment and joy. Having taught the Dharma to him with immeasurable skillful means, inspiring him with yearning and bringing him to fulfillment and joy, he immediately rose from his seat, took the householder Real Intention by the arm, and flew through the air with his supernormal power.

世尊知已，为实意居士说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，即从坐起，便接实意居士臂，以神足飞，乘虚而去。

The Buddha spoke thus. The householder Real Intention, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。实意居士闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
