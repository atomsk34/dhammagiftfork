MĀ 112

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 10 林品

112. （一一二）阿奴波经

Parallel to: AN 6.62

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the land of the Mallas, staying in the city of Anupiya.

一时，佛游跋耆瘦，在阿奴波跋耆都邑。

At that time, the World-Honored One rose from his seat at dusk, came down from the hall, and said, "Ānanda! Let us go together to the Airavana River to bathe."

尔时，世尊则于晡时从宴坐起，堂上来下，告曰：「阿难！共汝往至阿夷罗惒帝河浴。」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "Yes, sir."

尊者阿难白曰：「唯然。」

Then the World-Honored One, together with Venerable Ānanda, went to the Airavana River. He took off his robe on the bank and entered the water to bathe. After bathing, he came out, dried his body, and put on his robe. At that time, Venerable Ānanda held a fan and fanned the Buddha. Then the World-Honored One turned around and said, "Ānanda! Because of his indulgence, Devadatta has fallen into extreme suffering and hardship, and will definitely go to a bad place, be reborn in hell, and remain there for an eon, beyond any hope of rescue. Ānanda! Have you not heard from the monks that I have always predicted that Devadatta will definitely go to a bad place, be reborn in hell, and remain there for an eon, beyond any hope of rescue?"

于是，世尊将尊者阿难往至阿夷罗惒帝河，脱衣岸上，便入水浴，浴已还出，拭体著衣。尔时，尊者阿难执扇扇佛。于是，世尊回顾告曰：「阿难！提惒达哆以放逸故，堕极苦难，必至恶处，生地狱中，住至一劫不可救济。阿难！汝不曾从诸比丘闻，谓我一向记提惒达哆必至恶处，生地狱中，住至一劫不可救济耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "Yes, sir."

尊者阿难白曰：「唯然。」

At that time, a bhikṣu said to Venerable Ānanda, "Did the World-Honored One know Devadatta's mind with his mind-reading knowledge, and therefore predicted that Devadatta would definitely go to a bad place, be reborn in hell, and remain there for an eon, beyond any hope of rescue?"

尔时，有一比丘语尊者阿难：「世尊以他心智知提惒达哆心故，一向记提惒达哆必至恶处，生地狱中，住至一劫不可救济耶？」

The World-Honored One said, "Ānanda! That bhikṣu may have a small, medium, or great understanding, or he may be young and not know for himself. Why is that? Because the Tathāgata has already made a one-sided prediction about him, causing doubt. Ānanda! I do not see in this world, among gods, Māra, Brahmā, śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, from humans to gods, who would make a one-sided prediction like Devadatta. Why is that? Ānanda! I have made a one-sided prediction that Devadatta will definitely go to a bad place, be reborn in hell, and remain there for an eon, beyond any hope of rescue. Ānanda! If I saw even a single hair's worth of pure qualities in Devadatta, I would not make a one-sided prediction that Devadatta will definitely go to a bad place, be reborn in hell, and remain there for an eon, beyond any hope of rescue. Ānanda! Because I do not see even a single hair's worth of pure qualities in Devadatta, I make a one-sided prediction that Devadatta will definitely go to a bad place, be reborn in hell, and remain there for an eon, beyond any hope of rescue.

世尊告曰：「阿难！彼比丘或有小，或有中，或有大，或年少不自知。所以者何？如来已一向记彼故有疑惑。阿难！我不见此世，天及魔、梵、沙门、梵志，从人至天，谓我一向记如提惒达哆。所以者何？阿难！我一向记提惒达哆必至恶处，生地狱中，住至一劫不可救济。阿难！若我见提惒达哆有白净法如一毛许，我便不一向记提惒达哆必至恶处，生地狱中，住至一劫不可救济。阿难！我以不见提惒达哆有白净法如一毛许，是故我一向记提惒达哆必至恶处，生地狱中，住至一劫不可救济。

Ānanda! Not far from a village, there is a deep cesspit. If a person falls into it and sinks to the bottom, if someone comes and feels great compassion, pity, and sympathy for them, seeking justice and benefit, seeking peace and happiness, and after coming, looks around and says, 'Can I find a place on this person the size of a single hair that is not covered in excrement, so that I can grab them and pull them out?' Looking all around, they do not see a single clean place on that person the size of a single hair that is not covered in excrement, so that they can grab them and pull them out. In the same way, Ānanda! If I saw even a single hair's worth of pure qualities in Devadatta, I would not make a one-sided prediction that Devadatta will definitely go to a bad place, be reborn in hell, and remain there for an eon, beyond any hope of rescue. Ānanda! Because I do not see even a single hair's worth of pure qualities in Devadatta, I make a one-sided prediction that Devadatta will definitely go to a bad place, be reborn in hell, and remain there for an eon, beyond any hope of rescue.

「阿难！犹去村不远有大深厕，或人堕中，没在其底。若人来，为起大慈哀，怜念愍伤，求义及饶益，求安隐快乐，彼人来已，旋转视之而作是说：『此人可得一处如毛发许，粪所不污，令我得捉挽出之耶？』彼遍观视，不见此人有一净处如毛发许，粪所不污，可得手捉挽出之也。如是，阿难！若我见提惒达哆有白净法如一毛者，我不一向记提惒达哆必至恶处，生地狱中，住至一劫不可救济。阿难！以我不见提惒达哆有白净法如一毛许，是故我一向记提惒达哆必至恶处，生地狱中，住至一劫不可救济。」

Then Venerable Ānanda wept, wiping away his tears with his hand, and said, "World-Honored One! It is truly amazing! The World-Honored One has made a one-sided prediction that Devadatta will definitely go to a bad place, be reborn in hell, and remain there for an eon, beyond any hope of rescue."

于是，尊者阿难啼泣，以手抆泪，白曰：「世尊！甚奇！甚特！谓世尊一向记提惒达哆必至恶处，生地狱中，住至一劫不可救济。」

The World-Honored One said, "So it is, Ānanda! So it is, Ānanda! I have made a one-sided prediction that Devadatta will definitely go to a bad place, be reborn in hell, and remain there for an eon, beyond any hope of rescue. Ānanda! If you hear from the Tathāgata the knowledge of the faculties of a great person, you will surely develop supreme faith in the Tathāgata and give rise to joy."

世尊告曰：「如是。阿难！如是。阿难！我一向记提惒达哆必至恶处，生地狱中，住至一劫不可救济。阿难！若汝从如来闻大人根智分别者，必得上信如来而怀欢喜。」

Then Venerable Ānanda joined his palms towards the Buddha and said, "World-Honored One! Now is the time! Well-Gone One! Now is the time! If the World-Honored One would teach the knowledge of the faculties of a great person to the bhikṣus, the bhikṣus would listen well and uphold it."

于是，尊者阿难叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！今正是时。善逝！今正是时。若世尊为诸比丘说大人根智分别者，诸比丘从世尊闻，当善受持。」

The World-Honored One said, "Ānanda! Listen carefully and keep it in mind. I will now explain the knowledge of the faculties of a great person for you." Venerable Ānanda accepted the teaching and listened.

世尊告曰：「阿难！谛听，善思念之，我今为汝说大人根智分别。」尊者阿难受教而听。

The World-Honored One said, "Ānanda! The Tathāgata, using his knowledge of others' minds, sees that this person has both wholesome and unwholesome dharmas. Later, using his knowledge of others' minds, the Tathāgata sees that this person has abandoned wholesome dharmas and given rise to unwholesome dharmas. The wholesome dharmas of this person have been abandoned, and unwholesome dharmas have arisen. However, there are remaining wholesome roots that are not severed. From these wholesome roots, wholesome dharmas will arise again. In this way, this person will attain pure dharmas. Ānanda! It is like when the sun first rises in the morning, darkness is dispelled and light arises. What do you think, Ānanda? As the sun continues to rise until noon, has darkness already been dispelled and light already arisen?"

世尊告曰：「阿难！如来以他心智观他人心，知此人成就善法，亦成就不善法。如来后时以他心智复观此人心，知此人灭善法，生不善法。此人善法已灭，不善法已生，余有善根而不断绝，从此善根当复更生善，如是此人得清净法。阿难！犹如平旦日初出时，暗灭明生。阿难！于意云何？日转升上，至于食时，暗已灭，明已生耶？」

Venerable Ānanda said, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

尊者阿难白曰：「尔也。世尊！」

"So it is, Ānanda! The Tathāgata, using his knowledge of others' minds, sees that this person has both wholesome and unwholesome dharmas. Later, using his knowledge of others' minds, the Tathāgata sees that this person has abandoned wholesome dharmas and given rise to unwholesome dharmas. The wholesome dharmas of this person have been abandoned, and unwholesome dharmas have arisen. However, there are remaining wholesome roots that are not severed. From these wholesome roots, wholesome dharmas will arise again. In this way, this person will attain pure dharmas. Ānanda! It is like a grain seed that is not damaged, not broken, not rotten, not split, not harmed by wind or heat, and well-stored in the autumn. If a householder well-cultivates a good field and sows the seed in the field, watering it in a timely manner, what do you think, Ānanda? Will this seed grow and increase?"

「如是。阿难！如来以他心智观他人心，知此人成就善法，亦成就不善法。如来后时以他心智复观此人心，知此人灭善法，生不善法。此人善法已灭，不善法已生，余有善根而不断绝，从是善根当复更生善，如是此人得清净法。阿难！犹如谷种，不坏不破，不腐不剖，不为风热所伤，秋时密藏，若彼居士善治良田，以种洒中，随时雨溉。阿难！于意云何？此种宁得转增长不？」

Venerable Ānanda said, "So it is, World-Honored One!"

尊者阿难白曰：「尔也。世尊！」

So it is, Ānanda! The Tathāgata, using his knowledge of others' minds, sees that this person has both wholesome and unwholesome dharmas. Later, using his knowledge of others' minds, the Tathāgata sees that this person has abandoned wholesome dharmas and given rise to unwholesome dharmas. The wholesome dharmas of this person have been abandoned, and unwholesome dharmas have arisen. However, there are remaining wholesome roots that are not severed. From these wholesome roots, wholesome dharmas will arise again. In this way, this person will attain pure dharmas. Ānanda! This is called the Tathāgata's knowledge of the roots of the great person. The Tathāgata truly knows the origins of all dharmas.

「如是。阿难！如来以他心智观他人心，知此人成就善法，亦成就不善法。如来后时以他心智复观此人心，知此人灭善法，生不善法。此人善法已灭，不善法已生，余有善根而不断绝，从是善根当复更生善，如是此人得清净法。阿难！是谓如来大人根智，如是如来正知诸法本。

"Furthermore, Ānanda! The Tathāgata, using his knowledge of others' minds, sees that this person has both wholesome and unwholesome dharmas. Later, using his knowledge of others' minds, the Tathāgata sees that this person has abandoned wholesome dharmas and given rise to unwholesome dharmas. The wholesome dharmas of this person have been abandoned, and unwholesome dharmas have arisen. However, there are remaining wholesome roots that have not yet been severed, and will certainly be severed. In this way, this person will experience decline. Ānanda! It is like when the sun is about to set in the evening, light disappears and darkness arises. What do you think, Ānanda? Has the sun already set, the light already disappeared, and the darkness already arisen?"

「复次，阿难！如来以他心智观他人心，知此人成就善法，亦成就不善法。如来后时以他心智复观此人心，知此人灭善法，生不善法。此人善法已灭，不善法已生，余有善根而未断绝，必当断绝，如是此人得衰退法。阿难！犹如下晡日垂没时，明灭暗生。阿难！于意云何？彼日已没，明已灭，暗已生耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

尊者阿难白曰：「尔也。世尊！」

"So it is, Ānanda! The Tathāgata, using his knowledge of others' minds, sees that this person has both wholesome and unwholesome dharmas. Later, using his knowledge of others' minds, the Tathāgata sees that this person has abandoned wholesome dharmas and given rise to unwholesome dharmas. The wholesome dharmas of this person have been abandoned, and unwholesome dharmas have arisen. However, there are remaining wholesome roots that have not yet been severed, and will certainly be severed. In this way, this person will experience decline. Ānanda! It is like a grain seed that is not damaged, not broken, not rotten, not split, not harmed by wind or heat, and is carefully stored in the autumn. If a householder well cultivates a good field, sows the seed in the field, and the rain does not come at the right time. What do you think, Ānanda? Will this seed grow and increase?"

「如是。阿难！如来以他心智观他人心，知此人成就善法，亦成就不善法。如来后时以他心智复观此人心，知此人灭善法，生不善法。此人善法已灭，不善法已生，余有善根而未断绝，必当断绝，如是此人得衰退法。阿难！犹如谷种，不坏不破，不腐不剖，不为风热所伤，秋时密藏。若彼居士善治良田，以种洒中，雨不随时。阿难！于意云何？此种宁得转增长耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "No, World-Honored One!"

尊者阿难白曰：「不也。世尊！」

So it is, Ānanda! The Tathāgata, using his knowledge of others' minds, sees that this person has both wholesome and unwholesome dharmas. Later, using his knowledge of others' minds, the Tathāgata sees that this person has abandoned wholesome dharmas and given rise to unwholesome dharmas. The wholesome dharmas of this person have been abandoned, and unwholesome dharmas have arisen. However, there are remaining wholesome roots that have not yet been severed, and will certainly be severed. In this way, this person will experience decline. Ānanda! This is called the Tathāgata's knowledge of the roots of the great person. The Tathāgata knows the origin of all dharmas in this way.

「如是。阿难！如来以他心智观他人心，知此人成就善法，亦成就不善法。如来后时以他心智复观此人心，知此人灭善法，生不善法。此人善法已灭，不善法已生，余有善根而未断绝，必当断绝，如是此人得衰退法。阿难！是谓如来大人根智，如是如来正知诸法本。

"Furthermore, Ānanda! The Tathāgata, using his knowledge of others' minds, sees that this person has no pure dharmas even as small as a hair. This person is completely filled with evil and unwholesome dharmas, which are defiled, the basis for future existence, and the cause of suffering, birth, old age, sickness, and death. When this person's body perishes and life ends, they will certainly go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. Ānanda! It is like a seed that is rotten, broken, damaged by wind and heat, not properly stored in the autumn, and planted in poor soil by a farmer who does not know how to cultivate it. The rain does not come at the right time. What do you think, Ānanda? Will this seed grow and increase?"

「复次，阿难！如来以他心智观他人心，我不见此人有白净法如一毛许，此人恶不善法一向充满秽污，为当来有本烦热苦报、生老病死因，如是此人身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。阿难！犹如种子，腐坏破剖，为风热所伤，秋时不密藏，若彼居士，非是良田，又不善治，便下种子，雨不随时。阿难！于意云何？此种宁得转增长耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "No, World-Honored One!"

尊者阿难白曰：「不也。世尊！」

"So it is, Ānanda! The Tathāgata, using his knowledge of others' minds, sees that this person has no pure dharmas even as small as a hair. This person is completely filled with evil and unwholesome dharmas, which are defiled, the basis for future existence, and the cause of suffering, birth, old age, sickness, and death. When this person's body perishes and life ends, they will certainly go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. Ānanda! This is called the Tathāgata's knowledge of the faculties of great beings. The Tathāgata knows all dharmas in this way."

「如是。阿难！如来以他心智观他人心，我不见此人有白净法如一毛许，此人恶不善法一向充满秽污，为当来有本烦热苦报、生老病死因，如是此人身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。阿难！是谓如来大人根智，如是如来正知诸法本。」

Then Venerable Ānanda, with palms joined towards the Buddha, said, "The World-Honored One has already spoken of these three kinds of people. Could you please speak of other three kinds of people?"

于是，尊者阿难叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊已说如此三种之人，宁可更说异三种人耶？」

The World-Honored One said, "It can be spoken of. Ānanda! The Tathāgata, using his knowledge of others' minds, sees that this person has both unwholesome and wholesome dharmas. Later, the Tathāgata, using his knowledge of others' minds, again sees this person's mind and knows that this person has eliminated unwholesome dharmas and given rise to wholesome dharmas. This person's unwholesome dharmas have been eliminated and wholesome dharmas have arisen, but there are still unwholesome roots that have not been severed. From these unwholesome roots, unwholesome dharmas will arise again. This person has attained the Dharma of decline. Ānanda! It is like a fire that is just lit and burns up a whole bundle of firewood. Suppose someone then adds dry grass and withered wood. What do you think, Ānanda? Will that fire burn more fiercely?"

世尊告曰：「可说也。阿难！如来以他心智观他人心，知此人成就不善法，亦成就善法。如来后时以他心智复观此人心，知此人灭不善法而生善法。此人不善法已灭，善法已生，余有不善根而不断绝，从是不善根当复更生不善，如是此人得衰退法。阿难！犹如燃火，始然之时，尽然一［火＊佥］，彼或有人益以燥草，足以槁木。阿难！于意云何？彼火宁转增炽盛耶？」

Venerable Ānanda said, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

尊者阿难白曰：「尔也。世尊！」

So it is, Ānanda! The Tathāgata, using his knowledge of others' minds, sees that this person has both unwholesome and wholesome dharmas. Later, the Tathāgata, using his knowledge of others' minds, again sees this person's mind and knows that this person has eliminated unwholesome dharmas and given rise to wholesome dharmas. This person's unwholesome dharmas have been eliminated and wholesome dharmas have arisen, but there are still unwholesome roots that have not been severed. From these unwholesome roots, unwholesome dharmas will arise again. This person has attained the Dharma of decline. Ānanda! This is called the Tathāgata's knowledge of the faculties of great beings. In this way, the Tathāgata truly knows the fundamental dharmas.

「如是。阿难！如来以他心智观他人心，知此人成就不善法，亦成就善法。如来后时以他心智复观此人心，知此人灭不善法而生善法，此人不善法已灭，善法已生，余有不善根而不断绝，从是不善根当复更生不善，如是此人得衰退法。阿难！是谓如来大人根智，如是如来正知诸法本。

"Furthermore, Ānanda! The Tathāgata, using his knowledge of others' minds, sees that this person has both unwholesome and wholesome dharmas. Later, the Tathāgata, using his knowledge of others' minds, again sees this person's mind and knows that this person has eliminated unwholesome dharmas and given rise to wholesome dharmas. This person's unwholesome dharmas have been eliminated and wholesome dharmas have arisen, but there are still unwholesome roots that have not been severed. They will definitely be severed. This person has attained the pure Dharma. Ānanda! It is like a burning fire. If someone takes that burning fire and places it on a flat, clean ground or on a stone, what do you think? Will that fire increase and blaze even more?"

「复次，阿难！如来以他心智观他人心，成就不善法，亦成就善法。如来后时以他心智复观此人心，知此人灭不善法而生善法，此人不善法已灭，善法已生，余有不善根而未断绝，必当断绝，如是此人得清净法。阿难！犹如然火炽然之时，尽然一［火＊佥］，彼或有人从此盛火，置平净地，或著石上。阿难！于意云何？彼火宁转增炽盛耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "No, World-Honored One!"

尊者阿难白曰：「不也。世尊！」

So it is, Ānanda! The Tathāgata, using his knowledge of others' minds, sees that this person has both unwholesome and wholesome dharmas. Later, the Tathāgata, using his knowledge of others' minds, again sees this person's mind and knows that this person has eliminated unwholesome dharmas and given rise to wholesome dharmas. This person's unwholesome dharmas have been eliminated and wholesome dharmas have arisen, but there are still unwholesome roots that have not been severed. They will definitely be severed. This person has attained the pure Dharma. Ānanda! This is called the Tathāgata's knowledge of the roots of the great person. The Tathāgata truly knows the original nature of all dharmas.

「如是。阿难！如来以他心智观他人心，知此人成就不善法，亦成就善法。如来后时以他心智复观此人心，知此人灭不善法而生善法，此人不善法已灭，善法已生，余有不善根而未断绝，必当断绝，如是此人得清净法。阿难！是谓如来大人根智，如是如来正知诸法本。

"Furthermore, Ānanda! The Tathāgata, using his knowledge of others' minds, sees that this person has no unwholesome karma even as small as a hair. This person's wholesome dharmas are completely filled with happiness and the result of happiness. They will definitely be reborn in a happy place and attain long life. This person will definitely attain parinirvāṇa in this very life. Ānanda! It is like a fire ember that has long since gone out and cooled down. Suppose someone were to add dry grass and withered wood to it. What do you think, Ānanda? Could that dead fire ember be made to burn again?"

「复次，阿难！如来以他心智观他人心，我不见此人有黑业如一毛许，此人善法一向充满与乐乐报，必生乐处而得长寿，如是此人即于现世必得般涅槃。阿难！犹如火炭，久灭已冷，彼或有人虽益以燥草，足以槁木。阿难！于意云何？彼死火炭宁可复得炽然之耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "No, World-Honored One!"

尊者阿难白曰：「不也。世尊！」

So it is, Ānanda! The Tathāgata, using his knowledge of others' minds, sees that this person has no unwholesome karma even as small as a hair. This person's wholesome dharmas are completely filled with happiness and the result of happiness. They will definitely be reborn in a happy place and attain long life. This person will definitely attain parinirvāṇa in this very life. Ānanda! This is called the Tathāgata's knowledge of the faculties of great beings. The Tathāgata truly knows all dharmas in this way.

「如是。阿难！如来以他心智观他人心，我不见此人有黑业如一毛许，此人善法一向充满与乐乐报，必生乐处而得长寿，如是此人即于现世必得般涅槃。阿难！是谓如来大人根智，如是如来正知诸法本。

"Ānanda! The first person among the three mentioned earlier attains the pure Dharma, the second person attains the Dharma of decline, and the third person, when their body perishes and their life ends, will definitely go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. The first person among the three mentioned later attains the Dharma of decline, the second person attains the pure Dharma, and the third person attains parinirvāṇa in this very life. Ānanda! I have already explained the knowledge of the faculties of great beings for you. It is like a teacher giving rise to great compassion and pity for his disciples, seeking their benefit and well-being, seeking their peace and happiness. I have now done this for you. You should also do it yourselves. Go to places free from affairs, under trees in mountain forests, to empty and quiet places, and sit in meditation. Do not be negligent, but strive with diligence, so as not to regret it later. This is my instruction and admonition."

「阿难！前说三人者，第一人得清净法，第二人得衰退法，第三人身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。后说三人者，第一人得衰退法，第二人得清净法，第三人即于现世得般涅槃。阿难！我已为汝说大人根智，如尊师所为弟子起大慈哀，怜念愍伤，求义及饶益，求安隐快乐，我今已作。汝等当复自作，至无事处、山林树下、空安静处，宴坐思惟，勿得放逸，勤加精进，莫令后悔。此是我之教勅，是我训诲。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
