MĀ 114

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 10 林品

114. （一一四）优陀罗经

Parallel to: SN 35.103

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Udraka Rāmaputra, in the assembly, often said like this: 'In this life, observing and realizing this, one does not know the root of the boil, and then fully knows the root of the boil.' Udraka Rāmaputra, without all-knowing, claims to be all-knowing, and without any realization, claims to have realization. Udraka Rāmaputra sees and speaks like this: 'Existence is a disease, a boil, a thorn; if there is no thought, it is ignorance; if there is any realization, it is cessation, the most sublime, up to the realm of neither perception nor non-perception.' He himself delights in the body, himself experiences the body, himself becomes attached to the body, and cultivates up to the realm of neither perception nor non-perception. When the body perishes and life ends, he is reborn in the heaven of neither perception nor non-perception. When his lifespan is exhausted, he returns here and is reborn as a civet cat. This bhikṣu speaks correctly: 'In this life, observing and realizing this, one does not know the root of the boil, and then fully knows the root of the boil.'

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「优陀罗罗摩子，彼在众中，数如是说：『于此生中，观此觉此，不知痈本，然后具知痈本。』优陀罗罗摩子无一切知自称一切知，实无所觉自称有觉。优陀罗罗摩子如是见、如是说，有者，是病、是痈、是刺；设无想者，是愚痴也；若有所觉，是止息、是最妙，谓乃至非有想非无想处。彼自乐身，自受于身，自著身已，修习乃至非有想非无想处，身坏命终，生非有想非无想天中，彼寿尽已，复来此间，生于狸中。此比丘正说者，于此生中，观此觉此，不知痈本，然后具知痈本。

What is a bhikṣu's right view? A bhikṣu knows the six contacts, knows their origin, knows their cessation, knows their taste, knows their suffering, knows their escape, and knows them as they really are with wisdom. This is called a bhikṣu's right view. What is a bhikṣu's realization? A bhikṣu knows the three realizations, knows their origin, knows their cessation, knows their taste, knows their suffering, knows their escape, and knows them as they really are with wisdom. This is called a bhikṣu's realization. What is a bhikṣu's not knowing the root of the boil, and then fully knowing the root of the boil? A bhikṣu knows the cessation of craving, uproots its root, and it will never arise again. This is called a bhikṣu's not knowing the root of the boil, and then fully knowing the root of the boil. The boil refers to this body, which is composed of the four gross elements from the parents, nourished by food and drink, clothed, massaged, bathed, and endured with strength. It is impermanent, subject to decay and dispersal. This is called the boil. The root of the boil refers to the three kinds of craving: craving for desire, craving for form, and craving for the formless. This is called the root of the boil. The boil's entire leakage refers to the six sense bases. The eyes leak in seeing forms, the ears leak in hearing sounds, the nose leaks in smelling smells, the tongue leaks in tasting tastes, the body leaks in feeling touches, and the mind leaks in knowing dharmas. This is called the boil's entire leakage.

「云何比丘正观耶？比丘者，知六更触，知习、知灭、知味、知患、知出要，以慧知如真，是谓比丘正观也。云何比丘觉，比丘者，知三觉，知习、知灭、知味、知患、知出要，以慧知如真，是谓比丘觉。云何比丘不知痈本，然后具知痈本？比丘者，知有爱灭，拔其根本，至竟不复生，是谓比丘不知痈本，然后具知痈本。痈者，谓此身也。色麤四大，从父母生，饮食长养，衣被按摩，澡浴强忍，是无常法、坏法、散法，是谓痈也。痈本者，谓三爱也。欲爱、色爱、无色爱，是谓痈本。痈一切漏者，谓六更触处也。眼漏视色，耳漏闻声，鼻漏嗅香，舌漏尝味，身漏觉触，意漏知诸法，是谓痈一切漏。

"Bhikṣus! I have already explained the boil and the root of the boil for you. Just as a teacher out of great compassion and pity for his disciples, seeking their benefit and happiness, seeking their peace and joy, I have now done so. You should also do the same, going to secluded places, under trees in the forest, empty and quiet places, to sit in meditation and contemplation, not being negligent, diligently practicing, so as not to regret later. This is my instruction and admonition."

「比丘！我已为汝说痈、说痈本，如尊师所为弟子起大慈哀，怜念愍伤，求义及饶益，求安隐快乐者，我今已作。汝等亦当复自作，至无事处、山林树下、空安静处，燕坐思惟，勿得放逸，勤加精进，莫令后悔。此是我之教勅，是我训诲。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
