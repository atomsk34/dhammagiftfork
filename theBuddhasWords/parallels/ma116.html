MĀ 116

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 10 林品

116. （一一六）瞿昙弥经

Parallel to: AN 8.51

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the Śākya country, staying in the Nyagrodha Park in Kapilavastu, together with a great assembly of bhikṣus, spending the summer retreat.

一时，佛游释羇瘦，在迦维罗卫尼拘类树园，与大比丘众俱受夏坐。

At that time, Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī went to where the Buddha was, bowed her head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said, "World-Honored One! Can women attain the fourth fruit of the śramaṇa? For this reason, can women in this true teaching and discipline, with utmost faith, leave home, become homeless, and practice the path?"

尔时，瞿昙弥大爱往诣佛所，稽首佛足，却住一面，白曰：「世尊！女人可得第四沙门果耶？因此故，女人于此正法、律中，至信、舍家、无家、学道耶？」

The World-Honored One said, "Stop! Stop! Gautamī! Do not think like this: 'Women in this true teaching and discipline, with utmost faith, leave home, become homeless, and practice the path.' Gautamī! In this way, you should shave your head, wear the monastic robes, and practice pure conduct for the rest of your life."

世尊告曰：「止！止！瞿昙弥！汝莫作是念：『女人于此正法、律中，至信、舍家、无家、学道。』瞿昙弥！如是汝剃除头发，著袈裟衣，尽其形寿，净修梵行。」

Then Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī, being restrained by the Buddha, bowed her head at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and departed.

于是，瞿昙弥大爱为佛所制，稽首佛足，绕三匝而去。

At that time, the bhikṣus were mending the Buddha's robes. The World-Honored One had not long completed the summer retreat at Śrāvastī, and after mending the robes, he had passed the three months. He put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and was about to travel among the people. Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī heard that the bhikṣus were mending the Buddha's robes. The World-Honored One had not long completed the summer retreat at Śrāvastī, and after mending the robes, he had passed the three months. He put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and was about to travel among the people. Having heard this, Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī again went to where the Buddha was, bowed her head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said, "World-Honored One! Can women attain the fourth fruit of the śramaṇa? For this reason, can women in this true teaching and discipline, with utmost faith, leave home, become homeless, and practice the path?"

尔时，诸比丘为佛治衣，世尊不久于释羇瘦受夏坐竟，补治衣讫，过三月已，摄衣持钵，当游人间。瞿昙弥大爱闻诸比丘为佛治衣，世尊不久于释羇瘦受夏坐竟，补治衣讫，过三月已，摄衣持钵，当游人间。瞿昙弥大爱闻已，复诣佛所，稽首佛足，却住一面，白曰：「世尊！女人可得第四沙门果耶？因此故，女人于此正法、律中，至信、舍家、无家、学道耶？」

The World-Honored One also said twice, "Stop! Stop! Gautamī! Do not think like this: 'Women in this true teaching and discipline, with utmost faith, leave home, become homeless, and practice the path.' Gautamī! In this way, you shave your hair, put on the monastic robes, and practice pure conduct for the rest of your life."

世尊亦再告曰：「止！止！瞿昙弥！汝莫作是念：『女人于此正法、律中，至信、舍家、无家、学道。』瞿昙弥！如是汝剃除头发，著袈裟衣，尽其形寿，净修梵行。」

Then Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī was again restrained by the Buddha. She bowed her head at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and left.

于是，瞿昙弥大爱再为佛所制，稽首佛足，遶三匝而去。

At that time, the World-Honored One had completed the summer retreat at Śrāvastī, repaired his robes, and after three months, took up his robe and bowl and traveled through the human world. Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī heard that the World-Honored One had completed the summer retreat at Śrāvastī, repaired his robes, and after three months, took up his robe and bowl and traveled through the human world. Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī then followed the Buddha together with the elderly Śākya women, gradually arriving at Nādikā, where they stayed at the Nādikā nunnery. Then Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī again went to where the Buddha was, bowed her head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said, "World-Honored One! Can women attain the fourth fruit of the śramaṇa? Is it because of this that women in this true teaching and discipline, with utmost faith, leave home, become homeless, and practice the path?"

彼时，世尊于释羇瘦受夏坐竟，补治衣讫，过三月已，摄衣持钵，游行人间。瞿昙弥大爱闻世尊于释羇瘦受夏坐竟，补治衣讫，过三月已，摄衣持钵，游行人间。瞿昙弥大爱即与舍夷诸老母，俱随逐佛后，展转往至那摩提，住那摩提揵尼精舍。于是，瞿昙弥大爱复诣佛所，稽首佛足，却住一面，白曰：「世尊！女人可得第四沙门果耶？因此故，女人于此正法、律中，至信、舍家、无家、学道耶？」

The World-Honored One said up to the third time, "Stop! Stop! Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! Do not think like this: 'Women in this true teaching and discipline, with utmost faith, leave home, become homeless, and practice the path.' Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! In this way, you shave your head, wear the monastic robes, and practice pure conduct for the rest of your life."

世尊至三告曰：「止！止！瞿昙弥！汝莫作是念：『女人于此正法、律中，至信、舍家、无家、学道。』瞿昙弥大爱！如是汝剃除头发，著袈裟衣，尽其形寿，净修梵行。」

Then Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī was restrained by the World-Honored One three times. She bowed her head at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and left.

于是，瞿昙弥大爱三为世尊所制，稽首佛足，绕三匝而去。

At that time, Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī walked barefoot, her feet were dirty, her body was covered in dust, and she was exhausted and weeping, standing outside the gate. Venerable Ānanda saw Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī walking barefoot, her feet were dirty, her body was covered in dust, and she was exhausted and weeping, standing outside the gate. Seeing this, he asked, "Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī, why are you walking barefoot, your feet are dirty, your body is covered in dust, and you are exhausted and weeping, standing outside the gate?"

彼时，瞿昙弥大爱涂跣污足，尘土坌体，疲极悲泣，住立门外。尊者阿难见瞿昙弥大爱涂跣污足，尘土坌体，疲极悲泣，住立门外，见已，问曰：「瞿昙弥！以何等故，涂跣污足，尘土坌体，疲极悲泣，住立门外？」

Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī replied, "Venerable Ānanda, women cannot attain the supreme path of the right Dharma and Vinaya by leaving home and becoming homeless."

瞿昙弥大爱答曰：「尊者阿难！女人不得于此正法、律中，至信、舍家、无家、学道。」

Venerable Ānanda said, "Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī, please wait here for a moment. I will go to the Buddha and inform him of this matter."

尊者阿难语曰：「瞿昙弥！今且住此，我往诣佛，白如是事。」

Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī replied, "Yes, Venerable Ānanda!"

瞿昙弥大爱白曰：「唯然。尊者阿难！」

Then Venerable Ānanda went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "World-Honored One! Can women attain the fourth fruit of the Śramaṇa? Is it because of this that women can attain the supreme faith, leave home, become homeless, and practice the path in this true teaching and discipline?"

于是，尊者阿难往诣佛所，稽首佛足，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！女人可得第四沙门果耶？因此故，女人得于此正法、律中，至信、舍家、无家、学道耶？」

The World-Honored One said, "Stop, stop! Ānanda! Do not think like this: 'Women can attain the supreme faith, leave home, become homeless, and practice the path in this true teaching and discipline.' Ānanda! If women were to attain the supreme faith, leave home, become homeless, and practice the path in this true teaching and discipline, it would not allow this pure practice to last long. Ānanda! It is like a household with many daughters and few sons. Would this household be able to flourish?"

世尊告曰：「止！止！阿难！汝莫作是念：『女人得于此正法、律中，至信、舍家、无家、学道。』阿难！若使女人得于此正法、律中，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，令此梵行便不得久住。阿难！犹如人家多女少男者，此家为得转兴盛耶？」

Venerable Ānanda said, "No, World-Honored One!"

尊者阿难白曰：「不也。世尊！」

"So it is, Ānanda! If women were to attain the supreme faith, leave home, become homeless, and practice the path in this true teaching and discipline, it would not allow this pure practice to last long. Ānanda! It is like weeds growing in a rice field or wheat field, which will surely destroy the field. So it is, Ānanda! If women were to attain the supreme faith, leave home, become homeless, and practice the path in this true teaching and discipline, it would not allow this pure practice to last long."

「如是，阿难！若使女人得于此正法、律中，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，令此梵行不得久住。阿难！犹如稻田及麦田中，有秽生者必坏彼田。如是，阿难！若使女人得于此正法、律中，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，令此梵行不得久住。」

Venerable Ānanda again said, "World-Honored One! Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī has greatly benefited the World-Honored One. Why is that? After the World-Honored One's mother passed away, Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī raised the World-Honored One."

尊者阿难复白曰：「世尊！瞿昙弥大爱为世尊多所饶益。所以者何？世尊母亡后，瞿昙弥大爱鞠养世尊。」

The World-Honored One said, "So it is, Ānanda! So it is, Ānanda! Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī has greatly benefited me, for after my mother passed away, she raised me. Ānanda! I have also greatly benefited Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī. Why is that? Ānanda! Because of me, Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī has taken refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṅgha, has no doubts about the Three Jewels and the truths of suffering, origin, cessation, and path, has attained faith, upholds the precepts, studies widely, practices generosity and attains wisdom, abstains from killing and abandons killing, abstains from taking what is not given and abandons taking what is not given, abstains from sexual misconduct and abandons sexual misconduct, abstains from false speech and abandons false speech, abstains from alcohol and abandons alcohol. Ānanda! If someone, because of another person, takes refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṅgha, has no doubts about the Three Jewels and the truths of suffering, origin, cessation, and path, attains faith, upholds the precepts, studies widely, practices generosity and attains wisdom, abstains from killing and abandons killing, abstains from taking what is not given and abandons taking what is not given, abstains from sexual misconduct and abandons sexual misconduct, abstains from false speech and abandons false speech, abstains from alcohol and abandons alcohol, Ānanda! Even if that person were to provide that person with robes, food and drink, bedding, and medicine for the rest of their life, they would not be able to repay their kindness.

世尊告曰：「如是，阿难！如是，阿难！瞿昙弥大爱多饶益我，谓母亡后，鞠养于我。阿难！我亦多饶益于瞿昙弥大爱。所以者何？阿难！瞿昙弥大爱因我故，得归佛、归法、归比丘僧，不疑三尊及苦、习、灭、道，成就于信，奉持禁戒，修学博闻，成就布施而得智慧，离杀、断杀、离不与取、断不与取，离邪婬、断邪婬，离妄言、断妄言，离酒、断酒。阿难！若使有人因人故，得归佛、归法、归比丘僧，不疑三尊及苦、习、灭、道，成就于信，奉持禁戒，修学博闻，成就布施而得智慧，离杀、断杀，离不与取、断不与取，离邪婬、断邪婬，离妄言、断妄言，离酒、断酒，阿难！设使此人为供养彼人衣被、饮食、卧具、汤药、诸生活具，至尽形寿，不得报恩。

Ānanda! I now establish eight rules for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives. Ānanda! It is like a fisherman or his disciple digging a deep pond to protect the water from flowing out. In the same way, Ānanda! I now speak these eight rules for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives. What are the eight? Ānanda! A bhikṣuṇī should seek full ordination from a bhikṣu. Ānanda! I establish this first rule for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives.

「阿难！我今为女人施设八尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。阿难！犹如鱼师及鱼师弟子，深水作坞，为守护水，不令流出。如是，阿难！我今为女人说八尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。云何为八？阿难！比丘尼当从比丘求受具足。阿难！我为女人施设此第一尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。

Ānanda! A bhikṣuṇī should go to a bhikṣu every half month to receive instruction. Ānanda! I establish this second rule for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives.

「阿难！比丘尼半月半月往从比丘受教。阿难！我为女人施设此第二尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。」

Ānanda! If there are no bhikṣus in a place where one resides, a bhikṣuṇī should not spend the rainy season there. Ānanda! I establish this third rule for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives.

「阿难！若住止处设无比丘者，比丘尼便不得受夏坐。阿难！我为女人施设此第三尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。

Ānanda! After completing the rainy season retreat, a bhikṣuṇī should request three things from both communities: to see, hear, and doubt. Ānanda! I establish this fourth rule for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives.

「阿难！比丘尼受夏坐讫，于两部众中，当请三事，求见、闻、疑。阿难！我为女人施设此第四尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。

Ānanda! If a bhikṣu does not allow a bhikṣuṇī to ask questions, then a bhikṣuṇī should not ask a bhikṣu about the sūtras, vinaya, or abhidharma. If he allows her to ask, then a bhikṣuṇī may ask about the sūtras, vinaya, or abhidharma. Ānanda! I establish this fifth rule for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives.

「阿难！若比丘不听比丘尼问者，比丘尼则不得问比丘经、律、阿毘昙，若听问者，比丘尼得问经、律、阿毘昙。阿难！我为女人施设此第五尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。

Ānanda! A bhikṣuṇī should not speak about a bhikṣu's offenses, but a bhikṣu may speak about a bhikṣuṇī's offenses. Ānanda! I establish this sixth rule for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives.

「阿难！比丘尼不得说比丘所犯，比丘得说比丘尼所犯。阿难！我为女人施设此第六尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。

Ānanda! If a bhikṣuṇī commits a saṃghāvaśeṣa offense, she should perform the mānatva penance for fifteen days in both the bhikṣu and bhikṣuṇī saṃghas. Ānanda! I establish this seventh rule for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives.

「阿难！比丘尼若犯僧伽婆尸沙，当于两部众中，十五日行不慢。阿难！我为女人施设此第七尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。

Ānanda! Even if a bhikṣuṇī has received full ordination for a hundred years, she should still bow her head to the ground and pay homage to a bhikṣu who has just received full ordination, showing respect and serving him, joining her palms and greeting him. Ānanda! I establish this eighth rule for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives.

「阿难！比丘尼受具足虽至百岁，故当向始受具足比丘极下意稽首作礼，恭敬承事，叉手问讯。阿难！我为女人施设此第八尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。

Ānanda! I establish these eight rules for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives. Ānanda! If Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī upholds these eight rules, she may go forth in this true teaching and discipline, receive full ordination, and become a bhikṣuṇī.

「阿难！我为女人施设此八尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。阿难！若瞿昙弥大爱奉持此八尊师法者，是此正法、律中，出家学道，得受具足，作比丘尼。」

Then Venerable Ānanda, having heard the Buddha's words, received them well and upheld them well. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and departed. He went to Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī and said, "Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! Women may go forth in this true teaching and discipline, with true faith, leave home, and learn the path. Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! The World-Honored One has established these eight rules for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives. What are the eight? Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! A bhikṣuṇī should seek full ordination from a bhikṣu. Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! The World-Honored One has established this first rule for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives.

于是，尊者阿难闻佛所说，善受善持，稽首佛足，绕三匝而去，往诣瞿昙弥大爱所，语曰：「瞿昙弥！女人得于此正法、律中，至信、舍家、无] cf.［丽】家、学道。瞿昙弥大爱！世尊为女人施设此八尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。云何为八？瞿昙弥！比丘尼当从比丘求受具足。瞿昙弥！世尊为女人施设此第一尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。

Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! A bhikṣuṇī should go to a bhikṣu every half month to receive instruction. Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! The World-Honored One has established this second rule for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives.

「瞿昙弥！比丘尼半月半月往从比丘受教，瞿昙弥！世尊为女人施设此第二尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。

Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! If there are no bhikṣus in a place where one is staying, a bhikṣuṇī should not spend the rainy season there. Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! The World-Honored One has established this third rule for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives.

「瞿昙弥！若住止处无比丘者，比丘尼不得受夏坐。瞿昙弥！世尊为女人施设此第三尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。

Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! After completing the rainy season retreat, a bhikṣuṇī should request three things from both the bhikṣu and bhikṣuṇī saṃghas: to see, hear, and doubt. Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! The World-Honored One has established this fourth rule for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives.

「瞿昙弥！比丘尼受夏坐讫，于两部众中当请三事，求见、闻、疑。瞿昙弥！世尊为女人施设此第四尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。

Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! If a bhikṣu does not allow a bhikṣuṇī to ask questions, a bhikṣuṇī should not ask a bhikṣu about the sūtras, vinaya, or abhidharma. If he allows her to ask, a bhikṣuṇī may ask about the sūtras, vinaya, or abhidharma. Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! The World-Honored One has established this fifth rule for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives.

「瞿昙弥！若比丘不听比丘尼问者，比丘尼不得问比丘经、律、阿毘昙，若听问者，比丘尼得问经、律、阿毘昙。瞿昙弥！世尊为女人施设此第五尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。

Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! A bhikṣuṇī should not speak of a bhikṣu's offenses, but a bhikṣu may speak of a bhikṣuṇī's offenses. Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! The World-Honored One has established this sixth rule for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives.

「瞿昙弥！比丘尼不得说比丘所犯，比丘得说比丘尼所犯。瞿昙弥！世尊为女人施设此第六尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。

Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! If a bhikṣuṇī commits a saṃghāvaśeṣa offense, she should perform the mānatva penance for fifteen days in both the bhikṣu and bhikṣuṇī saṃghas. Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! The World-Honored One has established this seventh rule for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives.

「瞿昙弥！比丘尼若犯僧伽婆尸沙，当于两部众中，十五日行不慢。瞿昙弥！世尊为女人施设此第七尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。

Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! Even if a bhikṣuṇī has received full ordination for a hundred years, she should still bow her head to the feet of a bhikṣu who has just received full ordination, pay homage to him with respect and reverence, and greet him with joined palms. Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! The World-Honored One has established this eighth rule for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives.

「瞿昙弥！比丘尼受具足虽至百岁，故当向始受具足比丘极下意稽首作礼，恭敬承事，叉手问讯。瞿昙弥！世尊为女人施设此第八尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持，尽其形寿。

"Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! The World-Honored One has established these eight rules for women to respect and follow, which women should not violate and should uphold for the rest of their lives. Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī! The World-Honored One has said: 'If Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī upholds these eight rules for women to respect and follow, she may go forth in this true teaching and discipline, receive full ordination, and become a bhikṣuṇī.'"

「瞿昙弥！世尊为女人施设此八尊师法，谓女人不当犯，女人奉持、尽其形寿。瞿昙弥！世尊如是说：『若瞿昙弥大爱奉持此八尊师法者，是此正法、律中，出家学道，得受具足，作比丘尼。』」

Then Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī said, "Venerable Ānanda, let me give an analogy. The wise understand the meaning through analogies. Venerable Ānanda, it is like a noble lady, a brahmin lady, a householder lady, or a worker lady, who is beautiful, well-bathed and perfumed, wearing clean and bright clothes, adorned with various ornaments. If someone thinks of her and seeks to benefit, to bring her peace and happiness, and gives her a blue lotus stamen, or a jasmine garland, or a sumana flower stamen, or a pāṭala flower stamen, or an ati-mūrdha flower stamen, she will joyfully receive it with both hands and adorn her head with it. In the same way, Venerable Ānanda, I will uphold for the rest of my life the eight rules for women to respect and follow established by the World-Honored One."

于是，瞿昙弥大爱白曰：「尊者阿难！听我说喻，智者闻喻则解其义。尊者阿难！犹刹利女，梵志、居士、工师女，端正姝好，极净沐浴以香涂身，著明净衣，种种璎珞严饰其容。或复有人为念彼女，求利及饶益，求安隐] cf.［丽】快乐，以青莲华须、或瞻卜华鬘、或修摩那华须、或婆师华须、或阿提牟多华须，持与彼女。彼女欢喜，两手受之，以严其头。如是，尊者阿难！世尊为女人施设此八尊师法，我尽形寿顶受奉持。」

Then Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī went forth in the true teaching and discipline, received full ordination, and became a bhikṣuṇī. At that time, Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī became the leader of the bhikṣuṇīs, and the elder bhikṣuṇīs who were known to the king and had long practiced the holy life went together to Venerable Ānanda, bowed their heads at his feet, and stood to one side. They said, "Venerable Ānanda, please know that these elder bhikṣuṇīs are known to the king and have long practiced the holy life. Those bhikṣus are young and newly ordained, having entered this true teaching and discipline only recently. Please have those bhikṣus bow their heads at the feet of the bhikṣuṇīs according to their seniority, and respectfully serve them, joining their palms and greeting them.

尔时，瞿昙弥大爱于正法、律中，出家学道，得受具足，作比丘尼。彼时瞿昙弥大爱于后转成大比丘尼众，与诸长老上尊比丘尼为王者所识，久修梵行，共俱往诣尊者阿难所，稽首作礼，却住一面，白曰：「尊者阿难！当知此诸比丘尼长老上尊为王者所识，久修梵行；彼诸比丘年少新学，晚后出家，入此正法、律甫尔不久。愿令此诸比丘为诸比丘尼随其大小稽首作礼，恭敬承事，叉手问讯。」

Then Venerable Ānanda said, "Mahāprajāpatī, please wait here. I will go to the Buddha and tell him this matter."

于是，尊者阿难语曰：「瞿昙弥！今且住此，我往诣佛，白如是事。」

Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī said, "Yes, Venerable Ānanda!"

瞿昙弥大爱白曰：「唯然。尊者阿难！」

Then Venerable Ānanda went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "World-Honored One! Today Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī and the elder nuns who are respected by the king, who have practiced the holy life for a long time, came to me together, bowed their heads at my feet, stood to one side, joined their palms, and said to me, 'Venerable Ānanda! These elder nuns who are respected by the king, who have practiced the holy life for a long time, and those young monks who have recently gone forth and entered this true teaching and discipline for only a short while - may these young monks pay homage to the elder nuns according to their seniority, respect them, serve them, and greet them with joined palms.'"

于是，尊者阿难往诣佛所，稽首佛足，却住一面，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！今日瞿昙弥大爱与诸比丘尼长老上尊为王者所识，久修梵行，俱来诣我所，稽首我足，却住一面，叉手语我曰：『尊者阿难！此诸比丘尼长老上尊为王者所识，久修梵行；彼诸比丘年少新学，晚后出家，入此正法、律甫尔不久。愿令此诸比丘为诸比丘尼随其大小稽首作礼，恭敬承事，叉手问讯。』」

The World-Honored One said, "Stop, stop, Ānanda! Guard this speech and do not speak it. Ānanda, if you knew as I know, you would not speak a single word, let alone speak like this. Ānanda, if women were not allowed to go forth from the household life into homelessness in this true teaching and discipline, the brahmins and householders would spread their robes on the ground and say, 'The diligent śramaṇas can walk on this. The diligent śramaṇas practice what is difficult to practice, bringing long-term benefit, welfare, and happiness to us.' Ānanda, if women were not allowed to go forth from the household life into homelessness in this true teaching and discipline, the brahmins and householders would offer their hair and say, 'The diligent śramaṇas can walk on this. The diligent śramaṇas practice what is difficult to practice, bringing long-term benefit, welfare, and happiness to us.'"

世尊告曰：「止！止！阿难！守护此言，慎莫说是。阿难！若使汝知如我知者，不应说一句，况复如是说？阿难！若使女人不得于此] cf.［丽】正法、律中，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，诸梵志、居士当以衣布地而作是说：『精进沙门可于上行，精进沙门难行而行，令我长夜得利饶益，安隐快乐。』阿难！若女人不得于此正法、律中，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，诸梵志、居士当以头发布施而作是说：『精进沙门可于上行，精进沙门难行而行，令我长夜得利饶益，安隐快乐。』

"Ānanda, if women were not allowed to go forth from the household life into homelessness in this true teaching and discipline, the brahmins and householders would offer various foods with their hands to the śramaṇas by the roadside and say, 'Venerables, please accept this food and take it away as you wish, bringing long-term benefit, welfare, and happiness to us.' Ānanda, if women were not allowed to go forth from the household life into homelessness in this true teaching and discipline, the faithful brahmins would see diligent śramaṇas, respectfully embrace them, bring them inside, and offer various goods to the diligent śramaṇas, saying, 'Venerables, please accept this and take it away as you wish, bringing long-term benefit, welfare, and happiness to us.'"

「阿难！若女人不得于此正法、律中，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，诸梵志、居士若见沙门、当以手奉种种饮食，住道边待而作是说：『诸尊！受是食是，可持是去，随意所用，令我长夜得利饶益，安隐快乐。』阿难！若女人不得于此正法、律中，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，诸信梵志见精进沙门，敬心扶抱，将入于内，持种种财物与精进沙门而作是说：『诸尊！受是可持是去，随意所用，令我长夜得利饶益，安隐快乐。』

"Ānanda, if women were not allowed to go forth from the household life into homelessness in this true teaching and discipline, the moon and sun, with their great supernatural powers, great majesty, great merit, and great spiritual power, would still not match the spiritual power of diligent śramaṇas, let alone the emaciated non-Buddhist ascetics. Ānanda, if women were not allowed to go forth from the household life into homelessness in this true teaching and discipline, the true teaching would have lasted a thousand years, but now it has lost five hundred years, with only five hundred years remaining. Ānanda, you should know that women cannot do five things. If women were to become Tathāgatas, Arhats, Perfectly Enlightened Ones, or wheel-turning kings, or become Śakra, Māra, or Mahābrahmā, it would be impossible. You should know that men can do five things. If men were to become Tathāgatas, Arhats, Perfectly Enlightened Ones, or wheel-turning kings, or become Śakra, Māra, or Mahābrahmā, it would certainly be possible."

「阿难！若女人不得于此正法、律中，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，此日月有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，然于精进沙门威神之德犹不相及，况复死瘦异学耶？阿难！若女人不得于此正法、律中，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，正法当住千年，今失五百岁，余有五百年。阿难！当知女人不得行五事，若女人作如来、无所著、等正觉，及转轮王、天帝释、魔王、大梵天者，终无是处。当知男子得行五事，若男子作如来、无所著、等正觉，及转轮王、天帝释、魔王、大梵天者，必有是处。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Venerable Ānanda and the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said, and they were delighted and carried it out.

佛说如是。尊者阿难及诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

The tenth sūtra, the Sūtra on Gautami, is finished.

瞿昙弥经第十竟
