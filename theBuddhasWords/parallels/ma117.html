MĀ 117

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 11 大品

117. （一一七）柔软经

Parallel to: AN 3.39 / AN 5.57 (~) / EĀ 22.8 (~)

soft, elephant, place, impermanence, request, Champa, twenty
kotis, eight difficulties, poverty, desire, field of
merit, upasaka, enemy, teach, Dhammapada, subdue Mara
Rādha, Upāli, Śākya asks, and Sudinna, merchant
world, merit, stop, reach the end, simile

柔软、龙象、处　　无常、请、瞻波二十亿、八难　　贫穷、欲、福田优婆塞、怨家　　教昙弥、降魔赖咤、优婆离　　释问、及善生商人、世间、福　　息止、至边、喻

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One addressed the bhikṣus, saying: "Since I left home to study the path, I have been seeking ease, leisure, and supreme softness. When I was in the palace of King Śuddhodana, various palaces were built for me, spring palaces, summer palaces, and winter palaces, for my enjoyment. Not far from the palaces, various flower ponds were built, blue lotus ponds, red lotus ponds, pink lotus ponds, and white lotus ponds. In those ponds, various water flowers were planted, blue lotuses, red lotuses, pink lotuses, and white lotuses, always with water and flowers. People were ordered to guard them, not allowing anyone to enter, for my enjoyment. On the banks of those ponds, various land flowers were planted, such as sumana flowers, pāṭala flowers, jasmine flowers, śuṇḍa flowers, madhuka flowers, ati-mūrdha flowers, and pāruṣaka flowers, for my enjoyment. Four people were ordered to bathe me. After bathing me, red sandalwood incense was used to anoint my body. After anointing my body with incense, I wore new silk clothes, new inside and out, front and back. A parasol was always held over me day and night, so that the prince would not be touched by dew at night or scorched by the sun during the day. As for the coarse wheat gruel, bean soup, and ginger vegetables that others usually eat, they were considered the best food. In this way, the lowest servants of my father, King Śuddhodana, ate rice and delicacies as their best food.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「自我昔日出家学道，为从优游、从容闲乐、极柔輭来，我在父王悦头檀家时，为我造作种种宫殿，春殿、夏殿及以冬殿，为我好游戏故。去殿不远，复造种种若干华池，青莲华池、红莲华池、赤莲华池、白莲华池。于彼池中殖种种水华、青莲华、红莲华、赤莲华、白莲华，常水常华，使人守护，不通一切，为我好游戏故。于其池岸殖种种陆华，修摩那华、婆师华、瞻卜华、修揵提华、摩头揵提华、阿提牟多华、波罗头华，为我好游戏故。而使四人沐浴于我，沐浴我已，赤旃檀香用涂我身，香涂身已，著新缯衣，上下、内外、表里皆新，昼夜常以繖盖覆我，莫令太子夜为露所沾，昼为日所炙。如常他家麁［麸－夫＋广］、麦饭、豆羹、姜菜，为第一食，如是我父悦头檀家最下使人，粳粮肴馔为第一食。

Furthermore, if there were wild animals, the most beautiful ones, such as the tīrthyas, the kapiñjalas, the hemiraṇḍas, and the śīrṣaśālis, such wild animals, the most beautiful ones, were always prepared for me as food.

「复次，若有野田禽兽，最美禽兽，提帝逻惒咤、劫宾阇逻、奚米何犁泥奢施罗米，如是野田禽兽，最美禽兽，常为我设如是之食。

I remember that in the past, when King Śuddhodana ascended the main hall in the fourth month of summer, there were no men, only female entertainers for his own enjoyment. He did not come down at first. When I wanted to go out to the garden to see, thirty named riders selected the best mounts, with a procession in front and behind, serving and guiding. How much more so for the others? I have this kind of supernatural power, which is the most soft and tender.

「我忆昔时父悦头檀家，于夏四月升正殿上，无有男子，唯有女妓而自娱乐，初不来下，我欲出至园观之时，三十名骑，简选上乘，卤簿前后，侍从导引，况复其余？我有是如意足，此最柔软。

I also remember that in the past, when I was watching the farmers resting in the fields, I went to the Jambu tree, sat cross-legged, separated from desire, separated from evil and unwholesome dharmas, with initial and sustained thought, joy and happiness born of separation, and attained the first dhyāna, accomplishing and dwelling in it. I thought: 'The uneducated and ignorant common people have the dharmas of sickness themselves, and are not free from sickness. Seeing the sickness of others, they hate, despise, do not love, and are not pleased. They do not observe themselves.' I further thought: 'I have the dharmas of sickness myself, and am not free from sickness. If I see the sickness of others and hate, despise, do not love, and am not pleased, that is not right for me. Because I also have these dharmas.' Having observed this, the pride arising from not being sick immediately ceased. I further thought: 'The uneducated and ignorant common people have the dharmas of aging themselves, and are not free from aging. Seeing the aging of others, they hate, despise, do not love, and are not pleased. They do not observe themselves.'

「我复忆昔时看田作人止息田上，往诣阎浮树下，结跏趺坐，离欲、离恶不善之法，有觉、有观，离生喜、乐，得初禅成就游。我作是念：『不多闻愚痴凡夫自有病法，不离于病，见他人病，憎恶薄贱、不爱不喜，不自观己。』我复作是念：『我自有病法，不离于病，若我见他病而憎恶薄贱，不爱不喜者，我不宜然，我亦有是法故。』如是观已，因不病起贡高者，即便自灭。我复作是念：『不多闻愚痴凡夫自有老法，不离于老，见他人老，憎恶薄贱，不爱不喜，不自观己。』

"I further thought: 'I have the dharmas of aging myself, and am not free from aging. If I see the aging of others and hate, despise, do not love, and am not pleased, that is not right for me. Because I also have these dharmas.' Having observed this, the pride arising from longevity immediately ceased. The uneducated and ignorant common people are arrogant and negligent due to being free from sickness, and give rise to delusion due to desire, not practicing the holy life. The uneducated and ignorant common people are arrogant and negligent due to being young, and give rise to delusion due to desire, not practicing the holy life. The uneducated and ignorant common people are arrogant and negligent due to longevity, and give rise to delusion due to desire, not practicing the holy life."

「我复作是念：『我自有老法，不离于老，若我见他老而憎恶薄贱、不爱不喜者，我不宜然，我亦有是法故。』如是观已，若因寿起贡高者，即便自灭。不多闻愚痴凡夫为不病贡高豪贵放逸，因欲生痴，不行梵行。不多闻愚痴凡夫为少壮贡高豪贵放逸，因欲生痴，不行梵行。不多闻愚痴凡夫为寿贡高豪贵放逸，因欲生痴，不行梵行。」

Then the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

于是，世尊即说颂曰：

The dharmas of sickness, aging, and death are naturally present
The ignorant see them as evil. If I hate them
and am not liberated from these dharmas, that is not
right for me. Because I also have these dharmas. They
practice in this way, knowing the dharmas and being free
from birth. Being free from sickness, youth, and longevity, they
are arrogant. Cutting off all arrogance, they see the peace
of desirelessness. They awaken in this way, without fear of
desire, attaining the state of no-thought, practicing the pure holy life.

「病法老法，　　及死亡法，如法自有，　　凡夫见恶。若我憎恶，　　不度此法，我不宜然，　　亦有是法。彼如是行，　　知法离生，无病少壮，　　为寿贡高。断诸贡高，　　见无欲安，彼如是觉，　　无怖于欲，得无有想，　　行净梵行。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and upheld it.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
