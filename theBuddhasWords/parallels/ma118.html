MĀ 118

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 11 大品

118. （一一八）龙象经

Parallel to: AN 6.43 / Thag 15

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Shravasti, staying at the Deer Park Hall of the Mother of the Deer.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在东园鹿子母堂。

At that time, the World-Honored One rose from his seat at dusk, came down from the hall, and said, "Udayi! Let us go together to the eastern river to bathe."

尔时，世尊则于晡时从宴坐起，堂上来下，告曰：「乌陀夷！共汝往至东河澡浴。」

Venerable Udayi replied, "Yes, sir."

尊者乌陀夷白曰：「唯然。」

Then the World-Honored One led Venerable Udayi to the eastern river, took off his clothes on the bank, entered the water to bathe, and after bathing, returned to the shore, wiped his body, and put on his robe.

于是，世尊将尊者乌陀夷往至东河，脱衣岸上，便入水浴，浴已还出，拭体著衣。

At that time, King Prasenajit had an elephant named "Mindful" that played all kinds of music as it crossed the eastern river. When the people saw it, they said, "Is this a dragon among dragons, a great dragon king? Who is it?"

尔时，波斯匿王有龙象，名曰念，作一切妓乐，历度东河。众人见已，便作是说：「是龙中龙，为大龙王，为是谁耶？」

Venerable Udayi joined his palms towards the Buddha and said, "World-Honored One! When people see an elephant with a large body, they say, 'Is this a dragon among dragons, a great dragon king? Who is it?'"

尊者乌陀夷叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！象受大身，众人见已，便作是说：『是龙中龙，为大龙王，为是谁耶？』」

The World-Honored One said, "So it is, Udayi! So it is, Udayi! When people see an elephant with a large body, they say, 'Is this a dragon among dragons, a great dragon king? Who is it?' Udayi! When people see a horse, camel, cow, donkey, quadruped, human, or tree with a large form, they say, 'Is this a dragon among dragons, a great dragon king? Who is it?' Udayi! If there are any in the world, whether gods, demons, Brahmas, ascetics, Brahmins, from humans to gods, who do not harm with body, speech, or mind, I say they are dragons. Udayi! The Tathāgata, in the world, whether gods, demons, Brahmas, ascetics, Brahmins, from humans to gods, does not harm with body, speech, or mind. Therefore I am called a dragon."

世尊告曰：「如是。乌陀夷！如是。乌陀夷！象受大身，众人见已，便作是说：『是龙中龙，为大龙王，为是谁耶？』乌陀夷！马、骆驼、牛、驴、胸行、人、树，生大形，乌陀夷！众人见已，便作是说：『是龙中龙，为大龙王，为是谁耶？』乌陀夷！若有世间，天及魔、梵、沙门、梵志，从人至天，不以身、口、意害者，我说彼是龙。乌陀夷！如来于世间，天及魔、梵、沙门、梵志，从人至天，不以身、口、意害，是故我名龙。」

Then Venerable Udayi joined his palms towards the Buddha and said, "World-Honored One! May the World-Honored One grant me his power, may the Well-Gone One grant me his power, so that I may praise the World-Honored One with verses on dragons in front of the Buddha."

于是，尊者乌陀夷叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！唯愿世尊加我威力，善逝加我威力，令我在佛前，以龙相应颂颂赞世尊。」

The World-Honored One said, "As you wish."

世尊告曰：「随汝所欲。」

Then Venerable Udayi praised the World-Honored One with verses on dragons in front of the Buddha:

于是，尊者乌陀夷在于佛前，以龙相应颂赞世尊曰：

The Perfectly Enlightened One is born among humans, self-controlled, attaining right
concentration, practicing the traces of the Brahma way, able to delight
in tranquility. Honored by humans, transcending all dharmas, also respected by
the gods, the Unattached, Supreme Human. Transcending all bonds, leaving the
forest and the non-forest, abandoning desire and pleasure, without desire, like
stone giving rise to true gold. Universally heard, the Perfectly Enlightened
One, like the sun rising in the sky, the highest among
all dragons, like the mountains with a peak. Praised as the
Great Dragon, without harming, the Dragon among all dragons, the True
Unsurpassed Dragon. Gentle and without harm, these are the two feet
of the dragon. Asceticism and the Brahma way are called the
practice of the dragon. The Great Dragon's faith is its hand
the two merits are its tusks, mindfulness is its neck, wisdom
is its head, contemplating and discerning the Dharma. Holding all dharmas
in its belly, enjoying solitude with its two arms, abiding in
good tranquility, its inner mind reaching good concentration. The dragon's walking
stopping, and sitting are all concentrated, lying down is also concentrated
The dragon is concentrated at all times, this is called the
constant Dharma of the dragon. Not accepting food from a defiled
household, accepting it from a pure household. Obtaining impure food, abandoning
it like a lion. The offerings received are accepted out of
compassion for others. The dragon eats others' offerings, sustaining life without
attachment. Cutting off the great and small bonds, liberated from all
fetters, wherever it goes, the mind is unattached. Like a white
lotus flower, born and nurtured in water, mud and water cannot
stain it, with wonderful fragrance, loved and admired. In the same
way, the Supremely Enlightened One, born in the world, practices in
the world, not defiled by desire, like a flower not stained
by water. Just as a blazing fire stops when more fuel
is not added, without fuel, the fire does not spread, this
fire is called extinguished. The wise explain this analogy to make
one understand its meaning. This is known by the dragon, spoken
by the Dragon among Dragons. Far from lust and anger, cutting
off delusion and attaining the undefiled, the dragon abandons its body
this dragon is called extinguished.

「正觉生人间，　　自御得正定，修习行梵迹，　　息意能自乐。人之所敬重，　　越超一切法，亦为天所敬，　　无著至真人。越度一切结，　　于林离林去，舍欲乐无欲，　　如石出真金。普闻正尽觉，　　如日升虚空，一切龙中高，　　如众山有岳。称说名大龙，　　而无所伤害，一切龙中龙，　　真谛无上龙。温润无有害，　　此二是龙足，苦行及梵行，　　是谓龙所行。大龙信为手，　　二功德为牙，念项智慧头，　　思惟分别法。受持诸法腹，　　乐远离双臂，住善息出入，　　内心至善定。龙行止俱定，　　坐定卧亦定，龙一切时定，　　是谓龙常法。无秽家受食，　　有秽则不受，得恶不净食，　　舍之如师子。所得供养者，　　为他慈愍受，龙食他信施，　　存命无所著。断除大小结，　　解脱一切缚，随彼所游行，　　心无有系著。犹如白莲花，　　水生水长养，泥水不能著，　　妙香爱乐色。如是最上觉，　　世生行世间，不为欲所染，　　如华水不著。犹如然火炽，　　不益薪则止，无薪火不传，　　此火谓] cf.［丽】之灭。慧者说此喻，　　欲令解其义，是龙之所知，　　龙中龙所说。远离淫欲恚，　　断痴得无漏，龙舍离其身，　　此龙谓之灭。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Venerable Udayi, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。尊者乌陀夷闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
