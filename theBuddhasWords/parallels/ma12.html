MĀ 12

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 2 业相应品

12. To Vappa （一二）惒破经

Parallel to: AN 4.195

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the Śākya country of Kapilavastu, staying in the Nyagrodha Park.

一时，佛游释羇瘦迦维罗卫，在尼拘类园。

At that time, the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana was together with a group of bhikṣus. After the midday meal, for some reason, they gathered and sat in the Dharma hall. At that time, there was a disciple of the Nirgranthas, a Śākya named Āpaṇa, who was strolling around in the middle of the day and came to where the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana was. They exchanged greetings and then sat to one side.

尔时，尊者大目干连与比丘众俱，于中食后有所为故，集坐讲堂。是时，尼干有一弟子，释种，名曰惒破，中后彷徉至尊者大目干连所，共相问讯，却坐一面。

Then the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana asked about this matter: "What do you think, Āpaṇa? If there is a bhikṣu who guards his body, speech, and mind, have you ever seen a place where this gives rise to unwholesome taints that lead to the next world?"

于是，尊者大目干连问如此事：「于惒破意云何？若有比丘身、口、意护，汝颇见是处，因此生不善漏，令至后世耶？」

Āpaṇa replied: "Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If there is a bhikṣu who guards his body, speech, and mind, I see a place where this gives rise to unwholesome taints that lead to the next world. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If one has engaged in unwholesome conduct in a past life, this gives rise to unwholesome taints that lead to the next world."

惒破答曰：「大目干连！若有比丘身、口、意护，我见是处，因此生不善漏令至后世。大目干连！若有前世行不善行，因此生不善漏令至后世。」

Later, the World-Honored One was sitting in quiet meditation. With his pure divine hearing that surpasses that of humans, he heard the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana discussing this matter with the Nirgrantha disciple Āpaṇa. Having heard this, the World-Honored One then rose from his quiet meditation in the afternoon, went to the lecture hall, sat down in front of the assembly of bhikṣus, and asked: "Mahāmaudgalyāyana! What were you discussing with the Nirgrantha disciple Āpaṇa? And what was the reason for gathering in the lecture hall?"

后时，世尊静处宴坐，以净天耳出过于人，闻尊者大目干连与尼干弟子释惒破共论如是。世尊闻已，则于晡时从宴坐起，往诣讲堂比丘众前，敷座而坐。世尊坐已，问曰：「目干连！向与尼干弟子释惒破共论何事？复以何事集坐讲堂？」

The Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana replied: "World-Honored One! Today, after the midday meal, I gathered with the assembly of bhikṣus in the lecture hall for a certain purpose. The Nirgrantha disciple Āpaṇa came wandering to me in the afternoon, exchanged greetings, and sat to one side. I asked him: 'What do you think, Āpaṇa? If there is a bhikṣu who guards his body, speech, and mind, have you ever seen a place where this gives rise to unwholesome taints that lead to the next world?' The Nirgrantha disciple Āpaṇa then replied to me: 'If there is a bhikṣu who guards his body, speech, and mind, I have seen a place where this gives rise to unwholesome taints that lead to the next world. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If one has engaged in unwholesome conduct in a past life, this gives rise to unwholesome taints that lead to the next world.' World-Honored One! This is what I was discussing with the Nirgrantha disciple Āpaṇa, and this is why we gathered in the lecture hall."

尊者大目干连白曰：「世尊！我今日与比丘众俱，于中食后有所为故，集坐讲堂，此尼干弟子释惒破中后彷徉来至我所，共相问讯，却坐一面，我问如是：『于惒破意云何？若有比丘身、口、意护，汝颇见是处，因此生不善漏令至后世耶？』尼干弟子释惒破即答我言：『若有比丘身、口、意护，我见是处，因此生不善漏令至后世。大目干连！若有前世行不善行，因此生不善漏令至后世。』世尊！向与尼干弟子释惒破共论如是，以此事故，集坐讲堂。」

Then the World-Honored One said to the Nirgrantha disciple Āpaṇa: "If what I say is so, you should say it is so. If it is not so, you should say it is not so. If you have any doubts, you may ask me: 'śramaṇa Gautama! What is the meaning of this?' According to what I say, if you can accept it, I can discuss this matter with you."

于是，世尊语尼干弟子释惒破曰：「若我所说是者，汝当言是，若不是者，当言不是，汝有所疑，便可问我：『沙门瞿昙！此有何事，此有何义？』随我所说，汝若能受者，我可与汝共论此事。」

Āpaṇa replied: "śramaṇa Gautama! If what you say is so, I will say it is so. If it is not so, I will say it is not so. If I have any doubts, I will ask Gautama: 'Gautama! What is the meaning of this?' According to what the śramaṇa Gautama says, I will accept it. The śramaṇa Gautama should only discuss this matter with me."

惒破答曰：「沙门瞿昙！若所说是，我当言是，若不是者，当言不是，我若有疑，当问瞿昙：『瞿昙！此有何事，此有何义？』随沙门瞿昙所说，我则受持，沙门瞿昙但当与我共论此事。」

The World-Honored One asked: "What do you think, Āpaṇa? If a bhikṣu gives rise to unwholesome bodily actions, leaks, afflictions, and sorrow, then later on, those unwholesome bodily actions cease, he does not create new karma, and abandons past karma. In this very life, he attains final cessation, without afflictions, abiding eternally and unchanging, as seen and known by the wisdom of the noble ones. If he gives rise to unwholesome bodily actions, unwholesome verbal actions, unwholesome mental actions, unwholesome actions of ignorance, leaks, afflictions, and sorrow, then later on, those unwholesome actions of ignorance cease, he does not create new karma, and abandons past karma. In this very life, he attains final cessation, without afflictions, abiding eternally and unchanging, as seen and known by the wisdom of the noble ones. What do you think, Āpaṇa? For such a bhikṣu who guards body, speech, and mind, have you ever seen a place where unwholesome leaks arise from this, leading to the next life?"

世尊问曰：「于惒破意云何？若有比丘生不善身行、漏、烦热、忧戚，彼于后时不善身行灭，不更造新业，弃舍故业，即于现世便得究竟而无烦热，常住不变，谓圣慧所见、圣慧所知也。身生不善、口行不善、意行不善无明行、漏、烦热、忧戚，彼于后时不善无明行灭，不更造新业，弃舍故业，即于现世便得究竟而无烦热，常住不变，谓圣慧所见、圣慧所知。云何，惒破！如是比丘身、口、意护，汝颇见是处，因此生不善漏令至后世耶？」

Āpaṇa replied: "Gautama! If a bhikṣu guards body, speech, and mind in this way, I do not see a place where unwholesome leaks arise from this, leading to the next life."

惒破答曰：「瞿昙！若有比丘如是身、口、意护，我不见是处，因此生不善漏令至后世。」

The World-Honored One praised: "Excellent! Āpaṇa! What do you think, Āpaṇa? If a bhikṣu has exhausted ignorance and given rise to wisdom, then with the exhaustion of ignorance and the arising of wisdom, he knows the arising of subsequent body-consciousness and subsequent life-consciousness. When the body perishes and life ends, the lifespan is exhausted, and all subsequent consciousnesses cease and come to an end in this very life. It is like, Āpaṇa, a shadow arises from a tree. If someone were to come with a sharp axe and cut down the tree at the root, chopping it into pieces, dividing it into ten or a hundred parts, burning it to ashes, or blowing it away with a strong wind, or throwing it into the water. What do you think, Āpaṇa? The shadow arises from the tree. From then on, the cause for the shadow is cut off, and it ceases and does not arise again,

世尊叹曰：「善哉！惒破！云何，惒破！若有比丘无明已尽，明已生，彼无明已尽，明已生，生后身觉便知生后身觉，生后命觉便知生后命觉，身坏命终，寿已毕讫，即于现世一切所觉便尽止息，当知至竟冷。犹如惒破，因树有影，若使有人持利斧来斫彼树根，段段斩截，破为十分，或为百分，火烧成灰，或大风吹，或著水中。于惒破意云何？影因树有，彼影从是已绝其因，灭不生耶？」

Āpaṇa replied: "Yes, Gautama!"

惒破答曰：「如是。瞿昙！」

"Āpaṇa! Know that the bhikṣu is also like this. With the exhaustion of ignorance and the arising of wisdom, he knows the arising of subsequent body-consciousness and subsequent life-consciousness. When the body perishes and life ends, the lifespan is exhausted, and all subsequent consciousnesses cease and come to an end in this very life. It is like, Āpaṇa, a bhikṣu is also like this. With right mind liberation, he attains six good abodes. What are the six? Āpaṇa! A bhikṣu sees forms with the eyes, neither joyful nor sorrowful, abandoning seeking and attaining the unconditioned, with right mindfulness and right knowledge. Āpaṇa! A bhikṣu with right mind liberation attains the first good abode. Similarly, with the ears, nose, tongue, body, and mind knowing dharmas, neither joyful nor sorrowful, abandoning seeking and attaining the unconditioned, with right mindfulness and right knowledge. Āpaṇa! A bhikṣu with right mind liberation attains the sixth good abode. Āpaṇa! A bhikṣu with right mind liberation attains these six good abodes."

「惒破！当知比丘亦复如是，无明已尽，明已生，彼无明已尽，明已生，生后身觉便知生后身觉，生后命觉便知生后命觉，身坏命终，寿已毕讫，即于现世一切所觉便尽止息，当知至竟冷。惒破！比丘如是正心解脱，便得六善住处，云何为六？惒破！比丘眼见色，不喜不忧，舍求无为，正念正智，惒破！比丘如是正心解脱，是谓得第一善住处。如是，耳、鼻、舌、身、意知法，不喜不忧，舍求无为，正念正智，惒破！比丘如是正心解脱，是谓得第六善住处，惒破！比丘如是正心解脱，得此六善住处。」

Āpaṇa said: "Yes, Gautama! The well-learned noble disciple with right mind liberation attains six good abodes. What are the six? Gautama! The well-learned noble disciple sees forms with the eyes, neither joyful nor sorrowful, abandoning seeking and attaining the unconditioned, with right mindfulness and right knowledge. Gautama! The well-learned noble disciple with right mind liberation attains the first good abode. Similarly, with the ears, nose, tongue, body, and mind knowing dharmas, neither joyful nor sorrowful, abandoning seeking and attaining the unconditioned, with right mindfulness and right knowledge. Thus, Gautama! The well-learned noble disciple with right mind liberation attains the sixth good abode. Thus, Gautama! The well-learned noble disciple with right mind liberation attains these six good abodes."

惒破白曰：「如是，瞿昙！多闻圣弟子如是正心解脱，得六善住处。云何为六？瞿昙！多闻圣弟子眼见色，不喜不忧，舍求无为，正念正智，瞿昙！多闻圣弟子如是正心解脱，是谓得第一善住处。如是，耳、鼻、舌、身、意知法，不喜不忧，舍求无为，正念正智。如是，瞿昙！多闻圣弟子如是正心解脱，是谓得第六善住处。如是，瞿昙！多闻圣弟子如是正心解脱，得此六善住处。」

Then Āpaṇa said to the World-Honored One: "Gautama! I have understood, Well-Gone One! I have understood. Gautama is like a person with clear eyes who turns the upside down right-side up, pulls out the stuck, shows the way to the lost, and provides light in the dark, so that those with eyes can see forms. The Śramaṇa Gautama is also like this. He has taught me the Dharma with immeasurable skillful means, revealing the meaning according to the various paths. World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept me as a lay follower. From this day forth, until the end of my life, I take refuge with all my heart, until the end of my life.

于是，惒破白世尊曰：「瞿昙！我已知，善逝，我已解。瞿昙犹明目人，覆者仰之，覆者发之，迷者示道，暗中施明，若有眼者便见于色；沙门瞿昙亦复如是，为我无量方便说法现义，随其诸道。世尊！我今自归于佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。

World-Honored One! It is like a person raising a bad horse, hoping for profit, only tiring themselves without gaining profit. World-Honored One! I am also like this. That foolish Nirgrantha does not understand well, cannot comprehend, does not recognize the good field, and does not examine himself. For a long time, I have respectfully made offerings and paid homage to him, hoping for profit, but it is in vain and without benefit. World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha for a second time. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept me as a lay follower. From this day forth, until the end of my life, I take refuge with all my heart, until the end of my life.

「世尊！犹如有人养不良马，望得其利，徒自疲劳而不获利。世尊！我亦如是，彼愚痴尼干不善晓了，不能解知，不识良田而不自审，长夜奉敬供养礼事，望得其利，唐苦无益。世尊！我今再自归佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。

World-Honored One! I was originally ignorant and had faith and respect for the foolish Nirgrantha. From today, I will cut it off. Why? Because he deceived me. World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha for a third time. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept me as a lay follower. From this day forth, until the end of my life, I take refuge with all my heart, until the end of my life.

「世尊！我本无知，于愚痴尼干有信有敬，从今日断。所以者何？欺诳我故。世尊！我今三自归佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Śāriputra and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说如是。释惒破及诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
