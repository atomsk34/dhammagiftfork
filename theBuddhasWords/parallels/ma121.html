MĀ 121

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 11 大品

121. （一二一）请请经

Parallel to: SN 8.7 / SĀ 1212 / SĀ² 2.228 / EĀ 32.5

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was dwelling in the Bamboo Grove Kalanda-nivāpa near Rājagṛha, together with a great assembly of five hundred bhikṣus, all of whom were observing the summer retreat.

一时，佛游王舍城，在竹林加兰哆园，与大比丘众五百人俱，共受夏坐。

At that time, on the fifteenth day of the month, when the Buddha was about to give the Dharma talk on the characteristics of liberation, he sat on a prepared seat in front of the assembly of bhikṣus and said to them, "I am a brāhman, who has attained the end of cessation, the unsurpassed physician-king. This is my last body. I am a brāhman, who has attained the end of cessation, the unsurpassed physician-king. This is my last body. You are my true sons, born from my mouth, transformed by the Dharma. You are my true sons, born from my mouth, transformed by the Dharma. You should teach and transform each other.

尔时，世尊月十五日，说从解脱相请请时，在比丘众前敷座而坐，告诸比丘：「我是梵志，而得灭讫，无上医王，我今受身，最是后边。我是梵志，得灭讫后，无上医王，我今受身，最是后边。谓汝等辈是我真子，从口而生，法法所化。谓汝等辈是我真子，从口而生，法法所化，汝当教化，转相教诃。」

At that time, Venerable Sāriputta was also in the assembly. Then Venerable Sāriputta rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "World-Honored One! What you have just said: 'I am a brāhman, who has attained the end of cessation, the unsurpassed physician-king. This is my last body. I am a brāhman, who has attained the end of cessation, the unsurpassed physician-king. This is my last body. You are my true sons, born from my mouth, transformed by the Dharma. You are my true sons, born from my mouth, transformed by the Dharma. You should teach and transform each other.' World-Honored One! For those who are not disciplined, you discipline them; for those who are not at peace, you bring them peace; for those who are not liberated, you liberate them; for those who are not liberated from bondage, you liberate them; for those who have not attained the end of cessation, you bring them to the end of cessation; for those who have not attained the path, you bring them to the path; for those who have not established the practice of purity, you establish the practice of purity. You know the path, awaken to the path, recognize the path, and teach the path. The World-Honored One's disciples, having attained the Dharma, accept instruction, accept rebuke, accept instruction and rebuke, and then follow the World-Honored One's words and immediately practice accordingly, attaining their wishes and well understanding the true Dharma. World-Honored One! Do you not dislike my bodily, verbal, and mental actions?"

尔时，尊者舍梨子亦在众中。于是，尊者舍梨子即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！向之所说：『我是梵志，而得灭讫，无上医王，我今受身，最是后边。我是梵志，得灭讫后，无上医王，我今受身，最是后边。谓汝等辈是我真子，从口而生，法法所化。谓汝等辈是我真子，从口而生，法法所化，汝当教化，转相教诃。』世尊！诸不调者令得调御，诸不息者令得止息，诸不度者而令得度，诸不解脱者令得解脱，诸不灭讫者令得灭讫，未得道者令其得道，不施设梵行令施设梵行。知道、觉道、识道、说道，世尊弟子于后得法，受教、受诃、受教、诃已，随世尊语，即便趣行，得如其意，善知正法。唯然，世尊不嫌我身、口、意行耶？」

At that time, the World-Honored One said, "Sāriputta! I do not dislike your bodily, verbal, and mental actions. Why is that? Sāriputta! You have sharp wisdom, great wisdom, swift wisdom, quick wisdom, sharp wisdom, broad wisdom, deep wisdom, wisdom of transcendence, and penetrating wisdom. Sāriputta! You have accomplished true wisdom. Sāriputta! It is like a wheel-turning king who has a crown prince who does not go against the teachings and then receives the succession of the father king and is able to pass it on. In the same way, Sāriputta! I turn the wheel of the Dharma, and you are able to turn it again. Sāriputta! Therefore, I do not dislike your bodily, verbal, and mental actions."

彼时，世尊告曰：「舍梨子！我不嫌汝身、口、意行。所以者何？舍梨子！汝有聪慧、大慧、速慧、捷慧、利慧、广慧、深慧、出要慧、明达慧。舍梨子！汝成就实慧。舍梨子！犹转轮王而有太子，不越教已，则便受拜父王所传，而能复传。如是，舍梨子！我所转法轮，汝复能转。舍梨子！是故我不嫌汝身、口、意行。」

Venerable Sāriputta again joined his palms towards the Buddha and said, "World-Honored One! Do you not dislike my bodily, verbal, and mental actions? World-Honored One! Do you not dislike the bodily, verbal, and mental actions of these five hundred bhikṣus?"

尊者舍梨子复再叉手向佛，白曰：「唯然。世尊不嫌我身、口、意行；世尊不嫌此五百比丘身、口、意行耶？」

The World-Honored One said, "Sāriputta! I also do not dislike the bodily, verbal, and mental actions of these five hundred bhikṣus. Why is that? Sāriputta! These five hundred bhikṣus have all attained non-attachment, their defilements are exhausted, their holy life are established, their tasks are accomplished, they have laid down their burdens, their bonds are exhausted, and they have attained the true meaning and right wisdom and liberation. Except for one bhikṣu, whom I have also previously predicted to attain ultimate wisdom in this present life, to have exhausted birth, to have established holy life, to have accomplished what was to be done, to no longer take rebirth, and to know it as it truly is. Sāriputta! Therefore, I do not dislike the bodily, verbal, and mental actions of these five hundred bhikṣus."

世尊告曰：「舍梨子！我亦不嫌此五百比丘身、口、意行。所以者何？舍梨子！此五百比丘，尽得无著，诸漏已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，重担已舍，有结已尽，而得善义正智正解脱。唯除一比丘，我亦本已记于现法中得究竟智，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。舍梨子！是故我不嫌此五百比丘身、口、意行。」

Venerable Sāriputta again joined his palms towards the Buddha and said, "World-Honored One! You do not dislike my bodily, verbal, and mental actions, nor do you dislike the bodily, verbal, and mental actions of these five hundred bhikṣus. World-Honored One! Among these five hundred bhikṣus, how many bhikṣus have attained the three kinds of clear knowledge and insight? How many bhikṣus have attained liberation through both means? How many bhikṣus have attained liberation through wisdom?"

尊者舍梨子复三叉手向佛，白曰：「唯然。世尊不嫌我身、口、意行；亦不嫌此五百比丘身、口、意行。世尊！此五百比丘，几比丘得三明达？几比丘得俱解脱？几比丘得慧解脱耶？」

The World-Honored One said, "Sāriputta! Among these five hundred bhikṣus, ninety bhikṣus have attained the three kinds of clear knowledge and insight, ninety bhikṣus have attained liberation through both means, and the remaining bhikṣus have attained liberation through wisdom. Sāriputta! This assembly is without branches or leaves, without knots or bends, pure and true, and has attained correct abiding."

世尊告曰：「舍梨子！此五百比丘，九十比丘得三明达，九十比丘得俱解脱，余比丘得慧解脱。舍梨子！此众无枝无叶，亦无节戾，清净真实，得正住立。」

At that time, Venerable Ānanda was also in the assembly. Then Venerable Ānanda rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "Yes, World-Honored One! Please bestow your spiritual power upon me. May the Well-Gone One bestow his spiritual power upon me, enabling me to praise in accordance with the meaning in front of the Buddha and the assembly of bhikṣus."

尔时，尊者傍耆舍亦在众中。于是，尊者傍耆舍即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「唯然。世尊加我威力，唯愿善逝加我威力，令我在佛及比丘众前，以如义相应而作赞颂。」

The World-Honored One said, "Ānanda! Do as you wish."

世尊告曰：「傍耆舍！随汝所欲。」

Then Venerable Ānanda praised in accordance with the meaning in front of the Buddha and the assembly of bhikṣus,

于是，尊者傍耆舍在佛及比丘众前，以如义相应而赞颂曰：

Today, on the fifteenth day of the invitation, the assembly of
five hundred is gathered, cutting off all bonds and fetters, the
unimpeded one with the end of existence. Pure and radiant illumination
liberated from all existence, birth, old age, sickness, and death are
exhausted, the taints are extinguished, and what is to be done
is accomplished. The bonds of remorse and doubt, the taints of
pride are exhausted, the thorn of the bond of craving is
uprooted, the supreme physician has no more. Courageous like a lion
all fears are removed, having already crossed over birth and death
all taints are extinguished. Like a wheel-turning king, surrounded by ministers
ruling over all lands, even to the great ocean, so too
is the courageous conqueror, the supreme merchant leader, disciples joyfully pay
respect, the three knowledges are free from the fear of death
All are the Buddha's children, forever removing branches, leaves, and joints
turning the supreme Dharma wheel, paying homage to the foremost honored one.

「今十五请日，　　集坐五百众，断除诸结缚，　　无碍有尽仙。清净光明照，　　解脱一切有，生老病死尽，　　漏灭所作办。调悔及疑结，　　慢有漏已尽，拔断爱结刺，　　上医无复有。勇猛如师子，　　一切恐畏除，已度于生死，　　诸漏已灭讫。犹如转轮王，　　群臣所围绕，悉领一切地，　　乃至于大海。如是勇猛伏，　　无上商人主，弟子乐恭敬，　　三达离死怖。一切是佛子，　　永除枝叶节，转无上法轮，　　稽首第一尊。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
