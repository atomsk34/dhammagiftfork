MĀ 122

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 11 大品

122. （一二二）瞻波经

Parallel to: AN 8.10

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in Campā, by the side of the Ganges River.

一时，佛游瞻波，在恒伽池边。

At that time, on the fifteenth day of the month, when the Buddha was about to give the Dharma talk on the liberation of the mind, he sat on the prepared seat in front of the assembly of monks. After the Buddha had sat down, he immediately entered concentration and observed the minds of the assembly with his mind-reading ability. Having observed the minds of the assembly, he remained silent until the first watch of the night. Then, a monk rose from his seat, put on his robe, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "World-Honored One! The first watch of the night has already passed. The Buddha and the assembly of monks have been sitting here for a long time. I only wish that the World-Honored One would give the Dharma talk on the liberation of the mind." At that time, the World-Honored One remained silent and did not reply. Then, the World-Honored One continued to sit in silence until the middle watch of the night.

尔时，世尊月十五日说从解脱时，于比丘众前敷座而坐。世尊坐已，即便入定，以他心智观察众心，观众心已，至初夜竟，默然而坐。于是，有一比丘即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！初夜已讫，佛及比丘众集坐来久，唯愿世尊说从解脱。」尔时，世尊默然不答。于是，世尊复至中夜默然而坐。

That monk rose from his seat again, put on his robe, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "World-Honored One! The first watch of the night has already passed, and the middle watch is about to end. The Buddha and the assembly of monks have been sitting here for a long time. I only wish that the World-Honored One would give the Dharma talk on the liberation of the mind." The World-Honored One remained silent and did not reply again. Then, the World-Honored One continued to sit in silence until the last watch of the night.

彼一比丘再从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！初夜已过，中夜将讫，佛及比丘众集坐来久，唯愿世尊说从解脱。」世尊亦再默然不答。于是，世尊复至后夜默然而坐。

That monk rose from his seat again, put on his robe, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "World-Honored One! The first watch of the night has already passed, and the middle watch is also over. The last watch of the night is about to end, and dawn is approaching. It will not be long before dawn breaks. The Buddha and the assembly of monks have been sitting here for a long time. I only wish that the World-Honored One would give the Dharma talk on the liberation of the mind."

彼一比丘三从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！初夜既过，中夜复讫，后夜垂尽，将向欲明，明出不久，佛及比丘众集坐极久，唯愿世尊说从解脱。」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to that monk, "In this assembly, there is a monk who is not pure."

尔时，世尊告彼比丘：「于此众中有一比丘已为不净。」

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana was also in the assembly. Then Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana thought, "Why did the World-Honored One say that there is a monk in this assembly who is not pure? I should enter the samādhi of suchness and observe the minds of the assembly with the wisdom of knowing the minds of others through the samādhi of suchness."

彼时，尊者大目揵连亦在众中。于是，尊者大目揵连便作是念：「世尊为何比丘而说此众中有一比丘已为不净？我宁可入如其像定，以如其像定他心之智观察众心。」

Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana then entered the samādhi of suchness and observed the minds of the assembly with the wisdom of knowing the minds of others through the samādhi of suchness. Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana then knew for whom the World-Honored One had said that there was a monk in this assembly who was not pure. Then Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana rose from his samādhi, went before that monk, took him by the arm, led him out, opened the door, and placed him outside, saying, "Fool, go away! Do not stay here! You are no longer allowed to join the assembly of monks. From now on, you are not a monk." He closed the door, locked it, returned to the Buddha, bowed at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said, "World-Honored One, I have already expelled the monk for whom the World-Honored One said that there was a monk in this assembly who was not pure. World-Honored One, the first part of the night has passed, the middle part of the night is over, and the last part of the night is almost over. It is about to dawn, and it will not be long before dawn. The Buddha and the assembly of monks have been sitting here for a long time. I only wish that the World-Honored One would teach the liberation."

尊者大目揵连即入如其像定，以如其像定他心之智观察众心，尊者大目揵连便知世尊所为比丘说此众中有一比丘已为不净。于是，尊者大目揵连即从定起，至彼比丘前，牵臂将出，开门置外：「痴人远去，莫于此住，不复得与比丘众会，从今已去，非是比丘。」闭门下钥，还诣佛所，稽首佛足，却坐一面，白曰：「世尊所为比丘说此众中有一比丘已为不净者，我已逐出。世尊！初夜既过，中夜复讫，后夜垂尽，将向欲明，明出不久，佛及比丘众集坐极久，唯愿世尊说从解脱。」

The World-Honored One said, "Mahāmaudgalyāyana! That foolish person will incur great blame for disturbing the World-Honored One and the assembly of monks. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If the Tathāgata were to teach liberation in an impure assembly, that person's head would burst into seven pieces. Therefore, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! From now on, you should teach liberation. The Tathāgata will no longer teach liberation. Why is that? Mahāmaudgalyāyana! There may be a foolish person who knows right and wrong, observes well, bends and stretches, bows and looks up, with proper deportment, wears the saṃghāṭī robe and other robes, and carries the alms bowl, walking, standing, sitting, lying down, sleeping, waking, speaking, and being silent, all with right knowledge, appearing to be a true practitioner of the holy life, going to the places of true practitioners of the holy life, but they may not be recognized. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If the practitioners of the holy life recognize them, they will think, 'This monk is defiled, this monk is disgraced, this monk is hated, this monk is a thorn.' Having recognized them, they will then expel them. Why is that? So as not to pollute the practitioners of the holy life.

世尊告曰：「大目揵连！彼愚痴人当得大罪，触娆世尊及比丘众。大目揵连！若使如来在不净众说从解脱者，彼人则便头破七分。是故，大目揵连！汝等从今已后说从解脱，如来不复说从解脱。所以者何？如是，大目揵连！或有痴人正知出入，善观分别，屈申低仰，仪容庠序，善著僧伽梨及诸衣钵，行住坐卧、眠寤语默，皆正知之，似如真梵行，至诸真梵行所，彼或不知。大目揵连！若诸梵行知者，便作是念：『是沙门污，是沙门辱，是沙门憎，是沙门刺。』知已，便当共摈弃之。所以者何？莫令污染诸梵行者。

Mahāmaudgalyāyana! It is like a householder with a good rice field or wheat field, where there grows a weed called 'impure wheat'. Its roots are similar, and its stems, joints, leaves, and flowers are all also similar to wheat. Later, when the grains are ripe, the householder sees them and thinks, 'This wheat is defiled, this wheat is disgraced, this wheat is hated, this wheat is a thorn.' Having recognized them, he then pulls them out and throws them away. Why is that? So as not to pollute the good wheat. In this way, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! There may be a foolish person who knows right and wrong, observes well, bends and stretches, bows and looks up, with proper deportment, wears the saṃghāṭī robe and other robes, and carries the alms bowl, walking, standing, sitting, lying down, sleeping, waking, speaking, and being silent, all with right knowledge, appearing to be a true practitioner of the holy life, going to the places of true practitioners of the holy life, but they may not be recognized. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If the practitioners of the holy life recognize them, they will think, 'This monk is defiled, this monk is disgraced, this monk is hated, this monk is a thorn.' Having recognized them, they will then expel them. Why is that? So as not to pollute the practitioners of the holy life.

「大目揵连！犹如居士有良稻田，或有麦田，生草名秽麦，其根相似，茎、节、叶、花皆亦似麦，后生实已，居士见之，便作是念：『是麦污辱，是麦憎刺。』知已便拔，掷弃于外。所以者何？莫令污秽余真好麦。如是，大目揵连！或有痴人正知出入，善观分别，屈伸低仰，仪容庠序，善著僧伽梨及诸衣钵，行住坐卧、眠寤语默，皆正知之，似如真梵行，至诸真梵行所，彼或不知。大目揵连！若诸梵行知者，便作是念：『是沙门污，是沙门辱，是沙门憎，是沙门刺。』知已，便当共摈弃之。所以者何？莫令污染诸梵行者。

Mahāmaudgalyāyana! It is like a householder in autumn winnowing grain. If there are ripe grains in the grain pile, the winnowing stops. If they are unripe or chaff, they are blown away by the wind. The householder sees them and then takes a broom to sweep and clean them. Why is that? So as not to pollute the other good, pure rice. In this way, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! There may be a foolish person who knows right and wrong, observes well, bends and stretches, bows and looks up, with proper deportment, wears the saṃghāṭī robe and other robes, and carries the alms bowl, walking, standing, sitting, lying down, sleeping, waking, speaking, and being silent, all with right knowledge, appearing to be a true practitioner of the holy life, going to the places of true practitioners of the holy life, but they may not be recognized. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If the practitioners of the holy life recognize them, they will think, 'This monk is defiled, this monk is disgraced, this monk is hated, this monk is a thorn.' Having recognized them, they will then expel them. Why is that? So as not to pollute the practitioners of the holy life.

「大目揵连！犹如居士秋时扬谷，谷聚之中若有成实者，扬便止住，若不成实及粃糠者，便随风去，居士见已，即持扫帚，扫治令净。所以者何？莫令污杂余净好稻。如是，大目揵连！或有痴人正知出入，善观分别，屈伸低仰，仪容庠序，善著僧伽梨及诸衣钵，行住坐卧、眠寤语默，皆正知之，似如真梵行，至诸真梵行所，彼或不知。大目揵连！若诸梵行知者，便作是念：『是沙门污，是沙门辱，是沙门憎，是沙门刺。』知已，便当共摈弃之。所以者何？莫令污染诸梵行者。

Mahāmaudgalyāyana! It is like a householder who, in order to cross a stream, builds a bridge. Taking an axe, he enters the forest and knocks on the trees. If they are solid, the sound is small. If they are hollow, the sound is loud. The householder, having recognized this, then cuts off the knots and intends to make a bridge. In this way, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! There may be a foolish person who knows right and wrong, observes well, bends and stretches, bows and looks up, with proper deportment, wears the saṃghāṭī robe and other robes, and carries the alms bowl, walking, standing, sitting, lying down, sleeping, waking, speaking, and being silent, all with right knowledge, appearing to be a true practitioner of the holy life, going to the places of true practitioners of the holy life, but they may not be recognized. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If the practitioners of the holy life recognize them, they will think, 'This monk is defiled, this monk is disgraced, this monk is hated, this monk is a thorn.' Having recognized them, they will then expel them. Why is that? So as not to pollute the practitioners of the holy life.

「大目揵连！犹如居士为过泉水故，作通水槽，持斧入林，扣打诸树，若坚实者，其声便小，若空中者，其声便大，居士知已，便斫治节，拟作通水槽。如是，大目揵连！或有痴人正知出入，善观分别，屈伸低仰，仪容庠序，善著僧伽梨及诸衣钵，行住坐卧、眠寤语默，皆正知之，似如真梵行，至诸真梵行所，彼或不知。大目揵连！若诸梵行知者，便作是念：『是沙门污，是沙门辱，是沙门憎，是沙门刺。』知已，便当共摈弃之。所以者何？莫令污染诸梵行者。」

Then the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

于是，世尊说此颂曰：

Those who gather together should know: evil desires, hatred, anger, not speaking
harboring grudges, stinginess, jealousy, flattery, and deceit. In the assembly, they pretend
to be peaceful, but in private they claim to be śramaṇas. Secretly
they engage in evil deeds and have wrong views, not guarding themselves
Deceitfully speaking false words, such people should be known. They should not
be allowed to gather together, but be expelled and not allowed to
stay. Those who deceive and speak falsely many times, not being peaceful
but claiming to be peaceful, should be expelled and distanced from those
who know the time and practice purity. The pure should always associate
with the pure. By associating, one attains peace, and thus attains the
end of suffering.

「共会集当知，　　恶欲憎嫉恚，不语结恨悭，　　嫉妬谄欺诳。在众诈言息，　　屏处称沙门，阴作诸恶行，　　恶见不守护。欺诳妄语言，　　如是当知彼，往集不与会，　　摈弃不共止。欺诈诳说多，　　非息称说息，知时具净行，　　摈弃远离彼。清净共清净，　　常当共和合，和合得安隐，　　如是得苦边。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
