MĀ 124

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 11 大品

124. （一二四）八难经

Parallel to: AN 8.29 / EĀ 42.1

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus: "There are eight difficulties and eight inopportune times for practicing the holy life. What are the eight? If at that time the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, of right and universal knowledge, the Sugata, the knower of the world, the unsurpassed guide who tames beings, the teacher of gods and humans, the Buddha, the Blessed One, appears in the world and teaches the Dharma, leading to cessation, leading to extinction, leading to the path of awakening, and expounded by the Sugata, but that person is born in hell at that time. This is called the first difficulty and the first inopportune time for practicing the holy life.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「人行梵行而有八难、八非时也。云何为八？若时如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐，出世说法，趣向止息，趣向灭讫，趣向觉道，为善逝所演。彼人尔时生地狱中。是谓人行梵行第一难、第一非时。

Furthermore, if at that time the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, of right and universal knowledge, the Sugata, the knower of the world, the unsurpassed guide who tames beings, the teacher of gods and humans, the Buddha, the Blessed One, appears in the world and teaches the Dharma, leading to cessation, leading to extinction, leading to the path of awakening, and expounded by the Sugata, but that person is born among animals at that time, born among hungry ghosts, born among long-lived gods, born in border countries among barbarians who lack faith, gratitude, and reciprocity, and where there are no bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, or upāsikās. This is called the fifth difficulty and the fifth inopportune time for practicing the holy life.

「复次，若时如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐，出世说法，趣向止息，趣向灭讫，趣向觉道，为善逝所演。彼人尔时生畜生中，生饿鬼中，生长寿天中，生在边国夷狄之中，无信无恩，无有反复，若无比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆夷。是谓人行梵行第五难、第五非时。

Furthermore, if at that time the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, of right and universal knowledge, the Sugata, the knower of the world, the unsurpassed guide who tames beings, the teacher of gods and humans, the Buddha, the Blessed One, appears in the world and teaches the Dharma, leading to cessation, leading to extinction, leading to the path of awakening, and expounded by the Sugata, but that person is born in a central country at that time, but is deaf and mute like a sheep, always communicating with gestures, unable to understand or speak the meaning of good and evil. This is called the sixth difficulty and the sixth inopportune time for practicing the holy life.

「复次，若时如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐，出世说法，趣向止息，趣向灭讫，趣向觉道，为善逝所演。彼人尔时虽生中国，而聋痖如羊鸣，常以手语，不能知说善恶之义。是谓人行梵行第六难、第六非时。

Furthermore, if at that time the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, of right and universal knowledge, the Sugata, the knower of the world, the unsurpassed guide who tames beings, the teacher of gods and humans, the Buddha, the Blessed One, appears in the world and teaches the Dharma, leading to cessation, leading to extinction, leading to the path of awakening, and expounded by the Sugata, but that person is born in a central country at that time, not deaf, not mute, not like a sheep, not communicating with gestures, and able to understand and speak the meaning of good and evil, but has wrong views and inverted views, and holds and proclaims the view: 'There is no giving, no observance, no incantations, no good or bad deeds, no results of good or bad deeds, no this world or the next, no father or mother, no true person in the world who goes to a good place, goes well, goes in a good direction, who in this world or the next knows and awakens by themselves, and attains liberation by themselves.' This is called the seventh difficulty and the seventh inopportune time for practicing the holy life.

「复次，若时如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐，出世说法，趣向止息，趣向灭讫，趣向觉道，为善逝所演。彼人尔时虽生中国，不聋、不痖、不如羊鸣，不以手语，又能知说善恶之义，然有邪见及颠倒见，如是见、如是说：『无施、无斋，无有呪说，无善恶业，无善恶业报，无此世彼世，无父无母，世无真人往至善处、善去、善向，此世彼世自知自觉，自作证成就游。』是谓人行梵行第七难、第七非时。

Furthermore, if at that time the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, of right and universal knowledge, the Sugata, the knower of the world, the unsurpassed guide who tames beings, the teacher of gods and humans, the Buddha, the Blessed One, does not appear in the world and does not teach the Dharma, leading to cessation, leading to extinction, leading to the path of awakening, and expounded by the Sugata, but that person is born in a central country at that time, not deaf, not mute, not like a sheep, not communicating with gestures, and able to understand and speak the meaning of good and evil, and has right views, not inverted views, and holds and proclaims the view: 'There is giving, there is observance, there are incantations, there are good and bad deeds, there are results of good and bad deeds, there is this world and the next, there is father and mother, there are true persons in the world who go to a good place, go well, go in a good direction, who in this world or the next know and awaken by themselves, and attain liberation by themselves.' This is called the eighth difficulty and the eighth inopportune time for practicing the holy life.

「复次，若时如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐，不出于世，亦不说法，趣向止息，趣向灭讫，趣向觉道，为善逝所演。彼人尔时生于中国，不聋、不痖、不如羊鸣，不以手语，又能知说善恶之义，而有正见、不颠倒见，如是见、如是说：『有施、有斋，亦有呪说，有善恶业，有善恶业报，有此世彼世，有父有母，世有真人往至善处、善去、善向，此世彼世自知自觉，自作证成就游。』是谓人行梵行第八难、第八非时。

There is one difficulty and one opportune time for practicing the holy life. What is the one difficulty and the one opportune time? If at that time the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, of right and universal knowledge, the Sugata, the knower of the world, the unsurpassed guide who tames beings, the teacher of gods and humans, the Buddha, the Blessed One, appears in the world and teaches the Dharma, leading to cessation, leading to extinction, leading to the path of awakening, and expounded by the Sugata, but that person is born in a central country at that time, not deaf, not mute, not like a sheep, not communicating with gestures, and able to understand and speak the meaning of good and evil, and has right views, not inverted views, and holds and proclaims the view: 'There is giving, there is observance, there are incantations, there are good and bad deeds, there are results of good and bad deeds, there is this world and the next, there is father and mother, there are true persons in the world who go to a good place, go well, go in a good direction, who in this world or the next know and awaken by themselves, and attain liberation by themselves.' This is called the one difficulty and the one opportune time for practicing the holy life.

「人行梵行有一不难、有一是时。云何人行梵行有一不难、有一是时？若时如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐，出世说法，趣向止息，趣向灭讫，趣向觉道，为善逝所演。彼人尔时生于中国，不聋、不痖、不如羊鸣，不以手语，又能知说善恶之义，而有正见、不颠倒见，如是见、如是说：『有施、有斋，亦有呪说，有善恶业，有善恶业报，有此世彼世，有父有母，世有真人往至善处、善去、善向，此世彼世自知自觉，自作证成就游。』是谓人行梵行有一不难、有一是时。」

Then the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

于是，世尊说此颂曰：

If one obtains a human body, one speaks the most subtle Dharma. If
one does not attain the fruit, one will certainly not encounter the right
time. Speaking much about the difficulty of practicing the holy life, people are
in the later world. If one encounters the right time, it is very
difficult in this world. Wishing to obtain a human body again and hear
the subtle Dharma, one should diligently study, out of compassion for oneself. Speaking
and hearing the good Dharma, one should not miss the right time. If
one misses this time, one will certainly be worried and fall into hell
If one does not encounter the right time and does not hear the
teaching of good Dharma, it is like a merchant losing wealth, experiencing birth
and death without limit. If one obtains a human body and hears the
teaching of the right and good Dharma, following the teachings of the World-Honored
One, one will certainly encounter the right time. If one encounters this time
one is able to practice the right holy life, and accomplish the unsurpassed
eye, as taught by the Sun-like One. They always protect themselves, progress, and
are free from afflictions. They cut off all bonds, subdue the demons and
their retinue, and cross over the world, attaining the exhaustion of all taints.

「若得人身者，　　说最微妙法，若有不得果，　　必不遇其时。多说梵行难，　　人在于后世，若得遇其时，　　是世中甚难。欲得复人身，　　及闻微妙法，当以精勤学，　　人自哀愍故。谈说闻善法，　　莫令失其时，若失此时者，　　必忧堕地狱。若不遇其时，　　不闻说善法，如商人失财，　　受生死无量。若有得人身，　　闻说正善法，遵奉世尊教，　　必遭遇其时。若遭遇此时，　　堪任正梵行，成就无上眼，　　日亲之所说。彼为常自护，　　进行离诸使，断灭一切结，　　降魔魔眷属，彼度于世间，　　谓得尽诸漏。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
