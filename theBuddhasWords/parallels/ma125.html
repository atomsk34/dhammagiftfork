MĀ 125

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 11 大品

125. （一二五）贫穷经

Parallel to: AN 6.45

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Is it a great suffering for a person who desires to be poor?"

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「世有欲人贫穷，为大苦耶？」

The bhikṣus said, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

诸比丘白曰：「尔也。世尊！」

The World-Honored One again told the bhikṣus, "If a person who desires is poor and borrows property from others' homes, is it a great suffering in the world to borrow property from others' homes?"

世尊复告诸比丘曰：「若有欲人贫穷举贷他家财物，世中举贷他家财物，为大苦耶？」

The bhikṣus said, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

诸比丘白曰：「尔也。世尊！」

The World-Honored One again told the bhikṣus, "If a person who desires borrows property and is unable to repay it on time, and says, 'Extend the time,' is it a great suffering in the world to extend the time?"

世尊复告诸比丘曰：「若有欲人举贷财物，不得时还，白曰：『长息。』世中长息，为大苦耶？」

The bhikṣus said, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

诸比丘白曰：「尔也。世尊！」

The World-Honored One again told the bhikṣus, "If a person who desires extends the time and does not repay, and the creditor demands it, is it a great suffering in the world for the creditor to demand it?"

世尊复告诸比丘曰：「若有欲人长息不还，财主责索，世中财主责索，为大苦耶？」

The bhikṣus said, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

诸比丘白曰：「尔也。世尊！」

The World-Honored One again told the bhikṣus, "If a person who desires is demanded by the creditor but cannot repay, and the creditor frequently goes to them to demand it, is it a great suffering in the world for the creditor to frequently go to them to demand it?"

世尊复告诸比丘曰：「若有欲人财主责索，不能得偿，财主数往至彼求索，世中财主数往至彼求索，为大苦耶？」

The bhikṣus said, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

诸比丘白曰：「尔也。世尊！」

The World-Honored One again told the bhikṣus, "If a person who desires is frequently demanded by the creditor, but they still do not repay, they are then bound by the creditor. Is it a great suffering in the world to be bound by the creditor?"

世尊复告诸比丘曰：「若有欲人，财主数往至彼求索，彼故不还，便为财主之所收缚，世中为财主收缚，为大苦耶？」

The bhikṣus said, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

诸比丘白曰：「尔也。世尊！」

This is the great suffering of a person with desires being poor in the world. The great suffering of a person with desires borrowing money and goods in the world. The great suffering of a person with desires borrowing money and goods with interest in the world. The great suffering of a person with desires being demanded by the creditor in the world. The great suffering of a person with desires being frequently demanded by the creditor in the world. The great suffering of a person with desires being bound by the creditor in the world. In this way, if there is no faith in the good Dharma, no discipline, no extensive learning, no generosity, and no wisdom in the good Dharma, even if one has much gold, silver, lapis lazuli, crystal, maṇi, white shells, conch shells, coral, amber, agate, mother-of-pearl, agate, jade, red stone, and pearl, one is still poor and powerless. This is what I call the poverty of not being good in the holy Dharma.

「是为世中有欲人贫穷是大苦，世中有欲人举贷财物是大苦，世中有欲人举贷长息是大苦，世中有欲人财主责索是大苦，世中有欲人财主数往至彼求索是大苦，世中有欲人为财主收缚是大苦。如是若有于此圣法之中，无信于善法，无禁戒、无博闻、无布施、无智慧于善法，彼虽多有金、银、琉璃、水精、摩尼、白珂、螺璧、珊瑚、琥珀、码碯、瑇瑁、砗渠、碧玉、赤石、琁珠，然彼故贫穷，无有力势，是我圣法中说不善贫穷也。

Their evil bodily, verbal, and mental actions are what I call the unwholesomeness of borrowing in the holy Dharma.

「彼身恶行，口、意恶行，是我圣法中说不善举贷也。彼欲覆藏身之恶行，不自发露，不欲道说，不欲令人诃责，不顺求，欲覆藏口、意恶行，不自发露，不欲道说，不欲令人诃责，不顺求，是我圣法中说不善长息也。彼或行村邑及村邑外，诸梵行者见已，便作是说：『诸贤！此人如是作、如是行、如是恶、如是不净，是村邑刺。』彼作是说：『诸贤！我不如是作、不如是行、不如是恶、不如是不净，亦非村邑刺，是我圣法中说不善责索也。』

They may be in an unoccupied place, or under a tree in a mountain forest, or in an empty dwelling, thinking of the three unwholesome thoughts: thoughts of desire, anger, and harm. This is what I call the unwholesomeness of frequently demanding in the holy Dharma.

「彼或在无事处，或在山林树下，或在空闲居，念三不善念：欲念、恚念、害念，是我圣法中说不善数往求索也。彼作身恶行，口、意恶行，彼作身恶行，口、意恶行已，因此缘此，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中，是我圣法中说不善收缚也。我不见缚更有如是苦、如是重、如是麤、如是不可乐，如地狱、畜生、饿鬼缚也。此三苦缚，漏尽阿罗诃比丘已知灭尽，拔其根本，永无来生。」

Then the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

于是，世尊说此颂曰：

The suffering of poverty in the world is to borrow money from
others. Having borrowed money, they are troubled by others' demands. The creditors
come to demand it, and because of this, they are bound. This
bondage is very heavy and painful, yet the world delights in desires
It is the same in the holy Dharma. If one lacks right
faith, shamelessness, and non-embarrassment, one does evil and unwholesome actions. One does
evil bodily actions, and the same with verbal and mental actions. One
conceals them and does not want to speak, and does not delight
in being corrected and rebuked. If one frequently engages in them, one's
thoughts will be painful. Whether in a village or a quiet place
because of this, one will certainly regret. One's bodily and verbal practices
and the thoughts of the mind, increase evil karma, and one does
them again and again. That evil karma is without wisdom. Having done
much unwholesome, one follows one's birth to the end, and will certainly
go to the bondage of hell. This bondage is the most painful
which the heroic have left behind. One obtains wealth and benefits according
to the Dharma, and without debt, one attains peace. One gives and
attains joy, and both obtain benefits. Thus, these householders, due to giving
increase their merit and virtue. Thus, in the holy Dharma, if one
has good sincerity, is fully endowed with shame and embarrassment, and is
free from stinginess and greed, one has already abandoned the five hindrances
always delights in practicing diligence, accomplishes various dhyānas and samādhis, and is
fully endowed with constant joy. Having attained the joy of non-eating, it
is like the purity of bathing in water. The mind is liberated
without movement, and all bonds are exhausted. The absence of illness is
nirvāṇa, called the unsurpassed lamp. Without sorrow, without dust, and at peace
this is said to be immovable.

「世间贫穷苦，　　举贷他钱财，举贷钱财已，　　他责为苦恼。财主往求索，　　因此收系缚，此缚甚重苦，　　世间乐于欲。于圣法亦然，　　若无有正信，无慙及无愧，　　作恶不善行。身作不善行，　　口意俱亦然，覆藏不欲说，　　不乐正教诃。若有数数行，　　意念则为苦，或村或静处，　　因是必有悔。身口习诸行，　　及意之所念，恶业转增多，　　数数作复作。彼恶业无慧，　　多作不善已，随所生毕讫，　　必往地狱缚。此缚最甚苦，　　雄猛之所离，如法得财利，　　不负得安隐。施与得欢喜，　　二俱皆获利，如是诸居士，　　因施福增多。如是圣法中，　　若有好诚信，具足成慙愧，　　庶几无悭贪。已舍离五盖，　　常乐行精进，成就诸禅定，　　满具常弃乐。已得无食乐，　　犹如水浴净，不动心解脱，　　一切有结尽。无病为涅槃，　　谓之无上灯，无忧无尘安，　　是说不移动。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
