MĀ 126

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 11 大品

126. （一二六）行欲经

Parallel to: AN 4.61 (~) / AN 5.41 (~) / AN 10.91

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the householder Anāthapiṇḍada went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said, "World-Honored One! In the world, how many people engage in desire?"

尔时，给孤独居士往诣佛所，稽首佛足，却坐一面，白曰：「世尊！世中为有几人行欲？」

The World-Honored One said, "Householder! In the world, there are ten people who engage in desire. What are the ten? Householder, there is one person who engages in desire. They seek wealth through unrighteous and improper means. Having obtained wealth through unrighteous and improper means, they do not provide for their own comfort and that of their parents, wives, children, servants, and workers. They also do not make offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, causing them to ascend to the upper realms, experience pleasure, and receive the reward of being reborn in heaven and living a long life. This is one person who engages in desire. Furthermore, householder, there is one person who engages in desire. They seek wealth through unrighteous and improper means. Having obtained wealth through unrighteous and improper means, they are able to provide for their own comfort and that of their parents, wives, children, servants, and workers, but they do not make offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, causing them to ascend to the upper realms, experience pleasure, and receive the reward of being reborn in heaven and living a long life. This is one person who engages in desire. Furthermore, householder, there is one person who engages in desire. They seek wealth through unrighteous and improper means. Having obtained wealth through unrighteous and improper means, they are able to provide for their own comfort and that of their parents, wives, children, servants, and workers, and they also make offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, causing them to ascend to the upper realms, experience pleasure, and receive the reward of being reborn in heaven and living a long life. This is one person who engages in desire.

世尊告曰：「居士！世中凡有十人行欲。云何为十？居士，有一行欲人，非法无道求索财物，彼非法无道求财物已，不自养安隐及父母、妻子、奴婢、作使，亦不供养沙门、梵志，令升上与乐俱而受乐报，生天长寿。如是有一行欲人也。复次，居士！有一行欲人，非法无道求索财物，彼非法无道求财物已，能自养安隐及父母、妻子、奴婢、作使，而不供养沙门、梵志，令升上与乐俱而受乐报，生天长寿。如是有一行欲人也。复次，居士！有一行欲人，非法无道求索财物，彼非法无道求财物已，能自养安隐及父母、妻子、奴婢、作使，亦供养沙门、梵志，令升上与乐俱而受乐报，生天长寿。如是有一行欲人也。

Furthermore, householder, there is one person who engages in desire. They seek wealth through both righteous and unrighteous means. Having obtained wealth through both righteous and unrighteous means, they do not provide for their own comfort and that of their parents, wives, children, servants, and workers. They also do not make offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, causing them to ascend to the upper realms, experience pleasure, and receive the reward of being reborn in heaven and living a long life. This is one person who engages in desire. Furthermore, householder, there is one person who engages in desire. They seek wealth through both righteous and unrighteous means. Having obtained wealth through both righteous and unrighteous means, they are able to provide for their own comfort and that of their parents, wives, children, servants, and workers, but they do not make offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, causing them to ascend to the upper realms, experience pleasure, and receive the reward of being reborn in heaven and living a long life. This is one person who engages in desire. Furthermore, householder, there is one person who engages in desire. They seek wealth through both righteous and unrighteous means. Having obtained wealth through both righteous and unrighteous means, they are able to provide for their own comfort and that of their parents, wives, children, servants, and workers, and they also make offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, causing them to ascend to the upper realms, experience pleasure, and receive the reward of being reborn in heaven and living a long life. This is one person who engages in desire.

「复次，居士！有一行欲人，法非法求索财物，彼法非法求财物已，不自养安隐及父母、妻子、奴婢、作使，亦不供养沙门、梵志，令升上与乐俱而受乐报，生天长寿。如是有一行欲人也。复次，居士！有一行欲人，法非法求索财物，彼法非法求财物已，能自养安隐及父母、妻子、奴婢、作使，而不供养沙门、梵志，令升上与乐俱而受乐报，生天长寿。如是有一行欲人也。复次，居士！有一行欲人，法非法求索财物，彼法非法求财物已，能自养安隐及父母、妻子、奴婢、作使，亦供养沙门、梵志，令升上与乐俱而受乐报，生天长寿。如是有一行欲人也。」

Furthermore, householder, there is one person who engages in desire. They seek wealth through righteous and proper means. Having obtained wealth through righteous and proper means, they do not provide for their own comfort and that of their parents, wives, children, servants, and workers. They also do not make offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, causing them to ascend to the upper realms, experience pleasure, and receive the reward of being reborn in heaven and living a long life. This is one person who engages in desire. Furthermore, householder, there is one person who engages in desire. They seek wealth through righteous and proper means. Having obtained wealth through righteous and proper means, they are able to provide for their own comfort and that of their parents, wives, children, servants, and workers, but they do not make offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, causing them to ascend to the upper realms, experience pleasure, and receive the reward of being reborn in heaven and living a long life. This is one person who engages in desire.

「复次，居士！有一行欲人，如法以道求索财物，彼如法以道求财物已，不自养安隐及父母、妻子、奴婢、作使，亦不供养沙门、梵志，令升上与乐俱而受乐报，生天长寿。如是有一行欲人也。复次，居士！有一行欲人，如法以道求索财物，彼如法以道求财物已，能自养安隐及父母、妻子、奴婢、作使，而不供养沙门、梵志，令升上与乐俱而受乐报，生天长寿。如是有一行欲行人也。

Furthermore, householder, there is one person who engages in desire. They seek wealth through righteous and proper means. Having obtained wealth through righteous and proper means, they are able to provide for their own comfort and that of their parents, wives, children, servants, and workers. They also make offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, causing them to ascend to the upper realms, experience pleasure, and receive the reward of being reborn in heaven and living a long life. Having obtained wealth, they become attached and bound. Being bound, they become attached. They do not see the dangers and do not know the way out, and they use it. This is one person who engages in desire. Furthermore, householder, there is one person who engages in desire. They seek wealth through righteous and proper means. Having obtained wealth through righteous and proper means, they are able to provide for their own comfort and that of their parents, wives, children, servants, and workers. They also make offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, causing them to ascend to the upper realms, experience pleasure, and receive the reward of being reborn in heaven and living a long life. Having obtained wealth, they are not attached, not bound, not bound, not attached. They see the dangers and know the way out, and they use it. This is one person who engages in desire.

「复次，居士！有一行欲人，如法以道求索财物，彼如法以道求财物已，能自养安隐及父母、妻子、奴婢、作使，亦供养沙门、梵志，令升上与乐俱而受乐报，生天长寿。得财物已，染著缚缴，缴已染著，不见灾患，不知出要而用。如是有一行欲人也。复次，居士！有一行欲人，如法以道求索财物，彼如法以道求财物已，能自养安隐及父母、妻子、奴婢、作使，亦供养沙门、梵志，令升上与乐俱而受乐报，生天长寿。得财物已，不染不著，不缚不缴，不缴已染著，见灾患，知出要而用。如是有一行欲人也。

Householder, if there is a person who engages in desire, who seeks wealth through unrighteous and improper means, and having obtained wealth through unrighteous and improper means, does not provide for their own comfort and that of their parents, wives, children, servants, and workers, and also does not make offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, causing them to ascend to the upper realms, experience pleasure, and receive the reward of being reborn in heaven and living a long life, then this person who engages in desire is the lowest among those who engage in desire. Householder, if there is a person who engages in desire, who seeks wealth through righteous and unrighteous means, and having obtained wealth through righteous and unrighteous means, provides for their own comfort and that of their parents, wives, children, servants, and workers, and also makes offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, causing them to ascend to the upper realms, experience pleasure, and receive the reward of being reborn in heaven and living a long life, then this person who engages in desire is the highest among those who engage in desire.

「居士！若有一行欲人，非法无道求索财物，彼非法无道求财物已，不自养安隐及父母、妻子、奴婢、作使，亦不供养沙门、梵志，令升上与乐俱而受乐报，生天长寿者，此行欲人于诸行欲人为最下也。居士！若有一行欲人，法非法求索财物，彼法非法求财物已，自养安隐及父母、妻子、奴婢、作使，亦供养沙门、梵志，令升上与乐俱而受乐报，生天长寿者，此行欲人于诸行欲人为最上也。

Householder, if there is a person who engages in desire, who seeks wealth through righteous and proper means, and having obtained wealth through righteous and proper means, provides for their own comfort and that of their parents, wives, children, servants, and workers, and also makes offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, causing them to ascend to the upper realms, experience pleasure, and receive the reward of being reborn in heaven and living a long life. Having obtained wealth, they are not attached, not bound, not bound, not attached. They see the dangers and know the way out, and they use it. This person who engages in desire is the highest among those who engage in desire. It is like this: because of a cow there is milk, because of milk there is curd, because of curd there is fresh butter, because of fresh butter there is clarified butter, and because of clarified butter there is butter essence. Butter essence is the highest, the greatest, the most excellent, the most supreme, the most noble, and the most wonderful. In the same way, householder, this person who engages in desire is the highest, the greatest, the most excellent, the most supreme, the most noble, and the most wonderful among those who engage in desire.

「居士！若有一行欲人，如法以道求索财物，彼如法以道求财物已，自养安隐及父母、妻子、奴婢、作使，亦供养沙门、梵志，令升上与乐俱而受乐报，生天长寿。得财物已，不染不著，不缚不缴，不缴已染著，见灾患，知出要而用者，此行欲人于诸行欲人为最第一、最大、最上、最胜、最尊，为最妙也。犹如因牛有乳，因乳有酪，因酪有生酥，因生酥有熟酥，因熟酥有酥精，酥精者，为最第一、最大、最上、最胜、最尊，为最妙也。如是，居士！此行欲人于诸行欲人为最第一、最大、最上、最胜、最尊，为最妙也。」

Then the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

于是，世尊说此颂曰：

If one seeks wealth through unrighteous means, or through both righteous and unrighteous means
and does not provide for oneself, use it, or give it as charity, both
will have evil consequences, and one will be the lowest among those who engage
in desire. If one seeks wealth through righteous means, and obtains it through one's
own diligence, and provides for others, uses it oneself, and gives it as charity
both will have virtuous consequences, and one will be the highest among those who
engage in desire. If one attains the wisdom of liberation, and engages in desire
while dwelling at home, seeing the dangers and being content, and using wealth sparingly
one will attain the wisdom of liberation from desire, and be the highest among
those who engage in desire.

「若非法求财，　　及法非法求，不供不自用，　　亦不施为福，二俱皆有恶，　　于行欲最下。若如法求财，　　自身懃所得，供他及自用，　　亦以施为福，二俱皆有德，　　于行欲最上。若得出要慧，　　行欲住在家，见灾患知足，　　节俭用财物，彼得出欲慧，　　于行欲最上。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The Elder Anāthapiṇḍada and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说如是。给孤独居士及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
