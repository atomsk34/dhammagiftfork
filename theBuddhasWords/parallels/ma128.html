MĀ 128

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 11 大品

128. （一二八）优婆塞经

Parallel to: AN 5.179

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the householder Anāthapiṇḍada, together with a group of five hundred great upāsakas, went to where Venerable Śāriputra, bowed their heads at his feet, and sat to one side. The five hundred upāsakas also bowed their heads and sat to one side. After the householder Anāthapiṇḍada and the five hundred upāsakas had sat to one side, Venerable Śāriputra expounded the Dharma for them, inspiring and uplifting them, bringing them to a state of joy. Having used immeasurable skillful means to expound the Dharma for them, inspiring and uplifting them, bringing them to a state of joy, he immediately rose from his seat and went to where the Buddha was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side. Not long after Venerable Śāriputra had left, the householder Anāthapiṇḍada and the five hundred upāsakas also went to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side.

尔时，给孤独居士与大优婆塞众五百人俱，往诣尊者舍梨子所，稽首作礼，却坐一面，五百优婆塞亦为作礼，却坐一面。给孤独居士及五百优婆塞坐一面已，尊者舍梨子为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法。劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，即从座起，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，却坐一面。尊者舍梨子去后不久，给孤独居士及五百优婆塞亦诣佛所，稽首佛足，却坐一面。

When Venerable Śāriputra and the assembly were seated, the World-Honored One said, "Śāriputra! If you know that the white-robed noble disciples well protect the five practices and attain the four higher states of mind, they will dwell in the present bliss, easily and without difficulty. Śāriputra! You should know that the noble disciples have exhausted hell, animal realms, hungry ghosts, and all evil destinies. They have attained the stream-entry, do not fall into evil ways, are definitely heading for right awakening, will experience seven existences at most, and after seven transmigrations between heaven and human realms, they will reach the end of suffering.

尊者舍梨子及众坐已定，世尊告曰：「舍梨子！若汝知白衣圣弟子善护行五法及得四增上心，现法乐居，易不难得。舍梨子！汝当记别圣弟子地狱尽，畜生、饿鬼及诸恶处亦尽，得须陀洹，不堕恶法，定趣正觉，极受七有，天上人间七往来已而得苦边。

Śāriputra! How do the white-robed noble disciples well protect the five practices? The white-robed noble disciples abandon killing, cut off killing, discard weapons, have a sense of shame and conscience, have a compassionate mind, and benefit all beings, even down to insects. They purify their minds from killing. The white-robed noble disciples well protect this first practice.

「舍梨子！云何白衣圣弟子善护行五法，白衣圣弟子者，离杀、断杀，弃舍刀杖，有慙有愧，有慈悲心，饶益一切乃至蜫虫。彼于杀生净除其心，白衣圣弟子善护行，此第一法。

Furthermore, Śāriputra! The white-robed noble disciples abandon taking what is not given, cut off taking what is not given, take only what is given, delight in taking what is given, always love to give, rejoice in giving without stinginess, do not expect rewards, are not covered by stealing, and always protect themselves. They purify their minds from taking what is not given. The white-robed noble disciples well protect this second practice.

「复次，舍梨子！白衣圣弟子离不与取、断不与取，与而后取，乐于与取，常好布施，欢喜无恡，不望其报，不以偷所覆，常自护已。彼于不与取净除其心，白衣圣弟子善护行，此第二法。

Furthermore, Śāriputra! The white-robed noble disciples abandon sexual misconduct, cut off sexual misconduct. They may be protected by their father, mother, both parents, brothers, sisters, their parents-in-law, relatives, or clan members. They do not violate such women who are married to others, who are subject to punishment and fear, or who are engaged by name, debt, or flower garlands. They purify their minds from sexual misconduct. The white-robed noble disciples well protect this third practice.

「复次，舍梨子！白衣圣弟子离邪婬、断邪婬，彼或有父所护，或母所护，或父母所护，或兄弟所护，或姉妹所护，或妇父母所护，或亲亲所护，或同姓所护，或为他妇女，有鞭罚恐怖，及有名雇债至华鬘亲，不犯如是女。彼于邪淫净除其心，白衣圣弟子善护行，此第三法。

Furthermore, Śāriputra! The white-robed noble disciples abandon false speech, cut off false speech. They speak truthfully, delight in truth, abide in truth without wavering, are trustworthy, and do not deceive the world. They purify their minds from false speech. The white-robed noble disciples well protect this fourth practice.

「复次，舍梨子！白衣圣弟子离妄言、断妄言，真谛言，乐真谛，住真谛不移动，一切可信，不欺世间。彼于妄言净除其心，白衣圣弟子善护行，此第四法。

Furthermore, Śāriputra! The white-robed noble disciples abandon alcohol, cut off alcohol. They purify their minds from drinking alcohol. The white-robed noble disciples well protect this fifth practice.

「复次，舍梨子！白衣圣弟子离酒、断酒。彼于饮酒净除其心，白衣圣弟子善护行，此第五法。

Śāriputra! How do the white-robed noble disciples attain the four higher states of mind, dwell in happiness in the present life, and find it easy and not difficult? The white-robed noble disciples recollect the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, the one with perfect knowledge and conduct, the Sugata, the knower of the world, the unsurpassed guide who tames beings, the teacher of gods and humans, the Buddha, the Blessed One. Having recollected the Tathāgata in this way, any evil desires are immediately extinguished, and any unwholesome, defiled, sorrowful, and distressed thoughts in the mind are also extinguished. The white-robed noble disciples take the Tathāgata as an object of meditation, and their minds become peaceful and joyful. Any evil desires are immediately extinguished, and any unwholesome, defiled, sorrowful, and distressed thoughts in the mind are also extinguished. The white-robed noble disciples attain the first higher state of mind, dwell in happiness in the present life, and find it easy and not difficult.

「舍梨子！白衣圣弟子云何得四增上心，现法乐居，易不难得？白衣圣弟子念如来，彼如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐。如是念如来已，若有恶欲即便得灭，心中有不善、秽污、愁苦、忧戚亦复得灭。白衣圣弟子攀缘如来，心靖得喜。若有恶欲即便得灭，心中有不善、秽污、愁苦、忧戚亦复得灭。白衣圣弟子得第一增上心，现法乐居，易不难得。

Furthermore, Śāriputra! The white-robed noble disciples recollect the Dharma, the well-proclaimed Dharma by the World-Honored One, which is to be personally experienced by the wise, not to be grasped at, leading to peace, leading to direct knowledge, leading to enlightenment, leading to nirvāṇa. Having contemplated, awakened, known, and recollected the Dharma in this way, any evil desires are immediately extinguished, and any unwholesome, defiled, sorrowful, and distressed thoughts in the mind are also extinguished. The white-robed noble disciples take the Dharma as an object of meditation, and their minds become peaceful and joyful. Any evil desires are immediately extinguished, and any unwholesome, defiled, sorrowful, and distressed thoughts in the mind are also extinguished. The white-robed noble disciples attain this second higher state of mind.

「复次，舍梨子！白衣圣弟子念法，世尊善说法，必至究竟，无烦无热，常有不移动。如是观、如是觉、如是知、如是念法已，若有恶欲即便得灭，心中有不善、秽污、愁苦、忧戚亦复得灭。白衣圣弟子攀缘法，心靖得喜。若有恶欲即便得灭，心中有不善、秽污、愁苦、忧戚亦复得灭。白衣圣弟子得此第二增上心。

Furthermore, Śāriputra! The white-robed noble disciples recollect the Saṅgha, the noble Saṅgha of the Tathāgata, which is well-gone, rightly-gone, Dharma-gone, Dharma-following, and rightly-practicing. That Saṅgha truly has Arhats, those who are on the path to arahantship, those who are Non-returners, those who are on the path to Non-returning, those who are Once-returners, those who are on the path to Once-returning, and those who are Stream-enterers, those who are on the path to Stream-entry. This is called the four pairs, the eight types. That is to say, the Saṅgha of the Tathāgata is accomplished in morality, accomplished in concentration, accomplished in wisdom, accomplished in liberation, accomplished in the knowledge and vision of liberation, worthy of offerings, worthy of hospitality, worthy of gifts, worthy of salutations, an unsurpassed field of merit in the world. Having recollected the Saṅgha in this way, any evil desires are immediately extinguished, and any unwholesome, defiled, sorrowful, and distressed thoughts in the mind are also extinguished. The white-robed noble disciples take the Saṅgha as an object of meditation, and their minds become peaceful and joyful. Any evil desires are immediately extinguished, and any unwholesome, defiled, sorrowful, and distressed thoughts in the mind are also extinguished. The white-robed noble disciples attain this third higher state of mind.

「复次，舍梨子！白衣圣弟子念众，如来圣众善趣正趣，向法次法，顺行如法，彼众实有阿罗诃、趣阿罗诃，有阿那含、趣阿那含，有斯陀含、趣斯陀含，有须陀洹、趣须陀洹，是谓四双八辈。谓如来众成就尸赖，成就三昧，成就般若，成就解脱，成就解脱知见，可敬可重，可奉可供，世良福田。彼如是念如来众，若有恶欲即便得灭，心中有不善、秽污、愁苦、忧戚亦复得灭。白衣圣弟子攀缘如来众，心靖得喜，若有恶欲即便得灭，心中有不善、秽污、愁苦、忧戚亦复得灭。白衣圣弟子是谓得第三增上心，现法乐居，易不难得。

Furthermore, Śāriputra! The white-robed noble disciples recollect their own morality, which is unbroken, unholed, unspotted, unblemished, liberating, praised by the wise, and conducive to concentration. Having recollected their own morality in this way, any evil desires are immediately extinguished, and any unwholesome, defiled, sorrowful, and distressed thoughts in the mind are also extinguished. The white-robed noble disciples take their own morality as an object of meditation, and their minds become peaceful and joyful. Any evil desires are immediately extinguished, and any unwholesome, defiled, sorrowful, and distressed thoughts in the mind are also extinguished. The white-robed noble disciples attain this fourth higher state of mind.

「复次，舍梨子！白衣圣弟子自念尸赖，此尸赖不缺不穿，无秽无浊，住如地不虚妄，圣所称誉，具善受持。彼如是自念尸赖，若有恶欲即便得灭，心中有不善、秽污、愁苦、忧戚亦复得灭。白衣圣弟子攀缘尸赖，心靖得喜，若有恶欲即便得灭，心中有不善、秽污、愁苦、忧戚亦复得灭。白衣圣弟子是谓得第四增上心，现法乐居，易不难得。

"Śāriputra! If you know that the white-robed noble disciples, who practice these five methods well and attain these four higher states of mind, can easily attain the state of happiness in the present life, then Śāriputra! You should declare that the white-robed noble disciples have exhausted hell, animal births, the realm of hungry ghosts, and all evil destinies. They have attained the state of stream-entry, not falling into evil ways, definitely progressing towards right awakening, experiencing a maximum of seven existences, going back and forth between the heavens and the human realm seven times, and thereby reaching the end of suffering." Thereupon, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

「舍梨子！若汝知白衣圣弟子善护行此五法，得此四增上心，现法乐居，易不难得者，舍梨子！汝记别白衣圣弟子地狱尽，畜生、饿鬼及诸恶处亦尽，得须陀洹，不堕恶法，定趣正觉，极受七有，天上人间七往来已而得苦边。」于是，世尊说此颂曰：

The wise ones, dwelling in the householder's life, see the
terrors of hell, and because of upholding the holy Dharma
they remove all evil. They do not kill living beings
knowing and able to abandon them. They speak the truth
and not falsely, and do not steal others' wealth. Content
with their own wives, they do not enjoy others' wives
They abandon drinking alcohol, the root of confusion and madness
They always contemplate the right enlightenment and the good dharmas
They contemplate the aggregates and the body, and from this
they attain joy. Wishing to practice giving, they should expect
its blessings. First give to those with calm minds, and
thus the results will be fulfilled. I now speak of
calm mind. Śāriputra, listen well. Whether black, white, red, or
yellow, or variegated and lovely colors, cattle and various pigeons
wherever they are born, the good ox is in front
With fully developed physical strength, they go and come swiftly
and well. Take what they are capable of, do not
regard their color as wrong. Thus, in this human world
wherever one is born, whether a kṣatriya, brāhman, householder, or
artisan, wherever they are born, the elder who upholds pure
precepts, the world-renouncer, the well-gone one, gives great fruit. The
ignorant, without knowledge, without wisdom, without hearing, gives little fruit
without light, without illumination. If there is light to illuminate
the wise disciples of the Buddha, those who have faith
and turn towards the well-gone one, the faculties are born
and well established. They are born in a good place
going to the homes of the virtuous, and finally attain
nirvāṇa. Thus, each has its causes and conditions.

「慧者住在家，　　见地狱恐怖，因受持圣法，　　除去一切恶。不杀害众生，　　知而能舍离，真谛不妄言，　　不盗他财物。自有妇知足，　　不乐他人妻，舍离断饮酒，　　心乱狂痴本。常当念正觉，　　思惟诸善法，念众观尸赖，　　从是得欢喜。欲行其布施，　　当以望其福，先施于息心，　　如是成果报。我今说息心，　　舍梨当善听，若有黑及白，　　赤色之与黄，尨色爱乐色，　　牛及诸鸽鸟。随彼所生处，　　良御牛在前，身力成具足，　　善速往来快，取彼之所能，　　莫以色为非。如是此人间，　　若有所生处，刹帝丽梵志，　　居士本工师。随彼所生处，　　长老净持戒，世无著善逝，　　施彼得大果。愚痴无所知，　　无慧无所闻，施彼得果少，　　无光无所照。若光有所照，　　有慧佛弟子，信向善逝者，　　根生善坚住。彼是生善处，　　如意往人家，最后得涅槃，　　如是各有缘。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The Venerable Śāriputra and the bhikṣus, the householder Anāthapiṇḍada and the five hundred upāsakas, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced them.

佛说如是。尊者舍梨子及诸比丘，给孤独居士、五百优婆塞，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
