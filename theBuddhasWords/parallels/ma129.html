MĀ 129

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 11 大品

129. （一二九）怨家经

Parallel to: AN 7.64

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus: "There are seven ways of being an enemy that make one an enemy, namely, when men and women are angry. What are the seven? An enemy does not want the enemy to have a good complexion. Why? An enemy does not enjoy the enemy having a good complexion. When a person is angry, habitually angry, and covered by anger, the mind does not abandon anger. Although they may bathe well and anoint their body with fragrant oils, their complexion is still bad. Why? Because they are covered by anger and the mind does not abandon anger. This is called the first way of being an enemy that makes one an enemy, namely, when men and women are angry.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「有七怨家法而作怨家，谓男女辈瞋恚时来。云何为七？怨家者，不欲令怨家有好色。所以者何？怨家者，不乐怨家有好色，人有瞋恚，习瞋恚，瞋恚所覆，心不舍瞋恚，彼虽好沐浴，名香涂身，然色故恶。所以者何？因瞋恚所覆，心不舍瞋恚故。是谓第一怨家法而作怨家，谓男女辈瞋恚时来。

Furthermore, an enemy does not want the enemy to sleep peacefully. Why? An enemy does not enjoy the enemy sleeping peacefully. When a person is angry, habitually angry, and covered by anger, the mind does not abandon anger. Although they may sleep on a fine bed, covered with woolen blankets, with brocade and gauze, with a body-covering robe, with pillows at both ends, and with a canopy and curtains, they still suffer and sleep unhappily. Why? Because they are covered by anger and the mind does not abandon anger. This is called the second way of being an enemy that makes one an enemy, namely, when men and women are angry.

「复次，怨家者，不欲令怨家安隐眠。所以者何？怨家者，不乐怨家安隐眠，人有瞋恚，习瞋恚，瞋恚所覆，心不舍瞋恚。彼虽卧以御床，敷以氍氀、毾［毯－炎＋登］，覆以锦绮罗縠，有𠋆体被，两头安枕，加陵伽波惒逻、波遮悉多罗那，然故忧苦眠。所以者何？因瞋恚所覆，心不舍瞋恚故。是谓第二怨家法而作怨家，谓男女辈瞋恚时来。

Furthermore, an enemy does not want the enemy to obtain great benefits. Why? An enemy does not enjoy the enemy obtaining great benefits. When a person is angry, habitually angry, and covered by anger, the mind does not abandon anger. They should obtain benefits but do not obtain them, and should not obtain benefits but obtain them. These two dharmas contradict each other and greatly do not obtain benefits. Why? Because they are covered by anger and the mind does not abandon anger. This is called the third way of being an enemy that makes one an enemy, namely, when men and women are angry.

「复次，怨家者，不欲令怨家得大利。所以者何？怨家者，不乐怨家得大利，人有瞋恚，习瞋恚，瞋恚所覆，心不舍瞋恚。彼应得利而不得利，应不得利而得利。彼此二法更互相违，大得不利。所以者何？因瞋恚所覆，心不舍瞋恚故。是谓第三怨家法而作怨家，谓男女辈瞋恚时来。

Furthermore, an enemy does not want the enemy to have friends. Why? An enemy does not enjoy the enemy having friends. When a person is angry, habitually angry, and covered by anger, the mind does not abandon anger. If they have close friends, they will abandon and avoid them. Why? Because they are covered by anger and the mind does not abandon anger. This is called the fourth way of being an enemy that makes one an enemy, namely, when men and women are angry.

「复次，怨家者，不欲令怨家有朋友。所以者何？怨家者，不乐怨家有朋友，人有瞋恚，习瞋恚，瞋恚所覆，心不舍瞋恚。彼若有亲朋友，舍离避去。所以者何？因瞋恚所覆，心不舍瞋恚故。是谓第四怨家法而作怨家，谓男女辈瞋恚时来。

Furthermore, an enemy does not want the enemy to have a good reputation. Why? An enemy does not enjoy the enemy having a good reputation. When a person is angry, habitually angry, and covered by anger, the mind does not abandon anger. They will have a bad reputation and ugly sounds heard in all directions. Why? Because they are covered by anger and the mind does not abandon anger. This is called the fifth way of being an enemy that makes one an enemy, namely, when men and women are angry.

「复次，怨家者，不欲令怨家有称誉。所以者何？怨家者，不乐怨家有名称，人有瞋恚，习瞋恚，瞋恚所覆，心不舍瞋恚。彼恶名丑，声周闻诸方。所以者何？因瞋恚所覆，心不舍瞋恚故。是谓第五怨家法而作怨家，谓男女辈瞋恚时来。

Furthermore, an enemy does not want the enemy to be extremely wealthy. Why? An enemy does not enjoy the enemy being extremely wealthy. When a person is angry, habitually angry, and covered by anger, the mind does not abandon anger. They will engage in actions of body, speech, and mind that cause them to lose their wealth. Why? Because they are covered by anger and the mind does not abandon anger. This is called the sixth way of being an enemy that makes one an enemy, namely, when men and women are angry.

「复次，怨家者，不欲令怨家极大富。所以者何？怨家者，不乐怨家极大富，人有瞋恚，习瞋恚，瞋恚所覆，心不舍瞋恚。彼作如是身、口、意行，使彼大失财物。所以者何？因瞋恚所覆，心不舍瞋恚故。是谓第六怨家法而作怨家，谓男女辈瞋恚时来。

Furthermore, an enemy does not want the enemy to have their body destroyed and their life ended, and to definitely go to a good place, to be reborn in heaven. Why? An enemy does not enjoy the enemy going to a good place. When a person is angry, habitually angry, and covered by anger, the mind does not abandon anger. They engage in evil actions of body, speech, and mind. Having engaged in evil actions of body, speech, and mind, when their body is destroyed and their life ends, they will definitely go to a bad place, to be reborn in hell. Why? Because they are covered by anger and the mind does not abandon anger. This is called the seventh way of being an enemy that makes one an enemy, namely, when men and women are angry.

「复次，怨家者，不欲令怨家身坏命终，必至善处，生于天上。所以者何？怨家者，不乐怨家往至善处，人有瞋恚，习瞋恚，瞋恚所覆，心不舍瞋恚，身、口、意恶行，彼身、口、意恶行已，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。所以者何？因瞋恚所覆，心不舍瞋恚故。是谓第七怨家法而作怨家，谓男女辈瞋恚时来。此七怨家法而作怨家，谓男女辈瞋恚时来。」

Then the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

于是，世尊说此颂曰：

The angry one obtains an ugly appearance, sleeping uncomfortably, should obtain
great wealth, but instead obtains no benefit. Close relatives and good
friends, avoid the angry person. Frequently practicing anger, a bad reputation
spreads in all directions. The angry one performs bodily and verbal
actions, with the fetter of anger performing mental actions. When a
person is covered by anger, they lose all their wealth. Anger
produces no benefit, anger produces a defiled mind. Fear arises within
which people cannot perceive. The angry one does not know the
meaning, the angry one does not understand the Dharma. Blind and
dark, they are said to enjoy anger. When anger first arises
it has an evil appearance, like the beginning of a fire
with smoke. From this, hatred and jealousy arise, and because of
this, people become angry. If the angry one does what is
good or bad, later, when anger ceases, it burns like a
fire. The so-called burning karma and the fetters of all dharmas
I now explain them to you. Listen with a good mind
The angry one harms their father, and their brothers and sisters
They also kill their sisters and younger sisters. The angry one
destroys much. The one who is born and raised, who sees
this world, who lives because of them, the angry one also
harms their mother. Without shame or conscience, the angry one is
fettered and has nothing to say. When a person is covered
by anger, their mouth says everything. They create foolish and evil
karma, and shorten their own lives. When they do it, they
do not realize it. Because of anger, fear arises. Attached to
their own body, they love it to the utmost. Although they
love their own body, the angry one also harms themselves. They
stab themselves with a knife, or jump from a cliff, or
hang themselves with a rope, or take poison. Such anger is
like this. Death depends on anger. All of them are cut
off. Wisdom can perceive them. Even a small unwholesome karma, the
wise one can eliminate. One should be able to endure this
practice, to prevent an ugly appearance. Without anger, there is also
no sorrow. Without smoke, there is no arrogance. Controlling and cutting
off anger, it is extinguished and has no taints.

「瞋者得恶色，　　眠卧苦不安，应获得大财，　　反更得不利。亲亲善朋友，　　远离瞋恚人，数数习瞋恚，　　恶名流诸方。瞋作身口业，　　恚缠行意业，人为恚所覆，　　失一切财物。瞋恚生不利，　　瞋恚生心秽，恐怖生于内，　　人所不能觉。瞋者不知义，　　瞋者不晓法，无目盲暗塞，　　谓乐瞋恚人。恚初发恶色，　　犹火始起烟，从是生憎嫉，　　缘是诸人瞋。若瞋者所作，　　善行及不善，于后瞋恚止，　　烦热如火烧。所谓烦热业，　　及诸法所缠，彼彼我今说，　　汝等善心听。瞋者逆害父，　　及于诸兄弟，亦杀姉与妹，　　瞋者多所残。所生及长养，　　得见此世间，因彼得存命，　　此母瞋亦害。无羞无惭愧，　　瞋缠无所言，人为恚所覆，　　口无所不说。造作痴罪逆，　　而自夭其命，作时不自觉，　　因瞋生恐怖。系著自己身，　　爱乐无极已，虽爱念己身，　　瞋者亦自害。以刀而自刺，　　或从岩自投，或以绳自绞，　　及服诸毒药。如是像瞋恚，　　是死依于恚，彼彼一切断，　　用慧能觉了。小小不善业，　　慧者了能除，当堪耐是行，　　欲令无恶色。无恚亦无忧，　　除烟无贡高，调御断瞋恚，　　灭讫无有漏。」
