MĀ 130

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 11 大品

130. （一三〇）教昙弥经

Parallel to: AN 6.54

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Dhamika was the leader of the local elders, the head of the stūpa, and respected by the people. He was violent, hasty, and extremely rude, scolding and criticizing the monks. Because of this, the monks from the local area all left, no longer enjoying staying there. Then the lay followers of the local area saw that the monks from the local area all left, no longer enjoying staying there, and they thought: "For what reason do the monks from the local area all leave, no longer enjoying staying here?" The lay followers of the local area heard that the Venerable Dhamika, the leader of the local elders, the head of the stūpa, and respected by the people, was violent, hasty, and extremely rude, scolding and criticizing the monks. Because of this, the monks from the local area all left, no longer enjoying staying there. Having heard this, the lay followers of the local area then went together to where Venerable Dhamika was, and drove Dhamika out of the monasteries in the local area.

尔时，尊者昙弥为生地尊长，作佛图主，为人所宗，凶暴急弊，极为麤恶，骂詈责数于诸比丘，因此故生地诸比丘皆舍离去，不乐住此。于是，生地诸优婆塞见生地诸比丘皆舍离去，不乐住此，便作是念：「此生地诸比丘以何意故，皆舍离去，不乐住此？」生地诸优婆塞闻此生地尊者昙弥生地尊长，作佛图主，为人所宗，凶暴急弊，极为麤恶，骂詈责数于诸比丘，因此故生地诸比丘皆舍离去，不乐住此。生地诸优婆塞闻已，即共往诣尊者昙弥所，驱逐昙弥，令出生地诸寺中去。

Then Venerable Dhamika, driven out of the monasteries in the local area by the lay followers of the local area, put on his robe, took his bowl, and traveled to the country of Śrāvastī. Wandering there, he arrived at the country of Śrāvastī and stayed at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma. Then Venerable Dhamika went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said: "World-Honored One! I have done nothing wrong, said nothing wrong, and committed no offense to the lay followers of the local area, yet the lay followers of the local area unjustly drove me out of the monasteries in the local area.

于是尊者昙弥，为生地诸优婆塞所驱，令出生地诸寺中去，即摄衣持钵游行，往诣舍卫国，展转进至舍卫国，住胜林给孤独园。于是，尊者昙弥往诣佛所，稽首佛足，却坐一面，白曰：「世尊！我于生地诸优婆塞无所污、无所说、无所犯，然生地诸优婆塞横驱逐我，令出生地诸寺中去。」

At that time the World-Honored One said: "Enough, enough, Dhamika! What need is there to speak of this?"

彼时世尊告曰：「止止昙弥！何须说此？」

Venerable Dhamika joined his palms towards the Buddha and said again: "World-Honored One! I have done nothing wrong, said nothing wrong, and committed no offense to the lay followers of the local area, yet the lay followers of the local area unjustly drove me out of the monasteries in the local area.

尊者昙弥叉手向佛再白曰：「世尊！我于生地诸优婆塞，无所污、无所说、无所犯，然生地诸优婆塞横驱逐我，令出生地诸寺中去。」

The World-Honored One also said again: "Dhamika! In the past, there were merchants in this Jambudvipa who sailed on ships into the ocean with a seagull. They released the seagull not far into the ocean. If the seagull could reach the great shore, it would not return to the ship. If the seagull could not reach the great shore, it would return to the ship. In this way, Dhamika! You were driven out of the monasteries in the local area by the lay followers of the local area, so you came back to me. Stop, stop, Dhamika! What need is there to speak of this again?"

世尊亦再告曰：「昙弥！往昔之时，此阎浮洲有诸商人，乘船入海持视岸鹰行，彼入大海不远便放视岸鹰，若视岸鹰得至大海岸者，终不还船。若视岸鹰不得至大海岸者，便来还船。如是昙弥！为生地优婆塞所驱逐，令出生地诸寺故，便还至我所。止止昙弥！何须复说此？」

Venerable Dhamika again said for the third time: "World-Honored One! I have done nothing wrong, said nothing wrong, and committed no offense to the lay followers of the local area, yet the lay followers of the local area unjustly drove me out of the monasteries in the local area.

尊者昙弥复三白曰：「世尊我于生地诸优婆塞，无所污、无所说、无所犯，然生地诸优婆塞横驱逐我，令出生地诸寺中去。」

The World-Honored One also said for the third time: "Dhamika! Do you abide in the Dharma of a śramaṇa, and are you driven out of the monasteries in the local area by the lay followers of the local area?"

世尊亦复三告曰：「昙弥！汝住沙门法，为生地诸优婆塞所驱逐，令出生地诸寺耶？」

Then Venerable Dhamika rose from his seat, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said: "World-Honored One! How does a śramaṇa abide in the Dharma of a śramaṇa?"

于是，尊者昙弥即从座起，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！云何沙门住沙门法？」

The World-Honored One said: "Dhamika! In the past, there were people with a lifespan of eighty thousand years. Dhamika! When people had a lifespan of eighty thousand years, this Jambudvīpa was extremely wealthy and happy, with many people, villages close together like a chicken's flight. Dhamika! When people had a lifespan of eighty thousand years, women married at the age of five hundred. Dhamika! When people had a lifespan of eighty thousand years, there were these illnesses: defecation, urination, desire, not eating, and old age. Dhamika! When people had a lifespan of eighty thousand years, there was a king named Koravya, intelligent and wise, a wheel-turning king with a fourfold army, ruling the world according to the Dharma, a righteous Dharma king who accomplished the seven treasures. Those seven treasures were: the wheel treasure, the elephant treasure, the horse treasure, the jewel treasure, the woman treasure, the householder treasure, and the chief minister treasure - these were the seven. He was fully endowed with a thousand sons, with handsome faces, brave and fearless, able to subdue the armies of others. He would certainly rule over all this land up to the great ocean, not by sword or staff, but by the Dharma, bringing peace and happiness.

世尊告曰：「昙弥！昔时有人寿八万岁。昙弥！人寿八万岁时，此阎浮洲极大富乐，多有人民，村邑相近，如鸡一飞。昙弥！人寿八万岁时，女年五百岁乃嫁。昙弥！人寿八万岁时，有如是病，大便、小便、欲、不食、老。昙弥！人寿八万岁时，有王名高罗婆，聪明智慧，为转轮王，有四种军，整御天下，如法法王成就七宝。彼七宝者，轮宝、象宝、马宝、珠宝、女宝、居士宝、主兵臣宝，是为七。具足千子，颜貌端政，勇猛无畏，能伏他众，彼必统领此一切地乃至大海，不以刀杖，以法治化，令得安隐。

"Dhamika! King Koravya had a tree called the King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees. Dhamika! The King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees had five branches, the first branch was for the king and queen to eat, the second branch was for the prince and ministers to eat, the third branch was for the people of the country to eat, the fourth branch was for śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas to eat, and the fifth branch was for birds and beasts to eat. Dhamika! The fruit of the King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees was as big as a two-pint bottle, and tasted like pure honey pills. Dhamika! The fruit of the King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees had no guardian, and there was no stealing from each other. There was a man who came, hungry and thirsty, emaciated and haggard, wanting to eat fruit, he went to the King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees, ate his fill of fruit, broke off a branch, and took the fruit back home. The King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees had a god who relied on it to live, he thought: 'The people of Jambudvīpa are strange! They are ungrateful and do not repay. Why is that? After eating their fill of fruit from the King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees, they broke off a branch and took the fruit back home. It would be better if the King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees had no fruit to grow fruit.' The King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees then had no fruit, and did not grow fruit."

「昙弥！高罗婆王有树，名善住尼拘类王。昙弥！善住尼拘类树王而有五枝，第一枝者，王所食及皇后。第二枝者，太子食及诸臣。第三枝者，国人民食。第四枝者，沙门、梵志食。第五枝者，禽兽所食。昙弥！善住尼拘类树王果大如二升瓶，味如淳蜜丸。昙弥！善住尼拘类树王果无有护者，亦无更相偷。有一人来，饥渴极羸，颜色憔悴，欲得食果，往至善住尼拘类树王所，饱噉果已，毁折其枝，持果归去。善住尼拘类树王，有一天依而居之，彼作是念：『阎浮洲人异哉！无恩无有反复。所以者何？从善住尼拘类树王饱噉果已，毁折其枝，持果归去，宁令善住尼拘类树王无果不生果。』善住尼拘类树王即无果，亦不生果。」

Then another person came, hungry and thirsty, emaciated and haggard, wanting to eat fruit, he went to the King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees, saw that the King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees had no fruit, and did not grow fruit, then he went to King Koravya and said: 'Your Majesty! You should know that the King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees has no fruit, and does not grow fruit.' Having heard this, King Koravya, in the time it takes a strong man to bend and stretch his arm, disappeared from Kuru and appeared in the Heaven of the Thirty-three, standing before Sakra Devānām-Indra, and said: 'Kauśika! You should know that the King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees has no fruit, and does not grow fruit.' Then Sakra Devānām-Indra and King Koravya, in the time it takes a strong man to bend and stretch his arm, disappeared from the Heaven of the Thirty-three and appeared in Kuru, standing not far from the King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees. Sakra Devānām-Indra manifested his supernormal powers as he pleased, and with his supernormal powers as he pleased, he manifested a great flood of water and violent wind and rain. Having manifested a great flood of water and violent wind and rain, the King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees was uprooted and fell.

「复有一人来，饥渴极羸，颜色憔悴，欲得噉果，往诣善住尼拘类树王所，见善住尼拘类树王无果，亦不生果，即便往诣高罗婆王所，白曰：『天王！当知善住尼拘类树王无果，亦不生果。』高罗婆王闻已，犹如力士屈伸臂顷，如是高罗婆王于拘楼瘦没，至三十三天，住天帝释前，白曰：『拘翼！当知善住尼拘类树王无果，亦不生果。』于是，天帝释及高罗婆王犹如力士屈伸臂顷，如是天帝释及高罗婆王于三十三天中没，至拘楼瘦。去善住尼拘类树王不远住，天帝释作如其像如意足，以如其像如意足化作大水暴风雨，作大水暴风雨已，善住尼拘类树王拔根倒竖。

Then the god who lived in the King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees, because of this, was sorrowful, distressed, and tearful, standing before Sakra Devānām-Indra. Sakra Devānām-Indra asked: 'God! Why are you sorrowful, distressed, and tearful, standing before me?' The god said: 'Kauśika! You should know that the great flood of water and violent wind and rain uprooted and felled the King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees.' Then Sakra Devānām-Indra told the tree god: 'God! Is it the way of tree gods to live as tree gods that the great flood of water and violent wind and rain uprooted and felled the King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees?' The tree god said: 'Kauśika! What is the way of tree gods to live as tree gods?' Sakra Devānām-Indra said: 'God! If people want to take the roots of the tree, and take the trunk, branches, leaves, flowers, and fruits of the tree, the tree god should not be angry, hateful, or resentful. The tree god should dwell as a tree god with a mind of giving up. This is the way of tree gods to live as tree gods.'

「于是，善住尼拘类树王居止树天，因此故，忧苦愁戚，啼泣垂泪，在天帝释前立。天帝释问曰：『天！汝何意忧苦愁戚，啼泣垂泪，在我前立耶？』彼天白曰：『拘翼！当知大水暴风雨，善住尼拘类树王拔根倒竖。』时，天帝释告彼树天曰：『天！汝树天住树天法，大水暴风雨，善住尼拘类树王拔根倒竖耶？』树天白曰：『拘翼！云何树天住树天法耶？』天帝释告曰：『天！若使人欲得树根，持树根去，欲得树茎、树枝、树叶、树华、树果持去者，树天不应瞋恚，不应憎嫉，心不应恨。树天舍意而住树天，如是树天住树天法。』

"The god said again: 'Kauśika! I, a tree god, do not dwell in the way of tree gods. From today onwards, I, a tree god, will dwell in the way of tree gods. May the King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees return to its original state.' Then Sakra Devānām-Indra manifested his supernormal powers as he pleased, and having manifested his supernormal powers as he pleased, he again manifested a great flood of water and violent wind and rain. Having manifested a great flood of water and violent wind and rain, the King of Well-Abiding Nyagrodha Trees immediately returned to its original state. Thus, Tārukkha! If a bhikṣu is reviled, he does not revile in return; if angry, he is not angry in return; if broken, he is not broken in return; if struck, he does not strike in return. Thus, Tārukkha! A śramaṇa dwells in the Dharma of a śramaṇa."

「天复白曰：『拘翼！我树天不住树天法，从今日始树天住树天法，愿善住尼拘类树王还复如本。』于是，天帝释作如其像如意足，作如其像如意足已，复化作大水暴风雨，化作大水暴风雨已，善住尼拘类树王即复如故。如是，昙弥！若有比丘骂者不骂，瞋者不瞋，破者不破，打者不打。如是，昙弥！沙门住沙门法。」

Then the Venerable Tārukkha rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, put his palms together toward the Buddha, wept and shed tears, and said: "World-Honored One! I am not a śramaṇa dwelling in the Dharma of a śramaṇa. From today onwards, I will dwell in the Dharma of a śramaṇa."

于是，尊者昙弥即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，啼泣垂泪，白曰：「世尊！我非沙门住沙门法，从今日始沙门住沙门法。」

The World-Honored One said: "Tārukkha! In the past, there was a great teacher named Sunetra, who was revered by non-Buddhist ascetics. He abandoned desire and attained the supernormal powers. Tārukkha! Sunetra had countless hundreds of thousands of disciples. Tārukkha! Sunetra taught the Dharma of the Brahma Realm to his disciples. Tārukkha! When Sunetra taught the Dharma of the Brahma Realm, some of his disciples did not fully practice it. After their lives ended, they were reborn in the Heaven of the Four Heavenly Kings, the Heaven of the Thirty-three Gods, the Yama Heaven, the Tuṣita Heaven, the Nirmāṇarati Heaven, or the Paranirmitavaśavartin Heaven. Tārukkha! When Sunetra taught the Dharma of the Brahma Realm, some of his disciples fully practiced it. They cultivated the four Brahma abodes, abandoning desire. After their lives ended, they were reborn in the Brahma Heaven. Tārukkha! At that time, Sunetra thought: 'I should not go to the next world together with my disciples, being reborn in the same place. I should now further cultivate supreme loving-kindness. Having cultivated supreme loving-kindness, after my life ends, I will be reborn in the Heaven of Brightness.'

世尊告曰：「昙弥！昔有大师，名曰善眼，为外道仙人之所师宗，舍离欲爱，得如意足。昙弥！善眼大师有无量百千弟子。昙弥！善眼大师为诸弟子说梵世法。昙弥！若善眼大师为说梵世法时，诸弟子等有不具足奉行法者，彼命终已，或生四王天，或生三十三天，或生［火＊佥］磨天，或生兜率哆天，或生化乐天，或生他化乐天。昙弥！若善眼大师为说梵世法时，诸弟子等设有具足奉行法者，彼修四梵室，舍离于欲，彼命终已，得生梵天。昙弥！彼时善眼大师而作是念：『我不应与弟子等同俱至后世，共生一处，我今宁可更修增上慈，修增上慈已，命终得生晃昱天中。』

Tārukkha! At that time, Sunetra the great teacher then further cultivated supreme loving-kindness. Having cultivated supreme loving-kindness, after his life ended, he was reborn in the Heaven of Brightness. Tārukkha! Sunetra the great teacher and his disciples practiced the path without falsehood, attaining great rewards. Like Sunetra, there were also Mūlaparganda, Āraṇaka, the brāhman Gutaśīla, the brāhman Hāritīputra, the brāhman Dhṛtarāṣṭra, and the brāhman Sañjayin.

「昙弥！彼时善眼大师则于后时更修增上慈，修增上慈已，命终得生晃昱天中。昙弥！善眼大师及诸弟子学道不虚，得大果报，如善眼大师，如是牟犁破群那、阿罗那遮婆罗门、瞿陀梨舍哆、害提婆罗摩纳、储提摩丽桥鞞陀逻，及萨哆富楼奚哆。

Tārukkha! The seven Sañjayins also had countless hundreds of thousands of disciples. Tārukkha! The seven Sañjayins taught the Dharma of the Brahma Realm to their disciples. When the seven Sañjayins taught the Dharma of the Brahma Realm, some of their disciples did not fully practice it. After their lives ended, they were reborn in the Heaven of the Four Heavenly Kings, the Heaven of the Thirty-three Gods, the Yama Heaven, the Tuṣita Heaven, the Nirmāṇarati Heaven, or the Paranirmitavaśavartin Heaven. When the seven Sañjayins taught the Dharma of the Brahma Realm, some of their disciples fully practiced it. They cultivated the four Brahma abodes, abandoning desire. After their lives ended, they were reborn in the Brahma Heaven. Tārukkha! The seven Sañjayins thought: 'We should not go to the next world together with our disciples, being reborn in the same place. We should now further cultivate supreme loving-kindness. Having cultivated supreme loving-kindness, after our lives end, we will be reborn in the Heaven of Brightness.'

「昙弥！七富楼奚哆师亦有无量百千弟子。昙弥！七富楼奚哆师为诸弟子说梵世法。若七富楼奚哆师为说梵世法时，诸弟子等有不具足奉行法者，彼命终已，或生四王天，或生三十三天，或生［火＊佥］磨天，或生兜率哆天，或生化乐天，或生他化乐天。若七富楼奚哆师为说梵世法时，诸弟子等设有具足奉行法者，彼修四梵室，舍离于欲，彼命终已，得生梵天。昙弥！七富楼奚哆师而作是念：『我不应与弟子等同俱至后世，共生一处，我今宁可更修增上慈，修增上慈已，命终得生晃昱天中。』

"Tārukkha! At that time, the seven Sañjayins then further cultivated supreme loving-kindness. Having cultivated supreme loving-kindness, after their lives ended, they were reborn in the Heaven of Brightness. Tārukkha! The seven Sañjayins and their disciples practiced the path without falsehood, attaining great rewards. Tārukkha! If one curses those seven teachers and their countless hundreds of thousands of followers, and beats them with anger, one will definitely receive immeasurable suffering. If one curses and beats a single disciple of the Buddha who has attained right view and the lesser fruits, one will receive more suffering than that. Therefore, Tārukkha! You should each take turns to protect each other. Why is that? Apart from this, there is no other loss."

「昙弥！彼时七富楼奚哆师则于后时更修增上慈，修增上慈已，命终得生晃昱天中。昙弥！七富楼奚哆师及诸弟子学道不虚，得大果报。昙弥！若有骂彼七师及无量百千眷属，打破瞋恚责数者，必受无量罪。若有一成就正见佛弟子比丘得小果，骂詈打破瞋恚责数者，此受罪多于彼。是故，昙弥！汝等各各更迭相护。所以者何？离此过已，更无有失。」

Then the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

于是，世尊说此偈曰：

Sunetra, Maudgalyāyana, the brahmin Ārāda Kālāma, Gautama, the young brahmin Hāritīputra, the bridge-builder Sudhamma, and the Sañjayin ascetic.

「须涅、牟梨破群那、　阿逻那遮婆罗门、　瞿陀梨舍哆、　害提婆罗摩纳、　储提摩丽桥鞞陀逻、　萨哆富楼奚哆。

In the past, there were seven teachers of renown, Free from the
bonds of craving, delighting in compassion, with the fetters of desire exhausted
They had disciples, countless in the hundreds of thousands, Who were also
free from the fetters of desire, but did not attain final cessation
If those external ascetics, Who diligently practiced austerities, harbored hatred and jealousy
in their minds, and reviled others, they would incur much greater suffering
If one attains right view, A disciple of the Buddha abiding in
the lesser fruits, and reviles, reproaches, and strikes them, one would incur
much greater suffering than that. Therefore, you, Dhammapāla, Should each protect one
another. The reason for protecting one another is that there is no
greater sin than this. Such extremely grave suffering is also detested by
the noble ones, And one will certainly experience an ugly form, and
take up wrong views. This is the lowest kind of person, As
taught by the noble Dharma. It is said that one who has
not yet transcended sensual desire attains the subtle five faculties. Faith, diligence
mindfulness, right concentration, and right view. One attains this suffering, and previously
experienced its retribution. Having experienced its retribution oneself, One then harms others
If one can protect oneself, one will be able to protect others
Therefore, one should protect oneself. The wise do not delight in this.

「此在过去世，　　七师有名德，无爱缚乐悲，　　欲结尽过去。彼有诸弟子，　　无量百千数，彼亦离欲结，　　须臾不究竟。若彼外仙人，　　善护行苦行，心中怀憎嫉，　　骂者受罪多。若一得正见，　　佛子住小果，骂詈责打破，　　受罪多于彼。是故汝昙弥，　　各各更相护，所以更相护，　　重罪无过是。如是甚重苦，　　亦为圣所恶，必得受恶色，　　横取邪见处。此是最下人，　　圣法之所说，谓未离婬欲，　　得微妙五根。信精进念处，　　正定及正观，如是得此苦，　　前所受其殃。自受其殃已，　　于后便害他，若能自护者，　　彼为能护外。是故当自护，　　慧者无央乐。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The Venerable Dhammapāla and the other monks, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。尊者昙弥及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
