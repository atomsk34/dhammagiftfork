MĀ 131

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 11 大品

131. （一三一）降魔经

Parallel to: MN 50

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the country of Vārāṇasī, staying in the Deer Park at the Sītavana near the Rṣipatana.

一时，佛游婆奇瘦，在鼍山怖林鹿野园中。

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana was building a meditation hut for the Buddha and was walking back and forth in the open air. At that time, Māra the Evil One transformed into a fine form and entered Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana's abdomen. Then Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana thought: "My abdomen now feels like it has eaten beans. I should enter the samādhi of suchness and use the samādhi of suchness to observe my abdomen." At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana went to the end of the walking path, spread out his sitting mat, sat cross-legged, entered the samādhi of suchness, and used the samādhi of suchness to observe his abdomen. Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana then knew that Māra the Evil One was in his abdomen.

尔时，尊者大目犍连教授为佛而作禅屋，露地经行。彼时，魔王化作细形，入尊者大目揵连腹中。于是，尊者大目犍连即作是念：「我今腹中犹如食豆，我宁可入如其像定，以如其像定自观其腹。」是时，尊者大目犍连至经行道头，敷尼师檀，结跏趺坐，入如其像定，以如其像定自观其腹，尊者大目犍连便知魔王在其腹中。

Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana then awoke from samādhi and said to Māra the Evil One: "You, Pāpīyas, come out! You, Pāpīyas, come out! Do not disturb the Tathāgata, do not disturb the Tathāgata's disciples, do not bring about meaninglessness and no benefit for a long time, you will definitely be born in an evil place and experience immeasurable suffering."

尊者大目犍连即从定寤，语魔王曰：「汝波旬出！汝波旬出！莫触娆如来，亦莫触娆如来弟子，莫于长夜无义无饶益，必生恶处，受无量苦。」

At that time, Māra the Evil One thought: "This śramaṇa does not see or know, yet he says: 'You, Pāpīyas, come out! You, Pāpīyas, come out! Do not disturb the Tathāgata, do not disturb the Tathāgata's disciples, do not bring about meaninglessness and no benefit for a long time, you will definitely be born in an evil place and experience immeasurable suffering.' Your teacher has great supernatural powers, great authority, great merit, and great spiritual power, yet he cannot know or see quickly, let alone his disciples being able to know or see.

彼时，魔王便作是念：「此沙门不见不知而作是说：『汝波旬出！汝波旬出！莫触娆如来，亦莫触娆如来弟子，莫于长夜无义无饶益，必生恶处，受无量苦。』汝之尊师有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，彼犹不能速知速见，况复弟子能知见耶？」

Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana again said to Māra the Evil One: "I also know your intention. You think: 'This śramaṇa does not know or see, yet he says: "You, Pāpīyas, come out! You, Pāpīyas, come out! Do not disturb the Tathāgata, do not disturb the Tathāgata's disciples, do not bring about meaninglessness and no benefit for a long time, you will definitely be born in an evil place and experience immeasurable suffering. Your teacher has great supernatural powers, great authority, great merit, and great spiritual power, yet he cannot know or see quickly, let alone his disciples being able to know or see.

尊者大目揵连复语魔王：「我复知汝意，汝作是念：『此沙门不知不见而作是说：「汝波旬出！汝波旬出！莫触娆如来，亦莫触娆如来弟子，莫于长夜无义无饶益，必生恶处，受无量苦。汝之尊师有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，彼犹不能如是速知速见，况复弟子能知见耶？」』」

Māra Pāpīyas again thought: "This śramaṇa knows and sees me, so he says this." Then Māra Pāpīyas transformed into a fine form, emerged from his mouth, and stood before Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana.

彼魔波旬复作是念：「今此沙门知见我故，而作是说耳。」于是，魔波旬化作细形，从口中出，在尊者大目犍连前立。

Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana said: "Pāpīyas, in the past there was a Tathāgata named Jñānakūṭa, the Worthy One, the Perfectly Enlightened One. At that time I was a demon named Pāpīyas. I had a sister named Kṛṣṇā, and you are her son. Pāpīyas, for this reason you are my nephew. Pāpīyas, the Tathāgata Jñānakūṭa, the Worthy One, the Perfectly Enlightened One, had two great disciples, one named Śabda and the other named Saṃjñā. Pāpīyas, why is Venerable Śabda called Śabda? Pāpīyas, Venerable Śabda abides in the Brahma Heaven, filling a thousand worlds with his constant voice. There is no disciple whose voice is equal to, similar to, or superior to his. Pāpīyas, for this reason Venerable Śabda is called Śabda.

尊者大目揵连告曰：「波旬！昔有如来名觉砾拘荀大无所著、等正觉，我时作魔，名曰恶，我有妹，名黑，汝是彼子。波旬！因此事故，汝是我［外＊男］甥。波旬！觉砾拘荀大如来、无所著、等正觉有二大弟子，一者名音，二者名想。波旬！以何义故，尊者音名音耶？波旬！尊者音住梵天上，以常音声满千世界，更无有弟子音声与彼等者、相似者、胜者。波旬！以是义故，尊者音名音也。

Pāpīyas, why is Venerable Saṃjñā called Saṃjñā? Pāpīyas, Venerable Saṃjñā, when he goes to a village to wander, in the morning he puts on his robe, takes his bowl, enters the village for alms, well-protecting his body, well-restraining his faculties, and standing in right mindfulness. Having begged for alms, he eats, and after midday he puts away his robe and bowl, washes his hands and feet, puts his sitting mat over his shoulder, goes to a place without affairs, or to a mountain forest or the foot of a tree, or to a quiet and secluded place, spreads out his sitting mat, and quickly enters the saṃjñā-nirodha-samāpatti. At that time, if there are people tending cattle, sheep, or collecting firewood and grass, or travelers, who enter that mountain forest and see him in the saṃjñā-nirodha-samāpatti, they think: 'Now this śramaṇa is sitting in a place without affairs and has passed away. We should gather dry firewood and grass, pile it up, cover his body, and cremate him.' They then gather firewood and grass, pile it up, cover his body, set it on fire, and leave. Venerable Saṃjñā, having passed the night, arises from samādhi in the morning, shakes off his robe, and goes to the village where he is staying to wander, as usual putting on his robe, taking his bowl, entering the village for alms, well-protecting his body, well-restraining his faculties, and standing in right mindfulness. The people who tended cattle, sheep, or collected firewood and grass, or travelers, who entered that mountain forest and saw him before, think: 'Now this śramaṇa was sitting in a place without affairs and passed away. We already gathered dry firewood and grass, piled it up, covered his body, and cremated it. But this venerable one has again entered saṃjñā.' Pāpīyas, for this reason Venerable Saṃjñā is called Saṃjñā.

「波旬！复以何义尊者想名想耶？波旬！尊者想所依游行村邑，过夜平旦，著衣持钵，入村乞食，善护其身，善摄诸根，立于正念。彼乞食已，食讫，中后收举衣钵，澡洗手足，以尼师檀著于肩上，至无事处，或至山林树下，或至闲居静处，敷尼师檀，结加趺坐，速入想知灭定。彼时，若有放牛羊人、取樵草人，或行路人，入彼山林，见入想知灭定，便作是念：『今此沙门于无事处坐而命终，我等宁可以燥樵、草拾已，积聚覆其身上而耶维之。』即拾樵、草积覆其身，以火然之，便舍而去。彼尊者想过夜平旦，从定寤起，抖擞衣服，所依村邑游行，如常著衣，持钵入村乞食，善护其身，善摄诸根，立于正念。彼放牛羊人、取樵草人，或行路人，入彼山林人先见者，便作是念：『今此沙门在无事处坐坐而命终，我等昨已拾燥樵、草积覆其身，以火烧之，然已而去。然此贤者更复想也。』波旬！以是义故，尊者想名想也。

Pāpīyas, at that time the evil Māra thought: 'This shaven-headed śramaṇa is bound by Kṛṣṇā, cut off from offspring, and without a son. He studies meditation, with longing, increased longing, and repeated longing, like an ass carrying a heavy load all day, tied up in a manger, not getting any grain. For the sake of that grain, he longs, increases his longing, and repeatedly longs. So too, this shaven-headed śramaṇa is bound by Kṛṣṇā, cut off from offspring, and without a son. He studies meditation, with longing, increased longing, and repeated longing, like a cat by a mouse hole, wanting to catch a mouse, he longs, increases his longing, and repeatedly longs. So too, this shaven-headed śramaṇa is bound by Kṛṣṇā, cut off from offspring, and without a son. He studies meditation, with longing, increased longing, and repeated longing, like a hawk in a pile of dry firewood, wanting to catch a mouse, he longs, increases his longing, and repeatedly longs. So too, this shaven-headed śramaṇa is bound by Kṛṣṇā, cut off from offspring, and without a son. He studies meditation, with longing, increased longing, and repeated longing, like a crane on the bank of a pond, wanting to catch fish, he longs, increases his longing, and repeatedly longs. So too, this shaven-headed śramaṇa is bound by Kṛṣṇā, cut off from offspring, and without a son. He studies meditation, with longing, increased longing, and repeated longing. What does he long for? For what purpose does he long? What does he seek to long for? He is disturbed, crazy, ruined. I do not know where he comes from, nor where he goes, nor where he abides. I do not know his death or his birth. I should rather instruct the brāhmaṇas and householders: "You all come together to revile and beat the diligent śramaṇa." Why is that? Perhaps when reviling, beating, and accusing him, he may arouse an evil mind, allowing me to take advantage of him.'

「波旬！彼时恶魔便作是念：『此秃沙门以黑所缚，断种无子，彼学禅，伺、增伺、数数伺，犹若如驴，竟日负重，系在枥上，不得麦食，为彼麦故，伺、增伺、数数伺。如是，此秃沙门为黑所缚，断种无子，学禅，伺、增伺、数数伺，犹如猫子在鼠穴边，欲捕鼠故，伺、增伺、数数伺。如是，此秃沙门为黑所缚，断种无子，彼学禅，伺、增伺、数数伺，犹如鸺狐在燥樵积间，为捕鼠故，伺、增伺、数数伺。如是，此秃沙门为黑所缚，断种无子，学禅，伺、增伺、数数伺，犹如鹤鸟在水岸边，为捕鱼故，伺、增伺、数数伺。如是，此秃沙门为黑所缚，断种无子，学禅，伺、增伺、数数伺。彼何所伺？为何义伺？求何等伺？彼调乱狂发败坏，我不知彼何所从来？亦不知彼何所从去？亦不知住止，不知死不知生，我宁可教勅梵志、居士，汝等共来骂詈精进沙门，打破责数。所以者何？或骂打破责数时，傥能起恶心，令我得其便。』

Pāpīyas, at that time the evil Māra instructed the brāhmaṇas and householders, and those brāhmaṇas and householders reviled and beat the diligent śramaṇa, and accused him. Those brāhmaṇas and householders either hit him with a stick, or threw stones at him, or beat him with a staff, or injured the diligent śramaṇa's head, or tore his robe, or broke his alms bowl. At that time, if any of those brāhmaṇas and householders died, because of that, with the breakup of the body, after death, they certainly went to a bad place, to a hell. Having been born there, they thought: 'I should receive this suffering, and I will have to receive even more extreme suffering than this. Why is that? Because we previously performed evil actions towards the diligent śramaṇa.'

「波旬！彼时恶魔便教勅梵志、居士，彼梵志、居士骂詈精进沙门，打破责数。彼梵志、居士或以木打，或以石掷，或以杖挝，或伤精进沙门头，或裂坏衣，或破应器。尔时，梵志、居士若有死者，因此缘此，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。彼生已，作是念：『我应受此苦，当复更受极苦过是。所以者何？以我等向精进沙门行恶行故。』

"Pāpīyas, at that time the disciples of the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, having their heads injured, their robes torn, and their alms bowls broken, went to the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, Akṣobhya. At that time, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, Akṣobhya, surrounded and attended by an assembly of countless hundreds of thousands, was teaching the Dharma. The Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, Akṣobhya, saw from afar his disciples coming with their heads injured, their robes torn, and their alms bowls broken, and having seen them, he addressed the monks: 'Do you see, monks, how the evil Māra instructed the brāhmaṇas and householders: "You all come together to revile and beat the diligent śramaṇa." Why is that? Perhaps when reviling, beating, and accusing him, he may arouse an evil mind, allowing me to take advantage of him." 「波旬！觉砾拘荀大如来、无所著、等正觉弟子用伤其头，裂坏其衣，破其应器已，往诣觉砾拘荀大如来、无所著、等正觉所。尔时，觉砾拘荀大如来、无所著、等正觉无量百千眷属围遶而为说法。觉砾拘荀大如来、无所著、等正觉遥见弟子头伤、衣裂、钵破而来，见已，告诸比丘：『汝等见不？恶魔教勅梵志、居士：「汝等共来骂詈精进沙门，打破啧数。所以者何？或骂打破责数时，傥能起恶心，令我得其便。」比丘！汝等当以心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游。如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游，令恶魔求便不能得便。』

「波旬！觉砾拘荀大如来、无所著、等正觉以此教教诸弟子，彼即受教，心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游。如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是悲喜心与舍俱，无结无怨无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。以此故，彼恶魔求便不能得便。

"Pāpīyas, at that time the evil Māra again thought: 'I seek an opportunity with the diligent śramaṇa, but I cannot find it. I should rather instruct the brāhmaṇas and householders: "You all come together to pay homage, make offerings, and serve the diligent śramaṇa." Perhaps by paying homage, making offerings, and serving the diligent śramaṇa, he may arouse an evil mind, allowing me to take advantage of him." Pāpīyas, having been instructed by the evil Māra, the brāhmaṇas and householders then came together to pay homage, make offerings, and serve the diligent śramaṇa, spreading out their robes on the ground and saying: 'The diligent śramaṇa may walk on this. The diligent śramaṇa practices what is difficult to practice, bringing long-term benefit, peace, and happiness to us.' The brāhmaṇas and householders spread out their hair on the ground and said: 'The diligent śramaṇa may walk on this. The diligent śramaṇa practices what is difficult to practice, bringing long-term benefit, peace, and happiness to us.' The brāhmaṇas and householders held various foods and drinks in their hands, waiting by the road and saying: 'The diligent śramaṇa may accept this food and drink. Please take it and use it as you wish, bringing long-term benefit, peace, and happiness to us.' The faithful brāhmaṇas and householders saw the diligent śramaṇa, respectfully embraced him, and brought him inside, offering various goods to the diligent śramaṇa, saying: 'Please accept and use these. Please take them and use them as you wish.' At that time, the brāhmaṇas and householders who died because of this, due to this, upon the dissolution of the body, after death, certainly went to a good place, to a heavenly realm. Having been born there, they thought: 'We should experience this pleasure, and we will experience even greater pleasure than this. Why is that? Because we previously practiced good conduct towards the diligent śramaṇa.'

「波旬！彼时恶魔复作是念：『我以此事求精进沙门便，而不能得。我宁可教勅梵志、居士，汝等共来奉敬、供养、礼事精进沙门，或以奉敬、供养、礼事精进沙门，傥能起恶心，令我得其便。』波旬！彼梵志、居士为恶魔所教勅已，即共奉敬、供养、礼事精进沙门，以衣敷地而作是说：『精进沙门可于上行，精进沙门难行而行，令我长夜得利饶益，安隐快乐。』梵志、居士以发布地而作是说：『精进沙门可于上行，精进沙门难行而行，令我长夜得利饶益，安隐快乐。』梵志、居士以手捧持种种饮食，住道边待而作是说：『精进沙门受是食是，可持是去，随意而用，令我长夜得利饶益，安隐快乐。』诸信梵志、居士见精进沙门，敬心扶抱，将入于内，持种种财物与精进沙门，作如是说：『受是用是，可持是去，随意所用。』尔时，梵志、居士若有死者，因此缘此，身坏命终，必至善处，生于天上。生已，作是念：『我应受是乐，当复更受极乐胜是。所以者何？以我等向精进沙门行善行故。』

Pāpīyas, the disciples of the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, Akṣobhya, having received homage, offerings, and service, went to where the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, Akṣobhya, was. At that time, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, Akṣobhya, was surrounded by immeasurable hundreds of thousands of attendants, teaching the Dharma. The Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, Akṣobhya, saw from afar that the disciples had received homage, offerings, and service, and having seen them, he told the bhikṣus: 'Do you see? The evil Māra instructed the brāhmaṇas and householders: "You all come together to pay homage, make offerings, and serve the diligent śramaṇa. Perhaps by paying homage, making offerings, and serving the diligent śramaṇa, he may arouse an evil mind, allowing me to take advantage of him." Bhikṣus, you should observe that all conditioned phenomena are impermanent, observe that they are subject to arising and decay, observe that they are without desire, observe that they are to be abandoned, observe that they cease, observe that they are to be cut off, so that the evil Māra may seek an opportunity but not find it.' Pāpīyas, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, Akṣobhya, taught his disciples in this way, and they accepted the teaching, observing that all conditioned phenomena are impermanent, observing that they are subject to arising and decay, observing that they are without desire, observing that they are to be abandoned, observing that they cease, observing that they are to be cut off, so that the evil Māra may seek an opportunity but not find it.

「波旬！觉砾拘荀大如来、无所著、等正觉弟子得奉敬、供养、礼事已，往诣觉砾拘荀大如来、无所著、等正觉所。是时，觉砾拘荀大如来、无所著、等正觉无量百千眷属围遶而为说法。觉砾拘荀大如来、无所著、等正觉遥见弟子得奉敬、供养、礼事而来，见已，告诸比丘：『汝等见不？恶魔教勅梵志、居士：「汝等共来奉敬、供养、礼事精进沙门、或以奉敬、供养、礼事精进沙门，傥能起恶心，令我得其便。」比丘！汝等当观诸行无常，观兴衰法，观无欲，观舍离，观灭，观断，令恶魔求便而不能得。』波旬！觉砾拘荀大如来、无所著、等正觉以此教教诸弟子，彼即受教，观一切行无常，观兴衰法，观无欲，观舍离，观灭，观断，令恶魔求便而不能得。

Pāpīyas, at that time the evil Māra again thought: 'I seek an opportunity with the diligent śramaṇa, but I cannot find it. I should rather transform myself into a young man, holding a large staff in my hand, standing by the road, striking the head of Venerable Svara, causing it to break and blood to flow, staining his face.' Pāpīyas, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, Akṣobhya, later traveled to a village where he relied on. In the morning, he put on his robe, took his alms bowl, entered the village to beg for food, and Venerable Svara followed behind him. Pāpīyas, at that time, the evil Māra transformed himself into a young man, holding a large staff in his hand, standing by the road, striking the head of Venerable Svara, causing it to break and blood to flow, staining his face. Pāpīyas, after Venerable Svara's head was broken and blood flowed, he still followed behind the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, Akṣobhya, like a shadow never leaving.

「波旬！彼时恶魔复作是念：『我以此事求精进沙门便，而不能得。我宁可化作年少形，手执大杖，住其道边，打尊者音头，令破血流污面。』波旬！觉砾拘荀大如来、无所著、等正觉于后所依村邑游行，彼于平旦著衣持钵，入村乞食，尊者音在后侍从。波旬！尔时，恶魔化作年少形，手执大杖，住在道边，击尊者音头破血流污面。波旬！尊者音破头流血已，随从觉砾拘荀大如来、无所著、等正觉后，犹影不离。

"Pāpīyas, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, Akṣobhya, having arrived at the village, looked around with the full strength of his body, like a dragon, not afraid, not terrified, not startled, not fearful, observing the directions. Pāpīyas, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, Akṣobhya, saw Venerable Svara with a broken head, blood flowing and staining his face, following the Buddha, like a shadow never leaving, and said: 'This evil Māra is fierce and violent, with great power. This evil Māra does not know contentment.' Pāpīyas, before the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, Akṣobhya, had finished speaking, at that time, the evil Māra immediately fell into the great Avīci Hell. Pāpīyas, this great hell has four names: Avīci, Hundred Nails, Reverse Thorn, and Six More. There are wardens in that great hell who went to the evil Māra and said: 'You should know that if the nails and so forth are all together, it will be a hundred years full.'"

「波旬！觉砾拘荀大如来、无所著、等正觉至村邑已，极其身力右旋顾视，犹如龙视，不恐不怖，不惊不惧而观诸方。波旬！觉砾拘荀大如来、无所著、等正觉见尊者音头破血流污面，随佛后行，如影不离，便作是说：『此恶魔凶暴，大有威力，此恶魔不知厌足。』波旬！觉砾拘荀大如来、无所著、等正觉说语未讫，彼时，恶魔便于彼处，其身即堕无缺大地狱。波旬！此大地狱而有四名，一者无缺，二者百钉，三者逆刺，四者六更。彼大地狱其中有卒，往至恶魔所，语恶魔曰：『汝今当知，若钉钉等共合者，当知满百年。』」

Then, having heard this, Māra Pāpīyas was frightened, terrified, and his body hair stood on end. He spoke a verse to Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana:

于是，魔波旬闻说此已，即便心悸，恐怖惊惧，身毛皆竖，向尊者大目揵连即说颂曰：

How was it that in that hell, the evil Māra was
formerly in it, harassing the Buddha's pure conduct, and offending those bhikṣus?

「地狱名无缺，　　恶魔曾在中，娆害佛梵行，　　及犯彼比丘。彼铁钉有百，　　一切各逆刺，地狱名无缺，　　恶魔昔在中。若有不知者，　　比丘佛弟子，必得如是苦，　　受黑业之报。若干种园观，　　人者在于地，食自然粳米，　　居止在北洲。大须弥山岩，　　善修之所熏，修习于解脱，　　受持最后身。跱立在大泉，　　宫殿住至劫，金色可爱乐，　　犹火［火＊佥］晃昱。作诸众妓乐，　　往诣帝释所，本以一屋舍，　　善觉了为施。若释在前行，　　升毘阇延殿，见释大欢喜，　　天女各各舞。若见比丘来，　　还顾有惭愧，若毘阇延殿，　　见比丘问义。大仙颇能知，　　爱尽得解脱？比丘即为答，　　问者如其义。拘翼我能知，　　爱尽得解脱。闻彼之所答，　　释得欢喜乐。比丘多饶益，　　所说如其义。若毘阇延殿，　　问帝释天王，此殿名何等，　　汝释摄持城？释答大仙人，　　名毘阇延哆。是谓千世界，　　于千世界中，无有胜此殿，　　如毘阇延哆。天王天帝释，　　自在随所游，爱乐那游哆，　　化作一行百。毘阇延殿内，　　释得自在游，毘阇延大殿，　　足指能震动。天王眼所观，　　释得自在游。若鹿子母堂，　　筑基极深坚，难动不可震，　　如意足能摇。彼有琉璃地，　　圣人之所履，滑泽乐更触，　　布柔软绵褥。爱语共和合，　　天王常欢喜，善能作妓乐，　　音节善谐和，谓天来会聚，　　而说须陀洹。若干无量千，　　及百诸那术，至三十三天，　　慧眼者说法，闻彼所说法，　　欢喜而奉行。我亦有是法，　　如仙人所说，谓至梵天上，　　问彼梵天事，梵故有此见，　　谓见昔时有，我住有常存，　　恒有不变易。梵天为答彼，　　大仙我无见，谓见昔时有，　　我恒常不变，我见此境界，　　诸梵皆过去，我今何由说，　　恒常不变易？我见此世间，　　正觉之所说，随所因缘生，　　所往而转还，火无有思念，　　我烧愚痴人，火然若愚触，　　必自然得烧。如是汝波旬，　　触娆于如来，久作不善行，　　受报亦当久。魔汝莫厌佛，　　莫娆害比丘，一比丘降魔，　　住在于怖林。彼鬼愁忧戚，　　目连之所诃，恐怖无智慧，　　即于彼处没。」
