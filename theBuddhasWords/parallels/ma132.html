MĀ 132

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 11 大品

132. （一三二）赖咤惒罗经

Parallel to: MN 82

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in Kuru and Kaurava, together with a great assembly of bhikṣus. He went to Thūlu and stayed in the Śiṃśapā Grove north of the village of Thūlu.

一时，佛游拘楼瘦，与大比丘众俱，往至𨱎芦咤，住𨱎芦咤村北尸摄惒园中。

At that time, the brāhmaṇas and householders of Thūlu heard: "The śramaṇa Gautama, the son of the Śākya clan, having renounced his Śākya family and gone forth to practice the path, is traveling in Kuru and Kaurava, together with a great assembly of bhikṣus. He has come to Thūlu and is staying in the Śiṃśapā Grove north of the village of Thūlu. That śramaṇa Gautama has a great reputation that is heard in the ten directions. He is a Tathāgata, an Arhat, a Perfectly Enlightened One, accomplished in knowledge and conduct, a Sugata, an unsurpassed knower of the world, a charioteer of people to be tamed, a teacher of gods and men, a Buddha, a Blessed One. He has realized for himself and accomplished the supreme perfect enlightenment in this world, among gods, Māras, Brahmā, śramaṇas, and brāhmaṇas, from humans to gods. He teaches the Dharma that is good in the beginning, good in the middle, and good in the end, with the right meaning and phrasing, complete and pure, revealing the holy life. If one sees the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, and pays respect, makes offerings, and serves him, it is a great gain. We should all go together to see the śramaṇa Gautama and pay respect and make offerings to him."

尔时，𨱎芦咤梵志、居士闻：「沙门瞿昙释种子舍释宗族，出家学道，游拘楼瘦，与大比丘众俱，来至此𨱎芦咤，住𨱎芦咤村北尸摄惒园中。彼沙门瞿昙有大名称，周闻十方，沙门瞿昙如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐。彼于此世，天及魔、梵、沙门、梵志，从人至天，自知自觉，自作证成就游。彼若说法，初妙、中妙、竟亦妙，有义有文，具足清净，显现梵行。若见如来、无所著、等正觉，尊重礼拜，供养承事者，快得善利。我等应共往见沙门瞿昙礼拜供养。」

Having heard this, the brāhmaṇas and householders of Thūlu, each accompanied by their own kind of retinue, left Thūlu and went north to the Śiṃśapā Grove, wishing to see the World-Honored One, pay respect, and make offerings. Having approached the Buddha, some of the brāhmaṇas and householders of Thūlu bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet and then sat to one side, some greeted the Buddha and then sat to one side, some joined their palms towards the Buddha and then sat to one side, and some silently sat down upon seeing the Buddha from afar. At that time, when the brāhmaṇas and householders of Thūlu were each seated, the Buddha spoke the Dharma for them, inspiring them with yearning and bringing them to accomplishment and joy. Having used immeasurable skillful means to speak the Dharma for them, inspiring them with yearning and bringing them to accomplishment and joy, he remained silent. At that time, the brāhmaṇas and householders of Thūlu, having had the Dharma spoken for them by the Buddha, inspired with yearning and brought to accomplishment and joy, each rose from their seats, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated the Buddha three times, and departed.

𨱎芦咤梵志、居士闻已，各与等类眷属相随，从𨱎芦咤出，北行至尸摄惒园，欲见世尊礼拜、供养。往诣佛已，彼𨱎芦咤梵志、居士或稽首佛足，却坐一面，或问讯佛，却坐一面，或叉手向佛，却坐一面，或遥见佛已，默然而坐。彼时，𨱎芦咤梵志、居士各坐已定，佛为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，默然而住。时，𨱎芦咤梵志、居士，佛为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，各从坐起，稽首佛足，绕佛三匝而去。

At that time, the householder's son Rāṣṭrapāla remained seated. Then, not long after the brāhmaṇas and householders of Thūlu had left, the householder's son Rāṣṭrapāla rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said: "World-Honored One! According to my understanding of the Dharma taught by the Buddha, if I were to stay at home, I would be bound by fetters and would not be able to practice the pure holy life for the rest of my life. World-Honored One! I wish to go forth from the householder life to practice the path under the World-Honored One, to receive full ordination and become a bhikṣu, and to cultivate the pure holy life.

彼时，赖咤惒罗居士子故坐不起。于是，赖咤惒罗居士子，𨱎芦咤梵志、居士去后不久，即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！如我知佛所说法者，若我在家，为锁所锁，不得尽形寿清净行梵行。世尊！愿我得从世尊出家学道而受具足，得作比丘，净修梵行。」

The World-Honored One asked: "Householder's son! Do your parents allow you to go forth from the household life to practice the path in the true Dharma and Vinaya?"

世尊问曰：「居士子！父母听汝于正法、律中至信、舍家、无家、学道耶？」

The householder's son Rāṣṭrapāla said: "World-Honored One! My parents have not yet allowed me to go forth from the household life to practice the path in the true Dharma and Vinaya."

赖咤惒罗居士子白曰：「世尊！父母未听我于正法、律中至信、舍家、无家、学道。」

The World-Honored One said: "Householder's son! If your parents do not allow you to go forth from the household life to practice the path in the true Dharma and Vinaya, I cannot ordain you to practice the path.

世尊告曰：「居士子！若父母不听汝于正法、律中至信、舍家、无家、学道者，我不得度汝出家学道，亦不得受具足。」

The householder's son Rāṣṭrapāla said: "World-Honored One! I will find a way to ask my parents for permission to go forth from the household life to practice the path in the true Dharma and Vinaya."

赖咤惒罗居士子白曰：「世尊！我当方便从父母求，必令听我于正法、律中至信、舍家、无家、学道。」

The World-Honored One said: "Householder's son! Do as you wish."

世尊告曰：「居士子！随汝所欲。」

Then the householder's son Rāṣṭrapāla, having heard the Buddha's words, accepted them well and upheld them well. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and returned home. He said: "Venerable Ones! According to my understanding of the Dharma taught by the Buddha, if I stay at home, I will be bound by fetters and will not be able to practice the pure and holy life for the rest of my life. I only wish that the Venerable Ones would allow me to go forth from the household life to practice the path in the true Dharma and Vinaya."

于是，赖咤惒罗居士子闻佛所说，善受善持，稽首佛足，绕三匝还归。白曰：「二尊！如我知佛所说法者，若我在家，为锁所锁，不得尽形寿清净行梵行，唯愿二尊听我于正法、律中至信、舍家、无家、学道。」

Rāṣṭrapāla's parents said: "Rāṣṭrapāla! We only have one son, whom we love and cherish deeply. We are always delighted to see him and never tire of it. If you were to die, we would not want to abandon you, let alone be separated from you and not see you again."

赖咤惒罗父母告曰：「赖咤惒罗！我今唯有汝一子，极爱怜念，意常爱乐，见无厌足，若汝命终，我尚不欲相弃舍也，况生别离不见汝耶？」

The householder's son Rāṣṭrapāla then spoke a second and a third time, saying: "Venerable Ones! According to my understanding of the Dharma taught by the Buddha, if I stay at home, I will be bound by fetters and will not be able to practice the pure and holy life for the rest of my life. I only wish that the Venerable Ones would allow me to go forth from the household life to practice the path in the true Dharma and Vinaya."

赖咤惒罗居士子复至再三白曰：「二尊！如我知佛所说法者，若我在家，为锁所锁，不得尽形寿清净行梵行，唯愿二尊听我于正法、律中至信、舍家、无家、学道。」

Rāṣṭrapāla's parents also spoke a second and a third time, saying: "Rāṣṭrapāla! We only have one son, whom we love and cherish deeply. We are always delighted to see him and never tire of it. If you were to die, we would not want to abandon you, let alone be separated from you and not see you again."

赖咤惒罗居士子父母亦至再三告曰：「赖咤惒罗！我今唯有汝一子，极爱怜念，意常爱乐，见无厌足，若汝命终，我尚不欲相弃舍也，况生别离不见汝耶？」

Then Rāṣṭrapāla immediately lay down on the ground, saying: "From now on, I will not get up, not drink, not eat, until my parents allow me to go forth from the household life to practice the path in the true Dharma and Vinaya." Then Rāṣṭrapāla did not eat for one day, and then for two, three, four, and many days.

于是，赖咤惒罗居士子即时卧地：「从今不起，不饮不食，乃至父母听我于正法、律中至信、舍家、无家、学道。」于是，赖咤惒罗居士子一日不食，至二、三、四、多日不食。

Then Rāṣṭrapāla's parents went to their son and said: "Rāṣṭrapāla! You are very tender and your body is extremely good. You always sit and lie on good beds. Do you not know suffering now? Rāṣṭrapāla! You should quickly get up and practice giving and making merit. Why is that? Rāṣṭrapāla! The realm of the World-Honored One is very difficult, very difficult, and going forth to practice the path is also very difficult." At that time, Rāṣṭrapāla the householder's son remained silent and did not answer.

于是，赖咤惒罗居士子父母往至子所，告曰：「赖咤惒罗！汝至柔软，身体极好，常坐卧好床，汝今不知苦耶？赖咤惒罗！汝可速起，行欲布施，快修福业。所以者何？赖咤惒罗！世尊境界甚难甚难，出家学道亦复甚难。」尔时，赖咤惒罗居士子默然不答。

Then Rāṣṭrapāla's parents went to Rāṣṭrapāla's relatives and ministers and said: "You should all come to Rāṣṭrapāla and encourage him to get up from the ground."

于是，赖咤惒罗居士子父母往至赖咤惒罗亲亲及诸臣所，而作是语：「汝等共来至赖咤惒罗所，劝令从地起。」

Rāṣṭrapāla's relatives and ministers then went together to Rāṣṭrapāla and said: "Rāṣṭrapāla! You are very tender and your body is extremely good. You always sit and lie on good beds. Do you not know suffering now? Rāṣṭrapāla! You should quickly get up and practice giving and making merit. Why is that? The realm of the World-Honored One is very difficult, very difficult, and going forth to practice the path is also very difficult." At that time, Rāṣṭrapāla the householder's son remained silent and did not answer.

赖咤惒罗居士子亲亲及诸臣等即便共至赖咤惒罗所，语曰：「赖咤惒罗！汝至柔软，身体极好，常坐卧好床，汝今不知苦耶？赖咤惒罗！汝可速起，行欲布施，快修福业。所以者何？世尊境界甚难甚难，出家学道亦复甚难。」彼时，赖咤惒罗居士子默然不答。

Then Rāṣṭrapāla's parents went to Rāṣṭrapāla's good friends, companions, and contemporaries and said: "You should all come to Rāṣṭrapāla and encourage him to get up from the ground."

于是，赖咤惒罗居士子父母至赖咤惒罗居士子善知识、同伴、同时所，而作是语：「汝等共来至赖咤惒罗所，劝令从地起。」

Then Rāṣṭrapāla's good friends, companions, and contemporaries went together to Rāṣṭrapāla the householder's son and said: "Rāṣṭrapāla! You are very tender and your body is extremely good. You always sit and lie on good beds. Do you not know suffering now? Rāṣṭrapāla! You should quickly get up and practice giving and making merit. Why is that? Rāṣṭrapāla! The realm of the World-Honored One is very difficult, very difficult, and going forth to practice the path is also very difficult." At that time, Rāṣṭrapāla the householder's son remained silent and did not answer.

于是，赖咤惒罗居士子善知识、同伴、同时即共往诣赖咤惒罗居士子所，而作是语：「赖咤惒罗！汝至柔软，身体极好，常坐卧好床，汝今不知苦耶？赖咤惒罗！汝可速起，行欲布施，快修福业。所以者何？赖咤惒罗！世尊境界甚难甚难，出家学道亦复甚难。」彼时，赖咤惒罗居士子默然不答。

Then Rāṣṭrapāla's good friends, companions, and contemporaries went to Rāṣṭrapāla the householder's son's parents and said: "Please allow Rāṣṭrapāla to go forth and practice the path in the true Dharma and Vinaya. If he is happy, he can still see you in this life; if he is not happy, he will definitely come back to his parents. If you do not allow it now, he will definitely die without a doubt. What benefit is there in that?"

于是，赖咤惒罗居士子善知识、同伴、同时往至赖咤惒罗居士子父母所，作如是语：「可听赖咤惒罗于正法、律中至信、舍家、无家、学道，若其乐者，于此生中，故可相见；若不乐者，必自来还归父母所。今若不听，定死无疑，当何所益？」

Then Rāṣṭrapāla the householder's son's parents heard and said to Rāṣṭrapāla the householder's son's good friends, companions, and contemporaries: "We now allow Rāṣṭrapāla to go forth and practice the path in the true Dharma and Vinaya. If he comes back after practicing the path, we can still see him."

于是，赖咤惒罗居士子父母闻已，语赖咤惒罗居士子善知识、同伴、同时曰：「我今听赖咤惒罗于正法、律中至信、舍家、无家、学道，若学道来还，故可见也。」

Rāṣṭrapāla the householder's son's good friends, companions, and contemporaries then went together to Rāṣṭrapāla and said: "Householder's son! Your parents allow you to go forth and practice the path in the true Dharma and Vinaya. If you practice the path, you can still see your parents."

赖咤惒罗居士子善知识、同伴、同时即共往诣赖咤惒罗所，便作是语：「居士子！父母听汝于正法、律中至信、舍家、无家、学道，若学道已，还见父母。」

Having heard these words, Rāṣṭrapāla the householder's son was greatly delighted, joyful, and happy. He got up from the ground, gradually nourished his body, and when his body was healed, he left Trāyastriṃśa and went to where the Buddha was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and said: "World-Honored One! My parents allow me to go forth and practice the path in the true Dharma and Vinaya. I only wish that the World-Honored One would allow me to go forth and practice the path under the World-Honored One, receive full ordination, and become a bhikṣu."

赖咤惒罗居士子闻是语已，便大欢喜，生爱生乐，从地而起，渐养其身，身平复已，从𨱎芦咤出，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，白曰：「世尊！父母听我于正法、律中至信、舍家、无家、学道，唯愿世尊！听从世尊出家学道而受具足，得作比丘。」

Then the World-Honored One allowed Rāṣṭrapāla the householder's son to go forth and practice the path, gave him full ordination, and after giving him full ordination, stayed in Trāyastriṃśa for some time. Later, he put on his robe, took his bowl, and traveled to Śrāvastī, staying in the Jeta Grove, Anāthapiṇḍada's Park. Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla, having gone forth and received full ordination, stayed in seclusion, dwelling alone, without negligence, practicing diligently. Having stayed in seclusion, dwelling alone, without negligence, practicing diligently, he, a clansman who had shaved off his hair and beard, put on the saffron robes, and with true faith, left home for homelessness, only to complete the unsurpassed holy life, to know and realize for himself, to dwell having personally accomplished it, to end the cycle of rebirth, to establish the holy life, to do what needs to be done, to not depend on others for the knowledge that birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, there is no more of this mundane state - Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla, having known the Dharma, attained arahantship.

于是，世尊度赖咤惒罗居士子出家学道，授其具足，授具足已，于𨱎芦咤随住数时，于后则便摄衣持钵，游行展转，往至舍卫国，住胜林给孤独园。尊者赖咤惒罗出家学道，受具足已，在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精懃，彼在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精懃已。族姓子所为剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，唯无上梵行讫，于现法中自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。尊者赖咤惒罗知法已，至得阿罗诃。

Then Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla, having attained arahantship, thought after nine or ten years: "I originally promised to go home and see my parents after going forth and practicing the path. I should now fulfill that promise." Then Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said: "World-Honored One! I originally promised to go home and see my parents after going forth and practicing the path. World-Honored One! I now wish to take leave and go home to see my parents, fulfilling that promise."

于是，尊者赖咤惒罗得阿罗诃已后或九年十年，而作是念：「我本已许出家学道还见父母，我今宁可还赴本要。」于是，尊者赖咤惒罗往诣佛所，稽首佛足，却坐一面，白曰：「世尊！我本有要，出家学道已，还见父母。世尊！我今辞行，往见父母，赴其本要。」

At that time, the World-Honored One had this thought: "If this Rāṣṭrapāla clansman were to abandon the precepts, return to the household life, and engage in desires as before, it would not be possible." Knowing this, the World-Honored One said: "Go! Liberate those who are not liberated, free those who are not free, and extinguish those who have not extinguished. Rāṣṭrapāla! Now do as you wish."

尔时，世尊便作是念：「此赖咤惒罗族姓子，若使舍戒、罢道行欲如本者，必无是处。」世尊知已，告曰：「汝去！未度者度，未解脱者令得解脱，未灭讫者令得灭讫。赖咤惒罗！今随汝意。」

At that time, Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla, having heard the Buddha's teaching, accepted it well and upheld it well. He then rose from his seat, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and departed. Arriving at his own room, he packed his bedding, put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and traveled on to Tārāvat, staying in the Śiṃśapā Grove north of the village. Then, Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla, having passed the night, put on his robe, took his alms bowl, entered Tārāvat, and went for alms. Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla thought: "The World-Honored One praises begging for alms in sequence. I should now beg for alms in sequence in this Tārāvat." Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla then begged for alms in sequence in Tārāvat, gradually arriving at his own home.

彼时，尊者赖咤惒罗闻佛所说，善受善持，即从坐起，稽首佛足，绕三匝而去。至己房中，收举卧具，著衣持钵，游行展转，往至𨱎芦咤，住𨱎芦咤村北尸摄惒园。于是，尊者赖咤惒罗过夜平旦，著衣持钵，入𨱎芦咤而行乞食。尊者赖咤惒罗作如是念：「世尊称叹次第乞食，我今宁可于此𨱎芦咤次第乞食。」尊者赖咤惒罗便于𨱎芦咤次第乞食，展转至本家。

At that time, Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla's father was standing in the middle gate, grooming his beard and hair. Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla's father saw Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla coming from afar and said: "This shaven-headed śramaṇa is bound by darkness, cutting off the lineage and making it childless, destroying my family. I have a son whom I love and cherish, always willing to endure and enjoy, seeing him without ever tiring. He took him away to ordain. Do not give him food."

Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla said to his former wife: "Sisters! You should know that I do not practice the holy life for the sake of heavenly maidens. The meaning of what I practice is already attained, and what the Buddha taught me to do is now accomplished."'

尊者赖咤惒罗诸妇等却住一面，啼泣埀泪而作是语：「我非贤郎妹，然贤郎唤我为妹。」

Then the hunter searched the Tulasī forest and saw Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla sitting cross-legged under a Vibhītaka tree, spreading out his sitting mat. Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla, the son of the Rāṣṭrapāla clan, is now under a Vibhītaka tree in the Tulasī forest, spreading out his sitting mat and sitting cross-legged. The charioteer, having received the order, quickly prepared the chariot and returned to report, "Great King! You should know that the chariot is ready. Please do as you wish."

拘牢婆王曰：「今我虽到自己境界，然我意欲令赖咤惒罗族姓子请我令坐。」

I will give you wealth, Rāṣṭrapāla, and persuade you to abandon the precepts and return to lay life. Enjoy the pleasures of the senses and quickly practice giving and making merit. Why is this? If a person is long sick and afflicted, extremely heavy and very painful, they think, 'I am long sick and afflicted, extremely heavy and very painful. I really have desires, but I cannot act on them. I should now shave off hair and beard, put on the dyed robe, leave home out of faith, and go forth to practice the path.' Later, because of illness decline, they shave off hair and beard, put on the dyed robe, leave home out of faith, and go forth to practice the path. This is called illness decline.

「复次，赖咤惒罗！有四种衰，谓衰衰故，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。云何为四？病衰、老衰、财衰、亲衰。赖咤惒罗！云何病衰？或有一人长病疾患，极重甚苦，彼作是念：『我长病疾患，极重甚苦，我实有欲，不能行欲，我今宁可剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。』彼于后时以病衰故，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，是为病衰。

Rāṣṭrapāla, what is old age decline? If a person is old, with aged faculties, and their life is almost over, they think, 'I am old, with aged faculties, and my life is almost over. I really have desires, but I cannot act on them. I should now shave off hair and beard, put on the dyed robe, leave home out of faith, and go forth to practice the path.' Later, because of old age decline, they shave off hair and beard, put on the dyed robe, leave home out of faith, and go forth to practice the path. This is called old age decline.

「赖咤惒罗！云何老衰？或有一人年耆根熟，寿过垂讫，彼作是念：『我年耆根熟，寿过埀讫，我实有欲，不能行欲，我今宁可剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。』彼于后时以老衰故，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，是谓老衰。

Rāṣṭrapāla, what is wealth decline? If a person is poor and powerless, they think, 'I am poor and powerless. I should now shave off hair and beard, put on the dyed robe, leave home out of faith, and go forth to practice the path.' Later, because of wealth decline, they shave off hair and beard, put on the dyed robe, leave home out of faith, and go forth to practice the path. This is called wealth decline.

「赖咤惒罗！云何财衰？或有一人贫穷无力，彼作是念：『我贫穷无力，我今宁可剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。』彼于后时以财衰故，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，是谓财衰。

Rāṣṭrapāla, what is family decline? If a person's family line is cut off, and they have died out, they think, 'My family line is cut off, and they have died out. I should now shave off hair and beard, put on the dyed robe, leave home out of faith, and go forth to practice the path.' Later, because of family decline, they shave off hair and beard, put on the dyed robe, leave home out of faith, and go forth to practice the path. This is called family decline.

「赖咤惒罗！云何亲衰？或有一人亲里断种，死亡没尽，彼作是念：『我亲里断种，死亡没尽，我今宁可剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。』彼于后时以亲衰故，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，是谓亲衰。

Rāṣṭrapāla, in the past you were healthy, free from illness, with a balanced diet, neither cold nor hot, peaceful and comfortable, in harmony and without conflict. Therefore, your food was digested easily and you were able to transform peacefully. Rāṣṭrapāla did not shave off hair and beard, put on the dyed robe, leave home out of faith, and go forth to practice the path because of illness.

「赖咤惒罗昔时无病，安隐成就，平等食道，不冷不热，平正安乐，顺次不诤，由是之故，食噉含消，安隐得化。赖咤惒罗非以病衰故，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。

Rāṣṭrapāla, in the past you were young, with black and pure hair, and your body was strong and healthy. At that time, you were a performer, amusing yourself with music, adorning your body, and always enjoying games. At that time, your relatives did not want you to study the path. Your parents wept, were distressed and upset, and did not allow you to leave home to study the path. Yet you shaved off hair and beard, put on the dyed robe, left home out of faith, and went forth to practice the path. Rāṣṭrapāla did not shave off hair and beard, put on the dyed robe, leave home out of faith, and go forth to practice the path because of old age.

「赖咤惒罗往昔之时，年幼童子，发黑清净，身体盛壮，尔时作倡伎乐，极以自娱，庄严其身，常喜游戏。彼时亲属皆不欲使令其学道，父母啼泣，忧戚懊恼，亦不听汝出家学道。然汝剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。赖咤惒罗不以老衰故，剃除] cf.［丽】须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。

Rāṣṭrapāla, this is the first, best, supreme, and highest family of Tṛṣṇas, namely, wealth. Rāṣṭrapāla did not shave off hair and beard, put on the dyed robe, leave home out of faith, and go forth to practice the path because of the decline of wealth.

「赖咤惒罗！此𨱎芦咤第一家、最大家、最胜家、最上家，谓财物也。赖咤惒罗！不以财衰故，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。

Rāṣṭrapāla, this is the forest of Tṛṣṇas, where the great and noble relatives are all present. Rāṣṭrapāla did not shave off hair and beard, put on the dyed robe, leave home out of faith, and go forth to practice the path because of the decline of relatives.

「赖咤惒罗！此𨱎芦咤林间，大豪亲族亲皆存在，赖咤惒罗不以亲衰故，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。

"Rāṣṭrapāla, these four kinds of decline may cause some to shave off hair and beard, put on the dyed robe, leave home out of faith, and go forth to practice the path. I see that Rāṣṭrapāla has none of these declines. I can make Rāṣṭrapāla shave off hair and beard, put on the dyed robe, leave home out of faith, and go forth to practice the path. Rāṣṭrapāla, what knowledge and views do you have, and what have you heard, that you shave off hair and beard, put on the dyed robe, leave home out of faith, and go forth to practice the path?"

「赖咤惒罗，此四种衰，或有衰者，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。我见赖咤惒罗都无此衰，可使赖咤惒罗剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。赖咤惒罗！知见何等，为闻何等，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道？」

Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla replied, "Great King! The World-Honored One, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, spoke of four things that I wish to endure and enjoy. I know, see, and hear these, and therefore I shave off hair and beard, put on the dyed robe, leave home out of faith, and go forth to practice the path. What are the four? Great King! This world has no protector, no one to rely on. This world is heading towards old age. This world is impermanent and will be left behind. This world is not full and has no satisfaction. It is driven by craving.

尊者赖咤惒罗答曰：「大王！世尊知、见，如来、无所著、等正觉为说四事，我欲忍乐是，我知见闻是，是故剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。云何为四？大王！此世无护，无可依恃，此世一切趣向老法，此世非常，要当舍去，此世无满，无有厌足，为爱走使。」

King Kālāma asked, "Rāṣṭrapāla! What you said just now, 'Great King! This world has no protector, no one to rely on.' Rāṣṭrapāla! I have sons, grandsons, brothers, relatives, elephant troops, chariot troops, cavalry, and infantry, all skilled in archery and riding, strict, brave, princes, and strongmen. There are astrologers, strategists, calculators, those skilled in writing, and those skilled in debate. There are rulers and ministers, and relatives who know mantras and spells. They can subdue any fear in any direction. If what Rāṣṭrapāla said is true, 'Great King! This world has no protector, no one to rely on.' What is the meaning of what Rāṣṭrapāla said just now?"

Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla replied, "Great King! I will ask the king a question, and the king can answer as he pleases. Great King! Does this body ever get sick?"

尊者赖咤惒罗答曰：「大王！我今问王，随所解答。大王！此身颇有病耶？」

King Kālāma replied, "Rāṣṭrapāla! My body always has wind illness."

拘牢婆王答曰：「赖咤惒罗！今我此身常有风病。」

Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla asked, "Great King! When the wind illness flares up, causing extreme pain, Great King! Can you then tell your grandchildren, brothers, elephant troops, horse troops, chariot troops, infantry troops who are all skilled in archery and horsemanship, stern and brave, princes, strongmen, Tilo-khenti Mahā-naga, astrologers, strategists, calculators, those who know writing, those who are good at debate, ministers, relatives, those who hold mantras and know mantras, 'Come together and temporarily take on my extreme pain for me, so that I may be free from illness and at ease'?"

尊者赖咤惒罗问曰：「大王！风病发时，生极重甚苦者，大王！尔时可得语彼儿孙、兄弟、象军、马军、车军、步军皆能射御，严毅勇猛，王子、力士，𨱎逻骞提摩诃能伽，占相、策虑、计算、知书、善能谈论、君臣、眷属，持呪知呪，汝等共来暂代我受极重甚苦，令我无病得安乐耶？」

King Kālāma replied, "No. Why? Because I myself created the karma, and due to the causes and conditions of karma, I alone will experience the extreme suffering, the very heavy suffering."

拘牢婆王答曰：「不也。所以者何？我自作业，因业缘业，独受极苦，甚重苦也。」

Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla said, "Great King! For this reason, the World-Honored One said that in this world there is no protector, no one to rely on. I endure and enjoy this, I know, see, and hear this, therefore I shave my beard and hair, put on the monastic robe, with utmost faith, leave home, become homeless, and study the path."

尊者赖咤惒罗语曰：「大王！以是故，世尊说此世无护，无可依恃。我欲忍乐是，我知见闻是，是故剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。」

King Kālāma said, "Rāṣṭrapāla, if you say, 'Great King! In this world there is no protector, no one to rely on,' Rāṣṭrapāla, I also want to endure and enjoy this. Why? Because in this world there truly is no protector, no one to rely on."

拘牢婆王语曰：「若赖咤惒罗所说：『大王！此世无护，无可依恃。』赖咤惒罗！我亦欲是忍乐于是。所以者何？此世真实无护，无可依恃。」

King Kālāma further asked, "Rāṣṭrapāla, if you say, 'Great King! In this world, everything tends towards old age,' what is the meaning of what you said before?"

拘牢婆王复问曰：「若赖咤惒罗所说：『大王！此世一切趣向老法。』赖咤惒罗向所说，此复有何义？」

Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla replied, "Great King! I ask you, please answer as you see fit. If the Great King was twenty-four or twenty-five years old, what do you think? How is your speed now compared to then? How is your strength, body, and complexion now compared to then?"

尊者赖咤惒罗答曰：「大王！我今问王，随所解答。若大王年或二十四、或二十五者，于意云何？尔时速疾何如于今？尔时筋力、形体、颜色何如于今？」

King Kālāma replied, "Rāṣṭrapāla! If I was twenty-four or twenty-five years old, I remember that at that time, no one was faster, stronger, or more handsome than me. Rāṣṭrapāla! Now I am very old, my faculties are declining, my life is almost over, and I am eighty years old. I can no longer stand up."

拘牢婆王答曰：「赖咤惒罗！若我时年或二十四、或二十五，自忆尔时，速疾、筋力、形体、颜色无胜我者。赖咤惒罗！我今极老，诸根衰熟，寿过垂讫，年满八十，不复能起。」

Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla said, "Great King! For this reason, the World-Honored One said that all beings in this world are heading towards old age. I endure and enjoy this, I know, see, and hear this. Therefore, I shave my beard and hair, wear the monastic robe, leave home out of true faith, become a homeless monk, and practice the path."

尊者赖咤惒罗语曰：「大王！以是故，世尊说此世一切趣向老法。我欲忍乐是，我知见闻是，是故剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。」

King Kālāma said, "If Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla says, 'Great King! All beings in this world are heading towards old age,' I also endure and enjoy this. Why is that? It is true that all beings in this world are heading towards old age."

拘牢婆王语曰：「若赖咤惒罗所说：『大王！此世一切趣向老法。』我亦欲是忍乐于是。所以者何？此世真实一切趣向老法。」

Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla said, "Great King! I ask the king a question. Please answer as you see fit. Great King! Is there a prosperous country of Kuṇḍalā, a prosperous inner palace, and prosperous granaries?"

尊者赖咤惒罗语曰：「大王！我今问王，随所解答。大王！有丰拘楼国及丰后宫、丰仓库耶？」

King Kālāma replied, "Yes, there is."

拘牢婆王答曰：「如是。」

Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla further asked, "Great King! If there is a prosperous country of Kuṇḍalā, a prosperous inner palace, and prosperous granaries, but when the Dharma comes, one cannot rely on it, and one endures destruction, and all in the world will die and return to nothing. At that time, can the prosperous country of Kuṇḍalā, the prosperous inner palace, and the prosperous granaries be brought from this world to the next world?"

尊者赖咤惒罗复问曰：「大王！有丰拘楼国及丰后宫、丰仓库者，若时有法来不可依，忍乐破坏，一切世无不归死者。尔时丰拘楼国及丰后宫、丰仓库者，可得从此世持至后世耶？」

「我见世间人，　　有财痴不施，得财复更求，　　悭贪积聚物。王者得天下，　　整御随其力，海内无厌足，　　复求于海外。王及诸人民，　　未离欲命尽，散发妻子哭，　　呜呼苦难伏。衣被而埋藏，　　或积薪火烧，缘行至后世，　　烧已无慧念。死后财不随，　　妻子及奴婢，贫] cf.［丽】富俱共同，　　愚智亦复然。智者不怀忧，　　唯愚抱悒戚，是故智慧胜，　　逮得正觉道。深著于有有，　　愚痴作恶行，于法非法行，　　以力强夺他。少智习効他，　　愚多作恶行，趣胎至后世，　　数数受生死。已受出生世，　　独作众恶事，如贼他所缚，　　自作恶所害。如是此众生，　　至到于后世，为己所作业，　　自作恶所害。如果熟自堕，　　老少亦如斯，欲庄美爱乐，　　心趣好恶色。为欲所缚害，　　因欲恐怖生，王我见此觉，　　知是沙门妙。」

Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla spoke thus. King Prasenajit, having heard Venerable Rāṣṭrapāla's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

尊者赖咤惒罗所说如是。拘牢婆王闻尊者赖咤惒罗所说，欢喜奉行。
