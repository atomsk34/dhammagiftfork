MĀ 133

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 11 大品

133. （一三三）优婆离经

Parallel to: MN 56

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the country of Nandi, staying in the Pāpāṇaka forest.

一时，佛游那难陀，在波婆离［木＊奈］林。

At that time, the ascetic Nirgrantha, after wandering about in the middle of the day, went to where the Buddha was, exchanged greetings, and sat to one side. Then the World-Honored One asked: "Nirgrantha, how many practices do you establish to prevent people from doing evil and not committing evil deeds?"

尔时，长苦行尼揵中后彷佯，往诣佛所，共相问讯，却坐一面。于是，世尊问曰：「苦行尼揵亲子施设几行，令不行恶业，不作恶业？」

The ascetic Nirgrantha replied: "Gautama! Our teacher Nirgrantha does not establish any practices for us to prevent us from doing evil and not committing evil deeds. He only establishes punishments for us to prevent us from doing evil and not committing evil deeds."

长苦行尼揵答曰：「瞿昙！我尊师尼揵亲子不为我等施设于行，令不行恶业，不作恶业。但为我等施设于罚，令不行恶业，不作恶业。」

The World-Honored One again asked: "Nirgrantha, how many punishments does Nirgrantha establish to prevent people from doing evil and not committing evil deeds?"

世尊又复问曰：「苦行尼揵亲子施设几罚，令不行恶业，不作恶业？」

The ascetic Nirgrantha replied: "Gautama! Our teacher Nirgrantha establishes three punishments for us to prevent us from doing evil and not committing evil deeds. What are the three? Physical punishment, verbal punishment, and mental punishment."

长苦行尼揵答曰：「瞿昙！我尊师尼揵亲子为我等辈施设三罚，令不行恶业，不作恶业。云何为三？身罚、口罚及意罚也。」

The World-Honored One again asked: "Ascetic! How are physical punishment, verbal punishment, and mental punishment different from each other?"

世尊又复问曰：「苦行！云何身罚异、口罚异、意罚异耶？」

The ascetic Nirgrantha replied: "Gautama! Our physical punishment, verbal punishment, and mental punishment are different from each other."

长苦行尼揵答曰：「瞿昙！我等身罚异、口罚异、意罚异也。」

The World-Honored One again asked: "Ascetic! These three punishments are similar in this way. What punishment does Nirgrantha Jñātiputra establish as the heaviest, to prevent one from engaging in evil deeds and not creating evil karma? Is it physical punishment, verbal punishment, or mental punishment?"

世尊又复问曰：「苦行！此三罚如是相似，尼揵亲子施设何罚为最重，令不行恶业，不作恶业？为身罚、口罚，为意罚耶？」

The ascetic Nirgrantha replied: "Gautama! These three punishments are similar in this way. My teacher Nirgrantha Jñātiputra establishes physical punishment as the heaviest, to prevent one from engaging in evil deeds and not creating evil karma. Verbal punishment is not so, and mental punishment is the lowest, not as heavy as physical punishment."

长苦行尼揵答曰：「瞿昙！此三罚如是相似，我尊师尼揵亲子施设身罚为最重，令不行恶业] cf.［丽】，不作恶业。口罚不然，意罚最下，不及身罚极大甚重。」

The World-Honored One again asked: "Ascetic! You say that physical punishment is the heaviest?"

世尊又复问曰：「苦行！汝说身罚为最重耶？」

The ascetic Nirgrantha replied: "Gautama! Physical punishment is the heaviest."

长苦行尼揵答曰：「瞿昙！身罚最重。」

The World-Honored One again asked three times: "Ascetic! You say that physical punishment is the heaviest?"

世尊复再三问曰：「苦行！汝说身罚为最重耶？」

The ascetic Nirgrantha also answered three times: "Gautama! Physical punishment is the heaviest." Then, after the World-Honored One had repeatedly confirmed this matter with the ascetic Nirgrantha, he remained silent.

长苦行尼揵亦再三答曰：「瞿昙！身罚最重。」于是，世尊再三审定长苦行尼揵如此事已，便默然住。

The ascetic Nirgrantha asked: "śramaṇa Gautama, how many punishments do you establish to prevent people from doing evil and creating evil karma?"

长苦行尼揵问曰：「沙门瞿昙施设几罚，令不行恶业，不作恶业？」

At that time, the World-Honored One replied: "Ascetic! I do not establish punishments to prevent people from doing evil and creating evil karma. I only establish karma to prevent people from doing evil and creating evil karma."

尔时，世尊答曰：「苦行！我不施设罚，令不行恶业，不作恶业。我但施设业，令不行恶业，不作恶业。」

The ascetic Nirgrantha asked: "Gautama, how many karmas do you establish to prevent people from doing evil and creating evil karma?"

长苦行尼揵问曰：「瞿昙施设几业，令不行恶业，不作恶业。」

The World-Honored One again replied: "Ascetic! I establish three karmas to prevent people from doing evil and creating evil karma. What are the three? Karma of the body, karma of speech, and karma of the mind."

世尊又复答曰：「苦行！我施设三业，令不行恶业，不作恶业。云何为三？身业、口业及意业也。」

The ascetic Nirgrantha asked: "Gautama, is the karma of the body different from the karma of speech, and is the karma of the mind different from the karma of speech?"

长] cf.［丽】苦行尼揵问曰：「瞿昙！身业异、口业异、意业异耶？」

The World-Honored One again replied: "Ascetic! My karma of the body is different from the karma of speech, and my karma of the mind is different from the karma of speech."

世尊又复答曰：「苦行！我身业异、口业异、意业异也。」

The ascetic Nirgrantha asked: "Gautama, these three karmas are similar in this way. Which karma do you establish as the heaviest, so that one does not engage in evil karma and does not create evil karma? Is it the karma of the body or the karma of speech, or the karma of the mind?"

长苦行尼揵问曰：「瞿昙！此三业如是相似，施设何业为最重，令不行恶业，不作恶业？为身业、口业，为意业耶？」

The World-Honored One again replied: "Ascetic! These three karmas are similar in this way. I establish the karma of the mind as the heaviest, so that one does not engage in evil karma and does not create evil karma. The karma of the body and the karma of speech are not like that."

世尊又复答曰：「苦行！此三业如是相似，我施设意业为最重，令不行恶业，不作恶业。身业、口业则不然也。」

The ascetic Nirgrantha asked: "Gautama, do you establish the karma of the mind as the heaviest?"

长苦行尼揵问曰：「瞿昙！施设意业为最重耶？」

The World-Honored One again replied: "Ascetic! I establish the karma of the mind as the heaviest."

世尊又复答曰：「苦行！我施设意业为最重也。」

The ascetic Nirgrantha asked again and again: "Gautama, do you establish the karma of the mind as the heaviest?" The World-Honored One also answered again and again: "Ascetic! I establish the karma of the mind as the heaviest." Then the ascetic Nirgrantha, having ascertained this matter from the World-Honored One three times, rose from his seat, circumambulated the World-Honored One three times, and departed, going to where Nirgrantha Jñātiputra was.

长苦行尼揵复再三问曰：「瞿昙！施设意业为最重耶？」世尊亦再三答曰：「苦行！我施设意业为最重也。」于是，长苦行尼揵再三审定世尊如此事已，即从座起，绕世尊三匝而退还去，往诣尼揵亲子所。

Nirgrantha Jñātiputra saw from afar the ascetic Nirgrantha coming and asked: "Ascetic! Where are you coming from?"

尼揵亲子遥见长苦行尼揵来，即便问曰：「苦行！从何处来？」

The ascetic Nirgrantha replied: "Venerable! I am coming from the place of the śramaṇa Gautama in the Nandana Grove."

长苦行尼揵答曰：「尊！我从那难陀波婆离［木＊奈］林沙门瞿昙处来。」

Nirgrantha Jñātiputra asked: "Ascetic! Did you have any discussion with the śramaṇa Gautama?"

尼揵亲子问曰：「苦行！颇共沙门瞿昙有所论耶？」

The ascetic Nirgrantha replied: "We did discuss."

长苦行尼揵答曰：「共论。」

Nirgrantha Jñātiputra said: "Ascetic! If you had any discussion with the śramaṇa Gautama, please tell me all about it. Perhaps I can understand what they discussed."

尼揵亲子告曰：「苦行！若共沙门瞿昙有所论者，尽为我说，我或能知彼之所论。」

Then the ascetic Nirgrantha told Jñātiputra everything he had discussed with the World-Honored One. Upon hearing it, Jñātiputra praised: "Excellent! Ascetic! You practice the Dharma of a disciple towards your teacher. Your wisdom and eloquence are clear and decisive, peaceful, fearless, and well-controlled. You have attained great eloquence and the banner of immortality, and have personally realized and accomplished the state of immortality. Why is that? Because you have told the śramaṇa Gautama that the heaviest punishment is to punish the body, to prevent one from doing evil and creating evil karma. Punishment of speech is not as good, and punishment of the mind is the lightest, not as heavy as punishment of the body."

于是，长苦行尼揵共世尊有所论者尽向彼说，尼揵亲子闻便叹曰：「善哉！苦行！谓汝于师行弟子法，所作智辩聪明决定，安隐无畏成就调御，逮大辩才，得甘露幢，于甘露界自作证成就游。所以者何？谓汝向沙门瞿昙施设身罚为最重，令不行恶业，不作恶业。口罚不然，意罚最下，不及身罚极大甚重。」

At that time, the householder Upāli was sitting in the assembly with five hundred householders, with his hands folded towards Jñātiputra. Then the householder Upāli said to the ascetic Nirgrantha: "Venerable one, have you repeatedly confirmed this matter with the śramaṇa Gautama?"

是时，优婆离居士与五百居士俱集在众中，叉手向尼揵亲子。于是，优婆离居士语长苦行尼揵曰：「尊已再三审定沙门瞿昙如此事耶？」

The ascetic Nirgrantha replied: "Householder, I have repeatedly confirmed this matter with the śramaṇa Gautama."

长苦行尼揵答曰：「居士！我已再三审定沙门瞿昙如此事也。」

The householder Upāli said to the ascetic Nirgrantha: "I can also repeatedly confirm this matter with the śramaṇa Gautama, and follow his lead. Just as a strong man would take a long-haired sheep and lead it wherever he wants, I can also repeatedly confirm this matter with the śramaṇa Gautama and follow his lead. Just as a strong man would take a long-haired sheep and shake it to remove the dust, I can also repeatedly confirm this matter with the śramaṇa Gautama and follow his lead. Just as a wine seller or his disciple would take a wine strainer and put it in deep water, and lead it wherever they want, I can also repeatedly confirm this matter with the śramaṇa Gautama and follow his lead. Just as a sixty-year-old dragon elephant king, with its arrogance, great strength, and fully developed tusks and limbs, would be led by a strong man to wash its thighs, back, sides, belly, tusks, head, and play in the water, I can also repeatedly confirm this matter with the śramaṇa Gautama and follow his lead. I will go to where the śramaṇa Gautama is, discuss with him, and then return after defeating him."

优婆离居士语长苦行尼揵曰：「我亦能至再三审定沙门瞿昙如此事已，随所牵挽。犹如力士执长髦羊，随所牵挽。我亦如是，能至再三审定沙门瞿昙如此事已，随所牵挽。犹如力士手执髦裘，抖擞去尘。我亦如是，能至再三审定沙门瞿昙如此事已，随所牵挽。犹如沽酒师、沽酒弟子取漉酒囊，著深水中，随意所欲，随所牵挽。我亦如是，能至再三审定沙门瞿昙如此事已，随所牵挽。犹龙象王年满六十，而以憍傲摩诃能加牙足体具，筋力炽盛，力士将去以水洗髀、洗脊、洗脇、洗腹、洗牙、洗头及水中戏。我亦如是，能至再三审定沙门瞿昙如此事已，随其所洗。我往诣沙门瞿昙所，共彼谈论，降伏已还。」

The son of Nirgrantha said to the householder Upāli: "I can also defeat the śramaṇa Gautama, and you can also, and the ascetic Nirgrantha can also."

尼揵亲子语优婆离居士曰：「我亦可伏沙门瞿昙，汝亦可也，长苦行尼揵亦可也。」

Then the ascetic Nirgrantha said to the son of Nirgrantha: "I do not want the householder Upāli to go to where the śramaṇa Gautama is. Why? The śramaṇa Gautama knows the spell of illusion and can conjure up disciples, bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, and upāsikās. I am afraid that the householder Upāli will be transformed by the śramaṇa Gautama and become his disciple."

于是，长苦行尼揵白尼揵亲子曰：「我不欲令优婆离居士往诣沙门瞿昙所。所以者何？沙门瞿昙知幻化呪，能呪化作弟子，比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆私，恐优婆离居士受沙门瞿昙化，化作弟子。」

The son of Nirgrantha said: "Ascetic! If the householder Upāli becomes a disciple transformed by the śramaṇa Gautama, it is impossible. If the śramaṇa Gautama becomes a disciple transformed by the householder Upāli, it is possible."

尼揵亲子语曰：「苦行！若优婆离居士受沙门瞿昙化作弟子者，终无是处。若沙门瞿昙受优婆离居士化作弟子者，必有是处。」

The householder Upāli said to the son of Nirgrantha for the third time: "I will now go to where the śramaṇa Gautama is, discuss with him, and return after defeating him."

优婆离居士再三白尼揵亲子曰：「我今往诣沙门瞿昙所，共彼谈论，降伏已还。」

The son of Nirgrantha also answered for the third time: "You should go quickly! I can also subdue the śramaṇa Gautama, and you can also, and the long-suffering Nirgrantha can also."

尼揵亲子亦再三答曰：「汝可速往！我亦可伏沙门瞿昙，汝亦可也，长苦行尼揵亦可也。」

The long-suffering Nirgrantha also said for the third time: "I do not want the householder Upāli to go to where the śramaṇa Gautama is. Why? The śramaṇa Gautama knows the incantation of illusions and can conjure up disciples, bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, and upāsikās. I fear that the householder Upāli will be transformed by the śramaṇa Gautama and become his disciple."

长苦行尼揵复再三白曰：「我不欲令优婆离居士往诣沙门瞿昙所。所以者何？沙门瞿昙知幻化呪，能呪化作弟子，比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆私，恐优婆离居士受沙门瞿昙化，化作弟子。」

The son of Nirgrantha said: "Ascetic! If the householder Upāli becomes a disciple transformed by the śramaṇa Gautama, it is impossible. If the śramaṇa Gautama becomes a disciple transformed by the householder Upāli, it is possible. Householder Upāli! You may go as you please."

尼揵亲子语曰：「苦行！若优婆离居士受沙门瞿昙化作弟子者，终无是处。若沙门瞿昙受优婆离居士化作弟子者，必有是处。优婆离居士！汝去随意。」

Then the householder Upāli bowed his head at the feet of the son of Nirgrantha, circumambulated him three times to the right, and departed. He went to where the Buddha was, exchanged greetings, and sat to one side. He asked: "Gautama! Did the long-suffering Nirgrantha come here today?"

于是，优婆离居士稽首尼揵亲子足，绕三匝而去，往诣佛所，共相问讯，却坐一面，问曰：「瞿昙！今日长苦行尼揵来至此耶？」

The World-Honored One replied: "He came, householder!"

世尊答曰：「来也。居士！」

The householder Upāli asked: "Gautama! Did you have any discussion with the long-suffering Nirgrantha?"

优婆离居士问曰：「瞿昙！颇共长苦行尼揵有所论耶？」

The World-Honored One replied: "We had a discussion."

世尊答曰：「有所论也。」

The householder Upāli said: "Gautama! If you had any discussion with the long-suffering Nirgrantha, please tell me all about it. If I hear it, I may understand." Then the World-Honored One told him everything he had discussed with the long-suffering Nirgrantha.

优婆离居士语曰：「瞿昙！若共长苦行尼揵有所论者，尽为我说，若我闻已，或能知之。」于是，世尊共长苦行尼揵有所论者，尽向彼说。

At that time, the householder Upāli, having heard, praised: "Excellent! The ascetic practices the Dharma of a disciple towards the Venerable Teacher. His wisdom and eloquence are clear and decisive, peaceful and fearless, accomplished and well-tamed, attaining great eloquence, obtaining the banner of immortality, and personally realizing and accomplishing the realm of immortality. Why is this? It is because he has established the heaviest bodily punishment towards the śramaṇa Gautama, causing him not to engage in evil deeds and not to commit evil deeds. Verbal punishment is not so, and mental punishment is the lightest, not as heavy as bodily punishment."

尔时，优婆离居士闻便叹曰：「善哉！苦行！谓于尊师行弟子法，所作智辩聪明决定，安隐无畏成就调御，逮大辩才，得甘露幢，于甘露界自作证成就游。所以者何？谓向沙门瞿昙施设身罚最重，令不行恶业，不作恶业。口罚不然，意罚最下，不及身罚极大甚重。」

At that time, the World-Honored One said: "Householder! I wish to discuss this matter with you. If you abide in true reality, then answer with true reality."

彼时，世尊告曰：「居士！我欲与汝共论此事，汝若住真谛者，以真谛答。」

The householder Upāli replied: "Gautama! I abide in true reality and answer with true reality. śramaṇa Gautama! Just discuss this matter with me."

优婆离居士报曰：「瞿昙！我住真谛，以真谛答。沙门瞿昙！但当与我共论此事。」

The World-Honored One asked: "Householder! What do you think? If a Nirgrantha comes, delights in giving, takes pleasure in giving, without play, takes pleasure in not playing, and is extremely pure, extremely practicing incantations. If they come and go, killing many small and large insects, what do you think, householder? Would the Nirgrantha Jains give retribution for this killing?"

世尊问曰：「居士！于意云何？若有尼揵来，好喜于布施，乐行于布施，无戏、乐不戏，为极清净，极行呪也。若彼行来时，多杀大小虫，云何？居士！尼揵亲子于此杀生施设报耶？」

The householder Upāli replied: "Gautama! If there is intention, there is great sin; if there is no intention, there is no great sin."

优婆离居士答曰：「瞿昙！若思者有大罪，若无思者无大罪也。」

The World-Honored One asked: "Householder! What do you say is intention?"

世尊问曰：「居士！汝说思为何等耶？」

The householder Upāli replied: "Gautama! It is the intention of the mind."

优婆离居士答曰：「瞿昙！意业是也。」

The World-Honored One said: "Householder! You should consider before answering. What you say contradicts what you said before and what you will say later, and is not appropriate. Householder! You said in this assembly: 'Gautama! I abide in the truth and answer with the truth. śramaṇa Gautama! You should only discuss this matter with me.' Householder! What do you think? If a Nirgrantha came to drink hot water to stop the cold water, and when there was no hot water, he wanted to drink cold water, but could not get cold water, and then he died. Householder! How can you say that the Nirgrantha was born from the Nirgrantha?"

世尊告曰：「居士！汝当思量而后答也。汝之所说，前与后违，后与前违，则不相应。居士！汝在此众自说：『瞿昙！我住真谛，以真谛答。沙门瞿昙！但当与我共论此事。』居士！于意云何？若有尼揵来饮汤断冷水，彼无汤时，便欲饮冷水，不得冷水，彼便命终。居士！尼揵亲子云何可说彼尼揵所生耶？」

The householder Upāli replied: "Gautama! There is a god named Manovijnana, and when that Nirgrantha dies, if he dies with Manovijnana, he will definitely be reborn there."

优婆离居士答曰：「瞿昙！有天名意著，彼尼揵命终，若意著死者，必生彼处。」

The World-Honored One said: "Householder! You should consider before answering. What you say contradicts what you said before and what you will say later, and is not appropriate. Householder! You said in this assembly: 'Gautama! I abide in the truth and answer with the truth. śramaṇa Gautama! You should only discuss this matter with me.' Householder! What do you think? If someone came with a sharp knife and said: 'I will cut, chop, slice, peel, scrape, and carve all the beings in this Nālandā into a mass of flesh and a pile of flesh in one day.' Householder! What do you think? Could that person really cut, chop, slice, peel, scrape, and carve all the beings in this Nālandā into a mass of flesh and a pile of flesh in one day?"

世尊告曰：「居士！汝当思量而后答也。汝之所说，前与后违，后与前违，则不相应。汝在此众自说：『瞿昙！我住真谛，以真谛答。沙门瞿昙！但当与我共论此事。』居士！于意云何？若使有人持利刀来，彼作是说：『我于此那难陀内一切众生，于一日中斫剉斩截、剥裂削割，作一肉聚，作一肉积。』居士！于意云何？彼人宁能于此那难陀内一切众生，于一日中斫剉斩截、剥裂削割，作一肉聚，作一肉积耶？」

The householder Upāli replied: "No. Why? Because this Nālandā is extremely wealthy and prosperous, with many people. Therefore, that person would definitely not be able to cut, chop, slice, peel, scrape, and carve all the beings in this Nālandā into a mass of flesh and a pile of flesh in one day. Gautama! That person would be greatly troubled in vain."

优婆离居士答曰：「不也。所以者何？此那难陀内极大富乐，多有人民。是故彼人于此那难陀内一切众生，必不能得于一日中斫剉斩截、剥裂削割，作一肉聚，作一肉积。瞿昙！彼人唐大烦劳。」

"Householder! What do you think? If a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa came with great supernatural powers, great authority, great merit, and great spiritual power, with a mind of mastery, and said: 'I will generate one thought of anger and burn this entire Nālandā to ashes.' Householder! What do you think? Could that śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa really burn this entire Nālandā to ashes?"

「居士！于意云何？若有沙门、梵志来，有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，心得自在，彼作是说：『我以发一瞋念，令此一切那难陀内烧使成灰。』居士！于意云何？彼沙门、梵志宁能令此一切那难陀内烧成灰耶？」

The householder Upāli replied: "Gautama! Not only one Nālandā, not only two, three, or four. Gautama! If those śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas have great supernatural powers, great authority, great merit, and great spiritual power, with a mind of mastery, and generate one thought of anger, they can burn all the people in all the countries to ashes. How much more so one Nālandā?"

优婆离居士答曰：「瞿昙！何但一那难陀？何但二、三、四？瞿昙！彼沙门、梵志有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，心得自在，若发一瞋念，能令一切国一切人民烧使成灰，况一那难陀耶？」

The World-Honored One said: "Householder! You should consider before answering. What you said before contradicts what you said later, and what you said later contradicts what you said before. They do not correspond. You said in this assembly: 'Gautama! I abide in the truth and answer with the truth. Śramaṇa Gautama! You should only discuss this matter with me.'"

世尊告曰：「居士！汝当思量而后答也。汝之所说，前与后违，后与前违，则不相应。汝在此众自说：『瞿昙！我住真谛，以真谛答。沙门瞿昙！但当与我共论此事。』」

The World-Honored One asked: "Householder! Have you ever heard of the great wilderness being without affairs, the qilin being without affairs, the deer being without affairs, the quiet and secluded being without affairs, the empty wilderness being without affairs, and affairs being without affairs?"

世尊问曰：「居士！汝颇曾闻大泽无事、麒麟无事、麋鹿无事、静寂无事、空野无事、无事即无事耶？」

The householder Upāli replied: "Gautama! I have heard of it."

优婆离居士答曰：「瞿昙！我闻有也。」

"Householder! What do you think? Who is it that the great wilderness is without affairs, the qilin is without affairs, the deer is without affairs, the quiet and secluded is without affairs, the empty wilderness is without affairs, and affairs are without affairs?"

「居士！于意云何？彼为谁大泽无事、麒麟无事、麋鹿无事、静寂无事、空野无事、无事即无事耶？」

The householder Upāli remained silent and did not answer. The World-Honored One said: "Householder! Answer quickly! Householder! Answer quickly! Now is not the time for silence. Householder! You yourself said in this assembly: 'Gautama! I abide in the truth and answer with the truth. śramaṇa Gautama! You should only discuss this matter with me.'"

优婆离居士默然不答。世尊告曰：「居士！速答。居士！速答。今非默然时。居士在此众自说：『瞿昙！我住真谛，以真谛答。沙门瞿昙！但当与我共论此事。』」

Then the householder Upāli, after a moment of silence, said: "Gautama! I am not silent, I am just contemplating this meaning. Gautama! Those foolish Nirgranthas do not understand well, cannot comprehend, do not recognize good fields, and do not examine themselves. For a long time they have deceived me and misled me, saying that the heaviest punishment for the body is to be given by the śramaṇa Gautama, so that one will not do evil deeds or create evil karma, and that the punishment for speech and mind is not like that. As I understand the meaning from what the śramaṇa Gautama has said, a single angry thought of a sage can make the great wilderness without affairs, the qilin without affairs, the deer without affairs, the quiet and secluded without affairs, the empty wilderness without affairs, and affairs without affairs. World-Honored One! I have understood. Well-Gone One! I have comprehended. I now take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha of monks. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept me as a lay follower. From this day forth, for as long as I live, I take refuge until the end of my life."

于是，优婆离居士须臾默然已，语曰：「瞿昙！我不默然，我但思惟于此义耳。瞿昙！彼愚痴尼揵不善晓了，不能解知，不识良田，而不自审，长夜欺我，为彼所误，谓向沙门瞿昙施设身罚最重，令不行恶业，不作恶业，口罚、意罚而不如也。如我从沙门瞿昙所说知义，仙人发一瞋念，能令大泽无事、麒麟无事、麋鹿无事、寂静无事、空野无事、无事即无事。世尊！我已知。善逝！我已解。我今自归于佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。」

The World-Honored One said: "Householder! You should practice in silence, do not proclaim it. It is better for such a superior person to be silent."

世尊告曰：「居士！汝默然行，勿得宣言，如是胜人默然为善。」

The householder Upāli said: "World-Honored One! This is why I am even more delighted with the World-Honored One. Why is that? It is because the World-Honored One said: 'Householder! You should practice in silence, do not proclaim it. It is better for such a superior person to be silent.' World-Honored One! If I were to become a disciple of other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, they would then carry banners, flags, and parasols, and proclaim it everywhere in the city of Naḍakā, saying: 'The householder Upāli has become my disciple, the householder Upāli has become my disciple.' But the World-Honored One said: 'Householder! You should practice in silence, do not proclaim it. It is better for such a superior person to be silent.'"

优婆离居士白曰：「世尊！我以是故，复于世尊重加欢喜。所以者何？谓世尊作如是说：『居士！汝默然行，勿得宣言，如是胜人默然为善。』世尊！若我更为余沙门、梵志作弟子者，彼等便当持幢、幡、盖，遍行宣令于那难陀，作如是说：『优婆离居士为我作弟子，优婆离居士为我作弟子。然世尊作是说：居士！汝默然行，勿得宣言，如是胜人默然为善。』」

The householder Upāli said: "World-Honored One! From today onwards, I will not allow any Nirgranthas to enter my house, but I will allow the fourfold assembly of disciples of the World-Honored One - bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, and upāsikās - to enter."

优婆离居士白曰：「世尊！从今日始，不听诸尼揵入我家门，唯听世尊四众弟子，比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆私入。」

The World-Honored One said: "Householder! Those Nirgranthas and others have long been respected by you. If they come, you should provide for them according to your ability."

世尊告曰：「居士！彼尼揵等，汝家长夜所共尊敬，若其来者，汝当随力供养于彼。」

Upāli said: "World-Honored One! This is why I am even more delighted with the World-Honored One. Why is that? It is because the World-Honored One said: 'Householder! Those Nirgranthas and others have long been respected by you. If they come, you should provide for them according to your ability.' World-Honored One! I have heard the World-Honored One say: 'Give to me, not to others. Give to my disciples, not to the disciples of others. If you give to me, you will attain great merit. If you give to others, you will not attain great merit. If you give to my disciples, you will attain great merit. If you give to the disciples of others, you will not attain great merit.'"

优婆离白曰：「世尊！我以是故，复于世尊倍加欢喜。所以者何？谓世尊作如是说：『居士！彼尼揵等，汝家长夜所共尊敬，若其来者，汝当随力供养于彼。』世尊！我本闻世尊作如是说：『当施与我，莫施与他。当施与我弟子，莫施与他弟子。若施与我者，当得大福，若施与他，不得大福。施与我弟子，当得大福，施与他弟子，不得大福。』」

The World-Honored One said: "Householder! I did not say: 'Give to me, not to others. Give to my disciples, not to the disciples of others. If you give to me, you will attain great merit. If you give to others, you will not attain great merit. If you give to my disciples, you will attain great merit. If you give to the disciples of others, you will not attain great merit.' Householder! I said: 'Give to all, joyfully according to your mind. But giving to the indolent does not bring great merit, giving to the diligent brings great merit.'"

世尊告曰：「居士！我不如是说：『当施与我，莫施与他。施与我弟子，莫施与他弟子。若施与我者，当得大福，若施与他，不得大福。施与我弟子，当得大福，若施与他弟子，不得大福。』居士！我说如是，施与一切，随心欢喜，但施与不精进者，不得大福，施与精进者，当得大福。」

Upāli the householder said: "World-Honored One! May it not be so. I myself know whether to give to the Nirgranthas or not. World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha for a second time. May the World-Honored One accept me as a lay follower, from this day forward, for as long as I live, taking refuge until the end of my life." Then the World-Honored One taught the Dharma to Upāli the householder, inspiring, encouraging, and delighting him. Using countless skillful means, he taught the Dharma, inspiring, encouraging, and delighting him. Having done so, according to the way of the Buddhas, he first taught the correct Dharma, which is delightful to hear, namely, teaching generosity, teaching discipline, and teaching the Dharma of rebirth in the heavens, criticizing desire as a calamity, birth and death as defilement, praising the wonderful state of desirelessness, and the purity of the factors of the path. Having taught such Dharma, the Buddha knew that he had a joyful mind, a complete mind, a pliant mind, a patient mind, an elevated mind, a one-pointed mind, a mind free from doubt, a mind free from hindrances, and the ability to receive the true teaching, namely, the true essentials taught by the Buddhas. The World-Honored One then taught him the truths of suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path. Upāli the householder then saw the four noble truths of suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path, just as white cloth is easily dyed. In the same way, Upāli the householder then saw the four noble truths of suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path.

优婆离居士白曰：「世尊！愿无为也。我自知施与尼揵、不施与尼揵。世尊！我今再自归佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。」于是，世尊为优婆离居士说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰。成就欢喜已，如诸佛法，先说端正法，闻者欢悦，谓说施、说戒、说生天法，毁呰] cf.［丽】欲为灾患，生死为秽，称叹无欲为妙，道品白净。世尊为彼说如是法已，佛知彼有欢喜心、具足心、柔软心、堪耐心、升上心、一向心、无疑心、无盖心，有能有力，堪受正法，谓如诸佛所说正要。世尊便为彼说苦、习、灭、道。优婆离居士即于坐中见四圣谛，苦、习、灭、道，犹如白素，易染为色。如是优婆离居士即于坐中见四圣谛，苦、习、灭、道。

Then Upāli the householder saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, realized the pure Dharma, cut off doubts, crossed over perplexity, had no other masters, no longer followed others, was free from hesitation, and abided in the realization of the fruits. He then rose from his seat, bowed to the Buddha, and said: "World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha for a third time. May the World-Honored One accept me as a lay follower, from this day forward, for as long as I live, taking refuge until the end of my life."

于是，优婆离居士见法得法，觉白净法，断疑度惑，更无余尊，不复从他，无有犹豫，已住果证，于世尊法得无所畏。即从坐起，为佛作礼：「世尊！我今三自归佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。」

Then Upāli the householder, having heard the Buddha's teaching, received it well and upheld it well. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and returned. He instructed the gatekeeper, saying: "You should know that I am now a disciple of the World-Honored One. From today onward, do not allow any Nirgranthas to enter the gate. Only allow the World-Honored One's fourfold assembly of disciples, bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, and upāsikās to enter. If any Nirgranthas come, you should tell them: 'Venerable ones! Upāli the householder has now been transformed by the Buddha and become a disciple. He no longer allows any Nirgranthas to enter the gate. Only allows the World-Honored One's fourfold assembly of disciples, bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, and upāsikās to enter. If you need food, you may stay here. I will come out and give it to you.'"

于是，优婆离居士闻佛所说，善受善持，稽首佛足，绕三匝而归，勅守门者：「汝等当知，我今则为世尊弟子，从今日始，诸尼揵来，莫听入门，唯听世尊四众弟子，比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆私入。若尼揵来者，当语彼言：『尊者！优婆离居士今受佛化，化作弟子，则不听诸尼揵入门，唯听世尊四众弟子，比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆私入。若须食者，便可住此，当出食与。』」

Then the Nirgrantha ascetic Dīrghanakha heard that Upāli the householder had been transformed by the Śramaṇa Gautama and become his disciple, and that he no longer allowed any Nirgranthas to enter the gate, only allowing the Śramaṇa Gautama's disciples, bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, and upāsikās to enter. Having heard this, he went to the Nirgrantha ascetic Pūraṇa Kāśyapa and said: "Venerable Sir! This is what I originally said.

于是，长苦行尼揵闻优婆离居士受沙门瞿昙化，化作弟子，则不听诸尼揵入门，唯听沙门瞿昙弟子，比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆私入。长苦行尼揵闻已，往诣尼揵亲子所，白曰：「尊！此是我本所说。」

The Nirgrantha ascetic Pūraṇa Kāśyapa asked: "Ascetic! What is it that you originally said?"

尼揵亲子问曰：「苦行！何者是汝本所说耶？」

The Nirgrantha ascetic Dīrghanakha replied: "Venerable Sir! What I originally said was that I did not want Upāli the householder to go to the Śramaṇa Gautama. Why? The Śramaṇa Gautama knows the incantations of illusion and can conjure up disciples, bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, and upāsikās. I was afraid that Upāli the householder would be transformed by the Śramaṇa Gautama and become his disciple. Venerable Sir! Upāli the householder has now been transformed by the Śramaṇa Gautama. Having become his disciple, he no longer allows any Nirgranthas to enter the gate, only allowing the Śramaṇa Gautama's disciples, bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, and upāsikās to enter."

长苦行尼揵答曰：「尊！我本所说，不欲令优婆离居士往诣沙门瞿昙所。所以者何？沙门瞿昙知幻化呪，能呪化作弟子，比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆私，恐优婆离居士受沙门瞿昙化，化作弟子。尊！优婆离居士今已受沙门瞿昙化。化作弟子已，不听诸尼揵入门，唯听沙门瞿昙弟子，比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆私入。」

The Nirgrantha ascetic Pūraṇa Kāśyapa said: "Ascetic! If Upāli the householder has been transformed by the Śramaṇa Gautama into a disciple, it is impossible. If the Śramaṇa Gautama has been transformed by Upāli the householder into a disciple, it is certainly possible."

尼揵亲子语曰：「苦行！若优婆离居士受沙门瞿昙化作弟子者，终无是处。若沙门瞿昙受优婆离居士化作弟子者，必有是处。」

The Nirgrantha ascetic Dīrghanakha again said: "Venerable Sir! If you do not believe what I say, you can go yourself or send someone."

长苦行尼揵复白曰：「尊！若不信我所说者，尊自可往，亦可遣使。」

Then the Nirgrantha ascetic Pūraṇa Kāśyapa said: "Ascetic! You should go yourself and see for yourself whether Upāli the householder has been transformed by the Śramaṇa Gautama into a disciple, or whether the Śramaṇa Gautama has been transformed by Upāli the householder into a disciple."

于是，尼揵亲子告曰：「苦行！汝可自往，诣彼看之，为优婆离居士受沙门瞿昙化作弟子耶？为沙门瞿昙受优婆离居士化作弟子耶？」

The Nirgrantha ascetic Dīrghanakha, having received the instructions of the Nirgrantha ascetic Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, went to the house of Upāli the householder. The gatekeeper saw from afar the Nirgrantha ascetic Dīrghanakha coming and said: "Venerable Sir! Upāli the householder has now been transformed by the Buddha into a disciple, and does not allow any Nirgranthas to enter the house. Only the fourfold assembly of disciples of the World-Honored One, namely, monks, nuns, male lay followers, and female lay followers, are allowed to enter. If you wish to get food, you may stay here and I will bring it out to you."

长苦行尼揵受尼揵亲子教已，往诣优婆离居士家。守门人遥见长苦行尼揵来，而作是说：「尊者！优婆离居士今受佛化，化作弟子，则不听诸尼揵入门，唯听世尊四众弟子，比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆私入。若欲得食者，便可住此，当出食与。」

The Nirgrantha ascetic Dīrghanakha said: "Gatekeeper! I do not need food."

长苦行尼揵语曰：「守门人！我不用食。」

The Nirgrantha ascetic Dīrghanakha, having learned of this matter, shook his head and left, going to the Nirgrantha ascetic Pūraṇa Kāśyapa and saying: "Venerable Sir! This is just as I have said before."

长苦行尼揵知此事已，奋头而去，往诣尼揵亲子所，白曰：「尊！此是如我本所说。」

Pūraṇa Kāśyapa asked: "Ascetic! What is it that you have said before?"

尼揵亲子问曰：「苦行！何者是汝本所说耶？」

The Nirgrantha ascetic Dīrghanakha replied: "Venerable Sir! What I said before was that I did not want the householder Upāli to go to the śramaṇa Gautama. Why? The śramaṇa Gautama knows the spells of illusion and can conjure up disciples, bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, and upāsikās. I was afraid that the householder Upāli would be transformed by the śramaṇa Gautama and become his disciple. Venerable Sir! The householder Upāli has now been transformed by the śramaṇa Gautama and become his disciple. He does not allow any Nirgranthas to enter his house, but only allows the śramaṇa Gautama's disciples, bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, and upāsikās to enter."

长苦行尼揵答曰：「尊！我本所说，不欲令优婆离居士往诣沙门瞿昙所。所以者何？沙门瞿昙知幻化呪，能呪化作弟子，比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆私，恐优婆离居士受沙门瞿昙化，化作弟子。尊！优婆离居士今已受沙门瞿昙化，化作弟子已，不听诸尼揵入门，唯听沙门瞿昙弟子，比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆私入。」

Pūraṇa Kāśyapa said: "Ascetic! If the householder Upāli has been transformed by the śramaṇa Gautama and become his disciple, it is impossible. If the śramaṇa Gautama has been transformed by the householder Upāli and become his disciple, it is possible."

尼揵亲子告曰：「苦行！若优婆离居士受沙门瞿昙化作弟子者，终无是处。若沙门瞿昙受优婆离居士化作弟子者，必有是处。」

The Nirgrantha ascetic Dīrghanakha said: "Venerable Sir! If you do not believe what I say, please go yourself."

长苦行尼揵白曰：「尊！若不信我所说者，愿尊自往。」

Then Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, together with a group of five hundred Nirgrantha ascetics, went to the house of the householder Upāli. The gatekeeper saw from afar that Pūraṇa Kāśyapa was coming with a group of five hundred Nirgrantha ascetics, and said: "Venerable Sir! The householder Upāli has now been transformed by the Buddha and become his disciple. He does not allow any Nirgrantha ascetics to enter the house, but only allows the four groups of disciples of the World-Honored One - bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, and upāsikās - to enter. If you want to eat, you can stay here and I will bring food out to you."

于是，尼揵亲子与大尼揵众五百人俱，往诣优婆离居士家。守门人遥见尼揵亲子与大尼揵众五百人俱来，而作是说：「尊者！优婆离居士今受佛化，化作弟子，则不听诸尼揵入门，唯听世尊四众弟子，比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆私入。若欲得食者，便可住此，当出食与。」

Pūraṇa Kāśyapa said: "Gatekeeper! I do not need food, I just want to see the householder Upāli."

尼揵亲子语曰：「守门人！我不用食，但欲得见优婆离居士。」

The gatekeeper said: "May the Venerable Sir wait here. I will now go in and inform the Venerable Upāli, the householder."

守门人语曰：「愿尊住此，我今入白尊者优婆离居士。」

The gatekeeper then went in and said: "Householder, you should know that Pūraṇa Kāśyapa and a group of five hundred great Nirgranthas are standing outside the gate, saying: 'We wish to see the householder Upāli.'"

彼守门人即入白曰：「居士！当知尼揵亲子与大尼揵众五百人俱住在门外，作如是语：『我欲得见优婆离居士。』」

The householder Upāli told the gatekeeper: "Go to the middle gate, set up a bed and seat, and then come back and inform me."

优婆离居士告守门人：「汝至中门，敷设床座，讫还白我。」

The gatekeeper received the instruction, went to the middle gate, set up a bed and seat, and then returned to report: "Householder, you should know that the bed and seat have been set up. May the householder know the time himself."

守门人受教，往至中门，敷设床座讫，还白曰：「居士！当知敷床已讫，唯愿居士自当知时。」

The householder Upāli took the gatekeeper to the middle gate. If there was a bed and seat, it was extremely high, wide, and well-spread, namely the one that the householder Upāli had originally prepared for the son of the Nigantha Nataputta to sit on. The householder Upāli himself sat on it, cross-legged, and told the gatekeeper: "Go out to where the son of the Nigantha Nataputta is and say this: 'Noble one, the householder Upāli says: "If you wish to enter, you may do so at your own convenience."'

优婆离居士将守门人往至中门，若有床座，极高广大，极净好敷，谓优婆离居士本抱尼揵亲子所令坐者。优婆离居士自处其上，结加趺坐，告守门人：「汝出往至尼揵亲子所，作如是语：『尊人！优婆离居士言：「尊人欲入者，自可随意。」』」

The gatekeeper received the instruction and immediately went out to where the son of the Nigantha Nataputta was and said this: "Noble one, the householder Upāli says: 'If you wish to enter, you may do so at your own convenience.'" Then the son of the Nigantha Nataputta, together with a group of five hundred great Nigantha followers, entered and arrived at the middle gate.

彼守门人受教即出，至尼揵亲子所，作如是语：「尊人！优婆离居士言：『尊人欲入者，自可随意。』」于是，尼揵亲子与大尼揵众五百人俱入至中门。

The householder Upāli saw from afar that the son of the Nigantha Nataputta, together with a group of five hundred great Nigantha followers, was entering and said this: "Noble one, there is a seat. If you wish to sit, you may do so at your own convenience."

优婆离居士遥见尼揵亲子与大尼揵众五百人俱入，而作是语：「尊人！有座，欲坐随意。」

The son of the Nigantha Nataputta said: "Householder, should you do so? Sitting on a high seat, cross-legged, speaking with people, is no different from a renunciant who has left home to study the path."

尼揵亲子语曰：「居士！汝应尔耶？自上高座，结加趺坐，与人共语，如出家者学道无异。」

The householder Upāli said: "Noble one, I have my own things. If I want to give, I give. If I don't want to give, I don't give. This seat is mine, so I say: 'There is a seat. If you wish to sit, you may do so at your own convenience.'"

优婆离居士语曰：「尊人！我自有物，欲与便与，不与便不与，此座我有，是故我言：『有座，欲坐随意。』」

The son of the Nigantha Nataputta spread out a seat and sat down, saying: "Householder, why is it so? You wanted to subdue the śramaṇa Gautama, but you have been subdued yourself. It is like a person seeking eyes entering a forest, but losing their eyes and returning. In the same way, you wanted to go and subdue the śramaṇa Gautama, but you have been subdued by the śramaṇa Gautama. It is like a person thirsty entering a pond, but returning thirsty. The householder is also like this. You wanted to go and subdue the śramaṇa Gautama, but you have been subdued yourself. Householder, why is it so?"

尼揵亲子敷座而坐，语曰：「居士！何以故尔？欲降伏沙门瞿昙而反自降伏来。犹如有人求眼入林，而失眼还。如是，居士欲往降伏沙门瞿昙，反为沙门瞿昙所降伏来。犹如有人以渴入池，而反渴还。居士亦然，欲往降伏沙门瞿昙，而反自降伏还。居士！何以故尔？」

The householder Upāli said: "Noble one, please listen to my analogy. The wise understand the meaning through analogies. Noble one, it is like a certain brahman who had a young wife. She became pregnant and said to her husband: 'I am pregnant now. Please go to the market and buy some nice toys for the child.' At that time, the brahman said to his wife: 'As long as you have a safe delivery, what is there to worry about? If it is a boy, I will buy boy's toys for you. If it is a girl, I will also buy girl's toys for you.' The wife said this to her husband two or three times: 'I am pregnant now. Please go to the market and quickly buy some nice toys for the child.' The brahman also said this to his wife two or three times: 'As long as you have a safe delivery, what is there to worry about? If it is a boy, I will buy boy's toys for you. If it is a girl, I will also buy girl's toys for you.'

优婆离居士语曰：「尊人！听我说喻，慧者闻喻则解其义。尊人！譬一梵志，有年少] cf.［丽】妇，彼妇怀妊，语其夫曰：『我今怀妊，君去至市，可为儿买好戏具来。』时，彼梵志语其妇曰：『但令卿得安隐产已，何忧无耶？若生男者，当为卿买男戏具来，若生女者，亦当为买女戏具来。』妇至再三语其夫曰：『我今怀妊，君去至市，速为儿买好戏具来。』梵志亦至再三语其妇曰：『但令卿得安隐产已，何忧无耶？若生男者，当为卿买男戏具来，若生女者，亦当为买女戏具来。』

The brahman deeply loved his wife, so he asked: 'What toys do you want to buy for the child?' The wife replied: 'Please go and buy a nice monkey toy for the child.' Having heard this, the brahman went to the market and bought a monkey toy. Holding it back, he said to his wife: 'I have already bought a monkey toy for the child.' Seeing it, the wife disliked the color and said to her husband: 'Please take this monkey toy to the dye house and dye it yellow, making it very lovely, and pound it to make it shiny.' Having heard this, the brahman immediately took the monkey toy to the dye house and said: 'Please dye this monkey toy yellow, making it very lovely, and pound it to make it shiny.' At that time, the dye house master said to the brahman: 'It is possible to dye the monkey toy yellow, making it very lovely, but it cannot be pounded to make it shiny.' Then, the dye house master spoke this verse:

「彼梵志者极怜念妇，即便问曰：『卿欲为儿买何戏具？』妇报之曰：『君去为儿买猕猴子好戏具来。』梵志闻已，往至市中买猕猴子戏具。持还语其妇曰：『我已为儿买猕猴子戏具来还。』其妇见已，嫌色不好，即语夫曰：『君可持此猕猴戏具往至染家，染作黄色，令极可爱，捣使光生。』梵志闻已，即时持此猕猴戏具，往至染家而语之曰：『为我染此猕猴戏具，作好黄色，令极可爱，捣使光生。』尔时，染家便语梵志：『猕猴戏具染作黄色，令极可爱，此可尔也，然不可捣使光泽生。』于是，染家说此颂曰：

The monkey can endure the color, but cannot endure the
pounding. If pounded, it will die. It should not be
beaten. This is a bag of filth, filled with impurities.

「『猕猴忍受色，　　不能堪忍捣，若捣则命终，　　终不可椎打，此是臭秽囊，　　猕猴满不净。』

Venerable one! You should know that what the Nirgrantha Jains say is also like this. They cannot endure being questioned by others, and they cannot contemplate and observe. They are only stained by ignorance, not stained by wisdom. Venerable one! Listen further. It is like a pure cotton cloth. The owner takes it to the dye house and says: 'Please dye this cloth to make it very beautiful and lovely. Also pound it to make it shiny.' At that time, the dye house master says to the cloth owner: 'This cloth can be dyed to make it very beautiful and lovely. It can also be pounded to make it shiny.' Then, the dye house master spoke this verse:

「尊人！当知尼揵所说亦复如是，不能堪忍受他难问，亦不可得思惟观察，唯但染愚，不染慧也。尊人！复听，犹如清净波罗［木＊奈］衣，主持往至于彼染家，而语之曰：『为染此衣，作极好色，令可爱也。亦为极捣，使光泽生。』彼时染家语衣主曰：『此衣可染，作极好色，令可爱也。亦可极捣，使光泽生。』于是，染家说此颂曰：

Like a cotton cloth, white and pure, it can endure the color
After pounding, it becomes soft, and the luster and color increase in beauty.

「『如波罗［木＊奈］衣，　　白净忍受色，捣已则柔软，　　光色增益好。』

"Venerable one! You should know that what the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One says is also like this. It is very able to endure being questioned by others, and it is also delightful to contemplate and observe. It is only stained by wisdom, not stained by ignorance."

「尊人！当知诸如来、无所著、等正觉所说亦复如是，极能堪忍受他难问，亦快可得思惟观察。唯但染慧，不染愚也。」

The son of the Nigantha family said, "The householder has been transformed by the illusory spells of the śramaṇa Gautama."

尼揵亲子语曰：「居士为沙门瞿昙幻呪所化。」

The householder Upāli said, "Venerable one! Excellent illusory spells, extremely excellent illusory spells. Venerable one! Those illusory spells have caused my parents to benefit and be at ease and happy for a long time, as well as their wives, children, slaves, servants, workers, the king of the country of Nandi, and all the world, including gods, demons, Brahmā, śramaṇas, brahmins, from humans to gods, causing them to benefit and be at ease and happy for a long time."

优婆离居士语曰：「尊人！善幻化呪，极善幻化呪。尊人！彼幻化呪令我父母长夜得利饶益，安隐快乐，及其妻子、奴婢、作使、那难陀国王，及一切世间，天及魔梵、沙门、梵志，从人至天，令彼长夜得利饶益，安隐快乐。」

The son of the Nigantha family said, "Householder! If Nandi knows that the householder Upāli is a disciple of the Nigantha, then who is he a disciple of now?"

尼揵亲子语曰：「居士！举那难陀知优婆离居士是尼揵弟子，今者竟为谁弟子耶？」

Then the householder Upāli rose from his seat, touched his right knee to the ground, and if there were a Buddha, joined his palms towards him, and said, "Venerable one! Please listen to what I have to say.

于是，优婆离居士即从座起，右膝著地，若方有佛，叉手向彼，语曰：「尊人！听我所说也。

The son of the Nigantha family asked, "Householder! With what intention do you praise the śramaṇa Gautama?"

尼揵亲子问曰：「居士！汝以何意称叹沙门瞿昙耶？」

The householder Upāli replied, "Venerable one! Please listen to my analogy. The wise understand the meaning through analogies. It is like a garland maker or his disciple, who gathers various flowers and weaves them into various garlands with long threads. In the same way, venerable one! The Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One has immeasurable praises. I revere him, and therefore praise him."

优婆离居士报曰：「尊人！听我说喻，慧者闻喻则解其义。犹善鬘师、鬘师弟子，采种种华，以长𫄧结作种种鬘。如是，尊人！如来、无所著、等正觉有无量称叹，我之所尊，以故称叹。」

When this teaching was spoken, the householder Upāli was far removed from dust and defilements, and the Dharma eye of the Dharma arose for him. The son of the Nigantha family immediately vomited hot blood and went to the country of Pāvā, where he died from this illness.

说此法时，优婆离居士远尘离垢，诸法法眼生；尼揵亲子即吐热血，至波惒国，以此恶患，寻便命终。

The Buddha spoke thus. The householder Upāli, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。优婆离居士闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
