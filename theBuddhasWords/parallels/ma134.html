MĀ 134

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 11 大品

134. （一三四）释问经

Parallel to: DN 21 / DĀ 14

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Magadha, staying east of Rajagriha, north of the village of Venuvana, in the Indra Cave on the mountain of Vaitadha.

一时，佛游摩竭陀国，在王舍城东，［木＊奈］林村北，鞞陀提山因陀罗石室。

At that time, the Heavenly King Sakra heard that the Buddha was traveling in the country of Magadha, staying east of Rajagriha, north of the village of Venuvana, in the Indra Cave on the mountain of Vaitadha. The Heavenly King Sakra then told the five sons of Klesa, "I have heard that the World-Honored One is traveling in the country of Magadha, staying east of Rajagriha, north of the village of Venuvana, in the Indra Cave on the mountain of Vaitadha. Klesa, come, let us go together to see the Buddha."

尔时，天王释闻佛游摩竭陀国，在王舍城东，［木＊奈］林村北，鞞陀提山因陀罗石室。时，天王释告五结乐子：「我闻世尊游摩竭陀国，在王舍城东，［木＊奈］林村北，鞞陀提山因陀罗石室。五结！汝来共往见佛。」

The five sons of Klesa said, "Yes." Then the five sons of Klesa took their crystal zithers and followed the Heavenly King Sakra. The thirty-three heavens heard that the Heavenly King Sakra was going to see the Buddha with great sincerity, and the thirty-three heavens also followed the Heavenly King Sakra. Then the Heavenly King Sakra, the thirty-three heavens, and the five sons of Klesa, in the time it takes a strong man to bend and stretch his arm, suddenly disappeared from the thirty-three heavens and appeared in the country of Magadha, east of Rajagriha, north of the village of Venuvana, on the mountain of Vaitadha, not far from the stone chamber.

五结乐子白曰：「唯然。」于是，五结乐子挟琉璃琴从天王释行，三十三天闻天王释其意至重，欲往见佛，三十三天亦复侍从天王释行。于是，天王释及三十三天、五结乐子犹如力士屈伸臂顷，于三十三天忽没不现已，住摩竭陀国王舍城东，［木＊奈］林村北，鞞陀提山，去石室不远。

At that time, the light shining from the mountain of Vaitadha was extremely bright, like a blazing fire. The residents on the left and right of the mountain saw it and thought, "The mountain of Vaitadha is burning all over with fire."

尔时，鞞陀提山光耀极照，明如火［火＊佥］，彼山左右居民见之，便作是念：「鞞陀提山火烧普然。」

Then the Heavenly King Sakra, having stayed in one place, said, "Five Knots! The World-Honored One dwells in such places as mountains, forests, and under trees, enjoying high cliffs, quiet and soundless, far away, without evil, and without people, in accordance with sitting in meditation. He has great majestic virtue, together with the devas, enjoying that far-away sitting in meditation, peacefully and happily wandering. We have not yet announced ourselves, so we should not go forward. Five Knots! You go first to announce, and then we shall advance."

时，天王释住一处已，告曰：「五结！世尊如是住无事处山林树下，乐居高岩，寂无音声，远离，无恶，无有人民，随顺燕坐。有大威德，诸天共俱，乐彼远离，燕坐安隐，快乐游行。我等未通，不应便前。五结！汝往先通，我等然后当进。」

The son of the Five Knots said, "Yes." Then the son of the Five Knots, having received the instruction of the Heavenly King Sakra, took a crystal zither and went first to Indra's stone chamber. He then thought, "Knowing that this place is not far from the Buddha, let the Buddha know me and hear my sound." Having stayed there, he tuned the crystal zither and sang verses corresponding to desire, dragons, śramaṇas, and arhats, saying:

五结乐子白曰：「唯然。」于是，五结乐子受天王释教已，挟琉璃琴即先往至因陀罗石室，便作是念：「知此处离佛不近不远，令佛知我，闻我音声。」住彼处已，调琉璃琴，作欲相应偈、龙相应偈、沙门相应偈、阿罗诃相应偈，而歌颂曰：

Wise one, you are the son of your parents, the moon and Dambhalou
who are said to have given birth to you in a wondrous way
causing me to give rise to a joyful mind. Like the heat seeking
a cool breeze, like thirst wanting to drink cold water, so do I
love you, like Rahula loves the Dharma. Like it is very difficult to
collect water, so is it with attachment to desire. Meeting together for countless
lives, like giving without attachment. A pond with clear and cool water, with
golden sand on the bottom, like an elephant tormented by heat entering this
pond to bathe. Like a hook pulling an elephant, my mind is subdued
for you. You do not realize what I do, the charming one not
yet obtained. My mind is extremely attached to you, the vexation and resentment
burning my heart. Therefore I am not happy, like a person entering a
tiger's mouth. Like a Śākya son contemplating meditation, always enjoying being alone, like
a Muni attaining enlightenment, attaining your wondrous purity. Like the enjoyment of a
Muni, the unsurpassed perfect enlightenment, so do I enjoy it, always seeking to
obtain you. Like a sick person wanting medicine, like a hungry person wanting
food, wise one, stop my mind, like water extinguishing fire. If the merit
I have created is for making offerings to the unattached ones, they are
all pure and wondrous, and I will share the rewards with you. I
wish to be with you until the end, not leaving you alone. I
would rather die with you than be separated from you while alive. May
Śakra grant my wish, the honored one of the thirty-three heavens, you are
the unsurpassed honored one among humans, my wish is the most firm. Therefore
I pay homage to the Great Hero, bowing to the supreme among humans
severing the thorns of all attachments, I pay homage to the kinsman of the sun.

「贤礼汝父母，　　月及耽浮楼，谓生汝殊妙，　　令我发欢心。烦热求凉风，　　渴欲饮冷水，如是我爱汝，　　犹罗诃爱法。如收水甚难，　　著欲亦复然，无量生共会，　　如施与无著。池水清且凉，　　底有金粟沙，如龙象热逼，　　入此池水浴。犹如钩牵象，　　我意为汝伏，所行汝不觉，　　窈窕未得汝。我意极著汝，　　烦冤烧我心，是故我不乐，　　如人入虎口。如释子思禅，　　常乐在于一，如牟尼得觉，　　得汝妙净然。如牟尼所乐，　　无上正尽觉，如是我所乐，　　常求欲得汝。如病欲得药，　　如饥欲得食，贤汝止我心，　　犹如水灭火。若我所作福，　　供养诸无著，彼是悉净妙，　　我共汝受报。愿我共汝终，　　不离汝独沽，我宁共汝死，　　不用相离生。释为与我愿，　　三十三天尊，汝人无上尊，　　是我愿最坚。是故礼大雄，稽首人最上，断绝诸爱刺，　　我礼日之亲。」

Then the World-Honored One arose from samādhi and praised the son of the Five Knots, saying, "Excellent! Excellent! Five Knots! Your singing voice is in harmony with the sound of the zither, and the sound of the zither is in harmony with your singing voice. The singing voice does not go beyond the sound of the zither, and the sound of the zither does not go beyond the singing voice. Five Knots! Do you remember singing these verses corresponding to desire, dragons, śramaṇas, and arhats in the past?"

于是，世尊从三昧起，赞叹五结乐子曰：「善哉！善哉！五结！汝歌音与琴声相应，琴声与歌音相应，歌音不出琴声外，琴声不出歌音外。五结！汝颇忆昔时歌颂此欲相应偈、龙相应偈、沙门相应偈、阿罗诃相应偈耶？」

The son of the Five Knots said, "World-Honored One! Only the Great Sage himself knows. Great Sage! In the past, when the World-Honored One first attained the Way, he was wandering by the banks of the Nairañjanā River, under the Ajapāla Nyagrodha tree. At that time, there was a princess of King Dambhalou named Kṣemavatī. There was a god named Knot, the son of a charioteer named Māndhātṛ, who sought to marry her. Great Sage! When he sought to marry her, I also sought to obtain her. However, Great Sage! When seeking her, I was unable to obtain her. At that time, I stayed behind her and sang these verses corresponding to desire, dragons, śramaṇas, and arhats. Great Sage! When I sang these verses, she turned around, smiled serenely, and said to me, 'Five Knots! I have never seen that Buddha World-Honored One, but I have heard from the Thirty-three Heavens that he is the World-Honored One, Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, Perfect in Knowledge and Conduct, Well-Gone, Knower of the World, Unsurpassed, Tamer of Men, Teacher of Gods and Men, Buddha, Blessed One. Five Knots! If you can praise the World-Honored One often, then you may be with her.' Great Sage! We only met once, and I have not seen her since."

五结乐子白曰：「世尊！唯大仙人自当知之。大仙人！昔时世尊初得觉道，游欝鞞罗尼连禅河岸，阿阇惒罗尼拘类树下。尔时，躭浮楼乐王女，名贤月色。有天名结，摩兜丽御车子，求欲彼女。大仙人！彼当求欲于彼女时，我亦复求欲得彼女。然，大仙人！求彼女时竟不能得，我于尔时住彼女后，便歌颂此欲相应偈、龙相应偈、沙门相应偈、阿罗诃相应偈。大仙人！我歌颂此偈时，彼女回顾，怡然含笑而语我曰：『五结！我未曾见彼佛世尊，然我已从三十三天闻彼世尊、如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐。五结！若汝能数称叹世尊者，可与汝共事大仙人。』我唯一共会，自后不复见。」

Then Śakra, King of the Devas, had this thought: "The son of the Five Knots has already caused the World-Honored One to arise from samādhi. He has informed me of the Well-Gone One." At that time, Śakra, King of the Devas, said, "Five Knots! You should go there and bow your head at the Buddha's feet on my behalf. Inquire about the World-Honored One's well-being, health, strength, and ease. Say this: 'Great Sage! Śakra, King of the Devas, bows his head at the Buddha's feet and inquires about the World-Honored One's well-being, health, strength, and ease. Great Sage! Śakra, King of the Devas, and the Thirty-three Heavens wish to see the World-Honored One!'"

于是，天王释而作是念：「五结乐子已令世尊从定觉起已，通我于善逝。」彼时，天王释告曰：「五结！汝即往彼，为我稽首佛足，问讯世尊圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常耶？作如是语：『大仙人！天王释稽首佛足，问讯世尊圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常耶？大仙人！天王释及三十三天欲见世尊！』」

The son of the Five Knots said, "Yes, sir."

五结乐子白曰：「唯然。」

Then the son of the Five Knots put down his crystal zither, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "World-Honored One! Great Sage! Śakra, King of the Devas, bows his head at the Buddha's feet and inquires about the World-Honored One's well-being, health, strength, and ease. Great Sage! Śakra, King of the Devas, and the Thirty-three Heavens wish to see the World-Honored One!"

于是，五结乐子舍琉璃琴，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！唯大仙人！天王释稽首佛足，问讯世尊圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常耶？大仙人！天王释及三十三天欲见世尊！」

At that time, the World-Honored One said, "Five Knots! Today, King Śakra of the Devas is at ease and happy, as well as all the gods, humans, asuras, gandharvas, rākṣasas, and other various beings. Five Knots! If King Śakra of the Devas wishes to see me, he may do as he pleases."

尔时，世尊告曰：「五结！今天王释安隐快乐，及诸天、人、阿修罗、揵沓惒、罗刹及余种种身安隐快乐。五结！天王释欲见我者，随其所欲。」

Then the son of the Five Knots, having heard the Buddha's words, accepted them well and upheld them well. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and departed. He went to where King Śakra of the Devas was and said, "Heavenly King! I have already informed the World-Honored One. The World-Honored One is now waiting for the Heavenly King. May the Heavenly King know the right time himself."

于是，五结乐子闻佛所说，善受善持，稽首佛足，遶三匝而去，往诣天王释所，白曰：「天王！我已为白世尊，世尊今待天王，唯愿天王自当知时。」

Then King Śakra of the Devas, together with the Thirty-three Heavens and the son of the Five Knots, went to where the Buddha was. At that time, King Śakra of the Devas bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and thrice proclaimed his name and clan, saying, "Only the Great Ascetic! I am King Śakra of the Devas! I am King Śakra of the Devas!"

于是，天王释及三十三天、五结乐子往诣佛所。时，天王释稽首佛足，再三自称名姓言：「唯大仙人！我是天王释，我是天王释。」

The World-Honored One said, "So it is. So it is. Kauśika! You are King Śakra of the Devas."

世尊告曰：「如是。如是。拘翼！汝是天王释。」

At that time, King Śakra of the Devas proclaimed his name and clan thrice, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and then stood to one side. The Thirty-three Heavens and the son of the Five Knots also bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet and then stood to one side. At that time, King Śakra of the Devas said, "Only the Great Ascetic! Should I sit far or near the World-Honored One?"

时，天王释再三自称名姓，稽首佛足，却住一面；三十三天及五结乐子亦稽首佛足，却住一面。时天王释白曰：「唯大仙人！我去世尊近远坐耶？」

The World-Honored One said, "You should sit near me. Why is that? You have a great heavenly retinue." Then King Śakra of the Devas bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side. The Thirty-three Heavens and the son of the Five Knots also bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side. At that time, the Indra Stone Chamber suddenly became vast and spacious. Why was that? It was due to the Buddha's majestic virtue and the heavenly virtues.

世尊告曰：「汝近我坐。所以者何？汝有大天眷属。」于是，天王释稽首佛足，却坐一面，三十三天及五结乐子亦稽首佛足，却坐一面。尔时，因陀罗石室忽然广大。所以者何？佛之威神及诸天威德。

Then King Śakra of the Devas, having sat down, said, "Only the Great Ascetic! For a long time I have wished to see the World-Honored One and ask about the Dharma. Great Ascetic! Once in the past, the World-Honored One was dwelling in a stone cave in the country of Śrāvastī. Great Ascetic! At that time, I, for myself and for the Thirty-three Heavens, rode in a thousand-elephant chariot to the house of King Veśaṃpura. At that time, there was a concubine of King Veśaṃpura named Pañcānana. At that time, the World-Honored One was in concentration, silent. The concubine joined her palms and bowed at the feet of the World-Honored One. Great Ascetic! I said to her, 'Sister! I am not going to see the World-Honored One now. The World-Honored One is in concentration. If the World-Honored One awakens from concentration, then you should bow your head at the feet of the Buddha for me and ask the World-Honored One if his holy body is healthy, at ease, without illness, and if his daily activities are light and his strength is as usual. Say this: "Only the Great Ascetic! King Śakra of the Devas bows his head at the feet of the Buddha and asks if the World-Honored One's holy body is healthy, at ease, without illness, and if his daily activities are light and his strength is as usual."'" Great Ascetic! Did the sister bow her head at the feet of the Buddha for me and ask about the World-Honored One? Does the World-Honored One remember it?

时，天王释坐已，白曰：「唯大仙人！我于长夜欲见世尊，欲请问法。大仙人！往昔一时世尊游舍卫国，住石岩中，大仙人！我尔时自为及为三十三天，乘千象车，往至鞞沙门大王家。尔时，鞞沙门大王家有妾，名槃阇那，尔时，世尊入定寂然，彼妾叉手礼世尊足。大仙人！我语彼曰：『妹！我今非往见世尊时，世尊入定，若世尊从定寤者，妹便为我稽首佛足，问讯世尊圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常耶？作如是说：「唯大仙人！天王释稽首佛足，问讯世尊圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常耶？」』大仙人！彼妹为我稽首佛足，问讯世尊，世尊为忆不耶？」

The World-Honored One said, "Kauśika! That sister bowed her head at my feet for you and conveyed your intention, asking about me. I also remember it, Kauśika! When you left, I heard that sound and awakened from concentration.

世尊告曰：「拘翼！彼妹为汝稽首我足，具宣汝意，问讯于我，我亦忆，拘翼！当汝去时，闻此音声，便从定寤。」

Great Ascetic! I have heard that when a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, Perfect in Knowledge and Conduct, Sugata, Knower of the World, Supreme Trainer, Trainer of those who can be trained, Teacher of Gods and Men, Buddha, Bhagavān appears in the world, it increases the number of gods and decreases the number of asuras. Great Ascetic! I have seen with my own eyes that the World-Honored One's disciples, the bhikṣus, practice the holy life under the World-Honored One, abandon desire and are free from desire, and when their bodies perish and their lives end, they attain a good state of existence and are reborn in the heavens. Great Ascetic! The woman Kuśa was a disciple of the World-Honored One, and she also practiced the holy life under the World-Honored One. She hated this female body and loved the male form. She transformed her female body and received a male form. She abandoned desire and was free from desire. When her body perished and her life ended, she was reborn in the excellent state of the Thirty-three Heavens and became my son. After his birth, all the gods knew that the god Kuśa had great supernatural powers, great majesty, great merit, and great spiritual power.

「大仙人！昔时我闻，若如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐出于世时，增诸天众，减阿修罗。大仙人！我自眼见世尊弟子比丘从世尊修习梵行，舍欲离欲，身坏命终，得至善处，生于天中。大仙人！瞿毘释女是世尊弟子，亦从世尊修习梵行，憎恶是女身，爱乐男形，转女人身，受男子形，舍欲离欲，身坏命终，得生妙处三十三天，为我作子。彼既生已，诸天悉知，瞿婆天子有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神。

Great Ascetic! I also saw three bhikṣus who were disciples of the World-Honored One. They also practiced the holy life under the World-Honored One, but they did not abandon desire. When their bodies perished and their lives ended, they were reborn in other inferior places among musicians. After their birth, they came to the Thirty-three Heavens every day to serve the gods and attend to Prince Kuśa. When the prince saw them, he spoke a verse:

「大仙人！我复见有世尊弟子三比丘等，亦从世尊修习梵行，不舍离欲，身坏命终，生余下贱伎乐宫中，彼既生已，日日来至三十三天供事诸天，奉侍瞿婆天子。天子见彼已，而说颂曰：

The upāsikā with the eyes, my name is Kuśa, I pay
respect to the Buddha and the Dharma, and with pure intention
I make offerings to the Saṅgha. I have already received the
Buddha's grace, the great merit and virtue of the Śākya son
I was reborn in the Thirty-three Heavens, and they know me
as the son of Kuśa. Seeing those former bhikṣus reborn as
musicians, standing with joined palms in front of me, Kuśa spoke
a verse: "Those were originally sons of the Gautama, when I
was a human, they came to my house and I offered
them food and drink. They were originally equal to the sages
practicing the unsurpassed holy life, but now they are servants of
others, coming daily to serve the gods. I originally served you
hearing the sages' well-spoken Dharma, I attained faith and accomplished the
precepts, and was reborn in the Thirty-three Heavens. You originally received
service, practicing the unsurpassed holy life, but now you are servants
of others, coming daily to serve the gods. What do you
take as your face, having upheld the Buddha's Dharma, yet turning
your back and not facing the Dharma? This is the well-spoken
truth of the eyes. I saw you before, now reborn in
the inferior state of musicians, practicing non-Dharma yourself, and being reborn
in non-Dharma. When I was still at home, seeing my superior
virtues now, I transformed from a woman to a son of
the gods, freely enjoying the five desires. They criticized the Gautamas
being disgusted, praised the Gautama. I will now practice, the son
of the gods truly speaks the truth. The two of them
diligently practiced, remembering the Dharma and Vinaya of the Gautama, knowing
that desire has calamities, they immediately abandoned desire. Bound by the
fetters of desire, they immediately attained freedom, like an elephant breaking
free from its bonds, they crossed to the Thirty-three Heavens. Indra
Brahmā, and all came to assemble, they immediately sat and ascended
the heroic one abandoning worldly desires. Śakra saw and was disgusted

The eye sees forms, the ear hears sounds, the nose smells fragrances, the tongue tastes flavors, the body feels tactile sensations, and the mind knows dharmas. 复问曰：「大仙人！比丘者，趣向灭戏道迹，命存一时顷，复断几法，行几法耶？」

Great Ascetic! But we did not get to meet the Tathāgata, the Unattached, the Perfectly Enlightened One, and then we indulged in the five kinds of sensual pleasures. Great Ascetic! We were negligent, and after being negligent, a very powerful god died in a supreme place. Great Ascetic! When I saw that very powerful god die in a supreme place, I became extremely disgusted, and my body hair stood on end. I thought, 'Do not let me die here quickly!'

时，天王释答曰：「世尊！唯大仙人自当知之。大仙人！三十三天集在法堂，各怀愁戚，数数叹说，我等若值如来、无所著、等正觉者，必当往见。大仙人！然我等不得值如来、无所著、等正觉已，便行具足五欲功德。大仙人！我等放逸，行放逸已，大威德天子于极妙处，即便命终，大仙人！我见大威德天子于极妙处，即命终时，便生极厌，身毛皆竖，莫令我于此处速命终。

Having studied wisdom, if I attain wisdom, I will attain ultimate wisdom and reach the ultimate limit. Having studied wisdom, if I attain wisdom but do not attain ultimate wisdom, if there are gods with great merit and virtue, majestic appearance, radiant and brilliant, with great power, peaceful and joyful, dwelling in palaces for a long time, born in the supreme, I will be reborn among them.'"

时，天王释答曰：「大仙人！我作是念：『我于此命终，生于人间，彼若有族，极大富乐，资财无量，畜牧、产业不可称计，封户、食邑种种具足，谓刹利长者族、梵志长者族、居士长者族及余族，极大富乐，资财无量，畜牧、产业不可称计，封户、食邑种种具足。生如是族已，成就诸根，如来所说法、律有得信者。得信已，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，学智。学智已，若得智者，便得究竟智，得究竟边。学智，学智已，若得智、不得究竟智者。若有诸天，有大福祐，色像巍巍，光耀炜烨极有威力，安隐快乐，长住宫殿，生于最上，我生彼中。』」

Then the Heavenly King Śakra spoke a verse, saying:

于是，天王释而说颂曰：

Abandoning the heavenly body, coming down to be born among
humans, not foolishly entering the womb, following my desired intentions
Having attained a complete body, attaining the straight and true
path, practicing the complete brahmacarya, always delighting in begging for food.

「舍离于天身，　　来下生人间，不愚痴入胎，　　随我意所乐。得身具足已，　　逮质直正道，行具足梵行，　　常乐于乞食。

"Having learned wisdom, if one attains wisdom, one will attain ultimate wisdom and reach the ultimate limit. Having learned wisdom, if one attains wisdom but does not attain ultimate wisdom, one will become the supreme and wonderful god, the god whose name is heard by all gods in the realm of the ultimate form. Great Sage! I wish to attain the state of non-returner. Great Sage! I am now definitely attaining the state of stream-enterer."

「学智，学智已，若得智者，便得究竟智，得究竟边，学智，学智已，若得智、不得究竟智者，当作最上妙天，诸天闻名，色究竟天，往生彼中。大仙人！愿当得阿那含。大仙人！我今定得须陀洹。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Kauśika, what causes you to attain this supreme, lofty, and vast distinction, and to proclaim that you have attained the state of stream-enterer?"

世尊问曰：「拘翼！汝何因得此极好、极高、极广差降，而自称说得须陀洹耶？」

At that time, Śakra, King of the Devas, replied in verse:

时，天王释以偈答曰：

There is no other honored one, only the realm of the
World-Honored One, attaining the supreme distinction, never before seen. Great Sage
I sit here, in this very heavenly body, I will further
increase my lifespan, seeing it with my own eyes in this way.

「不更有余尊，　　唯世尊境界，得最上差降，　　未曾有此处。大仙我此坐，　　即于此天身，我更得增寿，　　如是自眼见。」

When this teaching was spoken, Śakra, King of the Devas, was free from defilement and obscuration, and the Dharma eye of the Dharma arose for him, as well as for the eighty thousand devas, who were also free from defilement and obscuration, and the Dharma eye of the Dharma arose for them. Then Śakra, King of the Devas, saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, realized the pure Dharma, cut off doubts, transcended confusion, had no other honored ones, did not follow others, was without hesitation, had already attained the realization of the fruits, and was fearless in the teachings of the World-Honored One. He then rose from his seat, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and said, "World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and the community of monks. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept me as a lay devotee. From this day forth, until the end of my life, I take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and the community of monks, until the end of my life."

说此法时，天王释远尘离垢，诸法法眼生，及八万诸天亦远尘离垢，诸法法眼生。于是，天王释见法得法，觉白净法，断疑度惑，更无余尊，不复从他，无有犹豫，已住果证，于世尊法得无所畏。即从坐起，稽首佛足，白曰：「世尊！我今自归佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。」

Then Śakra, King of the Devas, praised the five sons of Kṣemaṅkara, saying, "Excellent! Excellent! You five sons have greatly benefited me. Why is that? Because of you, the Buddha has awakened from concentration. Since you made the World-Honored One awaken from concentration first, it allowed us to see the Buddha later. Five sons! I will return from here and give you the daughter of the king of music, the virtuous moon-like girl, as your wife, and appoint you as the king of music in your father's kingdom of music."

于是，天王释称叹五结乐子曰：「善哉！善哉！汝五结大益于我。所以者何？由汝故，佛从定寤，以汝先使世尊从定寤故，令我等后得见佛。五结！我从此归，以躭浮楼伎乐王女贤月色嫁与汝作妇，及其父乐王本国拜与汝作伎乐王。」

Then Śakra, King of the Devas, addressed the Thirty-three Heavens, saying, "You all come together! If we were formerly the Brahma King, dwelling in the Brahma Heaven, and paid respect and served him three times, he now pays respect and serves the World-Honored One. Why is that? The World-Honored One is the Brahma Heaven, the Brahma Heaven is the most honored creator of beings, and all beings, past, present, and future, are known and seen by him."

于是，天王释告三十三天曰：「汝等共来！若我等本为梵天王，住梵天上，再三恭敬礼事者，彼今尽为世尊恭敬礼事。所以者何？世尊梵天，梵天当造化最尊生众生，众生有及当有彼所，可知尽知，可见尽见。」

Then Śakra, King of the Devas, the Thirty-three Heavens, and the five sons of Kṣemaṅkara, who had formerly been the Brahma King, dwelling in the Brahma Heaven, and paid respect and served him three times, all paid respect and served the World-Honored One, bowing to the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One. Then Śakra, King of the Devas, the Thirty-three Heavens, and the five sons of Kṣemaṅkara again paid respect and served the World-Honored One, bowed at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and then suddenly disappeared from that place.

于是，天王释及三十三天、五结乐子，若本为梵天，住梵天上，再三恭敬礼事者，彼尽为世尊恭敬礼事，稽首如来、无所著、等正觉。于是，天王释及三十三天、五结乐子再为世尊恭敬礼事，稽首佛足，遶三匝已，即于彼处忽没不现。

At that time, Brahmā's appearance was majestic and his radiance was brilliant. In the early morning, he went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and immediately addressed the World-Honored One in verse:

尔时，梵天色像巍巍，光耀炜烨，夜将向旦，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，却住一面，即时以偈白世尊曰：

For the sake of great benefit and meaning, seeing benefit and meaning is
called heaven. The wise one abides in Magadha, and Vāṣpa asks about matters.

「为多饶益义，　　见利义曰天，贤住摩竭国，　　婆娑婆问事。

When the great sage spoke this Dharma, the heavenly king Śakra was free from defilement and impurities, and the Dharma eye of the Dharma arose. And the eighty thousand gods were also free from defilement and impurities, and the Dharma eye of the Dharma arose." Then the World-Honored One said to Brahmā Heaven: "So it is, so it is. As Brahmā Heaven has said:

「大仙人说此法时，天王释远尘离垢，诸法法眼生，及八万诸天亦远尘离垢，诸法法眼生。」于是，世尊告梵天曰：「如是，如是。梵天所说：

For the sake of great benefit and meaning, seeing benefit and meaning is
called heaven. The wise one abides in Magadha, and Vāṣpa asks about matters.

「『为多饶益义，　　见利义曰天，贤住摩竭国，　　婆娑婆问事。』

"Brahmā Heaven! When I spoke the Dharma, the heavenly king Śakra was free from defilement and impurities, and the Dharma eye of the Dharma arose. And the eighty thousand gods were also free from defilement and impurities, and the Dharma eye of the Dharma arose."

「梵天！我说法时，天王释远尘离垢，诸法法眼生，及八万诸天亦远尘离垢，诸法法眼生。」

The Buddha spoke thus. At that time, the heavenly king Śakra, the thirty-three gods, the five sons of pleasure, and the great Brahmā Heaven, having heard the Buddha's words, joyfully received and practiced them.

佛说如是。时，天王释及三十三天、五结乐子并大梵天，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
