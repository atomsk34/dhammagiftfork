MĀ 135

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 11 大品

135. （一三五）善生经

Parallel to: DN 31 / DĀ 16

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the city of Rājagṛha, staying in the Frog-infested Forest.

一时，佛游王舍城，在饶虾蟆林。

At that time, the householder's son Sudinna, when his father was dying, instructed his son with the six directions, saying, "Sudinna! After my death, you should join your palms and pay homage to the six directions, saying: 'If there are sentient beings in the east, I pay homage to them with respect, offerings, and service. Having paid homage to them with respect, offerings, and service, they will also pay homage to me with respect, offerings, and service. In the same way, if there are sentient beings in the south, west, north, below, and above, I pay homage to them with respect, offerings, and service. Having paid homage to them with respect, offerings, and service, they will also pay homage to me with respect, offerings, and service.'"

尔时，善生居士子，父临终时，因六方故，遗勅其子，善教善诃曰：「善生！我命终后，汝当叉手向六方礼：『东方若有众生者，我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼。我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼已，彼亦当恭敬、供养、礼事我。如是南方、西方、北方、下方、上方，若有众生者，我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼。我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼已，彼亦当恭敬、供养、礼事我。』」

The householder's son Sudinna, having heard his father's instructions, said to his father, "Yes, I will do as you command."

善生居士子闻父教已，白父曰：「唯。当如尊勅。」

Then, after his father's death, the householder's son Sudinna bathed early in the morning, put on new linen clothes, took a fresh kuśa grass leaf in his hand, went to the water's edge, and paid homage to the six directions with joined palms, saying: "If there are sentient beings in the east, I pay homage to them with respect, offerings, and service. Having paid homage to them with respect, offerings, and service, they will also pay homage to me with respect, offerings, and service. In the same way, if there are sentient beings in the south, west, north, below, and above, I pay homage to them with respect, offerings, and service. Having paid homage to them with respect, offerings, and service, they will also pay homage to me with respect, offerings, and service."

于是，善生居士子，父命终后，平旦沐浴，著新𫇴磨衣，手执生拘舍叶，往至水边，叉手向六方礼：「东方若有众生者，我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼。我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼已，彼亦当恭敬、供养、礼事我。如是南方、西方、北方、下方、上方，若有众生者，我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼。我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼已，彼亦当恭敬、供养、礼事我。」

At that time, the World-Honored One, having passed the night, put on his robe in the early morning, took his alms bowl, and entered the city of Rājagṛha to beg for alms. When the World-Honored One entered the city of Rājagṛha to beg for alms, he saw from afar the householder's son Sudinna bathing early in the morning, putting on new linen clothes, taking a fresh kuśa grass leaf in his hand, going to the water's edge, and paying homage to the six directions with joined palms, saying: "If there are sentient beings in the east, I pay homage to them with respect, offerings, and service. Having paid homage to them with respect, offerings, and service, they will also pay homage to me with respect, offerings, and service. In the same way, if there are sentient beings in the south, west, north, below, and above, I pay homage to them with respect, offerings, and service. Having paid homage to them with respect, offerings, and service, they will also pay homage to me with respect, offerings, and service."

彼时，世尊过夜平旦，著衣持钵，入王舍城而行乞食。世尊入王舍城乞食时，遥见善生居士子平旦沐浴，著新𫇴磨衣，手执生拘舍叶，往至水边，叉手向六方礼：「东方若有众生者，我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼。我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼已，彼亦当恭敬、供养、礼事我。如是南方、西方、北方、下方、上方，若有众生者，我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼。我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼已，彼亦当恭敬、供养、礼事我。」

The World-Honored One, having seen this, went to the householder's son Sudinna and asked: "Householder's son! Who is the śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa whose teachings you follow, causing you to pay homage to them with respect, offerings, and service, bathing early in the morning, putting on new linen clothes, taking a fresh kuśa grass leaf in your hand, going to the water's edge, and paying homage to the six directions with joined palms, saying: 'If there are sentient beings in the east, I pay homage to them with respect, offerings, and service. Having paid homage to them with respect, offerings, and service, they will also pay homage to me with respect, offerings, and service. In the same way, if there are sentient beings in the south, west, north, below, and above, I pay homage to them with respect, offerings, and service. Having paid homage to them with respect, offerings, and service, they will also pay homage to me with respect, offerings, and service.'"

世尊见已，往至善生居士子所，问曰：「居士子！受何沙门、梵志教，教汝恭敬、供养、礼事，平旦沐浴，著新刍磨衣，手执生拘舍叶，往至水边，叉手向六方礼：『东方若有众生者，我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼。我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼已，彼亦当恭敬、供养、礼事我。如是南方、西方、北方、下方、上方，若有众生者，我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼。我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼已，彼亦当恭敬、供养、礼事我耶？』」

The householder's son Sudinna replied: "World-Honored One! I do not follow the teachings of any other śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa. World-Honored One! When my father was dying, he gave me good advice and admonished me, saying: 'Sudinna! After my death, you should pay homage to the six directions with joined palms, saying: "If there are sentient beings in the east, I pay homage to them with respect, offerings, and service. Having paid homage to them with respect, offerings, and service, they will also pay homage to me with respect, offerings, and service." In the same way, if there are sentient beings in the south, west, north, below, and above, I pay homage to them with respect, offerings, and service. Having paid homage to them with respect, offerings, and service, they will also pay homage to me with respect, offerings, and service." World-Honored One! I follow my father's instructions and pay homage to them with respect, offerings, and service. That is why I bathe early in the morning, put on new linen clothes, take a fresh kuśa grass leaf in my hand, go to the water's edge, and pay homage to the six directions with joined palms, saying: "If there are sentient beings in the east, I pay homage to them with respect, offerings, and service. Having paid homage to them with respect, offerings, and service, they will also pay homage to me with respect, offerings, and service." In the same way, if there are sentient beings in the south, west, north, below, and above, I pay homage to them with respect, offerings, and service. Having paid homage to them with respect, offerings, and service, they will also pay homage to me with respect, offerings, and service.

善生居士子答曰：「世尊！我不受余沙门、梵志教也。世尊！我父临命终时，因六方故，遗勅于我，善教善诃曰：『善生！我命终后，汝当叉手向六方礼：「东方若有众生者，我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼。我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼已，彼亦当恭敬、供养、礼事我。如是南方、西方、北方、下方、上方，若有众生者，我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼。我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼已，彼亦当恭敬、供养、礼事我。」』世尊！我受父遗教，恭敬、供养、礼事故，平旦沐浴，著新刍磨衣，手执生拘舍叶，往至水边，叉手向六方礼：『东方若有众生者，我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼。我尽恭敬，供养、礼事彼已，彼亦当恭敬、供养、礼事我。如是南方、西方、北方、下方、上方，若有众生者，我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼。我尽恭敬、供养、礼事彼已，彼亦当恭敬、供养、礼事我。』」

Having heard this, the World-Honored One said: "Householder's son! I speak of the existence of the six directions, not their non-existence. Householder's son! If a person can distinguish the six directions and be free from the four kinds of evil and unwholesome actions, they will be respected and honored in this life, and upon the dissolution of the body and the end of life, they will certainly go to a good place and be reborn in heaven. Householder's son! Sentient beings have four kinds of actions and four kinds of defilements. What are the four? Householder's son! Killing living beings is a kind of action and defilement for sentient beings. Taking what is not given, sexual misconduct, and false speech are also kinds of action and defilement for sentient beings." Thereupon, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

世尊闻已，告曰：「居士子！我说有六方，不说无也。居士子！若有人善别六方，离四方恶不善业垢，彼于现法可敬可重，身坏命终，必至善处，上生天中。居士子！众生有四种业、四种秽。云何为四？居士子！杀生者，是众生业种、秽种，不与取、邪婬、妄言者，是众生业种、秽种。」于是，世尊说此颂曰：

Killing living beings, taking what is not given, sexual misconduct
and false speech - the wise do not praise these.

「杀生不与取，　　邪婬犯他妻，所言不真实，　　慧者不称誉。

"Householder's son! People incur much blame due to four things. What are the four? Engaging in desire, anger, fear, and delusion." Thereupon, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

「居士子！人因四事故，便得多罪。云何为四？行欲、行恚、行怖、行痴。」于是，世尊说此颂曰：

Desire, anger, fear, and delusion - engaging in evil and unwholesome
conduct. Their fame will surely be extinguished, like the waning moon.

「欲恚怖及痴，　　行恶非法行，彼必灭名称，　　如月向尽没。

"Householder's son! People incur much merit due to four things. What are the four? Not engaging in desire, anger, fear, and delusion." Thereupon, the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

「居士子！人因四事故，便得多福。云何为四？不行欲、不行恚、不行怖、不行痴。」于是，世尊说此颂曰：

Cutting off desire, anger, fear, and delusion - engaging in the practice
of the Dharma. Their fame is widely known, like the waxing moon.

「断欲无恚怖，　　无痴行法行，彼名称普闻，　　如月渐盛满。

Householder's son! Those who seek wealth should know that there are six wrong ways. What are the six? First, seeking wealth through various games is a wrong way. Second, seeking wealth through untimely activities is a wrong way. Third, seeking wealth through drinking and indulgence is a wrong way. Fourth, seeking wealth through associating with bad friends is a wrong way. Fifth, seeking wealth through constantly enjoying music and entertainment is a wrong way. Sixth, seeking wealth through laziness is a wrong way.

「居士子！求财物者，当知有六非道。云何为六？一曰种种戏求财物者为非道。二曰非时行求财物者为非道。三曰饮酒放逸求财物者为非道。四曰亲近恶知识求财物者为非道。五曰常喜妓乐求财物者为非道。六曰懒惰求财物者为非道。

Householder's son! If a person engages in various games, there are six calamities to be known. What are the six? First, when losing, one gives rise to resentment. Second, when losing, one gives rise to shame. Third, when losing, one sleeps uncomfortably. Fourth, it makes enemies rejoice. Fifth, it causes relatives to be worried. Sixth, when speaking in a crowd, people do not believe. Householder's son! Those who gamble do not engage in work. Not engaging in work, their accomplishments are not completed. They cannot obtain wealth that has not been obtained, and their existing wealth is depleted.

「居士子！若人种种戏者，当知有六灾患。云何为六？一者负则生怨。二者失则生耻。三者负则眠不安。四者令怨家怀喜。五者使宗亲怀忧。六者在众所说人不信用。居士子！人博戏者，不经营作事，作事不营，则功业不成，未得财物，则不能得，本有财物，便转消耗。

Householder's son! Those who engage in untimely activities should know that there are six calamities. What are the six? First, they do not protect themselves. Second, they do not protect their wealth. Third, they do not protect their wives and children. Fourth, they are suspected by others. Fifth, they give rise to much suffering. Sixth, they are slandered by others. Householder's son! Those who engage in untimely activities do not engage in work. Not engaging in work, their accomplishments are not completed. They cannot obtain wealth that has not been obtained, and their existing wealth is depleted.

「居士子，人非时行者，当知有六灾患。云何为六？一者不自护。二者不护财物。三者不护妻子。四者为人所疑。五者多生苦患。六者为人所谤。居士子！人非时行者，不经营作事，作事不营，则功业不成，未得财物，则不能得，本有财物，便转消耗。

Householder's son! Those who drink alcohol and are negligent should know that there are six calamities. First, they lose their wealth. Second, they have many illnesses. Third, they increase disputes. Fourth, they reveal secrets. Fifth, they are not praised and are not protected. Sixth, they destroy wisdom and give rise to delusion. Householder's son! Those who drink alcohol and are negligent do not engage in work. Not engaging in work, their accomplishments are not completed. They cannot obtain wealth that has not been obtained, and their existing wealth is depleted.

「居士子！若人饮酒放逸者，当知有六灾患。一者现财物失。二者多有疾患。三者增诸鬪诤。四者隐藏发露。五者不称不护。六者灭慧生痴。居士子！人饮酒放逸者，不经营作事，作事不营，则功业不成，未得财物，则不能得，本有财物，便转消耗。

Householder's son! Those who associate with bad friends should know that there are six calamities. What are the six? First, they associate with thieves. Second, they associate with deceivers. Third, they associate with the insane and intoxicated. Fourth, they associate with the unrestrained. Fifth, they pursue gatherings and play. Sixth, they take these as friends and companions. Householder's son! Those who associate with bad friends do not engage in work. Not engaging in work, their accomplishments are not completed. They cannot obtain wealth that has not been obtained, and their existing wealth is depleted.

「居士子！若人亲近恶知识者，当知有六灾患。云何为六？一者亲近贼。二者亲近欺诳。三者亲近狂醉。四者亲近放恣。五者逐会嬉戏。六者以此为亲友，以此为伴侣。居士子！若人亲近恶知识者，不经营作事，作事不营，则功业不成，未得财物，则不能得，本有财物，便转消耗。

Householder's son! Those who enjoy music should know that there are six calamities. What are the six? First, they enjoy hearing singing. Second, they enjoy watching dancing. Third, they enjoy going to musical performances. Fourth, they enjoy watching bell-ringing. Fifth, they enjoy clapping their hands. Sixth, they enjoy large gatherings. Householder's son! Those who enjoy music do not engage in work. Not engaging in work, their accomplishments are not completed. They cannot obtain wealth that has not been obtained, and their existing wealth is depleted.

「居士子！若人憙伎乐者，当知有六灾患。云何为六？一者憙闻歌。二者憙见舞。三者憙往作乐。四者憙见弄铃。五者憙拍两手。六者憙大聚会。居士子！若人憙伎乐者，不经营作事，作事不营，则功业不成，未得财物，则不能得，本有财物，便转消耗。

Householder's son! Those who are lazy should know that there are six calamities. What are the six? First, they do not work early in the morning. Second, they do not work late at night. Third, they do not work when it is very cold. Fourth, they do not work when it is very hot. Fifth, they do not work when they are very full. Sixth, they do not work when they are very hungry. Householder's son! Those who are lazy do not engage in work. Not engaging in work, their accomplishments are not completed. They cannot obtain wealth that has not been obtained, and their existing wealth is depleted.

「居士子！若有懒惰者，当知有六灾患。云何为六？一者大早不作业。二者大晚不作业。三者大寒不作业。四者大热不作业。五者大饱不作业。六者大饥不作业。居士子！若人懒惰者不经营作事，作事不营则功业不成，未得财物则不能得，本有财物便转消耗。」于是世尊说此颂曰：

Various kinds of play and chasing after colors, enjoying alcohol and
delighting in music, associating with bad friends, lazy and not working
indulging and not guarding oneself - these are places that destroy
people. Going and coming without guarding, engaging in sexual misconduct and
violating others' wives, always harboring resentment in the mind, seeking without
benefit, drinking alcohol and thinking of women - these are places
that destroy people. Repeatedly doing unwholesome actions, being obstinate and not
accepting teachings, scolding śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, being upside down with wrong
views, being violent and performing black deeds - these are places
that destroy people. Being oneself lacking in wealth, drinking alcohol and
losing one's clothes, owing debts like a fountain, one will surely
destroy one's family. Frequently going to the wine furnace, associating with
bad friends, one should obtain wealth but does not - this
is the pleasure of being a companion. Having many bad friends
always following bad companions, in this world and the next, both
will be destroyed. Practicing evil decreases, practicing good increases, practicing the
superior increases more - therefore one should practice the superior. Practicing
the superior leads to attainment of the superior, always attaining the
superior of wisdom, and further attaining pure precepts and the subtle
and supreme. During the day, delighting in sleeping and lying down
at night, delighting in wandering about. Indulging and always drinking alcohol
one cannot succeed in household life. Great cold and great heat
are said to be for lazy people. Ultimately not accomplishing work
one will not obtain wealth and benefits. If it is cold
or very hot, not considering it as if it were grass
If a person performs this kind of work, they will never lose happiness.

「种种戏逐色，　　嗜酒憙作乐，亲近恶知识，　　懒惰不作业，放恣不自护，　　此处坏败人。行来不防护，　　邪婬犯他妻，心中常结怨，　　求愿无有利，饮酒念女色，　　此处坏败人。重作不善行，　　佷戾不受教，骂沙门梵志，　　颠倒有邪见，凶暴行黑业，　　此处坏败人。自乏无财物，　　饮酒失衣被，负债如涌泉，　　彼必坏门族。数往至酒𬬻，　　亲近恶朋友，应得财不得，　　是伴党为乐。多有恶朋友，　　常随不善伴，今世及后世，　　二俱得败坏。人习恶转减，　　习善转兴盛，习胜者转增，　　是故当习胜。习升则得升，　　常逮智慧升，转获清净戒，　　及与微妙上，昼则喜眠卧，　　夜则好游行。放逸常饮酒，　　居家不得成，大寒及大热，　　谓有懒惰人。至竟不成业，　　终不获财利，若寒及大热，　　不计犹如草。若人作是业，　　彼终不失乐。

"Householder's son! There are four who are not close but seem close. What are the four? First, knowing the matter is not close but seems close. Second, loving words in front are not close but seem close. Third, speech is not close but seems close. Fourth, companions in the evil destinies are not close but seem close. Householder's son! For four reasons, knowing the matter is not close but seems close. What are the four? First, taking wealth by knowing the matter. Second, taking much with little. Third, or by frightening. Fourth, or by befriending for profit." Thereupon the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

「居士子！有四不亲而似亲。云何为四？一者知事非亲似如亲。二者面前爱言非亲似如亲。三者言语非亲似如亲。四者恶趣伴非亲似如亲。居士子！因四事故，知事非亲似如亲。云何为四？一者以知事夺财。二者以少取多。三者或以恐怖。四者或为利狎习。」于是世尊说此颂曰：

People take knowing as their business, their speech is gentle, they
frighten for profit and befriend, knowing it is not close but
seems close, they should always avoid it, like a path with fear.

「人以知为事，　　言语至柔软，怖为利狎习，　　知非亲如亲，常当远离彼，　　如道有恐怖。

"Householder's son! For four reasons, loving words in front are not close but seem close. What are the four? First, commanding fine things. Second, teaching to do evil. Third, praising in front. Fourth, speaking of their evil behind the back." Thereupon the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

「居士子！因四事故，面前爱言非亲似如亲。云何为四？一者制妙事。二者教作恶。三者面前称誉。四者背说其恶。」于是世尊说此颂曰：

If commanding fine and good dharmas, teaching to do evil and
unwholesome, praising in front, speaking of their evil behind the back
If knowing the fine and evil, also being aware of the
two statements, that which is close is not close, knowing that
person is like this. Should always avoid it, like a path with fear.

「若制妙善法，　　教作恶不善，对面前称誉，　　背后说其恶。若知妙及恶，　　亦复觉二说，是亲不可亲，　　知彼人如是。常当远离彼，　　如道有恐怖。

"Householder's son! For four reasons, speech is not close but seems close. What are the four? First, recognizing past events. Second, necessarily discussing future events. Third, speaking falsely and unreal. Fourth, present events will definitely cease, recognizing and speaking of what I will do or not do." Thereupon the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

「居士子！因四事故言语非亲似如亲。云何为四？一者认过去事。二者必辩当来事。三者虚不真说。四者现事必灭，我当作不作认说。」于是世尊说此颂曰：

Recognizing the past and future, falsely discussing present events that will
cease, speaking of what I will do or not do. Knowing
the non-close as close, should always avoid it, like a path with fear.

「认过及未来，　　虚论现灭事，当作不作说，　　知非亲如亲，常当远离彼，　　如道有恐怖。

"Householder's son! For four reasons, companions in evil destinies are not close but seem close. What are the four? First, teaching various games. Second, teaching untimely conduct. Third, teaching drinking alcohol. Fourth, teaching to associate with bad friends." Thereupon the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

「居士子！因四事故，恶趣伴非亲似如亲。云何为四？一者教种种戏。二者教非时行。三者教令饮酒。四者教亲近恶知识。」于是世尊说此颂曰：

Teaching various games, drinking alcohol, committing adultery with others' wives
practicing the inferior and not the superior. They perish like
the waning moon. Should always avoid them, like a path with fear.

「教若干种戏，　　饮酒犯他妻，习下不习胜，　　彼灭如月尽，常当远离彼，　　如道有恐怖。

"Householder's son! One should know that there are four kinds of good friends. What are the four? First, sharing in happiness and suffering, one should know this is a good friend. Second, having compassion, one should know this is a good friend. Third, seeking benefit, one should know this is a good friend. Fourth, being helpful, one should know this is a good friend. Householder's son! Because of four things, sharing in happiness and suffering, one should know this is a good friend. What are the four? First, giving up oneself for them. Second, giving up wealth for them. Third, giving up one's wife and children for them. Fourth, being able to endure what is said." Thereupon the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

「居士子！善亲当知有四种。云何为四？一者同苦乐，当知是善亲。二者愍念，当知是善亲。三者求利，当知是善亲。四者饶益，当知是善亲。居士子！因四事故同苦乐，当知是善亲。云何为四？一者为彼舍己。二者为彼舍财。三者为彼舍妻子。四者所说堪忍。」于是世尊说此颂曰：

Giving up desires, wealth, and wife and children, being able to endure what is
said, knowing that friends share in happiness and suffering, the wise should associate with them.

「舍欲财妻子，　　所说能堪忍，知亲同苦乐，　　慧者当狎习。

"Householder's son! Because of four things, having compassion, one should know this is a good friend. What are the four? First, teaching the sublime Dharma. Second, restraining evil dharmas. Third, praising in front of others. Fourth, warding off enemies." Thereupon the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

「居士子！因四事故愍念，当知是善亲。云何为四？一者教妙法。二者制恶法。三者面前称说。四者却怨家。」于是世尊说此颂曰：

Teaching the sublime good, restraining the evil, praising in front of others, warding off
enemies, knowing the good friend who has compassion, the wise should associate with them.

「教妙善制恶，　　面称却怨家，知善亲愍念，　　慧者当狎习。

"Householder's son! Because of four things, seeking benefit, one should know this is a good friend. What are the four? First, revealing secret matters. Second, not concealing secrets. Third, rejoicing in gain. Fourth, not being sad in the absence of gain." Thereupon the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

「居士子！因四事故求利，当知是善亲。云何为四？一者密事发露。二者密不覆藏。三者得利为喜。四者不得利不忧。」于是世尊说此颂曰：

Revealing secret matters, not concealing secrets, rejoicing in gain, not being sad in the absence
of gain, knowing the good friend who seeks benefit, the wise should associate with them.

「密事露不藏，　　利喜无不忧，知善亲求利，　　慧者当狎习。

"Householder's son! Because of four things, benefiting, one should know this is a good friend. What are the four? First, knowing when wealth is exhausted. Second, knowing when wealth is exhausted, then providing things. Third, seeing indulgence, teaching to rebuke. Fourth, always with compassion." Thereupon the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

「居士子！因四事故饶益，当知是善亲。云何为四？一者知财物尽。二者知财物尽已便给与物。三者见放逸教诃。四者常以愍念。」于是世尊说此颂曰：

Knowing when wealth is exhausted, giving things, teaching to rebuke indulgence, and having
compassion. Knowing the good friend who benefits, the wise should associate with them.

「知财尽与物，　　放逸教愍念，知善亲饶益，　　慧者当狎习。

Householder's son! In the holy Dharma and Vinaya, there are six directions: east, south, west, north, below, and above. Householder's son! As for the east, a son should view his parents and should respectfully serve and support them with five things. What are the five? First, increasing wealth. Second, providing for various needs. Third, fulfilling their wishes. Fourth, not going against their wishes. Fifth, offering all private possessions. A son respectfully serves and supports his parents with these five things. Parents also think well of their son with five things. What are the five? First, loving their son. Second, providing without shortage. Third, not letting their son go into debt. Fourth, arranging a suitable marriage. Fifth, giving all their possessions to their son. Parents think well of their son with these five things. Householder's son! In this way, the east is distinguished in both ways. Householder's son! In the holy Dharma and Vinaya, the east refers to sons and parents. Householder's son! If one is filial and kind to one's parents, there will definitely be increase and no decline.

「居士子！圣法律中有六方，东方、南方、西方、北方、下方、上方。居士子！如东方者，如是子观父母，子当以五事奉敬供养父母。云何为五？一者增益财物。二者备办众事。三者所欲则奉。四者自恣不违。五者所有私物尽以奉上。子以此五事奉敬供养父母，父母亦以五事善念其子，云何为五？一者爱念儿子。二者供给无乏。三者令子不负债。四者婚娶称可。五者父母可意所有财物尽以付子。父母以此五事善念其子。居士子！如是东方二俱分别。居士子！圣法律中东方者，谓子、父母也。居士子！若慈孝父母者，必有增益则无衰耗。

Householder's son! As for the south, a disciple should view his teacher and should respectfully serve and support him with five things. What are the five? First, being well-behaved and obedient. Second, serving well. Third, rising quickly. Fourth, doing tasks well. Fifth, being able to respectfully serve the teacher. A disciple respectfully serves and supports his teacher with these five things. The teacher also thinks well of his disciple with five things. What are the five? First, teaching skills. Second, teaching quickly. Third, teaching all that is known. Fourth, placing in a good place. Fifth, entrusting to a good friend. The teacher thinks well of his disciple with these five things. Householder's son! In this way, the south is distinguished in both ways. Householder's son! In the holy Dharma and Vinaya, the south refers to disciples and teachers. Householder's son! If one is kind and obedient to one's teacher, there will definitely be increase and no decline.

「居士子！如南方者，如是弟子观师，弟子当以五事恭敬供养于师。云何为五？一者善恭顺。二者善承事。三者速起。四者所作业善。五者能奉敬师。弟子以此五事恭敬供养于师，师亦以五事善念弟子。云何为五？一者教技术。二者速教。三者尽教所知。四者安处善方。五者付嘱善知识。师以此五事善念弟子。居士子！如是南方二俱分别。居士子！圣法律中南方者，谓弟子、师也。居士子！若人慈顺于师者，必有增益则无衰耗。

Householder's son! As for the west, a husband should view his wife and children and should love, respect, and support them with five things. What are the five? First, having compassion for wife and children. Second, not being disrespectful. Third, making necklaces and ornaments. Fourth, having control in the household. Fifth, thinking of wife and relatives. A husband loves, respects, and supports his wife and children with these five things. Wife and children should respectfully serve their husband with thirteen things. What are the thirteen? First, cherishing and respecting husband. Second, cherishing and supporting husband. Third, thinking well of husband. Fourth, taking charge of work. Fifth, well-controlling relatives. Sixth, attending to husband in advance. Seventh, loving actions after. Eighth, speaking truthfully. Ninth, not forbidding the door. Tenth, welcoming with praise. Eleventh, preparing a bed. Twelfth, providing clean, delicious, and abundant food. Thirteenth, supporting śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas. Wife and children respectfully serve their husband with these thirteen things. Householder's son! In this way, the west is distinguished in both ways. Householder's son! In the holy Dharma and Vinaya, the west refers to husbands and wives. Householder's son! If one has compassion for one's wife and children, there will definitely be increase and no decline.

「居士子！如西方者，如是夫观妻子，夫当以五事爱敬供给妻子。云何为五？一者怜念妻子。二者不轻慢。三者为作璎珞严具。四者于家中得自在。五者念妻亲亲。夫以此五事爱敬供给妻子，妻子当以十三事善敬顺夫，云何十三？一者重爱敬夫。二者重供养夫。三者善念其夫。四者摄持作业。五者善摄眷属。六者前以瞻侍。七者后以爱行。八者言以诚实。九者不禁制门。十者见来赞善。十一者敷设床待。十二者施设净美丰饶饮食。十三者供养沙门梵志。妻子以此十三事善敬顺夫。居士子！如是西方二俱分别。居士子！圣法律中西方者，谓夫、妻子也。居士子！若人慈愍妻子者，必有增益则无衰耗。

Householder's son! As for the north, a master should view his servants and workers and should have compassion and provide for them with five things. What are the five? First, making them work according to their ability. Second, giving them food in a timely manner. Third, giving them drink in a timely manner. Fourth, giving them rest during the day. Fifth, providing medicine when sick. A master has compassion and provides for his servants and workers with these five things. Servants and workers should respectfully serve their master with nine things. What are the nine? First, working in a timely manner. Second, working with a concentrated mind. Third, working on everything. Fourth, attending to the master in advance. Fifth, loving actions after. Sixth, speaking truthfully. Seventh, not being far away at times of urgency. Eighth, praising when going to other places. Ninth, praising the master's family. Servants and workers respectfully serve their master with these nine things. Householder's son! In this way, the north is distinguished in both ways. Householder's son! In the holy Dharma and Vinaya, the north refers to masters and servants. Householder's son! If one has compassion for one's servants and workers, there will definitely be increase and no decline.

「居士子！如北方者，如是大家观奴婢使人，大家当以五事愍念给恤奴婢使人。云何为五？一者随其力而作业。二者随时食之。三者随时饮之。四者及日休息。五者病给汤药。大家以此五事愍念给恤奴婢使人，奴婢使人当以九事善奉大家。云何为九？一者随时作业。二者专心作业。三者一切作业。四者前以瞻侍。五者后以爱行。六者言以诚实。七者急时不远离。八者行他方时则便赞叹。九者称大家庶几。奴婢使人以此九事善奉大家。居士子！如是北方二俱分别。居士子！圣法律中北方者，谓大家、奴婢使人也。居士子！若有人慈愍奴婢使人者，必有增益则无衰耗。

Householder's son! As for the south, a friend should view his friend and minister and should love, respect, and support him with five things. What are the five? First, love and respect. Second, not being disrespectful. Third, not deceiving. Fourth, giving precious treasures. Fifth, thinking of the friend and minister. A friend loves, respects, and supports his friend and minister with these five things. A friend and minister also thinks well of his friend with five things. What are the five? First, knowing when wealth is exhausted. Second, knowing when wealth is exhausted and providing wealth. Third, teaching and admonishing when seeing indulgence. Fourth, loving. Fifth, being a refuge in times of urgency. A friend and minister thinks well of his friend with these five things. Householder's son! In this way, the south is distinguished in both ways. Householder's son! In the holy Dharma and Vinaya, the south refers to friends and ministers. Householder's son! If one has compassion for one's friend and minister, there will definitely be increase and no decline.

「居士子！如下方者，如是亲友观亲友臣，亲友当以五事爱敬供给亲友臣。云何为五？一者爱敬。二者不轻慢。三者不欺诳。四者施与珍宝。五者拯念亲友臣。亲友以此五事爱敬供给亲友臣，亲友臣亦以五事善念亲友，云何为五？一者知财物尽。二者知财物尽已供给财物。三者见放逸教诃。四者爱念。五者急时可归依。亲友臣以此五事善念亲友。居士子！如是下方二俱分别。居士子！圣法律中下方者，谓亲友、亲友臣也。居士子！若人慈愍亲友臣者，必有增益则无衰耗。

Householder's son! As for the east, a donor should view śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas and should respect and make offerings to them with five things. What are the five? First, not forbidding the gate. Second, praising well when seeing them come. Third, preparing a seat and waiting. Fourth, providing clean, delicious, and abundant food and drink. Fifth, protecting according to the Dharma. A donor respects and makes offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas with these five things. Śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas also think well of the donor with five things. What are the five? First, teaching faith, belief, and confidence. Second, teaching precepts. Third, teaching extensive learning. Fourth, teaching generosity. Fifth, teaching wisdom, establishing wisdom, and upholding wisdom. Śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas think well of the donor with these five things. Householder's son! In this way, the east is distinguished in both ways. Householder's son! In the holy Dharma and Vinaya, the east refers to donors and śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas. Householder's son! If one respects śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, there will definitely be increase and no decline.

「居士子！如上方者，如是施主观沙门梵志，施主当以五事尊敬供养沙门梵志。云何为五？一者不禁制门。二者见来赞善。三者敷设床待。四者施设净美丰饶饮食。五者拥护如法。施主以此五事尊敬供养沙门梵志，沙门梵志亦以五事善念施主。云何为五？一者教信行信念信。二者教禁戒。三者教博闻。四者教布施。五者教慧行慧立慧。沙门梵志以此五事善念施主。居士子！如是上方二俱分别。居士子！圣法律中上方者，谓施主、沙门梵志也。居士子！若人尊奉沙门梵志者，必有增益则无衰耗。

"Householder's son! There are four means of attracting disciples. What are the four? First, generosity. Second, kind words. Third, beneficial conduct. Fourth, equality." Thereupon the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

「居士子，有四摄事，云何为四？一者惠施。二者爱言。三者行利。四者等利。」于是世尊说此颂曰：

Generosity and kind words, always benefiting others, equality in benefiting
beings, the fame reaches far and wide, this then attracts
the world, like a charioteer. If there is no means
of attracting, a mother does not receive offerings and respect
because of her child, a father also receives offerings and
respect because of his child. If there is this means
of attracting, one then receives great blessings. Shining far like
sunlight, swift benefits are like a swift horse, not coarse
speech is wise, thus one attains fame. Definitely attaining without
effort is high, swift benefits are like a swift horse
accomplishing faith and diligence, thus one attains fame. Always being
diligent, joyfully giving food and drink to others, leading and
guiding correctly, thus one attains fame. Relatives and ministers share
the same concern, loving and enjoying within limits, being attracted
to relatives, as wonderful as a lion. First one should
learn skills, then seek wealth, having sought wealth, one should
divide it into four parts. One part is used for
food and drink, one part is used for farming, one
part is stored for emergencies, farming and merchants provide, one
part is used for interest, the fifth is for taking
a wife, the sixth is for building a house. If
a household has these six things, without increasing, one quickly
attains happiness. They will definitely have wealth, like the flowing
water in the ocean. They seek wealth like bees collecting
flowers, seeking wealth for a long time, one should enjoy
happiness oneself. Spending money should not be far away, nor
should it be scattered everywhere, one should not give money
to the violent and the powerful. The east is for
parents, the south is for teachers, the west is for
wives and children, the north is for slaves and servants
the lower direction is for relatives and ministers, and the
upper direction is for śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas. Wishing to pay
homage to these directions, both attain great fame. Having paid
homage to these directions, the donor is born in heaven.

「惠施及爱言，　　常为他行利，众生等同利，　　名称普远至，此则摄持世，　　犹如御车人。若无摄持者，　　母不因其子，得供养恭敬，　　父因子亦然，若有此法摄，　　故得大福祐。照远犹日光，　　速利翻捷疾，不麤说聪明，　　如是得名称。定获无功高，　　速利翻捷疾，成就信尸赖，　　如是得名称。常起不懒惰，　　憙施人饮食，将去调御正，　　如是得名称。亲友臣同恤，　　爱乐有齐限，谓摄在亲中，　　殊妙如师子。初当学技术，　　于后求财物，后求财物已，　　分别作四分。一分作饮食，　　一分作田业，一分举藏置，　　急时赴所须，耕作商人给，　　一分出息利，第五为取妇，　　第六作屋宅。家若具六事，　　不增快得乐，彼必饶钱财，　　如海中水流。彼如是求财，　　犹如蜂采花，长夜求钱财，　　当自受快乐。出财莫令远，　　亦勿令普漫，不可以财与，　　凶暴及豪强。东方为父母，　　南方为师尊，西方为妻子，　　北方为奴婢，下方亲友臣，　　上沙门梵志。愿礼此诸方，　　二俱得大称，礼此诸方已，　　施主得生天。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The householder's son, Sudatta, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced them.

佛说如是。善生居士子闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
