MĀ 138

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 11 大品

138. （一三八）福经

Parallel to: AN 7.62

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Do not fear merit, but delight in what is intended. Why is that? Merit is said to be happiness. Those who fear merit and do not delight in what is intended, why is that? Non-merit is said to be suffering. Because I remember in the past, for a long time I made merit, for a long time I received the reward, delighting in what was intended. In the past, for seven years I practiced loving-kindness, seven times I achieved and declined, not coming to this world. When the world declined, I was born in the Ābhāsvara Heaven. When the world was established, I descended and was born in the empty Brahmā palace. Among those Brahmās, I became the Great Brahmā Heaven. In other places, I was the Lord of the Heaven of the Thirty-three for a thousand times, and thirty-six times I was the Heavenly King Śakra. I was also the king of the Kṣatriyas born from the crown of the head for countless times.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「莫畏于福，爱乐意所念。所以者何？福者是说乐。畏于福，不爱乐意所念。所以者何？非福者，是说苦。何以故？我忆往昔长夜作福，长夜受报，爱乐意所念。我往昔时七年行慈，七返成败，不来此世，世败坏时，生晃昱天，世成立时，来下生空梵宫殿中，于彼梵中，作大梵天，余处千返作自在天王，三十六返作天帝释，复无量返作刹利顶生王。

Bhikṣus! When I was the Kṣatriya King born from the crown of the head, I had eighty-four thousand great elephants, adorned with fine riding equipment and various precious ornaments, covered with white pearl necklaces, with the elephant king Saha at the head. Bhikṣus! When I was the Kṣatriya King born from the crown of the head, I had eighty-four thousand horses, adorned with fine riding equipment and various precious ornaments, interwoven with gold and silver, with the horse king Māra at the head. Bhikṣus! When I was the Kṣatriya King born from the crown of the head, I had eighty-four thousand chariots, adorned with four kinds of ornaments and various fine things, woven with the spotted skins of lions, tigers, and leopards, with various ornaments, the most sharp and fast chariot named Leisong at the head. Bhikṣus! When I was the Kṣatriya King born from the crown of the head, I had eighty-four thousand great cities, extremely rich and happy, with many people, with the city of King Kuśāgra at the head. Bhikṣus! When I was the Kṣatriya King born from the crown of the head, I had eighty-four thousand towers, with four kinds of precious towers made of gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal, with the Zhengfa Hall at the head.

「比丘！我作刹利顶生王时，有八万四千大象，被好乘具，众宝珓饰，白珠珞覆，于娑贺象王为首。比丘！我作刹利顶生王时，有八万四千马，被好乘具，众宝严饰，金银交络，［马＊毛］马王为首。比丘！我作刹利顶生王时，有八万四千车，四种珓饰，庄以众好，师子、虎豹斑文之皮织成杂色，种种珓饰，极利疾名乐声车为首。比丘！我作刹利顶生王时，有八万四千大城，极大富乐，多有人民，拘舍惒提王城为首。比丘！我作刹利顶生王时，有八万四千楼，四种宝楼，金、银、琉璃及水精，正法殿为首。

Bhikṣus! When I was the Kṣatriya King born from the crown of the head, I had eighty-four thousand thrones, with four kinds of precious thrones made of gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal, covered with woolen blankets, spread with brocade, gauze, and fine silk, with a body-covering robe, with pillows at both ends, and with a Kālinga robe. Bhikṣus! When I was the Kṣatriya King born from the crown of the head, I had eighty-four thousand pairs of garments, with Kāśika cloth, brocade cloth, silk cloth, and Kālinga cloth. Bhikṣus! When I was the Kṣatriya King born from the crown of the head, I had eighty-four thousand women, with radiant and lustrous bodies, bright and pure, with beauty surpassing humans but slightly inferior to the gods, with pleasing and upright features, delightful to behold, fully adorned with various precious necklaces and ornaments, all from the Kṣatriya caste, with countless other clans.

「比丘！我作刹利顶生王时，有八万四千御座，四种宝座，金、银、琉璃及水精，敷以氍氀、毾［毯－炎＋登］，覆以锦绮罗縠，有𠋆体被，两头安枕，加陵伽波惒罗、波遮悉哆罗那。比丘！我作刹利顶生王时，有八万四千双衣，有初摩衣，有锦缯衣，有劫贝衣，有加陵伽波惒罗衣。比丘！我作刹利顶生王时，有八万四千女，身体光泽，皦洁明净，美色过人，小不及天，恣容端正，覩者欢悦，众宝璎珞严饰具足，尽刹利种女，余族无量。

Bhikṣus! When I was the Kṣatriya King born from the crown of the head, I had eighty-four thousand kinds of food, constantly offered day and night, prepared for me to eat. Bhikṣus! Among those eighty-four thousand kinds of food, there was one kind of food that was extremely delicious and pure, with countless flavors, which I always ate. Bhikṣus! Among those eighty-four thousand women, there was one Kṣatriya woman who was the most upright and beautiful, who always attended to me. Bhikṣus! Among those eighty-four thousand pairs of garments, there was one pair of garments, either Kāśika cloth, brocade cloth, silk cloth, or Kālinga cloth, which I always wore. Bhikṣus! Among those eighty-four thousand thrones, there was one throne, either made of gold, silver, lapis lazuli, or crystal, covered with woolen blankets, spread with brocade, gauze, and fine silk, with a body-covering robe, with pillows at both ends, and with a Kālinga robe, which I always sat on.

「比丘！我作刹利顶生王时，有八万四千种食，昼夜常供，为我故设，欲令我食。比丘！彼八万四千种食中，有一种食，极美净洁，无量种味，是我常所食。比丘！彼八万四千女中，有一刹利女，最端正姝妙，常奉侍我。比丘！彼八万四千双衣中，有一双衣，或初摩衣，或锦缯衣，或劫贝衣，或加陵伽波惒逻衣，是我常所著。比丘！彼八万四千御座中，有一御座，或金或银，或琉璃，或水精，敷以氍氀、毾［毯－炎＋登］，覆以锦绮罗縠，有𠋆体被，两头安枕，加陵伽波惒逻、波遮悉哆逻那，是我常所卧。

Bhikṣus! Among those eighty-four thousand pavilions, there was one pavilion, either made of gold, silver, lapis lazuli, or crystal, called the Dharma Hall, which I always stayed in. Bhikṣus! Among those eighty-four thousand great cities, there was one city that was extremely rich and happy, with many people, called Kuśāvatī, which I always lived in. Bhikṣus! Among those eighty-four thousand chariots, there was one chariot adorned with various beauties, woven with the spotted skins of lions, tigers, and leopards, with various decorations, extremely sharp and fast, called the Joyful Sound Chariot, which I always rode in to go to the pleasure garden to view. Bhikṣus! Among those eighty-four thousand horses, there was one horse with a dark blue body and a head like a crow, called the Horse King, which I always rode to go to the pleasure garden to view. Bhikṣus! Among those eighty-four thousand great elephants, there was one elephant with a completely white body and seven limbs perfectly straight, called the Elephant King, which I always rode to go to the pleasure garden to view.

「比丘！彼八万四千楼观中，有一楼观，或金或银，或琉璃，或水精，名正法殿，是我常所住。比丘！彼八万四千大城中，有一城极大富乐，多有人民，名拘舍惒提，是我常所居。比丘！彼八万四千车中而有一车，庄以众好，师子、虎豹斑文之皮织成杂色，种种庄饰，极利疾名乐声车，是我常所载，至观望园观。比丘！彼八万四千马中而有一马，体绀青色，头像如乌，名［马＊毛］马王，是我常所骑，至观望园观。比丘！彼八万四千大象中而有一象，举体极白，七支尽正，名于娑贺象王，是我常所乘，至观望园观。

"Bhikṣus! I thought like this: 'What are the fruits of karma, what are the results of karma, that cause me to have great supernatural powers, great authority, great blessings, and great spiritual power today?' Bhikṣus! I further thought like this: 'These are the fruits of the three karmas, the results of the three karmas, that cause me to have great supernatural powers, great authority, great blessings, and great spiritual power today. One is giving, two is taming, and three is guarding.'"

「比丘！我作此念：『是何业果，为何业报，令我今日有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神？』比丘！我复作此念：『是三业果，为三业报，令我今日有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神。一者布施，二者调御，三者守护。』」

Then the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

于是，世尊说此颂曰：

Looking at the rewards of this merit, it is wonderful, good, and very beneficial
Bhikṣus! In the past, I practiced loving-kindness for seven years. Seven times I experienced
the eons of formation and destruction, not returning to this world. When the world
was destroyed, I was reborn in the Ābhāsvara Heaven. When the world was formed
I was reborn in the Brahmā Heaven. In the Brahmā Heaven, I became the
Great Brahmā, and was reborn as the Lord of a Thousand Worlds a thousand
times. Thirty-six times I was born as Śakra, and countless times as the King
of the Topknot. As a Kṣatriya King with a Topknot, I was the most
honored among humans. Ruling the world according to the Dharma, without using swords or
weapons, I governed the world according to the Dharma, without adding any harm. I
peacefully and happily instructed according to the Dharma. Passing it on according to the
Dharma, it spread throughout the entire great earth. I was greatly wealthy, with much
money and wealth, and was born into such a clan. My wealth and grain
were fully abundant, and I accomplished the seven precious treasures. Because of this great
merit and blessings, I was born with mastery. The Buddhas rule the world, and
the Dharma they teach is known to be very wonderful. Seeing the supernatural powers
there are not many who do not believe. Who would know and not believe?
Being born in such darkness, therefore one should do it for oneself, seeking great
merit and blessings. One should respect the Dharma and always remember the Buddha's teachings
and the Dharma.

「观此福之报，　　妙善多饶益，比丘我在昔，　　七年修慈心。七反成败劫，　　不来还此世，世间败坏时，　　生于晃昱天。世间转成时，　　生于梵天中，在梵为大梵，　　千生自在天。三十六为释，　　无量百顶王，刹利顶生王，　　为人之最尊。如法非刀杖，　　政御于天下，如法不加抂，　　正安乐教授。如法转相传，　　遍一切大地，大富多钱财，　　生于如是族。财谷具足满，　　成就七宝珍，因此大福祐，　　所生得自在。诸佛御于世，　　彼佛之所说，知此甚奇特，　　见神通不少。谁知而不信，　　如是生于冥，是故当自为，　　欲求大福祐，当恭敬于法，　　常念佛法律。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
