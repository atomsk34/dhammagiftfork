MĀ 14

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 2 业相应品

14. To Rāhula （一四）罗云经

Parallel to: MN 61

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the city of Rājagṛha, staying in the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove.

一时，佛游王舍城，在竹林迦兰哆园。

At that time, Venerable Rāhula was also wandering in the Hot Spring Grove in Rājagṛha. Then, the World-Honored One, having passed the night, put on his robe, took his alms bowl, entered the city of Rājagṛha to beg for food, and after finishing his alms round, went to the Hot Spring Grove where Rāhula was staying. Venerable Rāhula saw the Buddha coming from afar, immediately went to greet him, took the Buddha's robe and bowl, prepared a seat, fetched water to wash the Buddha's feet, and after the Buddha washed his feet, he sat on Rāhula's seat.

尔时，尊者罗云亦游王舍城温泉林中。于是，世尊过夜平旦，著衣持钵入王舍城而行乞食，乞食已竟，至温泉林罗云住处。尊者罗云遥见佛来，即便往迎，取佛衣钵，为敷坐具，汲水洗足，佛洗足已，坐罗云座。

Then, the World-Honored One took the water vessel, left a little water, and asked, "Rāhula, do you see me taking this water vessel and leaving a little water?"

于是，世尊即取水器，泻留少水已，问曰：「罗云！汝今见我取此水器，泻留少水耶？」

Rāhula replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

罗云答曰：「见也。世尊！」

The Buddha told Rāhula, "I say that path is small, and it is also like this. It means that after knowing, one still speaks falsely, without shame or regret, without conscience or shame. Rāhula! They also do no evil. Therefore, Rāhula! One should study like this, not speaking falsely even in jest."

佛告罗云：「我说彼道少，亦复如是，谓知已妄言，不羞不悔，无惭无愧，罗云！彼亦无恶不作。是故，罗云！当作是学，不得戏笑妄言。」

The World-Honored One again took this small water vessel, emptied it, and asked, "Rāhula! Do you see me again taking the small water vessel and emptying it?"

世尊复取此少水器，尽泻弃已，问曰：「罗云！汝复见我取少水器，尽泻弃耶？」

Rāhula replied, "I see, World-Honored One!"

罗云答曰：「见也。世尊！」

The Buddha told Rāhula, "I say that the path of abandoning is also like this. That is, knowing that one has spoken falsely, one is not ashamed, does not regret it, has no sense of shame, and has no sense of decency. Rāhula! They also do no evil. Therefore, Rāhula! One should train like this: Do not speak falsely even in jest."

佛告罗云：「我说彼道尽弃，亦复如是，谓知已妄言，不羞不悔，无惭无愧，罗云！彼亦无恶不作。是故，罗云！当作是学，不得戏笑妄言。」

The World-Honored One again took this empty water vessel, turned it upside down on the ground, and asked, "Rāhula! Do you see me again taking the empty water vessel and turning it upside down on the ground?"

世尊复取此空水器，覆著地已，问曰：「罗云！汝复见我取空水器，覆著地耶？」

Rāhula replied, "I see, World-Honored One!"

罗云答曰：「见也。世尊！」

The Buddha told Rāhula, "I say that covering up that path is also like this, meaning that after knowing, one still speaks falsely, without shame or regret, without conscience or shame. Rāhula! They also do no evil. Therefore, Rāhula! One should study like this, not to speak falsely out of playfulness and laughter."

佛告罗云：「我说彼道覆，亦复如是，谓知已妄言，不羞不悔，无惭无愧，罗云！彼亦无恶不作。是故罗云！当作是学，不得戯笑妄言。」

The World-Honored One again took this covered water vessel, turned it upside down, and asked, "Rāhula! Do you see me again taking the covered water vessel and turning it upside down?"

世尊复取此覆水器，发令仰已，问曰：「罗云！汝复见我取覆水器，发令仰耶？」

Rāhula replied, "I see, World-Honored One!"

罗云答曰：「见也。世尊！」

The Buddha told Rāhula, "I say that path is also like that, meaning that knowing one has spoken falsely, one is not ashamed, does not regret, is not embarrassed, and does not feel guilty. Rāhula! They also do no evil. Therefore, Rāhula! One should train like this, not speaking falsely even in jest.

佛告罗云：「我说彼道仰，亦复如是，谓知已妄言，不羞不悔，不惭不愧，罗云！彼亦无恶不作。是故，罗云！当作是学，不得戏笑妄言。

Rāhula! It is like when a king's great elephant enters the battlefield to fight, it uses its front legs, back legs, tail, hips, spine, sides, neck, forehead, ears, and tusks, all of them, but only protects its trunk. The elephant trainer, seeing this, thinks, 'This king's great elephant still values its life. Why is that? This king's great elephant, when entering the battlefield to fight, uses its front legs, back legs, tail, hips, spine, sides, neck, forehead, ears, and tusks, all of them, but only protects its trunk.' Rāhula! If the king's great elephant, when entering the battlefield to fight, uses its front legs, back legs, tail, hips, spine, sides, neck, forehead, ears, tusks, and trunk, all of them, the elephant trainer, seeing this, thinks, 'This king's great elephant no longer values its life. Why is that? This king's great elephant, when entering the battlefield to fight, uses its front legs, back legs, tail, hips, spine, sides, neck, forehead, ears, tusks, and trunk, all of them.' Rāhula! If the king's great elephant, when entering the battlefield to fight, uses its front legs, back legs, tail, hips, spine, sides, neck, forehead, ears, tusks, and trunk, all of them, Rāhula! I say that this king's great elephant, when entering the battlefield to fight, does no evil. So it is, Rāhula! Meaning that knowing one has spoken falsely, one is not ashamed, does not regret, is not embarrassed, and does not feel guilty. Rāhula! I say that they also do no evil. Therefore, Rāhula! One should train like this, not speaking falsely even in jest.

「罗云！犹如王有大象入阵鬪时，用前脚、后脚、尾、髂、脊、脇、项、额、耳、牙，一切皆用，唯护于鼻，象师见已，便作是念：『此王大象犹故惜命。所以者何？此王大象入阵鬪时，用前脚、后脚、尾、髂、脊、脇、项、额、耳、牙，一切皆用，唯护于鼻。』罗云！若王大象入阵鬪时，用前脚、后脚、尾、髂、脊、脇、项、额、耳、牙、鼻，一切尽用，象师见已，便作是念：『此王大象不复惜命。所以者何？此王大象入阵鬪时，用前脚、后脚、尾、髂、脊、脇、项、额、耳、牙、鼻，一切尽用。』罗云！若王大象入阵鬪时，用前脚、后脚、尾、髂、脊、脇、项、额、耳、牙、鼻，一切尽用，罗云！我说此王大象入阵鬪时，无恶不作。如是，罗云！谓知已妄言，不羞不悔，无惭无愧，罗云！我说彼亦无恶不作。是故，罗云！当作是学，不得戏笑妄言。」

Then the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

于是，世尊即说颂曰：

A person commits one thing, namely false speech. They do
not fear the next world and do no evil. It
is better to eat iron balls, hot like fire, than
to accept the offerings of the world with broken precepts
If one fears suffering and does not cherish it, one
should not do evil in hidden or obvious places. If
one has done or is doing unwholesome karma, one will
not escape it and there is no place to avoid it.

「人犯一法，　　谓妄言是，不畏后世，　　无恶不作。宁噉铁丸，　　其热如火，不以犯戒，　　受世信施。若畏于苦，　　不爱念者，于隐显处，　　莫作恶业。若不善业，　　已作今作，终不得脱，　　亦无避处。」

After the Buddha spoke the verse, he asked Rahula again, "What do you think? What is a mirror used for?"

佛说颂已，复问罗云：「于意云何？人用镜为？」

Venerable Rahula replied, "World-Honored One! It is used to see one's face, to see if it is clean or not."

尊者罗云答曰：「世尊！欲观其面，见净不净。」

Rahula, if you are about to engage in bodily action, then contemplate that bodily action: 'The bodily action I am about to engage in, is it pure or impure, for my own benefit or for the benefit of others?' Rahula, if you contemplate, you will know that the bodily action you are about to engage in is pure, either for your own benefit or for the benefit of others, and it is unwholesome with a result of suffering, leading to a painful retribution. Rahula, you should abandon that bodily action you are about to engage in. Rahula, if you contemplate, you will know that the bodily action you are about to engage in is impure, either for your own benefit or for the benefit of others, and it is wholesome with a result of happiness, leading to a happy retribution. Rahula, you should accept that bodily action you are about to engage in.

「如是。罗云！若汝将作身业，即观彼身业：『我将作身业，彼身业为净、为不净，为自为、为他？』罗云！若观时则知，我将作身业，彼身业净，或自为，或为他，不善与苦果受于苦报。罗云！汝当舍彼将作身业。罗云！若观时则知，我将作身业，彼身业不净，或自为，或为他，善与乐果受于乐报。罗云！汝当受彼将作身业。

Rahula, if you are currently engaging in bodily action, then contemplate that bodily action: 'The bodily action I am currently engaging in, is it pure or impure, for my own benefit or for the benefit of others?' Rahula, if you contemplate, you will know that the bodily action you are currently engaging in is pure, either for your own benefit or for the benefit of others, and it is unwholesome with a result of suffering, leading to a painful retribution. Rahula, you should abandon that bodily action you are currently engaging in. Rahula, if you contemplate, you will know that the bodily action you are currently engaging in is impure, either for your own benefit or for the benefit of others, and it is wholesome with a result of happiness, leading to a happy retribution. Rahula, you should accept that bodily action you are currently engaging in.

「罗云！若汝现作身业，即观此身业：『若我现作身业，此身业为净、为不净，为自为、为他？』罗云！若观时则知，我现作身业，此身业净，或自为，或为他，不善与苦果受于苦报。罗云！汝当舍此现作身业。罗云！若观时则知，我现作身业，此身业不净，或自为，或为他，善与乐果受于乐报。罗云！汝当受此现作身业。

Rahula, if you have already engaged in bodily action, then contemplate that bodily action: 'The bodily action I have already engaged in, has it already passed, ceased, and changed? Is it pure or impure, for my own benefit or for the benefit of others?' Rahula, if you contemplate, you will know that the bodily action you have already engaged in has already passed, ceased, and changed, and that bodily action is pure, either for your own benefit or for the benefit of others, and it is unwholesome with a result of suffering, leading to a painful retribution. Rahula, you should go to a good friend, a person of pure conduct, and with a sincere mind confess and repent of that bodily action, be cautious and do not conceal it, and uphold it well. Rahula, if you contemplate, you will know that the bodily action you have already engaged in has already passed, ceased, and changed, and that bodily action is impure, either for your own benefit or for the benefit of others, and it is wholesome with a result of happiness, leading to a happy retribution. Rahula, you should joyfully abide in right mindfulness and right knowledge day and night, and the same is true for verbal action.

「罗云！若汝已作身业，即观彼身业：『若我已作身业，彼身业已过去、灭尽、变易，为净、为不净，为自、或为他？』罗云！若观时则知，我已作身业，彼身业已过去、灭尽、变易，彼身业净，或自为，或为他，不善与苦果受于苦报。罗云！汝当诣善知识、梵行人所，彼已作身业，至心发露，应悔过说，慎莫覆藏，更善持护。罗云！若观时则知，我已作身业，彼身业已过去灭尽变易，彼身业不净，或自为，或为他，善与乐果受于乐报。罗云！汝当昼夜欢喜，住正念正智，口业亦复如是。

Rahula, because of past actions, you have already engaged in mental action. Then contemplate that mental action: 'Because of past actions, the mental action I have already engaged in, is it pure or impure, for my own benefit or for the benefit of others?' Rahula, if you contemplate, you will know that the mental action you have already engaged in because of past actions has already passed, ceased, and changed, and that mental action is pure, either for your own benefit or for the benefit of others, and it is unwholesome with a result of suffering, leading to a painful retribution. Rahula, you should abandon that mental action you have already engaged in because of past actions. Rahula, if you contemplate, you will know that the mental action you have already engaged in because of past actions has already passed, ceased, and changed, and that mental action is impure, either for your own benefit or for the benefit of others, and it is wholesome with a result of happiness, leading to a happy retribution. Rahula, you should accept that mental action you have already engaged in because of past actions.

「罗云！因过去行故，已生意业，即观彼意业：『若因过去行故，已生意业，彼意业为净、为不净，为自为、为他？』罗云！若观时则知，因过去行故，已生意业，彼意业已过去、灭尽、变易，彼意业净，或自为，或为他，不善与苦果受于苦报。罗云！汝当舍彼过去意业，罗云！若观时则知，因过去行故，已生意业已过去、灭尽、变易，彼意业不净，或自为，或为他，善与乐果受于乐报。罗云！汝当受彼过去意业。

Rahula, because of future actions, you will engage in mental action. Then contemplate that mental action: 'Because of future actions, the mental action I will engage in, is it pure or impure, for my own benefit or for the benefit of others?' Rahula, if you contemplate, you will know that the mental action you will engage in because of future actions will be pure, either for your own benefit or for the benefit of others, and it is unwholesome with a result of suffering, leading to a painful retribution. Rahula, you should abandon that mental action you will engage in because of future actions. Rahula, if you contemplate, you will know that the mental action you will engage in because of future actions will be impure, either for your own benefit or for the benefit of others, and it is wholesome with a result of happiness, leading to a happy retribution. Rahula, you should accept that mental action you will engage in because of future actions.

「罗云！因未来行故，当生意业，即观彼意业：『若因未来行故，当生意业，彼意业为净、为不净，为自为、为他？』罗云！若观时则知，因未来行故，当生意业，彼意业净，或自为，或为他，不善与苦果受于苦报。罗云！汝当舍彼未来意业。罗云！若观时则知，因未来行故，当生意业，彼意业不净，或自为，或为他，善与乐果受于乐报。罗云！汝当受彼未来意业。

Rahula, because of present actions, you are engaging in mental action. Then contemplate that mental action: 'Because of present actions, the mental action I am engaging in, is it pure or impure, for my own benefit or for the benefit of others?' Rahula, if you contemplate, you will know that the mental action you are engaging in because of present actions will be pure, either for your own benefit or for the benefit of others, and it is unwholesome with a result of suffering, leading to a painful retribution. Rahula, you should abandon that mental action you are engaging in because of present actions. Rahula, if you contemplate, you will know that the mental action you are engaging in because of present actions will be impure, either for your own benefit or for the benefit of others, and it is wholesome with a result of happiness, leading to a happy retribution. Rahula, you should accept that mental action you are engaging in because of present actions.

「罗云！因现在行故，现生意业，即观此意业：『若因现在行故，现生意业，此意业为净、为不净，为自为、为他？』罗云！若观时则知，因现在行故，现生意业，此意业净，或自为，或为他，不善与苦果受于苦报。罗云！汝当舍此现在意业。罗云！若观时则知，因现在行故，现生意业，此意业不净，或自为，或为他，善与乐果受于乐报。罗云！汝当受此现在意业。

Rahula, if there were past śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who contemplated and purified their bodily, verbal, and mental actions, all of them contemplated and purified their bodily, verbal, and mental actions in this way. Rahula, if there will be future śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who will contemplate and purify their bodily, verbal, and mental actions, all of them will contemplate and purify their bodily, verbal, and mental actions in this way. Rahula, if there are present śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who are contemplating and purifying their bodily, verbal, and mental actions, all of them are contemplating and purifying their bodily, verbal, and mental actions in this way. Rahula, you should train thus: I am also contemplating and purifying my bodily, verbal, and mental actions in this way.

「罗云！若有过去沙门、梵志，身、口、意业，已观而观，已净而净，彼一切即此身、口、意业，已观而观，已净而净。罗云！若有未来沙门、梵志，身、口、意业，当观而观，当净而净，彼一切即此身、口、意业，当观而观，当净而净，罗云！若有现在沙门、梵志，身、口、意业，现观而观，现净而净，彼一切即此身、口、意业，现观而观，现净而净。罗云！汝当如是学，我亦即此身、口、意业，现观而观，现净而净。」

Then the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

于是，世尊复说颂曰：

Rahula, you should always contemplate the good and
bad dharmas of bodily, verbal, and mental actions
Having known, Rahula, do not speak falsely. How
can one who lives off others speak falsely?
Covering the Dharma of the śramaṇas, empty and
without reality, means speaking falsely and not guarding
one's mouth. Therefore, not speaking falsely is the
Dharma of the sons of the Perfectly Enlightened
One. Rahula, you should learn this. In the
prosperous and peaceful places, free from fear, Rahula
should go there, not harming others.

「身业、口业、　　意业，罗云！善不善法，　　汝应常观。知已妄言，　　罗云莫说，秃从他活，　　何可妄言？覆沙门法，　　空无真实，谓说妄言，　　不护其口。故不妄言，　　正觉之子，是沙门法，　　罗云当学。方方丰乐，　　安隐无怖，罗云至彼，　　莫为害他。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Venerable Rahula and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说如是。尊者罗云及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
