MĀ 142

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 12 梵志品

142. （一四二）雨势经

Parallel to: AN 7.22 / EĀ 40.2

Uṣa, Karṇa, Saṃkhyā, Gautama, Hastin
Śrutadharma, Duḥkha, Kāma, Uttara, Aśvajit,

雨势、歌罗、数　　瞿默、象迹喻闻德、何苦、欲　　欝瘦、阿摄惒

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time the Buddha was dwelling in Rājagṛha, in the Vulture Peak Mountain.

一时，佛游王舍城，在鹫岩山中。

At that time, King Ajātaśatru of Magadha, the son of Vaidehī, was hostile towards the Licchavis. He often said among his retinue, "The people of the Licchavi country have great supernatural powers, great virtue, great merit, and great spiritual power. I shall destroy the Licchavi people, destroy the Licchavis, and cause the Licchavis to suffer immeasurable calamities."

尔时，摩竭陀王未生怨鞞陀提子，与跋耆相憎，常在眷属数作是说：「跋耆国人有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，我当断灭跋耆人种，破坏跋耆，令跋耆人遭无量厄。」

Then King Ajātaśatru of Magadha, the son of Vaidehī, heard that the World-Honored One was dwelling in Rājagṛha, in the Vulture Peak Mountain. He then told his chief minister Varṣākāra, "I have heard that the Śramaṇa Gautama is dwelling in Rājagṛha, in the Vulture Peak Mountain. Varṣākāra, you go to where the Śramaṇa Gautama is. You should say on my behalf, 'Gautama, King Ajātaśatru of Magadha, the son of Vaidehī, asks about your well-being, whether you are free from illness and your strength is as usual?' You should also say, 'Gautama, King Ajātaśatru of Magadha, the son of Vaidehī, is hostile towards the Licchavis. He often says among his retinue, "The people of the Licchavi country have great supernatural powers, great virtue, great merit, and great spiritual power. I shall destroy the Licchavi people, destroy the Licchavis, and cause the Licchavis to suffer immeasurable calamities." What would the Śramaṇa Gautama say about this?' Varṣākāra, if the Śramaṇa Gautama says anything, you should remember it well. Why is that? Because such a person would never speak falsely.

于是，摩竭陀王未生怨鞞陀提子闻世尊游王舍城，在鹫岩山中，便告大臣雨势曰：「我闻沙门瞿昙游王舍城，在鹫岩山中。雨势！汝往至沙门瞿昙所，汝持我名问讯圣体安快无病，气力如常耶？当作是语：『瞿昙！摩竭陀王未生怨鞞陀提子问讯圣体安快无病，气力如常耶？瞿昙！摩竭陀王未生怨鞞陀提子与跋耆相憎，常在眷属数作是说：「跋耆国人有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，我当断灭跋耆人种，破坏跋耆，令跋耆人遭无量厄。」沙门瞿昙当何所说？』雨势！若沙门瞿昙有所说者，汝善受持。所以者何？如是之人，终不妄说。」

The chief minister Varṣākāra, having received the king's instructions, took the best carriage and left Rājagṛha with five hundred carriages. He then went to the Vulture Peak Mountain. He climbed the Vulture Peak Mountain, got off the carriage, and walked on foot to where the Buddha was. He then exchanged greetings with the World-Honored One and sat to one side. He said, "Gautama, King Ajātaśatru of Magadha, the son of Vaidehī, asks about your well-being, whether you are free from illness and your strength is as usual? Gautama, King Ajātaśatru of Magadha, the son of Vaidehī, is hostile towards the Licchavis. He often says among his retinue, "The people of the Licchavi country have great supernatural powers, great virtue, great merit, and great spiritual power. I shall destroy the Licchavi people, destroy the Licchavis, and cause the Licchavis to suffer immeasurable calamities." What would the Śramaṇa Gautama say about this?"

大臣雨势受王教已，乘最好乘，与五百乘俱出王舍城，即便往诣鹫岩山中。登鹫岩山，下车步进，往诣佛所，便与世尊共相问讯，却坐一面，白曰：「瞿昙！摩竭陀王未生怨鞞陀提子问讯圣体安快无病，气力如常耶？瞿昙！摩竭陀王未生怨鞞陀提子与跋耆相憎，常在眷属数作是说：『跋耆国人有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，我当断灭跋耆人种，破坏跋耆，令跋耆人遭无量厄。』沙门瞿昙当何所说？」

The World-Honored One, having heard this, said, "Varṣākāra, I once traveled to the Licchavi country. There was a temple there called Chagela. Varṣākāra, at that time I spoke to the people of the Licchavi country about the seven unwaning dharmas. The people of the Licchavi country then practiced the seven unwaning dharmas. Varṣākāra, if the people of the Licchavi country practice the seven unwaning dharmas without violating them, the Licchavis will certainly be victorious and will not decline."

世尊闻已，告曰：「雨势！我昔曾游于跋耆国，彼国有寺名遮惒逻。雨势！尔时我为跋耆国人说七不衰法，跋耆国人则能受行七不衰法。雨势！若跋耆国人行七不衰法而不犯者，跋耆必胜，则为不衰。」

The chief minister Varṣākāra said to the World-Honored One, "The Śramaṇa Gautama briefly spoke about this matter but did not explain it in detail. We cannot understand its meaning. May the Śramaṇa Gautama explain it in detail so that we may understand its meaning."

大臣雨势白世尊曰：「沙门瞿昙略说此事，不广分别，我等不能得解此义，愿沙门瞿昙广分别说，当令我等得知此义。」

The World-Honored One said, "Varṣākāra, listen carefully and keep it in mind. I will explain this meaning to you in detail." The chief minister Varṣākāra accepted the teaching and listened.

世尊告曰：「雨势！谛听，善思念之，我当为汝广说此义。」大臣雨势受教而听。

At that time, Venerable Ānanda was holding a whisk and attending to the Buddha. The World-Honored One turned to him and asked, "Ānanda, have you heard that the Vajjians often gather together in large numbers?"

是时，尊者阿难执拂侍佛，世尊回顾问曰：「阿难！颇闻跋耆数数集会，多聚集耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "World-Honored One! I have heard that the Vajjians often gather together in large numbers."

尊者阿难白曰：「世尊！我闻跋耆数数集会，多聚集也。」

The World-Honored One then said to the minister Varṣākāra, "If the Vajjians often gather together in large numbers, the Vajjians will surely prevail and not decline."

世尊即告大臣雨势：「若彼跋耆数数集会，多聚集者，跋耆必胜，则为不衰。」

The World-Honored One further asked Venerable Ānanda, "Have you heard that the Vajjians gather together, perform Vajjian affairs, and rise together?"

世尊复问尊者阿难：「颇闻跋耆共俱集会，俱作跋耆事，共俱起耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "World-Honored One! I have heard that the Vajjians gather together, perform Vajjian affairs, and rise together."

尊者阿难白曰：「世尊！我闻跋耆共俱集会，俱作跋耆事，共俱起也。」

The World-Honored One further told Minister Vāsava, "If the Vajjians gather together, perform Vajjian affairs, and rise together, the Vajjians will surely prevail and not decline."

世尊复告大臣雨势：「若彼跋耆共俱集会，俱作跋耆事，共俱起者，跋耆必胜，则为不衰。」

The World-Honored One further asked Venerable Ānanda, "Have you heard that the Vajjians do not establish what has not been established, and do not change what has been established, but uphold the old Vajjian practices well?"

世尊复问尊者阿难：「颇闻跋耆未施设者不更施设，本所施设而不改易，旧跋耆法善奉行耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "World-Honored One! I have heard that the Vajjians do not establish what has not been established, and do not change what has been established, but uphold the old Vajjian practices well."

尊者阿难白曰：「世尊！我闻跋耆未施设者不更施设，本所施设而不改易，旧跋耆法善奉行也。」

The World-Honored One further told Minister Vāsava, "If the Vajjians do not establish what has not been established, and do not change what has been established, but uphold the old Vajjian practices well, then the Vajjians will surely prevail and not decline."

世尊复告大臣雨势：「若彼跋耆未施设者不更施设，本所施设而不改易，旧跋耆法善奉行者，跋耆必胜，则为不衰。」

The World-Honored One further asked Venerable Ānanda, "Have you heard that the Vajjians do not use force to violate the wives or daughters of others?"

世尊复问尊者阿难：「颇闻跋耆不以力势而犯他妇、他童女耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "World-Honored One! I have heard that the Vajjians do not use force to violate the wives or daughters of others."

尊者阿难白曰：「世尊！我闻跋耆不以力势而犯他妇、他童女也。」

The World-Honored One further told Minister Vāsava, "If the Vajjians do not use force to violate the wives or daughters of others, the Vajjians will surely prevail and not decline."

世尊复告大臣雨势：「若彼跋耆不以力势而犯他妇、他童女者跋耆必胜，则为不衰。」

The World-Honored One further asked Venerable Ānanda, "Have you heard that the Vajjians all respect, honor, and make offerings to those of great virtue and respect, and that they listen to their teachings?"

世尊复问尊者阿难：「颇闻跋耆有名德尊重者，跋耆悉共宗敬、恭奉、供养，于彼闻教则受耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "World-Honored One! I have heard that the Vajjians all respect, honor, and make offerings to those of great virtue and respect, and that they listen to their teachings."

尊者阿难白曰：「世尊！我闻跋耆有名德尊重者，跋耆悉共宗敬、恭奉、供养，于彼闻教则受。」

The World-Honored One further told Minister Vāsava, "If the Vajjians all respect, honor, and make offerings to those of great virtue and respect, and that they listen to their teachings, then the Vajjians will surely prosper and not decline."

世尊复告大臣雨势：「若彼跋耆有名德尊重者，跋耆悉共宗敬、恭奉、供养，于彼闻教则受者，跋耆必胜，则为不衰。」

The World-Honored One further asked Venerable Ānanda, "Have you heard that the Vajjians all repair and maintain their old temples, honor and make offerings to them, and continue to make offerings as they did before, without reducing their efforts?"

世尊复问尊者阿难：「颇闻跋耆所有旧寺，跋耆悉共修饰，遵奉、供养、礼事，本之所施常作不废，本之所为不减损耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "World-Honored One! I have heard that the Vajjians all repair and maintain their old temples, honor and make offerings to them, and continue to make offerings as they did before, without reducing their efforts."

尊者阿难白曰：「世尊！我闻跋耆所有旧寺，跋耆悉共修饰，遵奉、供养、礼事，本之所施常作不废，本之所为不减损也。」

The World-Honored One further told Minister Vāsava, "If the Vajjians all repair and maintain their old temples, honor and make offerings to them, and continue to make offerings as they did before, without reducing their efforts, then the Vajjians will surely prevail and not decline."

世尊复告大臣雨势：「若彼跋耆所有旧寺，跋耆悉共修饰，遵奉、供养、礼事，本之所施常作不废，本之所为不减损者，跋耆必胜，则为不衰。」

The World-Honored One further asked Venerable Ānanda, "Have you heard that the Vajjians all protect the arhats with great love and respect, and always wish for future arhats to come, and for those who have come to stay for a long time, always providing them with robes, food and drink, bedding, medicine, and other necessities of life?"

世尊复问尊者阿难：「颇闻跋耆悉共拥护诸阿罗诃，极大爱敬，常愿未来阿罗诃者而欲令来，既已来者乐恒久住，常使不乏衣被、饮食、床榻、汤药、诸生活具耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "World-Honored One! I have heard that the Vajjians all protect the arhats with great love and respect, and always wish for future arhats to come, and for those who have come to stay for a long time, always providing them with robes, food and drink, bedding, medicine, and other necessities of life."

尊者阿难白曰：「世尊！我闻跋耆悉共拥护诸阿罗诃，极大爱敬，常愿未来阿罗诃者而欲令来，既已来者乐恒久住，常使不乏衣被、饮食、床榻、汤药、诸生活具。」

The World-Honored One further told Minister Vāsava, "If the Vajjians all protect the arhats with great love and respect, and always wish for future arhats to come, and for those who have come to stay for a long time, always providing them with robes, food and drink, bedding, medicine, and other necessities of life, the Vajjians will surely prevail and not decline. Vāsava! The Vajjians practice these seven undiminishing principles. Those who uphold these seven undiminishing principles, the Vajjians will surely prevail and not decline."

世尊复告大臣雨势：「若彼跋耆悉共拥护诸阿罗诃，极大爱敬，常愿未来阿罗诃者而欲令来，既已来者乐恒久住，常使不乏衣被、饮食、床榻、汤药、诸生活具者，跋耆必胜，则为不衰。雨势！跋耆行此七不衰法，诸受持此七不衰法者，跋耆必胜，则为不衰。」

Then Minister Vāsava rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "Gautama! If the Vajjians accomplish even one undiminishing principle, King Ajātaśatru of Magadha will not be able to subdue them, let alone the seven undiminishing principles. Gautama! I have many affairs in my country. I will now take leave and return."

于是，大臣雨势即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「瞿昙！设彼跋耆成就一不衰法者，摩竭陀王未生怨鞞陀提子不能伏彼，况复具七不衰法耶？瞿昙！我国事多，请退还归。」

The World-Honored One replied, "You may leave as you wish."

世尊报曰：「欲去随意。」

Then Minister Vāsava, having heard the Buddha's teachings, accepted them well and upheld them. He rose, circumambulated the World-Honored One three times, and departed.

于是，大臣雨势闻佛所说，则善受持，起绕世尊三匝而去。

Not long after Minister Vāsava left, the World-Honored One turned to Ānanda and said, "If there are any monks dwelling in various places on Vulture Peak Mountain, order them all to gather in the Dharma hall. When they have all gathered, come and inform me."

大臣雨势去后不久。于是，世尊回顾告曰：「阿难！若有比丘依鹫岩山处处住者，宣令一切尽集讲堂，一切集已，便来白我。」

Venerable Ānanda accepted the Buddha's instruction, saying, "Yes, World-Honored One!" Then Venerable Ānanda went to proclaim, "If there are any monks dwelling in various places on Vulture Peak Mountain, now order them all to gather in the Dharma hall." When they had all gathered, he returned to the Buddha, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said, "World-Honored One! I have already proclaimed, 'If there are any monks dwelling in various places on Vulture Peak Mountain, now order them all to gather in the Dharma hall.' They have all gathered now. May the World-Honored One know the time himself."

尊者阿难即受佛教：「唯然。世尊！」是时，尊者阿难便行宣令：「若有比丘依鹫岩山处处住者，今令一切尽集讲堂。」一切集已，还诣佛所，稽首作礼，却住一面，白曰：「世尊！我已宣令：『若有比丘依鹫岩山处处住者，悉令一切尽集讲堂。』今皆已集，唯愿世尊自知其时。」

Then the World-Honored One, together with Venerable Ānanda, went to the Dharma hall, sat down in front of the assembly of monks, and said to the monks, "Now I will explain to you the seven unwaning dharmas. Listen carefully and keep them in mind."

于是，世尊将尊者阿难往诣讲堂，于比丘众前敷座而坐，告诸比丘：「今为汝说七不衰法，汝等谛听，善思念之。」

At that time, the monks said, "Yes."

时，诸比丘白曰：「唯然。」

The Buddha said, "What are the seven? If the monks gather frequently and assemble in large numbers, the monks will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline. If the monks gather together, perform various tasks, and rise together, the monks will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline. If the monks do not establish new rules for things that have not been established, and do not change the rules that have been established, and if they practice the precepts I have taught well, the monks will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline. If the monks do not follow the arising of future desires, joy, and attachment, and do not delight in them, the monks will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline. If the monks respect and honor the elders who practice the holy life together, and receive their teachings, the monks will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline. If the monks dwell in secluded places in the mountains, forests, and high cliffs, in quiet places far from noise, away from evil and people, and enjoy sitting in meditation without leaving, the monks will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline. If the monks protect and cherish all those who practice the holy life, always wishing for future practitioners to come, and for those who have come to stay for a long time, and provide them with robes, food and drink, bedding, medicine, and other necessities for living, the monks will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline. If the monks practice and uphold these seven unwaning dharmas without violating them, the monks will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline."

佛言：「云何为七？若比丘数数集会，多聚集者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。若比丘共齐集会，俱作众事，共俱起者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。若比丘未施设事不更施设，本所施设而不改易，我所说戒善奉行者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。若比丘此『未来有爱，喜、欲共俱，爱乐彼彼』有起不随者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。若比丘有长老上尊俱学梵行，比丘悉共宗敬、恭奉、供养，于彼闻教则受者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。若比丘有无事处山林高岩，闲居静处，寂无音声，远离，无恶，无有人民，随顺宴坐，乐住不离者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。若比丘悉共拥护诸梵行者，至重爱敬，常愿未来诸梵行者而欲令来，既已来者乐恒久住，常使不乏衣被、饮食、床榻、汤药、诸生活具者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。若比丘行此七不衰法，受持不犯者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。」

Then the World-Honored One again said to the monks, "I will further explain the seven unwaning dharmas to you. Listen carefully and keep them in mind."

于是，世尊复告诸比丘曰：「我为汝等更说七不衰法，汝等谛听，善思念之。」

The monks replied, "Yes, World-Honored One."

时，诸比丘白曰：「唯然。」

The Buddha said, "What are the seven? If the monks respect, deeply revere, and serve their teachers, the monks will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline. If the monks respect, deeply revere, and serve the Dharma, the Sangha, the precepts, non-distraction, support, and concentration, the monks will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline. If the monks practice these seven unwaning dharmas and uphold them without transgression, the monks will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline."

佛言：「云何为七？若比丘尊师，恭敬、极重供养、奉事者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。若比丘法、众、戒、不放逸、供给、定，恭敬、极重供养、奉事者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。若比丘行此七不衰法，受持不犯者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。」

The World-Honored One again said to the monks, "I will further explain the seven unwaning dharmas for you. Listen carefully and keep them in mind."

世尊复告诸比丘曰：「我为汝等更说七不衰法，汝等谛听，善思念之。」

The monks replied, "Yes, World-Honored One."

时，诸比丘白曰：「唯然。」

The Buddha said, "What are the seven? If a monk does not engage in activities, does not enjoy activities, and does not practice activities, the monk will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline. Not engaging in frivolous talk, not enjoying frivolous talk, and not practicing frivolous talk; not engaging in gatherings, not enjoying gatherings, and not practicing gatherings; not engaging in mixed associations, not enjoying mixed associations, and not practicing mixed associations; not engaging in sleep, not enjoying sleep, and not practicing sleep; not for the sake of gain, not for the sake of fame, and not for the sake of others practicing the holy life; not for a short while, not for the sake of surpassing others, and in the meantime abandoning skillful means to surpass others; the monk will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline. If a monk practices these seven unwaning dharmas and upholds them without transgression, the monk will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline."

佛言：「云何为七？若比丘不行于业，不乐于业，不习业者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。不行哗说，不乐哗说，不习哗说者；不行聚会，不乐聚会，不习聚会者；不行杂合，不乐杂合，不习杂合者；不行睡眠，不乐睡眠，不习睡眠者；不为利，不为誉，不为他人行梵行者；不为暂尔，不为德胜，于其中间舍方便，令德胜者；比丘必胜，则法不衰。若比丘行此七不衰法，受持不犯者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。」

The World-Honored One again said to the monks, "I will further explain the seven unwaning dharmas for you. Listen carefully and keep them in mind."

世尊复告诸比丘曰：「我为汝等更说七不衰法，汝等谛听，善思念之。」

The monks replied, "Yes, World-Honored One."

时，诸比丘白曰：「唯然。」

The Buddha said, "What are the seven? If a monk possesses the wealth of faith, the wealth of precepts, the wealth of shame, the wealth of modesty, the wealth of extensive learning, the wealth of generosity, and the wealth of wisdom, then the monk will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline. If a monk practices these seven unwaning dharmas and upholds them without transgression, then the monk will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline."

佛言：「云何为七？若比丘成就信财、戒财、惭财、愧财、博闻财、施财，成就慧财者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。若比丘行此七不衰法，受持不犯者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。」

The World-Honored One again said to the monks, "I will further explain the seven unwaning dharmas for you. Listen carefully and contemplate them well."

世尊复告诸比丘曰：「我为汝等更说七不衰法，汝等谛听，善思念之。」

The monks replied, "Yes, indeed."

时，诸比丘白曰：「唯然。」

The Buddha said, "What are the seven? If a monk accomplishes the power of faith, the power of diligence, the power of shame, the power of remorse, the power of mindfulness, the power of concentration, and the power of wisdom, then the monk will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline. If a monk practices these seven unwaning dharmas and upholds them without transgression, then the monk will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline."

佛言：「云何为七？若比丘成就信力、精进力、惭力、愧力、念力、定力，成就慧力者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。若比丘行此七不衰法，受持不犯者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。」

The World-Honored One again said to the monks, "I will further explain the seven unwaning dharmas for you. Listen carefully and keep them in mind."

世尊复告诸比丘曰：「我为汝等更说七不衰法，汝等谛听，善思念之。」

The monks replied, "Yes, indeed."

时，诸比丘白曰：「唯然。」

The Buddha said, "What are the seven? If a monk cultivates the mindfulness factor of awakening based on renunciation, based on dispassion, based on cessation, leading to letting go, the investigation of phenomena, the energy, joy, tranquility, and concentration factors of awakening, based on renunciation, based on dispassion, based on cessation, leading to letting go, then the monk will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline. If a monk practices these seven unwaning dharmas and upholds them without transgression, then the monk will surely prevail and the Dharma will not decline."

佛言：「云何为七？若比丘修念觉支，依舍离、依无欲、依灭尽，趣向出要，择法、精进、喜、息、定；修舍觉支，依舍离、依无欲、依灭尽，趣向出要者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。若比丘行此七不衰法，受持不犯者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。」

The World-Honored One again said to the monks, "I will further explain the seven unwaning dharmas for you. Listen carefully and keep them in mind."

世尊复告诸比丘曰：「我为汝等更说七不衰法，汝等谛听，善思念之。」

The monks replied, "Yes, indeed."

时，诸比丘白曰：「唯然。」

The Buddha said, "What are the seven? If a monk should be given a face-to-face resolution, he should be given a face-to-face resolution; if he should be given a resolution by recollection, he should be given a resolution by recollection; if he should be given a resolution of non-delusion, he should be given a resolution of non-delusion; if he should be given self-disclosure, he should be given self-disclosure; if he should be given a resolution by the majority, he should be given a resolution by the majority; if he should be given a resolution by covering over as if with grass, he should be given a resolution by covering over as if with grass; if a dispute arises in the assembly, it should be resolved by the method of discarding as if it were filth. If the monk is victorious, the Dharma will not decline. If a monk practices these seven unwaning dharmas and upholds them without transgression, he will be victorious, and the Dharma will not decline."

佛言：「云何为七？若比丘应与面前律与面前律，应与忆律与忆律，应与不痴律与不痴律，应与自发露与自发露，应与居与居，应与展转与展转，众中起诤，当以如弃粪扫止诤法止之者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。若比丘行此七不衰法，受持不犯者，比丘必胜，则法不衰。」

The World-Honored One again said to the monks, "Now I will explain to you the six methods of consolation. Listen carefully and keep them in mind."

世尊复告诸比丘曰：「今为汝等说六慰劳法，汝等谛听，善思念之。」

The monks replied, "Yes, World-Honored One."

时，诸比丘白曰：「唯然。」

The Buddha said, "What are the six? To use compassionate physical actions towards those who practice the Brahma way is a method of consolation. It is to love the Dharma, enjoy the Dharma, to make them love, to make them respect, to make them follow, to make them revere, to make them practice, to make them embrace, to attain the state of a monk, to attain single-mindedness, to attain diligence, to attain nirvana. In the same way, compassionate verbal actions and compassionate mental actions. If there is any Dharma benefit, to obtain benefits according to the Dharma, to share one's own food, even down to what is in one's bowl, to give such benefits to those who practice the Brahma way is a method of consolation. It is to love the Dharma, enjoy the Dharma, to make them love, to make them respect, to make them follow, to make them revere, to make them practice, to make them embrace, to attain the state of a monk, to attain single-mindedness, to attain diligence, to attain nirvana. If one has precepts that are not lacking, not pierced, not defiled, not black, not following others, praised by the sages, fully upholding them, to give such precepts to those who practice the Brahma way is a method of consolation. It is to love the Dharma, enjoy the Dharma, to make them love, to make them respect, to make them follow, to make them revere, to make them practice, to make them embrace, to attain the state of a monk, to attain single-mindedness, to attain diligence, to attain nirvana. If one has a view that is noble, that leads to transcendence, that is clear and profound, that is able to truly end suffering, to give such a view to those who practice the Brahma way is a method of consolation. It is to love the Dharma, enjoy the Dharma, to make them love, to make them respect, to make them follow, to make them revere, to make them practice, to make them embrace, to attain the state of a monk, to attain single-mindedness, to attain diligence, to attain nirvana. The six methods of consolation that I mentioned earlier are based on this.

佛言：「云何为六？以慈身业向诸梵行，是慰劳法，爱法乐法，令爱令重，令奉令敬，令修令摄，得沙门，得一心，得精进，得涅槃。如是慈口业、慈意业。若有法利，如法得利，自所饭食，至在钵中，如是利分，布施诸梵行，是慰劳法。爱法乐法，令爱令重，令奉令敬，令修令摄，得沙门，得一心，得精进，得涅槃。若有戒不缺、不穿、无秽、无黑，如地不随他，圣所称誉，具善受持，如是戒分，布施诸梵行，是慰劳法。爱法乐法，令爱令重，令奉令敬，令修令摄，得沙门，得一心，得精进，得涅槃。若有见是圣出要，明了深达，能正尽苦，如是见分，布施诸梵行，是慰劳法。爱法乐法，令爱令重，令奉令敬，令修令摄，得沙门，得一心，得精进，得涅槃。我向所言六慰劳法者，因此故说。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The monks, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
