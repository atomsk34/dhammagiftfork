MĀ 143

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 12 梵志品

143. （一四三）伤歌逻经

Parallel to: AN 3.60

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the ascetic Saṅgārava was wandering about in the middle of the day and went to where the Buddha was. They exchanged greetings and then sat to one side. He said, "Gautama! I wish to ask something. Please listen before I speak."

尔时，伤歌逻摩纳中后彷徉，往诣佛所，共相问讯，却坐一面，白曰：「瞿昙！我欲有所问，听乃敢陈。」

The World-Honored One said, "Ascetic! If you have any doubts, ask as you please."

世尊告曰：「摩纳！若有疑者，恣汝所问。」

The ascetic Saṅgārava then asked, "Gautama! Brahmins practice begging for wealth according to the Dharma, or they hold their own or others' observances. Gautama! If one holds one's own or others' observances, they all practice immeasurable meritorious deeds. Because of the observances, the disciples of the śramaṇa Gautama shave off their hair and beards, put on the saffron robes, leave home out of true faith, become homeless, study the path, tame themselves, rest themselves, and extinguish themselves. In this way, the disciples of the śramaṇa Gautama practice one meritorious deed according to their family, not immeasurable meritorious deeds, because of studying the path."

伤歌逻摩纳即便问曰：「瞿昙！梵志如法行乞财物，或自作斋，或教作斋。瞿昙！若自作斋，教作斋者，彼一切行无量福迹。以因斋故，沙门瞿昙弟子随族剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，自调御，自息止，自灭讫。如是沙门瞿昙弟子随族行一福迹，不行无量福迹，因学道故。」

At that time, Venerable Ānanda was holding a whisk and attending to the Buddha. Then Venerable Ānanda asked, "Ascetic! Which of these two paths is the most supreme, the most wonderful, and the most excellent?"

尔时，尊者阿难执拂侍佛。于是，尊者阿难问曰：「摩纳！此二道迹，何者最上、最妙、最胜耶？」

The ascetic Saṅgārava said, "Ānanda! I respect, honor, and venerate both the śramaṇa Gautama and Ānanda."

伤歌逻摩纳语曰：「阿难！沙门瞿昙及阿难，我俱恭敬、尊重、奉祠。」

Venerable Ānanda said again, "Ascetic! I do not ask you who you respect, honor, and venerate; I only ask you which of these two paths is the most supreme, the most wonderful, and the most excellent?"

尊者阿难复语曰：「摩纳！我不问汝恭敬、尊重、奉祠谁；我但问汝此二道迹，何者最上、最妙、最胜耶？」

Venerable Ānanda asked a second and a third time, "Ascetic! Which of these two paths is the most supreme, the most wonderful, and the most excellent?"

尊者阿难至再三问曰：「摩纳！此二道迹，何者最上、最妙、最胜耶？」

The ascetic Saṅgārava also spoke a second and a third time, "Ānanda! I respect, honor, and venerate both the śramaṇa Gautama and Ānanda."

伤歌逻摩纳亦再三语曰：「阿难！沙门瞿昙及阿难，我俱恭敬、尊重、奉祠。」

Venerable Ānanda again said, "Ascetic! I do not ask you who you respect, honor, and venerate; I only ask you which of these two paths is the most supreme, the most wonderful, and the most excellent?"

尊者阿难复语曰：「摩纳！我不问汝恭敬、尊重、奉祠谁；我但问汝此二道迹，何者最上、最妙、最胜耶？」

Then the World-Honored One thought, "This ascetic Saṅgārava has been defeated by Ānanda. I should rescue him."

于是，世尊便作是念：「此伤歌逻摩纳为阿难所屈，我宁可救彼。」

The World-Honored One, knowing this, said, "Ascetic! In the past, the king and his ministers gathered for a great assembly. What were they discussing? What was the purpose of their gathering?"

世尊知已，告曰：「摩纳！昔日王及群臣普集大会，共论何事？以何事故共集会耶？」

Saṅgārava replied, "Gautama! In the past, the king and his ministers gathered for a great assembly. They discussed this: 'For what reason and condition did the śramaṇa Gautama establish few precepts in the past, yet many monks attained the path? For what reason and condition does the śramaṇa Gautama establish many precepts now, yet few monks attain the path?' Gautama! In the past, the king and his ministers gathered for a great assembly. They discussed this matter and gathered for this reason."

伤歌逻摩纳答曰：「瞿昙！昔日王及群臣普集大会，共论如此事：『何因何缘，昔沙门瞿昙施设少戒，然诸比丘多得道者？何因何缘，今沙门瞿昙施设多戒，然诸比丘少得道耶？』瞿昙！昔日王及群臣普集大会，共论此事，以此事故共集会耳。」

At that time, the World-Honored One said, "Ascetic! I now ask you, answer as you see fit. What do you think? If there is a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who practices such a path and such a track, and having practiced this path and this track, has exhausted all taints, attained the outflow-free, liberated mind and liberated wisdom, knows and realizes for himself, and dwells having accomplished it, with the end of birth, the establishment of the holy life, the task accomplished, and no more future lives, knowing it as it really is. He teaches others, 'I myself practice such a path and such a track, and having practiced this path and this track, have exhausted all taints, attained the outflow-free, liberated mind and liberated wisdom, know and realize for myself, and dwell having accomplished it, with the end of birth, the establishment of the holy life, the task accomplished, and no more future lives, knowing it as it really is.' You all should come together and also practice such a path and such a track, and having practiced this path and this track, have exhausted all taints, attained the outflow-free, liberated mind and liberated wisdom, know and realize for ourselves, and dwell having accomplished it, with the end of birth, the establishment of the holy life, the task accomplished, and no more future lives, knowing it as it really is. He also practices such a path and such a track, and having practiced this path and this track, has exhausted all taints, attained the outflow-free, liberated mind and liberated wisdom, knows and realizes for himself, and dwells having accomplished it, with the end of birth, the establishment of the holy life, the task accomplished, and no more future lives, knowing it as it really is. He teaches others, and others teach others, and so on, countless hundreds of thousands. What do you think, ascetic? Do my disciples, having shaved off their hair and beards, put on the yellow robe, gone forth from the household life into homelessness, and practiced one meritorious track, not practice countless meritorious tracks, because of practicing the path?"

尔时，世尊告曰：「摩纳！我今问汝，随所解答。于意云何？若使有一沙门梵志自行如是道、如是迹，行此道、行此迹已，诸漏已尽，得无漏，心解脱、慧解脱，自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。彼为他说，我自行如是道、如是迹，行此道、行此迹已，诸漏已尽，得无漏，心解脱、慧解脱，自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。汝等共来亦自行如是道、如是迹，行此道、行此迹已，诸漏已尽，得无漏，心解脱、慧解脱，自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。彼亦自行如是道、如是迹，行此道、行此迹已，诸漏已尽，得无漏，心解脱、慧解脱，自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。彼为他说，他为他说，如是展转无量百千。于摩纳意云何？我弟子随族剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，行一福迹，不行无量福迹，因学道故耶？」

Saṅgārava replied, "Gautama! According to my understanding of what the śramaṇa Gautama has said, the śramaṇa Gautama's disciples, having shaved off their hair and beards, put on the yellow robe, gone forth from the household life into homelessness, practice countless meritorious tracks, not one meritorious track, because of practicing the path."

伤歌逻摩纳答曰：「瞿昙！如我解沙门瞿昙所说义，彼沙门瞿昙弟子随族剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道、行无量福迹，不行一福迹，因学道故。」

The World-Honored One again said to Saṅgārava, "There are three kinds of demonstrations: demonstration of supernormal powers, demonstration of clairvoyance, and demonstration of instruction. Saṅgārava, what is the demonstration of supernormal powers? There is a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who has great supernormal powers, great authority, great merit, and great spiritual power. He has attained mastery of the mind in supernormal powers and practices countless supernormal powers, such as dividing one into many, combining many into one, abiding in one while knowing and seeing, unimpeded by walls, as if walking through space. He enters the earth as if it were water, walks on water as if it were earth, sits cross-legged in the sky, soaring like a bird. The sun and moon now have great supernormal powers, great authority, great merit, and great spiritual power. They can touch them with their hands and reach up to the Brahma Heaven. Saṅgārava, this is called the demonstration of supernormal powers.

世尊复告伤歌逻曰：「有三示现：如意足示现、占念示现、教训示现。摩纳！云何如意足示现？有一沙门梵志，有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，于如意足心得自在，行无量如意足之功德，谓分一为众，合众为一，一则住一，有知有见，不碍石壁，犹如行空。没地如水，履] cf.［丽】水如地，结加趺坐，上升虚空，犹如鸟翔。今此日月有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，以手扪摸，身至梵天。摩纳！是谓如意足示现。

Saṅgārava, what is the demonstration of clairvoyance? There is a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who knows the minds of others through their characteristics. He knows their minds, their thoughts, their true minds. He knows countless minds, not a few. All of that is true and not false. He does not know the minds of others through their characteristics, but only hears the sounds of gods and non-humans and knows the minds of others. He knows their minds, their thoughts, their true minds. He knows countless minds, not a few. All of that is true and not false. He does not know the minds of others through their characteristics, nor does he know the minds of others by hearing the sounds of gods and non-humans, but only knows the minds of others through their thoughts, intentions, and speech. Having heard the sounds, he knows the minds of others. He knows their minds, their thoughts, their true minds. He knows countless minds, not a few. All of that is true and not false. He does not know the minds of others through their characteristics, nor does he know the minds of others by hearing the sounds of gods and non-humans, nor does he know the minds of others through their thoughts, intentions, and speech, but only knows the minds of others by seeing them enter the concentration of non-perception and non-contemplation. Having seen, he thinks: 'This virtuous one does not think or contemplate, as he wishes.' That virtuous one awakens from that concentration, thinking like this. That one awakens from that concentration, thinking exactly like this. He also knows the past, the future, and the present. He also knows what was done and said a long time ago. He also knows the place of tranquility and dwelling in tranquility. He also knows the mind and mental factors. Saṅgārava, this is called the demonstration of clairvoyance.

「摩纳！云何占念示现？有一沙门梵志，以他相占他意，有是意、如是意、实有是意，无量占不少占，彼一切真谛而无有虚设。不以他相占他意者，但以闻天声及非人声而占他意，有是意、如是意、实有是意，无量占不少占，彼一切真谛而无有虚设。不以他相占他意，亦不闻天声及非人声占他意者，但以他念、他思、他说，闻声已，占他意，有是意、如是意、实有是意，无量占不少占，彼一切真谛而无有虚设。不以他相占他意，亦不以闻天声及非人声占他意，亦不以他念、他思、他说，闻声已占他意者，但以见他入无觉无观定，见已，作是念：『如此贤者不念不思，如意所愿。』彼贤者从此定寤，如是念。彼从此定寤即如是如是念，彼亦占过去，亦占未来，亦占现在，久所作、久所说，亦占安静处、住安静处，亦占至心、心所有法。摩纳！是谓占念示现。

"Saṅgārava, what is the demonstration of instruction? There is a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who practices the path and traces in this way. Having practiced this path and traces, he has exhausted all taints, attained the uncontaminated, and attained liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom. He knows and realizes for himself, and dwells having attained it. Birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and there will be no more continuation of existence. He knows this as it really is. He teaches others: 'I practice the path and traces in this way. Having practiced this path and traces, I have exhausted all taints, attained the uncontaminated, and attained liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom. I know and realize for myself, and dwell having attained it. Birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and there will be no more continuation of existence. I know this as it really is. Come, all of you, also practice the path and traces in this way. Having practiced this path and traces, you will exhaust all taints, attain the uncontaminated, and attain liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom. You will know and realize for yourselves, and dwell having attained it. Birth will be exhausted, the holy life will be established, what was to be done will be done, and there will be no more continuation of existence. You will know this as it really is.' He also practices the path and traces in this way. Having practiced this path and traces, he has exhausted all taints, attained the uncontaminated liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom. He knows and realizes for himself, and dwells having attained it. Birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and there will be no more continuation of existence. He knows this as it really is. He teaches others, and others teach others. In this way, it spreads to countless hundreds and thousands. Saṅgārava, this is called the demonstration of instruction. Among these three demonstrations, which is the most supreme, most wonderful, and most excellent?"

「摩纳！云何教训示现？有一沙门梵志自行如是道、如是迹，行此道、行此迹已，诸漏已尽，得无漏，心解脱、慧解脱，自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。彼为他说：『我自行如是道、如是迹，行此道、行此迹已，诸漏已尽，得无漏，心解脱、慧解脱，自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。汝等共来，亦自行如是道、如是迹，行此道、行此迹已，诸漏已尽，得无漏，心解脱、慧解脱，自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。』彼亦自行如是道、如是迹，行此道、行此迹已，诸漏已尽，得无漏心解脱、慧解脱，自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。彼为他说，他为他说，如是展转无量百千。摩纳！是谓教训示现。此三示现，何者示现最上、最妙、最胜耶？」

Saṅgārava replied: "Gautama! If there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who have great supernatural powers, great authority, great merit, and great spiritual power, who have attained mastery over the mind in regard to supernatural powers, and can reach the Brahma Heaven with their bodies, Gautama! This is self-made, self-caused, and self-experienced. Gautama! Among the various demonstrations, this demonstration is the great Dharma. Gautama! If there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who know the minds of others through their characteristics, and know the mental factors of others, Gautama! This is also self-made, self-caused, and self-experienced. Gautama! Among the various demonstrations, this is also a demonstration of the great Dharma. Gautama! If there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who practice the path and traces in this way, and having practiced this path and traces, have exhausted all taints, attained the uncontaminated, and attained liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom. They know and realize for themselves, and dwell having attained it. Birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and there will be no more continuation of existence. They know this as it really is. They teach others, and others teach others, spreading to countless hundreds and thousands. Gautama! Among the three demonstrations, this demonstration is the most supreme, most wonderful, and most excellent."

伤歌逻摩纳答曰：「瞿昙！若有沙门、梵志有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，于如意足心得自在，乃及身至梵天者，瞿昙！此自作自有，自受其报。瞿昙！于诸示现，此示现大法。瞿昙！若有沙门、梵志以他相占他意，乃至占心、心所有法者。瞿昙！此亦自作自有，自受其报。瞿昙！于诸示现，此亦示现大法。瞿昙！若有沙门、梵志自行如是道、如是迹，行此道、行此迹已，诸漏已尽，得无漏，心解脱、慧解脱，自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。彼为他说，他为他说，如是展转无量百千者。瞿昙！于三示现，此示现最上、最妙、最胜。」

The World-Honored One further asked Saṅgārava: "Among the three demonstrations, which demonstration do you praise?"

世尊复问伤歌逻曰：「于三示现，称叹何示现？」

Saṅgārava replied: "Gautama! Among the three demonstrations, I praise the Śramaṇa Gautama. Why is that? The Śramaṇa Gautama has great supernatural powers, great authority, great merit, and great spiritual power. He has attained mastery over the mind and can reach the Brahma Heaven with his body. The Śramaṇa Gautama knows the minds of others through their characteristics, and knows the mental factors of others. The Śramaṇa Gautama demonstrates such a path and traces. Having practiced this path and traces, he has exhausted all taints, attained the uncontaminated, and attained liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom. He knows and realizes for himself, and dwells having attained it. Birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and there will be no more continuation of existence. He knows this as it really is. The Śramaṇa Gautama teaches others, and others teach others, spreading to countless hundreds and thousands. Gautama! Therefore, among the three demonstrations, I praise the Śramaṇa Gautama!"

伤歌逻摩纳答曰：「瞿昙！于三示现，我称说沙门瞿昙。所以者何？沙门瞿昙有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，心得自在，乃及身至梵天；沙门瞿昙以他相占他意，乃至占心、心所有法；沙门瞿昙示现如是道、如是迹，行此道、行此迹已，诸漏已尽，得无漏，心解脱、慧解脱，自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。沙门瞿昙为他说，他为他说，如是展转无量百千。瞿昙！是故于三示现，我称叹沙门瞿昙！」

Then the World-Honored One said: "Mānava! You are well-versed in this discourse. Why is that? I have great supernatural powers, great authority, great merit, and great spiritual power. I have attained mastery over the mind and can reach the Brahma Heaven with my body. Mānava! I know the minds of others through their characteristics, and know the mental factors of others. Mānava! I myself practice such a path and traces. Having practiced this path and traces, I have exhausted all taints, attained the uncontaminated, and attained liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom. I know and realize for myself, and dwell having attained it. Birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and there will be no more continuation of existence. I know this as it really is. I teach others, and others teach others, spreading to countless hundreds and thousands. Mānava! Therefore, you are well-versed in this discourse. You should well receive and uphold it. Why is that? The meaning of what is said should be like this."

于是，世尊告曰：「摩纳！汝善达此论。所以者何？我有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，于如意足心得自在，乃及身至梵天。摩纳！我以他相占他意，乃至占心、心所有法。摩纳！我自行如是道、如是迹，行此道、行此迹已，诸漏已尽，得无漏，心解脱、慧解脱，自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。我为他说，他为他说，如是展转无量百千。摩纳！是故汝善达此论，汝当如是善受善持。所以者何？此所说义，应当如是。」

Then Saṅgārava Mānava said: "World-Honored One! I have understood. Well-Gone One! I have understood. World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the community of monks. May the World-Honored One accept me as a lay follower, from this day forth until life's end, taking refuge with utmost sincerity."

于是，伤歌逻摩纳白曰：「世尊！我已知。善逝！我已解。世尊！我今自归于佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Saṅgārava Mānava, Venerable Ānanda, and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说如是。伤歌逻摩纳、尊者阿难及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
