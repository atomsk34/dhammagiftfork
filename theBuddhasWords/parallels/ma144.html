MĀ 144

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 12 梵志品

144. （一四四）算数目揵连经

Parallel to: MN 107

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Shravasti, staying at the Deer Park Hall of the Mother of the Deer.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在东园鹿子母堂。

At that time, the brahman mathematician Maudgalyayana was strolling around in the middle of the day and went to where the Buddha was. They exchanged greetings and sat to one side. He said, "Gautama! I wish to ask something. Please listen before I speak."

尔时，算数梵志目揵连中后彷徉，往诣佛所，共相问讯，却坐一面，白曰：「瞿昙！我欲有所问，听乃敢陈。」

The World Honored One said, "Maudgalyayana! Ask whatever you wish. Do not hesitate."

世尊告曰：「目揵连！恣汝所问，莫自疑难。」

The mathematician Maudgalyayana then asked, "Gautama! This Deer Park Hall was gradually built and completed. Gautama! This Deer Park Hall was first ascended by one step, then two, three, four. Gautama! Thus this Deer Park Hall was gradually ascended. Gautama! This elephant trainer also gradually trained and completed it, namely by using hooks. Gautama! This horse trainer also gradually trained and completed it, namely by using reins. Gautama! This kshatriya also gradually attained completion, namely by taking up a bow and arrow. Gautama! These Brahmins also gradually attained completion, namely by studying the scriptures. Gautama! We study mathematics and make a living through mathematics. We also gradually attained completion. If there are disciples, whether male or female, we first teach them to count one, two, three, ten, hundred, thousand, ten thousand, gradually up to the highest. Gautama! Thus we study mathematics and make a living through mathematics, gradually attaining completion. śramaṇa Gautama! In this Dharma and Vinaya, how is it gradually accomplished?"

算数目揵连则便问曰：「瞿昙！此鹿子母堂渐次第作，转后成讫。瞿昙！此鹿子母堂椷梯，初升一隥，后二、三、四。瞿昙！如是此鹿子母堂渐次第上。瞿昙！此御象者，亦渐次第调御成讫，谓因钩故。瞿昙！此御马者，亦渐次第调御成讫，谓因［革＊玄］故。瞿昙！此刹利亦渐次第至成就讫，谓因捉弓箭故。瞿昙！此诸梵志亦渐次第至成就讫，谓因学经书故。瞿昙！我等学算数，以算数存命，亦渐次第至成就讫。若有弟子，或男或女，始教一一数，二、二、三、三、十、百、千、万，次第至上。瞿昙！如是我等学算数，以算数存命，渐次第至成讫。沙门瞿昙！此法、律中，云何渐次第作至成就讫？」

The World Honored One said, "Maudgalyayana! If there is right teaching that is gradually accomplished, then it is accomplished. Maudgalyayana! In my Dharma and Vinaya, it is called right teaching. Why is that? Maudgalyayana! In this Dharma and Vinaya, I gradually accomplished it. Maudgalyayana! If a young bhikkhu first comes to study the path and enters the Dharma and Vinaya, the Tathāgata first teaches him: 'Bhikkhu! You have come. Guard the body and life with purity, guard speech and mind with purity.' Maudgalyayana! If a bhikkhu guards the body and life with purity, and guards speech and mind with purity, the Tathāgata further teaches him: 'Bhikkhu! You have come. Observe the internal body as body, up to observe perception, mind, and dharmas as dharmas.' Maudgalyayana! If a bhikkhu observes the internal body as body, up to observes perception, mind, and dharmas as dharmas, the Tathāgata further teaches him: 'Bhikkhu! You have come. Observe the internal body as body, do not think thoughts associated with desire, up to observe perception, mind, and dharmas as dharmas, do not think thoughts associated with unwholesome dharmas.'

世尊告曰：「目揵连！若有正说渐次第作，乃至成讫。目揵连！我法、律中谓正说。所以者何？目揵连！我于此法、律渐次第作至成就讫。目揵连！若年少比丘初来学道，始入法、律者，如来先教：『比丘！汝来身护命清净，口、意护命清净。』目揵连！若比丘身护命清净，口、意护命清净者，如来复上教：『比丘！汝来观内身如身，至观觉、心、法如法。』目揵连！若比丘观内身如身，至观觉、心、法如法者，如来复上教：『比丘！汝来观内身如身，莫念欲相应念，至观觉、心、法如法，莫念非法相应念。』

Maudgalyayana! If a bhikkhu observes the internal body as body, does not think thoughts associated with desire, up to observes perception, mind, and dharmas as dharmas, does not think thoughts associated with unwholesome dharmas, the Tathāgata further teaches him: 'Bhikkhu! You have come. Guard the sense faculties, always remember to close them, be mindful and clearly understand, guard the mind with mindfulness and attain accomplishment, always arouse right knowledge. If the eye sees forms, do not grasp their characteristics, do not savor forms, because of anger and dispute, guard the eye faculty. Do not give rise to greed, sorrow, evil and unwholesome dharmas in the mind, heading towards them, guard the eye faculty. In this way, the ear, nose, tongue, body, if the mind knows dharmas, do not grasp their characteristics, do not savor dharmas, because of anger and dispute, guard the mind faculty. Do not give rise to greed, sorrow, evil and unwholesome dharmas in the mind, heading towards them, guard the mind faculty.'

「目揵连！若比丘观内身如身，不念欲相应念，至观觉、心、法如法，不念非法相应念者，如来复上教：『比丘！汝来守护诸根，常念闭塞，念欲明达，守护念心而得成就，恒起正知，若眼见色，然不受相，亦不味色，谓忿诤故，守护眼根。心中不生贪伺、忧戚、恶不善法，趣向彼故，守护眼根。如是耳、鼻、舌、身，若意知法，然不受相，亦不味法，谓忿诤故，守护意根。心中不生贪伺、忧戚、恶不善法，趣向彼故，守护意根。』

Maudgalyayana! If a bhikkhu guards the sense faculties, always remembers to close them, is mindful and clearly understands, guards the mind with mindfulness and attains accomplishment, always arouses right knowledge. If the eye sees forms, does not grasp their characteristics, does not savor forms, because of anger and dispute, guards the eye faculty. Does not give rise to greed, sorrow, evil and unwholesome dharmas in the mind, heading towards them, guards the eye faculty. In this way, the ear, nose, tongue, body, if the mind knows dharmas, does not grasp their characteristics, does not savor dharmas, because of anger and dispute, guards the mind faculty. Does not give rise to greed, sorrow, evil and unwholesome dharmas in the mind, heading towards them, guards the mind faculty. The Tathāgata further teaches him: 'Bhikkhu! You have come. Know right and wrong when going out and coming in, observe and discern well, bending, stretching, bowing, and raising the head, deportment and appearance, well wearing the saṃghāṭī robe and other robes and carrying the alms bowl, walking, standing, sitting, lying down, sleeping, waking, speaking, and being silent, all know them well.'

「目揵连！若比丘守护诸根，常念闭塞，念欲明达，守护念心而得成就，恒起正知，若眼见色，然不受相，亦不味色，谓忿诤故，守护眼根。心中不生贪伺、忧戚、恶不善法，趣向彼故，守护眼根。如是耳、鼻、舌、身，若意知法，然不受相，亦不味法，谓忿诤故，守护意根。心中不生贪伺、忧戚、恶不善法，趣向彼故，守护意根者，如来复上教：『比丘！汝来正知出入，善观分别，屈伸低仰，仪容庠序，善著僧伽梨及诸衣钵，行住坐卧、眠寤语默，皆正知之。』

Maudgalyayana! If a bhikkhu knows right and wrong when going out and coming in, observes and discerns well, bends, stretches, bows, and raises the head, deportment and appearance, well wears the saṃghāṭī robe and other robes and carries the alms bowl, walks, stands, sits, lies down, sleeps, wakes, speaks, and is silent, all knows them well, the Tathāgata further teaches him: 'Bhikkhu! You have come. Dwell alone in seclusion, in a place without affairs, or go to a place under a tree, an empty and quiet place, a mountain cave, a stone chamber, an open space, or a pile of straw, or go to a forest, or dwell in a charnel ground. You have already gone to a place without affairs, or to a place under a tree, an empty and quiet place, spread out a sitting mat, cross your legs, straighten your body and mind, and be mindful, not facing towards, cut off greed and craving, and have no disputes in the mind. Seeing others' wealth and living necessities, do not give rise to greed and craving, wishing to obtain them for yourself. You should purify your mind from greed and craving. In this way, for anger, drowsiness, agitation, doubt, and confusion, there is no hesitation about wholesome dharmas. You should purify your mind from doubt and confusion. You should cut off these five hindrances, defilements of the mind, and weakness of wisdom. Free from desire and evil and unwholesome dharmas, attain the fourth dhyāna and abide in accomplishment.' Maudgalyayana! If a bhikkhu is free from desire and evil and unwholesome dharmas, attains the fourth dhyāna and abides in accomplishment, Maudgalyayana! The Tathāgata has much benefit for the younger bhikkhus, namely, instructing and admonishing them. Maudgalyayana! If there are bhikkhus who are elders, superiors, and long-practiced in the holy life, the Tathāgata further teaches them, namely, the ultimate completion of all taints.

「目揵连！若比丘正知出入，善观分别，屈伸低仰，仪容庠序，善著僧伽梨及诸衣钵，行住坐卧、眠寤语默，皆正知者，如来复上教：『比丘！汝来独住远离，在无事处，或至树下空安静处、山岩石室、露地穰积，或至林中，或住在冢间。汝已在无事处，或至树下空安静处，敷尼师檀，结加趺坐，正身正愿，及念不向，断除贪伺，心无有诤。见他财物、诸生活具，莫起贪伺，欲令我得，汝于贪伺净除其心。如是瞋恚、睡眠、调悔、断疑、度惑，于诸善法无有犹豫，汝于疑惑净除其心。汝断此五盖、心秽、慧羸、离欲、离恶不善之法，至得第四禅成就游。』目揵连！若比丘离欲、离恶不善之法，至得第四禅成就游者，目揵连！如来为诸年少比丘多有所益，谓训诲教诃。目揵连！若有比丘、长老、上尊、旧学梵行，如来复上教，谓究竟讫一切漏尽。」

Sāṅkhya Maudgalyāyana then asked again: "Ascetic Gotama! Do all disciples who are instructed and admonished in this way attain ultimate knowledge and necessarily attain parinirvāṇa?"

算数目揵连即复问曰：「沙门瞿昙！一切弟子如是训诲，如是教诃，尽得究竟智必涅槃耶？」

The World-Honored One replied: "Maudgalyayana! It is not always so. Some attain it, while others do not."

世尊答曰：「目揵连！不一向得，或有得者，或不得者。」

Sāṅkhya Maudgalyāyana then asked again: "Gautama! What are the causes and conditions for nirvāṇa and the path to nirvāṇa? śramaṇa Gautama is the present guide. Some bhikṣus are instructed and admonished in this way, and attain ultimate nirvāṇa, while others do not."

算数目揵连复更问曰：「瞿昙！此中何因何缘，有涅槃、有涅槃道？沙门瞿昙现在导师，或有比丘如是训诲，如是教诃，得究竟涅槃，或复不得耶？」

The World-Honored One said: "Maudgalyāyana! I will ask you in return. Answer as you see fit. Maudgalyāyana! What do you think? Do you know the location of Rājagṛha and are familiar with the road there?"

世尊告曰：「目揵连！我还问汝，随所解答。目揵连！于意云何？汝知王舍城处，谙彼道耶？」

Sāṅkhya Maudgalyāyana replied: "Yes, I know the location of Rājagṛha and am familiar with the road there."

算数目揵连答曰：「唯然。我知王舍城处，亦谙彼道。」

The World-Honored One asked: "Maudgalyāyana! If someone comes and wants to see the king, to go to Rājagṛha. That person asks you: 'I want to see the king, to go to Rājagṛha. Sāṅkhya Maudgalyāyana knows the location of Rājagṛha and is familiar with the road. Can you show me the way?' You tell that person: 'From here, go east to that village. From that village, go to that town. In this way, go to Rājagṛha. If there are good gardens and forests outside Rājagṛha, with level ground, pavilions, ponds, various flower trees, long flowing rivers, and clear springs, you will see and know them all.' Having heard your words and received your instructions, that person goes east from here. Before long, they leave the right path and return by the wrong path. If there are good gardens and forests outside Rājagṛha, with level ground, pavilions, ponds, various flower trees, long flowing rivers, and clear springs, they will not see or know them all.

世尊问曰：「目揵连！若有人来欲见彼王，至王舍城。其人问汝：『我欲见王，至王舍城，算数目揵连知王舍城处，谙彼道径，可示语我耶？』汝告彼人曰：『从此东行至彼某村，从某村去当至某邑，如是展转至王舍城。若王舍城外有好园林，其地平正，楼观浴池，若干华树，侠长流河，又有清泉尽见尽知。』彼人闻汝语，受汝教已，从此东行，须臾不久，便舍正道，从恶道还。若王舍城外有好园林，其地平正，楼观浴池，若干华树，侠长流河，又有清泉，彼尽不见，亦不知也。

Again, someone comes and wants to see the king, to go to Rājagṛha. That person asks you: 'I want to see the king, to go to Rājagṛha. Sāṅkhya Maudgalyāyana knows the location of Rājagṛha and is familiar with the road. Can you show me the way?' You tell that person: 'From here, go east to that village. From that village, go to that town. In this way, go to Rājagṛha. If there are good gardens and forests outside Rājagṛha, with level ground, pavilions, ponds, various flower trees, long flowing rivers, and clear springs, you will see and know them all.' Having heard your words and received your instructions, that person immediately goes east from here to that village. From that village, they reach that town. In this way, they go to Rājagṛha. If there are good gardens and forests outside Rājagṛha, with level ground, pavilions, ponds, various flower trees, long flowing rivers, and clear springs, they see and know them all.

「复有人来欲见彼王，至王舍城。其人问汝：『我欲见王，至王舍城。算数目揵连知王舍城处，谙彼道径，可示语我耶？』汝告彼人曰：『从此东行至彼某村，从某村去当至某邑，如是展转至王舍城。若王舍城外有好园林，其地平正，楼观浴池，若干华树，侠长流河，又有清泉，尽见尽知。』彼人闻汝语，受汝教已，即从此东行至彼某村，从某村去得至某邑，如是展转至王舍城。若王舍城外有好园林，其地平正，楼观浴池，若干华树，侠长流河，又有清泉，尽见尽知。

"Maudgalyāyana! What are the causes and conditions for the existence of Rājagṛha and the path to Rājagṛha? You are the present guide. The first person follows your instructions, but before long, they leave the level path and return by the wrong path. If there are good gardens and forests outside Rājagṛha, with level ground, pavilions, ponds, various flower trees, long flowing rivers, and clear springs, do they not see or know them all? The second person follows your instructions and gradually reaches Rājagṛha by the level path. If there are good gardens and forests outside Rājagṛha, with level ground, pavilions, ponds, various flower trees, long flowing rivers, and clear springs, do they see and know them all?"

「目揵连！此中何因何缘，有彼王舍城，有王舍城道？汝现在导师，彼第一人随受汝教，于后不久，舍平正道，从恶道还。若王舍城外有好园林，其地平正，楼观浴池，若干华树，侠长流河，又有清泉，彼尽不见，亦不知耶？彼第二人随受汝教，从平正道展转得至于王舍城，若王舍城外有好园林，其地平正，楼观浴池，若干华树，侠长流河，又有清泉，彼尽见尽知耶？」

Sāṅkhya Maudgalyāyana replied: "Gautama! I have nothing to do with the existence of Rājagṛha and the path to Rājagṛha. I am the present guide. The first person does not follow my instructions, leaves the level path, and returns by the wrong path. If there are good gardens and forests outside Rājagṛha, with level ground, pavilions, ponds, various flower trees, long flowing rivers, and clear springs, do they not see or know them all? The second person follows my instructions and gradually reaches Rājagṛha by the level path. If there are good gardens and forests outside Rājagṛha, with level ground, pavilions, ponds, various flower trees, long flowing rivers, and clear springs, do they see and know them all?"

算数目揵连答曰：「瞿昙！我都无事，有彼王舍城，有王舍城道。我现在导师，彼第一人不随我教，舍平正道，从恶道还，若王舍城外有好园林，其地平正，楼观浴池，若干华树，侠长流河，又有清泉，彼尽不见，亦不知耶？彼第二人随顺我教，从平正道展转得至于王舍城，若王舍城外有好园林，其地平正，楼观浴池，若干华树，侠长流河，又有清泉，彼尽见尽知耶？」

The World-Honored One said: "So it is, Maudgalyāyana! I also have nothing to do with nirvāṇa and the path to nirvāṇa. I am the guide. I instruct and admonish the bhikṣus in this way, and some attain ultimate nirvāṇa, while others do not. Maudgalyāyana! Each bhikṣu follows his own practice. At that time, the World-Honored One then predicted their practice, namely, the ultimate exhaustion of defilements."

世尊告曰：「如是。目揵连！我亦无事，有彼涅槃、有涅槃道。我为导师，为诸比丘如是训诲，如是教诃，得究竟涅槃，或有不得。目揵连！但各自随比丘所行。尔时世尊便记彼行，谓究竟漏尽耳。」

Sāṅkhya Maudgalyāyana said: "Gautama! I have understood. Gautama! I have understood. Gautama! It is like a good piece of land with a śāla forest. There is a śāla forest keeper there, who is diligent and not lazy. He digs up the roots of the śāla trees in a timely manner, fills in the high places and levels the low places, fertilizes and irrigates them, not missing the right time. If any weeds grow on the edges, he pulls them all out and discards them. If there are any crooked and unstraight trees, he trims them all. If there are any very good straight trees, he then protects them, digging around them in a timely manner, fertilizing and irrigating them, not missing the right time. In this way, the good piece of land and śāla forest become more luxuriant and beautiful. Gautama! It is like a person who is flattering, deceitful, extremely unreliable, without faith, lazy, without mindfulness, without concentration, with bad wisdom, with a deranged mind, with scattered faculties, who holds the precepts loosely, and does not widely practice the duties of an ascetic. Gautama! Such a person cannot be associated with. Why? Gautama! Such a person defiles the pure practice. Gautama! If there is a person who is not flattering, not deceitful, reliable, faithful, diligent, not lazy, mindful, concentrated, and also wise, who is extremely respectful of the precepts, and widely practices the duties of an ascetic. Gautama! Such a person can be associated with. Why? Gautama! Such a person is pure in the practice.

算数目揵连白曰：「瞿昙！我已知。瞿昙！我已解。瞿昙！犹如良地有娑罗林，彼中有守娑罗林人，明健不懈，诸娑罗根以时锄掘，平高填下，粪沃溉灌，不失其时。若其边有秽恶草生，尽拔弃之；若有横曲不调直者，尽［利－禾＋名］治之；若有极好中直树者，便权养护，随时锄掘，粪沃溉灌，不失其时。如是良地娑罗树林转茂盛好。瞿昙！如是有人谀谄、欺诳、极不庶几无信、懈怠、无念、无定、恶慧、心狂、诸根掉乱，持戒宽缓，不广修沙门。瞿昙！如是之人不能共事。所以者何？瞿昙！如是人者，秽污梵行。瞿昙！若复有人不有谀谄，亦不欺诳，庶几有信，精进不懈，有念、有定，亦有智慧，极恭敬戒，广修沙门。瞿昙！如是之人，能共事也。所以者何？瞿昙！如是人者，清净梵行。

Gautama! It is like the fragrance of various roots, where the fragrance of agarwood is the best. Why? Gautama! Because agarwood is the best among the fragrances of various roots. Gautama! It is like the fragrance of various śāla trees, where the fragrance of red sandalwood is the best. Why? Gautama! Because red sandalwood is the best among the fragrances of various śāla trees. Gautama! It is like the flowers of various water plants, where the blue lotus flower is the best. Why? Gautama! Because the blue lotus flower is the best among the flowers of various water plants. Gautama! It is like the flowers of various land plants, where the sumana flower is the best. Why? Gautama! Because the sumana flower is the best among the flowers of various land plants. Gautama! It is like the various debaters in the world, where the Śramaṇa Gautama is the best. Why? Because the Śramaṇa Gautama is able to subdue all non-Buddhist debaters. World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and the Saṃgha of bhikṣus. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept me as a lay follower. From this day forth, until the end of my life, I take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and the Saṃgha of bhikṣus.

「瞿昙！犹诸根香，沈香为第一。所以者何？瞿昙！彼沈香者，于诸根香为最上故。瞿昙！犹诸娑罗树香，赤栴檀为第一。所以者何？瞿昙！赤栴檀者于诸娑罗树香为最上故。瞿昙！犹诸水华，青莲华为第一。所以者何？瞿昙！青莲华者于诸水华为最上故。瞿昙！犹诸陆华，修摩那花为第一。所以者何？瞿昙！修摩那花者于诸陆花为最上故。瞿昙！犹如世中诸有论士，沙门瞿昙为最第一。所以者何？沙门瞿昙论士能伏一切外道异学故。世尊！我今自归于佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Sāṅkhya Maudgalyāyana and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说如是。算数目揵连及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
