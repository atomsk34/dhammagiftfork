MĀ 145

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 12 梵志品

145. （一四五）瞿默目揵连经

Parallel to: MN 108 / SN 22.58 (~)

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, not long after the Buddha's parinirvāṇa, Venerable Ānanda was wandering in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛般涅槃后不久，尊者阿难游王舍城。

At that time, the Magadhan minister Vṛṣa was governing Rājagṛha to prevent the Vrjis. Then the Magadhan minister Vṛṣa sent a farmer named Gautama to the Bamboo Grove Kalandakanivāpa.

尔时，摩竭陀大臣雨势治王舍城，为防跋耆故。于是，摩竭陀大臣雨势遣瞿默目揵连田作人，往至竹林加兰哆园。

At that time, Venerable Ānanda, having passed the night, put on his robes, took his alms bowl, and entered Rājagṛha for alms. Then Venerable Ānanda thought: "Let me put off begging for alms in Rājagṛha. I shall go to the farmer Gautama." Then Venerable Ānanda went to the farmer Gautama.

尔时，尊者阿难过夜平旦，著衣持钵，为乞食故，入王舍城。于是，尊者阿难作是念：「且置王舍城乞食，我宁可往诣瞿默目揵连田作人所。」于是，尊者阿难往诣瞿默目揵连田作人所。

The brāhman Gautama saw Venerable Ānanda coming from afar, and immediately rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms towards Venerable Ānanda, and said: "Welcome, Ānanda! You have not come here for a long time. Please sit on this seat." Venerable Ānanda then sat on that seat.

梵志瞿默目揵连遥见尊者阿难来，即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向尊者阿难，白曰：「善来，阿难！久不来此，可坐此座。」尊者阿难即坐彼座。

The brāhman Gautama exchanged greetings with Venerable Ānanda, then sat to one side and said: "Ānanda! I wish to ask something. May I ask?"

梵志瞿默目揵连与尊者阿难共相问讯，却坐一面，白曰：「阿难！欲有所问，听我问耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied: "Gautama! You may ask. I shall think about it when I hear it."

尊者阿难报曰：「目揵连！汝便可问，我闻当思。」

He then asked: "Ānanda! Is there a monk who is equal to the śramaṇa Gautama?"

则便问曰：「阿难！颇有一比丘与沙门瞿昙等耶？」

When Venerable Ānanda was discussing this matter with the brāhman Gautama Mahānāma, at that time the Magadhan minister Vṛṣa was consoling the farmers and went to where the brāhman Gautama Mahānāma was.

尊者阿难与梵志瞿默目揵连共论此事时，尔时，摩竭陀大臣雨势慰劳田作人，往诣梵志瞿默目揵连田作人所。

The Magadhan minister Vṛṣa saw from afar Venerable Ānanda sitting among the farmers of the brāhman Gautama Mahānāma, and went to where Venerable Ānanda was. They greeted each other and sat down to one side. He asked: "Ānanda! What were you discussing with the brāhman Gautama Mahānāma? What was the reason for your meeting here?"

摩竭陀大臣雨势遥见尊者阿难坐在梵志瞿默目揵连田作人中，往诣尊者阿难所，共相问讯，却坐一面，问曰：「阿难！与梵志瞿默目揵连共论何事，以何事故共会此耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied: "Vṛṣa! The brāhman Gautama Mahānāma asked me: 'Ānanda! Is there any bhikṣu who is equal to the śramaṇa Gautama?'"

尊者阿难答曰：「雨势！梵志瞿默目揵连问我：『阿难！颇有一比丘与沙门瞿昙等耶？』」

The Magadhan minister Vṛṣa again asked: "Ānanda! How did you answer him?"

摩竭陀大臣雨势复问曰：「阿难！云何答彼？」

Venerable Ānanda replied: "Vṛṣa! There is no bhikṣu at all who is equal to the World-Honored One."

尊者阿难答曰：「雨势！都无一比丘与世尊等等。」

The Magadhan minister Vṛṣa again asked: "Yes, Ānanda! There is no bhikṣu at all who is equal to the World-Honored One. Is there a bhikṣu whom the Śramaṇa Gautama appointed during his lifetime, who will be the support for the bhikṣus after my parinirvāṇa, that is, the one you now rely on?"

摩竭陀大臣雨势复问曰：「唯然，阿难！无一比丘与世尊等等。颇有一比丘为沙门瞿昙在时所立，此比丘我般涅槃后，为诸比丘所依，谓令汝等今所依耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied: "Vṛṣa! There is no bhikṣu at all whom the World-Honored One knew and saw, whom the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One appointed during his lifetime, who will be the support for the bhikṣus after my parinirvāṇa, that is, the one you now rely on."

尊者阿难答曰：「雨势！都无一比丘为世尊所知、见，如来、无所著、等正觉在时所立，此比丘我般涅槃后，为诸比丘所依，谓令我等今所依者。」

The Magadhan minister Vṛṣa again asked: "Ānanda! Yes, there is no bhikṣu at all who is equal to the Śramaṇa Gautama. There is also no bhikṣu whom the Śramaṇa Gautama appointed during his lifetime, who will be the support for the bhikṣus after my parinirvāṇa, that is, the one you now rely on. Is there a bhikṣu who is in harmony with the assembly and is revered by them, who will be the support for the bhikṣus after the World-Honored One's parinirvāṇa, that is, the one you now rely on?"

摩竭陀大臣雨势复问曰：「阿难！唯然。无一比丘与沙门瞿昙等等；亦无一比丘为沙门瞿昙在时所立，此比丘我般涅槃后，为诸比丘所依，谓令汝等今所依者。颇有一比丘与众共和集拜，此比丘世尊般涅槃后，为诸比丘所依，谓令汝等今所依耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied: "Vṛṣa! There is also no bhikṣu who is in harmony with the assembly and is revered by them, who will be the support for the bhikṣus after the World-Honored One's parinirvāṇa, that is, the one you now rely on."

尊者阿难答曰：「雨势！亦无一比丘与众共和集拜，此比丘世尊般涅槃后，为诸比丘所依，谓令我等今所依者。」

The Magadhan minister Vṛṣa again asked: "Ānanda! Yes, there is no bhikṣu at all who is equal to the Śramaṇa Gautama. There is also no bhikṣu whom the Śramaṇa Gautama appointed during his lifetime, who will be the support for the bhikṣus after my parinirvāṇa, that is, the one you now rely on. There is also no bhikṣu who is in harmony with the assembly and is revered by them, who will be the support for the bhikṣus after the World-Honored One's parinirvāṇa, that is, the one you now rely on. Ānanda! Then, you have no support, but are in harmony, without dispute, at peace, with the same teaching, united like water and milk, and happily wandering, just as when the Śramaṇa Gautama was here?"

摩竭陀大臣雨势复问曰：「阿难！唯然。无一比丘与沙门瞿昙等等；亦无一比丘为沙门瞿昙在时所立，此比丘我般涅槃后，为诸比丘所依，谓令汝等今所依者；亦无一比丘与众共和集拜此比丘，世尊般涅槃后，为诸比丘所依，谓令汝等今所依者。阿难！若尔者，汝等无所依，共和合、不诤、安隐，同一一教，合一水乳，快乐游行，如沙门瞿昙在时耶？」

Venerable Ānanda said: "Vṛṣa! Do not say that we have no support. Why is that? We have support."

尊者阿难告曰：「雨势！汝莫作是说，言我等无所依。所以者何？我等有所依耳。」

The Magadhan minister Vṛṣa said: "Ānanda! How are your previous and later statements not contradictory? Ānanda said before: 'There is no bhikṣu at all who is equal to the World-Honored One. There is also no bhikṣu whom the World-Honored One knew and saw, the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One appointed during his lifetime, who will be the support for the bhikṣus after my parinirvāṇa, that is, the one we now rely on. There is also no bhikṣu who is in harmony with the assembly and is revered by them, who will be the support for the bhikṣus after the World-Honored One's parinirvāṇa, that is, the one we now rely on.' Ānanda! For what reason do you now say that we have support?"

摩竭陀大臣雨势白曰：「阿难！前后所说何不相应？阿难向如是说：『无一比丘与世尊等等；亦无一比丘为世尊所知、见，如来、无所著、等正觉在时所立，此比丘我般涅槃后，为诸比丘所依，谓令我等今所依者；亦无一比丘与众共和集拜，此比丘世尊般涅槃后，为诸比丘所依，谓令我等今所依者。』阿难！何因何缘，今说我有所依耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied: "Vṛṣa! We do not rely on people, but on the Dharma. Vṛṣa! If we travel relying on villages, on the fifteenth day when we recite the Prātimokṣa, we gather in one place. If there is a bhikṣu who knows the Dharma, we invite that bhikṣu to teach the Dharma for us. If that assembly is pure, we all joyfully accept and practice what that bhikṣu says. If that assembly is impure, we follow the Dharma as it is taught and instruct them accordingly."

尊者阿难答曰：「雨势！我等不依于人而依于法。雨势！我等若依村邑游行，十五日说从解脱时，集坐一处。若有比丘知法者，我等请彼比丘为我等说法。若彼众清净者，我等一切欢喜奉行彼比丘所说。若彼众不清净者，随法所说，我等教作是。」

The Magadhan minister Vṛṣa said: "Ānanda! It is not that you teach this, but the Dharma teaches this. Ānanda! If there is a small or large Dharma that can be upheld for a long time, then Ānanda and others will be in harmony, without dispute, at peace, with a single teaching, united like water and milk, happily wandering about, just as when the Śramaṇa Gautama was here."

摩竭陀大臣雨势白曰：「阿难！非汝等教作是，但法教作是。阿难！如是少法、多法可得久住者，如是阿难等共和合、不诤、安隐，同一一教，合一水乳，快乐游行，如沙门瞿昙在时。」

The Magadhan minister Vṛṣa further asked: "Ānanda! Is there anyone worthy of respect?"

摩竭陀大臣雨势复问曰：「阿难！颇有可尊敬耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied: "Vṛṣa! There is someone worthy of respect."

尊者阿难答曰：「雨势！有可尊敬。」

Vṛṣa said: "Ānanda! What you said before and now are not consistent. Ānanda said before: 'There is no bhikṣu who is equal to the World-Honored One; there is also no bhikṣu who was established by the World-Honored One during his lifetime as the support for bhikṣus after his parinirvāṇa, who would be the support for us now; there is also no bhikṣu who was unanimously chosen by the assembly as the support for bhikṣus after the World-Honored One's parinirvāṇa, who would be the support for us now.' Ānanda! For what reason do you now say there is someone worthy of respect?"

雨势白曰：「阿难！前后所说何不相应？阿难向如是说：『无一比丘与世尊共等等；亦无一比丘为世尊在时所立，此比丘我般涅槃后，为诸比丘所依，谓令我等今所依者；亦无一比丘与众共和集拜，此比丘世尊般涅槃后，为诸比丘所依，谓令我等今所依者。』阿难！汝何因何缘，今说有可尊敬耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied: "Vṛṣa! The World-Honored One, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, spoke of ten qualities that are worthy of respect. If we see a bhikṣu who possesses these ten qualities, we will all love, respect, honor, make offerings to, revere, and pay homage to that bhikṣu. What are the ten? Vṛṣa! A bhikṣu cultivates precepts, guards the precepts, and is well-disciplined in conduct and deportment. Seeing even the slightest fault, he is always fearful and upholds the precepts. Vṛṣa! If we see a bhikṣu who practices the precepts to the utmost, we will all love, respect, honor, make offerings to, revere, and pay homage to that bhikṣu.

尊者阿难答曰：「雨势！世尊知、见，如来、无所著、等正觉说有十法而可尊敬，我等若见比丘有此十法者，则共爱敬、尊重、供养、宗奉，礼事于彼比丘。云何为十？雨势！比丘修习禁戒，守护从解脱，又复善摄威仪礼节，见纤芥罪，常怀畏怖，受持学戒。雨势！我等若见比丘极行增上戒者，则共爱敬、尊重、供养、宗奉，礼事于彼比丘。

Furthermore, Vṛṣa! A bhikṣu extensively studies and memorizes, guarding and not forgetting, accumulating extensive learning. The teachings he upholds are wonderful in the beginning, wonderful in the middle, and wonderful in the end. They are meaningful, well-spoken, complete, pure, and clearly present the holy life. He extensively studies and memorizes such teachings, reciting and practicing them up to a thousand times, contemplating them with the mind, seeing them clearly and deeply. Vṛṣa! If we see a bhikṣu who is extremely learned, we will all love, respect, honor, make offerings to, revere, and pay homage to that bhikṣu.

「复次，雨势！比丘广学多闻，守持不忘，积聚博闻。所谓法者，初妙、中妙、竟亦妙，有义有文，具足清净，显现梵行。如是诸法广学，多闻诵习至千，意所推观，明见深远。雨势！我等若见比丘极多闻者，则共爱敬、尊重、供养、宗奉，礼事于彼比丘。

Furthermore, Vṛṣa! A bhikṣu becomes a good friend, a good companion, a good associate. Vṛṣa! If we see a bhikṣu who is an extremely good friend, we will all love, respect, honor, make offerings to, revere, and pay homage to that bhikṣu.

「复次，雨势！比丘作善知识，作善朋友，作善伴党。雨势！我等若见比丘极善知识者，则共爱敬、尊重、供养、宗奉，礼事于彼比丘。

Furthermore, Vṛṣa! A bhikṣu delights in dwelling in seclusion, accomplishing the two kinds of seclusion, of body and mind. Vṛṣa! If we see a bhikṣu who greatly delights in dwelling in seclusion, we will all love, respect, honor, make offerings to, revere, and pay homage to that bhikṣu.

「复次，雨势！比丘乐住远离，成就二远离，身及心也。雨势！我等若见比丘极乐住远离者，则共爱敬、尊重、供养、宗奉，礼事于彼比丘。

Furthermore, Vṛṣa! A bhikṣu delights in sitting in meditation, practicing inner stillness, not apart from contemplation, accomplishing insight, and increasing the practice of emptiness. Vṛṣa! If we see a bhikṣu who greatly delights in sitting in meditation, we will all love, respect, honor, make offerings to, revere, and pay homage to that bhikṣu.

「复次，雨势！比丘乐于燕坐，内行正止，亦不离伺，成就于观，增长空行。雨势！我等若见比丘极乐燕坐者，则共爱敬、尊重、供养、宗奉，礼事于彼比丘。

Furthermore, Vṛṣa! A bhikṣu is content, taking robes to cover the body, and food to fill the stomach. Wherever he goes, he carries his robes and bowl, walking without attachment. Like a bird of prey with its two wings, he soars through the sky. In this way, a bhikṣu is content, taking robes to cover the body, and food to fill the stomach. Wherever he goes, he carries his robes and bowl, walking without attachment. Vṛṣa! If we see a bhikṣu who is extremely content, we will all love, respect, honor, make offerings to, revere, and pay homage to that bhikṣu.

「复次，雨势！比丘知足，衣取覆形，食取充躯，随所游至，与衣钵俱，行无顾恋。犹如鹰鸟，与两翅俱，飞翔空中；如是比丘知足，衣取覆形，食取充躯，随所游至，与衣钵俱，行无顾恋。雨势！我等若见比丘极知足者，则共爱敬、尊重、供养、宗奉，礼事于彼比丘。

Furthermore, Vṛṣa! A bhikṣu always practices mindfulness, accomplishing right mindfulness. He always remembers and does not forget what he has practiced and heard for a long time. Vṛṣa! If we see a bhikṣu who has right mindfulness, we will all love, respect, honor, make offerings to, revere, and pay homage to that bhikṣu.

「复次，雨势！比丘常行于念，成就正念，久所曾习，久所曾闻，恒忆不忘。雨势！我等若见比丘极有正念者，则共爱敬、尊重、供养、宗奉，礼事于彼比丘。

Furthermore, Vṛṣa! A bhikṣu always practices diligence, abandoning unwholesome dharmas and cultivating wholesome dharmas. He always arouses his own mind, single-mindedly and firmly, for the sake of wholesome roots, not abandoning skillful means. Vṛṣa! If we see a bhikṣu who is extremely diligent, we will all love, respect, honor, make offerings to, revere, and pay homage to that bhikṣu.

「复次，雨势！比丘常行精进，断恶不善，修诸善法，恒自起意，专一坚固，为诸善本，不舍方便。雨势！我等若见比丘极精懃者，则共爱敬、尊重、供养、宗奉，礼事于彼比丘。

Furthermore, Vṛṣa! A bhikṣu practices wisdom, observing the dharmas of arising and decay. He attains such wisdom, with the bright and penetrating wisdom of the sages, discerning and understanding, to completely end suffering. Vṛṣa! If we see a bhikṣu who practices wisdom to the utmost, we will all love, respect, honor, make offerings to, revere, and pay homage to that bhikṣu.

「复次，雨势！比丘修行智慧，观兴衰法，得如此智，圣慧明达，分别晓了，以正尽苦。雨势！我等若见比丘极行慧者，则共爱敬、尊重、供养、宗奉，礼事于彼比丘。

Furthermore, Vṛṣa! A bhikṣu has exhausted all taints, attained the state free from taints, with liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom. He knows and realizes for himself, attains accomplishment through his own efforts, and abides in it. His births are exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and he will not be subject to further existence. He knows this as it truly is. Vṛṣa! If we see a bhikṣu who has exhausted all taints, we will all love, respect, honor, make offerings to, revere, and pay homage to that bhikṣu.

「复次，雨势！比丘诸漏已尽，而得无漏，心解脱、慧解脱，自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。雨势！我等若见比丘诸漏尽者，则共爱敬、尊重、供养、宗奉，礼事于彼比丘。

"Vṛṣa! The World-Honored One, the Tathāgata, the Unattached, the Perfectly Enlightened One, speaks of these ten qualities that are worthy of respect. Vṛṣa! If we see a bhikṣu who practices these ten qualities, we will all love, respect, honor, make offerings to, revere, and pay homage to that bhikṣu. Then the great assembly let out a loud and great sound, saying: 'The straight path can be practiced, not that it cannot be practiced. If the straight path can be practiced, not that it cannot be practiced, then the Arhat will be loved, respected, honored, and paid homage to in the world. If the venerable ones can practice the straight path and do practice it, then the Arhat will be loved, respected, honored, and paid homage to in the world.'"

「雨势！世尊知见，如来、无所著、等正觉说此十法而可尊敬。雨势！我等若见比丘行此十法者，则共爱敬、尊重、供养、宗奉，礼事于彼比丘。于是，彼大众放高大音声：『可修直道，非不可修，若修直道，非不可修者，随世中阿罗诃爱敬、尊重、供养、礼事。若诸尊可修直道而能修者，是故世中阿罗诃爱敬、尊重、供养、礼事。』」

Then the Magadhan minister Vṛṣa and his retinue asked: "Ānanda! Where are you going now?"

于是，摩竭陀大臣雨势及其眷属问曰：「阿难！今游何处？」

Venerable Ānanda replied: "I am now wandering in this Bamboo Grove Kalanda-nivāpa in Rājagṛha."

尊者阿难答曰：「我今游行此王舍城竹林加兰哆园。」

"Ānanda! The Bamboo Grove Kalanda-nivāpa is very delightful, peaceful and pleasant, not noisy during the day, quiet at night, no mosquitoes or flies, no fleas or lice, not cold or hot. Ānanda! Do you enjoy staying in the Bamboo Grove Kalanda-nivāpa?"

「阿难！竹林加兰哆园至可爱乐，政顿可喜，昼不喧閙，夜则静寂，无有蚊虻，亦无蝇蚤，不寒不热。阿难！乐住竹林加兰哆园耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied: "Yes, it is so, Uposatha! Yes, it is so. The Bamboo Grove Kalanda-nivāpa is very delightful, peaceful and pleasant, not noisy during the day, quiet at night, no mosquitoes or flies, no fleas or lice, not cold or hot. Uposatha! I enjoy staying in the Bamboo Grove Kalanda-nivāpa. Why is that? Because the World-Honored One protects me."

尊者阿难答曰：「如是。雨势！如是。雨势！竹林加兰哆园至可爱乐，政顿可喜，昼不喧閙，夜则静寂，无有蚊虻，亦无蝇蚤，不寒不热。雨势！我乐住竹林加兰哆园中。所以者何？以世尊拥护故。」

At that time, General Uposatha was in the assembly. General Uposatha said: "Yes, it is so, Uposatha! Yes, it is so. The Bamboo Grove Kalanda-nivāpa is very delightful, peaceful and pleasant, not noisy during the day, quiet at night, no mosquitoes or flies, no fleas or lice, not cold or hot. That Venerable One enjoys staying in the Bamboo Grove Kalanda-nivāpa. Why is that? Because that Venerable One practices contemplation and delights in contemplation."

是时，婆难大将在彼众中。婆难大将白曰：「如是。雨势！如是。雨势！竹林加兰哆园至可爱乐，政顿可喜，昼不喧閙，夜则静寂，无有蚊虻，亦无蝇蚤，不寒不热，彼尊者乐住竹林加兰哆园。所以者何？此尊者行伺、乐伺故。」

Having heard this, General Uposatha said: "General Uposatha! The Śramaṇa Gautama used to wander in the Kalandakanivāpa in the Bamboo Grove. General Uposatha! At that time, I often went to see the Śramaṇa Gautama. Why is that? Because the Śramaṇa Gautama practices contemplation and delights in contemplation, praising all forms of contemplation."

摩竭陀大臣雨势闻已，语曰：「婆难大将！沙门瞿昙昔时游行金鞞罗乐园中，婆难大将！尔时我数往诣彼，见沙门瞿昙。所以者何？沙门瞿昙行伺、乐伺，称叹一切伺。」

Venerable Ānanda heard this and said: "Uposatha! Do not say that the Śramaṇa Gautama praises all forms of contemplation. Why is that? The World-Honored One sometimes praises contemplation, sometimes does not praise it."

尊者阿难闻已，告曰：「雨势！莫作是说，沙门瞿昙称说一切伺。所以者何？世尊或称说伺，或不称说。」

The Magadhan minister Uposatha asked again: "Ānanda! The Śramaṇa Gautama does not praise all forms of contemplation. What kind of contemplation does he not praise?"

摩竭陀大臣雨势复问曰：「阿难！沙门瞿昙不称说伺，不称说何等伺？」

Venerable Ānanda replied: "Uposatha! There is one who is entangled in desire and gives rise to desire, but does not know the true way out. Because of being obstructed by desire, he contemplates, increases contemplation, and contemplates again. Uposatha! This is called the first contemplation that the World-Honored One does not praise. Furthermore, Uposatha! There is one who is entangled in anger and gives rise to anger, but does not know the true way out. Because of being obstructed by anger, he contemplates, increases contemplation, and contemplates again. Uposatha! This is called the second contemplation that the World-Honored One does not praise. Furthermore, Uposatha! There is one who is entangled in sleep and gives rise to sleep, but does not know the true way out. Because of being obstructed by sleep, he contemplates, increases contemplation, and contemplates again. Uposatha! This is called the third contemplation that the World-Honored One does not praise. Furthermore, Uposatha! There is one who is entangled in doubt and gives rise to doubt, but does not know the true way out. Because of being obstructed by doubt, he contemplates, increases contemplation, and contemplates again. Uposatha! This is called the fourth contemplation that the World-Honored One does not praise. Uposatha! The World-Honored One does not praise these four contemplations."

尊者阿难答曰：「雨势！或有一贪欲所缠而起贪欲，不知出要如真，彼为贪欲所障碍故，伺、增伺而重伺。雨势！是谓第一伺，世尊不称说。复次，雨势！或有一瞋恚所缠而起瞋恚，不知出要如真，彼为瞋恚所障碍故，伺、增伺而重伺。雨势！是谓第二伺，世尊不称说。复次，雨势！睡眠所缠而起睡眠，不知出要如真，彼为睡眠所障碍故，伺、增伺而重伺。雨势！是谓第三伺，世尊不称说。复次，雨势！疑惑所缠而起疑惑，不知出要如真，彼为疑惑所障碍故，伺、增伺而重伺。雨势！是谓第四伺，世尊不称说。雨势！世尊不称说此四伺。」

The Magadhan minister Uposatha said: "Ānanda! The Śramaṇa Gautama does not praise these four contemplations. Why is that? Because he is a perfect and complete Buddha."

摩竭陀大臣雨势白曰：「阿难！此四伺可增可增处，沙门瞿昙不称说。所以者何？正尽觉故。」

The Magadhan minister Uposatha asked again: "Ānanda! What contemplations does the Śramaṇa Gautama praise?"

摩竭陀大臣雨势复问曰：「阿难！何等伺沙门瞿昙所称说？」

Venerable Ānanda replied: "Uposatha! A monk, having abandoned desire, abandoned evil and unwholesome qualities, attains and abides in the fourth dhyāna. Uposatha! The World-Honored One praises these four contemplations."

尊者阿难答曰：「雨势！比丘者，离欲、离恶不善之法，至得第四禅成就游。雨势！世尊称说此四伺。」

The Magadhan minister Uposatha said: "Ānanda! These four contemplations are praiseworthy and worthy of praise by the Śramaṇa Gautama. Why is that? Because of his perfect awakening. Ānanda! I have many duties. I will now take my leave and return."

摩竭陀大臣雨势白曰：「阿难！此四伺可称可称处，沙门瞿昙所称。所以者何？以正尽觉故。阿难！我事烦猥，请退还归。」

Venerable Ānanda said: "You may return as you wish."

尊者阿难告曰：「欲还随意。」

Then the Magadhan minister Uposatha, having heard what was said by Venerable Ānanda, received it well and remembered it well. He then rose from his seat, circumambulated Venerable Ānanda three times to the right, and departed.

于是，摩竭陀大臣雨势闻尊者阿难所说，善受善持，即从坐起，绕尊者阿难三匝而去。

Then the brāhman Gautama, not long after the Magadhan minister Uposatha had left, said: "Ānanda! You did not answer my questions at all!"

是时，梵志瞿默目揵连于摩竭陀大臣雨势去后不久，白曰：「阿难！我所问事，都不答耶？」

Venerable Ānanda said: "Gautama! I really did not answer."

尊者阿难告曰：「目揵连！我实不答。」

The brāhman Gautama said: "Ānanda! I have more questions. May I ask them?"

梵志瞿默目揵连白曰：「阿难！我更有所问，听我问耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied: "Gautama! You may ask. I will think about it when I hear it."

尊者阿难答曰：「目揵连！汝便可问，我闻当思。」

The brāhman Gautama asked: "Ānanda! If the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, the liberated one, the one liberated through wisdom, and the Arhat are liberated, what is the difference between these three liberations? What is the superiority?"

梵志瞿默目揵连即问曰：「阿难！若如来、无所著、等正觉解脱，及慧解脱、阿罗诃解脱，此三解脱有何差别？有何胜如？」

Venerable Ānanda replied: "Gautama! If the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, the liberated one, the one liberated through wisdom, and the Arhat are liberated, there is no difference between these three liberations, nor is there any superiority."

尊者阿难答曰：「目揵连！若如来、无所著、等正觉解脱，及慧解脱、阿罗诃解脱，此三解脱无有差别，亦无胜如。」

The brāhman Gautama said: "Ānanda! Please eat here." Venerable Ānanda silently accepted.

梵志瞿默目揵连白曰：「阿难！可在此食。」尊者阿难默然而受。

The brāhman Gautama, knowing that he had silently accepted, immediately rose from his seat, served the water for washing, and offered various abundant and delicious food and drink, personally serving and pouring until he was fully satisfied. After the meal, he put away the dishes, served the water for washing, took a small seat, and sat separately to listen to the Dharma.

梵志瞿默目揵连知默然受已，即从坐起，自行澡水，极美净妙，种种丰饶食噉含消，自手斟酌，极令饱满，食讫举器，行澡水竟，取一小床，别坐听法。

Venerable Ānanda spoke the Dharma for him, inspiring him with longing and bringing about joy. Having used immeasurable skillful means to speak the Dharma for him, inspiring him with longing and bringing about joy, Venerable Ānanda spoke thus. The retinue of the Magadhan minister Varṣākāra and the brāhman Gautama heard what Venerable Ānanda had said, and they rejoiced and practiced it.

尊者阿难为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已。尊者阿难所说如是。摩竭陀大臣雨势眷属及梵志瞿默目揵连，闻尊者阿难所说，欢喜奉行。
