MĀ 148

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 12 梵志品

148. （一四八）何苦经

Parallel to: AN 5.31 (~)

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the Brāhman Sañjayin, after midday, leisurely went to where the Buddha was, exchanged greetings, sat to one side, and said, "Gautama! I wish to ask something. Please listen before I speak."

尔时，生闻梵志中后彷徉，往诣佛所，共相问讯，却坐一面，白曰：「瞿昙！我欲有所问，听乃敢陈。」

The World-Honored One said, "Brāhman! Ask as you please."

世尊告曰：「梵志！恣汝所问。」

The Brāhman Sañjayin then asked, "Gautama! What are the sufferings of a householder? What are the sufferings of one who has left home to study the path?"

生闻梵志即便问曰：「瞿昙！在家者有何苦？出家学道者有何苦耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Brāhman! For a householder, not being self-sufficient is suffering. For one who has left home to study the path, being self-sufficient is suffering."

世尊答曰：「梵志！在家者，以不自在为苦，出家学道者，以自在为苦。」

The Brāhman Sañjayin then asked, "Gautama! How is it that for a householder, not being self-sufficient is suffering? How is it that for one who has left home to study the path, being self-sufficient is suffering?"

生闻梵志复问曰：「瞿昙！在家者，云何以不自在为苦？出家学道者，云何以自在为苦耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Brāhman! If a householder's wealth does not increase, their gold, silver, pearls, lapis lazuli, and crystal do not increase, and their livestock, grain, and slaves and servants do not increase, then at that time, the householder is sorrowful, suffering, and distressed. Because of this, householders have much suffering and distress. Brāhman! If one who has left home to study the path acts according to their desires, acts according to their anger and delusion, then at that time, the one who has left home to study the path is sorrowful, suffering, and distressed. Because of this, those who have left home to study the path have much suffering and distress. Brāhman! In this way, for householders, not being self-sufficient is suffering, and for those who have left home to study the path, being self-sufficient is suffering."

世尊答曰：「梵志！若在家者，钱不增长，金、银、真珠、琉璃、水精悉不增长，畜牧、谷米及奴婢使亦不增长，尔时，在家忧苦愁戚。因此故在家者多有忧苦，多怀愁戚。梵志！若出家学道者行随其欲，行随恚、痴，尔时，出家学道忧苦愁戚。因此故出家学道者多有忧苦，多怀愁戚。梵志！如是在家者，以不自在为苦，出家学道者，以自在为苦。」

The Brāhman Sañjayin then asked, "Gautama! What is the happiness for a householder? What is the happiness for one who has left home to study the path?"

生闻梵志复问曰：「瞿昙！在家者有何乐？出家学道者有何乐耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Brāhman! For householders, being self-sufficient is happiness, and for those who have left home to study the path, not being self-sufficient is happiness."

世尊答曰：「梵志！在家者，以自在为乐，出家学道者，以不自在为乐。」

The Brāhman Sañjayin then asked, "Gautama! How is it that householders take self-sufficiency as happiness, and how is it that those who have left home to study the path take not being self-sufficient as happiness?"

生闻梵志复问曰：「瞿昙！在家者，云何以自在为乐？出家学道者，云何以不自在为乐耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Brāhman! If a householder's wealth increases, and their gold, silver, pearls, lapis lazuli, and crystal all increase, and their livestock, grain, and male and female servants also increase, then at that time the householder is happy and joyful. For this reason, householders have much happiness and joy. Brāhman! For those who have left home to study the path, if they practice not following desire, not following anger or delusion, then at that time those who have left home to study the path are happy and joyful. For this reason, those who have left home to study the path have much happiness and joy. Brāhman! In this way, for householders, self-sufficiency is happiness, and for those who have left home to study the path, not being self-sufficient is happiness."

世尊答曰：「梵志！若在家者钱得增长，金、银、真珠、琉璃、水精皆得增长，畜牧、谷米及奴婢使亦得增长，尔时，在家快乐欢喜。因此故在家者多快乐欢喜。梵志！出家学道者行不随欲，行不随恚、痴，尔时，出家学道快乐欢喜。因此故出家学道者多快乐欢喜。梵志！如是在家者，以自在为乐，出家学道者，以不自在为乐。」

The Brāhman Sañjayin then asked, "Gautama! What causes heaven and humans to definitely have no benefit and meaning? What causes heaven and humans to definitely have benefit and meaning?"

生闻梵志复问曰：「瞿昙！以何事故，令天及人必无利义？以何事故，令天及人必有利义？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Brāhman! If heaven and humans quarrel together, they will definitely have no benefit and meaning. If heaven and humans do not quarrel, they will definitely have benefit and meaning."

世尊答曰：「梵志！若天及人共诤者，必无利义，若天及人不诤者，必有利义。」

The Brāhman Sañjayin then asked, "Gautama! How do heaven and humans quarrel together, so that they definitely have no benefit and meaning? How do heaven and humans not quarrel, so that they definitely have benefit and meaning?"

生闻梵志复问曰：「瞿昙！云何天及人共诤者，必无利义？云何天及人不诤者，必有利义耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Brāhman! When heaven and humans quarrel and hate each other, at that time heaven and humans are sorrowful, suffering, and distressed. Because of this, heaven and humans have much sorrow and suffering, and much distress. Brāhman! When heaven and humans do not quarrel and hate each other, at that time heaven and humans are joyful and happy. Because of this, heaven and humans have much joy and happiness. Brāhman! In this way, when heaven and humans quarrel together, they definitely have no benefit and meaning. When heaven and humans do not quarrel, they definitely have benefit and meaning."

世尊答曰：「梵志！若时天及人鬪诤怨憎者，尔时，天及人忧苦愁戚，因此故天及人多有忧苦，多怀愁戚。梵志！若时天及人不鬪诤、不怨憎者，尔时，天及人快乐欢喜，因此故天及人多快乐、多欢喜。梵志！如是天及人共诤者，必无利义，天及人不诤者，必有利义。」

The Brāhman Sañjayin then asked, "Gautama! For what reason will heaven and humans definitely not obtain benefit and will definitely obtain suffering? For what reason will heaven and humans definitely obtain benefit and will definitely obtain happiness?"

生闻梵志复问曰：「瞿昙！以何事故，令天及人必不得饶益，必得其苦？以何事故，令天及人必得饶益，必得其乐？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Brāhman! If heaven and humans practice unrighteousness and evil, they will definitely not obtain benefit and will definitely obtain suffering. If heaven and humans can practice righteousness and not do evil, they will definitely obtain benefit and will definitely obtain happiness."

世尊答曰：「梵志！若天及人行于非法及行恶者，必不得益，必得其苦。若天及人能行如法，不行恶者，必得饶益，必得其乐。」

The Brāhman Sañjayin then asked, "Gautama! How do heaven and humans practice unrighteousness and evil, so that they will definitely not obtain benefit and will definitely obtain suffering? How do heaven and humans practice righteousness and not do evil, so that they will definitely obtain benefit and will definitely obtain happiness?"

生闻梵志复问曰：「瞿昙！天及人云何行于非法及行恶者，必不得益，必得其苦？天及人云何行如法，不行恶者，必得饶益，必得其乐？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Brāhman! If heaven and humans practice unrighteousness and evil with the body, speech, and mind, then heaven and humans will definitely decline and the asuras will definitely flourish. Brāhman! If heaven and humans practice righteousness with the body, guard the body, and practice righteousness with speech and mind, guarding speech and mind, then heaven and humans will definitely flourish and the asuras will definitely decline. Brāhman! In this way, heaven and humans who practice unrighteousness and evil will definitely not obtain benefit and will definitely obtain suffering. Brāhman! In this way, heaven and humans who practice righteousness and do not do evil will definitely obtain benefit and will definitely obtain happiness."

世尊答曰：「梵志！天及人身行非法及行恶，口、意行非法及行恶者，尔时，天及人必当减损，阿修罗必当兴盛。梵志！若天及人身行如法，守护其身，口、意行如法，守护口、意者，尔时，天及人必当兴盛，阿修罗必当减损。梵志！如是天及人行于非法及行恶者，必得不益，必得其苦。梵志！如是天及人能行如法，不行恶者，必得饶益，必得其乐。」

The Brāhman Sañjayin then asked, "Gautama! How does one view bad friends?"

生闻梵志复问曰：「瞿昙！云何观恶知识？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Brāhman! One should view bad friends as the moon."

世尊答曰：「梵志！当观恶知识犹如月也。」

The Brāhman Sañjayin then asked, "Gautama! How should one view bad friends as the moon?"

生闻梵志复问曰：「瞿昙！云何当观恶知识犹如月耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Brāhman! Just as the waning moon gradually diminishes in size, its palace also diminishes, its radiance also diminishes, and its form also diminishes, day by day it disappears. Brāhman! Sometimes the moon completely disappears and is no longer seen. Brāhman! A person with bad friends may gain faith in the Tathāgata's true Dharma and Vinaya. Having gained faith, they later become unfilial, disrespectful, their conduct is not in accordance, they do not establish right knowledge, and they do not proceed in accordance with the Dharma. They then lose their faith, and also lose their precepts, learning, diligence, and wisdom. Brāhman! Sometimes this bad friend destroys good dharmas, just like the waning moon. Brāhman! One should view bad friends in this way as the moon."

世尊答曰：「梵志！如向尽月，日日稍减，宫殿亦减，光明亦减，形色亦减，日日尽去。梵志！有时月乃至于尽，都不复见。梵志！恶知识人于如来正法、律亦得其信，彼得信已，则于后时而不孝顺，亦不恭敬，所行不顺，不立正智，不趣向法次法，彼便失信，持戒、博闻、庶几、智慧亦复失之。梵志！有时此恶知识教灭善法，犹如月尽。梵志！如是当观恶知识犹如月也。」

The Brāhman Sañjayin then asked, "Gautama! How should one view good friends?"

生闻梵志复问曰：「瞿昙！云何观善知识？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Brāhman! One should view good friends as the moon."

世尊答曰：「梵志！当观善知识犹如月也。」

The Brāhman Sañjayin then asked, "Gautama! How should one view good friends as the moon?"

生闻梵志复问曰：「瞿昙！云何当观善知识犹如月耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Brāhman! It is like the new moon, youthful, bright, and pure, growing day by day. Brāhman! Sometimes on the fifteenth day, the moon is full. Brāhman! In this way, a good friend gains faith in the Tathāgata's true Dharma and Vinaya. Having gained faith, they later become filial and respectful, their practice accords with the truth, they establish right knowledge, and proceed according to the Dharma. Their faith grows, and so do their precepts, learning, diligence, and wisdom. Brāhman! Sometimes that good friend is fully endowed with good qualities, like the full moon on the fifteenth day. Brāhman! In this way, one should view good friends as the moon."

世尊答曰：「梵志！犹如月初生，少壮明净，日日增长。梵志！或时月十五日，其殿丰满。梵志！如是善知识于如来正法、律得信，彼得信已，而于后时孝顺恭敬，所行随顺，立于正智，趣向法次法，彼增长信，持戒、博闻、庶几、智慧亦复增长。梵志！有时彼善知识善法具足，如十五日月。梵志！如是当观善知识犹如月也。」

Then the World-Honored One spoke this verse:

于是，世尊说此颂曰：

Just as the moon is spotless, roaming in the realm
of space, outshining the light of all the stars in
the world, in the same way, faith, learning, diligence, and
generosity outshine all the stinginess in the world. Just as
a great dragon rises up with clouds, thunder, and lightning
and the rain falls in torrents, filling all the ground
in the same way, faith, learning, diligence, and generosity, with
abundant food and drink, encourage and increase giving. Just as
there is great thunder and lightning, and the timely rain
falls from the sky, the rain of merit is vast
rained down by the giver. With much wealth and fame
one is born in a good place, and will receive
merit, reborn in heaven after death.

「譬如月无垢，　　游于虚空界，一切世星宿，　　悉翳其光明。如是信博闻，　　庶几无悭贪，世间一切悭，　　悉翳施光明。犹如有大龙，　　兴起云雷电，雨下极滂沛，　　充满一切地。如是信博闻，　　庶几无悭贪，施饮食丰足，　　乐劝增广施。如是极雷震，　　如天降时雨，彼福雨广大，　　施主之所雨。钱财多名誉，　　得生于善处，彼当受于福，　　死已生天上。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The Brahmacarin Sudatta, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。生闻梵志闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
