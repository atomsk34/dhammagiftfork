MĀ 15

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 2 业相应品

15. Intention （一五）思经

Parallel to: AN 10.217–219 / EĀ 48.1 (~)

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus: "If one intentionally commits an action, I say that one will definitely experience its retribution, either in this life or the next. If one does not intentionally commit an action, I say that one may not necessarily experience its retribution. Among them, the three unwholesome bodily actions and the three unwholesome mental actions, which produce suffering as their fruit, will experience the retribution of suffering.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「若有故作业，我说彼必受其报，或现世受，或后世受；若不故作业，我说此不必受报。于中，身故作三业，不善与苦果受于苦报；口有四业，意有三业，不善与苦果受于苦报。

What are the three unwholesome bodily actions that produce suffering as their fruit and experience the retribution of suffering? First, killing living beings, extremely evil and bloodthirsty, wishing to harm and not compassionate toward sentient beings, even insects. Second, taking what is not given, taking others' property with the intention to steal. Third, sexual misconduct, such as having sexual relations with a woman who is protected by her father, mother, parents, sisters, brothers, husband's parents, relatives, or clan, or who is married, or who is subject to punishment and fear, or who is a nominal wife, a hired wife, or a wife by flower garland. These are called the three unwholesome bodily actions that produce suffering as their fruit and experience the retribution of suffering.

「云何身故作三业，不善与苦果受于苦报？一曰杀生，极恶饮血，其欲伤害，不慈众生，乃至蜫虫。二曰不与取，著他财物以偷意取。三曰邪婬，彼或有父所护，或母所护，或父母所护，或姉妹所护，或兄弟所护，或妇父母所护，或亲亲所护，或同姓所护，或为他妇女，有鞭罚恐怖，及有名假赁至华鬘，亲犯如此女。是谓身故作三业，不善与苦果受于苦报。

What are the four unwholesome verbal actions that produce suffering as their fruit and experience the retribution of suffering? First, false speech, such as when in an assembly, among relatives, or in the king's court, if asked about something one knows, one says one knows, and if asked about something one does not know, one says one does not know, or if asked about something one has seen, one says one has seen, and if asked about something one has not seen, one says one has not seen, for oneself, for others, or for wealth, one speaks falsely. Second, divisive speech, wishing to separate others, hearing this from someone, one wishes to break this apart, hearing that from someone, one wishes to break that apart, those who are united one wishes to separate, those who are separated one wishes to separate further, forming factions, delighting in factions, praising factions. Third, harsh speech, if one speaks words that are harsh, coarse, evil-sounding, unpleasant to the ear, disliked by many, unloved by many, causing others suffering, preventing them from attaining concentration, speaking such words. Fourth, meaningless speech, speaking at the wrong time, speaking untruthfully, speaking without meaning, speaking unlawfully, speaking without cessation, and also praising things that do not cease, going against the time and not teaching well, nor rebuking well. These are called the four unwholesome verbal actions that produce suffering as their fruit and experience the retribution of suffering.

「云何口故作四业，不善与苦果受于苦报？一曰妄言，彼或在众，或在眷属，或在王家，若呼彼问，汝知便说，彼不知言知，知言不知，不见言见，见言不见，为己为他，或为财物，知已妄言。二曰两舌，欲离别他，闻此语彼，欲破坏此，闻彼语此，欲破坏彼，合者欲离，离者复离，而作群党，乐于群党，称说群党。三曰麤言，彼若有言，辞气麤犷，恶声逆耳，众所不喜，众所不爱，使他苦恼，令不得定，说如是言。四曰绮语，彼非时说，不真实说，无义说，非法说，不止息说，又复称叹不止息事，违背于时而不善教，亦不善诃。是谓口故作四业，不善与苦果受于苦报。

What are the three unwholesome mental actions that produce suffering as their fruit and experience the retribution of suffering? First, covetousness, seeing others' wealth and various necessities, always coveting and hoping to obtain them for oneself. Second, ill-will, harboring hatred and jealousy, thinking: 'Those sentient beings should be killed, bound, imprisoned, exiled, or expelled,' wishing for them to suffer immeasurable pain. Third, wrong views, holding inverted views and saying: 'There is no giving, no observance, no incantations, no good or bad karma, no retribution for good or bad karma, no this world or the next world, no father or mother, no true person in the world who goes to a good place, goes well, or goes in a good direction, in this world or the next world, who knows for oneself, awakens for oneself, and attains for oneself.' These are called the three unwholesome mental actions that produce suffering as their fruit and experience the retribution of suffering.

「云何意故作三业，不善与苦果受于苦报？一] cf.［丽】曰贪伺，见他财物诸生活具，常伺求望，欲令我得。二曰嫉恚，意怀憎嫉而作是念：『彼众生者，应杀、应缚、应收、应免、应逐摈出。』其欲令彼受无量苦。三曰邪见，所见颠倒，如是见、如是说：无施、无斋、无有呪说，无善恶业，无善恶业报，无此世彼世，无父无母，世无真人往至善处、善去、善向，此世彼世，自知、自觉、自作证成就游。是谓意故作三业，不善与苦果受于苦报。

"The well-versed noble disciple abandons unwholesome bodily actions and cultivates wholesome bodily actions, abandons unwholesome verbal and mental actions and cultivates wholesome verbal and mental actions. The well-versed noble disciple, thus endowed with diligence and virtue, accomplishes pure bodily actions, accomplishes pure verbal and mental actions, is free from anger and disputes, removes sleepiness, is free from arrogance, cuts off doubts, crosses over conceit, has right mindfulness and right knowledge, is free from delusion, their mind is filled with loving-kindness, pervading one direction, accomplishing and dwelling. Thus, in the second, third, and fourth directions, in the four intermediate directions, above and below, everywhere, their mind is filled with loving-kindness, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without disputes, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well cultivated, pervading the entire world, accomplishing and dwelling. They think: 'Previously, my mind was not well cultivated in this way, but now my mind is well cultivated in this way.' The well-versed noble disciple, thus well cultivating their mind in this way, if they previously followed bad friends and engaged in heedless conduct, creating unwholesome actions, they cannot be taken away, cannot be defiled, and will not follow them again. If there are young boys and girls who are born and immediately able to practice the liberation of loving-kindness, would they later create unwholesome actions with their body, speech, and mind?"

「多闻圣弟子舍身不善业，修身善业，舍口、意不善业，修口、意善业。彼多闻圣弟子如是具足精进戒德，成就身净业，成就口、意净业，离恚离诤，除去睡眠，无调贡高，断疑、度慢，正念正智，无有愚痴，彼心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游，如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。彼作是念：『我本此心少不善修，我今此心无量善修。』多闻圣弟子其心如是无量善修，若本因恶知识，为放逸行，作不善业，彼不能将去，不能秽污，不复相随。若有幼少童男、童女，生便能行慈心解脱者，而于后时，彼身、口、意宁可复作不善业耶？」

"The monk replied: "No, World-Honored One! Why is that? Since one does not create unwholesome actions oneself, how can unwholesome actions arise?

比丘答曰：「不也。世尊！所以者何？自不作恶业，恶业何由生？」

"Therefore, whether lay or ordained, one should always diligently cultivate the liberation of loving-kindness. If lay or ordained people cultivate the liberation of loving-kindness, they will not take this body to the next world, but will go according to the mind. The monk should think: 'Previously, I was heedless and created unwholesome actions. All of this can be experienced now, and will not be experienced in the next life.' If one cultivates the liberation of loving-kindness, compassion, joy, and equanimity in this way, one will definitely attain the state of non-returner, or even higher. Thus, with equanimity, free from knots, resentment, anger, and disputes, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well cultivated, pervading the entire world, accomplishing and dwelling, one thinks: 'Previously, my mind was not well cultivated in this way, but now my mind is well cultivated in this way.' The well-versed noble disciple, thus well cultivating their mind in this way, if they previously followed bad friends and engaged in heedless conduct, creating unwholesome actions, they cannot be taken away, cannot be defiled, and will not follow them again. If there are young boys and girls who are born and immediately able to practice the liberation of equanimity, would they later create unwholesome actions with their body, speech, and mind?"

「是以男女在家、出家，常当勤修慈心解脱，若彼男女在家、出家修慈心解脱者，不持此身往至彼世，但随心去此。比丘应作是念：『我本放逸，作不善业，是一切今可受报，终不后世。』若有如是行慈心解脱无量善修] cf.［丽】者，必得阿那含，或复上得。如是，悲、喜、心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游，彼作是念：『我本此心少不善修，我今此心无量善修。』多闻圣弟子其心如是无量善修，若本因恶知识，为放逸行，作不善业，彼不能将去，不能秽污，不复相随。若有幼少童男、童女，生便能行舍心解脱者，而于后时，彼身、口、意宁可复作不善业耶？」

"The monk replied: "No, World-Honored One! Why is that? Since one does not create unwholesome actions oneself, how can unwholesome actions arise?

比丘答曰：「不也。世尊！所以者何？自不作恶业，恶业何由生？」

"Therefore, whether lay or ordained, one should always diligently cultivate the liberation of equanimity. If lay or ordained people cultivate the liberation of equanimity, they will not take this body to the next world, but will go according to the mind. The monk should think: 'Previously, I was heedless and created unwholesome actions. All of this can be experienced now, and will not be experienced in the next life.' If one cultivates the liberation of equanimity in this way, one will definitely attain the state of non-returner, or even higher."

「是以男女在家、出家，常当勤修舍心解脱。若彼男女在家、出家修舍心解脱者，不持此身往至彼世，但随心去此。比丘应作是念：『我本放逸，作不善业，是一切今可受报，终不后世。』若有如是行舍心解脱无量善修者，必得阿那含，或复上得。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
