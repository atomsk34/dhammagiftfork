MĀ 151

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 12 梵志品

151. （一五一）中阿含梵志阿摄惒经第十（第三念诵）

Parallel to: MN 93 / EĀ 40.9 (~)

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, many brāhmaṇas gathered in a schoolhouse in Kośala to discuss this matter: "The brāhmaṇa caste is superior, the others are inferior; the brāhmaṇa caste is white, the others are all black; the brāhmaṇas attain purity, non-brāhmaṇas do not attain purity; the brāhmaṇas are the sons of Brahmā, born from his mouth, transformed by Brahmā. But the śramaṇa Gautama says that all four castes are pure, and teaches this." They thought, "Who among us has the ability to go to the śramaṇa Gautama and ask him about this matter according to the Dharma?" They further thought, "The brāhmaṇa student Ascetic is raised by his parents, born pure, and his parents' lineage is unbroken for seven generations. He is learned, has a good memory, recites the four Vedas, and deeply understands the five kinds of statements: cause, condition, right, text, and play. The brāhmaṇa student Ascetic has the ability to go to the śramaṇa Gautama and ask him about this matter according to the Dharma." "Let us all go to the brāhmaṇa student Ascetic and tell him about this. We will accept whatever the brāhmaṇa student Ascetic says.

尔时，众多梵志于拘萨罗，集在学堂，共论此事：「梵志种胜，余者不如；梵志种白，余者皆黑；梵志得清净，非梵志不得清净；梵志梵天子，从彼口生，梵梵所化，而沙门瞿昙说四种姓皆悉清净，施设显示。」彼作是念：「诸贤！为谁有力能至沙门瞿昙所，则以此事如法难诘？」彼复作是念：「阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳为父母所举，受生清净，乃至七世父母不绝种族，生生无恶，博闻总持，诵过四典经，深达因、缘、正、文、戏五句说。阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳有力能至沙门瞿昙所，则以此事如法难诘。」「诸贤！可共诣阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳所，向说此事，随阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳所说，我等当受。」

Then the many brāhmaṇas of Kośala went to the brāhmaṇa student Ascetic and exchanged greetings with him. They sat to one side and said, "Student, we many brāhmaṇas gathered in a schoolhouse in Kośala to discuss this matter: 'The brāhmaṇa caste is superior, the others are inferior; the brāhmaṇa caste is white, the others are all black; the brāhmaṇas attain purity, non-brāhmaṇas do not attain purity; the brāhmaṇas are the sons of Brahmā, born from his mouth, transformed by Brahmā. But the śramaṇa Gautama says that all four castes are pure, and teaches this.' We thought, 'Who among us has the ability to go to the śramaṇa Gautama and ask him about this matter according to the Dharma?' We further thought, 'The brāhmaṇa student Ascetic is raised by his parents, born pure, and his parents' lineage is unbroken for seven generations. He is learned, has a good memory, recites the four Vedas, and deeply understands the five kinds of statements: cause, condition, right, text, and play. The brāhmaṇa student Ascetic has the ability to go to the śramaṇa Gautama and ask him about this matter according to the Dharma.' We wish that the brāhmaṇa student Ascetic would go to the śramaṇa Gautama and ask him about this matter according to the Dharma."

于是，拘萨罗众多梵志即诣阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳所，共相问讯，却坐一面，语曰：「摩纳！我等众多梵志于拘萨罗，集在学堂，共论此事：『梵志种胜，余者不如；梵志种白，余者皆黑；梵志得清净，非梵志不得清净；梵志梵天子，从彼口生，梵梵所化，而沙门瞿昙说四种姓皆悉清净，施设显示。』我等作是念：『诸贤！为谁有力能至沙门瞿昙所，则以此事如法难诘？』我等复作是念：『阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳为父母所举，受生清净，乃至七世父母不绝种族，生生无恶博闻总持，诵过四典经，深达因、缘、正、文、戏五句说。阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳有力能至沙门瞿昙所，则以此事如法难诘。』愿阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳往诣沙门瞿昙所，则以此事如法难诘。」

The brāhmaṇa student Ascetic said to the brāhmaṇas, "The śramaṇa Gautama teaches the Dharma according to the Dharma. If one teaches the Dharma according to the Dharma, one cannot be questioned.

阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳语诸梵志曰：「诸贤！沙门瞿昙如法说法，若如法说法者，不可难诘也。」

The many brāhmaṇas of Kośala said, "Student, you have not yet been defeated, so do not be so quick to surrender. Why is that? The brāhmaṇa student Ascetic is raised by his parents, born pure, and his parents' lineage is unbroken for seven generations. He is learned, has a good memory, recites the four Vedas, and deeply understands the five kinds of statements: cause, condition, right, text, and play. The brāhmaṇa student Ascetic has the ability to go to the śramaṇa Gautama and ask him about this matter according to the Dharma. We wish that the brāhmaṇa student Ascetic would go to the śramaṇa Gautama and ask him about this matter according to the Dharma." The brāhmaṇa student Ascetic silently accepted the request from the many brāhmaṇas of Kośala.

拘萨罗众多梵志语曰：「摩纳！汝未有屈事，未可豫自伏。所以者何？阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳为父母所举，受生清净，乃至七世父母不绝种族，生生无恶，博闻总持，诵过四典经，深达因、缘、正、文、戏五句说。阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳有力能至沙门瞿昙所，则以此事如法难诘。愿阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳往诣沙门瞿昙所，则以此事如法难诘。」阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳为拘萨罗众多梵志默然而受。

Then the brāhmaṇa student Ascetic went with the many brāhmaṇas of Kośala to where the Buddha was. They exchanged greetings and sat to one side. He said, "Gautama, I wish to ask something. May I ask?"

于是，阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳与彼拘萨罗众多梵志往诣佛所，共相问讯，却坐一面，白曰：「瞿昙！欲有所问，听我问耶？」

The World-Honored One said, "Student, ask as you wish."

世尊告曰：「摩纳！恣汝所问。」

Ascetic then asked, "Gautama, the brāhmaṇas say, 'The brāhmaṇa caste is superior, the others are inferior; the brāhmaṇa caste is white, the others are black; the brāhmaṇas attain purity, non-brāhmaṇas do not; the brāhmaṇas are the sons of Brahmā, born from his mouth, created by Brahmā.' I do not know what the śramaṇa Gautama would say about this."

阿摄惒逻延多那便问曰：「瞿昙！诸梵志等作如是说：『梵志种胜，余者不如；梵志种白，余者皆黑；梵志得清净，非梵志不得清净；梵志梵天子，从彼口生，梵梵所化。』未知沙门瞿昙当云何说？」

The World-Honored One said, "I will ask you, answer as you know. Student, have you heard that in other countries such as Rājagṛha and Vaiśālī there are two kinds of families, the noble and the servants, where the noble become servants and the servants become noble?"

世尊告曰：「我今问汝，随所解答。摩纳！颇闻余尼及劒浮国有二种姓，大家及奴，大家作奴，奴作大家耶？」

The student Ascetic replied, "Gautama, I have heard that in other countries such as Rājagṛha and Vaiśālī there are two kinds of families, the noble and the servants, where the noble become servants and the servants become noble."

阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳答曰：「瞿昙！我闻余尼及劒浮国有二种姓，大家及奴，大家作奴，奴作大家也。」

"So it is, student. If a brahmin goes in the right direction, he will attain good understanding and know the Dharma for himself. If a kṣatriya, householder, or worker goes in the right direction, they will also attain good understanding and know the Dharma for themselves."

「如是。摩纳！梵志若正趣者，彼得善解，自知如法；刹利、居士、工师若正趣者，亦得善解，自知如法。」

The student Ascetic said, "Gautama, it is wonderful and marvelous how you have spoken this parable. But the brahmins say, 'The brahmin caste is superior, the others are inferior; the brahmin caste is white, the others are black; the brahmins attain purity, the non-brahmins do not; the brahmins are the sons of Brahmā, born from his mouth, created by Brahmā.'"

阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳白曰：「瞿昙！甚奇！甚特！快说此喻。但诸梵志作如是说：『梵志种胜，余者不如；梵志种白，余者皆黑；梵志得清净，非梵志不得清净；梵志梵天子，从彼口生，梵梵所化。』」

The World-Honored One asked, "What do you think, student? Can only brahmins be unattached and unfettered in this space, untouchable and unobstructed? Not kṣatriyas, householders, or workers?"

世尊问曰：「摩纳！于意云何？颇独有梵志于此虚空不著不缚，不触不碍；刹利、居士、工师为不然耶？」

The student Ascetic replied, "Gautama, brahmins are unattached and unfettered in this space, untouchable and unobstructed; so are kṣatriyas, householders, and workers."

阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳答曰：「瞿昙！梵志于此虚空不著不缚，不触不碍；刹利、居士、工师亦然。」

"So it is, student. If brahmins go in the right direction, they will attain good understanding and know the Dharma for themselves; if kṣatriyas, householders, and workers go in the right direction, they will also attain good understanding and know the Dharma for themselves."

「如是，摩纳！梵志若正趣者，彼得善解，自知如法；刹利、居士、工师若正趣者，亦得善解，自知如法。」

The student Ascetic said, "Gautama, it is wonderful, it is marvelous! You have spoken this parable well. But the brahmins say, 'The brahmin caste is superior, the others are inferior; the brahmin caste is white, the others are black; the brahmins attain purity, non-brahmins do not attain purity; the brahmins are the sons of Brahma, born from his mouth, created by Brahma.'"

阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳白曰：「瞿昙！甚奇！甚特！快说此喻。但诸梵志作如是说：『梵志种胜，余者不如；梵志种白，余者皆黑；梵志得清净，非梵志不得清净；梵志梵天子，从彼口生，梵梵所化。』」

The World-Honored One asked, "What do you think, student? Can only brahmins practice loving-kindness, without attachment, without resentment, without anger, without conflict; not so for kṣatriyas, householders, and workers?"

世尊问曰：「摩纳！于意云何？颇独有梵志能行慈心，无结无怨，无恚无诤；刹利、居士、工师不然耶？」

The student Ascetic replied, "Gautama, brahmins can practice loving-kindness, without attachment, without resentment, without anger, without conflict; so can kṣatriyas, householders, and workers."

阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳答曰：「瞿昙！梵志能行慈心，无结无怨，无恚无诤；刹利、居士、工师亦然。」

"So it is, student. If a brahmin goes in the right direction, he will attain good understanding and know the Dharma for himself; if a kṣatriya, householder, or worker goes in the right direction, they will also attain good understanding and know the Dharma for themselves."

「如是。摩纳！梵志若正趣者，彼得善解，自知如法；刹利、居士、工师若正趣者，亦得善解，自知如法。」

The student Ascetic said, "Gautama, it is wonderful, it is marvelous! You have spoken this parable well. But the brahmins say, 'The brahmin caste is superior, the others are inferior; the brahmin caste is white, the others are black; the brahmins attain purity, non-brahmins do not; the brahmins are born from the mouth of Brahma, created by Brahma.'"

阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳白曰：「瞿昙！甚奇！甚特！快说此喻。但诸梵志作如是说：『梵志种胜，余者不如；梵志种白，余者皆黑；梵志得清净，非梵志不得清净；梵志梵天子，从彼口生，梵梵所化。』」

The World-Honored One asked, "What do you think, student? If a hundred people come, and one of them says, 'Come together! Only those born in the kṣatriya or brahmin castes can take soap to the water and bathe, removing dirt and becoming extremely pure.' What do you think, student? Can only those in the kṣatriya or brahmin castes take soap to the water and bathe, removing dirt and becoming extremely pure? Can only those in the householder or worker castes not take soap to the water and bathe, removing dirt and becoming extremely pure? Can all one hundred people take soap to the water and bathe, removing dirt and becoming extremely pure?"

世尊问曰：「摩纳！于意云何？若百种人来，或有一人而语彼曰：『汝等共来！若生刹利族梵志族者，唯彼能持澡豆至水洗浴，去垢极净。』摩纳！于意云何？为刹利族、梵志族者，彼能持澡豆至水洗浴，去垢极净耶？为居士族、工师族者，彼不能持澡豆至水洗浴，去垢极净耶？为一切百种人皆能持澡豆至水洗浴，去垢极净耶？」

The student Ascetic replied, "Gautama, all one hundred people can take soap to the water and bathe, removing dirt and becoming extremely pure."

阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳答曰：「瞿昙！彼一切百种人皆能持澡豆至水洗浴，去垢极净。」

"So it is, student. If a brahmin goes in the right direction, he will attain good understanding and know the Dharma for himself. If a kṣatriya, householder, or worker goes in the right direction, he will also attain good understanding and know the Dharma for himself."

「如是。摩纳！梵志若正趣者，彼得善解，自知如法；刹利、居士、工师若正趣者，亦得善解，自知如法。」

The student Ascetic said, "Gautama, it is wonderful, it is marvelous! You have spoken this parable well. But the brahmins say, 'The brahmin caste is superior, the others are inferior; the brahmin caste is white, the others are black; the brahmins attain purity, non-brahmins do not attain purity; the brahmins are the sons of Brahmā, born from his mouth, created by Brahmā.'"

阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳白曰：「瞿昙！甚奇！甚特！快说此喻。但诸梵志作如是说：『梵志种胜，余者不如；梵志种白，余者皆黑；梵志得清净，非梵志不得清净；梵志梵天子，从彼口生，梵梵所化。』」

The World-Honored One asked, "What do you think, student? If a hundred people come, and one of them says, 'Come together! Only those born in the kṣatriya or brahmin castes can use very dry sāla and sandalwood wood as kindling, and by rubbing them together, produce fire and nurture it.' What do you think, student? Can only those in the kṣatriya and brahmin castes use very dry sāla and sandalwood wood as kindling, and by rubbing them together, produce fire and nurture it? Or can those in the householder and worker castes use dry pig and dog troughs, and wood from the ilandana tree and other inferior wood as kindling, and by rubbing them together, produce fire and nurture it? Or can all a hundred kinds of people use various kinds of wood as kindling, and by rubbing them together, produce fire and nurture it?"

世尊问曰：「摩纳！于意云何？若百种人来，或有一人而语彼曰：『汝等共来！若生刹利族、梵志族者，唯彼能以极燥娑罗及栴檀木用作火母，以钻钻之，生火长养。』摩纳！于意云何？为刹利族、梵志族者，彼能以极燥娑罗及栴檀木用作火母，以钻钻之，生火长养耶？为居士族、工师族者，彼当以燥猪狗槽及伊兰檀木及余弊木用作火母，以钻钻之，生火长养耶？为一切百种人皆能以若干种木用作火母，以钻钻之，生火长养耶？」

The student Ascetic replied, "Gautama, all a hundred kinds of people can use various kinds of wood as kindling, and by rubbing them together, produce fire and nurture it."

阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳答曰：「瞿昙！彼一切百种人皆能以若干种木用作火母，以钻钻之，生火长养。」

"So it is, student. If a brahmin is on the right path, he will attain good understanding and know the Dharma for himself. If a kṣatriya, householder, or worker is on the right path, he will also attain good understanding and know the Dharma for himself."

「如是。摩纳！梵志若正趣者，彼得善解，自知如法；刹利、居士、工师若正趣者，亦得善解，自知如法。」

The student Ascetic said, "Gautama, it is wonderful, it is marvelous! You have spoken this parable well. But the brahmins say, 'The brahmin caste is superior, the others are inferior; the brahmin caste is white, the others are black; the brahmins attain purity, non-brahmins do not; the brahmins are born from the mouth of Brahma, created by Brahma.'"

阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳白曰：「瞿昙！甚奇！甚特！快说此喻。但诸梵志作如是说：『梵志种胜，余者不如；梵志种白，余者皆黑；梵志得清净，非梵志不得清净；梵志梵天子，从彼口生，梵梵所化。』」

The World-Honored One asked, "What do you think, student? If those hundred people all use various kinds of wood to make fire-kindling and rub them to produce fire, and nurture it, will all those fires have smoke, color, heat, and light, and be able to perform the functions of fire? Is it that only that fire has smoke, color, heat, and light, and can perform the functions of fire? Or is it that only that fire has no smoke, color, heat, and light, and cannot perform the functions of fire? Or is it that all those fires have smoke, color, heat, and light, and can perform the functions of fire?"

世尊问曰：「摩纳！于意云何？若彼百种人皆以若干种木用作火母，以钻钻之，生火长养。彼一切火皆有［火＊佥］、有色、有热、有光，皆能作火事。为彼火独有［火＊佥］、有色、有热、有光，能作火事耶？为彼火独无［火＊佥］、无色、无热、无光，不能作火事耶？为彼一切火皆有［火＊佥］、有色、有热、有光，皆能作火事耶？」

The student Ascetic said, "Gautama, if those hundred people all use various kinds of wood to make fire-kindling and rub them to produce fire, and nurture it, will all those fires have smoke, color, heat, and light, and be able to perform the functions of fire? If it is that only that fire has smoke, color, heat, and light, and can perform the functions of fire, it is impossible. If it is that only that fire has no smoke, color, heat, and light, and cannot perform the functions of fire, it is also impossible. Gautama, but it is that all those fires have smoke, color, heat, and light, and can perform the functions of fire."

阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳白曰：「瞿昙！若彼百种人皆以若干种木用作火母，以钻钻之，生火长养者。彼一切火皆有［火＊佥］、有色、有热、有光，皆能作火事。若彼火独有［火＊佥］、有色、有热、有光，能为火事者，终无是处。若彼火独无［火＊佥］、无色、无热、无光，不能为火事者，亦无是处。瞿昙！但彼一切火皆有［火＊佥］、有色、有热、有光，皆能作火事。」

"So it is, student. If a brahmin goes in the right direction, he will attain good understanding and know the Dharma for himself. If a kshatriya, householder, or worker goes in the right direction, he will also attain good understanding and know the Dharma for himself."

「如是。摩纳！梵志若正趣者，彼得善解，自知如法；刹利、居士、工师若正趣者，亦得善解，自知如法。」

The student Ascetic said, "Gautama, it is wonderful, it is marvelous! You have spoken this parable well. But the brahmins say, 'The brahmin caste is superior, the others are inferior; the brahmin caste is white, the others are black; the brahmins attain purity, the non-brahmins do not attain purity; the brahmins are the sons of Brahma, born from his mouth, created by Brahma.'"

阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳白曰：「瞿昙！甚奇！甚特！快说此喻。但诸梵志作如是说：『梵志种胜，余者不如；梵志种白，余者皆黑；梵志得清净，非梵志不得清净；梵志梵天子，从彼口生，梵梵所化。』」

The World-Honored One said, "Student, if this body follows where it is born, it is counted as that. If born in the brahmin caste, it is counted as the brahmin caste; if born in the kshatriya, householder, or worker caste, it is counted as the worker caste. Student, it is like fire, which follows where it is born. If born from wood, it is counted as wood fire; if born from grass, dung, or firewood, it is counted as firewood fire. So it is, student. This body follows where it is born. If born in the brahmin caste, it is counted as the brahmin caste; if born in the kshatriya, householder, or worker caste, it is counted as the worker caste."

世尊告曰：「摩纳！若此身随所生者，即彼之数。若生梵志族者，即梵志族数；若生刹利、居士、工师族者，即工师族数。摩纳！犹若如火，随所生者，即彼之数；若因木生者，即木火数；若因草粪薪生者，即薪火数。如是，摩纳！此身随所生者，即彼之数。若生梵志族者，即梵志族数；若生刹利，居士、工师族者，即工师族数。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Student, what do you think? If a kshatriya woman and a brahmin man were to unite, and from that union a child was born, resembling either the father or the mother or neither, how would you describe it? Would it be a kshatriya or a brahmin?"

世尊问曰：「摩纳！于意云何？若刹利女与梵志男共合会者，彼因合会，后便生子，或似父，或似母，或不似父母，汝云何说？彼为刹利，为梵志耶？」

The student Asheghala-yanta-na-ma-na said, "Gautama, if a kshatriya woman and a brahmin man were to unite, and from that union a child was born, resembling either the father or the mother or neither, I would not say it is a kshatriya or a brahmin. Gautama, I would only say it is another person."

阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳答曰：「瞿昙！刹利女与梵志男共合会者，彼因合会，后便生子，或似父，或似母，或不似父母，我不说彼刹利，亦不说梵志。瞿昙！我但说彼他身。」

"So it is, student. This body belongs to the class it is born into. If born into the brahmin class, it is counted as brahmin; if born into the kshatriya, householder, or artisan class, it is counted as artisan."

「如是。摩纳！此身随所生者，即彼之数。若生梵志族者，即梵志族数；若生刹利、居士、工师族者，即工师族数。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Student, if a brahmin woman and a kshatriya man were to unite, and from that union a child was born, resembling either the father or the mother or neither, how would you describe it? Is it a brahmin or a kshatriya?"

世尊问曰：「摩纳！若梵志女与刹利男共合会者，彼因合会，后便生子，或似父，或似母，或不似父母，汝云何说？彼为梵志，为刹利耶？」

The student Asajjhaya Asajjhaya-putta replied, "Gautama, if a brahmin woman and a kshatriya man were to unite, and from that union a child was born, resembling either the father or the mother or neither, I would not say it is a brahmin or a kshatriya. Gautama, I would only say it is of another class."

阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳答曰：「瞿昙！梵志女与刹利男共合会者，彼因合会，后便生子，或似父，或似母，或不似父母，我不说彼梵志，亦不说刹利。瞿昙！我但说彼他身。」

"So it is, student. This body belongs to the class it is born into. If born into the brahmin class, it is counted as brahmin; if born into the kshatriya, householder, or artisan class, it is counted as artisan."

「如是，摩纳！此身随所生者，即彼之数。若生梵志族者，即梵志族数；若生刹利、居士、工师族者，即工师族数。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Student, what do you think? If someone has many grass horses and lets a male donkey mate with them, and from that union a foal is born, how would you describe it? Is it a donkey or a horse?"

世尊问曰：「摩纳！于意云何？若人有众多草马，放一父驴，于中一草马与父驴共合会，彼因合会，后便生驹，汝云何说？彼为驴，为马耶？」

The student Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya Asajjhaya As

阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳答曰：「瞿昙！若有马与驴共合会，彼因合会，后便生驹，我不说彼驴，亦不说马，瞿昙！我但说彼骡也。」

"So it is, student. If this body is born into a certain class, it is counted as that class. If born into the Brahmin class, it is counted as the Brahmin class; if born into the Kshatriya, Vaishya, or Shudra class, it is counted as the Shudra class."

「如是，摩纳！若此身随所生者，即彼之数。若生梵志族者，即梵志族数；若生刹利、居士、工师族者，即工师族数。」

The World-Honored One said, "Monks, in the past, there were many ascetics living together in a high place, and they gave rise to this evil view: 'The Brahmin caste is superior, the others are inferior; the Brahmin caste is white, the others are black; the Brahmins attain purity, non-Brahmins do not attain purity; the Brahmins are the sons of Brahma, created by Brahma.' Then the ascetic Asita Devala heard that the many ascetics living together in a high place had given rise to this evil view, and he put on a monastic robe, wrapped his head with a monastic cloth, carried a staff and umbrella, dressed in white clothes, and entered the dwelling place of the ascetics through a quiet room for walking meditation.

世尊告曰：「摩纳！乃往昔时有众多仙人共住无事高处，生如是恶见：『梵志种胜，余者不如；梵志种白，余者皆黑；梵志得清净，非梵志不得清净；梵志梵天子，从彼口生，梵梵所化。』于是，阿私罗仙人提鞞逻闻众多仙人共住无事高处，生如是恶见已，著袈裟衣，以袈裟巾裹头，拄杖持繖，著白衣变，不从门入，至仙人住处静室经行。

Then one of the ascetics living together in the high place saw Asita Devala wearing a monastic robe, wrapping his head with a monastic cloth, carrying a staff and umbrella, dressed in white clothes, and entering the dwelling place of the ascetics through a quiet room for walking meditation. Having seen this, he went to the many ascetics living together in the high place and said, 'Venerable ones! Now there is a person wearing a monastic robe, wrapping his head with a monastic cloth, carrying a staff and umbrella, dressed in white clothes, and entering the dwelling place of the ascetics through a quiet room for walking meditation. Should we all go together and curse him, saying, "You will become ashes, you will become ashes!"'

「于是，共住无事高处有一仙人，见阿私罗仙人提鞞逻著袈裟衣，以袈裟巾裹头，拄杖持繖，著白衣变，不从门入，至仙人住处静室经行。见已，往诣共住无事高处众多仙人所，便作是语：『诸贤！今有一人著袈裟衣，以袈裟巾裹头，拄杖持繖，著白衣变，不从门入，至仙人住处静室经行，我等宁可共往呪之：「汝作灰汝作灰耶？」』

Then the many ascetics living together in the high place went to Asita Devala and together cursed him, saying, 'You will become ashes, you will become ashes.' According to the method of their curse, they cursed him, saying, 'You will become ashes, you will become ashes.' As they did so, his radiant complexion became even more excellent and his body became radiant. The many ascetics then thought, 'When we originally cursed him, saying, "You will become ashes, you will become ashes," he did become ashes. Now we curse this person, saying, "You will become ashes, you will become ashes." According to the method of our curse, we curse this person, and his radiant complexion becomes even more excellent and his body becomes radiant. We should ask him.' They then asked him, 'Who are you?' Asita Devala replied, 'Venerable ones! Have you heard of the ascetic Asita Devala?' They replied, 'We have heard of the ascetic Asita Devala.' He said again, 'I am he.' The many ascetics then apologized to Asita Devala, saying, 'Please forgive us. We did not know that you were the ascetic Asita Devala.

「于是，共住无事高处众多仙人即往诣彼阿私罗仙人提鞞逻所，到已，共呪：『汝作灰，汝作灰。』如其呪法呪之，汝作灰汝作灰者，如是如是，光颜益好，身体悦泽。彼众多仙人便作是念：『我等本呪汝作灰汝作灰者，彼即作灰。我今呪此人汝作灰汝作灰，我等如其呪法呪此人，此人光颜益好，身体悦泽，我宁可问。』即便问之：『汝为是谁？』阿私罗仙人提鞞逻答曰：『诸贤！汝等颇闻有阿私罗仙人提鞞逻耶？』答曰：『闻有阿私罗仙人提鞞逻。』复语曰：『我即是也。』彼众多仙人即共辞谢阿私罗仙人提鞞逻曰：『愿为忍恕，我等不知尊是阿私罗仙人提鞞逻耳。』

Then Asita Devala said to the ascetics, 'I have forgiven you. You really have given rise to evil views: "The Brahmin caste is superior, the others are inferior; the Brahmin caste is white, the others are all black; Brahmins attain purity, non-Brahmins do not attain purity; Brahmins are the sons of Brahma, created by Brahma."'" The ascetics replied, "Yes." Asita Devala further asked the ascetics, "Do you know your own father?" The ascetics replied, "We know. The Brahmin takes a Brahmin wife, not a non-Brahmin. His father, and his father's father, up to seven generations of fathers, the Brahmin takes a Brahmin wife, not a non-Brahmin." Asita Devala further asked the ascetics, "Do you know your own mother?" The ascetics replied, "We know. The Brahmin takes a Brahmin husband, not a non-Brahmin. Her mother, and her mother's mother, up to seven generations of mothers, the Brahmin takes a Brahmin husband, not a non-Brahmin." Asita Devala further asked the ascetics, "Do you know when you were conceived?" The ascetics replied, "We know. Conception occurs when three things come together: the parents come together, there is no obstruction, and the fragrant essence has arrived. Asita! When these things come together, one enters the mother's womb.

「于是，阿私罗仙人提鞞逻语诸仙人曰：『我已相恕。汝等实生恶见：「梵志种胜，余者不如；梵志种白，余者皆黑；梵志得清净，非梵志不得清净；梵志梵天子，从彼口生，梵梵所化。」』彼诸仙人答曰：『如是。』阿私罗复问诸仙人曰：『汝等为自知己父耶？』彼诸仙人答曰：『知也。彼梵志取梵志妇，非非梵志，彼父复父，乃至七世父，彼梵志取梵志妇，非非梵志。』阿私罗复问诸仙人曰：『汝等为自知己母耶？』彼诸仙人答曰：『知也。彼梵志取梵志夫，非非梵志，彼母复母，乃至七世母，彼梵志取梵志夫，非非梵志。』阿私罗复问诸仙人曰：『汝等颇自知受胎耶？』彼诸仙人答曰：『知也。以三事等合会受胎，父母合会，无满堪耐，香阴已至。阿私罗！此事等会，入于母胎。』

"Asita Devala further asked the ascetics, 'Do you know whether you were born as a male or a female? Do you know where you came from? Did you come from the Kṣatriya caste, the Brahmin caste, the Vaishya caste, or the Shudra caste? Did you come from the east, south, west, or north?' The ascetics replied, 'We do not know.' Asita Devala further said to the ascetics, 'Venerable ones! Since you do not see or know this, you do not know where you were conceived. From where did you come? Were you born as a male or a female? Did you come from the Kṣatriya caste, the Brahmin caste, the Vaishya caste, or the Shudra caste? Did you come from the east, south, west, or north? Yet you say, "The Brahmin caste is superior, the others are inferior; the Brahmin caste is white, the others are all black; Brahmins attain purity, non-Brahmins do not attain purity; Brahmins are the sons of Brahma, created by Brahma."'" Mānava! Those many ascetics living in the high place were well-taught and well-rebuked by Asita Devala, yet they could not establish the purity of Brahmins. How much less so you and your disciples who wear fur robes?

「阿私罗复问诸仙人曰：『颇知受生为男？为女？知所从来？为从刹利族来？梵志、居士、工师族来耶？为从东方、南方、西方、北方来耶？』彼诸仙人答曰：『不知。』阿私罗复语彼仙人曰：『诸贤！不见不知此者，汝等不知受胎，谁从何处来？为男为女？为从刹利来？梵志、居士、工师来？为从东方、南方、西方、北方来？然作是说：「梵志种胜，余者不如；梵志种白，余者皆黑；梵志得清净，非梵志不得清净；梵志梵天子，从彼口生，梵梵所化。」』摩纳！彼住无事高处众多仙人为阿私罗仙人提鞞逻如是善教善诃，不能施设清净梵志，况汝师徒著皮草衣？」

Then the brahmin student Asita Devala was directly rebuked and scolded by the World-Honored One. He was inwardly distressed, lowered his head in silence, and lost his eloquence.

于是，阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳为世尊面诃诘啧，内怀愁戚，低头默然，失辩无言。

Then the World-Honored One, having directly rebuked and scolded the brahmin student Asita Devala, further delighted him and said, "Mānava! There was a brahmin who held a feast and gave alms. He had four sons, two of whom studied and two did not. What do you think, Mānava? Which of those sons would the brahmin first offer the best seat, the best water, and the best food to?"

「于是，世尊面诃诘啧阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳已，复令欢悦，即便告曰：『摩纳！有一梵志作斋行施，彼有四儿，二好学问，二不学问。于摩纳意云何？彼梵志为先施谁第一座、第一澡水、第一食耶？』」

Asita Devala replied, "Gautama! If that brahmin had two sons, those who studied would certainly be offered the best seat, the best water, and the best food first."

阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳答曰：「瞿昙！若彼梵志其有二儿，好学问者，必先施彼第一座、第一澡水、第一食也。」

The World-Honored One further asked, "Mānava! There was another brahmin who held a feast and gave alms. He had four sons, two of whom studied but were not diligent, delighting in evil practices, and two who did not study but were diligent, delighting in good practices. What do you think, Mānava? Which of those sons would the brahmin first offer the best seat, the best water, and the best food to?"

世尊复问曰：「摩纳！复有一梵志作斋行施，彼有四儿，二好学问，然不精进，喜行恶法，二不学问，然好精进，喜行妙法。于摩纳意云何？彼梵志为先施谁第一座、第一澡水、第一食耶？」

Asita Devala replied, "Gautama! If that brahmin had two sons, although they did not study, they were diligent and delighted in good practices. They would certainly be offered the best seat, the best water, and the best food first."

阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳答曰：「瞿昙！若彼梵志其有二儿，虽不学问，而好精进，喜行妙法者，必先施彼第一座、第一澡水、第一食也。」

The World-Honored One said, "Mānava! You first praised learning and then praised upholding the precepts. Mānava! I have explained that the four castes are all pure and have been established and revealed. You have also said that the four castes are all pure and have been established and revealed."

世尊告曰：「摩纳！汝先称叹学问，后称叹持戒。摩纳！我说四种姓皆悉清净施设显示，汝亦说四种姓皆悉清净施设显示。」

Then Asita Devala rose from his seat, wanting to bow at the Buddha's feet. At that time, the great assembly exclaimed in loud voices, "The Śramaṇa Gautama is truly amazing! He has great supernatural powers, great virtue, great merit, and great spiritual power. Why is that? Because the Śramaṇa Gautama has explained that the four castes are all pure and have been established and revealed, causing Asita Devala to also say that the four castes are all pure."

于是，阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳即从坐起，欲稽首佛足。尔时，彼大众唱高大音声：「沙门瞿昙甚奇！甚特！有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神。所以者何？如沙门瞿昙说，四种姓皆悉清净施设显示，令阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳，亦说四种姓皆悉清净。」

At that time, the World-Honored One knew the thoughts in the minds of the great assembly and said, "Stop! Stop! Asita Devala! Just be content in your mind and return to your seat. I will explain the Dharma for you."

尔时，世尊知彼大众心之所念，告曰：「止！止！阿摄惒逻延多那！但心喜足，可还复坐，我当为汝说法。」

Asita Devala bowed at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and the World-Honored One explained the Dharma for him, inspiring him with longing and bringing about joy. Having used immeasurable skillful means to explain the Dharma for him, inspiring him with longing and bringing about joy, he remained silent.

阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳稽首佛足，却坐一面，世尊为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，默然] cf.［丽】而住。

Then, Asita Devala, having been taught the Dharma by the Buddha, inspired with longing and brought to joy, rose from his seat, bowed at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and departed. At that time, the many Brahmins of Kosala, not far from where they had gone, criticized Asita Devala in various ways, saying, "What were you trying to do? You wanted to subdue the ascetic Gotama, but instead you were subdued by the ascetic Gotama and returned. It is like someone who enters a forest for the sake of the eyes, but returns without the eyes. Asita Devala, it is the same for you. You wanted to subdue the ascetic Gotama, but instead you were subdued by the ascetic Gotama and returned. It is like someone who enters a pond to drink, but returns thirsty. Asita Devala, it is the same for you. You wanted to subdue the ascetic Gotama, but instead you were subdued by the ascetic Gotama and returned. Asita Devala, what were you trying to do?"

于是，阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳，佛为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，即从坐起，稽首佛足，绕三匝而去。是时，拘萨罗众多梵志还去不远，种种言语责数阿摄惒逻延多那：「欲何等作？欲伏沙门瞿昙，而反为沙门瞿昙所降伏还。犹如有人，为眼入林中，而反失眼还。阿摄惒逻延多那！汝亦如是，欲伏沙门瞿昙，而反为沙门瞿昙所降伏还。犹如有人，为饮入池，而反渴还。阿摄惒逻延多那！汝亦如是，欲伏沙门瞿昙，而反为沙门瞿昙所降伏还。阿摄惒逻延多那！欲何等作？」

Then, Asita Devala said to the many Brahmins of Kosala, "Virtuous ones! I have already said: 'The ascetic Gotama speaks the Dharma according to the Dharma. If one speaks the Dharma according to the Dharma, one cannot be questioned.'"

于是，阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳语拘萨罗众多梵志曰：「诸贤！我前已说：『沙门瞿昙如法说法，若如法说法者，不可难诘也。』」

The Buddha spoke thus. Asita Devala, having heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced and practiced it.

佛说如是。阿摄惒逻延多那摩纳闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

The tenth chapter on Asita Devala is completed.
