MĀ 154

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 12 梵志品

154. （一五四）婆罗婆堂经

Parallel to: DN 27 / DĀ 5

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Shravasti, staying at the Deer Park Hall of the Mother of the Deer.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在东园鹿子母堂。

At that time, there were two people, the Brahmins Vasistha and Bharadvaja, who shaved their beards and hair, put on the saffron robes, and went forth from home to practice the path with true faith. When the Brahmins saw this, they strongly criticized and scolded them, saying, "The Brahmin caste is superior, the others are inferior; the Brahmin caste is white, the others are all black; only Brahmins can attain purity, non-Brahmins cannot; Brahmins are the sons of Brahma, born from his mouth, transformed by Brahma. You have abandoned the superior and followed the inferior, abandoned the white and followed the black. Those shaven-headed monks are bound by the black, cutting off the lineage and having no offspring. Therefore, what you have done is greatly evil, a grave offense."

尔时，有二人婆私咤及婆罗婆梵志族，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。诸梵志见已，极诃责数，甚急至苦，而语之曰：「梵志种胜，余者不如；梵志种白，余者皆黑；梵志得清净，非梵志不得清净；梵志梵天子，从彼口生，梵梵所化。汝等舍胜从不如，舍白从黑，彼秃沙门为黑所缚，断种无子。是故汝等所作大恶，极犯大过。」

At that time, the World-Honored One then rose from his afternoon meditation, came down from the hall, and walked in the open air in the shadow of the hall, expounding the profound and subtle Dharma to the monks. Venerable Vasistha saw from afar that the World-Honored One had risen from his afternoon meditation, come down from the hall, and was walking in the open air in the shadow of the hall, expounding the profound and subtle Dharma to the monks. Having seen this, he said, "Virtuous Bharadvaja! Know that the World-Honored One has risen from his afternoon meditation, come down from the hall, and is walking in the open air in the shadow of the hall, expounding the profound and subtle Dharma to the monks. Virtuous Bharadvaja! Let us go together to see the Buddha. Perhaps we can hear the Dharma from the Buddha because of this."

尔时，世尊则于晡时从燕坐起，堂上来下，于堂影中露地经行，为诸比丘说甚深微妙法。尊者婆私咤遥见世尊则于晡时从燕坐起，堂上来下，于堂影中露地经行，为诸比丘说甚深微妙法。尊者婆私咤见已，语曰：「贤者婆罗婆！当知世尊则于晡时从燕坐起，堂上来下，于堂影中露地经行，为诸比丘说甚深微妙法。贤者婆罗婆！可共诣佛，或能因此从佛闻法。」

Then Vasistha and Bharadvaja went to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads to his feet, and walked behind him.

于是，婆私咤及婆罗婆即诣佛所，稽首作礼，从后经行。

The World-Honored One turned around and said to the two of them, "Vasistha! When you two Brahmins left your Brahmin families, shaved your heads and beards, put on the saffron robes, left home out of true faith, became homeless, and studied the path, did the Brahmins not criticize you much?"

世尊回顾，告彼二人：「婆私咤！汝等二梵志舍梵志族，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，诸梵志见已，不大责数耶？」

They replied, "Yes, World-Honored One! When the Brahmins saw us, they criticized us very harshly, to the point of extreme suffering."

彼即答曰：「唯然。世尊！诸梵志见已，极诃责数，甚急至苦。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Vasistha! How did the Brahmins criticize you so harshly and severely?"

世尊问曰：「婆私咤！诸梵志见已，云何极诃责数，甚急至苦耶？」

They replied, "World-Honored One! When the Brahmins saw us, they said, 'The Brahmin caste is superior, the others are inferior; the Brahmin caste is white, the others are all black; Brahmins attain purity, non-Brahmins do not attain purity; Brahmins are born from the mouth of Brahma, created by Brahma. You have abandoned the superior and followed the inferior, abandoned the white and followed the black. Those shaven-headed monks are bound by the black, cutting off the lineage without offspring. Therefore, what you have done is greatly evil, a grave offense.' World-Honored One! When the Brahmins saw us, they criticized us so harshly and severely."

答曰：「世尊！诸梵志见我等已，而作是说：『梵志种胜，余者不如；梵志种白，余者皆黑；梵志得清净，非梵志不得清净；梵志梵天子，从彼口生，梵梵所化。汝等舍胜从不如，舍白从黑，彼秃沙门为黑所缚，断种无子。是故汝等所作大恶，极犯大过。』世尊！诸梵志见我等已，如是极诃责数，甚急至苦。」

The World-Honored One said, "Vasistha! What those Brahmins say is extremely evil and shameless. Why is that? It is because they are ignorant, do not understand well, do not know the good field, and cannot know themselves. They say, 'We Brahmins are born from the mouth of Brahma, created by Brahma.' Why is that? Vasistha! In my unsurpassed wisdom, practice, and realization, I do not speak of birth superiority, caste, or arrogance: 'They agree with my views, they do not agree with my views, because of sitting, because of water, because of the scriptures they study.' Vasistha! If there is marriage, then one should speak of birth, speak of caste, speak of arrogance: 'They agree with my views, they do not agree with my views, because of sitting, because of water, because of the scriptures they study.' Vasistha! If one calculates birth, caste, and arrogance, then one is far removed from my unsurpassed wisdom, practice, and realization. Vasistha! Speaking of birth, speaking of caste, speaking of arrogance: 'They agree with my views, they do not agree with my views, because of sitting, because of water, because of the scriptures they study' is different from my unsurpassed wisdom, practice, and realization. Furthermore, Vasistha! There are three kinds of people who quarrel with each other, mixing good and bad dharmas, which are praised and not praised by the sages. What are the three? The kshatriya caste, the brahmin caste, and the householder caste. Vasistha! What do you think? Is it not the case that a kshatriya commits killing, taking what is not given, engaging in sexual misconduct, false speech, up to wrong views, and so do householders, but not brahmins?"

世尊告曰：「婆私咤！彼诸梵志所说至恶，极自无赖。所以者何？谓彼愚痴，不善晓解，不识良田，不能自知，作如是说：『我等梵志是梵天子，从彼口生，梵梵所化。』所以者何？婆私咤！我此无上明、行、作证，不说生胜，不说种姓，不说憍慢：『彼可我意，不可我意，因坐因水，所学经书。』婆私咤！若有婚姻者，彼应说生，应说种姓，应说憍慢：『彼可我意，不可我意，因坐因水，所学经书。』婆私咤！若有计生、计姓、计慢者，彼极远离于我无上明、行、作证。婆私咤！说生、说姓、说慢：『彼可我意，不可我意，因坐因水，所学经书』者，于我无上明、行、作证别。复次，婆私咤！谓有三种，令非一切人人共诤，杂善不善法，彼则为圣所称不称。云何为三？刹利种、梵志种、居士种。婆私咤！于意云何？刹利杀生，不与取、行邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，居士亦然；非梵志耶？」

They replied, "World-Honored One! Kshatriyas can also commit killing, taking what is not given, engaging in sexual misconduct, false speech, up to wrong views, and Brahmins and householders are also like this."

答曰：「世尊！刹利亦可杀生、不与取、行邪婬、妄言，乃至邪见，梵志、居士亦复如是。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Vasistha! What do you think? Is it not the case that Brahmins are free from killing, have abandoned killing, are free from taking what is not given, engaging in sexual misconduct, false speech, up to are free from wrong views and have attained right views, and so are Kshatriyas and householders?"

世尊问曰：「婆私咤！于意云何？梵志离杀、断杀、不与取、行邪婬、妄言，乃至离邪见，得正见；刹利、居士为不然耶？」

They replied, "World-Honored One! Brahmins can also be free from killing, have abandoned killing, are free from taking what is not given, engaging in sexual misconduct, false speech, up to are free from wrong views and have attained right views, and so are Kshatriyas and householders."

答曰：「世尊！梵志亦可离杀、断杀、不与取、行邪婬、妄言，乃至离邪见得正见；刹利、居士亦复如是。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Vasistha! What do you think? Are there immeasurable evil and unwholesome dharmas that are practiced by Kshatriyas and householders, but not by Brahmins? Are there immeasurable wholesome dharmas that are practiced by Brahmins, but not by Kshatriyas and householders?"

世尊问曰：「婆私咤！于意云何？若有无量恶不善法，是刹利、居士所行；非梵志耶？若有无量善法，是梵志所行；非刹利、居士耶？」

They replied, "World-Honored One! If there are immeasurable evil and unwholesome dharmas, then Kshatriyas and householders can also practice them, and so can Brahmins. If there are immeasurable wholesome dharmas, then Brahmins can also practice them, and so can Kshatriyas and householders."

答曰：「世尊！若有无量恶不善法，彼刹利、居士亦可行，梵志亦复如是。若有无量善法，彼梵志亦可行，刹利、居士亦复如是。」

Vasistha! If there are immeasurable evil and unwholesome dharmas that are practiced exclusively by Kshatriyas and householders, not by Brahmins, and if there are immeasurable wholesome dharmas that are practiced exclusively by Brahmins, not by Kshatriyas and householders, then those Brahmins can say, 'We Brahmins are the sons of Brahma, born from his mouth, created by Brahma.' Why is that? Vasistha! When a Brahmin girl is first married, and after marriage, when she is seen to be pregnant, and after being pregnant, when she gives birth, whether a boy or a girl. Vasistha! In this way, those Brahmins are also like worldly people, born through the birth canal. Yet they falsely claim to be slandering Brahma, saying, 'We Brahmins are the sons of Brahma, born from his mouth, created by Brahma.'

「婆私咤！若有无量恶不善法，一向刹利、居士行，非梵志者。若有无量善法，一向梵志行，非刹利、居士者。彼诸梵志可作是说：『我等梵志是梵天子，从彼口生，梵梵所化。』所以者何？婆私咤！见梵志女始婚姻时，婚姻已后，见怀妊身时，怀妊身已后，见产生时，或童男，或童女。婆私咤！如是诸梵志亦如世法，随产道生，然彼妄言［言＊（一／（人*人）／工）］谤梵天而作是说：『我等梵志是梵天子，从彼口生，梵梵所化。』

"Vasistha! If a son of a noble family, of various noble families, of various names, renounces his various families, shaves his beard and hair, puts on the monastic robe, leaves home out of true faith, becomes a homeless ascetic, and studies the path under me, he should say, 'We Brahmins are the sons of Brahma, born from his mouth, created by Brahma.' Why is that? Vasistha! That son of a noble family enters my true Dharma and Vinaya, accepts my true Dharma and Vinaya, attains the other shore, cuts off doubts and crosses over confusion, without hesitation, and attains fearlessness in the World-Honored One's Dharma. Therefore he should say, 'We Brahmins are the sons of Brahma, born from his mouth, created by Brahma.' Vasistha! That Brahma is said to be the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One. Brahma is the Tathāgata, the cool one, the one without afflictions, the one without heat, the one who is not apart from suchness, that is the Tathāgata. Vasistha! What do you think? Do the Shakyas respect, honor, and serve King Prasenajit of Kosala with a humble mind?"

「婆私咤！若族姓子、若干种姓、若干种名，舍若干族，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家，从我学道，应作是说：『我等梵志是梵天子，从彼口生，梵梵所化。』所以者何？婆私咤！彼族姓子入我正法、律中，受我正法、律，得至彼岸，断疑度惑，无有犹豫，于世尊法得无所畏。是故彼应作是说：『我等梵志是梵天子，从彼口生，梵梵所化。』婆私咤！彼梵天者，是说如来、无所著、等正觉，梵是如来，冷是如来，无烦无热，不离如者，是如来也。婆私咤！于意云何？诸释下意爱敬至重，供养奉事于波斯匿拘娑罗王耶？」

They replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

彼则答曰：「如是。世尊！」

The World-Honored One asked, "Vasistha! What do you think? If the Shakyas respect, honor, and serve King Prasenajit of Kosala with a humble mind, does King Prasenajit of Kosala then respect, honor, and serve me with a humble mind?"

世尊问曰：「婆私咤！于意云何？若诸释下意爱敬至重，供养奉事于波斯匿拘娑罗王，如是波斯匿拘娑罗王则于我身下意爱敬至重，供养奉事我耶？」

He replied to the World-Honored One, "It is not extraordinary for the Shakyas to respect, honor, and serve King Prasenajit of Kosala with a humble mind. It is extraordinary for King Prasenajit of Kosala to respect, honor, and serve the World-Honored One with a humble mind."

答世尊曰：「诸释下意爱敬至重，供养奉事于波斯匿拘娑罗王者，此无奇特。若波斯匿拘娑罗王下意爱敬至重，供养奉事于世尊者，此甚奇特。」

The World-Honored One said, "Vasistha! King Prasenajit of Kosala does not think like this, and does not respect, honor, and serve me with a humble mind, thinking, 'The Shakyamuni Gautama has a very high caste, while my caste is low. The Shakyamuni Gautama has a lot of wealth, while I have little. The Shakyamuni Gautama has the most excellent appearance, while my appearance is not excellent. The Shakyamuni Gautama has great spiritual power, while my spiritual power is small. The Shakyamuni Gautama has good wisdom, while I have bad wisdom.' Vasistha! King Prasenajit of Kosala only respects, honors, and serves the Dharma with a humble mind, and respects, honors, and serves me with a humble mind for the sake of the Dharma.

世尊告曰：「婆私咤！波斯匿拘娑罗王不如是意，而于我身下意爱敬至重，供养奉事于我：『沙门瞿昙种族极高，我种族下。沙门瞿昙财宝甚多，我财宝少。沙门瞿昙形色至妙，我色不妙。沙门瞿昙有大威神，我威神小。沙门瞿昙有善智慧，我有恶智。』婆私咤！但波斯匿拘娑罗王爱敬于法，至重供养，为奉事故，而于我身下意爱敬至重，供养奉事于我。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Vasistha! Sometimes this world is completely destroyed. When this world is destroyed, if there are sentient beings born in the Ābhāsvara Heaven, they are born there with a wonderful form, with all limbs and faculties complete, subsisting on joy, with their own light, rising in the sky, with pure form, abiding for a long time. Vasistha! Sometimes this great earth is filled with water. On that great body of water, the wind blows and stirs it, causing it to coagulate and form a substance, combining and aggregating. It is like curdled milk, coagulated by churning, combining and aggregating. In this way, Vasistha! Sometimes this great earth is filled with water. On that great body of water, the wind blows and stirs it, causing it to coagulate and form a substance, combining and aggregating. From this, the taste of earth arises, with color, smell, and taste. What is the color? It is like the color of fresh butter and clarified butter. What is the taste? It is like the taste of honey balls.

尔时，世尊告比丘曰：「婆私咤！有时此世皆悉败坏。此世坏时，若有众生生晃昱天，彼于其中妙色意生，一切支节诸根具足，以喜为食，自身光明，升于虚空，净色久住。婆私咤！有时此大地满其中水，彼大水上以风吹搅，结搆为精，合聚和合，犹如熟酪，以抨抨乳，结搆为精，合聚和合。如是，婆私咤！有时此大地满其中水，彼大水上以风吹搅，结搆为精，合聚和合，从是生地味，有色香味。云何为色？犹如生酥及熟酥色。云何为味？如蜜丸味。

Vasistha! Sometimes when this world is being formed again, if there are sentient beings born in the Ābhāsvara Heaven, when their lifespan, karma, and blessings are exhausted, they are reborn as humans here. After being reborn here, they are born with a wonderful form, with all limbs and faculties complete, subsisting on joy, with their own light, rising in the sky, with pure form, abiding for a long time. Vasistha! At that time, there were no sun, moon, or stars in the world, no day or night, no months or half-months, no seasons or years. Vasistha! At that time, there were no fathers, mothers, sons, daughters, masters, or slaves, only sentient beings. Then, a sentient being was greedy and gluttonous, and thought, 'What is this earth essence? I should try it with my finger.' At that time, the sentient being tried the earth essence with his finger. When the sentient beings knew about the earth essence, they wanted to eat it. At that time, the sentient beings thought, 'Why eat the earth essence with my finger, tiring myself? I should now try to scoop it with my hand and eat it.' At that time, the sentient beings scooped the earth essence with their hands and ate it. Among those sentient beings, there were some who saw others scooping the earth essence with their hands and eating it, and thought, 'This is really good, this is really wonderful. We should also scoop the earth essence with our hands and eat it.' At that time, those sentient beings scooped the earth essence with their hands and ate it. When those sentient beings ate the earth essence with their hands, their bodies became thicker, heavier, and firmer. If they had pure form before, it disappeared and darkness naturally arose. Vasistha! It is the nature of the world that when darkness arises, the sun and moon will appear. When the sun and moon appear, the stars will appear. When the stars appear, day and night will be established. When day and night are established, there will be months and half-months, seasons and years. They lived on the earth essence for a long time.

「婆私咤！有时此世还复成时，若有众生生晃昱天，寿尽、业尽、福尽命终，生此为人，生此间已，妙色意生，一切支节诸根具足，以喜为食，自身光明，升于虚空，净色久住。婆私咤！尔时，世中无有日月，亦无星宿，无有昼夜，无月、半月，无时无岁。婆私咤！当尔之时，无父无母，无男无女，又无大家，复无奴婢，唯等众生。于是，有一众生贪餮不廉，便作是念：『云何地味？我宁可以指抄此地味尝。』彼时，众生便以指抄此地味尝。如是，众生既知地味，复欲得食。彼时，众生复作是念：『何故以指食此地味，用自疲劳？我今宁可以手撮此地味食之。』彼时，众生便以手撮此地味食。于彼众生中复有众生，见彼众生各以手撮此地味食，便作是念：『此实为善，此实为快，我等宁可亦以手撮此地味食。』时，彼众生即以手撮此地味食。若彼众生以手撮此地味食已，如是如是，身生转厚、转重、转坚，若彼本时有清净色，于是便灭，自然生暗。婆私咤！世间之法，自然有是，若生暗者，必生日月，生日月已，便生星宿，生星宿已，便成昼夜，成昼夜已，便有月、半月，有时、有岁。彼食地味，住世久远。

Vasistha! If there are sentient beings who eat a lot of earth essence, they will have an ugly appearance. If they eat little earth essence, they will have a wonderful appearance. From this, we know that there are superior and inferior appearances. Because of the superiority and inferiority of appearances, sentient beings will despise each other, saying, 'My appearance is superior, yours is inferior.' Because of the superiority and inferiority of appearances, they will give rise to contempt and evil dharmas. Therefore, the earth essence will disappear. After the earth essence disappears, those sentient beings will gather together, lamenting and crying, saying, 'Alas, the earth essence! Alas, the earth essence!' It is like people today who savor delicious things without speaking the original words. Although they receive and uphold them, they do not know the meaning. This explanation of contemplating the meaning is also like this. Vasistha! After the earth essence disappears, those sentient beings will find earth fat, with color, smell, and taste. What is the color? It is like the color of fresh butter and clarified butter. What is the taste? It is like the taste of honey balls. They will eat this earth fat and live for a long time.

「婆私咤！若有众生食地味多者，便生恶色，食地味少者，便有妙色，从是知色有胜有如，因色胜如故，众生众生共相轻慢言：『我色胜，汝色不如。』因色胜如而生轻慢及恶法故，地味便灭。地味灭已，彼众生等便共聚集，极悲啼泣而作是语：『奈何地味？奈何地味？』犹如今人含消美物，不说本字，虽受持而不知义，此说观义亦复如是。婆私咤！地味灭后，彼众生生地肥，有色香味。云何为色？犹如生酥及熟酥色。云何为味？如蜜丸味。彼食此地肥，住世久远。

From this, we know that there are superior and inferior appearances. Because of the superiority and inferiority of appearances, sentient beings will despise each other, saying, 'My appearance is superior, yours is inferior.' Because of the superiority and inferiority of appearances, they will give rise to contempt and evil dharmas. Therefore, the earth fat will disappear. After the earth fat disappears, those sentient beings will gather together, lamenting and crying, saying, 'Alas, the earth fat! Alas, the earth fat!' It is like people today who are criticized by others without speaking the original words. Although they receive and uphold them, they do not know the meaning. This explanation of contemplating the meaning is also like this. Vasistha! After the earth fat disappears, those sentient beings will find brahman, with color, smell, and taste. What is the color? It is like the color of a lotus flower. What is the taste? It is like the taste of a soft honey ball. They will eat this brahman and live for a long time.

「婆私咤！若有众生食地肥多者，便生恶色，食地肥少者，便有妙色，从是知色有胜有如，因色胜如故，众生众生共相轻慢言：『我色胜，汝色不如。』因色胜如而生轻慢及恶法故，地肥便灭。地肥灭已，彼众生等便共聚集，极悲啼泣而作是语：『奈何地肥？奈何地肥？』犹如今人为他所啧，不说本字，虽受持而不知义，此说观义亦复如是。婆私咤！地肥灭后，彼众生生婆罗，有色香味。云何为色？犹加昙华色。云何为味？如淖蜜丸味。彼食此婆罗，住世久远。

From this, we know that there are superior and inferior appearances. Because of the superiority and inferiority of appearances, sentient beings will despise each other, saying, 'My appearance is superior, yours is inferior.' Because of the superiority and inferiority of appearances, they will give rise to contempt and evil dharmas. Therefore, the brahman will disappear. After the brahman disappears, those sentient beings will gather together, lamenting and crying, saying, 'Alas, the brahman! Alas, the brahman!' It is like people today who are touched by the suffering of the dharmas without speaking the original words. Although they receive and uphold them, they do not know the meaning. This explanation of contemplating the meaning is also like this.

「婆私咤！若有众生食婆罗多者，便生恶色，食婆罗少者，便有妙色，从是知色有胜有如，因色胜如故，众生众生共相轻慢言：『我色胜，汝色不如。』因色胜如而生轻慢及恶法故，婆罗便灭。婆罗灭已，彼众生等便共聚集，极悲啼泣而作是语：『奈何婆罗？奈何婆罗？』犹如今人苦法所触，不说本字，虽受持而不知义，此说观义亦复如是。

Vasistha! After the brahman disappears, those sentient beings will find naturally grown rice, white and pure without husks, also without bran and chaff, four inches long, harvested in the morning and growing in the evening, harvested in the evening and growing in the morning. The cooked rice has a salty taste and no vitality. Sentient beings eat this naturally grown rice. After those sentient beings eat this naturally grown rice, those sentient beings will give rise to various forms, some sentient beings will be born with male forms, and some sentient beings will be born with female forms. If those sentient beings are born with male and female forms, when they see each other, they will say, 'Evil sentient beings are born, evil sentient beings are born.'

「婆私咤！婆罗灭后，彼众生生自然粳米，白净无皮，亦无有［麸－夫＋黄］［葶－丁＋呆］，长四寸，朝刈暮生，暮刈朝生，熟有盐味，无有生气。众生食此自然粳米，如彼众生食此自然粳米已，彼众生等便生若干形，或有众生而生男形，或有众生而生女形。若彼众生生男女形者，彼相见已，便作是语：『恶众生生，恶众生生。』

Vasistha! Those who are born as evil sentient beings refer to women. If those sentient beings are born with male and female forms, those sentient beings will then watch each other, and when they watch each other, they will look at each other with their eyes. When they look at each other, they will become attached to each other. When they are attached to each other, they will become passionate. When they are passionate, they will engage in sexual activity. When they see sexual activity, they will hit and throw them with sticks, stones, or clods of earth, and say, 'Oh! Evil sentient beings do improper things.' Why do sentient beings do this? It is like when people welcome a new bride today, they scatter flowers and hang flower garlands, saying, 'May the new bride be safe, may the new bride be safe.' What was once despised is now loved. Vasistha! If there are sentient beings who dislike, despise, and are ashamed of impure dharmas, they will then leave the crowd for one day, two days, up to six or seven days, half a month, one month, up to one year. Vasistha! If there are sentient beings who want to engage in this impure conduct, they will then build a house and say, 'Do evil here, do evil here.' Vasistha! This is the first cause and condition for the arising of the household system in the world. The first wisdom of the past is in accordance with the Dharma, not contrary to the Dharma. The person who upholds the Dharma is honored.

「婆私咤！恶众生生者，谓说妇人也。若彼众生生于男形及女形者，彼众生等则更相伺，更相伺已，眼更相视，更相视已，则更相染，更相染已，便有烦热，有烦热已，便相爱著，相爱著已，便行于欲。若见行欲时，便以木石，或以杖块而打掷之，便作是语：『咄！弊恶众生作非法事。』云何众生共作是耶？犹如今人迎新妇时，则以襆华散，或以华鬘垂，作如是言：『新妇安隐，新妇安隐。』本所可憎，今所可爱。婆私咤！若有众生恶不净法，憎恶羞耻，怀惭愧者，彼便离众一日、二日，至六、七日，半月、一月，乃至一岁。婆私咤！若有众生欲得行此不净行者，彼便作家而作是说：『此中作恶，此中作恶。』婆私咤！是谓初因初缘世中起家法，旧第一智，如法、非不如法，如法人尊。

Among them, there was a lazy sentient being who thought, 'Why do I have to go and get naturally grown rice every day? I might as well get enough for one day.' So he got enough rice for one day. Then a sentient being said to him, 'Sentient being! Come and get rice together.' He replied, 'I have already gotten enough for one day. You go and get it yourself.' When that sentient being heard this, he thought, 'This is really good, this is really wonderful. I might as well get enough rice for two days.' So he got enough rice for two days. Then a sentient being said to him, 'Sentient being! Come and get rice together.' He replied, 'I have already gotten enough rice for two days. You go and get it yourself.' When that sentient being heard this, he thought, 'This is really good, this is really wonderful. I might as well get enough rice for seven days.' So that sentient being then got enough rice for seven days. As those sentient beings took and accumulated naturally grown rice, that naturally grown rice then grew husks and bran. When harvested for seven days, it also grew husks and bran. Wherever it was harvested, it would not grow again.

「于中有一事懒惰众生，便作是念：『我今何为日日常取自然粳米，我宁可并取一日食直耶？』彼便并取一日食米。于是，有一众生语彼众生曰：『众生！汝来共行取米耶？』彼则答曰：『我已并取，汝自取去。』彼众生闻已，便作是念：『此实为善，此实为快，我亦宁可并取明日所食米耶？』彼便并取明日米来。复有一众生语彼众生曰：『众生！汝来共行取米耶？』彼则答曰：『我已并取明日米来，汝自取去。』彼众生闻已，便作是念：『此实为善，此实为快，我今宁可并取七日食米来耶？』时，彼众生即便并取七日米来。如彼众生自然粳米极取积聚，彼宿粳米便生皮［麸－夫＋黄］，刈至七日亦生皮［麸－夫＋黄］，随所刈处，即不复生。

"Then those sentient beings gathered together, wept bitterly, and said, 'We have given rise to evil and unwholesome dharmas, namely, storing up rice for later. Why is that? We originally had wonderful forms, were born from the mind, had all our faculties complete, subsisted on joy, had our own radiance, ascended through the sky, and abided in purity for a long time. We gave rise to earth flavors with form, smell, and taste. What is the form? It is like the color of fresh butter and clarified butter. What is the taste? It is like the taste of honey pills. We subsisted on earth flavors and abided in the world for a long time. If we ate too much earth flavor, we would have an ugly appearance. Those who ate little earth flavor had a wonderful appearance. From this we knew that there were superior and inferior forms. Because of the superiority and inferiority of forms, we each looked down on each other, saying, "My form is superior, your form is inferior." Because of the superiority and inferiority of forms, we gave rise to contempt and evil dharmas. Therefore, the earth flavor disappeared. After the disappearance of the earth flavor, we gave rise to earth fat with form, smell, and taste. What is the form? It is like the color of fresh butter and clarified butter. What is the taste? It is like the taste of honey pills. We subsisted on earth fat and abided in the world for a long time. If we ate too much earth fat, we would have an ugly appearance. Those who ate little earth fat had a wonderful appearance. From this we knew that there were superior and inferior forms. Because of the superiority and inferiority of forms, we each looked down on each other, saying, "My form is superior, your form is inferior." Because of the superiority and inferiority of forms, we gave rise to contempt and evil dharmas. Therefore, the earth fat disappeared. After the disappearance of the earth fat, we gave rise to naturally grown rice with form, smell, and taste. What is the form? It is like the color of a kadamba flower. What is the taste? It is like the taste of honey pills. We subsisted on naturally grown rice and abided in the world for a long time. If we ate too much naturally grown rice, we would have an ugly appearance. Those who ate little naturally grown rice had a wonderful appearance. From this we knew that there were superior and inferior forms. Because of the superiority and inferiority of forms, we each looked down on each other, saying, "My form is superior, your form is inferior." Because of the superiority and inferiority of forms, we gave rise to contempt and evil dharmas. Therefore, the naturally grown rice disappeared. After the disappearance of the naturally grown rice, we thought, 'Should we build fields and set up boundaries?'"

「于是，彼众生便共聚集，极悲啼泣，作如是语：『我等生恶不善之法，谓我曹等储畜宿米。所以者何？我等本有妙色意生，一切支节诸根具足，以喜为食，自身光明，升于虚空，净色久住，我等生地味，有色香味。云何为色？犹如生酥及熟酥色。云何为味？如蜜丸味。我等食地味，住世久远。我等若食地味多者，便生恶色，食地味少者，彼有妙色，从是知色有胜有如，因色胜如故，我等各各共相轻慢言：「我色胜，汝色不如。」因色胜如而生轻慢及恶法故，地味便灭，地味灭后，我等生地肥，有色香味。云何为色？犹如生酥及熟酥色。云何为味？如蜜丸味。我等食地肥，住世久远。我等若食地肥多者，便生恶色，食地肥少者，便有妙色，从是知色有胜有如，因色胜如故，我等各各共相轻慢言：「我色胜，汝色不如。」因色胜如而生轻慢及恶法故，地肥便灭。地肥灭后，我等生婆罗，有色香味。云何为色？犹加昙华色。云何为味？如淖蜜丸味。我等食婆罗，住世久远。我等若食婆罗多者，便生恶色，食婆罗少者，便有妙色，从是知色有胜有如，因色胜如故，我等各各共相轻慢言：「我色胜，汝色不如。」因色胜如而生轻慢及恶法故，婆罗便灭。婆罗灭后，我等生自然粳米，白净无皮，亦无有［麸－夫＋黄］［葶－丁＋呆］，长四寸，朝刈暮生，暮刈朝生，熟有盐味，无有生气，我等食彼自然粳米，如我等自然粳米，极取积聚，彼宿粳米便生皮［麸－夫＋黄］，刈至七日，亦生皮［麸－夫＋黄］，随所刈处，即不复生，我等宁可造作田种，立标牓耶？』

Then those sentient beings built fields and set up boundaries. Among them, there was a sentient being who had his own rice, but he entered someone else's field and stole their rice. The owner saw him and said, 'Oh no! Oh no! Evil sentient being, how could you do this? You have your own rice, but you enter someone else's field and steal their rice. You should leave now and never do it again.' But that sentient being went to steal someone else's rice again and again, and the owner saw him again and again. He then took him by the fist and dragged him to the assembly, saying, 'This sentient being has his own rice, but he enters my field and steals my rice.' But that sentient being also said to the assembly, 'This sentient being took me by the fist and dragged me to the assembly.'

「于是，众生等造作田种，竖立标牓。于中有一众生自有稻谷，而入他田窃取他稻，其主见已，便作是语：『咄！咄！弊恶众生，云何作是？汝自有稻，而入他田窃取他稻。汝今可去，后莫复作。』然彼众生复至再三窃取他稻，其主亦至再三见已，便以拳扠牵诣众所，语彼众曰：『此一众生自有稻谷，而入我田窃取我稻。』然彼一众生亦语众曰：『此一众生以拳扠我牵来诣众。』

Then those sentient beings gathered together and wept bitterly, saying, 'We have given rise to evil and unwholesome dharmas, namely, guarding fields. Why is that? Because of guarding fields, we have disputes, losses, and exhaustion. We accuse each other and fight with our fists. We should choose one among us who is the most upright and handsome, and establish him as the lord of the fields. If there is something to be scolded, he should scold. If there is something to be expelled, he should expel. The rice we obtain should be properly delivered to him.' Then among those sentient beings, they chose the most upright and handsome one to be the lord of the fields. If there was something to be scolded, he scolded. If there was something to be expelled, he expelled. If there was rice, it was properly delivered to him. This lord of the fields was called a kṣatriya. He made sentient beings enjoy the Dharma and protected those who practiced precepts. This king was called a king. This is the first cause and condition for the kṣatriya caste to appear in the world. The first wise one, according to the Dharma, not contrary to the Dharma, is the Dharma king.

「于是，彼诸众生共聚集会，极悲啼泣而作是语：『我等生恶不善之法，谓守田也。所以者何？因守田故，便共诤讼，有失有尽，有相道说，有拳相扠。我等宁可于其众中举一端正形色，极妙最第一者，立为田主，若可诃者，当令彼诃，若可摈者，当令彼摈，若我曹等所得稻谷，当以如法输送与彼。』于是，彼众生中，若有端正形色，极妙最第一者，众便共举，立为田主，若可诃者，彼便诃啧，若可摈者，彼便摈弃，若有稻者，便以如法输送与彼是田主。是田主谓之刹利也，令如法乐众生，守护行戒是王，是王谓之王也。婆私咤！是谓初因初缘世中刹利种，旧第一智，如法、非不如法，如法人尊。

Then those sentient beings who saw guarding as a disease, a boil, and a thorn, abandoned guarding and relied on non-affliction, building grass and leaf huts to practice meditation. They went into villages, towns, and royal cities every morning to beg for food. Many sentient beings saw them and gave them offerings, showing respect and reverence, saying, 'These sentient beings see guarding as a disease, a boil, and a thorn. They have abandoned guarding and rely on non-affliction, building grass and leaf huts to practice meditation. These venerable ones have abandoned harmful and unwholesome dharmas. They are brāhmaṇas. This is called a brāhmaṇa.

「于是，彼异众生以守为病，以守为痈，以守为箭刺，便弃舍守，依于无事，作草叶屋而学禅也。彼从无事，朝朝平旦入村邑王城而行乞食，彼多众生见便施与，恭敬尊重，而作是语：『此异众生以守为病，以守为痈，以守为箭刺，便弃舍守，依于无事，作草叶屋而学禅也。此诸尊舍害、恶不善法是梵志，是梵志谓之梵志也。』

Those sentient beings who practiced meditation but did not attain meditation, practiced austerities but did not attain austerities, practiced seclusion but did not attain seclusion, practiced single-mindedness but did not attain single-mindedness, practiced diligence but did not attain diligence, abandoned non-affliction and returned to villages, towns, and royal cities, building four-pillared houses and composing texts. When many sentient beings saw this, they no longer gave offerings, showed respect, or revered them, saying, 'These sentient beings originally saw guarding as a disease, a boil, and a thorn. They abandoned guarding and relied on non-affliction, building grass and leaf huts to practice meditation. They did not attain meditation, did not attain austerities, did not attain seclusion, did not attain single-mindedness, and did not attain diligence. They abandoned non-affliction and returned to villages, towns, and royal cities, building four-pillared houses and composing texts. These venerable ones are again learning extensive knowledge, not practicing meditation. This is extensive knowledge. This is called extensive knowledge.

「彼众生学禅不得禅，学苦行不得苦行，学远离不得远离，学一心不得一心，学精进不得精进，便舍无事，还村邑王城，作四柱屋，造立经书。彼多众生见如是已，便不复施与、恭敬、尊重，而作是语：『此异众生本以守为病，以守为痈，以守为箭刺，便弃舍守，依于无事，作草叶屋，而学于禅不能得禅，学苦行不得苦行，学远离不得远离，学一心不得一心，学精进不得精进，便舍无事，还村邑王城，作四柱屋，造立经书。此诸尊等更学博闻，不复学禅是博闻，是博闻谓之博闻。』婆私咤！是谓初因初缘世中有梵志种，旧第一智，如法、非不如法，如法人尊。

Then those sentient beings each went to various places to engage in farming. This farming in various places is called vaiśya. This is the first cause and condition for the existence of the vaiśya caste in the world, the oldest and foremost wisdom, in accordance with the Dharma, not contrary to the Dharma, revered by those who follow the Dharma.

「于是，彼异众生各各诣诸方而作田业，是各各诸方而作田业，是各各诸方而作田业，谓之鞞舍。婆私咤！是谓初因初缘世中有鞞舍种，旧第一智，如法、非不如法，如法人尊。

Vāseṭṭha, when these three castes arose in the world, then the fourth caste of śramaṇas was known. How was it known that when these three castes arose in the world, then the fourth caste of śramaṇas was known? Among the kṣatriya caste, a son of a noble family was able to rebuke and detest unwholesome dharmas, to shave off his hair and beard, put on the saffron robes, leave home out of true faith, become homeless, and practice the path, thinking: 'I shall become a śramaṇa and practice the holy life.' He then became a śramaṇa and practiced the holy life. Similarly, among the brāhmaṇa caste and the vaiśya caste, a son of a noble family was also able to rebuke and detest unwholesome dharmas, to shave off his hair and beard, put on the saffron robes, leave home out of true faith, become homeless, and practice the path, thinking: 'I shall become a śramaṇa and practice the holy life.' He then became a śramaṇa and practiced the holy life. Vāseṭṭha, when these three castes arose in the world, then the fourth caste of śramaṇas was known.

「婆私咤，世中起此三种姓已，便知有第四沙门种也。云何世中有此三种姓已，便知有第四沙门种耶？于刹利族族姓之子，能自诃啧恶不善法，自厌憎恶恶不善法，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，而作是念：『我当作沙门，行于梵行。』便作沙门行于梵行。如是，梵志种族、鞞舍种族族姓之子，亦自诃啧恶不善法，自厌憎恶恶不善法，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，亦作是念：『我当作沙门，行于梵行。』便作沙门行于梵行。婆私咤！如是世中起此三种姓已，便知有第四沙门种也。

Vāseṭṭha, I now explain these three castes in detail. How are they broadly divided into three? A son of a noble family of the kṣatriya caste, who practices unwholesome bodily, verbal, and mental actions, upon the destruction of the body and the end of life, will experience only suffering. Similarly, a son of a noble family of the brāhmaṇa caste and the vaiśya caste, who practices unwholesome bodily, verbal, and mental actions, upon the destruction of the body and the end of life, will experience only suffering. Vāseṭṭha, a son of a noble family of the kṣatriya caste, who practices wholesome bodily, verbal, and mental actions, upon the destruction of the body and the end of life, will experience only happiness. Similarly, a son of a noble family of the brāhmaṇa caste and the vaiśya caste, who practices wholesome bodily, verbal, and mental actions, upon the destruction of the body and the end of life, will experience only happiness. Vāseṭṭha, a son of a noble family of the kṣatriya caste, who practices both wholesome and unwholesome bodily, verbal, and mental actions, and also protects against unwholesome actions, upon the destruction of the body and the end of life, will experience both suffering and happiness. Similarly, a son of a noble family of the brāhmaṇa caste and the vaiśya caste, who practices both wholesome and unwholesome bodily, verbal, and mental actions, and also protects against unwholesome actions, upon the destruction of the body and the end of life, will experience both suffering and happiness.

「婆私咤！我今广说此三种姓。云何广有此三种耶？刹利种族族姓之子，身行不善法，口、意行不善法，彼身坏命终，一向受苦。如是，梵志种族、鞞舍种族族姓之子，身行不善法，口、意行不善法，彼身坏命终，一向受苦。婆私咤！刹利种族族姓之子，身行善法，口、意行善法，彼身坏命终，一向受乐。如是，梵志种族、鞞舍种族族姓之子，身行善法，口、意行善法，彼身坏命终，一向受乐。婆私咤！刹利种族族姓之子，身行二行及与护行，口、意行二行及与护行，彼身坏命终，受于苦乐。如是，梵志种族、鞞舍种族族姓之子，身行二行及与护行，口、意行二行及与护行，彼身坏命终，受于苦乐。

Vāseṭṭha, a son of a noble family of the kṣatriya caste, who cultivates the seven factors of awakening, contemplates well and observes well, knows and sees thus: his mind is liberated from the taints of desire, existence, and ignorance. Having been liberated, he knows and sees liberation, the end of rebirth, the establishment of the holy life, the task accomplished, the work done, and he does not enter into any future existence. Similarly, a son of a noble family of the brāhmaṇa caste and the vaiśya caste, who cultivates the seven factors of awakening, contemplates well and observes well, knows and sees thus: his mind is liberated from the taints of desire, existence, and ignorance. Having been liberated, he knows and sees liberation, the end of rebirth, the establishment of the holy life, the task accomplished, the work done, and he does not enter into any future existence. Vāseṭṭha, this is how these three castes are broadly distinguished. Brahmadatta spoke this verse:

「婆私咤！刹利种族族姓之子，修七觉法，善思善观，彼如是知、如是见，欲漏心解脱，有漏、无明漏心解脱，解脱已，便知解脱，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。如是，梵志种族、鞞舍种族族姓之子，修七觉法，善思善观，彼如是知、如是见，欲漏心解脱，有漏、无明漏心解脱，解脱已，便知解脱，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。婆私咤！如是此三种广分别也。梵天帝主说此偈曰：

The noble one of the kṣatriya caste, who is the most honored among humans, is said to
be of noble birth and lineage, who seeks learning and practice, is praised by gods and humans.

「『刹利二足尊，　　谓有种族姓，求学明及行，　　彼为天人称。』

Vāseṭṭha, Brahmadatta spoke this verse well, not badly. He sang it well, not badly. He recited it well, not badly. He said:

「婆私咤！梵天帝主善说此偈，非不善也。善歌讽诵，非不善也。善咏语言，非不善也。谓如是说：

The noble one of the kṣatriya caste, who is the most honored among humans, is said to
be of noble birth and lineage, who seeks learning and practice, is praised by gods and humans.

「『刹利二足尊，　　谓有种族姓，求学明及行，　　彼为天人称。』

Why is that? I also say:

「所以者何？我亦如是说：

The noble one of the kṣatriya caste, who is the most honored among humans, is said to
be of noble birth and lineage, who seeks learning and practice, is praised by gods and humans.

「『刹利二足尊，　　谓有种族姓，求学明及行，　　彼为天人称。』」

The Buddha spoke thus. The Venerable Vāsiṭṭha, Bhāradvāja, and the other bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。尊者婆私咤、婆罗婆等，及诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
