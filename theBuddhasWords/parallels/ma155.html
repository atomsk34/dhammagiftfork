MĀ 155

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 12 梵志品

155. （一五五）须达哆经

Parallel to: AN 9.20 / EĀ 27.3

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the householder Sudatta went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at his feet, and sat to one side.

尔时，须达哆居士往诣佛所，稽首作礼，却坐一面。

The World-Honored One asked, "Householder, do you practice giving in your home?"

世尊问曰：「居士家颇行施耶？」

The householder Sudatta replied, "Yes, World-Honored One! We practice giving in our home, but it is only coarse and not good. We offer rice with bran, hemp soup, and a piece of ginger and vegetables."

须达哆居士答曰：「唯然。世尊！家行布施，但为至麤，不能好也。糠饭麻羹，姜菜一片。」

The World-Honored One said, "Householder! Whether one gives coarse food or fine food, one will receive the same reward. Householder! If one practices coarse giving, without faith, intentionally, not with one's own hands, not going oneself, not contemplating, not giving out of faith, not observing the fruits of karma, one should know that one will receive such a reward. One's mind will not desire a good family, a good vehicle, good clothing, good food and drink, or the five sense pleasures. Why is this? Because one practices giving without sincerity. Householder! One should know that one will receive such a reward. Householder! If one practices coarse giving, with faith, intentionally, with one's own hands, going oneself, contemplating, giving out of faith, observing the fruits of karma, one should know that one will receive such a reward. One's mind will desire a good family, a good vehicle, good clothing, good food and drink, and the five sense pleasures. Why is this? Because one practices giving with sincerity. Householder! One should know that one will receive such a reward.

世尊告曰：「居士！若施麤食及施妙食，俱得报耳。居士！若行麤施，不信施、不故施、不自手施、不自往施、不思惟施、不由信施、不观业果报施者，当观如是受报。心不欲得好家，不欲得好乘，不欲得好衣被，不欲得好饮食，不欲得好五欲功德。所以者何？以不至心故行施也。居士！当知受报如是。居士！若行麤施，信施、故施、自手施、自往施、思惟施、由信施、观业果报施者，当观如是受报。心欲得好家，欲得好乘，欲得好衣被，欲得好饮食，欲得好五欲功德。所以者何？以其至心故行施也。居士！当知受报如是。

Householder! If one practices fine giving, without faith, not intentionally, not with one's own hands, not going oneself, not contemplating, not giving out of faith, not observing the fruits of karma, one should know that one will receive such a reward. One's mind will not desire a good family, a good vehicle, good clothing, good food and drink, or the five sense pleasures. Why is this? Because one practices giving without sincerity. Householder! One should know that one will receive such a reward. Householder! If one practices fine giving, with faith, intentionally, with one's own hands, going oneself, contemplating, giving out of faith, observing the fruits of karma, one should know that one will receive such a reward. One's mind will desire a good family, a good vehicle, good clothing, good food and drink, and the five sense pleasures. Why is this? Because one practices giving with sincerity. Householder! One should know that one will receive such a reward.

「居士！若行妙施，不信施、不故施、不自手施、不自往施、不思惟施、不由信施、不观业果报施者，当观如是受报。心不欲得好家，不欲得好乘，不欲得好衣被，不欲得好饮食，不欲得好五欲功德。所以者何？以不至心故行施也。居士！当知受报如是。居士！若行妙施，信施、故施、自手施、自往施、思惟施、由信施、观业果报施者，当观如是受报。心欲得好家，欲得好乘，欲得好衣被，欲得好饮食，欲得好五欲功德。所以者何？以其至心故行施也。居士！当知受报如是。

Householder! In the past, there was a brahman householder named Sūra, who was extremely wealthy and happy, with immeasurable wealth and property, with many precious treasures, livestock, and industries. His practice of giving was like this: he gave away 84,000 golden bowls filled with silver dust, practicing such great giving. He gave away 84,000 silver bowls filled with gold dust, practicing such great giving. He gave away 84,000 golden bowls filled with gold dust, practicing such great giving. He gave away 84,000 silver bowls filled with silver dust, practicing such great giving. He gave away 84,000 elephants, adorned and decorated, covered with white nets, practicing such great giving. He gave away 84,000 horses, adorned and decorated, covered with white nets and golden saddles, practicing such great giving. He gave away 84,000 cows, covered with leather ropes and leather, each producing one bushel of milk, practicing such great giving. He gave away 84,000 women, with beautiful appearances, pleasing to behold, adorned with various precious necklaces, practicing such great giving. Not to mention the other food and drink.

「居士！昔过去时有梵志大长者，名曰随蓝，极大富乐，资财无量，封户食邑多诸珍宝，畜牧产业不可称计。彼行布施其像如是，八万四千金钵盛满碎银，行如是大施。八万四千银钵盛满碎金，行如是大施。八万四千金钵盛满碎金，行如是大施。八万四千银钵盛满碎银，行如是大施。八万四千象，庄珓严饰，白络复上，行如是大施。八万四千马，庄严珓饰，白络金合霏那，行如是大施。八万四千牛，衣绳衣覆，［（壳－一）／牛］之皆得一斛乳汁，行如是大施。八万四千女，姿容端正，覩者欢悦，众宝璎珞，严饰具足，行如是大施。况复其余食噉含消？

Householder! If the brahman Sūra practiced such great giving, and if one were to give food to all the ordinary people in Jambudvīpa, this would be the most supreme giving. Householder! If the brahman Sūra practiced such great giving, and if one were to give food to all the ordinary people in Jambudvīpa, and if one were to give food to one srota-āpanna, this would be the most supreme giving. Householder! If the brahman Sūra practiced such great giving, and if one were to give food to all the ordinary people in Jambudvīpa, and if one were to give food to a hundred srota-āpannas, and if one were to give food to one sakṛd-āgāmin, this would be the most supreme giving. Householder! If the brahman Sūra practiced such great giving, and if one were to give food to all the ordinary people in Jambudvīpa, and if one were to give food to a hundred srota-āpannas, a hundred sakṛd-āgāmins, and if one were to give food to one anāgāmin, this would be the most supreme giving. Householder! If the brahman Sūra practiced such great giving, and if one were to give food to all the ordinary people in Jambudvīpa, and if one were to give food to a hundred srota-āpannas, a hundred sakṛd-āgāmins, a hundred anāgāmins, and if one were to give food to one arhat, this would be the most supreme giving.

「居士！若梵志随蓝行如是大施，若复有施满阎浮场凡夫食者，此于彼施为最胜也。居士！若梵志随蓝行如是大施，及施满阎浮场凡夫人食，若复有施一须陀洹食者，此于彼施最为胜也。居士！若梵志随蓝行如是大施，及施满阎浮场凡夫人食，施百须陀洹食，若复有施一斯陀含食者，此于彼施为最胜也。居士！若梵志随蓝行如是大施，及施满阎浮场凡夫人食，施百须陀洹、百斯陀含食，若复有施一阿那含食者，此于彼施为最胜也。居士！若梵志随蓝行如是大施，及施满阎浮场凡夫人食，施百须陀洹、百斯陀含、百阿那含食，若复有施一阿罗诃食者，此于彼施为最胜也。

Householder! If the brahman Sūra practiced such great giving, and if one were to give food to all the ordinary people in Jambudvīpa, and if one were to give food to a hundred srota-āpannas, a hundred sakṛd-āgāmins, a hundred anāgāmins, a hundred arhats, and if one were to give food to one pratyekabuddha, this would be the most supreme giving. Householder! If the brahman Sūra practiced such great giving, and if one were to give food to all the ordinary people in Jambudvīpa, and if one were to give food to a hundred srota-āpannas, a hundred sakṛd-āgāmins, a hundred anāgāmins, a hundred arhats, a hundred pratyekabuddhas, and if one were to give food to one Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, this would be the most supreme giving. Householder! If the brahman Sūra practiced such great giving, and if one were to give food to all the ordinary people in Jambudvīpa, and if one were to give food to a hundred srota-āpannas, a hundred sakṛd-āgāmins, a hundred anāgāmins, a hundred arhats, a hundred pratyekabuddhas, and if one were to build a dwelling and give it to the bhikṣu saṃgha of the four directions, this would be the most supreme giving.

「居士！若梵志随蓝行如是大施，及施满阎浮场凡夫人食，施百须陀洹、百斯陀含、百阿那含、百阿罗诃食，若复有施一辟支佛食者，此于彼施为最胜也。居士！若梵志随蓝行如是大施，及施满阎浮场凡夫人食，施百须陀洹、百斯陀含、百阿那含、百阿罗诃、百辟支佛食，若复有施一如来、无所著、等正觉食者，此于彼施为最胜也。居士！若梵志随蓝行如是大施，及施满阎浮场凡夫人食，施百须陀洹、百斯陀含、百阿那含、百阿罗诃、百辟支佛食，若有作房舍，施四方比丘众者，此于彼施为最胜也。

Householder! If the brahman Sūra practiced such great giving, and if one were to give food to all the ordinary people in Jambudvīpa, and if one were to give food to a hundred srota-āpannas, a hundred sakṛd-āgāmins, a hundred anāgāmins, a hundred arhats, a hundred pratyekabuddhas, and if one were to build a dwelling and give it to the bhikṣu saṃgha of the four directions, and if one were to joyfully take refuge in the Three Jewels - the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṃgha - and receive the precepts, this would be the most supreme giving. Householder! If the brahman Sūra practiced such great giving, and if one were to give food to all the ordinary people in Jambudvīpa, and if one were to give food to a hundred srota-āpannas, a hundred sakṛd-āgāmins, a hundred anāgāmins, a hundred arhats, a hundred pratyekabuddhas, and if one were to build a dwelling and give it to the bhikṣu saṃgha of the four directions, and if one were to joyfully take refuge in the Three Jewels - the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṃgha - and receive the precepts, and if one were to cultivate a mind of loving-kindness for all beings, even for the time it takes to milk a cow, this would be the most supreme giving.

「居士！若梵志随蓝行如是大施，及施满阎浮场凡夫人食，施百须陀洹、百斯陀含、百阿那含、百阿罗诃、百辟支佛食，作房舍施四方比丘众，若有欢喜心归命三尊佛、法、比丘众及受戒者，此于彼施为最胜也。居士！若梵志随蓝行如是大施，及施满阎浮场凡夫人食，施百须陀洹、百斯陀含、百阿那含、百阿罗诃、百辟支佛食，作房舍施四方比丘众，欢喜心归命三尊佛、法、比丘众及受戒，若有为彼一切众生行于慈心，乃至［（壳－一）／牛］牛顷者，此于彼施为最胜也。

Householder! If the brahman Sūra practiced such great giving, and if one were to give food to all the ordinary people in Jambudvīpa, and if one were to give food to a hundred srota-āpannas, a hundred sakṛd-āgāmins, a hundred anāgāmins, a hundred arhats, a hundred pratyekabuddhas, and if one were to build a dwelling and give it to the bhikṣu saṃgha of the four directions, and if one were to joyfully take refuge in the Three Jewels - the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṃgha - and receive the precepts, and if one were to cultivate a mind of loving-kindness for all beings, even for the time it takes to milk a cow, and if one were able to contemplate all dharmas as impermanent, suffering, empty, and non-self, this would be the most supreme giving.

「居士！若梵志随蓝行如是大施，及施满阎浮场凡夫人食，施百须陀洹、百斯陀含、百阿那含、百阿罗诃、百辟支佛食，作房舍施四方比丘众，欢喜心归命三尊佛、法、比丘众及受戒，为一切众生行于慈心，乃至［（壳－一）／牛］牛顷，若有能观一切诸法无常、苦、空及非神者，此于彼施为最胜也。

What do you think, householder? Was the brahman Sūra of old a different person? Do not think so. Why? Know that I was the brahman Sūra of old. Householder! At that time, I benefited myself, benefited others, benefited many people, had compassion for the world, sought benefit and happiness for gods and humans. At that time, the teaching did not reach the ultimate, did not reach the ultimate purity, did not reach the ultimate practice of the holy life, did not reach the ultimate completion of the holy life. At that time, I was not free from birth, old age, sickness, death, wailing, sorrow, and lamentation, and I was not yet able to escape all suffering.

「于居士意云何？昔时梵志大长者名随蓝者，谓异人耶？莫作斯念。所以者何？当知即是我也。我昔为梵志大长者，名曰随蓝。居士！我于尔时为自饶益，亦饶益他，饶益多人，愍伤世间，为天、为人求义及饶益，求安隐快乐。尔时说法不至究竟，不究竟白净、不究竟梵行、不究竟梵行讫。尔时，不离生老病死、啼哭忧戚，亦未能得脱一切苦。

Householder! Now I have appeared in the world as a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, Perfect in Knowledge and Conduct, Well-Gone, Knower of the Secular World, Unsurpassed Trainer of those who can be trained, Teacher of gods and men, Buddha, Blessed One. Now I benefit myself, benefit others, benefit many people, have compassion for the world, seek benefit and happiness for gods and humans. Now I teach the Dharma that reaches the ultimate, reaches the ultimate purity, reaches the ultimate practice of the holy life, reaches the ultimate completion of the holy life. Now I am free from birth, old age, sickness, death, wailing, sorrow, and lamentation, and now I have attained liberation from all suffering.

「居士！我今出世如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐。我今自饶益，亦饶益他，饶益多人，愍伤世间，为天、为人求义及饶益，求安隐快乐。我今说法得至究竟，究竟白净、究竟梵行、究竟梵行讫。我今已离生老病死、啼哭忧戚，我今已得脱一切苦。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The householder Sudatta and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。须达哆居士及诸比丘。闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
