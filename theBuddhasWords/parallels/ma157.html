MĀ 157

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 12 梵志品

157. （一五七）黄芦园经

Parallel to: AN 8.11

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the Venuvana, in the Yellow Reed Grove.

一时，佛游鞞兰若，在黄芦园中。

At that time, the Venuvana Brahmin was old and aged, his life was about to end, and he was one hundred and twenty years old, walking with a cane. In the middle of the day, he leisurely went to where the Buddha was, exchanged greetings, and stood leaning on his cane in front of the Buddha. He said, "Gautama! I have heard that the Shakyamuni Gautama is very young and newly ordained. If there are famous and virtuous shamans and Brahmins who come to visit, he does not pay respect, honor, or rise from his seat to invite them to sit. Gautama! This is very unacceptable."

尔时，鞞兰若梵志年耆宿老，寿将欲过，命埀至尽，年百二十，拄杖而行。中后彷徉，往诣佛所，共相问讯，当在佛前倚杖而立，白曰：「瞿昙！我闻沙门瞿昙年幼极少，新出家学。若有名德沙门梵志亲自来诣，而不礼敬，亦不尊重，不从坐起，不请令坐。瞿昙！此事大为不可。」

The World Honored One said, "Brahmin! I have never seen any god, demon, Brahma, shamans, or Brahmins, from humans to gods, who came to me and could make the Tathāgata pay respect, honor, rise from his seat, and invite them to sit. Brahmin! If there is someone who comes to make the Tathāgata pay respect, honor, rise from his seat, and invite them to sit, their head will surely split into seven pieces."

世尊告曰：「梵志！我初不见天及魔、梵、沙门、梵志，从人至天，谓自来诣，能令如来礼敬尊重，而从坐起，请令坐者。梵志！若有来诣，欲令如来礼敬尊重，而从坐起，请令坐者，彼人必当头破七分。」

The Brahmin again said, "Gautama is tasteless."

梵志复白：「瞿昙无味。」

The World Honored One said, "Brahmin! There is something that makes me tasteless, but it is not as you say. If there is the taste of form, the taste of sound, the taste of smell, and the taste of touch, the Tathāgata has cut off that knowledge, extinguished it, uprooted it, and it will never grow again. This is what makes me tasteless, but it is not as you say."

世尊告曰：「梵志！有事令我无味，然不如汝言。若有色味、声味、香味、触味者，彼如来断智绝灭拔根终不复生，是谓有事令我无味，然不如汝言。」

The Brahmin again said, "Gautama is without fear."

梵志复白：「瞿昙无恐怖。」

The World Honored One said, "Brahmin! There is something that makes me fearless, but it is not as you say. If there is fear of form, fear of sound, smell, taste, and touch, the Tathāgata has cut off that knowledge, extinguished it, uprooted it, and it will never grow again. This is what makes me fearless, but it is not as you say."

世尊告曰：「梵志！有事令我无恐怖，然不如汝言。若有色恐怖，声、香、味、触恐怖者，彼如来断智绝灭拔根终不复生，是谓有事令我无恐怖，然不如汝言。」

The Brahmin again said, "Gautama does not enter the womb."

梵志复白：「瞿昙不入胎。」

The World Honored One said, "Brahmin! There is something that makes me not enter the womb, but it is not as you say. If there are any ascetics or Brahmins who will cut off that knowledge, extinguish it, uproot it, and it will never grow again, I say they will not enter the womb. The Tathāgata will cut off that knowledge, extinguish it, uproot it, and it will never grow again, therefore I will not enter the womb. This is what makes me not enter the womb, but it is not as you say.

世尊告曰：「梵志！有事令我不入胎，然不如汝言。若有沙门、梵志当来胎床，断智绝灭拔根终不复生者，我说彼不入胎，如来当来胎床，断智绝灭拔根终不复生，是故令我不入胎。是谓有事令我不入胎，然不如汝言。

Brahmin! I have come to these beings with ignorance, delighting in ignorance, covered by ignorance, wrapped in the egg of ignorance. I first contemplated the Dharma, and I am the foremost among these beings. It is like a hen hatching eggs, whether ten or twelve, constantly thinking, constantly covering, constantly warming, constantly protecting. Later, if the hen becomes negligent, among those eggs, there may be a chick that pecks through the egg with its beak or claws and safely emerges. It is the foremost among those eggs. In the same way, I have come to these beings with ignorance, delighting in ignorance, covered by ignorance, wrapped in the egg of ignorance. I first contemplated the Dharma, and I am the foremost among these beings.

「梵志！我于此众生无明来，无明乐，无明复，无明卵之所裹，我先观法，我于众生为最第一。犹鸡生卵，或十或十二，随时念，随时覆，随时暖，随时拥护。彼于其后，鸡设放逸，于中有鸡子，或以口嘴，或以足爪，啄破其卵，安隐自出，彼于鸡子为最第一。我亦如是，于此众生无明来，无明乐、无明复，无明卵之所裹，我先观法，我于众生为最第一。

Brahmin! I took a bundle of mugwort and went to the Bodhi tree. I spread the grass under the tree, spread out my sitting mat, and sat cross-legged. I did not break my proper sitting until I attained the exhaustion of the taints. I did not break my proper sitting until I attained the exhaustion of the taints. Having sat properly, I was free from desire, free from unwholesome states, with initial and sustained thought, and attained the joy and bliss born of seclusion, and dwelt having attained the first absorption. This is called my attainment of the first supreme state of mind at that time. In this very life, I attained peaceful dwelling, which was easy to attain, not difficult. I dwelt happily, without fear, peacefully and blissfully, and was led to nirvana.

「梵志！我持蒿草往诣觉树，布草树下，敷尼师檀，结加趺坐，不破正坐，要至漏尽，我不破正坐，要至漏尽。我正坐已，离欲、离恶不善之法，有觉、有观，离生喜、乐，逮初禅成就游，是谓我尔时获第一增上心，即于现法得安乐居，易不难得，乐住无怖，安隐快乐，令升涅槃。

Furthermore, Brahmin! Having stilled initial and sustained thought, with inner tranquility and one-pointedness of mind, free from initial and sustained thought, with concentration born of seclusion, joy, and bliss, I attained the second absorption and dwelt having attained it. This is called my attainment of the second supreme state of mind at that time. In this very life, I attained peaceful dwelling, which was easy to attain, not difficult. I dwelt happily, without fear, peacefully and blissfully, and was led to nirvana.

「复次，梵志！我觉、观已息，内静、一心，无觉、无观，定生喜、乐，逮第二禅成就游，是谓我尔时获第二增上心，即于现法得安乐居，易不难得，乐住无怖，安隐快乐，令升涅槃。

Furthermore, Brahmin! Having been free from desire for joy, I dwelt without seeking, with right mindfulness and right knowledge, and experienced bodily bliss, which is said to be the noble relinquishment, mindfulness, bliss, and emptiness of the noble ones. I attained the third absorption and dwelt having attained it. This is called my attainment of the third supreme state of mind at that time. In this very life, I attained peaceful dwelling, which was easy to attain, not difficult. I dwelt happily, without fear, peacefully and blissfully, and was led to nirvana.

「复次，梵志！我离于喜欲舍无求游，正念正智而身觉乐，谓圣所说，圣所舍、念、乐住、空，逮第三禅成就游，是谓我尔时获第三增上心，即于现法得安乐居，易不难得，乐住无怖，安隐快乐，令升涅槃。复次，梵志！我乐灭、苦灭，喜、忧本已灭，不苦不乐、舍、念、清净，逮第四禅成就游，是谓我尔时获第四增上心，即于现法得安乐居，易不难得，乐住无怖，安隐快乐，令升涅槃。

Furthermore, Brahmin! Having attained such a concentrated mind, pure, undefiled, pliant, well-established, immovable, I attained the knowledge and vision of the recollection of past lives. With practice and characteristics, I recollected countless past lives, namely one birth, two births, a hundred births, a thousand births, an eon of world formation, an eon of world destruction, countless eons of world formation and destruction. I was named so-and-so, I was born there, with such a family, such a name, such a birth, such food, such experience of suffering and happiness, such a long life, such a long stay, such a life span. I died here and was born there, died there and was born here, born here with such a family, such a name, such a birth, such food, such experience of suffering and happiness, such a long life, such a long stay, such a life span. This is called my first clear knowledge attained in the first watch of the night. Due to the absence of heedlessness, delighting in seclusion, practicing diligently, namely the extinction of ignorance and the arising of knowledge, the destruction of darkness and the arising of light, the extinction of ignorance and the arising of knowledge, namely the clear knowledge and vision of the recollection of past lives.

「复次，梵志！我已得如是定心清净，无秽无烦，柔软善住，得不动心，觉忆宿命智通作证。我有行有相貌，忆本无量昔所经历，谓一生、二生、百生、千生、成劫、败劫、无量成败劫，彼众生名某，彼昔更历，我曾生彼，如是姓、如是字、如是生、如是饮食、如是受苦乐、如是长寿、如是久住、如是寿讫，此死生彼，彼死生此，我生在此，如是姓、如是字、如是生、如是饮食、如是受苦乐、如是长寿、如是久住、如是寿讫，是谓我尔时初夜得此第一明达。以本无放逸，乐住远离，修行精懃，谓无智灭而智生，暗坏而明成，无明灭而明生，谓忆宿命智作证明达。

Furthermore, Brahmin! Having attained such a concentrated mind, pure, undefiled, pliant, well-established, immovable, I attained the knowledge and vision of the extinction of the taints. With my purified divine eye, surpassing that of humans, I saw this assembly of beings dying and being born, of good color and bad color, of good and bad appearance, going to good and bad places, according to the deeds of these beings, I saw it as it really is. If these beings have practiced bad conduct of body, speech, and mind, have slandered the noble ones, and have wrong views, they have practiced wrong view. Because of that, when their body breaks up and their life ends, they will certainly go to a bad place, to a hell. If these beings have practiced good conduct of body, speech, and mind, have not slandered the noble ones, and have right views, they have practiced right view. Because of that, when their body breaks up and their life ends, they will certainly go to a good place, to a heavenly realm. This is called my second clear knowledge attained in the middle watch of the night. Due to the absence of heedlessness, delighting in seclusion, practicing diligently, namely the extinction of ignorance and the arising of knowledge, the destruction of darkness and the arising of light, the extinction of ignorance and the arising of knowledge, namely the knowledge and vision of the extinction of the taints.

「复次，梵志！我已得如是定心清净，无秽无烦，柔软善住，得不动心，学于生死智通作证，我以清净天眼出过于人，见此众生死时生时，好色恶色，妙与不妙，往来善处及不善处，随此众生之所作业，见其如真。若此众生成就身恶行] cf.［丽】，口、意恶行，诽谤圣人，邪见，成就邪见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。若此众生成就身妙行，口、意妙行，不诽谤圣人，正见，成就正见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必升善处，上生天中。是谓我尔时中夜得此第二明达。以本无放逸，乐住远离，修行精勤，谓无智灭而智生，暗坏而明成，无明灭而明生，谓生死智作证明达。

"Furthermore, Brahmin! Having attained such a concentrated mind, pure, undefiled, pliant, well-established, immovable, I attained the knowledge and vision of the extinction of the taints. I knew this suffering as it really is, I knew the origin of this suffering, I knew the cessation of this suffering, I knew the path to the cessation of this suffering. I knew these taints as they really are, I knew the origin of these taints, I knew the cessation of these taints, I knew the path to the cessation of these taints. Knowing and seeing thus, my mind was liberated from the outflow of desire, from the outflow of existence, and from the outflow of ignorance. Being liberated, I knew I was liberated. Birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, there will not be another existence." This is called my third clear knowledge attained in the last watch of the night. Due to the absence of heedlessness, delighting in seclusion, practicing diligently, namely the extinction of ignorance and the arising of knowledge, the destruction of darkness and the arising of light, the extinction of ignorance and the arising of knowledge, namely the knowledge and vision of the extinction of the taints.

「复次，梵志！我已得如是定心清净，无秽无烦，柔软善住，得不动心，学于漏尽智通作证，我知此苦如真，知此苦习、知此苦灭，知此苦灭道如真。知此漏如真，知此漏习、知此漏灭，知此漏灭道如真。我如是知、如是见，欲漏心解脱，有漏、无明漏心解脱，解脱已，便知解脱，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。是谓我尔时后夜得此第三明达。以本无放逸，乐住远离，修行精勤，谓无智灭而智生，暗坏而明成，无明灭而明生，谓漏尽智作证明达。

"Furthermore, Brahmin! If there is right speech that speaks of the Dharma of non-delusion, and sentient beings are born into the world, they are the most excellent of all sentient beings, not obscured by suffering or pleasure. Know that the speaker of right speech is me. Why is that? I speak of the Dharma of non-delusion, and sentient beings are born into the world, they are the most excellent of all sentient beings, not obscured by suffering or pleasure."

「复次，梵志！若有正说而说不痴法，众生生世，一切众生最胜，不为苦乐所覆，当知正说者即是我也。所以者何？我说不痴法，众生生世，一切众生最胜，不为苦乐所覆。」

Then the Brahmin Vilanaka immediately put down his staff, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and said to the World-Honored One: "The World-Honored One is the foremost, the World-Honored One is the greatest, the World-Honored One is the best, the World-Honored One is the superior, the World-Honored One is the equal, the World-Honored One is the unequal, the World-Honored One is without equal, the World-Honored One is unobstructed, the World-Honored One is unobstructed for others. World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha of monks. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept me as a lay follower. From this day forth, until the end of my life, I take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha of monks, until the end of my life."

于是，鞞兰若梵志即便舍杖，稽首佛足，白世尊曰：「世尊为第一，世尊为大，世尊为最，世尊为胜，世尊为等，世尊为不等，世尊无与等等，世尊无障，世尊无障人。世尊！我今自归于佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The Brahmin Vilanaka and the monks, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。鞞兰若梵志及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
