MĀ 158

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 12 梵志品

158. （一五八）头那经

Parallel to: AN 5.192

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the Brāhman Dhona was wandering about in the middle of the day, and went to where the Buddha was. They exchanged greetings and Dhona sat down to one side. The World Honored One asked, "Dhona, if someone asks you if you are a Brāhman, how would you describe yourself?"

尔时，头那梵志中后彷徉，往诣佛所，共相问讯，却坐一面，世尊问曰：「头那！若有问汝是梵志耶？汝梵志汝自称说。」

The Brāhman Dhona replied, "Gautama, if one truly describes a Brāhman, it is someone who is raised by parents, born pure, with parents for seven generations without interruption, born without evil, learned and retentive, reciting the four Vedas, and deeply understanding the five statements of cause, condition, truth, text, and play. Gautama, I am truly a Brāhman. Why is that? I am raised by parents, born pure, with parents for seven generations without interruption, born without evil, learned and retentive, reciting the four Vedas, and deeply understanding the five statements of cause, condition, truth, text, and play."

梵志头那答曰：「瞿昙！若有正称说梵志者，为父母所举，受生清净，乃至七世父母不绝种族，生生无恶，博闻总持，诵过四典经，深达因、缘、正、文、戏五句说。瞿昙！正称说梵志者，即是我也。所以者何？我为父母所举，受生清净，乃至七世父母不绝种族，生生无恶，博闻总持，诵过四典经，深达因、缘、正、文、戏五句说。」

The World Honored One said, "Dhona, I will ask you a question. Please answer as you see fit. Dhona, what do you think? If there were Brāhman ascetics in the past who passed away after their life ended, who recited and upheld the scriptures, spread the scriptures, and recited the scriptures, namely, the Yatha, the Bhama, the Bhamadeva, the Visamitra, the Yathaganina, the Angirasa, the Vasishtha, the Kasyapa, the Bharadvaja, and the Bhavya, these five kinds of Brāhman ascetics were established. Which of these five kinds of Brāhman ascetics are you like?"

世尊告曰：「头那！我今问汝，随所解答。头那！于意云何？若昔有梵志寿终命过，诵持经书，流布经书，诵习典经，所谓夜咤、婆摩、婆摩提婆、毗奢蜜哆逻、夜陀揵尼、应疑罗娑、婆私咤、迦叶、婆罗婆、婆和，谓此施设五种梵志，有梵志犹如梵，有梵志似如天，有梵志不越界，有梵志越界，有梵志旃茶罗第五。头那！此五种梵志，汝为似谁？」

Dhona said, "Gautama, please briefly explain this meaning without going into detail. I cannot understand it. I only wish that the Śramaṇa Gautama would explain it well so that I may understand the meaning."

头那白曰：「瞿昙略说此义，不广分别，我不能知。唯愿沙门瞿昙善说，令我知义。」

The World Honored One said, "Dhona, listen carefully and think about it well. I will explain it in detail for you."

世尊告曰：「头那！谛听，善思念之，我当为汝广分别说。」

Dhona said, "Yes, Gautama!" The Dhona Brahmins listened to the teachings.

头那白曰：「唯然。瞿昙！」头那梵志受教而听。

The Buddha said, "Dhona, how is a brahmin like Brahma? If a brahmin is raised by his parents, born pure, and his parents' lineage is unbroken for seven generations, and he has no evil in his births, he practices the brahmacarya for forty-eight years, wishing to obtain the scriptures and recite the sutras. Having obtained the scriptures and recited the sutras, he seeks wealth to support his teacher, in accordance with the Dharma, not contrary to the Dharma. How is it contrary to the Dharma? Not farming, not commerce, not writing, not calculation, not counting, not sealing, not calligraphy, not literature, not sutras, not poetry, not using weapons, not serving the king. Seeking wealth in accordance with the Dharma, he offers the wealth to support his teacher. Having made the offering, his mind is filled with loving-kindness, pervading one direction, and he dwells in accomplishment. Thus, in the second, third, fourth directions, the four intermediate directions, above and below, his mind is filled with loving-kindness, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without contention, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, and he dwells in accomplishment. Thus, with compassion and joy, his mind is filled with equanimity, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without contention, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, and he dwells in accomplishment. Dhona, such a brahmin is like Brahma.

佛言：「头那！云何梵志犹如梵耶？若有梵志为父母所举，受生清净，乃至七世父母不绝种族，生生无恶，彼四十八年行童子梵行，欲得经书，诵习典经。彼得经书，诵习典经已，为供养师求乞财物，如法，非不如法。云何不如法？非田作，非治生，非书，非算，非数，非印，非手笔，非文章，非经，非诗，非以刀杖，非王从事。如法求乞，求乞财物供养于师，布施财物已，心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游。如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。头那！如是梵志犹如梵也。

Dhona, how is a brahmin like the gods? If a brahmin is raised by his parents, born pure, and his parents' lineage is unbroken for seven generations, and he has no evil in his births, he practices the brahmacarya for forty-eight years, wishing to obtain the scriptures and recite the sutras. Having obtained the scriptures and recited the sutras, he seeks wealth to support his teacher, in accordance with the Dharma, not contrary to the Dharma. How is it contrary to the Dharma? Not farming, not commerce, not writing, not calculation, not counting, not sealing, not calligraphy, not literature, not sutras, not poetry, not using weapons, not serving the king. Seeking wealth in accordance with the Dharma, he offers the wealth to support his teacher. Having made the offering, he practices good conduct with the body, speech, and mind. Having practiced good conduct with the body, speech, and mind, he, due to this cause and condition, upon the dissolution of the body and the end of life, will certainly ascend to a good realm of existence and be reborn in a heavenly realm. Dhona, such a brahmin is like the gods.

「头那！云何梵志似如天耶？若有梵志为父母所举，受生清净，乃至七世父母不绝种族，生生无恶。彼四十八年行童子梵行，欲得经书，诵习典经，彼得经书，诵习典经已，为供养师求乞财物，如法，非不如法。云何不如法？非田作，非治生，非书，非算，非数，非印，非手笔，非文章，非经，非诗，非以刀杖，非王从事。如法求乞，求乞财物供养于师，布施财物已，行身妙行，口、意妙行，行身妙行，口、意妙行已，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必升善处，上生天中。头那！如是梵志似如天也。

Dhona, how is a brahmin not beyond the boundaries? If a brahmin is raised by his parents, born pure, and his parents' lineage is unbroken for seven generations, and he has no evil in his births, he practices the brahmacarya for forty-eight years, wishing to obtain the scriptures and recite the sutras. Having obtained the scriptures and recited the sutras, he seeks wealth to support his teacher, in accordance with the Dharma, not contrary to the Dharma. How is it contrary to the Dharma? Not farming, not commerce, not writing, not calculation, not counting, not sealing, not calligraphy, not literature, not sutras, not poetry, not using weapons, not serving the king. Seeking wealth in accordance with the Dharma, he offers the wealth to support his teacher. Having made the offering, he seeks a wife for himself, in accordance with the Dharma, not contrary to the Dharma. How is it contrary to the Dharma? A brahmin does not have such intentions towards a brahmin woman, causing them to love each other, to unite and come together. He seeks a brahmin woman, not a non-brahmin woman, nor a kshatriya woman, who is not pregnant, who does not give birth. Dhona, why does a brahmin not seek a pregnant woman? So that the man and woman are not called impure and lustful. Therefore, a brahmin does not seek a pregnant woman. Dhona, why does a brahmin not seek a woman who gives birth? So that the man and woman are not called impure and angry. Therefore, a brahmin does not seek a woman who gives birth. Dhona, what they seek is not for wealth, not for pride, not for adornment, not for decoration, but only for children. Having given birth to a child, if there are the vows, places, and boundaries of the brahmins, they abide in them, uphold them, and do not go beyond them. Dhona, such a brahmin is not beyond the boundaries.

「头那！云何梵志不越界耶？若有梵志为父母所举，受生清净，乃至七世父母不绝种族，生生无恶，彼四十八年行童子梵行，欲得经书，诵习典经。彼得经书，诵习典经已，为供养师求乞财物，如法，非不如法。云何不如法？非田作，非治生，非书，非算，非数，非印，非手笔，非文章，非经，非诗，非以刀杖，非王从事。如法求乞，求乞财物供养于师，布施财物已，为自求妻，如法，非不如法。云何不如法？梵志不如是，意向梵志女，令更相爱，相摄合会，彼趣梵志女，非不梵志女，亦非刹利女，不怀姙，不产生。头那！以何等故梵志非趣怀姙？莫令彼男及以女人名不净婬，是故梵志非趣怀姙。头那！以何等故梵志非趣产生？莫令彼男及以女人名不净恚，是故梵志不趣产生。头那！彼所趣向，不为财物，不为憍慠，不为庄严，不为珓餝，但为子故。彼生子已，若有故梵志要誓处所界障，住彼、持彼，不越于彼。头那！如是梵志不越界也。

Dhona, how is a brahmin beyond the boundaries? If a brahmin is raised by his parents, born pure, and his parents' lineage is unbroken for seven generations, and he has no evil in his births, he practices the brahmacarya for forty-eight years, wishing to obtain the scriptures and recite the sutras. Having obtained the scriptures and recited the sutras, he seeks wealth to support his teacher, in accordance with the Dharma, not contrary to the Dharma. How is it contrary to the Dharma? Not farming, not commerce, not writing, not calculation, not counting, not sealing, not calligraphy, not literature, not sutras, not poetry, not using weapons, not serving the king. Seeking wealth in accordance with the Dharma, he offers the wealth to support his teacher. Having made the offering, he seeks a wife for himself, in accordance with the Dharma, not contrary to the Dharma. How is it contrary to the Dharma? A brahmin does not have such intentions towards a brahmin woman, causing them to love each other, to unite and come together. He seeks a brahmin woman, not a non-brahmin woman, nor a kshatriya woman, who is not pregnant, who does not give birth. Dhona, why does a brahmin not seek a pregnant woman? So that the man and woman are not called impure and lustful. Therefore, a brahmin does not seek a pregnant woman. Dhona, why does a brahmin not seek a woman who gives birth? So that the man and woman are not called impure and angry. Therefore, a brahmin does not seek a woman who gives birth. Dhona, what they seek is not for wealth, not for pride, not for adornment, not for decoration, but only for children. Having given birth to a child, if there are the vows, places, and boundaries of the brahmins, they do not abide in them, do not uphold them, and go beyond them. Dhona, such a brahmin is called beyond the boundaries.

「头那！云何梵志为越界耶？若有梵志为父母所举，受生清净，乃至七世父母不绝种族，生生无恶，彼四十八年行童子梵行，欲得经书，诵习典经。彼得经书，诵习典经已，为供养师求乞财物，如法，非不如法。云何不如法？非田作，非治生，非书，非算，非数，非印，非手笔，非文章，非经，非诗，非以刀杖，非王从事。如法求乞，求乞财物供养于师，布施财物已，为自求妻，如法，非不如法。云何不如法？梵志不如是，意向梵志女，令更相爱，相摄合会，彼趣梵志女，非不梵志女，亦非刹利女，不怀姙，不产生。头那！以何等故梵志不趣怀姙？莫令彼男及以女人名不净婬，是故梵志不趣怀姙。头那！以何等故梵志不趣产生？莫令彼男及以女人名不净恚，是故梵志不趣产生。头那！彼所趣向，不为财物，不为憍慠，不为庄严，不为珓餝，但为子故。彼生子已，若有故梵志要誓处所界障，不住止彼，不受持彼，便越于彼。头那！如是梵志名越界也。

"Dhona, how is a brahmin a brahmin outcast? If a brahmin is raised by his parents, born pure, and his parents' lineage is unbroken for seven generations, and he has no evil in his births, he practices the brahmacarya for forty-eight years, wishing to obtain the scriptures and recite the sutras. Having obtained the scriptures and recited the sutras, he seeks wealth to support his teacher, in accordance with the Dharma, not contrary to the Dharma. How is it contrary to the Dharma? Not farming, not commerce, not writing, not calculation, not counting, not sealing, not calligraphy, not literature, not sutras, not poetry, not using weapons, not serving the king. Seeking wealth in accordance with the Dharma, he offers the wealth to support his teacher. Having made the offering, he seeks a wife for himself, in accordance with the Dharma, not contrary to the Dharma. How is it contrary to the Dharma? A brahmin does not have such intentions towards a brahmin woman, causing them to love each other, to unite and come together. He seeks a brahmin woman, not a non-brahmin woman, nor a kshatriya woman, who is not pregnant, who does not give birth. Dhona, why does a brahmin not seek a pregnant woman? So that the man and woman are not called impure and lustful. Therefore, a brahmin does not seek a pregnant woman. Dhona, why does a brahmin not seek a woman who gives birth? So that the man and woman are not called impure and angry. Therefore, a brahmin does not seek a woman who gives birth. Dhona, what they seek is not for wealth, not for pride, not for adornment, not for decoration, but only for children. Having given birth to a child, they engage in activities associated with kings, thieves, and wrong paths, and say: 'Brahmins should do all things. Brahmins are not defiled or stained by this, just as fire burns both the pure and the impure. Brahmins should not do all things. Brahmins are not defiled or stained by this.' Dhona, such a brahmin is a brahmin outcast. Dhona, which of these five kinds of brahmins are you like? "

「头那！云何梵志梵志旃茶罗？若有梵志为父母所举，受生清净，乃至七世父母不绝种族，生生无恶，彼四十八年行童子梵行，欲得经书，诵习典经。彼得经书，诵习典经已，为供养师求乞财物，如法，非不如法。云何不如法？非田作，非治生，非书，非算，非数，非印，非手笔，非文章，非经，非诗，非以刀杖，非王从事。如法求乞，求乞财物供养于师，布施财物已，为自求妻，如法，非不如法。云何不如法？梵志不如是，意向梵志女，令更相爱，相] cf.［丽】摄合会，趣彼梵志女，非不梵志女，亦不刹利女，不怀姙，不产生。头那！以何等故梵志不趣怀姙？莫令彼男及以女人名不净婬，是故梵志不趣怀姙。头那！以何等故梵志不趣产生？莫令彼男及以女人名不净恚，是故梵志不趣产生。头那！彼所趣向，不为财物，不为憍慠，不为庄严，不为珓饰，但为子故。彼生子已，作王相应事、贼相应事、邪道相应事，作如是说：『梵志应作一切事，梵志不以此染著，亦不秽污，犹若如火，净亦烧，不净亦烧。梵志不应作一切事，梵志不以此染著，亦不秽污。』头那！如是梵志梵志旃茶罗。头那！此五种梵志，汝为似谁？」

Dhona said, "Gautama, I am not even as good as the last kind of brahmin outcast you mentioned, let alone the others. World-Honored One, I have understood. Well-Gone One, I have understood. World-Honored One, I now take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha of monks. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept me as a lay follower, from this day forward for as long as I live, taking refuge until the end of my life."

头那白曰：「瞿昙！说此最后梵志旃茶罗者，我尚不及，况复余耶？世尊！我已知。善逝！我已解。世尊！我今自归于佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The brahmin Dhona, having heard the Buddha's words, joyfully accepted and practiced them.

佛说如是。头那梵志闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
