MĀ 16

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 2 业相应品

16. To the Kālāmas （一六）伽蓝经

Parallel to: AN 3.65

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was staying in the Jeta Grove, in Anāthapiṇḍada's Park near Śrāvastī, together with a great assembly of bhikṣus. He went to the village of Kallavāḷa and stayed in the Śiṃśapā Forest north of the village.

一时，佛游伽蓝园，与大比丘众俱，至羇舍子，住羇舍子村北尸摄惒林中。

At that time, the people of Kallavāḷa heard that the śramaṇa Gautama, the son of the Śākya clan, had renounced his family and gone forth to practice the path. He was traveling to the Kallavāḷa Grove with a great assembly of bhikṣus and had arrived at the village of Kallavāḷa, staying in the Śiṃśapā Forest north of the village. It was said of the śramaṇa Gautama, "He has a great reputation that is heard in the ten directions. The śramaṇa Gautama is a Tathāgata, an Arhat, a Perfectly Enlightened One, accomplished in knowledge and conduct, a Sugata, an unsurpassed knower of the world, a driver of tameable people, a teacher of gods and men, a Buddha, a Blessed One. He has realized for himself this world with its gods, Māra, Brahmā, and the generation of recluses and brahmins, and he dwells having personally realized it. He teaches a Dharma that is good in the beginning, good in the middle, and good in the end, with the right meaning and phrasing, revealing a holy life that is perfectly complete and pure. If one sees the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, and pays respect, makes offerings, and serves him, it is a great gain. We should all go together to see the śramaṇa Gautama and pay respect, make offerings, and serve him.

尔时，羇舍子伽蓝人闻沙门瞿昙释种子舍释宗族，出家学道，游伽蓝园，与大比丘众俱，来至此羇舍子，住羇舍子村北尸摄惒林中。彼沙门瞿昙有大名称，周闻十方，沙门瞿昙如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐。彼于此世，天及魔、梵，沙门、梵志，从人至天，自知自觉，自作证成就游。彼若说法，初善、中善、竟亦善，有义有文，具足清净，显现梵行，若见如来、无所著、等正觉，尊重礼拜，供养承事者，快得善利。我等应共往见沙门瞿昙，礼事供养。

Having heard this, the people of Kallavāḷa each went out of Kallavāḷa with their respective retinues, heading north to the Śiṃśapā Forest, wishing to see the World-Honored One, pay respect, and make offerings. Having approached the Buddha, some of the people of Kallavāḷa bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet and then sat to one side; some greeted the Buddha and then sat to one side; some joined their palms towards the Buddha and then sat to one side; some saw the Buddha from afar and then sat in silence. At that time, when the people of Kallavāḷa had each sat down, the Buddha taught them the Dharma, inspiring and uplifting them, bringing them to a state of joy. Having taught them the Dharma with immeasurable skillful means, inspiring and uplifting them, bringing them to a state of joy, he remained silent.

羇舍子伽蓝人闻已，各与等类眷属相随从羇舍子出，北行至尸摄惒林，欲见世尊礼事供养。往诣佛已，彼伽蓝人或稽首佛足，却坐一面；或问讯佛，却坐一面；或叉手向佛，却坐一面；或遥见佛已，默然而坐。彼时，伽蓝人各坐已定，佛为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，默然而住。

At that time, the people of Kallavāḷa, having been taught the Dharma by the Buddha, inspired and uplifted, brought to a state of joy, each rose from their seats, bared their right shoulders, joined their palms towards the Buddha, and said to the World-Honored One, "Gautama! There is a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who comes to the monastery and only praises his own knowledge and insight, while disparaging the knowledge and insight of others. Gautama! There is another śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who comes to the monastery and also praises his own knowledge and insight, while disparaging the knowledge and insight of others. Gautama! Having heard this, we become doubtful: 'Which of these śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa is true and which is false?'"

时，伽蓝人，佛为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，各从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白世尊曰：「瞿昙！有一沙门梵志来诣伽蓝！但自称叹己所知见，而呰毁他所知所见。瞿昙！复有一沙门梵志来诣伽蓝，亦自称叹己所知见，而呰毁他所知所见。瞿昙！我等闻已，便生疑惑：『此沙门梵志何者为实，何者为虚？』」

The World-Honored One said, "Kallavāḷa! Do not give rise to doubt. Why is that? Because of doubt, one gives rise to hesitation. Kallavāḷa! You yourselves do not have pure knowledge regarding whether there is an afterlife or not. Kallavāḷa! You also do not have pure knowledge regarding whether one's actions are sinful or not. Kallavāḷa! Know that there are three kinds of actions that have their roots in habitual tendencies. What are the three? Kallavāḷa! They are greed, which has its roots in habitual tendencies. Kallavāḷa! Hatred and delusion are also actions that have their roots in habitual tendencies. Kallavāḷa! Those who are greedy, being covered by greed, are insatiable in their minds. They may kill living beings, take what is not given, engage in sexual misconduct, speak falsely, or drink alcohol. Kallavāḷa! Those who are hateful, being covered by hatred, are insatiable in their minds. They may kill living beings, take what is not given, engage in sexual misconduct, speak falsely, or drink alcohol. Kallavāḷa! Those who are deluded, being covered by delusion, are insatiable in their minds. They may kill living beings, take what is not given, engage in sexual misconduct, speak falsely, or drink alcohol.

世尊告曰：「伽蓝！汝等莫生疑惑。所以者何？因有疑惑，便生犹豫。伽蓝！汝等自无净智，为有后世，为无后世？伽蓝！汝等亦无净智，所作有罪，所作无罪？伽蓝！当知诸业有三，因习本有。云何] cf.［丽】为三？伽蓝！谓贪是诸业，因习本有。伽蓝！恚及痴是诸业，因习本有。伽蓝！贪者为贪所覆，心无厌足，或杀生，或不与取，或行邪婬，或知已妄言，或复饮酒。伽蓝！恚者为恚所覆，心无厌足，或杀生，或不与取，或行邪婬，或知已妄言，或复饮酒。伽蓝！痴者为痴所覆，心无厌足，或杀生，或不与取，或行邪婬，或知已妄言，或复饮酒。

Kallavāḷa! The well-versed noble disciple abandons killing and refrains from killing. They discard weapons and have a sense of shame and conscience. They have a compassionate mind and benefit all beings, even insects. They purify their minds from killing. Kallavāḷa! The well-versed noble disciple abandons taking what is not given and refrains from taking what is not given. They take only what is given and delight in taking what is given. They are always generous and joyful, without stinginess, not expecting anything in return. They purify their minds from taking what is not given. Kallavāḷa! The well-versed noble disciple abandons sexual misconduct and refrains from sexual misconduct. They diligently cultivate the practice of celibacy, diligently practicing the sublime conduct, pure and undefiled, free from desire and abandoning sexual desire. They purify their minds from sexual misconduct. Kallavāḷa! The well-versed noble disciple abandons false speech and refrains from false speech. They speak the truth and delight in the truth. They abide in the truth without wavering. They are trustworthy and do not deceive the world. They purify their minds from false speech.

「伽蓝！多闻圣弟子离杀断杀，弃舍刀杖，有惭有愧，有慈悲心，饶益一切，乃至蜫虫，彼于杀生净除其心。伽蓝！多闻圣弟子离不与取，断不与取，与之乃取，乐于与取，常好布施，欢喜无悋，不望其报，彼于不与取净除其心。伽蓝！多闻圣弟子离非梵行，断非梵行，勤修梵行，精勤妙行，清净无秽，离欲断婬，彼于非梵行净除其心。伽蓝！多闻圣弟子离妄言，断妄言，真谛言，乐真谛，住真谛不移动，一切可信，不欺世间，彼于妄言净除其心。

Kallavāḷa! The well-versed noble disciple abandons divisive speech and refrains from divisive speech. They do not engage in divisive speech, which breaks apart others. They do not hear something from here and tell it there to break them apart. They do not hear something from there and tell it here to break them apart. They wish to reconcile those who are divided and delight in the harmony of those who are united. They do not form factions, do not enjoy factions, and do not praise factions. They purify their minds from divisive speech. Kallavāḷa! The well-versed noble disciple abandons harsh speech and refrains from harsh speech. If they speak, their words are harsh, coarse, offensive, unpleasant to the ear, disagreeable, hurtful, and disturbing, causing others to lose their composure. They abandon such speech. If they speak, their words are gentle, smooth, pleasant to the ear, agreeable, delightful, bringing peace and joy to others, with a clear voice. They do not frighten others and cause them to lose their composure. They speak such words. They purify their minds from harsh speech. Kallavāḷa! The well-versed noble disciple abandons idle chatter and refrains from idle chatter. They speak at the right time, speak the truth, speak the Dharma, speak the meaning, speak the cessation, delight in speaking the cessation, and speak in accordance with the occasion. They are good teachers and good reprovers. They purify their minds from idle chatter. Kallavāḷa! The well-versed noble disciple abandons covetousness and refrains from covetousness. Their minds are free from contention. Seeing others' wealth and possessions, they do not give rise to covetousness, wishing to obtain them for themselves. They purify their minds from covetousness.

「伽蓝！多闻圣弟子离两舌，断两舌，行不两舌，不破坏他，不闻此语彼，欲破坏此，不闻彼语此，欲破坏彼，离者欲合，合者欢喜，不作群党，不乐群党，不称群党，彼于两舌净除其心。伽蓝！多闻圣弟子离麤言，断麤言，若有所言，辞气麤犷，恶声逆耳，众所不喜，众所不爱，使他苦恼，令不得定，断如是言。若有所说，清和柔润，顺耳入心，可喜可爱，使他安乐，言声具了，不使人畏，令他得定，说如是言，彼于麤言净除其心。伽蓝！多闻圣弟子离绮语，断绮语，时说、真说、法说、义说、止息说，乐止息说，事顺时得宜，善教善诃，彼于绮语净除其心。伽蓝！多闻圣弟子离贪伺，断贪伺，心不怀诤，见他财物诸生活具，不起贪伺，欲令我得，彼于贪伺净除其心。

Kallavāḷa! The well-versed noble disciple abandons anger and refrains from anger. They have a sense of shame and conscience. They have a compassionate mind and benefit all beings, even insects. They purify their minds from anger and resentment. Kallavāḷa! The well-versed noble disciple abandons wrong views and refrains from wrong views. They practice right views and do not have distorted views. They see and speak thus: there is giving, there is sacrifice, there are offerings; there are good and bad deeds and their results; there is this world and the next world; there are mother and father; there are beings who are reborn spontaneously; there are ascetics and brahmins who are well attained, well gone, well-directed, who have attained the highest knowledge, who are well-restrained, well-liberated, and well-attained in wisdom, who have realized for themselves the highest knowledge and dwell in it. They purify their minds from wrong views.

「伽蓝！多闻圣弟子离恚，断恚，有惭有愧，有慈悲心，饶益一切，乃至蜫虫，彼于嫉恚净除其心。伽蓝！多闻圣弟子离邪见，断邪见，行于正见而不颠倒，如是见，如是说，有施有斋，亦有呪说，有善恶业报，有此世彼世，有父有母；世有真人往至善处、善去、善向，此世彼世，自知、自觉、自作证成就游，彼于邪见净除其心。

Thus, Kallavāḷa! The well-versed noble disciple accomplishes pure bodily, verbal, and mental actions. They are free from anger and disputes, have removed sleepiness, are free from arrogance, have cut through doubt and transcended conceit, have right mindfulness and right knowledge, and are free from delusion. Their mind is filled with loving-kindness and pervades one direction, accomplishing the practice. Thus, in the second, third, and fourth directions, in the four intermediate directions, above and below, everywhere, their mind is filled with loving-kindness, free from attachment, resentment, anger, and disputes. It is extremely vast, immeasurable, well-cultivated, and pervades the entire world, accomplishing the practice. Thus, with compassion and joy, their mind is filled with equanimity, free from attachment, resentment, anger, and disputes. It is extremely vast, immeasurable, well-cultivated, and pervades the entire world, accomplishing the practice.

「如是，伽蓝！多闻圣弟子成就身净业，成就口、意净业，离恚离诤，除去睡眠，无调贡高，断疑、度慢，正念正智，无有愚痴，彼心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游。如是，二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是，悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。

"Thus, Kallavāḷa! The well-versed noble disciple, with a mind free from attachment, resentment, anger, and disputes, attains four peaceful abodes. What are the four? There is this world and the next world, there are good and bad deeds and their results. I have attained this right view and its associated actions, and I uphold them fully. When my body perishes and my life ends, I will surely go to a good place and be reborn in heaven. Thus, Kallavāḷa! The well-versed noble disciple, with a mind free from attachment, resentment, anger, and disputes, attains the first peaceful abode. Furthermore, Kallavāḷa! There is no this world or the next world, no good or bad deeds and their results. Thus, in this present life, I am not criticized by others for this, but am praised by those with right knowledge and diligent people who see rightly. Thus, Kallavāḷa! The well-versed noble disciple, with a mind free from attachment, resentment, anger, and disputes, attains the second peaceful abode. Furthermore, Kallavāḷa! Whatever I do, I will never do evil. I do not think of evil. Why? Since I do not do evil, how can suffering arise? Thus, Kallavāḷa! The well-versed noble disciple, with a mind free from attachment, resentment, anger, and disputes, attains the third peaceful abode. Furthermore, Kallavāḷa! Whatever I do, I will never do evil. I do not violate worldly fears and non-fears. I always have compassion for all beings. My mind does not dispute with beings, and I am joyful without defilement. Thus, Kallavāḷa! The well-versed noble disciple, with a mind free from attachment, resentment, anger, and disputes, attains the fourth peaceful abode. Thus, Kallavāḷa! The well-versed noble disciple, with a mind free from attachment, resentment, anger, and disputes, attains these four peaceful abodes."

「如是，伽蓝！多闻圣弟子心无结无怨，无恚无诤，便得四安隐住处。云何为四？有此世彼世，有善恶业报，我得此正见相应业，受持具足，身坏命终，必至善处，乃生天上。如是，伽蓝！多闻圣弟子心无结无怨，无恚无诤，是谓得第一安隐住处。复次，伽蓝！无此世彼世，无善恶业报，如是我于现法中，非以此故为他所毁，但为正智所称誉，精进人、正见人说其有。如是，伽蓝！多闻圣弟子心无结无怨，无恚无诤，是谓得第二安隐住处。复次，伽蓝！若有所作，必不作恶，我不念恶。所以者何？自不作恶，苦何由生？如是，伽蓝！多闻圣弟子心无结无怨，无恚无诤，是谓得第三安隐住处。复次，伽蓝！若有所作，必不作恶，我不犯世怖与不怖，常当慈愍一切世间，我心不与众生共诤，无浊欢悦。如是，伽蓝！多闻圣弟子心无结无怨，无恚无诤，是谓得第四安隐住处。如是，伽蓝！多闻圣弟子心无结无怨，无恚无诤，是谓得四安隐住处。」

Kallavāḷa said to the World-Honored One, "Thus, Gotama! The well-versed noble disciple, with a mind free from attachment, resentment, anger, and disputes, attains four peaceful abodes. What are the four? There is this world and the next world, there are good and bad deeds and their results. I have attained this right view and its associated actions, and I uphold them fully. When my body perishes and my life ends, I will surely go to a good place and be reborn in heaven. Thus, Gotama! The well-versed noble disciple, with a mind free from attachment, resentment, anger, and disputes, attains the first peaceful abode. Furthermore, Gotama! If there is no this world or the next world, no good or bad deeds and their results, I am not criticized by others in this present life for this, but am praised by those with right knowledge and diligent people who see rightly. Thus, Gotama! The well-versed noble disciple, with a mind free from attachment, resentment, anger, and disputes, attains the second peaceful abode. Furthermore, Gotama! Whatever I do, I will never do evil. I do not think of evil. Why? Since I do not do evil, how can suffering arise? Thus, Gotama! The well-versed noble disciple, with a mind free from attachment, resentment, anger, and disputes, attains the third peaceful abode. Furthermore, Gotama! Whatever I do, I will never do evil. I do not violate worldly fears and non-fears. I always have compassion for all beings. My mind does not dispute with beings, and I am joyful without defilement. Thus, Gotama! The well-versed noble disciple, with a mind free from attachment, resentment, anger, and disputes, attains the fourth peaceful abode. Thus, Gotama! The well-versed noble disciple, with a mind free from attachment, resentment, anger, and disputes, attains these four peaceful abodes. Gotama! I have understood, Well-Gone One! I have understood, World-Honored One! We all take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha. We only wish that the World-Honored One would accept us as lay followers, from this day forward, for as long as we live, taking refuge until the end of our lives."

伽蓝白世尊曰：「如是，瞿昙！多闻圣弟子心无结无怨，无恚无诤，得四安隐住处。云何为四？有此世彼世，有善恶业报，我得此正见相应业，受持具足，身坏命终，必至善处，乃至天上。如是，瞿昙！多闻圣弟子心无结无怨，无恚无诤，是谓得第一安隐住处。复次，瞿昙！若无此世彼世，无善恶业报，我于现法中，非以此故为他所毁，但为正智所称誉，精进人、正见人说其有。如是，瞿昙！多闻圣弟子心无结无怨，无恚无诤，是谓得第二安隐住处。复次，瞿昙！若有所作，必不作恶，我不念恶。所以者何？自不作恶，苦何由生？如是，瞿昙！多闻圣弟子心无结无怨，无恚无诤，是谓得第三安隐住处。复次，瞿昙！若有所作，必不作恶，我不犯世怖与不怖，常当慈愍一切世间，我心不与众生共诤，无浊欢悦。如是，瞿昙！多闻圣弟子心无结无怨，无恚无诤，是谓得第四安隐住处。如是，瞿昙！多闻圣弟子心无结无怨，无恚无诤，是谓得四安隐住处。瞿昙！我已知，善逝，我已解。世尊！我等尽自归佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我等为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。」

The Buddha spoke thus. All the people of Kallavāḷa and the monks, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced them.

佛说如是。一切伽蓝人及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
