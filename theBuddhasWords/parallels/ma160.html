MĀ 160

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 12 梵志品

160. （一六〇）阿兰那经

Parallel to: AN 6.54 (~) / AN 7.66 (~) / AN 7.73–74

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the bhikṣus gathered in the Dharma hall after the midday meal and discussed this matter: "Virtuous ones! It is truly amazing! Human life is extremely short, and one must go to the next world. One should do good deeds and practice the holy life, for there is no birth without death. However, now people in this world do not practice the Dharma, do not practice the meaning, do not practice goodness, and do not practice the sublime, without striving.

尔时，诸比丘于中食后集坐讲堂，论如是事：「诸贤！甚奇！甚奇！人命极少，要至后世，应作善事，应行梵行，生无不死。然今世人于法行、于义行、于善行、于妙行，无为无求。」

At that time, the World-Honored One was in the daytime walking place. With his pure divine ear surpassing that of humans, he heard the bhikṣus gathered in the Dharma hall after the midday meal discussing this matter: "Virtuous ones! It is truly amazing! Human life is extremely short, and one must go to the next world. One should do good deeds and practice the holy life, for there is no birth without death. However, now people in this world do not practice the Dharma, do not practice the meaning, do not practice goodness, and do not practice the sublime, without striving.

彼时，世尊在昼行处，以净天耳出过于人，闻诸比丘于中食后集坐讲堂，论如是事：「诸贤！甚奇！甚奇！人命极少，要至后世，应作善事，应行梵行，生无不死。然今世人于法行、于义行、于善行、于妙行，无为无求。」

Having heard this, the World-Honored One then rose from his afternoon meditation and went to the Dharma hall. He sat in front of the assembly of bhikṣus and asked, "What were you discussing? Why did you gather in the Dharma hall?"

世尊闻已，则于晡时从燕坐起，往诣讲堂，在比丘众前敷座而坐，问诸比丘：「汝论何事？以何等故集坐讲堂？」

The bhikṣus then said, "World-Honored One! After the midday meal, we gathered in the Dharma hall and discussed this matter: 'Virtuous ones! It is truly amazing! Human life is extremely short, and one must go to the next world. One should do good deeds and practice the holy life, for there is no birth without death. However, now people in this world do not practice the Dharma, do not practice the meaning, do not practice goodness, and do not practice the sublime, without striving.' World-Honored One! We discussed this matter together and gathered in the Dharma hall for this reason."

时，诸比丘白曰：「世尊！我等众比丘于中食后集坐讲堂，论如是事。『诸贤！甚奇！甚奇！人命极少，要至后世，应作善事，应行梵行，生无不死。然今世人于法行、于义行、于善行、于妙行，无为无求。』世尊！我等共论此事，以此事故集坐讲堂。」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Bhikṣus! You say, 'Virtuous ones! It is truly amazing! Human life is extremely short, and one must go to the next world. One should do good deeds and practice the holy life, for there is no birth without death. However, now people in this world do not practice the Dharma, do not practice the meaning, do not practice goodness, and do not practice the sublime, without striving.' Why is that? Because I also say, 'It is truly amazing! Human life is extremely short, and one must go to the next world. One should do good deeds and practice the holy life, for there is no birth without death. However, now people in this world do not practice the Dharma, do not practice the meaning, do not practice goodness, and do not practice the sublime, without striving.' Why is that? In the past, there were beings with a lifespan of eighty thousand years. Bhikṣus! When the lifespan of humans was eighty thousand years, this Jambudvīpa was extremely prosperous and joyful, with abundant wealth and treasures. The villages were close together, like a chicken's flight. Bhikṣus! When the lifespan of humans was eighty thousand years, women would marry at the age of five hundred. Bhikṣus! When the lifespan of humans was eighty thousand years, there was only one kind of illness, namely, cold, heat, defecation, urination, desire, hunger, and old age, and no other afflictions.

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！比丘！谓汝作是说：『诸贤！甚奇！甚奇！人命极少，要至后世，应作善事，应行梵行，生无不死。然今世人于法行、于义行、于善行、于妙行，无为无求。』所以者何？我亦如是说：『甚奇！甚奇！人命极少，要至后世，应作善事，应行梵行，生无不死。然今世人于法行、于义行、于善行、于妙行，无为无求。』所以者何？乃过去世时，有众生寿八万岁，比丘！人寿八万岁时，此阎浮洲极大丰乐，饶财珍宝，村邑相近，如鸡一飞。比丘！人寿八万岁时，女年五百乃当出嫁。比丘！人寿八万岁时，唯有如是病，谓寒、热、大小便、欲、不食、老，更无余患。

Bhikṣus! When the lifespan of humans was eighty thousand years, there was a king named Kaurava, who was a wheel-turning king, intelligent and wise, with a fourfold army, ruling the world, self-reliant, a righteous Dharma king who accomplished the seven treasures. Those seven treasures were the wheel treasure, elephant treasure, horse treasure, jewel treasure, queen treasure, householder treasure, and general treasure. These were called the seven. He had a thousand sons, with handsome appearances, brave and fearless, able to subdue other armies. He would certainly rule over all these lands up to the great ocean, without using swords or weapons, but by teaching with the Dharma, bringing them peace.

「比丘！人寿八万岁时，有王名拘牢婆，为转轮王，聪明智慧，有四种军，整御天下，由己自在，如法法王成就七宝。彼七宝者，轮宝、象宝、马宝、珠宝、女宝、居士宝、主兵臣宝，是谓为七。千子具足，颜貌端正，勇猛无畏，能伏他众，必当统领此一切地，乃至大海，不以刀杖，以法教令，令得安隐。

Bhikṣus! King Kaurava had a brāhman named Aranna, a great elder, raised by his parents, pure in birth, up to seven generations of parents without interruption in the lineage, born without evil, learned and retentive, reciting more than the four Vedas, deeply understanding the five kinds of statements on causes, conditions, correctness, texts, and playfulness. Bhikṣus! The brāhman Aranna had countless hundreds of thousands of young brāhmans. The brāhman Aranna taught the scriptures to countless hundreds of thousands of young brāhmans in a place of no affairs.

「比丘！拘牢婆王有梵志，名阿兰那大长者，为父母所举，受生清净，乃至七世父母不绝种族，生生无恶博闻总持，诵过四典经，深达因、缘、正、文、戏五句说。比丘！梵志阿兰那有无量百千摩纳磨，梵志阿兰那为无量百千摩纳磨住一无事处，教学经书。

At that time, the brāhman Aranna lived alone in a quiet place, sitting in meditation and contemplating, thinking in his mind: "How wonderful! How wonderful! Human life is extremely short, and one must go to the next world. One should do good deeds and practice the brahman way of life. There is no birth without death. However, the people of this world do not practice the Dharma, do not practice the meaning, do not practice goodness, and do not practice the wonderful. They do not strive or seek. I should now shave my beard and hair, wear the dyed robes, leave home out of utmost faith, become homeless, and study the path." Then, the brāhman Aranna went to where many young brāhmans were gathered in various countries and said to them: "Young brāhmans! When I lived alone in a quiet place, sitting in meditation and contemplating, I thought in my mind: 'How wonderful! How wonderful! Human life is extremely short, and one must go to the next world. One should do good deeds and practice the brahman way of life. There is no birth without death; however, the people of this world do not practice the Dharma, do not practice the meaning, do not practice goodness, and do not practice the wonderful. They do not strive or seek. I should now shave my beard and hair, wear the dyed robes, leave home out of utmost faith, become homeless, and study the path.' Young brāhmans! What will you do now? "

「尔时，梵志阿兰那独住静处，燕坐思惟，心作是念：『甚奇！甚奇！人命极少，要至后世，应作善事，应行梵行，生无不死。然今世人于法行、于义行、于善行、于妙行，无为无求。我宁可剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。』于是，梵志阿兰那往至若干国众多摩纳磨所，而语彼曰：『诸摩纳磨！我独住静处，燕坐思惟，心作是念：「甚奇！甚奇！人命极少，要至后世，应作善事，应行梵行，生无不死；然今世人于法行、于义行、于善行、于妙行，无为无求。我今宁可剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。诸摩纳磨！我今欲剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。」汝等当作何等？』

The many young brāhmans from various countries said: "Venerable Teacher! All that we know is due to the kindness of the Teacher. If the Venerable Teacher shaves his beard and hair, wears the dyed robes, leaves home out of utmost faith, becomes homeless, and studies the path, we too will shave our beards and hair, wear the dyed robes, leave home out of utmost faith, become homeless, and study the path under that Venerable Teacher." Then, the brāhman Aranna shaved his beard and hair, wore the dyed robes, left home out of utmost faith, became homeless, and studied the path; the many young brāhmans from various countries also shaved their beards and hair, wore the dyed robes, left home out of utmost faith, became homeless, and studied the path under that Venerable Teacher, the brāhman Aranna. This is how the Venerable Teacher Aranna and the Venerable Teacher Aranna's disciples came to be known.

「彼若干国众多摩纳磨白曰：『尊师！我等所知，皆蒙师恩。若尊师剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，我等亦当剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家，从彼尊师出家学道。』于是，梵志阿兰那则于后时剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道；彼若干国众多摩纳磨亦剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家，从彼尊师梵志阿兰那出家学道。是为尊师阿兰那，是为尊师阿兰那弟子名号生也。

At that time, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples, saying: "Young brāhmans! How wonderful! How wonderful! Human life is extremely short, and one must go to the next world. One should do good deeds and practice the brahman way of life. There is no birth without death. However, the people of this world do not practice the Dharma, do not practice the meaning, do not practice goodness, and do not practice the wonderful. They do not strive or seek." At that time, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples, saying: "Young brāhmans! How wonderful! How wonderful! Human life is extremely short, and one must go to the next world. One should do good deeds and practice the brahman way of life. There is no birth without death. However, the people of this world do not practice the Dharma, do not practice the meaning, do not practice goodness, and do not practice the wonderful. They do not strive or seek." Thus, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples.

「尔时，尊师阿兰那为弟子说法：『诸摩纳磨！甚奇！甚奇！人命极少，要至后世，应作善事，应行梵行，生无不死。然今世人于法行、于义行、于善行、于妙行，无为无求。』尔时，尊师阿兰那为弟子说法：『诸摩纳磨！甚奇！甚奇！人命极少，要至后世，应作善事，应行梵行，生无不死。然今世人于法行、于义行、于善行、于妙行，无为无求。』如是尊师阿兰那为弟子说法。

Furthermore, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples, saying: "Young brāhmans! It is like morning dew drops on the grass, which disappear when the sun rises. They are temporary and do not last long. In the same way, young brāhmans! Human life is like morning dew. It is extremely difficult to obtain, has little taste, and is full of great suffering and calamity. The calamities are very many." Thus, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples. Furthermore, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples, saying: "Young brāhmans! It is like when it rains heavily, water drops form bubbles, which sometimes appear and sometimes disappear. In the same way, young brāhmans! Human life is like a bubble. It is extremely difficult to obtain, has little taste, and is full of great suffering and calamity. The calamities are very many." Thus, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples. Furthermore, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples, saying: "Young brāhmans! It is like throwing a stick into the water, which comes out quickly. In the same way, young brāhmans! Human life is like a stick thrown into the water, which comes out quickly. It is extremely difficult to obtain, has little taste, and is full of great suffering and calamity. The calamities are very many." Thus, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples.

「复次，尊师阿兰那为弟子说法：『摩纳磨！犹如朝露渧在草上，日出则消，暂有不久。如是，摩纳磨！人命如朝露，甚为难得，至少少味，大苦灾患，灾患甚多。』如是尊师阿兰那为弟子说法。复次，尊师阿兰那为弟子说法：『摩纳磨！犹大雨时，渧水成泡，或生或灭。如是，摩纳磨！人命如泡，甚为难得，至少少味，大苦灾患，灾患甚多。』如是尊师阿兰那为弟子说法。复次，尊师阿兰那为弟子说法：『摩纳磨！犹如以杖投著水中，还出至速。如是，摩纳磨！人命如杖，投水出速，甚为难得，至少少味，大苦灾患，灾患甚多。』如是尊师阿兰那为弟子说法。

Furthermore, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples, saying: "Young brāhmans! It is like a new clay pot thrown into the water, which quickly floats up and dries out in the wind and heat. In the same way, young brāhmans! Human life is like a new clay pot soaked in water and quickly dried out. It is extremely difficult to obtain, has little taste, and is full of great suffering and calamity. The calamities are very many." Thus, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples. Furthermore, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples, saying: "Young brāhmans! It is like a small piece of meat thrown into a large pot of boiling water, which quickly dissolves. In the same way, young brāhmans! Human life is like meat dissolving. It is extremely difficult to obtain, has little taste, and is full of great suffering and calamity. The calamities are very many." Thus, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples. Furthermore, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples, saying: "Young brāhmans! It is like a captured criminal being led to the execution ground to be killed. With each step they take, they are closer to death, closer to the end of their life. In the same way, young brāhmans! Human life is like a criminal being led to the execution ground to be killed. It is extremely difficult to obtain, has little taste, and is full of great suffering and calamity. The calamities are very many." Thus, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples.

「复次，尊师阿兰那为弟子说法：『摩纳磨！犹新瓦杅，投水即出，著风热中，干燥至速。如是，摩纳磨！人命如新瓦杅，水渍速燥，甚为难得，至少少味，大苦灾患，灾患甚多。』如是尊师阿兰那为弟子说法。复次，尊师阿兰那为弟子说法：『摩纳磨！犹如小段肉著大釜水中，下炽然火，速得消尽。如是，摩纳磨！人命如肉消，甚为难得，至少少味，大苦灾患，灾患甚多。』如是尊师阿兰那为弟子说法。复次，尊师阿兰那为弟子说法：『摩纳磨！犹缚贼送至标下杀，随其举足，步步趣死，步步趣命尽。如是，摩纳磨！人命如贼，缚送标下杀，甚为难得，至少少味，大苦灾患，灾患甚多。』如是尊师阿兰那为弟子说法。

Furthermore, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples, saying: "Young brāhmans! It is like a butcher leading a cow to be killed. With each step the cow takes, it is closer to death, closer to the end of its life. In the same way, young brāhmans! Human life is like a cow being led to be killed. It is extremely difficult to obtain, has little taste, and is full of great suffering and calamity. The calamities are very many." Thus, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples. Furthermore, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples, saying: "Young brāhmans! It is like a loom, which is almost finished with each thread that is woven. In the same way, young brāhmans! Human life is like a loom that is almost finished. It is extremely difficult to obtain, has little taste, and is full of great suffering and calamity. The calamities are very many." Thus, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples. Furthermore, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples, saying: "Young brāhmans! It is like a mountain stream, which flows swiftly and carries away many things. The water flows quickly and does not stop for a moment. In the same way, young brāhmans! Human life passes quickly, without a moment's pause. In the same way, young brāhmans! Human life is like a swiftly flowing stream. It is extremely difficult to obtain, has little taste, and is full of great suffering and calamity. The calamities are very many." Thus, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples.

「复次，尊师阿兰那为弟子说法：『摩纳磨！犹如屠儿牵牛杀之，随其举足，步步趣死，步步趣命尽。如是，摩纳磨！人命如牵牛杀，甚为难得，至少少味，大苦灾患，灾患甚多。』如是尊师阿兰那为弟子说法。复次，尊师阿兰那为弟子说法：『摩纳摩！犹如机织，随其行纬，近成近讫。如是，摩纳磨！人命如机织讫，甚为难得，至少少味，大苦灾患，灾患甚多。』如是尊师阿兰那为弟子说法。复次，尊师阿兰那为弟子说法：『摩纳磨！犹如山水，瀑涱流疾，多有所漂，水流速𫘝，无须臾停。如是，摩纳磨！人寿行速，去无一时住。如是，摩纳磨！人命如𫘝水流，甚为难得，至少少味，大苦灾患，灾患甚多。』如是尊师阿兰那为弟子说法。

Furthermore, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples, saying: "Young brāhmans! It is like throwing a stick into the darkness of night, which may land head down, head up, or on its side, or in a clean place or an unclean place. In the same way, young brāhmans! Beings, covered by ignorance and bound by craving, may be reborn in hell, as animals, as hungry ghosts, in the heavens, or as humans. In the same way, young brāhmans! Human life is like throwing a stick into the darkness. It is extremely difficult to obtain, has little taste, and is full of great suffering and calamity. The calamities are very many." Thus, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples.

「复次，尊师阿兰那为弟子说法：『摩纳磨！犹如夜暗以杖投地，或下头堕地，或上头堕地，或复卧堕，或堕净处，或堕不净处。如是，摩纳磨！众生为无明所覆，为爱所系，或生泥犁，或生畜生，或生饿鬼，或生天上，或生人间。如是，摩纳磨！人命如暗杖投地，甚为难得，至少少味，大苦灾患，灾患甚多。』如是尊师阿兰那为弟子说法。

Furthermore, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples, saying: "Young brāhmans! I have in this world eliminated greed and desire, and have no contention in my mind. Seeing the wealth and possessions of others, I do not give rise to greed and desire, wishing to obtain them for myself. I have purified my mind of greed and desire. In the same way, I have eliminated anger, sloth, and remorse. I have in this world eliminated doubt and confusion, and have no hesitation regarding wholesome dharmas. I have purified my mind of doubt and confusion. Young brāhmans! You should also in this world eliminate greed and desire, and have no contention in your mind. Seeing the wealth and possessions of others, do not give rise to greed and desire, wishing to obtain them for yourselves. You should purify your minds of greed and desire. In the same way, eliminate anger, sloth, and remorse. You should in this world eliminate doubt and confusion, and have no hesitation regarding wholesome dharmas." Thus, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples.

「复次，尊师阿兰那为弟子说法：『摩纳磨！我于世断除贪伺，心无有诤，见他财物诸生活具，不起贪伺，欲令我得，我于贪伺净除其心。如是瞋恚、睡眠、调悔，我于世断疑度惑。于诸善法无有犹豫，我于疑惑净除其心。摩纳磨！汝等于世亦当断除贪伺，心无有诤，见他财物诸生活具，不起贪伺，欲令我得，汝于贪伺净除其心。如是瞋恚、睡眠、调悔，汝于世断疑度惑，于诸善法无有犹豫。』如是尊师阿兰那为弟子说法。

Furthermore, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples, saying: "Young brāhmans! My mind is filled with loving-kindness, pervading one direction, and I dwell having accomplished it. In the same way, the second, third, and fourth directions, the four intermediate directions, above and below, everywhere, my mind is filled with loving-kindness, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without contention, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well cultivated, and I dwell having accomplished it throughout the entire world. In the same way, with compassion and joy, my mind is filled with equanimity, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without contention, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well cultivated, and I dwell having accomplished it throughout the entire world. Young brāhmans! You should also fill your minds with loving-kindness, pervading one direction, and dwell having accomplished it. In the same way, the second, third, and fourth directions, the four intermediate directions, above and below, everywhere, your minds should be filled with loving-kindness, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without contention, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well cultivated, and you should dwell having accomplished it throughout the entire world. In the same way, with compassion and joy, your minds should be filled with equanimity, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without contention, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well cultivated, and you should dwell having accomplished it throughout the entire world." Thus, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma to his disciples.

「复次，尊师阿兰那为弟子说法：『摩纳磨！我心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游。如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。摩纳磨！汝等亦当心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游。如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。』如是尊师阿兰那为弟子说法。

Furthermore, the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma of the Brahma Realm to his disciples. When the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma of the Brahma Realm, if his disciples did not fully practice the Dharma, after their lives ended, they were reborn in the Heaven of the Four Heavenly Kings, the Heaven of the Thirty-three Gods, the Yama Heaven, the Tuṣita Heaven, the Nirmāṇarati Heaven, or the Paranirmitavaśavartin Heaven. When the Venerable Teacher Aranna taught the Dharma of the Brahma Realm, if his disciples fully practiced the Dharma, cultivating the four Brahma abodes, and abandoning desire, after their lives ended, they were reborn in the Brahma Heaven. At that time, the Venerable Teacher Aranna thought: "I should not be reborn in the same place as my disciples in the next life. I should now further cultivate supreme loving-kindness. Having cultivated supreme loving-kindness, I will be reborn in the Heaven of Bright Light after my life ends." The Venerable Teacher Aranna then further cultivated supreme loving-kindness. Having cultivated supreme loving-kindness, he was reborn in the Heaven of Bright Light after his life ended. The Venerable Teacher Aranna and his disciples did not practice the path in vain, and attained great rewards.

「复次，尊师阿兰那为弟子说梵世法：『若尊师阿兰那为说梵世法时，诸弟子等有不具足奉行法者，彼命终已，或生四王天，或生三十三天，或生［火＊佥］摩天，或生兜瑟哆天，或生化乐天，或生他化乐天。若尊师阿兰那为说梵世法时，诸弟子等设有具足奉行法者，修四梵室，舍离于欲，彼命终已，得生梵天。』尔时，尊师阿兰那而作是念：『我不应与弟子等同，俱至后世共生一处，我今宁可更修增上慈，修增上慈已，命终得生晃昱天中。』尊师阿兰那则于后时更修增上慈，修增上慈已，命终得生晃昱天中。尊师阿兰那及诸弟子学道不虚，得大果报。

Monks! What do you think? Was the Venerable Teacher Aranna of that time someone else? Do not think so. Why? Monks! You should know that I was the Venerable Teacher Aranna at that time. At that time, I had countless hundreds of thousands of disciples. At that time, I taught the Dharma of the Brahma Realm to my disciples.

「比丘！于意云何？昔时尊师阿兰那者谓异人耶？莫作斯念。所以者何？比丘，当知即是我也。我于尔时名尊师阿兰那，我于尔时有无量百千弟子，我于尔时为诸弟子说梵世法。

When I taught the Dharma of the Brahma Realm, if my disciples did not fully practice the Dharma, after their lives ended, they were reborn in the Heaven of the Four Heavenly Kings, the Heaven of the Thirty-three Gods, the Yama Heaven, the Tuṣita Heaven, the Nirmāṇarati Heaven, or the Paranirmitavaśavartin Heaven. When I taught the Dharma of the Brahma Realm, if my disciples fully practiced the Dharma, cultivating the four Brahma abodes, and abandoning desire, after their lives ended, they were reborn in the Brahma Heaven. At that time, I thought: "I should not be reborn in the same place as my disciples in the next life. I should now further cultivate supreme loving-kindness." Having cultivated supreme loving-kindness, I was reborn in the Heaven of Bright Light after my life ended. At that time, I and my disciples did not practice the path in vain, and attained great rewards.

「我说梵世法时，诸弟子等有不具足奉行法者，彼命终已，或生四王天，或生三十三天，或生［火＊佥］摩天，或生兜瑟哆天，或生化乐天，或生他化乐天。我说梵世法时，诸弟子等设有具足奉行法者，修四梵室，舍离于欲，彼命终已，得生梵天。我于尔时而作是念：『我不应与弟子等同，俱至后世共生一处，我今宁可更修增上慈。』修增上慈已，命终得生晃昱天中。我于后时更修增上慈，修增上慈已，命终得生晃昱天中，我于尔时及诸弟子学道不虚，得大果报。

At that time, I benefited myself, benefited others, benefited many people, had compassion for the world, and sought benefit and happiness for gods and humans. At that time, my teaching did not reach the ultimate, did not reach the ultimate purity, did not reach the ultimate practice of the Brahma way, and did not reach the ultimate completion of the Brahma way. At that time, I was not free from birth, old age, sickness, death, crying, and sorrow, and I was not yet able to be free from all suffering. Monks! Now I have appeared in the world as a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, Perfect in Knowledge and Conduct, Well-Gone, Knower of the World, Unsurpassed, Tamer of Men, Teacher of Gods and Men, called Buddha, Blessed One. Now I benefit myself, benefit others, benefit many people, have compassion for the world, and seek benefit and happiness for gods and humans. Now my teaching reaches the ultimate, reaches the ultimate purity, reaches the ultimate practice of the Brahma way, and reaches the ultimate completion of the Brahma way. Now I am free from birth, old age, sickness, death, crying, and sorrow, and now I have attained freedom from all suffering.

「我于尔时自饶益，亦饶益他，饶益多人，愍伤世间，为天为人求义及饶益，求安隐快乐。我于尔时说法不至究竟，不究竟白净、不究竟梵行、不究竟梵行讫。我于尔时不离生老病死、啼哭忧戚，亦未能得脱一切苦。比丘！我今出世，如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐。我今自饶益，亦饶益他，饶益多人，愍伤世间，为天为人求义及饶益，求安隐快乐。我今说法得至究竟，究竟白净、究竟梵行、究竟梵行讫。我今已离生老病死、啼哭忧戚，我今已得脱一切苦。

Monks! If there is a correct statement: 'Human life is extremely short, and one must go to the next world. One should do good deeds and practice the Brahma way. There is no birth without death.' Monks! This is the correct statement. Why is that? Now if there is a long life, up to a hundred years, or a little more. If there is a long-lived one, their life lasts three hundred times, a hundred in spring, a hundred in summer, and a hundred in winter. This life lasts one thousand two hundred months, four hundred in spring, four hundred in summer, and four hundred in winter. If the life lasts one thousand two hundred months, it lasts two thousand four hundred half-months, eight hundred in spring, eight hundred in summer, and eight hundred in winter. If the life lasts two thousand four hundred half-months, it lasts thirty-six thousand days and nights, twelve thousand in spring, twelve thousand in summer, and twelve thousand in winter. If the life lasts thirty-six thousand days and nights, it lasts seventy-two thousand meals, with obstacles and breast milk. When there are obstacles, one does not eat due to suffering, anger, illness, or business. One does not eat when going or coming, when visiting the king, on fast days, or when unable to obtain food. This is called a life of one hundred years lasting for one hundred years, one hundred times, one hundred seasons, one hundred years and seasons, one hundred months, one hundred half-months, one hundred months and half-months, one hundred days, one hundred nights, one hundred days and nights, one hundred meals, one hundred obstacles, and one hundred meals with obstacles.

「比丘！若有正说者：『人命极少，要至后世，应行善事，应行梵行，生无不死。』比丘，今是正说。所以者何？今若有长寿，远至百岁，或复小过者。若有长寿者，命存三百时，春时百、夏时百、冬时百，是命存千二百月，春四百] cf.［丽】、夏四百、冬四百，命存千二百月者，命存二千四百半月，春八百、夏八百、冬八百，命存二千四百半月者，三万六千昼夜，春万二千、夏万二千、冬万二千，命存三万六千昼夜者，七万二千食，及障碍及母乳。于有障碍，苦不食，瞋不食，病不食，有事不食，行来不食，至王间不食，斋日不食，不得者不食，是谓比丘一百岁命存百岁数、时数、岁时数、月数、半月数、月半月数、昼数、夜数、昼夜数、食数、障碍数、食障碍数。

Monks! If a teacher has great compassion and pity for his disciples, seeking benefit and happiness for them, I have already done so. You should also do so, going to a place free from affairs, under a tree in a mountain forest, in an empty and quiet place, sitting in meditation and contemplating, do not be negligent, strive diligently, and do not regret it later. This is my instruction and admonition.

「比丘！若有尊师所为弟子起大慈哀，怜念愍伤，求义及饶益，求安隐快乐者，我今已作。汝亦当复作，至无事处、山林树下、空安静处，燕坐思惟，勿得放逸，懃加精进，莫令后悔。此是我之教勅，是我训诲。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those monks, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
