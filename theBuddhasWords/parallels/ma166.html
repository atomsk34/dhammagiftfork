MĀ 166

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 13 根本分别品

166. （一六六）释中禅室尊经

Parallel to: MN 134

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Rāhula was staying in the Śākya clan, in the meditation chamber of the non-afflictive meditation. Then, Venerable Rāhula, as the night was about to turn to dawn, emerged from the meditation chamber and sat in the shade of the open-air meditation chamber, spreading out his sitting mat on the rope bed and sitting cross-legged.

尔时，尊者卢夷强耆游于释中，在无事禅室。于是，尊者卢夷强耆夜将向旦，从彼禅室出，在露地禅室荫中，于绳床上敷尼师檀，结跏趺坐。

At that time, a god of supreme form and appearance, majestic and radiant, came to Venerable Rāhula as the night was about to turn to dawn, bowed his head in reverence, and stood to one side. The god's form and appearance were extremely majestic and radiant, illuminating the meditation chamber. Having stood to one side, the god said to Venerable Rāhula, "Bhikṣu! Do you uphold the verses and their meanings of the Vādirājya?"

尔时，有一天形体极妙，色像巍巍，夜将向旦，往诣尊者卢夷强耆所，稽首作礼，却住一面。彼天色像威神极妙，光明普照于其禅室。彼天却住于一面已，白尊者卢夷强耆曰：「比丘！受持跋地罗帝偈及其义耶？」

Venerable Rāhula replied to the god, "I do not uphold the verses of the Vādirājya, nor do I uphold their meanings."

尊者卢夷强耆答彼天曰：「我不受持跋地罗帝偈，亦不受义。」

He then asked the god, "Do you uphold the verses and their meanings of the Vādirājya?"

寻问彼天：「汝受持跋地罗帝偈及其义耶？」

The god replied, "I uphold the verses of the Vādirājya, but I do not uphold their meanings."

彼天答曰：「我受持跋地罗帝偈，然不受义。」

The Venerable Ruru then asked the god, "How do you uphold the verses of the Vādirājya but not their meanings?"

尊者卢夷强耆复问彼天：「云何受持跋地罗帝偈而不受义耶？」

The god replied, "Once, the World-Honored One was dwelling in the Bamboo Grove Kalandakanivāpa in Rājagṛha. At that time, the World-Honored One spoke the Vādirājya verses to the bhikṣus:

彼天答曰：「一时，世尊游王舍城，住竹林迦兰哆园。尔时，世尊为诸比丘说跋地罗帝偈：

Do not think of the past, nor wish for the
future. The past has already ceased, and the future has
not yet come. The present dharmas should also be contemplated
Thoughts have no strength. The wise perceive thus. If one
practices the path of the noble ones, who knows sorrow
from death? I will not meet them. The great suffering
and calamity will end. Thus practicing diligently, day and night
without laziness, therefore one should always recite the Vādirājya verses.

「『慎莫念过去，　　亦勿愿未来，过去事已灭，　　未来复未至。现在所有法，　　彼亦当为思，念无有坚强，　　慧者觉如是。若学圣人行，　　孰知愁于死，我要不会彼，　　大苦灾患终。如是行精勤，　　昼夜无懈怠，是故常当说，　　跋地罗帝偈。』

"Bhikṣus! I uphold the Vādirājya verses in this way, but I do not uphold their meaning."

「比丘！我如是受持跋地罗帝偈，不受持义。」

The Venerable Ruruqiangqi further asked the god, "Who upholds the Vādirājya verses and their meaning?"

尊者卢夷强耆复问彼天：「谁受持跋地罗帝偈及其义耶？」

The god replied, "The Buddha is staying in Śrāvastī, in the Jeta Grove, Anāthapiṇḍada's Park. He upholds the Vādirājya verses and their meaning. Bhikṣu! You should go and personally receive the Vādirājya verses and their meaning from the World-Honored One. Why is that? The Vādirājya verses and their meaning have meaning and Dharma, are the basis for the holy life, lead to knowledge, lead to awakening, lead to nirvāṇa, and for those of noble family who have perfect faith, leave home, become homeless, and study the path, they should well uphold and recite the Vādirājya verses and their meaning." Having spoken thus, the god bowed his head at the feet of the Venerable Ruruqiangqi, circumambulated him three times, and then disappeared on the spot.

彼天答曰：「佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园，彼受持跋地罗帝偈及其义也。比丘！可往面从世尊，善受持诵跋地罗帝偈及其义也。所以者何？跋地罗帝偈及其义者，有义有法，为梵行本，趣智、趣觉、趣于涅槃，族姓者至信、舍家、无家、学道，当以跋地罗帝偈及其义善受持诵。」彼天说如是，稽首尊者卢夷强耆足，绕三匝已，即彼处没。

Not long after the god disappeared, the Venerable Ruruqiangqi spent the summer retreat among the Śākyas. After three months, having repaired his robes, he put on his robe, took his bowl, and went to Śrāvastī. Traveling along, he arrived at Śrāvastī and stayed in the Jeta Grove, Anāthapiṇḍada's Park.

天没不久，于是，尊者卢夷强耆在释中受夏坐讫。过三月已，补治衣竟，摄衣持钵，往诣舍卫国，展转进前，至舍卫国，住胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the Venerable Ruruqiangqi went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at his feet, sat to one side, and said, "World-Honored One! Once, I was wandering among the Śākyas, in a meditation hut without affairs. World-Honored One! At that time, in the early morning, I came out of the meditation hut, sat in meditation in the shade of the open-air meditation hut, on a rope bed, with my sitting mat spread out, cross-legged. At that time, a god of extremely fine form and majestic appearance came to me in the early morning, bowed his head at my feet, and stood to one side. The god's appearance and spiritual power were extremely fine, and his radiance illuminated the meditation hut. After the god stood to one side, he said to me, 'Bhikṣu! Do you uphold the Vādirājya verses and their meaning?' I replied to the god, 'I do not uphold the Vādirājya verses, nor do I uphold their meaning.' I then asked the god, 'Do you uphold the Vādirājya verses and their meaning?' The god replied, 'I uphold the Vādirājya verses, but I do not uphold their meaning.' I further asked the god, 'How do you uphold the Vādirājya verses but not uphold their meaning?' The god answered me, 'Once, the Buddha was staying in Rājagṛha, at the Bamboo Grove, in the Kalandaka-nivāpa. At that time, the World-Honored One spoke the Vādirājya verses to the bhikṣus:

尔时，尊者卢夷强耆往诣佛所，稽首作礼，却坐一面，白曰：「世尊！我一时游于释中，在无事禅室。世尊！我于尔时夜将向旦，从彼禅室出，在露地禅室荫中，于绳床上敷尼师檀，结跏趺坐。尔时，有一天形体极妙，色像巍巍，夜将向旦，来诣我所，稽首作礼，却住一面。彼天色像威神极妙，光明普照于其禅室。彼天却住于一面已，而白我曰：『比丘！受持跋地罗帝偈及其义耶？』我答彼天：『不受持跋地罗帝偈，亦不受义。』寻问彼天：『汝受持跋地罗帝偈及其义耶？』彼天答曰：『我受持跋地罗帝偈，然不受义。』我复问天：『云何受持跋地罗帝偈而不受义耶？』天答我曰：『一时，佛游王舍城，住竹林迦兰哆园。尔时，世尊为诸比丘说跋地罗帝偈：

Be careful not to think of the past, nor wish
for the future. The past is already extinguished, and the
future has not yet arrived. The present phenomena, they too
should be contemplated. Thoughts have no strength. The wise perceive
it thus. If one practices the path of the noble
ones, who knows sorrow from death? I will not meet
them. The great suffering and calamity will end. Thus practicing
diligently, day and night without laziness, therefore always recite the Vādirājya verses.

「『「慎莫念过去，　　亦勿愿未来，过去事已灭，　　未来复未至。现在所有法，　　彼亦当为思，念无有坚强，　　慧者觉如是。若学圣人行，　　孰知愁于死，我要不会彼，　　大苦灾患终。如是行精勤，　　昼夜不懈怠，是故常当说，　　跋地罗帝偈。」』

"Bhikṣu! I uphold the Vādirājya verses in this way, but do not uphold their meaning." I further asked the god, "Who upholds the Vādirājya verses and their meaning?" The god answered me, "The Buddha was traveling in Śrāvastī, staying in the Jeta Grove, Anāthapiṇḍada's Park. He upholds the Vādirājya verses and their meaning. Bhikṣu! You should go and personally receive the Vādirājya verses and their meaning from the World-Honored One. Why is that? The Vādirājya verses and their meaning have meaning and Dharma, are the basis for the holy life, lead to wisdom, lead to enlightenment, lead to nirvāṇa. Those of noble family who have perfect faith, leave home, become homeless, and study the path, should well uphold and recite the Vādirājya verses and their meaning." The god spoke thus, bowed his head at my feet, circumambulated me three times, and then disappeared right there.

「『比丘！我如是受持跋地罗帝偈，不受持义也。』我复问天：『谁受持跋地罗帝偈及其义耶] cf.［丽】？』天答我曰：『佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园，彼受持跋地罗帝偈及其义也。比丘！可往面从世尊，善受持诵跋地罗帝偈及其义也] cf.［丽】。所以者何？跋地罗帝偈及其义者，有义有法，为梵行本，趣智、趣觉、趣于涅槃，族姓者至信、舍家、无家、学道，当以跋地罗帝偈及其义善受持诵。』彼天说如是，稽首我足，绕三匝已，即彼处没。」

Then the World-Honored One asked Venerable Ruru-jyana, "Do you know where that god came from? What is the name of that god?"

于是，世尊问尊者卢夷强耆：「汝知彼天从何处来？彼天名何耶？」

Venerable Ruru-jyana replied, "World-Honored One! I do not know where that god came from, nor do I know his name."

尊者卢夷强耆答曰：「世尊！我不知彼天从何处来，亦不知名也。」

The World-Honored One said, "Ruru-jyana! That god's name is Panna, and he is the general of the Thirty-three Heavens."

世尊告曰：「强耆！彼天子名般那，为三十三天军将。」

At that time, Venerable Ruru-jyana said, "World-Honored One! Now is the time. Well-Gone One! Now is the time. If the World-Honored One would speak the verses of the Vadhri-raja and their meaning for the monks, the monks would listen well and uphold them."

彼时，尊者卢夷强耆白曰：「世尊！今正是时。善逝！今正是时。若世尊为诸比丘说跋地罗帝偈及其义者，诸比丘从世尊闻已，当善受持。」

The World-Honored One said, "Ruru-jyana! Listen carefully and keep it in mind well. I will explain it to you in detail."

世尊告曰：「强耆！谛听，善思念之，我当为汝广说其义。」

Venerable Ruru-jyana said, "Yes, I will listen to the teaching."

尊者卢夷强耆白曰：「唯然。当受教听。」

The Buddha said:

佛言：

Do not think of the past, nor wish for
the future. The past is already extinguished, and the
future has not yet arrived. The present phenomena are
also impermanent. The wise should realize this. If one
practices the path of the sages, who knows about
sorrow at death? I will not meet with that
great suffering and calamity. Thus, one should practice diligently
day and night without laziness. Therefore, I will always
recite the Vaitulya verses.

「慎莫念过去，　　亦勿愿未来，过去事已灭，　　未来复未至。现在所有法，　　彼亦当为思，念无有坚强，　　慧者觉如是。若作圣人行，　　孰知愁于死，我要不会彼，　　大苦灾患终。如是行精勤，　　昼夜无懈怠，是故常当说，　　跋地罗帝偈。

Jñātiputra, how does a bhikṣu think of the past? If a bhikṣu delights in past form, desires, clings, and abides in it, and delights in past feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness, desires, clings, and abides in it, then that bhikṣu thinks of the past. Jñātiputra, how does a bhikṣu not think of the past? If a bhikṣu does not delight in past form, does not desire, cling, or abide in it, and does not delight in past feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness, does not desire, cling, or abide in it, then that bhikṣu does not think of the past. Jñātiputra, how does a bhikṣu wish for the future? If a bhikṣu delights in future form, desires, clings, and abides in it, and delights in future feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness, desires, clings, and abides in it, then that bhikṣu wishes for the future. Jñātiputra, how does a bhikṣu not wish for the future? If a bhikṣu does not delight in future form, does not desire, cling, or abide in it, and does not delight in future feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness, does not desire, cling, or abide in it, then that bhikṣu does not wish for the future.

「强耆！云何比丘念过去耶？若比丘乐过去色，欲、著、住，乐过去觉、想、行、识，欲、著、住，如是比丘念过去也。强耆！云何比丘不念过去？若比丘不乐过去色，不欲、不著、不住，不乐过去觉、想、行、识，不欲、不著、不住，如是比丘不念过去。强耆！云何比丘愿未来耶？若比丘乐未来色，欲、著、住，乐未来觉、想、行、识，欲、著、住，如是比丘愿未来也。强耆！云何比丘不愿未来？若比丘不乐未来色，不欲、不著、不住，不乐未来觉、想、行、识，不欲、不著、不住，如是比丘不愿未来。

Jñātiputra, how does a bhikṣu experience present phenomena? If a bhikṣu delights in present form, desires, clings, and abides in it, and delights in present feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness, desires, clings, and abides in it, then that bhikṣu experiences present phenomena. Jñātiputra, how does a bhikṣu not experience present phenomena? If a bhikṣu does not delight in present form, does not desire, cling, or abide in it, and does not delight in present feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness, does not desire, cling, or abide in it, then that bhikṣu does not experience present phenomena.

「强耆！云何比丘受现在法？若比丘乐现在色，欲、著、住，乐现在觉、想、行、识，欲、著、住，如是比丘受现在法。强耆！云何比丘不受现在法？若比丘不乐现在色，不欲、不著、不住，不乐现在觉、想、行、识，不欲、不著、不住，如是比丘不受现在法。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The Venerable Jñātiputra and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说如是。尊者卢夷强耆及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
