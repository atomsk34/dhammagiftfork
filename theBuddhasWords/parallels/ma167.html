MĀ 167

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 13 根本分别品

167. （一六七）阿难说经

Parallel to: MN 132

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Ānanda gathered with the bhikṣus in the lecture hall at night and spoke the verses of the Vāḍīra-emperor and their meanings.

尔时，尊者阿难为诸比丘夜集讲堂，说跋地罗帝偈及其义也。

At that time, a bhikṣu passed the night and at dawn went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at his feet, sat to one side, and said, "World-Honored One! Venerable Ānanda gathered with the bhikṣus in the lecture hall at night and spoke the verses of the Vāḍīra-emperor and their meanings.

尔时，有一比丘过夜平旦，往诣佛所，稽首作礼，却坐一面，白曰：「世尊！彼尊者阿难为诸比丘夜集讲堂，说跋地罗帝偈及其义也。」

Then the World-Honored One told a bhikṣu, "Go to the bhikṣu Ānanda and say, 'Ānanda! The World-Honored One calls you.'"

于是，世尊告一比丘：「汝往至阿难比丘所，作如是语：『阿难！世尊呼汝。』」

That bhikṣu received the World-Honored One's instruction, immediately rose from his seat, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and departed. He went to where the Venerable Ānanda was and said, "The World-Honored One calls the Venerable Ānanda!"

彼一比丘受世尊教，即从座起，稽首佛足，绕三匝而去，往至尊者阿难所而语曰：「世尊呼尊者阿难！」

The Venerable Ānanda then went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head in reverence, and stood to one side. The World-Honored One asked, "Ānanda! Did you really gather the bhikṣus in the lecture hall at night and recite the verses of the Vāḍiśā and explain their meaning?"

尊者阿难即往佛所，稽首作礼，却住一面，世尊问曰：「阿难！汝实为诸比丘夜集讲堂，说跋地罗帝偈及其义耶？」

The Venerable Ānanda replied, "Yes, indeed."

尊者阿难答曰：「唯然。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Ānanda! How did you explain the verses of the Vāḍiśā and their meaning to the bhikṣus?"

世尊问曰：「阿难！汝云何为诸比丘说跋地罗帝偈及其义耶？」

The Venerable Ānanda then explained,

尊者阿难即便说曰：

Do not think of the past, nor wish for
the future. The past has already ceased, and the
future has not yet arrived. The present phenomena, they
too should be contemplated. Thoughts have no substance; the
wise perceive it thus. Practicing the path of the
sages, who knows sorrow from death? I will not
meet them, the end of great suffering and calamity
Thus practicing diligently, day and night without laziness, therefore
always recite the Vāḍiśā verses.

「慎莫念过去，　　亦勿愿未来，过去事已灭，　　未来复未至。现在所有法，　　彼亦当为思，念无有坚强，　　慧者觉如是。为作圣人行，　　孰知愁于死，我要不会彼，　　大苦灾患终。如是行精进，　　昼夜无懈怠，是故常当说，　　跋地罗帝偈。」

The World-Honored One then asked again, "Ānanda! How does a bhikṣu recollect the past?"

世尊即复问曰：「阿难！云何比丘念过去耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "World-Honored One! If a bhikṣu delights in past form, desires, clings, and abides in it, and delights in past feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness, desires, clings, and abides in them, then such a bhikṣu recollects the past."

尊者阿难答曰：「世尊！若有比丘乐过去色，欲、著、住，乐过去觉、想、行、识，欲、著、住，如是比丘念过去也。」

The World-Honored One then asked again, "Ānanda! How does a bhikṣu not recollect the past?"

世尊即复问曰：「阿难！云何比丘不念过去？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "World-Honored One! If a bhikṣu does not delight in past form, does not desire, cling, or abide in it, and does not delight in past feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness, does not desire, cling, or abide in them, then such a bhikṣu does not recollect the past."

尊者阿难答曰：「世尊！若比丘不乐过去色，不欲、不著、不住，不乐过去觉、想、行、识，不欲、不著、不住，如是比丘不念过去。」

The World-Honored One then asked again, "Ānanda! How does a bhikṣu wish for the future?"

世尊即复问曰：「阿难！云何比丘愿未来耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "World-Honored One! If a bhikṣu delights in future form, desires, clings, and abides in it, and delights in future feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness, desires, clings, and abides in them, then such a bhikṣu wishes for the future."

尊者阿难答曰：「世尊！若比丘乐未来色，欲、著、住，乐未来觉、想、行、识，欲、著、住，如是比丘愿未来也。」

The World-Honored One then asked again, "Ānanda! How does a bhikṣu not wish for the future?"

世尊即复问曰：「阿难！云何比丘不愿未来？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "World-Honored One! If a bhikṣu does not delight in future form, does not desire, cling, or abide in it, and does not delight in future feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness, does not desire, cling, or abide in them, then such a bhikṣu does not wish for the future."

尊者阿难答曰：「世尊！若比丘不乐未来色，不欲、不著、不住，不乐未来觉、想、行、识，不欲、不著、不住，如是比丘不愿未来。」

The World-Honored One then asked again, "Ānanda! How does a bhikṣu accept present dharmas?"

世尊即复问曰：「阿难！云何比丘受现在法？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "World-Honored One! If a bhikṣu delights in present form, desires, clings, and abides in it, and delights in present feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness, desires, clings, and abides in them, then such a bhikṣu accepts present dharmas."

尊者阿难答曰：「世尊！若比丘乐现在色，欲、著、住，乐现在觉、想、行、识，欲、著、住，如是比丘受现在法。」

The World-Honored One then asked again, "Ānanda! How does a bhikṣu not accept present dharmas?"

世尊即复问曰：「阿难！云何比丘不受现在法？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "World-Honored One! If a bhikṣu does not delight in present form, does not desire, cling, or abide in it, and does not delight in present feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness, does not desire, cling, or abide in them, then such a bhikṣu does not accept present dharmas. World-Honored One! I have spoken these Vāgīśa verses and their meanings to the bhikṣus who gathered in the lecture hall at night.

尊者阿难答曰：「世尊！若比丘不乐现在色，不欲、不著、不住，不乐现在觉、想、行、识，不欲、不著、不住，如是比丘不受现在法。世尊！我以如是为诸比丘夜集讲堂，说跋地罗帝偈及其义也。」

Then the World-Honored One said to the bhikṣus, "Excellent! Excellent! Among my disciples, there are those with eyes, wisdom, meaning, and the Dharma. Why is that? It is because the disciple in front of the teacher speaks these phrases and these texts extensively explaining this meaning, just as the bhikṣu Ānanda has said. You should uphold it like this. Why is that? This explanation of the meaning should be like this.

于是，世尊告诸比丘：「善哉！善哉！我弟子中] cf.［丽】有眼、有智、有义、有法。所以者何？谓弟子在师面前如是句、如是文广说此义，实如阿难比丘所说，汝等应当如是受持。所以者何？此说观义应如是也。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Venerable Ānanda and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。尊者阿难及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
