MĀ 17

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 2 业相应品

To Gāmaṇi （一七）伽弥尼经

Parallel to: SN 42.6

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the Nandana Grove, in the village of Kalāpa.

一时，佛游那难陀园，在墙村捺林。

At that time, there was a son of the Asurā named Gamini, whose appearance was majestic and his radiance was brilliant. In the early morning, he went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and stood to one side.

尔时，阿私罗天有子名伽弥尼，色像巍巍，光耀炜晔，夜将向旦，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，却住一面。

The Asurā son Gamini said, "World-Honored One! The Brahmins are arrogant and serve various gods. If sentient beings' lives end, they can make them freely go to good places and be reborn in heaven. World-Honored One, you are the master of the Dharma. I only wish that the World-Honored One would make it so that when sentient beings' lives end, they can reach good places and be reborn in heaven."

阿私罗天子伽弥尼白曰：「世尊！梵志自高，事若干天，若众生命终者，彼能令自在往来善处，生于天上，世尊为法主，唯愿世尊使众生命终得至善处，生于天中。」

The World-Honored One said, "Gamini! I will now ask you, answer as you see fit. Gamini! What do you think? If in a village or town there are men and women who are lazy and indolent, engage in evil practices, and accomplish the ten unwholesome courses of action - killing, taking what is not given, sexual misconduct, false speech, up to wrong view - when their lives end, if many people come and each face them with folded hands, praising and requesting, saying, 'You men and women, being lazy and indolent, engage in evil practices, and accomplish the ten unwholesome courses of action - killing, taking what is not given, sexual misconduct, false speech, up to wrong view, because of this and that, when your bodies perish and your lives end, you will surely go to a good place and be reborn in heaven.' In this way, Gamini! Those men and women, being lazy and indolent, engage in evil practices, and accomplish the ten unwholesome courses of action - killing, taking what is not given, sexual misconduct, false speech, up to wrong view, would they, because of this and that, when their bodies perish and their lives end, attain a good place and be reborn in heaven?"

世尊告曰：「伽弥尼！我今问汝，随所解答。伽弥尼！于意云何？若村邑中或有男女，懈不精进，而行恶法，成就十种不善业道，杀生、不与取、邪婬、妄言，乃至邪见，彼命终时，若众人来，各叉手向称叹求索，作如是语：『汝等男女，懈不精进，而行恶法，成就十种不善业道，杀生、不与取、邪婬、妄言，乃至邪见，汝等因此缘此，身坏命终，必至善处，乃生天上。』如是，伽弥尼！彼男女等，懈不精进，而行恶法，成就十种不善业道，杀生、不与取、邪婬、妄言，乃至邪见，宁为众人各叉手向称叹求索，因此缘此，身坏命终，得至善处，生天上耶？」

Gamini replied, "No, World-Honored One!"

伽弥尼答曰：「不也。世尊！」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Gamini! Why is that? If those men and women, being lazy and indolent, engage in evil practices, and accomplish the ten unwholesome courses of action - killing, taking what is not given, sexual misconduct, false speech, up to wrong view - if they were to be praised and requested by many people facing them with folded hands, because of this and that, when their bodies perish and their lives end, attain a good place and be reborn in heaven, that is not so. Gamini! Not far from a village there is a deep pond. If someone were to throw a large heavy stone into the water, and if many people came and each faced it with folded hands, praising and requesting, saying, 'May the stone float up.' What do you think, Gamini? Would that large heavy stone, because of being praised and requested by many people facing it with folded hands, come up because of this and that?"

世尊叹曰：「善哉！伽弥尼！所以者何？彼男女等，懈不精进，而行恶法，成就十种不善业道，杀生、不与取、邪婬、妄言，乃至邪见，若为众人各叉手向称叹求索，因此缘此，身坏命终，得至善处，乃生天上者，是处不然。伽弥尼！犹去村不远有深水渊，于彼有人以大重石掷著水中，若众人来，各叉手向称叹求索，作如是语：『愿石浮出。』伽弥尼！于意云何？此大重石宁为众人各叉手向称叹求索，因此缘此，而当出耶？」

Gamini replied, "No, World-Honored One!"

伽弥尼答曰：「不也。世尊！」

So it is, Gamini! If those men and women, being lazy and indolent, engage in evil practices, and accomplish the ten unwholesome courses of action - killing, taking what is not given, sexual misconduct, false speech, up to wrong view - if they were to be praised and requested by many people facing them with folded hands, because of this and that, when their bodies perish and their lives end, attain a good place and be reborn in heaven, that is not so. Why is that? Because these ten unwholesome courses of action have black results, naturally lead to lower realms, and definitely lead to evil places.

「如是。伽弥尼！彼男女等，懈不精进，而行恶法，成就十种不善业道，杀生、不与取、邪婬、妄言，乃至邪见，若为众人各叉手向称叹求索，因此缘此，身坏命终，得至善处，生天上者，是处不然。所以者何？谓此十种不善业道，黑有黑报，自然趣下，必至恶处。

"Gamini! What do you think? If in a village or town there are men and women who diligently practice and engage in the sublime Dharma, accomplishing the ten wholesome courses of action - refraining from killing, abandoning killing, refraining from taking what is not given, sexual misconduct, false speech, up to refraining from wrong view, abandoning wrong view, and attaining right view - when they die, if many people come and face them with folded hands, praising and requesting them, saying, 'You men and women, diligently practicing and engaging in the sublime Dharma, accomplishing the ten wholesome courses of action - refraining from killing, abandoning killing, refraining from taking what is not given, sexual misconduct, false speech, up to refraining from wrong view, abandoning wrong view, and attaining right view - because of this and that, when your bodies perish and your lives end, you will go to an evil place and be reborn in hell.' Gamini! What do you think? Will those men and women, diligently practicing and engaging in the sublime Dharma, accomplishing the ten wholesome courses of action - refraining from killing, abandoning killing, refraining from taking what is not given, sexual misconduct, false speech, up to refraining from wrong view, abandoning wrong view, and attaining right view - be praised and requested by many people facing them with folded hands, because of this and that, when their bodies perish and their lives end, go to an evil place and be reborn in hell?"

「伽弥尼！于意云何？若村邑中或有男女，精进勤修，而行妙法，成十善业道，离杀、断杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言，乃至离邪见，断邪见，得正见，彼命终时，若众人来，各叉手向称叹求索，作如是语：『汝男女等，精进勤修，而行妙法，成十善业道，离杀、断杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言，乃至离邪见，断邪见，得正见，汝等因此缘此，身坏命终，当至恶处，生地狱中。』伽弥尼！于意云何？彼男女等，精进勤修，而行妙法，成十善业道，离杀、断杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言，乃至离邪见，断邪见，得正见，宁为众人各叉手向称叹求索，因此缘此，身坏命终，得至恶处，生地狱中耶？」

Gamini replied, "No, World-Honored One!"

伽弥尼答曰：「不也。世尊！」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Gamini! Why is that? Gamini! Those men and women who diligently practice and engage in the sublime Dharma, accomplishing the ten wholesome courses of action - refraining from killing, abandoning killing, refraining from taking what is not given, sexual misconduct, false speech, up to refraining from wrong view, abandoning wrong view, and attaining right view - if they were praised and requested by many people facing them with folded hands, because of this and that, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they would be reborn in an evil place and hell. This is not the case. Why is that? Gamini! These ten wholesome courses of action have a white result, naturally ascending to a good place. Gamini! Not far from a village there is a deep pond. If someone were to throw a pot of ghee or oil into the water and it broke, the pot shards would sink, while the ghee or oil would float. In the same way, Gamini! Those men and women who diligently practice and engage in the sublime Dharma, accomplishing the ten wholesome courses of action - refraining from killing, abandoning killing, refraining from taking what is not given, sexual misconduct, false speech, up to refraining from wrong view, abandoning wrong view, and attaining right view - when they die, their coarse physical form, the four great elements, born from their parents, nourished by clothing and food, sitting, lying down, massaging, bathing, and enduring, is subject to destruction, cessation, and dispersal. After their death, they may be pecked by crows and birds, eaten by tigers and wolves, burned, or buried, becoming dust. Their mind, intention, and consciousness are constantly perfumed by faith, diligence, learning, generosity, and wisdom. Because of this and that, they naturally ascend and are reborn in a good place. Gamini! For those who kill, refraining from killing, abandoning killing, is the path of gardens and groves, the path of ascent, the path to a good place. Gamini! For those who take what is not given, engage in sexual misconduct, speak falsely, up to those with wrong view, refraining from wrong view, attaining right view, is the path of gardens and groves, the path of ascent, the path to a good place. Gamini! There is also another path of gardens and groves, the path of ascent, the path to a good place. Gamini! What is the other path of gardens and groves, the path of ascent, the path to a good place? It is the eightfold noble path - right view, up to right concentration. These are the eight. Gamini! This is called the other path of gardens and groves, the path of ascent, the path to a good place."

世尊叹曰：「善哉！伽弥尼！所以者何？伽弥尼！彼男女等，精进勤修，而行妙法，成十善业道，离杀、断杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言，乃至离邪见，断邪见，得正见，若为众人各叉手向称叹求索，因此缘此，身坏命终，得生恶处，生地狱中者，是处不然。所以者何？伽弥尼！谓此十善业道，白有白报，自然升上，必至善处。伽弥尼！犹去村不远有深水渊，于彼有人以酥油瓶投水而破，滓瓦沈下，酥油浮上。如是，伽弥尼！彼男女等，精进勤修，而行妙法，成十善业道，离杀、断杀、不与取，邪婬、妾言乃至离邪见，断邪见，得正见。彼命终时，谓身麤色四大之种从父母生，衣食长养，坐卧按摩，澡浴强忍，是破坏法，是灭尽法，离散之法；彼命终后，或乌鸟啄，或虎狼食，或烧或埋，尽为粉尘，彼心、意、识常为信所熏，为精进、多闻、布施、智慧所熏，彼因此缘此，自然升上，生于善处。伽弥尼！彼杀生者，离杀、断杀，园观之道、升进之道、善处之道。伽弥尼！不与取、邪婬、妄言，乃至邪见者，离邪见，得正见，园观之道、升进之道、善处之道。伽弥尼！复有园观之道、升进之道、善处之道。伽弥尼！云何复有园观之道、升进之道、善处之道？谓八支圣道，正见乃至正定，是为八。伽弥尼！是谓复有园观之道、升进之道、善处之道。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Gamini and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。伽弥尼及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
