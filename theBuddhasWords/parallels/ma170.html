MĀ 170

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 13 根本分别品

170. （一七〇）鹦鹉经

Parallel to: MN 99 (~) / MN 135 / MĀ 152 (~)

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One, having passed the night, put on his robe in the morning, took his alms bowl, and entered Śrāvastī to beg for food. While begging for food, he went to the house of the householder's son Parrot. At that time, the householder's son Parrot had gone out for some business and was not at home. At that time, in the householder's son Parrot's house, there was a white dog that was eating from a golden bowl on a large bed. Then, the white dog saw the Buddha coming from afar, and upon seeing him, it barked. The World-Honored One said to the white dog, "You should not do that. You should go from licking to barking." Having heard this, the white dog became extremely angry, came down from the bed, and sorrowfully lay down by a pile of wood.

尔时，世尊过夜平旦著衣持钵，入舍卫乞食，于乞食时往诣鹦鹉摩纳都提子家。是时，鹦鹉摩纳都提子少有所为，出行不在。彼时，鹦鹉摩纳都提子家有白狗，在大床上金槃中食。于是，白狗遥见佛来，见已便吠。世尊语白狗：「汝不应尔，谓汝从呧至吠。」白狗闻已，极大瞋恚，从床来下，至木聚边忧戚愁卧。

Later, the householder's son Parrot returned home and saw his white dog extremely angry, having come down from the bed and sorrowfully lying down by a pile of wood. He asked his family members, "Who has disturbed my dog, causing it to become extremely angry, come down from the bed, and sorrowfully lie down by a pile of wood?"

鹦鹉摩纳都提子于后还家，见己白狗极大瞋恚，从床来下，至木聚边忧戚愁卧，问家人曰：「谁触娆我狗，令极大瞋恚，从床来下，至木聚边忧戚愁卧？」

The family members replied, "We have not disturbed the white dog at all, causing it to become extremely angry, come down from the bed, and sorrowfully lie down by a pile of wood. Householder's son, you should know that today the śramaṇa Gautama came here to beg for food. The white dog saw him and chased after him, barking. The śramaṇa Gautama said to the white dog, 'You should not do that. You should go from licking to barking.' Because of this, householder's son, the white dog became extremely angry, came down from the bed, and sorrowfully lay down by a pile of wood."

家人答曰：「我等都无触娆白狗，令大瞋恚，从床来下，至木聚边忧戚愁卧。摩纳！当知今日沙门瞿昙来此乞食，白狗见已，便逐吠之。沙门瞿昙语白狗曰：『汝不应尔，谓汝从呧至吠。』因是，摩纳！故令白狗极大瞋恚，从床来下，至木聚边忧戚愁卧。」

Having heard this, the householder's son Parrot became greatly angry, wanting to falsely accuse the World-Honored One, wanting to slander the World-Honored One, wanting to bring down the World-Honored One. Thus, wanting to falsely accuse, slander, and bring down the śramaṇa Gautama, he immediately left Śrāvastī and went to Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

鹦鹉摩纳都提子闻已，便大瞋恚，欲诬世尊，欲谤世尊，欲堕世尊。如是诬、谤、堕沙门瞿昙，即从舍卫出，往诣胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One was teaching the Dharma surrounded by a great assembly in front and behind. The World-Honored One saw from afar the householder's son Parrot coming and said to the bhikṣus, "Do you see the householder's son Parrot coming?"

彼时，世尊无量大众前后围绕而为说法，世尊遥见鹦鹉摩纳都提子来，告诸比丘：「汝等见鹦鹉摩纳都提子来耶？」

They replied, "We see him."

答曰：「见也。」

The World-Honored One said, "The householder's son Parrot has just died. In the time it takes to bend and stretch an arm, he will definitely be reborn in hell. Why is that? Because he was extremely angry towards me. If sentient beings, due to anger in their minds, have their bodies destroyed and their lives end, they will definitely go to a bad place and be reborn in hell."

世尊告曰：「鹦鹉摩纳都提子今命终者，如屈伸臂顷，必生地狱。所以者何？以彼于我极大瞋恚。若有众生因心瞋恚故，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。」

Then the householder's son Parrot went to where the Buddha was and said to the World-Honored One, "śramaṇa Gautama! Do you come to my house now to beg for food?"

于是，鹦鹉摩纳都提子往诣佛所，语世尊曰：「沙门瞿昙！今至我家乞食来耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "I am now going to your house to beg for food."

世尊答曰：「我今往至汝家乞食。」

"Gautama! What did you say to my white dog that made my white dog so angry that it came down from the bed and lay down sadly by the pile of wood?"

「瞿昙！向我白狗说何等事，令我白狗极大瞋恚，从床来下，至木聚边忧戚愁卧？」

The World-Honored One replied, "This morning, I put on my robe, took my bowl, and entered Śrāvastī to beg for food. I went to your house to beg for food. The white dog saw me coming from afar, and when it saw me, it barked. I said to the white dog, 'You should not do that. You went from licking to barking.' That is why the white dog was so angry that it came down from the bed and lay down sadly by the pile of wood."

世尊答曰：「我今平旦著衣持钵，入舍卫乞食，展转往诣汝家乞食，于是白狗遥见我来，见已而吠。我语白狗：『汝不应尔，谓汝从呧至吠。』是故白狗极大瞋恚，从床来下，至木聚边忧戚愁卧。」

The parrot-feathered ascetic asked the World-Honored One, "In the past life, what was the white dog to me?"

鹦鹉摩纳问世尊曰：「白狗前世是我何等？」

The World-Honored One said, "Stop! Stop! Ascetic, do not ask me about this. If you hear it, you will surely be displeased."

世尊告曰：「止！止！摩纳！慎莫问我，汝闻此已，必不可意。」

The parrot-feathered ascetic asked the World-Honored One three times, "In the past life, what was the white dog to me?"

鹦鹉摩纳复更再三问世尊曰：「白狗前世是我何等？」

The World-Honored One also said three times, "Stop! Stop! Ascetic, do not ask me about this. If you hear it, you will surely be displeased."

世尊亦至再三告曰：「止！止！摩纳！慎莫问我，汝闻此已，必不可意。」

The World-Honored One again said to the ascetic, "You have asked me three times, and I have not stopped you. Ascetic, know that the white dog in the past life was your father, named Duti."

世尊复告于摩纳曰：「汝至再三问我不止，摩纳！当知彼白狗者，于前世时即是汝父，名都提也。」

When the ascetic Parrot heard these words, he became even more greatly enraged. He wanted to falsely accuse the World-Honored One, slander the World-Honored One, and bring down the World-Honored One. He falsely accused, slandered, and brought down the śramaṇa Gautama, saying to the World-Honored One, "My father Duti practiced great giving and held great sacrificial offerings. When his body perished and his life ended, he was reborn in the heaven of Brahmā. For what reason was he reborn in this lowly dog?"

鹦鹉摩纳闻是语已，倍极大恚，欲诬世尊，欲谤世尊，欲堕世尊。如是诬、谤、堕沙门瞿昙，语世尊曰：「我父都提大行布施，作大斋祠，身坏命终，正生梵天。何因何缘，乃生于此下贱狗中？」

The World-Honored One said, "Your father Duti was reborn in this lowly dog due to this increased arrogance.

世尊告曰：「汝父都提以此增上慢，是故生于下贱狗中。

Brahmins with increased arrogance are reborn in these six
places: chickens, dogs, pigs, wolves, donkeys, and the five hells.

「梵志增上慢，　　此终六处生，鸡狗猪及犲，　　驴五地狱六。

"If you do not believe what I say, you can return and tell the white dog, 'If you were my father in the past, the white dog should return to the large bed.' The white dog will certainly return to the bed. 'If you were my father in the past, the white dog should eat from the golden bowl.' The white dog will certainly eat from the golden bowl. 'If you were my father in the past, show me the places where you have hidden gold, silver, crystal, and precious treasures that I do not know about.' The white dog will certainly show you the places where you have hidden gold, silver, crystal, and precious treasures that you do not know about."

「鹦鹉摩纳！若汝不信我所说者，汝可还归语白狗曰：『若前世时是我父者，白狗当还在大床上。』摩纳！白狗必还上床也。『若前世时是我父者，白狗还于金槃中食。』摩纳！白狗必当还于金槃中食也。『若前世时是我父者，示我所举金、银、水精、珍宝藏处，谓我所不知。』摩纳！白狗必当示汝已前所举金、银、水精、珍宝藏处，谓汝所不知。」

Then the parrot-feathered youth, having heard what the Buddha said, accepted it well, recited it, circumambulated the World-Honored One, and returned to his home. He said to the white dog, "If you were my father in the past, the white dog should return to the large bed." The white dog then returned to the large bed. "If you were my father in the past, the white dog should eat from the golden bowl." The white dog then returned to eat from the golden bowl. "If you were my father in the past, show me the places where my father has hidden gold, silver, crystal, and precious treasures that I do not know about." The white dog then came down from the bed and went to the place where it had previously stayed. It dug at the four legs of the bed with its mouth and feet. The parrot-feathered youth then obtained a great deal of treasure from that place.

于是，鹦鹉摩纳闻佛所说，善受持诵，绕世尊已，而还其家，语白狗曰：「若前世时是我父者，白狗当还在大床上。」白狗即还在大床上。「若前世时是我父者，白狗还于金槃中食。」白狗即还金槃中食。「若前世时是我父者，当示于我父本所举金、银、水精、珍宝藏处，谓我所不知。」白狗即从床上来下，往至前世所止宿处，以口及足掊床四脚下，鹦鹉摩纳便从彼处大得宝物。

Then the parrot-feathered youth, having obtained the treasures, was extremely delighted. He touched his right knee to the ground, joined his palms towards Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma, and raised his voice three times to praise the World-Honored One, saying, "The words of Śramaṇa Gautama are not false. The words of Śramaṇa Gautama are true. The words of Śramaṇa Gautama are as they are." Having praised him three times, he left Śrāvastī and went to Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

于是，鹦鹉摩纳都提子得宝物已，极大欢喜，以右膝著地，叉手向胜林给孤独园，再三举声，称誉世尊：「沙门瞿昙所说不虚，沙门瞿昙所说真谛，沙门瞿昙所说如实。」再三称誉已，从舍卫出，往诣胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One was surrounded by an immeasurable great assembly in front and behind, teaching the Dharma. The World-Honored One saw from afar the parrot-feathered youth coming and said to the bhikṣus, "Do you see the parrot-feathered youth coming?"

尔时，世尊无量大众前后围绕而为说法，世尊遥见鹦鹉摩纳来，告诸比丘：「汝等见鹦鹉摩纳来耶？」

They replied, "We see him."

答曰：「见也。」

The World-Honored One said, "The parrot-feathered youth has now passed away, in the time it takes to bend and stretch an arm, he will surely go to a good place. Why is that? He has great good intentions towards me. If sentient beings, because of good intentions, have their bodies destroyed and their lives end, they will surely go to a good place and be reborn in the heavens."

世尊告曰：「鹦鹉摩纳今命终者，如屈伸臂顷，必至善处。所以者何？彼于我极有善心。若有众生因善心故，身坏命终，必至善处，生于天中。」

At that time, the parrot-feathered youth went to where the Buddha was, exchanged greetings, and sat to one side. The World-Honored One said, "What do you think, youth? Is what I said about the white dog like this or not like this?"

尔时，鹦鹉摩纳往诣佛所，共相问讯，却坐一面，世尊告曰：「云何摩纳？如我所说白狗者为如是耶？不如是耶？」

The parrot-feathered youth said, "Gautama! It is indeed as you have said. Gautama! I also wish to ask something. Please listen before I speak."

鹦鹉摩纳白曰：「瞿昙！实如所说。瞿昙！我复欲有所问，听乃敢陈。」

The World-Honored One said, "Ask as you please."

世尊告曰：「恣汝所问。」

"Gautama! For what reason and cause do those beings, having all received human bodies, have high and low, the wonderful and the not wonderful? Why is this? Gautama! I see those with short lives and those with long lives, those with much illness and those with little illness, those who are not upright and those who are upright, those who lack dignity and those who have dignity, those who are of lowly families and those who are of noble families, those who lack wealth and those who have wealth, and those who have bad wisdom and those who have good wisdom."

「瞿昙！何因何缘，彼众生者，俱受人身而有高下、有妙不妙？所以者何？瞿昙！我见有短寿、有长寿者，见有多病、有少病者，见不端正、有端正者，见无威德、有威德者，见有卑贱族、有尊贵族者，见无财物、有财物者，见有恶智、有善智者。」

The World-Honored One replied, "Those beings, due to their own actions, obtain retribution through their actions, relying on actions, based on actions, and in accordance with their actions, they are placed in high and low, the wonderful and the not wonderful."

世尊答曰：「彼众生者，因自行业，因业得报，缘业、依业、业处，众生随其高下处妙不妙。」

The parrot-feathered ascetic said to the World-Honored One, "The words of the śramaṇa Gautama are concise and not extensively explained. I cannot understand them. May the śramaṇa Gautama explain them extensively for me, so that I may understand the meaning."

鹦鹉摩纳白世尊曰：「沙门瞿昙所说至略，不广分别，我不能知，愿沙门瞿昙为我广说，令得知义。」

The World-Honored One said, "Ascetic, listen attentively and consider it well. I shall explain it extensively for you."

世尊告曰：「摩纳！谛听，善思念之，我当为汝广分别说。」

The parrot-feathered ascetic said, "Yes, I shall listen attentively."

鹦鹉摩纳白曰：「唯然。当受教听。」

The Buddha said, "Ascetic, what causes and conditions lead to men and women having a very short lifespan? If men and women kill, are cruel and vicious, drink blood, have malicious intent, and lack compassion towards all beings, including insects, they will experience the consequences of these actions. When their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. If they are reborn as humans, their lifespan will be very short. Why is this? This path leads to a short lifespan, namely, men and women who kill, are cruel and vicious, and drink blood. Ascetic, you should know that this karma has such consequences. Ascetic, what causes and conditions lead to men and women having a very long lifespan? If men and women give up killing, abandon killing, put down weapons, have a sense of shame and conscience, have compassion, and benefit all beings, including insects, they will experience the consequences of these actions. When their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely ascend to a good place and be reborn in heaven. If they are reborn as humans, their lifespan will be very long. Why is this? This path leads to a long lifespan, namely, men and women who give up killing and abandon killing. Ascetic, you should know that this karma has such consequences.

佛言：「摩纳！何因、何缘男子女人寿命极短？若有男子女人杀生凶弊，极恶饮血，害意著恶，无有慈心于诸众生乃至蜫虫，彼受此业，作具足已，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中，来生人间，寿命极短。所以者何？此道受短寿，谓男子女人杀生凶弊，极恶饮血。摩纳！当知此业有如是报也。摩纳！何因、何缘男子女人寿命极长？若有男子女人离杀断杀，弃舍刀杖，有慙有愧，有慈悲心，饶益一切乃至蜫虫，彼受此业，作具足已，身坏命终，必升善处，生于天中，来生人间，寿命极长。所以者何？此道受长寿，谓男子女人离杀断杀。摩纳！当知此业有如是报也。

Ascetic, what causes and conditions lead to men and women having many illnesses? If men and women touch and harass sentient beings, using their fists, sticks, stones, or weapons to harass sentient beings, they will experience the consequences of these actions. When their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. If they are reborn as humans, they will have many illnesses. Why is this? This path leads to many illnesses, namely, men and women who touch and harass sentient beings. Ascetic, you should know that this karma has such consequences. Ascetic, what causes and conditions lead to men and women being free from illness? If men and women do not touch and harass sentient beings, not using their fists, sticks, stones, or weapons to harass sentient beings, they will experience the consequences of these actions. When their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely ascend to a good place and be reborn in heaven. If they are reborn as humans, they will be free from illness. Why is this? This path leads to freedom from illness, namely, men and women who do not touch and harass sentient beings. Ascetic, you should know that this karma has such consequences.

「摩纳！何因、何缘男子女人多有疾病？若有男子女人触娆众生，彼或以手拳，或以木石，或以刀杖触娆众生，彼受此业，作具足已，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中，来生人间，多有疾病。所以者何？此道受多疾病，谓男子女人触娆众生。摩纳！当知此业有如是报也。摩纳！何因、何缘男子女人无有疾病？若有男子女人不触娆众生，彼不以手拳，不以木石，不以刀杖触娆众生，彼受此业，作具足已，身坏命终，必升善处，生于天中，来生人间，无有疾病。所以者何？此道受无疾病，谓男子女人不触娆众生。摩纳！当知此业有如是报也。

Ascetic, what causes and conditions lead to men and women having an ugly appearance? If men and women are quick-tempered and irritable, they become very angry when they hear little, and they give rise to hatred, sorrow, and much anger. They experience the consequences of these actions. When their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. If they are reborn as humans, they will have an ugly appearance. Why is this? This path leads to an ugly appearance, namely, men and women who are quick-tempered and irritable. Ascetic, you should know that this karma has such consequences. Ascetic, what causes and conditions lead to men and women having a beautiful appearance? If men and women are not quick-tempered and irritable, they do not become very angry when they hear soft, coarse, or strong words, and they do not give rise to hatred, sorrow, or much anger. They experience the consequences of these actions. When their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely ascend to a good place and be reborn in heaven. If they are reborn as humans, they will have a beautiful appearance. Why is this? This path leads to a beautiful appearance, namely, men and women who are not quick-tempered and irritable. Ascetic, you should know that this karma has such consequences.

「摩纳！何因、何缘男子女人形不端正？若有男子女人急性多恼，彼少所闻，便大瞋恚，憎嫉生忧，广生诤怒，彼受此业，作具足已，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中，来生人间，形不端正。所以者何？此道受形不端正，谓男子女人急性多恼。摩纳！当知此业有如是报也。摩纳！何因、何缘男子女人形体端正？若有男子女人不急性多恼，彼闻柔软麤［麸－夫＋广］强言，不大瞋恚，不憎嫉生忧，不广生诤怒，彼受此业，作具足已，身坏命终，必升善处，生于天中，来生人间，形体端正。所以者何？此道受形体端正，谓男子女人不急性多恼。摩纳！当知此业有如是报也。

Ascetic, what causes and conditions lead to men and women having no dignity? If men and women are jealous within, they become jealous when they see others receiving offerings and respect. If they see others having something, they want it for themselves. They experience the consequences of these actions. When their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. If they are reborn as humans, they will have no dignity. Why is this? This path leads to no dignity, namely, men and women who are jealous within. Ascetic, you should know that this karma has such consequences. Ascetic, what causes and conditions lead to men and women having great dignity? If men and women are not jealous, they do not become jealous when they see others receiving offerings and respect. If they see others having something, they do not want it for themselves. They experience the consequences of these actions. When their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely ascend to a good place and be reborn in heaven. If they are reborn as humans, they will have great dignity. Why is this? This path leads to dignity, namely, men and women who are not jealous. Ascetic, you should know that this karma has such consequences.

「摩纳！何因、何缘男子女人无有威德？若有男子女人内怀嫉妬，彼见他得供养恭敬，便生嫉妬，若见他有物，欲令我得，彼受此业，作具足已，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中，来生人间，无有威德。所以者何？此道受无威德，谓男子女人内怀嫉妬。摩纳！当知此业有如是报也。摩纳！何因、何缘男子女人有大威德？若有男子女人不怀嫉妬，彼见他得供养恭敬，不生嫉妬，若见他有物，不欲令我得，彼受此业，作具足已，身坏命终，必升善处，生于天中，来生人间，有大威德。所以者何？此道受有威德，谓男子女人不怀嫉妬。摩纳！当知此业有如是报也。

Ascetic, what causes and conditions lead to men and women being born in a low caste? If men and women are arrogant and very conceited, they do not respect those who should be respected, do not honor those who should be honored, do not value those who should be valued, do not serve those who should be served, do not make offerings to those who should be made offerings to, do not give the path to those who should be given the path, do not give seats to those who should be given seats, do not bow to those who should be bowed to, and do not pay respects to those who should be paid respects to. They experience the consequences of these actions. When their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. If they are reborn as humans, they will be born in a low caste. Why is this? This path leads to being born in a low caste, namely, men and women who are arrogant and very conceited. Ascetic, you should know that this karma has such consequences. Ascetic, what causes and conditions lead to men and women being born in a noble family? If men and women are not arrogant and very conceited, they respect those who should be respected, honor those who should be honored, value those who should be valued, serve those who should be served, make offerings to those who should be made offerings to, give the path to those who should be given the path, give seats to those who should be given seats, bow to those who should be bowed to, and pay respects to those who should be paid respects to. They experience the consequences of these actions. When their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely ascend to a good place and be reborn in heaven. If they are reborn as humans, they will be born in a noble family. Why is this? This path leads to being born in a noble family, namely, men and women who are not arrogant and very conceited. Ascetic, you should know that this karma has such consequences.

「摩纳！何因、何缘男子女人生卑贱族？若有男子女人憍慠大慢，彼可敬不敬，可重不重，可贵不贵，可奉不奉，可供养不供养，可与道不与道，可与坐不与坐，可叉手向礼拜问讯不叉手向礼拜问讯，彼受此业，作具足已，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中，来生人间，生卑贱族。所以者何？此道受生卑贱族，谓男子女人憍慠大慢。摩纳！当知此业有如是报也。摩纳！何因、何缘男子女人生尊贵族？若有男子女人不憍慠大慢，彼可敬而敬，可重而重，可贵而贵，可奉事而奉事，可供养而供养，可与道而与道，可与坐而与坐，可叉手向礼拜问讯而叉手向礼拜问讯，彼受此业，作具足已，身坏命终，必升善处，生于天中，来生人间，生尊贵族。所以者何？此道受生尊贵族，谓男子女人不憍慠大慢。摩纳！当知此业有如是报也。

Ascetic, what causes and conditions lead to men and women having no wealth? If men and women are not generous and do not practice giving, they do not give food, clothing, garlands, perfumes, dwellings, beds, lamps, and servants to śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, the poor, the orphaned, and those who come from afar to beg. They experience the consequences of these actions. When their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. If they are reborn as humans, they will have no wealth. Why is this? This path leads to having no wealth, namely, men and women who are not generous and do not practice giving. Ascetic, you should know that this karma has such consequences. Ascetic, what causes and conditions lead to men and women having much wealth? If men and women are generous and practice giving, they give food, clothing, garlands, perfumes, dwellings, beds, lamps, and servants to śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, the poor, the orphaned, and those who come from afar to beg. They experience the consequences of these actions. When their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely ascend to a good place and be reborn in heaven. If they are reborn as humans, they will have much wealth. Why is this? This path leads to having much wealth, namely, men and women who are generous and practice giving. Ascetic, you should know that this karma has such consequences.

「摩纳！何因、何缘男子女人无有财物？若有男子女人不作施主，不行布施，彼不施与沙门、梵志、贫穷、孤独、远来乞者饮食、衣被、华鬘、涂香、屋舍、床榻、明灯、给使，彼受此业，作具足已，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中，来生人间，无有财物。所以者何？此道受无财物，谓男子女人不作施主，不行布施。摩纳！当知此业有如是报也。摩纳！何因、何缘男子女人多有财物？若有男子女人作施主，行布施，彼施与沙门、梵志、贫穷、孤独、远来乞者饮食、衣被、花鬘、涂香、屋舍、床榻、明灯、给使，彼受此业，作具足已，身坏命终，必升善处，生于天中，来生人间，多有财物。所以者何？此道受多有财物，谓男子女人作施主，行布施。摩纳！当知此业有如是报也。

Ascetic, what causes and conditions lead to men and women having bad wisdom? If men and women do not often go to ask about matters, and if there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas with good virtue, they do not often go to ask about the meaning: 'Venerables, what is good, what is not good? What is blameworthy, what is not blameworthy? What is excellent, what is not excellent? What is white, what is black? Where do white and black arise? What meaning has present retribution, what meaning has future retribution?' If they ask but do not practice, they experience the consequences of these actions. When their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. If they are reborn as humans, they will have bad wisdom. Why is this? This path leads to having bad wisdom, namely, men and women who do not often go to ask about matters. Ascetic, you should know that this karma has such consequences. Ascetic, what causes and conditions lead to men and women having good wisdom? If men and women are able to often go to ask about matters, and if there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas with good virtue, they often go to ask about the meaning: 'Venerables, what is good, what is not good? What is blameworthy, what is not blameworthy? What is excellent, what is not excellent? What is white, what is black? Where do white and black arise? What meaning has present retribution, what meaning has future retribution?' If they ask and are able to practice, they experience the consequences of these actions. When their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely ascend to a good place and be reborn in heaven. If they are reborn as humans, they will have good wisdom. Why is this? This path leads to having good wisdom, namely, men and women who are able to often go to ask about matters. Ascetic, you should know that this karma has such consequences.

「摩纳！何因、何缘男子女人有恶智慧？若有男子女人不数数往诣彼问事，彼若有名德沙门、梵志，不往诣彼，随时问义：『诸尊！何者为善，何者不善？何者为罪，何者非罪？何者为妙，何者不妙？何者为白，何者为黑？白黑从何生？何义现世报，何义后世报？』设问不行，彼受此业，作具足已，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中，来生人间，有恶智慧。所以者何？此道受恶智慧，谓男子女人不数数往诣彼问事。摩纳！当知此业有如是报也。摩纳！何因、何缘男子女人有善智慧？若有男子女人能数数往诣彼问事，彼若有名德沙门、梵志，数往诣彼，随时问义：『诸尊！何者为善，何者不善？何者为罪，何者非罪？何者为妙，何者不妙？何者为白，何者为黑？白黑从何生？何义现世报，何义后世报？』问已能行，彼受此业，作具足已，身坏命终，必升善处，生于天中，来生人间，有善智慧。所以者何？此道受善智慧，谓男子女人能数数往诣彼问事。摩纳！当知此业有如是报也。

"Ascetic, you should know that karma that leads to a short lifespan will definitely result in a short lifespan, karma that leads to a long lifespan will definitely result in a long lifespan, karma that leads to much illness will definitely result in much illness, karma that leads to little illness will definitely result in little illness, karma that leads to ugliness will definitely result in ugliness, karma that leads to beauty will definitely result in beauty, karma that leads to lack of dignity will definitely result in lack of dignity, karma that leads to dignity will definitely result in dignity, karma that leads to a low caste will definitely result in a low caste, karma that leads to a noble caste will definitely result in a noble caste, karma that leads to lack of wealth will definitely result in lack of wealth, karma that leads to much wealth will definitely result in much wealth, karma that leads to bad wisdom will definitely result in bad wisdom, and karma that leads to good wisdom will definitely result in good wisdom. Ascetic, this is what I said before, that beings are born due to their own karma, they obtain retribution due to karma, they depend on karma, they rely on karma, and beings are placed in high or low places according to their karma."

「摩纳！当知作短寿相应业必得短寿，作长寿相应业必得长寿，作多疾病相应业必得多疾病，作少疾病相应业必得少疾病，作不端正相应业必得不端正，作端正相应业必得端正，作无威德相应业必得无威德，作威德相应业必得威德，作卑贱族相应业必得卑贱族，作尊贵族相应业必得尊贵族，作无财物相应业必得无财物，作多财物相应业必得多财物，作恶智慧相应业必得恶智慧，作善智慧相应业必得善智慧。摩纳！此是我前所说，众生因自行业，因业得报，缘业、依业，业处众生随其高下处妙不妙。」

"Parrot Mānava Dutiya said: "World-Honored One! I understand. Well-Gone One! I know. World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha. May the World-Honored One accept me as a lay follower. From today onward, I take refuge for life until the end of my life. World-Honored One! From today, entering the Dutiya family is like entering the families of lay followers in this Śrāvastī region. May the Dutiya family gain long-term benefits, gain benefit, peace, and happiness.

鹦鹉摩纳都提子白曰：「世尊！我已解。善逝！我已知。世尊！我今自归于佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。世尊！从今日入都提家，如入此舍卫地优婆塞家，令都提家长夜得利义，得饶益安隐快乐。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Parrot Mānava Dutiya and countless others, having heard the Buddha's teachings, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。鹦鹉摩纳都提子及无量众，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
