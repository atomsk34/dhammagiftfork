MĀ 171

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 13 根本分别品

171. （一七一）分别大业经

Parallel to: MN 136

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the city of Rājagṛha, staying in the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove.

一时，佛游王舍城，在竹林迦兰哆园。

At that time, Venerable Samīti was also wandering in the city of Rājagṛha, staying in the meditation hut of the Unoccupied Forest.

尔时，尊者三弥提亦游王舍城，住无事禅屋中。

Then, the non-Buddhist teacher Pūraṇa Kāśyapa wandered about in the middle of the day and went to Venerable Samīti. They exchanged greetings and sat down to one side. "Venerable Samīti, I wish to ask something. May I ask?"

于是，异学哺罗陀子中后彷徉，往诣尊者三弥提所，共相问讯，却坐一面。「贤三弥提！我欲有所问，听我问耶？」

Venerable Samīti replied, "Teacher Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, ask what you wish. I will think about it after hearing it."

尊者三弥提答曰：「贤哺罗陀子！欲问便问，我闻已当思。」

The non-Buddhist teacher Pūraṇa Kāśyapa then asked, "Venerable Samīti, I heard and received from the śramaṇa Gautama face to face that bodily and verbal karma are false, only mental karma is true, and there is a certain concentration where a bhikṣu enters that concentration without any perception."

异学哺罗陀子便问曰：「贤三弥提！我面从沙门瞿昙闻，面从沙门瞿昙受，身、口业虚妄，唯意业真谛，或有定，比丘入彼定无所觉。」

Venerable Samīti said, "Teacher Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, do not say that. Do not slander the World-Honored One. Slandering the World-Honored One is not good. The World-Honored One did not say that. Teacher Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, the World-Honored One has said in countless ways that if one intentionally creates karma and it is completed, I say that one will definitely experience the result, either in this life or the next. If one does not intentionally create karma and it is completed, I do not say that one will definitely experience the result.

尊者三弥提告曰：「贤哺罗陀子！汝莫作是说，莫诬谤世尊，诬谤世尊者为不善也。世尊不如是说。贤哺罗陀子！世尊无量方便说，若故作业，作已成者，我说无不受报，或现世受，或后世受。若不故作业，作已成者，我不说必受报也。」

The non-Buddhist teacher Pūraṇa Kāśyapa spoke to Venerable Samīti again and again, saying, "Venerable Samīti, I heard and received from the śramaṇa Gautama face to face that bodily and verbal karma are false, only mental karma is true, and there is a certain concentration where a bhikṣu enters that concentration without any perception."

异学哺罗陀子至再三语尊者三弥提曰：「贤三弥提！我面从沙门瞿昙闻，面从沙门瞿昙受，身、口业虚妄，唯意业真谛，或有定，比丘入彼定无所觉。」

Venerable Samīti also spoke to him again and again, saying, "Teacher Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, do not say that. Do not slander the World-Honored One. Slandering the World-Honored One is not good. The World-Honored One did not say that. Teacher Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, the World-Honored One has said in countless ways that if one intentionally creates karma and it is completed, I say that one will definitely experience the result, either in this life or the next. If one does not intentionally create karma and it is completed, I do not say that one will definitely experience the result.

尊者三弥提亦再三告曰：「贤哺罗陀子！汝莫作是说，莫诬谤世尊，诬谤世尊者为不善也。世尊不如是说。贤哺罗陀子！世尊无量方便说，若故作业，作已成者，我说无不受报，或现世受，或后世受。若不故作业，作已成者，我不说必受报也。」

The non-Buddhist teacher Pūraṇa Kāśyapa asked Venerable Samīti, "If one intentionally creates karma and it is completed, what result will one experience?"

异学哺罗陀子问尊者三弥提：「若故作业，作已成者，当受何报？」

Venerable Samīti replied, "Teacher Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, if one intentionally creates karma and it is completed, one will definitely experience suffering.

尊者三弥提答曰：「贤哺罗陀子！若故作业，作已成者，必受苦也。」

The non-Buddhist teacher Pūraṇa Kāśyapa further asked Venerable Samīti, "Venerable Samīti, how long have you been studying the path in this Dharma and Vinaya?"

异学哺罗陀子复问尊者三弥提曰：「贤三弥提！汝于此法、律学道几时？」

Venerable Samīti replied, "Teacher Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, I have been studying the path in this Dharma and Vinaya for only a short time, just three years." Then the non-Buddhist teacher Pūraṇa Kāśyapa thought, "Even a young monk can protect his teacher, let alone those who have studied for a long time and are of high rank." Then the non-Buddhist teacher Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, having heard what Venerable Samīti said, neither approved nor disapproved. He then rose from his seat, shook his head, and left.

尊者三弥提答曰：「贤哺罗陀子！我于此法、律学道未久，始三年耳。」于是，异学哺罗陀子便作是念：「年少比丘尚能护师，况复旧学上尊上尊人耶？」于是，异学哺罗陀子闻尊者三弥提所说，不是不非，即从座起，奋头而去。

At that time, Venerable Mahācunda was not far from where Venerable Samīti was walking and sitting during the day. Then Venerable Mahācunda recited and memorized everything that Venerable Samīti had discussed with the non-Buddhist teacher Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, and after thoroughly upholding it, he rose from his seat and went to Venerable Ānanda. They exchanged greetings and sat to one side. Venerable Mahācunda told Venerable Ānanda everything that Venerable Samīti had discussed with the non-Buddhist teacher Pūraṇa Kāśyapa.

彼时，尊者大周那去尊者三弥提昼行坐处不远。于是，尊者大周那谓尊者三弥提与异学哺罗陀子所共论者，彼尽诵习，善受持已，即从座起，往诣尊者阿难所，共相问讯，却坐一面。谓尊者三弥提与异学哺罗陀子所共论者，尽向尊者阿难说之。

Venerable Ānanda, having heard it, said, "Venerable Cunda, because of this discussion, you should go see the Buddha and present it to the World-Honored One. Venerable Cunda, now let us go together to see the Buddha and explain this meaning to the World-Honored One. Perhaps we can hear different teachings from the World-Honored One because of this.

尊者阿难闻已，语曰：「贤者周那！得因此论，可往见佛，奉献世尊。贤者周那！今共诣佛，具向世尊而说此义，或能因是得从世尊闻异法也。」

Then Venerable Ānanda and Venerable Mahācunda went together to see the Buddha. Venerable Mahācunda bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side. Venerable Ānanda bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and stood to one side.

于是，尊者阿难、尊者大周那共往诣佛。尊者大周那稽首佛足，却坐一面，尊者阿难稽首佛足，却住一面。

At that time, Venerable Ānanda said, "Venerable Mahācunda, please speak, please speak.

彼时，尊者阿难语曰：「贤者大周那！可说，可说。」

Then the World-Honored One asked, "Ānanda, what does the monk Cunda want to say?"

于是，世尊问曰：「阿难！周那比丘欲说何事？」

Venerable Ānanda said, "World-Honored One, you will hear it yourself." Then Venerable Mahācunda told the World-Honored One everything that had been discussed between Venerable Samiddhi and the non-Buddhist Pūraṇa Kāśyapa.

尊者阿难白曰：「世尊！今自当闻。」于是，尊者大周那谓尊者三弥提与异学哺罗陀子所共论者尽向佛说。

Having heard this, the World-Honored One said, "Ānanda, look at the foolish and unskillful monk Samiddhi. Why? The non-Buddhist Pūraṇa Kāśyapa asked about something indefinite, but the foolish monk Samiddhi gave a one-sided answer.

世尊闻已，告曰：「阿难！看三弥提比丘痴人无道。所以者何？异学哺罗陀子问事不定，而三弥提比丘痴人一向答也。」

Venerable Ānanda said, "World-Honored One, if monk Samiddhi speaks about this matter, saying that all that is felt is suffering, what fault is there in that?"

尊者阿难白曰：「世尊！若三弥提比丘因此事说，所有觉者是苦，当何咎耶？」

The World-Honored One rebuked Venerable Ānanda, saying, "Look! Venerable Ānanda is also without skill. Ānanda, this foolish monk Samiddhi, when asked by the non-Buddhist Pūraṇa Kāśyapa about the three feelings - pleasant, painful, and neither pleasant nor painful - Ānanda, if the foolish monk Samiddhi were asked by the non-Buddhist Pūraṇa Kāśyapa and answered like this: 'Virtuous Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, if one intentionally creates pleasant karma, having done so, one will experience a pleasant result; if one intentionally creates painful karma, having done so, one will experience a painful result; if one intentionally creates neither pleasant nor painful karma, having done so, one will experience a neither pleasant nor painful result.' Ānanda, if the foolish monk Samiddhi were asked by the non-Buddhist Pūraṇa Kāśyapa and answered like this, the non-Buddhist Pūraṇa Kāśyapa would not even dare to look at the foolish monk Samiddhi, let alone ask such a question. Ānanda, if you ask the Tathāgata to explain the Great Karma Sūtra, you will increase your supreme faith and joy in the Tathāgata even more.

世尊呵尊者阿难曰：「看！阿难比丘亦复无道。阿难！此三弥提痴人，彼异学哺罗陀子尽问三觉，乐觉、苦觉、不苦不乐觉。阿难！若三弥提痴人为异学哺罗陀子所问，如是答者：『贤哺罗陀子！若故作乐业，作已成者，当受乐报；若故作苦业，作已成者，当受苦报；若故作不苦不乐业，作已成者，当受不苦不乐报。』阿难！若三弥提痴人为异学哺罗陀子所问，如是答者，异学哺罗陀子眼尚不敢视三弥提痴人，况复能问如是事耶？阿难！若汝从世尊问分别大业经者，于如来倍复增上心靖得喜。」

Then Venerable Ānanda, with palms joined towards the Buddha, said, "World-Honored One, now is the time. Well-Gone One, now is the time. If the World-Honored One would explain the Great Karma Sūtra to the monks, the monks, having heard it, will uphold it well.

于是，尊者阿难叉手向佛白曰：「世尊！今正是时。善逝！今正是时。若世尊为诸比丘说分别大业经者，诸比丘闻已，当善受持。」

The World-Honored One said, "Ānanda, listen carefully and keep it in mind. I will explain it to you in detail.

世尊告曰：「阿难！谛听，善思念之，我当为汝具分别说。」

Venerable Ānanda said, "Yes," and the monks listened as instructed.

尊者阿难白曰：「唯然。」时，诸比丘受教而听。

The Buddha said, "Ānanda, there are some who do not give up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views. Without giving them up and guarding against them, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in the good realms of the heavens. Ānanda, there are some who give up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views. Having given them up and guarded against them, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in the evil realms of hell. Ānanda, there are some who do not give up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views. Without giving them up and guarding against them, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in the evil realms of hell. Ānanda, there are some who give up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views. Having given them up and guarded against them, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in the good realms of the heavens.

佛言：「阿难！或有一不离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此不离、不护已，身坏命终，生善处天中。阿难！或有一离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此离、护已，身坏命终，生恶处地狱中。阿难！或有一不离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此不离、不护已，身坏命终，生恶处地狱中。阿难！或有一离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此离、护已，身坏命终，生善处天中。

Ānanda, if there is someone who does not give up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views, and without giving them up and guarding against them, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in the good realms of the heavens. If there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who attain the divine eye and accomplish the divine eye, and see them, and having seen them, think: 'There is no bad bodily action, and no retribution for bad bodily action, no bad verbal or mental action, and no retribution for bad verbal or mental action. Why is that? I see that they do not give up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views, and without giving them up and guarding against them, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in the good realms of the heavens. If there are others like this who do not give up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views, and without giving them up and guarding against them, they will all, when their bodies perish and their lives end, also be reborn in the good realms of the heavens.' If they see in this way, it is right view, but if they see otherwise, do they then follow the path of wisdom? If what they see and know is grasped at with all their might, and they insist that this alone is true and the rest is false,

「阿难！若有一不离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此不离、不护已，身坏命终，生善处天中者。若有沙门、梵志得天眼，成就天眼而见彼，见已，作是念：『无身恶行，亦无身恶行报，无口、意恶行，亦无口、意恶行报。所以者何？我见彼不离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此不离、不护已，身坏命终，生善处天中。若更有如是比不离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此不离、不护者，彼一切身坏命终，亦生善处天中。』如是见者，则为正见，异是见者，则彼智趣耶？若所见所知极力扪摸，一向著说，此是真谛，余皆虚妄。

Ānanda, if there is someone who gives up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views, and having given them up and guarded against them, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in the evil realms of hell. If there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who attain the divine eye and accomplish the divine eye, and see them, and having seen them, think: 'There is no good bodily action, and no retribution for good bodily action, no good verbal or mental action, and no retribution for good verbal or mental action. 「阿难！若有一离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此离、护已，身坏命终，生恶处地狱中者。若有沙门、梵志得天眼，成就天眼而见彼，见已，作是念：『无身妙行，亦无身妙行报，无口、意妙行，亦无口、意妙行报。所以者何？我见彼离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此离、护已，身坏命终，生恶处地狱中。若更有如是比离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此离、护者，彼一切身坏命终，亦生恶处地狱中。』如是见者，则为正见，异是见者，则彼智趣耶？若所见所知极力扪摸，一向著说，此是真谛，余皆虚妄。

「阿难！若有一不离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此不离、不护已，身坏命终，生恶处地狱中者。若有沙门、梵志得天眼，成就天眼而见彼，见已，作是念：『有身恶行，亦有身恶行报，有口、意恶行，亦有口、意恶行报。所以者何？我见彼不离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此不离、不护已，身坏命终，生恶处地狱中。若更有如是比不离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此不离、不护者，彼一切身坏命终，亦生恶处地狱中。』如是见者，则为正见，异是见者，则彼智趣耶？若所见所知极力扪摸，一向著说，此是真谛，余皆虚妄。

Ānanda, among them, if there is a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who attains the divine eye and accomplishes the divine eye, and makes such a statement: 'There is no bad bodily action, and no retribution for bad bodily action, no bad verbal or mental action, and no retribution for bad verbal or mental action.' I do not approve of them. If they say: 'I see that they do not give up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views, and without giving them up and guarding against them, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in the good realms of the heavens.' I approve of them. If they say: 'If there are others like this who do not give up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views, and without giving them up and guarding against them, they will all, when their bodies perish and their lives end, also be reborn in the good realms of the heavens.' I do not approve of them. If they say: 'Those who see in this way have right view, but those who see otherwise follow the path of wisdom.' I do not approve of them. If what they see and know is grasped at with all their might, and they insist that this alone is true and the rest is false,

「阿难！于中若有一沙门、梵志得天眼，成就天眼，作如是说：『无身恶行，亦无身恶行报，无口、意恶行，亦无口、意恶行报。』者，我不听彼。若作是说：『我见彼不离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此不离、不护已，身坏命终，生善处天中。』我听彼也。若作是说：『若更有如是比不离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言，乃至邪见，此不离、不护者，彼一切身坏命终，亦生善处天中。』者，我不听彼。若作是说：『如是见者，则为正见，异是见者，则彼智趣邪。』者，我不听彼。若所见所知极力扪摸，一向著说，此是真谛，余皆虚妄者，我不听彼。所以者何？阿难！如来知彼人异。

Ānanda, among them, if there is a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who attains the divine eye and accomplishes the divine eye, and makes such a statement: 'There is no good bodily action, and no retribution for good bodily action, no good verbal or mental action, and no retribution for good verbal or mental action.' I do not approve of them. If they say: 'I see that they give up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views, and having given them up and guarded against them, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in the evil realms of hell.' I approve of them. If they say: 'If there are others like this who give up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views, and having given them up and guarded against them, they will all, when their bodies perish and their lives end, also be reborn in the evil realms of hell.' I do not approve of them. If they say: 'Those who see in this way have right view, but those who see otherwise follow the path of wisdom.' I do not approve of them. If what they see and know is grasped at with all their might, and they insist that this alone is true and the rest is false,

「阿难！于中若有一沙门、梵志得天眼，成就天眼，作如是说：『无身妙行，亦无身妙行报，无口、意妙行，亦无口、意妙行报。』我不听彼。若作是说：『我见彼离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此离、护已，身坏命终，生恶处地狱中。』我听彼也。若作是说：『若更有如是比离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此离、护者，彼一切身坏命终，亦生恶处地狱中。』者，我不听彼。若作是说：『如是见者，则为正见，异是见者，则彼智趣邪。』者，我不听彼。若所见所知极力扪摸，一向著说，此是真谛，余皆虚妄者，我不听彼。所以者何？阿难！如来知彼人异。

Ānanda, among them, if there is a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who attains the divine eye and accomplishes the divine eye, and makes such a statement: 'There is bad bodily action, and retribution for bad bodily action, bad verbal or mental action, and retribution for bad verbal or mental action.' I approve of them. If they say: 'I see that they do not give up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views, and without giving them up and guarding against them, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in the evil realms of hell.' I approve of them. If they say: 'If there are others like this who do not give up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views, and without giving them up and guarding against them, they will all, when their bodies perish and their lives end, also be reborn in the evil realms of hell.' I do not approve of them. If they say: 'Those who see in this way have right view, but those who see otherwise follow the path of wisdom.' I do not approve of them. If what they see and know is grasped at with all their might, and they insist that this alone is true and the rest is false,

「阿难！于中若有一沙门、梵志得天眼，成就天眼，作如是说：『有身恶行，亦有身恶行报，有口、意恶行，亦有口、意恶行报。』我听彼也。若作是说：『我见彼不离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此不离、不护已，身坏命终，生恶处地狱中。』者，我听彼也。若作是说：『若更有如是比不离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此不离、不护者，彼一切身坏命终，亦生恶处地狱中。』者，我不听彼。若作是说：『如是见者，则为正见，异是见者，则彼智趣邪。』者，我不听彼。若所见所知极力扪摸，一向著说，此是真谛，余皆虚妄者，我不听彼。所以者何？阿难！如来知彼人异。

Ānanda, among them, if there is a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who attains the divine eye and accomplishes the divine eye, and makes such a statement: 'There is good bodily action, and retribution for good bodily action, good verbal or mental action, and retribution for good verbal or mental action.' I approve of them. If they say: 'I see that they give up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views, and having given them up and guarded against them, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in the good realms of the heavens.' I approve of them. If they say: 'If there are others like this who give up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views, and having given them up and guarded against them, they will all, when their bodies perish and their lives end, also be reborn in the good realms of the heavens.' I do not approve of them. If they say: 'Those who see in this way have right view, but those who see otherwise follow the path of wisdom.' I do not approve of them. If what they see and know is grasped at with all their might, and they insist that this alone is true and the rest is false,

「阿难！于中若有一沙门、梵志得天眼，成就天眼，作如是说：『有身妙行，亦有身妙行报，有口、意妙行，亦有口、意妙行报。』者，我听彼也。若作是说：『我见彼离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言，乃至邪见，此离、护已，身坏命终，生善处天中。』者，我听彼也。若作是说：『若更有如是比离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言，乃至邪见，彼一切身坏命终，亦生善处天中。』者，我不听彼。若作是说：『如是见者，则为正见，异是见者，则彼智趣邪。』者，我不听彼。若所见所知极力扪摸，一向著说，此是真谛，余皆虚妄者，我不听彼。所以者何？阿难！如来知彼人异。

Ānanda, if there is one who does not give up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views, and without giving them up and guarding against them, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in the good realms of the heavens. If they have previously committed unwholesome karma, having done so and accomplished it, because they did not give it up and guard against it, they will experience the retribution for that in the present life before being reborn there. Or, because of the retribution for that in the future, they will not, because of that cause and condition, when their bodies perish and their lives end, be reborn in the good realms of the heavens. Or, if they have previously committed wholesome karma, having done so and accomplished it, because they gave it up and guarded against it, they have not yet exhausted the retribution for that in the good realms. Because of that cause and condition, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in the good realms of the heavens. Or, at the time of death, they give rise to wholesome thoughts, associated with the mental factors and right view. Because of that cause and condition, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in the good realms of the heavens. Ānanda, the Tathāgata knows that person to be like this.

「阿难！若有一不离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此不离、不护已，身坏命终，生善处天中者，彼若本作不善业，作已成者，因不离、不护故，彼于现法中受报讫而生于彼，或复因后报故，彼不以此因、不以此缘，身坏命终，生善处天中。或复本作善业，作已成者，因离、护故，未尽应受善处报，彼因此缘此故，身坏命终，生善处天中。或复死时生善心，心所有法正见相应，彼因此缘此，身坏命终，生善处天中。阿难！如来知彼人为如是也。

Ānanda, if there is one who gives up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views, and having given them up and guarded against them, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in the evil realms of hell. If they have previously committed wholesome karma, having done so and accomplished it, because they gave it up and guarded against it, they will experience the retribution for that in the present life before being reborn there. Or, because of the retribution for that in the future, they will not, because of that cause and condition, when their bodies perish and their lives end, be reborn in the evil realms of hell. Or, if they have previously committed unwholesome karma, having done so and accomplished it, because they did not give it up and guard against it, they have not yet exhausted the retribution for that in the evil realms. Because of that cause and condition, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in the evil realms of hell. Ānanda, the Tathāgata knows that person to be like this.

「阿难！若有一离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此离、护已，身坏命终，生恶处地狱中者，彼若本作善业，作已成者，因离、护故，彼于现法中受报讫而生于彼。或复因后报故，彼不以此因、不以此缘，身坏命终，生恶处地狱中。或复本作不善业，作已成者，因不离、不护故，未尽应受地狱报，彼因此缘此，身坏命终，生恶处地狱中。或复死时生不善心，心所有法邪见相应，彼因此缘此，身坏命终，生恶处地狱中。阿难！如来知彼人为如是也。

Ānanda, if there is one who does not give up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views, and without giving them up and guarding against them, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in the evil realms of hell. If they have previously committed unwholesome karma, having done so and accomplished it, because they did not give it up and guard against it, they will experience the retribution for that in the present life before being reborn there. 「阿难！若有一不离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此不离、不护已，身坏命终，生恶处地狱中者，彼即因此缘此，身坏命终，生恶处地狱中。或复本作不善业，作已成者，因不离、不护故，未尽应受地狱报，彼因此缘此，身坏命终，生恶处地狱中。或复死时生不善心，心所有法邪见相应，彼因此缘此，身坏命终，生恶处地狱中。阿难！如来知彼人为如是也。

Ānanda, if there is one who gives up killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and even wrong views, and having given them up and guarded against them, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are reborn in the good realms of heaven. If they have previously committed wholesome karma, having done so and accomplished it, because they gave it up and guarded against it, they will experience the retribution for that in the present life before being reborn there. 「阿难！若有一离杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言乃至邪见，此离、护已，身坏命终，生善处天中者，彼即因此缘此，身坏命终，生善处天中。或复本作善业，作已成者，因离、护故，未尽应受报，彼因此缘此，身坏命终，生善处天中。或复死时生善心，心所有法正见相应，彼因此缘此，身坏命终，生善处天中。阿难！如来知彼人为如是也。

"Furthermore, there are four kinds of people. Some have what seems to be, but not really. Some have what seems to be not, but really is. Some have what seems to be not, and is not. Some have what seems to be, and is. Ānanda, it is like the four kinds of wood. Some wood is unripe but seems ripe. Some is ripe but seems unripe. Some is unripe and seems unripe. Some is ripe and seems ripe. In this way, Ānanda, the four kinds of wood are used as analogies for people. Some people have what seems to be, but not really. Some have what seems to be not, but really is. Some have what seems to be not, and is not. Some have what seems to be, and is."

「复次，有四种人，或有人无有似有，或有似无有，或无有似无有，或有似有。阿难！犹如四种［木＊奈］，或［木＊奈］不熟似熟，或熟似不熟，或不熟似不熟，或熟似熟。如是，阿难！四种［木＊奈］喻人，或有人无有似有，或有似无有，或无有似无有，或有似有。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The Venerable Ānanda and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说如是。尊者阿难及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

The tenth chapter of the Sutra on the Differentiation of Great Activities is completed.
