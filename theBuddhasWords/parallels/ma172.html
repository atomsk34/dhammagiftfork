MĀ 172

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 14 心品

172. （一七二）心经

Parallel to: AN 4.186

mind, floating, feeling, Dharma, two, practice, meditation, teaching
hunter, five branches, property owner, Gautami, many realms

心、浮、受法二　　行禅、说、猎师五枝财物主　　瞿昙弥、多界

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Shravasti, staying at the Jetavana Anathapindika Monastery in the Anathapindaka Grove.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, a certain bhikṣu was alone in a quiet place, sitting in meditation and reflecting, thinking in his mind, "Who takes the world away? Who is attached to it? Who creates self-mastery?" Then, at dusk, the bhikṣu rose from his meditation and went to where the Buddha was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said, "World-Honored One! I was alone in a quiet place, sitting in meditation and reflecting, thinking in my mind, 'Who takes the world away? Who is attached to it? Who creates self-mastery?'"

尔时，有一比丘独安靖处宴坐思惟，心作是念：「谁将世间去？谁为染著？谁起自在？」彼时，比丘则于晡时从宴坐起，往诣佛所，稽首礼足，却坐一面，白曰：「世尊！我今独安靖处宴坐思惟，心作是念：『谁将世间去？谁为染著？谁起自在？』」

Having heard this, the World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Bhikṣu! It is said that there is the noble path and noble contemplation, with supreme eloquence and good reflection. 'Who takes the world away? Who is attached to it? Who creates self-mastery?' Bhikṣu! Is your question like this?"

世尊闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！比丘！谓有贤道而有贤观，极妙辩才，有善思惟。『谁将世间去？谁为染著？谁起自在？』比丘！所问为如是耶？」

The bhikṣu replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「如是。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "Bhikṣu! The mind takes the world away, the mind is attached, the mind creates self-mastery. Bhikṣu! It is that which takes the world away, that which is attached, that which creates self-mastery. Bhikṣu! The well-learned noble disciple is not taken away by the mind, is not attached to the mind, and is not self-mastered by the mind. Bhikṣu! The well-learned noble disciple does not follow the mind's self-mastery, but the mind follows the well-learned noble disciple."

世尊告曰：「比丘！心将世间去，心为染著，心起自在。比丘！彼将世间去，彼为染著，彼起自在。比丘！多闻圣弟子非心将去，非心染著，非心自在。比丘！多闻圣弟子不随心自在，而心随多闻圣弟子。」

The bhikṣu said, "Excellent! Excellent! Yes, World-Honored One!" At that time, the bhikṣu, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

比丘白曰：「善哉！善哉！唯然。世尊！」彼时，比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

He asked, "World-Honored One! You speak of a well-learned bhikṣu. World-Honored One! What is a well-learned bhikṣu? How is a well-learned bhikṣu established?"

问曰：「世尊！多闻比丘，说多闻比丘。世尊！云何多闻比丘？云何施设多闻比丘？」

Having heard this, the World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Bhikṣu! There is the noble path and noble contemplation, with supreme eloquence and good contemplation. 'World-Honored One! You speak of a well-learned bhikṣu. World-Honored One! What is a well-learned bhikṣu? How is a well-learned bhikṣu established?' Bhikṣu! Is your question like this?"

世尊闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！比丘！谓有贤道而有贤观，极妙辩才，有善思惟。『世尊！多闻比丘，说多闻比丘。世尊！云何多闻比丘？云何施设多闻比丘？』比丘！所问为如是耶？」

The bhikṣu replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「如是。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "Bhikṣu! I have spoken much, namely, the true sūtras, songs, verses, causes and conditions, compilations, past lives, this teaching, birth stories, extensive explanations, the unheard-of Dharma, and the explanation of the meaning. Bhikṣu! If there is a noble son who knows the meaning and the Dharma of the four-line verses I have spoken, and who practices the Dharma and follows the Dharma, he is practicing the holy life. Bhikṣu! Speaking of a well-learned bhikṣu, there is no further surpassing this. Bhikṣu! A well-learned bhikṣu is established by the Tathāgata in this way."

世尊告曰：「比丘！我所说甚多，谓正经、歌咏、记说、偈他、因缘、撰录、本起、此说、生处、广解、未曾有法及说义。比丘！若有族姓子，我所说四句偈，知义知法，趣法向法，趣顺梵行。比丘！说多闻比丘无复过是。比丘！如是多闻比丘，如来如是施设多闻比丘。」

The bhikṣu said, "Excellent! Excellent! Yes, World-Honored One!" At that time, the bhikṣu, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

比丘白曰：「善哉！善哉！唯然。世尊！」彼时，比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

He asked, "World-Honored One! A well-learned bhikṣu is clear and wise. Speaking of a well-learned bhikṣu being clear and wise, World-Honored One! How is a well-learned bhikṣu clear and wise? How is a well-learned bhikṣu established as clear and wise?"

问曰：「世尊！多闻比丘明达智慧，说多闻比丘明达智慧。世尊！云何多闻比丘明达智慧？云何施设多闻比丘明达智慧？」

Having heard this, the World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Bhikṣu! There is the noble path and noble contemplation, with supreme eloquence and good contemplation. 'World-Honored One! A well-learned bhikṣu is clear and wise. Speaking of a well-learned bhikṣu being clear and wise, World-Honored One! How is a well-learned bhikṣu clear and wise? How is a well-learned bhikṣu established as clear and wise?' Bhikṣu! Is your question like this?"

世尊闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！比丘！谓有贤道而有贤观，极妙辩才，有善思惟。『世尊！多闻比丘明达智慧，说多闻比丘明达智慧。世尊！云何多闻比丘明达智慧？云何施设多闻比丘明达智慧？』比丘！所问为如是耶？」

The bhikṣu replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「如是。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "Bhikṣu! If a bhikṣu hears this suffering and further sees suffering with right wisdom as it truly is, and hears the origin, cessation, and path of suffering, and further sees the cessation of suffering and the path to the cessation of suffering with right wisdom as it truly is, bhikṣu! A well-learned bhikṣu is clear and wise in this way. The Tathāgata establishes a well-learned bhikṣu as clear and wise in this way."

世尊告曰：「比丘！若比丘闻此苦，复以慧正见苦如真者，闻苦习、苦灭、苦灭道，复以慧正见苦灭道如真者，比丘！如是多闻比丘明达智慧，如来如是施设多闻比丘明达智慧。」

The bhikṣu said, "Excellent! Excellent! Yes, World-Honored One!" At that time, the bhikṣu, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

比丘白曰：「善哉！善哉！唯然。世尊！」彼时，比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

"World-Honored One! The wise bhikṣu, the wise bhikṣu of great wisdom, speaks of the wise bhikṣu, the wise bhikṣu of great wisdom. World-Honored One! How is the wise bhikṣu, the wise bhikṣu of great wisdom? How is the wise bhikṣu, the wise bhikṣu of great wisdom, established?"

问曰：「世尊！聪明比丘黠慧广慧，说聪明比丘黠慧广慧。世尊！云何聪明比丘黠慧广慧？云何施设聪明比丘黠慧广慧？」

The World-Honored One, having heard this, praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Bhikṣu, there is the noble path and noble contemplation, the utmost fine eloquence, and good contemplation. 'World-Honored One! How is the wise bhikṣu, the wise bhikṣu of great wisdom? How is the wise bhikṣu, the wise bhikṣu of great wisdom, established?' Bhikṣu, is your question like this?"

世尊闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！比丘，谓有贤道而有贤观，极妙辩才，有善思惟。『世尊！云何聪明比丘黠慧广慧？云何施设聪明比丘黠慧广慧？』比丘！所问为如是耶？」

The bhikṣu replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「如是。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "If a bhikṣu does not think of harming himself, harming others, or harming both, but only thinks of benefiting himself, benefiting others, benefiting many people, having compassion for the world, seeking benefit and benefit for the sake of gods and humans, seeking peace and happiness, then such a wise bhikṣu is wise and has great wisdom. The Tathāgata establishes such a wise bhikṣu as wise and having great wisdom."

世尊告曰：「若比丘不念自害，不念害他，亦不念俱害，比丘但念自饶益及饶益他，饶益多人，愍伤世间，为天、为人求义及饶益，求安隐快乐。比丘！如是聪明比丘黠慧广慧，如是如来施设聪明比丘黠慧广慧。」

The bhikṣu said, "Excellent! Excellent! Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘白曰：「善哉！善哉！唯然。世尊！」

At that time, the bhikṣu, having heard the Buddha's teaching, received it well, upheld it well, and recited it well. He then rose from his seat, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and returned.

彼时，比丘闻佛所说，善受善持，善诵习已，即从坐起，稽首佛足，绕三匝而还。

At that time, the bhikṣu, having received the World-Honored One's teaching, dwelt in seclusion, alone, without negligence, practicing diligently. Having dwelt in seclusion, alone, without negligence, and practicing diligently, the noble son, for the sake of shaving his head and beard, putting on the dyed robe, with true faith leaving home, becoming a homeless one, and practicing the path, completed the unsurpassed holy life, in this very life, he himself knew, himself realized, himself accomplished, and dwelt, with the end of birth, the holy life established, what was to be done done, no more coming to any state of existence, knowing as it really is. Having known the Dharma, he attained arahantship.

彼时，比丘闻世尊教，在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤。彼在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤已，族姓子所为，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，唯无上梵行讫，于现法中自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。彼尊者知法已，乃至得阿罗诃。

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and upheld it.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
