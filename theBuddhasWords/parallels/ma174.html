MĀ 174

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 14 心品

174. （一七四）受法经

Parallel to: MN 45

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus: "In the world, there are truly four kinds of feelings. What are the four? There are feelings that are pleasant in the present but have painful consequences in the future, there are feelings that are painful in the present but have pleasant consequences in the future, there are feelings that are painful in the present and also have painful consequences in the future, and there are feelings that are pleasant in the present and also have pleasant consequences in the future.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「世间真实有四种受法。云何为四？或有受法现乐、当来受苦报，或有受法现苦、当来受乐报，或有受法现苦、当来亦受苦报，或有受法现乐、当来亦受乐报。

What are the feelings that are pleasant in the present but have painful consequences in the future? There are some śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who enjoy themselves with well-adorned women, saying: 'What fears and calamities do these śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas see in future desires that they abandon desires and establish the abandonment of desires? This well-adorned body of a woman is pleasurable and delightful to touch.' They enjoy themselves with these women, playing with them. Having perfected this practice, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they go to a bad place and are reborn in hell. Only then do they think: 'Those śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas saw these fears and calamities in future desires, and therefore abandoned desires and established the abandonment of desires. Because of desire, quarreling over desire, and relying on desire, we have experienced such extreme suffering and severe pain.'

「云何受法现乐、当来受苦报？或有沙门、梵志，快庄严女共相娱乐，作如是说：『此沙门、梵志于欲见当来有何恐怖，有何灾患，而断于欲，施设断欲？此快庄严于女身体乐更乐触。』彼与此女共相娱乐，于中游戏，彼受此法，成具足已，身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中，方作是念：『彼沙门、梵志于欲见此当来恐怖，见此灾患故，断于欲，施设断欲。我等因欲、诤欲、缘欲故，受如是极苦、甚重苦也。』

It is like the hottest time of day in the late spring, when a seed of a vine is scorched and bursts open, falling to the ground beneath a śāla tree. At that time, the śāla tree spirit is frightened by this. Then, the village spirits and spirits of the hundred grains, medicines, and trees who are close friends of the village spirits and spirits of the hundred grains, medicines, and trees see the future fears and calamities of the seed, and they go to the tree spirit and comfort it, saying: 'Tree spirit, do not be afraid! Tree spirit, do not be afraid! Now this seed may be eaten by deer, peacocks, or blown away by the wind, or burned by village fires, wild fires, or destroyed and become unviable. In this way, tree spirit, you will be safe. If this seed is not eaten by deer, peacocks, or blown away by the wind, or burned by village fires, wild fires, or destroyed and become unviable, and if the seed is not damaged, pierced, or split open, and is not harmed by wind, rain, or sun, and is soaked by heavy rain, it will quickly grow.'

「犹春后月，日中极热，有葛藤子，曰炙圯迸，堕一娑罗树下。彼时，娑罗树神因此故而生恐怖，于是，彼树神若边傍种子村神、村百谷药木有亲亲朋友树神，于种子见当来有恐怖、有灾患故，便往至彼树神所，而慰劳曰：『树神勿怖！树神勿怖！今此种子或为鹿食，或孔雀食，或风吹去，或村火烧，或野火烧，或败坏不成种子。如是，树神！汝得安隐。若此种子非为鹿食，非孔雀食，非风吹去，非村火烧，非野火烧，亦非败坏不成种子，此种子不缺不穿，亦不剖坼，不为风、雨、日所中伤，得大雨渍，便速生也。』

"The tree spirit thinks: 'For what reason do those village spirits and spirits of the hundred grains, medicines, and trees who are close friends of the village spirits and spirits of the hundred grains, medicines, and trees, see the future fears and calamities of the seed, and come to comfort me, saying: "Tree spirit, do not be afraid! Tree spirit, do not be afraid! Tree spirit, this seed may be eaten by deer, peacocks, or blown away by the wind, or burned by village fires, wild fires, or destroyed and become unviable. In this way, tree spirit, you will be safe. If this seed is not eaten by deer, peacocks, or blown away by the wind, or burned by village fires, wild fires, or destroyed and become unviable, and if the seed is not damaged, pierced, or split open, and is not harmed by wind, rain, or sun, and is soaked by heavy rain, it will quickly grow."'" The stem, branches, and leaves become soft and form joints, which are pleasant to the touch. The stem, branches, and leaves become soft and form joints, which are pleasant to the touch, and are pleasurable and delightful to touch. This causes the tree to form large branches, joints, and leaves, which wrap around the tree and cover it above. After covering it above, the tree spirit then thinks: 'Those village spirits and spirits of the hundred grains, medicines, and trees who are close friends of the village spirits and spirits of the hundred grains, medicines, and trees, see these future fears and calamities of the seed, and come to comfort me, saying: "Tree spirit, do not be afraid! Tree spirit, do not be afraid! This seed may be eaten by deer, peacocks, or blown away by the wind, or burned by village fires, wild fires, or destroyed and become unviable. In this way, tree spirit, you will be safe. If this seed is not eaten by deer, peacocks, or blown away by the wind, or burned by village fires, wild fires, or destroyed and become unviable, and if the seed is not damaged, pierced, or split open, and is not harmed by wind, rain, or sun, and is soaked by heavy rain, it will quickly grow." I experience this extreme suffering and severe pain because of the seed and relying on the seed.

「彼树神而作是念：『以何等故？彼边傍种子村神、村百谷药木亲亲朋友树神，于种子见当来有何恐怖？有何灾患？而来慰劳我言：「树神勿怖！树神勿怖！树神！此种] cf.［丽】子或为鹿食，或孔雀食，或风吹去，或村火烧，或野火烧，或败坏不成种子。如是，树神！汝得安隐。若此种子非为鹿食，非孔雀食，非风吹去，非村火烧，非野火烧，亦非败坏不成种子，此种子不缺不穿，亦不剖坼，不为风、雨、日所中伤，得大雨渍，便速生也。」』成茎枝叶柔软成节，触体喜悦，此茎枝叶柔软成节，触体喜悦，乐更乐触。此缘树成大枝节叶，缠裹彼树，覆盖在上。覆盖在上已，彼树神方作是念：『彼边傍种子村神、村百谷药木亲亲朋友树神，于种子见此当来恐怖，见此灾患故，而来慰劳我言：「树神勿怖！树神勿怖！此种子或为鹿食，或孔雀食，或风吹去，或村火烧，或野火烧，或败坏不成种子。如是，树神！汝得安隐。若此种子非为鹿食，非孔雀食，非风吹去，非村火烧，非野火烧，亦非败坏不成种子，此种子不缺不穿，亦不剖坼，不为风、雨、日所中伤，得大雨渍，便速生也。」我因种子、缘种子故，受此极苦甚重苦也。』

In the same way, some śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas enjoy themselves with a well-adorned woman, saying: 'These śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas see what fears and calamities in the future regarding desire, and therefore abandon desire and establish the abandonment of desire? This well-adorned woman's body is pleasurable and delightful to touch.' They enjoy themselves with this woman, play with her, and after attaining this teaching, when their bodies break up and their lives end, they go to a bad place, are born in hell, and only then think: 'Those śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas see these future fears and calamities regarding desire, and therefore abandon desire and establish the abandonment of desire. Because of desire, quarreling over desire, and relying on desire, we experience this extreme suffering and severe pain.' This is called experiencing pleasure in the present but experiencing suffering in the future.

「如是，或有沙门、梵志，快庄严女共相娱乐，作如是说：『此沙门、梵志于欲见当来有何恐怖，有何灾患，而断于欲，施设断欲？此快庄严于女身体乐更乐触。』彼与此女共相娱乐，于中游戏，彼受此法，成具足已，身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中，方作是念：『彼沙门、梵志于欲见此当来恐怖，见此灾患故，断于欲，施设断欲。我等因欲、诤欲、缘欲故，受如是极苦甚重苦也。』是谓受法现乐、当来受苦报。

What is the teaching of experiencing suffering in the present but experiencing pleasure in the future? Some naturally have heavy defilements of desire, anger, and delusion. They often follow a mind of desire, experiencing suffering and distress. They often follow a mind of anger and delusion, experiencing suffering and distress. They experience suffering and distress throughout their lives, practicing the holy life, even crying and shedding tears. After attaining this teaching, when their bodies break up and their lives end, they will definitely ascend to a good place and be born among the gods. This is called experiencing suffering in the present but experiencing pleasure in the future.

「云何受法现苦、当来受乐报？或有一自然重浊欲、重浊恚、重浊痴，彼数随欲心，受苦忧戚，数随恚心、痴心，受苦忧戚。彼以苦以忧，尽其形寿，修行梵行，乃至啼泣堕泪，彼受此法，成具足已，身坏命终，必升善处，生于天中，是谓受法现苦、当来受乐报。

What is the teaching of experiencing suffering in the present and experiencing suffering in the future? Some śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas go naked, without clothes, using hands as clothes, or leaves as clothes, or beads as clothes. They do not take water with a pot, or take water with a ladle. They do not eat food obtained by violence, or food obtained by deception. They do not go themselves, or send messages. They do not eat in the presence of a pregnant woman, or in the presence of a dog. If flies come to a house, they do not eat. They do not eat fish, or meat, or drink alcohol, or drink bad water, or drink at all. They practice the practice of not drinking. They eat one mouthful, considering one mouthful sufficient. Or two, three, four, up to seven mouthfuls, considering seven mouthfuls sufficient. They eat one meal, considering one meal sufficient. Or two, three, four, five, six, seven days, half a month, or one month, eating one meal, considering one meal sufficient. They eat vegetables, or eat millet, or eat rice, or eat mixed flour, or eat head-to-head food, or eat coarse food. They go to a place without affairs, relying on affairs without affairs. They eat roots, or eat fruits, or eat fruits that fall by themselves. They wear a robe made of rags, or wear a robe made of hair, or wear a robe made of head-to-head cloth, or wear a robe made of hair head-to-head cloth. They wear whole leather, or wear pierced leather, or wear whole and pierced leather. They wear loose hair, or wear braided hair, or wear loose and braided hair. Some shave their hair, some shave their beards, some shave their hair and beards. Some pull out their hair, some pull out their beards, some pull out their hair and beards. They stand still, cut off sitting, practice squatting, some lie on thorns, using thorns as beds, some lie on grass, using grass as beds. Some have water affairs, hand-pumping day and night. Some have fire affairs, burning them all. They serve the sun, moon, honored ones, and great virtuous ones, joining their palms towards them. They experience immeasurable suffering like this, practicing the practice of affliction. After attaining this teaching, when their bodies break up and their lives end, they will definitely go to a bad place and be born in hell. This is called experiencing suffering in the present and experiencing suffering in the future.

「云何受法现苦、当来亦受苦报？或有沙门、梵志裸形无衣，或以手为衣，或以叶为衣，或以珠为衣，或不以瓶取水，或不以櫆取水，不食刀杖劫抄之食，不食欺妄食，不自往，不遣信，不来尊，不善尊、不住尊。若有二人食，不在中食，不怀妊家食，不畜狗家食，家有粪蝇飞来而不食，不噉鱼，不食肉，不饮酒，不饮恶水，或都不饮，学无饮行。或噉一口，以一口为足，或二、三、四，乃至七口，以七口为足。或食一得，以一得为足，或二、三、四，乃至七得，以七得为足。或日一食，以一食为足，或二、三、四、五、六、七日、半月、一月一食，以一食为足。或食菜茹] cf.［丽】，或食稗子，或食［楑－天＋示］米，或食杂［麸－夫＋黄］，或食头头逻食，或食麁食。或至无事处，依于无事，或食根，或食果，或食自落果。或持连合衣，或持毛衣，或持头舍衣，或持毛头舍衣，或持全皮，或持穿皮，或持全穿皮。或持散发，或持编发，或持散编发，或有剃发，或有剃须，或剃须发，或有拔发，或有拔须，或拔须发。或住立断坐，或修蹲行，或有卧刺，以刺为床，或有卧草，以草为床。或有事水，昼夜手抒。或有事火，竟昔燃之。或事日、月、尊祐大德，叉手向彼。如此之比，受无量苦，学烦热行，彼受此法，成具足已，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中，是谓受法现苦、当来亦受苦报。

What is the teaching of experiencing pleasure in the present and experiencing pleasure in the future? Some naturally have no heavy defilements of desire, anger, and delusion. They do not often follow a mind of desire, experiencing suffering and distress. They do not often follow a mind of anger and delusion, experiencing suffering and distress. They experience pleasure and joy throughout their lives, practicing the holy life, even rejoicing in their minds. After attaining this teaching, with the five lower fetters exhausted, they are reborn there and attain parinirvāṇa, attaining the non-retrogressive state and not returning to this world. This is called experiencing pleasure in the present and experiencing pleasure in the future. There are indeed these four kinds of teachings in the world. Therefore it is said.

「云何受法现乐、当来亦受乐报？或有一自然不重浊欲、不重浊恚、不重浊痴，彼不数随欲心，受苦忧戚，不数随恚心、痴心，受苦忧戚，彼以乐以喜，尽其形寿，修行梵行，乃至欢悦心。彼受此法，成具足已，五下分结尽，化生于彼而般涅槃，得不退法，不还此世。是谓受法现乐、当来亦受乐报。世间真实有是四种受法者，因此故说。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced them.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
