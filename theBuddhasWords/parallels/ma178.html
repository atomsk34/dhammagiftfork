MĀ 178

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 14 心品

178. （一七八）猎师经

Parallel to: MN 25

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the city of Rājagṛha, staying in the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove.

一时，佛游王舍城，在竹林迦兰哆园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus: "The hunter feeds the deer not with the thought of making them fat, healthy, strong, happy, and long-lived. The hunter feeds the deer with the thought of feeding them only to get close to them, and once they are close, to make them arrogant, indulgent, and careless. Once they are careless, they will follow the hunter and his retinue. This is how the hunter feeds the deer.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「猎师饲鹿，不如是心，令鹿得肥、得色、得力、得乐、长寿。猎师饲鹿，如是心饲，唯欲近食，近食已，令憍恣放逸，放逸已，随猎师、猎师眷属，猎师饲鹿，如是心也。

The first group of deer gets close to the hunter's food, and once they get close, they become arrogant, indulgent, and careless. Once they are careless, they follow the hunter and his retinue. Thus, that first group of deer does not escape the hunter's and his retinue's domain.

「第一群鹿近食猎师食，彼近食已，便憍恣放逸，放逸已，便随猎师、猎师眷属。如是彼第一群鹿不脱猎师、猎师眷属境界。

The second group of deer thinks: 'The first group of deer gets close to the hunter's food, and once they get close, they become arrogant, indulgent, and careless. Once they are careless, they follow the hunter and his retinue. Thus, that first group of deer does not escape the hunter's and his retinue's domain. Should I now not eat the hunter's food, be free from fear, and rely on a place free from trouble to eat grass and drink water?' Having thought this, the second group of deer then abandons the hunter's food, is free from fear, and relies on a place free from trouble to eat grass and drink water. When the grass and water run out in the late spring months, their bodies become extremely emaciated and their strength declines, and they follow the hunter and his retinue. Thus, that second group of deer also does not escape the hunter's and his retinue's domain.

「第二群鹿而作是念：『第一群鹿近食猎师食，彼近食已，便憍恣放逸，放逸已，便随猎师、猎师眷属，如是第一群鹿不脱猎师、猎师眷属境界。我今宁可不食猎师食，离于恐怖，依无事处，食草饮水耶？』第二群鹿作是念已，便舍猎师食，离于恐怖，依无事处，食草饮水。彼春后月诸草水尽，身体极羸，气力衰退，便随猎师、猎师眷属。如是彼第二群鹿亦复不脱猎师、猎师眷属境界。

The third group of deer also thinks: 'The first and second groups of deer all do not escape the hunter's and his retinue's domain. Should I now be apart from the hunter and his retinue, and rely on a place not far away? Once I rely on a place not far away, I will not get close to the hunter's food, and once I do not get close, I will not become arrogant, indulgent, and careless. Once I am not careless, I will not follow the hunter and his retinue.' Having thought this, the third group of deer then is apart from the hunter and his retinue, and relies on a place not far away. Once they rely on a place not far away, they do not get close to the hunter's food, and once they do not get close, they do not become arrogant, indulgent, and careless. Once they are not careless, they do not follow the hunter and his retinue. The hunter and his retinue then think: 'The third group of deer is very cunning and extremely cunning. Why is that? They eat our food but cannot be caught. We should now set up a long enclosure, and once we set up a long enclosure, we will be able to catch the third group of deer where they rely on.' Having thought this, the hunter and his retinue then set up a long enclosure, and once they set up a long enclosure, they are able to catch the third group of deer where they rely. Thus, that third group of deer also does not escape the hunter's and his retinue's domain.

「第三群鹿亦作是念：『第一、第二群鹿一切不脱猎师、猎师眷属境界，我今宁可离猎师、猎师眷属，依住不远，住不远已，不近食猎师食，不近食已，便不憍恣放逸，不放逸已，便不随猎师、猎师眷属。』第三群鹿作是念已，便离猎师、猎师眷属，依住不远，住不远已，不近食猎师食，不近食已，便不憍恣放逸，不放逸已，便不随猎师、猎师眷属。彼猎师、猎师眷属便作是念：『第三群鹿甚奇谄黠，极谄黠。所以者何？食我食已，而不可得，我今宁可作长围罝，作长围罝已，便得第三群鹿所依住止。』猎师、猎师眷属作是念已，便作长围罝，作长围罝已，便得第三群鹿所依住止，如是第三群鹿亦复不脱猎师、猎师眷属境界。

The fourth group of deer also thinks: 'The first, second, and third groups of deer all do not escape the hunter's and his retinue's domain. Should I now rely on a place where the hunter and his retinue do not go? Once I rely on that place, I will not get close to the hunter's food, and once I do not get close, I will not become arrogant, indulgent, and careless. Once I am not careless, I will not follow the hunter and his retinue.' Having thought this, the fourth group of deer then relies on a place where the hunter and his retinue do not go. Once they rely on that place, they do not get close to the hunter's food, and once they do not get close, they do not become arrogant, indulgent, and careless. Once they are not careless, they do not follow the hunter and his retinue. The hunter and his retinue then think again: 'The fourth group of deer is very fierce and the most fierce. If we chase them, we will definitely not be able to catch them. The other deer will then be frightened and scatter. We should now abandon the enclosure and the fourth group of deer.' Having thought this, the hunter and his retinue then abandon the enclosure. Thus, that fourth group of deer is then able to escape the hunter's and his retinue's domain.

「第四群鹿亦作是念：『第一、第二、第三群鹿一切不脱猎师、猎师眷属境界，我今宁可依住猎师、猎师眷属所不至处，依住彼已，不近食猎师食，不近食已，便不憍恣放逸，不放逸已，便不随猎师、猎师眷属。』第四群鹿作是念已，便依住猎师、猎师眷属所不至处，依住彼已，便不近食猎师食，不近食已，便不憍恣放逸，不放逸已，便不随猎师、猎师眷属。彼猎师、猎师眷属复作是念：『第四群鹿甚奇猛俊，第一猛俊，若我逐彼，必不能得，余鹿则当恐怖惊散，我今宁可舍罝第四群鹿。』猎师、猎师眷属作是念已，则便舍罝。如是第四群鹿便得脱猎师、猎师眷属境界。

Bhikkhus! I tell this parable to make you understand the meaning. I now tell this, you should contemplate its meaning. The hunter's food should be known as the five sense pleasures: the eye knows forms, the ear knows sounds, the nose knows smells, the tongue knows tastes, and the body knows tactile objects. The hunter's food should be known as these five sense pleasures. The hunter should be known as the evil Mara. The hunter's retinue should be known as Mara's retinue. The herd of deer should be known as the śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas. The first śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa who approaches and eats the food of Mara, the food of worldly offerings, having approached and eaten it, becomes arrogant, indulgent, and careless. Having become careless, they follow Mara and Mara's retinue. Thus, the first śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa do not escape the domain of Mara. It is like the first herd of deer who approach and eat the hunter's food. Having approached and eaten it, they become arrogant, indulgent, and careless. Having become careless, they follow the hunter and the hunter's retinue. Thus, the first herd of deer do not escape the domain of the hunter and the hunter's retinue. Consider that the first śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa are also like this.

「比丘！我说此喻，欲令解义，我今说此，当观其义。猎师食者，当知五欲功德，眼知色、耳知声、鼻知香、舌知味、身知触。猎师食者，当知是五欲功德也。猎师者，当知是恶魔王也。猎师眷属者，当知是魔王眷属也。群鹿者，当知是沙门、梵志也。第一沙门、梵志近食魔王食，世间信施食，彼近食已，便憍恣放逸，放逸已，便随魔王、魔王眷属，如是第一沙门、梵志不脱魔王境界。犹如第一群鹿近食猎师食，彼近食已，便憍恣放逸，放逸已，便随猎师、猎师眷属，如是第一群鹿不脱猎师、猎师眷属境界。当观彼第一沙门、梵志亦复如是。

The second śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa also thinks: 'The first śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa approach and eat the food of Mara, the food of worldly offerings. Having approached and eaten it, they become arrogant, indulgent, and careless. Having become careless, they follow Mara and Mara's retinue. Thus, the first śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa do not escape the domain of Mara and Mara's retinue. Should I now abandon the food of worldly offerings, be free from fear, rely on a place free from troubles, and eat fruits and roots?' Having thought this, the second śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa then abandons the food of worldly offerings, is free from fear, relies on a place free from troubles, and eats fruits and roots. In the later spring months, all the fruits and roots are exhausted, their bodies become extremely emaciated, and their strength declines. Having declined, their mind liberation and wisdom liberation decline. Having declined in mind liberation and wisdom liberation, they follow Mara and Mara's retinue. Thus, the second śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa also do not escape the domain of Mara and Mara's retinue. It is like the second herd of deer who think: 'The first herd of deer approach and eat the hunter's food. Having approached and eaten it, they become arrogant, indulgent, and careless. Having become careless, they follow the hunter and the hunter's retinue. Thus, the first herd of deer do not escape the domain of the hunter and the hunter's retinue. Should we now abandon the hunter's food, be free from fear, rely on a place free from troubles, and eat grass and drink water?' The second herd of deer, having thought this, then abandon the hunter's food, are free from fear, rely on a place free from troubles, and eat grass and drink water. In the later spring months, all the grass and water are exhausted, their bodies become extremely emaciated, and their strength declines. They then follow the hunter and the hunter's retinue. Thus, the second herd of deer also do not escape the domain of the hunter and the hunter's retinue. Consider that the second śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa are also like this.

「第二沙门、梵志亦作是念：『第一沙门、梵志近食魔王食，世间信施食，彼近食已，便憍恣放逸，放逸已，便随魔王、魔王眷属，如是彼第一沙门、梵志不脱魔王、魔王眷属境界。我今宁可舍世间信施食，离于恐怖，依无事处，食果及根。』第二沙门、梵志作是念已，便舍世间信施食，离于恐怖，依无事处，食果及根。彼春后月诸果根尽，身体极羸，气力衰退，衰退已，便心解脱、慧解脱衰退，心解脱、慧解脱衰退已，便随魔王、魔王眷属，如是第二沙门、梵志亦不脱魔王、魔王眷属境界。犹如第二群鹿而作是念：『第一群鹿近食猎师食，彼近食已，便憍恣放逸，放逸已，便随猎师、猎师眷属，如是第一群鹿不脱猎师、猎师眷属境界。我今宁可舍猎师食，离于恐怖，依无事处，食草饮水耶？』第二群鹿作是念已，便舍猎师食，离于恐怖，依无事处，食草饮水。彼春后月诸草水尽，身体极羸，气力衰退，便随猎师、猎师眷属，如是第二群鹿亦不脱猎师、猎师眷属境界。当观彼第二沙门、梵志亦复如是。

The third śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa also thinks: 'The first and second śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa do not escape the domain of Mara and Mara's retinue. Should I now be free from Mara and Mara's retinue, rely on a place not far away, and having relied on a place not far away, not approach and eat the food of worldly offerings, and having not approached and eaten it, not become arrogant, indulgent, and careless. Having not become careless, not follow Mara and Mara's retinue.' Having thought this, the third śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa then is free from Mara and Mara's retinue, relies on a place not far away, and having relied on a place not far away, does not approach and eat the food of worldly offerings. Having not approached and eaten it, does not become arrogant, indulgent, and careless. Having not become careless, does not follow Mara and Mara's retinue. However, they uphold two views: the view of existence and the view of non-existence. Because they uphold these two views, they follow Mara and Mara's retinue. Thus, the third śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa also do not escape the domain of Mara and Mara's retinue.

「第三沙门、梵志亦作是念：『第一、第二沙门、梵志一切不脱魔王、魔王眷属境界，我今宁可离魔王、魔王眷属，依住不远，住不远已，不近食世间信施食，不近食已，便不憍恣放逸，不放逸已，便不随魔王、魔王眷属。』第三沙门、梵志作是念已，便离魔王、魔王眷属，依住不远，住不远已，便不近食世间信施食，不近食已，便不憍恣放逸，不放逸已，便不随魔王、魔王眷属。然受持二见，有见及无见。彼受此二见故，便随魔王、魔王眷属。如是第三沙门、梵志亦不脱魔王、魔王眷属境界。

It is like the third herd of deer who also think: 'The first and second herds of deer do not escape the domain of the hunter and the hunter's retinue. Should we now be free from the hunter and the hunter's retinue, rely on a place not far away, and having relied on a place not far away, not approach and eat the hunter's food. Having not approached and eaten it, not become arrogant, indulgent, and careless. Having not become careless, not follow the hunter and the hunter's retinue.' Having thought this, the third herd of deer then is free from the hunter and the hunter's retinue, relies on a place not far away, and having relied on a place not far away, does not approach and eat the hunter's food. Having not approached and eaten it, does not become arrogant, indulgent, and careless. Having not become careless, does not follow the hunter and the hunter's retinue. The hunter and the hunter's retinue then think: 'The third herd of deer is very cunning and extremely cunning. Why is that? Having eaten our food, they cannot be caught. Should we now set up a long enclosure, and having set up a long enclosure, we will then catch the third herd of deer where they rely and stay.' The hunter and the hunter's retinue, having thought this, then set up a long enclosure, and having set up a long enclosure, they then catch the third herd of deer where they rely and stay. Thus, the third herd of deer also do not escape the domain of the hunter and the hunter's retinue. The place they rely on should be known as the view of existence. The place they stay should be known as the view of non-existence. Consider that the third śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa are also like this.

「犹如第三群鹿亦作是念：『第一、第二群鹿一切不脱猎师、猎师眷属境界，我今宁可离猎师、猎师眷属，依住不远，住不远已，不近食猎师食，不近食已，便不憍恣放逸，不放逸已，便不随猎师、猎师眷属。』第三群鹿作是念已，便离猎师、猎师眷属，依住不远，住不远已，不近食猎师食，不近食已，便不憍恣放逸，不放逸已，便不随猎师、猎师眷属。彼猎师、猎师眷属便作是念：『第三群鹿甚奇谄黠，极谄黠。所以者何？食我食已，而不可得，我今宁可作长围罝，作长围罝已，便得第三群鹿所依住止。』猎师、猎师眷属作是念已，便作长围罝，作长围罝已，便得第三群鹿所依住止。如是第三群鹿亦不脱猎师、猎师眷属境界。所依者当知有见也。住止者当知无见也。当观彼第三沙门、梵志亦复如是。

The fourth śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa also thinks: 'The first, second, and third śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa do not escape the domain of Mara and Mara's retinue. Should I now rely on a place where Mara and Mara's retinue do not reach, and having relied on that place, not approach and eat the food of worldly offerings. Having not approached and eaten it, not become arrogant, indulgent, and careless. Having not become careless, not follow Mara and Mara's retinue.' Having thought this, the fourth śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa then relies on a place where Mara and Mara's retinue do not reach. Having relied on that place, does not approach and eat the food of worldly offerings. Having not approached and eaten it, does not become arrogant, indulgent, and careless. Having not become careless, does not follow Mara and Mara's retinue. Thus, the fourth śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa then escapes the domain of Mara and Mara's retinue.

「第四沙门、梵志亦作是念：『第一、第二、第三沙门、梵志一切不脱魔王、魔王眷属境界，我今宁可依住魔王、魔王眷属所不至处，依住彼已，不近食世间信施食，不近食已，便不憍恣放逸，不放逸已，便不随魔王、魔王眷属。』第四沙门、梵志作是念已，便依住魔王、魔王眷属所不至处，依住彼已，不近食世间信施食，不近食已，便不憍恣放逸，不放逸已，便不随魔王、魔王眷属。如是第四沙门、梵志便脱魔王、魔王眷属境界。

It is like the fourth herd of deer who also think: 'The first, second, and third herds of deer do not escape the domain of the hunter and the hunter's retinue. Should we now rely on a place where the hunter and the hunter's retinue do not reach, and having relied on that place, not approach and eat the hunter's food. Having not approached and eaten it, not become arrogant, indulgent, and careless. Having not become careless, not follow the hunter and the hunter's retinue.' Having thought this, the fourth herd of deer then relies on a place where the hunter and the hunter's retinue do not reach. Having relied on that place, does not approach and eat the hunter's food. Having not approached and eaten it, does not become arrogant, indulgent, and careless. Having not become careless, does not follow the hunter and the hunter's retinue. The hunter and the hunter's retinue then think: 'The fourth herd of deer is very swift and the foremost swift. If we pursue them, we will certainly not be able to catch them. The other deer will then be terrified and scatter. Should we now abandon the enclosure for the fourth herd of deer.' The hunter and the hunter's retinue, having thought this, then abandon the enclosure. Thus, the fourth herd of deer then escapes the domain of the hunter and the hunter's retinue. Consider that the fourth śramaṇa and brāhmaṇa are also like this.

「犹如第四群鹿亦作是念：『第一、第二、第三群鹿一切不脱猎师、猎师眷属境界，我今宁可依住猎师、猎师眷属所不至处，依住彼已，不近食猎师食，不近食已，便不憍恣放逸，不放逸已，便不随猎师、猎师眷属。』第四群鹿作是念已，便依住猎师、猎师眷属所不至处，依住彼已，不近食猎师食，不近食已，便不憍恣放逸，不放逸已，便不随猎师、猎师眷属。彼猎师、猎师眷属复作是念：『第四群鹿甚奇俊猛，第一俊猛，若我逐彼，必不能得，余鹿则当恐怖惊散，我今宁可舍罝第四群鹿。』彼猎师、猎师眷属作是念已，则便舍罝。如是第四群鹿便脱猎师、猎师眷属境界。当观彼第四沙门、梵志亦复如是。

Bhikkhus! You should train in such a place of reliance, where Mara and Mara's retinue cannot reach. What is the place where Mara and Mara's retinue cannot reach? It is when a bhikkhu, detached from sensual pleasures, detached from unwholesome states, enters and abides in the first jhāna, which is accompanied by thought and examination, with rapture and happiness born of detachment. This is called the place where Mara and Mara's retinue cannot reach. Furthermore, what is the place where Mara and Mara's retinue cannot reach? It is when a bhikkhu, with a mind that is full of loving-kindness, dwells pervading one direction, likewise the second, the third, and the fourth direction, and also the four intermediate directions, above, and below, all around, everywhere. With a mind full of loving-kindness, free from fetters and resentment, without ill will or quarrel, he dwells pervading the entire world boundless, exalted, immeasurable, and well cultivated. Likewise with compassion, sympathetic joy, and equanimity. This is called the place where Mara and Mara's retinue cannot reach.

「比丘！当学如是所依住止，令魔王、魔王眷属所不至处。何者魔王、魔王眷属所不至处？谓比丘离欲、离恶不善之法，至得第四禅成就游，是谓魔王、魔王眷属所不至处。复次，何者魔王、魔王眷属所不至处？谓比丘心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游，如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。是谓魔王、魔王眷属所不至处。

Furthermore, what is the place where Mara and Mara's retinue cannot reach? It is when a bhikkhu, having completely transcended the perception of form, with the disappearance of the perception of resistance, not attending to the perception of variety, aware that 'space is infinite', enters and abides in the base of the infinity of space. This is called the place where Mara and Mara's retinue cannot reach. Furthermore, what is the place where Mara and Mara's retinue cannot reach? It is when a bhikkhu, having completely transcended the base of the infinity of space, aware that 'consciousness is infinite', enters and abides in the base of the infinity of consciousness. This is called the place where Mara and Mara's retinue cannot reach. Furthermore, what is the place where Mara and Mara's retinue cannot reach? It is when a bhikkhu, having completely transcended the base of the infinity of consciousness, aware that 'there is nothing', enters and abides in the base of nothingness. This is called the place where Mara and Mara's retinue cannot reach. Furthermore, what is the place where Mara and Mara's retinue cannot reach? It is when a bhikkhu, having completely transcended the base of nothingness, enters and abides in the base of neither-perception-nor-non-perception. This is called the place where Mara and Mara's retinue cannot reach. Furthermore, what is the place where Mara and Mara's retinue cannot reach? It is when a bhikkhu, having completely transcended the base of neither-perception-nor-non-perception, enters and abides in the cessation of perception and feeling. This is called the place where Mara and Mara's retinue cannot reach. Bhikkhus! You should train in such a place of reliance, where Mara and Mara's retinue cannot reach.

「复次，何者魔王、魔王眷属所不至处？谓比丘度一切色想，至非有想非无想处成就游，是谓魔王、魔王眷属所不至处。复次，何者魔王、魔王眷属所不至处？谓比丘度一切非有想非无想处，想知灭身触成就游，慧见诸漏尽断知，是谓魔王、魔王眷属所不至处。比丘！如是所依住正，命魔王、魔王眷属所不至处，当学如是。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikkhus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced them.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
