MĀ 18

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 2 业相应品

18. To Sīha （一八）师子经

Parallel to: AN 8.12

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in Vesali, staying at the high pavilion by the Monkey River.

一时，佛游鞞舍离，在猕猴水边高楼台观。

At that time, many Vesali Licchavis were gathered in the assembly hall, repeatedly praising the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha. At that time, the Licchavi minister Simha was also in the assembly.

尔时，众多鞞舍离丽掣集在听堂，数称叹佛，数称叹法及比丘众。彼时，尼干弟子师子大臣亦在众中。

At that time, the Licchavi minister Simha wanted to go see the Buddha, make offerings, and pay respects. He first went to the Nirgranthas and said, "Venerables! I want to go see the śramaṇa Gautama."

是时，师子大臣欲往见佛，供养礼事，师子大臣则先往诣诸尼干所，白尼干曰：「诸尊！我欲往见沙门瞿昙。」

At that time, the Nirgrantha teacher scolded Simha, saying, "Do not want to see the śramaṇa Gautama. Why? The śramaṇa Gautama teaches the unwholesome as the basis, and also teaches others the unwholesome Dharma. Simha! If one sees the unwholesome as the basis, it is inauspicious and not beneficial. Making offerings and paying respects is also inauspicious and not beneficial."

彼时，尼干诃师子曰：「汝莫欲见沙门瞿昙。所以者何？沙门瞿昙宗本不可作，亦为人说不可作法。师子！若见宗本不可作则不吉利，供养礼事亦不吉利。」

At that time, many Licchavis gathered in the assembly hall and repeatedly praised the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha. At that time, the Licchavi minister Simha was also in that assembly. At that time, the Licchavi minister Simha also repeatedly wanted to go see the Buddha, make offerings, and pay respects.

彼众多鞞舍离丽掣再三集在听堂，数称叹佛，数称叹法及比丘众。彼时，尼干弟子师子大臣亦再三在彼众中。时，师子大臣亦复再三欲往见佛，供养礼事。

The Licchavi minister Simha then did not excuse himself from the Nirgrantha. He went to see the Buddha, exchanged greetings, and sat to one side. He said, "I have heard that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches the unwholesome as the basis, and also teaches others the unwholesome Dharma. Gautama! If it is said like this, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches the unwholesome as the basis, and also teaches others the unwholesome Dharma, does that not slander the śramaṇa Gautama? Is that true? Is that the Dharma? Does that teach the Dharma as it is? Is there no fault or difficulty in the Dharma as it is?"

师子大臣便不辞尼干，即往诣佛，共相问讯，却坐一面，而作是语：「我闻沙门瞿昙宗本不可作，亦为人说不可作法。瞿昙！若如是说，沙门瞿昙宗本不可作，亦为人说不可作法，彼不谤毁沙门瞿昙耶？彼说真实耶？彼说是法耶？彼说法如法耶？于如法无过、无难诘耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Simha! If it is said like this, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches the unwholesome as the basis, and also teaches others the unwholesome Dharma, that does not slander the śramaṇa Gautama! That is true, that is the Dharma, that teaches the Dharma as it is, and there is no fault or difficulty in the Dharma as it is. Why is that? Simha! There are things because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches the unwholesome as the basis, and also teaches others the unwholesome Dharma. Simha! There are also things because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches the wholesome as the basis, and also teaches others the wholesome Dharma. Simha! There are also things because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches the cessation of existence, and also teaches others the Dharma of the cessation of existence. Simha! There are also things because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches the repulsive, and also teaches others the Dharma of the repulsive. Simha! There are also things because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches the Dharma and Vinaya, and also teaches others the Dharma and Vinaya. Simha! There are also things because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches the practice of austerities, and also teaches others the Dharma of the practice of austerities. Simha! There are also things because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches not entering the womb, and also teaches others the Dharma of not entering the womb. Simha! There are also things because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches peace, and also teaches others the Dharma of peace.

世尊答曰：「师子！若如是说，沙门瞿昙宗本不可作，亦为人说不可作法，彼不谤毁沙门瞿昙！彼说真实，彼说是法，彼说如法，于法无过，亦无难诘。所以者何？师子！有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本不可作，亦为人说不可作法。师子！复有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本可作，亦为人说可作之法。师子！复有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本断灭，亦为人说断灭之法。师子！复有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本可恶，亦为人说可憎恶法。师子！复有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本法、律，亦为人说法、律之法。师子！复有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本苦行，亦为人说苦行之法。师子！复有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本不入于胎，亦为人说不入胎法。师子！复有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本安隐，亦为人说安隐之法。

Simha! What are the things because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches the unwholesome as the basis, and also teaches others the unwholesome Dharma? Simha! I say that bodily evil conduct should not be done, and verbal and mental evil conduct should also not be done. Simha! These and other immeasurable unwholesome and defiled dharmas are the basis for future existence, the cause of hot and painful retribution, birth, old age, sickness, and death. Simha! I say that these dharmas should all not be done. Simha! This is called the thing because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches the unwholesome as the basis, and also teaches others the unwholesome Dharma.

「师子！云何有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本不可作，亦为人说不可作法？师子！我说身恶行不可作，口、意恶行亦不可作。师子！若如是比无量不善秽污之法，为当来有本、烦热苦报、生老病死因。师子！我说此法尽不可作。师子！是谓有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本不可作，亦为人说不可作法。

Simha! What are the things because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches the wholesome as the basis, and also teaches others the wholesome Dharma? Simha! I say that bodily good conduct should be done, and verbal and mental good conduct should also be done. Simha! These and other immeasurable wholesome dharmas bring happiness, experience happy retribution, and are born in good places and attain long life. Simha! I say that these dharmas should all be done. Simha! This is called the thing because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches the wholesome as the basis, and also teaches others the wholesome Dharma.

「师子！云何复有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本可作，亦为人说可作之法？师子！我说身妙行可作，口、意妙行亦可作。师子！若如是比无量善法与乐果，受于乐报，生于善处而得长寿。师子！我说此法尽应可作。师子！是谓有事因此事故，不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本可作，亦为人说可作之法。

Simha! What are the things because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches the cessation of the basis, and also teaches others the Dharma of cessation? Simha! I say that bodily evil conduct should be ceased, and verbal and mental evil conduct should also be ceased. Simha! These and other immeasurable unwholesome and defiled dharmas are the basis for future existence, the cause of hot and painful retribution, birth, old age, sickness, and death. Simha! I say that these dharmas should all be ceased. Simha! This is called the thing because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches the cessation of the basis, and also teaches others the Dharma of cessation.

「师子！云何复有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本断灭，亦为人说断灭之法？师子！我说身恶行应断灭，口、意恶行亦应断灭。师子！若如是比无量不善秽污之法，为当来有本、烦热苦报、生老病死因。师子！我说此法尽应断灭。师子！是谓有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本断灭，亦为人说断灭之法。

Simha! What are the things because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches the unwholesome as the basis, and also teaches others the unwholesome Dharma? Simha! I say that bodily evil conduct is unwholesome, and verbal and mental evil conduct are also unwholesome. Simha! These and other immeasurable unwholesome and defiled dharmas are the basis for future existence, the cause of hot and painful retribution, birth, old age, sickness, and death. Simha! I say that these dharmas are all unwholesome. Simha! This is called the thing because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches the unwholesome as the basis, and also teaches others the unwholesome Dharma.

「师子！云何复有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本可恶，亦为人说可憎恶法？师子！我说身恶行可憎恶，口、意恶行亦可憎恶。师子！若如是比无量不善秽污之法，为当来有本、烦热苦报、生老病死因。师子！我说此法尽可憎恶。师子！是谓有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本可恶，亦为人说可憎恶法。

Simha! What are the things because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches the Dharma and Vinaya as the basis, and also teaches others the Dharma and Vinaya? Simha! I teach the Dharma and Vinaya in order to eliminate greed, hatred, and delusion. Simha! These and other immeasurable unwholesome and defiled dharmas are the basis for future existence, the cause of hot and painful retribution, birth, old age, sickness, and death. Simha! I teach the Dharma and Vinaya in order to eliminate them. Simha! This is called the thing because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches the Dharma and Vinaya as the basis, and also teaches others the Dharma and Vinaya.

「师子！云何复有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本法、律，亦为人说法、律之法？师子！我为断贪婬故而说法、律，断瞋恚、愚痴故而说法、律。师子！若如是比无量不善秽污之法，为当来有本、烦热苦报、生老病死因。师子！我为断彼故而说法、律。师子！是谓有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本法、律，亦为人说法、律之法。

Simha! What are the things because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches asceticism as the basis, and also teaches others the Dharma of asceticism? Simha! There are ascetics and Brahmins who go naked, without clothes, or use hands as clothes, or leaves as clothes, or beads as clothes, or do not take water with a pot, or do not take water with a ladle, do not eat food obtained by force or deceit, do not go themselves or send messages, do not come to honor or respect, do not stay in honor or respect. If two people eat, they do not eat in between. They do not eat in a house with pregnant women, or in a house with dogs. Even if flies come flying from a house, they do not eat. They do not eat fish, meat, drink alcohol, or drink bad water, or drink nothing at all, practicing not drinking. They eat one mouthful, considering one mouthful enough, or two, three, four, up to seven mouthfuls, considering seven mouthfuls enough. They eat one meal, considering one meal enough, or two, three, four, five, six, seven meals, half a month, or one month, considering one meal enough. They eat vegetables, millet, coarse rice, or mixed flour, or eat head-to-head food, or eat coarse food. They go to a place without affairs, relying on affairs without affairs. They eat roots, fruits, or fallen fruits. They wear loose clothes, woolen clothes, or head-to-head clothes, or woolen head-to-head clothes. They wear whole skins, pierced skins, or whole pierced skins. They wear loose hair, braided hair, or loose braided hair. Some shave their hair, some shave their beards, some shave their hair and beards. Some stand still, some sit cross-legged, some lie on thorns, using thorns as beds, some lie on fruits, using fruits as beds. Some serve water, stirring it with their hands day and night. Some serve fire, burning it all night. They serve the sun, moon, honored ones, and great virtuous ones, with joined palms facing them. They practice such immeasurable austerities. Simha! I do not say that these austerities do not exist. Simha! But these austerities are inferior practices, leading to suffering and hardship, practiced by ordinary people, not the noble path. Simha! If there are ascetics and Brahmins who know and eliminate the practice of these austerities, cutting off their roots so that they do not arise again, I say that they practice austerities. Simha! The Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One knows and eliminates the practice of these austerities, cutting off their roots so that they do not arise again. Therefore I practice austerities. 「师子！云何复有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本苦行，亦为人说苦行之法？师子！或有沙门、梵志裸形无衣，或以手为衣，或以叶为衣，或以珠为衣，或不以瓶取水，或不以魁取水，不食刀杖劫抄之食，不食欺妄食，不自往，不遣信，不来尊、不善尊、不住尊。若有二人食，不在中食，不怀妊家食，不畜狗家食，设使家有粪蝇飞来，便不食也。不噉鱼，不食肉，不饮酒，不饮恶水，或都无所饮，学无饮行；或噉一口，以一口为足，或二口、三、四，乃至七口，以七口为足；或食一得，以一得为足，或二、三、四，乃至七得，以七得为足；或日一食，以一食为足，或二、三、四、五、六、七日、半月、一月一食，以一食为足；或食菜茹，或食稗子，或食穄米，或食杂［麸－夫＋广］，或食头头逻食，或食麤食；或至无事处，依于无事，或食根，或食果，或食自落果，或持连合衣，或持毛衣，或持头舍衣，或持毛头舍衣，或持全皮，或持穿皮，或持全穿皮；或持散发，或持编发，或持散编发，或有剃发，或有剃须，或剃须发，或有拔发，或有拔须，或拔须发；或住立断坐，或修蹲行，或有卧刺，以刺为床，或有卧果，以果为床，或有事水，昼夜手抒，或有事火，竟昔然之，或事日、月、尊祐大德，叉手向彼，如此之比受无量苦，学烦热行。师子！有此苦行我不说无。师子！然此苦行为下贱业，至苦至困，凡人所行，非是圣道。师子！若有沙门、梵志，彼苦行法知断灭尽，拔绝其根，至竟不生者，我说彼苦行。师子！如来、无所著、等正觉，彼苦行法知断灭尽，拔绝其根，至竟不生，是故我苦行。师子！是谓有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本苦行，亦为人说苦行之法。

Simha! What are the things because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches non-entry into the womb as the basis, and also teaches others the Dharma of non-entry into the womb? Simha! If there are ascetics and Brahmins who know and eliminate the practice of future womb-born existence, cutting off its roots so that it does not arise again, I say that they do not enter the womb. Simha! The Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One knows and eliminates the practice of future womb-born existence, cutting off its roots so that it does not arise again. Therefore I do not enter the womb. Simha! This is called the thing because of which one

「师子！云何复有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本不入于胎，亦为人说不入胎法？师子！若有沙门、梵志当来胎生，知断灭尽，拔绝其根，至竟不生者，我说彼不入于胎。师子！如来、无所著、等正觉，当来有胎生知断灭尽，拔绝其根，至竟不生，是故我不入于胎。师子！是谓有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本不入于胎，亦为人说不入胎法。

"Simha! What are the things because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches tranquility as the basis, and also teaches others the Dharma of tranquility? Simha! When a noble son shaves off his beard and hair, puts on the monastic robe, leaves home out of true faith, and studies the path, he only completes the unsurpassed holy life. In this present life, he himself knows, himself awakens, himself realizes, and himself accomplishes. He knows that birth is exhausted, the holy life is established, what was to be done is done, and he will not experience another existence. He knows it as it really is. He himself is tranquil, and also tranquilizes other monks, nuns, laymen, and laywomen. He has already tranquilized them, and then gives rise to the Dharma of birth. Beings are liberated from the Dharma of birth, the Dharma of aging, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, and defilement. Beings are liberated from the Dharma of sorrow, lamentation, pain, and defilement. Simha! This is called the thing because of which one cannot slander the true Dharma, that the śramaṇa Gautama teaches tranquility as the basis, and also teaches others the Dharma of tranquility."

「师子！云何复有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本安隐，亦为人说安隐之法？师子！族姓子所有剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，唯无上梵行讫，我于现法自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真，我自安隐，亦安隐他比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆夷。我已安彼，便为生法，众生于生法解脱，老法、病法、死法、忧戚染污法，众生于忧戚染污法解脱。师子！是谓有事因此事故，于如实法不能谤毁，沙门瞿昙宗本安隐，亦为人说安隐之法。」

"Simha the minister said to the World-Honored One: "Gautama! I have understood. Well-Gone One! I have understood. Gautama! It is like a person with clear eyes, who lifts up what is covered, reveals what is hidden, shows the way to the lost, and provides light in the dark, so that those with eyes can see forms. The śramaṇa Gautama is also like this, teaching me the Dharma and revealing the meaning in countless ways, according to the various paths. World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept me as a lay follower, from this day forth for as long as I live, taking refuge until the end of my life. World-Honored One! It is like a person raising a bad horse, hoping to gain profit, only tiring themselves without gaining any profit. World-Honored One! I am also like this. That foolish Nirgrantha does not understand well, cannot know for himself, does not recognize good fields, and does not examine himself. For a long time I have respected, made offerings, and paid homage to him, hoping to gain profit, but in vain and without benefit. World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha for a second time. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept me as a lay follower, from this day forth for as long as I live, taking refuge until the end of my life. World-Honored One! I was originally ignorant and had faith and respect for the foolish Nirgrantha. From today I will cut it off. Why? Because he deceived me. World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha for a third time. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept me as a lay follower, from this day forth for as long as I live, taking refuge until the end of my life.

师子大臣白世尊曰：「瞿昙！我已知。善逝！我已解。瞿昙！犹明目人，覆者仰之，覆者发之，迷者示道，暗中施明，若有眼者，便见于色。沙门瞿昙亦复如是，为我无量方便说法、现义，随其诸道。世尊！我今自归于佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。世尊！犹如有人养不良马，望得其利，徒自疲劳而不获利。世尊！我亦如是，彼愚痴尼干不善晓了，不能自知，不识良田而不自审，长夜奉敬，供养礼事，望得其利，唐苦无益。世尊！我今再自归佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。世尊！我本无知，于愚痴尼干有信有敬，从今日断。所以者何？欺诳我故。世尊！我今三自归佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Simha the minister and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。师子大臣及诸比，丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
