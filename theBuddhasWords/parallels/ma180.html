MĀ 180

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 14 心品

180. （一八〇）瞿昙弥经

Parallel to: MN 142

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the country of the Śākyas and stayed at the Nyagrodha Park in Kapilavastu.

一时，佛游释羇瘦，在加鞞罗卫尼拘类树园。

At that time, Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī held a new golden-threaded yellow robe and went to where the Buddha was. She bowed her head at the Buddha's feet, stepped back to one side, and said, "World-Honored One! This new golden-threaded yellow robe I made myself for the World-Honored One. Out of compassion for me, please accept it."

尔时，摩诃簸逻阇钵提瞿昙弥持新金缕黄色衣往诣佛所，稽首佛足，却住一面，白曰：「世尊！此新金缕黄色衣我自为世尊作，慈愍我故，愿垂纳受。」

The World-Honored One said, "Gautamī! Give this robe to the assembly of bhikṣus. Having given it to the assembly of bhikṣus, you will then make offerings to me and also make offerings to the assembly."

世尊告曰：「瞿昙弥！持此衣施比丘众，施比丘众已，便供养我，亦供养众。」

Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī said this a second and a third time, "World-Honored One! This new golden-threaded yellow robe I made myself for the World-Honored One. Out of compassion for me, please accept it."

大生主瞿昙弥至再三白曰：「世尊！此新金缕黄色衣我自为世尊作，慈愍我故，愿垂纳受。」

The World-Honored One also said this a second and a third time, "Gautamī! Give this robe to the assembly of bhikṣus. Having given it to the assembly of bhikṣus, you will then make offerings to me and also make offerings to the assembly."

世尊亦至再三告曰：「瞿昙弥！持此衣施比丘众，施比丘众已，便供养我，亦供养众。」

At that time, Venerable Ānanda was standing behind the World-Honored One, holding a whisk to attend to the Buddha. Then Venerable Ānanda said, "World-Honored One! This Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī has greatly benefited the World-Honored One. After the World-Honored One's mother passed away, she breastfed the World-Honored One."

尔时，尊者阿难立世尊后执拂侍佛。于是，尊者阿难白曰：「世尊！此大生主瞿昙弥于世尊多所饶益，世尊母命终后乳养世尊。」

The World-Honored One said, "So it is, Ānanda! So it is, Ānanda! Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī has indeed greatly benefited me. After my mother passed away, she breastfed me." "Ānanda! I have also greatly benefited Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī. Why is that? Because of me, Mahāprajāpatī Gautamī has taken refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṅgha, has no doubts about the Three Jewels, suffering, origination, cessation, and the path, has accomplished faith, precepts, learning, generosity, and wisdom, has abandoned killing and refrained from killing, taking what is not given, sexual misconduct, false speech, and drinking alcohol. Ānanda! If someone, because of another person, takes refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṅgha, has no doubts about the Three Jewels, suffering, origination, cessation, and the path, has accomplished faith, precepts, learning, generosity, and wisdom, has abandoned killing and refrained from killing, taking what is not given, sexual misconduct, false speech, and drinking alcohol, then that person should make offerings to that person for as long as they live, with food and drink, clothing and bedding, medicine and various necessities of life. They cannot repay the kindness.

世尊告曰：「如是，阿难！如是，阿难！大生主瞿昙弥实于我多所饶益，我母命终后乳养于我。阿难！我亦于大生主瞿昙弥多所饶益。所以者何？大生主瞿昙弥因我故，得自归于佛、法及比丘众，不疑三尊、苦习灭道，成就信、戒、多闻、施、慧，离杀断杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言、离酒断酒。阿难！若有人因人故，得自归于佛、法及比丘众，不疑三尊、苦习灭道，成就信、戒、多闻、施、慧，离杀断杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言、离酒断酒者，此人供养于彼人至尽形寿，以饮食、衣被、床榻、汤药及若干种诸生活具，不得报恩。

Furthermore, Ānanda, there are seven groups of offerings and fourteen private offerings that bring great merit, great fruit, great virtue, and great extensive rewards. Ānanda, what are the seven groups of offerings that bring great merit, great fruit, great virtue, and great extensive rewards? Ānanda, when the Buddha is in the world, the Buddha is the foremost. Offering to the Buddha and the community of monks is called the first group of offerings that brings great merit, great fruit, great virtue, and great extensive rewards. Ānanda, when the World-Honored One has passed into parinirvāṇa, offering to the two groups of monks and nuns soon after is called the second group of offerings that brings great merit, great fruit, great virtue, and great extensive rewards. Ānanda, when the World-Honored One has passed into parinirvāṇa, offering to the two groups of monks and nuns after a long time is called the third group of offerings that brings great merit, great fruit, great virtue, and great extensive rewards. Ānanda, when the World-Honored One has passed into parinirvāṇa, offering to the two groups of monks and nuns after a long time, entering the monastery of the monks and announcing to the assembly, 'So many monks in the assembly, please make offerings to them,' and entering the nunnery and announcing to the assembly, 'So many nuns in the assembly, please make offerings to them,' is called the fifth group of offerings that brings great merit, great fruit, great virtue, and great extensive rewards.

「复次，阿难！有七施众，有十四私施，得大福，得大果，得大功德，得大广报。阿难！云何七施众，得大福，得大果，得大功德，得大广报？信族姓男、族姓女，佛在世时，佛为首，施佛及比丘众，是谓第一施众，得大福，得大果，得大功德，得大广报。信族姓男、族姓女，世尊般涅槃后不久施二部众，施比丘众、施比丘尼众，入比丘僧园而白众曰：『众中尔所比丘来，布施彼也。』入比丘尼僧房而白众曰：『众中尔所比丘尼来，布施彼也。』是谓第五施众，得大福，得大果，得大功德，得大广报。

Ānanda! In the future there will be monks with names, clans, and families who are not diligent. They wear the saffron robes. Because they are not diligent, their offerings depend on the assembly, are based on the assembly, are directed towards the assembly, and are caused by the assembly. I say that at that time the benefactors will attain immeasurable, incalculable, and inconceivable merit, attain goodness and happiness. How much more so for the monks who now accomplish practice, accomplish elimination, accomplish both practice and elimination, accomplish straightforwardness, accomplish gentleness, accomplish both straightforwardness and gentleness, accomplish patience, accomplish joy, accomplish both patience and joy, accomplish harmony, accomplish mediation, accomplish both harmony and mediation, accomplish dignified conduct, accomplish coming and going, accomplish both dignified conduct and coming and going, accomplish faith, accomplish precepts, accomplish learning, accomplish generosity, and accomplish wisdom, and accomplish faith, precepts, learning, generosity, and wisdom? This is called the seventh group of offerings that brings great merit, great fruit, great virtue, and great extensive rewards. These are called the seven groups of offerings that bring great merit, great fruit, great virtue, and great extensive rewards.

「阿难！当来时有比丘，名姓种，不精进，著袈裟衣，彼不精进，不精进故施，依众故，缘众故，上众故，因众故，我说尔时施主得无量不可数不可计福，得善得乐，况复今比丘成就行事，成就除事，成就行事除事，成就质直，成就柔软，成就质直柔软，成就忍，成就乐，成就忍乐，成就相应，成就经纪，成就相应经纪，成就威仪，成就行来游，成就威仪行来游，成就信，成就戒，成就多闻，成就施，成就慧，成就信、戒、多闻、施、慧耶？是谓第七施众，得大福，得大果，得大功德，得大广报。是谓有七施众，得大福，得大果，得大功德，得大广报。

Ānanda, what are the fourteen private offerings that bring great merit, great fruit, great virtue, and great extensive rewards? Ānanda, a faithful male or female lay follower makes offerings to the Tathāgata, to a Pratyekabuddha, to an Arhat, to one who is on the path to arahantship, to a Non-returner, to one who is on the path to Non-returning, to a Once-returner, to one who is on the path to Once-returning, to a Stream-enterer, to one who is on the path to Stream-entry, to a detached ascetic, to one who is diligent, to one who is not diligent, and to an animal. Ānanda, making offerings to an animal brings merit a hundred times, making offerings to one who is not diligent brings merit a thousand times, making offerings to one who is diligent brings merit a hundred thousand times, making offerings to a detached ascetic brings merit a hundred thousand times, making offerings to one who is on the path to Stream-entry is immeasurable, attaining Stream-entry is immeasurable, being on the path to Once-returning is immeasurable, attaining Once-returning is immeasurable, being on the path to Non-returning is immeasurable, attaining Non-returning is immeasurable, being on the path to arahantship is immeasurable, attaining arahantship is immeasurable, being a Pratyekabuddha is immeasurable, let alone the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One? These fourteen private offerings bring great merit, great fruit, great virtue, and great extensive rewards.

「阿难！云何有十四私施，得大福，得大果，得大功德，得大广报？有信族姓男、族姓女布施如来，施缘一觉，施阿罗诃，施向阿罗诃，施阿那含，施向阿那含，施斯陀含，施向斯陀含，施须陀洹，施向须陀洹，施离欲外仙人，施精进人，施不精进人，布施畜生。阿难！布施畜生得福百倍，施不精进人得福千倍，施精进人得福百千倍，施离欲外仙人得福亿百千倍，施向须陀洹无量，得须陀洹无量，向斯陀含无量，得斯陀含无量，向阿那含无量，得阿那含无量，向阿罗诃无量，得阿罗诃无量，缘一觉无量，况复如来、无所著、等正觉耶？此十四私施得大福，得大果，得大功德，得大广报。

Furthermore, Ānanda, there are four kinds of giving, three of which are pure. What are the four? There is giving where the giver is pure, not the recipient; there is giving where the recipient is pure, not the giver; there is giving where neither the giver nor the recipient is pure; and there is giving where both the giver and the recipient are pure. Ānanda, what is giving where the giver is pure, not the recipient? The giver practices the sublime Dharma, sees the truth, and says, 'There is giving, there is the fruit of giving.' The recipient does not practice diligently, engages in evil, does not see the truth, and says, 'There is no giving, there is no fruit of giving.' This is called giving where the giver is pure, not the recipient.

「复次，阿难！有四种布施，三净施。云何为四？或有布施，因施主净，非受者；或有布施，因受者净，非施主；或有布施，非因施主净，亦非受者；或有布施，因施主净，受者亦然。阿难！云何布施因施主净，非受者耶？施主精进行妙法，见来见果，如是见、如是说，有施有施果；受者不精进，行恶法，不见来不见果，如是见、如是说，无施无施果。是谓布施因施主净，非受者也。

Ānanda, what is giving where the recipient is pure, not the giver? The giver does not practice diligently, engages in evil, does not see the truth, and says, 'There is no giving, there is no fruit of giving.' The recipient practices the sublime Dharma, sees the truth, and says, 'There is giving, there is the fruit of giving.' This is called giving where the recipient is pure, not the giver.

「阿难！云何布施因受者净，非施主耶？施主不精进，行恶法，不见来不见果，如是见、如是说，无施无施果；受者精进行妙法，见来见果，如是见、如是说，有施有施果。是谓布施因受者净，非施主也。阿难！云何布施非因施主净，亦非受者耶？施主不精进，行恶法，不见来不见果，如是见、如是说，无施无施果；受者亦不精进，行恶法，不见来不见果，如是见、如是说，无施无施果。是谓布施非因施主净，亦非受者。阿难！云何布施因施主净，受者亦然耶？施主精进行妙法，见来见果，如是见、如是说，有施有施果；受者亦精进行妙法，见来见果，如是见、如是说，有施有施果。是谓布施因施主净，受者亦然。」于是，世尊说此颂曰：

Giving to
the diligent
by the
diligent, according
to the
Dharma, with
a joyful
mind, believing
in karma
and its
results, this
giving is
pure due
to the giver.

「精进施不精进，　　如法得欢喜心，信有业及果报，　　此施因施主净。不精进施精进，　　不如法非喜心，不信业及果报，　　此施因受者净。懈怠施不精进，　　不如法非喜心，不信业及果报，　　如是施无广报。精进施于精进，　　如法得欢喜心，信有业及果报，　　如是施有广报。奴婢及贫穷，　　自分施欢喜，信业信果报，　　此施善人称。正护善身口，　　舒手以法乞，离欲施离欲，　　是财施第一。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Venerable Ānanda and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说如是。尊者阿难及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
