MĀ 182

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 15 双品

182. （一八二）马邑经

Parallel to: MN 39 / EĀ 49.8

Māyā and Māyā, Gośīrṣa and Gośīrṣa, Gośīrṣa and
Gośīrṣa, the last is for the sake of liberation.

马邑及马邑　　牛角娑罗林牛角娑罗林　　求解最在后

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Aṅga, together with a great assembly of bhikṣus. He went to the town of Māyā and stayed at the Māyāvana Monastery, together with the assembly of bhikṣus.

一时，佛游鸯骑国，与大比丘众俱，往至马邑，住马林寺，及比丘众。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "When people see you as śramaṇas, and when people ask you if you are śramaṇas, do you call yourselves śramaṇas?"

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「人见汝等沙门，是沙门，人问汝等沙门，汝自称沙门耶？」

The bhikṣus replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

诸比丘白曰：「尔也。世尊！」

The Buddha again said, "Therefore, you should uphold this. With this śramaṇa, you should learn according to the śramaṇa's way and the brāhmaṇa's way. Having learned according to the śramaṇa's way and the brāhmaṇa's way, you should be a true śramaṇa, not a false śramaṇa. If you receive robes, food and drink, bedding, medicine, and various necessities of life, the offerings you receive will bring great merit, great fruit, great virtue, and great extensive rewards. You should learn in this way.

佛复告曰：「是以汝等以此，要以此沙门，当学如沙门法及如梵志法，学如沙门法及如梵志法已，要是真谛沙门、不虚沙门。若受衣被、饮食、床榻、汤药及若干种诸生活具者，彼所供给，得大福，得大果，得大功德，得大广报，汝等当学如是。

What is the śramaṇa's way and the brāhmaṇa's way? The body is pure, upright, open, well-guarded, and flawless. Based on this purity, one does not exalt oneself or belittle others, is free from defilement and turbidity, and is praised by all wise and pure practitioners. If you think, 'My bodily conduct is pure, I have accomplished what I should do, I will not learn further, I have already attained virtue and righteousness, and there is nothing higher to do,' bhikṣus! I tell you, do not let the meaning of seeking the śramaṇa path be lost. If you wish to learn further, bhikṣus! If the body is pure, what else should be done? One should learn to have pure verbal conduct, upright, open, well-guarded, and flawless. Based on this pure verbal conduct, one does not exalt oneself or belittle others, is free from defilement and turbidity, and is praised by all wise and pure practitioners.

「云何如沙门法及如梵志法？身行清净，仰向发露，善护无缺，因此清净，不自举，不下他，无秽无浊，为诸智梵行者所共称誉。若汝作是念：『我身行清净，我所作已办，不复更学，已成德义，无复上作。』比丘！我为汝说，莫令求沙门义失沙门义。若欲求上学者，比丘若身清净，当复作何等？当学口行清净，仰向发露，善护无缺。因此口行清净，不自举，不下他，无秽无浊，为诸智梵行者所共称誉。

If you think, 'My bodily and verbal conduct is pure, I have accomplished what I should do, I will not learn further, I have already attained virtue and righteousness, and there is nothing higher to do,' bhikṣus! I tell you, do not let the meaning of seeking the śramaṇa path be lost. If you wish to learn further, bhikṣus! If the body and speech are pure, what else should be done? One should learn to have pure mental conduct, upright, open, well-guarded, and flawless. Based on this pure mental conduct, one does not exalt oneself or belittle others, is free from defilement and turbidity, and is praised by all wise and pure practitioners.

「若汝等作是念：『我身、口行清净，我所作已办，不复更学，已成德义，无复上作。』比丘！我为汝说，莫令求沙门义失沙门义。若欲求上学者，比丘若身、口清净，当复作何等？当学意行清净，仰向发露，善护无缺。因此意行清净，不自举，不下他，无秽无浊，为诸智梵行者所共称誉。若汝等作是念：『我身、口、意行清净，我所作已办，不复更学，已成德义，无复上作。』比丘！我为汝说，莫令求沙门义失沙门义。若欲求上学者，比丘若身、口、意行清净，当复作何等？当学命行清净，仰向发露，善护无缺。因此命行清净，不自举，不下他，无秽无浊，为诸智梵行者所共称誉。

If you think, 'My bodily, verbal, mental, and livelihood conduct is pure, I have accomplished what I should do, I will not learn further, I have already attained virtue and righteousness, and there is nothing higher to do,' bhikṣus! I tell you, do not let the meaning of seeking the śramaṇa path be lost. If you wish to learn further, bhikṣus! If the body, speech, mind, and livelihood are pure, and the sense faculties are well-guarded, what else should be done? Bhikṣus! One should learn to be mindful of going out and coming in, to observe and discern bending, stretching, bowing, and looking up, to have dignified deportment, to wear the saṃghāṭī robe and other robes and carry the alms bowl well, and to be mindful of walking, standing, sitting, lying down, sleeping, waking, speaking, and being silent.

「若汝等作是念：『我身、口、意、命行清净，我所作已办，不复更学，已成德义，无复上作。』比丘！我为汝说，莫令求沙门义失沙门义。若欲求上学者，比丘身、口、意命行清净，当复作何等？比丘当学守护诸根，常念闭塞，念欲明达，守护念心而得成就，恒欲起意，若眼见色，然不受相，亦不味色，谓忿诤故，守护眼根。心中不生贪伺、忧戚、恶不善法，趣向彼故，守护眼根。如是耳、鼻、舌、身，若意知法，然不受相，亦不味法，谓忿诤故，守护意根。心中不生贪伺、忧戚、恶不善法，趣向彼故，守护意根。若汝等作是念：『我身、口、意、命行清净，守护诸根，我所作已办，不复更学，已成德义，无复上作。』比丘！我为汝说，莫令求沙门义失沙门义。若欲求上学者，比丘身、口、意、命行清净，守护诸根，当复作何等？比丘当学正知出入，善观分别，屈伸低仰，仪容庠序，善著僧伽梨及诸衣钵，行住坐卧、眠寤语默，皆正知之。

If you think, 'My bodily, verbal, mental, and livelihood conduct is pure, I have well-guarded the sense faculties, I am mindful of going out and coming in, I have accomplished what I should do, I will not learn further, I have already attained virtue and righteousness, and there is nothing higher to do,' bhikṣus! I tell you, do not let the meaning of seeking the śramaṇa path be lost. If you wish to learn further, bhikṣus! If the body, speech, mind, and livelihood are pure, the sense faculties are well-guarded, and one is mindful of going out and coming in, what else should be done? Bhikṣus! One should learn to dwell alone in seclusion, in a place free from affairs, such as under a tree, in an empty and peaceful place, a mountain cave, a stone chamber, an open space, a heap of straw, in a forest, or near a charnel ground. Having gone to a place free from affairs, or under a tree, in an empty and peaceful place, one should spread out a seat mat, sit cross-legged, straighten the body and mind, reflect inwardly, eliminate greed and craving, be free from disputes, and not give rise to greed and craving for others' wealth and possessions, wishing to obtain them. One should purify the mind of greed and craving.

「若汝等作是念：『我身、口、意、命行清净，守护诸根，正知出入，我所作已办，不复更学，已成德义，无复上作。』比丘！我为汝说，莫令求沙门义失沙门义。若欲求上学者，比丘身、口、意、命行清净，守护诸根，正知出入，当复作何等？比丘当学独住远离，在无事处，或至树下空安靖处、山岩石室、露地穰［卄／积］，或至林中，或在冢间。彼已在无事处，或至树下空安靖处，敷尼师坛，结跏趺坐，正身正愿，反念不向，断除贪伺，心无有诤，见他财物、诸生活具，不起贪伺，欲令我得，彼于贪伺净除其心。如是瞋恚、睡眠、掉悔，断疑度惑，于诸善法无有犹豫，彼于疑惑净除其心。彼断此五盖、心秽、慧羸，离欲、离恶不善之法，至得第四禅成就游。彼已得如是定心清净，无秽无烦，柔软善住，得不动心，趣向漏尽智通作证。彼便知此苦如真，知此苦习、知此苦灭、知此苦灭道如真。亦知此漏如真，知此漏习、知此漏灭、知此漏灭道如真。彼如是知、如是见已，则欲漏心解脱，有漏、无明漏心解脱。解脱已，便知解脱，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。是说沙门，说梵志，说圣，说净浴。

What is a śramaṇa? It means to stop all evil and unwholesome dharmas, all taints and defilements, which are the basis for future existence, are heated and painful, and are the cause of birth, old age, sickness, and death. This is called a śramaṇa.

「云何沙门？谓息止诸恶不善之法、诸漏秽污，为当来有本，烦热苦报，生、老、病、死因，是谓沙门。云何梵志？谓远离诸恶不善之法，诸漏秽污，为当来有本，烦热苦报，生、老、病、死因，是谓梵志。云何为圣？谓远离诸恶不善之法、诸漏秽污，为当来有本，烦热苦报，生、老、病、死因，是谓为圣。云何净浴？谓净浴诸恶不善之法、诸漏秽污，为当来有本，烦热苦报，生、老、病、死因，是谓净浴。是谓沙门，是谓梵志，是谓为圣，是谓净浴。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
