MĀ 183

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 15 双品

183. （一八三）马邑经

Parallel to: MN 40

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Anga, together with a great assembly of bhikṣus. He went to the town of Horse and stayed at the Horse Grove Temple, together with the assembly of bhikṣus.

一时，佛游鸯骑国，与大比丘众俱，往至马邑，住马林寺，及比丘众。

At that time, the World-Honored One addressed the bhikṣus, saying: "When people see you as śramaṇas, and when people ask you if you are śramaṇas, do you call yourselves śramaṇas?"

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「人见汝等沙门，是沙门，人问汝等沙门，汝自称沙门耶？」

The bhikṣus replied: "Yes, World-Honored One!"

诸比丘白曰：「尔也。世尊！」

The Buddha further said: "Therefore, you should uphold this and use this as the śramaṇa. You should study the path of the śramaṇa and not be a false śramaṇa. Having studied the path of the śramaṇa, you should be a true śramaṇa, a non-false śramaṇa. If you receive robes, food and drink, bedding, medicine, and various necessities of life, the offerings you receive will bring great merit, great fruit, great virtue, and great extensive rewards. You should study in this way.

佛复告曰：「是以汝等以此，要以此沙门，当学沙门道迹，莫非沙门；学沙门道迹已，要是真谛沙门、不虚沙门，若受衣被、饮食、床榻、汤药及若干种诸生活具者，彼所供给，得大福，得大果，得大功德，得大广报，汝等当学如是。

What is not the path of the śramaṇa, not the śramaṇa? If one has craving and does not cease craving, has anger and does not cease anger, has hatred and does not cease hatred, has speech and does not cease speech, has bonds and does not cease bonds, has stinginess and does not cease stinginess, has jealousy and does not cease jealousy, has flattery and does not cease flattery, has deceit and does not cease deceit, has shamelessness and does not cease shamelessness, has lack of conscience and does not cease lack of conscience, has evil desires and does not cease evil desires, has wrong views and does not cease wrong views, these are the defilements of the śramaṇa, the flattery and deceit of the śramaṇa, the falsehood and crookedness of the śramaṇa, leading to an evil destination, not yet exhausted, studying the path of the śramaṇa, not the śramaṇa.

「云何非沙门道迹，非沙门？若有贪伺不息贪伺，有恚不息恚，有瞋不息瞋，有不语不息不语，有结不息结，有悭不息悭，有嫉不息嫉，有谀谄不息谀谄，有欺诳不息欺诳，有无慙不息无慙，有无愧不息无愧，有恶欲不息恶欲，有邪见不息邪见，此沙门垢、沙门谀谄、沙门诈伪、沙门曲，趣至恶处未尽已，学非沙门道迹，非沙门。

It is like a newly made sharp axe, with a head and blade, wrapped in a saṃghāṭī robe. I say that the foolish one who studies the path of the śramaṇa is also like this. That is, having craving and not ceasing craving, having anger and not ceasing anger, having hatred and not ceasing hatred, having speech and not ceasing speech, having bonds and not ceasing bonds, having stinginess and not ceasing stinginess, having jealousy and not ceasing jealousy, having flattery and not ceasing flattery, having shamelessness and not ceasing shamelessness, having lack of conscience and not ceasing lack of conscience, having evil desires and not ceasing evil desires, having wrong views and not ceasing wrong views, holding the saṃghāṭī robe, I do not say that is a śramaṇa. If one holds the saṃghāṭī robe, having craving and ceasing craving, having anger and ceasing anger, having hatred and ceasing hatred, having speech and ceasing speech, having bonds and ceasing bonds, having stinginess and ceasing stinginess, having jealousy and ceasing jealousy, having flattery and ceasing flattery, having shamelessness and ceasing shamelessness, having lack of conscience and ceasing lack of conscience, having evil desires and ceasing evil desires, having wrong views and ceasing wrong views, then their relatives and friends go to them and say: 'Virtuous one! You should study and hold the saṃghāṭī robe. Virtuous one! You study and hold the saṃghāṭī robe, having craving and ceasing craving, having anger and ceasing anger, having hatred and ceasing hatred, having speech and ceasing speech, having bonds and ceasing bonds, having stinginess and ceasing stinginess, having jealousy and ceasing jealousy, having flattery and ceasing flattery, having shamelessness and ceasing shamelessness, having lack of conscience and ceasing lack of conscience, having evil desires and ceasing evil desires, having wrong views and ceasing wrong views.' If one holds the saṃghāṭī robe with the view of self, having craving, anger, hatred, speech, bonds, stinginess, jealousy, flattery, shamelessness, lack of conscience, evil desires, and wrong views, then one holds the saṃghāṭī robe. I say that is not a śramaṇa.

「犹如钺斧，新作极利，有头有刃，僧伽梨所裹，我说彼痴学沙门道亦复如是。谓有贪伺不息贪伺，有恚不息恚，有瞋不息瞋，有不语不息不语，有结不息结，有悭不息悭，有嫉不息嫉，有谀谄不息谀谄，有无慙不息无慙，有无愧不息无愧，有恶欲不息恶欲，有邪见不息邪见，持僧伽梨，我不说是沙门。若持僧伽梨者，有贪伺息贪伺，有恚息恚，有瞋息瞋，有不语息不语，有结息结，有悭息悭，有嫉息嫉，有谀谄息谀谄，有无慙息无慙，有无愧息无愧，有恶欲息恶欲，有邪见息邪见者，彼诸亲亲朋友往诣而作是说：『贤人！汝当学持僧伽梨。贤！汝学持僧伽梨，有贪伺息贪伺，有恚息恚，有瞋息瞋，有不语息不语，有结息结，有悭息悭，有嫉息嫉，有谀谄息谀谄，有无慙息无慙，有无愧息无愧，有恶欲息恶欲，有邪见息邪见。』若以我见持僧伽梨，有贪伺、恚、瞋、不语、结、悭、嫉、谀谄、无慙、无愧、恶欲、邪见，是以我持僧伽梨。我说非是沙门。

So it is with those who have no clothes, matted hair, do not sit, eat only once, always splash water, and hold water. I say that those who hold water are not śramaṇas. If one holds water, having craving and ceasing craving, having anger and ceasing anger, having hatred and ceasing hatred, having speech and ceasing speech, having bonds and ceasing bonds, having stinginess and ceasing stinginess, having jealousy and ceasing jealousy, having flattery and ceasing flattery, having shamelessness and ceasing shamelessness, having lack of conscience and ceasing lack of conscience, having evil desires and ceasing evil desires, having wrong views and ceasing wrong views, then their relatives and friends go to them and say: 'Virtuous one! You should hold water. Having held water, having craving and ceasing craving, having anger and ceasing anger, having hatred and ceasing hatred, having speech and ceasing speech, having bonds and ceasing bonds, having stinginess and ceasing stinginess, having jealousy and ceasing jealousy, having flattery and ceasing flattery, having shamelessness and ceasing shamelessness, having lack of conscience and ceasing lack of conscience, having evil desires and ceasing evil desires, having wrong views and ceasing wrong views.' If one holds water with the view of self, having craving, anger, hatred, speech, bonds, stinginess, jealousy, flattery, shamelessness, lack of conscience, evil desires, and wrong views, then one holds water. I say that is not a śramaṇa.

「如是无衣、编发、不坐、一食、常扬水、持水，持水者，我说非是沙门。若持水，有贪伺息贪伺，有恚息恚，有瞋息瞋，有不语息不语，有结息结，有悭息悭，有嫉息嫉，有谀谄息谀谄，有无慙息无慙，有无愧息无愧，有恶欲息恶欲，有邪见息邪见，彼诸亲亲朋友往诣而作是说：『贤！汝当持水。持水已，有贪伺息贪伺，有恚息恚，有瞋息瞋，有不语息不语，有结息结，有悭息悭，有嫉息嫉，有谀谄息谀谄，有无慙息无慙，有无愧息无愧，有恶欲息恶欲，有邪见息邪见。』若以我见持水，贪伺、恚、瞋、不语、结、悭、嫉、谀谄、无慙、无愧、有恶欲、有邪见、是以持水者，我说不是沙门。是谓非沙门道迹，非是沙门。

What is the path of the śramaṇa, not the non-śramaṇa? If one has craving and ceases craving, has anger and ceases anger, has hatred and ceases hatred, has speech and ceases speech, has bonds and ceases bonds, has stinginess and ceases stinginess, has jealousy and ceases jealousy, has flattery and ceases flattery, has shamelessness and ceases shamelessness, has lack of conscience and ceases lack of conscience, has evil desires and ceases evil desires, has wrong views and ceases wrong views, then one is jealous of the śramaṇa, flattering to the śramaṇa, deceitful to the śramaṇa, crooked to the śramaṇa, and goes to an evil place. Having practiced the path of the śramaṇa, one is not a non-śramaṇa. This is called the path of the śramaṇa, not the non-śramaṇa.

「云何沙门道迹，非不沙门？若有贪伺息贪伺，有恚息恚，有瞋息瞋，有不语息不语，有结息结，有悭息悭，有嫉息嫉，有谀谄息谀谄，有无慙息无慙，有无愧息无愧，有恶欲息恶欲，有邪见息邪见，此沙门嫉、沙门谀谄、沙门诈伪、沙门曲，趣至恶处尽已，学沙门道迹，非不沙门。是谓沙门道迹，非不沙门。

They attain such purity of morality, purity of mind, purity of view, and purity of liberation. They are free from craving, anger, and delusion. They are free from doubt, free from confusion, with right mindfulness and right knowledge. They are not foolish, but have a mind full of loving-kindness. They dwell pervading one direction, then a second, a third, and a fourth. Thus above, below, across, everywhere, they dwell pervading the entire world with a mind full of loving-kindness, vast, exalted, measureless, without enmity, without ill will. They dwell pervading one direction with a mind full of compassion, then a second, a third, and a fourth. Thus above, below, across, everywhere, they dwell pervading the entire world with a mind full of compassion, vast, exalted, measureless, without enmity, without ill will. They dwell pervading one direction with a mind full of sympathetic joy, then a second, a third, and a fourth. Thus above, below, across, everywhere, they dwell pervading the entire world with a mind full of sympathetic joy, vast, exalted, measureless, without enmity, without ill will. They dwell pervading one direction with a mind full of equanimity, then a second, a third, and a fourth. Thus above, below, across, everywhere, they dwell pervading the entire world with a mind full of equanimity, vast, exalted, measureless, without enmity, without ill will. They think: 'There is the coarse, there is the refined, there is the coming, there is the going, there is the escape.' Having known and seen thus, their mind is liberated from the taints, and they know: 'Birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, there is no more of this to come.'

「彼如是成就戒，身清净，口、意清净，无有贪伺，心中无恚，无有睡眠，无掉憍慠，断疑度惑，正念正智，无有愚痴，彼心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游。如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。彼作是念：『有麤、有妙、有想，来上出要，知如真。』彼如是知、如是见已，则欲漏心解脱，有漏、无明漏心解脱，解脱已，便知解脱，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。

Not far from the village, there is a good bathing pond, with clear springs flowing in, covered with green grass on the banks, and surrounded by flowering trees. Suppose someone comes from the east, hungry, thirsty, and exhausted. He takes off his clothes on the bank, enters the pond, and bathes happily. He removes dirt, cools off, and also relieves thirst and fatigue. In the same way, someone from the south, west, and north comes, hungry, thirsty, and exhausted. He takes off his clothes on the bank, enters the pond, and bathes happily. He removes dirt, cools off, and also relieves thirst and fatigue. In the same way, a kṣatriya youth shaves off his hair and beard, puts on the yellow robe, leaves home out of faith, becomes homeless, and studies the path. He practices inner tranquility and attains inner tranquility. I say that he is a śramaṇa, a brāhmaṇa, a noble one, and a pure bather. In the same way, a brāhmaṇa, householder, or worker youth shaves off his hair and beard, puts on the yellow robe, leaves home out of faith, becomes homeless, and studies the path. He practices inner tranquility and attains inner tranquility. I say that he is a śramaṇa, a brāhmaṇa, a noble one, and a pure bather.

「犹去村不远，有好浴池，清泉流盈，翠草被岸，花树四周。或于东方有一人来，饥渴疲极，脱衣岸上，入池快浴，去垢除热，亦除渴乏。如是南方、西方、北方有一人来，饥渴疲极，脱衣岸上，入池快浴，去垢除热，亦除渴乏。如是，刹利族姓子剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，内行止，令得内止，内止者，我说沙门，说梵志，说圣，说净浴。如是梵志、居士、工师族姓子剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，内行止，令得内止，内止者，我说沙门，说梵志，说圣，说净浴。

"What is a śramaṇa? It means to stop all evil and unwholesome dharmas, all defiled dharmas that are the basis for future existence, that are hot and painful, and that are the cause of birth, old age, sickness, and death. This is called a śramaṇa. What is a brāhmaṇa? It means to be far removed from all evil and unwholesome dharmas, all defiled dharmas that are the basis for future existence, that are hot and painful, and that are the cause of birth, old age, sickness, and death. This is called a brāhmaṇa. What is noble? It means to be far removed from all evil and unwholesome dharmas, all defiled dharmas that are the basis for future existence, that are hot and painful, and that are the cause of birth, old age, sickness, and death. This is called noble. What is pure bathing? It means to bathe in purity, to bathe in the removal of all evil and unwholesome dharmas, all defiled dharmas that are the basis for future existence, that are hot and painful, and that are the cause of birth, old age, sickness, and death. This is called pure bathing. This is called a śramaṇa, this is called a brāhmaṇa, this is called noble, this is called pure bathing."

「云何沙门？谓息止诸恶不善之法、诸漏秽污，为当来有本，烦热苦报，生、老、病、死因，是谓沙门。云何梵志？谓远离诸恶不善之法、诸漏秽污，为当来有本，烦热苦报，生、老、病、死因，是谓梵志。云何为圣？谓远离诸恶不善之法、诸漏秽污，为当来有本，烦热苦报，生、老、病、死因，是谓为圣。云何净浴？谓净浴诸恶不善之法、诸漏秽污，为当来有本，烦热苦报，生、老、病、死因，是谓净浴。是谓沙门，是谓梵志，是谓为圣，是谓净浴。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
