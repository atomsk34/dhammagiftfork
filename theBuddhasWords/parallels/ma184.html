MĀ 184

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 15 双品

184. （一八四）牛角娑罗林经

Parallel to: MN 32 / EĀ 37.3

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the country of the Vṛjis, staying in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove, together with many well-known and highly regarded elder bhikṣus, great disciples, and others. The Venerable Śāriputra, the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, the Venerable Mahākāśyapa, the Venerable Mahākapphiṇa, the Venerable Aniruddha, the Venerable Revata, and the Venerable Ānanda, these and other well-known and highly regarded elder bhikṣus, great disciples, were also wandering in the country of the Vṛjis, staying in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove, all dwelling near the Buddha's leaf hut.

一时，佛游跋耆瘦，在牛角娑罗林，及诸多知识上尊比丘大弟子等。尊者舍梨子、尊者大目揵连、尊者大迦叶、尊者大迦旃延、尊者阿那律陀、尊者离越哆、尊者阿难，如是比丘多知识上尊比丘大弟子等，亦游跋耆瘦，在牛角娑罗林，并共近佛叶屋边住。

Then, the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, the Venerable Mahākāśyapa, the Venerable Mahākapphiṇa, and the Venerable Aniruddha, having passed the night, went to where the Venerable Śāriputra was. The Venerable Ānanda saw them coming from afar and said, "Venerable Revata, you should know that these Venerables Mahāmaudgalyāyana, Mahākāśyapa, Mahākapphiṇa, and Aniruddha, having passed the night, have gone to where the Venerable Śāriputra is. Venerable Revata, now you should go together with them to where the Venerable Śāriputra is. Perhaps you can hear the Dharma from the Venerable Śāriputra." Then, the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, the Venerable Mahākāśyapa, the Venerable Mahākapphiṇa, the Venerable Aniruddha, the Venerable Revata, and the Venerable Ānanda, having passed the night, went to where the Venerable Śāriputra was.

于是，尊者大目揵连、尊者大迦叶、尊者大迦旃延、尊者阿那律陀，过夜平旦，往诣尊者舍梨子所。尊者阿难遥见彼诸尊往已，白曰：「贤者离越哆！当知此尊者大目揵连、尊者大迦叶、尊者大迦旃延、尊者阿那律陀，过夜平旦，往诣尊者舍梨子所。贤者离越哆！今可共彼诸尊往诣尊者舍梨子所，傥能因彼从尊者舍梨子少多闻法。」于是，尊者大目揵连、尊者大迦叶、尊者大迦旃延、尊者阿那律陀、尊者离越哆、尊者阿难，过夜平旦，往诣尊者舍梨子所。

The Venerable Śāriputra saw them coming from afar and said to them, "Welcome, Venerable Ānanda! Welcome, Ānanda! Welcome, Ānanda! The attendant of the World-Honored One, who understands the World-Honored One's intention, is always praised by the World-Honored One and by the wise and virtuous ones. I now ask Venerable Ānanda, this Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove is very delightful, with the bright moon at night and the wonderful fragrance of the Śāla trees, like heavenly flowers. Venerable Ānanda, what kind of bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove? "

尊者舍梨子遥见彼诸尊来已，尊者舍梨子因彼诸尊故说：「善来，贤者阿难！善来，阿难！善来，阿难！世尊侍者解世尊意，常为世尊之所称誉，及诸智梵行人。我今问贤者阿难，此牛角娑罗林甚可爱乐，夜有明月，诸娑罗树皆敷妙香，犹若天花。贤者阿难！何等比丘起发牛角娑罗林？」

The Venerable Ānanda replied, "Venerable Śāriputra, if there is a bhikṣu who extensively studies and memorizes, guarding and not forgetting, accumulating extensive learning, namely the Dharma that is wonderful in the beginning, wonderful in the middle, and wonderful in the end, with meaning and text, complete and pure, manifesting the holy life. Such a bhikṣu extensively studies and memorizes, mastering it up to a thousand times, contemplating it in his mind, clearly seeing and deeply penetrating it. He speaks the Dharma concisely and swiftly, in accordance with the truth, wishing to sever all bonds. Venerable Śāriputra, such a bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove."

尊者阿难答曰：「尊者舍梨子！若有比丘广学多闻，守持不忘，积聚博闻，所谓法者，初妙、中妙、竟亦妙，有义有文，具足清净，显现梵行。如是诸法广学多闻，玩习至千，意所惟观，明见深达，彼所说法简要捷疾，与正相应，欲断诸结。尊者舍梨子！如是比丘起发牛角娑罗林。」

The Venerable Śāriputra further asked, "Venerable Revata, Venerable Ānanda Bhikṣu has already spoken according to his knowledge. I now ask Venerable Revata, this Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove is very delightful, with the bright moon at night and the wonderful fragrance of the Śāla trees, like heavenly flowers. Venerable Revata, what kind of bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove? "

尊者舍梨子复问曰：「贤者离越哆！贤者阿难比丘已说随所知。我今复问贤者离越哆，此牛角娑罗林甚可爱乐，夜有明月，诸娑罗树皆敷妙香，犹若天花。贤者离越哆！何等比丘起发牛角娑罗林？」

The Venerable Revata replied, "Venerable Śāriputra, if there is a bhikṣu who delights in sitting in meditation, practicing inner tranquility, not neglecting sitting meditation, accomplishing contemplation, always delighting in solitude, and enjoying peaceful places, Venerable Śāriputra, such a bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove."

尊者离越哆答曰：「尊者舍梨子！若有比丘乐于燕坐，内行止，不废坐禅，成就于观，常好闲居，憙安靖处，尊者舍梨子！如是比丘起发牛角娑罗林。」

The Venerable Śāriputra further asked, "Venerable Aniruddha, Venerable Revata Bhikṣu has already spoken according to his knowledge. I now ask Venerable Aniruddha, this Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove is very delightful, with the bright moon at night and the wonderful fragrance of the Śāla trees, like heavenly flowers. Venerable Aniruddha, what kind of bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove? "

尊者舍梨子复问曰：「贤者阿那律陀！贤者离越哆比丘已说随所知。我今复问贤者阿那律陀，此牛角娑罗林甚可爱乐，夜有明月，诸娑罗树皆敷妙香，犹若天华。贤者阿那律陀！何等比丘起发牛角娑罗林？」

The Venerable Aniruddha replied, "Venerable Śāriputra, if there is a bhikṣu who has attained the divine eye, accomplished the divine eye, and can see in an instant all the thousand worlds with little effort. Venerable Śāriputra, it is like a person with eyes standing on a high building, looking down at the thousand earthen mounds on the open ground, and seeing them all in an instant with little effort. Venerable Śāriputra, if there is a bhikṣu who has attained the divine eye, accomplished the divine eye, and can see in an instant all the thousand worlds with little effort, Venerable Śāriputra, such a bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove."

尊者阿那律陀答曰：「尊者舍梨子！若有比丘逮得天眼，成就天眼，于千世界彼少方便须臾尽见。尊者舍梨子！犹有目人住高楼上，于下露地有千土墼，彼少方便须臾尽见。尊者舍梨子！如是若有比丘逮得天眼，成就天眼，于千世界彼少方便须臾尽见，尊者舍梨子！如是比丘起发牛角娑罗林。」

The Venerable Śāriputra further asked, "Venerable Kātyāyana, Venerable Aniruddha Bhikṣu has already spoken according to his knowledge. I now ask Venerable Kātyāyana, this Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove is very delightful, with the bright moon at night and the wonderful fragrance of the Śāla trees, like heavenly flowers. Venerable Kātyāyana, what kind of bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove? "

尊者舍梨子复问曰：「贤者迦旃延！贤者阿那律陀比丘已说随所知。我今复问贤者迦旃延，此牛角娑罗林甚可爱乐，夜有明月，诸娑罗树皆敷妙香，犹若天花。贤者迦旃延！何等比丘起发牛角娑罗林？」

The Venerable Mahākātyāyana replied, "Venerable Śāriputra, it is like two bhikṣu teachers discussing the profound Abhidharma together. They can understand and know well whatever is asked, and their answers are unhindered, with eloquent explanations of the Dharma. Venerable Śāriputra, such a bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove."

尊者大迦旃延答曰：「尊者舍梨子！犹二比丘法师共论甚深阿毘昙，彼所问事，善解悉知，答亦无碍，说法辩捷。尊者舍梨子！如是比丘起发牛角娑罗林。」

The Venerable Śāriputra further asked, "Venerable Mahākāśyapa, Venerable Kātyāyana Bhikṣu has already spoken according to his knowledge. I now ask Venerable Mahākāśyapa, this Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove is very delightful, with the bright moon at night and the wonderful fragrance of the Śāla trees, like heavenly flowers. Venerable Mahākāśyapa, what kind of bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove? "

尊者舍梨子复问曰：「尊者大迦叶！贤者迦旃延比丘已说随所知。我今复问尊者大迦叶，此牛角娑罗林甚可爱乐，夜有明月，诸娑罗树皆敷妙香，犹若天华。尊者大迦叶！何等比丘起发牛角娑罗林？」

The Venerable Mahākāśyapa replied, "Venerable Śāriputra, if a bhikṣu praises himself for having no affairs, praises himself for having few desires, praises himself for being content, praises himself for enjoying dwelling alone in seclusion, praises himself for practicing diligently, praises himself for establishing right mindfulness and right knowledge, praises himself for attaining concentration, praises himself for having wisdom, praises himself for having exhausted all taints, praises himself for inspiring others to be thirsty and eager, and praises himself for accomplishing joy, Venerable Śāriputra, such a bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove."

尊者大迦叶答曰：「贤者舍梨子！若有比丘自无事称说无事，自有少欲称说少欲，自有知足称说知足，自乐在远离独住称说乐在远离独住，自修行精勤称说修行精勤，自立正念正智称说立正念正智，自得定称说得定，自有智慧称说智慧，自诸漏已尽称说诸漏已尽，自劝发渴仰成就欢喜称说劝发渴仰成就欢喜，贤者舍梨子！如是比丘起发牛角娑罗林。」

The Venerable Śāriputra further asked, "Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, Venerable Mahākāśyapa has already spoken according to his knowledge. I now ask Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, this Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove is very delightful, with the bright moon at night and the wonderful fragrance of the Śāla trees, like heavenly flowers. Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, what kind of bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove? "

尊者舍梨子复问曰：「贤者目揵连！尊者大迦叶已说随所知。我今复问贤者目揵连，此牛角娑罗林甚可爱乐，夜有明月，诸娑罗树皆敷妙香，犹若天华。贤者目连！何等比丘起发牛角娑罗林？」

The Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana replied, "Venerable Śāriputra, if a bhikṣu has great supernatural powers, great authority, great merit, and great spiritual power, and is unlimited in his supernatural powers, he can transform one into many, combine many into one, remain in one, and have knowledge and insight, penetrating through stone walls as if through space without obstruction, entering and exiting the earth as if it were water, walking on water as if on land without sinking, rising into the sky and sitting cross-legged like a bird, and even touching the sun and moon with his hands, reaching as far as the Brahma Heaven with his body. Venerable Śāriputra, such a bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove."

尊者大目揵连答曰：「尊者舍梨子！若有比丘有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，自在无量如意足。彼行无量如意足，变一为众，合众为一，一则住一，有知有见，彻过石壁，如空无碍，出入于地，犹若如水，履水如地而不陷没，上升虚空，结跏趺坐，犹若如鸟，今此日月有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，以手扪摸，身至梵天。尊者舍梨子！如是比丘起发牛角娑罗林。」

The Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana asked, "Venerable Śāriputra, I and the other Venerables have each spoken according to our knowledge. I now ask Venerable Śāriputra, this Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove is very delightful, with the bright moon at night and the wonderful fragrance of the Śāla trees, like heavenly flowers. Venerable Śāriputra, what kind of bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove? "

尊者大目揵连问曰：「尊者舍梨子！我及诸尊已各自说随其所知。我今问尊者舍梨子，此牛角娑罗林甚可爱乐，夜有明月，诸娑罗树皆敷妙香，犹若天华。尊者舍梨子！何等比丘起发牛角娑罗林？」

The Venerable Śāriputra replied, "Virtuous Mahāmaudgalyāyana, if a bhikṣu has mastery over his mind and is not controlled by his mind, then wherever he dwells, he can roam about in the morning. If he wishes to roam about at noon or in the afternoon, he can do so wherever he dwells. Virtuous Mahāmaudgalyāyana, it is like a king or a king's minister who has many garments of various fine colors. If he wishes to wear them in the morning, he can put them on. If he wishes to wear them at noon or in the afternoon, he can put them on. Virtuous Mahāmaudgalyāyana, in the same way, if a bhikṣu has mastery over his mind and is not controlled by his mind, then wherever he dwells, he can roam about in the morning. If he wishes to roam about at noon or in the afternoon, he can do so wherever he dwells. Virtuous Mahāmaudgalyāyana, such a bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove."

尊者舍梨子答曰：「贤者目揵连！若有比丘随用心自在而不随心，彼若欲得随所住止，中前游行，即彼住止，中前游行。彼若欲得随所住止，日中、晡时游行，即彼住止，日中、晡时游行。贤者目揵连！犹王、王臣，衣服甚多，有若干种杂妙色衣，彼若欲得中前著者即取著之，彼若欲得日中、晡时著者即取著之。贤者目揵连！如是。若有比丘随用心自在而不随心，彼若欲得随所住止，中前游行，即彼住止，中前游行。彼若欲得随所住止，日中、晡时游行，即彼住止，日中、晡时游行，贤者目揵连！如是比丘起发牛角娑罗林。」

The Venerable Śāriputra said, "Virtuous Mahāmaudgalyāyana, I and the other Venerables have each spoken according to our knowledge. Virtuous Mahāmaudgalyāyana, let us go together to the Buddha and discuss this matter. We will know who speaks best among us." Then the Venerable Śāriputra, the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, the Venerable Mahākāśyapa, the Venerable Mahākappina, the Venerable Anuruddha, the Venerable Revata, and the Venerable Ānanda went to where the Buddha was. The Venerables bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side. The Venerable Ānanda also bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and stood to one side.

尊者舍梨子告曰：「贤者目揵连！我及诸贤已各自说随其所知。贤者目揵连！我等宁可共彼诸贤往诣佛所，向论此事，于中知谁最为善说。」于是，尊者舍梨子、尊者大目揵连、尊者大迦叶、尊者大迦旃延、尊者阿那律陀、尊者离越哆、尊者阿难，往诣佛所，诸尊者等稽首佛足，却坐一面，尊者阿难亦稽首佛足，却住一面。

The Venerable Śāriputra said, "World-Honored One, today the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, the Venerable Mahākāśyapa, the Venerable Mahākappina, the Venerable Anuruddha, the Venerable Revata, and the Venerable Ānanda came to me in the early morning. When I saw them coming from afar, I spoke to them, saying, 'Welcome, Venerable Ānanda! Welcome, Ānanda! Welcome, Ānanda! The World-Honored One's attendant understands the World-Honored One's intention and is always praised by the World-Honored One and the wise practitioners of the holy life. I now ask the Venerable Ānanda, this Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove is very delightful, with the bright moon at night and the wonderful fragrance of the śāla trees, like heavenly flowers. Venerable Ānanda, what kind of bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove?' The Venerable Ānanda then replied to me, saying, 'Venerable Śāriputra, if a bhikṣu is well-versed in the teachings, upholds them without forgetting, accumulates extensive learning, and is endowed with the teachings that are wonderful in the beginning, wonderful in the middle, and wonderful in the end, with meaning and expression, complete and pure, manifesting the holy life. He extensively studies and memorizes such teachings, contemplating them with his mind, clearly seeing and deeply understanding them. He speaks the Dharma concisely and swiftly, in accordance with the truth, wishing to sever all afflictions. Venerable Śāriputra, such a bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove.'"

尊者舍梨子白曰：「世尊！今日贤者大目揵连、尊者大迦叶、贤者迦旃延、贤者阿那律陀、贤者离越哆、贤者阿难，过夜平旦，来诣我所，我遥见彼诸贤来已，因彼诸贤故说：『善来，贤者阿难！善来，阿难！善来，阿难！世尊侍者解世尊意，常为世尊之所称誉，及诸智梵行人，我今问贤者阿难，此牛角娑罗林甚可爱乐，夜有明月，诸娑罗树皆敷妙香，犹若天华，贤者阿难！何等比丘起发牛角娑罗林？』贤者阿难即答我曰：『尊者舍梨子！若有比丘广学多闻，守持不忘，积聚博闻，所谓法者，初妙、中妙、竟亦妙，有义有文，具足清净，显现梵行。如是诸法广学多闻，玩习至千，意所惟观，明见深达，彼所说法简要捷疾，与正相应，欲断诸结。尊者舍梨子！如是比丘起发牛角娑罗林。』」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent, excellent! Śāriputra, it is indeed as the bhikṣu Ānanda has said. Why is that? The bhikṣu Ānanda is accomplished in extensive learning."

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！舍梨子！实如阿难比丘所说。所以者何？阿难比丘成就多闻。」

The Venerable Śāriputra said, "World-Honored One, after the Venerable Ānanda had spoken, I further asked the Venerable Revata, 'Venerable Revata, the bhikṣu Ānanda has already spoken according to his understanding. I now further ask the Venerable Revata, this Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove is very delightful, with the bright moon at night and the wonderful fragrance of the śāla trees, like heavenly flowers. Venerable Revata, what kind of bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove?' The Venerable Revata then replied to me, saying, 'Venerable Śāriputra, if a bhikṣu delights in sitting in meditation, practices inner tranquility, does not neglect sitting in meditation, is accomplished in contemplation, always enjoys solitude, and delights in peaceful places, Venerable Śāriputra, such a bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove.'"

尊者舍梨子白曰：「世尊！贤者阿难如是说已，我复问曰：『贤者离越哆！贤者阿难比丘已说随所知。我今复问贤者离越哆，此牛角娑罗林甚可爱乐，夜有明月，诸娑罗树皆敷妙香，犹若天华。贤者离越哆！何等比丘起发牛角娑罗林？』贤者离越哆即答我曰：『尊者舍梨子！若有比丘乐于燕坐，内行止，不废坐禅，成就于观，常好闲居，憙安靖处，尊者舍梨子！如是比丘起发牛角娑罗林。』」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent, excellent! Śāriputra, it is indeed as the bhikṣu Revata has said. Why is that? The bhikṣu Revata always delights in sitting in meditation."

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！舍梨子！如离越哆比丘所说。所以者何？离越哆比丘常乐坐禅。」

The Venerable Śāriputra said, "World-Honored One, after the Venerable Revata had spoken, I further asked the Venerable Anuruddha, 'Venerable Anuruddha, the bhikṣu Revata has already spoken according to his understanding. I now further ask the Venerable Anuruddha, this Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove is very delightful, with the bright moon at night and the wonderful fragrance of the śāla trees, like heavenly flowers. Venerable Anuruddha, what kind of bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove?' The Venerable Anuruddha then replied to me, saying, 'Venerable Śāriputra, if a bhikṣu has attained the divine eye, accomplished the divine eye, and can see a thousand worlds with little effort in an instant. Venerable Śāriputra, it is like a person with eyes standing on a high building, looking down at a thousand earthen mounds on the open ground, they can see them all with little effort in an instant. Venerable Śāriputra, in the same way, if a bhikṣu has attained the divine eye, accomplished the divine eye, and can see a thousand worlds with little effort in an instant. Venerable Śāriputra, such a bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove.'"

尊者舍梨子白曰：「世尊！贤者离越哆如是说已，我复问曰：『贤者阿那律陀！贤者离越哆比丘已说随所知。我今复问贤者阿那律陀，此牛角娑罗林甚可爱乐，夜有明月，诸娑罗树皆敷妙香，犹若天华。贤者阿那律陀！何等比丘起发牛角娑罗林？』贤者阿那律陀即答我曰：『尊者舍梨子！若有比丘逮得天眼，成就天眼，于千世界彼少方便须臾尽见。尊者舍梨子！犹有目人住高楼上，于下露地有千土墼，彼少有方便须臾尽见。尊者舍梨子！如是。若有比丘逮得天眼，成就天眼，于千世界微少方便须臾尽见。尊者舍梨子！如是比丘起发牛角娑罗林。』」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent, excellent! Śāriputra, it is indeed as the bhikṣu Anuruddha has said. Why is that? The bhikṣu Anuruddha has accomplished the divine eye."

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！舍梨子！如阿那律陀比丘所说。所以者何？阿那律陀比丘成就天眼。」

The Venerable Śāriputra said, "World-Honored One, after the Venerable Anuruddha had spoken, I further asked the Venerable Kaccāna, 'Venerable Kaccāna, the bhikṣu Anuruddha has already spoken according to his understanding. I now further ask the Venerable Kaccāna, this Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove is very delightful, with the bright moon at night and the wonderful fragrance of the śāla trees, like heavenly flowers. Venerable Kaccāna, what kind of bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove?' The Venerable Kaccāna then replied to me, saying, 'Venerable Śāriputra, it is like two bhikṣu teachers discussing the profound Abhidharma together. They can understand and answer well whatever questions are asked, and their explanations are eloquent. Venerable Śāriputra, such a bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove.'"

尊者舍梨子白曰：「世尊！贤者阿那律陀如是说已，我复问曰：『贤者迦旃延！贤者阿那律陀比丘已说随所知。我今复问贤者迦旃延，此牛角娑罗林甚可爱乐，夜有明月，诸娑罗树皆敷妙香，犹若天华。贤者迦旃延！何等比丘起发牛角娑罗林？』贤者迦旃延即答我曰：『尊者舍梨子！犹二比丘法师共论甚深阿毘昙，彼所问事，善解悉知，答亦无碍，说法辩捷。尊者舍梨子！如是比丘起发牛角娑罗林。』」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent, excellent! Śāriputra, it is indeed as the bhikṣu Kaccāna has said. Why is that? The bhikṣu Kaccāna is a teacher who analyzes the Dharma."

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！舍梨子！如迦旃延比丘所说。所以者何？迦旃延比丘分别法师。」

The Venerable Śāriputra said, "World-Honored One, after the Venerable Kaccāna had spoken, I further asked the Venerable Mahākāśyapa, 'Venerable Mahākāśyapa, the bhikṣu Kaccāna has already spoken according to his understanding. I now further ask the Venerable Mahākāśyapa, this Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove is very delightful, with the bright moon at night and the wonderful fragrance of the śāla trees, like heavenly flowers. Venerable Mahākāśyapa, what kind of bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove?' The Venerable Mahākāśyapa then replied to me, saying, 'Venerable Śāriputra, it is like a bhikṣu who speaks of his own lack of affairs, his own few desires, his own contentment, his own joy in dwelling in solitude, his own practice of diligence, his own establishment of right mindfulness and right knowledge, his own attainment of concentration, his own wisdom, his own exhaustion of all taints, and his own encouragement, aspiration, accomplishment, and joy. Venerable Śāriputra, such a bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove.'"

尊者舍梨子白曰：「世尊！贤者迦旃延如是说已，我复问曰：『尊者大迦叶！贤者迦旃延比丘已说随所知。我今复问尊者大迦叶，此牛角娑罗林甚可爱乐，夜有明月，诸娑罗树皆敷妙香，犹若天华。尊者大迦叶！何等比丘起发牛角娑罗林？』尊者大迦叶即答我曰：『贤者舍梨子！若有比丘自无事称说无事，自有少欲称说少欲，自有知足称说知足，自乐在远离独住称说乐在远离独住，自修行精勤称说修行精勤，自立正念正智称说立正念正智，自得定称说得定，自有智慧称说智慧，自诸漏已尽称说诸漏已尽，自劝发渴仰成就欢喜称说劝发渴仰成就欢喜。贤者舍梨子！如是比丘起发牛角娑罗林。』」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent, excellent! Śāriputra, it is indeed as the bhikṣu Mahākāśyapa has said. Why is that? The bhikṣu Mahākāśyapa always practices without affairs."

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！舍梨子！如迦叶比丘所说。所以者何？迦叶比丘常行无事。」

The Venerable Śāriputra said, "World-Honored One, after the Venerable Mahākāśyapa had spoken, I further asked the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, 'Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, the Venerable Mahākāśyapa has already spoken according to his understanding. I now further ask the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, this Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove is very delightful, with the bright moon at night and the wonderful fragrance of the śāla trees, like heavenly flowers. Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, what kind of bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove?' The Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana then replied to me, saying, 'Venerable Śāriputra, it is like a bhikṣu who has great supernatural powers, great authority, great merit, great spiritual power, and unlimited supernatural powers. He practices unlimited supernatural powers, transforming one into many, combining many into one, abiding in one, with knowledge and vision, penetrating through stone walls as if through space without obstruction, entering and exiting the earth as if it were water, walking on water as if on land without sinking, rising into the sky and sitting cross-legged like a bird. The sun and moon have great supernatural powers, great authority, great merit, and great spiritual power. They can touch them with their hands and reach the Brahma Heaven. Venerable Śāriputra, such a bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove.'"

尊者舍梨子白曰：「世尊！尊者大迦叶如是说已，我复问曰：『贤者目揵连！尊者大迦叶已说随所知。我今复问贤者目揵连，此牛角娑罗林甚可爱乐，夜有明月，诸娑罗树皆敷妙香，犹若天华。贤者目揵连！何等比丘起发牛角娑罗林？』贤者大目揵连即答我曰：『尊者舍梨子！若有比丘有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，自在无量如意足，彼行无量如意足，变一为众，合众为一，一则住一，有知有见，彻过石壁，如空无碍，出入于地，犹若如水，履水如地而不陷没，上升虚空，结跏趺坐，犹若如鸟，今此日月有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，以手扪摸，身至梵天。尊者舍梨子！如是比丘起发牛角娑罗林。』」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent, excellent! Śāriputra, it is indeed as the bhikṣu Mahāmaudgalyāyana has said. Why is that? The bhikṣu Mahāmaudgalyāyana has great supernatural powers."

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！舍梨子！如目揵连比丘所说。所以者何？目揵连比丘有大如意足。」

Then the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana rose from his seat, arranged his robe over one shoulder, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "World-Honored One, after we had spoken thus, I then addressed the Venerable Śāriputra, saying, 'Venerable Śāriputra, I and the other Venerables have each spoken according to our understanding. I now ask the Venerable Śāriputra, this Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove is very delightful, with the bright moon at night and the wonderful fragrance of the śāla trees, like heavenly flowers. Venerable Śāriputra, what kind of bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove?' The Venerable Śāriputra then replied to me, saying, 'Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, it is like a bhikṣu who has mastery over his mind and is not controlled by his mind. If he wishes to roam about in the middle of the day, he can do so. If he wishes to roam about at noon or in the afternoon, he can do so. Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, it is like a king or a king's minister who has many garments of various fine colors. If he wishes to wear them in the middle of the day, he can do so. If he wishes to wear them at noon or in the afternoon, he can do so. Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, in the same way, if a bhikṣu has mastery over his mind and is not controlled by his mind, if he wishes to roam about in the middle of the day, he can do so. If he wishes to roam about at noon or in the afternoon, he can do so. Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, such a bhikṣu would arise in the Gośīrṣa Śāla Grove.'"

于是，尊者大目揵连即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！我及诸尊如是说已。便白尊者舍梨子曰：『尊者舍梨子！我及诸尊已各自说随其所知，我今问尊者舍梨子，此牛角娑罗林甚可爱乐，夜有明月，诸娑罗树皆敷妙香，犹若天华。尊者舍梨子！何等比丘起发牛角娑罗林？』尊者舍梨子即答我曰：『贤者目揵连！若有比丘随用心自在而不随心，彼若欲得随所住止，中前游行，即彼住止，中前游行。彼若欲得随所住止，日中、晡时游行，即彼住止，日中、晡时游行。贤者目揵连！犹王、王臣，衣服甚多，有若干种杂妙色衣，彼若欲得中前著者即取著之，彼若欲得日中、晡时著者即取著之。贤者目揵连！如是。若有比丘随用心自在而不随心，彼若欲得随所住止，中前游行，即彼住止，中前游行。彼若欲得随所住止，日中、晡时游行，即彼住止，日中、晡时游行。贤者目揵连！如是比丘起发牛角娑罗林。』」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent, excellent! Mahāmaudgalyāyana, it is indeed as the bhikṣu Śāriputra has said. Why is that? The bhikṣu Śāriputra has mastery over his mind."

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！目揵连！如舍梨子比丘所说。所以者何？舍梨子比丘随用心自在。」

Then the Venerable Śāriputra rose from his seat, arranged his robe over one shoulder, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "World-Honored One, after we had spoken thus, I addressed the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, saying, 'Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, I and the other Venerables have each spoken according to our understanding. Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, let us go together to the Buddha and discuss this matter. Among us, who will speak best?' World-Honored One, who among us spoke best?"

于是，尊者舍梨子即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！我及诸贤如是说已，告曰：『贤者目揵连！我及诸贤已各自说随其所知。贤者目揵连！我等宁可共彼诸贤往诣佛所，向论此事，于中知谁最为善说。』世尊！我等谁为善说耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Śāriputra, all of them spoke well. Why is that? These teachings are all what I have spoken. Śāriputra, listen to what I say. Thus, a bhikṣu should arise in the Sāla Grove. Śāriputra, if a bhikṣu dwells in a city or village, he should, after passing the night, put on his robe, take his alms bowl, enter the village to beg for food, well protect his body, well restrain his faculties, and well establish his mindfulness. After begging for food, he should, after noon, put away his robe and bowl, wash his hands and feet, put his sitting mat on his shoulder, and go to a place without affairs, or to the foot of a tree, or to an empty and quiet place. He should spread out his sitting mat, sit cross-legged, and not uncross his legs until his taints are exhausted. He should not uncross his legs until his taints are exhausted. Śāriputra, such a bhikṣu should arise in the Sāla Grove."

世尊答曰：「舍梨子！一切悉善。所以者何？此诸法者，尽我所说。舍梨子！听我所说，如是比丘起发牛角娑罗林。舍梨子！若有比丘随所依住城郭村邑，彼过夜平旦，著衣持钵，入村乞食，善守护身，善敛诸根，善立其念。彼乞食已，过日中后，收举衣钵，澡洗手足，以尼师坛著于肩上，或至无事处，或至树下，或至空安靖处，敷尼师坛，结跏趺坐，不解结跏趺坐乃至漏尽，彼便不解结跏趺坐乃至漏尽。舍梨子！如是比丘起发牛角娑罗林。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
