MĀ 185

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 15 双品

185. （一八五）牛角娑罗林经

Parallel to: MN 31 / EĀ 24.8 (~)

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the country of Nāma-tī-śu, staying at the Gandhakuṭi Monastery.

一时，佛游那摩提瘦，在揵祁精舍。

At that time, the World-Honored One, having passed the night, put on his robe in the early morning, took his alms bowl, entered Nāma-tī-śu to beg for alms, and after eating, went to the Ox-horn Śāla Grove.

尔时，世尊过夜平旦，著衣持钵，入那摩提而行乞食，食讫中后，往诣牛角娑罗林。

At that time, in the Ox-horn Śāla Grove, there were three clansmen who lived together, namely Venerable Aniruddha, Venerable Nandi, and Venerable Kimbila. Their practice was like this: If one of them returned from begging for alms first, he would prepare the bed, fetch water, bring out the foot-washing vessel, place the foot-washing stand and foot-wiping towel, water bottle, and washbasin. If the alms food could be finished, he would finish it. If there was any left, he would cover it in a vessel and put it aside. After eating, he would put away the bowl, wash his hands and feet, put the sitting mat on his shoulder, enter the room, and sit in meditation. If another returned from begging for alms later, if he could finish it, he would finish it. If not enough, he would take the remaining food from before and eat it. If there was any left, he would pour it on a clean place or in water without insects. He would take the alms bowl, wash it, and put it aside. He would put away the bed, pick up the foot-washing stand, put away the foot-wiping towel, and put away the foot-washing vessel, water bottle, and washbasin. He would sweep and sprinkle the dining hall, clean it, put away the robes and bowls, wash his hands and feet, put the sitting mat on his shoulder, enter the room, and sit in meditation. Those Venerables, in the afternoon, if one rose from meditation first, and saw that the water bottle and washbasin were empty, he would go and fetch water. If he could carry it, he would bring it back and put it aside. If he could not, he would beckon a monk with his hand. The two of them would lift it together and put it aside, without speaking to each other or asking each other. Those Venerables gathered once every five days, either to discuss the Dharma or to be in noble silence.

尔时，牛角娑罗林有三族姓子共在中住，尊者阿那律陀、尊者难提、尊者金毘罗。彼尊者等所行如是，若彼乞食有前还者，便敷床汲水，出洗足器，安洗足橙及拭脚巾、水瓶、澡罐。若所乞食能尽食者，便尽食之。若有余者，器盛覆举，食讫收钵，澡洗手足，以尼师坛著于肩上，入室燕坐。若彼乞食有后还者，能尽食者亦尽食之，若不足者，取前余食，足而食之，若有余者，便泻著净地及无虫水中。取彼食器，净洗拭已，举著一面，收卷床席，拾洗足橙，收拭脚巾，举洗足器及水瓶、澡罐，扫洒食堂，粪除净已，收举衣钵，澡洗手足，以尼师坛著于肩上，入室燕坐。彼尊者等至于晡时，若有先从燕坐起者，见水瓶、澡罐空无有水，便持行取，若能胜者，便举持来，安著一面，若不能胜，则便以手招一比丘，两人共举，持著一面，各不相语，各不相问。彼尊者等五日一集，或共说法，或圣默然。

Then the forest keeper saw the World-Honored One coming from afar and stopped him, saying: "Monk! Monk! Do not enter this forest. Why? There are three clansmen in this forest, Venerable Aniruddha, Venerable Nandi, and Venerable Kimbila. If they see you, they may not be pleased."

于是，守林人遥见世尊来，逆呵止曰：「沙门！沙门！莫入此林。所以者何？今此林中有三族姓子，尊者阿那律陀、尊者难提、尊者金毘罗，彼若见汝，或有不可。」

The World-Honored One said: "Forest keeper! If they see me, they will be pleased, not displeased."

世尊告曰：「汝守林人！彼若见我，必可，无不可。」

Then Venerable Aniruddha saw the World-Honored One coming from afar and rebuked him, saying: "Forest keeper! Do not rebuke the World-Honored One! Forest keeper! Do not rebuke the Well-Gone One! Why? My Honored One is coming, my Well-Gone One is coming."

于是，尊者阿那律陀遥见世尊来，即呵彼曰：「汝守林人！莫呵世尊！汝守林人！莫呵善逝！所以者何？是我尊来，我善逝来。」

Venerable Aniruddha went out to welcome the World-Honored One, taking his robe and bowl. Venerable Nanda prepared a seat for the Buddha, and Venerable Kanthaka fetched water for the Buddha.

尊者阿那律陀出迎世尊，摄佛衣钵，尊者难提为佛敷床，尊者金毘罗为佛取水。

Then the World-Honored One washed his hands and feet, sat on the seat prepared by the Venerable One, and asked: "Aniruddha, are you always at ease and without any lack?"

尔时，世尊洗手足已，坐彼尊者所敷之座，坐已，问曰：「阿那律陀，汝常安隐，无所乏耶？」

Venerable Aniruddha replied: "World-Honored One! I am always at ease and without any lack."

尊者阿那律陀白曰：「世尊！我常安隐，无有所乏。」

The World-Honored One further asked: "Aniruddha! How are you at ease and without any lack?"

世尊复问：「阿那律陀！云何安隐，无所乏耶？」

Venerable Aniruddha replied: "World-Honored One! I think thus: 'I have good benefits and great merits, in that I practice together with such pure conduct.' World-Honored One! I always practice loving-kindness with the body, loving-kindness with speech, and loving-kindness with the mind towards those who practice pure conduct, whether seen or unseen, without any difference. World-Honored One! I think thus: 'I should now abandon my own mind and follow the minds of those virtuous ones.' I then abandoned my own mind and followed the minds of those virtuous ones. I have never had an unloving mind. World-Honored One! In this way, I am always at ease and without any lack."

尊者阿那律陀白曰：「世尊！我作是念：『我有善利，有大功德，谓我与如是梵行共行。』世尊！我常向彼梵行行慈身业，见与不见，等无有异，行慈口业，行慈意业，见与不见，等无有异。世尊！我作是念：『我今宁可自舍己心，随彼诸贤心。』我便自舍己心，随彼诸贤心，我未曾有一不可心。世尊！如是我常安隐，无有所乏。」

He asked Venerable Nandi, and the answer was also the same. He further asked Venerable Kimbila: "Are you always at ease and without any lack?"

问尊者难提，答亦如是。复问尊者金毘罗曰：「汝常安隐，无所乏耶？」

Venerable Kimbila replied: "World-Honored One! I am always at ease and without any lack."

尊者金毘罗白曰：「世尊！我常安隐，无有所乏。」

He asked: "Kimbila! How are you at ease and without any lack?"

问曰：「金毘罗！云何安隐，无所乏耶？」

Venerable Kimbila replied: "World-Honored One! I think thus: 'I have great benefits and merits, namely, that I practice together with such pure conduct.' World-Honored One! I always practice loving-kindness with the body, seeing and not seeing, being the same without difference, practicing loving-kindness with speech, practicing loving-kindness with the mind, seeing and not seeing, being the same without difference. World-Honored One! I think thus: 'I should now abandon my own mind and follow the minds of those virtuous ones.' I then abandoned my own mind and followed the minds of those virtuous ones. I have never had an unlikable mind. World-Honored One! Thus I am always at ease and without any lack."

尊者金毘罗白曰：「世尊！我作是念：『我有善利，有大功德，谓我与如是梵行共行。』世尊！我常向彼梵行行慈身业，见与不见，等无有异，行慈口业，行慈意业，见与不见，等无有异。世尊！我作是念：『我今宁可自舍己心，随彼诸贤心。』我便自舍己心，随彼诸贤心，我未曾有一不可心。世尊！如是我常安隐，无有所乏。」

The World-Honored One praised: "Excellent! Excellent! Anuruddha! Thus you all are always in harmony, at ease, without dispute, with one mind and one teacher, united like water and milk. Have you attained the supreme human Dharma, with differences and peaceful dwelling?"

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！阿那律陀！如是汝等常共和合，安隐无诤，一心一师，合一水乳，颇得人上之法，而有差降安乐住止耶？」

Venerable Anuruddha replied: "World-Honored One! Thus we are always in harmony, at ease, without dispute, with one mind and one teacher, united like water and milk, attaining the supreme human Dharma, with differences and peaceful dwelling. World-Honored One! We are separated from desire, separated from evil and unwholesome dharmas, and attain the fourth dhyāna, accomplishing and dwelling in it. World-Honored One! Thus we are always in harmony, at ease, without dispute, with one mind and one teacher, united like water and milk, attaining this supreme human Dharma, with differences and peaceful dwelling."

尊者阿那律陀白曰：「世尊！如是我等常共和合，安隐无诤，一心一师，合一水乳，得人上之法，而有差降安乐住止。世尊！我等离欲、离恶不善之法，至得第四禅成就游。世尊！如是我等常共和合，安隐无诤，一心一师，合一水乳，得此人上之法，而有差降安乐住止。」

The World-Honored One praised: "Excellent! Excellent! Anuruddha! Abandoning this dwelling, surpassing it, crossing over it, is there any other supreme human Dharma attained, with differences and peaceful dwelling?"

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！阿那律陀！舍此住止，过此度此，颇更有余得人上之法，而有差降安乐住止耶？」

Venerable Anuruddha replied: "World-Honored One! Abandoning this dwelling, surpassing it, crossing over it, there is indeed another supreme human Dharma attained, with differences and peaceful dwelling. World-Honored One! My mind is filled with loving-kindness, pervading one direction, accomplishing and dwelling in it. Thus in the second, third, and fourth directions, the four intermediate directions, above and below, everywhere, my mind is filled with loving-kindness, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without dispute, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, accomplishing and dwelling in it. Thus with compassion and joy, my mind is filled with equanimity, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without dispute, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, accomplishing and dwelling in it. World-Honored One! Abandoning this dwelling, surpassing it, crossing over it, this is indeed another supreme human Dharma attained, with differences and peaceful dwelling."

尊者阿那律陀白曰：「世尊！舍此住止，过此度此，更复有余得人上之法，而有差降安乐住止。世尊！我心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游。如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。世尊！舍此住止，过此度此，谓更有此余得人上之法，而有差降安乐住止。」

The World-Honored One praised: "Excellent! Excellent! Anuruddha! Abandoning this dwelling, surpassing it, crossing over it, is there any other supreme human Dharma attained, with differences and peaceful dwelling?"

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！阿那律陀！舍此住止，过此度此，颇更有余得人上之法，而有差降安乐住止耶？」

Venerable Anuruddha replied: "World-Honored One! Abandoning this dwelling, surpassing it, crossing over it, there is indeed another supreme human Dharma attained, with differences and peaceful dwelling. World-Honored One! We transcend all perceptions of form, and attain the state of neither perception nor non-perception, accomplishing and dwelling in it. World-Honored One! Abandoning this dwelling, surpassing it, crossing over it, this is indeed another supreme human Dharma attained, with differences and peaceful dwelling."

尊者阿那律陀白曰：「世尊！舍此住止，过此度此，更复有余得人上之法，而有差降安乐住止。世尊！我等度一切色想，至得非有想非无想处成就游。世尊！舍此住止，过此度此，谓更有此余得人上之法，而有差降安乐住止。」

The World-Honored One praised: "Excellent! Excellent! Anuruddha! Abandoning this dwelling, surpassing it, crossing over it, is there any other supreme human Dharma attained, with differences and peaceful dwelling?"

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！阿那律陀！舍此住止，过此度此，颇更有余得人上之法，而有差降安乐住止耶？」

Venerable Anuruddha replied: "World-Honored One! Abandoning this dwelling, surpassing it, crossing over it, there is indeed another supreme human Dharma attained, with differences and peaceful dwelling. World-Honored One! We attain the bases of spiritual power, divine hearing, knowledge of others' minds, knowledge of past lives, and knowledge of the end of birth and death. Our taints are ended, and we have attained the uncontaminated, with the mind liberated through the ending of taints, and the wisdom liberated through the ending of taints. In this very life, we know and see for ourselves, and we have attained the ending of birth and death, the ending of the round of rebirth, the knowledge that is true, the knowledge that is right, the knowledge that is proper, the knowledge that is perfect. World-Honored One! Abandoning this dwelling, surpassing it, crossing over it, this is indeed another supreme human Dharma attained, with differences and peaceful dwelling."

尊者阿那律陀白曰：「世尊！舍此住止，过此度此，更复有余得人上之法，而有差降安乐住止。世尊！我等得如意足、天耳智、他心智、宿命智、生死智，诸漏已尽，得无漏，心解脱、慧解脱，于现法中自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。世尊！舍此住止，过此度此，谓更有此余得人上之法，而有差降安乐住止。」

The World-Honored One praised: "Excellent! Excellent! Anuruddha! Abandoning this dwelling, surpassing it, crossing over it, is there any other supreme human Dharma attained, with differences and peaceful dwelling?"

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！阿那律陀！舍此住止，过此度此，颇更有余得人上之法，而有差降安乐住止耶？」

Venerable Anuruddha replied: "World-Honored One! Abandoning this dwelling, surpassing it, crossing over it, there is indeed no other supreme human Dharma attained, with differences and peaceful dwelling."

尊者阿那律陀白曰：「世尊！舍此住止，过此度此，更无有余得人上之法，而有差降安乐住止。」

Then the World-Honored One thought: "The places where these noble sons roam are peaceful and blissful. I should now teach the Dharma for them." Having thought this, the World-Honored One taught the Dharma to Venerable Anuruddha, Venerable Nandiya, and Venerable Kimbila, inspiring them with yearning, bringing them to accomplishment and joy. Having taught the Dharma to them with immeasurable skillful means, inspiring them with yearning, bringing them to accomplishment and joy, he rose from his seat and departed. Then Venerable Anuruddha, Nandiya, and Kimbila saw the World-Honored One off, going as far as was appropriate, and then returned to their dwelling.

于是，世尊便作是念：「此族姓子之所游行，安隐快乐，我今宁可为彼说法。」世尊作是念已，即为尊者阿那律陀、尊者难提、尊者金毘罗说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，从坐起去。于是，尊者阿那律陀、难提、金毘罗送世尊，随其近远，便还所住。

Venerable Nandiya and Venerable Kimbila praised Venerable Anuruddha, saying: "Excellent! Excellent! Venerable Anuruddha! We have never heard Venerable Anuruddha speak such words before. We have great supernatural powers, great authority, great merit, and great spiritual power, but Venerable Anuruddha has fully praised us to the World-Honored One.

尊者难提、尊者金毘罗叹尊者阿那律陀曰：「善哉！善哉！尊者阿那律陀！我等初不闻尊者阿那律陀说如是义，我等如是有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，然尊者阿那律陀尽向世尊极称誉我等。」

Venerable Anuruddha praised Venerable Nandiya and Kimbila, saying: "Excellent! Excellent! Venerable Ones! I have also never heard from you venerable ones before that you have such great supernatural powers, great authority, great merit, and great spiritual power. But for a long time I have known your minds with my mind, and you venerable ones have great supernatural powers, great authority, great merit, and great spiritual power. Therefore I have spoken to the World-Honored One in this way.

尊者阿那律陀叹尊者难提、金毘罗曰：「善哉！善哉！尊者！我亦初未曾从诸贤等闻，尊者如是有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神。然我长夜以心知尊者心，尊者有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，是故我向世尊如是如是说。」

Then the long-bodied ghost, with an extremely wonderful form and majestic radiance, went to where the Buddha was at dawn, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said to the World-Honored One: "Great Sage! The people of the Vajji clan have gained great benefits, for they now have the World-Honored One and the three clansmen, Venerable Anuruddha, Venerable Nandiya, and Venerable Kimbila.

于是，长鬼天形体极妙，光明巍巍，夜将向旦，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，却住一面，白世尊曰：「大仙人！诸跋耆人得大善利，谓现有世尊及三族姓子，尊者阿那律陀、尊者难提、尊者金毘罗。」

The earth spirit heard what the long-bodied ghost said and let out a loud voice: "Great Sage! The people of the Vajji clan have gained great benefits, for they now have the World-Honored One and the three clansmen, Venerable Anuruddha, Nandiya, and Kimbila.

地神从长鬼天闻所说，放高大音声：「大仙人！诸跋耆人得大善利，谓现有世尊及三族姓子，尊者阿那律陀、难提、金毘罗。」

The sound was heard by the sky spirit, the four heavenly kings, the thirty-three heavens, the Yama heavens, the Tuṣita heavens, the Nirmāṇarati heavens, the Paranirmitavaśavartin heavens, and in an instant the sound reached the Brahma heavens: "Great Sage! The people of the Vajji clan have gained great benefits, for they now have the World-Honored One and the three clansmen, Venerable Anuruddha, Nandiya, and Kimbila.

从地神闻声，虚空天、四王天、三十三天、［火＊佥］摩天、兜率哆天、化乐天、他化乐天，须臾声彻至于梵天：「大仙人！诸跋耆人得大善利，谓现有世尊及三族姓子，尊者阿那律陀、难提、金毘罗。」

The World-Honored One said, "So it is, so it is! Long-bodied ghost! The people of the Vajji clan have gained great benefits, for they now have the World-Honored One and the three clansmen, Venerable Anuruddha, Nandiya, and Kimbila. Long-bodied ghost! The earth spirit heard your voice and immediately let out a loud voice: 'Great Sage! The people of the Vajji clan have gained great benefits, for they now have the World-Honored One and the three clansmen, Venerable Anuruddha, Nandiya, and Kimbila.' From the earth spirit's voice, the sky spirit, the four heavenly kings, the thirty-three heavens, the Yama heavens, the Tuṣita heavens, the Nirmāṇarati heavens, the Paranirmitavaśavartin heavens, in an instant the sound reached the Brahma heavens: 'Great Sage! The people of the Vajji clan have gained great benefits, for they now have the World-Honored One and the three clansmen, Venerable Anuruddha, Nandiya, and Kimbila.' Long-bodied ghost! If those three clans remember the causes and practices of these three clansmen, they will also gain great benefits for a long time, and be at ease and happy. If the villages, gods, demons, Brahmas, monks, Brahmins, from humans to gods, remember the causes and practices of these three clansmen, they will also gain benefits for a long time, and be at ease and happy. Long-bodied ghost! These three clansmen have such great supernatural powers, great virtue, great blessings, and great spiritual power."

世尊告曰：「如是，如是。长鬼天！诸跋耆人得大善利，谓现有世尊及三族姓子，尊者阿那律陀、难提、金毘罗。长鬼天！地神闻汝声已，便放高大音声：『大仙人！诸跋耆人得大善利，谓现有世尊及三族姓子，尊者阿那律陀、难提、金毘罗。』从地神闻声，虚空天、四天王天三十三天、［火＊佥］摩天、兜率哆天、化乐天、他化乐天，须臾声彻至于梵天：『大仙人！诸跋耆人得大善利，谓现有世尊及三族姓子，尊者阿那律陀、难提、金毘罗。』长鬼天！若彼三族家，此三族姓子剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，彼三族家忆此三族姓子所因、所行者，彼亦长夜得大善利，安隐快乐。若彼村邑及天、魔、梵、沙门、梵志，从人至天，忆此三族姓子所因、所行者，彼亦长夜得利饶益，安隐快乐。长鬼天！此三族姓子如是有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The three clansmen and the long-bodied ghost, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。此三族姓子及长鬼天，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

Translated from Chinese using Gemma-2-Mitra (Buddhist-NLP).

This is the classical Chinese root text of Madhyamāgama, a parallel to the Pali sutta(s) listed above. Source: CBETA (Taishō edition).
