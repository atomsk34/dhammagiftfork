MĀ 188

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 15 双品

188. （一八八）阿夷那经

Parallel to: AN 10.115–116

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Shravasti, staying at the Deer Mother's Hall in the Eastern Garden.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在于东园鹿子母堂。

At that time, the World-Honored One rose from his meditation in the afternoon, came down from the hall, and walked in the open air in the shadow of the hall, expounding the profound and subtle Dharma to the monks. At that time, the non-Buddhist scholar Aiyana, a disciple of the monk Man-tou, saw from afar the World-Honored One rising from his meditation, coming down from the hall, and walking in the open air in the shadow of the hall, expounding the profound and subtle Dharma to the monks. The non-Buddhist scholar Aiyana, a disciple of the monk Man-tou, went to where the Buddha was, exchanged greetings, and walked with the Buddha. The World-Honored One turned around and asked, "Aiyana! The monk Man-tou really thinks five hundred times. If there are any other monks or Brahmins who claim to know and see everything, and to have no more to know and no more to see, they will see his faults and claim to have faults.

尔时，世尊则于晡时，从燕坐起，堂上来下，在堂影中露地经行，为诸比丘广说甚深微妙之法。彼时，异学阿夷那，沙门蛮头弟子，遥见世尊从燕坐起，堂上来下，在堂影中露地经行，为诸比丘广说甚深微妙之法。异学阿夷那，沙门蛮头弟子，往诣佛所，共相问讯，随佛经行。世尊回顾问曰：「阿夷那！沙门蛮头实思五百思。若有异沙门、梵志一切知、一切见者，自称我有无余、知无余，见彼有过、自称有过。」

The non-Buddhist scholar Aiyana, a disciple of the monk Man-tou, replied, "Gautama! The monk Man-tou really thinks five hundred times. If there are any other monks or Brahmins who claim to know and see everything, and to have no more to know and no more to see, they will see his faults and claim to have faults.

异学阿夷那，沙门蛮头弟子，答曰：「瞿昙！沙门蛮头实思五百思。若有异沙门、梵志一切知、一切见者，自称我有无余、知无余，见彼有过、自称有过。」

The World-Honored One further asked, "Aiyana! How does the monk Man-tou think five hundred times? If there are any other monks or Brahmins who claim to know and see everything, and to have no more to know and no more to see, they will see his faults and claim to have faults?"

世尊复问曰：「阿夷那！云何沙门蛮头思五百思？若有异沙门、梵志一切知、一切见者，自称我有无余、知无余，见彼有过、自称有过耶？」

The non-Buddhist scholar Aiyana, a disciple of the monk Man-tou, replied, "Gautama! The monk Man-tou says like this: 'Whether walking, standing, sitting, lying down, sleeping, waking, day or night, I always have unobstructed knowledge and vision. Sometimes I encounter runaway horses, runaway chariots, runaway soldiers, runaway men, or runaway women, or walk on such roads, encountering vicious elephants, vicious horses, vicious cows, or vicious dogs, or encountering a snake nest, or being hit by a clod, or being beaten with a stick, or falling into a ditch, or falling into a latrine, or riding a sleeping bull, or falling into a deep pit, or entering thorns, or seeing a village, asking about the name and the road, seeing men and women, asking about the surname and the name, or looking at an empty house, or entering a clan like this. After entering, they ask me: "Venerable one, where are you coming from?" I reply to them: "Virtuous ones! I am going to a bad path."' Gautama! The monk Man-tou thinks five hundred times like this. If there are any other monks or Brahmins who claim to know and see everything, and to have no more to know and no more to see, they will see his faults.

异学阿夷那，沙门蛮头弟子，答曰：「瞿昙！沙门蛮头作如是说：『若行、若住、若坐、若卧、若眠、若寤，或昼、或夜，常无碍知见。或时逢［马＊奔］象、逸马、［马＊奔］车、叛兵、走男、走女，或行如是道，逢恶象、恶马、恶牛、恶狗，或值蛇聚，或得块掷，或得杖打，或堕沟渎，或堕厕中，或乘卧牛，或堕深坑，或入刺中，或见村邑，问名问道，见男见女，问姓问名，或观空舍，或如是入族。彼既入已，而问我曰：「尊从何行？」我答彼曰：「诸贤！我趣恶道也。」』瞿昙！沙门蛮头如是比丘思五百思。若有异沙门、梵志一切知、一切见者，自称我有无余、知无余，见彼有过也。」

Then the World-Honored One, leaving his walking path, went to the head of the walking path, spread out his sitting mat, sat down cross-legged, and asked the monks, "Do you uphold what I have said about wisdom?" Those monks remained silent and did not reply.

于是，世尊离于经行，至经行头敷尼师檀，结跏趺坐，问诸比丘：「我所说智慧事，汝等受持耶？」彼诸比丘默然不答。

The World-Honored One went to ask a second and a third time, "Monks! Do you uphold what I have said about wisdom?" The monks also remained silent and did not reply.

世尊复至再三问曰：「诸比丘！我所说智慧事，汝等受持耶？」诸比丘亦至再三默然不答。

At that time, a certain monk rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "World-Honored One! Now is the time. Well-Gone One! Now is the time. If the World-Honored One would speak about wisdom for the monks, the monks would listen well and uphold it from the World-Honored One.

彼时，有一比丘即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！今正是时。善逝！今正是时。若世尊为诸比丘说智慧事，诸比丘从世尊闻，当善受持。」

The World-Honored One said, "Monk! Listen attentively and think well about it. I shall explain it in detail for you.

世尊告曰：「比丘！谛听，善思念之，我当为汝具分别说。」

The monks said, "Yes, we shall listen attentively.

时，诸比丘白曰：「唯然。当受教听。」

The Buddha said again, "There are two kinds of assemblies: the Dharma assembly and the non-Dharma assembly. What is the non-Dharma assembly? If someone practices non-Dharma and speaks non-Dharma, and the assembly also practices non-Dharma and speaks non-Dharma, then that person, dwelling before the non-Dharma assembly, speaks falsely about what they know, not truthfully, revealing and distinguishing their practices, spreading and explaining them in sequence, wishing to cut off others' evil intentions, but it is difficult to question and cannot be spoken of. In the true Dharma and Vinaya, one cannot establish what one knows. That person, dwelling before the non-Dharma assembly, claims to have wisdom and universal knowledge. If there are such statements about wisdom in that assembly, that is called the non-Dharma assembly. What is the Dharma assembly? If someone practices the Dharma and speaks the Dharma, and the assembly also practices the Dharma and speaks the Dharma, then that person, dwelling before the Dharma assembly, speaks truthfully about what they know, not falsely, revealing and distinguishing their practices, spreading and explaining them in sequence, wishing to cut off others' evil intentions, and it is possible to question and speak of it. In the true Dharma, one can establish what one knows. That person, dwelling before the Dharma assembly, claims to have wisdom and universal knowledge. If there are such statements about wisdom in that assembly, that is called the Dharma assembly. Therefore, you should know the Dharma and non-Dharma, the meaning and non-meaning. Knowing the Dharma and non-Dharma, the meaning and non-meaning, you should practice according to the Dharma and meaning."

佛复告曰：「凡有二众，一曰法众，二曰非法众。何者非法众？或有一行非法说非法，彼众亦行非法说非法，彼非法人住非法众前，自已所知，而虚妄言，不是真实，显示分别，施设其行，流布次第说法，欲断他意弊恶，难诘不可说也。于正法、律中，不可称立自已所知，彼非法人住非法众前，自称我有智慧普知，于中若有如是说智慧事者，是谓非法众。何者法众？或有一行法说法，彼众亦行法说法，彼法人住法众前，自已所知，不虚妄言，是真是实，显示分别，施设其行，流布次第说，欲断他意弊恶，难诘则可说也。于正法中而可称立自已所知，彼法人住法众前，自称我有智慧普知，于中若有如是说智慧事者，是谓法众。是故汝等当知法、非法，义与非义，知法、非法，义、非义已，汝等当学如法如义。」

The Buddha spoke thus and then rose from his seat, entered the room, and sat in meditation. Then the monks thought, "Venerable friends! You should know that the World-Honored One spoke briefly on this matter and did not elaborate, then rose from his seat and entered the room to sit in meditation. 'Therefore, you should know the Dharma and non-Dharma, the meaning and non-meaning. Knowing the Dharma and non-Dharma, the meaning and non-meaning, you should practice according to the Dharma and meaning.'" They further thought, "Venerable friends! Who can elaborate on the meaning briefly spoken by the World-Honored One?" They further thought, "Venerable Ānanda is the Buddha's attendant and knows the Buddha's intention. He is always praised by the World-Honored One and by the wise and virtuous ones. Venerable Ānanda can elaborate on the meaning briefly spoken by the World-Honored One. Venerable friends! Let us go together to Venerable Ānanda and request him to explain this meaning. If Venerable Ānanda explains it, we shall uphold it well."

佛说如是，即从座起，入室燕坐。于是诸比丘便作是念：「诸贤！当知世尊略说此义，不广分别，即从坐起，入室燕坐。『是故，汝等当知法、非法，义与非义，知法、非法，义、非义已，汝等当学如法如义。』」彼复作是念：「诸贤！谁能广分别世尊向所略说义？」彼复作是念：「尊者阿难是佛侍者而知佛意，常为世尊之所称誉，及诸智梵行人。尊者阿难能广分别世尊向所略说义。诸贤！共往诣尊者阿难所，请说此义。若尊者阿难为分别者，我等当善受持。」

Then the monks went to Venerable Ānanda, exchanged greetings, sat to one side, and said, "Venerable Ānanda! You should know that the World-Honored One spoke briefly on this matter and did not elaborate, then rose from his seat and entered the room to sit in meditation. 'You should know the Dharma and non-Dharma, the meaning and non-meaning. Knowing the Dharma and non-Dharma, the meaning and non-meaning, you should practice according to the Dharma and meaning.' We then thought, 'Venerable friends! Who can elaborate on the meaning briefly spoken by the World-Honored One?' We further thought, 'Venerable Ānanda is the Buddha's attendant and knows the Buddha's intention. He is always praised by the World-Honored One and by the wise and virtuous ones. Venerable Ānanda can elaborate on the meaning briefly spoken by the World-Honored One.' We only wish that Venerable Ānanda would explain it out of compassion."

于是，诸比丘往诣尊者阿难所，共相问讯，却坐一面，白曰：「尊者阿难！当知世尊略说此义，不广分别，即从坐起，入室燕坐。『汝等当知法、非法，义与非义，知法、非法，义、非义已，汝等当学如法如义。』我等便作是念：『诸贤！谁能广分别世尊向所略说义！』我等复作是念：『尊者阿难是佛侍者而知佛意，常为世尊之所称誉，及诸智梵行人。尊者阿难能广分别世尊向所略说义。』唯愿尊者阿难为慈愍故而广说之。」

Venerable Ānanda said, "Venerable friends! Listen to my parable. The wise, hearing a parable, understand its meaning. Venerable friends! It is like a person who wants to obtain the fruit. In order to obtain the fruit, he takes an axe and enters the forest. He sees a big tree with roots, trunk, joints, branches, leaves, flowers, and fruit. He does not touch the roots, trunk, joints, and fruit, but only touches the branches and leaves. What you say is also like this. The World-Honored One is present. You should go to him and ask about this matter. Why? Venerable friends! You should know that the World-Honored One is the eye, the knowledge, the meaning, the Dharma, the lord of the Dharma, the leader of the Dharma, who speaks the true meaning and manifests all meanings. Venerable friends! You should go to the World-Honored One and ask about this matter: 'World-Honored One! What is this? What is the meaning of this?' As the World-Honored One explains it, you should uphold it well."

尊者阿难告曰：「诸贤！听我说喻，慧者闻喻则解其义。诸贤！犹如有人欲得求实，为求实故，持斧入林，彼见大树成根、茎、节、枝、叶、华、实，彼人不触根、茎、节、实，但触枝、叶。诸贤所说亦复如是。世尊现在，舍来就我而问此义。所以者何？诸贤！当知世尊是眼、是智、是义、是法、法主、法将，说真谛义，现一切义由彼世尊，诸贤应往诣世尊所而问此义：『世尊！此云何？此何义？』如世尊说者，诸贤等当善受持。」

The monks said, "Yes, Venerable Ānanda! The World-Honored One is the eye, the knowledge, the meaning, the Dharma, the lord of the Dharma, the leader of the Dharma, who speaks the true meaning and manifests all meanings. Venerable Ānanda is the Buddha's attendant and knows the Buddha's intention. He is always praised by the World-Honored One and by the wise and virtuous ones. Venerable Ānanda can elaborate on the meaning briefly spoken by the World-Honored One. We only wish that Venerable Ānanda would explain it out of compassion."

时，诸比丘白曰：「唯然。尊者阿难！世尊是眼、是智、是义、是法、法主、法将，说真谛义，现一切义由彼世尊。然尊者阿难是佛侍者而知佛意，常为世尊之所称誉，及诸智梵行人。尊者阿难能广分别世尊向所略说义。唯愿尊者阿难为慈愍故而广说之。」

Venerable Ānanda said to the monks, "Venerable friends! Listen to what I have to say. Venerable friends! Wrong views are not the Dharma, right views are the Dharma. If wrong views give rise to immeasurable evil and unwholesome dharmas, that is called non-meaning. If right views give rise to immeasurable wholesome dharmas, that is called meaning. Venerable friends! Up to wrong knowledge is not the Dharma, right knowledge is the Dharma. If wrong knowledge gives rise to immeasurable evil and unwholesome dharmas, that is called non-meaning. If right knowledge gives rise to immeasurable wholesome dharmas, that is called meaning. Venerable friends! This is what the World-Honored One briefly spoke of, without elaborating. He then rose from his seat, entered the room, and sat in meditation. 'Therefore, you should know the Dharma and the non-Dharma, meaning and non-meaning. Having known the Dharma and the non-Dharma, meaning and non-meaning, you should practice according to the Dharma and according to meaning.' This is what the World-Honored One briefly spoke of, without elaborating. I have explained it in this way with these words and phrases. Venerable friends! You may go to the Buddha and report it. If it is as the World-Honored One has said, you may uphold it."

尊者阿难告诸比丘：「诸贤等！共听我所说。诸贤！邪见非法，正见是法。若有因邪见生无量恶不善法者，是谓非义；若因正见生无量善法者，是谓是义。诸贤！乃至邪智非法，正智是法，若因邪智生无量恶不善法者，是谓非义；若因正智生无量善法者，是谓是义。诸贤！谓世尊略说此义，不广分别，即从坐起，入室燕坐。『是故汝等当知法、非法，义与非义，知法、非法，义、非义已，汝等当学如法如义。』此世尊略说，不广分别义，我以此句、以此文广说如是，诸贤可往向佛具陈，若如世尊所说义者，诸贤等便可受持。」

Then the monks, having heard what Venerable Ānanda said, accepted and recited it well. They rose from their seats, circumambulated Venerable Ānanda three times to the right, and departed. They went to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at his feet, sat to one side, and said, "World-Honored One! The World-Honored One briefly spoke of this meaning earlier, without elaborating, and then rose from his seat, entered the room, and sat in meditation. Venerable Ānanda explained it in this way with these words and phrases."

于是，诸比丘闻尊者阿难所说，善受持诵，即从坐起，绕尊者阿难三匝而去，往诣佛所，稽首作礼，却坐一面，白曰：「世尊！向世尊略说此义，不广分别，即从坐起，入室燕坐。尊者阿难以此句、以此文而广说之。」

The World-Honored One, having heard this, praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Among my disciples, there are those with eyes, wisdom, the Dharma, and meaning. Why is that? Because the teacher briefly speaks of this meaning for the disciples, without elaborating, and the disciples explain it in this way with these words and phrases. As Ānanda has said, you should accept and uphold it like this. Why is that? Because explaining the meaning should be like this."

世尊闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！我弟子中有眼、有智、有法、有义。所以者何？谓师为弟子略说此义，不广分别，彼弟子以此句、以此文而广说之。如阿难所说，汝等应当如是受持。所以者何？以说观义应如是也。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those monks, having heard what the Buddha said, joyfully received and practiced it.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
