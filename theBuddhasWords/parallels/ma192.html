192. （一九二）加楼乌陀夷经

Parallel to: MN 66 / EĀ 49.7 (~)

Kāluḍāyi, Maudgalyāyana, Mahāmaudgalyāyana, Mahākauṣṭhila, Mahākapphiṇa, Mahākauṣṭhila, Mahākapphiṇa, Mahākauṣṭhila

加楼乌陀夷　　牟梨破群那跋陀、阿湿贝　　周那、优婆离调御、痴慧地　　阿梨咤] cf.［丽】、［口＊荼］帝

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Aṅga, together with a great assembly of bhikṣus. He went to the Jetavana Monastery in Anāthapiṇḍada's Park.

一时，佛游鸯伽国中，与大比丘众俱，往至阿惒那住揵若精舍。

At that time, the World-Honored One, after the night had passed, put on his robe, took his bowl, entered Anāthapiṇḍada's Park to beg for alms, finished eating in the middle of the day, put away his robe and bowl, washed his hands and feet, put his sitting mat on his shoulder, went to a certain forest, and wished to walk in meditation during the day. Venerable Udayin also, after the night had passed, put on his robe, took his bowl, entered Anāthapiṇḍada's Park to beg for alms, finished eating in the middle of the day, put away his robe and bowl, washed his hands and feet, put his sitting mat on his shoulder, followed behind the Buddha, and thought like this: "If the World-Honored One now wishes to walk in meditation during the day, I will also go there to walk in meditation during the day."

尔时，世尊过夜平旦，著衣持钵，入阿惒那而行乞食，食讫中后，收举衣钵，澡洗手足，以尼师檀著于肩上，往至一林，欲昼经行。尊者乌陀夷亦过夜平旦，著衣持钵，入阿惒那而行乞食，食讫中后，收举衣钵，澡洗手足，以尼师檀著于肩上，随侍佛后，而作是念：「若世尊今昼行者，我亦至彼昼行。」

Then the World-Honored One entered the forest, went to the foot of a tree, spread out his sitting mat, and sat down cross-legged. Venerable Udayin also entered the forest, not far from the Buddha, went to the foot of a tree, spread out his sitting mat, and sat down cross-legged. At that time, Venerable Udayin was alone in a quiet place, sitting in meditation and contemplating. His mind gave rise to this thought: "The World-Honored One has greatly benefited us, the Well-Gone One has greatly brought peace to us. The World-Honored One has removed the dharmas of suffering for us and increased the dharmas of happiness. The World-Honored One has removed the immeasurable bad and unwholesome dharmas for us and increased the immeasurable good and wonderful dharmas.

于是，世尊入于林中，至一树下，敷尼师檀，结跏趺坐。尊者乌陀夷亦入彼林，去佛不远，至一树下，敷尼师檀，结跏趺坐。尔时，尊者乌陀夷独在靖处燕坐思惟，心作是念：「世尊为我等多所饶益，善逝为我等多所安隐。世尊于我除众苦法，增益乐法。世尊于我除无量恶不善之法，增益无量诸善妙法。」

Venerable Udayin then rose from his sitting in meditation in the afternoon, went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and the World-Honored One said to him: "Udayin! Are you not lacking in anything? Are you at ease and happy, with your strength as usual?"

尊者乌陀夷则于晡时从燕坐起，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，却坐一面，世尊告曰：「乌陀夷！无有所乏，安隐快乐，气力如常耶？」

Venerable Udayin replied: "Yes, World-Honored One! I am not lacking in anything. I am at ease and happy, with my strength as usual."

尊者乌陀夷白曰：「唯然。世尊！我无所乏，安隐快乐，气力如常。」

The World-Honored One further asked: "Udayin! How is it that you are not lacking in anything, at ease and happy, with your strength as usual?"

世尊复问曰：「乌陀夷！云何汝无所乏，安隐快乐，气力如常耶？」

Venerable Udayin replied: "World-Honored One! When I was alone in a quiet place, sitting in meditation, I thought to myself: 'The World-Honored One has greatly benefited us, the Well-Gone One has greatly brought us peace. The World-Honored One has removed the dharmas of suffering for us and increased the dharmas of happiness. The World-Honored One has removed the immeasurable bad and unwholesome dharmas for us and increased the immeasurable wholesome and wonderful dharmas.' The World-Honored One once told the monks: 'You should stop eating after noon.' World-Honored One! When we heard this, we were unable to bear it, unwilling, and displeased. If there were faithful Brahmins and householders who came to the monastery to make extensive offerings, we would receive food with our own hands. But now the World-Honored One teaches us to stop this, the Well-Gone One teaches us to cut this off. He also says: 'This great monk cannot digest food.' However, we respect the World-Honored One's majestic and wonderful qualities and are unable to bear it. Therefore, we have stopped eating after noon.

尊者乌陀夷答曰：「世尊！我独在靖处燕坐思惟，心作是念：『世尊为我等多所饶益，善逝为我等多所安隐。世尊于我除众苦法，增益乐法。世尊于我除无量恶不善之法，增益无量诸善妙法。』世尊昔时告诸比丘：『汝等断过中食。』世尊！我等闻已，不堪不忍，不欲不乐。若有信梵志、居士往至众园，广施作福，我等自手受食，而世尊今教我断是，善逝教我绝是。复作是说：『此大沙门不能消食。』然我等于世尊威神妙德敬重不堪，是故我等断中后食。

Furthermore, the World-Honored One once told the monks: 'You should stop eating at night.' World-Honored One! When we heard this, we were unable to bear it, unwilling, and displeased. The best, most wonderful, most excellent, and most delicious of the two meals is now taught by the World-Honored One to be stopped, the Well-Gone One teaches us to cut it off. He also says: 'This great monk cannot digest food.' World-Honored One! Once there was a householder who brought back many kinds of pure and wonderful food and drink to his home and instructed his family members: 'You should receive this and put it in one place. We will all gather together to eat at night, not during the day.' World-Honored One! If there are families that provide the most wonderful and supreme food, it is only for the night meal. I am for the daytime meal. But now the World-Honored One teaches me to stop this, the Well-Gone One teaches me to cut it off. He also says: 'This great monk cannot digest food.' World-Honored One! We respect the World-Honored One's majestic and wonderful qualities and are unable to bear it. Therefore, we have stopped eating at night.

「复次，昔时世尊告诸比丘：『汝等断夜食。』世尊！我等闻已，不堪不忍，不欲不乐，于二食中最上、最妙、最胜、最美者，而世尊今教我断是，善逝教我绝是，复作是说：『此大沙门不能消食。』世尊！昔时有一居士，多持种种净妙食饮，还归其家，勅内人曰：『汝等受此举著一处，我当尽共集会夜食，不为朝中。』世尊！若于诸家施设极妙最上食者，唯有夜食，我为朝中，而世尊今教我断是，善逝教我绝是，复作是说：『此大沙门不能消食。』然我等于世尊威神妙德敬重不堪，是故我等断于夜食。

"World-Honored One! I further thought: 'If a monk enters a village at an improper time to beg for food, he may encounter thieves, or he may not encounter them. He may encounter tigers, deer, or both tigers and deer. He may encounter leopards, bears, or both leopards and bears. He may go to such places and encounter vicious elephants, horses, cows, or dogs. He may encounter a snake nest, or be hit by a clod, or beaten with a stick, or fall into a ditch, or fall into a latrine, or ride a sleeping bull, or fall into a deep pit, or enter thorns. He may see an empty house and enter such a house. If he enters, a woman may see him and call him to engage in evil and impure conduct.'"

「世尊！我复作是念：『若有比丘非时入村而行乞食，或能逢贼，作业不作业，或逢虎逢鹿，或逢虎鹿，或逢豹逢罴，或逢豹罴，或往如是处，或逢恶象、恶马、恶牛、恶狗，或值蛇聚，或得块掷，或得杖打，或堕沟渎，或堕厕中，或乘卧牛，或堕深坑，或入刺中，观见空家，入如是家，若彼入已，女人见之，或呼共行恶不净行。』

"World-Honored One! In the past, a monk walked into someone's house at an improper time to beg for food in the dark rain, with lightning flashing. The woman of the house was outside washing dishes at the time. In the flash of lightning, she saw the monk from afar and mistook him for a ghost. She was terrified, her hair stood on end, and she cried out in a loud voice. She then became pregnant and said: 'You are a ghost! You are a ghost!' The monk said to the woman: 'Sister! I am not a ghost, I am a monk, I have come to beg for food.' At that time, the woman scolded the monk bitterly and said: 'May this monk's life be cut short, may his parents die early, may his family line be cut off, may his stomach burst open, may this shaven-headed monk wrap himself in black, childless and cut off from offspring, may you rather use a sharp knife to cut open your own stomach, you should not walk around begging for food at an improper time at night, because of this monk, I have become pregnant.' World-Honored One! I remember this and feel joyful. World-Honored One! Because of this joy, it fills my body. With right mindfulness and right knowledge, I give rise to joy, tranquility, happiness, and concentration. World-Honored One! Because of this concentration, it fills my body. With right mindfulness and right knowledge, it is like this, World-Honored One! I have nothing lacking, I am at ease and happy, my strength is as usual."

「世尊！昔一比丘夜暗微雨，睒睒掣电，而非时行入他家乞食。彼家妇人尔时出外洗荡食器，彼时妇人于电光中遥见比丘，谓为是鬼，见已惊怖，身毛皆竖，失声大呼，即便堕娠，而作是语：『尊是鬼！尊是鬼！』时，彼比丘语妇人曰：『妹！我非鬼，我是沙门，今来乞食。』尔时，妇人恚骂比丘至苦至恶，而作是语：『令此沙门命根早断，令此沙门父母早死，令此沙门种族绝灭，令此沙门腹裂破坏，秃头沙门以黑自缠，无子断种，汝宁可持利刀自破其腹，不应非时夜行乞食，坐此沙门而堕我娠。』世尊！我忆彼已，便生欢悦。世尊！我因此欢悦遍充满体，正念正智，生喜、止、乐、定。世尊！我因此定遍充满体，正念正智。如是，世尊！我无所乏，安隐快乐，气力如常。」

Udāyin! It is like a poor person who has no money or wealth, and no power. He has a wife whose eyes are blind, ugly and unlovable, with only one house that is dilapidated and leaky, with birds nesting in it, unfit to live in. This is said to be transcended here. Udāyin! I say this is also not to be made non-existent, not to be abandoned, not to be transcended. Udāyin! Is there any one or many fetters that abide for a long time, which I say are not to be made non-existent, not to be abandoned, not to be transcended, that is, which I say are not to be cut off?"

「此中何等过度？乌陀夷！比丘度一切色想，灭有对想，不念若干想，无量空，是无量空处成就游，是谓此中过度。乌陀夷！我说此亦不得无、不得断、不得过度。此中何等过度？乌陀夷！比丘度一切无量空处，无量识，是无量识处成就游，是谓此中过度。乌陀夷！我说此亦不得无，不得断，不得过度。此中何等过度？乌陀夷！比丘度一切无量识处，无所有，是无所有处成就游，是谓此中过度。乌陀夷！我说此亦不得无，不得断，不得过度，此中何等过度。乌陀夷！比丘度一切无所有处，非有想非无想，是非有想非无想处成就游，是谓此中过度。乌陀夷！我说至非有想非无想处亦不得无，不得断，不得过度。乌陀夷！颇有一结，或多或少久住者，我说不得无，不得断，不得过度，谓我说不断耶？」

Venerable Udāyin replied, "No, World-Honored One!"

尊者乌陀夷白曰：「不也。世尊！」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Udāyin! You are not like those foolish people. Those foolish people, when I tell them to abandon something, they say, 'This is a small matter, why should we abandon it? Now the World-Honored One tells us to abandon it, the Well-Gone One tells us to cut it off.' They also say, 'This great śramaṇa cannot digest food, he does not abandon it. He only gives rise to dislike and intolerance towards us, and also towards other bhikṣus who uphold the precepts well.' Udāyin! Therefore, the bonds of those foolish people are extremely firm and tight, increasing and intensifying, unbreakable, and unable to be liberated. Udāyin! If there are sons of noble families whom I tell to abandon something, they do not say, 'This is a small matter, why should we abandon it? Now the World-Honored One tells us to abandon it, the Well-Gone One tells us to cut it off.' They also do not say, 'This great śramaṇa cannot digest food, he abandons it. He does not give rise to dislike and intolerance towards us, and also towards other bhikṣus who uphold the precepts well.' Udāyin! Therefore, the bonds of those sons of noble families are not firm or tight, not increasing or intensifying, and can be broken, and they can be liberated."

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！乌陀夷！汝不尔如彼痴人，彼愚痴人，我为其说：『汝等断此。』彼作是说：『此是小事，何足断之？而世尊今教我断此，善逝令我绝此。』亦如是说：『此大沙门不能消食，彼不断此，彼但于我生不可、不忍，及余比丘善护持戒者，亦复为彼生不可、不忍。』乌陀夷！是故彼痴人所缚极坚极牢，转增转急，不可断绝，不得解脱。乌陀夷！若有族姓子我为其说：『汝等断此。』彼不作是说：『此是小事，何足断之？而今世尊教我断此，善逝令我绝此。』亦不如是说：『此大沙门不能消食，彼便断此，彼不于我生不可、不忍，及余比丘善护持戒者，亦不为彼生不可、不忍。』乌陀夷！是故彼族姓子所缚不坚不牢，不转增急，而可断绝，则得解脱。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Venerable Udāyin, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。尊者乌陀夷闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
