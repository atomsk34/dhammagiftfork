MĀ 193

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 16 大品

193. （一九三）牟犁破群那经

Parallel to: MN 21 / EĀ 50.8 (~)

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, Maudgalyāyana often gathered with the nuns. If someone told Maudgalyāyana about the nuns, he would become angry and jealous, and so on, up to quarreling. If someone told the nuns about Maudgalyāyana, the nuns would become angry and jealous, and so on, up to quarreling.

尔时，牟犁破群那与比丘尼数共集会。若有人向牟犁破群那比丘道说比丘尼者，彼闻已，便瞋恚憎嫉，乃至鬪诤。若有人向诸比丘尼道说牟犁破群那比丘者，诸比丘尼闻已，便瞋恚憎嫉，乃至鬪诤。

When the many bhikṣus heard this, they went to the Buddha, bowed at his feet, sat to one side, and said, "World-Honored One! Maudgalyāyana often gathers with the nuns. If someone tells Maudgalyāyana about the nuns, he becomes angry and jealous, and so on, up to quarreling. If someone tells the nuns about Maudgalyāyana, the nuns become angry and jealous, and so on, up to quarreling."

众多比丘闻已，便往诣佛，稽首佛足，却坐一面，白曰：「世尊！牟犁破群那比丘与比丘尼数共集会。若有人向牟犁破群那比丘道说比丘尼者，彼闻已，便瞋恚憎嫉，乃至鬪诤。若有人向诸比丘尼道说牟犁破群那比丘者，诸比丘尼闻已，便瞋恚憎嫉，乃至鬪诤。」

The World-Honored One, having heard this, told a bhikṣu, "Go to Maudgalyāyana and tell him, 'The World-Honored One summons you.'"

世尊闻已，告一比丘：「汝往牟犁破群那比丘所，而语之曰：『世尊呼汝。』」

The bhikṣu, having heard this, said, "Yes, World-Honored One!" He then rose from his seat, bowed at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and departed. He went to Maudgalyāyana and said, "The World-Honored One summons you!" Maudgalyāyana, having heard this, came to where the Buddha was, made obeisance to the Buddha, and sat to one side.

一比丘闻已：「唯然世尊！」即从坐起，稽首佛足，绕三匝而去。至牟犁破群那比丘所，而语之曰：「世尊呼汝！」牟犁破群那闻已，来诣佛所，为佛作礼，却坐一面。

The World-Honored One said, "Maudgalyāyana! It is true that you often gather with bhikṣuṇīs. If someone tells you about bhikṣuṇīs, you become angry and jealous, and even quarrel. If someone tells the bhikṣuṇīs about you, the bhikṣuṇīs become angry and jealous, and even quarrel. Maudgalyāyana! Is this true?"

世尊告曰：「破群那！汝实与比丘尼数共集会。若有人向汝道说比丘尼者，汝闻已，便嗔恚憎嫉，乃至鬪诤。若有人向诸比丘尼道说汝者，诸比丘尼闻已，便嗔恚憎嫉，乃至鬪诤。破群那！汝实如是耶？」

Maudgalyāyana replied, "It is true, World-Honored One!"

破群那答曰：「实尔。世尊！」

The World-Honored One further asked, "Maudgalyāyana! Have you not come to have faith, left home, become a homeless one, and studied the path?"

世尊复问曰：「破群那！汝非至信、舍家、无家、学道耶？」

Maudgalyāyana replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

破群那答曰：「唯然。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "Maudgalyāyana! Therefore, if you have come to have faith, left home, become a homeless one, and studied the path, you should study thus. If there is desire and thought based on the household, cut it off. If there is desire and thought based on desirelessness, cultivate and practice it, and spread it widely. Maudgalyāyana! You should study thus."

世尊告曰：「破群那！是以汝至信、舍家、无家、学道者，应当学。若有欲有念依家，断是。若有欲有念依于无欲，是习是修，是广布也。破群那！汝当如是学。」

At that time, the World-Honored One asked the bhikṣus, "Did you not come to have faith, leave home, become homeless, and study the path?"

尔时，世尊问诸比丘曰：「汝等非至信、舍家、无家、学道耶？」

The bhikṣus replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

诸比丘答曰：「唯然。世尊！」

The World-Honored One again said to the bhikṣus, "Therefore, those who have come to have faith, leave home, become homeless, and study the path should learn thus. If there is desire and thought attached to the household, cut it off. If there is desire and thought attached to the desireless, this is practice, this is cultivation, this is spreading. You should learn thus. I once told the bhikṣus: 'Bhikṣus! If there are bhikṣus with many acquaintances and bhikṣus with few acquaintances, they should all learn to eat in one sitting. Having learned to eat in one sitting, they will be without doing and seeking, without illness or pain, with light bodies, strong energy, and peaceful happiness.' Those bhikṣus with many acquaintances and few acquaintances all learned to eat in one sitting. Having learned to eat in one sitting, they were without doing and seeking, without illness or pain, with light bodies, strong energy, and peaceful happiness. Those bhikṣus were pleasing to my mind, and I did not teach and rebuke them much. The bhikṣus gave rise to thoughts and followed the Dharma in succession, like a horse-drawn carriage, with the driver holding the reins in his left hand and the whip in his right hand, traveling along the eightfold path, going wherever he wishes. Thus, those bhikṣus were pleasing to my mind, and I did not teach and rebuke them much. The bhikṣus gave rise to thoughts and followed the Dharma in succession, like a good field with a grove of śāla trees. The grove manager was intelligent, wise, and not lazy. He managed the roots of the śāla trees according to the seasons, frequently weeding and watering them, digging down the high ones and filling up the low ones. If bad weeds grew on the sides, he pulled them out and discarded them. If the branches grew crooked and bad, he pulled them out and put them outside. If new straight and good branches grew near the sides, he managed them according to the seasons, frequently weeding and watering them. Thus, that good field of śāla trees became more and more luxuriant. Thus, those bhikṣus were pleasing to my mind, and I did not teach and rebuke them much. I did not praise them for their good words and obedience, which were due to robes, alms, bedding, medicine, and other necessities of life. Why is that? Those bhikṣus, if they did not obtain these, would not speak good words and be obedient, and would not accomplish the Dharma of good words and obedience.

世尊复告诸比丘曰：「是以汝等至信、舍家、无家、学道者，应当学。若有欲有念依家，断是。若有欲有念依于无欲，是习是修，是广布也，汝等当如是学。昔时我曾告诸比丘：『汝等！若有比丘多所知识，若有比丘少所知识，彼一切尽学一坐食，学一坐食已，无为无求，无有病痛，身体轻便，气力康强，安隐快乐。』彼诸比丘多所知识及少知识，尽学一坐食，学一坐食已，无为无求，无有病痛，身体轻便，气力康强，安隐快乐。彼诸比丘可于我心，我亦不多教诃。诸比丘因此生念向法次法，犹如马车，御者乘之，左手执辔，右手执策，随八道行，在意所至。如是诸比丘可于我心，我亦不多教诃。诸比丘因此生念向法次法，犹如良地有娑罗树林，彼治林者，聪明黠慧而不懈怠，彼随时治娑罗树根，数数锄粪，以水溉灌，高者掘下，下者填满。若边生恶草，薅除弃之，若并生曲戾恶不直者，拔根著外，若枝生横曲，则落治之，若近边新生调直好者，便随时治，数数锄粪，以水溉灌，如是彼良地娑罗树林转转茂盛。如是诸比丘可于我心，我亦不多教诃。彼我不说彼善语恭顺，谓因衣钵、饮食、床榻、汤药、诸生活具故。所以者何？彼比丘若不得是还不善语恭顺，成就不善语恭顺法。

If there are bhikṣus who are far removed, who abide in far removal, who speak well and are obedient, and who accomplish the Dharma of speaking well and being obedient, I say they speak well and are obedient. Why is that? There may be one who practices well and protects well, that is, because others do not speak ill of him. If others do not speak ill of him, he will not be angry, nor will he be hateful, nor will he be bound by sorrow, nor will he hate or be angry, nor will he reveal evil. When the other bhikṣus see this, they will think: 'This virtuous one is patient, gentle, and able to endure, well-restrained, well-concentrated, and well-pacified.' If others do speak ill of him, he will be angry, hateful, bound by sorrow, hateful, and reveal evil. When the other bhikṣus see this, they will think: 'This virtuous one is of bad character, hasty, rough, and unrestrained, not concentrated, not restrained, and not pacified.' Why is that?

「若有比丘为远离、依远离、住远离，善语恭顺，成就善语恭顺法者，我说彼善语恭顺。所以者何？或有一善护善逝行者，谓因他无恶语言也。若他不恶语言者，便不瞋恚，亦不憎嫉，不忧缠住，不憎嗔恚，不发露恶。彼诸比丘见已，便作是念：『此贤者忍辱温和堪耐，善制善定善息。』若他恶语言者，便嗔恚憎嫉，而忧缠住憎恚发恶。彼诸比丘见已，便作是念：『此贤者恶性急弊麤犷，不定不制不息。』所以者何？

Bhikṣus! In the past, there was a householder's wife named Vedehikā, who was extremely wealthy and happy, with abundant money and property, livestock, and industries beyond measure, with fiefs, food, and abundant rice and grain, as well as various necessities of life. At that time, the householder's wife Vedehikā had such a great reputation that it spread to all directions. The householder's wife Vedehikā was patient, able to endure, gentle, well-restrained, well-concentrated, and well-pacified. At that time, the householder's wife Vedehikā had a maid named Kālā, who had served her before and had excellent speech, practicing much good. That maid Kālā thought: 'My mistress, the householder's wife Vedehikā, has such a great reputation that it spreads to all directions. The householder's wife Vedehikā is patient, able to endure, gentle, well-restrained, well-concentrated, and well-pacified. I should now test my mistress, the householder's wife Vedehikā, to see if she is truly angry or not angry.'

「比丘！昔时有居士妇，名鞞陀提，极大富乐，多有钱财，畜牧产业不可称计，封户、食邑、米谷丰饶，及若干种诸生活具。尔时，居士妇鞞陀提如是大有名称，流布诸方，居士妇鞞陀提忍辱堪耐温和，善制善定善息。尔时，居士妇鞞陀提有婢名黑，本侍者，有妙善言，少多行善。彼黑婢作是念：『我大家居士妇鞞陀提如是有大名称，流布诸方，居士妇鞞陀提忍辱堪耐温和，善制善定善息，我今宁可试大家居士妇鞞陀提，为实嗔、为实不嗔耶？』

Then the maid Kālā lay down and did not get up early. The mistress called out: 'Maid Kālā! Why don't you get up early?' Having heard this, the maid Kālā thought: 'My mistress, the householder's wife Vedehikā, is truly angry, not not angry. But because I am good at managing the household affairs, good at planning, and good at keeping things, it causes my mistress, the householder's wife Vedehikā, to have such a great reputation that it spreads to all directions. The householder's wife Vedehikā is patient, able to endure, gentle, well-restrained, well-concentrated, and well-pacified. I should now further test my mistress, the householder's wife Vedehikā, to see if she is truly angry or not angry.'

「于是，黑婢卧不早起。夫人呼曰：『黑婢！何不早起耶？』黑婢闻已，便作是念：『我大家居士妇鞞陀提实瞋，非不瞋也。但因我善能料理家业、善经营、善持故，令我大家居士妇鞞陀提如是有极大名称，流布诸方，居士妇鞞陀提忍辱堪耐温和，善制善定善息。我今宁可复更大试大家居士妇鞞陀提，为实嗔、为实不嗔耶？』

Then the maid Kālā lay down very late and did not get up. The mistress called out: 'Maid Kālā! Why do you get up so late?' Having heard this, the maid Kālā thought: 'My mistress, the householder's wife Vedehikā, is truly angry, not not angry. But because I am good at managing the household affairs, good at planning, and good at keeping things, it causes my mistress, the householder's wife Vedehikā, to have such a great reputation that it spreads to all directions. The householder's wife Vedehikā is patient, able to endure, gentle, well-restrained, well-concentrated, and well-pacified. I should now further test my mistress, the householder's wife Vedehikā, to see if she is truly angry or not angry.'

「于是，黑婢卧极晚不起。夫人呼曰：『黑婢！何以极晚不起耶？』黑婢闻已，作是念：『我大家居士妇鞞陀提实瞋，非不瞋也。但因我善能料理家业、善经营、善持故，令我大家居士妇鞞陀提如是有极大名称，流布诸方，居士妇鞞陀提忍辱堪耐温和，善制善定善息耳。我今宁可复更极大试大家居士妇鞞陀提，为实瞋、为实不瞋耶？』

"Then the maid Kālā lay down until dusk before getting up. The mistress called out: 'Maid Kālā! Why do you get up so late? You do not do anything yourself, nor do you have others do it. This maid Kālā does not follow my instructions, this maid Kālā disrespects me.' She became very angry and resentful, with three veins on her forehead standing out, frowning, she went herself, closed the door and bolted it, and beat her on the head with a large stick, causing her head to break and blood to flow. Then the maid Kālā, with her head broken and blood flowing, went out to tell the neighbors, making a lot of noise and saying: 'Sirs, do you see this person who practices patience, endurance, gentleness, well-restraint, well-concentration, and well-pacification? She scolded me, saying: "Maid Kālā! Why do you get up so late? You do not do anything yourself, nor do you have others do it. This maid Kālā does not follow my instructions, this maid Kālā disrespects me." She became very angry and resentful, with three veins on her forehead standing out, frowning, she came herself, closed the door and bolted it, and beat me on the head with a large stick, causing my head to break and blood to flow.'" At that time, the householder's wife Vedehikā became very notorious, spreading to all directions, that the householder's wife Vedehikā was evil-natured, hasty, rough, and unrestrained, not concentrated, not restrained, and not pacified.

「于是，黑婢卧至晡时乃起。夫人呼曰：『黑婢！何以乃至晡时起，既不自作，亦不教作，此黑婢不随我教，此黑婢轻慢于我。』便大瞋恚而生憎嫉，额三脉起，皱面自往，闭户下关，手执大杖以打其头，头破血流。于是，黑婢头破血流，便出语比隣，讼声纷纭，多所道说：『尊等！见是忍辱行人堪耐温和，善制善定善息行耶？骂我曰：「黑婢！何以乃至晡时起，既不自作，亦不教作，此黑婢不随我教，此黑婢轻慢于我。」便大瞋恚而生憎嫉，额三脉起，皱面自来，闭户下关，手执大杖以打我头，头破血流。』尔时，居士妇鞞陀提如是便有极大恶名，流布诸方，居士妇鞞陀提恶性急弊麤犷，不定不制不息。

Thus, there may be one who practices good protection and good passing away, which means due to others not speaking ill. If others do not speak ill, then one will not be angry, nor resentful, nor dwell in affliction, nor increase anger, nor reveal evil. When the monks see this, they will think: 'This virtuous one is patient, gentle, able to endure, well-restrained, well-concentrated, and well-pacified.' If others do speak ill, then one will be angry, resentful, and dwell in affliction, resentful and revealing evil. When the monks see this, they will think: 'This virtuous one is evil-natured, hasty, rough, unrestrained, not concentrated, not restrained, and not pacified.'

「如是。或有一善护善逝行者，谓因他无恶语言也。若他不恶语言者，便不瞋恚，亦不憎嫉，不忧缠住，不增瞋恚，不发露恶。彼诸比丘见已，便作是念：『此贤者忍辱温和堪耐，善制善定善息。』若他恶语言者，便瞋恚憎嫉而忧缠住，憎恚发恶。彼诸比丘见已，便作是念：『此贤者恶性急弊麤犷，不定不制不息。』

"Furthermore, there is mutual speech. When others speak, it may be at the right time or the wrong time, true or untrue, soft or harsh, compassionate or angry, meaningful or meaningless. If your minds change or you speak ill when others speak to you, I say that you will certainly decline because of this. You should learn this mutual speech. When others speak to you, if your minds do not change and you do not speak ill, then towards your enemy's family, you should arouse a mind of compassion and kindness. With a mind full of kindness, you should pervade one direction and dwell in accomplishment. Like this, the second, third, and fourth directions, the four intermediate directions, above and below, everywhere, with a mind full of kindness, without attachment, without enmity, without anger, without dispute, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, dwelling in accomplishment. Like this, with compassion and joy, with a mind full of equanimity, without attachment, without enmity, without anger, without dispute, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, dwelling in accomplishment. You should learn this. Suppose someone came with a large hoe and said: 'I can make this great earth not be earth.' They then dug here and there, spat and defiled it, spoke ill, and said: 'Make this great earth not be earth.' What do you think? Can that person make this great earth not be earth by this method?"

「复次，有互言道，若他说者，或时或非时，或真或非真，或软或坚，或慈或恚，或有义或无义。汝等！此互言道，若他说时，或心变易者，或口恶言者，我说汝等因此必衰，汝等当学此互言道。若他说时，心不变易，口无恶言，向怨家人缘彼起慈愍心，心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游。如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游，汝等当学如是。犹如有人持大铧锹来，而作是语：『我能令此大地，使作非地。』彼便处处掘复掘，唾溺污之，说恶语言，作如是说：『令大地非地。』于意云何？彼人以此方便，能令此] cf.［丽】大地作非地耶？」

The monks replied, "No, World-Honored One. Why is that? This great earth is very deep, extremely vast, and immeasurable. Therefore, that person cannot make this great earth not be earth by this method. World-Honored One, it only causes that person to tire themselves in vain."

诸比丘答曰：「不也。世尊！所以者何？此大地甚深极广而不可量，是故彼人以此方便，不能令此大地使作非地。世尊！但使彼人唐自疲劳也。」

"Like this, this mutual speech, when others speak, it may be at the right time or the wrong time, true or untrue, soft or harsh, compassionate or angry, meaningful or meaningless. If your minds change or you speak ill when others speak to you, I say that you will certainly decline because of this. You should learn this mutual speech. When others speak to you, if your minds do not change and you do not speak ill, then towards the speaker, you should arouse a mind of compassion and kindness. With a mind like the earth, without attachment, without enmity, without anger, without dispute, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, dwelling in accomplishment. Like this, suppose someone came with a large torch of grass and said: 'I will use this torch of grass to heat the water of the Ganges River and make it boil.' What do you think? Can that person make the water of the Ganges River hot and boiling by this method?"

「如是，此互言道，若他说者，或时或非时，或真或不真，或软或坚，或慈或恚，或有义或无义。汝等！此互言道，若他说时，或心变易者，或口恶言者，我说汝等因此必衰，汝等当学此互言道。若他说时，心不变易，口无恶言，向言说者，缘彼起慈愍心，心行如地，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游，汝等当学如是。犹如有人持大草炬，作如是语：『我以此草炬，用热恒伽水，令作沸汤。』于意云何？彼人以此方便，能令恒伽水热作沸汤耶？」

The monks replied, "No, World-Honored One. Why is that? World-Honored One, that water of the Ganges is very deep, extremely vast, and immeasurable. Therefore, that person cannot make the water of the Ganges hot and boiling by this method. World-Honored One, it only causes that person to tire themselves in vain."

诸比丘答曰：「不也。世尊！所以者何？世尊！彼恒伽水甚深极广，不可度量。是故彼人以此方便，不能令恒伽水热使作沸汤。世尊！但使彼人唐自疲劳也。」

"Like this, this mutual speech, when others speak, it may be at the right time or the wrong time, angry or not angry, soft or harsh, compassionate or angry, meaningful or meaningless. If your minds change or you speak ill when others speak to you, I say that you will certainly decline because of this. You should learn this mutual speech. When others speak to you, if your minds do not change and you do not speak ill, then towards the speaker, you should arouse a mind of compassion and kindness. With a mind like the water of the Ganges, without attachment, without enmity, without anger, without dispute, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, dwelling in accomplishment. Like this, suppose a painter or a painter's apprentice came with various paints and said: 'I will paint a form in this empty space and decorate it with colors.' What do you think? Can that painter or painter's apprentice paint a form in empty space and decorate it with colors by this method?"

「如是。此互言道，若他说者，或时或非时，或嗔或不瞋，或软或坚，或慈或恚，或有义或无义。汝等！此互言道，若他说时，或心变易者，或口恶言者，我说汝等因此必衰。汝等当学此互言道，若他说时，心不变易，口无恶言，向言说者，缘彼起慈愍心，心行如恒伽水，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游，汝等当学如是。犹如画师、画师弟子，持种种彩来，彼作是说：『我于此虚空画作形像，以彩庄染。』于意云何？彼画师、画师弟子以此方便，宁能于虚空画作形像，以彩庄染耶？」

The monks replied, "No, World-Honored One. Why is that? World-Honored One, this empty space is not form, invisible, and without obstruction. Therefore, that painter or painter's apprentice cannot paint a form in empty space and decorate it with colors by this method. World-Honored One, it only causes that painter or painter's apprentice to tire themselves in vain."

诸比丘答曰：「不也。世尊！所以者何？世尊！此虚空非色，不可见无对。是故彼画师、画师弟子以此方便，不能于虚空画作形像，以彩庄染。世尊！但使彼画师、画师弟子唐自疲劳也。」

"Like this, this mutual speech, when others speak, it may be at the right time or the wrong time, true or untrue, soft or harsh, compassionate or angry, meaningful or meaningless. If your minds change or you speak ill when others speak to you, I say that you will certainly decline because of this. You should learn this mutual speech. When others speak to you, if your minds do not change and you do not speak ill, then towards the speaker, you should arouse a mind of compassion and kindness. With a mind like empty space, without attachment, without enmity, without anger, without dispute, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, dwelling in accomplishment. Like this, suppose there is a cat's skin bag, soft and supple, without the sound of a cart or a pot, without the sound of a cart or a pot. Someone may punch it with a fist, throw a stone at it, hit it with a stick, cut it with a knife, or throw it on the ground. What do you think? Will that cat's skin bag, soft and supple, without the sound of a cart or a pot, without the sound of a cart or a pot, make the sound of a cart or a pot again?"

「如是，此互言道，若他说者，或时或非时，或真或不真，或软或坚，或慈或恚，或有义或无义。汝等！此互言道，若他说时，或心变易者，或口恶言者，我说汝等因此必衰。汝等当学此互言道，若他说时，心不变易，口无恶言，向言说者，缘彼起慈愍心，心行如虚空，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游，汝等当学如是。犹如猫皮囊柔治极软，除［车／瓦］［车／瓦］声，无［车／瓦］［车／瓦］声。彼或有人以手拳扠，石掷杖打，或以刀斫，或扑著地，于意云何？彼猫皮囊柔治极软，除［车／瓦］［车／瓦］声，无［车／瓦］［车／瓦］声，彼宁复有［车／瓦］［车／瓦］声耶？」

The monks replied, "No, World-Honored One. Why is that? World-Honored One, that cat's skin bag, soft and supple, without the sound of a cart or a pot, without the sound of a cart or a pot, therefore does not make the sound of a cart or a pot again."

诸比丘答曰：「不也。世尊！所以者何？世尊！彼猫皮囊柔治极软，除［车／瓦］［车／瓦］声，无［车／瓦］［车／瓦］声。是故无复有［车／瓦］［车／瓦］声。」

"Like this, monks, if others punch you with a fist, throw stones at you, hit you with a stick, cut you with a knife, when others punch you with a fist, throw stones at you, hit you with a stick, cut you with a knife, if your minds change or you speak ill, I say that you will certainly decline because of this. You should learn, when others punch you with a fist, throw stones at you, hit you with a stick, cut you with a knife, if your minds do not change and you do not speak ill, then towards the one who strikes you, you should arouse a mind of compassion and kindness. With a mind like a cat's skin bag, without attachment, without enmity, without anger, without dispute, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, dwelling in accomplishment. If there are thieves who come and cut you limb from limb with sharp saws, when there are thieves who come and cut you limb from limb with sharp saws, if your minds change or you speak ill, I say that you will certainly decline because of this. You should learn, when there are thieves who come and cut you limb from limb with sharp saws, if your mind does not change and you do not speak ill towards the one who cuts you, you should arouse a mind of compassion and kindness. With a mind accompanied by loving-kindness, you should dwell pervading one direction, accomplishing and dwelling. Like this, the second, third, and fourth directions, the four intermediate directions, above and below, all around, everywhere. With a mind accompanied by loving-kindness, without attachment, without enmity, without anger, without dispute, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, dwelling in accomplishment. Like this, compassion and joy, with a mind accompanied by equanimity, without attachment, without enmity, without anger, without dispute, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, dwelling in accomplishment. You should learn like this."

「如是。诸比丘！若有他人拳扠石掷，杖打刀斫，汝等若为他人拳扠石掷，杖打刀斫时，或心变易者，或口恶言者，我说汝等因此必衰。汝等当学，若为他人拳扠石掷，杖打刀斫时，心不变易，口不恶言，向捶打人缘彼起慈愍心，心行如猫皮囊，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游，汝等当学如是。若有贼来，以利锯刀节节解截，汝等若有贼来，以利锯刀节节解截时，或心变易者，或口恶言者，我说汝等因此必衰。汝等当学，若有贼来，以利锯刀，节节解截，心不变易，口无恶言向割截人，缘彼起慈愍心，心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游。如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游，汝等当学如是。」

Then the World-Honored One praised the monks, saying: "Excellent, excellent! You should often contemplate the teaching of the monk's way of the sharp saw. Having often contemplated the teaching of the monk's way of the sharp saw, have you ever seen anyone speak unpleasant words to me, and upon hearing them, I was unable to bear it?"

于是，世尊叹诸比丘曰：「善哉！善哉！汝等当数数念利锯刀喻沙门教，汝等数数念利锯刀喻沙门教已，汝等颇见他不爱恶语言向我，我闻已，不堪耐耶？」

The monks replied: "No, World-Honored One!"

诸比丘答曰：「不也。世尊！」

The World-Honored One again praised the monks, saying: "Excellent, excellent! You should often contemplate the teaching of the monk's way of the sharp saw. Having often contemplated the teaching of the monk's way of the sharp saw, if you travel to the east, you will surely find peace and be free from suffering. If you travel to the south, west, or north, you will surely find peace and be free from suffering. Excellent, excellent! You should often contemplate the teaching of the monk's way of the sharp saw. Having often contemplated the teaching of the monk's way of the sharp saw, I do not even say that your good qualities will remain, let alone decline. You should only increase good qualities day and night without decline. Excellent, excellent! You should often contemplate the teaching of the monk's way of the sharp saw. Having often contemplated the teaching of the monk's way of the sharp saw, you will surely attain one of the two fruits, either attaining ultimate knowledge in this life or attaining the state of a non-returner.

世尊复叹诸比丘曰：「善哉！善哉！汝等当数数念利锯刀喻沙门教，汝等数数念利锯刀喻沙门教已，若汝游东方，必得安乐，无众苦患；若游南方、西方、北方者，必得安乐，无众苦患。善哉！善哉！汝等当数数念利锯刀喻沙门教，汝等数数念利锯刀喻沙门教已，我尚不说汝诸善法住，况说衰退？但当昼夜增长善法而不衰退。善哉！善哉！汝等当数数念利锯刀喻沙门教，汝等数数念利锯刀喻沙门教已，于二果中必得其一，或于现世得究竟智，或复有余得阿那含。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The monks, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
