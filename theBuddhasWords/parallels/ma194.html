MĀ 194

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 16 大品

194. （一九四）跋陀和利经

Parallel to: MN 65 / EĀ 49.7 (~)

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying in the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma, together with a great assembly of bhikṣus, and was spending the summer retreat.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园，与大比丘众俱而受夏坐。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "I eat in one sitting. After eating in one sitting, I am free from activity and seeking, without illness or pain, with a light body, strong energy, and peaceful happiness. You should also learn to eat in one sitting. After eating in one sitting, you will be free from activity and seeking, without illness or pain, with a light body, strong energy, and peaceful happiness."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「我一坐食，一坐食已，无为无求，无有病痛，身体轻便，气力康强，安隐快乐。汝等亦当学一坐食，一坐食已，无为无求，无有病痛，身体轻便，气力康强，安隐快乐。」

At that time, Venerable Bhaddāli was also in the assembly. Then Venerable Bhaddāli rose from his seat, arranged his robe over one shoulder, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "World-Honored One! I am not able to eat in one sitting. Why is that? If I eat in one sitting, I will not be able to finish, and will regret it with a distressed mind. World-Honored One! Therefore, I am not able to eat in one sitting."

尔时，尊者跋陀和利亦在众中。于是，尊者跋陀和利即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！我不堪任于一坐食。所以者何？若我一坐食者，同不了事，懊恼心悔。世尊！是故我不堪任一坐食也。」

The World-Honored One said, "Bhaddāli! If I am invited, you may also follow me. I allow you to accept the invitation and take it away to eat in one sitting. Bhaddāli! If it is like this, you will be able to live well."

世尊告曰：「跋陀和利！若我受请，汝亦随我，听汝请食，持去一坐食。跋陀和利！若如是者，快得生活。」

Venerable Bhaddāli again said, "World-Honored One! It is like this, I am also not able to eat in one sitting. Why is that? If I eat in one sitting, I will not be able to finish, and will regret it with a distressed mind. World-Honored One! Therefore, I am not able to eat in one sitting."

尊者跋陀和利又复白曰：「世尊！如是，我亦不堪于一坐食。所以者何？若我一坐食者，同不了事，懊恼心悔。世尊！是故我不堪任一坐食也。」

The World-Honored One again and again told the monks, "I eat in one sitting. After eating in one sitting, I am free from doing and seeking, have no illness or pain, have a light body, strong energy, and am peaceful and happy. You should also learn to eat in one sitting. After eating in one sitting, you will be free from doing and seeking, have no illness or pain, have a light body, strong energy, and be peaceful and happy."

世尊复至再三告诸比丘：「我一坐食，一坐食已，无为无求，无有病痛，身体轻便，气力康强，安隐快乐。汝等亦当学一坐食，一坐食已，无为无求，无有病痛，身体轻便，气力康强，安隐快乐。」

Venerable Bhaddāli also rose from his seat again and again, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "World-Honored One! I am not able to eat in one sitting. Why is that? If I eat in one sitting, I will not be able to finish, and will regret it with a distressed mind. World-Honored One! Therefore, I am not able to eat in one sitting."

尊者跋陀和利亦至再三从坐而起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！我不堪任于一坐食。所以者何？若我一坐食者，同不了事，懊恼心悔。世尊！是故我不堪任一坐食也。」

The World-Honored One again and again told him, "Bhaddāli! If I am invited, you can also follow me. I allow you to accept the invitation and take it away to eat in one sitting. Bhaddāli! If it is like this, you will be able to live well."

世尊复至再三告曰：「跋陀和利！若我受请，汝亦随我，听汝请食，持去一坐食。跋陀和利！若如是者，快得生活。」

Venerable Bhaddāli again and again said, "World-Honored One! It is like this, I am also not able to eat in one sitting. Why is that? If I eat in one sitting, I will not be able to finish, and will regret it with a distressed mind. World-Honored One! Therefore, I am not able to eat in one sitting."

尊者跋陀和利复至再三白曰：「世尊！如是我亦] cf.［丽】不堪于一坐食。所以者何？若我一坐食者，同不了事，懊恼心悔。世尊！是故我不堪任一坐食也。」

At that time, the World-Honored One established the precept of eating in one sitting for the assembly of bhikṣus. The assembly of bhikṣus all upheld the precepts and the subtle Dharma of the World-Honored One's realm. Only Venerable Bhaddāli said he was not able to, and got up from his seat and left. Why was that? Because he did not learn the complete precepts and the subtle Dharma of the World-Honored One's realm. Thus, Venerable Bhaddāli hid for the summer and did not see the World-Honored One. Why was that? Because he did not learn the complete precepts and the subtle Dharma of the World-Honored One's realm.

尔时，世尊为比丘众施设一坐食戒，诸比丘众皆奉学戒及世尊境界诸微妙法，唯尊者跋陀和利说不堪任，从坐起去。所以者何？不学具戒及世尊境界诸微妙法故。于是，尊者跋陀和利遂藏一夏，不见世尊。所以者何？以不学具戒及世尊境界诸微妙法故。

At that time, the bhikṣus were making robes for the Buddha. The World-Honored One had completed the summer retreat in Śrāvastī, and after three months, had finished mending his robes. He put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and was about to travel among the people. Venerable Bhaddāli heard that the bhikṣus were making robes for the Buddha, and that the World-Honored One had completed the summer retreat in Śrāvastī, and after three months, had finished mending his robes. He put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and was about to travel among the people. Having heard this, Venerable Bhaddāli went to where the bhikṣus were. The bhikṣus saw Venerable Bhaddāli coming from afar, and said, "Venerable Bhaddāli! You should know that this is being made into a robe for the Buddha. The World-Honored One has completed the summer retreat in Śrāvastī, and after three months, has finished mending his robes. He has put on his robe, taken his alms bowl, and is about to travel among the people. Bhaddāli, you should take good care of it there, so as not to cause much trouble later.

时，诸比丘为佛作衣，世尊于舍卫国受夏坐讫，过三月已，补治衣竟，摄衣持钵，当游人间。尊者跋陀和利闻诸比丘为佛作衣，世尊于舍卫国受夏坐讫，过三月已，补治衣竟，摄衣持钵，当游人间。尊者跋陀和利闻已，往诣诸比丘所，诸比丘遥见尊者跋陀和利来，便作是语：「贤者跋陀和利！汝当知此为佛作衣，世尊于舍卫国受夏坐讫，过三月已，补治衣竟，摄衣持钵，当游人间。跋陀和利，汝] cf.［丽】当彼处善自守护，莫令后时致多烦劳。」

Venerable Bhaddāli, having heard these words, went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and said, "World-Honored One! I truly have faults, I truly have faults. I am foolish, ignorant, unclear, and unskillful. Why is that? The World-Honored One has established the precept of eating in one sitting for the assembly of bhikṣus. The assembly of bhikṣus all uphold the precepts and the subtle Dharma of the World-Honored One's realm. Only I say I am not able to, and get up from my seat and leave. Why is that? Because I do not learn the complete precepts and the subtle Dharma of the World-Honored One's realm.

尊者跋陀和利闻此语已，即诣佛所，稽首佛足，白曰：「世尊！我实有过，我实有过，如愚如痴，如不了，如不善。所以者何？世尊为比丘众施设一坐食戒，诸比丘众皆奉学戒及世尊境界诸微妙法，唯我说不堪任，从坐起去。所以者何？以不学具戒及世尊境界诸微妙法故。」

The World-Honored One said, "Bhaddāli! At that time, you did not know that many bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs were spending the summer retreat in Śrāvastī. They knew me and saw me. There was a bhikṣu named Bhaddāli, a disciple of the World-Honored One, who did not learn the complete precepts and the subtle Dharma of the World-Honored One's realm. Bhaddāli! At that time, did you not know this? Bhaddāli! At that time, you did not know that many upāsakas and upāsikās were living in Śrāvastī. They knew me and saw me. There was a bhikṣu named Bhaddāli, a disciple of the World-Honored One, who did not learn the complete precepts and the subtle Dharma of the World-Honored One's realm. Bhaddāli! At that time, did you not know this? Bhaddāli! At that time, you did not know that many non-Buddhist śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas were spending the summer retreat in Śrāvastī. They knew me and saw me. There was a bhikṣu named Bhaddāli, a disciple of the Śramaṇa Gautama, who was renowned and virtuous, but did not learn the complete precepts and the subtle Dharma of the World-Honored One's realm. Bhaddāli! At that time, did you not know this? Bhaddāli! If there were bhikṣus who were both liberated, I would tell them, 'Come and enter the mud.' Bhaddāli! What do you think? Would I instruct those bhikṣus, and would those bhikṣus be able to stay and avoid it?"

世尊告曰：「跋陀和利！汝于尔时不知众多比丘、比丘尼于舍卫国而受夏坐，彼知我、见我，有比丘名跋陀和利，世尊弟子，不学具戒及世尊境界诸微妙法。跋陀和利！汝于尔时不知如此耶？跋陀和利！汝于尔时不知众多优婆塞、优婆夷居舍卫国，彼知我、见我，有比丘名跋陀和利，世尊弟子，不学具戒及世尊境界诸微妙法。跋陀和利！汝于尔时不知如此耶？跋陀和利！汝于尔时不知众多异学沙门、梵志于舍卫国而受夏坐，彼知我、见我，有比丘名跋陀和利，沙门瞿昙弟子，名德，不学具戒及世尊境界诸微妙法。跋陀和利！汝于尔时不知如此耶？跋陀和利！若有比丘俱解脱者，我语彼曰：『汝来入泥。』跋陀和利！于意云何？我教彼比丘，彼比丘宁当可住而移避耶？」

Venerable Bhaddāli replied, "No, World-Honored One."

尊者跋陀和利答曰：「不也。」

The World-Honored One said, "Bhaddāli! If there were bhikṣus who were not both liberated, but had wisdom liberation, if they were not wisdom liberated, but had body realization, if they were not body realized, but had insight attainment, if they were not insight attained, but had faith liberation, if they were not faith liberated, but had Dharma practice, if they were not Dharma practitioners, but had faith practice, I would tell them, 'Come and enter the mud.' Bhaddāli! What do you think? Would I instruct those bhikṣus, and would those bhikṣus be able to stay and avoid it?"

世尊告曰：「跋陀和利！若有比丘，设非俱解脱有慧解脱，设非慧解脱有身证者，设非身证有见到者，设非见到有信解脱，设非信解脱有法行者，设非法行有信行者，我语彼曰：『汝来入泥。』跋陀和利！于意云何？我教彼比丘，彼比丘宁当可住而移避耶？」

Venerable Bhaddāli replied, "No, World-Honored One."

尊者跋陀和利答曰：「不也。」

The World-Honored One said, "Bhaddāli! What do you think? At that time, did you attain faith practice, Dharma practice, faith liberation, insight attainment, body realization, wisdom liberation, or both liberation?"

世尊告曰：「跋陀和利！于意云何？汝于尔时得信行、法行、信解脱、见到、身证、慧解脱、俱解脱耶？」

Venerable Bhaddāli replied, "No, World-Honored One."

尊者跋陀和利答曰：「不也。」

The World-Honored One said, "Bhaddāli! At that time, were you not like an empty house?"

世尊告曰：「跋陀和利！汝于尔时非如空屋耶？」

Then, Venerable Bhaddāli, having been rebuked by the World-Honored One face to face, was inwardly distressed, lowered his head in silence, lost his eloquence, and was as if waiting for something.

于是，尊者跋陀和利为世尊面呵责已，内怀忧戚，低头默然，失辩无言，如有所伺。

Then, the World-Honored One, having rebuked Venerable Bhaddāli face to face, further wished to delight him and said, "Bhaddāli! At that time, you had no faith in my teachings, no love for my teachings, and no tranquility in my teachings. Why is that? I have established the precept of eating one meal a day for the assembly of monks, and all the monks practice the precepts and the subtle teachings of the World-Honored One's realm. Only you say you are unable to do so and leave your seat. Why is that? Because you do not practice the complete precepts and the subtle teachings of the World-Honored One's realm."

于是，世尊面呵责尊者跋陀和利已，复欲令欢喜，而告之曰：「跋陀和利！汝当尔时，于我无信法靖，无爱法靖，无靖法靖。所以者何？我为比丘众施设一坐食戒，诸比丘众皆奉学戒及世尊境界诸微妙法，唯汝说不堪任，从坐起去。所以者何？以不学具戒及世尊境界诸微妙法故。」

Venerable Bhaddāli said, "Indeed it is so. Why is that? The World-Honored One has established the precept of eating one meal a day for the assembly of monks, and all the monks practice the precepts and the subtle teachings of the World-Honored One's realm. Only I say I am unable to do so and leave my seat. Why is that? Because I do not practice the complete precepts and the subtle teachings of the World-Honored One's realm. May the World-Honored One accept my faults. Having seen my faults, I will repent myself and from now on guard against them and not do them again."

尊者跋陀和利白曰：「实尔。所以者何？世尊为比丘众施设一坐食戒，诸比丘众皆奉学戒及世尊境界诸微妙法，唯我说不堪任，从坐起去。所以者何？以不学具戒及世尊境界诸微妙法故。唯愿世尊受我过失，我见过已，当自悔过，从今护之，不复更作。」

The World-Honored One said, "Bhaddāli! It is so. You are indeed foolish, ignorant, unclear, and unskillful. Why is that? I have established the precept of eating one meal a day for the assembly of monks, and all the monks practice the precepts and the subtle teachings of the World-Honored One's realm. Only you say you are unable to do so and leave your seat. Why is that? Because you do not practice the complete precepts and the subtle teachings of the World-Honored One's realm. Bhaddāli! If you have faults, see them and repent yourself. From now on guard against them and do not do them again. Bhaddāli! It is like this. It is beneficial and not harmful in the noble Dharma and Vinaya. If you have faults, see them and repent yourself. From now on guard against them and do not do them again. Bhaddāli! What do you think? If there is a monk who does not practice the complete precepts, he dwells in a place free from affairs, in a mountain forest under a tree, or in a high cave with no sound, far away, without evil, and without people. He follows the practice of sitting in meditation. Dwelling in a place far away, he practices diligently and attains the supreme mind, dwelling happily in the present life. Dwelling in a place far away, he practices diligently, peacefully and happily. He falsely slanders the World-Honored One's precepts, and also falsely slanders the wise and pure Brahmins of the heavens, and also falsely slanders his own precepts. Having falsely slandered the World-Honored One's precepts, and also falsely slanders the wise and pure Brahmins of the heavens, and also falsely slanders his own precepts, he does not give rise to joy. Not giving rise to joy, he does not give rise to delight. Not giving rise to delight, he does not stop the body. Not stopping the body, he does not experience bliss. Not experiencing bliss, his mind is not concentrated. Bhaddāli! When the noble disciples' minds are not concentrated, they do not see things as they really are and know them as they truly are.

世尊告曰：「跋陀和利！如是，汝实如愚如痴，如不了、如不善。所以者何？我为比丘众施设一坐食戒，诸比丘众皆奉学戒及世尊境界诸微妙法，唯汝说不堪任，从坐起去。所以者何？以汝不学具戒及世尊境界诸微妙法故。跋陀和利！若汝有过，见已自悔，从今护之，不更作者。跋陀和利！如是则于圣法、律中益而不损。若汝有过，见已自悔，从今护之，不更作者，跋陀和利！于意云何？若有比丘不学具戒者，彼住无事处山林树下，或居高岩寂无音声，远离，无恶，无有人民，随顺燕坐，彼住远离处，修行精勤，得增上心，现法乐居，彼住远离处，修行精勤，安隐快乐，以诬谤世尊戒，及诬谤天诸智梵行者，亦诬谤自戒。彼诬谤世尊戒，及诬谤天诸智梵行者，亦诬谤自戒已，便不生欢悦，不生欢悦已，便不生喜，不生喜已，便不止身，不止身已，便不觉乐，不觉乐已，便心不定。跋陀和利！贤圣弟子心不定已，便不见如实、知如真。

Bhaddāli! What do you think? If there is a monk who practices the complete precepts, he dwells in a place free from affairs, in a mountain forest under a tree, or in a high cave with no sound, far away, without evil, and without people. He follows the practice of sitting in meditation. Dwelling in a place far away, he practices diligently and attains the supreme mind, dwelling happily in the present life. Dwelling in a place far away, he practices diligently, peacefully and happily. He does not falsely slander the World-Honored One's precepts, and also does not falsely slander the wise and pure Brahmins of the heavens, and also does not falsely slander his own precepts. Having not falsely slandered the World-Honored One's precepts, and also not falsely slandered the wise and pure Brahmins of the heavens, and also not falsely slandered his own precepts, he gives rise to joy. Giving rise to joy, he gives rise to delight. Giving rise to delight, he stops the body. Stopping the body, he experiences bliss. Experiencing bliss, his mind is concentrated.

「跋陀和利！于意云何？若有比丘学具戒者，彼住无事处山林树下，或居高岩寂无音声，远离，无恶，无有人民，随顺] cf.［丽】燕坐。彼住远离处，修行精勤，得增上心，现法] cf.［丽】乐居。彼住远离处，修行精勤，安隐快乐已，不诬谤世尊戒，不诬谤天、诸智梵行者，亦不诬谤自戒。彼不诬谤世尊戒，不诬谤天、诸智梵行者，亦不诬谤自戒已，便生欢悦，生欢悦已，便生喜，生喜已，便止身，止身已，便觉乐，觉乐已，便心定。

Bhaddāli! The noble disciples, with their minds concentrated, then see things as they really are and know them as they truly are. Having seen things as they really are and known them as they truly are, they then separate from desire, separate from evil and unwholesome dharmas, have initial and sustained thought, and with the joy and bliss born of separation, attain the first concentration and abide in it. Bhaddāli! This is called attaining the first supreme mind at that time, attaining peaceful dwelling in the present life, which is easy to attain, not difficult, and blissful dwelling without fear, peaceful and blissful, leading to nirvāṇa. Bhaddāli! They then cease initial and sustained thought, internally calm, with one-pointed mind, without initial and sustained thought, with concentration born joy and bliss, attain the second concentration and abide in it. Bhaddāli! This is called attaining the second supreme mind at that time, attaining peaceful dwelling in the present life, which is easy to attain, not difficult, and blissful dwelling without fear, peaceful and blissful, leading to nirvāṇa. Bhaddāli! They then separate from joy, abandon seeking, dwell with right mindfulness and right knowledge, and experience bodily bliss, which is said to be the bliss of the noble ones, the bliss of the noble ones' equanimity, mindfulness, bliss, and emptiness, attain the third concentration and abide in it. Bhaddāli! This is called attaining the third supreme mind at that time, attaining peaceful dwelling in the present life, which is easy to attain, not difficult, and blissful dwelling without fear, peaceful and blissful, leading to nirvāṇa. Bhaddāli! They then cease bliss, cease suffering, joy and sorrow have already ceased, neither suffering nor bliss, equanimity, mindfulness, and purity, attain the fourth concentration and abide in it. Bhaddāli! This is called attaining the fourth supreme mind at that time, attaining peaceful dwelling in the present life, which is easy to attain, not difficult, and blissful dwelling without fear, peaceful and blissful, leading to nirvāṇa.

「跋陀和利！贤圣弟子心定已，便见如实、知如真，见如实、知如真已，便离欲、离恶不善之法，有觉、有观，离生喜、乐，得初禅成就游。跋陀和利！是谓彼于尔时得第一增上心，即于现法得安乐居，易不难得，乐住无怖，安隐快乐，令升涅槃。彼觉、观已息，内靖、一心，无觉、无观，定生喜、乐，得第二禅成就游。跋陀和利！是谓彼于尔时得第二增上心，即于现法得安乐居，易不难得，乐住无怖，安隐快乐，令升涅槃。彼离于喜欲，舍无求游，正念正智而身觉乐，谓圣所说，圣所舍、念、乐住、空，得第三禅成就游。跋陀和利！是谓彼于尔时得第三增上心，即于现法得安乐居，易不难得，乐住无怖，安隐快乐，令升涅槃。彼乐灭、苦灭，喜、忧本已灭，不苦不乐、舍、念、清净，得第四禅成就游。跋陀和利！是谓彼于尔时得第四增上心，即于现法得安乐居，易不难得，乐住无怖，安隐快乐，令升涅槃。

They attain such a concentrated mind, pure, undefiled, pliant, well-established, immovable, and attain the knowledge and vision of recollecting past lives. They have practice and characteristics, recollecting countless past lives, such as one life, two lives, a hundred lives, a thousand lives, an eon of formation, an eon of destruction, and countless eons of formation and destruction. They know, 'Those beings were named so-and-so, and I was born there before, with such a family name, such a given name, such a birth, such food and drink, such suffering and happiness, such a long life, such a long stay, such a life span. I died here and was born there, and died there and was born here, with such a family name, such a given name, such a birth, such food and drink, such suffering and happiness, such a long life, such a long stay, such a life span.' Bhaddāli! This is called attaining this first knowledge and vision at that time, with previous non-distraction, delighting in dwelling in seclusion, practicing diligently, that is, the extinction of ignorance and the arising of knowledge, the destruction of darkness and the arising of light, the extinction of ignorance and the arising of light, that is, the knowledge and vision of recollecting past lives.

「彼如是得定心清净，无秽无烦，柔软善住，得不动心，觉忆宿命智通作证，彼有行有相貌，忆本无量昔所经历，谓一生、二生、百生、千生，成劫、败劫、无量成败劫。彼众生名某，彼昔更历，我曾生彼，如是姓、如是字、如是生、如是饮食、如是受苦乐、如是长寿、如是久住、如是寿讫，此死生彼，彼死生此，我生在此，如是姓、如是字、如是生、如是饮食、如是受苦乐、如是长寿、如是久住、如是寿讫。跋陀和利！是谓彼于尔时得此第一明达，以本无放逸，乐住远离，修行精勤，谓无智灭而智生，暗坏而明成，无明灭而明生，谓忆宿命智作证明达。

They attain such a concentrated mind, pure, undefiled, pliant, well-established, immovable, and attain the knowledge and vision of the passing away and rebirth of beings. With their pure divine eye, surpassing that of humans, they see beings passing away and being reborn, inferior and superior, beautiful and ugly, in good and bad destinies, according to their karma. They know, 'These beings, having done evil deeds by body, speech, and mind, slandered the noble ones, held wrong views, and performed wrong deeds, because of that, at the breaking up of the body, after death, will certainly go to a bad realm of existence, to hell. These beings, having done good deeds by body, speech, and mind, not slandering the noble ones, holding right views, and performing right deeds, because of that, at the breaking up of the body, after death, will certainly go to a good realm of existence, to a heavenly realm.' Bhaddāli! This is called attaining this second knowledge and vision at that time, with previous non-distraction, delighting in dwelling in seclusion, practicing diligently, that is, the extinction of ignorance and the arising of knowledge, the destruction of darkness and the arising of light, the extinction of ignorance and the arising of light, that is, the knowledge and vision of the passing away and rebirth of beings.

「彼如是得定心清净，无秽无烦，柔软善住，得不动心，学于生死智通作证。彼以清净天眼出过于人，见此众生死时生时，好色恶色，妙与不妙，往来善处及不善处，随此众生之所作业，见其如真。若此众生成就身恶行，口、意恶行，诽谤圣人，邪见成就邪见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。若此众生成就身妙行，口、意妙行，不诽谤圣人，正见成就正见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必升善处，上生天中。跋陀和利！是谓彼于尔时得第二明达，以本无放逸，乐住远离，修行精勤，无智灭而智生，暗坏而明成，无明灭而明生，谓生死智作证明达。

They attain such a concentrated mind, pure, undefiled, pliant, well-established, immovable, and attain the knowledge and vision of the destruction of the taints. They know this suffering as it really is, know the arising of this suffering, know the cessation of this suffering, and know the path to the cessation of this suffering as it really is. They know these taints as they really are, know the arising of these taints, know the cessation of these taints, and know the path to the cessation of these taints as it really is. Knowing and seeing thus, their mind is liberated from the taint of sensual desire, from the taint of existence, and from the taint of ignorance. Being liberated, they know they are liberated. The supreme knowledge arises: 'Birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, there is no more of this state.' Bhaddāli! This is called attaining this third knowledge and vision at that time, with previous non-distraction, delighting in dwelling in seclusion, practicing diligently, that is, the extinction of ignorance and the arising of knowledge, the destruction of darkness and the arising of light, the extinction of ignorance and the arising of light, that is, the knowledge and vision of the destruction of the taints.

「彼如是得定心清净，无秽无烦，柔软善住，得不动心，学漏尽智通作证。彼知此苦如真，知此苦习、知此苦灭、知此苦灭道如真，知此漏如真，知此漏习、知此漏灭、知此漏灭道如真。彼如是知、如是见，欲漏心解脱，有漏、无明漏心解脱，解脱已便知解脱，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。跋陀和利！是谓彼于尔时得第三明达，以本无放逸，乐住远离，修行精勤，无智灭而智生，暗坏而明成，无明灭而明生，谓漏尽智作证明达。」

Then the Venerable Bhaddāli rose from his seat, arranged his robe over one shoulder, bowed with his head at the Buddha’s feet, and said, “World-Honored One! For what reason do the monks, when they commit the same offense, receive different punishments, some being punished and some not being punished?”

于是，尊者跋陀和利即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！何因何缘，诸比丘等同犯于戒，或有苦治，或不苦治。」

The World-Honored One replied, "Bhaddāli! There are monks who repeatedly break the precepts. Because of repeatedly breaking the precepts, they are criticized by the virtuous. They are criticized by the virtuous for what they have seen, heard, or suspected from others. After being criticized by the virtuous for what they have seen, heard, or suspected from others, they then speak various other matters, become angry and jealous, give rise to great anger and malice, disturb the assembly, and despise the assembly, saying, 'I will now do what pleases the assembly.' Bhaddāli! The monks then think, 'This venerable one repeatedly breaks the precepts. Because of repeatedly breaking the precepts, he is criticized by the virtuous. He is criticized by the virtuous for what he has seen, heard, or suspected from others. After being criticized by the virtuous for what he has seen, heard, or suspected from others, he then speaks various other matters, becomes angry and jealous, gives rise to great anger and malice, disturbs the assembly, and despises the assembly, saying, "I will now do what pleases the assembly." Having seen this, he says, "Venerables! Please observe and let him stay for a long time."'

世尊答曰：「跋陀和利！或有比丘数数犯戒，因数数犯戒故，为诸梵行诃，所见闻从他疑者，彼为诸梵行诃，所见闻从他疑已，便说异异论外余事，瞋恚憎嫉，发怒广恶，触娆于众，轻慢于众，作如是说：『我今当作令众欢喜而可意，作如是意。』跋陀和利！诸比丘便作是念：『然此贤者数数犯戒，因数数犯戒故，为诸梵行诃，所见闻从他疑者，彼为诸梵行诃，所见闻从他疑已，便说异异论外余事，瞋恚憎嫉，发怒广恶，触娆于众，轻慢于众，作如是说：「我今当作令众欢喜而可意。」见已作是语：「诸尊！当观令久住。」』跋陀和利！诸比丘如是观令久住。

There are monks who repeatedly break the precepts. Because of repeatedly breaking the precepts, they are criticized by the virtuous. They are criticized by the virtuous for what they have seen, heard, or suspected from others. After being criticized by the virtuous for what they have seen, heard, or suspected from others, they do not speak various other matters, do not become angry and jealous, do not give rise to great anger and malice, do not disturb the assembly, and do not despise the assembly, saying, 'I will now do what pleases the assembly.' Bhaddāli! The monks then think, 'This venerable one repeatedly breaks the precepts. Because of repeatedly breaking the precepts, he is criticized by the virtuous. He is criticized by the virtuous for what he has seen, heard, or suspected from others. After being criticized by the virtuous for what he has seen, heard, or suspected from others, he does not speak various other matters, does not become angry and jealous, does not give rise to great anger and malice, does not disturb the assembly, and does not despise the assembly, saying, "I will now do what pleases the assembly." Having seen this, he says, "Venerables! Please observe and let him be eliminated early."'

「或有比丘数数犯戒，因数数犯戒故，为诸梵行诃，所见闻从他疑者，彼为诸梵行诃，所见闻从他疑已，不说异异论外余事，不瞋恚憎嫉，发怒广恶，不触娆众，不轻慢众，不如是说：『我今当作令众欢喜而可意，不作如是意。』跋陀和利！诸比丘便作是念：『然此贤者数数犯戒，因数数犯戒故，为诸梵行诃，所见闻从他疑者，彼为诸梵行诃，所见闻从他疑已，不说异异论外余事，不瞋恚憎嫉，发怒广恶，不触娆众，不轻慢众，不如是说：「我今当作令众欢喜而可意。」见已而作是语：「诸尊！当观令早灭。」』跋陀和利！诸比丘如是观令早灭；轻犯禁戒亦复如是。

"Bhaddāli! There are monks who have faith, affection, and tranquility. This monk has faith, affection, and tranquility. If we punish this venerable one, he will be cut off because of his faith, affection, and tranquility. We should rather protect this venerable one well together.' The monks then protect him well together. Bhaddāli! It is like a person who has only one eye. His relatives pity and protect him, seeking benefit and welfare, seeking peace and happiness, protecting him well, not letting him be cold, hot, hungry, thirsty, sick, or worried, not letting him be dusty, smoky, or dusty and smoky. Why? For fear that this person might lose his only eye. Therefore, his relatives protect him well. Bhaddāli! In the same way, a monk has little faith, affection, and tranquility. The monks then think, 'This monk has little faith, affection, and tranquility. If we punish this venerable one, he will be cut off because of his little faith, affection, and tranquility. We should rather protect this venerable one well together.' Therefore, the monks protect him well together, just like relatives protecting a person with only one eye."

「跋陀和利！或有比丘有信、有爱、有靖，今此比丘有信、有爱、有靖。『若我等苦治于此贤者，今此贤者有信、有爱、有靖，因此必断，我等宁可善共将护于此贤者。』诸比丘便善共将护。跋陀和利！譬若如人唯有一眼，彼诸亲属为怜念愍伤，求利及饶益，求安隐快乐，善共将护，莫令此人寒热、饥渴、有病、有忧有病忧，莫尘、莫烟、莫尘烟。所以者何？复恐此人失去一眼，是故亲属善将护之。跋陀和利！如是比丘少信、少爱、少有靖，诸比丘等便作是念：『今此比丘少信、少爱、少有靖，若我等苦治于此贤者，今此贤者少信、少爱、少有靖，因此必断，我等宁可善共将护于此贤者。』是故诸比丘善共将护，犹如亲属护一眼人。」

Then the Venerable Bhaddāli rose from his seat, arranged his robe over one shoulder, and with palms joined, said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! For what reason and condition did the Buddha in the past establish few precepts, and many monks upheld them? For what reason and condition does the World-Honored One today establish many precepts, and few monks uphold them?"

于是，尊者跋陀和利即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！何因何缘，昔日少施设戒，多有比丘遵奉持者？何因何缘，世尊今日多施设戒，少有比丘遵奉持者？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Bhaddāli! If the Sangha does not gain benefits, the Sangha will not give rise to the Dharma of joy and delight. If the Sangha gains benefits, the Sangha will give rise to the Dharma of joy and delight. Having given rise to the Dharma of joy and delight, the World-Honored One, wishing to cut off this Dharma of joy and delight, then establishes precepts for the disciples. Thus, the praise is vast and widely known by the great king, with great merit and much learning. Bhaddāli! If the Sangha is not learned, the Sangha will not give rise to the Dharma of joy and delight. If the Sangha is learned, the Sangha will give rise to the Dharma of joy and delight. Having given rise to the Dharma of joy and delight, the World-Honored One, wishing to cut off this Dharma of joy and delight, then establishes precepts for the disciples. Bhaddāli! It is not for the sake of cutting off the defilements of this present life that I establish precepts for the disciples, but for the sake of cutting off the defilements of future lives. Bhaddāli! Therefore, I establish precepts for the disciples to cut off the defilements, and they accept my teachings. Bhaddāli! I have previously spoken to the monks about the Dharma of the pure horse simile. Do you remember the reason for this?"

世尊答曰：「跋陀和利！若比丘众不得利者，众便无憙好法，若众得利者，众便生憙好法。生喜好法已，世尊欲断此憙好法故，便为弟子施设于戒。如是称誉广大，上尊王所识知，大有福、多学问。跋陀和利！若众不多闻者，众便不生憙好法，若众多闻者，众便生憙好法。众生憙好法已，世尊欲断此憙好法故，便为弟子施设戒。跋陀和利！不以断现世漏故，为弟子施设戒，我以断后世漏故，为弟子施设戒。跋陀和利！是故我为弟子断漏故施设戒，至受我教。跋陀和利！我于昔时为诸比丘说清净马喻法，此中何所因，汝忆不耶？」

The Venerable Bhaddāli said, "World-Honored One! There is a reason for this. Why is that? The World-Honored One established the precept of eating in one sitting for the monks, and the Sangha all upheld the precepts and the subtle Dharma of the World-Honored One's realm. Only I said I was incapable, and left my seat, because I did not uphold the complete precepts and the subtle Dharma of the World-Honored One's realm. World-Honored One! This is the reason for this."

尊者跋陀和利白曰：「世尊！此中有所因。所以者何？世尊为诸比丘施设一坐食戒，诸比丘众皆奉学戒及世尊境界诸微妙法，唯我说不堪任，从坐起去，以不学具戒及世尊境界诸微妙法故。世尊！是谓此中有所因。」

The World-Honored One again said, "Bhaddāli! This is not the only reason. Bhaddāli! If I were to speak to the monks about the Dharma of the pure horse simile, you would certainly not listen with a single-minded, respectful, and attentive mind. Bhaddāli! This is another reason for this."

世尊复告曰：「跋陀和利！此中不但因是，跋陀和利！若我为诸比丘当说清净马喻法者，汝必不一心，不善恭敬，不思念听。跋陀和利！是谓此中更有因也。」

Then the Venerable Bhaddāli rose from his seat, arranged his robe over one shoulder, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "World-Honored One! Now is the time. Well-Gone One! Now is the time. If the World-Honored One would speak to the monks about the Dharma of the pure horse simile, the monks would listen well and uphold it."

于是，尊者跋陀和利即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！今正是时。善逝！今正是时。若世尊为诸比丘说清净马喻法者，诸比丘从世尊闻已，当善受持。」

The World-Honored One said, "Bhaddāli! It is like a horse trainer who obtains a pure, good horse. That horse trainer first trains its mouth. Having trained its mouth, it becomes disinclined to movement, either wanting or not wanting. Why is that? Because it has never been trained before. Bhaddāli! If a pure, good horse is trained by a horse trainer, and the first training is accomplished, that horse trainer then further trains it by restraining its mouth and legs, restraining its legs and restraining its mouth, and makes it drive. It is made to ascend the threshold, fit to be ridden by the king, with supreme movement and supreme rest. All its limbs are trained and accomplished, and it becomes disinclined to movement, either wanting or not wanting. Why is that? Because it is repeatedly trained. Bhaddāli! If a pure, good horse is repeatedly trained by that horse trainer and becomes accomplished, then at that time it is tamed, well-tamed, attains supreme taming, attains the foremost supreme taming, supreme movement, attains the foremost movement, and is then fit to be ridden by the king, eating the king's rice, and is praised as the king's horse.

世尊告曰：「跋陀和利！犹如知御马者得清净良马，彼知御者先治其口，治其口已，则有不乐于动转，或欲或不欲。所以者何？以未曾治故。跋陀和利！若清净良马从御者治，第一治得成就，彼御马者，然复更治勒口绊脚、绊脚勒口而令驱行，用令上阈，堪任王乘无上行，无上息治诸支节，悉御令成，则有不乐于动转，或欲或不欲。所以者何？以数数治故。跋陀和利！若清净良马，彼御马者数数治时得成就者，彼于尔时调、善调，得无上调、得第一无上调，无上行、得第一行，便中王乘，食于王粟，称说王马。

"Bhaddāli! It is like this. When a wise and intelligent person accomplishes the ten unlearned dharmas, unlearned right view, up to unlearned right knowledge, then at that time they are tamed, well-tamed, attain supreme taming, attain the foremost supreme taming, supreme stopping, attain the foremost stopping, eliminate all crookedness, eliminate all defilement, eliminate all fear, eliminate all delusion, eliminate all deceit, stop all dust, purify all defilement without attachment, are worthy of respect, worthy of honor, worthy of offerings, worthy of worship, and are a good field of merit for all gods and humans."

「跋陀和利！如是。若时贤良智人成就十无学法，无学正见，乃至无学正智者，彼于尔时调、善调，得无上调、得第一无上调，无上止、得第一止，除一切曲，除一切秽，除一切怖，除一切痴，除一切谄，止一切尘，净一切垢而无所著，可敬可重，可奉可祠，一切天人良福田也。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The Venerable Bhaddāli and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。尊者跋陀和利及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
