MĀ 196

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 16 大品

196. （一九六）周那经

Parallel to: MN 104

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of the Mallas, staying at the village of Sāma.

一时，佛游跋耆，在舍弥村。

At that time, the novice monk Cunda was spending the summer retreat in Pāvā. In Pāvā, there was a Nirgrantha named Putta, who had passed away. Not long after his death, the disciples of the Nirgrantha Putta were each destroyed, not united, and each spoke of the destruction and disunity, quarreling, binding each other, hating, and disputing, saying, "I know this Dharma, you do not know. What Dharma do you know, as I know? I am neat, you are not neat. I am in accord, you are not in accord. I should speak first but speak later, I should speak later but speak first. I am superior, you are inferior. I ask you a question, you cannot answer. I have already defeated you, and I will ask again. If you move, I will bind you again." They were mutually arrogant, only seeking to speak of victory, without anyone rebuking them. The lay disciples of the Nirgrantha Putta were all disgusted with the disciples of the Nirgrantha Putta. Why? Because their teachings were evil Dharma and Vinaya, not leading to liberation, not leading to right enlightenment, and not spoken by the Well-Gone One. They were destroyed without a pillar, without any support. Their revered teacher was also not a Tathāgata, one who has nothing more to learn, a fully enlightened one.

尔时，沙弥周那于彼波和中而受夏坐，彼波和中有一尼揵，名曰亲子，在彼命终。终后不久，尼揵亲子诸弟子等各各破坏，不共和合，各说破坏，不和合事，鬪讼相缚，相憎共诤：「我知此法，汝不知也。汝知何法，如我所知？我齐整，汝不齐整。我相应，汝不相应。应说前而说后，应说后而说前。我胜，汝不如。我问汝事，汝不能答。我已伏汝，当复更问。若汝动者，我重缚汝。」更互憍慠，但求胜说，而无诃者。尼揵亲子若有在家白衣弟子，彼皆厌患此尼揵亲子诸弟子等。所以者何？以其所说恶法、律故，非是出要，不趣正觉，亦非善逝之所说也。崩坏无柱，无所依怙，彼所尊师，亦非如来、无所著、等正觉也。

Then, after the novice monk Cunda had spent the summer retreat, after three months, having repaired his robes, he put on his robe, took his bowl, and went to the village of Sāma, staying in the Śiṣyaka Grove north of the village. The novice monk Cunda went to where Venerable Ānanda was, bowed at his feet, and sat to one side. Venerable Ānanda asked, "Virtuous one Cunda! Where have you come from? Where did you spend the summer retreat? "

于是，沙弥周那受夏坐讫，过三月已，补治衣竟，摄衣持钵，往舍弥村，住舍弥村北尸摄和林。沙弥周那往诣尊者阿难所，到已礼足，却坐一面，尊者阿难问曰：「贤者周那！从何所来，何处夏坐？」

The novice monk Cunda replied, "Venerable Ānanda! I have come from Pāvā, where I spent the summer retreat. Venerable Ānanda! In Pāvā, there was a Nirgrantha named Putta, who has passed away. Not long after his death, the disciples of the Nirgrantha Putta are each destroyed, not united, and each speaks of the destruction and disunity, quarreling, binding each other, hating, and disputing, saying, 'I know this Dharma, you do not know. What Dharma do you know, as I know? I am neat, you are not neat. I am in accord, you are not in accord. I should speak first but speak later, I should speak later but speak first. I am superior, you are inferior. I ask you a question, you cannot answer. I have already defeated you, and I will ask again. If you move, I will bind you again.' They are mutually arrogant, only seeking to speak of victory, without anyone rebuking them. The lay disciples of the Nirgrantha Putta are all disgusted with the disciples of the Nirgrantha Putta. Why? Because their teachings are evil Dharma and Vinaya, not leading to liberation, not leading to right enlightenment, and not spoken by the Well-Gone One. They are destroyed without a pillar, without any support. Their revered teacher is also not a Tathāgata, one who has nothing more to learn, a fully enlightened one.

沙弥周那答曰：「尊者阿难！我从波和来，于波和中而受夏坐。尊者阿难！彼波和中有一尼揵，名曰亲子，在彼命终。终后不久，尼揵亲子诸弟子等各各破坏，不共和合，各说破坏，不和合事，鬪讼相缚，相憎共诤：『我知此法，汝不知也。汝知何法，如我所知？我齐整，汝不齐整。我相应，汝不相应。应说前而说后，应说后而说前。我胜，汝不如。我问汝事，汝不能答。我已伏汝，当复更问。若汝动者，我重缚汝。』更互憍慠，但求胜说，而无诃者。尼揵亲子若有在家白衣弟子，彼皆厌患此尼揵亲子诸弟子等。所以者何？以其所说恶法、律故，非是出要，不趣正觉，亦非善逝之所说也。崩坏无柱，无所依怙，彼所尊师，亦非如来、无所著、等正觉也。」

Venerable Ānanda, having heard, said, "Virtuous Cunda! You may go to see the Buddha and offer it to the World-Honored One. Virtuous Cunda! Now let us go together to see the Buddha and tell the World-Honored One about this matter. Perhaps we can hear different teachings from the World-Honored One because of this.

尊者阿难闻已，语曰：「贤者周那！得因此说，可往见佛，奉献世尊。贤者周那！今共诣佛，具向世尊而说此事，傥能因此得从世尊闻异法也。」

Then Venerable Ānanda and the novice monk Cunda went together to see the Buddha, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, and Venerable Ānanda stood to one side, while the novice monk Cunda sat to one side.

于是，尊者阿难与沙弥周那俱往诣佛，稽首佛足，尊者阿难却住一面，沙弥周那却坐一面。

Venerable Ānanda said, "World-Honored One! Today the novice monk Cunda came to me, bowed his head at my feet, and sat to one side. I asked him, 'Virtuous Cunda! Where have you come from? Where did you spend the summer retreat?' The novice monk Cunda then replied to me, 'Venerable Ānanda! I have come from Pāvā, where I spent the summer retreat. Venerable Ānanda! There was a Nirgrantha named Putta in Pāvā who has passed away. Not long after his passing, the Nirgrantha Putta's disciples have each become divided and not in harmony, each speaking of their division and lack of harmony, quarreling, binding each other, hating, and disputing, saying, "I know this teaching, but you do not. What do you know that I know? I am correct, but you are not. I am in accord, but you are not. You should speak first, but you speak last. You should speak last, but you speak first. I am superior, but you are inferior. I ask you a question, but you cannot answer. I have already defeated you, and I will ask again. If you move, I will bind you again." They are mutually arrogant, only seeking to speak of victory, without anyone rebuking them. If the Nirgrantha Putta had any lay disciples, they would all be disgusted with the Nirgrantha Putta's disciples. Why is that? Because their teachings are evil and not the Dharma, not leading to liberation, not leading to right awakening, and not spoken by the Well-Gone One. They are collapsing and have no support. The teacher they revere is also not a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One.'

尊者阿难白曰：「世尊！今日沙弥周那来诣我所，稽首我足，却坐一面，我问曰：『贤者周那！从何所来？何处夏坐？』沙弥周那即答我曰：『尊者阿难！我从波和来，于波和中而受夏坐。尊者阿难！彼波和中有一尼揵，名曰亲子，在彼命终，终后不久，尼揵亲子诸弟子等各各破坏，不共和合，各说破坏，不和合事，鬪讼相缚，相憎共诤：「我知此法，汝不知也。汝知何法，如我所知？我齐整，汝不齐整。我相应，汝不相应。应说前而说后，应说后而说前。我胜，汝不如。我问汝事，汝不能答。我已伏汝，当复更问。若汝动者，我重缚汝。」更互憍慠，但求胜说，而无诃者。尼揵亲子若有在家白衣弟子，彼皆厌患此尼揵亲子诸弟子等。所以者何？以其所说恶法、律故，非是出要，不趣正觉，亦非善逝之所说也。崩坏无柱，无所依怙，彼所尊师，亦非如来、无所著、等正觉也。』

"World-Honored One! Having heard this, I was terrified, frightened, and my body hair stood on end. I hope that after the World-Honored One has passed away, no bhikṣus will cause such disputes in the assembly, which will not benefit many people, bring suffering to many people, be unrighteous, unbeneficial, and not lead to peace, happiness, and joy, up to the birth of extreme suffering and affliction for gods and humans. World-Honored One! I saw a bhikṣu sitting in front of the World-Honored One, with utmost respect and reverence for the World-Honored One, well-protected and well-guarded. World-Honored One! Having seen this, I thought: 'If this bhikṣu were to cause such disputes in the assembly after the World-Honored One has passed away, which would not benefit many people, bring suffering to many people, be unrighteous, unbeneficial, and not lead to peace, happiness, and joy, up to the birth of extreme suffering and affliction for gods and humans.'"

「世尊！我闻此已，恐怖惊惧，举身毛竖，莫令有比丘于世尊去后而在众中起如是鬪诤，谓此鬪诤不益多人，多人有苦，非义非饶益，非安隐快乐，乃至天人生极苦患。世尊！我见一比丘坐世尊前，至心敬重世尊，善护善逝。世尊！我见此已，便作是念：『若令此比丘于世尊去后，而在众中起如是鬪诤，谓此鬪诤不益多人，多人有苦，非义非饶益，非安隐快乐，乃至天人生极苦患。』」

Then the World-Honored One asked, "Ānanda! Which assembly did you see that caused disputes, which did not benefit many people, brought suffering to many people, was unrighteous, unbeneficial, and did not lead to peace, happiness, and joy, up to the birth of extreme suffering and affliction for gods and humans?"

于是，世尊问曰：「阿难！汝见何等众中有鬪诤者，谓此鬪诤不益多人，多人有苦，非义非饶益，非安隐快乐，乃至天人生极苦患耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "World-Honored One! It refers to disputes arising from superior precepts, superior concentration, and superior insight within that assembly. World-Honored One! This kind of dispute does not benefit many people, brings suffering to many people, is unrighteous, unbeneficial, and does not lead to peace, happiness, and joy, up to the birth of extreme suffering and affliction for gods and humans."

尊者阿难答曰：「世尊！谓有鬪诤，因增上戒、增上心、增上观，于其众中生而生者。世尊！谓此鬪诤不益多人，多人有苦，非义非饶益，非安隐快乐，乃至天人生极苦患。」

The World-Honored One said, "Ānanda! This kind of dispute is very rare, arising from superior precepts, superior concentration, and superior insight. Ānanda! If there is a dispute arising from the path and the path's traces within that assembly, Ānanda! This kind of dispute does not benefit many people, brings suffering to many people, is unrighteous, unbeneficial, and does not lead to peace, happiness, and joy. Ānanda! Have you seen two monks there who have different intentions and give rise to disputes, saying, 'This is the Dharma, this is not the Dharma; this is the Vinaya, this is not the Vinaya; this is an offense, this is not an offense; this is light, this is heavy; this is reprehensible, this is not reprehensible; this is to be guarded against, this is not to be guarded against; this has remainder, this has no remainder; this arises, this does not arise.' Ānanda! What do you think? If I have the Dharma-aggregate, which I know, realize, and personally verify, the four establishments of mindfulness, the four right efforts, the four bases of supernormal power, the five faculties, the five powers, the seven factors of awakening, and the eightfold noble path. Ānanda! The son of the Nirgrantha Jains is not truly a fully awakened one, yet he claims to be fully awakened. Ānanda! If the son of the Nirgrantha Jains truly knows and sees everything, then why does he establish six bases for disputes for his disciples, saying, 'This should be heard and stopped'?"

世尊告曰：「阿难！此鬪诤甚少，谓因增上戒、增上心、增上观。阿难！若有鬪诤，因道、因道迹，于其众中生而生者，阿难！谓此鬪诤不益多人，多人有苦，非义非饶益，非安隐快乐。阿难！汝见其中有二比丘，各各异意而起鬪诤，是法、是非法，是律、是非律，是犯、是非犯，或轻、或重，可悔、不可悔，可护、不可护，有余、无余，起、不起。阿难！于意云何？若我法聚，自知自觉自作证，四念处、四正断、四如意足、五根、五力、七觉支、八支圣道。阿难！尼揵亲子实非萨云若，而自称萨云若。阿难！若尼揵亲子一切知、一切见者，彼为弟子施设六诤本，谓可闻而止。」

Then Venerable Ānanda, with palms joined towards the Buddha, said, "World-Honored One! Now is the time. Well-Gone One! Now is the time. If the World-Honored One would explain the six bases for disputes to the monks, the monks would listen well and uphold them."

于是，尊者阿难叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！今正是时。善逝！今正是时。若世尊为诸比丘说六诤本者，诸比丘从世尊闻，当善受持。」

The World-Honored One said, "Ānanda! Listen carefully and keep it in mind. I will explain it to you in detail."

世尊告曰：「阿难！谛听，善思念之，我当为汝具分别说。」

Venerable Ānanda said, "Yes, I will listen attentively."

尊者阿难白曰：「唯然。当受教听。」

The Buddha said, "Ānanda! There are some people who are bound by anger and resentment. Ānanda! Those who are bound by anger and resentment do not respect their teachers, do not see the Dharma, and do not uphold the precepts. Having not respected their teachers, not seen the Dharma, and not upheld the precepts, they then give rise to such disputes within the assembly. That is, this dispute does not benefit many people, brings suffering to many people, is not beneficial, not conducive to well-being and happiness, and even leads to extreme suffering and affliction for humans and gods. Ānanda! If you see such disputes within and without, in order to eliminate them, you should quickly seek means, study with utmost diligence, be mindful and wise, and endure without retreating. Ānanda! It is like a person whose head or clothes are on fire, urgently seeking means to save their head or clothes. Similarly, if you see such disputes within and without, in order to eliminate them, you should quickly seek means, study with utmost diligence, be mindful and wise, and endure without retreating. Ānanda! If you see the end of such disputes within and without, you should further protect your mind, always being vigilant, in order to stop these disputes. In this way, you will eliminate the root of these disputes. Ānanda! It is like a person whose head or clothes are on fire, urgently seeking means to save their head or clothes. Similarly, if you see the end of such disputes within and without, you should further protect your mind, always being vigilant, in order to stop these disputes. In this way, you will eliminate the root of these disputes.

佛言：「阿难！或有一人瞋恼者结缠。阿难！谓人瞋恼者结缠，彼不敬师，不见法，不护戒，彼不敬师，不见法，不护戒已，便于众中起如是诤。谓此鬪诤不益多人，多人有苦，非义非饶益，非安隐快乐，乃至天人生极苦患。阿难！如是鬪诤，汝于内外见而不尽者，为断此诤故，汝当速求方便，学极精勤，正念正智，忍莫令退。阿难！犹人为火烧头、烧衣，急求方便，救头、救衣。如是鬪诤，汝于内外见而不尽者，为断此诤故，汝当速求方便，学极精勤，正念正智，忍莫令退。阿难！如是鬪诤，汝于内外见尽者，汝当重护彼心，常无放逸，欲止此诤故，如是此诤，汝断根本。阿难！犹人为火烧头、烧衣，急求方便，救头、救衣。如是鬪诤，汝于内外见尽者，汝当重护彼心，常无放逸，欲止此诤故，如是此诤，汝断根本。

Ānanda! There are some people who are bound by anger and resentment, stinginess and jealousy, deceit and guile, shamelessness and lack of conscience, evil desires, wrong views, and evil temperaments that are difficult to control. Ānanda! If there is someone with evil desires, wrong views, and an evil temperament that is difficult to control, they will not respect their teachers, not see the Dharma, and not uphold the precepts. Having not respected their teachers, not seen the Dharma, and not upheld the precepts, they will then give rise to such disputes within the assembly. That is, this dispute does not benefit many people, brings suffering to many people, is not beneficial, not conducive to well-being and happiness, and even leads to extreme suffering and affliction for humans and gods. Ānanda! If you see such disputes within and without, in order to eliminate them, you should quickly seek means, study with utmost diligence, be mindful and wise, and endure without retreating. Ānanda! It is like a person whose head or clothes are on fire, urgently seeking means to save their head or clothes. Similarly, if you see such disputes within and without, in order to eliminate them, you should quickly seek means, study with utmost diligence, be mindful and wise, and endure without retreating. Ānanda! If you see the end of such disputes within and without, you should further protect your mind, always being vigilant, in order to stop these disputes. In this way, you will eliminate the root of these disputes. Ānanda! It is like a person whose head or clothes are on fire, urgently seeking means to save their head or clothes. Similarly, if you see the end of such disputes within and without, you should further protect your mind, always being vigilant, in order to stop these disputes. In this way, you will eliminate the root of these disputes.

「如是不语结、悭、嫉、谄诳、无惭、无愧、恶欲、邪见、恶性不可制。阿难！若有一人恶欲、邪见、恶性不可制，彼不敬师，不见法，不护戒，彼不敬师，不见法，不护戒已，便于众中起如是诤，谓此鬪诤不益多人，多人有苦，非义非饶益，非安隐快乐，乃至天人生极苦患。阿难！如是鬪诤，汝于内外见而不尽者，为断此诤故，汝当速求方便，学极精勤，正念正智，忍莫令退。阿难！犹人为火烧头、烧衣，急求方便，救头、救衣。如是鬪诤，汝于内外见而不尽者，为断此诤故，汝当速求方便，学极精勤，正念正智，忍莫令退。阿难！如是鬪诤，汝于内外见尽者，汝当重护彼心，常无放逸，欲止此诤故，如是此诤，汝断根本。阿难！犹人为火烧头、烧衣，急求方便，救头、救衣。如是鬪诤，汝于内外见尽者，汝当重护彼心，常无放逸，欲止此诤故，如是此诤，汝断根本。

Furthermore, Ānanda! There are seven methods for resolving disputes: 1. The method of resolving disputes by facing each other. 2. The method of resolving disputes by recollection. 3. The method of resolving disputes by non-delusion. 4. The method of resolving disputes by self-confession. 5. The method of resolving disputes by the majority. 6. The method of resolving disputes by passing around. 7. The method of resolving disputes by discarding like rubbish. Ānanda! What is the method of resolving disputes by facing each other? How does this resolve disputes? It is by the method of resolving disputes by facing each other. Ānanda! One person is admonished by one person, protected by the Dharma and Vinaya as instructed by the teacher, and made to be joyful in the presence of the others. One person is admonished by two people, one person is admonished by many people, one person is admonished by the assembly, protected by the Dharma and Vinaya as instructed by the teacher, and made to be joyful in the presence of the others. Two people are admonished by two people, protected by the Dharma and Vinaya as instructed by the teacher, and made to be joyful in the presence of the others. Two people are admonished by many people, two people are admonished by the assembly, two people are admonished by one person, protected by the Dharma and Vinaya as instructed by the teacher, and made to be joyful in the presence of the others. Many people are admonished by many people, protected by the Dharma and Vinaya as instructed by the teacher, and made to be joyful in the presence of the others. Many people are admonished by the assembly, many people are admonished by one person, many people are admonished by two people, protected by the Dharma and Vinaya as instructed by the teacher, and made to be joyful in the presence of the others. The assembly is admonished by the assembly, protected by the Dharma and Vinaya as instructed by the teacher, and made to be joyful in the presence of the others. The assembly is admonished by one person, the assembly is admonished by two people, the assembly is admonished by many people, protected by the Dharma and Vinaya as instructed by the teacher, and made to be joyful in the presence of the others. Ānanda! This is called the method of resolving disputes by facing each other. This resolves disputes by the method of resolving disputes by facing each other.

「复次，阿难！有七止诤，一者应与面前止诤律。二者应与忆止诤律。三者应与不痴止诤律。四者应与自发露止诤律。五者应与君止诤律。六者应与展转止诤律。七者应与如弃粪扫止诤律。阿难！云何应与面前止诤律？云何断此诤？谓因面前止诤律也。阿难！一人者一人教诃，护以法、律如尊师教，面前令欢喜。一人者二人、一人者多人、一人者众教诃，护以法、律，如尊师教，面前令欢喜。二人者二人教诃，护以法、律，如尊师教，面前令欢喜。二人者多人、二人者众、二人者一人教诃，护以法、律，如尊师教，面前令欢喜。多人者多人教诃，护以法、律，如尊师教，面前令欢喜。多人者众、多人者一人、多人者二人教诃，护以法、律，如尊师教，面前令欢喜。众者众教诃，护以法、律，如尊师教，面前令欢喜。众者一人、众者二人、众者多人教诃，护以法、律，如尊师教，面前令欢喜。阿难！是谓应与面前止诤律，如是断此诤，谓因面前止诤律也。

Ānanda! What is the method of resolving disputes by recollection? How does this resolve disputes? It is by the method of resolving disputes by recollection. Ānanda! If there is a person who has committed an offense but does not remember it, the other monks see it and tell them, 'You have committed an offense but do not remember it. You should request the assembly for the method of recollection, and the assembly will give it to you.' Ānanda! If there is a gathering of the assembly, that monk should go there, put on his robe over one shoulder, take off his shoes, enter the assembly, bow at the feet of the elder and senior monks, kneel with palms joined, and say to the elder and senior monks, 'Venerable ones! Please listen. I have committed an offense but do not remember it. I now request the assembly for the method of recollection. May the harmonious assembly give me the method of recollection.' Ānanda! For the sake of that monk, the assembly should gather and give him the method of recollection, according to the Dharma and Vinaya, as instructed by the teacher, and make him joyful in the presence of the others. Ānanda! This is called the method of resolving disputes by recollection. This resolves disputes by the method of resolving disputes by recollection.

「阿难！云何应与忆止诤律？云何断此诤？谓因忆止诤律也。阿难！若有一人犯戒而不忆，诸比丘见已，便语彼曰：『汝曹犯戒而不自忆，汝应从众求于忆律，众当共与贤者忆律。』阿难！若处有众和集会者，彼比丘应诣，偏袒著衣，脱屣入众，稽首礼长老上尊比丘足，长跪叉手，白长老上尊比丘曰：『诸尊！听我曾犯戒而不忆，我今从众求于忆律，愿众和合与我忆律。』阿难！为彼比丘故，众共和集，应与忆律，以法以律，如尊师教，面前令欢喜。阿难！是谓应与忆止诤律，如是断此诤，谓因忆止诤律也。

Ānanda! What is the method of resolving disputes by non-delusion? How does this resolve disputes? It is by the method of resolving disputes by non-delusion. Ānanda! If there is a person who is insane and has a deranged mind, and after becoming insane and having a deranged mind, commits many impure acts that are not the practice of a monk, and speaks of violating the precepts, then later when he regains his sanity, the other monks see it and tell him, 'You were insane and had a deranged mind. After becoming insane and having a deranged mind, you committed many impure acts that are not the practice of a monk, and spoke of violating the precepts. Now that you have regained your sanity, you should request the assembly for the method of non-delusion, and the assembly will give it to you.' Ānanda! If there is a gathering of the assembly, that monk should go there, put on his robe over one shoulder, take off his shoes, enter the assembly, bow at the feet of the elder and senior monks, kneel with palms joined, and say to the elder and senior monks, 'Venerable ones! Please listen. I was insane and had a deranged mind. After becoming insane and having a deranged mind, I committed many impure acts that are not the practice of a monk, and spoke of violating the precepts. Now that I have regained my sanity, I now request the assembly for the method of non-delusion. May the harmonious assembly give me the method of non-delusion.' Ānanda! For the sake of that monk, the assembly should gather and give him the method of non-delusion, according to the Dharma and Vinaya, as instructed by the teacher, and make him joyful in the presence of the others. Ānanda! This is called the method of resolving disputes by non-delusion. This resolves disputes by the method of resolving disputes by non-delusion.

「阿难！云何应与不痴止诤律？云何断此诤？谓因不痴止诤律也。阿难！若有一人狂发而心颠倒，彼狂发心颠倒已，多不净行，非沙门法，不顺法行而说违犯。彼于后时还得本心，诸比丘见已，便语彼曰：『汝曹狂发而心颠倒，狂发心颠倒已，多不净行，非沙门法，不顺法行而说违犯。贤者于后还得本心，贤者可从众求不痴律，众当共与贤者不痴律。』阿难！若处有众和集会者，彼比丘应诣，偏袒著衣，脱屣入众，稽首礼长老上尊比丘足，长跪叉手，白长老上尊比丘曰：『诸尊！听我曾狂发而心颠倒，狂发心颠倒已，多不净行，非沙门法，不顺法行而说违犯。我于后时还得本心，我今从众求不痴律，愿众和合与我不痴律。』阿难！为彼比丘故，众共和集，应与不痴律，以法以律，如尊师教，面前令欢喜。阿难！是谓应与不痴止诤律，如是断此诤，谓因不痴止诤律也。

Ānanda! What is the method of resolving disputes by self-disclosure? How does this resolve disputes? It is by the method of resolving disputes by self-disclosure. Ānanda! If there is a person who has committed an offense, whether someone tells him or not, whether he remembers it or not, Ānanda! If there is a gathering of the assembly, that monk should go there, put on his robe over one shoulder, take off his shoes, enter the assembly, bow at the feet of the elder and senior monks, kneel with palms joined, and say to the elder and senior monks, 'Venerable ones! Please listen. I have committed such-and-such an offense. I now sincerely disclose and confess it to the elder and senior monks. I dare not conceal it. I will better protect and uphold it in the future and not do it again.' Ānanda! The assembly of monks should ask that monk, 'Do you see your offense?' He should answer, 'I indeed see my offense.' The assembly should tell him, 'Better protect and uphold it. Do not do it again.' Ānanda! This is called the method of resolving disputes by self-disclosure. This resolves disputes by the method of resolving disputes by self-disclosure.

「阿难！云何应与自发露止诤律？云何断此诤？谓因自发露止诤律也。阿难！若有一人犯戒，或有语者，或不语者，或有忆者，或不忆者。阿难！若处有众和集会者，彼比丘应诣，偏袒著衣，脱屣入众，稽首礼长老上尊比丘足，长跪叉手，白长老上尊比丘曰：『诸尊！听！我犯某戒，我今向长老上尊比丘至心发露，自说显示，不敢覆藏，更善护持，后不复作。』阿难！诸比丘众当问彼比丘曰：『贤者自见所犯耶？』彼应答曰：『实自见所犯。』众当语彼：『更善护持，莫复作也。』阿难！是谓应与自发露止诤律，如是断此诤，谓因自发露止诤律也。

Ānanda! What is the method of resolving disputes by the king? How does this resolve disputes? It is by the method of resolving disputes by the king. Ānanda! If there is a person who is shameless, does not regret what he has seen or heard, and follows the evil desires of others, and after committing an offense, claims to know something in one place, claims to see something in one place, claims to know something in one place after claiming to see something in one place, claims to see something in one place after claiming to know something in one place, claims to know something in one place in the assembly, claims to see something in one place in the assembly, claims to know something in one place after claiming to see something in one place, claims to see something in one place after claiming to know something in one place. Ānanda! For the sake of that monk, the assembly should gather and give him the method of the king, which is without the Dharma and Vinaya, and is evil and unwholesome. Why is that? It is because after committing an offense, he claims to know something in one place, claims to see something in one place, claims to know something in one place after claiming to see something in one place, claims to see something in one place after claiming to know something in one place, claims to know something in one place in the assembly, claims to see something in one place in the assembly, claims to know something in one place after claiming to see something in one place, claims to see something in one place after claiming to know something in one place. Ānanda! This is called the method of resolving disputes by the king. This resolves disputes by the method of resolving disputes by the king.

「阿难！云何应与君止诤律？云何断此诤？谓因与君止诤律也。阿难！若有一人不知羞耻，不悔见闻，从他疑者恶欲，彼犯戒已，称一处知，称一处见，称一处知已，称一处见，称一处见已，称一处知，在众中称一处知，在众中称一处见，称一处知已，称一处见，称一处见已，称一处知。阿难！为彼比丘故，众共和集，应与君律，君无道无理，君恶不善。所以者何？谓君犯戒已，称一处知，称一处见，称一处知已，称一处见，称一处见已，称一处知，在众中称一处知，在众中称一处见，称一处知已，称一处见，称一处见已，称一处知。阿难！是谓应与君止诤律，如是断此诤，谓因与君止诤律也。

Ānanda! What is the method of resolving disputes by mutual confession? How does this resolve disputes? It is by the method of resolving disputes by mutual confession. Ānanda! If there are two monks who have arisen from various intentions in dispute, saying that this is the Dharma and that is not the Dharma, this is the Vinaya and that is not the Vinaya, this is an offense and that is not an offense, or it is light or heavy, it can be spoken or cannot be spoken, it can be guarded or cannot be guarded, it has remainder or does not have remainder, it can be regretted or cannot be regretted. Ānanda! If those monks stop this dispute in a secluded place, if it is stopped in a secluded place, this dispute is said to be stopped. If it is not stopped in a secluded place, this dispute should be reported to the assembly. If it is stopped in the assembly, this dispute is said to be stopped. If it is not stopped in the assembly, Ānanda! If there are monks who uphold the sūtras, uphold the Vinaya, and uphold the mother texts, they should go together to those monks and speak about this dispute. If it is stopped on the way, this dispute is said to be stopped. If it is not stopped on the way, this dispute should be reported to the assembly again. If it is stopped in the assembly, this dispute is said to be stopped. If it is not stopped in the assembly, Ānanda! If there are many companions who uphold the sūtras, uphold the Vinaya, and uphold the mother texts, Ānanda! Those monks should stop this dispute according to the Dharma and Vinaya, as taught by the elders, and make them rejoice in front of them. Ānanda! This is called the method of resolving disputes by mutual confession. This resolves disputes by the method of resolving disputes by mutual confession.

「阿难！云何应与展转止诤律？云何断此诤？谓因展转止诤律也。阿难！有二比丘于其中间若干意起诤，谓是法、非法，是律、非律，是犯、非犯，或轻、或重，可说、不可说，可护、不可护，有余、无余，可悔、不可悔。阿难！彼比丘猥处止此诤，若猥处止者，此诤当言止，若猥处不止者，此诤可白众，若于众中止者，此诤当言止，若于众中不止者，阿难！相近住者，于中若有比丘持经、持律、持母者，此比丘共往至彼，说此诤事，若在道路止者，此诤当言止，若道路不止者，此诤当复向众说，若在众止者，此诤当言止，若在众不止者，阿难！若多伴助者，持经、持律、持母者，阿难！彼比丘应者止此诤，以法以律，如尊师教，面前令欢喜。阿难！是谓应与展转止诤律，如是断此诤，谓因展转止诤律也。

Ānanda! What is the method of resolving disputes by throwing out like garbage? How does this resolve disputes? It is by the method of resolving disputes by throwing out like garbage. Ānanda! If there are monks in a dwelling place who quarrel and hate each other, and quarrel together, Ānanda! Those monks divide into two groups. Having divided into two groups, if there are elders and superiors in one group, or those who are next in line, or those who are the leaders, or those who are next in line, Ānanda! Those monks should say to those monks: 'Venerable ones! Listen! We are without the Dharma and Vinaya, and we are evil and unwholesome. Why is that? Because we, having attained the well-proclaimed Dharma and Vinaya, having gone forth from the home to homelessness, quarrel and hate each other, and quarrel together. Venerable ones! Because of this dispute, we have committed offenses except for pārājika offenses, except for offenses related to householders, we have done so for ourselves and also for the sake of those venerable ones. Now we sincerely confess to the venerable ones, reveal ourselves, and show ourselves, not daring to conceal them, and we will better protect and uphold them in the future and not do them again.'

「阿难！云何应与如弃粪扫止诤律？云何断此诤？谓因如弃粪扫止诤律也。阿难！若有住处诸比丘众鬪讼憎嫉，相憎共诤。阿难！彼诸比丘分立二部，分立二部已，若于一部中有长老上尊者，或有次者，有宗主者，或有次者。阿难！此比丘语彼比丘曰：『诸贤！听！我等无道无理，我等恶不善。所以者何？我等于此善说法、律，至信、舍家、无家、学道，鬪讼憎嫉，相憎共诤。诸贤！因此诤，我等犯戒者除偷罗柘，除家相应，我自为己，亦为彼诸贤故，今向贤至心发露，自说显示，不敢覆藏，更善护持，后不复作。』

Ānanda! If there is not a single monk in this group who is suitable, Ānanda! Those monks should go to that second group. Having arrived, they should bow their heads at the feet of the elders and superior monks, kneel on both knees with palms joined, and say to the elders and superior monks: 'Venerable ones! Listen! We are without the Dharma and Vinaya, and we are evil and unwholesome. Why is that? Because we, having attained the well-proclaimed Dharma and Vinaya, having gone forth from the home to homelessness, quarrel and hate each other, and quarrel together. Venerable ones! Because of this dispute, we have committed offenses except for pārājika offenses, except for offenses related to householders, we have done so for ourselves and also for the sake of those venerable ones. Now we sincerely confess to the elders and superior ones, reveal ourselves, and show ourselves, not daring to conceal them, and we will better protect and uphold them in the future and not do them again.' Ānanda! Those monks should say to those monks: 'Venerable ones! Do you see that you have committed offenses?' They should answer: 'Indeed, we see that we have committed offenses.' They should say to them: 'Better protect and uphold them, and do not do them again.' The second group is also like this. Ānanda! This is called the method of resolving disputes by throwing out like garbage. This resolves disputes by the method of resolving disputes by throwing out like garbage.

「阿难！若此部中无一比丘应者，阿难！此比丘应往至彼第二部，到已稽首，礼长老上尊比丘足，长跪叉手，白长老上尊比丘曰：『诸尊！听！我等无道无理，我等恶不善。所以者何？我等于此善说法、律，至信、舍家、无家、学道，鬪讼憎嫉，相憎共诤。诸贤！因此诤，我等犯戒者除偷罗柘，除家相应，我自为己，亦为彼诸贤故，今向长老上尊至心发露，自说显示，不敢覆藏，更善护持，后不复作。』阿难！彼比丘当语此比丘曰：『贤者！汝自见犯戒耶？』彼应答曰：『实自见所犯。』彼当语此：『更善护持，莫复作也。』第二部亦复如是。阿难！是谓应与如弃粪扫止诤律，如是断此诤，谓因如弃粪扫止诤律也。

"Ānanda! I now instruct you to speak the six methods of consolation. Listen carefully, listen carefully, and consider them well."

「阿难！我今令汝说六慰劳法，谛听，谛听，善思念之。」

Venerable Ānanda said, "Yes, I will listen to the instruction."

尊者阿难白曰：「唯然。当受教听。」

The Buddha said, "What are the six? Loving physical actions towards those who practice the Dharma, this is the method of consolation, the method of love, the method of joy, causing love, causing respect, causing reverence, causing practice, causing absorption, attaining the state of a śramaṇa, attaining single-mindedness, attaining diligence, attaining nirvāṇa. Loving verbal actions, loving mental actions. If one obtains benefits according to the Dharma, and one's own obtained food and drink, up to what is in the bowl, one distributes these benefits and gives them to those who practice the Dharma. This is the method of consolation, the method of love, the method of joy, causing love, causing respect, causing reverence, causing practice, causing absorption, attaining the state of a śramaṇa, attaining single-mindedness, attaining diligence, attaining nirvāṇa. If one has precepts that are not broken, not pierced, not defiled, not different, not following others, praised by the sages, and fully upholds such precepts, one distributes them to those who practice the Dharma. This is the method of consolation, the method of love, the method of joy, causing love, causing respect, causing reverence, causing practice, causing absorption, attaining the state of a śramaṇa, attaining single-mindedness, attaining diligence, attaining nirvāṇa. If one has the noble view of liberation, clear insight, deep understanding, and the ability to truly end suffering, one distributes such views to those who practice the Dharma. This is the method of consolation, the method of love, the method of joy, causing love, causing respect, causing reverence, causing practice, causing absorption, attaining the state of a śramaṇa, attaining single-mindedness, attaining diligence, attaining nirvāṇa. Ānanda! The six methods of consolation I mentioned earlier are spoken for this reason.

佛言：「云何为六？慈身业，向诸梵行，法是慰劳法、爱法、乐法，令爱令重，令奉令敬，令修令摄，得沙门，得一心，得精进，得涅槃，慈口业，慈意业。若法利如法得，自所得饭食，至在钵中，如是利分布，施诸梵行，是法慰劳法、爱法、乐法，令爱令重，令奉令敬，令修令摄，得沙门，得一心，得精进，得涅槃。若有戒不缺不穿，无秽无异，如地不随他，圣所称誉，具足善受持如是戒分，布施诸梵行，是法慰劳法、爱法、乐法，令爱令重，令奉令敬，令修令摄，得沙门，得一心，得精进，得涅槃。若有圣见出要，明见深达，能正尽苦，如是见分布，施诸梵行，是法慰劳法、爱法、乐法，令爱令重，令奉令敬，令修令摄，得沙门，得一心，得精进，得涅槃。阿难！我向所说六慰劳法者，因此故说。

"Ānanda! If you can stop and eliminate these six roots of dispute, and use these seven methods to stop disputes that arise in the assembly, and use the method of discarding like discarded filth to stop disputes, and then practice these six methods of consolation, Ānanda! In this way, after my passing, you should all be harmonious, joyful, without disputes, with a single mind, a single teaching, united like water and milk, happily wandering."

「阿难！若汝等此六诤本止绝断者，及此七止诤，众中起鬪诤，以如弃粪扫止诤律止者，复行此六慰劳法。阿难！如是汝于我去后共同和合，欢喜不诤，同一一心，同一一教，合一水乳，快乐游行，如我在时。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Venerable Ānanda and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。尊者阿难及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
