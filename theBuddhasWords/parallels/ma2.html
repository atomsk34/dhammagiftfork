MĀ 2

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 1 七法品

2. The Coral Tree （二）昼度树经（初一日诵）

Parallel to: AN 7.69 / EĀ 39.2

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One addressed the bhikṣus, saying: "When the leaves of the Caitra tree of the Thirty-three Heavens wither and turn yellow, the Thirty-three Heavens are delighted and joyful, thinking that the leaves will soon fall. When the leaves of the Caitra tree of the Thirty-three Heavens have fallen, the Thirty-three Heavens are delighted and joyful, thinking that the leaves will soon grow back. When the leaves of the Caitra tree of the Thirty-three Heavens have grown back, the Thirty-three Heavens are delighted and joyful, thinking that the tree will soon grow a net-like canopy. When the Caitra tree of the Thirty-three Heavens has grown a net-like canopy, the Thirty-three Heavens are delighted and joyful, thinking that the tree will soon grow like a bird's beak. When the Caitra tree of the Thirty-three Heavens has grown like a bird's beak, the Thirty-three Heavens are delighted and joyful, thinking that the tree will soon open like a bowl. When the Caitra tree of the Thirty-three Heavens has opened like a bowl, the Thirty-three Heavens are delighted and joyful, thinking that the tree will soon fully bloom. When the Caitra tree of the Thirty-three Heavens has fully bloomed, its light shines, its colors reflect, and its fragrance permeates for a hundred yojanas. At that time, the Thirty-three Heavens spend four months of the summer there, fully enjoying the five heavenly pleasures and their merits. This is called the assembly and enjoyment under the Caitra tree of the Thirty-three Heavens.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「若三十三天昼度树叶萎黄，是时，三十三天悦乐欢喜，昼度树叶不久当落。复次，三十三天昼度树叶已落，是时，三十三天悦乐欢喜，昼度树叶不久当还生。复次，三十三天昼度树叶已还生，是时，三十三天悦乐欢喜，昼度树不久当生网。复次，三十三天昼度树已生网，是时，三十三天悦乐欢喜，昼度树不久当生如鸟喙。复次，三十三天昼度树已生如鸟喙，是时，三十三天悦乐欢喜，昼度树不久当开如钵。复次，三十三天昼度树已开如钵，是时，三十三天悦乐欢喜，昼度树不久当尽敷开。若昼度树已尽敷开，光所照、色所映、香所熏，周百由延，是时，三十三天于中夏四月，以天五欲功德具足而自娱乐，是谓三十三天于昼度树下集会娱乐也。

In the same way, the noble disciple also thinks of leaving home. At that time, the noble disciple is called 'yellow leaves', just like the withered and yellow leaves of the Caitra tree of the Thirty-three Heavens. Furthermore, the noble disciple shaves off his beard and hair, puts on the monastic robes, leaves home out of true faith, becomes a homeless one, and studies the path. At that time, the noble disciple is called 'fallen leaves', just like the fallen leaves of the Caitra tree of the Thirty-three Heavens. Furthermore, the noble disciple, having separated from desire and evil and unwholesome dharmas, with initial and sustained thought, attains the joy and bliss born of separation, and dwells having attained the first concentration. At that time, the noble disciple is called 'regrown leaves', just like the regrown leaves of the Caitra tree of the Thirty-three Heavens. Furthermore, the noble disciple, with the cessation of initial and sustained thought, with inner tranquility and one-pointedness of mind, without initial and sustained thought, with the arising of concentration, attains the joy and bliss of the second concentration, and dwells having attained it. At that time, the noble disciple is called 'grown net', just like the grown net of the Caitra tree of the Thirty-three Heavens. Furthermore, the noble disciple, having separated from the desire for joy, dwells without seeking, with right mindfulness and right knowledge, and experiences bodily bliss, which is said to be the noble relinquishment, the bliss of mindfulness, and the purity of emptiness, and attains the third concentration, and dwells having attained it. At that time, the noble disciple is called 'grown like a bird's beak', just like the Caitra tree of the Thirty-three Heavens growing like a bird's beak. Furthermore, the noble disciple, with the cessation of bliss and suffering, with the disappearance of joy and displeasure, without suffering and without bliss, with equanimity, with mindfulness and purity, attains the fourth concentration, and dwells having attained it. At that time, the noble disciple is called 'grown like a bowl', just like the Caitra tree of the Thirty-three Heavens growing like a bowl.

「如是义，圣弟子亦复尔，思念出家，是时圣弟子名为叶黄，犹三十三天昼度树叶萎黄也。复次，圣弟子剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，是时圣弟子名为叶落，犹三十三天昼度树叶落也。复次，圣弟子离欲、离恶不善之法，有觉、有观，离生喜乐，得初禅成就游，是时，圣弟子名叶还生，犹三十三天昼度树叶还生也。复次，圣弟子觉观已息，内静、一心，无觉无观，定生喜乐，得第二禅成就游，是时，圣弟子名为生网，犹三十三天昼度树生网也。复次，圣弟子离于喜欲，舍无求游，正念正智而身觉乐，谓圣所说、圣所舍、念、乐住、空，得第三禅成就游，是时，圣弟子名生如鸟喙，犹三十三天昼度树如鸟喙也。复次，圣弟子乐灭、苦灭，喜忧本已灭，不苦不乐、舍、念、清净，得第四禅成就游，是时，圣弟子名生如钵，犹三十三天昼度树如钵也。

Furthermore, the noble disciple, with all taints exhausted, attains liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom, and in this very life, he knows and realizes for himself, attains, and abides in the accomplishment of the goal. With birth exhausted, the holy life fulfilled, the task accomplished, no more to be, he knows and realizes for himself. At that time, the noble disciple is called 'fully bloomed', just like the fully bloomed Caitra tree of the Thirty-three Heavens. For the bhikṣu who has exhausted the taints, the Thirty-three Heavens gather in the Sudharma Hall and praise him, saying: 'The noble disciple so-and-so, in such-and-such village, shaved off his beard and hair, put on the monastic robes, left home out of true faith, became a homeless one, and studied the path. With all taints exhausted, he attains liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom, and in this very life, he knows and realizes for himself, attains, and abides in the accomplishment of the goal. With birth exhausted, the holy life fulfilled, the task accomplished, no more to be, he knows and realizes for himself.' This is called the assembly of those who have exhausted the taints, just like the assembly under the Caitra tree of the Thirty-three Heavens.

「复次，圣弟子诸漏已尽，心解脱、慧解脱，于现法中自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真，是时，圣弟子名尽敷开，犹三十三天昼度树尽敷开也。彼为漏尽阿罗诃比丘，三十三天集在善法正殿，咨嗟称叹：『某尊弟子于某村邑剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，诸漏已尽，心解脱、慧解脱，于现法中自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。』是谓漏尽阿罗诃共集会也，如三十三天昼度树下共集会也。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
