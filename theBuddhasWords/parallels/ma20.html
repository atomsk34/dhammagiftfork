MĀ 20

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 2 业相应品

To Pāṭaliya （二〇）波罗牢经

Parallel to: SN 42.13

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Kuru, together with a great assembly of bhikṣus. He went to the northern village and stayed in the Śiṣyagandha forest north of the northern village.

一时，佛游拘丽瘦，与大比丘众俱，往至北村，住北村北尸摄惒林中。

At that time, the householder Pāṭaliya heard that the śramaṇa Gautama, the son of the Śākya clan, had renounced his family and gone forth to practice the path. He was traveling in the country of Kuru, together with a great assembly of bhikṣus, and had arrived at the northern village, staying in the Śiṣyagandha forest north of the northern village. It was said of the śramaṇa Gautama: "He is a Tathāgata, an Arhat, a Perfectly Enlightened One, accomplished in knowledge and conduct, a Sugata, an unsurpassed knower of the world, a driver of tameable people, a teacher of gods and men, a Buddha, a Blessed One. He has realized for himself this world - with its gods, Māra, Brahmā, and the generation of recluses and brahmins, and this population with its gods and men - and he teaches it to others. He teaches the Dharma that is good in the beginning, good in the middle, and good in the end, with the right meaning and phrasing, revealing a holy life that is perfectly complete and purified. If one sees the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, and pays respect, honors, and makes offerings to him, it is a great gain. I should go and see the śramaṇa Gautama! Pay respect, honor, and make offerings to him!"

尔时，波罗牢伽弥尼闻沙门瞿昙释种子，舍释宗族，出家学道，游拘丽瘦，与大比丘众俱，至此北村，住北村北尸摄惒林中。彼沙门瞿昙有大名称，周闻十方，沙门瞿昙如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐。彼于此世，天及魔、梵、沙门、梵志，从人至天，自知自觉，自作证成就游，彼若说法，初善、中善、竟亦善，有义有文，具足清净，显现梵行。若见如来、无所著、等正觉，尊重礼拜，供养承事者，快得善利。彼作是念：「我应往见沙门瞿昙！礼事供养。」

Having heard this, Pāṭaliya left the northern village and went north to the Śiṣyagandha forest, wishing to see the World-Honored One, pay respect, and make offerings. From afar, Pāṭaliya saw the World-Honored One in the forest, upright and beautiful, like the moon among the stars, shining brightly, dazzling like a golden mountain, with perfect marks and majestic spiritual power. His faculties were tranquil and undisturbed, he had accomplished self-control, and his mind was at peace and silent. Having seen the Buddha from afar, Pāṭaliya went to where the Buddha was, exchanged greetings, and sat to one side. He said to the World-Honored One: "I have heard that the śramaṇa Gautama knows that illusions are illusions. Gautama! If one says this, is one not slandering the śramaṇa Gautama? Is what one says true? Is what one says the Dharma? Does what one say accord with the Dharma? Is there no fault or difficulty in what is in accordance with the Dharma?"

波罗牢伽弥尼闻已，从北村出，北行至尸摄惒林，欲见世尊礼事供养。波罗牢伽弥尼遥见世尊在林树间，端正姝好，犹星中月，光曜𬀩晔，晃若金山，相好具足，威神巍巍，诸根寂定，无有蔽碍，成就调御，息心静默。波罗牢伽弥尼遥见佛已，前至佛所，共相问讯，却坐一面，白世尊曰：「我闻沙门瞿昙知幻是幻，瞿昙！若如是说，沙门瞿昙知幻是幻，彼不谤毁沙门瞿昙耶？彼说真实耶？彼说是法耶？彼说法如法耶？于如法无过、无难诘耶？」

The World-Honored One replied: "Pāṭaliya! If one says this, one is not slandering the śramaṇa Gautama! What one says is true, what one says is the Dharma, what one says accords with the Dharma, and there is no fault or difficulty in what is in accordance with the Dharma. Why is that? Pāṭaliya! I know that illusion is illusion, but I myself am not an illusion."

世尊答曰：「伽弥尼！若如是说，沙门瞿昙知幻是幻，彼不谤毁沙门瞿昙！彼说真实，彼说是法，彼说法如法，于法无过，亦无难诘。所以者何？伽弥尼！我知彼幻，我自非幻。」

Pāṭaliya said: "What those śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas say is true, but I do not believe that they say that the śramaṇa Gautama knows that illusion is illusion."

波罗牢说曰：「彼沙门、梵志所说真实，而我不信彼说沙门瞿昙知幻是幻。」

The World-Honored One said: "Pāṭaliya! If one knows illusion, is that illusion?"

世尊告曰：「伽弥尼！若知幻者，即是幻耶？」

Pāṭaliya said: "Yes, World-Honored One! Yes, Well-Gone One!"

波罗牢白曰：「如是。世尊！如是。善逝！」

The World-Honored One said: "Pāṭaliya! Do not deceive yourself and slander me. If one slanders me, one will only harm oneself. There is dispute and transgression, which is detested by the sages and wise, and one will incur great sin. Why is that? This is not as you have said. Pāṭaliya! Have you heard of the death of Kūṭadanta?"

世尊告曰：「伽弥尼！汝莫自误，谤毁于我，若谤毁我者，则便自损。有诤有犯，圣贤所恶，而得大罪。所以者何？此实不如汝之所说。伽弥尼！汝闻拘丽瘦有卒耶？」

He replied: "I have heard of it."

答曰：「闻有。」

"Pāṭaliya! What do you think? Did Kūṭadanta use this death?"

「伽弥尼！于意云何？拘丽瘦用是卒为？」

He replied: "Gautama! He kept this soldier for the purpose of killing the thief."

答曰：「瞿昙！通使杀贼，为此事故，拘丽瘦畜是卒也。」

"Pāṭaliya! What do you think? Did Kūṭadanta's soldier have precepts or not?"

「伽弥尼！于意云何？拘丽瘦卒为有戒，为无戒耶？」

He replied: "Gautama! If there are those in the world who lack the virtue of precepts, none surpasses Kūṭadanta's soldier. Why is that? Kūṭadanta's soldier is extremely unpreceptful, only engaging in evil dharmas."

答曰：「瞿昙！若世间有无戒德者，无过拘丽瘦卒。所以者何？拘丽瘦卒极犯禁戒，唯行恶法。」

He further asked: "Pāṭaliya! You have such views and such knowledge. I will not ask you, but if others ask you: 'Pāṭaliya knows that Kūṭadanta's soldier is extremely unpreceptful, only engaging in evil dharmas. Because of this, Pāṭaliya is extremely unpreceptful, only engaging in evil dharmas.' If you speak like this, is it true?"

复问：「伽弥尼！汝如是见、如是知，我不问汝，若他问汝：『波罗牢伽弥尼知拘丽瘦卒极犯禁戒，唯行恶法，因此事故，波罗牢伽弥尼极犯禁戒，唯行恶法。』若如是说，为真说耶？」

He replied: "No, Gautama! Why is that? Kūṭadanta's soldier has different views, different desires, and different aspirations. Kūṭadanta's soldier is extremely unpreceptful, only engaging in evil dharmas; I am extremely preceptful, not engaging in evil dharmas."

答曰：「非也。瞿昙。所以者何？拘丽瘦卒见异、欲异，所愿亦异，拘丽瘦卒极犯禁戒，唯行恶法；我极持戒，不行恶法。」

He further asked: "Pāṭaliya! You know that Kūṭadanta's soldier is extremely unpreceptful, only engaging in evil dharmas, but you do not consider this to be unpreceptful, only engaging in evil dharmas. How can the Tathāgata not know illusion and yet not be illusory? Why is that? I know illusion, the person of illusion, the retribution of illusion, and the cessation of illusion. Pāṭaliya! I also know killing, the person who kills, the retribution of killing, and the cessation of killing. Pāṭaliya! I know taking what is not given, the person who takes what is not given, the retribution of taking what is not given, and the cessation of taking what is not given. Pāṭaliya! I know false speech, the person who speaks falsely, the retribution of false speech, and the cessation of false speech. Pāṭaliya! I know and see like this. If someone says that the śramaṇa Gautama knows illusion and is illusion, they have not yet abandoned this speech. Hearing their mind, their desire, their aspiration, their hearing, their thought, and their contemplation, in the time it takes to bend and stretch an arm, they will be reborn in hell."

复问：「伽弥尼！汝知拘丽瘦卒极犯禁戒，唯行恶法，然不以此为犯禁戒，唯行恶法；如来何以不得知幻而自非幻？所以者何？我知幻，知幻人，知幻报，知断幻。伽弥尼！我亦知杀生，知杀生人，知杀生报，知断杀生。伽弥尼！我知不与取，知不与取人，知不与取报，知断不与取。伽弥尼！我知妄言，知妄言人，知妄言报，知断妄言。伽弥尼！我如是知、如是见。若有作是说，沙门瞿昙知幻即是幻者，彼未断此语，闻彼心、彼欲、彼愿、彼闻、彼念、彼观，如屈伸臂顷，命终生地狱中。」

Pāṭaliya, having heard this, was frightened, terrified, and his body hair stood on end. He immediately rose from his seat, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, knelt on both knees with palms joined, and said to the World-Honored One: "I repent, Gautama! From now on, Well-Gone One! Like a fool, like a deluded one, like an unstable one, like an unwholesome one. Why is that? I falsely said that the śramaṇa Gautama is an illusion. I only wish that the Gautama would accept my repentance, see my faults and confess them. After I have repented, I will guard against doing it again."

波罗牢伽弥尼闻已，怖惧战栗，身毛皆竖，即从坐起，头面礼足，长跪叉手，白世尊曰：「悔过，瞿昙！自昔，善逝！如愚、如痴、如不定、如不善。所以者何？我以妄说沙门瞿昙是幻，唯愿瞿昙受我悔过，见罪发露，我悔过已，护不更作。」

The World-Honored One said: "So it is, Pāṭaliya! You really are like a fool, like a deluded one, like an unstable one, like an unwholesome one. Why is that? It is because you falsely said that the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One is an illusion. However, you are able to repent, see your faults and confess them, and guard against doing it again. So it is, Pāṭaliya! If one repents, sees one's faults and confesses them, and guards against doing it again, then one cultivates the holy Dharma and does not lose it."

世尊告曰：「如是，伽弥尼！汝实如愚、如痴、如不定、如不善。所以者何？谓汝于如来、无所著、等正觉妄说是幻。然汝能悔过，见罪发露，护不更作。如是，伽弥尼！若有悔过，见罪发露，护不更作者，则长养圣法而无有失。」

Then Pāṭaliya, with palms joined towards the Buddha, said to the World-Honored One: "Gautama! There are some śramaṇa-brāhmaṇas who hold this view and make this statement: 'If one kills living beings, they will all receive retribution in this very life, and because of that, they will give rise to sorrow and suffering. If one takes what is not given, or speaks falsely, they will all receive retribution in this very life, and because of that, they will give rise to sorrow and suffering.' What does the śramaṇa Gautama think about this?"

于是，波罗牢伽弥尼叉手向佛，白世尊曰：「瞿昙！有一沙门梵志如是见、如是说：『若有杀生者，彼一切即于现法受报，因彼生忧苦；若有不与取、妄言，彼一切即于现法受报，因彼生忧苦。』沙门瞿昙！于意云何？」

The World-Honored One said: "Pāṭaliya! I now ask you, answer as you see fit. Pāṭaliya! What do you think? In a village, if there is someone who wears a flower garland on their head, anoints their body with various fragrances, and entertains themselves with singing and dancing, only enjoying themselves as a female entertainer, as joyful as a king. If someone asks: 'What did this person do in the past, that now they wear a flower garland on their head, anoint their body with various fragrances, and entertain themselves with singing and dancing, only enjoying themselves as a female entertainer, as joyful as a king?' If someone answers: 'This person killed the enemy of the king, and the king was delighted, so he immediately bestowed rewards upon them. Therefore, this person wears a flower garland on their head, anoints their body with various fragrances, and entertains themselves with singing and dancing, only enjoying themselves as a female entertainer, as joyful as a king.' Pāṭaliya! Have you seen or heard this?"

世尊告曰：「伽弥尼！我今问汝，随所解答。伽弥尼！于意云何？若村邑中，或有一人，头冠华鬘，杂香涂身，而作倡乐，歌舞自娱，唯作女妓，欢乐如王。若有问者：『此人本作何等，今头冠华鬘，杂香涂身，而作倡乐，歌舞自娱，唯作女妓，欢乐如王？』或有答者：『此人为王杀害怨家，王欢喜已，即与赏赐，是以此人头冠华鬘，杂香涂身，而作倡乐，歌舞自娱，唯作女妓，欢乐如王。』伽弥尼！汝如是见、如是闻不？」

He replied: "I have seen it, Gautama! I have heard it, and I will hear it in the future."

答曰：「见也。瞿昙！已闻、当闻。」

"Pāṭaliya! You also see the king capturing criminals, binding their hands behind their back, beating drums and proclaiming it, taking them out the southern gate of the city, sitting them under a high pole to behead them. If someone asks: 'What crime did this person commit, that they are executed by the king?' If someone answers: 'This person unjustly killed an innocent person of the king's family. Therefore, the king ordered this execution.' Pāṭaliya! Have you seen or heard this?"

「伽弥尼！又复见王收捕罪人，反缚两手，打鼓唱令，出南城门，坐高标下而枭其首。若有问者：『此人何罪，为王所戮？』或有答者：『此人枉杀王家无过之人，是以王教如是行刑。』伽弥尼！汝如是见、如是闻不？」

He replied: "I have seen it, Gautama! I have heard it, and I will hear it in the future."

答曰：「见也。瞿昙！已闻、当闻。」

"Pāṭaliya! If there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who have such views and say: 'If there is killing, they will all receive retribution in this life and give rise to suffering because of it.' Is this true or false?"

「伽弥尼！若有沙门、梵志如是见、如是说：『若有杀生，彼一切即于现法受报，因彼生忧苦。』彼为真说，为虚妄言？」

He replied: "False, Gautama!"

答曰：「妄言。瞿昙！」

"If they say false words, do you believe them?"

「若彼说妄言，汝意信不？」

He replied: "I do not believe them, Gautama!"

答曰：「不信也。瞿昙！」

The World-Honored One praised: "Excellent! Excellent! Village Chief!" He then asked again: "Village Chief! What do you think? In a village, if there is a person who wears a flower crown, applies various fragrances to the body, performs music, sings and dances for entertainment, and only engages in female entertainment, enjoying happiness like a king. If someone asks: 'What did this person do in the past, that now they wear a flower crown, apply various fragrances to the body, perform music, sing and dance for entertainment, and only engage in female entertainment, enjoying happiness like a king?' If someone answers: 'This person did not give to others in another country, and therefore this person wears a flower crown, applies various fragrances to the body, performs music, sings and dances for entertainment, and only engages in female entertainment, enjoying happiness like a king.' Village Chief! Have you seen or heard this?"

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！伽弥尼！」复问：「伽弥尼！于意云何？若村邑中，或有一人，头冠华鬘，杂香涂身，而作倡乐，歌舞自娱，唯作女妓，欢乐如王。若有问者：『此人本作何等，今头冠华鬘，杂香涂身，而作倡乐，歌舞自娱，唯作女妓，欢乐如王？』或有答者：『此人于他国中而不与取，是以此人头冠华鬘，杂香涂身，而作倡乐，歌舞自娱，唯作女妓，欢乐如王。』伽弥尼！汝如是见、如是闻不？」

He replied: "I have seen it, Gautama! I have heard it, and I will hear it in the future."

答曰：「见也。瞿昙！已闻、当闻。」

"Village Chief! You have also seen the king capture criminals, tie their hands behind their backs, beat drums and proclaim it, take them out the southern gate of the city, and behead them under a high gallows. If someone asks: 'What crime did this person commit that they were executed by the king?' If someone answers: 'This person stole from the king's country, and therefore the king ordered this punishment.' Village Chief! Have you seen or heard this?"

「伽弥尼！又复见王收捕罪人，反缚两手，打鼓唱令，出南城门，坐高标下而枭其首。若有问者：『此人何罪，为王所戮？』或有答者：『此人于王国而不与取，是以王教如是行刑。』伽弥尼！汝如是见、如是闻不？」

He replied: "I have seen it, Gautama! I have heard it, and I will hear it in the future."

答曰：「见也。瞿昙！已闻、当闻。」

"Village Chief! If there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who have such views and say: 'If one steals, they will all receive retribution in this life and give rise to sorrow and suffering because of it,' are they speaking truthfully or falsely?"

「伽弥尼！若有沙门、梵志如是见、如是说：『若有不与取，彼一切即于现法受报，因彼生忧苦。』彼为真说，为虚妄言？」

He replied: "False words, Gautama!"

答曰：「妄言。瞿昙！」

"If they say false words, do you believe them?"

「若彼说妄言，汝意信不？」

He replied: "I do not believe them, Gautama!"

答曰：「不信也。瞿昙！」

The World-Honored One praised: "Excellent! Excellent! Village Chief!" He then asked again: "Village Chief! What do you think? In a village, if there is a person who wears a flower crown, applies various fragrances to the body, performs music, sings and dances for entertainment, and only entertains as a female entertainer, enjoying happiness like a king. If someone asks: 'What did this person do in the past, that now they wear a flower crown, apply various fragrances to the body, perform music, sing and dance for entertainment, and only entertain as a female entertainer, enjoying happiness like a king?' If someone answers: 'This person is a female entertainer, able to joke and laugh. They use false words to delight the king. When the king is delighted, they are rewarded. Therefore, this person wears a flower crown, applies various fragrances to the body, performs music, sings and dances for entertainment, and only entertains as a female entertainer, enjoying happiness like a king.' Village Chief! Have you seen or heard this?"

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！伽弥尼！」复问：「伽弥尼！于意云何？若村邑中，或有一人，头冠华鬘，杂香涂身，而作倡乐，歌舞自娱，唯作女妓，欢乐如王。若有问者：『此人本作何等，今头冠华鬘，杂香涂身，而作倡乐，歌舞自娱，唯作女妓，欢乐如王？』或有答者：『此人作妓，能戏调笑，彼以妄言令王欢喜，王欢喜已，即与赏赐，是以此人头冠华鬘，杂香涂身，而作倡乐，歌舞自娱，唯作女妓，欢乐如王。』伽弥尼！汝如是见、如是闻不？」

He replied: "I have seen it, Gautama! I have heard it, and I will hear it in the future."

答曰：「见也。瞿昙！已闻、当闻。」

"Village Chief! You have also seen the king capture criminals, beat them to death with sticks, place them in a wooden cage, load them on a cart, take them out through the north gate, and throw them into a ditch. If someone asks: 'What crime did this person commit that they were killed by the king?' If someone answers: 'This person falsely testified in front of the king. They deceived the king with false words. Therefore, the king ordered them to be treated like this.' Village Chief! Have you seen or heard this?"

「伽弥尼！又复见王收捕罪人，用棒打杀，盛以木槛，露车载之，出北城门，弃著壍中。若有问者：『此人何罪，为王所杀？』或有答者：『此人在王前妄有所证，彼以妄言欺诳于王，是以王教取作如是。』伽弥尼！汝如是见、如是闻不？」

He replied: "I have seen it, Gautama! I have heard it, and I will hear it in the future."

答曰：「见也。瞿昙！已闻、当闻。」

"Village Chief! What do you think? If there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who have such views and say: 'If there is false speech, they will all receive retribution in this very life, and it will cause them to experience sorrow and suffering.' Is that true or false?"

「伽弥尼！于意云何？若有沙门、梵志如是见、如是说：『若有妄言，彼一切即于现法受报，因彼生忧苦。』彼为真说，为虚妄言？」

He replied: "False speech, Gautama!"

「复次，伽弥尼！多闻圣弟子离杀断杀，断不与取、邪婬、妄言，至断邪见，得正见，彼于昼日教田作耕稼，至暮放息，入室坐定，过夜晓时而作是念：『我离杀断杀，断不与取、邪婬、妄言，至断邪见，得正见。』彼便自见，我断十恶业道，念十善业道。彼自见断十恶业道，念十善业道已，便生欢悦，生欢悦已，便生于喜，生于喜已，便止息身，止息身已，便身觉乐，身觉乐已，便得一心。伽弥尼！多闻圣弟子得一心已，则心与舍俱，遍满一方成就游。如是，二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。彼作是念：『若有沙门、梵志如是见、如是说，自作、教作，自断、教断，自煮、教煮，愁烦忧戚，搥胸懊恼，啼哭愚痴，杀生、不与取、邪婬、妄言、饮酒，穿墙开藏，至他巷劫，害村坏邑，破城灭国；作如是者，实为作恶。又以铁轮利如剃刀，彼于此地一切众生，于一日中斫截斩剉，剥裂剬割，作一肉段，一分一积，因是有恶业，因是有恶业报，恒水南岸杀、断、煮去，恒水北岸施与、作斋、呪说而求，因是有罪有福，因是有罪福报。施与、调御、守护、摄持、称誉、饶益，惠施、爱言、利及等利，因是有福，因是有福报。若沙门、梵志所说真实者，我不犯世怖与不怖，常当慈愍一切世间，我心不与众生共诤，无浊欢悦，我得无上人上之法，升进得乐居，谓远离法定。』彼于沙门、梵志所说不是不非，不是不非已，得内心止。伽弥尼！是谓法定，名曰远离，汝因此定，可得正念，可得一心，如是于现法便断疑惑，而得升进。」

When this teaching was spoken, the householder Pāyāsi's dust and dirt were removed, and the pure Dharma eye arose. Then the householder Pāyāsi saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, awakened to the pure Dharma, cut off doubt, transcended uncertainty, and would no longer follow others. He had no more hesitation, had attained the fruit of the path, and had attained fearlessness in the World-Honored One's teachings. He rose from his seat, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and said: "World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept me as a lay follower. From this day forward, until life ends, I take refuge with utmost sincerity."

说此法时，波罗牢伽弥尼远尘离垢，诸法净眼生。于是，波罗牢伽弥尼见法、得法，觉白净法，断疑度惑，更无余尊，不复从他，无有犹豫，已住果证，于世尊法得无所畏。即从坐起，稽首佛足，白曰：「世尊！我今自归佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Pāyāsi the householder and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced them.

佛说如是。波罗牢伽弥尼及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

The tenth chapter of the Pāyāsi Sūtra is completed.
