MĀ 201

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 16 大品

201. （二〇一）［口＊荼］帝经

Parallel to: MN 38 / EĀ 21.3 (~)

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the monk Kaudāyin, the son of Kaudā, gave rise to such an evil view: "I know that the World-Honored One taught the Dharma like this: 'This consciousness goes to rebirth without changing.'" When the monks heard this, they went to Kaudāyin and asked: "Kaudāyin, is it true that you said: 'I know that the World-Honored One taught the Dharma like this: "This consciousness goes to rebirth without changing."'"

尔时，［口＊荼］帝比丘鸡和哆子生如是恶见：「我知世尊如是说法：『今此识，往生不更异。』」诸比丘闻已，往至［口＊荼］帝比丘所，问曰：「［口＊荼］帝！汝实如是说：『我知世尊如是说法：「今此识，往生不更异耶？」』」

Kaudāyin replied: "Virtuous ones, I really know that the World-Honored One taught the Dharma like this: 'This consciousness goes to rebirth without changing.'"

［口＊荼］帝比丘答曰：「诸贤！我实知世尊如是说法：『今此识，往生不更异。』」

At that time, the monks rebuked Kaudāyin, saying: "Do not say that! Do not slander the World-Honored One. Slandering the World-Honored One is not good. The World-Honored One did not teach like that. Kaudāyin, this consciousness arises due to conditions. The World-Honored One taught countless skillful means about the conditions for the arising of consciousness. With conditions, it arises; without conditions, it ceases. Kaudāyin, you should quickly abandon this evil view."

时，诸比丘诃［口＊荼］帝比丘曰：「汝莫作是说，莫诬谤世尊，诬谤世尊者不善，世尊亦不如是说。［口＊荼］帝比丘！今此识，因缘故起，世尊无量方便说识因缘故起，有缘则生，无缘则灭。［口＊荼］帝比丘！汝可速舍此恶见也。」

After being rebuked by the monks, Kaudāyin strongly adhered to this evil view and insisted: "This is true, the rest is false." He said this three times.

［口＊荼］帝比丘为诸比丘所诃已，如此恶见其强力执，而一向说：「此是真实，余者虚妄。」如是再三。

The many monks were unable to make Kaudāyin abandon this evil view. They rose from their seats and went to where the Buddha was. They bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, then sat to one side and said: "World-Honored One, Kaudāyin has given rise to this evil view: 'I know that the World-Honored One teaches the Dharma like this: "This consciousness goes to rebirth without changing."'" World-Honored One, after hearing this, we went to where Kaudāyin was and asked: "Kaudāyin, is it true that you say: 'I know that the World-Honored One teaches the Dharma like this: "This consciousness goes to rebirth without changing."'" Kaudāyin replied to us: 'Venerable ones, I truly know that the World-Honored One teaches the Dharma like this: "This consciousness goes to rebirth without changing."'" World-Honored One, we rebuked him, saying: "Kaudāyin, do not say that! Do not slander the World-Honored One. Slandering the World-Honored One is not good. The World-Honored One did not teach like that. Kaudāyin, this consciousness arises due to conditions. The World-Honored One taught countless skillful means about the conditions for the arising of consciousness. Consciousness arises with conditions, ceases without conditions. Kaudāyin, you should quickly abandon this evil view." We rebuked him, but this evil view was strongly adhered to, and he insisted: 'This is true, the rest is false.' He said this three times. World-Honored One, we were unable to make Kaudāyin abandon this evil view. We rose from our seats and left."

众多比丘不能令［口＊荼］帝比丘舍此恶见，从坐起去，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，却坐一面，白曰：「世尊！［口＊荼］帝比丘生如是恶见：『我知世尊如是说法：「今此识，往生不更异。」』世尊！我等闻已，往诣［口＊荼］帝比丘所，问曰：『［口＊荼］帝！汝实如是说：「我知世尊如是说法：『今此识，往生不更异也。』」』［口＊荼］帝比丘答我等曰：『诸贤！我实知世尊如是说法：「今此识，往生不更异。」』世尊！我等诃曰：『［口＊荼］帝比丘！汝莫作是说，莫诬谤世尊，诬谤世尊者不善，世尊亦不如是说。［口＊荼］帝比丘！今此识，因缘故起，世尊无量方便说识因缘故起，识有缘则生，无缘则灭。［口＊荼］帝比丘！汝可速舍此恶见也。』我等诃已，如此恶见其强力执，而一向说：『此是真实，余者虚妄。』如是再三。世尊！如我等不能令［口＊荼］帝比丘舍此恶见，从坐起去。」

The World-Honored One, having heard this, told a monk: "Go to Kaudāyin and say: 'The World-Honored One calls you.'"

世尊闻已，告一比丘：「汝往［口＊荼］帝比丘所，作如是语：『世尊呼汝！』」

Then a monk, having received the World-Honored One's instruction, rose from his seat, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and left. He went to where Kaudāyin was and said: "The World-Honored One calls you!" Kaudāyin then went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side.

于是，一比丘受世尊教，即从坐起，稽首佛足，绕三匝而去，至［口＊荼］帝比丘所，即语彼曰：「世尊呼汝！」［口＊荼］帝比丘即诣佛所，稽首佛足，却坐一面。

The World-Honored One asked: "Is it true that you said: 'I know that the World-Honored One teaches the Dharma like this: "This consciousness goes to rebirth without changing."'"

世尊问曰：「汝实如是说：『我知世尊如是说法：「今此识，往生不更异也。」』」

Kaudāyin replied: "World-Honored One! I truly know that the World-Honored One teaches the Dharma like this: 'This consciousness goes to rebirth without changing.'"

［口＊荼］帝比丘答曰：「世尊！我实知世尊如是说法：『今此识，往生不更异也。』」

The World-Honored One asked: "What is consciousness?"

世尊问曰：「何者识耶？」

Kaudāyin replied: "World-Honored One! It is said that this consciousness is cognition, action, instigation, arising, and equal arising. It is said that one experiences the results of good and bad actions."

［口＊荼］帝比丘答曰：「世尊！谓此识说、觉、作、教作、起、等起，谓彼作善恶业而受报也。」

The World-Honored One rebuked him: "Kaudāyin! How do you know that I teach the Dharma like this? From whose mouth did you hear me teach the Dharma like this? You foolish man! I do not teach one-sidedly, yet you teach one-sidedly. You foolish man! When you hear the monks criticizing you, you should answer according to the Dharma: 'I will now ask the monks.'"

世尊呵曰：「［口＊荼］帝！汝云何知我如是说法？汝从何口闻我如是说法？汝愚痴人！我不一向说，汝一向说耶？汝愚痴人！闻诸比丘共诃汝时，应如法答：『我今当问诸比丘也。』」

Then the World-Honored One asked the monks: "Do you also know that I teach the Dharma like this: 'Now this consciousness is reborn without changing?'"

于是，世尊问诸比丘：「汝等亦如是知我如是说法：『今此识，往生不更异耶？』」

The monks replied: "No, we do not."

时，诸比丘答曰：「不也。」

The World-Honored One asked: "How do you know that I teach the Dharma?"

世尊问曰：「汝等云何知我说法？」

The monks replied: "We know that the World-Honored One teaches the Dharma like this: 'Consciousness arises due to conditions. The World-Honored One says that consciousness arises due to conditions. When there are conditions, consciousness arises. When there are no conditions, it ceases.' We know that the World-Honored One teaches the Dharma like this."

诸比丘答曰：「我等知世尊如是说法，识因缘故起，世尊说识因缘故起，识有缘则生，无缘则灭，我等知世尊如是说法。」

The World-Honored One praised: "Excellent! Excellent! Monks, you understand my teaching of the Dharma like this. Why is that? Because I also teach like this: 'Consciousness arises due to conditions.' I say that consciousness arises due to conditions. When there are conditions, consciousness arises. When there are no conditions, it ceases. Consciousness arises based on conditions. I say that consciousness arises based on conditions. It is like fire, which arises based on conditions. I say that fire arises based on wood. I say that fire arises based on grass and dung. In the same way, consciousness arises based on conditions. I say that consciousness arises based on conditions. It is like the eye and form giving rise to consciousness. Having arisen, I say it is eye consciousness. In the same way, the ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind and dharmas give rise to consciousness. Having arisen, I say it is mental consciousness."

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！诸比丘！汝等知我如是说法。所以者何？我亦如是说，识因缘故起，我说识因缘故起，识有缘则生，无缘则灭，识随所缘生，即彼缘。说缘眼色生识，生识已说眼识，如是耳、鼻、舌、身，意法生识，生识已说意识。犹若如火，随所缘生，即彼缘，说缘木生火，说木火也。缘草粪聚火，说草粪聚火。如是识随所缘生，即彼缘，说缘眼色生识，生识已说眼识。如是耳、鼻、舌、身，缘意法生识，生识已说意识。」

The World-Honored One praised: "Excellent! Excellent! You understand my teaching of the Dharma like this. However, this Kudaṭṭhā bhikkhu is a foolish person who has misunderstood the meaning and the words. Because of his own misunderstanding, he falsely slanders me, harming himself, committing an offense and incurring blame, disliked by wise and virtuous practitioners, and incurring great sin. You foolish person! Do you know about this evil and unwholesome state?"

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！汝等知我如是说法。然此［口＊荼］帝比丘愚痴之人，颠倒受解义及文也。彼因自颠倒受解故，诬谤于我，为自伤害，有犯有罪，诸智梵行者所不喜也，而得大罪。汝愚痴人！知有此恶不善处耶？」

Then, after being directly rebuked by the World-Honored One, the Kudaṭṭhā bhikkhu was inwardly distressed, lowered his head in silence, lost his eloquence, and had nothing to say.

于是，［口＊荼］帝比丘为世尊面呵责已，内怀忧戚，低头默然，失辩无言，如有所伺。

Then, after directly rebuking the Kudaṭṭhā bhikkhu, the World-Honored One told the bhikkhus: "I will teach you the Dharma that is ultimate, free from afflictions and heat, eternally unchanging, and seen by all wise ones. Listen well, listen well, and consider it carefully." At that time, the bhikkhus accepted the teaching and listened.

于是，世尊面呵［口＊荼］帝比丘已，告诸比丘：「我当为汝说法究竟，无烦无热，恒有不变，诸智慧观如是，谛听，谛听，善思念之。」时，诸比丘受教而听。

The Buddha said, "Do you see the true teaching?"

佛言：「真说见耶？」

The bhikkhu replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「唯然。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "Do you see the Tathāgata's true teaching?"

世尊告曰：「如来真说见耶？」

The bhikkhu replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「唯然。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "After the Tathāgata's extinction, do you see that all that is true will also be extinguished?"

世尊告曰：「如来灭已，所有真彼亦灭法，见耶？」

The bhikkhu replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「唯然。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "Have you seen the true teaching?"

世尊告曰：「真说已见耶？」

The bhikkhu replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「唯然。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "Have you seen the Tathāgata's true teaching?"

世尊告曰：「如来真说已见耶？」

The bhikkhu replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「唯然。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "After the Tathāgata's extinction, have you seen the true Dharma that also becomes extinct?"

世尊告曰：「如来灭已，所有真彼亦灭法，已见耶？」

The bhikkhu replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「唯然。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "Is the true teaching free from doubt?"

世尊告曰：「真说无有疑惑耶？」

The bhikkhu replied, "No, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「不也。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "Is the Tathāgata's true teaching free from doubt?"

世尊告曰：「如来真说无有疑惑耶？」

The bhikkhu replied, "No, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「不也。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "After the Tathāgata's parinirvana, will the true Dharma also be extinguished without doubt?"

世尊告曰：「如来灭已，所有真彼亦灭法，无有疑惑耶？」

The bhikkhu replied, "No, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「不也。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "The true teaching is like this. The wisdom that sees the truth, will those doubts be extinguished?"

世尊告曰：「真说如是，慧见如真，所有疑惑彼灭耶？」

The bhikkhu replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「唯然。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "The Tathāgata's true teaching is like this. The wisdom that sees the truth, will those doubts be extinguished?"

世尊告曰：「如来真说如是，慧见如真，所有疑惑彼灭耶？」

The bhikkhu replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「唯然。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "After the Tathāgata's parinirvana, all that is true will also cease to exist. The wisdom that sees the truth like this, will those doubts be extinguished?"

世尊告曰：「如来灭已，所有真彼亦灭法，如是慧见如真，所有疑惑彼灭耶？」

The bhikkhu replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「唯然。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "Is there no doubt about the true teaching?"

世尊告曰：「真说已无疑惑耶？」

The bhikkhu replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「唯然。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "Is there no doubt about the Tathāgata's true teaching?"

世尊告曰：「如来真说已无疑惑耶？」

The bhikkhu replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「唯然。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "The Tathāgata has already passed away, and all that is true of him has also passed away. Is there no doubt about this?"

世尊告曰：「如来已灭，所有真彼亦灭法，已无疑惑耶？」

The bhikkhu replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「唯然。世尊！」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Excellent! If you know and see in this way, and say that your view is thus pure, and you cling to it, cherish it, and guard it, not wanting to let go of it, do you know that I have long taught the parable of the raft, and having understood it, have you opened the blocked flow?"

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！若汝等如是知、如是见，谓我此见如是清净，著彼、惜彼、守彼，不欲令舍者，汝等知我长夜说栰喻法，知已所塞流开耶？」

The bhikkhu replied, "No, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「不也。世尊！」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Excellent! If you know and see in this way, and say that your view is thus pure, you do not cling to it, do not cherish it, do not guard it, and want to let go of it. Do you know that I have long taught the parable of the raft, and having understood it, have you opened the blocked flow?"

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！若汝等如是知、如是见，谓我此见如是清净，不著彼、不惜彼、不守彼，欲令舍者，汝等知我长夜说栰喻法，知已所塞流开耶？」

The bhikkhu replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘答曰：「唯然。世尊！」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Excellent! If a non-Buddhist scholar comes and asks you, 'Virtuous ones! If you have such a pure view, what is its meaning, what is its purpose, what are its merits?' How would you answer?"

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！若有异学来问汝等：『贤者！汝等若有如是清净见，彼何义、何为、何功德？』汝等云何答耶？」

The bhikkhu replied, "World-Honored One! If a non-Buddhist scholar comes and asks me, 'Virtuous ones! If you have such a pure view, what is its meaning, what is its purpose, what are its merits?' I would answer like this: 'Virtuous ones! It is for the meaning of disgust, the meaning of desirelessness, the meaning of seeing and knowing as it truly is.' World-Honored One! If a non-Buddhist scholar asks me, I would answer like this."

比丘答曰：「世尊！若有异学来问我：『贤者！汝等若有如是清净见，彼何义、何为、何功德？』者，我等当如是答：『诸贤！为厌义，为无欲义，为见知如真义故。』世尊！若异学来问我者，我等当如是答。」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Excellent! If a non-Buddhist scholar asks you, you should answer like this. Why is that? The contemplation spoken of here is as follows: the first is coarse and fine food, the second is contact, the third is mental attention, and the fourth is consciousness. What are the causes and conditions of these four kinds of food? From what do they arise? Due to what do they exist? Those four kinds of food are caused by craving, conditioned by craving, arise from craving, and exist due to craving. What are the causes and conditions of craving? From what does it arise? Due to what does it exist? Craving is caused by perception, conditioned by perception, arises from perception, and exists due to perception. What are the causes and conditions of perception? From what does it arise? Due to what does it exist? Perception is caused by contact, conditioned by contact, arises from contact, and exists due to contact. What are the causes and conditions of contact? From what does it arise? Due to what does it exist? Contact is caused by the six sense bases, conditioned by the six sense bases, arises from the six sense bases, and exists due to the six sense bases. What are the causes and conditions of the six sense bases? From what do they arise? Due to what do they exist? The six sense bases are caused by name and form, conditioned by name and form, arise from name and form, and exist due to name and form. What are the causes and conditions of name and form? From what do they arise? Due to what do they exist? Name and form are caused by consciousness, conditioned by consciousness, arise from consciousness, and exist due to consciousness. What are the causes and conditions of consciousness? From what does it arise? Due to what does it exist? Consciousness is caused by formations, conditioned by formations, arises from formations, and exists due to formations. What are the causes and conditions of formations? From what do they arise? Due to what do they exist? Formations are caused by ignorance, conditioned by ignorance, arise from ignorance, and exist due to ignorance.

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！若异学来问汝，汝等应如是答。所以者何？此所说观，一曰抟食麤细，二曰更乐，三曰意念，四曰识也。此四食何因、何习？从何而生？由何有耶？彼四食者，因爱、习爱，从爱而生，由爱有也。爱何因、何习，从何而生？由何有耶？爱者，因觉、习觉，从觉而生，由觉有也。觉何因、何习？从何而生？由何有耶？觉者，因更乐、习更乐，从更乐生，由更乐有也。更乐何因、何习？从何而生？由何有耶？更乐者，因六处、习六处，从六处生，由六处有也。六处何因、何习？从何而生？由何有耶？六处者，因名色、习名色，从名色生，由名色有也。名色何因、何习？从何而生？由何有耶？名色者，因识习识，从识而生，由识有也。识何因、何习？从何而生？由何有耶？识者，因行、习行，从行而生，由行有也。行何因、何习？从何而生？由何有耶？行者，因无明、习无明，从无明生，由无明有也。

"This is called: due to ignorance there are formations, due to formations there is consciousness, due to consciousness there is name and form, due to name and form there are the six sense bases, due to the six sense bases there is contact, due to contact there is feeling, due to feeling there is craving, due to craving there is grasping, due to grasping there is becoming, due to becoming there is birth, due to birth there is old age and death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, grief, and despair. Thus this great mass of suffering arises. Due to birth there is old age and death. What do you think about this?"

「是为缘无明有行，缘行有识，缘识有名色，缘名色有六处，缘六处有更乐，缘更乐有觉，缘觉有爱，缘爱有受，缘受有有，缘有有生，缘生有老死、愁戚、啼哭、忧苦、懊恼，如是此等大苦阴生。缘生有老死，此说缘生有老死，于汝等意云何？」

The bhikṣus replied, "World-Honored One! We think that due to birth there is old age and death. Why is that? Because due to birth there is old age and death."

比丘答曰：「世尊！缘生有老死，我等意如是。所以者何？缘生有老死也。」

"Due to becoming there is birth. This is said to be due to becoming there is birth. What do you think about this?"

Therefore, I previously said, 'I teach the Dharma for you, to the ultimate, free from afflictions and heat, eternally unchanging, to be known, seen, and realized by right knowledge.' Furthermore, when three conditions come together, one enters the mother's womb: the parents come together, the mother is fertile and capable, and the fragrant breath has arrived. When these three conditions come together, one enters the mother's womb. The mother may carry the child for nine or ten months before giving birth. After birth, the child is nourished with blood. In the noble Dharma, this is called breast milk. Later, as the child's faculties grow and mature, they eat coarse rice, millet, and are anointed with ghee and oil. They see forms with the eyes, delighting in beautiful forms and disliking ugly forms. They do not establish mindfulness of the body, limited mental liberation, and wisdom liberation. They do not know the truth, and the evil and unwholesome dharmas that arise are not extinguished without remainder, and do not perish without remainder. Similarly, they experience sounds with the ears, smells with the nose, tastes with the tongue, tactile objects with the body, and dharmas with the mind, delighting in good dharmas and disliking bad dharmas. They do not establish mindfulness of the body, limited mental liberation, and wisdom liberation. They do not know the truth, and the evil and unwholesome dharmas that arise are not extinguished without remainder, and do not perish without remainder. They experience pleasant, painful, or neither-pleasant-nor-painful feelings according to their likes and dislikes. They seek and cling to those feelings, and having sought and clung to them, if they experience pleasant feelings, that is feeling. Based on feeling, there is clinging. Based on clinging, there is existence. Based on existence, there is birth. Based on birth, there is aging and death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, sadness, and distress arise. Thus, this great mass of suffering arises. Bhikṣus, is this not the complete continuity of attachment and bondage, like the bhikṣu Kīṭāgiri's son?"

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！我正御汝等，于毕究竟无烦无热，恒不变易法，正智所知、正智所见、正智所觉，因此故我向者说，我为汝说法，毕究竟不烦热，恒不变易法，正智所知、正智所见、正智所觉。复次三事合会入于母胎，父母聚集一处、母满精堪耐、香阴已至。此三事合会入于母胎，母胎或持九月十月便] cf.［丽】生，生已以血长养，血者于圣法中，谓是母乳也。彼于后时诸根转大、根转成就，食麤饭𪎊苏油涂身，彼眼见色，乐著好色、憎恶恶色，不立身念少心心解脱、慧解脱，不知如真，所生恶不善法，不灭尽无余，不败坏无余。如是耳、鼻、舌、身、意知法，乐著好法、憎恶恶法，不立身念少心心解脱、慧解脱，不知如真，所生恶不善法，不灭尽无余，不败坏无余。彼如是随憎不憎所受觉，或乐或苦或不苦不乐，彼乐彼觉求著受彼觉，彼乐彼觉求著受彼觉已，若乐觉者是为受，彼缘受有有，缘有有生，缘生有老死，愁戚啼哭忧苦懊恼可得生，如是此淳大苦阴生。比丘！非为具足爱所系相续，如［口＊荼］帝比丘鸡和哆子耶？」

The bhikṣus replied, "Yes, World-Honored One! The complete continuity of attachment and bondage is like the bhikṣu Kīṭāgiri's son. When a Tathāgata appears in the world, an Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, with perfect knowledge and conduct, a Sugata, knower of the world, unsurpassed trainer of those who can be trained, teacher of gods and humans, a Buddha, a Blessed One, he sees forms with the eyes, not delighting in beautiful forms, not disliking ugly forms, establishing mindfulness of the body, immeasurable mind, mental liberation, and wisdom liberation, knowing the truth, the evil and unwholesome dharmas that arise are extinguished without remainder, and perish without remainder. Similarly, he experiences sounds with the ears, smells with the nose, tastes with the tongue, tactile objects with the body, and dharmas with the mind, not delighting in good dharmas, not disliking bad dharmas, establishing mindfulness of the body, immeasurable mind, mental liberation, and wisdom liberation, knowing the truth, the evil and unwholesome dharmas that arise are extinguished without remainder, and perish without remainder. He experiences pleasant, painful, or neither-pleasant-nor-painful feelings according to his likes and dislikes. He does not delight in those feelings, does not seek or cling to them. Having not sought or clung to them, if he experiences pleasant feelings, they cease. With the cessation of feeling, there is the cessation of clinging. With the cessation of clinging, there is the cessation of existence. With the cessation of existence, there is the cessation of birth. With the cessation of birth, there is the cessation of aging and death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, sadness, and distress cease. Thus, this great mass of suffering ceases."

比丘答曰：「如是，世尊！具足爱所系相续，如［口＊荼］帝比丘鸡和哆子也。若时如来出世，无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐。彼眼见色，于好色而不乐著，于恶色而不憎恶，立身念无量心，心解脱、慧解脱，知如真，所生恶不善法，灭尽无余，败坏无余。如是耳、鼻、舌、身、意知法，不著好法、不恶恶法，立身念无量心，心解脱、慧解脱，知如真，所生恶不善法，灭尽无余，败坏无余。彼如是灭憎不憎所受觉，或乐或苦或不苦不乐，彼不乐彼觉，不求不著不受觉，彼不乐彼觉，不求不著不受觉已，若乐觉者彼便灭，乐灭则受灭，受灭则有灭，有灭则生灭，生灭则老死灭，愁戚啼哭忧苦懊恼可得灭，如是此淳大苦阴灭。」

"Bhikṣus, is this not the complete cessation of attachment and liberation?"

「比丘！非为具足爱尽解脱耶？」

The bhikṣus replied, "Yes, World-Honored One! The complete cessation of attachment and liberation is like the bhikṣu Kīṭāgiri's son."

比丘答曰：「如是，世尊！具足爱尽解脱也。」

When this teaching was spoken, this trichiliocosm shook three times, moved, shook completely, trembled, trembled completely, and quaked, quaked completely. Therefore, this sūtra is called the Cessation of Attachment and Liberation.

说是法时，此三千大千世界三反震动，动、尽动，战、尽战，震、尽震。是故此经称爱尽解脱。

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and upheld it.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

The Tenth Chapter of the Kuda-ti Sūtra is completed.
