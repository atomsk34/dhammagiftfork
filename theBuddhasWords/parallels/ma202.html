Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 17 晡利多品

202. （二〇二）持斋经

Parallel to: AN 3.70 / AN 8.42 (~) / AN 8.43 (~) / EĀ 24.6 (~)

Upavāna, Upāli, Rāma, five lower
fetters, arrow-hair, two, Sumagadha, to
study the Dharma, nun, Kūṭadanta later

持斋、晡利多　　罗摩、五下分心秽、箭毛二　　随摩那修学法乐比丘尼　　拘𫄨罗在后

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Deer Mother's Hall in the Eastern Garden.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在于东园鹿子母堂。

At that time, the laywoman Visākhā bathed early in the morning, put on clean white clothes, and was surrounded by her daughters-in-law and relatives. She went to where the Buddha was, bowed her head at his feet, and stood to one side. The World-Honored One asked, "Laywoman, are you bathing now?"

尔时，鹿子母毘舍佉平旦沐浴，著白净衣，将子妇等眷属围绕，往诣佛所，稽首作礼，却住一面。世尊问曰：「居士妇！今沐浴耶？」

She replied, "World-Honored One! I am now fasting. Well-Gone One! I am now fasting."

答曰：「世尊！我今持斋。善逝！我今持斋。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Laywoman, what kind of fast are you observing now? There are three kinds of fasts. What are the three? First, the fast of a cowherd, second, the fast of a Nirgrantha, and third, the fast of the eight noble limbs. Laywoman, what is called the fast of a cowherd? If a cowherd lets the cows graze in the fields in the morning and collects them back to the village in the evening, when returning to the village, he thinks like this: 'Today I let the cows graze here, tomorrow I will let them graze there. Today I let the cows drink here, tomorrow I will let them drink there. Today the cows stay here, tomorrow they will stay there.' Laywoman, if a person observes the fast like this, he thinks like this: 'Today I eat such food, tomorrow I will eat such food. Today I drink such drink, tomorrow I will drink such drink. Today I digest such digestion, tomorrow I will digest such digestion.' That person indulges in desires day and night, this is called the fast of a cowherd. If one observes the fast like this, the fast of a cowherd, one does not attain great benefit, does not attain great fruit, does not have great merit, and does not attain widespread practice.

世尊问曰：「居士妇！今持何等斋耶？斋有三种。云何为三？一者放牛儿斋，二者尼揵斋，三者圣八支斋。居士妇！云何名为放牛儿斋？若放牛儿朝放泽中，晡收还村，彼还村时，作如是念：『我今日在此处放牛，明日当在彼处放牛。我今日在此处饮牛，明日当在彼处饮牛。我牛今在此处宿止，明日当在彼处宿止。』居士妇！如是有人若持斋时，作是思惟：『我今日食如此之食，明日当食如彼食也。我今日饮如此之饮，明日当饮如彼饮也。我今含消如此含消，明日含消如彼含消。』其人于此昼夜乐著欲过，是谓名曰放牛儿斋。若如是持斋放牛儿斋者，不获大利，不得大果，无大功德，不得广布。

Laywoman, what is called the fast of a Nirgrantha? If someone has renounced the world to study the Nirgrantha teachings, they will encourage others, saying, 'Beyond a hundred yojanas to the east, there are sentient beings. For their sake, abandon weapons. Likewise, to the south, west, and north, beyond a hundred yojanas, there are sentient beings. For their sake, abandon weapons.' This is how they encourage others. They may protect sentient beings with thoughts, or without thoughts, they may not protect sentient beings. On the fifteenth day, when they speak of liberation, they should take off their clothes, stand naked facing east, and say, 'I have no parents, nor do I belong to parents. I have no wife or children, nor do I belong to wife or children. I have no slaves, nor do I belong to slaves.' Laywoman, they want to encourage true words, but instead they encourage false words. They see their parents every day and think, 'They are my parents.' The parents see their children every day and think, 'They are our children.' They see their wife and children and think, 'They are my wife and children.' The wife and children see them and think, 'They are our elders.' They see their slaves and think, 'They are my slaves.' The slaves see them and think, 'They are our masters.' They use this desire without giving, not giving, this is called the fast of a Nirgrantha. If one observes the fast like this, the fast of a Nirgrantha, one does not attain great benefit, does not attain great fruit, does not have great merit, and does not attain widespread practice.

「居士妇！云何名为尼揵斋耶？若有出家学尼揵者，彼劝人曰：『汝于东方过百由延外有众生者，拥护彼故，弃舍刀杖，如是南方、西方、北方过百由延外有众生者，拥护彼故，弃舍刀杖，是为彼劝进人。或有想护众生，或无想不护众生，汝当十五日说从解脱时，脱衣裸形，东向住立，作如是说：「我无父母，非父母有，我无妻子，非妻子有，我无奴婢，非奴婢生。」』居士妇！彼欲] cf.［丽】劝进于真谛语，而反劝进虚妄之言，彼人日日见其父母，便作此念：『是我父母。』父母日日见其儿子，亦作此念：『是我儿子。』彼见妻子，而作此念：『是我妻子。』妻子见彼，亦作此念：『是我尊长。』彼见奴婢，复作此念：『是我奴婢。』奴婢见彼，亦作此念：『是我大家。』彼用此欲，不与而用，非是与用，是谓名曰尼揵斋也。若如是持尼揵斋者，不获大利，不得大果，无大功德，不得广布。

Laywoman, what is called the fast of the eight noble limbs? When a learned noble disciple observes the fast, they think like this: 'The arhat truly renounces killing for life, abandons killing, puts down weapons, has a sense of shame and conscience, has a compassionate mind, benefits all beings, even insects. They purify their mind from killing. I also renounce killing for life, abandon killing, put down weapons, have a sense of shame and conscience, have a compassionate mind, benefit all beings, even insects. I now purify my mind from killing. I am equal to the arhat in this limb.' Therefore, it is called the fast.

「居士妇！云何名为圣八支斋？多闻圣弟子若持斋时，作是思惟：『阿罗诃真人尽形寿离杀、断杀，弃舍刀杖，有慙有愧，有慈悲心，饶益一切，乃至蜫虫，彼于杀生净除其心，我亦尽形寿离杀、断杀，弃舍刀杖，有慙有愧，有慈悲心，饶益一切，乃至蜫虫。我今于杀生净除其心，我以此支于阿罗诃等同无异。』是故说斋。

Furthermore, laywoman, when a learned noble disciple observes the fast, they think like this: 'The arhat truly renounces stealing for life, abandons stealing, takes only what is given, takes with joy, always likes to give, delights in letting go, rejoices without stinginess, does not expect reward, does not cover the mind with theft, is able to restrain oneself. They purify their mind from stealing. I also renounce stealing for life, abandon stealing, take only what is given, take with joy, always like to give, delight in letting go, rejoice without stinginess, do not expect reward, do not cover the mind with theft, am able to restrain myself. I purify my mind from stealing. I am equal to the arhat in this limb.' Therefore, it is called the fast.

「复次，居士妇！多闻圣弟子若持斋时，作是思惟：『阿罗诃真人尽形寿离不与取、断不与取，与而后取，乐于与取，常好布施，心乐放舍，欢喜无恡，不望其报，不以盗覆心，能自制己。彼于不与取净除其心，我亦尽形寿离不与取、断不与取，与而后取，乐于与取，常好布施，心乐放舍，欢喜无恡，不望其报，不以盗覆心，能自制己。我于不与取净除其心，我以此支于阿罗诃等同无异。』是故说斋。

Furthermore, laywoman, when a learned noble disciple observes the fast, they think like this: 'The arhat truly renounces sexual misconduct for life, abandons sexual misconduct, practices pure conduct, with sincere and pure mind, free from defilement, renounces desire and breaks sexual ties. They purify their mind from sexual misconduct. I also renounce sexual misconduct for this day and night, abandon sexual misconduct, practice pure conduct, with sincere and pure mind, free from defilement, renounce desire and break sexual ties. I purify my mind from sexual misconduct. I am equal to the arhat in this limb.' Therefore, it is called the fast.

「复次，居士妇！多闻圣弟子若持斋时，作是思惟：『阿罗诃真人尽形寿离非梵行、断非梵行，修行梵行，至诚心净，行无臭秽，离欲断婬。彼于非梵行净除其心，我于此日此夜离非梵行、断非梵行，修行梵行，至诚心净，行无臭秽，离欲断婬。我于非梵行净除其心，我以此支于阿罗诃等同无异。』是故说斋。

Furthermore, laywoman, when a learned noble disciple observes the fast, they think like this: 'The arhat truly renounces false speech for life, abandons false speech, speaks the truth, delights in the truth, abides in the truth, is trusted by others, and does not deceive the world. They purify their mind from false speech. I also renounce false speech for life, abandon false speech, speak the truth, delight in the truth, abide in the truth, am trusted by others, and do not deceive the world. I purify my mind from false speech. I am equal to the arhat in this limb.' Therefore, it is called the fast.

「复次，居士妇！多闻圣弟子若持斋时，作是思惟：『阿罗诃真人尽形寿离妄言、断妄言，真谛言，乐真谛，住真谛，为人所信，不欺世间。彼于妄言净除其心，我亦尽形寿离妄言、断妄言，真谛言，乐真谛，住真谛，为人所信，不欺世间。我于妄言净除其心，我以此支于阿罗诃等同无异。』是故说斋。

Furthermore, laywoman, when a learned noble disciple observes the fast, they think like this: 'The arhat truly renounces intoxicants that cause heedlessness for life, abandons intoxicants that cause heedlessness. They purify their mind from intoxicants that cause heedlessness. I also renounce intoxicants that cause heedlessness for life, abandon intoxicants that cause heedlessness. I purify my mind from intoxicants that cause heedlessness. I am equal to the arhat in this limb.' Therefore, it is called the fast.

「复次，居士妇！多闻圣弟子若持斋时，作是思惟：『阿罗诃真人尽形寿离酒放逸、断酒放逸。彼于酒放逸净除其心，我亦尽形寿离酒放逸、断酒放逸。我于酒放逸净除其心，我以此支于阿罗诃等同无异。』是故说斋。

Furthermore, laywoman, when a learned noble disciple observes the fast, they think like this: 'The arhat truly renounces high and wide beds for life, abandons high and wide beds, delights in low seats and beds, or a bed or a mat of grass. They purify their mind from high and wide beds. On this day and night, I renounce high and wide beds, abandon high and wide beds, delight in low seats and beds, or a bed or a mat of grass. I purify my mind from high and wide beds. I am equal to the arhat in this limb.' Therefore, it is called the fast.

「复次，居士妇！多闻圣弟子若持斋时，作是思惟：『阿罗诃真人尽形寿离高广大床、断高广大床，乐下坐卧，或床或敷草。彼于高广大床净除其心，我于此日此夜离高广大床、断高广大床，乐下坐卧，或床或敷草。我于高广大床净除其心，我以此支于阿罗诃等同无异。』是故说斋。

Furthermore, laywoman, when a learned noble disciple observes the fast, they think like this: 'The arhat truly renounces garlands, perfumes, cosmetics, dancing, singing, and attending performances for life, abandons garlands, perfumes, cosmetics, dancing, singing, and attending performances. They purify their mind from garlands, perfumes, cosmetics, dancing, singing, and attending performances. On this day and night, I renounce garlands, perfumes, cosmetics, dancing, singing, and attending performances, abandon garlands, perfumes, cosmetics, dancing, singing, and attending performances. I purify my mind from garlands, perfumes, cosmetics, dancing, singing, and attending performances. I am equal to the arhat in this limb.' Therefore, it is called the fast.

「复次，居士妇！多闻圣弟子若持斋时，作是思惟：『阿罗诃真人尽形寿离华鬘、璎珞、涂香、脂粉、歌舞、倡伎及往观听，断华鬘、璎珞、涂香、脂粉、歌舞、倡伎及往观听。彼于华鬘、璎珞、涂香、脂粉、歌舞、倡伎及往观听净除其心。我于此日此夜离华鬘、璎珞、涂香、脂粉、歌舞、倡伎及往观听，断华鬘、璎珞、涂香、脂粉、歌舞、倡伎及往观听。我于华鬘、璎珞、涂香、脂粉、歌舞、倡伎及往观听净除其心，我以此支于阿罗诃等同无异。』是故说斋。

Furthermore, laywoman, when a learned noble disciple observes the fast, they think like this: 'The arhat truly renounces eating at improper times for life, abandons eating at improper times, eats one meal a day, not at night, and delights in eating at the proper time. They purify their mind from eating at improper times. On this day and night, I renounce eating at improper times, abandon eating at improper times, eat one meal a day, not at night, and delight in eating at the proper time. I purify my mind from eating at improper times. I am equal to the arhat in this limb.' Therefore, it is called the fast.

「复次，居士妇！多闻圣弟子若持斋时，作是思惟：『阿罗诃真人尽形寿离非时食、断非时食、一食，不夜食、乐于时食。彼于非时食净除其心，我于此日此夜离非时食、断非时食、一食，不夜食、乐于时食。我于非时食净除其心，我以此支于阿罗诃等同无异。』是故说斋。

Having abided in this noble eight-limbed fast, one should further cultivate five dharmas. What are the five? Laywoman, when a learned noble disciple observes the fast, they recollect the Tathāgata: 'That Blessed One, Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, of Right and Universal Knowledge, the Well-Gone One, Knower of the World, Supreme Trainer, Trainer of those to be tamed, Teacher of Gods and Men, Buddha, the Blessed One.' Having recollected the Tathāgata in this way, if there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated. Laywoman, the learned noble disciple, through recollecting the Tathāgata, attains peace and joy in the mind. If there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated.

「彼住] cf.［丽】此圣八支斋已，于上当复修习五法。云何为五？居士妇！多闻圣弟子若持斋时，忆念如来：『彼世尊、如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐。』彼作如是忆念如来已，若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭。居士妇！多闻圣弟子缘如来故，心靖得喜。若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭。

It is like a person whose head is dirty. Due to oil, warm water, and the effort of washing, it becomes clean. In the same way, when a learned noble disciple observes the fast, they recollect the Tathāgata: 'That Blessed One, Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, of Right and Universal Knowledge, the Well-Gone One, Knower of the World, Supreme Trainer, Trainer of those to be tamed, Teacher of Gods and Men, Buddha, the Blessed One.' Having recollected the Tathāgata in this way, if there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated. Laywoman, the learned noble disciple, through recollecting the Tathāgata, attains peace and joy in the mind. If there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated. This is called the learned noble disciple observing the Brahma fast, associating with the Brahma, and through the Brahma, attaining peace and joy in the mind. If there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated.

「譬若如人，头有垢腻，因膏泽、暖汤、人力、洗沐故，彼便得净。如是多闻圣弟子若持斋时，忆念如来：『彼世尊、如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐。』彼作如是忆念如来已，若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭。居士妇！多闻圣弟子缘如来故，心靖得喜。若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭，是谓多闻圣弟子持梵斋，梵共会，因梵故，心靖得喜。若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭。

Furthermore, Laywoman, when a learned noble disciple observes the fast, they recollect the Dharma: 'This Dharma is well-proclaimed by the World-Honored One, ultimate, lasting, not subject to change, to be known by right knowledge, to be seen by right wisdom, to be realized by right wisdom.' Having recollected the Dharma in this way, if there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated. Laywoman, the learned noble disciple, through recollecting the Dharma, attains peace and joy in the mind. If there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated.

「复次，居士妇！多闻圣弟子若持斋时，忆念于法：『此法世尊善说，究竟，恒不变易，正智所知，正智所见，正智所觉。』彼作如是忆念法已，若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭。居士妇！多闻圣弟子缘于法故，心靖得喜。若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭。

It is like a person whose body is dirty and impure. Due to flour, soap nuts, warm water, and the effort of bathing, the body becomes clean. In the same way, when a learned noble disciple observes the fast, they recollect the Dharma: 'This Dharma is well-proclaimed by the World-Honored One, ultimate, lasting, not subject to change, to be known by right knowledge, to be seen by right wisdom, to be realized by right wisdom.' Having recollected the Dharma in this way, if there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated. Laywoman, the learned noble disciple, through recollecting the Dharma, attains peace and joy in the mind. If there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated.

「犹人身有垢腻不净，因麸、澡豆、暖汤、人力，极洗浴故，身便得净。如是多闻圣弟子若持斋时，忆念于法：『此法世尊善说，究竟，恒不变易，正智所知，正智所见，正智所觉。』彼作如是忆念法已，若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭。居士妇！多闻圣弟子缘于法故，心靖得喜。若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭，居士妇！是谓多闻圣弟子持法斋，法共会，因法故，心靖得喜。若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭。

Furthermore, Laywoman, when a learned noble disciple observes the fast, they recollect the Saṅgha: 'The disciples of the World-Honored One are well-directed, straightforward, practicing the essentials, practicing the way, and heading towards it. In the Saṅgha of the Tathāgata, there are indeed arhats who have attained the state of arahantship, those who are heading towards the state of non-returner and have attained the fruit of non-returner, those who are heading towards the state of once-returner and have attained the fruit of once-returner, those who are heading towards the state of stream-enterer and have attained the fruit of stream-enterer. These are the four pairs, the eight types of noble ones. This is called the Saṅgha of disciples of the World-Honored One, who have attained morality, concentration, wisdom, liberation, and the knowledge and vision of liberation. They can be called, invited, offered to, served, and respected. They are a field of merit for gods and humans.' Having recollected the Saṅgha in this way, if there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated. Laywoman, the learned noble disciple, through recollecting the Saṅgha, attains peace and joy in the mind. If there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated.

「复次，居士妇！多闻圣弟子若持斋时，忆念于众：『世尊弟子众善趣向质直，行要行趣，如来众中实有阿罗诃真人趣、阿罗诃果证、阿那含趣、阿那含果证、斯陀含趣、斯陀含果证、须陀洹趣、须陀洹果证，是为四双人八辈圣士。是谓世尊弟子众，成就戒、定、慧、解脱、解脱见智，可呼、可请、可供养、可奉事、可敬重，则为天人良福之田。』彼作如是忆念众已，若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭。居士妇！多闻圣弟子缘于众故，心靖得喜。若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭。

It is like a person whose clothes are dirty and impure. Due to ashes, soap nuts, soap powder, water, and the effort of washing, they become clean. In the same way, when a learned noble disciple observes the fast, they recollect the Saṅgha: 'The disciples of the World-Honored One are well-directed, straightforward, practicing the essentials, practicing the way, and heading towards it. In the Saṅgha of the Tathāgata, there are indeed arhats who have attained the state of arahantship, those who are heading towards the state of non-returner and have attained the fruit of non-returner, those who are heading towards the state of once-returner and have attained the fruit of once-returner, those who are heading towards the state of stream-enterer and have attained the fruit of stream-enterer. These are the four pairs, the eight types of noble ones. This is called the Saṅgha of disciples of the World-Honored One, who have attained morality, concentration, wisdom, liberation, and the knowledge and vision of liberation. They can be called, invited, offered to, served, and respected. They are a field of merit for gods and humans.' Having recollected the Saṅgha in this way, if there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated. Laywoman, the learned noble disciple, through recollecting the Saṅgha, attains peace and joy in the mind. If there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated.

「犹如人衣有垢腻不净，因灰、皂荚、澡豆、汤水、人力浣故，彼便得净。如是多闻圣弟子若持斋时，忆念于众：『世尊弟子众善趣向质直，行要行趣，如来众中实有阿罗诃真人趣、阿罗诃果证、阿那含趣、阿那含果证、斯陀含趣、斯陀含果证、须陀洹趣、须陀洹果证，是为四双人八辈圣士。是谓世尊弟子众，成就戒、定、慧、解脱、解脱见智，可呼、可请、可供养、可奉事、可敬重，则为天人良福之田。』彼作如是忆念众已，若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭。居士妇！多闻圣弟子缘于众故，心靖得喜。若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭，是谓多闻圣弟子持众斋，众共会，因众故，心靖得喜。若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭。

Furthermore, Laywoman, when a learned noble disciple observes the fast, they recollect their own precepts: 'They are not broken, not pierced, not defiled, not stained, extremely broad, extremely great, not expecting any reward, praised by the wise, well-endowed, well-directed, well-received, and well-maintained.' Having recollected their own precepts in this way, if there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated. Laywoman, the learned noble disciple, through recollecting their own precepts, attains peace and joy in the mind. If there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated.

「复次，居士妇！多闻圣弟子若持斋时，忆念自戒：『不缺不穿，无秽无污，极广极大，不望其报，智者称誉，善具善趣，善受善持。』彼作如是忆念自戒已，若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭。居士妇！多闻圣弟子缘于戒故，心靖得喜。若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭。

It is like a mirror that is unclear due to dirt. By polishing it with a stone and the effort of a person, it becomes clear and bright. In the same way, when a learned noble disciple observes the fast, they recollect their own precepts: 'They are not broken, not pierced, not defiled, not stained, extremely broad, extremely great, not expecting any reward, praised by the wise, well-endowed, well-directed, well-received, and well-maintained.' Having recollected their own precepts in this way, if there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated. Laywoman, the learned noble disciple, through recollecting their own precepts, attains peace and joy in the mind. If there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated.

「犹若如镜，生垢不明，因石磨锃莹，由人力治，便得明净。如是多闻圣弟子若持斋时，忆念自戒：『不缺不穿，无秽无污，极广极大，不望其报，智者称誉，善具善趣，善受善持。』彼作如是忆念自戒已，若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭。居士妇！多闻圣弟子缘于戒故，心靖得喜。若有恶伺，彼便] cf.［丽】得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭，是谓多闻圣弟子持戒斋，戒共会，因戒故，心靖得喜。若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭。

Furthermore, Laywoman, when a learned noble disciple observes the fast, they recollect the gods: 'There truly are the Four Heavenly Kings. If those gods attain faith, they will be reborn there upon the end of their lives. I also have that faith. If those gods attain morality, learning, generosity, and wisdom, they will be reborn there upon the end of their lives. I also have that wisdom. There truly are the Thirty-three Gods, the Yāma Gods, the Tuṣita Gods, the Nirmāṇarati Gods, and the Paranirmitavaśavartin Gods. If those gods attain faith, they will be reborn there upon the end of their lives. I also have that faith. If those gods attain morality, learning, generosity, and wisdom, they will be reborn there upon the end of their lives. I also have that wisdom.' Having recollected this, and the faith, morality, learning, generosity, and wisdom of the gods, if there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated. Laywoman, the learned noble disciple, through recollecting the gods, attains peace and joy in the mind. If there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated.

「复次，居士妇！多闻圣弟子若持斋时，忆念诸天：『实有四王天，彼天若成就信，于此命终，得生彼间，我亦有彼信。彼天若成就戒、闻、施、慧，于此命终，得生彼间，我亦有彼慧。实有三十三天、焰摩天、兜率哆天、化乐天、他化乐天，彼天若成就信，于此命终，得生彼间，我亦有彼信。彼天若成就戒、闻、施、慧，于此命终，得生彼间，我亦有彼慧。』彼作如是忆念已，及诸天信、戒、闻、施、慧。若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭，居士妇，多闻圣弟子缘诸天故，心靖得喜。若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭。

It is like gold of the highest color, which becomes impure due to dirt. By polishing it with fire, a hammer, red clay, and the effort of a person, it becomes clear and bright. In the same way, when a learned noble disciple observes the fast, they recollect the gods: 'There truly are the Four Heavenly Kings. If those gods attain faith, they will be reborn there upon the end of their lives. I also have that faith. If those gods attain morality, learning, generosity, and wisdom, they will be reborn there upon the end of their lives. I also have that wisdom. There truly are the Thirty-three Gods, the Yāma Gods, the Tuṣita Gods, the Nirmāṇarati Gods, and the Paranirmitavaśavartin Gods. If those gods attain faith, they will be reborn there upon the end of their lives. I also have that faith. If those gods attain morality, learning, generosity, and wisdom, they will be reborn there upon the end of their lives. I also have that wisdom.' Having recollected this, and the faith, morality, learning, generosity, and wisdom of the gods, if there are evil intentions, they are eliminated, and all defiled and unwholesome dharmas are also eliminated.

「犹如上色金，生垢不净，因火排钻椎赤土、人力，磨拭莹治，便得明净。如是多闻圣弟子若持斋时，忆念诸天：『实有四王天，彼天若成就信，于此命终，得生彼间，我亦有彼信。彼天若成就戒、闻、施、慧，于此命终，得生彼间，我亦有彼慧。实有三十三天、焰摩天、兜率多天、化乐天、他化乐天，彼天若成就信，于此命终，得生彼间，我亦有彼信。彼天若成就戒、闻、施、慧，于此命终，得生彼间，我亦有彼慧。』彼作如是忆念已，及诸天信、戒、闻、施、慧，若有恶伺，彼便得灭，所有秽污恶不善法，彼亦得灭。

Laywoman, if one practices the noble eight-part fast, even if there are sixteen great countries, namely: Anga, Magadha, Kasi, Kosala, Kuru, Pancala, Asmaka, Avanti, Videha, Vatsa, Vamsa, Suhma, Surastra, Yavana, Kamboja, and Gandhara. If someone were to be the king of these countries, freely using their money, treasures, gold, silver, jewels, pearls, lapis lazuli, conch shells, agate, coral, amber, beryl, agate, carnelian, crystal, red stone, and turquoises, it would not be worth a sixteenth part of the noble eight-part fast.

「居士妇！若行如是圣八支斋，若有十六大国，谓一者鸯迦，二者摩竭陀，三者迦尸，四者拘萨罗，五者拘楼，六者般阇罗，七者阿摄贝，八者阿和檀提，九者枝提，十者跋耆，十一者跋蹉，十二跋罗，十三苏摩，十四苏罗咤，十五喻尼，十六剑浮。此诸国中所有钱宝、金、银、摩尼、真珠、琉璃、坏伽、碧玉、珊瑚、留邵、鞞留、鞞勒、马瑙、蝳蝐] cf.［丽】、赤石、旋珠，设使有人于中作王，随用自在者，彼一切比丘持圣八支斋，不直十六分。

Laywoman, for this reason I say that human kings are not as happy as the gods. If fifty human years is one day and night for the Four Heavenly Kings, then thirty such days and nights make a month, and twelve months make a year. Thus, five hundred such years is the lifespan of the Four Heavenly Kings. Laywoman, it is possible that if a noble male or female upholds the noble eight-part fast, then upon the dissolution of the body and the end of life, they will be reborn among the Four Heavenly Kings. Laywoman, for this reason I say that human kings are not as happy as the gods. If one hundred human years is one day and night for the Thirty-three Gods, then thirty such days and nights make a month, and twelve months make a year. Thus, one thousand such years is the lifespan of the Thirty-three Gods. Laywoman, it is possible that if a noble male or female upholds the noble eight-part fast, then upon the dissolution of the body and the end of life, they will be reborn among the Thirty-three Gods.

「居士妇！我因此故说，人王者不如天乐。若人五十岁是四王天一昼一夜，如是三十昼夜为一月，十二月为一岁，如此五百岁是四王天寿。居士妇！必有是处，若族姓男、族姓女持圣八支斋，身坏命终，生四天王中。居士妇！我因此故说，人王者不如天乐。若人百岁是三十三天一昼一夜，如是三十昼夜为一月，十二月为一岁，如此千岁是三十三天寿。居士妇！必有是处，若族姓男、族姓女持圣八支斋，身坏命终，生三十三天中。

Laywoman, for this reason I say that human kings are not as happy as the gods. If two hundred human years is one day and night for the Yāma Gods, then thirty such days and nights make a month, and twelve months make a year. Thus, two thousand such years is the lifespan of the Yāma Gods. Laywoman, it is possible that if a noble male or female upholds the noble eight-part fast, then upon the dissolution of the body and the end of life, they will be reborn among the Yāma Gods. Laywoman, for this reason I say that human kings are not as happy as the gods. If four hundred human years is one day and night for the Tuṣita Gods, then thirty such days and nights make a month, and twelve months make a year. Thus, four thousand such years is the lifespan of the Tuṣita Gods. Laywoman, it is possible that if a noble male or female upholds the noble eight-part fast, then upon the dissolution of the body and the end of life, they will be reborn among the Tuṣita Gods.

「居士妇！我因此故说，人王者不如天乐。若人二百岁是焰摩天一昼一夜，如是三十昼夜为一月，十二月为一岁，如此二千岁是焰摩天寿。居士妇！必有是处，若族姓男、族姓女持圣八支斋，身坏命终，生焰摩天中。居士妇！我因此故说，人王者不如天乐。若人四百岁是兜率陀天一昼一夜，如是三十昼夜为一月，十二月为一岁，如此四千岁是兜率陀天寿。居士妇！必有是处，若族姓男、族姓女持圣八支斋，身坏命终，生兜率哆天中。

Laywoman, for this reason I say that human kings are not as happy as the gods. If eight hundred human years is one day and night for the Nirmāṇarati Gods, then thirty such days and nights make a month, and twelve months make a year. Thus, eight thousand such years is the lifespan of the Nirmāṇarati Gods. Laywoman, it is possible that if a noble male or female upholds the noble eight-part fast, then upon the dissolution of the body and the end of life, they will be reborn among the Nirmāṇarati Gods. Laywoman, for this reason I say that human kings are not as happy as the gods. If one thousand six hundred human years is one day and night for the Paranirmitavaśavartin Gods, then thirty such days and nights make a month, and twelve months make a year. Thus, sixteen thousand such years is the lifespan of the Paranirmitavaśavartin Gods. Laywoman, it is possible that if a noble male or female upholds the noble eight-part fast, then upon the dissolution of the body and the end of life, they will be reborn among the Paranirmitavaśavartin Gods.

「居士妇！我因此故说，人王者不如天乐。若人八百岁是化乐天一昼一夜，如是三十昼夜为一月，十二月为一岁，如此八千岁是化乐天寿。居士妇！必有是处，若族姓男、族姓女持圣八支斋，身坏命终，生化乐天中。居士妇！我因此故说，人王者不如天乐。若人千六百岁是他化乐天一昼一夜，如是三十昼夜为一月，十二月为一岁，如此万六千岁是他化乐天寿。居士妇！必有是处，若族姓男、族姓女持圣八支斋，身坏命终，生他化乐天中。」

Then the householder's wife Visākhā, the mother of Migāra, with her hands joined in respect, said to the Buddha: "World-Honored One! The noble eight-part fast is truly wonderful and marvelous, with great benefits, great fruits, great merit, and great spread. World-Honored One! From now on, for as long as I live, I will uphold the noble eight-part fast, and according to my means, I will give alms and cultivate merit."

于是，鹿子母毘舍佉叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！圣八支斋甚奇！甚特！大利大果，有大功德，有大广布。世尊！我从今始，自尽形寿持圣八支斋，随其事力，布施修福。」

Then the householder's wife Visākhā, the mother of Migāra, having heard the Buddha's words, remembered them well. She bowed her head to the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and departed.

于是，鹿子母闻佛所说，善持，稽首稽首佛足，绕三匝而去。

The Buddha spoke thus. The householder's wife Visākhā, the mother of Migāra, and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。鹿子母毘舍佉及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
