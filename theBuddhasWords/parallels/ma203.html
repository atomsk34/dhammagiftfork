MĀ 203

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 17 晡利多品

203. （二〇三）晡利多经

Parallel to: MN 54

I have heard thus.

我闻如是。

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the country of Nadi, staying in the Pāvārika Mango Grove.

一时，佛游那难大，在波和利［木＊奈］园之中。

At that time, the householder Pāṭaliya was wearing white, clean clothes, with a white turban wrapped around his head, holding a staff and an umbrella, wearing secular sandals, wandering from garden to garden, from pavilion to pavilion, from grove to grove, roaming around. If he saw any śramaṇas or brāhmaṇas, he would say, "Virtuous ones! You should know that I have renounced the world, cut off worldly affairs, and abandoned worldly matters."

尔时，晡利多居士著白净衣，白巾裹头，拄杖执盖，著世俗屣，从园至园，从观至观，从林至林，遍游从行彷徉。若见诸沙门、梵志者，便作是说：「诸贤！当知我离俗断俗，舍诸俗事。」

Those śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas spoke to him in gentle and soft words, saying, "Yes, virtuous Pāṭaliya has renounced the world, cut off worldly affairs, and abandoned worldly matters."

彼诸沙门、梵志以濡软柔和语曰：「唯然。贤晡利多离俗断俗，舍诸俗事。」

Then, the householder Pāṭaliya wandered and roamed around, going to where the Buddha was. They exchanged greetings and stood in front of the Buddha, leaning on a staff. The World-Honored One said, "Householder! There is a seat. If you wish to sit, please sit down."

于是，晡利多居士遍游行彷徉，往诣佛所，共相问讯，当在佛前，拄杖而立，世尊告曰：「居士！有座，欲坐便坐。」

The householder Pāṭaliya said, "Gautama! This is not so, this is not possible. Why is that? I have renounced the world, cut off worldly affairs, and abandoned worldly matters. Yet the śramaṇa Gautama calls me a householder?"

晡利多居士白曰：「瞿昙！此事不然，此事不可。所以者何？我离俗断俗，舍诸俗事，而沙门瞿昙唤我为居士耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "You have the marks and signs of a householder. Therefore, I call you 'householder!' There is a seat. If you wish to sit, please sit down."

世尊答曰：「汝有相幖帜如居士，是故我唤汝：『居士！有座，欲坐便坐。』」

The World-Honored One spoke thus a second and a third time, saying, "Householder! There is a seat. If you wish to sit, please sit down."

世尊如是复至再三告曰：「居士！有座，欲坐便坐。」

The householder Pāṭaliya also spoke thus a second and a third time, saying, "Gautama! This is not so, this is not possible. I have left the household life, renounced worldly affairs, and yet the śramaṇa Gautama calls me a householder?"

晡利多居士亦至再三白曰：「瞿昙！此事不然，此事不可。我离俗断俗，舍诸俗事，而沙门瞿昙唤我为居士耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "You have the marks and characteristics of a householder, therefore I call you 'householder! There is a seat. If you wish to sit, please sit down.'"

世尊答曰：「汝有相幖帜如居士，是故我唤汝：『居士！有座，欲坐便坐。』」

The World-Honored One asked, "How have you left the household life, renounced worldly affairs, and given up worldly matters?"

世尊问曰：「汝云何离俗断俗，舍诸俗事耶？」

The householder Pāṭaliya replied, "Gautama! I have given all my wealth to my children. I have nothing to do and nothing to seek. I only go to get food to sustain my life. In this way, I have left the household life, renounced worldly affairs, and given up worldly matters."

晡利多居士答曰：「瞿昙！我家一切所有财物尽持施儿，我无为无求游，唯往取食，存命而已，如是我离俗断俗，舍诸俗事。」

The World-Honored One said, "Householder! In the noble Dharma and Vinaya, it is not like this to cut off worldly affairs. Householder! In the noble Dharma and Vinaya, there are eight branches to cut off worldly affairs."

世尊告曰：「居士！圣法、律中不如是断绝俗事。居士！圣法、律中有八支断俗事也。」

Then the householder Pāṭaliya put down his staff and umbrella, took off his sandals, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "Gautama! In the noble Dharma and Vinaya, what are the eight branches to cut off worldly affairs?"

于是，晡利多居士舍杖却盖，及脱俗屣，叉手向佛，白曰：「瞿昙！圣法、律中云何八支断俗事耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Householder! A noble disciple of much learning relies on abandoning killing and cutting off killing, relies on abandoning taking what is not given and cutting off taking what is not given, relies on abandoning sexual misconduct and cutting off sexual misconduct, relies on abandoning false speech and cutting off false speech, relies on abandoning greed and cutting off greed, relies on abandoning malice and cutting off malice, relies on abandoning resentment and cutting off resentment, and relies on abandoning conceit and cutting off conceit. Householder! How does a noble disciple of much learning rely on abandoning killing and cutting off killing? A noble disciple of much learning thinks like this: 'Killing definitely brings bad consequences in this life and the next. If I kill, I will harm myself, slander others, and be criticized by the gods and wise practitioners of the Brahma way. My bad reputation will spread everywhere. When my body perishes and my life ends, I will definitely go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. In this way, killing brings bad consequences in this life and the next. Should I now rely on abandoning killing and cutting off killing?' Then they rely on abandoning killing and cutting off killing. In this way, a noble disciple of much learning relies on abandoning killing and cutting off killing.

世尊答曰：「居士！多闻圣弟子依离杀、断杀，依离不与取、断不与取，依离邪婬、断邪婬，依离妄言，断妄言，依无贪著、断贪著，依无害恚、断害恚，依无憎嫉恼、断憎嫉恼，依无增上慢、断增上慢。居士！多闻圣弟子云何依离杀、断杀耶？多闻圣弟子作是思惟：『杀者必受恶报，现世及后世，若我杀者，便当自害，亦诬谤他，天及诸智梵行者道说我戒，诸方悉当闻我恶名，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。如是杀者受此恶报，现世及后世，我今宁可依离杀、断杀耶？』便依离杀、断杀。如是多闻圣弟子依离杀、断杀也。

Householder! How does a noble disciple of much learning rely on abandoning taking what is not given and cutting off taking what is not given? A noble disciple of much learning thinks like this: 'Taking what is not given definitely brings bad consequences in this life and the next. If I take what is not given, I will harm myself, slander others, and be criticized by the gods and wise practitioners of the Brahma way. My bad reputation will spread everywhere. When my body perishes and my life ends, I will definitely go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. In this way, taking what is not given brings bad consequences in this life and the next. Should I now rely on abandoning taking what is not given and cutting off taking what is not given?' Then they rely on abandoning taking what is not given and cutting off taking what is not given. In this way, a noble disciple of much learning relies on abandoning taking what is not given and cutting off taking what is not given.

「居士！多闻圣弟子云何依离不与取、断不与取耶？多闻圣弟子作是思惟：『不与取者必受恶报，现世及后世，若我不与取者，便当自害，亦诬谤他，天及诸智梵行者道说我戒，诸方悉当闻我恶名，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。如是不与取者受此恶报，现世及后世，我今宁可依离不与取、断不与取耶？』便依离不与取、断不与取。如是多闻圣弟子依离不与取、断不与取也。

Householder! How does a noble disciple of much learning rely on abandoning sexual misconduct and cutting off sexual misconduct? A noble disciple of much learning thinks like this: 'Sexual misconduct definitely brings bad consequences in this life and the next. If I engage in sexual misconduct, I will harm myself, slander others, and be criticized by the gods and wise practitioners of the Brahma way. My bad reputation will spread everywhere. When my body perishes and my life ends, I will definitely go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. In this way, sexual misconduct brings bad consequences in this life and the next. Should I now rely on abandoning sexual misconduct and cutting off sexual misconduct?' Then they rely on abandoning sexual misconduct and cutting off sexual misconduct. In this way, a noble disciple of much learning relies on abandoning sexual misconduct and cutting off sexual misconduct.

「居士！多闻圣弟子云何依离邪婬、断邪婬？多闻圣弟子作是思惟：『邪婬者必受恶报，现世及后世，若我邪婬者，便当自害，亦诬谤他，天及诸智梵行者道说我戒，诸方悉当闻我恶名，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。如是邪婬者受此恶报，现世及后世，我今宁可依离邪婬、断邪婬耶？』便依离邪婬、断邪婬。如是多闻圣弟子依离邪婬、断邪婬也。

Householder! How does a noble disciple of much learning rely on abandoning false speech and cutting off false speech? A noble disciple of much learning thinks like this: 'False speech definitely brings bad consequences in this life and the next. If I speak falsely, I will harm myself, slander others, and be criticized by the gods and wise practitioners of the Brahma way. My bad reputation will spread everywhere. When my body perishes and my life ends, I will definitely go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. In this way, false speech brings bad consequences in this life and the next. Should I now rely on abandoning false speech and cutting off false speech?' Then they rely on abandoning false speech and cutting off false speech. In this way, a noble disciple of much learning relies on abandoning false speech and cutting off false speech.

「居士！多闻圣弟子云何依离妄言、断妄言耶？多闻圣弟子作是思惟：『妄言者必受恶报，现世及后世，若我妄言者，便当自害，亦诬谤他，天及诸智梵行者道说我戒，诸方悉当闻我恶名，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。如是妄言者受此恶报，现世及后世，我今宁可依离妄言、断妄言耶？』便依离妄言、断妄言。如是多闻圣弟子依离妄言、断妄言也。

Householder! How does a noble disciple of much learning rely on abandoning greed and cutting off greed? A noble disciple of much learning thinks like this: 'Greed definitely brings bad consequences in this life and the next. If I am greedy, I will harm myself, slander others, and be criticized by the gods and wise practitioners of the Brahma way. My bad reputation will spread everywhere. When my body perishes and my life ends, I will definitely go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. In this way, greed brings bad consequences in this life and the next. Should I now rely on abandoning greed and cutting off greed?' Then they rely on abandoning greed and cutting off greed. In this way, a noble disciple of much learning relies on abandoning greed and cutting off greed.

「居士！多闻圣弟子云何依无贪著、断贪著耶？多闻圣弟子作是思惟：『贪著者必受恶报，现世及后世，若我贪著者，便当自害，亦诬谤他，天及诸智梵行者道说我戒，诸方悉当闻我恶名，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。如是贪著者受此恶报，现世及后世，我今宁可依无贪著、断贪著耶？』便依无贪著、断贪著。如是多闻圣弟子依无贪著、断贪著也。

Householder! How does a noble disciple of much learning rely on abandoning harm and cutting off harm? A noble disciple of much learning thinks like this: 'Harm and anger definitely bring bad consequences in this life and the next. If I am harmful and angry, I will harm myself, slander others, and be criticized by the gods and wise practitioners of the Brahma way. My bad reputation will spread everywhere. When my body perishes and my life ends, I will definitely go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. In this way, harm and anger bring bad consequences in this life and the next. Should I now rely on abandoning harm and cutting off harm?' Then they rely on abandoning harm and cutting off harm. In this way, a noble disciple of much learning relies on abandoning harm and cutting off harm.

「居士！多闻圣弟子云何依无害恚断害恚耶？多闻圣弟子作是思惟：『害恚者必受恶报，现世及后世，若我害恚者，便当自害，亦诬谤他，天及诸智梵行者道说我戒，诸方悉当闻我恶名，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。如是害恚者受此恶报，现世及后世，我今宁可依无害恚、断害恚耶？』便依无害恚、断害恚。如是多闻圣弟子依无害恚、断害恚也。

Householder! How does a noble disciple of much learning rely on abandoning hatred and cutting off hatred? A noble disciple of much learning thinks like this: 'Hatred definitely brings bad consequences in this life and the next. If I am hateful, I will harm myself, slander others, and be criticized by the gods and wise practitioners of the Brahma way. My bad reputation will spread everywhere. When my body perishes and my life ends, I will definitely go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. In this way, hatred brings bad consequences in this life and the next. Should I now rely on abandoning hatred and cutting off hatred?' Then they rely on abandoning hatred and cutting off hatred. In this way, a noble disciple of much learning relies on abandoning hatred and cutting off hatred.

「居士！多闻圣弟子云何依无憎嫉恼、断憎嫉恼耶？多闻圣弟子作是思惟：『憎嫉恼者必受恶报，现世及后世，若我憎嫉恼者，便当自害，亦诬谤他，天及诸智梵行者道说我戒，诸方悉当闻我恶名，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。如是憎嫉恼者受此恶报，现世及后世，我今宁可依无憎嫉恼、断憎嫉恼耶？』便依无憎嫉恼、断憎嫉恼。如是多闻圣弟子依无憎嫉恼、断憎嫉恼也。

Householder! How does a noble disciple of much learning rely on abandoning conceit and cutting off conceit? A noble disciple of much learning thinks like this: 'Conceit definitely brings bad consequences in this life and the next. If I am conceited, I will harm myself, slander others, and be criticized by the gods and wise practitioners of the Brahma way. My bad reputation will spread everywhere. When my body perishes and my life ends, I will definitely go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. In this way, conceit brings bad consequences in this life and the next. Should I now rely on abandoning conceit and cutting off conceit?' Then they rely on abandoning conceit and cutting off conceit. In this way, a noble disciple of much learning relies on abandoning conceit and cutting off conceit.

「居士！多闻圣弟子云何依无增上慢、断增上慢耶？多闻圣弟子作是思惟：『增上慢者必受恶报，现世及后世，若我增上慢者，便当自害，亦诬谤他，天及诸智梵行者道说我戒，诸方悉当闻我恶名，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。如是增上慢者受此恶报，现世及后世，我今宁可依无增上慢、断增上慢耶？』便依无增上慢、断增上慢。如是多闻圣弟子依无增上慢、断增上慢也。是谓圣法、律中有八支断绝俗事。」

The householder asked: "Gautama! In the noble Dharma and Vinaya, is it only about cutting off worldly affairs, or is there more?"

居士问曰：「瞿昙！圣法、律中但是断俗事，复更有耶？」

The World-Honored One replied: "In the noble Dharma and Vinaya, it is not only about cutting off worldly affairs, but there are also eight branches that can be realized through cutting off worldly affairs."

世尊答曰：「圣法、律中不但有是断绝俗事，更有八支断绝俗事得作证也。」

Having heard this, the householder Pāṭaliya immediately removed his white turban, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said: "Gautama! In the noble Dharma and Vinaya, how are there further eight branches that can be realized through cutting off worldly affairs?"

晡利多居士闻已，便脱白巾，叉手向佛，白曰：「瞿昙！圣法、律中云何更有八支断绝俗事得作证耶？」

The World-Honored One replied: "Householder! It is like a dog, hungry and emaciated, going to a butcher's place. The butcher or his apprentice carefully removes the meat and throws the bones to the dog. The dog, having obtained the bones, bites here and there, breaking its lips and teeth, or injuring its throat. But the dog cannot use this to relieve its hunger. Householder! The well-learned noble disciple also thinks like this: 'Desire is like a bone-fetter. The World-Honored One says that desire is like a bone-fetter, with little pleasure and much suffering, and many calamities. One should distance oneself from it.' If one can let go of desire and abandon unwholesome states, then all worldly food and drink will be completely exhausted without remainder. One should cultivate that. Householder! Not far from a village, there is a small piece of meat fallen on the ground. A crow or a hawk carries the meat away, and other crows and hawks compete to chase after it. What do you think, householder? If the crow or hawk does not quickly let go of the small piece of meat, will the other crows and hawks compete to chase after it?"

世尊答曰：「居士！犹如有狗，饥饿羸乏，至屠牛处，彼屠牛师、屠牛弟子净摘除肉，掷骨与狗。狗得骨已，处处咬啮，破唇缺齿，或伤咽喉，然狗不得以此除饥。居士！多闻圣弟子亦复作是思惟：『欲如骨鏁。世尊说欲如骨鏁，乐少苦多，多有灾患，当远离之。』若有此舍离欲、离恶不善之法，谓此一切世间饮食永尽无余，当修习彼。居士！犹去村不远，有小肉脔，堕在露地，或乌或鵄，持彼肉去，余乌鵄鸟竞而逐之。于居士意云何？若此乌鵄不速舍此小肉脔者，致余乌鵄竞而逐耶？」

The householder replied: "Yes, Gautama!"

居士答曰：「唯然。瞿昙！」

"Householder! What do you think? If the crow or hawk can quickly let go of the small piece of meat, will the other crows and hawks compete to chase after it?"

「于居士意云何？若此乌鵄能速舍此小肉脔者，余乌鵄鸟当复竞逐耶？」

The householder replied: "No, Gautama!"

居士答曰：「不也。瞿昙！」

"Householder! The well-versed noble disciple also thinks like this: 'Desires are like a piece of meat. The World-Honored One says desires are like a piece of meat, with little pleasure and much suffering, and many calamities. One should stay far away from them.' If one can let go of desires and abandon unwholesome states, then all worldly food and drink will be completely exhausted without remainder. One should cultivate that. Householder! It is like a person holding a torch against the wind. What do you think, householder? If that person does not quickly let go, will it burn their hand or other limbs?"

「居士！多闻圣弟子亦复作是思惟：『欲如肉脔。世尊说欲如肉脔，乐少苦多，多有灾患，当远离之。』若有此舍离欲、离恶不善之法，谓此一切世间饮食永尽无余，当修习彼。居士！犹如有人，手把火炬、向风而行。于居士意云何，若使此人不速舍者，必烧其手、余支体耶？」

The householder replied: "Yes, Gautama!"

居士答曰：「唯然。瞿昙！」

"What do you think, householder? If that person quickly lets go of the torch, will it burn their hand or other limbs?"

「于居士意云何？若使此人速舍炬者，当烧其手、余支体耶？」

The householder replied: "No, Gautama!"

居士答曰：「不也。瞿昙！」

"Householder, the well-versed noble disciple also thinks like this: 'Desire is like a torch. The World-Honored One says desire is like a torch, with little pleasure and much suffering, and many calamities, one should distance oneself from it.' If one abandons desire and the unwholesome dharmas, then all worldly food and drink will be completely exhausted without remainder. One should cultivate that. Householder, not far from a village, there is a large fire pit filled with fire, without smoke or flames. If a person comes who is not foolish or deluded, not upside down, abiding in their original mind, free and independent, using pleasure and not using suffering, greatly despising suffering, using life and not using death, greatly despising death. What do you think, householder? Would this person rather enter the fire pit?"

「居士！多闻圣弟子亦复作是思惟：『欲如火炬。世尊说欲如火炬，乐少苦多，多有灾患，当远离之。』若有此舍离欲、离恶不善之法，谓此一切世间饮食永尽无余，当修习彼。居士！犹去村不远，有大火坑，满其中火，而无烟［火＊佥］。若有人来，不愚不痴，亦不颠倒，自住本心，自由自在，用乐不用苦，甚憎恶苦，用活不用死，甚憎恶死。于居士意云何？此人宁当入火坑耶？」

The householder replied: "No, Gautama! Why is that? Because seeing the fire pit, one thinks like this: 'If one falls into the fire pit, one will surely die without a doubt. Even if one does not die, one will definitely suffer extreme pain.' Seeing the fire pit, one then thinks to distance oneself, wishing to abandon it."

居士答曰：「不也。瞿昙！所以者何？彼见火坑，便作是思惟：『若堕火坑，必死无疑，设不死者，定受极苦。』彼见火坑，便思远离，愿求舍离。」

"Householder, the well-versed noble disciple also thinks like this: 'Desire is like a poisonous snake. The World-Honored One says desire is like a poisonous snake, with little pleasure and much suffering, and many calamities, one should distance oneself from it.' If one abandons desire and the unwholesome dharmas, then all worldly food and drink will be completely exhausted without remainder. One should cultivate that. Householder, not far from a village, there is a large poisonous snake, extremely vicious and painful, black in color and terrifying. If a person comes who is not foolish or deluded, not upside down, abiding in their original mind, free and independent, using pleasure and not using suffering, greatly despising suffering, using life and not using death, greatly despising death. What do you think, householder? Would this person rather take the hand or other limbs of that person and say: 'Bite me, bite me'?"

「居士！多闻圣弟子亦复作是思惟：『欲如火坑。世尊说欲如火坑，乐少苦多，多有灾患，当远离之。』若有此舍离欲、离恶不善之法，谓此一切世间饮食永尽无余，当修习彼。居士！犹去村不远，有大毒蛇，至恶苦毒，黑色可畏。若有人来，不愚不痴，亦不颠倒，自住本心，自由自在，用乐不用苦，甚憎恶苦，用活不用死，甚憎恶死。于居士意云何？此人宁当以手授与及余支体，作如是说：『蜇我蜇我耶？』」

The householder replied: "No, Gautama! Why is that? Because seeing the poisonous snake, one thinks like this: 'If I use my hand or other limbs to let the snake bite me, I will surely die without a doubt. Even if I do not die, I will definitely suffer extreme pain.' Seeing the poisonous snake, one then thinks to distance oneself, wishing to abandon it."

居士答曰：「不也。瞿昙！所以者何？彼见毒蛇，便作是思惟：『若我以手及余支体使蛇蜇者，必死无疑，设不死者，定受极苦。』彼见毒蛇，便思远离，愿求舍离。」

Householder, the well-versed noble disciple also thinks like this: 'Desire is like a dream. The World-Honored One says desire is like a dream. With little pleasure and much suffering, and many calamities, one should distance oneself from it.' If one abandons desire and the unwholesome dharmas, then all worldly food and drink will be completely exhausted without remainder. One should cultivate that. Householder, it is like a person who, in a dream, fully enjoys the five desires. When they wake up, they do not see even one. Householder, the well-versed noble disciple also thinks like this: 'Desire is like a dream. The World-Honored One says desire is like a dream. With little pleasure and much suffering, and many calamities, one should distance oneself from it.' If one abandons desire and the unwholesome dharmas, then all worldly food and drink will be completely exhausted without remainder. One should cultivate that.

「居士！多闻圣弟子亦复作是思惟：『欲如毒蛇。世尊说欲如毒蛇，乐少苦多，多有灾患，当远离之。』若有此舍离欲、离恶不善之法，谓此一切世间饮食永尽无余，当修习彼。居士！犹如有人，梦得具足五欲自娱，彼若悟已，都不见一。居士！多闻圣弟子亦复作是思惟：『欲如梦也。世尊说欲如梦也。乐少苦多，多有灾患，当远离之。』若有此舍离欲、离恶不善之法，谓此一切世间饮食永尽无余，当修习彼。

Householder, it is like a person borrowing pleasure objects, such as palaces, pavilions, gardens, ponds, elephants, horses, carriages, silk, cotton, rings, bracelets, fragrant garlands, necklaces, golden flower garlands, or fine clothes. Many people see this and all say: 'This is good, this is wonderful. If one has wealth, one should enjoy oneself like this.' The owner of the objects, at will, takes them back or instructs others to take them back. Then he takes them back himself or instructs others to take them back. Many people see this and all say: 'The borrower is truly deceiving, not borrowing.' Why is that? The owner of the objects, at will, takes them back or instructs others to take them back. Then he takes them back himself or instructs others to take them back. Householder, the well-versed noble disciple also thinks like this: 'Desire is like borrowing. The World-Honored One says desire is like borrowing. With little pleasure and much suffering, and many calamities, one should distance oneself from it.' If one abandons desire and the unwholesome dharmas, then all worldly food and drink will be completely exhausted without remainder. One should cultivate that.

「居士！犹如有人假借乐具，或宫殿楼阁，或园观浴池，或象马车乘，或缯绵被，或指环、臂钏，或香璎珞颈钳，或金宝华鬘，或名衣上服，多人见已，而共叹曰：『如是为善，如是为快。若有财物，应作如是极自娱乐。』其物主者，随所欲夺，或教人夺，即便自夺，或教人夺，多人见已，而共说曰：『彼假借者，实为欺诳，非是假借。』所以者何？其物主者，随所欲夺，或教人夺，即便自夺，或教人夺。居士！多闻圣弟子亦复作是思惟：『欲如假借，世尊说欲如假借，乐少苦多，多有灾患，当远离之。』若有此舍离欲、离恶不善之法，谓此一切世间饮食永尽无余，当修习彼。

"Householder, not far from a village, there is a large fruit tree, which always has many good and delicious fruits. If someone comes, hungry and emaciated, wanting to eat the fruits, they think: 'This tree always has many good and delicious fruits. I am hungry and emaciated, wanting to eat the fruits. But there are no fruits that have fallen by themselves here that I can eat to my fill and take back. I can climb the tree. Should I climb this tree now?' Thinking this, they climb up. Then another person comes, hungry and emaciated, wanting to eat the fruits. They bring an extremely sharp axe. They think: 'This tree always has many good and delicious fruits. But there are no fruits that have fallen by themselves here that I can eat to my fill and take back. I cannot climb the tree. Should I cut this tree down now?' They then cut it down. What do you think, householder? If the person in the tree does not come down quickly, when the tree falls to the ground, will it surely break their arms or other limbs?"

「居士！犹去村不远，有大果树，此树常多有好美果。若有人来，饥饿羸乏，欲得食果，彼作是念：『此树常多有好美果，我饥羸乏，欲得食果，然此树下无自落果可得饱食及持归去，我能缘树，我今宁可上此树耶？』念已便上。复有一人来，饥饿羸乏，欲得食果，持极利斧，彼作是念：『此树常多有好美果，然此树下无自落果可得饱食及持归去，我不能缘树，我今宁可斫倒此树耶？』即便斫倒。于居士意云何？若树上人不速来下者，树倒地时，必折其臂、余支体耶？」

The householder replied, "Yes, Gautama!"

居士答曰：「唯然。瞿昙！」

"Householder, what do you think? If the person in the tree comes down quickly, when the tree falls to the ground, will it surely break their arms or other limbs?"

「于居士意云何？若树上人速来下者，树倒地时，宁折其臂、余支体耶？」

The householder replied, "No, Gautama!"

居士答曰：「不也。瞿昙！」

Householder, the well-versed noble disciple also thinks like this: 'Desire is like a tree fruit. The World-Honored One said that desire is like a tree fruit, with little pleasure and much suffering, and many calamities. One should stay far away from it.' If one abandons desire and abandons unwholesome states, then all worldly food and drink will be completely exhausted without remainder. One should cultivate that. This is called the further eight branches of the noble Dharma and Vinaya that cut off worldly affairs and attain realization.

「居士！多闻圣弟子亦复作是思惟，欲如树果，世尊说欲如树果，乐少苦多，多有灾患，当远离之。若有此舍离欲、离恶不善之法，谓此一切世间饮食永尽无余，当修习彼，是谓圣法、律中更有此八支断绝俗事而得作证。

"Householder, they have stopped initial and sustained thought, their mind is internally serene and one-pointed, without initial or sustained thought, and they attain the joy and bliss of the second dhyāna. They are already free from the desire for joy, and dwell without seeking, with right mindfulness and right knowledge, and experience bliss with the body. They attain the third dhyāna, which is said to be the noble one's relinquishment, mindfulness, bliss, and emptiness. They attain the fourth dhyāna, where bliss and suffering have ceased, joy and sorrow have already ceased, and they dwell in neither suffering nor bliss, with equanimity and pure mindfulness. Their mind is already thus settled, pure, undefiled, unperturbed, pliant, well-established, immovable, and they cultivate the knowledge and vision of the exhaustion of the taints, and attain realization. They know this suffering as it truly is, know the origin of this suffering, know the cessation of this suffering, and know the path to the cessation of this suffering as it truly is. They know these taints as they truly are, know the origin of these taints, know the cessation of these taints, and know the path to the cessation of these taints as it truly is. They know and see thus, and their mind is liberated from the taints of desire, existence, and ignorance. Once liberated, they know liberation. Birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, there will be no more continuation of existence, and they know it as it truly is."

「居士！彼有觉、有观息，内靖、一心，无觉、无观，定生喜、乐，得第二禅成就游。彼已离喜欲，舍无求游，正念正智而身觉乐，谓圣所说，圣所舍、念、乐住、空，得第三禅成就游。彼乐灭、苦灭，喜、忧本已灭，不苦不乐、舍、念、清净，得第四禅成就游。彼已如是定心清净，无秽无烦，柔软善住，得不动心，修学漏尽智通作证。彼知此苦如真，知此苦习、知此苦灭、知此苦灭道如真。知此漏如真，知此漏习、知此漏灭、知此漏灭道如真。彼如是知、如是见，欲漏心解脱，有漏、无明漏心解脱，解脱已便知解脱，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。」

When this teaching was spoken, the householder Pāṭaliya was free from defilements, and the Dharma eye arose for him. Then the householder Pāṭaliya saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, realized the pure Dharma, cut off doubts, crossed over perplexity, had no other teacher, was not dependent on others, was without hesitation, and abided in the realization of the fruits. He attained fearlessness in the World-Honored One's teachings, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and said: "World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept me as a lay follower, from this day forward for as long as I live. World-Honored One! I used to wear white clothes, wrap my head in white cloth, carry a staff, hold an umbrella, and wear lay shoes. I would wander from garden to garden, from pavilion to pavilion, from grove to grove, and say to the śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas I saw: 'Noble ones! I am leaving the household life, severing worldly ties, and abandoning worldly affairs.' Those śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas were gentle and kind, and said to me: 'Yes, noble Pāṭaliya is leaving the household life, severing worldly ties, and abandoning worldly affairs.' World-Honored One! At that time, I was actually without wisdom, yet I relied on wisdom. I was actually without wisdom in making offerings, and also without wisdom in eating. I was actually without wisdom in serving as a wise person. World-Honored One! From today onward, the Sangha and the World-Honored One's disciples are actually wise, relying on wisdom. They are actually wise in making offerings, and also wise in eating. They are actually wise in serving as wise people. World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha for a second time. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept me as a lay follower, from this day forward for as long as I live. World-Honored One! I have cut off my former faith, respect, and esteem for non-Buddhist śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas. World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha for a third time. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept me as a lay follower, from this day forward for as long as I live."

说此法时，晡利多居士远尘离垢，诸法法眼生。于是，晡利多居士见法得法，觉白净法，断疑度惑，更无余尊，不复由他，无有犹豫，已住果证，于世尊法得无所畏，稽首佛足，白曰：「世尊！我今自归于佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。世尊！我本著白净衣，白巾裹头，拄杖执盖，及著俗屣，从园至园，从观至观，从林至林，遍游行彷徉，若见诸沙门、梵志者，便作是语：『诸贤！我离俗、断俗，舍诸俗事。』彼诸沙门、梵志濡软柔和，语我言：『唯然。贤晡利多离俗、断俗，舍诸俗事。』世尊！我于尔时，彼实无智安著智处，实无智祠，又无智食，实无智，奉事如智慧人。世尊！我从今日，诸比丘众及世尊弟子，此实有智，安著智慧处，实有智祠，又有智食，实有智，奉事智慧人也。世尊！我今再自归佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。世尊！我本所信敬重外道沙门、梵志者，从今日断。世尊！我从今三自归佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The householder Pāṭaliya and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and practiced it.

佛说如是。晡利多居士及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
