MĀ 206

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 17 晡利多品

206. （二〇六）心秽经

Parallel to: DN 33 / DN 34 / MN 16 / AN 5.205 / AN 5.206 / AN 9.71 / AN 9.72 / AN 9.74–82 / AN 9.84–92 / AN 10.14 / DĀ 10 / EĀ 51.4

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One addressed the bhikṣus, saying, "If bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs do not uproot the five defilements in the mind and do not untie the five bonds in the mind, then I say that those bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs will certainly regress. What are the five defilements that are not uprooted? There may be one who has doubts about the World-Honored One, wavering, not opening the mind, not understanding the mind, and the mind not being at peace. If there is one who has doubts about the World-Honored One, wavering, not opening the mind, not understanding the mind, and the mind not being at peace, this is called not uprooting the first defilement in the mind, namely, towards the World-Honored One. It is the same for the Dharma, precepts, and teachings. If there are various virtuous practices praised by the World-Honored One, one then criticizes, belittles, troubles, and harms them, not opening the mind, not understanding the mind, and the mind not being at peace. This is called the fifth defilement not uprooted in the mind, namely, towards the virtuous practices.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「若比丘、比丘尼不拔心中五秽，不解心中五缚者，是为比丘、比丘尼说必退法。云何不拔心中五秽？或有一疑世尊，犹豫、不开意、不解意、意不靖。若有一疑世尊，犹豫、不开意、不解意、意不靖者，是谓不拔第一心秽，谓于世尊也，如是法、戒、教。若有诸梵行，世尊所称誉，彼便责数、轻易、触娆、侵害，不开意、不解意、意不靖，是谓第五不拔心中秽，谓于梵行也。

What are the five bonds in the mind that are not untied? There may be one whose body is not free from defilement, desire, attachment, and thirst. If one's body is not free from defilement, desire, attachment, and thirst, then one's mind does not incline towards it, is not peaceful, does not abide, and does not understand, and one practices solitary sitting by oneself. If one's mind does not incline towards it, is not peaceful, does not abide, and does not understand, and one practices solitary sitting by oneself, this is called the first bond in the mind that is not untied, namely, the body.

「云何不解心中五缚？或有一身不离染、不离欲、不离爱、不离渴。若有身不离染、不离欲、不离爱、不离渴者，彼心不趣向，不靖、不住、不解，自方便断燕坐。若有此心不趣向，不靖、不住、不解，自方便断燕坐者，是谓第一不解心缚，谓身也。复次，于欲不离染、不离欲、不离爱、不离渴。若有于欲不离染、不离欲、不离爱、不离渴者，彼心不趣向，不靖、不住、不解，自方便断燕坐。若有此心不趣向，不靖、不住、不解，自方便断燕坐者，是谓第二不解心缚，谓欲也。

Furthermore, there may be one who speaks of the holy meaning, which is soft and free from the obstructions of doubt, namely, speaking of precepts, concentration, wisdom, liberation, the knowledge and vision of liberation, diminishing, not gathering, few desires, contentment, cutting off, being without desire, extinguishing, solitary sitting, and dependent origination. If a bhikṣu's mind does not incline towards such a teaching by the śramaṇa, is not peaceful, does not abide, and does not understand, and he practices solitary sitting by himself, if his mind does not incline towards it, is not peaceful, does not abide, and does not understand, and he practices solitary sitting by himself, this is called the third bond in the mind that is not untied, namely, speaking.

「复次，有一所说圣义相应，柔软无疑盖，谓说戒、说定、说慧、说解脱、说解脱知见、说损、说不聚会、说少欲、说知足、说断、说无欲、说灭、说燕坐、说缘起，如是比丘，沙门所说者，彼心不趣向，不靖、不住、不解，自方便断燕坐。若有此心不趣向，不靖、不住、不解，自方便断燕坐者，是谓第三不解心缚，谓说也。复次，数道俗共会，调乱、憍慠、不学问。若有数道俗共会，调乱、憍慠、不学问者，彼心不趣向、不靖、不住、不解，自方便断燕坐。若此心不趣向，不靖、不住、不解，自方便断燕坐者，是谓第四不解心缚，谓聚会也。

Furthermore, because of having attained a little, one abides in the middle and does not seek further progress. If one abides in the middle because of having attained a little and does not seek further progress, then one's mind does not incline towards it, is not peaceful, does not abide, and does not understand, and one practices solitary sitting by oneself. If one's mind does not incline towards it, is not peaceful, does not abide, and does not understand, and one practices solitary sitting by oneself, this is called the fifth bond in the mind that is not untied, namely, progress.

「复次，少有所得故，于其中间住，不复求升进。若有少所得故，于其中间住，不复求升进者，彼心不趣向，不靖、不住、不解，自方便断燕坐。若此心不趣向，不靖、不住、不解，自方便断燕坐者，是谓第五不解心缚，谓升进也。若有比丘、比丘尼不拔此心中五秽，及不解此心中五缚者，是谓比丘、比丘尼必退法也。若有比丘、比丘尼善拔心中五秽，善解心中五缚者，是谓比丘、比丘尼清净法。

What is meant by well uprooting the five impurities in the mind? There may be one who has no doubts about the World-Honored One, is not hesitant, has an open mind, has mental understanding, and has a peaceful mind. If one has no doubts about the World-Honored One, is not hesitant, has an open mind, has mental understanding, and has a peaceful mind, this is called the first well-uprooted impurity in the mind, namely, towards the World-Honored One, the Dharma, the precepts, and the teachings.

「云何善拔心中五秽？或有一不疑世尊，不犹豫、开意、意解、意靖。若有不疑世尊，不犹豫、开意、意解、意靖者，是谓第一善拔心中秽，谓于世尊也，如是法、戒、教。若有梵行，世尊所称誉，彼不责数、不轻易、不触娆、不侵害，开意、意解、意靖，是谓第五善拔心中秽，谓于梵行也。

What is meant by unbinding the five bonds in the mind? There may be one who is free from defilement, desire, attachment, and thirst in the body. If one is free from defilement, desire, attachment, and thirst in the body, then one's mind inclines towards it, is peaceful, abides, and understands, and one practices solitary sitting by oneself. If one's mind inclines towards it, is peaceful, abides, and understands, and one practices solitary sitting by oneself, this is called the first unbinding of the bond in the mind, namely, towards the body.

「云何解心中五缚？或有一身离染、离欲、离爱、离渴。若有身离染、离欲、离爱、离渴者，彼心趣向，靖、住、解，自方便断燕坐。若有此心趣向，靖、住、解，自方便断燕坐者，是谓第一解心中缚，谓身也。复次，于欲离染、离欲、离爱、离渴。若有于欲离染、离欲、离爱、离渴者，彼心趣向，靖、住、解，自方便断燕坐。若有此心趣向，靖、住、解，自方便断燕坐者，是谓第二解心中缚，谓欲也。

Furthermore, there may be one who speaks in accordance with the meaning of the noble ones, is gentle, and has no doubts or obscurations, namely, speaking about precepts, concentration, wisdom, liberation, the knowledge and vision of liberation, diminishing, not gathering, few desires, contentment, abandoning, desirelessness, cessation, solitary sitting, and dependent origination. For such a bhikkhu, the teachings of the renunciant, his mind inclines towards it, is peaceful, abides, and understands, and he practices solitary sitting by himself. If his mind inclines towards it, is peaceful, abides, and understands, and he practices solitary sitting by himself, this is called the third unbinding of the bond in the mind, namely, towards the teachings.

「复次，有一所说，圣义相应，柔软无疑葢，谓说戒、说定、说慧、说解脱、说解脱知见、说损、说不聚会、说少欲、说知足、说断、说无欲、说灭、说燕坐、说缘起，如是比丘，沙门所说者，彼心趣向，靖、住、解，自方便断燕坐。若有此心趣向，靖、住、解，自方便断燕坐者，是谓第三解心中缚，谓说也。复次，不数道俗共会，不调乱、不憍慠学问。若有不数道俗共会，不调乱、不憍慠学问者，彼心趣向，靖、住、解，自方便断燕坐。若有此心趣向，靖、住、解，自方便断燕坐者，是谓第四解心中缚，谓不聚会也。

Furthermore, because of having attained a little, one does not dwell in between, but seeks further progress. If one, because of having attained a little, does not dwell in between, but seeks further progress, then one's mind inclines towards it, is peaceful, abides, and understands, and one practices solitary sitting by oneself. If one's mind inclines towards it, is peaceful, abides, and understands, and one practices solitary sitting by oneself, this is called the fifth unbinding of the bond in the mind, namely, towards further progress.

「复次，少有所得故，于其中间不住，复求升进。若有少所得故，于其中间不住，复求升进者，彼心趣向，靖、住、解，自方便断燕坐。若此心趣向，靖、住、解，自方便断燕坐者，是谓第五解心中缚，谓升进也。若有比丘、比丘尼善拔此心中五秽，及善解此心中五缚者，是谓比丘、比丘尼清净法。彼住此十支已，复修习五法。

"What are the five? Cultivating the concentration of desire, accomplishing the destruction of the supernormal power of the mind, based on detachment, based on lack of desire, based on cessation, based on letting go, heading towards the non-category. Cultivating the concentration of effort, the concentration of mind, and the concentration of contemplation, accomplishing the destruction of the supernormal power of contemplation, based on detachment, based on lack of desire, based on cessation, based on letting go, heading towards the non-category. Enduring the fifth, he accomplishes these fifteen qualities of endurance and self-acceptance. For one who accomplishes self-acceptance, there is definitely knowledge, definitely seeing, definitely right full awakening, definitely reaching the gate of immortality, definitely approaching nirvana. I say there is no one who does not reach nirvana. It is like a hen hatching ten or twelve eggs, covering them, warming them, and watching over them at the right times. If the hen is negligent, some of the eggs may be pecked open by the chicks with their beaks and they emerge safely. That is the best. In the same way, a bhikkhu who accomplishes these fifteen qualities of endurance and self-acceptance, self-acceptance, definitely knows, definitely sees, definitely right full awakens, definitely reaches the gate of immortality, definitely approaches nirvana. I say there is no one who does not attain nirvana."

「云何为五？修欲定心成就断如意足，依离、依无欲、依灭、依舍，趣向非品，修精进定、心定、思惟定成就断如意足，依离、依无欲、依灭、依舍，趣向非品，堪任第五，彼成就此堪任等十五法。成就自受者，必知必见，必正尽觉，至甘露门，近住涅槃，我说无不至涅槃。犹如鸡生十卵，或十二，随时覆盖，随时温暖，随时看视，鸡设有放逸者，彼中或鸡子以［此／束］以足，啄破其卵，自安隐出者，彼为第一。如是比丘成就此堪任等十五法自受者，必知必见，必正尽觉，必至甘露门，近住涅槃，我说无不得涅槃。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
