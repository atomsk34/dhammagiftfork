MĀ 207

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 17 晡利多品

207. （二〇七）箭毛经

Parallel to: MN 77

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was dwelling in the Bamboo Grove Kalanda-nivāpa near Rājagṛha, together with a great assembly of 1,250 bhikṣus, who were spending the summer retreat.

一时，佛游王舍城，在竹林伽兰哆园，与大比丘众俱，千二百五十人而受夏坐。

At that time, the World-Honored One, having passed the night, put on his robe in the early morning, took his alms bowl, and entered the city of Rājagṛha to beg for alms. Having finished begging for alms, he put away his robe and bowl, washed his hands and feet, placed his sitting mat on his shoulder, and went to the park of the non-Buddhist practitioners in the Peacock Grove.

尔时，世尊过夜平旦，著衣持钵，入王舍城而行乞食。行乞食已，收举衣钵，澡洗手足，以尼师檀著于肩上，往至孔雀林异学园中。

At that time, in the park of the non-Buddhist practitioners in the Peacock Grove, there was a non-Buddhist practitioner named Ajaramūka, a teacher of great renown and respect, who led a large assembly of five hundred non-Buddhist practitioners. He was making a great commotion in the assembly, speaking loudly in various animal-like ways, discussing kings, thieves, fighting, food, clothing, women, girls, prostitutes, the world, the wilderness, the ocean, and the people of the country. They were sitting together discussing such animal-like topics. When Ajaramūka saw the Buddha coming from afar, he ordered his assembly, "All of you, be silent. That Śramaṇa Gautama is coming. His assembly is always silent, they enjoy silence, and they praise silence. If he sees this assembly silent, he may come to see us." Having made his assembly silent, Ajaramūka himself remained silent.

尔时，孔雀林异学园中有一异学，名曰箭毛，名德宗主，众人所师，有大名誉，众所敬重，领大徒众，五百异学之所尊也。彼在大众喧閙娆乱，放高大音声，说种种畜生之论，谓论王、论贼、论鬪、论食、论衣服、论妇人、论童女、论婬女、论世间、论空野、论海中、论国人民。彼共集坐论如是比畜生之论。异学箭毛遥见佛来，勅己众曰：「汝等默然住，彼沙门瞿昙来。彼众默然，常乐默然，称说默然。彼若见此众默然者，或来相见。」异学箭毛令众默然已，自默然住。

The World-Honored One went to where the non-Buddhist practitioner Ajaramūka was. Ajaramūka immediately rose from his seat, bared one shoulder, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "Welcome, Śramaṇa Gautama! Śramaṇa Gautama has not come here for a long time. Please sit on this seat." The World-Honored One then sat on the seat prepared by the non-Buddhist practitioner Ajaramūka. Ajaramūka then exchanged greetings with the World-Honored One and sat to one side.

世尊往诣异学箭毛所，异学箭毛即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「善来，沙门瞿昙！沙门瞿昙久不来此，愿坐此坐。」世尊便坐异学箭毛所敷之座，异学箭毛则与世尊共相问讯，却坐一面。

The World-Honored One asked, "Udayin, what were you discussing just now? What was the matter that brought you all together here?"

世尊问曰：「优陀夷！向论何等？以何事故共集坐此？」

The non-Buddhist practitioner Ajaramūka replied, "Gautama, let us put aside this discussion. It is not a good discussion. If the Śramaṇa Gautama wishes to hear this discussion, it will not be difficult to hear it later."

异学箭毛答曰：「瞿昙！且置此论，此论非妙，沙门瞿昙欲闻此论，后闻不难。」

The World-Honored One asked this way twice more, "Udayin, what were you discussing just now? What was the matter that brought you all together here?"

世尊如是再三问曰：「优陀夷！向论何等？以何事故共集坐此？」

The non-Buddhist practitioner Ajaramūka also answered this way twice more, "Gautama, let us put aside this discussion. It is not a good discussion. If the Śramaṇa Gautama wishes to hear this discussion, it will not be difficult to hear it later. If the Śramaṇa Gautama asks this way twice more, if he wishes to hear it, I will now explain it. Gautama, we and many brahmins from the country of Kosala were all gathered together in the Kosala schoolhouse, discussing this: 'The people of the countries of Aṅga and Magadha have great benefits, the people of Aṅga and Magadha have great benefits, because such a great field of merit is together in Rājagṛha for the summer retreat, namely, Nirgrantha Jñātiputra. Why is that? Gautama, Nirgrantha Jñātiputra is the leader of a famous and virtuous school, the teacher of many people, with great fame and respect, leading a large group of five hundred non-Buddhist practitioners who revere him. He is together in Rājagṛha for the summer retreat, as well as Maskarin Gośālīputra, Sañjayin Vairaṭīputra, Ajita Keśakambalin, Kakuda Kātyāyana, and Ajita Keśakambalin.

异学箭毛亦再三答曰：「瞿昙！且置此论，此论非妙，沙门瞿昙欲闻此论，后闻不难，沙门瞿昙若至再三，其欲闻者，今当说之。瞿昙！我等与拘萨罗国众多梵志，悉共集坐拘萨罗学堂，说如是论。鸯伽摩竭陀国人有大善利，鸯伽摩竭陀国人得大善利，如此大福田众在王舍城共受夏坐，谓不兰迦叶。所以者何？瞿昙！不兰迦叶名德宗主，众人所师，有大名誉，众所敬重，领大徒众，五百异学之所尊也。于此王舍城共受夏坐，如是摩息迦利瞿舍利子、娑若鞞罗迟子、尼揵亲子、彼复迦栴、阿夷哆鸡舍剑婆利。

Gautama, Ajita Keśakambalin is the leader of a famous and virtuous school, the teacher of many people, with great fame and respect, leading a large group of five hundred non-Buddhist practitioners who revere him. He is together in Rājagṛha for the summer retreat. We also discussed this: 'The Śramaṇa Gautama, the Śramaṇa Gautama is the leader of a famous and virtuous school, the teacher of many people, with great fame and respect, leading a large group of one thousand two hundred and fifty bhikṣus who revere him. He is also together in Rājagṛha for the summer retreat.' Gautama, we also thought like this: 'Now, which of these venerable śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas are revered, respected, honored, and venerated by their disciples? Are they not reviled by their disciples according to the Dharma? Do their disciples not challenge their teachers? This is unacceptable, it is not appropriate, it is not equal to what is said, and then they leave.' Gautama, we also thought like this: 'Nirgrantha Jñātiputra is not revered, respected, honored, and venerated by his disciples. He is reviled by his disciples according to the Dharma. Many of his disciples challenge their teacher. This is unacceptable, this is not appropriate, this is not equal to what is said, and then they leave.'

「瞿昙！阿夷哆鸡舍剑婆利名德宗主，众人所师，有大名誉，众所敬重，领大徒众，五百异学之所尊也，于此王舍城共受夏坐。向者亦论沙门瞿昙！此沙门瞿昙名德宗主，众人所师，有大名誉，众所敬重，领大比丘众，千二百五十人之所尊也，亦在此王舍城共受夏坐。瞿昙！我等复作是念：『今此诸尊沙门、梵志，谁为弟子所恭敬、尊重、供养奉] cf.［丽】事耶？非为弟子法骂所骂，亦无弟子难师，此一向不可、不相应，不等说已，便舍而去。』瞿昙！我等复作是念：『此不兰迦叶不为弟子所恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事，为弟子法骂所骂，众多弟子难师，此不可，此不相应，此不等说已，便舍而去。』

Gautama, in the past, Nirgrantha Jñātiputra often raised his hand in the midst of his disciples and shouted, 'You should stay. No one will come to ask you about things. If someone asks me about things, you cannot resolve this matter. I can resolve this matter.' But in the meantime, his disciples discussed other matters and did not wait for the teacher to finish speaking. Gautama, we also thought like this: 'In this way, Nirgrantha Jñātiputra is not revered, respected, honored, and venerated by his disciples. He is reviled by his disciples according to the Dharma. Many of his disciples challenge their teacher. This is unacceptable, it is not appropriate. This is not equal to what is said, and then they leave.' In this way, Maskarin Gośālīputra, Sañjayin Vairaṭīputra, Ajita Keśakambalin, Kakuda Kātyāyana, and Ajita Keśakambalin. Gautama, we also thought like this: 'Ajita Keśakambalin is not revered, respected, honored, and venerated by his disciples. He is reviled by his disciples according to the Dharma. Many of his disciples challenge their teacher. This is unacceptable, it is not appropriate. This is not equal to what is said, and then they leave.' Gautama, in the past, Ajita Keśakambalin often raised his hand in the midst of his disciples and shouted, 'You should stay. No one will come to ask you about things. If someone asks me about things, you cannot resolve this matter. I can resolve this matter.' But in the meantime, his disciples discussed other matters and did not wait for the teacher to finish speaking.

「瞿昙！昔时不兰迦叶数在弟子众举手大唤：『汝等可住，无有人来问汝等事，人问我事，汝等不能断此事，我能断此事。』而弟子于其中间更论余事，不待师说事讫。瞿昙！我等复作是念：『如是此不兰迦叶不为弟子所恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事，为弟子法骂所骂，众多弟子难师：此不可，此不相应。此不等说已，便舍而去。』如是摩息加利瞿舍利子、娑若鞞罗迟子、尼揵亲子、彼复迦旃、阿夷哆鸡舍剑婆利。瞿昙！我等作如是念：『此阿夷哆鸡舍剑婆利不为弟子所恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事，为弟子法骂所骂，众多弟子难师：此不可，此不相应，此不等说已，便舍而去。』瞿昙！昔时阿夷哆鸡舍剑婆利数在弟子众举手大唤：『汝等可住，无有人来问汝等事，人问我事，汝等不能断此事，我能断此事。』而弟子于其中间更论余事，不待师说事讫。

Gautama, we also thought like this: 'In this way, Ajita Keśakambalin is not revered, respected, honored, and venerated by his disciples. He is reviled by his disciples according to the Dharma. Many of his disciples challenge their teacher. This is unacceptable, it is not appropriate. This is not equal to what is said, and then they leave.'

「瞿昙！我等复作是念：『如是此阿夷哆鸡舍剑婆利不为弟子所恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事，为弟子法骂所骂，众多弟子难师：此不可，此不相应，此不等说已，便舍而去。』

"Gautama, we also thought like this: 'This Śramaṇa Gautama is revered, respected, honored, and venerated by his disciples. He is not reviled by his disciples according to the Dharma. His disciples do not challenge their teacher. This is acceptable, it is appropriate. This is equal to what is said, and they do not leave.' Gautama, in the past, Śramaṇa Gautama often spoke the Dharma surrounded by a great assembly of countless hundreds of thousands. In the midst of that, one person snored loudly. Another person said to that person, 'Do not snore loudly! Do you not want to hear the subtle Dharma spoken by the World-Honored One, like nectar?' That person then fell silent. Gautama, we also thought like this: 'In this way, this Śramaṇa Gautama is revered, respected, honored, and venerated by his disciples. He is not reviled by his disciples according to the Dharma. His disciples do not challenge their teacher. This is acceptable, it is appropriate. This is equal to what is said, and they do not leave.'"

「瞿昙！我等复作是念：『此沙门瞿昙为弟子所恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事，不为弟子法骂所骂，亦无弟子难师：此不可，此不相应，此不等说已，便舍而去。』瞿昙！昔时沙门瞿昙数在大众，无量百千众围遶说法，于其中有一人鼾眠作声，又有一人语彼人曰：『莫鼾眠作声！汝不欲闻世尊说微妙法，如甘露耶？』彼人即便默然无声。瞿昙！我等复作是念：『如是此沙门瞿昙为弟子所恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事，不为弟子法骂所骂，亦无弟子难师：此不可，此不相应，此不等说已，便舍而去。』」

The World-Honored One, having heard this, asked the non-Buddhist Ārādakālāma, "Udayin, what do you see as the qualities I possess that cause my disciples to revere, respect, honor, and venerate me, and always follow me without leaving?"

世尊闻已，问异学箭毛曰：「优陀夷！汝见我有几法，令诸弟子恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事我，常随不离耶？」

The non-Buddhist Ārādakālāma replied, "Gautama, I see that the Gautama has five qualities that cause his disciples to revere, respect, honor, and venerate him, and always follow him without leaving. What are the five? The Gautama is content with coarse robes, and praises contentment with coarse robes. If the Gautama is content with coarse robes, and praises contentment with coarse robes, this is the first quality I see in the Gautama that causes his disciples to revere, respect, honor, and venerate him, and always follow him without leaving. Furthermore, the Gautama is content with coarse food, and praises contentment with coarse food. If the Gautama is content with coarse food, and praises contentment with coarse food, this is the second quality I see in the Gautama that causes his disciples to revere, respect, honor, and venerate him, and always follow him without leaving.

异学箭毛答曰：「瞿昙！我见瞿昙有五法，令诸弟子恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事，常随不离。云何为五？沙门瞿昙麤衣知足，称说麤衣知足，若沙门瞿昙麤衣知足，称说麤衣知足者，是谓我见沙门瞿昙有第一法，令诸弟子恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事，常随不离。复次，沙门瞿昙麤食知足，称说麤食知足，若沙门瞿昙麤食知足，称说麤食知足者，是谓我见沙门瞿昙有第二法，令诸弟子恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事，常随不离。

"Furthermore, the Gautama eats little, and praises eating little. If the Gautama eats little, and praises eating little, this is the third quality I see in the Gautama that causes his disciples to revere, respect, honor, and venerate him, and always follow him without leaving. Furthermore, the Gautama is content with coarse dwellings, beds, and seats, and praises contentment with coarse dwellings, beds, and seats. If the Gautama is content with coarse dwellings, beds, and seats, and praises contentment with coarse dwellings, beds, and seats, this is the fourth quality I see in the Gautama that causes his disciples to revere, respect, honor, and venerate him, and always follow him without leaving. Furthermore, the Gautama practices meditation, and praises meditation. If the Gautama practices meditation, and praises meditation, this is the fifth quality I see in the Gautama that causes his disciples to revere, respect, honor, and venerate him, and always follow him without leaving. These are the five qualities I see in the Gautama that cause his disciples to revere, respect, honor, and venerate him, and always follow him without leaving."

「复次，沙门瞿昙少食，称说少食，若沙门瞿昙少食，称说少食者，是谓我见沙门瞿昙有第三法，令诸弟子恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事，常随不离。复次，沙门瞿昙麤住止床座知足，称说麤住止床座知足，若沙门瞿昙麤住止床座知足，称说麤住止床座知足者，是谓我见沙门瞿昙有第四法，令诸弟子恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事，常随不离。复次，沙门瞿昙燕坐，称说燕坐，若沙门瞿昙燕坐，称说燕坐者，是谓我见沙门瞿昙有第五法，令诸弟子恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事，常随不离。是谓我见沙门瞿昙有五法，令诸弟子恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事，常随不离。」

The World-Honored One said, "Udayin, I do not use these five qualities to cause my disciples to revere, respect, honor, and venerate me, and always follow me without leaving. Udayin, the robes I wear are cut and dyed with the power of the sages, and are of a bad color. Such are the robes of the sages, dyed with a bad color. Udayin, my disciples may say that they wear robes discarded by others for their entire lives, and also say, 'Our World-Honored One is content with coarse robes, and praises contentment with coarse robes.' Udayin, if my disciples praise me because of contentment with coarse robes, they will not revere, respect, honor, and venerate me because of that, nor will they follow me. Furthermore, Udayin, I eat cooked rice with no bran and many flavors. Udayin, my disciples may beg for food discarded by others for their entire lives, and also say, 'Our World-Honored One is content with coarse food, and praises contentment with coarse food.' Udayin, if my disciples praise me because of contentment with coarse food,

世尊告曰：「优陀夷！我不以此五法，令诸弟子恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事我，常随不离。优陀夷！我所持衣，随圣力割截，染污恶色，如是圣衣染污恶色。优陀夷！或我弟子谓尽形寿衣所弃舍粪扫之衣，亦作是说：『我世尊麤衣知足，称说麤衣知足。』优陀夷！若我弟子因麤衣知足故，称说我者，彼因此处故，不恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事我，亦不相随。复次，优陀夷！我食粳粮成熟，无［麸－夫＋黄］无量杂味。优陀夷！或我弟子尽其形寿而行乞食所弃舍食，亦作是说：『我世尊麤食知足，称说麤食知足。』优陀夷！若我弟子因麤食知足故，称说我者，彼因此处故，不恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事我，亦不相随。

Furthermore, Udayin, I eat one meal, or half a meal. Udayin, my disciples may eat one meal, or half a meal, and also say, 'Our World-Honored One eats little, and praises eating little.' Udayin, if my disciples praise me because of eating little, they will not revere, respect, honor, and venerate me because of that, nor will they follow me. Furthermore, Udayin, I sometimes stay in a high building or a pavilion. Udayin, my disciples may spend more than nine or ten months staying under a roof at night, and also say, 'Our World-Honored One is content with coarse dwellings, beds, and seats, and praises contentment with coarse dwellings, beds, and seats.' Udayin, if my disciples praise me because of contentment with coarse dwellings, beds, and seats,

「复次，优陀夷！我食如一鞞罗食，或如半鞞罗，优陀夷！或我弟子食如一拘拖，或如半拘拖，亦作是说：『我世尊少食，称说少食。』优陀夷！若我弟子因少食故，称说我者，彼因此处故，不恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事我，亦不相随。复次，优陀夷！我或住高楼，或住棚阁。优陀夷！或我弟子彼过九月、十月，一夜于覆处宿，亦作是说：『我世尊麤住止床座知足，称说麁住止床座知足。』优陀夷！若我弟子因麤住止床座知足故，称说我者，彼因此处故，不恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事我，亦不相随。

Furthermore, Udayin, I always associate with noisy monks, nuns, male lay followers, and female lay followers. Udayin, my disciples may enter the assembly once every half month for the sake of purity in the Dharma, and also say, 'Our World-Honored One is solitary, and praises solitude.' Udayin, if my disciples praise me because of solitude, they will not revere, respect, honor, and venerate me because of that, nor will they follow me. Udayin, I do not have these five qualities to cause my disciples to revere, respect, honor, and venerate me, and always follow me without leaving.

「复次，优陀夷！我常作閙比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆夷，或我弟子过半月一入众，为法清净故，亦作是说：『我世尊燕坐，称说燕坐。』优陀夷！若我弟子因燕坐故，称说我者，彼因此处故，不恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事我，亦不相随。优陀夷！我无此五法，令诸弟子恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事我，常随不离。

Udayin, I have five more qualities that cause my disciples to revere, respect, honor, and venerate me, and always follow me without leaving. What are the five? Udayin, I have disciples who praise me for unsurpassed morality, saying, 'The World-Honored One practices morality and great morality. As he speaks, so he acts. As he acts, so he speaks.' Udayin, if my disciples praise me because of unsurpassed morality, they will revere, respect, honor, and venerate me because of that, and always follow me without leaving. Furthermore, Udayin, I have disciples who praise me for unsurpassed wisdom, saying, 'The World-Honored One practices wisdom and great wisdom. When there is discussion and debate, he is able to subdue it. He says that in the true Dharma and Vinaya, there is nothing to be said, and nothing to be said about what he has said.' Udayin, if my disciples praise me because of unsurpassed wisdom,

「优陀夷！我更有五法，令诸弟子恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事我，常随不离。云何为五？优陀夷！我有弟子，谓无上戒称说我：『世尊行戒大戒，如所说所作亦然，如所作所说亦然。』优陀夷！若我弟子因无上戒称说我者，彼因此处，恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事我，常随不离。复次，优陀夷！我有弟子，谓无上智慧称说我：『世尊行智慧，极大智慧。若有谈论来相对者，必能伏之，谓于正法、律不可说，于自所说不可得说。』优陀夷！若我弟子因无上智慧故，称说我者，彼因此处，恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事我，常随不离。

Furthermore, Udayin, I have disciples who praise me for unsurpassed knowledge and vision, saying, 'The World-Honored One knows what is knowable and sees what is visible. He teaches the Dharma to his disciples, with causes and not without causes, with conditions and not without conditions, and can be answered and not cannot be answered, with detachment and not without detachment.' Udayin, if my disciples praise me because of unsurpassed knowledge and vision, they will revere, respect, honor, and venerate me because of that, and always follow me without leaving. Furthermore, Udayin, I have disciples who come to ask me about the arrow of craving, saying, 'Suffering is suffering, origination is origination, cessation is cessation, and path is path.' I then answer them, 'Suffering is suffering, origination is origination, cessation is cessation, and path is path.' Udayin, if my disciples come to ask me, and I answer to their satisfaction and delight them, they will revere, respect, honor, and venerate me because of that, and always follow me without leaving.

「复次，优陀夷！我有弟子，谓无上知见称说我：『世尊游知非不知，游见非不见，彼为弟子说法，有因非无因，有缘非无缘，可答非不可答，有离非无离。』优陀夷！若我弟子因无上知见故，称说我者，彼因此处，恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事我，常随不离。复次，优陀夷！我有弟子，谓厌爱箭而来问我：『苦是苦，习是习，灭是灭，道是道。』我即答彼：『苦是苦，习是习，灭是灭，道是道。』优陀夷！若我弟子而来问我，我答可意令欢喜者，彼因此处，恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事我，常随不离。

"Furthermore, Udayin, I use the supernormal power of knowledge of past lives to prove and realize for my disciples, or the supernormal power of knowledge of the exhaustion of taints to prove and realize for them. Udayin, if my disciples attain acceptance, attainment, and the other shore in this true Dharma and Vinaya, without doubt or confusion, and are without hesitation in the good Dharma, they will revere, respect, honor, and venerate me because of that, and always follow me without leaving. Udayin, these are the five more qualities that cause my disciples to revere, respect, honor, and venerate me, and always follow me without leaving."

「复次，优陀夷！我为弟子，或说宿命智通作证明达，或说漏尽智通作证明达。优陀夷！若我弟子于此正法、律中得受得度，得至彼岸，无疑无惑，于善法中无有犹豫者，彼因此处，恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事我，常随不离。优陀夷！是谓我更有五法，令诸弟子恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事我，常随不离。」

Then the wanderer Arittha got up from his seat, put his upper robe over one shoulder, put his palms together toward the Buddha, and said, "Gautama, it is wonderful, it is marvelous! You have spoken well about the wonderful things that nourish my body like nectar. Gautama, just as a great rain moistens the high and low places on this earth, so the śramaṇa Gautama has spoken well to us about the wonderful things that nourish our bodies like nectar. World-Honored One, I understand! Well-Gone One, I know! World-Honored One, I now take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha of monks. May the World-Honored One accept me as a lay follower, from this day forward for as long as I live, taking refuge until the end of my life."

于是，异学箭毛即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「瞿昙！甚奇！甚特！善说妙事，润泽我体，犹如甘露。瞿昙！犹如大雨，此地高下，普得润泽。如是，沙门瞿昙为我等善说妙事，润泽我体，犹如甘露。世尊！我已解。善逝！我已知。世尊！我今自归于佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The wanderer Arittha, having heard the Buddha's words, was delighted and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。异学箭毛闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
