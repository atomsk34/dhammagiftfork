MĀ 208

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 17 晡利多品

208. （二〇八）箭毛经

Parallel to: MN 79

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was dwelling in the Bamboo Grove, in the Kalandaka Park, in the city of Rājagṛha.

一时，佛游王舍城，在竹林加兰哆园。

At that time, the World-Honored One, having passed the night, put on his robe, took his alms bowl, entered the city of Rājagṛha to beg for alms. Having finished begging for alms, he put away his robe and bowl, washed his hands and feet, placed his sitting mat on his shoulder, and went to the park of the non-Buddhist practitioners in the Peacock Forest.

尔时，世尊过夜平旦，著衣持钵，入王舍城而行乞食。行乞食已，收举衣钵，澡洗手足，以尼师檀著于肩上，往至孔雀林异学园中。

At that time, in the park of the non-Buddhist practitioners in the Peacock Forest, there was a non-Buddhist practitioner named Ajaramāta, a teacher of great virtue and fame, respected and revered by many people, leading a large group of five hundred non-Buddhist practitioners. He was making a great commotion in the assembly, speaking loudly in various animal-like discussions, such as discussions about kings, thieves, fighting, food, clothing, women, girls, prostitutes, the world, the wilderness, the ocean, and the people of the country. They were sitting together discussing such animal-like topics. Ajaramāta saw the Buddha coming from afar and ordered his assembly, "All of you, be silent. That Śramaṇa Gautama is coming. His assembly is always silent, he praises silence, and he speaks of silence. If he sees this assembly silent, he may come to see us." After Ajaramāta ordered his assembly to be silent, he himself remained silent.

尔时，孔雀林异学园中有一异学，名曰箭毛，名德宗主，众人所师，有大名誉，众所敬重，领大徒众，五百异学之所尊也。彼在大众喧閙娆乱，放高大音声，说种种畜生之论，谓论王、论贼、论鬪、论食、论衣服、论妇人、论童女、论婬女、论世间、论空野、论海中、论国人民。彼共集坐说如是比畜生之论。异学箭毛遥见佛来，勅己众曰：「汝等默然住，彼沙门瞿昙来，彼众默然，常乐默然，称说默然，彼若见此众默然者，或来相见。」异学箭毛命众默然已，自默然住。

The World-Honored One went to where the non-Buddhist practitioner Ajaramāta was. Ajaramāta immediately rose from his seat, bared one shoulder, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "Welcome, Śramaṇa Gautama! Śramaṇa Gautama has not come here for a long time. Please sit on this seat." The World-Honored One then sat on the seat prepared by the non-Buddhist practitioner Ajaramāta. Ajaramāta then exchanged greetings with the World-Honored One and sat to one side.

世尊往诣异学箭毛所，异学箭毛即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「善来！沙门瞿昙！沙门瞿昙久不来此，愿坐此坐。」世尊便坐异学箭毛所敷之座，异学箭毛便与世尊共相问讯，却坐一面。

The World-Honored One asked, "Udayin! What were you discussing just now? What was the matter that you were sitting together here for?"

世尊问曰：「优陀夷！向论何等？以何事故共集坐此？」

The non-Buddhist practitioner Ajaramāta replied, "Gautama! Let us put aside this discussion. This discussion is not wonderful. If the Śramaṇa Gautama wishes to hear this discussion, it will not be difficult to hear it later."

异学箭毛答曰：「瞿昙！且置此论，此论非妙，沙门瞿昙欲闻此论，后闻不难。」

The World-Honored One asked this way twice, saying, "Udayin! What were you discussing just now? What was the matter that you were sitting together here for?"

世尊如是再三问曰：「优陀夷！向论何等？以何事故共集坐此？」

The non-Buddhist practitioner Ajaramāta also answered this way twice, saying, "Gautama! Let us put aside this discussion. This discussion is not wonderful. If the Śramaṇa Gautama wishes to hear this discussion, it will not be difficult to hear it later. If the Śramaṇa Gautama asks this way three times, if he wishes to hear it, I will now explain it. Gautama! I have contemplation, have reflection, dwell in the ground of contemplation, dwell in the ground of reflection, have wisdom, have eloquence, have the real, have the realization of all, know all, see all, have no more to know, have no more to see. I went to ask about matters, but he did not know. Gautama! I thought, 'What is this?'"

异学箭毛亦再三答曰：「瞿昙！且置此论，此论非妙，沙门瞿昙欲闻此论，后闻不难，沙门瞿昙若至再三，其欲闻者，今当说之。瞿昙！我有策虑，有思惟，住策虑地，住思惟地，有智慧，有辩才，有说实有萨云然，一切知，一切见，无余知，无余见，我往问事，然彼不知。瞿昙！我作是念：『此是何等耶？』」

The World-Honored One asked, "Udayin! Do you have contemplation, have reflection, dwell in the ground of contemplation, dwell in the ground of reflection, have wisdom, have eloquence, who says there is the real realization of all, knows all, sees all, has no more to know, has no more to see, you went to ask about matters, but he did not know?"

世尊问曰：「优陀夷！汝有策虑，有思惟，住] cf.［丽】策虑地，住思惟地，有智慧，有辩才，谁说实有萨云然，一切知，一切见，无余知，无余见，汝往问事，而彼不知耶？」

The non-Buddhist practitioner Ajaramāta answered, "Gautama! It is Nirgrantha Jñātiputra. Why is that? Gautama! Nirgrantha Jñātiputra himself says there is the real realization of all, knows all, sees all, has no more to know, has no more to see. I have contemplation, have reflection, dwell in the ground of contemplation, dwell in the ground of reflection, have wisdom, have eloquence, I went to ask about matters, but he did not know. Gautama! Therefore I thought, 'What is this?'" In the same way, "Māsika, Kūṭadanta, Sañjayin Vairaṭṭīputra, Nigantha Nātaputta, Pukkusāti, and Ajita Keśakambalin. Gautama! Ajita Keśakambalin himself says there is the real realization of all, knows all, sees all, has no more to know, has no more to see. I have contemplation, have reflection, dwell in the ground of contemplation, dwell in the ground of reflection, have wisdom, have eloquence, I went to ask about matters, but he did not know. Gautama! Therefore I thought, 'What is this?'" Gautama! I further thought, 'If I were to go to the Śramaṇa Gautama and ask about past matters, the Śramaṇa Gautama would certainly be able to answer me about past matters. I should go to the Śramaṇa Gautama and ask about future matters, the Śramaṇa Gautama would certainly be able to answer me about future matters. Moreover, if I were to ask the Śramaṇa Gautama about any matter, the Śramaṇa Gautama would certainly also answer me about any matter I ask.'

异学箭毛答曰：「瞿昙！谓不兰迦叶是。所以者何？瞿昙！不兰迦叶自说实有萨云然，一切知，一切见，无余知，无余见也。我有策虑，有思惟，住策虑地，住思惟地，有智慧，有辩才，我往问事，然彼不知。瞿昙！是故我作是念：『此是何等耶？』如是摩息迦利瞿舍利子、娑若鞞罗迟子、尼揵亲子、彼复迦旃、阿夷哆鸡舍剑婆利。瞿昙！阿夷哆鸡舍剑婆利自说实有萨云然，一切知，一切见，无余知，无余见也。我有策虑，有思惟，住策虑地，住思惟地，有智慧，有辩才，我往问事，然彼不知。瞿昙！是故我作是念：『此是何等耶？』瞿昙！我复作是念：『若我当往诣沙门瞿昙所，问过去事者，沙门瞿昙必能答我过去事也。我当往诣沙门瞿昙所，问未来事者，沙门瞿昙必能答我未来事也。复次，若我随所问沙门瞿昙事者，沙门瞿昙必亦答我随所问事。』」

The World-Honored One said, "Udayin! Stop! Stop! You have long had different views, different tolerance, different joy, different desires, and different intentions, so you cannot fully know the meaning of what I say. Udayin! I have disciples who, with causes and conditions, remember countless past lives, namely one life, two lives, a hundred lives, a thousand lives, an eon of formation, an eon of destruction, countless eons of formation and destruction. They say, 'I was once born there, with such a surname, such a name, such a birth, such food and drink, such suffering and happiness, such a long life, such a long stay, such a life span. I died there and was born here, I died here and was born there, I was born here, with such a surname, such a name, such a birth, such food and drink, such suffering and happiness, such a long life, such a long stay, such a life span.' They come to ask me about past matters, and I answer them about past matters. I also go to ask them about past matters, and they also answer me about past matters. I ask them about any matter, and they also answer me about any matter I ask.

世尊告曰：「优陀夷！止！止！汝长夜异见、异忍、异乐、异欲、异意故，不得尽知我所说义。优陀夷！我有弟子有因有缘，忆无量过去本昔所生，谓一生、二生、百生、千生，成劫、败劫、无量成败劫，众生名某，我曾生彼，如是姓、如是字，如是生、如是饮食、如是受苦乐、如是长寿、如是久住、如是寿讫。此死生彼，彼死生此，我生在此，如是姓、如是字、如是生、如是饮食、如是受苦乐、如是长寿、如是久住、如是寿讫。彼来问我过去事，我答彼过去事，我亦往问彼过去事，彼亦答我过去事，我随所问彼事，彼亦答我随所问事。

Furthermore, Udayin! I have disciples who, with the pure divine eye surpassing that of humans, see this assembly of beings dying and being born, good color and bad color, good and bad, going and coming to good places and bad places, according to the deeds of these beings, seeing them as they truly are. If these beings have perfected evil bodily action, evil verbal and mental action, have slandered the noble ones, have wrong views, and have perfected wrong view karma, because of this, when their body breaks up and their life ends, they will definitely go to a bad place, to a hell. If these beings have perfected good bodily action, good verbal and mental action, have not slandered the noble ones, have right views, and have perfected right view karma, because of this, when their body breaks up and their life ends, they will definitely ascend to a good place, to be born in heaven. They come to ask me about future matters, and I answer them about future matters. I also go to ask them about future matters, and they also answer me about future matters. I ask them about any matter, and they also answer me about any matter I ask.

「复次，优陀夷！我有弟子，谓清净天眼出过于人，见此众生死时生时、好色恶色、妙与不妙，往来善处及不善处，随此众生之所作业，见其如真。若此众生成就身恶行，成就口、意恶行，诬谤圣人，邪见，成就邪见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。若此众生成就身妙行，成就口、意妙行，不诬谤圣人，正见，成就正见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必升善处，得生天中。彼来问我未来事，我答彼未来事，我亦往问彼未来事，彼亦答我未来事，我随所问彼事，彼亦答我随所问事。」

The non-Buddhist Āraṇyaka said, "Gautama! If it is so, I am even more ignorant, I am even more blind, even more foolish and deluded, namely the śramaṇa Gautama says: 'Udayin! Stop! Stop! You have long had different views, different tolerance, different joy, different desires, and different intentions, so you cannot fully know the meaning of what I say. Udayin! I have disciples who, with causes and conditions, remember countless past lives, namely one life, two lives, a hundred lives, a thousand lives, an eon of formation, an eon of destruction, countless eons of formation and destruction. They say, 'I was once born there, with such a surname, such a name, such a birth, such food and drink, such suffering and happiness, such a long life, such a long stay, such a life span. I died there and was born here, I died here and was born there, I was born here, with such a surname, such a name, such a birth, such food and drink, such suffering and happiness, such a long life, such a long stay, such a life span.' They come to ask me about past matters, and I answer them about past matters. I also go to ask them about past matters, and they also answer me about past matters. I ask them about any matter, and they also answer me about any matter I ask.

异学箭毛白曰：「瞿昙！若如是者，我转不知，我转不见，转痴堕痴，谓沙门瞿昙如是说：『优陀夷！止！止！汝长夜异见、异忍、异乐、异欲、异意故，不得尽知我所说义。优陀夷！我有弟子有因有缘，忆无量过去本昔所生，谓一生、二生、百生、千生，成劫、败劫、无量成败劫，众生名某，我曾生彼，如是姓、如是字，如是生、如是饮食、如是受苦乐、如是长寿、如是久住、如是寿讫。此死生彼，彼死生此，我生在此，如是姓、如是字、如是生、如是饮食、如是受苦乐、如是长寿、如是久住、如是寿讫。彼来问我过去事，我答彼过去事，我亦往问彼过去事，彼亦答我过去事，我随所问彼事，彼亦答我随所问事。

Furthermore, Udayin! I have disciples who, with the pure divine eye surpassing that of humans, see this assembly of beings dying and being born, good color and bad color, good and bad, going and coming to good places and bad places, according to the deeds of these beings, seeing them as they truly are. If these beings have perfected evil bodily action, evil verbal and mental action, have slandered the noble ones, have wrong views, and have perfected wrong view karma, because of this, when their body breaks up and their life ends, they will definitely go to a bad place, to a hell. If these beings have perfected good bodily action, good verbal and mental action, have not slandered the noble ones, have right views, and have perfected right view karma, because of this, when their body breaks up and their life ends, they will definitely ascend to a good place, to be born in heaven. They come to ask me about future matters, and I answer them about future matters. I also go to ask them about future matters, and they also answer me about future matters. I ask them about any matter, and they also answer me about any matter I ask.

「『复次，优陀夷！我有弟子，谓清净天眼出过于人，见此众生死时生时、好色恶色、妙与不妙，往来善处及不善处，随此众生之所作业，见其如真。若此众生成就身恶行，成就口、意恶行，诬谤圣人，邪见，成就邪见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。若此众生成就身妙行，成就口、意妙行，不诬谤圣人，正见，成就正见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必升善处，得生天中。彼来问我未来事，我答彼未来事，我亦往问彼未来事，彼亦答我未来事，我随所问彼事，彼亦答我随所问事。』

"Gautama! I cannot even remember what I did in this life and what I obtained, let alone being able to remember with causes and conditions countless past lives. Gautama! I cannot even see the wind-blown ghosts, let alone having the pure divine eye surpassing that of humans, seeing this assembly of beings dying and being born, good color and bad color, good and bad, going and coming to good places and bad places, according to the deeds of these beings, seeing them as they truly are. Gautama! I think like this: 'If the śramaṇa Gautama asks me about the Dharma learned from a teacher, perhaps I can answer him to his satisfaction.'"

「瞿昙！我于此生作本所作，得本所得，尚不能忆，况复能忆有因有缘，无量本昔所生事耶？瞿昙！我尚不能见飃风鬼，况复清净天眼出过于人，见此众生死时生时，善色恶色、妙与不妙，趣至善处及不善处，随此众生之所作业，见其如真耶？瞿昙！我作是念：『若沙门瞿昙问我从师学法者，傥能答彼，令可意也。』」

The World-Honored One asked, "Udayin! How did you learn the Dharma from your teacher?"

世尊问曰：「优陀夷！汝从师学其法云何？」

The non-Buddhist Ārāda Kālāma replied, "Gautama! They say that form surpasses form, that form is supreme, that form is the highest."

异学箭毛答曰：「瞿昙！彼说色过于色，彼色最胜，彼色最上。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Udayin! What kind of form is it?"

世尊问曰：「优陀夷！何等色耶？」

The non-Buddhist Ārāda Kālāma replied, "Gautama! If there is no form that surpasses form, it is the most supreme, the most wonderful, and the most excellent. That form is supreme, that form is the highest."

异学箭毛答曰：「瞿昙！若色更无有色最上、最妙，为最胜也。彼色最胜，彼色最上。」

The World-Honored One said, "Udayin! It is like someone saying, 'I want to obtain the most wonderful woman in this country.' If someone asks them, 'Do you know the most wonderful woman in this country, her family name, her given name, her place of birth, her height and complexion, whether she is fair or dark or neither fair nor dark, whether she is a kṣatriya woman, a brahmin, a householder, or a worker, whether she is from the east, south, west, or north?' That person would reply, 'I do not know.' Then they would be asked, 'You do not know or see the most wonderful woman in this country, her family name, given name, place of birth, height and complexion, whether she is fair or dark or neither fair nor dark, whether she is a kṣatriya woman, a brahmin, a householder, or a worker, whether she is from the east, south, west, or north, yet you say, "I want to obtain her"?'" In this way, Udayin! You say, 'That form surpasses form, that form is supreme, that form is the highest.' When asked, 'Do you know that form?' you do not know.

世尊告曰：「优陀夷！犹如有人作如是说：『若此国中有女最妙，我欲得彼。』彼若有人如是问者：『君知国中有女最妙，如是姓、如是名、如是生耶？为长短麤细，为白、黑，为不白不黑？为刹利女，为梵志、居士、工师女？为东方、南方、西方、北方耶？』彼人答曰：『我不知也。』复问彼人：『君不知、不见国中有女最妙，如是姓、如是名、如是生，长短麤细、白、黑、不白不黑，刹利女，梵志、居士、工师女，东方、南方、西方、北方者，而作是说：「我欲得彼女耶？」』如是，优陀夷！汝作是说：『彼说色过于色，彼色最胜，彼色最上。』问曰：『汝彼色？』然不知也。」

The non-Buddhist Ārāda Kālāma said, "Gautama! It is like a piece of purple gold, extremely fine and pure, well polished by a goldsmith, and placed in the sun on a white cloth. Its color is extremely wonderful and radiant. In this way, Gautama! I say that form surpasses form, that form is supreme, that form is the highest."

异学箭毛白曰：「瞿昙！犹如紫磨，极妙金精，金师善磨，莹治令净，借以白练，安著日中，其色极妙，光明照耀。如是，瞿昙！我说彼色过于色，彼色最胜，彼色最上。」

The World-Honored One said, "Udayin! I will ask you a question. Answer as you see fit. Udayin! What do you think? The color of a piece of purple gold, well polished by a goldsmith, placed in the sun on a white cloth, is extremely wonderful and radiant. Compared to the light of a firefly in the dark of night, which light is supreme and best?"

世尊告曰：「优陀夷！我今问汝，随所解答。优陀夷！于意云何？谓紫磨金精，借以白练，安著日中，其色极妙，光明照耀，及萤火虫在夜暗中光明照耀，于中光明，何者最上、为最胜耶？」

Ārāda Kālāma replied, "Gautama! The light of a firefly is supreme and best compared to the light of a piece of purple gold."

异学箭毛答曰：「瞿昙！萤火光明于紫磨金精光明，最上、为最胜也。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Udayin! What do you think? The light of a firefly in the dark of night, compared to the light of an oil lamp in the dark of night, which light is supreme and best?"

世尊问曰：「优陀夷！于意云何？谓萤火虫在夜暗中光明照耀，及燃油灯在夜暗中光明照耀，于中光明，何者最上、为最胜耶？」

Ārāda Kālāma replied, "Gautama! The light of an oil lamp is supreme and best compared to the light of a firefly."

异学箭毛答曰：「瞿昙！燃灯光明于萤火虫光明，最上、为最胜也。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Udayin! What do you think? The light of an oil lamp in the dark of night, compared to the light of a large burning wood pile in the dark of night, which light is supreme and best?"

世尊问曰：「优陀夷！于意云何？谓燃油灯在夜暗中光明照耀，及燃大木积火在夜暗中光明照耀，于中光明，何者最上、为最胜耶？」

Ārāda Kālāma replied, "Gautama! The light of a large burning wood pile is supreme and best compared to the light of an oil lamp."

异学箭毛答曰：「瞿昙！燃大木积火之光明于燃油灯光明，最上、为最胜也。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Udayin! What do you think? The light of a large burning wood pile in the dark of night, compared to the light of the morning star when the sky is clear, which light is supreme and best?"

世尊问曰：「优陀夷！于意云何？谓燃大木积火，在夜暗中光明照耀，及太白星平旦无曀光明照耀，于中光明，何者最上、为最胜耶？」

Ārāda Kālāma replied, "Gautama! The light of the morning star is supreme and best compared to the light of a large burning wood pile."

异学箭毛答曰：「瞿昙！太白星光于燃大木积火光，最上、为最胜也。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Udayin! What do you think? The light of the morning star when the sky is clear, compared to the light of the moon palace at midnight when the sky is clear, which light is supreme and best?"

世尊问曰：「优陀夷！于意云何？谓太白星平旦无曀光明照耀，及月殿光夜半无曀光明照耀，于中光明，何者最上、为最胜耶？」

Ārāda Kālāma replied, "Gautama! The light of the moon palace is supreme and best compared to the light of the morning star."

异学箭毛答曰：「瞿昙！月殿光明于太白星光，最上、为最胜也。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Udayin! What do you think? The light of the moon palace at midnight when the sky is clear, compared to the light of the sun palace at noon in autumn when the sky is clear, which light is supreme and best?"

世尊问曰：「优陀夷！于意云何？谓月殿光夜半无曀光明照曜，及日殿光秋时向中，天净无曀光明照耀，于中光明，何者最上、为最胜耶？」

Ārāda Kālāma replied, "Gautama! The light of the sun palace is supreme and best compared to the light of the moon palace."

异学箭毛答曰：「瞿昙！日殿光明于月殿光，最上、为最胜也。」

The World-Honored One said, "Udayin! There are many devas. Although the sun and moon have great supernatural powers, great majesty, great merit, and great spiritual power, their light is still inferior to the light of the devas. I have once gathered with the devas to discuss matters. What I said pleased the devas. But I did not say, 'That form surpasses form, that form is supreme, that form is best.' Udayin! You speak of the light of a firefly, the worst and ugliest form, as surpassing form, supreme, and best. When asked, you do not know."

世尊告曰：「优陀夷！多有诸天，今此日月虽有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，然其光明故不及诸天光明也。我昔曾与诸天共集，共彼论事，我之所说，可彼天意，然我不作是说：『彼色过于色，彼色最胜，彼色最上。』优陀夷！而汝于萤火虫光色最弊最丑，说彼色过于色，彼色最胜，彼色最上，问已不知。」

Ārāda Kālāma said, "World-Honored One! I repent of this statement. Well-Gone One! I repent of this statement."

异学箭毛白曰：「世尊！悔过此说。善逝！悔过此说。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Udayin! What is your intention in saying, 'World-Honored One! I repent of this statement. Well-Gone One! I repent of this statement'?"

世尊问曰：「优陀夷！汝何意如是说：『世尊！悔过此说。善逝！悔过此说。』耶？」

Ārāda Kālāma replied, "Gautama! I said that those forms are superior to forms, those forms are supreme, those forms are the highest. Now, Śramaṇa Gautama has well examined me, well instructed and well rebuked me, making my falsehoods nonexistent. Gautama! Therefore I say, 'World-Honored One! I repent of this statement. Well-Gone One! I repent of this statement.' Ārāda Kālāma said, 'Gautama! There is a path in the afterlife that leads to one-sided happiness and one-sided realization of the world.'"

异学箭毛答曰：「瞿昙！我作是说：『彼色过于色，彼色最胜，彼色最上。』沙门瞿昙今善检我，善教善诃，令我虚妄无所有也。瞿昙！是故我如是说：『世尊！悔过此说。善逝！悔过此说。』」异学箭毛语曰：「瞿昙！后世一向乐，有一道迹一向作世证。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Udayin! How is there one-sided happiness in the afterlife? How is there one path that leads to one-sided realization of the world?"

世尊问曰：「优陀夷！云何后世一向乐？云何有一道迹一向作世证耶？」

Ārāda Kālāma replied, "Gautama! There is one who abandons killing, taking what is not given, sexual misconduct, false speech, and so on, up to abandoning wrong views and attaining right views. Gautama! This is called one-sided happiness in the afterlife, this is called one path that leads to one-sided realization of the world."

异学箭毛答曰：「瞿昙！或有一离杀断杀、不与取、邪婬、妄言，乃至离邪见得正见。瞿昙！是谓后世一向乐，是谓有一道迹一向作世证。」

The World-Honored One said, "Udayin! I will ask you, answer as you see fit. Udayin! What do you think? If there is one who abandons killing and abstains from killing, is that one-sided happiness or mixed with suffering?"

世尊告曰：「优陀夷！我今问汝，随所解答。优陀夷！于意云何？若有一离杀断杀，彼为一向乐、为杂苦耶？」

Ārāda Kālāma replied, "Gautama! That is mixed with suffering."

异学箭毛答曰：「瞿昙！是杂苦也。」

"If there is one who abandons taking what is not given, sexual misconduct, false speech, and so on, up to abandoning wrong views and attaining right views, is that one-sided happiness or mixed with suffering?"

「若有一离不与取、邪婬、妄言，乃至离邪见得正见，彼为一向乐、为杂苦耶？」

Ārāda Kālāma replied, "Gautama! That is mixed with suffering."

异学箭毛答曰：「瞿昙！是杂苦也。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Udayin! Is it not for the sake of such a mixed path of happiness and suffering that one attains the world-honored state?"

世尊问曰：「优陀夷！非为如是杂苦乐道迹作世证耶？」

Ārāda Kālāma replied, "Gautama! It is for the sake of such a mixed path of happiness and suffering that one attains the world-honored state."

异学箭毛答曰：「瞿昙！如是杂苦乐道迹作世证也。」

Ārāda Kālāma said, "World-Honored One! I regret this statement. Well-Gone One! I regret this statement."

异学箭毛白曰：「世尊！悔过此说。善逝！悔过此说。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Udayin! What is the meaning of your statement, 'World-Honored One! I regret this statement. Well-Gone One! I regret this statement'?"

世尊问曰：「优陀夷！汝何意故作如是说：『世尊！悔过此说，善逝。悔过此说。』耶？」

Ārāda Kālāma replied, "Gautama! I previously said that there is a path that leads to the attainment of the highest happiness in the afterlife. Now, Gautama, you have thoroughly examined me, instructed me well, and rebuked me well, making my false claims nothing. Therefore, Gautama, I say this: 'World-Honored One! I regret this statement. Well-Gone One! I regret this statement.'"

异学箭毛答曰：「瞿昙！我向者说后世一向乐，有一道迹一向作世证。沙门瞿昙今善检我，善教善诃，令我虚妄无所有也。瞿昙！是故我如是说：『世尊！悔过此说。善逝！悔过此说。』」

The World-Honored One said, "Udayin! There is indeed a path that leads to the attainment of the highest happiness in the afterlife.

世尊告曰：「优陀夷！世有一向乐，有一道迹一向作世证也。」

Ārāda Kālāma asked, "Gautama! How is there a world of highest happiness? How is there a path that leads to the attainment of the highest happiness in the afterlife?"

异学箭毛问曰：「瞿昙！云何世一向乐？云何一道迹一向作世证耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Udayin! When a Tathāgata, an Arhat, a Perfectly Enlightened One, a one who has perfect knowledge and conduct, a Sugata, a knower of the world, an unsurpassed guide who tames beings, a teacher of gods and humans, a Buddha, a Blessed One appears in the world, he abandons the five hindrances, defilements of the mind that weaken wisdom. He is detached from sensual desires, detached from unwholesome states, and attains the joy and bliss born of detachment. He dwells in the first absorption, which is accompanied by initial and sustained thought, and is born of detachment. He does not share the same precepts, mind, and views as those gods. When his initial and sustained thought cease, his mind is internally serene, unified, free from initial and sustained thought, and attains the joy and bliss born of concentration. He dwells in the second absorption, which is accompanied by joy and bliss, and is born of concentration. He does not share the same precepts, mind, and views as those gods. He is detached from joy, abandons desire, and dwells in equanimity, mindful and clearly comprehending, experiencing bliss himself. He dwells in the third absorption, which is devoid of joy, and is born of detachment, mindfulness, and equanimity. He does not share the same precepts, mind, and views as those gods. Udayin! This is called the world of highest happiness."

世尊答曰：「优陀夷！若时如来出世，无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐，彼断，乃至五盖、心秽、慧羸，离欲、离恶不善之法，有觉、有观、离生喜、乐，得初禅成就游，不共彼天戒等、心等、见等也。彼觉、观已息，内靖、一心，无觉、无观，定生喜、乐，得第二禅成就游，不共彼天戒等、心等、见等也。彼离于喜欲，舍无求游，正念正智而身觉乐，谓圣所说，圣所舍、念、乐住、空，得第三禅成就游，不共彼天戒等、心等、见等也。优陀夷！是谓世一向乐。」

Ārāda Kālāma asked, "Gautama! Is this the highest happiness in the world?"

异学箭毛问曰：「瞿昙！世中一向乐，唯极是耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "The highest happiness in the world is not only that. Udayin! There is another path that leads to the highest realization in the world."

世尊答曰：「世中一向乐，不但极是也。优陀夷！更有一道迹一向作世证。」

Ārāda Kālāma asked, "Gautama! What is the other path that leads to the highest realization in the world?"

异学箭毛问曰：「瞿昙！云何更有一道迹一向作世证耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Udayin! A monk, having separated from sensual desires, separated from evil and unwholesome states, with initial and sustained thought, and with joy and happiness born of separation, attains and abides in the first absorption. He attains the same precepts, mind, and views as those gods. With the subsiding of initial and sustained thought, he attains and abides in the second absorption, with internal clarity, unified mind, without initial or sustained thought, with joy and happiness born of concentration. He attains the same precepts, mind, and views as those gods. With the fading away of joy, he abides in equanimity, mindful and clearly comprehending, experiencing happiness in the body, and attains and abides in the third absorption, which the noble ones declare to be without joy. He attains the same precepts, mind, and views as those gods. Udayin! This is called the other path that leads to the highest realization in the world."

世尊答曰：「优陀夷！比丘离欲、离恶不善之法，有觉、有观，离生喜、乐，得初禅成就游，得共彼天戒等、心等、见等也。彼觉、观已息，内靖、一心，无觉、无观，定生喜、乐，得第二禅成就游，得共彼天戒等、心等、见等也。彼离于喜欲，舍无求游，正念正智而身觉乐，谓圣所说，圣所舍、念、乐住、空，得第三禅成就游，得共彼天戒等、心等、见等也。优陀夷！是谓一道迹一向作世证。」

Ārāda Kālāma asked, "Gautama! Do the disciples of the śramaṇa Gautama practice the holy life with the śramaṇa Gautama for the sake of the highest happiness in this world, because of the one path that leads to the highest realization in the world?"

异学箭毛问曰：「瞿昙！沙门瞿昙弟子为此世一向乐故，一道迹一向作世证故，从沙门瞿昙学梵行耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Udayin! My disciples do not practice the holy life with me for the sake of the highest happiness in this world, nor because of the one path that leads to the highest realization in the world. Udayin! There is something even higher, more sublime, and more excellent, for the sake of which my disciples practice the holy life with me."

世尊答曰：「优陀夷！我弟子不为世一向乐故，亦不为一道迹一向作世证故，从我学梵行也。优陀夷！更有最上、最妙、最胜，为作证故，我弟子从我学梵行也。」

Then the great assembly let out a loud and high-pitched sound, saying, "That is the highest, most sublime, and most excellent, for the sake of which the disciples of the śramaṇa Gautama practice the holy life with the śramaṇa Gautama."

于是，彼大众放高大音声：「彼是最上、最妙、最胜，为作证故，沙门瞿昙弟子从沙门瞿昙学梵行也。」

Then the non-Buddhist Ārāda instructed his assembly to be silent, and said, "Gautama! What is the highest, most sublime, and most excellent, for the sake of which the disciples of the śramaṇa Gautama practice the holy life with the śramaṇa Gautama?"

于是，异学箭毛勅己众，令默然已，白曰：「瞿昙！云何最上、最妙、最胜，为作证故，沙门瞿昙弟子从沙门瞿昙学梵行耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Udayin! A monk, with the cessation of pleasure and pain, and the earlier cessation of joy and sorrow, without pleasure or pain, with pure equanimity and mindfulness, attains and abides in the fourth meditation. Udayin! This is called the highest, most sublime, and most excellent, for the sake of which my disciples practice the holy life with me."

世尊答曰：「优陀夷！比丘者乐灭、苦灭，喜、忧本已灭，不苦不乐、舍、念、清净，得第四禅成就游。优陀夷！是谓最上、最妙、最胜，为作证故，我弟子从我学梵行也。」

Then the non-Buddhist Ārāda rose from his seat, intending to bow at the Buddha's feet. Then the non-Buddhist Ārāda's disciples, the non-Buddhist Brahmins, said to the non-Buddhist Ārāda, "Now is the time for you to be the teacher. Do you want to be a disciple of the śramaṇa Gautama? It is not the time for you to be the teacher. Be a disciple of the śramaṇa Gautama. This is an obstacle for the non-Buddhist Ārāda's disciples who study the holy life, namely, studying the holy life with the World-Honored One."

于是，异学箭毛即从坐起，欲稽首佛足。于是，异学箭毛诸弟子异学梵行者白异学箭毛曰：「尊今应作师时，欲为沙门瞿昙作弟子耶？尊不应作师时，为沙门瞿昙作弟子也。是为异学箭毛诸弟子学梵行者，为异学箭毛而作障碍，谓从世尊学梵行也。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The non-Buddhist Ārāda, having heard the Buddha's words, was delighted and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。异学箭毛闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
