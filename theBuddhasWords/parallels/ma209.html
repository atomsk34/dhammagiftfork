MĀ 209

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 17 晡利多品

209. （二〇九）鞞摩那修经

Parallel to: MN 80

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the non-Buddhist Vimalakīrti was strolling in the middle of the day and went to where the Buddha was. After exchanging greetings, he asked: "Gautama, the most beautiful form, the most beautiful form. Gautama, the most beautiful form."

尔时，异学鞞摩那修中后仿佯往诣佛所，相问讯已，问曰：「瞿昙！最色最色。瞿昙！最色。」

The World-Honored One asked: "Kātyāyana, what kind of form is it?"

世尊问曰：「迦旃！何等色也？」

The non-Buddhist Vimalakīrti replied: "Gautama, if there is no form that is more supreme, more wonderful, and more excellent than that form, Gautama, that form is supreme, that form is the best."

异学鞞摩那修答曰：「瞿昙！若色更无有色最上、最妙、最胜，瞿昙！彼色最胜，彼色最上。」

The World-Honored One said: "Kātyāyana, it is like someone saying: 'I want to get the most wonderful woman in this country.' If someone asks them: 'Do you know the most wonderful woman in this country, her surname, name, and family? Is she tall or short, coarse or fine, white or black, or neither white nor black? Is she a kṣatriya woman, a brahmin, householder, or worker woman? Is she from the east, south, west, or north?' That person replies: 'I don't know.' Then they ask that person: 'You don't know or see the most wonderful woman in this country, her surname, name, and family, her height, coarseness or fineness, whiteness or blackness, or neither whiteness nor blackness, whether she is a kṣatriya woman, a brahmin, householder, or worker woman, or whether she is from the east, south, west, or north, yet you say: "I want to get that woman"?'" In this way, Kātyāyana, you say: 'That wonderful form is the most wonderful form, that form is supreme, that form is the best.' I ask you about that form, but you don't know.

世尊告曰：「迦旃！犹如有人作如是说：『若此国中有女最妙，我欲得彼。』彼若有人如是问者：『君知国中有女最妙，如是姓、如是名、如是生耶？为长短麤细，为白、黑，为不白不黑？为刹利女，为梵志、居士、工师女？为东方、南方、西方、北方耶？』彼人答曰：『我不知也。』复问彼人：『君不知、不见国中有女最妙，如是姓、如是名、如是生，长短麤细、白、黑、不白不黑，刹利女，梵志、居士、工师女，东方、南方、西方、北方者，而作是说：「我欲得彼女耶？」』如是。迦旃！汝作是说：『彼妙色最妙色，彼色最胜，彼色最上。』问汝彼色，然不知也。」

The non-Buddhist Vimalakīrti said: "Gautama, it is like a piece of purple gold, extremely wonderful and pure, well polished by a goldsmith, and placed in the sun on a white cloth. Its color is extremely wonderful and radiant. In this way, Gautama, I say that that wonderful form is the most wonderful form, that form is supreme, that form is the best."

异学鞞摩那修白曰：「瞿昙！犹如紫磨，极妙金精，金师善磨，莹治令净，借以白练，安著日中，其色极妙，光明照耀。如是，瞿昙！我说彼妙色最妙色，彼色最胜，彼色最上。」

The World-Honored One said: "Kātyāyana, I now ask you, answer as you see fit. Kātyāyana, what do you think? The color of a piece of purple gold, well polished by a goldsmith, placed in the sun on a white cloth, is extremely wonderful and radiant. The light of a firefly in the dark of night is also radiant. Which light is the best and most supreme?"

世尊告曰：「迦旃！我今问汝，随所解答。迦旃！于意云何？谓紫磨金精，借以白练，安著日中，其色极妙，光明照耀，及萤火虫在夜暗中光明照耀，于中光明，何者最上、为最胜耶？」

The non-Buddhist Vimalakīrti replied: "Gautama, the light of a firefly is the best and most supreme compared to the light of a piece of purple gold." The World-Honored One asked: "Kātyāyana, what do you think? The light of a firefly in the dark of night is radiant. The light of an oil lamp in the dark of night is also radiant. Which light is the best and most supreme?"

异学鞞摩那修答曰：「瞿昙！萤火光明于紫磨金精光明，最上、为最胜也。」世尊问曰：「迦旃！于意云何？谓萤火虫在夜暗中光明照耀，及燃油灯在夜暗中光明照耀，于中光明，何者最上、为最胜耶？」

The non-Buddhist Vimalakīrti replied: "Gautama, the light of an oil lamp is the best and most supreme compared to the light of a firefly."

异学鞞摩那修答曰：「瞿昙！燃灯光明于萤火虫光明，最上、为最胜也。」

The World-Honored One asked: "Kātyāyana, what do you think? The light of an oil lamp in the dark of night is radiant. The light of a large pile of burning wood in the dark of night is also radiant. Which light is the best and most supreme?"

世尊问曰：「迦旃！于意云何？谓燃油灯在夜暗中光明照耀，及燃大木积火在夜暗中光明照耀，于中光明，何者最上、为最胜耶？」

The non-Buddhist Vimalakīrti replied: "Gautama, the light of a large pile of burning wood is the best and most supreme compared to the light of an oil lamp."

异学鞞摩那修答曰：「瞿昙！燃大木积火之光明于燃油灯光明，最上、为最胜也。」

The World-Honored One asked: "Kātyāyana, what do you think? The light of a large pile of burning wood in the dark of night is radiant. The light of the planet Venus in the early morning without obstruction is also radiant. Which light is the best and most supreme?"

世尊问曰：「迦旃！于意云何？谓燃大木积火在夜暗中光明照耀，及太白星平旦无曀光明照耀，于中光明，何者最上、为最胜耶？」

The non-Buddhist Vimalakīrti replied: "Gautama, the light of the planet Venus is the best and most supreme compared to the light of a large pile of burning wood."

异学鞞摩那修答曰：「瞿昙！太白星光于燃大木积火光，最上、为最胜也。」

The World-Honored One asked: "Kātyāyana, what do you think? The light of the planet Venus in the early morning without obstruction is radiant. The light of the moon palace in the middle of the night without obstruction is also radiant. Which light is the best and most supreme?"

世尊问曰：「迦旃！于意云何？谓太白星平旦无曀光明照耀，及月殿光夜半无曀光明照耀，于中光明，何者最上、为最胜耶？」

The non-Buddhist Vimalakīrti replied: "Gautama, the light of the moon palace is the best and most supreme compared to the light of the planet Venus."

异学鞞摩那修答曰：「瞿昙！月殿光明于太白星光，最上、为最胜也。」

The World-Honored One asked: "Kātyāyana, what do you think? The light of the moon palace in the middle of the night without obstruction is radiant. The light of the sun palace in the autumn when the sky is clear and without obstruction is also radiant. Which light is the best and most supreme?"

世尊问曰：「迦旃！于意云何？谓月殿光夜半无曀光明照曜，及日殿光秋时向中，天净无曀光明照耀，于中光明，何者最上、为最胜耶？」

The non-Buddhist Vimalakīrti replied: "Gautama, the light of the sun palace is the best and most supreme compared to the light of the moon palace."

异学鞞摩那修答曰：「瞿昙！日殿光明于月殿光，最上、为最胜也。」

The World-Honored One said: "Kātyāyana, there are many gods. Although the sun and moon have great supernatural powers, great majesty, great merit, and great spiritual power, their light is still inferior to the light of the gods. I have once gathered with the gods to discuss matters. What I said pleased the gods, but I did not say that their light is the best and most supreme. Kātyāyana, you speak of the light of fireflies, which is the worst and ugliest, as the best and most supreme. When asked, you do not know." Thus, after being directly rebuked by the World-Honored One, the non-Buddhist Vimalakīrti was inwardly distressed, lowered his head in silence, lost his eloquence, and was speechless, as if waiting for something.

世尊告曰：「迦旃！多有诸天，今此日月虽有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，然其光明故不及诸天光明也。我昔曾与诸天共集，共彼论事，我之所说，可彼天意，然我不作是说，彼妙色最妙色，彼色最胜，彼色最上。迦旃！而汝于萤火虫光色最弊最丑，说彼妙色最妙色，彼色最胜，彼色最上，问已不知。」于是，异学鞞摩那修为世尊面诃责已，内怀忧戚，低头默然，失辩无言，如有所伺。

Then, after directly rebuking him, the World-Honored One further delighted him, saying: "Kātyāyana, there are five kinds of sensual pleasures that are delightful, pleasing, and associated with joy. The eye knows forms, the ear knows sounds, the nose knows smells, the tongue knows tastes, and the body knows tactile objects. Kātyāyana, forms may be loved or not loved. If a person finds a certain form pleasing, agreeable, joyful, satisfying, and fulfilling, then even if other forms are superior and supreme, they will not desire, think of, wish for, or seek them. For them, those forms are the most supreme and excellent. Kātyāyana, it is the same with sounds, smells, tastes, and tactile objects. Kātyāyana, tactile objects may be loved or not loved. If a person finds a certain tactile object pleasing, agreeable, joyful, satisfying, and fulfilling, then even if other tactile objects are superior and supreme, they will not desire, think of, wish for, or seek them. For them, those tactile objects are the most supreme and excellent."

于是，世尊面诃责已，复令欢悦，告曰：「迦旃！有五欲功德，可喜、意、令乐欲相应乐，眼知色，耳知声，鼻知香，舌知味，身知触。迦旃！色或有爱者，或不爱者。若有一人，彼于此色可意、称意、乐意、足意、满愿意，彼于余色虽最上、最胜，而不欲、不思、不愿、不求，彼于此色最胜、最上。迦旃！如是声、香、味、触，迦旃！触或有爱者，或不爱者。若有一人，彼于此触可意、称意、乐意、足意、满愿意。彼于余触虽最上、最胜，而不欲、不思、不愿、不求。彼于此触最胜、最上。」

Then, with palms joined towards the Buddha, the non-Buddhist Vimalakīrti said: "Gautama, it is truly amazing! The Śramaṇa Gautama has used countless skillful means to explain the pleasures of desire and the supreme pleasures of desire. Gautama, it is like using a grass fire to ignite a wood fire, and using a wood fire to ignite a grass fire. In this way, the Śramaṇa Gautama has used countless skillful means to explain the pleasures of desire and the supreme pleasures of desire."

于是，异学鞞摩那修叉手向佛，白曰：「瞿昙！甚奇！甚特！沙门瞿昙为我无量方便说欲乐、欲乐第一。瞿昙！犹如因草火燃木火，因木火燃草火，如是沙门瞿昙为我无量方便说欲乐、欲乐第一。」

The World-Honored One said: "Stop, stop, Kātyāyana! You have long held different views, different tolerance, different joy, different desires, and different intentions. Therefore, you cannot fully understand the meaning of what I say. Kātyāyana, I say that my disciples who do not sleep or lie down during the early and late parts of the night, who are rightly concentrated and focused, cultivating the factors of the path, who have ended rebirth, established the holy life, accomplished what is to be done, and will not be reborn again, knowing it as it really is, they fully understand what I say."

世尊告曰：「止！止！迦旃！汝长夜异见、异忍、异乐、异欲、异意故，不得尽知我所说义。迦旃！谓我弟子初夜后夜常不眠卧，正定正意，修习道品，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真，彼尽知我所说。」

Then, with anger and resentment towards the Buddha, the non-Buddhist Vimalakīrti tried to slander and accuse the World-Honored One, saying: "Gautama, there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who do not know the past limit of the world, do not know the future limit of the world, and do not know the endless cycle of birth and death. Yet they claim to have attained perfect knowledge, that birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been accomplished, and they will not be reborn again, knowing it as it really is. Gautama, I think: 'How can these śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, who do not know the past limit of the world, do not know the future limit of the world, and do not know the endless cycle of birth and death, claim to have attained perfect knowledge, that birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been accomplished, and they will not be reborn again, knowing it as it really is?'"

于是，异学鞞摩那修向佛瞋恚，生憎嫉、不可，欲诬谤世尊，欲堕世尊，如是诬谤世尊，如是堕世尊，语曰：「瞿昙！有沙门、梵志，不知世前际，不知世后际，不知无穷生死，而记说得究竟智，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。瞿昙！我如是念：『云何此沙门、梵志，不知世前际，亦不知世后际，不知无穷生死，而记说得究竟智，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真耶？』」

Then the World-Honored One had this thought: "This non-Buddhist Vimalakīrti is angry and resentful towards me, wanting to slander and accuse me, saying: 'Gautama, there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who do not know the past limit of the world, do not know the future limit of the world, and do not know the endless cycle of birth and death. Yet they claim to have attained perfect knowledge, that birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been accomplished, and they will not be reborn again, knowing it as it really is. Gautama!' I think: 'How can these śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, who do not know the past limit of the world, do not know the future limit of the world, and do not know the endless cycle of birth and death, claim to have attained perfect knowledge, that birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been accomplished, and they will not be reborn again, knowing it as it really is?'"

于是，世尊便作是念：「此异学鞞摩那修向我瞋恚，生憎嫉、不可，欲诬谤我，欲堕于我，如是诬谤我，如是堕我，而语我曰：『瞿昙！有一沙门梵志，不知世前际，不知世后际，不知无穷生死，而记说得究竟智，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。瞿昙！』我作是念：『云何此沙门、梵志，不知世前际，不知世后际，不知无穷生死，而记说得究竟智，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真耶？』」

The World-Honored One, knowing this, said: "Kātyāyana, if there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who do not know the past limit of the world, do not know the future limit of the world, and do not know the endless cycle of birth and death, yet claim to have attained perfect knowledge, that birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been accomplished, and they will not be reborn again, knowing it as it really is, they should say: 'Leave aside the past limit of the world, leave aside the future limit of the world.' Kātyāyana, I say: 'Leave aside the past limit of the world, leave aside the future limit of the world.' If they do not remember one lifetime, my disciple bhikṣus come, not flattering, not deceitful, straightforward. I teach them, and if they follow my teachings and practice accordingly, they will certainly know the true Dharma. Kātyāyana, it is like a baby lying on its back, with its hands and feet bound by its parents. Later, when it grows up and its faculties are fully developed, its parents untie its hands and feet. It only remembers the time of being untied, not the time of being tied. In the same way, Kātyāyana, I say: 'Leave aside the past limit of the world, leave aside the future limit of the world.' If they do not remember one lifetime, my disciple bhikṣus come, not flattering, not deceitful, straightforward. I teach them. If they follow my teachings and practice accordingly, they will certainly know the true Dharma. Kātyāyana, it is like a lamp burning with oil and a wick. If no one adds more oil and changes the wick, the previous oil is exhausted and no more is added. Without anything to sustain it, it will quickly go out. In the same way, Kātyāyana, I say: 'Leave aside the past limit of the world, leave aside the future limit of the world.' If they do not remember one lifetime, my disciple bhikṣus come, not flattering, not deceitful, straightforward. I teach them. If they follow my teachings and practice accordingly, they will certainly know the true Dharma. Kātyāyana, it is like a pile of ten pieces of wood, twenty, thirty, forty, fifty, or sixty pieces of wood, burning fiercely. Later, if no one adds more grass, wood, chaff, or rubbish, the previous fuel is exhausted and no more is added. Without anything to sustain it, it will quickly go out. In the same way, Kātyāyana, I say: 'Leave aside the past limit of the world, leave aside the future limit of the world.' If they do not remember one lifetime, my disciple bhikṣus come, not flattering, not deceitful, straightforward

世尊知已，告曰：「迦旃！若有沙门、梵志，不知世前际，不知世后际，不知无穷生死，而记说得究竟智，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真者，彼应如是说：『置世前际，置世后际。』迦旃！我如是说：『置世前际，置世后际。』设不忆一生，我弟子比丘来，不谀谄、无欺诳、质直，我教化之，若随我教化，如是行者，必得知正法。迦旃！犹如婴孩童子，少年柔软，仰向卧，父母缚彼手足，彼于后转大，诸根成就，父母解彼手足，彼唯忆解缚时，不忆缚时也。如是，迦旃！我如是说：『置世前际，置世后际。』设令不忆一生，我弟子比丘来，不谀谄、不欺诳、质直，我教化之。若随我教化，如是行者，必得知正法。迦旃！譬若因油因炷而燃灯也，无人益油，亦不易炷者，前油已尽，后不更益，无所受已，自速灭也。如是，迦旃！我如是说：『置世前际，置世后际。』设令不忆一生，我弟子比丘来，不谀谄、不欺诳、质直，我教化之，若随我教化，如是行者，必得知正法。迦旃！犹如十木聚，二十、三十、四十、五十、六十木聚，以火烧之，洞然俱炽，遂见火焰，后无有人更益草、木、糠、粪扫者，前薪已尽，后不更益，无所受已，自速灭也。如是，迦旃！我如是说：『置世前际，置世后际。』设令不忆一生，我弟子比丘来，不谀谄、不欺诳、质直，我教化之。若随我教化，如是行者，必得知正法。」

When this teaching was spoken, the non-Buddhist Vimalakīrti, far from dust and defilements, gave rise to the Dharma eye of the Dharma with regard to all dharmas. Then the non-Buddhist Vimalakīrti saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, realized the pure Dharma, and had no other teacher. He was no longer dependent on others, had cut off doubts and crossed over confusion, was without hesitation, had attained the fruit of realization, and had attained fearlessness in the World-Honored One's teachings. He bowed his head to the Buddha's feet and said: "World-Honored One! I wish to go forth from the Buddha, practice the path, receive full ordination, become a bhikṣu, and practice the holy life."

说此法时，异学鞞摩那修远尘离垢，诸法法眼生。于是，异学鞞摩那修见法得法，觉白净法，更无余尊，不复由他，断疑度惑，无有犹豫，已住果证，于世尊法得无所畏，稽首佛足，白曰：「世尊！愿得从佛出家学道，受具足，得比丘，行梵行。」

The World-Honored One said: "Excellent! Bhikṣu! Practice the holy life."

世尊告曰：「善哉！比丘！行梵行也。」

The non-Buddhist went forth from the Buddha to practice the path, received full ordination, became a bhikṣu, and practiced the holy life. Venerable Vimalakīrti went forth to practice the path, received full ordination, and having done so, knew the Dharma, saw the Dharma, and attained arahantship.

异学从佛得出家学道，即受具足，得比丘，行梵行。尊者鞞摩那修出家学道，受具足已，知法见法，乃至得阿罗诃。

The Buddha spoke thus. Venerable Vimalakīrti and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。尊者鞞摩那修及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
