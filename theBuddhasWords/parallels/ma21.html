MĀ 21

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 3 舍梨子相应品

21. On an Even Mind （二一）等心经

Parallel to: AN 2.36

equanimity, attainment of precepts, knowledge, lion, water simile, goṇi, dhāraṇī, brahmanic
teaching, illness, kṣudraka, elephant-track simile, analysis of the four noble truths last

等心、得戒、智、师子　　水喻、瞿尼、陀然梵教病、拘𫄨、象迹喻　　分别四谛最在后

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra was with a group of bhikṣus in the Dharma hall at night, discussing internal and external bonds, explaining their meaning to the bhikṣus: "Virtuous ones! There are indeed two kinds of people in the world. What are the two? There are those with internal bonds who are anāgāmins, not returning to this world, and those with external bonds who are not anāgāmins, returning to this world. Virtuous ones! How are those with internal bonds anāgāmins, not returning to this world? If there is a person who cultivates precepts, without holes, without flaws, without defilement, without turbidity, with many difficulties, praised by the sages, well cultivated and well equipped. Because of cultivating precepts, without holes, without flaws, without defilement, without turbidity, with many difficulties, praised by the sages, well cultivated and well equipped, they further study to be disgusted with desire, desireless, and cut off desire. Because of studying to be disgusted with desire, desireless, and cut off desire, they attain tranquility and liberation of the mind. Having attained it, they cherish it in pleasure and do not part from it. In this present life, they do not attain ultimate knowledge. When their body perishes and life ends, they transcend the heavens of material food and are reborn in other heavens of mental food. Having been reborn there, they think: 'When I was a human, I cultivated precepts, without holes, without flaws, without defilement, without turbidity, with many difficulties, praised by the sages, well cultivated and well equipped. Because of cultivating precepts, without holes, without flaws, without defilement, without turbidity, with many difficulties, praised by the sages, well cultivated and well equipped, I further studied to be disgusted with desire, desireless, and cut off desire. Because of studying to be disgusted with desire, desireless, and cut off desire, I attained tranquility and liberation of the mind. Having attained it, I cherished it in pleasure and did not part from it. In this present life, I did not attain ultimate knowledge. When my body perished and life ended, I transcended the heavens of material food and was reborn in other heavens of mental food. I am here now.'

尔时，尊者舍梨子与比丘众夜集讲堂，因内结、外结，为诸比丘分别其义：「诸贤！世实有二种人。云何为二？有内结人阿那含，不还此间，有外结人非阿那含，还来此间。诸贤！云何内结人阿那含，不还此间？若有一人修习禁戒，无穿无缺，无秽无浊，极多无难，圣所称誉，善修善具。彼因修习禁戒，无穿无缺，无秽无浊，极多无难，圣所称誉，善修善具故，复学厌欲、无欲、断欲。因学厌欲、无欲、断欲故，得息、心解脱。得已乐中，爱惜不离，于现法中不得究竟智，身坏命终，过抟食天，生余意生天中。既生彼已，便作是念：『我本为人时，修习禁戒，无穿无缺，无秽无浊，极多无难，圣所称誉，善修善具。因修习禁戒，无穿无缺，无秽无浊，极多无难，圣所称誉，善修善具故，复学厌欲、无欲、断欲。因学厌欲、无欲、断欲故，得息、心解脱。得已乐中，爱惜不离，于现法中不得究竟智，身坏命终，过抟食天，生余意生天在于此中。』

Virtuous ones! There is also a person who cultivates precepts, without holes, without flaws, without defilement, without turbidity, with many difficulties, praised by the sages, well cultivated and well equipped. Because of cultivating precepts, without holes, without flaws, without defilement, without turbidity, with many difficulties, praised by the sages, well cultivated and well equipped, they further study to cut off form, desire, and karma, and study to abandon desire. Because of studying to cut off form, desire, and karma, and studying to abandon desire, they attain tranquility and liberation of the mind. Having attained it, they cherish it in pleasure and do not part from it. In this present life, they do not attain ultimate knowledge. When their body perishes and life ends, they transcend the heavens of material food and are reborn in other heavens of mental food. Having been reborn there, they think: 'When I was a human, I cultivated precepts, without holes, without flaws, without defilement, without turbidity, with many difficulties, praised by the sages, well cultivated and well equipped. Because of cultivating precepts, without holes, without flaws, without defilement, without turbidity, with many difficulties, praised by the sages, well cultivated and well equipped, I further studied to cut off form, desire, and karma, and studied to abandon desire. Because of studying to cut off form, desire, and karma, and studying to abandon desire, I attained tranquility and liberation of the mind. Having attained it, I cherished it in pleasure and did not part from it. In this present life, I did not attain ultimate knowledge. When my body perished and life ended, I transcended the heavens of material food and was reborn in other heavens of mental food. I am here now.' Virtuous ones! This is called a person with internal bonds who is anāgāmin, not returning to this world.

「诸贤！复有一人修习禁戒，无穿无缺，无秽无浊，极多无难，圣所称誉，善修善具。彼因修习禁戒，无穿无缺，无秽无浊，极多无难，圣所称誉，善修善具故，复学色有断、贪断业，学欲舍离。因学色有断、贪断业，学欲舍离故，得息、心解脱。得已乐中，爱惜不离，于现法中不得究竟智，身坏命终，过抟食天，生余意生天中。既生彼已，便作是念：『我本为人时，修习禁戒，无穿无缺，无秽无浊，极多无难，圣所称誉，善修善具。因修习禁戒，无穿无缺，无秽无浊，极多无难，圣所称誉，善修善具故，复学色有断、贪断业，学欲舍离。因学色有断、贪断业，学欲舍离故，得息、心解脱。得已乐中，爱惜不离，于现法中不得究竟智，身坏命终，过抟食天，生余意生天在于此中。』诸贤！是谓内结人阿那含，不还此间。

"Virtuous ones! There is also a person who cultivates precepts, guards from liberation, and also well restrains deportment and etiquette. Seeing even the slightest fault, they always have fear and uphold the precepts. Virtuous ones! This is called a person with external bonds who is not anāgāmin, returning to this world."

「诸贤！云何外结人非阿那含，来还此间？若有一人修习禁戒，守护从解脱，又复善摄威仪礼节，见纤芥] cf.［丽】罪，常怀畏怖，受持学戒。诸贤！是谓外结人非阿那含，还来此间。」

Then, many gods of the equal-minded heaven, majestic in appearance and radiant, came to the Buddha at dawn, bowed their heads at his feet, stood to one side, and said, "World-Honored One! Last night Venerable Sāriputta was with the assembly of monks in the lecture hall, discussing the meaning of internal and external bonds, saying, 'Virtuous ones! There are indeed two kinds of people in the world, those with internal bonds and those with external bonds.' World-Honored One! The assembly was delighted. May the World-Honored One, out of compassion, please go to the lecture hall." At that time, the World-Honored One silently consented to the gods of the equal-minded heaven. The gods of the equal-minded heaven, knowing the World-Honored One's silent consent, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and then disappeared on the spot.

于是，众多等心天色像巍巍，光辉𬀩晔，夜将向旦，来诣佛所，稽首作礼，却住一面，白曰：「世尊！尊者舍梨子昨夜与比丘众集在讲堂，因内结、外结，为诸比丘分别其义：『诸贤！世实有二种人，内结人、外结人。』世尊！众已欢喜，唯愿世尊慈哀愍念，往至讲堂。」彼时，世尊为诸等心天默然而许。诸等心天知世尊默然许可，稽首佛足，绕三匝已，即彼处没。

Not long after the gods of the equal-minded heaven left, the World-Honored One went to the lecture hall, sat before the assembly of monks, and spread his seat. Having sat down, the World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Sāriputta! You are extremely good. Why is that? Last night you were with the assembly of monks in the lecture hall, discussing the meaning of internal and external bonds, saying, 'Virtuous ones! There are indeed two kinds of people in the world, those with internal bonds and those with external bonds.' Sāriputta! At dawn, the gods of the equal-minded heaven came to me, bowed their heads at my feet, stood to one side, and said to me, 'World-Honored One! Last night Venerable Sāriputta was with the assembly of monks in the lecture hall, discussing the meaning of internal and external bonds, saying, "Virtuous ones! There are indeed two kinds of people in the world, those with internal bonds and those with external bonds." World-Honored One! The assembly was delighted. May the World-Honored One, out of compassion, please go to the lecture hall.' Sāriputta! I then silently consented to those gods of the equal-minded heaven. The gods of the equal-minded heaven, knowing my silent consent, bowed their heads at my feet, circumambulated me three times, and then disappeared on the spot.

诸等心天去后不久，于是世尊往至讲堂比丘众前，敷座而坐。世尊坐已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！舍梨子！汝极甚善。所以者何？汝于昨夜与比丘众集在讲堂，因内结、外结，为诸比丘分别其义。诸贤！世实有二种人，内结人、外结人。舍梨子！昨夜向旦，诸等心天来诣我所，稽首礼已，却住一面，白我言：『世尊！尊者舍梨子昨夜与比丘众集在讲堂，因内结、外结，为诸比丘分别其义：「诸贤！世实有二种人，内结人、外结人。」世尊！众已欢喜，唯愿世尊慈哀愍念，往至讲堂。』舍梨子！我便为彼诸等心天默然而许。诸等心天知我默然许可，稽首我足，绕三匝已，即彼处没。

"Sāriputta! The gods of the equal-minded heaven may live together in the space of a needle tip, each not obstructing the other. Sāriputta! It is not that the gods of the equal-minded heaven, having been born there, only recently cultivated good minds, which are extremely vast and great, causing the gods of the equal-minded heaven to live together in the space of a needle tip, each not obstructing the other. Sāriputta! The gods of the equal-minded heaven, when they were human beings, had already cultivated good minds, which are extremely vast and great. Because of this, the gods of the equal-minded heaven may live together in the space of a needle tip, each not obstructing the other. Therefore, Sāriputta! One should study tranquility, with tranquil faculties, tranquil mind and thoughts, tranquil bodily, verbal, and mental actions, and approach the World-Honored One and the wise ones who practice the holy life. Sāriputta! The false non-Buddhist practitioners will decline and lose out for a long time. Why is that? It is because they do not get to hear such wonderful teachings."

「舍梨子！诸等心天或十、二十，或三十、四十，或五十、六十，共住锥头处，各不相妨。舍梨子！诸等心天非生彼中，甫修善心，极广甚大，令诸等心天或十、二十，或三十、四十，或五十、六十，共住锥头处，各不相妨。舍梨子！诸等心天本为人时，已修善心，极广甚大，因是故，令诸等心天或十、二十，或三十、四十，或五十、六十，共住锥头处，各不相妨。是故，舍梨子！当学寂静，诸根寂静，心意寂静，身、口、意业寂静，向于世尊及诸智梵行。舍梨子！虚伪异学，长衰永失。所以者何？谓不得闻如此妙法。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
