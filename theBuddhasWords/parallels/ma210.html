MĀ 210

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 17 晡利多品

210. （二一〇）法乐比丘尼经

Parallel to: MN 44

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the upāsikā Viśākhā went to the bhikṣuṇī Dharmapāla, bowed her head at her feet, sat to one side, and said to the bhikṣuṇī Dharmapāla, "Noble one! I wish to ask something. May I ask?"

尔时，毘舍佉优婆夷往诣法乐比丘尼所，稽首礼足，却坐一面，白法乐比丘尼曰：「贤圣！欲有所问，听我问耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapāla replied, "Viśākhā! Ask what you wish. I will think about it after hearing it."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「毘舍佉！欲问便问，我闻已当思。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā then asked, "Noble one! Speaking of one's own body, what is one's own body?"

毗舍佉优婆夷便问曰：「贤圣！自身，说自身。云何为自身耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapāla replied, "The World-Honored One spoke of the five aggregates of clinging. The aggregate of form, the aggregate of feeling, the aggregate of perception, the aggregate of volitional formations, and the aggregate of consciousness. These are what the World-Honored One spoke of as the five aggregates of clinging."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「世尊说五盛阴，自身色盛阴，觉、想、行、识盛阴，是谓世尊说五盛阴。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent!" Having praised the noble one, the upāsikā Viśākhā rejoiced and upheld it.

毗舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣。」毗舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! What is the view of the self?"

复问曰：「贤圣！云何为自身见耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "The ignorant and foolish who have not heard much, not seeing good friends, not knowing the holy Dharma, not practicing the holy Dharma, they see form as the self, see the self as having form, see the self as having form in the self, see the self as having form in form. They see feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness as the self, see the self as having consciousness, see the self as having consciousness in the self, see the self as having consciousness in consciousness. This is called the view of the self.

法乐比丘尼答曰：「不多闻愚痴凡夫不见善知识，不知圣法，不御圣法，彼见色是神，见神有色，见神中有色，见色中有神也。见觉、想、行、识是神，见神有识，见神中有识，见识中有神也。是谓自身见也。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised, the upāsikā Viśākhā rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

毗舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毗舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! How is there no view of the self?"

复问曰：「贤圣！云何无身见耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "The well-versed noble disciple sees good friends, knows the noble Dharma, and skillfully practices the noble Dharma. He does not see form as the self, does not see the self as having form, does not see the self as having form, does not see form as having the self. He does not see feeling, perception, volition, or consciousness as the self, does not see the self as having consciousness, does not see the self as having consciousness, does not see consciousness as having the self. This is called the absence of the view of the self.

法乐比丘尼答曰：「多闻圣弟子见善知识，知圣法，善御圣法，彼不见色是神，不见神有色，不见神中有色，不见色中有神也。不见觉、想、行、识是神，不见神有识，不见神中有识，不见识中有神也。是谓无身见也。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised, the upāsikā Viśākhā joyfully accepted and practiced it.

毗舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毘舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! How does one extinguish one's own body?"

复问曰：「贤圣！云何灭自身耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapramada replied, "The destruction of the aggregate of form without remainder, abandoning, vomiting, exhausting, non-defilement, cessation, pacification, and extinction. The destruction of the aggregates of feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness without remainder, abandoning, vomiting, exhausting, non-defilement, cessation, pacification, and extinction. This is called the extinction of one's own body."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「色盛阴断无余，舍、吐、尽、不染、灭、息、没也。觉、想、行、识盛阴断无余，舍、吐、尽、不染、灭、息、没也。是谓自身灭。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised, the upāsikā Viśākhā rejoiced and upheld it.

毗舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毗舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! You speak of the aggregates and the aggregate-possessor. Are the aggregates the aggregate-possessor, and the aggregate-possessor the aggregates? Are the aggregates different from the aggregate-possessor, and the aggregate-possessor different from the aggregates?"

复问曰：「贤圣！阴说阴盛，阴说盛阴，阴即是盛阴，盛阴即是阴耶？为阴异、盛阴异耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "Sometimes the aggregates are the aggregate-possessor, sometimes the aggregates are not the aggregate-possessor. How are the aggregates the aggregate-possessor? If form is with taints and with feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness are with taints and with feeling, this is called the aggregates being the aggregate-possessor. How are the aggregates not the aggregate-possessor? If form is without taints and without feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness are without taints and without feeling, this is called the aggregates not being the aggregate-possessor."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「或阴即是盛阴，或阴非盛阴。云何阴即是盛阴？若色有漏有受，觉、想、行、识有漏有受，是谓阴即是盛阴。云何阴非盛阴？色无漏无受，觉、想、行、识无漏无受，是谓阴非盛阴。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised thus, the upāsikā Viśākhā rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

毗舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毗舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! What is the eightfold noble path?"

复问曰：「贤圣！云何八支圣道耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "The eightfold noble path refers to right view, up to right concentration. These are called the eight, which are called the eightfold noble path."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「八支圣道者，正见乃至正定，是谓为八，是谓八支圣道。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised, the upāsikā Viśākhā joyfully accepted and practiced it.

毗舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毗舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! Is the eightfold noble path conditioned?"

复问曰：「贤圣！八支圣道有为耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "Yes, this eightfold noble path is conditioned."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「如是八支圣道有为也。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised her, the upāsikā Viśākhā joyfully accepted it.

毗舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毗舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! How many aggregates are there?"

复问曰：「贤圣！有几聚耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "There are three aggregates: the aggregate of morality, the aggregate of concentration, and the aggregate of wisdom."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「有三聚。戒聚、定聚、慧聚。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised thus, the upāsikā Viśākhā rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

毗舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毗舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! Are the three aggregates included in the eightfold noble path, or is the eightfold noble path included in the three aggregates?"

复问曰：「贤圣！八支圣道摄三聚，为三聚摄八支圣道耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "The three aggregates are not included in the eightfold noble path, but the eightfold noble path is included in the three aggregates. Right speech, right action, and right livelihood - these three aspects of the path are included in the aggregate of noble morality. Right mindfulness and right concentration - these two aspects of the path are included in the aggregate of noble concentration. Right view, right intention, and right effort - these three aspects of the path are included in the aggregate of noble wisdom. This is called the three aggregates not being included in the eightfold noble path, but the eightfold noble path being included in the three aggregates."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「非八支圣道摄三聚。三聚摄八支圣道。正语、正业、正命，此三道支圣戒聚所摄。正念、正定，此二道支圣定聚所摄。正见、正志、正方便，此三道支圣慧聚所摄。是谓非八支圣道摄三聚，三聚摄八支圣道。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised her, the upāsikā Viśākhā joyfully accepted it.

毗舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毗舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! Is there an end to opposition?"

复问曰：「贤圣！灭有对耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "There is an end to opposition."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「灭无对也。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised thus, the upāsikā Viśākhā rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

毗舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毗舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! How many limbs are there in the first dhyāna?"

复问曰：「贤圣！初禅有几支耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "The first dhyāna has five limbs: initial inquiry, sustained thought, joy, bliss, and one-pointedness of mind. These are said to be the five limbs of the first dhyāna."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「初禅有五支，觉、观、喜、乐、一心，是谓初禅有五支。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised her, the upāsikā Viśākhā joyfully accepted it.

毗舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毗舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! What is concentration? What are the characteristics of concentration? What is the power of concentration? What are the benefits of concentration? How does one cultivate concentration?"

复问曰：「贤圣！云何定] cf.［丽】？云何定相？云何定力？云何定功？云何修定耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "If one can unify the good mind, that is called concentration. The four foundations of mindfulness are called the characteristics of concentration. The four right efforts are called the power of concentration. The four bases of supernormal power are called the benefits of concentration. If one practices and cultivates these wholesome dharmas repeatedly, that is called the cultivation of concentration."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「若善心得一者，是谓定也。四念处，是谓定相也。四正断，是谓定力也。四如意足，是谓定功也。若习此诸善法服，数数专修精勤者，是谓定修也。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised, the upāsikā Viśākhā rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

毗舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毘舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! How many dharmas cause one's body to be discarded in a charnel ground like a lifeless tree after death?"

复问曰：「贤圣！有几法生身死已，身弃冢间，如木无情？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "There are three dharmas that cause one's body to be discarded in a charnel ground like a lifeless tree after death. What are the three? First, lifespan, second, warmth, and third, consciousness. These are the three dharmas that cause one's body to be discarded in a charnel ground like a lifeless tree after death."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「有三法生身死已，身弃冢间，如木无情。云何为三？一者寿，二者暖，三者识，是谓三法生身死已，身弃冢间，如木无情。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised her, the upāsikā Viśākhā joyfully accepted it.

毘舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毘舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! What is the difference between death and entering the state of cessation?"

复问曰：「贤圣！若死及入灭尽定者，有何差别？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "For the dead, life has ceased, warmth has departed, and the faculties have decayed. For a bhikṣu who enters the state of cessation, life has not ceased, warmth has not departed, and the faculties have not decayed. This is the difference between death and entering the state of cessation."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「死者寿命灭讫，温暖已去，诸根败坏。比丘入灭尽定者，寿不灭讫，暖亦不去，诸根不败坏，若死及入灭尽定者，是谓差别。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised, the upāsikā Viśākhā rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

毘舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毘舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! What is the difference between entering the state of cessation and entering the state of non-conception?"

复问曰：「贤圣！若入灭尽定及入无想定者，有何差别？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "For a bhikṣu who enters the state of cessation, perception and cognition cease. For one who enters the state of non-conception, perception and cognition do not cease. This is the difference between entering the state of cessation and entering the state of non-conception."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「比丘入灭尽定者，想及知灭。入无想定者，想知不灭。若入灭尽定及入无想定者，是谓差别。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised her, the upāsikā Viśākhā joyfully accepted it.

毘舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毘舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! What is the difference between arising from the state of cessation and arising from the state of non-conception?"

复问曰：「贤圣！若从灭尽定起及从无想定起者，有何差别？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "When a bhikṣu arises from the state of cessation, he does not think, 'I have arisen from the state of cessation.' When a bhikṣu arises from the state of non-conception, he thinks, 'Do I have conception? Do I not have conception?' This is the difference between arising from the state of cessation and arising from the state of non-conception."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「比丘从灭尽定起时，不作是念：『我从灭尽定起。』比丘从无想定起时，作如是念：『我为有想，我为无想？』若从灭尽定起及从无想定起者，是谓差别。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised her, the upāsikā Viśākhā joyfully accepted it.

毘舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毘舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! When a bhikṣu enters the state of cessation, does he think, 'I am entering the state of cessation'?"

复问曰：「贤圣！比丘入灭尽定时，作如是念：『我入灭尽定耶？』」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "When a bhikṣu enters the state of cessation, he does not think, 'I am entering the state of cessation.' However, he has previously cultivated the mind in this way, and therefore he proceeds in this way.

法乐比丘尼答曰：「比丘入灭尽定时，不作是念：『我入灭尽定。』然本如是修习心，以是故如是趣向。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised her, the upāsikā Viśākhā joyfully accepted it.

毘舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毘舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! When a bhikṣu arises from the state of cessation, does he think, 'I have arisen from the state of cessation'?"

复问曰：「贤圣！比丘从灭尽定起，作如是念：『我从灭尽定起耶？』」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "When a bhikṣu arises from the state of cessation, he does not think, 'I have arisen from the state of cessation.' However, due to this body and the six sense bases, the life faculty arises, and therefore one arises from that state.

法乐比丘尼答曰：「比丘从灭尽定起时，不作是念：『我从灭尽定起。』然因此身及六处缘命根，是故从定起。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised her, the upāsikā Viśākhā joyfully accepted it.

毘舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毘舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! After a bhikṣu arises from the state of cessation, what does his mind delight in, what does it pursue, what does it follow?"

复问曰：「贤圣！比丘从灭尽定起已，心何所乐，何所趣，何所顺耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "After a bhikṣu arises from the state of cessation, his mind delights in detachment, pursues detachment, and follows detachment."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「比丘从灭尽定起已，心乐离、趣离、顺离。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised her, the upāsikā Viśākhā rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

毘舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毘舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one, how many kinds of awakening are there?"

复问曰：「贤圣！有几觉耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "There are three kinds of awakening: the awakening of pleasure, the awakening of pain, and the awakening of neither pleasure nor pain. What causes these? They are caused by contact with sense objects.

法乐比丘尼答曰：「有三觉：乐觉、苦觉、不苦不乐觉。此何缘有耶？缘更乐有。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised her, the upāsikā Viśākhā joyfully accepted it.

毘舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毘舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

She further asked, "Noble one! What is the awakening of pleasure? What is the awakening of pain? What is the awakening of neither pleasure nor pain?"

复问曰：「贤圣！云何乐觉？云何苦觉？云何不苦不乐觉耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "If pleasure arises from contact with the sense of pleasure, and one is well-aware of the pleasure of body and mind, this awareness is called the awakening of pleasure. If pain arises from contact with the sense of pleasure, and one is aware of the suffering of body and mind, this awareness is called the awakening of pain. If neither pleasure nor pain arises from contact with the sense of pleasure, and one is aware of neither the pleasure nor pain of body and mind, this awareness is called the awakening of neither pleasure nor pain."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「若乐更乐所触生，身心乐善觉，是觉谓乐觉也。若苦更乐所触生，身心苦不善觉，是觉谓苦觉也。若不苦不乐更乐所触生，身心不苦不乐，非善非不善觉，是觉谓不苦不乐觉。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised, she joyfully accepted it.

毘舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毘舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! What is pleasure in the awakening of pleasure? What is suffering? What is impermanence? What are the afflictions? What are the fetters? What is pleasure in the awakening of suffering? What is suffering? What is impermanence? What are the afflictions? What are the fetters? What is pleasure in the awakening of neither pleasure nor suffering? What is suffering? What is impermanence? What are the afflictions? What are the fetters?"

复问曰：「贤圣！乐觉者，云何乐？云何苦？云何无常？云何灾患？云何使耶？苦觉者，云何乐？云何苦？云何无常？云何灾患？云何使耶？不苦不乐觉者，云何乐？云何苦？云何无常？云何灾患？云何使耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapramada replied, "The awakening of pleasure is born of pleasure, abides in pleasure, and changes into suffering. Impermanence is affliction. Desire is the fetter. The awakening of suffering is born of suffering, abides in suffering, and changes into pleasure. Impermanence is affliction. Anger is the fetter. The awakening of neither pleasure nor suffering is not knowing suffering, not knowing pleasure. Impermanence is change. Ignorance is the fetter.

法乐比丘尼答曰：「乐觉者，生乐住乐，变易苦，无常者即是灾患，欲使也。苦觉者，生苦住苦，变易乐，无常者即是灾患，恚使也。不苦不乐觉者，不知苦、不知乐，无常者即是变易，无明使也。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised, the upāsikā Viśākhā rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

毘舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毘舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one, are all awakenings of pleasure fettered by desire? Are all awakenings of suffering fettered by anger? Are all awakenings of neither pleasure nor suffering fettered by ignorance?"

复问曰：「贤圣，一切乐觉欲使耶？一切苦觉恚使耶？一切不苦不乐觉无明使耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "Not all awakenings of pleasure are fettered by desire, not all awakenings of suffering are fettered by anger, and not all awakenings of neither pleasure nor suffering are fettered by ignorance. How is it that not all awakenings of pleasure are fettered by desire? If a bhikṣu, having separated from desire, separated from evil and unwholesome dharmas, with initial and sustained thought, has joy and happiness born of separation, and dwells having attained the first dhyāna, this is called an awakening of pleasure that is not fettered by desire. Why is that? Because this cuts off desire. How is it that not all awakenings of suffering are fettered by anger? If one seeks the happiness of higher liberation, and seeks the vow, one gives rise to sorrow and suffering. This is called an awakening of suffering that is not fettered by anger. Why is that? Because this cuts off anger. How is it that not all awakenings of neither pleasure nor suffering are fettered by ignorance? When joy and suffering cease, and the basis of joy and sorrow has already ceased, with neither pleasure nor suffering, with equanimity, mindfulness, and purity, one attains and dwells in the fourth dhyāna. This is called an awakening of neither pleasure nor suffering that is not fettered by ignorance. Why is that? Because this cuts off ignorance.

法乐比丘尼答曰：「非一切乐觉欲使也，非一切苦觉恚使也，非一切不苦不乐觉无明使也。云何乐觉非欲使耶？若比丘离欲、离恶不善之法，有觉、有观，离生喜、乐，得初禅成就游，是谓乐觉非欲使也。所以者何？此断欲故。云何苦觉非恚使耶？若求上解脱乐，求愿悒悒生忧苦，是谓苦觉非恚使也。所以者何？此断恚故。云何不苦不乐觉非无明使耶？乐灭、苦灭，喜、忧本已灭，不苦不乐、舍、念、清净，得第四禅成就游，是谓不苦不乐觉非无明使也。所以者何？此断无明故。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised her, the upāsikā Viśākhā joyfully accepted it.

毗舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毗舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! What is the antidote to the awakening of pleasure?"

复问曰：「贤圣！乐觉者有何对耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "The awakening of pleasure is countered by the awakening of suffering."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「乐觉者以苦觉为对。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised her, the upāsikā Viśākhā joyfully accepted it.

毗舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毗舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one, what counters the awakening of suffering?"

复问曰：「贤圣！苦觉者有何对耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "The awakening of suffering is countered by the awakening of happiness."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「苦觉者以乐觉为对。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised her, the upāsikā Viśākhā joyfully accepted it.

毘舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毘舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! What is the counter to the awakening of happiness and the awakening of suffering?"

复问曰：「贤圣！乐觉苦觉者有何对耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "The awakening of happiness and the awakening of suffering are countered by the non-suffering and non-happiness."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「乐觉苦觉者以不苦不乐为对。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised, the upāsikā Viśākhā rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

毘舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毘舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one! What is the counter to the awakening of neither suffering nor happiness?"

复问曰：「贤圣！不苦不乐觉者有何对耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "The awakening of neither suffering nor happiness is countered by ignorance."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「不苦不乐觉者以无明为对。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised her, the upāsikā Viśākhā joyfully accepted it.

毘舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毘舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one, what counters ignorance?"

复问曰：「贤圣！无明者有何对耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "Ignorance is countered by wisdom."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「无明者以明为对。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised her, the upāsikā Viśākhā joyfully accepted it.

毘舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毘舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one, what is the counteragent to wisdom?"

复问曰：「贤圣！明者有何对耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā replied, "Wisdom has nirvāṇa as its counteragent."

法乐比丘尼答曰：「明者以涅槃为对。」

The upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Noble one!" Having praised her, the upāsikā Viśākhā joyfully accepted it.

毘舍佉优婆夷闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤圣！」毘舍佉优婆夷叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Noble one, what is the counteragent to nirvāṇa?"

复问曰：「圣贤！涅槃者有何对耶？」

The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā said, "You may ask endless questions, but you will not be able to exhaust my knowledge. Nirvāṇa is without counteragent. Nirvāṇa is the transcendence of all snares and the complete cessation of snares. For this reason, I practice the holy life under the World-Honored One.

法乐比丘尼告曰：「君欲问无穷事，然君问事不能得穷我边也。涅槃者，无对也。涅槃者，以无羂过、羂羂灭讫。以此义故，从世尊行梵行。」

Then, the upāsikā Viśākhā, having heard the bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā's teaching, received it well, upheld it well, and recited it well. She then rose from her seat, bowed her head at the feet of the bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā, circumambulated her three times, and departed.

于是，毘舍佉优婆夷闻法乐比丘尼所说，善受善持，善诵习已，即从坐起，稽首礼法乐比丘尼足，绕三匝而去。

Then, not long after the upāsikā Viśākhā had left, the bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā went to where the Buddha was, bowed her head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and told the Buddha everything she had discussed with the upāsikā Viśākhā. Joining her palms towards the Buddha, she said, "World-Honored One! Did I speak and answer like this without slandering the World-Honored One? Did I speak the truth, speak in accordance with the Dharma, and speak in accordance with the Dharma's sequence? Is there no contradiction, dispute, or fault in what I said in accordance with the Dharma?"

于是，法乐比丘尼见毘舍佉优婆夷去后不久，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，却坐一面，与毘舍佉优婆夷所共论者，尽向佛说，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！我如是说、如是答，非为诬谤世尊耶？说真实、说如法、说法次法耶？于如法中，非有相违、有诤、有咎耶？」

The World-Honored One replied, "Bhikṣuṇī! You spoke and answered like this without slandering me. You spoke the truth, spoke in accordance with the Dharma, and spoke in accordance with the Dharma's sequence. There is no contradiction, dispute, or fault in what you said in accordance with the Dharma. Bhikṣuṇī! If the upāsikā Viśākhā were to come and ask me about this point and this text, I would answer her with this meaning, this point, and this text. Bhikṣuṇī! This meaning is as you have said. You should uphold it like this. Why is that? Because this is the meaning."

世尊答曰：「比丘尼！汝如是说、如是答，不诬谤我，汝说真实，说如法、说法次法，于如法中而不相违，无诤咎也。比丘尼！若毘舍佉优婆夷以此句、以此文来问我者，我为毘舍佉优婆夷亦以此义、以此句、以此文而答彼也。比丘尼！此义如汝所说，汝当如是持。所以者何？此说即是义故。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The bhikṣuṇī Dharmapradīpā and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。法乐比丘尼及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
