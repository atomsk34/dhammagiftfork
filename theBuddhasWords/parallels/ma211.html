MĀ 211

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 17 晡利多品

211. （二一一）大拘𫄨罗经

Parallel to: MN 43 (~) / AN 4.175 (~) / SĀ 251 (~)

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the city of Rājagṛha, staying in the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove.

一时，佛游王舍城，在竹林迦兰哆园。

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra rose from his afternoon meditation and went to where Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila was. They greeted each other and sat down to one side.

尔时，尊者舍黎子则于晡时从燕坐起，往诣尊者大拘𫄨罗所，共相问讯，却坐一面。

Venerable Śāriputra said, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila, I wish to ask something. May I ask?"

尊者舍黎子语曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！欲有所问，听我问耶？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "Venerable Śāriputra, ask as you wish. I will think about it after hearing it."

尊者大拘𫄨罗白曰：「尊者舍黎子！欲问便问，我闻已当思。」

Venerable Śāriputra asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila, you speak of unwholesome and unwholesome roots. What is unwholesome and what are the unwholesome roots?"

尊者舍黎子问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！不善者说不善，不善根者说不善根。何者不善，何者不善根耶？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "Bodily unwholesome conduct, verbal and mental unwholesome conduct, are unwholesome. Craving, anger, and delusion, are the unwholesome roots. These are called unwholesome, and these are called the unwholesome roots."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「身恶行，口、意恶行，是不善也。贪、恚、痴，是不善根也。是谓不善，是谓不善根。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Having praised, Venerable Śāriputra joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! You speak of wholesome and wholesome roots. What is wholesome, and what are the wholesome roots?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！善者说善，善根者说善根。何者为善，何者善根耶？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "Good bodily, verbal, and mental actions are wholesome. Non-greed, non-hatred, and non-delusion are wholesome roots. These are called wholesome, and these are called wholesome roots."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「身妙行，口、意妙行，是善也。不贪、不恚、不痴，是善根也。是谓为善，是谓善根。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Venerable Śāriputra, having praised this, rejoiced and practiced it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! You speak of wisdom. What is wisdom?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！智慧者说智慧，何者智慧？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "It is because of knowing thus that wisdom is spoken of. What does one know? One knows this suffering as it truly is, knows this origin of suffering, knows this cessation of suffering, and knows this path to the cessation of suffering as it truly is. It is because of knowing thus that wisdom is spoken of."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「知如是故说智慧。知何等耶？知此苦如真，知此苦习、知此苦灭、知此苦灭道如真，知如是故说智慧。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Having praised thus, Venerable Śāriputra joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! You speak of consciousness. What is consciousness?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！识者说识，何者识耶？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "Consciousness is called consciousness. What is consciousness? It is consciousness of form, sound, smell, taste, touch, and dharmas. Consciousness is called consciousness because it is conscious of these."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「识识是故说识。识何等耶？识色，识声、香、味、触、法，识识是故说识。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Venerable Śāriputra, having praised him, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! Are wisdom and consciousness the same or different? Can these two dharmas be separately established?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！智慧及识，此二法为合为别，此二法可得别施设耶？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "These two dharmas are the same and not separate. These two dharmas cannot be separately established. Why is that? What is known by wisdom is also known by consciousness. Therefore, these two dharmas are the same and not separate. These two dharmas cannot be separately established."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「此二法合不别，此二法不可别施设。所以者何？智慧所知，即是识所识。是故此二法合不别，此二法不可别施设。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Having praised him, Venerable Śāriputra joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! What do you know?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！知者汝以何等知？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "Knower, I know with wisdom."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「知者我以智慧知。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Venerable Śāriputra, having praised him, joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! What is the meaning of wisdom? What are its excellences? What are its merits?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！智慧有何义，有何胜，有何功德？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "Wisdom means the meaning of disenchantment, the meaning of desirelessness, and the meaning of seeing as it truly is."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「智慧者有厌义、无欲义、见如真义。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Having praised him, Venerable Śāriputra joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila, what is right view?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！云何正见？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "Knowing suffering as it truly is, knowing the origin, cessation, and path of suffering as they truly are, this is called right view."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「知苦如真，知苦习、灭、道如真者，是谓正见。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Venerable Śāriputra, having praised him, joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! How many causes and conditions give rise to right view?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！几因几缘生正见耶？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "Right view arises from two causes and two conditions. What are the two? First, hearing from others, second, contemplating within oneself. These are the two causes and two conditions that give rise to right view."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「二因二缘而生正见。云何为二？一者从他闻，二者内自思惟。是谓二因二缘而生正见。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Venerable Śāriputra, having praised, joyfully accepted and practiced it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! How many factors are included in right view, leading to the fruit of liberation through concentration, the fruit of liberation through wisdom, the merits of liberation through concentration, and the merits of liberation through wisdom?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！有几支摄正见，得心解脱果、慧解脱果，得心解脱功德、慧解脱功德耶？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "There are five factors included in right view, leading to the fruit of liberation through concentration, the fruit of liberation through wisdom, the merits of liberation through concentration, and the merits of liberation through wisdom. What are the five? First, what is included in the truth; second, what is included in precepts; third, what is included in extensive learning; fourth, what is included in tranquility; fifth, what is included in contemplation. These are said to be the five factors included in right view, leading to the fruit of liberation through concentration, the fruit of liberation through wisdom, the merits of liberation through concentration, and the merits of liberation through wisdom."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「有五支摄正见，得心解脱果、慧解脱果，得心解脱功德、慧解脱功德。云何为五？一者真谛所摄，二者戒所摄，三者博闻所摄，四者止所摄，五者观所摄。是谓有五支摄正见，得心解脱果、慧解脱果，得心解脱功德、慧解脱功德。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Venerable Śāriputra, having praised this, joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila, how does one give rise to future existence?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！云何生当来有？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "Ignorant ordinary beings, lacking knowledge and much learning, are covered by ignorance and bound by the fetter of craving. They do not see good friends, do not know the noble Dharma, and do not practice the noble Dharma. This is called giving rise to future existence."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「愚痴凡夫无知、不多闻，无明所覆，爱结所系，不见善知识，不知圣法，不御圣法，是谓生当来有。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Having praised him, Venerable Śāriputra joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila, how does one not give rise to future existence?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！云何不生当来有？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "If ignorance is exhausted and understanding arises, one will certainly exhaust suffering. This is called not giving rise to future existence."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「若无明已尽，明已生者，必尽苦也。是谓不生于当来有。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Venerable Śāriputra, having praised this, joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila, how many kinds of awakening are there?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！有几觉耶？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "There are three kinds of awakening: the awakening of pleasure, the awakening of pain, and the awakening of neither pleasure nor pain. What causes these? They are caused by contact."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「有三觉：乐觉、苦觉、不苦不乐觉：此缘何有耶？缘更乐有。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Having praised him, Venerable Śāriputra joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! Are the three dharmas of awakening, conception, and thought combined or separate? Can these three dharmas be separately established?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！觉、想、思，此三法为合为别？此三法可别施设耶？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "The three dharmas of awakening, conception, and thought are combined and not separate. These three dharmas cannot be separately established. Why is that? What is awakened by awakening is what is conceived by conception, and what is thought by thought. Therefore, these three dharmas are combined and not separate. These three dharmas cannot be separately established."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「觉、想、思，此三法合不别，此三法不可别施设。所以者何？觉所觉者，即是想所想，思所思。是故此三法合不别，此三法不可别施设。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Venerable Śāriputra, having praised this, joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! What is the opposite of cessation?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！灭者有何对？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "Cessation has no opposite."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「灭者无有对。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Venerable Śāriputra, having praised this, joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! The five faculties have different functions and realms, each experiencing its own realm. The eye faculty, the ear, nose, tongue, and body faculties - these five faculties have different functions and realms, each experiencing its own realm. Who experiences the complete realm for them? Who is their support?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！有五根异行、异境界，各各受自境界，眼根，耳、鼻、舌、身根，此五根异行、异境界，各各受自境界，谁为彼尽受境界，谁为彼依耶？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "The five faculties have different functions and realms, each experiencing its own realm. The eye faculty, the ear, nose, tongue, and body faculties - these five faculties have different functions and realms, each experiencing its own realm. The mind experiences the complete realm for them. The mind is their support."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「五根异行、异境界，各各自受境界，眼根，耳、鼻、舌、身根，此五根异行、异境界，各各受自境界，意为彼尽受境界，意为彼依。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Having praised him, Venerable Śāriputra joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! What does the mind depend on to abide?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！意者依何住耶？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "The mind depends on life, it abides depending on life."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「意者依寿，依寿住。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Venerable Śāriputra, having praised, joyfully accepted and practiced it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! What does life depend on?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！寿者依何住耶？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "Life depends on warmth, it abides depending on warmth."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「寿者依暖，依暖住。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Venerable Śāriputra, having praised, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! Life and warmth, are these two dharmas combined or separate? Can these two dharmas be separately established?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！寿及暖，此二法为合为别？此二法可得别施设耶？」

Venerable Mahā-Kauṣṭhila replied, "Life and warmth, these two dharmas are combined, not separate. These two dharmas cannot be separately established. Why is that? Because of life there is warmth, because of warmth there is life. If there is no life, there is no warmth. If there is no warmth, there is no life. It is like because of oil and wick, a lamp can burn. In it, because of fire there is light. Because of light there is fire. If there is no fire, there is no light. If there is no light, there is no fire. In this way, because of life there is warmth. Because of warmth there is life. If there is no life, there is no warmth. If there is no warmth, there is no life. Therefore, these two dharmas are combined, not separate. These two dharmas cannot be separately established."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「寿及暖，此二法合不别，此二法不可别施设。所以者何？因寿故有暖，因暖故有寿，若无寿者则无暖，无暖者则无寿。犹如因油因炷，故得燃灯，彼中因［火＊佥］故有光，因光故有［火＊佥］，若无［火＊佥］者则无光，无光者则无［火＊佥］。如是因寿故有暖，因暖故有寿，若无寿者则无暖，无暖者则无寿。是故此二法合不别，此二法不可别施设。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Venerable Śāriputra, having praised this, joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! How many dharmas arise, after the body dies, the body is discarded in the charnel ground, like a wooden object without sentience?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！有几法生身死已，身弃冢间，如木无情？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "There are three dharmas that arise, after the body dies, the body is discarded in the charnel ground, like a wooden object without sentience. What are the three? First, life force, second, warmth, and third, consciousness. These three dharmas arise, after the body dies, the body is discarded in the charnel ground, like a wooden object without sentience."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「有三法生身死已，身弃冢间，如木无情。云何为三？一者寿，二者暖，三者识。此三法生身死已，身弃冢间，如木无情。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard, praised, saying, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Venerable Śāriputra, having praised, joyfully accepted and practiced.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! What is the difference between death and entering the state of cessation?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！若死及入灭尽定者，有何差别？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "For the dead, life has ceased, warmth has departed, and the faculties have decayed. For a monk entering the state of cessation, life has not ceased, warmth has not departed, and the faculties have not decayed. This is the difference between death and entering the state of cessation."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「死者寿命灭讫，温暖已去，诸根败坏。比丘入灭尽定者，寿不灭讫，暖亦不去，诸根不败坏。死及入灭尽定者，是谓差别。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Having praised him, Venerable Śāriputra joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! What is the difference between entering the state of cessation and entering the state of non-conception?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！若入灭尽定及入无想定者，有何差别？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "Bhikṣus who enter the state of cessation have extinguished perception and cognition. Bhikṣus who enter the state of non-conception have not extinguished perception and cognition. This is the difference between entering the state of cessation and entering the state of non-conception."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「比丘入灭尽定者，想及知灭。比丘入无想定者，想知不灭。若入灭尽定及入无想定者，是谓差别。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Having praised him, Venerable Śāriputra joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! What is the difference between arising from the state of cessation and arising from the state of non-conception?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！若从灭尽定起及从无想定起者，有何差别？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "When a bhikṣu arises from the state of cessation, he does not think, 'I have arisen from the state of cessation.' When a bhikṣu arises from the state of non-conception, he thinks, 'Do I have conception? Do I not have conception?' This is the difference between arising from the state of cessation and arising from the state of non-conception."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「比丘从灭尽定起时，不如是念：『我从灭尽定起。』比丘从无想定起时，作如是念：『我为有想，我为无想？』从灭尽定起及从无想定起者，是谓差别。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Having praised him, Venerable Śāriputra joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! When a bhikṣu enters the state of cessation, which dharmas does he cease first? Is it bodily action, or verbal and mental action?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！比丘入灭尽定时先灭何法？为身行，为口、意行耶？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "When a bhikṣu enters the state of cessation, he first ceases bodily action, then verbal action, and finally mental action."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「比丘入灭尽定时，先灭身行，次灭口行，后灭意行。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Having praised, Venerable Śāriputra joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! When a bhikṣu arises from the state of cessation, which arises first - bodily action, verbal action, or mental action?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！比丘从灭尽定起时，先生何法？为身行，口、意行耶？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "When a bhikṣu arises from the state of cessation, mental action arises first, then verbal action, and finally bodily action."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「比丘从灭尽定起时，先生意行，次生口行，后生身行。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Having praised him, Venerable Śāriputra joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! When a bhikṣu arises from the state of cessation, how many contacts does he touch?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！比丘从灭尽定起时触几触？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "When a bhikṣu arises from the state of cessation, he touches three contacts. What are the three? They are the contact of non-movement, the contact of nothing whatsoever, and the contact of signlessness. When a bhikṣu arises from the state of cessation, he touches these three contacts."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「比丘从灭尽定起时触三触。云何为三？一者不移动触，二者无所有触，三者无相触。比丘从灭尽定起时触此三触。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Having praised him, Venerable Śāriputra joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila! Are emptiness, signlessness, and wishlessness different in meaning and expression, or are they the same in meaning but different in expression?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！空、无愿、无相，此三法异义、异文耶？为一义、异文耶？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "Emptiness, signlessness, and wishlessness are different in meaning and expression."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「空、无愿、无相，此三法异义、异文。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Having praised him, Venerable Śāriputra joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila, how many causes and conditions give rise to the immovable samādhi?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！有几因几缘生不移动定耶？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "There are four causes and four conditions that give rise to the immovable samādhi. What are the four? If a bhikṣu, having separated from desires, separated from evil and unwholesome dharmas, attains and abides in the fourth dhyāna, this is called the four causes and four conditions that give rise to the immovable samādhi."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「有四因四缘生不移动定。云何为四？若比丘离欲、离恶不善之法，至得第四禅成就游，是谓四因四缘生不移动定。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Having praised him, Venerable Śāriputra joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila, how many causes and conditions give rise to the samādhi of nothingness?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！有几因几缘生无所有定耶？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "There are three causes and three conditions that give rise to the samādhi of nothingness. What are the three? If a bhikṣu transcends all perceptions of form and attains the state of nothingness, this is said to be the three causes and three conditions that give rise to the samādhi of nothingness."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「有三因三缘生无所有定。云何为三？若比丘度一切色想，至得无所有处成就游，是谓有三因三缘生无所有定。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Having praised him, Venerable Śāriputra joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila, how many causes and conditions give rise to the samādhi of non-perception?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！有几因几缘生无想定？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "There are two causes and two conditions that give rise to the samādhi of non-perception. What are the two? First, not contemplating any thoughts, and second, contemplating the realm of non-perception. These are the two causes and two conditions that give rise to the samādhi of non-perception."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「有二因二缘生无想定。云何为二？一者不念一切想，二者念无想界。是谓二因二缘生无想定。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Venerable Śāriputra, having praised him, rejoiced and upheld it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila, how many causes and conditions are there for abiding in the samādhi of non-perception?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！有几因几缘住无想定耶？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "There are two causes and two conditions for abiding in the samādhi of non-perception. What are the two? First, not contemplating any thoughts; second, contemplating the realm of non-perception. These are the two causes and two conditions for abiding in the samādhi of non-perception."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「有二因二缘住无想定。云何为二？一者不念一切想，二者念无想界。是谓二因二缘住无想定。」

Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent! Virtuous Kauṣṭhila!" Venerable Śāriputra, having praised him, joyfully accepted it.

尊者舍黎子闻已，叹曰：「善哉！善哉！贤者拘𫄨罗！」尊者舍黎子叹已，欢喜奉行。

He further asked, "Virtuous Kauṣṭhila, how many causes and conditions are there for arising from the samādhi of non-perception?"

复问曰：「贤者拘𫄨罗！有几因几缘从无想定起？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "There are three causes and three conditions for arising from the samādhi of non-perception. What are the three? First, recollecting all perceptions, second, not recollecting the realm of non-perception, and third, based on this body and the six sense bases, the condition of the life faculty. These are the three causes and three conditions for arising from the samādhi of non-perception."

尊者大拘𫄨罗答曰：「有三因三缘从无想定起。云何为三？一者念一切想，二者不念无想界，三者因此身因六处缘命根，是谓三因三缘，从无想定起。」

Thus, the two Venerables praised each other, saying "Excellent! Excellent!" They spoke to each other and joyfully practiced what was said. They rose from their seats and departed.

如是，彼二尊更相称叹善哉善哉，更互所说，欢喜奉行。从坐起去。

The Sūtra of Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila, the tenth, is completed.
