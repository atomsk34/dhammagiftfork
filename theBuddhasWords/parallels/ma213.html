MĀ 213

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 18 例品

213. （二一三）法庄严经

Parallel to: MN 89 / AN 10.30 / EĀ 38.10

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling among the Śākyas, at a Śākya town called Miḷiya.

一时，佛游释中，在释家都邑，名弥娄离。

At that time, King Pasenadi of Kosala was going to a town named Nagara for some business, together with the chief minister. When King Pasenadi arrived at the park, he saw that under the trees there was silence, seclusion, no evil, no people, and it was suitable for meditation. Having seen this, he remembered the World-Honored One. King Pasenadi said, "Chief minister, this place under the trees is silent, secluded, without evil, without people, and suitable for meditation. I often come here to see the Buddha. Chief minister, where is the World-Honored One now? I wish to go and see him."

尔时，拘萨罗王波斯匿与长作共俱有所为故，出诣邑名城。拘萨罗王波斯匿至彼园观，见诸树下寂无音声，远离，无恶，无有人民，随顺燕坐，见已，忆念世尊。拘萨罗王波斯匿告曰：「长作！今此树下寂无音声，远离，无恶，无有人民，随顺燕坐，此处我数往见佛。长作！世尊今在何处？我欲往见。」

The chief minister replied, "Your Majesty! I have heard that the World-Honored One is traveling among the Śākyas, at a Śākya town called Miḷiya.

长作答曰：「天王！我闻世尊游释中，在释家都邑，名弥娄离。」

King Pasenadi of Kosala asked again, "Chief minister, how far is the Śākya town called Miḷiya from here?"

拘萨罗王波斯匿复问曰：「长作！释家都邑名弥娄离，去此几许？」

The chief minister replied, "Your Majesty! It is three krośas from here."

长作答曰：「天王！去此三拘娄舍。」

King Pasenadi of Kosala said, "Chief minister! Order the carriage to be prepared. I wish to go to the Buddha."

拘萨罗王波斯匿告曰：「长作！可勅严驾，我欲诣佛。」

The chief minister received the order and immediately ordered the carriage to be prepared, saying, "Your Majesty! The carriage is ready. Please do as you wish." King Pasenadi of Kosala then ascended the carriage and left the city, going to the city of the Śākyas named Mi-lou-li.

长作受教，即勅严驾，白曰：「天王！严驾已讫，随天王意。」拘萨罗王波斯匿即升乘出城外，往至释家都邑，名弥娄离。

At that time, many bhikṣus were walking in the open air outside the gate of Mi-lou-li. King Pasenadi of Kosala went to where the bhikṣus were and asked, "Venerable ones! Where is the World-Honored One walking during the day now?"

尔时，弥娄离门外众多比丘露地经行，拘萨罗王波斯匿往诣诸比丘所，问曰：「诸尊！世尊今在何处昼行？」

The many bhikṣus replied, "Great King! In the large building to the east, with windows open and doors closed, the World-Honored One is walking during the day there now. Great King! If you wish to see him, go there. Having arrived, stand outside, cough and knock on the door. If the World-Honored One hears, he will surely open the door for you."

众多比丘答曰：「大王！彼东向大屋，开窓闭户，世尊今在彼中昼行。大王！欲见便往诣彼，到已住外，謦欬敲户，世尊闻者，必为开户。」

King Pasenadi of Kosala then got down from his carriage. If a king of the kṣatriya class with a crown comes to a place where people live and rules the earth, he has five adornments: a sword, an umbrella, a flower garland, a jeweled-handled whisk, and decorated shoes. He takes them all off and gives them to the attendant. The attendant thinks, "The king will now enter alone. We should all stay here and wait."

拘萨罗王波斯匿即便下车。若有王刹利顶来而得人处，教令大地，有五仪饰，剑、葢、华鬘及珠柄拂、严饰之屣，彼尽脱已，授与长作，长作念曰：「天王今者必当独入，我等应共住此待耳。」

Then King Pasenadi of Kosala, surrounded by his retinue, walked to the large building facing east. Having arrived, he stood outside, coughed and knocked on the door. The World-Honored One heard and immediately opened the door. King Pasenadi of Kosala then entered the building, went before the Buddha, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and thrice declared his name, saying, "I am King Pasenadi of Kosala. I am King Pasenadi of Kosala."

于是，拘萨罗王波斯匿眷属围遶，步往至彼东向大屋，到已住外，謦欬敲户，世尊闻已，即为开户，拘萨罗王波斯匿便入彼屋，前至佛所，稽首礼足，再三自称姓名：「我是拘萨罗王波斯匿。我是拘萨罗王波斯匿。」

The World-Honored One replied, "So it is, great king! You are King Pasenadi of Kosala. You are King Pasenadi of Kosala." After King Pasenadi of Kosala declared his name thrice, he bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side.

世尊答曰：「如是。大王！汝是拘萨罗王波斯匿。汝是拘萨罗王波斯匿。」拘萨罗王波斯匿再三自称姓名已，稽首佛足，却坐一面。

The World-Honored One asked, "Great king! What meaning do you see in me that you bow your head and pay homage, making offerings and serving me?"

世尊问曰：「大王！见我有何等义，而自下意稽首礼足，供养承事耶？」

King Pasenadi of Kosala replied, "World-Honored One! I have a sense of peace in the Dharma regarding the Buddha. Therefore, I think, 'The Dharma taught by the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One is good, and the community of the World-Honored One's disciples is well-directed.' World-Honored One! When I sit in the capital, I see mothers quarreling with their sons, sons quarreling with their mothers, fathers, brothers, sisters, and relatives quarreling with each other. When they quarrel, the mothers speak ill of their sons, the sons speak ill of their mothers, fathers, brothers, sisters, and relatives speak ill of each other, let alone others. I see the community of the World-Honored One's disciples, the bhikṣus, who practice the holy life under the World-Honored One. Some bhikṣus have many disputes and abandon the precepts, give up the path, and do not speak ill of the Buddha, the Dharma, or the community. They only reproach themselves, 'I am evil, I am without virtue. Why is that? Because I am unable to practice the holy life under the World-Honored One for the rest of my life.' This is what I mean by having a sense of peace in the Dharma regarding the Buddha. Therefore, I think, 'The Dharma taught by the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One is good, and the community of the World-Honored One's disciples is well-directed.'

拘萨罗王波斯匿答曰：「世尊！我于佛而有法靖，因此故，我作是念：『如来、无所著、正尽觉所说法善，世尊弟子众善趣向也。』世尊！我坐都坐时，见母共子诤，子共母诤，父子、兄弟、姉妹、亲属展转共诤，彼鬪诤时，母说子恶，子说母恶，父子、兄弟、姉妹、亲属相更说恶，况复他人？我见世尊弟子诸比丘众从世尊行梵行，或有比丘少多起诤，舍戒罢道，不说佛恶，不说诸法恶，不说众恶，但自责数：『我为恶，我为无德。所以者何？以我不能从世尊自尽形寿修行梵行。』是谓我于佛而有法靖，因此故，我作是念：『如来、无所著、正尽觉所说法善，世尊弟子众善趣向也。』

Furthermore, World-Honored One! I see a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who practices the holy life for nine or ten months, but then abandons it and becomes defiled by desire, attached to desire, bound by desire, arrogantly accepting it, not seeing the danger, not seeing the way out, and still enjoying desire. World-Honored One! I see the community of the World-Honored One's disciples, the bhikṣus, who practice the holy life for the rest of their lives, up to billions of times. I do not see such pure holy life outside of this. This is what I mean by having a sense of peace in the Dharma regarding the Buddha. Therefore, I think, 'The Dharma taught by the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One is good, and the community of the World-Honored One's disciples is well-directed.'

「复次，世尊！我见一沙门梵志，或九月或十月，少多学行梵行，舍随本服，复为欲所染，染欲著欲，为欲所缚，憍慠受入，不见灾患，不见出要而乐行欲。世尊！我见世尊弟子诸比丘众，自尽形寿修行梵行，乃至亿数。我于此外，不见如是清净梵行，如世尊家，是谓我于佛而有法靖，因此故，我作是念：『如来、无所著、正尽觉所说法善，世尊弟子众善趣向也。』

Furthermore, World-Honored One! I see a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who is emaciated, haggard, with an extremely ugly complexion, and covered in white blisters, unpleasant to look at. I think, 'Why are these venerable ones emaciated, haggard, with an extremely ugly complexion, and covered in white blisters, unpleasant to look at? These venerable ones must not enjoy practicing the holy life, or they have physical ailments, or they do evil in private. That is why these venerable ones are emaciated, haggard, with an extremely ugly complexion, and covered in white blisters, unpleasant to look at.' I go to ask them, 'Venerable ones, why are you emaciated, haggard, with an extremely ugly complexion, and covered in white blisters, unpleasant to look at? Do you not enjoy practicing the holy life? Do you have physical ailments? Do you do evil in private? That is why you are emaciated, haggard, with an extremely ugly complexion, and covered in white blisters, unpleasant to look at.' They reply to me, 'Great King! It is the white disease. Great King! It is the white disease.'

「复次，世尊！我见一沙门梵志羸瘦憔悴，形色极恶，身生白疱，人不憙见，我作是念：『此诸尊何以羸瘦憔悴，形色极恶，身生白疱，人不憙见？此诸尊必不乐行梵行，或身有患，或屏处作恶，以是故诸尊羸瘦憔悴，形色极恶，身生白疱，人不憙见。』我往问彼：『诸尊何故羸瘦憔悴，形色极恶，身生白疱，人不憙见？诸尊不乐行梵行耶？为身有患耶？为屏处作恶耶？是故诸尊羸瘦憔悴，形色极恶，身生白疱，人不憙见。』彼答我曰：『大王！是白病。大王！是白病。』

World-Honored One! I see the community of the World-Honored One's disciples, the bhikṣus, who enjoy practicing the holy life, with upright conduct, pleasant complexions, clean bodies, without striving or seeking, guarding others' wives like deer, practicing the holy life for the rest of their lives. Having seen this, I think, 'Why do these venerable ones enjoy practicing the holy life, with upright conduct, pleasant complexions, clean bodies, without striving or seeking, guarding others' wives like deer, practicing the holy life for the rest of their lives? These venerable ones have attained freedom from desire, or attained the higher mind, and dwell in the happiness of the present life, which is easy to attain.' Therefore, these venerable ones enjoy practicing the holy life, with upright conduct, pleasant complexions, clean bodies, without striving or seeking, guarding others' wives like deer, practicing the holy life for the rest of their lives. If one practices desire and enjoys upright conduct, I should enjoy upright conduct. Because it is easy to attain the five sense pleasures. If these venerable ones attain freedom from desire, attain the higher mind, and dwell in the happiness of the present life, which is easy to attain, therefore these venerable ones enjoy practicing the holy life, with pleasant complexions, clean bodies, without striving or seeking, guarding others' wives like deer, practicing the holy life for the rest of their lives. This is what I mean by having a sense of peace in the Dharma regarding the Buddha. Therefore, I think, 'The Dharma taught by the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One is good, and the community of the World-Honored One's disciples is well-directed.'

「世尊！我见世尊弟子诸比丘众，乐行端正，面色悦泽，形体净洁，无为无求，护他妻食如鹿，自尽形寿修行梵行，我见已，作是念：『此诸尊何故乐行端正，面色悦泽，形体净洁，无为无求，护他妻食如鹿，自尽形寿修行梵行？此诸尊或得离欲，或得增上心，现法乐居，易不难得。』是故此诸尊乐行端正，面目悦泽，形体净洁，无为无求，护他妻食如鹿，自尽形寿修行梵行。若行欲乐行端正者，我应乐行端正。何以故？我得五欲功德，易不难得。若此诸尊得离欲，得增上心，于现法乐居，易不难得，是故此诸尊乐行端正，面色悦泽，形体净洁，无为无求，护他妻食如鹿，自尽形寿修行梵行，是谓我于佛而有法靖。因此故，我作是念：『如来、无所著、正尽觉所说法善，世尊弟子众善趣向也。』

Furthermore, World-Honored One! I see a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who is intelligent and wise, who proclaims his own intelligence and wisdom, who is learned and decisive, who is well-versed in the scriptures, who subdues strong opponents, who speaks with clarity and understanding, whose fame and virtue are widely known, who is renowned throughout the world, who goes to various places and destroys the views and doctrines of others, and who establishes his own doctrines, saying, 'We should go to the śramaṇa Gautama and ask him such and such questions. If he can answer, we will challenge him; if he cannot answer, we will also challenge him and leave.' Having heard that the World-Honored One is traveling to a certain village or town, they go to where the Buddha is, but they do not dare to ask the World-Honored One about anything, let alone challenge him. This is what I mean by having a sense of peace in the Dharma regarding the Buddha. Therefore, I think, 'The Dharma taught by the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One is good, and the community of the World-Honored One's disciples is well-directed.'

「复次，世尊！我见一沙门梵志聪明智慧，自称聪明智慧，博闻决定，谙识诸经，制伏强敌，谈论觉了，名德流布，一切世间无不闻知，所游至处坏诸见宗，輙自立论，而作是说：『我等往至沙门瞿昙所，问如是如是事，若能答者，当难诘彼；若不能答，亦难诘已，舍之而去。』彼闻世尊游某村邑，往至佛所，尚不敢问于世尊事，况复欲难诘耶？是谓我于佛而有法靖，因此故，我作是念：『如来、无所著、正尽觉所说法善，世尊弟子众善趣向也。』

Furthermore, World-Honored One! I see a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who is intelligent and wise, who proclaims his own intelligence and wisdom, who is learned and decisive, who is well-versed in the scriptures, who subdues strong opponents, who speaks with clarity and understanding, whose fame and virtue are widely known, who is renowned throughout the world, who goes to various places and destroys the views and doctrines of others, and who establishes his own doctrines, saying, 'We should go to the śramaṇa Gautama and ask him such and such questions. If he can answer, we will challenge him; if he cannot answer, we will also challenge him and leave.' Having heard that the World-Honored One is traveling to a certain village or town, they go to where the Buddha is and ask the World-Honored One about something. The World-Honored One answers them, and having heard the answer, they are delighted. They bow their heads at the Buddha's feet, circumambulate him three times, and leave. This is what I mean by having a sense of peace in the Dharma regarding the Buddha. Therefore, I think, 'The Dharma taught by the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One is good, and the community of the World-Honored One's disciples is well-directed.'

「复次，世尊！我见一沙门梵志聪明智慧，自称聪明智慧，博闻决定，谙识诸经，制伏强敌，谈论觉了，名德流布，一切世间无不闻知，所游至处坏诸见宗，輙自立论，而作是说：『我等往至沙门瞿昙所，问如是如是事，若能答者，当难诘彼；若不能答，亦难诘已，舍之而去。』彼闻世尊游某村邑，往至佛所，问世尊事，世尊为答，彼闻答已，便得欢喜，稽首佛足，绕三匝而去，是谓我于佛而有法靖。因此故，我作是念：『如来、无所著、正尽觉所说法善，世尊弟子众善趣向也。』

Furthermore, World-Honored One! I see a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who is intelligent and wise, who proclaims his own intelligence and wisdom, who is learned and decisive, who is well-versed in the scriptures, who subdues strong opponents, who speaks with clarity and understanding, whose fame and virtue are widely known, who is renowned throughout the world, who goes to various places and destroys the views and doctrines of others, and who establishes his own doctrines, saying, 'We should go to the śramaṇa Gautama and ask him such and such questions. If he can answer, we will challenge him; if he cannot answer, we will also challenge him and leave.' Having heard that the World-Honored One is traveling to a certain village or town, they go to where the Buddha is and ask the World-Honored One about something. The World-Honored One answers them, and having heard the answer, they are delighted. They take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the community of monks. The World-Honored One accepts them as lay followers, who take refuge for life until the end of their lives. This is what I mean by having a sense of peace in the Dharma regarding the Buddha. Therefore, I think, 'The Dharma taught by the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One is good, and the community of the World-Honored One's disciples is well-directed.'

「复次，世尊！我见一沙门梵志聪明智慧，自称聪明智慧，博闻决定，谙] cf.［丽】识诸经，制伏强敌，谈论觉了，名德流布，一切世间无不闻知，所游至处坏诸见宗，輙自立论，而作是说：『我等往至沙门瞿昙所，问如是如是事，若能答者，当难诘彼；若不能答，亦难诘已，舍之而去。』彼闻世尊游某村邑，往至佛所，问世尊事，世尊为答，彼闻答已，便得欢喜，即自归佛、法及比丘众，世尊受彼为优婆塞，终身自归，乃至命尽，是谓我于佛而有法靖。因此故，我作是念：『如来、无所著、正尽觉所说法善，世尊弟子众善趣向也。』

Furthermore, World-Honored One! I see a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who is intelligent and wise, who proclaims his own intelligence and wisdom, who is learned and decisive, who is well-versed in the scriptures, who subdues strong opponents, who speaks with clarity and understanding, whose fame and virtue are widely known, who is renowned throughout the world, who goes to various places and destroys the views and doctrines of others, and who establishes his own doctrines, saying, 'We should go to the śramaṇa Gautama and ask him such and such questions. If he can answer, we will challenge him; if he cannot answer, we will also challenge him and leave.' Having heard that the World-Honored One is traveling to a certain village or town, they go to where the Buddha is and ask the World-Honored One about something. The World-Honored One answers them, and having heard the answer, they are delighted. They then request the World-Honored One to allow them to go forth and practice, and to receive full ordination, to attain the state of a bhikṣu. The World-Honored One then accepts them and gives them full ordination, allowing them to attain the state of a bhikṣu.

「复次，世尊！我见一沙门梵志聪明智慧，自称聪明智慧，博闻决定，谙] cf.［丽】识诸经，制伏强敌，谈论觉了，名德流布，一切世间无不闻知，所游至处坏诸见宗，輙自立论，而作是说：『我等往至沙门瞿昙所，问如是如是事，若能答者，当难诘彼；若不能答，亦难诘已，舍之而去。』彼闻世尊游某村邑，往至佛所，问世尊事，世尊为答。彼闻答已，便得欢喜，即从世尊求出家学，而受具足，得比丘法，佛便度彼而授具足，得比丘法。

If those venerable ones go forth to practice the path, receive full ordination, and attain the state of a bhikṣu, then they will dwell in solitude, far away, with a mind free from carelessness, practicing diligently. Having dwelt in solitude, far away, with a mind free from carelessness, practicing diligently, if those sons of noble families shave off their hair and beards, don the saffron robes, leave home out of true faith, become homeless, and practice the path, then they will complete the unsurpassed holy life, know and realize it for themselves in this very life, and dwell having attained it by their own supernormal power. With the end of birth, the holy life has been lived, what was to be done has been done, there is no more of this mundane state - this they know for themselves. If those venerable ones know the Dharma, and attain arahantship, then they will think: 'Venerable ones! We were formerly ignorant and lost. Why is that? We formerly were not śramaṇas but claimed to be śramaṇas, not holy ones but claimed to be holy ones, not arhats but claimed to be arhats. Now we are śramaṇas, holy ones, and arhats.' This is what I mean by having a sense of peace in the Dharma regarding the Buddha. Therefore, I think, 'The Dharma taught by the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One is good, and the community of the World-Honored One's disciples is well-directed.'

「若彼诸尊出家学道而受具足，得比丘法已，独住远离，心无放逸，修行精勤。彼独住远离，心无放逸，修行精勤已，若族姓子所为，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，唯无上梵行讫，于现法中自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。若彼诸尊知法已，乃至得阿罗诃，得阿罗诃已，便作是念：『诸贤！我本几了几失？所以者何？我本非沙门称沙门，非梵行称梵行，非阿罗诃称阿罗诃，我等今是沙门，是梵行，是阿罗诃。』是谓我于佛而有法靖。因此故，我作是念：『如来、无所著、正尽觉所说法善，世尊弟子众善趣向也。』

Furthermore, World-Honored One! When I am in the country, I order the execution of those who have committed no offense, and the execution of those who have committed an offense. However, when I am in the capital, I cannot say, 'You all stay here. No one asks you about matters. When people ask me about matters, you cannot resolve them. I can resolve them.' In the meantime, we discuss other matters, and before the previous discussion is finished, I often see the World-Honored One surrounded by a great assembly teaching the Dharma. Among them, someone snores loudly. Someone tells him, 'Sir, do not snore loudly. Do you not want to hear the World-Honored One's Dharma teaching like sweet dew?' Having heard this, he immediately falls silent. I think, 'The Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, the Tamer of those to be tamed, is truly wonderful and extraordinary! Why is that? Because without weapons, he peacefully and happily teaches the Dharma according to the Dharma.' This is what I mean by having a sense of peace in the Dharma regarding the Buddha. Therefore, I think, 'The Dharma taught by the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One is good, and the community of the World-Honored One's disciples is well-directed.'

「复次，世尊！我自若居国，无过者令杀，有过者令杀，然在都坐，我故不得作如是说：『卿等并住，无人问卿事，人问我事，卿等不能断此事，我能断此事。』于其中间竞论余事，不待前论讫，我数见世尊大众围绕说法，彼中一人鼾眠作声，有人语彼：『君莫鼾眠作声，君不用闻世尊说法如甘露耶？』彼人闻已，即便默然。我作是念：『如来、无所著、正尽觉、众调御士，甚奇！甚特！所以者何？以无刀杖，皆自如法，安隐快乐。』是谓我于佛而有法靖。因此故，我作是念：『如来、无所著、正尽觉所说法善，世尊弟子众善趣向也。』

Furthermore, World-Honored One! I give money and wealth to the ministers Xianyu and Sujiu, and I always praise them. Their lives depend on me. However, I cannot make them humble, respectful, reverent, and devoted to me, as they are to the World-Honored One. This is what I mean by having a sense of peace in the Dharma regarding the Buddha. Therefore, I think, 'The Dharma taught by the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One is good, and the community of the World-Honored One's disciples is well-directed.'

「复次，世尊！我于仙余及宿旧二臣出钱财赐，亦常称誉，彼命由我，然不能令彼仙余及宿旧二臣下意恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事于我，如为世尊下意恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事也，是谓我于佛而有法靖。因此故，我作是念：『如来、无所著、正尽觉所说法善，世尊弟子众善趣向也。』

Furthermore, World-Honored One! When I was a hermit, I stayed in a small hut to test the ministers Xianyu and Sujiu, to see in which direction they would sleep, towards me or towards the World-Honored One. Then, in the first watch of the night, the ministers Xianyu and Sujiu sat cross-legged in silence. In the middle watch of the night, they heard the World-Honored One in a certain place, and then they turned their heads towards him and their feet towards me. Seeing this, I thought, 'These ministers Xianyu and Sujiu are not in the present superior state, and therefore they do not humble themselves, respect, revere, and serve me as they do the World-Honored One.' This is what I mean by having a sense of peace in the Dharma regarding the Buddha. Therefore, I think, 'The Dharma taught by the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One is good, and the community of the World-Honored One's disciples is well-directed.'

「复次，世尊！我昔出仙，宿一小屋中，欲试仙余、宿旧二臣，知彼头向何处眠耶？为向我，为向世尊？于是，仙余、宿旧二臣则于初夜结跏趺坐，默然燕坐，至中夜闻世尊在某方处，便以头向彼，以足向我。我见已，作是念：『此仙余及宿旧二臣不在现胜事，是故彼不下意恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事于我，如为世尊下意恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事也。』是谓我于佛而有法靖。因此故，我作是念：『如来、无所著、正尽觉所说法善，世尊弟子众善趣向也。』

"Furthermore, World-Honored One! I am also a king, and the World-Honored One is also a Dharma King. I am also a kshatriya, and the World-Honored One is also a kshatriya. I am also from Kosala, and the World-Honored One is also from Kosala. I am also eighty years old, and the World-Honored One is also eighty years old. For these reasons, World-Honored One, I am able to humble myself, respect, revere, and serve the World-Honored One for the rest of my life. World-Honored One! I have many affairs now and wish to take leave and return."

「复次，世尊！我亦国王，世尊亦法王，我亦刹利，世尊亦刹利，我亦拘萨罗，世尊亦拘萨罗，我年八十，世尊亦八十。世尊以此事故，我堪耐为世尊尽形寿，下意恭敬、尊重、供养、奉事。世尊！我今多事，欲还请辞。」

The World-Honored One said, "Great King! Know the time yourself." Then King Pasenadi of Kosala, having heard what the Buddha taught, received and remembered it well. He then rose from his seat, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and departed.

世尊告曰：「大王！自知时。」于是，拘萨罗王波斯匿闻佛所说，善受持诵，即从坐起，稽首佛足，绕三匝而去。

At that time, Venerable Ānanda was standing behind the World-Honored One, holding a whisk to attend to the Buddha. Then the World-Honored One turned around and said, "Ānanda! If there are any monks staying in the Mīlāruvana, have them all gather in the Dharma hall."

尔时，尊者阿难住世尊后，执拂侍佛。于是，世尊回顾告曰：「阿难！若有比丘依弥娄离林住者，令彼一切集在讲堂。」

Then Venerable Ānanda, having received the Buddha's instruction, had all the monks staying in the Mīlāruvana gather in the Dharma hall. He then returned to the Buddha and said, "World-Honored One! All the monks staying in the Mīlāruvana have already gathered in the Dharma hall. May the World-Honored One know the time himself."

于是，尊者阿难受佛教已，若诸比丘依弥娄离林住者，令彼一切集在讲堂，还诣佛所，白曰：「世尊！若有比丘依弥娄离林住者，彼一切已集讲堂，唯愿世尊自当知时。」

Then the World-Honored One, together with Venerable Ānanda, went to the Dharma hall in front of the assembly of monks, spread out a seat, and sat down. He said, "Monks! After King Pasenadi of Kosala spoke this Dharma Adornment Sutra in front of me, he rose from his seat, bowed at my feet, circumambulated me three times, and left. Monks! You should uphold this Dharma Adornment Sutra, recite it well, and practice it well. Why is that? Monks! This Dharma Adornment Sutra is in accordance with the meaning and the Dharma, is the basis for the practice of purity, leads to wisdom, leads to enlightenment, and leads to nirvana. If a noble son of a good family, with utmost faith, leaves home and family to study the path, he should also uphold, recite, and practice this Dharma Adornment Sutra."

于是，世尊将尊者阿难往至讲堂比丘众前，敷座而坐，告曰：「比丘！今拘萨罗王波斯匿在我前说此法庄严经已，即从座起，稽首我足，绕三匝而去。比丘！汝等当受持此法庄严经，善诵善习。所以者何？比丘！此法庄严经，如义如法，为梵行本，趣智趣觉，趣至涅槃。若族姓子至信、舍家、无家、学道者，亦当受持，当诵当习此法庄严经。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those monks, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
