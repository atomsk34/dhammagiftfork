MĀ 217

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 18 例品

217. （二一七）八城经

Parallel to: MN 52 / AN 11.16

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, not long after the Buddha's parinirvāṇa, many eminent and virtuous bhikṣus were dwelling in the Chicken Grove in the city of Vārāṇasī.

一时，佛般涅槃后不久，众多上尊名德比丘游波罗利子城，住在鸡园。

At that time, the tenth householder from the eight cities brought many fine goods to the city of Vārāṇasī to trade. There, the tenth householder from the eight cities quickly sold his many fine goods, making a great profit. Joyful and elated, he left the city of Vārāṇasī and went to the Chicken Grove, where many eminent and virtuous bhikṣus were dwelling. He bowed his head at their feet, sat to one side, and the eminent and virtuous bhikṣus taught him the Dharma, inspiring and uplifting him, bringing him to a state of joy. Having taught him the Dharma with immeasurable skillful means, inspiring and uplifting him, bringing him to a state of joy, they remained silent.

是时，第十居士八城持多妙货，往至波罗利子城治生贩卖。于是，第十居士八城，彼多妙货，货卖速售，大得财利，欢喜踊跃，出波罗利子城，往诣鸡园众多上尊名德比丘所，稽首礼足，却坐一面。时，诸上尊名德比丘为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，默然而住。

At that time, the eminent bhikṣus taught him the Dharma, inspiring and uplifting him, bringing him to a state of joy. Then the tenth householder from the eight cities said, "Venerable ones, where is Venerable Ānanda now? I wish to go and see him."

时，诸上尊比丘为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已。于是，第十居士八城白曰：「上尊！尊者阿难今在何处？我欲往见。」

The eminent bhikṣus replied, "Householder, Venerable Ānanda is now at the high pavilion by the Monkey River in Vaiśālī. If you wish to see him, go there."

诸上尊比丘答曰：「居士！尊者阿难今在鞞舍离猕猴江边高楼台观，若欲见者，可往至彼。」

At that time, the tenth householder from the eight cities immediately rose from his seat, bowed his head at the feet of the eminent bhikṣus, circumambulated them three times, and departed. He went to where Venerable Ānanda was, bowed his head at his feet, sat to one side, and said, "Venerable Ānanda, I wish to ask something. May I ask?"

尔时，第十居士八城即从坐起，稽首诸上尊比丘足，绕三匝而去，往诣尊者阿难所，稽首礼足，却坐一面，白曰：「尊者阿难！欲有所问，听我问耶？」

Venerable Ānanda said, "Householder, ask as you wish. I shall think about it after hearing."

尊者阿难告曰：「居士！欲问便问，我闻已当思。」

The householder asked, "Venerable Ānanda, the World-Honored One, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, who has accomplished the wisdom eye and sees the ultimate truth, has he ever spoken of a single Dharma, such that if a noble disciple abides in it, the taints are completely exhausted without remainder, and they attain mental liberation?"

居士问曰：「尊者阿难！世尊、如来、无所著、正尽觉成就慧眼，见第一义，颇说一法，若圣弟子住漏尽无余，得心解脱耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "Yes."

尊者阿难答曰：「如是。」

The householder asked, "Venerable Ānanda, the World-Honored One, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, who has accomplished the wisdom eye and sees the ultimate truth, how does he speak of a single Dharma, such that if a noble disciple abides in it, the taints are completely exhausted without remainder, and they attain mental liberation?"

居士问曰：「尊者阿难！世尊、如来、无所著、正尽觉成就慧眼，见第一义。云何说有一法，若圣弟子住漏尽无余，得心解脱耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "Householder, the well-learned noble disciple, having separated from desire, separated from evil and unwholesome states, attains and abides in the fourth dhyāna. They observe the Dharma according to the Dharma based on that state. If they abide in that state and attain the exhaustion of the taints, it is possible. If they abide in that state and do not attain the exhaustion of the taints, they may desire, love, delight in, and be at peace with that Dharma, and joyfully cut off the five lower fetters, be reborn there, and attain parinirvāṇa, attaining the non-retrogressive state, never returning to this world.

尊者阿难答曰：「居士！多闻圣弟子离欲、离恶不善之法，至得第四禅成就游。彼依此处，观法如法，彼依此处，观法如法，住彼得漏尽者，或有是处。若住彼，不得漏尽者，或因此法，欲法、爱法、乐法、靖法，爱乐欢喜，断五下分结尽，化生于彼而般涅槃，得不退法，终不还此。

Furthermore, householder, the well-learned noble disciple, with a mind imbued with loving-kindness, dwells pervading one direction, likewise the second, the third, and the fourth direction, and also the four intermediate directions, above, and below, all around, everywhere. With a mind imbued with loving-kindness, free from enmity and ill-will, without rivalry or dispute, as vast and exalted as the sky, without enmity and ill-will, he dwells pervading the entire world with a mind imbued with loving-kindness, abundant, exalted, immeasurable, without enmity and ill-will. He observes the Dharma according to the Dharma based on that state. If he abides in that state and attains the exhaustion of the taints, it is possible. If he abides in that state and does not attain the exhaustion of the taints, he may desire, love, delight in, and be at peace with that Dharma, and joyfully cut off the five lower fetters, be reborn there, and attain parinirvāṇa, attaining the non-retrogressive state, never returning to this world.

「复次，居士！多闻圣弟子心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游。如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。彼依此处，观法如法，彼依此处，观法如法，住彼得漏尽者，或有是处，若住彼，不得漏尽者，或因此法，欲法、爱法、乐法、靖法，爱乐欢喜，断五下分结尽，化生于彼而般涅槃，得不退法，终不还此。是谓如来、无所著、正尽觉成就慧眼，见第一义，说有一法，若圣弟子住漏尽无余，得心解脱。

Furthermore, householder, the well-learned noble disciple, having transcended all perceptions of form, and so on, up to the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception, dwells having accomplished it. They observe the Dharma according to the Dharma based on that state. If they abide in that state and attain the exhaustion of the taints, it is possible. If they abide in that state and do not attain the exhaustion of the taints, they may desire, love, delight in, and be at peace with that Dharma, and joyfully cut off the five lower fetters, be reborn there, and attain parinirvāṇa, attaining the non-retrogressive state, never returning to this world.

「复次，居士！多闻圣弟子度一切色想，乃至非有想非无想处成就游。彼于此处，观法如法，彼于此处，观法如法，住彼得漏尽者，或有是处。若住彼，不得漏尽者，或因此法，欲法、爱法、乐法、靖法，爱乐欢喜，断五下分结尽，化生于彼而般涅槃，得不退法，终不还此。是谓如来、无所著、正尽觉成就慧眼，见第一义，说有一法，若圣弟子住漏尽无余，得心解脱。」

Then the tenth householder of Aṭṭhaka rose from his seat, arranged his upper robe over one shoulder, and with palms together he said to Venerable Ānanda: "Venerable Ānanda, it is wonderful, it is marvelous! I asked Venerable Ānanda about one gateway to the deathless, but Venerable Ānanda has explained to me twelve gateways to the deathless. Now these twelve gateways to the deathless will certainly lead to safety and deliverance according to the basis. Venerable Ānanda, it is like a large house not far from a village, with twelve doors. If someone were to enter that house for a purpose, and another person were to come and not seek the welfare and benefit of that person, not seeking safety, and were to set fire to that house, Venerable Ānanda, that person would certainly be able to escape through any of these twelve doors, and find safety. In the same way, I asked Venerable Ānanda about one gateway to the deathless, but Venerable Ānanda has explained to me twelve gateways to the deathless. Now these twelve gateways to the deathless will certainly lead to safety and deliverance according to the basis. Venerable Ānanda, even in the teachings of the brahmins and contemplatives, where they speak of unwholesome teachings and practices, they still honor their teachers. How much more so should I not honor my great teacher, Venerable Ānanda?"

于是，第十居士八城即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手白曰：「尊者阿难！甚奇！甚特！我问尊者阿难一甘露门，而尊者阿难一时为我说于十二甘露法门，今此十二甘露法门必随所依，得安隐出。尊者阿难！犹去村不远，有大屋舍，开十二户，若人所为故，入彼屋中。复一人来，不为彼人求义及饶益，不求安隐而烧彼屋。尊者阿难！彼人必得于此十二户随所依出，得自安隐。如是我问尊者阿难一甘露门，而尊者阿难一时为我说于十二甘露法门，今此十二甘露法门必随所依，得安隐出。尊者阿难！梵志法、律中说不善法、律，尚供养师，况复我不供养大师尊者阿难耶？」

Then the tenth householder of Aṭṭhaka, during the night, prepared a pure, fine, abundant meal of food to eat and digest. Having prepared the meal, in the morning he set out seats and invited the Saṅgha of the Chicken Grove and the Saṅgha of Vesālī to gather in one place. He personally served water for washing, and then with a pure, fine, abundant meal of food to eat and digest, he personally served and satisfied them. After they had finished eating and put away the bowls, he served water for washing. Having finished, he offered five hundred items to buy a house and separately gave it to Venerable Ānanda. Venerable Ānanda accepted it and gave it to the Saṅgha of the four directions.

于是，第十居士八城即于夜中，施设极妙净美丰饶食噉含消，施设食已，平旦敷座，请鸡园众及鞞舍离众皆集一处，自行澡水，则以极妙净美丰饶食噉含消，手自斟酌，令得饱满，食讫收器，行澡水竟，持五百种物买屋别施尊者阿难。尊者阿难受已，施与招提僧。

Venerable Ānanda spoke thus. The tenth householder of Aṭṭhaka, having heard what Venerable Ānanda said, was delighted and remembered it well.

尊者阿难所说如是。第十居士八城闻尊者阿难所说，欢喜奉行。
