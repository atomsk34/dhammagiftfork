MĀ 22

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 3 舍梨子相应品

22. Perfecting the Precepts （二二）成就戒经

Parallel to: AN 5.166

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra said to the bhikṣus, "If a bhikṣu accomplishes morality, concentration, and wisdom, he will be able to enter and exit the sañjñāvedayitanirodha-samāpatti in this present life. There is such a place. If he does not attain ultimate knowledge in this present life, when his body perishes and his life ends, he will transcend the heaven of material food and be reborn in the heaven of other mind-made beings. There is such a place where he can enter and exit the sañjñāvedayitanirodha-samāpatti."

尔时，尊者舍梨子告诸比丘：「若比丘成就戒，成就定，成就慧者，便于现法出入想知灭定，必有此处；若于现法不得究竟智，身坏命终，过抟食天，生余意生天中，于彼出入想知灭定，必有此处。」

At that time, Venerable Udāyin was also in the assembly. Venerable Udāyin said, "Venerable Śāriputra! If a bhikṣu is reborn in the heaven of other mind-made beings and enters and exits the sañjñāvedayitanirodha-samāpatti, there is no such place."

是时，尊者乌陀夷共在众中，尊者乌陀夷白曰：「尊者舍梨子！若比丘生余意生天中，出入想知灭定者，终无此处。」

Venerable Śāriputra said to the bhikṣus for a second time, "If a bhikṣu accomplishes morality, concentration, and wisdom, he will be able to enter and exit the sañjñāvedayitanirodha-samāpatti in this present life. There is such a place. If he does not attain ultimate knowledge in this present life, when his body perishes and his life ends, he will transcend the heaven of material food and be reborn in the heaven of other mind-made beings. There is such a place where he can enter and exit the sañjñāvedayitanirodha-samāpatti."

尊者舍梨子再三告诸比丘：「若比丘成就戒，成就定，成就慧者，便于现法出入想知灭定，必有此处；若于现法不得究竟智，身坏命终，过抟食天，生余意生天中，于彼出入想知灭定，必有此处。」

Venerable Udāyin also said to Venerable Śāriputra for a second time, "Venerable Śāriputra! If a bhikṣu is reborn in the heaven of other mind-made beings and enters and exits the sañjñāvedayitanirodha-samāpatti, there is no such place."

尊者乌陀夷亦复再三白曰：「尊者舍梨子！若比丘生余意生天中，出入想知灭定者，终无此处。」

Then Venerable Śāriputra thought, "This bhikṣu has denied my words up to three times. Not a single bhikṣu has praised my words. I should go to the World-Honored One." Then Venerable Śāriputra went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side.

于是，尊者舍梨子便作是念：「此比丘乃至再三非我所说，无一比丘叹我所说，我宁可往至世尊所。」于是，尊者舍梨子往诣佛所，稽首作礼，却坐一面。

Not long after Venerable Śāriputra left, Venerable Udāyin and the other bhikṣus also went to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. Among them, Venerable Śāriputra again told the bhikṣus, "If a bhikṣu accomplishes morality, concentration, and wisdom, he can enter and exit the sañjñāvedayitanirodha-samāpatti in this very life. There is such a place. If in this very life he does not attain ultimate knowledge, when his body perishes and his life ends, he will transcend the heaven of material food and be reborn in the heaven of other mind-made beings. There he can enter and exit the sañjñāvedayitanirodha-samāpatti. There is such a place."

尊者舍梨子去后不久，尊者乌陀夷及诸比丘亦往诣佛所，稽首作礼，却坐一面。于中，尊者舍梨子复告诸比丘：「若比丘成就戒，成就定，成就慧者，便于现法出入想知灭定，必有此处；若于现法不得究竟智，身坏命终，过抟食天，生余意生天中，于彼出入想知灭定，必有此处。」

Venerable Udāyin again said, "Venerable Śāriputra! If a bhikṣu is reborn in the heaven of other mind-made beings and enters and exits the sañjñāvedayitanirodha-samāpatti, there is no such place."

尊者乌陀夷复白曰：「尊者舍梨子！若比丘生余意生天中，出入想知灭定者，终无此处。」

Venerable Śāriputra again told the bhikṣus three times, "If a bhikṣu accomplishes morality, concentration, and wisdom, he can enter and exit the sañjñāvedayitanirodha-samāpatti in this very life. There is such a place. If in this very life he does not attain ultimate knowledge, when his body perishes and his life ends, he will transcend the heaven of material food and be reborn in the heaven of other mind-made beings. There he can enter and exit the sañjñāvedayitanirodha-samāpatti. There is such a place."

尊者舍梨子复再三告诸比丘：「若比丘成就戒，成就定，成就慧者，便于现法出入想知灭定，必有此处；若于现法不得究竟智，身坏命终，过抟食天，生余意生天中，于彼出入想知灭定，必有此处。」

Venerable Udāyin also said three times, "Venerable Śāriputra! If a bhikṣu is reborn in the heaven of other mind-made beings and enters and exits the sañjñāvedayitanirodha-samāpatti, there is no such place."

尊者乌陀夷亦复再三白曰：「尊者舍梨子！若比丘生余意生天中，出入想知灭定者，终无此处。」

Venerable Śāriputra then thought, "This bhikṣu has denied my words three times in the presence of the World-Honored One, and no bhikṣu has praised my words. I should remain silent."

尊者舍梨子复作是念：「此比丘于世尊前再三非我所说，亦无一比丘叹我所说，我宜默然。」

Then the World-Honored One asked, "Udāyin! Do you say that the mind-made heaven is form?"

于是，世尊问曰：「乌陀夷！汝说意生天为是色耶？」

Venerable Udāyin replied to the World-Honored One, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

尊者乌陀夷白世尊曰：「是也。世尊！」

The World-Honored One rebuked Udāyin, saying, "You foolish man! Blind and without eyes, how can you discuss the profound Abhidharma?" Thereupon, Venerable Udāyin, having been rebuked by the Buddha, was inwardly distressed, lowered his head in silence, lost his eloquence, and was speechless, as if in deep thought.

世尊面诃乌陀夷曰：「汝愚痴人！盲无有目，以何等故，论甚深阿毘昙？」于是，尊者乌陀夷为佛面诃已，内怀忧戚，低头默然，失辩无言，如有所思。

Having rebuked Venerable Udāyin, the World-Honored One said to Venerable Ānanda, "When an elder bhikṣu of great virtue is questioned, why do you let it go unchecked? You foolish man! Without compassion, you abandon an elder bhikṣu of great virtue."

世尊面诃尊者乌陀夷已，语尊者阿难曰：「上尊名德长老比丘为他所诘，汝何以故，纵而不捡？汝愚痴人！无有慈心，舍背上尊名德长老。」

Thereupon, the World-Honored One, having rebuked Venerable Udāyin and Venerable Ānanda, addressed the bhikṣus, "If a bhikṣu accomplishes morality, concentration, and wisdom, he will be able to enter and exit the cessation of perception and feeling in this very life. There is such a place. If he does not attain ultimate knowledge in this very life, when his body perishes and his life ends, he will transcend the heaven of material food and be reborn in the heaven of other mind-made beings. There is such a place where he can enter and exit the cessation of perception and feeling." The Buddha spoke thus and then entered the meditation chamber, sitting in silence.

于是，世尊面诃尊者乌陀夷及尊者阿难已，告诸比丘：「若比丘成就戒，成就定，成就慧者，便于现法出入想知灭定，必有此处；若于现法不得究竟智，身坏命终，过抟食天，生余意生天中，于彼出入想知灭定，必有此处。」佛说如是，即入禅室，宴坐默然。

At that time, Venerable Pañcakaṇikā was in the assembly. Venerable Ānanda said to Venerable Pañcakaṇikā, "He did it, but I am blamed. Venerable Pañcakaṇikā! The World-Honored One will surely come out of the meditation chamber in the afternoon, go before the assembly of bhikṣus, spread his seat, and sit down to discuss this matter. Venerable Pañcakaṇikā should answer this matter. I am extremely ashamed before the World-Honored One and the virtuous ones.

尔时，尊者白净比丘在于众中，尊者阿难白尊者白净：「是他所作，而我得责。尊者白净！世尊晡时必从禅室出，至比丘众前，敷座而坐，共论此义。尊者白净应答此事，我极惭愧于世尊所及诸梵行。」

Then, the World-Honored One came out of the meditation chamber in the afternoon, went before the assembly of bhikṣus, spread his seat, and sat down. He said, "Pañcakaṇikā! What are the few qualities that the elder bhikṣus are loved, respected, and revered by the virtuous ones? "

于是，世尊则于晡时从禅室出，至比丘众前，敷座而坐，告曰：「白净！长老比丘为有几法，为诸梵行者爱敬尊重？」

Venerable Pañcakaṇikā said, "World-Honored One! If an elder bhikṣu has five qualities, he is loved, respected, and revered by the virtuous ones. What are the five? World-Honored One! An elder bhikṣu practices the precepts, guards the precepts, and is well-disciplined in deportment and etiquette. Seeing even the slightest fault, he is always fearful and upholds the precepts. World-Honored One! An elder bhikṣu who upholds the precepts is loved, respected, and revered by the virtuous ones. Furthermore, World-Honored One! An elder bhikṣu extensively studies and memorizes, guarding and not forgetting, accumulating extensive learning. The teachings he upholds are good in the beginning, good in the middle, and good in the end, with meaning and expression, complete and pure, manifesting the holy life. He extensively studies and memorizes such teachings, mastering them a thousand times, contemplating them with the mind, clearly seeing and deeply understanding. World-Honored One! An elder bhikṣu who is learned is loved, respected, and revered by the virtuous ones. Furthermore, World-Honored One! An elder bhikṣu attains the four immeasurably vast states of mind, dwelling in the present with ease, not difficult to attain. World-Honored One! An elder bhikṣu who practices meditation is loved, respected, and revered by the virtuous ones. Furthermore, World-Honored One! An elder bhikṣu practices wisdom, observing the arising and ceasing of phenomena, attaining such wisdom, with the illumination of holy wisdom, discerning and understanding, with right knowledge to completely end suffering. World-Honored One! An elder bhikṣu who is wise is loved, respected, and revered by the virtuous ones. Furthermore, World-Honored One! An elder bhikṣu has exhausted all taints, has no further bonds, is liberated in mind and wisdom, and in this very life, knows and realizes for himself, attains accomplishment, and abides in it. Birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and there will be no more continuation of existence, knowing it as it really is. World-Honored One! An elder bhikṣu who has exhausted the taints is loved, respected, and revered by the virtuous ones. World-Honored One! If an elder bhikṣu accomplishes these five qualities, he is loved, respected, and revered by the virtuous ones."

尊者白净白曰：「世尊！长老比丘若有五法，为诸梵行者爱敬尊重。云何为五？世尊！长老比丘修习禁戒，守护从解脱，又复善摄威仪礼节，见纤芥] cf.［丽】罪，常怀畏怖，受持学戒。世尊！禁戒长老上尊比丘，为诸梵行者爱敬尊重。复次，世尊！长老比丘广学多闻，守持不忘，积聚博闻，所谓法者，初善、中善、竟亦善，有义有文，具足清净，显现梵行，如是诸法广学多闻，玩习至千，意所惟观，明见深达。世尊！多闻长老上尊比丘，为诸梵行者爱敬尊重。复次，世尊！长老比丘得四增上心，现法乐居，易不难得。世尊！禅伺长老上尊比丘，为诸梵行者爱敬尊重。复次，世尊！长老比丘修行智慧，观兴衰法，得如是智，圣慧明达，分别晓了，以正尽苦。世尊！智慧长老上尊比丘，为诸梵行者爱敬尊重。复次，世尊！长老比丘诸漏已尽，无复有结，心解脱、慧解脱，于现法中自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。世尊！漏尽长老上尊比丘，为诸梵行者爱敬尊重。世尊！长老比丘若成就此五法，为诸梵行者爱敬尊重。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Pañcakaṇikā! If an elder bhikṣu does not have these five qualities, what other reasons would make the virtuous ones love, respect, and revere him?" Venerable Pañcakaṇikā said, "World-Honored One! If an elder bhikṣu does not have these five qualities, there is no other reason for the virtuous ones to love, respect, and revere him, except for his old age, gray hair, and falling teeth, his prime gradually declining, his bent body and crooked legs, his heavy body and wheezing breath, leaning on a cane to walk, his skin sagging and wrinkled like hemp seeds, his faculties deteriorating and his complexion ugly. They would love, respect, and revere him for these reasons."

世尊问曰：「白净！若长老比丘无此五法，当以何义使诸梵行者爱敬尊重？」尊者白净白曰：「世尊！若长老比丘无此五法者，更无余事使诸梵行爱敬尊重，唯以老耄、头白、齿落，盛壮日衰，身曲脚戾，体重气上，柱杖而行，肌缩皮缓，皱如麻子，诸根毁熟，颜色丑恶，彼因此故，使诸梵行爱敬尊重。」

The World-Honored One said, "So it is! So it is! If an elder bhikṣu does not have these five qualities, there is no other reason for the virtuous ones to love, respect, and revere him, except for his old age, gray hair, and falling teeth, his prime gradually declining, his bent body and crooked legs, his heavy body and wheezing breath, leaning on a cane to walk, his skin sagging and wrinkled like hemp seeds, his faculties deteriorating and his complexion ugly. They would love, respect, and revere him for these reasons. Pañcakaṇikā! Śāriputra bhikṣu has these five qualities. You should love, respect, and revere him. Why is that? Pañcakaṇikā! Śāriputra bhikṣu practices the precepts, guards the precepts, and is well-disciplined in deportment and etiquette. Seeing even the slightest fault, he is always fearful and upholds the precepts. Furthermore, Pañcakaṇikā! Śāriputra bhikṣu extensively studies and memorizes, guarding and not forgetting, accumulating extensive learning. The teachings he upholds are good in the beginning, good in the middle, and good in the end, with meaning and expression, complete and pure, manifesting the holy life. He extensively studies and memorizes such teachings, mastering them a thousand times, contemplating them with the mind, clearly seeing and deeply understanding. Furthermore, Pañcakaṇikā! Śāriputra bhikṣu attains the four immeasurably vast states of mind, dwelling in the present with ease, not difficult to attain. Furthermore, Pañcakaṇikā! Śāriputra bhikṣu practices wisdom, observing the arising and ceasing of phenomena, attaining such wisdom, with the illumination of holy wisdom, discerning and understanding, with right knowledge to completely end suffering. Furthermore, Pañcakaṇikā! Śāriputra bhikṣu has exhausted all taints, has no further bonds, is liberated in mind and wisdom, and in this very life, knows and realizes for himself, attains accomplishment, and abides in it. Birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and there will be no more continuation of existence, knowing it as it really is. Pañcakaṇikā! Śāriputra bhikṣu has accomplished these five qualities. You should love, respect, and revere him."

世尊告曰：「如是如是。若长老比丘无此五法，更无余事使诸梵行爱敬尊重，唯以老耄、头白、齿落，盛壮日衰，身曲脚戾，体重气上，柱杖而行，肌缩皮缓，皱如麻子，诸根毁熟，颜色丑恶，彼因此故，使诸梵行爱敬尊重。白净！舍梨子比丘有此五法，汝等应当爱敬尊重。所以者何？白净！舍梨子比丘修习禁戒，守护从解脱，又复善摄威仪礼节，见纤芥罪，常怀畏怖，受持学戒。复次，白净！舍梨子比丘广学多闻，守持不忘，积聚博闻，所谓法者，初善、中善、竟亦善，有义有文，具足清净，显现梵行，如是诸法，广学多闻，玩习至千，意所惟观，明见深达。复次，白净！舍梨子比丘得四增上心，现法乐居，易不难得。复次，白净！舍梨子比丘修行智慧，观兴衰法，得如是智，圣慧明达，分别晓了，以正尽苦。复次，白净！舍梨子比丘诸漏已尽，无复有结，心解脱、慧解脱，于现法中自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。白净！舍梨子比丘成就此五法，汝等应共爱敬尊重。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Venerable Pañcakaṇikā and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说如是。尊者白净及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
