MĀ 220

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 18 例品

220. （二二〇）见经

Parallel to: AN 7.54

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, not long after the Buddha's parinirvāṇa, Venerable Ānanda was wandering in Rājagṛha, staying in the Bamboo Grove at Kalandakanivāpa.

一时，佛般涅槃后不久，尊者阿难游王舍城，在竹林迦兰哆园。

Then a certain non-Buddhist brāhman ascetic, who was a friend of Venerable Ānanda before he left home, wandered around in the middle of the day and went to where Venerable Ānanda was. They greeted each other and sat down to one side. He said to Venerable Ānanda, "I wish to ask something. May I ask?"

于是，有一异学梵志，是尊者阿难未出家时友，中后彷徉，往诣尊者阿难所，共相问讯，却坐一面，语尊者阿难：「欲有所问，听我问耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "Brāhman! Ask what you wish. I will think about it after hearing it."

尊者阿难答曰：「梵志！欲问便问，我闻已当思。」

The non-Buddhist brāhman ascetic then asked, "What is meant by setting aside and removing these views, not fully explaining them, namely, the world is eternal, the world is not eternal, the world has an end, the world has no end, life is the body, life is different from the body, the Tathāgata perishes, the Tathāgata does not perish, the Tathāgata both perishes and does not perish, and the Tathāgata neither perishes nor does not perish? Does the śramaṇa Gautama know these views as they should be known?"

异学梵志即便问曰：「所谓此见舍置除却，不尽通说，谓世有常，世无有常，世有底，世无底，命即是身，为命异身异，如来终，如来不终，如来终不终，如来亦非终亦非不终耶？沙门瞿昙知此诸见如应知耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "Brāhman! What is meant by the World-Honored One, Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One setting aside and removing these views, not fully explaining them, namely, the world is eternal, the world is not eternal, the world has an end, the world has no end, life is the body, life is different from the body, the Tathāgata perishes, the Tathāgata does not perish, the Tathāgata both perishes and does not perish, and the Tathāgata neither perishes nor does not perish? The World-Honored One, Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One knows these views as they should be known."

尊者阿难答曰：「梵志！所谓此见，世尊、如来、无所著、正尽觉舍置除却，不尽通说，谓世有常，世无有常，世有底，世无底，命即是身，为命异身异，如来终，如来不终，如来终不终，如来亦非终亦非不终耶？世尊、如来、无所著、正尽觉知此诸见如应也。」

The non-Buddhist brāhman ascetic then asked again, "What is meant by the śramaṇa Gautama setting aside and removing these views, not fully explaining them, namely, the world is eternal, the world is not eternal, the world has an end, the world has no end, life is the body, life is different from the body, the Tathāgata perishes, the Tathāgata does not perish, the Tathāgata both perishes and does not perish, and the Tathāgata neither perishes nor does not perish? How does the śramaṇa Gautama know these views as they should be known?"

异学梵志又复问曰：「所谓此见，沙门瞿昙舍置除却，不尽通说，谓世有常，世无有常，世有底，世无底，命即是身，为命异身异，如来终，如来不终，如来终不终，如来亦非终亦非不终耶？沙门瞿昙云何知此诸见如应耶？」

Venerable Ānanda replied, "Brāhman! What is meant by the World-Honored One, Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One setting aside and removing these views, not fully explaining them, namely, the world is eternal, the world is not eternal, the world has an end, the world has no end, life is the body, life is different from the body, the Tathāgata perishes, the Tathāgata does not perish, the Tathāgata both perishes and does not perish, and the Tathāgata neither perishes nor does not perish? The non-Buddhist brāhman ascetic knows this as it is, accepts it as it is, takes it as it is, is born as it is, and attains the afterlife as it is. What is meant by the World-Honored One, Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One setting aside and removing these views, not fully explaining them, namely, the world is eternal, the world is not eternal, the world has an end, the world has no end, life is the body, life is different from the body, the Tathāgata perishes, the Tathāgata does not perish, the Tathāgata both perishes and does not perish, and the Tathāgata neither perishes nor does not perish? Knowing these views in this way, these views should be known in this way."

尊者阿难答曰：「梵志！所谓此见，世尊、如来、无所著、正尽觉舍置除却，不尽通说，谓世有常，世无有常，世有底，世无底，命即是身，为命异身异，如来终，如来不终，如来终不终，如来亦非终亦非不终耶？异学梵志如是具、如是受、如是趣、如是生、如是至后世，所谓此是世尊、如来、无所著、正尽觉舍置除却，不尽通说，谓世有常，世无有常，世有底，世无底，命即是身，为命异身异，如来终，如来不终，如来终不终，如来亦非终亦非不终耶？如是知此诸见，此诸见应如是知。」

The non-Buddhist brāhman ascetic said, "I now take refuge in Ānanda."

异学梵志白曰：「我今自归于阿难。」

Venerable Ānanda said, "Brāhman! Do not take refuge in me, but take refuge in the Buddha as I do."

尊者阿难告曰：「梵志！汝莫自归于我，如我自归于佛，汝亦应自归。」

The non-Buddhist brāhman ascetic said, "Ānanda! I now take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha of bhikṣus. May the World-Honored One accept me as a lay devotee. From this day forth, I take refuge for life, until the end of my life."

异学梵志白曰：「阿难！我今自归于佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。」

Venerable Ānanda spoke thus. The non-Buddhist brāhman ascetic, having heard what Venerable Ānanda said, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

尊者阿难所说如是。彼异学梵志闻尊者阿难所说，欢喜奉行。
