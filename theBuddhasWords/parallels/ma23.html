MĀ 23

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 3 舍梨子相应品

23. Wisdom （二三）智经

Parallel to: SN 12.32

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the monk Maudgalyāyana renounced the precepts and left the monastic life. The monk Kāśyapa heard that Maudgalyāyana had renounced the precepts and left the monastic life. He then went to Venerable Śāriputra, bowed at his feet, sat to one side, and said, "Venerable Śāriputra, you should know that Maudgalyāyana has renounced the precepts and left the monastic life."

尔时，牟利破群［少／兔］比丘舍戒罢道，黑齿比丘闻牟利破群［少／兔］比丘舍戒罢道，即诣尊者舍梨子所，稽首礼足，却坐一面，坐已，白曰：「尊者舍梨子！当知牟利破群［少／兔］比丘舍戒罢道。」

Venerable Śāriputra said, "Did Maudgalyāyana take delight in this teaching?"

尊者舍梨子曰：「牟利破群［少／兔］比丘于此法中而爱乐耶？」

The monk Kāśyapa asked, "Venerable Śāriputra, did you take delight in this teaching?"

黑齿比丘问曰：「尊者舍梨子！于此法中而爱乐耶？」

Venerable Śāriputra replied, "Kāśyapa, I have no doubts about this teaching."

尊者舍梨子答曰：「黑齿！我于此法无有疑惑。」

The monk Kāśyapa then asked again, "Venerable Śāriputra, what about the future?"

黑齿比丘即复问曰：「尊者舍梨子！于当来事复云何耶？」

Venerable Śāriputra replied, "Kāśyapa, I have no doubts about the future either."

尊者舍梨子答曰：「黑齿！我于来事亦无犹预。」

Having heard this, the monk Kāśyapa rose from his seat, went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said, "World-Honored One, Venerable Śāriputra now declares that he has attained knowledge, that his births are exhausted, that the holy life has been established, that what was to be done has been accomplished, that he will not again experience rebirth, and that he knows this to be true."

黑齿比丘闻如是已，即从坐起，往诣佛所，稽首作礼，却坐一面，白曰：「世尊！尊者舍梨子今自称说得智，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。」

Having heard this, the World-Honored One told a monk, "Go to Śāriputra and say, 'Śāriputra, the World-Honored One calls you.'"

世尊闻已，告一比丘：「汝往舍梨子所，语：『舍梨子！世尊呼汝。』」

The monk, having received the instruction, rose from his seat, bowed to the Buddha, and left. He went to where Venerable Śāriputra was and said, "The World-Honored One calls Venerable Śāriputra." Having heard this, Venerable Śāriputra went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side.

一比丘受教已，即从坐起，礼佛而去，往诣尊者舍梨子所，白曰：「世尊呼尊者舍梨子。」尊者舍梨子闻已，即往诣佛，稽首作礼，却坐一面。

The World-Honored One asked, "Śāriputra! Do you truly claim to have attained knowledge, that birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, the task is accomplished, no more to be reborn, and you know it as it really is?"

世尊问曰：「舍梨子！汝今实自称说得智，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真耶？」

Venerable Śāriputra said, "World-Honored One! It is not by this text, not by this phrase, but I only speak of the meaning."

尊者舍梨子白曰：「世尊！不以此文，不以此句，我但说义。」

The World-Honored One said, "Śāriputra! A clansman speaks according to his means, and one who has attained knowledge speaks of having attained knowledge."

世尊告曰：「舍梨子！族姓子随其方便称说，得智者即说得智。」

Venerable Śāriputra said, "World-Honored One! I have already said that it is not by this text, not by this phrase, but I only speak of the meaning."

尊者舍梨子白曰：「世尊！我向已说，不以此文，不以此句，我但说义。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Śāriputra! If the various virtuous ones come and ask you, 'Venerable Śāriputra! How do you know, how do you see, that you can claim to have attained knowledge, that birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and that there will be no more rebirth, knowing it as it really is?' Śāriputra! Having heard this, how would you answer?"

世尊问曰：「舍梨子！若诸梵行来问汝言：『尊者舍梨子！云何知、云何见，自称说得智，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真耶？』舍梨子！汝闻此已当云何答？」

Venerable Śāriputra said, "World-Honored One! If the various virtuous ones come and ask me, 'Venerable Śāriputra! How do you know, how do you see, that you can claim to have attained knowledge, that birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and that there will be no more rebirth, knowing it as it really is?' World-Honored One! Having heard this, I would answer like this: 'Virtuous ones! Birth has a cause, this cause of birth is exhausted. Having known the exhaustion of the cause of birth, I myself claim to have attained knowledge, that birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and that there will be no more rebirth, knowing it as it really is.' World-Honored One! If the various virtuous ones come and ask like this, I would answer like this."

尊者舍梨子白曰：「世尊！若诸梵行来问我言：『尊者舍梨子！云何知、云何见，自称说得智，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真？』世尊！我闻此已，当如是答：『诸贤！生者有因，此生因尽，知生因尽已，我自称说得智，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。』世尊！若诸梵行来问如此，我当如是答。」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Śāriputra! If the various virtuous ones come and ask like this, you should answer like this. Why is that? Because if one speaks like this, one should know the meaning of it."

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！舍梨子！若诸梵行来问如此，汝应如是答。所以者何？如此说者，当知是义。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Śāriputra! If the various virtuous ones come and ask you, 'Venerable Śāriputra! What is the cause and condition of birth? From what is birth born? What is its basis?' Having heard this, how would you answer?"

世尊问曰：「舍梨子！若诸梵行来问汝言：『尊者舍梨子！生者何因何缘，为从何生，以何为本？』汝闻此已，当云何答？」

Venerable Śāriputra said, "World-Honored One! If the various virtuous ones come and ask me, 'Venerable Śāriputra! What is the cause and condition of birth? From what is birth born? What is its basis?' World-Honored One! Having heard this, I would answer like this: 'Virtuous ones! Birth is caused by existence, conditioned by existence, born from existence, and has existence as its basis.' World-Honored One! If the various virtuous ones come and ask like this, I would answer like this."

尊者舍梨子白曰：「世尊！若诸梵行来问我言：『尊者舍梨子！生者何因何缘，为从何生，以何为本？』世尊！我闻此已，当如是答：『诸贤！生者因有缘有，从有而生，以有为本。』世尊！若诸梵行来问如此，我当如是答。」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Śāriputra! If the various virtuous ones come and ask like this, you should answer like this. Why is that? Because if one explains like this, one should know the meaning."

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！舍梨子！若诸梵行来问如此，汝应如是答。所以者何？如此说者，当知是义。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Śāriputra! If the various virtuous ones come and ask you, 'Venerable Śāriputra! What is the cause and condition of existence? From what is existence born? What is its basis?' Having heard this, how would you answer?"

世尊问曰：「舍梨子！若诸梵行来问汝言：『尊者舍梨子！有者何因何缘，为从何生，以何为本？』汝闻此已，当云何答？」

Venerable Śāriputra said, "World-Honored One! If the various virtuous ones come and ask me, 'Venerable Śāriputra! What is the cause and condition of existence? From what is existence born? What is its basis?' World-Honored One! Having heard this, I would answer like this: 'Virtuous ones! Existence is caused by feeling, conditioned by feeling, born from feeling, and has feeling as its basis.' World-Honored One! If the various virtuous ones come and ask like this, I would answer like this."

尊者舍梨子白曰：「世尊！若诸梵行来问我言：『尊者舍梨子！有者何因何缘，为从何生，以何为本？』世尊！我闻此已，当如是答：『诸贤！有者因受、缘受，从受而生，以受为本。』世尊！若诸梵行来问如此，我当如是答。」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Śāriputra! If the various virtuous ones come and ask like this, you should answer like this. Why is that? Because if you speak like this, you will understand the meaning."

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！舍梨子！若诸梵行来问如此，应如是答。所以者何？如此说者，当知是义。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Śāriputra! If the various virtuous ones come and ask you, 'Venerable Śāriputra! What is the cause and condition of feeling? From what is feeling born? What is its basis?' Having heard this, how would you answer?"

世尊问曰：「舍梨子！若诸梵行来问汝言：『尊者舍梨子！受者何因何缘，为从何生，以何为本？』汝闻此已，当云何答？」

Venerable Śāriputra said, "World-Honored One! If the various virtuous ones come and ask me, 'Venerable Śāriputra! What is the cause and condition of feeling? From what is feeling born? What is its basis?' World-Honored One! Having heard this, I would answer like this: 'Virtuous ones! Feeling is caused by craving, conditioned by craving, born from craving, and has craving as its basis.' World-Honored One! If the various virtuous ones come and ask like this, I would answer like this."

尊者舍梨子白曰：「世尊！若诸梵行来问我言：『尊者舍梨子！受者何因何缘，为从何生，以何为本？』世尊！我闻此已，当如是答：『诸贤！受者因爱、缘爱，从爱而生，以爱为本。』世尊！若诸梵行来问如此，我当如是答。」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Śāriputra! If the various virtuous ones come and ask like this, you should answer like this. Why is that? Because if one explains like this, one understands the meaning."

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！舍梨子！若诸梵行来问如此，汝应如是答。所以者何？如此说者，当知是义。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Śāriputra! If the various virtuous ones come and ask you, 'Venerable Śāriputra! What is craving?' Having heard this, how would you answer?"

世尊问曰：「舍梨子！若诸梵行来问汝言：『尊者舍梨子！云何为爱？』汝闻此已，当云何答？」

Venerable Śāriputra said, "World-Honored One! If the various virtuous ones come and ask me, 'Venerable Śāriputra! What is craving?' World-Honored One! Having heard this, I would answer like this: 'Virtuous ones! There are three feelings: pleasant feeling, painful feeling, and neither painful nor pleasant feeling. Desiring what is pleasant among them is called craving.' World-Honored One! If the various virtuous ones come and ask like this, I would answer like this."

尊者舍梨子白曰：「世尊！若诸梵行来问我言：『尊者舍梨子！云何为爱？』世尊！我闻此已，当如是答：『诸贤！谓有三觉，乐觉、苦觉、不苦不乐觉，于中乐欲著者，是谓为爱。』世尊！若诸梵行来问如此，我当如是答。」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Śāriputra! If the various virtuous ones come and ask like this, you should answer like this. Why is that? Because if one explains like this, one should know the meaning."

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！舍梨子！若诸梵行来问如此，汝应如是答。所以者何？如此说者，当知是义。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Śāriputra! If the various virtuous ones come and ask you, 'Venerable Śāriputra! How does one know, how does one see, so as to have no desire and attachment to the three feelings?' Having heard this, how would you answer?"

世尊问曰：「舍梨子！若诸梵行来问汝言：『尊者舍梨子！云何知、云何见，于三觉中无乐欲著？』汝闻此已，当云何答？」

Venerable Śāriputra said, "World-Honored One! If the various virtuous ones come and ask me, 'Venerable Śāriputra! How does one know, how does one see, so as to have no desire and attachment to the three feelings?' World-Honored One! Having heard this, I would answer like this: 'Virtuous ones! It is because these three feelings are impermanent phenomena, suffering phenomena, and ceasing phenomena. Impermanent phenomena are suffering. Having seen suffering, one then has no desire and attachment to the three feelings.' World-Honored One! If the various virtuous ones come and ask like this, I would answer like this."

尊者舍梨子白曰：「世尊！若诸梵行来问我言：『尊者舍梨子！云何知、云何见，于三觉中无乐欲著？』世尊！我闻此已，当如是答：『诸贤！谓此三觉无常法、苦法、灭法，无常法即是苦，见苦已，便于三觉无乐欲著。』世尊！若诸梵行来问如此，我当如是答。」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Śāriputra! If the various virtuous ones come and ask like this, you should answer like this. Why is that? Because if one explains like this, one should know the meaning."

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！舍梨子！若诸梵行来问如此，汝应如是答。所以者何？如此说者，当知是义。」

At that time, the World-Honored One said, "Śāriputra! This explanation can also be answered briefly. Śāriputra! What is the meaning that this explanation can be answered briefly? All that is felt and experienced is suffering. Śāriputra! This is called another meaning that this explanation can be answered briefly."

尔时，世尊告曰：「舍梨子！此说复有义，可得略答。舍梨子！复有何义，此说可得略答？所觉所为，即皆是苦。舍梨子！是谓复有义，此说可得略答。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Śāriputra! If the various virtuous ones come and ask you, 'Venerable Śāriputra! How do you claim to have attained knowledge by turning away from and not facing it, knowing that birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been accomplished, and one will not enter into any future existence again?'"

世尊问曰：「舍梨子！若诸梵行来问汝言：『尊者舍梨子！云何背不向自称说得智，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真？』」

Venerable Śāriputra said, "World-Honored One! If the various virtuous ones come and ask me, 'Venerable Śāriputra! How do you claim to have attained knowledge by turning away from and not facing it, knowing that birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been accomplished, and one will not enter into any future existence again?' World-Honored One! Having heard this, I would answer like this: 'Virtuous ones! I myself have turned away from and not faced it within, and all craving is exhausted. Without alarm, fear, doubt, or confusion, I practice such protection. Having practiced such protection, I do not give rise to unwholesome taints.' World-Honored One! If the various virtuous ones come and ask like this, I would answer like this."

尊者舍梨子白曰：「世尊！若诸梵行来问我言：『尊者舍梨子！云何背不向自称说得智，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真？』世尊！我闻此已，当如是答：『诸贤！我自于内背而不向则诸爱尽，无惊无怖，无疑无惑，行如是守护，如其守护已，不生不善漏。』世尊！若诸梵行来问如此，我当如是答。」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Śāriputra! If the various virtuous ones come and ask like this, you should answer like this. Why is that? Because if one explains like this, one should know the meaning."

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！舍梨子！若诸梵行来问如此，汝应如是答。所以者何？如此说者，当知是义。」

The World-Honored One said, "Śāriputra! Furthermore, there is a meaning to this that can be answered briefly. If the śramaṇas speak of the fetters, those fetters are not mine. Practicing such protection, having practiced such protection, unwholesome taints do not arise. Śāriputra! This is called another meaning to this that can be answered briefly." Having spoken thus, the World-Honored One rose from his seat, entered the room, and sat in meditation.

世尊告曰：「舍梨子！复次有义，此说可得略答。若诸结沙门所说，彼结非我有，行如是守护，如其守护已，不生不善漏。舍梨子！是谓复有义，此说可得略答。」世尊说如是已，即从坐起，入室燕坐。

Not long after the World-Honored One entered the room, Venerable Śāriputra told the bhikṣus, "Venerable friends! I had not yet considered it, but the World-Honored One suddenly asked about this meaning. I thought, 'I fear I cannot answer.' Venerable friends! When I first explained one meaning, it was praised and approved by the World-Honored One. I further thought, 'If the World-Honored One were to ask me about this meaning with different words and phrases for one day and night, I would be able to answer the World-Honored One with different words and phrases for one day and night. If the World-Honored One were to ask me about this meaning with different words and phrases for two, three, four, up to seven days and nights, I would also be able to answer the World-Honored One with different words and phrases for two, three, four, up to seven days and nights.'"

世尊入室不久，尊者舍梨子告诸比丘：「诸贤！我始未作意，而世尊卒问此义，我作是念：『恐不能答。』诸贤！我初说一义，便为世尊之所赞可，我复作是念：『若世尊一日一夜，以异文异句问我此义者，我能为世尊一日一夜，以异文异句而答此义；若世尊二、三、四，至七日七夜，以异文异句问我此义者，我亦能为世尊二、三、四，至七日七夜，以异文异句而答此义。』」

The bhikṣu Black Teeth, having heard what Venerable Śāriputra had said, immediately rose from his seat, quickly went to where the Buddha was, and said to the World-Honored One, "Not long after the World-Honored One entered the room, what Venerable Śāriputra said was extremely lofty, a lion's roar in one direction: 'Venerable friends! I had not yet considered it, but the World-Honored One suddenly asked about this meaning. I thought, "I fear I cannot answer." Venerable friends! When I first explained one meaning, it was praised and approved by the World-Honored One. I further thought, "If the World-Honored One were to ask me about this meaning with different words and phrases for one day and night, I would be able to answer the World-Honored One with different words and phrases for one day and night. Venerable friends! If the World-Honored One were to ask me about this meaning with different words and phrases for two, three, four, up to seven days and nights, I would also be able to answer the World-Honored One with different words and phrases for two, three, four, up to seven days and nights."'

黑齿比丘闻尊者舍梨子说如是已，即从坐起，疾诣佛所，白世尊曰：「世尊入室不久，尊者舍梨子所说至高，一向师子吼：『诸贤！我始未作意，而世尊卒问此义，我作是念：「恐不能答。」诸贤！我初说一义，便为世尊之所赞可，我复作是念：「若世尊一日一夜，以异文异句问我此义者，我能为世尊一日一夜，以异文异句而答此义。诸贤！若世尊二、三、四，至七日七夜，以异文异句问我此义者，我亦能为世尊二、三、四，至七日七夜，以异文异句而答此义。」』」

The World-Honored One said, "Black Teeth! It is so, it is so. If I were to ask bhikṣu Śāriputra about this meaning with different words and phrases for one day and night, bhikṣu Śāriputra would definitely be able to answer me with different words and phrases for one day and night. Black Teeth! If I were to ask bhikṣu Śāriputra about this meaning with different words and phrases for two, three, four, up to seven days and nights, bhikṣu Śāriputra would also be able to answer me with different words and phrases for two, three, four, up to seven days and nights. Why is that? Black Teeth! Because bhikṣu Śāriputra has profound understanding of the Dharma realm."

世尊告曰：「黑齿！如是，如是。若我一日一夜，以异文异句问舍梨子比丘此义者，舍梨子比丘必能为我一日一夜，以异文异句而答此义。黑齿！若我二、三、四，至七日七夜，以异文异句问舍梨子比丘此义者，舍梨子比丘亦能为我二、三、四，至七日七夜，以异文异句而答此义。所以者何？黑齿！舍梨子比丘深达法界故。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Venerable Śāriputra and the bhikṣus, having heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced and upheld it.

佛说如是。尊者舍梨子及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
