MĀ 24

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 3 舍梨子相应品

24. The “Lion’s Roar” of Sāriputta （二四）师子吼经

Parallel to: AN 9.11 / EĀ 37.6

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One was together with a great assembly of bhikṣus, spending the summer retreat in the country of Śrāvastī. Venerable Śāriputra was also spending the summer retreat in the country of Śrāvastī. Then, after Venerable Śāriputra had completed the summer retreat in the country of Śrāvastī, after three months had passed, having repaired his robes, he put on his robe, took his bowl, went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said: "World-Honored One! I have completed the summer retreat in the country of Śrāvastī. World-Honored One! I wish to travel and wander among the people.

尔时，世尊与大比丘众俱，于舍卫国而受夏坐，尊者舍梨子亦游舍卫国而受夏坐。于是，尊者舍梨子舍卫国受夏坐讫，过三月已，补治衣竟，摄衣持钵，往诣佛所，稽首礼足，却坐一面，白曰：「世尊！我于舍卫国受夏坐讫。世尊！我欲游行人间。」

The World-Honored One said: "Śāriputra! Go, as you wish. Those who are not liberated should be liberated, those who are not freed should be freed, those who have not attained parinirvāṇa should attain parinirvāṇa. Śāriputra! Go, as you wish.

世尊告曰：「舍梨子！汝去，随所欲，诸未度者当令得度，诸未脱者当令得脱，诸未般涅槃者令得般涅槃。舍梨子！汝去，随所欲。」

Then Venerable Śāriputra, having heard the Buddha's words, received them well and upheld them well. He immediately rose from his seat, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and departed. He returned to his own room, put away his bed and seat, put on his robe, took his bowl, and immediately went out to wander among the people.

于是，尊者舍梨子闻佛所说，善受善持，即从坐起，稽首佛足，绕三匝而去，还至己房，收举床座，摄衣持钵，即便出去游行人间。

Not long after Venerable Śāriputra left, a certain practitioner committed an offense in the presence of the Buddha and said to the World-Honored One: "Today Venerable Śāriputra has slighted me and is wandering among the people.

尊者舍梨子去后不久，有一梵行在于佛前犯相违法，白世尊曰：「今日尊者舍梨子轻慢我已，游行人间。」

Having heard this, the World-Honored One told a certain monk: "Go to Śāriputra and tell him, 'The World-Honored One calls you. Not long after you left, a certain practitioner committed an offense in my presence and said, "World-Honored One! Today Venerable Śāriputra has slighted me and is wandering among the people.

世尊闻已，告一比丘：「汝往舍梨子所，语舍梨子：『世尊呼汝。汝去不久，有一梵行在于我前犯相违法，而作是语：「世尊！今日尊者舍梨子轻慢我已，游行人间。」』」

The monk, having received the instruction, immediately rose from his seat, paid homage to the Buddha, and departed. Then Venerable Ānanda stood behind the World-Honored One, holding a whisk to attend to the Buddha.

一比丘受教已，即从坐起，礼佛而去。于是，尊者阿难住世尊后执拂侍佛。

Not long after the monk left, Venerable Ānanda took the key and went to all the rooms. Seeing the monks, he said, "Excellent! Venerables! Quickly go to the lecture hall. Now Venerable Śāriputra will roar like a lion in front of the Buddha. If what Venerable Śāriputra says is profound, the most subtle of all subtleties, if he speaks like this, then you and I will be able to hear it and recite and uphold it well." When the monks heard Venerable Ānanda's words, they all went to the lecture hall.

一比丘去后不久，尊者阿难即持户钥，遍至诸房，见诸比丘便作是语：「善哉！诸尊！速诣讲堂，今尊者舍梨子当在佛前而师子吼，若尊者舍梨子所说甚深，息中之息，妙中之妙，如是说者，诸尊及我得闻此已，当善诵习，当善受持。」彼时，诸比丘闻尊者阿难语已，悉诣讲堂。

At that time, a monk went to Venerable Śāriputra and said, "The World-Honored One calls you. Not long after you left, a fellow practitioner in front of me committed an offense and said, 'World-Honored One! Today Venerable Śāriputra has slighted me and is wandering among the people.'"

尔时，一比丘往诣尊者舍梨子所，白曰：「世尊呼汝。汝去不久，有一梵行在于我前犯相违法，而作是语：『世尊！今日尊者舍梨子轻慢我已，游行人间。』」

Then Venerable Śāriputra, having heard this, immediately rose from his seat and returned to the Buddha. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side. The Buddha then said, "Śāriputra! Not long after you left, a fellow practitioner in front of me committed an offense and said, 'World-Honored One! Today Venerable Śāriputra has slighted me and is wandering among the people.' Śāriputra! Did you really slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people?"

于是，尊者舍梨子闻已，即从坐起，便还诣佛，稽首礼足，却坐一面。佛便告曰：「舍梨子！汝去不久，有一梵行在于我前犯相违法，而作是语：『世尊！今日尊者舍梨子轻慢我已，游行人间。』舍梨子！汝实轻慢一梵行已而游人间耶？」

Venerable Śāriputra replied, "World-Honored One! If one does not have mindfulness of the body with regard to the body, then one will slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people. World-Honored One! I have good mindfulness of the body with regard to the body. How could I slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people? World-Honored One! It is like a castrated bull that is very patient, gentle, well-trained, and well-tamed. It goes from village to village, from street to street, and wherever it goes, it does no harm. World-Honored One! I am also like this. My mind is like a castrated bull, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without disputes, extremely vast, very great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, and accomplished in wandering. World-Honored One! If one does not have mindfulness of the body with regard to the body, then one will slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people. World-Honored One! I have good mindfulness of the body with regard to the body. How could I slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people? "

尊者舍梨子白曰：「世尊！若无身身念者，彼便轻慢于一梵行而游人间。世尊！我善有身身念，我当云何轻慢一梵行而游人间？世尊！犹截角牛，至忍温良，善调善御，从村至村，从巷至巷，所游行处，无所侵犯。世尊！我亦如是，心如截角牛，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。世尊！若无身身念者，彼便轻慢于一梵行而游人间。世尊！我善有身身念，我当云何轻慢一梵行而游人间？

"World-Honored One! It is like a son of a caṇḍāla whose hands have been cut off. His mind is very low. He goes from village to village, from town to town, and wherever he goes, he does no harm. World-Honored One! I am also like this. My mind is like the caṇḍāla whose hands have been cut off, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without disputes, extremely vast, very great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, and accomplished in wandering. World-Honored One! If one does not have mindfulness of the body with regard to the body, then one will slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people. World-Honored One! I have good mindfulness of the body with regard to the body. How could I slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people? "

「世尊！犹旃陀罗子而截两手，其意至下，从村至村，从邑至邑，所游行处，无所侵犯。世尊！我亦如是，心如截手旃陀罗子，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。世尊！若无身身念者，彼便轻慢于一梵行而游人间。世尊！我善有身身念，我当云何轻慢一梵行而游人间？

"World-Honored One! It is like the earth, which accepts both pure and impure things, such as excrement, urine, snot, and spittle. The earth does not feel aversion or attachment towards these, nor does it feel shame or embarrassment, nor does it feel remorse. World-Honored One! I am also like this. My mind is like the earth, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without disputes, extremely vast, very great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, and accomplished in wandering. World-Honored One! If one does not have mindfulness of the body with regard to the body, then one will slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people. World-Honored One! I have good mindfulness of the body with regard to the body. How could I slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people? "

「世尊！犹若如地，净与不净，大便、小便、涕、唾悉受，地不以此而有憎爱，不羞不慙，亦不愧耻。世尊！我亦如是，心如彼地，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。世尊！若无身身念者，彼便轻慢于一梵行而游人间。世尊！我善有身身念，我当云何轻慢一梵行而游人间？

"World-Honored One! It is like water, which washes both pure and impure things, such as excrement, urine, snot, and spittle. Water does not feel aversion or attachment towards these, nor does it feel shame or embarrassment, nor does it feel remorse. World-Honored One! I am also like this. My mind is like water, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without disputes, extremely vast, very great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, and accomplished in wandering. World-Honored One! If one does not have mindfulness of the body with regard to the body, then one will slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people. World-Honored One! I have good mindfulness of the body with regard to the body. How could I slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people? "

「世尊！犹若如水，净与不净，大便、小便、涕、唾悉洗，水不以此而有憎爱，不羞不慙，亦不愧耻。世尊！我亦如是，心如彼水，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。世尊！若无身身念者，彼便轻慢于一梵行而游人间。世尊！我善有身身念，我当云何轻慢一梵行而游人间？

"World-Honored One! It is like fire, which burns both pure and impure things, such as excrement, urine, snot, and spittle. Fire does not feel aversion or attachment towards these, nor does it feel shame or embarrassment, nor does it feel remorse. World-Honored One! I am also like this. My mind is like fire, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without disputes, extremely vast, very great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, and accomplished in wandering. World-Honored One! If one does not have mindfulness of the body with regard to the body, then one will slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people. World-Honored One! I have good mindfulness of the body with regard to the body. How could I slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people? "

「世尊！犹若如火，净与不净，大便、小便、涕、唾悉烧，火不以此而有憎爱，不羞不慙，亦不愧耻。世尊！我亦如是，心如彼火，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。世尊！若无身身念者，彼便轻慢于一梵行而游人间。世尊！我善有身身念，我当云何轻慢一梵行而游人间？

"World-Honored One! It is like wind, which blows both pure and impure things, such as excrement, urine, snot, and spittle. Wind does not feel aversion or attachment towards these, nor does it feel shame or embarrassment, nor does it feel remorse. World-Honored One! I am also like this. My mind is like wind, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without disputes, extremely vast, very great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, and accomplished in wandering. World-Honored One! If one does not have mindfulness of the body with regard to the body, then one will slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people. World-Honored One! I have good mindfulness of the body with regard to the body. How could I slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people? "

「世尊！犹若如风，净与不净，大便、小便、涕、唾悉吹，风不以此而有憎爱，不羞不慙，亦不愧耻。世尊！我亦如是，心如彼风，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。世尊！若无身身念者，彼便轻慢于一梵行而游人间。世尊！我善有身身念，我当云何轻慢一梵行而游人间？

"World-Honored One! It is like a broom, which sweeps both pure and impure things, such as excrement, urine, snot, and spittle. The broom does not feel aversion or attachment towards these, nor does it feel shame or embarrassment, nor does it feel remorse. World-Honored One! I am also like this. My mind is like a broom, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without disputes, extremely vast, very great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, and accomplished in wandering. World-Honored One! If one does not have mindfulness of the body with regard to the body, then one will slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people. World-Honored One! I have good mindfulness of the body with regard to the body. How could I slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people? "

「世尊！犹如扫帚，净与不净，大便、小便、涕、唾悉扫，帚不以此而有憎爱，不羞不慙，亦不愧耻。世尊！我亦如是，心如扫帚，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。世尊！若无身身念者，彼便轻慢于一梵行而游人间。世尊！我善有身身念，我当云何轻慢一梵行而游人间？

"World-Honored One! It is like a cloth, which wipes both pure and impure things, such as excrement, urine, snot, and spittle. The cloth does not feel aversion or attachment towards these, nor does it feel shame or embarrassment, nor does it feel remorse. World-Honored One! I am also like this. My mind is like a cloth, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without disputes, extremely vast, very great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, pervading the entire world, and accomplished in wandering. World-Honored One! If one does not have mindfulness of the body with regard to the body, then one will slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people. World-Honored One! I have good mindfulness of the body with regard to the body. How could I slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people? "

「世尊！犹晡旃尼，净与不净，大便、小便、涕、唾悉拭，晡旃尼不以此故而有憎爱，不羞不慙，亦不愧耻。世尊！我亦如是，心如晡旃尼，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。世尊！若无身身念者，彼便轻慢于一梵行而游人间。世尊！我善有身身念，我当云何轻慢一梵行而游人间？

"World-Honored One! It is like an oil bottle with holes all over, filled with oil and placed in the sun, leaking oil everywhere. If a person with eyes comes and stands to one side, they will see this oil bottle with holes all over, filled with oil and placed in the sun, leaking oil everywhere. World-Honored One! I also constantly observe this body with its nine orifices, leaking impurities everywhere. World-Honored One! If one does not have mindfulness of the body with regard to the body, then one will slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people. World-Honored One! I have good mindfulness of the body with regard to the body. How could I slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people? "

「世尊！犹如膏瓶处处裂破，盛满膏已而著日中，漏遍漏津遍津。若有目人，来住一面，见此膏瓶处处裂破，盛满膏已而著日中，漏遍漏津遍津。世尊！我亦如是，常观此身九孔不净，漏遍漏津遍津。世尊！若无身身念者，彼便轻慢于一梵行而游人间。世尊！我善有身身念，我当云何轻慢一梵行而游人间？

"World-Honored One! It is like a young man who bathes and washes, anoints himself with perfume, wears clean white clothes, adorns himself with necklaces, shaves his beard and hair, and wears a flower garland on his head. If he were to have three corpses, a dead snake, a dead dog, and a dead person, blue and swollen, extremely stinking and rotten, with impurities flowing everywhere, tied around his neck, he would feel ashamed and disgusted by the extreme filth. World-Honored One! I also constantly observe this body as a stinking place of impurity, feeling ashamed and disgusted by the extreme filth. World-Honored One! If one does not have mindfulness of the body with regard to the body, then one will slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people. World-Honored One! I have good mindfulness of the body with regard to the body. How could I slight a fellow practitioner and wander among the people? "

「世尊！犹如有一自喜年少，沐浴澡洗，熏以涂香，著白净衣，璎珞自严，剃须治发，头冠华鬘，若以三尸，死蛇、死狗及以死人，青瘀膖胀，极臭烂坏，不净流漫，系著咽颈，彼怀羞慙，极恶秽之。世尊！我亦如是，常观此身臭处不净，心怀羞慙，极恶秽之。世尊！若无身身念者，彼便轻慢于一梵行而游人间。世尊！我善有身身念，我当云何轻慢一梵行而游人间？」

Then that bhikkhu rose from his seat, bowed his head at the Buddha’s feet, and said to the World-Honored One: “I repent, World-Honored One! I confess, Well-Gone One! I was foolish, ignorant, unstable, and unskillful. Why is that? It is because I falsely accused the pure and virtuous bhikkhu Sāriputta. World-Honored One! I now repent and wish for your acceptance. Having seen it, I reveal it and will not do it again in the future.”

于是，彼比丘即从坐起，稽首佛足，白世尊曰：「悔过，世尊！自首，善逝！如愚如痴，如不定，如不善。所以者何？谓我以虚妄言诬谤清净梵行舍梨子比丘。世尊！我今悔过，愿为受之，见已发露，后不更作。」

The World-Honored One said: "So it is, bhikkhu! You really are foolish, ignorant, unstable, and unskillful. Why is that? It is because you falsely accused the pure and virtuous bhikkhu Sāriputta with empty and false words. You are able to repent, see it, reveal it, and not do it again in the future. If one can repent, see it, reveal it, and not do it again in the future, then one will cultivate and grow in the noble Dharma and Vinaya, and will not decline."

世尊告曰：「如是。比丘！汝实如愚如痴，如不定，如不善。所以者何？谓汝以虚妄言空无真实，诬谤清净梵行舍梨子比丘。汝能悔过，见已发露，后不更作。若有悔过，见已发露，后不更作者，如是长养于圣法、律则不衰退。」

Then the Buddha said to Venerable Sāriputta: "You should quickly accept the repentance of that foolish person, so that this bhikkhu does not have his head split into seven pieces right in front of you."

于是，佛告尊者舍梨子：「汝速受彼痴人悔过，莫令彼比丘即于汝前头破七分。」

Venerable Sāriputta, out of compassion for that bhikkhu, then accepted his repentance.

尊者舍梨子即为哀愍彼比丘故，便受悔过。

The Buddha spoke thus. Venerable Sāriputta and the other bhikkhus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说如是。尊者舍梨子及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
