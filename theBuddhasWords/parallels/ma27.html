MĀ 27

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 3 舍梨子相应品

27. To Dhānañjāni （二七）梵志陀然经

Parallel to: MN 97

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

Once, the Buddha was staying in the Bamboo Grove at the Kalandaka Park in Rājagṛha, together with a great assembly of bhikṣus, spending the summer retreat. At that time, Venerable Śāriputra was also spending the summer retreat in Śrāvastī.

一时，佛游王舍城，在竹林加兰哆园，与大比丘众俱，共受夏坐。尔时，尊者舍梨子在舍卫国亦受夏坐。

At that time, a bhikṣu had completed the summer retreat in Rājagṛha, and after three months, having repaired his robes, he put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and went from Rājagṛha to Śrāvastī, staying in the Jeta Grove at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park. That bhikṣu went to Venerable Śāriputra, bowed his head at his feet, and sat to one side.

是时，有一比丘于王舍城受夏坐讫，过三月已，补治衣竟，摄衣持钵，从王舍城往舍卫国，住胜林给孤独园。彼一比丘往诣尊者舍梨子所，稽首礼足，却坐一面。

Venerable Śāriputra asked, "Virtuous one! Where have you come from, and where did you spend the summer retreat?"

尊者舍梨子问曰：「贤者！从何处来，于何夏坐？」

That bhikṣu replied, "Venerable Śāriputra! I have come from Rājagṛha, and I spent the summer retreat in Rājagṛha."

彼一比丘答曰：「尊者舍梨子！我从王舍城来，在王舍城受夏坐。」

He further asked, "Virtuous one! The World-Honored One spent the summer retreat in Rājagṛha. Is his body healthy, comfortable, without illness, and is his daily life light and his strength as usual?"

复问：「贤者！世尊在王舍城受夏坐，圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常耶？」

He replied, "Yes, Venerable Śāriputra! The World-Honored One spent the summer retreat in Rājagṛha. His body is healthy, comfortable, without illness, and his daily life is light and his strength is as usual."

答曰：「如是。尊者舍梨子！世尊在王舍城受夏坐，圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常。」

He further asked, "Virtuous one! The bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs spent the summer retreat in Rājagṛha. Are their bodies healthy, comfortable, without illness, and is their daily life light and their strength as usual? Do they wish to see the Buddha often and enjoy hearing the Dharma?"

复问：「贤者！比丘众、比丘尼众在王舍城受夏坐，圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常，欲数见佛，乐闻法耶？」

He replied, "Yes, Venerable Śāriputra! The bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs spent the summer retreat in Rājagṛha. Their bodies are healthy, comfortable, without illness, and their daily life is light and their strength is as usual. They wish to see the Buddha often and all enjoy hearing the Dharma."

答曰：「如是。尊者舍梨子！比丘众、比丘尼众在王舍城受夏坐，圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常，欲数见佛，尽乐闻法。」

He further asked, "Virtuous one! The upāsaka and upāsikā communities reside in Rājagṛha. Are their bodies healthy, comfortable, without illness, and is their daily life light and their strength as usual? Do they wish to see the Buddha often and enjoy hearing the Dharma?"

复问：「贤者！优婆塞众、优婆夷众住王舍城，身体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常，欲数见佛，乐闻法耶？」

He replied, "Yes, Venerable Śāriputra! The upāsaka and upāsikā communities reside in Rājagṛha. Their bodies are healthy, comfortable, without illness, and their daily life is light and their strength is as usual. They wish to see the Buddha often and all enjoy hearing the Dharma."

答曰：「如是。尊者舍梨子！优婆塞众、优婆夷众住王舍城，身体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常，欲数见佛，尽乐闻法。」

He further asked, "Virtuous one! The various non-Buddhist śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who are staying in Rājagṛha for the summer retreat, are their bodies healthy, comfortable, without illness, and is their daily life light and their strength as usual? Do they wish to see the Buddha often and enjoy hearing the Dharma?"

复问：「贤者！若干异学沙门、梵志在王舍城受夏坐，身体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常，欲数见佛，乐闻法耶？」

He replied, "Yes, Venerable Śāriputra! The various non-Buddhist śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who are staying in Rājagṛha for the summer retreat, their bodies are healthy, comfortable, without illness, and their daily life is light and their strength is as usual. They wish to see the Buddha often and all enjoy hearing the Dharma."

答曰：「如是。尊者舍梨子！若干异学沙门、梵志在王舍城受夏坐，身体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常，欲数见佛，尽乐闻法。」

He further asked, "Virtuous one! In Rājagṛha there is a brāhmaṇa named Dhāraṇa, who was a friend of mine before I left home. Do you know him?"

复问：「贤者！在王舍城有一梵志，名曰陀然，是我昔日未出家友，贤者识耶？」

He replied, "I know him."

答曰：「识之。」

He further asked, "Virtuous one! Does the brāhmaṇa Dhāraṇa, residing in Rājagṛha, have a healthy body, is he at ease and free from illness, does he move about lightly and with ease, and does he have the same strength as before? Does he wish to see the Buddha often and enjoy hearing the Dharma?"

复问：「贤者！梵志陀然住王舍城，身体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常，欲数见佛，乐闻法耶？」

He replied, "Venerable Śāriputra! The brāhmaṇa Dhāraṇa, residing in Rājagṛha, has a healthy body, is at ease and free from illness, moves about lightly and with ease, and has the same strength as before. He does not wish to see the Buddha, nor does he enjoy hearing the Dharma. Why is that? Venerable Śāriputra! The brāhmaṇa Dhāraṇa is not diligent and violates the precepts. He relies on the king to deceive the brāhmaṇas and householders, and relies on the brāhmaṇas and householders to deceive the king."

答曰：「尊者舍梨子！梵志陀然住王舍城，身体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常，不欲见佛，不乐闻法，所以者何？尊者舍梨子！梵志陀然而不精进，犯于禁戒，彼依傍于王，欺诳梵志、居士，依恃梵志、居士，欺诳于王。」

Having heard this, the Venerable Śāriputra, after completing the summer retreat in Śrāvastī and three months had passed, mended his robes, put on his robe and took his alms bowl, went to Rājagṛha from Śrāvastī, and stayed in the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove.

尊者舍梨子闻已，于舍卫国受夏坐讫，过三月已，补治衣竟，摄衣持钵，从舍卫国往诣王舍城，住竹林加兰哆园。

Then, the Venerable Śāriputra, after the night had passed and it was early morning, put on his robe, took his alms bowl, entered Rājagṛha, and went for alms in order. Having finished his alms round, he went to the house of the brāhmaṇa Dhāraṇa. At that time, the brāhmaṇa Dhāraṇa had come out of his house and was suffering from the people at the well. The brāhmaṇa Dhāraṇa saw from afar the Venerable Śāriputra coming, rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms towards the Venerable Śāriputra, and praised him, saying, "Welcome, Śāriputra! It has been a long time since you came here." Then, the brāhmaṇa Dhāraṇa respectfully embraced the Venerable Śāriputra and took him into his house. He prepared a good seat and invited him to sit. The Venerable Śāriputra then sat on the seat. Seeing that the Venerable Śāriputra had sat down, the brāhmaṇa Dhāraṇa took a golden vessel and invited the Venerable Śāriputra to eat.

于是，尊者舍梨子过夜平旦，著衣持钵，入王舍城，次行乞食，乞食已，竟往至梵志陀然家。是时，梵志陀然从其家出，至泉水边苦治居民。梵志陀然遥见尊者舍梨子来，从坐而起，偏袒著衣，叉手向尊者舍梨子赞曰：「善来，舍梨子！舍梨子久不来此。」于是，梵志陀然敬心扶抱尊者舍梨子，将入家中，为敷好床，请使令坐。尊者舍梨子即坐其床，梵志陀然见尊者舍梨子坐已，执金澡灌，请尊者舍梨子食。

The Venerable Śāriputra said, "Stop, stop, Dhāraṇa! Just be content with your mind."

尊者舍梨子曰：「止！止！陀然！但心喜足。」

The brāhmaṇa Dhāraṇa again invited him to eat three times, and the Venerable Śāriputra also said three times, "Stop, stop, Dhāraṇa! Just be content with your mind."

梵志陀然复再三请食，尊者舍梨子亦再三语曰：「止！止！陀然，但心喜足。」

At that time, the brāhmaṇa Dhāraṇa asked, "Śāriputra! Why did you enter such a house but not eat?"

是时，梵志陀然问曰：「舍梨子！何故入如是家而不肯食？」

He replied, "Dhāraṇa! You are not diligent, you violate the precepts, you rely on the king to deceive the brāhmaṇas and householders, and you rely on the brāhmaṇas and householders to deceive the king."

答曰：「陀然！汝不精进，犯于禁戒，依傍于王，欺诳梵志、居士，依傍梵志、居士，欺诳于王。」

The brāhmaṇa Dhāraṇa replied, "Śāriputra! You should know that I am now a householder, and my affairs are related to my family business. I should provide for myself, support my parents, care for my wife and children, provide for my servants, pay taxes to the king, make offerings to the gods, make sacrifices to my ancestors, and make offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, in order to be reborn in heaven and attain long life, and to attain the fruit of happiness. Śāriputra! All these matters are beyond doubt, and I follow the Dharma exclusively."

梵志陀然答曰：「舍梨子！当知我今在家，以家业为事，我应自安隐，供养父母，瞻视妻子，供给奴婢，当输王租，祠祀诸天，祭餟先祖及布施沙门、梵志，为后生天而得长寿，得乐果报故。舍梨子！是一切事不可得疑，一向从法。」

Then Venerable Śāriputra said, "Dhāraṇa! I ask you, answer as you see fit. Dhāraṇa, what do you think? If someone commits evil deeds for the sake of their parents, and because of committing evil deeds, upon the destruction of the body and the end of life, they go to a bad place and are born in hell. Once born in hell, when the jailers seize and severely punish them, they say to the jailers, 'Jailers! You should know, do not punish me severely. Why? I committed evil deeds for the sake of my parents.' What do you think, Dhāraṇa? Can that person be released from the suffering of hell by the jailers?"

于是，尊者舍梨子告曰：「陀然！我今问汝，随所解答。梵志陀然！于意云何？若使有人为父母故而行作恶，因行恶故，身坏命终趣至恶处，生地狱中。生地狱已，狱卒执捉，极苦治时，彼向狱卒而作是语：『狱卒！当知，莫苦治我。所以者何？我为父母故而行作恶。』云何？陀然！彼人可得从地狱卒脱此苦耶？」

He replied, "No."

答曰：「不也。」

He further asked, "Dhāraṇa! What do you think? If someone commits evil deeds for the sake of their wife and children, and because of committing evil deeds, upon the destruction of the body and the end of life, they go to a bad place and are born in hell. Once born in hell, when the jailers seize and severely punish them, they say to the jailers, 'Jailers! You should know, do not punish me severely. Why? I committed evil deeds for the sake of my wife and children.' What do you think, Dhāraṇa? Can that person be released from the suffering of hell by the jailers?"

复问：「陀然！于意云何？若复有人为妻子故而行作恶，因行恶故，身坏命终趣至恶处生地狱中。生地狱已，狱卒执捉，极苦治时，彼向狱卒而作是语：『狱卒！当知，莫苦治我。所以者何？我为妻子故而行作恶。』云何？陀然！彼人可得从地狱卒脱此苦耶？」

He replied, "No."

答曰：「不也。」

He further asked, "Dhāraṇa! What do you think? If someone commits evil deeds for the sake of slaves and servants, and because of committing evil deeds, upon the destruction of the body and the end of life, they go to a bad place and are born in hell. Once born in hell, when the jailers seize and severely punish them, they say to the jailers, 'Jailers! You should know, do not punish me severely. Why? I committed evil deeds for the sake of slaves and servants.' What do you think, Dhāraṇa? Can that person be released from the suffering of hell by the jailers?"

复问：「陀然！于意云何？若复有人为奴婢故，而行作恶，因行恶故，身坏命终趣至恶处，生地狱中。生地狱已，狱卒执捉，极苦治时，彼向狱卒而作是语：『狱卒！当知，莫苦治我。所以者何？我为奴婢故而行作恶。』云何？陀然，彼人可得从地狱卒脱此苦耶？」

He replied, "No."

答曰：「不也。」

He further asked, "Dhāraṇa! What do you think? If someone commits evil deeds for the sake of the king, the gods, ancestors, śramaṇas, and brāhmaṇas, and because of committing evil deeds, upon the destruction of the body and the end of life, they go to a bad place and are born in hell. Once born in hell, when the jailers seize and severely punish them, they say to the jailers, 'Jailers! You should know, do not punish me severely. Why? I committed evil deeds for the sake of the king, the gods, ancestors, śramaṇas, and brāhmaṇas.' What do you think, Dhāraṇa? Can that person be released from the suffering of hell by the jailers?"

复问：「陀然！于意云何？若复有人为王、为天、为先祖，为沙门、梵志故，而行作恶，因行恶故，身坏命终趣至恶处，生地狱中。生地狱已，狱卒执捉，极苦治时，彼向狱卒而作是语：『狱卒！当知，莫苦治我。所以者何？我为王、为天、为先祖，为沙门、梵志故，而行作恶。』云何？陀然，彼人可得从地狱卒脱此苦耶？」

He replied, "No."

答曰：「不也。」

Dhāraṇa! A clansman can obtain wealth according to the Dharma, according to his deeds, and according to his merits. He should respect, honor, and support his parents, practice meritorious deeds, and not do evil deeds. Dhāraṇa! If a clansman obtains wealth according to the Dharma, according to his deeds, and according to his merits, respects, honors, and supports his parents, practices meritorious deeds, and does not do evil deeds, then he will be loved and cherished by his parents, who will say, 'May you be strong and healthy, with a long life span. Why? Because of you, we are at ease and happy.' Dhāraṇa! If someone is greatly loved and cherished by his parents, his virtues will increase day by day, and he will never decline.

「陀然！族姓子可得如法、如业、如功德得钱财，尊重奉敬孝养父母，行福德业，不作恶业。陀然！若族姓子如法、如业、如功德得钱财，尊重奉敬孝养父母，行福德业，不作恶业者，彼便为父母之所爱念，而作是言：『令汝强健，寿考无穷。所以者何？我由汝故，安隐快乐。』陀然！若有人极为父母所爱念者，其德日进，终无衰退。

Dhāraṇa! A clansman can obtain wealth according to the Dharma, according to his deeds, and according to his merits. He should love and cherish his wife and children, provide for and care for them, practice meritorious deeds, and not do evil deeds. Dhāraṇa! If a clansman obtains wealth according to the Dharma, according to his deeds, and according to his merits, loves and cherishes his wife and children, provides for and cares for them, practices meritorious deeds, and does not do evil deeds, then he will be respected by his wife and children, who will say, 'May you be strong and healthy, with a long life span. Why? Because of you, we are at ease and happy.' Dhāraṇa! If someone is greatly respected by his wife and children, his virtues will increase day by day, and he will never decline.

「陀然！族姓子可得如法、如业、如功德得钱财，爱念妻子，供给瞻视，行福德业，不作恶业。陀然！若族姓子如法、如业、如功德得钱财，爱念妻子，供给瞻视，行福德业，不作恶业者，彼便为妻子之所尊重，而作是言：『愿尊强健，寿考无穷。所以者何？我由尊故，安隐快乐。』陀然！若有人极为妻子所尊重者，其德日进，终无衰退。

Dhāraṇa! A clansman can obtain wealth according to the Dharma, according to his deeds, and according to his merits. He should have compassion for his servants and maids, provide for and care for them, practice meritorious deeds, and not do evil deeds. Dhāraṇa! If a clansman obtains wealth according to the Dharma, according to his deeds, and according to his merits, has compassion for his servants and maids, provides for and cares for them, practices meritorious deeds, and does not do evil deeds, then he will be respected by his servants and maids, who will say, 'May the master be strong and healthy, with a long life span. Why? Because of the master, we are at ease.' Dhāraṇa! If someone is greatly respected by his servants and maids, his virtues will increase day by day, and he will never decline.

「陀然！族姓子可得如法、如业、如功德得钱财，愍伤奴婢，给恤瞻视，行福德业，不作恶业。陀然！若族姓子如法、如业、如功德得钱财，愍伤奴婢，给恤瞻视，行福德业，不作恶业者，彼便为奴婢之所尊重，而作是言：『愿令大家强健，寿考无穷。所以者何？由大家故，我得安隐。』陀然！若有人极为奴婢所尊重者，其德日进，终无衰退。

Dhāraṇa! A clansman can obtain wealth according to the Dharma, according to his deeds, and according to his merits. He should respect and make offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, practice meritorious deeds, and not do evil deeds. Dhāraṇa! If a clansman obtains wealth according to the Dharma, according to his deeds, and according to his merits, respects and makes offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, practices meritorious deeds, and does not do evil deeds, then he will be greatly loved by śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, who will say, 'May the donor be strong and healthy, with a long life span. Why? Because of the donor, we are at ease and happy.' Dhāraṇa! If someone is greatly loved by śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, his virtues will increase day by day, and he will never decline.

「陀然！族姓子可得如法、如业、如功德得钱财，尊重供养沙门、梵志，行福德业，不作恶业。陀然！若族姓子如法、如业、如功德得钱财，尊重供养沙门、梵志，行福德业，不作恶业者，彼便极为沙门、梵志之所爱念，而作是言：『令施主强健，寿考无穷。所以者何？我由施主故，得安隐快乐。』陀然！若有人极为沙门、梵志所爱念者，其德日进，终无衰退。」

Then the brāhmaṇa Dhāraṇa rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, put his palms together toward Venerable Sāriputta, and said, "Sāriputta! I have a beloved wife named Sundarī. Because of my infatuation with her, I have been negligent and committed great evil deeds. Sāriputta! From today onward, I renounce my wife Sundarī and take refuge in Venerable Sāriputta."

于是，梵志陀然即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向尊者舍梨子白曰：「舍梨子！我有爱妇，名曰端正，我惑彼故，而为放逸，大作罪业。舍梨子！我从今日始，舍端正妇，自归尊者舍梨子。」

Venerable Sāriputta replied, "Dhāraṇa! Do not take refuge in me. You should take refuge in the Buddha, whom I take refuge in."

尊者舍梨子答曰：「陀然！汝莫归我，我所归佛汝应自归。」

The brāhmaṇa Dhāraṇa said, "Venerable Sāriputta! From today onward, I take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Saṅgha of monks. I only wish that Venerable Sāriputta would accept me as a lay follower of the Buddha, taking refuge for life until the end of my days."

梵志陀然白曰：「尊者舍梨子！我从今日自归于佛、法及比丘众，唯愿尊者舍梨子受我为佛优婆塞，终身自归，乃至命尽。」

Then Venerable Sāriputta taught the Dharma to the brāhmaṇa Dhāraṇa, inspiring and gladdening him. Having taught the Dharma in countless ways, inspiring and gladdening him, he rose from his seat and departed. He traveled to Rājagṛha and stayed there for several days. Then he put on his robe, took his bowl, left Rājagṛha, and went to the southern mountains, staying in the Śiṃśapā Forest north of the village on the southern mountains.

于是，尊者舍梨子为梵志陀然说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，从坐起去，游王舍城，住经数日，摄衣持钵，从王舍城出，往诣南山，住南山村北尸摄惒林中。

At that time, a certain monk was wandering in Rājagṛha and stayed there for several days. He put on his robe, took his bowl, left Rājagṛha, and also went to the southern mountains, staying in the Śiṃśapā Forest north of the village on the southern mountains.

彼时，有一比丘游王舍城，住经数日，摄衣持钵，从王舍城出，亦至南山，住南山村北尸摄惒林中。

Then that monk went to Venerable Sāriputta, bowed his head at his feet, and sat to one side.

于是，彼一比丘往诣尊者舍梨子所，稽首礼足，却坐一面。

Venerable Sāriputta asked, "Virtuous one! Where have you come from? Where have you been wandering?"

尊者舍梨子问曰：「贤者！从何处来？何处游行？」

The monk replied, "Venerable Sāriputta! I have come from Rājagaha, wandering in Rājagaha."

比丘答曰：「尊者舍梨子！我从王舍城来，游行王舍城。」

He further asked, "Virtuous one! Do you know that there is a brahmin named Dīrghanakha in Rājagaha, who was my friend before I left home?"

复问：「贤者！知王舍城有一梵志，名曰陀然，是我昔日未出家友耶？」

He replied, "I know."

答曰：「知也。」

He further asked, "Virtuous one! Does the brahmin Dīrghanakha, residing in Rājagaha, have a healthy body, is he at ease and free from illness, does he move about lightly and with ease, and does he have the usual strength, and does he wish to see the Buddha often and enjoy hearing the Dharma?"

复问：「贤者！梵志陀然住王舍城，身体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常，欲数见佛，乐闻法耶？」

He replied, "Venerable Śāriputra! The brahmin Dīrghanakha wishes to see the Buddha often and to hear the Dharma often, but he is not at ease and his strength is declining. Why is that? Venerable Śāriputra! The brahmin Dīrghanakha is now sick, extremely ill and gravely ill, and may die from it."

答曰：「尊者舍梨子！梵志陀然欲数见佛，欲数闻法，但不安快，气力转衰。所以者何？尊者舍梨子！梵志陀然今者疾病，极困危笃，或能因此而至命终。」

Having heard these words, Venerable Śāriputra immediately put on his robe, took his alms bowl, left Nan-shan, and arrived at Rājagṛha, staying at the Bamboo Grove Kalanda-nivāpa. Then, Venerable Śāriputra, after the night had passed, put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and went to the house of the brahmin Dīrghanakha. The brahmin Dīrghanakha saw Venerable Śāriputra coming from afar, and upon seeing him, he wanted to get up from his bed. Venerable Śāriputra saw the brahmin Dīrghanakha wanting to get up from his bed, and he stopped him, saying, "Brahmin Dīrghanakha! Lie down, do not get up. There are other beds. I will sit separately."

尊者舍梨子闻是语已，即摄衣持钵，从南山出，至王舍城，住竹林加兰哆园。于是，尊者舍梨子过夜平旦，著衣持钵，往诣梵志陀然家。梵志陀然遥见尊者舍梨子来，见已便欲从床而起，尊者舍梨子见梵志陀然欲从床起，便止彼曰：「梵志陀然！汝卧勿起，更有余床，我自别坐。」

Then, Venerable Śāriputra sat on the bed and asked, "Dīrghanakha! How is your illness now? How much do you eat and drink? Does the illness decrease or increase?"

于是，尊者舍梨子即坐其床，坐已，问曰：「陀然！所患今者何似，饮食多少，疾苦转损，不至增耶？」

Dīrghanakha replied, "My illness is very severe. I cannot eat or drink. The illness only increases and does not decrease. Venerable Śāriputra! It is like a strong man stabbing his head with a sharp knife, only causing extreme pain. My headache now is also like this. Venerable Śāriputra! It is like a strong man binding his head with a tight rope, only causing extreme pain. My headache now is also like this. Venerable Śāriputra! It is like a butcher cutting open a cow's belly with a sharp knife, only causing extreme pain. My stomach pain now is also like this. Venerable Śāriputra! It is like two strong men holding a weak man over a fire to roast him, only causing extreme pain. My body pain now, with pain all over the body, only increases and does not decrease, is also like this."

陀然答曰：「所患至困，饮食不进，疾苦但增而不觉损。尊者舍梨子！犹如力士以利刀刺头，但生极苦，我今头痛亦复如是。尊者舍梨子！犹如力士以紧索绳而缠络头，但生极苦，我今头痛亦复如是。尊者舍梨子！犹屠牛儿而以利刀破于牛腹，但生极苦，我今腹痛亦复如是。尊者舍梨子！犹两力士捉一羸人在火上炙，但生极苦，我今身痛，举体生苦，但增不减，亦复如是。」

Venerable Śāriputra said, "Dīrghanakha! I now ask you, answer as you see fit. Dīrghanakha, what do you think? Between hell and the animal realm, which is better?"

尊者舍梨子告曰：「陀然！我今问汝，随所解答。梵志陀然！于意云何？地狱、畜生，何者为胜？」

Dīrghanakha replied, "The animal realm is better."

陀然答曰：「畜生胜也。」

He further asked, "Dīrghanakha! Between the animal realm and the ghost realm, which is better?"

复问：「陀然！畜生、饿鬼，何者为胜？」

Dīrghanakha replied, "The ghost realm is better."

陀然答曰：「饿鬼胜也。」

He further asked, "Dīrghanakha! Between the ghost realm and the human realm, which is better?"

复问：「陀然！饿鬼比人，何者为胜？」

Dīrghanakha replied, "The human realm is better."

陀然答曰：「人为胜也。」

He further asked, "Dīrghanakha! Between humans and the Four Heavenly Kings, which is better?"

复问：「陀然！人、四王天，何者为胜？」

Dīrghanakha replied, "The Four Heavenly Kings are better."

陀然答曰：「四王天胜。」

He further asked, "Dīrghanakha! Between the Four Heavenly Kings and the Thirty-three Heavens, which is better?"

复问：「陀然！四王天、三十三天，何者为胜？」

Dīrghanakha replied, "The Thirty-three Heavens are better."

陀然答曰：「三十三天胜。」

He further asked, "Dīrghanakha! Between the Thirty-three Heavens and the Yāma Heaven, which is better?"

复问：「陀然！三十三天、［火＊佥］摩天，何者为胜？」

Dīrghanakha replied, "The Yāma Heaven is better."

陀然答曰：「［火＊佥］摩天胜。」

He further asked, "Dīrghanakha! Between the Yāma Heaven and the Tuṣita Heaven, which is better?"

复问：「陀然！［火＊佥］摩天、兜率陀天，何者为胜？」

Dīrghanakha replied, "The Tuṣita Heaven is better."

陀然答曰：「兜率陀天胜。」

He further asked, "Dīrghanakha! Between the Tuṣita Heaven and the Nirmāṇarati Heaven, which is better?"

复问：「陀然！兜率陀天、化乐天，何者为胜？」

Dīrghanakha replied, "The Nirmāṇarati Heaven is better."

陀然答曰：「化乐天胜。」

He further asked, "Dīrghanakha! Between the Nirmāṇarati Heaven and the Paranirmitavaśavartin Heaven, which is better?"

复问：「陀然！化乐天、他化乐天、何者为胜？」

Dīrghanakha replied, "The Paranirmitavaśavartin Heaven is better."

陀然答曰：「他化乐天胜。」

He further asked, "Dīrghanakha! Between the Paranirmitavaśavartin Heaven and the Brahmā Heaven, which is better?"

复问：「陀然！他化乐天、梵天，何者为胜？」

Dīrghanakha replied, "The Brahmā Heaven is the best, the Brahmā Heaven is the best."

陀然答曰：「梵天最胜，梵天最胜。」

Venerable Sāriputta said, "Dīrghanakha! The World-Honored One, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, speaks of four Brahma-dwellings, which, when cultivated and practiced by noble sons and daughters, can lead to the cessation of desire, the relinquishment of desire, and, upon the dissolution of the body and the end of life, rebirth in the Brahma-heaven. What are the four? Dīrghanakha! The noble disciple with much learning dwells pervading one direction with a mind imbued with loving-kindness, likewise the second, the third, and the fourth direction, the four intermediate directions, above, and below, all around, everywhere. With a mind imbued with loving-kindness, free from enmity and ill-will, without rivalry or dispute, as vast and boundless as the sky, he dwells pervading the entire world with a mind imbued with loving-kindness, likewise with compassion, sympathetic joy, and equanimity. These, Dīrghanakha! are the four Brahma-dwellings spoken of by the World-Honored One, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, which, when cultivated and practiced by noble sons and daughters, can lead to the cessation of desire, the relinquishment of desire, and, upon the dissolution of the body and the end of life, rebirth in the Brahma-heaven."

尊者舍梨子告曰：「陀然！世尊知见，如来、无所著、等正觉说四梵室，谓族姓男、族姓女修习多修习，断欲、舍欲念，身坏命终，生梵天中。云何为四？陀然！多闻圣弟子心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游，如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游，如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。是谓，陀然！世尊知见，如来、无所著、等正觉说四梵室，谓族姓男、族姓女修习多修习，断欲、舍欲念，身坏命终，生梵天中。」

Then Venerable Sāriputta, having instructed Dīrghanakha in the Brahma-dwelling teachings, rose from his seat and departed. Before Venerable Sāriputta reached the Bamboo Grove Kalandakanivāpa, the brahmin Dīrghanakha practiced the four Brahma-dwellings, cut off desire, relinquished desire, and, upon the dissolution of the body and the end of life, was reborn in the Brahma-heaven.

于是，尊者舍梨子教化陀然，为说梵天法已，从坐起去。尊者舍梨子从王舍城出，未至竹林加兰哆园，于其中间，梵志陀然修习四梵室，断欲、舍欲念，身坏命终，生梵天中。

At that time, the World-Honored One was teaching the Dharma to a great assembly of immeasurable people. The World-Honored One saw Venerable Sāriputta coming from afar and told the monks, "The monk Sāriputta is wise, quick-witted, sharp-witted, intelligent, broad-minded, deep-minded, able to escape, clear-minded, eloquent, and has accomplished true wisdom. This monk Sāriputta has instructed the brahmin Dīrghanakha in the Brahma-dwelling teachings. If he further instructs him, he will quickly know the Dharma as it is."

是时，世尊无量大众前后围绕而为说法，世尊遥见尊者舍梨子来，告诸比丘：「舍梨子比丘聪慧、速慧、捷慧、利慧、广慧、深慧、出要慧、明达慧、辩才慧，舍梨子比丘成就实慧。此舍梨子比丘教化梵志陀然，为说梵天法来，若复上化者，速知法如法。」

Then Venerable Sāriputta went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. The World-Honored One said, "Sāriputta! Why didn't you instruct the brahmin Dīrghanakha beyond the Brahma-dwelling teachings? If he further instructed him, he would quickly know the Dharma as it is."

于是，尊者舍梨子往诣佛所，稽首礼足，却坐一面，世尊告曰：「舍梨子！汝何以不教梵志陀然过梵天法，若上化者，速知法如法。」

Venerable Sāriputta said, "World-Honored One! Those brahmins have long loved, delighted in, and attained the Brahma-heaven. They consider the Brahma-heaven to be supreme, and they say, 'The Brahma-heaven truly exists, and it is for me.' Therefore, World-Honored One, I should do so."

尊者舍梨子白曰：「世尊！彼诸梵志长夜爱著梵天，乐于梵天，究竟梵天，是尊梵天，实有梵天，为我梵天。是故，世尊！我如是应。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Venerable Sāriputta and countless hundreds of thousands of people, having heard the Buddha's teachings, rejoiced and practiced them.

佛说如是。尊者舍梨子及无量百千众，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
