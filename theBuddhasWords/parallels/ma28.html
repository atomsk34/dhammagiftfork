MĀ 28

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 3 舍梨子相应品

28. On Teaching the Ill (Anāthapiṇḍika) （二八）教化病经

Parallel to: MN 143 (~) / SN 2.20 (~) / SN 10.8 (~) / SN 55.26 / SĀ 592 (~) / SĀ 593 (~) / SĀ 1032 (~) / SĀ² 2.186 (~) / SĀ² 2.187 (~) / EĀ 51.8 (~)

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the elder Anāthapiṇḍada was gravely ill. He then told a servant, "Go to the Buddha and bow on my behalf at the feet of the World-Honored One. Inquire after the World-Honored One's well-being, whether his body is healthy, strong, and free from illness, and whether he is at ease and his strength is as usual. Say this: 'The elder Anāthapiṇḍada bows at the feet of the Buddha and inquires after the World-Honored One's well-being: whether his body is healthy, strong, and free from illness, and whether he is at ease and his strength is as usual.' After you have inquired after the Buddha on my behalf, go to Venerable Śāriputra and bow on my behalf at his feet. Inquire after Venerable Śāriputra's well-being, whether his body is healthy, strong, and free from illness, and whether he is at ease and his strength is as usual. Say this: 'The elder Anāthapiṇḍada bows at the feet of Venerable Śāriputra and inquires after Venerable Śāriputra's well-being: whether his body is healthy, strong, and free from illness, and whether he is at ease and his strength is as usual.' Venerable Śāriputra! The elder Anāthapiṇḍada is gravely ill and now in critical condition. The elder Anāthapiṇḍada sincerely wishes to see Venerable Śāriputra, but his body is extremely weak and he is unable to come to see Venerable Śāriputra. Excellent! Venerable Śāriputra! Out of compassion, please go to the elder Anāthapiṇḍada's house."

尔时，长者给孤独疾病危笃，于是，长者给孤独告一使人：「汝往诣佛，为我稽首礼世尊足，问讯世尊，圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常耶？作如是语：『长者给孤独稽首佛足，问讯世尊：「圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常耶？」』汝既为我问讯佛已，往诣尊者舍梨子所，为我稽首礼彼足已，问讯尊者，圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常不？作如是语：『长者给孤独稽首尊者舍梨子足，问讯尊者：「圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常不？」尊者舍梨子！长者给孤独疾病极困，今至危笃，长者给孤独至心欲见尊者舍梨子，然体至羸乏，无力可来诣尊者舍梨子所。善哉！尊者舍梨子！为慈愍故，愿往至长者给孤独家。』」

Then the servant, having received the elder Anāthapiṇḍada's instructions, went to where the Buddha was, bowed at his feet, and stood to one side. He said, "World-Honored One! The elder Anāthapiṇḍada bows at the Buddha's feet and inquires after the World-Honored One's well-being: whether his body is healthy, strong, and free from illness, and whether he is at ease and his strength is as usual."

于是，使人受长者给孤独教已，往诣佛所，稽首礼足，却住一面，白曰：「世尊！长者给孤独稽首佛足，问讯世尊：『圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常耶？』」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the servant, "May the elder Anāthapiṇḍada be at ease and happy, and may the gods, humans, asuras, gandharvas, rākṣasas, and other various kinds of beings be at ease and happy."

尔时，世尊告使人曰：「令长者给孤独安隐快乐，令天及人、阿修罗、揵塔惒、罗刹及余种种身安隐快乐。」

Then the servant, having heard what the Buddha said, accepted it well and remembered it well. He bowed at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and departed. He went to where Venerable Śāriputra was, bowed at his feet, sat to one side, and said, "Venerable Śāriputra! The elder Anāthapiṇḍada bows at Venerable Śāriputra's feet and inquires after Venerable Śāriputra's well-being: whether his body is healthy, strong, and free from illness, and whether he is at ease and his strength is as usual. Venerable Śāriputra! The elder Anāthapiṇḍada is extremely ill and now in critical condition. The elder Anāthapiṇḍada sincerely wishes to see Venerable Śāriputra, but his body is so weak that he is unable to come to where Venerable Śāriputra is. Excellent! Venerable Śāriputra! Please go out of compassion to the elder Anāthapiṇḍada's house."

于是，使人闻佛所说，善受善持，稽首佛足，绕三匝而去，往诣尊者舍梨子所，稽首礼足，却坐一面，白曰：「尊者舍梨子！长者给孤独稽首尊者舍梨子足，问讯尊者：『圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常不？』尊者舍梨子！长者给孤独疾病极困，今至危笃，长者给孤独至心欲见尊者舍梨子，然体至羸乏，无力可来诣尊者舍梨子所。善哉！尊者舍梨子！为慈愍故，往诣长者给孤独家。」

Venerable Śāriputra then accepted it silently for his sake. Then the servant, knowing that Venerable Śāriputra had accepted it silently, immediately rose from his seat, bowed at his feet, circumambulated him three times, and departed.

尊者舍梨子即为彼故，默然而受。于是，使人知尊者舍梨子默然受已，即从坐起，稽首作礼，绕三匝而去。

Venerable Śāriputra, having passed the night, put on his robe in the early morning, took his alms bowl, and went to the house of the elder Anāthapiṇḍada. The elder Anāthapiṇḍada saw Venerable Śāriputra coming from afar, and upon seeing him, he immediately wanted to get up from his bed.

尊者舍梨子过夜平旦，著衣持钵，往诣长者给孤独家。长者给孤独遥见尊者舍梨子来，见已，便欲从床而起。

Venerable Śāriputra saw the elder wanting to get up from the bed, and he immediately stopped him, saying: "Elder, do not get up! Elder, do not get up! There are other beds. I will sit on another one myself."

尊者舍梨子见彼长者欲从床起，便止彼曰：「长者莫起！长者莫起！更有余床，我自别坐。」

Venerable Śāriputra then sat on the bed and asked: "Elder, how is your illness now? How much are you eating and drinking? Is the illness getting worse or better?"

尊者舍梨子即坐其床，坐已，问曰：「长者所患今复何似，饮食多少，疾苦转损，不至增耶？」

The elder replied: "The illness is very severe, I cannot eat or drink, and the illness is only getting worse, not better."

长者答曰：「所患至困，饮食不进，疾苦但增而不觉损。」

Venerable Śāriputra said: "Elder, do not be afraid! Do not be afraid! Why? If an ignorant ordinary person does not cultivate faith, when their body perishes and their life ends, they will go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. The elder does not have disbelief today, but only the highest faith. Because of the highest faith, the elder may eliminate suffering and give rise to supreme happiness. Because of the highest faith, he may attain the fruit of once-returning or non-returning. The elder has already attained the fruit of stream-entry.

尊者舍梨子告曰：「长者莫怖！长者莫怖！所以者何？若愚痴凡夫成就不信，身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中。长者今日无有不信，唯有上信，长者因上信故，或灭苦痛，生极快乐，因上信故，或得斯陀含果，或阿那含果，长者本已得须陀洹。

Elder, do not be afraid! Do not be afraid! Why? If an ignorant ordinary person because of bad precepts, when their body perishes and their life ends, they will go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. The elder does not have bad precepts, but only good precepts. Because of the good precepts, the elder may eliminate suffering and give rise to supreme happiness. Because of the good precepts, he may attain the fruit of once-returning or non-returning. The elder has already attained the fruit of stream-entry.

「长者莫怖！长者莫怖！所以者何？若愚痴凡夫因恶戒故，身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中。长者无有恶戒，唯有善戒，长者因善戒故，或灭苦痛，生极快乐，因善戒故，或得斯陀含果，或阿那含果，长者本已得须陀洹。

Elder, do not be afraid! Do not be afraid! Why? If an ignorant ordinary person because of not hearing much, when their body perishes and their life ends, they will go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. The elder does not not hear much, but only hears much. Because of hearing much, the elder may eliminate suffering and give rise to supreme happiness. Because of hearing much, he may attain the fruit of once-returning or non-returning. The elder has already attained the fruit of stream-entry.

「长者莫怖！长者莫怖！所以者何？若愚痴凡夫因不多闻，身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中。长者无不多闻，唯有多闻，长者因多闻故，或灭苦痛，生极快乐，因多闻故，或得斯陀含果，或阿那含果，长者本已得须陀洹。

Elder, do not be afraid! Do not be afraid! Why? If an ignorant ordinary person because of stinginess and greed, when their body perishes and their life ends, they will go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. The elder does not have stinginess and greed, but only generosity. Because of generosity, the elder may eliminate suffering and give rise to supreme happiness. Because of generosity, he may attain the fruit of once-returning or non-returning. The elder has already attained the fruit of stream-entry.

「长者莫怖！长者莫怖！所以者何？若愚痴凡夫因悭贪故，身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中。长者无有悭贪，唯有惠施，长者因惠施故，或灭苦痛，生极快乐，因惠施故，或得斯陀含果，或阿那含果，长者本已得须陀洹。

Elder, do not be afraid! Do not be afraid! Why? If an ignorant ordinary person because of bad wisdom, when their body perishes and their life ends, they will go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. The elder does not have bad wisdom, but only good wisdom. Because of good wisdom, the elder may eliminate suffering and give rise to supreme happiness. Because of good wisdom, he may attain the fruit of once-returning or non-returning. The elder has already attained the fruit of stream-entry.

「长者莫怖！长者莫怖！所以者何？若愚痴凡夫因恶慧故，身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中。长者无有恶慧，唯有善慧，长者因善慧故，或灭苦痛，生极快乐，因善慧故，或得斯陀含果，或阿那含果，长者本已得须陀洹。

Elder, do not be afraid! Do not be afraid! Why? If an ignorant ordinary person because of wrong views, when their body perishes and their life ends, they will go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. The elder does not have wrong views, but only right views. Because of right views, the elder may eliminate suffering and give rise to supreme happiness. Because of right views, he may attain the fruit of once-returning or non-returning. The elder has already attained the fruit of stream-entry.

「长者莫怖！长者莫怖！所以者何？若愚痴凡夫因邪见故，身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中。长者无有邪见，唯有正见，长者因正见故，或灭苦痛，生极快乐，因正见故，或得斯陀含果，或阿那含果，长者本已得须陀洹。

Elder, do not be afraid! Do not be afraid! Why? If an ignorant ordinary person because of wrong intentions, when their body perishes and their life ends, they will go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. The elder does not have wrong intentions, but only right intentions. Because of right intentions, the elder may eliminate suffering and give rise to supreme happiness. Because of right intentions, he may attain the fruit of once-returning or non-returning. The elder has already attained the fruit of stream-entry.

「长者莫怖！长者莫怖！所以者何？若愚痴凡夫因邪志故，身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中。长者无有邪志，唯有正志，长者因正志故，或灭苦痛，生极快乐，因正志故，或得斯陀含果，或阿那含果，长者本已得须陀洹。

Elder, do not be afraid! Do not be afraid! Why? If an ignorant ordinary person because of wrong understanding, when their body perishes and their life ends, they will go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. The elder does not have wrong understanding, but only right understanding. Because of right understanding, the elder may eliminate suffering and give rise to supreme happiness. Because of right understanding, he may attain the fruit of once-returning or non-returning. The elder has already attained the fruit of stream-entry.

「长者莫怖！长者莫怖！所以者何？若愚痴凡夫因邪解故，身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中。长者无有邪解，唯有正解，长者因正解故，或灭苦痛，生极快乐，因正解故，或得斯陀含果，或阿那含果，长者本已得须陀洹。

Elder, do not be afraid! Do not be afraid! Why? If an ignorant ordinary person because of wrong liberation, when their body perishes and their life ends, they will go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. The elder does not have wrong liberation, but only right liberation. Because of right liberation, the elder may eliminate suffering and give rise to supreme happiness. Because of right liberation, he may attain the fruit of once-returning or non-returning. The elder has already attained the fruit of stream-entry.

「长者莫怖！长者莫怖！所以者何？若愚痴凡夫因邪脱故，身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中。长者无有邪脱，唯有正脱，长者因正脱故，或灭苦痛，生极快乐，因正脱故，或得斯陀含果，或阿那含果，长者本已得须陀洹。

Elder, do not be afraid! Do not be afraid! Why? If an ignorant ordinary person because of wrong knowledge, when their body perishes and their life ends, they will go to a bad place and be reborn in hell. The elder does not have wrong knowledge, but only right knowledge. Because of right knowledge, the elder may eliminate suffering and give rise to supreme happiness. Because of right knowledge, he may attain the fruit of once-returning or non-returning. The elder has already attained the fruit of stream-entry.

「长者莫怖！长者莫怖！所以者何？若愚痴凡夫因邪智故，身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中。长者无有邪智，唯有正智，长者因正智故，或灭苦痛，生极快乐，因正智故，或得斯陀含果，或阿那含果，长者本已得须陀洹。」

Then the elder's illness was immediately cured and he recovered as before. He got up from his bed and sat down, praising Venerable Sāriputta, saying: "Excellent! Excellent! To teach the Dharma to the sick. How wonderful! How extraordinary! Venerable Sāriputta! When I heard the teaching on the Dharma for the sick, my suffering was immediately eliminated and I gave rise to supreme happiness. Venerable Sāriputta! My illness is now cured and I have recovered as before. Venerable Sāriputta! In the past, I had some business and stayed overnight in the house of an elder in Rājagṛha. At that time, the elder was going to offer a meal to the Buddha and the bhikṣus the next day. The elder, having passed the night and approaching dawn, instructed his grandchildren, servants, and attendants, saying: 'You should all get up early and prepare together.' They each received the instruction and together prepared the kitchen, providing various delicious dishes. The elder himself arranged the high seats, decorating them in countless ways.

于是，长者病即得差，平复如故，从卧起坐，叹尊者舍梨子曰：「善哉！善哉！为病说法。甚奇！甚特！尊者舍梨子！我闻教化病法，苦痛即灭，生极快乐。尊者舍梨子！我今病差，平复如故。尊者舍梨子！我往昔时，少有所为，至王舍城寄宿一长者家。时，彼长者明当饭佛及比丘众。时，彼长者过夜向晓，教勅儿孙、奴使、眷属：『汝等早起，当共严办。』彼各受教，共设厨宰，供办肴馔、种种腆美，长者躬自敷置高座，无量严饰。

Venerable Sāriputta! Having seen this, I thought: 'Is this elder having a wedding, a marriage ceremony, inviting the king, summoning ministers, or holding a feast and making great offerings?' Venerable Sāriputta! Having thought this, I then asked the elder: 'Are you having a wedding, a marriage ceremony, inviting the king, summoning ministers, or holding a feast and making great offerings?' At that time, the elder replied to me, saying: 'I have no wedding, am not having a marriage ceremony, am not inviting the king or summoning ministers, but am only holding a feast and making great offerings. Tomorrow I will offer a meal to the Buddha and the bhikṣus.

「尊者舍梨子！我既见已，便作是念：『今此长者为婚姻事，为迎妇节会，为请国王，为呼大臣，为作斋会施设大施耶？』尊者舍梨子！我既念已，便问长者：『汝为婚姻事，为迎妇节会，为请国王，为呼大臣，为作斋会施设大施耶？』时，彼长者而答我曰：『吾无婚姻事，亦不迎妇，不为节会，不请国王及呼大臣，但为斋会施设大施，明当饭佛及比丘众。』

Venerable Sāriputta! I had never heard the name Buddha before. When I heard it, my body hair stood on end. I then asked: 'Elder, when you speak of the Buddha, what is meant by Buddha?' At that time, the elder replied to me, saying: 'Have you not heard? A son of the Śākya clan, having renounced his Śākya clan, shaved off his beard and hair, donned the saffron robes, with utmost faith, left home for homelessness, and studied the path, attained unsurpassed perfect enlightenment. This is called Buddha.' I further asked: 'Elder, when you speak of the assembly, what is meant by assembly?' At that time, the elder again replied to me, saying: 'There are various clans, different names and lineages, who have shaved off their beards and hair, donned the saffron robes, with utmost faith, left home for homelessness, and study the path under the Buddha. This is called the assembly. This Buddha and the assembly are whom I have invited.' Venerable Sāriputta! I then asked the elder: 'Where is the World-Honored One now? I wish to go and see him.' At that time, the elder again replied to me, saying: 'The World-Honored One is now in the Bamboo Grove Kalandakanivāpa near Rājagṛha. You may go as you wish.'

「尊者舍梨子！我未曾闻佛名，闻已举身毛竖，即复问曰：『长者说佛，何名为佛？』时，彼长者而答我曰：『君不闻乎？有释种子舍释宗族，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，得无上等正觉，是名为佛。』我复问曰：『长者说众，何名为众？』时，彼长者复答我曰：『有若干姓异名异] cf.［丽】族，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家，从佛学道，是名为众，此佛及众，吾之所请。』尊者舍梨子！我即复问彼长者曰：『世尊于今为在何处？我欲往见。』时，彼长者复答我曰：『世尊今在此王舍城竹林加兰哆园，欲往随意。』

Venerable Sāriputta! I thought: 'If I can understand quickly, I will go quickly to see the Buddha.' Venerable Sāriputta! At that time, I was wholeheartedly wishing to go and see the Buddha. I immediately had the thought of daytime brightness in the night, and then left the elder's house and went to the city gate. At that time, there were two gatekeepers at the city gate. One was in charge of the early night, allowing incoming travelers without obstruction. The other was in charge of the later night, allowing outgoing travelers without obstruction. Venerable Sāriputta! I then thought: 'It is not yet dawn.' Why is that? Because at the city gate there were two gatekeepers. One was in charge of the early night, allowing incoming travelers without obstruction. The other was in charge of the later night, allowing outgoing travelers without obstruction. Venerable Sāriputta! Not long after leaving the city gate, the light disappeared and it became dark again. Venerable Sāriputta! I was then terrified, with my body hair standing on end, fearing that humans or non-humans would come and disturb me.

「尊者舍梨子！我作是念：『若速晓者，疾往见佛。』尊者舍梨子！我时至心欲往见佛，即于其夜生昼明想，便从长者家出，往至城息门。是时，城息门中有二直士，一直初夜，外客使入，不令有碍，一直后夜，若客使出，亦不作碍。尊者舍梨子！我复作是念：『夜尚未晓。』所以者何？城息门中有二直士，一直初夜，外客使入，不令有碍，一直后夜，若客使出，亦不作碍。尊者舍梨子！出城息门，出外不久，明灭还暗。尊者舍梨子！我便恐怖，举身毛竖，莫令人、非人来触娆我。

At that time, there was a god at the city gate who came from Rājagṛha to the Bamboo Grove Kalandakanivāpa, illuminating everywhere with his light, and said to me: 'Elder, do not be afraid! Do not be afraid! Why is that? I was your friend in a past life, named Secret Vessel, and we were very fond of each other when we were young. Elder! I once went to Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, bowed my head at his feet, sat to one side, and Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana taught me the Dharma, inspiring me with longing and bringing me to fulfillment and joy. Having taught me the Dharma with immeasurable skillful means, inspiring me with longing and bringing me to fulfillment and joy, he granted me the three refuges and the five precepts. Elder! Because of the three refuges and the five precepts, when my body perished and my life ended, I was reborn in the Heaven of the Four Heavenly Kings and dwell in this city gate. Go quickly, Elder! Go quickly! It is better to go than to stay.' That god encouraged me and spoke this verse:

「时，城息门而有一天，从王舍城至竹林加兰哆园，光明普照，来语我言：『长者莫怖！长者莫怖！所以者何？我本前世是汝朋友，名密器，年少极相爱念。长者！我本昔时往诣尊者大目干连所，稽首礼足，却坐一面，尊者大目干连为我说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为我说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，赐三自归，见授五戒。长者！我因三归，受持五戒，身坏命终，生四天王天，住此城息门中。长者速去，长者速去，去实胜住。』彼天劝我而说颂曰：

He said: 'One hundred horses, one hundred ministers, one hundred women, one
hundred chariots filled with precious treasures, going to the Buddha for one
step, is not worth one-sixteenth. One hundred best white elephants, with golden
and silver saddles and bridles, going to the Buddha for one step
is not worth one-sixteenth. One hundred women of perfect appearance, adorned with
necklaces and flowers, going to the Buddha for one step, is not
worth one-sixteenth. The revered one of the Wheel-Turning King, the foremost of
the seven treasures, going to the Buddha for one step, is not worth one-sixteenth.

「『得马百臣女，　　车百满珍宝，往诣佛一步，　　不当十六分。白象百最上，　　金银鞍勒被，往诣佛一步，　　不当十六分。女百色端正，　　璎珞花严身，往诣佛一步，　　不当十六分。转轮王所敬，　　玉女宝第一，往诣佛一步，　　不当十六分。』

"After the god spoke the verse, he further encouraged me, saying: 'Elder, go quickly! Go quickly! It is better to go than to stay.' Venerable Sāriputta, I then thought: 'The Buddha is revered and virtuous, the Dharma and the community of monks are also revered and virtuous. Why is that? Even the gods want to see them.'"

「天说颂已，而复劝曰：『长者速去！长者速去！去实胜住。』尊者舍梨子！我复作是念：『佛尊祐德，法及比丘众亦尊祐德。所以者何？乃至于天，亦欲使见。』

Venerable Sāriputta, I went to the Bamboo Grove Kalandakanivāpa by this light. At that time, the World-Honored One, in the early morning, came out of the meditation room, walked in the open air, and waited for me. Venerable Sāriputta, I saw from afar that the Buddha was upright and handsome, like the moon among stars, shining brightly, dazzling like a golden mountain, with perfect marks and majestic spiritual power, with calm faculties and no obstructions, accomplished in taming and pacifying the mind. Seeing this, I was delighted and went to where the Buddha was. I bowed at his feet, followed him in walking, and asked about his well-being with a verse in the manner of an elder:

「尊者舍梨子！我因此光明往至竹林加兰哆园。尔时，世尊夜其向旦，从禅室出，露地经行而待于我。尊者舍梨子！我遥见佛端正姝好，犹星中月，光耀𬀩晔，晃若金山，相好具足，威神巍巍，诸根寂定，无有蔽碍，成就调御，息心静默。见已欢喜，前诣佛所，接足作礼，随佛经行，以长者法说颂问讯：

'Is the World-Honored One sleeping peacefully, sleeping well, like
a brahman who has attained nirvana, not defiled by
desires? Having abandoned all wishes, attaining peace, the mind
is free from afflictions and heat, happily and joyfully sleeping.

「『世尊寐安隐，　　至竟眠快耶？如梵志灭度，　　以不染于欲。舍离一切愿，　　逮得至安隐，心除无烦热，　　自乐欢喜眠。』

Then the World-Honored One went to the end of the walking path, spread out his sitting mat, and sat down cross-legged. Venerable Sāriputta, I bowed at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and the World-Honored One taught me the Dharma, inspiring and gladdening me with countless skillful means. Having taught me the Dharma, inspiring and gladdening me with countless skillful means, the World-Honored One, knowing that my mind was joyful, complete, pliant, receptive, uplifted, one-pointed, free from doubt, and unobscured, with the ability to receive the true teaching, taught me the true essence as taught by all Buddhas. The World-Honored One taught me the truth of suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path. Venerable Sāriputta, I saw the four noble truths of suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path right there in that seat, just as white cloth is easily dyed. In the same way, I saw the four noble truths of suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path right there in that seat.

「于是，世尊即便往至经行道头，敷尼师檀，结加趺坐。尊者舍梨子！我礼佛足，却坐一面，世尊为我说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为我说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已。如诸佛法，先说端正法，闻者欢悦，谓，说施、说戒、说生天法，毁呰欲为灾患，生死为秽，称叹无欲为妙道品白净。世尊为我说如是法已，佛知我有欢喜心、具足心、柔软心、堪耐心、升上心、一向心、无疑心、无盖心，有能有力，堪受正法，谓如诸佛所说正要，世尊即为我说苦、习、灭、道。尊者舍梨子！我即于坐中见四圣谛苦、习、灭、道，犹如白素易染为色，我亦如是，即于坐中见四圣谛苦、习、灭、道。

Venerable Sāriputta, I saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, realized the pure Dharma, cut off doubt, crossed over uncertainty, and became free from perplexity. There is no other teacher, I follow none other, and I am without doubt. I have attained the fruit of the path and have attained fearlessness in the teachings of the World-Honored One. I then rose from my seat, bowed to the Buddha, and said: 'World-Honored One, I now take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha of bhikṣus. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept me as a lay follower, from this day forward for as long as I live.' Venerable Sāriputta, I then joined my palms and said: 'World-Honored One, I wish to invite you to spend the summer retreat in Śrāvastī, together with the Sangha of bhikṣus.' The Buddha then asked me: 'What is your name? How do the people of Śrāvastī address you?' I replied: 'My name is Sudatta, and because I provide for the orphaned and destitute, the people of Śrāvastī call me Anāthapiṇḍada.' The World-Honored One then asked me again: 'Are there any dwellings in Śrāvastī?' I replied: 'There are no dwellings in Śrāvastī.' The World-Honored One then told me: 'Householder, you should know that if there are dwellings, bhikṣus can come and go and stay.' I then said: 'Yes, World-Honored One, I will build dwellings so that bhikṣus can come and go and stay in Śrāvastī. I only wish that the World-Honored One would appoint someone to assist me.' At that time, the World-Honored One appointed Venerable Sāriputta to assist me.

「尊者舍梨子！我已见法得法，觉白净法，断疑度惑，更无余尊，不复从他，无有犹豫，已住果证，于世尊法得无所畏，即从坐起，为佛作礼：『世尊！我今自归于佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。』尊者舍梨子！我即叉手白曰：『世尊！愿受我请，于舍卫国而受夏坐及比丘众。』时，佛问我：『汝名何等？舍卫国人呼汝云何？』我即答曰：『我名须达哆，以我供给诸孤独者，是故舍卫国人呼我为给孤独。』尔时，世尊复问我曰：『舍卫国中有房舍未？』我复答曰：『舍卫国中无有房舍。』尔时，世尊而告我曰：『长者当知！若有房舍，比丘可得往来，可得住止。』我复白曰：『唯然。世尊！我当如是为起房舍，比丘可得往来，于舍卫国可得住止，唯愿世尊差一佐助。』尔时，世尊即差尊者舍梨子，遣尊者舍梨子令见佐助。

"At that time, having heard the Buddha's words, I accepted them well and remembered them well. I then rose from my seat, bowed to the Buddha, circumambulated him three times, and departed. Having completed what was to be done in Rājagṛha, I went together with Venerable Sāriputta to Śrāvastī. Without entering the city of Śrāvastī or returning home, I walked around the outskirts of the city, looking for a place that was pleasant to come and go, not noisy during the day, quiet at night, free from mosquitoes and flies, without flies and fleas, not cold or hot, suitable for building dwellings to offer to the Buddha and the Sangha. Venerable Sāriputta, I only saw the pleasure garden of Prince Jeta as being pleasant to come and go, not noisy during the day, quiet at night, free from mosquitoes and flies, without flies and fleas, not cold or hot. Having seen this, I thought: 'Only this place is good, suitable for building dwellings to offer to the Buddha and the Sangha.'"

「我于尔时闻佛所说，善受善持，即从坐起，为佛作礼，绕三匝而去。于王舍城所作已讫，与尊者舍梨子俱往至舍卫国，不入舍卫城亦不归家，便于城外周遍行地，为于何处往来极好，昼不喧閙，夜则寂静，无有蚊虻，亦无蝇蚤，不寒不热，可立房舍施佛及众。尊者舍梨子！我时唯见童子胜园往来极好，昼不喧閙，夜则寂静，无有蚊虻，亦无蝇蚤，不寒不热。我见此已，便作是念：『唯此处好，可立房舍施佛及众。』

"Venerable Sāriputta, I then entered the city of Śrāvastī, but did not return home. I went directly to Prince Jeta and said: 'Prince, can you sell this garden to me?' At that time, Prince Jeta said to me: 'Householder, you should know that I will not sell the garden.' In this way, I asked him three times, saying: 'Prince, can you sell this garden to me?' At that time, Prince Jeta also said to me three times: 'I will not sell the garden, even for a hundred million.' I then said: 'Prince, you have already determined the price. You should just take the money.' Venerable Sāriputta, I and Prince Jeta argued back and forth, saying either that the price was determined or that it was not determined. We then went together to the great court of justice in the city of Śrāvastī to decide this matter. At that time, the great judge of the city of Śrāvastī said to Prince Jeta: 'Prince, you have already determined the price. You should just take the money.'"

「尊者舍梨子！我于尔时入舍卫国，竟不还家，便先往诣童子胜所，白曰：『童子！可卖此园持与我耶？』尔时，童子便语我曰：『长者当知，吾不卖园。』如是，再三白曰：『童子！可卖此园持与我耶？』尔时，童子亦复再三而语我曰：『吾不卖园，至亿亿布满。』我即白曰：『童子今已决断价数，但当取钱。』尊者舍梨子！我与童子或言断价，或言不断，大共𫍛讼，即便俱往至舍卫国大决断处判论此事。时，舍卫国大决断人语童子胜曰：『童子已自决断价数，但当取钱。』

Venerable Sāriputta, I then entered the city of Śrāvastī and returned home to fetch the money. I carried it out in elephants, horses, carts, and on the backs of men. I spread out the hundred million coins, but there was still a small area that was not covered. Venerable Sāriputta, I thought: 'What treasure should I take, not too large or too small, to cover this remaining area?' At that time, Prince Jeta said to me: 'Householder, if you regret it, the money will return to you and the garden will be mine.' I said to Prince Jeta: 'I really do not regret it.' But I was thinking: 'What treasure should I take, not too large or too small, to cover this remaining area?' At that time, Prince Jeta thought: 'The Buddha must be greatly honored and have great virtue and blessings, and the Dharma and the Sangha of monks must also be greatly honored and have great virtue and blessings. Why is this? It is because he can cause the householder to make such great offerings and be so light with his wealth. I should now build a gatehouse right here and offer it to the Buddha and the Sangha.' At that time, Prince Jeta said to me: 'Householder, stop! Do not spread any more money here. I will build a gatehouse right here and offer it to the Buddha and the Sangha.' Venerable Sāriputta, out of compassion, I then gave this place to Prince Jeta.

「尊者舍梨子！我即入舍卫国，还家取钱，以象马车举负辇载，出亿亿布地，少处未遍。尊者舍梨子！我作是念：『当取何藏，不大不小，可此余处持来布满？』时，童子胜便语我曰：『长者若悔，钱自相归，园地还吾。』我语童子：『实不悔也。』但自思念：『当取何藏，不大不小，可此余处持来满耳？』时，童子胜便作是念：『佛必大尊，有大德祐，法及比丘众亦必大尊，有大德祐。所以者何？乃令长者施设大施，轻财乃尔。吾今宁可即于此处造立门屋施佛及众。』时，童子胜便语我曰：『长者且止！莫复出钱布此处也，吾于此处造立门屋施佛及众。』尊者舍梨子！我为慈愍故，即以此处与童子胜。

"Venerable Sāriputta, I then built sixteen large houses and sixty huts during the summer. Venerable Sāriputta, you saw and assisted in the construction. Venerable Sāriputta, your teaching on the Dharma for curing illness is truly wonderful and extraordinary! After hearing this teaching on curing illness, my severe illness was immediately cured and I experienced great happiness. Venerable Sāriputta, I am now free from illness and extremely at ease. I wish that Venerable Sāriputta would take a meal here." At that time, Venerable Sāriputta silently accepted the invitation.

「尊者舍梨子！我即于此夏起十六大屋、六十拘𫄨。尊者舍梨子时见佐助，然尊者舍梨子说教化病法，甚奇！甚特！我闻此教化病法已，极重疾苦即得除愈，生极快乐。尊者舍梨子！我今无病，极得安隐，愿尊者舍梨子于此饭食。」时，尊者舍梨子默然受请。

Then the householder, knowing that Venerable Sāriputta had silently accepted, rose from his seat, personally served water for washing, and offered various delicious and pure food and drink, serving it with his own hands until he was satisfied. After the meal, he picked up the utensils, served water for washing, spread a small seat, and sat separately to listen to the Dharma. After the householder sat down, Venerable Sāriputta spoke the Dharma for him, inspiring and uplifting him, bringing him to accomplishment and joy. With immeasurable skillful means, he spoke the Dharma for him, inspiring and uplifting him, bringing him to accomplishment and joy. Having done so, he rose from his seat and departed.

于是长者知尊者舍梨子默然受已，即从坐起，自行澡水，以极美净妙种种丰饶食噉含消，手自斟酌，令得充满，食讫，举器行澡水竟，敷一小床，别坐听法。长者坐已，尊者舍梨子为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，从坐起去。

At that time, the World-Honored One was teaching the Dharma surrounded by a great assembly in front and behind. The World-Honored One saw Venerable Sāriputta coming from afar and told the bhikṣus, "Bhikṣus, Venerable Sāriputta is wise, quick-witted, sharp-witted, intelligent, broad-minded, deep-minded, able to transcend, discerning, eloquent, and has the wisdom of skillful means. Venerable Sāriputta has accomplished true wisdom. Why is that? I have briefly spoken of the four types of srota-āpannas, and Venerable Sāriputta has extensively explained them in ten ways to the householder Anāthapiṇḍada."

是时，世尊无量大众前后围绕而为说法，世尊遥见尊者舍梨子来，告诸比丘：「舍梨子比丘聪慧、速慧、捷慧、利慧、广慧、深慧、出要慧、明达慧、辩才慧，舍梨子比丘成就实慧。所以者何？我所略说四种须陀洹，舍梨子比丘为长者给孤独十种广说来。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
