MĀ 35

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 4 未曾有法品

35. To the Asura （三五）阿修罗经

Parallel to: AN 8.19 / EĀ 42.4

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in Vīrāna and staying in the Yellow Reed Grove.

一时，佛游鞞兰若，在黄芦园。

At that time, the Asura King Vāruṇa and the Asura son Maudgalyāyana, with majestic appearance and radiant splendor, went to where the Buddha was in the early morning, bowed at the feet of the World-Honored One, and stood to one side.

尔时，婆罗逻阿修罗王、牟梨遮阿修罗子，色像巍巍，光耀𬀩晔，夜将向旦，往诣佛所，礼世尊足，却住一面。

The World-Honored One asked, "Vāruṇa! Do the Asuras in the great ocean not have any decline in Asura lifespan, Asura appearance, Asura happiness, and Asura strength? Do the Asuras enjoy the great ocean?"

世尊问曰：「婆罗逻！大海中阿修罗无有衰退阿修罗寿、阿修罗色、阿修罗乐、阿修罗力，诸阿修罗乐大海中耶？」

The Asura King Vāruṇa and the Asura son Maudgalyāyana replied, "World-Honored One! We Asuras in the great ocean do not have any decline in Asura lifespan, Asura appearance, Asura happiness, and Asura strength. We Asuras enjoy the great ocean."

婆罗逻阿修罗王、牟梨遮阿修罗子答曰：「世尊！我大海中诸阿修罗无有衰退于阿修罗寿、阿修罗色、阿修罗乐、阿修罗力，诸阿修罗乐大海中。」

The World-Honored One further asked, "Vāruṇa! How many unprecedented phenomena are there in the great ocean that make the Asuras enjoy it when they see them?"

世尊复问曰：「婆罗逻！大海中有几未曾有法，令诸阿修罗见已乐中。」

Vāruṇa replied, "World-Honored One! There are eight unprecedented phenomena in my great ocean that make the Asuras enjoy it when they see them. What are the eight? World-Honored One! From the bottom to the top, my great ocean gradually widens and evenly turns upward to form the shore. The water is always full and never overflows. World-Honored One! If my great ocean gradually widens from the bottom to the top, evenly turns upward to form the shore, and the water is always full and never overflows, this is called the first unprecedented phenomenon in my great ocean, which makes the Asuras enjoy it when they see it.

婆罗逻答曰：「世尊！我大海中有八未曾有法，令诸阿修罗见已乐中。云何为八？世尊！我大海从下至上，周回渐广，均调转上，以成于岸，其水常满，未曾流出。世尊！若我大海从下至上，周回渐广，均调转上，以成于岸，其水常满，未曾流出者，是谓我大海中第一未曾有法，诸阿修罗见已乐中。

Furthermore, World-Honored One! The tides in my great ocean never miss their time. World-Honored One! If the tides in my great ocean never miss their time, this is called the second unprecedented phenomenon in my great ocean, which makes the Asuras enjoy it when they see it.

「复次，世尊！我大海潮未曾失时。世尊！若我大海潮未曾失时者，是谓我大海中第二未曾有法，诸阿修罗见已乐中。

Furthermore, World-Honored One! The water in my great ocean is very deep and bottomless, extremely wide and boundless. World-Honored One! If the water in my great ocean is very deep and bottomless, extremely wide and boundless, this is called the third unprecedented phenomenon in my great ocean, which makes the Asuras enjoy it when they see it.

「复次，世尊！我大海水甚深无底，极广无边。世尊！若我大海甚深无底，极广无边者，是谓我大海中第三未曾有法，诸阿修罗见已乐中。

Furthermore, World-Honored One! The water in my great ocean is salty and all has the same taste. World-Honored One! If the water in my great ocean is salty and all has the same taste, this is called the fourth unprecedented phenomenon in my great ocean, which makes the Asuras enjoy it when they see it.

「复次，世尊！我大海水咸，皆同一味。世尊！若我大海水咸，皆同一味者，是谓我大海中第四未曾有法，诸阿修罗见已乐中。

Furthermore, World-Honored One! There are many precious treasures in my great ocean, countless rare and wonderful things, various precious gems, filling it. The names of the precious treasures are gold, silver, crystal, lapis lazuli, maṇi, pearls, green jade, white shells, conch shells, coral, tiger-eye, agate, amber, red stone, and moonstone. World-Honored One! If there are many precious treasures in my great ocean, countless rare and wonderful things, various precious gems, filling it, and the names of the precious treasures are gold, silver, crystal, lapis lazuli, maṇi, pearls, green jade, white shells, conch shells, coral, tiger-eye, agate, amber, red stone, and moonstone, this is called the fifth unprecedented phenomenon in my great ocean, which makes the Asuras enjoy it when they see it.

「复次，世尊！我大海中多有珍宝，无量𪻺异，种种珍琦，充满其中，珍宝名者，谓金、银、水精、琉璃、摩尼、真珠、碧玉、白珂、螺璧、珊瑚、虎珀、马瑙、瑇瑁、赤石、琁珠。世尊！若我大海中多有珍宝，无量𪻺异、种种珍琦，充满其中，珍宝名者，谓金、银、水精、琉璃、摩尼、真珠、碧玉、白珂、螺璧、珊瑚、虎珀、马瑙、瑇瑁、赤石、琁珠者，是谓我大海中第五未曾有法，诸阿修罗见已乐中。

Furthermore, World-Honored One! There are great deities dwelling in my great ocean, and the names of the great deities are Asuras, Gandharvas, Rākṣasas, fish, makaras, turtles, alligators, varāha, timi, timiṅgila, and timiṅgila. Furthermore, in the great ocean, there are very strange and extraordinary beings with bodies a hundred yojanas long, two hundred yojanas long, three hundred yojanas long, up to seven hundred yojanas long, all dwelling in the ocean. World-Honored One! If there are great deities dwelling in the great ocean, and the names of the great deities are Asuras, Gandharvas, Rākṣasas, fish, makaras, turtles, alligators, varāha, timi, timiṅgila, and timiṅgila. Furthermore, in the great ocean, there are very strange and extraordinary beings with bodies a hundred yojanas long, two hundred yojanas long, three hundred yojanas long, up to seven hundred yojanas long, all dwelling in the ocean, this is called the sixth unprecedented phenomenon in my great ocean, which makes the Asuras enjoy it when they see it.

「复次，世尊！我大海中大神所居，大神名者，谓阿修罗、干塔惒、罗刹、鱼摩竭、龟、鼍、婆留泥、帝麑、帝麑伽罗、提帝麑伽罗。复次，大海中甚奇！甚特！众生身体有百由延，有二百由延，有] cf.［丽】三百由延，有至七百由延，身皆居海中。世尊！若大海中大神所居，大神名者，谓阿修罗、干塔惒、罗刹、鱼摩竭、龟、鼍、婆留泥、帝麑、帝麑伽罗、提帝麑伽罗。复次，大海中甚奇！甚特！众生身体有百由延，有二百由延，有三百由延，有至七百由延，身皆居海中者，是谓我大海中第六未曾有法，诸阿修罗见已乐中。

Furthermore, World-Honored One! My great ocean is pure and does not accept corpses. If there is a death, the wind blows it to the shore overnight. World-Honored One! If my great ocean is pure and does not accept corpses. If there is a death, the wind blows it to the shore overnight, this is called the seventh unprecedented phenomenon in my great ocean, which makes the Asuras enjoy it when they see it.

「复次，世尊！我大海清净，不受死尸。若有命终者，过夜风便吹著岸上。世尊！若我大海清净，不受死尸。若有命终者，过夜风便吹著岸上者，是谓我大海中第七未曾有法，诸阿修罗见已乐中。

Furthermore, World-Honored One! There are five great rivers in Jambudvīpa that enter my great ocean, namely the Ganges, the Yāmuna, the Sarabhū, the Airāvati, and the Mahī. Having entered, they each abandon their original names and are all called the great ocean. World-Honored One! If there are five great rivers in Jambudvīpa that enter my great ocean, namely the Ganges, the Yāmuna, the Sarabhū, the Airāvati, and the Mahī. Having entered, they each abandon their original names and are all called the great ocean, this is called the eighth unprecedented phenomenon in my great ocean, which makes the Asuras enjoy it when they see it.

「复次，世尊！我大海阎浮洲中有五大河，一曰恒伽，二曰摇尤那，三曰舍牢浮，四曰阿夷罗婆提，五曰摩企，悉入大海，既入中已，各舍本名，皆曰大海。世尊！若我大海阎浮洲中有五大河，一曰恒伽，二曰摇尤那，三曰舍牢浮，四曰阿夷罗婆提，五曰摩企，悉入大海，既入中已，各舍本名，皆曰大海者，是谓我大海中第八未曾有法，诸阿修罗见已乐中。

"World-Honored One! These are the eight unprecedented phenomena in my great ocean, which make the Asuras enjoy it when they see it. World-Honored One! In the true Dharma and Vinaya of the Buddha, how many unprecedented phenomena are there that make the bhikṣus enjoy it when they see it?"

「世尊！是谓我大海中有八未曾有法，诸阿修罗见已乐中。世尊！于佛正法、律中有几未曾有法，令诸比丘见已乐中。」

The World-Honored One replied, "Brahman! In my true Dharma and Vinaya, there are also eight unprecedented phenomena that make the bhikṣus enjoy it when they see it. What are the eight? Brahman! Just as the great ocean gradually widens from bottom to top, evenly turning upward to form the shore, its water is always full and never overflows. Brahman! My true Dharma and Vinaya are also like this, gradually practicing, gradually studying, gradually exhausting, and gradually teaching. Brahman! If in my true Dharma and Vinaya, there is gradual practice, gradual study, gradual exhaustion, and gradual teaching, this is called the first unprecedented phenomenon in my true Dharma and Vinaya, which makes the bhikṣus enjoy it when they see it.

世尊答曰：「婆罗逻！我正法、律中亦有八未曾有法，令诸比丘见已乐中。云何为八？婆罗逻！如大海从下至上，周回渐广，均调转上，以成于岸，其水常满，未曾流出。婆罗逻！我正法、律亦复如是，渐作渐学，渐尽渐教。婆罗逻！若我正法、律中渐作渐学，渐尽渐教者，是谓我正法、律中第一未曾有法，令诸比丘见已乐中。

Furthermore, Brahman! Just as the tides of the great ocean never miss the time, my true Dharma and Vinaya are also like this. For bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, and upāsikās, I establish precepts. The sons and daughters of noble families will not violate the precepts until the end of their lives. Brahman! If in my true Dharma and Vinaya, I establish precepts for bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, and upāsikās, and the sons and daughters of noble families will not violate the precepts until the end of their lives, this is called the second unprecedented phenomenon in my true Dharma and Vinaya, which makes the bhikṣus enjoy it when they see it.

「复次，婆罗逻！如大海潮，未曾失时。婆罗逻！我正法、律亦复如是，为比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆私施设禁戒，诸族姓子乃至命尽，终不犯戒。婆罗逻！若我正法、律中为比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆私施设禁戒，诸族姓子乃至命尽，终不犯戒者，是谓我正法、律中第二未曾有法，令诸比丘见已乐中。

Furthermore, Brahman! Just as the water of the great ocean is very deep and bottomless, extremely wide and boundless, my true Dharma and Vinaya are also like this. The dharmas are very deep, very deep and bottomless, extremely wide and boundless. Brahman! If in my true Dharma and Vinaya, the dharmas are very deep, very deep and bottomless, extremely wide and boundless, this is called the third unprecedented phenomenon in my true Dharma and Vinaya, which makes the bhikṣus enjoy it when they see it.

「复次，婆罗逻！如大海水，甚深无底，极广无边。婆罗逻！我正法、律亦复如是，诸法甚深，甚深无底，极广无边。婆罗逻！若我正法、律中诸法甚深，甚深无底，极广无边者，是谓我正法、律中第三未曾有法，令诸比丘见已乐中。

Furthermore, Brahman! Just as the water of the great ocean is salty and all has the same taste, my true Dharma and Vinaya are also like this. The taste of desirelessness, the taste of awakening, the taste of cessation, and the taste of the path. Brahman! If in my true Dharma and Vinaya, the taste of desirelessness, the taste of awakening, the taste of cessation, and the taste of the path, this is called the fourth unprecedented phenomenon in my true Dharma and Vinaya, which makes the bhikṣus enjoy it when they see it.

「复次，婆罗逻！如大海水咸，皆同一味，婆罗逻！我正法、律亦复如是，无欲为味，觉味、息味及道味。婆罗逻！若我正法、律中无欲为味，觉味、息味及道味者，是谓我正法、律中第四未曾有法，令诸比丘见已乐中。

Furthermore, Brahman! Just as there are many precious treasures in the great ocean, countless rare and various precious gems are filled within it. The names of precious treasures are gold, silver, crystal, lapis lazuli, maṇi, pearls, green jade, white shells, conch shells, coral, tiger amber, agate, mother-of-pearl, red stones, and crystal balls. Brahman! My true Dharma and Vinaya are also like this. There are many precious treasures, countless rare and various precious gems, filled within it. The names of precious treasures are the four foundations of mindfulness, the four right efforts, the four bases of supernormal power, the five faculties, the five powers, the seven factors of awakening, and the eightfold noble path. Brahman! If in my true Dharma and Vinaya, there are many precious treasures, countless rare and various precious gems, filled within it. The names of precious treasures are the four foundations of mindfulness, the four right efforts, the four bases of supernormal power, the five faculties, the five powers, the seven factors of awakening, and the eightfold noble path, this is called the fifth unprecedented phenomenon in my true Dharma and Vinaya, which makes the bhikṣus enjoy it when they see it.

「复次，婆罗逻！如大海中多有珍宝，无量𪻺异、种种珍琦，充满其中，珍宝名者，谓金、银、水精、琉璃、摩尼、真珠、碧玉、白珂、螺璧、珊瑚、虎珀、马瑙、瑇瑁、赤石、琁珠。婆罗逻！我正法、律亦复如是，多有珍宝，无量𪻺异，种种珍琦，充满其中，珍宝名者，谓四念处、四正勤、四如意足、五根、五力、七觉支、八支圣道。婆罗逻！若我正法、律中多有珍宝，无量𪻺异，种种珍琦，充满其中，珍宝名者，谓四念处、四正勤、四如意足、五根、五力、七觉支、八支圣道者，是谓我正法、律中第五未曾有法，令诸比丘见已乐中。

Furthermore, Brahman! Just as there are great gods dwelling in the great ocean, the names of the great gods are asuras, gandharvas, rākṣasas, fish, makaras, turtles, crocodiles, varāha, timi, timiṅgala, and timiṅgalatimika. Furthermore, in the great ocean, there are very strange and extraordinary beings with bodies of a hundred yojanas, two hundred yojanas, three hundred yojanas, up to seven hundred yojanas, all dwelling in the ocean. Brahman! My true Dharma and Vinaya are also like this. The great gods of the noble assembly all dwell within it. The names of the great gods are arhats, those who aspire to arahantship, non-returners, those who aspire to non-return, once-returners, those who aspire to once-return, stream-enterers, and those who aspire to stream-entry. Brahman! If in my true Dharma and Vinaya, the great gods of the noble assembly all dwell within it. The names of the great gods are arhats, those who aspire to arahantship, non-returners, those who aspire to non-return, once-returners, those who aspire to once-return, stream-enterers, and those who aspire to stream-entry, this is called the sixth unprecedented phenomenon in my true Dharma and Vinaya, which makes the bhikṣus enjoy it when they see it.

「复次，婆罗逻！如大海中大神所居，大神名者，谓阿修罗、干塔惒、罗刹、鱼摩竭、龟、鼍、婆留泥、帝麑、帝麑伽罗、提帝麑伽罗。复次，大海中甚奇！甚特！众生身体有百由延，有二百由延，有三百由延，有至七百由延，身皆居海中。婆罗逻！我正法、律亦复如是，圣众大神皆居其中，大神名者，谓阿罗诃、向阿罗诃、阿那含、向阿那含、斯陀含、向斯陀含、须陀洹、向须陀洹。婆罗逻！若我正法、律中圣众大神皆居其中，大神名者，谓阿罗诃、向阿罗诃、阿那含、向阿那含、斯陀含、向斯陀含、须陀洹、向须陀洹者，是谓我正法、律中第六未曾有法，令诸比丘见已乐中。

Furthermore, Brahman! Just as the great ocean is pure and does not accept corpses. If there is someone who dies, the wind blows them to the shore overnight. Brahman! My true Dharma and Vinaya are also like this. The noble assembly is pure and does not accept corpses. If there are people who are not diligent, who are evil, who claim to be celibate but are not, who claim to be monastics but are not, although they may fall within the noble assembly, they are far from the noble assembly, and the noble assembly is also far from them. Brahman! If in my true Dharma and Vinaya, the noble assembly is pure and does not accept corpses. If there are people who are not diligent, who are evil, who claim to be celibate but are not, who claim to be monastics but are not, although they may fall within the noble assembly, they are far from the noble assembly, and the noble assembly is also far from them, this is called the seventh unprecedented phenomenon in my true Dharma and Vinaya, which makes the bhikṣus enjoy it when they see it.

「复次，婆罗逻！如大海清净，不受死尸，若有命终者，过夜风便吹著岸上。婆罗逻！我正法、律亦复如是，圣众清净，不受死尸。若有不精进人恶生，非梵行称梵行，非沙门称沙门，彼虽堕在圣众之中，然去圣众远，圣众亦复去离彼远。婆罗逻！若我正法、律中圣众清净，不受死尸。若有不精进人恶生，非梵行称梵行，非沙门称沙门，彼虽堕在圣众之中，然去圣众远，圣众亦复去离彼远者，是谓我正法、律中第七未曾有法，令诸比丘见已乐中。

Furthermore, Brahman! Just as the great ocean in Jambudvīpa has five great rivers, namely the Ganges, the Yāmuna, the Sarabhū, the Aciravatī, and the Mahī. They all enter the great ocean, and upon entering, they each abandon their original names and are all called the great ocean. Brahman! My true Dharma and Vinaya are also like this. If a noble son of the kṣatriya caste shaves off his beard and hair, puts on the monastic robe, with utmost faith leaves home, becomes homeless, and studies the path, he abandons his original name and is called a śramaṇa. If a noble son of the brāhmaṇa caste, the householder caste, or the artisan caste shaves off his beard and hair, puts on the monastic robe, with utmost faith leaves home, becomes homeless, and studies the path, he abandons his original name and is called a śramaṇa. Brahman! If in my true Dharma and Vinaya, a noble son of the kṣatriya caste shaves off his beard and hair, puts on the monastic robe, with utmost faith leaves home, becomes homeless, and studies the path, he abandons his original name and is called a śramaṇa. If a noble son of the brāhmaṇa caste, the householder caste, or the artisan caste shaves off his beard and hair, puts on the monastic robe, with utmost faith leaves home, becomes homeless, and studies the path, he abandons his original name and is called a śramaṇa, this is called the eighth unprecedented phenomenon in my true Dharma and Vinaya, which makes the bhikṣus enjoy it when they see it.

「复次，婆罗逻！如大海阎浮洲中有五大河，一曰恒伽，二曰摇尤那，三曰舍牢浮，四曰阿夷罗婆提，五曰摩企，悉入大海，既入中已，各舍本名，皆曰大海。婆罗逻！我正法、律亦复如是，刹利种族姓子，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，彼舍本名，同曰沙门，梵志种、居士种、工师种族姓子，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，彼舍本名，同曰沙门。婆罗逻！若我正法、律中刹利种族姓子剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，彼舍本名，同曰沙门，梵志种、居士种、工师种族姓子，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，彼舍本名，同曰沙门者，是谓我正法、律中第八未曾有法，令诸比丘见已乐中。

"Brahman! These are called the eight unprecedented phenomena in the true Dharma and Vinaya, which make the bhikṣus enjoy them when they see them. Brahman! What do you think? If in my true Dharma and Vinaya there are eight unprecedented phenomena, and if in your great ocean there are eight unprecedented phenomena, which of these two kinds of unprecedented phenomena is superior, excellent, wonderful, and supreme?"

「婆罗逻！是谓正法、律中有八未曾有法，令诸比丘见已乐中。婆罗逻！于意云何？若我正法、律中有八未曾有法，若汝大海中有八未曾有法，此二种未曾有法，何者为上、为胜、为妙、为最？」

"Brahman said: "World-Honored One! The eight unprecedented phenomena in my great ocean are not equal to the eight unprecedented phenomena of the Tathāgata. They are not even a thousandth, a hundred thousandth, incomparable, inexpressible, immeasurable, and innumerable. Only the eight unprecedented phenomena of the World-Honored One are superior, excellent, wonderful, and supreme. World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and the Saṅgha of bhikṣus. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept me as a lay devotee. From this day forth, I take refuge for life, until the end of my life.

婆罗逻白曰：「世尊！我大海中有八未曾有法，不及如来八未曾有法，不如千倍万倍，不可比，不可喻，不可称，不可数，但世尊八未曾有法为上、为胜、为妙、为最。世尊！我今自归于佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The asura king Brahmā and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。婆罗逻阿修罗王及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
