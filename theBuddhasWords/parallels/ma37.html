MĀ 37

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 4 未曾有法品

37. Campā （三七）瞻波经

Parallel to: AN 8.20 / EĀ 48.2

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in Campā, by the side of the Ganges River.

一时，佛游瞻波，在恒伽池边。

At that time, on the fifteenth day of the month, when the World-Honored One was about to give the Dharma talk on the liberation of the mind, he sat on a prepared seat in front of the assembly of monks. After the World-Honored One had sat down, he immediately entered concentration and observed the minds of the assembly with his mind-reading knowledge. Having observed the minds of the assembly, he remained silent until the first watch of the night.

尔时，世尊月十五日说从解脱时，于比丘众前敷座而坐。世尊坐已，即便入定，以他心智观察众心，观众心已，至初夜竟，默然而坐。

Then a certain monk rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "World-Honored One! The first watch of the night has already passed. The Buddha and the assembly of monks have been sitting here for a long time. I only wish that the World-Honored One would speak on the liberation of the mind." At that time, the World-Honored One remained silent and did not reply.

于是，有一比丘即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！初夜已讫，佛及比丘众集坐来久，唯愿世尊说从解脱。」尔时，世尊默然不答。

Then the World-Honored One again remained silent and sat until the middle of the night. That monk rose from his seat again, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "World-Honored One! The first watch of the night has already passed, and the middle watch is about to end. The Buddha and the assembly of monks have been sitting here for a long time. I only wish that the World-Honored One would speak on the liberation of the mind." The World-Honored One also remained silent and did not reply.

于是，世尊复至中夜默然而坐。彼一比丘再从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！初夜已过，中夜将讫，佛及比丘众集坐来久，唯愿世尊说从解脱。」世尊亦再默然不答。

Then the World-Honored One again remained silent and sat until the last watch of the night. That monk rose from his seat three times, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "World-Honored One! The first watch of the night has already passed, and the middle watch is also over. The last watch is about to end, and dawn is approaching. It will not be long before dawn breaks. The Buddha and the assembly of monks have been sitting here for a very long time. I only wish that the World-Honored One would speak on the liberation of the mind."

于是，世尊复至后夜默然而坐。彼一比丘三从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！初夜既过，中夜复讫，后夜垂尽，将向欲明，明出不久，佛及比丘众集坐极久，唯愿世尊说从解脱。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told that monk, "In this assembly, there is a monk who has already become impure." At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana was also in the assembly. Then Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana thought, "For which monk did the World-Honored One say that there is one monk in this assembly who has already become impure? I should enter the samādhi of suchness and use the wisdom of knowing the minds of others in the samādhi of suchness to observe the minds of the assembly." Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana then entered the samādhi of suchness and used the wisdom of knowing the minds of others in the samādhi of suchness to observe the minds of the assembly. Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana then knew for which monk the World-Honored One had said that there was one monk in this assembly who had already become impure.

尔时，世尊告彼比丘：「于此众中，有一比丘已为不净。」彼时尊者大目干连亦在众中，于是，尊者大目干连便作是念：「世尊为何比丘而说此众中有一比丘已为不净？我宁可入如其像定，以如其像定他心之智，观察众心。」尊者大目干连即入如其像定，以如其像定他心之智，观察众心，尊者大目干连便知世尊所为比丘说，此众中有一比丘已为不净。

Then Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana rose from his samādhi, went before that monk, took him by the arm, and led him out, opening the door and placing him outside. "Fool, go far away! Do not stay here! You are no longer allowed to join the assembly of monks. From now on, you are not a monk." He closed the door and returned to where the Buddha was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said, "World-Honored One! The monk for whom the World-Honored One said that there was one monk in this assembly who had already become impure, I have already expelled. World-Honored One! The first watch of the night has already passed, and the middle watch is also over. The last watch is about to end, and dawn is approaching. It will not be long before dawn breaks. The Buddha and the assembly of monks have been sitting here for a very long time. I only wish that the World-Honored One would speak on the liberation of the mind."

于是，尊者大目干连即从定起，至彼比丘前，牵臂将出，开门置外：「痴人远去，莫于此住，不复得与比丘众会，从今已去，非是比丘。」闭门下钥，还诣佛所，稽首佛足，却坐一面，白曰：「世尊所为比丘说，此众中有一比丘已为不净，我已逐出。世尊！初夜既过，中夜复讫，后夜垂尽，将向欲明，明出不久，佛及比丘众集坐极久，唯愿世尊说从解脱。」

The World-Honored One said, "Mahāmaudgalyāyana! That foolish person will incur great sin for disturbing the World-Honored One and the assembly of monks. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If the Tathāgata were to speak on the liberation of the mind in an impure assembly, that person's head would burst into seven pieces. Therefore, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! From now on, you should speak on the liberation of the mind. The Tathāgata will no longer speak on the liberation of the mind. Why is that? Mahāmaudgalyāyana! Just as the great ocean, from the bottom to the top, gradually widens, evenly balanced, and forms the shore, its water is always full and never overflows. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! My true Dharma and Vinaya are also like this. It is gradually practiced, gradually studied, gradually exhausted, and gradually taught. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If my true Dharma and Vinaya are gradually practiced, gradually studied, gradually exhausted, and gradually taught, this is called an unprecedented teaching in my true Dharma and Vinaya.

世尊告曰：「大目干连！彼愚痴人当得大罪，触娆世尊及比丘众。大目干连，若使如来在不净众说从解脱者，彼人则便头破七分。是故大目干连！汝等从今已后说从解脱，如来不复说从解脱。所以者何？大目干连！如彼大海，从下至上，周回渐广，均调转上，以成于岸，其水常满，未曾流出。大目干连！我正法、律亦复如是，渐作渐学，渐尽渐教。大目干连！若我正法、律渐作渐学，渐尽渐教者，是谓我正法、律中未曾有法。

Furthermore, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! Just as the tides of the great ocean never miss their time, my true Dharma and Vinaya are also like this. For bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, and upāsikās, I establish precepts. The noble sons will not violate the precepts until the end of their lives. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If my true Dharma and Vinaya establish precepts for bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, and upāsikās, and the noble sons will not violate the precepts until the end of their lives, this is called an unprecedented teaching in my true Dharma and Vinaya.

「复次，大目干连！如大海潮，未曾失时。大目干连！我正法、律亦复如是，为比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆私施设禁戒，诸族姓子乃至命尽，终不犯戒。大目干连！若我正法、律为比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆私施设禁戒，诸族姓子乃至命尽，终不犯戒者，是谓我正法、律中未曾有法。

Furthermore, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! Just as the waters of the great ocean are very deep and bottomless, and extremely vast and boundless, my true Dharma and Vinaya are also like this. The dharmas are very deep, very deep and bottomless, and extremely vast and boundless. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If the dharmas of my true Dharma and Vinaya are very deep, very deep and bottomless, and extremely vast and boundless, this is called an unprecedented teaching in my true Dharma and Vinaya.

「复次，大目干连！如大海水，甚深无底，极广无边。大目干连！我正法、律亦复如是，诸法甚深，甚深无底，极广无边。大目干连！若我正法、律诸法甚深，甚深无底，极广无边者，是谓我正法、律中未曾有法。

Furthermore, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! Just as the waters of the great ocean are salty and all have the same taste, my true Dharma and Vinaya are also like this. They have the taste of desirelessness, the taste of awakening, the taste of cessation, and the taste of the path. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If my true Dharma and Vinaya have the taste of desirelessness, the taste of awakening, the taste of cessation, and the taste of the path, this is called an unprecedented teaching in my true Dharma and Vinaya.

「复次，大目干连！如海水咸，皆同一味。大目干连！我正法、律亦复如是，无欲为味，觉味、息味及道味。大目干连！若我正法、律无欲为味，觉味、息味及道味者，是谓我正法、律中未曾有法。

Furthermore, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! Just as the great ocean is full of many precious treasures, countless rare and wonderful things, and various precious gems, the precious treasures are called gold, silver, crystal, lapis lazuli, maṇi jewels, pearls, jade, white shells, carnelian, coral, tiger-eye, agate, mother-of-pearl, red stones, and crystal balls. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! My true Dharma and Vinaya are also like this. They are full of many precious treasures, countless rare and wonderful things, and various precious gems. The precious treasures are called the four foundations of mindfulness, the four right efforts, the four bases of supernormal power, the five faculties, the five powers, the seven factors of awakening, and the eightfold noble path. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If my true Dharma and Vinaya are full of many precious treasures, countless rare and wonderful things, and various precious gems, and the precious treasures are called the four foundations of mindfulness, the four right efforts, the four bases of supernormal power, the five faculties, the five powers, the seven factors of awakening, and the eightfold noble path, this is called an unprecedented teaching in my true Dharma and Vinaya.

「复次，大目干连！如大海中多有珍宝，无量𪻺异，种种珍奇，充满其中，珍宝名者，谓金、银、水精、琉璃、摩尼、真珠、碧玉、白珂、车璩、珊瑚、虎珀、马瑙、瑇瑁、赤石、琁珠。大目干连！我正法、律亦复如是，多有珍宝，无量𪻺异，种种珍琦，充满其中。珍宝名者，谓四念处、四正勤、四如意足、五根、五力、七觉支、八支圣道。大目干连！若我正法、律多有珍宝，无量𪻺异，种种珍奇，充满其中，珍宝名者，谓四念处、四正勤、四如意足、五根、五力、七觉支、八支圣道者，是谓我正法、律中未曾有法。

Furthermore, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! Just as the great ocean is the abode of great beings, and the names of the great beings are asuras, gandharvas, rākṣasas, fish, makaras, turtles, alligators, varāha, timi, timiṅgala, and timiṅgalatimis. Furthermore, the great ocean is very wonderful and extraordinary, with beings whose bodies are a hundred yojanas, two hundred yojanas, three hundred yojanas, up to seven hundred yojanas, all dwelling in the ocean. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! My true Dharma and Vinaya are also like this. The assembly of noble beings and great beings all dwell within it. The names of the great beings are arhats, those who aspire to arahantship, non-returners, those who aspire to non-returning, once-returners, those who aspire to once-returning, stream-enterers, and those who aspire to stream-entry. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If the assembly of noble beings and great beings all dwell within my true Dharma and Vinaya, and the names of the great beings are arhats, those who aspire to arahantship, non-returners, those who aspire to non-returning, once-returners, those who aspire to once-returning, stream-enterers, and those who aspire to stream-entry, this is called an unprecedented teaching in my true Dharma and Vinaya.

「复次，大目干连！如大海中，大神所居，大神名者，谓阿修罗、干沓惒、罗刹、鱼摩竭、龟、鼍、婆留泥、帝麑、帝麑伽罗、提帝麑伽罗。复次，大海中甚奇甚特，众生身体有百由延，有二百由延，有三百由延，有至七百由延，身皆居海中。大目干连！我正法、律亦复如是，圣众大神皆居其中。大神名者，谓阿罗诃、向阿罗诃、阿那含、向阿那含、斯陀含、向斯陀含、须陀洹、向须陀洹。大目干连！若我正法、律圣众大神皆居其中，大神名者，谓阿罗诃、向阿罗诃、阿那含、向阿那含，斯陀含、向斯陀含、须陀洹、向须陀洹者，是谓我正法、律中未曾有法。

Furthermore, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! Just as the great ocean is pure and does not accept corpses. If there is someone who dies, the wind blows them to the shore overnight. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! My true Dharma and Vinaya are also like this. The assembly of noble ones is pure and does not accept corpses. If there are people who are not diligent, who are evil, who claim to be celibate but are not, who claim to be monastics but are not, even if they follow among the assembly of noble ones, they are far from the assembly of noble ones, and the assembly of noble ones is also far from them. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If the assembly of noble ones in my true Dharma and Vinaya is pure and does not accept corpses. If there are people who are not diligent, who are evil, who claim to be celibate but are not, who claim to be monastics but are not, even if they follow among the assembly of noble ones, they are far from the assembly of noble ones, and the assembly of noble ones is also far from them, this is called an unprecedented teaching in my true Dharma and Vinaya.

「复次，大目干连！如大海清净，不受死尸。若有命终者，过夜风便吹著岸上。大目干连！我正法、律亦复如是，圣众清净，不受死尸。若有不精进人恶生，非梵行称梵行，非沙门称沙门，彼虽随在圣众之中，然去圣众远，圣众亦复去离彼远。大目干连！若我正法、律圣众清净，不受死尸。若有不精进人恶生，非梵行称梵行，非沙门称沙门，彼虽随在圣众之中，然去圣众远，圣众亦复去离彼远者，是谓我正法、律中未曾有法。

Furthermore, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! Just as the great ocean in Jambudvīpa has five great rivers, namely the Ganges, the Yāmuna, the Sarabhū, the Aciravatī, and the Mahī, all of which flow into the great ocean, and the dragon water rains down from the sky into the great ocean, falling like chariot wheels, but none of this water can cause the great ocean to increase or decrease. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! My true Dharma and Vinaya are also like this. If a kṣatriya youth shaves off his hair and beard, puts on the yellow robe, leaves home out of faith, becomes homeless, and studies the path, and attains the liberation of the unshakable mind, and dwells having realized it for himself, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! The liberation of the unshakable mind does not increase or decrease in my true Dharma and Vinaya. Similarly, if a brahmin, vaiśya, or śūdra youth shaves off his hair and beard, puts on the yellow robe, leaves home out of faith, becomes homeless, and studies the path, and attains the liberation of the unshakable mind, and dwells having realized it for himself, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! The liberation of the unshakable mind does not increase or decrease in my true Dharma and Vinaya. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If in my true Dharma and Vinaya a kṣatriya youth shaves off his hair and beard, puts on the yellow robe, leaves home out of faith, becomes homeless, and studies the path, and attains the liberation of the unshakable mind, and dwells having realized it for himself, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! The liberation of the unshakable mind does not increase or decrease in my true Dharma and Vinaya. Similarly, if a brahmin, vaiśya, or śūdra youth shaves off his hair and beard, puts on the yellow robe, leaves home out of faith, becomes homeless, and studies the path, and attains the liberation of the unshakable mind, and dwells having realized it for himself, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! The liberation of the unshakable mind does not increase or decrease in my true Dharma and Vinaya. This is called an unprecedented teaching in my true Dharma and Vinaya.

「复次，大目干连！如彼大海阎浮洲中有五大河，一曰恒伽，二曰摇尤那，三曰舍牢浮，四曰阿夷罗婆提，五曰摩企，皆入大海，及大海中龙水从空雨堕，渧如车钏，是一切水不能令大海有增减也。大目干连！我正法、律亦复如是，刹利种族姓子剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，不移动心解脱，自作证成就游。大目干连！不移动心解脱，于我正法、律中无增无减，如是梵志种、居士种、工师种族姓子剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，不移动心解脱，自作证成就游。大目干连！不移动心解脱，于我正法、律中无增无减。大目干连！若我正法、律刹利种族姓子剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，不移动心解脱，自作证成就游。大目干连！不移动心解脱，于我正法、律中无增无减，如是梵志种、居士种、工师种族姓子剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，不移动心解脱，自作证成就游。大目干连！不移动心解脱，于我正法、律中无增无减者，是谓我正法、律中未曾有法。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana and the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said, and they were delighted and carried it out.

佛说如是。尊者大目干连及诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
