MĀ 38

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 4 未曾有法品

38. The Householder Ugga (1) （三八）郁伽长者经

Parallel to: AN 8.21–22

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in Vesali and staying in the Great Forest.

一时，佛游鞞舍离，住大林中。

At that time, the elder Ugra was attended by only women, and he left Vesali with the women in front. In the middle of the Great Forest of Vesali, he only entertained with female music, enjoying himself like a king. Then the elder Ugra became greatly intoxicated with alcohol and abandoned the women. He went to the Great Forest. The elder Ugra, greatly intoxicated with alcohol, saw from afar the World-Honored One in the forest, upright and beautiful, like the moon among the stars, shining brightly, dazzling like a golden mountain, endowed with the marks of a great person, majestic and dignified, with his faculties tranquil and undisturbed, having accomplished self-control, with his mind at peace and silent. Having seen the Buddha, he immediately sobered up. When the elder Ugra had sobered up, he went to the Buddha, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side.

尔时，郁伽长者唯妇女侍从，在诸女前从鞞舍离出，于鞞舍离大林中间唯作女妓，娱乐如王。于是郁伽长者饮酒大醉，舍诸妇女，至大林中，郁伽长者饮酒大醉，遥见世尊在林树间，端正姝好，犹星中月，光耀𬀩晔，晃若金山，相好具足，威神巍巍，诸根寂定，无有蔽碍，成就调御，息心静默。彼见佛已，即时醉醒，郁伽长者醉既醒已，便往诣佛，稽首礼足，却坐一面。

At that time, the World-Honored One taught the Dharma for him, inspiring him with longing and bringing about joy. Using immeasurable skillful means, he taught the Dharma for him, inspiring him with longing and bringing about joy. Having done so, according to the way of the Buddhas, he first spoke the Dharma of uprightness, which delights the listener. He spoke about giving, about precepts, and about the Dharma of rebirth in the heavens. He criticized desire as a calamity and saṃsāra as impure. He praised the wonderful state of being desireless and the purity of the factors of the path. Having spoken this Dharma for him, the Buddha knew that he had a joyful mind, a complete mind, a pliant mind, a patient mind, a superior mind, a one-pointed mind, a mind free from doubt, a mind free from hindrances, and the ability and strength to receive the true Dharma. He then spoke to him the essential teachings of the true Dharma, as all Buddhas do. The World-Honored One then taught him about suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path. At that time, the elder Ugra saw the four noble truths of suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path, just as white cloth is easily dyed. The elder Ugra was also like this. While sitting, he saw the four noble truths of suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path.

尔时，世尊为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已。如诸佛法先说端正法，闻者欢悦，谓说施、说戒、说生天法，毁呰欲为灾患，生死为秽，称叹无欲为妙，道品白净。世尊为彼说如是法已，佛知彼有欢喜心、具足心、柔软心、堪耐心、胜上心、一向心、无疑心、无盖心，有能、有力堪受正法，谓如诸佛说正法要。世尊即为彼说苦、习、灭、道，彼时郁伽长者即于坐中见四圣谛苦、习、灭、道，犹如白素，易染为色，郁伽长者亦复如是，即于坐中见四圣谛苦、习、灭、道。

Then the elder Ugra, having seen the Dharma, attained the Dharma, realized the pure Dharma, cut off doubts, crossed over perplexity, had no other masters, no longer followed others, was free from hesitation, had attained the fruit of realization, and had gained fearlessness in the World-Honored One's teachings. He then rose from his seat, bowed to the Buddha, and said, "World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha of bhikṣus. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept me as a lay devotee. From this day forward, I take refuge for my entire life, until the end of my days. World-Honored One! From this day forward, I will follow the World-Honored One for the rest of my life, taking the five precepts with the practice of Brahma-conduct as the foremost.

于是，郁伽长者已见法得法，觉白净法，断疑度惑，更无余尊，不复从他，无有犹豫，已住果证，于世尊法得无所畏，即从坐起，为佛作礼，白曰：「世尊！我今自归于佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。世尊！我从今日，从世尊自尽形寿，梵行为首，受持五戒。」

Having taken the five precepts with the practice of Brahma-conduct as the foremost from the World-Honored One for the rest of his life, the elder Ugra bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and departed. Returning to his home, he immediately gathered all the women and said to them, "Do you know? I have taken the five precepts with the practice of Brahma-conduct as the foremost from the World-Honored One for the rest of my life. If you wish to stay here, you may stay and practice giving and making merit. If you do not wish to stay, you may return to your own homes. If you wish to be married, I will marry you off.

郁伽长者从世尊自尽形寿，梵行为首，受持五戒已，稽首佛足，绕三匝而去。还归其家，即集诸妇人，集已，语曰：「汝等知不？我从世尊自尽形寿，梵行为首，受持五戒，汝等欲得住于此者，便可住此，行施作福，若不欲住者，各自还归，若汝欲得嫁者，我当嫁汝。」

Then the eldest wife said to the elder Ugra, "If you have taken the five precepts with the practice of Brahma-conduct as the foremost from the Buddha for the rest of your life, then you may give me to so-and-so.

于是，最大夫人白郁伽长者：「若尊从佛自尽形寿，梵行为首，受持五戒者，便可以我与彼某甲。」

The elder Ugra then called the man and, holding the eldest wife's arm with his left hand and a golden water pot with his right hand, said to the man, "I now give you my eldest wife as your wife.

郁伽长者即为呼彼人，以左手执大夫人臂，右手执金澡罐，语彼人曰：「我今以大夫人与汝作妇。」

Upon hearing this, the man was greatly terrified, his body hair standing on end, and he said to the elder Ugra, "Elder, do you want to kill me? Do you want to kill me?"

彼人闻已，便大恐怖，身毛皆竖，白郁伽长者：「长者！欲杀我耶？欲杀我耶？」

The elder replied, "I will not kill you, but I have from the Buddha taken the five precepts, with the Brahma conduct as the foremost, for as long as I live. Therefore, I give you my eldest wife as your wife." The elder Ugra had already given his eldest wife, and when he was about to give and when he gave, he had no regret.

长者答曰：「我] cf.［丽】不杀汝，然我从佛自尽形寿，梵行为首，受持五戒。是故我以最大夫人与汝作妇耳。」郁伽长者已与大夫人，当与、与时都无悔心。

At that time, the World-Honored One was surrounded by immeasurable hundreds of thousands of great assemblies, among whom he praised the elder Ugra, saying that the elder Ugra had eight unprecedented dharmas.

是时，世尊无量百千大众围绕，于中咨嗟称叹郁伽长者，郁伽长者有八未曾有法。

Then, a bhikṣu, having passed the night at dawn, put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and went to the house of the elder Ugra. The elder Ugra saw from afar the bhikṣu coming, immediately rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms towards the bhikṣu, and said, "Venerable one, welcome! Venerable one, you have not come here for a long time. Please sit on this seat." At that time, the bhikṣu immediately sat on the seat. The elder Ugra bowed at the bhikṣu's feet, then sat to one side.

于是，有一比丘过夜平旦，著衣持钵，往诣郁伽长者家，郁伽长者遥见比丘来，即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向比丘白曰：「尊者善来！尊者久不来此，愿坐此床。」彼时，比丘即坐其床，郁伽长者礼比丘足，却坐一面。

The bhikṣu said, "Elder, you have good benefits and great merits. Why is that? It is because the World-Honored One, surrounded by immeasurable hundreds of thousands of great assemblies, praises among them that the elder Ugra has eight unprecedented dharmas. Elder, what are those dharmas?"

比丘告曰：「长者！汝有善利，有大功德。所以者何？谓世尊为汝无量百千大众围绕，于中咨嗟称叹，郁伽长者有八未曾有法。长者！汝有何法？」

The elder Ugra replied to the bhikṣu, "Venerable one, the World-Honored One did not say anything different. However, I do not know why the World-Honored One said it. But please listen, Venerable one, I have a dharma. Once, the World-Honored One was dwelling in the great forest near Vaiśālī. Venerable one, at that time, I was only attended by women, and I was the first to leave Vaiśālī. In the great forest between Vaiśālī and the forest, I only played music for women, entertaining like a king. Venerable one, at that time, I was greatly intoxicated by drinking alcohol, and I left the women to go to the great forest. Venerable one, at that time, I was greatly intoxicated, and I saw from afar the World-Honored One in the forest, upright and beautiful, like the moon among the stars, shining brightly, dazzling like a golden mountain, with perfect marks and majestic spiritual power, with calm faculties, without any obstruction, accomplished in taming and pacifying the mind, and silent. Having seen the Buddha, I immediately sobered up. Venerable one, I have this dharma."

郁伽长者答比丘曰：「尊者！世尊初不说异，然我不知世尊为何因说？但尊者听，谓我有法。一时，世尊游鞞舍离，住大林中。尊者！我于尔时唯妇女侍从，我最在前出鞞舍离，于鞞舍离大林中间唯作女妓，娱乐如王。尊者！我于尔时饮酒大醉，舍诸妇女，至大林中。尊者！我时大醉，遥见世尊在林树间，端正姝好，犹星中月，光耀𬀩晔，晃若金山，相好具足，威神巍巍，诸根寂定，无有蔽碍，成就调御，息心静默，我见佛已，即时醉醒。尊者！我有是法。」

The bhikṣu exclaimed, "Elder, if you have this dharma, it is very wonderful and extraordinary!"

比丘叹曰：「长者！若有是法，甚奇！甚特！」

"Venerable one, I do not only have this dharma. Furthermore, Venerable one, after I sobered up, I went to see the Buddha, bowed my head at his feet, and sat to one side. The World-Honored One taught the Dharma for me, inspiring and uplifting me, bringing about joy. With immeasurable skillful means, he taught the Dharma for me, inspiring and uplifting me, bringing about joy. Having done so, like the way the Buddhas first teach the correct Dharma, which delights the listeners, he spoke about giving, precepts, and the Dharma of rebirth in the heavens, criticizing desire as a calamity and saṃsāra as impure, praising the wonderful state of being desireless and the purity of the factors of the path. After the World-Honored One taught me such a Dharma, the Buddha knew that I had a joyful mind, a complete mind, a pliant mind, a patient mind, an excellent mind, a one-pointed mind, a mind without doubt, and a mind without afflictions, and that I had the ability and strength to receive the true Dharma. That is, like the way the Buddhas teach the essential true Dharma, the World-Honored One then taught me about suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path. At that time, I immediately saw the four noble truths of suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path, while sitting there, just as white cloth is easily dyed into color. Venerable one, it was the same for me. While sitting there, I immediately saw the four noble truths of suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path. Venerable one, I have this dharma."

「尊者！我不但有是法，复次，尊者！我醉醒已，便往诣佛，稽首礼足，却坐一面。世尊为我说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为我说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已。如诸佛法先说端正法，闻者欢悦，谓说施、说戒、说生天法，毁呰欲为灾患，生死为秽，称叹无欲为妙，道品白净。世尊为我说如是法已，佛知我有欢喜心、具足心、柔软心、堪耐心、胜上心、一向心、无疑心、无盖心，有能、有力堪受正法。谓如诸佛说正法要，世尊即为我说苦、习、灭、道。我尔时即于坐中见四圣谛苦、习、灭、道，犹如白素，易染为色，尊者！我亦如是，即于坐中见四圣谛苦、习、灭、道，尊者！我有是法。」

The bhikṣu exclaimed, "Elder, if you have this dharma, it is very wonderful and extraordinary!"

比丘叹曰：「长者！若有是法，甚奇！甚特！」

"Venerable one, I do not only have this dharma. Furthermore, Venerable one, having seen the Dharma, attained the Dharma, realized the pure Dharma, cut off doubts, crossed over perplexity, having no other teacher, not following others, without hesitation, having already attained the fruits of the path, I attained fearlessness in the World-Honored One's teachings. Venerable one, at that time, I immediately rose from my seat, bowed my head at the Buddha's feet, and said: 'World-Honored One, I now take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha of bhikṣus. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept me as an upāsaka, from this day forward, taking refuge for as long as I live, until the end of my life. World-Honored One, from today onward, I will follow the World-Honored One, taking the Brahma-conduct as the foremost, and upholding the five precepts for as long as I live.' Venerable one, if I follow the World-Honored One, taking the Brahma-conduct as the foremost, and uphold the five precepts for as long as I live, I have never known to violate the precepts. Venerable one, I have this dharma."

「尊者！我不但有是法。复次，尊者！我见法得法，觉白净法，断疑度惑，更无余尊，不复从他，无有犹豫，已住果证，于世尊法得无所畏。尊者！我尔时即从坐起，稽首佛足：『世尊！我今自归于佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。世尊！我从今日，从世尊自尽形寿，梵行为首，受持五戒。』尊者！若我从世尊自尽形寿，梵行为首，受持五戒，未曾知己犯戒。尊者！我有是法。」

The bhikṣu exclaimed, "Elder, if you have this dharma, it is very wonderful and extraordinary!"

比丘叹曰：「长者！若有是法，甚奇！甚特！」

"Venerable one, I do not only have this dharma. Furthermore, Venerable one, having followed the World-Honored One, taking the Brahma-conduct as the foremost, and upheld the five precepts for as long as I live, I bowed my head at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and departed. Returning to my home, I gathered all the women and said to them: 'Do you know? I have followed the World-Honored One, taking the Brahma-conduct as the foremost, and upheld the five precepts for as long as I live. If you wish to stay here, you may stay and practice giving and making merit. If you do not wish to stay, you may return to your own homes. If you wish to be married, I will marry you.' Then the eldest wife came to me and said: 'If you have followed the Buddha, taking the Brahma-conduct as the foremost, and upheld the five precepts for as long as you live, then you may give me to so-and-so.' Venerable one, I then called that man, took the eldest wife's arm with my left hand, held a golden water pot with my right hand, and said to the man: 'I now give the eldest wife to you as your wife.' Having heard this, the man was greatly terrified, his body hair standing on end, and he said to me: 'Elder, do you want to kill me? Do you want to kill me?' Venerable one, I said to him: 'I do not want to kill you. Rather, I have followed the Buddha, taking the Brahma-conduct as the foremost, and upheld the five precepts for as long as I live. Therefore, I am giving the eldest wife to you as your wife.' Venerable one, having already given the eldest wife, and giving her now, I have no regret at all. Venerable one, I have this dharma."

「尊者！我不但有是法。复次，尊者！我尔时从世尊自尽形寿，梵行为首，受持五戒已，稽首佛足，绕三匝而去，还归其家，集诸妇女，集已，语曰：『汝等知不？我从世尊自尽形寿，梵行为首，受持五戒，汝等欲得住于此者，便可住此，行施作福，若不欲住者，各自还归，若汝欲得嫁者，我当嫁汝。』于是，最大夫人来白我曰：『若尊从佛自尽形寿，梵行为首，受持五戒者，便可以我与彼某甲。』尊者！我尔时即为呼彼人，以左手执大夫人臂，右手执金澡罐，语彼人曰：『我今以大夫人与汝作妇。』彼人闻已，便大恐怖，身毛皆竖，而白我曰：『长者欲杀我耶？长者欲杀我耶？』尊者！我语彼曰：『不欲杀汝，然我从佛自尽形寿，梵行为首，受持五戒。是故我以最大夫人与汝作妇耳。』尊者！我已与大夫人，当与、与时都无悔心。尊者！我有是法。」

The bhikṣu exclaimed, "Elder, if you have this dharma, it is very wonderful and extraordinary!"

比丘叹曰：「长者！若有是法，甚奇！甚特！」

"Venerable one, I do not only have this dharma. Furthermore, Venerable one, when I go to the Sangha's garden, if I first see a bhikṣu, I pay homage to him. If that bhikṣu is walking, I also follow him in walking. If he is sitting, I also sit to one side. Having sat, I listen to the Dharma. He teaches the Dharma to me, and I also teach the Dharma to him. He asks me about matters, and I also ask him about matters. He answers me about matters, and I also answer him about matters. Venerable one, I have never remembered disrespecting elder bhikṣus of the upper, middle, or lower ranks. Venerable one, I have this dharma."

「尊者！我不但有是法。复次，尊者！我诣众园时，若初见一比丘，便为作礼，若彼比丘经行者，我亦随经行，若彼坐者，我亦于一面坐，坐已听法。彼尊为我说法，我亦为彼尊说法，彼尊问我事，我亦问彼尊事，彼尊答我事，我亦答彼尊事。尊者！我未曾忆轻慢上中下长老上尊比丘。尊者！我有是法。」

The bhikṣu exclaimed, "Elder, if you have this dharma, it is very wonderful and extraordinary!"

比丘叹曰：「长者！若有是法，甚奇！甚特！」

"Venerable one, I do not only have this dharma. Furthermore, Venerable one, when I am giving alms to the assembly of bhikṣus, the gods dwelling in the sky tell me, 'Elder, this is an arhat, this is one who is heading towards arahantship, this is a non-returner, this is one who is heading towards non-returning, this is a once-returner, this is one who is heading towards once-returning, this is a stream-enterer, this is one who is heading towards stream-entry, this is diligent, this is not diligent.' Venerable one, when I am giving alms to the assembly of bhikṣus, I have never remembered having a discriminating mind. Venerable one, I have this dharma."

「尊者！我不但有是法。复次，尊者！我在比丘众行布施时，天住虚空而告我曰：『长者！此是阿罗诃，此是向阿罗诃，此是阿那含，此是向阿那含，此是斯陀含，此是向斯陀含，此是须陀洹，此是向须陀洹，此精进，此不精进。』尊者！我施比丘众时，未曾忆有分别意。尊者！我有是法。」

The bhikṣu exclaimed, "Elder, if you have this dharma, it is very wonderful and extraordinary!"

比丘叹曰：「长者！若有是法，甚奇！甚特！」

"Venerable one, I do not only have this dharma. Furthermore, Venerable one, when I am giving alms to the assembly of bhikṣus, a god dwells in the sky and tells me, 'Elder, there is a Tathāgata, an Arhat, a Perfectly Enlightened One, a World-Honored One who well preaches the Dharma, and the Tathāgata's noble assembly well practices it.' Venerable one, I do not believe that god, I do not desire to please that god, I do not hear from that god, but I myself have pure knowledge, knowing that there is a Tathāgata, an Arhat, a Perfectly Enlightened One, a World-Honored One who well preaches the Dharma, and the Tathāgata's noble assembly well practices it. Venerable one, I have this dharma."

「尊者！我不但有是法。复次，尊者！我在比丘众行布施时，有天住虚空中而告我曰：『长者！有如来、无所著、等正觉、世尊善说法，如来圣众善趣向。』尊者！我不从彼天信，不从彼欲乐，不从彼所闻，但我自有净智，知有如来、无所著、等正觉、世尊善说法，如来圣众善趣向。尊者！我有是法。」

The bhikṣu exclaimed, "Elder, if you have this dharma, it is very wonderful and extraordinary!"

比丘叹曰：「长者！若有是法，甚奇！甚特！」

"Venerable one, I do not only have this dharma. Furthermore, Venerable one, the Buddha has spoken of the five lower fetters: sensual desire, ill will, holding to rules and rituals, doubt, and ignorance. I see none of these five that would bind me to return to this world and enter a womb. Venerable one, I have this dharma."

「尊者！我不但有是法。复次，尊者！谓佛所说五下分结，贪欲、瞋恚、身见、戒取、疑、我见此五，无一不尽令缚我还此世间，入于胎中。尊者！我有是法。」

The bhikṣu exclaimed, "Elder, if you have this dharma, it is very wonderful and extraordinary!"

比丘叹曰：「长者！若有是法，甚奇！甚特！」

The Elder Ugra said to the bhikṣu, "May the Venerable one take a meal here."

郁伽长者白比丘曰：「愿尊在此食。」

The bhikṣu, for the sake of the Elder Ugra, silently accepted the invitation. The Elder Ugra, knowing that the bhikṣu had silently accepted, immediately rose from his seat, personally served water for washing, and offered various abundant and delicious food and drink, personally serving and pouring, until he was satisfied. After finishing the meal and putting away the dishes, he personally served water for washing. Then he took a small seat and sat separately to listen to the Dharma. The bhikṣu spoke the Dharma for the Elder, inspiring him with longing and bringing about joy. Having used immeasurable skillful means to speak the Dharma for him, inspiring him with longing and bringing about joy, he rose from his seat and left, going to where the Buddha was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and told the Buddha in detail about the original discussion he had with the Elder Ugra.

比丘为郁伽长者故，默然受请。郁伽长者知彼比丘默然受已，即从坐起，自行澡水，以极净美种种丰饶食噉含消，自手斟酌，令得饱满，食讫收器，行澡水竟，持一小床，别坐听法。比丘为长者说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，从坐起去，往诣佛所，稽首礼足，却坐一面，谓与郁伽长者本所共论，尽向佛广说。

Then the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "It is for this reason that I praise the Elder Ugra for having eight unprecedented qualities."

于是，世尊告诸比丘：「我以是故，咨嗟称叹郁伽长者有八未曾有法。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
