MĀ 40

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 4 未曾有法品

40. To the Householder Hatthaka （四〇）手长者经

Parallel to: AN 8.24

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the country of Alavī and stayed in the Bamboo Grove.

一时，佛游阿逻鞞伽逻，在惒林中。

At that time, the householder Hand and five hundred great householders went to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at his feet, and sat to one side. The five hundred householders also bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side.

尔时，手长者与五百大长者俱，往诣佛所，稽首礼足，却坐一面，五百长者亦礼佛足，却坐一面。

The World-Honored One said, "Householder Hand! You now have this extremely large assembly. Householder! By what means do you gather this large assembly?"

世尊告曰：「手长者！汝今有此极大众，长者！汝以何法摄此大众？」

At that time, the householder Hand said, "World-Honored One! There are four ways of gathering, as taught by the World-Honored One: first, giving; second, kind words; third, benefiting; fourth, equality. World-Honored One! I gather the great assembly with these: sometimes by giving, sometimes by kind words, sometimes by benefiting, sometimes by equality."

彼时手长者白曰：「世尊！谓有四事摄，如世尊说，一者惠施，二者爱言，三者以利，四者等利。世尊！我以此摄于大众，或以惠施，或以爱言，或以利，或以等利。」

The World-Honored One praised, "Excellent! Excellent! Householder Hand! You are able to gather the great assembly with the Dharma, and also gather the great assembly with the gate, and gather the great assembly with the causes and conditions. Householder Hand! If in the past there were śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who gathered the great assembly with the Dharma, they all gathered it with these four things, or with something else. Householder Hand! If in the future there will be śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who gather the great assembly with the Dharma, they will all gather it with these four things, or with something else. Householder Hand! If at present there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who gather the great assembly with the Dharma, they will all gather it with these four things, or with something else."

世尊叹曰：「善哉！善哉！手长者！汝能以如法摄于大众，又以如门摄于大众，以如因缘摄于大众。手长者！若过去有沙门、梵志以如法摄大众者，彼一切即此四事摄于中或有余。手长者！若有未来沙门、梵志以如法摄大众者，彼一切即此四事摄，于中或有余。手长者！若有现在沙门、梵志以如法摄大众者，彼一切即此四事摄于中或有余。」

Then the World-Honored One taught the Dharma to Householder Hand, inspiring him with yearning and bringing him to accomplishment and joy. Having used immeasurable skillful means to teach the Dharma, inspiring him with yearning and bringing him to accomplishment and joy, he remained silent. Then Householder Hand, having been taught the Dharma by the Buddha, inspired with yearning and brought to accomplishment and joy, rose from his seat, paid homage to the Buddha, circumambulated him three times, and departed, returning to his home. Having arrived at the outer gate, he taught the Dharma to anyone there, inspiring them with yearning and bringing them to accomplishment and joy; at the middle gate, the inner gate, and within, he taught the Dharma to anyone there, inspiring them with yearning and bringing them to accomplishment and joy. Having ascended the hall and spread out his seat, he sat cross-legged, his mind accompanied by loving-kindness, completely filling one direction and accomplishing the practice. Thus in the second, third, and fourth directions, the four intermediate directions, and above and below, his mind accompanied by loving-kindness, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without conflict, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, completely filling the entire world and accomplishing the practice. Thus with compassion and joy, his mind accompanied by equanimity, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without conflict, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, completely filling the entire world and accomplishing the practice.

于是，世尊为手长者说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，默然而住。于是，手长者佛为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，即从坐起，为佛作礼，绕三匝而去，还归其家。到外门已，若有人者，尽为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜；中门、内门及入在内，若有人者尽为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，升堂敷床，结跏趺坐，心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游。如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。

At that time, the Thirty-three Gods gathered in the Dharma Hall, praising and extolling Householder Hand: "Virtuous ones! Householder Hand has great good fortune and great merit. Why is that? Because the Buddha taught the Dharma to Householder Hand, inspiring him with yearning and bringing him to accomplishment and joy. Having risen from his seat, he paid homage to the Buddha, circumambulated him three times, and departed, returning to his home. Having arrived at the outer gate, he taught the Dharma to anyone there, inspiring them with yearning and bringing them to accomplishment and joy; at the middle gate, the inner gate, and within, he taught the Dharma to anyone there, inspiring them with yearning and bringing them to accomplishment and joy. Having ascended the hall and spread out his seat, he sat cross-legged, his mind accompanied by loving-kindness, completely filling one direction and accomplishing the practice. Thus in the second, third, and fourth directions, the four intermediate directions, and above and below, his mind accompanied by loving-kindness, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without conflict, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, completely filling the entire world and accomplishing the practice. Thus with compassion and joy, his mind accompanied by equanimity, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without conflict, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, completely filling the entire world and accomplishing the practice."

尔时，三十三天集在法堂，咨嗟称叹手长者：「诸贤！手长者有大善利，有大功德。所以者何？彼手长者，佛为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，即从坐起，为佛作礼，绕三匝而去，还归其家。到外门已，若有人者尽为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜；中门、内门及入在内，若有人者尽为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，升堂敷床，结加趺坐，心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游。如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。」

Then the great king Vaiśravaṇa, majestic in appearance, radiant and brilliant, came to Householder Hand's house in the early morning and said, "Householder! You have great good fortune and great merit. Why is that? Now the Thirty-three Gods have gathered in the Dharma Hall and are praising and extolling you: 'Householder Hand has great good fortune and great merit. Why is that? The Buddha taught the Dharma to Householder Hand, inspiring him with yearning and bringing him to accomplishment and joy. Having risen from his seat, he paid homage to the Buddha, circumambulated him three times, and departed, returning to his home. Having arrived at the outer gate, he taught the Dharma to anyone there, inspiring them with yearning and bringing them to accomplishment and joy; at the middle gate, the inner gate, and within, he taught the Dharma to anyone there, inspiring them with yearning and bringing them to accomplishment and joy. Having ascended the hall and spread out his seat, he sat cross-legged, his mind accompanied by loving-kindness, completely filling one direction and accomplishing the practice. Thus in the second, third, and fourth directions, the four intermediate directions, and above and below, his mind accompanied by loving-kindness, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without conflict, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, completely filling the entire world and accomplishing the practice. Thus with compassion and joy, his mind accompanied by equanimity, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without conflict, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, completely filling the entire world and accomplishing the practice.'"

于是，毘沙门大天王色像巍巍，光耀𬀩晔，夜将向旦，往诣手长者家，告曰：「长者！汝有善利，有大功德。所以者何？今三十三天为长者集在法堂，咨嗟称叹：『手长者有大善利，有大功德。所以者何？诸贤！彼手长者，佛为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，即从坐起，为佛作礼，绕三匝而去，还归其家。到外门已，若有人者尽为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜；中门、内门及入在内，若有人者尽为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，升堂敷床，结加趺坐，心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游。如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。』」

At that time, Householder Hand remained silent, not speaking or looking at the great king Vaiśravaṇa. Why was that? Because he was respecting concentration and guarding concentration.

是时，手长者默然不语，不观、不视毘沙门大天王。所以者何？以尊重定，守护定故。

At that time, the World-Honored One, among a multitude of immeasurable hundreds of thousands, praised and extolled Householder Hand: "Householder Hand has seven unprecedented qualities. When I taught the Dharma to Householder Hand, inspiring him with yearning and bringing him to accomplishment and joy, he rose from his seat, paid homage to me, circumambulated me three times, and departed, returning to his home. Having arrived at the outer gate, he taught the Dharma to anyone there, inspiring them with yearning and bringing them to accomplishment and joy; at the middle gate, the inner gate, and within, he taught the Dharma to anyone there, inspiring them with yearning and bringing them to accomplishment and joy. Having ascended the hall and spread out his seat, he sat cross-legged, his mind accompanied by loving-kindness, completely filling one direction and accomplishing the practice. Thus in the second, third, and fourth directions, the four intermediate directions, and above and below, his mind accompanied by loving-kindness, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without conflict, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, completely filling the entire world and accomplishing the practice. Thus with compassion and joy, his mind accompanied by equanimity, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without conflict, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, completely filling the entire world and accomplishing the practice.

尔时，世尊于无量百千众中，咨嗟称叹手长者：「手长者有七未曾有法。彼手长者，我为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，即从坐起，为我作礼，绕三匝而去，还归其家。到外门已，若有人者尽为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜；中门、内门及入在内，若有人者尽为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，升堂敷床，结加趺坐，心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游。如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。

Now the thirty-three gods have gathered in the Dharma hall to praise and extol: 'Householder Hand has great good fortune and great merit. Why is that? The virtuous ones! Householder Hand, the Buddha taught him the Dharma, inspiring him with yearning and bringing him to accomplishment and joy. He then rose from his seat, paid homage to the Buddha, circumambulated him three times, and departed, returning to his home. Having arrived at the outer gate, he taught the Dharma to anyone there, inspiring them with yearning and bringing them to accomplishment and joy; at the middle gate, the inner gate, and within, he taught the Dharma to anyone there, inspiring them with yearning and bringing them to accomplishment and joy. Having ascended the hall and spread out his seat, he sat cross-legged, his mind accompanied by loving-kindness, completely filling one direction and accomplishing the practice. Thus in the second, third, and fourth directions, the four intermediate directions, and above and below, his mind accompanied by loving-kindness, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without conflict, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, completely filling the entire world and accomplishing the practice. Thus with compassion and joy, his mind accompanied by equanimity, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without conflict, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, completely filling the entire world and accomplishing the practice.'

「今三十三天为彼集在法堂，咨嗟称叹：『手长者有大善利，有大功德。所以者何？诸贤！彼手长者，佛为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，即从坐起，为佛作礼，绕三匝而去，还归其家。到外门已，若有人者尽为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜；中门、内门及入在内，若有人者尽为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，升堂敷床，结加趺坐，心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游。如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。』

Now the great king Vaiśravaṇa, with his majestic appearance and radiant brilliance, came to Householder Hand's house in the early morning and said: 'Householder! You have great good fortune and great merit. Why is that? Now the thirty-three gods have gathered in the Dharma hall to praise and extol: "Householder Hand has great good fortune and great merit. Why is that? The virtuous ones! Householder Hand, the Buddha taught him the Dharma, inspiring him with yearning and bringing him to accomplishment and joy. He then rose from his seat, paid homage to the Buddha, circumambulated him three times, and departed, returning to his home. Having arrived at the outer gate, he taught the Dharma to anyone there, inspiring them with yearning and bringing them to accomplishment and joy; at the middle gate, the inner gate, and within, he taught the Dharma to anyone there, inspiring them with yearning and bringing them to accomplishment and joy. Thus with compassion and joy, his mind accompanied by equanimity, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without conflict, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, completely filling the entire world and accomplishing the practice."'

「今毘沙门大天王色像巍巍，光曜𬀩晔，夜将向旦，诣手长者家，告曰：『长者！汝有善利，有大功德。所以者何？今三十三天为长者集在法堂，咨嗟称叹：「手长者有大善利，有大功德。所以者何？诸贤！彼手长者，佛为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，即从坐起，为佛作礼，绕三匝而去，还归其家。到外门已，若有人者尽为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜；中门、内门及入在内，若有人者尽为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，升堂敷床，结加趺坐，心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游。如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。」』」

Then, in the early morning, a certain monk put on his robe, took his bowl, and went to Householder Hand's house. Householder Hand saw the monk coming from afar, immediately rose from his seat, joined his palms towards the monk, and said: "Venerable one, welcome! Venerable one, you have not come here for a long time. Please sit on this seat." At that time, the monk then sat on the seat. Householder Hand bowed at the monk's feet and sat to one side.

于是，有一比丘过夜平旦，著衣持钵，往诣手长者家，手长者遥见比丘来，即从坐起，叉手向比丘白曰：「尊者善来！尊者久不来此，愿坐此床。」彼时比丘即坐其床，手长者礼比丘足，却坐一面。

The monk said: "Householder! You have great good fortune and great merit. Why is that? The World-Honored One, among countless hundreds of thousands of people, praised and extolled Householder Hand: 'Householder Hand has seven uncommon qualities. The World-Honored One taught him the Dharma, inspiring him with yearning and bringing him to accomplishment and joy. He then rose from his seat, paid homage to the World-Honored One, circumambulated him three times, and departed, returning to his home. Having arrived at the outer gate, he taught the Dharma to anyone there, inspiring them with yearning and bringing them to accomplishment and joy; at the middle gate, the inner gate, and within, he taught the Dharma to anyone there, inspiring them with yearning and bringing them to accomplishment and joy. Having ascended the hall and spread out his seat, he sat cross-legged, his mind accompanied by loving-kindness, completely filling one direction and accomplishing the practice. Thus in the second, third, and fourth directions, the four intermediate directions, and above and below, his mind accompanied by loving-kindness, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without conflict, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, completely filling the entire world and accomplishing the practice. Thus with compassion and joy, his mind accompanied by equanimity, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without conflict, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, completely filling the entire world and accomplishing the practice.'"

比丘告曰：「长者！汝有善利，有大功德。所以者何？世尊为汝于无量百千众中，咨嗟称叹手长者：『手长者有七未曾有法，手长者我为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，即从坐起，为我作礼，绕三匝而去，还归其家。到外门已，若有人者尽为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜；中门、内门及入在内，若有人者尽为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，升堂敷床，结加趺坐，心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游。如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。

The Buddha taught the Dharma to Householder Hand, inspiring him with yearning and bringing him to accomplishment and joy. He then rose from his seat, paid homage to the Buddha, circumambulated him three times, and departed, returning to his home. "Then the great king Vaiśravaṇa, majestic in appearance, radiant and shining, came to Householder Hand's house in the early morning and said: 'Householder! Thus with compassion and joy, his mind accompanied by equanimity, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without conflict, extremely vast, extremely great, immeasurable, well-cultivated, completely filling the entire world and accomplishing the practice.'" At that time, Householder Hand remained silent and did not look at the great king Vaiśravaṇa. Why is that? Because he was reverent towards concentration and guarded concentration.

「『于是，毘沙门大天王色像巍巍，光耀𬀩晔，夜将向旦，诣手长者家，告曰：「长者！汝有善利，有大功德。所以者何？今三十三天为手长者集在法堂，咨嗟称叹：『手长者有大善利，有大功德。所以者何？诸贤！彼手长者，佛为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，即从坐起，为佛作礼，绕三匝而去，还归其家。到外门已，若有人者尽为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜；中门、内门及入在内，若有人者尽为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，升堂敷床，结加趺坐，心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游。如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。』」是时，手长者默然不语，亦不观视毘沙门大天王。所以者何？以尊重定、守护定故。』」

"Then Householder Hand said to the monk: 'Venerable One! Was there no layman at that time?'"

于是，手长者白比丘曰：「尊者！是时无白衣耶？」

The monk replied: 'There was no layman.' He further asked: 'If there had been a layman, what would have been the fault?'

比丘答曰：「无白衣也。」又问曰：「若有白衣者，当有何咎？」

The householder replied: 'Venerable One! Those who do not believe the words of the World-Honored One will suffer for a long time, being unrighteous and unenduring, born in extremely evil places, experiencing immeasurable suffering. Those who believe the words of the Buddha will, because of this matter, be able to respect, venerate, and pay homage to me. Venerable One! I also do not wish for that. Venerable One! Please take your meal here.'

长者答曰：「尊者！或有不信世尊语者，彼当长夜不义不忍，生极恶处，受苦无量。若有信佛语者，彼因此事故，便能尊重恭敬礼事我。尊者！我亦不欲令尔也。尊者！愿在此食。」

The monk, for the sake of the householder Hand, silently accepted the invitation. The householder Hand, knowing that the monk had silently accepted, immediately rose from his seat, personally served water for washing, and offered various abundant and delicious food and drink, personally serving and pouring, until he was satisfied. After finishing the meal and putting away the dishes, he personally served water for washing again. He then took a small seat and sat separately to listen to the Dharma. The monk spoke the Dharma for the householder Hand, inspiring him with longing and bringing about joy. Using immeasurable skillful means, he spoke the Dharma for him, inspiring him with longing and bringing about joy. Having done so, he rose from his seat and left, going to where the Buddha was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and told the Buddha everything he had discussed with the householder Hand.

彼比丘为手长者故，默然受请。手长者知彼比丘默然受已，即从坐起，自行澡水，以极净美种种丰饶食噉含消，自手斟酌，令得饱满，食讫收器，行澡水已，取一小床，别坐听法。彼比丘为手长者说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，从坐起去，往诣佛所，稽首礼足，却坐一面，谓与手长者本所共论尽向佛说。

Then the World-Honored One told the monks: "For this reason, I praise the householder Hand for having seven uncommon qualities. Moreover, you should know that the householder Hand also has an eighth uncommon quality, which is that he has no desires and no attachments.

于是，世尊告诸比丘：「我以是故，称说手长者有七未曾有法。复次汝等当知，手长者复有第八未曾有法，手长者无求无欲。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The monks, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
