MĀ 41

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 4 未曾有法品

On the Householder Hatthaka （四一）手长者经

Parallel to: AN 8.23

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the country of Alavī and stayed in the Bamboo Grove.

一时，佛游阿逻鞞伽逻，在惒林中。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus: "The householder Hand has eight uncommon qualities. What are the eight? The householder Hand has few desires, faith, shame, modesty, diligence, mindfulness, concentration, and wisdom. When it is said that the householder Hand has few desires, it means that he himself has few desires and does not want others to know that he has few desires. He has faith, shame, modesty, diligence, mindfulness, concentration, and wisdom. The householder Hand himself has wisdom and does not want others to know that he has wisdom. The householder Hand has few desires, and for this reason it is said.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「手长者有八未曾有法。云何为八？手长者有少欲、有信、有惭、有愧、有精进、有念、有定、有慧。手长者有少欲者，此何因说？手长者自少欲，不欲令他知我少欲，有信、有惭、有愧、有精进、有念、有定、有慧，手长者自有慧，不欲令他知我有慧。手长者有少欲者，因此故说。

The householder Hand has faith. For what reason is this said? The householder Hand has attained firm faith, deeply attached to the Tathāgata, with the foundation of faith established, never following other śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, gods, Māra, Brahmā, or any other worldly beings. The householder Hand has faith, and for this reason it is said.

「手长者有信者，此何因说？手长者得信坚固，深著如来，信根已立，终不随外沙门、梵志、若天、魔、梵及余世间。手长者有信者，因此故说。

The householder Hand has shame. For what reason is this said? The householder Hand always practices shame, knowing what is shameful and shameful. Evil and unwholesome dharmas, defiled afflictions, and receiving all evil retributions, create the basis for birth and death. The householder Hand has shame, and for this reason it is said.

「手长者有惭者，此何因说？手长者常行惭耻，可惭知惭，恶不善法、秽污烦恼受诸恶报，造生死本。手长者有惭者，因此故说。

The householder Hand has modesty. For what reason is this said? The householder Hand always practices modesty, knowing what is modest and modest. Evil and unwholesome dharmas, defiled afflictions, and receiving all evil retributions, create the basis for birth and death. The householder Hand has modesty, and for this reason it is said.

「手长者有愧者，此何因说？手长者常行羞] cf.［丽】愧，可] cf.［丽】愧知愧，恶不善法，秽污烦恼受诸恶报，造生死本。手长者有愧者，因此故说。

The householder Hand is diligent. For what reason is this said? The householder Hand always practices diligence, eliminating evil and unwholesome dharmas, cultivating all wholesome dharmas, always generating intention, being single-minded and firm, for the sake of all wholesome roots, not abandoning skillful means. The householder Hand is diligent, and for this reason it is said.

「手长者有精进者，此何因说？手长者常行精进，除恶不善，修诸善法，恒自起意，专一坚固，为诸善本，不舍方便。手长者有精进者，因此故说。

The householder Hand is mindful. For what reason is this said? The householder Hand observes the internal body as the body, observes internal feelings, mind, and dharmas as dharmas. The householder Hand is mindful, and for this reason it is said.

「手长者有念者，此何因说？手长者观内身如身，观内觉、心、法如法。手长者有念者，因此故说。

The householder Hand is concentrated. For what reason is this said? The householder Hand, having abandoned desire, abandoned evil and unwholesome dharmas, attained the fourth dhyāna, and abides in it. The householder Hand is concentrated, and for this reason it is said.

「手长者有定者，此何因说？手长者离欲、离恶不善之法，至得第四禅成就游。手长者有定者，因此故说。

The householder Hand is wise. For what reason is this said? The householder Hand practices wisdom, observes the arising and falling away of dharmas, attains such wisdom, with the bright and penetrating wisdom of the noble ones, discerning and understanding, to completely end suffering. The householder Hand is wise, and for this reason it is said. The householder Hand has eight uncommon qualities, and for this reason it is said.

「手长者有慧者，此何因说？手长者修行智慧，观兴衰法，得如此智，圣慧明达，分别晓了，以正尽苦。手长者有慧者，因此故说。手长者有八未曾有法者，因此故说。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

The Sūtra of the Householder Hand, the tenth.
