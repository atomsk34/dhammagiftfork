MĀ 44

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 5 习相应品

44. Mindfulness （四四）念经

Parallel to: AN 5.21 (~) / AN 5.22 (~) / AN 5.24 (~) / AN 5.168 (~) / AN 6.50 (~) / AN 7.65 (~) / AN 8.81 / AN 10.3 (~) / AN 10.4 (~) / AN 11.3 (~) / AN 11.4 (~) / MĀ 45 (~) / MĀ 46 (~) / MĀ 47 (~) / MĀ 48 (~) / MĀ 49 (~) / MĀ 50 (~) / SĀ 495 (~)

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "If a bhikṣu is forgetful and lacks right knowledge, he will harm right mindfulness and right knowledge. If he lacks right mindfulness and right knowledge, he will harm guarding the sense faculties, guarding the precepts, non-regret, joy, delight, tranquility, bliss, concentration, seeing things as they really are, knowing things as they really are, disenchantment, dispassion, and liberation. If he lacks liberation, he will harm nirvāṇa. If a bhikṣu is not forgetful and has right knowledge, he will cultivate right mindfulness and right knowledge. If he has right mindfulness and right knowledge, he will cultivate guarding the sense faculties, guarding the precepts, non-regret, joy, delight, tranquility, bliss, concentration, seeing things as they really are, knowing things as they really are, disenchantment, dispassion, and liberation. If he has liberation, he will cultivate nirvāṇa."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「若比丘多忘无正智，便害正念正智；若无正念正智，便害护诸根、护戒、不悔、欢悦、喜、止、乐、定、见如实、知如真、厌、无欲、解脱；若无解脱，便害涅槃。若比丘不多忘有正智，便习正念正智；若有正念正智，便习护诸根、护戒、不悔、欢悦、喜、止、乐、定、见如实、知如真、厌、无欲、解脱；若有解脱，便习涅槃。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
