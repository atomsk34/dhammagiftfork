MĀ 5

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 1 七法品

5. The Heap of Wood （五）木积喻经（初一日诵）

Parallel to: AN 7.72 / EĀ 33.10

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in Kosala, among the people, accompanied by a large assembly of monks. At that time, the World-Honored One suddenly saw a large pile of wood burning fiercely along the road. Having seen it, he then left the road, went to another tree, spread out his sitting mat, and sat cross-legged.

一时，佛游拘萨罗，在人间，与大比丘众翼从而行。尔时，世尊则于中路，忽见一处有大木积，洞燃俱炽，世尊见已，便下道侧，更就余树敷尼师檀，结加趺坐。

Having sat down, the World-Honored One told the monks, "Do you see that large pile of wood burning fiercely there?"

世尊坐已，告诸比丘：「汝等见彼有大木积洞燃俱炽耶？」

The monks replied, "We see it, World-Honored One!"

时，诸比丘答曰：「见也。世尊！」

The World-Honored One again told the monks, "What do you think? If one were to embrace, sit, or lie with a large pile of burning wood, or if one were to embrace, sit, or lie with a young woman of the warrior caste, brahmin, householder, or worker caste, who is in the prime of her youth, bathed, perfumed, wearing bright and clean clothes, adorned with flower garlands and necklaces, which would be more pleasant?"

世尊复告诸比丘曰：「于汝意云何？谓大木积洞燃俱炽，若抱、若坐、若卧，谓刹利女，梵志、居士、工师女，年在盛时，沐浴香薰，著明净衣，华鬘、璎珞严饰其身，若抱、若坐、若卧，何者为乐？」

The monks replied, "World-Honored One! Embracing, sitting, or lying with a large pile of burning wood is very painful. World-Honored One! Embracing, sitting, or lying with a young woman of the warrior caste, brahmin, householder, or worker caste, who is in the prime of her youth, bathed, perfumed, wearing bright and clean clothes, adorned with flower garlands and necklaces, is very pleasant. World-Honored One!"

时，诸比丘白曰：「世尊！谓大木积洞燃俱炽，若抱、若坐、若卧，甚苦。世尊！谓刹利女，梵志、居士、工师女，年在盛时，沐浴香薰，著明净衣，华鬘、璎珞严饰其身，若抱、若坐、若卧，甚乐。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "I tell you this not to make you lose the path of the renunciant. If you wish to accomplish the unsurpassed holy life, it is better to embrace a large pile of burning wood, sit or lie with it, and although you may suffer or die because of it, you will not, upon the dissolution of the body and the end of life, go to a bad realm of existence and be born in hell. But if a foolish person violates the precepts, is not diligent, gives rise to unwholesome and unskillful states, falsely claims to be a holy one when not, and falsely claims to be a renunciant when not; if he embraces a young woman of the warrior caste, brahmin, householder, or worker caste, who is in the prime of her youth, bathed, perfumed, wearing bright and clean clothes, adorned with flower garlands and necklaces, and sits or lies with her, that foolish person will, because of that, for a long time not be wholesome or righteous, and will experience the consequences of unwholesome states. Upon the dissolution of the body and the end of life, he will go to a bad realm of existence and be born in hell. Therefore, you should consider your own benefit, the benefit of others, and the benefit of both. You should think like this: 'My renunciation and practice are not in vain or empty. They have results, consequences, and supreme peace and happiness. I will be born in good realms and attain long life. I will receive the offerings of robes, food and drink, bedding, and medicine from the faithful. I will cause the benefactors to attain great blessings, great results, and great illumination.' You should train like this."

世尊告曰：「我为汝说，不令汝等学沙门失沙门道。汝欲成无上梵行者，宁抱木积洞燃俱炽，若坐、若卧，彼虽因此受苦或死，然不以是身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中；若愚痴人犯戒不精进，生恶不善法，非梵行称梵行，非沙门称沙门；若抱刹利女，梵志、居士、工师女，年在盛时，沐浴香薰，著明净衣，华鬘、璎珞严饰其身，若坐、若卧者，彼愚痴人因是长夜不善不义，受恶法报，身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中。是故汝等当观自义、观彼义、观两义，当作是念：『我出家学，不虚不空，有果有报，有极安乐，生诸善处而得长寿，受人信施衣被、饮食、床褥、汤药，令诸施主得大福祐，得大果报，得大光明者。』当作是学。」

The World-Honored One again said to the monks, "What do you think? If a strong man were to bind your shins with a tight rope made of animal hair, cutting through the skin, cutting through the flesh, cutting through the tendons, cutting through the bones, and stopping at the marrow, and if you were to receive offerings of massage for your body, limbs, and hands and feet from a warrior, brahmin, householder, or worker, which would be more pleasant?"

世尊复告诸比丘曰：「于意云何？若有力士以紧索毛绳绞勒其［蹲－酋＋（十／田／ㄙ）］断皮，断皮已断肉，断肉已断筋，断筋已断骨，断骨已至髓而住，若从刹利、梵志、居士、工师受其信施，按摩身体、支节、手足，何者为乐？」

The monks said, "World-Honored One! If a strong man were to bind our shins with a tight rope made of animal hair, cutting through the skin, cutting through the flesh, cutting through the tendons, cutting through the bones, and stopping at the marrow, it would be very painful. World-Honored One! If we were to receive offerings of massage for our body, limbs, and hands and feet from a warrior, brahmin, householder, or worker, it would be very pleasant. World-Honored One!"

时，诸比丘白曰：「世尊！若有力士以紧索毛绳绞勒其[蹲-酋+（十/田/ㄙ）断皮，断皮已断肉，断肉已断筋，断筋已断骨，断骨已至髓而住，甚苦。世尊！若从刹利、梵志、居士、工师受其信施，按摩身体、支节、手足，甚乐。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "I tell you this not to make you lose the path of the renunciant. If you wish to accomplish the unsurpassed holy life, it is better to have a strong man bind your shins with a tight rope made of animal hair, cutting through the skin, cutting through the flesh, cutting through the tendons, cutting through the bones, and stopping at the marrow. Although you may suffer or die from this, it will not lead to a bad destination after the body's disintegration and the end of life, to rebirth in hell. But if a foolish person violates the precepts, is not diligent, gives rise to unwholesome and unskillful states, falsely claims to be a holy one when not, and falsely claims to be a renunciant when not, and receives offerings of massage for the body, limbs, and hands and feet from a warrior, brahmin, householder, or worker, that foolish person will, because of that, not be wholesome or righteous for a long time, and will experience the results of unwholesome states. When the body disintegrates and life ends, they will go to a bad destination, to rebirth in hell. Therefore, you should consider your own benefit, the benefit of others, and the benefit of both. You should think like this: 'My renunciation and practice should not be in vain or empty, but should have results, rewards, and supreme peace and happiness. I should be reborn in good places and attain long life, and receive the offerings of robes, food and drink, bedding, and medicine from the faithful. I should cause the donors to attain great blessings, great results, and great illumination.' You should train like this."

世尊告曰：「我为汝说，不令汝等学沙门失沙门道。汝欲成无上梵行者，宁令力士以紧索毛绳绞勒其[蹲-酋+（十/田/ㄙ）断皮，断皮已断肉，断肉已断筋，断筋已断骨，断骨已至髓而住，彼虽因此受苦或死，然不以是身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中；若愚痴人犯戒不精进，生恶不善法，非梵行称梵行，非沙门称沙门，从刹利、梵志、居士、工师受其信施，按摩身体、支节、手足，彼愚痴人因是长夜不善不义，受恶法报，身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中。是故汝等当观自义、观彼义、观两义，当作是念：『我出家学，不虚不空，有果有报，有极安乐，生诸善处而得长寿，受人信施衣被、饮食、床褥、汤药，令诸施主得大福祐，得大果报，得大光明者。』当作是学。」

The World-Honored One again said to the monks, "What do you think? If a strong man were to cut off your thighs with a sharp, polished knife, or if you were to receive offerings of respect, prostrations, and welcoming from warriors, brahmins, householders, and workers, which would be more pleasant?"

世尊复告诸比丘曰：「于意云何？若有力士以莹磨利刀截断其髀，若从刹利、梵志、居士、工师受信施、礼拜，恭敬将迎，何者为乐？」

The monks said, "World-Honored One! If a strong man were to cut off our thighs with a sharp, polished knife, it would be very painful. World-Honored One! If we were to receive offerings of respect, prostrations, and welcoming from warriors, brahmins, householders, and workers, it would be very pleasant. World-Honored One!"

时，诸比丘白曰：「世尊！若有力士以莹磨利刀截断其髀，甚苦。世尊！若从刹利、梵志、居士、工师受信施、礼拜，恭敬将迎，甚乐。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "I tell you this not to make you lose the path of the renunciant. If you wish to accomplish the unsurpassed holy life, it is better to have a strong man cut off your thighs with a sharp, polished knife. Although you may suffer or die from this, you will not, upon the breaking up of the body at death, go to a bad realm of existence and be born in hell. But if a foolish person violates the precepts, is not diligent, gives rise to unwholesome and unskillful states, falsely claims to be a holy person when not, and falsely claims to be a renunciant when not, and receives offerings of respect, prostrations, and welcoming from warriors, brahmins, householders, and workers, that foolish person, because of this, will for a long time not be good or righteous, will experience the consequences of unwholesome states, and upon the breaking up of the body at death, will go to a bad realm of existence and be born in hell. Therefore, you should consider your own benefit, consider the benefit of others, consider the benefit of both, and think like this: 'I have gone forth to study, not in vain, not in emptiness, but with fruit, with result, with supreme peace and happiness, being born in good places and attaining long life, receiving the offerings of robes, food and drink, bedding and mats, and medicine from donors, causing those donors to attain great blessings, attain great fruit, and attain great illumination.' You should train like this."

世尊告曰：「我为汝说，不令汝等学沙门失沙门道。汝欲成无上梵行者，宁令力士以莹磨利刀截断其髀，彼虽因此受苦或死，然不以是身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中；若愚痴人犯戒不精进，生恶不善法，非梵行称梵行，非沙门称沙门，从刹利、梵志、居士、工师受信施、礼拜，恭敬将迎，彼愚痴人因是长夜不善不义，受恶法报，身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中。是故汝等当观自义、观彼义、观两义，当作是念：我出家学，不虚不空，有果有报，有极安乐，生诸善处而得长寿，受人信施衣被、饮食床褥、汤药，令诸施主得大福祐，得大果报，得大光明者。当作是学。」

The World-Honored One again said to the monks, "What do you think? If a strong man were to wrap your body with burning, blazing iron copper sheets, or if you were to receive offerings of clothing from warriors, brahmins, householders, and workers, which would be more pleasant?"

世尊复告诸比丘曰：「于意云何？若有力士以铁铜鍱洞燃俱炽，缠络其身，若从刹利、梵志、居士、工师受信施衣服，何者为乐？」

The monks said, "World-Honored One! If a strong man were to wrap our bodies with burning, blazing iron copper sheets, it would be very painful. World-Honored One! If we were to receive offerings of clothing from warriors, brahmins, householders, and workers, it would be very pleasant. World-Honored One!"

时，诸比丘白曰：「世尊！若有力士以铁铜鍱洞燃俱炽，缠络其身，甚苦。世尊！若从刹利、梵志、居士、工师受信施衣服，甚乐。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "I tell you this not to make you lose the path of the renunciant. If you wish to accomplish the unsurpassed holy life, it is better for a strong man to wrap your body with burning, blazing iron copper sheets. Although you may suffer or die from this, it will not lead to a bad destination after the body's destruction and the end of life, to rebirth in hell. But if a foolish person violates the precepts, is not diligent, generates unwholesome and unvirtuous qualities, falsely claims to be a holy one when not, and falsely claims to be a renunciant when not, and receives offerings of clothing from warriors, brahmins, householders, and workers, that foolish person will, because of this, not be wholesome or righteous for a long time, will experience the consequences of unwholesome qualities, and after the body's destruction and the end of life, will go to a bad destination, to rebirth in hell. I should be reborn in good places and attain long life, and receive the offerings of clothing, food and drink, bedding, and medicine from the faithful donors. I should cause the donors to attain great blessings, great results, and great illumination.' You should train like this."

世尊告曰：「我为汝说，不令汝等学沙门失沙门道。汝欲成无上梵行者，宁令力士以铁铜鍱洞燃俱炽，缠络其身，彼虽因此受苦或死，然不以是身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中；若愚痴人犯戒不精进，生恶不善法，非梵行称梵行，非沙门称沙门，从刹利、梵志、居士、工师受信施衣服，彼愚痴人因是长夜不善不义，受恶法报，身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中。是故汝等当观自义、观彼义、观两义，当作是念：『我出家学，不虚不空，有果有报，有极安乐，生诸善处而得长寿，受人信施衣被、饮食、床褥、汤药，令诸施主得大福祐，得大果报，得大光明者。』当作是学。」

The World-Honored One again said to the monks, "What do you think? If a strong man were to use a hot iron tongs to pry open your mouth and then put a burning iron ball into your mouth, that burning iron ball would burn your lips, having burned your lips it would burn your tongue, having burned your tongue it would burn your palate, having burned your palate it would burn your throat, having burned your throat it would burn your heart, having burned your heart it would burn your intestines and stomach, having burned your intestines and stomach it would pass below. If you were to receive offerings of food with countless flavors from warriors, brahmins, householders, and workers, which would be more pleasant?"

世尊复告诸比丘曰：「于意云何？若有力士以热铁钳钳开其口，便以铁丸洞燃俱炽，著其口中，彼热铁丸烧唇，烧唇已烧舌，烧舌已烧龂，烧龂已烧咽，烧咽已烧心，烧心已烧肠胃，烧肠胃已下过，若从刹利、梵志、居士、工师受信施食无量众味，何者为乐？」

At that time, the monks said, "World-Honored One! If a strong man were to use a hot iron tongs to pry open our mouths and then put a burning iron ball into our mouths, that burning iron ball would burn our lips, having burned our lips it would burn our tongues, having burned our tongues it would burn our palates, having burned our palates it would burn our throats, having burned our throats it would burn our hearts, having burned our hearts it would burn our intestines and stomachs, having burned our intestines and stomachs it would pass below. It would be very painful. World-Honored One! If we were to receive offerings of food with countless flavors from warriors, brahmins, householders, and workers, it would be very pleasant. World-Honored One!"

时，诸比丘白曰：「世尊！若有力士以热铁钳钳开其口，便以铁丸洞燃俱炽，著其口中，彼热铁丸烧唇，烧唇已烧舌，烧舌已烧龂，烧龂已烧咽，烧咽已烧心，烧心已烧肠胃，烧肠胃已下过，甚苦。世尊！若从刹利、梵志、居士、工师受信施食无量众味，甚乐。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "I tell you this not to make you lose the path of the renunciant. If you wish to accomplish the unsurpassed holy life, it is better for a strong man to use a hot iron tongs to pry open your mouth and then put a burning iron ball into your mouth, that burning iron ball would burn your lips, having burned your lips it would burn your tongue, having burned your tongue it would burn your palate, having burned your palate it would burn your throat, having burned your throat it would burn your heart, having burned your heart it would burn your intestines and stomach, having burned your intestines and stomach it would pass below. Therefore, you should consider your own benefit, consider the benefit of others, consider the benefit of both, and think like this: 'My renunciation and practice will not be in vain or empty, will have results, will have rewards, will have great peace and happiness, will be born in good places and attain long life, and will receive the offerings of clothing, food and drink, bedding, and medicine from the faithful donors. It will cause the donors to obtain great blessings, obtain great results, and obtain great illumination.' You should practice like this."

The World-Honored One again said to the monks, "What do you think? If a strong man were to force a person to sit or lie on a burning iron copper bed, or if one were to receive the offerings of beds, mats, and bedding from warriors, brahmins, householders, and workers, which would be more pleasant?"

世尊复告诸比丘曰：「于意云何？若有力士以铁铜床洞燃俱炽，强逼使人坐卧其上，若从刹利、梵志、居士、工师受其信施床［木＊翕］卧具，何者为乐？」

The monks said, "World-Honored One! If a strong man were to force a person to sit or lie on a burning iron copper bed, it would be very painful. World-Honored One! If one were to receive the offerings of beds, mats, and bedding from warriors, brahmins, householders, and workers, it would be very pleasant. World-Honored One!"

时，诸比丘白曰：「世尊！若有力士以铁铜床洞燃俱炽，强逼使人坐卧其上，甚苦。世尊！若从刹利、梵志、居士、工师受其信施床［木＊翕］卧具，甚乐。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "I tell you this not to make you lose the path of the renunciant. If you wish to accomplish the unsurpassed holy life, it is better to have a strong man force a person to sit or lie on a burning iron copper bed. Although one may suffer or die from this, one will not, upon the destruction of the body and the end of life, go to a bad place and be born in hell. You should think like this: 'My renunciation and practice are not in vain or empty, but have results, consequences, and utmost peace and happiness. I am born in good places and attain long life. I receive the offerings of clothing, food and drink, bedding, and medicine from the faithful. I cause the donors to obtain great blessings, great results, and great illumination.' You should practice like this."

世尊告曰：「我为汝说，不令汝等学沙门失沙门道。汝欲成无上梵行者，宁令力士以铁铜床洞燃俱炽，强逼使人坐卧其上，彼虽因此受苦或死，然不以是身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中；若愚痴人犯戒不精进，生恶不善法，非梵行称梵行，非沙门称沙门，从刹利、梵志、居士、工师受其信施床［木＊翕］卧具，彼愚痴人因是长夜不善不义，受恶法报，身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中。是故汝等当观自义、观彼义、观两义，当作是念：『我出家学，不虚不空，有果有报，有极安乐，生诸善处而得长寿，受人信施衣被、饮食、床褥、汤药，令诸施主得大福祐，得大果报，得大光明者。』当作是学。」

The World-Honored One again said to the monks, "What do you think? If a strong man were to take a large iron copper cauldron, burning and blazing, pick up a person, and turn them upside down into the cauldron, or if one were to receive the offerings of a house from warriors, brahmins, householders, and workers, plastered and whitewashed, with windows and doors securely closed, and a warm stove fire, which would be more pleasant?"

世尊复告诸比丘曰：「于意云何？若有力士以大铁铜釜洞燃俱炽，撮举人已，倒著釜中，若从刹利、梵志、居士、工师受信施房舍，泥治垩洒，窗户牢密，炉火煴暖，何者为乐？」

The monks said, "World-Honored One! If a strong man were to take a large iron copper cauldron, burning and blazing, pick up a person, and turn them upside down into the cauldron, it would be very painful. World-Honored One! If one were to receive the offerings of a house from warriors, brahmins, householders, and workers, plastered and whitewashed, with windows and doors securely closed, and a warm stove fire, it would be very pleasant. World-Honored One!"

时，诸比丘白曰：「世尊！若有力士以大铁铜釜洞燃俱炽，撮举人已，倒著釜中，甚苦。世尊！若从刹利、梵志、居士、工师受信施房舍，泥治垩洒，窗户牢密，炉火煴暖，甚乐。世尊！」

The World-Honored One said, "I tell you this not to make you lose the path of the renunciant. If you wish to accomplish the unsurpassed holy life, it is better for a strong man to take a large iron copper cauldron, burning and blazing, pick up a person, and turn them upside down into the cauldron. Although they may suffer or die because of this, they will not, upon the dissolution of the body and the end of life, go to a bad realm of existence and be born in hell. But if a foolish person violates the precepts, is not diligent, gives rise to unwholesome and unskillful states, falsely claims to be a holy one when not, and falsely claims to be a renunciant when not, and receives the offerings of a house from warriors, brahmins, householders, and workers, plastered and whitewashed, with windows and doors securely closed, and a warm stove fire, that foolish person, because of that, will for a long time not be wholesome or righteous, will experience the results of unwholesome states, and upon the dissolution of the body and the end of life, will go to a bad realm of existence and be born in hell. Therefore, you should consider your own benefit, consider the benefit of others, consider the benefit of both, and think like this: 'My renunciation and practice will not be in vain or empty, will have results, will have rewards, will have supreme peace and happiness, will be born in good realms and attain long life, and will receive the offerings of clothing, food and drink, bedding, and medicine from the faithful donors. May the donors attain great blessings, attain great results, attain great illumination.' You should practice like this."

世尊告曰：「我为汝说，不令汝等学沙门失沙门道。汝欲成无上梵行者，宁令力士以大铁铜釜洞燃俱炽，撮举人已，倒著釜中，彼虽因此受苦或死，然不以是身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中；若愚痴人犯戒不精进，生恶不善法，非梵行称梵行，非沙门称沙门，从刹利、梵志、居士、工师受信施房舍，泥治垩洒，窗户牢密，炉火煴暖，彼愚痴人因是长夜不善不义，受恶法报，身坏命终，趣至恶处，生地狱中。是故汝等当观自义、观彼义、观两义，当作是念：『我出家学，不虚不空，有果有报，有极安乐，生诸善处而得长寿，受人信施衣被、饮食、床褥、汤药，令诸施主得大福祐，得大果报，得大光明者。』当作是学。」

When this teaching was spoken, sixty monks attained the exhaustion of the taints and the unbinding of the mind, while sixty monks abandoned the precepts and returned to lay life. Why was this? The World-Honored One's instructions were very profound and difficult, and the path of practice was also very profound and difficult.

说此法时，六十比丘漏尽结解，六十比丘舍戒还家。所以者何？世尊教诫甚深、甚难，学道亦复甚深、甚难。

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
