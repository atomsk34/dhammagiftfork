MĀ 51

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 5 习相应品

51. Beginning （五一）本际经

Parallel to: AN 10.61 (~) / AN 10.62 (~) / MĀ 52 (~) / MĀ 53 (~)

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One addressed the bhikṣus, saying: "For those who have desire, the original basis is unknowable. Originally there was no desire, but now that desire has arisen, the cause of desire can be known. For those who have desire, there is practice, not no practice. What is the practice of desire? The answer is: ignorance is the practice. Ignorance also has practice, not no practice. What is the practice of ignorance? The answer is: the five hindrances are the practice. The five hindrances also have practice, not no practice. What is the practice of the five hindrances? The answer is: the three unwholesome actions are the practice. The three unwholesome actions also have practice, not no practice. What is the practice of the three unwholesome actions? The answer is: not guarding the sense faculties is the practice. Not guarding the sense faculties also has practice, not no practice. What is the practice of not guarding the sense faculties? The answer is: incorrect mindfulness and incorrect wisdom are the practice. Incorrect mindfulness and incorrect wisdom also have practice, not no practice. What is the practice of incorrect mindfulness and incorrect wisdom? The answer is: incorrect thought is the practice. Incorrect thought also has practice, not no practice. What is the practice of incorrect thought? The answer is: lack of faith is the practice. Lack of faith also has practice, not no practice. What is the practice of lack of faith? The answer is: hearing evil teachings is the practice. Hearing evil teachings also has practice, not no practice. What is the practice of hearing evil teachings? The answer is: associating with bad friends is the practice. Associating with bad friends also has practice, not no practice. What is the practice of associating with bad friends? The answer is: bad people are the practice."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「有爱者，其本际不可知，本无有爱，然今生有爱，便可得知，所因有爱。有爱者，则有习，非无习。何谓有爱习？答曰无明为习。无明亦有习，非无习。何谓无明习？答曰五葢为习。五葢亦有习，非无习。何谓五葢习？答曰三恶行为习。三恶行亦有习，非无习。何谓三恶行习？答曰不护诸根为习。不护诸根亦有习，非无习。何谓不护诸根习？答曰不正念、不正智为习。不正念、不正智亦有习，非无习。何谓不正念、不正智习？答曰不正思惟为习。不正思惟亦有习，非无习。何谓不正思惟习？答曰不信为习。不信亦有习，非无习。何谓不信习？答曰闻恶法为习。闻恶法亦有习，非无习。何谓闻恶法习？答曰亲近恶知识为习。亲近恶知识亦有习，非无习。何谓亲近恶知识习？答曰恶人为习。」

This is how, having accumulated bad people, one accumulates associating with bad friends. Having accumulated associating with bad friends, one accumulates hearing evil teachings. Having accumulated hearing evil teachings, one accumulates lack of faith. Having accumulated lack of faith, one accumulates incorrect thought. Having accumulated incorrect thought, one accumulates incorrect mindfulness and incorrect wisdom. Having accumulated incorrect mindfulness and incorrect wisdom, one accumulates not guarding the sense faculties. Having accumulated not guarding the sense faculties, one accumulates the three unwholesome actions. Having accumulated the three unwholesome actions, one accumulates the five hindrances. Having accumulated the five hindrances, one accumulates ignorance. Having accumulated ignorance, one accumulates desire. Thus, this desire is accumulated in this way.

「是为具恶人已，便具亲近恶知识。具亲近恶知识已，便具闻恶法。具闻恶法已，便具生不信。具生不信已，便具不正思惟。具不正思惟已，便具不正念、不正智。具不正念、不正智已，便具不护诸根。具不护诸根已，便具三恶行。具三恶行已，便具五葢。具五葢已，便具无明。具无明已，便具有爱。如是此有爱展转具成。

"Enlightenment and liberation also have practice, not no practice. What is the practice of enlightenment and liberation? The answer is: the seven factors of enlightenment are the practice. The seven factors of enlightenment also have practice, not no practice. What is the practice of the seven factors of enlightenment? The answer is: the four foundations of mindfulness are the practice. The four foundations of mindfulness also have practice, not no practice. What is the practice of the four foundations of mindfulness? The answer is: the three wholesome actions are the practice. The three wholesome actions also have practice, not no practice. What is the practice of the three wholesome actions? The answer is: guarding the sense faculties is the practice. Guarding the sense faculties also has practice, not no practice. What is the practice of guarding the sense faculties? The answer is: correct mindfulness and correct wisdom are the practice. Correct mindfulness and correct wisdom also have practice, not no practice. What is the practice of correct mindfulness and correct wisdom? The answer is: correct thought is the practice. Correct thought also has practice, not no practice. What is the practice of correct thought? The answer is: faith is the practice. Faith also has practice, not no practice. What is the practice of faith? The answer is: hearing good teachings is the practice. Hearing good teachings also has practice, not no practice. What is the practice of hearing good teachings? The answer is: associating with good friends is the practice. Associating with good friends also has practice, not no practice. What is the practice of associating with good friends? The answer is: good people are the practice."

「明、解脱亦有习，非无习。何谓明、解脱习？答曰七觉支为习。七觉支亦有习，非无习。何谓七觉支习？答曰四念处为习。四念处亦有习，非无习。何谓四念处习？答曰三妙行为习。三妙行亦有习，非无习。何谓三妙行习？答曰护诸根为习。护诸根亦有习，非无习。何谓护诸根习？答曰正念、正智为习。正念、正智亦有习，非无习。何谓正念、正智习？答曰正思惟为习。正思惟亦有习，非无习。何谓正思惟习？答曰信为习。信亦有习，非无习。何谓信习？答曰闻善法为习。闻善法亦有习，非无习。何谓闻善法习？答曰亲近善知识为习。亲近善知识亦有习，非无习。何谓亲近善知识习？答曰善人为习。

This is how, having accumulated good people, one accumulates associating with good friends. Having accumulated associating with good friends, one accumulates hearing good teachings. Having accumulated hearing good teachings, one accumulates faith. Having accumulated faith, one accumulates correct thought. Having accumulated correct thought, one accumulates correct mindfulness and correct wisdom. Having accumulated correct mindfulness and correct wisdom, one accumulates guarding the sense faculties. Having accumulated guarding the sense faculties, one accumulates the three wholesome actions. Having accumulated the three wholesome actions, one accumulates the four foundations of mindfulness. Having accumulated the four foundations of mindfulness, one accumulates the seven factors of enlightenment. Having accumulated the seven factors of enlightenment, one accumulates enlightenment and liberation. Thus, this enlightenment and liberation are accumulated in this way.

「是为具善人已，便具亲近善知识。具亲近善知识已，便具闻善法。具闻善法已，便具生信。具生信已，便具正思惟。具正思惟已，便具正念、正智。具正念、正智已，便具护诸根。具护诸根已，便具三妙行。具三妙行已，便具四念处。具四念处已，便具七觉支。具七觉支已，便具明、解脱，如是此明、解脱展转具成。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
