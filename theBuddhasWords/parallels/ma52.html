MĀ 52

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 5 习相应品

52. Nutriments (1) （五二）食经

Parallel to: AN 10.61 (~) / AN 10.62 / MĀ 51 (~) / MĀ 53

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One addressed the bhikṣus, saying: "For those who have craving, the original basis is unknowable. Originally there was no craving, but now that craving has arisen, one can know the cause of craving. For those who have craving, there is sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of craving? The answer is: ignorance is the sustenance. Ignorance also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of ignorance? The answer is: the five hindrances are the sustenance. The five hindrances also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the five hindrances? The answer is: the three unwholesome actions are the sustenance. The three unwholesome actions also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the three unwholesome actions? The answer is: not guarding the sense faculties is the sustenance. Not guarding the sense faculties also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of not guarding the sense faculties? The answer is: incorrect mindfulness and incorrect wisdom are the sustenance. Incorrect mindfulness and incorrect wisdom also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of incorrect mindfulness and incorrect wisdom? The answer is: incorrect thought is the sustenance. Incorrect thought also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of incorrect thought? The answer is: lack of faith is the sustenance. Lack of faith also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of lack of faith? The answer is: hearing evil teachings is the sustenance. Hearing evil teachings also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of hearing evil teachings? The answer is: associating with bad friends is the sustenance. Associating with bad friends also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of associating with bad friends? The answer is: bad people are the sustenance.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「有爱者，其本际不可知，本无有爱，然今生有爱，便可得知，所因有爱。有爱者，则有食，非无食。何谓有爱食？答曰无明为食。无明亦有食，非无食。何谓无明食？答曰五盖为食。五盖亦有食，非无食。何谓五盖食？答曰三恶行为食。三恶行亦有食，非无食。何谓三恶行食？答曰不护诸根为食。不护诸根亦有食，非无食。何谓不护诸根食？答曰不正念、不正智为食。不正念、不正智亦有食，非无食。何谓不正念、不正智食？答曰不正思惟为食。不正思惟亦有食，非无食。何谓不正思惟食？答曰不信为食。不信亦有食，非无食。何谓不信食？答曰闻恶法为食。闻恶法亦有食，非无食。何谓闻恶法食？答曰亲近恶知识为食。亲近恶知识亦有食，非无食。何谓亲近恶知识食？答曰恶人为食。

This is to say, having bad people, one then has associating with bad friends. Having associated with bad friends, one then has hearing evil teachings. Having heard evil teachings, one then has the arising of disbelief. Having the arising of disbelief, one then has incorrect thought. Having incorrect thought, one then has incorrect mindfulness and incorrect wisdom. Having incorrect mindfulness and incorrect wisdom, one then has not guarding the sense faculties. Having not guarded the sense faculties, one then has the three unwholesome actions. Having the three unwholesome actions, one then has the five hindrances. Having the five hindrances, one then has ignorance. Having ignorance, one then has craving. In this way, this craving is gradually perfected.

「是为具恶人已，便具亲近恶知识。具亲近恶知识已，便具闻恶法。具闻恶法已，便具生不信。具生不信已，便具不正思惟。具不正思惟已，便具不正念、不正智。具不正念、不正智已，便具不护诸根。具不护诸根已，便具三恶行。具三恶行已，便具五葢。具五葢已，便具无明。具无明已，便具有爱。如是此有爱展转具成。

The great ocean also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the great ocean? The answer is: the great rivers are the sustenance. The great rivers also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the great rivers? The answer is: the small rivers are the sustenance. The small rivers also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the small rivers? The answer is: the large streams are the sustenance. The large streams also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the large streams? The answer is: the small streams are the sustenance. The small streams also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the small streams? The answer is: mountain cliffs, streams, and level fields are the sustenance. Mountain cliffs, streams, and level fields also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of mountain cliffs, streams, and level fields? The answer is: rain is the sustenance. Sometimes there is heavy rain, and after heavy rain, the water in mountain cliffs, streams, and level fields fills up. When the water in mountain cliffs, streams, and level fields fills up, then the small streams fill up. When the small streams fill up, then the large streams fill up. When the large streams fill up, then the small rivers fill up. When the small rivers fill up, then the great rivers fill up. When the great rivers fill up, then the great ocean fills up. In this way, that great ocean is gradually filled.

「大海亦有食，非无食。何谓大海食？答曰大河为食。大河亦有食，非无食。何谓大河食？答曰小河为食。小河亦有食，非无食。何谓小河食？答曰大川为食。大川亦有食，非无食。何谓大川食？答曰小川为食。小川亦有食，非无食。何谓小川食？答曰山岩溪涧、平泽为食。山岩溪涧、平泽亦有食，非无食。何谓山岩溪涧、平泽食？答曰雨为食。有时大雨，大雨已，则山岩溪涧、平泽水满。山岩溪涧、平泽水满已，则小川满。小川满已，则大川满。大川满已，则小河满。小河满已，则大河满。大河满已，则大海满。如是彼大海展转成满。

Craving also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of craving? The answer is: ignorance is the sustenance. Ignorance also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of ignorance? The answer is: the five hindrances are the sustenance. The five hindrances also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the five hindrances? The answer is: the three unwholesome actions are the sustenance. The three unwholesome actions also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the three unwholesome actions? The answer is: not guarding the sense faculties is the sustenance. Not guarding the sense faculties also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of not guarding the sense faculties? The answer is: incorrect mindfulness and incorrect wisdom are the sustenance. Incorrect mindfulness and incorrect wisdom also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of incorrect mindfulness and incorrect wisdom? The answer is: incorrect thought is the sustenance. Incorrect thought also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of incorrect thought? The answer is: disbelief is the sustenance. Disbelief also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of disbelief? The answer is: hearing evil teachings is the sustenance. Hearing evil teachings also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of hearing evil teachings? The answer is: associating with bad friends is the sustenance. Associating with bad friends also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of associating with bad friends? The answer is: bad people are the sustenance.

「如是有爱亦有食，非无食。何谓有爱食？答曰无明为食。无明亦有食，非无食。何谓无明食？答曰五盖为食。五盖亦有食，非无食。何谓五盖食？答曰三恶行为食。三恶行亦有食，非无食。何谓三恶行食？答曰不护诸根为食。不护诸根亦有食，非无食。何谓不护诸根食？答曰不正念、不正智为食。不正念、不正智亦有食，非无食。何谓不正念、不正智食？答曰不正思惟为食。不正思惟亦有食，非无食。何谓不正思惟食？答曰不信为食。不信亦有食，非无食。何谓不信食？答曰闻恶法为食。闻恶法亦有食，非无食。何谓闻恶法食？答曰亲近恶知识为食。亲近恶知识亦有食，非无食。何谓亲近恶知识食？答曰恶人为食。

Thus, having accumulated bad people, one then accumulates associating with bad friends. Having accumulated associating with bad friends, one then accumulates hearing evil teachings. Having accumulated hearing evil teachings, one then accumulates giving rise to disbelief. Having accumulated giving rise to disbelief, one then accumulates incorrect thought. Having accumulated incorrect thought, one then accumulates incorrect mindfulness and incorrect wisdom. Having accumulated incorrect mindfulness and incorrect wisdom, one then accumulates not guarding the sense faculties. Having accumulated not guarding the sense faculties, one then accumulates the three unwholesome actions. Having accumulated the three unwholesome actions, one then accumulates the five hindrances. Having accumulated the five hindrances, one then accumulates ignorance. Having accumulated ignorance, one then accumulates craving. Thus, this craving is gradually accumulated.

「是为具恶人已，便具亲近恶知识。具亲近恶知识已，便具闻恶法。具闻恶法已，便具生不信。具生不信已，便具不正思惟。具不正思惟已，便具不正念、不正智。具不正念、不正智已，便具不护诸根。具不护诸根已，便具三恶行。具三恶行已，便具五盖。具五盖已，便具无明。具无明已，便具有爱。如是此有爱展转具成。

Enlightenment and liberation also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of enlightenment and liberation? The answer is: the seven factors of enlightenment are the sustenance. The seven factors of enlightenment also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the seven factors of enlightenment? The answer is: the four foundations of mindfulness are the sustenance. The four foundations of mindfulness also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the four foundations of mindfulness? The answer is: the three wholesome actions are the sustenance. The three wholesome actions also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the three wholesome actions? The answer is: guarding the sense faculties is the sustenance. Guarding the sense faculties also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of guarding the sense faculties? The answer is: correct mindfulness and correct wisdom are the sustenance. Correct mindfulness and correct wisdom also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of correct mindfulness and correct wisdom? The answer is: correct thought is the sustenance. Correct thought also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of correct thought? The answer is: faith is the sustenance. Faith also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of faith? The answer is: hearing good teachings is the sustenance. Hearing good teachings also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of hearing good teachings? The answer is: associating with good friends is the sustenance. Associating with good friends also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of associating with good friends? The answer is: good people are the sustenance.

「明、解脱亦有食，非无食。何谓明、解脱食？答曰七觉支为食。七觉支亦有食，非无食。何谓七觉支食？答曰四念处为食。四念处亦有食，非无食。何谓四念处食？答曰三妙行为食。三妙行亦有食，非无食。何谓三妙行食？答曰护诸根为食。护诸根亦有食，非无食。何谓护诸根食？答曰正念、正智为食。正念、正智亦有食，非无食。何谓正念、正智食？答曰正思惟为食。正思惟亦有食，非无食。何谓正思惟食？答曰信为食。信亦有食，非无食。何谓信食？答曰闻善法为食。闻善法亦有食，非无食。何谓闻善法食？答曰亲近善知识为食。亲近善知识亦有食，非无食。何谓亲近善知识食？答曰善人为食。

Thus, having accumulated good people, one then accumulates associating with good friends. Having accumulated associating with good friends, one then accumulates hearing good teachings. Having accumulated hearing good teachings, one then accumulates giving rise to faith. Having accumulated giving rise to faith, one then accumulates correct thought. Having accumulated correct thought, one then accumulates correct mindfulness and correct wisdom. Having accumulated correct mindfulness and correct wisdom, one then accumulates guarding the sense faculties. Having accumulated guarding the sense faculties, one then accumulates the three wholesome actions. Having accumulated the three wholesome actions, one then accumulates the four foundations of mindfulness. Having accumulated the four foundations of mindfulness, one then accumulates the seven factors of enlightenment. Having accumulated the seven factors of enlightenment, one then accumulates enlightenment and liberation. Thus, this enlightenment and liberation are gradually accumulated.

「是为具善人已，便具亲近善知识。具亲近善知识已，便具闻善法。具闻善法已，便具生信。具生信已，便具正思惟。具正思惟已，便具正念、正智。具正念、正智已，便具护诸根。具护诸根已，便具三妙行。具三妙行已，便具四念处。具四念处已，便具七觉支。具七觉支已，便具明、解脱。如是此明、解脱展转具成。

The great ocean also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the great ocean? The answer is: the great rivers are the sustenance. The great rivers also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the great rivers? The answer is: the small rivers are the sustenance. The small rivers also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the small rivers? The answer is: the large streams are the sustenance. The large streams also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the large streams? The answer is: the small streams are the sustenance. The small streams also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the small streams? The answer is: mountain streams, ravines, and plains are the sustenance. Mountain streams, ravines, and plains also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of mountain streams, ravines, and plains? The answer is: rain is the sustenance. When there is heavy rain, and after heavy rain, then the water in mountain streams, ravines, and plains is full. When the water in mountain streams, ravines, and plains is full, then the small streams are full. When the small streams are full, then the large streams are full. When the large streams are full, then the small rivers are full. When the small rivers are full, then the great rivers are full. When the great rivers are full, then the great ocean is full. Thus, that great ocean is gradually filled.

「大海亦有食，非无食。何谓大海食？答曰大河为食。大河亦有食，非无食。何谓大河食？答曰小河为食。小河亦有食，非无食。何谓小河食？答曰大川为食。大川亦有食，非无食。何谓大川食？答曰小川为食。小川亦有食，非无食。何谓小川食？答曰山岩溪涧、平泽为食。山岩溪涧、平泽亦有食，非无食。何谓山岩溪涧、平泽食？答曰雨为食。有时大雨，大雨已，则山岩溪涧、平泽水满。山岩溪涧、平泽水满已，则小川满。小川满已，则大川满。大川满已，则小河满。小河满已，则大河满。大河满已，则大海满。如是彼大海展转成满。

Thus, this enlightenment and liberation also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of enlightenment and liberation? The answer is: the seven factors of enlightenment are the sustenance. The seven factors of enlightenment also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the seven factors of enlightenment? The answer is: the four foundations of mindfulness are the sustenance. The four foundations of mindfulness also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the four foundations of mindfulness? The answer is: the three wholesome actions are the sustenance. The three wholesome actions also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of the three wholesome actions? The answer is: guarding the sense faculties is the sustenance. Guarding the sense faculties also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of guarding the sense faculties? The answer is: correct mindfulness and correct wisdom are the sustenance. Correct mindfulness and correct wisdom also have sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of correct mindfulness and correct wisdom? The answer is: correct thought is the sustenance. Correct thought also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of correct thought? The answer is: faith is the sustenance. Faith also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of faith? The answer is: hearing good teachings is the sustenance. Hearing good teachings also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of hearing good teachings? The answer is: associating with good friends is the sustenance. Associating with good friends also has sustenance, not no sustenance. What is the sustenance of associating with good friends? The answer is: good people are the sustenance.

「如是明、解脱亦有食，非无食。何谓明、解脱食？答曰七觉支为食。七觉支亦有食，非无食。何谓七觉支食？答曰四念处为食。四念处亦有食，非无食。何谓四念处食？答曰三妙行为食。三妙行亦有食，非无食。何谓三妙行食？答曰护诸根为食。护诸根亦有食，非无食。何谓护诸根食？答曰正念、正智为食。正念、正智亦有食，非无食。何谓正念、正智食？答曰正思惟为食。正思惟亦有食，非无食。何谓正思惟食？答曰信为食。信亦有食，非无食。何谓信食？答曰闻善法为食。闻善法亦有食，非无食。何谓闻善法食？答曰亲近善知识为食。亲近善知识亦有食，非无食。何谓亲近善知识食？答曰善人为食。

Thus, having good people, one then associates with good friends. Having associated with good friends, one then hears good teachings. Having heard good teachings, one then gives rise to faith. Having given rise to faith, one then has correct thought. Having correct thought, one then has correct mindfulness and correct wisdom. Having correct mindfulness and correct wisdom, one then guards the sense faculties. Having guarded the sense faculties, one then has the three wholesome actions. Having the three wholesome actions, one then has the four foundations of mindfulness. Having the four foundations of mindfulness, one then has the seven factors of enlightenment. Having the seven factors of enlightenment, one then has enlightenment and liberation. Thus, this enlightenment and liberation are gradually accomplished.

「是为具善人已，便具亲近善知识。具亲近善知识已，便具闻善法。具闻善法已，便具生信。具生信已，便具正思惟。具正思惟已，便具正念、正智。具正念、正智已，便具护诸根。具护诸根已，便具三妙行。具三妙行已，便具四念处。具四念处已，便具七觉支。具七觉支已，便具明、解脱。如是此明、解脱展转具成。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
