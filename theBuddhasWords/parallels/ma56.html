MĀ 56

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 5 习相应品

56. Meghiya （五六）弥醯经

Parallel to: AN 9.3 / Ud 4.1

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the country of Magadha, staying in the Mangnai Forest Cave in the village of Jatu.

一时，佛游摩竭陀国，在阇鬪村莽［木＊奈］林窟。

At that time, Venerable Mihe was his attendant. Then, Venerable Mihe, having passed the night, put on his robe, took his bowl, entered the village of Jatu to beg for alms, and after finishing his alms round, went to the bank of the River Kanvi. He saw that the ground was level and had a beautiful mango grove, and the water of the River Kanvi was extremely delightful, with clear springs flowing gently, cool and warm in harmony. Having seen it, he was delighted and thought: "This ground is level and has a beautiful mango grove. The water of the River Kanvi is extremely delightful, with clear springs flowing gently, cool and warm in harmony. If a noble son wishes to practice cessation, he should do so in this place. I also have something to cease. Should I practice cessation in this quiet place?"

尔时，尊者弥醯为奉侍者。于是，尊者弥醯过夜平旦，著衣持钵，入阇鬪村而行乞食。乞食已竟，往至金鞞河边，见地平正，名好［木＊奈］林，金鞞河水极妙可乐，清泉徐流，冷暖和适，见已欢喜，便作是念：「此地平正，名好［木＊奈］林，金鞞河水极妙可乐，清泉徐流，冷暖和适，若族姓子欲学断者，当于此处，我亦有所断，宁可在此静处学断耶？」

Then, Mihe, having finished his meal, after putting away his robe and bowl, washing his hands and feet, and putting his sitting mat on his shoulder, went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said: "World-Honored One! I, this morning, put on my robe, took my bowl, entered the village of Jatu to beg for alms. After finishing my alms round, I went to the bank of the River Kanvi. I saw that the ground was level and had a beautiful mango grove. The water of the River Kanvi was extremely delightful, with clear springs flowing gently, cool and warm in harmony. Having seen it, I was delighted and thought: 'This ground is level and has a beautiful mango grove. The water of the River Kanvi is extremely delightful, with clear springs flowing gently, cool and warm in harmony. If a noble son wishes to practice cessation, he should do so in this place. I also have something to cease. Should I practice cessation in this quiet place?' World-Honored One! I now wish to go to that mango grove, a quiet place, to practice cessation."

于是，弥醯食讫，中后举衣钵已，澡洗手足，以尼师檀著于肩上，往诣佛所，稽首礼足，却住一面，白曰：「世尊！我今平旦著衣持钵，入阇鬪村而行乞食。乞食已竟，往至金鞞河边，见地平正，名好［木＊奈］林，金鞞河水极妙可乐，清泉徐流，冷暖和适，我见喜已，便作是念：『此地平正，名好［木＊奈］林，金鞞河水极妙可乐，清泉徐流，冷暖和适，若族姓子欲学断者，当于此处，我亦有所断，宁可在此静处学断耶？』世尊！我今欲往至彼［木＊奈］林静处学断。」

At that time, the World-Honored One said: "Mihe! Do you know? I am alone, without an attendant. You should stay a little while, until a bhikkhu comes to be my attendant. Then you may go to that mango grove, a quiet place, to practice cessation."

尔时，世尊告曰：「弥醯！汝今知不？我独无人，无有侍者，汝可小住，须比丘来为吾侍者，汝便可去，至彼［木＊奈］林静处而学。」

Venerable Mihe spoke up a second and a third time, saying: "World-Honored One! I now wish to go to that mango grove, a quiet place, to practice cessation."

尊者弥醯乃至再三白曰：「世尊！我今欲往至彼［木＊奈］林静处学断。」

The World-Honored One also spoke up a second and a third time, saying: "Mihe! Do you know? I am alone, without an attendant. You should stay a little while, until a bhikkhu comes to be my attendant. Then you may go to that mango grove, a quiet place, to practice cessation."

世尊亦复再三告曰：「弥醯！汝今知不？我独无人，无有侍者，汝可小住，须比丘来为吾侍者，汝便可去，至彼［木＊奈］林静处而学。」

Mihe again said: "The World-Honored One is without fabrication, without action, and without observation. World-Honored One! I have fabrication, action, and observation. World-Honored One! I will go to that mango grove, a quiet place, to practice cessation."

弥醯复白曰：「世尊无为无作，亦无所观。世尊！我有为有作而有所观。世尊！我至彼［木＊奈］林静处学断。」

The World-Honored One said: "Mihe! If you wish to seek cessation, what more can I say? Mihe! Go, as you wish." Then Venerable Mihe, having heard the Buddha's words, accepted them well, upheld them well, and recited them well. He then bowed at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and departed. He went to that mango grove. Having entered the grove, he went to the foot of a tree, spread out his sitting mat, and sat cross-legged.

世尊告曰：「弥醯！汝欲求断者，我复何言？弥醯！汝去，随意所欲。」于是，尊者弥醯闻佛所说，善受善持而善诵习，即礼佛足，绕三匝而去，诣彼［木＊奈］林。入林中已，至一树下，敷尼师檀，结加趺坐。

Venerable Mihe, staying in the mango grove, gave rise to three evil and unwholesome thoughts: thoughts of desire, anger, and harm. Because of this, he then thought of the World-Honored One. Then Mihe, in the afternoon, rose from his sitting and went to where the Buddha was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stepped back and stood to one side, and said: "World-Honored One! I went to the mango grove, sat in a quiet place, and gave rise to three evil and unwholesome thoughts: thoughts of desire, anger, and harm. Because of this, I then thought of the World-Honored One."

尊者弥醯住［木＊奈］林中便生三恶不善之念，欲念、恚念及与害念，彼由此故便念世尊。于是，弥醯则于晡时从燕坐起，往诣佛所，稽首礼足，却住一面，白曰：「世尊！我至［木＊奈］林，于静处坐便生三恶不善之念，欲念、恚念及与害念，我由此故便念世尊。」

The World-Honored One said: "Mihe! The mind liberation is not yet ripe, but you wish to ripen it. There are five practices. What are the five? Mihe! A bhikṣu associates with good friends, is together with good friends, and is united with good friends. Mihe! The mind liberation is not yet ripe, but you wish to ripen it. This is called the first practice. Furthermore, Mihe! A bhikṣu cultivates precepts, guards the precepts, and also well restrains deportment and etiquette. Seeing even the slightest fault, he is always fearful and upholds the precepts. Mihe! The mind liberation is not yet ripe, but you wish to ripen it. This is called the second practice. Furthermore, Mihe! A bhikṣu speaks what is meaningful for the noble ones, causing the mind to be soft and free from hindrances, namely speaking about precepts, concentration, wisdom, liberation, the knowledge and insight of liberation, gradual reduction, not enjoying gatherings, few desires, contentment, cutting off, being without desires, extinction, sitting in meditation, and dependent origination. Obtaining such teachings of the śramaṇas is easy and not difficult to obtain. Mihe! The mind liberation is not yet ripe, but you wish to ripen it. This is called the third practice. Furthermore, Mihe! A bhikṣu always practices diligence, cutting off evil and unwholesome dharmas, cultivating all wholesome dharmas, always arousing his own mind, single-mindedly firm, for the sake of all wholesome roots, not abandoning skillful means. Mihe! The mind liberation is not yet ripe, but you wish to ripen it. This is called the fourth practice. Furthermore, Mihe! A bhikṣu cultivates wisdom, observing the arising and ceasing of dharmas, attaining such wisdom, the bright and penetrating wisdom of the noble ones, discerning and understanding, with right ending of suffering. Mihe! The mind liberation is not yet ripe, but you wish to ripen it. This is called the fifth practice.

世尊告曰：「弥醯！心解脱未熟，欲令熟者有五习法。云何为五？弥醯！比丘者自善知识与善知识俱，善知识共和合。弥醯！心解脱未熟，欲令熟者，是谓第一习法。复次，弥醯！比丘者，修习禁戒，守护从解脱，又复善摄威仪礼节，见纤芥罪，常怀畏怖，受持学戒。弥醯！心解脱未熟，欲令熟者，是谓第二习法。复次，弥醯！比丘者，谓所可说圣有义，令心柔软，使心无盖，谓说戒、说定、说慧、说解脱、说解脱知见、说渐损、说不乐聚会、说少欲、说知足、说断、说无欲、说灭、说燕坐、说缘起，得如是比沙门所说，具得易，不难得。弥醯！心解脱未熟，欲令熟者，是谓第三习法。复次，弥醯！比丘者，常行精进，断恶不善，修诸善法，恒自起意，专一坚固，为诸善本，不舍方便。弥醯！心解脱未熟，欲令熟者，是谓第四习法。复次，弥醯！比丘者，修行智慧，观兴衰法，得如是智，圣慧明达，分别晓了，以正尽苦。弥醯！心解脱未熟，欲令熟者，是谓第五习法。

Having these five practices, he further cultivates four dharmas. What are the four? Cultivating impurity to cut off desire; cultivating loving-kindness to cut off anger; cultivating inhalation and exhalation to cut off distracted thoughts; cultivating the perception of impermanence to cut off the conceit of self. Mihe! If a bhikṣu associates with good friends, is together with good friends, and is united with good friends, he will definitely cultivate precepts, guard the precepts, and also well restrain deportment and etiquette. Seeing even the slightest fault, he will always be fearful and uphold the precepts. Mihe! If a bhikṣu associates with good friends, is together with good friends, and is united with good friends, he will definitely obtain what is meaningful for the noble ones, causing the mind to be soft and free from hindrances, namely speaking about precepts, concentration, wisdom, liberation, the knowledge and insight of liberation, gradual reduction, not enjoying gatherings, few desires, contentment, cutting off, being without desires, extinction, sitting in meditation, and dependent origination. Obtaining such teachings of the śramaṇas is easy and not difficult to obtain.

「彼有此五习法已，复修四法。云何为四？修恶露，令断欲；修慈，令断恚；修息出息入，令断乱念；修无常想，令断我慢。弥醯！若比丘自善知识与善知识俱，善知识共和合，当知必修习禁戒，守护从解脱，又复善摄威仪礼节，见纤芥罪，常怀畏怖，受持学戒。弥醯！若比丘自善知识与善知识俱，善知识共和合，当知必得所可说圣有义，令心柔软，使心无盖，谓说戒、说定、说慧、说解脱、说解脱知见、说渐损、说不乐聚会、说少欲、说知足、说断、说无欲、说灭、说燕坐、说缘起，得如是比沙门所说，具得易，不难得。

Mihe! If a bhikṣu associates with good friends, is together with good friends, and is united with good friends, he will definitely practice diligence, cutting off evil and unwholesome dharmas, cultivating all wholesome dharmas, always arousing his own mind, single-mindedly firm, for the sake of all wholesome roots, not abandoning skillful means. Mihe! If a bhikṣu associates with good friends, is together with good friends, and is united with good friends, he will definitely cultivate wisdom, observing the arising and ceasing of dharmas, attaining such wisdom, the bright and penetrating wisdom of the noble ones, discerning and understanding, with right ending of suffering. Mihe! If a bhikṣu associates with good friends, is together with good friends, and is united with good friends, he will definitely cultivate impurity to cut off desire; cultivate loving-kindness to cut off anger; cultivate inhalation and exhalation to cut off distracted thoughts; cultivate the perception of impermanence to cut off the conceit of self. Mihe! If a bhikṣu attains the perception of impermanence, he will definitely attain the perception of no-self. Mihe! If a bhikṣu attains the perception of no-self, he will in this very life cut off all the conceit of self, attain tranquility, cessation, extinction, the unconditioned, and nirvāṇa.

「弥醯！若比丘自善知识与善知识俱，善知识共和合，当知必行精进，断恶不善，修诸善法，恒自起意，专一坚固，为诸善本，不舍方便。弥醯！若比丘自善知识与善知识俱，善知识共和合，当知必行智慧，观兴衰法，得如此智，圣慧明达，分别晓了，以正尽苦。弥醯！若比丘自善知识与善知识俱，善知识共和合，当知必修恶露，令断欲；修慈，令断恚；修息出息入，令断乱念；修无常想，令断我慢。弥醯！若比丘得无常想者，必得无我想。弥醯！若比丘得无我想者，便于现法断一切我慢，得息、灭、尽、无为、涅槃。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The Venerable Mihe and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说如是。尊者弥醯及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
