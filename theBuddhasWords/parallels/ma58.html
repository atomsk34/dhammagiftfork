MĀ 58

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 6 王相应品

58. The Seven Treasures （五八）七宝经

Parallel to: SN 46.42 / EĀ 39.7

seven treasures, marks, four continents cow dung, Magadha
king Vepulla, Lingji the last of the messengers

七宝、相、四洲　　牛粪、摩竭王鞞婆丽陵耆　　天使最在后

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Shravasti, staying at the Jetavana Anathapindika-arya-rama.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "When a wheel-turning king appears in the world, know that the seven treasures will appear in the world. What are the seven? The wheel treasure, elephant treasure, horse treasure, jewel treasure, woman treasure, householder treasure, and general treasure - these are called the seven. When a wheel-turning king appears in the world, know that these seven treasures will appear in the world. Likewise, when a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One appears in the world, know that the seven factors of awakening will appear in the world. What are the seven? The treasure of the factor of awakening of mindfulness, the treasure of the factor of awakening of investigation of phenomena, the treasure of the factor of awakening of energy, the treasure of the factor of awakening of joy, the treasure of the factor of awakening of tranquility, the treasure of the factor of awakening of concentration, and the treasure of the factor of awakening of equanimity - these are called the seven. When a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One appears in the world, know that these seven treasures of the factors of awakening will appear in the world."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「若转轮王出于世时，当知便有七宝出世。云何为七？轮宝、象宝、马宝、珠宝、女宝、居士宝、主兵臣宝，是谓为七。若转轮王出于世时，当知有此七宝出世。如是如来、无所著、等正觉出于世时，当知亦有七觉支宝出于世间。云何为七？念觉支宝、择法觉支、精进觉支、喜觉支、息觉支、定觉支、舍觉支宝，是谓为七。如来、无所著、等正觉出于世时，当知有此七觉支宝出于世间。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
