MĀ 59

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 6 王相应品

59. The Thirty-two Marks （五九）三十二相经

Parallel to: DN 30

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the bhikṣus gathered in the Dharma hall after the midday meal and discussed this matter: "Virtuous ones! It is truly amazing! If a person with the thirty-two marks of a great being accomplishes them, there are only two true and non-false paths. If he stays at home, he will definitely become a wheel-turning king, wise and intelligent, with a fourfold army, ruling the world, freely as he wishes. As a Dharma king, he will accomplish the seven treasures. Those seven treasures are the wheel treasure, elephant treasure, horse treasure, jewel treasure, woman treasure, householder treasure, and general treasure. These are the seven. He will have a thousand sons, with handsome appearances, brave and fearless, able to subdue the armies of others. He will definitely rule over all these lands up to the great ocean, without using weapons, but by teaching with the Dharma, bringing them peace and happiness. If he shaves his head and beard, puts on the monastic robes, leaves home out of true faith, becomes a homeless monk, and studies the path, he will definitely attain the state of a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, with his fame spreading and being heard in the ten directions."

尔时，诸比丘于中食后集坐讲堂，共论此事：「诸贤！甚奇！甚特！大人成就三十二相，必有二处真谛不虚。若在家者，必为转轮王，聪明智慧，有四种军，整御天下，由己自在；如法法王成就七宝，彼七宝者，轮宝、象宝、马宝、珠宝、女宝、居士宝、主兵臣宝，是为七，千子具足，颜貌端正，勇猛无畏，能伏他众，彼必统领此一切地乃至大海，不以刀杖，以法教令，令得安乐。若剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，必得如来、无所著、等正觉，名称流布，周闻十方。」

At that time, the World-Honored One was sitting in meditation. With his pure divine ear surpassing that of human beings, he heard the bhikṣus gathered in the Dharma hall after the midday meal discussing this matter: "Virtuous ones! It is truly amazing! If a person with the thirty-two marks of a great being accomplishes them, there are only two true and non-false paths. If he stays at home, he will definitely become a wheel-turning king, wise and intelligent, with a fourfold army, ruling the world, freely as he wishes. As a Dharma king, he will accomplish the seven treasures. Those seven treasures are the wheel treasure, elephant treasure, horse treasure, jewel treasure, woman treasure, householder treasure, and general treasure. These are the seven. He will have a thousand sons, with handsome appearances, brave and fearless, able to subdue the armies of others. He will definitely rule over all these lands up to the great ocean, without using weapons, but by teaching with the Dharma, bringing them peace and happiness. If he shaves his head and beard, puts on the monastic robes, leaves home out of true faith, becomes a homeless monk, and studies the path, he will definitely attain the state of a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, with his fame spreading and being heard in the ten directions."

尔时，世尊在于燕坐，以净天耳出过于人，闻诸比丘于中食后集坐讲堂，共论此事：「诸贤！甚奇！甚特！大人成就三十二相，必有二处真谛不虚。若在家者，必为转轮王，聪明智慧，有四种军，整御天下，由己自在；如法法王成就七宝，彼七宝者，轮宝、象宝、马宝、珠宝、女宝、居士宝、主兵臣宝，是为七，千子具足，颜貌端正，勇猛无畏，能伏他众，彼必统领此一切地乃至大海，不以刀杖，以法教令，令得安乐。若剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，必得如来、无所著、等正觉，名称流布，周闻十方。」

The World-Honored One, having heard this, then rose from his meditation in the afternoon, went to the Dharma hall, sat before the assembly of bhikṣus, and asked, "What were you discussing when you gathered in the Dharma hall today?"

世尊闻已，则于晡时从燕坐起，往诣讲堂比丘众前敷座而坐，问诸比丘：「汝等今日共论何事集坐讲堂？」

At that time, the bhikṣus said, "World-Honored One! Today we gathered in the Dharma hall to discuss this matter: 'Virtuous ones! It is truly amazing! If a person with the thirty-two marks of a great being accomplishes them, there are only two true and non-false paths. If he stays at home, he will definitely become a wheel-turning king, wise and intelligent, with a fourfold army, ruling the world, freely as he wishes. As a Dharma king, he will accomplish the seven treasures. Those seven treasures are the wheel treasure, elephant treasure, horse treasure, jewel treasure, woman treasure, householder treasure, and general treasure. These are the seven. He will have a thousand sons, with handsome appearances, brave and fearless, able to subdue the armies of others. He will definitely rule over all these lands up to the great ocean, without using weapons, but by teaching with the Dharma, bringing them peace and happiness. If he shaves his head and beard, puts on the monastic robes, leaves home out of true faith, becomes a homeless monk, and studies the path, he will definitely attain the state of a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, with his fame spreading and being heard in the ten directions.' World-Honored One! This is why we gathered in the Dharma hall to discuss this matter."

时，诸比丘白曰：「世尊！我等今日集坐讲堂共论此事：『诸贤！甚奇！甚特！大人成就三十二相，必有二处真谛不虚。若在家者，必为转轮王，聪明智慧，有四种军，整御天下，由己自在；如法法王成就七宝，彼七宝者，轮宝、象宝、马宝、珠宝、女宝、居士宝、主兵臣宝，是为七，千子具足，颜貌端正，勇猛无畏，能伏他众，彼必统领此一切地乃至大海，不以刀杖，以法教令，令得安乐。若剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，必得如来、无所著、等正觉，名称流布，周闻十方。』世尊！我等共论如此事故集坐讲堂。」

Then the World-Honored One said, "Bhikṣus! Do you wish to hear from the Tathāgata about the thirty-two marks of a great being? That is to say, if a person accomplishes them, there are only two true and non-false paths. If he stays at home, he will definitely become a wheel-turning king, wise and intelligent, with a fourfold army, ruling the world, freely as he wishes. As a Dharma king, he will accomplish the seven treasures. Those seven treasures are the wheel treasure, elephant treasure, horse treasure, jewel treasure, woman treasure, householder treasure, and general treasure. These are the seven. He will have a thousand sons, with handsome appearances, brave and fearless, able to subdue the armies of others. He will definitely rule over all these lands up to the great ocean, without using weapons, but by teaching with the Dharma, bringing them peace and happiness. If he shaves his head and beard, puts on the monastic robes, leaves home out of true faith, becomes a homeless monk, and studies the path, he will definitely attain the state of a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, with his fame spreading and being heard in the ten directions."

于是，世尊告曰：「比丘！汝等欲得从如来闻三十二相耶？谓大人所成，必有二处真谛不虚。若在家者，必为转轮王，聪明智慧，有四种军，整御天下，由己自在；如法法王成就七宝，彼七宝者，轮宝、象宝、马宝、珠宝、女宝、居士宝、主兵臣宝，是为七，千子具足，颜貌端正，勇猛无畏，能伏他众，彼必统领此一切地乃至大海，不以刀杖，以法教令，令得安乐。若剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，必得如来、无所著、等正觉，名称流布，周闻十方。」

Then the bhikṣus said, "World-Honored One! Now is the time. Sugata! Now is the time. If the World-Honored One would explain the thirty-two marks to the bhikṣus, the bhikṣus would listen and remember them well."

时，诸比丘闻已，白曰：「世尊！今正是时。善逝！今正是时。若世尊为诸比丘说三十二相者，诸比丘闻已当善受持。」

The World-Honored One said, "Bhikṣus! Listen carefully, listen carefully! Consider it well, and I will explain it to you in detail." The bhikṣus accepted the teaching and listened.

世尊告曰：「诸比丘！谛听，谛听！善思念之，吾当为汝广分别说。」时，诸比丘受教而听。

The Buddha said, "Bhikṣus! The feet of a great person are stable and upright, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, a great person has a wheel with a thousand spokes growing on the soles of their feet, complete in all aspects, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, the toes of a great person are slender and long, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, the feet of a great person are straight and upright, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, the heels and ankles of a great person are full and even on both sides, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, the ankles of a great person are rounded, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, the body hair of a great person grows upward, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, the hands and feet of a great person have webbed fingers, like a king goose, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, the hands and feet of a great person are extremely delicate and soft, like a cotton flower, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, the skin of a great person is soft and fine, dust and water do not adhere to it, this is called a mark of a great person.

佛言：「大人足安平立，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人足下生轮，轮有千辐，一切具足，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人足指纤长，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人足周正直，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人足跟踝后两边平满，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人足两踝佣，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人身毛上向，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人手足网缦，犹如鴈王，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人手足极妙柔弱软敷，犹兜罗华，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人肌皮软细，尘水不著，是谓大人大人之相。

Furthermore, a great person has one hair growing from each pore, the color of the hair is like a blue-green conch shell, growing to the right, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, a great person has intestines like a deer king, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, a great person has a hidden male organ like a fine horse king, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, a great person has a well-proportioned body, like a nyagrodha tree, the upper and lower parts are well-proportioned, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, a great person has an unbent body, standing upright, stretching the arms to touch the knees, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, a great person has a body the color of yellow gold, like refined gold, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, a great person has seven parts of the body that are full, the two hands, two feet, two shoulders, and neck, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, a great person has a large upper body, like a lion, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, a great person has lion-like cheeks, this is called a mark of a great person.

「复次，大人一一毛，一一毛者，身一孔一毛生，色若绀青，如螺右旋，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人鹿［蹲－酋＋（十／田／ㄙ）］肠，犹如鹿王，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人阴马藏，犹良马王，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人身形圆好，犹如尼拘类树，上下圆相称，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人身不阿曲，身不曲者，平立申手以摩其膝，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人身黄金色，如紫磨金，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人身七处满，七处满者，两手、两足、两肩及颈，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人其上身大，犹如师子，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人师子颊车，是谓大人大人之相。

Furthermore, a great person has a straight back, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, a great person has well-connected shoulders and a full neck, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, a great person has forty teeth, even teeth, no gaps between the teeth, white teeth, and the first taste of flavor, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, a great person has a pleasant voice like a kalavinka bird, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, a great person has a broad and long tongue, the tongue can come out of the mouth and cover the face, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, a great person has full tear ducts, like an ox king, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, a great person has eyes the color of blue-green, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, a great person has a flesh-covered crown on the head, round and well-proportioned, with hair growing to the right, this is called a mark of a great person. Furthermore, a great person has hair growing between the eyebrows, pure and white, growing to the right, this is called a mark of a great person.

「复次，大人脊背平直，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人两肩上连，通颈平满，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人四十齿牙、平齿、不踈齿、白齿、通味第一味，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人梵音可爱，其声犹如加罗毘伽，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人广长舌，广长舌者，舌从口出遍覆其面，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人承泪处满，犹如牛王，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人眼色绀青，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人顶有肉髻，团圆相称，发螺右旋，是谓大人大人之相。复次，大人眉间生毛，洁白右萦，是谓大人大人之相。

"Bhikṣus! If a great person possesses these thirty-two marks, there are two true and non-false places. If he stays at home, he will definitely become a wheel-turning king, wise and intelligent, with four kinds of armies, ruling the world, self-reliant; as a Dharma king, he will accomplish the seven treasures. Those seven treasures are the wheel treasure, elephant treasure, horse treasure, jewel treasure, woman treasure, householder treasure, and general treasure, these are the seven. He will have a thousand sons, with handsome appearances, brave and fearless, able to subdue other armies. He will definitely rule over all these lands up to the great ocean, without using weapons, but by teaching with the Dharma, bringing them peace and happiness. If he shaves his beard and hair, puts on the monastic robe, with utmost faith, leaves home, becomes homeless, and studies the path, he will definitely attain the state of Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, with his fame spreading and being heard in the ten directions."

「诸比丘！大人成就此三十二相，必有二处真谛不虚。若在家者，必为转轮王，聪明智慧，有四种军，整御天下，由己自在；如法法王成就七宝，彼七宝者，轮宝、象宝、马宝、珠宝、女宝、居士宝、主兵臣宝，是为七，千子具足，颜貌端正，勇猛无畏，能伏他众，彼必统领此一切地乃至大海，不以刀杖，以法教令，令得安乐。若剃除须发] cf.［丽】，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，必得如来、无所著、等正觉，名称流布，周闻十方。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
