MĀ 63

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 6 王相应品

63. At Vebhaḷiṅga （六三）鞞婆陵耆经

Parallel to: MN 81

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Kosala.

一时，佛游拘萨罗国。

At that time, the World-Honored One was traveling with a great assembly of bhikṣus. On the way, he smiled happily. Venerable Ānanda saw the World-Honored One smile, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "World-Honored One, what is the cause and condition for your smile? Tathāgatas, Arhats, Perfectly Enlightened Ones do not smile without cause or condition. I wish to hear the meaning of it."

尔时，世尊与大比丘众俱行道，中路欣然而笑。尊者阿难见世尊笑，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！何因缘笑？诸佛如来、无所著、等正觉若无因缘，终不妄笑。愿闻其意。」

At that time, the World-Honored One said, "Ānanda! In this place, Kāśyapa Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One sat here and taught the Dharma to his disciples."

彼时，世尊告曰：「阿难！此处所中，迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉在此处坐，为弟子说法。」

Then Venerable Ānanda quickly prepared a seat there, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said, "World-Honored One! I only wish that the World-Honored One would also sit here and teach the Dharma to the disciples, so that this place will be frequented by two Tathāgatas, Arhats, Perfectly Enlightened Ones."

于是，尊者阿难即在彼处速疾敷座，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！唯愿世尊亦坐此处，为弟子说法，如是此处为二如来、无所著、等正觉所行。」

Then the World-Honored One sat on the seat prepared by Venerable Ānanda. Having sat down, he said, "Ānanda! In this place, Kāśyapa Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One had a lecture hall. Kāśyapa Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One sat there and taught the Dharma to his disciples. Ānanda! In this place, there was formerly a village named Vebhaṅga, which was extremely prosperous, peaceful, and had many people. Ānanda! In the village of Vebhaṅga, there was a great brāhman householder named Amita, who was extremely wealthy, with immeasurable wealth, livestock, and industries. He had many households and fiefs, and was well-endowed with various things. Ānanda! The great brāhman householder Amita had a son named Udraka Rāmaputra, who was raised by his parents and born pure. For seven generations, his parents' lineage was unbroken. He was born without evil, was well-versed, and recited the four Vedas. He deeply understood the five kinds of statements: causes, conditions, right, texts, and plays. Ānanda! The young man Udraka had a good friend named Nātaputta, a potter, who was always loved and delighted by the young man Udraka.

尔时，世尊便于彼处坐尊者阿难所敷之座，坐已，告曰：「阿难！此处所中，迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉有讲堂，迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉于中坐已，为弟子说法。阿难！此处所中昔有村邑，名鞞婆陵耆，极大丰乐，多有人民。阿难！鞞婆陵耆村邑之中有梵志大长者，名曰无恚，极大富乐，资财无量，畜牧产业不可称计，封户食邑种种具足。阿难！梵志大长者无恚有子，名优多罗摩纳，为父母所举，受生清净，乃至七世父母不绝种族，生生无恶，博闻总持，诵过四典经，深达因、缘、正、文、戏五句说。阿难！优多罗童子有善朋友，名难提波罗陶师，常为优多罗童子之所爱念，喜见无厌。

Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta took refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Saṃgha. He had no doubts about the Three Jewels, and was not confused about suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path. He attained faith, upheld the precepts, was learned, generous, and accomplished wisdom. He gave up killing, stopped killing, and abandoned weapons. He had a sense of shame and conscience, had compassion, and benefited all beings, even insects. He purified his mind from killing. Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta gave up stealing, stopped stealing, and took only what was given. He enjoyed giving and was always happy to give, without holding back. He did not expect anything in return. He purified his mind from stealing. Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta gave up unwholesome conduct, stopped unwholesome conduct, diligently practiced wholesome conduct, diligently practiced the sublime conduct, was pure and undefiled, free from desire and stopped sexual activity. He purified his mind from unwholesome conduct.

「阿难！难提波罗陶师归佛、归法、归比丘众，不疑三尊，不惑苦、习、灭、道，得信、持戒、博闻、惠施、成就智慧，离杀、断杀、弃舍刀杖，有惭有愧，有慈悲心，饶益一切乃至蜫虫，彼于杀生净除其心。阿难！难提波罗陶师离不与取，断不与取，与] cf.［丽】之乃取，乐于与取，常好布施，欢喜无悋，不望其报，彼于不与取净除其心。阿难！难提波罗陶师离非梵行，断非梵行，勤修梵行，精勤妙行，清净无秽，离欲断婬，彼于非梵行净除其心。

Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta gave up false speech, stopped false speech, spoke the truth, enjoyed the truth, abided in the truth without wavering, was trustworthy, and did not deceive the world. He purified his mind from false speech. Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta gave up divisive speech, stopped divisive speech, practiced non-divisive speech, did not break others apart, did not hear this and tell that in order to break them apart, did not hear that and tell this in order to break them apart, wanted to reconcile those who were divided, and brought joy to those who were united. He did not form factions, did not enjoy factions, and did not praise factions. He purified his mind from divisive speech. Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta gave up harsh speech, stopped harsh speech. Whatever he spoke was gentle, soft, pleasant to the ear, agreeable, delightful, and brought peace and happiness to others. His voice was clear and did not frighten others, and it brought others to concentration. He spoke such words. He purified his mind from harsh speech. Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta gave up idle chatter, stopped idle chatter. He spoke at the right time, spoke the truth, spoke the Dharma, spoke the meaning, spoke about cessation, spoke about the joy of cessation, and spoke about matters that were timely and appropriate. He gave good advice and good rebuke. He purified his mind from idle chatter.

「阿难！难提波罗陶师离妄言，断妄言，真谛言，乐真谛，住真谛不移动，一切可信，不欺世间，彼于妄言净除其心。阿难！难提波罗陶师离两舌，断两舌，行不两舌，不破坏他，不闻此语彼，欲破坏此，不闻彼语此，欲破坏彼，离者欲合，合者欢喜，不作群党，不乐群党，不称群党，彼于两舌净除其心。阿难！难提波罗陶师离麤言，断麤言。若有所言辞气麤犷，恶声逆耳，众所不喜，众所不爱，使他苦恼，令不得定，断如是言。若有所说清和柔润，顺耳入心，可喜可爱，使他安乐，言声具了，不使人畏，令他得定，说如是言，彼于麤言净除其心。阿难！难提波罗陶师离绮语，断绮语。时说、真说、法说、义说、止息说、乐止息说，事顺时得宜，善教善诃，彼于绮语净除其心。

Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta gave up commerce, stopped commerce, abandoned weighing and measuring, abandoned receiving goods, did not bind others, did not hope for a discount, and did not deceive others with small profits. He purified his mind from commerce. Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta gave up taking widows and girls, stopped taking widows and girls. He purified his mind from taking widows and girls. Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta gave up taking slaves and servants, stopped taking slaves and servants. He purified his mind from taking slaves and servants. Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta gave up taking elephants, horses, cattle, and sheep, stopped taking elephants, horses, cattle, and sheep. He purified his mind from taking elephants, horses, cattle, and sheep. Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta gave up taking chickens and pigs, stopped taking chickens and pigs. He purified his mind from taking chickens and pigs. Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta gave up taking fields, businesses, and shops, stopped taking fields, businesses, and shops. He purified his mind from taking fields, businesses, and shops. Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta gave up taking raw rice, wheat, and beans, stopped taking raw rice, wheat, and beans. He purified his mind from taking raw rice, wheat, and beans.

「阿难！难提波罗陶师离治生，断治生，弃舍称量及斗斛，弃舍受货，不缚束人，不望折斗量，不以小利侵欺于人，彼于治生净除其心。阿难！难提波罗陶师离受寡妇、童女，断受寡妇、童女，彼于受寡妇、童女净除其心。阿难！难提波罗陶师离受奴婢，断受奴婢，彼于受奴婢净除其心。阿难！难提波罗陶师离受象、马、牛、羊，断受象、马、牛、羊，彼于受象、马、牛、羊净除其心。阿难！难提波罗陶师离受鸡、猪，断受鸡、猪，彼于受鸡、猪净除其心。阿难！难提波罗陶师离受田业、店肆，断受田业、店肆，彼于受田业、店肆净除其心。阿难！难提波罗陶师离受生稻、麦、豆，断受生稻、麦、豆，彼于受生稻、麦、豆净除其心。

Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta gave up alcohol, stopped alcohol. He purified his mind from drinking alcohol. Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta gave up high and wide beds, stopped high and wide beds. He purified his mind from high and wide beds. Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta gave up garlands, necklaces, perfumes, and cosmetics, stopped garlands, necklaces, perfumes, and cosmetics. He purified his mind from garlands, necklaces, perfumes, and cosmetics. Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta gave up singing, dancing, and acting, and going to see them, stopped singing, dancing, and acting, and going to see them. He purified his mind from singing, dancing, and acting, and going to see them. Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta gave up receiving gold and silver, stopped receiving gold and silver. He purified his mind from receiving gold and silver. Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta gave up eating after the midday meal, stopped eating after the midday meal. He ate at one time, not at night, and learned to eat at the right time. He purified his mind from eating after the midday meal.

「阿难！难提波罗陶师离酒、断酒，彼于饮酒净除其心。阿难！难提波罗陶师离高广大床，断高广大床，彼于高广大床净除其心。阿难！难提波罗陶师离华鬘、璎珞、涂香、脂粉，断华鬘、璎珞、涂香、脂粉，彼于华鬘、璎珞、涂香、脂粉净除其心。阿难！难提波罗陶师离歌舞倡妓及往观听，断歌舞倡妓及往观听，彼于歌舞倡妓及往观听净除其心。阿难！难提波罗陶师离受生色像宝，断受生色像宝，彼于生色像宝净除其心。阿难！难提波罗陶师离过中食，断过中食，常一食，不夜食，学时食，彼于过中食净除其心。

Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta, for as long as he lived, kept his hands away from hoes and shovels, did not dig the earth himself, nor did he instruct others. If the bank of a river collapsed or a rat damaged the earth, he would take it to make vessels. He would put them aside and say to the buyers, 'If you have peas, rice, wheat, sesame, mung beans, mustard seeds, pour them into the vessels and take them away as you wish.' Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta, for as long as he lived, served his parents. His parents were blind and relied on others. Therefore he served them.

「阿难！难提波罗陶师尽形寿手离铧锹，不自掘地，亦不教他，若水岸崩土及鼠伤土，取用作器，举著一面，语买者曰：『汝等若有豌豆、稻、麦、大小麻豆、豍豆、芥子，泻已持器去，随意所欲。』阿难！难提波罗陶师尽形寿供侍父母，父母无目，唯仰于人，是故供侍。

Ānanda! The potter Nātaputta, having passed the night, went to the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa in the morning. Having arrived, he paid homage, sat to one side, and the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa taught him the Dharma, inspiring, arousing, and gladdening him. Having taught him the Dharma with countless skillful means, inspiring, arousing, and gladdening him, he remained silent. Ānanda! Then the potter Nātaputta, having been taught the Dharma by the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa, inspired, aroused, and gladdened, rose from his seat, paid homage at the feet of the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa, circumambulated him three times, and departed.

「阿难！难提波罗陶师过夜平旦，往诣迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉所，到已作礼，却坐一面。迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，默然而住。阿难！于是，难提波罗陶师，迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉为其说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，即从坐起，礼迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉足已，绕三匝而去。

At that time, the young man Uttara, riding in a white horse-drawn carriage with five hundred young men, passed the night and in the morning left the village of Vepacitti to go to a place without people, intending to teach the disciples from various countries to read the Brāhmanical texts. Then the young man Uttara saw from afar the potter Nātaputta coming, and upon seeing him, asked, 'Nātaputta! Where are you coming from?' Nātaputta replied, 'I am coming from paying homage and serving the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa. Uttara! You should come with me to pay homage and serve the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa.' Then the young man Uttara replied, 'Nātaputta! I do not want to see a shaven-headed monk. A shaven-headed monk cannot attain the path, because the path is difficult to attain.' Then the potter Nātaputta grabbed the topknot of the young man Uttara's head and pulled him down from the carriage. Then the young man Uttara thought, 'This potter Nātaputta is always not playful, not crazy, not foolish. Now he grabs my topknot, there must be a reason.' Having thought so, he said, 'Nātaputta! I will follow you, I will follow you.' Nātaputta was pleased and said again, 'It is very good to go.

「尔时，优多罗童子乘白马车，与五百童子俱，过夜平旦，从鞞婆陵耆村邑出，往至一无事处，欲教若干国来诸弟子等，令读梵志书。于是，优多罗童子遥见难提波罗陶师来，见已便问：『难提波罗！汝从何来？』难提波罗答曰：『我今从迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉所供养礼事来。优多罗！汝可共我往诣迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉所供养礼事。』于是，优多罗童子答曰：『难提波罗！我不欲见秃头沙门，秃沙门不应得道，道难得故。』于是，难提波罗陶师捉优多罗童子头髻，牵令下车。于是，优多罗童子便作是念：『此难提波罗陶师常不调戏，不狂不痴，今捉我头髻，必当有以。』念已，语曰：『难提波罗！我随汝去，我随汝去。』难提波罗喜，复语曰：『去者甚善。』

Then the potter Nātaputta and the young man Uttara went together to where the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa was. Having arrived, they paid homage and sat to one side. The potter Nātaputta said to the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa, 'World-Honored One! This young man Uttara is my friend. He always loves me and is always happy to see me, never tiring of it. He has no faith and respect for the World-Honored One. I only pray that the World-Honored One will teach him the Dharma well, so that he may be delighted and attain faith and respect.' Then the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa taught the Dharma to the potter Nātaputta and the young man Uttara, inspiring, arousing, and gladdening them. Having taught them the Dharma with countless skillful means, inspiring, arousing, and gladdening them, he remained silent. Then the potter Nātaputta and the young man Uttara, having been taught the Dharma by the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa, inspired, aroused, and gladdened, rose from their seats, paid homage at the feet of the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa, circumambulated him three times, and departed.

「于是，难提波罗陶师与优多罗童子共往诣迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉所，到已作礼，却坐一面。难提波罗陶师白迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉曰：『世尊！此优多罗童子是我朋友，彼常见爱，常喜见我，无有厌足。彼于世尊无信敬心，唯愿世尊善为说法，令彼欢喜得信敬心。』于是，迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉为难提波罗陶师及优多罗童子说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，默然而住。于是，难提波罗陶师及优多罗童子，迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉为其说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，即从坐起，礼迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉足，绕三匝而去。

Then the young man Uttara returned not far and asked, 'Nātaputta! Having heard such subtle and wonderful Dharma from the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa, why do you stay at home? Are you unable to leave and study the noble path?' Then the potter Nātaputta replied, 'Uttara! You should know that I have served my parents for my whole life. My parents are blind and rely on others. I serve and attend to my parents.' Then the young man Uttara asked Nātaputta, 'May I go forth from the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa to study the path, receive the full ordination, become a bhikṣu, and practice the holy life?' Then the potter Nātaputta and the young man Uttara went together again to where the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa was. Having arrived, they paid homage and sat to one side.

「于是，优多罗童子还去不远，问曰：『难提波罗！汝从迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉得闻如是微妙之法，何意住家？不能舍离学圣道耶？』于是，难提波罗陶师答曰：『优多罗！汝自知我尽形寿供养父母，父母无目，唯仰于人，我以供养侍父母故。』于是，优多罗童子问难提波罗：『我可得从迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉出家学道，受于具足，得作比丘，行梵行耶？』于是，难提波罗陶师及优多罗童子即从彼处复往诣迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉所，到已作礼，却坐一面。

The potter Nātaputta said to the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa, 'World-Honored One! Not long after the young man Uttara left, he asked me, "Nātaputta! Having heard such subtle and wonderful Dharma from the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa, why do you stay at home? Are you unable to leave and study the noble path?" World-Honored One! I replied to him, "Uttara! You should know that I have served my parents for my whole life. My parents are blind and rely on others. I serve and attend to my parents." Uttara then asked me again, "Nātaputta! May I go forth from the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa to study the path, receive the full ordination, become a bhikṣu, and practice the holy life?" I pray that the World-Honored One will allow him to go forth to study the path, grant him the full ordination, and make him a bhikṣu.' The Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa silently accepted Nātaputta's request. Then the potter Nātaputta, knowing that the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa had silently accepted, rose from his seat, paid homage, circumambulated him three times, and departed.

「难提波罗陶师白迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉曰：『世尊！此优多罗童子还去不远，而问我言：「难提波罗！汝从迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉得闻如是微妙之法，何意住家？不能舍离学圣道耶？」世尊！我答彼曰：「优多罗！汝自知我尽形寿供养父母，父母无目，唯仰于人，我以供养侍父母故。」优多罗复问我曰：「难提波罗！我可得从迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉出家学道，受于具足，得作比丘，行梵行耶？」愿世尊度彼出家学道，授与具足，得作比丘。』迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉为难提波罗默然而受。于是，难提波罗陶师知迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉默然受已，即从坐起，稽首作礼，绕三匝而去。

Then, not long after the potter Nātaputta left, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa allowed the young man Uttara to go forth to study the path, granted him the full ordination, and made him a bhikṣu. Having granted him the full ordination, he stayed in the village of Vebhaṅga for several days, taking up his robe and bowl. Together with a large assembly of bhikṣus, he traveled to the city of Benares, wandering and traveling, and arrived at the city of Benares, wandering and staying in the Deer Park at the place of the seers.

「于是，迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉，难提波罗去后不久，度优多罗童子出家学道，授与具足，出家学道。授与具足已，于鞞婆陵耆村邑随住数日，摄持衣钵，与大比丘众俱共游行，欲至波罗［木＊奈］迦私国邑，展转游行，便到波罗［木＊奈］迦私国邑，游波罗［木＊奈］住仙人处鹿野园中。

Then King Udayana heard that the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa was traveling in the country of Kāsi, together with a large assembly of bhikṣus, and had arrived at the Deer Park at the place of the seers in the city of Benares. Having heard this, he told his charioteer, 'You should prepare the chariot. I wish to go to where the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa is.' At that time, the charioteer received the king's order, immediately prepared the chariot, and after preparing it, returned to inform the king, 'The chariot is ready. Please do as you wish, Your Majesty.'

「于是，颊鞞王闻迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉游行迦私国，与大比丘众俱，到此波罗［木＊奈］住仙人处鹿野园中。颊鞞王闻已，告御者曰：『汝可严驾，我今欲往诣迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉所。』时，彼御者受王教已，即便严驾。严驾已讫，还白王曰：『已严好车，随天王意。』

Then King Udayana, having mounted the fine chariot, left the city of Benares and went to the Deer Park at the place of the seers where the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa was. At that time, King Udayana saw from afar the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa among the trees, upright and beautiful, like the moon among the stars, shining brightly, dazzling like a golden mountain, with all the marks complete, majestic and majestic, with his faculties calm and collected, without obstruction, accomplished in self-control, with his mind at peace and silent. Having seen him, he got down from the chariot and walked to where the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa was. Having arrived, he paid homage and sat to one side. After King Udayana had sat to one side, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa taught the Dharma for him, inspiring and uplifting him, bringing about joy and happiness. Having taught the Dharma for him with immeasurable skillful means, inspiring and uplifting him, bringing about joy and happiness, he remained silent.

「于是，颊鞞王乘好车已，从波罗［木＊奈］出，往诣仙人住处鹿野园中。时，颊鞞王遥见树间迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉，端正姝好，犹星中月，光耀炜晔，晃若金山，相好具足，威神巍巍，诸根寂定，无有蔽碍，成就调御，息心静默。见已下车，步诣迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉所，到已作礼，却坐一面。颊鞞王坐一面已，迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，默然而住。

Then King Udayana, for whom the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa had taught the Dharma, inspiring and uplifting him, bringing about joy and happiness, immediately rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms, and said to the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa, 'May the World-Honored One, together with the assembly of bhikṣus, accept my invitation tomorrow.' The Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa accepted King Udayana's invitation with silence. Then King Udayana, knowing that the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa had accepted with silence, bowed his head in homage, circumambulated him three times, and departed, returning to his own residence. During the night, he prepared various abundant and delicious food and drink. Having prepared it during the night, in the morning he arranged the seats and announced, 'The World-Honored One! The time has come. The meal is ready. May the World-Honored One please come at his convenience.'

「于是，颊鞞王，迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉为其说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手而向，白迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉曰：『唯愿世尊明受我请，及比丘众。』迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉为颊鞞王默然受请。于是，颊鞞王知迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉默然受已，稽首作礼，绕三匝而去，还归其家。于夜施设极美净妙种种丰饶食噉含消，即于其夜供办已讫，平旦敷床唱曰：『世尊！今时已到，食具已办，唯愿世尊以时临顾。』

Then the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa, having passed the night, in the morning, put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and together with the assembly of bhikṣus, went to the residence of King Udayana. In the assembly of bhikṣus, he spread out a seat and sat down. Then King Udayana, seeing the Buddha and the assembly of bhikṣus seated, personally served the water for washing, and with various abundant and delicious food and drink, served it himself, causing them to be satisfied. After they had finished eating and the bowls were put away, he served the water for washing. Having finished, he spread out a small seat, sat separately, and listened to the Dharma. After King Udayana had sat down, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa taught the Dharma for him, inspiring and uplifting him, bringing about joy and happiness. Having taught the Dharma for him with immeasurable skillful means, inspiring and uplifting him, bringing about joy and happiness, he remained silent.

「于是，迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉过夜平旦，著衣持钵，诸比丘众侍从世尊，往诣颊鞞王家，在比丘众上敷座而坐。于是，颊鞞王见佛及比丘众坐已，自行澡水，以极美净妙种种丰饶食噉含消，手自斟酌，令得饱满，食讫收器，行澡水竟，敷一小床，别坐听法。颊鞞王坐已，迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，默然而住。

Then King Udayana, for whom the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa had taught the Dharma, inspiring and uplifting him, bringing about joy and happiness, immediately rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms, and said to the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa, 'May the World-Honored One, together with the assembly of bhikṣus, accept my invitation tomorrow.' The Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa accepted King Udayana's invitation with silence. Then King Udayana, knowing that the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa had accepted with silence, bowed his head in homage, circumambulated him three times, and departed, returning to his own residence. During the night, he prepared various abundant and delicious food and drink. Having prepared it during the night, in the morning he arranged the seats and announced, 'The World-Honored One! The time has come. The meal is ready. May the World-Honored One please come at his convenience.'

「于是，颊鞞王，迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉为其说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手而向，白迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉曰：『唯愿世尊于此波罗［木＊奈］受我夏坐，及比丘众，我为世尊作五百房、五百床褥，及施拘执如此白粳米。王之所食种种诸味，饭饲世尊及比丘众。』迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉告颊鞞王曰：『止！止！大王！但心喜足。』颊鞞王如是至再三，叉手而向，白迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉曰：『唯愿世尊于此波罗［木＊奈］受我夏坐，及比丘众，我为世尊作五百房、五百床褥，及施拘执如此白粳米。王之所食种种诸味，饭饲世尊及比丘众。』迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉亦再三告颊鞞王曰：『止！止！大王！但心喜足。』

Then King Udayana, unable to bear it, unwilling, greatly distressed, thought, 'The Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa will not accept my invitation to spend the summer retreat here in Vārāṇasī, together with the assembly of bhikṣus.' Having thought this, King Udayana said to the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa, 'World-Honored One! Is there any other layperson who serves the World-Honored One as I do?'

「于是，颊鞞王不忍不欲，心大忧戚：『迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉不能为我于此波罗［木＊奈］而受夏坐，及比丘众。』作是念已，颊鞞王白迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉曰：『世尊！颇更有在家白衣，奉事世尊如我者耶？』

The Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa said to King Udayana, 'There is, in your realm, in the village of Vepacitti, which is very prosperous and peaceful, with many people. Great King! In the village of Vepacitti there is a potter named Nanda-prabha. Great King! Nanda-prabha takes refuge in the Buddha, takes refuge in the Dharma, takes refuge in the assembly of bhikṣus, has no doubts about the Three Jewels, is not confused about suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path, has faith, upholds the precepts, is learned, generous, and accomplished in wisdom. He has given up killing, stopped killing, abandoned weapons, has a sense of shame, a sense of decency, a compassionate mind, and benefits all beings, even insects. He has purified his mind of killing. Great King! Nanda-prabha has given up stealing, stopped stealing, takes only what is given, delights in taking what is given, is always fond of giving, joyfully gives without stinginess, and does not expect anything in return. He has purified his mind of stealing. Great King! Nanda-prabha has given up sexual misconduct, stopped sexual misconduct, diligently practices celibacy, diligently practices the sublime practices, is pure and undefiled, has given up desire and stopped sexual activity. He has purified his mind of sexual misconduct.

「迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉告颊鞞王曰：『有，在王境界鞞婆陵耆村极大丰乐，多有人民。大王！彼鞞婆陵耆村中有难提波罗陶师。大王！难提波罗陶师归佛、归法、归比丘众，不疑三尊，不惑苦、习、灭、道，得信、持戒、博闻、惠施、成就智慧，离杀、断杀，弃舍刀杖，有慙有愧，有慈悲心，饶益一切乃至蜫虫，彼于杀生净除其心。大王！难提波罗陶师离不与取，断不与取，与之乃取，乐于与取，常好布施，欢喜无悋，不望其报，彼于不与取净除其心。大王！难提波罗陶师离非梵行，断非梵行，勤修梵行，精懃妙行，清净无秽，离欲断婬，彼于非梵行净除其心。

Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter has given up false speech, stopped false speech, speaks the truth, delights in the truth, abides in the truth without wavering, is trustworthy, does not deceive the world. He has purified his mind of false speech. Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter has given up divisive speech, stopped divisive speech, practices non-divisive speech, does not break others apart, does not hear this and tell that in order to break them apart, does not hear that and tell this in order to break them apart. He wants to reconcile those who are divided, delights those who are united, does not form factions, does not delight in factions, does not praise factions. He has purified his mind of divisive speech. Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter has given up harsh speech, stopped harsh speech. Whatever he says is gentle, soft, pleasant to the ear, agreeable, pleasing to the masses, not causing distress, leading to concentration. He has stopped such speech. Whatever he says is clear, gentle, soft, pleasant to the ear, agreeable, pleasing to the masses, causing happiness, with a voice that is distinct, not causing fear, leading to concentration. He speaks such words. He has purified his mind of harsh speech. Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter has given up idle chatter, stopped idle chatter. He speaks at the right time, speaks the truth, speaks the Dharma, speaks the meaning, speaks cessation, speaks delight in cessation, speaks in accordance with the matter, at the right time, appropriately, teaches well, and reproaches well. He has purified his mind of idle chatter.

「『大王！难提波罗陶师离妄言，断妄言，真谛言，乐真谛，住真谛不移动，一切可信，不欺世间，彼于妄言净除其心。大王！难提波罗陶师离两舌，断两舌，行不两舌，不破坏他，不闻此语彼，欲破坏此，不闻彼语此，欲破坏彼，离者欲合，合者欢喜，不作群党，不乐群党，不称群党，彼于两舌净除其心。大王！难提波罗陶师离麤言，断麤言。若有所言辞气麤犷，恶声逆耳，众所不喜，众所不爱，使他苦恼，令不得定，断如是言。若有所说清和柔润，顺耳入心，可喜可爱，使他安乐，言声具了，不使人畏，令他得定，说如是言，彼于麤言净除其心。大王！难提波罗陶师离绮语，断绮语，时说、真说、法说、义说、止息说、乐止息说，事顺时得宜，善教善诃，彼于绮语净除其心。

Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter has given up commerce, stopped commerce, abandoned weighing and measuring, also does not accept goods, does not bind people, does not hope for a discount, does not deceive others with small profits. He has purified his mind of commerce. Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter has given up accepting widows and girls, stopped accepting widows and girls. He has purified his mind of accepting widows and girls. Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter has given up accepting slaves and servants, stopped accepting slaves and servants. He has purified his mind of accepting slaves and servants. Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter has given up accepting elephants, horses, cattle, and sheep, stopped accepting elephants, horses, cattle, and sheep. He has purified his mind of accepting elephants, horses, cattle, and sheep. Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter has given up accepting chickens and pigs, stopped accepting chickens and pigs. He has purified his mind of accepting chickens and pigs. Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter has given up accepting fields, businesses, and shops, stopped accepting fields, businesses, and shops. He has purified his mind of accepting fields, businesses, and shops. Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter has given up accepting raw rice, wheat, and beans, stopped accepting raw rice, wheat, and beans. He has purified his mind of accepting raw rice, wheat, and beans.

「『大王！难提波罗陶师离治生，断治生，弃舍称量及斗斛，亦不受货，不缚束人，不望折斗量，不以小利侵欺于人，彼于治生净除其心。大王！难提波罗陶师离受寡妇、童女，断受寡妇、童女，彼于受寡妇、童女净除其心。大王！难提波罗陶师离受奴婢，断受奴婢，彼于受奴婢净除其心。大王！难提波罗陶师离受象、马、牛、羊，断受象、马、牛、羊，彼于受象、马、牛、羊净除其心。大王！难提波罗陶师离受鸡、猪，断受鸡、猪，彼于受鸡、猪净除其心。大王！难提波罗陶师离受田业、店肆，断受田业、店肆，彼于受田业、店肆净除其心。大王！难提波罗陶师离受生稻、麦、豆，断受生稻、麦、豆，彼于受生稻、麦、豆净除其心。

Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter has given up alcohol, stopped alcohol. He has purified his mind of drinking alcohol. Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter has given up high and wide beds, stopped high and wide beds. He has purified his mind of high and wide beds. Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter has given up garlands, necklaces, perfumes, and cosmetics, stopped garlands, necklaces, perfumes, and cosmetics. He has purified his mind of garlands, necklaces, perfumes, and cosmetics. Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter has given up singing, dancing, and acting, and going to see them, stopped singing, dancing, and acting, and going to see them. He has purified his mind of singing, dancing, and acting, and going to see them. Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter has given up accepting raw silk and jewels, stopped accepting raw silk and jewels. He has purified his mind of accepting raw silk and jewels. Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter has given up eating after noon, stopped eating after noon. He always eats one meal a day, not at night, and practices eating at the right time. He has purified his mind of eating after noon.

「『大王！难提波罗陶师离酒、断酒，彼于饮酒净除其心。大王！难提波罗陶师离高广大床，断高广大床，彼于高广大床净除其心。大王！难提波罗陶师离华鬘、璎珞、涂香、脂粉，断华鬘、璎珞、涂香、脂粉，彼于华鬘、璎珞、涂香、脂粉净除其心。大王！难提波罗陶师离歌舞倡妓及往观听，断歌舞倡妓及往观听，彼于歌舞倡妓及往观听净除其心。大王！难提波罗陶师离受生色像宝，断受生色像宝，彼于受生色像宝净除其心。大王！难提波罗陶师离过中食，断过中食，常一食，不夜食，学时食，彼于过中食净除其心。

Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter, for as long as he lives, keeps his hands away from hoes and shovels, does not dig the ground himself, nor does he instruct others. If the bank of a river collapses or a rat damages the soil, he takes it to make vessels, puts them aside, and tells the buyers, "If you have peas, rice, wheat, sesame, mung beans, mustard seeds, pour them in and take the vessels as you wish." Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter, for as long as he lives, serves his parents. His parents are blind and rely on others. Therefore he serves them.

「『大王！难提波罗陶师尽形寿手离铧锹，不自掘地，亦不教他，若水岸崩土及鼠伤土，取用作器，举著一面，语买者言：「汝等若有豌豆、稻、麦、大小麻豆、豍豆、芥子，泻已持器去，随意所欲。」大王！难提波罗陶师尽形寿供侍父母，父母无目，唯仰于人，是故供侍。』

Great King! I remember in the past, I was wandering near the village of Vipulya. Great King! At that time, in the morning, I put on my robe, took my bowl, entered the village of Vipulya to beg for food, and gradually begged for food. I went to Nanda-prabha the potter's house. At that time, Nanda-prabha was out for a small matter. Great King! I asked Nanda-prabha the potter's parents, "Elders! Where is the potter now?" They replied to me, "World-Honored One! The attendant is temporarily out for a small matter. Well-Gone One! The attendant is temporarily out for a small matter. World-Honored One! There is rice in the basket and bean soup in the pot. Please take it as you wish out of compassion." Great King! I then accepted the Uḍḍiyāna method and took the soup and rice from the basket and pot and left.

「『大王！我忆昔时依鞞婆陵耆村邑游行。大王！我尔时平旦著衣持钵，入鞞婆陵耆村邑乞食，次第乞食，往到难提波罗陶师家。尔时，难提波罗为小事故，出行不在。大王！我问难提波罗陶师父母曰：「长老！陶师今在何处？」彼答我曰：「世尊！侍者为小事故，暂出不在。善逝！侍者为小事故，暂出不在。世尊！箩中有麦饭，釜中有豆羹，唯愿世尊为慈愍故随意自取。」大王！我便受郁单曰法，即于箩釜中取羹饭而去。

Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter returned home later and saw that the rice in the basket was less and the soup in the pot was reduced. He asked his parents, "Who took the soup and rice?" His parents replied, "Good son! Today the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa came here to beg for food. He took the soup and rice from the basket and pot." Hearing this, Nanda-prabha the potter thought, "I have good benefits and great merit. The Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa freely took the soup and rice in my house." He was delighted and cross-legged, his mind at peace and silent, for seven days. During the fifteenth day, he was joyful. His parents were also joyful for seven days.

「『难提波罗陶师于后还家，见箩中饭少，釜中羹减，白父母曰：「谁取羹饭？」父母答曰：「贤子！今日迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉至此乞食，彼于箩釜中取羹饭去。」难提波罗陶师闻已，便作是念：「我有善利，有大功德，迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉于我家中随意自在。」彼以此欢喜结跏趺坐，息心静默，至于七日，于十五日中而得欢乐，其家父母于七日中亦得欢乐。

Great King! I remember in the past, I was wandering near the village of Vipulya. Great King! At that time, in the morning, I put on my robe, took my bowl, entered the village of Vipulya to beg for food, and gradually begged for food. I went to Nanda-prabha the potter's house. At that time, Nanda-prabha was out for a small matter. Great King! I asked Nanda-prabha the potter's parents, "Elders! Where is the potter now?" They replied to me, "World-Honored One! The attendant is temporarily out for a small matter. Well-Gone One! The attendant is temporarily out for a small matter. World-Honored One! There is cooked rice in the large pot and soup in the small pot. Please take it as you wish out of compassion." Great King! I then accepted the Uḍḍiyāna method and took the soup and rice from the large and small pots and left.

「『复次，大王！我忆昔时依鞞婆陵耆村邑游行。大王！我尔时平旦著衣持钵，入鞞婆陵耆村邑乞食，次第乞食，往到难提波罗陶师家。尔时，难提波罗为小事故，出行不在。大王！我问难提波罗陶师父母曰：「长老！陶师今在何处？」彼答我曰：「世尊！侍者为小事故，暂出不在。善逝！侍者为小事故，暂出不在。世尊！大釜中有粳米饭，小釜中有羹，唯愿世尊为慈愍故随意自取。」大王！我便受欝单曰法，即于大小釜中取羹饭去。

Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter returned home later and saw that the rice in the large pot was less and the soup in the small pot was reduced. He asked his parents, "Who took the rice from the large pot and the soup from the small pot?" His parents replied, "Good son! Today the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa came here to beg for food. He took the soup and rice from the large and small pots and left." Hearing this, Nanda-prabha the potter thought, "I have good benefits and great merit. The Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa freely took the soup and rice in my house." He was delighted and cross-legged, his mind at peace and silent, for seven days. During the fifteenth day, he was joyful. His parents were also joyful for seven days.

「『难提波罗陶师于后还家，见大釜中饭少，小釜中羹减，白父母曰：「谁大釜中取饭，小釜中取羹？」父母答曰：「贤子！今日迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉至此乞食，彼于大小釜中取羹饭去。」难提波罗陶师闻已，便作是念：「我有善利，有大功德，迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉于我家中随意自在。」彼以此欢喜结跏趺坐，息心静默，至于七日，于十五日中而得欢乐，其家父母于七日中亦得欢乐。

"Great King! I remember in the past, I spent the summer retreat near the village of Vipulya. Great King! At that time, I had a new house that was not yet covered. Nanda-prabha the potter had a new roof for his old pottery house. Great King! I told my attendant monks, "You should go and dismantle Nanda-prabha the potter's old pottery house and bring it to cover my house." The attendant monks accepted my instruction, went to Nanda-prabha the potter's house, pulled down the old pottery house, made bundles of it, and brought it to cover my house. Nanda-prabha the potter's parents heard that the old pottery house was being dismantled. Hearing this, they asked, "Who is dismantling Nanda-prabha's old pottery house?" The monks replied, "Elders! We are the attendant monks of the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa. We are dismantling Nanda-prabha the potter's old pottery house, making bundles of it, to cover the house of the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa." Nanda-prabha the potter's parents said, "Gentlemen! Take it as you wish. There is no one to stop you.

「『复次，大王！我忆昔时依鞞婆陵耆村邑而受夏坐。大王！我尔时新作屋未覆，难提波罗陶师故陶屋新覆。大王！我告瞻侍比丘曰：「汝等可去坏难提波罗陶师故陶屋，持来覆我屋。」瞻侍比丘即受我教，便去往至难提波罗陶师家，挽坏故陶屋，作束持来用覆我屋。难提波罗陶师父母闻坏故陶屋，闻已，问曰：「谁坏难提波罗故陶屋耶？」比丘答曰：「长老！我等是迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉瞻侍比丘，挽坏难提波罗陶师故陶屋，作束用覆迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉屋。」难提波罗父母语曰：「诸贤！随意持去，无有制者。」

Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter returned home later and saw that the old pottery house was being dismantled. He asked his parents, "Who is dismantling my old pottery house?" His parents replied, "Good son! Today the attendant monks of the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa dismantled the old pottery house, made bundles of it, and took it away to cover the house of the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa." Hearing this, Nanda-prabha the potter thought, "I have good benefits and great merit. The Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa freely took the soup and rice in my house." He was delighted and cross-legged, his mind at peace and silent, for seven days. During the fifteenth day, he was joyful. His parents were also joyful for seven days.

「『难提波罗陶师于后还家，见挽坏故陶屋，白父母曰：「谁挽坏我故陶屋耶？」父母答曰：「贤子！今日迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉瞻侍比丘挽坏故陶屋，作束持去，用覆迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉屋。」难提波罗陶师闻已，便作是念：「我有善利，有大功德，迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉于我家中随意自在。」彼以此欢喜结加趺坐，息心静默，至于七日，于十五日中而得欢乐，其家父母于七日中亦得欢乐。

Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter's old pottery house did not leak at all during the four months of summer. Why? Because of the Buddha's spiritual power. Great King! Nanda-prabha the potter had no unwillingness, no unwillingness, no sorrow in his mind: "The Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa freely took the soup and rice in my house." Great King! You are unwilling, you are unwilling, your mind is greatly sorrowful: "The Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa did not accept my invitation to spend the summer retreat here in Vārāṇasī, together with the assembly of monks." Then the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa taught the Dharma for King Udayana, inspiring him with yearning, bringing him to accomplishment and joy. Having taught the Dharma for him with immeasurable skillful means, inspiring him with yearning, bringing him to accomplishment and joy, he rose from his seat and departed.

「『大王！难提波] cf.［丽】罗陶师故陶屋，竟夏四月都不患漏。所以者何？蒙佛威神故。大王！难提波罗陶师无有不忍，无有不欲，心无忧戚：「迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉于我家中随意自在。」大王！汝有不忍，汝有不欲，心大忧戚：「迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉不受我请，于此波罗［木＊奈］而受夏坐，及比丘众。」于是，迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉为颊鞞王说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，从坐起去。

Great King! Not long after the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One Kāśyapa left, King Udayana ordered his attendants, "You should load five hundred carts with white rice and various delicacies from the king's table, and take them to Nanda-prabha the potter's house, and say to him: 'Nanda-prabha! These five hundred carts are loaded with white rice and various delicacies from the king's table. King Udayana sends them to you as a gift out of compassion. You should accept them now.'" At that time, the attendants received the king's order and loaded five hundred carts with white rice and various delicacies from the king's table, and sent them to Nanda-prabha the potter's house. Having arrived, they said, "Nanda-prabha the potter! These five hundred carts are loaded with white rice and various delicacies from the king's table. King Udayana sends them to you as a gift out of compassion. You should accept them now." Then Nanda-prabha the potter declined and did not accept, and said to the attendants, "Gentlemen! King Udayana's kingdom has many important affairs and wide expenditures. I know this, and therefore I do not accept."'

「『时，颊鞞王于迦叶如来、无所著、等正觉去后不久，便勅侍者：「汝等可以五百乘车载满白粳米，王之所食种种诸味，载至难提波罗陶师家，而语之曰：『难提波罗！此五百乘车载满白粳米，王之所食种种诸味，颊鞞王送来饷汝，为慈愍故，汝今当受。』」时，彼侍者受王教已，以五百乘车载满白粳米，王之所食种种诸味，送诣难提波罗陶师家。到已，语曰：「难提波罗陶师！此五百乘车载满白粳米，王之所食种种诸味，颊鞞王送来饷汝，为慈愍故，汝今当受。」于是，难提波罗陶师辞让不受，语侍者曰：「诸贤！颊鞞王家国大事多，费用处广，我知如此，以故不受。」』」

The Buddha told Ānanda, "What do you think? Do you think that the young man Utpalavarṇa at that time was someone else? Do not think like that. You should know that he was me. Ānanda! At that time, I spoke the Dharma for the benefit of myself, for the benefit of others, for the benefit of many people, out of compassion for the world, for the sake of benefit and happiness for gods and humans. At that time, the Dharma I spoke did not reach the ultimate, did not reach the ultimate purity, did not reach the ultimate practice of the holy life, did not reach the ultimate completion of the practice of the holy life. At that time, I was not free from birth, old age, sickness, death, wailing, and sorrow, and I was not yet able to be free from all suffering. Ānanda! Now I have appeared in the world as a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, Perfect in Knowledge and Conduct, Well-Gone, Knower of the Secular World, Unsurpassed Person, Tamer of Men, Teacher of Gods and Men, called the Buddha, the Fortunate One. Now I speak the Dharma for my own benefit, for the benefit of others, for the benefit of many people, out of compassion for the world, for the sake of benefit and happiness for gods and humans. Now the Dharma I speak reaches the ultimate, reaches the ultimate purity, reaches the ultimate practice of the holy life, reaches the ultimate completion of the practice of the holy life. Now I am free from birth, old age, sickness, death, wailing, and sorrow. Now I have attained freedom from all suffering."

佛告阿难：「于意云何？尔时童子优多罗者，汝谓异人耶？莫作斯念，当知即是我也。阿难！我于尔时为自饶益，亦饶益他，饶益多人，愍伤世间，为天、为人求义及饶益，求安隐快乐。尔时说法不至究竟，不究竟白净，不究竟梵行，不究竟梵行讫。尔时不离生老病死、啼哭忧戚，亦未能得脱一切苦。阿难！我今出世，如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐，我今自饶益，亦饶益他，饶益多人，愍伤世间，为天、为人求义及饶益，求安隐快乐。我今说法得至究竟，究竟白净，究竟梵行，究竟梵行讫。我今已离生老病死、啼哭忧戚，我今已得脱一切苦。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The Venerable Ānanda and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。尊者阿难及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
