Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 6 王相应品

64. The Divine Messengers （六四）天使经

Parallel to: MN 130 / AN 3.36 (~) / DĀ 30 (~) / EĀ 32.4

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One addressed the bhikṣus, saying: "With my pure divine eye, surpassing that of humans, I see the passing away and rebirth of beings, their good and bad forms, whether fine or not, and their going and coming to good and bad places, according to the actions of those beings. I see it as it truly is. If these beings have practiced evil bodily, verbal, and mental actions, have slandered the noble ones, and have accomplished wrong views and wrong view karma, because of this, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely go to an evil place and be reborn in hell. If these beings have practiced fine bodily, verbal, and mental actions, have not slandered the noble ones, and have accomplished right views and right view karma, because of this, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely ascend to a good place and be reborn in heaven.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「我以净天眼出过于人，见此众生死时、生时，好色、恶色，或妙、不妙，往来善处及不善处，随此众生之所作业，见其如真。若此众生成就身恶行，口、意恶行，诽谤圣人，邪见成就邪见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。若此众生成就身妙行，口、意妙行，不诽谤圣人，正见成就正见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必升善处，乃生天上。

Just as when it rains heavily, bubbles appear and disappear on the water's surface. If a person with eyes stands in one place and observes their arising and disappearing, I am also like this. With my pure divine eye, surpassing that of humans, I see the passing away and rebirth of beings, their good and bad forms, whether fine or not, and their going and coming to good and bad places, according to the actions of those beings. I see it as it truly is. If these beings have practiced evil bodily, verbal, and mental actions, have slandered the noble ones, and have accomplished wrong views and wrong view karma, because of this, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely go to an evil place and be reborn in hell. If these beings have practiced fine bodily, verbal, and mental actions, have not slandered the noble ones, and have accomplished right views and right view karma, because of this, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely ascend to a good place and be reborn in heaven.

「犹大雨时，水上之泡，或生或灭，若有目人住一处，观生时、灭时；我亦如是，以净天眼出过于人，见此众生死时、生时，好色、恶色，或妙、不妙，往来善处及不善处，随此众生之所作业，见其如真。若此众生成就身恶行，口、意恶行，诽谤圣人，邪见成就邪见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。若此众生成就身妙行，口、意妙行，不诽谤圣人，正见成就正见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必升善处，乃生天上。

Just as when it rains heavily, raindrops fall up and down. If a person with eyes stands in one place and observes their rising and falling, I am also like this. With my pure divine eye, surpassing that of humans, I see the passing away and rebirth of beings, their good and bad forms, whether fine or not, and their going and coming to good and bad places, according to the actions of those beings. I see it as it truly is. If these beings have practiced evil bodily, verbal, and mental actions, have slandered the noble ones, and have accomplished wrong views and wrong view karma, because of this, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely go to an evil place and be reborn in hell. If these beings have practiced fine bodily, verbal, and mental actions, have not slandered the noble ones, and have accomplished right views and right view karma, because of this, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely ascend to a good place and be reborn in heaven.

「犹大雨时，雨堕之渧或上或下，若有目人住一处，观上时、下时；我亦如是，以净天眼出过于人，见此众生，死时、生时，好色、恶色，或妙、不妙，往来善处及不善处，随此众生之所作业，见其如真。若此众生成就身恶行，口、意恶行，诽谤圣人，邪见成就邪见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。若此众生成就身妙行，口、意妙行，不诽谤圣人，正见成就正见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必升善处，乃生天上。

Just as a lapis lazuli gem, naturally pure and flawless, with eight facets well-polished, strung on a fine string, may be blue, yellow, red, black, or white. If a person with eyes stands in one place and observes this lapis lazuli gem, naturally pure and flawless, with eight facets well-polished, strung on a fine string, may be blue, yellow, red, black, or white; I am also like this. With my pure divine eye, surpassing that of humans, I see the passing away and rebirth of beings, their good and bad forms, whether fine or not, and their going and coming to good and bad places, according to the actions of those beings. I see it as it truly is. If these beings have practiced evil bodily, verbal, and mental actions, have slandered the noble ones, and have accomplished wrong views and wrong view karma, because of this, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely go to an evil place and be reborn in hell. If these beings have practiced fine bodily, verbal, and mental actions, have not slandered the noble ones, and have accomplished right views and right view karma, because of this, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely ascend to a good place and be reborn in heaven.

「犹琉璃珠，清净自然，生无瑕秽，八楞善治，贯以妙绳，或青或黄，或赤、黑、白。若有目人住一处，观此琉璃珠，清净自然，生无瑕秽，八楞善治，贯以妙绳，或青或黄，或赤、黑、白；我亦如是，以净天眼出过于人，见此众生死时、生时，好色、恶色，或妙、不妙，往来善处及不善处，随此众生之所作业，见其如真。若此众生成就身恶行，口、意恶行，诽谤圣人，邪见成就邪见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。若此众生成就身妙行，口、意妙行，不诽谤圣人，正见成就正见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必升善处，乃生天上。

Just as two houses share a common door, with many people coming and going. If a person with eyes stands in one place and observes their coming and going, I am also like this. With my pure divine eye, surpassing that of humans, I see the passing away and rebirth of beings, their good and bad forms, whether fine or not, and their going and coming to good and bad places, according to the actions of those beings. I see it as it truly is. If these beings have practiced evil bodily, verbal, and mental actions, have slandered the noble ones, and have accomplished wrong views and wrong view karma, because of this, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely go to an evil place and be reborn in hell. If these beings have practiced fine bodily, verbal, and mental actions, have not slandered the noble ones, and have accomplished right views and right view karma, because of this, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely ascend to a good place and be reborn in heaven.

「犹如两屋共一门，多人出入。若有目人住一处，观出时入时；我亦如是，以净天眼出过于人，见此众生死时、生时，好色、恶色，或妙、不妙，往来善处及不善处，随此众生之所作业，见其如真。若此众生成就身恶行，口、意恶行，诽谤圣人，邪见成就邪见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。若此众生成就身妙行，口、意妙行，不诽谤圣人，正见成就正见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必升善处，乃生天上。

Just as a person with eyes stands on a high building and observes the people below coming and going, sitting, lying down, running, and jumping, I am also like this. With my pure divine eye, surpassing that of humans, I see the passing away and rebirth of beings, their good and bad forms, whether fine or not, and their going and coming to good and bad places, according to the actions of those beings. I see it as it truly is. If these beings have practiced evil bodily, verbal, and mental actions, have slandered the noble ones, and have accomplished wrong views and wrong view karma, because of this, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely go to an evil place and be reborn in hell. If these beings have practiced fine bodily, verbal, and mental actions, have not slandered the noble ones, and have accomplished right views and right view karma, because of this, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely ascend to a good place and be reborn in heaven.

「若有目人住高楼上，观于下人往来周旋、坐卧走踊；我亦如是，以净天眼出过于人，见此众生死时、生时，好色、恶色，或妙、不妙，往来善处及不善处，随此众生之所作业，见其如真。若此众生成就身恶行，口、意恶行，诽谤圣人，邪见成就邪见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必至恶处，生地狱中。若此众生成就身妙行，口、意妙行，不诽谤圣人，正见成就正见业，彼因缘此，身坏命终，必升善处，乃生天上。

If there are beings born among humans who are not filial to their parents, do not respect śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, do not practice the truth, do not perform meritorious deeds, and do not fear the sins of the next world, because of this, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will be born in the realm of King Yama. The people of King Yama will take them to the king and say: 'Your Majesty! This being, when he was a human, was not filial to his parents, did not respect śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, did not practice the truth, did not perform meritorious deeds, and did not fear the sins of the next world. I only pray that Your Majesty will punish him for his crimes.'

「若有众生生于人间，不孝父母，不知尊敬沙门、梵志，不行如实，不作福业，不畏后世罪，彼因缘此，身坏命终，生阎王境界。阎王人收送诣王所，白曰：『天王！此众生本为人时，不孝父母，不知尊敬沙门、梵志，不行如实，不作福业，不畏后世罪，唯愿天王处当其罪。』

"Then King Yama will first ask the messenger to question, examine, teach, and admonish him: 'Have you ever seen the first messenger come?' That person will reply: 'No, Your Majesty!' King Yama will further ask: 'Have you ever seen in a village or town a boy or girl, young and weak, lying on their back in their own excrement and urine, unable to speak to their parents, who then pick them up and place them in a clean place, bathe their body, and make them clean?' That person will reply: 'Yes, Your Majesty!' King Yama will further ask: 'When you became aware later, why did you not think: "I myself have the nature of birth, not apart from birth. I should practice excellent bodily, verbal, and mental actions."'" That person will say: 'Your Majesty! Am I really ruined, long decayed, and forever lost?' King Yama will tell him: 'You are really ruined, long decayed, and forever lost. Now I will punish you, as is done to those who practice heedlessness and are heedless. This evil karma of yours was not done by your parents, nor by the king, nor by the gods, nor by śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas. You yourself did evil and unwholesome karma. Therefore you will now definitely receive retribution.'

「于是，阎王以初天使善问、善捡、善教、善诃：『汝颇曾见初天使来耶？』彼人答曰：『不见也。天王！』阎王复问：『汝本不见一村邑中，或男或女，幼小婴孩，身弱柔软，仰向自卧大小便中，不能语父母，父母抱移离不净处，澡浴其身，令得净洁。』彼人答曰：『见也。天王！』阎王复问：『汝于其后有识知时，何不作是念：「我自有生法，不离于生，我应行妙身、口、意业。」』彼人白曰：『天王！我了败坏，长衰永失耶？』阎王告曰：『汝了败坏，长衰永失，今当考汝，如治放逸行、放逸人，汝此恶业非父母为，非王非天，亦非沙门、梵志所为，汝本自作恶不善业。是故汝今必当受报。』

"King Yama, having first asked the messenger to question, examine, teach, and admonish him, will further ask the second messenger to question, examine, teach, and admonish him: 'Have you ever seen the second messenger come?' That person will reply: 'No, Your Majesty!' King Yama will further ask: 'Have you ever seen in a village or town a man or woman who is very old, with a life span that has reached its limit, with a life that is about to end, with falling teeth, white hair, a bent body, and a walking stick, with a trembling body?' That person will reply: 'Yes, Your Majesty!' King Yama will further ask: 'When you became aware later, why did you not think: "I myself have the nature of aging, not apart from aging. I should practice excellent bodily, verbal, and mental actions."'" That person will say: 'Your Majesty! Am I really ruined, long decayed, and forever lost?' King Yama will tell him: 'You are really ruined, long decayed, and forever lost. Now I will punish you, as is done to those who practice heedlessness and are heedless. This evil karma of yours was not done by your parents, nor by the king, nor by the gods, nor by śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas. You yourself did evil and unwholesome karma. Therefore you will now definitely receive retribution.'

「阎王以此初天使善问、善捡、善教、善诃已，复以第二天使善问、善捡、善教、善诃：『汝颇曾见第二天使来耶？』彼人答曰：『不见也。天王！』阎王复问：『汝本不见一村邑中，或男或女，年耆极老，寿过苦极，命埀欲讫，齿落头白，身曲軁步，拄杖而行，身体战动耶？』彼人答曰：『见也。天王！』阎王复问：『汝于其后有识知时，何不作是念：「我自有老法，不离于老，我应行妙身、口、意业。」』彼人白曰：『天王！我了败坏，长衰永失耶？』阎王告曰：『汝了败坏，长衰永失，今当考汝，如治放逸行、放逸人，汝此恶业非父母为，非王非天，亦非沙门、梵志所为，汝本自作恶不善业。是故汝今必当受报。』

"King Yama, having first asked the messenger to question, examine, teach, and admonish him, will further ask the third messenger to question, examine, teach, and admonish him: 'Have you ever seen the third messenger come?' That person will reply: 'No, Your Majesty!' King Yama will further ask: 'Have you ever seen in a village or town a man or woman who is seriously ill, lying on a bed, a couch, or the ground, with extreme pain in their body, very heavy pain, unbearable, causing their life to be cut short?' That person will reply: 'Yes, Your Majesty!' King Yama will further ask: 'When you became aware later, why did you not think: "I myself have the nature of illness, not apart from illness. I should practice excellent bodily, verbal, and mental actions."'" That person will say: 'Your Majesty! Am I really ruined, long decayed, and forever lost?' King Yama will tell him: 'You are really ruined, long decayed, and forever lost. Now I will punish you, as is done to those who practice heedlessness and are heedless. This evil karma of yours was not done by your parents, nor by the king, nor by the gods, nor by śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas. You yourself did evil and unwholesome karma. Therefore you will now definitely receive retribution.'

「阎王以此第二天使善问、善捡、善教、善诃已，复以第三天使善问、善捡、善教、善诃：『汝颇曾见第三天使来耶？』彼人答曰：『不见也。天王！』阎王复问：『汝本不见一村邑中，或男或女，疾病困笃，或坐卧床，或坐卧榻，或坐卧地，身生极苦甚重苦，不可爱念，令促命耶？』彼人答曰：『见也。天王！』阎王复问：『汝于其后有识知时，何不作是念：「我自有病法，不离于病，我应行妙身、口、意业。」』彼人白曰：『天王！我了败坏，长衰永失耶？』阎王告曰：『汝了败坏，长衰永失，今当考汝，如治放逸行、放逸人，汝此恶业] cf.［丽】非父母为，非王非天，亦非沙门、梵志所为，汝本自作恶不善业，是故汝今必当受报。』

"King Yama, having first asked the messenger to question, examine, teach, and admonish him, will further ask the fourth messenger to question, examine, teach, and admonish him: 'Have you ever seen the fourth messenger come?' That person will reply: 'No, Your Majesty!' King Yama will further ask: 'Have you ever seen in a village or town a man or woman who has died, for one, two, up to six or seven days, pecked at by crows and kites, eaten by foxes and wolves, burned by fire, buried in the ground, or rotten and decayed?' That person will reply: 'Yes, Your Majesty!' King Yama will further ask: 'When you became aware later, why did you not think: "I myself have the nature of death, not apart from death. I should practice excellent bodily, verbal, and mental actions."'" That person will say: 'Your Majesty! Am I really ruined, long decayed, and forever lost?' King Yama will tell him: 'You are really ruined, long decayed, and forever lost. Now I will punish you, as is done to those who practice heedlessness and are heedless. This evil karma of yours was not done by your parents, nor by the king, nor by the gods, nor by śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas. You yourself did evil and unwholesome karma. Therefore you will now definitely receive retribution.'

「阎王以此第三天使善问、善捡、善教、善诃已，复以第四天使善问、善捡、善教、善诃：『汝颇曾见第四天使来耶？』彼人答曰：『不见也。天王！』阎王复问：『汝本不见一村邑中，或男或女，若死亡时，或一、二日，至六、七日，乌鵄所啄，犲狼所食，或以火烧，或埋地中，或烂腐坏耶？』彼人答曰：『见也。天王！』阎王复问：『汝于其后有识知时，何不作是念：「我自有死法，不离于死，我应行妙身、口、意业。」』彼人白曰：『天王！我了败坏，长衰永失耶？』阎王告曰：『汝了败坏，长衰永失，今当考汝，如治放逸行、放逸人，汝此恶业非父母为，非王非天，亦非沙门、梵志所为，汝本自作恶不善业，是故汝今必当受报。』

"King Yama, having first asked the messenger to question, examine, teach, and admonish him, will further ask the fifth messenger to question, examine, teach, and admonish him: 'Have you ever seen the fifth messenger come?' That person will reply: 'No, Your Majesty!' King Yama will further ask: 'Have you ever seen the king's men catch criminals and punish them in various ways, cutting off their hands, feet, or both hands and feet, cutting off their ears, noses, or both ears and noses, or cutting them up, pulling out their beards and hair, or both beards and hair, or wrapping them in cloth and burning them with fire, or burying them in sand and burning them with fire, or putting them in an iron donkey's belly, or putting them in an iron pig's mouth, or putting them in an iron tiger's mouth and burning them, or putting them in a copper cauldron, or putting them in an iron cauldron and boiling them, or cutting them into pieces, or piercing them with sharp stakes, or hooking them with hooks, or lying them on an iron bed and pouring boiling oil on them, or sitting them on an iron mortar and pounding them with an iron pestle, or using dragons, snakes, or other creatures, or whipping them with whips, or beating them with sticks, or hitting them with clubs, or impaling them alive on high poles, or beheading them?' That person will reply: 'Yes, Your Majesty!' King Yama will further ask: 'When you became aware later, why did you not think: "I now see evil and unwholesome dharmas."'" That person will say: 'Your Majesty! Am I really ruined, long decayed, and forever lost?' King Yama will tell him: 'You are really ruined, long decayed, and forever lost. Now I will punish you, as is done to those who practice heedlessness and are heedless. This evil karma of yours was not done by your parents, nor by the king, nor by the gods, nor by śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas. You yourself did evil and unwholesome karma. Therefore you will now definitely receive retribution.'

「阎王以此第四天使善问、善捡、善教、善诃已，复以第五天使善问、善捡、善教、善诃：『汝颇曾见第五天使来耶？』彼人答曰：『不见也。天王！』阎王复问：『汝本不见王人捉犯罪人，种种考治，截手、截足、或截手足，截耳、截鼻、或截耳鼻，或脔脔割，拔须、拔发、或拔须发，或著槛中衣裹火烧，或以沙壅草缠火烧，或内铁驴腹中，或著铁猪口中，或置铁虎口中烧，或安铜釜中，或著铁釜中煑，或段段截，或利叉刺，或以钩钩，或卧铁床以沸油浇，或坐铁臼以铁杵捣，或以龙蛇［虫＊哲］，或以鞭鞭，或以杖挝，或以棒打，或生贯高标上，或枭其首耶？』彼人答曰：『见也。天王！』阎王复问：『汝于其后有识知时，何不作是念：「我今现见恶不善法。」』彼人白曰：『天王！我了败坏，长衰永失耶？』阎王告曰：『汝了败坏，长衰永失。今当考汝，如治放逸行、放逸人，汝此恶业非父母为，非王非天，亦非沙门、梵志所为，汝本自作恶不善业，是故汝今必当受报。』

That person will reply: 'No, Your Majesty!' King Yama will further ask: 'Have you ever seen the king's men catch criminals and punish them in various ways, cutting off their hands, feet, or both hands and feet, cutting off their ears, noses, or both ears and noses, or cutting them up, pulling out their beards and hair, or both beards and hair, or wrapping them in cloth and burning them with

「阎王以此第五天使善问、善捡、善教、善诃已，即付狱卒。狱卒便捉持，著四门大地狱中，于是颂曰：

The four pillars have four gates, the walls are square with
twelve corners, made of iron as a fence, covered with iron
above. The ground of hell is made of iron, covered with
blazing iron fire, deep immeasurable yojanas, reaching to the bottom of
the earth. Extremely evil, unbearable, the color of fire is hard
to look at, seeing it, the body hair stands on end
fearful and terrified, very painful. Those who fall into hell, feet
up and head down, slander the sages, the well-tamed and well-disciplined.

「『四柱有四门，　　壁方十二楞，以铁为垣墙，　　其上铁覆盖。地狱内铁地，　　炽燃铁火布，深无量由延，　　乃至地底住。极恶不可受，　　火色难可视，见已身毛竖，　　恐惧怖甚苦。彼堕生地狱，　　脚上头在下，诽谤诸圣人，　　调御善清善。』

After a long time, for the sake of those beings, the eastern gate of the great hell with four pillars opens. When the eastern gate opens, those beings run towards it, seeking refuge and protection. When they gather there in immeasurable hundreds of thousands, the eastern gate of hell closes again. They suffer extremely severe pain there, crying and wailing, fainting and lying on the ground, but they cannot die. They must exhaust their evil and unwholesome karma. After a long time, the southern, western, and northern gates also open. When the northern gate opens, those beings run towards it, seeking refuge and protection. When they gather there in immeasurable hundreds of thousands, the northern gate of hell closes again. They suffer extremely severe pain there, crying and wailing, fainting and lying on the ground, but they cannot die. They must exhaust their evil and unwholesome karma.

「有时于后极大久远，为彼众生故，四门大地狱东门便开。东门开已，彼众生等走来趣向，欲求安处，求所归依，彼若集聚无量百千已，地狱东门便还自闭，彼于其中受极重苦，啼哭唤呼，心闷卧地，终不得死，要令彼恶不善业尽。极大久远，南门、西门、北门复开。北门开已，彼众生等走来趣向，欲求安处，求所归依，彼若集聚无量百千已，地狱北门复还自闭，彼于其中受极重苦，啼哭唤呼，心闷卧地，终不得死，要令彼恶不善业尽。

After a long time, those beings emerge from the great hell with four pillars through the four gates. Next to the great hell with four gates is the hell of rocky peaks, filled with fire, without smoke or flames, causing them to walk on it, going back and forth. The skin, flesh, and blood of their two feet are exhausted when they step down, but when they lift their feet, they grow back, as before. They are punished like this for immeasurable hundreds of thousands of years, suffering extremely severe pain, but they cannot die. They must exhaust their evil and unwholesome karma.

「复于后时极大久远，彼众生等从四门大地狱出，四门大地狱次生峰岩地狱，火满其中，无烟无焰，令行其上，往来周旋。彼之两足皮肉及血，下足则尽，举足则生，还复如故。治彼如是无量百千岁，受极重苦，终不得死，要令彼恶不善业尽。

After a long time, those beings emerge from the hell of rocky peaks and enter the great hell of excrement, filled with excrement to a depth of immeasurable hundreds of feet. Those beings all fall into it. In the great hell of excrement, many worms are born, called Lingqulai, with white bodies and black heads, their beaks like needles. These worms drill through the feet of those beings, after drilling through the feet, they drill through the shin bones, after drilling through the shin bones, they drill through the thigh bones, after drilling through the thigh bones, they drill through the hip bones, after drilling through the hip bones, they drill through the spine bones, after drilling through the spine bones, they drill through the shoulder bones, neck bones, and skull bones. After drilling through the skull bones, they eat the brain until it is all gone. Those beings are oppressed like this for immeasurable hundreds of thousands of years, suffering extremely severe pain, but they cannot die. They must exhaust their evil and unwholesome karma.

「复于后时极大久远，彼众生等从峰岩大地狱出，峰岩大地狱次生粪屎大地狱，满中粪屎，深无量百丈。彼众生等尽堕其中，彼粪屎大地狱中生众多虫，虫名凌瞿来，身白头黑，其觜如针，此虫钻破彼众生足，破彼足已，复破䏝膓骨，破䏝膓骨已，复破髀骨，破髀骨已，复破𣎑骨，破𣎑骨已，复破脊骨，破脊骨已，复破肩骨、颈骨、头骨，破头骨已，食头脑尽。彼众生等如是逼迫无量百千岁，受极重苦，终不得死，要令彼恶不善业尽。

After a long time, those beings emerge from the great hell of excrement and enter the great hell of iron-clad forest. They see it and think of it as cool, thinking, 'We should go there to cool off.' Those beings run towards it, seeking shelter and refuge. When they gather there in immeasurable hundreds of thousands, they enter the great hell of iron-clad forest. In the great hell of iron-clad forest, a great hot wind comes from the four directions. When the hot wind comes, the iron-clad forest falls down. When the iron-clad forest falls, it cuts off hands, feet, or both hands and feet, cuts off ears, nose, or both ears and nose, and cuts off other limbs and joints. The blood from the cut body is smeared for immeasurable hundreds of thousands of years, suffering extremely severe pain, but they cannot die. They must exhaust their evil and unwholesome karma.

「复于后时极大久远，彼众生等从粪屎大地狱出，粪屎大地狱次生铁鍱林大地狱，彼众生见已，起清凉想，便作是念：『我等往彼，快得清凉。』彼众生等走往趣向，欲求安处，求所归依，彼若集聚无量百千已，便入铁鍱林大地狱中。彼铁鍱林大地狱中，四方则有大热风来，热风来已，铁鍱便落，铁鍱落时，截手、截足、或截手足，截耳、截鼻、或截耳鼻及余支节，截身血涂无量百千岁，受极重苦，终不得死，要令彼恶不善业尽。复次，彼铁鍱林大地狱中生极大狗，牙齿极长，擥彼众生，从足剥皮，至头便食，从头剥皮，至足便食。彼众生等如是逼迫无量百千岁，受极重苦，终不得死，要令彼恶不善业尽。复次，彼铁鍱林大地狱中生大乌鸟，两头铁喙，住众生额，生挑眼吞，啄] cf.［丽】破头骨，取脑而食。彼众生等如是逼迫无量百千岁，受极重苦，终不得死，要令彼恶不善业尽。

After a long time, those beings emerge from the great hell of iron-clad forest and enter the great hell of iron sword tree forest. The great sword trees are one yojana tall, with thorns six feet long, causing those beings to climb up and down. When they climb up the trees, the thorns point downward, and when they climb down, the thorns point upward. The thorns of the sword trees pierce those beings, piercing their hands, feet, or both hands and feet, piercing their ears, nose, or both ears and nose, and piercing other limbs and joints. The blood from the pierced body is smeared for immeasurable hundreds of thousands of years, suffering extremely severe pain, but they cannot die. They must exhaust their evil and unwholesome karma.

「复于后时极大久远，彼众生等从铁鍱林大地狱出，铁鍱林大地狱次生铁剑树林大地狱，彼大剑树高一由延，刺长尺六，令彼众生使缘上下，彼上树时，刺便下向，若下树时，刺便上向。彼剑树刺贯刺众生，刺手、刺足、或刺手足，刺耳、刺鼻、或刺耳鼻及余支节，刺身血涂无量百千岁，受极重苦，终不得死，要令彼恶不善业尽。

After a long time, those beings emerge from the great hell of iron sword tree forest and enter the great hell of ash river. The two banks are extremely high, all around are thorns, filled with boiling ash water, and it is extremely dark. Those beings see it and think of it as cold water, thinking, 'There is cold water.' Having had this thought, they think, 'We should go there to bathe, drink to our fill, and enjoy the cool pleasure.' Those beings rush towards it, wanting to seek a pleasant place, seeking refuge. When they gather there in immeasurable hundreds of thousands, they fall into the ash river. Having fallen into the ash river, they flow along with the current, against the current, or both along and against the current. When those beings flow along with the current, against the current, or both along and against the current, their skin is cooked and falls off, their flesh is cooked and falls off, or their skin and flesh are cooked and fall off together, leaving only the bone chain. On the two banks of the ash river, there are hell wardens holding swords, large sticks, and iron hooks. Those beings want to cross to the other shore, but at that time the hell wardens push them back into the middle.

「复于后时极大久远，彼众生等从铁剑树林大地狱出，铁剑树林大地狱次生灰河，两岸极高，周遍生刺，沸灰汤满，其中极暗，彼众生见已，起冷水想，当有冷水。彼起想已，便作是念：『我等往彼，于中洗浴，恣意饱饮，快得凉乐。』彼众生等竞走趣向，入于其中，欲求乐处，求所归依，彼若集聚无量百千已，便堕灰河。堕灰河已，顺流、逆流、或顺逆流，彼众生等顺流、逆流、顺逆流时，皮熟堕落，肉熟堕落，或皮肉熟俱时堕落，唯骨锁在。灰河两岸有地狱卒，手捉刀剑、大棒、铁叉，彼众生等欲度上岸，彼时狱卒还推著中。

Furthermore, on the two banks of the ash river, there are hell wardens holding hooks and nets, who hook out the beings from the ash river and place them on a hot iron ground, blazing and burning, and they violently throw those beings onto the ground, rolling them on the ground, and ask, 'Where do you come from?' Those beings all answer, 'We do not know where we come from. We are only suffering from great hunger.' Those hell wardens then grab the beings and place them on a hot iron bed, blazing and burning, forcing them to sit on it, and with hot iron tongs they pry open their mouths and put a hot iron ball, blazing and burning, into their mouths. That hot iron ball burns their lips, having burned their lips it burns their tongues, having burned their tongues it burns their gums, having burned their gums it burns their throats, having burned their throats it burns their hearts, having burned their hearts it burns their large intestines, having burned their large intestines it burns their small intestines, having burned their small intestines it burns their stomachs, having burned their stomachs it passes out from their bodies. They are thus tormented for immeasurable hundreds of thousands of years, suffering extremely severe pain, but they cannot die. They must exhaust their evil and unwholesome karma.

「复次，灰河两岸有地狱卒，手捉钩羂，钩挽众生从灰河出，著热铁地，洞燃俱炽，举彼众生极扑著地，在地旋转，而问之曰：『汝从何来？』彼众生等佥共答曰：『我等不知所从来处，但我等今唯患大饥。』彼地狱卒便捉众生著热铁床，洞然俱炽，强令坐上，以热铁钳钳开其口，以热铁丸洞然俱炽，著其口中。彼热铁丸烧唇，烧唇已烧舌，烧舌已烧龂，烧龂已烧咽，烧咽已烧心，烧心已烧大肠，烧大肠已烧小肠，烧小肠已烧胃，烧胃已从身下过。彼如是逼迫无量百千岁，受极重苦，终不得死，要令彼恶不善业尽。

Furthermore, those hell wardens ask the beings, 'Where do you want to go?' The beings answer, 'We do not know where to go. We are only suffering from great thirst.' Those hell wardens then grab the beings and place them on a hot iron bed, blazing and burning, forcing them to sit on it, and with hot iron tongs they pry open their mouths and pour molten copper, boiling and burning, into their mouths. That molten copper burns their lips, having burned their lips it burns their tongues, having burned their tongues it burns their gums, having burned their gums it burns their throats, having burned their throats it burns their hearts, having burned their hearts it burns their large intestines, having burned their large intestines it burns their small intestines, having burned their small intestines it burns their stomachs, having burned their stomachs it passes out from their bodies. They are thus tormented for immeasurable hundreds of thousands of years, suffering extremely severe pain, but they cannot die. They must exhaust their evil and unwholesome karma.

「复次，彼地狱卒问众生曰：『汝欲何去？』众生答曰：『我等不知欲何所去？但患大渴。』彼地狱卒便捉众生著热铁床，洞然俱炽，强令坐上，以热铁钳钳开其口，以沸洋铜灌其口中，彼沸洋铜烧唇，烧唇已烧舌，烧舌已烧龂，烧龂已烧咽，烧咽已烧心，烧心已烧大肠，烧大肠已烧小肠，烧小肠已烧胃，烧胃已从身下过。彼如是逼迫无量百千岁，受极重苦，终不得死，要令彼恶不善业尽。

If those beings in hell have not exhausted all their evil and unwholesome karma, have not exhausted it all, and have not exhausted it without remainder, then those beings will again fall into the ash river, again ascend and descend the iron sword tree forest great hell, again enter the iron-clad forest great hell, again fall into the excrement great hell, again go to the peak and cliff great hell, and again enter the four-door great hell. If those beings in hell have exhausted all their evil and unwholesome karma, have exhausted it all, and have exhausted it without remainder, then they will later be reborn as animals, or fall into the realm of hungry ghosts, or be reborn in the heavens. If those beings were, as humans, unfilial to their parents, did not know how to respect śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, did not practice the truth, did not create meritorious karma, and did not fear the sins of the next world, then they will experience such unpleasant, unmindful, and disagreeable painful consequences, just as in that hell. If those beings were, as humans, filial to their parents, knew how to respect śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, practiced the truth, created meritorious karma, and feared the sins of the next world, then they will experience such pleasant, mindful, and agreeable happy consequences, just as in the heavenly palace in the sky.

「若彼众生地狱恶不善业不悉尽、不一切尽、尽无余者，彼众生等复堕灰河中，复上下铁剑树林大地狱，复入铁鍱林大地狱，复堕粪屎大地狱，复往来峰岩大地狱，复入四门大地狱中。若彼众生地狱恶不善业悉尽、一切尽、尽无余者，彼于其后或入畜生，或堕饿鬼，或生天中。若彼众生本为人时，不孝父母，不知尊敬沙门、梵志，不行如实，不作福业，不畏后世罪，彼受如是不爱、不念、不喜苦报，譬犹若彼地狱之中。若彼众生本为人时，孝顺父母，知尊敬沙门、梵志，行如实事，作福德业，畏后世罪，彼受如是可爱、可念、可喜乐报，犹虚空神宫殿之中。

Once, King Yama made this vow in his garden: 'When my life ends, may I be reborn among humans. If there are clans with great wealth and happiness, immeasurable wealth, livestock and industries beyond measure, fiefs, food, and estates of various kinds, what are they? They are the clans of great kṣatriya householders, great brāhmaṇa householders, and great householder householders. If there are other such clans with great wealth and happiness, immeasurable wealth, livestock and industries beyond measure, fiefs, food, and estates of various kinds, may I be born into such a family. Having been born, may I attain the faculties of awakening, and with the true Dharma and Vinaya taught by the Tathāgata, may I attain pure faith. Having attained pure faith, may I shave off my hair and beard, put on the saffron robes, leave home out of faith, and go forth to practice the path. The reason why sons of noble families shave off their hair and beard, put on the saffron robes, leave home out of faith, and go forth to practice the path is only to complete the unsurpassed holy life, to know and realize for oneself in this very life, to accomplish it by oneself, to dwell having personally realized it, to end birth, establish the holy life, do what needs to be done, and not be subject to rebirth, knowing it as it really is.'

「昔者阎王在园观中而作是愿：『我此命终，生于人中。若有族姓极大富乐，资财无量，畜牧产业不可称计，封户食邑种种具足。彼为云何？谓刹利大长者族、梵志大长者族、居士大长者族。若更有如是族极大富乐，资财无量，畜牧产业不可称计，封户食邑种种具足，生如是家。生已觉根成就，如来所说正法之律，愿得净信，得净信已，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，族姓子所为剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，唯无上梵行讫，于现法中自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。』

Once, King Yama made this vow in his garden. Then he spoke this verse:

「昔者阎王在园观中而作是愿，于是颂曰：

Those who are reproached by the messengers of the Lord of Death, and who are
thus negligent, will long be sorrowful and distressed, because they are covered by bad desires
Those who are reproached by the messengers of the Lord of Death, and who are
truly superior people, will never be negligent again, because they have been well taught the
wonderful holy Dharma. Seeing the terror of the messengers, they seek the end of birth
and old age, the extinction without remainder, and thus they have ended birth and old
age. They have attained peace and happiness, and in this very life they have attained
extinction. They have crossed over all terrors, and they have also crossed over the world's defilements.

「『为天使所诃，　　人故放逸者，长夜则忧戚，　　谓弊欲所覆。为天使所诃，　　真实有上人，终不复放逸，　　善说妙圣法。见受使恐怖，　　求愿生老尽，无受灭无余，　　便为生老讫。彼到安隐乐，　　现法得灭度，度一切恐怖，　　亦度世间洒。』」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

The seventh sūtra on the messengers of the Lord of Death is completed.
