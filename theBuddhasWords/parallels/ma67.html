MĀ 67

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 6 王相应品

67. Mahādeva’s Mango Grove （六七）大天［木＊奈］林经

Parallel to: MN 83 / EĀ 1.1 (~) / EĀ 50.4

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Vidéha, together with a great assembly of bhikṣus. He went to the city of Mīṣāśva and stayed in the great Tāmalinda Grove.

一时，佛游鞞陀提国，与大比丘众俱，往至弥萨罗，住大天［木＊奈］林中。

At that time, the World-Honored One, while walking on the road, smiled with joy. Venerable Ānanda, seeing the World-Honored One smile, joined his palms towards the Buddha and said, "World-Honored One, for what reason did you smile? Tathāgatas, Arhats, Perfectly Enlightened Ones do not smile without a reason. I wish to hear the meaning of it."

尔时，世尊行道中路欣然而笑。尊者阿难见世尊笑，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！何因缘笑？诸如来、无所著、等正觉若无因缘，终不妄笑，愿闻其意。」

At that time, the World-Honored One said, "Ānanda! In the past, in this Tāmalinda Grove of Mīṣāśva, there was a king named Mahādeva, a wheel-turning monarch, wise and intelligent, with a fourfold army, ruling the world, self-controlled, a righteous Dharma king, who had accomplished the seven treasures and attained the four kinds of human supernatural powers. Ānanda! What are the seven treasures accomplished by that great heavenly king? They are the wheel treasure, elephant treasure, horse treasure, jewel treasure, woman treasure, householder treasure, and general treasure. These are called the seven.

彼时，世尊告曰：「阿难！在昔异时此弥萨罗［木＊奈］林之中，于彼有王，名曰大天，为转轮王，聪明智慧，有四种军，整御天下，由己自在，如法法王成就七宝，得人四种如意之德。阿难！彼大天王成就七宝，为何谓耶？谓轮宝、象宝、马宝、珠宝、女宝、居士宝、主兵臣宝，是谓为七。

Ānanda! How is it that the great heavenly king is said to have accomplished the wheel treasure? Ānanda! When the great heavenly king, on the fifteenth day of the lunar month, the day of the full moon, the day of liberation, bathed and ascended to the main hall, a heavenly wheel treasure appeared from the east, with a thousand spokes, complete in all aspects, pure and natural, not made by humans, the color of fire, its light shining brightly. Seeing this, the great heavenly king was overjoyed and thought to himself, 'The wise wheel treasure has appeared, the wonderful wheel treasure has appeared. I have also heard from the ancients that if a kṣatriya king, on the fifteenth day of the lunar month, the day of the full moon, the day of liberation, bathes and ascends to the main hall, a heavenly wheel treasure appears from the east, with a thousand spokes, complete in all aspects, pure and natural, not made by humans, the color of fire, its light shining brightly, then he will surely become a wheel-turning king. Could it be that I will become a wheel-turning king?'

「阿难！彼大天王云何名为成就轮宝？阿难！时，大天王于月十五日说从解脱时，沐浴澡洗，升正殿上，有天轮宝从东方来，轮有千辐，一切具足，清净自然，非人所造，色如火［火＊佥］，光明昱烁。大天王见已，欢喜踊跃，心自念曰：『生贤轮宝，生妙轮宝，我亦曾从古人闻之，若顶生刹利王于月十五日说从解脱时，沐浴澡洗，升正殿上，有天轮宝从东方来，轮有千辐，一切具足，清净自然，非人所造，色如火［火＊佥］，光明昱烁，彼必当作转轮王也。我将无作转轮王耶？』

Ānanda! In the past, the great heavenly king wanted to test the heavenly wheel treasure. At that time, he assembled the four divisions of the army - the elephant division, horse division, chariot division, and infantry division. Having assembled the four divisions of the army, he went to the heavenly wheel treasure, stroked it with his left hand, turned it with his right hand, and said, 'Follow the heavenly wheel treasure, follow where the heavenly wheel treasure turns.' Ānanda! The heavenly wheel treasure turned and then went towards the east. At that time, the great heavenly king also followed behind with the four divisions of the army. Wherever the heavenly wheel treasure stopped, the great heavenly king also stayed there with the four divisions of the army. Then, the kings of the small countries in the east all came to where the great heavenly king was and said, 'Heavenly king! Welcome, heavenly king! These lands are extremely prosperous and peaceful, with many people. They all belong to the heavenly king. I only hope that the heavenly king will teach them with the Dharma. We will also assist the heavenly king.' Then, the great heavenly king told the small kings, 'You should each rule your own territories. You must all use the Dharma, not unrighteousness. Do not let there be evil deeds and non-virtuous people in your countries.'

「阿难！昔大天王将欲自试天轮宝。时，集四种军，象军、马军、车军、步军，集四种军已，诣天轮宝所，以左手抚轮，右手转之，而作是语：『随天轮宝，随天轮宝之所转去。』阿难！彼天轮宝转已即去，向于东方。时，大天王亦自随后及四种军，若天轮宝有所住处，时，大天王即彼止宿及四种军。于是，东方诸小国王，彼皆来诣大天王所，白曰：『天王！善来。天王！此诸国土极大丰乐，多有人民，尽属天王，唯愿天王以法教之，我等亦当辅佐天王。』于是，大天王告诸小王曰：『卿等各各自领境界，皆当以法，莫以非法，无令国中有诸恶业、非梵行人。』

Ānanda! When the heavenly wheel treasure passed through the east and crossed the eastern ocean, it turned back to the south, west, and north. Ānanda! When the heavenly wheel treasure turned around and went, the great heavenly king also followed behind with the four divisions of the army. Wherever the heavenly wheel treasure stopped, the great heavenly king also stayed there with the four divisions of the army. Then, the kings of the small countries in the north all came to where the great heavenly king was and said, 'Heavenly king! Welcome, heavenly king! These lands are extremely prosperous and peaceful, with many people. They all belong to the heavenly king. I only hope that the heavenly king will teach them with the Dharma. We will also assist the heavenly king.' Then, the great heavenly king told the small kings, 'You should each rule your own territories. You must all use the Dharma, not unrighteousness. Do not let there be evil deeds and non-virtuous people in your countries.' Ānanda! When the heavenly wheel treasure passed through the north and crossed the northern ocean, it immediately quickly returned to the king's original city. The great heavenly king sat on the main hall and judged financial matters. At that time, the heavenly wheel treasure stayed in the sky. This is called the great heavenly king accomplishing such a heavenly wheel treasure.

「阿难！彼天轮宝过东方去，度东大海，回至南方、西方、北方。阿难！随天轮宝周回转去时，大天王亦自随后及四种军，若天轮宝有所住处，时，大天王即彼止宿及四种军。于是，北方诸小国王，彼皆来诣大天王所白曰：『天王！善来。天王！此诸国土极大丰乐，多有人民，尽属天王，唯愿天王以法教之，我等亦当辅佐天王。』于是，大天王告诸小王曰：『卿等各各自领境界，皆当以法，莫以非法，无令国中有诸恶业、非梵行人。』阿难！彼天轮宝过北方去，度北大海，即时速还至本王城，彼大天王坐正殿上断理财物。时，天轮宝住于虚空，是谓大天王成就如是天轮之宝。

Ānanda! How is the great heavenly king said to have accomplished the elephant treasure? Ānanda! At that time, the great heavenly king gave birth to an elephant treasure. The elephant was extremely white with seven limbs. The elephant was named Uṣaṇa. The great heavenly king, having seen it, was overjoyed and leaped. If it was tamable, he made it extremely well-behaved. Ānanda! The great heavenly king then later told the elephant trainer, 'Quickly train the elephant to be extremely well-behaved. When the elephant is trained, come and tell me.' At that time, the elephant trainer, having received the king's order, went to where the elephant treasure was and quickly trained the elephant treasure to be extremely well-behaved. At that time, the elephant treasure, having received extreme training, was quickly well-trained. It was like an excellent elephant of the past, living for countless hundreds of thousands of years, and having been trained for countless hundreds of thousands of years, it was quickly well-trained. The elephant treasure was also like this, having received extreme training, it was quickly well-trained. Ānanda! At that time, the elephant trainer quickly trained the elephant treasure to be extremely well-behaved. When the elephant treasure was trained, he went to where the great heavenly king was and said, 'Heavenly king! You should know that I have trained it to the utmost. The elephant treasure is now trained and follows the heavenly king's intention.' Ānanda! In the past, when the great heavenly king tested the elephant treasure, at dawn when the sun rose, he went to where the elephant treasure was, rode the elephant treasure, roamed all the lands up to the great ocean, and then quickly returned to his original king's city. This is called the great heavenly king accomplishing such a white elephant treasure.

「阿难！彼大天王云何名为成就象宝？阿难！时，大天王而生象宝，彼象极白而有七支，其象名曰于娑贺。大天王见已，欢喜踊跃，若可调者，极令贤善。阿难！彼大天王则于后时告象师曰：『汝速御象，令极善调，若象调已，便来白我。』尔时，象师受王教已，至象宝所，速御象宝，令极善调。彼时象宝受极御治，疾得善调，犹昔良象寿无量百千岁，以无量百千岁受极御治，疾得善调，彼象宝者亦复如是，受极御治，疾得善调。阿难！尔时，象师速御象宝，令极善调，象宝调已，便诣大天王所，白曰：『天王！当知我以极御治之，象宝已调，随天王意。』阿难！昔大天王试象宝时，平旦日出，至象宝所，乘彼象宝，游一切地乃至大海，即时速还至本王城，是谓大天王成就如是白象之宝。

Ānanda! How is the great heavenly king said to have accomplished the horse treasure? Ānanda! At that time, the great heavenly king gave birth to a horse treasure. The horse treasure was extremely dark blue in color, with a head like a crow, and adorned with hair. It was named the King of Horses. The heavenly king, having seen it, was overjoyed and leaped. If it was tamable, he made it extremely well-behaved. Ānanda! The great heavenly king then later told the horse trainer, 'Quickly train the horse to be extremely well-behaved. When the horse is trained, come and tell me.' At that time, the horse trainer, having received the king's order, went to where the horse treasure was and quickly trained the horse treasure to be extremely well-behaved. At that time, the horse treasure, having received extreme training, was quickly well-trained. It was like an excellent horse of the past, living for countless hundreds of thousands of years, and having been trained for countless hundreds of thousands of years, it was quickly well-trained. The horse treasure was also like this, having received extreme training, it was quickly well-trained. Ānanda! At that time, the horse trainer quickly trained the horse treasure to be extremely well-behaved. When the horse treasure was trained, he went to where the great heavenly king was and said, 'Heavenly king! You should know that I have trained it to the utmost. The horse treasure is now trained and follows the heavenly king's intention.' Ānanda! In the past, when the great heavenly king tested the horse treasure, at dawn when the sun rose, he went to where the horse treasure was, rode the horse treasure, roamed all the lands up to the great ocean, and then quickly returned to his original king's city. This is called the great heavenly king accomplishing such a dark blue horse treasure.

「阿难！彼大天王云何名为成就马宝？阿难！时，大天王而生马宝，彼马宝者，极绀青色，头像如乌，以毛严身，名髦马王。天王见已，欢喜踊跃，若可调者，极令贤善。阿难！彼大天王则于后时告马师曰：『汝速御马，令极善调，若马调已，便来白我。』尔时，马师受王教已，至马宝所，速御马宝，令极善调。彼时马宝受极御治，疾得善调，犹昔良马寿无量百千岁，以无量百千岁受极御治，疾得善调，彼马宝者亦复如是，受极御治，疾得善调。阿难！尔时，马师速御马宝，令极善调，马宝调已，便诣大天王所，白曰：『天王！当知我以极御治之，马宝已调，随天王意。』阿难！昔大天王试马宝时，平旦日出，至马宝所，乘彼马宝，游一切地乃至大海，即时速还至本王城，是谓大天王成就如是绀马之宝。

Ānanda! How is the great heavenly king said to have accomplished the jewel treasure? Ānanda! At that time, the great heavenly king gave birth to a jewel treasure. The jewel treasure was bright and pure, naturally without a maker, with eight facets and no impurities, extremely well-polished, strung with a five-colored string of blue, yellow, red, white, and black. Ānanda! At that time, the great heavenly king, in the inner palace, wanted to have lamplight, so he used the jewel treasure. Ānanda! In the past, when the great heavenly king tested the jewel treasure, he gathered the four kinds of armies: elephant troops, cavalry, chariot troops, and infantry. Having gathered the four kinds of armies, he set up a tall banner in the dark night, placed the jewel on top, and went out to the garden. The light of the jewel illuminated the four kinds of armies, and the light reached half a yojana. This is called the great heavenly king accomplishing such a bright jewel treasure.

「阿难！彼大天王云何名为成就珠宝？阿难！时，大天王而生珠宝，彼珠宝者，明净自然，无有造者，八楞无垢，极好磨治，贯以五色绳，青、黄、赤、白、黑。阿难！时，大天王内宫殿中欲得灯明，即用珠宝。阿难！昔大天王试珠宝时，便集四种军，象军、马军、车军、步军，集四种军已，于夜暗中竖立高幢，安珠置上，出至园观，珠之光耀照四种军，明之所及方半由延，是谓大天王成就如是明珠之宝。

Ānanda! How is the great heavenly king said to have accomplished the female treasure? Ānanda! At that time, the great heavenly king gave birth to a female treasure. The female treasure had a lustrous body, clear and bright, with a beautiful color surpassing humans, slightly inferior to the gods. Her appearance was upright and beautiful, pleasing to behold. Her mouth emitted the fragrance of blue lotus flowers, and her pores emitted the fragrance of sandalwood. In winter, her body was warm, and in summer, her body was cool. She single-mindedly served the king, her words were delightful, her actions were swift, she was intelligent and wise, and she joyfully practiced goodness. She always kept the king in mind, never leaving her heart, let alone her body and speech. This is called the great heavenly king accomplishing such a beautiful female treasure.

「阿难！彼大天王云何名为成就女宝？阿难！时，大天王而生女宝，彼女宝者，身体光泽，曒洁明净，美色过人，少不及天，姿容端正，覩者欢悦，口出芬馥青莲华香，身诸毛孔出栴檀馨，冬则身温，夏则身凉。彼女至心承事于王，发言悦乐，所作捷疾，聪明智慧，欢喜行善。彼女念王，常不离心，况身、口行，是谓大天王成就如是美女之宝。

Ānanda! How is the great heavenly king said to have accomplished the householder treasure? Ānanda! At that time, the great heavenly king gave birth to a householder treasure. The householder treasure was extremely wealthy, with immeasurable wealth, many livestock, fiefs, and food-producing lands, and was fully endowed with the rewards of meritorious deeds, and had the heavenly eye; he could see all treasure troves, empty or full, and those guarded or unguarded, gold treasures, money treasures, made or unmade, he could see them all. Ānanda! The householder treasure went to the great heavenly king and said, 'Heavenly King! If you wish to obtain gold and money treasures, Heavenly King, do not worry, I know the right time.' Ānanda! In the past, when the great heavenly king tested the householder treasure, the king rode a boat into the Ganges River and said, 'Householder! I wish to obtain gold and money treasures.' The householder said, 'Heavenly King! May the boat reach the shore.' At that time, the great heavenly king said, 'Householder! I want to obtain them right here, right here.' The householder said, 'Heavenly King! May the boat stay.' Ānanda! Then the householder treasure knelt at the front of the boat, stretched out his hand, and raised four treasures from the water, gold treasures, money treasures, made treasures, and unmade treasures, and said, 'Heavenly King! Take whatever gold and money treasures you wish.' After using them, the remaining ones were returned to the water. This is called the great heavenly king accomplishing such a householder treasure.

「阿难！彼大天王云何成就居士之宝？阿难！时，大天王生居士宝，彼居士宝极大丰富，资财无量，多有畜牧、封户、食邑，种种具足福业之报，而得天眼；见诸宝藏，空、有悉见，见有守护、无守护者，金藏、钱藏，作以不作皆悉见之。阿难！彼居士宝诣大天王，白曰：『天王！若欲得金及钱宝者，天王莫忧，我自知时。』阿难！昔大天王试居士宝时，彼王乘船，入恒水中，告曰：『居士！我欲得金及以钱宝。』居士白曰：『天王！愿船至岸。』时，大天王告曰：『居士！正欲此中得，正欲此中得。』居士白曰：『天王！愿令船住。』阿难！时，居士宝至船前头，长跪申手，便于水中举四藏，出金藏、钱藏、作藏、不作藏，白曰：『天王！随意所欲，金及钱宝恣其所用。』用已余者，还著水中。是谓大天王成就如是居士之宝。

Ānanda! How is the great heavenly king said to have accomplished the minister treasure? Ānanda! At that time, the great heavenly king gave birth to a minister treasure. The minister treasure was intelligent, wise, eloquent, and discerning. The minister treasure set forth the meaning of the present world for the great heavenly king and encouraged him to establish it, set forth the meaning of the future world and encouraged him to establish it, and set forth the meaning of the present world and the future world and encouraged him to establish it. The minister treasure for the great heavenly king, when wishing to assemble the four divisions of the army, was able to assemble them, when wishing to dismiss them, was able to dismiss them, and when wishing to prevent the great heavenly king's four divisions of the army from becoming fatigued, and to encourage them, the same was true for the ministers. This is called the great heavenly king accomplishing such a minister treasure. Ānanda! This is called the great heavenly king accomplishing the seven treasures.

「阿难！彼大天王云何成就主兵臣宝？阿难！时，大天王生主兵宝，彼主兵臣聪明智慧，辩才巧言，多识分别。主兵臣宝为大天王设现世义，劝安立之，设后世义，劝安立之，设现世义、后世义，劝安立之。彼主兵臣为大天王，欲合军众，便能合之，欲解便解，欲令大天王四种军众不使疲乏，及劝助之，诸臣亦然，是谓大天王成就如是主兵臣宝。阿难！是谓大天王成就七宝。

Ānanda! How is the great heavenly king said to have attained the four kinds of supernatural powers? Ānanda! The great heavenly king had an extremely long lifespan of eighty-four thousand years, played as a youth for eighty-four thousand years, was a minor king for eighty-four thousand years, was a great king for eighty-four thousand years, shaved off his beard and hair, put on the saffron robes, left home out of faith, became a homeless monk, studied the path, studied the king of sages, and practiced the holy life. He stayed in this Vaiśālī, dwelling in the great Mahāvana forest. Ānanda! If the great heavenly king had an extremely long lifespan of eighty-four thousand years, played as a youth for eighty-four thousand years, was a minor king for eighty-four thousand years, was a great king for eighty-four thousand years, shaved off his beard and hair, put on the saffron robes, left home out of faith, became a homeless monk, studied the path, studied the king of sages, and practiced the holy life, staying in this Vaiśālī, dwelling in the great Mahāvana forest, this is called the great heavenly king's first supernatural power.

「阿难！彼大天王云何得人四种如意之德？彼大天王寿命极长八万四千岁，为童子嬉戏八万四千岁，作小国王八万四千岁，为大国王八万四千岁，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道、学仙人王修行梵行，在此弥萨罗，住大天［木＊奈］林中。阿难！若大天王寿命极长八万四千岁，为童子嬉戏八万四千岁，作小国王八万四千岁，为大国王八万四千岁，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道，学仙人王修行梵行，在此弥萨罗，住大天［木＊奈］林中者，是谓大天王第一如意之德。

Furthermore, Ānanda! The great heavenly king was free from illness, accomplished the path of equal food and taste, neither cold nor hot, peaceful and without conflict, and therefore his food and drink were easily digested. Ānanda! If the great heavenly king was free from illness, accomplished the path of equal food and taste, neither cold nor hot, peaceful and without conflict, and therefore his food and drink were easily digested, this is called the great heavenly king's second supernatural power.

「复次，阿难！彼大天王无有疾病，成就平等食味之道，不冷不热，安隐无诤，由是之故，其所饮食而得安消。阿难！若大天王无有疾病，成就平等食味之道，不冷不热，安隐无诤，由是之故，其所饮食而得安消者，是谓大天王第二如意之德。

Furthermore, Ānanda! The great heavenly king had a lustrous body, bright and pure, with a beautiful complexion surpassing that of humans, slightly inferior to that of the gods, upright and handsome, delightful to behold. Ānanda! 「阿难！我今出世，如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐。我今为自饶益，亦饶益他，饶益多人，愍伤世间，为天、为人求义及饶益，求安隐快乐。我今说法得至究竟，究竟白净，究竟梵行，究竟梵行讫，我今得离生老病死，啼哭忧戚，我今已得脱一切苦。

"Ānanda! I now turn the Dharma wheel of succession to you, and you should also turn the Dharma wheel of succession to others, so that the Buddha's lineage is not cut off. Ānanda! How do I now turn the Dharma wheel of succession to you, and you should also turn the Dharma wheel of succession to others, so that the Buddha's lineage is not cut off? It means the eightfold noble path, namely right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right concentration. Ānanda! This is what I now turn the Dharma wheel of succession to you, and you should also turn the Dharma wheel of succession to others, so that the Buddha's lineage is not cut off."

「阿难！我今为汝转相继法，汝亦当复转相继法，莫令佛种断。阿难！云何我今为汝转相继法，汝亦当复转相继法，莫令佛种断？谓八支圣道，正见乃至正定为八。阿难！是谓我今为汝转相继法，汝亦当复转相继法，莫令佛种断。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Venerable Ānanda and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them respectfully.

佛说如是。尊者阿难及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
