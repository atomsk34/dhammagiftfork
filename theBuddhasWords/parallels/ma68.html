MĀ 68

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 6 王相应品

68. Mahāsudassana （六八）大善见王经

Parallel to: DN 17 / DĀ 2 (~) / T 5 (~) / T 6 (~) / T 7 (~)

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the city of Kuśinagara, staying in the Śāla Grove of the Subhadra Park.

一时，佛游拘尸城，住惒跋单力士娑罗林中。

At that time, when the World-Honored One was about to enter parinirvāṇa for the last time, he said, "Ānanda! Go to the twin śāla trees and prepare a bed for the Tathāgata with his head to the north. The Tathāgata will enter parinirvāṇa in the middle of the night."

尔时，世尊最后欲取般涅槃时，告曰：「阿难！汝往至双娑罗树间，可为如来北首敷床，如来中夜当般涅槃。」

Venerable Ānanda received the Tathāgata's instruction and went to the twin śāla trees. Between the twin śāla trees, he prepared a bed for the Tathāgata with his head to the north. After preparing the bed, he returned to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said, "World-Honored One! I have prepared a bed for the Tathāgata between the twin śāla trees with his head to the north. May the World-Honored One know the time himself." Thereupon, the World-Honored One, together with Venerable Ānanda, went to the twin śāla trees. He spread his uttarāsanga robe four times on the bed, folded his saṃghāṭī robe as a pillow, lay down on his right side, with one foot on top of the other, about to enter parinirvāṇa for the last time.

尊者阿难受如来教，即诣双树，于双树间而为如来北首敷床。敷床已讫，还诣佛所，稽首礼足，却住一面，白曰：「世尊！已为如来于双树间北首敷床，唯愿世尊自当知时。」于是，世尊将尊者阿难至双树间，四叠郁多罗僧以敷床上，襞僧伽梨作枕，右脇而卧，足足相累，最后欲取般涅槃。

At that time, Venerable Ānanda was holding a whisk and attending to the Buddha. Venerable Ānanda joined his palms towards the Buddha and said, "World-Honored One! There are other great cities, namely Campā, Sāvatthī, Vaiśālī, Rājagṛha, Pāṭaligāma, and Kapilavastu. The World-Honored One did not enter parinirvāṇa in those places. Why is it that you are entering parinirvāṇa in this small city, which is the lowest among all the cities?"

时，尊者阿难执拂侍佛，尊者阿难叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！更有余大城，一名瞻波，二名舍卫，三名鞞舍离，四名王舍城，五名波罗［木＊奈］，六名加维罗卫。世尊不于彼般涅槃，何故正在此小土城，诸城之中此最为下？」

At that time, the World-Honored One said, "Ānanda! Do not say that this is a small city, the lowest among all cities. Why is that? In the past, this Kuśinagara was called Kuśinagara, extremely prosperous and joyful, with many people. Ānanda! Kuśinagara was twelve yojanas long and seven yojanas wide. Ānanda! The watchtowers were built as high as one person, or two, three, four, up to seven people. Ānanda! Kuśinagara was surrounded by seven moats outside, which were built with bricks of four treasures - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. The bottom was covered with sand of four kinds of treasures - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. Ānanda! Kuśinagara was surrounded by seven layers of walls outside, which were also built with bricks of four treasures - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. Ānanda! Kuśinagara was surrounded by seven layers of trees of four treasures - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. The golden trees had silver leaves, flowers, and fruits. The silver trees had golden leaves, flowers, and fruits. The lapis lazuli trees had crystal leaves, flowers, and fruits. The crystal trees had lapis lazuli leaves, flowers, and fruits.

是时，世尊告曰：「阿难！汝莫说此为小土城，诸城之中此最为下。所以者何？乃过去时，此拘尸城名拘尸王城，极大丰乐，多有人民。阿难！拘尸王城长十二由延，广七由延。阿难！造立楼橹，高如一人，或二、三、四，至高七人。阿难！拘尸王城于外周匝有壍七重，其壍则以四宝塼垒金、银、琉璃及水精，其底布以四种宝沙金、银、琉璃及水精。阿难！拘尸王城周匝外有垣墙七重，其墙亦以四宝塼垒金、银、琉璃及水精。阿难！拘尸王城周匝七重，行四宝多罗树金、银、琉璃及水精，金多罗树银叶华实，银多罗树金叶华实，琉璃多罗树水精叶华实，水精多罗树琉璃叶华实。

Ānanda! Between those trees were various flower ponds, with blue lotus ponds, red lotus, pink lotus, and white lotus ponds. Ānanda! The banks of those flower ponds were built with bricks of four treasures - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. The bottom was covered with sand of four kinds of treasures - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. Ānanda! Those ponds had staircases of four treasures - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. The golden stairs had silver steps, the silver stairs had golden steps, the lapis lazuli stairs had crystal steps, and the crystal stairs had lapis lazuli steps.

「阿难！彼多罗树间作种种华池，青莲华池，红莲、赤莲、白莲华池。阿难！其华池岸四宝塼垒金、银、琉璃及水精，其底布以四种宝沙金、银、琉璃及水精。阿难！彼池中有四宝梯陛金、银、琉璃及水精，金陛银蹬，银陛金蹬，琉璃陛水精蹬，水精陛琉璃蹬。阿难！彼池周匝有四宝钩栏金、银、琉璃及水精，金栏银钩，银栏金钩，琉璃栏水精钩，水精栏琉璃钩。阿难！彼池覆以罗网，铃悬其间，彼铃四宝金、银、琉璃及水精，金铃银舌，银铃金舌，琉璃铃水精舌，水精铃琉璃舌。

Ānanda! In those ponds were planted various water flowers, blue lotuses, red lotuses, pink lotuses, and white lotuses. They were always in bloom, without anyone tending to them, and open to all people. Ānanda! On the banks of those ponds were planted various land flowers, such as sumana flowers, vāsanti flowers, jasmine flowers, suketu flowers, madhuka flowers, ati-mudgara flowers, and pāṭala flowers.

「阿难！于彼池中殖种种水华，青莲华，红莲、赤莲、白莲华，常水常华，无守视者，通一切人。阿难！于彼池岸殖种种陆华，修摩那华、婆师华、瞻卜华、修揵提华、摩头揵提华、阿提牟哆华、波罗头华。

Ānanda! On the banks of those flower ponds were many women, with radiant and lustrous bodies, bright and pure, with beauty surpassing that of humans, but slightly less than that of the gods. Their appearance was upright and beautiful, pleasing to behold. They were adorned with various precious necklaces and ornaments. They practiced giving, providing whatever was needed - food and drink, clothing and vehicles, houses, beds, blankets, servants, and lamps.

「阿难！其华池岸有众多女，身体光泽，曒洁明净，美色过人，少不及天，姿容端正，覩者欢悦，众宝璎珞，严饰具足。彼行惠施，随其所须，饮食、衣被、车乘、屋舍、床褥、氍氀、给使、明灯，悉以与之。

Ānanda! When the leaves of the tāla trees were blown by the wind, there was an extremely wonderful and melodious sound, like the music of the five kinds of musicians, with the most wonderful and harmonious sounds. Ānanda! When the leaves of the tāla trees were blown by the wind, it was also like this. Ānanda! In the city of Kuśinagara, even the most inferior and evil people, if they wished for the music of the five kinds of instruments, would go together to the grove of tāla trees and all be able to indulge in their desires and enjoy themselves to the utmost. Ānanda! In the royal city of Kuśinagara there were always twelve kinds of sounds that were never interrupted - the sounds of elephants, horses, chariots, infantry, conch shells, drums, bherī drums, musical drums, singing, dancing, eating and drinking, and giving.

「阿难！其多罗树叶，风吹之时，有极上妙音乐之声，犹五种妓工师作乐，极妙上好谐和之音。阿难！其多罗树叶，风吹之时，亦复如是。阿难！拘尸城中设有弊恶极下之人，其有欲得五种妓乐者，即共往至多罗树间，皆得自恣，极意娱乐。阿难！拘尸王城常有十二种声，未曾断绝，象声、马声、车声、步声、吹螺声、鼓声、薄洛鼓声、伎鼓声、歌声、舞声、饮食声、惠施声。

Ānanda! In the city of Kuśinagara there was a king named Mahāsudarśana, a wheel-turning king, wise and intelligent, with a well-disciplined army ruling the world, self-reliant, a righteous Dharma king who had attained the seven treasures and the four kinds of human supernormal powers. How did he attain the seven treasures and the four kinds of human supernormal powers? As previously described, the seven treasures and the four kinds of human supernormal powers. Ānanda! Then the brahmins and householders of the royal city of Kuśinagara took many precious jewels and gold coins, loaded them onto carts, and went to King Mahāsudarśana, saying: 'Your Majesty! These many precious jewels and gold coins, please accept them out of compassion.' King Mahāsudarśana told the brahmins and householders: 'You have sent them as offerings, but I do not need them. I also have them myself.'

「阿难！拘尸城中有王，名大善见，为转轮王，聪明智慧，有四种军整御天下，由己自在，如法法王成就七宝，得人四种如意之德。云何成就七宝，得人四种如意之德？如前所说七宝、四种人如意之德。阿难！于是，拘尸王城梵志、居士，多取珠宝、钳婆罗宝，载诣大善见王，白曰：『天王！此多珠宝、钳婆罗宝，天王当为见慈愍故，愿埀纳受。』大善见王告梵志、居士曰：『卿等送献，我所不须，吾亦自有。』

Ānanda! Furthermore, eighty-four thousand minor kings went to King Mahāsudarśana and said: 'Your Majesty! We wish to build a palace for the Heavenly King.' King Mahāsudarśana told the minor kings: 'You wish to build a proper palace for me, but I do not need it. I already have a proper palace.' The eighty-four thousand minor kings joined their palms, bowed three times, and said: 'Your Majesty! We wish to build a palace for the Heavenly King. We wish to build a palace for the Heavenly King.' Then King Mahāsudarśana, for the sake of the eighty-four thousand minor kings, silently consented. At that time, the eighty-four thousand minor kings, knowing that King Mahāsudarśana had silently consented, bowed, took leave, circumambulated three times, and departed, each returning to their own country. They loaded eighty-four thousand carts with gold and silver, and with each precious jeweled pillar, they went to the city of Kuśinagara. Not far from the city, they built a great proper palace.

「阿难！复有八万四千诸小国王诣大善见王，白曰：『天王！我等欲为天王作殿。』大善见王告诸小王：『卿等欲为我作正殿，我所不须，自有正殿。』八万四千诸小国王皆叉手向，再三白曰：『天王！我等欲为天王作殿，我等欲为天王作殿。』于是，大善见王为八万四千诸小王故，默然而听。尔时，八万四千诸小国王知大善见王默然听已，拜谒辞退，绕三匝而去，各还本国，以八万四千车载金自重，并及其钱作以不作，复以一一珠宝之柱载往拘尸城，去城不远，作大正殿。

Ānanda! That great proper palace is one yojana long and one yojana wide. Ānanda! That great proper palace is built with four kinds of precious bricks - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. Ānanda! That great proper palace has four kinds of precious staircases - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. The gold staircase has silver steps, the silver staircase has gold steps, the lapis lazuli staircase has crystal steps, and the crystal staircase has lapis lazuli steps. Ānanda! The great proper palace has eighty-four thousand pillars made of four kinds of precious materials - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. The gold pillars have silver capitals and bases, the silver pillars have gold capitals and bases, the lapis lazuli pillars have crystal capitals and bases, and the crystal pillars have lapis lazuli capitals and bases. Ānanda! Inside the great proper palace, eighty-four thousand towers are built, made of four kinds of precious materials - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. The gold towers are covered with silver, the silver towers are covered with gold, the lapis lazuli towers are covered with crystal, and the crystal towers are covered with lapis lazuli.

「阿难！彼大正殿长一由延，广一由延。阿难！彼大正殿四宝塼垒金、银、琉璃及水精。阿难！彼大正殿四宝梯陛金、银、琉璃及水精，金陛银蹬，银陛金蹬，琉璃陛水精蹬，水精陛琉璃蹬。阿难！大正殿中有八万四千柱，以四宝作金、银、琉璃及水精，金柱银栌磉，银柱金栌磉，琉璃柱水精栌磉，水精柱琉璃栌磉。阿难！大正殿内立八万四千楼，以四宝作金、银、琉璃及水精，金楼银覆，银楼金覆，琉璃楼水精覆，水精楼琉璃覆。

Ānanda! Inside the great proper palace, eighty-four thousand thrones are set up, also made of four kinds of precious materials - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. The gold towers have silver thrones, covered with woolen blankets, spread with brocade and gauze, with a body-covering cloth, two pillows at both ends, and a canopy of fine silk. The silver towers have gold thrones, the lapis lazuli towers have crystal thrones, and the crystal towers have lapis lazuli thrones, covered with woolen blankets, spread with brocade and gauze, with a body-covering cloth, two pillows at both ends, and a canopy of fine silk. Ānanda! Around the great proper palace, there are four kinds of precious railings - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. The gold railings have silver hooks, the silver railings have gold hooks, the lapis lazuli railings have crystal hooks, and the crystal railings have lapis lazuli hooks. Ānanda! The great proper palace is covered with a net, with bells hanging in between. Those bells are made of four kinds of precious materials - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. The gold bells have silver tongues, the silver bells have gold tongues, the lapis lazuli bells have crystal tongues, and the crystal bells have lapis lazuli tongues.

「阿难！大正殿中设八万四千御座，亦四宝作金、银、琉璃及水精，金楼设银御座，敷以氍氀、毾［毯－炎＋登］，覆以锦绮罗縠，有衬体被，两头安枕，加陵伽波惒逻、波遮悉多罗那，如是银楼设金御座，琉璃楼设水精御座，水精楼设琉璃御座，敷以氍氀、毾［毯－炎＋登］，覆以锦绮罗縠，有衬体被，两头安枕，加陵伽波惒逻、波遮悉多罗那。阿难！彼大正殿周匝绕有四宝钩栏金、银、琉璃及水精，金栏银钩，银栏金钩，琉璃栏水精钩，水精栏琉璃钩。阿难！彼大正殿覆以罗网，铃悬其间，彼铃四宝金、银、琉璃及水精，金铃银舌，银铃金舌，琉璃铃水精舌，水精铃琉璃舌。

Ānanda! After the great proper palace was completed, the eighty-four thousand minor kings built a large flower pond not far from the palace. Ānanda! That large flower pond is one yojana long and one yojana wide. Ānanda! That large flower pond is built with four kinds of precious bricks - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. The bottom is covered with sand of four kinds of precious materials - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. Ānanda! That large flower pond has four kinds of precious staircases - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. The gold staircase has silver steps, the silver staircase has gold steps, the lapis lazuli staircase has crystal steps, and the crystal staircase has lapis lazuli steps.

「阿难！彼大正殿具足成已，八万四千诸小国王去殿不远，作大华池。阿难！彼大华池长一由延，广一由延。阿难！彼大华池四宝塼垒金、银、琉璃及水精，其底布以四种宝沙金、银、琉璃及水精。阿难！彼大华池有四宝梯陛金、银、琉璃及水精，金陛银蹬，银陛金蹬，琉璃陛水精蹬，水精陛琉璃蹬。

Ānanda! Around the large flower pond, there are four kinds of precious railings - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. The gold railings have silver hooks, the silver railings have gold hooks, the lapis lazuli railings have crystal hooks, and the crystal railings have lapis lazuli hooks. Ānanda! The large flower pond is covered with a net, with bells hanging in between. Those bells are made of four kinds of precious materials - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. The gold bells have silver tongues, the silver bells have gold tongues, the lapis lazuli bells have crystal tongues, and the crystal bells have lapis lazuli tongues.

「阿难！彼大华池周匝绕有四宝钩栏金、银、琉璃及水精，金栏银钩，银栏金钩，琉璃栏水精钩，水精栏琉璃钩。阿难！彼大华池覆以罗网，铃悬其间，彼铃四宝金、银、琉璃及水精，金铃银舌，银铃金舌，琉璃铃水精舌，水精铃琉璃舌。

Ānanda! In that large flower pond, there are various water flowers - blue lotuses, red lotuses, pink lotuses, and white lotuses. The water is always there, and the flowers are always blooming. There is a guard who does not allow everyone to enter. Ānanda! On the banks of that large flower pond, there are various land flowers - sumana flowers, vāsanti flowers, jasmine flowers, śuṇḍa flowers, mahāśuṇḍa flowers, ati-mudgara flowers, and pāṭalā flowers.

「阿难！彼大华池其中则有种种水华，青莲华，红莲、赤莲、白莲华，常水常华，有守视者，不通一切人。阿难！彼大华池其岸则有种种陆华，修摩那华、婆师华、瞻卜华、修揵提华、摩头揵提华、阿提牟哆华、波罗赖华。

Ānanda! When the great palace and the large flower pond were completed, the eighty-four thousand minor kings built a Tārā garden not far from the palace. Ānanda! The Tārā garden was one yojana long and one yojana wide. Ānanda! In the Tārā garden, eighty-four thousand Tārā trees were planted, made of four kinds of precious materials - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. The golden Tārā trees had silver leaves, flowers, and fruits. The silver Tārā trees had golden leaves, flowers, and fruits. The lapis lazuli Tārā trees had crystal leaves, flowers, and fruits. The crystal Tārā trees had lapis lazuli leaves, flowers, and fruits. Ānanda! Around the Tārā garden, there were four kinds of precious railings - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. The golden railings had silver hooks, the silver railings had golden hooks, the lapis lazuli railings had crystal hooks, and the crystal railings had lapis lazuli hooks. Ānanda! The Tārā garden was covered with a net, with bells hanging in between. Those bells were made of four kinds of precious materials - gold, silver, lapis lazuli, and crystal. The golden bells had silver tongues, the silver bells had golden tongues, the lapis lazuli bells had crystal tongues, and the crystal bells had lapis lazuli tongues.

「阿难！如是大殿及大华池具足成已，八万四千诸小国王去殿不远，作多罗园。阿难！彼多罗园长一由延，广一由延。阿难！多罗园中殖八万四千多罗树，则以四宝金、银、琉璃及水精，金多罗树银叶华实，银多罗树金叶华实，琉璃多罗树水精叶华实，水精多罗树琉璃叶华实。阿难！彼多罗园周匝有四宝钩栏金、银、琉璃及水精，金栏银钩，银栏金钩，琉璃栏水精钩，水精栏琉璃钩。阿难！彼多罗园覆以罗网，铃悬其间，彼铃四宝金、银、琉璃及水精，金铃银舌，银铃金舌，琉璃铃水精舌，水精铃琉璃舌。

Ānanda! When the great palace, flower pond, and Tārā garden were completed, the eighty-four thousand minor kings went together to King Mahāsudarśana and said, 'Your Majesty! Please know that the great palace, flower pond, and Tārā garden are all complete. We only wish that Your Majesty will do as you please.' Ānanda! At that time, King Mahāsudarśana had this thought: 'I should not ascend this great palace first. Now my teaching reaches the ultimate, is ultimately pure, is ultimately the practice of purity, and is ultimately the completion of the practice of purity. Now I am free from birth, old age, sickness, death, crying, and sorrow, and now I have attained freedom from all suffering.

「阿难！我今出世，如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐。我今为自饶益，亦饶益他，饶益多人，愍伤世间，为天、为人求义及饶益，求安隐快乐。我今说法得至究竟，究竟白净，究竟梵行，究竟梵行讫。我今得离生老病死、啼哭忧戚，我今已得脱一切苦。

Ānanda! From Kuśinagara, from the Śāla Grove of the Mallas, from the Nairañjanā River, from the Ajiravatī River, from the Devadaha Monastery, from the place where they prepared a bed for me, I have given up my body seven times in between. Six times I was a wheel-turning king, and now the seventh time I am a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One.

「阿难！从拘尸城，从惒跋单力士娑罗林，从尼连然河，从求求河，从天冠寺，从为我敷床处，我于其中间七反舍身，于中六反为转轮王，今第七如来、无所著、等正觉。

Ānanda! I no longer see in the world any deva, Māra, Brahmā, śramaṇa, or brāhmaṇa, from the devas down to humans, who will give up their body again. This is impossible. Ānanda! Now I have my last birth, last existence, last body, last form, and last self. I declare this to be the end of suffering.

「阿难！我不复见世中天及魔、梵、沙门、梵志，从天至人，更复舍身者，是处不然。阿难！我今最后生、最后有、最后身、最后形、得最后我，我说是苦边。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Venerable Ānanda and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。尊者阿难及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
