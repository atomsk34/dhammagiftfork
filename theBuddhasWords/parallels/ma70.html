Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 6 王相应品

70. The Wheel-turning Monarch （七〇）转轮王经

Parallel to: DN 26 / DĀ 6

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the country of Magadha, staying at the bank of the Nairañjanā River in the Uruvilvā forest.

一时，佛游摩兜丽刹利，在［木＊奈］林驶河岸。

At that time, the World-Honored One addressed the bhikṣus, saying: "Bhikṣus! You should rely on the natural Dharma lamp and take refuge in your own Dharma. Do not rely on other lamps and take refuge in other teachings. Bhikṣus! If you rely on the natural Dharma lamp and take refuge in your own Dharma, and do not rely on other lamps and take refuge in other teachings, then you will be able to seek and learn, and attain immeasurable benefits and blessings. Why is this so? Bhikṣus! In the past, there was a king named Dṛḍhabala, who was a wheel-turning sage king, intelligent and wise, with a well-disciplined army of four divisions ruling the world, freely and according to the Dharma. He accomplished the seven treasures and attained the four kinds of human supernatural powers. How did he accomplish the seven treasures and attain the four kinds of human supernatural powers? As explained before, he accomplished the seven treasures and attained the four kinds of human supernatural powers.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「诸比丘！当自然法灯，自归己法，莫然余灯，莫归余法。诸比丘！若自然法灯，自归己法，不然余灯，不归余法者，便能求学得利，获福无量。所以者何？比丘！昔时有王名曰坚念，为转轮王，聪明智慧，有四种军整御天下，由己自在，如法法王，成就七宝，得人四种如意之德。云何成就七宝，得人四种如意之德？如前所说成就七宝，得人四种如意之德。

Then, King Dṛḍhabala, at a later time, the heavenly wheel treasure suddenly moved from its original place. Someone saw it and went to King Dṛḍhabala, saying: 'Your Majesty! You should know that the heavenly wheel treasure has moved away from its original place.' Upon hearing this, King Dṛḍhabala said: 'Prince! My heavenly wheel treasure has moved away from its original place. Prince! I have heard from the ancients that if the heavenly wheel treasure of a wheel-turning sage king moves away from its original place, then that king will not live long and his life will not be long. Prince! I have already obtained human desires, and now I will seek heavenly desires. Prince! I wish to shave my beard and hair, wear the dyed robe, leave home out of true faith, become a homeless ascetic, and practice the path. Prince! I now entrust this four-continent world to you. You should rule according to the Dharma, not by unrighteousness. Do not let there be evil deeds and non-virtuous people in the country. Prince! If you later see the heavenly wheel treasure move away from its original place, you should also entrust this country to your prince and instruct him well. After entrusting the country to the prince, you should also shave your beard and hair, wear the dyed robe, leave home out of true faith, become a homeless ascetic, and practice the path.' King Dṛḍhabala entrusted the country to the prince and instructed him well, then shaved his beard and hair, wore the dyed robe, left home out of true faith, became a homeless ascetic, and practiced the path.

「于是，坚念王而于后时，天轮宝移，忽离本处。有人见之，诣坚念王白曰：『天王！当知天轮宝移离于本处。』坚念王闻已，告曰：『太子！我天轮宝移离于本处。太子！我自曾从古人闻之，若转轮王天轮宝移离本处者，彼王必不久住，命不久存。太子！我已得人间之欲，今当复求于天上欲。太子，我欲剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。太子！我今以此四天下付授于汝，汝当如法治化，莫以非法，无令国中有诸恶业、非梵行人。太子！汝后若见天轮宝移离本处者，汝亦当复以此国政授汝太子，善教勅之。授太子国已，汝亦当复剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道于是。』坚念王授太子国，善教勅已，便剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。

Then, seven days after King Dṛḍhabala left home to practice the path, that heavenly wheel treasure disappeared and was no longer visible. After losing the heavenly wheel treasure, King Kṣatriya-Uṣṇīṣa-Śiras was greatly distressed, sorrowful, and unhappy. King Kṣatriya-Uṣṇīṣa-Śiras then went to his father, King Dṛḍhabala, the sage. Upon arriving, he said: 'Your Majesty! You should know that seven days after Your Majesty left home to practice the path, that heavenly wheel treasure disappeared and was no longer visible.' His father, the sage King Dṛḍhabala, told his son, King Kṣatriya-Uṣṇīṣa-Śiras: 'Do not be distressed because of losing the heavenly wheel treasure. Why is this? You did not obtain this heavenly wheel from your father.' King Kṣatriya-Uṣṇīṣa-Śiras then said to his father: 'Your Majesty! What should I do now?'

「时，坚念王出家学道七日之后，彼天轮宝即没不现，失天轮已，刹利顶生王便大忧恼，愁戚不乐，刹利顶生王即诣父坚念王仙人所。到已，白曰：『天王！当知天王学道七日之后，彼天轮宝便没不现。』父坚念王仙人告子刹利顶生王曰：『汝莫以失天轮宝故而怀忧戚。所以者何？汝不从父得此天轮。』刹利顶生王复白父曰：『天王！我今当何所为？』

His father, the sage King Dṛḍhabala, told his son: 'You should learn the succession method. If you learn the succession method, then on the fifteenth day, when the observance of the fast is proclaimed, after bathing and ascending the main hall, that heavenly wheel treasure will definitely come from the east, with a thousand spokes, all complete, pure and natural, not made by humans, the color of fire, its light shining brightly.' King Kṣatriya-Uṣṇīṣa-Śiras then said to his father: 'Your Majesty! What is the succession method that you want me to learn? After I have learned it, on the fifteenth day, when the observance of the fast is proclaimed, after bathing and ascending the main hall, that heavenly wheel treasure will come from the east, with a thousand spokes, all complete, pure and natural, not made by humans, the color of fire, its light shining brightly.'

「父坚念王仙人告其子曰：『汝当应学相继之法，汝若学相继之法者，于十五日说从解脱时，沐浴澡洗，升正殿已，彼天轮宝必从东方来，轮有千辐，一切具足，清净自然，非人所造，色如火［火＊佥］，光明昱烁。』刹利顶生王复白父曰：『天王！云何相继之法欲令我学？令我学已，于十五日说从解脱时，沐浴澡洗，升正殿已，彼天轮宝从东方来，轮有千辐，一切具足，清净自然，非人所造，色如火［火＊佥］，光明昱烁。』

His father, the sage King Dṛḍhabala, again told his son: 'You should observe the Dharma according to the Dharma, practice the Dharma according to the Dharma. You should observe the fast with the crown prince, consorts, maids, ministers, people, śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, and even insects. On the eighth, fourteenth, and fifteenth days of the month, practice giving, giving to the poor, śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, the poor, the orphaned, and those who come from afar, with food and drink, clothing, vehicles, flower garlands, scattered flowers, ointments, houses, beds, blankets, pillows, servants, and lamps. If there are eminent and virtuous śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas in your country, you should go to them from time to time, ask about the Dharma and receive the Dharma: "Venerables! What is good Dharma? What is not good Dharma? What is sinful? What is meritorious? What is wonderful? What is not wonderful? What is black? What is white? From where do black and white dharmas arise? What is the meaning of this world? What is the meaning of the next world? How does one practice to receive good and not receive evil?" Having heard from them, practice as they say. If there are poor people in your country, give them wealth to support them. Your Majesty! This is called the succession method. You should learn it well. After you have learned it well, on the fifteenth day, when the observance of the fast is proclaimed, after bathing and ascending the main hall, that heavenly wheel treasure will definitely come from the east, with a thousand spokes, all complete, pure and natural, not made by humans, the color of fire, its light shining brightly.'

「父坚念王仙人复告子曰：『汝当观法如法，行法如法，当为太子、后妃、婇女，及诸臣民、沙门、梵志，乃至蜫虫奉持法斋，月八日、十四日、十五日修行布施，施诸穷乏沙门、梵志、贫穷、孤独、远来乞者，以饮食、衣被、车乘、华鬘、散华、涂香、屋舍、床褥、氍［毯－炎＋数］、綩𫄧、给使、明灯。若汝国中有上尊、名德、沙门、梵志者，汝当随时往诣彼所，问法受法：「诸尊！何者善法？何者不善法？何者为罪？何者为福？何者为妙？何者非妙？何者为黑？何者为白？黑白之法从何而生？何者现世义？何者后世义？云何作行受善不受恶？」从彼闻已，行如所说。若汝国中有贫穷者，当出财物，以给恤之。天王！是谓相继之法，汝当善学。汝善学已，于十五日说从解脱时，沐浴澡洗，升正殿已，彼天轮宝必从东方来，轮有千辐，一切具足，清净自然，非人所造，色如火焰，光明昱烁。』

King Kṣatriya-Uṣṇīṣa-Śiras then observed the Dharma according to the Dharma, practiced the Dharma according to the Dharma, and observed the fast with the crown prince, consorts, maids, ministers, people, śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, and even insects. On the eighth, fourteenth, and fifteenth days of the month, he practiced giving, giving to the poor, śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, the poor, the orphaned, and those who came from afar, with food and drink, clothing, vehicles, flower garlands, scattered flowers, ointments, houses, beds, blankets, pillows, servants, and lamps. If there were eminent and virtuous śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas in his country, he would go to them from time to time, ask about the Dharma and receive the Dharma: 'Venerables! What is good Dharma? What is not good Dharma? What is sinful? What is meritorious? What is wonderful? What is not wonderful? What is black? What is white? From where do black and white dharmas arise? What is the meaning of this world? What is the meaning of the next world? How does one practice to receive good and not receive evil?' Having heard from them, he practiced as they said. If there were poor people in his country, he would give them wealth to support them according to the time. King Kṣatriya-Uṣṇīṣa-Śiras then, on the fifteenth day, when the observance of the fast was proclaimed, after bathing and ascending the main hall, that heavenly wheel treasure came from the east, with a thousand spokes, all complete, pure and natural, not made by humans, the color of fire, its light shining brightly. He also became a wheel-turning king, also accomplished the seven treasures, and also attained the four kinds of human supernormal powers. What are the seven treasures and the four kinds of human supernormal powers? As explained before.

「刹利顶生王便于后时观法如法，行法如法，而为太子、后妃、婇女及诸臣民、沙门、梵志，乃至蜫虫奉持法斋，月八日、十四日、十五日修行布施，施诸穷乏沙门、梵志、贫穷、孤独、远来乞者，以饮食、衣被、车乘、华鬘、散华、涂香、屋舍、床褥、氍［毯－炎＋数］、綩𫄧，给使、明灯。若其国中有上尊、名德、沙门、梵志者，便自随时往诣彼所，问法受法：『诸尊！何者善法？何者不善法？何者为罪？何者为福？何者为妙？何者非妙？何者为黑？何者为白？黑白之法从何而生？何者现世义？何者后世义？云何作行受善不受恶？』从彼闻已，行如所说。若其国中有贫穷者，即出财物，随时给恤。刹利顶生王于后十五日说从解脱时，沐浴澡洗，升正殿已，彼天轮宝从东方来，轮有千辐，一切具足，清净自然，非人所造，色如火［火＊佥］，光明昱烁，彼亦得转轮王，亦成就七宝，亦得人四种如意之德。云何成就七宝、得人四种如意之德？亦如前说。

That wheel-turning king then, at a later time, the heavenly wheel treasure suddenly moved from its original place. Someone saw it and went to the wheel-turning king to say: 'Your Majesty! You should know that the heavenly wheel treasure has moved from its original place.' The wheel-turning king, having heard this, said: 'Crown Prince! My heavenly wheel treasure has moved from its original place. Crown Prince! I once heard from my father, the sage King Dṛḍhabala, that if the heavenly wheel treasure of a wheel-turning king moves from its original place, that king will certainly not live long and his life will not last long. Crown Prince! I have already obtained human desires, now I will seek heavenly desires again. Crown Prince! I wish to shave my beard and hair, wear the monastic robe, leave home with utmost faith, become homeless, and study the path. Crown Prince! I now entrust this four-continent world to you. You should rule according to the Dharma, not by unrighteousness. Do not let there be evil deeds and non-brahminical people in the country. Crown Prince! If you later see the heavenly wheel treasure move from its original place, you should also entrust this country to your crown prince and instruct him well. Having entrusted the country to the crown prince, you should also shave your beard and hair, wear the monastic robe, leave home with utmost faith, become homeless, and study the path.' The wheel-turning king entrusted the country to the crown prince and instructed him well, then shaved his beard and hair, wore the monastic robe, left home with utmost faith, became homeless, and studied the path.

「彼转轮王而于后时，天轮宝移，忽离本处。有人见之，诣转轮王白曰：『天王！当知天轮宝移离于本处。』转轮王闻已，告曰：『太子！我天轮宝移离本处。太子！我曾从父坚念王仙人闻之：若转轮王天轮宝移离本处者，彼王必不久住，命不久存。太子！我已得人间之欲，今当复求于天上欲。太子！我欲剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。太子！我今以此四天下付授于汝，汝当如法治化，莫以非法，无令国中有诸恶业、非梵行人。太子！汝后若见天轮宝移离本处者，汝亦当复以此国政授汝太子，善教勅之。授太子国已，汝亦当复剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道于是。』转轮王授太子国，善教勅已，便剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道。

Seven days after that wheel-turning king left home to study the path, that heavenly wheel treasure disappeared and was no longer visible. Having lost the heavenly wheel, the Kṣatriya Uṣṇīṣa-Śiras king was not distressed, but was only attached to desire, greedy for desire, insatiable, bound by desire, touched by desire, controlled by desire, not seeing the calamity, not knowing the way out. He then ruled the country according to his own intention. Because he ruled the country according to his own intention, the country declined and did not increase again, just like the previous wheel-turning kings who followed the Dharma of succession, and the countries and people became more prosperous and did not decline. The Kṣatriya Uṣṇīṣa-Śiras king was also like this. Because he ruled the country according to his own intention, the country declined and did not increase again.

「彼转轮王出家学道七日之后，彼天轮宝即没不现，失天轮已，刹利顶生王而不忧戚，但染欲著欲，贪欲无厌，为欲所缚，为欲所触，为欲所使，不见灾患，不知出要，便自出意治国，以自出意治国故，国遂衰减，不复增益，犹如昔时诸转轮王学相继法，国土人民转增炽盛，无有衰减。刹利顶生王亦复如是，自出意治国，以自出意治国故，国遂衰减，不复增益。

Then the royal priest, the brāhman, while inspecting the country, saw that the people were declining and not increasing again. He then thought: 'The Kṣatriya Uṣṇīṣa-Śiras king rules the country according to his own intention. Because he rules the country according to his own intention, the people are declining and not increasing again, just like the previous wheel-turning kings who followed the Dharma of succession, and the countries and people became more prosperous and did not decline. This Kṣatriya Uṣṇīṣa-Śiras king is also like this. Because he rules the country according to his own intention, the countries and people are declining and not increasing again.'

「于是，国师梵志案行国界，见国人民转就衰减，不复增益，便作是念：『刹利顶生王自出意治国，以自出意治国故，国土人民转就衰减，不复增益，犹如昔时诸转轮王学相继法，国土人民转增炽盛，无有衰减。此刹利顶生王亦复如是，自出意治国，以自出意治国故，国土人民转就衰减，不复增益。』

Then the royal priest, the brāhman, went together to the Kṣatriya Uṣṇīṣa-Śiras king and said: 'Your Majesty should know that Your Majesty rules the country according to your own intention. Because you rule the country according to your own intention, the people are declining and not increasing again, just like the previous wheel-turning kings who followed the Dharma of succession, and the countries and people became more prosperous and did not decline. Your Majesty is also like this. Because you rule the country according to your own intention, the countries and people are declining and not increasing again.' The Kṣatriya Uṣṇīṣa-Śiras king, having heard this, said: 'Brāhman, what should I do?' The royal priest, the brāhman, said: 'Your Majesty, there are people in the country who are intelligent and wise, who are skilled in calculation. There are ministers and attendants in the country who study and understand the scriptures, recite and uphold the Dharma of succession, just like all of us attendants. Your Majesty should study the Dharma of succession. Having studied the Dharma of succession, on the fifteenth day of the lunar month, when the fast is observed, after bathing and washing, ascending to the main hall, that heavenly wheel treasure will surely come from the east, with a thousand spokes, all complete, pure and natural, not made by humans, the color like fire, with brilliant light.'

「国师梵志即共往诣刹利顶生王，白曰：『天王！当知天王自出意治国，以自出意治国故，国土人民转就衰减，不复增益，犹如昔时诸转轮王学相继法，国土人民转增炽盛，无有衰减。今天王亦复如是，自出意治国，以自出意治国故，国土人民转就衰减，不复增益。』刹利顶生王闻已告曰：『梵志！我当云何？』国师梵志白曰：『天王！国中有人聪明智慧，明知算数，国中有大臣眷属学经明经，诵习受持相继之法，犹如我等一切眷属。天王！当学相继之法。学相继法已，于十五日说从解脱时，沐浴澡洗，升正殿已，彼天轮宝必从东方来，轮有千辐，一切具足，清净自然，非人所造，色如火［火＊佥］，光明昱烁。』

The Kṣatriya Uṣṇīṣa-Śiras king further asked: 'Brāhman, how is the Dharma of succession that you want me to study? Having studied it, on the fifteenth day of the lunar month, when the fast is observed, after bathing and washing, ascending to the main hall, that heavenly wheel treasure will surely come from the east, with a thousand spokes, all complete, pure and natural, not made by humans, the color like fire, with brilliant light.' The royal priest, the brāhman, said: 'Your Majesty, you should observe the Dharma as the Dharma, practice the Dharma as the Dharma. You should observe the Dharma fast for the crown prince, consorts, court ladies, ministers, people, śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, and even insects. On the eighth, fourteenth, and fifteenth days of the lunar month, practice giving, giving to the poor, śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, the poor, the orphaned, and those who come from afar, with food and drink, clothing, vehicles, flower garlands, scattered flowers, ointments, houses, beds, blankets, and bedding, servants, and lamps. If there are eminent and virtuous śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas in the king's country, you should go to them at the right time to ask about the Dharma and receive the Dharma: "Venerables, what is good Dharma? What is not good Dharma? What is sinful? What is meritorious? What is wonderful? What is not wonderful? What is black? What is white? From where do black and white dharmas arise? What is the meaning of this world? What is the meaning of the next world? How does one practice to receive good and not receive evil?" Having heard from them, practice as they say. If there are poor people in the king's country, you should give them wealth. Your Majesty, this is called the Dharma of succession, which you should learn well. Having learned it well, on the fifteenth day of the lunar month, when the fast is observed, after bathing and washing, ascending to the main hall, that heavenly wheel treasure will surely come from the east, with a thousand spokes, all complete, pure and natural, not made by humans, the color like fire, with brilliant light.'
