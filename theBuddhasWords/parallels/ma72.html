Chapter 7 长寿王品

72. （七二）长寿王本起经

Parallel to: MN 128 / Dhp 320–333 / Snp 1.3 / EĀ 24.8 (~) / T 210.31 / T 212.15 / T 213.14

long life, heaven, eight recollections pure, immovable
path, Upagupta said, Śākya, three clans, Brahmā
Heaven invited the Buddha superior heaven, Kātyāyana
recollection of the body, separation most honored
elder, sleep, thornless, true person said, last
in the list

长寿、天、八念　　净不移动道郁伽支罗说　　娑鸡三族姓梵天迎请佛　　胜天、伽𫄨那念身、支离弥　　上尊长老眠无刺及真人　　说处最在后

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in Kuśinagara, staying at the Ghoṣila Grove.

一时，佛游拘舍弥，在瞿师罗园。

At that time, the monks in Kuśinagara often quarreled. Then the World-Honored One said to the monks in Kuśinagara: "Monks! You should not quarrel. Why is that?

尔时，拘舍弥诸比丘数共鬪诤。于是，世尊告拘舍弥诸比丘曰：「比丘！汝等莫共鬪诤。所以者何？

If one uses disputes to stop disputes, in the end one will not see an
end. Only patience can stop disputes, this is the Dharma that is worthy of respect.

「若以诤止诤，　　至竟不见止；唯忍能止诤，　　是法可尊贵。

Why is that? In the past, there was a king of the country of Kuśāvatī named Longevity, and there was also a king of the country of Aśoka named Brahmadatta. Those two kings were always quarreling. Then King Brahmadatta of the country of Aśoka raised four kinds of armies: elephant troops, cavalry, chariot troops, and infantry. Having raised the four kinds of armies, King Brahmadatta of the country of Aśoka himself led the army to go and fight with King Longevity of the country of Kuśāvatī. King Longevity of the country of Kuśāvatī heard that King Brahmadatta of the country of Aśoka had raised the four kinds of armies: elephant troops, cavalry, chariot troops, and infantry, and that he had come to fight with him. Having heard this, King Longevity of the country of Kuśāvatī also raised the four kinds of armies: elephant troops, cavalry, chariot troops, and infantry. Having raised the four kinds of armies, King Longevity of the country of Kuśāvatī himself led the army out and went to the border, arrayed the troops to fight, and immediately defeated them. Then King Longevity of the country of Kuśāvatī completely captured the four kinds of armies of Brahmadatta of the country of Aśoka: elephant troops, cavalry, chariot troops, and infantry, and also captured King Brahmadatta of the country of Aśoka himself. Having captured him, he immediately released him and said to him: 'You are a destitute person. I now pardon you. Do not do it again in the future.'

「所以者何？昔过去时，有拘娑罗国王，名曰长寿，复有加赦国王，名梵摩达哆，彼二国王常共战诤。于是，加赦国王梵摩达哆兴四种军，象军、马军、车军、步军。兴四种军已，加赦国王梵摩达哆自引军往，欲与拘娑罗国王长寿共战。拘娑罗国王长寿闻加赦国王梵摩达哆兴四种军，象军、马军、车军、步军，兴四种军已，来与我战。拘娑罗国王长寿闻已，亦兴四种军，象军、马军、车军、步军。兴四种军已，拘娑罗国王长寿自引军出，往至界上，列阵共战，即摧破之。于是，拘娑罗国王长寿尽夺取彼梵摩达哆四种军众] cf.［丽】，象军、马军、车军、步军，乃复生擒加赦国王梵摩达哆身，得已，即放而语彼曰：『汝穷厄人，今原赦汝，后莫复作。』

"King Brahmadatta of the country of Aśoka raised the four kinds of armies again and again: elephant troops, cavalry, chariot troops, and infantry. Having raised the four kinds of armies, he again led the army to fight with King Longevity of the country of Kuśāvatī. King Longevity of the country of Kuśāvatī heard that King Brahmadatta of the country of Aśoka had raised the four kinds of armies: elephant troops, cavalry, chariot troops, and infantry, and that he had come to fight with him. Having heard this, King Longevity of the country of Kuśāvatī then thought: 'I have already defeated him. Why should I defeat him again? I have already subdued him. Why should I subdue him again? I have already killed him. Why should I kill him again? Just an empty bow is enough to subdue him.' Having thought this, King Longevity of the country of Kuśāvatī calmly did not raise the four kinds of armies: elephant troops, cavalry, chariot troops, and infantry, and did not go himself. Then King Brahmadatta of the country of Aśoka came and defeated him, completely capturing the four kinds of armies of King Longevity of the country of Kuśāvatī: elephant troops, cavalry, chariot troops, and infantry."

「加赦国王梵摩达哆复再三兴四种军，象军、马军、车军、步军。兴四种军已，复自引军往与拘娑罗国王长寿共战。拘娑罗国王长寿闻加赦国王梵摩达哆复兴四种军，象军、马军、车军、步军，兴四种军已，来与我战。拘娑罗国王长寿闻已，便作是念：『我已克彼，何须复克？我已伏彼，何足更伏？我已害彼，何须复害？但以空弓，足能伏彼。』拘娑罗国王长寿作是念已，晏然不复兴四种军，象军、马军、车军、步军，亦不自往。于是，加赦国王梵摩达哆得来破之，尽夺取拘娑罗国王长寿四种军众，象军、马军、车军、步军。」

Then King Longevity of the country of Kuśāvatī heard that King Brahmadatta of the country of Aśoka had come and completely captured the four kinds of armies of King Longevity of the country of Kuśāvatī: elephant troops, cavalry, chariot troops, and infantry, and he then thought: 'Fighting is very strange! Fighting is very evil! Why is that? One who has conquered should conquer again, one who has subdued should subdue again, one who has killed should kill again. I should now rather take my wife alone and ride in a single chariot to go to Benares.' Then King Longevity of the country of Kuśāvatī took his wife alone and rode in a single chariot to go to Benares. King Longevity of the country of Kuśāvatī then thought: 'I should now rather go to villages and towns to study and learn widely.' Having thought this, King Longevity of the country of Kuśāvatī then went to villages and towns to study and learn widely. Because of his wide learning, he was then renamed Longevity the Doctor.

「于是，拘娑罗国王长寿闻加赦国王梵摩达哆来，尽夺取我四种军众，象军、马军、车军、步军已，复作是念：『鬪为甚奇！鬪为甚恶！所以者何？克当复克，伏当复伏，害当复害。我今宁可独将一妻，共乘一车，走至波罗［木＊奈］。』于是，拘娑罗国王长寿即独将妻，共乘一车，走至波罗［木＊奈］。拘娑罗国王长寿复作是念：『我今宁可至村村邑邑，受学博闻。』拘娑罗国王长寿作是念已，即便往至村村邑邑，受学博闻，以博闻故，即转名为长寿博士。

Longevity the Doctor then thought: 'What I have studied, I have now attained. I should now rather go to the city of Benares and live in the streets and alleys, playing music with a joyful expression. When the noble and wealthy families of Benares hear it, they will be extremely joyful and entertained.' Having thought this, Longevity the Doctor then went to the city of Benares and lived in the streets and alleys, playing music with a joyful expression. When the noble and wealthy families of Benares heard it, they were extremely joyful and entertained. Then King Brahmadatta of the country of Aśoka heard it from his outer retinue, and his inner retinue and the brahmin chief minister also heard it. Having heard it, the brahmin chief minister called him to see him. Then Longevity the Doctor went to where the brahmin chief minister was, stood before him, and played music with a joyful expression. When the brahmin chief minister heard it, he was extremely joyful and entertained. Then the brahmin chief minister said to Longevity the Doctor: 'From today onwards, you can live with me and I will provide for you.' Longevity the Doctor said: 'Venerable sir! I have a wife. What should I do with her?' The brahmin chief minister replied: 'Doctor! You can bring her to live in my house and I will provide for her.' Then Longevity the Doctor brought his wife to live in the house of the brahmin chief minister, and the brahmin chief minister provided for them.

「长寿博士复作是念：『所为学者，我今已得，我宁可往波罗［木＊奈］都邑中，住街街巷巷，以欢悦颜色作妙音伎，如是波罗［木＊奈］诸贵豪族闻已，当极欢喜而自娱乐。』长寿博士作是念已，便往至波罗［木＊奈］都邑中，住街街巷巷，以欢悦颜色作妙音伎，如是波罗［木＊奈］诸贵豪族闻已，极大欢喜而自娱乐。于是，加赦国王梵摩达哆外眷属闻，中眷属、内眷属及梵志国师展转悉闻。梵志国师闻已，便呼见之。于是，长寿博士往诣梵志国师所，向彼而立，以欢悦颜色作妙音伎，梵志国师闻已，极大欢喜而自娱乐。于是，梵志国师告长寿博士：『汝从今日可依我住，当相供给。』长寿博士白曰：『尊者！我有一妻，当如之何？』梵志国师报曰：『博士！汝可将来依我家住，当供给之。』于是，长寿博士即将其妻依梵志国师家住，梵志国师即便供给彼。

Later, Longevity the Doctor's wife was sorrowful and thought: 'I want to see the four kinds of armies arrayed in battle formation, with drawn swords, slowly passing by, and I also want to drink the water used to sharpen the swords.' Having thought this, Longevity the Doctor's wife then said to Longevity the Doctor: 'I am sorrowful and have this thought: "I want to see the four kinds of armies arrayed in battle formation, with drawn swords, slowly passing by, and I also want to drink the water used to sharpen the swords."' Longevity the Doctor then said to his wife: 'Do not think like that. Why is that? We are now destroyed by King Brahmadatta. How can you see the four kinds of armies arrayed in battle formation, with drawn swords, slowly passing by, and drink the water used to sharpen the swords?' His wife said again: 'If you can do it, I have hope of living. If you cannot, I will surely die without a doubt.'

「于后时长寿博士妻心怀忧戚，作如是念：『欲令四种军阵列卤簿，拔白露刃，徐庠而过，我欲遍观，亦复欲得磨刀水饮。』长寿博士妻作是念已，便白长寿博士：『我心怀忧戚，作如是念：「欲令四种军阵列卤簿，拔白露刃，徐庠而过，我欲遍观，亦复欲得磨刀水饮。」』长寿博士即告妻曰：『卿莫作是念。所以者何？我等今为梵摩达哆王所破坏，卿当何由得见四种军阵列卤簿，拔白露刃，徐庠而过，我欲遍观，亦复欲得磨刀水饮耶？』妻复白曰：『尊若能得者，我有活望，若不得者，必死无疑。』

Longevity the Doctor then went to where the brahmin chief minister was, stood before him, with a sorrowful expression and in a weak voice, playing music. The brahmin chief minister heard it and was not joyful. Then the brahmin chief minister asked: 'Doctor! When you stood before me before, with a joyful expression and playing beautiful music, I was extremely joyful and entertained when I heard it. Why are you now standing before me, with a sorrowful expression and in a weak voice, playing music? I am not joyful when I hear it. Longevity the Doctor! Are you not sick in body and sorrowful in mind?' Longevity the Doctor said: 'Venerable sir! I am not sick in body, but only sorrowful in mind. Venerable sir! My wife is sorrowful and has this thought: "I want to see the four kinds of armies arrayed in battle formation, with drawn swords, slowly passing by, and I also want to drink the water used to sharpen the swords." I then said to my wife: "Do not think like that. Why is that? We are now like this. How can you see the four kinds of armies arrayed in battle formation, with drawn swords, slowly passing by, and drink the water used to sharpen the swords?" My wife then said to me: "If you can do it, I have hope of living. If you cannot, I will surely die without a doubt." Venerable sir! If my wife is not complete, I also have no reason.

「长寿博士即便往诣梵志国师所，向彼而立，颜色愁惨，以恶微声作诸音伎，梵志国师闻已不得欢喜。于是，梵志国师问曰：『博士！汝本向我立，以欢悦颜色作妙音伎，我闻已极大欢喜而自娱乐。汝今何以向我立，颜色愁惨，以恶微声作诸音伎？我闻已不得欢喜。长寿博士！汝身无疾患，意无忧戚耶？』长寿博士白曰：『尊者！我身无患，但意有忧戚耳。尊者！我妻心怀忧戚，作如是念：「我欲得四种军阵列卤簿，拔白露刃，徐庠而过，我欲遍观，亦复欲得磨刀水饮。」我即报妻曰：「卿莫作是念，所以者何？我今如此，卿当何由得四种军阵列卤簿，拔白露刃，徐庠而过，我欲遍观，亦复欲得磨刀水饮耶？」妻复白我曰：「尊若能得者，我有活望，若不得者，必死无疑。」尊者！若妻不全，我亦无理。』

The brahmin chief minister asked: 'Doctor! Can your wife be seen?' He said: 'Venerable sir! She can be seen.' Then the brahmin chief minister took Longevity the Doctor to his wife. At that time, Longevity the Doctor's wife was pregnant with a virtuous child. The brahmin chief minister, seeing that Longevity the Doctor's wife was pregnant with a virtuous child, then knelt on his right knee, joined his palms towards Longevity the Doctor's wife, and praised her twice, saying: 'Give birth to a king of the Kusu kingdom. Give birth to a king of the Kusu kingdom.' He instructed those around him: 'Do not let anyone know.' The brahmin chief minister said: 'Doctor! Do not be sorrowful. I can make your wife see the four kinds of armies arrayed in battle formation, with drawn swords, slowly passing by, and also make her drink the water used to sharpen the swords.'

「梵志国师问曰：『博士！汝妻可得见不？』白曰：『尊者！可得见耳。』于是，梵志国师将长寿博士往至妻所。是时，长寿博士妻怀有德子，梵志国师见长寿博士妻怀有德子故，便以右膝跪地，叉手向长寿博士妻，再三称说：『生拘娑罗国王。生拘娑罗国王。』教勅左右曰：『莫令人知。』梵志国师告曰：『博士！汝勿忧戚，我能令汝妻得见四种军阵列卤簿，拔白露刃，徐庠而过，亦能令得磨刀水饮。』

Then the brahmin chief minister went to King Brahmadatta of the Kasi country and said: 'Your Majesty! Know that the virtuous star has appeared. May Your Majesty please array the four kinds of armies, with drawn swords, slowly leading them out, displaying the majesty of the army, and sharpening the swords with water. May Your Majesty please go out to see for yourself. Your Majesty! If you do this, there will surely be auspicious signs.' King Brahmadatta of the Kasi country then ordered the commander-in-chief: 'You should know that the virtuous star has appeared. You should quickly array the four kinds of armies, with drawn swords, slowly leading them out, displaying the majesty of the army, and sharpening the swords with water. I will go out to see for myself. If I do this, there will surely be auspicious signs.' At that time, the commander-in-chief then received the king's order and arrayed the four kinds of armies, with drawn swords, slowly leading them out, displaying the majesty of the army, and sharpening the swords with water. Brahmadatta then went out to see for himself.

「于是，梵志国师往诣加赦国王梵摩达哆所，到已，白曰：『天王！当知有德星现，唯愿天王严四种军，阵列卤簿，拔白露刃，徐庠导引，出曜军威，以水磨刀，唯愿天王自出观视。天王！若作是者，必有吉应。』加赦国王梵摩达哆即勅主兵臣：『卿！今当知有德星现，卿宜速严四种之军，阵列卤簿，拔白露刃，徐庠导引，出曜军威，以水磨刀，我自出观，若作是者，必有吉应。』时，主兵臣即受王教，严四种军，阵列卤簿，拔白露刃，徐庠导引，出曜军威，以水磨刀，梵摩达哆即自出观。

Because of this, Longevity the Doctor's wife was able to see the four kinds of armies arrayed in battle formation, with drawn swords, slowly leading them out, displaying the majesty of the army, and also drink the water used to sharpen the swords. After drinking the water used to sharpen the swords, her worries and distress were immediately removed, and she gave birth to a virtuous child. She then gave him the name 'Long Life'. She entrusted him to someone to raise him secretly. As he gradually grew up, Long Life the child, if he were a chakravartin king born from the top of the head of a kshatriya, would rule the world and obtain a great country. He was skilled in various arts, such as riding elephants, riding horses, controlling and galloping, archery, hand-to-hand combat, throwing nets, throwing hooks, riding chariots, and riding palanquins. He was well-versed in all these various wonderful arts. He was outstanding in various wonderful tactile experiences, courageous and extraordinary, intelligent and outstanding, and was well-versed in all the profound and hidden.

「因是，长寿博士妻得见四种军阵列卤簿，拔白露刃，徐庠导引，出曜军威，并亦复得磨刀水饮。饮磨刀水已，忧戚即除，寻生德子，便为作字，名长生童子。寄人密养，渐已长大，长生童子若诸刹利顶生王者，整御天下得大国土，种种伎艺，乘象、骑马、调御、驰骤、射戏、手搏、掷羂、掷钩、乘车、坐辇，如是种种诸妙伎艺皆善知之，若干种妙触事殊胜，猛毅超世，聪明挺出，幽微隐远，无不博达。

Then Brahmadatta heard that King Longevity of the Kasi country had become a doctor here in the city of Varanasi. Brahmadatta then ordered his attendants: 'You should quickly go and arrest King Longevity of the Kasi country. Tie his hands behind his back, make him ride a donkey, beat a broken drum that sounds like a donkey braying. After proclaiming this order, take him out of the southern gate of the city and sit him under a high banner to question him.' The attendants received the order and immediately went to arrest King Longevity of the Kasi country. They tied his hands behind his back, made him ride a donkey, beat a broken drum that sounded like a donkey braying, proclaimed the order, took him out of the southern gate of the city, and sat him under a high banner to question him. At that time, Long Life the child followed his father and said to him: 'Your Majesty, do not be afraid! Your Majesty, do not be afraid! I will definitely be able to save you right here!' King Longevity of the Kasi country said: 'Child, please bear with it! Child, please bear with it! Do not give rise to resentment.' When the people heard King Longevity say this, they asked the king: 'What do you mean?' The king replied to the people: 'This child is intelligent. He will definitely understand my words.'

「于是，梵摩达哆闻拘娑罗国王长寿彼作博士，转名在此波罗［木＊奈］城中，梵摩达哆即勅左右：『卿等速往收拘娑罗国王长寿，反缚两手，令彼骑驴，打破败鼓，声如驴鸣。遍宣令已，从城南门出，坐高标下，诘问其辞。』左右受教，即便往收拘娑罗国王长寿，反缚两手，令彼骑驴，打破败鼓，声如驴鸣，遍宣令已，从城南门出，坐高标下，诘问其辞。是时，长生童子寻随父后，或在左右而白父曰：『天王勿怖！天王勿怖！我即于此，必能拔济，必能拔济。』拘娑罗王长寿告曰：『童子可忍！童子可忍！莫起怨结] cf.［丽】，但当行慈。』众人闻长寿王而作此语，便问于王：『所道何等？』王答众人曰：『此童子聪明，必解我语。』

At that time, Long Life the child exhorted the nobles and wealthy clans in the city of Varanasi: 'Gentlemen! Practice giving and cultivate merit, and make prayers for King Longevity of the Kasi country. May this merit of giving be for the sake of King Longevity of the Kasi country, so that he may be at ease and attain liberation.' Then the nobles and wealthy clans in the city of Varanasi, exhorted by Long Life the child, practiced giving and cultivating merit, and made prayers for King Longevity of the Kasi country: 'May this merit of giving be for the sake of King Longevity of the Kasi country, so that he may be at ease and attain liberation.' King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi heard that the nobles and wealthy clans in the city of Varanasi were practicing giving and cultivating merit, and making prayers for King Longevity of the Kasi country: 'May this merit of giving be for the sake of King Longevity of the Kasi country, so that he may be at ease and attain liberation.' Hearing this, he was greatly frightened and his body hair stood on end: 'Do not let these nobles and wealthy clans in the city of Varanasi turn against me! Forget about that matter for now. I must quickly put an end to this matter first.' Then King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi ordered his attendants: 'You should go quickly and kill King Longevity of the Kasi country, and cut him into seven pieces.' The attendants received the order and immediately went to kill King Longevity and cut him into seven pieces.

「尔时，长生童子劝波罗［木＊奈］城中诸贵豪族：『诸君！行施修福，为拘娑罗国王长寿呪愿，以此施福，愿拘娑罗国王长寿令安隐，得解脱。』于是，波罗［木＊奈］城中诸贵豪族为长生童子所劝，行施修福，为拘娑罗国王长寿呪愿：『以此施福，愿拘娑罗国王长寿令安隐，得解脱。』加赦国王梵摩达哆闻此波罗［木＊奈］诸贵豪族行施修福，为拘娑罗国王长寿呪愿：『以此施福愿拘娑罗国王长寿令安隐，得解脱。』闻即大怖，身毛皆竖：『莫令此波罗［木＊奈］城中诸贵豪族反于我耶？且置彼事，我今急当先灭此事。』于是，加赦国王梵摩达哆教勅左右：『汝等速去，杀拘娑罗国王长寿，斩作七段。』左右受教，即便速往，杀长寿王，斩作七段。

"Then Long Life the child exhorted the nobles and wealthy clans in the city of Varanasi, saying: 'Gentlemen, look at this! King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi is cruel and unjust. He took my father, King Longevity of the Kasi country, an innocent man, and seized his country, treasury, and wealth. He unjustly killed him and cut him into seven pieces. Gentlemen, you should go and collect my father's body with new silk cloth. Take the seven pieces of the body, pile them with all kinds of fragrant wood, cremate them, and build a temple for him. Write a letter for me to Brahmadatta, saying: "The child of King Longevity of the Kasi country says this: Do you not fear that your descendants will become a calamity?" Then the nobles and wealthy clans in the city of Varanasi, exhorted by Long Life the child, immediately went to collect the body with new silk cloth. They took the seven pieces of the body, piled them with all kinds of fragrant wood, cremated them, built a temple, and also wrote a letter to Brahmadatta, saying: "The child of King Longevity of the Kasi country says this: Do you not fear that your descendants will become a calamity?

「于是，长生童子劝波罗［木＊奈］城中诸贵豪族而作是语：『诸君看此，加赦国王梵摩达哆酷暴无道，彼取我父拘娑罗国王长寿无过之人，夺取其国仓库财物，怨酷枉杀，斩作七段。诸君可往，以新缯叠收敛我父，取七段尸，以一切香、香木积聚而阇维之，立于庙堂，为我作书与梵摩达哆言：「拘娑罗国王长生童子！彼作是语，汝不畏后为子孙作患耶？」』于是，波罗［木＊奈］诸贵豪族为长生童子所劝，以新缯叠即往敛，取彼七段尸，以一切香、香木积聚而阇维之，为立庙堂，亦为作书与梵摩达哆言：『拘娑罗国王长生童子！彼作是语，汝不畏后为子孙作患耶？』

Then the wife of King Longevity told Long Life the child: 'You should know that King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi is cruel and unjust. He took your father, King Longevity of the Kasi country, an innocent man, and seized his country, treasury, and wealth. He unjustly killed him and cut him into seven pieces. Child, come with me! Let us ride in the same carriage and flee from the city of Varanasi. If we do not leave, calamity will befall you.' Then the wife of King Longevity rode in the same carriage with Long Life the child and fled from the city of Varanasi. At that time, Long Life the child thought: 'I should go to villages and towns to study and broaden my knowledge.' Having thought this, Long Life the child went to villages and towns to study and broaden his knowledge. Because of his extensive knowledge, he was then renamed Long Life the Doctor.

「于是，长寿王妻告长生童子曰：『汝当知此加赦国王梵摩达哆酷暴无道，彼取汝父拘娑罗国王长寿无过之人，夺取其国仓库财物，怨酷枉杀，斩作七段。童子汝来！共乘一车，走出波罗［木＊奈］，若不去者，祸将及汝。』于是，长寿王妻与长生童子共乘一车，走出波罗［木＊奈］。尔时，长生童子作如是念：『我宁可往至村村邑邑，受学博闻。』长生童子作是念已，便往至村村邑邑，受学博闻，以博闻故，即转名为长生博士。

Long Life the Doctor then thought: 'What I have studied, I have now attained. I should go to the city of Varanasi and stay in the streets and alleys, playing music with a joyful expression. When the nobles and wealthy clans of Varanasi hear it, they will be greatly delighted and entertained.' Having thought this, Long Life the Doctor went to the city of Varanasi and stayed in the streets and alleys, playing music with a joyful expression. When the nobles and wealthy clans of Varanasi heard it, they were greatly delighted and entertained. Then the outer relatives of King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi heard, as did the inner relatives, the brahmin chief minister, and so on, up to King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi himself. He then summoned Long Life the Doctor to see him.

「长生博士复作是念：『所为学者，我今已得，我宁可往波罗［木＊奈］都邑中，住街街巷巷，以欢悦颜色作妙音伎，如是波罗［木＊奈］诸贵豪族闻已，当大欢喜而自娱乐。』长生博士作是念已，便往至波罗［木＊奈］都邑中，住街街巷巷，欢悦颜色作妙音伎，如是波罗［木＊奈］诸贵豪族闻已，极大欢喜而自娱乐。于是，加赦国王梵摩达哆外眷属闻，中眷属、内眷属、梵志国师，展转乃至加赦国王梵摩达哆闻，便呼见。

Then Long Life the Doctor went to King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi and stood before him, playing music with a joyful expression. When King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi heard it, he was greatly delighted and entertained. Then King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi said: 'Doctor! From today onwards, you may rely on me to live. I will provide for you.' Then Long Life the Doctor relied on him to live, and King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi provided for him. Later, he was trusted and entrusted with everything, and was even given the sword that guarded his body.

「于是，长生博士即往诣加赦国王梵摩达哆所，向彼而立，以欢悦颜色作妙音伎，如是加赦国王梵摩达哆闻已，极大欢喜而自娱乐。于是，加赦国王梵摩达哆告曰：『博士！汝从今日可依我住，当相供给。』于是，长生博士即依彼住，加赦国王梵摩达哆即供给之，后遂信任，一以委付，即持卫身刀剑授与长生博士。

Then King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi ordered his charioteer: 'Prepare the chariot. I wish to go hunting.' The charioteer received the order and immediately prepared the chariot. Returning, he said: 'The chariot is ready. Please do as the king wishes.' Then King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi rode in the chariot with Long Life the Doctor. Long Life the Doctor then thought: 'This King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi is cruel and unjust. He took my father, the righteous King Jīvaka of the country of Kauśala, and seized his country, treasury, and wealth. He unjustly killed him and cut him into seven pieces. I should now drive the chariot so that the four divisions of the army are each in a different place.' Having thought this, Long Life the Doctor drove the chariot so that the four divisions of the army were each in a different place.

「尔时，加赦国王梵摩达哆便勅御者：『汝可严驾，我欲出猎。』御者受教，即便严驾讫，还白曰：『严驾已办，随天王意。』于是，加赦国王梵摩达哆便与长生博士共乘车出，长生博士即作是念：『此加赦国王梵摩达哆酷暴无道，彼取我父拘娑罗国王长寿无过之人，夺取其国仓库财物，怨酷枉杀，斩作七段。我今宁可御车，使离四种军众各在异处。』长生博士作是念已，即便御车离四种军，各在异处。

At that time, King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi was exhausted from the journey, oppressed by wind and heat, and thirsty and tired. He wanted to lie down and immediately got off the chariot, resting his head on Long Life the Doctor's lap and falling asleep. Then Long Life the Doctor again thought: 'This King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi is cruel and unjust. He took my father, a righteous man, and seized his country, treasury, and wealth. He unjustly killed him and cut him into seven pieces. But now he is in my hands. I should repay the enmity.' Having thought this, Long Life the Doctor immediately drew his sharp sword and placed it on King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi's neck, saying: 'I will kill you! I will kill you!' Long Life the Doctor then thought: 'Am I not wrong? Why? I remember my father's words when he was on the execution ground, as he was about to die: "Child, be patient! Child, be patient! Do not give rise to enmity, but only practice kindness."'

「彼时，加赦国王梵摩达哆冒涉涂路，风热所逼，烦闷渴乏，疲极欲卧，即便下车，枕长生博士膝眠。于是，长生博士复作是念：『此加赦国王梵摩达哆酷暴无道，彼取我父无过之人，夺取其国仓库财物，怨酷枉杀，斩作七段。然于今日已在我手，但当报怨。』长生博士作是念已，即拔利刀，著加赦国王梵摩达哆颈上而作是语：『我今杀汝！我今杀汝！』长生博士复作是念：『我为不是。所以者何？忆父昔日在标下时，临终语我：「童子可忍！童子可忍！莫起怨结，但当行慈。」』忆已，举刀还内鞘中。

"At that time, King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi had a dream in which he saw Long Life the Doctor, the son of King Jīvaka of the country of Kauśala, drawing a sharp sword and placing it on his neck, saying: 'I will kill you! I will kill you!' Having seen this, he was terrified and his body hair stood on end. He then quickly woke up in shock and said to Long Life the Doctor: 'You should know that in my dream I saw Long Life the Doctor, the son of King Jīvaka of the country of Kauśala, drawing a sharp sword and placing it on my neck, saying: "I will kill you! I will kill you!"'" Long Life the Doctor, having heard this, said: 'Your Majesty, do not be afraid! Your Majesty, do not be afraid! Why? That Long Life the Doctor, the son of King Jīvaka of the country of Kauśala, is none other than myself. Your Majesty, I thought: "King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi is cruel and unjust. He took my father, a righteous man, and seized his country, treasury, and wealth. He unjustly killed him and cut him into seven pieces. But now he is in my hands. I should repay the enmity." Your Majesty, I drew a sharp sword and placed it on the king's neck, saying: "I will kill you! I will kill you!" Your Majesty, I then thought: "Am I not wrong? Why? I remember my father's words when he was on the execution ground, as he was about to die: 'Child, be patient! Child, be patient! Do not give rise to enmity, but only practice kindness.'" Having remembered this, I raised the sword and returned it to its sheath.

King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi said: 'Child, you said: "Child, be patient! Child, be patient!" I already understand this meaning. You also said: "Do not give rise to enmity, but only practice kindness." What does this mean?' Long Life the Doctor replied: 'Your Majesty, not giving rise to enmity and practicing kindness means just this.' King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi, having heard this, said: 'Child, from today onwards, I will give you my entire kingdom. I will return your father's original country to you. Why? What you have done is very difficult, you have granted me life.' Long Life the Doctor, having heard this, said: 'Your Majesty, your original country belongs to Your Majesty. My father's original country can be returned.

「加赦国王梵摩达哆语曰：『童子！汝作是说：「童子可忍！童子可忍！」我已知此义。童子又言：「莫起怨结，但当行慈」者，此谓何义？』长生童子答曰：『天王！莫起怨结，但当行慈者，即谓此也。』加赦国王梵摩达哆闻已，语曰：『童子！从今日始，我所领国尽以相与，汝父本国还持付卿。所以者何？汝所作甚难，乃惠我命。』长生童子闻已，白曰：『天王本国自属天王，我父本国可以见还。』

Then King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi and Long Life the Doctor returned together, entered the city of Vārāṇasī, and sat on the main hall. He told the ministers: 'If you see Long Life the Doctor, the son of King Jīvaka of the country of Kauśala, what will you do?' The ministers, having heard this, some said: 'Your Majesty, if we see him, we should cut off his hands.' Some said: 'Your Majesty, if we see him, we should cut off his feet.' Some said: 'We should take his life.' King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi told the ministers: 'If you want to see Long Life the Doctor, the son of King Jīvaka of the country of Kauśala, he is right here. You should not have evil intentions towards this child. Why? This child has done something very difficult, he has granted me life.' Then King Brahmadatta of the city of Varanasi had Long Life the Doctor bathed with the king's bath, anointed with the king's fragrance, dressed in the king's clothes, seated on a golden throne, and married to a princess, returning him to his original country.

「于是，加赦国王梵摩达哆与长生童子共载还归，入波罗［木＊奈］城，坐正殿上，告诸臣曰：『卿等若见拘娑罗国王长寿儿长生童子者，当云何耶？』诸臣闻已，或有白曰：『天王！若见彼者，当截其手。』或复作是语：『天王！若见彼者，当截其足。』或复作是语：『当断其命。』加赦国王梵摩达哆告诸臣曰：『卿等欲见拘娑罗国王长寿儿长生童子者，即此是也。汝等莫起恶意向此童子。所以者何？此童子所作甚难，惠与我命。』于是，加赦国王梵摩达哆以王沐浴浴长生童子，涂以王香，衣以王服，令坐金御床，以女妻之，还其本国。」

"Monks, those kings, the kings of the Śākya clan, were the great rulers of the world, governing the world with patience, and they praised patience; they practiced loving-kindness, and they praised loving-kindness; they practiced generosity, and they praised generosity. Monks, you should also be like this, having utmost faith, leaving home, becoming homeless, and learning the path, you should practice patience, and praise patience; practice loving-kindness, and praise loving-kindness; practice generosity, and praise generosity."

「比丘！彼诸国王刹利顶生王，为大国主，整御天下，自行忍辱，复称叹忍；自行慈心，复称叹慈；自行恩惠，复称恩惠。诸比丘！汝亦应如是，至信、舍家、无家、学道，当行忍辱，复称叹忍；自行慈心，复称叹慈；自行恩惠，复称恩惠。」

Then the monks, having heard the Buddha's words, said: "The World-Honored One, the Dharma Master, should stay here for now. He teaches us, how can we not teach him?"

于是，诸比丘闻佛所说，有作是言：「世尊法主今且住也，彼导说我，我那得不导说彼。」

Then the World-Honored One was displeased with the conduct, deportment, learning, and practice of the monks from Kośala, and he rose from his seat and spoke a verse:

于是，世尊不悦可拘舍弥诸比丘所行威仪、礼节、所学、所习，即从坐起，而说颂曰：

With various words, they destroy the most honored assembly. When destroying
the holy assembly, no one can rebuke and stop it. Crushing
the body to the point of death, depriving elephants, cows, horses
and wealth, destroying the country and bringing it to ruin, they
can still reconcile. How much more so with your small words
of scolding, which cannot control and unite. If you do not
contemplate the true meaning, how can enmity be resolved? Scolding, blaming
and accusing can control and unite. If you contemplate the true
meaning, enmity will surely be resolved. If one uses disputes to
stop disputes, one will never see an end. Only patience can
stop disputes, which is the most honorable Dharma. To be angry
towards a wise sage, speaking worthless words, slandering the sage Muni
is base and not wise. Others do not understand the meaning
only I alone can know. If there is someone who can
understand the meaning, their anger will be resolved. If one obtains
a companion in concentration, a wise one practices goodness together, abandoning
one's original intention, joyfully always following each other. If one does
not obtain a companion in concentration, a wise one practices goodness
alone, like a king governing the country, like an elephant alone
in the wild. Walking alone, do not do evil, like an
elephant alone in the wild. Walking alone is better than doing
good, do not associate with evil. If one does not find
a good friend, one who is equal to oneself, one should
firmly dwell alone, do not associate with evil.

「以若干言语，　　破坏最尊众，破坏圣众时，　　无有能诃止。碎身至断命，　　夺象牛马财，破国灭亡尽，　　彼犹故和解。况汝小言骂，　　不能制和合，若不思真义，　　怨结焉得息。骂詈责数说，　　而能制和合，若思真实义，　　怨结必得息。若以诤止诤，　　至竟不见止，唯忍能止诤，　　是法可尊贵。瞋向慧真人，　　口说无赖言，诽谤牟尼圣，　　是下贱非智。他人不解义，　　唯我独能知，若有能解义，　　彼恚便得息。若得定为侣，　　慧者共修善，舍本所执意，　　欢喜常相随。若不得定伴，　　慧者独修善，如王严治国，　　如象独在野。独行莫为恶，　　如象独在野，独行为善胜，　　勿与恶共会。学不得善友，　　不与己等者，当坚意独住，　　勿与恶共会。」

Then the World-Honored One, having spoken this verse, immediately used his supernatural power to fly through the air and arrived at the village of Vārāṇasī. At that time, in the village of Vārāṇasī, there was an elder named Vāgīśa, who was a son of the Śākya clan. He practiced diligently day and night, his mind was always focused on the Dharma, and he abided in the principles of the path. When Elder Vāgīśa saw the Buddha coming from afar, he went to welcome him, received the Buddha's robe and alms bowl, prepared a seat for the Buddha, fetched water to wash the Buddha's feet, and after the Buddha had washed his feet, he sat on the seat of Elder Vāgīśa. Having sat down, he said, "Elder Vāgīśa, are you always at ease and without any lack?"

尔时，世尊说此颂已，即以如意足乘虚而去，至婆罗楼罗村。于是婆罗楼罗村，有尊者婆咎释家子，昼夜不眠，精勤行道，志行常定，住道品法。尊者释家子遥见佛来，见已往迎，摄佛衣钵，为佛敷床，汲水洗足，佛洗足已，坐尊者释家子婆咎座，坐已，告曰：「婆咎比丘！汝常安隐，无所乏耶？」

Elder Vāgīśa said, "World-Honored One! I am always at ease and without any lack."

尊者释家子婆咎白曰：「世尊！我常安隐，无有所乏。」

The World-Honored One again asked, "Elder Vāgīśa, how are you at ease and without any lack?"

世尊复问：「婆咎比丘！云何安隐，无所乏耶？」

Elder Vāgīśa said, "World-Honored One! I do not sleep day or night, diligently practicing the path, always focused in conduct, abiding in the Dharma of the path. World-Honored One! In this way, I am always at ease and without any lack."

尊者婆咎白曰：「世尊！我昼夜不眠，精勤行道，志行常定，住道品法。世尊！如是我常安隐，无有所乏。」

The World-Honored One further thought, "This noble son is traveling in comfort. I should now teach the Dharma for him." Having thought this, he then taught the Dharma to Elder Vāgīśa, inspiring him with longing and bringing about joy. Having used immeasurable skillful means to teach the Dharma for him, inspiring him with longing and bringing about joy, he rose from his seat and departed, going to the forest of the Guardian Monastery. Entering the forest of the Guardian Monastery, he went to the foot of a tree, spread out his sitting mat, and sat cross-legged.

世尊复念：「此族姓子游行安乐，我今宁可为彼说法。」作是念已，便为尊者婆咎说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，从坐起去，往至护寺林。入护寺林中，至一树下，敷尼师檀，结跏趺坐。

The World-Honored One further thought, "I have already been freed from those bhikṣus of Kośala who repeatedly quarrel, defeat each other, hate each other, and dispute with anger. I do not think of that place with joy, namely the place where those bhikṣus of Kośala reside."

世尊复念：「我已得脱彼拘舍弥诸比丘辈，数数鬪讼，相伏相憎，相瞋共诤，我不喜念彼方，谓拘舍弥诸比丘辈所住处也。」

At that time, there was a great elephant who was the king of the herd. He left the elephant herd and roamed alone, also going to the forest of the Guardian Monastery. Entering the forest of the Guardian Monastery, he went to the wise śāla tree and stood leaning against the wise śāla tree. At that time, the great elephant had this thought: "I have already been freed from those elephant herds, the female elephants, male elephants, and young elephants. Those elephant herds always go ahead, trampling the grass and muddying the water. I used to eat the trampled grass and drink the muddy water. Now I eat fresh grass and drink clear water."

当尔之时，有一大象为众象王，彼离象众而独游行，亦至护寺林。入护寺林中，至贤娑罗树，倚贤娑罗树立。尔时，大象而作是念：「我已得脱彼群象辈，牝象、牡象，大小象子，彼群象辈常在前行，草为之蹋，水为之浑，我于尔时，食彼蹋草，饮浑浊水，我今饮食新草、清水。」

Then the World-Honored One, from the forest of the temple protector, put on his robe, took his bowl, and went to the forest of the temple of Banamandaka. At that time, there were three clansmen living together in the forest of the temple of Banamandaka, Venerable Aniruddha, Venerable Nandi, and Venerable Kimbila. Those Venerables practiced like this: If one of them returned from alms-round first, he would prepare the bed, fetch water, wash the foot basin, place the foot wiper, the water pot, and the washbasin, and if the alms-food could be finished, he would finish it; if there was any left, he would cover it in a vessel. After eating, he would put away the bowl, wash his hands and feet, put the sitting mat on his shoulder, and enter the room to sit in meditation. If one of them returned from alms-round later, and if the food could be finished, he would finish it; if it was not enough, he would take the remaining food from before and finish it. If there was any left, he would pour it on a clean place or in insect-free water, take the food vessel, wash it, wipe it, and put it aside. He would put away the bed, collect the foot wiper, put away the foot basin, the water pot, and the washbasin, sweep and sprinkle the dining hall, clean it, put away the robe and bowl, wash his hands and feet, put the sitting mat on his shoulder, and enter the room to sit in meditation. Those Venerables would gather together every five days, either to discuss the Dharma or to be in noble silence.
