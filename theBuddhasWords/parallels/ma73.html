MĀ 73

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 7 长寿王品

73. （七三）天经

Parallel to: AN 8.64

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the country of Jīti, staying in the forest by the water.

一时，佛游枝提瘦，在水渚林中。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "When I had not yet attained the unsurpassed perfect true path, I thought like this: 'I should be able to generate light, and through that light see forms. In this way, my wisdom and vision will be extremely bright and pure.' For the sake of having extremely bright and pure wisdom and vision, I then stayed in seclusion, dwelling alone, with no negligence, practicing diligently. Because I stayed in seclusion, dwelling alone, with no negligence, practicing diligently, I then attained light and was able to see forms. However, I had not yet gathered together with those devas, had not yet consoled and addressed each other, had not yet discussed anything, had not yet responded to anything.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「我本未得觉无上正真道时，而作是念：『我宁可得生其光明，因其光明而见形色。如是我智见极大明净。』我为智见极明净故，便在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤。我因在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤故，即得光明，便见形色也。然我未与彼天共同集会，未相慰劳，未有所论说，未有所答对。

I further thought like this: 'I should be able to generate light, and through that light see forms, and also gather together with those devas, console and address each other, discuss things, and respond to things. In this way, my wisdom and vision will be extremely bright and pure.' For the sake of having extremely bright and pure wisdom and vision, I then stayed in seclusion, dwelling alone, with no negligence, practicing diligently. Because I stayed in seclusion, dwelling alone, with no negligence, practicing diligently, I then attained light, was able to see forms, and gathered together with those devas, consoled and addressed each other, discussed things, and responded to things. However, I did not know those devas' surnames, names, and origins.

「我复作是念：『我宁可得生其光明，因其光明而见形色，及与彼天共同集会，共相慰劳，有所论说，有所答对。如是我智见极大明净。』我为智见极明净故，便在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤。我因在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤故，即得光明，便见形色，及与彼天共同集会，共相慰劳，有所论说，有所答对也。然我不知彼天如是姓、如是字、如是生。

I further thought like this: 'I should be able to generate light, and through that light see forms, and also gather together with those devas, console and address each other, discuss things, and respond to things; and also know those devas' surnames, names, and origins. In this way, my wisdom and vision will be extremely bright and pure.' For the sake of having extremely bright and pure wisdom and vision, I then stayed in seclusion, dwelling alone, with no negligence, practicing diligently. Because I stayed in seclusion, dwelling alone, with no negligence, practicing diligently, I then attained light, was able to see forms, and gathered together with those devas, consoled and addressed each other, discussed things, and responded to things; and also knew those devas' surnames, names, and origins. However, I did not know those devas' food and their experience of suffering and happiness.

「我复作是念：『我宁可得生其光明，因其光明而见形色，及与彼天共同集会，共相慰劳，有所论说，有所答对；亦知彼天如是姓、如是字、如是生。如是我智见极大明净。』我为智见极明净故，便在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤。我因在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤故，即得光明，便见形色，及与彼天共同集会，共相慰劳，有所论说，有所答对；亦知彼天如是姓、如是字、如是生也。然我不知彼天如是食、如是受苦乐。

I further thought like this: 'I should be able to generate light, and through that light see forms, and also gather together with those devas, console and address each other, discuss things, and respond to things; and also know those devas' surnames, names, and origins; and also know those devas' food and their experience of suffering and happiness. In this way, my wisdom and vision will be extremely bright and pure.' For the sake of having extremely bright and pure wisdom and vision, I then stayed in seclusion, dwelling alone, with no negligence, practicing diligently. Because I stayed in seclusion, dwelling alone, with no negligence, practicing diligently, I then attained light, was able to see forms, and gathered together with those devas, consoled and addressed each other, discussed things, and responded to things; and also knew those devas' surnames, names, and origins; and also knew those devas' food and their experience of suffering and happiness. However, I did not know those devas' lifespan, duration of life, and end of life.

「我复作是念：『我宁可得生其光明，因其光明而见形色，及与彼天共同集会，共相慰劳，有所论说，有所答对；亦知彼天如是姓、如是字、如是生；亦知彼天如是食、如是受苦乐。如是我智见极大明净。』我为智见极明净故，便在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤。我因在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤故，即得光明，便见形色，及与彼天共同集会，共相慰劳，有所论说，有所答对；亦知彼天如是姓、如是字、如是生；亦知彼天如是食、如是受苦乐也。然我不知彼天如是长寿、如是久住、如是命尽。

I further thought like this: 'I should be able to generate light, and through that light see forms, and also gather together with those devas, console and address each other, discuss things, and respond to things; and also know those devas' surnames, names, and origins; and also know those devas' food and their experience of suffering and happiness; and also know those devas' lifespan, duration of life, and end of life. In this way, my wisdom and vision will be extremely bright and pure.' For the sake of having extremely bright and pure wisdom and vision, I then stayed in seclusion, dwelling alone, with no negligence, practicing diligently. Because I stayed in seclusion, dwelling alone, with no negligence, practicing diligently, I then attained light, was able to see forms, and gathered together with those devas, consoled and addressed each other, discussed things, and responded to things; and also knew those devas' surnames, names, and origins; and also knew those devas' food and their experience of suffering and happiness; and also knew those devas' lifespan, duration of life, and end of life. However, I did not know those devas' having done such and such actions, dying here and being born there.

「我复作是念：『我宁可得生其光明，因其光明而见形色，及与彼天共同集会，共相慰劳，有所论说，有所答对；亦知彼天如是姓、如是字、如是生；亦知彼天如是食、如是受苦乐，亦知彼天如是长寿、如是久住、如是命尽。如是我智见极大明净。』我为智见极明净故，便在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤。我因在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤故，即得光明，便见形色，及与彼天共同集会，共相慰劳，有所论说，有所答对；亦知彼天如是姓、如是字、如是生；亦知彼天如是食、如是受苦乐；亦知彼天如是长寿、如是久住、如是命尽也。然我不知彼天作如是如是业已，死此生彼。

I further thought like this: 'I should be able to generate light, and through that light see forms, and also gather together with those devas, console and address each other, discuss things, and respond to things; and also know those devas' surnames, names, and origins; and also know those devas' food and their experience of suffering and happiness; and also know those devas' lifespan, duration of life, and end of life; and also know those devas' having done such and such actions, dying here and being born there. In this way, my wisdom and vision will be extremely bright and pure.' For the sake of having extremely bright and pure wisdom and vision, I then stayed in seclusion, dwelling alone, with no negligence, practicing diligently. Because I stayed in seclusion, dwelling alone, with no negligence, practicing diligently, I then attained light, was able to see forms, and gathered together with those devas, consoled and addressed each other, discussed things, and responded to things; and also knew those devas' surnames, names, and origins; and also knew those devas' food and their experience of suffering and happiness; and also knew those devas' lifespan, duration of life, and end of life; and also knew those devas' having done such and such actions, dying here and being born there. However, I did not know those devas' among those devas.

「我复作是念：『我宁可得生其光明，因其光明而见形色，及与彼天共同集会，共相慰劳，有所论说，有所答对；亦知彼天如是姓、如是字、如是生；亦知彼天如是食、如是受苦乐；亦知彼天如是长寿、如是久住、如是命尽；亦知彼天作如是如是业已，死此生彼。如是我智见极大明净。』我为智见极明净故，便在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤。我因在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤故，即得光明，便见形色，及与彼天共同集会，共相慰劳，有所论说，有所答对；亦知彼天如是姓、如是字、如是生；亦知彼天如是食、如是受苦乐；亦知彼天如是长寿、如是久住、如是命尽；亦知彼天作如是如是业已，死此生彼也。然我不知彼天、彼彼天中。

I further thought like this: 'I should be able to generate light, and through that light see forms, and also gather together with those devas, console and address each other, discuss things, and respond to things; and also know those devas' surnames, names, and origins; and also know those devas' food and their experience of suffering and happiness; and also know those devas' lifespan, duration of life, and end of life; and also know those devas' having done such and such actions, dying here and being born there; and also know those devas' among those devas. In this way, my wisdom and vision will be extremely bright and pure.' For the sake of having extremely bright and pure wisdom and vision, I then stayed in seclusion, dwelling alone, with no negligence, practicing diligently. Because I stayed in seclusion, dwelling alone, with no negligence, practicing diligently, I then attained light, was able to see forms, and gathered together with those devas, consoled and addressed each other, discussed things, and responded to things; and also knew those devas' surnames, names, and origins; and also knew those devas' food and their experience of suffering and happiness; and also knew those devas' lifespan, duration of life, and end of life; and also knew those devas' having done such and such actions, dying here and being born there; and also knew those devas' among those devas. However, I did not know those devas' among those devas.

「我复作是念：『我宁可得生其光明，因其光明而见形色，及与彼天共同集会，共相慰劳，有所论说，有所答对；亦知彼天如是姓、如是字、如是生；亦知彼天如是食、如是受苦乐；亦知彼天如是长寿、如是久住、如是命尽；亦知彼天作如是如是业已，死此生彼；亦知彼天、彼彼天中。如是我智见极大明净。』我为智见极明净故，便在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤。我因在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤故，即得光明，便见形色，及与彼天共同集会，共相慰劳，有所论说，有所答对；亦知彼天如是姓、如是字、如是生；亦知彼天如是食、如是受苦乐；亦知彼天如是长寿、如是久住、如是命尽；亦知彼天作如是如是业已，死此生彼；亦知彼天、彼彼天中也。然我不知彼天上，我曾生中、未曾生中。

I further thought like this: 'I should be able to generate light, and through that light see forms, and also gather together with those devas, console and address each other, discuss things, and respond to things; and also know those devas' surnames, names, and origins; and also know those devas' food and their experience of suffering and happiness; and also know those devas' lifespan, duration of life, and end of life; and also know those devas' having done such and such actions, dying here and being born there; and also know those devas' among those devas; and also know those devas' among those devas.

「我复作是念：『我宁可得生其光明，因其光明而见形色，及与彼天共同集会，共相慰劳，有所论说，有所答对；亦知彼天如是姓、如是字、如是生；亦知彼天如是食、如是受苦乐；亦知彼天如是长寿、如是久住、如是命尽；亦知彼天作如是如是业已，死此生彼；亦知彼天、彼彼天中；亦知彼天上，我曾生中、未曾生中。如是我智见极大明净。』我为智见极明净故，便在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤。我因在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤故，即得光明，便见形色，及与彼天共同集会，共相慰劳，有所论说，有所答对；亦知彼天如是姓、如是字、如是生；亦知彼天如是食、如是受苦乐；亦知彼天如是长寿、如是久住、如是命尽；亦知彼天作如是如是业已，死此生彼；亦知彼天、彼彼天中；亦知彼天上，我曾生中、未曾生中也。

"If I do not correctly know and attain these eight things, then I cannot be said to have attained them, and I also do not know that I have attained supreme perfect enlightenment; I also cannot be said to be superior to all the world, including the gods, demons, Brahmas, and ascetics and Brahmins; I also have not attained various kinds of liberation; I also have not yet abandoned all delusions, and have not realized the end of birth, established the Brahma-practice, accomplished what was to be done, and will not again experience existence, knowing it as it really is. If I correctly know and attain these eight things, then I can be said to have attained them, and I also know that I have attained supreme perfect enlightenment; I also can be said to be superior to all the world, including the gods, demons, Brahmas, and ascetics and Brahmins; I also have attained various kinds of liberation, and my mind has abandoned all delusions, the end of birth has been realized, the Brahma-practice has been established, what was to be done has been accomplished, and I will not again experience existence, knowing it as it really is."

「若我不正知得此八行者，便不可一向说得，亦不知我得觉无上正真之道；我亦于此世间，诸天、魔、梵、沙门、梵志不能出过其上；我亦不得解脱种种解脱；我亦未离诸颠倒，未生已尽、梵行已立、所作已办、不更受有，知如真。若我正知得此八行者，便可一向说得，亦知我得觉无上正真之道；我亦于此世间，诸天、魔、梵、沙门、梵志出过其上；我亦得解脱种种解脱，我心已离诸颠倒，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
