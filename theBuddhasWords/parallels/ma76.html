MĀ 76

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 7 长寿王品

76. （七六）郁伽支罗经

Parallel to: SN 47.3 (~) / AN 8.63

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in Ukkattha, on the banks of the Ganges River.

一时，佛游郁伽支罗，在恒水池岸。

At that time, a certain bhikṣu rose from his afternoon meditation, went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said, "World-Honored One! Please briefly teach the Dharma for me. Having heard it from the World-Honored One, I will dwell in seclusion, alone, without negligence, practicing diligently. Because of dwelling in seclusion, alone, without negligence, practicing diligently, the purpose of the clansmen who shave their heads and beards, put on the saffron robes, go forth from the household to homelessness, and practice the path, is to complete the unsurpassed holy life, to know and realize for oneself in this very life, to accomplish and dwell in it, to end birth, establish the holy life, do what is to be done, and not be subject to another existence, knowing and seeing it as it really is."

尔时，一比丘则于晡时从燕坐起，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，却坐一面，白曰：「世尊！唯愿为我善略说法。从法世尊闻已，在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤。因在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤故，族姓子所为，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，唯无上梵行讫，于现法中自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。」

The World-Honored One said, "Bhikṣu! You should train like this: to make the mind dwell internally, without moving, to cultivate immeasurable goodness, and to contemplate the internal body as it really is. Practice diligently, establish right mindfulness and right knowledge, and well control your mind, to be free from stinginess and greed, and to have no sorrow or distress. Also contemplate the external body as it really is. Practice diligently, establish right mindfulness and right knowledge, and well control your mind, to be free from stinginess and greed, and to have no sorrow or distress. Also contemplate the internal and external body as it really is. Practice diligently, establish right mindfulness and right knowledge, and well control your mind, to be free from stinginess and greed, and to have no sorrow or distress. Bhikṣu! Such samādhi should be well cultivated when going, coming, standing, sitting, lying down, sleeping, waking, and when sleeping and waking. Furthermore, you should cultivate samādhi with initial and sustained thought, samādhi with initial thought and little sustained thought, and samādhi without initial thought and sustained thought. You should also cultivate samādhi accompanied by joy, samādhi accompanied by pleasure, samādhi accompanied by samādhi, and samādhi accompanied by equanimity.

世尊告曰：「比丘！当如是学，令心得住，在内不动无量善修，复观内身如身，行极精勤，立正念、正智，善自御心，令离悭贪，意无忧戚。复观外身如身，行极精勤，立正念、正智，善自御心，令离悭贪，意无忧戚。复观内外身如身，行极精勤，立正念、正智，善自御心，令离悭贪，意无忧戚。比丘！如此之定，去时、来时当善修习，住时、坐时、卧时、眠时、寤时、眠寤时亦当修习。复次，亦当修习有觉有观定、无觉少观定，修习无觉无观定，亦当修习喜共俱定、乐共俱定、定共俱定，修习舍共俱定。

Bhikṣu! If you cultivate this samādhi well, bhikṣu! You should further cultivate contemplating internal feelings as they really are. Practice diligently, establish right mindfulness and right knowledge, and well control your mind, to be free from stinginess and greed, and to have no sorrow or distress. Also contemplate external feelings as they really are. Practice diligently, establish right mindfulness and right knowledge, and well control your mind, to be free from stinginess and greed, and to have no sorrow or distress. Also contemplate internal and external feelings as they really are. Practice diligently, establish right mindfulness and right knowledge, and well control your mind, to be free from stinginess and greed, and to have no sorrow or distress. Bhikṣu! Such samādhi should be well cultivated when going, coming, standing, sitting, lying down, sleeping, waking, and when sleeping and waking. Furthermore, you should cultivate samādhi with initial and sustained thought, samādhi with initial thought and little sustained thought, and samādhi without initial thought and sustained thought. You should also cultivate samādhi accompanied by joy, samādhi accompanied by pleasure, samādhi accompanied by samādhi, and samādhi accompanied by equanimity.

「比丘！若修此定极善修者，比丘！当复更修观内觉如觉，行极精勤，立正念、正智，善自御心，令离悭贪，意无忧戚。复观外觉如觉，行极精勤，立正念、正智，善自御心，令离悭贪，意无忧戚。复观内外觉如觉，行极精勤，立正念、正智，善自御心，令离悭贪，意无忧戚。比丘！如此之定，去时、来时当善修习，住时、坐时、卧时、眠时、寤时、眠寤时亦当修习。复次，亦当修习有觉有观定、无觉少观定，修习无觉无观定，亦当修习喜共俱定、乐共俱定、定共俱定，修习舍共俱定。

Bhikṣu! If you cultivate this samādhi well, bhikṣu! You should further cultivate contemplating the internal mind as it really is. Practice diligently, establish right mindfulness and right knowledge, and well control your mind, to be free from stinginess and greed, and to have no sorrow or distress. Also contemplate the external mind as it really is. Practice diligently, establish right mindfulness and right knowledge, and well control your mind, to be free from stinginess and greed, and to have no sorrow or distress. Also contemplate the internal and external mind as it really is. Practice diligently, establish right mindfulness and right knowledge, and well control your mind, to be free from stinginess and greed, and to have no sorrow or distress. Bhikṣu! Such samādhi should be well cultivated when going, coming, standing, sitting, lying down, sleeping, waking, and when sleeping and waking. Furthermore, you should cultivate samādhi with initial and sustained thought, samādhi with initial thought and little sustained thought, and samādhi without initial thought and sustained thought. You should also cultivate samādhi accompanied by joy, samādhi accompanied by pleasure, samādhi accompanied by samādhi, and samādhi accompanied by equanimity.

「比丘！若修此定极善修者，比丘！当复更修观内心如心，行极精勤，立正念、正智，善自御心，令离悭贪，意无忧戚。复观外心如心，行极精勤，立正念、正智，善自御心，令离悭贪，意无忧戚。复观内外心如心，行极精勤，立正念、正智，善自御心，令离悭贪，意无忧戚。比丘！如此之定，去时、来时当善修习，住时、坐时、卧时、眠时、寤时、眠寤时亦当修习。复次，亦当修习有觉有观定、无觉少观定，修习无觉无观定，亦当修习喜共俱定、乐共俱定、定共俱定，修习舍共俱定。

Bhikṣu! If you cultivate this samādhi well, bhikṣu! You should further cultivate contemplating internal dharmas as they really are. Practice diligently, establish right mindfulness and right knowledge, and well control your mind, to be free from stinginess and greed, and to have no sorrow or distress. Also contemplate external dharmas as they really are. Practice diligently, establish right mindfulness and right knowledge, and well control your mind, to be free from stinginess and greed, and to have no sorrow or distress. Also contemplate internal and external dharmas as they really are. Practice diligently, establish right mindfulness and right knowledge, and well control your mind, to be free from stinginess and greed, and to have no sorrow or distress. Bhikṣu! Such samādhi should be well cultivated when going, coming, standing, sitting, lying down, sleeping, waking, and when sleeping and waking. Furthermore, you should cultivate samādhi with initial and sustained thought, samādhi with initial thought and little sustained thought, and samādhi without initial thought and sustained thought. You should also cultivate samādhi accompanied by joy, samādhi accompanied by pleasure, samādhi accompanied by samādhi, and samādhi accompanied by equanimity.

「比丘！若修此定极善修者，比丘！当复更修观内法如法，行极精勤，立正念、正智，善自御心，令离悭贪，意无忧戚。复观外法如法，行极精勤，立正念、正智，善自御心，令离悭贪，意无忧戚。复观内外法如法，行极精勤，立正念、正智，善自御心，令离悭贪，意无忧戚。比丘！如此之定，去时、来时当善修习，住时、坐时、卧时、眠时、寤时、眠寤时亦当修习。复次，亦当修习有觉有观定、无觉少观定，修习无觉无观定，亦当修习喜共俱定、乐共俱定、定共俱定，修习舍共俱定。

Bhikṣu! If you cultivate this samādhi well, bhikṣu! Your mind should be accompanied by loving-kindness, and pervade one direction, accomplishing and dwelling. Thus, the second, third, and fourth directions, the four intermediate directions, above and below, everywhere, your mind is accompanied by loving-kindness, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without conflict, extremely vast, very great, immeasurable, well cultivated, and pervades all worlds, accomplishing and dwelling; thus, compassion and joy, your mind is accompanied by equanimity, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without conflict, extremely vast, very great, immeasurable, well cultivated, and pervades all worlds, accomplishing and dwelling.

「比丘！若修此定极善修者，比丘！心当与慈俱，遍满一方成就游，如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游；如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。

Bhikṣu! If you cultivate this samādhi well, if you travel to the east, you will surely attain peace and happiness, without any suffering or affliction; if you travel to the south, west, or north, you will surely attain peace and happiness, without any suffering or affliction. Bhikṣu! If you cultivate this samādhi well, I do not even say that your wholesome dharmas will remain, let alone say that they will decline. You should only increase wholesome dharmas day and night, without decline. Bhikṣu! If you cultivate this samādhi well, you will surely attain one of two fruits, either attaining final knowledge in this life, or else attaining the state of a non-returner.

「比丘！若汝修习此定极善修者，若游东方，必得安乐，无众苦患；若游南方、西方、北方者，必得安乐，无众苦患。比丘！若汝修习此定极善修者，我尚不说汝诸善法住，况说衰退？但当昼夜增长善法而不衰退。比丘！若汝修习此定极善修者，汝于二果必得其一，或于现世得究竟智，或复有余得阿那含。」

Then that bhikṣu, having heard the Buddha's words, accepted them well and remembered them well. He immediately rose from his seat, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and departed. He upheld the Buddha's teachings, dwelling in seclusion, alone, without negligence, practicing diligently. Because of dwelling in seclusion, alone, without negligence, and practicing diligently, the noble son, for the sake of which he shaved off his hair and beard, donned the saffron robes, left home out of faith, and went forth to practice the path, only completed the unsurpassed holy life in this very life, knowing and realizing it for himself, accomplishing and dwelling in it. Birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, there will be no more of this mundane state - he knew it as it really is. That venerable one, having known the Dharma, attained arahantship.

于是，彼比丘闻佛所说，善受善持，即从坐起，稽首佛足，绕三匝而去，受持佛教，在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤。因在远离独住，心无放逸，修行精勤故，族姓子所为，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，唯无上梵行讫，于现法中自知自觉，自作证成就游，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。彼尊者知法已至，得阿罗诃。

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
