MĀ 78

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 7 长寿王品

78. （七八）梵天请佛经

Parallel to: MN 49

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, there was a Brahmā god dwelling in the Brahma Heaven, who gave rise to such a wrong view: "This place is permanent, this place is constant, this place is eternal, this place is necessary, this place is not subject to cessation, this place is the way out, there is no way out that surpasses this way out, there is superiority, there is excellence, there is the most excellent one."

尔时，有一梵天住梵天上，生如是邪见：「此处有常，此处有恒，此处长存，此处是要，此处不终法，此处出要，此出要更无出要过其上，有胜、有妙、有最者。」

Then, the World-Honored One, with his knowledge of others' minds, knew what that Brahmā god was thinking. He immediately entered the samādhi of suchness, and with the samādhi of suchness, in the time it takes a strong man to bend and stretch his arm, he suddenly disappeared from the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī and went to the Brahma Heaven.

于是，世尊以他心智，知彼梵天心之所念，即入如其像定，以如其像定，犹若力士屈申臂顷，于舍卫国胜林给孤独园忽没不现，往梵天上。

At that time, that Brahmā god saw the World-Honored One coming and invited him, saying: "Welcome, great sage! This place is permanent, this place is constant, this place is eternal, this place is necessary, this place is not subject to cessation, this place is the way out, there is no way out that surpasses this way out, there is superiority, there is excellence, there is the most excellent one."

时，彼梵天见世尊来，即请世尊：「善来大仙人！此处有常，此处有恒，此处长存，此处是要，此处不终法，此处出要，此出要更无出要过其上，有胜、有妙、有最者。」

Then, the World-Honored One said: "Brahmā god! You call the impermanent permanent, the inconstant constant, the non-abiding abiding, the unnecessary necessary, the subject to cessation not subject to cessation, the non-way out the way out, and this way out has no way out that surpasses it, there is superiority, there is excellence, there is the most excellent one. Brahmā god! You have this ignorance. Brahmā god! You have this ignorance."

于是，世尊告曰：「梵天！汝无常称说常，不恒称说恒，不存称说存，不要称说要，终法称说不终法，非出要称说出要，此出要更无出要过其上，有胜、有妙、有最者。梵天！汝有是无明。梵天！汝有是无明。」

At that time, Māra Pāpīyas was in the assembly. Then, Māra Pāpīyas said to the World-Honored One: "Monk! Do not go against what this Brahmā god says, do not go against what this Brahmā god says. Monk! If you go against what this Brahmā god says, go against what this Brahmā god says, it is like someone who rejects good fortune when it comes. Monk! What you say is also like this. Therefore, monk! I tell you not to go against what this Brahmā god says, not to go against what this Brahmā god says. Monk! If you go against what this Brahmā god says, go against what this Brahmā god says, it is like someone falling from a mountain, though they may reach out with their hands and feet to grasp at the empty air, they will find nothing. Monk! What you say is also like this. Therefore, monk! I tell you not to go against what this Brahmā god says, not to go against what this Brahmā god says. Monk! If you go against what this Brahmā god says, go against what this Brahmā god says, it is like someone falling from a tree, though they may reach out with their hands and feet to grasp at the branches and leaves, they will find nothing. Monk! What you say is also like this. Therefore, monk! I tell you not to go against what this Brahmā god says, not to go against what this Brahmā god says. Why is that? This Brahmā god is the creator, the most honored, the creator, the maker, the father, from whom all beings have come and will come. He knows all that is to be known, sees all that is to be seen.

时，魔波旬在彼众中。于是，魔波旬语世尊曰：「比丘！莫违此梵天所说，莫逆此梵天所说。比丘！若汝违此梵天所说，逆此梵天所说者，是为比丘犹如有人吉祥事来，而排却之。比丘所说亦复如是，是故，比丘！我语汝莫违此梵天所说，莫逆此梵天所说。比丘！若汝违此梵天所说，逆此梵天所说者，是为，比丘！犹如有人从山上堕，虽以手足扪摸于空而无所得。比丘所说亦复如是，是故，比丘！我语汝莫违此梵天所说，莫逆此梵天所说。比丘！若汝违此梵天所说，逆此梵天所说者，是为，比丘！犹如有人从树上堕，虽以手足扪摸枝叶而无所得。比丘所说亦复如是，是故，比丘！我语汝莫违此梵天所说，莫逆此梵天所说。所以者何？此梵天梵，福祐、能化、最尊、能作、能造、是父，已有、当有一切众生皆从是生，此所知尽知、所见尽见。

"Great seer! If there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who hate and revile the earth, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely be reborn among the inferior gandharvas. Similarly, those who hate and revile the water, fire, wind, gods, heaven, and Brahmā will also be reborn among the inferior gandharvas. Great seer! If there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who love and praise the earth, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will definitely be reborn in the most honored Brahmā heaven. Similarly, those who love and praise the water, fire, wind, gods, heaven, and Brahmā will also be reborn in the most honored Brahmā heaven. Great seer! Do you not see this great retinue of Brahmā sitting like us?"

「大仙人！若有沙门、梵志，憎恶地、毁呰地者，彼身坏命终，必生余下贱妓乐神中，如是水、火、风、神、天、生主，憎恶梵天、毁呰梵天者，彼身坏命终，必生余下贱妓乐神中。大仙人！若有沙门、梵志，爱乐地、称叹地者，彼身坏命终，必生最上尊梵天中，如是水、火、风、神、天、生主，爱乐梵天、称叹梵天者，彼身坏命终，必生最上尊梵天中。大仙人！汝不见此梵天大眷属坐如我辈耶？」

Māra Pāpīyas is not a Brahmā god, nor is he a member of the Brahmā god's retinue, yet he claims to be a Brahmā god. At that time, the World-Honored One had this thought: "This Māra Pāpīyas is not a Brahmā god, nor is he a member of the Brahmā god's retinue, yet he claims to be a Brahmā god; if one speaks of the existence of Māra Pāpīyas, this is Māra Pāpīyas himself."

彼魔波旬非是梵天，亦非梵天眷属，然自称说我是梵天。尔时，世尊便作是念：「此魔波旬非是梵天，亦非梵天眷属，然自称说我是梵天；若说有魔波旬者，此即是魔波旬。」

The World-Honored One, knowing this, said, "Māra Pāpīyas! You are not a Brahmā god, nor are you a member of the Brahmā god's retinue, yet you claim to be a Brahmā god; if one speaks of the existence of Māra Pāpīyas, you are Māra Pāpīyas himself." Then Māra Pāpīyas thought, "The World-Honored One knows me, the Well-Gone One sees me." Knowing this, he became anxious and suddenly disappeared from that place.

世尊知已，告曰：「魔波旬！汝非梵天，亦非梵天眷属，然汝自称说我是梵天；若说有魔波旬者，汝即是魔波旬。」于是，魔波旬而作是念：「世尊知我，善逝见我。」知已愁忧，即于彼处忽没不现。

At that time, the Brahmā god repeatedly invited the World-Honored One, saying, "Welcome, great sage! Here is the eternal, here is the constant, here is the enduring, here is the necessary, here is the imperishable, here is the way out, and there is no way out that surpasses it. There is the supreme, the wonderful, and the most excellent."

时，彼梵天至再三请世尊：「善来大仙人！此处有常，此处恒有，此处长存，此处是要，此处不终法，此处出要，此出要更无出要过其上，有胜、有妙、有最者。」

The World-Honored One also said this repeatedly, "Brahmā god! You call the impermanent permanent, the inconstant constant, the non-enduring enduring, the unnecessary necessary, the perishable imperishable, the non-way-out way-out, and there is no way-out that surpasses it. There is the supreme, the wonderful, and the most excellent. Brahmā god! You have this ignorance. Brahmā god! You have this ignorance."

世尊亦至再三告曰：「梵天！汝无常称说常，不恒称说恒，不存称说存，不要称说要，终法称说不终法，非出要称说出要，此出要更无出要过其上，有胜、有妙、有最者。梵天！汝有是无明。梵天！汝有是无明。」

Then, the Brahmā god said to the World-Honored One, "Great sage! In the past, there were śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas with extremely long lifespans, who lived for a very long time. Great sage! Your lifespan is very short, not even a single sitting session of those śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas. Why is that? Because they know everything that is to be known, and see everything that is to be seen. If there is a true way out, there is no other way out that surpasses it, which is supreme, wonderful, and most excellent. If there is no true way out, there is no other way out that surpasses it, which is supreme, wonderful, and most excellent. Great sage! You do not think about the way out, but think about the way out. In this way, you will not attain the way out, and will become greatly deluded. Why is that? Because there is no object of knowledge. Great sage! If there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who delight in the earth and praise the earth, they are under my control, they do as I wish, and they do as I command. Likewise, those who delight in water, fire, wind, spirits, gods, and Brahmā, and praise Brahmā, are under my control, they do as I wish, and they do as I command. Great sage! If you delight in the earth and praise the earth, you are also under my control, you do as I wish, and you do as I command. Likewise, those who delight in water, fire, wind, spirits, gods, and Brahmā, and praise Brahmā, you are also under my control, you do as I wish, and you do as I command."

于是，梵天白世尊曰：「大仙人！昔有沙门、梵志，寿命极长，存住极久。大仙人！汝寿至短，不如彼沙门、梵志一燕坐顷。所以者何？彼所知尽知、所见尽见，若实有出要者，更无余出要过其上，有胜、有妙、有最者；若无有实出要者，更无余出要过其上，有胜、有妙、有最者。大仙人！汝于出要不出要想，不出要出要想，如是汝不得出要，便成大痴。所以者何？以无境界故。大仙人！若有沙门、梵志，爱乐地、称叹地者，彼为我自在，为随我所欲，为随我所使。如是水、火、风、神、天、生主，爱乐梵天、称叹梵天者，彼为我自在，为随我所欲，为随我所使。大仙人！若汝爱乐地、称叹地者，汝亦为我自在，为随我所欲，为随我所使。如是水、火、风、神、天、生主，爱乐梵天、称叹梵天者，汝亦为我自在，为随我所欲，为随我所使。」

Then, the World-Honored One said, "Brahmā god! It is true what you say, Brahmā god, that if there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who delight in the earth and praise the earth, they are under your control, they do as you wish, and they do as you command. Likewise, those who delight in water, fire, wind, spirits, gods, and Brahmā, and praise Brahmā, are under your control, they do as you wish, and they do as you command. Brahmā god! If I delight in the earth and praise the earth, I am also under your control, I do as you wish, and I do as you command. Likewise, those who delight in water, fire, wind, spirits, gods, and Brahmā, and praise Brahmā, I am also under your control, I do as you wish, and I do as you command.

于是，世尊告曰：「梵天！如是梵天所说真谛：『若有沙门、梵志，爱乐地、称叹地者，彼为汝自在，为随汝所欲，为随汝所使。如是水、火、风、神、天、生主，爱乐梵天、称叹梵天者，彼为汝自在，为随汝所欲，为随汝所使。』梵天！若我爱乐地、称叹地者，我亦为汝自在，为随汝所欲，为随汝所使。如是水、火、风、神、天、生主，爱乐梵天、称叹梵天者，我亦为汝自在，为随汝所欲，为随汝所使。

"Brahmā god! If these eight things are what I delight in and praise, then they are also like that. Brahmā god! I know where you have come from, where you will go, where you dwell, where you will end, and where you will be reborn. If there is a Brahmā god with great supernatural power, great virtue, great merit, and great spiritual power."

「梵天！若此八事，我随其事爱乐称叹者，彼亦有如是。梵天！我知汝所从来处、所往至处、随所住、随所终、随所生。若有梵天有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神。」

Then, Brahmā god said to the World-Honored One, "Great Sage! How do you know what I know and see what I see? How do you recognize me? Just as the sun is self-luminous and illuminates all directions, this is the thousand worlds. Do you have mastery over the thousand worlds? Do you know that in those places there is no day or night? Great Sage, have you ever been there and experienced it many times?"

于是，梵天白世尊曰：「大仙人！汝云何知我所知、见我所见。云何识悉我？如日自在，明照诸方，是为千世界，于千世界中，汝得自在耶？知彼彼处无有昼夜，大仙人曾更历彼，数经历彼耶？」

The World-Honored One said, "Brahmā god! The sun is self-luminous and illuminates all directions, this is the thousand worlds. In the thousand worlds, I have mastery. I also know that in those places there is no day or night. Brahmā god! I have been there and experienced it many times. Brahmā god! There are three kinds of gods, the Light Gods, the Pure Light Gods, and the All-Pervading Pure Light Gods. Brahmā god! If those three kinds of gods have knowledge and vision, I also have that knowledge and vision. Brahmā god! If those three kinds of gods have no knowledge and vision, I also have knowledge and vision myself. Brahmā god! If those three kinds of gods and their retinues have knowledge and vision, I also have that knowledge and vision. Brahmā god! If those three kinds of gods and their retinues have no knowledge and vision, I also have knowledge and vision myself. Brahmā god! If you have knowledge and vision, I also have this knowledge and vision. Brahmā god! If you have no knowledge and vision, I also have knowledge and vision myself. Brahmā god! If you and your retinues have knowledge and vision, I also have this knowledge and vision. Brahmā god! If you and your retinues have no knowledge and vision, I also have knowledge and vision myself. Brahmā god! You are not equal to me in everything, not equal to me in all respects. But I am the most supreme and highest among you.

世尊告曰：「梵天！如日自在，明照诸方，是为千世界，于千世界中，我得自在，亦知彼彼处无有昼夜。梵天！我曾更历彼，我数经历彼。梵天！有三种天，光天、净光天、遍净光天。梵天！若彼三种天有知有见者，我亦有彼知见。梵天！若彼三种天无知无见者，我亦自有知见。梵天！若彼三种天及眷属有知有见者，我亦有彼知见。梵天！若彼三种天及眷属无知无见者，我亦自有知见。梵天！若汝有知有见者，我亦有此知见。梵天！若汝无知无见者，我亦自有知见。梵天！若汝及眷属有知有见者，我亦有此知见。梵天！若汝及眷属无知无见者，我亦自有知见。梵天！汝不与我一切等，不与我尽等，但我于汝最胜最上。」

Then, Brahmā god said to the World-Honored One, "Great Sage! How do you have the knowledge and vision of those three kinds of gods? If those three kinds of gods have no knowledge and vision, you also have knowledge and vision yourself. If those three kinds of gods and their retinues have knowledge and vision, you also have that knowledge and vision. If those three kinds of gods and their retinues have no knowledge and vision, you also have knowledge and vision yourself. If I have knowledge and vision, you also have this knowledge and vision. If I have no knowledge and vision, you also have knowledge and vision yourself. If I and my retinues have knowledge and vision, you also have this knowledge and vision. If I and my retinues have no knowledge and vision, you also have knowledge and vision yourself. Great Sage! Is this not just empty talk? Asking without knowing only increases ignorance. Why is that? Because the realm of knowledge is immeasurable, with immeasurable knowledge, immeasurable vision, and immeasurable distinctions. I know each distinction separately. The earth knows the earth, water, fire, wind, spirits, gods, and the lord of birth. Brahmā god knows Brahmā god."

于是，梵天白世尊曰：「大仙人！何由得彼三种天有知有见者，汝亦有彼知见；若彼三种天无知无见者，汝亦自有知见；若彼三种天及眷属有知有见者，汝亦有彼知见，若彼三种天及眷属无知无见者，汝亦自有知见。若我有知有见者，汝亦有此知见；若我无知无见者，汝亦自有知见；若我及眷属有知有见者，汝亦有此知见；若我及眷属无知无见者，汝亦自有知见。大仙人！非为爱言耶？问已不知增益愚痴。所以者何？以识无量境界故，无量知、无量见、无量种别，我各各知别，是地知地，水、火、风、神、天、生主，是梵天知梵天。」

Then, the World-Honored One said, "Brahmā god! If there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who have the notion of earth regarding earth, they think, 'Earth is mine, earth belongs to me, I am possessed by earth.' Having regarded earth as 'mine,' they do not know earth. Thus, regarding water, fire, wind, spirits, gods, the lord of birth, Brahmā god, the trouble-free, the heatless, and purity, they have the notion of purity, they think, 'Purity is mine, purity belongs to me, I am possessed by purity.' Having regarded purity as 'mine,' they do not know purity. Brahmā god! If there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who know earth as earth, earth is not mine, earth does not belong to me, I am not possessed by earth; having not regarded earth as 'mine,' they know earth. Thus, regarding water, fire, wind, spirits, gods, the lord of birth, Brahmā god, the trouble-free, the heatless, and purity, they know purity as purity, purity is not mine, purity does not belong to me, I am not possessed by purity; having not regarded purity as 'mine,' they know purity. Brahmā god! I know earth as earth, earth is not mine, earth does not belong to me, I am not possessed by earth; having not regarded earth as 'mine,' I know earth. Thus, regarding water, fire, wind, spirits, gods, the lord of birth, Brahmā god, the trouble-free, the heatless, and purity, I know purity as purity, purity is not mine, purity does not belong to me, I am not possessed by purity; having not regarded purity as 'mine,' I know purity."

于是，世尊告曰：「梵天！若有沙门、梵志于地有地想，地是我，地是我所，我是地所；彼计地是我已，便不知地。如是于水、火、风、神、天、生主、梵天、无烦、无热，净有净想，净是我，净是我所，我是净所；彼计净是我已，便不知净。梵天！若有沙门、梵志，地则知地，地非是我，地非我所，我非地所；彼不计地是我已，彼便知地。如是水、火、风、神、天、生主、梵天、无烦、无热，净则知净，净非是我，净非我所，我非净所；彼不计净是我已，彼便知净。梵天！我于地则知地，地非是我，地非我所，我非地所；我不计地是我已，我便知地。如是水、火、风、神、天、生主、梵天、无烦、无热，净则知净，净非是我，净非我所，我非净所；我不计净是我已，我便知净。」

Then, Brahmā god said to the World-Honored One, "Great Sage! These beings love existence, delight in existence, and are accustomed to existence. You have already uprooted the root of existence. Why is that? Because of the Tathāgata, the Unattached, the Perfectly Enlightened One." He then spoke a verse, saying:

于是，梵天白世尊曰：「大仙人！此众生爱有、乐有、习有，汝已拔有根本。所以者何？谓如来、无所著、等正觉故。」便说颂曰：

Those who see existence as terrifying do not fear non-existence. Therefore
do not delight in existence. What is there that cannot be eliminated?

「于有见恐怖，　　无有见不惧，是故莫乐有，　　有何不可断。

"Great Sage! I now wish to make myself invisible."

「大仙人！我今欲自隐形。」

The World-Honored One said, "Brahma Heaven! If you wish to make yourself invisible, then do as you please."

世尊告曰：「梵天！汝若欲自隐形者，便随所欲。」

Then Brahma Heaven immediately made himself invisible wherever he was. The World-Honored One immediately knew, "Brahma Heaven! You are there, you are here, you are in between."

于是，梵天即随所处自隐其形。世尊即知，「梵天！汝在彼，汝在此，汝在中。」

Then Brahma Heaven fully displayed his supernatural powers, wishing to make himself invisible but unable to do so. He returned to the Brahma Heaven. Then the World-Honored One said, "Brahma Heaven! I now also wish to make myself invisible."

于是，梵天尽现如意，欲自隐形而不能隐，还住梵天中。于是，世尊告曰：「梵天！我今亦欲自隐其形。」

Brahma Heaven said to the World-Honored One, "Great Sage! If you wish to make yourself invisible, then do as you wish."

梵天白世尊曰：「大仙人！若欲自隐形者，便随所欲。」

Then the World-Honored One thought, "I should now manifest the supernatural power of the image as it is, emit extremely wonderful light, illuminate all Brahma Heavens, and hide myself, so that the Brahma Heavens and their retinues only hear my voice but do not see my form." Then the World-Honored One manifested the supernatural power of the image as it is, emitted extremely wonderful light, illuminated all Brahma Heavens, and hid himself, so that the Brahma Heavens and their retinues only heard his voice but did not see his form. Then the Brahma Heavens and their retinues each thought, "The Śramaṇa Gautama is truly extraordinary! He has great supernatural power, great majesty, great merit, and great spiritual power. Why is that? It is because he emits extremely wonderful light, illuminates all Brahma Heavens, and hides himself, so that we and our retinues only hear his voice but do not see his form."

于是，世尊而作是念：「我今宁可现如其像如意足，放极妙光明，照一切梵天而自隐住，使诸梵天及梵天眷属但闻我声而不见形。」于是，世尊即现如其像如意足，放极妙光明，照一切梵天，便自隐住，使诸梵天及梵天眷属但闻其声而不见其形。于是，梵天及梵天眷属各作是念：「沙门瞿昙甚奇！甚特！有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神。所以者何？谓放极妙光明，照一切梵天而自隐住，使我等及眷属但闻彼声而不见形。」

Then the World-Honored One again thought, "I have already converted this Brahma Heaven and its retinue. I should now withdraw the supernatural power." The World-Honored One then withdrew the supernatural power and returned to the Brahma Heaven.

于是，世尊复作是念：「我已化此梵天及梵天眷属，我今宁可摄如意足。」世尊便摄如意足，还住梵天中。

Then the Demon King also went there twice or thrice in the assembly. At that time, the Demon King said to the World-Honored One, "Great Sage, you see well, know well, and understand well. But do not instruct and admonish your disciples, do not teach the Dharma for your disciples, do not be attached to your disciples; do not be attached to your disciples, and when your body perishes and your life ends, you will be reborn in other inferior and lowly celestial realms of music. Practice the formless, and attain peace and happiness in this present life. Why is that? Great Sage, this is just self-affliction. Great Sage, in the past, there were śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who instructed and admonished their disciples, taught the Dharma for their disciples, and were attached to their disciples. Because of being attached to their disciples, when their bodies perished and their lives ended, they were reborn in other inferior and lowly celestial realms of music. Great Sage, therefore I tell you, do not instruct and admonish your disciples, do not teach the Dharma for your disciples, do not be attached to your disciples; do not be attached to your disciples, and when your body perishes and your life ends, you will be reborn in other inferior and lowly celestial realms of music. Practice the formless, and attain peace and happiness in this present life. Why is that? Great Sage, you are just afflicting yourself in vain."

于是，魔王亦至再三在彼众中。尔时，魔王白世尊曰：「大仙人善见、善知、善达，然莫训诲教呵弟子，亦莫为弟子说法，莫著弟子；莫为著弟子故，身坏命终，生余下贱妓乐神中。行无为，于现世受安乐。所以者何？大仙人！此唐自烦劳。大仙人！昔有沙门、梵志训诲弟子，教呵弟子，亦为弟子说法，乐著弟子；彼以著弟子故，身坏命终，生余下贱妓乐神中。大仙人！是故我语汝，莫得训诲教呵弟子，亦莫为弟子说法，莫著弟子；莫为著弟子故，身坏命终，生余下贱妓乐神中。行无为，于现世受安乐。所以者何？大仙人！汝唐自烦劳。」

Then the World-Honored One said, "Demon King Pāpīyas, you speak to me not for the sake of truth, not for the sake of benefit, not for the sake of happiness, not for the sake of peace: 'Do not instruct and admonish your disciples, do not teach the Dharma for your disciples, do not be attached to your disciples, do not be attached to your disciples, and when your body perishes and your life ends, you will be reborn in other inferior and lowly celestial realms of music. Practice the formless, and attain peace and happiness in this present life. Why is that? Great Sage, you are just afflicting yourself in vain.' Demon King Pāpīyas, you think: 'This Śramaṇa Gautama teaches the Dharma for his disciples, and when his disciples hear the Dharma, they will leave my realm.' Demon King Pāpīyas, therefore you now speak to me: 'Do not instruct and admonish your disciples, do not teach the Dharma for your disciples, do not be attached to your disciples; do not be attached to your disciples, and when your body perishes and your life ends, you will be reborn in other inferior and lowly celestial realms of music. Practice the formless, and attain peace and happiness in this present life. Why is that? Great Sage, you are just afflicting yourself in vain.'

于是，世尊告曰：「魔波旬！汝不为我求义故说，非为饶益故，非为乐故，非为安隐故：『莫得训诲教呵弟子，莫为弟子说法，莫著弟子，莫为著弟子故，身坏命终，生余下贱妓乐神中。行无为，于现世受安乐。所以者何？大仙人！汝唐自烦劳。』魔波旬！汝作是念：『此沙门瞿昙为弟子说法，彼弟子闻法已，出我境界。』魔波旬！是故汝今语我：『莫得训诲教呵弟子，亦莫为弟子说法，莫著弟子；莫为著弟子故，身坏命终，生余下贱妓乐神中。行无为，于现世受安乐。所以者何？大仙人！汝唐自烦劳。』

"Demon King Pāpīyas, if there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who instruct and admonish their disciples, teach the Dharma for their disciples, and are attached to their disciples; because of being attached to their disciples, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will be reborn in other inferior and lowly celestial realms of music. Those śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas are not true śramaṇas, they claim to be śramaṇas, not true brāhmaṇas, they claim to be brāhmaṇas, not true arhats, they claim to be arhats, not true perfect buddhas, they claim to be perfect buddhas. Demon King Pāpīyas, I am truly a śramaṇa, I claim to be a śramaṇa, truly a brāhmaṇa, I claim to be a brāhmaṇa, truly an arhat, I claim to be an arhat, truly a perfect buddha, I claim to be a perfect buddha. Demon King Pāpīyas, whether I teach the Dharma for my disciples or not, you may go now. I myself know when it is appropriate to teach the Dharma for my disciples and when it is not appropriate to teach the Dharma for my disciples."

「魔波旬！若有沙门、梵志训诲弟子，教呵弟子，为弟子说法，乐著弟子；为著弟子故，身坏命终，生余下贱妓乐神中。彼沙门、梵志，彼非沙门称说沙门，非梵志称说梵志，非阿罗诃称说阿罗诃，非等正觉称说等正觉。魔波旬！我实沙门称说沙门，实梵志称说梵志，实阿罗诃称说阿罗诃，实等正觉称说等正觉。魔波旬！若我为弟子说法，若不说者，汝且自去，我今自知应为弟子说法、不应为弟子说法。」

This is the request of Brahmā Heaven, and the Demon King Pāpīyas goes against the World-Honored One and speaks in accordance with it. Therefore, this sūtra is named "Requested by Brahmā Heaven."

是为梵天请，魔波旬违逆世尊随顺说，是故此经名梵天请佛。

The Buddha spoke thus. Brahmā Heaven and the retinue of Brahmā Heaven, upon hearing the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。梵天及梵天眷属，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
