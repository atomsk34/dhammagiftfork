MĀ 79

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 7 长寿王品

79. （七九）有胜天经

Parallel to: MN 127

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

Then the householder Xianyu instructed a servant, saying, "Go to the Buddha and bow on my behalf at the feet of the World-Honored One. Inquire after the World-Honored One's well-being, health, strength, and ease of rising. Say this: 'The householder Xianyu bows at the feet of the Buddha and inquires after the World-Honored One's well-being, health, strength, and ease of rising.' After you have inquired on my behalf, go to Venerable Aniruddha and bow at his feet. Inquire after his well-being, health, strength, and ease of rising. Say this: 'The householder Xianyu bows at the feet of Venerable Aniruddha and inquires after his well-being, health, strength, and ease of rising.' The householder Xianyu invites Venerable Aniruddha and four others to a meal tomorrow. If he accepts the invitation, say this: 'Venerable Aniruddha, the householder Xianyu has many affairs and duties as a minister of the king. I only hope that Venerable Aniruddha, out of compassion, will come to the householder Xianyu's residence tomorrow morning with four others.'"

于是，仙余财主告一使人：「汝往诣佛，为我稽首，礼世尊足，问讯世尊圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常耶？作如是语：『仙余财主稽首佛足，问讯世尊圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常耶？』汝既为我问讯佛已，往诣尊者阿那律陀所，为我稽首礼彼足已，问讯尊者圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常不？作如是语：『仙余财主稽首尊者阿那律陀足，问讯尊者圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常不？仙余财主请尊者阿那律陀四人俱，供明日食。』若受请者，复作是语：『尊者阿那律陀！仙余财主多事多为，为王众事断理臣佐，唯愿尊者阿那律陀为慈愍故，与四人俱，明日早来至仙余财主家。』」

Then the servant, having received the householder Xianyu's instructions, went to where the Buddha was, bowed at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said, "World-Honored One, the householder Xianyu bows at the Buddha's feet and inquires after the World-Honored One's well-being, health, strength, and ease of rising."

于是，使人受仙余财主教已，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，却住一面，白曰：「世尊！仙余财主稽首佛足，问讯世尊圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常耶？」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the servant, "May the householder Xianyu be at peace and happy, and may the gods, humans, asuras, gandharvas, rakshasas, and other various beings be at peace and happy."

尔时，世尊告使人曰：「令仙余财主安隐快乐，令天及人、阿修罗、揵塔和、罗刹及余种种身安隐快乐。」

Then the servant, having heard the Buddha's words, accepted them well and upheld them. He bowed at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and departed. He went to where Venerable Aniruddha was, bowed at his feet, sat to one side, and said, "Venerable Aniruddha, the householder Xianyu bows at Venerable Aniruddha's feet and inquires after the Venerable's well-being, health, strength, and ease of rising. The householder Xianyu invites Venerable Aniruddha and four others to a meal tomorrow."

于是，使人闻佛所说，善受善持，稽首佛足，绕三匝而去，往诣尊者阿那律陀所，稽首礼足，却坐一面，白曰：「尊者阿那律陀！仙余财主稽首尊者阿那律陀足，问讯尊者圣体康强，安快无病，起居轻便，气力如常不？仙余财主请尊者阿那律陀四人俱，供明日食。」

At that time, Venerable Kaccana was not far from Venerable Aniruddha, also in meditation. Then Venerable Aniruddha said, "Venerable Kaccana, what I said earlier about entering the city of Savatthi for alms tomorrow is exactly this. Now the householder Xianyu has sent someone to invite the four of us to a meal tomorrow."

是时，尊者真迦旃延去尊者阿那律陀不远而燕坐也。于是，尊者阿那律陀告曰：「贤者迦旃延！我向所道，明日我等为乞食故，入舍卫国，正谓此也。今仙余财主遣人请我等四人，供明日食。」

Venerable Kaccana immediately said, "May Venerable Aniruddha accept the invitation for the sake of that person. We will leave this dark forest tomorrow and enter the city of Savatthi for alms." Venerable Aniruddha accepted the invitation for the sake of that person.

尊者真迦旃延即时白曰：「愿尊者阿那律陀为彼人故，默然受请。我等明日出此暗林，为乞食故，入舍卫国。」尊者阿那律陀为彼人故，默然而受。

Then the messenger, knowing that Venerable Aniruddha had accepted in silence, again said, "The householder Xianyu tells Venerable Aniruddha that he has many affairs and is busy with the king's affairs. He hopes Venerable Aniruddha will come to his house early tomorrow with the four of them out of compassion."

于是，使人知尊者阿那律陀默然受已，寻复白曰：「仙余财主白尊者阿那律陀！仙余财主多事多为，为王众事断理臣佐，愿尊者阿那律陀为慈愍故，与四人俱，明日早来，至仙余财主家。」

Venerable Aniruddha told the messenger, "You may return. I will know the time myself." Then the messenger rose from his seat, bowed his head, circumambulated three times, and left.

尊者阿那律陀告使人曰：「汝便还去，我自知时。」于是，使人即从坐起，稽首作礼，绕三匝而去。

Then Venerable Aniruddha, having passed the night, put on his robe, took his bowl, and went with the four of them to the house of the householder Xianyu. At that time, the householder Xianyu was surrounded by his concubines, standing at the middle gate, waiting for Venerable Aniruddha. The householder Xianyu saw Venerable Aniruddha coming from afar, and upon seeing him, joined his palms towards Venerable Aniruddha and praised, "Welcome, Venerable Aniruddha! It has been a long time since Venerable Aniruddha came here." Then the householder Xianyu respectfully embraced Venerable Aniruddha and led him into the house. He prepared a good seat and invited him to sit.

于是，尊者阿那律陀过夜平旦，著衣持钵，四人共俱，往诣仙余财主家。尔时，仙余财主婇女围遶，住中门下，待尊者阿那律陀。仙余财主遥见尊者阿那律陀来，见已，叉手向尊者阿那律陀，赞曰：「善来尊者阿那律陀！尊者阿那律陀久不来此。」于是，仙余财主敬心扶抱尊者阿那律陀，将入家中，为敷好床，请使令坐。

Venerable Aniruddha then sat on the bed. The householder Xianyu bowed his head at Venerable Aniruddha's feet and sat to one side. Having sat down, he said, "Venerable Aniruddha, I wish to ask something. Please allow me to speak."

尊者阿那律陀即坐其床，仙余财主稽首尊者阿那律陀足，却坐一面，坐已，白曰：「尊者阿那律陀！欲有所问，唯愿见听。」

Venerable Aniruddha said, "Householder, ask as you wish. I will think about it when I hear it."

尊者阿那律陀告曰：「财主！随汝所问，闻已当思。」

The householder Xianyu then asked Venerable Aniruddha, "There are some śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who come to me and say, 'Householder, you should cultivate the liberation of the great mind.' Venerable Aniruddha, there are also some śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who come to me and say, 'Householder, you should cultivate the liberation of the immeasurable mind.' Venerable Aniruddha, are these two liberations, the liberation of the great mind and the liberation of the immeasurable mind, different in words and different in meaning, or are they different in words but the same in meaning?"

仙余财主便问尊者阿那律陀：「或有沙门、梵志来至我所，语我：『财主！汝当修大心解脱。』尊者阿那律陀！复有沙门、梵志来至我所，语我：『财主！汝当修无量心解脱。』尊者阿那律陀！大心解脱、无量心解脱，此二解脱，为文异义异耶？为一义文异耶？」

Venerable Aniruddha said, "Householder, you asked this question before, and you answered it yourself first. I will answer it later."

尊者阿那律陀告曰：「财主！汝前问此事，汝先自答，我当后答。」

The householder Xianyu said, "Venerable Aniruddha, the liberation of the great mind and the liberation of the immeasurable mind, these two liberations are the same in meaning but different in words. The householder Xianyu cannot answer this."

仙余财主白曰：「尊者阿那律陀！大心解脱、无量心解脱，此二解脱一义文异，仙余财主不能答此事。」

Venerable Aniruddha said, "Householder, listen to me as I explain the liberation of the great mind and the liberation of the immeasurable mind. As for the liberation of the great mind, if a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa is in a secluded place, or goes to the foot of a tree or an empty and quiet place, relying on one tree, they understand the liberation of the great mind and dwell in it, pervading it completely. That is the limit of their mind liberation, no more than that. If they do not rely on one tree, they should rely on two or three trees, and understand the liberation of the great mind, dwelling in it, pervading it completely. That is the limit of their mind liberation, no more than that. If they do not rely on two or three trees, they should rely on one grove. If they do not rely on one grove, they should rely on two or three groves. If they do not rely on two or three groves, they should rely on one village. If they do not rely on one village, they should rely on two or three villages. If they do not rely on two or three villages, they should rely on one country. If they do not rely on one country, they should rely on two or three countries. If they do not rely on two or three countries, they should rely on this great earth up to the great ocean. They understand the liberation of the great mind and dwell in it, pervading it completely. That is the limit of their mind liberation, no more than that. This is called the liberation of the great mind.

尊者阿那律陀告曰：「财主！当听我为汝说大心解脱、无量心解脱。大心解脱者，若有沙门、梵志在无事处，或至树下空安静处，依一树意解大心解脱遍满成就游，彼齐限是心解脱不过是。若不依一树者，当依二三树，意解大心解脱遍满成就游，彼齐限是心解脱不过是。若不依二三树者，当依一林，若不依一林者，当依二三林，若不依二三林者，当依一村，若不依一村者，当依二三村，若不依二三村者，当依一国，若不依一国者，当依二三国，若不依二三国者，当依此大地乃至大海。意解大心解脱遍满成就游，彼齐限是心解脱不过是，是谓大心解脱。

"Householder, what is the liberation of the immeasurable mind? If a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa is in a secluded place, or goes to the foot of a tree or an empty and quiet place, their mind is filled with loving-kindness, pervading one direction completely and dwelling in it. Thus, in the second, third, and fourth directions, in the four intermediate directions, above and below, their mind is filled with loving-kindness, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without conflict, extremely vast, very great, immeasurable, well cultivated, pervading the entire world completely and dwelling in it. Thus, with compassion and joy, their mind is filled with equanimity, without knots, without resentment, without anger, without conflict, extremely vast, very great, immeasurable, well cultivated, pervading the entire world completely and dwelling in it. This is called the liberation of the immeasurable mind. Householder, the liberation of the great mind and the liberation of the immeasurable mind, are these two liberations different in meaning and different in words, or are they the same in meaning but different in words?"

「财主！云何无量心解脱？若有沙门、梵志在无事处，或至树下空安静处，心与慈俱，遍满一方成就游，如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心与慈俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修遍满一切世间成就游；如是悲、喜心与舍俱，无结无怨，无恚无诤，极广甚大，无量善修遍满一切世间成就游，是谓无量心解脱。财主！大心解脱，无量心解脱，此二解脱为义异文异，为一义文异耶？」

The householder Xianyu said to Venerable Aniruddha, "As I understand it from the Venerable, the meaning of these two liberations is different and the words are also different."

仙余财主白尊者阿那律陀曰：「如我从尊者闻，则解其义，此二解脱义既异文亦异。」

Venerable Aniruddha said, "Householder, there are three kinds of gods: the Light Gods, the Pure Light Gods, and the All-Pervading Pure Light Gods. Among them, the Light Gods, when born in one place, do not think, 'This is mine, that is mine.' But the Light Gods go wherever they want and are happy there. Householder, it is like a fly on a piece of meat, not thinking, 'This is mine, that is mine.' But the fly goes wherever the meat goes and is happy there. In the same way, the Light Gods do not think, 'This is mine, that is mine.' But the Light Gods go wherever they want and are happy there. Sometimes the Light Gods gather in one place, although their bodies are different, their light is not different. Householder, it is like someone lighting countless lamps in a room. Although the lamps are different, their light is not different. In the same way, the Light Gods gather in one place, although their bodies are different, their light is not different. Sometimes the Light Gods disperse. When they disperse, their bodies are different and their light is also different. Householder, it is like someone taking many lamps from a room and placing them in different rooms. The lamps are then different and their light is also different. In the same way, the Light Gods disperse. When they disperse, their bodies are different and their light is also different."

尊者阿那律陀告曰：「财主！有三种天，光天、净光天、遍净光天。于中光天者，彼生在一处，不作是念：『此我所有，彼我所有。』但光天随其所往，即乐彼中。财主！犹如蝇在肉段，不作是念：『此我所有，彼我所有。』但蝇随肉段去，即乐彼中。如是彼光天不作是念：『此我所有，彼我所有。』但光天随其所往，即乐彼中。有时光天集在一处，虽身有异而光不异。财主！犹如有人然无量灯，著一室中，彼灯虽异而光不异。如是彼光天集在一处，虽身有异而光不异。有时光天各自散去，彼] cf.［丽】各散去时，其身既异，光明亦异。财主！犹如有人从一室中出众多灯，分著诸室，彼灯即异，光明亦异。如是彼光天各自散去，彼各散去时，其身既异，光明亦异。」

Then Venerable Kaccana the Great said, "Venerable Aniruddha, can one know whether the Light Gods are superior or inferior, or whether they are good or bad, when they are born in one place?"

于是，尊者真迦旃延白曰：「尊者阿那律陀！彼光天生在一处，可知有胜如、妙不妙耶？」

Venerable Aniruddha replied, "Venerable Kaccana the Great, one can say that when the Light Gods are born in one place, one can know whether they are superior or inferior, or whether they are good or bad."

尊者阿那律陀答曰：「贤者迦旃延！可说彼光天生在一处，知有胜如、妙与不妙。」

Venerable Kaccana the Great further asked, "Venerable Aniruddha, when the Light Gods are born in one place, what causes and conditions can one know whether they are superior or inferior, or whether they are good or bad?"

尊者真迦旃延复问曰：「尊者阿那律陀！彼光天生在一处，何因何缘知有胜如、妙与不妙耶？」

Venerable Aniruddha replied, "Venerable Kaccana the Great, if a monk or brahman goes to a secluded place, or to the foot of a tree or an empty and quiet place, and with a single tree in mind, he achieves and abides in the perception of light, with his mind fully immersed in the perception of light. That is the limit of his mind's liberation, no further. If he does not rely on a single tree, but on two or three trees, with his mind he achieves and abides in the perception of light, with his mind fully immersed in the perception of light. That is the limit of his mind's liberation, no further. Venerable Kaccana the Great, between these two kinds of mind liberation, which one is superior, excellent, sublime, and supreme?"

尊者阿那律陀答曰：「贤者迦旃延。若有沙门、梵志在无事处，或至树下空安静处，依一树，意解作光明想成就游，心作光明想极盛，彼齐限是心解脱不过是。若不依一树者，或依二三树，意解作光明想成就游，心作光明想极盛，彼齐限是心解脱不过是。贤者迦旃延！此二心解脱，何解脱为上、为胜、为妙、为最耶？」

Venerable Kaccana the Great replied, "Venerable Aniruddha, if a monk or brahman does not rely on a single tree, but on two or three trees, with his mind he achieves and abides in the perception of light, with his mind fully immersed in the perception of light. That is the limit of his mind's liberation, no further. Venerable Aniruddha, between these two kinds of mind liberation, this one is superior, excellent, sublime, and supreme."

尊者真迦旃延答曰：「尊者阿那律陀！若有沙门、梵志不依一树者，或依二三树，意解作光明想成就游，心作光明想极盛，彼齐限是心解脱不过是。尊者阿那律陀！二解脱中，此解脱为上、为胜、为妙、为最。」

Venerable Aniruddha further asked, "Venerable Kaccana the Great, if one does not rely on two or three trees, but on a single grove... if one does not rely on a single grove, but on two or three groves... if one does not rely on two or three groves, but on a single village... if one does not rely on a single village, but on two or three villages... if one does not rely on two or three villages, but on a single country... if one does not rely on a single country, but on two or three countries... if one does not rely on two or three countries, but on this great earth up to the great ocean. With the mind, one achieves and abides in the perception of light, with the mind fully immersed in the perception of light. That is the limit of his mind's liberation, no further. Venerable Kaccana the Great, between these two kinds of mind liberation, which one is superior, excellent, sublime, and supreme?"

尊者阿那律陀复问曰：「贤者迦旃延！若不依二三树者，或依一林，若不依一林者，或依二三林，若不依二三林者，或依一村，若不依一村者，或依二三村，若不依二三村者，或依一国，若不依一国者，或依二三国，若不依二三国者，或依此大地乃至大海。意解作光明想成就游，心作光明想极盛，彼齐限是心解脱不过是。贤者迦旃延！此二解脱，何解脱为上、为胜、为妙、为最？」

Venerable Kaccana the Great replied, "Venerable Aniruddha, if a monk or brahman does not rely on two or three trees, but on a single grove... if one does not rely on a single grove, but on two or three groves... if one does not rely on two or three groves, but on a single village... if one does not rely on a single village, but on two or three villages... if one does not rely on two or three villages, but on a single country... if one does not rely on a single country, but on two or three countries... if one does not rely on two or three countries, but on this great earth up to the great ocean. With the mind, one achieves and abides in the perception of light, with the mind fully immersed in the perception of light. That is the limit of his mind's liberation, no further. Venerable Aniruddha, between these two kinds of mind liberation, this one is superior, excellent, sublime, and supreme."

尊者真迦旃延答曰：「尊者阿那律陀！若有沙门、梵志不依二三树者，或依一林，若不依一林者，或依二三林，若不依二三林者，或依一村，若不依一村者，或依二三村，若不依二三村者，或依一国，若不依一国者，或依二三国，若不依二三国者，或依此大地乃至大海。意解作光明想成就游，心作光明想极盛，彼齐限是心解脱不过是。尊者阿那律陀！二解脱中此解脱为上、为胜、为妙、为最。」

Venerable Aniruddha said, "Kaccana the Great, because of this and that, the gods of light are born in one place, knowing there are those who are superior, equal, and inferior. Why is that? Because of the superiority of the minds of people, their practice becomes refined or coarse. Because of the refined or coarse practice, people attain superiority. Venerable Kaccana the Great, the World-Honored One also said that people have superiority."

尊者阿那律陀告曰：「迦旃延！因是缘是，彼光天生在一处，知有胜如、妙与不妙。所以者何？因人心胜如故，修便有精麤，因修有精麤故，得人则有胜如。贤者迦旃延！世尊亦如是说，人有胜如。」

Venerable Kaccana the Great asked again, "Venerable Aniruddha, can the gods of pure light, born in one place, know there are those who are superior, equal, and inferior?"

尊者真迦旃延复问曰：「尊者阿那律陀！彼净光天生在一处，可知有胜如、妙与不妙耶？」

Venerable Aniruddha replied, "Venerable Kaccana the Great, it can be said that the gods of pure light, born in one place, know there are those who are superior, equal, and inferior."

尊者阿那律陀答曰：「贤者迦旃延！可说彼净光天生在一处，知有胜如、妙与不妙。」

Venerable Kaccana the Great asked again, "Venerable Aniruddha, how do the gods of pure light, born in one place, know there are those who are superior, equal, and inferior?"

尊者真迦旃延复问曰：「尊者阿那律陀！彼净光天生在一处，何因何缘，知有胜如、妙与不妙耶？」

Venerable Aniruddha replied, "Venerable Kaccana the Great, if there are ascetics and brahmins who, in a secluded place or at the foot of a tree, attain the concentration of the pure light gods and abide in it, but do not cultivate, practice, or extensively cultivate it, and do not fully accomplish it. When their bodies break up and their lives end, they are reborn among the pure light gods. But when they are born there, they do not attain complete tranquility, complete peace, or the end of their lives. Venerable Kaccana the Great, it is like blue, red, and white lotuses, which grow in water and are born and grow in water. When they are under the water, their roots, stems, leaves, and flowers are all soaked in water, sprinkled with water, and moistened by water, without any part not soaked. Venerable Kaccana the Great, if there are ascetics and brahmins who, in a secluded place or at the foot of a tree, attain the concentration of the pure light gods and abide in it, but do not cultivate, practice, or extensively cultivate it, and do not fully accomplish it. When their bodies break up and their lives end, they are reborn among the pure light gods. But when they are born there, they do not attain complete tranquility, complete peace, or the end of their lives.

尊者阿那律陀答曰：「贤者迦旃延！若有沙门、梵志在无事处，或至树下空安静处，意解净光天遍满成就游，彼此定不修、不习、不广，不极成就。彼于后时，身坏命终，生净光天中。彼生已，不得极止息，不得极寂静，亦不得尽寿讫。贤者迦旃延！犹青莲华，红、赤、白莲，水生水长，在水底时，尔时根、茎、叶、华，彼一切水渍水浇，水所润，无处不渍。贤者迦旃延！若有沙门、梵志在无事处，或至树下空安静处，意解净光天遍满成就游，彼此定不修、不习、不广，不极成就，彼身坏命终，生净光天中。彼生已，不得极止息，不得极寂静，亦不得尽寿讫。

Venerable Kaccana the Great, there are also ascetics and brahmins who, in a secluded place or at the foot of a tree, attain the concentration of the pure light gods and abide in it, and cultivate, practice, and extensively cultivate it, and fully accomplish it. When their bodies break up and their lives end, they are reborn among the pure light gods. When they are born there, they attain complete tranquility, complete peace, and the end of their lives. Venerable Kaccana the Great, it is like blue, red, and white lotuses, which grow in water and are born and grow in water. When they emerge from the water and stand above the water, they are not soaked by the water. Venerable Kaccana the Great, there are also ascetics and brahmins who, in a secluded place or at the foot of a tree, attain the concentration of the pure light gods and abide in it, and cultivate, practice, and extensively cultivate it, and fully accomplish it. When their bodies break up and their lives end, they are reborn among the pure light gods. When they are born there, they attain complete tranquility, complete peace, and the end of their lives.

「贤者迦旃延！复有沙门、梵志意解净光天遍满成就游，彼此定数修、数习、数广，极成就，彼身坏命终，生净光天中。彼生已，得极止息，得极寂静，亦得寿尽讫。贤者迦旃延！犹青莲华，红、赤、白莲，水生水长，出水上住，水所不渍。贤者迦旃延！如是复有沙门、梵志在无事处，或至树下空安静处，意解净光天遍满成就游，彼此定数修、数习、数广，极成就，彼身坏命终，生净光天中。彼生已，得极止息，得极寂静，亦得寿尽讫。

Venerable Kaccana the Great, for this reason and condition, the pure light gods are born in one place, knowing there are those who are superior, equal, and inferior. Why is that? Because of the superiority of the minds of people, there are differences in cultivation. Because of the differences in cultivation, there are differences in the people who attain it. Venerable Kaccana the Great, the World-Honored One also said that there are differences among people.

「贤者迦旃延！因是缘是，彼净光天生在一处，知有胜如、妙与不妙。所以者何？因人心胜如故，修便有精麤，因修有精麤故，得人则有胜如。贤者迦旃延！世尊亦如是说，人有胜如。」

Venerable Kaccana the Great again asked, "Venerable Anuruddha, are the pure light gods born in one place, knowing there are those who are superior, equal, and inferior?"

尊者真迦旃延复问曰：「尊者阿那律陀！彼遍净光天生在一处，可知有胜如、妙与不妙耶？」

Venerable Anuruddha replied, "Venerable Kaccana the Great, it can be said that the pure light gods are born in one place, knowing there are those who are superior, equal, and inferior."

尊者阿那律陀答曰：「贤者迦旃延！可说彼遍净光天生在一处，知有胜如、妙与不妙。」

Venerable Kaccana the Great again asked, "Venerable Anuruddha, how do the pure light gods, born in one place, know there are those who are superior, equal, and inferior?"

尊者真迦旃延复问曰：「尊者阿那律陀！彼遍净光天生在一处，何因何缘知有胜如、妙与不妙耶？」

Venerable Anuruddha replied, "Venerable Kaccana the Great, if there are ascetics and brahmins who, in a secluded place or under a tree in an empty, quiet place, attain the liberation of mind and liberation by wisdom, and dwell pervading the pure light gods with a mind imbued with equanimity, but they do not fully restrain sleep, and do not skillfully regulate their breathing, then when their body breaks up and their life ends, they will be reborn among the pure light gods. When they are born there, their light will not be fully pure. Venerable Kaccana the Great, it is like a burning lamp. If the oil is impure and the wick is not clean, then the light that arises will not be fully pure. Venerable Kaccana the Great, in the same way, if there are ascetics and brahmins who, in a secluded place or under a tree in an empty, quiet place, attain the liberation of mind and liberation by wisdom, and dwell pervading the pure light gods with a mind imbued with equanimity, but they do not fully restrain sleep, and do not skillfully regulate their breathing, then when their body breaks up and their life ends, they will be reborn among the pure light gods. When they are born there, their light will not be fully pure.

尊者阿那律陀答曰：「贤者迦旃延！若有沙门、梵志在无事处，或至树下空安静处，意解遍净光天遍满成就游。彼不极止睡眠，不善息调悔，彼于后时身坏命终，生遍净光天中。彼生已，光不极净。贤者迦旃延！譬如燃灯，因缘油炷，若油有滓，炷复不净，因是灯光生不明净。贤者迦旃延！如是若有沙门、梵志在无事处，或至树下空安静处，意解遍净光天遍满成就游，彼不极止睡眠，不善息调悔，彼身坏命终，生遍净光天中。彼生已，光不极净。

Venerable Kaccana the Great, if there are ascetics and brahmins who, in a secluded place or under a tree in an empty, quiet place, attain the liberation of mind and liberation by wisdom, and dwell pervading the pure light gods with a mind imbued with equanimity, and they fully restrain sleep, and skillfully regulate their breathing, then when their body breaks up and their life ends, they will be reborn among the pure light gods. When they are born there, their light will be fully pure. Venerable Kaccana the Great, it is like a burning lamp. If the oil is free from impurities and the wick is very clean, then the light that arises will be fully pure.

「贤者迦旃延！复有沙门、梵志在无事处，或至树下空安静处，意解遍净光天遍满成就游。彼极止睡眠，善息调悔，彼身坏命终，生遍净光天中。彼生已，光极明净。贤者迦旃延！譬如然灯，因缘油炷，若油无滓，炷复极净，因是灯光生极明净。

Venerable Kaccana the Great, in the same way, if there are ascetics and brahmins who, in a secluded place or under a tree in an empty, quiet place, attain the liberation of mind and liberation by wisdom, and dwell pervading the pure light gods with a mind imbued with equanimity, and they fully restrain sleep, and skillfully regulate their breathing, then when their body breaks up and their life ends, they will be reborn among the pure light gods. When they are born there, their light will be fully pure.

「贤者迦旃延！如是复有沙门、梵志在无事处，或至树下空安静处，意解遍净光天遍满成就游。彼极止睡眠，善息调悔，彼身坏命终，生遍净光天中。彼生已，光极明净。

Venerable Kaccana the Great, for this reason and condition, the pure light gods are born in one place, knowing there are those who are superior, equal, and inferior. Why is that? Because of the superiority of the minds of people, their practice has distinctions of refinement and coarseness. Because of the distinctions of refinement and coarseness in practice, people have distinctions of superiority. Venerable Kaccana the Great, the World-Honored One also said in the same way that there are distinctions of superiority among people.

「贤者迦旃延！因是缘是，彼遍净光天生在一处，知有胜如、妙与不妙。所以者何？因人心胜如故，修便有精麤，因修有精麤故，得人则有胜如。贤者迦旃延！世尊亦如是说，人有胜如。」

Then Venerable Kaccana the Great praised Xianyu the householder, saying: "Excellent, excellent! Householder, you have greatly benefited us. Why is that? When I first asked Venerable Anuruddha about the superior gods, we had never heard such a teaching from Venerable Anuruddha before. That is the god, there is that god, and that is that god.

于是，尊者真迦旃延叹仙余财主曰：「善哉！善哉！财主！汝为我等多所饶益。所以者何？初问尊者阿那律陀有胜天，我等未曾从尊者阿那律陀闻如是义，是谓彼天、有彼天、如是彼天。」

Then Venerable Anuruddha said: "Venerable Kaccana the Great, there are many such gods, namely the sun and moon, who have such great supernatural power, great majesty, great merit, and great spiritual power, outshining the light of others. They gather with me, exchange greetings, discuss, and respond to each other. However, I do not say, 'That is the god, there is that god, and that is that god.'"

于是，尊者阿那律陀告曰：「贤者迦旃延！多有彼天，谓此日月如是有大如意足，有大威德，有大福祐，有大威神，以光不及光，彼与我集，共相慰劳，有所论说，有所答对，然我不如是说，是谓彼天、有彼天、如是彼天。」

Then Xianyu the householder, knowing that the Venerable had finished speaking, rose from his seat, served water for washing, and with his own hands served various abundant and delicious food and drink until he was satisfied. After the meal, he took a small seat, sat separately, and listened to the Dharma. When Xianyu the householder was seated, Venerable Anuruddha spoke the Dharma for him, inspiring him with yearning, bringing about joy. Having used immeasurable skillful means to speak the Dharma for him, inspiring him with yearning, and bringing about joy, he rose from his seat and departed.

尔时，仙余财主知彼尊者所说已讫，即从坐起，自行澡水，以极净美种种丰饶食噉含消，手自斟酌，令得饱满。食讫举器，行澡水已，取一小床，别坐听法。仙余财主坐已，尊者阿那律陀而为说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，从坐起去。

The Venerable Anuruddha spoke thus. Xianyu the householder and the monks, having heard what the Venerable Anuruddha said, were delighted and carried it out.

尊者阿那律陀所说如是。仙余财主及诸比丘，闻尊者阿那律陀所说，欢喜奉行。
