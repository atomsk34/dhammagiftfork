MĀ 8

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 1 七法品

8. The Seven Suns （八）七日经（初一日诵）

Parallel to: AN 7.66 / EĀ 40.1

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in Vesali, staying in the Ambapali Grove.

一时，佛游鞞舍离，在［木＊奈］氏树园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus: "All conditioned phenomena are impermanent, not lasting, quickly changing, and unreliable. Such conditioned phenomena should not be clung to with delight, but should be seen as afflictions, to be sought to be abandoned, and to be sought to be liberated from. Why is this? When there is no rain, all the trees, grains, and medicinal plants will wither, break, and perish, and cannot remain permanent. Therefore, all conditioned phenomena are impermanent, not lasting, quickly changing, and unreliable. Such conditioned phenomena should not be clung to with delight, but should be seen as afflictions, to be sought to be abandoned, and to be sought to be liberated from.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「一切行无常，不久住法、速变易法、不可猗法，如是诸行不当乐著，当患厌之，当求舍离，当求解脱。所以者何？有时不雨，当不雨时，一切诸树、百谷、药木皆悉枯槁，摧碎灭尽，不得常住。是故一切行无常，不久住法、速变易法、不可猗法，如是诸行不当乐著，当患厌之，当求舍离，当求解脱。

Furthermore, when two suns appear in the world, at that time all the ditches, streams, and rivers will dry up and cannot remain permanent. Therefore, all conditioned phenomena are impermanent, not lasting, quickly changing, and unreliable. Such conditioned phenomena should not be clung to with delight, but should be seen as afflictions, to be sought to be abandoned, and to be sought to be liberated from.

「复次，有时二日出世，二日出时，诸沟渠川流皆悉竭尽，不得常住。是故一切行无常，不久住法、速变易法、不可猗法，如是诸行不当乐著，当患厌之，当求舍离，当求解脱。

Furthermore, when three suns appear in the world, at that time all the great rivers will dry up and cannot remain permanent. Therefore, all conditioned phenomena are impermanent, not lasting, quickly changing, and unreliable. Such conditioned phenomena should not be clung to with delight, but should be seen as afflictions, to be sought to be abandoned, and to be sought to be liberated from.

「复次，有时三日出世，三日出时，诸大江河皆悉竭尽，不得常住。是故一切行无常，不久住法、速变易法，不可猗法，如是诸行不当乐著，当患厌之，当求舍离，当求解脱。

Furthermore, when four suns appear in the world, at that time all the great springs that flow from the five rivers of Jambudvīpa - namely, the Ganges, the Yāmuna, the Sarabhū, the Airāvati, and the Mahī - will all dry up and cannot remain permanent. Therefore, all conditioned phenomena are impermanent, not lasting, quickly changing, and unreliable. Such conditioned phenomena should not be clung to with delight, but should be seen as afflictions, to be sought to be abandoned, and to be sought to be liberated from.

「复次，有时四日出世，四日出时，诸大泉源从阎浮洲五河所出：一曰恒伽，二曰摇尤那，三曰舍牢浮，四曰阿夷罗婆提，五曰摩企，彼大泉源皆悉竭尽，不得常住。是故一切行无常，不久住法、速变易法，不可猗法，如是诸行不当乐著，当患厌之，当求舍离，当求解脱。

Furthermore, when five suns appear in the world, at that time the water level of the great ocean will decrease by one hundred yojanas, and continue to decrease until it is seven hundred yojanas. When five suns appear, the remaining water in the great ocean will be seven hundred yojanas, and continue to decrease until it is one hundred yojanas. When five suns appear, the water level of the great ocean will decrease by one tāla tree, and continue to decrease until it is seven tāla trees. When five suns appear, the remaining water in the great ocean will be seven tāla trees, and continue to decrease until it is one tāla tree. When five suns appear, the water level of the great ocean will decrease by one person, and continue to decrease until it is seven people. When five suns appear, the remaining water in the great ocean will be seven people, and continue to decrease until it is one person. When five suns appear, the water level of the great ocean will decrease until it reaches the neck, shoulders, waist, hips, knees, and ankles, and sometimes the water in the great ocean will completely evaporate, not even enough to wet a finger. Therefore, all conditioned phenomena are impermanent, not lasting, quickly changing, and unreliable. Such conditioned phenomena should not be clung to with delight, but should be seen as afflictions, to be sought to be abandoned, and to be sought to be liberated from.

「复次，有时五日出世，五日出时，大海水减一百由延，转减乃至七百由延；五日出时，海水余有七百由延，转减乃至一百由延；五日出时，大海水减一多罗树，转减乃至七多罗树；五日出时，海水余有七多罗树，转减乃至一多罗树；五日出时，海水减一人，转减乃至七人；五日出时，海水余有七人，转减乃至一人；五日出时，海水减至颈、至肩、至腰、至［月＊奇］、至膝、至踝，有时海水消尽，不足没指。是故一切行无常，不久住法、速变易法、不可猗法，如是诸行不当乐著，当患厌之，当求舍离，当求解脱。

Furthermore, when six suns appear in the world, at that time all the great earth and Mount Sumeru will all emit smoke, combining into one smoke, like when a potter first starts a kiln, all emitting smoke, combining into one smoke. When six suns appear, all the great earth and Mount Sumeru will all emit smoke, combining into one smoke. Therefore, all conditioned phenomena are impermanent, not lasting, quickly changing, and unreliable. Such conditioned phenomena should not be clung to with delight, but should be seen as afflictions, to be sought to be abandoned, and to be sought to be liberated from.

「复次，有时六日出世，六日出时，一切大地须弥山王皆悉烟起，合为一烟，譬如陶师始爨灶时，皆悉烟起，合为一烟。如是六日出时，一切大地须弥山王皆悉烟起，合为一烟。是故一切行无常，不久住法、速变易法，不可猗法，如是诸行不当乐著，当患厌之，当求舍离，当求解脱。

Furthermore, when seven suns appear in the world, at that time all the great earth and Mount Sumeru will all burn and blaze together, combining into one great fire; when seven suns appear, all the great earth and Mount Sumeru will all burn and blaze together, combining into one great fire. The wind blows the fire, up to the Brahma Heaven. At that time, the newly born gods who are not familiar with the formation and destruction of the world, who have not seen the formation and destruction of the world, and who do not know the formation and destruction of the world, upon seeing the great fire, all become terrified with their hair standing on end, thinking: 'Does the fire not come here? Does the fire not come here?' The previously born gods who are familiar with the formation and destruction of the world, who have seen the formation and destruction of the world, and who know the formation and destruction of the world, upon seeing the great fire, console the gods, saying: 'Do not be afraid. The fire will stop there and will not come here.' When seven suns appear, Mount Sumeru will collapse and be destroyed, from one hundred yojanas to two hundred yojanas, three hundred yojanas, up to seven hundred yojanas, collapsing and being destroyed. When seven suns appear, Mount Sumeru and this great earth will be burned, destroyed, and extinguished, without any remaining ashes, like burning ghee or oil, being boiled and extinguished, without any remaining smoke or soot. In this way, when seven suns appear, Mount Sumeru and this great earth will be without any remaining ashes. Therefore, all conditioned phenomena are impermanent, not lasting, quickly changing, and unreliable. Such conditioned phenomena should not be clung to with delight, but should be seen as afflictions, to be sought to be abandoned, and to be sought to be liberated from.

「复次，有时七日出世，七日出时，一切大地须弥山王洞燃俱炽，合为一［火＊佥］；如是七日出时，一切大地须弥山王洞燃俱炽，合为一［火＊佥］，风吹火［火＊佥］，乃至梵天。是时，晃昱诸天始生天者，不谙世间成败，不见世间成败，不知世间成败，见大火已，皆恐怖毛竖而作是念：『火不来至此耶？火不来至此耶？』前生诸天谙世间成败，见世间成败，知世间成败，见大火已，慰劳诸天曰：『莫得恐怖，火法齐彼，终不至此。』七日出时，须弥山王百由延崩散坏灭尽，二百由延、三百由延，乃至七百由延崩散坏灭尽；七日出时，须弥山王及此大地烧坏消灭，无余栽烬，如燃酥油，煎熬消尽，无余烟墨；如是七日出时，须弥山王及此大地无余烖烬。是故一切行无常，不久住法、速变易法、不可猗法，如是诸行不当乐著，当患厌之，当求舍离，当求解脱。

I now tell you that Mount Sumeru will collapse and be completely destroyed. Who can believe this? Only those who have seen the truth. I now tell you that the great ocean water will dry up and be completely exhausted. Who can believe this? Only those who have seen the truth. I now tell you that the entire great earth will be burned and completely destroyed. Who can believe this? Only those who have seen the truth. Why is this? Bhikṣus! In the past, there was a great teacher named Sunetra, who was revered by non-Buddhist ascetics. He abandoned desire and attained the bases of supernatural power. Sunetra had countless hundreds of thousands of disciples. Sunetra taught the Dharma of the Brahma world to his disciples. When Sunetra taught the Dharma of the Brahma world, some of his disciples did not fully practice it. After their lives ended, they were reborn in the Heaven of the Four Heavenly Kings, the Heaven of the Thirty-three Gods, the Yama Heaven, the Tuṣita Heaven, the Nirmāṇarati Heaven, or the Paranirmitavaśavartin Heaven. When Sunetra taught the Dharma of the Brahma world, some of his disciples fully practiced it. They cultivated the four brahmavihāras and abandoned desire. After their lives ended, they were reborn in the Brahma Heaven. At that time, Sunetra thought: 'I should not go to the next world together with my disciples and be reborn in the same place. I should now cultivate supreme loving-kindness. After cultivating supreme loving-kindness, I will be reborn in the Ābhāsvara Heaven after my life ends.' At that time, Sunetra then cultivated supreme loving-kindness later. After cultivating supreme loving-kindness, he was reborn in the Ābhāsvara Heaven after his life ended. Sunetra and his disciples did not practice the path in vain, and attained great rewards.

「我今为汝说须弥山王当崩坏尽，谁有能信？唯见谛者耳，我今为汝说大海水当竭消尽，谁有能信？唯见谛者耳。我今为汝说一切大地当烧燃尽，谁有能信？唯见谛者耳。所以者何？比丘！昔有大师名曰善眼，为外道仙人之所师宗，舍离欲爱，得如意足。善眼大师有无量百千弟子，善眼大师为诸弟子说梵世法，若善眼大师为说梵世法时，诸弟子等有不具足奉行法者，彼命终已，或生四王天，或生三十三天，或生［火＊佥］摩天，或生兜率哆天，或生化乐天，或生他化乐天。若善眼大师为说梵世法时，诸弟子等设有具足奉行法者，彼修四梵室，舍离于欲，彼命终已，得生梵天。彼时善眼大师而作是念：『我不应与弟子等同俱至后世共生一处，我今宁可更修增上慈。修增上慈已，命终得生晃昱天中。』彼时善眼大师则于后时更修增上慈，修增上慈已，命终得生晃昱天中。善眼大师及诸弟子学道不虚，得大果报。

Bhikṣus! What do you think? Do you think that the great teacher Sunetra who was revered by non-Buddhist ascetics, abandoned desire, and attained the bases of supernatural power in the past was someone else? Do not think so. Know that it was I. At that time, I was named Sunetra, revered by non-Buddhist ascetics, abandoned desire, and attained the bases of supernatural power. At that time, I had countless hundreds of thousands of disciples. At that time, I taught the Dharma of the Brahma world to my disciples. When I taught the Dharma of the Brahma world, some of my disciples did not fully practice it. After their lives ended, they were reborn in the Heaven of the Four Heavenly Kings, the Heaven of the Thirty-three Gods, the Yama Heaven, the Tuṣita Heaven, the Nirmāṇarati Heaven, or the Paranirmitavaśavartin Heaven. When I taught the Dharma of the Brahma world, some of my disciples fully practiced it. They cultivated the four brahmavihāras and abandoned desire. After their lives ended, they were reborn in the Brahma Heaven. At that time, I thought: 'I should not go to the next world together with my disciples and be reborn in the same place. I should now cultivate supreme loving-kindness. After cultivating supreme loving-kindness, I will be reborn in the Ābhāsvara Heaven after my life ends.' Later, I cultivated supreme loving-kindness. After cultivating supreme loving-kindness, I was reborn in the Ābhāsvara Heaven after my life ended. At that time, I and my disciples practiced the path without being in vain, and attained great rewards. At that time, I personally practiced this path, for my own benefit, and also for the benefit of others, for the benefit of many people, out of compassion for the world, for the sake of gods and humans, seeking righteousness and benefit, seeking peace and happiness. At that time, my teaching did not reach the ultimate, did not reach the ultimate purity, did not reach the ultimate practice of purity, did not reach the ultimate completion of the practice of purity. At that time, I was not free from birth, old age, sickness, death, crying, and sorrow. I was not yet able to escape all suffering.

「诸比丘！于意云何？昔善眼大师为外道仙人之所师宗，舍离欲爱，得如意足者，汝谓异人耶？莫作斯念，当知即是我也。我于尔时名善眼大师，为外道仙人之所师宗，舍离欲爱，得如意足；我于尔时有无量百千弟子，我于尔时为诸弟子说梵世法。我说梵世法时，诸弟子等有不具足奉行法者，彼命终已，或生四王天，或生三十三天，或生［火＊佥］摩天，或生兜率哆天，或生化乐天，或生他化乐天。我说梵世法时，诸弟子等设有具足奉行法者，修四梵室，舍离于欲，彼命终已，得生梵天。我于尔时而作是念：『我不应与弟子等同俱至后世共生一处，我今宁可更修增上慈。修增上慈已，命终得生晃昱天中。』我于后时更修增上慈，修增上慈已，命终得生晃昱天中。我于尔时及诸弟子学道不虚，得大果报。我于尔时亲行斯道，为自饶益，亦饶益他，饶益多人，愍伤世间，为天、为人求义及饶益，求安隐快乐，尔时说法不至究竟，不究竟白净，不究竟梵行，不究竟梵行讫，尔时不离生、老、病、死、啼哭、忧戚，亦未能得脱一切苦。

Bhikṣus! I now appear in the world as a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, Perfect in Knowledge and Conduct, Well-Gone, Knower of the World, Unsurpassed, Tamer of Men, Teacher of Gods and Men, Buddha, Bhagavān. I now benefit myself, and also benefit others, benefit many people, have compassion for the world, for the sake of gods and humans, seeking righteousness and benefit, seeking peace and happiness. I now teach the Dharma that reaches the ultimate, the ultimate purity, the ultimate practice of purity; the ultimate completion of the practice of purity. I now am free from birth, old age, sickness, death, crying, and sorrow. I now have attained liberation from all suffering.

「比丘！我今出世，如来、无所著、等正觉、明行成为、善逝、世间解、无上士、道法御、天人师，号佛、众祐；我今自饶益，亦饶益他，饶益多人，愍伤世间，为天、为人求义及饶益，求安隐快乐；我今说法得至究竟，究竟白净，究竟梵行；究竟梵行讫，我今已离生、老、病、死、啼哭、忧戚，我今已得脱一切苦。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
