MĀ 83

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 7 长寿王品

83. （八三）长老上尊睡眠经

Parallel to: MN 37 / AN 7.61 / EĀ 19.3

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the country of Vārāṇasī, staying in the Deer Park at the Sītavana near the Rṣipatana.

一时，佛游婆耆瘦，在鼍山怖林鹿野园中。

At that time, the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana was wandering in the country of Magadha, staying in the village of Good Friends. Then, the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana alone in a quiet place, sat in meditation and fell asleep. The World-Honored One, knowing from afar that the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana alone in a quiet place, sat in meditation and fell asleep, having known this, immediately entered the samādhi of suchness, and with the samādhi of suchness, in the time it takes a strong man to bend and stretch his arm, disappeared from the Deer Park at the Sītavana near the Rṣipatana in Vārāṇasī and appeared before the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana in the village of Good Friends in Magadha. Then, the World-Honored One, having awakened from samādhi, said, "Mahāmaudgalyāyana! You are sleeping. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! You are sleeping."

尔时，尊者大目揵连游摩竭国，在善知识村中。于是，尊者大目揵连独安静处宴坐思惟而便睡眠。世尊遥知尊者大目揵连独安静处宴坐思惟而便睡眠。世尊知已，即入如其像定，以如其像定，犹若力士屈申臂顷，从婆耆瘦鼍山怖林鹿野园中忽没不现，往摩竭国善知识村尊者大目揵连前。于是，世尊从定而寤，告曰：「大目揵连！汝著睡眠。大目揵连！汝著睡眠。」

The Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana said to the World-Honored One, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

尊者大目揵连白世尊曰：「唯然。世尊！」

The Buddha said again, "Mahāmaudgalyāyana! Do not cultivate or spread the characteristics of sleep as you have described them. If you do not eliminate sleep in this way, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! You should follow the teachings you have heard, uphold them, spread them, recite them, and cultivate them. If you do not eliminate sleep in this way, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! You should follow the teachings you have heard, uphold them, and explain them to others. If you do not eliminate sleep in this way, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! You should follow the teachings you have heard, uphold them, contemplate them with your mind, and reflect on them. If you do not eliminate sleep in this way, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! You should rub your ears with both hands. If you do not eliminate sleep in this way,

佛复告曰：「大目揵连！如所相著睡眠，汝莫修彼相，亦莫广布，如是睡眠便可得灭。若汝睡眠故不灭者，大目揵连！当随本所闻法，随而受持广布诵习，如是睡眠便可得灭。若汝睡眠故不灭者，大目揵连！当随本所闻法，随而受持为他广说，如是睡眠便可得灭。若汝睡眠故不灭者，大目揵连！当随本所闻法，随而受持、心念、心思，如是睡眠便可得灭。若汝睡眠故不灭者，大目揵连！当以两手扪摸于耳，如是睡眠便可得灭。

If you do not eliminate sleep in this way, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! You should wash your face and eyes with cold water and sprinkle your body. If you do not eliminate sleep in this way, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! You should go outside the room, look at the four directions, and gaze at the stars. If you do not eliminate sleep in this way, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! You should go outside the room, go to the edge of the room, and walk in the open air, guarding the senses, with the mind at ease within, with the thought of rising later. If you do not eliminate sleep in this way, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! You should give up the walking path, go to the edge of the walking path, spread out your sitting mat, cross your legs, and sit. If you do not eliminate sleep in this way, Mahāmaudgalyāyana! You should return to the room, spread out your robe in four layers on the bed, fold your saṃghāṭī robe to make a pillow, lie on your right side, place one foot on the other, think of light, establish right mindfulness and right knowledge, and always think of rising.

「若汝睡眠故不灭者，大目揵连！当以冷水澡洗面目及洒身体，如是睡眠便可得灭。若汝睡眠故不灭者，大目揵连！当从室出，外观四方，瞻视星宿，如是睡眠便可得灭。若汝睡眠故不灭者，大目揵连！当从室出，而至屋头，露地经行，守护诸根，心安在内，于后前想，如是睡眠便可得灭。若汝睡眠故不灭者，大目揵连！当舍经行道，至经行道头，敷尼师檀，结跏趺坐，如是睡眠便可得灭。若汝睡眠故不灭者，大目揵连！当还入室，四叠优多罗僧以敷床上，襞僧伽梨作枕，右脇而卧，足足相累，心作明想，立正念正智，常欲起想。

Mahāmaudgalyāyana! Do not think of the pleasure of the bed, the ease of lying down, or the delight of sleeping. Do not be greedy for wealth and profit, or attached to fame. Why is this? I say that all dharmas are not to be associated with, and yet I also say that they are to be associated with. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! What dharmas do I say are not to be associated with? Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If the dharmas of the monastic and lay life are combined and associated, I say that these dharmas are not to be associated with. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If the dharmas of the monastic and lay life are combined and associated, then there will be much speaking. If there is much speaking, then there will be agitation. If there is agitation, then the mind will not be still. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If the mind is not still, then the mind will be far from concentration. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! Therefore I say that they are not to be associated with. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! What dharmas do I say are to be associated with? Mahāmaudgalyāyana! The place without affairs, I say that this dharma is to be associated with, the mountain forests, the foot of trees, empty and quiet places, high cliffs, stone chambers, places of silence and soundlessness, far away, without evil, without people, suitable for sitting in meditation. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! I say that this dharma is to be associated with.

「大目揵连！莫计床乐眠卧安快，莫贪财利，莫著名誉。所以者何？我说一切法不可与会，亦说与会。大目揵连！我说何法不可与会？大目揵连！若道俗法共合会者，我说此法不可与会。大目揵连！若道俗法共合会者，便多有所说，若多有所说者，则便有调，若有调者，便心不息。大目揵连！若心不息者，便心离定。大目揵连！是故我说不可与会。大目揵连！我说何法可与共会？大目揵连！彼无事处，我说此法可与共会，山林树下空安静处，高岩石室寂无音声，远离，无恶，无有人民，随顺宴坐。大目揵连！我说此法可与共会。

Mahāmaudgalyāyana! When you enter the village to beg for alms, you should be disgusted with gain, offerings, and respect. If you are disgusted with gain, offerings, and respect, then you should enter the village to beg for alms. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! Do not enter the village to beg for alms with a lofty and great mind. Why is this? In the houses of the elders, there are such things. When a bhikṣu comes to beg for alms, it causes the elders not to pay attention. The bhikṣu then thinks: 'Who has ruined my elder's house? Why is this? When I enter the elder's house, the elder does not pay attention.' Because of this, sorrow arises. Because of sorrow, agitation arises. Because of agitation, the mind does not rest. Because the mind does not rest, the mind is far from concentration. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! When you expound the Dharma, do not speak with contention. If you speak with contention, then you will speak much. Because of speaking much, agitation arises. Because of the arising of agitation, the mind does not rest. Because the mind does not rest, the mind is far from concentration. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! When you expound the Dharma, do not speak forcefully, but expound the Dharma like a lion. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! When you expound the Dharma, speak with a humble mind, abandon strength, destroy strength, and break down strength. You should speak without force, like a lion. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! You should train like this.

「大目揵连！汝若入村行乞食者，当以厌利，厌供养、恭敬，汝若于利、供养、恭敬心作厌已，便入村乞食。大目揵连！莫以高大意入村乞食。所以者何？诸长者家有如是事，比丘来乞食，令长者不作意，比丘便作是念：『谁坏我长者家？所以者何？我入长者家，长者不作意。』因是生忧，因忧生调，因调生心不息，因心不息，心便离定。大目揵连！汝说法时莫以诤说，若诤说者，便多有所说，因多说故，则便生调，因生调故，便心不息，因心不息故，便心离定。大目揵连！汝说法时莫强，说法如师子。大目揵连！汝说法时，下意说法，舍力、灭力、破坏于力，当以不强，说法如师子。大目揵连！当学如是。」

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms towards the Buddha, and said: "World-Honored One! How can a bhikṣu attain the ultimate, the ultimate purity, the ultimate practice of the Brahma-way, and the ultimate completion of the Brahma-way?"

尔时，尊者大目揵连即从坐起，偏袒著衣，叉手向佛，白曰：「世尊！云何比丘得至究竟，究竟白净、究竟梵行、究竟梵行讫？」

The World-Honored One said: "Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If a bhikṣu perceives pleasure, pain, or neither pleasure nor pain, he should observe that these perceptions are impermanent, arising and ceasing, to be abandoned, desireless, to be extinguished, and to be let go. Having observed that these perceptions are impermanent, arising and ceasing, to be abandoned, desireless, to be extinguished, and to be let go, he does not accept this world. Because of not accepting this world, he is not weary. Because of not being weary, he attains parinirvāṇa, with the end of birth, the establishment of the Brahma-way, the completion of what was to be done, and the knowledge of not being reborn again. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! Such a bhikṣu attains the ultimate, the ultimate purity, the ultimate practice of the Brahma-way, and the ultimate completion of the Brahma-way.

世尊告曰：「大目揵连！比丘若觉乐、觉苦、觉不苦不乐者，彼此觉观无常、观兴衰、观断、观无欲、观灭、观舍。彼此觉观无常、观兴衰、观断、观无欲、观灭、观舍已，不受此世；因不受世已，便不疲劳；因不疲劳已，便般涅槃，生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。大目揵连！如是比丘得至究竟，究竟白净、究竟梵行、究竟梵行讫。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。尊者大目揵连闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
