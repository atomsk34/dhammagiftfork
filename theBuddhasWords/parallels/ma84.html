MĀ 84

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 7 长寿王品

84. （八四）无刺经

Parallel to: AN 10.72

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in Vesali, staying at the High Tower Pavilion by the Monkey River. These venerable, senior, and great disciples, such as Chala, Upachala, Kshanti, Kshantivadin, Anupadhika, and Yasas, were also wandering in Vesali at the High Tower Pavilion by the Monkey River, all staying near the Buddha's leaf hut.

一时，佛游鞞舍离，在猕猴江边高楼台观。此诸名德、长老上尊、大弟子等，谓遮罗、优簸遮罗、贤善、贤患、无患、耶舍上称，如是此诸名德、长老上尊、大弟子等，亦游鞞舍离猕猴江边高楼台观，并皆近佛叶屋边住。

The Licchavis of Vesali heard that the World-Honored One was wandering in Vesali at the High Tower Pavilion by the Monkey River. They thought, "We should perform great supernatural powers, with the majesty of a king, and proclaim it loudly, leaving Vesali to go to where the Buddha is to make offerings and pay respects."

诸鞞舍离丽掣闻世尊游鞞舍离猕猴江边高楼台观，便作是念：「我等宁可作大如意足，作王威德，高声唱传，出鞞舍离，往诣佛所供养礼事。」

Then the venerable, senior, and great disciples heard that the Licchavis of Vesali were performing great supernatural powers, with the majesty of a king, and proclaiming it loudly, leaving Vesali to go to where the Buddha was to make offerings and pay respects. They thought, "Meditation is pierced by sound. The World-Honored One also said that meditation is pierced by sound. We should go to the Ox-horn Sala Grove, where there is no disturbance, to dwell in seclusion, in a quiet place, and sit in meditation." So the venerable, senior, and great disciples went to the Ox-horn Sala Grove, where there was no disturbance, to dwell in seclusion, in a quiet place, and sat in meditation.

时，诸名德、长老上尊、大弟子等，闻诸鞞舍离丽掣作大如意足，作王威德，高声唱传，出鞞舍离，来诣佛所供养礼事，便作是念：「禅以声为刺，世尊亦说禅以声为刺，我等宁可往诣牛角娑罗林，在彼无乱，远离独住，闲居静处，宴坐思惟。」于是，诸名德、长老上尊、大弟子等，往诣牛角娑罗林，在彼无乱，远离独住，闲居静处，宴坐思惟。

At that time, many Licchavis of Vesali performed great supernatural powers, with the majesty of a king, and proclaimed it loudly, leaving Vesali to go to where the Buddha was to make offerings and pay respects. Some Licchavis bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side, some exchanged greetings with the Buddha and sat to one side, some joined their palms towards the Buddha and sat to one side, and some sat silently upon seeing the Buddha from afar.

尔时，众多鞞舍离丽掣作大如意足，作王威德，高声唱传，出鞞舍离，往诣佛所供养礼事。或有鞞舍离丽掣，稽首佛足，却坐一面，或有与佛共相问讯，却坐一面，或有叉手向佛，却坐一面，或有遥见佛已，默然而坐。

At that time, the many Licchavis of Vesali each sat in concentration. The World-Honored One taught the Dharma for them, inspiring them with yearning and bringing them to fulfillment and joy. Having taught the Dharma for them with immeasurable skillful means, inspiring them with yearning and bringing them to fulfillment and joy, he remained silent. Then the many Licchavis of Vesali, for whom the World-Honored One had taught the Dharma, inspiring them with yearning and bringing them to fulfillment and joy, rose from their seats, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, circumambulated him three times, and departed.

彼时，众多鞞舍离丽掣各坐已定，世尊为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜，无量方便为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，默然而住。于是，众多鞞舍离丽掣，世尊为彼说法，劝发渴仰，成就欢喜已，即从坐起，稽首佛足，绕三匝而去。

Not long after the Licchavis of Vesali left, the World-Honored One asked the bhikshus, "Where have the elders, the great disciples, gone?"

鞞舍离丽掣去后不久。于是，世尊问诸比丘：「诸长老上尊、大弟子等为至何许？」

The bhikshus replied, "World-Honored One! The elders, the great disciples, heard the Licchavis of Vesali perform great supernatural powers, proclaim their royal majesty, and loudly proclaim their departure from Vesali to come to the Buddha to make offerings and pay homage. They thought, 'Meditation is pierced by sound. The World-Honored One also said that meditation is pierced by sound. We should go to the Gosirigaha Forest, where there is no disturbance, to dwell in seclusion, in a quiet place, to sit in meditation and contemplate.' World-Honored One! The elders, the great disciples, have all gone there."

诸比丘白曰：「世尊！诸长老上尊、大弟子等，闻诸鞞舍离丽掣作大如意足，作王威德，高声唱传，出鞞舍离，来诣佛所供养礼事，便作是念：『禅以声为刺，世尊亦说禅以声为刺。我等宁可往诣牛角娑罗林，在彼无乱，远离独住，闲居静处，宴坐思惟。』世尊！诸长老上尊、大弟子等共往诣彼。」

Then the World-Honored One, having heard this, praised, "Excellent! Excellent! If the elders, the great disciples, should say, 'Meditation is pierced by sound. The World-Honored One also said that meditation is pierced by sound.' Why is that? Because I indeed said that meditation has thorns. For those who uphold precepts, breaking precepts is a thorn. For those who guard the sense faculties, adorning the body is a thorn. For those who cultivate the impurity of the body, the appearance of purity is a thorn. For those who cultivate loving-kindness, anger is a thorn. For those who abstain from alcohol, drinking alcohol is a thorn. For those who practice the brahma-conduct, seeing female form is a thorn. For those who enter the first dhyana, sound is a thorn. For those who enter the second dhyana, initial and sustained thought are thorns. For those who enter the third dhyana, joy is a thorn. For those who enter the fourth dhyana, inhalation and exhalation are thorns. For those who enter the sphere of infinite space, the perception of form is a thorn. For those who enter the sphere of infinite consciousness, the perception of the sphere of infinite space is a thorn. For those who enter the sphere of nothingness, the perception of the sphere of infinite consciousness is a thorn. For those who enter the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception, the perception of the sphere of nothingness is a thorn. For those who enter the cessation of perception and feeling, perception and feeling are thorns.

于是，世尊闻已叹曰：「善哉！善哉！若长老上尊、大弟子等应如是说：『禅以声为刺，世尊亦说禅以声为刺。』所以者何？我实如是说，禅有刺，持戒者以犯戒为刺，护诸根者以严饰身为刺，修习恶露者以净相为刺，修习慈心者以恚为刺，离酒者以饮酒为刺，梵行者以见女色为刺。入初禅者以声为刺，入第二禅者以觉观为刺，入第三禅者以喜为刺，入第四禅者以入息出息为刺，入空处者以色想为刺，入识处者以空处想为刺，入无所有处者以识处想为刺，入无想处者以无所有处想为刺，入想知灭定者以想知为刺。

"Furthermore, there are three thorns: the thorn of desire, the thorn of anger, and the thorn of ignorance. These three thorns have been cut off, known, uprooted, and extinguished by the arhat who has exhausted the taints. They will not arise again. This is called the arhat being without thorns, the arhat being free from thorns, the arhat being without thorns and free from thorns."

「复次，有三刺：欲刺、恚刺、愚痴之刺。此三刺者，漏尽阿罗诃已断、已知，拔绝根本，灭不复生，是为阿罗诃无刺、阿罗诃离刺、阿罗诃无刺离刺。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
