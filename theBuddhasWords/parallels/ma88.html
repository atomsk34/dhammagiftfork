MĀ 88

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 8 秽品

88. （八八）求法经

Parallel to: MN 3 / EĀ 18.3

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Kusumapura, together with a great assembly of bhikṣus. He went to the Śiṣyagandha forest north of the five śāla trees, together with the renowned, venerable, and senior great disciples, namely Venerable Śāriputra, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, Venerable Mahākāśyapa, Venerable Mahākātyāyana, Venerable Aniruddha, Venerable Revata, Venerable Ānanda, and other such renowned, venerable, and senior great disciples. They were all staying near the Buddha's leaf hut in the five śāla trees.

一时，佛游拘娑罗国，与大比丘众俱，往诣五娑罗村北尸摄惒林中，及诸名德、上尊长老、大弟子等，谓尊者舍梨子、尊者大目揵连、尊者大迦叶、尊者大迦旃延、尊者阿那律陀、尊者丽越、尊者阿难，如是比余名德、上尊长老、大弟子等，亦在五娑罗村并皆近佛叶屋边住。

At that time, the World-Honored One addressed the bhikṣus, saying, "You should seek the Dharma, not seek food and drink. Why is that? It is because I have compassion for my disciples and want them to seek the Dharma, not seek food and drink. If you seek food and drink, not seek the Dharma, then you will be evil, and I will have no renown. If you seek the Dharma, not seek food and drink, then you will be good, and I will have renown.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「汝等当行求法，莫行求饮食。所以者何？我慈愍弟子故，欲令行求法，不行求饮食。若汝等行求饮食，不行求法者，汝等既自恶，我亦无名称。若汝等行求法，不行求饮食者，汝等既自好，我亦有名称。

How do the disciples rely on the Buddha to seek food and drink, not seek the Dharma? After I have eaten my fill, if there is leftover food, later two bhikṣus come, hungry and weak. I tell them, 'I have eaten my fill, if there is leftover food, you may take and eat it. If you do not take it, I will take it and pour it on the clean ground, or pour it into insect-free water.' The first bhikṣu then thinks, 'The World-Honored One has eaten his fill, if there is leftover food, if I do not take it, the World-Honored One will definitely take it and pour it on the clean ground, or pour it into insect-free water. I might as well take it and eat it.' He then takes and eats it.

「云何诸弟子为求饮食故而依佛行，非为求法？我饱食讫，食事已办，犹有残食，于后有二比丘来，饥渴力羸，我语彼曰：『我饱食讫，食事已办，犹有残食，汝等欲食者便取食之。若汝不取者，我便取以泻著净地，或复泻著无虫水中。』彼二比丘，第一比丘便作是念：『世尊食讫，食事已办，犹有残食，若我不取者，世尊必取泻著净地，或复泻著无虫水中，我今宁可取而食之。』即便取食。

After that bhikṣu takes that food, although he is happy and at ease for a day and night, that bhikṣu cannot please the Buddha because of taking that food. Why is that? Because that bhikṣu cannot be content with little, cannot know satisfaction, cannot be easy to support, cannot be easy to fill, cannot know the time, cannot know moderation, cannot be diligent, cannot sit in meditation, cannot practice purity, cannot be far removed, cannot have single-minded concentration, cannot be diligent, and cannot attain nirvāṇa. Therefore, that bhikṣu cannot please the Buddha because of taking that food. This is called the disciples relying on the Buddha to seek food and drink, not seeking the Dharma.

「彼比丘取此食已，虽一日一夜乐而得安隐，但彼比丘因取此食故，不可佛意。所以者何？彼比丘因取此食故，不得少欲，不知厌足，不得易养，不得易满，不得知时，不知节限，不得精进，不得宴坐，不得净行，不得远离，不得一心，不得精勤，亦不得涅槃。是以彼比丘因取此食故，不可佛意。是谓诸弟子为行求饮食故而依佛行，非为求法。

The second bhikṣu then thinks, 'The World-Honored One has eaten his fill, if there is leftover food, if I do not take it, the World-Honored One will definitely take it and pour it on the clean ground, or pour it into insect-free water. The World-Honored One also said that the worst food is leftover food. I might as well not take this food.' Having thought this, he then does not take it.

「云何诸弟子行求法，不行求饮食？彼二比丘，第二比丘便作是念：『世尊食讫，食事已办，犹有残食，若我不取者，世尊必取泻著净地，或复泻著无虫水中；又世尊说食中之下极者，谓残余食也，我今宁可不取此食。』作是念已，即便不取。

After that bhikṣu does not take that food, although he is miserable and uncomfortable for a day and night, that bhikṣu can please the Buddha because of not taking that food. Why is that? Because that bhikṣu can be content with little, can know satisfaction, can be easy to support, can be easy to fill, can know the time, can know moderation, can be diligent, can sit in meditation, can practice purity, can be far removed, can have single-minded concentration, can be diligent, and can attain nirvāṇa. Therefore, that bhikṣu can please the Buddha because of not taking that food. This is called the disciples relying on the Buddha to seek the Dharma, not seeking food and drink.

「彼比丘不取此食已，虽一日一夜苦而不安隐，但彼比丘因不取此食故，得可佛意。所以者何？彼比丘因不取此食故，得少欲，得知足，得易养，得易满，得知时，得节限，得精进，得宴坐，得净行，得远离，得一心，得精勤，亦得涅槃。是以彼比丘因不取此食故，得可佛意。是谓诸弟子为行求法故而依佛行，非为求饮食。」

Then the World-Honored One told the disciples, "If in a Dharma and Vinaya, the honored teacher likes to dwell in seclusion, but the upper disciples do not like to dwell in seclusion, then that Dharma and Vinaya does not benefit many people, many people do not find happiness, it is not for the sake of compassion for the world, nor is it for the sake of gods and humans seeking meaning and benefit, seeking peace and happiness. If in a Dharma and Vinaya, the honored teacher likes to dwell in seclusion, but the middle and lower disciples do not like to dwell in seclusion, then that Dharma and Vinaya does not benefit many people, many people do not find happiness, it is not for the sake of compassion for the world, nor is it for the sake of gods and humans seeking meaning and benefit, seeking peace and happiness. If in a Dharma and Vinaya, the honored teacher likes to dwell in seclusion, and the upper disciples also like to dwell in seclusion, then that Dharma and Vinaya benefits many people, many people find happiness, it is for the sake of compassion for the world, and it is for the sake of gods and humans seeking meaning and benefit, seeking peace and happiness. If in a Dharma and Vinaya, the honored teacher likes to dwell in seclusion, and the middle and lower disciples also like to dwell in seclusion, then that Dharma and Vinaya benefits many people, many people find happiness, it is for the sake of compassion for the world, and it is for the sake of gods and humans seeking meaning and benefit, seeking peace and happiness."

于是，世尊告诸弟子：「若有法、律尊师乐住远离，上弟子不乐住远离者，彼法、律不饶益多人，多人不得乐，非为愍伤世间，亦非为天为人求义及饶益，求安隐快乐。若有法、律尊师乐住远离，中、下弟子不乐住远离者，彼法、律不饶益多人，多人不得乐，非为愍伤世间，亦非为天为人求义及饶益，求安隐快乐。若有法、律尊师乐住远离，上弟子亦乐住远离者，彼法、律饶益多人，多人得乐，为愍伤世间，亦为天为人求义及饶益，求安隐快乐。若有法、律尊师乐住远离，中、下弟子亦乐住远离者，彼法、律饶益多人，多人得乐，为愍伤世间，亦为天为人求义及饶益，求安隐快乐。」

At that time, Venerable Sariputra was also in the assembly. Then the World-Honored One said, "Sariputra! You should teach the Dharma according to the Dharma for the monks. I have a backache now and wish to rest a little."

是时，尊者舍梨子亦在众中。彼时，世尊告曰：「舍梨子！汝为诸比丘说法如法，我患背痛，今欲小息。」

Venerable Sariputra accepted the Buddha's instruction, saying, "Yes, World-Honored One!" Then the World-Honored One spread out his upper robe four times on the bed, folded his saṃghāṭī robe as a pillow, lay on his right side, placed one foot on the other, contemplated the light, with right mindfulness and right knowledge, always mindful of rising.

尊者舍梨子即受佛教：「唯然。世尊！」于是，世尊四叠优多罗僧以敷床上，卷僧伽梨作枕，右脇而卧，足足相累，作光明想，正念正智，常念欲起。

At that time, Venerable Sariputra addressed the monks, saying, "Virtuous ones! You should know that the World-Honored One has just spoken the Dharma in brief. If in a Dharma and Vinaya, the honored teacher delights in dwelling in seclusion, but the superior disciples do not delight in dwelling in seclusion, then that Dharma and Vinaya do not benefit many people, many people do not find happiness, it is not for the sake of compassion for the world, nor is it for the sake of seeking benefit and happiness for gods and humans. If in a Dharma and Vinaya, the honored teacher delights in dwelling in seclusion, but the middling and inferior disciples do not delight in dwelling in seclusion, then that Dharma and Vinaya do not benefit many people, many people do not find happiness, it is not for the sake of compassion for the world, nor is it for the sake of seeking benefit and happiness for gods and humans. If in a Dharma and Vinaya, the honored teacher delights in dwelling in seclusion, and the superior disciples also delight in dwelling in seclusion, then that Dharma and Vinaya benefits many people, many people find happiness, it is for the sake of compassion for the world, and it is for the sake of seeking benefit and happiness for gods and humans. If in a Dharma and Vinaya, the honored teacher delights in dwelling in seclusion, and the middling and inferior disciples also delight in dwelling in seclusion, then that Dharma and Vinaya benefits many people, many people find happiness, it is for the sake of compassion for the world, and it is for the sake of seeking benefit and happiness for gods and humans. But the World-Honored One has spoken this Dharma in brief. How do you understand the meaning? How do you extensively explain it?"

是时，尊者舍梨子告诸比丘：「诸贤！当知世尊向略说法。若有法、律尊师乐住远离，上弟子不乐住远离者，彼法、律不饶益多人，多人不得乐，不为愍伤世间，亦非为天为人求义及饶益，求安隐快乐。若有法、律尊师乐住远离，中、下弟子不乐住远离者，彼法、律不饶益多人，多人不得乐，不为愍伤世间，亦非为天为人求义及饶益，求安隐快乐。若有法、律尊师乐住远离，上弟子亦乐住远离者，彼法、律饶益多人，多人得乐，为愍伤世间，亦为天为人求义及饶益，求安隐快乐。若有法、律尊师乐住远离，中、下弟子亦乐住远离者，彼法、律饶益多人，多人得乐，为愍伤世间，亦为天为人求义及饶益，求安隐快乐。然世尊说此法极略，汝等云何解义？云何广分别？」

At that time, some monks in the assembly said, "Venerable Sariputra! If the elders and superiors themselves say, 'I have attained ultimate knowledge, my births are exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done is done, I will not be reborn again, knowing it as it really is,' then the Brahma-faring ones, hearing that monk say, 'I have attained ultimate knowledge,' will be delighted.

彼时，众中或有比丘作如是说：「尊者舍梨子！若诸长老上尊自说：『我得究竟智，我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已办，不更受有，知如真。』诸梵行者闻彼比丘自说我得究竟智，便得欢喜。」

Again, some monks said, "Venerable Sariputra! If the middling and inferior disciples seek the unsurpassed nirvana, the Brahma-faring ones, seeing their practice, will be delighted. Thus, that monk speaks this meaning, which cannot be the intention of Venerable Sariputra.

复有比丘作如是说：「尊者舍梨子！若中、下弟子求愿无上涅槃，诸梵行者见彼行已，便得欢喜。如是彼比丘而说此义，不可尊者舍梨子意。」

Venerable Sariputra said to those monks, "Virtuous ones! Listen to me speaking for you. Virtuous ones! If in a Dharma and Vinaya where the teacher delights in dwelling in seclusion, the superior disciples do not delight in dwelling in seclusion, then the superior disciples have three things to be criticized. What are the three? The teacher delights in dwelling in seclusion, but the superior disciples do not learn to abandon it. The superior disciples are criticized for this. If the teacher speaks of dharmas to be abandoned, the superior disciples do not abandon those dharmas. The superior disciples are criticized for this. The attainments to be realized, the superior disciples abandon the means. The superior disciples are criticized for this. If in a Dharma and Vinaya where the teacher delights in dwelling in seclusion, the superior disciples do not delight in dwelling in seclusion, then the superior disciples have these three things to be criticized. Virtuous ones! If in a Dharma and Vinaya where the teacher delights in dwelling in seclusion, the middling and inferior disciples do not delight in dwelling in seclusion, then the middling and inferior disciples have three things to be criticized. What are the three? The teacher delights in dwelling in seclusion, but the middling and inferior disciples do not learn to abandon it. The middling and inferior disciples are criticized for this. If the teacher speaks of dharmas to be abandoned, the middling and inferior disciples do not abandon those dharmas. The middling and inferior disciples are criticized for this. The attainments to be realized, the middling and inferior disciples abandon the means. The middling and inferior disciples are criticized for this. If in a Dharma and Vinaya where the teacher delights in dwelling in seclusion, the middling and inferior disciples do not delight in dwelling in seclusion, then the middling and inferior disciples have these three things to be criticized.

尊者舍梨子告彼比丘：「诸贤等！听我为汝说。诸贤！若有法、律尊师乐住远离，上弟子不乐住远离者，上弟子有三事可毁。云何为三？尊师乐住远离，上弟子不学舍离，上弟子以此可毁。尊师若说可断法，上弟子不断彼法，上弟子以此可毁。所可受证，上弟子而舍方便，上弟子以此可毁。若有法、律尊师乐住远离，上弟子不乐住远离者，上弟子有此三事可毁。诸贤！若有法、律尊师乐住远离，中、下弟子不乐住远离者，中、下弟子有三事可毁。云何为三？尊师乐住远离，中、下弟子不学舍离，中、下弟子以此可毁。尊师若说可断法，中、下弟子不断彼法，中、下弟子以此可毁。所可受证，中、下弟子而舍方便，中、下弟子以此可毁。若有法、律尊师乐住远离，中、下弟子不乐住远离者，中、下弟子有此三事可毁。

"Virtuous ones! If in a Dharma and Vinaya where the teacher delights in dwelling in seclusion, the superior disciples also delight in dwelling in seclusion, then the superior disciples have three things to be praised. What are the three? The teacher delights in dwelling in seclusion, and the superior disciples also learn to abandon it. The superior disciples are praised for this. If the teacher speaks of dharmas to be abandoned, the superior disciples then abandon those dharmas. The superior disciples are praised for this. The attainments to be realized, the superior disciples diligently study and do not abandon the means. The superior disciples are praised for this. If in a Dharma and Vinaya where the teacher delights in dwelling in seclusion, the superior disciples also delight in dwelling in seclusion, then the superior disciples have these three things to be praised. Virtuous ones! If in a Dharma and Vinaya where the teacher delights in dwelling in seclusion, the middling and inferior disciples also delight in dwelling in seclusion, then the middling and inferior disciples have three things to be praised. What are the three? The teacher delights in dwelling in seclusion, and the middling and inferior disciples also learn to abandon it. The middling and inferior disciples are praised for this. If the teacher speaks of dharmas to be abandoned, the middling and inferior disciples then abandon those dharmas. The middling and inferior disciples are praised for this. The attainments to be realized, the middling and inferior disciples diligently study and do not abandon the means. The middling and inferior disciples are praised for this. If in a Dharma and Vinaya where the teacher delights in dwelling in seclusion, the middling and inferior disciples also delight in dwelling in seclusion, then the middling and inferior disciples have these three things to be praised."

「诸贤！若有法、律尊师乐住远离，上弟子亦乐住远离者，上弟子有三事可称。云何为三？尊师乐住远离，上弟子亦学舍离，上弟子以此可称。尊师若说可断法，上弟子便断彼法，上弟子以此可称。所可受证，上弟子精进勤学，不舍方便，上弟子以此可称。诸贤！若有法、律尊师乐住远离，上弟子亦乐住远离者，上弟子有此三事可称。诸贤！若有法、律尊师乐住远离，中、下弟子亦乐住远离者，中、下弟子有三事可称。云何为三？尊师乐住远离，中、下弟子亦学舍离，中、下弟子以此可称。尊师若说可断法，中、下弟子便断彼法，中、下弟子以此可称。所可受证，中、下弟子精进勤学，不舍方便，中、下弟子以此可称。诸贤！若有法、律尊师乐住远离，中、下弟子亦乐住远离者，中、下弟子有此三事可称。」

Venerable Sariputra further said to the monks, "Virtuous ones! There is a middle way that can attain mental stillness, attain concentration and happiness, accord with the Dharma and the next Dharma, attain the supernormal powers and awakening, and also attain nirvana. Virtuous ones! What is the middle way that can attain mental stillness, attain concentration and happiness, accord with the Dharma and the next Dharma, attain the supernormal powers and awakening, and also attain nirvana? Virtuous ones! Desire is evil, and evil thoughts of desire are also evil. One should cut off thoughts of desire and also cut off evil thoughts of desire. Likewise, anger, resentment, stinginess, jealousy, deceit, flattery, shamelessness, lack of conscience, pride, conceit, arrogance, negligence, arrogance, and hatred. Virtuous ones! Greed is also evil, and attachment is also evil. One should cut off greed and also cut off attachment. Virtuous ones! This is called the middle way that can attain mental stillness, attain concentration and happiness, accord with the Dharma and the next Dharma, attain the supernormal powers and awakening, and also attain nirvana.

尊者舍梨子复告诸比丘：「诸贤！有中道能得心住，得定得乐，顺法次法，得通得觉，亦得涅槃。诸贤！云何有中道能得心住，得定得乐，顺法次法，得通得觉，亦得涅槃？诸贤！念欲恶，恶念欲亦恶，彼断念欲，亦断恶念欲；如是恚、怨结、悭嫉、欺诳、谀谄、无慙、无愧、慢、最上慢、贡高、放逸、豪贵、憎诤。诸贤！贪亦恶，著亦恶，彼断贪，亦断著。诸贤！是谓中道能得心住，得定得乐，顺法次法，得通得觉，亦得涅槃。

"Virtuous ones! There is also a middle way that can attain mental stillness, attain concentration and happiness, accord with the Dharma and the next Dharma, attain the supernormal powers and awakening, and also attain nirvana. Virtuous ones! What is the middle way that can attain mental stillness, attain concentration and happiness, accord with the Dharma and the next Dharma, attain the supernormal powers and awakening, and also attain nirvana? It refers to the eightfold noble path, namely right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right concentration. These are the eight. Virtuous ones! This is called the middle way that can attain mental stillness, attain concentration and happiness, accord with the Dharma and the next Dharma, attain the supernormal powers and awakening, and also attain nirvana."

「诸贤！复有中道能得心住，得定得乐，顺法次法，得通得觉，亦得涅槃。诸贤！云何复有中道能得心住，得定得乐，顺法次法，得通得觉，亦得涅槃？谓八支圣道，正见乃至正定，是为八。诸贤！是谓复有中道能得心住，得定得乐，顺法次法，得通得觉，亦得涅槃。」

Then the World-Honored One's illness was immediately cured and he felt at ease. He got up from his bed, sat cross-legged, and praised Venerable Sariputra, saying, "Excellent! Excellent! Sariputra has taught the Dharma to the monks according to the Dharma. Sariputra! You should continue to teach the Dharma to the monks according to the Dharma. Sariputra! You should often teach the Dharma to the monks according to the Dharma."

于是，世尊所患即除而得安隐，从卧寤起，结跏趺坐，叹尊者舍梨子：「善哉！善哉！舍梨子为诸比丘说法如法。舍梨子！汝当复为诸比丘说法如法。舍梨子！汝当数数为诸比丘说法如法。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the monks, "You should all together accept the Dharma according to the Dharma, recite and uphold it. Why is that? This Dharma according to the Dharma has the Dharma and the meaning, is the basis for the holy life, leads to the supernormal powers and awakening, and also leads to nirvana. For those noble sons who shave their heads and beards, put on the saffron robes, leave home out of true faith, become homeless, and study the path, this Dharma according to the Dharma should be well received and upheld."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「汝等当共受法如法，诵习执持。所以者何？此法如法，有法有义，为梵行本，得通得觉，亦得涅槃。诸族姓子剃除须发，著袈裟衣，至信、舍家、无家、学道者，此法如法，当善受持。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Venerable Sariputra and the monks, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说如是。尊者舍梨子及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
