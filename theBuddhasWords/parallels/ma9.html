MĀ 9

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 1 七法品

9. The Seven Chariots （九）七车经（初一日诵）

Parallel to: MN 24 / EĀ 39.10

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

Once, the Buddha was staying in the Bamboo Grove Monastery in Rājagṛha, together with a great assembly of bhikṣus, while the Venerable Pūrṇa Maitrāyaniputra was also staying for the summer retreat in his hometown. At that time, the bhikṣus in his hometown, having completed the summer retreat and three months, repaired their robes, put on their robes, took their bowls, left their hometown, and went to Rājagṛha. Traveling along the way, they arrived at Rājagṛha and stayed at the Bamboo Grove Monastery.

一时，佛游王舍城，在竹林精舍，与大比丘众共受夏坐，尊者满慈子亦于生地受夏坐。是时，生地诸比丘受夏坐讫，过三月已，补治衣竟，摄衣持钵，从生地出，向王舍城，展转进前，至王舍城，住王舍城竹林精舍。

At that time, the bhikṣus from his hometown went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed their heads at his feet, and sat to one side.

是时，生地诸比丘诣世尊所，稽首作礼，却坐一面。

The World-Honored One asked, "Bhikṣus, where have you come from? Where did you spend the summer retreat?"

世尊问曰：「诸比丘！从何所来？何处夏坐？」

The bhikṣus from his hometown said, "World-Honored One! We have come from our hometown and spent the summer retreat in our hometown."

生地诸比丘白曰：「世尊！从生地来，于生地夏坐。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Among the bhikṣus in your hometown, which bhikṣu is praised by the other bhikṣus for having few desires and being content himself, and for praising those with few desires and contentment; for being secluded himself, and praising seclusion; for being diligent himself, and praising diligence; for having right mindfulness himself, and praising right mindfulness; for having one-pointedness of mind himself, and praising one-pointedness of mind; for having wisdom himself, and praising wisdom; for having exhausted the taints himself, and praising the exhaustion of the taints; for inspiring others with yearning and bringing about joy himself, and praising inspiring others with yearning and bringing about joy?"

世尊问曰：「于彼生地诸比丘中，何等比丘为诸比丘所共称誉，自少欲、知足，称说少欲、知足；自闲居，称说闲居；自精进，称说精进；自正念，称说正念；自一心，称说一心；自智慧，称说智慧；自漏尽，称说漏尽；自劝发渴仰、成就欢喜，称说劝发渴仰、成就欢喜？」

The bhikṣus from his hometown said, "World-Honored One! Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana is praised by the other bhikṣus in our hometown for having few desires and being content himself, and for praising those with few desires and contentment; for being secluded himself, and praising seclusion; for being diligent himself, and praising diligence; for having right mindfulness himself, and praising right mindfulness; for having one-pointedness of mind himself, and praising one-pointedness of mind; for having wisdom himself, and praising wisdom; for having exhausted the taints himself, and praising the exhaustion of the taints; for inspiring others with yearning and bringing about joy himself, and praising inspiring others with yearning and bringing about joy."

生地诸比丘白曰：「世尊！尊者满慈子于彼生地，为诸比丘所共称誉，自少欲、知足，称说少欲、知足；自闲居，称说闲居；自精进，称说精进；自正念，称说正念；自一心，称说一心；自智慧，称说智慧；自漏尽，称说漏尽；自劝发渴仰、成就欢喜，称说劝发渴仰、成就欢喜。」

At that time, Venerable Sāriputta was sitting in the assembly. Venerable Sāriputta thought, "The World-Honored One is asking the bhikṣus from his hometown about this matter. The bhikṣus from his hometown are greatly praising Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana for having few desires and being content himself, and for praising those with few desires and contentment; for being secluded himself, and praising seclusion; for being diligent himself, and praising diligence; for having right mindfulness himself, and praising right mindfulness; for having one-pointedness of mind himself, and praising one-pointedness of mind; for having wisdom himself, and praising wisdom; for having exhausted the taints himself, and praising the exhaustion of the taints; for inspiring others with yearning and bringing about joy himself, and praising inspiring others with yearning and bringing about joy."

是时，尊者舍梨子在众中坐，尊者舍梨子作如是念：「世尊如事问彼生地诸比丘辈，生地诸比丘极大称誉贤者满慈子，自少欲、知足，称说少欲、知足；自闲居，称说闲居；自精进，称说精进；自正念，称说正念；自一心，称说一心；自智慧，称说智慧；自漏尽，称说漏尽；自劝发渴仰、成就欢喜，称说劝发渴仰，成就欢喜。」

Venerable Sāriputta further thought, "When will I be able to gather with Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana to ask him about the meaning of few things, and perhaps he will listen to my questions."

尊者舍梨子复作是念：「何时当得与贤者满慈子共聚集会，问其少义，彼或能听我之所问。」

At that time, the World-Honored One, having completed the summer retreat in Rājagṛha, after three months, having repaired his robes, took up his robe and bowl and left Rājagṛha, heading for Śrāvastī. Traveling along, he arrived at Śrāvastī and stayed at Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma. Venerable Sāriputta and the bhikṣus from his hometown stayed in Rājagṛha for a few days, took up their robes and bowls, and headed for Śrāvastī. Traveling along, they arrived at Śrāvastī and stayed together at Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

尔时，世尊于王舍城受夏坐讫，过三月已，补治衣竟，摄衣持钵，从王舍城出，向舍卫国，展转进前，至舍卫国，即住胜林给孤独园。尊者舍梨子与生地诸比丘于王舍城共住少日，摄衣持钵，向舍卫国，展转进前，至舍卫国，共住胜林给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, having completed the summer retreat in his hometown, after three months, having repaired his robes, took up his robe and bowl and left his hometown, heading for Śrāvastī. Traveling along, he arrived at Śrāvastī and also stayed at Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma. Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat down to one side.

是时，尊者满慈子于生地受夏坐讫，过三月已，补治衣竟，摄衣持钵，从生地出，向舍卫国，展转进前，至舍卫国，亦住胜林给孤独园。尊者满慈子诣世尊所，稽首作礼，于如来前敷尼师檀，结加趺坐。

At that time, Venerable Sāriputta asked other bhikṣus, "Venerable ones, who is Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana?"

时，尊者舍梨子问余比丘：「诸贤！何者是贤者满慈子耶？」

The bhikṣus said to Venerable Sāriputta, "Yes, Venerable One, he is the one sitting in front of the Tathāgata, fair and prominent-nosed, like a parrot's beak."

诸比丘白尊者舍梨子：「唯然，尊者在如来前坐，白皙隆鼻，如鹦鹉［口＊隽］，即其人也。」

At that time, Venerable Sāriputta, having seen Mahāmaudgalyāyana's appearance, remembered it well.

时，尊者舍梨子知满慈子色貌已，则善记念。

Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, having passed the night, at dawn, put on his robe, took his bowl, entered Śrāvastī to beg for food, finished eating in the middle of the day, returned, put away his robe and bowl, washed his hands and feet, put his sitting mat on his shoulder, and went to the place for walking meditation in the Andha forest. Venerable Sāriputta, having passed the night, at dawn, put on his robe, took his bowl, entered Śrāvastī to beg for food, finished eating in the middle of the day, returned, put away his robe and bowl, washed his hands and feet, put his sitting mat on his shoulder, and went to the place for walking meditation in the Andha forest.

尊者满慈子过夜平旦，著衣持钵，入舍卫国而行乞食，食讫中后，还举衣钵，澡洗手足，以尼师檀著于肩上，至安陀林经行之处。尊者舍梨子亦过夜平旦，著衣持钵，入舍卫国而行乞食，食讫中后，还举衣钵，澡洗手足，以尼师檀著于肩上，至安陀林经行之处。

Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, having arrived at the Andha forest, spread out his sitting mat under a tree and sat cross-legged. Venerable Sāriputta also went to the Andha forest, not far from Mahāmaudgalyāyana, spread out his sitting mat under a tree and sat cross-legged.

时，尊者满慈子到安陀林，于一树下敷尼师檀，结加趺坐。尊者舍梨子亦至安陀林，离满慈子不远，于一树下敷尼师檀，结加趺坐。

Venerable Sāriputta, having risen from his cross-legged sitting in the afternoon, went to where Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana was, exchanged greetings with him, and then sat to one side. He asked Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, "Virtuous one! Do you practice the holy life under the śramaṇa Gautama?"

尊者舍梨子则于晡时从燕坐起，往诣尊者满慈子所，共相问讯，却坐一面，则问尊者满慈子曰：「贤者！从沙门瞿昙修梵行耶？」

He replied, "Yes."

答曰：「如是。」

"How is it? Virtuous one! Do you practice the holy life under the śramaṇa Gautama because of the purity of precepts?"

「云何？贤者！以戒净故，从沙门瞿昙修梵行耶？」

He replied, "No."

答曰：「不也。」

"Is it because of the purity of mind, the purity of view, the purity of the cover of doubt, the purity of the knowledge and vision of what is and is not the path, the purity of the knowledge and vision of the path, and the purity of the knowledge and vision of the path's elimination that you practice the holy life under the śramaṇa Gautama?"

「以心净故、以见净故、以疑盖净故、以道非道知见净故、以道迹知见净故、以道迹断智净故，从沙门瞿昙修梵行耶？」

He replied, "No."

答曰：「不也。」

He further asked, "I asked the virtuous one before, 'Do you practice the holy life under the śramaṇa Gautama?' You said 'yes'. Now I ask the virtuous one, 'Is it because of the purity of precepts that you practice the holy life under the śramaṇa Gautama?' You said 'no'. Is it because of the purity of mind, the purity of view, the purity of the cover of doubt, the purity of the knowledge and vision of what is and is not the path, the purity of the knowledge and vision of the path, and the purity of the knowledge and vision of the path's elimination that you practice the holy life under the śramaṇa Gautama?' You said 'no'. Then for what reason do you practice the holy life under the śramaṇa Gautama?"

又复问曰：「我向问贤者从沙门瞿昙修梵行耶？则言如是。今问贤者以戒净故从沙门瞿昙修梵行耶？便言不也。以心净故、以见净故、以疑盖净故、以道非道知见净故、以道迹知见净故、以道迹断智净故，从沙门瞿昙修梵行耶？便言不也。然以何义，从沙门瞿昙修梵行耶？」

He replied, "Virtuous one! It is because of the nirvāṇa without remainder."

答曰：「贤者！以无余涅槃故。」

He further asked, "How is it, virtuous one! Is it because of the purity of precepts that the śramaṇa Gautama establishes the nirvāṇa without remainder?"

又复问曰：「云何？贤者！以戒净故，沙门瞿昙施设无余涅槃耶？」

He replied, "No."

答曰：「不也。」

"Is it because of the purity of mind, the purity of view, the purity of the cover of doubt, the purity of the knowledge and vision of what is and is not the path, the purity of the knowledge and vision of the path, and the purity of the knowledge and vision of the path's elimination that the śramaṇa Gautama establishes the nirvāṇa without remainder?"

「以心净故、以见净故、以疑盖净故、以道非道知见净故、以道迹知见净故、以道迹断智净故，沙门瞿昙施设无余涅槃耶？」

He replied, "No."

答曰：「不也。」

He further asked, "I asked you before, why did you say that the śramaṇa Gautama establishes the nirvāṇa without remainder because of the purity of morality? You said no. Because of the purity of mind, the purity of view, the purity of the cover of doubt, the purity of the knowledge and vision of what is and is not the path, the purity of the knowledge and vision of the path, and the purity of the knowledge and vision of the path's elimination, the śramaṇa Gautama establishes the nirvāṇa without remainder? You said no. What is the meaning of what you said? How can one know?"

又复问曰：「我向问仁，云何贤者以戒净故，沙门瞿昙施设无余涅槃耶？贤者言不。以心净故、以见净故、以疑盖净故、以道非道知见净故、以道迹知见净故、以道迹断智净故，沙门瞿昙施设无余涅槃耶？贤者言不。贤者所说为是何义？云何得知？」

He replied, "Virtuous one! If the World-Honored Śramaṇa Gautama establishes the nirvāṇa without remainder because of the purity of morality, then he would be using the term 'without remainder' to refer to something that still has remainder. Because of the purity of mind, the purity of view, the purity of the cover of doubt, the purity of the knowledge and vision of what is and is not the path, the purity of the knowledge and vision of the path, and the purity of the knowledge and vision of the path's elimination, the World-Honored Śramaṇa Gautama establishes the nirvāṇa without remainder, then he would be using the term 'without remainder' to refer to something that still has remainder. Virtuous one! If the World-Honored One establishes the nirvāṇa without remainder apart from these dharmas, then ordinary beings would also attain nirvāṇa, because ordinary beings are also apart from these dharmas. Virtuous one! But it is because of the purity of morality that one attains the purity of mind, because of the purity of mind that one attains the purity of view, because of the purity of view that one attains the purity of the cover of doubt, because of the purity of the cover of doubt that one attains the purity of the knowledge and vision of what is and is not the path, because of the purity of the knowledge and vision of what is and is not the path that one attains the purity of the knowledge and vision of the path, because of the purity of the knowledge and vision of the path that one attains the purity of the knowledge and vision of the path's elimination, and because of the purity of the knowledge and vision of the path's elimination that the World-Honored Śramaṇa Gautama establishes the nirvāṇa without remainder.

答曰：「贤者！若以戒净故，世尊沙门瞿昙施设无余涅槃者，则以有余称说无余；以心净故、以见净故、以疑盖净故、以道非道知见净故、以道迹知见净故、以道迹断智净故，世尊沙门瞿昙施设无余涅槃者，则以有余称说无余。贤者！若离此法，世尊施设无余涅槃者，则凡夫亦当般涅槃，以凡夫亦离此法故。贤者！但以戒净故，得心净，以心净故，得见净，以见净故，得疑盖净，以疑葢净故，得道非道知见净，以道非道知见净故，得道迹知见净，以道迹知见净故，得道迹断智净，以道迹断智净故，世尊沙门瞿昙施设无余涅槃也。

Virtuous one! Listen again. In the past, King Prasenajit of Kosala had business in the city of Bālikā. He thought, 'By what means can I travel from Śrāvastī to Bālikā in one day?' He further thought, 'I shall now arrange seven chariots between Śrāvastī and Bālikā.' At that time, he traveled from Śrāvastī to Bālikā, arranging seven chariots along the way. Having arranged the seven chariots, he departed from Śrāvastī, reached the first chariot, abandoned the first chariot and rode the second chariot, reached the third chariot, abandoned the second chariot and rode the third chariot, reached the fourth chariot, abandoned the third chariot and rode the fourth chariot, reached the fifth chariot, abandoned the fourth chariot and rode the fifth chariot, reached the sixth chariot, abandoned the fifth chariot and rode the sixth chariot, reached the seventh chariot, rode the seventh chariot, and arrived in Bālikā in one day.

「贤者！复听。昔拘萨罗王波斯匿在舍卫国，于婆鸡帝有事，彼作是念：『以何方便，令一日行，从舍卫国至婆鸡帝耶？』复作是念：『我今宁可从舍卫国至婆鸡帝，于其中间布置七车。』尔时，即从舍卫国至婆鸡帝，于其中间布置七车。布七车已，从舍卫国出，至初车，乘初车，至第二车，舍初车，乘第二车，至第三车，舍第二车，乘第三车，至第四车，舍第三车，乘第四车，至第五车，舍第四车，乘第五车，至第六车，舍第五车，乘第六车，至第七车，乘第七车，于一日中至婆鸡帝。

After he had finished his business in Bālikā, surrounded by his ministers, he sat on the royal throne. The ministers said, 'How did the Heavenly King travel from Śrāvastī to Bālikā in one day?' The king said, 'So it was.' 'Did the Heavenly King ride the first chariot from Śrāvastī to Bālikā in one day?' The king said, 'No.' 'Did he ride the second chariot, the third chariot, up to the seventh chariot from Śrāvastī to Bālikā?' The king said, 'No.' Virtuous one! How should the ministers of King Prasenajit of Kosala answer when asked this question?

「彼于婆鸡帝办其事已，大臣围绕，坐王正殿，群臣白曰：『云何，天王！以一日行从舍卫国至婆鸡帝耶？』王曰：『如是。』『云何天王，乘第一车一日从舍卫国至婆鸡帝耶？』王曰：『不也。』『乘第二车乘第三车至第七车，从舍卫国至婆鸡帝耶？』王曰：『不也。』云何贤者！拘萨罗王波斯匿群臣复问，当云何说？

The king replied to the ministers, 'I had business in Bālikā. I thought, "By what means can I travel from Śrāvastī to Bālikā in one day?" I further thought, "I shall now arrange seven chariots between Śrāvastī and Bālikā." I then traveled from Śrāvastī to Bālikā, arranging seven chariots along the way. Having arranged the seven chariots, I departed from Śrāvastī, reached the first chariot, abandoned the first chariot and rode the second chariot, reached the third chariot, abandoned the second chariot and rode the third chariot, reached the fourth chariot, abandoned the third chariot and rode the fourth chariot, reached the fifth chariot, abandoned the fourth chariot and rode the fifth chariot, reached the sixth chariot, abandoned the fifth chariot and rode the sixth chariot, reached the seventh chariot, rode the seventh chariot, and arrived in Bālikā in one day.

「王答群臣：『我在舍卫国，于婆鸡帝有事，我作是念：「以何方便，令一日行，从舍卫国至婆鸡帝耶？」我复作是念：「我今宁可从舍卫国至婆鸡帝，于其中间布置七车。」我时即从舍卫国至婆鸡帝，于其中间布置七车。布七车已，从舍卫国出，至初车，乘初车，至第二车，舍初车，乘第二车，至第三车，舍第二车，乘第三车，至第四车，舍第三车，乘第四车，至第五车，舍第四车，乘第五车，至第六车，舍第五车，乘第六车，至第七车，乘第七车，于一日中至婆鸡帝。』

Virtuous one! King Prasenajit of Kosala answered the ministers' questions in this way. Virtuous one! Because of the purity of precepts, one attains the purity of mind. Because of the purity of mind, one attains the purity of view. Because of the purity of view, one attains the purity of doubt and obscuration. Because of the purity of doubt and obscuration, one attains the purity of knowledge and vision of the path and what is not the path. Because of the purity of knowledge and vision of the path and what is not the path, one attains the purity of knowledge and vision of the path and its stages. Because of the purity of knowledge and vision of the path and its stages, one attains the purity of knowledge and vision of the cessation of the path and its stages. Because of the purity of knowledge and vision of the cessation of the path and its stages, the World-Honored One establishes the nirvāṇa without remainder.

「如是，贤者！拘萨罗王波斯匿答对群臣所问如是。如是，贤者！以戒净故，得心净，以心净故，得见净，以见净故，得疑盖净，以疑葢净故，得道非道知见净，以道非道知见净故，得道迹知见净，以道迹知见净故，得道迹断智净，以道迹断智净故，世尊施设无余涅槃。」

Then Venerable Sāriputta asked Venerable Mahācunda, "What is the name of the virtuous one? How are the virtuous ones called?"

于是，尊者舍梨子问尊者满慈子：「贤者名何等？诸梵行人云何称贤者耶？」

Venerable Mahācunda replied, "Virtuous one! I am called 'Full'. My mother's name is 'Compassionate', so the virtuous ones call me 'Mahācunda'."

尊者满慈子答曰：「贤者！我号满也。我母名慈，故诸梵行人称我为满慈子。」

Venerable Sāriputta praised, "Excellent, excellent! The virtuous Mahācunda is a disciple of the Tathāgata. His wisdom, eloquence, and determination are clear and decisive. He is at ease and fearless, accomplished in self-mastery, and has attained great eloquence. He has attained the banner of immortality and has personally realized and accomplished the state of immortality. Because he is able to answer the profound questions asked of him. The virtuous Mahācunda, the virtuous ones have gained great benefit in being able to meet the virtuous Mahācunda and pay homage to him at any time. I too have gained great benefit in being able to meet him and pay homage to him at any time. The virtuous ones should wear the virtuous Mahācunda on their heads as a crown, for they have gained great benefit. I too have gained great benefit in being able to meet him and pay homage to him at any time."

尊者舍梨子叹曰：「善哉，善哉！贤者满慈子，为如来弟子，所作智辩聪明决定，安隐无畏，成就调御，逮大辩才，得甘露幢，于甘露界自作证成就游，以问贤者甚深义尽能报故。贤者满慈子！诸梵行人为得大利，得值贤者满慈子，随时往见，随时礼拜，我今亦得大利，随时往见，随时礼拜。诸梵行人应当萦衣顶上戴贤者满慈子，为得大利，我今亦得大利，随时往见，随时礼拜。」

Venerable Mahācunda asked Venerable Sāriputta, "Virtuous one! What is your name, and how are you called by the virtuous ones?"

尊者满慈子问尊者舍梨子：「贤者名何等，诸梵行人云何称贤者耶？」

Venerable Sāriputta replied, "Virtuous one! My name is Upatiṣya, and my mother is named Śāri, so the virtuous ones call me Śāriputra."

尊者舍梨子答曰：「贤者！我字优波鞮舍，我母名舍梨，故诸梵行人称我为舍梨子。」

Venerable Mahācunda praised, "I now discuss with the disciples of the World-Honored One and do not know, discuss with the second honored one and do not know, discuss with the Dharma general and do not know, discuss with the one who turns the Dharma wheel and the disciples who turn it and do not know. If I knew Venerable Sāriputta, I would not be able to answer a single sentence, let alone so many profound discussions! Excellent, excellent! Venerable Sāriputta! You are a disciple of the Tathāgata, with wisdom, eloquence, and determination. You are at ease, fearless, accomplished in self-control, attained great eloquence, and obtained the banner of immortality. You have personally realized and accomplished the state of immortality. Venerable Sāriputta! The virtuous ones have gained great benefits, being able to meet Venerable Sāriputta, to visit him from time to time, and to pay homage to him from time to time. I too have gained great benefits, being able to visit him from time to time and pay homage to him from time to time. The virtuous ones should wrap their robes around their heads and wear Venerable Sāriputta on top, for they have gained great benefits. I too have gained great benefits, being able to visit him from time to time and pay homage to him from time to time."

尊者满慈子叹曰：「我今与世尊弟子共论而不知，第二尊共论而不知，法将共论而不知，转法轮复转弟子共论而不知。若我知尊者舍梨子者，不能答一句，况复尔所深论？善哉，善哉！尊者舍梨子！为如来弟子，所作智辩聪明决定，安隐无畏、成就调御、逮大辩才、得甘露幢，于甘露界自作证成就游，以尊者甚深甚深问故。尊者舍梨子！诸梵行人为得大利，得值尊者舍梨子，随时往见，随时礼拜，我今亦得大利，随时往见，随时礼拜。诸梵行人应当萦衣顶上戴尊者舍梨子，为得大利，我今亦得大利，随时往见，随时礼拜。」

Thus the two virtuous ones praised each other and spoke to each other. Having praised each other, they joyfully accepted it and practiced it. They then rose from their seats and returned to their respective abodes.

如是二贤更相称说，更相赞善已，欢喜奉行，即从坐起，各还所止。
