MĀ 93

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 8 秽品

93. （九三）水净梵志经

Parallel to: MN 7 / SĀ 1185 (~) / SĀ² 2.98 (~) / EĀ 13.5

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was wandering in the Upavattana Deer Park near the Nairañjanā River, under the Ajapāla-nigrodha tree, shortly after attaining enlightenment.

一时，佛游欝鞞罗尼连然河岸，在阿耶惒罗尼拘类树下，初得道时。

Then, a certain water-purifying brahman, in the middle of the day, went to where the Buddha was. The World-Honored One, seeing from afar the water-purifying brahman coming, spoke to the bhikṣus because of the water-purifying brahman: "If there are twenty-one defilements in the mind, one will definitely go to an evil place and be reborn in hell. What are the twenty-one defilements? A defiled mind of wrong views, a defiled mind of unwholesome desires, a defiled mind of evil greed, a defiled mind of wrong teachings, a defiled mind of greed, a defiled mind of anger, a defiled mind of sleepiness, a defiled mind of agitation and remorse, a defiled mind of doubt, a defiled mind of anger and entanglement, a defiled mind of unwholesome bonds, a defiled mind of stinginess, a defiled mind of jealousy, a defiled mind of deceit and flattery, a defiled mind of flattery and deceit, a defiled mind of shamelessness, a defiled mind of lack of conscience, a defiled mind of pride, a defiled mind of great pride, a defiled mind of arrogant pride, and a defiled mind of heedlessness. If there are these twenty-one defilements in the mind, one will definitely go to an evil place and be reborn in hell. What are the twenty-one defilements? A defiled mind of wrong views, a defiled mind of unwholesome desires, a defiled mind of evil greed, a defiled mind of wrong teachings, a defiled mind of greed, a defiled mind of anger, a defiled mind of sleepiness, a defiled mind of agitation and remorse, a defiled mind of doubt, a defiled mind of anger and entanglement, a defiled mind of unwholesome bonds, a defiled mind of stinginess, a defiled mind of jealousy, a defiled mind of deceit and flattery, a defiled mind of flattery and deceit, a defiled mind of shamelessness, a defiled mind of lack of conscience, a defiled mind of pride, a defiled mind of great pride, a defiled mind of arrogant pride, and a defiled mind of heedlessness. If there are these twenty-one defilements in the mind, one will definitely go to an evil place and be reborn in hell.

于是，有一水净梵志，中后仿佯往诣佛所。世尊遥见水净梵志来，因水净梵志故，告诸比丘：「若有二十一秽污于心者，必至恶处，生地狱中。云何二十一秽？邪见心秽、非法欲心秽、恶贪心秽、邪法心秽、贪心秽、恚心秽、睡眠心秽、调悔心秽、疑惑心秽、瞋缠心秽、不语结心秽、悭心秽、嫉心秽、欺诳心秽、谀谄心秽、无慙心秽、无愧心秽、慢心秽、大慢心秽、慢慠心秽、放逸心秽。若有此二十一秽污于心者，必至恶处，生地狱中。犹垢腻衣持与染家，彼染家得，或以淳灰、或以澡豆、或以土渍极浣，令净此垢腻衣。染家虽治，或以淳灰、或以澡豆、或以土渍极浣令净，然此污衣故有秽色。如是，若有二十一秽污于心者，必至恶处，生地狱中。云何二十一秽？邪见心秽、非法欲心秽、恶贪心秽、邪法心秽、贪心秽、恚心秽、睡眠心秽、调悔心秽、疑惑心秽、瞋缠心秽、不语结心秽、悭心秽、嫉心秽、欺诳心秽、谀谄心秽、无慙心秽、无愧心秽、慢心秽、大慢心秽、慢慠心秽、放逸心秽。若有此二十一秽污于心者，必至恶处，生地狱中。

If there are twenty-one defilements that do not defile the mind, one will definitely go to a good place and be reborn in heaven. What are the twenty-one defilements? A pure mind of right views, a pure mind of wholesome desires, a pure mind of good greed, a pure mind of right teachings, a pure mind of non-greed, a pure mind of non-anger, a pure mind of non-sleepiness, a pure mind of non-agitation and remorse, a pure mind of non-doubt, a pure mind of non-anger and entanglement, a pure mind of non-unwholesome bonds, a pure mind of non-stinginess, a pure mind of non-jealousy, a pure mind of non-deceit and flattery, a pure mind of non-flattery and deceit, a pure mind of shame, a pure mind of conscience, a pure mind of non-pride, a pure mind of non-great pride, a pure mind of non-arrogant pride, and a pure mind of non-heedlessness. If there are these twenty-one defilements that do not defile the mind, one will definitely go to a good place and be reborn in heaven. It is like a white and pure pāṭalī robe given to a dyer. The dyer, having received it, uses either strong ash, soap nuts, or earth to wash it thoroughly to make it pure. Although the dyer treats this white and pure pāṭalī robe with either strong ash, soap nuts, or earth to wash it thoroughly to make it pure, this white and pure pāṭalī robe is already pure and becomes pure again. In the same way, if there are twenty-one defilements that do not defile the mind, one will definitely go to a good place and be reborn in heaven. What are the twenty-one defilements? A pure mind of right views, a pure mind of wholesome desires, a pure mind of good greed, a pure mind of right teachings, a pure mind of non-greed, a pure mind of non-anger, a pure mind of non-sleepiness, a pure mind of non-agitation and remorse, a pure mind of non-doubt, a pure mind of non-anger and entanglement, a pure mind of non-unwholesome bonds, a pure mind of non-stinginess, a pure mind of non-jealousy, a pure mind of non-deceit and flattery, a pure mind of non-flattery and deceit, a pure mind of shame, a pure mind of conscience, a pure mind of non-pride, a pure mind of non-great pride, a pure mind of non-arrogant pride, and a pure mind of non-heedlessness. If there are these twenty-one defilements that do not defile the mind, one will definitely go to a good place and

「若有二十一秽不污心者，必至善处，生于天上。云何二十一秽？邪见心秽、非法欲心秽、恶贪心秽、邪法心秽、贪心秽、恚心秽、睡眠心秽、调悔心秽、疑惑心秽、瞋缠心秽、不语心心秽、悭心秽、嫉心秽、欺诳心秽、谀谄心秽、无慙心秽、无愧心秽、慢心秽、大慢心秽、憍慠心秽、放逸心秽。若有此二十一秽不污心者，必至善处，生于天上。犹如白净波罗奈衣持与染家，彼染家得，或以淳灰、或以澡豆、或以土渍极浣令净；此白净波罗奈衣，染家虽治，或以淳灰、或以澡豆、或以土渍极浣令净，然此白净波罗奈衣本已净而复净。如是若有二十一秽不污心者，必至善处，生于天上。云何二十一秽？邪见心秽、非法欲心秽、恶贪心秽、邪法心秽、贪心秽、恚心秽、睡眠心秽、调悔心秽、疑惑心秽、瞋缠心秽、不语结心秽、悭心秽、嫉心秽、欺诳心秽、谀谄心秽、无慙心秽、无愧心秽、慢心秽、大慢心秽、憍慠心秽、放逸心秽。若有此二十一秽不污心者，必至善处，生于天上。

"If one knows that wrong views are defilements of the mind, one should know and then eliminate them. In the same way, unwholesome desires, evil greed, wrong teachings, greed, anger, sleepiness, agitation and remorse, doubt, anger and entanglement, unwholesome bonds, stinginess, jealousy, deceit and flattery, flattery and deceit, shamelessness, lack of conscience, pride, great pride, arrogant pride, and heedlessness. If one knows that heedlessness is a defilement of the mind, one should know and then eliminate it. One's mind is then filled with loving-kindness, pervading the ten directions, and one dwells in accomplishment. In the same way, one does so in the second, third, and fourth directions, the four intermediate directions, above and below, everywhere, with a mind filled with loving-kindness, without knots, resentment, anger, or disputes, extremely vast and great, well cultivated and immeasurable, pervading the entire world, dwelling in accomplishment. In the same way, with compassion, joy, and equanimity, without knots, resentment, anger, or disputes, extremely vast and great, well cultivated and immeasurable, pervading the entire world, dwelling in accomplishment. Brahmins! This is called washing the inner mind, not washing the outer body."

「若知邪见是心秽者，知已便断。如是非法欲心秽、恶贪心秽、邪法心秽、贪心秽、恚心秽、睡眠心秽、调悔心秽、疑惑心秽、瞋缠心秽、不语结心秽、悭心秽、嫉心秽、欺诳心秽、谀谄心秽、无慙心秽、无愧心秽、慢心秽、大慢心秽、憍慠心秽，若知放逸是心秽者，知已便断，彼心与慈俱，遍满十方成就游。如是二三四方、四维上下，普周一切，心] cf.［丽】与慈俱，无结、无怨、无恚、无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。如是，悲、喜心与舍俱，无结、无怨、无恚、无诤，极广甚大，无量善修，遍满一切世间成就游。梵志！是谓洗浴内心，非浴外身。」

At that time, the brahmin said to the World-Honored One, "Gautama! Please go to the river to bathe."

尔时，梵志语世尊曰：「瞿昙！可诣多水河浴。」

The World-Honored One asked, "Brahmin! If one goes to the river to bathe, what does one obtain?"

世尊问曰：「梵志！若诣多水河浴者，彼得何等？」

The brahmin replied, "Gautama! The river is a sign of purity, a sign of liberation, and a sign of merit in the world. Gautama! If one goes to the river to bathe, one will purify all evil."

梵志答曰：「瞿昙！彼多水河者，此是世间斋洁之相、度相、福相。瞿昙！若诣多水河浴者，彼则净除于一切恶。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse for the sake of the brahmin:

尔时，世尊为彼梵志而说颂曰：

Brahmin Sumada, if you enter the river, it is the play
of a fool, unable to purify black karma. Sumada, where do
you go to the spring? What is the meaning of the
river? When people do unwholesome karma, how can clean water help?
The pure one is without defilement, the pure one always speaks
precepts, the pure one has pure white karma, and always attains
pure practice. If you do not kill, always do not take
what is not given, speak the truth and not falsehood, and
always have right mindfulness and right knowledge, brahmin, study like this
all beings will be at ease. Brahmin, why do you return
home? The spring at home is not pure. Brahmin, you should
study, wash yourself with good dharmas. Why need you use filthy
water, just remove the dirt from your body." The brahmin said
to the Buddha, "I also have this thought, to wash myself
with good dharmas, why need I use filthy water." The brahmin
heard the Buddha's teaching, his mind greatly rejoiced, he immediately bowed
at the Buddha's feet, and took refuge in the Buddha, Dharma and Sangha.

「妙好首梵志，　　若入多水河，是愚常游戏，　　不能净黑业。好首何往泉，　　何义多水河，人作不善业，　　清水何所益？净者无垢秽，　　净者常说戒，净者清白业，　　常得清净行。若汝不杀生，　　常不与不取，真谛不妄语，　　常正念正知。梵志如是学，　　一切众生安，梵志何还家，　　家泉无所净。梵志汝当学，　　净洗以善法，何须弊恶水，　　但去身体垢。」梵志白佛曰：　　「我亦作是念，净洗以善法，　　何须弊恶水。」梵志闻佛教，　　心中大欢喜，即时礼佛足，　　归命佛法众。

The brahmin said, "World-Honored One! I have understood. Well-Gone One! I have understood. I now take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha of monks. I only wish the World-Honored One would accept me as a lay follower. From today onward, I take refuge for life, until the end of my life."

梵志白曰：「世尊！我已知。善逝！我已解。我今自归佛、法及比丘众，唯愿世尊受我为优婆塞，从今日始，终身自归，乃至命尽。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Sumada, the water-purifying brahmin, and the monks, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说如是。好首水净梵志及诸比丘，闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
