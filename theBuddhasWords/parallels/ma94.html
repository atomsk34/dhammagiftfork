MĀ 94

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 8 秽品

94. （九四）黑比丘经

Parallel to: AN 10.87

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Deer Mother's Hall in the Eastern Garden.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在东园鹿母堂。

At that time, the black monk, the son of the deer mother, was always fond of quarreling and went to where the Buddha was. The World-Honored One saw the black monk coming from afar. Because of the black monk, he told the monks: "There is a person who always likes to quarrel and does not praise stopping quarrels. If there is a person who always likes to quarrel and does not praise stopping quarrels, this Dharma is not enjoyable, not lovable, cannot cause love, cannot cause respect, cannot cause cultivation, cannot cause absorption, cannot cause the attainment of the state of a śramaṇa, cannot cause the attainment of single-mindedness, cannot cause the attainment of nirvāṇa.

是时，黑比丘鹿母子常憙鬪诤，往诣佛所。世尊遥见黑比丘来，因黑比丘故，告诸比丘：「或有一人常喜鬪诤，不称止诤。若有一人常喜鬪诤，不称止诤者，此法不可乐，不可爱喜，不能令爱念，不能令敬重，不能令修习，不能令摄持，不能令得沙门，不能令得一意，不能令得涅槃。

There is a person who has evil desires and does not praise stopping evil desires. If there is a person who has evil desires and does not praise stopping evil desires, this Dharma is not enjoyable, not lovable, cannot cause love, cannot cause respect, cannot cause cultivation, cannot cause absorption, cannot cause the attainment of the state of a śramaṇa, cannot cause the attainment of single-mindedness, cannot cause the attainment of nirvāṇa.

「或有一人恶欲，不称止恶欲。若有一人恶欲，不称止恶欲者，此法不可乐，不可爱憙，不能令爱念，不能令敬重，不能] cf.［丽】令修习，不能令摄持，不能令得沙门，不能令得一意，不能令得涅槃。

There is a person who violates the precepts, transgresses the precepts, is deficient in the precepts, has holes in the precepts, and defiles the precepts, and does not praise upholding the precepts. If there is a person who violates the precepts, transgresses the precepts, is deficient in the precepts, has holes in the precepts, defiles the precepts, and does not praise upholding the precepts, this Dharma is not enjoyable, not lovable, cannot cause love, cannot cause respect, cannot cause cultivation, cannot cause absorption, cannot cause the attainment of the state of a śramaṇa, cannot cause the attainment of single-mindedness, cannot cause the attainment of nirvāṇa.

「或有一人犯戒、越戒、缺戒、穿戒、污戒，不称持戒。若有一人犯戒、越戒、缺戒、穿戒、污戒、不称持戒者，此法不可乐，不可爱憙，不能令爱念、不能令敬重，不能令修习，不能令摄持，不能令得沙门，不能令得一意，不能令得涅槃。

There is a person who has the fetter of anger, the knot of not speaking, stinginess and jealousy, flattery, deceit and deception, and shamelessness and lack of conscience, and does not praise shame and conscience. If there is a person who has the fetter of anger, the knot of not speaking, stinginess and jealousy, flattery, deceit and deception, and shamelessness and lack of conscience, and does not praise shame and conscience, this Dharma is not enjoyable, not lovable, cannot cause love, cannot cause respect, cannot cause cultivation, cannot cause absorption, cannot cause the attainment of the state of a śramaṇa, cannot cause the attainment of single-mindedness, cannot cause the attainment of nirvāṇa.

「或有一人有瞋缠、有不语结、有悭嫉、有谀谄欺诳、有无慙无愧，不称慙愧。若有一人有瞋缠、有不语结、有悭嫉、有谀谄欺诳、有无慙无愧、不称慙愧者，此法不可乐，不可爱憙，不能令爱念，不能令敬重，不能令修习，不能令摄持，不能令得沙门，不能令得一意，不能令得涅槃。

There is a person who does not endure hardships for the sake of the various practices, and does not praise enduring hardships for the sake of the various practices. If there is a person who does not endure hardships for the sake of the various practices, and does not praise enduring hardships for the sake of the various practices, this Dharma is not enjoyable, not lovable, cannot cause love, cannot cause respect, cannot cause cultivation, cannot cause absorption, cannot cause the attainment of the state of a śramaṇa, cannot cause the attainment of single-mindedness, cannot cause the attainment of nirvāṇa.

「或有一人不经劳诸梵行，不称经劳诸梵行。若有一人不经劳诸梵行，不称经劳诸梵行者，此法不可乐，不可爱憙，不能令爱念，不能令敬重，不能令修习，不能令摄持，不能令得沙门，不能令得一意，不能令得涅槃。

There is a person who does not contemplate the various dharmas, and does not praise contemplating the various dharmas. If there is a person who does not contemplate the various dharmas, and does not praise contemplating the various dharmas, this Dharma is not enjoyable, not lovable, cannot cause love, cannot cause respect, cannot cause cultivation, cannot cause absorption, cannot cause the attainment of the state of a śramaṇa, cannot cause the attainment of single-mindedness, cannot cause the attainment of nirvāṇa.

「或有一人不观诸法，不称观诸法。若有一人不观诸法，不称观诸法者，此法不可乐，不可爱憙，不能令爱念，不能令敬重，不能令修习，不能令摄持，不能令得沙门，不能令得一意，不能令得涅槃。

There is a person who does not sit in meditation, and does not praise sitting in meditation. If there is a person who does not sit in meditation, and does not praise sitting in meditation, this Dharma is not enjoyable, not lovable, cannot cause love, cannot cause respect, cannot cause cultivation, cannot cause absorption, cannot cause the attainment of the state of a śramaṇa, cannot cause the attainment of single-mindedness, cannot cause the attainment of nirvāṇa.

「或有一人不宴坐，不称宴坐。若有一人不宴坐，不称宴坐者，此法不可乐，不可爱憙，不能令爱念，不能令敬重，不能令修习，不能令摄持，不能令得沙门，不能令得一意，不能令得涅槃。此人虽作是念：『令诸梵行者供养、恭敬、礼事于我。』然诸梵行者不供养、恭敬、礼事于彼。所以者何？彼人有此无量恶法，因彼有此无量恶法故，令诸梵行者不供养、恭敬、礼事于彼。犹如恶马系在枥养，虽作是念：『令人系我著安隐处，与我好饮食、好看视我。』然人不系著安隐处，不与好饮食、不好看视。所以者何？彼马有恶法，谓极麁弊、不温良故，令人不系著安隐处，不与好饮食、不好看视。如是，此人虽作是念：『令诸梵行者供养、恭敬、礼事于我。』然诸梵行者不供养、恭敬、礼事于彼。所以者何？彼人有此无量恶法，因彼有此无量恶法故，令诸梵行者不供养、恭敬、礼事于彼。

There is a person who does not like disputes and praises ending disputes. If there is a person who does not like disputes and praises ending disputes, this Dharma is enjoyable, lovable, and delightful, can cause love, can cause respect, can cause cultivation, can cause absorption, can cause the attainment of the state of a śramaṇa, can cause the attainment of single-mindedness, can cause the attainment of nirvāṇa.

「或有一人不憙鬪诤，称誉止诤。若有一人不憙鬪诤，称誉止诤者，此法可乐、可爱、可憙，能令爱念，能令敬重，能令修习，能令摄持，能令得沙门，能令得一意，能令得涅槃。

There is a person who does not have evil desires and praises ending evil desires. If there is a person who does not have evil desires and praises ending evil desires, this Dharma is enjoyable, lovable, and delightful, can cause love, can cause respect, can cause cultivation, can cause absorption, can cause the attainment of the state of a śramaṇa, can cause the attainment of single-mindedness, can cause the attainment of nirvāṇa.

「或有一人不恶欲，称誉止恶欲。若有一人不恶欲，称誉止恶欲者，此法可乐、可爱、可憙，能令爱念，能令敬重，能令修习，能令摄持，能令得沙门，能令得一意，能令得涅槃。

There is a person who does not violate the precepts, does not transgress the precepts, does not have holes in the precepts, does not have cracks in the precepts, and does not defile the precepts, and praises upholding the precepts. If there is a person who does not violate the precepts, does not transgress the precepts, does not have holes in the precepts, does not have cracks in the precepts, and does not defile the precepts, and praises upholding the precepts, this Dharma is enjoyable, lovable, and delightful, can cause love, can cause respect, can cause cultivation, can cause absorption, can cause the attainment of the state of a śramaṇa, can cause the attainment of single-mindedness, can cause the attainment of nirvāṇa.

「或有一人不犯戒、不越戒、不缺戒、不穿戒、不污戒，称誉持戒。若有一人不犯戒、不越戒、不缺戒、不穿戒、不污戒，称誉持戒者，此法可乐、可爱、可喜，能令爱念，能令敬重，能令修习，能令摄持，能令得沙门、能令得一意，能令得涅槃。

There is a person who is without the fetter of anger, without the knot of not speaking, without stinginess and jealousy, without flattery, deceit, and deception, without shamelessness and disregard, and praises shame and conscience. If there is a person who is without the fetter of anger, without the knot of not speaking, without stinginess and jealousy, without flattery, deceit, and deception, without shamelessness and disregard, and praises shame and conscience, this Dharma is enjoyable, lovable, and delightful, can cause love, can cause respect, can cause cultivation, can cause absorption, can cause the attainment of the state of a śramaṇa, can cause the attainment of single-mindedness, can cause the attainment of nirvāṇa.

「或有一人无瞋缠、无不语结、无悭嫉、无谀谄欺诳、无无慙无愧，称誉慙愧。若有一人无瞋缠、无不语结、无悭嫉、无谀谄欺诳、无无慙无愧，称誉慙愧者，此法可乐、可爱、可喜，能令爱念，能令敬重，能令修习，能令摄持，能令得沙门，能令得一意，能令得涅槃。

There is a person who has practiced with all the virtuous practices, and praises practicing with all the virtuous practices. If there is a person who has practiced with all the virtuous practices, and praises practicing with all the virtuous practices, this Dharma is enjoyable, lovable, and delightful, can cause love, can cause respect, can cause cultivation, can cause absorption, can cause the attainment of the state of a śramaṇa, can cause the attainment of single-mindedness, can cause the attainment of nirvāṇa.

「或有一人经劳诸梵行，称誉经劳诸梵行。若有一人经劳诸梵行，称誉经劳诸梵行者，此法可乐、可爱、可喜，能令爱念，能令敬重，能令修习，能令摄持，能令得沙门，能令得一意，能令得涅槃。

There is a person who contemplates all dharmas, and praises contemplating all dharmas. If there is a person who contemplates all dharmas, and praises contemplating all dharmas, this Dharma is enjoyable, lovable, and delightful, can cause love, can cause respect, can cause cultivation, can cause absorption, can cause the attainment of the state of a śramaṇa, can cause the attainment of single-mindedness, can cause the attainment of nirvāṇa.

「或有一人观诸法，称誉观诸法。若有一人观诸法，称誉观诸法者，此法可乐、可爱、可憙，能令爱念，能令敬重，能令修习，能令摄持，能令得沙门，能令得一意，能令得涅槃。

There is a person who sits in meditation, and praises sitting in meditation. If there is a person who sits in meditation, and praises sitting in meditation, this Dharma is enjoyable, lovable, and delightful, can cause love, can cause respect, can cause cultivation, can cause absorption, can cause the attainment of the state of a śramaṇa, can cause the attainment of single-mindedness, can cause the attainment of nirvāṇa.

「或有一人宴坐，称誉宴坐。若有一人宴坐，称誉宴坐者，此法可乐、可爱、可憙，能令爱念，能令敬重，能令修习，能令摄持，能令得沙门，能令得一意，能令得涅槃。此人虽不作是念：『令诸梵行者供养、恭敬、礼事于我。』然诸梵行者供养、恭敬、礼事于彼。所以者何？彼人有此无量善法，因彼有此无量善法故，令诸梵行者供养、恭敬、礼事于彼。犹如良马系在枥养，虽不作是念：『令人系我著安隐处，与我好饮食、好看视我。』然人系彼著安隐处，与好饮食、好看视之。所以者何？彼马有善法，谓软调好、极温良故，令人系著于安隐处，与好饮食、好看视之。如是，此人虽不作是念：『令诸梵行者供养、恭敬、礼事于我。』然诸梵行者供养、恭敬、礼事于彼。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
