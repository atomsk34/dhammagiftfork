MĀ 95

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 8 秽品

95. （九五）住法经

Parallel to: AN 10.17 (~) / AN 10.18 (~) / AN 10.53

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One addressed the bhikṣus, saying: "I say that those who retreat from the good Dharma do not abide in it and do not increase it. I say that those who abide in the good Dharma do not retreat from it and do not increase it. I say that those who increase the good Dharma do not retreat from it and do not abide in it. How do those who retreat from the good Dharma not abide in it and not increase it? Bhikṣus, if one has faith, precepts, learning, generosity, wisdom, eloquence, the teachings, and what one has attained, but that person retreats from this Dharma, does not abide in it, and does not increase it, this is called retreating from the good Dharma, not abiding in it, and not increasing it. How do those who abide in the good Dharma not retreat from it and not increase it? Bhikṣus, if one has faith, precepts, learning, generosity, wisdom, eloquence, the teachings, and what one has attained, and that person abides in this Dharma without retreating and without increasing it, this is called abiding in the good Dharma, not retreating from it, and not increasing it. How do those who increase the good Dharma not retreat from it and not abide in it? Bhikṣus, if one has faith, precepts, learning, generosity, wisdom, eloquence, the teachings, and what one has attained, and that person increases this Dharma without retreating and without abiding in it, this is called increasing the good Dharma, not retreating from it, and not abiding in it.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「我说退善法不住、不增，我说住善法不退、不增，我说增善法不退、不住。云何退善法不住、不增？比丘者，若有笃信、禁戒、博闻、布施、智慧、辩才，阿含及其所得，彼人于此法退、不住、不增，是谓退善法不住、不增。云何住善法不退、不增？比丘者，若有笃信、禁戒、博闻、布施、智慧、辩才、阿含及其所得，彼人于此法住不退、不增，是谓住善法不退、不增。云何增善法不退、不住？比丘者，若有笃信、禁戒、博闻、布施、智慧、辩才、阿含及其所得，彼人于此法增不退、不住，是谓增善法不退、不住。

Bhikṣus, if a bhikṣu contemplates in this way, it will be of great benefit: 'Do I have much mental agitation or not? Do I have much ill-will or not? Do I have much sloth and torpor or not? Do I have much restlessness and remorse or not? Do I have much doubt or not? Do I have much bodily conflict or not? Do I have much mental defilement or not? Do I have much faith or not? Do I have much diligence or not? Do I have much mindfulness or not? Do I have much concentration or not? Do I have much bad wisdom or not?'

「比丘者作如是观，必多所饶益：『我为多行增伺，为多行无增伺？我为多行瞋恚心，为多行无瞋恚心？我为多行睡眠缠，为多行无睡眠缠？我为多行调贡高，为多行无调贡高？我为多行疑惑，为多行无疑惑？我为多行身诤，为多行无身诤？我为多行秽污心，为多行无秽污心？我为多行信，为多行不信？我为多行精进，为多行懈怠？我为多行念，为多行无念？我为多行定，为多行无定？我为多行恶慧，为多行无恶慧？』

If a bhikṣu contemplates and knows that he has much mental agitation, ill-will, sloth and torpor, restlessness and remorse, doubt, bodily conflict, mental defilement, lack of faith, laziness, lack of mindfulness, lack of concentration, and bad wisdom, then that bhikṣu, wishing to eliminate these evil and unwholesome dharmas, will quickly seek means, study with utmost diligence, right mindfulness, right knowledge, and endure without retreating. It is like a person whose head or clothes are on fire, urgently seeking means to save their head or clothes. In the same way, a bhikṣu, wishing to eliminate these evil and unwholesome dharmas, will quickly seek means, study with utmost diligence, right mindfulness, right knowledge, and endure without retreating.

「若比丘观时，则知我多行增伺、瞋恚心、睡眠缠、调贡高、疑惑、身诤、秽污心、不信、懈怠、无念、无定、多行恶慧者，彼比丘欲灭此恶不善法故，便以速求方便，学极精勤，正念正智，忍不令退。犹人为火烧头、烧衣，急求方便救头、救衣。如是比丘欲灭此恶不善法故，便以速求方便，学极精勤，正念正智，忍不令退。

If a bhikṣu contemplates and knows that he has much mental non-agitation, non-ill-will, non-sloth and torpor, non-restlessness and remorse, non-doubt, non-bodily conflict, non-mental defilement, faith, diligence, mindfulness, concentration, and non-bad wisdom, then that bhikṣu, wishing to abide in these wholesome dharmas, not forgetting, not regressing, and cultivating and spreading them, will quickly seek means, study with utmost diligence, right mindfulness, right knowledge, and endure without retreating. It is like a person whose head or clothes are on fire, urgently seeking means to save their head or clothes. In the same way, a bhikṣu, wishing to abide in these wholesome dharmas, not forgetting, not regressing, and cultivating and spreading them, will quickly seek means, study with utmost diligence, right mindfulness, right knowledge, and endure without retreating.

「若比丘观时，则知我多行无贪伺、] cf.［丽】无瞋恚心、无睡眠缠、无调贡高、无疑惑、无身诤、无秽污心、有信、有进、有念、有定、多行无恶慧者，彼比丘欲住此善法，不忘、不退修行广布故，便以速求方便，学极精勤，正念正智，忍不令退。犹人为火烧头、烧衣，急求方便救头、救衣。如是比丘欲住此善法，不忘、不退修行广布故，便以速求方便，学极精勤，正念正智，忍不令退。」

The Buddha spoke thus. Those bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说如是。彼诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
