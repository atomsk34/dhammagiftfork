MĀ 96

Madhyamāgama 中阿含经

Chapter 8 秽品

96. （九六）无经

Parallel to: AN 10.55

I have heard thus:

我闻如是：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Śrāvastī, staying at the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma.

一时，佛游舍卫国，在胜林给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra addressed the bhikṣus, saying: "Virtuous ones! If there are bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs who have not heard the Dharma cannot hear it, and those who have heard it forget it. If there are those who have practiced the Dharma in the past, widely recited and studied it, and understood it through wisdom, they no longer remember it and know it but do not know it. Virtuous ones! This is called the decline of the pure Dharma for bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs.

尔时，尊者舍梨子告诸比丘：「诸贤！若有比丘、比丘尼未闻法者不得闻，已闻法者便忘失，若使有法本所修行，广布诵习，慧之所解，彼不复忆，知而不知。诸贤！是谓比丘、比丘尼净法衰退。

Virtuous ones! If there are bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs who have not heard the Dharma can hear it, and those who have heard it do not forget it. If there are those who have practiced the Dharma in the past, widely recited and studied it, and understood it through wisdom, they always remember it and know it. This is called the increase of the pure Dharma for bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs.

「诸贤！若有比丘、比丘尼未闻法者便得闻，已闻法者不忘失，若使有法本所修行，广布诵习，慧之所解，彼常忆念，知而复知。是谓比丘、比丘尼净法转增。

Virtuous ones! A bhikṣu should contemplate thus: 'Do I have increased craving or not? Do I have a mind of anger or not? Do I have the fetter of sleepiness or not? Do I have the pride of self-aggrandizement or not? Do I have doubt or not? Do I have bodily conflict or not? Do I have a defiled mind or not? Do I have faith or not? Do I have diligence or not? Do I have mindfulness or not? Do I have concentration or not? Do I have evil wisdom or not?'

「诸贤！比丘者当作如是观：『我为有增伺，为无有增伺？我为有瞋恚心，为无有瞋恚心？我为有睡眠缠，为无有睡眠缠？我为有调贡高，为无有调贡高？我为有疑惑，为无有疑惑？我为有身诤，为无有身诤？我为有秽污心，为无有秽污心？我为有信，为无有信？我为有进，为无有进？我为有念，为无有念？我为有定，为无有定？我为有恶慧，为无有恶慧？』

Virtuous ones! If a bhikṣu contemplates and knows that he has increased craving, a mind of anger, the fetter of sleepiness, the pride of self-aggrandizement, doubt, bodily conflict, a defiled mind, no faith, no diligence, no mindfulness, no concentration, and evil wisdom, then, virtuous ones! That bhikṣu, wishing to eliminate these evil and unwholesome dharmas, should quickly seek means, study with utmost diligence, with right mindfulness and right knowledge, and endure without retreating. Virtuous ones! It is like a person whose head or clothes are on fire, urgently seeking means to save their head or clothes. Virtuous ones! In this way, a bhikṣu, wishing to eliminate these evil and unwholesome dharmas, should quickly seek means, study with utmost diligence, with right mindfulness and right knowledge, and endure without retreating.

「诸贤！若比丘观时，则知我有增伺、有瞋恚心、有睡眠缠、有调贡高、有疑惑、有身诤、有秽污心，无信、无进、无念、无定，有恶慧者。诸贤！彼比丘欲灭此恶不善法故，便以速求方便，学极精勤，正念正智，忍不令退。诸贤！犹人为火烧头、烧衣，急求方便救头、救衣。诸贤！如是比丘欲灭此恶不善法故，便以速求方便，学极精勤，正念正智，忍不令退。

Virtuous ones! If a bhikṣu contemplates and knows that he has no increased craving, no mind of anger, no fetter of sleepiness, no pride of self-aggrandizement, no doubt, no bodily conflict, no defiled mind, faith, diligence, mindfulness, concentration, and no evil wisdom, then, virtuous ones! That bhikṣu, wishing to abide in these wholesome dharmas, not forgetting or regressing, and to practice and spread them, should quickly seek means, study with utmost diligence, with right mindfulness and right knowledge, and endure without retreating. It is like a person whose head or clothes are on fire, urgently seeking means to save their head or clothes. Virtuous ones! In this way, a bhikṣu, wishing to abide in these wholesome dharmas, not forgetting or regressing, and to practice and spread them, should quickly seek means, study with utmost diligence, with right mindfulness and right knowledge, and endure without retreating.

「诸贤！若比丘观时，则知我无增伺、无瞋恚心、无睡眠缠、无调贡高、无有疑惑、无有身诤、无秽污心，有信、有进、有念、有定、无恶慧者，彼比丘欲住此善法，不忘不退，修行广布故，便以速求方便，学极精勤，正念正智，忍不令退。犹人为火烧头、烧衣，急求方便救头、救衣。诸贤！如是比丘欲住此善法，不忘不退，修行广布故，便以速求方便，学极精勤，正念正智，忍不令退。」

The Venerable Sāriputta spoke thus. The monks, having heard what the Venerable Sāriputta said, were delighted and remembered it well.

尊者舍梨子所说如是，彼诸比丘闻尊者舍梨子所说，欢喜奉行。

The tenth sūtra is completed.

无经第十竟
