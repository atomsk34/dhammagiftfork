SĀ² 2.101

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 101（一〇一）

Parallel to: SN 6.2 / AN 4.21 / SĀ 1188

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was in the village of Uļūka, on the banks of the Nairañjanā River, under the Bodhi tree, not long after attaining Buddhahood. At that time, the World-Honored One was sitting alone in meditation, and had this thought: "If people are disrespectful, do not obey their elders, do not accept instruction, are not afraid of anything, and indulge themselves, they will lose all benefit and be bound by suffering. If people are filial, serve their elders, respect and fear them, follow them without going against them, they will fulfill their wishes and gain great benefit. If they do so, they will be at ease in all they do." He further thought: "In the entire world, whether gods, humans, the world of gods, the world of humans, the world of demons, the world of Brahmā, śramaṇas, or brahmins, among all the beings in the world, if there is anyone with superior morality, concentration, wisdom, liberation, or knowledge and insight of liberation than me, I should approach them, rely on them, make offerings to them, and respect them." Having thoroughly observed, he did not see anyone in the world, whether gods, humans, demons, Brahmā, śramaṇas, or brahmins, who was superior to him in morality, concentration, wisdom, liberation, or knowledge and insight of liberation, whom he could rely on. He further thought: "The Dharma I have realized, I should now approach, make offerings to, respect, and sincerely revere. Because all the Buddhas of the past have all approached, relied on, made offerings to, respected, and revered this Dharma. The Buddhas of the future and present will also approach, rely on, make offerings to, and respect this Dharma, and give rise to a mind of reverence. I should now also be like the Buddhas of the past, future, and present, approaching, relying on, making offerings to, respecting, and revering the Dharma."

一时，佛在优楼频螺聚落，泥连河岸菩提树下，成佛未久。尔时，世尊独坐思惟，而作是念：「夫人无敬心，不能恭顺于其尊长，不受教诲，无所畏惮，纵情自逸，永失义利。若如是者，众苦缠集。若人孝事尊长，敬养畏慎，随顺不逆，所愿满足，得大义利。若如是者，触事安乐。」复作是念：「一切世间，若天、若人，若天世界、若人世界、若魔世界、若梵世界、沙门、婆罗门，一切世间有生类中，若有戒、定、慧、解脱、解脱知见胜于我者，我当亲近，依止于彼，供养恭敬。遍观察已，都不见于世间人、天、魔、梵、沙门、婆罗门，一切世间有胜于我戒、定、慧、解脱、解脱知见，为我依止。」复作是念：「我所觉法，我今应当亲近、供养、恭敬、诚心、尊重。何以故？过去诸佛，一切皆悉亲近、依止、供养、恭敬、尊重斯法，未来、现在诸佛，亦复亲近、依止斯法，供养、恭敬，生尊重心。我今亦当如过去、未来、现在诸佛，亲近、依止、供养、恭敬、尊重于法。」

At that time, the Lord of the Brahmas, the Heavenly King, knew from afar that the World-Honored One was in the village of Uļūka, on the banks of the Nairañjanā River, under the Bodhi tree. He thought: "Observing the world, whether gods, humans, demons, Brahmā, śramaṇas, or brahmins, all beings, if there is anyone superior to me in morality, concentration, wisdom, liberation, or knowledge and insight of liberation, I should rely on them. But I do not see anyone who can surpass me. Moreover, observing the Buddhas of the past, future, and present, they all have approached, relied on, made offerings to, respected, and given rise to a mind of reverence for the Dharma. I should now also follow the Buddhas of the three times and do what is appropriate, approaching, relying on, making offerings to, respecting, and revering the Dharma." At that time, the Lord of the Brahmas further thought: "I should disappear from here and go to where the Buddha is." At that time, the Lord of the Brahmas, in the time it takes a strong man to bend and stretch his arm, came to where the Buddha was. He said to the Buddha: "World-Honored One! It is indeed as you have thought, it is indeed as you have thought." He then spoke a verse:

尔时，梵主天王遥知世尊在优楼频螺聚落，泥连河岸菩提树下而作是念：「观察世间，若天、若人、若魔、若梵、沙门、婆罗门，一切生类，若有胜我戒、定、慧、解脱、解脱知见者，我当依止，然都不见有能胜我者。又复观察过去、未来、现在诸佛，悉皆亲近、依止于法，供养、恭敬，生尊重心，我今亦当随三世佛之所，应作亲近、依止、供养、恭敬、尊重于法。」时，梵主天复作是念：「我当从此处没，往到佛所。」时，梵主天譬如壮士屈申臂顷，来至佛所。白佛言：「世尊！实如所念，诚如所念。」即说偈言：

The Tathāgatas of the past and present, and all the Buddhas of the future, these Perfectly
Enlightened Ones who can remove afflictions, all rely on the Dharma as their teacher. Approaching the
Dharma, relying on it and abiding in it, this is the Dharma of the Buddhas of
the three times. Therefore, those who wish to honor themselves should first respect and honor that
Dharma. One should remember the teachings of the Buddha and respect and make offerings to the unsurpassed Dharma.

「过去现在诸如来，　　未来世中一切佛，是诸正觉能除恼，　　一切皆依法为师。亲近于法依止住，　　斯是三世诸佛法，是故欲尊于己者，　　应先尊重敬彼法。宜当忆念佛所教，　　尊重供养无上法。」

At that time, the Brahma King praised the World-Honored One, deeply rejoicing, paid homage, and departed.

尔时，梵王赞叹世尊，深生随喜，作礼而去。
