SĀ² 2.107

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 107（一〇七）

Parallel to: SN 6.6 / SĀ 1194

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, two gods, named Lesser Excellent Good Closure Brahmā and Lesser Excellent Light Brahmā, wanted to come to see the Buddha. At that time, the Brahmā of the Pure Abodes saw these two Brahmas and asked them, "Where do you want to go?" The two Brahmas replied, "We want to go to see the World-Honored One to pay respects and make offerings." At that time, the Brahmā of the Pure Abodes spoke a verse,

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。时，有二天，一名小胜善闭梵，二名小胜光梵，欲来诣佛。时，婆迦梵见此二梵，即问之曰：「欲何所至？」二梵答言：「我等欲往诣世尊所，问讯礼敬」。时，婆迦梵即说偈言：

The four Brahmas are called cranes and sparrows, the three Brahmas are
called gold, and the seventy-two hundred and fifty are called the rest
of the Brahmas. Look at my golden color, shining brightly, with all
the radiance and light, outshining the Brahmas in heaven. Why do you
not look at me, but want to go to where the Buddha is?

「四梵字鹳雀，　　三梵名为金，七十二五百，　　名曰为余毘。汝观我金色，　　赫然而明盛，所有威光明，　　晖光蔽梵天，云何不观我，　　乃欲诣佛所？」

At that time, the two Brahmas replied in verse:

尔时，二梵以偈答言：

You now have a little light that outshines the Brahmas in
heaven. Know that this light and color all have various faults
The wise who attain liberation do not delight in this light and color.

「汝今有少光，　　映蔽于梵天，当知此光色，　　皆有诸过患，明智得解脱，　　不乐斯光色。」

At that time, after the two Brahmas had spoken this verse, they came to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. The two Brahmas said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! We wanted to come and see the Buddha. At that time, Brahma-king Prajāpati asked us, 'Where do you wish to go?' We replied, 'We wish to go to where the Buddha is.' At that time, Brahma-king Prajāpati spoke this verse:

尔时，二梵说是偈已，来诣佛所，顶礼佛足，在一面坐。二梵白佛言：「世尊！我等欲来见佛。时，婆迦梵因问我言：『欲何所至？』我等答言：『欲诣佛所。』时，婆迦梵说此偈言：

The four Brahmas are called cranes, the three Brahmas are called
gold, the seventy-two and five hundred are called the others. Look
at my golden color, blazing and greatly radiant. The light of
my body outshines the Brahmas in heaven. Why do you not
look at me, but wish to go to where the Buddha is?

「『四梵名鹳雀，　　三梵名为金，七十二五百，　　名曰为余毘。汝观我金色，　　赫然大炽盛，所有身光明，　　晖光蔽梵天，云何不观我，　　乃欲诣佛所？』

"We then replied in verse:"

「我等即便说偈答言：

You now have a little light that outshines the Brahmas in
heaven. Know that this light and color all have faults. The
wise attain liberation and do not delight in this light and color.

「『汝今有少光，　　映蔽于梵天，当知此光色，　　皆有诸过患，智者得解脱，　　不乐斯光色。』」

The Buddha said, "Brahma Heaven, it is indeed so, indeed so. Although the Brahmas have a little light and color that outshines the Brahmas in heaven, know that the light and color all have faults. The wise understand and should not delight in this."

佛言：「梵天！实尔，实尔。彼梵虽复少有光色，映蔽梵天，当知光色皆有过患，智者解了，不应乐此。」

The Buddha taught the Dharma to the two Brahmas, showing, teaching, benefiting, and delighting them. The two Brahmas heard the Dharma, rejoiced, bowed their heads, and returned to their heavenly palaces.

佛为二梵种] cf.【丽】种说法，示教利喜。二梵闻法，欢喜顶礼，还于天宫。
