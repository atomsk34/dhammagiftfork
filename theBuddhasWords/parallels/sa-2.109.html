SĀ² 2.109

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 109（一〇九）

Parallel to: SN 6.5 / SĀ 1196

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, a brahman had a great wrong view, saying, "I have never seen anyone born in my palace here, let alone surpassing me."

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。时，有一梵起大邪见，而作是言：「我此处常不见有能生于我宫，况复有能过于我上者？」

At that time, the World-Honored One entered samādhi, disappeared from Jambudvīpa, and appeared sitting in the sky above the Brahma-top. Venerable Ānanda, with his pure divine eye, looked for where the World-Honored One had gone and knew that the Tathāgata was sitting in the sky above the Brahma-top. Then Ānanda also entered that samādhi, disappeared from here, appeared above the Brahma-top, below the Tathāgata, in the eastern direction.

尔时，世尊入于三昧，从阎浮提没，现于梵顶虚空中坐。尊者憍陈如以净天眼观于世尊为至何处？即知如来在梵顶上虚空中坐。时，憍陈如亦入是定，于此而没，现梵顶上，处如来下，在于东面。

At that time, Venerable Mahākāśyapa, with his pure divine eye, looked for where the Tathāgata had gone and knew that the World-Honored One was above the Brahma-top. He also entered that samādhi, disappeared from here, appeared below the Tathāgata above the Brahma-top, in the southern direction.

时，尊者摩诃迦叶以净天眼观于如来为至何处？寻知世尊在梵顶上，复入此定，于此处没，在如来下，现梵顶上，在于南面。

Venerable Maudgalyāyana, with his pure divine eye, looked for where the Tathāgata had gone and knew that the World-Honored One was above the Brahma-top. He also entered that samādhi, disappeared from here, appeared below the Tathāgata above the Brahma-top, in the western direction.

尊者目连以净天眼，观于如来为至何处？寻知世尊在梵顶上，即入是定，于此处没，现梵顶上，处如来下，现于西面。

At that time, Venerable Aniruddha also looked with his pure divine eye for where the Tathāgata had gone and knew that the World-Honored One was above the Brahma-top. He also entered that samādhi, disappeared from here, appeared below the Tathāgata above the Brahma-top, in the northern direction.

时，尊者阿那律复以净天眼观察如来为至何处？寻知世尊在梵顶上，亦入是定，于此处没，现梵顶上，处如来下，在于北面。

At that time, the World-Honored One said to Brahma, "Have you given up what you saw before?" He further said to Brahma, "You previously thought, 'I do not see anyone who can give birth to my palace, let alone surpass it.' Now, look at these heavenly bodies, their appearance and radiance, are they not superior to yours?" Brahma said to the Buddha, "Yes, I have seen it. Now, seeing this, their radiance is something I have never seen before. Now, seeing this, their radiance is truly extraordinary. From now on, I dare not say that this place is permanent, unchanging, and eternal."

尔时，世尊告梵天言：「汝本所见，为舍已不？」复告梵天：「汝本心念：『我不见有能生我宫者，况能出过？』汝今试观此等天身，容貌光明，胜汝已不？」时，梵白佛：「唯然！已见。而今见之，斯等光明，昔所不见，而今见之，斯等光明，真为殊胜。自今已后，更不敢言此处常恒无有变易。」

The Buddha told Brahma, "This place is impermanent, empty, and not self-controlled." The Buddha taught the Dharma for Brahma in various ways, showing, teaching, benefiting, and delighting him. He entered this samādhi, disappeared from there, and returned to Jetavana. Venerable Kāśyapa, Mahākāśyapa, Aniruddha, and others also taught the Dharma for Brahma in various ways, showing, teaching, benefiting, and delighting him. They also entered this samādhi, disappeared from there, and returned to Jetavana; only Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana remained sitting there.

佛告梵天：「此处无常，空不自在。」佛为彼梵种种说法，示教利喜，入如是三昧，从彼梵没，还于祇洹。尊者憍陈如、摩诃迦叶、阿那律等，亦为彼梵种种说法，示教利喜，亦入是定，从彼处没，还于祇洹；唯尊者大目犍连在彼而坐。

At that time, the Brahma asked Maudgalyāyana, "Does the World-Honored One's disciple have anyone with great power and supernatural feet like you?" Maudgalyāyana replied, "The other śrāvakas also have such power and supernatural feet." Venerable Maudgalyāyana then spoke a verse:

尔时，彼梵问目连言：「世尊弟子颇有如汝有大威德、神足者不？」目连答言：「诸余声闻亦有如是威德、神足。」尊者目连即说偈言：

The great arhats of the Muni's disciples have great power and the three insights. They
have exhausted all taints and know the minds of others. They can manifest supernatural transformations
for sentient beings. There are many such śrāvakas. Therefore, you should now pay respect to them.

「牟尼弟子大罗汉，　　有大威德具三明，得尽诸漏知他心，　　能现神变化群生，如是声闻甚众多，　　是故汝今宜恭敬。」

At that time, after Venerable Maudgalyāyana spoke this verse, he taught the Dharma in various ways, benefiting and delighting them. He also entered this samādhi. He then disappeared from that Brahma realm and returned to Jetavana.

时，尊者目连说是偈已，种种说法，示教利喜，亦入是定，从彼梵没，还于祇洹。
