SĀ² 2.112

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 112（一一二）

Parallel to: SN 16.4 / SĀ 1137

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, the Buddha told the bhikṣus, "If a bhikṣu is about to go to a donor's house, he should first think like this: 'If there is anything to give, may they quickly give it to me, not slowly. May they give it with sincerity, not insincerely. May I receive much, not little. May they give me fine things, not coarse things.' If he thinks like this with a determined mind, he will go to the donor's house. Even if the donor gives, they may not give with sincerity, or respectfully. Even if they give food and drink, it may not be abundant. They may give coarse things, not fine things. Even if they give, it may be slow, not quick. Because this bhikṣu's wishes are not fulfilled, he may feel ashamed, sorrowful, and have a mind of loss. This bhikṣu should think like this: 'When I go to the donor's house, it is not my own home. How can I expect to have my wishes fulfilled? Why do I think that the donor should give quickly, not slowly, and give fine things, not coarse things?' If he thinks like this, even if he receives nothing, his mind will not regret or resent, and he will be free from increase or decrease, without any resentment. Even if the donor gives little, not sincerely, slowly, not quickly, and gives coarse things, not fine things, this bhikṣu's mind will not resent or be ashamed, and will be free from increase or decrease. Kāśyapa bhikṣu should think like this when going to a donor's house: 'It is not my own home. How can I expect to have my wishes fulfilled, hoping that the donor will give sincerely, quickly, not slowly, and give fine things, not coarse things?' Kāśyapa bhikṣu should think like this when going to a donor's house. Even if he receives nothing, he will not be ashamed or have a mind of loss. Therefore, bhikṣus should think like this when going to others' homes. They should not think, 'Give to me quickly, and give fine things.' Therefore, you should train like this: like Kāśyapa bhikṣu going to a donor's house."

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，佛告诸比丘：「若有比丘将欲往诣于檀越家，先作是念：『若有所施，当速与我，勿令迟晚，至心施我，莫不至诚，愿使多得，勿令寡少。惠我精细，勿得麁澁。』若作是念，决定意者，往檀越家。檀越虽与，不至心施，不恭敬与，虽施饮食，不令丰足，与其麁澁，不与精细，设有施与，迟缓不速。而此比丘，不称意故，羞耻愁忧，生损减心。而此比丘应作是念：『至檀越舍，彼非己家，云何而得称遂其心？何故生念，欲令檀越速施不迟，乃至精细，不用麁澁？』若作是念，设无所得，心不悔恨，离于增减，无有怨嫌。设彼檀越少有所施，不至心与，迟晚不速，乃至与麁，不与精细，如是比丘心不嫌恨，亦不愧耻，心无增减。迦叶比丘作如是心，至檀越所，斯非己家，云何而得自称其意，望彼至心，速施不迟，乃至精细，莫得麁澁？迦叶比丘作如是念，至檀越家，虽不得施，都无惭耻，心不损减。是故比丘，应作是心，至于他家。不应生念，速施于我，乃至精细。是故汝等当作是学，如迦叶比丘往檀越家。」

At that time, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
