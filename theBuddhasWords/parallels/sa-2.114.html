SĀ² 2.114

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 114（一一四）

Parallel to: SN 16.7 / SĀ 1139

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, Venerable Mahākāśyapa was staying in the hall of Viśākhā in the old garden. Then, at sunset, Venerable Kāśyapa arose from his meditation, went to where the Buddha was, bowed at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. The Buddha said to Kāśyapa, "You should instruct the bhikṣus and expound the Dharma for them. Why is that? I always instruct, and you should be the same. I always expound the Dharma for the bhikṣus, and you should be the same."

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，尊者摩诃迦叶住旧园林毘舍佉讲堂中。时，尊者迦叶于日没时，从禅定起，往至佛所，礼佛足已，在一面坐。佛告迦叶：「汝可教授诸比丘等，当为说法。所以者何？我恒教授，汝亦应尔。我常为诸比丘说法，汝亦应尔。」

Kāśyapa said to the Buddha, "These bhikṣus are difficult to instruct and cannot accept the teachings."

迦叶白佛：「是诸比丘，难可教授，不能受语。」

The Buddha said to Kāśyapa, "What causes and conditions do you see now that you do not expound the Dharma?"

佛告迦叶：「汝于今者，见何因缘，而不为说？」

Kāśyapa replied, "If they do not believe, they will lose good dharmas and become lazy, without shame or embarrassment. They will be ignorant and unwise, greedy for others' possessions, with minds of anger and harm, covered by the obscuration of sleep, agitated and restless, doubtful about the Dharma, deeply attached to the view of self, with minds defiled by afflictions. They will be joyful and angry, forgetful, without temporary stability. They will have such various unwholesome evil dharmas, definitely possessing them. Such people will not even have a little goodness, let alone progress in good dharmas. If there are people who possess faith, do not lose good dharmas, diligently practice without weariness, are able to cultivate shame and embarrassment, are wise people who practice good dharmas, are without greedy thoughts, are far from anger and resentment, eliminate the obscuration of sleep, have minds that are not agitated, are without doubt, are not attached to the view of self, have pure minds without defilement, are not joyful or angry, are able to abide in mindful thoughts, possess meditative concentration, and do not regress in good dharmas. If there are people who possess such various good dharmas, I would not say that their good dharmas remain still, let alone not increasing. Such people will increase in good dharmas day and night."

迦叶对曰：「若不信者，退失善法，便生懈怠，无有惭愧，愚痴无智，贪著他物，有恚害心，睡盖所覆，掉动不停，于法疑惑，深著我见，具于烦恼垢污之心，喜瞋失念，无暂定，有如是等种种不善恶法，决定具有。如斯等人，尚无少善，况复增进善法，无有退失？若复有人，具于信心，不退善法，精进不倦，能修惭愧，有智之人，具行善法，无有贪想，远离瞋嫌，除睡眠盖，心不掉动，无有疑惑，不著身见，心净无染，不喜瞋恚，能住心念，具于禅定，善法不退，若有具上种种善法，我尚不说彼人善法停住，况不增长？如斯等人，于日夜中，善法增长。」

The Buddha said to Kāśyapa, "So it is! So it is! As you have said. If they do not believe, they will lose good dharmas, and so on, up to such people will not even have a little goodness, let alone increase. If there are people who possess faith, do not lose good dharmas, and so on, up to I would not say that their good dharmas remain still, let alone increase."

佛告迦叶：「如是！如是！如汝所说。若不信者，退失善法，乃至如斯等人，尚无少善，况复增长？若复有人，具信心者，不退善法，乃至我尚不说彼人善法停住，况不增长？」

At that time, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully practiced it.

时，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
