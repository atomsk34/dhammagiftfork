SĀ² 2.115

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 115（一一五）

Parallel to: SN 16.8 / SĀ 1140

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, Venerable Mahākāśyapa was staying at the lecture hall of Viśākhā in the old garden. Then, at sunset, Venerable Kāśyapa arose from his meditation and went to where the Buddha was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side. The Buddha told Kāśyapa, "You should instruct and teach the bhikṣus. Why is that? I always instruct, and you should be the same. I always teach them the essential Dharma, and you should be the same."

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，尊者摩诃迦叶住旧园林毘舍佉讲堂。时，尊者迦叶于日没时，从禅定起，往诣佛所，顶礼佛足，在一面坐。佛告迦叶：「汝可教授诸比丘等，为其说法。所以者何？我常教授，汝亦应尔。我常为彼而说法要，汝亦应尔。」

Kāśyapa said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! These bhikṣus cannot accept instruction and are difficult to teach."

迦叶白佛言：「世尊！是诸比丘不能受语，难可教授。」

The Buddha told Kāśyapa, "Why do you not instruct and teach them?"

佛告迦叶：「汝何以故，而不教授，为其说法？」

Kāśyapa replied, "The World-Honored One is the root of the Dharma, the guide of the Dharma, and the support of the Dharma. Excellent! World-Honored One, please explain it. After I hear it, I will accept it with utmost sincerity and uphold it."

迦叶对曰：「世尊是法根本，是法之导，法所依凭。善哉！世尊，愿为敷演，我闻语已，至心受持。」

The Buddha told Kāśyapa, "Listen well now, accept and uphold it, and remember it. I will explain it to you in detail."

佛告迦叶：「汝今善听，受持忆念，吾当为汝分别解说。」

Kāśyapa said to the Buddha, "Yes, World-Honored One, I wish to hear it with great joy."

迦叶白佛：「唯然，世尊，愿乐欲闻。」

The Buddha told Kāśyapa, "In the past, there was a bhikṣu who practiced the conduct of an araṇya ascetic and praised those who practiced the conduct of an araṇya ascetic. He himself begged for food and wore rag robes, praising those who begged for food and wore rag robes. He was content with few desires, always enjoying solitude and tranquility, diligently cultivating and practicing, his mind not wandering, always delighting in meditation, and ending his own afflictions, praising those who ended their afflictions. For this reason, all the bhikṣus came to be close to him and inquired after him. This bhikṣu said to those who came, 'Welcome, bhikṣu! Please sit here. What is your name? Whose disciple are you? Your conduct is virtuous and in accord with the Dharma of the śramaṇa. One who has left home should be like you, becoming a śramaṇa. If one sees you and learns from you, one will soon attain one's own benefit.' When the newly ordained bhikṣus saw this, they thought, 'There are bhikṣus who respect each other. I should also practice their conduct. I will practice the conduct of an araṇya ascetic and praise those who practice the conduct of an araṇya ascetic. I will beg for food and wear rag robes, praising those who beg for food and wear rag robes. I will be content with few desires, always enjoying solitude and tranquility, diligently cultivating and practicing, my mind not wandering, always delighting in meditation, and ending my own afflictions, praising those who end their afflictions. For this reason, all the bhikṣus will come to be close to me and comfort and inquire after me. This bhikṣu will say to those who come, "Welcome, bhikṣu! Please sit here. What is your name? Whose disciple are you? Your conduct is virtuous and in accord with the Dharma of the śramaṇa. One who has left home should be like you, becoming a śramaṇa. If one sees you and learns from you, one will soon attain one's own benefit."'" If the newly ordained ones give rise to this thought, it will be beneficial for them for a long time, they will attain meaning and happiness, and it will be called self-liberation. It will enable the true Dharma to remain in the world for a long time. Such a person will progress and never regress.

佛告迦叶：「昔有比丘自修阿练若行，赞叹修阿练若行者，自行乞食，著粪扫衣，赞叹乞食著、粪扫衣者。少欲知足，常乐空闲寂静之处，勤修精进，心不驰散，恒乐禅定，自尽诸漏，赞尽漏者。以是之故，一切比丘咸来亲近，而问讯之。而此比丘语诸来者：『善来比丘！可就此坐] cf.【丽】。汝名为何？是谁弟子？履行贤良，应沙门法。夫出家者，宜应如汝，作于沙门。若见汝者，学汝所为，不久必当获于己利。』新学比丘，覩斯事已，而作是念：『彼有比丘，共相恭敬，我今亦当习学其行，自修阿练若行，赞叹修阿练若行者，自行乞食，著粪扫衣，赞叹乞食著粪扫衣者。少欲知足，常乐空闲寂静之处，勤修精进，心不驰散，恒乐禅定，自尽诸漏，赞尽漏者。以是之故，一切比丘咸来亲近，安慰问讯。而此比丘语诸来者：「善来比丘！可就此坐。汝名为何？是谁弟子？履行贤良，应沙门法。出家之人，宜应如汝，作于沙门。若见汝者，学汝所为，不久必当获己义利。」』诸新学者，若生是念，长夜利益，得义得乐，名自济拔，能令正法得久住世，是人进趣，终不退没。」

The Buddha told Kāśyapa, "If there is a bhikṣu who is born with merit and, when he first leaves home, obtains much support, with clothing, medicine, bedding, and utensils, the four things being abundant. There is also a bhikṣu who sees this bhikṣu, approaches him, speaks with him, and comforts and inquires after him. At that time, this bhikṣu says to that bhikṣu, 'What is your name? Whose disciple are you? You are born with merit and obtain much support, with clothing, medicine, bedding, and utensils, the four things being abundant. If there is a bhikṣu who approaches you, the four things will not be lacking.' If there are newly ordained bhikṣus who see this, they should think, 'There are bhikṣus born with merit who respect each other. I should also practice such conduct. I will provide clothing, bedding, food, and medicine, the four things being abundant, and always be bountiful.' If the newly ordained bhikṣus give rise to such thoughts and learn such things, this is called long-term decline, without any benefit or benefit and happiness. It is not the Dharma of the śramaṇa, experiencing various sufferings, called self-depreciation, the practice of purity not established, sinking in the mud, deceived by evil, possessing afflictions, repeatedly experiencing various existences, called giving rise to distress, obtaining the retribution of suffering, and certainly experiencing birth, old age, sickness, and death."

佛告迦叶：「若有比丘生则有福，初始出家，多得利养，衣服、汤药、床敷] cf.【丽】、卧具，四事丰饶。复有比丘，见是比丘，亲近谈语，安慰问讯。时，此比丘语彼比丘：『汝名何等？是谁弟子？生则有福，多得利养，衣服、汤药、床敷、卧具，四事丰饶。若有比丘亲近汝者，四事不乏。』若有新学比丘，覩斯事已，应作是念：『彼有生福比丘，共相恭敬，我今亦当修如是行，衣服、卧具、饮食、汤药，四事供养，亦常丰饶。』若新学比丘作如是意，学如是事已，是名长夜衰耗，都无利益，及以利乐，非沙门法，受诸苦恼，名自轻毁，梵行不立，没于淤泥，为恶所欺，具于结使，数受诸有，名生热恼，获得苦报，必当受于生老病死。」

At that time, Mahākāśyapa and the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully upheld it.

时，大迦叶及诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
