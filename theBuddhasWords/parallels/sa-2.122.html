SĀ² 2.122

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 122（一二二）

Parallel to: SN 42.2 / SĀ 907

At that time, the World-Honored One was staying in the Bamboo Grove of Kalandaka in Rājagṛha. At that time, there was a master of entertainers named Moving Hair who went to where the Buddha was. Having arrived, he bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said, "Gautama! I have heard from an extremely old entertainer in the past that when various entertainments are set up in an entertainment venue, and hundreds of thousands of people come to watch, playing the zither, singing, drumming, and playing stringed instruments, laughing and joking in various ways, and when it is finished, after death, one is reborn in the Heaven of Light Illumination." Is this said to be true or false?" The Buddha told him, "Stop! Stop! You should not ask about this matter now." The master of entertainers asked this way a second and a third time, but the Buddha did not answer.

尔时，世尊在王舍城迦兰陀竹林。时，彼城中有伎人主，号曰动发，往诣佛所。到佛所已，头面礼足，却坐一面，而作是言：「瞿昙！我于昔者曾从宿旧极老伎人边闻：『于伎场上，施设戏具，百千万人皆来观看，弹琴作倡，鼓乐弦歌，种种戏笑。所作讫已，命终之后，生光照天。』如是所说为实为虚？」佛告之曰：「止！止！汝今莫问是事。」时，彼伎主第二第三，亦如是问，佛悉不答。

At that time, the Tathāgata said to the master of entertainers, "I will ask you now, and you can answer as you wish. If there is an entertainer who sets up various entertainments in an entertainment venue, plays the zither, sings, drums, and plays stringed instruments, and hundreds of thousands of people all gather to watch because of this, then these people are already bound by desire, anger, and ignorance. If they create more frivolous things, will it not increase their greed, anger, and ignorance? It is like a person who is bound by a rope and is sprinkled with water, which makes it even tighter. In this way, these people are already bound by the three poisons, and if they further create various entertainments in the entertainment venue, it will only increase their three poisons, making them as blazing as this. Master of entertainers, if you say that after death you will be reborn in the Heaven of Light Illumination because of this, it is impossible. If there is a person who thinks that by creating various entertainments in the entertainment venue, after death they will be reborn in the Heaven of Light Illumination, I say that such a person is called one with wrong views. The result of wrong views leads to rebirth in two places, either in hell or in the animal realm." After the Buddha spoke thus, the master of entertainers wept and shed tears. The Buddha told the master of entertainers, "For this reason, you asked three times, but I did not speak for you.

尔时，如来语伎主言：「我今问汝，随汝意答。若有伎人，于伎场上，施设戏具，弹琴作倡，鼓乐弦歌，以是事故，百千种人，皆悉来集。如此诸人，本为爱欲、瞋恚、愚痴之所缠缚，复更造作放逸之事，岂不增其贪、恚、痴耶？譬如有人，为绳所缚，以水浇之，逾增其急。如是诸人，先为三毒之所缠缚，复更于彼伎场之上作倡伎乐，唯当增其三毒，炽盛如是。伎主汝为斯事，命终得生光照天者，无有是处。若有人计于伎场上作众伎乐，命终生于光照天者，我说是人名为邪见。邪见之果，生于二处，若堕地狱，或堕畜生。」佛说是已，时，彼伎主悲泣堕泪。佛告伎主：「以是因缘故，汝三请，我不为汝说。」

At that time, the master of entertainers said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I do not weep because of hearing the Buddha's words. I pity these entertainers who are childish, ignorant, and do unwholesome things. They have such views for a long time, and in the future they will suffer greatly, always being deceived and looked down upon by others. If there are entertainers who say, 'By creating various entertainments in the entertainment venue, after death one will be reborn in the Heaven of Light Illumination,' such words are called great lies. If one is reborn in the Heaven of Light Illumination because of this karma, it is impossible. World-Honored One! From today onwards, I will no longer create such evil karma." The Buddha then told him, "You are now truly sincere. In the future, you will definitely be reborn in a good place.

尔时，伎主白佛言：「世尊！我今不以闻佛语故而便涕泣，我愍如斯诸伎人等，𪧀愚无智，所作不善。彼于长夜，作如是见，于未来世，当受大苦，常被欺诳，为人所轻。若有伎人作如是言：『于伎场上作倡伎乐，命终生彼光照天。』者，如是之言名大妄语。若以此业，生光照天，无有是处。世尊！我从今日更不造彼如是恶业。」佛即告言：「汝今真实，于未来世，必生善处。」

At that time, the master of entertainers and the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

尔时，伎主及诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
