SĀ² 2.134

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 134（一三四）

Parallel to: SN 1.47 / SĀ 997

Thus have I heard. Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, a certain deity, with radiance and splendor surpassing the usual, came to the Buddha's place in the middle of the night, paid homage at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. At that time, the radiance of this deity was extremely bright, illuminating the Jetavana Grove, making it all very bright. Then, this deity sat to one side and spoke a verse, saying:

如是我闻：一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。时，有一天光色倍常，于其夜中，来诣佛所，礼佛足已，在一面坐。时，此天光甚为炽盛，普照祇洹，悉皆大明。尔时，此天却坐一面，而说偈言：

How can one increase one's meritorious deeds day and night? Upholding the
precepts according to the Dharma, who will proceed to the heavenly path?

「云何于昼夜，　　福业常增长？如法而持戒，　　何人趣天道？」

At that time, the World-Honored One again spoke a verse,

尔时，世尊复说偈言：

Planting gardens, groves, and forests, building bridges and boats across great rivers, constructing
good wells in wilderness areas, and building inns on important roads - such
a person will increase their meritorious deeds day and night. Upholding the pure
precepts according to the true Dharma, they will proceed to the heavenly path
in this way.

「种植园苑林，　　洪流置桥船，旷野造好井，　　要路造客舍，是人于日夜，　　福业常增长，正法净持戒，　　如是趣天道。」

The gods praised in verse:

天赞偈曰：

In the past, I have already seen a brahmin enter nirvana. Disgust and fear
have long been abandoned, and I am able to cross over the world's attachments.

「往昔已曾见，　　婆罗门涅槃，嫌怖久弃舍，　　能度世间爱。」

At that time, after this god had spoken this verse, he joyfully returned to his palace.

尔时，此天说此偈已，欢喜还宫。
