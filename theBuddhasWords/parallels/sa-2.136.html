SĀ² 2.136

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 136（一三六）

Parallel to: SN 2.23 / SĀ 999

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī. At that time, a deity with radiance twice as bright as usual came to where the Buddha was in the middle of the night. His majestic radiance illuminated Jetavana, making it very bright. At that time, the deity bowed at the Buddha's feet, then sat to one side and spoke a verse, saying:

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。时，有一天光色倍常，于其夜中，来诣佛所，威光炽盛，遍照祇洹，悉皆大明。尔时，此天礼佛足已，却坐一面，而说偈言：

In the world, gods and humans, through food and drink, give rise to joy. In
the world, there is nothing that does not give rise to joy through food and drink.

「世间天及人，　　饮食生欢喜，世间都无有，　　饮食不生喜。」

At that time, the World-Honored One again spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊复说偈言：

If one is able to give with faith, causing the mind to be extremely
pure, in this world or the next, the merit of food and drink will follow.

「若有能信施，　　使心极清净，今世若后世，　　饮食福随逐。」

At that time, this god heard what the Buddha said and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! It is truly rare! You have well spoken this verse." He further said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I remember in the past, there was a king named Slow. That king gave food and drink at the four city gates, and also gave food and drink in the city and market. At that time, the king's wife said to the king, 'King, now you are making merit. Please allow us to assist the king in making merit.' Hearing her words, the king gave the food given at the eastern city gate to the queen. The king's prince also said to his father, 'Parents are cultivating merit. I also wish to cultivate.' Hearing his words, the king gave the food given at the southern city gate to the prince. The prime minister later said to the king, 'King, now you are cultivating goodness. The queen, prince, and prime minister are all cultivating meritorious deeds. Please allow me to assist in cultivating meritorious deeds.' Hearing his words, the king gave the food given at the western city gate to the prime minister. At that time, the ministers also said to the king, 'The queen, prince, and prime minister are all cultivating merit and virtue. We now also wish to assist in cultivation.' Hearing their words, the king then gave the food given at the northern city gate to the ministers. At that time, the people of the country also said to the king, 'The queen, prince, prime minister, and others are all cultivating merit and virtue. Please allow us to assist in cultivating meritorious deeds.' Hearing their words, the king further gave the alms to the people. At that time, the almsmaster said to the king, 'All that the king has given at the four city gates and in alms has been given to the queen, prince, prime minister, ministers, and people of the country. The king's alms have been cut off, and the treasury is exhausted.' The king then replied, 'What has been given before has already been given out. From now on, half of the tribute from the small countries will enter the treasury, and half will be used to cultivate merit.' World-Honored One! At that time, I cultivated merit for a long time. For a long time, I obtained excellent rewards, always feeling joy and happiness. The merit and rewards I received were inexhaustible, with no end in sight. As I received great fruit and rewards, I now know that the World-Honored One has well spoken this verse."

尔时，此天闻佛所说，白佛言：「世尊！实为希有！善说斯偈。」复白佛言：「世尊！我忆过去，有一人王名曰迟缓，然彼国王于四城门施于饮食，城中及市亦施饮食。时，王夫人白于王言：『王今作福，愿听我等助王为福。』王闻其言，以城东门所施之食，回与夫人。王之太子亦白父言：『父母修福，我亦乐修。』王闻其言，以城南门所施之食，回与太子。辅相后来，启白王言：『王今修善，夫人、太子皆修福业，唯愿听我助修福业。』王闻其言，以城西门所施之食，回与辅相。时，有诸臣复白王言：『夫人、太子及以辅相咸修福德，我等今者亦乐助修。』王闻其言，即以北门所施之食，回与诸臣。时，国中人复白王言：『夫人、太子、辅相咸修福德，愿听我等助修福业。』王闻其言，复以布施回与人民。时，典施人白于王言：『王之所有，于四城门及以布施，悉皆回与夫人、太子、辅相、大臣、国中人民，断于王施，兼竭库藏。』王即答言：『先所与者，已尔与尽，自今已后，他方小国所可贡献，半入库藏，半用修福。』世尊！我于尔时，长夜修福，我于长夜获得胜报，常怀喜乐，所受福报，无有穷尽，不见边际。如我所受，得大果报，乃知世尊善说斯偈。」

At that time, the god Slow, having heard the Buddha's teaching, was overjoyed. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and returned to his heavenly palace.

时，迟缓天子闻佛所说，欢喜踊跃，顶礼佛足，还于天宫。
