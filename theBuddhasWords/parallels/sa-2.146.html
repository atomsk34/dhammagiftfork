SĀ² 2.146

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 146（一四六）

Parallel to: AN 3.96–98 / SĀ 920

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, the Buddha told the bhikṣus, "There are three kinds of well-trained horses that should be ridden by kings and princes. What are the three? They are: fully endowed with speed, fully endowed with appearance, and fully endowed with riding. There are three kinds of bhikṣus who are like those three horses. If a bhikṣu is fully endowed with these three things, he should be worshipped, offered to, bowed to, and praised with joined palms. This is called being fully endowed with three things. What is meant by being fully endowed with appearance? It means fully upholding the precepts, being able to well protect the Prātimokṣa, going and coming, entering and leaving, with all proper deportment. Being greatly afraid of even minor faults, upholding the precepts without any damage. This is called being fully endowed with appearance. What is meant by being fully endowed with strength? It means that before unwholesome dharmas arise, one is able to prevent them from arising, and for unwholesome dharmas that have already arisen, one uses skillful means to eliminate them. For wholesome dharmas that have not yet arisen, one uses skillful means to cause them to arise, and for wholesome dharmas that have already arisen, one uses skillful means to cause them to increase and expand. This is called being fully endowed with strength. What is meant by being fully endowed with speed? It means that within the Buddha's teachings, one truly knows and sees the Four Noble Truths. This is called being fully endowed with speed."

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，佛告诸比丘：「如是三种善调乘马，应为王者及王子乘。何等三种？所谓骏疾具足、色具足、乘具足。三种比丘，如彼三马。若比丘具足三事，宜应礼拜供养，合掌赞叹，是名三事具足。云何色具足？具持禁戒，于波罗提木叉善能护持，往返出入，具诸威仪。于小罪中，心生大怖，坚持禁戒，无有毁损，是名色具足。云何力具足？恶法未生，能令不生恶法，已生方便令灭；善法未生方便令生，善法已生方便令增广。是名力具足。云何骏疾具足？若佛法中，如实知见四真谛已，是名骏疾具足。」

Having spoken thus, the Buddha finished what he had to say. The bhikṣus, having heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说是已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
