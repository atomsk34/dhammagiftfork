SĀ² 2.152

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 152（一五二）

Parallel to: SN 55.37 / SĀ 927

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was in the Niṣadha Forest in Kapilavastu. Then Śrīmān went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! What is meant by the meaning of an upāsaka? I only wish that the Tathāgata would explain it for me."

一时，佛在迦毘罗卫国尼拘陀林。时释摩男往诣佛所，顶礼佛足，在一面坐，而白佛言：「世尊！云何名为优婆塞义？唯愿如来为我敷演。」

The Buddha told Śrīmān, "A layperson who takes refuge in the Three Jewels is called an upāsaka. You are one of them."

佛告释摩男：「在家白衣归依三宝，以是义故，名优婆塞，汝即其人。」

Then Śrīmān again said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! What is meant by an upāsaka's faith?"

时，释摩男复白佛言：「世尊！云何名优婆塞信？」

The Buddha told Śrīmān, "Deeply generating faith in the Tathāgata, abiding in that faith, one will not be destroyed by those who do not believe, such as śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, gods, demons, Brahmā, or humans. This is called an upāsaka's faith."

佛告释摩男：「于如来所，深生信心，安住信中，终不为彼沙门、婆罗门、若天、若魔、若梵、若人，不信所坏，是名优婆塞信。」

Then Śrīmān again said to the Buddha, "What is meant by an upāsaka's precepts?"

时释摩男复白佛言：「云何优婆塞戒？」

The Buddha told Śrīmān, "Not killing, not stealing, not engaging in sexual misconduct, not deceiving, and not drinking alcohol, etc. are called an upāsaka's precepts."

佛告释摩男：「不杀、不盗、不婬、不欺，及不饮酒等，是名优婆塞戒。」

He further asked, "What is meant by perfect giving?"

又问：「云何施具足？」

The Buddha told Śrīmān, "An upāsaka should abandon stinginess and greed. All sentient beings are all covered by greed and jealousy. For this reason, one should abandon stinginess, greed, and jealous thoughts, give rise to a mind of letting go, and personally give without weariness. This is called perfect giving."

佛告释摩男：「优婆塞法应舍悭贪，一切众生，皆悉为彼贪嫉所覆，以是义故，应离悭贪及嫉妬意，生放舍心，躬自施与，无有疲厌，是名施具足。」

He further asked, "What is meant by perfect wisdom?"

又问：「云何智慧具足？」

The Buddha told Śrīmān, "An upāsaka truly knows suffering, truly knows the origin of suffering, truly knows the cessation of suffering, and truly knows the path to the cessation of suffering. Knowing these four truths is definite and clear. This is called perfect wisdom."

佛告释摩男：「优婆塞如实知苦，如实知苦集，如实知苦灭，如实知苦灭道，知此四谛，决定明了，是名慧具足。」

Having spoken thus, the bhikṣus rejoiced in the Buddha's words.

佛说是已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
