SĀ² 2.157

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 157（一五七）

Parallel to: AN 11.11 / SĀ 932

At that time, the World-Honored One was staying in the Nyagrodha Forest in Kapilavastu for the summer retreat. At that time, many bhikṣus, at the end of the summer, were sewing robes in the Dharma hall. After the bhikṣus had finished sewing their robes, they thought, "Now that we have finished sewing our robes, we should follow the Buddha on his travels." At that time, Śrīmān heard that the bhikṣus had finished sewing their robes and wanted to follow the Buddha on his travels. Having heard this, he went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! My body and mind are now very heavy and dull, and I am confused about the directions. Although I listen to the Dharma, my mind is not joyful. Why is this? I heard that the bhikṣus have finished sewing their robes and will follow the Buddha on his travels. I then thought, 'When will I be able to see the World-Honored One and the bhikṣus who cultivate the mind again?'"

尔时，世尊在迦毘罗卫国尼拘陀林夏坐安居。尔时，众多比丘于夏欲末，在讲堂中，为佛缝衣。诸比丘等缝衣已讫，作是思惟：「我等于今，缝衣已竟，当逐佛游行。」时，释摩男闻诸比丘缝衣已讫，欲随佛游行，闻斯语已，即往佛所，稽首礼足，在一面坐，而白佛言：「世尊！我今身心甚为重钝，迷于诸方，虽复听法，心不甘乐。所以者何？我闻诸比丘等，缝衣已竟，当随佛游行，即生念言：『何时当复还见世尊，及以修心诸比丘等？』」

The Buddha told him, "Although I and the bhikṣus may go elsewhere, if you always wish to see the Tathāgata and the bhikṣus, you should observe with the Dharma eye and always cultivate five things. What are the five? They are: because of having faith, one can follow the teachings, not because of having no faith; because of upholding pure precepts, one can follow the teachings, not because of violating the precepts; because of being well-versed, one can follow the teachings, not because of being poorly-versed; not because of stinginess can one practice giving, but because of a mind of giving; not because of ignorance can one cultivate wisdom, but because of a mind of wisdom can one recognize the characteristics of dharmas. Therefore, Mahānāman! If you wish to see the Buddha and the bhikṣus, you should always cultivate these five things, as well as the six recollections. If you do so, I and the bhikṣus will always be in front of you. What is meant by 'saṃgha' is harmony."

佛告之曰：「我及比丘虽去余处，汝若恒欲见于如来及比丘者，应以法眼至心观察，常修五事。何等为五？所谓以具信故，能随顺教，非是无信，能随顺教。持净戒故，能随顺教，非是毁禁能顺教也。以多闻故，能随顺教，非以少闻能随顺教。非以悭悋能行布施，以舍心故，能行布施。非以愚痴能修智慧，以慧心故，能识法相。是故摩诃男！若欲见佛及比丘者，恒应修习如是五事，并六念法，若如是者，我及比丘便常在前。所谓僧者，名为和合。」

At that time, Mahānāman, having heard the Buddha's teaching, was delighted and bowed at the Buddha's feet before departing.

时摩诃男闻佛所说，欢喜礼足而去。
