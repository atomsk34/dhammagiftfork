SĀ² 2.167

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 167（一六七）

Parallel to: SN 2.9 / SĀ 583

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, Rāhu, the asura king, was obstructing the moon. The moon god was extremely frightened, his body hair standing on end. He went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and spoke a verse, saying:

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，罗睺罗阿修罗王手障于月。时月天子极大惊怖，身毛为竖，往诣佛所，顶礼佛足，即说偈言：

The Tathāgata's great diligence, I now take refuge and
pay homage. Able to attain liberation everywhere, now encountering
great difficulties, I pray to be your refuge. The
well-gone one of the world, the worthy one, the
arhat, I now come to take refuge. The Tathāgata
who has compassion for the world, may Rāhu naturally release me.

「如来大精进，　　我今归命礼，能于一切处，　　悉皆得解脱。今遭大艰难，　　愿作我归依，世间之善逝，　　应供阿罗汉，我今来归依，　　如来愍世间，使彼罗睺罗，　　自然放舍我。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse in reply:

尔时，世尊说偈答曰：

The moon is in the sky, able to dispel all
darkness, with great bright light illuminating, clear and pure, all
clearly understood. The moon is the world's bright lamp, Rāhu
should quickly release it." Rāhu, having heard the verse, was
frightened in his heart, sweating like a bath, and quickly
released the moon.

「月处虚空中，　　能灭一切暗，有大光明照，　　清白悉明了。月是世明灯，　　罗睺应速放。」罗睺闻偈已，　　心中怀战栗，流汗如沐浴，　　即速放彼月。

At that time, Vāruṇī saw the Asura King quickly release the moon, and spoke a verse, saying:

时，跋罗蒲卢旃见阿修罗王速疾放月，即说偈言：

Why are you frightened? Quickly release the moon, your
body sweating like a bath, trembling like a sick person?

「汝何故惊惧？　　速疾放于月，身汗如沐浴，　　掉动如病者？」

At that time, the Asura again spoke a verse,

时，阿修罗复说偈言：

I heard the Buddha speak a verse, if I do not release the moon
my head will break into seven pieces, I will never see peace and happiness.

「我闻佛说偈，　　若不放月者，头当破七分，　　终不见安乐。」

At that time, Bhāradvāja again spoke a verse,

时，跋罗蒲卢旃复说偈言：

The Buddha's appearance is unprecedented, seeing him brings peace and
tranquility. The asura heard the verse and immediately released the moon.

「佛出未曾有，　　见者得安隐，阿修闻说偈，　　即时放于月。」
