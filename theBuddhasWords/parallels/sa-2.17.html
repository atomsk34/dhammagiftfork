SĀ² 2.17

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 17（一七）

Parallel to: SN 1.20 / SĀ 1078

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying in the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove near Rājagṛha. At that time, a bhikṣu went to the river before dawn, folded his robes and placed them in one place, entered the river to bathe, and stood on the riverbank with his body exposed, drying it in the sunlight. A deity emitted a light that illuminated the riverbank and said to the bhikṣu, "You have not been ordained for long. You are young and have good hair. Why do you not enjoy the five desires? It is not the right time to go forth." The bhikṣu replied, "My going forth now is the right time, and I have attained what is not the right time." The deity said to the śramaṇa, "How is it that going forth is the right time, and attaining what is not the right time?" The śramaṇa replied, "The World-Honored Buddha said that the five desires are the right time, and the Buddha's teachings are not the right time. The pleasures of the five desires have very little taste, but their troubles are abundant, and they are the source of worries. In the teachings of our Buddha, one can attain realization in this very life without any troubles. Whatever one does, one does not consider the time or day. By planting a few small causes, one can attain great rewards."

一时，佛在王舍城迦兰陀竹林中。尔时，有一比丘，天未明晓，往趣河边，襞叠衣服，安著一处，入河洗浴，露形出水，于河岸上，晞干其身。有天放光，照彼河岸，语比丘言：「汝出家未久，盛壮好发，何不受五欲乐，非时出家？」比丘答言：「我今出家，正是其时，获于非时。」天语沙门：「云何出家是时，获于非时。」沙门答言：「佛世尊说五欲是时，佛法是非时。五欲之乐，受味甚少，其患滋多，忧恼所集。我佛法中，现身受证，无诸热恼。诸有所作，不观时日。种少微缘，获大果报。」

The deity asked again, "How does the Buddha say that the five desires are the right time, and how does he say that the Buddha's teachings are not the right time?" The bhikṣu replied, "I am still young, and I have not been ordained for long. My studies are still shallow. How can I proclaim the profound meaning of the Tathāgata? The Bhagavān is now near the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove. You can go there yourself and ask about your doubts." The deity replied, "Now the Buddha is surrounded by deities of great majesty. I am weak and cannot see him. You should go and inform the World-Honored One on my behalf. If the Tathāgata is compassionate and allows it, I will go there and ask about my doubts." The bhikṣu replied, "If you can go, I will inform the World-Honored One on your behalf." The deity replied again, "I will follow you to where the World-Honored One is.

天复问言：「佛云何说五欲是时？云何佛法名为非时？」比丘答言：「我既年稚，出家未久，学日又浅，岂曰能宣如来至真广大深义。婆伽婆今者，在近迦兰陀竹林，尔可自往，问其疑惑。」天答之言：「今佛侍从大威德天，盈集左右，如我弱劣，不能得见。汝今为我往白世尊，如来慈矜，若垂听许，我当诣彼，咨启所疑。」比丘答言：「汝若能往，] cf.【丽】我当为汝启白世尊。」天复答言：「我随汝往诣世尊所。」

At that time, the bhikṣu went to where the Buddha was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and fully informed the World-Honored One of the deity's questions. At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时比丘往诣佛所。顶礼佛足，在一面立，具以天问而白世尊。尔时，世尊即说偈言：

The arising of name and form is considered to be truly existent
Know that such a person is on the path to death. If
one understands that name and form are originally empty and without nature
this is called respecting the Buddha and permanently leaving all realms of existence.

「名色中生相，　　谓为真实有，当知如斯人，　　是名属死径。若识于名色，　　本空无有性，是名尊敬佛，　　永离于诸趣。」

The Buddha asked the deity, "Do you understand yet?" The deity replied, "I do not understand, World-Honored One!" The Buddha spoke another verse,

佛问天曰：「汝解已未？」天即答言：「未解。世尊！」佛复说偈言：

Superior pride, equal pride, and inferior pride - those who
have these three kinds of pride can have disputes. Eliminating
these three kinds of pride is called the immovable thought.

「胜慢及等慢，　　并及不如慢，有此三慢者，　　是可有诤论；灭除此三慢，　　是名不动想。」

The Buddha asked the deity, "Do you understand yet?" The deity replied to the Buddha, "I do not understand, World-Honored One!" The Buddha spoke another verse,

佛告天曰：「汝解已未？」天答佛言：「未解。世尊！」佛复说偈言：

Cutting off desire and form, eliminating the three kinds of
pride; not touching on desires, eliminating anger and hatred; uprooting
the roots of poison, all thoughts and wishes exhausted. If
one can do this, one will cross the ocean of
birth and death.

「断爱及名色，　　灭除三种慢；不触于诸欲，　　灭除于瞋恚；拔除诸毒根，　　诸想愿欲尽。若能如是者，　　得度生死海。」

The deity said to the Buddha, "I now understand."

天白佛言：「我今已解。」

The bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
