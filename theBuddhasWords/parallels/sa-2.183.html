SĀ² 2.183

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 183（一八三）

Parallel to: SN 1.28 / SĀ 589

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī. At that time, a god, with his radiance doubled, came to where the Buddha was in the middle of the night. His majestic light illuminated the entire Jetavana, brightly shining everywhere. He then sat to one side and spoke a verse:

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。时有一天，光色倍常，于其夜中，来诣佛所，威光普照，遍于祇洹，赫然大明，却坐一面，而说偈言：

The merchants of the country of Rāṣṭrapāla are extremely wealthy. Each of
them is greedy for profit, seeking greedily without being satisfied. They fight
over wealth and property, bound and carried away by the currents of
desire and love. Who among them can give up desire and love?

「罗咤国商估，　　财产极巨富，各各相贪利，　　贪求无厌足。为财产鬪诤，　　爱欲结流漂，如斯之等类，　　谁能舍欲爱？」

At that time, the World-Honored One replied in verse:

尔时，世尊以偈答曰：

Abandoning all conditions, wives, children, and livestock, all that
is cherished and loved, removing desire, greed, and delusion
Abandoning desire and leaving home, this can sever the
bonds of desire, forever abandoning all that is carried
away and disputed.

「弃舍于众缘，　　妻子及六畜，一切所玩爱，　　除去欲贪痴。舍欲而出家，　　此能断欲结，永舍于一切，　　漂没及诤讼。」

At that time, the god again praised in verse:

尔时天复以偈赞言：

In the past, I have already seen a brahmin enter parinirvāṇa, Disgusted and
afraid, I have long abandoned it, able to liberate the world from attachment.

「往昔已曾见，　　婆罗门涅槃，嫌怖久弃舍，　　能度世间爱。」

At that time, after this god had spoken this verse, he joyfully returned to his palace.

尔时，此天说此偈已，欢喜还宫。
