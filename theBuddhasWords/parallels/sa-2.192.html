SĀ² 2.192

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 192（一九二）

Parallel to: SN 44.8 / SĀ 959 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha. At that time, the Brāhmaṇa Kaulita went to where the Buddha was, greeted the Buddha, and sat to one side. He said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! For what reason do other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, when asked, all answer in accordance with the question, saying, 'I die here and am born there,' 'I die here and am not born there,' 'I die here and am both born there and not born there,' 'I am neither born there nor not born there'?" Kaulita further said, "Gautama! Why can't you answer such questions in accordance with them?"

一时，佛在王舍城迦兰陀竹林。尔时，犊子梵志往诣佛所，问讯佛已，在一面坐。白佛言：「世尊！以何因缘，诸余沙门，及婆罗门，若有所问，皆称顺答说，我死此生彼，我死此不生彼，我死此亦生彼亦不生彼，我非生彼非非生彼。」犊子复言：「瞿昙！如斯之难，何故不能称顺而答？」

The Buddha told him, "Other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas do not know the arising of form, do not know the cessation of form, do not know the faults of form, do not know the taste of form, and do not know the way out of form. Because they do not know the arising of form, up to the way out of form, they all grasp at form, thinking, 'I die here and am born there,' 'I die here and am not born there,' 'I die here and am both born there and not born there,' 'I am neither born there nor not born there.' The same is true for feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness." He further told Kaulita, "The Tathāgata is not like that. He knows the cause of form, knows the cessation of form, knows the taste of form, knows the faults of form, and knows the way out of form. He truly knows them. The Tathāgata truly knows the cause, cessation, faults, taste, and way out of form. He knows form, and does not grasp at 'I die here and am born there,' up to 'I am neither born there nor not born there.' The same is true for feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness."

佛告之曰：「诸余沙门、婆罗门，不知色从因生，不知色灭，不知色过，不知色味，不知色出要。以不能知色从因生，乃至不知色出要故，而于色，我死此生彼，死此不生彼，死此亦生彼亦不生彼，非生彼非非生彼，悉皆取著。受想行识，亦复如是。」复告犊子：「如来不尔，知色因，知色灭，知色味，知色过，知色出要。如实知之，如来如实能知色因、色灭、色过、色味、色出要，能知色，我死此生彼，乃至非生非非生彼，悉皆不著。受想行识，亦复如是。」

The Buddha told Kaulita, "Therefore, this meaning is very profound, vast, immeasurable, and boundless, beyond calculation." He further told Kaulita, "For this reason, other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas do not understand the meaning and answer questions forcefully. If they ask the Tathāgata, 'I am born there, not born there, both born there and not born there, neither born there nor not born there,' because it is meaningless, he does not answer. I am born there, up to neither born there nor not born there, he does not answer all of them."

佛告犊子：「是故此义甚深广大，无量无边，非算数所及。」复告犊子：「以是因缘，诸余沙门、婆罗门等，不达义趣，随问强答。若问如来，我色生彼，不生彼，亦生彼亦不生彼，非生彼非非生彼，以无义理，置而不答。我已生彼，乃至非生非非生，悉皆不答。」

Kaulita said, "It is rare, Gautama! You and your disciples, the meaning, the phrases, and the flavor of the phrases you speak are all the same." Kaulita further said, "At another time, I went to the śramaṇa Maudgalyāyana. At that time, I asked him with these phrases and flavors. He answered me with these meanings, phrases, and flavors. Gautama! What you have just spoken is no different from that. Therefore, I now call it rare. Such a teaching has never existed before, nor has it ever been spoken. The meanings are in accord and well answer this question."

犊子言：「希有。瞿昙！汝及弟子，义与义句，及与句味，所说之事，等无差别。」犊子复言：「我于异时，至沙门目犍连所，我于尔时，以此句味问彼目连，彼以此义句味而答于我。瞿昙！汝今所可宣说，与彼无异，是故我今称为希有。如此教法，昔所未有，亦未曾说，义理相顺，善答斯问。」

The brāhmaṇa Kaulita, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and departed.

犊子梵志闻佛所说，欢喜而去。
