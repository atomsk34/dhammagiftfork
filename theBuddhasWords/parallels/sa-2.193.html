SĀ² 2.193

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 193（一九三）

Parallel to: SN 44.11 / SĀ 959 (~)

At that time, Venerable Saṃgati Kātyāyana was staying at the residence of Puṇḍarīka in the city of Nādikā. At that time, the Brāhman Gautama went to that city on some business. Having finished his business, he went to Venerable Saṃgati Kātyāyana, exchanged greetings, and sat to one side. He said to the Venerable One: "I have a doubt and wish to ask you. If you are free, please listen to my question and explain it for me."

尔时，尊者僧提迦旃延在那提城群寔迦所住之处。尔时，犊子梵志以缘事故，往诣彼城。既至彼已，营事已讫，即便往彼尊者僧提迦旃延所，相问讯已，在一面坐。白尊者言：「我有所疑，欲相咨问，汝若闲裕，听我所问，愿为解说。」

The Venerable One said: "Gautama! I will listen to your question and then know."

尊者告言：「犊子！我听汝问，然后乃知。」

Gautama asked: "For what reason do other Śramaṇas and Brāhmaṇas, when asked by someone about dying here and being born there, dying here and not being born there, up to neither being born there nor not being born there, are all able to answer; but the Śramaṇa Gautama, when asked about the death of form here and being born there, up to neither being born there nor not being born there, does not answer because it is meaningless?"

犊子问言：「以何因缘故，诸余沙门、婆罗门，有人来问，死此生彼，死此不生彼，乃至非生彼非非生彼，悉皆能答；沙门瞿昙为以此问，色死此生彼，乃至非生彼非非生彼，无义理故，置不答乎？」

The Venerable One said: "I will now ask you, answer me according to your understanding. What do you think? If it is the cause, the condition, the action, the root, the arising of the action, the form, the formless, the conceptual, the non-conceptual, with this cause, this condition, this action, this root, this arising of the action, without remainder, completely extinguished, without thought, if the Tathāgata could say that dying here one is born there, up to neither being born there nor not being born there, with such causes and conditions, without action, without characteristics, and the Dharma of complete extinction?"

尊者告言：「我今问汝，随汝所解，而答于我。于汝意云何？若因若缘，若行若根本，若行所从生，若色若无色，若有想若无想，以此因，以此缘，以此行，以此根本，以此行所从生，无余寂灭，无想尽处，若如是等，无有因缘，无行无相，及尽灭法，如来宁可说，死此生彼，乃至说非生彼非非生彼耶？」

The calf said: "Kātyāyana! Such causes, such conditions, such actions, such roots, such arising of actions, such forms, such formlessness, such concepts, such non-conceptuality, these dharmas all completely extinguish without remainder, the extinction of thought, these dharmas have no causes and conditions. How could the Tathāgata speak of them?" Having heard this, the calf was delighted in his mind. He asked the Venerable One: "You are a disciple of the Buddha. How long has it been since you came?"

犊子言：「迦旃延！如是因，如是缘，如是行，如是根本，如是行所从生，是色是无色，是想是无想，此等诸法，皆至无余尽灭，无想灭尽，是等诸法，无有因缘，如来云何而当说之？」犊子闻已，心怀欢喜，问尊者言：「汝为佛弟子，从来久近？」

The Venerable One replied: "It has been more than three years since I became a disciple of the Buddha."

尊者答言：「我为佛弟子，始过三年。」

The calf said: "Kātyāyana! You have gained great benefits. You can speak with such eloquence and wisdom in the assembly. To be able to accomplish these things in such a short time is truly rare." The calf said: "I have some matters to attend to now and wish to return to where I am staying."

犊子言：「迦旃延！汝获大利，能于众中，身口智慧辩才如是，于少时中，能具斯事，实为希有。」犊子言：「我今缘事，欲还所止。」

The Venerable One said: "It is time for you to know."

尊者言：「宜知是时。」

The calf brāhmaṇa, having heard the Venerable One's words, departed joyfully.

犊子梵志闻尊者语，欢喜而去。
