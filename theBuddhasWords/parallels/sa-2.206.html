SĀ² 2.206

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 206（二〇六）

Parallel to: AN 4.185 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying in the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha. At that time, the non-Buddhist practitioners of Magadha gathered at the Sumāgadhā Pond, discussing, "This is the truth of the brāhmaṇas, this is the truth of the brāhmaṇas." At that time, the Tathāgata was in his dwelling, and with his pure divine ear he heard their discussion. He then arose from his concentration and went to the Sumāgadhā Pond.

一时，佛在王舍城迦兰陀竹林中。当于是时，摩竭提国诸外道辈，相与聚集须摩竭陀池上，作斯论言：「此是婆罗门谛，此是婆罗门谛。」尔时，如来在于精舍，以禅净天耳闻其所说，即从定觉，往诣于彼须摩竭陀池上。

The brāhmaṇas saw the Buddha coming from afar, all rose from their seats, prepared a seat for the Buddha, and said, "Please take a seat."

诸婆罗门遥见佛来，悉从座起，为佛敷座，白佛：「就坐] cf.【丽】。」

The Buddha then took his seat and said to them, "What kind of discussion were you having when you gathered here?"

佛即就坐，而告之曰：「汝等聚集作何谈论？」

The brāhmaṇas each said to the Buddha, "Gautama should know that today we gathered together and said, 'This is the truth of the brāhmaṇas, this is the truth of the brāhmaṇas.'"

诸婆罗门各白佛言：「瞿昙当知，我等今日共相聚集，作是说言：『此是婆罗门谛，此是婆罗门谛。』」

The Buddha said, "So it is! So it is! When I was seeking the path, I first attained perfect enlightenment and realized it completely. To put it simply, all the worlds are no more than three truths. I shall explain them. What are the three? Namely, all in the world should not kill. This is the truth, not false speech. If this is true, one should diligently cultivate compassion for all beings. This is the first truth of the brāhmaṇas. Having realized this, I extensively teach it to others. Furthermore, brāhmaṇas! All suffering and its causes are born and cease. This statement is true and not false. If this is true, one should diligently cultivate the contemplation of birth and cessation in the midst of this. One should abide thus. This is called the second truth of the brāhmaṇas. Because I have realized this characteristic of birth and cessation, I have attained perfect enlightenment and always teach this Dharma to sentient beings. Furthermore, brāhmaṇas! The third truth is the absence of self and mine, the true absence of self. If one can be free from these three characteristics, one can be far removed from all evil. If this is true, one should diligently cultivate the seeking of freedom from all evil, abiding thus." Having spoken thus, the many non-Buddhist practitioners heard what the Buddha said and sat in silence.

佛告之曰：「如是！如是！我昔求道，初成正觉，已证知竟。取要言之，一切世间不过三谛，吾当分别。何等为三？所谓一切不杀，此语是实，非虚妄说。此事若实，应勤精进，于诸众生，恒生慈心，此是婆罗门初谛。我知是已，广为人说。复次，婆罗门！一切苦集是生灭法，如斯之言，真实不虚。此事若实，应懃精进，于其中间，常宜修心，作生灭相，应如是住，是名婆罗门第二谛。我以知此生灭相故，成等正觉，常为众生说如是法。复次，婆罗门！第三谛者，离我、我所，真实无我。若离如是三法相者，便能远离一切诸恶。此事若实，应懃精进，求离众恶，应如是住。」佛说是已，众多外道闻佛所说，默然而坐。

At that time, the World-Honored One thought, "These foolish people are always covered by the māras. In this great assembly, not a single person is able to believe these words, give rise to the thought of learning, and cultivate the holy life." Having had this thought, the Tathāgata rose from his seat and left.

尔时，世尊而作是念：「斯愚痴人常为诸魔之所覆蔽，是大众中，乃至无有一人能信斯语，生志学想，修持梵行。」于时如来作斯念已，从坐起去。

Not long after the Buddha left, the deity of the Sumāgadhā Lake spoke a verse, saying:

佛去不久，尔时，须摩竭陀池神而说偈言：

It is like drawing on water to seek traces, planting seeds in salty soil to seek crops
perfuming foul odors with fragrant incense, seeking moisture in the waves of water, blowing on an iron
pestle to seek a wonderful sound, seeking a wild horse in the midst of winter - those
non-Buddhist practitioners are also like this, though they hear the wonderful Dharma, they do not believe or accept it.

「譬如画水欲求迹，　　下种卤地求苗稼，如以芳香熏臭秽，　　水浸注波求濡弱，吹彼铁杵求妙声，　　如于盛冬求野马，彼诸外道亦如是，　　虽闻妙法不信受。」

At that time, the brāhmaṇas, having heard the verses spoken by the deity of the lake, all followed behind the Buddha, seeking to renounce the householder's life. The Buddha immediately granted their request. Having renounced the householder's life, they diligently practiced the path and attained the fruit of arahantship.

尔时，诸婆罗门闻此池神说是偈已，竞随佛后，求索出家，佛即听许。既出家已，精懃修道，得阿罗汉果。

The Buddha having spoken this, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and upheld it.

佛说是已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
