SĀ² 2.216

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 216（二一六）

Parallel to: SN 5.3 / SĀ 1200

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, the bhikṣuṇī Śāriputra put on her robe, took her alms bowl, entered the city to beg for food, finished eating, washed her bowl, put away her sitting mat, went to the forest of the blind, sat under a tree, and abided in the divine abode. At that time, Māra the Evil One thought this: "The Śramaṇa Gautama is at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. The bhikṣuṇī Śāriputra put on her robe, took her alms bowl, entered the city to beg for food, finished eating, washed her bowl, put away her sitting mat, went to the forest of the blind, sat under a tree, and abides in the divine abode." Having thought this, he transformed himself into a young brahmin, wishing to disturb her, and spoke a verse:

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，翅舍憍昙弥比丘尼著衣持钵，入城乞食，食讫洗钵，收摄坐具，至得眼林，坐一树下，住于天住。尔时，魔王波旬作是心念：「沙门瞿昙在于舍卫国祇树给孤独园，有比丘尼名翅舍憍昙弥著衣持钵，入城乞食，食讫洗钵，收摄坐具，至得眼林，坐一树下，住于天住。」作是念已，化作摩纳，欲为扰乱，即说偈言：

Why are you sitting under a tree now
weeping and shedding tears? Have you lost your
son? Are you alone in the forest, seeking a man?

「汝今者何为，　　怀忧坐树下，歔欷而流泪？　　将不丧子乎？独处于林中，　　欲求男子耶？」

At that time, the bhikṣuṇī thought: "Who is this? It is very deceitful. Is it a human or a non-human? Wishing to greatly disturb me." Entering concentration to observe, knowing it was Māra the Evil One, she spoke a verse:

时比丘尼而作是念：「此为是谁？甚为欺诈。为是人耶？是非人乎？而欲为我作大扰乱。」入定观察，知是魔王，即说偈言：

I have already cut off affection and love, without desire, without
the thought of a son, sitting upright in the forest, without
sorrow, without distress. Having cut off all love, extinguished the darkness
of ignorance, attained the state of extinction, abiding in the undefiled
Dharma. Therefore, know that Pāpīyān has fallen into a losing position.

「我断恩爱已，　　无欲无子想，端坐林树间，　　无愁无热恼。断除一切爱，　　灭诸无明暗，逮得于灭尽，　　安住无漏法。以是故当知，　　波旬堕负处。」

At that time, Pāpīyān thought: "The bhikṣuṇī Śāriputra knows my mind well." He was distressed, remorseful, ashamed, and returned to his palace.

尔时，波旬而作是念：「翅舍憍昙弥比丘尼，善知我心。」忧愁悔恨，惭愧还宫。
