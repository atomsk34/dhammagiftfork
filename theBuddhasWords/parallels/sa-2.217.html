SĀ² 2.217

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 217（二一七）

Parallel to: SN 5.5 / SĀ 1201

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, the bhikṣuṇī Padmavatī, in the morning, put on her robe, took her alms bowl, entered the city to beg for food, finished eating, washed her bowl, put away her sitting mat, and washed her feet. She then went to the forest of the eye-obtainer, sat under a tree, and peacefully contemplated, abiding in the divine state. At that time, Māra the Evil One thought, "The Śramaṇa Gautama is at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. The bhikṣuṇī Padmavatī, having put on her robe and taken her alms bowl, entered the city to beg for food, finished eating, washed her bowl, put away her sitting mat, and went to the forest of the eye-obtainer. She is sitting under a tree, abiding in the divine state. I shall disturb her." Having thought this, he transformed himself into a young brahmin and went to her, speaking a verse:

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时莲华色比丘尼，于其晨朝，著衣持钵，入城乞食，食讫洗钵，收摄坐具，并洗足已，入得眼林，坐一树下，端坐思惟，住于天住。尔时，魔王而作是念：「沙门瞿昙在舍卫国祇树给孤独园，莲华色比丘尼，著衣持钵，入城乞食，食讫洗钵，收摄坐具，至得眼林中，坐一树下，住于天住。我当为其而作扰乱。」作是念已，化为摩纳，往至其所，而说偈言：

Sitting under the śāla trees, like a well-opened
flower, alone, bhikṣuṇī, are you now meditating? Without
a second companion, are you not afraid of delusion?

「娑罗树下坐，　　如华善开敷，独一比丘尼，　　汝今坐禅耶？更无第二伴，　　能不畏愚痴？」

At that time, the bhikṣuṇī Padmavatī thought, "Who is this, disturbing me, very deceitful, is it a human or a non-human?" Entering concentration to observe, knowing it was Māra, she spoke a verse:

尔时，莲华比丘尼即作是念：「此为是谁？扰乱于我，甚为欺诈。为是人耶？是非人乎？」入定观察，知是波旬，即说偈言：

Hundreds of thousands of deceitful thieves, all like you, cannot
move a single hair of mine, therefore I am fearless alone.

「百千奸伪贼，　　皆悉令如汝，不动我一毛，　　故独无所畏。」

At that time, Māra, the evil one, again spoke a verse:

尔时，魔王复说偈言：

I will now hide my form and enter the center of your belly
or enter between your eyebrows, causing you to be unable to see me.

「我今自隐形，　　入汝腹中央，或入汝眉间，　　令汝不得见。」

At that time, the bhikṣuṇī again replied in verse:

时比丘尼复以偈答：

My mind has attained mastery, well cultivating the meditative concentration of
the mind. Severing the great fetters, I am not at all
afraid of you. I have already vomited out all the fetters
attained the uprooting of the three defilements. The root of fear
is exhausted, therefore I have nothing to fear. I now dwell
here, not at all afraid of you. Even if your entire
army comes, I am still not afraid. Having severed all craving
extinguished the darkness of ignorance, attained the cessation of cessation, dwelling
in the undefiled Dharma. Therefore you should know, Māra, you have
fallen into a losing position.

「我心得自在，　　善修如意定，断绝大系缚，　　终不怖畏汝。我已吐诸结，　　得拔三垢根，怖畏根本尽，　　故我无所畏。我今住于此，　　都无畏汝心，汝军众尽来，　　我亦不怖畏。断除一切爱，　　灭诸无明暗，逮得于尽灭，　　安住无漏法。以是故当知，　　波旬堕负处。」

At that time, Māra thought, "The bhikṣuṇī Padmāvatī well understands my mind." He was sorrowful, regretful, ashamed, and returned to his palace.

尔时，波旬而作是念：「莲华色比丘尼善解我心。」忧愁悔恨，惭愧还宫。
