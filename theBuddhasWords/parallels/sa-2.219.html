SĀ² 2.219

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 219（二一九）

Parallel to: SN 5.9 / SĀ 1203

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, there was a bhikṣuṇī named Nāsikā who was staying at the royal park vihāra in Śrāvastī. In the morning, she put on her robes, took her alms bowl, and entered the city to beg for food. After eating, she washed her bowl, put away her sitting mat, and headed for the Netravana Grove. At that time, Māra the Evil One thought, "The Śramaṇa Gautama is at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. The bhikṣuṇī Nāsikā has put on her robes, taken her alms bowl, entered the city to beg for food, eaten, washed her bowl, put away her sitting mat, and is heading for the Netravana Grove. I shall create disturbances for her." Having thought this, he transformed himself into a young brahmin and spoke a verse at the side of the road:

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。时有比丘尼，名曰鼻［口＊（利／尒）］，在舍卫国王园精舍，于其晨朝，著衣持钵，入城乞食，食讫洗钵，收摄坐具，向得眼林。于时魔王而作是念：「沙门瞿昙在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。有鼻［口＊（利／尒）］比丘尼著衣持钵，入城乞食，食讫洗钵，收摄坐具，向得眼林。我当为其而作扰乱。」作是念已，化为摩纳，即于路侧，而说偈言：

Who creates form and appearance? Form and appearance create whom? From where
do form and appearance arise? Where do form and appearance go to?

「谁造于色像？　　色像造作谁？色像从何出？　　色像何所趣？」

At that time, the bhikṣuṇī, having heard this verse, thought, "Who is this, disturbing me, so deceitful? Is it a human or a non-human?" She entered concentration to observe and knew it was Māra the Evil One. She spoke a verse in reply:

时] cf.【丽】比丘尼闻斯偈已，而作是念：「此为是谁？恼乱于我，甚为欺诈。为是人耶？是非人乎？」入定观察，知是魔王，说偈报言：

Form and appearance are not self-made, nor are they created by
others. They arise from conditions, and when conditions are separated, they
disperse and cease. It is like planting seeds, which grow from
the earth. The aggregates, sense bases, and sense fields are combined
and this is form and appearance. Because of suffering, they grow
and because of suffering, they are destroyed. Cutting off all craving
extinguishing the darkness of ignorance, attaining the cessation of cessation, abiding
in the undefiled Dharma - therefore know that Pāpīyān has fallen into defeat.

「色像非自作，　　亦非他所造，众缘起而有，　　缘离则散灭。譬如殖种子，　　因地而生长，阴界诸入等，　　和合是色像。因苦故生长，　　因苦故散坏，断除一切爱，　　灭诸无明暗。逮得于尽灭，　　安住无漏法，以是故当知，　　波旬堕负处。」

At that time, Pāpīyān thought, "This bhikṣuṇī knows my mind well." He was distressed, remorseful, ashamed, and returned to his palace.

尔时，波旬而作是念：「此比丘尼善知我心。」忧愁悔恨，惭愧还宫。
