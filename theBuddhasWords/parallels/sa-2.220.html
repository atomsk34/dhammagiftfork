SĀ² 2.220

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 220（二二〇）

Parallel to: SN 5.4 / SĀ 1204

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, the bhikṣuṇī Vijaya, from the royal park monastery, put on her robe, took her alms bowl, entered the city to beg for food, finished eating, washed her bowl, put away her sitting mat, went to the forest of the blind, sat under a tree, and dwelled in the divine abode. At that time, Māra the Evil One thought: "The Śramaṇa Gautama is at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. The bhikṣuṇī Vijaya, from the royal park monastery, put on her robe, took her alms bowl, entered the city to beg for food, finished eating, washed her bowl, put away her sitting mat, went to the forest of the blind, sat under a tree, and dwelled in the divine abode. I shall disturb her." Having thought this, he transformed himself into a young brahmin and went to her, speaking a verse:

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，毘阇耶比丘尼从王园精舍，著衣持钵，入城乞食，食讫洗钵，收摄坐具，至得眼林，坐一树下，住于天住。尔时，魔王而作是念：「沙门瞿昙在舍卫国祇树给孤独园王园精舍。毘阇耶比丘尼著衣持钵，入城乞食，食讫洗钵，收摄坐具，至得眼林，坐一树下，住于天住。我当为其而作扰乱。」作是念已，化为摩纳，往诣其所，而说偈言：

You are now at the peak of youth and beauty, and
I am also young. Let us enjoy the five desires together
indulging in pleasure. Why do you sit here alone, not joining me?

「汝今极盛壮，　　我年亦复少，五欲共欢娱，　　放意而受乐，何以独坐此，　　而不与我俱？」

When the bhikṣuṇī heard this verse, she thought: "Who is this, coming to disturb me, so deceitful? Is it a human or a non-human?" She entered concentration to observe and knew it was Māra the Evil One. She spoke a verse in reply:

时比丘尼闻是偈已，而作是念：「此为是谁？来恼于我，甚为欺诈。为是人耶？是非人乎？」入定观察，知是魔王，说偈报言：

Let me indulge in music, dancing, and the other five desires
and give them all to you. They are not suitable for
me. All the pleasures of the human world, as well as
the five desires of the heavens, I will give them all
to you. They are not suitable for me at all. I
have cut off all craving, extinguished the darkness of ignorance, attained
the state of complete cessation, and abide in the undefiled Dharma
Therefore, know that Pāpīyān has lost.

「作乐纵歌舞，　　及余五欲乐，尽回用与汝，　　非我之所宜。人间一切乐，　　并及天五欲，尽回用与汝，　　都非我所宜。我断一切爱，　　灭诸无明暗，逮得于尽灭，　　安住无漏法。以是故当知，　　波旬堕负处。」

At that time, Pāpīyān thought: "This bhikṣuṇī knows my mind well." He was distressed, remorseful, and ashamed, and returned to his palace.

尔时，波旬而作是念：「此比丘尼善知我心。」忧愁悔恨，惭愧还宫。
