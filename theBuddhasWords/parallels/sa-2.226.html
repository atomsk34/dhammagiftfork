SĀ² 2.226

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 226（二二六）

Parallel to: SN 8.6 / SĀ 1210

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, Venerable Śāriputra was in the Dharma hall teaching the assembly. His words were complete, able to bring joy to the minds of the listeners. His speech was straightforward, causing those who heard to understand. His mind was without attachment. His explanations were clear. The assembly of bhikṣus listened with utmost sincerity. The listeners were delighted, respected, and paid homage with a sincere mind. They joyfully listened to the Dharma with equal joy.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，尊者舍利弗在讲堂中，为众说法，言音满足，能使听者心意喜乐，言辞正直，闻者开解，心无所为，所说辩了，诸比丘众，至心听受，听者悦豫，尊重恭敬，至心忆念，等同欢喜，听受其法。

At that time, Venerable Vāgīśa was in the assembly. He thought in his mind, "I wish to praise Śāriputra with a verse." Having thought this, he adjusted his robes, rose from his seat, joined his palms, and said to Śāriputra, "May the Venerable listen to what I have to say."

尔时，尊者婆耆奢在于会中，心作是念：「我欲以偈赞舍利弗。」作是念已，即正衣服，从坐而起，合掌白舍利弗言：「唯愿尊者听我所说。」

At that time, the Venerable told Vāgīśa, "If you have something to say, I will listen to your intention."

尔时，尊者告婆耆言：「若有所说，恣听汝意。」

He then spoke a verse, saying:

即说偈言：

Excellent, Śāriputra! You clearly know the path and the non-path, and
for the sake of the bhikṣu saṃgha, you have briefly and
extensively explained it. This Upaśānta has uttered a subtle sound, hearing
which all are delighted. His voice is harmonious, elegant, and wonderful
pleasant and very lovable, and the great assembly listens without weariness.

「善哉舍利弗！　　明知道非道，为诸比丘僧，　　略广而宣说。此优波室驶，　　出于微妙音，闻者皆悦豫，　　出声和雅妙，可乐甚可爱，　　大众听无厌。」

At that time, after Vāgīśa had spoken this verse, he was overjoyed and returned to his abode.

时婆耆奢说此偈已，欢喜踊跃，还于所止。
