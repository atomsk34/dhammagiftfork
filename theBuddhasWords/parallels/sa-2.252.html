SĀ² 2.252

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 252（二五二）

Parallel to: SN 8.12 / SĀ 1217

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, Venerable Vāgīśa was alone in a quiet place, diligently practicing and striving, not being negligent. Abiding in such a state, he attained the three insights. At that time, Venerable Vāgīśa thought, "I am now alone in a quiet place, having attained the three insights. I wish to praise the three insights I have attained." He then spoke a verse, saying:

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，尊者婆耆奢独处闲静，善能修己，勤行精进，终不放逸，住如是地，逮得三明。时尊者婆耆奢作是念：「我今独处闲静，逮得三明，我欲赞己所得三明。」即说偈言：

In the past, I was like a drunken fool, wandering through
various cities and towns. While wandering, I encountered the Buddha and
immediately received great blessings and benefits. The compassionate Gautama taught me
the true Dharma, and upon hearing the true Dharma, I immediately
attained pure faith. Thinking of leaving home, the great guide of
the world, guiding and transforming all without exception, men, women, the
young, the old, the sick, the Buddha is their friend, able
to show the way to goodness. Sentient beings are blind with
ignorance, and he leads and shows them the way. What is
meant by the way? It refers to the Four Noble Truths
From causes arises suffering. From suffering, one leaves home and sees
the Eightfold Right Path, extracting all sentient beings and leading them
to the peace of nirvāṇa. I practiced non-negligence in the forests
wilderness, and empty places, and attained the three insights, completing the Buddha's teachings.

「我昔如荒醉，　　经历诸城邑，游行得值佛，　　即蒙大福利。瞿昙大悲愍，　　为我说正法，我闻正法已，　　即得清净信。思惟出家者，　　世间大导师，导化无不普，　　男女及长幼，中年及老病，　　佛曰是亲友，能示善方所，　　众生无明盲，将导示其门。　　云何名为门？所谓四真谛，　　从因则生苦，从苦得出家，　　见于八正道，拔出诸众生，　　安隐趣涅槃。我修不放逸，　　林野空寂处，获得于三明，　　作佛教已讫。」
