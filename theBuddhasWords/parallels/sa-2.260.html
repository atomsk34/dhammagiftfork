SĀ² 2.260

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 260（二六〇）

Parallel to: AN 5.31 (~) / SĀ 94

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, there was a young brahmin named Saṅgharakṣa who went to where the Buddha was, greeted the Buddha, and sat to one side. He then said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! How does one observe an unwholesome person?"

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，有摩纳名僧伽罗，往诣佛所，问讯佛已，在一面坐，即白佛言：「世尊！云何观察不善丈夫？」

The Buddha said, "It is like observing the moon."

佛言：「譬如观月。」

He further asked, "How does one observe a superior person of good qualities?"

又问：「云何观察善胜丈夫？」

The Buddha again replied, "It is also like observing the moon."

佛复答曰：「亦如观月。」

At that time, the brahmin student again addressed the Buddha, saying, "How does one observe an inferior person as if it were the moon?"

尔时，摩纳复白佛言：「云何不善丈夫观之如月？」

The Buddha said, "An inferior person is like the moon on the sixteenth day, gradually decreasing in brightness until it is completely gone. In the Buddha's teachings, one may have faith and uphold the precepts, but read and recite little, or practice giving little. Later, one becomes lazy and diligent, gradually losing faith, breaking the precepts, and not giving. Associating with bad friends, one does not visit the monastery to inquire about the Dharma. Not listening to the Dharma, one creates unwholesome actions with body, speech, and mind. Because of creating evil, when the body perishes and life ends, one falls into the evil realms. Therefore, know that an inferior person is like the moon gradually decreasing until it is completely gone."

佛言：「不善丈夫如十六日月光，渐减圆满，转亏，乃至于尽损减不现。如佛法中不能信心受持禁戒，少所读诵，若修少施，于其后时，懈怠不勤，渐失信心，毁犯禁戒，复不布施，亲近恶友，不至僧坊咨受于法，不听法故，身口意业造于不善，以造恶故，身坏命终，堕于恶道。是故当知恶丈夫者，犹如彼月渐渐损减，乃至灭尽。」

He further asked, "How is a superior person also like the moon?"

又问：「云何善胜丈夫亦复如月？」

The Buddha said, "It is like the first day of the lunar month, with bright light gradually increasing until the fifteenth day is full. In the Buddha's teachings, one may have faith and practice the precepts, study widely, practice giving, eliminate wrong views, and cultivate right views. In the Buddha's teachings, one attains pure faith, upholds the precepts, studies widely, gives generously without stinginess, and possesses right views. With faith, upholding the precepts, studying widely, and giving generously, one gradually increases. This superior person's physical, verbal, and mental actions associate with good friends and cultivate all goodness. When the body perishes and life ends, one is reborn in the heavens. Therefore, know that a superior person is also like the moon."

佛言：「譬如初月，光明炽然，渐渐增长，至十五日圆满具足。如佛法中，能有信心修行禁戒，习于多闻，修于布施，却除邪见，修于正见，于佛法中，得纯信心，坚持禁戒，善修多闻，能施不悭，具于正见，信心持戒，多闻惠施，以渐增长，是善丈夫身口意行，亲近善友，具修诸善，身坏命终，得生天上。是故当知善丈夫者亦复如月。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

It is like the full moon, in the midst of the sky
its round light shining brightly, outshining all the stars. Also like a
person with faith, upholding precepts, learning, giving, and free from greed and
jealousy, among those who are jealous, like the moon outshining the stars.

「譬如盛满月，　　处于虚空中，圆光甚晖曜，　　映蔽于诸星。亦如具信人，　　戒闻舍贪嫉，于诸嫉妬者，　　如月蔽众星。」

At that time, the novice monk Saṃgharakṣa, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and departed. The bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

时僧伽罗摩纳闻佛所说，踊跃而去。诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
