SĀ² 2.261

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 261（二六一）

Parallel to: AN 3.57 / SĀ 95 / SĀ³ 3.2

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, there was a brāhman named Jñātiputra who went to where the Buddha was, exchanged greetings, and sat to one side. He then said to the Buddha, "I have heard people say that the World-Honored One said, 'Only give to me, not to others; only give to my disciples, not to the disciples of others. If you give to me and my disciples, you will receive great rewards; if you give to others and the disciples of others, you will not receive rewards.' Is this true? Are these not slanderous words of the world?"

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，有婆罗门名曰生听，往诣佛所，问讯已讫，在一面坐，即白佛言：「我曾闻人说：『世尊言：「但施于我，莫施余人，但施我弟子，莫施他弟子。若能施我及我弟子，得大果报；若施他人及余弟子，不得果报。」』实说是不？将非世人诽谤者乎？」

The Buddha said, "This is false and slanderous. I never said such words. If one speaks like this, it creates two kinds of difficulties: one is the difficulty of obstruction, and the other is the difficulty of the recipient receiving less. If one speaks like this, it greatly diminishes. When the body perishes and life ends, one will fall into the three evil paths. You should now know that even the water used to wash the alms bowl, I say that giving it to insects and ants brings great merit. How much more so for giving to me? I truly said, 'Giving to those who uphold the precepts brings great merit, while giving to those who break the precepts brings little merit.'"

佛言：「此实虚妄，诽谤于我，都无此语。若如是说，作二种难：一者遮难，二者受者得减损难。若如此说，作大损减，身坏命终，堕三恶道。汝今应知，乃至洗钵之水，我尚说言：『施与虫蚁，获大福报。』况于施我？实作是语：『施持戒者，得福甚多，施破戒者，得福尠少。』」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

I always praise all acts of giving, but giving to the unvirtuous
brings little merit, while giving to the virtuous brings great fruit. Black
white, red, and blue oxen, when they give birth to calves, each
is different. When used to pull a cart, one takes their strength
not caring about their birth. People are also like this. Whether they
are kshatriyas, brahmins, vaishyas, shudras, candalas, or rich, if they can uphold
pure precepts, giving to them brings great fruit. It is like carrying
a bundle of hemp and discarding it to take precious jewels. The
foolish and ignorant, who have never heard the Dharma, are unable to
cultivate the holy life. Giving to them brings little fruit. If one
associates with the wise and holy, the fully enlightened ones and their
shravakas, and is able to believe in the Tathāgata, with the roots
of faith firmly established, one will be born in noble circumstances and
ultimately attain nirvana.

「一切布施处，　　我常生赞叹，破戒得福少，　　持戒获大果。黑白赤青牛，　　生犊各差别，驾用取其力，　　不择其产种。人亦复如是，　　刹利婆罗门，毘舍首陀罗，　　真陀罗富且，能持于净戒，　　施之得大果，如似赍麻干，　　舍之取珍宝。𪧀愚无智者，　　未曾听闻法，不能修梵行，　　施之获少果。若亲近贤圣，　　正觉及声闻，能信于善逝，　　信根立坚固，生处恒尊贵，　　最后得涅槃。」

At that time, Sheng Tingwen joyfully accepted and practiced what the Buddha taught.

尔时，生听闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
