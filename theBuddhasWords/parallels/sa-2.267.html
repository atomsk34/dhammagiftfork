SĀ² 2.267

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 267（二六七）

Parallel to: AN 4.36 / SĀ 101 / EĀ 38.3 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Kosala and arrived at the village of Śālavana. At that time, the World-Honored One left the road and sat upright under a tree, with his mind focused in front of him. There was then a brāhman named Dhūma who came later than the Buddha. Seeing the thousand-spoked wheel marks in the Buddha's footprints, he was amazed and had never seen anything like it before. He thought to himself, "I have never seen anyone with such footprints. I should follow these footprints to find out whose they are." Having thought this, he immediately followed the footprints and went to where the Buddha was. He looked up at the Buddha's dignified face, with a pleasant expression that inspired faith and respect. The Buddha's features were serene and his mind was also serene, having attained the supreme state of mind, the peace of tranquility. His body was the true golden color, like a golden tower. He then said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! You must be a heavenly being.

一时，佛在憍萨罗国游行，至沙林聚落。尔时，世尊舍于道次，在一树下，正身端坐，系念在前。时有一婆罗门，姓曰烟氏，在佛后来，见佛迹中，千辐轮相，怪未曾有。即自思惟：「我未见人有如是迹，我当推寻是何人迹。」作是念已，即寻其迹，往] cf.【丽】至佛所，瞻仰尊颜，容色悦豫，覩者信敬，诸相寂定，心意亦定，得最上调心，寂灭之寂，身真金色，犹如金楼。即白佛言：「世尊！汝当得天也。」

The Buddha said, "Brāhman! I am not a heavenly being.

佛言：「婆罗门！我不得天也。」

The brāhman said, "Are you an asura, a nāga, a gandharva, a yakṣa, a kiṃnara, or a mahoraga?"

婆罗门言：「当得阿修罗，为得龙、揵闼婆、夜叉、紧那罗、摩睺罗伽也。」

The Buddha said, "I am none of these."

佛言：「我皆不得。」

The brāhman said, "Then you are a human being."

婆罗门言：「汝得人也。」

The Buddha said, "I am not a human being either."

佛言：「我不得人也。」

The brāhman said, "I asked you if you are a god, a dragon, an asura, a gandharva, a yakṣa, a kiṃnara, a mahoraga, or a human being, and you said you are not any of these. Then what are you?"

婆罗门言：「我问汝为得天及龙、阿修罗、揵闼婆、夜叉、紧那罗、摩睺罗伽，及人，汝皆言不得，为何所得也？」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

I am not a god, dragon, asura, kiṃnara, mahoraga, gandharva, nor a yakṣa or
human being. My taints are exhausted and afflictions are severed. Although I am tamed
like a dragon or elephant, I am not controlled by others. In order to
sever doubts and control others, I sever craving, attain liberation, and leave all realms
of existence. I know everything and sever future births, like a fully bloomed lotus
Abiding in the water, I grow without being attached to the water. Pure, fragrant
and delightful to people, I am not defiled by the eight dharmas like a
lotus. I am also like this, born in the world, not attached to worldly
dharmas. For immeasurable eons, I have always observed the suffering of conditioned phenomena and
their objects. All those who are born will eventually pass away. I have removed
dust and defilements, severed all habits, pulled out the poisonous arrow, severed afflictions, and
attained the end of birth and death.

「我非天龙阿修罗，　　紧那摩睺干闼婆，亦非夜叉及以人，　　我漏已尽断烦恼。我虽调驯如龙象，　　终不为他所制御，不为他制断疑故，　　断爱解脱离诸趣。一切尽知断后生，　　如芬陀利善开敷，处于水中得增长，　　终不为水之所著。清净香洁人所乐，　　八法不污如莲华，我亦如是生世间，　　同于世法不染著。无量劫来常观察，　　诸行所缘受苦恼，诸受生者皆终没，　　远尘离垢断众习，拔出毒箭断烦恼，　　皆得尽于生死际。

"For this reason, he is called the Buddha."

「以是之故，号之为佛。」

The Brāhman of the Smoke Clan, having heard the Buddha's teaching, was delighted and left.

烟姓婆罗门闻佛所说，欢喜而去。

Pride, Ujjayinī, Born to Listen, Extremely Old, Bhikṣu, Planting, and Brahmā Heaven, Buddha, and Wheel Mark as the tenth.

慢、优竭提　生听、极老　比丘、种作　及梵天　佛陀、轮相为第十
