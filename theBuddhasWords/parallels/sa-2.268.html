SĀ² 2.268

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 268（二六八）

Parallel to: Snp 1.7 / SĀ 102

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was in Rajagriha, at the Kalanda Bamboo Grove. At that time, the World-Honored One put on his robe, took his alms bowl, entered the city to beg for food, and arrived at the house of the Brahmin Dala of the Fire Clan. At that time, Dala of the Fire Clan was making offerings to the fire at the middle gate. When the Buddha came to the gate, Dala saw the Buddha coming from afar and said to him, "Stop! Stop! Chandalas! Do not come here."

一时，佛在王舍城迦兰陀竹林。尔时，世尊著衣持钵，入城乞食，到火姓达赖殊婆罗门家。尔时，火姓达赖殊于中门前祀祠于火，佛来至门，达赖殊遥见佛来，而语之言：「住！住！旃陀罗！莫来至此。」

The Buddha told him, "Do you recognize a chandala? Do you know the ways of a chandala?"

佛告之言：「汝识旃陀罗，知旃陀罗法不？」

The Brahmin replied, "I do not recognize or know a chandala or the ways of a chandala. Do you recognize a chandala and know the ways of a chandala?"

婆罗门答言：「不识不知旃陀罗、旃陀罗法，汝识旃陀罗，知旃陀罗法耶？」

The Buddha told him, "I recognize a chandala and know the ways of a chandala."

佛告之言：「我识旃陀罗，知旃陀罗法。」

At that time, the Brahmin rose from his seat, prepared a seat for the Buddha, and said to the Buddha, "Please explain the ways of a chandala to me."

时婆罗门于坐处起，为佛敷座，而白佛言：「汝为我说旃陀罗法。」

At that time, the World-Honored One sat on the seat and spoke a verse:

尔时，世尊即坐其座，而说偈言：

「恶性喜瞋久嫌恨，　　为人刚恶怀憍慢，行于倒见及幻惑，　　当知此是旃陀罗。含忿嫉妬好恶欲，　　难可调化无惭愧，行如是者旃陀罗。　　伤害胎生及卵生，心无慈愍害生类，　　伤残毁害于四生，当知是此旃陀罗。　　若人赍物涉道路，聚落空处有财宝，　　悉能劫夺并害命，行如是者旃陀罗。　　造行众恶无悔恨，是则名为旃陀罗。　　舍自己妻及婬女，邪奸他妇无所避，　　如是亦名旃陀罗。于己种姓及亲友，　　如是之处造邪恶，不择好恶而奸婬，　　是亦名为旃陀罗。以理问义为倒说，　　如是亦名旃陀罗。自叹己德毁呰他，　　贪著我慢下贱行，如是亦名旃陀罗。　　横加诽谤极愚痴，为小利故生诽谤，　　如是亦名旃陀罗。自己有过推与他，　　专行欺诳谤毁人，如是亦名旃陀罗。　　多有财宝饶亲族，自食好美与他恶，　　如是亦名旃陀罗。己到他家得美食，　　他若来时与麁恶，如是亦名旃陀罗。　　父母衰老离盛壮，而不孝顺加供养，　　如是亦名旃陀罗。父母兄弟及姊妹，　　骂詈恶口无逊弟，如是亦名旃陀罗。　　沙门及与婆罗门，中时垂至不施与，　　更加骂詈而瞋恚，如是亦名旃陀罗。　　沙门及与婆罗门，贫穷乞匃请向家，　　不与饮食亦不施，如是亦名旃陀罗。　　毁骂于佛及声闻，出家在家加毁骂，　　如是亦名旃陀罗。竟非罗汉诈称是，　　于天人中为残贼，生于大家婆罗门，　　韦陀典籍悉通利，数数造作诸恶业，　　种姓不能遮谤毁，亦不能遮地狱报，　　现前为人所骂辱，未来之世堕恶趣，　　生旃陀罗须陀延。得好名称必不闻，　　又得盛乐生梵处，种姓不能遮梵处，　　现在称叹终生天。我今为汝显示说，　　如是之事应当知，种姓不是婆罗门，　　种姓不是旃陀罗，净业得作婆罗门，　　恶行得为旃陀罗。」

The brahmin, having heard this verse, praised, saying, "Indeed, indeed! Great Vows! It is truly as you have said, Great Sage! It is not because of one's family that one is a brahmin, nor because of one's family that one is a chandala. One who practices well is a brahmin, and one who does evil is a chandala." Having heard this verse, the brahmin was delighted and believed. He filled a bowl with food and offered it to the Buddha, but the Buddha did not accept it. Why? Because he had already received the food of the Dharma. The brahmin said to the Buddha, "To whom should this food be given?"

婆罗门闻是偈，赞言：「如是！如是！大精进！实如所说，大牟尼！不以种姓是婆罗门，不以种姓旃陀罗。善能修行是婆罗门，作恶行者旃陀罗。」婆罗门闻是偈已，欢喜信解，盛满钵饮食，持以奉佛，佛不为受。何以故？以是说法食故。婆罗门白佛言：「此食当以施谁？」

The Buddha said, "I do not see any śramaṇa, brāhmaṇa, god, demon, or Brahmā who can digest this food. It is impossible. You should put this food in clean water free from insects."

佛言：「我不见沙门、婆罗门、若天、若魔、若梵，能消此食者，无有是处。当以此食著净无虫水中。」

The brahmin then put the food in water free from insects. Smoke and flames arose together, with a hissing sound. At that time, the brahmin gave rise to an unprecedented thought, "Even the World-Honored Buddha displays great supernatural powers with regard to food." The brahmin returned to where the Buddha was and said to the Buddha, "I only wish that the World-Honored One would allow me to go forth and attain the stages of the path."

婆罗门即以此食著无虫水中，烟炎俱起，滑滑大声。尔时，婆罗门生未曾有想，佛世尊尚于食所，出大神足。婆罗门还至佛所，白佛言：「唯愿世尊听我出家，得及道次。」

The Buddha said, "Welcome, monk! Your beard and hair fall out by themselves, and the Dharma robes cover your body." He immediately attained full ordination, as is the way of going forth. In an empty and quiet place, he diligently practiced alone. The reason for the sons of noble families to shave their beards and hair and wear the Dharma robes is to practice the unsurpassed holy life. When the sons of noble families have established the holy life, accomplished what is to be done, not accepted future existence, and attained arahantship, they attain the bliss of liberation. They speak a verse, saying:

佛言：「善来比丘！须发自落，法服著身。」即得具戒，如出家法，于空静处，独己精修。所以族姓子剃除须发，被服法衣者，为正修无上梵行故。族姓子梵行已立，所作已办，不受后有，成阿罗汉，得解脱乐。而说偈言：

In the past, I was deluded about the true and genuine, foolishly creating wrong
practices. I did not recognize the path of purity, nor did I know the
path of death. With deluded thoughts, I gave rise to false calculations, toiling and
serving fire. It was all for empty and false things, in vain, with nothing
gained. Now I have encountered the Heaven among Heavens, and the darkness is illuminated
by the light of kindness. I have attained the bliss within bliss, fully ordained
and attained the three insights. Within the teachings of the Buddha, what is to
be done is all completed. Although I was originally a brahmin, I was truly
a chandala. Today, I am truly a brahmin of pure conduct, far removed from
the mud. Having purified myself and bathed, I have gone beyond the other shore
of the Vedas.

「我昔迷正真，　　愚惑造邪行，不识清净道，　　又不知死路。妄想生横计，　　劬劳承奉火，空为虚妄事，　　徒损无所获。今遇天中天，　　黑暗蒙惠光，获得乐中乐。　　具戒得三明，于佛教法中，　　所作皆已讫。本虽婆罗门，　　实是旃陀罗，今日真实是，　　净行婆罗门，远离于淤泥，　　净自澡浴已，过韦陀彼岸。」
