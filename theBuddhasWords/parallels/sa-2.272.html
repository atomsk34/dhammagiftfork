SĀ² 2.272

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 272（二七二）

Parallel to: SN 1.39 / SĀ 1274

Thus have I heard. Once, the Buddha was staying at the Monkey's Opposite Shore Monastery north of Vaiśālī. At that time, the celestial maidens Pāpīyānī and Śacī, their radiance doubled as usual, went to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. At that time, these two celestial maidens emitted a great light, illuminating the monkeys and Vaiśālī, all becoming very bright. At that time, the celestial maiden Śacī spoke a verse, saying:

如是我闻：一时，佛在毘舍离北猕猴彼岸精舍之中。时波纯提天女、拙罗天女，光色倍常，往诣佛所，顶礼佛足，在一面坐。时此二天女放大光明，遍照猕猴及毘舍离，悉皆大明。时拙罗天女即说偈言：

The World-Honored One, the Bhagavān, the unsurpassed, perfect, and complete enlightenment
is staying in the great forest of Vaiśālī. The celestial maidens
Pāpīyānī, Śacī, and Pāpīyānī, having bowed their heads at the feet
of the Honored One, said: 'We have heard that in the
past, the one who was able to skillfully proclaim the Dharma
the Muni, the World-Honored One, is now expounding it. Those who
give rise to criticism and slander of such profound teachings are
called foolish and will certainly fall into evil destinies in the
future. Those who are able to praise the holy Dharma and
accomplish mindfulness are called wise and will certainly be reborn in
good places in the future.

「世尊婆伽婆，　　无上等正觉，在于毘舍离，　　住于大林中。求迦尼娑天，　　并及于拙罗，波纯提女等，　　稽首尊足下。我往] cf.【丽】昔曾闻，　　能善称说法，牟尼世尊者，　　今现在演说。诸有生讥毁，　　如斯深法者，是则名愚痴，　　后必堕恶趣。有能赞圣法，　　成就具于念，是名有智者，　　后必生善处。」

At that time, the celestial maiden Pāpīyānī also spoke a verse, saying:

时求迦尼娑天女复说偈言：

One should cultivate goodness with mouth and mind, and should not create
various evils. The body should not inflict small evils upon the world
Observing the nature and characteristics of desire as empty, cultivate the mind
of mindfulness. If one does not enjoy suffering oneself, one should not
create diminishing karma.

「口意宜修善，　　不应造众恶，身不以小恶，　　加害于世间。观欲性相空，　　修于念觉意，若自不乐苦，　　莫作损减业。」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the celestial maiden, "So it is! So it is! As you have said." When the celestial maidens heard the Buddha's words, they rejoiced and departed.

尔时，世尊告天女言：「如是！如是！如汝所说。」时天女等闻佛说已，欢喜而去。
