SĀ² 2.276

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 276（二七六）

Parallel to: SN 6.9 / SN 6.10 / AN 4.3 (~) / AN 10.89 / Snp 3.10 / SĀ 1278 / EĀ 21.5

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at the Karanda Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha. At that time, Devadatta's friend Kātyāyana went to where the Buddha was and stood to one side. The Buddha said to Kātyāyana, "You have a connection with Śāriputra. Do not give rise to resentment. Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana are pure in their practice of the Brahma way of life, their minds are gentle. Do not give rise to resentment, for you will suffer for a long time."

一时，佛在王舍城迦兰陀竹林。尔时，提婆达多友瞿迦梨，往诣佛所，在一面立。佛告瞿迦梨：「汝于舍利弗有缘，莫生嫌想。舍利弗、目连净修梵行，心意柔软，汝莫生嫌恨，长夜受诸苦恼。」

Kātyāyana said, "I believe the Buddha's words. I follow the Buddha. But Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana truly have evil desires. Evil desires truly have mastery over them. They follow evil desires."

瞿迦梨言：「我信佛语，我随于佛，然舍利弗、目连实有恶欲，恶欲于彼，实得自在，彼随恶欲。」

The Buddha again told Kātyāyana, "Do not give rise to resentment towards those two." This was repeated up to three times. Although Kātyāyana heard the Buddha's words, his evil mind did not change. He left the Buddha and went away. Not far from the Buddha, a malignant tumor grew on his body. At first it was the size of a mustard seed, but in a moment it was the size of a bean. It gradually grew larger, like a jujube fruit. His body rotted and oozed pus and blood. His body decayed and he died, falling into the Great Lotus Hell.

佛复告瞿迦梨：「汝今勿于彼二人所，生嫌恨心。」如是至三。而瞿迦梨虽闻佛言，恶心不改，舍佛而去。去佛不远，身生恶疮，初如芥子，须臾之顷，犹如豆许，复渐长大，如毘梨果，身体烂溃，脓血流出，身坏命终，堕大莲华地狱。

At that time, three gods, their radiance more than usual, came to where the Buddha was in the middle of the night. They bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet and stood to one side. The first god said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Kātyāyana died last night." The second god said, "He fell into the Great Lotus Hell." The third god then spoke a verse:

时有三天，光色倍常，于其夜中，来至佛所，顶礼佛足，在一面立。第一天白佛言：「世尊！瞿迦梨是夜命终。」第二天言：「堕大莲华地狱。」第三天即说偈言：

The axe of a person's life
is in their mouth, because of
their evil words, they cut themselves
They should praise but slander, they
should slander but praise, their mouths
utter frivolous words, later they will
suffer the consequences. Frivolous words can
steal wealth, therefore small faults, slandering
the Buddha and sages, is called
a great affliction. Suffering for a
long time, fully a hundred thousand
entering the Nīrabuta and thirty-six, entering
the Avīci, and even falling into
those five Avīcis. Slandering the sages
with mouth and mind creating evil
entering these hells.

「夫人生世，　　斧在口中，　　由其恶言，自斩其身。　　应赞而毁，　　应毁而赞，口出绮语，　　后受苦殃。　　绮语夺财，是故小过，　　谤佛圣贤，　　是名大患。受苦长远，　　具满百千，　　入尼罗浮，及三十六，　　入阿浮陀，　　乃至堕彼，五阿浮陀。　　诽谤贤圣，　　口意造恶，入斯地狱。」

At that time, the three gods bowed at the Buddha's feet and returned to their heavenly palaces.

时彼三天礼佛足已，还于天宫。

At that time, the Buddha told the bhikṣus, "Do you wish to hear about the lifespan of the Avīci Hell?"

尔时，佛告诸比丘：「汝等欲闻彼阿浮陀地狱寿命长短以不？」

The bhikṣus said, "Please tell us. We will listen and remember it."

比丘白言：「愿为我说，我等闻已，信受忆持。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Twenty kārṣāpaṇa measures of sesame can fill a cart, and a long-lived person can take one grain every hundred years. In this way, all the sesame will be exhausted, but the lifespan in Avīci Hell is still not exhausted. Twenty Avīci hells make one Nirarbuda Hell. Twenty Nirarbuda hells make one Hahava Hell. Twenty Hahava hells make one Aṭaṭa Hell. Twenty Aṭaṭa hells make one Huhuva Hell. Twenty Huhuva hells make one Padma Hell. Twenty Padma hells make one Mahāpadma Hell. The bhikṣu Gauḍīya, due to slandering Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana, fell into this Mahāpadma Hell."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「二十佉利胡麻得波罗捺，满溢一车，有长寿人，足满百年取其一粒，如是胡麻一切都尽，此阿浮陀地狱所得寿命，犹故未尽。此二十阿浮陀觝，成一尼罗浮陀。二十尼罗浮陀觝，一呵咤咤。二十呵咤咤觝，一睺睺。二十睺睺觝，一莲华地狱。二十莲华地狱觝，一大莲华地狱。瞿迦梨比丘以谤舍利弗、目连故，堕是大莲华地狱中。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "Even a burnt pillar should not be slandered, let alone sentient beings."

佛告诸比丘：「被烧燋柱，尚不应谤，况情识类。」

Having spoken this, the Buddha finished. The bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them.

佛说是已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
