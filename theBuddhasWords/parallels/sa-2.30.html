SĀ² 2.30

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 30（三〇）

Parallel to: SN 4.23 / SĀ 1091

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying at the Vipula Mountain in Rajagriha, in the cave of the seven-leaved tree. At that time, there was a bhiksu named Sudatta, who lived alone in the black stone cave on the mountain of the solitary immortals in Rajagriha. He diligently practiced diligently, without being negligent, and eliminated the view of self, attaining temporary liberation, realizing it for himself. However, he regressed and lost it. The second, third, up to the sixth time, he also regressed and lost it. The bhiksu thought: "Now I am alone, practicing diligently, but I have regressed six times. If I regress again, I will cut myself with a knife." The Mara King knew that the Buddha was staying at the Vipula Mountain in Rajagriha, in the cave of the seven-leaved tree, and that the disciple of Gautama named Sudatta was also living alone in the black stone cave on the mountain of the solitary immortals in Rajagriha, diligently practicing diligently, with an undistracted mind, attaining temporary liberation, realizing it for himself. After attaining it, he regressed and lost it, like this six times. At that time, the Mara King thought: "If the bhiksu Sudatta attains it a seventh time, he will definitely injure himself and leave the realm of Mara." Having thought this, he took a lapis lazuli zither and went to where the Buddha was, strumming the zither and speaking a verse:

一时，佛在王舍城毘婆波世山七叶窟中。尔时，有一比丘名曰求[真／心，独住仙山黑石窟中，处于闲静，勤行精进，以不放逸，断于我见，得时解脱，自身作证，复还退失。第二第三，乃至第六，亦还退失。比丘念言：「我今独处，修行精进，六返退失，若更退失，以刀自割。」魔王波旬知佛在王舍城毘婆波世山七叶窟中，瞿昙弟子名曰求［真／心］，亦在王舍城独住仙山黑石窟中，勤行精进，心不放逸，得时解脱，自身作证。得已，退失，如是六返。尔时，魔王而作是念：「求［真／心］比丘若第七得，必自伤害，出魔境界。」作是念已，捉琉璃] cf.【丽】琴，往到佛所，扣琴作偈：

Great wisdom, great diligence, with great supernatural powers, having attained
mastery over the Dharma, with blazing radiance. Your disciples, the
Shravakas, are now about to harm themselves. The supreme one
among humans, you should now stop them. Why do they
delight in your Dharma? Why do they learn to die for others?

「大智大精进，　　有大神通达，于法得自在，　　威光极炽盛。汝声闻弟子，　　今将欲自害，人中最上者，　　汝今应遮断。云何乐汝法？　　何故学他死？」

At that time, after the Mara King had spoken this verse, the Buddha told Mara, "O Mara! You are now the great friend of all the negligent ones. What you have just said is for yourself, not for that bhiksu."

尔时，魔王说是偈已，佛告魔言：「波旬！汝今乃是诸放逸者之大亲友，汝今所说自为说耳，乃不为彼比丘说也。」

At that time, the World Honored One again spoke a verse:

尔时，世尊复说偈言：

If one is not timid and weak, and diligently practices
diligently, always delighting in meditation, cultivating various good deeds day
and night, drying up the afflictions of desire, destroying your
army of Mara, now abandoning the last body, forever entering into nirvana.

「若人不怯弱，　　坚修行精进，恒乐于禅定，　　昼夜修众善。干竭爱欲使，　　坏汝魔军众，今舍后边身，　　永入于涅槃。」

At that time, the Demon King was sorrowful, suffering and distressed, losing the crystal zither. He was sorrowful, regretful and remorseful, returning to his own palace.

尔时，魔王忧悲苦恼，失琉璃琴，愁毒悔恨，还本宫殿。

The Buddha said to the bhikṣus, "I will go with you to the mountain of the seer to find the bhikṣu Sthavira." The Buddha led the bhikṣus to where Sthavira was. He saw that Sthavira's body in the east was like a mass of smoke. The Buddha said to the bhikṣus, "Do you see this mass of smoke?" The bhikṣus said, "We see it, World-Honored One." The same was the case in the south, west, and north. The Buddha said to the bhikṣus, "This is Māra hiding his form and circling around Sthavira, seeking his mind and consciousness." The Buddha said to the bhikṣus, "The bhikṣu Sthavira has entered nirvāṇa, without any consciousness, and has gone nowhere.

佛告诸比丘：「当共汝等诣仙人山求［真／心］比丘所。」佛将诸比丘诣求［真／心］所，见求［真／心］尸东犹如烟聚。佛告诸比丘：「汝等见此烟聚已不？」诸比丘言：「已见，世尊。」尸南西北亦如是聚。佛告比丘：「此是波旬隐形遶求［真／心］所，觅其心识。」佛告比丘：「求［真／心］比丘以入涅槃，无有神识，无所至方。」

At that time, the Demon King transformed his form into a monk and spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，魔王化形摩] cf.【丽】纳，而说偈言：

Above, below, and in the four directions, searching for Sthavira's consciousness, I
do not know where it has gone. Where has his consciousness gone?

「上下及四方，　　推求求［真／心］识，莫知所至方，　　神识竟何趣？」

At that time, the World Honored One told Pāpīyān, "This strong man has defeated your army and entered nirvāṇa."

尔时，世尊告波旬言：「如此健夫，破汝军众，以入涅槃。」

The Buddha having spoken thus, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说是已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
