SĀ² 2.307

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 307（三〇七）

Parallel to: SN 2.30 / SĀ 1308

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha. At that time, there were six celestial beings who were originally followers of the six non-Buddhist teachers. Their names were: Duryodhana, Vaśavartin, Vibhūṣaṇa, Jayanta, Kāla, and Māyā. These six celestial beings all came to where the Buddha was in the night, sat to one side, and their celestial light was twice as bright as usual, illuminating the Jetavana Grove with a great radiance. At that time, Duryodhana spoke a verse, saying:

一时，佛在王舍城迦兰陀竹林。当于尔时，有六天子，本是外道六师徒党，一名难胜，二名自在，三名显现，四名决胜，五名时起，六名轻弄。此六天子咸于其夜，来诣佛所，在一面坐。斯诸天光倍胜于常，遍照祇洹，赫然大明。尔时，难胜即说偈言：

The bhikṣus who are criticized and reviled should restrain themselves in the four seasons
Having seen and heard of their dwelling, this person is free from all evil.

「可讥毁比丘，　　四时自禁制，见闻其住已，　　是人离诸恶。」

The celestial being Vaśavartin also spoke a verse, saying:

自在天子复说偈言：

The ascetic who is criticized and reviled should restrain his own body. Cutting off
evil speech and anger, suffering and happiness are the same as the World-Honored One
In the presence of the Lord of the Dharma, do not create various evils.

「苦行可讥毁，　　捡摄于己身，断恶口忿诤，　　苦乐同世尊，于其法主所，　　不造作众恶。」

The celestial being Abhibhū also spoke a verse, saying:

显现天子复说偈言：

Cutting, injuring, sacrificing, burning in fire, etc., all have
no retribution of good or evil, as taught by Kāśyapa.

「斩截及伤害，　　祠祀火烧等，皆无善恶报，　　迦叶之所说。」

The celestial being Vijitāvin also spoke a verse, saying:

决胜天子复说偈言：

The Nirgrantha Jñātiputra always speaks such words: 'Practicing asceticism
for a long time, cutting off false speech, not
far from arahantship, falling into the number of the World-Honored One.

「尼干若提子，　　常说如是言，长夜修苦行，　　断除于妄语，离罗汉不远，　　堕于世尊数。」

At that time, the World-Honored One replied in verse:

尔时，世尊以偈答言：

From now on, I will let you, whether alone or in
a group, I see you all as contemptible and filthy, all
the same as a corpse. How can a jackal be the
same as the lion king? Your honored naked ascetics, extremely evil
and fond of false speech, such non-Buddhists are far from arahantship.

「从今令汝等，　　独己若多众，我观皆鄙秽，　　悉同于死尸，云何以野干，　　同彼师子王？汝尊裸形众，　　极恶喜妄语，如斯外道等，　　彼去罗汉远。」

At that time, another god-son also spoke a verse:

时有天子复说偈言：

Those who practice austerities are deeply worthy of criticism and ridicule. Although
dwelling in solitude, they only engage in toilsome activities. I wish to
protect them and be their guide. They will definitely proceed to the
realm of form and be born in the Brahma world with joy.

「作彼苦行者，　　深为可讥毁，虽处于闲静，　　徒为劳苦事。愿当拥护彼，　　为其作教导，必趣于色有，　　生梵世欢喜。」

At that time, the World-Honored One again replied in verse:

尔时，世尊复以偈答：

All forms in this world, here and
elsewhere, and in the sky, with great
light, all of these are caught in
Māra's snare, like a fisherman covering fish
with a net.

「世界所有色，　　此处及他处，并在虚空中，　　有大光明者，如是等一切，　　悉入魔羂弶，譬如捕鱼师，　　以网掩众鱼。」

Another god also spoke a verse:

又有一天复说偈言：

Speaking of the faults of existence and desire, and all the
evils of delusion and illusion, completely cutting them off, praising the
cutting off of the bonds of desire. One should pay homage
to them, make offerings and praise them, because they are the World-Honored One.

「说有及欲过，　　并诸痴幻恶，一切悉断除，　　赞叹断欲结。应向彼礼拜，　　供养以称赞，所以如是者，　　彼即世尊故。」

Another god also spoke a verse:

有一天子复说偈言：

Speaking of the faults of existence and anger, and all the
evils of delusion and illusion, completely cutting them off, praising the
cutting off of the bonds of anger. One should pay homage
to them, make offerings and praise them, because they are the World-Honored One.

「说有及瞋过，　　并诸痴幻恶，一切悉断除，　　赞叹断瞋结。应向彼礼拜，　　供养以赞叹，所以如是者，　　彼即世尊故。」

At that time, another god also spoke a verse:

时一天子复说偈言：

Speaking of the faults of existence and delusion, and all the evils of
delusion and illusion, completely cutting them off, praising the one who cuts off delusion.

「说有及痴过，　　并诸痴幻恶，一切悉断除，　　赞叹断痴者。」

At that time, another god also spoke a verse:

时一天子复说偈言：

Speaking of the faults of pride and arrogance, and all the evils of arrogance
and illusion, completely cutting them off, praising the one who cuts off pride and arrogance.

「说有憍慢过，　　并诸慢幻恶，一切悉断除，　　赞叹断憍慢。」

At that time, another god also spoke a verse:

时一天子复说偈言：

Speaking of the faults of various views, and all the evils of views
and illusions, completely cutting them off, praising the one who cuts off views.

「说有诸见过，　　并诸见幻恶，一切悉断除，　　赞叹断见者。」

At that time, another god also spoke a verse:

时一天子复说偈言：

Speaking of the faults of attachment and love, and all the evils of
love and illusions, completely cutting them off, praising the one who cuts off love.

「说有爱著过，　　并诸爱幻恶，一切悉断除，　　赞叹断爱者。」

At that time, another god also spoke a verse:

有一天子复说偈言：

Among the mountains of Rajagriha, Vepulla is the best; among the mountains
of the great earth, the Snow Mountain King is the best. Among
the worlds in the four directions, above, below, and in the four
intermediate directions, among all gods and humans, the Tathāgata is the most honored.

「王舍城诸山，　　毘富罗最上，大地诸山中，　　雪山王最上。四方诸世界，　　上下及四维，一切天人中，　　如来最为尊。」

At that time, the gods heard the Buddha's teachings, each spoke a verse, and then joyfully bowed their heads and returned to their heavenly palaces.

时诸天子闻佛所说，各说偈已，欢喜顶礼，还于天宫。

Indra asks about lifespan, cutting off all bonds, speaking
well, the elder Shanghui asks about co-abiding, asking about
the limits of speed, Bahu asks about great joy,

因陀罗问寿　　断于一切结说善称长者　　尸毘问共住速疾问边际　　婆睺咨大喜

Bahu asks about great joy, Vijñā asks about holding
Sudharma asks about the foremost, non-Buddhists ask about various views,

大喜毘忸问　　般阇罗揵持须深摩问第一　　有外道问诸见
