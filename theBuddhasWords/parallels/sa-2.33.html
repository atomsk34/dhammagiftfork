SĀ² 2.33

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 33（三三）

Parallel to: SN 11.11 / SĀ 1104 / SĀ³ 3.21

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, the Buddha told the bhikṣus, "If one upholds seven practices, one will surely attain the position of Śakra. Why is that? In the past, when Śakra was a human, he first practiced filial piety towards his parents, respect towards elders, gentle speech, refraining from divisive speech, generosity, being free from stinginess, always practicing truthful speech, never deceiving, and not giving rise to anger. Even if resentment arose, he would quickly eliminate it."

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，佛告诸比丘：「坚持七行，必得帝释。何以故？昔者，帝释为人之时，发初履行：孝顺父母；恭敬尊长；所言柔软，断于两舌；好施；无悭；恒修实语，终不欺诳；不起瞋恚，设生嫌恨，寻思灭之。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

To parents, one is able
to be extremely filial, to
elders, one deeply respects them
Always speaking softly and kindly
one cuts off divisive speech
Stinginess and anger are eliminated
The thirty-three heavens each say:
'Those who practice like this
are superior to us. They
should live separately and become
the heavenly king.

「于父母所，　　极能孝顺，　　于诸尊长，深心恭敬。　　恒作软善，　　恩柔好语，断于两舌，　　悭贪瞋恚。　　三十三天，各作是语：　　『如是行者，　　胜我等辈，应当别住，　　以为天王。』」

The Buddha having spoken thus, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说是已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
