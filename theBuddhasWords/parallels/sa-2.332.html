SĀ² 2.332

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 332（三三二）

Parallel to: SN 15.4 / SĀ 939

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, the Buddha told the bhikṣus, "The cycle of birth and death is long and without end. No one can know its source. All sentient beings are covered by ignorance, bound by the fetters of craving, and wander in the cycle of birth and death without end. The suffering of the past eons is immeasurable and cannot be known." He further told the bhikṣus, "It is like the Ganges River flowing into the four seas. Compared to the milk drunk by sentient beings in the vast cycle of birth and death in the past, which is greater? "

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，佛告诸比丘言：「生死长远，无有边际，无有能知其根源者。一切众生皆为无明之所覆盖，爱结缠缚，流转生死，无有穷已，过去亿苦无能知者。」复告比丘：「譬如恒河流注四海，于昔过去生死旷远，饮于母乳比恒河水，何者为多？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "According to my understanding of the Buddha's meaning, the milk drunk by sentient beings in the vast cycle of birth and death in the past is greater than the Ganges River and the four seas."

比丘白佛：「如我解佛所说义者，过去久远所饮母乳，多彼恒河，及四海水。」

"The forms we have taken are immeasurable and boundless. We have been elephants, horses, camels, donkeys, cows, sheep, deer, and other animals. The milk we have drunk is incalculable. It is like a flood carrying all kinds of grass and trees, piling them up to block the road. The foam of craving is also like this, able to block the path to the holy." The rest is as above.

「受形已来，无量无边，或受象、马、驼、驴、牛、羊、鹿等，种种畜兽，所饮母乳，不可称计。譬如瀑流，漂诸草木，合成聚集，妨塞途路。爱之聚沫，亦复如是，能遮圣道。」余如上说。
