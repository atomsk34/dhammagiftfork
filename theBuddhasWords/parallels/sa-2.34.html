SĀ² 2.34

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 34（三四）

Parallel to: SN 11.13 / SĀ 1105

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at the Great Lecture Hall on the Monkey's Other Shore in Vaiśālī. A Licchavi named Mahā-Licchavi came to where the Buddha was, bowed at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One, have you ever seen Śakra?" The Buddha said, "I have seen him." The Licchavi said to the Buddha, "There is a yakṣa ghost who looks like Śakra. Could it be that what the World-Honored One saw was that yakṣa ghost?" The Buddha told the Licchavi, "I recognize Śakra's body well. I also recognize the form of the yakṣa that resembles Śakra. I also know all about Śakra's past actions and deeds. When Śakra was a human, he was extremely filial to his parents; respectful to elders; his words were gentle, and he avoided divisive speech; he removed stinginess and jealousy; he always liked to give; his mouth always spoke the truth; he removed anger and did not give rise to resentment."

一时，佛在毘舍离猕猴彼岸大讲堂中。有一离车，名摩诃离，来诣佛所，礼佛足已，在一面坐。白佛言：「世尊！颇曾见帝释不？」佛言：「我见。」离车白佛言：「有夜叉鬼，状似帝释，世尊所见将无是彼夜叉鬼耶？」佛告离车：「是帝释身，我善识之。夜叉之形如帝释者，我亦识知。帝释本行，及所行事，我亦尽知。帝释本为人时，极孝顺父母；敬于尊长；所言柔软，断于两舌；除去悭嫉；常好布施；口常实语；除于瞋恚，不起嫌恨。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

To parents, he was extremely filial; to elders
he was deeply respectful; he always spoke softly
and kindly; he cut off divisive speech; he
removed stinginess and jealousy; he always liked to
give; his mouth always spoke the truth; he
removed anger and did not give rise to
resentment." The Thirty-three Heavens each said, "One who
practices like this is superior to us. He
should live separately and be the Heavenly King.

「于父母所，　　极能孝顺，　　于诸尊长，深心恭敬，　　恒作软善，　　恩柔好语，断于两舌，　　悭贪瞋恚。　　三十三天，各作是语：　　『如是行者，　　胜我等辈，应当别住，　　以为天王。』」

The Buddha having spoken thus, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说是已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
