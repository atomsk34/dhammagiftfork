SĀ² 2.363

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 363（三六三）

Parallel to: SN 2.25 / SN 9.13 / SĀ 1343

At that time, there were also many bhikṣus dwelling in the forest in the country of Kuśinagara. Those many bhikṣus were agitated and restless, lacking in shame, impetuous and arrogant, with unstable mindfulness, their minds agitated, and their faculties scattered. At that time, a deity had this thought: "The way of the bhikṣus should not be like this. This is very bad. I shall speak an awakening verse for them." He then spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，复有众多比丘在俱萨罗国，止住彼林。众多比丘掉动不停，少于惭耻，轻躁佷戾，识念不定，心意惶惶，诸根驰散。尔时，天神作是念：「比丘之法不应如是，斯甚不善。我当为其说觉悟偈。」即说偈言：

The disciples of Gautama, living by the right livelihood
begging for food and dwelling, always contemplate impermanence. While
dwelling, sitting, and lying down, they also contemplate impermanence
Already difficult to nourish, with impetuous and scattered minds
it is like worldly people, after eating, all sleeping
abandoning their own homes, and associating with others' homes
Like being forced by others, compelled to become a
monk, without reality or faith, not seeking to leave
home, forcibly wearing the saṃghāṭī robe, like an old
ox pulling a plow.

「瞿昙诸弟子，　　正命用自活，乞食及住时，　　常思于无常，于彼住坐卧，　　亦复思无常。已自难将养，　　佷戾心驰散，譬如世俗人，　　食讫皆睡眠，弃于自己舍，　　亲近著他家。如为人所迫，　　强逼作沙门，无实无信心，　　亦不求出家，强著僧伽梨，　　如老牛驾犁。」

At that time, the bhikṣus replied, "Do you now wish to criticize us?" The deity again spoke a verse in reply,

尔时，诸比丘即答之言：「今者汝欲讥我等耶？」天复说偈答言：

I do not see the family, nor do I praise the name
I now pay homage to the Sangha, those who criticize and destroy
If one can abide in diligence, I now also pay homage at their feet.

「我不见种姓，　　亦不称名字，我今敬礼僧，　　讥毁作过者，若能住精进，　　我今亦礼足。」
