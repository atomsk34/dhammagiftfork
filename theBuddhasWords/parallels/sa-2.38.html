SĀ² 2.38

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 38（三八）

Parallel to: SN 11.5 / SĀ 1109

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "In the past, Śakra, Lord of the Devas, together with the hosts of devas, was about to go to battle with the asuras. At that time, Śakra, Lord of the Devas, said to the asura king Vemacitrin, 'We do not need to bring many troops to harm each other. Let us just discuss and decide the winner.' Vemacitrin said to Śakra, Lord of the Devas, 'Kauśika! If we discuss and decide the winner, who will judge?' Śakra replied, 'Among our hosts, including the asuras, there are also wise, intelligent, and eloquent ones who can discern right and wrong and decide the winner.' Vemacitrin said, 'Śakra, you should speak first.' Śakra replied, 'I can also speak. You are an old deva, so you should speak first.'

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「昔释提桓因将诸天众，将欲往与阿修罗战。时，释提桓因语毘摩质多罗阿修罗王言：『我等今者，不必苟须多将人众，共相伤害，但共讲论，以决胜负。』毘摩质多罗语释提桓因言：『憍尸迦！我等讲论，若有胜负，谁当分别？』释提桓因言：『我等众中，并阿修罗，亦有聪哲、智慧、辩才，能当善恶，决胜负者。』毘摩质多言：『帝释汝今先说。』帝释答言：『我亦能说；汝是旧天，应当先说。』

Vemacitrin then spoke a verse:

「毘摩质多即说偈言：

Now I see that patience is a fault, and fools call it the Dharma of
patience. They are afraid, so they give rise to patience, and then consider themselves superior.

「『今我见忍过，　　愚者谓忍法，彼怖故生忍，　　便以己为胜。』

Śakra then spoke a verse:

「释提桓因复说偈言：

Following their words out of fear, their own benefit is supreme
Wealth and all benefits cannot surpass the one who is patient.

「『随彼言怖畏，　　己利最为胜，财宝及诸利，　　无胜忍辱者。』

Vimalakīrti then spoke a verse:

「毘摩质多罗复说偈言：

The foolish lack wisdom, they must be restrained. It is
like the later cows jumping over the earlier cows. Therefore
one needs a sword and staff to subdue the foolish.

「『愚者无智慧，　　要当须止制，譬如彼后牛，　　腾陌先牛上，是故须刀杖，　　摧伏于愚者。』

Śakra replied in verse:

「释提桓因以偈答言：

I observe that restraining the foolish, silently enduring is the
best. When extremely angry and resentful, enduring pacifies them. Those
without anger and harm are the sages, also the disciples
of the sages, who should always be close to them
Those with anger and resentment, their heavy anger is like
a mountain. When angry, if one can restrain it a
little, that is called good karma, like reins controlling a bad horse.

「『我观止制愚，　　默忍最为胜，极大瞋恚忿，　　能忍彼自息。无瞋无害者，　　彼即是贤圣，亦贤圣弟子，　　常应亲近之。诸有瞋恚者，　　瞋重障如山。若有瞋恚时，　　能少禁制者，是名为善业，　　如辔制恶马。』

The wise among the gods and asuras deliberated together, weighing their victory and defeat. Since the asuras take fighting as their basis, and Śakra stops disputes and has no anger or contention, the asuras are defeated and Śakra is victorious.

「诸天及阿修罗众有智慧者，详共评议，量其胜负。以阿修罗说诤鬪为本，释提桓因止息诤讼，心无忿竞，以阿修罗负，帝释为胜。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "Śakra is the lord of the heavens, enduring for a long time, praising the Dharma of endurance. You bhikṣus, if you can endure and praise endurance, it is in accord with the Dharma of leaving home."

佛告诸比丘：「释提桓因天中自在，长夜忍辱，赞忍辱法，汝等比丘，若能忍辱，赞叹忍者，称出家法。」

Having spoken this, the bhikṣus rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说是已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
