SĀ² 2.41

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 41（四一）

Parallel to: SN 11.18 / SĀ 1112

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, the World-Honored One addressed the bhikṣus, saying: "In the past, when Śakra, Lord of the Devas, wanted to go to the pleasure garden, he ordered his charioteer, 'Harness the chariot drawn by a thousand horses.' At that time, after the charioteer had harnessed the chariot, he went to where Śakra was and said to him, 'The chariot is ready. Please know the time.' At that time, Śakra came out of the Vimaloṣṇīṣa Hall and joined his palms facing south. At that time, the charioteer, seeing this, was also startled and dropped the whip and reins. Śakra said to him, 'What did you see that frightened you so much?' The charioteer said, 'O husband of Śakra's daughter! I now see you joining your palms facing south, and my mind is afraid, causing me to drop the whip and reins. All beings respect you. All the lords of the earth are under your control. The Four Heavenly Kings and the Thirty-three Heavens all pay homage to you. Who else is more virtuous than you? Joining your palms, you stand facing south.' Śakra replied, 'All respect me, as you say. The one respected by all gods and humans is called the Dharma. I now pay homage to the Dharma of fully endowed precepts.' At that time, Śakra spoke a verse, saying:

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「昔释提桓因欲诣游戏园，勅御者摩得梨：『汝驾千马车。』时，摩得梨寻驾车已，诣帝释所，即白释言：『严驾已讫，宜知是时。』尔时，帝释出毘禅延堂，合掌南向。时，摩得梨见已，心亦惊怕，失鞭及辔。帝释语言：『汝见何事，惊怖乃尔？』摩得梨言：『摩佉释脂之夫！我今见汝合掌南向，心怀惧故，致失鞭辔。一切有生，皆敬于汝。一切地主，尽属于汝。四天王天及三十三天，皆礼敬汝。谁复有德，胜于汝者？叉手合掌，南向而立。』帝释答言：『一切敬我，信如汝言。一切天人，所恭敬者，名之为法，我今恭敬礼具足戒法。』尔时，帝释即说偈言：

There are those who have gone forth, who cultivate non-negligence
enter deep concentration for long periods, and cultivate the supreme
brahma-conduct. Abandoning the three poisons, they attain the Dharma of
liberation. There are such dharmas, to which I now pay
homage. The great arhats who are far removed from desire
who can extinguish the darkness of ignorance and sever the
fetters, as well as those at home who cultivate goodness
and do not do evil - to these sons of
the true Dharma, I now pay homage.

「『有诸出家者，　　以修不放逸，长夜入寂定，　　修最上梵行。舍弃于三毒，　　能得解脱法，有如是等法，　　我今恭敬礼。诸大阿罗汉，　　远离于欲者，能灭无明暗，　　断除诸结使；并在家修善，　　不作恶业者，如是正法子，　　今我皆敬礼。』

"The charioteer said, 'You pay homage to the supreme one, and I wish to follow and pay homage.' At that time, after Śakra had spoken these words, he joined his palms in respect, rode in the chariot, and departed."

「摩得梨言：『汝礼最胜，我愿随礼。』尔时，帝释作是语已，合掌敬礼，乘辇而去。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "Śakra, dwelling among humans and devas, has attained mastery and is still able to respectfully pay homage to the Dharma. How much more so should you bhikṣus, having shaved your heads and beards, gone forth to study the path, not diligently respect the Dharma?"

佛告诸比丘：「帝释处于人天，而得自在，尚能恭敬礼敬于法，况汝比丘，剃除须发，出家学道，而当不勤恭敬于法？」

Having spoken this, the Buddha finished. The bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them respectfully.

佛说是已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
