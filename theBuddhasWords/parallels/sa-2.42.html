SĀ² 2.42

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 42（四二）

Parallel to: SN 11.20 / SĀ 1113

Thus have I heard. Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "Once, Śakra, lord of the devas, wanted to go to the pleasure garden. He ordered his charioteer, 'Prepare a chariot drawn by a thousand horses.' When the chariot was prepared, the charioteer went to Śakra and said, 'The chariot is ready. Please know the time.' At that time, Śakra came out of the Vimaloṣṇīṣa Hall and joined his palms facing west. Seeing this, the charioteer was also frightened and dropped the whip and reins. Śakra asked, 'What did you see that frightened you so much?' The charioteer said, 'O husband of Śakra's daughter! I saw you join your palms facing west, and my heart was afraid, so I dropped the whip and reins. All beings respect you. All the lords of the earth are under your control. The Four Heavenly Kings and the Thirty-three Heavens all pay homage to you. Who is more virtuous than you? Joining your palms and respectfully facing the west.' Śakra replied, 'All respect me, as you say. The one respected by all devas and humans is called the Saṃgha. Now I respectfully believe and face the Saṃgha.' At that time, the charioteer asked in verse:

如是我闻：一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。佛告诸比丘：「昔释提桓因欲诣游戏园，勅御者摩得梨：『汝严驾千马车。』时，摩得梨驾车已讫，诣帝释所，白帝释言：『严驾已讫，宜知是时。』尔时，帝释出毘禅延堂，合掌西向。时，御者摩得梨见是事已，亦生惊惧，失鞭及辔。帝释语言：『汝见何事，惊怖乃尔？』摩得梨言：『摩佉释脂之夫！我今见汝合掌西向，心怀惧，故致失鞭辔。一切有生，皆敬于汝。一切地主，尽属于汝。四天王天及三十三天，皆礼敬汝。谁复有德，胜于汝者？叉手合掌，敬向西方。』帝释答言『一切敬我，如汝所言。一切天人，所恭敬者，名之为僧，今我恭敬信向于僧。』尔时，摩得梨说偈问言：

「『汝礼者最胜，　　我亦随恭敬，摩佉之所礼，　　我今随汝礼。』

"Having spoken this verse, Śakra rode away in his chariot."

「说是偈已，帝释乘舆而去。」

The Buddha told the monks, "That Śakra, dwelling in the freedom of humans and gods, is still able to respect the Sangha. How much more so should you monks, who have left home to cultivate the path, each respect the Sangha."

佛告诸比丘：「彼帝释者，处人天自在，尚能敬僧，况汝比丘，出家修道，各宜敬僧。」

The Buddha having spoken this, the monks, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them respectfully.

佛说是已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

binding and respecting the Buddha, respecting the Dharma, paying homage to the Sangha.
