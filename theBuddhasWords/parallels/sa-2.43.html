SĀ² 2.43

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 43（四三）

Parallel to: SN 11.1 / SĀ 1114

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, the World-Honored One addressed the bhikṣus, saying, "In the past, the asuras assembled the four divisions of their army - elephants, horses, chariots, and infantry - all fully equipped for battle, intending to go to the palace of the Trāyastriṃśa Heaven to fight with the gods. At that time, Śakra, the Lord of the Gods, heard that the asuras had arrayed the four divisions of their army and immediately told the god Subāhu, 'I have heard that the asuras have arrayed the four divisions of their army. You should also array the four divisions of your army and go to fight with them.' Subāhu said, 'This is the best course of action.' Having said this, he became indulgent and attached to pleasure, forgetting about this matter. Śakra heard that the asuras had already come out of the city and again summoned Subāhu, saying, 'The asuras have now come out of the city. You should array the four divisions of your army and go to fight with them.' Subāhu said, 'Kauśika, this is a good thing.' Subāhu still became attached to pleasure and did not prepare for battle. The asuras arrayed the four divisions of their army and had already reached the summit of Mount Sumeru, gradually approaching. Śakra again said, 'I have heard that the asuras are gradually coming closer. You should lead the four divisions of your army to strike them.' Subāhu then spoke a verse:

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「昔阿修罗集诸四兵象马车步，悉皆严备鬪战之具，欲诣忉利天宫，与诸天共鬪。尔时，帝释闻阿修罗庄严四兵，即告须毘罗天子：『我闻阿修罗庄严四兵，汝亦庄严四兵，往与共鬪。』须毘罗白言：『此事最善。』作是语已，纵逸著乐，不忆此事。帝释闻阿修罗已来出城，复召须毘罗言：『阿修罗今已出城，汝可庄严四兵往彼共鬪。』须毘罗白言：『憍尸迦！此是善事。』须毘罗仍尔著乐，不修战备。阿修罗庄严四兵，已至须弥山上，渐欲近来。帝释复言：『我闻阿修罗渐来逼近，汝将四兵，可往击之。』须毘罗即说偈言：

If there is a place that is quiet and free from affairs, I only wish to have such a place.

「『若有清闲无事处，　　唯愿与我如此处。』

Śakra then spoke a verse in reply:

「帝释即说偈答言：

If there is such a place of leisure and joy, you should take me there together.

「『若有如此闲乐处，　　汝当将我共至彼。』

Subhadra then spoke a verse:

「须毘罗复说偈言：

I am now lazy and do not want to rise. Although I have heard and know, I am not
adorned. The five desires of the heavenly maidens shine all around. I only wish that Śakra grants this wish.

「『我今懈怠不欲起，　　虽具闻知不庄严，天女五欲光四塞，　　唯愿帝释与此愿。』

Śakra replied in verse:

「帝释以偈答言：

If there is such a place of laziness and indolence, surrounded by hundreds of thousands of
heavenly maidens, indulging in the five desires and enjoying happiness, if you go there, come with me.

「『若有如此懈堕处，　　百千天女而围遶，五欲自恣受快乐，　　汝若往彼与我俱。』

Subāhu again spoke in verse:

「须毘罗复说偈言：

If the heavenly king has no affairs to attend to, let me share in your happiness without suffering.

「『天王若无事役处，　　与我无苦受斯乐。』

The king of the gods replied in verse:

「帝释以偈答言：

You, Sumbha, have such a place, and I will share your happiness with you
Have you ever seen or heard of anyone who can live and enjoy happiness
without work? If you have such a place now, go quickly and follow your
own wishes. You should fear and like quiet places, and should quickly head for nirvana.

「『汝须毘罗有如是，　　我当与汝同是乐，颇曾见闻无事业，　　而得生活受乐者？汝今若有如是处，　　可疾速往可随汝，汝当畏事好闲处，　　应当速疾向涅槃。』

Having heard these words, Sumbha immediately assembled his four divisions and went out to fight the asuras. The gods won the battle, and the asuras retreated in defeat. The asuras, having been defeated, returned to their palace in various states of disarray.

「闻是语已，须毘罗即集四兵，出与阿修罗战。时，诸天得胜，阿修罗退坏，阿修罗已种种庄严而来还宫。」

The Buddha told the monks, "Even though Sakra Devendra is the king of the gods and has great freedom, he still diligently praises diligence. How much more so should you, who have left home with faith, put on the Dharma robes, and not diligently praise diligence? If you can diligently praise diligence, this is the proper way to leave home."

佛告诸比丘：「释提桓因处天王位得大自在，犹自精勤，赞叹精进，况复汝等，信心出家，被服法衣，而当不勤精进，赞叹精进？若能精进，赞叹精进，如是为应出家之法。」

Having spoken thus, the Buddha finished. The monks, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说是已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
