SĀ² 2.46

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 46（四六）

Parallel to: AN 3.37–38 / SĀ 1117 / EĀ 24.6

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, the Buddha told the bhikṣus, "On the eighth day of the month, the Four Heavenly Kings send messengers to inspect the world, to observe who in the world is filial to their parents, respectful to elders, and serves śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, practicing good dharmas, and who commits evil. Therefore, one should practice good dharmas, eliminate all evil, and restrain one's emotions to keep the precepts. On the fourteenth day, the Four Heavenly Kings again send their princes to inspect the world. On the fifteenth day, the Four Heavenly Kings themselves inspect and observe, also like this. When the Four Heavenly Kings have inspected, they go to the Hall of Good Dharma of Śakra, the Lord of the Devas, and inform Śakra, saying, 'In the world of humans, there are many who are not filial to their parents, who do not respect śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, who do not serve teachers and elders in the family, and who do not keep the precepts.' When Śakra, the Lord of the Devas, and the devas hear this, they are saddened and unhappy. The devas all say, 'This will diminish the devas and increase the asuras.' If in the world, there are always people who are filial to their parents, who make offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, and who keep the precepts, the Four Heavenly Kings will inform Śakra, the Lord of the Devas. Then the devas will be extremely joyful and say, 'The people in the world practice good deeds, are extremely virtuous, do what should be done, increase the devas, and diminish the asuras.' Śakra, the Lord of the Devas, joyfully spoke a verse:

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，佛告诸比丘：「月八日，四天王遣使者案行天下，伺察世间，有慈孝父母，敬顺尊长，奉事沙门、婆罗门，修于善法，及行恶者，是故宜应修行善法，灭除众恶，捡情守戒。至十四日，四天王复遣太子案行天下。至十五日，四天王自案行伺察，亦复如是。时，四天王既伺察已，往帝释善法堂上，启白帝释并语：『诸天世间人中，多有不孝父母，不敬沙门、婆罗门者，不奉事师及家尊长，乃至无有多持戒者。』尔时，帝释及诸天众闻斯语已，惨然不乐，诸天咸作是言：『损诸天众，益阿修罗。』若世间中，有人常能孝顺父母，供养沙门、婆罗门，乃至多能持戒。四天王上启帝释，时诸天等极大欢喜，咸作是言：『世间人中，修行善事，极为贤善，作所应作，增益诸天，损阿修罗。』帝释欢喜，即说偈言：

On the eighth, fourteenth, and fifteenth days of the month
and also on the full moon days, those who uphold
pure precepts will be reborn in heaven, with merit like mine.

「『月八十四日，　　及以十五日，并及神足月，　　受持清净戒，是人得生天，　　功德如我身。』」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "What Śakra said is not a good explanation. Why is that? If an arhat who has ended the taints and accomplished what was to be done were to speak a verse,

佛告诸比丘：「帝释所说，不名善说。所以者何？若漏尽阿罗汉，所作已办，应作是偈：

On the eighth, fourteenth, and fifteenth days of the
month, and also on the full moon days, those
who uphold pure precepts will attain victory and merit like mine.

「『月八十四日，　　及以十五日，并及神足月，　　受持清净戒，斯人获胜利，　　功德如我身。』

"The Buddha and the arhats should speak this verse. To speak with a true name is called good speech."

「佛与罗汉应说斯偈，名称实说，名为善说。」

Having spoken thus, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha had said and joyfully upheld it.

佛说是已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
