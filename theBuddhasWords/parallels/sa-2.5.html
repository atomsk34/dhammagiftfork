SĀ² 2.5

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 5（五）

Parallel to: SN 21.8 / SĀ 1067 / EĀ 18.6

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, the elder Nanda wore clean and pure robes, held a fine alms bowl, and was arrogant and contemptuous of others, boasting, "I am the Buddha's younger brother, the son of his aunt." At that time, many bhikṣus went to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! The bhikṣu Nanda wears clean and pure robes, holds a pure alms bowl, claims to be the Buddha's younger brother, and says he is the son of his aunt. He is arrogant within and contemptuous of others." Having heard these words, the Buddha sent a bhikṣu to summon Nanda. At that time, a bhikṣu, having received the Buddha's order, went to where he was and said to Nanda, "The World-Honored One summons you." Having heard this, Nanda went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and stood to one side.

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，长老难陀著鲜净衣，执持好钵，意气憍慢，陵蔑余人，自贡高言：「我是佛弟姨母之子。」尔时众多比丘往至佛所，顶礼佛足，在一面坐。白佛言：「世尊！难陀比丘著鲜洁衣，手持净钵，称是佛弟，云是姨子，内自憍慢，陵蔑余人。」佛闻语已，遣一比丘往召难陀。时，一比丘受佛勅已，往至其所，语难陀言：「世尊唤汝。」难陀闻已，即诣佛所，顶礼佛足，在一面立。

The Buddha said to Nanda, "Is it true that you really wear clean and pure robes, hold a fine alms bowl, claim to be the Buddha's younger brother, the son of his aunt, and are arrogant and contemptuous of others?" Nanda replied, "It is true, World-Honored One."

佛告难陀：「汝实著鲜洁衣，手持好钵，称是佛弟姨母之子，憍慢于人，有是事不？」难陀答言：「实尔，世尊。」

The Buddha said to Nanda, "You should not do such things now. You should now enjoy dwelling in remote places, under trees near burial grounds, wearing rag robes, and begging for food. If you are my younger brother, the son of my aunt, you should practice such things."

佛告难陀：「汝今不应作如是事，汝今应当乐阿练若，处冢间树下，纳衣乞食。若是我弟姨母所生，应当修行如是等事。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

How should I see? Nanda enjoys ascetic practices, like those
in the wilderness, sitting near burial grounds, begging for food
in the mountains and forests, quiet places, abandoning desires and entering concentration.

「我当云何见？　　难陀乐苦行，如彼阿练若，　　冢间坐乞食，山林闲静处，　　舍欲而入定。」

After the Buddha spoke this verse, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully upheld it.

佛说是偈已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
