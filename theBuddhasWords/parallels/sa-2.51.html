SĀ² 2.51

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 51（五一）

Parallel to: SN 11.14 / SĀ 1223

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at the Bamboo Grove in the Kalandaka-nivāpa near Rājagṛha. At that time, there was a poor man in Rājagṛha who was extremely impoverished and pitiful. He had pure faith in the Buddha's teachings, was able to uphold the precepts, read and recite the sūtras a little, and also made small offerings. Due to these four causes and conditions, when his body perished and his life ended, he was reborn in the excellent and wonderful place of the Trāyastriṃśa Heaven. This newly reborn god was superior in three ways: his appearance was superior, his fame was superior, and his lifespan was superior. When the other gods saw him, they all respectfully went to Śakra and said, "There is a newly reborn god who is superior in three ways to the other gods." Śakra said, "I have seen before that this newly reborn god, when he was a human, was poor, suffering, and extremely cold. He only had pure faith towards the Three Jewels, was able to uphold the precepts, and made small offerings. Now he has been reborn in this Trāyastriṃśa Heaven."

一时，佛在王舍城迦] cf.【丽】兰陀竹林。尔时，王舍城中有一贫人，极为穷困，甚可怜愍，于佛法中，生清净信，能净持戒，少读诵经，亦能小施。有此四事因缘果报，身坏命终，生忉利天胜妙善处。此新生天有三事胜：一色貌胜，二名称胜，三寿命胜。诸天见已，皆共恭敬，往帝释所，白帝释言：「有新生天，有三事胜于余诸天。」帝释言：「我先曾见，彼新生天，本为人时，贫穷困苦，极为寒悴，直以信心，向于三宝，能净持戒，少多修施，今得生此忉利天上。」

At that time, Śakra spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，帝释即说偈言：

If one gives rise to pure faith in the Three Jewels, with a firm and
unwavering mind, and upholds the precepts received without violating them, then one is not considered
poor. One is called a person of wisdom and longevity, because of respecting the unsurpassed
Three Jewels. One will be reborn in heaven and experience supreme bliss. Therefore, one should study this.

「若于三宝生净信，　　其心坚固不动转，持所受戒不毁犯，　　当知此人不名贫。名为智慧寿命人，　　以敬无上三宝故，得生天上受胜乐，　　是故应当作斯学。」

At that time, upon hearing these verses, the devas joyfully accepted them, bowed, and returned to their palaces.

尔时，诸天闻此偈已，欢喜信受，作礼还宫。
