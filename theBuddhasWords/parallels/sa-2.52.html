SĀ² 2.52

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 52（五二）

Parallel to: SN 11.16 / EĀ 13.6

Thus have I heard. Once, the Buddha was staying at Vulture Peak in Rajagriha. At that time, there were ninety-six kinds of non-Buddhist practitioners in Rajagriha, each performing their own sacrifices. If there were any donors who had faith in the non-Buddhist teacher Caraka, they would say, "First, we should make offerings to my teacher Caraka." If they had faith in the non-Buddhist teacher Vararuci, they would say, "First, we should make offerings to my teacher Vararuci." If they had faith in the non-Buddhist teacher Gandhara, they would all say, "First, we should give a large donation to my teacher Gandhara and then to the others." If they had faith in the non-Buddhist teacher called the Three Waters, they would say, "We should make offerings to my teacher the Three Waters." If they had faith in the non-Buddhist teacher called the Old Sravaka, they would say, "First, we should make offerings to my teacher the Old Sravaka." If they had faith in the non-Buddhist teacher called the Great Sravaka, they would also say, "We should make offerings to my teacher the Great Sravaka." If they had faith in the Buddha, they would all say, "First, we should make offerings to my teacher the Tathāgata and the Sangha.

如是我闻：一时，佛在王舍城耆阇崛山中。尔时，王舍城有九十六种外道，各各祠祀。设有檀越，信心于外道遮勒者，言当先供养我师遮勒；若信外道婆罗婆寔者，亦言先当供养我师婆罗婆寔；若信外道干陀者，咸言先与我师干陀大嚫后与余者；若信外道名三水者，言当供养我师三水；若信外道名老声闻者，言先供养我师老声闻；若信外道大声闻者，亦言供养我师大声闻；若信佛者，咸言应先供养我师如来及以众僧。

At that time, Sakra Devendra thought, "Now the people of Rajagriha have given rise to great wrong views. If the Buddha and the Sangha are in the world and wrong views arise, it is called unwholesome." At that time, Sakra Devendra transformed himself into an old brahman with an upright appearance, riding a white chariot drawn by white horses, surrounded by young men on the left and right, heading towards the temple grounds, passing straight through the middle. At that time, the people of Rajagriha all thought, "Where did this old brahman go first? We shall follow him." At that time, Sakra Devendra knew what the people were thinking, turned the chariot to the south, and headed towards Vulture Peak. Arriving at the place where the carriages were parked, he stopped there. Getting off the carriage and walking forward, he went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side.

时] cf.【丽】释提桓因作是念言：「今王舍城人生大邪见，佛僧在世，若生邪见，名为不善。」帝释尔时寻自变身为老婆罗门，容貌端正，乘以白车，驾以白马，诸摩纳等围遶左] cf.【丽】右，向于寺场，当中直过。时，王舍城人咸作是念：「今此] cf.【丽】老婆罗门先向何处，我等随从。」尔时，帝释知诸人等心之所念，回车南旋，向灵鹫山。到诸乘驾所住之处，于中而止。下车前进，往至佛所，顶礼佛足，在一面坐。

At that time, Sakra Devendra spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，帝释即说偈言：

The Wheel-Turning Sage King can ferry across the shore of suffering, without
enmity, hatred, or fear. I now bow my head in reverence. If
a person wishes to cultivate merit, where should they give? If they
wish to seek merit, they should give with pure faith and respect
If they practice giving today, they will receive good rewards in the
future. In which field of merit will they receive great fruit from giving little?

「转法轮圣王，　　能度苦彼岸，无怨憎恐怖，　　我今稽首礼。设人欲修福，　　当于何处施？又欲精求福，　　应生净信敬。今日修布施，　　来世得善报，于何福田中，　　少施获大果？」

At that time, the World-Honored One was on Vulture Peak, expounding to Sakra Devendra the most excellent of sacrifices, and answered in verse:

尔时，世尊在耆阇崛山中，为天帝释敷演祠中最为胜者，以偈答言：

The four fruits and four stages, dhyāna, concentration, and wisdom, the power
of merit and virtue is profound, like the water of the great
ocean. This is called the true and excellent disciple of the Tamer
who can light the lamp of wisdom in the great darkness. Always
for the sake of all beings, expounding the Dharma and showing the
path, this is called the field of merit of the Saṅgha, vast
and boundless. If one gives to this field of merit, it is
called giving well. If one sacrifices to this field of merit, it
is called sacrificing well. Burning things to sacrifice to heaven is in
vain and useless, and is not called burning well. If one performs
little meritorious deeds in the field of merit, one will later obtain
great benefits, and it is then called burning well. Sakra Devendra should
know that this is called a good field of merit. Giving to
one person in the Saṅgha will later definitely obtain great fruit. This
is what the Knower of the World says. The Buddha with immeasurable
merit praises the Saṅgha with a hundred verses. The best of sacrifices
is none other than the field of merit of the Saṅgha. If
a person plants a little goodness, the reward is immeasurable. Therefore, the
good and wise should give to the Saṅgha. Those who can uphold
the Dharma are called the Saṅgha. It is like the great ocean
which has many precious treasures. The ocean of the Saṅgha is also
like this, rich in the treasures of merit and virtue. If one
can give to the treasure of the Saṅgha, it is called a
good and wise person. Having already obtained joyful faith, if one can
give with faith, one should know that such a person will obtain
joy in the three times. Because of joy in the three times
one can cross the three evil paths, remove all dust and defilements
and be free from the poisonous arrows of afflictions. With a pure
mind, giving with one's own hands, benefiting oneself and others, being able
to perform such a sacrifice, such a person is called a wise
and intelligent one in the world. With pure faith, one can reach
the unconditioned state, the utmost bliss in the world. The wise can
be reborn there.

「四果及四向，　　禅定明行足，功德力甚深，　　犹如大海水。此名为实胜，　　调御之弟子，于大黑暗中，　　能燃智慧灯。常为诸众生，　　说法而示道，是名僧福田，　　广大无漄际。若施斯福田，　　是名为善与，若祀斯福田，　　是名为善祀。焚物而祭天，　　徒费而无补，不名为善烧。　　若于福田所，少作诸功业，　　后获大富利，乃名为善烧。　　帝释应当知，是名良福田，　　施僧次一人，后必获大果。　　此事是时说，世间解所说，　　无量功德佛，以百偈赞僧。　　祠祀中最上，无过僧福田，　　若人种少善，获报无有量。　　是以善丈夫，应当施于僧，　　能总持法者，是则名为僧。　　譬如大海中，多有众珍宝，　　僧海亦如是，多饶功德宝，　　若能施僧宝，是名善丈夫。　　已获欢喜信，若能信心施，　　当知如此人，得三时欢喜，　　以三时喜故，能度三恶道，　　除祛诸尘垢，离烦恼毒箭。　　净心手自施，自利亦利彼，　　能设如此祠，是人则名为，　　世间明智者。信心既清净，　　得至无为处，世间之极乐，　　智者得生彼。」

Having heard these verses, Sakra Devendra was overjoyed. He disappeared from his seat and returned to the heavenly palace. Not long after Sakra returned to the palace, the elders and Brahmins of Rajagriha rose from their seats, bared their right shoulders, touched their right knees to the ground, joined their palms towards the Buddha, and said to the Buddha, "May the World-Honored One and the Sangha accept our invitation to a great sacrifice tomorrow morning." At that time, the Tathāgata silently accepted the invitation. The Brahmins and elders of Rajagriha, knowing that the Buddha had silently accepted their invitation, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet and returned to their abodes. When they returned home, they prepared various fragrant and delicious foods, pure and fragrant offerings. Having prepared them, they laid out seats in the morning, provided clean water, and sent a messenger to Vulture Peak to inform the World-Honored One, saying, "The meal is ready." At that time, the Tathāgata put on his robe, took his bowl, and was surrounded by the Sangha. The World-Honored One went ahead, going to the city and to the place of the great sacrifice. Having arrived there, the Tathāgata laid out his seat and sat in front of the Sangha. The people of the city laid out good seats and sat with the Sangha. At that time, the elders and others, seeing that the assembly was seated, each served clean water. The Brahmins and elders personally served various fragrant and delicious foods. The people each encouraged and benefited.

帝释闻是偈已，踊跃欢喜，于坐处没，还于天宫。帝释还宫未久之间，王舍城中长者婆罗门即从坐起，偏袒右肩，右膝著地，合掌向佛，而白佛言：「唯愿世尊，及比丘僧，于明晨朝，受大祠欢喜请。」尔时，如来默然许之。时，王舍城婆罗门长者知佛默然受已请已，顶礼佛足，各还所止。时，诸人等既还家已，各各办诸香美饮食，清净香洁供设。办已，晨朝敷座，具行净水，遣使往诣灵鹫山中，白世尊言：「食时已到。」尔时，如来著衣持钵，众僧围遶，世尊在前，往诣彼城，到大祠所。既至彼已，如来敷座，于僧前坐，彼城中人，敷好床座，与僧而坐。尔时，诸长者等察众坐定，各行净水，诸婆罗门长者手自斟酌种种香美饮食。时，诸人等各各劝益。

At that time, the World-Honored One saw that the Sangha had finished their meal and immediately put away the bowls and handed them to Ānanda. Then the people each spread out their seats and sat in front of the Buddha, respectfully looking up and seeking to hear the Dharma. At that time, the Tathāgata praised their offerings and spoke a verse:

尔时，世尊观诸众僧饭食已讫，即时收钵，付于阿难。时，诸人等各自敷座，在佛前坐，专心敬仰，求欲听法。尔时，如来赞其所施，而说偈言：

Among the Brahmins' scriptures, fire sacrifice is the best. Among
the non-Buddhist texts, the Vāpīśīla is the best. Among all
worldly people, the king is the foremost. Among the many
rivers, the ocean is called the best. Among the stars
and constellations, the moonlight is the best. Among all lights
the sunlight is the best. Above, below, and in the
four directions, in the world and among gods and humans
among the sages and saints, the Buddha is the most
honored and foremost.

「婆罗门经书，　　祠祀火为最。外道典籍中，　　婆比室为最。于诸世人中，　　王者最为首。百川众流中，　　巨海名为最。星辰诸宿中，　　月光名为最。于众明之中，　　日光最为最。上下及四方，　　世间及天人，诸贤圣众中，　　佛最第一尊。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke various teachings for the people of Rājagṛha, showing, teaching, benefiting, and delighting them. The people rejoiced and rose from their seats to leave.

尔时，世尊为王舍城人种种说法，示教利喜，诸人踊跃，从坐而退。

Having spoken this, the Buddha finished. The bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

佛说是已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

Sūbila Sage, anger, eighth day of the month, illness, and upholding
one precept, bird's nest, and Vālin, poor man, and great sacrifice

须毘罗仙人　　灭瞋、月八日病并持一戒　　鸟巢及婆梨贫人及大祠
