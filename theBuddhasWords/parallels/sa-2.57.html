SĀ² 2.57

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 57（五七）

Parallel to: SN 3.7 / SĀ 1231

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, King Prasenajit was in a quiet place, thinking like this: "In this world, few can attain wealth and status. Even if they attain wealth and status, few are not arrogant and extravagant, but are upright, content, restrained in their desires, and do not harm sentient beings. In this world, many people attain superior wealth and status, but are arrogant, indulgent, greedy for desires, and harm sentient beings. This is the majority." King Prasenajit, having thought this in a quiet place, rose from his seat and went to where the Buddha was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha: "World-Honored One! I was in a quiet place, thinking like this: 'In this world, if there are people who attain superior wealth and status, are content in their minds, are not arrogant or indulgent, are not greedy for desires, and do not harm others, such people are few. If there are people who attain superior wealth and status, are arrogant, indulgent, greedy for desires, and harm sentient beings, such people are the majority.'"

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，波斯匿王于闲静处，作是思惟：「世界之中，少有能得富贵财业。设得财业，不尚骄奢，贞廉知足，节于嗜欲，不恼众生，如是人少。世界多有众人，得胜财业，骄逸自恣，贪嗜于欲，加恼众生，如是人多。」波斯匿王于闲静处，思惟是已，从坐而起，往诣佛所，顶礼佛足，在一面坐，即白佛言：「世尊！我于静处作是思惟：『世界之中，若设有人，得胜财业，心自知足，能不骄恣，不嗜于欲，不恼于人，如是人少。若复有人，得胜基业，憍逸自恣，贪嗜于欲，加恼众生，如是人多。』」

The Buddha said, "Great King! It is indeed so, it is indeed so. In this world, many people, having attained rank and salary, are arrogant, indulgent, greedy for desires, and torment sentient beings. Such foolish people will suffer for a long time, suffer great loss, and after death, will certainly enter hell."

佛言：「大王！实尔，实尔。世界之中，多有众人，得封禄已，憍慢自恣，贪嗜于欲，苦楚众生。如是愚人，长夜受苦，得大损减，命终之后，必入地狱。」

The Buddha said, "Great King! It is like a fisherman and his disciples, skilled in the methods of catching fish, using a fine net to block the current and drag it. Fish, turtles, alligators, and other aquatic creatures caught in the net are all gathered in the fisherman's hands, pulled and turned, at the fisherman's will. In this world, many people, having attained superior rank and salary, are arrogant, indulgent, greedy for the five desires, and torment sentient beings, it is also like this. Why is this? Such foolish people enter the demon's net, are caught in the net, and their movements are controlled by the demon's will."

佛言：「大王！譬如鱼师及其弟子，于捕鱼法善巧方便，以细密网截流而拖，鱼鼈鼋鼍，水性之属，为网所得。此水性等，入网之者，悉皆集在鱼师之手，牵挽旋转，任鱼师意。世间之中，多有众人，得胜封禄，憍慢自恣，贪嗜五欲，加恼众生，亦复如是。所以者何？如斯愚人，即入魔网，为网所获，周回举动，住魔所为。」

At that time, the World Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

Indulging in work and affairs, confused and addicted to the
five desires, not knowing there are evil consequences, like fish
entering a dense net, this karma is already accomplished, greatly suffering.

「纵逸著事业，　　荒迷嗜五欲，不知有恶果，　　如鱼入密网，此业已成就，　　极受大苦恼。」

Having spoken this, the Buddha finished. The bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and upheld it.

佛说是已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
