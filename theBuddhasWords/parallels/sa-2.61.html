SĀ² 2.61

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 61（六一）

Parallel to: SN 3.9 / SĀ 1234

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Thus have I heard:

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。当于尔时，波斯匿王欲设大祀，养千牛王，皆系于柱，并及牦牛、水牛、乳牛犊，及小牛，尽各数千；牂羖羊等，亦复数千；种种畜生，皆系祀场。时，余国中，诸婆罗门闻王大祀，自远而至，进集舍卫城。时，众多比丘于其晨朝，著衣持钵，入城乞食，闻波斯匿王欲设大祀，养千牛王，并及牦牛、水牛、乳牛、小牛及犊，尽各数千；牂羖羊等，亦复数千。如是种种，诸畜生等，皆悉系著于彼祀场。又闻余国诸婆罗门，闻波斯匿王施设大祀，一切云集在舍卫城。诸比丘乞食已讫，摄于衣钵，洗足已去，往诣佛所，顶礼佛足，在一面坐，白佛言：「世尊！我等今日入城乞食，闻如是事。」尔时，世尊闻是语已，即说偈言：

Sacrificing a hundred thousand times each month, practicing this to seek
blessings, is not as good as one-sixteenth of believing in the
Buddha. Sacrificing a hundred thousand times each month, practicing this to
seek blessings, is not as good as one-sixteenth of believing in
the Dharma. Sacrificing a hundred thousand times each month, practicing this
to seek blessings, is not as good as one-sixteenth of believing
in the Sangha. Sacrificing a hundred thousand times each month, practicing
this to seek blessings, is not as good as one-sixteenth of
a compassionate mind. Sacrificing a hundred thousand times each month, practicing
this to seek blessings, is not as good as one-sixteenth of
compassion for sentient beings. Sacrificing a hundred thousand times each month
practicing this to seek blessings, is not as good as one-sixteenth
of compassion for ghosts and spirits. Sacrificing a hundred thousand times
each month is not as good as one-sixteenth of a kind
mind, compassion for the animal realm. Sacrificing a hundred thousand times
each month is not as good as one-sixteenth of giving rise
to faith and delight in the Buddha's teachings. Even if one
practices various sacrifices and the rites of serving fire, practicing this
to seek blessings, performing these sacrifices, for a full year, it
is not as good as one-fourth of standing upright and paying
homage to the Buddha with one prostration.

「月月百千祀，　　修此以求福，不如一信佛，　　十六分中一。月月百千祀，　　修此以求福，不如一信法，　　十六分中一。月月百千祀，　　修此以求福，不如一信僧，　　十六分中一。月月百千祀，　　修此以求福，不如一慈心，　　十六分中一。月月百千祀，　　修此以求福，不如怜众生，　　十六分中一。月月百千祀，　　修此以求福，不如怜鬼神，　　十六分中一。月月百千祀，　　不如一善心，怜愍畜生类，　　十六分中一。月月百千祀，　　不如于佛说，生信而爱乐，　　十六分中一。假使修诸祀，　　及与事火法，修此欲求福，　　行此诸祠祀，满足一年中，　　不如正身立，一礼敬向佛，　　四分中之一。」

Having spoken this, the World-Honored One, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, the one with perfect wisdom and conduct, the Sugata, the knower of the world, the unsurpassed guide who tames beings, the teacher of gods and humans, the Buddha, the Bhagavān, having spoken this, the World-Honored One,

佛说是已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
