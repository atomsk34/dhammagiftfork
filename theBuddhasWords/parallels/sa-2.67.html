SĀ² 2.67

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 67（六七）

Parallel to: SN 3.3 / SĀ 1240 / EĀ 26.6

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Thus have I heard:

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，波斯匿王于闲静处，作是思惟：「世有三法：一者可憎，二不可爱，三不可追念。何谓可憎？所谓老也。何谓不可爱？所谓病也。何谓不可追念，所谓死也。」波斯匿王思惟是已，即从坐起，往诣佛所，顶礼佛已，在一面坐。白佛言：「世尊！我于静处作是思惟：『世有三法：一者可憎，二者不可爱，三者不可追念。何谓可憎？所谓老也。何谓不可爱？所谓病也。何谓不可追念？所谓死也。』」

The Buddha said to the king: "So it is! So it is! These three things are indeed as the king has said." The Buddha said: "Great King! If there were no these three things in the world, the Buddha would not appear in the world, nor would he teach the Dharma. It is because of these three things that the Buddha appears in the world to teach the Dharma to the multitude."

佛告王曰：「如是！如是！此三种法实如王言。」佛言：「大王！世间若无此三，佛不出世，亦不说法。以有此三故，佛出世为众说法。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

The king's chariot is adorned and splendid, beautifully decorated, but after a long time
its color fades and decays, just as the body must return to old age
The true Dharma is without aging and decay, but it is passed down from
one to another. Alas! The thief of old age is evil, it can destroy
even the most beautiful and wonderful appearance. Even if one lives to be a
hundred years old, one will surely enter the path of death. Illness comes and
robs one of strength, old age hands one over to death. Therefore, always delight
in meditation, restraining the mind and diligently practicing, understanding the limit of birth, surpassing
the multitude of demonic armies, and crossing to the shore of birth and death.

「王车严饰盛，　　庄挍甚奇妙，久故色毁败，　　如身必归老，实法无衰老，　　展转相付故。咄哉老贼恶，　　端正殊妙色，汝能坏败也。　　设寿满百年，必入于死径。　　病来夺其力，老将付与死。　　是故常乐禅，捡心勤精进，　　了知生边际，胜彼魔军众，　　度有生死岸。」

The Buddha spoke thus. The bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说是已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
