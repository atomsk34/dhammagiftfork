SĀ² 2.73

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 73（七三）

Parallel to: SN 3.13 / SĀ 1150

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, King Prasenajit was very obese and had very coarse breathing. He went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. The Buddha said to the king, "Your body is now very heavy. It is very difficult for you to move, come and go, and breathe."

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，波斯匿王身体肥大，喘息极麁，往诣佛所，顶礼佛足，在一面坐。佛告王言：「王今身体极为重大，至于动转，出入息时，极为大难。」

The king said to the Buddha, "Yes, yes. As the World-Honored One teaches, I now suffer from this body and am ashamed of it, criticizing myself."

王白佛言：「如是，如是。如世尊教，今患此身，以为惭愧，用自讥呵。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

A person should always remember to eat and drink in moderation, so that the body
is light and suffering is reduced, and digestion is proper, protecting life and prolonging it.

「夫人常当自忆念，　　若得饮食应知量，身体轻便受苦少，　　正得消化护命长。」

At that time, Udayin the brahmin was in the assembly. The king said to Udayin, "Can you recite this verse for me at mealtimes? If you can, I will reward you with a hundred gold coins a day, and you can eat before me." Udayin replied, "I can." The Buddha further taught King Prasenajit the Dharma, showing, teaching, benefiting, and delighting him, and then remained silent. King Prasenajit paid homage to the Buddha and departed. Udayin stayed behind a little and received and upheld this verse. The Buddha told Udayin, "When the king eats, always recite this verse for the king."

尔时，乌带摩纳在于众中。王告摩纳言：「汝能受持此偈，于我食时，常能为我诵此偈不？若能诵者，我当赏汝日百金钱，我之所食常当听汝先我前食。」乌带摩纳尔时答言：「我能。」佛重为波斯匿王种种说法，示教利喜，默然而住。波斯匿王礼佛而退，乌带摩纳小在后住，受持此偈。佛告摩纳：「王若食时，恒为王说如此上偈。」

At that time, King Prasenajit gradually reduced his food every day, and his body gradually became lighter and more comfortable. Later, King Prasenajit went to where the Buddha was. His body was light and comfortable, and he became more handsome. He said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I now follow the Buddha's instructions and teachings, and in this life I experience immeasurable happiness. I pay homage to the Buddha, the Bhagavān, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One! You know the present benefits and present benefits for me, because of eating in moderation."

尔时，波斯匿王日日减食，身体日日转小，渐得轻便。波斯匿王后至佛所，身体轻便，转得端正。白佛言：「世尊！我于今者奉佛勅教，现身之中受无量乐。南无佛、婆伽婆、至真、等正觉！知我现报现前利益，由节食故。」

victory, destruction, from the Buddha's teaching, one Dharma, field of merit, to be
despised, light and dark, rocky mountain, wearing one robe, various kings, breathing, named Vāg

得胜、毁坏、从佛教　　一法、福田、可厌患明暗、石山、著一衣　　诸王、喘息名跋瞿
