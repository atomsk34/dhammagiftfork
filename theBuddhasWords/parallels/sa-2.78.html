SĀ² 2.78

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 78（七八）

Parallel to: SN 7.16 / SĀ 1155 / SĀ³ 3.7

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Kusumapura and returned to the Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī. At that time, there was a brahmin named Retaliation who heard that the World-Honored One was traveling in Kusumapura and returning to the Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī. The brahmin thought, "I shall go to where the Śramaṇa Gautama is. Whatever he says, I shall retaliate." Having thought this, the brahmin went to where the Buddha was. At that time, the World-Honored One was surrounded by thousands of millions of people in front and behind, and was teaching the Dharma. The World-Honored One saw the brahmin coming from afar and remained silent, saying nothing. The brahmin came to where the Buddha was and said to the Buddha, "Why are you not teaching the Dharma? I wish to listen to it."

一时，佛游俱萨罗国，还至舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，有婆罗门名曰返戾，闻世尊游俱萨罗还舍卫祇树给孤独园。时，婆罗门而作是念：「我当往至沙门瞿昙所，彼有所说，我当返戾。」时，婆罗门作是念已，即诣佛所。尔时，世尊数千亿众前后围遶，而为说法。世尊于时遥见彼婆罗门来，默无所说。时，婆罗门来到佛所，语佛言：「何不说法？我欲听之。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

If one seeks faults and shortcomings, wishing to criticize others'
deficiencies, with an impure mind, angry and extremely resentful, one
will not be able to understand the Dharma taught by
the Buddhas. One should be well-disciplined, free from disputes, and
remove unfaithful thoughts. One should be far from all afflictions
and jealous thoughts. If one can do this, listen well
I will explain for you.

「若求过短者，　　意欲讥彼阙，汝不清净心，　　瞋恚极怀忿，诸佛所说法，　　终不能解悟。善顺离诤讼，　　并祛不信心，远离诸恼害，　　及以嫉妬想，若能如此者，　　善听为汝说。」

At that time, the brāhman thought, "The Śramaṇa Gautama already knows my mind." He then rose, paid homage to the Buddha, and said, "I truly have faults. My thoughts are not good. I only wish that the World-Honored One would accept my repentance." At that time, the World-Honored One, out of compassion, accepted his repentance. He was delighted and joyfully bowed his head in reverence before departing.

时，婆罗门而作是念：「瞿昙沙门已知我心。」即起礼佛，而作是言：「我实有过，所念不善，唯愿世尊受我忏悔。」尔时，世尊以怜愍故，受其忏悔。返戾欢喜，顶礼而去。
