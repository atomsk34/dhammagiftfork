SĀ² 2.8

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 8（八）

Parallel to: SN 21.7 / AN 4.48 / SĀ 1069

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, the bhikṣu Viśākha, son of Pañcaśikha, was teaching the Dharma to the assembly in the Dharma hall, with complete eloquence and unhindered speech, delighting the great assembly and causing them to listen without weariness and attain understanding. When the bhikṣus heard his teaching, they rejoiced and listened attentively, making offerings and paying respect, focusing their minds and concentrating their thoughts to listen to his teaching of the Dharma. It was not for the sake of material gain or fame, but for the sake of the meaning and eloquence, which were inexhaustible, causing those who heard to remember and not forget. The great assembly all listened in this way. Some bhikṣus went to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! The bhikṣu Viśākha, son of Pañcaśikha, is teaching the Dharma to the assembly in the Dharma hall, not for the sake of material gain, fame, or praise, but for the sake of the meaning and eloquence, which are inexhaustible, causing those who hear to remember and not forget."

一时，佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，毘舍佉沙门般阇罗子于讲堂上，集诸比丘而为说法，言辞圆满，所说无滞，能令大众闻者悦豫，听之无厌，即得悟解。时，诸比丘闻其所说，踊跃欢喜，至心听受，供养恭敬，捡心专意，听其说法，不为利养及与名称，应义才辩，无有穷尽，能令闻者忆持不忘，时，会大众皆如是听。有诸比丘往诣佛所，顶礼佛足，在一面立。白佛言：「世尊！毘舍佉比丘般阇罗子在讲堂上为众说法，不为利养、名称、赞叹，应义辩才，无有穷尽，能令闻者忆持不忘。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "You should go and summon that Viśākha, son of Pañcaśikha." The bhikṣus, having received the instruction, went to summon Viśākha. Having received the order, he came to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and stood to one side. The Buddha asked Viśākha, "Is it true that you gathered the bhikṣus to teach them the Dharma, and even caused the bhikṣus to listen attentively, and so on? Is this true?" He replied, "It is true."

佛告诸比丘：「汝可往唤彼毘舍佉般阇罗子。」时，诸比丘受教，往唤毘舍佉。既受勅已，来诣佛所，顶礼佛足，在一面立。佛问毘舍佉言：「汝实集诸比丘为其说法，乃至令诸比丘至心听受。有是事不？」答言：「实尔。」

The Buddha praised him, saying, "Excellent! Excellent! Viśākha! You gathered the bhikṣus in the Dharma hall to teach them the Dharma, and you did not do it for the sake of material gain or fame. Your words were complete and those who heard rejoiced and listened with sincere faith. From now on, you should always teach the Dharma in this way to benefit others. All of you bhikṣus, whether many or few, should engage in two things: first, you should expound the essential teachings of the Dharma. Second, if there is nothing to be said, you should remain silent and not discuss worldly matters. Do not underestimate silence now, for silence has great benefits."

佛赞之言：「善哉！善哉！毘舍佉！汝集诸比丘在讲堂上为其说法，又复不为利养名称，言辞圆满，闻者欢喜，至心信受。汝自今已后，常应如是说法饶益。汝诸比丘，若多若少，应行二事：一者、应说法要。二者、若无所说，应当] cf.【丽】默然，不得论说诸余俗事。汝等今者，莫轻默然。而默然者，有大利益。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

If in the great assembly, fools and sages gather together, if
nothing is spoken, people will not know the difference; if something
is revealed, then they will know the difference. Therefore, you should
now always expound the essential teachings of the Dharma, blazing the
torch of the Dharma, erecting the banner of the sages and
immortals. All the arhats and others take the wonderful Dharma as
their banner; all the sages and superior people take good words
as their banner.

「若诸大众中，　　愚智共聚集，若未有所说，　　人则不别知；若有所显说，　　然后乃别知。是故汝今者，　　常应说法要，炽然于法炬，　　竖立仙圣幢。诸阿罗汉等，　　咸妙法为幢；诸仙胜人等，　　以善语为幢。」

The Buddha having spoken this, the bhikṣus and others, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and upheld it.

佛说是已，诸比丘等闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
