SĀ² 2.95

Saṃyuktāgama (2nd)别译杂阿含经

SA-2 95（九五）

Parallel to: SN 7.13 / SĀ 1181

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in Kosala. At that time, there was a Brahmin named Tianjing in that country, and in his village there was a guest house. At that time, Venerable Upamana, as the Buddha's attendant, was staying in the guest house. At that time, the Tathāgata had a slight wind disorder and was suffering from back pain. Venerable Upamana put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and went to the house of Brahmin Tianjing. At that time, Tianjing was sitting at the gate shaving his hair. The Brahmin saw Venerable Upamana from afar and spoke a verse:

一时，佛游拘萨罗。尔时，彼国有婆罗门名曰天敬，其聚落中，有止客舍。尔时，尊者优波摩那为佛侍者，止客舍中。如来于时微患风动，苦于背痛。尊者优婆摩那著衣持钵，诣天敬婆罗门家。尔时，天敬在于门中剃发而坐。彼婆罗门遥见尊者，即说偈言：

Shaving your head, wearing the Dharma robes, holding the alms bowl
in your hand, standing at my gate, what do you seek?

「落发服法衣，　　手中执应器，住立我门侧，　　将欲何所求？」

At that time, Venerable Upamana replied in verse:

时，尊者优波摩那以偈答言：

The great arhat, the well-gone one, the sage suffering from back pain, needs
a little hot medicine water, therefore I have come to beg from you.

「大罗汉善逝，　　牟尼患背痛，须少燸药水，　　故来从汝乞。」

At that time, the brahmin immediately took a bowl full of ghee and oil, a basket of black honey, and a cart of hot medicine water, and gave them to him. At that time, the Venerable One, having obtained them, brought them to the Buddha. He used the oil and hot medicine water to wash and apply to the Buddha's body, and drank the black honey drink. The back pain was immediately cured. At that time, the brahmin Tianjing went to where the Buddha was the next morning, inquired about the World-Honored One's well-being, and sat to one side. At that time, the World-Honored One asked the brahmin in verse:

时，婆罗门即取钵盛满苏油，黑石蜜一箧，燸药水一车，而以与之。尔时，尊者既得之已，赍诣佛所，即以此油，并燸药水洗涂佛身，饮黑蜜浆，背痛即愈。时，天敬婆罗门于后日朝，往诣佛所，问讯世尊，在一面坐。尔时，世尊以偈问婆罗门言：

How does a brahmin practice the dharma of a brahmin? What
giving brings great fruit? When is it the time for giving?
In what field of merit does one obtain the supreme reward?

「云何婆罗门，　　行婆罗门法，施何获大果？　　何者是施时？于何福田中，　　获得于胜报？」

At that time, the brahmin again replied in verse:

时，婆罗门复以偈答言：

There are those with much learning, who are well-versed and able
to retain, whose parents are truly pure, and whose appearance is
all dignified. Such are called the three-wise brahmins. If one gives
to such places, one can obtain great fruit and reward. Giving
clothes and food at the right time is called the supreme
field of merit.

「有多教学者，　　多闻能总持，父母真正净，　　颜容悉端严，如是等名为，　　三明婆罗门。若施如斯处，　　能获大果报，随时施衣食，　　是名胜福田。」

At that time, the brahmin again asked the Buddha in verse:

时，婆罗门复以偈问佛：

Gautama, what kind of person do you call a brahmin? What
are the three knowledges? Giving what can obtain great fruit? What
is the time for giving? What is the supreme field of merit?

「瞿昙说何种，　　名为婆罗门？云何为三明？　　施何得大果？何者是施时？　　云何胜福田？」

At that time, the World-Honored One replied in verse:

尔时，世尊以偈答言：

Clearly knowing the three times, seeing the good and bad destinies of humans
and gods, attaining the end of birth and death, and obtaining various supernatural
powers, with the mind attaining liberation, this is called the three knowledges. Giving
to them obtains great fruit, this is called the supreme field of merit.

「明知于三世，　　见人天恶趣，得尽于生死，　　并获诸神通，心智得解脱，　　是谓为三明。施彼得大果，　　是名胜福田。」

At that time, the Brahman Tikshna heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced, and departed.

时，天敬婆罗门闻佛所说，欢喜而去。
