SĀ³ 3.18

Saṃyuktāgama (3rd)杂阿含经

SA-3 18（一八）

Parallel to: AN 4.152 / SĀ 667 (~)

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

Once, the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. The Buddha then addressed the bhikṣus, saying: "There are four kinds of power. What are the four? First is the power of intention, second is the power of diligence, third is the power of non-transgression, and fourth is the power of guarding. What is the power of intention? If a bhikṣu knows what is good and what is bad, what is defiled and what is pure, what is transgressed and what is not transgressed, what is to be practiced and what is not to be practiced, what is not and what is increased, what is white and what is black, and what is to be attained from defilement, he knows it as it really is. This is called the power of intention. What is the power of diligence? If a bhikṣu is in a defiled place, he speaks of what is evil, what is transgressed, what is not to be practiced, what is black, and what is not to be advanced. He abandons such things. If he is in a defiled place where there is good speech, non-transgression, what is to be practiced, what is to be spoken, what is white, and what is to be spoken, he practices such defilements. He practices diligence for the sake of intention and for the sake of controlling intention. This is called the power of diligence. What is the power of non-transgression? If a bhikṣu abides in non-transgression in body, speech, and mind, this is called the power of non-transgression. What is the power of guarding? It refers to the four groups. What are the four? First is gathering, second is giving, third is compassion, and fourth is mutual assistance in good practice. This is called the power of guarding."

一时佛在舍卫国，行在祇树给孤独园。佛便告比丘：「有四力。何等为四力？一者意力，二者精进力，三者不犯力，四者守力。意力为何等？若有比丘知善恶浊如至诚知，亦知犯亦知不犯，亦知可行亦知不可行，亦知非亦知增，亦知白亦知黑，亦知从得浊如谛知，是名为意力。精进力为何等？在有比丘在有浊所恶说，所犯说，所不可说，所黑说，不用进人说，如是辈为弃之，若所为浊好说，不犯说、可习说、可说、白说、所道说，如是辈浊，为行为贪欲，为行为精进，为受意为制意？是名为精进力。不犯力为何等？在有比丘为不犯身受行止，为不犯口，为不犯心受行止，是名为不犯力。守力为何等？谓四辈。何等为四辈？一为摄，二为布施，三为相哀，四为相助善行，是名为守力。」

Thus have I heard:

佛说如是。
