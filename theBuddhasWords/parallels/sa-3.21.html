SĀ³ 3.21

Saṃyuktāgama (3rd)杂阿含经

SA-3 21（二一）

Parallel to: SN 11.11 / SĀ 1104 / SĀ² 2.33

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "When Śakra, the king of the devas, was in the human realm in the past, he had seven vows. He was like this in his life, like this in his pursuit, and like this in his practice. Therefore, he became Śakra. What were the seven? He vowed to be filial to his parents in his life. He vowed to see the elderly and pay respect to them in his life. He vowed not to speak harsh words. He vowed to speak in accordance with the Dharma in his life. He vowed not to be angry, slander, or speak falsely in his life. He vowed to speak truthfully in his life, to be joyful and at peace with truthfulness, and to always be trustworthy and not deceive the world. He vowed not to be stingy in his life, and he vowed not to follow stingy families in his mind. He practiced giving and always gave what was requested, known as 'good giving,' giving equally. These were the seven vows that Śakra, the king of the devas, made in his life in the human realm. He was like this in his life, like this in his pursuit, and like this in his practice. Therefore, he became Śakra." From then on, he spoke of the ultimate:

一时佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。佛告比丘：「天上释为故世在人中有七愿，为如至命、为如求就、为所从本，故为释。何等为七？到命要当为父母孝。到命要当为见老为礼。当为不出口炎言。当为随意法语言。当为至命要不怒谗妄语。当为至命要至诚语，至诚喜至诚止，常信不欺天下。当为至命要天下悭，我当为意中不随悭家中。行布施放手常与所求，名好布施，布施等分。为是释天王故世在人中为是七愿，说为至命要具行就，为从是本释释得。」从后说绝：

To be filial to one's parents, to pay respect to the elderly in the family, not to speak harshly, to speak in accordance with the Dharma, to be free from anger, slander, and false speech, to be truthful, joyful, and at peace with truthfulness, to always be trustworthy and not deceive the world, to be free from stinginess, and to practice giving and always give what is requested, known as 'good giving,' giving equally.

「为孝父母，　姓中有老人礼，　不炎说随意说谗妄言弃，　从悭自出谛不怒喜行言。

"Therefore, one can attain the heavens. Those who wish to practice this should pay respect to the heavens in this way."

「为是故能得上天，在所人欲行是，当为天上礼如是。」

The Buddha spoke thus.

佛说如是。
