SĀ³ 3.5

Saṃyuktāgama (3rd)杂阿含经

SA-3 5（五）

Parallel to: SN 6.7–9 / SĀ 1193 / SĀ² 2.106

Thus have I heard:

闻如是：

Once, the Buddha was at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. At that time, the deity Brahmā, who was bright and radiant, came to the Buddha in the middle of the night. At that time, the Buddha was walking in the fire-walking meditation. The deity Brahmā thought, "It is still early. I should go see the Buddha. Now the Buddha is walking in the fire-walking meditation, causing me to be in front." He went to the monk Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, who was in the group of Devadatta. The deity Brahmā then went to the monk Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, who was in the group of Devadatta. Having arrived, he said to the monk Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, "Thus, Pūraṇa! Pūraṇa should hold good intentions towards Śāriputra, Maudgalyāyana, and other monks who practice wisdom." The monk Pūraṇa Kāśyapa then said, "Who are you?" The deity Brahmā said, "I am Brahmā." The monk Pūraṇa Kāśyapa replied, "Did the Buddha say that you are a non-returner?" The deity Brahmā said, "Yes." The monk Pūraṇa Kāśyapa asked, "What is the reason for coming here?" The deity Brahmā then thought, "Why is there no compassion here?" He then spoke this verse:

一时佛在舍卫国祇树给孤独园。是时，自梵自明夜亦明梵往至佛，在时，佛火神足行，在时，自梵念：「是尚早至佛见，今佛亦火神足行令我今居前。」到俱披犁比丘调达部，便自梵至俱披犁比丘调达部，已到为告俱披犁调达部：「如是俱披犁！俱披犁为持好意向舍利弗、目干连比丘，亦余慧行道者。」俱披犁调达部便言：「卿为谁？」梵言：「我为梵。」俱披犁调达部报言：「佛说卿阿那含不？」梵言：「是。」俱披犁调达部报言：「何因缘得来到是间？」便梵思惟念：「是何以无有悲意？」便自梵说是绝：

"The immeasurable should not be measured, yet the world is of little wisdom. The immeasurable is sought to be measured, thus the world is self-obscured."

「不可量欲量，　为是故世间少慧，　不可量欲作量，　如是世间自覆盖。」

Then the deity Brahmā went to the Buddha, bowed at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. Having sat to one side, the deity Brahmā said to the Buddha, "I am self-luminous, and the night is also bright. I have come to the Buddha. At that time, the Buddha was walking in the fire-walking meditation. I then thought, 'It is still early. I should go see the Buddha.' I have already seen the Buddha walking in the fire-walking meditation. I then thought, 'May I be in front.' I went to the monk Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, who was in the group of Devadatta. I then went to the monk Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, who was in the group of Devadatta. Having arrived, I said to the monk Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, 'Pūraṇa! Pūraṇa should hold good intentions towards Śāriputra, Maudgalyāyana, and other monks who practice the same path.' He then said, 'Who are you?' I said, 'Brahmā.' He then said to me, 'Did the Buddha say that you are a non-returner?' I said, 'Yes.' He then said, 'What is the reason for coming here?' I then thought, 'Alas! Why is there no compassion here?'

便梵行至佛，已到为佛足下礼，一处止。已一处止，自梵为佛说：「是我为自光明，夜亦已明为至佛，已至是时火神足行，我便思惟念：『尚早至见佛。』我已到见佛火神足，我便念令我居前，行俱披犁比丘调达部，我为便至俱披犁比丘调达部，已至俱披犁比丘调达部，我便告：『俱披犁俱披犁，持好心向舍利弗、目干连比丘，亦同道行者。』便言：『卿为谁？』我言：『梵。』便报我：『佛说卿阿那含不？』我言：『是。』便报：『若何因缘得来到是？』我便思惟念：『咄！是何以无有悲意？』

"The immeasurable should not be measured, thus the world is of little wisdom. The immeasurable is sought to be measured, the world's people think they know themselves."

「『不可量欲量，　是故世间少慧，　不可量说量，世间人意计我自知。』」

The Buddha then said, "The group of Devadatta is breaking up." At that time, this was said: "The immeasurable should not be measured, thus the world is of little wisdom. The immeasurable is sought to be measured, thus the world is self-obscured."

佛便说：「俱披犁调达部破戏亦［口＊建］。」是时说是绝：「不可量欲量，　故世间难得慧，　不可量说量，　从是世间自覆盖。」

The Buddha spoke thus.

佛说如是。
