SĀ 1008

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1008（一〇〇八）

Parallel to: SN 1.70 / SN 1.74 / SĀ² 2.234 / SĀ² 2.235

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time a god with an extremely handsome appearance came to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha’s feet, and his whole body emitted a bright light that illuminated Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park.

时，有天子容色绝妙，来诣佛所，稽首佛足，身诸光明遍照祇树给孤独园。

At that time the god spoke a verse, saying:

时，彼天子而说偈言：

Among the beings who rise from the ground, who is the
most excellent? Among those who fall from the sky, who is
the most excellent? Among all places of prayer, which is the
most excellent? Among all languages, which is the most excellent eloquence?

「从地起众生，　　何者为最胜？于空堕落者，　　复以何胜上？凡所祈请处，　　何者最第一？于诸言语中，　　何者为上辩？」

At that time a god who had previously been a farmer's son, now reborn in heaven, spoke a verse in reply to that god, according to his previous habits:

时，有一天子本为田家子，今得生天上，以本习故，即便说偈答彼天子：

The five grains grow from the ground, which is the most
excellent. Seeds falling from the sky to the ground are the
most excellent. Cows nourish people, which is the most excellent among
supports. The words of a beloved child are the most excellent among speech.

「五谷从地生，　　是则为最胜，种子于空中，　　落地为最胜。［绛-糸＋牛］牛资养人，　　是则依中胜，爱子有所说，　　是则言中胜。」

The god who had asked the question said to the one who had answered, "I did not ask you. Why do you speak so lightly and rashly? I myself asked the World-Honored One in verse:

彼发问天子语答者言：「我不问汝，何故多言轻躁妄说，我自说偈问世尊言：

Among beings born from the earth, who is the most excellent? Among
those who fall from the sky to the ground, who is again
the most excellent? Among all places of prayer, which is the most
excellent? Among all forms of speech, which is the most excellent eloquence?

「从地起众生，　　何者为最胜？于空堕地者，　　复以何为胜？凡所祈请处，　　何者为最胜？于诸言语中，　　何者为上辩？」

At that time, the World-Honored One replied in verse:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

Among those who rise from below, the three insights are the most excellent
Among those who descend from the sky, the three insights are also the
foremost. The Sangha of noble disciples is the most excellent teacher to rely
upon. The teachings spoken by the Tathāgata are the most excellent eloquence among all teachings.

「从下踊出者，　　三明为最上，从空流下者，　　三明亦第一，贤圣弟子僧，　　是师依之上，如来之所说，　　诸说之最辩。」

At that time, the god-son again spoke a verse:

时，彼天子复说偈言：

How many dharmas arise in the world? How many dharmas are agreeable? How many dharmas
are taken and loved in the world? How many dharmas are diminished in the world?

「世间几法起？　　几法相顺可？世几法取爱？　　世几法损减？」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse in reply:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

The six dharmas arise in the world, the six dharmas are agreeable, the six dharmas
are taken and loved in the world, the six dharmas are diminished in the world.

「世六法等起，　　世六法顺可，世六法取爱，　　世六法损减。」

At that time, the god-son spoke another verse:

时，彼天子复说偈言：

Long have I seen the brāhman attain parinirvāṇa, all
fears have passed, forever transcending worldly love and affection.

「久见婆罗门，　　逮得般涅槃，一切怖已过，　　永超世恩爱。」

At that time, the god-son, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and was delighted. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and then disappeared.

时，彼天子闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，稽首佛足，即没不现。
