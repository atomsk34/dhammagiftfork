SĀ 102

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 102（一〇二） 旃陀罗

Parallel to: Snp 1.7 / SĀ² 2.268

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, the World-Honored One, in the morning, put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and entered Rājagṛha to beg for food. He gradually begged for food and arrived at the house of the brāhman Pāṭikāputra.

尔时，世尊晨朝著衣持钵，入王舍城乞食。次第乞食，至婆罗豆婆遮婆罗门舍。

At that time, the brāhman was holding a wooden ladle, offering food and drink to the fire, and standing by the door. When he saw the Buddha coming from afar, he said to the Buddha, "Stop! Stop! You leader of the low caste! Do not come near my door!"

时，婆罗门手执木杓，盛诸饮食，供养火具，住于门边，遥见佛来。见已，白佛作是言：「住！住！领群特！慎勿近我门！」

The Buddha said to the brāhman, "Do you know the leader of the low caste and the Dharma of the leader of the low caste?"

佛告婆罗门：「汝知领群特、领群特法耶？」

The brāhman said, "I do not know the leader of the low caste, nor do I know the Dharma of the leader of the low caste. Does the śramaṇa Gautama know the leader of the low caste and the Dharma of the leader of the low caste?"

婆罗门言：「我不知领群特，亦不知领群特法，沙门瞿昙知领群特及领群特法不？」

The Buddha said, "I know well the leader of the low caste and the Dharma of the leader of the low caste."

佛言：「我善知是领群特及领群特法。」

At that time, the brāhman immediately put down his fire-making implements, quickly prepared a seat, invited the Buddha to sit, and said, "Gautama! Please explain to me the leader of the low caste and the Dharma of the leader of the low caste."

是时，婆罗门即放事火具，疾敷床座，请佛令坐，白言：「瞿昙！为我说领群特及领群特法。」

The Buddha then took his seat and spoke a verse for him:

佛即就座为说偈言：

The
Buddha
then
spoke
a
verse
for him:

「瞋恚心怀恨，　　隐覆诸过恶，犯戒起恶见，　　虚伪不真实，如是等士夫，　　当知领群特。憋暴贪悋惜，　　恶欲悭谄伪，无惭无愧心，　　当知领群特。一生二生者，　　一切皆杀害，无有慈愍心，　　是为领群特。若杀缚椎打，　　聚落及城邑，无道以切责，　　当知领群特。住止及行路，　　为众之导首，苦切诸群下，　　恐怛相迫愶，取利以供己，　　当知领群特。聚落及空地，　　有主无主物，掠护为己有，　　当知领群特。自弃薄其妻，　　又不入婬舍，侵陵他所爱，　　当知领群特。内外诸亲属，　　同心善知识，侵掠彼所受，　　当知领群特。妄语欺诳人，　　诈取无证财，他索而不还，　　当知领群特。为己亦为他，　　举责及财与，或复顺他语，　　妄语为他证，如是妄语者，　　当知领群特。作恶不善业，　　无有人知者，隐讳覆藏恶，　　当知领群特。若人问其义，　　而答以非义，颠倒欺诳人，　　当知领群特。实空无所有，　　而轻毁智者，愚痴为利故，　　当知领群特。高慢自称举，　　毁坏于他人，是极卑鄙慢，　　当知领群特。自造诸过恶，　　移过诬他人，妄语谤清白，　　当知领群特。前受他利养，　　他人来诣己，无有敬报心，　　当知领群特。沙门婆罗门，　　如法来乞求，呵责而不与，　　当知领群特。若父母年老，　　少壮气已谢，不勤加奉养，　　当知领群特。父母诸尊长，　　兄弟亲眷属，实非阿罗汉，　　自显罗汉德，世间之大贼，　　当知领群特。初上种姓生，　　习婆罗门典，而于其中间，　　习行诸恶业，不以胜生故，　　障呵责恶道，现法受呵责，　　后世堕恶道，生旃陀罗家，　　世称须陀夷，名闻遍天下，　　旃陀罗所无，婆罗门刹利，　　大姓所供养，乘于净天道，　　平等正直住，不以生处障，　　令不生梵天，现法善名誉，　　后世生善趣，二生汝当知，　　如我所显示，不以所生故，　　名为领群特。不以所生故，　　名为婆罗门，业为领群特，　　业为婆罗门。」

The brahmin said to the Buddha:

婆罗门白佛言：

The Buddha
then spoke
a verse for him:

「如是大精进，　　如是大牟尼，不以所生故，　　名为领群特。不以所生故，　　名为婆罗门，业故领群特，　　业故婆罗门。」

At that time, the fire-worshipping brahmin Douba Zhe gained faith and offered a full bowl of delicious food to the World-Honored One. The World-Honored One did not accept it, because it was obtained through reciting a verse, as explained above.

时，事火婆罗豆婆遮婆罗门转得信心，以满钵好食奉上世尊。世尊不受，以说偈得故，偈如上说。

At that time, the fire-worshipping brahmin Douba Zhe, having seen the auspicious signs of the food, increased his faith and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! May I now be allowed to go forth and receive full ordination in the true Dharma and Vinaya?"

时，事火婆罗豆婆遮婆罗门见食瑞应已，增其信心，白佛言：「世尊！我今可得为正法、律出家、受具足不？」

The Buddha told the brahmin, "You may now go forth and receive full ordination in the true Dharma and Vinaya." He then went forth and, dwelling in solitude, contemplated as before, and attained arahantship with his mind well liberated.

佛告婆罗门：「汝今可得于正法、律出家、受具足戒。」即得出家，独静思惟，如前说，乃至得阿罗汉，心善解脱。

At that time, the brahmin Douba Zhe attained arahantship, with his mind well liberated. He joyfully spoke a verse, saying:

时，婆罗豆婆遮婆罗门得阿罗汉，心善解脱，自觉喜乐，即说偈言：

Seeking purity through non-Dharma, making offerings and worshipping fire, not
knowing the path of purity, like one born blind. Now
I have attained peace and happiness, gone forth and received
full ordination, attained the three insights, and accomplished what the
Buddha taught. Formerly a brahmin's challenge, now a brahmin, bathing
free from dust and defilements, crossing to the other shore
of the heavens.

「非道求清净，　　供养祠祀火，不识清净道，　　犹如生盲者。今已得安乐，　　出家受具足，逮得于三明，　　佛所教已作。先婆罗门难，　　今为婆罗门，沐浴离尘垢，　　度诸天彼岸。」

This sutra and this scroll, the text and meaning of the two versions of the countries of Korea and Song are completely the same, all having nineteen sutras, totaling twenty-five sheets. The Korean version has fifteen sutras, if written according to the Song canon style, it can be made into twenty-seven sheets. Moreover, its text and meaning are completely different from the two versions of Korea and Song, not knowing which to take and which to leave. Now, examining the Korean and Song versions of the sutra, it is the forty-second scroll of this sutra in the lower flow case. The Song canon mistakenly reprinted that scroll here as the first four scrolls, and Korea also followed it, which is a mistake! Therefore, it is necessary to remove that and take this Korean version of the sutra.

此经此卷，国、宋二本文义全同，皆有十九经，总二十五纸。丹本有十五经，若依宋藏式] cf.【丽】写之，可成二十七纸。又其文义与国、宋二本全别，未知去取。今捡国、宋本经，则下流函中，此经第四十二卷耳。宋藏错将彼卷重刊于此，为初四卷，国亦仍之者，错也！故令去彼，取此丹本经焉。
