SĀ 1023

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1023（一〇二三） 叵求那

Parallel to: AN 6.56

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Thus have I heard:

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。时，有尊者叵求那住东园鹿母讲堂，疾病困笃。

Venerable Ānanda went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stepped back to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Venerable Pūrṇa is staying at the Deer Park Lecture Hall in the Eastern Garden, suffering from a serious illness. Many sick monks die like this. Excellent, World-Honored One! Please go to the Deer Park Lecture Hall in the Eastern Garden where Venerable Pūrṇa is, out of compassion!"

尊者阿难往诣佛所，稽首礼足，退住一面，白佛言：「世尊！尊者叵求那住东园鹿母讲堂，疾病困笃，如是病比丘多有死者。善哉！世尊！愿至东园鹿母讲堂尊者叵求那所，以哀愍故！」

At that time, the World-Honored One silently accepted. In the afternoon, he arose from meditation and went to the Deer Park Lecture Hall in the Eastern Garden, to Venerable Pūrṇa's room. He spread out his seat and sat down, expounding the Dharma for Venerable Pūrṇa in various ways, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting him. Having shown, taught, illuminated, and delighted him, he rose from his seat and left.

尔时，世尊默然而许，至日晡时，从禅觉，往诣东园鹿母讲堂，至尊者叵求那房，敷座而坐，为尊者叵求那种种说法，示、教、照、喜；示、教、照、喜已，从坐起去。

After the World-Honored One left, Venerable Pūrṇa soon passed away. At the time of his passing, his faculties were joyful, his complexion was pure, and his skin was fair.

尊者叵求那，世尊去后，寻即命终。当命终时，诸根喜悦，颜貌清净，肤色鲜白。

At that time, Venerable Ānanda, having made offerings to Venerable Pūrṇa's relics, went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and addressed the Buddha, saying, "World-Honored One! After the World-Honored One left, Venerable Pūrṇa soon passed away. At the time of his passing, his faculties were joyful, his complexion was pure, and his skin was fair and lustrous. I do not know, World-Honored One! In what realm will he be reborn? How will he take rebirth? What will be his future state?"

时，尊者阿难供养尊者叵求那舍利已，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，却住一面，白佛言：「世尊！尊者叵求那，世尊来后，寻便命终，临命终时，诸根喜悦，肤色清净，鲜白光泽。不审世尊！彼当生何趣？云何受生，后世云何？」

The Buddha told Ānanda, "If a bhikṣu, before becoming ill, has not yet severed the five lower fetters, but when he becomes aware of illness and his body is afflicted with pain, his mind is not at ease, and his faculties are weak, he hears the great teacher's instructions, teachings, and various Dharma teachings. Having heard the Dharma, he severs the five lower fetters. Ānanda! This is the benefit and blessing of the great teacher's instructions and teachings.

佛告阿难：「若有比丘先未病时，未断五下分结，若觉病起，其身苦患，心不调适，生分微弱，得闻大师教授、教诫种种说法，彼闻法已，断五下分结。阿难！是则大师教授设法福利。

Furthermore, Ānanda! If a bhikṣu, before becoming ill, has not yet severed the five lower fetters, then when illness arises, his body is afflicted with pain, and his faculties become weaker. He does not receive the great teacher's instructions, teachings, and Dharma teachings, but he encounters other learned and virtuous practitioners of the holy life who instruct, teach, and give Dharma teachings. Having heard the Dharma, he severs the five lower fetters. Ānanda! This is called the benefit and blessing of receiving instructions, teachings, and listening to the Dharma.

「复次，阿难！若有比丘先未病时，未断五下分结，然后病起，身遭苦患，生分转微，不蒙大师教授、教诫说法，然遇诸余多闻大德修梵行者教授、教诫说法，得闻法已，断五下分结。阿难！是名教授、教诫听法福利。

Furthermore, Ānanda! If a bhikṣu, before becoming ill, has not yet severed the five lower fetters, and his faculties become weaker, he does not hear the great teacher's instructions, teachings, and Dharma teachings, nor does he hear the instructions, teachings, and Dharma teachings of other learned and virtuous practitioners of the holy life. However, he contemplates and examines the Dharma he has previously received, and thus severs the five lower fetters. Ānanda! This is called the benefit and blessing of contemplating and examining the Dharma one has previously heard.

「复次，阿难！若比丘先未病时，不断五下分结，乃至生分微弱，不闻大师教授、教诫说法，复不闻余多闻大德诸梵行者教授、教诫说法，然彼先所受法，独静思惟，称量观察，得断五下分结。阿难！是名思惟观察先所闻法所得福利。

Furthermore, Ānanda! If a bhikṣu, before becoming ill, has severed the five lower fetters, but has not attained the unsurpassed liberation from craving, and has not eliminated the contaminants, his mind is well liberated. Then, when he becomes ill, his body is afflicted with pain, and his faculties become weaker. He hears the great teacher's instructions, teachings, and Dharma teachings, and attains the unsurpassed liberation from craving, eliminates the contaminants, and attains liberation through detachment. Ānanda! This is called the benefit and blessing of the great teacher's Dharma teachings.

「复次，阿难！若有比丘先未病时，断五下分结，不得无上爱尽解脱，不起诸漏，心善解脱，然后得病，身遭苦患，生分微弱，得闻大师教授、教诫说法，得无上爱尽解脱，不起诸漏，离欲解脱。阿难！是名大师说法福利。

Furthermore, Ānanda! If a bhikṣu, before becoming ill, has severed the five lower fetters, but has not attained the unsurpassed liberation from craving, and has not eliminated the contaminants, he attains liberation through detachment. He becomes aware of an illness in his body, and is severely afflicted with pain. He does not hear the great teacher's instructions, teachings, and Dharma teachings, but he hears the instructions, teachings, and Dharma teachings of other learned and virtuous practitioners of the holy life, and attains the unsurpassed liberation from craving, eliminates the contaminants, and attains liberation through detachment. Ānanda! This is called the benefit and blessing of hearing the instructions, teachings, and Dharma teachings.

「复次，阿难！若有比丘先未病时，断五下分结，不得无上爱尽解脱，不起诸漏，离欲解脱，觉身病起，极遭苦患，不得大师教授、教诫说法，然得诸余多闻大德诸梵行者教授、教诫说法，得无上爱尽解脱，不起诸漏，离欲解脱。阿难！是名教授教诫闻法福利。

Furthermore, Ānanda! If a bhikṣu, before becoming ill, has severed the five lower fetters, but has not attained the unsurpassed liberation from craving, and has not eliminated the contaminants, he attains liberation through detachment. He becomes aware of an illness in his body, and is severely afflicted with pain. He does not hear the great teacher's instructions, teachings, and Dharma teachings, nor does he hear the instructions, teachings, and Dharma teachings of other learned and virtuous practitioners of the holy life. However, he reflects, contemplates, and examines the Dharma he has previously heard, alone in a quiet place, and attains the unsurpassed liberation from craving, eliminates the contaminants, and attains liberation through detachment. Ānanda! This is called the benefit and blessing of reflecting on the Dharma one has previously heard.

「复次，阿难！若有比丘先未病时，断五下分结，不得无上爱尽解脱，不起诸漏，离欲解脱。其身病起，极生苦患，不得大师教授、教诫说法，不得诸余多闻大德教授、教诫说法，然先所闻法，独一静处，思惟称量观察，得无上爱尽解脱，不起诸漏，离欲解脱。阿难！是名思惟先所闻法所得福利。

Why is it that the monk Pūrṇa does not have a joyful mind, a clear complexion, and a radiant body?" "Before Pūrṇa became ill, he had not severed the five lower fetters. He personally heard the instructions, teachings, and Dharma teachings from the great teacher, severed the five lower fetters, and the World-Honored One predicted for him that he would become a non-returner.

「何缘叵求那比丘不得诸根欣悦，色貌清净，肤体鲜泽？叵求那比丘先未病时，未断五下分结，彼亲从大师闻教授、教诫说法，断五下分结，世尊为彼尊者叵求那受阿那含记。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha said to Venerable Ānanda, "Having heard the Buddha's words, Ānanda rejoiced and paid homage before departing.

佛说此经已，尊者阿难闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
