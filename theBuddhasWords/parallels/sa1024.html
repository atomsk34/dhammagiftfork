SĀ 1024

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1024（一〇二四） 阿湿波誓

Parallel to: SN 22.88

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Asvajit was staying in the Deer Mother’s Lecture Hall in the Eastern Garden, suffering from a serious illness, in great pain. Venerable Pūrṇa Maitrāyaṇīputra attended to him and provided for him...as extensively described in the previous Sūtra on Vakkali, namely, speaking of the three feelings, up to "increasing without diminishing."

尔时，尊者阿湿波誓住东园鹿母讲堂，身遭重病，极生苦患。尊者富隣尼瞻视供给……如前跋迦梨修多罗广说，谓说三受，乃至「转增无损。」

The Buddha told Asvajit, "Do not change your mind!"

佛告阿湿波誓：「汝莫变悔！」

Asvajit said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I really do have regrets."

阿湿波誓白佛言：「世尊！我实有变悔。」

The Buddha asked Asvajit, "Have you broken the precepts?"

佛告阿湿波誓：「汝得无破戒耶？」

Asvajit said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I have not broken the precepts."

阿湿波誓白佛言：「世尊！我不破戒。」

The Buddha said to Asvajit, "Since you have not broken the precepts, why do you regret it?"

佛告阿湿波誓：「汝不破戒，何为变悔？」

Asvajit said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Before I was sick, I often cultivated the concentration of physical and breath bliss. Today I am unable to enter that samādhi again. I thought to myself, 'Will I lose this samādhi?'"

阿湿波誓白佛言：「世尊！我先未病时，得身息乐正受多修习；我于今日不复能得入彼三昧，我作是思惟：『将无退失是三昧耶？』」

The Buddha said to Asvajit, "I will ask you now, answer me as you wish. Asvajit, do you see form as being me, different from me, or in a relationship with me?"

佛告阿湿波誓：「我今问汝，随意答我。阿湿波誓，汝见色即是我、异我、相在不？」

Asvajit said to the Buddha, "No, World-Honored One!"

阿湿波誓白佛言：「不也，世尊！」

He further asked, "Do you see feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness as being me, different from me, or in a relationship with me?"

复问：「汝见受、想、行、识是我、异我、相在不？」

Asvajit said to the Buddha, "No, World-Honored One!"

阿湿波誓白佛言：「不也，世尊！」

The Buddha told Asvajit, "Since you do not see form as being me, different from me, or in a relationship with me, and you do not see feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness as being me, different from me, or in a relationship with me, why do you regret it?"

佛告阿湿波誓：「汝既不见色是我、异我、相在，不见受、想、行、识是我、异我、相在，何故变悔？」

Asvajit said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! It is because of improper contemplation."

阿湿波誓白佛言：「世尊！不正思惟故。」

The Buddha told Asvajit, "If a śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa is firm and equal in samādhi, if they cannot enter that samādhi, they should not think, 'I have regressed in samādhi.' If a noble disciple does not see form as being me, different from me, or in a relationship with me, and does not see feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness as being me, different from me, or in a relationship with me, they should only be aware of this: 'Desire, anger, and delusion are completely extinguished without remainder.' When desire, anger, and delusion are completely extinguished without remainder, all taints are exhausted, and one is liberated by the uncontaminated mind and wisdom. In this very life, one knows and realizes for oneself: 'My birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and I know that I will not be subject to any future lives.'"

佛告阿湿波誓：「若沙门、婆罗门三昧坚固，三昧平等，若不得入彼三昧，不应作念：『我于三昧退减。』若复圣弟子不见色是我、异我、相在，不见受、想、行、识是我、异我、相在，但当作是觉知：『贪欲永尽无余，瞋恚、愚痴永尽无余。』贪、恚、痴永尽无余已，一切漏尽，无漏心解脱、慧解脱，现法自知作证：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』」

When the Buddha taught this Dharma, Venerable Asvajit's taints ceased, his mind was liberated, and he was joyful and elated. Because of his joy and elation, his physical illness was cured.

佛说是法时，尊者阿湿波誓不起诸漏，心得解脱，欢喜踊悦。欢喜踊悦故，身病即除。

The Buddha taught this sūtra, causing Venerable Asvajit to rejoice and delight. After that, he rose from his seat and departed.

佛说此经，令尊者阿湿波誓欢喜随喜已，从坐起而去。

The Sūtra on Cāma is explained in the same way as the Five Aggregates.

差摩迦修多罗如五受阴处说。
