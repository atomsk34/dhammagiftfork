SĀ 1029

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1029（一〇二九） 疾病

Parallel to: SN 36.8

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Thus have I heard:

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园……如上说。时，有众多比丘集会迦梨隶讲堂，多有疾病……如上说。差别者：「乃至圣弟子如是观者，于色解脱，于受、想、行、识解脱，我说是等解脱生、老、病、死。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

The wise and learned ones do not fail to be aware of all
feelings. If they discern and clearly understand the feelings of suffering and pleasure
they should know the firm truth. Ordinary people have ups and downs. They
are not attached to pleasure and do not waver in suffering. Knowing the
arising of feelings and non-feelings depends on the perception of greed and anger
Having eliminated these, their minds are well liberated. Focusing on the wonderful object
they are rightly oriented, waiting for the end. If a bhikṣu diligently practices
his right knowledge is unshakable. Regarding all these feelings, the wise ones are
able to be aware of them. Having understood all feelings, they exhaust all
taints in this present life. Relying on wisdom to end their lives, they
attain nirvāṇa and do not fall into the cycle of birth and death.

「智慧多闻者，　　非不觉诸受，若于苦乐受，　　分别谛明了。当知坚固事，　　凡夫有升降，于乐不染著，　　于苦不倾动。知受不受生，　　依于贪恚觉，断除斯等已，　　其心善解脱。系念缘妙境，　　正向待终期，若比丘精勤，　　正智不倾动。于此一切受，　　慧者能觉知，了知诸受已，　　现法尽诸漏。依慧而命终，　　涅槃不堕数。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha said, "All bhikṣus, rejoice!"

佛说此经已，诸比丘欢喜，作礼而去。
