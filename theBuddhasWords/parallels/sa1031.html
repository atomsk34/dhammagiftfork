SĀ 1031

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1031（一〇三一） 给孤独

Parallel to: SN 55.27

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Ānanda heard that the elder Anāthapiṇḍada was suffering from illness. He went to his house. The elder, seeing Ānanda from afar, tried to get up from his bed...and so on, up to the three feelings, as extensively explained in the previous sūtra, up to "The illness only increases and does not decrease."

时，尊者阿难闻给孤独长者身遭苦患，往诣其舍。长者遥见阿难，冯床欲起……乃至说三受，如前叉摩修多罗广说，乃至「苦患但增不损。」

At that time, Venerable Ānanda said to the elder, "Do not be afraid! If foolish, ignorant ordinary people do not believe in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Saṃgha, and do not have the holy precepts, then they will be afraid and fear death and the suffering of the next world. But now you have already cut off and know what is to be cut off and known. You have pure faith in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Saṃgha, and have accomplished the holy precepts."

时，尊者阿难告长者言：「勿恐怖！若愚痴无闻凡夫不信于佛，不信法、僧，圣戒不具，故有恐怖，亦畏命终及后世苦。汝今不信已断、已知，于佛净信具足，于法、僧净信具足，圣戒成就。」

The elder said to Venerable Ānanda, "What am I afraid of now? When I first saw the World-Honored One in the cold forest cemetery in Rājagṛha, I attained the unshakable faith in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Saṃgha, and accomplished the holy precepts. Since then, I have given all the wealth in my family to the Buddha, his disciples, bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, and upāsikās."

长者白尊者阿难：「我今何所恐怖？我始于王舍城寒林中丘塜间见世尊，即得于佛不坏净，于法、僧不坏净，圣戒成就。自从是来，家有钱财悉与佛、弟子，比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆夷共。」

Venerable Ānanda said, "Excellent! Elder, you yourself have declared this to be the fruit of stream-entry."

尊者阿难言：「善哉！长者！汝自记说是须陀洹果。」

The elder said to Venerable Ānanda, "Please take your meal here."

长者白尊者阿难：「可就此食。」

Venerable Ānanda silently accepted the invitation.

尊者阿难默然受请。

He prepared various pure and delicious foods to offer to Venerable Ānanda. After the meal, he further taught the elder various teachings, showing, guiding, illuminating, and delighting him. Then he rose from his seat and departed.

即办种种净美饮食，供养尊者阿难，食已，复为长者种种说法，示、教、照、喜已，从坐起而去。
