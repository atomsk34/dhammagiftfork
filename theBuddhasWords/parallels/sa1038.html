SĀ 1038

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1038（一〇三八） 摩那提那

Parallel to: SN 47.30

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying at the Jētavana Monastery in Sàvatthi.

一时，佛住瞻婆国竭伽池侧。时，有摩那提那长者疾病新差。

At that time, the elder Manatittha said to a man, "Good man! Go to Venerable Anuruddha and pay homage at his feet on my behalf. Inquire about his well-being, ease, and comfortable dwelling. Tomorrow, please accept my invitation for four people. If he accepts, please tell him on my behalf: 'I am a layperson with many affairs of the royal court. I am unable to go personally to welcome you. I only hope that the Venerable will come to my invitation at the appointed time, together with four people, out of compassion!'"

时，摩那提长者语一士夫言：「善男子！汝往尊者阿那律所，为我稽首阿那律足，问讯起居轻利、安乐住不？明日通身四人愿受我请。若受请者，汝复为我白言：『我俗人多有王家事，不能得自往奉迎，唯愿尊者时到，通身四人来赴我请，哀愍故！』」

At that time, the man accepted the elder's instructions, went to Venerable Anuruddha, paid homage at his feet, and said to the Venerable, "The elder Manatittha pays homage and inquires: 'Are you free from illness and affliction, and dwelling in ease and comfort? I only hope that the Venerable, together with four people, will accept my invitation at noon tomorrow out of compassion!'" At that time, Venerable Anuruddha silently accepted the invitation.

时，彼男子受长者教，诣尊者阿那律所，稽首礼足。白言尊者：「摩那提那长者敬礼问讯：『少病少恼、起居轻利、安乐住不？唯愿尊者通身四人明日日中，哀受我请。』」时，尊者阿那律默然受请。

At that time, the man again conveyed the elder Manatittha's words to Venerable Anuruddha, saying, "I am a layperson with many affairs of the royal court. I am unable to go personally to welcome you. I only hope that the Venerable, together with four people, will come to my invitation at the appointed time out of compassion!"

时，彼士夫复以摩那提那长者语白尊者阿那律：「我是俗人，多有王家事，不得躬自奉迎，唯愿尊者通身四人明日日中，哀受我请，怜愍故！」

Venerable Anuruddha said, "You may rest at ease. I will know the time myself. Tomorrow, I will go to his house together with four people."

尊者阿那律陀言：「汝且自安！我自知时，明日通身四人往诣其舍。」

At that time, the man received Venerable Anuruddha's instructions and returned to inform the elder, saying, "Elder! You should know that I have already gone to Venerable Anuruddha and conveyed your intentions. Venerable Anuruddha said, 'You may rest at ease. I will know the time myself.'"

时，彼士夫受尊者阿那律教，还白长者：「阿梨！当知我已诣尊者阿那律，具宣尊意。尊者阿那律言：『汝且自安，我自知时。』」

The elder Manatīna prepared pure and delicious food and drink that night. In the morning, he again told the man, "Go to Venerable Anuruddha and say, 'The time has come.'"

彼长者摩那提那夜办净美饮食。晨朝复告彼士夫：「汝往至彼尊者阿那律所，白言：『时到。』」

At that time, the man received the instructions and went to Venerable Anuruddha. He bowed his head at Anuruddha's feet and said, "The offerings are ready. Please know the time!"

时，彼士夫即受教，行诣尊者阿那律所，稽首礼足，白言：「供具已办，唯愿知时！」

At that time, Venerable Anuruddha put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and together with four others went to the elder's house.

时，尊者阿那律著衣持钵，通身四人诣长者舍。

At that time, the elder Manatittha was surrounded by his concubines, standing to the left of the inner gate. Seeing Venerable Anuruddha, he raised his body, held his feet in respect, and bowed. He led him in and sat down, each bowing separately, inquiring about his well-being, and then sitting to one side.

时，摩那提那长者婇女围遶，住内门左，见尊者阿那律，举体执足敬礼，引入就坐，各别稽首，问讯起居，退坐一面。

Venerable Anuruddha inquired of the elder, "Are you able to endure and dwell in peace and happiness?"

尊者阿那律问讯长者：「堪忍安乐住不？」

The elder replied, "Yes, Venerable One! I am able to endure and dwell in happiness. I had a disease before, which was very serious, but now I have recovered."

长者答言：「如是，尊者！堪忍乐住。先遭疾病，当时委笃，今已蒙差。」

Venerable Anuruddha asked the elder, "What kind of dwelling did you stay in that allowed your illness and suffering to be cured?"

尊者阿那律问长者言：「汝住何住，能令疾病苦患时得除差？」

The elder said, "Venerable Anuruddha! I dwell in the four foundations of mindfulness, diligently cultivating mindfulness. Therefore, the bodily afflictions and suffering are temporarily relieved. What are the four? They are: dwelling in mindfulness of the body observing the internal body, diligently applying right mindfulness and right knowledge to subdue worldly greed and sorrow. The external body, the internal and external body, internal feelings, external feelings, internal and external feelings, internal mind, external mind, internal and external mind, internal phenomena, external phenomena, internal and external phenomena, dwelling in mindfulness of phenomena observing the internal phenomena, diligently applying right mindfulness and right knowledge to subdue worldly greed and sorrow. Thus, Venerable Anuruddha! Because I dwell in the four foundations of mindfulness, the bodily afflictions and suffering are temporarily relieved. Venerable Anuruddha, because of dwelling in this way, the bodily afflictions and suffering are temporarily relieved."

长者白言：「尊者阿那律！我住四念处，专修系念故，身诸苦患时得休息。何等为四？谓内身身观念住，精勤方便，正念正智，调伏世间贪忧。外身、内外身，内受、外受、内外受，内心、外心、内外心，内法、外法、内外法法观念住，精勤方便，正念正智，调伏世间贪忧。如是，尊者阿那律！我于四念处系心住故，身诸苦患时得休息，尊者阿那律，住故，身诸苦患时得休息。」

Venerable Anuruddha told the elder, "You have now attained the fruit of non-returner."

尊者阿那律告长者言：「汝今自记阿那含果。」

At that time, the elder Manatittha offered various pure and delicious foods with his own hands, satisfying him until he was full. After eating and washing up, the elder Manatittha sat on a low seat again to listen to the Dharma. Venerable Anuruddha taught him the Dharma in various ways, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting him. Then he rose from his seat and left.

时，摩那提那长者以种种净美饮食自手供养，自恣饱满。食已，澡漱毕，摩那提那长者复坐卑床，听说法。尊者阿那律种种说法，示、教、照、喜已，从坐起去。
