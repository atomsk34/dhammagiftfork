SĀ 1039

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1039（一〇三九） 淳 陀

Parallel to: AN 10.176

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Thus have I heard:

一时，佛住王舍城金师精舍。时，有淳陀长者来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面。

At that time, the World-Honored One asked the elder Cunda, “What kind of śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa’s pure practice do you now delight in?”

尔时，世尊问淳陀长者：「汝今爱乐何等沙门、婆罗门净行？」

Cunda said to the Buddha, “There are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who serve water, serve the god Viṣṇu, hold staffs and water pots, and always wash their hands. Such upright ones can speak the Dharma well, saying, ‘Good man! On the fifteenth day of the month, wash your hair with sesame powder and āmalaka powder, practice the fasting precepts, wear a new, clean, long, white cotton robe, and lie down on cow dung smeared on the ground. Good man! Get up early in the morning, touch the ground with your hand, and say, “This ground is pure, and I am pure like this.” Hold a ball of cow dung and fresh grass in your hand, and say, “This is pure, and I am pure like this.” If it is like this, it is seen as pure, and if it is not like this, it is never pure.’ World-Honored One! I revere such śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas as being pure.

淳陀白佛：「有沙门、婆罗门，奉事于水，事毘湿波天，执杖澡罐，常净其手。如是正士能善说法言：『善男子！月十五日，以胡麻屑、庵摩罗屑以澡其发，修行斋法，被著新净长发白［叠＊毛］，牛粪涂地而卧其上。善男子！晨朝早起，以手触地，作如是言：「此地清净，我如是净。」手执牛粪团并把生草，口说是言：「此是清净，我如是净。」若如是者，见为清净，不如是者，永不清净。』世尊！如是像类沙门、婆罗门，若为清净，我所宗仰。」

The Buddha told Cunda, “There are black dharmas and black retribution, impure and impure fruits, heavy burdens going downward. Those who accomplish such evil dharmas, even if they get up early in the morning, touch the ground with their hands, and proclaim purity, are still impure; if they do not touch it, they are also impure. Holding a ball of cow dung and fresh grass, proclaiming purity, is also impure; if they do not touch it, it is also impure.

佛告淳陀：「有黑法、黑报，不净、不净果，负重向下。成就如此诸恶法者，虽复晨朝早起，以手触地，唱言清净，犹是不净；正复不触，亦不清净。执牛粪团，并及生草，唱言清净，亦复不净；正复不触，亦不清净。

Cunda, what are the black dharmas and black retribution, the impure and impure fruits, the heavy burdens going downward, up to touching and not touching, all being impure? Cunda, it means the evil karma of killing, with hands always bloody and smelly, always thinking of beating and killing, without shame or remorse, stingy and greedy, not giving up killing towards all sentient beings, down to insects. Stealing others' wealth, villages, and empty lands, not giving up stealing. Practicing wrong sexual conduct, with parents, brothers, sisters, husbands, relatives, down to those who give flower garlands, not giving up wrong sexual conduct by forcefully violating such protected ones. False speech, either in the royal court, in places where truthful speech is valued, or in large gatherings seeking to speak, making untrue statements, saying one has seen what one has not seen, saying one has not seen what one has seen, saying one has heard what one has not heard, saying one has not heard what one has heard, saying one knows what one does not know, saying one does not know what one knows, either for oneself or for others, or for the sake of wealth, knowing and speaking falsely, not giving up, this is called false speech. Divisive speech, spreading this to that, spreading that to this, completely destroying them, separating those who are united, delighting those who are separated, this is called divisive speech. Not giving up harsh speech and scolding, if someone speaks softly, pleasing to the ears and mind, straightforward and easy to understand, pleasant to hear and without attachment, loved by many, agreeable and in accord with samādhi, abandoning such, speaking harshly, disliked by many, unloved, disagreeable, and not in accord with samādhi. Such words, not giving up harshness, is called evil speech. Frivolous speech, untimely speech, untrue speech, meaningless speech, unrighteous speech, thoughtless speech, such are called frivolous speech.

「淳陀！何等为黑、黑报，不净、不净果，负重向下，乃至触以不触，悉皆不净？淳陀！谓杀生恶业，手常血腥，心常思惟挝捶杀害，无惭无愧，悭贪悋惜，于一切众生乃至昆虫，不离于杀。于他财物、聚落、空地，皆不离盗。行诸邪婬，若父母、兄弟、姊妹、夫主、亲族，乃至授花鬘者，如是等护，以力强干，不离邪婬。不实妄语，或于王家、真实言家、多众聚集，求当言处，作不实说，不见言见，见言不见，不闻言闻，闻言不闻，知言不知，不知言知，因自因他，或因财利，知而妄语，而不舍离，是名妄语。两舌乖离，传此向彼，传彼向此，遍相破坏，令和合者离，离者欢喜，是名两舌。不离恶口骂，若人软语说，悦耳心喜，方正易知，乐闻无依说，多人爱念，适意、随顺三昧。舍如是等，而作刚强，多人所恶，不爱、不适意、不顺三昧说。如是等言，不离麁澁，是名恶心。绮饰坏语，不时言、不实言、无义言、非法言、不思言，如是等，名坏语。

Not giving up greed, being greedy for others' wealth, thinking, 'If I had this wealth, it would be good.' Not giving up anger and evil, thinking, 'Those sentient beings should be bound, beaten, subdued, and killed.' Wishing to cause birth difficulties. Not giving up wrong views and inverted views, having such views and saying, 'There is no giving, no retribution, no merit, no good or evil actions, no results of good or evil karma, no this world, no other world, no parents, no sentient beings born in the world, no world, no arhats, no paths, no directions, no this world or other world, no self-realization by oneself that "my birth is ended, the holy life is established, what was to be done is done, I know no more of this state of existence after this".' Cunda, this is called black, with black retribution, impure, with impure fruits, up to touching and not touching, all being impure.

「不舍离贪，于他财物而起贪欲，言：『此物我有者好。』不舍瞋恚弊恶，心思惟言：『彼众生应缚、应鞭、应伏、应杀。』欲为生难。不舍邪见颠倒，如是见、如是说：『无施、无报、无福，无善行恶行、无善恶业果报，无此世、无他世，无父母、无众生生世间，无世阿罗汉等趣等向此世他世自知作证：「我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。」』淳陀！是名黑、黑报，不净、不净果，乃至触以不触，皆悉不净。

Cunda, there is the white, with white retribution, the pure, with pure fruits, the light and immortal ascending, having accomplished it, touching the ground in the morning, this purity and my purity, also attain purity; if not touching, also attain purity, holding a cow dung ball, holding fresh grass, the pure cause and pure fruit, holding and not holding, also attain purity.

「淳陀！有白、白报，净有、净果，轻仙上升，成就已，晨朝触地，此净我净者，亦得清净；若不触者，亦得清净，把牛粪团，手执生草，净因净果者，执与不执，亦得清净。

Cunda, what is the white, with white retribution, up to holding and not holding, also attaining purity? It means that a person does not kill living beings, abstains from killing living beings, abandons weapons, feels shame, and has compassion for all sentient beings. Not stealing, being far from stealing, taking what is given, not taking what is not given, with a pure mind, not being greedy. Abstaining from sexual misconduct, whether protected by parents, up to those who give a single flower garland, all do not forcibly engage in sexual misconduct. Abstaining from false speech, speaking truthfully and reliably, being far from divisive speech, not transmitting this to that, transmitting that to this, causing mutual destruction, reconciling those who are divided, rejoicing in those who are united, being far from harsh speech, not being rough, many people delight in what is said. Abstaining from meaningless speech, speaking truthfully, speaking at the right time, speaking the truth, speaking the meaning, speaking the Dharma, speaking the view. Abstaining from greed, not regarding others' wealth and possessions as one's own, and giving rise to attachment, being far from anger, not thinking: 'Strike, bind, kill, and cause difficulties.' Having right view, not having inverted views, believing in giving, speaking, retribution, and merit, believing in the results of good and evil actions, believing in this world, believing in parents, believing in sentient beings being born, believing in the world, believing in arhats, believing in this world and the other world, believing in self-realization by oneself in this world and the other world that 'my birth is ended, the holy life is established, what was to be done is done, I know no more of this state of existence after this.' Cunda, this is called white, with white retribution, up to touching and not touching, all being pure.

「淳陀！何等为白、白报，乃至执以不执，亦得清净？谓有人不杀生，离杀生，舍刀杖，惭愧，悲念一切众生。不偷盗，远离偷盗，与者取，不与不取，净心不贪。离于邪婬，若父母护，乃至授一花鬘者，悉不强干起于邪婬，离于妄语，审谛实说，远离两舌，不传此向彼，传彼向此，共相破坏，离者令和，和者随喜，远离恶口，不刚强，多人乐其所说。离于坏语，谛说、时说、实说、义说、法说、见说。离于贪欲，不于他财、他众具作己有想，而生贪著，离于瞋恚，不作是念：『挝打缚杀，为作众难。』正见成就，不颠倒见，有施、有说报、有福，有善恶行果报，有此世，有父母、有众生生，有世阿罗汉于此世他世现法自知作证：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』淳陀！是名白、白报，乃至触与不触，皆悉清净。」

At that time, the elder Cunda, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully rejoiced, paid homage, and departed.

尔时，淳陀长者闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
