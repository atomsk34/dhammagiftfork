SĀ 104

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 104（一〇四） 焰

Parallel to: SN 22.85

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time there was a bhikṣu named Yamaka who gave rise to wrong views and said, "According to my understanding of the Dharma taught by the Buddha, when an arhat with exhausted taints perishes and their life ends, there is nothing more."

尔时，有比丘名焰摩迦，起恶邪见，作如是言：「如我解佛所说法，漏尽阿罗汉身坏命终更无所有。」

At that time, many bhikṣus heard what he said and went to him, saying to the bhikṣu Yamaka, "Did you really say, 'According to my understanding of the Dharma taught by the Buddha, when an arhat with exhausted taints perishes and their life ends, there is nothing more'?"

时，有众多比丘闻彼所说，往诣其所，语焰摩迦比丘言：「汝实作是说：『如我解佛所说法，漏尽阿罗汉身坏命终更无所有』耶？」

He replied, "Indeed, Venerables!"

答言：「实尔。诸尊！」

At that time, the bhikṣus said to Yamaka, "Do not slander the World-Honored One! Slandering the World-Honored One is not good. The World-Honored One did not say this. You should completely abandon this evil and wrong view."

时，诸比丘语焰摩迦：「勿谤世尊！谤世尊者不善，世尊不作是说，汝当尽舍此恶邪见。」

When the bhikṣus spoke these words, the bhikṣu Yamaka still held onto his evil and wrong views, saying, "Venerables! Only this is true, anything else is false." He said this three times.

诸比丘说此语时，焰摩迦比丘犹执恶邪见，作如是言：「诸尊！唯此真实，异则虚妄。」如是三说。

At that time, the bhikṣus were unable to subdue the bhikṣu Yamaka. They then left and went to Venerable Śāriputra, saying to Venerable Śāriputra, "Venerable One! You should know that the bhikṣu Yamaka has given rise to such evil and wrong views, saying, 'I understand and know the Dharma taught by the Buddha. An arhat whose defilements are exhausted, upon the destruction of the body and the end of life, has nothing further.' Having heard what he said, we went to ask the bhikṣu Yamaka, 'Do you really have such knowledge and views?' He replied to us, 'Venerables! It is indeed so. Anything else is foolish talk.' We then said to him, 'Do not slander the World-Honored One! The World-Honored One did not say this. You should abandon this evil and wrong view.' We admonished him like this three times, but he still did not abandon his evil and wrong views. That is why we now come to you, Venerable One. We only hope that the Venerable One will cause the bhikṣu Yamaka to cease his evil and wrong views, out of compassion for him! "

时，诸比丘不能调伏焰摩迦比丘，即便舍去，往诣尊者舍利弗所，语尊者舍利弗言：「尊者！当知彼焰摩迦比丘起如是恶邪见言：『我解知佛所说法，漏尽阿罗汉身坏命终更无所有。』我等闻彼所说已，故往问焰摩迦比丘：『汝实作如是知见耶？』彼答我言：『诸尊！实尔，异则愚说。』我即语言：『汝勿谤世尊！世尊不作此语，汝当舍此恶邪见。』再三谏彼，犹不舍恶邪见，是故我今诣尊者所，唯愿尊者，当令焰摩迦比丘息恶邪见，怜愍彼故！」

Śāriputra said, "So be it. I shall cause him to cease his evil and wrong views."

舍利弗言：「如是，我当令彼息恶邪见。」

At that time, the many bhikṣus, having heard Śāriputra's words, rejoiced and followed his words, returning to their original places.

时，众多比丘闻舍利弗语，欢喜随喜，而还本处。

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra, in the morning, put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and entered Śrāvastī City to beg for food. After eating, he left the city and returned to the monastery. Having put away his robe and bowl, he went to the place of Bhikṣu Yajñadatta. When Bhikṣu Yajñadatta saw Venerable Śāriputra coming from afar, he immediately prepared a seat, washed his feet, placed a footstool, welcomed him, took his robe and bowl, and invited him to sit down.

尔时，尊者舍利弗晨朝著衣持钵，入舍卫城乞食。食已，出城，还精舍举衣钵已，往诣焰摩迦比丘所。时，焰摩迦比丘遥见尊者舍利弗来，即为敷座洗足，安停脚机奉迎，为执衣钵，请令就座。

Venerable Śāriputra sat down and washed his feet. He then said to Bhikṣu Yajñadatta, "Did you really say, 'I understand the Dharma taught by the World-Honored One. An arhat whose defilements are exhausted and whose body and life end has nothing'?"

尊者舍利弗就座、洗足已，语焰摩迦比丘：「汝实作如是语：『我解知世尊所说法，漏尽阿罗汉身坏命终无所有』耶？」

Bhikṣu Yajñadatta said to Śāriputra, "It is true, Venerable Śāriputra!"

焰摩迦比丘白舍利弗言：「实尔，尊者舍利弗！」

Śāriputra said, "I will ask you now. Answer me as you wish. Yajñadatta, is form permanent or impermanent?"

舍利弗言：「我今问汝，随意答我。云何，焰摩迦！色为常耶？为非常耶？」

He replied, "Venerable Śāriputra, it is impermanent."

答言：「尊者舍利弗！无常。」

He further asked, "If it is impermanent, is it suffering?"

复问：「若无常者，是苦不？」

He replied, "It is suffering."

答言：「是苦。」

He further asked, "If it is impermanent and suffering, a changeable and unstable phenomenon, would a learned noble disciple see self, other, or characteristics in it?"

复问：「若无常、苦，是变易法，多闻圣弟子宁于中见我、异我、相在不？」

He replied, "No, Venerable Sariputra!"

答言：「不也，尊者舍利弗！」

The same applies to feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness." He further asked, "Is form the Tathāgata?

「受、想、行、识亦复如是。」复问：「云何，焰摩迦！色是如来耶？」

He replied, "No, Venerable Sariputra!"

答言：「不也，尊者舍利弗！」

Is feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness the Tathāgata?

「受、想、行、识是如来耶？」

He replied, "No, Venerable Sariputra!"

答言：「不也，尊者舍利弗！」

He further asked, "Then, Yamariga, is the Tathāgata different from form? Is the Tathāgata different from feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness?"

复问：「云何，焰摩迦！异色有如来耶？异受、想、行、识有如来耶？」

He replied, "No, Venerable Sariputra!"

答言：「不也，尊者舍利弗！」

He further asked, "Is the Tathāgata in form? Is the Tathāgata in feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness?"

复问：「色中有如来耶？受、想、行、识中有如来耶？」

He replied, "No, Venerable Sariputra!"

答言：「不也，尊者舍利弗！」

He further asked, "Is form in the Tathāgata? Are feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness in the Tathāgata?"

复问：「如来中有色耶？如来中有受、想、行、识耶？」

He replied, "No, Venerable Sariputra!"

答言：「不也，尊者舍利弗！」

He further asked, "Is the Tathāgata apart from form, feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness?"

复问：「非色、受、想、行、识有如来耶？」

He replied, "No, Venerable Sariputra!"

答言：「不也，尊者舍利弗！」

"So it is, Yamariga! The Tathāgata sees the truth of the Dharma as it is, abides in it, without attainment, without establishment. How can you say, 'I understand the World-Honored One's teaching that the body and life of an arhat who has exhausted defilements perishes and ceases to exist'? Is this the right time to say this?"

「如是，焰摩迦！如来见法真实、如住，无所得、无所施设，汝云何言：『我解知世尊所说，漏尽阿罗汉身坏命终无所有。』为时说耶？」

He replied, "No, Venerable Sariputra!"

答言：「不也，尊者舍利弗！」

He asked again, "Yamariga! You said earlier, 'I understand the World-Honored One's teaching that the body and life of an arhat who has exhausted defilements perishes and ceases to exist.' Why do you now say it is not so?"

复问：「焰摩迦！先言：『我解知世尊所说，漏尽阿罗汉身坏命终无所有。』云何今复言非耶？」

The bhiksu Yamariga said, "Venerable Sariputra! Because of my previous ignorance and delusion, I made such evil and wrong views. Having heard the Venerable Sariputra's explanation, all my ignorance and delusion are now completely severed."

焰摩迦比丘言：「尊者舍利弗！我先不解、无明故，作如是恶邪见说，闻尊者舍利弗说已，不解、无明，一切悉断。」

He asked again, "Yamariga! If asked again, 'Bhiksu! As you previously said in your evil and wrong views, what do you now know and see to have completely abandoned?' How would you answer?"

复问：「焰摩迦！若复问：『比丘！如先恶邪见所说，今何所知见一切悉得远离？』汝当云何答？」

Yamariga replied, "Venerable Sariputra! If someone were to ask me, I would answer like this: 'The arhat who has exhausted the taints knows that form is impermanent. What is impermanent is suffering. Suffering is tranquil, cool, and eternally extinguished. Feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness are also like this.' If someone were to ask me, I would answer like this."

焰摩迦答言：「尊者舍利弗！若有来问者，我当如是答：『漏尽阿罗汉色无常，无常者是苦，苦者寂静、清凉、永没。受、想、行、识亦复如是。』有来问者，作如是答。」

Sariputra said, "Excellent! Excellent! Bhiksu Yamariga! You should answer like this. Why is that? The arhat who has exhausted the taints knows that form is impermanent. What is impermanent is suffering. If it is impermanent and suffering, it is a dharma of arising and ceasing. Feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness are also like this."

舍利弗言：「善哉！善哉！焰摩迦比丘！汝应如是答。所以者何？漏尽阿罗汉色无常，无常者是苦，若无常、苦者，是生灭法。受、想、行、识亦复如是。」

When the Venerable Sariputra spoke this Dharma, the bhiksu Yamariga was far from defilements and impurities, and attained the purity of the Dharma eye.

尊者舍利弗说是法时，焰摩迦比丘远尘离垢，得法眼净。

The Venerable Sariputra said to the bhiksu Yamariga, "Now I will speak a parable. The wise understand through parables. It is like a rich man's son who has much wealth and widely seeks servants. He guards his wealth well. Then an enemy, a wicked person, deceitfully comes to befriend him and becomes his servant. He always watches for an opportunity. He sleeps late and gets up early, attending to his master's needs. He is respectful in his actions and humble in his words, making the master feel pleased. He thinks of him as a friend and a son, trusting him completely without any doubt. He does not guard himself. Then, holding a sharp knife in his hand, he cuts off the master's life. Yamariga, what do you think? Is that wicked enemy a friend of the rich man? Is it not the initial expedient means? The intention to harm is always watching for an opportunity until the end. Yet the rich man is unable to be aware of it and now suffers harm."

尊者舍利弗语焰摩迦比丘：「今当说譬，夫智者以譬得解。如长者子，长者子大富多财，广求仆从，善守护财物。时，有怨家恶人，诈来亲附，为作仆从，常伺其便，晚眠早起，侍息左右，谨敬其事，逊其言辞，令主意悦，作亲友想、子想，极信不疑，不自防护，然后手执利刀，以断其命。焰摩迦比丘！于意云何？彼恶怨家，为长者亲友，非为初始方便，害心常伺其便，至其终耶？而彼长者，不能觉知，至今受害。」

He replied, "Indeed, Venerable!"

答言：「实尔。尊者！」

Sariputra said to the bhiksu Yamariga, "What do you think? If the rich man had known that the person was deceitfully befriending him with the intention to harm him, would he have been able to protect himself well and not be harmed?"

舍利弗语焰摩迦比丘：「于意云何？彼长者本知彼人诈亲欲害，善自防护，不受害耶？」

He replied, "Indeed, Venerable Sariputra!"

答言：「如是，尊者舍利弗！」

"So it is, Yamariga Bhiksu! The ignorant and unlearned common person has the notion of permanence, peace, health, self, and possession towards the five aggregates of grasping, and guards and cherishes them. They will eventually be harmed by the enemies of the five aggregates. It is like how the rich man was harmed by the deceitful enemy without realizing it. Yamariga! The learned noble disciple observes the five aggregates of grasping as being like a disease, a boil, a thorn, and a killer, impermanent, suffering, empty, selfless, and not belonging to a self. They are not attached to the five aggregates of grasping, do not grasp, and because of not grasping, they are not attached. Because of not being attached, they realize nirvana for themselves: 'My birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and I know that I will not be subject to any future lives.'"

「如是，焰摩迦比丘！愚痴无闻凡夫于五受阴作常想、安隐想、不病想、我想、我所想，于此五受阴保持护惜，终为此五受阴怨家所害。如彼长者，为诈亲怨家所害而不觉知。焰摩迦！多闻圣弟子于此五受阴，观察如病、如痈、如刺、如杀，无常、苦、空、非我、非我所，于此五受阴不著、不受、不受故不著，不著故自觉涅槃：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』」

When Venerable Sariputra spoke this Dharma, Yamariga Bhiksu became free from defilements, his mind was liberated. After Venerable Sariputra had spoken Dharma to Yamariga Bhiksu, showing, teaching, illuminating, and gladdening him, he rose from his seat and left.

尊者舍利弗说是法时，焰摩迦比丘不起诸漏，心得解脱，尊者舍利弗为焰摩迦比丘说法，示、教、照、喜已，从座起去。
