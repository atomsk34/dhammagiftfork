SĀ 1040

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1040（一〇四〇） 舍行

Parallel to: AN 10.119 (~) / AN 10.167

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the goldsmith’s dwelling in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城金师精舍。

At that time, there was a certain brāhman who, on the fifteenth day, had washed his head, observed the precepts, put on a new long white woolen robe, held fresh grass in his hand, and came to where the Buddha was. He exchanged greetings and polite conversation with the World-Honored One, and then sat to one side.

时，有异婆罗门于十五日，洗头已，受斋法，被新长发白［叠＊毛］，手执生草，来诣佛所，与世尊面相问讯慰劳已，退坐一面。

At that time, the Buddha said to the brāhman, "You have washed your head and put on a new long white woolen robe. Whose family custom is this?"

尔时，佛告婆罗门：「汝洗头被新长发白［叠＊毛］，是谁家法？」

The brāhman said to the Buddha, "Gautama! This is the practice of abandoning."

婆罗门白佛：「瞿昙！是学舍法。」

The Buddha said to the brāhman, "What is the brāhman's practice of abandoning?"

佛告婆罗门：「云何婆罗门舍法？」

The brāhman said to the Buddha, "Gautama! On the fifteenth day, washing the head and observing the Dharma fast, wearing new and clean long hair and white cloth, holding fresh grass in hand, giving according to one's ability, and making merit. Gautama! This is called the practice of abandoning by the brāhman."

婆罗门白佛言：「瞿昙！如是十五日，洗头受持法斋，著新净长发白［叠＊毛］，手执生草，随力所能，布施作福。瞿昙！是名婆罗门修行舍行。」

The Buddha said to the brāhman, "The practice of abandoning in the noble Dharma and Vinaya is different from this."

佛告婆罗门：「贤圣法、律所行舍行异于此也。」

The brāhman said to the Buddha, "Gautama! What is the practice of abandoning in the noble Dharma and Vinaya?"

婆罗门白佛：「瞿昙！云何为贤圣法、律所行舍行？」

The Buddha said to the brāhman, "It means abandoning killing, not delighting in killing..." and so on, as extensively explained in the section on purity. "Based on not killing, abandoning killing..." and so on, as extensively explained in the section on purity. "Abandoning stealing, not delighting in stealing, based on not stealing, abandoning taking what is not given. Abandoning sexual misconduct, not delighting in sexual misconduct, based on not engaging in sexual misconduct, abandoning non-celibacy. Abandoning false speech, not delighting in false speech, based on not speaking falsely, abandoning untrue speech. Abandoning divisive speech, not delighting in divisive speech, based on not engaging in divisive speech, abandoning divisive actions. Abandoning harsh speech, not delighting in harsh speech, based on not speaking harshly, abandoning coarse speech. Abandoning meaningless speech, not delighting in meaningless speech, based on not engaging in meaningless speech, abandoning meaningless speech. Cutting off greed and desire, being far removed from suffering and greed, based on a non-greedy mind, abandoning attachment. Cutting off anger and resentment, not giving rise to anger and resentment, based on non-anger, abandoning anger and resentment. Cultivating right view, not giving rise to inverted views, based on right view, abandoning wrong views. Brāhman! This is called the practice of abandoning in the noble Dharma and Vinaya."

佛告婆罗门：「谓离杀生，不乐杀生……」如前清净分广说。「依于不杀，舍离杀生……」乃至如前清净分广说。「离偷盗，不乐于盗，依于不盗，舍不与取。离诸邪婬，不乐邪婬，依于不婬，舍非梵行。离于妄语，不乐妄语，依不妄语，舍不实言。离诸两舌，不乐两舌，依不两舌，舍别离行。离于恶口，不乐恶口，依不恶口，舍于麁言。离诸绮语，不乐绮语，依不绮语，舍无义言。断除贪欲，远离苦贪，依无贪心，舍于爱著。断除瞋恚，不生忿恨，依于无恚，舍彼瞋恨。修习正见，不起颠倒，依于正见，舍彼邪见。婆罗门！是名贤圣法、律所行舍行。」

The brāhman said to the Buddha, "Excellent! The practice of abandoning in the noble Dharma and Vinaya of the virtuous Gautama."

婆罗门白佛：「善哉！瞿昙贤圣法、律所行舍行。」

At that time, the brāhman, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully rejoiced and rose from his seat to depart.

时，婆罗门闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，从坐起去。
