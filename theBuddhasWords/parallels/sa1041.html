SĀ 1041

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1041（一〇四一） 生闻

Parallel to: AN 10.177

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, the Brāhman Sage Jīvaka came to where the Buddha was, exchanged greetings with the World-Honored One, and sat to one side. He said to the Buddha, "Gautama! I have relatives whom I love very much. They have suddenly passed away. I make offerings out of faith for their sake. World-Honored One, will they be able to receive them?"

时，有生闻梵志来诣佛所，与世尊面相问讯慰劳已，退坐一面，白佛言：「瞿昙！我有亲族，极所爱念，忽然命终，我为彼故，信心布施。云何？世尊！彼得受不？」

The Buddha told the Brāhman, "It is not always received. If your relatives are reborn in hell, they can receive the food of the hell beings to sustain their lives, but they cannot receive the food and drink you offer in faith. If they are reborn as animals, hungry ghosts, or humans, they can receive the food and drink of those humans, but not what you offer. Brāhman! There is a place in the realm of hungry ghosts called the Entering Place Hungry Ghosts. If your relatives are reborn in that Entering Place Hungry Ghosts, they can receive your offerings.

佛告婆罗门：「非一向得。若汝亲族生地狱中者，得彼地狱众生食，以活其命，不得汝所信施饮食；若生畜生、饿鬼、人中者，得彼人中饮食，不得汝所施者。婆罗门！饿鬼趣中有一处，名为入处饿鬼，若汝亲族生彼入处饿鬼中者，得汝施食。」

The Brāhman said to the Buddha, "If my relatives are not reborn in the Entering Place Hungry Ghosts, who should eat my offerings in faith?"

婆罗门白佛：「若我亲族不生入处饿鬼趣中者，我信施，谁应食之？」

The Buddha told the Brāhman, "If your relatives for whom you make offerings in faith are not reborn in the Entering Place Hungry Ghosts, there must be other relatives and acquaintances reborn in the Entering Place Hungry Ghosts who can eat them."

佛告婆罗门：「若汝所可为信施亲族不生入处饿鬼趣者，要有余亲族知识生入处饿鬼趣中者，得食之。」

The Brāhman said to the Buddha, "Gautama! If my relatives for whom I make offerings in faith are not reborn in the Entering Place Hungry Ghosts, and there are no other relatives and acquaintances reborn in the Entering Place Hungry Ghosts, who will eat these offerings in faith?"

婆罗门白佛：「瞿昙！若我所为信施亲族不生入处饿鬼趣中，亦无更余亲族知识生入处饿鬼趣者，此信施食，谁当食之？」

The Buddha told the Brāhman, "Even if the relatives and acquaintances you make offerings to are not reborn in the Entering Place Hungry Ghosts, and there are no other acquaintances reborn as hungry ghosts, you will still receive the merit of your offerings in faith. The offerings you make in faith will not be wasted."

佛告婆罗门：「设使所为施亲族知识不生入处饿鬼趣中，复无诸余知识生饿鬼者，且信施而自得其福，彼施者所作信施，而彼施者不失达嚫。」

The Brāhman said to the Buddha, "How does the giver of offerings receive the merit of their offerings?"

婆罗门白佛：「云何施者行施，施者得彼达嚫？」

The Buddha told the Brāhman, "There are people who kill and do evil, their hands are always bloody, up to the ten unwholesome paths of action..." as extensively explained in the Sūtra on Cunda, "...and yet they make offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, up to the poor and beggars, giving them money, clothing, food and drink, lamps, and various adornments. Brāhman! If that benefactor of offerings also breaks the precepts and is reborn as an elephant, because they once gave money, clothing, food and drink, up to various adornments, to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, even though they are in the form of an elephant, they will still receive the rewards of those offerings, clothing, food and drink, up to various adornments.

佛告婆罗门：「有人杀生行恶，手常血腥，乃至十不善业迹……」如淳陀修多罗广说，「而复施诸沙门、婆罗门，乃至贫穷、乞士，悉施钱财、衣被、饮食、灯明、诸庄严具。婆罗门！彼惠施主若复犯戒，生象中者，以彼曾施沙门、婆罗门钱财、衣被、饮食，乃至庄严众具故，虽在象中，亦得受彼施报，衣服、饮食，乃至种种庄严众具。

If they are reborn as various animals such as cows, horses, donkeys, mules, and so forth, due to the merit of their past giving, they will receive the rewards, according to the place where they are reborn, and will obtain them all. Brāhman! If the benefactor of offerings upholds the precepts, does not kill, does not steal, up to having right views, and makes offerings to śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, up to beggars, of money, clothing, food and drink, up to lamps, due to this merit, they will be reborn in the human realm and directly receive the rewards of clothing, food and drink, up to lamps and various necessities.

「若复生牛、马、驴、骡等种种畜生趣中，以本施惠功德，悉受其报，随彼生处所应受用，皆悉得之。婆罗门！若复施主持戒，不杀、不盗，乃至正见，布施诸沙门、婆罗门乃至乞士钱财、衣服、饮食，乃至灯明，缘斯功德，生人道中，坐受其报，衣被、饮食，乃至灯明众具。

Furthermore, Brāhman! If they uphold the precepts and are reborn in the heavens, they will receive the rewards of their past giving in the heavens, of wealth, treasures, clothing, food and drink, up to various adornments. Brāhman! This is called the giver giving, the giver receiving the rewards, and the fruit of the retribution not being lost.

「复次，婆罗门！若复持戒生天上者，彼诸惠施天上受报，财宝、衣服、饮食，乃至庄严众具。婆罗门！是名施者行施，施者受达嚫，果报不失。」

At that time, the Brāhman Śroṇakoṭīviṃśa heard what the Buddha said, and with joy and delight, rose from his seat and departed.

时，生闻婆罗门闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，从坐起去。
