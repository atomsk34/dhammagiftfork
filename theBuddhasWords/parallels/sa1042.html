SĀ 1042

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1042（一〇四二） 鞞闻摩

Parallel to: MN 41 / MN 42 / AN 2.6 (~) / SĀ 1043

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling in the country of Kosala, staying in the forest of Sineru to the north of the village of Vairambha.

一时，佛在拘萨罗国人间游行，住鞞罗磨聚落北身恕林中。

The brahmins and elders of Vairambha heard that the World-Honored One was staying in the forest of Sineru to the north of the village, and upon hearing this, they gathered together and went to where the Buddha was. They bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, withdrew and sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! What causes and conditions lead to sentient beings, after their bodies perish and lives end, being reborn in hell?"

鞞罗聚落婆罗门长者闻世尊住聚落北身恕林中，闻已，共相招集，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！何因、何缘有众生身坏命终，生地狱中？」

The Buddha told the brahmins and elders, "It is because of engaging in unwholesome practices and dangerous practices that, after the body perishes and life ends, one is reborn in hell." The brahmins and elders said to the Buddha, "What unwholesome practices and dangerous practices lead to being reborn in hell after the body perishes and life ends?"

佛告诸婆罗门长者：「行非法行、行危崄行因缘故，身坏命终，生地狱中。」诸婆罗门长者白佛：「行何等非法行、危崄行，身坏命终，生地狱中？」

The Buddha told the brahmins and elders, "It is because of killing, up to wrong views, and fully engaging in the ten unwholesome causes and conditions that, after the body perishes and life ends, one is reborn in hell. Brahmins! This is unwholesome practice and dangerous practice, leading to being reborn in hell after the body perishes and life ends."

佛告婆罗门长者：「杀生，乃至邪见，具足十不善业因缘故。婆罗门！是非法行、危崄行，身坏命终，生地狱中。」

The brahmins said to the Buddha, "What causes and conditions lead to sentient beings being reborn in heaven after the body perishes and life ends?"

婆罗门白佛：「何因缘诸众生身坏命终，得生天上？」

The Buddha told the brahmins and elders, "It is because of practicing the Dharma and practicing the right path that, after the body perishes and life ends, one is reborn in heaven."

佛告婆罗门长者：「行法行、行正行，以是因缘故，身坏命终，得生天上。」

They asked again, "World-Honored One! What kind of Dharma practice and what kind of right practice lead to being reborn in heaven after the body perishes and life ends?"

复问：「世尊！行何等法行、何等正行，身坏命终，得生天上？」

The Buddha told the brahmins and elders, "It is because of abandoning killing, up to right view, and the ten wholesome karmic paths that, after the body perishes and life ends, one is reborn in heaven. Brahmins and elders! If one practices this Dharma practice and this right practice, and wishes to be reborn in the great families of kṣatriyas, brahmins, or householders, one will be able to be reborn there. Why is that? It is because of the causes and conditions of Dharma practice and right practice.

佛告婆罗门长者：「谓离杀生，乃至正见，十善业迹因缘故，身坏命终，得生天上。婆罗门长者！若有行此法行、行此正行者，欲求刹利大性家、婆罗门大性家、居士大性家，悉得往生。所以者何？以法行、正行因缘故。

If one further wishes to be reborn in the Heaven of the Four Heavenly Kings, the Heaven of the Thirty-three Gods, up to the Heaven of the Delighting in Transformations, one will be able to be reborn there. Why is that? It is because of Dharma practice and right practice. Those who practice pure precepts will naturally attain whatever they wish in their minds.

「若复欲求生四王、三十三天，乃至他化自在天，悉得往生。所以者何？以法行、正行故，行净戒者，其心所愿，悉自然得。

If one further practices this Dharma practice and right practice, and wishes to be reborn in the Heaven of Brahmā, one will be able to be reborn there. Why is that? It is because of practicing right practice and Dharma practice. Those who uphold pure precepts, with minds free from desire, will definitely attain their wishes.

「若复如是法行、正行者，欲求生梵天，亦得往生。所以者何？以行正行、法行故，持戒清净，心离爱欲，所愿必得。

If one further wishes to be reborn in the Heaven of Sound of Light, the Heaven of Pure Abodes, up to the Heaven of No-more-striving, it is also like this. Why is that? It is because they uphold pure precepts and their minds are free from desire.

「若复欲求往生光音、遍净，乃至阿伽尼咤，亦复如是。所以者何？以彼持戒清净，心离欲故。

If one further wishes to be free from desire and evil and unwholesome dharmas, with initial and sustained thought, up to fully abiding in the fourth dhyāna, one will all attain accomplishment. Why is that? It is because of their Dharma practice and right practice. Those who uphold pure precepts, with minds free from desire, will definitely attain their wishes.

「若复欲求离欲、恶不善法，有觉有观，乃至第四禅具足住，悉得成就。所以者何？以彼法行、正行故，持戒清净，心离爱欲，所愿必得。

If one wishes to attain loving-kindness, compassion, joy, and equanimity, the sphere of infinite space, the sphere of infinite consciousness, the sphere of nothingness, and the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception, one will all attain them. Why is that? It is because of their Dharma practice and right practice. Those who uphold pure precepts, with minds free from desire, will definitely attain their wishes.

「欲求慈、悲、喜、舍，空入处、识入处、无所有入处、非想非非想入处皆悉得。所以者何？以法行、正行故，持戒清净，心离爱欲，所愿必得。

If one wishes to attain the severance of the three fetters, the fruits of stream-entry, once-returning, and non-returning, immeasurable supernormal powers, the divine ear, knowledge of others' minds, knowledge of past lives, knowledge of death and rebirth, and knowledge of the exhaustion of taints, one will all attain them. Why is that? It is because of their Dharma practice and right practice. Those who uphold pure precepts, with minds free from desire, will definitely attain their wishes.

「欲求断三结，得须陀洹、斯陀含、阿那含果，无量神通，天耳、他心智、宿命智、生死智、漏尽智皆悉得。所以者何？以法行、正行故，持戒、离欲，所愿必得。」

At that time, the brāhman householders, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully rejoiced, paid homage, and departed.

时，婆罗门长者闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
