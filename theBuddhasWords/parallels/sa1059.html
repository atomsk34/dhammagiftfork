SĀ 1059

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1059（一〇五九） 四十法

Parallel to: AN 10.224

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "There are forty dharmas that, when accomplished, are like an iron spear thrown into water. When the body perishes and life ends, one will be reborn in the evil realms of hell. What are the forty dharmas? They are: personally killing living beings, teaching others to kill, praising killing, rejoicing in seeing others kill, up to personally practicing wrong views, teaching others to practice, praising wrong views, and rejoicing in seeing others practice wrong views. These are called the forty dharmas that, when accomplished, are like an iron spear thrown into water. When the body perishes and life ends, one will be reborn in the evil realms of hell. There are forty dharmas that, when accomplished, are like an iron spear piercing the sky. When the body perishes and life ends, one will be reborn in the heavens. What are the forty? They are: not killing living beings, teaching others not to kill, always praising the merits of not killing, rejoicing in seeing those who do not kill; up to personally practicing right views, teaching others to practice, always praising the merits of right views, and rejoicing in seeing those who practice. These are called the forty dharmas that, when accomplished, are like an iron spear piercing the sky. When the body perishes and life ends, one will be reborn in the heavens."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「有四十法成就，如铁枪投水，身坏命终，下生恶趣泥犁中。何等为四十法？谓手自杀生，教人令杀，赞叹杀生，见人杀生心随欢喜，乃至自行邪见，教人令行，赞叹邪见，见行邪见心随欢喜，是名四十法成就。如铁枪投水，身坏命终，下生恶趣泥犁中。有四十法成就，如铁枪钻空，身坏命终，上生天上。何等为四十？谓不杀生，教人不杀，口常赞叹不杀功德，见不杀者心随欢喜；乃至自行正见，教人令行，亦常赞叹正见功德，见人行者心随欢喜，是名四十法成就。如铁枪钻空，身坏命终，上生天上。」

After the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus who heard what the Buddha taught rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
