SĀ 106

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 106（一〇六） 阿[少兔]罗

Parallel to: SN 22.86 / SN 44.2

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha. At that time, there was a bhikṣu named Ārāḍa Kālāma, staying on Vulture Peak.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。尔时，有比丘名阿［少／兔］罗度，住耆阇崛山。

At that time, many non-Buddhist renunciants went to where Ārāḍa Kālāma was, exchanged greetings, and after exchanging greetings, stood to one side and said to Ārāḍa Kālāma, "We wish to ask something. Is there time to explain it for us?"

时，有众多外道出家往诣阿［少／兔］罗度所，共相问讯。共相问讯已，于一面住，白阿［少／兔］罗度言：「欲有所问，宁有闲暇为解释不？」

Ārāḍa Kālāma said to the non-Buddhist renunciants, "Ask as you wish. I will answer what I know."

阿［少／兔］罗度语诸外道言：「随所欲问，知者当答。」

The non-Buddhist renunciants further asked, "Venerable one! After the Tathāgata's death, does he exist?"

诸外道复问：「云何？尊者！如来死后为有耶？」

Ārāḍa Kālāma said, "As the World-Honored One said, this is indeterminate."

阿［少／兔］罗度言：「如世尊说，此是无记。」

He further asked, "After the Tathāgata's death, does he not exist?"

又问：「如来死后为无耶？」

Ārāḍa Kālāma said, "As the World-Honored One said, this is also indeterminate."

阿［少／兔］罗度言：「如世尊说，此亦无记。」

He further asked, "After the Tathāgata's death, does he exist or not exist? Does he neither exist nor not exist?"

又问：「如来死后有无耶？非有非无耶？」

Ārāḍa Kālāma said, "As the World-Honored One said, this is also indeterminate."

阿［少／兔］罗度言：「如世尊说，此亦无记。」

He further asked Ārāḍa Kālāma, "How is it, Venerable One? Does the Tathāgata exist after death? You say it is indeterminate. Does he not exist after death? You say it is indeterminate. Does he both exist and not exist after death? You say it is indeterminate. Does he neither exist nor not exist after death? You say it is indeterminate. How is it, Venerable One? Is Śramaṇa Gautama ignorant and does he not see?"

复问阿［少／兔］罗度言：「云何？尊者！如来死后有耶？说言无记。死后无耶？说言无记。死后有无耶？非有非无耶？说言无记。云何？尊者！沙门瞿昙为不知、不见耶？」

Ārāḍa Kālāma said, "The World-Honored One is not ignorant and does not not see."

阿［少／兔］罗度言：「世尊非不知、非不见。」

At that time, the non-Buddhist practitioners were displeased with what Ārāḍa Kālāma said. After scolding him, they got up from their seats and left.

时，诸外道于阿［少／兔］罗度所说，心不喜悦，呵骂已，从座起去。

At that time, Ārāḍa Kālāma knew that the non-Buddhist practitioners had left. He went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and told the Buddha in detail about the questions asked by the non-Buddhist practitioners. He said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! They asked me these questions, and I answered like this. Is it in accordance with the Dharma? Will it not slander the World-Honored One? Is it in accordance with the Dharma? Is it against the Dharma? Will it not lead to others coming to criticize and rebuke me?"

时，阿［少／兔］罗度知诸外道去已，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，于一面住，以诸外道所问，向佛广说，白佛言：「世尊！彼如是问，我如是答，为顺诸法说耶？得无谤世尊耶？为顺法耶？为违法耶？无令他来难诘，堕呵责处耶？」

The Buddha told Ārāḍa Kālāma, "I will ask you now. Answer as you wish. Ārāḍa Kālāma! Is form permanent or impermanent?"

佛告阿［少／兔］罗度言：「我今问汝，随所问答。阿［少／兔］罗度！色为常耶？为无常耶？」

He replied, "Impermanent."

答言：「无常。」

"Are feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness permanent or impermanent?"

「受、想、行、识，为常、无常耶？」

He replied, "Impermanent, World-Honored One!"

答言：「无常。世尊！」

As extensively explained in the Yamaka Sūtra, up to "Is consciousness the Tathāgata?"

如焰摩迦契经广说，乃至「识是如来耶？」

He replied, "No."

答曰：「不也。」

The Buddha told Ārāḍa Kālāma, "If one speaks in this way, it accords with all the teachings and does not slander the Tathāgata. It is not going beyond the sequence. As the Tathāgata teaches, all the teachings are sequential. No one can come and criticize it. Why is that? I truly know form, the origin of form, the cessation of form, and the path leading to the cessation of form. Ārāḍa Kālāma! If one abandons the teachings of the Tathāgata and speaks without knowledge or insight, that is not an equal teaching."

佛告阿［少／兔］罗度：「作如是说者，随顺诸记，不谤如来，非为越次；如如来说，诸次法说，无有能来难诘诃责者。所以者何？我于色如实知，色集、色灭、色灭道迹如实知。阿［少／兔］罗度！若舍如来所作，无知无见说者，此非等说。」

After the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, Ārāḍa Kālāma, having heard the Buddha's words, joyfully received and practiced them.

佛说此经已，阿［少／兔］罗度闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
