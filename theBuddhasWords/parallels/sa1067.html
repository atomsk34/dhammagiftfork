SĀ 1067

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1067（一〇六七） 难陀

Parallel to: SN 21.8 / SĀ² 2.5 / EĀ 18.6

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Nanda, the Buddha’s maternal cousin, liked to wear fine clothes, dyed and polished to a glossy sheen. He carried a fine alms bowl and liked to play and joke around.

尔时，尊者难陀是佛姨母子，好著好衣，染色，捣治光泽，执持好钵，好作嬉戏调笑而行。

At that time, many bhikṣus came to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at the Buddha’s feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, “World-Honored One! Venerable Nanda, the Buddha’s maternal cousin, likes to wear fine clothes, polished to a glossy sheen. He carries a fine alms bowl and likes to play and joke around.”

时，有众多比丘来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！尊者难陀是佛姨母子，好著好衣，捣治光泽，执持好钵，好作嬉戏调笑而行。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told a bhikṣu, “Go to the bhikṣu Nanda and say, ‘Nanda! The Master calls you.’”

尔时，世尊告一比丘：「汝往诣难陀比丘所，语言：『难陀！大师语汝。』」

At that time, the bhikṣu received the World-Honored One's instruction and went to tell Nanda, "The World-Honored One calls you."

时，彼比丘受世尊教，往语难陀言：「世尊语汝。」

Upon hearing this, Nanda went to where the Buddha was, bowed at the Buddha's feet, and stood to one side.

难陀闻已，即诣佛所，稽首佛足，退住一面。

The Buddha said to Nanda, "Is it true that you really like to wear fine clothes, polish them to a shine, and play and joke around while walking?"

佛告难陀：「汝实好著好衣，捣治光泽，好作嬉戏调笑而行不？」

Nanda said to the Buddha, "It is true, World-Honored One!"

难陀白佛：「实尔。世尊！」

The Buddha said to Nanda, "You are the son of the Buddha's aunt, born into a noble family. You should not wear fine clothes, polish them to a shine, carry a fine alms bowl, and play and joke around while walking. You should think like this: 'I am the son of the Buddha's aunt, born into a noble family. I should practice as an araṇya ascetic, beg for food, wear rag robes, always praise wearing rag robes, always dwell in mountains and forests, and not care for the five desires.'"

佛告难陀：「汝佛姨母子，贵姓出家，不应著好衣服，捣令光泽，执持好钵，好作嬉戏调笑而行。汝应作是念：『我是佛姨母子，贵姓出家，应作阿练若，乞食，著粪扫衣，常应赞叹著粪扫衣，常处山泽，不顾五欲。』」

At that time, Nanda accepted the Buddha's teaching and practiced the way of the araṇya ascetic, begging for food, wearing rag robes, and always praising those who wore rag robes. He enjoyed dwelling in mountains and forests, not caring for sensual desires. At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，难陀受佛教已，修阿兰若行，乞食，著粪扫衣，亦常赞叹著粪扫衣者，乐处山泽，不顾爱欲。尔时，世尊即说偈言：

What did Nanda see that he practiced the way of the
araṇya ascetic, begging from house to house, wearing rag robes, enjoying
dwelling in mountains and forests, not caring for the five desires?

「难陀何见汝，　　修习阿兰若，家家行乞食，　　身著粪扫衣，乐处于山泽，　　不顾于五欲。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished. Venerable Nanda, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说此经已，尊者难陀闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
