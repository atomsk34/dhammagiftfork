SĀ 107

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 107（一〇七） 长者

Parallel to: SN 22.1 / EĀ 13.4

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was dwelling in the deep forest of the Deer Park on the mountain of Śiśupāragiri in the country of Vṛji.

一时，佛住婆祇国设首婆罗山鹿野深林中。

At that time, there was an elder named Nakula who was 120 years old, old and feeble, weak and suffering from illness. He wanted to see the World-Honored One and the venerable monks he had respected in the past. He came to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I am old and feeble, weak and suffering from illness. I have mustered my strength to come and see the World-Honored One and the venerable monks I have respected in the past. I only hope that the World-Honored One will teach me the Dharma, so that I may be at ease for a long time to come!"

尔时，有那拘罗长者，百二十岁，年耆根熟，羸劣苦病，而欲觐见世尊及先所宗重知识比丘，来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！我年衰老，羸劣苦病，自力勉励，觐见世尊及先所宗重知识比丘，唯愿世尊为我说法，令我长夜安乐！」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the elder Nakula, "Excellent! Elder! You are indeed old and feeble, weak and suffering from illness, yet you have mustered your strength to come and see the Tathāgata and the venerable monks you have respected in the past. Elder, you should know that in this body that is subject to suffering and illness, you should always cultivate a body that is not subject to suffering and illness."

尔时，世尊告那拘罗长者：「善哉！长者！汝实年老根熟，羸劣苦患，而能自力觐见如来并余宗重知识比丘。长者当知，于苦患身，常当修学不苦患身。」

At that time, the World-Honored One instructed, taught, illuminated, and delighted the elder Nakula, and then remained silent.

尔时，世尊为那拘罗长者示、教、照、喜，默然而住。

The elder Nakula, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and followed the Buddha's teachings. He paid homage and departed.

那拘罗长者闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，礼佛而去。

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra was sitting under a tree not far from the World-Honored One. The elder Nakula went to where Venerable Śāriputra was, bowed his head at his feet, and sat to one side. Venerable Śāriputra then asked the elder, "Your faculties are harmonious and joyful, your complexion is radiant. Have you heard the profound Dharma from the World-Honored One?"

时，尊者舍利弗去世尊不远，坐一树下。那拘罗长者往诣尊者舍利弗所，稽首礼足，退坐一面。时，尊者舍利弗问长者言：「汝今诸根和悦，貌色鲜明，于世尊所得闻深法耶？」

The elder Nakula said to Śāriputra, "Today the World-Honored One taught the Dharma for me, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting me. With the Dharma of immortality, he has infused my body and mind. That is why my faculties are now harmonious and joyful, and my complexion is radiant."

那拘罗长者白舍利弗：「今日世尊为我说法，示、教、照、喜，以甘露法，灌我身心，是故我今诸根和悦，颜貌鲜明。」

Venerable Śāriputra asked the elder, "What kind of Dharma did the World-Honored One teach for you, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting you, with the nectar-like nourishment?"

尊者舍利弗问长者言：「世尊为汝说何等法，示、教、照、喜，甘露润泽？」

The elder Nakula said to Śāriputra, "I went to where the World-Honored One was and said to the World-Honored One, 'I am old and weak, suffering from illness. I came here by my own strength to see the World-Honored One and the venerable bhikṣus I respect.' The Buddha told me, 'Excellent! Elder, you are indeed old and weak, suffering from illness, but you can come here by your own strength to see me and the venerable bhikṣus you respect. You should now cultivate the body free from suffering and illness in this body that is suffering and ill.'

那拘罗长者白舍利弗：「我向诣世尊所，白世尊言：『我年衰老，羸劣苦患，自力而来，觐见世尊及所宗重知识比丘。』佛告我言：『善哉！长者！汝实衰老，羸劣苦患，而能自力诣我及见先所宗重比丘。汝今于此苦患之身，常当修学不苦患身。』

"The World-Honored One taught me such a Dharma, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting me, with the nectar-like nourishment."

「世尊为我说如是法，示、教、照、喜，甘露润泽。」

Venerable Śāriputra asked the elder, "Why didn't you ask the World-Honored One again, 'What is a body and mind afflicted by suffering? What is a body afflicted by suffering but a mind not afflicted by suffering?'"

尊者舍利弗问长者言：「汝向何不重问世尊：『云何苦患身、苦患心？云何苦患身、不苦患心？』」

The elder replied, "It is for this meaning that I have come to the Venerable One. I only wish that the Venerable One would briefly explain the essential teachings for me."

长者答言：「我以是义故，来诣尊者，唯愿为我略说法要。」

Venerable Śāriputra said to the elder, "Excellent! Elder! Listen attentively now! I shall explain it for you. Ignorant and uneducated ordinary people do not truly know the origin, cessation, faults, taste, and detachment from form. Because they do not truly know, they delight in form, saying, 'Form is mine, it belongs to me,' and grasp and accept it. When that form changes or becomes different, the mind and consciousness follow and turn, giving rise to affliction and suffering. Once affliction and suffering arise, there is fear, obstruction, concern, sorrow, and attachment. The same is true for feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness. This is called affliction and suffering of body and mind.

尊者舍利弗语长者言：「善哉！长者！汝今谛听！当为汝说。愚痴无闻凡夫于色集、色灭、色患、色味、色离不如实知；不如实知故，爱乐于色，言色是我、是我所，而取摄受。彼色若坏、若异，心识随转，恼苦生；恼苦生已，恐怖、障阂、顾念、忧苦、结恋。于受、想、行、识亦复如是。是名身心苦患。

"What is affliction and suffering of body but not of mind? The well-versed noble disciple truly knows the origin, cessation, taste, faults, and detachment from form. Having truly known, they do not give rise to delight, seeing form as 'me' or 'mine.' When that form changes or becomes different, the mind does not follow and turn, giving rise to affliction and suffering. When the mind does not follow and turn, affliction and suffering do not arise, and one attains non-fear, non-obstruction, non-concern, and non-attachment. The same is true for feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness. This is called affliction and suffering of body but not of mind."

「云何身苦患、心不苦患？多闻圣弟子于色集、色灭、色味、色患、色离如实知；如实知已，不生爱乐，见色是我、是我所，彼色若变、若异，心不随转恼苦生；心不随转恼苦生已，得不恐怖、障碍、顾念、结恋。受、想、行、识亦复如是。是名身苦患、心不苦患。」

When Venerable Śāriputra explained this teaching, Elder Nakula attained the pure Dharma eye. At that time, Elder Nakula saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, knew the Dharma, entered the Dharma, transcended all doubts, and was not dependent on others. He attained fearlessness in the true Dharma. He rose from his seat, adjusted his robes, joined his palms respectfully, and said to Venerable Śāriputra, "I have transcended and crossed over. I now take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṃgha as an upāsaka. Please bear witness to me. I now take refuge in the Three Jewels for as long as I live."

尊者舍利弗说是法时，那拘罗长者得法眼净。尔时，那拘罗长者见法、得法、知法、入法，度诸狐疑，不由于他，于正法中，心得无畏。从座起，整衣服，恭敬合掌，白尊者舍利弗：「我已超、已度，我今归依佛、法、僧宝，为优婆塞，证知我，我今尽寿归依三宝。」

At that time, Elder Nakula, having heard what Venerable Śāriputra said, rejoiced and paid homage before departing.

尔时，那拘罗长者闻尊者舍利弗所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
