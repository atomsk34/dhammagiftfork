SĀ 1074

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1074（一〇七四） 结发

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in the human realm of Magadha. He was accompanied by a thousand monks, all of whom had renounced the world in ancient times, had attained arahantship, had exhausted all taints, had accomplished what was to be done, had laid down the heavy burden, had attained their own benefit, had exhausted all bonds of existence, had right knowledge, were well liberated, and had arrived at the well-established caitya staff forest, where they were staying.

一时，佛在摩竭提国人间游行。与千比丘俱，皆是古昔萦发出家，皆得阿罗汉，诸漏已尽，所作已作，舍诸重担，逮得己利，尽诸有结，正智，善解脱，到善建立支提杖林中住。

King Bimbisāra of Magadha heard that the World-Honored One was traveling in the human realm of Magadha and had arrived at the well-established caitya staff forest, where he was staying. He, together with the vassal kings, ministers, and attendants, in twelve thousand carriages, eighteen thousand mounted on horses, and countless on foot, and all the brahmins and elders of Magadha, followed.

摩竭提王瓶沙闻世尊摩竭提国人间游行，至善建立支提杖林中住，与诸小王群臣羽从，车万二千、乘马万八千、步逐众无数，摩竭提婆罗门长者悉皆从。

The king left Rājagṛha and went to where the World-Honored One was, to pay respects and make offerings. At the entrance of the road, he got down from the carriage and walked. At the inner gate, he removed the five adornments, took off his crown and umbrella, put away his fan and sword, took off his leather shoes, went before the Buddha, arranged his robes, bared his right shoulder, paid homage to the Buddha, circumambulated him three times to the right, announced his name, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I am King Bimbisāra of Magadha."

王出王舍城，诣世尊所，恭敬供养。到于道口，下车步进，及于内门，除去五饰，脱冠却盖，除扇去剑刀，脱革屣，到于佛前，整衣服，偏露右肩，为佛作礼，右遶三匝，自称姓名，白佛言：「世尊！我是摩竭提王瓶沙。」

The Buddha told Bimbisāra, "So it is, great king! You are Bimbisāra. You may sit here, as you are comfortable."

佛告瓶沙：「如是，大王！汝是瓶沙，可就此坐，随其所安。」

At that time, King Bimbisāra again bowed at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side. The kings, ministers, brahmins, and householders all bowed at the Buddha's feet and sat in order.

时，瓶沙王重礼佛足，退坐一面。诸王大臣、婆罗门居士悉礼佛足，次第而坐。

At that time, Uruvilvā Kāśyapa was also in the assembly.

时，欝鞞罗迦叶亦在座中。

At that time, the brāhman householders of Magadha thought: "Does the Great Śramaṇa practice the holy life under Uruvilvā Kāśyapa, or does Uruvilvā Kāśyapa practice the holy life under the Great Śramaṇa?"

时，摩竭提婆罗门长者作是念：「为大沙门从欝鞞罗迦叶所修梵行耶？为欝鞞罗迦叶于大沙门所修梵行耶？」

At that time, the World-Honored One, knowing the thoughts in the minds of the brāhman householders of Magadha, spoke a verse and asked:

尔时，世尊知摩竭提婆罗门长者心之所念，即说偈而问言：

Uruvilvā Kāśyapa, what benefit did you see here, that you
abandoned the various practices you previously upheld, such as serving
fire? Now please explain the reason for abandoning serving fire.

「欝鞞罗迦叶，　　于此见何利，弃汝先所奉，　　事火等众事。今可说其义，　　舍事火之由。」

Uruvilvā Kāśyapa spoke a verse to the Buddha:

欝鞞迦叶说偈白佛：

The taste of wealth and so forth, the fruits of women and
the five desires, observing the future suffering, all of this is greatly
defiled. Therefore I have abandoned all the previous practices of serving fire.

「钱财等滋味，　　女色五欲果，观察未来受，　　斯皆大垢秽，是故悉弃舍，　　先诸奉火事。」

At that time, the World-Honored One again spoke a verse and asked:

尔时，世尊复说偈问言：

You are not attached to worldly wealth and the tastes of the five colors
Why then do you abandon the heavens and humans? Kāśyapa, please explain the meaning.

「汝不著世间，　　钱财五色味，复何舍天人，　　迦叶随义说。」

Kāśyapa again replied to the World-Honored One in verse:

迦叶复以偈答世尊言：

Seeing the path, one is free from the rest, and in tranquil extinction
there are no traces left. Without existence, one is not attached, and there
is no different destination or path. Therefore, I have completely abandoned the previous
practice of fire worship, and the great assembly upholds and serves water and
fire. The foolish are immersed in this, aspiring for the path of liberation
Blind without the eyes of wisdom, they face birth, old age, sickness, and
death. Not seeing the right path, they are permanently separated from the path
of birth and death. Now, for the first time, I have seen the
unconditioned path due to the World-Honored One. The power spoken by the great
dragon enables one to cross to the other shore. The Muni widely liberates
and comforts immeasurable beings. Now, for the first time, I know that Gautama
is truly the one who transcends.

「见道离有余，　　寂灭无余迹，无所有不著，　　无异趣异道。是故悉弃舍，　　先修奉火事，大会等受持，　　奉事于水火。愚痴没于中，　　志求解脱道，盲无智慧目，　　向生老病死。不见于正路，　　永离生死道，今始因世尊，　　得见无为道。大龙所说力，　　得度于彼岸，牟尼广济度，　　安慰无量众。今始知瞿昙，　　真谛超出者。」

The Buddha again spoke a verse in praise of Kāśyapa:

佛复说偈叹迦叶言：

Excellent, you Kāśyapa, who previously did not have evil thoughts
but sought in stages and eventually reached the superior place.

「善哉汝迦叶，　　先非恶思量，次第分别求，　　遂至于胜处。

"Now, Kāśyapa! You should comfort the minds of your disciples."

「汝今，迦叶！当安慰汝徒众之心。」

At that time, Udraka Rāmaputra immediately entered right concentration and, with the power of supernatural feet, rose into the sky in the eastern direction, performing the four kinds of supernatural transformations of walking, standing, sitting, and lying down. He entered the fire samadhi, with his whole body blazing blue, yellow, red, white, and crystal-colored. Water flowed from his upper body and fire from his lower body, burning his body. Water flowed from his upper body to douse his body, or fire flowed from his upper body to burn his body, and water flowed from his lower body to douse his body. Having shown these various manifestations of supernatural powers, he bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! The Buddha is my teacher, and I am his disciple."

时，欝鞞罗迦叶即入正受，以神足力向于东方，上升虚空，作四种神变，行、住、坐、卧，入火三昧，举身洞然，青、黄、赤、白、颇梨红色。身上出水，身下出火，还烧其身，身上出水以灌其身，或身上出火，以烧其身，身下出水，以灌其身。如是种种现化神通息已，稽首佛足，白佛言：「世尊！佛是我师，我是弟子。」

The Buddha told Kāśyapa, "I am your teacher, and you are my disciple. Sit down wherever you are comfortable."

佛告迦叶：「我是汝师，汝是弟子，随汝所安，复座而坐。」

At that time, Udraka Rāmaputra returned to his original seat.

时，欝鞞罗迦叶还复故坐。

At that time, the brāhman householders of Magadha thought, "Udraka Rāmaputra must be practicing the holy life under the Great Śramaṇa."

尔时，摩竭提婆罗门长者作是念：「欝鞞罗迦叶定于大沙门所修行梵行。」

Having spoken this sūtra, King Bimbisāra of Magadha and the brāhman householders rejoiced in what the Buddha had said. They bowed and departed.

佛说此经已，摩竭提王瓶沙及诸婆罗门长者闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
