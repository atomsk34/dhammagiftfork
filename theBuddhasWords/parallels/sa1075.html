SĀ 1075

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1075（一〇七五） 陀骠

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, there was a son of a Dīpaṅkara, who had long been living in Rājagṛha, in charge of the food and drink of the monks, and arranging the seating in order, not allowing anyone to skip their turn.

时，有陀骠摩罗子，旧住王舍城，典知众僧饮食，床座随次差请，不令越次。

At that time, there was a bhikṣu named Maitrakanyaka, who had been given coarse food three times in a row. When it was time to eat, he was distressed and thought, "Alas! What great suffering! The bhikṣu son of Dīpaṅkara is so compassionate that he has given me coarse food to eat, causing me great suffering. How can I do something unbeneficial to him?"

时，有慈地比丘，频三过次得麤食处，食时辛苦，作是念：「怪哉！大苦！陀骠摩罗子比丘有情故，以麤食恼我，令我食时极苦，我当云何为其作不饶益事？」

At that time, there was a bhikṣuṇī named Madri, who was the sister of the bhikṣu Maitrakanyaka. She was staying in the bhikṣuṇī community in the King's Park in Rājagṛha. The bhikṣuṇī Madri came to the bhikṣu Maitrakanyaka, bowed at his feet, and stood to one side. The bhikṣu Maitrakanyaka did not look at her or speak to her. The bhikṣuṇī Madri said to the bhikṣu Maitrakanyaka, "Venerable one! Why do you not look at me or speak to me?"

时，慈地比丘有姊妹比丘尼，名蜜多罗，住王舍城王园比丘尼众中。蜜多罗比丘尼来诣慈地比丘，稽首礼足，于一面住。慈地比丘不顾眄，不与语。蜜多罗比丘尼语慈地比丘：「阿梨！何故不见顾眄，不共言语？」

The bhikṣu Maitrakanyaka said, "The bhikṣu son of Dīpaṅkara has repeatedly given me coarse food to eat, causing me great suffering. You have also abandoned me."

慈地比丘言：「陀骠摩罗子比丘数以麤食恼我，令我食时极苦，汝复弃我。」

The bhikṣuṇī said, "What should be done?"

比丘尼言：「当如何？」

The bhikṣu Maitrakanyaka said, "You should go to the World-Honored One and say, 'World-Honored One! The bhikṣu son of Dīpaṅkara is acting unlawfully and improperly, engaging in non-celibate conduct with me, committing the pārājika offense.' I will then testify, 'Yes, World-Honored One! As the sister says.'"

慈地比丘言：「汝可至世尊所，白言：『世尊！陀骠摩罗子比丘非法不类，共我作非梵行波罗夷罪。』我当证言：『如是。世尊！如妹所说。』」

The bhikṣuṇī said, "Venerable! How can I falsely accuse a bhikṣu of pure conduct of committing the pārājika offense?"

比丘尼言：「阿梨！我当云何于梵行比丘所，以波罗夷谤？」

The bhikṣu Maitrakanyaka said, "If you do not do this, I will cut off relations with you. We will no longer speak or look at each other."

慈地比丘言：「汝若不如是者，我与汝绝，不复来往言语、共相瞻视。」

At that time, the bhikṣuṇī silently thought for a moment and said, "Venerable! If you want me to do this, I will follow your instructions."

时，比丘尼须臾默念，而作是言：「阿梨！欲令我尔，当从其教。」

The bhikṣu Maitrakanyaka said, "Wait for me first to go to the World-Honored One. You can come later."

慈地比丘言：「汝且待我先至世尊所，汝随后来。」

At that time, the bhikṣu Maitrakanyaka went to pay homage at the feet of the World-Honored One and stood to one side. The bhikṣuṇī Maitrāyaniyā immediately followed and paid homage at the Buddha's feet, standing to one side. She said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! How unwholesome and unseemly of Dīrghanakha! He committed the pārājika offense of engaging in non-celibate conduct with me."

时，慈地比丘即往稽首，礼世尊足，退住一面。蜜多罗比丘尼即随后至，稽首佛足，退住一面，白佛言：「世尊！一何不善不类陀骠摩罗子！于我所作非梵行波罗夷罪。」

The bhikṣu Maitrakanyaka again said to the Buddha, "As the sister has said, I also knew it before."

慈地比丘复白佛言：「如妹所说，我先亦知。」

At that time, the bhikṣu Dīrghanakha was in that great assembly.

尔时，陀骠摩罗子比丘即在彼大众中。

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the bhikṣu Dīrghanakha, "Did you hear these words?"

尔时，世尊告陀骠摩罗子比丘：「汝闻此语不？」

The bhikṣu Dīrghanakha said, "I have heard it, World-Honored One!"

陀骠摩罗子比丘言：「已闻，世尊！」

The Buddha said to the bhikṣu Dīrghanakha, "What do you think now?"

佛告陀骠摩罗子比丘：「汝今云何？」

Dīrghanakha said to the Buddha, "As the World-Honored One knows, as the Well-Gone One knows."

陀骠摩罗子白佛：「如世尊所知，如善逝所知。」

The Buddha said to Dīrghanakha, "You say, 'As the World-Honored One knows, as the Well-Gone One knows.' Now is not the time. You should say what you remember, and what you do not remember, you should say that you do not remember."

佛告陀骠摩罗子：「汝言如世尊所知，今非是时，汝今忆念，当言忆念；不忆念，当言不忆念。」

Dīrghanakha said, "I do not remember myself."

陀骠摩罗子言：「我不自忆念。」

At that time, Venerable Rāhula was standing behind the Buddha, holding a fan and fanning him. He said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! It is not good and improper for this bhikṣuṇī to say, 'Venerable Dīrghanakha and I engaged in non-celibate conduct.' The bhikṣu Cīrā said, 'Yes, World-Honored One! I already knew as the sister said.'"

尔时，尊者罗睺罗住于佛后，执扇扇佛，白佛言：「世尊！不善不类是比丘尼言：『尊者陀骠摩罗子共我作非梵行。』慈地比丘言：『如是，世尊！我先已知，如妹所说。』」

The Buddha said to Rāhula, "I now ask you, answer me as you wish. If the bhikṣuṇī Maitrāyānī comes to me and says, 'World-Honored One! It is not good and improper for Rāhula to engage in non-celibate conduct with me, the pārājika offense.' And the bhikṣu Cīrā again says to me, 'Yes, World-Honored One! As the sister says, I already knew before.' What would you say?"

佛告罗睺罗：「我今问汝，随意答我。若蜜多罗比丘尼来语我言：『世尊！不善不类罗睺罗，共我作非梵行波罗夷罪。』慈地比丘复白我言：『如是，世尊！如妹所说，我先亦知』者，汝当云何？」

Rāhula said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! If I remember, I will say I remember; if I do not remember, I will say I do not remember."

罗睺罗白佛：「世尊！我若忆念，当言忆念；不忆念，当言不忆念。」

The Buddha said, "Rāhula! Even a foolish person like you can still say this, how can the pure bhikṣu Dīrghanakha not be able to say this?"

佛言：「罗睺罗！愚痴人汝尚得作此语，陀骠摩罗子清净比丘何以不得作如是语？」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "The bhikṣu Dīrghanakha should remember, the bhikṣuṇī Mitrā should be expelled by her own confession, and the bhikṣu Cīvaradatta should be severely admonished and instructed: 'What did you see? Where did you see it? Why did you go to see it?'" After the World-Honored One gave this instruction, he rose from his seat, entered the room, and sat in meditation.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「于陀骠摩罗子比丘当忆念，蜜多罗比丘尼当以自言灭，慈地比丘僧当极善呵谏教诫：『汝云何见？何处见？汝何因往见？』」世尊如是教已，从座起，入室坐禅。

At that time, the bhikṣus remembered the bhikṣu Dīrghanakha, the bhikṣuṇī Mitrā was expelled by her own confession, and the bhikṣu Cīvaradatta was severely admonished and instructed, saying, "What did you see? Where did you see it? Why did you go to see it?"

尔时，诸比丘于陀骠摩罗子比丘忆念，蜜多罗比丘尼与自言灭，慈地比丘极善呵谏教诫言：「汝云何见？何处见？何因往见？」

When admonished in this way, he said, "Dīrghanakha did not engage in non-celibacy, he did not commit a pārājika offense. However, the bhikṣu Dīrghanakha frightened me with coarse and evil food three times, making it difficult for me to eat. I spoke out of love, hatred, delusion, and fear for the bhikṣu Dīrghanakha. He is pure and without fault."

如是谏时，彼作是言：「彼陀骠摩罗子不作非梵行，不犯波罗夷，然陀骠摩罗子比丘三以麤恶食恐怖，令我食时辛苦。我于陀骠摩罗子比丘爱恚痴怖，故作是说，然陀骠摩罗子清净无罪。」

At that time, the World-Honored One, having emerged from meditation in the afternoon, went to the front of the great assembly, prepared a seat, and sat down. The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! We have remembered the bhikṣu Dīrghanakha, the bhikṣuṇī Mitrā was expelled by her own confession, and the bhikṣu Cīvaradatta was severely admonished, up to his saying, 'Dīrghanakha is pure and without fault.'"

尔时，世尊晡时从禅觉，至大众前敷座而坐。诸比丘白佛言：「世尊！我等于陀骠摩罗子比丘所忆念持，蜜多罗比丘尼与自言灭，慈地比丘极善呵谏，乃至彼言：『陀骠摩罗子清净无罪。』」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "How foolish! Knowing, one speaks falsely because of food and drink." At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse,

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「云何愚痴？以因饮食故，知而妄语。」尔时，世尊即说偈言：

If one can abandon one Dharma, knowing and speaking falsely, not
considering the afterlife, there is no evil one will not do
It is better to eat molten iron balls, like burning charcoal
than to eat the offerings of the Sangha with broken precepts.

「若能舍一法，　　知而故妄语，不计于后世，　　无恶而不为。宁食热铁丸，　　如炽然炭火，不以犯禁戒，　　而食僧信施。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished. The bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
