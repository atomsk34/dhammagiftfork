SĀ 1078

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1078（一〇七八） 散倒咤

Parallel to: SN 1.20 / SĀ² 2.17

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, a certain bhikṣu, at the time when the night was bright, went to the bank of the Toraṇavatī River, took off his clothes and put them on the bank, entered the water to bathe, and after bathing, came out and put on one robe, waiting for his body to dry.

时，有异比丘，于夜明相出时，出搨补河边，脱衣著岸边，入水洗浴。浴已上岸，被一衣，待身干。

At that time, a certain god emitted a radiant light, illuminating the bank of the Toraṇavatī River, and said to the bhikṣu: "You left home at a young age, with white hair and black, in the prime of your youth, you should enjoy the five desires, adorn yourself with jewelry, apply fragrant ointments and flower garlands, and amuse yourself with the five kinds of music. But at this time, you disobeyed your parents, turned your back on worldly life, wept in separation, shaved your beard and hair, put on the saffron robes, with right faith left home and entered the monastic life. How can you abandon present pleasures and seek benefits at an improper time?"

时，有一天子放身光明，普照搨补河侧，语比丘言：「汝少出家，鲜白发黑，年始盛美，应习五欲，庄严璎珞，涂香华鬘，五乐自娱，而于是时，违亲背俗，悲泣别离，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，正信非家，出家学道。如何舍现前乐，而求非时之利？」

The bhikṣu replied: "I do not abandon present pleasures to seek pleasures at an improper time. I now abandon pleasures at an improper time to obtain present pleasures."

比丘答言：「我不舍现前乐求非时乐，我今乃是舍非时乐得现前乐。」

The god asked the bhikṣu: "How do you abandon pleasures at an improper time to obtain present pleasures?"

天问比丘：「云何舍非时乐得现前乐？」

The bhikṣu replied: "As the World-Honored One has said, desires at an improper time have little taste and much suffering, little benefit and much difficulty. I have now, in this present life, already abandoned burning passions, not waiting for a time, able to realize and observe for myself, and know through my own experience. Thus, Heavenly Son! This is called abandoning pleasures at an improper time to obtain present pleasures."

比丘答言：「如世尊说，非时之欲，少味多苦，少利多难。我今于现法中，已离炽然，不待时节，能自通达，现前观察，缘自知觉。如是，天子！是名舍非时乐得现前乐。」

The god again asked the bhikṣu: "How is it that the Tathāgata has said that desires at an improper time have little pleasure and much suffering? How is it that the Tathāgata has said that present-life benefits and pleasures, up to knowing through one's own experience?"

天复问比丘：「云何复是如来所说，非时之欲，少乐多苦？云何复是如来所说，现法利乐，乃至缘自觉知？」

The bhikṣu replied: "I have left home at a young age and cannot extensively proclaim the true Dharma and Vinaya taught by the Tathāgata. The World-Honored One is nearby in the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove. You may go to the Tathāgata and ask your doubts, and as the World-Honored One says, remember and uphold them."

比丘答言：「我年少出家，不能广宣如来所说正法、律仪。世尊近在迦兰陀竹园。汝可往诣如来，问其所疑，如世尊说，随忆受持。」

The god again said: "Bhikṣu! The Tathāgata is surrounded by many powerful gods. I have not asked before and cannot easily approach him. Bhikṣu! If you can first inform the World-Honored One, I can follow and go."

天子复言：「比丘！于如来所，有诸力天众多围遶，我先无问，未易可诣。比丘！汝若能为先白世尊者，我可随往。」

The bhikṣu replied: "I will go for you!"

比丘答言：「当为汝去！」

The god said to the bhikṣu: "Yes, Venerable One! I will follow behind."

天白比丘：「唯然，尊者！我随后来。」

At that time, the bhikṣu went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and stood to one side. He told the World-Honored One in detail about the questions and answers he had exchanged with the god, saying: "World-Honored One! If that god is speaking truthfully, he should arrive soon. If he is not speaking truthfully, he will not come by himself."

时，彼比丘往诣佛所，稽首礼足，退住一面，以向天子往反问答具白世尊：「今者，世尊！彼天子诚实言者须臾应至，不诚实者自当不来。」

At that time, the god spoke from afar to the bhikṣu: "I am already here, I am already here."

时，彼天子遥语比丘：「我已在此，我已在此。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

Sentient beings follow their thoughts of love, abiding in their thoughts of
love. Because they do not know love, it becomes a means of death.

「众生随爱想，　　以爱想而住，以不知爱故，　　则为死方便。」

The Buddha said to the celestial being, "If you understand this verse, then you may ask your question."

佛告天子：「汝解此偈者，便可发问。」

The celestial being said to the Buddha, "I do not understand, World-Honored One! I do not understand, Well-Gone One!" The Buddha again spoke a verse to the celestial being, saying:

天子白佛：「不解，世尊！不解，善逝！」佛复说偈而告天子曰：

If one knows what is loved, one does not give rise to love
for it. Both self and other are non-existent, and others cannot speak of it.

「若知所爱者，　　不于彼生爱，彼此无所有，　　他人莫能说。」

The Buddha said to the celestial being, "If you understand this meaning, you may ask about it."

佛告天子：「汝解此义者，便可发问。」

The celestial being said to the Buddha, "I do not understand, World-Honored One! I do not understand, Well-Gone One!"

天子白佛：「不解，世尊！不解，善逝！」

The Buddha again spoke a verse, saying:

佛复说偈言：

When one sees the superiority and inferiority of others, then verbal disputes arise. When
the three things are not swayed, then there is no softness, middle, or superiority.

「见等胜劣者，　　则有言论生，三事不倾动，　　则无软中上。」

The Buddha said to the celestial being, "If one understands this meaning, then one may ask a question."

佛告天子：「解此义者，则可发问。」

The celestial being said to the Buddha, "I do not understand, World-Honored One! I do not understand, Well-Gone One!"

天子白佛：「不解，世尊！不解，善逝！」

The Buddha again spoke a verse, saying:

佛复说偈言：

Cutting off desire and form, eliminating arrogance and being unattached
peacefully extinguishing anger and hatred, severing bonds and cutting off
hopes, not seeing in this world or the other world.

「断爱及名色，　　除慢无所系，寂灭息瞋恚，　　离结绝悕望，不见于人天，　　此世及他世。」

The Buddha said to the celestial being, "One who understands this meaning may ask a question."

佛告天子：「解此义者，乃可发问。」

The celestial being said to the Buddha, "I understand, World-Honored One! I understand, Well-Gone One!"

天子白佛：「已解，世尊！已解，善逝！」

Having spoken this sūtra, the celestial being, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and was delighted. He then disappeared.

佛说此经已，彼天子闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，即没不现。
