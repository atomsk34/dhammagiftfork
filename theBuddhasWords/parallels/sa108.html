SĀ 108

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 108（一〇八） 西

Parallel to: SN 22.2 / EĀ 41.4 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Śākya village of Devakūṭa.

一时，佛住释氏天现聚落。

At that time, many bhikṣus from the west wanted to return to the west for the summer retreat. They went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side.

尔时，有西方众多比丘欲还西方安居，诣世尊所，稽首佛足，退坐一面。

At that time, the World-Honored One taught the Dharma for them, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting them. Having shown, taught, illuminated, and delighted them in various ways, the many bhikṣus from the west rose from their seats, joined their palms, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! We, the many bhikṣus from the west, want to return to the west for the summer retreat. We now take our leave."

尔时，世尊为其说法，示、教、照、喜。种种示、教、照、喜已，时西方众多比丘从座起，合掌白佛言：「世尊！我西方众多比丘欲还西方安居，今请奉辞。」

The Buddha said to the bhikṣus from the west, "Have you taken leave of Śāriputra?"

佛告西方诸比丘：「汝辞舍利弗未？」

They replied, "We have not taken leave yet."

答言：「未辞。」

The Buddha said to the bhikṣus from the west, "Śāriputra has practiced pure conduct. You should take leave of him. It will bring you benefit and happiness for a long time."

佛告西方诸比丘：「舍利弗淳修梵行，汝当奉辞，能令汝等以义饶益，长夜安乐。」

At that time, the bhikṣus from the west took leave and were about to depart. Venerable Śāriputra was sitting under a firm tree not far from the Buddha. The bhikṣus from the west went to where Venerable Śāriputra was, bowed their heads at his feet, and sat to one side. They said to Venerable Śāriputra, "We wish to return to the west to enter the rainy season retreat. Therefore, we have come to take leave of you."

时，西方诸比丘辞退欲去。时，尊者舍利弗去佛不远，坐一坚固树下，西方诸比丘往诣尊者舍利弗所，稽首礼足，退坐一面，白尊者舍利弗言：「我等欲还西方安居，故来奉辞。」

Śāriputra said, "Have you taken leave of the World-Honored One?"

舍利弗言：「汝等辞世尊未？」

They replied, "We have already taken leave."

答言：「已辞。」

Śāriputra said, "When you return to the Western lands, in various countries and among various assemblies, they will certainly ask you. Now, having heard the well-spoken Dharma from the World-Honored One, you should well receive, well uphold, well contemplate, and well enter it. Will you be able to fully proclaim it for them without slandering the Buddha? Will you not fall into a position of difficulty when questioned and examined by those assemblies?"

舍利弗言：「汝等还西方，处处异国，种种异众，必当问汝。汝等今于世尊所，闻善说法，当善受、善持、善观、善入，足能为彼具足宣说，不毁佛耶？不令彼众难问、诘责、堕负处耶？」

Those bhikṣus said to Śāriputra, "We have come to the Venerable One to hear the Dharma. We only wish that the Venerable One would fully explain it for us out of compassion!"

彼诸比丘白舍利弗：「我等为闻法故，来诣尊者，唯愿尊者具为我说，哀愍故！」

The Venerable Śāriputra said to the bhikṣus, "The people of Jambudvīpa are intelligent and sharp-witted. Whether they are kṣatriyas, brāhmaṇas, householders, or śramaṇas, they will certainly ask you, 'What does your teacher teach? What does he instruct you with?' You should answer, 'Our teacher only teaches the subduing of desire and greed. This is what he instructs us with.'

尊者舍利弗告诸比丘：「阎浮提人聪明利根，若刹利、若婆罗门、若长者、若沙门，必当问汝：『汝彼大师云何说法？以何教教汝？』当答言：『大师唯说调伏欲贪，以此教教。』

They will further ask you, 'In which dharmas do you subdue desire and greed?' You should further answer, 'Our teacher only teaches the subduing of desire and greed in relation to the aggregate of form, the aggregate of feeling, the aggregate of perception, the aggregate of volitional formations, and the aggregate of consciousness. This is how our teacher teaches the Dharma.'

「当复问汝：『于何法中调伏欲贪？』当复答言：『大师唯说于彼色阴调伏欲贪，于受、想、行、识阴调伏欲贪，我大师如是说法。』

They will further ask, 'What are the faults of desire and greed that the teacher teaches the subduing of desire and greed in relation to form, feeling, perception, volitional formations, and consciousness?' You should further answer, 'If desire, greed, attachment, recollection, and thirst are not severed in relation to form, then when form changes or transforms, one will give rise to sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress. The same is true for feeling, perception, volitional formations, and consciousness. Seeing that desire and greed have such faults, one subdues desire and greed in relation to form, feeling, perception, volitional formations, and consciousness.'

「彼当复问：『欲贪有何过患故，大师说于色调伏欲贪？受、想、行、识调伏欲贪？』汝复应答言：『若于色欲不断、贪不断、爱不断、念不断、渴不断者，彼色若变、若异，则生忧、悲、恼、苦。受、想、行、识亦复如是。见欲贪有如是过故，于色调伏欲贪，于受、想、行、识调伏欲贪。』

They will further ask, 'Seeing the severance of desire and greed, what benefits are there that the teacher teaches the subduing of desire and greed in relation to form, feeling, perception, volitional formations, and consciousness?' You should further answer, 'If desire, greed, recollection, attachment, and thirst are severed in relation to form, then when form changes or transforms, one will not give rise to sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress. The same is true for feeling, perception, volitional formations, and consciousness.'

「彼复当问：『见断欲贪，有何福利故，大师说于色调伏欲贪，于受、想、行、识调伏欲贪？』当复答言：『若于色断欲、断贪、断念、断爱、断渴，彼色若变、若异，不起忧、悲、恼、苦。受、想、行、识亦复如是。』

Venerable ones! If one experiences present happiness, non-suffering, non-obstruction, non-distress, and non-heat due to the causes and conditions of unwholesome dharmas, and is reborn in a good place after the body perishes and life ends, the World-Honored One would never say: 'One should abandon all unwholesome dharmas.' He would also not teach others to cultivate the brahma-practices in the Buddha's teachings, to end suffering. Because one experiences present suffering, obstruction, heat, and distress due to the causes and conditions of unwholesome dharmas, and after the body perishes and life ends, one falls into evil paths. Therefore, the World-Honored One says: 'One should abandon unwholesome dharmas, cultivate the brahma-practices in the Buddha's teachings, equally end suffering, and ultimately reach the end of suffering.'

「诸尊！若受诸不善法因缘故，今得现法乐住，不苦、不碍、不恼、不热，身坏命终生于善处者，世尊终不说言：『当断诸不善法。亦不教人于佛法中修诸梵行，得尽苦边。以受诸不善法因缘故，今现法苦住，障碍热恼，身坏命终，堕恶道中。』是故世尊说言：『当断不善法，于佛法中修诸梵行，平等尽苦，究竟苦边。』

"If one experiences present suffering, obstruction, heat, and distress due to the causes and conditions of wholesome dharmas, and after the body perishes and life ends, one falls into evil paths, the World-Honored One would never say to uphold wholesome dharmas, to cultivate the brahma-practices in the Buddha's teachings, to equally end suffering, and to ultimately reach the end of suffering. Upholding wholesome dharmas, one experiences present happiness, non-suffering, non-obstruction, non-distress, and non-heat, and after the body perishes and life ends, one is reborn in a good place. Therefore, the World-Honored One praises and teaches others to uphold wholesome dharmas, to cultivate the brahma-practices in the Buddha's teachings, to equally end suffering, and to ultimately reach the end of suffering."

「若受诸善法因缘，现法苦住，障碍热恼，身坏命终堕恶道中者，世尊终不说受持善法，于佛法中，修诸梵行，平等尽苦，究竟苦边。受持善法，现法乐住，不苦、不碍、不恼、不热，身坏命终，生于善处，是故世尊赞叹、教人受诸善法，于佛法中，修诸梵行，平等尽苦，究竟苦边。」

When Venerable Śāriputra spoke this Dharma, the bhikṣus in the west were free from all defilements and attained liberation. When Venerable Śāriputra spoke this Dharma, the bhikṣus rejoiced and paid homage before departing.

尊者舍利弗说是法时，西方诸比丘不起诸漏，心得解脱。尊者舍利弗说是法时，诸比丘欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
