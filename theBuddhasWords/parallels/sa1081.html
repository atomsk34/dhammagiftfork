SĀ 1081

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1081（一〇八一） 苦子

Parallel to: AN 3.128 / SĀ² 2.20

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying in the Deer Park near the city of Bārāṇasī.

一时，佛住波罗［木＊奈］国鹿野苑中。

At that time, the World-Honored One, in the morning, put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and entered the city of Bārāṇasī to beg for alms. At that time, a certain bhikṣu, having put on his robe and taken his alms bowl, entered the city to beg for alms. He stopped under a tree by the roadside and gave rise to unwholesome thoughts, based on evil desires.

尔时，世尊晨朝著衣持钵，入波罗［木＊奈］城乞食。时，有异比丘著衣持钵，入城乞食，于其路边，住一树下，起不善觉，以依恶贪。

At that time, the World-Honored One saw that bhikṣu standing under a tree, giving rise to unwholesome thoughts, based on evil desires, and said to him, "Bhikṣu! Bhikṣu! Do not plant the seeds of suffering, giving rise to stench and leaking fluids. If a bhikṣu plants the seeds of suffering, giving rise to stench and leaking fluids, and wishes for flies not to gather around, it is impossible!"

尔时，世尊见彼比丘住一树下，以生不善觉，依恶贪嗜，而告之曰：「比丘！比丘！莫种苦种，而发熏生臭，汁漏流出。若比丘种苦种子，自发生臭，汁漏流出者，欲令蛆蝇不竞集者，无有是处！」

At that time, that bhikṣu thought, "The World-Honored One knows the evil thoughts in my mind." He then became terrified, and his body hair stood on end.

时，彼比丘作是念：「世尊知我心之恶念。」即生恐怖，身毛皆竖。

At that time, the World-Honored One entered the city to beg for alms. After returning to the monastery, he put away his robes and bowl, washed his feet, and sat in meditation in the room. In the afternoon, he emerged from meditation and went to the assembly. He sat in front of the assembly, arranged his seat, and said to the bhikṣus, "This morning, I put on my robes, took my bowl, entered the city to beg for alms, and saw a bhikṣu standing under a tree, giving rise to unwholesome thoughts, based on evil desires. Having seen this, I said to him, 'Bhikṣu! Bhikṣu! Do not plant the seeds of suffering, giving rise to stench and leaking fluids. If a bhikṣu plants the seeds of suffering, giving rise to stench and leaking fluids, and wishes for flies not to gather around, it is impossible!' At that time, that bhikṣu thought, 'The Buddha already knows the thoughts in my mind.' He was ashamed, terrified, and his hair stood on end as he left along the road.

尔时，世尊入城乞食毕，还精舍，举衣钵，洗足已，入室坐禅。晡时从禅觉，至于僧中，于众前敷座而坐，告诸比丘：「我今晨朝著衣持钵，入城乞食，见一比丘住于树下，以生不善觉，依恶贪嗜。我时见已，即告之言：『比丘！比丘！莫种苦种，发熏生臭，恶汁流出。若有比丘种苦种子，发熏生臭，恶汁流出，蛆蝇不集，无有是处！』时，彼比丘即思念：『佛已知我心之所念。』惭愧恐怖，心惊毛竖，随路而去。」

At that time, a certain bhikṣu rose from his seat, arranged his robes, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! What are the seeds of suffering? What is the stench? What is the leaking fluid? What are the flies?"

时，有异比丘从坐起，整衣服，偏袒右肩，合掌白佛：「世尊！云何苦种？云何生臭？云何汁流？云何蛆蝇？」

The Buddha told the bhikṣu, "Anger and resentment are called the seeds of suffering. The five desires and merits are called the stench. Not restraining the six sense bases is called the leaking fluid. That is, not restraining the sense bases, greed, sorrow, and various evil and unwholesome thoughts arise, which are called the flies." At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

佛告比丘：「忿怒烦怨，名曰苦种。五欲功德，名为生臭。于六触入处不摄律仪，是名汁流。谓触入处不摄已，贪、忧、诸恶不善心竞生，是名蛆蝇。」尔时，世尊即说偈言：

If the ears and eyes are not guarded, greed arises from them. This
is called the seeds of suffering. The stench, the leaking fluid, and the
flavors of various perceptions and contemplations arise from evil greed and craving. Whether
in villages or empty places, day or night, if one practices the Brahma
way of life, one will reach the end of suffering. If the mind
is internally tranquil, one will definitely know the truth clearly. Sleeping and waking
are always peaceful and blissful, and the evil flies are eliminated. The path
of the virtuous and sages is what the righteous ones practice and teach
Knowing the eightfold right path, one will not return to take rebirth again.

「耳目不防护，　　贪欲从是生，是名为苦种。　　生臭汁潜流，诸觉观气味，　　依于恶贪嗜，聚落及空处，　　若于昼若夜，远离修梵行，　　究竟于苦边，若内心寂静，　　决定谛明了。卧觉常安乐，　　诸恶蛆蝇灭，正士所习近，　　善说贤圣路。了知八正道，　　不还更受身。」

After the Buddha spoke this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully practiced it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
