SĀ 1082

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1082（一〇八二） 覆疮

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One, in the morning, put on his robe, took his bowl, and entered the city of Śrāvastī to beg for alms. After eating, he returned to the monastery, washed his feet, and entered the Andhavana forest to sit in meditation.

尔时，世尊晨朝著衣持钵，入舍卫城乞食。食毕，还精舍，洗足已，入安陀林坐禅。

At that time, another bhikṣu also, in the morning, put on his robe, took his bowl, and entered the city of Śrāvastī to beg for alms. After eating, he returned to the monastery, washed his feet, and entered the Andhavana forest, sitting under a tree, entering the midday meditative absorption. When this bhikṣu entered the midday meditative absorption, evil and unwholesome thoughts arose, based on a mind of craving.

时，有异比丘亦复晨朝著衣持钵，入舍卫城乞食。食毕，还精舍，洗足已，入安陀林，坐一树下，入昼正受。是比丘入昼正受时，有恶不善觉起，依贪嗜心。

At that time, a deity who resided in the Andhavana forest thought, "This bhikṣu is not good, not proper. Sitting in meditation in the Andhavana forest, he gives rise to unwholesome thoughts, with a mind attached to evil craving. I should go and rebuke him." Having thought this, he went to the bhikṣu and said, "Bhikṣu! Bhikṣu! Are you making a sore or a wart?"

时，有天神，依安陀林住止者，作是念：「此比丘不善不类，于安陀林坐禅而起不善觉，心依恶贪，我当往呵责。」作是念已，往语比丘言：「比丘！比丘！作疮疣耶？」

The bhikṣu replied, "I will treat it to make it heal."

比丘答言：「当治令愈！」

The deity said to the bhikṣu, "The sore is like an iron cauldron. How can it be healed?"

天神语比丘：「疮如铁镬，云何可复？」

The bhikṣu replied, "Right mindfulness and right wisdom are sufficient to heal it."

比丘答言：「正念正智，足能令复。」

The deity said, "Excellent! Excellent! This is the true wise one's treatment of sores. Such treatment of sores can ultimately heal them, without recurrence."

天神白言：「善哉！善哉！此是真贤治疮，如是治疮，究竟能愈，无有发时。」

At that time, the World Honored One, in the afternoon, arose from meditation and returned to Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma. He entered the assembly, spread his seat in front of the great assembly, and sat down. He addressed the bhikṣus, saying, "This morning, I put on my robe, took my alms bowl, entered the city of Śrāvastī to beg for food. After begging for food, I returned to Andhavana and sat in meditation, entering the midday samādhi. There was a bhikṣu who also begged for food and returned to Andhavana. He sat under a tree and entered the midday samādhi. That bhikṣu gave rise to unwholesome thoughts, his mind attached to evil desires. There was a deity dwelling in Andhavana who said to the bhikṣu, 'Bhikṣu! Bhikṣu! Are you creating sores?'" The above is extensively explained, up to "Thus, bhikṣu! Excellent! Excellent! This is the treatment of sores by the wise." At that time, the World Honored One then spoke a verse:

尔时，世尊晡时从禅觉，还祇树给孤独园。入僧中，于大众前敷座而坐，告诸比丘：「我今晨朝著衣持钵，入舍卫城乞食。乞食还，至安陀林坐禅，入昼正受。有一比丘亦乞食还至安陀林，坐一树下，入昼正受，而彼比丘起不善觉，心依恶贪。有天神依安陀林住，语比丘言：『比丘！比丘！作疮疣耶？』……」如上广说，乃至「『如是，比丘！善哉！善哉！此治众贤。』」尔时，世尊即说偈言：

A person creates sores and afflictions for himself, seeking worldly
desires, his mind attached to evil desires. Because of creating
sores, flies swarm and gather. Desire and lust are sores
with flies being various unwholesome thoughts. And various greedy minds
all arise from the mind. They drill and chisel the
mind of a person, seeking fame and profit. The fire
of desire burns fiercely, with deluded unwholesome thoughts. Body and
mind are emaciated day and night, far from the path
of tranquility. If the inner mind is tranquil, with definite
wisdom and clarity, there are no such sores, seeing the
peaceful path of the Buddha. The path traveled by the
noble ones, well proclaimed by the sages, the path known
by the wise, no longer experiencing various existences.

「士夫作疮疣，　　自生于苦患，愿求世间欲，　　心依于恶贪。以生疮疣故，　　蛆蝇竞来集，爱欲为疮疣，　　蛆蝇诸恶觉。及诸贪嗜心，　　皆悉从意生，钻凿士夫心，　　以求华名利。欲火转炽然，　　妄想不善觉，身心日夜羸，　　远离寂静道。若内心寂静，　　决定智明了，无有斯疮疣，　　见佛安隐路。正士所游迹，　　贤圣善宣说，明智所知道，　　不复受诸有。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully practiced it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
