SĀ 1091

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1091（一〇九一） 求德

Parallel to: SN 4.23 / SĀ² 2.30

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying in the Vipula Forest on the slopes of Mount Vebhara in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城毘婆罗山七叶树林石室中。

At that time, Venerable Kutudanta was staying in a black stone cave on the slopes of the Immortal Mountain in Rājagṛha, practicing diligently in solitude, cultivating self-benefit. He attained the liberation of the mind and realized it with the body, but he often regressed. He regressed once, twice, three, four, five, and six times, but he regained it and realized the liberation of the mind and the body, only to regress again.

时，有尊者瞿低迦，住王舍城仙人山侧黑石室中，独一思惟，不放逸行，修自饶益，时受意解脱身作证，数数退转；一、二、三、四、五、六反退，还复得，时受意解脱身作证，寻复退转。

Venerable Kutudanta thought, "I practiced diligently in solitude, cultivating self-benefit. I attained the liberation of the mind and realized it with the body, but I often regressed. Up to six times, I still regressed. I will now kill myself with a knife, so that I do not regress a seventh time."

彼尊者瞿低迦作是念：「我独一静处思惟，不放逸行，精勤修习，以自饶益，时受意解脱身作证，而复数数退转；乃至六反，犹复退转。我今当以刀自杀，莫令第七退转。」

Then Māra Pāpīyān thought, "The śramaṇa Gautama is staying in a cave in the forest of seven-leaf trees on the slopes of Mount Vipula near Rājagṛha. His disciple Kutudanta is staying in a black stone cave on the slopes of the Immortal Mountain in Rājagṛha, practicing diligently in solitude, attaining the liberation of the mind and realizing it with the body, but regressing six times. He thinks, 'I have regressed six times and regained it. Do not let me regress a seventh time. I would rather kill myself with a knife than regress a seventh time.' If that bhikṣu kills himself with a knife, do not let him do so. Leave my realm. I will now go and tell his teacher."

时，魔波旬作是念：「沙门瞿昙住王舍城毘婆罗山侧七叶树林石窟中，有弟子瞿低迦住王舍城仙人山侧黑石室中，独一静处，专精思惟，得时受意解脱身作证，六反退转，而复还得。彼作是念：『我已六反退，而复还得，莫令我第七退转，我宁以刀自杀，莫令第七退转。』若彼比丘以刀自杀者，莫令自杀，出我境界去，我今当往告彼大师。」

At that time, Pāpīyān Māra took a lute with a crystal handle and went to where the World-Honored One was. He plucked the strings and spoke a verse:

尔时，波旬执琉璃柄琵琶，诣世尊所，鼓弦说偈：

Great wisdom, great skillful means, great self-mastery, and great spiritual power, to obtain
a disciple who is blazing with energy, but now wants to take his
own life. The Great Sage should restrain him, do not let him kill
himself. How can one hear of the World-Honored Buddha, the disciples of the
true Dharma and Vinaya, studying what they cannot attain, and then taking their own lives?

「大智大方便，　　自在大神力，得炽然弟子，　　而今欲取死。大牟尼当制，　　勿令其自杀，何闻佛世尊，　　正法律声闻。学其所不得，　　而取于命终。」

At that time, after Māra had spoken this verse, the World-Honored One replied in verse:

时，魔说此偈已，世尊说偈答言：

Māra, you are negligent and come for your own sake. The
steadfast and fully accomplished one always abides in wondrous meditation. Day
and night diligently practicing, not caring for life and death. Seeing
the three realms of existence as fearful, cutting off those desires
and afflictions. Having already defeated the demon army, Gautama will attain
parinirvāṇa. Māra's mind is distressed, and his lute falls to the
ground. Having already felt inner sorrow, he immediately disappears and is not seen.

「波旬放逸种，　　以自事故来，坚固具足士，　　常住妙禅定。昼夜勤精进，　　不顾于性命，见三有可畏，　　断除彼爱欲。已摧伏魔军，　　瞿低般涅槃，波旬心忧恼，　　琵琶落于地。内怀忧戚已，　　即没而不现。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "You should come with me to the side of the mountain where the sages are, to the black stone chamber, to see how the bhikṣu Gautama killed himself with a knife."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「汝等当来，共至仙人山侧黑石室所，观瞿低迦比丘以刀自杀。」

At that time, the World-Honored One, together with many bhikṣus, went to the side of the mountain where the sages are, to the black stone chamber. Seeing that the bhikṣu Gautama had killed himself and was lying on the ground, he told the bhikṣus, "Do you see that the bhikṣu Gautama has killed himself and is lying on the ground?"

尔时，世尊与众多比丘往至仙人山侧黑石室中，见瞿低迦比丘杀身在地，告诸比丘：「汝等见此瞿低迦比丘杀身在地不？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "Yes, we see it, World-Honored One!"

诸比丘白佛：「唯然，已见，世尊！」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "Do you see that the bhikṣu Gautama is surrounded by black smoke, filling the four directions?"

佛告比丘：「汝等见瞿低迦比丘周匝遶身黑暗烟起，充满四方不？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "We see it, World-Honored One!"

比丘白佛：「已见，世尊！」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "This is the evil Māra Pāpīyas seeking the consciousness of the good man Gautama, but the bhikṣu Gautama, with an unsettled mind, killed himself with a knife."

佛告比丘：「此是恶魔波旬于瞿低迦善男子身侧，周匝求其识神，然比丘瞿低迦以不住心，执刀自杀。」

At that time, the World-Honored One gave the first prediction to the bhikṣu Gautama.

尔时，世尊为瞿低迦比丘受第一记。

At that time, Pāpīyas spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，波旬而说偈言：

Above, below, and in all directions, I search for that consciousness
but I do not see where it is. Where has Gautama gone?

「上下及诸方，　　遍求彼识神，都不见其处，　　瞿低何所之？」

At that time, the World-Honored One also spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊复说偈言：

Thus, this steadfast one, seeking nothing at all, has uprooted
the root of affection and love. Gautama has attained parinirvāṇa.

「如是坚固士，　　一切无所求，拔恩爱根本，　　瞿低般涅槃。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished. The bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
