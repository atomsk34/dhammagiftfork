SĀ 110

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 110（一一〇） 萨遮

Parallel to: MN 35 / EĀ 37.10

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying at the Monkey Pond in Vaiśālī. In the country of Vaiśālī, there was a Nirgrantha who was intelligent, wise, and skilled in all treatises. He was arrogant due to his intelligence. He had gathered many treatises, and his wisdom penetrated the subtle. When he taught the Dharma to the assembly, he surpassed all the teachers. He always thought: "Among all the śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, there is none who can rival me, even the Tathāgata can debate with me. When the teachers of treatises hear my name, their foreheads, armpits, and pores sweat. My wind of debate can bend grass and break trees, crush gold and stone, and subdue all dragons and elephants. How could there be any teachers of treatises in the human world who could match me!"

一时，佛住毘舍离猕猴池侧。毘舍离国有尼揵子，聪慧明哲，善解诸论，有聪明慢。所广集诸论，妙智入微，为众说法，超诸论师，每作是念：「诸沙门、婆罗门无敌我者，乃至如来亦能共论。诸论师辈，闻我名者，头额津腋下汗，毛孔流水；我论议风，能偃草折树，摧破金石，伏诸龙象，何况人间诸论师辈，能当我者！」

At that time, there was a bhikṣu named Aśvajit. In the morning, he put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and entered the city to beg for food with dignified deportment, looking straight ahead and walking evenly. At that time, the Nirgrantha Sāṇavāsi had some minor matters to attend to, and he went to various villages. As he was leaving the city gate, he saw from afar the bhikṣu Aśvajit. He immediately went to him and asked, "How does the Śramaṇa Gautama teach the Dharma to his disciples? With what kind of Dharma does he instruct his disciples to practice?"

时，有比丘名阿湿波誓，晨朝著衣持钵，威仪详序，端视平涉，入城乞食。尔时，萨遮尼揵子，有少缘事，诣诸聚落，从城门出，遥见比丘阿湿波誓，即诣其所，问言：「沙门瞿昙为诸弟子云何说法？以何等法教诸弟子，令其修习？」

Aśvajit said, "Householder of the Fire Clan! The World-Honored One teaches the Dharma to his disciples in this way, instructing them to practice accordingly. He says: 'Bhikṣus! Regarding form, one should contemplate the absence of self. Regarding feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness, one should contemplate the absence of self. Diligent in contemplating these five aggregates of clinging, they are like a disease, a boil, a thorn, and a killer. They are impermanent, suffering, empty, and not-self.'"

阿湿波誓言：「火种居士！世尊如是说法教诸弟子，令随修学。言：『诸比丘！于色当观无我，受、想、行、识当观无我。此五受阴勤方便观，如病、如痈、如刺、如杀，无常、苦、空、非我。』」

Having heard these words, the Nirgrantha Sāṇavāsi was displeased and said, "Aśvajit! You must have misunderstood. The Śramaṇa Gautama would never say such a thing. If the Śramaṇa Gautama did say such a thing, it would be a wrong view. I shall go to him and challenge him to stop it."

萨遮尼揵子闻此语，心不喜，作是言：「阿湿波誓！汝必误听，沙门瞿昙终不作是说。若沙门瞿昙作是说者，则是邪见，我当诣彼难诘令止。」

At that time, the Nirgrantha Sāṇavāsi went to the assembly place of the Licchavis in the village and said to the Licchavis, "Today I saw the Śramaṇa Gautama's foremost disciple named Aśvajit. We had a brief discussion. If what he said is true, I shall go to that Śramaṇa Gautama and discuss with him. Whether advancing or retreating, turning or not, it will certainly be according to my intention.

尔时，萨遮尼犍子往诣聚落，诸离车等集会之处，语诸离车言：「我今日见沙门瞿昙第一弟子，名阿湿波誓，薄共论议，若如其所说者，我当诣彼沙门瞿昙，与共论议，进却回转，必随我意。

It is like a man cutting and pulling out grass, holding the stem in his hand, shaking it in the air to remove the impurities. I will also do the same. When discussing and challenging the Śramaṇa Gautama, holding his key points, advancing or retreating, turning or not, according to his wishes, I will remove his false teachings.

「譬如士夫刈拔茇草，手执其茎，空中抖擞，除诸乱秽；我亦如是，与沙门瞿昙论议难诘，执其要领，进却回转，随其所欲，去其邪说。

It is like a wine seller holding a wine bag, pressing it to extract the clear and pure wine, removing the dregs. I will also do the same. When discussing and challenging the Śramaṇa Gautama, advancing or retreating, turning or not, taking the clear and true, removing the false teachings.

「如沽酒家执其酒囊，压取清醇，去其糟滓；我亦如是，诣沙门瞿昙论议] cf.【丽】难诘，进却回转，取其清真，去诸邪说。

It is like a mat weaver who uses a mat to hold dirty things. When wanting to sell it, he washes it with water to remove the foul smell. I will also do the same. When going to the place of the Śramaṇa Gautama to discuss and debate, advancing or retreating, holding the main points, removing the impure teachings.

「如织席师，以席盛诸秽物，欲市卖时，以水洗泽，去诸臭秽；我亦如是，诣沙门瞿昙所，与共论议，进却回转，执其纲领，去诸秽说。

It is like a royal elephant trainer who leads a drunken elephant into deep water to wash its body, four limbs, ears, and nose, bathing it all over to remove the coarse impurities. I will also do the same. When going to the place of the Śramaṇa Gautama to discuss and debate, advancing or retreating, freely holding the main points, removing the impure teachings. You Licchavis should also go together to observe his gains and losses.

「譬如王家调象之师，牵大醉象，入深水中，洗其身体、四支、耳、鼻，周遍沐浴，去诸麁秽；我亦如是，诣沙门瞿昙所，论议难诘，进却回转，随意自在，执其要领，去诸秽说。汝诸离车，亦应共往观其得失。」

Among them, some Licchavis said, "It is impossible for Sācatya to be able to discuss and debate with the Śramaṇa Gautama.

中有离车作如是言：「若萨遮尼犍子能与沙门瞿昙共论议者，无有是处。」

Some said, "Sācatya is intelligent and sharp-witted, he can discuss and debate.

复有说言：「萨遮尼犍子聪慧利根，能共论议。」

At that time, five hundred Licchavis went together with Sācatya to where the Buddha was, for the sake of discussing and debating.

时有五百离车与萨遮尼犍子共诣佛所，为论议故。

At that time, the World-Honored One was sitting under a tree in the great forest, abiding in the heavenly abode. At that time, many bhikṣus were walking in the forest outside their rooms. They saw Sācatya coming from afar, gradually approaching the bhikṣus, and asked the bhikṣus, "Where is Śramaṇa Gautama staying?"

尔时，世尊于大林中，坐一树下，住于天住。时，有众多比丘出房外林中经行，遥见萨遮尼犍子来，渐渐诣诸比丘所，问诸比丘言：「沙门瞿昙住在何所？」

The bhikṣus replied, "He is in the great forest, sitting under a tree, abiding in the heavenly abode."

比丘答言：「在大林中，依一树下，住于天住。」

Sācatya then went to where the Buddha was, paid respects, and sat to one side. The Licchavi elders also went to where the Buddha was. Some paid respects, some joined their palms and asked after his well-being. After asking after his well-being, they stood to one side.

萨遮尼犍子即诣佛所，恭敬问讯，于一面坐。诸离车长者亦诣佛所，有恭敬者，有合掌问讯者，问讯已，于一面住。

At that time, Sācatya said to the Buddha, "I heard the Gautama teach the Dharma and instruct his disciples like this - he instructs his disciples to observe the selflessness of form, feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness, and to diligently observe these five aggregates as being like a disease, a boil, a thorn, a killer, impermanent, suffering, empty, and selfless. Is this the teaching of the Gautama, or is it the slander of the transmitters? Is it as it is said, or not as it is said? Is it said according to the Dharma, or in accordance with the Dharma? Is there no difference in tolerance to come and challenge, causing one to fall into defeat?"

时，萨遮尼犍子白佛言：「我闻瞿昙作如是说法，作如是教授诸弟子——教诸弟子于色观察无我，受、想、行、识观察无我，此五受阴勤方便观察，如病、如痈、如刺、如杀，无常、苦、空、非我。——为是瞿昙有如是教，为是传者毁瞿昙耶？如说说耶？不如说说耶？如法说耶？法次法说耶？无有异忍来相难诘，令堕负处耶？」

The Buddha told Sācatya, "As you have heard, it is as it is said, as it is according to the Dharma, and in accordance with the Dharma. It is not slanderous, and there is no challenging question that can cause one to fall into defeat. Why is that? I truly teach the Dharma to my disciples like that. I truly always instruct my disciples to follow the Dharma teachings and observe the selflessness of form, feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness. I observe these five aggregates as being like a disease, a boil, a thorn, a killer, impermanent, suffering, empty, and selfless."

佛告萨遮尼犍子：「如汝所闻，彼如说说、如法说、法次法说，非为谤毁，亦无难问令堕负处。所以者何？我实为诸弟子如是说法，我实常教诸弟子，令随顺法教，令观色无我。受、想、行、识无我，观此五受阴如病，如痈、如刺、如杀，无常、苦、空、非我。」

Sācatya said to the Buddha, "Gautama! I will now give an analogy."

萨遮尼犍子白佛言：「瞿昙！我今当说譬。」

The Buddha told Sācatya, "It is the right time."

佛告萨遮尼犍子：「宜知是时。」

"It is like all worldly activities relying on the earth. In this way, form is the self and person, from which good and evil arise; feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness are the self and person, from which good and evil arise. Moreover, it is like the human realm, the divine realm, herbs, and trees, all growing and flourishing based on the earth; in this way, form is the self and person, feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness are the self and person."

「譬如世间一切所作皆依于地。如是色是我人，善恶从生；受、想、行、识是我人，善恶从生。又复譬如人界、神界、药草、树木，皆依于地而得生长；如是色是我人，受、想、行、识是我人。」

The Buddha said to the householder Kāśyapa, "You say that form is the self and person, and feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness are the self and person, is that so?"

佛告火种居士：「汝言色是我人，受、想、行、识是我人耶？」

He replied, "So it is, Gautama! Form is the self and person, and feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness are the self and person. All these people say this."

答言：「如是，瞿昙！色是我人，受、想、行、识是我人。此等诸众悉作是说。」

The Buddha said to the householder Kāśyapa, "First establish your own thesis, and then lead others?"

佛告火种居士：「且立汝论本，用引众人为？」

The son of the Śākyas said to the Buddha, "Form is indeed the self and person."

萨遮尼犍子白佛言：「色实是我人。」

The Buddha said to the householder Kāśyapa, "I will now ask you, please answer as you wish. For example, if a king in his own country has someone who commits a crime, he can kill, bind, banish, whip, or cut off their hands and feet; if someone has merit, he can bestow upon them elephants, horses, carriages, cities, and treasures. Is this so?"

佛告火种居士：「我今问汝，随意答我。譬如国王，于自国土有罪过者，若杀、若缚、若摈、若鞭、断绝手足；若有功者，赐其象马、车乘、城邑、财宝，悉能尔不？」

He replied, "It is so, Gautama!"

答言：「能尔。瞿昙！」

The Buddha said to the householder Kāśyapa, "Can all masters be self-willed?"

佛告火种居士：「凡是主者，悉得自在不？」

He replied, "It is so, Gautama!"

答言：「如是，瞿昙！」

The Buddha said to the householder Kāśyapa, "You say that form is me, and feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness are me. Can you be self-willed and make them so, or not so?"

佛告火种居士：「汝言色是我，受、想、行、识即是我，得随意自在，令彼如是，不令如是耶？」

At that time, the son of the Sāṃkhya teacher remained silent.

时，萨遮尼犍子默然而住。

The Buddha said to the householder Kāśyapa, "Speak quickly! Why are you silent?"

佛告火种居士：「速说！速说！何故默然？」

The Sāṃkhya teacher's son remained silent again.

如是再三，萨遮尼犍子犹故默然。

At that time, a Vajrapāṇi spirit held a vajra staff with blazing flames in the sky above the head of the Sāṃkhya teacher's son, saying, "The World-Honored One has asked you three times, why do you not answer? I will smash your head into seven pieces with this vajra staff."

时，有金刚力鬼神持金刚杵，猛火炽然，在虚空中临萨遮尼犍子头上，作是言：「世尊再三问，汝何故不答？我当以金刚杵碎破汝头，令作七分。」

By the Buddha's spiritual power, only the Sāṃkhya teacher's son saw the Vajra spirit, while the others did not see it. The Sāṃkhya teacher's son was greatly terrified and said to the Buddha, "No, Gautama!"

佛神力故，唯令萨遮尼犍子见金刚神，余众不见。萨遮尼犍子得大恐怖，白佛言：「不尔。瞿昙！」

The Buddha said to the Sāṃkhya teacher's son, "Think carefully and then explain. You previously said in the assembly that form is me, and feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness are me. Do you now say that? It contradicts your previous statements. You previously always said, 'Form is me, and feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness are me.' Fire-born householder! I now ask you, is form permanent or impermanent?"

佛告萨遮尼犍子：「徐徐思惟，然后解说。汝先于众中说色是我，受、想、行、识是我，而今言不？前后相违。汝先常说言：『色是我，受、想、行、识是我。』火种居士！我今问汝，色为常耶？为无常耶？」

He replied, "Impermanent, Gautama!"

答言：「无常。瞿昙！」

He further asked, "Is what is impermanent suffering?"

复问：「无常者，是苦耶？」

He replied, "It is suffering, Gautama!"

答言：「是苦。瞿昙！」

He further asked, "Is what is impermanent and suffering a changeable phenomenon? Can the learned noble disciples see self, other, or characteristics in it?"

复问：「无常、苦者，是变易法，多闻圣弟子宁于中见我、异我、相在不？」

He replied, "No, Gautama!"

答曰：「不也，瞿昙！」

The same is said of feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness.

受、想、行、识亦如是说。

The Buddha said to the householder Huizhong, "Think carefully before you speak."

佛告火种居士：「汝好思而后说。」

He further asked the householder Huizhong, "If one has not yet abandoned desire, craving, recollection, affection, and thirst for form, and that form changes or becomes different, will one then give rise to sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress?"

复问火种居士：「若于色未离贪、未离欲、未离念、未离爱、未离渴，彼色若变、若异，当生忧、悲、恼、苦不？」

He replied, "Yes, Gautama!"

答曰：「如是，瞿昙！」

The same can be said for feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness.

受、想、行、识亦如是说。

He further asked, "Householder Kāśyapa, when one is free from craving, desire, thought, affection, and thirst for form, and that form changes or transforms, does one not give rise to sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress?"

复问：「火种居士！于色离贪、离欲、离念、离爱、离渴，彼色若变、若异，则不生忧、悲、恼、苦耶？」

He replied, "So it is, Gautama! It is true and not otherwise."

答曰：「如是。瞿昙！如实无异。」

The same can be said for feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness.

受、想、行、识亦如是说。

"Householder, it is like a person whose body is afflicted with many sufferings, always accompanied by suffering. If that suffering is not cut off or abandoned, can they attain happiness?"

「火种居士！譬如士夫身婴众苦，常与苦俱，彼苦不断不舍，当得乐不？」

He replied, "No, Gautama!"

答言：「不也，瞿昙！」

So it is, Householder! The body is afflicted with many sufferings, always accompanied by suffering. If that suffering is not cut off or abandoned, happiness cannot be attained. Householder, it is like a person carrying an axe into the forest, seeking solid wood. Seeing a large and straight banana tree, they cut off its roots and leaves, peeled off its bark, and exhausted it, but there was no solidity at all. Householder, you are also like this, setting up your own thesis. I now seek the true meaning well, but there is no solidity at all, like a banana tree. Yet you dare to speak in this assembly. I do not see any śramaṇa or brāhmaṇa who can discuss with the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, without being defeated. Yet you say, 'My wind of debate can bend grass and break trees, destroy gold and stone, subdue dragons and elephants, and make their foreheads sweat, their armpits sweat, and their pores drip.' Now you yourself debate your own meaning without establishing it. The characteristics you boasted of before to subdue others, you now take for yourself, but you cannot move a single hair of the Tathāgata.

「如是，火种居士！身婴众苦，常与苦俱，彼苦不断、不舍，不得乐也。火种居士！譬如士夫持斧入山，求坚实材。见芭蕉树洪大佣直，即断其根叶，剽剥其皮，乃至穷尽，都无坚实。火种居士！汝亦如是，自立论端。我今善求真实之义，都无坚实，如芭蕉树也，而于此众中敢有所说。我不见沙门、婆罗门中，所知、所见能与如来、应、等正觉所知、所见共论议，不摧伏者。而便自说：『我论议风，偃草折树，能破金石，调伏龙象，要能令彼额津腋汗，毛孔水流。』汝今自论己义而不自立，先所夸说能伏彼相，今尽自取，而不能动如来一毛。」

At that time, the World-Honored One, in the midst of the great assembly, lifted up his upper robe to reveal his chest, saying, "Look, can you move a single hair of the Tathāgata?"

尔时，世尊于大众中，被欝多罗僧，现胸而示：「汝等试看，能动如来一毛以不？」

At that time, the son of the Sāṃkhya teacher was silent, his head lowered, ashamed and pale. At that time, there was an outcaste named Tumoqie in the assembly. He rose from his seat, adjusted his robes, joined his palms, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Please listen to my analogy.

尔时，萨遮尼犍子默然低头，惭愧失色。尔时，众中有一离车，名突目佉，从座起，整衣服，合掌白佛言：「世尊！听我说譬。」

The Buddha told Tumoqie, "It is the right time."

佛告突目佉：「宜知是时。」

Tumoqie said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! It is like someone holding a bushel or a peck, taking two or three bushels from a large heap of grain. Now this son of the Sāṃkhya teacher is also like this. World-Honored One! It is like a wealthy elder with many possessions suddenly committing an offense, and all his wealth and possessions are confiscated by the king. The son of the Sāṃkhya teacher is also like this. All his eloquence is absorbed by the Tathāgata.

突目佉白佛言：「世尊！譬如有人执持斗斛，于大聚谷中，取二三斛，今此萨遮尼犍子亦复如是。世尊！譬如长者巨富多财，忽有罪过，一切财物悉入王家，萨遮尼犍子亦复如是。所有才辩悉为如来之所摄受。

"It is like a large body of water near a city or village, where men, women, and children all go to play in the water. They catch crabs and cut off their legs, then place them on land. Without legs, they cannot return to the water. The son of the Sāṃkhya teacher is also like this. All his eloquence is cut off by the Tathāgata and he will never dare to come to the Tathāgata again to debate."

「譬如城邑聚落边有大水，男女大小悉入水戏，取水中蟹，截断其足，置于陆地，以无足故，不能还复入于大水。萨遮尼犍子亦复如是。诸有才辩悉为如来之所断截，终不复敢重诣如来命敌论议。」

At that time, the son of the Sāṃkhya teacher was filled with anger and cursed and spat at Tumoqie, saying, "You coarse and ignorant one! How dare you speak without thinking! I am debating with the śramaṇa Gautama myself. What business is it of yours?"

尔时，萨遮尼犍子忿怒炽盛，骂唾突目佉离车言：「汝麁疏物！不审谛何为其鸣？吾自与沙门瞿昙论，何豫汝事？」

After rebuking Tumoqie, the son of the Sāṃkhya teacher said to the Buddha, "Forget about those vulgar and contemptible words. I now have something else to ask."

萨遮尼犍子呵骂突目佉已，复白佛言：「置彼凡辈鄙贱之说，我今别有所问。」

The Buddha told the son of the Sāṃkhya teacher, "Ask as you please. I will answer according to your questions."

佛告萨遮尼犍子：「恣汝所问，当随问答。」

"How does Gautama teach his disciples to be free from doubt?"

「云何？瞿昙！为弟子说法，令离疑惑？」

The Buddha said to the householder of the fire clan, "I teach my disciples to truly observe all existing forms, whether past, future, or present, internal or external, coarse or subtle, beautiful or ugly, far or near, as not being the self, not different from the self, or not in the self. The same applies to feelings, perceptions, mental formations, and consciousness. They must see the traces of their practice, not destroy them, and be able to accomplish them. They must be disgusted, know, and see, and guard the gate of immortality. Although they may not fully attain everything, they still head towards nirvāṇa. Such disciples can be free from doubt according to my teachings."

佛告火种居士：「我为诸弟子说诸所有色，若过去、若未来、若现在，若内、若外，若麁、若细，若好、若丑，若远、若近，彼一切如实观察非我、非异我、不相在；受、想、行、识亦复如是。彼学必见迹不断坏，堪任成就，厌离知见，守甘露门，虽非一切悉得究竟，且向涅槃。如是弟子从我教法，得离疑惑。」

He further asked, "Gautama! How do you teach your disciples to attain the exhaustion of all taints, the non-outflow, liberation of the mind, liberation through wisdom, and to know and realize for themselves in this very life: 'My birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and I know that I will not be subject to any future lives'?"

复问：「瞿昙！复云何教诸弟子，于佛法得尽诸漏、无漏，心解脱、慧解脱，现法自知作证：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』？」

The Buddha said to the householder of the fire clan, "It is precisely through this teaching that all existing forms, whether past, future, or present, internal or external, coarse or subtle, beautiful or ugly, far or near, are truly known as not being the self, not different from the self, or not in the self. The same applies to feelings, perceptions, mental formations, and consciousness. At that time, they accomplish three kinds of unsurpassed - unsurpassed wisdom, unsurpassed liberation, and unsurpassed liberation through wisdom and insight. Having accomplished these three kinds of unsurpassed, they respectfully honor and make offerings to the great teacher as if he were the Buddha. The World-Honored One awakens to all dharmas and uses these dharmas to tame his disciples, enabling them to attain peace, fearlessness, taming, tranquility, and ultimate nirvāṇa. The World-Honored One teaches the Dharma for the sake of nirvāṇa. Householder of the fire clan! My disciples attain the exhaustion of all taints in this teaching, attain liberation of the mind, liberation through wisdom, and know and realize for themselves in this very life: 'My birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and I know that I will not be subject to any future lives'."

佛告火种居士：「正以此法，诸所有色，若过去、若未来、若现在，若内、若外，若麁、若细，若好、若丑，若远、若近，彼一切如实知非我、非异我、不相在；受、想、行、识亦复如是。彼于尔时成就三种无上——智无上、解脱无上、解脱知见无上。成就三种无上已，于大师所恭敬、尊重、供养如佛。世尊觉一切法，即以此法调伏弟子，令得安隐、令得无畏、调伏寂静、究竟涅槃。世尊为涅槃故，为弟子说法。火种居士！我诸弟子于此法中，得尽诸漏，得心解脱，得慧解脱，于现法中自知作证：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』」

Sāṇakavāsa said to the Buddha, "Gautama! It is like a strong man with a sharp blade descending in disorder, which can still be avoided; but Gautama's discussion is difficult to escape. It is like a venomous snake, which can still be avoided; a raging fire in a vast wilderness, which can still be avoided; a ferocious drunken elephant, which can also be avoided; a mad and hungry lion, which can all be avoided; but it is difficult to escape from the discussion in the hands of the śramaṇa Gautama. I am not an ordinary person, a frivolous and contemptible man. I am not fully equipped for discussion. It is for the sake of discussion that I have come to Gautama.

萨遮尼犍子白佛言：「瞿昙！犹如壮夫，锋刃乱下，犹可得免；瞿昙论手，难可得脱。如盛毒蛇，犹可得避；旷泽猛火，犹可得避；凶恶醉象，亦可得免；狂饿师子，悉可得免；沙门瞿昙论议手中，难可得脱。非我凡品，轻躁鄙夫，论具不备，以论议故，来诣瞿昙。

Śramaṇa Gautama! This Vaiśālī is a prosperous and peaceful country. There are the Śītavana stūpa, the lacquer aśoka tree stūpa, the many sons stūpa, the Gautama at the Koṭigāma stūpa, the Brahmā upholds stūpa, the abandoning of heavy burdens stūpa, and the Mālaya stūpa. May the World-Honored One find peace and happiness in this Vaiśālī country. May the devas, māras, Brahmā, śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, and all the world always be able to pay respect, serve, and make offerings to the World-Honored One. May these devas, māras, Brahmā, śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, and all the world find peace and happiness for a long time. Please stay here. Tomorrow, together with the great assembly, accept my humble meal.

「沙门瞿昙！此毘舍离丰乐国土，有遮波梨支提、漆庵罗树支提、多子支提、瞿昙在拘楼陀支提、婆罗受持支提、舍重担支提、力士宝冠支提。世尊！当安乐于此毘舍离国，诸天、魔、梵、沙门、婆罗门，及诸世间，于世尊所，常得恭敬、奉事、供养，令此诸天、魔、梵、沙门、婆罗门，长夜安乐。唯愿止此，明朝与诸大众，受我薄食。」

At that time, the World-Honored One silently accepted. Sāṇakavāsa, knowing that the Buddha had silently accepted the invitation, was delighted and followed the Buddha's wishes. He rose from his seat and left.

尔时，世尊默然而许。时，萨遮尼犍子知佛世尊默然受请已，欢喜随喜，从座起去。

At that time, Sāṇakavāsa spoke to the Licchavis on the road, saying: "I have already invited Śramaṇa Gautama and the great assembly to a meal. Each of you should prepare a pot of food and bring it to me."

尔时，萨遮尼犍子于彼道中，语诸离车：「我已请沙门瞿昙及诸大众，供设饭食，汝等人各办一釜食，送至我所。」

The Licchavis each returned to their homes, prepared the food overnight, and brought it to Sāṇakavāsa in the morning. Sāṇakavāsa swept and arranged the seats in the morning, prepared clean water, and sent a messenger to the Buddha, saying: "The time has come."

诸离车各还其家，星夜供办，晨朝送至萨遮尼犍子所。萨遮尼犍子晨朝洒扫敷座，供办净水，遣使诣佛，白言：「时到。」

At that time, the World-Honored One, together with the great assembly, put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and went to where Sāṇakavāsa was. The great assembly sat in front. Sāṇakavāsa personally offered pure food and drink, satisfying the great assembly. After eating, they washed their bowls. Sāṇakavāsa, knowing that the Buddha had finished eating and washing his bowl, took a low seat and sat in front of the Buddha. At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse of rejoicing for Sāṇakavāsa, saying:

尔时，世尊与诸大众，著衣持钵，往萨遮尼犍子所，大众前坐。萨遮尼犍子自手奉施清净饮食，充足大众。食已，洗钵竟。萨遮尼犍子知佛食竟，洗钵已，取一卑床于佛前坐。尔时，世尊为萨遮尼犍子说随喜偈言：

Among all great assemblies, offering fire is the best; among the Veda
scriptures, the Brāhmaṇa is the best; among humans, the king is the
best; among rivers and seas, the ocean is the best; among stars
the moon is the best; among days, the sun is the best
among the devas and humans of the ten directions, the Perfectly Enlightened
One is the best.

「于诸大会中，　　奉火为其最；闱陀经典中，　　婆毘谛为最；人中王为最，　　诸河海为最，诸星月为最，　　诸明日为最，十方天人中，　　等正觉为最。」

At that time, the World-Honored One, for Sāṇakavāsa, spoke the Dharma in various ways, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting him, and then returned to his original place.

尔时，世尊为萨遮尼犍子种种说法，示、教、照、喜已，还归本处。

At that time, the bhikṣus on the road discussed together: "The five hundred Licchavis each prepare food and drink for Sāṇakavāsa. Where will those Licchavis obtain merit? Where will Sāṇakavāsa obtain merit?"

时，诸比丘于彼道中众共论议：「五百离车各为萨遮尼犍子供办饮食，彼诸离车于何得福？萨遮尼犍子于何得福？」

At that time, the bhikṣus returned to their own abodes, took up their robes and bowls, washed their feet, went to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at his feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha: "World-Honored One! We were discussing on the road just now: The five hundred Licchavis prepare food and drink for Sāṇakavāsa to make offerings to the World-Honored One and the great assembly. Where will those Licchavis obtain merit? Where will Sāṇakavāsa obtain merit?"

尔时，诸比丘还自住处，举衣钵，洗足已，至世尊所，头面礼足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！我等向于路中自共论议，五百离车为萨遮尼犍子供办饮食，供养世尊、诸大众。彼诸离车于何得福？萨遮尼犍子于何得福？」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus: "Those Licchavis prepare food and drink for Sāṇakavāsa. They obtain merit through the connection with Sāṇakavāsa. Sāṇakavāsa obtains merit through the Buddha's merits. Those Licchavis obtain the result of giving with attachment, anger, and delusion as the cause. Sāṇakavāsa obtains the result of giving without attachment, anger, and delusion as the cause."

佛告诸比丘：「彼诸离车供办饮食，为萨遮尼犍子，于萨遮尼犍子所因缘得福，萨遮尼犍子得福佛功德。彼诸离车得施有贪、恚、痴因缘果报，萨遮尼犍子得施无贪、恚、痴因缘果报。」
