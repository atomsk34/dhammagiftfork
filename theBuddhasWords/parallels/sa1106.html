SĀ 1106

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1106（一一〇六） 以何因

Parallel to: SN 11.12 / SĀ² 2.35

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying at the Monkey Pond in the city of Vesālī, in a two-storied lecture hall.

一时，佛住鞞舍离国猕猴池侧重阁讲堂。

At that time, a certain bhikṣu came to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! For what reason is Śakra Devānām-Indra called Śakra Devānām-Indra?"

时，有异比丘来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退住一面，白佛言：「世尊！何因、何缘释提桓因名释提桓因？」

The Buddha told the bhikṣu, "When Śakra Devānām-Indra was a human, he practiced giving generously. To śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, the poor, the destitute, those seeking life, travelers, and beggars, he gave food, drink, money, grain, silk, flowers, incense, ornaments, bedding, and lamps. Because of his ability, he is called Śakra Devānām-Indra."

佛告比丘：「释提桓因本为人时，行于顿施；沙门、婆罗门、贫穷、困苦、求生行路乞，施以饮食、钱财、谷、帛、华香、严具、床卧、灯明，以堪能故，名释提桓因。」

The bhikṣu again asked the Buddha, "World-Honored One! For what reason is Śakra Devānām-Indra also called Vṛtraghna?"

比丘复白佛言：「世尊！何因、何缘故，释提桓因复名富兰陀罗？」

The Buddha told the bhikṣu, "When Śakra Devānām-Indra was a human, he often practiced giving clothing, food, drink, and even lamps. For this reason, he is called Vṛtraghna."

佛告比丘：「彼释提桓因本为人时，数数行施衣被、饮食，乃至灯明。以是因缘，故名富兰陀罗。」

The bhikṣu again asked the Buddha, "For what reason is he also called Māghavam?"

比丘复白佛言：「何因、何缘故，复名摩伽婆。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣu, "When Śakra Devānām-Indra was a human, his name was Māghavam. Therefore, Śakra Devānām-Indra is named Māghavam after his original name."

佛告比丘：「彼释提桓因本为人时，名摩伽婆故，释提桓因即以本名，名摩伽婆。」

The bhikṣu again asked the Buddha, "For what reason is he also called Sāpāya?"

比丘复白佛言：「何因、何缘复名娑婆婆？」

The Buddha told the bhikṣu, "When Śakra Devānām-Indra was a human, he often used his Vāsa robe to make offerings and pay respects. For this reason, Śakra Devānām-Indra is named Sāpāya."

佛告比丘：「彼释提桓因本为人时，数以婆诜私衣布施供养。以是因缘故，释提桓因名娑婆婆。」

The bhikṣu again asked the Buddha, "World-Honored One! For what reason is Śakra Devānām-Indra also named Kauśika?"

比丘复白佛言：「世尊！何因、何缘释提桓因复名憍尸迦？」

The Buddha told the bhikṣu, "When Śakra Devānām-Indra was a human, he was a member of the Kauśika clan. For this reason, Śakra Devānām-Indra is also named Kauśika."

佛告比丘：「彼释提桓因本为人时，为憍尸族姓人。以是因缘故，彼释提桓因复名憍尸迦。」

The bhikṣu asked the Buddha, "World-Honored One! For what reason is Śakra Devānām-Indra named Śacīpati?"

比丘问佛言：「世尊！何因、何缘彼释提桓因名舍脂钵低？」

The Buddha told the bhikṣu, "The asura woman named Śacī is Śakra Devānām-Indra's first heavenly queen. Therefore, Śakra Devānām-Indra is named Śacīpati."

佛告比丘：「彼阿修罗女名曰舍脂，为天帝释第一天后，是故帝释名舍脂钵低。」

The bhikṣu said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! For what reason is Śakra Devānām-Indra also named Thousand-Eyed?"

比丘白佛言：「世尊！何因、何缘释提桓因复名千眼？」

The Buddha told the bhikṣu, "When Śakra Devānām-Indra was a human, he was intelligent and wise. In a single sitting, he contemplated a thousand kinds of meanings, observing and weighing them. For this reason, that heavenly king is also named Thousand-Eyed."

佛告比丘：「彼释提桓因本为人时，聪明智慧，于一坐间，思千种义，观察称量。以是因缘，彼天帝释复名千眼。」

The bhikṣu said to the Buddha, "For what reason is Śakra Devānām-Indra also named Indratīra?"

比丘白佛：「何因、何缘彼释提桓因复名因提利？」

The Buddha told the bhikṣu, "That heavenly king is the king and lord of the thirty-three heavens. For this reason, that heavenly king is named Indratīra."

佛告比丘：「彼天帝释于诸三十三天，为王为主。以是因缘故，彼天帝释名因提利。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣu, "That Śakra Devānām-Indra, when he was a human, upheld seven kinds of precepts. For this reason, he became Śakra Devānām-Indra. What are the seven? Śakra Devānām-Indra, when he was a human, honored his parents; up to practicing equal giving. These are the seven kinds of precepts. For this reason, he became Śakra Devānām-Indra."

佛告比丘：「然彼释提桓因本为人时，受持七种受。以是因缘，得天帝释。何等为七？释提桓因本为人时，供养父母；乃至等行惠施，是为七种受。以是因缘，为天帝释。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, extensively as above.

尔时，世尊即说偈言……如上广说。

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished. The bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
