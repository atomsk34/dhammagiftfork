SĀ 1110

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1110（一一一〇） 缚系

Parallel to: SN 11.4 / SĀ² 2.39 / EĀ 34.8

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "In the past, there was a Śakra Devānām-Indra. The gods and asuras were arrayed in battle. Śakra said to the thirty-three gods, 'Today, if the gods win the battle against the asuras, and the asuras are defeated, we should capture the asura king Vemacitrin alive, bind him with five ropes, and bring him back to the heavenly palace.' The asura king Vemacitrin told the asura assembly, 'Today, if the asuras win the battle against the gods, and the gods are defeated, we should capture Śakra Devānām-Indra alive, bind him with five ropes, and bring him back to the asura palace.' When the battle began, the gods won and the asuras were defeated.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「过去世时，有天帝释，天、阿修罗对阵欲战。释提桓因语三十三天众言：『今日诸天与阿修罗军战，诸天得胜，阿修罗不如者，当生擒毘摩质多罗阿修罗王，以五系缚，将还天宫。』毘摩质多罗阿修罗王告阿修罗众：『今日诸天、阿修罗共战，若阿修罗胜，诸天不如者，当生擒释提桓因，以五系缚，将还阿修罗宫。』当其战时，诸天得胜，阿修罗不如。

At that time, the gods captured the asura king Vemacitrin, bound him with five ropes, and brought him back to the heavenly palace. They bound him in front of the gate of Śakra's hall of judgment. When Śakra entered and exited through this gate, the asura king Vemacitrin, bound at the side of the gate, angrily cursed.

「时，彼诸天捉得毘摩质多罗阿修罗王，以五系缚，将还天宫，缚在帝释断法殿前门下。帝释从此门入出之时，毘摩质多罗阿修罗缚在门侧瞋恚骂詈。

At that time, Śakra's charioteer saw the asura king bound with five ropes at the side of the gate, angrily cursing whenever Śakra entered and exited. Having seen this, he spoke a verse to Śakra, saying:

「时，帝释御者见阿修罗王，身被五缚，在于门侧，帝释出入之时辄瞋恚骂詈。见已即便说偈，白帝释言：

Does Śakra now fear him, or is his strength insufficient?
He can endure the asura cursing in front of him.

「『释今为畏彼，　　为力不足耶，能忍阿修罗，　　面前而骂辱。』

Śakra replied:

「释即答言：

I endure not out of fear, nor because of insufficient
strength. How can a wise person be opposed by a fool?

「『不以畏故忍，　　亦非力不足，何有黠慧人，　　而与愚夫对。』

The charioteer said again:

「御者复白言：

'If one only practices patience, there will be a deficiency in
affairs. The foolish will say that one practices patience out of
fear. Therefore, one should use wisdom to subdue foolishness by means of suffering.'

「『若但行忍者，　　于事则有阙，愚痴者当言，　　畏怖故行忍。是故当苦治，　　以智制愚痴。』

Indra replied:

「帝释答言：

'I always observe them, the subduer of those foolish men. Seeing
the foolish ones with intense anger, the wise subdue them with
silence. Non-force becomes force, that is the force of their foolishness
Foolishness goes against the Dharma, there is no path for it
If one has great strength and can endure the weak, that
is the highest endurance. Without strength, how can there be endurance?
When others insult and revile, the strong can endure, that is
the highest endurance. Without strength, what is there to endure? For
oneself and others, well-guarding the great terror, knowing their intense anger
one still keeps silent. In both meanings, one is fully equipped
benefiting oneself and others. Those who say "foolish men" do so
because they do not see the Dharma. The foolish think that
endurance is superior, further increasing their harsh words. Not knowing how
to endure their insults, one always gains victory over them. Enduring
those who are superior to oneself is called fearful endurance. Enduring
those who are equal to oneself is called endurance in disputes
Enduring those who are inferior to oneself is the highest endurance.

「『我常观察彼，　　制彼愚夫者，见愚者瞋盛，　　智以静默伏。非力而为力，　　是彼愚痴力，愚痴违远法，　　于道则无有。若使有大力，　　能忍于劣者，是则为上忍。　　无力何有忍？于他极骂辱，　　大力者能忍，是则为上忍。　　无力何所忍？于己及他人，　　善护大恐怖，知彼瞋恚盛，　　还自守静默。于二义俱备，　　自利亦利他。谓言愚夫者，　　以不见法故，愚夫谓胜忍，　　重增其恶口，未知忍彼骂，　　于彼常得胜。于胜己行忍，　　是名恐怖忍。于等者行忍，　　是名忍诤忍。于劣者行忍，　　是则为上忍。』」

The Buddha told the monks, "Sakra Devendra is the king of the Thirty-three Heavens, always practicing patience and praising patience. You monks, having renounced the household life with right faith, should also practice patience and praise patience. You should learn this!"

佛告诸比丘：「释提桓因于三十三天为自在王，常行忍辱，赞叹于忍。汝等比丘正信非家，出家学道，亦应如是行忍，赞叹于忍，应当学！」

After the Buddha spoke this sūtra, the monks who heard what the Buddha taught rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
