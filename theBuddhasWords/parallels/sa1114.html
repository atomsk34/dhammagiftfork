SĀ 1114

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1114（一一一四） 须毘罗

Parallel to: SN 11.1 / SĀ² 2.43

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "In the past, the Asura King raised four kinds of troops: elephant troops, cavalry, chariot troops, and infantry. At that time, the Thirty-three Heavens wanted to fight with them. Then, Śakra, King of the Gods, heard that the Asura King had raised four kinds of troops - elephant troops, cavalry, chariot troops, and infantry - and was coming to fight with them. Having heard this, he immediately told the god Suyāma, 'Do you know, Suyāma? The Asuras have raised four kinds of troops - elephant troops, cavalry, chariot troops, and infantry - and want to fight with the Thirty-three Heavens. Suyāma, you should order the Thirty-three Heavens to raise four kinds of troops - elephant troops, cavalry, chariot troops, and infantry - to fight with those Asuras.' At that time, the god Suyāma received Śakra's order and returned to his own heavenly palace, but he was slow and indulgent, not diligent in his efforts.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「过去世时，阿修罗王兴四种兵——象兵、马兵、车兵、步兵。时，三十三天欲共鬪战。时，天帝释闻阿修罗王兴四种兵——象兵、马兵、车兵、步兵，来欲共战。闻已，即告宿毘梨天子言：『阿公知不？阿修罗兴四种兵——象兵、马兵、车兵、步兵，欲与三十三天共战。阿公可勅三十三天兴四种兵——象兵、马兵、车兵、步兵，与彼阿修罗共战。』尔时，宿毘梨天子受帝释教，还自天宫，慢缓宽纵，不勤方便。

The Asura troops have already come out on the road. Śakra, having heard this, again told the god Suyāma, 'Suyāma! The Asura army is already on the road. Suyāma, you should quickly order the raising of the four kinds of troops to fight with the Asuras.' The god Suyāma, having received Śakra's order, then returned to his palace, but was lazy and indulgent.

「阿修罗众已出在道路，帝释闻已，复告宿毘梨天子：『阿公！阿修罗军已在道路，阿公可速告令起四种兵与阿修罗战。』宿毘梨天子受帝释教已，即复还宫，懈怠宽纵。

At that time, the Asura King's army was already approaching. Śakra, having heard that the Asura army was already near the road, again told the god Suyāma, 'Suyāma! Do you know? The Asura army is already near the road. Suyāma, you should quickly tell the gods to raise the four kinds of troops.'

「时，阿修罗王军已垂至，释提桓因闻阿修罗军已在近路，复告宿毘梨天子：『阿公知不？阿修罗军已在近路，阿公！速告诸天起四种兵。』

At that time, the god Suyāma spoke a verse, saying:

「时，宿毘梨天子即说偈言：

If there is a place that is not arisen, unconditioned, peaceful and
blissful, one who attains such a place has no action and no
sorrow. Grant me such a place, so that I may attain peace and tranquility.

「『若有不起处，　　无为安隐乐，得如是处者，　　无作亦无忧。当与我是处，　　令我得安隐。』

At that time, Śakra replied in verse,

「尔时，帝释说偈答言：

If there is a place that is not arisen, unconditioned, peaceful and
blissful, one who attains such a place has no action and no
sorrow. If you have attained such a place, you should also take me there.

「『若有不起处，　　无为安隐乐，若得是处者，　　无作亦无忧。汝得是处者，　　亦应将我去。』

The god of the past, Vīra, also spoke in verse:

「宿毘梨天子复说偈言：

If there is a place without skillful means, not arisen, peaceful and
blissful, one who attains such a place has no action and no
sorrow. Give me that place, so that I may attain peace and bliss.

「『若处无方便，　　不起安隐乐，若得彼处者，　　无作亦无忧。当与我是处，　　令我得安隐。』

Then Śakra, Lord of the Devas, again spoke in verse:

「时，天帝释复说偈答言：

If there is a place without skillful means, not arisen, peaceful and
blissful, one who attains such a place has no action and no
sorrow. If you have attained such a place, you should also take me there.

「『若处无方便，　　不起安隐乐，若人得是处，　　无作亦无忧。汝得是处者，　　亦应将我去。』

The god Sukhavihāra again spoke in verse:

「宿毘梨天子复说偈言：

If there is a place of non-distraction, not arisen, peaceful and blissful
one who attains such a place has no action and no sorrow
Give me such a place, so that I may attain peaceful bliss.

「『若处不放逸，　　不起安隐乐，若人得是处，　　无作亦无忧。当与我是处，　　令得安隐乐。』

Then Śakra, Lord of the Devas, again spoke in verse:

「时，天帝释复说偈言：

If there is a place of non-distraction, not arisen, peaceful and blissful
one who attains such a place has no action and no sorrow
If you have attained such a place, you should also take me there.

「『若处不放逸，　　不起安隐乐，若人得是处，　　无作亦无忧。汝得是处者，　　亦应将我去。』

Then Sūrya spoke in verse:

「宿毘梨复说偈言：

Lazy and inactive, not knowing what is done and what is not
done, all actions of desire will be exhausted. Give me such a place.

「『懒惰无所起，　　不知作已作，行欲悉皆会，　　当与我是处。』

Then Śakra, Lord of the Devas, spoke in verse:

「时，天帝释复说偈言：

Lazy and inactive, attaining ultimate peace and happiness, if
you attain that place, you should also take me there.

「『懒惰无所起，　　得究竟安乐，汝得彼处者，　　亦应将我去。』

The god Sukhavihāra spoke in verse:

「宿毘梨天子复说偈言：

Without affairs, one can also find happiness; without action, one is also free
from sorrow. If you give me a place, let me attain peace and happiness.

「『无事亦得乐，　　无作亦无忧，若与我是处，　　令我得安乐。』

The god Śakra spoke in verse:

「天帝释复说偈言：

If you see or hear of sentient beings without any
action, if you obtain this place, you should also take
me there. If you fear action and do not think
of conditioned things, you should quickly purify the path to nirvāṇa.

「『若见若复闻，　　众生无所作，汝得是处者，　　亦应将我去。汝若畏所作，　　不念于有为，但当速净除，　　涅槃之径路。』

At that time, the god of the Pūrvavideha region, together with the four divisions of his army - elephant troops, cavalry, chariot troops, and infantry - fought with the asuras. The asuras were defeated, and the gods returned to their heavenly palaces in victory.

「时，宿毘梨天子严四兵——象兵、马兵、车兵、步兵，与阿修罗战，摧阿修罗众，诸天得胜，还归天宫。」

The Buddha told the monks, "Śakra, lord of the devas, raised four divisions of troops to fight with the asuras. He diligently won the victory. Monks, Śakra, lord of the devas, is the sovereign king of the Trāyastriṃśa Heaven. He always diligently praises the virtue of diligence. You monks, having renounced the household life with right faith, should diligently practice and praise diligence."

佛告诸比丘：「释提桓因兴四种兵，与阿修罗战，精勤得胜。诸比丘！释提桓因于三十三天为自在王，常以精勤方便，亦常赞叹精勤之德。汝等比丘正信非家，出家学道，当勤精进，赞叹精勤。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the monks, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
