SĀ 1115

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1115（一一一五） 仙人

Parallel to: SN 11.9–10 / SĀ² 2.44

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "In the past, there was a village where some sages lived in a secluded place near the village.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「过去世时，有一聚落，有诸仙人于聚落边空闲处住止。

At that time, the devas and asuras were arrayed for battle not far from the village.

「时，有诸天、阿修罗去聚落不远，对阵战鬪。

At that time, the asura king Vemacitra removed his five adornments, took off his heavenly crown, put away his umbrella, put aside his sword, dismissed his jeweled whisk, took off his leather shoes, went to the sage's dwelling place, entered through the gate, looked around, did not glance at the sages, and did not greet them. After looking, he went out.

「尔时，毘摩质多罗阿修罗王除去五饰，脱去天冠，却伞盖，除剑刀，屏宝拂，脱革屣，至彼仙人住处，入于门内，周向看视，不顾眄诸仙人，亦不问讯。看已，还出。

At that time, a certain sage saw from afar the asura king Vemacitra removing his five adornments and entering the garden, looking and then going out. Having seen this, he said to the other sages: 'Who is this person? He has an untamed appearance, not like a human form, not in proper conduct, like a country bumpkin, not a son of a householder. Having removed his five adornments, he entered the garden gate, looked up and did not glance at or greet the sages.' Another sage replied: 'This is the asura king Vemacitra, who removed his five adornments and looked before leaving.' The sage said: 'This is not a virtuous person, not good or wholesome, not virtuous or proper. Having removed his five adornments, he came into the garden gate, looked and then left, also not glancing at or greeting the sages. Therefore, we should know that the heavenly hosts are increasing and the asuras are decreasing.'

「时，有一仙人遥见毘摩质多罗阿修罗王除去五饰入园，看已，还出。见已，语诸仙人言：『此何等人？有不调伏色，不似人形，非威仪法，似田舍儿，非长者子，除去五饰，入于园门，高视观看，亦不顾眄问讯诸仙人。』有一仙人答言：『此是毘摩质多罗阿修罗王，除去五饰，观看而去。』彼仙人言：『此非贤士，不好不善，非贤非法，除去五饰，来入园门，看已，还去，亦不顾眄问讯诸仙人。以是故，当知天众增长，阿修罗损减。』

At that time, Sakra Devendra removed his five adornments and entered the sage's dwelling place, exchanging greetings and pleasantries with the sages before leaving.

「时，释提桓因除去五饰，入仙人住处，与诸仙人面相问讯慰劳，然后还出。

There was also a sage who saw Sakra Devendra removing his five adornments and entering the garden gate, inquiring all around. Having seen this, he asked the other sages: 'Who is this person? Entering the garden, he has a disciplined appearance, a pleasing human appearance, a dignified appearance, not like a country bumpkin, resembling a noble son. Having removed his five adornments, he came into the garden gate, inquired all around, and then left.' A sage replied: 'This is Sakra Devendra, who removed his five adornments, came into the garden gate, inquired all around, and then left.' The sage said: 'This is a virtuous person, good and true, with dignified conduct. Having removed his five adornments, he came into the garden gate, inquired all around, and then left. Therefore, we should know that the heavenly hosts are increasing and the asura hosts are decreasing.'

「复有仙人见天帝释除去五饰，入于园门，周遍问讯。见已，问诸仙人：『此是何人？入于园林，有调伏色，有可适人色，有威仪色，非田舍儿，似族姓子，除去五饰，来入园门，周遍问讯，然后还出。』有仙人答言：『此是天帝释，除去五饰，来入园门，周遍问讯，然后还去。』彼仙人言：『此是贤士，善好真实威仪法，除去五饰，来入园门，周遍问讯，然后还去，以是当知天众增长，阿修罗众损减。』

At that time, the asura king Vemacitra heard the sage praising the gods. Having heard this, he was filled with intense anger.

「时，毘摩质多罗阿修罗王闻仙人称叹诸天，闻已，瞋恚炽盛。

At that time, the sage in the empty place heard that the asura king was filled with intense anger. He went to the asura king Vemacitra and spoke a verse:

「时，彼空处仙人闻阿修罗王瞋恚炽盛，往诣毘摩质多罗阿修罗王所，而说偈言：

The sage has come here for this, seeking to beg for fearlessness
If you can grant fearlessness, bestow the teaching of the sage's grace.

「『仙人故来此，　　求乞施无畏，汝能施无畏，　　赐牟尼恩教。』

Vemacitra replied in verse:

「毘摩质多罗以偈答言：

You have no fearlessness to give
to you sages, because you go
against the asuras and are close to Sakra.

「『于汝仙人所，　　无有施无畏，违背阿修罗，　　习近帝释故。于此诸无畏，　　当遗以恐怖。』

The sage spoke another verse:

「仙人复说偈言：

According to the seeds sown, the fruits are born according to
their kind. When begging for fearlessness, leave with terror. One will
attain inexhaustible fearlessness because of planting the seeds of giving fearlessness.

「『随行殖种子，　　随类果报生，来乞于无畏，　　遗之以恐怖。当获无尽畏，　　施畏种子故。』

At that time, the sages spoke the mantra in front of the asura king Vimalacitra, and then departed through the air.

「时，诸仙人于毘摩质多罗阿修罗王面前说呪已，凌虚而逝。

That very night, the asura king Vimalacitra was startled three times in his sleep, and heard an evil voice saying: 'Sakra devanam indra is raising four kinds of troops to fight with the asuras!' Startled and terrified, fearing defeat in battle, he retreated and returned to the asura palace.

「即于是夜，毘摩质多罗阿修罗王心惊三起，眠中闻恶声言：『释提桓因兴四种兵，与阿修罗战！』惊觉恐怖，虑战必败，退走而还阿修罗宫。

At that time, Sakra devanam indra, having defeated the enemy, went to the hermitage of the sages, paid homage at the feet of the sages, and then sat to the west of the sages, facing east.

「时，天帝释敌退得胜已，诣彼空闲仙人住处，礼诸仙人足已，退于西面诸仙人前，东向而坐。

At that time, an eastern wind arose. An extraordinary sage spoke a verse:

「时，东风起，有异仙人即说偈言：

Now these sages, having gone forth for many days
have armpit sweat that stinks. Do not sit downwind
The thousand-eyed one can move his seat. This stench is unbearable.

「『今此诸牟尼，　　出家来日久，腋下流汗臭，　　莫顺坐风下。千眼可移坐，　　此臭难可堪。』

At that time, Śakra, Lord of the Devas, spoke a verse in reply:

「时，天帝释说偈答言：

Various kinds of fragrant flowers are made into flower garlands. The
fragrance I smell now surpasses that. How can I bear to
smell this fragrance for a long time without ever becoming weary?

「『种种众香华，　　结以为华鬘，今之所闻香，　　其香复过是，宁久闻斯香，　　未曾生厌患。』」

The Buddha told the monks, "Śakra, Lord of the Devas, is the sovereign king of the Thirty-three Heavens. He respects those who have left home life and always praises the virtues of respecting and praising them. You monks, having renounced the household life with right faith, should always respect those who practice the holy life and also praise the virtues of respect and praise."

佛告诸比丘：「彼天帝释于三十三天为自在王，恭敬出家人，亦常赞叹出家人，亦常赞叹恭敬之德。汝等比丘正信非家，出家学道，常应恭敬诸梵行者，亦当赞叹恭敬之德。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished what he had to say. The monks, having heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
