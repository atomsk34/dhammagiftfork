SĀ 1118

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1118（一一一八） 病

Parallel to: SN 11.23 / SĀ² 2.47

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "In the past, when the asura king Vemacitra was gravely ill, he went to Śakra, lord of the devas, and said, 'Kauśika, you should know that I am now gravely ill. Please treat me and make me well.' Śakra said to Vemacitra, 'You should teach me the asura magic. I will treat your illness and make you well.' Vemacitra said to Śakra, 'I will ask the asura assembly. If they allow it, I will teach Śakra the asura magic.'

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「过去世时，毘摩质多罗阿修罗王疾病困笃，往诣释提桓因所，语释提桓因言：『憍尸迦！当知我今疾病困笃，为我疗治，令得安隐！』释提桓因语毘摩质多罗阿修罗言：『汝当授我幻法，我当疗治汝病，令得安隐。』毘摩质多罗阿修罗语帝释言：『我当还问诸阿修罗众，听我者，当授帝释阿修罗幻法。』

At that time, Vemacitra went to the asura assembly and said to the asuras, 'You should know that I am now gravely ill. I went to Śakra, lord of the devas, to seek treatment for my illness. He said to me, "If you can teach me the asura magic, I will treat your illness and make you well." I will now go and teach him the asura magic.'

「尔时，毘摩质多罗阿修罗即往至诸阿修罗众中，语诸阿修罗言：『诸人当知，我今疾病困笃，往诣释提桓因所，求彼治病。彼语我言：「汝能授我阿修罗幻法者，当治汝病，令得安隐。」我今当往为彼说阿修罗幻法。』

At that time, a deceitful asura said to Vemacitra, 'That Śakra, lord of the devas, is honest and trustworthy, not deceitful. Just tell him, "Heavenly King! This asura magic, if studied, will cause one to fall into hell and suffer for countless hundreds of thousands of years." He will certainly give up and not seek to study it anymore. He will say, "Go! I will make you well and at ease!"'

「时，有一诈伪阿修罗语毘摩质多罗阿修罗：『其彼天帝释质直好信不虚伪，但语彼言：「天王！此阿修罗幻法，若学者，令人堕地狱，受罪无量百千岁。」彼天帝释必当息意，不复求学，当言：「汝去！令汝病差，可得安隐！」』

At that time, Vemacitra again went to Śakra and spoke in verse, saying:

「时，毘摩质多罗阿修罗复往帝释所，说偈白言：

The heavenly king with a thousand eyes, the asura's illusory arts
are all false and deceptive, causing one to fall into hell
For countless hundreds of thousands of years, one suffers without rest.

「『千眼尊天王，　　阿修罗幻术，皆是虚诳法，　　令人堕地狱。无量百千岁，　　受苦无休息。』

At that time, Śakra, Lord of the Devas, said to Vemacitra, Asura King: 'Stop! Stop! Such illusory arts are not what I need. You should return now. May your physical illness be pacified and you regain strength and well-being!'

「时，天帝释语毘摩质多罗阿修罗言：『止！止！如是幻术，非我所须，汝且还去，令汝身病寂灭休息，得力安隐！』」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus: "Śakra, Lord of the Devas, is the sovereign king of the Thirty-three Heavens. He has been true, un-deceptive, and honest for a long time. You bhikṣus who have renounced the household life and gone forth to study the path should also be like this, un-deceptive, honest, and straightforward. You should train like this."

佛告诸比丘：「释提桓因于三十三天为自在王，长夜真实，不幻不伪，贤善质直。汝等比丘正信非家，出家学道，亦应如是不幻不伪，贤善质直，当如是学。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus were delighted and received it respectfully.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
