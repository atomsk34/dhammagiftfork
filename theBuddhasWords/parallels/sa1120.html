SĀ 1120

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1120（一一二〇） 持一戒

Parallel to: SN 11.7 / SĀ² 2.48

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "In the past, the Heavenly King Śakra said to the Buddha, 'World-Honored One! I now accept this precept: "As long as the Buddha's teachings remain in the world, for as long as I live, if anyone harms me, I will not retaliate and harm them in return." At that time, the Asura King Vemacitrin heard that the Heavenly King Śakra had accepted this precept: "As long as the Buddha's teachings remain in the world, for as long as I live, if anyone harms me, I will not retaliate and harm them in return." Having heard this, he took a sharp sword and came against him.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「过去世时，有天帝释白佛言：『世尊！我今受如是戒：「乃至佛法住世，尽其形寿，有恼我者，要不反报加恼于彼。」』时，毘摩质多罗阿修罗王闻天帝释受如是戒：『乃至佛法住世，尽其形寿，有恼我者，我不反报加恼于彼。』闻已，执持利剑，逆道而来。

At that time, the Heavenly King Śakra saw from afar that the Asura King Vemacitrin was coming against him with a sharp sword, and immediately called out to him from afar, 'Asura! Stop! Bind you and do not move!'

「时，天帝释遥见毘摩质多罗阿修罗王执持利剑，逆道而来，即遥告言：『阿修罗！住！缚汝勿动！』

"The Asura King Vemacitrin was then unable to move and said to Śakra, 'Do you not now accept this precept: "As long as the Buddha's teachings remain in the world, for as long as I live, if anyone harms me, I will definitely not retaliate."?" Śakra replied, 'I indeed accept this precept, but you should stop and be bound.' The Asura said, 'Now please release me!' Śakra replied, 'If you swear not to cause trouble, then I will release you.' The Asura said, 'If released, I will act according to the Dharma.' Śakra replied, 'Act according to the Dharma first, then I will release you!'

「毘摩质多罗阿修罗王即不得动，语帝释言：『汝今岂不受如是戒：「若佛法住世，尽其形寿，有恼我者，必不还报。」耶？』天帝释答言：『我实受如是戒，但汝息住受缚。』阿修罗言：『今且放我！』帝释答言：『汝若约誓不作乱者，然后放汝。』阿修罗言：『放我当如法作。』帝释答言：『先如法作，然后放汝！』

At that time, the Asura King Vemacitrin spoke a verse, saying:

「时，毘摩质多罗阿修罗王即说偈言：

The course of greed, the course of anger, the course of false
speech, the course of slandering the sages - if I were to
cause trouble, I would go to the same course as those courses.

「『贪欲之所趣，　　及瞋恚所趣，妄语之所趣，　　谤毁贤圣趣。我若娆乱者，　　趣同彼趣趣。』

"Śakra, King of the Gods, said again, 'I release you to go, as you please."

「释提桓因复告言：『放汝令去，随汝所安。』

At that time, the Heavenly King Śakra, having made the oath with the Asura King, went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, 'World-Honored One! I have received such precepts in front of the Buddha: "As long as the Buddha's teachings remain in the world, I will not retaliate against those who harm me, even at the cost of my life." The Asura King Vemacitra heard that I had received the precepts and came along the road with a sharp sword in hand. When I saw him from afar, I said, "Asuras! Stop! Stop! I will bind you, do not move!" The Asura said, "Have you not received the precepts?" I then replied, "I have indeed received the precepts. But you should stay now, I will bind you, do not move." He then sought to be released. I told him, "If you make an oath not to cause trouble, I will let you go." The Asura said, "First release me, then I will make the oath." I then said, "First make the oath, then I will release you." He then spoke a verse, making the oath, saying:

「尔时，天帝释令阿修罗王作约誓已，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面，白佛言：『世尊！我于佛前受如是戒：「乃至佛法住世，尽其形寿，有恼我者，我不反报。」毘摩质多罗阿修罗王闻我受戒，手执利剑，随路而来。我遥见已，语言：「阿修罗！住！住！缚汝勿动！」彼阿修罗言：「汝不受戒耶？」我即答言：「我实受戒，且汝今住，缚汝勿动。」彼即求脱，我告彼言：「若作约誓不作乱者，当令汝脱。」阿修罗言：「且当放我，当说约誓。」我即告言：「先说约誓，然后放汝。」彼即说偈，作约誓言：

The course of greed, the course of anger, the course of
lying, the course of slandering the sages, if I were to
cause trouble, I would go to the same course as those courses.

「『贪欲之所趣，　　及瞋恚所趣，妄语之所趣，　　谤毁贤圣趣，我若作娆乱，　　趣同彼趣趣。』

"Thus, World-Honored One! I made the Asura King speak the oath. Is this the Dharma? Did the Asura cause trouble again?"

「『如是。世尊！我要彼阿修罗王令说约誓，为是法不？彼阿修罗复为娆乱不？』

The Buddha told Indra, the Lord of the Devas, "Excellent! Excellent! You made him take the oath according to the Dharma, without violating it. He will not dare to cause trouble again." At that time, Indra, the Lord of the Devas, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully rejoiced, paid homage, and departed.

「佛告天帝释：『善哉！善哉！汝要彼约誓，如法不违，彼亦不复敢作娆乱。』尔时，天帝释闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Indra, the Lord of the Devas, is the sovereign king of the Thirty-three Heavens. He does not cause trouble and always praises the Dharma of non-disturbance. You bhikṣus, having renounced the household life with right faith, should also practice non-disturbance and praise the Dharma of non-disturbance.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「彼天帝释于三十三天为自在王，不为娆乱，亦常赞叹不娆乱法。汝等比丘正信非家，出家学道，亦应如是行不娆乱，亦当赞叹不扰乱法。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished. The bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
