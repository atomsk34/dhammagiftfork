SĀ 1121

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1121（一一二一） 释氏

Parallel to: AN 10.46

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Thus have I heard:

一时，佛住迦毘罗卫国尼拘律园。时，有众多释氏来诣佛所，稽首礼足，退坐一面。

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the Śākya clansmen, "You Śākya clansmen, do you observe the fast on the fast day and the full moon day, and practice meritorious deeds?"

尔时，世尊告诸释氏：「汝等诸瞿昙！于法斋日及神足月受持斋戒，修功德不？」

The Śākya clansmen said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Sometimes we are able to observe the fast on the fast day and the full moon day, and sometimes we are not. Sometimes we are able to practice meritorious deeds in the month of supernatural powers, and sometimes we are not."

诸释氏白佛言：「世尊！我等于诸斋日有时得受斋戒，有时不得；于神足月有时斋戒，修诸功德，有时不得。」

The Buddha said to the Śākya clansmen, "Gautama! You do not obtain good benefits. You are arrogant, afflicted, sorrowful, and troubled. Why is it that sometimes you are able to observe the fast on the fast day and the full moon day, and sometimes you are not? Why is it that sometimes you are able to observe the fast in the month of supernatural powers and practice meritorious deeds, and sometimes you are not? Gautama! It is like a person seeking profit, increasing day by day - one day one coin, two days two coins, three days four coins, four days eight coins, five days sixteen coins, six days thirty-two coins. Would such a person's wealth increase day by day, on the eighth day, ninth day, up to a month, and increase more and more?"

佛告诸释氏：「瞿昙！汝等不获善利，汝等是憍慢者、烦恼人、忧悲人、恼苦人。何故于诸斋日或得斋戒，或不得？于神足月或得斋戒，作诸功德，或不得？诸瞿昙！譬人求利，日日增长，一日一钱，二日两钱，三日四钱，四日八钱，五日十六钱，六日三十二钱。如是士夫日常增长，八日、九日乃至一月，钱财转增广耶？」

The elder said to the Buddha, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

长者白佛：「如是，世尊！」

The Buddha said to the Śākya clansmen, "What do you think, Gautama? If such a person's wealth increases, will he naturally obtain increasing wealth, and then want me to dwell in meditation for ten years with a joyful mind and much delight?"

佛告释氏：「云何？瞿昙！如是士夫钱财转增，当得自然钱财增广，复欲令我于十年中一向喜乐心乐，多住禅定，宁得以不？」

The Śākya clansmen replied, "No, World-Honored One!"

释氏答言：「不也，世尊！」

The Buddha said to the Śākya clansmen, "If he can dwell in meditation for nine years, eight years, seven years, six years, five years, four years, three years, two years, or one year with a joyful mind and much delight, will he be able to do so?"

佛告释氏：「若得九年、八年、七年、六年、五年、四年、三年、二年、一年喜乐心乐，多住禅定以不？」

The Śākya clansmen replied, "No, World-Honored One!"

释氏答言：「不也，世尊！」

The Buddha said to the Śākya clansmen, "Let us set aside the years. Can he dwell in meditation for ten months, nine months, eight months, up to one month with a joyful mind and much delight? Let us set aside one month. Can he dwell in meditation for ten days, nine days, eight days, up to one day and one night with a joyful mind and much delight?"

佛告释氏：「且置年岁，宁得十月、九月、八月乃至一月喜乐心乐，多住禅定以不？复置一月，宁得十日、九日、八日乃至一日一夜喜乐心乐，禅定多住以不？」

The Śākya clansmen replied, "No, World-Honored One!"

释氏答言：「不也，世尊！」

The Buddha said to the Śākya clansmen, "I now tell you, among my śrāvakas, those with a sincere mind, who are not deceitful or deceptive, I can teach and transform for ten years. For this reason, they will be able to dwell in meditation for hundreds of thousands of years with a joyful mind and much delight. This is possible! Let us set aside ten years. If for nine years, eight years, up to one year, ten months, nine months, up to one month, ten days, nine days, up to one day and one night, I teach and transform them, by the next morning, I can cause them to make progress. Teaching and transforming them in the morning, up to the evening, I can cause them to make progress. For this reason, they will be able to dwell in meditation for hundreds of thousands of years with a joyful mind and much delight, and accomplish the two fruits, either the fruit of once-returning or the fruit of non-returning, because those individuals have already attained the fruit of stream-entry."

佛告释氏：「我今语汝，我声闻中有直心者，不谄不幻，我于彼人，十年教化，以是因缘，彼人则能百千万岁一向喜乐心乐，多住禅定，斯有是处！复置十年，若九年、八年乃至一年，十月、九月乃至一月，十日、九日乃至一日一夜，我教化，至其明旦，能令胜进。晨朝教化，乃至日暮，能令胜进，以是因缘，得百千万岁一向喜乐心乐，多住禅定，成就二果，或斯陀含果、阿那含果，以彼士夫先得须陀洹故。」

The Śākya clansmen said to the Buddha, "Excellent, World-Honored One! From today onwards, on the days of fasting, we shall observe the precepts, up to the eight branches. During the month of supernatural powers, we shall observe the fast and give according to our ability, cultivating various merits."

释氏白佛：「善哉！世尊！我从今日，于诸斋日当修斋戒，乃至八支，于神足月受持斋戒，随力惠施，修诸功德。」

The Buddha said to the Śākya clansmen, "Excellent, Gautama! It is truly essential."

佛告释氏：「善哉！瞿昙！为真实要。」

Having spoken this sūtra, at that time the Śākya clansmen, having heard what the Buddha had said, rejoiced and followed his joy, bowed and departed.

佛说此经已，时诸释种闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
