SĀ 1122

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1122（一一二二） 疾病

Parallel to: SN 55.54

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying in the NiKuLa grove in the country of Kapilavastu.

一时，佛住迦毘罗卫国尼拘律园中。

At that time, many Śākyas gathered in the discussion hall and had this discussion. A Śākya said to Śākya Nanda, "Sometimes I am able to go to the Tathāgata to pay respect and make offerings, and sometimes I am not able to. Sometimes I am able to be close to and make offerings to knowledgeable bhikṣus, and sometimes I am not able to. Moreover, I do not know about the wise upāsakas, the wise upāsikās who are sick and suffering. How should I teach, instruct, and preach the Dharma to them? Now let us go together to where the World-Honored One is and ask about this meaning. As the World-Honored One teaches, we shall receive and practice it."

时，有众多释氏集论议堂，作如是论议。时，有释氏语释氏难提：「我有时得诣如来，恭敬供养，有时不得；有时得亲近供养知识比丘，有时不得，又复不知有诸智慧优婆塞，有余智慧优婆塞、智慧优婆夷疾病困苦，复云何教化、教诫说法，今当共往诣世尊所，问如此义，如世尊教，当受奉行。」

Then Nanda and the Śākyas went together to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at his feet, stood to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! We Śākyas gathered in the discussion hall and had this discussion. Some Śākyas said to me, 'Nanda! Sometimes we see the Tathāgata and pay respect and make offerings, and sometimes we do not see him. Sometimes we go to see the knowledgeable bhikṣus and be close to and make offerings to them, and sometimes we are not able to...'" In this way, he spoke extensively, up to "As the Buddha teaches, we shall receive and practice it." We now ask the World-Honored One, 'If there are wise upāsakas, other wise upāsakas and upāsikās who are sick and suffering, how should we teach, instruct, and preach the Dharma to them?'

尔时，难提与诸释氏俱诣佛所，稽首礼足，退住一面，白佛言：「世尊！我等诸释氏集论议堂，作如是论议，有诸释氏语我言：『难提！我等或时见如来，恭敬供养，或时不见，或时往见诸知识比丘，亲近供养，或时不得……』」如是广说，乃至「『如佛所教诫，当受奉行。』我等今日请问世尊，若智慧优婆塞，有余智慧优婆塞、优婆夷疾病困苦。云何教化、教诫说法？」

The Buddha told Nanda, "If there are wise upāsakas, they should go to the places of other wise upāsakas and upāsikās who are sick and suffering, and teach them with the three kinds of places of relief, saying, 'Good sir! You should accomplish the unshakable purity of faith in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Saṃgha.' Having taught them with these three kinds of places of relief, they should further ask, 'Do you have any attachment to your parents?' If they have attachment to their parents, they should be taught to let go of it. They should be told, 'If you can live by having attachment to your parents, then have attachment. But since you cannot live by having attachment, what is the use of having attachment?' If they say they do not have attachment to their parents, they should be praised and rejoiced in. They should further ask, 'Do you have any concern for your wife, children, servants, money, or possessions?' If they say they have concern, they should be taught to let go of it, just as they were taught to let go of attachment to their parents. If they say they do not have concern, they should be praised and rejoiced in. They should further ask, 'Do you have any concern for the five desires of the human realm?' If they say they have concern, they should be told, 'The five desires of the human realm are impure, foul, and stinky. They are not as good as the superior five desires of the heavens.' They should be taught to let go of the five desires of the human realm and to aspire for the superior five desires of the heavens. If they say their minds have already let go of the five desires of the human realm and they have already concerned themselves with the superior five desires of the heavens, they should be praised and rejoiced in. They should further be told, 'The superior five desires of the heavens are impermanent, suffering, empty, and subject to change. There is the bliss of the cessation of existence and nirvāṇa.' If they say they have already let go of concern for the superior five desires of the heavens and concerned themselves with the bliss of the cessation of existence, they should be praised and rejoiced in.

佛告难提：「若有智慧优婆塞，当诣余智慧优婆塞、优婆夷疾病困苦者所，以三种稣息处而教授之言：『仁者！汝当成就于佛不坏净，于法、僧不坏净。』以是三种稣息处而教授已，当复问言：『汝顾恋父母不？』彼若有顾恋父母者，当教令舍，当语彼言：『汝顾恋父母得活者，可顾恋耳，既不由顾恋而得活，用顾恋为？』彼若言不顾恋父母者，当叹善随喜，当复问言：『汝于妻子、奴仆、钱财诸物有顾念不？』若言顾念，当教令舍，如舍顾恋父母法；若言不顾念，叹善随喜，当复问言：『汝于人间五欲顾念以不？』若言顾念，当为说言：『人间五欲恶露不净，败坏臭处，不如天上胜妙五欲。』教令舍离人间五欲，教令志愿天上五欲。若复彼言心已远离人间五欲，先已顾念天胜妙欲，叹善随喜，复语彼言：『天上妙欲无常、苦、空、变坏之法，诸天上有身胜天五欲。』若言已舍顾念天欲，顾念有身胜欲，叹善随喜，当复教言：『有身之欲，亦复无常、变坏之法，有行灭、涅槃、出离之乐，汝当舍离有身顾念，乐于涅槃寂灭之乐为上、为胜。』彼圣弟子已能舍离有身顾念，乐涅槃者，叹善随喜。

"Thus, Nanda! Those noble disciples teach and instruct in stages, causing them to attain the nirvāṇa of non-arising, just like a bhikṣu with a lifespan of a hundred years and liberation nirvāṇa."

「如是，难提！彼圣弟子先后次第教诫、教授，令得不起、涅槃，犹如比丘百岁寿命，解脱涅槃。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha said, "The Śākya Nanda and others, having heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced and paid homage before departing.

佛说此经已，释氏难提等闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
