SĀ 1136

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1136（一一三六） 月喻

Parallel to: SN 16.3 / SĀ² 2.111

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalanda Bamboo Grove in the city of Shravasti.

一时，佛住舍卫国迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "You should abide like the moon, like a beginner, with shame and humility, controlling your mind and restraining your appearance when entering others' homes. It is like a person with clear eyes approaching a precipice or climbing a peak, controlling their mind and restraining their appearance, proceeding with difficulty and speed. In the same way, bhikṣus should abide like the moon, like beginners, with shame and humility, controlling their minds and restraining their appearances when entering others' homes. The bhikṣu Kāśyapa abides like the moon, like a beginner, with shame and humility, controlling the arrogance of the proud, controlling his mind and restraining his appearance when entering others' homes. It is like a person with clear eyes approaching a precipice or climbing a peak, controlling his mind and restraining his appearance, proceeding with right observation."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「当如月譬住，如新学，惭愧软下，摄心敛形而入他家。如明目士夫临深登峰，摄心敛形，难速前进。如是，比丘如月譬住，亦如新学，惭愧软下，御心敛形而入他家。迦叶比丘如月譬住，亦如新学，惭愧软下诸高慢，御心控形而入他家。如明目士夫临深登峰，御心控形，正观而进。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "What kind of appearance should a bhikṣu have when entering others' homes?"

佛告比丘：「于意云何？比丘为何等像类应入他家？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "The World-Honored One is the root of the Dharma, the eye of the Dharma, and the reliance of the Dharma. Please explain it extensively! When we have heard it, we will accept and uphold it."

诸比丘白佛言：「世尊是法根、法眼、法依，唯愿广说！诸比丘闻已，当受奉行。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "Listen carefully and consider it well, I will explain it to you. If a bhikṣu is not attached to and delights in others' homes, and is happy for others' gains and merits as if they were his own, without giving rise to jealousy, not elevating himself or belittling others, a bhikṣu of this kind should enter others' homes."

佛告诸比丘：「谛听，善思，当为汝说。若有比丘于他家心不缚著贪乐，于他得利，他作功德，欣若在己，不生嫉想，亦不自举，亦不下人。如是像类比丘应入他家。」

At that time, the World-Honored One touched the empty space with his hand and said to the bhikṣus, "Does my hand now touch, bind, or stain the empty space?"

尔时，世尊以手扪摸虚空，告诸比丘：「我今此手，宁著空、缚空、染空不？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "No, World-Honored One!"

比丘白佛：「不也，世尊！」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "The way of bhikṣus is always like this: not attached, not bound, not defiled in mind, entering others' homes. Only the bhikṣu Kāśyapa enters others' homes with an unattached, unbound, and undefiled mind, rejoicing in others' gains and merits as if they were his own, not giving rise to jealous thoughts, not elevating himself, and not belittling others. Only the bhikṣu Kāśyapa should enter others' homes."

佛告比丘：「比丘之法常如是：不著、不缚、不染心而入他家。唯迦叶比丘以不著、不缚、不染之心而入他家，于他得利及作功德，欣若在己，不生嫉想、不自举、不下人，其唯迦叶比丘应入他家。」

At that time, the World-Honored One again touched the empty space with his hand and said to the bhikṣus, "What do you think? Is my hand now attached to the empty space, bound to the empty space, or defiled by the empty space?"

尔时，世尊复以手扪摸虚空，告诸比丘：「于意云何？我今此手，宁著空、缚空、染空以不？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "No, World-Honored One!"

诸比丘白佛言：「不也，世尊！」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "Only the bhikṣu Kāśyapa has a mind like this, entering others' homes with a mind that is not attached, not bound, and not defiled."

佛告比丘：「其唯迦叶比丘心常如是，以不著、不缚、不染之心入于他家。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "What kind of bhikṣu should give a pure Dharma talk?"

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「何等像类比丘应清净说法？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "The World-Honored One is the root of the Dharma, the eye of the Dharma, and the reliance of the Dharma. Please explain it extensively! When the bhikṣus hear it, they will accept and uphold it."

诸比丘白佛：「世尊是法根、法眼、法依，唯愿广说！诸比丘闻已，当受奉行。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣu, "Listen carefully and think well, I will explain it for you. If a bhikṣu has such a mind when teaching others the Dharma, thinking, 'What kind of person will have pure faith in me and provide me with robes, food and drink, bedding, and medicine?' Such a teacher is called one who gives an impure Dharma talk.

佛告比丘：「谛听，善思，当为汝说。若有比丘作如是心为人说法：『何等人于我起净信心，为本已当得供养衣被、饮食、卧具、汤药。』如是说者，名不清净说法。

If a bhikṣu teaches the Dharma to others with the thought, 'The World-Honored One has revealed the true Dharma and Vinaya, which is free from all burning, not dependent on time, and leads to the realization of nirvāṇa in this very life. But sentient beings are immersed in old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress. If these sentient beings hear the true Dharma, they will be greatly benefited and find long-lasting peace.' With such true Dharma as the cause and condition, and with a mind of kindness, compassion, pity, and a desire for the true Dharma to endure, they teach others. This is called giving a pure Dharma talk. Only the bhikṣu Kāśyapa has such a pure mind to teach others the Dharma, using the true Dharma and Vinaya of the Tathāgata, and even with the intention of making the Dharma endure. Therefore, bhikṣus! You should study and teach the Dharma in this way, using the true Dharma and Vinaya of the Tathāgata, and even with the intention of making the Dharma endure.

「若复比丘为人说法，作如是念：『世尊显现正法、律，离诸炽然，不待时节，即此现身，缘自觉知，正向涅槃，而诸众生沈溺老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦，如此众生闻正法者，以义饶益，长夜安乐。』以是正法因缘，以慈心、悲心、哀愍心、欲令正法久住心而为人说，是名清净说法。唯迦叶比丘有如是清净心为人说法，以如来正法、律，乃至令法久住心而为人说。是故，诸比丘！当如是学、如是说法，于如来正法、律，乃至令法久住心为人说法。」

After the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus who heard what the Buddha had said rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
