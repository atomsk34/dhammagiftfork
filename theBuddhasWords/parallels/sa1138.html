SĀ 1138

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1138（一一三八） 负胜

Parallel to: SN 16.6 / SĀ² 2.113 / EĀ 31.11 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the Venerable Mahākāśyapa was staying in the Deer Park Lecture Hall in the Eastern Garden of Śrāvastī. In the afternoon, he emerged from meditation and went to where the Buddha was. He bowed his head at the Buddha’s feet and sat to one side.

尔时，尊者摩诃迦叶住舍卫国东园鹿子母讲堂。时，尊者摩诃迦叶晡时从禅觉，往诣佛所，稽首礼足，退坐一面。

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the Venerable Mahākāśyapa, "You should teach and instruct the bhikṣus. Why is that? I always teach and instruct the bhikṣus, and you should do the same."

尔时，世尊告尊者摩诃迦叶：「汝当为诸比丘说法教诫、教授。所以者何？我常为诸比丘说法教诫、教授，汝亦应尔。」

The Venerable Mahākāśyapa said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! It is difficult to teach and instruct the bhikṣus of this world. Some bhikṣus cannot bear to hear the teachings."

尊者摩诃迦叶白佛言：「世尊！今世比丘难可教授，或有比丘不忍闻说。」

The Buddha said to Mahākāśyapa, "What is the reason for your statement?"

佛告摩诃迦叶：「汝何因缘作如是说？」

Mahākāśyapa said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I saw two bhikṣus, one named Panchou, a disciple of Ānanda, and the other named Afubi, a disciple of Mahāmaudgalyāyana. Those two argued about who had more learning, each saying, 'Come, let us discuss who knows more and who knows better.'"

摩诃迦叶白佛言：「世尊！我见有两比丘，一名槃稠，是阿难弟子；二名阿浮毘，是摩诃目揵连弟子。彼二人共诤多闻，各言：『汝来当共论议，谁所知多？谁所知胜？』」

At that time, Venerable Ānanda was standing behind the Buddha, fanning him, and said to Venerable Mahākāśyapa, "Stop, Venerable Mahākāśyapa! Be patient, Venerable Kāśyapa! This young bhikṣu is of little wisdom and bad wisdom."

时，尊者阿难住于佛后，以扇扇佛，语尊者摩诃迦叶言：「且止！尊者摩诃迦叶！且忍！尊者迦叶！此年少比丘少智、恶智。」

Venerable Mahākāśyapa said to Venerable Ānanda, "Be silent for now! Do not let me ask you about this matter in the assembly."

尊者摩诃迦叶语尊者阿难言：「汝且默然！莫令我于僧中问汝事。」

At that time, Venerable Ānanda remained silent.

时，尊者阿难即默然住。

At that time, the World-Honored One told a bhikṣu, "Go to those bhikṣus Pañcaśikha and Āfupin and say, 'The Master calls you.'" The bhikṣu then accepted the instruction and went to the bhikṣus Pañcaśikha and Āfupin, saying, "The Master calls you."

尔时，世尊告一比丘：「汝往至彼槃禂比丘、阿浮毘比丘所，作是言：『大师语汝。』」时，彼比丘即受教，至槃禂比丘、阿浮毘比丘所，作是言：「大师语汝。」

At that time, the bhikṣus Pañcaśikha and Āfupin replied, "We will obey," and went together to where the Buddha was. They bowed their heads at his feet and stood to one side.

时，槃禂比丘、阿浮毘比丘答言奉教，即俱往佛所，稽首礼足，退住一面。

At that time, the World-Honored One asked the two bhikṣus, "Did you two really argue together, each saying, 'Come, let us discuss together. Who is more and who is superior'?"

尔时，世尊告二比丘：「汝等二人，实共诤论，各言：『汝来试共论议，谁多谁胜』耶？」

The two bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "Indeed, World-Honored One!"

二比丘白佛言：「实尔。世尊！」

The Buddha said to the two bhikṣus, "You uphold the sūtras, gāthās, prophecies, gāthās, udāna, nidāna, avadāna, itivṛttaka, jātaka, vaipulya, ābhidharma, and abhidharma-piṭaka taught by me, and yet you argue together, each saying, 'Come, let us discuss together. Who is more and who is superior'?"

佛告二比丘：「汝等持我所说修多罗、祇夜、受记、伽陀、优陀那、尼陀那、阿波陀那、伊帝目多伽、阇多伽、毘富罗、阿浮多达摩、优波提舍等法，而共诤论，各言：『汝来试共论议，谁多谁胜』耶？」

The two bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "No, World-Honored One!"

二比丘白佛：「不也，世尊！」

The Buddha said to the two bhikṣus, "Do you not use the sūtras I have taught, up to the abhidharma-piṭaka, to discipline yourselves, to rest yourselves, and to seek nirvāṇa for yourselves?"

佛告二比丘：「汝等不以我所说修多罗，乃至优波提舍，而自调伏，自止息，自求涅槃耶？」

The two bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

二比丘白佛：「如是，世尊！」

The Buddha said to the two bhikṣus, "Do you know that the sūtras I have taught, up to the abhidharma-piṭaka, are for you foolish people to argue with each other about who has more and who is superior?"

佛告二比丘：「汝知我所说修多罗，乃至优波提舍，汝愚痴人应共诤论，谁多谁胜耶？」

At that time, the two bhikṣus bowed their heads to the Buddha's feet and said to him again, "We repent, World-Honored One! We repent, Well-Gone One! We are foolish and ignorant, not skillful and discerning, and we have argued with each other."

时，二比丘前礼佛足，重白佛言：「悔过！世尊！悔过！善逝！我愚我痴，不善不辩，而共诤论。」

The Buddha said to the two bhikṣus, "You truly know your faults and repent. You are foolish and ignorant, not skillful and discerning, and you have argued with each other. Now you have recognized your faults and seen your faults. Knowing and seeing, you repent. In the future, you will generate the precepts of the Vinaya. I now accept you out of compassion, so that your good Dharma may increase and never decline. Why is that? If one recognizes one's faults, sees one's faults, knows and sees, and repents, then in the future one will generate the precepts of the Vinaya and never decline."

佛告二比丘：「实知罪悔过，愚痴，不善不辩，而共诤论。今已自知罪，自见罪，知见悔过，于未来世律仪戒生。我今受汝，怜愍故，令汝善法增长，终不退减。所以者何？若有自知罪，自见罪，知见悔过，于未来世律仪戒生，终不退减。」

At that time, the two bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully rejoiced, bowed, and departed.

时，二比丘闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
