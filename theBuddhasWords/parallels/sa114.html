SĀ 114

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 114（一一四） 知苦

Parallel to: SN 35.81

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying on Mount Makura, with the attendant monk named Rādha.

一时，佛住摩拘罗山，侍者比丘名曰罗陀。

At that time, many non-Buddhist ascetics went to the Venerable Rādha, exchanged greetings, and sat to one side. They asked Rādha, "For what reason did you leave home to practice the holy life under the śramaṇa Gautama?"

时，有众多外道出家至尊者罗陀所，共相问讯已，退坐一面，问罗陀言：「汝为何等故，于沙门瞿昙所出家修梵行？」

Rādha replied, "I left home to practice the holy life under the World-Honored One in order to know suffering."

罗陀答言：「我为知苦故，于世尊所出家修梵行。」

When the non-Buddhist ascetics heard Rādha's words, they were displeased. They got up from their seats, scolded him, and left.

时，诸外道闻罗陀所说，心不喜，从坐起，呵骂而去。

At that time, Rādha emerged from meditation in the afternoon and went to where the Buddha was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side. He told the Buddha in detail what had happened earlier, saying, "World-Honored One! Did what I said just now not defame the World-Honored One? Will it not cause others to question and rebuke me, causing me to fall into a losing position? Is it not saying what is not so, saying what is not in accordance with the Dharma, or saying what is not in accordance with the Dharma?"

尔时，罗陀晡时从禅觉，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面。以其上事具白佛言：「世尊！我向所说，得无毁谤世尊耶？将不令他难问诘责堕负处耶？不如说说、非如法说、非法次法说耶？」

The Buddha told Rādha, "What you truly said does not defame the Tathāgata, and it will not cause others to question and rebuke you, causing you to fall into a losing position. It is saying what is so, saying what is in accordance with the Dharma, and saying what is in accordance with the Dharma. Why is that? Form is suffering. In order to know that suffering, one leaves home to practice the holy life under the Tathāgata. Feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness are suffering. In order to know that suffering, one leaves home to practice the holy life under the Tathāgata."

佛告罗陀：「汝真实说，不毁如来，不令他人难问诘责堕负处也，是如说说、如法说、法次法说。所以者何？色是苦，为知彼苦故，于如来所出家修梵行；受、想、行、识是苦，为知彼苦故，于如来所出家修梵行。」

After the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣu Rādha, having heard what the Buddha had said, rejoiced and upheld it.

佛说此经已，罗陀比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
