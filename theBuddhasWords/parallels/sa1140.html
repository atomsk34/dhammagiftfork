SĀ 1140

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1140（一一四〇） 佛为根本

Parallel to: SN 16.8 / SĀ² 2.115

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the Venerable Mahākāśyapa was staying in the Deer Park Lecture Hall in the Eastern Garden of Śrāvastī. In the afternoon, he emerged from meditation and went to where the Buddha was. He bowed his head at the Buddha’s feet and sat to one side.

尔时，尊者摩诃迦叶住舍卫国东园鹿子母讲堂，晡时从禅觉，来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面。

At that time, the World-Honored One said to Mahākāśyapa, "You should teach and instruct the bhikṣus. Why is that? I always teach and instruct the bhikṣus, and you should do the same."

尔时，世尊告摩诃迦叶：「汝当为诸比丘说法教诫、教授。所以者何？我常为诸比丘说法教诫、教授，汝亦应尔。」

The Venerable Mahākāśyapa said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! It is difficult to teach and instruct the bhikṣus now. Some bhikṣus, upon hearing the Dharma, are impatient and displeased."

尊者摩诃迦叶白佛言：「世尊！今诸比丘难可为说法教诫、教授，有诸比丘闻所说法，不忍、不喜。」

The Buddha said to Mahākāśyapa, "What is the reason for your statement?"

佛告摩诃迦叶：「汝何因缘作如是说？」

Mahākāśyapa said to the Buddha, "The World-Honored One is the root of the Dharma, the eye of the Dharma, and the reliance of the Dharma. I only wish that the World-Honored One would teach the Dharma for the bhikṣus. Having heard it, the bhikṣus will accept and uphold it."

摩诃迦叶白佛言：「世尊是法根、法眼、法依，唯愿世尊为诸比丘说法，诸比丘闻已，当受奉行。」

The Buddha said to Kāśyapa, "Listen carefully and think well. I will explain it for you."

佛告迦叶：「谛听，善思，当为汝说。」

The Buddha told Kāśyapa, "In the past, the bhikṣus who practiced in the wilderness praised the practice of the wilderness to the bhikṣus who practiced in the wilderness, praised the merits of begging for food to the bhikṣus who begged for food, praised the merits of wearing rags to the bhikṣus who wore rags. If there were bhikṣus who were content with few desires, practiced detachment, diligently applied themselves, were mindful and concentrated, had wisdom and exhausted the taints, and had attained realization through their own bodies, they would praise and extol their practices. Kāśyapa! If one praises the practice of the wilderness to those who practice in the wilderness, and praises the attainment of realization through one's own body to those who have exhausted the taints, if one sees such people, one should speak with them, comfort them, and welcome them, asking, 'What is your name? Who is your teacher?' One should give up one's seat and invite them to sit, praising their virtue and goodness, according to the Dharma and their characteristics. There is the meaning of a śramaṇa, there is the desire of a śramaṇa. When praising in this way, if those who live and travel together with them, they will then certainly follow their practices. Before long, they will also have the same views and desires."

佛告迦叶：「昔日，阿练若比丘于阿练若比丘所，叹说阿练若法，于乞食比丘所，叹说乞食功德；于粪扫衣比丘所，叹说粪扫衣功德。若少欲知足，修行远离，精勤方便，正念正定，智慧漏尽，身作证比丘所，随其所行，赞叹称说。迦叶！若于阿练若所，叹说阿练若法，乃至漏尽比丘所，叹说漏尽身作证。若见其人，悉共语言，随宜慰劳善来者：『汝名何等？为谁弟子？』让座令坐，叹其贤善，如其法像类，有沙门义、有沙门欲。如是赞叹时，若彼同住同游者，则便决定随顺彼行，不久亦当同其所见，同其所欲。」

The Buddha told Kāśyapa, "If a young bhikṣu sees a bhikṣu who practices in the wilderness coming, he should praise the practice of the wilderness to him, and praise the attainment of realization through one's own body to him who has exhausted the taints. That young bhikṣu should rise to greet him, pay respect and bow to him, and ask after his well-being. If those who live and travel together with him, they will soon attain their own benefit and advantage. If one pays respect in this way, one will attain peace and benefit for a long time."

佛告迦叶：「若是年少比丘见彼阿练若比丘来赞叹阿练若法，乃至漏尽身作证，彼年少比丘应起出迎，恭敬礼拜问讯，乃至彼同住者，不久当得自义饶益。如是恭敬者，长夜当得安乐饶益。」

The Buddha told Kāśyapa, "Today, if a bhikṣu sees someone coming who is greatly virtuous and can inspire offerings of robes, food, bedding, and medicine, he should speak with them, pay respect and ask after their well-being, praising them as they arrive, asking their name and who their teacher is. He should praise their merit and virtue, saying they can inspire great benefits, robes, food, bedding, and medicine. If those who live and travel together with the venerable one, they will also be abundantly provided with robes, food, bedding, and medicine. If a young bhikṣu sees someone coming who is greatly wise and virtuous, and can inspire offerings of robes, food, bedding, and medicine, he should quickly rise to greet them, pay respect and ask after their well-being, praising them as they arrive, saying they are greatly wise and virtuous, and can inspire great benefits, robes, food, bedding, and medicine. Kāśyapa! Such a young bhikṣu will for a long time not attain what is beneficial and will suffer harm.

佛告迦叶：「今日比丘见彼来者，知见大德，能感财利、衣被、饮食、床卧、汤药者，与共言语，恭敬问讯，叹言善来：『何某名字？为谁弟子？』叹其福德，能感大利、衣被、饮食、卧具、汤药。若与尊者相习近者，亦当丰足衣被、饮食、卧具、汤药。若复年少比丘见彼来者，大智大德，能感财利、衣被、饮食、卧具、汤药者，疾起出迎，恭敬问讯，叹言善来大智大德，能感大利、衣被、饮食、卧具、汤药。迦叶！如是年少比丘，长夜当得非义不饶益苦。

"Thus, Kāśyapa! These bhikṣus are afflicted by the state of a śramaṇa, they are submerged in the brahmacarya, they are greatly obscured by evil and unwholesome dharmas, and afflicted by afflictions. They will repeatedly experience existence, burn with birth and death, and in the future will experience the results of suffering, birth, old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress. Therefore, Kāśyapa! You should train thus: 'For the sake of the practice of the wilderness, praise and extol the practice of the wilderness, the wearing of rags, begging for food, few desires, contentment, practicing seclusion, diligent effort, right mindfulness, right concentration, right wisdom, and the realization of the exhaustion of the taints through one's own body.' You should train thus."

「如是，迦叶！斯等比丘为沙门患，为梵行溺，为大映障、恶不善法、烦恼之患，重受诸有，炽燃生死，未来苦报，生、老、病、死、忧、悲、苦、恼。是故，迦叶！当如是学：『为阿练若，于阿练若所，称誉赞叹粪扫衣、乞食，少欲知足，修行远离，精勤方便，正念正定，正智漏尽，身作证者，称誉赞叹。』当如是学。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the World-Honored One, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, the one with perfect knowledge and conduct, the Sugata, the knower of the world, the unsurpassed guide who tames beings, the teacher of gods and humans, the Buddha, the Bhagavān, having spoken this, the Venerable Mahākā

佛说此经已，尊者摩诃迦叶闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
