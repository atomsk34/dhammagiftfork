SĀ 1141

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1141（一一四一） 极老

Parallel to: SN 16.5 / SĀ² 2.116 / EĀ 12.6 / EĀ 41.5

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the Venerable Mahākāśyapa was staying in the Deer Park Lecture Hall in the Eastern Garden of Śrāvastī. In the afternoon, he emerged from meditation and went to where the World-Honored One was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side.

尔时，尊者摩诃迦叶住舍卫国东园鹿子母讲堂，晡时从禅觉，诣世尊所，稽首礼足，退坐一面。

At that time, the World-Honored One said to Mahākāśyapa, "You are now old, your faculties are mature, and the rag robes are heavy. My robes are light and good. You should now stay among the monks and wear the light robes of the householders' discarded colors."

尔时，世尊告摩诃迦叶言：「汝今已老，年耆根熟，粪扫衣重，我衣轻好，汝今可住僧中，著居士坏色轻衣。」

Kāśyapa said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I have long practiced the ascetic practices, praised the ascetic practices, rag robes, and begging for alms.

迦叶白佛言：「世尊！我已长夜习阿练若，赞叹阿练若、粪扫衣、乞食。」

The Buddha said to Kāśyapa, "You see how many kinds of meanings, practicing the ascetic practices, praising the ascetic practices, rag robes, and begging for alms, praising the Dharma of rag robes and begging for alms?"

佛告迦叶：「汝观几种义，习阿练若，赞叹阿练若、粪扫衣、乞食，赞叹粪扫衣、乞食法？」

Kāśyapa said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I see two kinds of meanings, the meaning of attaining peaceful dwelling in the present life, and also making great illumination for future sentient beings. Future sentient beings will think like this: 'The past elders with the six supernatural powers, who left home for a long time, with pure practice, praised by the World-Honored One, revered by those with wisdom and pure conduct, practiced the ascetic practices for a long time, praised the ascetic practices, rag robes, and begging for alms, praised the Dharma of rag robes and begging for alms. Those who hear it will rejoice with a pure mind, and for a long time all will attain peace, benefit, and well-being.'"

迦叶白佛言：「世尊！我观二种义，现法得安乐住义，复为未来众生，而作大明。未来世众生当如是念：『过去上座六神通，出家日久，梵行纯熟，为世尊所叹，智慧梵行者之所奉事。彼于长夜习阿练若，赞叹阿练若、粪扫衣、乞食，赞叹粪扫衣、乞食法。诸有闻者，净心随喜，长夜皆得安乐饶益。』」

The Buddha said to Kāśyapa, "Excellent! Excellent! Kāśyapa! You have benefited many for a long time, bringing peace and happiness to sentient beings, having compassion for the world, bringing peace and happiness to gods and humans."

佛告迦叶：「善哉！善哉！迦叶！汝则长夜多所饶益，安乐众生，哀愍世间，安乐天人。」

The Buddha said to Kāśyapa, "If one disparages the dhuta practices, one disparages me; if one praises the dhuta practices, one praises me. Why is this? Because the dhuta practices are what I have praised and extolled for a long time. Therefore, Kāśyapa! Those who practice asceticism should praise the practice of asceticism; those who wear rag robes and beg for alms should praise the Dharma of wearing rag robes and begging for alms."

佛告迦叶：「若有毁呰头陀法者，则毁于我；若有称叹头陀法者，则称叹我。所以者何？头陀法者，我所长夜称誉赞叹。是故，迦叶！阿练若者，当称叹阿练若；粪扫衣、乞食者，当称叹粪扫衣、乞食法。」

Having spoken this sūtra, Mahākāśyapa, having heard what the Buddha said, was delighted and followed the Buddha's teachings. He bowed and departed.

佛说此经已，摩诃迦叶闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
