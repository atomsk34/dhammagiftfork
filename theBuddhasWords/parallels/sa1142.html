SĀ 1142

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1142（一一四二） 极老

Parallel to: SN 16.9 / SĀ² 2.117

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the Venerable Mahākāśyapa had been staying for a long time in a secluded place in Śrāvastī, with long beard and hair, wearing a tattered hempen robe. He came to where the Buddha was, and at that time the World-Honored One was surrounded by countless great assemblies, expounding the Dharma.

尔时，尊者摩诃迦叶久住舍卫国阿练若床坐处，长须发，著弊纳衣，来诣佛所。尔时，世尊、无数大众围绕说法。

At that time, the bhikṣus saw Mahākāśyapa coming from afar. Having seen him, they gave rise to a disrespectful mind towards the Venerable Mahākāśyapa, saying: "What kind of bhikṣu is this? His robes are coarse and shabby, without any dignity. He comes here, his robes tattered and worn out."

时，诸比丘见摩诃迦叶从远而来，见已，于尊者摩诃迦叶所起轻慢心，言：「此何等比丘？衣服麁陋，无有仪容而来，衣服佯佯而来。」

At that time, the World-Honored One knew the thoughts in the minds of the bhikṣus. He said to Mahākāśyapa: "Welcome, Kāśyapa! On this half seat, I now know who left home first, you or me?"

尔时，世尊知诸比丘心之所念，告摩诃迦叶：「善来！迦叶！于此半座，我今竟知谁先出家，汝耶？我耶？」

Those bhikṣus were terrified, their body hairs standing on end. They said to each other: "How amazing! Venerable One! That Venerable Mahākāśyapa is a great virtuous one with great power, a disciple of the Great Teacher. He invites you to share the seat with him."

彼诸比丘心生恐怖，身毛皆竖，并相谓言：「奇哉！尊者！彼尊者摩诃迦叶，大德大力，大师弟子，请以半座。」

At that time, Venerable Mahākāśyapa joined his palms and said to the Buddha: "World-Honored One! The Buddha is my teacher, and I am his disciple."

尔时，尊者摩诃迦叶合掌白佛言：「世尊！佛是我师，我是弟子。」

The Buddha told Kāśyapa: "So it is! So it is! I am the great teacher, and you are the disciple. Now please sit down, as you are comfortable."

佛告迦叶：「如是！如是！我为大师，汝是弟子，汝今且坐，随其所安。」

Venerable Mahākāśyapa bowed at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side.

尊者摩诃迦叶稽首佛足，退坐一面。

At that time, the World-Honored One, wishing to awaken the bhikṣus again, and also to show the assembly the same extraordinary and vast merits and virtues attained by Venerable Mahākāśyapa, said to the bhikṣus: "I, having abandoned sensual desires and unwholesome states, with initial and sustained thought, dwell in the first dhyāna, either during the day or night or both day and night; Mahākāśyapa is also like me, having abandoned sensual desires and unwholesome states, up to dwelling in the first dhyāna, either during the day or night or both day and night. I dwell in the second, third, and fourth dhyānas, either during the day or night or both day and night; Mahākāśyapa is also like that. Up to dwelling in the fourth dhyāna, either during the day or night or both day and night. I dwell in loving-kindness, compassion, joy, and equanimity, as much as I wish, in the sphere of the base of infinite space, the base of infinite consciousness, the base of nothingness, and the base of neither-perception-nor-non-perception, in the sphere of the supernormal powers, in the knowledge of the divine ear, the knowledge of others' minds, the knowledge of past lives, the knowledge of death and rebirth, and the knowledge of the exhaustion of taints, either during the day or night or both day and night; Mahākāśyapa is also like that. Up to dwelling in the knowledge of the exhaustion of taints, either during the day or night or both day and night."

尔时，世尊复欲警悟诸比丘，复以尊者摩诃迦叶同己所得殊胜广大功德为现众故，告诸比丘：「我离欲、恶不善法，有觉有观，初禅具足住，若日、若夜、若日夜；摩诃迦叶亦复如我，离欲、恶不善法，乃至初禅具足住，若日、若夜、若日夜。我欲第二、第三、第四禅具足住，若日、若夜、若日夜，彼摩诃迦叶亦复如是。乃至第四禅具足住，若日、若夜、若日夜。我随所欲，慈、悲、喜、舍，空入处、识入处、无所有入处、非想非非想入处，神通境界，天耳、他心智、宿命智、生死智、漏尽智具足住，若日、若夜、若日夜；彼迦叶比丘亦复如是。乃至漏尽智具足住，若日、若夜、若日夜。」

At that time, the World-Honored One, having praised Mahākāśyapa's same vast and wonderful merits and virtues in the midst of an immeasurable great assembly, the bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them with respect.

尔时，世尊于无量大众中称叹摩诃迦叶同己广大胜妙功德已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
