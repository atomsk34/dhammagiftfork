SĀ 1143

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1143（一一四三） 此时

Parallel to: SN 16.10 / SĀ² 2.118

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha, while Venerable Mahākāśyapa and Venerable Ānanda were staying at Vulture Peak.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。尊者摩诃迦叶、尊者阿难住耆阇崛山。

At that time Venerable Ānanda went to Venerable Mahākāśyapa and said to him, "Now we should go down from Vulture Peak and enter Rājagṛha to beg for alms."

时，尊者阿难诣尊者摩诃迦叶所，语尊者摩诃迦叶言：「今可共出耆阇崛山，入王舍城乞食。」

Venerable Mahākāśyapa silently consented.

尊者摩诃迦叶默然而许。

At that time Venerable Mahākāśyapa and Venerable Ānanda put on their robes, took their bowls, and entered Rājagṛha to beg for alms. Venerable Ānanda said to Venerable Mahākāśyapa, "It is too early. Let us go to the nunnery for a while." They then went there.

时，尊者摩诃迦叶、尊者阿难著衣持钵，入王舍城乞食。尊者阿难语尊者摩诃迦叶：「日时太早，可共暂过比丘尼精舍。」即便往过。

At that time the nuns saw Venerable Mahākāśyapa and Venerable Ānanda coming from afar. They quickly prepared seats and invited them to sit down. The nuns then bowed to Venerable Mahākāśyapa and Ānanda, and sat to one side.

时，诸比丘尼遥见尊者摩诃迦叶、尊者阿难从远而来，疾敷床座，请令就坐。时，诸比丘尼礼尊者摩诃迦叶、阿难足已，退坐一面。

Venerable Mahākāśyapa taught the nuns the Dharma in various ways, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting them. Having shown, taught, illuminated, and delighted them, the nun Sthūlanandā was displeased and spoke these evil words: "How can Venerable Mahākāśyapa teach the Dharma to the nuns in front of Venerable Ānanda, the Vidiṭṭhamuni? It is like a needle seller trying to sell needles to a needle maker. Venerable Mahākāśyapa is also like this, teaching the Dharma to the nuns in front of Venerable Ānanda, the Vidiṭṭhamuni.

尊者摩诃迦叶为诸比丘尼种种说法，示、教、照、喜。示、教、照、喜已，时偷罗难陀比丘尼不喜悦，说如是恶言：「云何？阿梨摩诃迦叶于阿梨阿难，鞞提诃牟尼前为比丘尼说法？譬如贩针儿于针师家卖，阿梨摩诃迦叶亦复如是，于阿梨阿难，鞞提诃牟尼前为诸比丘尼说法。」

Venerable Mahākāśyapa heard the nun Sthūlanandā's displeasure and evil words. Having heard it, he said to Venerable Ānanda: "Look, Ānanda! This nun Sthūlanandā is displeased and speaks evil words. What do you think, Ānanda? Am I a needle seller trying to sell needles in front of you, the needle maker?"

尊者摩诃迦叶闻偷罗难陀比丘尼心不喜悦，口说恶言。闻已，语尊者阿难：「汝看！是偷罗难陀比丘尼心不喜悦，口说恶言。云何？阿难！我是贩针儿，汝是针师，于汝前卖耶？」

Venerable Ānanda said to Venerable Mahākāśyapa: "Please stop! Be patient! This foolish old woman has little wisdom and has never cultivated it."

尊者阿难语尊者摩诃迦叶：「且止！当忍！此愚痴老妪，智慧薄少，不曾修习故。」

"Ānanda! Have you not heard the World-Honored One, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, who knows and sees, teach and instruct in the assembly with the Moon Simile Sūtra? Bhikṣus should abide like the moon, always like a beginner. Thus it is extensively taught. Ānanda! Do you abide like the moon, always like a beginner?"

「阿难！汝岂闻世尊、如来、应、等正觉所知见，于大众中说月譬经，教诫、教授。比丘！当如月譬住，常如新学。如是广说为说，阿难！如月譬住，常如新学耶？」

Ānanda replied, "No, Venerable Mahākāśyapa!"

阿难答言：「不也，尊者摩诃迦叶！」

"Ānanda! Have you heard the World-Honored One, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, who knows and sees, say, 'Bhikṣus should abide like the moon, always like a beginner. Only the bhikṣu Mahākāśyapa'?"

「阿难！汝闻世尊、如来、应、等正觉所知所见说言：『比丘！当如月譬住，常如新学，其唯摩诃迦叶比丘！』？」

Ānanda replied, "Yes, Venerable Mahākāśyapa!"

阿难答言：「如是，尊者摩诃迦叶！」

"Ānanda! Have you ever been known and seen by the World-Honored One, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, to be invited to sit in an assembly of immeasurable people? And has the World-Honored One praised you with his own vast virtues, saying, 'Ānanda is free from desire, evil and unwholesome dharmas, up to the attainment of the supernormal knowledges'?"

「阿难！汝曾为世尊、如来、应、等正觉所知所见，于无量大众中请汝来坐耶？又复世尊以同己广大之德称叹汝：『阿难离欲、恶不善法，乃至漏尽通。』称叹？」

He replied, "No, Venerable Mahākāśyapa!"

答言：「不也，尊者摩诃迦叶！」

"In the same way, Ānanda! The World-Honored One, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, in an assembly of immeasurable people, himself said, 'Welcome, Mahākāśyapa! I invite you to share my seat.' And in the assembly, did he praise Mahākāśyapa with his own vast virtues, saying, 'Free from desire, evil and unwholesome dharmas, up to the attainment of the supernormal knowledges'?"

「如是，阿难！世尊、如来、应、等正觉于无量大众中，口自说言：『善来摩诃迦叶！请汝半座。』复于大众中以同己广大功德，离欲、恶不善法，乃至漏尽通，称叹摩诃迦叶耶？」

Ānanda replied, "Yes, Venerable Mahākāśyapa!"

阿难答言：「如是，尊者摩诃迦叶！」

At that time, Mahākāśyapa had finished his lion's roar among the bhikṣuṇīs.

时，摩诃迦叶于比丘尼众中师子吼已。
