SĀ 1144

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1144（一一四四） 众减少

Parallel to: SN 16.11 / SĀ² 2.119

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Venerable Mahākāśyapa and the Venerable Ānanda were staying in Rājagṛha on Mount Gṛdhrakūṭa, not long after the Tathāgata's parinirvāṇa. At that time, there was a famine in the world and it was difficult to obtain alms.

一时，尊者摩诃迦叶、尊者阿难住王舍城耆阇崛山中，世尊涅槃未久。时，世饥馑，乞食难得。

At that time, the Venerable Ānanda was with a group of young bhikṣus who were unable to properly restrain their faculties, ate without moderation, and were not diligent in meditation during the early and late parts of the night. They enjoyed sleeping and always sought worldly benefits. While traveling in the human world, they arrived in southern India, where thirty of the young disciples abandoned the precepts and returned to lay life, leaving many young boys behind. At that time, the Venerable Ānanda was traveling in the southern mountain region with a small group of disciples and returned to Rājagṛha. The Venerable Ānanda then put away his robes and alms bowl, washed his feet, and went to where the Venerable Mahākāśyapa was. He bowed his head at Mahākāśyapa's feet, withdrew and sat to one side.

时，尊者阿难与众多年少比丘俱，不能善摄诸根，食不知量，不能初夜、后夜精懃禅思，乐著睡眠，常求世利。人间游行至南天竺，有三十年少弟子舍戒还俗，余多童子。时，尊者阿难于南山国土游行，以少徒众还王舍城。时，尊者阿难举衣钵，洗足已，至尊者摩诃迦叶所，稽首礼足，退坐一面。

At that time, the Venerable Mahākāśyapa asked the Venerable Ānanda, "Where have you come from? Your disciples are few in number."

时，尊者摩诃迦叶问尊者阿难：「汝从何来？徒众尠少？」

Ānanda replied, "I was traveling in the human world in the southern mountain region. Thirty of the young bhikṣus abandoned the precepts and returned to lay life, and the remaining disciples are mostly young boys."

阿难答言：「从南山国土人间游行，年少比丘三十人舍戒还俗，徒众损减，又今在者多是童子。」

The Venerable Mahākāśyapa said to Ānanda, "What are the few benefits that the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One knows and sees, allowing the rule of eating in groups for three or more people?"

尊者摩诃迦叶语阿难言：「有几福利，如来、应、等正觉所知所见，听三人已上制群食戒？」

Ānanda replied, "It is for two reasons. What are the two? First, for the sake of poor and small families. Second, to prevent many evil people from becoming their companions and destroying each other. Do not allow evil people to live in the Sangha, receive the name of the Sangha, obstruct the great assembly, and form two separate groups, hating and disputing with each other."

阿难答言：「为二事故。何等为二？一者为贫小家，二者多诸恶人以为伴党，相破坏故。莫令恶人于僧中住，而受众名，映障大众，别为二部，互相嫌诤。」

The Venerable Kāśyapa said to Ānanda, "You know this meaning. How can you travel to the southern mountains and countries with many young disciples during a famine, causing thirty people to abandon the precepts and return to lay life, reducing the number of disciples? The rest are mostly boys. Ānanda! Your disciples are disappearing. You are a boy yourself, not knowing how to plan."

尊者迦叶语阿难言：「汝知此义，如何于饥馑时，与众多年少弟子南山国土游行，令三十人舍戒还俗，徒众损减，余者多是童子？如阿难！汝徒众消灭，汝是童子，不知筹量。」

Ānanda replied, "Venerable Mahākāśyapa, how can I be called a boy just because my hair is two-colored?"

阿难答言：「云何？尊者摩诃迦叶！我以头发二色，犹言童子？」

The Venerable Mahākāśyapa said, "You traveled through the human realm with young disciples during a famine, causing thirty disciples to abandon the precepts and return to lay life. The rest are also boys. The assembly is disappearing. You do not know how to plan. You say the assembly of elders is destroyed. Ānanda! The assembly is completely destroyed. Ānanda! You are a boy. You do not know how to plan."

尊者摩诃迦叶言：「汝于饥馑世，与诸年少弟子人间游行，致令三十弟子舍戒还俗，其余在者复是童子。徒众消灭，不知筹量，而言宿士众坏，阿难！众极坏，阿难！汝是童子，不筹量故。」

At that time, the bhikṣuṇī Dīpā heard that the Venerable Mahākāśyapa blamed the Venerable Ānanda and the Venerable Vītaśoka as boys. Having heard this, she was displeased and spoke these evil words: "How can the Venerable Mahākāśyapa, a former non-Buddhist, blame the Venerable Ānanda and the Venerable Vītaśoka as boys, causing the name 'boy' to spread?"

时，低舍比丘尼闻尊者摩诃迦叶以童子责尊者阿难，毘提诃牟尼，闻已不欢喜，作是恶言：「云何？阿梨摩诃迦叶本外道闻，而已童子呵责阿梨阿难，毘提诃牟尼，令童子名流行？」

The Venerable Mahākāśyapa heard with his divine ear that the bhikṣuṇī Dīpā was displeased and spoke evil words. Having heard this, he said to the Venerable Ānanda, "Look! This bhikṣuṇī Dīpā is displeased and speaks evil words, saying, 'Mahākāśyapa, having heard the non-Buddhists, blames the Venerable Ānanda and the Venerable Vītaśoka as boys, causing the name 'boy' to spread.'"

尊者摩诃迦叶以天耳闻低舍比丘尼心不欢喜，口出恶言。闻已，语尊者阿难：「汝看！是低舍比丘尼心不欢喜，口说恶语，言：『摩诃迦叶本闻外道，而责阿梨阿难，毘提诃牟尼，令童子名流行。』」

The Venerable Ānanda replied, "Stop it, Venerable Mahākāśyapa! Be patient, Venerable Mahākāśyapa! This foolish old woman lacks self-nature wisdom."

尊者阿难答言：「且止！尊者摩诃迦叶！忍之！尊者摩诃迦叶！此愚痴老妪无自性智。」

The Venerable Mahākāśyapa said to Ānanda, "Since I went forth, I have never known of any other teacher except the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One. Before I went forth, I always thought of birth, old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, suffering, and distress. Knowing that household life is busy and full of afflictions, I went forth into the empty and secluded life, difficult for ordinary people to dwell in. I dwelt in non-home, single-mindedly pure, fulfilling the pure and bright practice of the brahman to the end of my life. I shaved my beard and hair, put on the saffron robes, and with right faith left home to study the path. I cut up a hundred thousand pieces of precious clothing into a saṃghāṭī robe. If there were arhats in the world, I would have gone forth in darkness. After I went forth, between Rājagṛha and Nālaka, at the stūpa of the many sons, I encountered the World-Honored One sitting upright with a dignified body, with wondrous and extraordinary characteristics, with tranquil faculties, the foremost cessation of breath, like a golden mountain.

尊者摩诃迦叶语阿难言：「我自出家，都不知有异师，唯如来、应、等正觉。我未出家时，常念生、老、病、死、忧、悲、恼苦，知在家荒务，多诸烦恼，出家空闲，难可俗人处于非家，一向鲜洁，尽其形寿，纯一满净，梵行清白。当剃须发，著袈裟衣，正信非家，出家学道，以百千金贵价之衣，段段割截为僧伽梨，若世间阿罗汉者，暗从出家。我出家已，于王舍城那罗聚落中间多子塔所，遇值世尊正身端坐，相好奇特，诸根寂静，第一息灭，犹如金山。

When I saw this, I thought, 'This is my teacher! This is the World-Honored One! This is the Arhat! This is the Perfectly Enlightened One!' I then single-mindedly joined my palms in respect and said to the Buddha, 'This is my great teacher! I am your disciple.'

「我时见已，作是念：『此是我师！此是世尊！此是罗汉！此是等正觉！』我时一心合掌敬礼，白佛言：『是我大师！我是弟子。』

"The Buddha told me, 'So it is, Kāśyapa! I am your teacher! You are my disciple. Kāśyapa! You now accomplish such true and pure respect. Not knowing, you say you know; not seeing, you say you see. Truly not an arhat, you say you are an arhat. Not a perfectly enlightened one, you say you are a perfectly enlightened one. You should naturally be shattered into seven pieces. Kāśyapa! I now know and say I know; see and say I see. Truly an arhat, I say I am an arhat. Truly a perfectly enlightened one, I say I am a perfectly enlightened one. Kāśyapa! I now have causes and conditions, and therefore teach the Dharma to the hearers, not without causes and conditions. Relying, not without relying; with spiritual power, not without spiritual power. Therefore, Kāśyapa! If you wish to hear the Dharma, you should study thus: "If you wish to hear the Dharma, for the sake of meaning and benefit, you should unify your mind, respect and honor it, listen attentively, and think thus: 'I should correctly observe the arising and ceasing of the five aggregates, the arising and ceasing of the six sense bases, and abide in joy with right mindfulness in the four foundations of mindfulness. Cultivate the seven factors of awakening and the eight liberations, realize them with the body, always be mindful of the body, never interrupted, free from shamelessness and lack of conscience, and always abide in shame and conscience towards the great teacher and the great virtuous ones.'" You should study thus!'"

「佛告我言：『如是，迦叶！我是汝师！汝是弟子。迦叶！汝今成就如是真实净心所恭敬者。不知言知，不见言见，实非罗汉而言罗汉，非等正觉言等正觉者，应当自然身碎七分。迦叶！我今知故言知，见故言见，真阿罗汉言阿罗汉，真等正觉言等正觉。迦叶！我今有因缘故，为声闻说法，非无因缘故。依，非无依；有神力，非无神力。是故，迦叶！若欲闻法，应如是学：「若欲闻法，以义饶益，当一其心，恭敬尊重，专心侧听，而作是念：『我当正观五阴生灭，六触入处集起、灭没，于四念处正念乐住，修七觉分、八解脱身作证，常念其身，未甞断绝，离无惭愧，于大师所及大德梵行常住惭愧。』」如是应当学！』

At that time, the World-Honored One taught the Dharma for me, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting me. Having shown, taught, illuminated, and delighted me, he rose from his seat and left. I also followed him to his dwelling place. I cut up a robe worth a hundred thousand in price and made it into a seat. At that time, the World-Honored One knew my sincere mind and went down the road here and there. I then spread out the robe and offered it as a seat, inviting the Buddha to sit. The World-Honored One then sat down and stroked the robe with his hand, praising, 'Kāśyapa! This robe is light and fine, this robe is soft.' I then said, 'So it is, World-Honored One! This robe is light and fine, this robe is soft. I only wish the World-Honored One would accept this robe of mine!' The Buddha told Kāśyapa, 'You should accept my rag robe, and I will accept your saṃghāṭī.' The Buddha then personally handed me a rag robe, and I then offered the saṃghāṭī to the Buddha. In this way, he gradually instructed me. Within eight days, I accepted alms food through the Dharma of learning, and on the ninth day, I attained the state of no more learning.

「尔时，世尊为我说法，示、教、照、喜；示、教、照、喜已，从座起去。我亦随去，向于住处。我以百千价直衣割截僧伽梨，四摄为座。尔时，世尊知我至心，处处下道，我即敷衣，以为坐具，请佛令坐。世尊即坐，以手摩衣，叹言：『迦叶！此衣轻细，此衣柔软。』我时白言：『如是。世尊！此衣轻细，此衣柔软，唯愿世尊受我此衣！』佛告迦叶：『汝当受我粪扫衣，我当受汝僧伽梨。』佛即自手授我粪扫纳衣，我即奉佛僧伽梨。如是渐渐教授，我八日之中，以学法受于乞食，至第九日，起于无学。

"Ānanda! If there is a correct question: 'Who is the World-Honored One's Dharma son, born from the Buddha's mouth, born from the Dharma, entrusted with the Dharma wealth, the various dhyānas, liberations, samādhis, and samāpattis?' One should answer that I am. This is the correct explanation. It is like the eldest son of a wheel-turning sage king, who should be enthroned and enjoy the five desires of a king, effortlessly and naturally. I am also like this, as the Buddha's Dharma son, born from the Buddha's mouth, born from the Dharma, obtaining the remaining Dharma wealth, the dhyānas, liberations, samādhis, and samāpattis, effortlessly and naturally. It is like the precious elephant of a wheel-turning sage king, seven or eight cubits tall, which can be obscured by a single palm leaf. So too, the six supernormal knowledges I have accomplished can be obscured. If there is any doubt about the knowledge of the realm of supernormal knowledge, I can explain and record it for them. If there is any doubt about the knowledge of divine hearing, mind-reading, past lives, death and rebirth, and the realization of the destruction of the taints, I can explain and record it for them, causing them to attain certainty."

「阿难！若有正问：『谁是世尊法子，从佛口生、从法化生，付以法财，诸禅、解脱、三昧、正受？』应答我是，是则正说。譬如转轮圣王第一长子，当以灌顶，住于王位，受王五欲，不苦方便自然而得，我亦如是，为佛法子，从佛口生、从法化生，得法余财法，禅、解脱、三昧、正受，不苦方便自然而得。譬如转轮圣王宝象，高七八肘，一多罗叶能映障者。如是我所成就六神通智，则可映障，若有于神通境界智证有疑惑者，我悉能为分别记说，天耳、他心通、宿命智、生死智、漏尽作证智通有疑惑者，我悉能为分别记说，令得决定。」

Venerable Ānanda said to Venerable Mahākāśyapa, "So it is! So it is! Mahākāśyapa! It is like the precious elephant of a wheel-turning sage king, seven or eight cubits tall, which can be obscured by a single palm leaf. So too, Venerable Mahākāśyapa's six supernormal knowledges can be obscured. If there is any doubt about the knowledge of the realm of supernormal knowledge, up to the knowledge of the realization of the destruction of the taints, Venerable Mahākāśyapa can explain and record it for them, causing them to attain certainty. I have long respected and revered Venerable Mahākāśyapa because of his great supernatural powers.

尊者阿难语尊者摩诃迦叶：「如是！如是！摩诃迦叶！如转轮圣王宝象，高七八肘，欲以一多罗叶能映障者。如是，尊者摩诃迦叶六神通智则可映障，若有于神通境界作证智，乃至漏尽作证智有疑惑者，尊者摩诃迦叶能为记说，令其决定。我于长夜敬信尊重尊者摩诃迦叶，以有如是大德神力故。」

When Venerable Mahākāśyapa had spoken these words, Venerable Ānanda, having heard them, joyfully accepted and upheld them.

尊者摩诃迦叶说是语时，尊者阿难闻其所说，欢喜受持。
