SĀ 1150

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1150（一一五〇） 喘息

Parallel to: SN 3.13 / SĀ² 2.73

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time King Prasenajit, whose body was large and fat, came to where the Buddha was, sweating all over. He bowed his head at the Buddha’s feet, sat to one side, and breathed heavily.

时，波斯匿王其体肥大，举体流汗，来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面，气息长喘。

At that time the World-Honored One said to King Prasenajit, “Great King, your body is extremely fat.”

尔时，世尊告波斯匿王：「大王身体极肥盛。」

The Great King said to the Buddha, “Yes, World-Honored One! I am afflicted by my large and fat body. Because of this extremely large and fat body, I am ashamed and weary of suffering.”

大王白佛言：「如是，世尊！患身肥大，常以此身极肥大故，惭耻厌苦。」

At that time the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

People should bind their minds and know moderation in eating
This will reduce all afflictions, digest peacefully, and preserve life.

「人当自系念，　　每食知节量，是则诸受薄，　　安消而保寿。」

At that time, there was a young man named Uttara sitting in the assembly. King Prasenajit said to Uttara, "Can you receive the verse just spoken by the World-Honored One and recite it for me at mealtimes? If you can, I will give you one hundred thousand gold coins and also provide you with food."

时，有一年少，名欝多罗，于会中坐。时，波斯匿王告欝多罗：「汝能从世尊受向所说偈，每至食时，为我诵不？若能尔者，赐金钱十万，亦常与食。」

Uttara said to the king, "I will respectfully recite it as instructed."

欝多罗白王：「奉教当诵。」

At that time, King Prasenajit, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully rejoiced, paid homage, and departed.

时，波斯匿王闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。

At that time, Uttara, knowing that the king had left, went before the World-Honored One, received the verses spoken, and recited them at the king's mealtime. He said to the great king, "As the Buddha, the World-Honored One, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, knows and sees, he speaks these verses:

时，欝多罗知王去已，至世尊前，受所说偈，于王食时，食食为诵，白言大王：「如佛世尊、如来、应、等正觉所知所见，而说斯偈：

One should bind one's own mind, and at every meal know moderation. Then the
afflictions will be thin, and one will be at ease, digesting and preserving life.

「『人当自系念，　　每食知节量，是则诸受薄，　　安消而保寿。』」

Thus, King Prasenajit gradually, after some time, became physically emaciated, with a handsome appearance. Standing on a pavilion, he joined his palms in respect towards the Buddha's abode, touched his right knee to the ground, and said three times, "I pay homage to the World-Honored One, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One. I pay homage to the World-Honored One, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One. He brings me present-life benefits, future-life benefits, and present-life and future-life benefits. It is because of knowing moderation in food."

如是，波斯匿王渐至后时，身体佣细，容貌端正，处楼阁上，向佛住处合掌恭敬，右膝著地，三说是言：「南无敬礼世尊、如来、应、等正觉，南无敬礼世尊、如来、应、等正觉，与我现法利益、后世利益、现法后世利益，以其饭食知节量故。」
