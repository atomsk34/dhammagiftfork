SĀ 1157

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1157（一一五七） 罗阁

Parallel to: SN 7.12 / SĀ² 2.80

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha. In the morning, the World-Honored One put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and entered Rājagṛha to beg for alms. He walked in order, begging for alms, until he reached the house of the brāhman Hu-yu.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。世尊晨朝著衣持钵，入王舍城乞食，次第行乞至火与婆罗门舍。

The brāhman Hu-yu saw the Buddha coming from afar, and immediately filled his bowl with delicious food.

火与婆罗门遥见佛来，即具众美饮食，满钵与之。

For two days and three days, he begged for alms and came to his house again.

如是二日、三日，乞食复至其舍。

The brāhman Hu-yu saw the Buddha coming from afar and thought, "Why does this bald-headed śramaṇa come so often, greedy for delicious food?"

火与婆罗门遥见佛来，作是念：「秃头沙门何故数来，贪美食耶？」

At that time, the World-Honored One knew the thoughts of the brāhman Hu-yu and immediately spoke a verse:

尔时，世尊知火与婆罗门心念已，即说偈言：

The king's heaven rains daily, the farmer plows day and night, planting seeds
again and again, reaping grain again and again. Like a woman who is
pregnant again and again, a milk cow that calves again and again, those
who have many requests are able to give again and again. Because they
give again and again, they always gain great fame. They discard corpses again
and again, crying and grieving again and again. They are born again and
again, die again and again, and grieve and suffer again and again. They
are burned by fire again and again, eaten by insects again and again
If one attains the noble path, one does not again and again undergo
existence, nor does one again and again be born and die, nor does
one again and again grieve and suffer. One is not burned by fire
again and again, nor eaten by insects again and again.

「王天日日雨，　　田夫日夜耕，数数殖种子，　　是田数收谷。如人数怀妊，　　乳牛数怀犊，数数有求者，　　则能数惠施。数数惠施故，　　常得大名称，数数弃死尸，　　数数哭悲恋。数数生数死，　　数数忧悲苦，数数以火烧，　　数数诸虫食。若得贤圣道，　　不数受诸有，亦不数生死，　　不数忧悲苦。不数数火烧，　　不数诸虫食。」

At that time, the brāhman Hu-yu heard the Buddha speak the verse and regained his faith. He again filled the bowl with various foods and offered it to him. The World-Honored One did not accept it. Because it was offered due to the cause of speaking the verse, he again spoke a verse:

时，火与婆罗门闻佛说偈，还得信心，复以种种饮食满钵与之，世尊不受，以因说偈而施故，复说偈言：

Because of the Dharma of speaking verses, one should not accept food
and drink. One should observe one's own Dharma and not accept food
while speaking the Dharma. The brāhman should know that this is the
livelihood of purity. One should make other offerings to the great sage
who is pure. Having exhausted all afflictions, defiled dharmas have all been
cut off. Making offerings with food and drink is a good field
of merit. Those who seek merit and virtue should make me their good field.

「因为说偈法，　　不应受饮食，当观察自法，　　说法不受食。婆罗门当知，　　斯则净命活，应以余供养，　　纯净大仙人。已尽诸有漏，　　秽法悉已断，供养以饮食，　　于其良福田。欲求福德者，　　则我田为良。」

The brāhman Hu-yu said to the Buddha, "Now, where should I put this food?"

火与婆罗门白佛：「今以此食，应著何所？」

The Buddha said to the brāhman, "I do not see any gods, demons, Brahmas, śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, devas, humans, or anyone else in the world who can eat this offering of faith and make the body feel at ease. Take this food and throw it into water that is free from insects and on land with little grass."

佛告婆罗门：「我不见诸天、魔、梵、沙门、婆罗门、天神、世人有能食此信施，令身安乐。汝持是食去弃于无虫水中，及少生草地。」

At that time, the brāhman took the food and put it in water free from insects. The water immediately emitted smoke and made a sizzling sound. It was like throwing a red-hot iron ball into water, causing the water to emit smoke and make a sizzling sound. When the brāhman put the food in the water, the water immediately emitted smoke and made a sizzling sound. At that time, the brāhman Hu-yu praised, "Very wonderful! Gautama! The Venerable One has great power, able to make this food perform a miracle."

时，婆罗门即以此食持著无虫水中，水即烟出，沸声啾啾；譬如铁丸烧令火色，掷著水中，水即烟起，沸声啾啾，亦复如是。婆罗门持此饮食著水中，水即烟出，沸声啾啾，于时火与婆罗门叹言：「甚奇！瞿昙！大德大力，能令此食而作神变。」

At that time, the brāhman Hu-yu, because of the miraculous power of the food, gained faith and respect. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and stood to one side, saying to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! May I now be able to go forth and receive full ordination in the true Dharma to practice the holy life?"

时，火与婆罗门因此饭食神变，得信敬心，稽首佛足，退住一面，白佛言：「世尊！我今可得于正法中出家、受具足，修梵行不？」

The Buddha told the brāhman, "You may now go forth and receive full ordination in the true Dharma."

佛告婆罗门：「汝今可得于正法中出家、受具足。」

Having gone forth, he thought, "The reason sons of noble families shave their heads and beards, put on the saffron robes, leave home out of right faith, and go forth to practice the path..." and so forth, up to attaining arahantship with well-liberated minds.

彼即出家已，作是思惟：「所以族姓子剃除发须，著袈裟衣，正信非家，出家学道……。」乃至得阿罗汉，心善解脱。
