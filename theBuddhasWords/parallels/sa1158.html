SĀ 1158

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1158（一一五八） 婆私咤

Parallel to: SN 1.71 / SN 2.3 / SN 7.1 / SN 11.21 / SĀ 1116 / SĀ 1309 / SĀ² 2.45 / SĀ² 2.81 / SĀ² 2.308

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, in Śrāvastī, there was a brāhman woman named Vārṣaka who had faith in the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṅgha. She took refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṅgha, and was free from doubts regarding the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṅgha. She was also free from doubts regarding suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path. She saw the truths and attained the fruits, and gained uninterrupted wisdom. Her husband was a brāhman of the Brāhmaṇḍa clan. Whenever she was doing work and had a little gain or loss, she would say, "Namu Buddha!" facing the direction where the Tathāgata was staying, and with palms joined, she would say three times, "Namu Tathāgata, Arhat, Samyak-Saṃbuddha, whose body is pure golden in color, with a halo of light one fathom in diameter, whose body is square and full like a nyagrodha tree, who speaks the wonderful Dharma well, who is the most venerable of sages, the foremost of seers, my great teacher!"

时，舍卫国中婆肆咤婆罗门女信佛、法、僧，归佛、归法、归比丘僧。于佛、法、僧已离狐疑，于苦习尽道亦离疑惑；见谛得果，得无间慧。其夫是婆罗豆婆遮种姓婆罗门。每至左右所为作时，有小得失，即称南无佛，向如来所住方面，随方合掌，三说是言：「南无多陀阿伽度、阿罗呵、三藐三佛陀，身纯金色，圆光一寻，方身圆满如尼拘律树，善说妙法，牟尼之尊，仙人上首，是我大师！」

When her husband, the brāhman, heard this, he became angry and said to his wife, "Are you possessed by a ghost? This makes no sense. To abandon the brāhman masters of the three knowledges and praise that bald-headed śramaṇa, who is of the dark and inferior class, is not praised by the world. I will now go and debate with your great teacher to see who is superior."

时，夫婆罗门闻之，瞋恚不喜，语其妇言：「为鬼著耶？无有此义，舍诸三明大德婆罗门，而称叹彼秃头沙门。黑暗之分，世所不称。我今当往共汝大师论议，足知胜如。」

The wife said to her husband, "I have never seen any gods, demons, Brahmā, śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, spirits, or worldly people who can debate with the World-Honored One - the Tathāgata, Arhat, Samyak-Saṃbuddha, whose body is golden in color, with a halo of light one fathom in diameter, whose body is square and full like a nyagrodha tree, whose speech is subtle and wonderful, who is the foremost of sages, my great teacher." "However, you, brāhman, may go now and find out for yourself!"

妇语夫言：「不见诸天、魔、梵、沙门、婆罗门、诸神、世人，能共世尊——如来、应、等正觉，金色之身，圆光一寻，如尼拘律树圆满之身，言说微妙，仙人上首，我之大师——共论议者。然今婆罗门且往，自可知之！」

Then the brāhman went to where the Buddha was, exchanged greetings and pleasantries, and sat to one side. He spoke in verse:

时，婆罗门即往诣佛，面相问讯慰劳已，退坐一面。而说偈言：

For killing what can one sleep peacefully? For
killing what can one's mind be free from
sorrow? For killing what is praised by Gautama?

「为杀于何等，　　而得安隐眠？为杀于何等，　　令心得无忧？为杀于何等，　　瞿昙所称叹？」

At that time, the World-Honored One knew the thoughts in the brāhman's mind and spoke in verse:

尔时，世尊知婆罗门心之所念，而说偈言：

Killing anger and resentment, one can sleep peacefully. Killing anger
and resentment, one's mind can be free from sorrow. Anger
and resentment are the root of poison, able to harm
the sweet seeds. Able to harm them, the sages praise
it. If one can harm them, one's mind will be
free from sorrow.

「杀于瞋恨者，　　而得安隐眠，杀于瞋恚者，　　而心得无忧。瞋恚为毒本，　　能害甘种子，能害于彼者，　　贤圣所称叹。若能害彼者，　　其心得无忧？」

At that time, the brāhman Parāparāparā heard the Buddha's teaching, guidance, illumination, and joy, and the gradual teaching of the Dharma, namely, teaching giving, teaching precepts, teaching the Dharma of rebirth in heaven, teaching the flavors and attachments of desire as afflictions, purity, liberation, and detachment, following the benefits of purity, and explaining in detail. It is like pure white cloth, which is easy to be dyed. In this way, the brāhman Parāparāparā attained unobstructed equality with regard to the four noble truths right on his seat, namely, suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path.

时，婆罗豆婆遮婆罗门闻佛所说，示、教、照、喜，次第说法，谓说施，说戒，说生天法，说欲味著为灾患烦恼，清净、出要、远离，随顺福利清净，分别广说；譬如清净白［叠＊毛］，易为染色。如是，婆罗豆婆遮婆罗门即于座上，于四圣谛得无阂等，所谓苦、集、灭、道。

This brāhman saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, knew the Dharma, entered the Dharma, transcended all doubts, and was not led by others. He attained fearlessness in the true Dharma and Vinaya. He then rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms, and said to the Buddha, "I am liberated, World-Honored One! I am liberated, Well-Gone One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Saṅgha of bhikṣus. May you bear witness that I am a lay devotee for as long as I live!"

是婆罗门见法、得法、知法、入法，度诸疑惑，不由他度，于正法、律得无所畏。即从座起，偏露右肩，合掌白佛：「已度，世尊！已度，善逝！我今归佛、归法、归比丘僧已，尽其寿命为优婆塞，证知我！」

At that time, the brāhman Parāparāparā, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and followed it. He bowed and departed, returning to his own home.

时，婆罗豆婆遮婆罗门闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去，还归自家。

His wife, the upāsikā, saw her husband coming from afar. Seeing him, she said, "Have you already discussed with the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One - the one with a body of pure gold, a halo one fathom in diameter, a body as full as a nyagrodha tree, the foremost in eloquent teaching, the foremost among sages, the Great Sage - my teacher?"

其妇优婆夷遥见夫来，见已，白言：「已与如来、应、等正觉——纯金色身，圆光一寻，如尼拘律树圆满之身，妙说之上，仙人之首，大牟尼尊，为我大师——共论议耶？」

Her husband replied, "I have never seen any gods, demons, Brahmas, śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, spirits, or worldly people who can discuss with the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One - the one with a body of pure gold, a halo one fathom in diameter, a body as full as a nyagrodha tree, the foremost in eloquent teaching, the foremost among sages, the Great Sage, your teacher." "Now, please make good Dharma robes for me. I will take them to the World-Honored One to renounce the householder life and study the path."

其夫答言：「我未甞见诸天、魔、梵、沙门、婆罗门、诸神、世人，有能与如来、应、等正觉——真金色身，圆光一寻，如尼拘律树圆满之身，妙说之上，诸仙之首，牟尼之尊，汝之大师——共论议也。汝今与我作好法衣，我持至世尊所出家学道。」

At that time, the wife used fresh and clean white cloth to make Dharma robes.

时，妇悉以鲜洁白［叠＊毛］，令作法衣。

At that time, the brāhmaṇa took the robes and went to where the World-Honored One was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stepped back to one side, and said, "World-Honored One! May I now be able to renounce the householder life and study the path, practicing the holy life in the World-Honored One's Dharma?"

时，婆罗门持衣往诣世尊所，稽首礼足，退住一面，白言：「世尊！我今可得于世尊法中出家学道，修梵行不？」

The Buddha told the brāhmaṇa, "You may now renounce the householder life and study the path in this Dharma and Vinaya, practicing the holy life."

佛告婆罗门：「汝今可得于此法、律出家学道，修诸梵行。」

Having renounced the householder life, he thought to himself in solitude, "The reason good sons shave their heads, put on the saffron robes, renounce the householder life, and study the path..." and so forth, up to attaining arahantship with a well-liberated mind.

即出家已，独静思惟：「所以善男子剃除须发，著袈裟衣，出家学道……。」乃至得阿罗汉，心善解脱。
