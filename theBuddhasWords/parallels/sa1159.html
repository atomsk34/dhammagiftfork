SĀ 1159

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1159（一一五九） 摩佉

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, a certain Mātaṇga brāhman came to where the Buddha was, and after exchanging greetings with the World-Honored One, he sat to one side and said to the Buddha, "Gautama! I always practice giving in my home. If one person comes, I give to one person; if two or three people, up to hundreds or thousands, I give to them all. Do I gain much merit by giving like this?"

时，有魔瞿婆罗门来诣佛所，与世尊面相问讯慰劳已，退坐一面，白佛言：「瞿昙！我于家中常行布施，若一人来，施于一人；若二人、三人，乃至百千，悉皆施与。我如是施，得多福不？」

The Buddha told the brāhman, "Your giving like this truly gains great merit. Why is that? Because you always practice giving in your home. When one person comes to beg, you give to one person. When two or three people, up to hundreds or thousands, come, you give to them all. Therefore, you gain great merit."

佛告婆罗门：「汝如是施，实得大福。所以者何？以于家中常行布施，一人来乞，即施一人，二人、三人，乃至百千，悉皆施与故，即得大福。」

At that time, the Mātaṇga brāhman spoke a verse, saying:

时，魔瞿婆罗门即说偈言：

The deeds done at home, giving and great assemblies, because
of this giving, seeking great merit. Now I ask the
Sage, what I should know, the same as the vision
of Brahma Heaven, please explain to me. What is liberation
the supreme and wonderful good destination? How to cultivate means
to be born in the Brahma Heaven? How to give
according to pleasure, to be born in the bright and
supreme Brahma Heaven?

「在家所为作，　　布施复大会，因此惠施故，　　欲求大功德。今问于牟尼，　　我之所应知，同梵天所见，　　为我分别说。云何为解脱，　　胜妙之善趣？云何修方便，　　得生于梵世？云何随乐施，　　生明胜梵天？」

At that time, the World Honored One spoke a verse in reply,

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

The giver sets up a great assembly, giving according to
their loved ones' wishes, with joyful and pure faith, taking
hold of the good merits. With what they have established
seeking to leave all faults and evils, far removed from
greed and desire, their minds are well liberated. Cultivating the
mind of loving-kindness, their merits are immeasurable, let alone adding
sincerity and widely setting up a great assembly. If in
the meantime, the good minds obtained, correctly heading towards good
liberation, or other pure good destinations. Such supreme causes and
conditions, can be born in the Brahma Heaven, such giving
because the mind is equal. Can be born in the
Brahma Heaven, their lifespan is prolonged.

「施者设大会，　　随彼爱乐施，欢喜净信心，　　攀缘善功德。以其所建立，　　求离诸过恶，远离于贪欲，　　其心善解脱。修习于慈心，　　其功德无量，况复加至诚，　　广施设大会。若于其中间，　　所得诸善心，正向善解脱，　　或余纯善趣。如是胜因缘，　　得生于梵世，如是之惠施，　　其心平等故。得生于梵世，　　其寿命延长。」

At that time, the Brahmaṇas of the Māra clan heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced, and left their seats.

时，魔瞿婆罗门闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，从座起而去。
