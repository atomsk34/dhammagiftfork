SĀ 1160

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1160（一一六〇） 刹利

Parallel to: SN 7.7 / SĀ² 2.83

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, a brāhman named Dhṛtarāṣṭra, holding a golden parasol and wearing a robe, came to where the Buddha was. He exchanged greetings with the World-Honored One and sat to one side. He then spoke a verse, saying:

时，有持金盖、著舍勒导从婆罗门来诣佛所，与世尊面相问讯慰劳已，退坐一面。而说偈言：

Not a true brāhman, their conduct is pure
a kṣatriya practicing austerities, also deviates from tranquility
The brāhman of the three Vedas, that is
truly pure, such purity is not in other beings.

「无非婆罗门，　　所行为清净，刹利修苦行，　　于静亦复乖。三典婆罗门，　　是则为清净，如是清净者，　　不在余众生。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse in reply,

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

Not knowing the path of purity, nor the supreme purity
seeking tranquility elsewhere, there is never a time of purity.

「不知清净道，　　及诸无上净，于余求静者，　　至竟无净时。」

The brāhman said to the Buddha, "Gautama, you speak of the path of purity and the supreme purity. What is the path of purity? What is the supreme purity?"

婆罗门白佛：「瞿昙说清净道及无上清净耶？何等为清净道？何等为无上清净？」

The Buddha told the brāhman, "Right view is the path of purity. Cultivating and practicing right view extensively, one can eliminate greed, eliminate anger, and eliminate delusion. If a brāhman permanently eliminates greed, anger, and delusion, and all afflictions, this is called supreme purity. Right intention, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right concentration are called the path of purity. Cultivating and practicing right concentration extensively, one can eliminate greed, eliminate anger, and eliminate delusion. If a brāhman permanently eliminates greed, anger, and delusion, and all afflictions, this is called supreme purity."

佛告婆罗门：「正见者为清净道，正见修习多修习，断贪欲、断瞋恚、断愚痴。若婆罗门贪欲永断，瞋恚、愚痴永断，一切烦恼永断，是名无上清净。正志、正语、正业、正命、正方便、正念、正定，是名清净道；正定修习多修习已，断贪欲、断瞋恚、断愚痴。若婆罗门贪欲永断，瞋恚、愚痴永断，一切烦恼永断，是名无上清净。」

The brāhman said to the Buddha, "Gautama, you speak of the path of purity and the supreme purity. Gautama, I have many affairs to attend to in the world. I must now take leave and return."

婆罗门白佛言：「瞿昙说清净道、无上清净耶？瞿昙！世务多事，今且辞还。」

The Buddha told the brāhman, "It is time for you to know."

佛告婆罗门：「宜知是时。」

The brāhman with the flower parasol and the shellac, and the brāhman followers, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and followed along. They rose from their seats and departed.

持华盖著舍勒导从婆罗门闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，从座起去。
