SĀ 1164

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1164（一一六四） 波罗延

Parallel to: AN 6.61

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying in the Deer Park at the Sage's Abode in the country of Varanasi.

一时，佛住波罗［木＊奈］国仙人住处鹿野苑中。

At that time, many bhikṣus gathered in the Dharma hall and discussed, "Venerable ones! As the World-Honored One said in response to the questions of Parāyana Tissa,

时，有众多比丘集于讲堂，作如是论：「诸尊！如世尊说波罗延低舍弥德勒所问：

If one knows the two extremes, one will never be attached to them
Such a person is called a true hero, not caring for the five
desires, free from the fetters of afflictions, transcending the worries of worldly entanglements.

「『若知二边者，　　于中永无著，说名大丈夫，　　不顾于五欲，无有烦恼鏁，　　超出缝紩忧。』

"Venerable ones! What is the meaning of this? What are the extremes? What are the two extremes? What is the middle? What is the entangling net? What is thought? To know with wisdom, to understand clearly; what is known by wisdom, what is understood by understanding, to make the extreme of suffering, to be liberated from suffering."

「诸尊！此有何义？云何边？云何二边？云何为中？云何为缝紩？云何思？以智知，以了了；智所知，了所了，作苦边，脱于苦。」

One answered, "The six internal sense bases are one extreme, the six external sense bases are the two extremes, feeling is in the middle, craving is the entangling net. One who practices feeling obtains various causes, the body gradually increases and is born. This is the Dharma, known by wisdom, understood clearly, known by wisdom, understood, making the extreme of suffering, liberated from suffering."

有一答言：「六内入处是一边，六外入处是二边，受是其中，爱为缝紩。习于受者，得彼彼因，身渐转增长出生，于此即法，以智知，以了了，智所知，了所了，作苦边，脱于苦。」

Another said, "The past world is one extreme, the future world is the two extremes, the present world is called the middle, craving is the entangling net. Practicing this craving, the causes of various things, the body gradually touches and increases, and is born, up to liberation from suffering."

复有说言：「过去世是一边，未来世是二边，现在世名为中，爱为缝紩。习近此爱，彼彼所因，身渐触增长出生，乃至脱苦。」

Another said, "Pleasurable feeling is one extreme, painful feeling is the two extremes, neither painful nor pleasurable is in the middle, craving is the entangling net. Practicing this craving, the things obtained by various means, the body gradually touches and increases, and is born, up to making suffering."

复有说言：「乐受者是一边，苦受者是二边，不苦不乐是其中，爱为缝紩，习近此爱，彼彼所得，自身渐触增长出生，乃至作苦。」

Another said, "Existence is one extreme, accumulation is the two extremes, experience is in the middle, craving is the entangling net..." and so on, up to "liberation from suffering."

复有说言：「有者是一边，集是二边，受是其中，爱为缝紩……」如是广说，乃至「脱苦。」

Another said, "The body is one extreme, the accumulation of the body is the two extremes, craving is the entangling net..." and so on, up to "liberation from suffering."

复有说言：「身者是一边，身集是二边，爱为缝紩……」如是广说，乃至「脱苦。」

Another said, "All of us have different explanations. The various explanations mentioned earlier cannot be understood. How can the World-Honored One have other explanations in the Parayan Dīpaṃkara Sūtra? We should go and ask the World-Honored One in detail. We will uphold the explanations given by the World-Honored One."

复有说言：「我等一切所说不同，所谓向来种种异说，要不望知。云何世尊有余之说，波罗延低舍弥德勒所问经？我等应往具问世尊，如世尊所说，我等奉持。」

At that time, many bhikṣus went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed their heads at his feet, withdrew and sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! A while ago, the bhikṣus gathered in the lecture hall and said, 'In the Parayan Dīpaṃkara Sūtra spoken by the World-Honored One, it is said that there are two extremes, up to liberation from suffering.' Some say, 'The six internal sense bases are said to be one extreme, the six external sense bases are said to be the two extremes, and feeling is in between, with craving as the entangling net...' and so on, as explained in detail above. They are all not definitive. We have come here today to ask the World-Honored One in detail about this meaning. Among our explanations, who has grasped the meaning?"

尔时，众多比丘诣世尊所，稽首礼足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！向诸比丘集于讲堂，作如是言：『于世尊所说波罗延低舍弥德勒所问经，所谓二边，乃至脱苦。』有人说言：『内六入处是说一边，外六入处是说二边，受是其中，爱为缝紩……』如前广说，悉不决定，今日故来请问世尊，具问斯义，我等所说，谁得其义？」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "What you have said is all well-said. I will now explain the sūtra with the remainder for you. I explained the sūtra with the remainder for Parayan Dīpaṃkara, saying that contact is one extreme, the accumulation of contact is the two extremes, feeling is in between, with craving as the entangling net. Having become attached to craving, one obtains various things. The body is conditioned by contact, which increases and produces. With wisdom, one knows and understands this Dharma. What is known by wisdom and understood by understanding is the extreme of suffering, and one is liberated from suffering."

佛告诸比丘：「汝等所说，皆是善说，我今当为汝等说有余经。我为波罗延低舍弥德勒有余经说，谓触是一边，触集是二边，受是其中，爱为缝紩。习近爱已，彼彼所得，身缘触增长出生，于此法，以智知，以了了；智所知，了所了，作苦边，脱于苦。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus were delighted and practiced accordingly.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
