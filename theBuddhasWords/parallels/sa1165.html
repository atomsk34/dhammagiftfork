SĀ 1165

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1165（一一六五） 宾头卢

Parallel to: SN 35.127

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Venerable Pindola Bharadvaja was staying in the Kalandakanivāpa near Rājagaha.

一时，尊者宾头卢住拘睒弥国瞿师罗园。

At that time, King Udayana of the country of Vatsa went to where the Venerable Pindola Bharadvaja was, exchanged greetings, and sat to one side. King Udayana of Vatsa said to the Venerable Pindola Bharadvaja, "I have a question to ask. Is there time to answer it?"

时，有婆蹉国王，名优陀延那，诣尊者宾头卢所，共相问讯；问讯已，退坐一面。婆蹉王优陀延那白尊者宾头卢言：「欲有所问，宁有闲睱见答已不？」

The Venerable Pindola Bharadvaja replied, "Great King! Great King, ask your question. I will answer what I know."

尊者宾头卢答言：「大王！大王且问，知者当答。」

King Udayana of Vatsa asked the Venerable Pindola Bharadvaja, "For what reasons do newly ordained young monks, who have not been ordained for long in this Dharma and Vinaya, live in great ease, with joyful faculties, clear complexions, and bright skin, delighting in tranquility and moving little, living off others, with minds like wild beasts, able to practice pure and genuine celibacy for the rest of their lives?"

婆蹉王优陀延那问尊者宾头卢：「何因何缘，新学年少比丘于此法、律，出家未久，极安乐住，诸根欣悦，颜貌清净，肤色鲜白，乐静少动，任他而活，野兽其心，堪能尽寿，修持梵行，纯一清净？」

The Venerable Pindola Bharadvaja replied, "As the Buddha said, the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One knows and sees, and teaches the monks: 'You monks! If you see an elder, think of her as your mother; if you see someone in the middle, think of her as your sister; if you see a young one, think of her as your daughter. For these reasons, young monks, who have not been ordained for long in this Dharma and Vinaya, live in great ease, with joyful faculties, clear complexions, and bright skin, delighting in tranquility and moving little, living off others, with minds like wild beasts, able to practice pure and genuine celibacy for the rest of their lives.'"

尊者宾头卢答言：「如佛所说，如来、应、等正觉所知所见，为比丘说：『汝诸比丘！若见宿人，当作母想；见中间者，作姊妹想；见幼稚者，当作女想。以是因缘，年少比丘于此法、律，出家未久，安隐乐住，诸根敷悦，颜貌清净，肤色鲜白，乐静少动，任他而活，野兽其心，堪能尽寿，修持梵行，纯一清净。』」

King Udayana of Vatsa said to the Venerable Pindola Bharadvaja, "Now in the world, if one sees an elder, one thinks of her as one's mother; if one sees someone in the middle, one thinks of her as one's sister; if one sees a young one, one thinks of her as one's daughter. At that time, the mind also arises accordingly, burning with desire, burning with anger, burning with delusion. Will there be any other superior causes and conditions?"

婆蹉王优陀延那语尊者宾头卢言：「今诸世间贪求之心，若见宿人，而作母想；见中年者，作姊妹想；见幼稚者，而作女想。当于尔时，心亦随起，贪欲烧燃、瞋恚烧燃、愚痴烧燃，要当更有胜因缘不？」

The Venerable Pindola Bharadvaja said to King Udayana of Vatsa, "There are other causes and conditions. As the World-Honored One said, the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One knows and sees, and teaches the monks: 'This body, from the feet to the top of the head, is a skeleton smeared with flesh, covered with thin skin, filled with various impurities; observing all around, hair, nails, teeth, dust, dirt, saliva, skin, flesh, white bones, tendons, veins, heart, liver, lungs, spleen, kidneys, intestines, stomach, raw flesh, cooked flesh, bladder, tears, sweat, snot, foam, fat, marrow, phlegm, pus, blood, brain, fluid, feces, and urine.' Your Majesty! For this reason, young monks, who have not been ordained for long in this Dharma and Vinaya, live in great ease, up to pure and genuine.

尊者宾头卢语婆蹉王优陀延那：「更有因缘，如世尊说，如来、应、等正觉所知所见，为比丘说：『此身从足至顶，骨干肉涂，覆以薄皮，种种不净充满其中；周遍观察，发、毛、爪、齿、尘垢、流唌、皮、肉、白骨、筋、脉、心、肝、肺、脾、肾、肠、肚、生藏、熟藏、胞、泪、汗、涕、沫、肪、脂、髓、痰、癊、脓、血、脑、汁、屎、溺。』大王！此因此缘故，年少比丘于此法、律，出家未久，安隐乐住，乃至纯一满净。」

King Udayana of Vatsa said to the Venerable Pindola Bharadvaja, "The human mind is fickle. If one contemplates impurity, the thought of purity arises. Are there any other causes and conditions that can make young monks, who have not been ordained for long in this Dharma and Vinaya, live in great ease, up to pure and genuine?"

婆蹉王优陀延那语尊者宾头卢：「人心飘疾，若观不净，随净想现。颇更有因缘，令年少比丘于此法、律，出家未久，安隐乐住，乃至纯一满净不？」

The Venerable Pindola Bharadvaja said, "Your Majesty! There are causes and conditions. As the World-Honored One said, the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One knows and sees, and tells the monks: 'You should guard the sense-doors and control your minds well. When the eyes see forms, do not grasp their characteristics or their attractive features, and do not cling to them. If one does not restrain the eye-faculty, then worldly greed, desire, and evil and unwholesome dharmas will leak into one's mind. Therefore, one should uphold the eye-discipline. The same applies to the ear and sounds, the nose and smells, the tongue and tastes, the body and tactile objects, and the mind and dharmas. One should uphold the mind-discipline.'"

尊者宾头卢言：「大王！有因有缘，如世尊说，如来、应、等正觉所知所见，告诸比丘：『汝等应当守护根门，善摄其心。若眼见色时，莫取色相，莫取随形好，增上执持。若于眼根不摄敛住，则世间贪、爱、恶不善法则漏其心，是故此等当受持眼律仪。耳声、鼻香、舌味、身触、意法亦复如是。乃至受持意律仪。』」

At that time, King Udayana of Vatsa said to the Venerable Pindola Bharadvaja, "Excellent! Well-spoken Dharma, up to upholding the discipline of the faculties. Venerable Pindola Bharadvaja! I am also like this. Sometimes I do not guard my body, do not uphold the discipline of the faculties, and do not unify my thoughts. When I enter the palace, my mind is extremely filled with burning greed and delusion. Even if I am alone in a quiet room, the three poisons still burn my mind, let alone the palace. Sometimes I well protect my body, well control the faculties, and unify my thoughts. When I enter the palace, greed, anger, and delusion do not arise to burn my mind. Even in the inner palace, it does not burn my body, let alone my mind. Therefore, for this reason and condition, it can make young monks, who have not been ordained for long in this Dharma and Vinaya, live in great ease, up to pure and genuine."

尔时，婆蹉王优陀延那语尊者宾头卢：「善哉！善说法，乃至受持诸根律仪。尊者宾头卢！我亦如是，有时不守护身，不持诸根律仪，不一其念，入于宫中，其心极生贪欲炽燃、愚痴烧燃；正使闲房独处，亦复三毒烧燃其心，况复宫中！又我有时善护其身，善摄诸根，专一其念，入于宫中，贪欲、恚、痴不起烧燃其心，于内宫中尚不烧身，亦不烧心，况复闲独！以是之故，此因此缘，能令年少比丘于此法、律，出家未久，安隐乐住，乃至纯一满净。」

At that time, King Udayana of Vatsa, having heard the Venerable Pindola Bharadvaja's words, joyfully assented and rose from his seat to depart.

时，婆蹉王优陀延那闻尊者宾头卢所说，欢喜随喜，从坐起去。
