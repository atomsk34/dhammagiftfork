SĀ 1176

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1176（一一七六） 漏法

Parallel to: SN 35.243

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the World-Honored One was traveling in the human realm of the Śākya clan, and arrived at the city of Kapilavastu, staying in the Nīguṇṭha Garden.

一时，世尊释氏人间游行，至迦毘罗卫国，住尼拘娄陀园。

At that time, the Śākyas of Kapilavastu had built a new lecture hall, but no śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, young Śākyas, or people had stayed in it yet. Hearing that the World-Honored One had come to the human realm of the Śākyas and was staying in the Nīguṇṭha Garden, discussing the meaning of suffering and happiness, they thought, "This hall is newly built and no one has stayed in it yet. We should invite the World-Honored One and his great assembly to make offerings in it. We will obtain the merit and blessings of long-term peace and tranquility, and we will also be able to use it ourselves." Having discussed this, they all went out of the city to where the World-Honored One was, bowed their heads at his feet, and sat to one side.

尔时，迦毘罗卫释氏作新讲堂，未有诸沙门、婆罗门、释迦年少及诸人民在中住者，闻世尊来至释氏迦毘罗卫人间游行，住尼拘娄陀园，论苦乐义。此堂新成，未有住者，可请世尊与诸大众于中供养，得功德福报，长夜安隐，然复我等当随受用。作是议已，悉共出城，诣世尊所，稽首礼足，退坐一面。

At that time, the World-Honored One expounded the essential Dharma for the Śākyas, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting them, then remained silent.

尔时，世尊为诸释氏演说要法，示、教、照、喜已，默然而住。

At that time, the Śākyas rose from their seats, adjusted their robes, bowed to the Buddha, placed their right knees on the ground, joined their palms, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! We Śākyas have newly built a lecture hall, but no one has stayed in it yet. We now invite the World-Honored One and the great assembly to make offerings in it. We will obtain the merit and blessings of long-term peace and tranquility, and then we will be able to use it ourselves." At that time, the World-Honored One silently accepted the invitation.

时，诸释氏从座起，整衣服，为佛作礼，右膝著地，合掌白佛言：「世尊！我等释氏新作讲堂，未有住者，今请世尊及诸大众于中供养，得功德福利，长夜安隐，然后我等当随受用。」尔时，世尊默然受请。

At that time, the Śākyas, knowing that the World-Honored One had accepted the invitation, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, returned to their places, and on that very day, arranged for the transportation of the various necessities by carts and palanquins. They adorned the new hall, set up seats, spread soft grass on the ground, and prepared fragrant oil lamps. When all the arrangements were made, they went to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads, and said, "All the arrangements are complete. The Sage knows the time.

时，诸释氏知世尊受请已，稽首佛足，各还其所，即以其日，以车舆经纪，运其众具，庄严新堂，敷置床座，软草布地，备香油灯。众事办已，往诣佛所，稽首白言：「众事办已，惟圣知时。」

At that time, the World-Honored One, surrounded by the great assembly in front and behind, went to the outside of the new hall, washed his feet, and then ascended the hall, sitting under the central pillar facing east. At that time, the bhikṣus also washed their feet, followed into the lecture hall, sat behind the World-Honored One facing east, and sat in order. At that time, the Śākyas sat facing west.

尔时，世尊与诸大众前后围绕，至新堂外，洗足已，然后上堂，于中柱下，东向而坐。时，诸比丘亦洗足已，随入讲堂，于世尊后，西面东向，次第而坐。时，诸释氏即于东面西向而坐。

At that time, the World-Honored One extensively spoke the essential Dharma for the Śākyas, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting them. Having finished speaking, he said to the Śākyas, "Gautama! The first watch of the night has already passed. It is time to return to Kapilavastu." At that time, the Śākyas, having heard what the Buddha said, joyfully followed and rejoiced, paid homage, and departed.

尔时，世尊为诸释氏广说要法，示、教、照、喜已，语诸释氏：「瞿昙！初夜已过，于时可还迦毘罗越。」时，诸释氏闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。

At that time, the World-Honored One, knowing that the Śākyas had left, told Mahāmaudgalyāyana, "You should teach the Dharma for the bhikṣus. I now have a backache and will rest myself." At that time, Mahāmaudgalyāyana silently accepted the instruction.

尔时，世尊知释氏去已，告大目揵连：「汝当为诸比丘说法，我今背疾，当自消息。」时，大目揵连默然受教。

At that time, the World-Honored One placed the four layers of the upper robe under his side, folded the saṃghāṭī robe, and placed it under his head. He lay on his right side, with his knees drawn up and his feet stacked, focused on the dawn, and contemplated the thought of rising.

尔时，世尊四亵欝多罗僧安置脇下，卷襞僧伽梨，置于头下，右脇而卧，屈膝累足，系念明相，作起想思惟。

At that time, Mahāmaudgalyāyana said to the bhikṣus, "The Dharma spoken by the Buddha is good in the beginning, middle, and end, with good meaning and good taste, pure and complete, and the pure practice of the Brahmins. I will now explain the dharmas of taints and non-taints. Listen attentively! What are the dharmas of taints? Ignorant and unlearned ordinary people, having seen forms with the eye, give rise to attachment to desirable forms and aversion to undesirable forms. They do not abide in the contemplation of the body, and have no wisdom in the liberation of the mind and wisdom. They give rise to various evil and unwholesome dharmas, which are not completely extinguished and not completely exhausted. The same is true for the ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind. Bhikṣus! In this way, Māra, the evil one, approaches them, seeking their weaknesses. He finds their weaknesses in the eye and forms, and in the ear and sounds, nose and smells, tongue and tastes, body and tactile objects, and mind and dharmas.

尔时，大目揵连语诸比丘：「佛所说法，初、中、后善，善义善味，纯一满净，清白梵行。我今当说漏、不漏法，汝等谛听！云何为漏法？愚痴无闻凡夫眼见色已，于可念色而起乐著，不可念色而起憎恶，不住身念处，于心解脱、慧解脱无少分智，而起种种恶不善法，不无余灭，不无余永尽；耳、鼻、舌、身、意亦复如是。比丘！如是者，天魔波旬往诣其所，伺其虚短，于其眼色，即得其阙；耳声、鼻香、舌味、身触、意法亦复如是，即得其阙。

It is like a pile of dry grass, which is immediately burned when fires arise from the four directions. In this way, bhikṣus! Māra, the evil one, finds their weaknesses in the eye and forms. In this way, bhikṣus! They cannot overcome forms, and in the ear and sounds, nose and smells, tongue and tastes, body and tactile objects, and mind and dharmas, they are controlled by the dharmas and cannot overcome the dharmas. They cannot overcome forms, sounds, smells, tastes, tactile objects, and dharmas. They also cannot overcome unwholesome mental dharmas, afflictions, and the burning of suffering, as well as future birth, old age, sickness, and death. Venerable ones! I personally received this from the World-Honored One. This is called the sūtra on the dharmas of taints.

「譬如枯干草积，四方火起，寻时即烧。如是，比丘！于其眼色，天魔波旬即得其阙。如是，比丘！不胜于色，于耳声、鼻香、舌味、身触、意法，受制于法，不能胜法，不胜色，不胜声、香、味、触、法，亦复不胜意不善法、诸烦恼炽然苦报，及未来世生、老、病、死。诸尊！我从世尊亲受于此诸有漏法，是名有漏法经。

What is the sūtra on the dharmas of non-taints? The learned noble disciple, having seen forms with the eye, does not give rise to attachment to desirable forms and aversion to undesirable forms. They abide in mindfulness, and with the liberation of the immeasurable mind and wisdom, they truly know and completely extinguish the arisen evil and unwholesome dharmas. The same is true for the ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind.

「云何无漏法经？多闻圣弟子眼见色，于可念色不起乐著，不可念色不起憎恶，系念而住，无量心解脱、慧解脱如实知，于彼已起恶不善法无余灭尽；耳、鼻、舌、身、意亦复如是。

Such bhikṣus, when Māra, the evil one, approaches them, seeks their weaknesses in the eye and forms, but cannot find them. In the ear and sounds, nose and smells, tongue and tastes, body and tactile objects, and mind and dharmas, he seeks their weaknesses but cannot find them. It is like a tower with solid walls, tightly closed windows, and thick plaster. Fires from the four directions cannot burn it. These bhikṣus are also like this. When Māra, the evil one, approaches them, he seeks their weaknesses but cannot find them. Such bhikṣus can overcome those forms and are not overcome by those forms. They overcome sounds, smells, tastes, tactile objects, and dharmas, and are not overcome by those dharmas. If they can overcome forms, sounds, smells, tastes, tactile objects, and dharmas, they can also overcome evil and unwholesome dharmas, the burning of afflictions and suffering, and future birth, old age, sickness, and death. I personally received this from the World-Honored One. This is called the sūtra on the dharmas of non-taints.

「如是像类比丘，弊魔波旬往诣其所，于其眼色伺求其短，不得其短；于耳声、鼻香、舌味、身触、意法伺求其短，不得其短。譬如楼阁，墙壁牢固，窓户重闭，埿涂厚密，四方火起，不能烧然。斯等比丘亦复如是，弊魔波旬往诣其所，伺求其短，不得其短。如是比丘能胜彼色，不为彼色之所胜也；胜于声、香、味、触、法，不为彼法之所胜也。若胜于色，胜于声、香、味、触、法已，亦复胜于恶不善法、烦恼炽燃苦报，及未来世生、老、病、死。我亲从世尊面受此法，是名无漏法经。」

At that time, the World-Honored One, knowing that Mahāmaudgalyāyana had finished speaking the Dharma, rose from his seat, sat with proper posture, and focused his mind in front. He said to Mahāmaudgalyāyana, "Excellent! Excellent! Maudgalyāyana! Speaking this sūtra for others brings great benefit and liberation, and brings long-lasting peace and happiness to gods and humans.

尔时，世尊知大目揵连说法竟，起正身坐，系念在前，告大目揵连：「善哉！善哉！目揵连！为人说此经法，多所饶益，多所过度，长夜安乐诸天世人。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "You should uphold the sūtra on the dharmas of taints and non-taints, and widely explain it to others. Why is that? Because it is complete in meaning, complete in the Dharma, complete in the holy life, it reveals supernatural powers, and correctly leads to nirvāṇa; up to the faithful good sons, whether at home or gone forth, should uphold, read, recite, and widely explain it to others.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「汝当受持漏、无漏法经，广为人说。所以者何？义具足故，法具足故，梵行具足故，开发神通，正向涅槃；乃至信心善男子，在家、出家，当受持读诵，广为人说。」

After the Buddha spoke this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully practiced it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
