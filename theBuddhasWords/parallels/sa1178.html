SĀ 1178

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1178（一一七八） 婆私咤

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying in the mango grove in the country of Mithila.

一时，佛住弥𫄨罗国庵罗园中。

At that time there was a Brahmin woman named Vasati who had six sons who died one after another. Grieving for her sons, she went mad, ran naked with disheveled hair along the road, and came to the mango grove in Mithila.

时，有婆四咤婆罗门尼，有六子相续命终，念子发狂，裸形被发，随路而走，至弥𫄨罗庵罗园中。

At that time the World-Honored One was teaching the Dharma surrounded by an immeasurable great assembly. The Brahmin woman Vasati saw the World-Honored One from afar. Upon seeing him, she regained her original mind, felt ashamed and embarrassed, and crouched down with her body covered.

尔时，世尊无量大众围绕说法，婆四咤婆罗门尼遥见世尊，见已，即得本心，惭愧羞耻，歛身蹲坐。

At that time the World-Honored One told Venerable Ānanda, "Take your uttarasanga and give it to the Brahmin woman Vasati to wear while listening to the Dharma." Venerable Ānanda then accepted the Buddha's instruction and took the robe to give her.

尔时，世尊告尊者阿难：「取汝郁多罗僧与彼婆四咤婆罗门尼，令著听法。」尊者阿难即受佛教，取衣令著。

The Brahmin woman, having obtained the robe, put it on and went before the Buddha. She bowed her head at the Buddha's feet and then sat to one side.

时，婆罗门尼得衣著已，至于佛前，稽首礼佛，退坐一面。

At that time, the World-Honored One taught the Dharma for her, guiding, illuminating, and delighting her. As is the constant practice of the Buddha, he taught the Dharma in stages, up to the point where she had pure faith and accepted the Three Refuges. Having heard the Buddha's teachings, she rejoiced and paid homage before departing.

尔时，世尊为其说法，示、教、照、喜已，如佛常法，说法次第，乃至信心清净，受三自归，闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。

Later, the upāsikā's seventh child suddenly died. The upāsikā did not cry or grieve. At that time, the upāsikā's husband spoke a verse to the upāsikā, saying:

彼婆四咤优婆夷于后时，第七子忽复命终，彼优婆夷都不啼哭忧悲恼苦。时，婆四咤优婆夷夫说偈而告婆四咤优婆夷言：

When your previous children died, you grieved for them
not eating or drinking day and night, even going
mad. Now, having lost seven brothers, why are you not grieving?

「先诸子命终，　　念子生忧苦，昼夜不饮食，　　乃至发狂乱。今丧弟七子，　　而不生忧苦？」

The upāsikā then spoke a verse in reply to her husband, saying:

婆四咤优婆夷即复说偈答其夫言：

The grandchildren are numerous, born from the combination of causes and conditions
They have passed away for a long time, and so have you
and I. The descendants and clans are countless, each born in different
places, devouring each other. If one knows the essentials of birth, why
should one grieve? I have already understood the way out of birth
and death, the characteristics of existence and non-existence. I no longer give
rise to grief and suffering, because I have entered the true teaching
of the Buddha.

「儿孙有千数，　　因缘和合生，长夜迁过去，　　我与君亦然。子孙及宗族，　　其数无限量，彼彼所生处，　　更互相残食。若知生要者，　　何足生忧苦？我已知出离，　　生死存亡相，不复生忧苦，　　入佛正教故。」

At that time, the upāsaka husband spoke a verse in praise, saying:

时，婆四咤优婆夷夫说偈叹曰：

I have never heard such a teaching before, but now I hear you speak it
Where did you hear the teaching that does not think of one's children and grieve?

「未曾所闻法，　　而今闻汝说，何处闻说法，　　不念子忧悲？」

The upāsikā Vasiṣṭhā spoke a verse in reply, saying:

婆四咤优婆夷说偈答言：

Today the Perfectly Enlightened One is in the country of Mithilā
in the mango grove, forever free from all suffering, expounding all
suffering, the origin of suffering, the cessation of suffering, and the
noble eightfold path, leading to the peace of nirvāṇa! That is
my great teacher, deeply delighting in his true teaching. I have
already understood the true Dharma, able to open the suffering of my children.

「今日等正觉，　　在弥𫄨罗国，庵罗树园中，　　永离一切苦，演说一切苦、　　苦习、苦寂灭、贤圣八正道，　　安隐趣涅槃！则是我大师，　　深乐其正教。我已知正法，　　能开子忧苦。」

The husband, the brāhmaṇa, again spoke a verse, saying:

其夫婆罗门复说偈言：

I too will now go to the mango grove of Mithilā
where the World-Honored One will also open the suffering of my children.

「我今亦当往，　　弥𫄨庵罗园，彼世尊亦当，　　开我子忧苦。」

The upāsikā again spoke a verse, saying:

优婆夷复说偈言：

Look at the Perfectly Enlightened One, with a soft golden body; he can
tame the untamed and widely liberate those drowning in the sea of existence.

「当观等正觉，　　柔软金色身；不调者能调，　　广度海流人。」

At that time, the brāhmaṇa immediately prepared a horse-drawn carriage and went to the mango grove of Mithila. Seeing the World-Honored One from afar, his faith and joy increased, and he went before the Great Teacher.

尔时，婆罗门即严驾乘于马车，诣弥𫄨罗庵罗园。遥见世尊，转增信乐，诣大师前。

At that time, the Great Teacher spoke a verse for him, opening his Dharma eye to the truths of suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path, leading directly to nirvāṇa. He then saw the Dharma and attained the state of no more learning. Having understood the Dharma, he requested to go forth.

彼时，大师即为说偈，开其法眼，苦、习、灭、道，正向涅槃。彼即见法，成无间等。既知法已，请求出家。

At that time, the brāhmaṇa was able to go forth. He contemplated in solitude and attained arahantship. The World-Honored One predicted, "On the third night, he will attain the three insights." Having attained the three insights, the Buddha then told him, "Send the charioteer back home in the carriage to inform the upāsikā Po-shih-cha to rejoice, saying, 'The brāhmaṇa went to see the World-Honored One, gained pure faith, served the Great Teacher, and was taught the Dharma, opening the Dharma eye to see the noble truth of suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the noble eightfold path, peacefully heading to nirvāṇa, attaining the state of no more learning. Having understood the Dharma, he requested to go forth. The World-Honored One predicted, "On the third night, he will be fully endowed with the three insights."'

时，婆罗门即得出家，独静思惟，乃至得阿罗汉。世尊记说：「于第三夜，逮得三明。」得三明已，佛即告之：「命遣御者乘车还家，告婆四咤优婆夷，令发随喜，语言：『婆罗门往见世尊，得净信心，奉事大师，即为说法，为开法眼，见苦圣谛、苦集、苦灭、贤圣八道，安趣涅槃，成无间等。既知法已，即求出家。世尊记说：「于第三夜，具足三明。」』」

At that time, the charioteer obeyed the order and quickly returned. The upāsikā Po-shih-cha saw from afar that the charioteer's carriage was empty as he returned. She immediately asked from afar, "Did the brāhmaṇa see the Buddha? Did the Buddha teach him the Dharma, opening the Dharma eye for him to see the noble truths?"

时，彼御者奉教疾还。时，婆四咤优婆夷遥见御者空车而还。即遥问言：「婆罗门为见佛不？佛为说法，开示法眼，见圣谛不？」

The charioteer said, "The brāhmaṇa has already seen the World-Honored One, gained pure faith, served the Great Teacher, had the Dharma eye opened for him, heard about the four noble truths, and attained the state of no more learning. Having understood the Dharma, he requested to go forth and is diligently contemplating. The World-Honored One predicted, 'On the third night, he will be fully endowed with the three insights.'"

御者白言：「婆罗门已见世尊，得净信心，奉事大师，为开法眼，说四圣谛，成无间等。既知法已，即求出家，专精思惟。世尊记说：『于第三夜，具足三明。』」

At that time, the upāsikā was delighted in her heart and said to the charioteer, "The carriage and horses are yours. I will also give you a thousand gold coins. Tell him on my behalf, 'The brāhmaṇa Sukātideva has attained the three insights.' This will bring me joy."

时，优婆夷心即随喜，语御者言：「车马属汝，加复赐汝金钱一千，已汝传信言：『婆罗门宿阇谛，已得三明。』令我欢喜故。」

The charioteer said, "What use do I have for the carriage, horses, and gold coins? I will return the carriage, horses, and gold coins to the upāsikā. I will now return to where the brāhmaṇa is and go forth with him."

御者白言：「我今何用车马金钱为？车马金钱还优婆夷，我今当还婆罗门所，随彼出家。」

The upāsikā said, "If that is your intention, then you may return quickly. Before long, you will also attain the three insights like him and go forth after him."

优婆夷言：「汝意如此，便可速还。不久亦当如彼所得，具足三明，随后出家。」

The charioteer said, "Yes, upāsikā! I will certainly go forth like him."

御者白言：「如是，优婆夷！如彼出家，我亦当然。」

The upāsikā said, "When your father goes forth, you will follow him. Before long, I will also follow him. Just as the great dragon in the empty wilderness soars through the sky, and all the other dragons, dragon sons, and dragon daughters follow him, so it is with me. I will hold my robes and bowl, easy to support and easy to fill." The charioteer said, "Upāsikā! If it is so, your wish will certainly be fulfilled. Before long, you will see the upāsikā with few desires and contentment, holding her robes and bowl, accepting what is discarded by others to eat, shaving her hair and dyeing her robes, cutting off desire for the aggregates, sense bases, and sense fields, free from attachment and bondage, and exhausting all defilements."

优婆夷言：「汝父出家，汝随出家，我今不久亦当随去。如空野大龙，乘虚而游，其余诸龙、龙子、龙女悉皆随去，我亦如是，执持衣钵，易养易满。」御者白言：「优婆夷！若如是者，所愿必果，不久当见优婆夷少欲知足，执持衣钵，人所弃者，乞受而食，剃发染衣，于阴、界、入断除爱欲，离贪系缚，尽诸有漏。」

The brāhman, his charioteer, the upāsikā Vasiṣṭhā, and the upāsikā girl Sundarī all went forth and reached the end of suffering.

彼婆罗门及其御者、婆四咤优婆夷、优婆夷女孙陀槃梨，悉皆出家，究竟苦边。
