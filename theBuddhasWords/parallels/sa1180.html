SĀ 1180

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1180（一一八〇） 构巢处

Parallel to: SN 7.22 / SĀ² 2.94

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying in the Śāla Grove, a Brahmin village. At that time, the World-Honored One, in the morning, put on his robe, took his bowl, and entered the Brahmin village to beg for alms. There was an untimely cloud.

一时，佛住娑罗树林婆罗门聚落。尔时，世尊晨朝著衣持钵，入婆罗门聚落乞食，有非时云起。

At that time, the World-Honored One had this thought: "I should now go to the great assembly hall of the Brahmin village and the great householder." Having this thought, he immediately went to the great assembly hall.

尔时，世尊作是念：「我今当往婆罗门聚落婆罗长者大会堂中。」作是念已，即往向彼大会堂所。

At that time, the Brahmins and householders were all gathered in the hall. Seeing the World-Honored One from afar, they said to each other: "That shaven-headed monk, does he know any Dharma?"

时，婆罗门长者悉集堂上，遥见世尊，共相谓言：「彼剃头沙门竟知何法？」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the Brahmins and householders of the Brahmin village: "Among the Brahmins, there are those who know the Dharma and those who do not. Among the Kshatriya householders, there are also those who know the Dharma and those who do not know the Dharma."

尔时，世尊告彼婆罗聚落婆罗门长者言：「诸婆罗门有知法者，有不知者；刹利长者亦有知法者，有不知法者。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

Not wanting to surpass one's friends, a king does not subdue the unyielding
a wife does not seek to surpass her husband, a son does not
respect his father. There are no gatherings without wise ones, without wisdom, one
does not speak the Dharma. Cutting off greed, anger, and delusion is called
a wise one.

「非朋欲胜朋，　　王不伏难伏，妻不求胜夫，　　无子不恭父。无会无智者，　　无智不法言，贪恚痴悉断，　　是则名智者。」

At that time, the brāhmaṇa householder said to the Buddha, "Good man Gautama! Good man, you may enter this hall and take a seat." After the World-Honored One was seated, he said, "Gautama, please teach the Dharma. We wish to listen."

时，彼婆罗门长者白佛言：「善士瞿昙！善士夫可入此堂，就座而坐。」世尊坐已。即白言：「瞿昙说法，我等乐听。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke various teachings for the great assembly of brāhmaṇa householders, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting them. He then spoke a verse:

尔时，世尊为彼大会婆罗门长者种种说法，示、教、照、喜已，复说偈言：

The assembly of fools and wise, without speaking, how can they know the truth?
The one who can speak the path of tranquility, through speaking, wisdom is then
expressed. The speaker reveals the true Dharma, establishing the banner of the Great Sage
Speaking well is the banner of the sages, the Dharma is the banner of the Arhats.

「愚智群聚会，　　非说孰知明？能说寂静道，　　因说智则辩。说者显正法，　　建立大仙幢，善说为仙幢，　　法为罗汉幢。」

At that time, the World-Honored One established the true Dharma for the brāhmaṇa householders of the brāhmaṇa village, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting them. Having shown, taught, illuminated, and delighted them, he rose from his seat and departed.

尔时，世尊为婆罗聚落婆罗门长者建立正法，示、教、照、喜；示、教、照、喜已，从座起而去。
