SĀ 1181

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1181（一一八一） 天敬

Parallel to: SN 7.13 / SĀ² 2.95

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling among the people of Kosala, and arrived at the village of Fuli. He stayed at the mango grove of the brahman Tendopa, with Venerable Upamâ as his attendant.

一时，佛在拘萨罗人间游行，至浮梨聚落，住天作婆罗门庵罗园中，尊者优波摩为侍者。

At that time, the World-Honored One was suffering from a backache. He told Venerable Upamâ, "Take my robes and alms bowl, and go to the house of the brahman Tendopa."

尔时，世尊患背痛，告尊者优波] cf.【丽】摩：「汝举衣钵已，往至天作婆罗门舍。」

At that time, the brahman Tendopa was in the central hall, having his hair and beard shaved by a barber. Seeing Venerable Upamâ standing at the outer gate, he spoke a verse:

时，天作婆罗门处于中堂，令梳头者理剃须发，见尊者优波摩于外门住，见已，即说偈言：

Why are you shaving your beard and hair, wearing
a monk's robe, standing at the gate, seeking what?

「何等剃须发，　　身著僧迦梨？住于彼门外，　　为欲何所求？」

Venerable Upamâ replied in verse:

尊者优波摩说偈答言：

The Arhat, the Well-Gone One of the world, has a back
wind illness. Is there any soothing water to cure the sage's illness?

「罗汉世善逝，　　所患背风疾，颇有安乐水，　　疗牟尼疾不？」

At that time, the brāhman Tiṣya filled a bowl with ghee, carried a bottle of oil and a bottle of rock candy, and had someone carry them, along with warm water, to where the Venerable Upamâ was. He applied it to the body of the World-Honored One, washed him with warm water, and made him drink ghee and honey. The World-Honored One's back illness immediately became peaceful.

时，天作婆罗门以满钵酥、一瓶油、一瓶石蜜，使人担持，并持暖水，随尊者优波摩诣世尊所，以涂其体，暖水洗之，酥蜜作饮，世尊背疾即得安隐。

At that time, the brāhman Tiṣya rose early in the morning, went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side, speaking a verse:

时，天作婆罗门晨朝早起，往诣佛所，稽首礼足，退坐一面，而说偈言：

What is meant by brāhman? What giving brings great fruit?
What is timely giving? What is a pure field of merit?

「何言婆罗门？　　施何得大果？何等为时施？　　云何净福田？」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse in reply:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

If one attains the knowledge of past lives, sees the definite birth
in the heavens, and exhausts all taints, the Sage attains the three
insights. Knowing well the liberation of the mind, liberated from all craving
is called a brāhman. Giving to them brings great fruit. Giving to
them is timely giving, according to one's wishes, a field of merit.

「若得宿命智，　　见天定趣生，得尽诸有漏，　　牟尼起三明，善知心解脱，　　解脱一切贪；说名婆罗门，　　施彼得大果，施彼为时施，　　随所欲福田。」

At that time, the brāhman Devadatta, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully rejoiced, paid homage, and departed.

时，天作婆罗门闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
