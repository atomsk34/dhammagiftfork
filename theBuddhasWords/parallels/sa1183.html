SĀ 1183

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1183（一一八三） 聚薪

Parallel to: SN 7.18 / SĀ² 2.97

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling through the human realm of Kosala and spent the night in a shala forest.

一时，佛在拘萨罗人间游行，夜宿一娑罗林中。

At that time, a brahmin was nearby in the forest with five hundred young brahmins. The brahmin always praised and admired, wishing to see the World-Honored One. "When will he come to this forest? I will then have the opportunity to see him and ask about my doubts. Will he have the leisure to explain it for me?"

时，有一婆罗门，近彼林侧，与五百年少婆罗门共。彼婆罗门常称叹钦想：「欲见世尊，何时游于此林，我因得见遇，问所疑，颇有闲暇为我记说？」

At that time, a young disciple of the brahmin went into the forest to gather firewood. He saw from afar the World-Honored One sitting under a tree, with dignified appearance, tranquil faculties, his mind peacefully concentrated, his form like a golden mountain, his radiance shining brightly. Having seen this, he thought, "My teacher, the brahmin, always praises and admires, wishing to see Gautama and ask about his doubts. Now that the śramaṇa Gautama has come to this forest, I should quickly go and inform my teacher." He then took his bundle of firewood and quickly returned to the schoolhouse. Having put down the bundle of firewood, he went to his teacher and said, "Teacher, you should know that you, teacher, have always praised and admired, wishing to see the śramaṇa Gautama, and ask about your doubts. Now that Gautama has come to this forest, teacher, you should know the time!"

时，彼婆罗门年少弟子为采薪故，入于林中，遥见世尊坐一树下，仪容端正，诸根寂静，其心寂定，形若金山，光明彻照。见已，作是念：「我和上婆罗门常称叹钦仰，欲见瞿昙，问其所疑，今此沙门瞿昙到此林中，我当疾往白和上令知。」即持薪束，疾还学堂。舍薪束已，诣和上所，白言：「和上当知，和上由来常所称叹钦仰欲见沙门瞿昙，脱到此林，当问所疑，今日瞿昙已到此林，和上知时！」

At that time, the brahmin went to where the World-Honored One was, exchanged greetings and pleasantries, and then sat to one side. He spoke in verse:

时，婆罗门即诣世尊所，面相问讯慰劳已，退坐一面。而说偈言：

Alone entering this terrifying, deep and secluded forest, firmly standing
without wavering, well practicing the method of right effort. Without
singing, dancing, or music, dwelling in silence and emptiness; I
have never seen one who enjoys the deep forest alone
seeking to be the supreme lord of the world? For
the thirty-three heavens, the blissful enjoyment of the heavenly realm?
Why do you practice austerities and wither yourself in the deep forest?

「独入此恐怖，　　深邃丛林中，坚住不倾动，　　善修正勤法。无歌舞音乐，　　寂默住空闲；我所未曾见，　　独乐深林者，欲求于世间，　　自在增上主？为三十三天，　　天上自在乐？何故深林中，　　苦行自枯槁？」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke in verse:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

If one desires to seek various things, being attached
to the many realms, they are all the root
of ignorance. I have long since vomited out all
such seeking. Not seeking, not flattering, not touching anything
In all dharmas, there is only the pure view
Attaining unsurpassed bodhi, not delighting in meditation and cultivation.

「若欲种种求，　　诸界多种著；彼一切皆是，　　愚痴之根本，如是一切求，　　我久悉已吐，不求不谄伪，　　一切无所触。于一切诸法，　　唯一清净观，得无上菩提，　　禅思修不乐。」

The brāhman again spoke in verse:

婆罗门复说偈言：

I now pay homage to you, the greatly tranquil and sage honored
one, the wondrous king of meditation and contemplation, the one who awakens
to boundless great awakening; the Tathāgata, the savior of devas and humans
majestic like a golden mountain. Liberated from the thicket, never attached to
the thicket; having pulled out the deep and sharp thorns, pure without
any trace left. The foremost among teachers, with the most excellent eloquence
in speech; the lion among men, roaring in the deep forest. Manifesting
the noble truth of suffering, the origin, cessation, and the eightfold path
able to exhaust the mass of suffering, riding out to be pure
and immaculate. Having liberated oneself from all suffering, liberating those sentient beings
suffering; in order to bring peace and happiness to sentient beings, one
expounds the true Dharma. Having severed all affection and love, having distanced
oneself from the net of desires; having cut off all the bonds
of attachment to existence. Like a lotus born in water, not stained
by dust or water; like the sun suspended in the sky, pure
and unobstructed by clouds. Excellent! Today I have come to the Kosa
forest and have seen the great teacher, the most honored one among
humans, the one with the most excellent two feet, the great forest
the great vigor, attaining the foremost wide liberation; the foremost of charioteers
paying homage to the fearless one.

「我今敬礼汝，　　大寂牟尼尊，禅思之妙王，　　觉无边大觉；如来天人救，　　巍巍若金山。解脱于丛林，　　于林永不著；已拔深利刺，　　清净无余迹。论师之上首，　　言说最胜辩；人中雄师子，　　震吼于深林。显现苦圣谛、　　集、灭、八正道；能尽众苦聚，　　乘出净无垢。自脱一切苦，　　济彼苦众生；安乐众生故，　　演说于正法。已断于恩爱，　　远离于欲网；断除于一切，　　有爱之结缚。如水生莲花，　　尘水不染著；如日停虚空，　　清净无云翳。善哉我今日，　　至拘萨罗林，得见于大师，　　两足之胜尊，大林大精进，　　得第一广度；调御师之首，　　敬礼无所畏。」

At that time, the brāhman spoke this extensive verse, praising the Buddha. Having heard the Buddha's teaching, he rejoiced and paid homage before departing.

时，婆罗门广说斯偈，赞叹佛已，闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
