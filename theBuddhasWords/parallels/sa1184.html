SĀ 1184

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1184（一一八四） 孙陀利

Parallel to: SN 7.9 / SĀ² 2.99

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling through the human realm of Kosala and stayed by the banks of the Sundari River.

一时，佛在拘萨罗人间游行，宿于孙陀利河侧。

At that time, not long after the World-Honored One had his hair shaved, in the latter part of the night, he sat cross-legged, with his body upright, contemplating with focused attention, covering his head with his robe.

尔时，世尊剃发未久，于后夜时，结跏趺坐，正身思惟，系念在前，以衣覆头。

At that time, there was a brahmin living by the banks of the Sundari River. He got up at night to perform a sacrifice, but the remaining food was not enough. When it was time, he went to the river, seeking a great and virtuous brahmin to offer it to.

时，孙陀利河侧有婆罗门住止，夜起持祠，余食不尽，时至河边，欲求大德婆罗门以奉之。

At that time, the World-Honored One heard the voice of the brahmin by the river. Having heard it, he cleared his throat, removed his robe, and showed his head.

尔时，世尊闻河边婆罗门声。闻已，謦咳作声，却衣现头。

At that time, the brahmin by the banks of the Sundari River saw the Buddha and thought, "This shaven-headed śramaṇa is not a brahmin." He wanted to take the food back. The brahmin then thought, "It's not only śramaṇas who shave their heads. There are also brahmins who shave their heads. I should go to him and ask about his birth." The brahmin by the banks of the Sundari River went to the World-Honored One and asked, "What is your family name?"

时，孙陀利河侧婆罗门见佛已，作是念：「是剃头沙门，非婆罗门。」欲持食还去。彼婆罗门复作是念：「非独沙门是剃头者，婆罗门中亦有剃头，应往至彼，问其所生。」时，孙陀利河侧婆罗门诣世尊所，而问之言：「为何姓生？」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

Do not ask about birth, but ask about practice. Carving
wood to make a drill can also produce fire; among
the lowly castes, the firm and solid Muni was born
With wisdom, shame, and remorse, diligent and well-tamed, reaching the
ultimate limit of great illumination, practicing pure and chaste conduct
Now is the right time to offer the remaining food.

「汝莫问所生，　　但当问所行。刻木为钻燧，　　亦能生于火；下贱种姓中，　　生坚固牟尼。智慧有惭愧，　　精进善调伏，究竟大明际，　　清净修梵行。而今正是时，　　应奉施余食。」

At that time, the brāhmaṇa Sundarī spoke a verse, saying:

时，孙陀利河侧婆罗门复说偈言：

Today is an auspicious day for me, seeking blessings and performing offerings. I
have encountered the great being, the most supreme and honored one of the
three times. If I do not see the Buddha, I shall offer to others instead.

「我今吉良日，　　求福修供养，遇得见大士，　　三时最胜尊，若不见佛者，　　当更施余人。」

At that time, the brāhmaṇa Sundarī on the banks of the Sundarī River gained faith and offered the remaining food to the World-Honored One. The World-Honored One did not accept it, because it was obtained through speaking verses. As explained above, he obtained the food through speaking verses.

尔时，孙陀利河侧婆罗门转得信心，即持余食以奉世尊，世尊不受，以说偈得故，如上因说偈而得食广说。

The brāhmaṇa Sundarī on the banks of the Sundarī River said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Where should this offered food be placed now?"

孙陀利河侧婆罗门白佛言：「世尊！今此施食当置何所？」

The Buddha told the brāhmaṇa, "I do not see any gods, demons, Brahmā, śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, devas, humans, or others in the world who can eat this food and make the body peaceful. Take this food and place it in water with no insects or on land with little growing grass."

佛告婆罗门：「我不见诸天、魔、梵、沙门、婆罗门、天神、世人有能食此食，令身安隐者。汝持此食去著无虫水中，及少生草地。」

At that time, the brāhmaṇa took the food and placed it in water with no insects. The water immediately smoked, boiled, and hissed, like a red-hot iron ball thrown into cold water, smoking, boiling, and hissing. In the same way, when the food was placed in water with no insects, it smoked, boiled, and hissed. The brāhmaṇa on the banks of the Sundarī River was frightened, his body hair standing on end, thinking it was a disaster. He ran up the bank, gathered dry wood, and made offerings to the fire to avert the calamity.

时，婆罗门即持此食著无虫水中，水即烟起涌沸，啾啾作声，如烧铁丸投之冷水，烟起涌沸，啾啾作声。如是彼食著无虫水中，烟起涌沸，啾啾作声。孙陀利河侧婆罗门心欲恐怖，身毛皆竖，谓为灾变，驰走上岸，集聚干木，供养祠火，令息灾恠。

The World-Honored One saw them gathering dry wood, making offerings to the fire, hoping to avert the calamity. Having seen this, he spoke a verse:

世尊见彼集聚干木，供养祠火，望息灾恠。见已。即说偈言：

The brāhmaṇa worships the fire, burning dry wood and grass, thinking it
is the pure path that can avert all calamities. This is a
bad offering, yet they call it wise. Making such causes and conditions
the non-Buddhists take it as purification. Now abandon the firewood and fire
and arouse the blazing inner fire. Always practice without negligence, always rich
in offerings. Generate pure faith everywhere, widely arranging great assemblies. The mind
and intention are the firewood, with black smoke rising from anger. False
speech is the flavor of dust, the tongue is the wooden ladle
The chest and abdomen are the place where fire burns, with the
fire of desire always blazing. One should skillfully subdue oneself, extinguishing the
fire of the person. Right faith is the great river, pure precepts
are the means of crossing. The clear and pure flowing water is
praised by the wise! The pure virtues of the gods among humans
should be used for bathing. Entering the water does not cling to
the body, peacefully and happily crossing to the other shore. The true
Dharma is the deep abyss, with merit and virtue as the means
of crossing. The clear and pure water is full, praised by the
wise! The pure virtues of the gods among humans should be used
for bathing. Entering the water does not cling to the body, peacefully
and happily crossing to the other shore. The true meaning is well-tamed
protecting and cultivating the practice of the Brahma-way. Compassion is the practice
of suffering, with a truly pure mind. Bathing with the true Dharma
is praised by the wise.

「婆罗门祠火，　　焚烧干草木，莫呼是净道，　　能却诸灾患。此则恶供养，　　而谓为黠慧；作如是因缘，　　外道取修净。汝今弃薪火，　　起内火炽然，常修不放逸，　　常富于供养。处处兴净信，　　广施设大会，心意为束薪，　　瞋恚黑烟起，妄语为尘味，　　口舌为木杓，胸怀燃火处，　　欲火常炽然。当善自调伏，　　消灭士夫火，正信为大河，　　净戒为度济，澄净清流水，　　智者之所叹！人中净天德，　　当于中洗浴，涉水不著身，　　安乐度彼岸。正法为深渊，　　福德为下济，澄净水充满，　　智者所赞叹！人中天净德，　　当于中洗浴，涉水不著身，　　安乐度彼岸。真谛善调御，　　摄护修梵行，慈悲为苦行，　　真实心清净，沐浴以正法，　　智者所称叹。」

At that time, the brāhmaṇa by the Sunandā River, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully followed and returned.

尔时，孙陀利河侧婆罗门闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，复道而去。
