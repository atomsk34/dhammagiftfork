SĀ 1189

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1189（一一八九） 梵天

Parallel to: SN 47.43 / SĀ² 2.102 / SĀ³ 3.4

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying at the Nairañjanā River near the Bodhi Tree in the village of Uruvilvā, not long after his enlightenment.

一时，佛住欝毘罗聚落尼连禅河侧菩提树下，成佛未久。

At that time, the World-Honored One was alone in quiet contemplation, and had this thought: "There is a single vehicle path that can purify beings, liberate them from all sorrows and griefs, eliminate suffering and distress, and attain the true nature of reality. It is called the four foundations of mindfulness. What are the four? Mindfulness of the body observing the body, mindfulness of feelings, mindfulness of the mind, and mindfulness of dharmas observing dharmas. If there are people who do not delight in the four foundations of mindfulness, they will not delight in the noble Dharma; those who do not delight in the noble Dharma will not delight in the noble path; those who do not delight in the noble path will not delight in the Dharma of immortality; those who do not delight in the Dharma of immortality will not be liberated from birth, old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress. If one delights in cultivating the four foundations of mindfulness, one will delight in cultivating the noble Dharma; those who delight in cultivating the noble Dharma will delight in the noble path; those who delight in the noble path will delight in the Dharma of immortality; those who delight in the Dharma of immortality will attain liberation from birth, old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress."

尔时，世尊独静思惟，作是念：「有一乘道能净众生，度诸忧悲，灭除苦恼，得真如法，谓四念处。何等为四？身身观念处，受、心、法法观念处。若有人不乐四念处者，则不乐如圣法；不乐如圣法者，则不乐如圣道；不乐如圣道者，则不乐甘露法；不乐甘露法者，则不解脱生、老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦。若乐修四念处者，则乐修如圣法；乐修如圣法者，则乐如圣道；乐如圣道者，则乐甘露法；乐甘露法者，得解脱生、老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦。」

At that time, the Lord of the Sahā World, the Brahmā King, knew the Buddha's thoughts. In the time it takes for a strong man to bend and stretch his arm, he disappeared from the Brahmā Heaven and appeared before the Buddha, praising him, saying: "So it is, World-Honored One! So it is, Well-Gone One! There is a single vehicle path that can purify beings, namely the four foundations of mindfulness, up to liberation from birth, old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress."

尔时，娑婆世界主梵天王知佛心念已，譬如力士屈伸臂顷，于梵天没，住于佛前，作是叹言：「如是，世尊！如是，善逝！有一乘道能净众生，谓四念处，乃至解脱生、老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦。」

At that time, the Brahmā King also spoke a verse, saying:

时，梵天王复说偈言：

That is, there is a single vehicle path that sees the end
of all existence. Expounding the true Dharma, comforting suffering beings. The past
honored ones have crossed over by this path; The future honored ones
will also cross over by this path. The present honored ones, the
right and perfect buddhas, cross over by this path, crossing the stream
of birth and death. Having perfected the end of birth and death
their minds are well-tamed and pure. In the cycle of birth and
death, they have completely exhausted it all; Knowing the various realms, the
wisdom eye reveals the right path. It is like the flowing waters
of the Ganges, all flowing into the great ocean, rushing and surging
far away. The right path is also like this. The vast wisdom
well reveals, attaining the Dharma of immortality. The supreme true Dharma wheel
never heard before, is turned for the sake of all beings out
of compassion. Protecting the multitude of gods and humans, enabling them to
cross to the other shore of existence. Therefore, all beings bow their
heads in reverence.

「谓有一乘道，　　见生诸有边，演说于正法，　　安慰苦众生。过去诸世尊，　　以乘斯道度；当来诸世尊，　　亦度乘斯道。现在尊正觉，　　乘此度海流，究竟生死际，　　调伏心清净，于生死轮转，　　悉已永消尽；知种种诸界，　　慧眼显正道。譬若恒水流，　　悉归趣大海，激流浚漂远，　　正道亦如是。广智善显示，　　逮得甘露法，殊胜正法轮，　　本所未曾闻，哀愍众生故，　　而为众生转。覆护天人众，　　令度有彼岸，是故诸众生，　　咸皆稽首礼。」

At that time, the Brahmā King, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully rejoiced and bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, then disappeared.

尔时，梵天王闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，稽首佛足，即没不现。
