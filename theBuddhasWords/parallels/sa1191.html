SĀ 1191

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1191（一一九一） 空闲处

Parallel to: SN 6.13 / SĀ² 2.104

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in the human realm of Kosala, staying in an empty and secluded place without villages, together with a group of bhikkhus. At that time, the World-Honored One spoke to the bhikkhus about the Dharma that accords with the practice of forest dwelling.

一时，佛在拘萨罗人间游行，住止空闲无聚落处，与比丘众夜宿其中。尔时，世尊为诸比丘说随顺阿练若法。

At that time, the Lord of the Sahā World, the Brahmā King, had this thought: "Now the World-Honored One is traveling in the human realm of Kosala, staying in an empty and secluded place without villages, together with a great assembly, spending the night in the wilderness. At that time, the World-Honored One spoke to the great assembly about the Dharma that accords with emptiness. I should go now to praise and support him!"

时，娑婆世界主梵天王作是念：「今者世尊在拘萨罗人间游行，住一空闲无聚落处，与诸大众止宿空野。尔时，世尊为诸大众说随顺空法，我今当往随顺赞叹！」

Just as quickly as a strong man bends and stretches his arm, he disappeared from the Brahma Heaven and appeared before the Buddha. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, then sat to one side and spoke in verse:

譬如力士屈伸臂顷，于梵天没，住于佛前，稽首佛足，退坐一面，而说偈言：

Approaching the forest seat, cutting off all afflictions, If
not delighting in solitude, entering the assembly to restrain
oneself. Subduing one's own mind, going from house to
house for alms, Controlling the faculties, concentrating the mind
Then practicing solitude, the seat of the forest, far
from all terrors, dwelling in fearlessness and peace. If
there are those fierce and dangerous, evil snakes and
poisonous harm, Dark clouds and great darkness, with thunder
and lightning flashing, Because of being free from afflictions
dwelling in peace day and night. As I have
heard the Dharma, not yet fully understood, practicing pure
conduct alone, not fearing a thousand deaths at the
hands of Māra. If practicing the path of awakening
not fearing the many. All those who attain the
stream-enterer, or the once-returner, and the non-returner, their number
is also immeasurable, unable to determine their number, fearing false speech.

「习近边林座，　　断除诸烦恼；若不乐空闲，　　入众自摄护。自调伏其心，　　家家行乞食，摄持于诸根，　　专精系心念；然后习空闲，　　阿练若床座，远离诸恐怖，　　无畏安隐住。若彼诸凶险，　　恶蛇众毒害，黑云大暗冥；　　震雷曜电光，离诸烦恼故，　　昼夜安隐住。如我所闻法，　　乃至不究竟，独一修梵行，　　不畏千死魔，若修于觉道，　　不畏于万数。一切须陀洹，　　或得斯陀含，及阿那含者，　　其数亦无量，不能定其数，　　恐怖于妄说。」

At that time, the Lord of the Sahā World, the Brahma King, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and followed his joy. He bowed to the Buddha and then disappeared.

时，娑婆世界主梵天王闻佛所说，欢喜随喜已，为佛作礼，即没不现。
