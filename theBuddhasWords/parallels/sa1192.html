SĀ 1192

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1192（一一九二） 集会

Parallel to: DN 20 / SN 1.37 / DĀ 19 (~) / SĀ² 2.105

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Thus have I heard:

一时，佛住迦毘罗卫迦毘罗卫林中，与五百比丘俱，皆是罗汉，诸漏已尽，所作已作，离诸重担，逮得己利，尽诸有结，正智心善解脱。尔时，世尊为诸大众说涅槃相应法。

At that time, the powerful devas from the great assemblies of the worlds in the ten directions all came to the gathering to make offerings to the World-Honored One and the assembly of monks. Furthermore, the Brahma kings dwelling in the Brahma realm had this thought: "Today the Buddha is staying in the country of Kapilavastu..." and so on, in detail, up to "...making offerings to the World-Honored One and the great assembly. We should now go to each praise!"

时，有十方世界大众威力诸天皆悉来会，供养世尊及比丘僧。复有诸梵天王住于梵世，作是念：「今日佛住迦毘罗卫国……」如上广说，乃至「供养世尊及诸大众，我今当往各各赞叹！」

Having had this thought, they disappeared from the Brahma realm in an instant, like a strong man bending and stretching his arm, and appeared before the Buddha. The foremost Brahma then spoke a verse, saying:

作是念已，譬如力士屈伸臂顷，从梵天没，住于佛前。第一梵天即说偈言：

In this great forest, the great assembly has gathered like clouds. The heavenly
beings from the ten directions have all come to pay respect. Therefore, I
have come from afar to pay homage to the most supreme and invincible Sangha.

「于此大林中，　　大众普云集，十方诸天众，　　皆悉来恭敬，故我远来礼，　　最胜难伏僧。」

The second Brahma Heaven also spoke a verse, saying:

第二梵天复说偈言：

These bhikṣus are truly diligent in their minds. In
this great forest, they gather their faculties and seek liberation.

「是诸比丘僧，　　真实心精进，于此大林中，　　摄诸根求度。」

The third Brahma Heaven then spoke a verse, saying:

第三梵天次说偈言：

Skillfully melting away the deep and sharp thorns of
affection and love, unshakable like Indra's banner. Crossing the
deep moats and flowing waters, pure and not seeking
desires, the great dragon tamer, the ferryman who skillfully guides.

「善方便消融，　　恩爱深利刺；坚固不倾动，　　如因陀罗幢。度深堑水流，　　清净不求欲，善度之导师，　　诸调伏大龙。」

The fourth Brahma Heaven then spoke a verse, saying:

第四梵天次说偈言：

Those who take refuge in the Buddha will never fall into evil destinies
they can sever the human body and attain the heavenly body to experience bliss.

「归依于佛者，　　终不堕恶趣；能断人中身，　　得天身受乐。」

After each had spoken a verse, the bodies of the four Brahma Heavens immediately disappeared.

各说偈已，四梵天身即没不现。
