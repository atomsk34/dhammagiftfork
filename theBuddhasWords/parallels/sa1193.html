SĀ 1193

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1193（一一九三） 瞿迦黎

Parallel to: SN 6.7–9 / SĀ² 2.106 / SĀ³ 3.5

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha. At that time, the Lord of the Sahā World, the Brahmā King, diligently went to where the Buddha was every day to pay respect and make offerings.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。时，有娑婆世界主梵天王，日日精勤，往诣佛所，尊重供养。

At that time, the Lord of the Sahā World thought: "It is too early today to come and see the Buddha. The World-Honored One is just entering a great samādhi. Let us go to the room of the monk Kālika, who is an associate of Devadatta." Having thought this, he went to their room and knocked on the door with his finger, saying: "Kālika! Kālika! Give rise to pure faith in Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana. Do not bring upon yourself harm and suffering for a long time!"

时，娑婆世界主作是念：「今旦太早而来见佛，正值世尊入大三昧，我等且当入提婆达多伴党瞿迦梨比丘房中。」作是念已，即入彼房，至房户中，以指扣户，口说是言：「瞿迦梨！瞿迦梨！于舍利弗、目连所起净信心，汝莫长夜得不饶益苦！」

Kālika said: "Who are you?"

瞿迦梨言：「汝是谁？」

Brahmā replied: "I am the Lord Brahmā of the Sahā World."

梵天答言：「娑婆世界主梵天王。」

Kālika said: "The World-Honored One did not predict that you would attain the state of non-returner, did he?"

瞿迦梨言：「世尊不记汝得阿那含耶？」

The Lord Brahmā said: "So it is, monk!"

梵天王言：「如是，比丘！」

Kālika said: "Why have you come?"

瞿迦梨言：「汝何故来？」

The Lord Brahmā, ruler of the Sahā world, replied: "This is incurable." He then spoke a verse:

娑婆世界主梵天王答言：「此不可治。」即说偈言：

In countless places, one gives rise to the mind to measure. Who
has the wisdom to give rise to this notion? The immeasurable that
one wants to measure is the obscuration of the aggregates of ordinary beings.

「于无量处所，　　生心欲筹量；何有黠慧者，　　而生此觉想？无量而欲量，　　是阴盖凡夫。」

At that time, the Lord Brahmā, ruler of the Sahā world, went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. He addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! I always come to the Buddha's place every day to pay respects and make offerings. I think to myself: 'It is too early today to come and see the World-Honored One. The World-Honored One is just entering a great samādhi. I will go to the room of the monk Devadatta's companion, Kāḷa.' I then stood at the door, gently knocked, and said: 'Kāḷa! Kāḷa! You should give rise to pure faith in the wise and intelligent ones such as Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana, so that you will not suffer harm for a long time!' Kāḷa said: 'Who are you?' I replied: 'I am the Lord Brahmā, ruler of the Sahā world.' Kāḷa said: 'Has the World-Honored One not predicted that you will attain the state of non-returner?' I replied: 'Yes.' Kāḷa then said: 'Why have you come?' I thought: 'This is incurable.' I then spoke a verse:

时，娑婆世界主梵天王往诣佛所，稽首礼足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！我常日日勤到佛所，亲觐供养，我作是念：『今旦太早来见世尊，正值世尊入大三昧，我且当入提婆达多伴党瞿迦梨比丘房中。』即住户中，徐徐扣户，口说是言：『瞿迦梨！瞿迦梨！当于舍利弗、目揵连贤善智慧者所起净信心，莫长夜得不饶益苦！』瞿迦梨言：『汝是谁？』我即答言：『是娑婆世界主梵天王。』瞿迦梨言：『世尊不记汝得阿那含耶？』我即答言：『如是。』瞿迦梨复言：『汝何故来？』我作是念：『此不可治。』即说偈言：

In the immeasurable, one gives rise to the mind to measure. The immeasurable
that one wants to measure is the obscuration of the aggregates of ordinary beings.

「『于不可量处，　　发心欲筹量，不可量欲量，　　是阴盖凡夫。』」

The Buddha told the Brahmā King, "It is so, it is so. Brahmā King!

佛语梵王：「如是，如是。梵王！

In the immeasurable, one gives rise to the mind to measure. What
wise person would give rise to this delusion? The immeasurable that one
wants to measure is the obscuration of the aggregates of ordinary beings.

「于不可量处，　　而发心欲量；何有智慧人，　　而生此妄想？不可量欲量，　　是阴盖凡夫。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Lord of the Sahā World, the Brahmā King, having heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced and was delighted. He rose from his seat, bowed to the Buddha, and disappeared.

佛说此经已，娑婆世界主梵天王闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，从座起，为佛作礼，即没不现。
