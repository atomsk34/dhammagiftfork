SĀ 1194

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1194（一一九四） 梵天

Parallel to: SN 6.6 / SĀ² 2.107

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, the great Brahma King and other Brahma gods, such as the Brahma god Good Arm, would go to see and make offerings to the World-Honored One every day.

尔时，大梵天王及余别梵天——善臂别梵天，日日方便，往见供养世尊。

At that time, the Brahma god Vāseṭṭha saw the Brahma god Good Arm diligently making offerings and asked, "Where are you going?"

时，有婆句梵天见别梵天——善臂梵天，精勤方便，而问言：「汝欲何之？」

He replied, "I wish to see the World-Honored One and make offerings with respect."

彼即答言：「欲见世尊，恭敬供养。」

At that time, the Brahma god Vāseṭṭha spoke a verse, saying:

时，婆句梵天即说偈言：

There are four white cranes, three golden palaces
five hundred and seventy-two practitioners of meditation, with
blazing golden bodies illuminating the palace of Brahma
Heaven. Look at my body now, why go there?

「彼有四鹄鸟，　　三种金色宫，五百七十二，　　修行禅思者，炽焰金色身，　　普照梵天宫；汝且观我身，　　何用至彼为？」

At that time, the Brahma King of Goodness and the Brahma King of Distinction, the Brahma King of Goodness and Distinction, spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，善梵王、别梵王——善臂别梵王复说偈言：

Although there is a golden body illuminating the palace of Brahma
Heaven, those with wisdom know that form has afflictions. The wise
do not delight in form, but are liberated in their minds.

「虽有金色身，　　普照梵天宫，其有智慧者，　　知色有烦恼，智者不乐色，　　于其心解脱。」

At that time, the Brahma King of Goodness and the Brahma King of Distinction, the Brahma King of Goodness and Distinction, went to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, withdrew and sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I now wish to come and see the World-Honored One, to pay respect and make offerings. There was a Brahma King named Vāgīśa who saw my intention and asked me, 'Where do you wish to go?' I then replied, 'I wish to go and see the World-Honored One, to pay respect and make offerings.' The Brahma King Vāgīśa then spoke a verse:

时，彼善梵天、别梵天——善臂别梵天，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！我今方便，欲来见世尊，恭敬供养。有婆句梵天，见我方便，而问我言：『汝今方便，欲何所之？』我即答言：『欲往见世尊，礼事供养。』婆句梵天即说偈言：

There are four kinds of white cranes, three kinds of
golden palaces, five hundred and seventy-two, where they meditate. Observing
my golden body illuminating the palace of Brahma Heaven; you
should observe my body, what is the use of going there?

「『有四种鹄鸟，　　三种金色宫，五百七十二，　　于中而禅思，观我身金色，　　普照梵天宫；汝且观我身，　　何用至彼为？』

"I then spoke a verse in reply:"

「我即说偈而答彼言：

Although there is a golden body illuminating the palace of Brahma
Heaven, one should know that true gold is the matter of
afflictions. The wise are liberated from form and do not delight in form.

「『虽有金色身，　　普照梵天宫，当知真金色，　　是则烦恼事。智者解脱色，　　于色不复乐。』」

The Buddha told Brahma Heaven: "So it is, Brahma Heaven! So it is, Brahma Heaven!"

佛告梵天：「如是，梵天！如是，梵天！

Although there is true gold illuminating the palace of Brahma Heaven
one should know that true gold is the matter of afflictions
The wise are liberated from form and do not delight in form.

「虽有真金色，　　普照梵天宫，当知真金色，　　则是烦恼事，智者解脱色，　　于色不复乐。」

At that time, the Brahma Heaven spoke a verse for the sake of the monk Kasyapa:

时，彼梵天为迦咤务陀低沙比丘故，说偈言：

The sharp axe of the mouth cuts one's own
body. This is due to evil speech. One should
praise those who deserve praise, but instead one slanders
them. Evil speech increases their faults, and one is
born without happiness! Gambling and drinking destroy wealth, but
their faults are very few. Having an evil mind
towards the Tathāgata is a great fault! There are
hundreds of thousands of hells named Nirarbuda, thirty-six hundred
and five Apapura. These are all hells for slandering
the sages, due to evil intentions and speech.

「夫士生世间，　　利斧在口中；还自斩其身，　　斯由恶言故。应毁者称誉，　　应誉而反毁；恶口增其过，　　所生无安乐！博弈酒丧财，　　其过失甚少；恶心向善逝，　　是则为大过！地狱有百千，　　名尼罗浮地，三十有六百，　　及五阿浮陀；斯皆谤圣狱，　　口意恶愿故。」

Having spoken this sutra, the Buddha said, "Those Brahma Heavens, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and followed them. They bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet and then disappeared."

佛说此经已，彼诸梵天闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，稽首佛足，即没不现。
