SĀ 1196

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1196（一一九六） 邪见

Parallel to: SN 6.5 / SĀ² 2.109

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, a certain Brahmā god, dwelling in the Brahma Heaven, gave rise to this wrong view, saying: “This place is permanent, constant, and unchanging. It is pure and solitary. I have never seen anyone come to this place, let alone go beyond it.”

时，有一梵天住梵天上，起如是邪见言：「此处常、恒、不变易，纯一出离，未曾见有来至此处，况复有过此上者？」

At that time, the World-Honored One knew the thoughts of that Brahmā god. He then entered samādhi. As he was absorbed in samādhi, he disappeared from Śrāvastī and appeared in the palace of the Brahmā Heaven, sitting cross-legged in the sky above the head of that Brahmā god, with his body straight and focused.

尔时，世尊知彼梵天心之所念，即入三昧，如其正受，于舍卫国没，现梵天宫，当彼梵天顶上，于虚空中结跏趺坐，正身系念。

At that time, Venerable Ājñātakauṇḍinya thought, "Where is the World-Honored One now?" He then used his heavenly eye, pure and surpassing human, to see the World-Honored One in the Brahma Heaven. Having seen, he then entered samādhi. As he was absorbed in samādhi, he disappeared from Śrāvastī and appeared in the Brahma Heaven, sitting cross-legged in the east, facing west towards the Buddha, with his body straight and focused, below the Buddha's seat and above the Brahma seat.

尔时，尊者阿若俱隣作是念：「今日世尊为在何所？」即以天眼净过人间眼，观见世尊在梵天上。见已，即入三昧，如其正受，于舍卫国没，现彼梵世，在于东方，西面向佛，结跏趺坐，端身系念，在佛座下，梵天座上。

At that time, Venerable Mahākāśyapa thought, "Where is the World-Honored One now?" He then used his heavenly eye, pure and surpassing human, to see the World-Honored One in the Brahma Heaven. Having seen, he then entered samādhi. As he was absorbed in samādhi, he disappeared from Śrāvastī and appeared in the Brahma Heaven, sitting cross-legged in the south, facing north towards the Buddha, with his body straight and focused, below the Buddha's seat and above the Brahma seat.

尔时，尊者摩诃迦叶作是念：「今日世尊为在何所？」即以天眼净过于人眼，见世尊在梵天上。见已，即入三昧，如其正受，于舍卫国没，现梵天上，在于南方，北面向佛，结跏趺坐，端身系念，在佛座下，梵天座上。

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra thought, "Where is the World-Honored One now?" He then used his heavenly eye, pure and surpassing human, to see the World-Honored One in the Brahma Heaven. Having seen, he then entered samādhi. As he was absorbed in samādhi, he disappeared from Śrāvastī and appeared in the Brahma Heaven, sitting cross-legged in the west, facing east towards the Buddha, with his body straight and focused, below the Buddha's seat and above the Brahma seat.

时，尊者舍利弗作是念：「世尊今者为在何所？」即以天眼净过于人眼，见世尊在梵天上。见已，即入三昧，如其正受，于舍卫国没，住梵天上，在于西方，东面向佛，结跏趺坐，端身系念，在佛座下，梵天座上。

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana thought, "Where is the World-Honored One now?" He then used his heavenly eye, pure and surpassing human, to see the World-Honored One in the Brahma Heaven. Having seen, he then entered samādhi. As he was absorbed in samādhi, he disappeared from Śrāvastī and appeared in the Brahma Heaven, sitting cross-legged in the north, facing south towards the Buddha, with his body straight and focused, below the Buddha's seat and above the Brahma seat.

尔时，尊者大目揵连即作是念：「今日世尊为在何所？」以天眼净过于人眼，遥见世尊在梵天上。见已，即入三昧，如其正受，于舍卫国没，住梵天上，在于北方，南面向佛，结跏趺坐，端身系念，在佛座下，梵天座上。

At that time, the World-Honored One said to Brahmā, "Do you now again have this view: 'From the beginning, I have never seen anyone superior to me'?"

尔时，世尊告梵天曰：「汝今复起是见：『从本已来，未曾见有过我上者』不？」

Brahmā said to the Buddha, "I now dare not say again: 'I have never seen anyone superior to me.' I only see that the light of Brahmā is obstructed." At that time, the World-Honored One, having taught, shown, instructed, and delighted Brahmā in various ways, then entered samādhi. As he was absorbed in samādhi, he disappeared from the Brahma Heaven and returned to Śrāvastī.

梵天白佛：「我今不敢复言：『我未曾见有过我上者。』唯见梵天光明被障。」尔时，世尊为彼梵天种种说法，示、教、照、喜已，即入三昧，如其正受，于梵天上没，还舍卫国。

Venerable Ājñātakauṇḍinya, Mahākāśyapa, and Śāriputra taught, showed, instructed, and delighted Brahmā in various ways, then entered samādhi. As they were absorbed in samādhi, they disappeared from the Brahma Heaven and returned to Śrāvastī. Only Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana remained there.

尊者阿若俱隣、摩诃迦叶、舍利弗为彼梵天种种说法，示、教、照、喜已，即入三昧，如其正受，于梵天没，还舍卫国，唯尊者大目揵连仍于彼住。

At that time, Brahmā asked Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, "Do all the other disciples of the World-Honored One have such great virtue and great power?"

时，彼梵天问尊者大目揵连：「世尊诸余弟子悉有如是大德大力不？」

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana spoke a verse, saying:

时，尊者大目揵连即说偈言：

The great virtuous ones possess the three knowledges, penetrate and observe others'
minds, and have exhausted the taints, the number of arhats is immeasurable.

「大德具三明，　　通达观他心，漏尽诸罗汉，　　其数无有量。」

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana spoke the Dharma for that Brahma-king in various ways, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting him. Having done so, he then entered samadhi. In accordance with his samadhi, he disappeared from the Brahma-realm and returned to the country of Shravasti.

时，尊者大目揵连为彼梵天种种说法，示、教、照、喜已，即入三昧，如其正受，于梵天没，还舍卫国。
