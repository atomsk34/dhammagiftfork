SĀ 1197

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1197（一一九七） 入灭

Parallel to: SN 6.15 / SĀ² 2.110

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying in the country of Kuśinagara, in the grove of the twin śāla trees.

一时，佛住俱尸那竭国力士生地坚固双树林。

At that time, the World-Honored One, about to enter parinirvāṇa, said to Venerable Ānanda, "Between the twin śāla trees, prepare a rope bed with the head facing north. The Tathāgata will enter parinirvāṇa without remainder in the middle of the night today."

尔时，世尊临般涅槃，告尊者阿难：「汝于坚固双树间敷绳床，北首，如来今日中夜于无余涅槃而般涅槃。」

Then Venerable Ānanda, following the World-Honored One's instructions, prepared a rope bed between the twin śāla trees, with the head facing north. He returned to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and said to the World-Honored One, "I have prepared a rope bed between the twin śāla trees, with the head facing north, for the Tathāgata."

时，尊者阿难奉世尊教，于双坚固树间为世尊敷绳床，北首已，还世尊所，稽首礼足，白言世尊：「已为如来于双坚固树间敷绳床，令北首。」

Then the World-Honored One went to the rope bed, lay down on his right side, with his head facing north, placing one foot on top of the other, and focused his mind on the appearance of dawn.

于是，世尊往就绳床，右脇著地，北首而卧，足足相累，系念明相。

Then the World-Honored One entered into the nirvāṇa without remainder in the middle of the night. After his nirvāṇa, the twin śāla trees immediately produced flowers, which hung down all around to make offerings to the World-Honored One.

尔时，世尊即于中夜，于无余涅槃而般涅槃。般涅槃已，双坚固树寻即生花，周匝垂下，供养世尊。

At that time, a certain bhikṣu spoke a verse, saying:

时，有异比丘即说偈言：

The good and firm trees, with their branches hanging down to pay homage to
the Buddha, with wonderful flowers making offerings, to the great teacher who has entered nirvāṇa.

「善好坚固树，　　枝条垂礼佛，妙花以供养，　　大师般涅槃。」

At that time, Śakra, Lord of the Devas, spoke a verse, saying:

寻时，释提桓因说偈：

All conditioned phenomena are impermanent, they are all phenomena of arising
and ceasing. Although they arise, they soon cease. This cessation is bliss.

「一切行无常，　　斯皆生灭法，虽生寻以灭，　　斯寂灭为乐。」

At that time, the Lord of the Sahā World, the Brahmā King, spoke another verse, saying:

寻时，娑婆世界主梵天王次复说偈言：

All beings in the world, those who are established, will
all abandon. Such a holy great teacher, unparalleled in the
world. Attaining the power of the Tathāgata, universally being the
eye for the world, will eventually return to destruction, entering
nirvāṇa without remainder.

「世间一切生，　　立者皆当舍，如是圣大师，　　世间无有比。逮得如来力，　　普为世间眼，终归会磨灭，　　入无余涅槃。」

The Venerable Aniruddha then spoke another verse, saying:

尊者阿那律陀次复说偈言：

The breath coming in and going out, the mind is well guarded
and protected. Coming from the basis, entering nirvāṇa in the world. The
appearance of great terror, causing the body hairs to stand on end
The one with all the powers of practice, the great teacher enters
nirvāṇa. The mind is not lazy, also not dwelling in all attachments
The mind gradually becomes liberated, like fire extinguished when firewood is exhausted.

「出息入息住，　　立心善摄护，从所依而来，　　世间般涅槃。大恐怖相生，　　令人身毛竖，一切行力具，　　大师般涅槃。其心不懈怠，　　亦不住诸爱，心法渐解脱，　　如薪尽火灭。」

Seven days after the Tathāgata's nirvāṇa, Venerable Ānanda went to the śiśira place and spoke a verse, saying:

如来涅槃后七日，尊者阿难往枝提所，而说偈言：

The guide's precious body went to the Brahma heaven. With
such great spiritual power, the inner fire burned the body
Five hundred [stacked] woolen blankets wrapped the body, all burned
and destroyed. A thousand fine woolen garments were used to
cover the Tathāgata's body. Only two were not burned, the
uppermost and the undergarment.

「导师此宝身，　　往诣梵天上，如是大神力，　　内火还烧身。五百［叠＊毛］缠身，　　悉烧令磨灭，千领细［叠＊毛］衣，　　以衣如来身。唯二领不烧，　　最上及衬身。」

When Venerable Ānanda spoke this verse, the bhikṣus were silent, both sad and joyful!

尊者阿难说是偈时，诸比丘默然悲喜！
