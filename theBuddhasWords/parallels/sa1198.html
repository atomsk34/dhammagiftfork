SĀ 1198

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1198（一一九八） 旷野

Parallel to: SN 5.1 / SĀ² 2.214

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, there was a bhikṣuṇī named Ārāḍī who was staying in the Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī. One morning, Ārāḍī bhikṣuṇī put on her robes, took her alms bowl, and entered Śrāvastī to beg for food. After eating, she returned to the monastery, put away her robes and bowl, washed her feet, took her sitting mat, put it on her right shoulder, and went to the Andhavana forest to sit in meditation.

时，有阿臈毘比丘尼，住舍卫国王园精舍比丘尼众中。时，阿臈毘比丘尼晨朝著衣持钵，入舍卫城乞食。食已，还精舍，举衣钵，洗足，持尼师坛，著右肩上，入安陀林坐禅。

At that time, Māra the Evil One thought, "Now the Śramaṇa Gautama is staying at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. He has a disciple named Ārāḍī bhikṣuṇī who is staying in the Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī. This morning, Ārāḍī bhikṣuṇī put on her robes, took her alms bowl, and entered Śrāvastī to beg for food. After eating, she returned to the monastery, put away her robes and bowl, washed her feet, took her sitting mat, put it on her right shoulder, and went to the Andhavana forest to sit in meditation. I shall now go there to create obstacles for her." He then transformed himself into a young man of handsome appearance and went to where the bhikṣuṇī was. He said to the bhikṣuṇī, "Elder Sister! Where are you going?"

时，魔波旬作是念：「今沙门瞿昙住舍卫国祇树给孤独园，有弟子阿臈毘比丘尼，住舍卫国王园精舍比丘尼众中，晨朝著衣持钵，入舍卫城乞食。食已，还精舍，举衣钵，洗足已，持尼师坛，著右肩上，入安陀林坐禅。我今当往，为作留难。」即化作年少，容貌端正，往诣彼比丘尼所，语比丘尼言：「阿姨！欲何处去？」

The bhikṣuṇī replied, "Virtuous one! I am going to a place of solitude."

比丘尼答言：「贤者！到远离处去。」

At that time, Māra the Evil One spoke a verse, saying:

时，魔波旬即说偈言：

In the world, there is no escape. What is the use of
seeking solitude? Return to enjoying the five desires, lest you regret it later.

「世间无有出，　　用求远离为，还服食五欲，　　勿令后变悔。」

At that time, the bhikṣuṇī Ārāḍī had this thought: "Who is this, wanting to frighten me? Is it a human or a non-human, a deceitful person?"

时，阿臈毘比丘尼作是念：「是谁？欲恐怖我。为是人耶？为非人耶？奸狡人耶？」

The mind immediately thought: "This must be the evil Māra wanting to disturb me." Having realized it, he spoke a verse:

心即念言：「此必恶魔欲乱我耳。」觉知已，而说偈言：

There is an escape from the world, I myself know what
I have attained. You, the lowly evil Māra, do not know
that path. Just as a sharp knife harms, so too are
the five desires. Just as cutting flesh forms, so too is
the suffering aggregate. As you said before, those who enjoy the
five desires, that is not enjoyable, it is a place of
great terror. Free from all joy and pleasure, abandoning all great
darkness, extinguishing and realizing it, dwelling peacefully, free from all taints
Recognizing you, the evil Māra, you will soon disappear on your own.

「世间有出要，　　我自知所得，鄙下之恶魔，　　汝不知其道。譬如利刀害，　　五欲亦如是，譬如斩肉形，　　苦受阴亦然。如汝向所说，　　服乐五欲者，是则不可乐，　　大恐怖之处。离一切喜乐，　　舍诸大暗冥，以灭尽作证，　　安住离诸漏。觉知汝恶魔，　　寻即自灭去。」

At that time, Māra Pāpīyān thought: "That bhikṣuṇī Ārāḍā has already known my mind." He was distressed and unhappy, and then disappeared.

时，魔波旬作是念：「彼阿臈毘比丘尼已知我心。」愁忧不乐，即没不现。
