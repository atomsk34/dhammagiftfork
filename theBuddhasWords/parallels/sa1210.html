SĀ 1210

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1210（一二一〇） 舍利弗

Parallel to: SN 8.6 / SĀ² 2.226

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Jētavana Monastery in Sàvatthi.

一时，佛住瞻婆国揭伽池侧。

At that time, Venerable Sàriputta was in the Dharma Hall, where many bhikṣus had gathered to listen to his teaching. His words were full of meaning, his eloquence was pure and clear, easy to understand and delightful to hear, without obstruction or interruption, revealing profound truths. The bhikṣus listened attentively, with respect and mindfulness, giving their full attention.

时，尊者舍利弗在供养堂，有众多比丘集会而为说法，句味满足，辩才简净，易解乐闻，不阂不断，深义显现。彼诸比丘专至乐听，尊重忆念，一心侧听。

At that time, Venerable Vāgīśa was in the assembly and thought, "I will praise Venerable Sàriputta in verse right in front of him." Having thought this, he rose, joined his palms, and said to Venerable Sàriputta, "I wish to speak.

时，尊者婆耆舍在于会中，作是念：「我当于尊者舍利弗面前说偈赞叹。」作是念已，即起，合掌白尊者舍利弗：「我欲有所说。」

Sàriputta said, "Speak as you wish!"

舍利弗告言：「随所乐说！」

Venerable Vāgīśa then spoke in verse:

尊者婆耆舍即说偈言：

Able to concisely explain the Dharma, causing the assembly to widely
understand, the wise Upatissa proclaims in the great assembly. When speaking
the Dharma, the throat emits a beautiful voice, a voice that
is pleasing and lovable, a voice that is harmonious and gradually
advancing. Hearing the voice, all are delighted and focused without wavering.

「善能略说法，　　令众广开解，贤优婆提舍，　　于大众宣畅。当所说法时，　　咽喉出美声，悦乐爱念声，　　调和渐进声。闻声皆欣乐，　　专念不移转。」

When Venerable Vāgīśa spoke these words, the bhikṣus who heard them were all greatly delighted.

尊者婆耆舍说此语时，诸比丘闻其所说，皆大欢喜。
