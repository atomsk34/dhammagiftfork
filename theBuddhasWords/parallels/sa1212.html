SĀ 1212

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1212（一二一二） 自恣

Parallel to: SN 8.7 / MĀ 121 / SĀ² 2.228 / EĀ 32.5

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying in the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha during the summer retreat, together with a great assembly of five hundred bhikṣus, all of whom were arhats, their taints exhausted, their work done, their heavy burden laid down, their own goal attained, their fetters of existence completely severed, their minds well liberated by right knowledge, except for one bhikṣu, namely Venerable Ānanda, whom the World-Honored One had predicted would attain the state of non-knowledge in this very life.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园夏安居，与大比丘众五百人俱，皆是阿罗汉，诸漏已尽，所作已作，离诸重担，断除有结，正智心善解脱，除一比丘，谓尊者阿难，世尊记说彼现法当得无知证。

At that time, the World-Honored One, on the fifteenth day of the month, at the time of the meal, in front of the great assembly, spread out his seat and sat down. Having sat down, he addressed the bhikṣus, saying: "I am a brāhman, attaining parinirvāṇa, holding the last body, as a great physician, pulling out the sword-like thorns. I am a brāhman, attaining parinirvāṇa, holding this last body, as the unsurpassed physician, able to pull out the sword-like thorns. You are my sons, born from my mouth, born from the Dharma, inheriting the remaining wealth of the Dharma. You should bear me in mind, so that there are no faults to be reproached in my body, speech, or mind."

尔时，世尊临十五日月食受时，于大众前敷座而坐，坐已，告诸比丘：「我为婆罗门，得般涅槃，持后边身，为大医师，拔诸剑刺。我为婆罗门，得般涅槃，持此后边身，无上医师，能拔剑刺。汝等为子，从我口生，从法化生，得法余财，当怀受我，莫令我若身、若口、若心有可嫌责事。」

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra, in the assembly, rose from his seat, adjusted his robes, made obeisance to the Buddha, joined his palms, and said to the Buddha: "The World-Honored One just said: 'I am a brāhman, attaining parinirvāṇa, holding the last body, as the unsurpassed great physician, able to pull out the sword-like thorns. You are my sons, born from my mouth, born from the Dharma, inheriting the remaining wealth of the Dharma. Bhikṣus! You should bear me in mind, so that there are no faults to be reproached in my body, speech, or mind.' We do not see any faults to be reproached in the World-Honored One's body, speech, or mind. Why is that? The World-Honored One can subdue those who are not subdued, pacify those who are not pacified, bring relief to those who are not relieved, and attain parinirvāṇa for those who have not attained parinirvāṇa. The Tathāgata knows the path, speaks of the path, and points to the path. Then the śrāvakas accomplish the path, follow the path, and uphold the teachings of the teacher. According to the teachings, they correctly aspire towards the true and good Dharma.

尔时，尊者舍利弗在众会中，从座起，整衣服，为佛作礼，合掌白佛：「世尊向者作如是言：『我为婆罗门，得般涅槃，持最后身，无上大医，能拔剑刺。汝为我子，从佛口生，从法化生，得法余财。诸比丘！当怀受我，莫令我身、口、心有可嫌责。』我等不见世尊身、口、心有可嫌责事。所以者何？世尊不调伏者能令调伏，不寂静者能令寂静，不稣息者能令稣息，不般涅槃者能令般涅槃。如来知道，如来说道，如来向道，然后声闻成就，随道、宗道，奉受师教，如其教授，正向欣乐真如善法。

I do not see any faults to be reproached in the World-Honored One's body, speech, or mind. I now beg the World-Honored One to bear in mind any faults to be reproached in my body, speech, or mind that may be seen, heard, or suspected.

「我于世尊都不见有可嫌责身、口、心行。我今于世尊所，乞愿怀受见闻疑罪，若身、口、心有嫌责事。」

The Buddha told Śāriputra: "I do not see any faults to be reproached in your body, speech, or mind that may be seen, heard, or suspected. Why is that? You, Śāriputra, uphold the precepts, have much learning, have few desires, are content, practice seclusion, diligently apply skillful means, have right mindfulness and right concentration, have swift wisdom, sharp wisdom, wisdom of liberation, wisdom of disenchantment, great wisdom, vast wisdom, deep wisdom, incomparable wisdom, and have accomplished the treasure of wisdom. You instruct, illuminate, and delight others, and always praise instructing, illuminating, and delighting. You teach the Dharma for the sake of the assembly, never tiring.

佛告舍利弗：「我不见汝有见闻疑身、口、心可嫌责事。所以者何？汝舍利弗持戒多闻，少欲知足，修行远离，精勤方便，正念正受，捷疾智慧、明利智慧、出要智慧、厌离智慧、大智慧、广智慧、深智慧、无比智慧，智宝成就，示、教、照、喜，亦常赞叹示、教、照、喜，为众说法，未曾疲倦。

It is like the eldest son of a wheel-turning sage king who is to be crowned but has not yet been crowned. He already abides in the coronation rites and practices, and whatever the father turns, he also turns. You are now like this. You are my eldest son, neighboring the coronation but not yet crowned. Abiding in the rites and practices, whatever Dharma wheel I should turn, you also turn, attaining the state of no more arising, exhausting all taints, and having well-liberated mind. Thus, Śāriputra! I do not see, hear, or suspect any faults to be reproached in your body, speech, or mind.

「譬如转轮圣王，第一长子应受灌顶而未灌顶，已住灌顶仪法，如父之法，所可转者亦当随转。汝今如是，为我长子，隣受灌顶而未灌顶，住于仪法，我所应转法轮，汝亦随转，得无所起，尽诸有漏，心善解脱。如是，舍利弗！我于汝所，都无见闻疑身、口、心可嫌责事。」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha: "World-Honored One! If I have no faults to be reproached in my body, speech, or mind that may be seen, heard, or suspected, do these five hundred bhikṣus have no faults to be reproached in their body, speech, or mind that may be seen, heard, or suspected?"

舍利弗白佛言：「世尊！若我无有见闻疑身、口、心可嫌责事，此诸五百诸比丘得无有见闻疑身、口、心可嫌责事耶？」

The Buddha told Śāriputra: "I also do not see any faults to be reproached in the body, speech, or mind of these five hundred bhikṣus that may be seen, heard, or suspected. Why is that? These five hundred bhikṣus are all arhats, their taints have already ceased, their work is done, they have already cast off the heavy burden, cut off the fetters of existence, have right knowledge and well-liberated mind, except for one bhikṣu, namely Venerable Ānanda, whom I have predicted will attain the state of no more learning in this present life. Therefore, I do not see any faults in the body, speech, or mind of these five hundred bhikṣus that may be seen, heard, or suspected and are to be reproached.

佛告舍利弗：「我于此五百比丘亦不见有见闻疑身、口、心可嫌责事。所以者何？此五百比丘皆是阿罗汉，诸漏已尽，所作已作，已舍重担，断诸有结，正智心善解脱，除一比丘，谓尊者阿难，我记说彼于现法中得无知证。是故，诸五百比丘我不见其有身、口、心见闻疑罪可嫌责者。」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha: "World-Honored One! Since these five hundred bhikṣus have no faults to be reproached in their body, speech, or mind that may be seen, heard, or suspected, how many of these bhikṣus have attained the three knowledges? How many are liberated in both ways? How many are liberated by wisdom?"

舍利弗白佛言：「世尊！此五百比丘既无有见闻疑身、口、心可嫌责事，然此中几比丘得三明？几比丘俱解脱？几比丘慧解脱？」

The Buddha told Śāriputra: "Among these five hundred bhikṣus, ninety have attained the three knowledges, ninety are liberated in both ways, and the rest are liberated by wisdom. Śāriputra! These bhikṣus are free from all agitation, without skin or flesh, truly firm and solid."

佛告舍利弗：「此五百比丘中，九十比丘得三明，九十比丘得俱解脱，余者慧解脱。舍利弗！此诸比丘离诸飘转，无有皮肤，真实坚固。」

At that time, Venerable Vāgīśa was in the assembly. He thought: "I will now praise and recite the verses in front of the World-Honored One and the great assembly." Having thought this, he immediately rose from his seat, adjusted his robes, bowed to the Buddha, placed his right knee on the ground, joined his palms, and said to the Buddha: "World-Honored One! I wish to speak. Well-Gone One! I wish to speak."

时，尊者婆耆舍在众会中，作是念：「我今当于世尊及大众面前叹说怀受偈。」作是念已，即从座起，整衣服，为佛作礼，右膝著地，合掌白佛：「世尊！欲有所说。善逝！欲有所说。」

The Buddha told Vāgīśa: "Speak as you wish!"

佛告婆耆舍：「随所乐说！」

At that time, Vāgīśa spoke a verse, saying:

时，婆耆舍即说偈言：

On the fifteenth day of purity, the five hundred people in
the assembly, having severed all bonds, are great sages with the
end of existence. Practicing purity, widely liberated, no longer accepting any
existence, birth and death forever cut off. What was to be
done is done, all taints exhausted, the five hindrances like clouds
removed, the root of craving pulled out. The lion is fearless
free from all remaining existence, destroying all remaining bonds, transcending the
realm of remaining existence. All the enemies of taints are all
hidden, like a wheel-turning king, embracing all dependents. With a compassionate
mind, widely spreading the teachings, the whole country obeys and uses
them. Able to subdue the enemies of Māra, he is the
unsurpassed guide. With a respectful mind, serving the three knowledges, old
age and death extinguished, he is the true son of the
Dharma, without the affliction of being blown about. Pulling out the
thorns of afflictions, I pay homage to the offspring of the Sun-like family.

「十五清净日，　　其众五百人，断除一切结，　　有尽大仙人。清净相习近，　　清净广解脱，不更受诸有，　　生死已永绝。所作者已作，　　得一切漏尽，五盖已云除，　　拔刺根本爱。师子无所畏，　　离一切有余，害诸有怨结，　　超越有余境。诸有漏怨敌，　　皆悉已潜伏，犹如转轮王，　　怀受诸眷属。慈心广宣化，　　海内悉奉用，能伏魔怨敌，　　为无上导师。信敬心奉事，　　三明老死灭，为法之真子，　　无有飘转患。拔诸烦恼刺，　　敬礼日种胤。」

After the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus who heard what the Buddha had said rejoiced and upheld it.

佛说是经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
