SĀ 1216

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1216（一二一六） 憍慢

Parallel to: SN 8.3 / SĀ² 2.251

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Vāgīśa, being able to skillfully expound the Dharma with his own wisdom and being intelligent in the Dharma, gave rise to a mind of arrogance towards those who had given rise to the holy life. He then thought to himself, "It is not beneficial for me, I do not obtain benefits, I obtain suffering and do not obtain happiness. I myself, with my wisdom, belittle those who are intelligent in the holy life. I should now speak verses that can give rise to disgust." He then spoke the verses:

时，尊者婆耆舍自以智慧堪能善说，于法聪明梵行所生憍慢心，即自心念：「我不利，不得利，得苦不得乐，我自以智慧轻慢于彼聪明梵行者，我今当说能生厌离偈。」即说偈言：

Gautama, do not give rise to arrogance, cut off arrogance without remainder
do not give rise to the notion of arrogance, do not retreat
and give rise to regret. Do not conceal from others, the plow
of mud kills arrogance and falls. Right acceptance can eliminate sorrow, seeing
the path and abiding in the right path. The mind obtains joy
and happiness, seeing the path and self-restraint. Therefore, unobstructed eloquence, pure and
free from all hindrances. Cutting off all arrogance, giving rise to all
places of clarity, right mindfulness in the three kinds of supernormal knowledge
the supernormal power of knowing others' minds.

「瞿昙莫生慢，　　断慢令无余，莫起慢觉想，　　莫退生变悔。莫隐覆于他，　　埿犁杀慢堕，正受能除忧，　　见道住正道。其心得喜乐，　　见道自摄持，是故无碍辩，　　清净离诸盖。断一切诸慢，　　起一切明处，正念于三明，　　神足他心智。」

At that time, after Venerable Vāgīśa spoke these verses that give rise to disgust, his mind became pure.

时，尊者婆耆舍说此生厌离偈已，心得清净。
