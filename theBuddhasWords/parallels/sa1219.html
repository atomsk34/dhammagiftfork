SĀ 1219

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1219（一二一九） 龙胁

Parallel to: SN 8.8

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying on the side of Nagas Mountain in Rajagriha, together with a thousand bhikkhus, all of whom were arhats, who had exhausted all taints, accomplished what was to be done, laid down the heavy burden, attained their own goal, exhausted all fetters, and were well liberated in mind through right knowledge.

一时，佛住王舍城那伽山侧，与千比丘俱，皆是阿罗汉，尽诸有漏，所作已作，离诸重担，逮得己利，尽诸有结，正智心善解脱。

At that time, Venerable Vāgīśa was staying in the charnel ground in the Cold Forest in Rājagṛha, and he thought: "Now the World-Honored One is staying on the side of Nāga Mountain in Rājagṛha, together with a thousand bhikkhus, all of whom are arhats, who have exhausted all taints, accomplished what was to be done, laid down the heavy burden, attained their own goal, exhausted all fetters, and are well liberated in mind through right knowledge. I should now go to each praise the World-Honored One and the Sangha of bhikkhus." Having thought this, he went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and stood to one side. He then spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，尊者婆耆舍住王舍城寒林中丘塜间，作是念：「今世尊住王舍城那伽山侧，与千比丘俱，皆是阿罗汉，诸漏已尽，所作已作，离诸重担，逮得己利，尽诸有结，正智心善解脱。我今当往，各别赞叹世尊及比丘僧。」作是念已，即往诣佛所，稽首礼足，退住一面。而说偈言：

The unsurpassed guide, dwelling on the side of Nāga Mountain, with a
retinue of a thousand bhikkhus, serving the Tathāgata. The Great Teacher extensively
expounds the Dharma, the path of the cool nirvāṇa, attentively listening to
the pure Dharma, spoken by the Perfectly Enlightened One. Honored by the
Perfectly Enlightened One, dwelling in the midst of the great assembly, the
great dragon with the virtue-covering, the foremost of the sages. Raising the
dense clouds of merit, universally raining down on the assembly of śrāvakas
arising from the right samādhi during the day, coming to pay homage
to the Great Teacher. The disciple Vāgīśa bows his head in reverence.

「无上之导师，　　住那伽山侧，千比丘眷属，　　奉事于如来。大师广说法，　　清凉涅槃道，专听清白法，　　正觉之所说。正觉尊所敬，　　处于大众中，德阴之大龙，　　仙人之上首。兴功德密云，　　普雨声闻众，起于昼正受，　　来奉觐大师。弟子婆耆舍，　　稽首而顶礼。

"World-Honored One! I wish to speak. Yes, Well-Gone One! I wish to speak."

「世尊！欲有所说。唯然，善逝！欲有所说。」

The Buddha told Vāgīśa, "Speak as you wish, without prior deliberation."

佛告婆耆舍：「随汝所说，莫先思惟。」

At that time, Vāgīśa spoke a verse, saying:

时，婆耆舍即说偈言：

Mara, arouse slight evil, and quickly suppress it. You can conceal
and obstruct all demons, causing them to realize their faults. Observe
and understand the bonds, and distinguish the pure and white dharmas
Illuminating like the sun and moon, you are the king of
various paths. Transcending wisdom and realizing, you expound the foremost Dharma
Emerging from the afflictions and various flows, you expound the path
in immeasurable ways. Establishing the ambrosia, seeing the true Dharma of
the truths, following this path in this way, such a teacher
is difficult to encounter. Establishing the path of ambrosia, seeing the
truths and upholding detachment, the World-Honored One skillfully expounds the Dharma
able to remove the veils of people. Clearly seeing all dharmas
in order to tame and follow the teachings.

「波旬起微恶，　　潜制令速灭，能掩障诸魔，　　令自觉知过。观察解结缚，　　分别清白法，明照如日月，　　为诸异道王。超出智作证，　　演说第一法，出烦恼诸流，　　说道无量种。建立于甘露，　　见谛真实法，如是随顺道，　　如是师难得。建立甘露道，　　见谛崇远离，世尊善说法，　　能除人阴盖。明见于诸法，　　为调伏随学。」

After the Venerable Vāgīśa spoke this verse, the bhikṣus who heard his words were all greatly delighted.

尊者婆耆舍说是偈已，诸比丘闻其所说，皆大欢喜。
