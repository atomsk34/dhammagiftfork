SĀ 1221

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1221（一二二一） 尼瞿陀劫宾

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, Venerable Nikhṛṣṭa was staying in a wild place inhabited by birds and beasts, and was seriously ill. Venerable Vāgīśa was attending to him as a patient, caring for and serving him.

时，有尊者尼拘律想住于旷野禽兽之处，疾病委笃，尊者婆耆舍为看病人，瞻视供养。

Venerable Nikhṛṣṭa passed away due to his illness.

彼尊者尼拘律想以疾病故，遂般涅槃。

At that time, Venerable Vāgīśa thought, "Does my teacher have a remainder of nirvana or not? I should now seek its characteristics."

时，尊者婆耆舍作是念：「我和上为有余涅槃？无余涅槃？我今当求其相。」

At that time, after Venerable Vāgīśa had made offerings to the relics of Venerable Nikhṛṣṭa, he took his robes and bowl and went to Rājagṛha. Arriving in Rājagṛha, he put away his robes and bowl, washed his feet, went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and stood to one side, speaking a verse:

尔时，尊者婆耆舍供养尊者尼拘律想舍利已，持衣钵，向王舍城。次第到王舍城，举衣钵，洗足已，诣佛所，稽首礼足，退住一面。而说偈言：

I now pay homage to the great teacher, the perfectly enlightened
one who is not extinguished. In this present life, all doubts
and nets are severed. The bhikṣu who dwelt in the wilderness
has passed away and attained parinirvāṇa. His deportment and restrained faculties
were praised by the virtuous ones in the world. The World-Honored
One named him Nikhṛṣṭa. I now ask the World-Honored One about
his unshakable liberation. With diligent effort and skillful means, please explain
his merits to me. I am a descendant of the Śākya
clan, a disciple of the World-Honored One's Dharma. And the others
all wish to know what is said by the one with
the perfect path eye. We who dwell here all wish to
hear. The World-Honored One is the great teacher, the unsurpassed savior
of the world, who cuts off doubts and is a great
sage. His wisdom is fully equipped. He illuminates the divine path
with his perfect eye, shining brightly on the four assemblies, just
like Śakra Devānām-Indra shining on the thirty-three heavens. All greed, desire
and doubts arise from ignorance. If one encounters the Tathāgata, they
are all eradicated without remainder. The World-Honored One's divine path eye
is the supreme in the world. It eliminates the ignorance of
beings, like the wind scattering floating dust. All the worlds are
obscured and submerged by afflictions. If there were none other with
eyes of wisdom like the Buddha, the light of wisdom would
illuminate all, causing them to be equally diligent. I only pray
that the greatly wise Honored One will explain it for the
sake of the assembly. Speaking with a subtle voice, we will
listen with single-minded attention. The soft sound will be proclaimed, heard
throughout all worlds. Just as one tormented by heat seeks cool
water, the Buddha knows without diminution. We also seek to know.

「我今礼大师，　　等正觉无灭，于此现法中，　　一切疑网断。旷野住比丘，　　命终般涅槃，威仪摄诸根，　　大德称于世。世尊为制名，　　名尼拘律想，我今问世尊，　　彼不动解脱。精进勤方便，　　功德为我说，我为释迦种，　　世尊法弟子。及余皆欲知，　　圆道眼所说，我等住于此，　　一切皆欲闻。世尊为大师，　　无上救世间，断疑大牟尼，　　智慧已具备。圆照神道眼，　　光明显四众，犹如天帝释，　　曜三十三天。诸贪欲疑惑，　　皆从无明起，若得遇如来，　　断灭悉无余。世尊神道眼，　　世间为最上，灭除众生愚，　　如风飘游尘。一切诸世间，　　烦恼覆隐没，设余悉无有，　　明目如佛者。慧光照一切，　　令同大精进，唯愿大智尊，　　当为众记说。言出微妙声，　　我等专心听，柔软音演说，　　诸世间普闻。犹如热渴逼，　　求索清凉水，如佛无减知，　　我等亦求知。」

Venerable Vāgīśa spoke another verse:

尊者婆耆舍复说偈言：

Now hearing the Unsurpassed One's prediction and explanation of his merits
I joyfully cultivate the holy life without emptiness. As taught and
practiced, I follow the disciples of the Sage, extinguishing the long
bonds of birth and death, the fetters of false and illusory
transformations. By seeing the World-Honored One, I can cut off all
attachments, cross to the other shore of birth and death, and
no longer undergo any existence.

「今闻无上士，　　记说其功德，不空修梵行，　　我闻大欢喜。如说随说得，　　顺牟尼弟子，灭生死长縻，　　虚伪幻化缚。以见世尊故，　　能断除诸爱，度生死彼岸，　　不复受诸有。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished. Venerable Vāgīśa, having heard the Buddha's words, joyfully rejoiced, bowed, and departed.

佛说此经已，尊者婆耆舍闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
