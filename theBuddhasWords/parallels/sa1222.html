SĀ 1222

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1222（一二二二） 鸟巢

Parallel to: SN 11.6 / SĀ² 2.49

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "In the past, the gods and asuras fought a battle, and the asuras won while the gods were defeated. At that time, the army of Śakra, Lord of the Devas, was destroyed and scattered, and they were extremely frightened. Riding in a chariot, they fled north and returned to the heavenly palace.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「过去世时，天、阿修罗对阵鬪战，阿修罗胜，诸天不如。时，天帝释军坏退散，极生恐怖，乘车北驰，还归天宫。

"The road below Mount Sumeru passes through a forest. Under the forest is a nest of garuḍas, with many garuḍa chicks. At that time, Śakra, Lord of the Devas, was afraid that the passing chariots and horses would trample the chicks to death. He told the charioteer, 'Turn the chariot around and return. Do not kill the chicks.' The charioteer said to the king, 'The asura army is chasing after us. If we turn back, we will be trapped by them.' Śakra said, 'It is better to turn back and be killed by the asuras than to have the army trample and kill sentient beings.' On the road, the charioteer turned the chariot to face south. The asura army saw from afar that Śakra, Lord of the Devas, was turning the chariot around and returning. They thought it was a battle strategy and immediately retreated in fear. The army was greatly terrified, their formation was destroyed, and they scattered and returned to the asura palace."

「须弥山下道迳丛林，林下有金翅鸟巢，多有金翅鸟子。尔时，帝释恐车马过，践杀鸟子，告御者言：『可回车还，勿杀鸟子。』御者白王：『阿修罗军后来逐人，若回还者，为彼所困。』帝释告言：『宁当回还为阿修罗杀，不以军众蹈杀众生。』于道，御者转乘南向。阿修罗军遥见帝释转乘而还，谓为战策，即还退走，众大恐怖，坏阵流散，归阿修罗宫。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "That Śakra, Lord of the Devas, is the sovereign king of the Thirty-three Heavens. Because of his compassionate mind, his power subdued the asura army. He also always praised the merits of the compassionate mind. You bhikṣus, having renounced the household life with right faith, should cultivate the compassionate mind and also praise the merits of the compassionate mind."

佛告诸比丘：「彼天帝释于三十三天为自在王，以慈心故，威力摧伏阿修罗军，亦常赞叹慈心功德。汝等比丘正信非家，出家学道，当修慈心，亦应赞叹慈心功德。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully upheld it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
