SĀ 1223

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1223（一二二三） 贫人

Parallel to: SN 11.14 / SĀ² 2.51

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, there was a man in Rājagṛha who was poor and suffering. He took refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṃgha, upheld the precepts, was learned and widely read, practiced generosity, and had right view. When his body perished and his life ended, he was reborn in the heavens, in the Heaven of the Thirty-three. He was superior to the other gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three in three ways. What were the three? First, his heavenly lifespan, second, his heavenly appearance, and third, his heavenly renown. The gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three saw that this god was especially superior in three ways: his heavenly lifespan, heavenly appearance, and heavenly renown.

时，王舍城中有一士夫，贫穷辛苦，而住佛、法、僧，受持禁戒，多闻广学，力行惠施，正见成就。彼身坏命终，得生天上，生三十三天，有三事胜于余三十三天。何等为三？一者天寿，二者天色，三者天名称。诸三十三天见是天子三事特胜，天寿、天色、天名称胜。

The other gods saw this and went to Śakra, Lord of the Gods, saying, "Kauśika should know that a god has just been born here, and in three ways he is superior to the other gods: heavenly lifespan, heavenly appearance, and heavenly renown."

余诸天见已，往诣天帝释所，作如是言：「憍尸迦当知，有一天子始生此天，于先诸天三事特胜，天寿、天色及天名称。」

Then Śakra, Lord of the Gods, said to the god, "Good sirs! I see that this person was a man in Rājagṛha who was poor and suffering. He gained faith in the Tathāgata's Dharma and Vinaya, and attained right view. When his body perished and his life ended, he was reborn in this heaven, and in three ways he is superior to the other gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three: heavenly lifespan, heavenly appearance, and heavenly renown." Then Śakra, Lord of the Gods, spoke a verse:

时，天帝释告彼天子：「诸仁者！我见此人于王舍城作一士夫，贫穷辛苦，于如来法、律得信向心，乃至正见成就。身坏命终，来生此天，于诸三十三天三事特胜，天寿、天色及天名称。」时，天帝释即说偈言：

Right faith in the Tathāgata, definitely unshakable, upholding the true
precepts, the holy precepts without weariness. The mind is pure
towards the Buddha, accomplishing right view, one should know that
it is not poverty and suffering, not living in vain
Therefore, towards the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṃgha, one should give
rise to pure faith, increasing the power of wisdom, contemplating
the Buddha's true teachings.

「正信于如来，　　决定不倾动，受持真实戒，　　圣戒无厌者。于佛心清净，　　成就于正见，当知非贫苦，　　不空而自活。故于佛法僧，　　当生清净信，智慧力增明，　　思念佛正教。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully practiced it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
