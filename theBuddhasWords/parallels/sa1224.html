SĀ 1224

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1224（一二二四） 大祠

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying at Vulture Peak in Rājagṛha. At that time, the people of Rājagṛha held a great assembly and invited various non-Buddhist practitioners.

一时，佛住王舍城耆阇崛山中。尔时，王舍城人普设大会，悉为请种种异道。

There was a Śāṇavāsika non-Buddhist practitioner who thought, "I will now invite the Śāṇavāsika deity to be the first field of merit." There was a non-Buddhist practitioner who had gone forth, a Nirgrantha practitioner, an old disciple, a fire disciple, and a Buddhist disciple monk, who all thought, "Now we should make the Sangha in front of the Buddha the first field of merit."

有事遮罗迦外道者作是念：「我今请遮罗迦道天，先作福田。」或有事外道出家者，有事尼乾子道者，有事老弟子者，有事火弟子者，有事佛弟子僧者，咸作是念：「今当令佛面前僧，先作福田。」

At that time, Śakra, lord of the devas, thought, "Do not let the people of Rājagṛha abandon the Sangha in front of the Buddha and serve other paths, seeking fields of merit. I shall quickly go and establish a field of merit for the people of Rājagṛha." He then transformed himself into a great brāhman, dignified in appearance, riding in a white horse carriage, with a crowd of young brāhman disciples leading the way in front and behind, holding a golden parasol. He went to Rājagṛha and approached the great assemblies in various places.

时，天帝释作是念：「莫令王舍城诸人舍佛面前僧，而奉事余道，求索福田。我当疾往，为王舍城人建立福田。」即化作大婆罗门，仪容严整，乘白马车，诸年少婆罗门众前后导从，持金斗繖盖，至王舍城，诣诸处处大众会中。

All the men and women of Rājagṛha had this thought: "We shall only observe where this great brāhman pays homage. We shall first make offerings to that place, as it will be a good field of merit."

诸王舍城一切士女咸作是念：「但当观望此大婆罗门所奉事处，我当从彼而先供养，为良福田。」

At that time, Śakra, lord of the devas, knew the thoughts in the minds of all the men and women of Rājagṛha. He drove his carriage and led his followers to Vulture Peak Mountain. When he reached outside the gate, he removed his five adornments and went to where the Buddha was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side. He then spoke a verse:

时，天帝释知王舍城一切士女心之所念，驾乘导从，迳诣耆阇崛山，至于门外，除去五饰，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面。而说偈言：

Well able to distinguish and reveal the other shore of all dharmas
able to ferry across all fears, therefore I bow to Gautama. All
the people have set up assemblies, wishing to seek great merit, each
setting up great giving, always wishing for remaining fruits. Please explain the
field of merit for them, causing the fruits of their giving to be accomplished.

「善分别显示，　　一切法彼岸，悉度诸恐怖，　　故稽首瞿昙。诸人普设会，　　欲求大功德，各各设大施，　　常愿有余果。愿为说福田，　　令斯施果成。」

The great Indra, the lord of devas, asked about this on Vulture
Peak Mountain, and the great teacher explained it for him. All the
people have set up assemblies, wishing to seek great merit, each setting
up great giving, always wishing for remaining fruits. Now I shall explain
the field of merit, the place where giving yields great fruits. There
are four who are heading in the right direction, the four noble
ones abiding in the fruits. This is called the field of merit
of the Sangha, with wisdom, practice, and concentration complete. The field of
merit of the Sangha is vast and immeasurable, surpassing the great ocean
The teacher who tames people and his disciples illuminate and reveal the
true Dharma. Those who make offerings to them, the excellent field of
merit of the Sangha, the Buddha says will attain great fruits. Because
the Sangha is free from the five hindrances, pure and worthy of
praise, giving to them, the supreme field, yields great benefits from little
giving. Therefore, all people should give to the field of merit of
the Sangha, increasing to attain the supreme Dharma, with wisdom, practice, and
concentration. Making offerings to this precious Sangha, the benefactors are joyful. Arousing
three kinds of minds, giving clothing and food. Free from the thorns
of defilements, transcending all evil destinies, personally practicing and inviting, giving equally
with one's own hands. Benefiting oneself and also benefiting others, this giving
attains great benefits. The wise give like this, with pure faith and
liberation. Giving without fault and bliss, riding wisdom to be reborn there.

「帝释大自在，　　天王之所问，于耆阇崛山，　　大师为记说。诸人普设会，　　欲求大功德，各各设大施，　　常愿有余果。今当说福田，　　施得大果处。正向者有四，　　四圣住于果，是名僧福田，　　明行定具足。僧福田增广，　　无量逾大海，调人师弟子，　　照明显正法。斯等善供养，　　施僧良福田，于僧良福田，　　佛说得大果。以僧离五盖，　　清净应赞叹，施彼最上田，　　少施收大利。是故诸人者，　　当施僧福田，增得胜妙法，　　明行定相应。供此珍宝僧，　　施主心欢喜，起于三种心，　　施衣服饮食。离尘垢劒刺，　　超度诸恶趣，躬自行启请，　　自手平等与。自利亦利他，　　是施获大利，慧者如是施，　　净信心解脱。无罪安乐施，　　乘智往生彼。」

At that time, Sakra, Lord of the Devas, having heard the Buddha's teaching, was delighted and followed his joy. He bowed to the Buddha and then disappeared.

时，天帝释闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，为佛作礼，即没不现。

At that time, the people of Rājagṛha all rose from their seats, adjusted their robes, bowed to the Buddha, joined their palms, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Please accept our offerings together with the great assembly." At that time, the World-Honored One silently accepted the invitation.

尔时，王舍城诸人民即从座起，整衣服，为佛作礼，合掌白佛言：「世尊！唯愿世尊与诸大众受我供养。」尔时，世尊默然受请。

Knowing that the World-Honored One had silently accepted their invitation, the people of Rājagṛha bowed and returned. They prepared the food and arranged the seats in the assembly hall. In the morning, they sent a messenger to inform the Buddha, "The time has come. Please know the time!"

是王舍城人民知世尊默然受其请已，作礼而归，到诸人会处，具饮食，布置床座，晨朝遣使，白佛：「时到，唯愿知时！」

At that time, the World-Honored One, together with the great assembly, put on his robes, took his alms bowl, went to the assembly hall, and sat on the seat prepared in front of the great assembly.

尔时，世尊与诸大众著衣持钵，至大会所，于大众前敷座而坐。

Knowing that the Buddha was seated, the people of Rājagṛha personally served various kinds of delicious food. After the meal, washing the bowls and rinsing their mouths, they returned to their seats to listen to the Buddha's teaching.

王舍城人知佛坐定，自行种种丰美饮食。食讫，洗钵澡漱毕，还复本座，听佛说法。

At that time, the World-Honored One, for the people of Rājagṛha, spoke various teachings, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting them. Having arisen from his seat, he departed.

尔时，世尊为王舍城人种种说法，示、教、照、喜已，从座起而去。
