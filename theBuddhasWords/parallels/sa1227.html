SĀ 1227

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1227（一二二七） 母

Parallel to: SN 3.22 / SĀ² 2.54 / EĀ 26.7

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time King Prasenajit had a grandmother whom he greatly respected. When she suddenly passed away, he left the city to cremate her and make offerings to her relics. After that, he came to where the Buddha was, wearing tattered clothes and disheveled hair. He bowed his head at the Buddha’s feet and sat to one side.

时，波斯匿王有祖母，极所敬重，忽尔命终。出城阇维，供养舍利毕，弊衣乱发，来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面。

At that time the World-Honored One said to King Prasenajit, "Great King! Where have you come from, wearing tattered clothes and disheveled hair?"

尔时，世尊告波斯匿王：「大王！从何所来，弊衣乱发？」

King Prasenajit said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! My grandmother, whom I greatly respected, passed away and I left the city to cremate her and make offerings to her. After that, I came to where the World-Honored One is."

波斯匿王白佛：「世尊！我亡祖母，极所敬重，舍我命终，出于城外阇维，供养毕，来诣世尊。」

The Buddha said to the great king, "Do you greatly love, respect, and think of your ancestors and relatives?"

佛告大王：「极爱重敬念祖亲耶？」

King Prasenajit said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I greatly respect and love her. World-Honored One! If the elephants, horses, seven treasures, and even the position of king in the country were to be given to someone who could save my grandmother's life, I would give them all. Since I could not save her, I am forever separated from her in life and death. I am so sad and sorrowful that I cannot bear it. I once heard the World-Honored One say: 'All sentient beings, all insects, and all spirits, those who are born will all die, without exception. There is no one who is born and does not die.' Only now do I know that the World-Honored One speaks well."

波斯匿王白佛：「世尊！极敬重爱恋。世尊！若国土所有象马七宝，乃至国位，悉持与人，能救祖母命者，悉当与之。既不能救，生死长辞，悲恋忧苦，不自堪胜！曾闻世尊所说：『一切众生、一切虫、一切神，生者皆死，无不穷尽，无有出生而不死者。』今日乃知世尊善说。」

The Buddha said, "Great King! It is so! It is so! All sentient beings, all insects, and all spirits, those who are born will all die, and eventually come to an end. There is no one who is born and does not die."

佛言：「大王！如是！如是！一切众生、一切虫、一切神，生者辄死，终归穷尽，无有一生而不死者。」

The Buddha told the great king, "Even if it is the great families of Brahmins, Kshatriyas, and householders, those who are born will all die, without exception. Even if it is a Kshatriya king who is enthroned and rules the four continents, having power and freedom, and subduing all enemy countries, he will eventually come to an end, without exception. If, great king, one is born in the long-life heavens and rules the heavenly palace, with freedom and happiness, it will also come to an end, without exception. If, great king, an Arhat bhikṣu has exhausted all taints, is free from all burdens, has done what was to be done, has attained his own benefit, has exhausted all bonds, is well liberated in mind with right wisdom, he will also come to an end, abandon the body, and enter parinirvāṇa. If, great king, a Pratyekabuddha is well-tamed, well-pacified, and exhausts this body and life, he will also enter parinirvāṇa. The Buddhas of the world, endowed with the ten powers, the four fearlessnesses, and the lion's roar of victory, will also abandon the body and attain parinirvāṇa. By this comparison, great king, you should know that all sentient beings, all insects, and all spirits, those who are born will all die, and eventually come to an end, without exception." At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

佛告大王：「正使婆罗门大姓、刹利大姓、长者大姓，生者皆死，无不死者。正使刹利大王灌顶居位，王四天下，得力自在，于诸敌国无不降伏，终归有极，无不死者。若复，大王！生长寿天，王于天宫，自在快乐，终亦归尽，无不死者。若复，大王！罗汉比丘诸漏已尽，离诸重担，所作已作，逮得己利，尽诸有结，正智心善解脱，彼亦归尽，舍身涅槃。若复缘觉善调善寂，尽此身命，终归涅槃，诸佛世尊十力具足，四无所畏，胜师子吼，终亦舍身，取般涅槃。以如是比，大王当知，一切众生、一切虫、一切神，有生辄死，终归磨灭，无不死者！」尔时，世尊复说偈言：

All sentient beings, with life, will eventually die. Each follows the path
of their karma, experiencing the fruits of good and evil. Due to
evil karma, one falls into hell; due to goodness, one ascends to
heaven. Cultivating the supreme and wondrous path, one exhausts the taints and
attains parinirvāṇa. The Tathāgatas, Pratyekabuddhas, and the disciples of the Buddha will
also abandon their bodies and lives. How much more so for ordinary worldly people.

「一切众生类，　　有命终归死，各随业所趣，　　善恶果自受。恶业堕地狱，　　为善上升天，修习胜妙道，　　漏尽般涅槃。如来及缘觉，　　佛声闻弟子，会当舍身命，　　何况俗凡夫。」

Having spoken this sūtra, King Prasenajit, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and paid homage before departing.

佛说此经已，波斯匿王闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
