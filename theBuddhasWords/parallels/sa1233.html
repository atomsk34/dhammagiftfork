SĀ 1233

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1233（一二三三） 命终

Parallel to: SN 3.20 / SĀ² 2.60

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, in Śrāvastī there was an elder named Mahānāma who had passed away without any sons or heirs. The wealth of the king Prasenajit, which had no sons or relatives, all entered the royal household. The king Prasenajit, who was daily inspecting the wealth and covered in dust, came to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side.

尔时，舍卫国有长者，名摩诃男，命终无有儿息。波斯匿王以无子、无亲属之财，悉入王家。波斯匿王日日挍阅财物，身蒙尘土，来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面。

At that time, the World-Honored One said to King Prasenajit, "Great King! Where have you come from? Your body is covered in dust, as if you are tired."

尔时，世尊告波斯匿王：「大王！从何所来？身蒙尘土，似有疲惓。」

King Prasenajit said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! The elder Mahānāma of this country has passed away without any sons. All his wealth without sons or relatives has entered the royal household. Supervising and managing it has caused fatigue, and dust has covered my body. I have come from his house."

波斯匿王白佛：「世尊！此国长者摩诃男命终，有无子之财，悉入王家。瞻视料理，致令疲劳，尘土坌身，从其舍来。」

The Buddha asked King Prasenajit, "Was the elder Mahānāma very wealthy and had many possessions?"

佛问波斯匿王：「彼摩诃男长者大富多财耶？」

King Prasenajit said to the Buddha, "Very wealthy, World-Honored One! He had many hundreds of thousands of gold coins and treasures, not to mention other wealth. World-Honored One! When Mahānāma was alive, he wore coarse clothes and ate poor food..." and so on, as explained above.

波斯匿王白佛：「大富，世尊！钱财甚多，百千巨亿金钱宝物，况复余财！世尊！彼摩诃男在世之时，麤衣恶食……」如上广说。

The Buddha told King Prasenajit, "In the past, Mahānāma encountered the pratyekabuddha Takkarakassapa and offered him a meal. He did not offer it with pure faith, nor did he offer it respectfully or with his own hands. After offering it, he regretted it, saying, 'This meal could have been used to feed my servants. It is unjust to give it to a śramaṇa!' Because of this merit from giving, he was reborn in the Heaven of the Thirty-three times seven times, and seven times he was reborn in the most distinguished and wealthy families in Śrāvastī. Because he offered to the pratyekabuddha with impure faith, did not offer with his own hands, did not offer respectfully, and regretted it after offering, wherever he was reborn, although he obtained wealth, he still had to use coarse clothes, coarse food, coarse bedding, houses, and vehicles. He never experienced the best colors, sounds, smells, tastes, and textures to comfort his body.

佛告波斯匿王：「彼摩诃男过去世时，遇多迦罗尸弃辟支佛，施一饭食，非净信心，不恭敬与，不自手与，施后变悔，言：『此饭食自可供给我诸仆使，无辜持用，施于沙门！』由是施福，七反往生三十三天，七反生此舍卫国中最胜族姓，最富钱财。以彼施辟支佛时，不净信心，不手自与，不恭敬与，施后随悔故，在所生处，虽得财富，犹故受用麤衣、麤食、麤弊卧具、屋舍、车乘，初不尝得上妙色、声、香、味、触，以自安身。

"Furthermore, Your Majesty! At that time, the elder Mahānāma killed his half-brother and took his wealth. Because of this crime, he fell into hell for hundreds of thousands of years. The remaining karmic retribution caused him to be reborn in Śrāvastī seven times, always without children, and his wealth was confiscated by the king. Your Majesty! The elder Mahānāma has now ended his life. The past merit from giving is exhausted. In this life, because of his stinginess and carelessness with wealth, he committed offenses. After ending this life, he will fall into hell and experience extreme suffering!"

「复次，大王！时，彼摩诃男长者杀其异母兄，取其财物，缘斯罪故，经百千岁，堕地狱中，彼余罪报生舍卫国，七反受身，常以无子，财没入王家。大王！摩诃男长者今此寿终，过去施报尽，于此身，以彼悭贪，于财放逸，因造过恶，于此命终已，堕地狱受极苦恼！」

King Prasenajit asked the Buddha, "World-Honored One! After the elder Mahānāma's life ends, will he enter hell and experience suffering?"

波斯匿王白佛言：「世尊！摩诃男长者命终已，入地狱受苦痛耶？」

The Buddha said, "Yes, Your Majesty! He has already entered hell!"

佛言：「如是，大王！已入地狱！」

At that time, King Prasenajit, thinking of his grief and tears, wiped his tears with his robe and spoke a verse:

时，波斯匿王念彼悲泣，以衣拭泪，而说偈言：

Wealth, real gold and jewels, elephants, horses, and ornaments, slaves
servants, and all kinds of fields and houses - all
are abandoned. Naked, the spirit wanders alone. The number of
blessings is exhausted, forever leaving the human body. What does
he have now? What does he hold to go? What
does he not abandon, like a shadow following a form?

「财物真金宝，　　象马庄严具，奴仆诸僮使，　　及诸田宅等。一切皆遗弃，　　裸神独游往，福运数已穷，　　永舍于人身。彼今何所有，　　何所持而去，于何事不舍，　　如影之随形。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse in reply:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

Only the karma of sin and merit, if a person has already
done it, is then his own, and he always holds it to
go. Birth and death have never been abandoned, like a shadow following
a form. It is like a person with little provisions, traveling far
and encountering hardships. Those who do not cultivate merit will definitely experience
the suffering of the evil paths. It is like a person with
abundant provisions, traveling far with ease. Those who cultivate merit and are
sincere will enjoy long-lasting happiness in the good realms. It is like
a person traveling far, returning home after many years in peace. Relatives
and good friends gather joyfully in a meeting. Those who cultivate merit
well will be reborn in other worlds after death. Those relatives and
family members will be delighted to see them. Therefore, one should cultivate
merit, accumulating it for a long time. Merit can establish happiness in
other worlds for people. Merit is praised by the gods, because they
practice correctly. In this life, people do not slander them, and in
the end, they are reborn in heaven.

「唯有罪福业，　　若人已作者，是则己之有，　　彼则常持去。生死未曾舍，　　如影之随形，如人少资粮，　　涉远遭苦难。不修功德者，　　必经恶道苦，如人丰资粮，　　安乐以远游。修德淳厚者，　　善趣长受乐，如人远游行，　　岁久安隐归。宗亲善知识，　　欢乐欣集会，善修功德者，　　此没生他世。彼诸亲眷属，　　见则心欢喜，是故当修福，　　积集期永久。福德能为人，　　建立他世乐，福德天所叹，　　等修正行故。现世人不毁，　　终则生天上。」

Having spoken this sutra, King Prasenajit, having heard the Buddha's words, was delighted and followed the teachings. He bowed and departed.

佛说此经已，波斯匿王闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
