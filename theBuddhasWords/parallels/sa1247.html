SĀ 1247

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1247（一二四七） 铸金者

Parallel to: AN 3.102

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "You should wholeheartedly apply skillful means and contemplate the three characteristics at the right time. What are the three? Contemplate the characteristic of stopping at the right time, contemplate the characteristic of lifting at the right time, and contemplate the characteristic of letting go at the right time. If a bhikṣu exclusively contemplates the characteristic of stopping, then in that case his mind will be inferior. If he exclusively contemplates the characteristic of lifting, then in that case a distracted mind will arise. If he exclusively contemplates the characteristic of letting go, then in that case he will not attain right concentration and exhaust all afflictions.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「应当专心方便，随时思惟三相。云何为三？随时思惟止相，随时思惟举相，随时思惟舍相。若比丘一向思惟止相，则于是处其心下劣。若复一向思惟举相，则于是处掉乱心起，若复一向思惟舍相，则于是处不得正定，尽诸有漏。

"Because that bhikṣu contemplates the characteristic of stopping at the right time, contemplates the characteristic of lifting at the right time, and contemplates the characteristic of letting go at the right time, his mind is then rightly concentrated and exhausts all afflictions. It is like a skilled goldsmith or goldsmith's apprentice who puts raw gold into a furnace and adds fire, fanning it at the right time, sprinkling water on it at the right time, and leaving it alone at the right time. If he exclusively fans it, then in that case the raw gold will be burned out. If he exclusively sprinkles water on it, then in that case the raw gold will be hardened. If he exclusively leaves it alone, then in that case the raw gold will not be cooked and will be useless. Therefore, the skilled goldsmith or goldsmith's apprentice fans the raw gold at the right time, sprinkles water on it at the right time, and leaves it alone at the right time. In this way, the raw gold is evenly tempered and can be used as needed. In the same way, bhikṣu! With a concentrated mind, skillfully contemplate and remember the three characteristics from time to time, until the afflictions are exhausted."

「以彼比丘随时思惟止相，随时思惟举相，随时思惟舍相故，心则正定，尽诸有漏。如巧金师、金师弟子以生金著于炉中增火，随时扇𫖔，随时水洒，随时俱舍。若一向鼓𫖔者，即于是处生金焦尽。一向水洒，则于是处，生金坚强。若一向俱舍，则于是处生金不熟，则无所用。是故，巧金师、金师弟子于彼生金随时鼓𫖔，随时水洒，随时两舍。如是生金，得等调适，随事所用。如是，比丘！专心方便，时时思惟，忆念三相，乃至漏尽。」

After the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus who heard what the Buddha had said rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说是经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
