SĀ 1248

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1248（一二四八） 牧牛者

Parallel to: MN 34 / EĀ 43.6

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "In the past, in the country of Magadha, there was a cowherd who was foolish and ignorant. At the end of summer and beginning of autumn, he did not observe the near shore of the Ganges River well, nor did he observe the far shore well. He drove his herd of cows down the steep bank and up the steep bank, causing many difficulties in the whirlpools in between. Bhikṣus! In the past, in the country of Magadha, there was a cowherd who was not foolish or ignorant. He had skillful means and wisdom. At the end of summer and beginning of autumn, he was able to observe the near shore of the Ganges River well, and also observe the far shore well. He skillfully ferried his cows to a place with level plains, mountains, valleys, good water, and grass. When he first ferried them across, he first ferried the large cows that could lead the herd, cutting off the swift current. Next he drove the second group of strong young cows to follow and cross, and then the third group of weak and small ones to follow and go downstream, all of them safely crossing in order. The newborn calves, out of affection for their mothers, also followed behind and reached the far shore.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「过去世时，摩竭提国有牧牛者，愚痴无慧，夏末秋初，不善观察恒水此岸，亦不善观恒水彼岸，而駈群牛峻岸而下，峻岸而上，中间洄澓，多起患难。诸比丘！过去世时，摩竭提国有牧牛人，不愚不痴者，有方便慧，夏末秋初，能善观察恒水此岸，亦善观察恒水彼岸，善度其牛，至平博山谷好水草处。彼初度时，先度大牛能领群者，断其急流。次駈第二多力少牛，随后而度，然后第三駈羸小者，随逐下流，悉皆次第安隐得度。新生犊子爱恋其母，亦随其后，得度彼岸。

Thus, bhikṣus! I have given this analogy. You should understand its meaning. The cowherd in Magadha was foolish and ignorant. The six teachers, Pūraṇa and the others, are also like this. They practice wrong views and go towards the wrong path. Like that foolish and ignorant cowherd, at the end of summer and beginning of autumn, he did not observe the near shore and far shore well, the steep mountains and cliffs, going down the steep bank and up the steep bank, causing many difficulties in the whirlpools in between. Similarly, the six teachers, Pūraṇa and the others, are foolish and ignorant. They do not observe the near shore, which refers to this world; they do not observe the far shore, which refers to the other world; the whirlpools in between refer to the realms of the māras. They themselves encounter suffering and difficulties. Those who hold views and practice what they have learned also encounter difficulties.

「如是，比丘！我说斯譬，当知其义。彼摩竭提牧牛者，愚痴无慧；彼诸六师富兰那等亦复如是，习诸邪见，向于邪道。如彼牧牛人愚痴无慧，夏末秋初，不善观察此岸彼岸，高峻山崄，从峻岸下，峻岸而上，中间洄澓，多生患难。如是六师富兰那等愚痴无慧，不观此岸，谓于此世；不观彼岸，谓于他世；中间洄澓，谓境诸魔，自遭苦难。彼诸见者，习其所学，亦遭患难。

The good cowherd in Magadha who was not foolish or ignorant, who had skillful means and wisdom, refers to the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One. Like the cowherd who observes the near shore well and observes the far shore well, and skillfully ferries his cows to a place with level plains, mountains, valleys, good water, and grass, first ferrying the large cows that can lead the herd, cutting off the swift current and safely ferrying them to the far shore. Similarly, my śrāvakas who can exhaust all taints, and know for themselves that they will not undergo future existence, cut off the worldly current of the evil māra and safely ferry across to the far shore of birth and death.

「彼摩竭提善牧牛者，不愚不痴，有方便慧，谓如来、应、等正觉。如牧牛者善观此岸，善观彼岸，善度其牛，于平博山谷，先度大牛能领群者，横截急流，安度彼岸。如是我声闻能尽诸漏，乃至自知不受后有。横截恶魔世间贪流，安隐得度生死彼岸。

Like the cowherd in Magadha who next ferries the second large cow with great strength, cutting across the current and ferrying it across. Similarly, my śrāvakas who have severed the five lower fetters, attain the state of anāgāmin, and are reborn there, not returning to this world, also cut off the current of the evil māra's greed and safely ferry across to the far shore of birth and death.

「如摩竭提国善牧牛者，次度第二多力少牛，截流横度。如是我诸声闻断五下分结，得阿那含，于彼受生，不还此世，亦复断截恶魔贪流，安隐得度生死彼岸。

Like the cowherd in Magadha who drives the third small cow with little strength, following the lower current and safely ferrying it across. Similarly, my śrāvakas who have severed the three fetters, and whose greed, anger, and delusion are weakened, attain the state of sakṛdāgāmin, and come to this world once, ultimately ending suffering, cutting off the current of the evil māra's greed, and safely ferry across to the far shore of birth and death.

「如摩竭提国善牧牛者，駈其第三羸小少牛，随其下流，安隐得度。如是我声闻断三结，贪、恚、痴薄，得斯陀含，一来此世，究竟苦边，横截于彼恶魔贪流，安隐得度生死彼岸。

Like the cowherd in Magadha who ferries the newborn calf that loves its mother, also safely ferrying it across. Similarly, my śrāvakas who have severed the three fetters, attain the state of srota-āpanna, do not fall into evil destinies, are definitely headed for supreme perfect enlightenment, are reborn seven times among gods and humans, and ultimately end suffering, cutting off the current of the evil māra's greed, and safely ferry across to the far shore of birth and death.

「如摩竭提国善牧牛者，新生犊子爱恋其母，亦随得度。如是我声闻断三结，得须陀洹，不堕恶趣，决定正向三菩提，七有天人往生，究竟苦边，断截恶魔贪流，安隐得度生死彼岸。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

In this world and the other world, the wise and intelligent
are clearly revealed. The māras, whether attained or not attained, even
up to the māra of death, all know them. The wisdom
of the Tathāgata cuts off the flow of māras and destroys
them, opening the gate of immortality and revealing the true path
The mind is always greatly joyful and attains a place of
peace and tranquility.

「此世及他世，　　明智善显现，诸魔得未得，　　乃至于死魔。一切悉知者，　　三藐三佛智，断截诸魔流，　　破坏令消亡。开示甘露门，　　显现正真道，心常多欣悦，　　逮得安隐处。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished. The bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
