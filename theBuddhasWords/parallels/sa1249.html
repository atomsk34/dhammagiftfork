SĀ 1249

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1249（一二四九） 牧牛者

Parallel to: MN 33 / AN 11.17 (~) / EĀ 49.1

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "If a cowherd possesses eleven qualities, he will not be able to increase the herd, nor will he be able to protect the large herd and bring them peace and happiness. What are the eleven? They are: not knowing the color, not knowing the characteristics, not removing parasites, not being able to cover their wounds, not being able to make smoke, not knowing how to choose the path, not knowing how to choose the place, not knowing how to cross the place, not knowing where to graze, milking them dry, and not being skilled in managing the herd. These are called the eleven qualities that, when possessed, cannot protect the large herd.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「若牧牛人成就十一法者，不能令牛增长，亦不能拥护大群牛，令等安乐。何等为十一？谓不知色、不知相、不去虫、不能覆护其疮、不能起烟、不知择路、不知择处、不知度处、不知食处、尽［（壳-一）／牛］其乳、不善料理能领群者，是名十一法成就，不能党护大群牛。

In the same way, bhikṣus who possess eleven qualities cannot bring peace to themselves or others. What are the eleven? They are: not knowing the color, not knowing the characteristics, not being able to remove harmful parasites, not covering their wounds, not being able to make smoke, not knowing the right path, not knowing where to stop, not knowing where to cross, not knowing where to graze, milking them dry, and if there are elders who are learned, of long standing in the practice of the holy life, praised by the great teachers, they do not praise their virtues to those who are wise and have practiced the holy life, causing them to respect, serve, and make offerings to them.

「如是，比丘成就十一法者，不能自安，亦不安他。何等为十一？谓不知色、不知相、不能除其害虫、不覆其疮、不能起烟、不知正路、不知止处、不知度处、不知食处、尽［（壳-一）／牛］其乳、若有上座多闻耆旧，久修梵行，大师所叹，不向诸明智修梵行者称誉其德，悉令宗敬、奉事、供养。

What is meant by 'not knowing the color'? All forms, whether the four great elements or what is formed from the four great elements, are called 'color'. Not knowing this is as it truly is.

「云何名不知色？诸所有色，彼一切四大，及四大造，是名为色不如实知。

What is meant by 'not knowing the characteristics'? The characteristics of actions are faults, and the characteristics of actions are wisdom. Not knowing this as it truly is is called 'not knowing the characteristics'.

「云何不知相？事业是过相，事业是慧相，是不如实知，是名不知相。

What is meant by 'not knowing the going worm'? The arising of sensual desire, anger, and harmfulness, which are not abandoned, not realized, and not extinguished, is called 'the going worm'.

「云何名不知去虫？所起欲觉能安，不离、不觉、不灭，所起瞋恚、害觉能安，不离，不觉、不灭，是名不去虫。

What is meant by 'not covering the wound'? It means that when the eyes see forms, they grasp their characteristics, and do not guard the eye faculty. Worldly greed, sorrow, evil, and unwholesome dharmas arise in the mind, and one is unable to guard against them. The same is true for the faculties of the ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind. This is called 'not covering the wound'.

「云何不覆疮？谓眼见色，随取形相，不守眼根，世间贪忧，恶不善法，心随生漏，不能防护；耳、鼻、舌、身、意根亦复如是。是名不覆其疮。

What is meant by 'not producing smoke'? It means that one is unable to explain and reveal the Dharma one has heard and received to others. This is called 'not producing smoke'.

「云何不起烟？如所闻，如所受法，不能为人分别显示，是名不起烟。

What is meant by 'not knowing the right path'? The eightfold right path and the holy Dharma and Vinaya are called the path. Not knowing them as they truly are is called 'not knowing the path'.

「云何不知正道？八正道及圣法、律是名为道，彼不如实知，是名不知道。

What is meant by 'not knowing the place of rest'? It means not finding joy, delight, excellence, liberation, and benefit in the Dharma known by the Tathāgata. This is called 'not knowing the place of rest'.

「云何不知止处？谓于如来所知法，不得欢喜、悦乐、胜妙、出离、饶益，是名不知止处。

What is meant by 'not knowing the place of crossing'? It means not knowing the Sūtra, Vinaya, and Abhidharma, and not going to them at the right time to ask and receive: 'What is good? What is bad? What is sinful? What is sinless? What kind of practice is superior and not evil?' Not being able to reveal hidden teachings, not being able to ask extensively about revealed teachings, and not being able to extensively proclaim and reveal what one knows of the profound meaning, this is called 'not knowing the place of crossing'.

「云何不知度处？谓彼不知修多罗、毘尼、阿毘昙，不随时往到其所，咨问请受：『云何为善？云何不善？云何有罪？云何无罪？作何等法为胜非恶？』于隐密法不能开发，于显露法不能广问，于甚深句义自所知者，不能广宣显示，是名不知度处。

What is meant by 'not knowing the place of grazing'? The four foundations of mindfulness and the noble Dharma and Vinaya are called the place of grazing. Not knowing them as they truly are is called 'not knowing the place of grazing'.

「云何不知放牧处？谓四念处及贤圣法、律是名放牧处，于此不如实知，是名不知放牧处。

What is meant by 'milking the cow dry'? When kṣatriyas, brahmins, and householders freely give clothing, food and drink, bedding, medicine, and necessities, if the bhikṣu who receives them does not know moderation, this is called 'milking the cow dry'.

「云何为尽［（壳-一）／牛］其乳？彼刹利、婆罗门长者自在施与衣被、饮食、床卧、医药、资生众具，彼比丘受者不知限量，是名尽［（壳-一）／牛］其乳。

What is meant by 'not causing the elders, the learned, and the wise to be respected, honored, and served by the wise and virtuous'? It means that the bhikṣu does not praise those elders, the learned, and the wise, and does not cause the wise and virtuous to approach them, to serve them with body, speech, and mind, and to respect them. This is called 'not causing the elders, the learned, and the wise to be respected, honored, and served by the wise and virtuous'.

「云何为上座大德多闻耆旧，乃至不向诸胜智梵行者所称其功德，令其宗重承事供养，令得悦乐？谓比丘不称彼上座，乃至令诸智慧梵行者往诣其所，以随顺身、口、意业承望奉事，是名不于上座多闻耆旧，乃至令智慧梵行往诣其所，承望奉事，令得悦乐。

"The cowherd who possesses these eleven qualities is able to make the herd of cows grow, protect them, and bring them joy. What are the eleven? They are knowing the color, knowing the characteristics, as explained in the section on purity, up to being able to lead the herd, and taking care of them at the right time to bring them peace and happiness. These are called the eleven qualities of the cowherd that enable the herd of cows to grow, be protected, and find peace and happiness. In the same way, a bhikṣu who possesses eleven qualities is able to bring peace and happiness to himself and others. What are the eleven? They are knowing the color, knowing the characteristics, up to the eleventh..." as explained in detail in the section on purity, "These are called the eleven qualities of the bhikṣu that enable him to bring peace and happiness to himself and others."

「彼牧牛者成就十一法，堪能令彼群牛增长，拥护群牛，令其悦乐。何等为十一？谓知色、知相，如上清净分说，乃至能领群者，随时料理，令得安乐，是名牧牛者十一事成就，能令群牛增长拥护，令得安乐。如是，比丘成就十一法者，能自安乐，亦能安他。何等十一？谓知色、知相乃至十一……」如清净分广说，「是名比丘十一事成就，自安安他。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished what he had to say. The bhikṣus, having heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
