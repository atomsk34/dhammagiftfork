SĀ 1250

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1250（一二五〇） 那提迦

Parallel to: AN 5.30 (~) / AN 8.86

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling among the people of Kosala and arrived at a village called Śāṇakavāsa, where he stayed in a forest called Śāṇakavāsa.

一时，佛在拘萨罗人间游行，至一奢能伽罗聚落，住一奢能伽罗林中。

At that time, there was an elder named Nāṭakavāsi who had been living in the village of Śāṇakavāsa for a long time. The śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas of the village of Śāṇakavāsa heard that the śramaṇa Gautama was traveling among the people of Kosala and had arrived at the village of Śāṇakavāsa, where he was staying in the Śāṇakavāsa forest. Having heard this, they each prepared a pot of food and placed it by the door, thinking, "I will offer it to the World-Honored One first! I will offer it to the Well-Gone One first!" They each shouted loudly, making such proclamations.

时，有尊者那提迦，旧住一奢能伽罗聚落。一奢能伽罗聚落沙门、婆罗门闻沙门瞿昙拘萨罗国人间游行，至一奢能伽罗聚落，住一奢能伽罗林中。闻已，各办一釜食，著门边，作是念：「我先供养世尊！我先供养善逝！」各各高声大声，作如是唱。

At that time, the World-Honored One heard the loud voices of many people in the garden and forest. He said to Elder Nāṭakavāsi, "For what reason and on what occasion are there the loud voices of many people in the garden and forest, proclaiming such things?"

尔时，世尊闻园林内有多人众高声大声，语尊者那提伽：「何因、何缘园林内有众多人高声大声唱说之声？」

Elder Nāṭakavāsi said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! The kṣatriyas, brāhmaṇas, and elders of this village of Śāṇakavāsa heard that the World-Honored One was staying in this forest and each prepared a pot of food, placing it in the garden and forest. They each proclaimed, 'I will offer it to the World-Honored One first! I will offer it to the Well-Gone One first!' For this reason, there are the loud voices of many people in this forest, proclaiming such things. May the World-Honored One please accept their food."

尊者那提伽白佛言：「世尊！此一奢能伽罗聚落诸刹利、婆罗门长者闻世尊住此林中，各作一釜食置园林内，各自唱言：『我先供养世尊！我先供养善逝！』以是故，于此林中多人高声大声唱说之声。唯愿世尊当受彼食。」

The Buddha told Nāṭakavāsi, "Do not benefit me, for I do not seek benefit; do not praise me, for I do not seek praise. Nāṭakavāsi! If one can attain the joy of liberation, detachment, tranquility, and perfect enlightenment through the Tathāgata in this way, then would one delight in and seek the benefits and happiness arising from that? Nāṭakavāsi! I alone attain the joy of liberation, detachment, tranquility, and perfect enlightenment in this way, without seeking and without suffering. Why would I delight in and seek the benefits and happiness arising from that? Nāṭakavāsi! You do not attain the joy of liberation, detachment, tranquility, and perfect enlightenment in this way, so you do not attain the joy of not seeking and not suffering. Nāṭakavāsi! The gods also do not attain the joy of liberation, detachment, tranquility, and perfect enlightenment in this way, the joy of not seeking and not suffering. Only I attain the joy of liberation, detachment, tranquility, and perfect enlightenment in this way, the joy of not seeking and not suffering. Why would I delight in and seek the benefits and happiness arising from that?"

佛告那提伽：「莫以利我，我不求利；莫以称我，我不求称。那提伽！若于如来如是便得出要、远离、寂灭、等正觉乐者，则于彼彼所起利乐，若味若求？那提伽！唯我于此像类得出要、远离、寂灭、等正觉乐，不求而得，不苦而得，于何彼彼所起利乐，若味若求？那提伽！汝等于如是像类色不得出要、远离、寂灭、等正觉乐故，不得不求之乐、不苦之乐。那提迦！天亦不得如是像类出要、远离、寂灭、等正觉乐、不求之乐、不苦之乐。唯有我得如是像类出要、远离、寂灭、等正觉乐、不求之乐、不苦之乐。于何彼彼所起利乐，若味若求？」

Nāṭakavāsi said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I now wish to speak a parable."

那提伽白佛言：「世尊！我今欲说譬。」

The Buddha told Nāṭakavāsi, "The time is right."

佛告那提伽：「宜知是时。」

Nāṭakavāsi said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! It is like when it rains, the water flows downward. Wherever the World-Honored One resides, there the kṣatriyas, brāhmaṇas, and householders revere and serve him with faith and respect. Because the World-Honored One's precepts are pure and his right view is true and straight. Therefore, I now make this request. May the World-Honored One kindly accept their invitation."

那提迦白佛言：「世尊！譬如天雨，水流顺下，随其彼彼世尊住处，于彼彼处刹利、婆罗门长者信敬奉事，以世尊戒德清净，正见真直。是故，我今作如是说，唯愿世尊哀受彼请。」

The Buddha told Nāṭakavāsi, "Do not benefit me, for I do not seek benefit. How can there be any taste or seeking in benefiting others? Nāṭakavāsi! I see that when bhikṣus eat good food, they lie on their backs, panting and sighing. Seeing this, I think, 'This elder cannot attain the joy of liberation, detachment, tranquility, perfect enlightenment, the joy of non-seeking, and the joy of non-suffering.' Furthermore, Nāṭakavāsi! I see that when two bhikṣus eat good food, they become full and pant, walking with their arms outstretched. I think, 'Those elders cannot attain the joy of liberation, detachment, tranquility, perfect enlightenment, the joy of non-seeking, and the joy of non-suffering.'

佛告那提伽：「莫以利我，我不求利。乃至云何于彼彼所起利乐，有味有求？那提迦！我见比丘食好食已，仰腹而卧，急喘长息。我见已，作是思惟：『如此长老不得出要、远离、寂灭、等正觉乐、不求之乐、不苦之乐。』复次，那提迦！我见此有二比丘食好食已，饱腹喘息，偃阐而行。我作是念：『非彼长老能得出要、远离、寂灭、等正觉之乐、不求之乐、不苦之乐。』

Nāṭakavāsi! I see that many bhikṣus, after eating good food, go from garden to garden, from room to room, from person to person, from group to group. Seeing this, I think, 'Those elders cannot attain the joy of liberation, detachment, tranquility, perfect enlightenment, the joy of non-seeking, and the joy of non-suffering; I have attained such liberation, detachment, tranquility, perfect enlightenment, the joy of non-seeking, and the joy of non-suffering.'

「那提迦！我见众多比丘食好食已，从园至园，从房至房，从人至人，从群聚至群聚。我见是已，而作是念：『非彼长老如是能得出要、远离、寂灭、等正觉乐、不求之乐、不苦之乐；我得如是像类出要、远离、寂灭、等正觉乐、不求之乐、不苦之乐。』

"Furthermore, Nāṭakavāsi! Once, while traveling along the road, I saw a bhikṣu walking far ahead, and another bhikṣu walking far behind. At that time, I was alone, without any activity, and without the trouble of defecation and urination. Why is that? Because of relying on food and drink, and delighting in flavors, there is defecation and urination, which is relying. Observing the arising and ceasing of the five aggregates of clinging, and dwelling in disgust and detachment, this is relying. Observing the arising and ceasing of the six sense bases, and dwelling in disgust and detachment, this is relying. Delight in the pleasure of the group, and diligently practicing in the group, is disgust and detachment from solitude, which is relying. Delight in practicing solitude, and diligently practicing solitude, is disgust and detachment from the group, which is relying. Therefore, Nāṭakavāsi! You should train like this: 'Observe the arising and ceasing of the five aggregates of clinging, observe the arising and ceasing of the six sense bases, delight in solitude, and diligently practice solitude.' You should train like this."

「复次，那提迦！我于一时随道行，见有比丘于前远去，复有比丘于后来亦远。我于尔时，闲静无为，亦无有便利之劳。所以者何？依于食饮，乐著滋味，故有便利，此则为依。观五受阴生灭，而厌离住，此则为依。于六触入处观察集灭，厌离而住，此则为依。于群聚之乐勤习群聚，厌于远离，是则为依。乐修远离，则勤于远离，厌离群聚，是则为依。是故，那提迦！当如是学：『于五受阴观察生灭，于六触入处观察集灭，乐于远离，精勤远离。』当如是学。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha said to Venerable Nāṭakavāsi, "Having heard the Buddha's words, Venerable Nāṭakavāsi rejoiced and paid homage before departing.

佛说此经已，尊者那提迦闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
