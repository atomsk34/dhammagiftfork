SĀ 1251

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1251（一二五一） 那提迦

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling among the people of Kosala and arrived at the village of Nalenggara……as above, in detail, up to their various pursuits of profit.

一时，佛在拘萨罗人间游行，至那楞伽罗聚落……如上广说，乃至彼彼所起求利。

The Buddha told Natika: "I saw a monastery near a village, with a bhikkhu sitting in meditation. Having seen this, I thought: 'Now this venerable one, a villager or a novice, comes and goes, making noise and disturbance, obstructing his meditation, disturbing his concentration, preventing him from reaching what he has not yet reached, obtaining what he has not yet obtained, and realizing what he has not yet realized.' Natika! I do not like that bhikkhu staying in the monastery near the village.

佛告那提迦：「我见聚落边有精舍，有比丘坐禅。我见已，作如是念：『今此尊者聚落人，此或沙弥，来往声响作乱，障其禅思，觉其正受，于不到欲到、不获欲获、不证欲证而作留难。』那提迦！我不喜彼比丘住聚落精舍。

Natika! I saw a bhikkhu staying in a secluded place, lying on his back and sighing. Having seen this, I thought: 'May that bhikkhu awaken from sleep and contemplate the thought of seclusion.' Natika! I also do not like such a bhikkhu staying in seclusion.

「那提迦！我见比丘住空闲处，仰卧吁咄。我见是已，而作是念：『令彼比丘觉寤睡眠，思空闲想。』那提迦！我亦不喜如是比丘住空闲处。

Natika! I also saw a bhikkhu staying in a secluded place, sitting and dozing with a swaying body. Having seen this, I thought: 'Now this bhikkhu, when waking from sleep, will not attain concentration. Only one with a concentrated mind can attain liberation.' Therefore, Natika! I do not like such a bhikkhu staying in seclusion.

「那提迦！我复见比丘住空闲处，摇身坐睡。见已，作是念：『今此比丘于睡觉寤，不定得定，定心者得解脱。』是故，那提迦！我不喜如是比丘住空闲处。

Natika! I also saw a bhikkhu staying in a secluded place, sitting upright in meditative absorption. Having seen this, I thought: 'May this bhikkhu, if not yet liberated, quickly attain liberation; and if already liberated, may he guard himself well so as not to regress.' Natika! I like such a bhikkhu staying in seclusion.

「那提迦！我复见比丘住空闲处，端坐正受。我见已，作是念：『令此比丘不解脱者，疾得解脱；已解脱者，令自防护，使不退失。』那提迦！我喜如是比丘住空闲处。

Natika! I also saw a bhikkhu staying in a secluded place, who later abandoned the secluded place, gathered up his bed and bedding, and returned to the village to receive a bed and bedding. Natika! I also do not like such a bhikkhu returning to the village.

「那提迦！我复见比丘住空闲处，彼于后时，远离空处集舍床卧具，还入聚落受床卧具。那提迦！我亦不喜如是比丘还入聚落。

Furthermore, Natika! I saw a bhikkhu staying in a village monastery, with a great reputation and able to attract material benefits, robes, food and drink, medicine, and requisites. Later, he abandoned the material benefits and village beds and seats, and went to a secluded place to rest on a bed and bedding. Natika! I like such a bhikkhu abandoning the material benefits and village beds and seats, and staying in seclusion. Natika! A bhikkhu should train in this way.

「复次，那提迦！我见比丘住聚落精舍，名闻大德能感财利、衣被、饮食、汤药、众具。彼于后时，集舍利养聚落床座，至于空闲，床卧安止。那提迦！我喜如是比丘集舍利养聚落床卧，住于空闲。那提迦！比丘当如是学。」

After the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, the bhikkhu Natika was delighted and joyful, paid homage, and departed.

佛说此经已，那提迦比丘欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
