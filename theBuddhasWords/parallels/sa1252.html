SĀ 1252

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1252（一二五二） 枕木

Parallel to: SN 20.8

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying at the Monkey Pond in the city of Vesālī, in a two-storied lecture hall.

一时，佛住鞞舍离国猕猴池侧重阁讲堂。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "The Licchavis always sleep on wooden pillows, their hands and feet are cracked and rough, and they are suspicious and fearful, not wanting to give King Ajātaśatru of Magadha, son of Vaidehī, an opportunity. Therefore, they always vigilantly guard themselves and abide without negligence. Because they abide without negligence, King Ajātaśatru of Magadha, son of Vaidehī, cannot find an opportunity. In the future, before long, the Licchavis will indulge in leisure and idleness, their hands and feet will become soft and smooth, they will use silk and cotton for pillows, and lie down comfortably. They will not get up when the sun rises, and abide in negligence. Because they abide in negligence, King Ajātaśatru of Magadha, son of Vaidehī, will find an opportunity.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「诸离车子常枕木枕，手足龟坼，疑畏莫令摩竭陀王阿阇世毘提希子得其间便。是故，常自儆策，不放逸住；以彼不放逸住故，摩竭陀王阿阇世毘提希子不能伺求得其间便。于未来世，不久，诸离车子恣乐无事，手足柔软，缯纩为枕，四体安卧，日出不起，放逸而住；以放逸住故，摩竭陀王阿阇世毘提希子得其间便。

Thus, bhikṣus, diligently and skillfully, firmly capable, not abandoning the good Dharma, the skin and flesh waste away, the muscles and bones are connected. Diligent and skillful, not abandoning the good Dharma, until one attains what should be attained, not abandoning diligence, always concentrating the mind, abiding without negligence. Because of abiding without negligence, Māra, the Evil One, cannot find an opportunity.

「如是，比丘精勤方便，坚固堪能，不舍善法，肌肤损瘦，筋连骨立。精勤方便，不舍善法，乃至未得所应得者，不舍精进，常摄其心，不放逸住，以不放逸住故；魔王波旬不得其便。

"In the future, there will be bhikṣus who indulge in leisure and idleness, their hands and feet become soft and smooth, they use silk and cotton for pillows, and lie down comfortably. They will not get up when the sun rises, and abide in negligence. Because they abide in negligence, Māra, the Evil One, will find an opportunity. Therefore, bhikṣus, you should train like this: 'Diligent and skillful, until one attains what should be attained, not abandoning skillful means.'"

「当来之世，有诸比丘恣乐无事，手足柔软，缯纩为枕，四体安卧，日出不起，放逸而住；以放逸住故，恶魔波旬伺得其便。是故，比丘！当如是学：『精勤方便，乃至不得未得，不舍方便。』」

After the Buddha spoke this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully practiced it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
