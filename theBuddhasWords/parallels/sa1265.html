SĀ 1265

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1265（一二六五） 跋迦黎

Parallel to: SN 22.87 / EĀ 26.10

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha. At that time, Venerable Vakkali was staying at the Goldsmith's Rest House in Rājagṛha, suffering from a serious illness. Venerable Pūrṇa was attending to and providing for him.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。尔时，有尊者跋迦梨住王舍城金师精舍，疾病困苦，尊者富隣尼瞻视供养。

Then Vakkali said to Pūrṇa, "You should go to the World-Honored One and bow on my behalf at the feet of the World-Honored One. Inquire about the World-Honored One's well-being, whether he is free from illness and affliction, whether he is light and at ease, and whether he is dwelling in comfort. Say: 'Vakkali is staying at the Goldsmith's Rest House, suffering from a serious illness. He is confined to his bed and wishes to see the World-Honored One. Due to his serious illness, he is weak and exhausted, and is unable to come to see you. I only hope that the World-Honored One will come to the Goldsmith's Rest House out of compassion.'"

时，跋迦梨语富隣尼：「汝可诣世尊所，为我稽首礼世尊足，问讯世尊少病少恼、起居轻利、安乐住不？言：『跋迦梨住金师精舍，疾病困笃，委积床褥，愿见世尊。疾病困苦，气力羸惙，无由奉诣。唯愿世尊降此金师精舍，以哀愍故！』」

At that time, Pūrṇa, having received Vakkali's instructions, went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed at his feet, and stood to one side. He said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Venerable Vakkali bows at the feet of the World-Honored One and inquires about the World-Honored One's well-being, whether he is free from illness and affliction, whether he is light and at ease, and whether he is dwelling in comfort."

时，富隣尼受跋迦梨语已，诣世尊所，稽首礼足，退住一面，白佛言：「世尊！尊者跋迦梨稽首世尊足，问讯世尊少病少恼、起居轻利、安乐住不？」

The World-Honored One replied, "May he be at ease."

世尊答言：「令彼安乐。」

Pūrṇa said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Venerable Vakkali is staying at the goldsmith's dwelling, gravely ill and confined to his bed. He wishes to see the World-Honored One but has no strength to come to the World-Honored One. Excellent, World-Honored One! Please go to the goldsmith's dwelling out of compassion!"

富隣尼白佛言：「世尊！尊者跋迦梨住金师精舍，疾病困笃，委在床褥，愿见世尊，无有身力来诣世尊。善哉！世尊！诣金师精舍，以哀愍故！」

At that time, the World-Honored One silently consented. Pūrṇa, knowing that the World-Honored One had consented, bowed at his feet and left.

尔时，世尊默然听许。时，富隣尼知世尊听许已，礼足而去。

At that time, the World-Honored One, in the afternoon, arose from meditation and went to the goldsmith's dwelling, to the dwelling where Vakkali was staying. The bhikṣu Vakkali, seeing the World-Honored One from afar, tried to rise from his bed.

尔时，世尊晡时从禅觉，往诣金师精舍，至跋迦梨住房。跋迦梨比丘遥见世尊，从床欲起。

The Buddha told Vakkali, "Stop! Do not rise!"

佛告跋迦梨：「且止！勿起！」

The World-Honored One then sat on a separate bed and said to Vakkali, "Can you endure this illness? Is your illness increasing or decreasing?"

世尊即坐异床，语跋迦梨：「汝心堪忍此病苦不？汝身所患，为增、为损？」

Vakkali said to the Buddha...as in the previous sutra of Maudgalyayana. "World-Honored One! The pain in my body is extremely difficult to endure. I wish to use a knife to kill myself. I do not enjoy being born in suffering."

跋迦梨白佛……如前又摩比丘修多罗广说。「世尊！我身苦痛，极难堪忍，欲求刀自杀，不乐苦生。」

The Buddha said to Vakkali, "I will now ask you, answer me as you wish. What do you think, Vakkali? Is form permanent or impermanent?"

佛告跋迦梨：「我今问汝，随意答我。云何？跋迦梨！色是常耶？为非常耶？」

Vakkali replied, "Impermanent, World-Honored One!"

跋迦梨答言：「无常。世尊！」

He further asked, "If it is impermanent, is it suffering?"

复问：「若无常，是苦耶？」

He replied, "It is suffering, World-Honored One!"

答言：「是苦。世尊！」

He further asked, "Vakkali! If it is impermanent and suffering, it is a changing and transforming phenomenon. Is there anything in it that can be desired and loved?"

复问：「跋迦梨！若无常、苦者，是变易法，于中宁有可贪、可欲不？」

Vakkali said to the Buddha, "No, World-Honored One!" The same was said for feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness.

跋迦梨白佛：「不也，世尊！」受、想、行、识亦如是说。

The Buddha told Vakkali, "If there is nothing in that body that can be desired and loved, then it is a good end, and the next life will also be good."

佛告跋迦梨：「若于彼身无可贪、可欲者，是则善终，后世亦善。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke various teachings for Vakkali, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting him. After rising from his seat, he departed. That very night, Venerable Vakkali contemplated liberation, wanting to kill himself with a knife, not wishing to live long.

尔时，世尊为跋迦梨种种说法，示、教、照、喜已，从座起去。即于彼夜，尊者跋迦梨思惟解脱，欲执刀自杀，不乐久生。

At that time, two celestial beings of extremely upright bodies came to where the World-Honored One was in the latter part of the night, bowed their heads at his feet, withdrew and stood to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Venerable Vakkali is suffering from illness and wants to contemplate liberation. He wants to kill himself with a knife, not wishing to live long.

时，有二天身极端正，于后夜时诣世尊所，稽首礼足，退住一面，白佛言：「世尊！尊者跋迦梨疾病困苦，思惟解脱，欲执刀自杀，不乐久生。」

The second celestial being said, "That Venerable Vakkali has already attained liberation through the good liberation." Having said this, they both bowed at the Buddha's feet and disappeared.

第二天言：「彼尊者跋迦梨已于善解脱而得解脱。」说此语已，俱礼佛足，即没不现。

At that time, the World-Honored One, after the night had passed, sat down on a prepared seat in front of the great assembly and addressed the monks, saying, "Last night, two celestial beings of upright bodies came to where I was, bowed their heads, withdrew and stood to one side, and said, 'Venerable Vakkali is staying at the goldsmith's residence, suffering from illness and contemplating liberation. He wants to kill himself with a knife, not wishing to live long.' The second celestial being said, 'Venerable Vakkali has already attained liberation through the good liberation.' Having said this, they bowed their heads, and then disappeared.

尔时，世尊夜过晨朝，于大众前敷座而坐，告诸比丘：「昨夜有二天子，形体端正，来诣我所，稽首作礼，退住一面。而作是言：『尊者跋迦梨住金师精舍，疾病困苦，思惟解脱，欲执刀自杀，不乐久生。』第二天言：『尊者跋迦梨已于善解脱而得解脱。』说此语已，稽首作礼，即没不现。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told a monk, "You should go to Venerable Vakkali and say to him, 'Last night, two celestial beings came to where I was, bowed their heads, withdrew and stood to one side, and said to me, "Venerable Vakkali is seriously ill and contemplating liberation. He wants to kill himself with a knife, not wishing to live long." The second celestial being said, "Venerable Vakkali has attained liberation through the good liberation." Having said this, they disappeared. This is the celestial message. The Buddha also prophesied to you, "If you do not give rise to desire for this body, it will be a good end, and the next life will also be good."'

尔时，世尊告一比丘：「汝当往诣尊者跋迦梨比丘所，语跋迦梨言：『昨夜有二天来诣我所，稽首作礼，退住一面，语我言：「尊者跋迦梨疾病困笃，思惟解脱，欲执刀自杀，不乐久生。」第二天言：「尊者跋迦梨于善解脱而得解脱。」说此语已，即没不现。此是天语，佛复记汝：「汝于此身不起贪欲，是则善终，后世亦善。」』」

At that time, the monk received the World-Honored One's instruction and went to the goldsmith's residence and Vakkali's room.

时，彼比丘受世尊教已，诣金师精舍跋迦梨房。

At that time, Vakkali said to the attendant, "You all hold the bed and lift my body outside the residence. I want to kill myself with a knife. I do not wish to live long.

尔时，跋迦梨语侍病者：「汝等持绳床，共举我身，著精舍外，我欲执刀自杀，不乐久生。」

At that time, many monks were walking in the open air outside their rooms. The messenger monk went to the many monks and asked them, "Venerable ones! Where is the monk Vakkali staying?"

时，有众多比丘出房舍，露地经行。受使比丘诣众多比丘所，问众多比丘言：「诸尊！跋迦梨比丘住在何所？」

The monks replied, "The monk Vakkali told his attendant to lift the bed outside the residence and kill himself with a knife, not wishing to live long." The messenger monk then went to Vakkali.

诸比丘答言：「跋迦梨比丘告侍病者，令举绳床，出精舍外，欲执刀自杀，不乐久生。」受使比丘即诣跋迦梨所。

The monk Vakkali saw the messenger monk coming from afar and told his attendant, "Put the bed on the ground. That monk is coming quickly, he seems to be sent by the World-Honored One." The attendant then put the bed on the ground.

跋迦梨比丘遥见使比丘来，语侍病者：「下绳床著地，彼比丘疾来，似世尊使。」彼侍病者即下绳床著地。

At that time, the messenger monk said to Vakkali, "The World-Honored One has a teaching and the gods have something to say.

时，彼使比丘语跋迦梨：「世尊有教及天有所说。」

At that time, Vakkali said to his attendant, "Support me to the ground. I cannot receive the teaching of the World-Honored One and the words of the gods while lying on the bed." At that time, the attendant supported Vakkali and put him on the ground.

时，跋迦梨语侍病者：「扶我著地，不可于床上受世尊教及天所说。」时，侍病者即扶跋迦梨，下置于地。

At that time, Vakkali said, "You should proclaim the instructions of the World-Honored One and the words of the gods.

时，跋迦梨言：「汝可宣示世尊告勅及天所说。」

The monk said, "Vakkali! The Master tells you, 'Two gods came to me last night and said, "The monk Vakkali is gravely ill and is contemplating liberation. He wants to kill himself with a knife and does not wish to live long." The second god said, "The monk Vakkali has already attained liberation through good liberation." Having said this, they disappeared.' The World-Honored One also predicted that you will have a good end to your life and a good afterlife.

使比丘言：「跋迦梨！大师告汝：『夜有二天来白我言：「跋迦梨比丘疾病困笃，思惟解脱，欲执刀自杀，不乐久生。」第二天言：「跋迦梨比丘已于善解脱而得解脱。」说此语已，即没不现。』世尊复记说，汝善于命终，后世亦善。」

Vakkali said, "Venerable one! The Master knows what is to be known and sees what is to be seen. Those two gods also know what is to be known and see what is to be seen. But today, I have no doubt about whether form is permanent or impermanent; if it is impermanent, then it is suffering. If it is impermanent and suffering, then it is a changing phenomenon. There is nothing to be desired or sought after in it. I have no doubt about this. The same is true for feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness. But today, my illness and pain still follow my body. I want to kill myself with a knife and do not wish to live long." He then took a knife and killed himself.

跋迦梨言：「尊者！大师善知所知，善见所见；彼二天者亦善知所知，善见所见。然我今日于色常、无常，决定无疑；无常者是苦，决定无疑。若无常、苦者，是变易法，于彼无有可贪、可欲，决定无疑。受、想、行、识亦复如是。然我今日疾病苦痛犹故随身，欲刀自杀，不乐久生。」即执刀自杀。

The messenger monk, having made offerings to Vakkali's dead body, returned to where the Buddha was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I have fully informed Venerable Vakkali of what the World-Honored One instructed. He said, 'The Master knows what is to be known and sees what is to be seen. Those two gods also know what is to be known and see what is to be seen. But today, I have no doubt about whether form is permanent or impermanent...'" and so on, up to "he took a knife and killed himself"

时，使比丘供养跋迦梨死身已，还诣佛所，稽首礼足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！我以世尊所勅，具告尊者跋迦梨。彼作是言：『大师善知所知，善见所见；彼二天者亦善知所知，善见所见……』」广说乃至「执刀自杀。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the monks, "Let us all go to the goldsmith's hut where Vakkali's body is." Seeing Vakkali's dead body, there was a look of detachment. Having seen it, he said to the monks, "Do you see that Vakkali monk's dead body on the ground, with a look of detachment?"

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「共诣金师精舍跋迦梨尸所。」见跋迦梨死身，有远离之色。见已，语诸比丘：「汝等见是跋迦梨比丘死身在地，有远离之色不？」

The monks said to the Buddha, "We have seen it, World-Honored One!"

诸比丘白佛：「已见。世尊！」

He further said to the monks, "Does the body of Vakkali, when circumambulated, have a dark and gloomy appearance surrounding it?"

复告诸比丘：「遶跋迦梨身，四面周匝，有暗冥之相围遶身不？」

The monks said to the Buddha, "We have seen it, World-Honored One!"

诸比丘白佛：「已见。世尊！」

The Buddha said to the monks, "This is the form of Mara, who is searching all around for the good man Vakkali's consciousness to see where it will be reborn."

佛告诸比丘：「此是恶魔之像，周匝求觅跋迦梨善男子识神当生何处。」

The Buddha said to the monks, "The good man Vakkali did not dwell in consciousness and killed himself with a knife."

佛告诸比丘：「跋迦梨善男子不住识神，以刀自杀。」

At that time, the World Honored One gave the first prediction to Vakkali.

尔时，世尊为彼跋迦梨说第一记。

Having spoken this sutra, the Buddha said to the monks, "Having heard the Buddha's words, the monks rejoiced and practiced accordingly."

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
