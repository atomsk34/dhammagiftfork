SĀ 1266

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1266（一二六六） 阐陀

Parallel to: MN 144 / SN 35.87

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha. At that time, Venerable Chanda was staying in the mango grove of the good-clothed in the village of Nālaka, suffering from a serious illness.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。时，有尊者阐陀，住那罗聚落好衣庵罗林中，疾病困笃。

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra heard that Venerable Chanda was suffering from a serious illness in the mango grove of the good-clothed in the village of Nālaka. Having heard this, he said to Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila, "Venerable one, do you know that the monk Chanda is suffering from a serious illness in the mango grove of the good-clothed in the village of Nālaka? We should go together to see him." Mahākauṣṭhila silently agreed.

时，尊者舍利弗闻尊者阐陀在那罗聚落好衣庵罗林中，疾病困笃。闻已，语尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗：「尊者知不？阐陀比丘在那罗聚落好衣庵罗林中疾病困笃，当往共看。」摩诃拘𫄨罗默然许之。

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra and Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila went together to the mango grove of the good-clothed in the village of Nālaka, to the dwelling of Venerable Chanda.

时，尊者舍利弗与尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗共诣那罗聚落好衣庵罗林中，至尊者阐陀住房。

Venerable Chanda saw from afar Venerable Śāriputra and Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila, and tried to get up from his bed.

尊者阐陀遥见尊者舍利弗、尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗，凭床欲起。

Venerable Śāriputra said to Venerable Chanda, "You need not get up!"

尊者舍利弗语尊者阐陀：「汝且莫起！」

Venerable Śāriputra and Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila sat on different beds and asked Venerable Chanda, "How is it, Venerable Chanda? Is your illness bearable? Is it getting better or worse?..." As in the previous sūtra, it is explained in detail.

尊者舍利弗、尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗坐于异床，问尊者阐陀：「云何？尊者阐陀！所患为可堪忍不？为增、为损？……」如前叉摩修多罗广说。

Venerable Chanda said, "My physical illness is extremely painful and difficult to bear. The illness that has arisen only increases and does not diminish. I only wish to take a knife and kill myself. I do not enjoy living in pain."

尊者阐陀言：「我今身病，极患苦痛，难可堪忍。所起之病，但增无损，唯欲执刀自杀，不乐苦活。」

Venerable Śāriputra said, "Venerable Chanda! You should make an effort and not harm yourself! If you are in the world, I will come and go with you. If you lack anything, I will provide you with proper medicine. If you have no one to care for you, I will care for you, making sure you are comfortable, not uncomfortable."

尊者舍利弗言：「尊者阐陀！汝当努力，莫自伤害！若汝在世，我当与汝来往周旋；汝若有乏，我当给汝如法汤药；汝若无看病人，我当看汝，必令适意，非不适意。」

Chanda replied, "I have offerings. The brahmins and elders of the village of Nālaka all see to my needs. I lack nothing in clothing, food, bedding, or medicine. I have disciples who practice pure conduct and attend to me as I wish, not unpleasantly. But my illness is painful and oppresses my body, difficult to bear. I only wish to kill myself, not to live in pain."

阐陀答言：「我有供养，那罗聚落诸婆罗门长者悉见看视，衣被、饮食、卧具、汤药无所乏少；自有弟子修梵行者随意瞻病，非不适意。但我疾病苦痛逼身，难可堪忍，唯欲自杀，不乐苦生。」

Śāriputra said, "I now ask you, answer me as you wish. Chanda! The eye, eye consciousness, and the forms cognized by the eye, are they me, different from me, or in a relationship with me?"

舍利弗言：「我今问汝，随意答我。阐陀！眼及眼识、眼所识色，彼宁是我、异我、相在不？」

Chanda replied, "No."

阐陀答言：「不也。」

Venerable Śāriputra further asked, "Chanda! The ear, nose, tongue, body, mind, mental consciousness, and the dharmas cognized by mental consciousness, are they me, different from me, or in a relationship with me?"

尊者舍利弗复问：「阐陀！耳、鼻、舌、身、意及意识、意识所识法，彼宁是我、异我、相在不？」

Chanda replied, "No."

阐陀答言：「不也。」

Venerable Śāriputra further asked, "Chanda! Regarding the eye, eye consciousness, and form, what do you see, cognize, and know, that you say the eye, eye consciousness, and form are not me, not different from me, and not in a relationship with me?"

尊者舍利弗复问：「阐陀！汝于眼、眼识及色，为何所见、何所识、何所知故，言眼、眼识及色，非我、不异我、不相在？」

Chanda replied, "I see the cessation and know the cessation of the eye, eye consciousness, and form, and therefore see the eye, eye consciousness, and form as not me, not different from me, and not in a relationship with me."

阐陀答言：「我于眼、眼识及色，见灭、知灭故，见眼、眼识及色，非我、不异我、不相在。」

He further asked, "Chanda! Regarding the ear, nose, tongue, body, mind, mental consciousness, and dharmas, what do you see and know, that you see the mind, mental consciousness, and dharmas as not me, not different from me, and not in a relationship with me?"

复问：「阐陀！汝于耳、鼻、舌、身、意、意识及法，何所见、何所知故，于意、意识及法，见非我、不异我、不相在？」

Chanda replied, "Venerable Sariputra! I see the cessation and know the cessation of the mind, mental consciousness, and dharmas, and therefore see the mind, mental consciousness, and dharmas as not me, not different from me, and not in a relationship with me. Venerable Sariputra! However, I am suffering from a physical illness today and cannot bear the pain. I wish to kill myself with a knife, as I do not enjoy suffering in life."

阐陀答言：「尊者舍利弗！我于意、意识及法，见灭、知灭故，于意、意识及法，见非我、不异我、不相在。尊者舍利弗！然我今日身病苦痛，不能堪忍，欲以刀自杀，不乐苦生。」

At that time, Venerable Maha-Kausthila said to Venerable Chanda, "You should now cultivate right mindfulness in the Great Teacher, as it is said: 'Those who rely on something are shaken; those who are shaken have a destination; those who have a destination do not rest; those who do not rest follow the course of coming and going; those who follow the course of coming and going have future birth and death; because of future birth and death, there is future emergence and submergence; because of future emergence and submergence, there is birth, aging, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, suffering, and distress. Thus, the great mass of suffering arises.' As it is said: 'Those who do not rely on anything are unshaken; those who are unshaken have no destination; those who have no destination rest; because of resting, they do not follow the course of coming and going; those who do not follow the course of coming and going have no future emergence and submergence; those who have no future emergence and submergence have no birth, aging, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, suffering, and distress. Thus, the great mass of suffering ceases.'"

时，尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗语尊者阐陀：「汝今当于大师修习正念，如所说句：『有所依者，则为动摇；动摇者，有所趣向；趣向者，为不休息；不休息者，则随趣往来；随趣往来者，则有未来生死；有未来生死故，有未来出没；有未来出没故，则有生、老、病、死、忧、悲、苦、恼。如是纯一苦聚集。』如所说句：『无所依者，则不动摇；不动摇者，得无趣向；无趣向者，则有止息；有止息故，则不随趣往来；不随趣往来，则无未来出没，无未来出没者；则无生、老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦。如是纯大苦聚灭。』」

Chanda said, "Venerable Maha-Kausthila! The service to the World-Honored One is now complete! The following of the Well-Gone One is now complete! It is pleasing, not displeasing. The disciples' duties are now done. If there are other disciples who serve their teachers, they should also serve the Great Teacher like this, pleasing, not displeasing. However, I am suffering from a physical illness today and cannot bear the pain. I only wish to kill myself with a knife, as I do not enjoy suffering in life." At that time, Venerable Chanda then killed himself with a knife in the good clothing mango grove in the village of Naraka.

阐陀言：「尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗！供养世尊事，于今毕矣！随顺善逝，今已毕矣！适意，非不适意。弟子所作，于今已作。若复有余弟子所作供养师者，亦当如是供养大师，适意，非不适意。然我今日身病苦痛，难可堪忍，唯欲以刀自杀，不乐苦生。」尔时，尊者阐陀即于那罗聚落好衣庵罗林中以刀自杀。

At that time, Venerable Sariputra, having made offerings to Venerable Chanda's relics, went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Venerable Chanda killed himself with a knife in the good clothing mango grove in the village of Naraka. How is it, World-Honored One? Where will that Venerable Chanda go? How will he be reborn? What will be his future state?"

时，尊者舍利弗供养尊者阐陀舍利已，往诣佛所，稽首礼足，退住一面，白佛言：「世尊！尊者阐陀于那罗聚落好衣庵罗林中以刀自杀。云何？世尊！彼尊者阐陀当至何趣？云何受生？后世云何？」

The Buddha told Venerable Sariputra, "He did not say to himself, 'Venerable Maha-Kausthila! My service to the World-Honored One is now complete! My following of the Well-Gone One is now complete! It is pleasing, not displeasing. If there are others who serve the Great Teacher, they should do it like this, pleasing, not displeasing.'"

佛告尊者舍利弗：「彼不自记说言：『尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗！我供养世尊，于今已毕；随顺善逝，于今已毕，适意，非不适意。若复有余供养大师者，当如是作，适意，非不适意。』耶？」

At that time, Venerable Sariputra asked again, "World-Honored One! That Venerable Chanda previously had a family that made offerings, a very close family, and a family that spoke well in the village of Zhenzheni Brahmins.

尔时，尊者舍利弗复问：「世尊！彼尊者阐陀先于镇珍尼婆罗门聚落，有供养家、极亲厚家、善言语家。」

The Buddha told Sariputra, "So it is, Sariputra! For a good man with right knowledge and right liberation, there are families that make offerings, families that are close, and families that speak well. Sariputra! I do not say that they have great faults. If one abandons this body and there is a continuation of other bodies, I say that they have great faults; if one abandons this body and there is no continuation of other bodies, I do not say that they have great faults. Because they have no great faults, they killed themselves with a knife in the good clothing mango grove in the village of Naras."

佛告舍利弗：「如是，舍利弗！正智、正善解脱善男子，有供养家、亲厚家、善言语家。舍利弗！我不说彼有大过。若有舍此身，余身相续者，我说彼等则有大过；若有舍此身已，余身不相续者，我不说彼有大过也。无大过故，于那罗聚落好衣庵罗林中以刀自杀。」

Thus, the World-Honored One gave the first prediction to that Venerable Chanda.

如是，世尊为彼尊者阐陀说第一记。

Having spoken this sūtra, the World-Honored One, Venerable Śāriputra, and the great assembly all rejoiced and praised it.

佛说此经已，尊者舍利弗欢喜作礼而去。
