SĀ 1274

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1274（一二七四） 波纯提女

Parallel to: SN 1.39 / SĀ² 2.272

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying at the Monkey Pond in the two-storied lecture hall in Vaiśālī.

一时，佛住毘舍离猕猴池侧重阁讲堂。

At that time, the celestial maidens Kakanasa and Juluoduo, with extremely beautiful appearances, came to where the Buddha was in the latter part of the night, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. Their bodies emitted a bright light that illuminated the entire Monkey Pond.

时，有拘迦那娑天女、朱卢陀天女，容色绝妙，于后夜时来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面。其身光明遍照一切猕猴池侧。

At that time, the celestial maiden Juluoduo spoke a verse to the Buddha:

时，朱卢陀天女说偈白佛：

The great teacher, the perfectly enlightened one, abides in the country of Vesali. Kakanasa
and Juluoduo bow their heads in respect and pay homage. I have never before
heard the true Dharma and Vinaya of the Sage. Now I am able to
see it in person and hear the true Dharma being spoken. If one gives
rise to aversion with evil wisdom towards the true Dharma and Vinaya of the
sages, one will certainly fall into evil paths and experience suffering for a long
time. If one is mindful and equipped with the precepts in the true Dharma
and Vinaya of the sages, one will be reborn in the heavens and experience
happiness for a long time.

「大师等正觉，　　住毘舍离国，拘迦那朱卢，　　稽首恭敬礼。我昔未曾闻，　　牟尼正法律，今乃得亲见，　　现前说正法。若于圣法律，　　恶慧生厌恶，必当堕恶道，　　长夜受诸苦。若于圣法律，　　正念律仪备，彼则生天上，　　长夜受安乐。」

The celestial maiden Kakanasa also spoke a verse:

拘迦娑天女复说偈言：

The mind does not engage in evil, nor does the body and speech of
the world. The five desires are all false and deceptive. Right wisdom and right
mindfulness do not cultivate the various sufferings, nor do they associate with what is not beneficial.

「其心不为恶，　　及身口世间，五欲悉虚伪，　　正智正系念。不习近众苦，　　非义和合者。」

The Buddha told the celestial maiden, "It is so, it is so, as you have said:

佛告天女：「如是，如是，如汝所说：

The mind does not engage in evil, nor does the body and speech of
the world. The five desires are all false and deceptive. Right wisdom and right
mindfulness do not cultivate the various sufferings, nor do they associate with what is not beneficial.

「『其心不为恶，　　及身口世间，五欲悉虚伪，　　正智正系念。不习近众苦，　　非义和合者。』」

At that time, the celestial maiden, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and followed along. She then disappeared.

时，彼天女闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，即没不现。

At that time, the World-Honored One, after the night had passed and the morning had come, entered the assembly of monks, arranged his seat and sat down. He addressed the monks, saying: "Last night, two celestial maidens of exceptional beauty came to where I was, paid homage to me, and sat to one side. The celestial maiden Zhu Lu Tuo spoke a verse:

尔时，世尊夜过晨朝入僧中，敷座而坐，告诸比丘：「昨后夜时，有二天女，容色绝妙，来诣我所，为我作礼，退坐一面。朱卢陀天女而说偈言：

The great teacher, the Perfectly Enlightened One, abides in the country
of Vaiśālī. I, Kākānanā, and Zhu Lu Tuo, these two celestial
maidens, bow our heads at the Buddha's feet. We have never
before heard the true Dharma and Vinaya of the Sage. Now
we see the Perfectly Enlightened One expounding the subtle Dharma. If
one detests and abides in evil wisdom in the true Dharma
and Vinaya, one will surely fall into evil paths and suffer
greatly for a long time. If one has right mindfulness and
is equipped with the precepts in the true Dharma and Vinaya,

「『大师等正觉，　　住毘舍离国，我拘迦那娑，　　及以朱卢陀。如是二天女，　　稽首礼佛足，我昔未曾闻，　　牟尼正法律。今乃见正觉，　　演说微妙法，若于正法律，　　厌恶住恶慧。必堕于恶道，　　长夜受大苦，若于正法律，　　正念律仪备。生善趣天上，　　长夜受安乐。』

The celestial maiden Kākānanā also spoke a verse:

「拘迦那天女复说偈言：

The mind does not do evil, nor does the body or speech in the world
The five desires are all false and deceptive. With right wisdom and right mindfulness, one
does not cultivate the various sufferings, nor does one associate with those who are not beneficial.

「『其心不为恶，　　及身口世间，五欲悉虚伪，　　正智正系念。不习近众苦，　　非义和合者。』

I replied, 'It is so, it is so, as you have said:

「我时答言：『如是，如是，如汝所说：

The mind does not do evil, nor does the body or speech in the world
The five desires are all false and deceptive. With right wisdom and right mindfulness, one
does not cultivate the various sufferings, nor does one associate with those who are not beneficial.

「『「其心不为恶，　　及身口世间，五欲悉虚伪，　　正智正系念。不习近众苦，　　非义和合者。」』」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha had said and joyfully upheld it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
