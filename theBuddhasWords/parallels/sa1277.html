SĀ 1277

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1277（一二七七） 嫌责

Parallel to: SN 1.35 / SĀ² 2.275

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, a god with an extremely handsome appearance came to where the Buddha was in the latter part of the night. He bowed his head at the Buddha’s feet, and his entire body emitted a bright light that illuminated Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park.

时，有一天子，容色绝妙，于后夜时来诣佛所，稽首佛足，身诸光明遍照祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the god spoke a verse, saying:

时，彼天子而说偈言：

One cannot attain the path by always speaking or listening one-sidedly, but by
firm and correct transcendence. Contemplating the good and tranquil extinction, liberating oneself from
the bonds of Māra, one can speak of what can be practiced, but
not of what cannot be practiced. If one speaks of what cannot be
practiced, the wise will know it is not so. Not practicing what one
should, and saying one has done it, is the same as a thief.

「不可常言说，　　亦不一向听，而得于道迹，　　坚固正超度。思惟善寂灭，　　解脱诸魔缚，能行说之可，　　不行不应说。不行而说者，　　智者则知非，不行己所应，　　不作而言作。是则同贼非。」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the god, "Do you have any complaints now?"

尔时，世尊告天子言：「汝今有所嫌责耶？」

The god said to the Buddha, "I repent, World-Honored One! I repent, Well-Gone One!"

天子白佛：「悔过，世尊！悔过，善逝！」

At that time, the World-Honored One smiled with joy.

尔时，世尊熙怡微笑。

At that time, the god spoke another verse:

时，彼天子复说偈言：

I now regret my mistake, but the World-Honored One does not
accept it. Harboring evil thoughts, I complain and do not let go.

「我今悔其过，　　世尊不纳受，内怀于恶心，　　抱怨而不舍。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse in reply:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

Though you speak words of regret, if your mind does not cease
how can you put an end to enmity? What is called cultivating goodness?

「言说悔过辞，　　内不息其心，云何得息怨？　　何名为修善？」

At that time, the god-son spoke another verse:

时，彼天子复说偈言：

Who does not have faults? Who is without sin? Who
is without foolishness? Who can always be firm and steadfast?

「谁不有其过？　　何人无有罪？谁复无愚痴？　　孰能常坚固？」

At that time, the god-son spoke another verse:

时，彼天子复说偈言：

I have long seen the brāhmaṇa attain parinirvāṇa
beyond all fears, forever transcending worldly love and affection.

「久见婆罗门，　　逮得般涅槃，一切怖已过，　　永超世恩爱。」

At that time, the god-son, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and was delighted. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and then disappeared.

时，彼天子闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，稽首佛足，即没不现。
