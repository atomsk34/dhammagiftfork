SĀ 1278

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1278（一二七八） 瞿迦黎

Parallel to: SN 6.9 / SN 6.10 / AN 4.3 (~) / AN 10.89 / Snp 3.10 / SĀ² 2.276 / EĀ 21.5

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, there was a monk named Kūṭadanta, who was an ally of Devadatta. He came to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side.

时，有瞿迦梨比丘，是提婆达多伴党，来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面。

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the monk Kūṭadanta, "Kūṭadanta! Why do you have impure thoughts towards the pure and pure conduct of Śāriputra and Maudgalyāyana? You will experience unbeneficial suffering for a long time.

尔时，世尊告瞿迦梨比丘：「瞿迦梨！汝何故于舍利弗、目揵连清净梵行所，起不清净心？长夜当得不饶益苦。」

The monk Kūṭadanta said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I now believe the words of the World-Honored One, which are not different. But Śāriputra and Mahāmaudgalyāyana have evil desires in their minds." Thus he spoke a second and a third time. The monk Kūṭadanta, an ally of Devadatta, spoke against the Buddha three times and left his seat. After leaving, blisters appeared all over his body, the size of chestnuts, gradually growing larger, the size of peaches and plums.

瞿迦梨比丘白佛言：「世尊！我今信世尊语，所说无异，但舍利弗、大目揵连心有恶欲。」如是第二、第三说，瞿迦梨比丘——提婆达多伴党于世尊所再三说中，违反不受，从座起去。去已，其身周遍生诸疱疮，皆如栗，渐渐增长，皆如桃李。

At that time, the monk Kūṭadanta suffered pain and said with his mouth, "It burns! It burns!" Pus and blood flowed out, and his body disintegrated and died, taking rebirth in the great Avīci hell.

时，瞿迦梨比丘患苦痛，口说是言：「极烧！极烧！」脓血流出，身坏命终，生大钵昙摩地狱。

At that time, three celestial sons of exceptional appearance came to where the Buddha was in the latter part of the night, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. Then one celestial son said to the Buddha, "The monk Kūṭadanta, an ally of Devadatta, has now passed away." Then the second celestial son said, "Venerable ones, you should know that the monk Kūṭadanta has passed away and fallen into hell." The third celestial son then spoke a verse:

时，有三天子，容色绝妙，于后夜时来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面。时，一天子白佛言：「瞿迦梨比丘——提婆达多伴党今已命终。」时，第二天子作是言：「诸尊当知，瞿迦梨比丘命终堕地狱中。」第三天子即说偈言：

When a person is born in the world, an axe grows in
their mouth, and they cut themselves with it. This is due to
their evil words. They should praise what should be praised, but instead
they slander. The sin arises from the mouth, and upon death they
fall into evil realms. Gambling and losing wealth is a great fault
but slandering the Buddha and the Shravakas is an even greater fault.

「士夫生世间，　　斧在口中生，还自斩其身，　　斯由其恶言。应毁便称誉，　　应誉而便毁，其罪生于口，　　死堕恶道中。博弈亡失财，　　是非为大咎，毁佛及声闻，　　是则为大过。」

After the three celestial sons spoke this verse, they disappeared.

彼三天子说是偈已，即没不现。

Then the World Honored One, after the night had passed, entered the assembly in the morning, spread his seat in front of the great assembly, and sat down. He told the monks: "Last night, three celestial sons came to me, bowed at my feet, and sat to one side. The first celestial son said to me: 'World Honored One! The monk Gaukāli, a companion of Devadatta, has now died.' The second celestial son said to the other celestial son: 'The monk Gaukāli has died and fallen into hell.' The third celestial son then spoke a verse:

尔时，世尊夜过晨朝，来入僧中，于大众前敷座而坐，告诸比丘：「昨后夜时，有三天子来诣我所，稽首我足，退坐一面。第一天子语我言：『世尊！瞿迦梨比丘——提婆达多伴党今已命终。』第二天子语余天子言：『瞿迦梨比丘命终堕地狱中。』第三天子即说偈言：

The axe is born in the mouth of a man, and it cuts
his own body. This is due to his evil words. He should praise
but instead criticizes, and he should criticize but instead praises. The sin is
born in the mouth, and when he dies, he falls into evil paths.

「『士夫生世间，　　斧在口中生，还自斩其身，　　斯由其恶言。应毁便称誉，　　应誉而便毁，其罪口中生，　　死则堕恶道。』

Having spoken this verse, he disappeared. "Monks, do you wish to hear the lifespan of the beings in the Avīci hell?"

「说是偈已，即没不现。诸比丘！汝等欲闻生阿浮陀地狱众生其寿齐限不？」

The monks said to the Buddha, "Now is the right time. We only wish that the World-Honored One would speak for the sake of the great assembly about the lifespan of the beings in the Avīci hell. When the monks hear it, they will accept and uphold it."

诸比丘白佛：「今正是时，唯愿世尊为诸大众说阿浮陀地狱众生寿命齐限。诸比丘闻已，当受奉行。」

The Buddha told the monks, "Listen carefully and think well, I will explain it for you. Suppose in the country of Kosala, four dou is one a-luo, four a-luo is one du-kong-na, sixteen du-kong-na is one sha-mo-na, sixteen sha-mo-na is one ma-ni, twenty ma-ni is one qiu-li, twenty qiu-li is one granary, filled with mustard seeds. If someone were to take one mustard seed every hundred years, and so on, until the granary of mustard seeds was completely emptied, the lifespan of the beings in the Avīci hell would still not be exhausted. Twenty Avīci hells are equal to the lifespan of one Nirarbuda hell being, twenty Nirarbuda hells are equal to the lifespan of one Aṭaṭa hell being, twenty Aṭaṭa hells are equal to the lifespan of one Apapa hell being, twenty Apapa hells are equal to the lifespan of one Aśvaśva hell being, twenty Aśvaśva hells are equal to the lifespan of one Utpala hell being, twenty Utpala hells are equal to the lifespan of one Paduma hell being, twenty Paduma hells are equal to the lifespan of one Mahāpaduma hell being.

佛告比丘：「谛听，善思，当为汝说。譬如拘萨罗国，四斗为一阿罗，四阿罗为一独笼那，十六独笼那为一阇摩那，十六阇摩那为一摩尼，二十摩尼为一佉梨，二十佉梨为一仓，满中芥子。若使有人百年百年取一芥子。如是乃至满仓芥子都尽，阿浮陀地狱众生寿命犹故不尽。如是二十阿浮陀地狱众生寿等一尼罗浮陀地狱众生寿，二十尼罗浮陀地狱众生寿等一阿咤咤地狱众生寿，二十阿咤咤地狱众生寿等一阿波波地狱众生寿，二十阿波波地狱众生寿等一阿休休地狱众生寿，二十阿休休地狱众生寿等一优钵罗地狱众生寿，二十优钵罗地狱众生寿等一钵昙摩地狱众生寿，二十钵昙摩地狱众生寿等一摩诃钵昙摩地狱众生寿。

"Monks! That śramaṇa Gautama will die and fall into the Mahāpaduma hell, because he gave rise to evil thoughts and slandered the Venerable Śāriputra and Mahāmaudgalyāyana. Therefore, monks! You should study like this: 'We do not even want to destroy a burnt wick, let alone destroy sentient beings with consciousness.'" The Buddha told the monks, "You should study like this."

「比丘！彼瞿迦梨比丘命终堕摩诃钵昙摩地狱中，以彼于尊者舍利弗、大目揵连比丘生恶心、诽谤故。是故，诸比丘！当作是学：『于彼烧燋炷所，尚不欲毁坏，况毁坏有识众生？』」佛告诸比丘：「当如是学。」

After the Buddha spoke this sūtra, the monks who heard what the Buddha said rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
