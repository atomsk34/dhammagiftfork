SĀ 1286

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1286（一二八六） 种别

Parallel to: SN 1.36 / SĀ² 2.284

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, a god with an extremely handsome appearance came to where the Buddha was in the latter part of the night, bowed his head at the Buddha’s feet, and sat to one side. His body’s radiance illuminated Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park.

时，有天子容色绝妙，于后夜时来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面。其身光明遍照祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the god spoke a verse to ask the Buddha:

时，彼天子说偈问佛：

If a person indulges in carelessness, they are foolish and
devoid of wisdom. Meditating without carelessness, they quickly exhaust all taints.

「若人行放逸，　　愚痴离恶慧，禅思不放逸，　　疾得尽诸漏。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse in reply:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

The affairs of the world are not desire. The mind’s dharmas
run and conceptualize. This is called the desire of a person
The various affairs of the world are always in the world
Wisdom cultivates meditation. Desire and passion are forever hidden. Faith is
the companion of a person. Without faith, one is not liberated
Faith increases one’s reputation. At the end of life, one is
reborn in heaven. The empty thought of the body is called
form and name. They are not solid. Not grasping form and
name is to be far from accumulation. Observing this true meaning
like liberation and compassion, by this wisdom, the world praises and
makes offerings. Able to cut off various characteristics, transcending the flow
of birth and death, having transcended all flows, this is called a bhikṣu.

「非世间众事，　　是则之为欲，心法驰觉想，　　是名士夫欲。世间种种事，　　常在于世间，智慧修禅思，　　爱欲永潜伏。信为士夫伴，　　不信则不度，信增其名称，　　命终得生天。于身虚空想，　　名色不坚固，不著名色者，　　远离于积聚。观此真实义，　　如解脱哀愍，由斯智慧故，　　世称叹供养。能断众杂相，　　超绝生死流，超度诸流已，　　是名为比丘。」

At that time, the god-son again spoke a verse:

时，彼天子复说偈言：

I have long seen the brāhmaṇa attain parinirvāṇa
beyond all fears, forever transcending worldly affection and love.

「久见婆罗门，　　逮得般涅槃，一切怖已过，　　永超世恩爱。」

Then the god-son, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and was delighted. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and vanished on the spot.

于是，天子闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，稽首佛足，即没不现。
