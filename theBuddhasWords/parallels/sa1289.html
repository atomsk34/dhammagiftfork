SĀ 1289

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1289（一二八九） 八天

Parallel to: SN 1.38 / SĀ² 2.287

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Golden-clad Mountain in Rājagṛha, in the cave where the Golden-clad spirits lived.

一时，佛住王舍城金婆罗山金婆罗鬼神住处石室中。

At that time, the World-Honored One was pierced in the foot by a golden spear. Not long after, he experienced physical pain, but he was able to let go of his mind, with right knowledge and right mindfulness, enduring and at ease, without any thought of retreat.

尔时，世尊金鎗刺足，未经几时，起身苦痛；能得舍心，正智正念，堪忍自安，无退减想。

There were eight mountain spirit princes who thought, "Today the World-Honored One is staying at the Golden-clad Mountain in Rājagṛha, in the cave where the Golden-clad spirits live. He was pierced in the foot by a golden spear and experienced physical pain, but he was able to let go of his mind, with right mindfulness and right knowledge, enduring and at ease, without any retreat. We should go before him and praise him." Having thought this, they went to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at his feet, and stood to one side.

彼有山神天子八人，作是念：「今日世尊住王舍城金婆罗山金婆罗鬼神住处石室中，金鎗刺足，起身苦痛而能舍心，正念正智，堪忍自安，无所退减，我等当往面前赞叹。」作是念已，往诣佛所，稽首礼足，退住一面。

The first spirit prince spoke a verse in praise, saying:

第一天神说偈叹言：

The Śramaṇa Gautama, the lion among
men, though his body suffers pain
endures and is at ease, with
right knowledge and right mindfulness, without any retreat.

「沙门瞿昙，　　人中师子，　　身遭苦痛，堪忍自安，　　正智正念，　　无所退减。」

The second celestial prince also praised, saying:

第二天子复赞叹言：

The great being, the great dragon, the great being, the
bull king, the great man's courage and strength, the great
man's good horse, the great man's leader, the great man's victor.

「大士之大龙，　　大士之牛王，大士夫勇力，　　大士夫良马，大士夫上首，　　大士夫之胜。」

The third celestial prince also praised, saying:

第三天子复赞叹言：

This śramaṇa Gautama, the man's lotus, with the arising of various pains
in the body, is able to practice the mind of giving up
abides in right knowledge and right mindfulness, endures and comforts himself, without any decrease.

「此沙门瞿昙，　　士夫分陀利，身生诸苦痛，　　而能行舍心，正智正念住，　　堪忍以自安，而无所退减。」

The fourth celestial prince also praised, saying: "If there are those who go against and criticize what is said by the śramaṇa Gautama, the man's lotus, know that they will for a long time not obtain beneficial happiness, except for those who do not know the truth."

第四天子复赞叹言：「若有于沙门瞿昙士夫分陀利所说违反嫌责，当知斯等长夜当得不饶益苦，唯除不知真实者。」

The fifth celestial prince also spoke a verse, saying:

第五天子复说偈言：

Contemplating that samādhi concentration, well abiding in the right
absorption, liberated and free from all defilements, not elated
or depressed, the mind peacefully abides, and attains mental liberation.

「观彼三昧定，　　善住于正受，解脱离诸尘，　　不踊亦不没，其心安隐住，　　而得心解脱。」

The sixth celestial prince also spoke a verse, saying:

第六天子复说偈言：

Having gone through five hundred years, reciting the Brāhman scriptures
diligently practicing austerities, not understanding liberation and freedom from defilements
this is the inferior class, not attaining the other shore.

「经历五百岁，　　诵婆罗门典，精勤修苦行，　　不解脱离尘，是则卑下类，　　不得度彼岸。」

The seventh celestial prince also spoke a verse, saying:

第七天子复说偈言：

Being pressed by desire, bound by precepts, bravely
practicing austerities, going through a hundred years, the
mind not liberated, not free from defilements, this
is the inferior class, not crossing to the other shore.

「为欲之所迫，　　持戒之所缚，勇捍行苦行，　　经历于百年，其心不解脱，　　不离于尘垢，是则卑下类，　　不度于彼岸。」

The eighth celestial prince also spoke a verse, saying:

第八天子复说偈言：

The mind dwells in arrogance and desire, unable to subdue itself
not attaining the concentration and equipoise of the sage, dwelling alone
in the mountains and forests, the mind always negligent, not able
to cross to the other shore of the army of the
demon of death.

「心居憍慢欲，　　不能自调伏，不得三昧定，　　牟尼之正受，独一居山林，　　其心常放逸，于彼死魔军，　　不得度彼岸。」

At that time, the eight celestial princes of the mountain spirits each praised in this way, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, and then disappeared.

时，彼山神天子八人各各赞叹已，稽首佛足，即没不现。
