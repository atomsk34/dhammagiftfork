SĀ 1307

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1307（一三〇七） 边际

Parallel to: SN 2.26 / AN 4.45 / AN 4.46 / SĀ² 2.306 / EĀ 43.1 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, a red horse celestial being of exceptional beauty came to where the Buddha was in the latter part of the night, bowed his head at the Buddha’s feet, and sat to one side. His body’s radiance illuminated Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park.

时，有赤马天子，容色绝妙，于后夜时来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面。其身光明遍照祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the red horse celestial being said to the Buddha, “World-Honored One! Is there a way to go beyond the limits of the world and reach a place where there is no birth, aging, or death?”

时，彼赤马天子白佛言：「世尊！颇有能行过世界边，至不生、不老、不死处不？」

The Buddha told the red horse, “There is no way to go beyond the limits of the world and reach a place where there is no birth, aging, or death.”

佛告赤马：「无有能过世界边，至不生、不老、不死处者。」

The red horse celestial being said to the Buddha, “How wonderful! World-Honored One! You have well explained this meaning. As the World-Honored One has said, there is no way to go beyond the limits of the world and reach a place where there is no birth, aging, or death. Why is that? World-Honored One! I remember my past life. I was named Red Horse. I was a non-Buddhist ascetic who attained supernatural powers and was free from desires. I thought at that time, 'I have such swift supernatural feet. Like a strong man shooting an arrow across the shadow of a palm tree, I can climb one Mount Sumeru and reach another Mount Sumeru. I can step on the Eastern Sea and reach the Western Sea.' I then thought, 'I have now accomplished such swift supernatural power. Today, I should seek the limits of the world.' Having thought this, I immediately set out. Except for eating, relieving myself, and reducing sleep, I always walked for a hundred years. I died there and still could not go beyond the limits of the world and reach a place where there is no birth, aging, or death.

赤马天子白佛言：「奇哉！世尊！善说斯义。如世尊说言：『无过世界边，至不生、不老、不死处者。』所以者何？世尊！我自忆宿命，名曰赤马，作外道仙人，得神通，离诸爱欲。我时，作是念：『我有如是捷] cf.【丽】疾神足，如健士夫，以利箭横射过多罗树影之顷，能登一须弥，至一须弥，足蹑东海，超] cf.【丽】至西海。』我时，作是念：『我今成就如是捷] cf.【丽】疾神力，今日宁可求世界边。』作是念已，即便发行，唯除食息便利，减节睡眠，常行百岁，于彼命终，竟不能得过世界边，至不生、不老、不死之处。」

The Buddha told the red horse, "I now only speak of the world, the origin of the world, the cessation of the world, and the path leading to the cessation of the world with a body of one fathom. Red Horse Celestial Being! What is the world? It refers to the five aggregates of clinging. What are the five? The form aggregate of clinging, the feeling aggregate of clinging, the perception aggregate of clinging, the volitional formations aggregate of clinging, and the consciousness aggregate of clinging. This is called the world. What is the origin of the world? It refers to the craving for future existence, accompanied by delight and desire, seeking delight in this and that, which is called the origin of the world. What is the cessation of the world? It refers to the complete abandonment, relinquishment, renunciation, exhaustion, absence of desire, cessation, pacification, and cessation of that craving for future existence, accompanied by delight and desire, seeking delight in this and that. This is called the cessation of the world. What is the path leading to the cessation of the world? It refers to the eightfold noble path, namely right view, right intention, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right concentration. This is called the path leading to the cessation of the world.

佛告赤马：「我今但以一寻之身，说于世界、世界集、世界灭、世界灭道迹。赤马天子！何等为世间？谓五受阴。何等为五？色受阴、受受阴、想受阴、行受阴、识受阴，是名世间。何等为色集？谓当来有爱，贪、喜俱，彼彼染著，是名世间集。云何为世间灭？若彼当来有爱，贪、喜俱，彼彼染著无余断、舍、离、尽、无欲、灭、息、没，是名世间灭。何等为世间灭道迹？谓八圣道，正见、正志、正语、正业、正命、正方便、正念、正定，是名世间灭道迹。

Red Horse! Knowing the world, one abandons the world; knowing the origin of the world, one abandons the origin of the world; knowing the cessation of the world, one realizes the cessation of the world; knowing the path leading to the cessation of the world, one cultivates that path leading to the cessation of the world. Red Horse! If a bhikṣu knows and abandons worldly suffering, knows and abandons the origin of the world, knows and realizes the cessation of the world, and knows and cultivates the path leading to the cessation of the world, Red Horse! This is called reaching the limits of the world and transcending worldly desires.

「赤马！了知世间，断世间；了知世间集，断世间集；了知世间灭，证世间灭；了知世间灭道迹，修彼灭道迹。赤马！若比丘于世间苦若知、若断，世间集若知、若断，世间灭若知、若证，世间灭道迹若知、若修。赤马！是名得世界边，度世间爱。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke the following verses:

尔时，世尊重说偈言：

Never before have I traveled far, yet I have reached the limits of
the world. Without reaching the limits of the world, one will never end
the limits of suffering. Therefore, the Sage is able to know the limits
of the world, and skillfully understands the limits of the world, establishing all
practices of the Brahmins. Regarding those who equally realize the limits of the
world, this is called the practice of the sages, transcending the world to
the other shore.

「未曾远游行，　　而得世界边，无得世界边，　　终不尽苦边。以是故牟尼，　　能知世界边，善解世界边，　　诸梵行已立。于彼世界边，　　平等觉知者，是名贤圣行，　　度世间彼岸。」

At that time, the Heavenly Son of the Red Horse, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully rejoiced, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and then disappeared.

是时，赤马天子闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，稽首佛足，即没不现。
