SĀ 1308

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1308（一三〇八） 外道诸见

Parallel to: SN 2.30 / SĀ² 2.307

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying at Mount Vipula near Rajagriha. There were six celestial beings who had previously renounced the householder's life as non-Buddhist ascetics. Their names were Abhibhu, Abhibhūtarājā, Saṃpratijñā, Vīra, Ajita, and Kālanemi. They came to where the Buddha was.

一时，佛住王舍城毘富罗山侧。有六天子，本为外道出家，一名阿毘浮、二名增上阿毘浮、三名能求、四名毘蓝婆、五名阿俱咤、六名迦蓝，来诣佛所。

The celestial being Abhibhu then spoke a verse:

阿毘浮天子即说偈言：

Monks, with utmost sincerity, always practice renunciation. In the first
and last watches of the night, contemplate and restrain well
Seeing and hearing what is said, one will not fall into hell.

「比丘专至心，　　常修行厌离，于初夜后夜，　　思惟善自摄，见闻其所说，　　不堕于地狱。」

The celestial being Abhibhūparama then spoke a verse:

增上阿毘浮天子复说偈言：

Renouncing darkness, the mind always protects itself
forever leaving worldly disputes and arguments. From
the great teacher Tathāgata, receiving the Dharma
of śramaṇas, well protecting the world, not
causing various evils.

「厌离于黑暗，　　心常自摄护，永离于世间，　　言语诤论法。从如来大师，　　禀受沙门法，善摄护世间，　　不令造众恶。」

The celestial being Arhat then spoke a verse:

能求天子复说偈言：

Cutting, beating, and killing to make offerings to Kāśyapa, I do
not see it as evil, nor do I see it as merit.

「断截椎打杀，　　供养施迦叶，不见其为恶，　　亦不见为福。」

The celestial being Vīra then spoke a verse:

毘蓝婆天子复说偈言：

I say that those Nirgrantha ascetics, the followers of Jāti, who go
forth to practice the path, cultivate difficult practices for a long time
and in the community of the great teacher, are far removed from
false speech, I say that such people are not far from arhats.

「我说彼尼干，　　外道若提子，出家行学道，　　长夜修难行。于大师徒众，　　远离于妄语，我说如是人，　　不远于罗汉。」

At that time the World Honored One spoke a verse in reply:

尔时世尊说偈答言：

A skinny wild fox always roams with a lion
all day long it is small and weak, unable
to be like a lion. The Nirgrantha master's assembly
falsely praises themselves, this evil-minded false speech is very
far from arhats.

「死瘦之野狐，　　常共师子游，终日小羸劣，　　不能为师子。尼干大师众，　　虚妄自称叹，是恶心妄语，　　去罗汉甚远。」

At that time the heavenly demon king Mara, possessing the god Aghora, spoke a verse:

尔时天魔波旬著阿俱咤天子而说偈言：

Diligent in abandoning darkness, always guarding and distancing, deeply attached
to subtle forms, delighting in the Brahma world. I teach
and transform them, causing them to be born in the Brahma Heaven.

「精勤弃暗冥，　　常守护远离，深著微妙色，　　贪乐于梵世。我教化斯等，　　令得生梵天。」

At that time, the World Honored One had this thought: "If the verses spoken by this Aghora god are due to the power added by the heavenly demon king Mara, and not spoken by the mind of this Aghora god himself, then he says:

尔时，世尊作是念：「若此阿俱咤天子所说偈，此是天魔波旬加其力故，非彼阿俱咤天子自心所说，作是说言：

Diligent in abandoning darkness, guarding and distancing, deeply attached to
subtle forms, delighting in the Brahma world." I will teach
and transform them, causing them to be born in the Brahma Heaven.

「『精勤弃暗冥，　　守护于远离，深著微妙色，　　贪乐于梵世。』当教化斯等，　　令得生梵天。」

At that time, the World Honored One again spoke a verse:

尔时，世尊复说偈言：

All the forms that exist, here and there
or in the sky, each shining brightly, should
be known as not apart from Māra's bondage
like a baited hook, hooking the swimming fish.

「若诸所有色，　　于此及与彼，或复虚空中，　　各别光照耀。当知彼一切，　　不离魔魔缚，犹如垂钩饵，　　钩钓于游鱼。」

At that time, those heavenly beings all thought to themselves, "The verse spoken today by the heavenly being Aketu was said by the śramaṇa Gautama to be spoken by Māra. Why did the śramaṇa Gautama say it was spoken by Māra?"

时，彼天子咸各念言：「今日阿俱咤天子所说偈，沙门瞿昙言是魔所说。何故沙门瞿昙言是魔说？」

At that time, the World Honored One knew the thoughts in the minds of those heavenly beings and said to them, "The verse spoken by the heavenly being Aketu was not spoken by that heavenly being himself, but because the demon Māra added his power to it." He said this:

尔时，世尊知诸天子心中所念，而告之言：「今阿俱咤天子所说偈，非彼天子自心所说，时魔波旬加其力故。」作是说言：

Diligent in abandoning darkness, guarding the state of seclusion, deeply attached to subtle
forms, delighting in the Brahma Heaven. I shall teach and transform them, enabling
them to be born in the Brahma Heaven. Therefore, I speak this verse:

「精勤弃暗冥，　　守护于远离，深著微妙色，　　贪乐于梵天。当教化斯等，　　令得生梵天。是故我说偈：

All the forms that exist, here and there
or in the sky, each shining brightly, should
be known as not being free from Māra's
bondage. They are like a baited hook, hooking
the swimming fish.

「『若诸所有色，　　于此及与彼，或复虚空中，　　各别光照耀。当知彼一切，　　不离魔魔缚，犹如垂钩饵，　　钩钓于游鱼。』」

At that time, the heavenly beings again thought: "How amazing! The Śramaṇa Gautama has great spiritual power and virtue, able to see the heavenly demon Pāpīyas, while we do not see him. We should also each speak verses in praise of the Śramaṇa Gautama." They then spoke the verses:

时，诸天子复作是念：「奇哉！沙门瞿昙！神力大德，能见天魔波旬，而我等不见，我等当复各各说偈赞叹沙门瞿昙。」即说偈言：

Eliminate all attachment to the body, and let this well-protected one
eliminate all false speech. If one wishes to eliminate desire and
attachment, one should make offerings to the great teacher. Eliminate attachment
to the three realms of existence and destroy false speech. Having
eliminated attachment to views, one should make offerings to the great
teacher. The first in Rājagṛha is called Mount Vipula. Among the
snow mountains, it is the best. Among the garuḍas, it is
named. In the eight directions and above and below, in all
the realms of sentient beings, among the gods and humans, the
Perfectly Enlightened One is the best.

「断除于一切，　　有身爱贪想，令此善护者，　　除一切妄语。若欲断欲爱，　　应供养大师。断除三有爱，　　破坏于妄语。已断于见贪，　　应供养大师。王舍城第一，　　名毘富罗山。雪山诸山最，　　金翅鸟中名。八方及上下，　　一切众生界，于诸天人中，　　等正觉最上。」

At that time, after the heavenly beings had spoken verses in praise of the Buddha and heard what the Buddha had said, they rejoiced and followed his joy. They bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet and then disappeared.

时，诸天子说偈赞佛已，闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，稽首佛足，即没不现。
