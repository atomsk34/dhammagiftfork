SĀ 1320

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1320（一三二〇） 白山

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying on Mount Magadha, with Venerable Nāgapaṇḍita as his attendant.

一时，佛住摩鸠罗山，尊者那伽波罗为亲侍者。

At that time, the World-Honored One, in the darkness of night, with a slight drizzle and lightning flashing, went outside the room and walked in the open air.

尔时，世尊于夜暗时，天小微雨，电光睒现，出于房外，露地经行。

At that time, Sakra, Lord of the Devas, had this thought: "Today the World-Honored One is staying on Mount Magadha, with Venerable Nāgapaṇḍita as his attendant. In the dark night, with a slight drizzle and lightning flashing, the World-Honored One has gone outside the room and is walking in the open air. I shall transform myself into a multi-storied pavilion of lapis lazuli and hold the pavilion as the Buddha walks." Having had this thought, he immediately transformed himself into a multi-storied pavilion of lapis lazuli and went to where the Buddha was. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and followed the Buddha as he walked.

是时，天帝释作是念：「今日世尊住摩鸠罗山，尊者那伽波罗亲侍供养，其夜暗冥，天时微雨，电光睒] cf.【丽】现，世尊出房，露地经行，我当化作毘琉璃重阁，执持重阁，随佛经行。」作是念已，即便化作鞞琉璃重阁，持诣佛所，稽首佛足，随佛经行。

At that time, the people of Magadha, whether male or female, when crying at night, were frightened by the Magadha ghost and immediately stopped. The method of serving and attending the disciples was to wait for the teacher to wake up from meditation before going to sleep. At that time, the World-Honored One walked with Sakra, Lord of the Devas, for a long time in the night.

尔时，摩竭提国人若男若女，夜啼之时，以摩鸠罗鬼恐之即止，亲侍供养弟子之法，待师禅觉，然后乃眠。尔时，世尊为天帝释夜经行久。

At that time, Venerable Nāgapaṇḍita had this thought: "The World-Honored One has been walking for a long time tonight. I shall now take on the form of a Magadha ghost to frighten him!" At that time, the monk Nāgapaṇḍita turned his body so that the long hair was on the outside. He went to the head of the path where the World-Honored One was walking and said to the Buddha, "The Magadha ghost is coming! The Magadha ghost is coming!"

尔时，尊者那伽波罗作是念：「世尊今夜经行至久，我今当作摩鸠罗鬼形而恐怖之！」时，那伽波罗比丘即反被俱执，长毛在外，往在世尊经行道头，白佛言：「摩鸠罗鬼来！摩鸠罗鬼来！」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the monk Nāgapaṇḍita, "You foolish person, Nāgapaṇḍita, do you want to frighten the Buddha with the form of a Magadha ghost? You cannot move even a single hair of the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One. The Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One has long been free from fear!"

尔时，世尊告那伽波罗比丘：「汝那伽波罗愚痴人，以摩鸠罗鬼神像恐怖佛耶？不能动如来、应、等正觉一毛发也，如来、应、等正觉久离恐怖！」

At that time, Śakra, Lord of the Devas, said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Is there also such a person in the World-Honored One's true Dharma and Vinaya?"

尔时，天帝释白佛言：「世尊！世尊正法、律中亦复有此人耶？」

The Buddha said, "Kauśika! The house of Gautama is extremely vast and spacious. They will also obtain the pure Dharma in the future."

佛言：「憍尸迦！瞿昙家中极大广阔，斯等于未来世亦当使得清净之法。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

If a brahmin attains the other shore in
his own Dharma, even a piśāca and a
mātaṅga surpass him. If a brahmin observes and
extinguishes all feelings and perceptions in his own
practiced Dharma, if a brahmin attains the other
shore in his own Dharma, all causes and
conditions are completely extinguished. If a brahmin attains
the other shore in his own Dharma, all
notions of self are completely extinguished. If a
brahmin attains the other shore in his own
Dharma, he has transcended birth, old age, sickness and death.

「若复婆罗门，　　于自所得法，得到于彼岸。　　若一毘舍遮，及与摩鸠罗，　　皆悉超过去。若复婆罗门，　　于自所行法，一切诸受觉，　　观察皆已灭。若复婆罗门，　　自法度彼岸，一切诸因缘，　　皆悉已灭尽。若复婆罗门，　　自法度彼岸，一切诸人我，　　皆悉已灭尽。若复婆罗门，　　自法度彼岸，于生老病死，　　皆悉已超过。」

Having spoken this sūtra, Śakra, Lord of the Devas, upon hearing the Buddha's words, rejoiced and followed them. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and then disappeared.

佛说此经已，释提桓因闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，稽首佛足，即没不现。
