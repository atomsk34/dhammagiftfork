SĀ 1322

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1322（一三二二） 富那婆薮

Parallel to: SN 10.7 / SĀ² 2.321

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in the human realm of Magadha, together with a large assembly, and stayed overnight at the abode of the ghost mother Punapavasu.

一时，佛在摩竭提国人间游行，与大众俱，到富那婆薮鬼子母住处宿。

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke to the bhikṣus about the Dharma associated with the four noble truths, namely, the noble truth of suffering, the noble truth of the origin of suffering, the noble truth of the cessation of suffering, and the noble truth of the path leading to the cessation of suffering.

尔时，世尊为诸比丘说四圣谛相应法，所谓苦圣谛、苦集圣谛、苦灭圣谛、苦灭道迹圣谛。

At that time, the ghost mother Punapavasu, her son Punapavasu, and the ghost girl Uttara, the two young ghosts, cried at night. Then the ghost mother Punapavasu taught her children and spoke a verse:

尔时，富那婆薮鬼母，儿富那婆薮及鬼女欝多罗，二鬼小儿夜啼。时，富那婆薮鬼母教其男女故，而说偈言：

You, Punapavasu, Uttara, do not cry, Let me hear the
Dharma spoken by the Tathāgata. Neither parents can liberate their
children from suffering, Hearing the Tathāgata's teaching, suffering is liberated
Worldly people follow desires, oppressed by various sufferings, The Tathāgata
teaches the Dharma, destroying birth and death. I now wish
to hear the Dharma, you should be silent." Then Punapavasu
and the ghost girl Uttara, All accepted their mother's words
listening silently and peacefully, Saying to their mother, "Excellent! We
also wish to hear the Dharma." This Perfectly Enlightened World-Honored
One, on the excellent mountain of Magadha, For the sake
of all beings, expounds the Dharma of liberation from suffering
Speaking of suffering and the cause of suffering, the cessation
of suffering and the path to the cessation of suffering
From these four noble truths, one peacefully proceeds to nirvāṇa
Mother, now just listen well to the Dharma spoken by
the World-Honored One.

「汝富那婆薮，　　欝多罗莫啼，令我得听闻，　　如来所说法。非父母能令，　　其子解脱苦，闻如来说法，　　其苦得解脱。世人随爱欲，　　为众苦所迫，如来为说法，　　令破坏生死。我今欲闻法，　　汝等当默然，时富那婆薮，　　鬼女欝多罗。悉受其母语，　　默然而静听，语母言善哉，　　我亦乐闻法。此正觉世尊，　　于摩竭胜山，为诸众生类，　　演说脱苦法。说苦及苦因，　　苦灭灭苦道，从此四圣谛，　　安隐趣涅槃。母今但善听，　　世尊所说法。」

At that time, the ghost mother Punapavasu spoke a verse:

时，富那婆薮鬼母即说偈言：

How wonderful, my wise son, you are able to follow
my mind. You, Punapavasu, praise the Buddha, the guide, well
You, Punapavasu, and you, Uttara, should give rise to a
mind of joy and approval, for I have already seen
the noble truths.

「奇哉智慧子，　　善能随我心，汝富那婆薮，　　善叹佛导师。汝富那婆薮，　　及汝欝多罗，当生随喜心，　　我已见圣谛。」

When the ghost mother Punapavasu spoke this verse, the ghost children and their children joyfully remained silent.

时，富那婆薮鬼母说是偈时，鬼子男女随喜默然。
