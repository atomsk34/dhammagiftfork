SĀ 1323

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1323（一三二三） 曼尼遮闻

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in the human realm of Magadha, and arrived at the abode of the Manesara ghost to spend the night with the great assembly.

一时，佛在摩竭提国人间游行，与诸大众至摩尼遮罗鬼住处夜宿。

At that time, the Manesara ghost gathered all the ghosts and spirits in one place.

尔时，摩尼遮罗鬼会诸鬼神，集在一处。

At that time, a woman brought fragrant garlands, decorations, and food to the abode of the Manesara ghost. Seeing the World-Honored One sitting in the abode of the Manesara ghost from afar, she thought, "I now see the Manesara ghost." She then spoke a verse:

时，有一女人，持香花鬘饰、饮食，至彼摩尼遮罗鬼神住处。彼女人遥见世尊在摩尼遮罗鬼神住处坐，见已，作是念：「我今现见摩尼遮罗鬼神。」即说偈言：

Excellent, Manesara, dwelling in the country of Magadha, the people of
Magadha have their wishes fulfilled. How can one always dwell in
peace and happiness in this world, and in the next world
how can one attain the bliss of rebirth in the heavens?

「善哉摩尼遮，　　住摩伽陀国，摩伽陀国人，　　所求悉如愿。云何于此世，　　常得安乐住？后世复云何，　　而得生天乐？」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse in reply:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

Do not be negligent, arrogant, or indulgent, using the Manesara ghost. If you cultivate
what you should do, you can attain the bliss of rebirth in the heavens.

「莫放逸慢恣，　　用摩尼鬼为，若自修所作，　　能得生天乐。」

At that time, the woman thought, "This is not the Manesara ghost, but the śramaṇa Gautama." Knowing this, she immediately offered fragrant flowers and garlands to the World-Honored One, bowed her head at his feet, and sat to one side. She spoke a verse, saying:

时，彼女人作是念：「此非摩尼遮罗鬼，是沙门瞿昙。」如是知已，即以香花鬘饰供养世尊，稽首礼足，退坐一面。而说偈言：

What path leads to peace and happiness? What practices should be cultivated, so that in this world one
is always at ease, and in the next life one is reborn in the heavens and experiences bliss?

「何道趣安乐？　　当修何等行，此世常安隐，　　后世生天乐？」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse in reply:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

Generosity and well-disciplined mind, delight in guarding the faculties
right view and cultivating virtuous conduct, associating with śramaṇas
Living by right livelihood, rebirth in the heavens in
the next life, what use are the sufferings of
the thirty-three heavens? One should unify the mind, cut
off desire and love. I shall explain the pure
nectar-like Dharma, listen well.

「布施善调心，　　乐执护诸根，正见修贤行，　　亲近于沙门。以正命自活，　　他世生天乐，何用三十三，　　诸天之苦网。但当一其心，　　断除于爱欲，我当说离垢，　　甘露法善听。」

At that time, the woman heard the World-Honored One's teaching, instruction, illumination, and delight. As is the Buddha's constant teaching, he spoke of generosity, upholding precepts, the merit of rebirth in the heavens, the taste and suffering of desire, defilements, purity, liberation, and the benefits of detachment. He gradually explained the pure Buddha Dharma. It is like a fresh and pure white cloth that is easily dyed. At that time, the woman was also like this. Right there on her seat, she attained the equal contemplation of the four noble truths: suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path.

时，彼女人闻世尊说法，示、教、照、喜。如佛常法，谓布施、持戒，生天之福，欲味欲患烦恼，清净、出要、远离，功德福利，次第演说清净佛法。譬如鲜净白［叠＊毛］，易染其色。时，彼女人亦复如是。即于坐上，于四圣谛得平等观苦、集、灭、道。

At that time, the woman saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, knew the Dharma, entered the Dharma, transcended all doubts, and was not dependent on others. She attained fearlessness in the true Dharma and Vinaya. She then rose from her seat, adjusted her robes, joined her palms, and said to the Buddha, "I am liberated, World-Honored One! I am liberated, Well-Gone One! From this day forth, I take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha of monks for as long as I live."

时，彼女人见法、得法、知法、入法，度诸疑惑，不由于他，于正法、律得无所畏。即从座起，整衣服，合掌白佛：「已度，世尊！已度，善逝！我从今日，尽寿命，归佛、归法、归比丘僧。」

At that time, the woman, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and followed the Buddha's teaching. She paid homage to the Buddha and departed.

时，彼女人闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，礼佛而去。
