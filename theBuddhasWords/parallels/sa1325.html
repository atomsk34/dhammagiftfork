SĀ 1325

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1325（一三二五） 受斋

Parallel to: SN 10.5 / SĀ² 2.324

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, there was a son of an upāsikā who observed the eight precepts but soon broke the precepts and was possessed by a ghost.

时，有优婆夷子，受八支斋，寻即犯戒，即为鬼神所持。

At that time, the upāsikā spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，优婆夷即说偈言：

On the fourteenth and fifteenth days, and on the eighth day of each
month, the month of auspicious omens and spiritual powers, well-abiding in the eight
precepts, observing the precepts and vows, not being possessed by ghosts, I have
asked the World-Honored One about this many times, and he has said this.

「十四十五日，　　及月分八日，神通瑞应月，　　八支善正受，受持于斋戒，　　不为鬼所持，我昔数咨问，　　世尊作是说。」

At that time, the ghost spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，彼鬼即说偈言：

On the fourteenth and fifteenth days, and on the eighth day of
each month, the month of auspicious omens and spiritual powers, well-abiding in
the eight precepts. Abiding in purity and solemnity, well-guarding the virtues of
the precepts, not being toyed with by ghosts, it is good to
hear from the Buddha. You should say to release, and I will
release your child. All those with slow and lax karma, defiled conduct
and suffering practice, with impure pure conduct, will not attain the great
fruit. It is like pulling sugarcane grass, holding it loosely will hurt
the hand. If a śramaṇa touches evil, he will fall into hell
It is like pulling sugarcane grass, grasping it tightly will not hurt
the hand. If a śramaṇa well restrains himself, he will reach parinirvāṇa.

「十四十五日，　　及月分八日，神足瑞应月，　　八支修正受。斋肃清净住，　　戒德善守护，不为鬼戏弄，　　善哉从佛闻。汝当说言放，　　我当放汝子。诸有慢缓业，　　染污行苦行，梵行不清净，　　终不得大果。譬如拔菅草，　　执缓则伤手，沙门行恶触，　　当堕地狱中。譬如拔菅草，　　急捉不伤手，沙门善摄持，　　则到般涅槃。」

At that time, the ghost immediately released the upāsikā's son. Then the upāsikā spoke a verse to her son, saying:

时，彼鬼神即放优婆夷子。尔时，优婆夷说偈告子言：

Son, now listen to me, speaking of what the ghost said
If there are slow and lax karmas, defiled practices and suffering
practices, with impure pure conduct, one will not attain the great
fruit. It is like pulling sugarcane grass, holding it loosely will
hurt the hand. If a śramaṇa arouses evil contact, he will
fall into hell. It is like grasping sugarcane grass tightly, then
it will not hurt the hand. If a śramaṇa well grasps
and protects, he will attain parinirvāṇa.

「子汝今听我，　　说彼鬼神说，若有慢缓业，　　秽污修苦行。不清净梵行，　　彼不得大果，譬如拔葌草，　　执缓则伤手。沙门起恶触，　　当堕地狱中，如急执葌草，　　则不伤其手。沙门善执护，　　逮得般涅槃。」

At that time, the upāsikā's son, having been awakened in this way, shaved his beard and hair, put on the kāṣāya robe, with right faith left home to study the path, but his mind was not joyful. He returned to his own home. His mother saw him from afar and spoke a verse, saying:

时，彼优婆夷子如是觉悟已，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，正信非家，出家学道，心不得乐，还归自家，母遥见子，而说偈言：

Having left home to renounce the world, why return to the village? Like burning
a house and quickly taking out the valuables, how could one return to the fire?

「迈世而出家，　　何为还聚落，烧舍急出财，　　岂还投火中？」

The bhikṣu son spoke a verse in reply, saying:

其子比丘说偈答言：

Only thinking of my mother's death, the living and the dead do not see each other
Therefore, I have come back to pay my respects. Why do you see your son with displeasure?

「但念母命终，　　存亡不相见，故来还瞻视，　　何见子不欢。」

At that time, the upāsikā mother spoke a verse in reply,

时，母优婆夷说偈答言：

Giving up desire and going forth, then wanting to eat and drink
again, therefore I am sad and worried, fearing to be controlled by Māra.

「舍欲而出家，　　还欲服食之，是故我忧悲，　　恐随魔自在。」

At that time, the upāsikā awakened her son like this and like this; like this, her son returned to a secluded place, diligently contemplated, eliminated all afflictions and bonds, and attained the fruit of arahantship.

是时，优婆夷如是如是发悟其子；如是，其子还空闲处，精勤思惟，断除一切烦恼结缚，得阿罗汉果证。
