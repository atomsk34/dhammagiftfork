SĀ 1326

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1326（一三二六） 矌野

Parallel to: SN 10.12 / Snp 1.10 / SĀ 603 / SĀ² 2.325

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling among the people of Magadha and spent the night in a place inhabited by alligators.

一时，佛在摩竭提国人间游行，到阿臈鬼住处夜宿。

At that time, the alligators had gathered together with the ghosts and spirits. Then, a ghost named Khadga saw the World-Honored One spending the night in the alligator's dwelling place. Having seen this, he went to the alligator and said, "Lord of the village! You have gained great benefit, for the Tathāgata is spending the night in your dwelling place."

时，阿臈鬼集会诸鬼神。时，有竭昙鬼见世尊在阿臈鬼住处夜宿。见已，至阿臈鬼所，语阿臈鬼言：「聚落主！汝获大利，如来宿汝住处。」

The alligator said, "Is a living person staying in my house today? I will now find out if it is the Tathāgata or not."

阿臈鬼言：「生人今日在我舍住耶？今当令知，为是如来？为非如来？」

Then, when the alligators and the ghosts and spirits had finished their gathering, they returned to their own homes and said to the World-Honored One, "Leave, ascetic!"

时，阿臈鬼诸鬼神聚会毕，还归自家，语世尊曰：「出去！沙门！」

At that time, the World-Honored One left the house because it was someone else's home.

尔时，世尊以他家故，即出其舍。

The alligator ghost said again, "Ascetic! Come in!" The Buddha then entered again. It was to eliminate arrogance, and so it happened three times.

阿臈鬼复言：「沙门！来入！」佛即还入。以灭慢故，如是再三。

At that time, the alligator ghost said to the World-Honored One for the fourth time, "Ascetic! Get out!"

时，阿臈鬼第四复语世尊言：「沙门！出去！」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the alligator ghost, "Head of the village! You have invited me three times, but I will not go out now."

尔时，世尊语阿臈鬼言：「聚落主！已三见请，今不复出。」

The alligator ghost said, "Now I ask the ascetic. If the ascetic answers me and makes me happy, that is good; if he cannot make me happy, I will destroy his heart, tear open his chest, and make hot blood flow from his face. I will grab his two hands and throw him to the other shore of the Ganges River!"

阿臈鬼言：「今问沙门，沙门答我，能令我喜者善；不能令我喜者，我当坏其心，裂其胸，亦令热血从其面出，执持两手掷著恒水彼岸！」

The World-Honored One said, "Head of the village! I do not see any gods, demons, Brahmas, ascetics, Brahmins, devas, humans, or anyone else in the world who can destroy my heart, tear open my chest, make hot blood flow from my face, grab my two hands, and throw me to the other shore of the Ganges River. However, head of the village! You may now ask, and I will explain it to you, making your mind joyful."

世尊告言：「聚落主！我不见诸天、魔、梵、沙门、婆罗门、天神、世人，有能坏我心，裂我胸，令我热血从面而出，执持两手掷著恒水彼岸者。然，聚落主！汝今但问，当为汝说。令汝心喜。」

At that time, the alligator ghost spoke a verse to ask the Buddha:

时，阿臈鬼说偈问佛：

What is called the supreme human affair? Practicing what
Dharma can one attain the blissful result? What is
the most delicious? How does one excel in longevity?

「说何等名为，　　胜士夫事物？行于何等法，　　得安乐果报？何等为美味？　　云何寿中胜？」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse in reply:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

Pure faith is the supreme human affair. Practicing the Dharma leads
to blissful results. Liberation is the most delicious. Wisdom eliminates old
age and death. This is the best in terms of longevity.

「净信为最胜，　　士夫之事物，行法得乐果，　　解脱味中上，智慧除老死，　　是为寿中胜。」

At that time, the hungry ghost Alaputa also spoke a verse:

时，阿臈鬼复说偈言：

How can one attain fame?" As stated in the above verses.

「云何得名称？」　　如上所说偈。

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse in reply:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

The fame of upholding precepts flows." As stated in the above verses.

「持戒名称流。」　　如上所说偈。

At that time, the hungry ghost Alaputa spoke a verse:

时，阿臈鬼复说偈言：

How many dharmas arise in the world? How many dharmas are agreeable and acceptable? How
many dharmas are grasped and accepted in the world? How many dharmas diminish in the world?

「几法起世间？　　几法相顺可？世几法取受？　　世几法损减？」

At that time, the World-Honored One replied in verse:

尔时，世尊以偈答言：

The world arises from six dharmas, six dharmas are agreeable and acceptable, the
world grasps and accepts six dharmas, the world diminishes due to six dharmas.

「世六法等起，　　六法相顺可，世六法取受，　　世六法损减。」

The hungry ghost Alaputa further asked the Buddha in verse:

阿臈鬼复说偈问佛：

Who can cross the currents, diligently practicing day and
night? Without support or dwelling, who can avoid sinking?

「谁能度诸流，　　昼夜勤方便？无攀无住处，　　孰能不沉没？」

At that time, the World-Honored One replied in verse:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

One who is fully endowed with all precepts, wisdom, and
proper acceptance, with right mindfulness and inner contemplation, can cross
the difficult-to-cross currents. Not delighting in the five desires, also
transcending attachment to form, without support or dwelling, can avoid sinking.

「一切戒具足，　　智慧善正受，正念内思惟，　　能度难度流。不乐于五欲，　　亦超度色爱，无攀无住处，　　是能不没溺。」

At that time, the hungry ghost Alaputa again asked the Buddha in verse:

时，阿臈鬼复说偈问佛：

By what method can one cross the currents? By what can one cross the
great ocean? By what can one abandon suffering? By what can one attain purity?

「以何法度流？　　以何度大海？以何舍离苦？　　以何得清净？」

At that time, the World-Honored One replied in verse:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

By faith one crosses the currents of the river, by non-distraction one crosses
the ocean, by diligence one can remove suffering, by wisdom one attains purity
You should ask further about the Dharma of the śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, the
Dharma that is without fault, the true and real, and the bestowal of discipline.

「以信度河流，　　不放逸度海，精进能除苦，　　以慧得清净。汝当更问余，　　沙门梵志法，其法无有过，　　真谛施调伏。」

At that time, the Rākṣasa ghost again asked the Buddha in verse:

时，阿臈鬼复说偈问佛：

Why bother asking about the Dharma of other śramaṇas and
brāhmaṇas? It is said to be the most excellent person
to manifest the great Dharma torch. In that Jetavana Grove
one should always repay the kindness, telling us the perfectly
enlightened, the unsurpassed guiding teacher. I will then go from
village to village, personally attending the perfectly enlightened one, listening
to the Dharma he speaks.

「何烦更问余，　　沙门梵志法，即曰最胜士，　　以显大法炬。于彼竭昙摩，　　常当报其恩，告我等正觉，　　无上导御师。我即日当行，　　从村而至村，亲侍等正觉，　　听受所说法。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished. The Rākṣasa ghost was delighted and joyfully paid homage before departing.

佛说此经已，阿臈鬼欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
