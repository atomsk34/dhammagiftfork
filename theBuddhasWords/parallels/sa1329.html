SĀ 1329

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 1329（一三二九） 七岳雪山

Parallel to: Snp 1.9 / SĀ² 2.328

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, the Sāḍagiri and Hemavati devas made an agreement, saying, "If there is a treasure in our palace, we must tell each other; if we do not tell each other, we will be guilty of breaking the agreement."

时，有娑多耆利天神、醯魔波低天神共作约誓：「若其宫中有宝物出者，必当相语；不相语者，得违约罪。」

At that time, there was an unprecedented treasure in the palace of the Hemavati deva, a padma flower with a thousand petals, as large as a chariot wheel, with a golden stem.

时，醯魔波低天神宫中有未曾有宝，波昙摩华出，华有千叶，大如车轮，金色宝茎。

At that time, the Hemavati deva sent a messenger to inform the Sāḍagiri deva, saying, "Village chief! Now there is an unprecedented treasure in my palace, a padma flower with a thousand petals, as large as a chariot wheel, with a golden stem. Please come and see it!"

时，醯魔波低天神遣使告语娑多耆利：「聚落主！今我宫中忽生未曾有宝，波昙摩花，华有千叶，大如车轮，金色宝茎，可来观看！」

The Sāḍagiri deva sent a messenger to the Hemavati deva's abode, saying, "Village chief! What use are these padma flowers worth a hundred thousand? Now there is an unprecedented treasure in my palace - the appearance of the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, Perfect in Knowledge and Conduct, Well-Gone, Knower of the Secular World, Unsurpassed Person, Supreme Trainer, Teacher of Gods and Men, Buddha, World-Honored One. You should come and pay homage and make offerings."

娑多耆利天神遣使诣醯魔波低舍，告言：「聚落主！用是波昙摩百千为？今我宫中有未曾有宝——大波昙摩出，所谓如来、应、等正觉、明行足、善逝、世间解、无上士、调御丈夫、天人师、佛世尊，汝便可来奉事供养。」

At that time, the Hemavati deva immediately went with five hundred attendants to the Sāḍagiri deva's abode and asked in verse:

时，醯魔波低天神即与五百眷属往诣娑多耆利天神所，说偈问言：

On the fifteenth day, a good time, the night is auspicious and joyful
We should speak of what fast to observe, and receive it from an arhat.

「十五日良时，　　天夜遇欢会，当说受何斋，　　从阿罗汉受。」

At that time, Sāḍagiri replied in verse:

时，娑多耆利说偈答言：

Today, the Buddha, the World-Honored One, is in the excellent country of Magadha
dwelling in the Bamboo Grove of Kalandaka in the city of Rājagṛha, expounding
the subtle Dharma, eliminating the suffering of sentient beings, the suffering of suffering
the origin of suffering, the complete realization of the cessation of suffering, and
the eightfold noble path that leads to the cessation of suffering, the peaceful
path to nirvāṇa. I shall go to make offerings to my arhat World-Honored One.

「今日佛世尊，　　在摩竭胜国，住于王舍城，　　迦兰陀竹园，演说微妙法，　　灭除众生苦，苦苦及苦集，　　苦灭尽作证，八圣出苦道，　　安隐趣涅槃，当往设供养，　　我罗汉世尊。」

Hīmavati spoke in verse, asking:

醯魔波低说偈问言：

Do they have the aspiration to compassionately benefit sentient
beings? Are their thoughts of acceptance and non-acceptance equal?

「彼有心愿乐，　　慈济众生不，彼于受不受，　　心想平等不？」

Sataghari spoke in verse, answering:

娑多耆利说偈答言：

Their wonderful aspiration and compassionate mind liberates all sentient
beings. Their thoughts of acceptance and non-acceptance are always equal.

「彼妙愿慈心，　　度一切众生，于诸受不受，　　心想常平等。」

At that time, Hemavati spoke in verse, asking:

时，醯魔波低说偈问言：

Do you have complete understanding and realization, having already accomplished
the path, with all taints completely extinguished, not accepting future existence?

「为具足明达，　　已行成就不，诸漏永灭尽，　　不受后有耶？」

Sataghari replied in verse:

娑多耆利说偈答言：

Understanding and realization are well-fulfilled, the right practice is already accomplished
all taints are completely extinguished, and future existence is no longer accepted.

「明达善具足，　　正行已成就，诸漏永已尽，　　不复受后有。」

Himāvati asked in verse:

醯摩波低说偈问言：

Is the Muni's mental activity fulfilled, and his bodily and verbal
actions? Are understanding and practice fully complete, and praised with the Dharma?

「牟尼意行满，　　及身口业耶？明行悉具足，　　以法赞叹耶？」

Sataghari replied in verse:

娑多耆利说偈答言：

The Muni's mind is complete, and his bodily and verbal actions are
fulfilled. Understanding and practice are fully complete, and praised with the Dharma.

「具足牟尼心，　　及业身口满，明行悉具足，　　以法而赞叹。」

Himāvati asked in verse:

醯摩波低说偈问言：

Far from harming living beings, not giving, not taking
not for the sake of indulgence, not apart from meditation?

「远离于害生，　　不与不取不，为远于放荡，　　不离禅思不？」

Sataghari spoke in verse:

娑多耆利复说偈言：

Always not harming living beings, not giving, not falsely
taking, far from indulgence, day and night always contemplating meditation.

「常不害众生，　　不与不妄取，远离于放荡，　　日夜常思禅。」

Himavati spoke in verse and asked:

醯魔波低复说偈问言：

Are you not fond of the five desires, is your mind not turbid
and confused, do you have the pure Dharma eye, have you extinguished ignorance?

「为不乐五欲，　　心不浊乱不，有清净法眼，　　灭尽愚痴不？」

Sataghari spoke in verse and replied:

娑多耆利说偈答言：

The mind is always not fond of desire, and there is no turbid or
confused mind. The Buddha's Dharma eye is pure, and ignorance is completely extinguished without remainder.

「心常不乐欲，　　亦无浊乱心，佛法眼清净，　　愚痴尽无余。」

Himavati spoke in verse and asked again:

醯魔波低复说偈问言：

To be sincere and not speak falsely, to have no harsh
words, to attain non-separation in speech, to have no insincere speech no?

「至诚不妄语，　　麁澁言无有，得无别离说，　　无不诚说不？」

Sataghari spoke in verse and replied:

娑多耆利说偈答言：

To be sincere and not speak falsely, to have no harsh words, to
not be separated from close friends, to always speak according to the Dharma.

「至诚不妄语，　　亦无麁澁言，不离他亲厚，　　常说如法言。」

Himāpati then spoke in verse and asked:

醯摩波低复说偈问言：

Do you uphold pure precepts, right mindfulness, and tranquility? Do you
possess the liberation of equality, the great wisdom of the Tathāgata?

「为持清净戒，　　正念寂灭不，具足等解脱，　　如来大智不？」

Sataghari spoke in verse and replied:

娑多耆利说偈答言：

Pure precepts are fully possessed, right mindfulness is always tranquil, liberation through
equality is accomplished, and the great wisdom of the Tathāgata is attained.

「净戒悉具足，　　正念常寂静，等解脱成就，　　得如来大智。」

Himavati spoke in verse and asked again:

醯魔波低复说偈问言：

Clear understanding is fully possessed, right practice is already pure, all
taints are exhausted, and one will not again experience future existence.

「明达悉具足，　　正行已清净，所有诸漏尽，　　不复受后有。」

Sataghari spoke in verse and replied:

娑多耆利说偈答言：

Clear understanding is fully possessed, right practice is already pure
all taints are exhausted, and there will be no further rebirth.

「明达悉具足，　　正行已清净，一切诸漏尽，　　无复后生有。」

Himavati spoke in verse again:

醯魔波低复说偈言：

The Muni is endowed with good mind, and with the traces of bodily and
verbal karma, he has accomplished clear practice and conduct, therefore I praise his Dharma.

「牟尼善心具，　　及身口业迹，明行悉成就，　　故赞叹其法。」

Sataghari spoke in verse in reply:

娑多耆利说偈答言：

The Muni is endowed with good mind, and with the traces of bodily and
verbal karma, he has accomplished clear practice and conduct, therefore I praise his Dharma.

「牟尼善心具，　　及身口业迹，明行悉成就，　　赞叹于其法。」

Himavati spoke in verse again:

醯魔波低复说偈言：

The deer of the Inayana, the supreme mark of the sage, eating little
and abandoning the body out of greed, the Muni dwells in the forest
in meditation. You should now go together and pay homage to that Gautama.

「伊尼延鹿[跳-兆＋专，　　仙人之胜相，少食舍身贪，　　牟尼处林禅，汝今当共行，　　敬礼彼瞿昙。」

At that time, surrounded by hundreds of thousands of ghost spirits and retinues, Satagiri and Himavati quickly arrived before the Buddha, paid homage and made offerings, arranged their robes, bared their right shoulders, joined their palms in respect, and spoke in verse:

时，有百千鬼神眷属围绕，娑多耆利、醯魔波低速至佛前礼拜供养，整衣服，偏袒右肩，合掌敬礼，而说偈言：

The deer of the Inayana, the supreme mark of the
sage, eating little and without greed, the Muni delights in
forest meditation. We have now come to ask Gautama, the
lion walks alone, the great dragon has no fear. We
have now come to ask, the Muni, please resolve our
doubts, how can one attain liberation from suffering? How can
one be liberated from suffering? Please explain liberation, where does suffering cease?

「伊尼延鹿[跳-兆＋专，　　仙人之胜相，少食无贪嗜，　　牟尼乐林禅。我等今故来，　　请问于瞿昙，师子独游步，　　大龙无恐畏。今故来请问，　　牟尼愿决疑，云何得出苦？　　云何苦解脱？唯愿说解脱，　　苦于何所灭？」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke in verse:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

The five sense pleasures and the sixth mental pleasure in the
world, being free from desire for them, one is liberated from
all suffering. Thus one is liberated from suffering, thus one is
liberated from suffering. Now I answer your question, suffering ceases from this.

「世五欲功德，　　及说第六意，于彼欲无贪，　　解脱一切苦。如是从苦出，　　如是解脱苦，今答汝所问，　　苦从此而灭。」

Sataghari and Himāvati spoke in verse and asked the Buddha:

娑多耆利、醯魔波低复说偈问佛：

From where do the springs turn back? Why do the evil paths not turn
back? Where do the sufferings and pleasures of the world come to an end?

「泉从何转还，　　恶道何不转，世间诸苦乐，　　于何而灭尽？」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke in verse and replied:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

The sense bases of the eyes, ears, nose, tongue, body, and
mind, as well as form and name, are completely extinguished without
remainder. The springs turn back there, the paths do not turn
back there, and the sufferings and pleasures there attain complete extinction without remainder.

「眼耳鼻舌身，　　及以意入处，于彼名及色，　　永灭尽无余。于彼泉转还，　　于彼道不转，于彼苦及乐，　　得无余灭尽。」

Sataghari and Hemavati further spoke in verse and asked the Buddha:

娑多耆利、醯魔波低复说偈问佛：

How many dharmas arise in the world? How many dharmas are in harmony with the world?
How many dharmas grasp and accept the world? How many dharmas cause the world to cease?

「世间几法起？　　几法世和合？几法取受世？　　几法令世灭？」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke in verse and replied:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

Six dharmas arise in the world, six dharmas are in harmony with the world
six dharmas grasp and accept the world, six dharmas cause the world to be destroyed.

「六法起世间，　　六法世和合，六法取受世，　　六法世损灭。」

Sataghari and Himāvati spoke in verse and asked:

娑多耆利、醯魔波低复说偈问：

How does one cross the streams, diligently applying skillful means day and
night, without clinging or dwelling, yet not sinking into the deep abyss?

「云何度诸流，　　日夜勤方便，无攀无住处，　　而不溺深渊？」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke in verse and replied:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

One who is fully endowed with all precepts, with wisdom and
proper acceptance, who contemplates and focuses the mind, can cross the
deep abyss. Not delighting in thoughts of desire, also transcending the
bonds of form, without clinging or dwelling, does not sink into
the deep abyss.

「一切戒具足，　　智慧善正受，如思惟系念，　　是能度深渊。不乐诸欲想，　　亦超色诸结，无攀无住处，　　不溺于深渊。」

Sataghari and Hemavati also spoke in verse and asked the Buddha:

娑多耆利、醯魔波低复说偈问佛：

What method crosses the streams? How does one cross the great
ocean? How does one abandon suffering? How does one attain purity?

「何法度诸流？　　以何度大海？云何舍离苦？　　云何得清净？」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke in verse and replied:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

Right faith crosses the streams, non-distraction crosses the
ocean, diligence can cut off suffering, wisdom attains purity.

「正信度河流，　　不放逸度海，精进能断苦，　　智慧得清净。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke in verse again:

尔时，世尊复说偈言：

You may ask other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas about the true teaching
of giving and taming. Apart from this, there is no other path.

「汝可更问余，　　沙门梵志法，真实施调伏，　　除此更无法。」

Then Himavat spoke in verse again:

醯魔波低复说偈言：

What else can I ask about the teachings of śramaṇas and
brāhmaṇas? Today, Mahāprajñā has fully explained the good guidance. I will
now repay the kindness of Sādhukīrti, who was able to inform
us of the guiding teacher. We will go from village to
village, from house to house, following the Buddha, serving, paying homage
and making offerings, and hearing the true Dharma from the Buddha
These hundreds of thousands of ghosts and spirits all join their
palms in respect. All take refuge in the Buddha, the great
sage. Having attained the unsurpassed name, they will surely see the
true meaning, accomplish great wisdom, and not be attached to desires
The wise should observe the savior of the world, who has
attained the path of the noble ones. This is the great sage.

「更余何所问，　　沙门梵志法，大精进今日，　　已具善开导。我今当报彼，　　娑多耆利恩，能以导御师，　　告语于我等。我当诣村村，　　家家而随佛，承事礼供养，　　从佛闻正法。此百千鬼神，　　悉合掌恭敬，一切归依佛，　　牟尼之大师。得无上之名，　　必见真实义，成就大智慧，　　于欲不染着。慧者当观察，　　救护世间者，得贤圣道迹，　　是则大仙人。」

After the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, Sādhukīrti, Himavat, and their retinues, as well as the five hundred ghosts and spirits, having heard what the Buddha had said, all greatly rejoiced, joyfully paid homage to the Buddha, and departed.

佛说是经已，娑多耆利、醯魔波低及诸眷属] cf.【丽】五百鬼神闻佛所说，皆大欢喜，随喜礼佛而去。
