SĀ 19

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 19（一九） 结系

Parallel to: SN 22.70

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, a certain bhikṣu rose from his seat, bowed to the Buddha, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Please briefly teach me the essential Dharma. Having heard the Dharma, I will dwell alone in a quiet place, diligently contemplating and not being negligent. Having dwelled diligently, I will contemplate the reason why a good man, with right faith, leaves home for homelessness, and know for myself that I will not be reborn again."

尔时，有异比丘从坐起，为佛作礼，而白佛言：「世尊！为我略说法要，我闻法已，当独一静处，专精思惟，不放逸住。不放逸住已，思惟：『所以善男子正信家非家出家，乃至自知不受后有。』」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to that bhikṣu, "Excellent! Excellent! You now say this: 'Excellent, World-Honored One! Please briefly teach me the essential Dharma. Having heard the Dharma, I will dwell alone in a quiet place, diligently contemplating and not being negligent, and know for myself that I will not be reborn again.'"

尔时，世尊告彼比丘：「善哉！善哉！汝今作是说：『善哉！世尊！为我略说法要，我闻法已，当独一静处，专精思惟，不放逸住，乃至自知不受后有。』耶？」

The bhikṣu said to the Buddha, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘白佛言：「如是，世尊！」

The Buddha told the bhikṣu, "Listen carefully! Listen carefully! Consider it well, and I will explain it to you. Bhikṣu! The bound and fettered dharmas should be quickly eliminated and cut off. Having eliminated those dharmas, there will be beneficial meaning and long-lasting peace and happiness."

佛告比丘：「谛听！谛听！善思念之，当为汝说。比丘！结所系法宜速除断，断彼法已，以义饶益，长夜安乐。」

At that time, the bhikṣu said to the Buddha, "I understand, World-Honored One! I understand, Well-Gone One!"

时，彼比丘白佛言：「知已。世尊！知已。善逝！」

The Buddha said to the bhikṣu, "How did you understand the meaning of my brief teaching?"

佛告比丘：「汝云何于我略说法中，广解其义？」

The bhikṣu said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Form is a bound phenomenon, a bound phenomenon that should be quickly eliminated and severed. Having severed that phenomenon, there is benefit and long-lasting peace. Likewise, feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness are bound phenomena, bound phenomena that should be quickly eliminated and severed. Having severed those phenomena, there is benefit and long-lasting peace. Therefore, I have understood the meaning of the World-Honored One's brief teaching."

比丘白佛言：「世尊！色是结所系法，是结所系法宜速除断。断彼法已，以义饶益，长夜安乐。如是受、想、行、识结所系法，是结所系法宜速除断。断彼法已，以义饶益，长夜安乐。是故我于世尊略说法中，广解其义。」

The Buddha said to the bhikṣu, "Excellent! Excellent! You have understood the meaning of my brief teaching. Why is that? Form is a bound phenomenon, a phenomenon that should be quickly eliminated and severed. Having severed that phenomenon, there is benefit and long-lasting peace. Likewise, feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness are bound phenomena, phenomena that should be quickly eliminated and severed. Having severed those phenomena, there is benefit and long-lasting peace."

佛告比丘：「善哉！善哉！汝于我略说法中，广解其义。所以者何？色是结所系法，此法宜速除断，断彼法已，以义饶益，长夜安乐。如是受、想、行、识是结所系法，此法宜速除断，断彼法已，以义饶益，长夜安乐。」

At that time, the bhikṣu, having heard the Buddha's teaching, was greatly delighted. He bowed to the Buddha and left. He stayed in a solitary place, diligently contemplating and not being negligent. He attained liberation of the mind and became an arhat.

时，彼比丘闻佛所说，心大欢喜，礼佛而退，独一静处，专精思惟，不放逸住，乃至心得解脱，成阿罗汉。
