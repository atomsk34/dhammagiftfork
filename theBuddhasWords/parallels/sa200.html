SĀ 200

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 200（二〇〇） 罗睺罗

Parallel to: MN 147 (~) / SN 35.121 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Rāhula went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha’s feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, “Excellent, World-Honored One! Please teach the Dharma for me. Having heard the Dharma, I will dwell in solitude, diligently contemplating and not being negligent. Having dwelt in solitude, diligently contemplating and not being negligent, I will think thus: ‘The reason why a noble son shaves his head and beard, with right faith leaves home, goes forth, and practices the holy life, is to see the Dharma, to know and realize for himself: “My birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, there will be no more of this state of existence.”’”

尔时，尊者罗睺罗往诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面，白佛言：「善哉！世尊！为我说法，我闻法已，独一静处，专精思惟，不放逸住；独一静处，专精思惟，不放逸住已，如是思惟：『所以族姓子剃除须发，正信非家，出家学道，修持梵行，见法自知作证：「我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。」』」

At that time, the World-Honored One observed Rāhula's mind. The wisdom of liberation was not yet ripe, and he was not yet able to receive the supreme Dharma. He asked Rāhula, "Have you taught others the five aggregates of grasping?"

尔时，世尊观察罗睺罗心，解脱慧未熟，未堪任受增上法。问罗睺罗言：「汝以授人五受阴未？」

Rāhula said to the Buddha, "Not yet, World-Honored One!"

罗睺罗白佛：「未也。世尊！」

The Buddha told Rāhula, "You should teach others the five aggregates of grasping."

佛告罗睺罗：「汝当为人演说五受阴。」

At that time, Rāhula received the Buddha's instruction and at another time taught others the five aggregates of grasping. Having taught, he returned to where the Buddha was, bowed at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I have already taught others the five aggregates of grasping. I only wish that the World-Honored One would teach me the Dharma. Having heard the Dharma, I will dwell alone in a quiet place, diligently contemplating and not being negligent, until I know for myself that I will not be reborn again."

尔时，罗睺罗受佛教已，于异时为人演说五受阴，说已，还诣佛所，稽首佛足，退住一面，白佛言：「世尊！我已为人说五受阴，唯愿世尊为我说法，我闻法已，独一静处，专精思惟，不放逸住，乃至自知不受后有。」

At that time, the World-Honored One again observed Rāhula's mind. The wisdom of liberation was not yet ripe, and he was not yet able to receive the supreme Dharma. He asked Rāhula, "Have you taught others the six sense bases?"

尔时，世尊复观察罗睺罗心，解脱智未熟，不堪任受增上法。问罗睺罗言：「汝为人说六入处未？」

Rāhula said to the Buddha, "Not yet, World-Honored One!"

罗睺罗白佛：「未也。世尊！」

The Buddha told Rāhula, "You should teach others the six sense bases."

佛告罗睺罗：「汝当为人演说六入处。」

At that time, Rāhula, at another time, taught others the six sense bases. After teaching the six sense bases, he came to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I have already taught others the six sense bases. I only wish that the World-Honored One would teach me the Dharma. After hearing the Dharma, I will dwell alone in a quiet place, diligently contemplating and not being negligent, until I know for myself that I will not be reborn again."

尔时，罗睺罗于异时为人演说六入处，说六入处已，来诣佛所，稽首礼足，退住一面，白佛言：「世尊！我已为人演说六入处，唯愿世尊为我说法，我闻法已，当独一静处，专精思惟，不放逸住，乃至自知不受后有。」

At that time, the World-Honored One observed Rāhula's mind. The wisdom of liberation was not yet ripe, and he was not yet able to receive the supreme Dharma. He asked Rāhula, "Have you already taught others the nidāna Dharma?"

尔时，世尊观察罗睺罗心，解脱智未熟，不堪任受增上法。问罗睺罗言：「汝已为人说尼陀那法未？」

Rāhula said to the Buddha, "No, World-Honored One!"

罗睺罗白佛言：「未也。世尊！」

The Buddha told Rāhula, "You should teach others the nidāna Dharma."

佛告罗睺罗：「汝当为人演说尼陀那法。」

At that time, Rāhula, having taught the nidāna Dharma to others on another occasion, came to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Please teach me the Dharma. Having heard the Dharma, I will dwell in solitude, diligently contemplating and not being negligent, until I know for myself that I will not be reborn again."

尔时，罗睺罗于异时为人广说尼陀那法已，来诣佛所，稽首礼足，退住一面，白佛言：「世尊！为我说法，我闻法已，独一静处，专精思惟，不放逸住，乃至自知不受后有。」

At that time, the World-Honored One again observed Rāhula's mind, knowing that the wisdom of liberation was not yet ripe... He then told Rāhula, "You should contemplate the meaning of the above teachings alone in a quiet place."

尔时，世尊复观察罗睺罗心，解脱智未熟……广说乃至告罗睺罗言：「汝当于上所说诸法，独于一静处，专精思惟，观察其义。」

At that time, Rāhula received the Buddha's instructions and contemplated and weighed the teachings he had heard, observing their meaning. He thought, "All these teachings lead to nirvāṇa, flow into nirvāṇa, and abide in nirvāṇa in the end." At that time, Rāhula went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I have already contemplated and weighed the teachings I have heard alone in a quiet place, observing their meaning. I know that all these teachings lead to nirvāṇa, flow into nirvāṇa, and abide in nirvāṇa in the end."

尔时，罗睺罗受佛教勅，如上所闻法、所说法思惟称量，观察其义，作是念：「此诸法一切皆顺趣涅槃、流注涅槃、后住涅槃。」尔时，罗睺罗往诣佛所，稽首礼足，退住一面，白佛言：「世尊！我已于如上所闻法、所说法独一静处，思惟称量，观察其义，知此诸法皆顺趣涅槃、流注涅槃、后住涅槃。」

At that time, the World-Honored One observed Rāhula's mind, knowing that the wisdom of liberation was ripe and he was fit to receive the supreme teachings. He told Rāhula, "Rāhula! All is impermanent. What are the impermanent phenomena? They are the impermanence of the eye, form, eye consciousness, eye contact..." and so on, as extensively explained above.

尔时，世尊观察罗睺罗心，解脱智熟，堪任受增上法。告罗睺罗言：「罗睺罗！一切无常。何等法无常？谓眼无常，若色、眼识、眼触……」如上无常广说。

At that time, Rāhula, having heard the Buddha's teachings, joyfully followed and practiced them. He bowed to the Buddha and left.

尔时，罗睺罗闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，礼佛而退。

At that time, Rāhula, having received the Buddha's teachings, stayed in a solitary place, diligently contemplating and practicing diligently. He thought, "The reason why a noble son shaves his beard and hair, puts on the monastic robe, with right faith leaves home to practice the path, and purely cultivates the brahma-conduct, is to see the Dharma and personally know and realize: 'My birth is ended, the brahma-conduct is established, what was to be done is done, I know for myself that I will not be subject to future existence.'" He attained arahantship and his mind was well liberated.

尔时，罗睺罗受佛教已，独一静处，专精思惟，不放逸住：「所以族姓子剃除须发，著袈裟衣，正信非家，出家学道，纯修梵行，乃至见法自知作证：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』」成阿罗汉，心善解脱。

After the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, Rāhula, having heard the Buddha's teachings, joyfully practiced them.

佛说此经已，罗睺罗闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
