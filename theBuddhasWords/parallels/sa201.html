SĀ 201

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 201（二〇一） 比丘尽一切结

Parallel to: SN 35.53–59 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, a certain bhikṣu came to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha’s feet, stood to one side, and said to the Buddha, “World-Honored One! How does one know and see in order to quickly attain the exhaustion of the taints?”

时，有异比丘来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退住一面，白佛言：「世尊！云何知、云何见，次第疾得漏尽？」

At that time, the World-Honored One told that bhikṣu, "You should contemplate impermanence. What are the impermanent dharmas? They are the impermanence of the eye, form, eye consciousness, eye contact, and the feelings that arise from eye contact as conditions, whether pleasant, unpleasant, or neither pleasant nor unpleasant. You should contemplate their impermanence. The ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind are also impermanent. The dharmas, mind consciousness, mind contact, and the feelings that arise from mind contact as conditions, whether pleasant, unpleasant, or neither pleasant nor unpleasant, are also impermanent. Bhikṣu! Knowing and seeing in this way, one will gradually exhaust the taints."

尔时，世尊告彼比丘：「当正观无常。何等法无常？谓眼无常，若色、眼识、眼触、眼触因缘生受，若苦、若乐、不苦不乐，当观无常。耳、鼻、舌、身、意当观无常。若法、意识、意触、意触因缘生受，若苦、若乐、不苦不乐，彼亦无常。比丘！如是知、如是见，次第尽有漏。」

At that time, that bhikṣu, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully bowed and departed.

时，彼比丘闻佛所说，欢喜作礼而去。

Thus, the bhikṣu spoke the sūtra. If there are differences, it is said: "How does one know and see, gradually exhausting all bonds, cutting off all fetters, cutting off all defilements, cutting off all upper afflictions, cutting off all bonds, cutting off all taints, cutting off all yokes, cutting off all grasping, cutting off all contacts, cutting off all hindrances, cutting off all entanglements, cutting off all taints, cutting off all attachments, cutting off all intentions, cutting off wrong views and generating right views, cutting off ignorance and generating wisdom? Bhikṣu! Thus contemplating the impermanence of the eye, and so on, knowing and seeing in this way, gradually ignorance is cut off and wisdom arises."

如是，比丘所说经。若差别者：「云何知、云何见，次第尽一切结，断一切缚、断一切使、断一切上烦恼、断一切结、断诸流、断诸轭、断诸取、断诸触、断诸盖、断诸缠、断诸垢、断诸爱、断诸意、断邪见生正见、断无明生明。比丘！如是观眼无常，乃至如是知、如是见，次第无明断，明生。」

At that time, that bhikṣu, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully bowed and departed.

时，彼比丘闻佛所说欢喜，欢喜已，作礼而去。
