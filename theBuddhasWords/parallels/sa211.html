SĀ 211

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 211（二一一） 世间五欲

Parallel to: SN 35.117

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Jīvaka Kūṭadanta Mango Grove in Vaiśālī.

一时，佛住毘舍离耆婆拘摩罗药师庵罗园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "When I had not yet attained perfect enlightenment, I was alone in a quiet place, contemplating and reflecting, 'Where does my mind often observe?' My mind often pursued the five sense pleasures of the past, less so the five sense pleasures of the present, and even less so those of the future. Having observed that my mind often pursued the five sense pleasures of the past, I made great efforts and diligently guarded myself, no longer allowing it to follow the five sense pleasures of the past.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「我昔未成正觉时，独一静处，禅思思惟：『自心多向何处观察？』自心多逐过去五欲功德，少逐现在五欲功德，逐未来世转复微少；我观多逐过去五欲心已，极生方便，精勤自护，不复令随过去五欲功德。

Because of this diligent self-guarding, I gradually approached anuttara-samyak-saṃbodhi. You bhikṣus also often pursue the five sense pleasures of the past, less so those of the present and future. Now, because you also often pursue the five sense pleasures of the past, you should increase your self-guarding. You will not be far from exhausting all taints, attaining liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom, and knowing and realizing for yourself in the present life: 'My birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and I know that I will not be subject to any future lives.'

「我以是精勤自护故，渐渐近阿耨多罗三藐三菩提。汝等诸比丘亦复多逐过去五欲功德，现在、未来亦复微少，汝今亦当以心多逐过去五欲功德故，增加自护，亦当不久得尽诸漏，无漏心解脱、慧解脱，现法自知作证：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』

Why is this? The eye sees forms, and from this arises internal feelings, whether painful, pleasant, or neither. The ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind perceive dharmas, and from this arises internal feelings, whether painful, pleasant, or neither. Therefore, bhikṣus! You should be aware of these sense bases. If the eye ceases, then the perception of forms is eliminated. If the ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind cease, then the perception of dharmas is eliminated.

「所以者何？眼见色因缘生内受，若苦、若乐、不苦不乐。耳、鼻、舌、身、意法因缘生内受，若苦、若乐、不苦不乐。是故，比丘！于彼入处当觉知，若眼灭，色想则离。耳、鼻、舌、身、意灭，法想则离。」

The Buddha said: "You should be aware of the six sense bases." Having spoken, he entered the room and sat in meditation. At that time, many bhikṣus, after the World-Honored One had left, discussed this: "The World-Honored One briefly taught the essentials of the Dharma to us, without elaborating, and then entered the room to sit in meditation. The World-Honored One said: 'You should be aware of the six sense bases. If the eye ceases, then the perception of forms is eliminated; if the ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind cease, then the perception of dharmas is eliminated.' We still do not understand the World-Honored One's brief teaching. Who in this assembly has the wisdom to be able to explain the meaning of the World-Honored One's brief teaching for us?"

佛说：「当觉六入处。」言已，入室坐禅。时，有众多比丘，世尊去后，作此论议：「世尊为我等略说法要，不广分别，而入室坐禅。世尊说言：『当觉六入处，若彼眼灭，色想则离；耳、鼻、舌、身、意灭，法想则离。』我等今日于世尊略说法中犹故不解，今此众中，谁有慧力，能为我等于世尊略说法中广为我等演说其义？」

They further thought: "Only Venerable Ānanda, who has always attended the World-Honored One, is always praised by the Great Master for his wisdom and pure conduct. Only Venerable Ānanda is capable of explaining the meaning of the World-Honored One's brief teaching for us. We should all go together to Venerable Ānanda and ask him about the essential meaning. We will uphold whatever Ānanda says."

复作是念：「唯有尊者阿难，常侍世尊，常为大师之所赞叹，聪慧梵行。唯有尊者阿难堪能为我等于世尊略说法中演说其义，我等今日皆共往诣尊者阿难所，问其要义，如阿难所说，悉当奉持。」

At that time, many bhikṣus went to Venerable Ānanda and exchanged greetings. They then sat to one side and said to Venerable Ānanda: "Venerable Ānanda, you should know that the World-Honored One briefly taught the essentials of the Dharma to us, as mentioned above. Please explain the meaning in detail for us."

尔时，众多比丘往诣尊者阿难所，共相问讯已，于一面坐。白尊者阿难言：「尊者！当知世尊为我等略说法要，如上所说，具问阿难，当为我等广说其义。」

Venerable Ānanda said to the bhikṣus: "Listen attentively and consider well. I will explain the meaning of the World-Honored One's brief teaching for you. The World-Honored One's brief teaching is a further explanation of the cessation of the six sense bases. Therefore, he said: 'When the eye sense base ceases, the conception of forms is abandoned; when the ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind sense bases cease, the conception of dharmas is abandoned.' After the World-Honored One briefly taught this Dharma, he entered the room to sit in meditation. I have now explained the meaning for you.

尊者阿难语诸比丘：「谛听，善思，于世尊略说法中，当为汝等广说其义，世尊略说者，即是灭六入处有余之说，故言：『眼处灭，色想则离；耳、鼻、舌、身、意入处灭，法想则离。』世尊略说此法已，入室坐禅，我今已为汝等分别说义。」

After Venerable Ānanda explained this meaning, the bhikṣus, having heard his words, joyfully accepted and practiced them.

尊者阿难说此义已，诸比丘闻其所说，欢喜奉行。
