SĀ 215

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 215（二一五） 富留那

Parallel to: SN 35.70

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Pūrṇa the Elder went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha’s feet, stood to one side, and said to the Buddha, “World-Honored One, you have spoken of the present Dharma, the cessation of burning, the timely, the right direction, the seeing of this, and the dependent awakening. World-Honored One, what is the present Dharma, up to the dependent awakening?”

尔时，尊者富留那比丘往诣佛所，稽首佛足，退住一面，白佛言：「世尊说现法、说灭炽然、说不待时、说正向、说即此见、说缘自觉。世尊！云何为现法，乃至缘自觉？」

The Buddha told Pūrṇa, “Excellent! Pūrṇa, you are able to ask this. Pūrṇa, listen carefully and consider it well, I will explain it to you. Pūrṇa, when a bhikṣu sees form with the eye, he is aware of form and aware of the greed for form. He knows that there is eye consciousness and greed for form within him. He knows that there is eye consciousness and greed for form within him as it really is. Pūrṇa, if, when seeing form with the eye, one is aware of form and aware of the greed for form, and knows that there is eye consciousness and greed for form within oneself as it really is, this is called seeing the present Dharma.

佛告富留那：「善哉！富留那！能作此问。富留那！谛听，善思，当为汝说。富留那！比丘眼见色已，觉知色、觉知色贪，我此内有眼识色贪，我此内有眼识色贪如实知。富留那！若眼见色已，觉知色、觉知色贪，我此内有眼识色贪如实知者，是名现见法。

“What is the cessation of burning? What is the timely? What is the right direction? What is the seeing of this? What is the dependent awakening? Pūrṇa, when a bhikṣu sees form with the eye, he is aware of form, but does not arouse the awareness of greed for form. He knows that there is no eye consciousness and greed for form within him. He does not arouse the awareness of greed for form, and knows it as it really is. Pūrṇa, if a bhikṣu, when seeing form with the eye, is aware of form, but does not arouse the awareness of greed for form, and knows form as it really is, and does not arouse the awareness of greed for form as it really is, this is called the cessation of burning, the timely, the right direction, the seeing of this, and the dependent awakening. The same is true for the ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind.

「云何灭炽然？云何不待时？云何正向？云何即此见？云何缘自觉？富留那！比丘眼见色已，觉知色，不起色贪觉，我无有内眼识色贪，不起色贪觉，如实知。若，富留那！比丘眼见色已，觉知已，不起色贪觉，如实知色，不起色贪觉如实知，是名灭炽然、不待时、正向、即此见、缘自觉。耳、鼻、舌、身、意亦复如是。」
