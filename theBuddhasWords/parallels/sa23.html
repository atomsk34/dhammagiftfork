Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 23（二三） 罗睺罗所问

Parallel to: SN 18.21 / SN 22.91 / SĀ 198

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, Venerable Rāhula went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! How can I know and see this body of consciousness and all external objects, so as to eliminate the view of 'I' and 'mine,' the conceit of 'I,' and the afflictions and attachments of 'I'?"

尔时，尊者罗睺罗往诣佛所，头面礼足，却住一面，白佛言：「世尊！云何知、云何见我此识身及外境界一切相，能令无有我、我所见、我慢使系著？」

The Buddha told Rāhula, "Excellent! Excellent! You are able to ask the Tathāgata: 'How can I know and see this body of consciousness and all external objects, so as to eliminate the view of "I" and "mine," the conceit of "I," and the afflictions and attachments of "I"?'"

佛告罗睺罗：「善哉！善哉！能问如来：『云何知、云何见我此识身及外境界一切相，令无有我、我所见、我慢使系著？』耶。」

Rāhula said to the Buddha, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

罗睺罗白佛言：「如是，世尊！」

佛告罗睺罗：「善哉！谛听！谛听！善思念之，当为汝说。罗睺罗！当观若所有诸色，若过去、若未来、若现在，若内、若外，若麁、若细，若好、若丑，若远、若近，彼一切悉皆非我、不异我、不相在，如是平等慧正观。如是受、想、行、识，若过去、若未来、若现在，若内、若外，若麁、若细，若好、若丑，若远、若近，彼一切非我、不异我、不相在。如是平等慧如实观。

Thus it is, Rāhula! A bhikṣu knows and sees thus. One who knows and sees thus, with regard to this body of consciousness and external objects, has no view of self, mine, or conceit, and is not bound by the fetters of conceit.

「如是，罗睺罗！比丘如是知、如是见。如是知、如是见者，于此识身及外境界一切相，无有我、我所见、我慢使系著。

Rāhula! If a bhikṣu, with regard to this body of consciousness and external objects, has no view of self, mine, or conceit, and is not bound by the fetters of conceit, then that bhikṣu is said to have cut off craving, turned away from all fetters, and attained the right state of equanimity, ultimately ending suffering.

「罗睺罗！比丘若如是于此识身及外境界一切相，无有我、我所见、我慢使系著者，比丘是名断爱欲，转去诸结，正无间等，究竟苦边。」

At that time, Rāhula, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully received and practiced it.

时，罗睺罗闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
