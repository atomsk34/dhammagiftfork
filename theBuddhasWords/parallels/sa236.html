SĀ 236

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 236（二三六） 清净乞食住

Parallel to: MN 151 / EĀ 45.6 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra put on his robe in the morning, took his alms bowl, and entered Śrāvastī to beg for alms. After begging for alms, he returned to the monastery, put away his robe and bowl, washed his feet, took his sitting mat, and went into the forest to sit in meditation during the day. When Śāriputra awoke from his meditation, he went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side.

尔时，尊者舍利弗晨朝著衣持钵，入舍卫城乞食。乞食已，还精舍，举衣钵，洗足已，持尼师檀，入林中，昼日坐禅。时，舍利弗从禅觉，诣世尊所，稽首礼足，退坐一面。

At that time, the Buddha asked Śāriputra, "Where have you come from?"

尔时，佛告舍利弗：「汝从何来？」

Śāriputra replied, "World-Honored One! I have come from sitting in meditation during the day in the forest."

舍利弗答言：「世尊！从林中昼日坐禅来。」

The Buddha said to Śāriputra, "What kind of meditation state did you enter now?"

佛告舍利弗：「今入何等禅住？」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I now entered the emptiness samādhi meditation state in the forest."

舍利弗白佛言：「世尊！我今于林中入空三昧禅住。」

The Buddha said to Śāriputra, "Excellent! Excellent! Śāriputra, you now entered the meditation state of the elder and sat in meditation. If bhikṣus wish to enter the meditation state of the elder, they should study like this:

佛告舍利弗：「善哉！善哉！舍利弗，汝今入上座] cf.【丽】禅住而坐禅。若诸比丘欲入上座禅者，当如是学：

'When entering the city, when going for alms, or when leaving the city, one should contemplate thus: "Do I now see forms with the eyes, and give rise to desire, affection, and attachment?"'

『若入城时、若行乞食时、若出城时，当作是思惟：「我今眼见色，颇起欲、恩爱、爱念著不？」』

Śāriputra! When a bhikṣu contemplates in this way, if eye consciousness is attached to forms, that bhikṣu should diligently make effort to cut off evil and unwholesome [afflictions]. It is like a person whose head or clothes are on fire. In order to completely extinguish it, one should make supreme effort and diligently put it out. That bhikṣu should also be like this, making supreme effort and diligently practicing with focused attention.

「舍利弗！比丘作如是观时，若眼识于色有爱念染著者，彼比丘为断恶不善故，当勤欲方便，堪能系念修学。譬如有人，火烧头衣，为尽灭故，当起增上方便，勤教令灭。彼比丘亦复如是，当起增上勤欲方便，系念修学。

If a bhikṣu observes in this way, whether walking on the road, in the village begging for alms, or leaving the village, if eye consciousness is not attached to forms, that bhikṣu should wish to use this joyful and wholesome root to diligently practice with focused attention day and night. This is called a bhikṣu purifying begging for alms while walking, standing, sitting, or lying down. Therefore, this sūtra is called the practice of pure begging for alms.

「若比丘观察时，若于道路、若聚落中行乞食、若出聚落，于其中间，眼识于色，无有爱念染著者，彼比丘愿以此喜乐善根，日夜精勤，系念修习，是名比丘于行、住、坐、卧净除乞食，是故此经名清净乞食住。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the World-Honored One, the Venerable Śāriputra, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说此经已，尊者舍利弗闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
