SĀ 252

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 252（二五二） 优波先那

Parallel to: SN 35.69

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, there was a bhikṣu named Upasena, who was staying in the snake-headed rock cave in the cold forest cemetery in Rājagṛha, practicing the Kālaṅga practice. At that time, Venerable Upasena was alone sitting in meditation.

时，有比丘名优波先那，住王舍城寒林中塜间蛇头岩下迦陵伽行处。时，尊者优波先那独一于内坐禅。

At that time, there was a poisonous snake, about a foot long, that fell from a rock onto Upasena's body. Upasena called to Śāriputra, saying, "Tell the bhikṣus that a poisonous snake has fallen on my body. I am poisoned. You should quickly come and support my body, taking it outside. Do not let my body disintegrate inside like a pile of chaff."

时，有恶毒蛇长尺许，于上石间堕优波先那身上，优波先那唤舍利弗：「语诸比丘，毒蛇堕我身上，我身中毒，汝等驶来，扶持我身，出置于外，莫令于内身坏碎，如糠糟聚。」

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra was staying under a tree nearby. Hearing Upasena's words, he went to Upasena and said, "I now observe your appearance and faculties, which are no different from usual. Yet you say you are poisoned and want me to take your body out, lest it disintegrates like a pile of chaff. What is the reason for this?"

时，尊者舍利弗于近处，住一树下，闻优波先那语，即诣优波先那所，语优波先那言：「我今观汝色貌，诸根不异于常，而言中毒，持我身出，莫令散坏，如糠糟聚，竟为云何？」

Upasena said to Śāriputra, "If one were to say, 'My eyes are mine, they belong to me. Ears, nose, tongue, body, and mind. Ears, nose, tongue, body, and mind are mine, they belong to me. Forms, sounds, smells, tastes, tactile objects, and dharmas. Forms, sounds, smells, tastes, tactile objects, and dharmas are mine, they belong to me. The earth element, the earth element is mine, it belongs to me. The water, fire, wind, space, and consciousness elements. The water, fire, wind, space, and consciousness elements are mine, they belong to me. The form aggregate, the form aggregate is mine, it belongs to me. The feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness aggregates. The feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness aggregates are mine, they belong to me,' then one's complexion and faculties would change. But I am not like that. The eyes are not mine, they do not belong to me; up to the consciousness aggregate is not mine, it does not belong to me. Therefore my complexion and faculties do not change."

优波先那语舍利弗言：「若当有言：『我眼是我、我所。耳、鼻、舌、身、意。耳、鼻、舌、身、意是我、我所。色、声、香、味、触、法，色、声、香、味、触、法是我、我所。地界，地界是我、我所。水、火、风、空、识界，水、火、风、空、识界是我、我所。色阴，色阴是我、我所。受、想、行、识阴，受、想、行、识阴是我、我所』者，面色诸根应有变异。我今不尔，眼非我、我所；乃至识阴非我、我所，是故面色诸根无有变异。」

Śāriputra said, "So it is, Upasena! If you have long been free from the bonds of 'I,' 'mine,' and 'I-making,' and have severed their root like cutting off the top of a palm tree, so that they will never arise again in the future, how could your complexion and faculties change?"

舍利弗言：「如是，优波先那！汝若长夜离我、我所、我慢系著使，断其根本，如截多罗树头，于未来世永不复起，云何面色诸根当有变异。」

Then Śāriputra immediately supported Upasena's body around and brought him out of the cave. Upasena's body was poisoned and broken apart, like a pile of bran.

时，舍利弗即周匝扶持优波先那身出于窟外，优波先那身中毒碎坏，如聚糠糟。

Then Śāriputra spoke a verse, saying:

时，舍利弗即说偈言：

Having long cultivated various holy life, well cultivating the eightfold noble
path, joyfully giving up life, like discarding a poisoned bowl. Having
long cultivated various holy life, well cultivating the eightfold noble path
joyfully giving up life, like a person who has recovered from
a serious illness. Having long cultivated various holy life, well cultivating
the eightfold noble path, like escaping from a burning house, at
the time of death there is no sorrow or regret. Having
long cultivated various holy life, well cultivating the eightfold noble path
contemplating the world with wisdom, like filthy grass and trees, no
longer seeking anything else, and nothing else continues.

「久殖诸梵行，　　善修八圣道，欢喜而舍寿，　　犹如弃毒钵。久殖诸梵行，　　善修八圣道，欢喜而舍寿，　　如人重病愈。久殖诸梵行，　　善修八圣道，如出火烧宅，　　临死无忧悔，久殖诸梵行，　　善修八圣道，以慧观世间，　　犹如秽草木，不复更求余，　　余亦不相续。」

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra having made offerings to Upasena's corpse, went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Venerable Upasena had a small poisonous snake, like a needle for treating the eyes, that fell on his body, and his body immediately disintegrated like a pile of chaff and husks."

时，尊者舍利弗供养优波先那尸已，往诣佛所，稽首礼足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！尊者优波先那有小恶毒蛇，如治眼筹，堕其身上，其身即坏，如聚糠糟。」

The Buddha told Śāriputra, "If Upasena had recited this verse, he would not have been poisoned and his body would not have disintegrated like a pile of chaff and husks."

佛告舍利弗：「若优波先那诵此偈者，则不中毒，身亦不坏，如聚糠糟。」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! What kind of verse was recited? What were the words and phrases?"

舍利弗白佛言：「世尊！诵何等偈？何等辞句？」

The Buddha then spoke a verse to Śāriputra,

"Udana-pari udana-pari, hangu-pava udana-nadi, sukha-nadi, ksha-nadi, manasi, sama-nasi, dharati, nira-kishi, vara-kute udana-pari, udana-pari" Sipa-ha

「坞躭婆隷　躭婆隷航陆波婆躭陆［木＊奈］渧　肃［木＊奈］渧　抧跋渧　文那移　三摩移檀谛　尼罗枳施　婆罗拘閇坞隷　坞娱隷」悉波呵

"Śāriputra! When the good man Upasena spoke this verse and this chapter, the snake poison could not harm his body, and his body was not destroyed, like a pile of chaff."

「舍利弗！优波先那善男子尔时说此偈，说此章句者，蛇毒不能中其身，身亦不坏，如糠糟聚。」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Upasena has never heard this verse, nor has he heard this chapter of incantations. The World-Honored One is speaking this now only for the future."

舍利弗白佛言：「世尊！优婆先那未曾闻此偈，未曾闻此呪术章句，世尊今日说此，正为当来世耳。」

The Venerable Śāriputra, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully bowed and departed.

尊者舍利弗闻佛所说，欢喜作礼而去。
