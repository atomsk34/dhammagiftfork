SĀ 253

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 253（二五三） 毗纽迦旃延

Parallel to: SN 35.133

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Venerable Udayin was wandering in the country of Kosala, and arrived at the village of Kusañjī, where he stayed in the mango grove of the Brahmin lady Venuka Kaccānayanī.

一时，尊者优陀夷往拘萨罗国人间游行，至拘磐茶聚落，到毘纽迦旃延氏婆罗门尼庵罗园中住。

At that time, the young disciples of the Brahmin lady Venuka Kaccānayanī were out gathering firewood. They saw the Venerable Udayin sitting under a tree, with a dignified appearance, calm faculties, and a peaceful mind, having attained the highest tranquility. Having seen him, they approached him, exchanged greetings, and then sat to one side. The Venerable Udayin then spoke various teachings to the young men, encouraging them, and then remained silent. The young men, having heard the Venerable Udayin's teachings, rejoiced and followed his teachings. They then rose from their seats and departed.

时，毘纽迦旃延氏婆罗门尼有诸年少弟子，游行采樵，至庵罗园中，见尊者优陀夷坐一树下，容貌端正，诸根寂静，心意安谛，成就第一调伏。见已，往诣其所，共相问讯已，退坐一面。时，优陀夷为诸年少种种说法，劝励已，默然而住，彼诸年少闻尊者优陀夷所说，欢喜随喜，从坐起去。

At that time, the young men, carrying bundles of firewood, returned to the Brahmin lady Venuka Kaccānayanī. They placed the bundles of firewood on the ground and went to the Brahmin lady Venuka Kaccānayanī. They said, "Our teacher, you should know that in the mango grove there is a śramaṇa named Udayin, a member of the Gautama clan, who is staying there. He speaks the Dharma very well."

时，诸年少担持束薪，还至毘纽迦旃延氏婆罗门尼所，置薪束于地，诣毘纽迦旃延氏婆罗门尼所，白言：「我和上尼，当知庵罗园中有沙门优陀夷，姓瞿昙氏，依于彼住，极善说法。」

The Brahmin lady Venuka Kaccānayanī said to the young men, "You should go and invite the śramaṇa Udayin, a member of the Gautama clan, to have a meal here tomorrow."

毘纽迦旃延氏婆罗门尼语诸年少言：「汝可往请沙门优陀夷瞿昙氏，明日于此饭食。」

At that time, the young disciples received the instructions of the Brahmin lady Venuka Kaccānayanī. They went to Venerable Udayin and said to Udayin, "Venerable One, you should know that our teacher, the Brahmin lady Venuka Kaccānayanī, invites Venerable Udayin to have a meal tomorrow."

时，诸年少弟子受毘纽迦旃延氏婆罗门尼教已，往诣尊者优陀夷所，白优陀夷言：「尊者当知，我和上毘纽迦旃延氏婆罗门尼请尊者优陀夷明旦饭食。」

At that time, Udayin silently accepted the invitation.

时，优陀夷默然受请。

At that time, those young men, knowing that Udayin had accepted the invitation, returned to their teacher, the Brahmin lady Venuka Kaccānayanī, and said, "Teacher, I have invited Venerable Udayin on your behalf. Venerable Udayin silently accepted the invitation. Teacher, you should know the time.

时，彼诸年少知优陀夷受请已，还归和上毘纽迦旃延氏婆罗门尼所，白言：「和上尼，我以和上尼语，请尊者优陀夷，尊者优陀夷默然受请，和上尼自知时。」

At that time, Venerable Udayin, after the night had passed, put on his robes, took his bowl, and went to the house of the Brahmin lady Venuka Kaccānayanī. At that time, the Brahmin lady Venuka Kaccānayanī saw Venerable Udayin coming from afar, quickly prepared a seat, invited him to sit down, and offered various kinds of food and drink with her own hands, abundantly and beautifully. After eating, washing, and putting away the bowl, he returned to his original seat.

尔时，尊者优陀夷夜过晨朝，著衣持钵，往诣毘纽迦旃延氏婆罗门尼舍。时，毘纽迦旃延氏婆罗门尼遥见尊者优陀夷来，疾敷床座，请令就坐，设种种饮食，自手供养，丰美满足。食已，澡嗽、洗钵讫，还就本座。

At that time, the Brahmin lady Venuka Kaccānayanī, knowing that the meal was finished, put on good leather shoes, covered her head with a robe, prepared a high bed separately, showed off her light appearance, and sat arrogantly, saying to Udayin, "I have something to ask. Is there time to answer me?"

时，毘纽迦旃延氏婆罗门尼知食已讫，著好革屣，以衣覆头，别施高床，现起轻相，慠慢而坐，语优陀夷言：「欲有所问，宁有闲暇见答与不？」

Udayin replied, "Sister! Now is not the time." Having said this, he got up from his seat and left.

优陀夷答言：「姊妹！今是非时。」作此语已，从坐起去。

In the same way, the next day, the disciples again went to the mango grove to gather firewood and listen to the Dharma. They returned and again informed the nun preceptor. The nun preceptor again sent them to invite a meal, as before three times. Even when invited to teach the Dharma, she replied, "It is not the right time." She did not teach the Dharma.

如是。明日诸弟子复至庵罗园采樵听法，还复白和上尼，和上尼复遣诣请食，如前三返，乃至请法，答言：「非时。」不为说法。

The young disciples again informed the nun preceptor, "The śramaṇa Udayin in the mango grove is very good at teaching the Dharma.

诸年少弟子复白和上尼，庵罗园中沙门优陀夷极善说法。

The nun preceptor replied, "I also know that he is very good at teaching the Dharma. I have invited him three times, provided a meal, and asked for the Dharma, but he always says it is not the right time and leaves without teaching.

和上尼答言：「我亦知彼极善说法，再三请来，设食问法，常言非时，不说而去。」

The disciples said to the nun preceptor, "He wears nice leather shoes, covers his head with his robe, and sits without respect. How can he teach? Why is that? Because that Venerable Udayin does not teach out of respect for the Dharma.

诸弟子言和上尼：「著好革屣，以衣覆头，不恭敬坐，彼云何说？所以者何？彼尊者优陀夷以敬法故，不说而去。」

The nun preceptor replied, "If that is so, please invite him for me again.

和上尼答言：「若如是者，更为我请彼。」

The disciples accepted the instruction and again invited him for offerings as before. When the nun preceptor knew that he had finished eating, she took off her leather shoes, arranged her robes, sat on a lower seat again, and respectfully said, "I have a question to ask. Is there time to answer it or not?"

诸弟子受教，更请供养如前。时，和上尼知食讫已，脱革屣，整衣服，更坐卑床，恭敬白言：「欲有所问，宁有闲暇见答与不？」

Udayin replied, "You may ask now. I will explain for you."

优陀夷答言：「汝今宜问，当为汝说。」

He then asked, "There are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who say that suffering and happiness are self-made. Some say that suffering and happiness are made by others. Some say that suffering and happiness are made by both self and others. Some say that suffering and happiness are neither self-made nor made by others. Venerable one, what do you say?"

彼即问言：「有沙门、婆罗门说苦乐自作，复有说言苦乐他作，复有说言苦乐自他作，复有说言苦乐非自非他作。尊者！复云何？」

Venerable Udayin replied, "Sister, the Arhat speaks of suffering and happiness as arising from different causes, not as you have said."

尊者优陀夷答言：「姊妹！阿罗诃说苦乐异生，非如是说。」

The brāhman woman then asked, "What is the meaning of that?"

婆罗门尼复问：「其义云何？」

Udayin replied, "The Arhat says that all suffering and happiness arise from their causes and conditions."

优陀夷答言：「阿罗诃说：『从其因缘，生诸苦乐。』」

Udayin then said to the brāhman woman, "I will ask you a question. Please answer me as you wish. What do you think? Is there an eye?"

优陀夷复语婆罗门尼言：「我今问汝，随意答我。于意云何？有眼不？」

She replied, "There is."

答言：「有。」

"Is there form?"

「有色不？」

She replied, "There is."

答言：「有。」

"Is there eye-consciousness, eye-contact, and feeling born from eye-contact, which is experienced internally as painful, pleasant, or neither painful nor pleasant?"

「有眼识、眼触、眼触因缘生受，内觉若苦、若乐、不苦不乐不？」

She replied, "Yes, Venerable Udayin!"

答言：「如是，尊者优陀夷！」

Udayin further asked, "Is there ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind, and feeling born from mind-contact, which is experienced internally as painful, pleasant, or neither painful nor pleasant?"

优陀夷复问：「有耳、鼻、舌、身、意、意触因缘生受，内觉若苦、若乐、不苦不乐不？」

She replied, "Yes, Venerable Udayin!"

答言：「如是，尊者优陀夷！」

Udayin said, "This is what the Arhat said: 'From its causes and conditions, suffering and happiness arise.'"

优陀夷言：「此是阿罗诃说：『从其因缘，生于苦乐。』」

The Brahmin woman said, "Venerable Udayin! Does the Arhat say that suffering and happiness arise from their causes and conditions?"

婆罗门尼言：「尊者优陀夷！如是阿罗诃说从其因缘生苦乐耶？」

Udayin replied, "Yes, Brahmin woman!"

优陀夷答言：「如是，婆罗门尼！」

The Brahmin woman asked again, "Ascetic! How does the Arhat say that suffering, happiness, and neither-suffering-nor-happiness arise from causes and conditions, and how does he say they cease?"

婆罗门尼复问：「沙门！云何阿罗诃说因缘生苦、乐、不苦不乐灭？」

Udayin replied, "I will ask you, and you can answer as you wish. Brahmin woman! If all eyes cease without remainder at all times, is there still feeling arising from eye contact and inner sensations of suffering, happiness, or neither-suffering-nor-happiness?"

优陀夷答言：「我今问汝，随意答我。婆罗门尼！一切眼、一切时灭无余，犹有眼触因缘生受，内觉若苦、若乐、不苦不乐耶？」

She replied, "No, Ascetic!"

答言：「无也，沙门！」

"Is it the same for the ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind? If all of them cease forever without remainder, is there still feeling arising from mind contact and inner sensations of suffering, happiness, or neither-suffering-nor-happiness?"

「如是，耳、鼻、舌、身、意，一切时灭永尽无余，犹有意触因缘生受，内觉若苦、若乐、不苦不乐耶？」

She replied, "No, Ascetic!"

答言：「无也，沙门。」

"So it is, Brahmin woman! This is what the Arhat says: 'Suffering, happiness, and neither-suffering-nor-happiness arise from causes and conditions, and cease when those causes and conditions cease.'"

「如是，婆罗门尼！是为阿罗诃说：『因缘生苦、乐、不苦不乐灭。』」

When Venerable Udayin had spoken this teaching, the Brahmin woman Venuka-Kaccana-yanika was freed from defilements and attained the pure Dharma-eye. At that time, the Brahmin woman Venuka-Kaccana-yanika saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, knew the Dharma, entered the Dharma, crossed over doubt, and did not rely on others to enter the Buddha's teachings. She attained fearlessness in the Dharma. She rose from her seat, arranged her robes, respectfully joined her palms, and said to Venerable Udayin: "Today I have entered the definitive state. From today onwards, I take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha. From today onwards, I take refuge in the Three Jewels for as long as I live."

尊者优陀夷说是法时，毘纽迦旃延氏婆罗门尼远尘离垢，得法眼净。尔时，毘纽迦旃延氏婆罗门尼见法、得法、知法、入法，度疑惑，不由于他入佛教法，于法得无所畏。从坐起，整衣服，恭敬合掌，白尊者优陀夷：「我今日超入决定，我从今日归依佛、归依法、归依僧，我从今日尽寿归依三宝。」

At that time, Udayin taught the Dharma to the Brahmin woman, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting her. After rising from his seat, he departed.

尔时，优陀夷为婆罗门尼说法，示、教、照、喜已，从坐起去。
