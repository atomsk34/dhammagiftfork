SĀ 255

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 255（二五五） 鲁醯遮

Parallel to: SN 35.132

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Venerable Maha-Kaccayana was staying in the country of Aparantaka, at the Monkey Cave in the Simsapa forest by the banks of the Himi River. There was a Rucika Brahmin who revered and served him as if he were an arhat.

一时，尊者摩诃迦旃延住阿磐提国湿摩陀江侧，猕猴室阿练若窟。有鲁醯遮婆罗门，恭敬承事，如罗汉法。

At that time, the Venerable Maha-Kaccayana put on his robes in the morning, took his bowl, entered the Monkey Cave village, and went for alms. After returning from alms-round, he put away his robes and bowl, washed his feet, and sat in meditation in his room.

尔时，尊者摩诃迦旃延晨朝著衣持钵，入猕猴室聚落，次行乞食，乞食还，举衣钵，洗足已，入室坐禅。

At that time, the young disciples of the Rucika Brahmin were gathering firewood in the forest near the Venerable Maha-Kaccayana's cave. They were joking and saying, "A shaveling monk lives here, a dark and ignorant person, not a superior person in the world. Yet the Rucika Brahmin reveres and worships him as if he were an arhat.

时，鲁醯遮婆罗门有诸年少弟子，游行采薪，至尊者摩诃迦旃延窟边，共戏笑言：「此中剃发沙门住，是黑暗人，非世胜人，而鲁醯遮婆罗门尊重供养，如罗汉法。」

At that time, the Venerable Maha-Kaccayana said to the young men, "Young men! Young men! Do not make a noise.

时，尊者摩诃迦旃延语诸年少言：「年少！年少！莫作声。」

After the young men finished speaking, they did not dare to speak again. This happened three times, but they still did not stop.

诸年少] cf.【丽】言终，不敢复言。如是再三，语犹不止。

Then the Venerable Maha-Kaccayana went outside the door and said to the young men, "Young men! Young men! You should not speak. I will now teach the Dharma for you. Please listen.

于是尊者摩诃迦旃延出户外，语诸年少言：「年少！年少！汝等莫语。我今当为汝等说法，汝等且听。」

The young men said, "Yes, please teach the Dharma. We will listen and accept it.

诸年少言：「诺，唯愿说法，我当听受。」

Then the Venerable Maha-Kaccayana spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，尊者摩诃迦旃延即说偈言：

The ancient Brahmins practiced supreme and wonderful precepts, attained the knowledge
of past lives, enjoyed the true meditation of tranquility, always abided
in loving-kindness and compassion, closed the doors of the senses, controlled
the faults of speech - the ancients practiced like this. Abandoning
their original true practices, they clung to false things, guarding their
clan names and being negligent, following the six sense objects. Fasting
dwelling in charnel grounds, bathing three times, reciting the three Vedas
- not guarding the sense doors is like dreaming of obtaining
treasures. Braiding hair, wearing leather robes, stealing precepts, smearing ashes on
the body, wearing coarse clothes to cover the body, carrying a
staff and water bottle - falsely taking the form of a
Brahmin to seek offerings. Well-restrained and protected, pure and free from
defilement, not harming sentient beings - this is the Brahmin of the path.

「古昔婆罗门，　　修习胜妙戒，得生宿命智，　　娱乐真谛禅，常住于慈悲，　　关闭诸根门，调伏于口过，　　古昔行如是。舍本真实行，　　而存虚伪事，守族姓放逸，　　从诸根六境，自饿居冢间，　　三浴诵三典，不守护根门，　　犹如梦得宝。编发衣皮褐，　　戒盗灰坌身，麁衣以蔽形，　　执杖持水瓶，假形婆罗门，　　以求于利养。善摄护其身，　　澄净离尘垢，不恼于众生，　　是道婆罗门。」

At that time, the young Brahmins, angry and displeased, said to the Venerable Maha-Kaccayana, "You slander our scriptures, destroy what is said, and insult the Brahmins." They took bundles of firewood and returned to the Brahmin Lohicca, saying to him, "Master, do you know? That Maha-Kaccayana slanders the scriptures, criticizes what is said, and insults the Brahmins."

尔时，诸年少婆罗门瞋恚不喜，语尊者摩诃迦旃延：「谤我经典，毁坏所说，骂辱婆罗门。」执持薪束，还鲁醯遮婆罗门所，语鲁醯遮婆罗门言：「和上知不？彼摩诃迦旃延诽谤经典，毁呰言说，骂辱婆罗门。」

The Brahmin Lohicca said to the young ones, "Young ones! Do not speak like that. Why? Maha-Kaccayana is deeply respected for his precepts and virtues. He should not slander the scriptures, criticize what is said, and insult the Brahmins."

鲁醯遮婆罗门语诸年少：「诸年少！莫作是语。所以者何？摩诃迦旃延宿重戒德，不应谤毁经典，毁呰言说，骂婆罗门。」

The young ones said, "Master, if you do not believe our words, you should go and see for yourself."

诸年少言：「和上不信我言，当自往看。」

At that time, the Brahmin Lohicca did not believe the words of the young ones. He went to Maha-Kaccayana and exchanged greetings and pleasantries with him. After sitting down to one side, he said to Maha-Kaccayana, "Did my young disciple-students come here?"

时，鲁醯遮婆罗门不信诸年少语，往诣摩诃迦旃延，共相问讯慰劳已，退坐一面。语摩诃迦旃延言：「我诸年少弟子来到此不？」

He replied, "They came here."

答言：「到此。」

"Did you speak with them much or little?"

「少多与共言语不？」

He replied, "I spoke with them."

答云：「与共言语。」

The brahmin Rūpaśīla said, "You spoke with the young men. Now please tell me all about it."

鲁醯遮婆罗门言：「汝与诸年少共语，今可为我尽说是。」

Mahākātyāyana then explained in detail. At that time, the brahmin Rūpaśīla also became angry and displeased, and said to Mahākātyāyana, "I did not believe the words of the young men before. Now Mahākātyāyana truly slanders the scriptures, criticizes and speaks, and insults the brahmins." Having said this, he paused for a moment. After a while, he again said to Mahākātyāyana, "What you said about the 'gate'. What is the 'gate'?"

摩诃迦旃延即为广说。时，鲁醯遮婆罗门亦复瞋恚，心得不喜，语摩诃迦旃延：「我先不信诸年少语，今摩诃迦旃延真实诽谤经典，毁呰而说，骂辱婆罗门。」作此语已，小默然住。须臾，复语摩诃迦旃延：「仁者所说『门』。何等为『门』？」

Mahākātyāyana said, "Excellent! Excellent! Brahmin! Your question is in accordance with the Dharma. I will now explain the 'gate' for you. Brahmin! The eye is the gate because it sees forms. The ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind are the gates because they cognize phenomena."

摩诃迦旃延言：「善哉！善哉！婆罗门！所问如法，我今当为汝说『门』。婆罗门！眼是门，以见色故。耳、鼻、舌、身、意是门，以识法故。」

The brahmin said, "Amazing! Mahākātyāyana! I asked about the gate, and you explained the gate. As Mahākātyāyana said, the unguarded gate. What is the unguarded gate?"

婆罗门言：「奇哉！摩诃迦旃延！我问其门，即说其门，如摩诃迦旃延所说不守护门。云何不守护门？」

Mahākātyāyana said, "Excellent! Excellent! Brahmin! Asking about the unguarded gate is a proper question. I will now explain the unguarded gate for you.

摩诃迦旃延言：「善哉！善哉！婆罗门！问不守护门，是如法问，今当为汝说不守护门。

"Brahmin! Ignorant and uneducated ordinary people, having seen forms with the eye, give rise to attachment to desirable forms and aversion to undesirable forms. Not abiding in the mindfulness of the body, they do not truly know about liberation of the mind and liberation through wisdom. They give rise to various evil and unwholesome dharmas, which are not completely extinguished. They hinder liberation of the mind and liberation through wisdom, and do not attain fulfillment. Because liberation of the mind and liberation through wisdom are not fulfilled, they fully engage in evil conduct and do not rest. Their minds are not tranquil. Because they are not tranquil, they do not subdue, guard, or cultivate the sense faculties. It is the same with the ear and sounds, the nose and smells, the tongue and tastes, the body and tactile objects, and the mind and dharmas."

「婆罗门！愚痴无闻凡夫眼见色已，于可念色而起缘著，不可念色而起瞋恚。不住身念处，故于心解脱、慧解脱无如实知。于彼起种种恶不善法，不得无余灭尽，于心解脱、慧解脱妨碍，不得满足；心解脱、慧解脱不满故，身满恶行，不得休息，心不寂静，以不寂静故，于其根门则不调伏、不守护、不修习。如眼色，耳声、鼻香、舌味、身触、意法，亦复如是。」

The brāhman Rūhīka said, "Excellent! Excellent! Mahākātyāyana! I asked about the unguarded gate, and you explained the unguarded gate for me. Mahākātyāyana! What is meant by the well-guarded gate?"

鲁醯遮婆罗门言：「奇哉！奇哉！摩诃迦旃延，我问不守护门，即为我说不守护门。摩诃迦旃延！云何复名善守护门？」

Mahākātyāyana said to the brāhman, "Excellent! Excellent! You are able to ask me about the meaning of the well-guarded gate. Listen well and think carefully, and I will explain the meaning of the well-guarded gate for you.

摩诃迦旃延语婆罗门言：「善哉！善哉！汝能问我善守护门义。谛听，善思，当为汝说守护门义。

"The learned noble disciple, having seen forms with the eye, does not give rise to attachment to desirable forms, nor to aversion to undesirable forms; always keeping the mind in the body contemplation, with immeasurable mind liberation and wisdom liberation, he truly knows and in them the arisen evil and unwholesome dharmas are completely extinguished, and he attains satisfaction in mind liberation and wisdom liberation; having attained satisfaction in liberation, the bodily contact with evil conduct is all rested, and the mind attains right mindfulness. This is called the first gate, well-tamed, guarded, and cultivated. As with the eye and forms, so it is with the ear and sounds, the nose and smells, the tongue and tastes, the body and tangibles, and the mind and dharmas."

「多闻圣弟子眼见色已，于可念色不起缘著，不可念色不起瞋恚；常摄其心住身念处，无量心解脱、慧解脱如实知，于彼所起恶不善法寂灭无余，于心解脱、慧解脱而得满足；解脱满足已，身触恶行悉得休息，心得正念，是名初门善调伏守护修习。如眼及色，耳声、鼻香、舌味、身触、意法，亦复如是。」

The brāhman Rūhyaka said, "Amazing! Mahākātyāyana! I asked about the meaning of guarding, and you explained the meaning of the well-guarded gate for me. It is like a man seeking poisonous herbs but obtaining nectar. Now it is like this for me. I came here with anger and sat on this seat, but Mahākātyāyana rained down the great Dharma rain on me, like raining nectar. Mahākātyāyana! There are many affairs at home. I now ask to return home."

鲁醯遮婆罗门言：「奇哉！摩诃迦旃延！我问守护问义，即为我说守护门义。譬如士夫求毒药草反得甘露，今我如是，瞋恚而来至此座坐，而摩诃迦旃延以大法雨，雨我身中，如雨甘露。摩诃迦旃延！家中多事，今请还家。」

Mahākātyāyana said, "Brāhman! It is time to know."

摩诃迦旃延言：「婆罗门！宜知是时。」

At that time, the brāhman Rūhyaka heard what Mahākātyāyana said, and he rejoiced in accordance with it. He rose from his seat and left.

时，鲁醯遮婆罗门闻摩诃迦旃延所说，欢喜随喜，从坐起去。
