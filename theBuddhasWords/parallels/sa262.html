SĀ 262

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 262（二六二） 阐陀

Parallel to: SN 12.15 (~) / SN 22.90 / SĀ 301 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, a large number of senior bhikṣus were dwelling in the Deer Park at the Sages' Grove in the country of Bārāṇasī, not long after the Buddha's parinirvāṇa.

一时，有众多上座比丘住波罗［木＊奈］国仙人住处鹿野苑中，佛般泥洹未久。

At that time, the elder Cunda put on his robe in the morning, took his alms bowl, and entered the city of Bārāṇasī to beg for food. After eating, he returned, put away his robe and bowl, washed his feet, took a key, and went from grove to grove, from dwelling to dwelling, from walking place to walking place, inviting the bhikṣus everywhere, saying: "Please instruct me, teach me the Dharma, so that I may know the Dharma and see the Dharma. I shall know the Dharma as it is and view it as it is."

时，长老阐陀晨朝著衣持钵，入波罗［木＊奈］城乞食。食已，还摄衣钵，洗足已，持户钩，从林至林，从房至房，从经行处至经行处，处处请诸比丘言：「当教授我，为我说法，令我知法、见法，我当如法知、如法观。」

At that time, the bhikṣus said to Cunda: "Form is impermanent, feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness are impermanent. All conditioned phenomena are impermanent. All phenomena are without self. Nirvāṇa is quiescent and extinguished."

时，诸比丘语阐陀言：「色无常，受、想、行、识无常，一切行无常，一切法无我，涅槃寂灭。」

Cunda said to the bhikṣus: "I have already known that form is impermanent, feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness are impermanent. All conditioned phenomena are impermanent. All phenomena are without self. Nirvāṇa is quiescent and extinguished."

阐陀语诸比丘言：「我已知色无常，受、想、行、识无常，一切行无常，一切法无我，涅槃寂灭。」

Cunda further said: "However, I do not like to hear that 'all conditioned phenomena are empty and quiescent, unattainable, with the exhaustion of craving, detachment from desire, and nirvāṇa.' How can there be a self in this, and to say 'knowing thus, seeing thus, this is called seeing the Dharma'?" He spoke like this a second and third time.

阐陀复言：「然我不喜闻：『一切诸行空寂、不可得、爱尽、离欲、涅槃。』此中云何有我而言：『如是知、如是见，是名见法。』？」第二、第三亦如是说。

Cunda further said: "Who here has the ability to teach the Dharma for me, enabling me to know the Dharma and see the Dharma?" He further thought: "The Venerable Ānanda is now in the Kuśinagara Guśīra Garden. He has previously served and attended the World-Honored One. He is praised by the Buddha and recognized by all the virtuous practitioners. He will surely be able to teach the Dharma for me, enabling me to know the Dharma and see the Dharma."

阐陀复言：「是中谁复有力堪能为我说法，令我知法、见法？」复作是念：「尊者阿难今在拘睒弥国瞿师罗园，曾供养亲觐世尊，佛所赞叹，诸梵行者皆悉识知。彼必堪能为我说法，令我知法、见法。」

Then, after passing the night, Cunda put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and entered the city of Vārāṇasī to beg for food. After eating, he returned, put away his bedding, took his robe and bowl, and went to the country of Kuśinagara. Gradually traveling, he arrived in the country of Kuśinagara, put away his robe and bowl, washed his feet, and went to where Venerable Ānanda was. After exchanging greetings, he sat to one side.

时，阐陀过此夜已，晨朝著衣持钵，入波罗［木＊奈］城乞食。食已，还摄举卧具，摄卧具已，持衣钵诣拘睒弥国，渐渐游行到拘睒弥国，摄举衣钵，洗足已，诣尊者阿难所，共相问讯已，却坐一面。

At that time, Cunda said to Venerable Ānanda: "Once, the elder bhikṣus were dwelling in the Deer Park at the Squirrels' Feeding Ground in the country of Vārāṇasī. At that time, I put on my robe in the morning, took my alms bowl, and entered the city of Vārāṇasī to beg for food. After eating, I returned, put away my robe and bowl, washed my feet, took my key, and went from grove to grove, from dwelling to dwelling, from walking place to walking place, seeing various bhikṣus everywhere and requesting them to teach me the Dharma, enabling me to know the Dharma and see the Dharma. At that time, the bhikṣus taught the Dharma for me, saying: 'Form is impermanent, feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness are impermanent, all conditioned phenomena are impermanent, all phenomena are without self, and nirvāṇa is quiescent.' At that time, I said to the bhikṣus: 'I already know that form is impermanent, feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness are impermanent, all conditioned phenomena are impermanent, all phenomena are without self, and nirvāṇa is quiescent. However, I do not delight in hearing that "all conditioned phenomena are empty, unattainable, the exhaustion of desire, the detachment from desire, and nirvāṇa." How can there be a self in this, and to say that "knowing and seeing in this way is called seeing the Dharma"?'" At that time, I had this thought: 'Who here has the ability to teach the Dharma for me, enabling me to know the Dharma and see the Dharma?' I then had this thought: 'The Venerable Ānanda is now in the Guśīra Garden in the country of Kuśinagara. He has previously served and attended the World-Honored One. He is praised by the Buddha and recognized by all the virtuous practitioners. He will surely be able to teach the Dharma for me, enabling me to know the Dharma and see the Dharma.' Excellent! Venerable Ānanda should now teach the Dharma for me, enabling me to know the Dharma and see the Dharma.

时，阐陀语尊者阿难言：「一时，诸上座比丘住波罗［木＊奈］国仙人住处鹿野苑中。时，我晨朝著衣持钵入波罗［木＊奈］城乞食。食已，还摄衣钵，洗足已，持户钩，从林至林，从房至房，从经行处至经行处，处处见诸比丘，而请之言：『当教授我，为我说法，令我知法、见法。』时，诸比丘为我说法言：『色无常，受、想、行、识无常，一切行无常，一切法无我，涅槃寂灭。』我尔时语诸比丘言：『我已知色无常，受、想、行、识无常，一切行无常，一切法无我，涅槃寂灭。然我不喜闻：「一切诸行空寂、不可得、爱尽、离欲、涅槃。」此中云何有我而言：「如是知、如是见，是名见法。」？』我尔时作是念：『是中谁复有力堪能为我说法，令我知法、见法？』我时复作是念：『尊者阿难今在拘睒弥国瞿师罗园，曾供养亲觐世尊，佛所赞叹，诸梵行者皆悉知识。彼必堪能为我说法，令我知法、见法。』善哉！尊者阿难今当为我说法，令我知法、见法。」

At that time, Venerable Ānanda said to Cunda: "Excellent! Cunda! I am greatly pleased with your mind. I rejoice that you can reveal without concealment in front of the virtuous practitioners, breaking through the thorns of falsehood. Cunda! The ignorant ordinary person cannot understand that form is impermanent, feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness are impermanent, all conditioned phenomena are impermanent, all phenomena are without self, and nirvāṇa is quiescent. You are now able to receive the supreme and wonderful Dharma. Listen attentively, and I shall explain it for you."

时，尊者阿难语阐陀言：「善哉！阐陀！我意大喜，我庆仁者能于梵行人前，无所覆藏，破虚伪刺。阐陀！愚痴凡夫所不能解色无常，受、想、行、识无常，一切诸行无常，一切法无我，涅槃寂灭。汝今堪受胜妙法，汝今谛听，当为汝说。」

At that time, Cunda had this thought: "I am now joyful and have attained the supreme and wonderful mind, the mind of joy and delight. I am now able to receive the supreme and wonderful Dharma."

时，阐陀作是念：「我今欢喜得胜妙心、得踊悦心，我今堪能受胜妙法。」

At that time, Ānanda said to Cunda: "I have personally heard the Buddha instruct Mahākātyāyana, saying: 'Worldly people are deluded and cling to two extremes, existence and non-existence. Worldly people grasp at various objects and then become attached to them. Kātyāyana! If one does not accept, grasp, dwell, or cling to the self, then when this suffering arises, it arises, and when it ceases, it ceases. Kātyāyana! If one is not doubtful or deluded about this, and can know it for oneself without relying on others, this is called right view, as taught by the Tathāgata. Why is this? Kātyāyana! If one truly and correctly observes the arising of the world, one will not give rise to the view of non-existence of the world. If one truly and correctly observes the cessation of the world, one will not give rise to the view of existence of the world. Kātyāyana! The Tathāgata, having transcended these two extremes, speaks of the middle way, namely, because this exists, that exists; because this arises, that arises. That is to say, conditioned by ignorance, there are volitional formations, and so on, up to birth, old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, suffering, and distress arise. That is to say, because this does not exist, that does not exist; because this ceases, that ceases. That is to say, with the cessation of ignorance, volitional formations cease, and so on, up to the cessation of birth, old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, suffering, and distress cease.'"

尔时，阿难语阐陀言：「我亲从佛闻，教摩诃迦旃延言：『世人颠倒依于二边，若有、若无，世人取诸境界，心便计著。迦旃延！若不受、不取、不住、不计于我，此苦生时生、灭时灭。迦旃延！于此不疑、不惑、不由于他而能自知，是名正见，如来所说。所以者何？迦旃延！如实正观世间集者，则不生世间无见，如实正观世间灭，则不生世间有见。迦旃延！如来离于二边，说于中道，所谓此有故彼有，此生故彼生，谓缘无明有行，乃至生、老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦集；所谓此无故彼无，此灭故彼灭，谓无明灭则行灭，乃至生、老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦灭。』」

When Venerable Ānanda spoke this Dharma, the bhikṣu Cunda was free from defilements, attained the pure Dharma-eye, saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, understood the Dharma, and transcended doubt. He was not dependent on others, and in the teachings of the Great Teacher, he attained fearlessness. He respectfully joined his palms and said to Venerable Ānanda: "It should be just like this. This wisdom, this practice of the holy life, this good friend, this instruction, this admonition, and this teaching of the Dharma are all like this. Now, from Venerable Ānanda, I have heard this Dharma. In all practices, I see emptiness, tranquility, the unobtainable, the exhaustion of craving, the detachment from desire, the cessation, and nirvāṇa. My mind joyfully abides in liberation, no longer turning back. I no longer see the self, but only see the true Dharma."

尊者阿难说是法时，阐陀比丘远尘离垢，得法眼净。尔时，阐陀比丘见法、得法、知法、起法，超越狐疑，不由于他，于大师教法，得无所畏。恭敬合掌白尊者阿难言：「正应如是。如是智慧梵行，善知识教授教诫说法。我今从尊者阿难所，闻如是法，于一切行皆空、皆悉寂、不可得、爱尽、离欲、灭尽、涅槃，心乐正住解脱，不复转还，不复见我，唯见正法。」

At that time, Ānanda said to Cunda: "You have now obtained great benefits. In the profound Buddha Dharma, you have attained the wisdom-eye of the noble ones."

时，阿难语阐陀言：「汝今得大善利，于甚深佛法中，得圣慧眼。」

At that time, the two noble ones praised each other and rose from their seats, each returning to their own place.

时，二正士展转随喜，从坐而起，各还本处。

Sūrpāraka has three kinds, ignorance also has three
kinds. The uninterrupted path and cessation, Pūrṇa and Cunda.

输屡那三种　　无明亦有三无间等及灭　　富留那、阐陀
