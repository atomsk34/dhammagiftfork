SĀ 264

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 264（二六四） 小土塼

Parallel to: SN 22.96 / MĀ 61

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, a certain bhikṣu was contemplating in meditation and thought, “Is there any form that is permanent, constant, unchanging, and rightly abiding? Is there any feeling, perception, volition, or consciousness that is permanent, constant, unchanging, and rightly abiding?”

尔时，有异比丘于禅中思惟，作是念：「颇有色常、恒、不变易、正住耶？如是受、想、行、识，常、恒、不变易、正住耶？」

That bhikṣu rose from his meditation in the afternoon, went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! While contemplating in meditation, I thought, 'Is there any form that is permanent, constant, unchanging, and rightly abiding? Is there any feeling, perception, volition, or consciousness that is permanent, constant, unchanging, and rightly abiding?' Does the World-Honored One today have any form that is permanent, constant, unchanging, and rightly abiding? Does he have any feeling, perception, volition, or consciousness that is permanent, constant, unchanging, and rightly abiding?"

是比丘晡时从禅起，往诣佛所，头面礼足，却住一面，白佛言：「世尊！我于禅中思惟，作是念：『颇有色常、恒、不变易、正住耶？如是受、想、行、识，常、恒、不变易、正住耶？』今日世尊，颇有色常、恒、不变易、正住耶？颇有受、想、行、识，常、恒、不变易、正住耶？」

At that time, the World-Honored One took a small ball of earth in his hand and said to that bhikṣu, "Do you see this ball of earth in my hand?"

尔时，世尊手执小土抟，告彼比丘言：「汝见我手中土抟不？」

The bhikṣu said to the Buddha, "I see it, World-Honored One!"

比丘白佛：「已见。世尊！」

"Bhikṣu! This small amount of earth cannot be found by me. If it could be found by me, it would be permanent, constant, unchanging, and abiding in the right way."

「比丘！如是少土，我不可得。若我可得者，则是常、恒、不变易、正住法。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣu, "I remember from my past lives that I practiced merit for a long time and attained various excellent, wonderful, and delightful karmic results. Once, for seven years, I cultivated the mind of loving-kindness. After seven kalpas of formation and destruction, I did not return to this world. When the seven kalpas were destroyed, I was reborn in the Ābhāsvara Heaven. When the seven kalpas were formed, I was reborn in the Brahma world, becoming the Great Brahma King in the empty palace, without equal or superior, ruling over a thousand worlds. After that, I became Śakra, Lord of the Devas, thirty-six times. I also became a wheel-turning sage king, ruling over the four continents, governing with the right Dharma, possessing the seven treasures, namely, the wheel treasure, elephant treasure, horse treasure, jewel treasure, queen treasure, chief minister treasure, and general treasure; with a thousand sons, all brave and strong; within the four seas, the land was level and free from thorns, without coercion or oppression, governed by the Dharma.

佛告比丘：「我自忆宿命，长夜修福，得诸胜妙可爱果报之事。曾于七年中，修习慈心，经七劫成坏，不还此世。七劫坏时生光音天，七劫成时还生梵世，空宫殿中作大梵王，无胜、无上，领千世界。从是已后，复三十六反，作天帝释。复百千反，作转轮圣王，领四天下，正法治化，七宝具足，所谓轮宝、象宝、马宝、摩尼宝、玉女宝、主藏臣宝、主兵臣宝；千子具足，皆悉勇健；于四海内，其地平正，无诸毒刺，不威、不迫，以法调伏。

The king's law of consecration has eighty-four thousand dragon elephants, all adorned with various treasures, covered with jeweled nets, and with jeweled banners erected. The elephant king Puṣpadanta leads the way, gathering in front of the palace twice a day. I thought at that time, 'These great herds of elephants go back and forth every day, trampling to death countless beings. I wish that the forty-two thousand elephants come once every hundred years.' It was as I wished. Among the eighty-four thousand elephants, forty-two thousand elephants came once every hundred years.

「灌顶王法有八万四千龙象，皆以众宝庄严而挍餝之，宝网复上，建立宝幢，布萨象王最为导首，朝、晡二时自会殿前。我时念言：『是大群象，日日再反往来，蹈杀众生无数，愿令四万二千象百年一来。』即如所愿，八万四千象中，四万二千象百年一至。

The king's law of consecration also has eighty-four thousand horses, all with pure gold carriages, covered with golden nets, with the horse king Balāhaka leading the way.

「灌顶王法复有八万四千匹马，亦以纯金为诸乘具，金网复上，婆罗马王为其导首。

The king's law of consecration has eighty-four thousand four kinds of jeweled carriages, namely, golden carriages, silver carriages, lapis lazuli carriages, and crystal carriages, covered with lion, tiger, leopard skins, and various colored cotton cloth, with the carriage of the sound of Vāḍava and Nanda leading the way.

「灌顶王法有八万四千四种宝车，所谓金车、银车、琉璃车、颇梨车，师子、虎、豹皮、杂色钦婆罗以为覆衬，跋求毗阇耶难提音声之车为其导首。

The king's law of consecration governs eighty-four thousand cities, which are peaceful, prosperous, and densely populated, with Kuśāvatī City as the foremost.

「灌顶王法领八万四千城，安隐丰乐，人民炽盛，拘舍婆提城而为上首。

The king's law of consecration has eighty-four thousand four kinds of palaces, namely, golden, silver, lapis lazuli, crystal, and maṇi lapis lazuli, with the Yugandhara Palace as the foremost.

「灌顶王法有八万四千四种宫殿，所谓金、银、琉璃、颇梨、摩尼琉璃，由诃而为上首。

Bhikṣus! The king's law of consecration has eighty-four thousand four kinds of jeweled beds, namely, golden, silver, lapis lazuli, crystal, with various silk cushions, woolen blankets, and kāliṅga bedding spread on them, with red pillows placed.

「比丘！灌顶王法有八万四千四种宝床，所谓金、银、琉璃、颇梨，种种缯褥、氍氀、毾［登＊毛］、迦陵伽卧具以敷其上，安置丹枕。

Furthermore, bhikṣus! The king's law of consecration also has eighty-four thousand four kinds of clothing, namely, kāśika fine cloth, cūma cloth, tūla cloth, and kuśa cloth.

「复次，比丘！灌顶王法复有八万四千四种衣服，所谓迦尸细衣、刍摩衣、头鸠罗衣、拘沾婆衣。

Furthermore, bhikṣus! The king's law of consecration has eighty-four thousand jade maidens, namely, kṣatriya maidens, kṣatriya-like maidens, let alone other maidens.

「复次，比丘！灌顶王法有八万四千玉女，所谓刹利女、似刹利女，况复余女。

Furthermore, bhikṣus! The king's law of consecration has eighty-four thousand foods and drinks, with all flavors complete. Bhikṣus! Among the eighty-four thousand jade maidens, only one is appointed as an attendant; among the eighty-four thousand jeweled garments, only one is worn; among the eighty-four thousand jeweled beds, only one is slept on; among the eighty-four thousand palaces, only one is dwelled in; among the eighty-four thousand cities, only one is resided in, named Kuśāvatī; among the eighty-four thousand jeweled chariots, only one is ridden, named Vijaya-nandin-guśa, for going out of the city to observe; among the eighty-four thousand jeweled horses, only one is ridden, named Varāha, with a blue-black mane and tail; among the eighty-four thousand dragon elephants, only one is ridden, named Puṣpadanta, for going out of the city to observe.

「复次，比丘！灌顶王法有八万四千饮食，众味具足。比丘！八万四千玉女中，唯以一人以为给侍；八万四千宝衣，唯著一衣；八万四千宝床，唯卧一床；八万四千宫殿，唯处一殿；八万四千城，唯居一城，名拘舍婆提；八万四千宝车，唯乘一车，名毘阇耶难提瞿沙，出城游观；八万四千宝马，唯乘一马，名婆罗诃，毛尾绀色；八万四千龙象，唯乘一象，名布萨陀，出城游观。

"Bhikṣus! What kind of karmic retribution is this, to attain such majestic and sovereign power? This is the retribution of three kinds of karma. What are the three? First, giving; second, taming; third, cultivating the path. Bhikṣus should know, ordinary beings are addicted to the five desires and have no contentment. The wisdom of the noble ones is perfected, and they are always content. Bhikṣus! All conditioned phenomena of the past have ceased and changed. Those naturally accumulated resources and fame all perish. Therefore, bhikṣus! Permanently cease conditioned phenomena, be disgusted, cut off desire, and be liberated. Bhikṣus! Is form permanent or impermanent?"

「比丘！此是何等业报，得如是威德自在耶？此是三种业报，云何为三？一者布施，二者调伏，三者修道。比丘当知，凡夫染习五欲，无有厌足，圣人智慧成满，而常知足。比丘！一切诸行，过去尽灭、过去变易，彼自然众具及以名称，皆悉磨灭。是故，比丘！永息诸行，厌离、断欲、解脱。比丘！色为常？无常？」

The bhikṣu said to the Buddha, "Impermanent, World-Honored One!"

比丘白佛言：「无常。世尊！」

"If it is impermanent, is it suffering?"

「若无常者，是苦耶？」

The bhikṣu said to the Buddha, "It is suffering, World-Honored One!"

比丘白佛言：「是苦。世尊！」

"Bhikṣu! If it is impermanent and suffering, and subject to change, would a noble disciple still grasp at 'self,' 'other,' or 'both' in relation to it?"

「比丘！若无常、苦，是变易法，圣弟子宁复于中计我、异我、相在不？」

The bhikṣu said to the Buddha, "No, World-Honored One!"

比丘白佛：「不也，世尊！」

"Is feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness permanent or impermanent?"

「如是受、想、行、识，为常、为无常。」

The bhikṣu said to the Buddha, "Impermanent, World-Honored One!"

比丘白佛言：「无常。世尊！」

"If it is impermanent, is it suffering?"

「若无常者，是苦耶？」

The bhikṣu said to the Buddha, "It is suffering, World-Honored One!"

比丘白佛言：「是苦。世尊！」

"Bhikṣu! If it is impermanent and suffering, and subject to change, would a noble disciple still grasp at 'self,' 'other,' or 'both' in relation to it?"

「比丘！若无常、苦，是变易法，圣弟子宁复于中计我、异我、相在不？」

The bhikṣu said to the Buddha, "No, World-Honored One!"

比丘白佛：「不也，世尊！」

The Buddha told the bhikṣu, "All forms, whether past, future, or present, internal or external, coarse or subtle, beautiful or ugly, far or near, are not the self, not different from the self, nor do they coexist with the self. Similarly, feelings, perceptions, mental formations, and consciousness, whether past, future, or present, internal or external, coarse or subtle, beautiful or ugly, far or near, are not the self, not different from the self, nor do they coexist with the self. Bhikṣu! One should generate disgust, revulsion, detachment, and liberation towards form. Similarly, towards feelings, perceptions, mental formations, and consciousness, one should generate disgust, revulsion, detachment, and liberation, and know and see liberation: 'My birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and I know that I will not be subject to any future lives.'"

佛告比丘：「诸所有色，若过去、若未来、若现在，若内、若外，若麤、若细，若好、若丑，若远、若近，彼一切非我、不异我、不相在。如是受、想、行、识，若过去、若未来、若现在，若内、若外，若麁、若细，若好、若丑，若远、若近，彼一切非我、不异我、不相在。比丘！于色当生厌离、厌、离欲、解脱。如是于受、想、行、识，当生厌、离欲、解脱，解脱知见：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』」

At that time, the bhikṣu, having heard the Buddha's teaching, was overjoyed and paid homage before departing. Always contemplating the teaching of the clay ball, he dwelled alone in a quiet place, diligently cultivating and not being negligent. Having dwelled without negligence, he thought: "The reason good sons shave their heads and beards, with right faith leave home, and go forth to practice the path, is to ultimately realize the unsurpassed holy life, see the Dharma, and personally know and realize: 'My birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and I know that I will not be subject to any future lives.'"

时，彼比丘闻佛所说，踊跃欢喜，作礼而去。常念土抟譬教授，独一静处，精勤思惟，不放逸住；不放逸住已：「所以善男子剃除须发，正信非家，出家学道，为究竟无上梵行，见法自知身作证：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』」

At that time, the Venerable also personally knew the Dharma, attained liberation of the mind, and became an arhat.

时，彼尊者亦自知法，心得解脱，成阿罗汉。
