SĀ 284

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 284（二八四） 大树

Parallel to: SN 12.55–56 / SN 12.58 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "If one gives rise to a taste for, and clings to, the things one grasps, and the mind is bound, then the mind will chase after name and form. Name and form conditions the six sense bases, the six sense bases condition contact, contact conditions feeling, feeling conditions craving, craving conditions grasping, grasping conditions becoming, becoming conditions birth, and birth conditions old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress. In this way, there is the great mass of suffering. It is like a large tree with a trunk, branches, leaves, flowers, and fruits. The roots below are deeply rooted and firmly established, covered with manure and watered. That tree is firm and will not decay for a long time. In the same way, bhikṣus, if one gives rise to a taste for, and clings to, the things one grasps, and the mind is bound, then the mind will chase after name and form. Name and form conditions the six sense bases, the six sense bases condition contact, contact conditions feeling, feeling conditions craving, craving conditions grasping, grasping conditions becoming, becoming conditions birth, and birth conditions old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress. In this way, there is the great mass of suffering.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「若于所取法随生味著、顾念、缚心，其心驱驰，追逐名色，名色缘六入处，六入处缘触，触缘受，受缘爱，爱缘取，取缘有，有缘生，生缘老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦。如是如是纯大苦聚集，譬大树根干、枝条、柯叶、华果，下根深固，壅以粪土，溉灌以水，彼树坚固，永世不朽。如是，比丘！于所取法随生味著、顾念、心缚，其心驱驰，追逐名色，名色缘六入处，六入处缘触，触缘受，受缘爱，爱缘取，取缘有，有缘生，生缘老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦。如是如是纯大苦聚集。

"If one contemplates the impermanence of the things one grasps, abides in the contemplation of arising and ceasing, of desirelessness, cessation, and disenchantment, and does not give rise to thoughts of attachment, then consciousness will not chase after name and form. Then name and form will cease, name and form ceasing, the six sense bases will cease, the six sense bases ceasing, contact will cease, contact ceasing, feeling will cease, feeling ceasing, craving will cease, craving ceasing, grasping will cease, grasping ceasing, becoming will cease, becoming ceasing, birth will cease, birth ceasing, old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress will cease. In this way, the great mass of suffering will cease. It is like planting a tree, but not caring for it properly, not covering it with manure, not watering it properly, not providing the right temperature, and not allowing it to grow. If one were to cut off its roots, cut its branches, chop it into pieces, analyze it into parts, expose it to the wind and sun, burn it with fire, burn it to ashes, or scatter it with a strong wind or throw it into a stream, what do you think, bhikṣus? Would that not be cutting off the roots of that tree, burning it, and causing it to cease, so that it will not arise in the future?"

「若于所取法随顺无常观，住生灭观、无欲观、灭观、厌观，心不顾念，无所缚著，识则不驱驰、追逐名色，则名色灭，名色灭则六入处灭，六入处灭则触灭，触灭则受灭，受灭则爱灭，爱灭则取灭，取灭则有灭，有灭则生灭，生灭则老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦灭。如是如是则纯大苦聚灭，犹如种树，不随时爱护令其安隐，不壅粪土，不随时溉灌，冷暖不适，不得增长；若复断根、截枝，段段斩截，分分解析，风飘日炙，以火焚烧，烧以成粪，或飏以疾风，或投之流水。比丘！于意云何？非为彼树断截其根，乃至焚烧，令其磨灭，于未来世成不生法耶？」

He replied, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

答言：「如是，世尊！」

"Thus, bhikṣus, if one contemplates the impermanence of the things one grasps, abides in the contemplation of arising and ceasing, of desirelessness, cessation, and relinquishment, and does not give rise to thoughts of attachment, then consciousness will not chase after name and form. Then name and form will cease, name and form ceasing, the six sense bases will cease, the six sense bases ceasing, contact will cease, contact ceasing, feeling will cease, feeling ceasing, craving will cease, craving ceasing, grasping will cease, grasping ceasing, becoming will cease, becoming ceasing, birth will cease, birth ceasing, old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress will cease. In this way, the great mass of suffering will cease."

「如是，比丘！于所取法随顺无常观，住生灭观、无欲观、灭观、舍观，不生顾念，心不缚著，识不驱驰、追逐名色，则名色灭，名色灭则六入处灭，六入处灭则触灭，触灭则受灭，受灭则爱灭，爱灭则取灭，取灭则有灭，有灭则生灭，生灭则老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦灭，如是纯大苦聚灭。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished. The bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
