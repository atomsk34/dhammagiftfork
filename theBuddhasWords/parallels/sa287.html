SĀ 287

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 287（二八七） 城邑

Parallel to: DN 14 (~) / SN 12.65 / DĀ 1 (~) / EĀ 38.4 / EĀ 48.4 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "I remember in my past lives before I attained perfect enlightenment, when I was alone in a quiet place, diligently practicing meditation, I had this thought: 'What exists because of what? What is the condition for what?' I then contemplated correctly, and realized that birth exists because of existence, and birth is the condition for existence. In the same way, existence, grasping, craving, feeling, contact, the six sense bases, and name-and-form exist because of what? What is the condition for name-and-form?' I then contemplated correctly, and realized that name-and-form exists because of consciousness, and consciousness is the condition for name-and-form. When I contemplated in this way, I could not go beyond consciousness, that is, name-and-form arises based on consciousness, the six sense bases arise based on name-and-form, contact arises based on the six sense bases, feeling arises based on contact, craving arises based on feeling, grasping arises based on craving, existence arises based on grasping, birth arises based on existence, and aging, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress arise based on birth. In this way, the great mass of suffering arises.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「我忆宿命未成正觉时，独一静处，专精禅思，作是念：『何法有故老死有？何法缘故老死有？』即正思惟，生如实无间等，生有故老死有，生缘故老死有。如是有、取、爱、受、触、六入处、名色。何法有故名色有？何法缘故名色有？即正思惟，如实无间等生，识有故名色有，识缘故有名色有。我作是思惟时，齐识而还不能过彼，谓缘识名色，缘名色六入处，缘六入处触，缘触受，缘受爱，缘爱取，缘取有，缘有生，缘生老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦。如是如是纯大苦聚集。

"I then thought, 'What does not exist, then old age and death do not exist? What ceases, then old age and death cease?' I then contemplated correctly, and realized that birth does not exist, then old age and death do not exist, and the cessation of birth causes the cessation of old age and death. In the same way, existence, grasping, craving, feeling, contact, the six sense bases, name-and-form, consciousness, and formations..."

「我时作是念：『何法无故则老死无？何法灭故老死灭？』即正思惟，生如实无间等，生无故老死无，生灭故老死灭，如是生、有、取、爱、受、触、六入处、名色、识、行……」广说。

I then thought, 'What does not exist, then formations do not exist? What ceases, then formations cease?' I then contemplated correctly, and realized that ignorance does not exist, then formations do not exist, and the cessation of ignorance causes the cessation of formations. The cessation of formations causes the cessation of consciousness, the cessation of consciousness causes the cessation of name-and-form, the cessation of name-and-form causes the cessation of the six sense bases, the cessation of the six sense bases causes the cessation of contact, the cessation of contact causes the cessation of feeling, the cessation of feeling causes the cessation of craving, the cessation of craving causes the cessation of grasping, the cessation of grasping causes the cessation of existence, the cessation of existence causes the cessation of birth, and the cessation of birth causes the cessation of old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress. In this way, the great mass of suffering ceases.

「我复作是思惟：『何法无故行无？何法灭故行灭？』即正思惟，如实无间等，无明无故行无，无明灭故行灭，行灭故识灭，识灭故名色灭，名色灭故六入处灭，六入处灭故触灭，触灭故受灭，受灭故爱灭，爱灭故取灭，取灭故有灭，有灭故生灭，生灭故老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦灭，如是如是纯大苦聚灭。

I then thought, 'I have attained the path of the ancient sages, the path of the ancient sages, the trace of the path of the ancient sages. The ancient sages went from here, and I now follow them.' It is like a person wandering in a wilderness, searching for a path through the wilderness, suddenly encountering an ancient path where people have walked before. They then follow that path, gradually proceeding, and see an ancient city, an ancient royal palace, gardens, ponds, and forests that are pure. They think, 'I should go and inform the king.' They then go and inform the king, 'Great King, you should know that while I was wandering in the wilderness, searching for a path through the wilderness, I suddenly saw an ancient path where people have walked before. I then followed that path. After following that path, I saw an ancient city, an ancient royal palace, gardens, ponds, and forests that are pure. Great King, you should go and dwell there.' The king then goes there and dwells there, enjoying abundance, peace, and a flourishing population.

「我时作是念：『我得古仙人道、古仙人迳、古仙人道迹，古仙人从此迹去，我今随去。』譬如有人游于旷野，披荒觅路，忽遇故道古人行处，彼则随行，渐渐前进，见故城邑、故] cf.【丽】王宫殿、园观浴池、林木清净。彼作是念：『我今当往白王令知。』即往白王：『大王当知，我游旷野，披荒求路，忽见故道古人行处，我即随行；我随行已，见故城邑、故王宫殿、园观浴池、林流清净，大王可往居止其中。』王即往彼，止住其中，丰乐安隐，人民炽盛。

"Now I am like this, having attained the path of the ancient sages, the path of the ancient sages, the trace of the path of the ancient sages. The ancient sages went from here, and I have attained to follow them. That is, the eightfold noble path: right view, right intention, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right concentration. I saw from that path old age and death, the origin of old age and death, the cessation of old age and death, the path leading to the cessation of old age and death. I saw birth, existence, grasping, craving, feeling, contact, the six sense bases, name and form, consciousness, formations, the origin of formations, the cessation of formations, the path leading to the cessation of formations. I personally knew and personally awakened to these teachings, and attained perfect enlightenment. For the sake of bhikṣus, bhikṣuṇīs, upāsakas, upāsikās, and other non-Buddhist śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, householders, and renunciants, those four assemblies heard the Dharma, correctly inclined, believed and delighted in it, understood the Dharma well, and increased their pure conduct, benefiting many, revealing and manifesting it."

「今我如是，得古仙人道、古仙人迳、古仙人迹，古仙人去处，我得随去，谓八圣道，正见、正志、正语、正业、正命、正方便、正念、正定。我从彼道见老病死、老病死集、老病死灭、老病死灭道迹，见生、有、取、爱、受、触、六入处、名色、识、行、行集、行灭、行灭道迹。我于此法自知自觉，成等正觉，为比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆夷，及余外道沙门、婆罗门、在家、出家，彼诸四众闻法正向、信乐，知法善，梵行增广，多所饶益，开示显发。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully practiced it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
