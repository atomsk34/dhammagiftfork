SĀ 288

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 288（二八八） 芦

Parallel to: SN 12.67

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra and Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila were on Vulture Peak.

尔时，尊者舍利弗、尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗在耆阇崛山。

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra woke up from meditation in the afternoon and went to Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila. After exchanging greetings and pleasantries, he sat to one side and said to Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila, "I have a question to ask. Do you have the leisure to answer it?"

尔时，尊者舍利弗晡时从禅觉，诣尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗，共相问讯庆慰已，于一面坐。语尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗：「欲有所问，宁有闲暇见答与不？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila said to Venerable Śāriputra, "Ask, Venerable One. I will answer what I know."

尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗语尊者舍利弗言：「仁者且问，知者当答。」

Venerable Śāriputra asked Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila, "Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila, is there aging?"

尊者舍利弗问尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗：「云何？尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗，有老不？」

He replied, "There is."

答言：「有。」

Venerable Śāriputra further asked, "Is there death?"

尊者舍利弗复问：「有死不？」

He replied, "There is."

答言：「有。」

He further asked, "How is it? Is old age and death self-made, made by others, made by both self and others, or is it not self-made, not made by others, and not made by both self and others, but without cause?"

复问：「云何？老死自作耶？为他作耶？为自他作耶？为非自非他无因作耶？」

He replied, "Venerable Śāriputra! Old age and death are not self-made, not made by others, not made by both self and others, nor are they not self-made, not made by others, and not made by both self and others without cause. Rather, because of the condition of birth, there is old age and death."

答言：「尊者舍利弗！老死非自作、非他作、非自他作、亦非非自他作无因作，然彼生缘故有老死。」

"So it is with birth, existence, grasping, craving, feeling, contact, the six sense bases, and name and form - are they self-made, made by others, made by both self and others, or are they not self-made, not made by others, and not made by both self and others without cause?"

「如是生、有、取、爱、受、触、六入处、名色，为自作、为他作、为自他作、为非自他无因作？」

He replied, "Venerable Śāriputra! Name and form are not self-made, not made by others, not made by both self and others, nor are they not self-made, not made by others, and not made by both self and others without cause. Rather, name and form arise conditioned by consciousness."

答言：「尊者舍利弗！名色非自作、非他作、非自他作、非非自他作无因作，然彼名色缘识生。」

He further asked, "So is that consciousness self-made, made by others, made by both self and others, or is it not self-made, not made by others, and not made by both self and others without cause?"

复问：「彼识为自作、为他作、为自他作、为非自非他无因作？」

He replied, "Venerable Śāriputra! That consciousness is not self-made, not made by others, not made by both self and others, nor is it not self-made, not made by others, and not made by both self and others without cause. Rather, that consciousness arises conditioned by name and form."

答言：「尊者舍利弗！彼识非自作、非他作、非自他作、非非自他作无因作，然彼识缘名色生。」

Venerable Śāriputra further asked, "Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila! Previously you said that name and form are not self-made, not made by others, not made by both self and others, nor are they not self-made, not made by others, and not made by both self and others without cause. Rather, name and form arise conditioned by consciousness. But now you say that name and form arise conditioned by consciousness. What is the meaning of this?"

尊者舍利弗复问：「尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗！先言名色非自作、非他作、非自他作、非非自他作无因作，然彼名色缘识生，而今复言名色缘识，此义云何？」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila replied, "Now I will give an analogy, for the wise understand through analogies. It is like three reeds standing on open ground, supporting each other, and standing upright. If one is removed, the other two will also not stand. If two are removed, the one will also not stand. Supporting each other, they stand upright. Consciousness conditioned by name and form is also like this. Supporting each other, they grow and develop."

尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗答言：「今当说譬，如智者因譬得解。譬如三芦立于空地，展转相依，而得竖立，若去其一，二亦不立，若去其二，一亦不立，展转相依，而得竖立，识缘名色亦复如是。展转相依，而得生长。」

Venerable Śāriputra said, "Excellent! Excellent! Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila! Among the Buddha's disciples, those who are wise, clear-sighted, well-tamed, fearless, who have seen the Dharma of immortality and fully realized it with their own bodies, are Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila. He is able to answer various difficult questions with such profound and meaningful explanations, like a priceless jewel that is revered by the world. I now revere Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila in the same way. I have gained great benefits from you. Other virtuous practitioners who frequently visit you will also gain great benefits, because Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila teaches the Dharma well. I now praise and rejoice in Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila's teaching with thirty kinds of praise."

尊者舍利弗言：「善哉！善哉！尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗！世尊声闻中，智慧明达，善调无畏，见甘露法，以甘露法具足身作证者，谓尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗，乃有如是甚深义辩，种种难问，皆悉能答，如无价宝珠，世所顶戴，我今顶戴尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗亦复如是。我今于汝所，快得善利，诸余梵行数诣其所，亦得善利，以彼尊者善说法故。我今以此尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗所说法故，当以三十种赞叹称扬随喜。」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila said, "One who is disgusted with old age and death, who is detached from it and has extinguished it, is called a Dharma teacher. One who is disgusted with birth, existence, grasping, craving, feeling, contact, the six sense bases, name and form, and consciousness, who is detached from them and has extinguished them, is called a Dharma teacher. If a bhikṣu is disgusted with old age and death, is detached from it and has extinguished it, and is heading towards it, he is called a Dharma teacher. Up to being disgusted with consciousness, being detached from it and having extinguished it, he is called a Dharma teacher. If a bhikṣu is disgusted with old age and death, is detached from it and has extinguished it, does not arouse the contaminants, and is well liberated in mind, he is called a Dharma teacher. Up to being disgusted with consciousness, being detached from it and having extinguished it, does not arouse the contaminants, and is well liberated in mind, he is called a Dharma teacher."

尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗说：「老死厌患、离欲、灭尽，是名法师；说生、有、取、爱、受、触、六入处、名色、识，厌患、离欲、灭尽，是名法师；若比丘于老死厌患、离欲、灭尽向，是名法师；乃至识厌患、离欲、灭尽向，是名法师；若比丘于老死厌患、离欲、灭尽，不起诸漏，心善解脱，是名法师；乃至识厌患、离欲、灭尽，不起诸漏，心善解脱，是名法师。」

Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila said to Venerable Śāriputra, "Excellent! Excellent! Among the Buddha's disciples, those who are wise, clear-sighted, well-tamed, fearless, who have seen the Dharma of immortality and fully realized it with their own bodies, are Venerable Śāriputra. He is able to ask such various profound and correct questions, like a priceless jewel that is revered by the world. You are now like this, revered, respected, and served by all virtuous practitioners. I have gained great benefits today, being able to discuss the wonderful meaning with you."

尊者摩诃拘𫄨罗语尊者舍利弗言：「善哉！善哉！于世尊声闻中，智慧明达，善调无畏，见甘露法，以甘露法具足身作证者，谓尊者舍利弗，能作如是种种甚深正智之问，犹如世间无价宝珠，人皆顶戴，汝今如是，普为一切诸梵行者之所顶戴、恭敬、奉事。我于今日快得善利，得与尊者共论妙义。」

At that time, the two noble ones rejoiced in each other's words and returned to their respective abodes.

时，二正士更相随喜，各还所住。
