SĀ 294

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 294（二九四） 愚痴点慧

Parallel to: SN 12.19

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Ignorant and unlearned ordinary people, covered by ignorance and bound by the conditions of craving, obtain this body of consciousness. Internally, there is this body of consciousness, externally there are name and form, and these two conditions give rise to contact. Touched by these six sense bases, ignorant and unlearned ordinary people experience various feelings of pleasure and pain. What are the six? The sense base of eye contact, the sense bases of ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind contact. If the wise are covered by ignorance and bound by the conditions of craving, they obtain this body of consciousness. Thus, internally there is a body of consciousness, externally there are name and form, and these two conditions give rise to the six sense bases. Because of being touched by the six sense bases, the wise experience various feelings of pleasure and pain. What are the six? The sense base of eye contact, the sense bases of ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind contact. What is the difference between the ignorant and the wise in cultivating the brahman way with me?"

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「愚痴无闻凡夫无明复、爱缘系，得此识身。内有此识身，外有名色，此二因缘生触。此六触入所触，愚痴无闻凡夫苦乐受觉，因起种种。云何为六？眼触入处，耳、鼻、舌、身、意触入处。若黠慧者无明复，爱缘系得此识身。如是内有识身，外有名色，此二缘生六触入处，六触所触故，智者生苦乐受觉，因起种种。何等为六？眼触入处，耳、鼻、舌、身、意触入处。愚夫、黠慧，彼于我所修诸梵者，有何差别？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "The World-Honored One is the root of the Dharma, the eye of the Dharma, and the reliance of the Dharma. Excellent! World-Honored One! Please explain. When the bhikṣus hear it, they will accept and uphold it."

比丘白佛言：「世尊是法根、法眼、法依。善哉！世尊！唯愿演说，诸比丘闻已，当受奉行。」

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Listen carefully and contemplate well, I will explain for you. Bhikṣus! Those ignorant and unlearned ordinary people, covered by ignorance and bound by the conditions of craving, obtain this body of consciousness. If their ignorance is not eliminated and the conditions of craving are not exhausted, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will again take on a body. Because they take on a body again, they do not attain liberation from birth, old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress. Why is this? Because these ignorant ordinary people have not cultivated the brahman way in the past, heading towards the complete exhaustion of suffering and the ultimate end of suffering. Therefore, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will again take on a body. Because they take on a body again, they do not attain liberation from birth, old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「谛听，善思，当为汝说。诸比丘！彼愚痴无闻凡夫无明所覆，爱缘所系，得此识身，彼无明不断，爱缘不尽，身坏命终，还复受身；还受身故，不得解脱生、老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦。所以者何？此愚痴凡夫本不修梵行，向正尽苦，究竟苦边故，是故身坏命终，还复受身；还受身故，不得解脱生、老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦。

If the wise and intelligent ones are covered by ignorance and bound by the conditions of craving, and obtain this body of consciousness, then their ignorance is eliminated and the conditions of craving are exhausted. Because their ignorance is eliminated and the conditions of craving are exhausted, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will not take on a body again. Because they do not take on a body again, they attain liberation from birth, old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress. Why is this? Because they have previously cultivated the brahman way, heading towards the complete exhaustion of suffering and the ultimate end of suffering. Therefore, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they will not take on a body again. Because they do not take on a body again, they attain liberation from birth, old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress. This is called the various distinctions in the cultivation of the brahman way by ordinary people and the wise and intelligent ones in relation to me.

「若黠慧者无明所覆，爱缘所系，得此识身，彼无明断，爱缘尽；无明断，爱缘尽故，身坏命终，更不复受；不更受故，得解脱生、老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦。所以者何？彼先修梵行，正向尽苦，究竟苦边故，是故彼身坏命终，更不复受；更不受故，得解脱生、老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦，是名凡夫及黠慧者，彼于我所修诸梵行，种种差别。」

After the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha had said and joyfully practiced it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
