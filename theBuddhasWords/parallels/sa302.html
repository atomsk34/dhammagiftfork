SĀ 302

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 302（三〇二） 阿支罗

Parallel to: SN 12.17

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Vulture Peak in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城耆阇崛山。

At that time, the World-Honored One, in the morning, put on his robe, took his alms bowl, left Vulture Peak, and entered Rājagṛha to beg for alms.

尔时，世尊晨朝著衣持钵，出耆阇崛山，入王舍城乞食。

At that time, Ārāḍa Kālāma was leaving Rājagṛha for some minor matter and heading towards Vulture Peak. He saw the World-Honored One from afar. Having seen him, he went to where the Buddha was and said to the Buddha, "Gautama! I wish to ask something. Is there time to answer me?"

时，有阿支罗迦叶为营小事，出王舍城，向耆阇崛山，遥见世尊。见已，诣佛所，白佛言：「瞿昙！欲有所问，宁有闲暇见答与不？」

The Buddha told Kālāma, "Now is not the time for discussion. I am now entering the city to beg for alms. When I return, that will be the time. I will explain it to you."

佛告迦叶：「今非论时，我今入城乞食，来还则是其时，当为汝说。」

The second time, he said the same thing. The third time, he asked again, "Gautama! Why do you obstruct me? Gautama! How is there any difference? I now wish to ask something. Please explain it to me."

第二亦如是说，第三复问：「瞿昙！何为我作留难？瞿昙！云何有异？我今欲有所问，为我解说。」

The Buddha told Ājñātakauṇḍinya, "Ask as you wish."

佛告阿支罗迦叶：「随汝所问。」

Ājñātakauṇḍinya asked the Buddha, "What do you think, Gautama? Is suffering self-created?"

阿支罗迦叶白佛言：「云何？瞿昙！苦自作耶？」

The Buddha told Kauṇḍinya, "If suffering is self-created, that is indeterminate."

佛告迦叶：「苦自作者，此是无记。」

Kauṇḍinya further asked, "What do you think, Gautama? Is suffering created by others?"

迦叶复问：「云何？瞿昙！苦他作耶？」

The Buddha told Kauṇḍinya, "If suffering is created by others, that is also indeterminate."

佛告迦叶：「苦他作者，此亦无记。」

Kauṇḍinya further asked, "What do you think, Gautama? Is suffering created by oneself and others?"

迦叶复问：「苦自他作耶？」

The Buddha told Kauṇḍinya, "If suffering is created by oneself and others, that is also indeterminate."

佛告迦叶：「苦自他作，此亦无记。」

Kauṇḍinya further asked, "What do you think, Gautama? Is suffering not created by oneself or others, but without a cause?"

迦叶复问：「云何？瞿昙！苦非自非他无因作耶？」

The Buddha told Kauṇḍinya, "If suffering is not created by oneself or others, that is also indeterminate."

佛告迦叶：「苦非自非他，此亦无记。」

Kauṇḍinya further asked, "What is meant by 'without a cause or creator'? Gautama, is suffering created by oneself?" He replied, "Indeterminate." "Is it created by others, by oneself and others, or without a cause or creator?" He replied, "Indeterminate." "Is there no suffering now?"

迦叶复问：「云何无因作者？瞿昙！所问苦自作耶？」答言：「无记。」「他作耶？自他作耶？非自非他无因作耶？」答言：「无记。」「今无此苦耶？」

The Buddha told Kauṇḍinya, "There is no absence of suffering, but there is suffering."

佛告迦叶：「非无此苦，然有此苦。」

Kauṇḍinya said to the Buddha, "Excellent! Gautama! You speak of the existence of suffering. Please teach me the Dharma so that I may know and see suffering."

迦叶白佛言：「善哉！瞿昙！说有此苦，为我说法，令我知苦见苦。」

The Buddha told Kauṇḍinya, "If one experiences suffering oneself, I would say that suffering is self-created. If another experiences suffering, it is created by another. If one experiences suffering oneself and another experiences suffering, then it is both self-created and created by another. I do not say this. If suffering arises without cause, depending neither on oneself nor another, I also do not say this. Apart from these extremes, I teach the middle way. The Buddha teaches the Dharma thus: 'Because this exists, that exists. Because this arises, that arises.' That is, conditioned by ignorance, there are karmic formations, and so on, up to the great mass of suffering. When ignorance ceases, karmic formations cease, and so on, up to the cessation of the great mass of suffering."

佛告迦叶：「若受即自受者，我应说苦自作，若他受他即受者，是则他作，若受自受他受，复与苦者。如是者自他作，我亦不说，若不因自他，无因而生苦者，我亦不说。离此诸边，说其中道，如来说法，此有故彼有，此起故彼起，谓缘无明行，乃至纯大苦聚集，无明灭则行灭，乃至纯大苦聚灭。」

After the Buddha taught this sūtra, Ājñātakauṇḍinya was free from defilements and attained the purity of the Dharma eye.

佛说此经已，阿支罗迦叶远尘离垢，得法眼净。

At that time, Ājñātakauṇḍinya saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, knew the Dharma, entered the Dharma, transcended all doubts, did not rely on others to know, and was not liberated by others. He attained fearlessness in the true Dharma and Vinaya. He joined his palms and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I am now liberated. From this day forth, I take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Saṃgha. I will be an upāsaka for the rest of my life. Please bear witness to me."

时，阿支罗迦叶见法、得法、知法、入法，度诸狐疑，不由他知、不因他度，于正法、律心得无畏，合掌白佛言：「世尊！我今已度，我从今日，归依佛、归依法、归依僧，尽寿作优婆塞，证知我。」

Ājñātakauṇḍinya, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and paid homage before departing.

阿支罗迦叶闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。

Not long after Ājñātakauṇḍinya took leave of the World-Honored One, he was gored to death by a cow protecting her calf. At the time of his death, his faculties were clear and his complexion was radiant.

时，阿支罗迦叶辞世尊去不久，为护犊牸牛所触杀，于命终时，诸根清净，颜色鲜白。

At that time, the World-Honored One entered the city to beg for alms. At that time, many bhikṣus also entered the city of Rājagṛha to beg for alms. They heard it said: "Ājñātakauṇḍinya heard the Dharma from the World-Honored One, and not long after taking leave, he was gored to death by a cow. At the time of his death, his faculties were clear and his complexion was radiant." The bhikṣus, having finished begging for alms, returned, put away their robes and bowls, washed their feet, went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed their heads at his feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha: "World-Honored One! This morning, many bhikṣus entered the city to beg for alms. We heard that Ājñātakauṇḍinya heard the Dharma and Vinaya from the World-Honored One, and not long after taking leave, he was gored to death by a cow protecting her calf. At the time of his death, his faculties were clear and his complexion was radiant. World-Honored One! Where was he reborn? Where did he take birth? What did he attain?"

尔时，世尊入城乞食。时，有众多比丘亦入王舍城乞食，闻有传说：「阿支罗迦叶从世尊闻法，辞去不久，为牛所触杀，于命终时，诸根清净，颜色鲜白。」诸比丘乞食已，还出，举衣钵，洗足，诣世尊所，稽首礼足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！我今晨朝众多比丘入城乞食，闻阿支罗迦叶从世尊闻法、律，辞去不久，为护犊牛所触杀，于命终时，诸根清净，颜色鲜白。世尊！彼生何趣？何处受生？彼何所得？」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus: "He has already seen the Dharma, known the Dharma, followed the Dharma, and not grasped at the Dharma. He has already attained parinirvāṇa. You should go and make offerings to his body."

佛告诸比丘：「彼已见法、知法、次法、不受于法，已般涅槃，汝等当往供养其身。」

At that time, the World-Honored One gave the first prediction to Ājñātakauṇḍinya.

尔时，世尊为阿支罗迦叶受第一记。
