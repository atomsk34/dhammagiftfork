SĀ 311

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 311（三一一） 富楼那

Parallel to: MN 145 / SN 35.88

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time Venerable Pūrṇa came to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha’s feet, and stood to one side. He said to the Buddha, “Excellent, World-Honored One! Please teach me the Dharma. When I sit alone in a quiet place, diligently contemplate, and abide without negligence, I will know for myself that I will not be reborn again.”

尔时，尊者富楼那来诣佛所，稽首礼足，退住一面，白佛言：「善哉！世尊！为我说法，我坐独一静处，专精思惟，不放逸住，乃至自知不受后有。」

The Buddha said to Pūrṇa, “Excellent, excellent! You are able to ask the Tathāgata about such a matter. Listen carefully and think well, I will explain it to you. If there is a bhikṣu who sees with the eye forms that are desirable, pleasing, memorable, and agreeable, which nourish desire; having seen them, he delights in them, praises them, and clings to them. Having delighted in them, praised them, and clung to them, he is joyful. Having been joyful, he is attached to pleasure. Having been attached to pleasure, he is greedy and attached. Because of being joyful, attached to pleasure, greedy, and attached, he is far from nirvāṇa. The same is said for the ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind.

佛告富楼那：「善哉！善哉！能问如来如是之义。谛听，善思，当为汝说。若有比丘！眼见可爱、可乐、可念、可意，长养欲之色；见已欣悦、赞叹、系著，欣悦、赞叹、系著已欢喜，欢喜已乐著，乐著已贪爱，贪爱已阨碍。欢喜、乐著、贪爱、阨碍故，去涅槃远。耳、鼻、舌、身、意亦如是说。

Pūrṇa, if a bhikṣu sees with the eye forms that are desirable, pleasing, memorable, and agreeable, which nourish desire; having seen them, he does not delight in them, does not praise them, and does not cling to them. Because he does not delight in them, praise them, and cling to them, he is not joyful. Because he is not joyful, he does not deeply enjoy them. Because he does not deeply enjoy them, he is not greedy and attached. Because he is not greedy and attached, he is not obstructed. Because he is not joyful, does not deeply enjoy, is not greedy, and is not obstructed, he gradually approaches nirvāṇa. The same is said for the ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind.

「富楼那！若比丘，眼见可爱、乐、可念、可意，长养欲之色；见已不欣悦、不赞叹、不系著，不欣悦、不赞叹、不系著故不欢喜，不欢喜故不深乐，不深乐故不贪爱，不贪爱故不阨碍。不欢喜、不深乐、不贪爱、不阨碍故，渐近涅槃。耳、鼻、舌、身、意亦如是说。」

The Buddha said to Pūrṇa, "I have already briefly explained the Dharma teachings. Where do you wish to stay?"

佛告富楼那：「我已略说法教，汝欲何所住？」

Pūrṇa said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I have already received the World-Honored One's brief teachings. I wish to travel in the human realm of Śruṇā in the west."

富楼那白佛言：「世尊！我已蒙世尊略说教诫，我欲于西方输卢那人间游行。」

The Buddha said to Pūrṇa, "The people of Śruṇā in the west are fierce, impetuous, rude, and fond of scolding. Pūrṇa! If you hear them being fierce, impetuous, rude, fond of scolding, and insulting, what will you do?"

佛告富楼那：「西方输卢那人凶恶、轻躁、弊暴、好骂。富楼那！汝若闻彼凶恶、轻躁、弊暴、好骂、毁辱者，当如之何？」

Pūrṇa said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! If the people of Śruṇā in the west are fierce, scolding, and insulting in front of me, I will think: 'The people of Śruṇā in the west are wise and intelligent. Although they are fierce, rude, scolding, and insulting in front of me, they still do not hit or throw stones at me.'"

富楼那白佛言：「世尊！若彼西方输卢那国人，面前凶恶、诃骂、毁辱者。我作是念：『彼西方输卢那人贤善智慧，虽于我前凶恶、弊暴、骂、毁辱我，犹尚不以手、石而见打掷。』」

The Buddha said to Pūrṇa, "If the people of Śruṇā in the west are not only fierce, impetuous, rude, and scolding, but also hit and throw stones at you, what will you do?"

佛告富楼那：「彼西方输卢那人但凶恶、轻躁、弊暴、骂辱，于汝则可脱，复当以手、石打掷者，当如之何？」

Pūrṇa said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! If the people of Śruṇā in the west do not hit me with their hands or stones, I will think: 'The people of Śruṇā in the west are wise and intelligent. Although they hit me with their hands or stones, they do not use knives or weapons.'"

富楼那白佛言：「世尊！西方输卢那人脱以手、石加于我者，我当念言：『输卢那人贤善智慧，虽以手、石加我，而不用刀杖。』」

The Buddha said to Pūrṇa, "If the people of Śruṇā in the west do not hit you with knives or weapons, what will you do?"

佛告富楼那：「若当彼人脱以刀杖而加汝者，复当云何？」

Pūrṇa said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! If the people of Śruṇā in the west do not hit me with knives or weapons, I will think: 'The people of Śruṇā in the west are wise and intelligent. Although they hit me with knives or weapons, they do not kill me.'"

富楼那白佛言：「世尊！若当彼人脱以刀杖，而加我者，当作是念：『彼输卢那人贤善智慧，虽以刀杖而加于我，而不见杀。』」

The Buddha said to Pūrṇa, "If the people of Śruṇā in the west do not kill you, what will you do?"

佛告富楼那：「假使彼人脱杀汝者，当如之何？」

Pūrṇa said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! If the people of Śruṇā in the west do not kill me, I will think: 'There are disciples of the World-Honored One who are disgusted with the body. Some kill themselves with knives, some take poison, some hang themselves, and some jump into deep pits. The wise and intelligent people of Śruṇā in the west, with little effort, can liberate me from this decaying body.'"

富楼那白佛言：「世尊！若西方输卢那人脱杀我者，当作是念：『有诸世尊弟子，当厌患身，或以刀自杀，或服毒药，或以绳自系，或投深坑；彼西方输卢那人贤善智慧，于我朽败之身，以少作方便，便得解脱。』」

The Buddha said, "Excellent! Pūrṇa! You have well learned patience. You are now able to dwell in the land of Śruṇā. You should now go to liberate the unliberated, to bring peace to the unpeaceful, and to attain nirvāṇa for those who have not yet attained nirvāṇa."

佛言：「善哉！富楼那！汝善学忍辱，汝今堪能于输卢那人间住止，汝今宜去度于未度，安于未安，未涅槃者令得涅槃。」

At that time, Pūrṇa, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully followed and paid homage before departing.

尔时，富楼那闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。

At that time, the Venerable Pūrṇa, having passed the night and at dawn, put on his robe, took his bowl, entered the city of Śrāvastī to beg for alms, ate, returned, entrusted his bedding, took his robe and bowl, and went to the land of Śruṇā in the west to wander. Having arrived, he spent the summer retreat, teaching the Dharma to five hundred upāsakas, establishing five hundred monasteries, with all the necessary supplies such as rope beds and bedding fully provided. After three months, he attained the three knowledges and entered parinirvāṇa without remainder in that place.

尔时，尊者富楼那夜过晨朝，著衣持钵，入舍卫城乞食，食已还出，付嘱卧具，持衣钵去，至西方输卢那人间游行。到已，夏安居，为五百优婆塞说法，建立五百僧伽蓝，绳床、卧褥、供养众具悉皆备足。三月过已，具足三明，即于彼处入无余涅槃。
