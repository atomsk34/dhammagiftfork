SĀ 347

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 347（三四七） 须深

Parallel to: SN 12.70

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha. The king, ministers, brahmins, elders, householders, and other worldly people all respected, revered, and made offerings to the Buddha and the assembly of śrāvakas, who received abundant offerings of robes, food and drink, bedding, and medicine. They did not respect, revere, or make offerings to the various heterodox paths in terms of robes, food and drink, bedding, and medicine.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。若王、大臣、婆罗门、长者、居士及余世人所共恭敬、尊重、供养，佛及诸声闻众大得利养，衣被、饮食、卧具、汤药；都不恭敬、尊重、供养众邪异道衣被、饮食、卧具、汤药。

At that time, many non-Buddhist paths gathered in the hall and discussed: "In the past, we were always respected, revered, and made offerings to by the king, ministers, elders, householders, and others in terms of robes, food and drink, bedding, and medicine. Now, all that has been cut off. They only respect, revere, and make offerings to the śramaṇa Gautama and his assembly of śrāvakas in terms of robes, food and drink, bedding, and medicine. Now, who among us has wisdom and strength, who is able to secretly go to the assembly of the śramaṇa Gautama, become a monk, hear his teachings, return and explain them in detail, so that we can again use those teachings to transform the kings, ministers, elders, and householders of the countries, causing them to believe and enjoy them, so that we can again receive offerings as before?"

尔时，众多异道聚会未曾讲堂，作如是论：「我等昔来常为国王、大臣、长者、居士及余一切之所奉事，恭敬供养衣被、饮食、卧具、汤药，今悉断绝，但恭敬供养沙门瞿昙、声闻大众衣被、饮食、卧具、汤药。今此众中，谁有智慧、大力，堪能密往诣彼沙门瞿昙众中出家，闻彼法已，来还广说，我等当复用彼闻法化诸国王、大臣、长者、居士，令其信乐，可得还复供养如前？」

Then someone said: "There is a young man named Subhadra who is intelligent and wise. He is capable of secretly going to the assembly of the śramaṇa Gautama, becoming a monk, hearing his teachings, and returning to explain them in detail."

时，有人言：「有一年少，名曰须深，聪明黠慧，堪能密往沙门瞿昙众中出家，听彼法已，来还宣说。」

At that time, the non-Buddhists went to Subhadra and said: "Today, our great assembly gathered in the hall and discussed: 'In the past, we were respected, revered, and made offerings to by the kings, ministers, elders, householders, and others in the world in terms of robes, food and drink, bedding, and medicine. Now, all that has been cut off. The kings, ministers, elders, householders, and others in the world all respect, revere, and make offerings to the śramaṇa Gautama and his assembly of śrāvakas. Who among us is intelligent and wise, who is capable of secretly going to the assembly of the śramaṇa Gautama, becoming a monk, hearing his teachings, returning and explaining them in detail, transforming the kings, ministers, elders, householders, and others in the world, causing us to be respected, revered, and made offerings to again?' Among them, some said: 'Only Subhadra is intelligent and wise. He is capable of secretly going to the Dharma of Gautama, becoming a monk, hearing his teachings, being able to uphold them all, and returning to explain them in detail.' Therefore, we have come here to invite you. You should go.

时，诸外道诣须深所，而作是言：「我今日大众聚集未曾讲堂，作如是论：『我等先来为诸国王、大臣、长者、居士及诸世人之所恭敬奉事，供养衣被、饮食、卧具、汤药，今悉断绝，国王、大臣、长者、居士及诸世间悉共奉事沙门瞿昙、声闻大众。我此众中，谁有聪明黠慧，堪能密往沙门瞿昙众中出家学道，闻彼法已，来还宣说，化诸国王、大臣、长者、居士、令我此众还得恭敬、尊重、供养？』其中有言：『唯有须深聪明黠慧，堪能密往瞿昙法中出家学道，闻彼说法，悉能受持，来还宣说。』是故我等故来相请，仁者当行。」

At that time, Subhadra silently accepted the invitation and went to the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

时，彼须深默然受请，诣王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, many bhikṣus were walking in the open air outside their dwellings. Then Subhadra went to the many bhikṣus and said: "Venerable ones! Can I now go forth and receive full ordination in the true Dharma to practice the holy life?"

时，众多比丘出房舍外露地经行。尔时，须深诣众多比丘，而作是言：「诸尊！我今可得于正法中出家受具足，修梵行不？」

At that time, the many bhikṣus brought Subhadra to where the World-Honored One was, bowed their heads at his feet, and withdrew to one side. They said to the Buddha: "World-Honored One! This non-Buddhist Subhadra now wishes to go forth and receive full ordination in the true Dharma to practice the holy life."

时，众多比丘将彼须深诣世尊所，稽首礼足，退住一面，白佛言：「世尊！今此外道须深欲求于正法中出家受具足，修梵行。」

At that time, the World-Honored One knew the thoughts in the mind of the non-Buddhist Subhadra. He told the bhikṣus: "You should ordain that non-Buddhist Subhadra and let him go forth." At that time, the bhikṣus wished to ordain Subhadra.

尔时，世尊知外道须深心之所念，告诸比丘：「汝等当度彼外道须深，令得出家。」时，诸比丘愿度须深。

After half a month of going forth, a bhikṣu said to Subhadra: "Subhadra should know, our births and deaths are already exhausted, the holy life is already established, what was to be done is already done, we know for ourselves that we will not accept future rebirths."

出家已经半月，有一比丘语须深言：「须深当知，我等生死已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。」

At that time, Subhadra said to the bhikṣu: "Venerable one! How is it that one who has studied to be free from desires and evil and unwholesome dharmas, with initial and sustained thought, has joy and happiness born of separation, and is fully endowed with the first dhyāna, without giving rise to any taints, and is well liberated in mind?"

时，彼须深语比丘言：「尊者！云何？学离欲、恶不善法，有觉有观，离生喜乐，具足初禅，不起诸漏，心善解脱耶？」

The bhikṣu replied: "No, Subhadra!"

比丘答言：「不也，须深！」

He further asked: "How is it that one who is free from initial and sustained thought, with inner purity and one-pointed mind, without initial and sustained thought, has joy and happiness born of concentration, is fully endowed with the second dhyāna, without giving rise to any taints, and is well liberated in mind?"

复问：「云何？离有觉有观，内净一心，无觉无观，定生喜乐，具足第二禅，不起诸漏，心善解脱耶？」

The bhikṣu replied: "No, Subhadra!"

比丘答言：「不也，须深！」

He further asked: "How is it that the Venerable One, free from joy, dwells in equanimity with right mindfulness and right knowledge, experiencing physical and mental bliss, as described by the sages, fully endowed with the third dhyāna, without giving rise to any taints, and is well liberated in mind?"

复问：「云何？尊者离喜，舍心住正念正智，身心受乐，圣说及舍，具足第三禅，不起诸漏，心善解脱耶？」

He replied: "No, Subhadra!"

答言：「不也，须深！」

He further asked: "How is it that the Venerable One, free from suffering and cessation of pleasure, with joy and sorrow already cut off, dwells in equanimity, with pure mindfulness and one-pointed mind, fully endowed with the fourth dhyāna, without giving rise to any taints, and is well liberated in mind?"

复问：「云何？尊者离苦息乐，忧喜先断，不苦不乐舍，净念一心，具足第四禅，不起诸漏，心善解脱耶？」

He replied: "No, Subhadra!"

答言：「不也，须深！」

He further asked: "If one again gives rise to form and formless states, and dwells in full possession of the body's realization of tranquility and liberation, without giving rise to any taints, is one well liberated in mind?"

复问：「若复寂静解脱起色、无色，身作证具足住，不起诸漏，心善解脱耶？」

He replied: "No, Subhadra!"

答言：「不也，须深！」

Subhadra further asked: "How is it that the Venerable's words are inconsistent, contradicting each other before and after? How can one not attain dhyāna concentration and yet still make predictions?"

须深复问：「云何？尊者所说不同，前后相违。云何不得禅定而复记说？」

The bhikṣu replied: "I am one liberated by wisdom."

比丘答言：「我是慧解脱也。」

Having said this, many bhikṣus rose from their seats and left.

作是说已，众多比丘各从座起而去。

At that time, Subhūti, knowing that the many bhikṣus had left, had this thought: "The words of these Venerables are inconsistent, contradictory before and after, saying they do not attain right concentration, yet they also declare that they know for themselves." Having had this thought, he went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said to the Buddha: "World-Honored One! Those many bhikṣus declared in front of me: 'Our births are exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and we know for ourselves that we will not undergo further rebirth.' I then asked those Venerables: 'Have you attained liberation from desire and evil and unwholesome dharmas, up to personally realizing it with the body, not giving rise to the taints, and having well liberated minds?' They replied to me: 'No, Subhūti!' I then asked: 'Your words are inconsistent, contradictory before and after, saying you do not enter right concentration, yet you also declare that you know for yourselves.' They replied to me: 'We are liberated by wisdom.' Having said this, they each rose from their seats and left. I now ask the World-Honored One: 'How is it that their words are inconsistent, contradictory before and after, they do not attain right concentration, yet they also say that they know for themselves?'"

尔时，须深知众多比丘去已，作是思惟：「此诸尊者所说不同，前后相违，言不得正受，而复记说自知作证。」作是思惟已，往诣佛所，稽首礼足，退住一面，白佛言：「世尊！彼众多比丘于我面前记说：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』我即问彼尊者：『得离欲、恶不善法，乃至身作证，不起诸漏，心善解脱耶？』彼答我言：『不也，须深！』我即问言：『所说不同，前后相违，言不入正受，而复记说，自知作证。』彼答我言：『得慧解脱。』作此说已，各从座起而去。我今问世尊：『云何彼所说不同，前后相违，不得正受，而复说言：「自知作证。」？』」

The Buddha told Subhūti: "They first know the abiding of the Dharma, and later know nirvāṇa. Those good sons, in a solitary place, diligently contemplate, practice the Dharma of non-negligence, are free from the view of self, do not give rise to the taints, and have well liberated minds."

佛告须深：「彼先知法住，后知涅槃。彼诸善男子独一静处，专精思惟，不放逸法，离于我见，不起诸漏，心善解脱。」

Subhūti said to the Buddha: "I do not know now what it means to first know the abiding of the Dharma and later know nirvāṇa. Those good sons, in a solitary place, diligently contemplate, practice the Dharma of non-negligence, are free from the view of self, do not give rise to the taints, and have well liberated minds."

须深白佛：「我今不知先知法住，后知涅槃。彼诸善男子独一静处，专精思惟，不放逸法，离于我见，不起诸漏，心善解脱。」

The Buddha told Subhūti: "I do not ask whether you know or do not know. First know the abiding of the Dharma, and later know nirvāṇa. Those good sons, in a solitary place, diligently contemplate, practice the Dharma of non-negligence, are free from the view of self, and have well liberated minds."

佛告须深：「不问汝知不知，且自先知法住，后知涅槃。彼诸善男子独一静处，专精思惟，不放逸住，离于我见，心善解脱。」

Subhūti said to the Buddha: "I only wish that the World-Honored One would teach the Dharma for me, enabling me to know the wisdom of abiding in the Dharma and to see the wisdom of abiding in the Dharma."

须深白佛：「唯愿世尊为我说法，令我得知法住智，得见法住智。」

The Buddha said to Subhūti: "I now ask you, answer me as you wish. Subhūti! What do you think? Is there old age and death because of birth, and is there old age and death apart from birth?"

佛告须深：「我今问汝，随意答我。须深！于意云何？有生故有老死，不离生有老死耶？」

Subhūti replied: "Yes, World-Honored One!"

须深答曰：「如是，世尊！」

"Because of birth, there is old age and death, and there is old age and death apart from birth. In the same way, because of birth, there is existence, grasping, craving, feeling, contact, the six sense bases, name and form, consciousness, formations, and ignorance. Because of ignorance, there are formations, and are there formations apart from ignorance?"

「有生故有老死，不离生有老死。如是生、有、取、爱、受、触、六入处、名色、识、行、无明，有无明故有行，不离无明而有行耶？」

Subhūti said to the Buddha: "Yes, World-Honored One! Because of ignorance, there are formations, and are there formations apart from ignorance?"

须深白佛：「如是，世尊！有无明故有行，不离无明而有行。」

The Buddha said to Subhūti: "Because of no birth, there is no old age and death, and is there no old age and death apart from the cessation of birth?"

佛告须深：「无生故无老死，不离生灭而老死灭耶？」

Subhūti said to the Buddha: "Yes, World-Honored One! Because of no birth, there is no old age and death, and is there no old age and death apart from the cessation of birth?"

须深白佛言：「如是，世尊！无生故无老死，不离生灭而老死灭。」

"Is it like this, up to because of no ignorance, there are no formations, and is there no cessation of formations apart from the cessation of ignorance?"

「如是，乃至无无明故无行，不离无明灭而行灭耶？」

Subhūti said to the Buddha: "Yes, World-Honored One! Because of no ignorance, there are no formations, and is there no cessation of formations apart from the cessation of ignorance?"

须深白佛：「如是，世尊！无无明故无行，不离无明灭而行灭。」

The Buddha told Subhūti: "If one knows and sees in this way, is there detachment from desire and evil and unwholesome dharmas, up to personally realizing and abiding in them?"

佛告须深：「作如是知、如是见者，为有离欲、恶不善法，乃至身作证具足住不？」

Subhūti said to the Buddha: "No, World-Honored One!"

须深白佛：「不也，世尊！」

The Buddha told Subhūti: "This is called first knowing the abiding of dharmas, and then knowing nirvāṇa. Those good sons, in a solitary and quiet place, diligently contemplate and abide without negligence, free from the view of self, not giving rise to the taints, their minds well liberated."

佛告须深：「是名先知法住，后知涅槃。彼诸善男子独一静处，专精思惟，不放逸住，离于我见，不起诸漏，心善解脱。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Venerable Subhūti was free from dust and defilements, and attained the purity of the Dharma eye.

佛说此经已，尊者须深远尘离垢，得法眼净。

At that time, Subhūti saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, realized the Dharma, crossed over doubts, did not rely on others' faith, did not rely on others' crossing over, and attained fearlessness in the true Dharma. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and said to the Buddha: "World-Honored One! I now repent. I secretly left home in the true Dharma. Therefore, I repent."

尔时，须深见法得法，觉法度疑，不由他信，不由他度，于正法中心得无畏，稽首佛足，白佛言：「世尊！我今悔过，我于正法中盗密出家，是故悔过。」

The Buddha said to Subhūti: "How did you secretly leave home in the true Dharma?"

佛告须深：「云何于正法中盗密出家？」

Subhūti said to the Buddha: "World-Honored One! Many non-Buddhists came to me and said: 'Subhūti, you should know that we were previously respected and offered by kings, ministers, elders, householders, and other worldly people, but now it has been cut off. We all now offer to the śramaṇa Gautama and his great assembly of disciples. You should secretly go to the śramaṇa Gautama and his assembly of disciples to leave home and receive the Dharma. Having obtained their Dharma, come back and proclaim it. We will then use that heard Dharma to teach and transform the world, causing them to respect and offer as before.' Therefore, World-Honored One! I secretly left home in the true Dharma and Vinaya. Today I repent. I only pray that the World-Honored One will allow me to repent out of compassion."

须深白佛言：「世尊！有众多外道来诣我所，语我言：『须深当知，我等先为国王、大臣、长者、居士及余世人恭敬供养，而今断绝，悉共供养沙门瞿昙、声闻大众。汝今密往沙门瞿昙、声闻众中出家受法，得彼法已，还来宣说我等，当以彼闻法教化世间，令彼恭敬供养如初。』是故，世尊！我于正法、律中盗密出家，今日悔过，唯愿世尊听我悔过，以哀愍故。」

The Buddha said to Subhūti: "I accept your repentance. You should fully explain: 'I was foolish, unskillful, and ignorant in the past. I secretly left home in the true Dharma and Vinaya. Today I repent. I see my own faults and know my own faults. In the future, I will accomplish the precepts, increase my merits, and never regress.' Why is this? Because ordinary people who have faults, see them themselves, know them themselves, and repent, will accomplish the precepts, increase their merits, and never regress in the future."

佛告须深：「受汝悔过，汝当具说：『我昔愚痴、不善、无智，于正法、律盗密出家，今日悔过，自见罪、自知罪，于当来世律仪成就，功德增长，终不退减。』所以者何？凡人有罪，自见、自知而悔过者，于当来世律仪成就，功德增长，终不退减。」

The Buddha said to Subhūti: "Now I will give an analogy. The wise understand through analogies. It is like a king having a guard who captures thieves, binds them, and brings them to the king, saying: 'Great King! This person is a thief. Please punish him.' The king says: 'Take the criminal away, bind his hands behind his back, proclaim his evil deeds throughout the country, and then take him outside the city to the place of execution, and pierce his body with a hundred spears.' The executioner receives the king's order, takes the criminal, binds his hands behind his back, proclaims his evil deeds throughout the city, and takes him outside the city to the place of execution, and pierces his body with a hundred spears. At noon, the king asks: 'Is the criminal still alive?' The minister says: 'He is still alive.' The king again orders the minister: 'Pierce him with another hundred spears.' At dusk, he is pierced with another hundred spears, but he still does not die."

佛告须深：「今当说譬，其智慧者，以譬得解。譬如国王有防逻者，捉捕盗贼，缚送王所，白言：『大王！此人劫盗，愿王处罪。』王言：『将罪人去，反缚两手，恶声宣令，周遍国中，然后将出城外刑罪人处，遍身四体，劖以百矛。』彼典刑者受王教令，送彼罪人，反缚两手，恶声宣唱，周遍城邑，将出城外刑罪人处，遍身四体，劖以百矛。日中，王问：『罪人活耶？』臣白言：『活。』王复勅臣：『复劖百矛。』至日晡时，复劖百矛，彼犹不死。」

The Buddha said to Subhūti: "The king punished that criminal by piercing him with three hundred spears. Was there any part of his body that was not pierced, like the palm of a hand?"

佛告须深：「彼王治罪，劖以三百矛，彼罪人身宁有完处如手掌不？」

Subhūti said to the Buddha: "No, World-Honored One!"

须深白佛：「无也，世尊！」

He further asked Subhūti: "At that time, did the criminal who was pierced with three hundred spears suffer extreme pain?"

复问须深：「时彼罪人，劖以三百矛因缘，受苦极苦剧不？」

Subhūti said to the Buddha: "Extremely painful, World-Honored One! If pierced with one spear, the pain is unbearable, how could it be bearable with three hundred spears?"

须深白佛：「极苦。世尊！若劖以一矛，苦痛难堪，况三百矛，当可堪忍？」

The Buddha told Subhūti: "This is still bearable. If one secretly becomes a monk in the true Dharma and Vinaya, steals the Dharma, and expounds it to others, the pain they will suffer will be many times worse than that."

佛告须深：「此尚可耳，若于正法、律盗密出家，盗受持法，为人宣说，当受苦痛倍过于彼。」

When the Buddha spoke this Dharma, the non-Buddhist Subhūti attained the destruction of the taints and understood. After the Buddha spoke this sūtra, the Venerable Subhūti, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说是法时，外道须深漏尽意解，佛说此经已，尊者须深闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
