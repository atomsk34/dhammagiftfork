SĀ 348

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 348（三四八） 十力

Parallel to: SN 12.22

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time the World-Honored One addressed the bhikṣus, saying: "The Tathāgata has accomplished the ten powers, attained the four fearlessnesses, knows the abodes of the past buddhas, and is able to turn the wheel of the Dharma in the great assembly, proclaiming: 'Because this exists, that exists; because this arises, that arises. That is to say, conditioned by ignorance, there are karmic formations...' and so on, in detail, up to 'the great mass of suffering arises, the great mass of suffering ceases.' Bhikṣus! This is the manifestation of the true teaching, which cuts off the flow of birth and death, and so on, up to the full manifestation of that person. Thus, the manifestation of the true teaching cuts off the flow of birth and death, and is sufficient to cause a good man of right faith to renounce the household life, practice diligently, abide without negligence, and diligently practice the path in the true Dharma and Vinaya, with skin, sinews, and bones standing out, blood and flesh dried up; if he has not yet attained what he should attain, he does not abandon earnest effort, but practices with firm resolve and endurance. Why is this? Because dwelling in laziness and suffering gives rise to various evil and unwholesome dharmas, which bind one to future existence, burning and increasing, and in future lives one is born, ages, falls ill, and dies, losing the great meaning. But for one who diligently dwells alone, various evil and unwholesome dharmas do not arise, and the bonds of future existence do not burn and increase. In future lives, birth, aging, illness, and death do not increase, and the great meaning is fulfilled, attaining the supreme field of the teaching. That is to say, in the presence of the great teacher, personally receiving the teaching, attaining the peace of nirvāṇa, the right path to bodhi, the well-gone, and right enlightenment. Therefore, bhikṣus! You should observe self-benefit, benefiting others, and benefiting both self and others, diligently practicing and studying. I now renounce the household life, not foolishly or deludedly, with fruit and happiness. Those who make offerings of clothing, food and drink, bedding, and medicine will all attain great fruit, great merit, and great benefit. You should train in this way."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「如来成就十种力，得四无畏，知先佛住处，能转梵轮，于大众中震师子吼言：『此有故彼有，此起故彼起，谓缘无明行……』」广说乃至「『纯大苦聚集，纯大苦聚灭。』诸比丘！此是真实教法显现，断生死流，乃至其人悉善显现。如是真实教法显现，断生死流，足令善男子正信、出家，方便修习，不放逸住，于正法、律精勤苦行，皮筋骨立，血肉枯竭；若其未得所当得者，不舍慇懃精进，方便坚固堪能。所以者何？懈怠苦住，能生种种恶不善法，当来有结，炽然增长，于未来世生、老、病、死，退其大义故。精进乐独住者，不生种种恶不善法，当来有结，炽然苦报，不于未来世增长生、老、病、死，大义满足，得成第一教法之场。所谓大师面前，亲承说法，寂灭涅槃，菩提正向，善逝、正觉。是故，比丘！当观自利、利他、自他俱利，精勤修学，我今出家，不愚不惑，有果有乐，诸所供养衣服、饮食、卧具、汤药者，悉得大果、大福、大利，当如是学。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha addressed the bhikṣus, saying: "The Tathāgata has accomplished the ten powers, attained the four fearlessnesses, knows the abodes of the past buddhas, and is able to turn the wheel of the Dharma in the great assembly, proclaiming: 'Because this exists, that exists; because this arises, that arises. That

佛说是经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
