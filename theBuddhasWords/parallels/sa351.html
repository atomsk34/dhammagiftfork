SĀ 351

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 351（三五一） 茂师罗

Parallel to: SN 12.68

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Venerable Nārada, the Venerable Maudgalyāyana, the Venerable Uttama, and the Venerable Ānanda were staying at the Elephant Ear Pond near Śrāvastī.

一时，尊者那罗、尊者茂师罗、尊者殊胜、尊者阿难住舍卫国象耳池侧。

At that time, the Venerable Nārada said to the Venerable Maudgalyāyana, "Is there a different faith, desire, hearing, perception, and view, and is there a different right self-awakened knowledge and vision that arises, namely, that because of birth there is old age and death, and that old age and death are not separate from birth?" The Venerable Maudgalyāyana said, "There is a different faith, desire, hearing, perception, and view, and there is a different right self-awakened knowledge and vision that arises, namely, that because of birth there is old age and death, and that old age and death are not different from birth. It is said to be so.

尔时，尊者那罗语尊者茂师罗言：「有异信、异欲、异闻、异行觉想、异见审谛忍，有如是正自觉知见生，所谓生故有老死，不离生有老死耶？」尊者茂师罗言：「有异信、异欲、异闻、异行觉想、异见审谛忍，有如是正自觉知见生，所谓有生故有老死，不异生有老死。如是说有。」

"Venerable Maudgalyāyana, is there a different faith, up to a different acceptance, and does one attain the right self-awakened knowledge and vision that arises, namely, that there is cessation, peace, and nirvāṇa?"

「尊者茂师罗！有异信，乃至异忍，得自觉知见生，所谓有灭、寂灭、涅槃耶？」

The Venerable Maudgalyāyana replied, "There is a different faith, up to a different acceptance, and one attains the right self-awakened knowledge and vision that arises, namely, that there is cessation, peace, and nirvāṇa."

尊者茂师罗答言：「有异信，乃至异忍，得自觉知见生，所谓有灭、寂灭、涅槃。」

He further asked, "Venerable Maudgalyāyana, is it that cessation is peace and nirvāṇa? You now are an arhat, with all taints exhausted?"

复问：「尊者茂师罗！有灭则寂灭、涅槃，说者汝今便是阿罗汉，诸漏尽耶？」

The Venerable Maudgalyāyana remained silent and did not answer. He was asked a second and third time, but he still remained silent and did not answer.

尊者茂师罗默然不答，第二、第三问亦默然不答。

At that time, the Venerable Viśiṣṭa said to the Venerable Maudgalyāyana, "You should stop now. I will answer the Venerable Nārada on your behalf."

尔时，尊者殊胜语尊者茂师罗：「汝今且止，我当为汝答尊者那罗。」

The Venerable Maudgalyāyana said, "I will stop now. You answer on my behalf."

尊者茂师罗言：「我今且止，汝为我答。」

At that time, the Venerable Viśiṣṭa said to the Venerable Nārada, "There is a different faith, up to a different acceptance, that one can attain self-realization and insight, namely, that with cessation there is peace, nirvāṇa."

尔时，尊者殊胜语尊者那罗：「有异信，乃至异忍，得自觉知见生，所谓有灭则寂灭、涅槃。」

Then the Venerable Nārada asked the Venerable Viśiṣṭa, "Is it that with a different faith, up to a different acceptance, one can attain self-realization and insight, namely, that with cessation there is peace, nirvāṇa? Are you now an arhat with exhausted defilements?"

时，尊者那罗问尊者殊胜言：「有异信，乃至异忍，得自觉知见生，所谓有灭则寂灭、涅槃者，汝今便是漏尽阿罗汉耶？」

The Venerable Viśiṣṭa said, "I speak of cessation as peace, nirvāṇa, but I am not an arhat with exhausted defilements."

尊者殊胜言：「我说有灭则寂灭、涅槃，而非漏尽阿罗汉也。」

The Venerable Nārada said, "What is said is inconsistent, contradicting each other. As the Venerable has said, with cessation there is peace, nirvāṇa, but then he says he is not an arhat with exhausted defilements?"

尊者那罗言：「所说不同，前后相违，如尊者所说，有灭则寂灭、涅槃，而复言非漏尽阿罗汉耶？」

The Venerable Viśiṣṭa said to the Venerable Nārada, "Now I will give an analogy. The wise understand through analogies. Like a well on the side of a wilderness road, without a rope or bucket, one can still get water from it. At that time, a traveler, tormented by heat and thirst, circled the well looking for a rope or bucket, but seeing the well water as it really is, he did not touch his body. In the same way, I say that with cessation there is peace, nirvāṇa, but I myself do not attain the arahantship with exhausted defilements."

尊者殊胜语尊者那罗言：「今当说譬，夫智者以譬得解，如旷野路边有井，无绳无罐，得取其水。时，有行人，热渴所逼，绕井求觅，无绳无罐，谛观井水，如实知见，而不触身。如是，我说有灭则寂灭、涅槃，而自不得漏尽阿罗汉。」

At that time, the Venerable Ānanda said to the Venerable Nārada, "What do you think of what was said by the Venerable Viśiṣṭa?"

尔时，尊者阿难语尊者那罗言：「彼尊者殊胜所说，汝复云何？」

The Venerable Nārada said to the Venerable Ānanda, "The Venerable Viśiṣṭa spoke well of the truth. What more is there to say?"

尊者那罗语尊者阿难言：「尊者殊胜善说真实，知复何言。」

At that time, those noble ones, having each spoken, rose from their seats and departed.

时，彼正士各各说已，从座起去。
