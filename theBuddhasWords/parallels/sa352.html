SĀ 352

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 352（三五二） 沙门婆罗门

Parallel to: SN 12.14

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "If śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas do not know the Dharma as it really is, do not know the origin, cessation, and path of the cessation of the Dharma, they are not śramaṇas or śramaṇa-like, not brāhmaṇas or brāhmaṇa-like, and do not have the meaning of śramaṇas or brāhmaṇas. They do not see the Dharma and know for themselves that they have attained: 'My birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and I know that I will not be subject to any future lives.'

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「若诸沙门、婆罗门于法不如实知，法集、法灭、法灭道迹不如实知，彼非沙门、沙门数，非婆罗门、婆罗门数，彼亦非沙门义、婆罗门义，见法自知作证：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』

What is not knowing the Dharma as it really is? What is not knowing the origin of the Dharma? What is not knowing the cessation of the Dharma? What is not knowing the path leading to the cessation of the Dharma? It means not knowing old age and death as it really is, not knowing the origin, cessation, and path leading to the cessation of old age and death. Thus, not knowing birth, existence, grasping, craving, feeling, contact, and the six sense bases as they really are, not knowing the origin, cessation, and path leading to the cessation of the six sense bases. Thus, not knowing these dharmas as they really are, not knowing the origin, cessation, and path leading to the cessation of the Dharma.

「云何法不如实知？云何法集不如实知？云何法灭不如实知？云何法灭道迹不如实知？谓于老死法不如实知，老死集、老死灭、老死灭道迹不如实知。如是生、有、取、爱、受、触、六入处不如实知，六入处集、六入处灭、六入处灭道迹不如实知。如是诸法不如实知，法集、法灭、法灭道迹不如实知。

If śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas know the Dharma as it really is, know the origin, cessation, and path leading to the cessation of the Dharma, they are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, śramaṇas among śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas among brāhmaṇas. They have the meaning of śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas. They see the Dharma and know for themselves that they have attained: 'My birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and I know that I will not be subject to any future lives.'

「若诸沙门、婆罗门于法如实知，法集、法灭、法灭道迹如实知，当知是沙门、婆罗门，沙门之沙门数、婆罗门之婆罗门数。彼以沙门义、婆罗门义，见法自知作证：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』

What is knowing the Dharma as it really is? What is knowing the origin, cessation, and path leading to the cessation of the Dharma? It means knowing old age and death as it really is, knowing the origin, cessation, and path leading to the cessation of old age and death. Thus, knowing birth, existence, grasping, craving, feeling, contact, and the six sense bases as they really are, knowing the origin, cessation, and path leading to the cessation of the six sense bases. Thus, knowing these dharmas as they really are, knowing the origin, cessation, and path leading to the cessation of the Dharma.

「何等法如实知？何等法集、法灭、法灭道迹如实知？谓老死法如实知，老死集、老死灭、老死灭道迹如实知。如是生、有、取、爱、受、触、六入处如实知，六入处集、六入处灭、六入处灭道迹如实知。如是诸法如实知，法集、法灭、法灭道迹如实知。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished what he had to say. The bhikṣus, having heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说是经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
