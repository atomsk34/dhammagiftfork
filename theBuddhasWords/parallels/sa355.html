SĀ 355

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 355（三五五） 老死

Parallel to: SN 12.28

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus: "You should be aware of old age and death, the arising of old age and death, the cessation of old age and death, and the path leading to the cessation of old age and death. In this way, you should be aware of formations, the arising of formations, the cessation of formations, and the path leading to the cessation of formations.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「当觉知老死，觉知老死集、老死灭、老死灭道迹。如是，乃至当觉知行、行集、行灭、行灭道迹。

"How should one be aware of old age and death? One should be aware that old age and death arise due to birth. In this way, one is aware of old age and death. How is the arising of old age and death to be known? The arising of birth is the arising of old age and death. In this way, one is aware of the arising of old age and death. How is the cessation of old age and death to be known? The cessation of birth is the cessation of old age and death. In this way, one is aware of the cessation of old age and death. How is the path leading to the cessation of old age and death to be known? The Noble Eightfold Path is the path leading to the cessation of old age and death. In this way, one is aware of the path leading to the cessation of old age and death. How should one be aware of formations? There are three kinds of formations: bodily formations, verbal formations, and mental formations. In this way, one is aware of formations. How is the arising of formations to be known? The arising of ignorance is the arising of formations. In this way, one is aware of the arising of formations. How is the cessation of formations to be known? The cessation of ignorance is the cessation of formations. In this way, one is aware of the cessation of formations. How is the path leading to the cessation of formations to be known? The Noble Eightfold Path is the path leading to the cessation of formations. In this way, one is aware of the path leading to the cessation of formations."

「云何当觉知老死？觉知缘生故有老死，如是老死觉知。云何老死集？生集是老死集，如是老死集觉知。云何老死灭觉知？谓生灭是老死灭，如是老死灭觉知。云何老死灭道迹觉知？谓八圣道是老死灭道迹，如是老死灭道迹觉知，乃至云何行觉知？谓三行，身行、口行、意行，如是行觉知。云何行集觉知？谓无明集是行集，如是行集觉知。云何行灭觉知？无明灭是行灭，如是行灭觉知。云何行灭道迹觉知？谓八圣道是行灭道迹，如是行灭道迹觉知。」

After the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus who heard what the Buddha taught rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说是经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
