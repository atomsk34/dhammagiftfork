SĀ 37

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 37（三七） 我

Parallel to: SN 22.94 / SĀ 38 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One addressed the bhikṣus, saying: "I do not dispute with the world, but the world disputes with me. Why is that? Bhikṣus! If one speaks in accordance with the Dharma, one does not dispute with the world. The worldly wise say it exists, and I also say it exists. What do the worldly wise say exists, and I also say exists? Bhikṣus! Form is impermanent, suffering, and subject to change. The worldly wise say it exists, and I also say it exists. Likewise, feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness are impermanent, suffering, and subject to change. The worldly wise say it exists, and I also say it exists. The worldly wise say it does not exist, and I also say it does not exist. That is to say, form is permanent, stable, unchanging, and rightly abiding. The worldly wise say it does not exist, and I also say it does not exist. Feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness are permanent, stable, unchanging, and rightly abiding. The worldly wise say it does not exist, and I also say it does not exist. This is called what the worldly wise say does not exist, and I also say does not exist. Bhikṣus! There is the world and worldly dharmas. I myself know and realize them, and I explain and reveal them to others. The blind and eyeless in the world do not know or see them. That is not my fault.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「我不与世间诤，世间与我诤。所以者何？比丘！若如法语者，不与世间诤，世间智者言有，我亦言有。云何为世间智者言有，我亦言有。比丘！色无常、苦、变易法，世间智者言有，我亦言有。如是受、想、行、识，无常、苦、变易法，世间智者言有，我亦言有。世间智者言无，我亦言无；谓色是常、恒、不变易、正住者，世间智者言无，我亦言无。受、想、行、识，常、恒、不变易、正住者，世间智者言无，我亦言无，是名世间智者言无，我亦言无。比丘！有世间世间法，我亦自知自觉，为人分别演说显示，世间盲无目者不知不见，非我咎也。

"Bhikṣus! What are the worldly dharmas of the world that I know and realize myself, and explain and reveal to others? The blind and eyeless do not know or see them. What can I do for those blind and eyeless who do not know or see them?"

「诸比丘！云何为世间世间法，我自知，我自觉，为人演说，分别显示，盲无目者不知不见？是比丘！色无常、苦、变易法，是名世间世间法；如是受、想、行、识，无常、苦，是世间世间法。比丘！此是世间世间法，我自知自觉，为人分别演说显示，盲无目者不知不见。我于彼盲无目不知不见者，其如之何！」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished what he had to say. The bhikṣus, having heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
