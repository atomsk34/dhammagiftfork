SĀ 379

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 379（三七九） 转法轮

Parallel to: SN 56.11 (~) / SN 56.12

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying in the Deer Park at Bārāṇasī, the abode of the seers.

一时，佛住波罗［木＊奈］鹿野苑中仙人住处。

At that time the World-Honored One said to the five bhikṣus: "This is the noble truth of suffering, a truth that has not been heard before. One should contemplate it correctly. At that time, the eye, knowledge, wisdom, and understanding arise. This is the noble truth of the origin of suffering, the noble truth of the cessation of suffering, and the noble truth of the path leading to the cessation of suffering. These are truths that have not been heard before. One should contemplate them correctly. At that time, the eye, knowledge, wisdom, and understanding arise.

尔时，世尊告五比丘：「此苦圣谛，本所未曾闻法，当正思惟。时，生眼、智、明、觉，此苦集、此苦灭、此苦灭道迹圣谛，本所未曾闻法，当正思惟。时，生、眼、智、明、觉。

Furthermore, one should know the knowledge of the noble truth of suffering. One should contemplate it correctly. At that time, the eye, knowledge, wisdom, and understanding arise. One should know the noble truth of the origin of suffering and abandon it. This is a truth that has not been heard before. One should contemplate it correctly. At that time, the eye, knowledge, wisdom, and understanding arise.

「复次，苦圣谛智当复知，本所未闻法，当正思惟。时，生眼、智、明、觉，苦集圣谛已知当断，本所未曾闻法，当正思惟。时，生眼、智、明、觉。

Furthermore, one should know the noble truth of the cessation of suffering and realize it. This is a truth that has not been heard before. One should contemplate it correctly. At that time, the eye, knowledge, wisdom, and understanding arise. One should know the noble truth of the path leading to the cessation of suffering and cultivate it. This is a truth that has not been heard before. One should contemplate it correctly. At that time, the eye, knowledge, wisdom, and understanding arise.

「复次，苦集灭，此苦灭圣谛已知当知作证，本所未闻法，当正思惟。时，生眼、智、明、觉，复以此苦灭道迹圣谛已知当修，本所未曾闻法，当正思惟。时，生眼、智、明、觉。

Furthermore, monks! One should know the noble truth of suffering. Having known it, one should emerge from it. This is a truth that has not been heard before. One should contemplate it correctly. At that time, the eye, knowledge, wisdom, and understanding arise. One should know the noble truth of the origin of suffering. Having known it, one should abandon it and emerge from it. This is a truth that has not been heard before. One should contemplate it correctly. At that time, the eye, knowledge, wisdom, and understanding arise.

「复次，比丘！此苦圣谛已知，知已出，所未闻法，当正思惟。时，生眼、智、明、觉；复次，此苦集圣谛已知，已断出，所未闻法，当正思惟。时，生眼、智、明、觉。

Furthermore, monks! One should know the noble truth of the cessation of suffering. Having known it, one should realize it and emerge from it. This is a truth that has not been heard before. One should contemplate it correctly. At that time, the eye, knowledge, wisdom, and understanding arise. One should know the noble truth of the path leading to the cessation of suffering. Having known it, one should cultivate it and emerge from it. This is a truth that has not been heard before. One should contemplate it correctly. At that time, the eye, knowledge, wisdom, and understanding arise.

「复次，苦灭圣谛已知、已作证出，所未闻法，当正思惟。时，生眼、智、明、觉；复次，苦灭道迹圣谛已知、已修出，所未曾闻法，当正思惟。时，生眼、智、明、觉。

Monks! If I had not given rise to the eye, knowledge, wisdom, and understanding through the three turnings and twelve aspects of these four noble truths, I would not have been able to attain liberation, emancipation, and freedom from the assemblies of gods, demons, Brahmas, śramaṇas, and brahmins. I would not have been able to realize and attain supreme perfect enlightenment by myself. But because I have given rise to the eye, knowledge, wisdom, and understanding through the three turnings and twelve aspects of these four noble truths, I have been able to attain liberation, emancipation, and freedom from the assemblies of gods, demons, Brahmas, śramaṇas, and brahmins. I have been able to realize and attain supreme perfect enlightenment by myself.

「诸比丘！我于此四圣谛三转十二行不生眼、智、明、觉者，我终不得于诸天、魔、梵、沙门、婆罗门闻法众中，为解脱、为出、为离，亦不自证得阿耨多罗三藐三菩提。我已于四圣谛三转十二行生眼、智、明、觉，故于诸天、魔、梵、沙门、婆罗门闻法众中，得出、得脱，自证得成阿耨多罗三藐三菩提。」

At that time, when the World-Honored One spoke this Dharma, Venerable Channa and eighty thousand gods were freed from defilements, attained pure Dharma-eyes, and saw the Dharma.

尔时，世尊说是法时，尊者憍陈如及八万诸天远尘离垢，得法眼净。

"At that time, the World-Honored One said to Venerable Channa, "Do you understand the Dharma?

尔时，世尊告尊者憍陈如：「知法未？」

Channa said to the Buddha, "I understand, World-Honored One!"

憍陈如白佛：「已知。世尊！」

He again asked Venerable Channa, "Do you understand the Dharma?"

复告尊者憍陈如：「知法未？」

Channa said to the Buddha, "I understand, Well-Gone One!"

拘隣白佛：「已知。善逝！」

Venerable Channa understood the Dharma, so he was called Ājñātakauṇḍinya.

尊者拘隣已知法故，是故名阿若拘隣。

Venerable Ājñātakauṇḍinya understood the Dharma, and the earth deity raised her voice and proclaimed, "All you noble ones! The World-Honored One has turned the wheel of the Dharma three times with twelve aspects at the Deer Park in the Deer Park of the Sages in the country of Vārāṇasī. It has never been turned before by any śramaṇa, brāhmaṇa, god, Māra, or Brahmā. It is of great benefit, great peace, compassion for the world, benefit through meaning, benefit and peace for gods and humans, increasing the hosts of gods, and decreasing the hosts of asuras.

尊者阿若拘隣知法已，地神举声唱言：「诸仁者！世尊于波罗［木＊奈］国仙人住处鹿野苑中三转十二行法轮，诸沙门、婆罗门、诸天、魔、梵所未曾转，多所饶益，多所安乐，哀愍世间，以义饶益，利安天人，增益诸天众，减损阿修罗众。」

After the earth deity proclaimed it, the gods in the sky, the Four Heavenly Kings, the Thirty-three Heavens, the Yāma Heaven, the Tuṣita Heaven, the Nirmāṇarati Heaven, and the Paranirmitavaśavartin Heaven passed it on in succession. In an instant, it reached the Brahmā Heaven. The Brahmā Heaven, riding on the sound, proclaimed, "All you noble ones! The World-Honored One has turned the wheel of the Dharma three times with twelve aspects at the Deer Park in the Deer Park of the Sages in the country of Vārāṇasī. It has never been turned before by any śramaṇa, brāhmaṇa, god, Māra, or Brahmā. It is of great benefit, great peace, compassion for the world, benefit through meaning, benefit and peace for gods and humans, increasing the hosts of gods, and decreasing the hosts of asuras.

地神唱已，闻虚空神天、四天王天、三十三天、炎魔天、兜率陀天、化乐天、他化自在天展转传唱，须臾之间，闻于梵天身。梵天乘声唱言：「诸仁者，世尊于波罗［木＊奈］国仙人住处鹿野苑中三转十二行法轮，诸沙门、婆罗门、诸天、魔、梵，及世间闻法未所曾转，多所饶益，多所安乐，以义饶益诸天世人，增益诸天众，减损阿修罗众。」世尊于波罗［木＊奈］国仙人住处鹿野苑中转法轮，是故此经名转法轮经。

After the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus who heard what the Buddha had said rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
