SĀ 418

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 418（四一八） 受持

Parallel to: SN 56.16

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time the World-Honored One said to the bhikṣus, "Do you uphold the four noble truths that I have spoken?"

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「汝持我所说四圣谛不？」

Then a certain bhikṣu rose from his seat, arranged his robes, bowed to the Buddha, joined his palms, and said to the Buddha, "Yes, World-Honored One, I uphold the four noble truths that you have spoken. What are the four truths? The World-Honored One has spoken of the noble truth of suffering, which I uphold. The noble truth of the origin of suffering, the noble truth of the cessation of suffering, and the noble truth of the path leading to the cessation of suffering, I uphold all of them."

时，有异比丘从座起，整衣服，为佛作礼，合掌白佛言：「唯然，世尊所说四圣谛，我悉持之。云何四谛？世尊说苦圣谛，我悉持之，苦集圣谛、苦灭圣谛、苦灭道迹圣谛，我悉持之。」

The Buddha said to that bhikṣu, "Excellent! Excellent! You uphold the four noble truths as I have spoken them. Bhikṣus, if any śramaṇas or brāhmaṇas say, 'We will abandon the noble truth of suffering as spoken by the śramaṇa Gautama and establish another noble truth of suffering,' they will only have words. If asked, they will not know, and their doubts will increase, because it is not within their realm. The noble truth of the origin of suffering, the noble truth of the cessation of suffering, and the noble truth of the path leading to the cessation of suffering, we will now abandon and establish other four noble truths,' they will only have words. If asked, they will not know, and their doubts will increase, because it is not within their realm. Therefore, bhikṣus, those who have not yet attained the uninterrupted equality of the four noble truths should diligently cultivate supreme desire and study to attain uninterrupted equality."

佛告彼比丘：「善哉！善哉！如我所说四圣谛，汝悉持之。诸比丘！若沙门、婆罗门作如是说，如沙门瞿昙所说苦圣谛，我当舍，更立苦圣谛者，但有言数，问已不知，增其疑惑，以非其境界故。苦集圣谛、苦灭圣谛、苦灭道迹圣谛，我今当舍，更立余四圣谛者，彼但有言数，问已不知，增其疑惑，以非其境界故。是故，比丘！于四圣谛未无间等者，当勤方便，起增上欲，学无间等。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha said to the bhikṣus, "Excellent! Excellent!" The bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and upheld it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
