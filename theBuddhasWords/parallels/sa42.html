SĀ 42

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 42（四二） 七处

Parallel to: SN 22.57 / SĀ³ 3.27

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus: "There are seven kinds of skillful means and three kinds of contemplation. By exhausting these dharmas, one can attain the exhaustion of defilements, attain the undefiled, and attain liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom. In this very life, one can know for oneself, realize, and dwell in fulfillment, thinking: 'My birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and I know that I will not experience another existence after this one.' What are the seven kinds of skillful means for bhikṣus? Bhikṣus should know the truth about form, the origin of form, the cessation of form, the path leading to the cessation of form, the taste of form, the danger of form, and the escape from form. Similarly, they should know the truth about feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness, the origin of consciousness, the cessation of consciousness, the path leading to the cessation of consciousness, the taste of consciousness, the danger of consciousness, and the escape from consciousness.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「有七处善、三种观义。尽于此法得漏尽，得无漏，心解脱、慧解脱，现法自知身作证具足住：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』云何比丘七处善？比丘！如实知色、色集、色灭、色灭道迹、色味、色患、色离如实知；如是受、想、行、识，识集、识灭、识灭道迹、识味、识患、识离如实知。

What is the truth about form? All forms, the four great elements and the forms derived from the four great elements, are called form. This is the truth about form. What is the origin of form? Desire and joy are called the origin of form. This is the origin of form. What is the cessation of form? The cessation of desire and joy is called the cessation of form. This is the cessation of form. What is the path leading to the cessation of form? It is the eightfold noble path, namely: right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right concentration. This is the path leading to the cessation of form. What is the taste of form? It is the joy and pleasure that arise from form. This is the taste of form. What is the danger of form? If form is impermanent, suffering, and subject to change, this is the danger of form. This is the danger of form. What is the escape from form? It is the subduing of desire and greed for form, the cutting off of desire and greed, and transcending desire and greed. This is the escape from form. This is the escape from form.

「云何色如实知？诸所有色、一切四大及四大造色，是名为色，如是色如实知。云何色集如实知？爱喜是名色集，如是色集如实知。云何色灭如实知？爱喜灭是名色灭，如是色灭如实知。云何色灭道迹如实知？谓八圣道——正见、正志、正语、正业、正命、正方便、正念、正定，是名色灭道迹，如是色灭道迹如实知。云何色味如实知？谓色因缘生喜乐，是名色味，如是色味如实知。云何色患如实知？若色无常、苦、变易法，是名色患，如是色患如实知。云何色离如实知？谓于色调伏欲贪、断欲贪、越欲贪，是名色离，如是色离如实知。

What is the truth about feeling? It refers to the six kinds of feeling: feeling arising from contact with the eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind. This is feeling. This is the truth about feeling. What is the origin of feeling? The origin of contact is the origin of feeling. This is the origin of feeling. What is the cessation of feeling? The cessation of contact is the cessation of feeling. This is the cessation of feeling. What is the path leading to the cessation of feeling? It is the eightfold noble path, namely: right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right concentration. This is the path leading to the cessation of feeling. What is the taste of feeling? It is the joy and pleasure that arise from feeling. This is the taste of feeling. What is the danger of feeling? If feeling is impermanent, suffering, and subject to change, this is the danger of feeling. This is the danger of feeling. What is the escape from feeling? It is the subduing of desire and greed for feeling, the cutting off of desire and greed, and transcending desire and greed. This is the escape from feeling. This is the escape from feeling.

「云何受如实知？谓六受——眼触生受，耳、鼻、舌、身、意触生受，是名受，如是受如实知。云何受集如实知？触集是受集，如是受集如实知。云何受灭如实知？触灭是受灭，如是受灭如实知。云何受灭道迹如实知？谓八圣道——正见乃至正定，是名受灭道迹，如是受灭道迹如实知。云何受味如实知？受因缘生喜乐，是名受味，如是受味如实知。云何受患如实知？若无常、苦、变易法，是名受患，如是受患如实知。云何受离如实知？若于受调伏欲贪、断欲贪、越欲贪，是名受离，如是受离如实知。

What is the truth about perception? It refers to the six kinds of perception: perception arising from contact with the eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind. This is perception. This is the truth about perception. What is the origin of perception? The origin of contact is the origin of perception. This is the origin of perception. What is the cessation of perception? The cessation of contact is the cessation of perception. This is the cessation of perception. What is the path leading to the cessation of perception? It is the eightfold noble path, namely: right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right concentration. This is the path leading to the cessation of perception. What is the taste of perception? It is the joy and pleasure that arise from perception. This is the taste of perception. What is the danger of perception? If perception is impermanent, suffering, and subject to change, this is the danger of perception. This is the danger of perception. What is the escape from perception? It is the subduing of desire and greed for perception, the cutting off of desire and greed, and transcending desire and greed. This is the escape from perception. This is the escape from perception.

「云何想如实知？谓六想——眼触生想，耳、鼻、舌、身、意触生想，是名为想，如是想如实知。云何想集如实知？触集是想集，如是想集如实知。云何想灭如实知？触灭是想灭，如是想灭如实知。云何想灭道迹如实知？谓八圣道——正见乃至正定，是名想灭道迹，如是想灭道迹如实知。云何想味如实知？想因缘生喜乐，是名想味，如是想味如实知。云何想患如实知？若想无常、苦、变易法，是名想患，如是想患如实知。云何想离如实知？若于想调伏欲贪、断欲贪、越欲贪，是名想离，如是想离如实知。

What is the truth about volitional formations? It refers to the six kinds of volitional formations: volitional formations arising from contact with the eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind. This is volitional formations. This is the truth about volitional formations. What is the origin of volitional formations? The origin of contact is the origin of volitional formations. This is the origin of volitional formations. What is the cessation of volitional formations? The cessation of contact is the cessation of volitional formations. This is the cessation of volitional formations. What is the path leading to the cessation of volitional formations? It is the eightfold noble path, namely: right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right concentration. This is the path leading to the cessation of volitional formations. What is the taste of volitional formations? It is the joy and pleasure that arise from volitional formations. This is the taste of volitional formations. What is the danger of volitional formations? If volitional formations are impermanent, suffering, and subject to change, this is the danger of volitional formations. This is the danger of volitional formations. What is the escape from volitional formations? It is the subduing of desire and greed for volitional formations, the cutting off of desire and greed, and transcending desire and greed. This is the escape from volitional formations. This is the escape from volitional formations.

「云何行如实知？谓六思身——眼触生思，耳、鼻、舌、身、意触生思，是名为行，如是行如实知。云何行集如实知？触集是行集，如是行集如实知。云何行灭如实知？触灭是行灭，如是行灭如实知。云何行灭道迹如实知？谓八圣道——正见乃至正定，是名行灭道迹，如是行灭道迹如实知。云何行味如实知？行因缘生喜乐，是名行味，如是行味如实知。云何行患如实知？若行无常、苦、变易法，是名行患，如是行患如实知。云何行离如实知？若于行调伏欲贪、断欲贪、越欲贪，是名行离，如是行离如实知。

What is the truth about consciousness? It refers to the six kinds of consciousness: eye consciousness, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind consciousness. This is consciousness. This is the truth about consciousness. What is the origin of consciousness? The origin of name and form is the origin of consciousness. This is the origin of consciousness. What is the cessation of consciousness? The cessation of name and form is the cessation of consciousness. This is the cessation of consciousness. What is the path leading to the cessation of consciousness? It is the eightfold noble path, namely: right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right concentration. This is the path leading to the cessation of consciousness. What is the taste of consciousness? It is the joy and pleasure that arise from consciousness. This is the taste of consciousness. What is the danger of consciousness? If consciousness is impermanent, suffering, and subject to change, this is the danger of consciousness. This is the danger of consciousness. What is the escape from consciousness? It is the subduing of desire and greed for consciousness, the cutting off of desire and greed, and transcending desire and greed. This is the escape from consciousness. This is the escape from consciousness.

「云何识如实知？谓六识身——眼识，耳、鼻、舌、身、意识身，是名为识，如是识如实知。云何识集如实知？名色集是识集，如是识集如实知。云何识灭如实知？名色灭是识灭，如是识灭如实知。云何识灭道迹如实知？谓八圣道——正见乃至正定，是名识灭道迹，如是识灭道迹如实知。云何识味如实知？识因缘生喜乐，是名识味，如是识味如实知。云何识患如实知？若识无常、苦、变易法，是名识患，如是识患如实知。云何识离如实知？若识调伏欲贪、断欲贪、越欲贪，是名识离如实知。比丘！是名七处善。

"What are the three kinds of contemplation? If a monk, in a secluded place, under a tree, or in the open, contemplates the aggregates, sense bases, and sense fields, and contemplates their meaning with right effort, this is called the three kinds of contemplation. This is called the monk's sevenfold excellence and three kinds of contemplation. By exhausting these dharmas, one attains the exhaustion of defilements, attains the undefiled, and attains liberation of mind and liberation by wisdom, and abides in the realization of the fruit of liberation in the present life, knowing and realizing for oneself: 'My birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, there is no more of this state.'"

「云何三种观义？比丘！若于空闲、树下、露地，观察阴、界、入，正方便思惟其义，是名比丘三种观义。是名比丘七处善、三种观义。尽于此法得漏尽，得无漏，心解脱、慧解脱，现法自知作证具足住：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully practiced it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
