SĀ 441

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 441（四四一） 土等

Parallel to: SN 13.5 (~) / SN 13.9 (~) / SN 13.11 (~) / SN 56.49 (~) / SN 56.55 (~) / SN 56.59 (~) / SĀ 439 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One took a lump of earth in his hand, the size of a pear fruit, and said to the bhikṣus, "What do you think, bhikṣus? Is the lump of earth in my hand more, or is the earth and stone in the Great Snowy Mountains more?"

尔时，世尊手捉团土，大如梨果，告诸比丘：「云何？比丘！我手中此团土为多？大雪山中土石为多？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "The lump of earth in the World-Honored One's hand is very small. The earth and stone in that Snowy Mountain King are very much more, hundreds of thousands of koṭis of nayutas, and so on, not comparable by calculation or analogy."

诸比丘白佛言：「世尊手中团土少少耳，彼雪山王，其土石甚多，百千亿那由他，乃至算数譬类不得为比。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "Just as the lump of earth I am holding, so too are those beings who truly know the noble truth of suffering, the noble truth of the origin of suffering, the noble truth of the cessation of suffering, and the noble truth of the path leading to the cessation of suffering. Just as the earth and stone of the Great Snowy Mountain King, so too are those beings who do not truly know the noble truth of suffering, the noble truth of the origin of suffering, the noble truth of the cessation of suffering, and the noble truth of the path leading to the cessation of suffering. Therefore, bhikṣus, those who have not yet attained uninterrupted equality regarding the four noble truths should diligently arouse supreme desire and strive for uninterrupted equality."

佛告诸比丘：「如我所捉团土。如是，众生于苦圣谛如实知，于苦集圣谛、苦灭圣谛、苦灭道迹圣谛如实知者，亦复如是。如大雪山王土石者。如是，众生于苦圣谛不如实知，于苦集圣谛、苦灭圣谛、苦灭道迹圣谛不如实知者，亦复如是。是故，比丘！于四圣谛未无间等者，当勤方便，起增上欲，学无间等。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished what he had to say. The bhikṣus, having heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说是经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

Just as the Great Snowy Mountain King, so too are the Nimindhara Mountain, the Vinataka Mountain, the Horse Ear Mountain, the Good View Mountain, the Kaitara Mountain, the Iśādhara Mountain, the Yugandhara Mountain, the Sumeru Mountain King, and the earth and stone of the great earth.

如雪山王，如是尼民陀罗山、毘那多迦山、马耳山、善见山、佉提罗迦山、伊沙陀罗山、由揵陀罗山、须弥山王，及大地土石，亦复如是。

Just as a jujube fruit, so too are an amla fruit, a bhadra fruit, a kara fruit, a bean fruit, and even a garlic seed.

如梨果，如是阿摩勒迦果、跋陀罗果、迦罗迦果、豆果，乃至蒜子譬，亦复如是。
