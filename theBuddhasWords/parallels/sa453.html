SĀ 453

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 453（四五三） 受

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Various realms give rise to various contacts, various contacts give rise to various feelings, and various feelings give rise to various desires. What are the various realms? They are the eighteen realms: the eye realm, form realm, eye consciousness realm, up to the mind realm, dharma realm, and mind consciousness realm. These are called the various realms.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「缘种种界生种种触，缘种种触生种种受，缘种种受生种种爱。云何种种界？谓十八界，眼界、色界、眼识界，乃至意界、法界、意识界，是名种种界。

How do various realms give rise to various contacts, various contacts give rise to various feelings, and various feelings give rise to various desires? It is like this: the eye realm gives rise to eye contact, but eye contact does not give rise to the eye realm. Only the eye realm gives rise to eye contact; eye contact gives rise to eye feeling, but eye feeling does not give rise to eye contact. Only eye contact gives rise to eye feeling; eye feeling gives rise to eye desire, but eye desire does not give rise to eye feeling. Only eye feeling gives rise to eye desire. In the same way, the ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind realms give rise to mind contact, but mind contact does not give rise to the mind realm. Only the mind realm gives rise to mind contact; mind contact gives rise to mind feeling, but mind feeling does not give rise to mind contact. Only mind contact gives rise to mind feeling; mind feeling gives rise to mind desire, but mind desire does not give rise to mind feeling. Only mind feeling gives rise to mind desire.

「云何缘种种界生种种触？缘种种触生种种受？缘种种受生种种爱？谓缘眼界生眼触，非缘眼触生眼界，但缘眼界生眼触；缘眼触生眼受，非缘眼受生眼触，但缘眼触生眼受；缘眼受生眼爱，非缘眼爱生眼受，但缘眼受生眼爱。如是耳、鼻、舌、身、意界缘生意触，非缘意触生意界，但缘意界生意触；缘意触生意受，非缘意受生意触，但缘意触生意受；缘意受生意爱，非缘意爱生意受，但缘意受生意爱。

Therefore, bhikṣus! Various desires do not give rise to various feelings, various feelings do not give rise to various contacts, and various contacts do not give rise to various realms; but various realms give rise to various contacts, various contacts give rise to various feelings, and various feelings give rise to various desires. This is called what bhikṣus should discern well about various realms.

「是故，比丘！非缘种种爱生种种受，非缘种种受生种种触，非缘种种触生种种界；但缘种种界生种种触，缘种种触生种种受，缘种种受生种种爱，是名比丘当善分别种种界。」

After the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus who heard what the Buddha taught rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说是经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
