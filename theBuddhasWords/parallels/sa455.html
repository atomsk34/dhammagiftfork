SĀ 455

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 455（四五五） 想

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus: "Because of the various elements, various contacts arise. Because of various contacts, various perceptions arise. Because of various perceptions, various desires arise. Because of various desires, various thoughts arise. Because of various thoughts, various passions arise. Because of various passions, various pursuits arise.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「缘界种种故生种种触，缘种种触生种种想，缘种种想生种种欲，缘种种欲生种种觉，缘种种觉生种种热，缘种种热生种种求。

What are the various elements? They are the eighteen elements: the eye element, up to the dharma element.

「云何种种界？谓十八界，眼界，乃至法界。

"What is meant by various elements giving rise to various contacts? Up to what is meant by various passions giving rise to various pursuits? It means that the eye element gives rise to eye contact, but eye contact does not give rise to the eye element. It only gives rise to eye contact. Eye contact gives rise to eye perception, but eye perception does not give rise to eye contact. It only gives rise to eye perception. Eye perception gives rise to eye desire, but eye desire does not give rise to eye perception. It only gives rise to eye desire. Eye desire gives rise to eye thought, but eye thought does not give rise to eye desire. It only gives rise to eye thought. Eye thought gives rise to eye passion, but eye passion does not give rise to eye thought. It only gives rise to eye passion. Eye passion gives rise to eye pursuit, but eye pursuit does not give rise to eye passion. It only gives rise to eye pursuit. In the same way, the ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind elements give rise to mind contact, up to mind passion giving rise to mind pursuit. This is called a bhikṣu's various elements giving rise to various contacts, up to various passions giving rise to various pursuits. It is not that various pursuits give rise to various passions, up to various contacts giving rise to various elements. It only gives rise to various contacts, up to various pursuits."

「云何缘种种界生种种触？云何？乃至缘种种热生种种求？谓缘眼界生眼触，非缘眼触生眼界，但缘眼界生眼触；缘眼触生眼想，非缘眼想生眼触，但缘眼触生眼想；缘眼想生眼欲，非缘眼欲生眼想，但缘眼想生眼欲；缘眼欲生眼觉，非缘眼觉生眼欲，但缘眼欲生眼觉；缘眼觉生眼热，非缘眼热生眼觉，但缘眼觉生眼热；缘眼热生眼求，非缘眼求生眼热，但缘眼热生眼求。如是耳、鼻、舌、身、意界缘生意触，乃至缘意热生意求，亦如是广说。是名比丘缘种种界生种种触，乃至缘种种热生种种求，非缘种种求生种种热，乃至非缘种种触生种种界，但缘种种界生种种触，乃至缘种种热生种种求。」

After the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus who heard what the Buddha taught rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说是经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

The six internal sense bases are like this, and the six external sense bases are also explained in the same way.

如内六入处，外六入处亦如是说。
