SĀ 457

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 457（四五七） 说

Parallel to: SN 14.13

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying at the Deer Park Lecture Hall in the Eastern Garden of Shravasti.

一时，佛住舍卫国东园鹿子母讲堂。

At that time, the World-Honored One, in the afternoon, arose from meditation and, in the shade of the lecture hall, arranged his seat in front of the great assembly and sat down. He spoke the Udana verses and addressed the bhikshus, saying, "Because of the realms, there is birth, not without the realms. Because of the realms, there is seeing, not without the realms. Because of the realms, there is thought, not without the realms. Because of the inferior realms, I say there is inferior speech, inferior views, inferior thoughts, inferior intentions, inferior wishes, inferior men, inferior deeds, inferior arrangements, inferior establishments, inferior divisions, inferior displays, and inferior rebirth. So it is with the middling. So it is with the superior realms. Because of the superior realms, I say there is superior speech, superior views, superior thoughts, superior intentions, superior wishes, superior men, superior deeds, superior arrangements, superior establishments, superior divisions, superior displays, and superior rebirth."

尔时，世尊晡时从禅觉，于讲堂阴中敷座，于大众前坐，说优檀那句，告诸比丘：「缘界故生说，非不界，缘界故生见，非不界，缘界故生想，非不界。缘下] cf.【丽】界，我说生下说、下见、下想、下思、下欲、下愿、下士夫、下所作、下施设、下建立、下部分、下显示、下受生。如是中。如是胜界，缘胜界，我说彼生胜说、胜见、胜想、胜思、胜愿、胜士夫、胜所作、胜施设、胜建立、胜部分、胜显示、胜受生胜。」

At that time, the bhiksu Pokkharasati was behind the Buddha, holding a fan and fanning him. He addressed the Buddha, saying, "World-Honored One! If one regards the Tathāgata as not the Tathāgata, does that view also arise in dependence on the realms?"

时，有婆迦利比丘在佛后执扇扇佛，白佛言：「世尊！若于三藐三佛陀起非三藐三佛陀见，彼见亦缘界而生耶？」

The Buddha told the bhiksu, "The view of regarding the Tathāgata as not the Tathāgata also arises in dependence on the realms, not without the realms. Why is that? The realm of ordinary beings is the realm of ignorance. As I have previously explained, it gives rise to inferior speech, inferior views, and so forth, up to inferior rebirth. The middling and superior realms give rise to superior speech, superior views, and so forth, up to superior rebirth."

佛告比丘：「于三藐三佛陀起非三藐三佛陀见，亦缘界而生，非不界。所以者何？凡夫界者，是无明界，如我先说，缘下界生下说、下见，乃至下受生，中胜界生胜说、胜见，乃至胜受生。」

Having spoken this sutra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha had said and joyfully practiced it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
