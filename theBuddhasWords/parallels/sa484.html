SĀ 484

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 484（四八四） 跋陀罗

Parallel to: AN 5.170

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Bhadrika and Venerable Ānanda were both staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove.

尔时，尊者跋陀罗比丘及尊者阿难俱住祇树给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Ānanda went to where Venerable Bhadrika was, exchanged greetings and pleasantries, and sat to one side. Venerable Ānanda then asked Venerable Bhadrika, "What is the best view? What is the best hearing? What is the best joy? What is the best conception? What is the best existence?"

尔时，尊者阿难往诣尊者跋陀罗所，共相问讯慰劳已，于一面住。时，尊者阿难问尊者跋陀罗比丘言：「云何名为见第一？云何闻第一？云何乐第一？云何想第一？云何有第一？」

Venerable Bhadrika said to Venerable Ānanda, "There is a Brahmā Heaven, where one can freely create and transform as one wishes. It is the father of the world. If one sees that Brahmā Heaven, it is called the best view.

尊者跋陀罗语尊者阿难言：「有梵天自在造作、化如意，为世之父，若见彼梵天者，名曰见第一。

Ānanda! There are sentient beings who experience the joy and bliss of detachment from birth, which permeates and delights every part of their bodies, filling them completely without exception. This is called the joy and bliss of detachment from birth. When they arise from samādhi, they proclaim to the great assembly, 'The most tranquil is the joy and bliss of detachment from birth. The most blissful is the joy and bliss of detachment from birth.' Those who hear that voice are called the best hearing.

「阿难！有众生离生喜乐，处处润泽，处处敷悦，举身充满，无不满处。所谓离生喜乐，彼从三昧起，举声唱说，遍告大众：『极寂静者，离生喜乐，极乐者，离生喜乐。』诸有闻彼声者，是名闻第一。

Furthermore, Ānanda! There are sentient beings who experience the joy and bliss of detachment from joy, which permeates and delights every part of their bodies, filling them completely without exception. This is called the joy and bliss of detachment from joy. This is called the best bliss.

「复次，阿难！有众生于此身离喜之乐润泽，处处润泽，敷悦充满，举身充满，无不满处，所谓离喜之乐，是名乐第一。

What is the best perception? Ānanda! There are sentient beings who transcend the realm of all consciousness, and abide fully in the realm of nothingness. If one generates perception of that, it is called the best perception.

「云何想第一？阿难！有众生度一切识入处无所有，无所有入处具足住，若起彼想者，是名想第一。

What is the best existence? Furthermore, Ānanda! There are sentient beings who transcend the realm of nothingness, and abide fully in the realm of neither perception nor non-perception. If one generates existence of that, it is called the best existence.

「云何有第一？复次，阿难！有众生度一切无所有入处，非想非非想入处具足住，若起彼有者，是名有第一。」

Venerable Ānanda said to Venerable Bhadrika, the bhikṣu: "Many people have this view and say this, and you are the same as them. What is the difference? I will ask you a question. Listen carefully and I will tell you. If one observes in this way and gradually exhausts all taints, this is called the best view. If one hears in this way and gradually exhausts all taints, this is called the best hearing. If one experiences pleasure in this way and gradually exhausts all taints, this is called the best pleasure. If one perceives in this way and gradually exhausts all taints, this is called the best perception. If one truly observes in this way and gradually exhausts all taints, this is called the best existence.

尊者阿难语尊者跋陀罗比丘言：「多有人作如是见、如是说，汝亦同彼，有何差别？我作方便问汝，汝当谛听，当为汝说。如其所观，次第尽诸漏，是为见第一。如其所闻，次第尽诸漏，是名闻第一。如所生乐，次第尽诸漏者，是名乐第一。如其所想，次第尽诸漏者，是名想第一。如实观察，次第尽诸漏，是名有第一。」

At that time, the two noble ones had finished discussing and left their seats.

时，二正士共论说已，从座起去。
