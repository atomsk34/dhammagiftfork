SĀ 492

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 492（四九二） 泥水

Parallel to: AN 4.178

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Kalandakanivāpa in the Bamboo Grove near Rājagṛha. At that time Venerable Śāriputra was also staying there.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。尔时，尊者舍利弗亦在彼住。

At that time Venerable Śāriputra said to the bhikṣus, "If a bhikṣu attains the immeasurable samādhi, realizes it in his body, and abides in it, but his mind is not attached to the nirvāṇa of the cessation of the body, and he still cares about the body, it is like a man whose hand is stuck to a tree branch. Why is that? Because his hand is stuck. Bhikṣus, if one attains the immeasurable samādhi in one's body, but one's mind is not attached to the nirvāṇa of the cessation of the body, and one still cares about the body, one will never be able to be free from it, and one will not be able to see the Dharma and follow the teachings of the Dharma until the end of one's life. One will not attain anything, and will return to be reborn in this realm. One will never be able to destroy ignorance. It is like a pond near a village, with mud so deep and thick, and a long drought without rain, the pond water dries up and the ground cracks. In the same way, bhikṣus, if one does not see the Dharma and follow the teachings of the Dharma until the end of one's life, one will not attain anything, and will return to be reborn in this realm.

时，尊者舍利弗语诸比丘：「若有比丘得无量三昧，身作证具足住，于有身灭涅槃心不乐著，顾念有身。譬如士夫胶著于手，以执树枝，手即著树，不能得离。所以者何？胶著手故。比丘！无量三摩提身作证，心不乐著有身灭涅槃，顾念有身，终不得离，不得现法随顺法教，乃至命终，亦无所得，还复来生此界，终不能得破于痴冥。譬如聚落傍有泥池，泥极深溺，久旱不雨，池水干消，其地破裂。如是，比丘！不得见法随顺法教，乃至命终，亦无所得，来生当复还堕此界。

"If a bhikṣu attains the immeasurable samādhi, realizes it in his body, and abides in it, but his mind is attached to the nirvāṇa of the cessation of the body, and he does not care about the body, it is like a man with a clean hand holding a tree branch, his hand does not stick to the tree. Why is that? Because his hand is clean. In the same way, bhikṣus, if one attains the immeasurable samādhi in one's body, and one's mind is attached to the nirvāṇa of the cessation of the body, and one does not care about the body, one will be able to see the Dharma and follow the teachings of the Dharma in this life, and one will not return to be reborn in this realm. Therefore, bhikṣus, one should diligently make an effort to destroy ignorance. It is like a pond near a village, with water constantly flowing in from the four directions and frequent rains from the heavens, the pond is always full and overflowing with filth, but the water in the pond is clear and pure. In the same way, if one sees the Dharma and follows the teachings of the Dharma in this life, one will not return to be reborn in this realm. Therefore, bhikṣus, one should diligently make an effort to destroy ignorance."

「若有比丘得无量三昧，身作证具足住，于有身灭涅槃心生信乐，不念有身。譬如士夫以干净手执持树枝，手不著树。所以者何？以手净故。如是，比丘！得无量三昧，身作证具足住，于有识灭涅槃心生信乐，不念有身，现法随顺法教，乃至命终，不复来还生于此界。是故，比丘！当勤方便，破坏无明。譬如聚落傍有泥池，四方流水及数天雨，水常入池，其水盈溢，秽恶流出，其池清净。如是皆得现法随顺法教，乃至命终，不复还生此界。是故，比丘！当勤方便，破坏无明。」

After Venerable Śāriputra had spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus who heard it rejoiced and received it respectfully.

尊者舍利弗说此经已，诸比丘闻其所说，欢喜奉行。
