SĀ 494

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 494（四九四） 枯树

Parallel to: AN 6.41

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha, while Venerable Śāriputra was on Vulture Peak.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。尊者舍利弗在耆阇崛山中。

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra, in the morning, put on his robe, took his alms bowl, left Vulture Peak, entered Rājagṛha to beg for alms, and on the way, saw a large dead tree. He then sat down under the tree, arranged his sitting mat, and sat upright. He said to the bhikṣus, "If a bhikṣu cultivates meditation, attains the power of spiritual faculties, and gains mental mastery, he can transform this dead tree into earth. Why is that? Because there is an earth element within this dead tree. Therefore, a bhikṣu who attains the power of spiritual faculties and mentally understands it as earth, it becomes earth without difference.

尔时，尊者舍利弗晨朝著衣持钵，出耆阇崛山，入王舍城乞食，于路边见一大枯树，即于树下敷坐具，歛身正坐，语诸比丘：「若有比丘修习禅思，得神通力，心得自在，欲令此枯树成地，即时为地。所以者何？谓此枯树中有地界。是故，比丘得神通力，心作地解，即成地不异。

If a bhikṣu attains the power of spiritual faculties and is able to do as he wishes, he can transform this tree into water, fire, wind, gold, silver, and other things, all of which can be accomplished without difference. Why is that? Because this dead tree has a water element. Therefore, a bhikṣu who attains the power of spiritual faculties and is able to do as he wishes, if he wishes for this dead tree to become gold, it will immediately become gold without difference, and the same applies to various other things, all of which can be accomplished without difference. Why is that? Because this dead tree has various elements. Therefore, a bhikṣu who attains the power of spiritual faculties and is able to do as he wishes, if he wishes for various things to be accomplished without difference, it can be done. Bhikṣus should know that the realm of spiritual faculties and spiritual powers through meditation is inconceivable. Therefore, bhikṣus should diligently cultivate meditation and learn various spiritual faculties and spiritual powers.

「若有比丘得神通力，自在如意，欲令此树为水、火、风、金、银等物，悉皆成就不异。所以者何？谓此枯树有水界故。是故，比丘！禅思得神通力，自在如意，欲令枯树成金，即时成金不异，及余种种诸物，悉成不异。所以者何？以彼枯树有种种界故。是故，比丘！禅思得神通力，自在如意，为种种物悉成不异，比丘当知，比丘禅思神通境界不可思议。是故，比丘！当勤禅思，学诸神通。」

After Śāriputra had spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus who heard it rejoiced and practiced accordingly.

舍利弗说是经已，诸比丘闻其所说，欢喜奉行。
