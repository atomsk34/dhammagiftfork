SĀ 497

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 497（四九七） 举罪

Parallel to: AN 5.167

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Śāriputra went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha’s feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, “World-Honored One! If a bhikṣu who is accusing wants to accuse another, how many dharmas should he have in order to accuse another?”

尔时，尊者舍利弗诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！若举罪比丘欲举他罪者，令心安住，几法得举他罪？」

The Buddha told Śāriputra, “If a bhikṣu has his mind well-established, he can accuse another with five dharmas. What are the five? Truth, not falsehood; time, not not time; beneficial meaning, not not beneficial meaning; gentleness, not harshness; compassion, not anger. Śāriputra! If a bhikṣu who is accusing has these five dharmas, he can accuse another.”

佛告舍利弗：「若比丘令心安住，五法得举他罪。云何为五？实非不实、时不非时、义饶益非非义饶益、柔软不麁澁、慈心不瞋恚。舍利弗！举罪比丘具此五法，得举他罪。」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha, “World-Honored One! If a bhikṣu who is being accused, how many dharmas should he have in order to settle his mind?”

舍利弗白佛言：「世尊！被举比丘复以几法自安其心？」

The Buddha told Śāriputra, “If a bhikṣu who is being accused, he should have five dharmas to settle his mind. He should think, 'Where did he get it? Let it be true, not false. Let it be timely, not untimely. Let it be beneficial, not not beneficial. Let it be gentle, not harsh. Let it be compassionate, not angry.' Śāriputra! If a bhikṣu who is being accused has these five dharmas, he can settle his mind.

佛告舍利弗：「被举比丘当以五法令安其心。念言：『彼何处得，为实莫令不实，令时莫令非时，令是义饶益莫令非义饶益，柔软莫令麁澁，慈心莫令瞋恚。』舍利弗！被举比丘当具此五法，自安其心。」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I see those who accuse others of faults, who are not true, not true, not timely, not timely, not beneficial, not beneficial, harsh, not gentle, angry, not compassionate. World-Honored One! For those bhikṣus who accuse others of faults, how many dharmas should be used to benefit them and make them repent?"

舍利弗白佛言：「世尊！我见举他罪者，不实非实、非时非是时、非义饶益非为义饶益、麁澁不柔软、瞋恚非慈心。世尊！于不实举他罪比丘，当以几法饶益令其改悔？」

The Buddha told Śāriputra, "If a bhikṣu who accuses others of faults is not true, he should be benefited with five dharmas to make him repent. He should be told, 'Elder! Your accusation is not true, not true. You should repent. It is not timely, not timely, not beneficial, not beneficial, harsh, not gentle, angry, not compassionate. You should repent.' Śāriputra! If a bhikṣu who accuses others of faults is not true, he should be benefited with these five dharmas to make him repent. It will also prevent bhikṣus in the future from falsely accusing others of faults."

佛告舍利弗：「不实举罪比丘当以五法饶益令其改悔，当语之言：『长老！汝今举罪，不实非是实，当改悔；不时非是时、非义饶益非是义饶益、麁澁非柔软、瞋恚非慈心，汝当改悔。』舍利弗！不实举他罪比丘当以此五法饶益令其改悔，亦令当来世比丘不为不实举他罪。」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! If a bhikṣu who is accused of faults is not true, how many dharmas should he use to prevent repentance?"

舍利弗白佛言：「世尊！被不实举罪比丘复以几法令不变悔？」

The Buddha told Śāriputra, "If a bhikṣu who is accused of faults is not true, he should use five dharmas to stabilize his mind and not repent. He should think, 'That bhikṣu accused me of faults, but it is not true, not true. It is not timely, not timely. It is not beneficial, not beneficial. It is harsh, not gentle. It is angry, not compassionate. I am truly not repenting.' If a bhikṣu who is accused of faults is not true, he should use these five dharmas to stabilize his mind and not repent."

佛告舍利弗：「被不实举罪比丘当以五法不自变悔。彼应作是念：『彼比丘不实举罪非是实、非时非是时、非义饶益非是义饶益、麁澁非柔软、瞋恚非慈心，我真不变悔。』被不实举罪比丘当以此五法自安其心，不自变悔。」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! If a bhikṣu accuses another bhikṣu of faults, and it is true, not false, timely, not untimely, beneficial, not unbeneficial, gentle, not harsh, compassionate, not angry, how many dharmas should the bhikṣu who accuses the other bhikṣu of faults use to benefit him and prevent him from changing?"

舍利弗白佛言：「世尊！有比丘举罪实非不实、时不非时、义饶益不非义饶益、柔软非麁澁、慈心非瞋恚，实举罪比丘当以几法饶益令不改变？」

The Buddha told Śāriputra, "If a bhikṣu who accuses another bhikṣu of faults is true, he should use five dharmas to benefit him and prevent him from changing. He should say, 'Elder! You accused me of faults, and it is true, not false. It is timely, not untimely. It is beneficial, not unbeneficial. It is gentle, not harsh. It is compassionate, not angry.' Śāriputra! If a bhikṣu who accuses another bhikṣu of faults is true, he should use these five dharmas to benefit him and prevent him from changing. It will also prevent bhikṣus who accuse others of faults in the future from changing."

佛告舍利弗：「实举罪比丘当以五法饶益令不变悔，当作是言：『长老！汝实举罪非不实、时不非时、义饶益不非义、柔软非麁澁、慈心非瞋恚。』舍利弗！实举罪比丘当以此五法义饶益令不变悔，亦令来世实举罪比丘而不变悔。」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! If a bhikṣu is accused of faults by another bhikṣu, and it is true, how many dharmas should he use to benefit himself and prevent himself from changing?"

舍利弗白佛言：「世尊！被实举罪比丘当以几法饶益令不变悔？」

The Buddha told Śāriputra, "If a bhikṣu is accused of faults by another bhikṣu, and it is true, he should use five dharmas to benefit himself and prevent himself from changing. He should say, 'That bhikṣu accused me of faults, and it is true, not false. You should not change. It is timely, not untimely. It is beneficial, not unbeneficial. It is gentle, not harsh. It is compassionate, not angry. You should not change.'"

佛告舍利弗：「被举罪比丘当以五法饶益令不变悔，当作是言：『彼比丘实举罪非不实，汝莫变悔；时不非时、义饶益不非义饶益、柔软非麁澁、慈心非瞋恚，汝莫变悔。』」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I see bhikṣus who are accused of faults by others, and it is true, but they are angry. World-Honored One! How many dharmas should a bhikṣu who is accused of faults by others, and it is true, but is angry, use to awaken himself from his anger and hatred?"

舍利弗白佛言：「世尊！我见被实举罪比丘有瞋恚者。世尊！被实举罪瞋恚比丘当以几法令于瞋恨而自开觉？」

The Buddha told Śāriputra, "If a bhikṣu is accused of faults by another bhikṣu, and it is true, but he is angry, he should use five dharmas to awaken himself from his anger and hatred. He should say to the other bhikṣu, 'Elder! That bhikṣu accused you of faults, and it is true, not false. You should not be angry and hateful. It is timely, not untimely. It is beneficial, not unbeneficial. It is gentle, not harsh. It is compassionate, not angry. You should not be angry and hateful.' Śāriputra! If a bhikṣu is accused of faults by another bhikṣu, and it is true, but he is angry, he should use these five dharmas to awaken himself from his anger and hatred."

佛告舍利弗：「被实举罪瞋恚比丘当以五法令自开觉，当语彼言：『长老！彼比丘实举汝罪，非不实，汝莫瞋恨……』乃至『慈心非瞋恚，汝莫瞋恨。』舍利弗！被实举罪瞋恚比丘当以此五法，令于恚恨而得开觉。」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! If someone accuses me of faults, whether true or false, I will calm my mind towards those two people. If it is true, I will know it myself. If it is false, I will awaken myself and say, 'This is false. I now know for myself that I do not have this fault.' World-Honored One! I will do as the World-Honored One has said in the Sutra on the Simile of the Woodcutter, teaching the śramaṇas: 'If thieves come and saw your body apart, you should not give rise to evil thoughts and evil words towards the thieves, for that would create obstacles for yourselves. Therefore, bhikṣus! If your bodies are sawn apart, you should not give rise to evil thoughts or change your minds, or give rise to evil words, creating obstacles for yourselves. Towards those people, you should give rise to a mind of compassion, without resentment or hatred. You should fully abide in the right absorption of compassion towards the four directions. You should train accordingly.' Therefore, World-Honored One! I will do as the World-Honored One has said. I will calmly endure the suffering of having my body sawn apart, let alone small suffering and small slander that I cannot endure? The benefits and desires of a śramaṇa are to cut off unwholesome dharmas and cultivate wholesome dharmas. Here, one should diligently and skillfully cut off unwholesome dharmas and cultivate wholesome dharmas, guarding oneself well, focusing one's attention, and practicing without negligence. One should train accordingly."

舍利弗白佛言：「世尊！有实、不实举我罪者，于彼二人，我当自安其心。若彼实者，我当自知，若不实者，当自开解言：『此则不实，我今自知无此法也。』世尊！我当如是，如世尊所说解材譬经说，教诸沙门：『若有贼来，执汝以锯解身，汝等于贼起恶念恶言者，自生障碍。是故，比丘！若以锯解汝身，汝当于彼勿起恶心变易及起恶言，自作障碍。于彼人所，当生慈心，无怨无恨，于四方境界慈心正受具足住，应当学。』是故，世尊！我当如是，如世尊所说，解身之苦，当自安忍，况复小苦、小谤而不安忍？沙门利、沙门欲，欲断不善法，欲修善法；于此不善法当断，善法当修，精勤方便，善自防护，系念思惟，不放逸行，应当学。」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! If I accuse another bhikṣu of faults, whether true or false, timely or untimely, beneficial or unbeneficial, gentle or harsh, compassionate or angry, but that accused bhikṣu is angry and hateful.

舍利弗白佛言：「世尊！我若举他比丘罪，实非不实、时非不时、义饶益非非义饶益、柔软非麁澁、慈心不瞋恚，然彼被举比丘有怀瞋恚者。」

The Buddha asked Śāriputra, "What kind of bhikṣu, when accused of faults, gives rise to anger and hatred?"

佛问舍利弗：「何等像类比丘闻举其罪而生瞋恚？」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! If that bhikṣu is deceitful, fraudulent, untrustworthy, shameless, lazy, forgetful, unfocused, of poor wisdom, slow, contrary to seclusion, disrespectful of the precepts, not caring for the monastic life, not diligent in practice, not self-reflective, having renounced for the sake of livelihood, not seeking nirvāṇa, then such a person, when I accuse them of faults, will give rise to anger and hatred."

舍利弗白佛言：「世尊！若彼比丘谄曲幻伪、欺诳不信、无惭无愧、懈怠失念、不定恶慧、慢缓、违于远离、不敬戒律、不顾沙门、不勤修学、不自省察、为命出家、不求涅槃。如是等人，闻我举罪，则生瞋恚。」

The Buddha asked Śāriputra, "What kind of bhikṣu, when accused by you, does not become angry and hateful?"

佛问舍利弗：「何等像类比丘闻汝举罪而不瞋恨？」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! If there is a bhikṣu who is not deceitful, not fraudulent, not deceptive, who has faith, shame, diligence, right mindfulness, right concentration, wisdom, not slow, not abandoning seclusion, deeply respecting the precepts, caring for the monastic practices, honoring nirvāṇa, having renounced for the sake of the Dharma, not for the sake of life. Such a bhikṣu, when I accuse them of faults, will joyfully accept it, like drinking sweet dew. It is like a kṣatriya or brāhman woman, having bathed and purified herself, obtaining a wonderful flower, loving it and wearing it on her head. In the same way, a bhikṣu who is not deceitful, not fraudulent, not deceptive, who has right faith, shame, diligence, right mindfulness, right concentration, wisdom, not slow, keeping in mind seclusion, deeply respecting the precepts, caring for the monastic practices, diligently practicing self-reflection, having renounced for the sake of the Dharma, aspiring for nirvāṇa. Such a bhikṣu, when I accuse them of faults, will joyfully accept it, like drinking sweet dew."

舍利弗白佛言：「世尊！若有比丘不谄曲、不幻伪、不欺诳、有信、惭愧、精勤正念、正定智慧、不慢缓、不舍远离、深敬戒律、顾沙门行、尊崇涅槃、为法出家、不为性命。如是比丘闻我举罪，欢喜顶受，如饮甘露。譬如刹利、婆罗门女，沐浴清净，得好妙华，爱乐顶戴，以冠其首。如是，比丘不谄曲、不幻伪、不欺诳、正信、惭愧、精勤正念、正定智慧、不慢缓、心存远离、深敬戒律、顾沙门行、勤修自省、为法出家、志求涅槃。如是比丘闻我举罪，欢喜顶受，如饮甘露。」

The Buddha told Śāriputra, "If that bhikṣu is deceitful, fraudulent, deceptive, without faith, without shame, lazy, forgetful, unfocused, of bad wisdom, slow, going against seclusion, not respecting the precepts, not caring for the monastic practices, not seeking nirvāṇa, having renounced for the sake of life. Such a bhikṣu should not be instructed or spoken to. Why? Because such bhikṣus destroy the holy life. If that bhikṣu is not deceitful, not fraudulent, not deceptive, has faith, shame, diligence, right mindfulness, right concentration, wisdom, not slow, keeping in mind seclusion, deeply respecting the precepts, caring for the monastic practices, aspiring for nirvāṇa, having renounced for the sake of the Dharma. Such a bhikṣu should be instructed. Why? Because such bhikṣus can cultivate the holy life and can establish themselves.

佛告舍利弗：「若彼比丘谄曲幻伪、欺诳、不信、无惭无愧、懈怠失念、不定恶慧、慢缓、违于远离、不敬戒律、不顾沙门行、不求涅槃、为命出家。如是比丘不应教授，与共言语。所以者何？此等比丘破梵行故。若彼比丘不谄曲、不幻伪、不欺诳、信心、惭愧、精勤正念、正定智慧、不慢缓、心存远离、深敬戒律、顾沙门行、志崇涅槃、为法出家。如是比丘应当教授。所以者何？如是比丘能修梵行，能自建立故。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Venerable Śāriputra rejoiced and upheld it.

佛说此经已，尊者舍利弗闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
