SĀ 498

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 498（四九八） 那罗犍陀

Parallel to: DN 28 (~) / SN 47.12 / DĀ 18 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the mango grove of the cloth-seller Naradatta.

一时，佛住那罗揵陀卖衣者庵罗园。

At that time, Śāriputra went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. He said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I deeply believe that the wisdom of all śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, and others in the past, future, and present is not equal to the World-Honored One's bodhi, let alone surpassing it."

尔时，舍利弗诣世尊所，稽首礼足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！我深信世尊，过去、当来、今现在，诸沙门、婆罗门所有智慧，无有与世尊菩提等者，况复过上？」

The Buddha told Śāriputra, "Excellent! Excellent! Śāriputra, excellent indeed! Your words are the foremost, able to roar like a lion in the assembly. You say that you deeply believe in the World-Honored One, and that the wisdom of all śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, and others in the past, future, and present is not equal to the Buddha's bodhi, let alone surpassing it." The Buddha asked Śāriputra, "Can you truly know the superior precepts of the past Tathāgatas?"

佛告舍利弗：「善哉！善哉！舍利弗，善哉所说，第一之说，能于众中作师子吼，自言深信世尊，言过去、当来、今现在，沙门、婆罗门所有智慧，无有与佛菩提等者，况复过上？」佛问舍利弗：「汝能审知过去三藐三佛陀所有增上戒？」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha, "I do not know."

舍利弗白佛言：「不知。」

The World-Honored One further asked, "Śāriputra! Do you know such dharmas, such wisdom, such illumination, such liberation, such abiding?"

世尊复问：「舍利弗！知如是法、如是慧、如是明、如是解脱、如是住不？」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha, "I do not know, World-Honored One!"

舍利弗白佛言：「不知。世尊！」

The Buddha said to Śāriputra, "Do you also know the supreme precepts of the future Tathāgatas, such dharmas, such wisdom, such illumination, such liberation, such abiding?"

佛告舍利弗：「汝复知未来三藐三佛陀所有增上戒。如是法、如是慧、如是明、如是解脱、如是住不？」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha, "I do not know, World-Honored One!"

舍利弗白佛言：「不知。世尊！」

The Buddha said to Śāriputra, "Do you also know the supreme precepts of the present Buddhas, such dharmas, such wisdom, such illumination, such liberation, such abiding?"

佛告舍利弗：「汝复能知今现在佛所有增上戒。如是法、如是慧、如是明、如是解脱、如是住不？」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha, "I do not know, World-Honored One!"

舍利弗白佛言：「不知。世尊！」

The Buddha said to Śāriputra, "If you do not know the dharmas in the minds of the past, future, and present Buddhas, how can you praise them like this and roar like a lion in the great assembly, saying, 'I deeply believe that the wisdom of the past and future śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas is equal to the Buddha's enlightenment, let alone surpassing it'?"

佛告舍利弗：「汝若不知过去、未来、今现在诸佛世尊心中所有诸法。云何如是赞叹？于大众中作师子吼，说言：『我深信世尊，过去、当来诸沙门、婆罗门所有智慧，无有与世尊菩提等者，况复过上』？」

Śāriputra said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I cannot know the extent of the minds of the past, future, and present Buddhas, but I can know the extent of the Buddhas' Dharma. I hear the World-Honored One's teachings, which become deeper and deeper, more and more excellent, more and more superior, more and more wonderful. I hear the World-Honored One's teachings, knowing that one dharma is cut off, one dharma is realized, one dharma is cultivated, and one ultimately attains the Dharma. I have pure faith in the Great Teacher and my mind is purified.

舍利弗白佛言：「世尊！我不能知过去、当来、今现在诸佛世尊心之分齐，然我能知诸佛世尊法之分齐。我闻世尊说法，转转深、转转胜、转转上、转转妙，我闻世尊说法，知一法即断一法，知一法即证一法，知一法即修习一法，究竟于法，于大师所得净信，心得净。

"The World-Honored One is the Perfectly Enlightened One. World-Honored One! It is like a king's border city, square and straight, solid and firm, with only one gate, no second gate, and a gatekeeper. The people entering and leaving all go through this gate, whether entering or leaving. Although the gatekeeper may not know the number of people, he knows that the people only go through this gate and there is no other way. In this way, I know that the past Buddhas, Tathāgatas, Arhats, Perfectly Enlightened Ones all cut off the five hindrances that afflict the mind, weaken the power of wisdom, cause one to fall into the category of obstacles, and not head towards nirvāṇa. They abide in the four establishments of mindfulness, cultivate the seven factors of awakening, and attain unsurpassed, perfect enlightenment. Those future Buddha-World-Honored Ones will also cut off the five hindrances that afflict the mind, weaken the power of wisdom, cause one to fall into the category of obstacles, and not head towards nirvāṇa. They abide in the four establishments of mindfulness, cultivate the seven factors of awakening, and attain unsurpassed, perfect enlightenment. The present Buddhas, Tathāgatas, Arhats, Perfectly Enlightened Ones also cut off the five hindrances that afflict the mind, weaken the power of wisdom, cause one to fall into the category of obstacles, and not head towards nirvāṇa. They abide in the four establishments of mindfulness, cultivate the seven factors of awakening, and attain unsurpassed, perfect enlightenment."

「世尊是等正觉。世尊！譬如国王有边城，城周匝方直，牢固坚密，唯有一门，无第二门，立守门者，人民入出皆从此门，若入若出，其守门者，虽复不知人数多少，要知人民唯从此门，更无他处。如是，我知过去诸佛、如来、应、等正觉悉断五盖恼心，令慧力羸、堕障碍品、不向涅槃者，住四念处，修七觉分，得阿耨多罗三藐三菩提。彼当来世诸佛世尊亦断五盖恼心，令慧力羸、堕障碍品、不向涅槃者，住四念处，修七觉分，得阿耨多罗三藐三菩提。今现在诸佛世尊、如来、应、等正觉亦断五盖恼心，令慧力羸、堕障碍品、不向涅槃者，住四念处，修七觉分，得阿耨多罗三藐三菩提。」

The Buddha told Śāriputra, "So it is, so it is! Śāriputra! The past, future, and present Buddhas all cut off the five hindrances that afflict the mind, weaken the power of wisdom, cause one to fall into the category of obstacles, and not head towards nirvāṇa. They abide in the four establishments of mindfulness, cultivate the seven factors of awakening, and attain unsurpassed, perfect enlightenment."

佛告舍利弗：「如是，如是。舍利弗！过去、未来、今现在佛悉断五盖恼心，慧力羸、堕障碍品、不向涅槃者，住四念处，修七觉分，得阿耨多罗三藐三菩提。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Venerable Śāriputra, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说是经已，尊者舍利弗闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
