SĀ 501

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 501（五〇一） 圣默然

Parallel to: SN 21.1 / SN 40.2 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha. At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana was on Vulture Peak Mountain in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。尔时，尊者大目揵连在王舍城耆阇崛山中。

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana addressed the bhikṣus, saying: "Once, the World-Honored One was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha. At that time, I was staying on Vulture Peak Mountain in Rājagṛha. While alone in a quiet place, I thought: 'What is meant by the noble silence?' I further thought: 'If a bhikṣu ceases to have perception and contemplation, with inner purity and single-mindedness, the samādhi of non-perception and non-contemplation arises, giving rise to joy and pleasure, and he dwells in perfect fulfillment in the second dhyāna, this is called the noble silence.' I further thought: 'I should now also dwell in the noble silence, ceasing to have perception and contemplation, with inner purity and single-mindedness, the samādhi of non-perception and non-contemplation arises, giving rise to joy and pleasure, and I shall dwell in perfect fulfillment, dwelling in it for a long time; having dwelt in it for a long time, the mind with perception and contemplation will arise again.' At that time, the World-Honored One knew my thoughts. He disappeared from the bamboo grove monastery and appeared before me on Vulture Peak Mountain, saying to me: 'Maudgalyāyana! You should dwell in the noble silence, do not be negligent.' Having heard the World-Honored One's words, I then again ceased to have perception and contemplation, with inner purity and single-mindedness, the samādhi of non-perception and non-contemplation arose, giving rise to joy and pleasure, and I dwelt in perfect fulfillment in the second dhyāna. In this way, a second and a third time, the Buddha also instructed me twice: 'You should dwell in the noble silence, do not be negligent.' I then again ceased to have perception and contemplation, with inner purity and single-mindedness, the samādhi of non-perception and non-contemplation arose, giving rise to joy and pleasure, and I dwelt in perfect fulfillment in the third dhyāna.

尔时，尊者大目揵连告诸比丘：「一时，世尊住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。我于此耆阇崛山中住，我独一静处，作如是念：『云何为圣默然？』复作是念：『若有比丘息有觉有观，内净一心，无觉无观三昧生喜乐，第二禅具足住，是名圣默然。』复作是念：『我今亦当圣默然，息有觉有观，内净一心，无觉无观三昧生喜乐，具足住多住；多住已，复有觉有观心起。』尔时，世尊知我心念，于竹园精舍没，于耆阇崛山中现于我前，语我言：『目揵连！汝当圣默然，莫生放逸。』我闻世尊说已，即复离有觉有观，内净一心，无觉无观三昧生喜乐，第二禅具足住。如是再三，佛亦再三教我：『汝当圣默然，莫放逸。』我即复息有觉有观，内净一心，无觉无观三昧生喜乐，第三禅具足住。

"If one truly speaks of a Buddha's child born from the Buddha's mouth, born from the Dharma, and inheriting the Buddha's Dharma, then I am that person. Why is that? I am a Buddha's child, born from the Buddha's mouth, born from the Dharma, and inheriting the Buddha's Dharma. With little effort, I attained dhyāna, liberation, samādhi, and samāpatti. It is like the eldest son of a wheel-turning sage king, who, although not yet enthroned, has already inherited the king's Dharma and can enjoy the five kinds of sensual pleasures without much effort. I am also like this. As a Buddha's child, without much effort, I attained dhyāna, liberation, samādhi, and samāpatti. The World-Honored One came to me three times in one day with his supernormal powers and gave me three instructions, establishing me in the state of a great person."

「若正说佛子从佛口生，从法化生，得佛法分者，则我身是也。所以者何？我是佛子，从佛口生，从法化生，得佛法分，以少方便，得禅、解脱、三昧、正受。譬如转轮圣王长太子，虽未灌顶，已得王法，不勤方便，能得五欲功德。我亦如是，为佛之子，不勤方便，得禅、解脱、三昧、正受，于一日中，世尊以神通力三至我所，三教授我，以大人处所建立于我。」

Having spoken this sūtra, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, the bhikṣus, having heard what was said, rejoiced and upheld it.

尊者大目揵连说此经已，诸比丘闻其所说，欢喜奉行。
