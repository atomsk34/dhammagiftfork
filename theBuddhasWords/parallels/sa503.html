SĀ 503

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 503（五〇三） 寂灭

Parallel to: SN 21.3

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying at Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī. At that time, Venerable Śāriputra, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, and Venerable Ānanda were staying together in a room at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，尊者舍利弗、尊者大目揵连、尊者阿难在王舍城迦兰陀竹园，于一房共住。

Then, Venerable Śāriputra said to Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana in the latter part of the night: "How wonderful, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana! You have abided in the concentration of cessation tonight."

时，尊者舍利弗于后夜时告尊者目揵连：「奇哉！尊者目揵连！汝于今夜住寂灭正受。」

Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana heard Venerable Śāriputra say to him: "I do not hear the sound of your breathing at all."

尊者目揵连闻尊者舍利弗语尊者目揵连言：「我都不闻汝喘息之声。」

Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana said: "This is not the concentration of cessation, but the abiding in a coarse concentration. Venerable Śāriputra! I spoke with the World-Honored One tonight."

尊者目揵连言：「此非寂灭正受，麁正受住耳。尊者舍利弗！我于今夜与世尊共语。」

Venerable Śāriputra said: "Mahāmaudgalyāyana! The World-Honored One is staying in Śrāvastī, in the Jeta Grove, Anāthapiṇḍada's Park, far from here. How can you speak with him? You are now in the Bamboo Grove. How can you speak with him? Did you go to the World-Honored One by your supernatural powers? Or did the World-Honored One come to you by his supernatural powers?"

尊者舍利弗言：「目揵连！世尊住舍卫国祇树给孤独园，去此极远。云何共语？汝今在竹园，云何共语？汝以神通力至世尊所？为是世尊神通力来至汝所？」

Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana said to Venerable Śāriputra: "I did not go to the World-Honored One by my supernatural powers, nor did the World-Honored One come to me by his supernatural powers. However, I heard in Śrāvastī and Rājagṛha that the World-Honored One and I both attained the divine eye and divine ear. Therefore, I was able to ask the World-Honored One: 'What is called diligent practice?' The World-Honored One answered me: 'Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If this bhikṣu walks and sits during the day, purifying his mind with unobstructed dharmas. In the early night, he sits and walks, purifying his mind with unobstructed dharmas. In the middle of the night, he goes outside the room to wash his feet, returns to the room, lies on his right side, with his feet stacked, mindful of the dawn, with right mindfulness and right awareness, contemplating arising. In the later night, he slowly wakes up, slowly gets up, sits and walks, purifying his mind with unobstructed dharmas. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! This is called a bhikṣu's diligent practice.'"

尊者目揵连语尊者舍利弗：「我不以神通力诣世尊所，世尊不以神通力来至我所，然我于舍卫国王舍城中闻，世尊及我俱得天眼、天耳故。我能问世尊：『所谓慇懃精进。云何名为慇懃精进？』世尊答我言：『目揵连！若此比丘昼则经行、若坐，以不障碍法自净其心。初夜若坐、经行，以不障碍法自净其心。于中夜时，出房外洗足，还入房，右脇而卧，足足相累，系念明相，正念正知，作起思惟。于后夜时，徐觉徐起，若坐亦经行，以不障碍法自净其心，目揵连！是名比丘慇懃精进。』」

Venerable Śāriputra said to Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana: "You, Mahāmaudgalyāyana, truly have great supernatural powers and great merit. You sit comfortably. I also have great power and can sit with you. Mahāmaudgalyāyana! It is like a large mountain. If someone throws a small stone at it, the color and taste of the mountain will all be the same. I am also like this. I can sit with the Venerable One, who has great virtue and great power. It is like the world's pure and fine things, which people all wear on their heads. In the same way, the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana's great virtue and great power should be worn on the heads of all those who practice the Dharma. Those who have the opportunity to associate with, visit, and make offerings to the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana will greatly benefit. Now I can also associate with the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana and will also greatly benefit."

尊者舍利弗语尊者目揵连言：「汝大目揵连真为大神通力、大功德力，安坐而坐，我亦大力，得与汝俱。目揵连！譬如大山，有人持一小石，投之，大山色味悉同，我亦如是，得与尊者大力大德，同座而坐。譬如世间鲜净好物，人皆顶戴。如是，尊者目揵连大德大力，诸梵行者皆应顶戴。诸有得遇尊者目揵连交游往来，恭敬供养者，大得善利，今亦得与尊者大目揵连交游往来，亦得善利。」

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana said to Venerable Śāriputra: "Now I can sit with the Venerable One, who has great wisdom and great virtue, like throwing a small stone at a large mountain and having the same color. I am also like this. I can sit with the Venerable One, who has great wisdom and great virtue, as the second companion."

时，尊者大目揵连语尊者舍利弗：「我今得与大智大德尊者舍利弗同座而坐，如以小石投之大山，得同其色，我亦如是，得与尊者大智舍利弗同座而坐，为第二伴。」

At that time, after the two noble ones had discussed, they each rose from their seats and departed.

时，二正士共论议已，各从座起而去。
