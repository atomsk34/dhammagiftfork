SĀ 505

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 505（五〇五） 爱尽

Parallel to: MN 37 / EĀ 19.3

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Rājagṛha. At that time Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana was staying on Vulture Peak.

一时，佛住王舍城。时，尊者大目揵连在耆阇崛山中。

At that time Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana was alone in a quiet place, absorbed in meditation, and he had this thought: "In the past, Śakra, Lord of the Devas, asked the World-Honored One about the meaning of liberation through the exhaustion of craving. The World-Honored One explained it for him, and upon hearing it, he rejoiced. It seemed like he wanted to ask about something else. I should go now and ask about his joyful intention." Having had this thought, in the time it takes for a strong man to bend and stretch his arm, he disappeared from Vulture Peak and arrived in the Heaven of the Thirty-three, not far from the Airavaṇa Pond.

尔时，尊者大目揵连独一静处禅思，作是念：「昔有时，释提桓因于界隔山石窟中，问世尊爱尽解脱之义，世尊为说，闻已随喜，似欲更有所问义，我今当往问其喜意。」作是念已，如力士屈申臂顷，于耆阇崛山没，至三十三天，去一分陀利池不远而住。

At that time Śakra, Lord of the Devas, was playing in the pond with five hundred celestial maidens, and the celestial maidens had beautiful and melodious voices. At that time Śakra, Lord of the Devas, saw from afar Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana and said to the celestial maidens, "Do not sing. Do not sing." The celestial maidens then fell silent. Śakra, Lord of the Devas, then went to where Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana was, bowed his head at his feet, and stood to one side.

时，天帝释与五百婇女游戏浴池，有诸天女，音声美妙。尔时，帝释遥见尊者大目揵连，语诸天女言：「莫歌。莫歌。」时，诸天女即便默然，天帝释即诣尊者大目犍连所，稽首礼足，退住一面。

Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana asked Śakra, Lord of the Devas, "You previously asked the World-Honored One about the meaning of liberation through the exhaustion of craving at the mountain cave in the boundary-separating mountain. Upon hearing it, you rejoiced. What is your intention? Do you rejoice because you heard the explanation? Or do you rejoice because you want to ask something else?"

尊者大目揵连问帝释言：「汝先于界隔山中问世尊爱尽解脱义，闻已随喜，汝意云何？为闻说随喜？为更欲有所问，故随喜耶？」

Śakra, Lord of the Devas, said to Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, "The thirty-three heavens are often attached to the pleasures of indulgence, and sometimes I remember past events, and sometimes I do not. The World-Honored One is now in the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha. Venerable One, if you want to know the matter I asked about at the mountain cave in the boundary-separating mountain, you should go ask the World-Honored One now. You should uphold it as the World-Honored One explains it. However, there is a good pavilion here that was newly built not long ago. You can enter and take a look."

天帝释语尊者大目揵连：「我三十三天多著放逸乐，或忆先事，或时不忆。世尊今在王舍城迦兰陀竹园，尊者欲知我先界隔山中所问事者，今可往问世尊，如世尊说，汝当受持。然我此处有好堂观，新成未久，可入观看。」

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana silently accepted the invitation and entered the pavilion with Śakra, Lord of the Devas. When the heavenly maidens saw Śakra coming from afar, they all played heavenly music, singing and dancing. The heavenly maidens wore necklaces and ornaments on their bodies, producing wonderful sounds, combining the five kinds of music, like well-played music, without any difference in sound. When the heavenly maidens saw Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, they all felt ashamed and hid in the room.

时，尊者大目揵连默然受请，即与天帝释共入堂观。彼诸天女遥见帝释来，皆作天乐，或歌或舞。诸天女辈著身璎珞庄严之具，出妙音声，合于五乐，如善作乐，音声不异。诸天女辈既见尊者大目揵连，悉皆惭愧，入室藏隐。

At that time, Śakra, Lord of the Devas, said to Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, "Look at this pavilion. The ground is good and level, and the walls, pillars, beams, multi-storied pavilions, windows, lattices, curtains, and screens are all beautifully adorned."

时，天帝释语尊者大目揵连：「观此堂观地好平正，其壁、柱、梁、重阁、牕牖、罗网、帘障，悉皆严好。」

Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana said to Śakra, Lord of the Devas, "Kauśika! It is due to the causes and conditions of cultivating good dharmas and meritorious virtues in the past that this wonderful fruit is attained."

尊者大目揵连语帝释言：「憍尸迦！先修善法福德因缘，成此妙果。」

In this way, Śakra praised himself three times, and asked Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana. Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana also answered him three times.

如是，帝释三自称叹，问尊者大目揵连，尊者大目揵连亦再三答。

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana thought, "Now this Śakra is extremely self-indulgent, attached to the divine abodes, praising this hall. I shall cause him to give rise to disgust." He then entered concentration and, with his supernormal powers, kicked the hall with one toe, causing it to shake. Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana then disappeared.

时，尊者大目揵连作是念：「今此帝释极自放逸，著界神住，叹此堂观，我当令彼心生厌离。」即入三昧，以神通力，以一足指撇其堂观，悉令震动。时，尊者大目揵连即没不现。

The assembly of goddesses saw the hall shaking and swaying, and were terrified, running around in all directions. They said to Śakra, "Is this the great power of your master, Kauśika?"

诸天女众见此堂观震掉动摇，颠沛恐怖，东西驰走，白帝释言：「此是憍尸迦大师，有此大功德力耶？」

At that time, Śakra said to the goddesses, "This is not my master, but a disciple of the great master, Mahāmaudgalyāyana. He is pure in conduct, of great virtue and great power."

时，天帝释语诸天女：「此非我师，是大师弟子大目揵连，梵行清净，大德大力者。」

The goddesses said, "Excellent! Kauśika! You have such a pure conduct, great virtue, and great power as a fellow student. How much greater must be the virtue and power of your master!"

诸天女言：「善哉！憍尸迦！乃有如此梵行大德大力同学，大师德力当复如何？」
