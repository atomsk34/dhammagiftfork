SĀ 506

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 506（五〇六） 帝释

Parallel to: SN 40.10 / EĀ 36.5 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying on a soft, dappled stone in the Heaven of the Thirty-three, not far from the pāri jāta and kuṭaja trees, during the summer retreat, teaching the Dharma to his mother and the gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three. At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana was staying in the Jeta Grove of Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住三十三天骢色虚软石上，去波梨耶多罗、拘毘陀罗香树不远夏安居，为母及三十三天说法。尔时，尊者大目揵连在舍卫国祇树给孤独园安居。

At that time, the four assemblies went to Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, bowed their heads at his feet, and sat to one side. They said to Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, "Do you know where the World-Honored One is staying for the summer retreat?"

时，诸四众诣尊者大目揵连所，稽首礼足，退坐一面，白尊者大目揵连：「知世尊夏安居处不？」

Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana replied, "I have heard that the World-Honored One is staying on a soft, dappled stone in the Heaven of the Thirty-three, not far from the pāri jāta and kuṭaja trees, during the summer retreat, teaching the Dharma to his mother and the gods of the Heaven of the Thirty-three."

尊者大目揵连答言：「我闻世尊在三十三天骢色虚软石上，去波梨耶多罗、拘毘陀罗香树不远夏安居，为母及三十三天说法。」

At that time, the four assemblies, having heard what Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana had said, rejoiced and delighted in it. They each rose from their seats, bowed, and departed.

时，诸四众闻尊者大目揵连所说，欢喜随喜，各从座起，作礼而去。

Then, after the four assemblies had completed the three-month summer retreat, they again went to Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, bowed at his feet, and sat to one side. At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana spoke various teachings to the four assemblies, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting them. Having shown, taught, illuminated, and delighted them, he remained silent.

时，诸四众过三月安居已，复诣尊者大目揵连所，稽首礼足，退坐一面。时，尊者大目揵连为诸四众种种说法，示、教、照、喜；示、教、照、喜已，默然而住。

Then the four assemblies rose from their seats, bowed, and said to Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, "Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana! Know that we have not seen the World-Honored One for a long time. The assembly is very thirsty to see the World-Honored One. Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana! If you are not weary, please go on our behalf to the Heaven of the Thirty-three to inquire after the World-Honored One on our behalf, asking if he is free from illness and affliction, if he is at ease, and if he is dwelling in peace. Also tell the World-Honored One that the four assemblies of Jambudvīpa wish to see the World-Honored One, but they do not have the spiritual power to ascend to the Heaven of the Thirty-three to pay homage to the World-Honored One. The Heaven of the Thirty-three has its own spiritual power to come down to the human realm. We only wish that the World-Honored One would return to Jambudvīpa out of compassion." At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana silently accepted.

时，诸四众从座而起，稽首作礼，白尊者大目揵连：「尊者大目揵连！当知我等不见世尊已久，众甚虚渴欲见世尊。尊者大目揵连！若不惮劳者，愿为我等往诣三十三天，普为我等问讯世尊：『少病少恼，起居轻利，安乐住不？』又白世尊：『阎浮提四众愿见世尊，而无神力升三十三天礼敬世尊，三十三天自有神力来下人中，唯愿世尊还阎浮提，以哀愍故。』」时，尊者大目犍连默然而许。

Then the four assemblies, knowing that Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana had silently accepted, each rose from their seats, bowed, and departed.

时，诸四众知尊者大目犍连默然许已，各从座起，作礼而去。

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, knowing that the four assemblies had left, immediately entered concentration. As he was properly absorbed, in the time it takes a strong man to bend and stretch his arm, he disappeared from Śrāvastī and appeared on a soft, dappled stone in the Heaven of the Thirty-three, not far from the pāri jāta and kuṭaja fragrant trees. At that time, the World-Honored One was teaching the Dharma to the assembly of the Heaven of the Thirty-three, surrounded by countless attendants.

尔时，尊者大目犍连知四众去已，即入三昧，如其正受，如大力士屈伸臂顷，从舍卫国没，于三十三天骢色虚软石上，去波梨耶多罗、拘毘陀罗香树不远而现。尔时，世尊与三十三天众无量眷属围绕说法。

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana saw the World-Honored One from afar. He was overjoyed and thought, "Today the World-Honored One is teaching the Dharma to the great assembly of the gods, no different from the assembly in Jambudvīpa.

时，尊者大目犍连遥见世尊，踊跃欢喜，作是念：「今日世尊，诸天大众围绕说法，与阎浮提众会不异。」

At that time, the World-Honored One, knowing the thoughts in Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana's mind, said to Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, "Mahāmaudgalyāyana! It is not by my own power. I wish to teach the Dharma to the gods, and they immediately gather. If I wish for them to leave, they immediately return. They come and go according to their own minds.

尔时，世尊知尊者大目犍连心之所念，语尊者大目犍连言：「大目犍连！非为自力，我欲为诸天说法，彼即来集，欲令其去，彼即还去，彼随心来，随心去也。」

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, withdrew and sat to one side, and said to the World-Honored One, "The various great assemblies of gods have gathered. Among those heavenly beings, are there any who have heard the Dharma taught by the Buddha, World-Honored One, and attained unshakable faith, who, after their bodies perish and their lives end, have been reborn here?"

尔时，尊者大目犍连稽首佛足，退坐一面，白世尊言：「种种诸天大众云集，彼天众中，有曾从佛世尊闻所说法，得不坏净，身坏命终，来生于此？」

The Buddha told Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, "Indeed, indeed. Among the various gods who have gathered here, there are some who have heard the Dharma from their past lives, attained the unshakable faith in the Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha, and accomplished the noble precepts. After their bodies perish and their lives end, they have been reborn here."

佛告尊者大目犍连：「如是，如是。此中种种诸天来云集者，有从宿命闻法，得佛不坏净，法、僧不坏净，圣戒成就，身坏命终，来生于此。」

At that time, Śakra, Lord of the Devas, saw the World-Honored One and Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana praising and speaking to the assembly of devas. He said to Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, "Indeed, indeed. Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana! All the various assemblies here are indeed from past lives who have heard the true Dharma, attained the unshakable faith in the Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha, and accomplished the noble precepts. After their bodies perish and their lives end, they have been reborn here."

时，天帝释见世尊与尊者大目犍连叹说，诸天众共语已，语尊者大目犍连：「如是，如是。尊者大目犍连！此中种种众会皆是宿命，曾闻正法，得于佛不坏净，法、僧不坏净，圣戒成就，身坏命终，来生于此。」

At that time, another bhikṣu saw the World-Honored One, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, and Śakra, Lord of the Devas, speaking well and praising each other. He said to Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, "Indeed, indeed. Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana! All the various devas who have gathered here are indeed from past lives who have heard the true Dharma, attained the unshakable faith in the Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha, and accomplished the noble precepts. After their bodies perish and their lives end, they have been reborn here."

时，有异比丘见世尊与尊者大目犍连及天帝释语言善相述可已，语尊者大目犍连：「如是，如是。尊者大目犍连！是中种种诸天来会此者，皆是宿命曾闻正法，得于佛不坏净，法、僧不坏净，圣戒成就，身坏命终，而来生此。」

At that time, a certain deva rose from his seat, arranged his robes, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I have also attained the unshakable faith in the Buddha, and for that reason I have been reborn here."

时，有一天子从座起，整衣服，偏袒右肩，合掌白佛：「世尊！我亦成就于佛不坏净，故来生此。」

Another deva said, "I have attained the unshakable faith in the Dharma."

复有天子言：「我得法不坏净。」

Some said they had attained the unshakable faith in the Sangha, and some said they had accomplished the noble precepts, and for that reason they had been reborn there. In this way, countless thousands of devas, in the presence of the World-Honored One, each declared and spoke of attaining the Dharma of stream-entry. They all disappeared right in front of the Buddha.

有言得僧不坏净，有言圣戒成就，故来生此。如是诸天无量千数，于世尊前各自记说得须陀洹法，悉于佛前即没不现。

At that time, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana knew that the heavenly beings had left. He rose from his seat, adjusted his robes, bared his right shoulder, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! The four assemblies of Jambudvīpa bow their heads in respect and pay homage at the feet of the World-Honored One, inquiring about the World-Honored One's well-being: 'Are you free from illness and affliction, are you light and at ease, and are you dwelling in peace?' The four assemblies long to see the World-Honored One. They also say to the World-Honored One, 'We in the human realm do not have the divine power to ascend to the Heaven of the Thirty-three to pay homage to the World-Honored One. However, those heavenly beings have great virtue and power and can all come down to Jambudvīpa. We only wish that the World-Honored One would return to Jambudvīpa out of compassion for the four assemblies.'"

时，尊者大目犍连知诸天众去，不久从座起，整衣服，偏袒右肩，白佛言：「世尊！阎浮提四众稽首敬礼世尊足，问讯世尊：『少病少恼，起居轻利，安乐住不？』四众思慕，愿见世尊。又白世尊：『我等人间无有神力升三十三天礼觐世尊，然彼诸天有大德力悉能来下至阎浮提，唯愿世尊还阎浮提，愍四众故。』」

The Buddha told Maudgalyāyana, "You may return and tell the people of Jambudvīpa that after seven days, the World-Honored One will return from the Heaven of the Thirty-three to Jambudvīpa, to the city of Sāmkāśya, under the udumbara tree outside the gate.

佛告目犍连：「汝可还彼，语阎浮提人：『却后七日，世尊当从三十三天还阎浮提僧迦舍城，于外门外优昙钵树下。』」

Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana accepted the Buddha's instruction and immediately entered samādhi. In the time it takes a strong man to bend and stretch his arm, he disappeared from the Heaven of the Thirty-three and arrived in Jambudvīpa, where he told the four assemblies, "All of you should know that after seven days, the World-Honored One will return from the Heaven of the Thirty-three to Jambudvīpa, to the city of Sāmkāśya, under the udumbara tree outside the gate.

尊者大目揵连受世尊教，即入三昧。譬如力士屈伸臂顷，从三十三天没，至阎浮提，告诸四众：「诸人当知，世尊却后七日，从三十三天还阎浮提僧迦舍城，于外门外优昙钵树下。」

As promised, after seven days, the World-Honored One descended from the Heaven of the Thirty-three to the udumbara tree outside the gate of the city of Sāmkāśya in Jambudvīpa. Devas, nāgas, ghosts, spirits, and even Brahmā all came down. At that time, this assembly was named the Assembly of the Descent from Heaven.

如期七日，世尊从三十三天下阎浮提僧迦舍城优昙钵树下，天龙鬼神，乃至梵天，悉从来下，即于此时，名此会名天下处。
