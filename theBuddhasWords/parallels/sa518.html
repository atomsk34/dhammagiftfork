SĀ 518

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 518（五一八） 何器锻铜师

Parallel to: SN 19.10

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Rajagriha. He said to the monks, "I saw a being on the road whose egg-like body was like a jar. When sitting, it sat on top of it, and when walking, it carried it on its shoulders. The Buddha told the monks, 'This being, in a past life, was a copper smith in Rajagriha who deceived people with fake vessels. Because of this karma, he has already experienced immeasurable suffering in hell. The remaining karma from hell has caused him to be reborn in this body, continuing to experience this suffering. Monks, what Mahāmaudgalyāyana saw is true and not false. You should uphold it.'"

一时，佛住王舍城。乃至我于路中见一众生，阴卵如瓮，坐则踞上，行则肩担。乃至佛告诸比丘：「此众生者，过去世时，于王舍城作锻铜师，伪器欺人，缘斯罪故，已地狱中受无量苦，地狱余罪，今得此身，续受斯苦。诸比丘！如大目犍连所见，真实不异，当受持之。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully upheld it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

Just as the copper smith, so too are those who deceive with weights and measures, village heads, and market inspectors.

如锻铜师，如是斗秤欺人、村主、市监，亦复如是。
