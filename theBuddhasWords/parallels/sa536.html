SĀ 536

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 536（五三六） 独一

Parallel to: SN 52.1 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī. The Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana asked the Venerable Anuruddha, "What is meant by cultivating and practicing the four foundations of mindfulness?"

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。乃至尊者大目犍连问尊者阿那律：「云何名为四念处修习多修习？」

The Venerable Anuruddha said to the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, "If a monk generates a thought of disgust towards the internal body, a thought of non-disgust towards the internal body, and a thought of neither disgust nor non-disgust towards the internal body, he abides with right mindfulness and right attentiveness. As with the internal body, so with the external body, the internal and external body, internal feelings, external feelings, internal and external feelings, internal mind, external mind, internal and external mind, internal phenomena, external phenomena, internal and external phenomena, he generates a thought of disgust, a thought of non-disgust, and a thought of neither disgust nor non-disgust, abiding with right mindfulness and right attentiveness. Thus, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, this is called cultivating and practicing the four foundations of mindfulness."

尊者阿那律语尊者大目犍连言：「若比丘于内身起厌离想，于内身起不厌离想、厌离不厌离俱舍想，正念正知。如内身，如是外身、内外身，内受、外受、内外受，内心、外心、内外心，内法、外法、内外法，作厌离想、不厌离想、厌离不厌离俱舍想，住正念正知。如是，尊者大目犍连！是名四念处修习多修习。」

At that time, the Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana immediately entered concentration and, by the power of concentration and supernormal abilities, in the time it takes a strong man to bend and stretch his arm, returned from the Jeta Grove in Śrāvastī to the place where the birds and beasts dwelt in the terrifying and dense forest on the mountain of the demon Rāhu in the village of Vṛji.

时，尊者大目犍连即入三昧，从舍卫国松林精舍入三昧神通力，如力士屈伸臂顷，还到跋祇聚落失收摩罗山恐怖稠林禽兽住处。
