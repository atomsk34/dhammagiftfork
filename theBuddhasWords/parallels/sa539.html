SĀ 539

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 539（五三九） 松林

Parallel to: SN 52.23

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying at Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī. Venerable Śāriputra, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, Venerable Ānanda, and Venerable Aniruddha were staying near the Hand-formed Bathing Pool in Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尊者舍利弗、尊者大目犍连、尊者阿难、尊者阿那律住舍卫国手成浴池侧。

At that time, Venerable Ānanda went to Venerable Aniruddha and exchanged greetings and pleasantries. After sitting down to one side, Venerable Ānanda asked Venerable Aniruddha, "By cultivating which merit and practicing it extensively, did you attain such great virtue, great power, and great supernatural powers?"

尔时，尊者阿难往尊者阿那律所，共相问讯慰劳已，于一面坐。尊者阿难问尊者阿那律：「于何功德修习多修习，成就如是大德大力大神通？」

Venerable Aniruddha said to Venerable Ānanda, "I cultivated the four foundations of mindfulness extensively and attained this great virtue and great power. What are the four? Fixing the mind on the foundation of mindfulness of the body observing the internal body, diligently applying effort, with right mindfulness and right knowledge, eliminating worldly greed and sorrow. Similarly, the external body, the internal and external body, internal feelings, external feelings, internal and external feelings, internal mind, external mind, internal and external mind, internal phenomena, external phenomena, and internal and external phenomena, fixing the mind on the foundation of mindfulness of phenomena, diligently applying effort, eliminating worldly greed and sorrow. Thus, Venerable Ānanda, I cultivated these four foundations of mindfulness extensively, with little effort, and with the pure divine eye surpassing that of humans and gods, I saw all beings, when dying and being born, with good and bad appearances, superior and inferior appearances, good and bad destinies, taking rebirth according to their karma, all as they truly are. These beings, with bad bodily, verbal, and mental actions, slandering the sages, due to wrong views, at the end of their lives, are reborn in hell. These beings, with good bodily, verbal, and mental actions, not slandering the sages, with right views accomplished, due to these causes and conditions, at the end of their lives, are reborn in heaven.

尊者阿那律语尊者阿难：「我于四念处修习多修习，成此大德大力。何等为四？内身身观念处系心住，精勤方便，正念正知，除世间贪忧。如是外身、内外身，内受、外受、内外受，内心、外心、内外心，内法、外法、内外法观念处系心住，精勤方便，除世间贪忧。如是，尊者阿难！我于此四念处修习多修习，少方便，以净天眼过天、人眼，见诸众生，死时、生时，好色、恶色，上色、下色，善趣、恶趣，随业受生，皆如实见。此诸众生身恶行，口、意恶行，诽谤贤圣，邪见因缘，身坏命终，生地狱中。如是众生，身善行，口，意善行，不谤贤圣，正见成就，以是因缘，身坏命终，得生天上。

"It is like a person with clear eyes standing at a crossroads, seeing people coming, going, sitting, or lying down. I am also like this. By cultivating the four foundations of mindfulness extensively, I have attained this great virtue, great power, and great supernatural powers, seeing all beings, when dying and being born, in good or bad destinies. Thus, these beings, with bad bodily, verbal, and mental actions, slandering the sages, due to wrong views, are reborn in hell. Thus, these beings, with good bodily, verbal, and mental actions, not slandering the sages, with right views accomplished, at the end of their lives, are reborn in heaven. Thus, Venerable Ānanda, I have cultivated the four foundations of mindfulness extensively and attained this great virtue, great power, and great supernatural powers."

「譬如明目士夫住四衢道，见诸人民若来、若去、若坐、若卧，我亦如是，于四念处修习多修习，成此大德大力神通，见诸众生，死时、生时，善趣、恶趣。如是众生身恶行，口、意恶行，诽谤贤圣，邪见因缘，生地狱中。如是众生身善行，口、意善行，不谤贤圣，正见因缘，身坏命终，得生天上。如是，尊者阿难！我于四念处修习多修习，成此大德大力神通。」

At that time, the two noble ones had a discussion together, then each rose from their seats and departed.

时，二正士共论议已，各从座起而去。
