SĀ 546

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 546（五四六） 澡灌杖

Parallel to: AN 2.37

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Thus have I heard:

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尔时，尊者摩诃迦旃延在跋兰那聚落乌泥池侧。

At that time, a brāhman carrying a water pot came to Mahākātyāyana and exchanged greetings with him. After sitting down to one side, he asked Mahākātyāyana, "What are the causes and conditions for kings fighting with other kings, and brāhman householders fighting with other brāhman householders?"

时，有执澡灌杖梵志诣摩诃迦旃延所，共相问讯慰劳已，于一面坐，问摩诃迦旃延言：「何因何缘王、王共诤，婆罗门居士、婆罗门居士共诤？」

Mahākātyāyana replied to the brāhman, "It is because of the causes and conditions of attachment to greed that kings fight with other kings, and brāhman householders fight with other brāhman householders."

摩诃迦旃延答梵志言：「贪欲系著因缘故，王、王共诤，婆罗门居士、婆罗门居士共诤。」

The brāhman further asked, "What are the causes and conditions for renunciants fighting with other renunciants?"

梵志复问：「何因何缘出家、出家而复共诤？」

Mahākātyāyana replied, "It is because of the attachment to views that renunciants fight with other renunciants."

摩诃迦旃延答言：「以见欲系著故，出家、出家而复共诤。」

The brāhman further asked, "Mahākātyāyana! Is there anyone who can be free from the attachment to desire and the attachment to views?"

梵志复问：「摩诃迦旃延！颇有能离贪欲系著及离此见欲系著不？」

Venerable Mahākātyāyana replied, "Brahman! There is. My teacher, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, the One with Perfect Knowledge and Conduct, the Sugata, the Knower of the World, the Unsurpassed One, the Tamer of Men, the Teacher of Gods and Men, the Buddha, the Blessed One, is able to be free from the attachment to desire and the attachment to views."

尊者摩诃迦旃延答言：「梵志！有，我大师如来、应、等正觉、明行足、善逝、世间解、无上士、调御丈夫、天人师、佛世尊能离此贪欲系著及见欲系著。」

The brāhman further asked, "Where is the Blessed Buddha now?"

梵志复问：「佛世尊今在何所？」

He replied, "The Blessed Buddha is now among the Brāhmaṇas, in the country of Kosala, in the Jeta Grove of Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in Śrāvastī."

答言：「佛世尊今在婆罗耆人中，拘萨罗国舍卫城祇树给孤独园。」

At that time, the brāhman rose from his seat, adjusted his robes, bared his right shoulder, placed his right knee on the ground, and with palms joined, praised the place where the Buddha was.

尔时，梵志从座起，整衣服，偏袒右肩，右膝著地，向佛所住处合掌赞叹。

"Homage, homage to the Blessed Buddha, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, who is able to abandon the fetters of desire and attachment, who is able to completely abandon the bonds of desire and views, and who purifies the fundamental basis."

「南无南无佛世尊、如来、应供、等正觉，能离欲贪诸系著，悉能远离贪欲缚及诸见欲，净根本。」

At that time, the brāhman who held the bathing staff, having heard what was said by the Venerable Mahākātyāyana, rejoiced and followed his words. He rose from his seat and departed.

时，持澡灌杖梵志闻尊者摩诃迦旃延所说，欢喜随喜，从座起去。
