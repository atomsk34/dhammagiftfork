SĀ 547

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 547（五四七） 执杖

Parallel to: AN 2.38 / EĀ 19.9

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying at Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī. Venerable Mahākātyāyana was at the pond of the Brahmins in Uṇṇāḍa, gathered with many bhikṣus in the dining hall, discussing matters concerning robes.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尊者摩诃迦旃延在婆罗那乌泥池侧，与众多比丘集于食堂，为持衣事。

At that time, there was an old and aged Brāhman with a staff who went to the dining hall, leaned on a pillar on one side, and stood there. After a while, he said to the bhikṣus in silence: "Elder ones! Why do you not speak, greet, or respectfully invite to sit an elder like me?"

时，有执杖梵志年耆根熟，诣食堂所，于一面柱杖而住。须臾默然已，语诸比丘：「诸长老！汝等何故见老宿士不共语问讯，恭敬命坐？」

At that time, Venerable Mahākātyāyana was also sitting in the assembly. Venerable Mahākātyāyana said to the Brāhman: "In our Dharma, when elders come, we all speak, greet, pay respect, and invite them to sit down."

时，尊者摩诃迦旃延亦在众中坐。时，尊者摩诃迦旃延语梵志言：「我法有宿老来，皆共语问讯，恭敬礼拜，命之令坐。」

The Brāhman said: "I see no one in this assembly older than me who is not respected, paid homage to, or invited to sit down. How can you say: 'In our Dharma, when elders come, we all speak, greet, pay respect, and invite them to sit down'?"

梵志言：「我见此众中无有老于我者，不恭敬礼拜、命坐，汝云何言：『我法见有宿老，恭敬礼拜，命其令坐。』？」

Mahākātyāyana said: "Brāhman! If there are those who are eighty or ninety years old, with white hair and falling teeth, but have the qualities of youth, they are not elders. Although there are those who are twenty-five years old, with fair skin, black hair, and in the prime of youth, if they have the qualities of elders, they are counted as elders."

摩诃迦旃延言：「梵志！若有耆年八十、九十，发白齿落，成就年少法者，此非宿士；虽复年少年二十五，色白发黑，盛壮美满，而彼成就耆年法者，为宿士数。」

The Brāhman asked: "What is meant by being eighty or ninety years old, with white hair and falling teeth, but still having the qualities of youth? And what is meant by being twenty-five years old, with fair skin, black hair, and in the prime of youth, being counted as an elder?"

梵志问言：「云何名为八十、九十，发白齿落，而复成就年少之法，年二十五，肤白发黑，盛壮美色，为宿士数？」

Venerable Mahākātyāyana said to the Brāhman: "There are five kinds of sensual pleasures, namely, the eye cognizing forms with desire, joy, and recollection; the ear cognizing sounds, the nose cognizing smells, the tongue cognizing tastes, and the body cognizing tactile objects with desire, joy, and recollection. If one is not free from desire, not free from passion, not free from love, not free from recollection, and not free from defilement towards these five kinds of sensual pleasures, Brāhman! If this is the case, then even if one is eighty or ninety years old, with white hair and falling teeth, one is said to have the qualities of youth. Even if one is twenty-five years old, with fair skin, black hair, and in the prime of youth, if one is free from desire, free from passion, free from love, free from recollection, and free from defilement towards the five kinds of sensual pleasures, then even if one is twenty-five years old, with fair skin, black hair, and in the prime of youth, having the qualities of an elder, one is counted as an elder."

尊者摩诃迦旃延语梵志言：「有五欲功德。谓眼识色爱、乐、念，耳识声、鼻识香、舌识味、身识触爱、乐、念。于此五欲功德不离贪、不离欲、不离爱、不离念、不离浊。梵志！若如是者，虽复八十、九十，发白齿落，是名成就年少之法。虽年二十五，肤白发黑，盛壮美色，于五欲功德离贪、离欲、离爱、离念、离浊，若如是者，虽复年少年二十五，肤白发黑，盛壮美色，成就老人法，为宿士数。」

At that time, the Brāhman said to Venerable Mahākātyāyana: "According to what the Venerable has said, I reflect on myself. Although old, I am young. Although you are young, you have the qualities of an elder. There are many things in the world. Please return."

尔时，梵志语尊者摩诃迦旃延：「如尊者所说义，我自省察，虽老则少，汝等虽少，成耆年法，世间多事，令便请还。」

Venerable Mahākātyāyana said: "Brāhman! You know the time yourself."

尊者摩诃迦旃延言：「梵志！汝自知时。」

At that time, the Brāhman, having heard what was said by Venerable Mahākātyāyana, rejoiced and returned to his own place.

尔时，梵志闻尊者摩诃迦旃延所说，欢喜随喜，还其本处。
