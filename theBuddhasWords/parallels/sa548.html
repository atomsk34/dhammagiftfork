SĀ 548

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 548（五四八） 摩偷罗

Parallel to: MN 84

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying at Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī. Venerable Mahākātyāyana was staying in a dense forest.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尊者摩诃迦旃延在稠林中住。

At that time, the king of Mathurā was a prince from the west. He went to Venerable Mahākātyāyana, bowed at Mahākātyāyana's feet, sat to one side, and asked Venerable Mahākātyāyana, "Brahmins say, 'We are the best, others are inferior. We are white, others are black. Brahmins are pure, not non-Brahmins. Brahmins are born from the mouth, transformed by Brahmins, and belong to Brahmins.' Venerable Mahākātyāyana, what is the meaning of this?"

时，摩偷罗国王是西方王子，诣尊者摩诃迦旃延所，礼摩诃迦旃延足，退坐一面，问尊者摩诃迦旃延：「婆罗门自言：『我第一，他人卑劣，我白，余人黑，婆罗门清净，非非婆罗门；是婆罗门子从口生，婆罗门所化，是婆罗门所有。』尊者摩诃迦旃延！此义云何？」

Venerable Mahākātyāyana said to King Mathurā, "Great King! This is just worldly speech. Worldly speech says, 'Brahmins are the best, others are inferior. Brahmins are white, others are black. Brahmins are pure, not non-Brahmins. Brahmins are born from Brahmins, born from the mouth, transformed by Brahmins, and belong to Brahmins.' Great King, you should know that the true reality is based on karma."

尊者摩诃迦旃延语摩偷罗王言：「大王！此是世间言说耳。世间言说言：『婆罗门第一，余人卑劣，婆罗门白，余人黑，婆罗门清净，非非婆罗门。婆罗门从婆罗门生，生从口生，婆罗门所化，是婆罗门所有。』大王当知，业真实者，是依业者。」

The king said to Venerable Mahākātyāyana, "This is a brief explanation. I do not understand. Please explain it again."

王语尊者摩诃迦旃延：「此则略说，我所不解，愿重分别。」

Venerable Mahākātyāyana said, "I will ask you now. Answer me as you please." He then asked, "Great King! You are a Brahmin king. Do you summon all the Brahmins, Kṣatriyas, householders, and elders in your own country, and provide them with wealth and power to serve and guard you, rising before them and retiring after them, and do all their bidding as you please?"

尊者摩诃迦旃延言：「今当问汝，随问答我。」即问言：「大王！汝为婆罗门王，于自国土，诸婆罗门、刹利、居士、长者，此四种人悉皆召来，以财以力使其侍卫，先起后卧，及诸使令，悉如意不？」

He replied, "As I please."

答言：「如意。」

He further asked, "Great King! If a Kṣatriya becomes king, a householder becomes king, or an elder becomes king, do they summon all the four castes in their own country, provide them with wealth and power to serve and guard them, rise before them and retire after them, and do all their bidding as they please?"

复问：「大王！刹利为王、居士为王、长者为王，于自国土所有四姓悉皆召来，以财以力令其侍卫，先起后卧，及诸使令，皆如意不？」

He replied, "As I please."

答言：「如意。」

He further asked, "Great King! Are these four castes all equal? What are the differences? Know, Great King! The four castes are all equal, without any difference in superiority or inferiority."

复问：「大王！如是四姓悉皆平等，有何差别？当知，大王！四种姓者，皆悉平等，无有胜如差别之异。」

King Māthura said to Venerable Mahākātyāyana, "Indeed, Venerable One! The four castes are all equal, without any difference in superiority or inferiority."

摩偷罗王白尊者摩诃迦旃延：「实尔，尊者！四姓皆等，无有种种胜如差别。」

"Therefore, Great King! Know that the four castes are only distinguished by worldly speech, and even in terms of occupation, there is no real difference. Furthermore, Great King! If there are Brahmins in this country who commit theft, what should be done?"

「是故，大王！当知四姓，世间言说为差别耳，乃至依业，真实无差别也。复次，大王！此国土中有婆罗门，有偷盗者，当如之何？」

The king said to Venerable Mahākātyāyana, "If there are Brahmins who commit theft, they may be beaten, bound, driven out of the country, fined gold, or have their hands, feet, ears, and noses cut off. If the crime is serious, they may be killed. But those who steal are still called Brahmins."

王白尊者摩诃迦旃延：「婆罗门中有偷盗者，或鞭、或缚，或驱出国，或罚其金，或截手足耳鼻，罪重则杀，及其盗者。然婆罗门，则名为贼。」

He further asked, "Great King! If there are thieves among the Kṣatriyas, householders, and elders, what should be done?"

复问：「大王！若刹利、居士、长者中有偷盗者，当复如何？」

The king said to Venerable Mahākātyāyana, "They should also be beaten, bound, driven out of the country, fined gold, and have their hands, feet, ears, and noses cut off. If the crime is serious, they should be killed."

王白尊者摩诃迦旃延：「亦鞭、亦缚，亦驱出国，亦罚其金，亦复断截手足耳鼻，罪重则杀。」

"Is it not so, Great King? Are not the four castes all equal? Are there not various distinctions and differences?"

「如是，大王！岂非四姓悉平等耶？为有种种差别异不？」

The king said to Venerable Mahākātyāyana, "As for this meaning, there are indeed no various distinctions of superiority and inferiority."

王白尊者摩诃迦旃延：「如是义者，实无种种胜如差别。」

Venerable Mahākātyāyana again said to the king, "Know this, Great King! As for the four castes, worldly speech says: 'Brahmins are the first, the others are inferior. Brahmins are white, the others are black. Brahmins are pure, not non-Brahmins.' Should one rely on the truth of karma, or on karma?"

尊者摩诃迦旃延复语王言：「当知，大王！四种姓者，世间言说言：『婆罗门第一，余悉卑劣，婆罗门白，余人悉黑，婆罗门清净，非非婆罗门。』当依业真实、业依耶？」

He further asked, "Great King! If Brahmins commit the ten unwholesome actions of killing, stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, harsh speech, divisive speech, idle chatter, greed, anger, and wrong views, will they be reborn in the lower realms or the higher realms? What did you hear from the Arhat?"

复问：「大王！婆罗门杀生、偷盗、邪婬、妄言、恶口、两舌、绮语、贪、恚、邪见，作十不善业迹已，为生恶趣耶？善趣耶？于阿罗呵所为何所闻？」

The king said to Venerable Mahākātyāyana, "Brahmins who commit the ten unwholesome actions will fall into the lower realms. This is what I heard from the Arhat. The same is said of Kshatriyas, Vaishyas, and Shudras."

王白尊者摩诃迦旃延：「婆罗门作十不善业迹，当堕恶趣，阿罗呵所，作如是闻。刹利、居士、长者亦如是说。」

He further asked, "Great King! If Brahmins practice the ten wholesome actions, abstaining from killing and so forth, and have right view, where will they be reborn? In the higher realms or the lower realms? What did you hear from the Arhat?"

复问：「大王！若婆罗门行十善业迹，离杀生，乃至正见，当生何所？为善趣耶？为恶趣耶？于阿罗呵所，为何所闻？」

The king said to Venerable Mahākātyāyana, "If Brahmins practice the ten wholesome actions, they will be reborn in the higher realms. This is what I heard from the Arhat. The same is said of Kshatriyas, Vaishyas, and Shudras."

王白尊者摩诃迦旃延：「若婆罗门行十善业迹者，当生善趣，阿罗呵所，作如是、闻如是，刹利、居士、长者亦如是说。」

He further asked, "How is it, Great King? Are these four castes equal or are there distinctions of superiority and inferiority?"

复问：「云何？大王！如是四姓，为平等不？为有种种胜如差别？」

The king said to Venerable Mahākātyāyana, "In this way, there is equality and no distinctions of superiority and inferiority."

王白尊者摩诃迦旃延：「如是义者，则为平等，无有种种胜如差别。」

"Therefore, Great King, you should know that the four castes are all equal, without distinctions of superiority and inferiority. It is because of worldly speech that there are statements such as: 'Brahmins are the best, Brahmins are white, the others are all black, Brahmins are pure, not non-Brahmins; Brahmins are born, born from the mouth, Brahmins create, Brahmins transform, Brahmins possess.' You should know that karma is true and karma is the basis."

「是故，大王当知，四姓悉平等耳，无有种种胜如差别。世间言说故有：『婆罗门第一，婆罗门白，余者悉黑，婆罗门清净，非非婆罗门；婆罗门生，生从口生，婆罗门作，婆罗门化，婆罗门所有。』当知业真实、业依。」

The king said to Venerable Mahākātyāyana, "It is indeed as you have said. It is all because of worldly speech that there are statements such as: 'Brahmins are superior, the others are inferior, Brahmins are white, the others are all black, Brahmins are pure, not non-Brahmins; Brahmins are born, born from the mouth, Brahmins transform, Brahmins possess.' It is all because karma is true and based on karma."

王白尊者摩诃迦旃延：「实如所说，皆是世间言说，故有：『婆罗门胜，余者卑劣，婆罗门白，余者悉黑，婆罗门清净，非非婆罗门；婆罗门生，生从口生，婆罗门化，婆罗门所有。』皆是业真实、依于业。」

At that time, King Mātaṅga, having heard what Venerable Mahākātyāyana had said, joyfully assented, paid homage, and departed.

尔时，摩偷罗王闻尊者摩诃迦旃延所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
