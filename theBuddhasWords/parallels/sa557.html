SĀ 557

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 557（五五七） 阇知罗

Parallel to: AN 9.37 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying at the Ghosita Monastery in the country of Kusinara. At that time, Venerable Ānanda was also staying there.

一时，佛住拘睒弥国瞿师罗园。尔时，尊者阿难亦在彼住。

At that time, the nun Jnanaloka went to Venerable Ānanda, bowed her head at his feet, sat to one side, and asked Venerable Ānanda, "If one dwells in the formless concentration of mind, neither rising nor sinking, liberated and abiding, abiding and liberated, Venerable Ānanda, what fruit and merit does the World-Honored One say this leads to?"

时，有阇知罗比丘尼诣尊者阿难所，稽首礼足，退坐一面，问尊者阿难：「若无相心三昧，不涌、不没，解脱已住，住已解脱。尊者阿难！世尊说此何果、何功德？」

Venerable Ānanda said to the nun Jnanaloka, "If one dwells in the formless concentration of mind, neither rising nor sinking, liberated and abiding, abiding and liberated, the World-Honored One says this leads to the fruit and merit of wisdom."

尊者阿难语阇知罗比丘尼：「若无相心三昧，不涌、不没，解脱已住，住已解脱，世尊说是智果、智功德。」

The nun Jnanaloka said, "Wonderful! Venerable Ānanda! The Master and his disciples have the same words, the same flavor, and the same meaning. Venerable Ānanda! Once, the Buddha was dwelling in the Ancestral Forest in the city of Savatthi. At that time, many nuns went to the Buddha and asked about this meaning. At that time, the World-Honored One explained with these words, this flavor, and this meaning to the nuns. Therefore, we should know that it is wonderful that the Master and his disciples have the same words, the same flavor, and the same meaning, namely, the meaning of the first phrase."

阇知罗比丘尼言：「奇哉！尊者阿难！大师及弟子同句、同味、同义。尊者阿难！昔于一时，佛在娑祇城安禅林中。时，有众多比丘尼往诣佛所，问如此义。尔时，世尊以如是句、如是味、如是义为诸比丘尼说，是故当知奇特，大师弟子所说同句、同味、同义，所谓第一句义。」

At that time, the nun Jnanaloka, having heard what Venerable Ānanda said, joyfully rejoiced, paid homage, and departed.

时，阇知罗比丘尼闻尊者阿难所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。

Just as the nun Jnanaloka, so too was the nun Kalavika.

如阇知罗比丘尼，迦罗跋比丘尼亦尔。
