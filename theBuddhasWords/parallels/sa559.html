SĀ 559

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 559（五五九） 迦摩

Parallel to: SN 35.233

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying in the country of Pāṭaligāma, together with Venerable Ānanda and Venerable Kāma, at the Kukkuṭārāma Monastery.

一时，佛住波罗利弗妬路国，尊者阿难及尊者迦摩亦在波罗利弗妬路鸡林精舍。

At that time, Venerable Kāma went to where Venerable Ānanda was, exchanged greetings and pleasantries, and sat to one side. He said to Venerable Ānanda, "Amazing! Venerable Ānanda, with eyes and forms, ears and sounds, nose and smells, tongue and tastes, body and tactile objects, mind and mental phenomena, there are bhikṣus who have these things but are unable to be aware of them. How is it, Venerable Ānanda? Are those bhikṣus unable to be aware because they have thoughts, or are they unable to be aware because they have no thoughts?"

时，尊者迦摩诣尊者阿难所，共相问讯慰劳已，于一面坐。语尊者阿难：「奇哉！尊者阿难！有眼有色、有耳有声、有鼻有香、有舌有味、有身有触、有意有法，而有比丘有是等法，能不觉知。云何？尊者阿难！彼比丘为有想不觉知，为无想故不觉知？」

Venerable Ānanda said to Bhikṣu Kāma, "Those with thoughts are also unable to be aware, let alone those without thoughts."

尊者阿难语迦摩比丘言：「有想者亦不觉知，况复无想。」

He further asked Venerable Ānanda, "What are the thoughts that are not aware of existence?"

复问尊者阿难：「何等为有想于有而不觉知？」

Venerable Ānanda said to Bhikṣu Kāma, "If a bhikṣu, having abandoned sensual desires and unwholesome states, with initial and sustained thought, dwells in the first dhyāna endowed with joy and bliss born of seclusion, he has thoughts but is not aware of the dharmas. Similarly, the second, third, and fourth dhyānas, the sphere of infinite space, the sphere of infinite consciousness, and the sphere of nothingness are fully accomplished. He has thoughts but is not aware of the dharmas. How is it that he has no thoughts but is not aware of the dharmas? Such a bhikṣu does not recollect any thoughts, and dwells in the formless concentration of the absence of thought, having realized it in person. This is called a bhikṣu having no thoughts but not being aware of the dharmas.

尊者阿难语迦摩比丘言：「若比丘离欲、恶不善法，有觉有观，离生喜乐，初禅具足住。如是有想比丘有法而不觉知。如是第二、第三、第四禅，空入处、识入处、无所有入处具足住。如是有想比丘有法而不觉知。云何无想有法而不觉知？如是比丘一切想不忆念，无想心三昧身作证具足住，是名比丘无想于有法而不觉知。」

Venerable Kāma Bhikṣu further asked Venerable Ānanda, "If a bhikṣu dwells in the formless concentration of the absence of thought, neither rising nor sinking, liberated and abiding, abiding and liberated, what fruit and merit does the World-Honored One say this is?"

尊者迦摩比丘复问尊者阿难：「若比丘无想心三昧，不涌不没，解脱已住，住已解脱，世尊说此是何果、何功德？」

Venerable Ānanda said to Bhikṣu Kāma, "If a bhikṣu dwells in the formless concentration of the absence of thought, neither rising nor sinking, liberated and abiding, abiding and liberated, the World-Honored One says this is the fruit and merit of wisdom."

尊者阿难语迦摩比丘言：「若比丘无想心三昧，不涌不没，解脱已住，住已解脱，世尊说此是智果、智功德。」

At that time, after the two noble ones had discussed together, they rejoiced in accordance with their joy, and each rose from their seats and departed.

时，二正士共论议已，欢喜随喜，各从坐起去。
