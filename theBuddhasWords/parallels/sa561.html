SĀ 561

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 561（五六一） 婆罗门

Parallel to: SN 51.15

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in the country of Śrāvastī, together with Venerable Ānanda.

一时，佛住俱睒弥国瞿师罗园，尊者阿难亦在彼住。

At that time, a certain brāhman went to where Venerable Ānanda was, exchanged greetings and pleasantries, sat to one side, and asked Venerable Ānanda, "Why do you practice the holy life under the śramaṇa Gautama?"

时，有异婆罗门诣尊者阿难所，共相问讯慰劳已，于一面坐，问尊者阿难：「何故于沙门瞿昙所修梵行？」

Venerable Ānanda said to the brāhman, "To put an end to it."

尊者阿难语婆罗门：「为断故。」

He further asked, "What does the Venerable put an end to?"

复问：「尊者何所断？」

He replied, "I put an end to craving."

答言：「断爱。」

He further asked, "Venerable Ānanda, what do you rely on to put an end to craving?"

复问：「尊者阿难！何所依而得断爱？」

He replied, "Brahmin, I rely on desire to put an end to craving."

答言：「婆罗门！依于欲而断爱。」

He further asked, "Venerable Ānanda, isn't it limitless?"

复问：「尊者阿难！岂非无边际？」

He replied, "Brahmin, it is not limitless. It is limited, not limitless."

答言：「婆罗门！非无边际。如是有边际，非无边际。」

He further asked, "Venerable Ānanda, how is it limited, not limitless?"

复问：「尊者阿难！云何有边际，非无边际。」

He replied, "Brahmin, I will ask you, please answer me as you wish. Brahmin, what do you think? Did you have any desire to come to the monastery before?"

答言：「婆罗门！我今问汝，随意答我。婆罗门！于意云何？汝先有欲来诣精舍不？」

The brahmin replied, "Yes, Ānanda!"

婆罗门答言：「如是，阿难！」

"So it is, brahmin! Having come to the monastery, did that desire cease?"

「如是，婆罗门！来至精舍已，彼欲息不？」

He replied, "So it is, Venerable Ānanda! He came to the monastery with diligence, skillful means, and consideration."

答言：「如是，尊者阿难！彼精进、方便、筹量，来诣精舍。」

He further asked, "Having come to the monastery, did that diligence, skillful means, and consideration cease?"

复问：「至精舍已，彼精进、方便、筹量息不？」

He replied, "So it is, Venerable Ānanda!"

答言：「如是，尊者阿难！」

He further said to the brāhmaṇa, "So it is, brāhmaṇa! The Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, knowing and seeing, speaks of the four bases of supernormal power, purifying beings through the one vehicle, extinguishing suffering and distress, and cutting off sorrow and lamentation. What are the four? The bases of supernormal power accomplished through the practice of concentration on desire, the bases of supernormal power accomplished through the practice of concentration on effort, the bases of supernormal power accomplished through the practice of concentration on mind, and the bases of supernormal power accomplished through the practice of concentration on contemplation. Thus, the noble disciple cultivates the bases of supernormal power accomplished through the practice of concentration on desire, relying on detachment, relying on lack of desire, relying on transcendence, relying on cessation, and inclining towards relinquishment, up to the cessation of craving. When craving ceases, desire also ceases. Cultivating the bases of supernormal power accomplished through the practice of concentration on effort, mind, and contemplation, relying on detachment, relying on lack of desire, relying on transcendence, relying on cessation, and inclining towards relinquishment, up to the exhaustion of craving. When craving is exhausted, contemplation also ceases. What do you think, brāhmaṇa? Is this not the ultimate?"

复语婆罗门：「如是，婆罗门！如来、应、等正觉所知所见，说四如意足，以一乘道净众生、灭苦恼、断忧悲。何等为四？欲定断行成就如意足，精进定、心定、思惟定断行成就如意足。如是，圣弟子修欲定断行成就如意足，依离、依无欲、依出要、依灭、向于舍，乃至断爱，爱断已，彼欲亦息。修精进定、心定、思惟定断行成就，依离、依无欲、依出要、依灭、向于舍，乃至爱尽，爱尽已，思惟则息。婆罗门！于意云何？此非边际耶？」

The brāhmaṇa said, "Venerable Ānanda! This is the ultimate, not the non-ultimate."

婆罗门言：「尊者阿难！此是边际，非不边际。」

At that time, the brāhmaṇa, having heard what was said by Venerable Ānanda, joyfully assented and rose from his seat to depart.

尔时，婆罗门闻尊者阿难所说，欢喜随喜，从座起去。
