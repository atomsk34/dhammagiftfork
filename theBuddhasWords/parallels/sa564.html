SĀ 564

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 564（五六四） 比丘尼

Parallel to: AN 4.159

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī, together with Venerable Ānanda.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。尊者阿难亦在彼住。

At that time, a certain bhikṣuṇī had a lustful mind towards Venerable Ānanda, and sent a messenger to Venerable Ānanda, saying: "My body is afflicted by illness. I only wish that the Venerable One would pity me and come to see me."

时，有异比丘尼于尊者阿难所，起染著心，遣使白尊者阿难：「我身遇病苦，唯愿尊者哀愍见看。」

Venerable Ānanda, in the morning, put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and went to the bhikṣuṇī.

尊者阿难晨朝著衣持钵，往彼比丘尼所。

The bhikṣuṇī saw Venerable Ānanda coming from afar, and lay down naked on the bed. Venerable Ānanda saw the bhikṣuṇī's body from afar, and immediately restrained his senses, turned his back, and stood there. The bhikṣuṇī saw Venerable Ānanda restrain his senses, turn his back, and stand there. She was ashamed of herself, got up, put on her clothes, spread out a sitting mat, went out to welcome Venerable Ānanda, invited him to take a seat, bowed her head at his feet, and stood to one side.

彼比丘尼遥见尊者阿难来，露身体卧床上。尊者阿难遥见彼比丘尼身，即自摄歛诸根，回身背住。彼比丘尼见尊者阿难摄歛诸根，回身背住，即自惭愧，起著衣服，敷坐具，出迎尊者阿难，请令就座，稽首礼足，退住一面。

At that time, Venerable Ānanda spoke the Dharma for her, saying: "Sister! This body is nourished by impure food, nourished by arrogance, nourished by craving, nourished by sexual desire. Sister! Relying on impure food, one should cut off impure food; relying on arrogance, one should cut off arrogance; relying on craving, one should cut off craving. Sister! What is meant by relying on impure food and cutting off impure food? It means that a noble disciple contemplates the food he eats, without attachment, without arrogance, without polishing, without adorning, for the sake of sustaining the body, for the sake of nourishing, for the sake of curing hunger and thirst, for the sake of upholding the holy life, to extinguish past feelings and not give rise to new feelings, to cultivate and nourish strength, happiness, and touch. One should abide like this. It is like a merchant who anoints his cart with ghee and oil, without attachment, without arrogance, without polishing, without adorning, for the sake of transporting. It is like a person with a wound applying ghee and oil, without attachment, without arrogance, without polishing, without adorning, for the sake of healing the wound. In the same way, a noble disciple contemplates the food he eats, without attachment, without arrogance, without polishing, without adorning, for the sake of sustaining, for the sake of curing hunger and thirst, for the sake of upholding the holy life, to separate from past feelings and not give rise to new feelings, to cultivate and nourish strength, happiness, and guiltless touch, and to abide in peace. Sister! This is called relying on food and cutting off food.

时，尊者阿难为说法言：「姊妹！如此身者，秽食长养、憍慢长养、爱所长养、婬欲长养。姊妹！依秽食者，当断秽食，依于慢者，当断憍慢，依于爱者，当断爱欲。姊妹！云何名依于秽食，当断秽食？谓圣弟子于食计数思惟而食，无著乐想、无憍慢想、无摩拭想、无庄严想，为持身故、为养活故、治饥渴病故、摄受梵行故，宿诸受令灭，新诸受不生，崇习长养，若力、若乐、若触，当如是住。譬如商客以酥油膏以膏其车，无染著想、无憍慢想、无摩拭想、无庄严想，为运载故，如病疮者涂以酥油，无著乐想、无憍慢想、无摩拭想、无庄严想，为疮愈故。如是，圣弟子计数而食，无染著想、无憍慢想、无摩拭想、无庄严想，为养活故、治饥渴故、摄受梵行故，宿诸受离，新诸受不起，若力、若乐、若无罪触安隐住。姊妹！是名依食断食。

Relying on arrogance to cut off arrogance. What is meant by relying on arrogance to cut off arrogance? It means that a disciple hears that a certain Venerable One and a certain Venerable One's disciple have exhausted all taints, attained liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom, and in this very life, they know and realize for themselves: 'My birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, there is no more of this state.' Having heard this, they think: 'Those noble disciples have exhausted all taints, and they know for themselves that they will not be reborn again. Why can't I exhaust all taints? Why can't I know for myself that I will not be reborn again?' At that time, they are able to exhaust all taints, and know for themselves that they will not be reborn again. Sister! This is called relying on arrogance to cut off arrogance.

「依慢断慢者。云何依慢断慢？谓弟子闻某尊者、某尊者弟子尽诸有漏，无漏心解脱、慧解脱，现法自知作证：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』闻已，作是念：『彼圣弟子尽诸有漏，乃至自知不受后有。我今何故不尽诸有漏，何故不自知不受后有。』当于尔时则能断诸有漏，乃至自知不受后有。姊妹！是名依慢断慢。

"Sister! What is meant by relying on craving to cut off craving? It means that a noble disciple hears that a certain Venerable One and a certain Venerable One's disciple have exhausted all taints, and so on, and know for themselves that they will not be reborn again. They think: 'Why can't we exhaust all taints, and know for ourselves that we will not be reborn again?' At that time, they are able to exhaust all taints, and know for themselves that they will not be reborn again. Sister! This is called relying on craving to cut off craving. Sister! The one who does nothing cuts off sexual desire and severs the bridge of union."

「姊妹！云何依爱断爱？谓圣弟子闻某尊者、某尊者弟子尽诸有漏，乃至自知不受后有。我等何不尽诸有漏，乃至自知不受后有。彼于尔时能断诸有漏，乃至自知不受后有。姊妹！是名依爱断爱。姊妹！无所行者，断截婬欲、和合桥梁。」

When the Venerable Ānanda taught this Dharma, that bhikṣuṇī became free from defilements, attained the pure Dharma-eye, saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, realized the Dharma, entered the Dharma, crossed over doubt, and was no longer dependent on others. She attained fearlessness in the true Dharma and Vinaya. She bowed to the Venerable Ānanda's feet and said to the Venerable Ānanda:

尊者阿难说是法时，彼比丘尼远尘离垢，得法眼净，彼比丘尼见法、得法、觉法、入法，度狐疑，不由于他，于正法、律，心得无畏。礼尊者阿难足，白尊者阿难：

"I now confess and repent: I was foolish and unskillful, and did such things that are not in accord with the Dharma. Now, in the presence of the Venerable Ānanda, I see my faults and know my faults. I confess and repent, out of compassion!"

「我今发露悔过：愚痴不善，脱作如是不流类事；今于尊者阿难所，自见过、自知过，发露忏悔，哀愍故！」

The Venerable Ānanda said to the bhikṣuṇī: "You now truly see your faults and know your faults. You were foolish and unskillful, and you know that you committed faults that are not in accord with the Dharma. You now know and see them and repent. In the future, you will attain the complete precepts. I now accept your repentance out of compassion, so that your good Dharma may increase and never decline. Why is that? If one sees one's faults, knows one's faults, and is able to repent, one will attain the complete precepts in the future, and the good Dharma will increase and never decline."

尊者阿难语比丘尼：「汝今真实自见罪、自知罪，愚痴不善，汝自知作不类之罪，汝今自知、自见而悔过，于未来世得具足戒。我今受汝悔过，哀愍故，令汝善法增长，终不退灭。所以者何？若有自见罪、自知罪，能悔过者，于未来世得具足戒，善法增长，终不退灭。」

The Venerable Ānanda taught the bhikṣuṇī in various ways, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting her. Then he rose from his seat and left.

尊者阿难为彼比丘尼种种说法，示、教、照、喜已，从座起去。
