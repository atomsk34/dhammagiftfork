SĀ 565

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 565（五六五） 婆头

Parallel to: AN 4.194

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling in the human realm of Qiaochi, together with Venerable Ānanda, and arrived at the north of the country of Potou, in the forest of the body-forgiving.

一时，佛在桥池人间游行，与尊者阿难俱，至婆头聚落国北身恕林中。

At that time, the young men of Potou Village heard that Venerable Ānanda was traveling in the human realm of Qiaochi and staying in the forest of the body-forgiving, north of the country of Potou. Having heard this, they called each other to gather and went to where Venerable Ānanda was. They bowed their heads to Venerable Ānanda's feet and sat to one side.

尔时，婆头聚落诸童子闻尊者阿难桥池人间游行，住婆头聚落国北身恕林中。闻已，相呼聚集，往诣尊者阿难所，稽首礼尊者阿难足，退坐一面。

At that time, Venerable Ānanda said to the young men, "Imperial lineage! The Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, speaks of four kinds of purity: purity of precepts, purity of mind, purity of view, and purity of liberation.

时，尊者阿难语诸童子言：「帝种！如来、应、等正觉说四种清净，戒清净、心清净、见清净、解脱清净。

What is purity of precepts? It means that a noble disciple abides in precepts, the precepts of the Prātimokṣa, increasing them, being fully endowed with dignified conduct, able to give rise to fear even for minor offenses, and upholding the precepts. For those whose precepts are not yet complete, it enables them to be fulfilled; for those who have already fulfilled them, it enables them to uphold them in accordance. With the desire, effort, and skillful means to surpass, one diligently strives with vigor and is able to embrace all mental and physical dharmas. This is called the purity of precepts that is to be abandoned.

「云何为戒清净？谓圣弟子住于戒，波罗提木叉，戒增长，威仪具足，于微细罪能生恐怖，受持学戒。戒身不满者，能令满足；已满者，随顺执持，欲精进方便超出，精勤勇猛，堪能诸身心法，常能摄受，是名戒净断。

Kṣatriya! What is meant by the purity of mind that is to be abandoned? It means that a noble disciple, having abandoned sensual desires and unwholesome states, abides fully in the fourth dhyāna. For those whose concentration is not yet complete, it enables them to be fulfilled; for those who have already fulfilled it, it enables them to uphold it in accordance. With the desire, effort, and so forth, up to always upholding it, this is called the purity of mind that is to be abandoned.

「苦种！云何名为心净断？谓圣弟子离欲、恶不善法，乃至第四禅具足住；定身未满者令满，已满者随顺执受，欲精进乃至常执受，是名心净断。

Kṣatriya! What is meant by the purity of view that is to be abandoned? It means that a noble disciple hears the Dharma taught by the Great Teacher. As he teaches the Dharma in such and such a way, he enters into the correct contemplation of reality in such and such a way. In this way, he attains joy, follows joy, and follows the Buddha.

「苦种！云何名为见净断？谓圣弟子闻大师说法。如是如是说法，则如是如是入如实正观。如是如是得欢喜、得随喜、得从于佛。

Furthermore, a noble disciple does not hear the Dharma taught by the Great Teacher, but hears it from other wise and venerable practitioners of the holy life. As he hears the venerable practitioners of the holy life teach in such and such a way, he enters into the correct contemplation of reality in such and such a way. As he contemplates in this way, he attains joy, follows joy, and has faith in the true Dharma.

「复次，圣弟子不闻大师说法，然从余明智尊重梵行者说，闻尊重梵行者如是如是说，则如是如是入如实观察。如是如是观察，于彼法得欢喜、随喜，信于正法。

Furthermore, a noble disciple does not hear the Dharma taught by the Great Teacher, nor does he hear it from other wise and venerable practitioners of the holy life. He recites and practices what he has previously heard and received. As he recites and practices what he has previously heard and received in such and such a way, he enters into those teachings in such and such a way, and so on, up to having faith in the true Dharma.

「复次，圣弟子不闻大师说法，亦复不闻明智尊重梵行者说，随先所闻受持者重诵习，随先所闻受持者如是如是重诵已。如是如是得入彼法，乃至信于正法。

Furthermore, a noble disciple does not hear the Dharma taught by the Great Teacher, nor does he hear it from other wise and venerable practitioners of the holy life. He is also unable to recite and practice what he has previously heard and received. However, he explains in detail the teachings he has previously heard. As he explains in detail the teachings he has previously heard in such and such a way, he enters into the teachings in such and such a way, correctly contemplates with right knowledge, and so on, up to having faith in the true Dharma.

「复次，圣弟子不闻大师说法，不闻明智尊重梵行者说，又复不能先所受持重诵习，然先所闻法为人广说，先所闻法如是如是为人广说。如是如是得入于法，正智观察，乃至信于正法。

Furthermore, a noble disciple does not hear the Dharma taught by the Great Teacher, nor does he hear it from other wise and venerable practitioners of the holy life. He is also unable to recite and practice what he has previously heard and received, nor does he explain in detail the teachings he has previously heard. However, he alone in a quiet place contemplates and observes the teachings he has previously heard. As he contemplates and observes in such and such a way, he enters into the true Dharma in such and such a way, and so on, up to having faith in the true Dharma. Thus, hearing from others and contemplating correctly within, this is called causing the arising of right view that has not yet arisen, and causing the increase of right view that has already arisen. This is called causing the completion of the body of precepts that has not yet been completed, and causing the acceptance and support of those who have already completed it. The desire, effort, and means, up to the constant acceptance and support, are called the purification of view.

「复次，圣弟子不闻大师说法，复不闻明智尊重梵行者说，又复不能先所受持重诵习，亦复不以先所闻法为人广说，然于先所闻法独一静处思惟观察。如是如是思惟观察，如是如是得入正法，乃至信于正法。如是从他闻，内正思惟，是名未起正见令起，已起正见令增广；是名未满戒身令满，已满者随顺摄受，欲精进方便，乃至常摄受，是名见净断。

Kuda, what is the purification of liberation? It means that a noble disciple is liberated from desire, anger, and delusion. Such liberation, if not yet complete, causes it to be completed, and if already complete, causes it to be accepted and supported. The desire, effort, and means, up to the constant acceptance and support, are called the purification of liberation.

「苦种！云何为解脱清净断？谓圣弟子贪心无欲解脱，恚、痴心无欲解脱。如是解脱，未满者令满，已满者随顺摄受，欲精进乃至常摄受，是名解脱净断。」

Kuda, when the Venerable Ānanda spoke this teaching, the young men of the village of Bathula, having heard the Venerable Ānanda's words, rejoiced and paid homage before departing.

苦种！尊者阿难说是法时，婆头聚落诸童子闻尊者阿难所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
