SĀ 569

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 569（五六九） 黎犀达多

Parallel to: SN 41.2

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying in the mango grove of Ambapālī's grove near Rājagṛha, together with a large assembly of elder bhikṣus.

一时，佛住庵罗聚落庵罗林中，与众多上座比丘俱。

At that time, the elder Citta went to the elder bhikṣus, bowed his head at their feet, and sat to one side. The elder bhikṣus taught the Dharma to the elder Citta in various ways, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting him. Having shown, taught, illuminated, and delighted him, they remained silent.

时，质多罗长者诣诸上座比丘所，稽首礼足，于一面坐。诸上座比丘为质多罗长者种种说法，示、教、照、喜；示、教、照、喜已，默然住。

At that time, the elder Citta rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, touched his right knee to the ground, joined his palms, and invited the elders, saying, "May the Venerables please accept my humble meal." The elders silently accepted the invitation.

时，质多罗长者从座起，偏袒右肩，右膝著地，合十指掌，请诸上座言：「唯愿诸尊受我薄食。」时，诸上座默然受请。

Knowing that the elders had silently accepted his invitation, the elder bowed at their feet and left. Returning to his own home, he prepared various foods and drinks, laid out seats, and in the morning sent a messenger to announce, "The time has come."

时，彼长者知诸上座默然受请已，礼足而去。还归自家，办种种饮食，敷床座，晨朝遣使白：「时到。」

Then the elders put on their robes, took their bowls, went to the elder's house, took their seats, and the elder bowed at the elders' feet and sat to one side. He said to the elders, "What are the various realms that are spoken of?"

时，诸上座著衣持钵，至长者舍，就座而坐，长者稽首礼诸上座足，于一面坐。白诸上座：「所谓种种界，云何为种种界？」

The elders remained silent. This happened three times.

时，诸上座默然而住。如是再三。

Then Venerable Risidatta, sitting in the lower seat in the assembly, said to the elder monks, "Venerables! I wish to answer the elder's question."

尔时，尊者梨犀达多众中下坐，白诸上座比丘言：「诸尊！我欲答彼长者所问。」

The elders replied, "Yes."

诸上座答言：「可。」

The elder Jāṇussoṇi then asked, "Venerable! What are the various realms you speak of?"

长者质多罗即问言：「尊者！所谓种种界，何等种种界？」

Rāhula replied, "Elder! The eye realm is different, the form realm is different, and the eye consciousness realm is different; the ear realm is different, the sound realm is different, and the ear consciousness realm is different; the nose realm is different, the smell realm is different, and the nose consciousness realm is different; the tongue realm is different, the taste realm is different, and the tongue consciousness realm is different; the body realm is different, the touch realm is different, and the body consciousness realm is different; the mind realm is different, the dharma realm is different, and the mind consciousness realm is different. Thus, elder! These are called the various realms."

梨犀达多答言：「长者！眼界异、色界异、眼识界异；耳界异、声界异、耳识界异；鼻界异、香界异、鼻识界异；舌界异、味界异、舌识界异；身界异、触界异、身识界异；意界异、法界异、意识界异。如是，长者！是名种种界。」

At that time, the elder Jāṇussoṇi served various pure and delicious foods. After the Sangha had eaten, washed their hands and rinsed their mouths, and put away their bowls, the elder Jāṇussoṇi spread out a low seat in front of the senior monk and sat to listen to the Dharma.

尔时，质多罗长者下种种净美饮食供养。众僧食已，澡嗽、洗钵讫，质多罗长者敷一卑床，于上座前坐听法。

At that time, the senior monk spoke various teachings to the elder, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting him. Having arisen from his seat, he departed.

尔时，上座为长者种种说法，示、教、照、喜已，从座起去。

At that time, the senior monks said to Rāhula on the road, "Excellent! Excellent! Rāhula Bhikṣu! You are truly eloquent and know how to speak. If it were another time, you should always be like this.

时，诸上座于路中语梨犀达多：「善哉！善哉！梨犀达多比丘！汝真辩捷，知事而说，若于余时，汝应常如此应。」

At that time, the senior monks, having heard what Rāhula said, joyfully received and practiced it.

时，诸上座闻梨犀达多所说，欢喜奉行。
