SĀ 57

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 57（五七） 阴根

Parallel to: SN 22.81

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One put on his robe, took his alms bowl, entered the city of Śrāvastī to beg for food, returned, and without informing the assembly or his attendant, alone and without a companion, traveled through the human realms in the western lands.

尔时，世尊著衣持钵，入舍卫城乞食，还持衣钵，不语众，不告侍者，独一无二，于西方国土人间游行。

At that time, there was a bhikṣu in the Andha forest who saw from afar that the World-Honored One did not inform the assembly or his attendant, but alone and without a companion, went out for a walk. Having seen this, he approached Venerable Ānanda and said, "Venerable Ānanda, you should know that the World-Honored One did not inform the assembly or his attendant, but alone and without a companion, went out for a walk."

时，安陀林中有一比丘，遥见世尊不语众，不告侍者，独一无二。见已，进诣尊者阿难所，白阿难言：「尊者！当知世尊不语众，不告侍者，独一无二而出游行。」

At that time, Ānanda said to that bhikṣu, "If the World-Honored One does not inform the assembly or his attendant, but alone and without a companion, goes out for a walk, you should not follow him. Why is that? Today the World-Honored One wishes to dwell in the cessation of cessation for the sake of reducing afflictions."

尔时，阿难语彼比丘：「若使世尊不语众，不告侍者，独一无二而出游行，不应随从。所以者何？今日世尊欲住寂灭灭少事故。」

At that time, the World-Honored One traveled north to the country of Half-Jāṅgala, to the village of Pāṭali, and stayed under a banyan tree in a forest guarded by people. At that time, many bhikṣus went to Ānanda and said, "We have heard that the World-Honored One is staying somewhere. Where is he now?"

尔时，世尊游行北至半阇国波陀聚落，于人所守护林中，住一跋陀萨罗树下。时有众多比丘诣阿难所，语阿难言：「今闻世尊住在何所？」

Ānanda replied, "I have heard that the World-Honored One has gone north to the country of Half-Jāṅgala, to the village of Pāṭali, and is staying under a banyan tree in a forest guarded by people."

阿难答曰：「我闻世尊北至半阇国波陀聚落，人所守护林中跋陀萨罗树下。」

At that time, the bhikṣus said to Ānanda, "Venerable one, we know that we have not seen the World-Honored One for a long time. If you do not mind the trouble, please go with us to see the World-Honored One out of compassion." Ānanda knew the right time and silently agreed.

时，诸比丘语阿难曰：「尊者知我等不见世尊已久，若不惮劳者，可共往诣世尊？」哀愍故，阿难知时，默然而许。

At that time, Venerable Ānanda, together with many bhikṣus, passed the night and at dawn, put on their robes, took their alms bowls, and entered Śrāvastī to beg for food. After begging for food, they returned to the monastery, put away their bedding, took their robes and bowls, and went to wander among the people in the west. They went north to the country of Half-Jāṅgala, to the village of Pāṭali, to the forest guarded by people. At that time, Venerable Ānanda and the many bhikṣus put away their robes and bowls, washed their feet, and went to where the World-Honored One was. They bowed their heads at his feet and sat to one side.

尔时，尊者阿难与众多比丘夜过晨朝，著衣持钵，入舍卫城乞食。乞食已，还精舍，举卧具，持衣钵，出至西方人间游行，北至半阇国波陀聚落人守护林中。时，尊者阿难与众多比丘置衣钵，洗足已，诣世尊所，头面礼足，于一面坐。

At that time, the World-Honored One was teaching the Dharma to many bhikṣus, showing, teaching, benefiting, and delighting them.

尔时，世尊为众多比丘说法，示、教、利、喜。

At that time, among the assembly, a bhikṣu had this thought: "How does one know and see in order to quickly attain the exhaustion of the taints?" At that time, the World-Honored One knew the thoughts in the bhikṣu's mind and said to the bhikṣus: "If there is a bhikṣu in this assembly who has this thought: 'How does one know and see in order to quickly attain the exhaustion of the taints?' I have already taught the Dharma, saying: 'One should observe the aggregates, namely the four foundations of mindfulness, the four right efforts, the four bases of supernormal power, the five faculties, the five powers, the seven factors of awakening, and the eightfold noble path.' I have already taught such a Dharma for observing the aggregates. But now there are still good sons who are not diligent in desire, not diligent in joy, not diligent in mindfulness, not diligent in faith, and are lazy and arrogant, unable to progress and attain the exhaustion of the taints. If there are also good sons who diligently desire, diligently enjoy, diligently remember, and diligently have faith in the Dharma I have taught, observing the aggregates, they will be able to quickly attain the exhaustion of the taints. Ignorant and unlearned ordinary people see form as 'I', or see 'I' in form. This is called 'practice'. What is the cause of that practice? What is its origin? What is its arising? What is its occurrence? It arises from contact born of ignorance, and craving arises based on contact. What is the cause of that craving? What is its origin? What is its arising? What is its occurrence? That craving has feeling as its cause, feeling as its origin, feeling as its arising, and feeling as its occurrence. What is the cause of that feeling? What is its origin? What is its arising? What is its occurrence? That feeling has contact as its cause, contact as its origin, contact as its arising, and contact as its occurrence. What is the cause of that contact? What is its origin? What is its arising? What is its occurrence? That contact has the six sense bases as its cause, the six sense bases as its origin, the six sense bases as its arising, and the six sense bases as its occurrence. Those six sense bases are impermanent, conditioned, and dependently arisen. That contact, feeling, practice, and craving are also impermanent, conditioned, and dependently arisen. Those who observe in this way see form as 'I' ... do not see form as 'I', but see form as 'mine' ... do not see form as 'mine', but see form as in me ... do not see form as in me, but see 'I' in form ... do not see 'I' in form, but see feeling as 'I' ... do not see feeling as

尔时，座中有一比丘作是念：「云何知、云何见，疾得漏尽？」尔时，世尊知彼比丘心之所念，告诸比丘：「若有比丘于此座中作是念：『云何知、云何见，疾得漏尽？』者，我已说法言：『当善观察诸阴，所谓四念处、四正勤、四如意足、五根、五力、七觉分、八圣道分。』我已说如是法，观察诸阴。而今犹有善男子不勤欲作、不勤乐、不勤念、不勤信，而自慢惰，不能增进得尽诸漏。若复善男子于我所说法，观察诸阴，勤欲、勤乐、勤念、勤信，彼能疾得尽诸漏。愚痴无闻凡夫于色见是我，若见我者，是名为行。彼行何因？何集？何生？何转？无明触生爱，缘爱起彼行。彼爱何因？何集？何生？何转？彼爱受因、受集、受生、受转。彼受何因？何集？何生？何转？彼受触因、触集、触生、触转。彼触何因？何集？何生？何转？谓彼触六入处因、六入处集、六入处生、六入处转。彼六入处无常、有为、心缘起法；彼触、受、行、爱，亦无常、有为、心缘起法……如是观者，而见色是我……不见色是我，而见色是我所……不见色是我所，而见色在我……不见色在我，而见我在色……不见我在色，而见受是我……不见受是我，而见受是我所……不见受是我所，而见受在我……不见受在我，而见我在受……不见我在受，而见想是我……不见想是我，而见想是我所……不见想是我所，而见想在我……不见想在我，而见我在想……不见我在想，而见行是我……不见行是我，而见行是我所……不见行是我所，而见行在我……不见行在我，而见我在行……不见我在行，而见识是我……不见识是我，而见识是我所……不见识是我所，而见识在我……不见识在我，而见我在识……不见我在识，复作断见、坏有见。不作断见、坏有见，而不离我慢。不离我慢者，而复见我，见我者即是行。彼行何因？何集？何生？何转？如前所说，乃至我慢。作如是知、如是见者，疾得漏尽。」

After the Buddha had spoken the sūtra, the bhikṣus who heard what the Buddha had said rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
