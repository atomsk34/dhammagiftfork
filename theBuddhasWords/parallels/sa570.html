SĀ 570

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 570（五七〇） 黎犀达多

Parallel to: SN 41.3

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying in the mango grove of Ambapālī's grove near Rājagṛha, together with a large assembly of elder bhikṣus.

一时，佛住庵罗聚落庵罗林中，与众多上座比丘俱。

At that time, the elder Citta went to the elder bhikṣus, bowed his head at their feet, and sat to one side. He said to the elder bhikṣus, "The various views in the world, some say there is a self, some say there are sentient beings, some say there is life, some say there is good and bad in the world. Venerable ones, what is the basis, accumulation, arising, and turning of these different views?"

时，质多罗长者诣诸上座所，稽首礼足，退坐一面，白诸上座言：「诸世间所见，或说有我，或说众生，或说寿命，或说世间吉凶。云何？尊者！此诸异见，何本、何集、何生、何转？」

At that time, the elder bhikṣus remained silent and did not answer. They remained silent in this way three times when asked.

时，诸上座默然不答。如是三问，亦三默然。

At that time, there was a junior bhikṣu named Lishi Datou who said to the elder bhikṣus, "I wish to answer the questions of the elder."

时，有一下座比丘名梨犀达多，白诸上座言：「我欲答彼长者所问。」

The elder bhikṣus said, "Those who can answer well should answer."

诸上座言：「善能答者答。」

At that time, the elder asked Lishi Datou, "Venerable one! What is the basis, accumulation, birth, and turning of what is seen in the world?"

时，长者即问梨犀达多：「尊者！凡世间所见，何本、何集、何生、何转？」

Venerable Lishi Datou replied, "Elder! What is seen in the world, whether it is said to be the self, sentient beings, life, or the auspicious and inauspicious of the world, all these views are based on the view of the body, accumulated from the view of the body, born from the view of the body, and turn from the view of the body."

尊者梨犀达多答言：「长者！凡世间所见，或言有我，或说众生，或说寿命，或说世间吉凶，斯等诸见，一切皆以身见为本，身见集、身见生、身见转。」

He further asked, "Venerable one! What is the view of the body?"

复问：「尊者！云何为身见？」

He replied, "Elder! Ignorant and unlearned ordinary people see form as the self, form as different from the self, the self in form, or form in the self. They see feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness as the self, consciousness as different from the self, the self in consciousness, or consciousness in the self. Elder! This is called the view of the body."

答言：「长者！愚痴无闻凡夫见色是我、色异我、色中我、我中色，受、想、行、识见是我、识异我、我中识、识中我。长者！是名身见。」

He further asked, "Venerable one! How can one be free from this view of the body?"

复问：「尊者！云何得无此身见？」

He replied, "Elder! It means that the learned noble disciples do not see form as the self, do not see form as different from the self, do not see the self in form or form in the self. They do not see feeling, perception, volition, or consciousness as the self, do not see consciousness as different from the self, do not see the self in consciousness or consciousness in the self. This is called being free from the view of the body."

答言：「长者！谓多闻圣弟子不见色是我，不见色异我，不见我中色、色中我；不见受、想、行、识是我，不见识异我，不见我中识、识中我，是名得无身见。」

He further asked, "Venerable one! What is the name of your father? Where were you born?"

复问：「尊者！其父何名？于何所生？」

He replied, "Elder! I was born into a wealthy merchant's family in the hinterlands."

答言：「长者！我生于后方长者家。」

The elder Jīvaka said to Venerable Rāhula, "Our two fathers were originally good friends."

质多罗长者语尊者梨犀达多：「我及尊者二父本是善知识。」

Rāhula replied, "So it is, elder!"

梨犀达多答言：「如是，长者！」

The elder Jīvaka said to Rāhula, "Venerable one, if you can stay in this mango grove, I will provide you with robes, food and drink, and medicine for illness for as long as I live." Rāhula silently accepted the invitation.

质多罗长者语梨犀达多言：「尊者若能住此庵罗林中，我尽形寿供养衣服、饮食、随病汤药，尊者梨犀达多默然受请。」

At that time, Venerable Rāhula accepted the invitation of the elder Jīvaka and was hindered by the offerings. He did not go to see the World-Honored One for a long time.

时，尊者梨犀达多受质多罗长者请，供养障碍故，久不诣世尊所。

At that time, the elder bhikkhus taught the elder Jīvaka in various ways, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting him. Having shown, taught, illuminated, and delighted him, the elder Jīvaka was delighted and delighted in return, paid homage, and departed.

时，诸上座比丘为质多罗长者种种说法，示、教、照、喜；示、教、照、喜已，质多罗长者欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
