SĀ 571

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 571（五七一） 摩诃迦

Parallel to: SN 41.4

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying in the mango grove of Ambapālī's grove near Rājagṛha, together with a large assembly of elder bhikṣus.

一时，佛住庵罗聚落庵罗林中，与众多上座比丘俱。

At that time, the elder Citta went to the elder bhikṣus, bowed his head at their feet, and sat to one side. He said to the elder bhikṣus, "May the Venerables accept my invitation to a meal in the cow pasture."

时，有质多罗长者诣诸上座所，稽首礼足，退坐一面，白诸上座比丘言：「唯愿诸尊于牛牧中受我请食。」

The elder bhikṣus silently accepted the invitation.

时，诸上座默然受请。

The elder Citta, knowing that the elder bhikṣus had silently accepted the invitation, returned home. He prepared various kinds of food and drink throughout the night, and in the morning, he set out seats. He sent a messenger to inform the elder bhikṣus, "The time has come."

质多罗长者知诸上座默然受请已，即] cf.【丽】自还家。星夜备具种种饮食，晨朝敷座，遣使白诸上座：「时到。」

The elder bhikṣus put on their robes and took their almsbowls, and went to the house of the elder Citta in the cow pasture. They took their seats.

诸上座著衣持钵，至牛牧中质多罗长者舍，就座而坐。

At that time, the elder Citta personally served various kinds of food and drink. After they had finished eating, washed their bowls, and rinsed their mouths, the elder Citta spread out a low seat in front of the elder bhikṣus and sat down to listen to the Dharma.

时，质多罗长者自手供养种种饮食。食已，洗钵、澡漱毕，质多罗长者敷一卑床，于上座前坐听法。

At that time, the elder bhikṣus spoke various kinds of Dharma to the elder, teaching, illuminating, and delighting him. Having taught, illuminated, and delighted him, they rose from their seats and departed. The elder Citta also followed behind them.

时，诸上座为长者说种种法，示、教、照、喜；示、教、照、喜已，从座起去，质多罗长者亦随后去。

The elder bhikṣus had eaten ghee, curds, and honey and were full. In the hot season of the later spring months, they were exhausted from walking on the road.

诸上座食诸酥酪蜜饱满，于春后月热时，行路闷极。

At that time, a junior bhikṣu named Mahākā said to the elder bhikṣus, "It is very hot today. May I create clouds, rain, and a gentle breeze?"

尔时，有一下座比丘名摩诃迦，白诸上座：「今日大热，我欲起云雨微风，可尔不？」

The elder bhikṣus replied, "If you can do that, it would be excellent."

诸上座答言：「汝能尔者，佳。」

Then Mahākā entered samādhi as he wished. Immediately clouds gathered, a fine rain fell, and a gentle breeze came from the four directions to the gate of the monastery. Venerable Mahākā said to the elder bhikṣus, "May the work be stopped?"

时，摩诃迦即入三昧，如其正受。应时云起，细雨微下，凉风亹亹从四方来，至精舍门，尊者摩诃迦语诸上座言：「所作可止？」

They replied, "May it be stopped."

答言：「可止。」

Then Venerable Mahākā stopped his supernatural power and returned to his own room.

时，尊者摩诃迦即止神通，还于自房。

At that time, the elder Jīvaka thought: "The lowest-ranking monk can have such great supernatural powers, let alone the middle-ranking and highest-ranking monks." He then bowed at the feet of the highest-ranking monks, followed Venerable Mahākā to his dwelling place, bowed at Venerable Mahākā's feet, sat to one side, and said, "Venerable! I wish to see the venerable's superhuman Dharma and supernatural transformations.

时，质多罗长者作是念：「最下座比丘而能有此大神通力，况复中座及与上座。」即礼诸上座比丘足，随摩诃迦比丘至所住房，礼尊者摩诃迦足，退坐一面，白言：「尊者！我欲得见尊者过人法神足现化。」

Venerable Mahākā said, "Elder! Do not be afraid.

尊者摩诃迦言：「长者！勿见恐怖。」

He made this request three times, but was refused three times. The elder then made a repeated request, wishing to see the venerable's supernatural transformations.

如是三请，亦三不许。长者由复重请愿见尊者神通变化。

Venerable Mahākā said to the elder, "You should go outside, gather dry grass and wood, and then cover it with a sheet of cloth.

尊者摩诃迦语长者言：「汝且出外，取干草木积聚已，以一张［叠＊毛］复上。」

The elder Jīvaka immediately did as he was instructed, gathered the firewood, and came to inform Venerable Mahākā, "The firewood is gathered and covered with a sheet of cloth.

质多罗长者即如其教，出外聚薪成［卄／积］，来白尊者摩诃迦：「薪［卄／积］已成，以［叠＊毛］复上。」

At that time, Venerable Mahākā entered the firelight samādhi, and flames came out from the keyhole, burning the firewood completely, but the white cloth was not burned. He said to the elder, "Do you see this now?"

时，尊者摩诃迦即入火光三昧，于户钩孔中出火焰光，烧其积薪都尽，唯白［叠＊毛］不然，语长者言：「汝今见不？」

He replied, "I see. Venerable! It is truly extraordinary."

答言：「已见。尊者！实为奇特。」

Venerable Mahākā said to the elder, "Know that this is all based on non-distraction, accumulated from non-distraction, born from non-distraction, transformed by non-distraction. Because of non-distraction, one attains anuttara-samyak-saṃbodhi. Therefore, elder! This and other merits are all based on non-distraction, accumulated from non-distraction, born from non-distraction, transformed by non-distraction. Because of non-distraction, one attains anuttara-samyak-saṃbodhi and other dharmas of the path."

尊者摩诃迦语长者言：「当知此者皆以不放逸为本，不放逸集、不放逸生、不放逸转，不放逸故得阿耨多罗三藐三菩提。是故，长者！此及余功德，一切皆以不放逸为本，不放逸集、不放逸生、不放逸转，不放逸故得阿耨多罗三藐三菩提，及余道品法。」

The elder Jīvaka said to the elder, "I wish to invite the Venerable to stay in this forest. I will provide clothing, food and drink, and medicine for illness for the rest of my life."

质多罗长者白尊者摩诃迦：「愿常住此林中，我当尽寿衣、被、饮食、随病汤药。」

Venerable Mahākā had a reason for not accepting the invitation.

尊者摩诃迦有行因缘故，不受其请。

The elder Jīvaka, having heard the Dharma, rejoiced and followed the teaching. He then rose from his seat, paid homage, and departed.

质多罗长者闻说法已，欢喜随喜，即从座起，作礼而去。

Venerable Mahākā did not want the offerings and benefits to obstruct his karmic retribution, so he rose from his seat and left, never returning.

尊者摩诃迦不欲令供养利障罪故，即从座起去，遂不复还。
