SĀ 573

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 573（五七三） 阿鲁毗迦

Parallel to: SN 41.9

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying in the mango grove. At that time, there was an Ājīvaka non-Buddhist who was a close friend of the elder Jīvaka's family. He came to visit Jīvaka and exchanged greetings and pleasantries. He then stood to one side,

一时，佛住庵罗林中。时，有阿耆毘外道是质多罗长者先人亲厚，来诣质多罗长者所，共相问讯慰劳已，于一面住。

Jīvaka asked the Ājīvaka non-Buddhist, "How long have you been a renunciant?"

质多罗长者问阿耆毘外道：「汝出家几时？」

He replied, "Elder, it has been over twenty years since I became a renunciant."

答言：「长者！我出家已来二十余年。」

Jīvaka asked, "After over twenty years of being a renunciant, have you attained the Dharma beyond the human realm, realized the truth, and attained peace and happiness?"

质多罗长者问言：「汝出家来过二十年，为得过人法，究竟知见，安乐住不？」

He replied, "Elder, although I have been a renunciant for over twenty years, I have not attained the Dharma beyond the human realm, realized the truth, and attained peace and happiness. I only wander naked, pulling out my hair, begging for food, and sleeping on the ground."

答言：「长者！虽出家过二十年，不得过人法，究竟知见，安乐住，唯有躶形、拔发、乞食，人间游行，卧于土中。」

Jīvaka said, "This is not the Dharma and Vinaya of the Buddha. This is a bad understanding, not the path of renunciation, not the realization of equality, not a place of praise, not a basis for reliance. It is meaningless to be a renunciant for over twenty years, wandering naked, pulling out hair, begging for food, wandering among humans, and sleeping in ashes and dirt."

质多罗长者言：「此非名称法、律，此是恶知，非出要道，非曰等觉，非赞叹处，不可依止。唐名出家过二十年，躶形、拔发、乞食，人间游行，卧灰土中。」

Ājīvaka asked Jīvaka, "How long have you been a disciple of the śramaṇa Gautama?"

阿耆毘问质多罗长者：「汝为沙门瞿昙作弟子，于今几时？」

Jīvaka replied, "It has been over twenty years since I became a disciple of the World-Honored One."

质多罗长者答言：「我为世尊弟子过二十年。」

He further asked Jīvaka, "Having been a disciple of the śramaṇa Gautama for over twenty years, have you attained any superhuman qualities, any supreme and ultimate knowledge and insight?"

复问质多罗长者：「汝为沙门瞿昙弟子过二十年，复得过人法，胜、究竟知见不？」

Jīvaka replied, "You should know that Jīvaka will no longer be reborn through the womb, will not increase in the charnel ground, and will not arise from blood and breath. As the World-Honored One has said, I do not see a single lower fetter that is not severed. If a single fetter is not severed, one will be reborn in this world again."

质多罗长者答言：「汝今当知，质多罗长者要不复经由胞胎而受生，不复增于丘塜，不复起于血气，如世尊所说五下分结，不见一结而不断者，若一结不断，当复还生此世。」

When this was said, Aṅgulimāla lamented and wept, wiping his face with his robe. He said to Jīvaka, "What should I do now?"

如是说时，阿耆毘迦悲叹涕泪，以衣拭面，谓质多罗长者言：「我今当作何计？」

Jīvaka replied, "If you can go forth in the true Dharma and Vinaya, I will provide you with robes, alms bowl, and necessities for the body."

质多罗长者答言：「汝若能于正法、律出家者，我当给汝衣钵供身之具。」

Aṅgulimāla pondered for a moment and said to Jīvaka, "I now rejoice. Show me what to do."

阿耆毘迦须臾思惟已，语质多罗长者言：「我今随喜，示我所作。」

At that time, Jīvaka took Aṅgulimāla to the elders, bowed at the feet of the elders, and sat to one side. He said to the elder monks, "Venerables! This Aṅgulimāla is a close friend of my ancestors. He now seeks to go forth as a monk. I hope the elders will ordain him. I will provide him with robes, alms bowl, and necessities."

时，质多罗长者将彼阿耆毘迦往诣诸上座所，礼诸上座足，于一面坐。白诸上座比丘言：「尊者！此阿耆毘迦是我先人亲厚，今求出家作比丘，愿诸上座度令出家，我当供给衣钵众具。」

The elders then ordained him, shaved his head and beard, and had him put on the saffron robes. After going forth, he thought, "The reason good sons shave their heads and beards, put on saffron robes, go forth, progress in learning the path, and cultivate pure conduct..." and attained arahantship.

诸上座即令出家，剃除须发，著袈裟衣。出家已，思惟：「所以善男子剃除须发，著袈裟衣，出家增进学道，净修梵行……。」得阿罗汉。
