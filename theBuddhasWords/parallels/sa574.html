SĀ 574

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 574（五七四） 尼干

Parallel to: SN 41.8

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying in the mango grove of Ambapālī's grove, together with a group of senior bhikṣus.

一时，佛住庵罗聚落庵罗林中，与诸上座比丘俱。

At that time, a Nirgrantha Jñātiputra, together with five hundred followers, went to the mango grove, intending to convert the elder Citta as his disciple. When the elder Citta heard that the Nirgrantha Jñātiputra, together with five hundred followers, was coming to the mango grove, intending to convert him as his disciple, he went to where he was. After exchanging greetings, they sat down to one side.

时，有尼犍若提子与五百眷属诣庵罗林中，欲诱质多罗长者以为弟子。质多罗长者闻尼犍若提子将五百眷属来诣庵罗林中，欲诱我为弟子，闻已，即往诣其所，共相问讯毕，各于一面坐。

At that time, the Nirgrantha Jñātiputra said to the elder Citta, "Do you believe that the śramaṇa Gautama has attained the samādhi of no perception and no discrimination?"

时，尼犍若提子语质多罗长者言：「汝信沙门瞿昙得无觉无观三昧耶？」

The elder Citta replied, "I did not come here out of faith."

质多罗长者答言：「我不以信故来也。」

Ājīvaka said, "Elder, you are not deceitful, not illusory, straightforward, and born of straightforwardness. Elder, if one can stop having perception and discrimination, one can also bind the wind with a rope. If one can stop having perception and discrimination, one can also stop the flow of the Ganges River with a handful of earth. I have constant wisdom and insight in walking, standing, sitting, and lying down."

阿耆毘言：「长者！汝不谄、不幻、质直、质直所生。长者！若能息有觉有观者，亦能以绳系缚于风，若能息有觉有观者，亦可以一把土断恒水流，我于行、住、坐、卧智见常生。」

The elder Citta asked Nirgrantha Jñātiputra, "Do you believe that faith comes first or wisdom comes first? Which comes first, faith or wisdom? Which is superior?"

质多罗长者问尼犍若提子：「为信在前耶？为智在前耶？信之与智，何者为先？何者为胜？」

Nirgrantha Jñātiputra replied, "Faith should come first, then wisdom. Comparing faith and wisdom, wisdom is superior."

尼犍若提子答言：「信应在前，然后有智，信智相比，智则为胜。」

The elder Citta said to Nirgrantha Jñātiputra, "I have already attained the cessation of perception and feeling, the inner purity of single-minded concentration, the birth of joy and bliss without perception and feeling, and the complete abiding in the second dhyāna. I abide in this samādhi during the day and also at night. I always abide in this samādhi throughout the night. With such wisdom, why do I need to believe in the World-Honored One?"

质多罗长者语尼犍若提子：「我已求得息有觉有观，内净一心，无觉无观，三昧生喜乐，第二禅具足住。我昼亦住此三昧，夜亦住此三昧，终夜常住此三昧，有如是智，何用信世尊为？」

Nirgrantha Jñātiputra said, "You are deceitful, deceptive, crooked, and born from crookedness."

尼犍若提子言：「汝谄曲、幻伪、不直、不直所生。」

The elder Citta said, "You previously said that I am not deceitful, not deceptive, straightforward, and born from straightforwardness. Now you say I am deceitful, deceptive, crooked, and born from crookedness. If your previous statement is true, then your later statement is false. If your later statement is true, then your previous statement is false. You previously said that you know and see constantly while walking, standing, sitting, and lying down. You do not know even small matters before and after. How can you know and see the affairs of the path of peace and happiness?" The elder further asked Nirgrantha Jñātiputra, "Do you have one question, one statement, and one record of discussion, up to ten questions, ten statements, and ten records of discussion? If you do not have one question, one statement, and one record of discussion, up to ten questions, ten statements, and ten records of discussion, how can you lead me and come to this mango grove to deceive me?"

质多罗长者言：「汝先言我不谄曲、不幻、质直、质直所生，今云何言谄曲、幻伪、不直、不直所生耶？若汝前实者，后则虚，后实者，前则虚。汝先言：『我于行、住、坐、卧知见常生。』汝于前后，小事不知。云何知过人法？若知、若见、安乐住事？」长者复问尼犍若提子：「有于一问、一说、一记论，乃至十问、十说、十记论，汝有此不？若无一问、一说、一记论，乃至十问、十说、十记论。云何能诱于我，而来至此庵罗林中欲诱诳我？」

Then Nirgrantha Jñātiputra closed his eyes, turned his head, and left with his arms folded, not looking back.

于是尼犍若提子息闭掉头，反拱而出，不复还顾。
