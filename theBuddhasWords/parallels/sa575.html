SĀ 575

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 575（五七五） 病相

Parallel to: SN 41.10

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying in the mango grove of Ambapālī's grove near Rājagṛha, together with a large assembly of elder bhikṣus.

一时，佛住庵罗聚落庵罗林中，与众多上座比丘俱。

At that time, the elder Citta was suffering from illness, surrounded by his relatives. Many devas came to the elder and said to him, "Elder, you should make a vow to become a wheel-turning king."

尔时，质多罗长者病苦，诸亲围遶，有众多诸天来诣长者所，语质多罗长者言：「长者！汝当发愿得作转轮王。」

The elder Citta said to the devas, "Even if one becomes a wheel-turning king, it is also impermanent, suffering, empty, and without self."

质多罗长者语诸天言：「若作转轮王，彼亦无常、苦、空、无我。」

At that time, the elder's relatives said to him, "You should focus your attention. You should focus your attention."

时，长者亲属语长者：「汝当系念。汝当系念。」

The elder Citta said to his relatives, "Why do you tell me to focus my attention? To focus on what?"

质多罗长者语亲属：「何故汝等教我系念，系念？」

His relatives said, "You said, 'impermanent, suffering, empty, and selfless.' That's why we tell you to focus your attention, to focus your attention."

彼亲属言：「汝作是言：『无常、苦、空、无我。』是故教汝系念、系念也。」

The elder said to his relatives, "There were heavenly beings who came to me and said, 'You should make a vow to become a wheel-turning sage king, and you will attain the fruit of your vow.' I then replied, 'That wheel-turning king is also impermanent, suffering, empty, and selfless.'"

长者语诸亲属：「有诸天人来至我所，语我言：『汝当发愿得作转轮圣王，随愿得果。』我即答言：『彼转轮王亦复无常、苦、空、非我。』」

His relatives said to the elder Jālinīprabha, "What is there about the wheel-turning king that those heavenly beings are urging you to seek?"

彼诸亲属语质多罗长者：「转轮王有何，而彼诸天教汝愿求？」

The elder replied, "The wheel-turning king rules with the right Dharma. That is why the heavenly beings, seeing such benefits and blessings, come to urge me to make a vow."

长者答言：「转轮王者以正法治化，是故诸天见如是福利故，而来教我为发愿求。」

His relatives said, "What will you do now?"

诸亲属言：「汝今用心，当如之何？」

The elder replied, "Relatives! I now make this resolve: I will no longer see birth through the womb, I will not increase the mounds of earth, I will not receive blood and breath. As the World-Honored One said, I do not see the five lower fetters. I do not see a single fetter that is not severed. If a fetter is not severed, one will be reborn in this world."

长者答言：「诸亲属！我今作心，唯不复见胞胎受生，不增丘塜，不受血气，如世尊说，五下分结我不见有，我不自见一结不断，若结不断，则还生此世。」

Then the elder rose from his bed, sat cross-legged, with right mindfulness in front, and spoke a verse:

于是长者即从床起，结加趺坐，正念在前，而说偈言：

By accumulating food and drink, widely crossing over difficulties, giving
to the supreme field of merit, planting these five powers
With this meaning and desire, a layperson dwelling in a
household, I have attained all these benefits, already escaped from
difficulties. What is heard and practiced in the world, far
from difficult matters, it is difficult to know the slight
happiness, following the Perfectly Enlightened One. Making offerings to those
who uphold precepts, well cultivating various holy life, arhats with
exhausted taints, and the sage śrāvakas. Transcending such views, in
the superior realms, always practicing the giving of a person
ultimately attaining great fruits. Practicing many kinds of giving, giving
to various excellent fields of merit, at the end of
this life, reborn in the heavens. With the five desires
fully complete, immeasurable mental joy and pleasure, attaining this wonderful
fruit, due to being free from stinginess. Wherever one is
reborn, one is never unhappy.

「服食积所积，　　广度于众难，施上进福田，　　殖斯五种力。以斯义所欲，　　俗人处于家，我悉得此利，　　已免于众难。世间所闻习，　　远离众难事，生乐知稍难，　　随顺等正觉。供养持戒者，　　善修诸梵行，漏尽阿罗汉，　　及声闻牟尼。如是超越见，　　于上诸胜处，常行士夫施，　　克终获大果。习行众多施，　　施诸良福田，于此世命终，　　化生于天上。五欲具足满，　　无量心悦乐，获斯妙果报，　　以无悭悋故。在所处受生，　　未曾不欢喜。」

Having spoken this verse, the elder Citta passed away and was reborn in the heaven of the non-afflicted.

质多罗长者说此偈已，寻即命终，生于不烦热天。

At that time, the Citta Devaputra thought: "I should not stay here, but go to Jambudvīpa to pay homage to the elder bhikṣus." In the time it takes for a strong man to bend and stretch his arm, he used his divine power to arrive in the mango grove, emitting the heavenly light of his body, illuminating the mango grove.

尔时，质多罗天子作是念：「我不应停此，当往阎浮提礼拜诸上座比丘。」如力士屈伸臂顷，以天神力至庵罗林中，放身天光，遍照庵罗林。

At that time, a certain bhikṣu got up in the night, left his room, and walked in the open air. Seeing the superior light illuminating the grove, he spoke a verse:

时，有异比丘夜起出房，露地经行，见胜光明普照树林。即说偈言：

Who is this wondrous celestial form, dwelling in the sky, like
a pure golden mountain, with the pure light of the Jambūnada?

「是谁妙天色，　　住于虚空中，譬如纯金山，　　阎浮檀净光。」

The god Citraratha spoke a verse in reply:

质多罗天子说偈答言：

I am the king of gods and humans, the son
of the renowned Gautama. In this mango grove, I am
the elder Citraratha. With perfect discipline, I focus my mind
in tranquility. Liberated in body, and likewise in wisdom, I
come knowing the Dharma. You should know, the noble one
that I will attain nirvana here. This is the way
of the Dharma.

「我是天人王，　　瞿昙名称子，是庵罗林中，　　质多罗长者，以净戒具足，　　系念自寂静，解脱身具足，　　智慧身亦然，我知法故来，　　仁者应当知，当于彼涅槃，　　此法法如是。」

Having spoken this verse, the god Citraratha disappeared.

质多罗天子说此偈已，即没不现。
