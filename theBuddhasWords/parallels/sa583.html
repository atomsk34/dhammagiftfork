SĀ 583

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 583（五八三） 月天子

Parallel to: SN 2.9 / SĀ² 2.167

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the asura king Rāhu was obstructing the moon deity. The moon deities were all terrified and came to where the Buddha was. They bowed their heads at the Buddha’s feet and withdrew to one side. They praised the Buddha in verse:

尔时，罗睺罗阿修罗王障月天子。时，诸月天子悉皆恐怖，来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退住一面。说偈叹佛：

Now I pay homage to the supreme and awakened one, who can
free from all obstacles. I am now suffering and distressed, so I
come to take refuge. We moon deities take refuge in the Well-Gone
One. The Buddha has compassion for the world. Please free us from the asuras.

「今礼最胜觉，　　能脱一切障，我今遭苦恼，　　是故来归依。我等月天子，　　归依于善逝，佛哀愍世间，　　愿解阿修罗。」

At that time, the World-Honored One replied in verse:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

Destroying all darkness, the light shines in the sky
Now Vairocana's pure light shines. Rahu avoids the sky
quickly releasing the rabbit-like image. Rahu, the asura, immediately
abandons the moon and returns. The whole body is
stained, trembling in fear, not at ease. The spirit
is confused and deluded, like a seriously ill person.

「破坏诸暗冥，　　光明照虚空，今毘卢遮那，　　清净光明显。罗睺避虚空，　　速放飞兔像，罗睺阿修罗，　　即舍月而还。举体悉流污，　　战怖不自安，神昬志迷乱，　　犹如重病人。」

At that time, there was an asura named Vajra. Seeing Rahu Asura quickly abandon the moon and return, he spoke a verse:

时，有阿修罗名曰婆稚，见罗睺罗阿修罗疾舍月还，便说偈言：

Rahu Asura, abandoning the moon so quickly, the divine
body is all stained, like a seriously ill person.

「罗睺阿修罗，　　舍月一何速，神体悉流污，　　犹如重病人。」

Rahu Asura spoke a verse in reply:

罗睺阿修罗说偈答言：

Gautama, if you speak a spell and do not quickly release the moon, your
head will break into seven pieces and you will suffer the pain of death.

「瞿昙说呪偈，　　不速舍月者，或头破七分，　　受诸隣死苦。」

The Asura Pārijāta also spoke a verse:

婆稚阿修罗复说偈言：

The Buddha has arisen, unprecedented, bringing peace to the world. He
speaks a spell that can cause Rāhu to release the moon.

「佛兴未曾有，　　安隐于世间，说呪偈能令，　　罗睺罗舍月。」

Having spoken this sūtra, at that time the Moon Celestial King, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully rejoiced, paid homage, and departed.

佛说此经已，时月天子闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
