SĀ 590

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 590（五九〇） 古客

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "In the past, in the country of Kosala, there were merchants who traveled together in five hundred carts to do business. They reached a wilderness, and five hundred bandits followed behind, waiting for an opportunity to rob them. At that time, there was a god who lived by the side of the road in the wilderness.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「过去世时，拘萨罗国有诸商人，五百乘车，共行治生，至旷野中。旷野有五百群贼在后随逐，伺便欲作劫盗。时，旷野中有一天神，止住路侧。

At that time the god thought, 'I should go to those merchants from the country of Kosala and ask them about the meaning. If those merchants are pleased with my questions and explain them, I will find a way to ensure their safety and help them escape the danger of bandits; if they are not pleased with my questions, I will let them go, like other gods.'

「时，彼天神作是念：『当往诣彼拘萨罗国诸商人所，问其义理。若彼商人喜我所问，时解说者，我当方便令其安隐，得脱贼难；若不喜我所问者，当放舍之，如余天神。』

Having thought this, the god immediately emitted a light from his body, illuminating the merchants' camp, and spoke a verse:

「时，彼天神作是念已，即放身光，遍照商人车营，而说偈言：

Who is awake while others sleep, and who is asleep while others
are awake? Who understands this meaning, and who can explain it to me?

「『谁于觉睡眠，　　谁复睡眠觉，谁有解此义，　　谁能为我说。』

At that time, among the merchants there was an upāsaka who believed in the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṃgha. He single-mindedly turned towards the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṃgha, took refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṃgha, was free from doubts about the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṃgha, was free from doubts about suffering, its origin, cessation, and the path, saw the four noble truths, attained the first fruit of non-return, and was traveling with the merchants. That upāsaka, in the latter part of the night, sat upright in meditation, focused his mind in front, and observed the twelve links of dependent origination in reverse and forward, namely: 'Because this exists, that exists; because this arises, that arises.' That is to say, dependent on ignorance are volitional formations, dependent on volitional formations is consciousness, dependent on consciousness is name-and-form, dependent on name-and-form are the six sense bases, dependent on the six sense bases is contact, dependent on contact is feeling, dependent on feeling is craving, dependent on craving is grasping, dependent on grasping is becoming, dependent on becoming is birth, dependent on birth are old age, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, distress, and anguish. Thus there is an accumulation of this great mass of suffering. Thus, with the cessation of ignorance, volitional formations cease; with the cessation of volitional formations, consciousness ceases; with the cessation of consciousness, name-and-form ceases; with the cessation of name-and-form, the six sense bases cease; with the cessation of the six sense bases, contact ceases; with the cessation of contact, feeling ceases; with the cessation of feeling, craving ceases; with the cessation of craving, grasping ceases; with the cessation of grasping, becoming ceases; with the cessation of becoming, birth ceases; with the cessation of birth, old age, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, distress, and anguish cease. Thus there is the cessation of this great mass of suffering.

「尔时，商人中有一优婆塞信佛、信法、信比丘僧，一心向佛、法、僧，归依佛、法、僧，于佛离疑，于法、僧离疑，于苦、集、灭、道离疑，见四圣谛得第一无间等果，在商人中与诸商人共为行侣。彼优婆塞于后夜时端坐思惟，系念在前，于十二因缘逆顺观察，所谓是事有故是事有，是事起故是事起。谓缘无明行，缘行识，缘识名色，缘名色六入处，缘六入处触，缘触受，缘受爱，缘爱取，缘取有，缘有生，缘生老、死、忧、悲、恼、苦。如是纯大苦聚集；如是无明灭则行灭，行灭则识灭，识灭则名色灭，名色灭则六入处灭，六入处灭则触灭，触灭则受灭，受灭则爱灭，爱灭则取灭，取灭则有灭，有灭则生灭，生灭则老、死、忧、悲、恼、苦灭，如是如是纯大苦聚灭。

At that time, the upāsaka, having contemplated in this way, spoke a verse, saying:

「时，彼优婆塞如是思惟已，而说偈言：

I am awake from sleep, I am awake from sleep
I understand this meaning, I can explain it to others.

「『我于觉睡眠，　　我于睡眠觉，我解知此义，　　能为人记说。』

At that time, the god asked the upāsaka: 'How are you awake from sleep? How are you awake from sleep? How do you understand? How can you explain?'

「时，彼天神问优婆塞：『云何觉睡眠？云何睡眠觉？云何能解知？云何能记说？』

At that time, the upāsaka spoke a verse in reply:

「时，优婆塞说偈答言：

Those who have abandoned desire, anger, and delusion, who have exhausted
the taints and are arhats, who are liberated with right knowledge
and right wisdom, they are awake, while I am asleep. I
do not know the cause of suffering, nor the origin of
suffering. I have not attained the complete cessation of all suffering
nor do I know the right path leading to the cessation
of suffering. They are always asleep, while I am awake. Thus
I am awake from sleep, thus I am asleep from sleep
thus I know the meaning well, thus I am able to explain it.

「『贪欲及瞋恚，　　愚痴得离欲，漏尽阿罗汉，　　正智心解脱，彼则为觉悟，　　我于彼睡眠，不知因生苦，　　及苦因缘集，于此一切苦，　　得无余灭尽，又不知正道，　　等趣息苦处，斯等为常眠，　　我于彼则觉，如是觉睡眠，　　如是睡眠觉，如是善知义，　　如是能记说。』

At that time, the god again spoke a verse:

「时，彼天神复说偈言：

Excellent is the one who is awake from sleep, excellent is the
one who is asleep from sleep, excellent is the one who understands
the meaning, excellent is the one who can explain it. After a
long time, I now see my brothers come. It is due to
your kindness that the merchants have escaped the robbers and gone safely
on their way.

「『善哉觉睡眠，　　善哉眠中觉，善哉解知义，　　善哉能记说，久远乃今见，　　诸兄弟而来，缘汝恩力故，　　令诸商人众，得免于劫贼，　　随道安乐去。』

"Thus, monks, all the merchants in the Kasi wilderness were able to safely emerge from the wilderness."

「如是，诸比丘！彼拘萨罗泽中诸商人众皆得安隐从旷野出。」

Having spoken this sutra, the Buddha finished. The monks, having heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
