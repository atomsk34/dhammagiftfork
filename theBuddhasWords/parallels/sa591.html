SĀ 591

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 591（五九 输波罗一）

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "In the past, a householder from the island in the ocean went to the house of another householder for a gathering. He strongly criticized desire, saying, 'This desire is false and unreal, a deceptive method, like an illusion deceiving infants.' When he returned to his own house, he indulged in the five desires. There was a deity dwelling in that householder's house. At that time, that deity thought, 'This householder is not superior or comparable. In the gathering of other householders, he strongly criticized desire, saying, "Such desire is false and unreal, a deceptive method, like deceiving infants." After returning to his own house, he indulges in the five desires. I should now arouse him to awaken.' And spoke a verse:

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「过去世时，海洲上优婆塞至他优婆塞舍会坐，极毁呰欲，言：『此欲者，虚妄不实，欺诳之法，犹如幻化，诳于婴儿。』还自己舍，恣于五欲，是优婆塞舍有天神止住。时，彼天神作是念：『是优婆塞不胜不类，于余优婆塞舍会坐众中极毁呰欲，言：「如是欲者，虚伪不实，欺诳之法，如诳婴儿。」还己舍已，自恣五欲，我今宁可发令觉悟。』而说偈言：

In a great gathering, criticizing desire as impermanent, you yourself
sink into desire, like an ox mired in deep mud
I observe in that gathering the householders, who are learned
and understand the Dharma, upholding pure precepts. You see them
delighting in the Dharma, yet you speak of desire as
impermanent. How can you indulge in desire, not cutting off
craving and attachment? Why do you delight in the world
keeping wives, children, and relatives?

「『于大聚会中，　　毁呰欲无常，自没于爱欲，　　如牛溺深泥。我观彼会中，　　诸优婆塞等，多闻明解法，　　奉持于净戒。汝见彼乐法，　　而说欲无常，如何自恣欲，　　不断于贪爱，何故乐世间，　　畜妻子眷属。』

At that time, the deity awakened the householder in this way. After the householder was awakened in this way, he shaved off his hair and beard, put on the saffron robes, left home out of true faith, and went forth to practice the path. He diligently practiced and exhausted all taints, attaining arahantship.

「时，彼天神如是如是开觉彼优婆塞已。如是如是彼优婆塞觉悟已，剃除须发，著袈裟衣，正信非家，出家学道，精勤修习，尽诸有漏，得阿罗汉。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished what he had to say. The bhikṣus, having heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
