SĀ 592

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 592（五九二） 须达

Parallel to: SN 10.8 / MĀ 28 (~) / SĀ² 2.186

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying in the cold forest near the charnel ground in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城寒林中丘冢间。

At that time, the elder Anāthapiṇḍada had a small matter to attend to in Rājagṛha, and stayed overnight at the elder's house. At night, he saw the elder instructing his wife, children, servants, and workers, saying: "You all get up, break firewood to light a fire, cook rice and make pastries, prepare various flavors, and decorate the hall.

时，给孤独长者有小因缘至王舍城，止宿长者舍。夜见长者告其妻子、仆使、作人言：「汝等皆起，破樵然火，炊饭作饼调和众味，庄严堂舍。」

The elder Anāthapiṇḍada, seeing this, thought: "What is this elder doing? Is he marrying off a daughter or taking a wife? Is he inviting guests, the king, or ministers?" Having thought this, he then asked the elder: "What are you doing? Are you marrying off a daughter or taking a wife? Are you inviting guests, the king, or ministers?"

给孤独长者见已，作是念：「今此长者何所为作，为嫁女娶妇耶？为请宾客、国王、大臣耶？」念已，即问长者：「汝何所作？为嫁女娶妇？为请宾客、国王、大臣耶？」

At that time, the elder replied to the elder Anāthapiṇḍada, saying: "I am not marrying off a daughter or taking a wife, nor am I inviting the king or ministers. I only wish to invite the Buddha and the bhikṣus to make offerings."

时，彼长者答给孤独长者言：「我不嫁女娶妇，亦不请呼国王、大臣，唯欲请佛及比丘僧，设供养耳。」

At that time, the elder Anāthapiṇḍada, having heard the name of the Buddha for the first time, was greatly delighted in his heart, and all the pores of his body were filled with joy. He asked the elder: "What is the name of the Buddha?"

时，给孤独长者闻未曾闻佛名字已，心大欢喜，身诸毛孔皆悉怡悦，问彼长者言：「何名为佛？」

The elder replied: "There is a śramaṇa named Gautama, a son of the Śākya clan. He shaved his beard and hair, put on the kāṣāya robe, and with right faith left home to study the path. He attained anuttarā-samyak-saṃbodhi. This is called the Buddha."

长者答言：「有沙门瞿昙，是释种子，于释种中剃除须发，著袈裟衣，正信非家，出家学道，得阿耨多罗三藐三菩提，是名为佛。」

The elder Anāthapiṇḍada said: "What is called the Saṃgha?"

给孤独长者言：「云何名僧？」

The elder said: "If a Brahmin shaves his beard and hair, puts on the kāṣāya robe, with right faith leaves home for the non-home, and follows the Buddha in leaving home; or if a Kṣatriya, Vaiśya, or Śūdra good man shaves his beard and hair, puts on the kāṣāya robe, with right faith leaves home for the non-home, and follows the Buddha in leaving home, this is called the Saṃgha. Today I invite the Buddha and the Saṃgha present to make offerings."

彼长者言：「若婆罗门种剃除须发，著袈裟衣，信家非家，而随佛出家；或刹利种、毘舍种、首陀罗种善男子等剃除须发，著袈裟衣，正信非家，彼佛出家而随出家，是名为僧。今日请佛及现前僧，设诸供养。」

The elder Anāthapiṇḍada asked the elder: "Can I now go and see the World-Honored One?"

给孤独长者问彼长者言：「我今可得往见世尊不？」

The elder replied: "You stay here. I invite the World-Honored One to come to my house. You can see him here."

彼长者答言：「汝且住此，我请世尊来至我舍，于此得见。」

At that time, the elder Anāthapiṇḍada single-mindedly contemplated the Buddha that night and was able to sleep. Before dawn, he suddenly saw the light and thought it was already light. He wanted to leave his house and go to the city gate. When he reached the city gate, it was only the second watch of the night. The city gate had not yet opened. The king's family had a rule that they would wait for distant messengers to come and go until the first watch of the night was over, then the city gate would be closed. At the end of the middle watch, the gate would be opened again, so that travelers could come and go early.

时，给孤独长者即于其夜至心念佛，因得睡眠。天犹未明，忽见明相，谓天已晓，欲出其舍，行向城门，至城门下，夜始二更，城门未开，王家常法，待远使命来往，至初夜尽，城门乃闭，中夜已尽，辄复开门，欲令行人早得往来。

At that time, the elder Anāthapiṇḍada saw the city gate open and thought: "It must be that the night has passed and the gate is open." He went out of the city gate following the light. After leaving the city gate, the light disappeared and it became dark again. The elder Anāthapiṇḍada was frightened and his body hair stood on end. He wondered if it was a person or a non-human, or a treacherous person, who wanted to frighten him. He then wanted to return.

尔时，给孤独长者见城门开，而作是念：「定是夜过天晓门开。」乘明相出于城门，出城门已，明相即灭，辄还暗冥。给孤独长者心即恐怖，身毛为竖，得无为人及非人，或奸姣人恐怖我耶？即便欲还。

At that time, a heavenly spirit was dwelling beside the city gate. The heavenly spirit then emitted its own light, from the city gate to the cold forest cemetery, illuminating everywhere. It told the elder Anāthapiṇḍada: "You should go forward. You will attain victory. Do not retreat."

尔时，城门侧有天神住。时，彼天神即放身光，从其城门至寒林丘塜间光明普照，告给孤独长者言：「汝且前进，可得胜利，慎勿退还。」

At that time, the heavenly spirit spoke a verse, saying:

时，彼天神即说偈言：

One hundred good horses, one hundred catties of gold, one
hundred mule carts and horse carts, each with one hundred
carriages, various precious treasures loaded on them, planted good roots
in past lives, and obtained such a reward. If a
person with a reverent mind takes a step towards the
Buddha, it is one-sixteenth of the merit, surpassing the merit
of the previous.

「善良马百匹，　　黄金满百斤，骡车及马车，　　各各有百乘，种种诸珍奇，　　重宝载其上，宿命种善根，　　得如此福报，若人宗重心，　　向佛行一步，十六分之一，　　过前福之上。

"Therefore, elder, you should advance and not retreat!" He then spoke a verse:

「是故，长者！汝当前进，慎勿退还！」即复说偈：

A great dragon elephant in the snowy mountains, adorned with pure gold
with large tusks, to give such an elephant to a person, is
not as good as the merit of going towards the Buddha, one-sixteenth of it.

「雪山大龙象，　　纯金为庄饰，巨身长大牙，　　以此象施人，不及向佛福，　　十六分之一。

"Therefore, elder, you should quickly advance and obtain great benefit, not retreat." He then spoke a verse:

「是故，长者！当速前进，得其大利，非退还也。」复说偈言：

The women of the country of King Puśya, numbering a
hundred, adorned with various wonderful jewels and necklaces, to give
them away like this, is not as good as the
merit of taking a single step towards the Buddha, one-sixteenth of it.

「金菩阇国女，　　其数有百人，种种众妙宝，　　璎珞具庄严，以是持施与，　　不及行向佛，一步之功德，　　十六分之一。

"Therefore, elder, you should quickly advance and obtain the victory, not retreat."

「是故，长者！当速前进，得其胜利，非退还也。」

At that time, the elder Anāthapiṇḍada asked the heavenly spirit, "Wise one! Who are you?"

时，给孤独长者问天神言：「贤者！汝是何人？」

The heavenly spirit replied, "I am the great youth Mahāmaudgalyāyana, a good friend of the elder. I gave rise to a mind of respect towards Venerable Śāriputra and Mahāmaudgalyāyana. Due to this merit, I was reborn in the heavens and am in charge of this city gate. Therefore, I told the elder, 'You should only advance, and be careful not to retreat. Advancing will bring benefits, not retreating.'"

天神答言：「我是摩头息揵大摩那婆先，是长者善知识，于尊者舍利弗、大目揵连所起信敬心，缘斯功德，今得生天，典此城门，是故告长者：『但当进前，慎莫退还，前进得利，非退还也。』」

At that time, the elder Anāthapiṇḍada thought, "The Buddha's appearance in the world is no small matter; hearing the true Dharma is also no small matter. Therefore, the heavenly spirit encourages me to advance and go see the World-Honored One." The elder Anāthapiṇḍada followed the light and went to the Śītavana charnel ground.

时，给孤独长者作是念：「佛兴于世，非为小事；得闻正法，亦非小事。是故天神劝我令进，往见世尊。」时，给孤独长者寻其光明，迳至寒林丘塜间。

At that time, the World-Honored One was walking in the open air outside his room. The elder Anāthapiṇḍada saw the Buddha from afar, and went before him. He greeted him with the etiquette of a layperson, saying, "How is it, World-Honored One? Did you sleep well?"

尔时，世尊出房露地经行，给孤独长者遥见佛已，即至其前，以俗人礼法恭敬问讯：「云何？世尊！安隐卧不？」

At that time, the World-Honored One replied in verse:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

Brahmins attain nirvana, which is truly peaceful and blissful, unstained
by desires and attachments, completely liberated without remainder. Cutting off
all hopes and desires, taming the burning mind, the mind
attains tranquility and cessation, peacefully and blissfully resting in cessation.

「婆罗门涅槃，　　是则常安乐，爱欲所不染，　　解脱永无余。断一切希望，　　调伏心炽燃，心得寂止息，　　止息安隐眠。」

At that time, the World-Honored One took Anāthapiṇḍada and entered the room, took his seat, and focused his mind. The World-Honored One then taught him the Dharma, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting him. The World-Honored One said, "All phenomena are impermanent. One should perform meritorious deeds of giving, upholding precepts, and being reborn in heaven. The taste of desire, the suffering of desire, and the liberation from desire are meritorious deeds of detachment."

尔时，世尊将给孤独长者往入房中，就座而坐，端身系念。尔时，世尊为其说法，示、教、照、喜已，世尊说：「诸法无常，宜布施福事、持戒福事、生天福事，欲味、欲患、欲出，远离之福。」

Anāthapiṇḍada heard the Dharma, saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, entered the Dharma, and understood the Dharma. He transcended all doubts, did not rely on others' faith, and was not liberated by others. He entered the true Dharma and Vinaya, and his mind attained fearlessness. He then rose from his seat, adjusted his robes, bowed to the Buddha, placed his right knee on the ground, joined his palms, and said to the Buddha, "I am liberated, World-Honored One! I am liberated, Well-Gone One! From this day forth, for as long as I live, I take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha of monks. I am a lay devotee. Please bear witness to me."

给孤独长者闻法、见法、得法、入法、解法，度诸疑惑，不由他信，不由他度，入正法、律，心得无畏，即从座起，正衣服，为佛作礼，右膝著地，合掌白佛言：「已度。世尊！已度。善逝！我从今日尽其寿命，归佛、归法、归比丘僧，为优婆塞，证知我。」

At that time, the World-Honored One asked Anāthapiṇḍada, "What is your name?"

尔时，世尊问给孤独长者：「汝名何等？」

The elder said to the Buddha, "My name is Sudatta. Because I always give to the poor and needy with hardship, people call me Anāthapiṇḍada."

长者白佛：「名须达多。以常给孤贫辛苦故，时人名我为给孤独。」

The World-Honored One further asked, "Where do you reside?"

世尊复问：「汝居何处？」

The elder said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! In the human realm of Kosala, there is a city called Śrāvastī. I only wish that the World-Honored One would come to Śrāvastī. I will provide for you for as long as I live, with robes, food and drink, dwellings, bedding, and medicine for illness."

长者白佛言：「世尊！在拘萨罗人间，城名舍卫，唯愿世尊来舍卫国，我当尽寿供养衣被、饮食、房舍、床卧、随病汤药。」

The Buddha asked the elder, "Is there a monastery in Śrāvastī?"

佛问长者：「舍卫国有精舍不？」

The elder said to the Buddha, "No, World-Honored One!"

长者白佛：「无也，世尊！」

The Buddha told the elder, "You should establish a monastery there for the monks to come and stay."

佛告长者：「汝可于彼建立精舍，令诸比丘往来宿止。」

The elder said to the Buddha, "If the World-Honored One comes to Śrāvastī, I will build a monastery and dwelling places for the monks to come and stay." At that time, the World-Honored One silently accepted the invitation.

长者白佛：「但使世尊来舍卫国，我当造作精舍僧房，令诸比丘往来止住。」尔时，世尊默然受请。

At that time, the elder knew that the World-Honored Buddha had silently accepted the invitation. He rose from his seat, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and left.

时，长者知佛世尊默然受请已，从座起，稽首佛足而去。
