SĀ 594

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 594（五九四） 首长者生天

Parallel to: AN 3.127 / SĀ² 2.188

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the wilderness monastery.

一时，佛住旷野精舍。

At that time, the elder of the wilderness had died of illness and was reborn in the heaven of no heat. Having been reborn there, he thought, "I should not stay here for long without seeing the World-Honored One." Having thought this, in the time it takes for a strong man to bend and stretch his arm, he disappeared from the heaven of no heat and appeared before the Buddha.

时，有旷野长者疾病命终，生无热天。生彼天已，即作是念：「我今不应久于此住，不见世尊。」作是念已，如力士屈申臂顷，从无热天没，现于佛前。

At that time, the heavenly being's heavenly body collapsed on the ground and could not stand up by itself, like butter or oil that has collapsed on the ground and cannot stand up by itself. In the same way, the heavenly being's heavenly body was fine and soft, and could not stand up by itself.

时，彼天子天身委地，不能自立，犹如酥油委地，不能自立。如是，彼天子天身细软，不自持立。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the heavenly being, "You should transform into this coarse body and stand on the ground."

尔时，世尊告彼天子：「汝当变化作此麁身，而立于地。」

At that time, the heavenly being immediately transformed his form into this coarse body and stood on the ground. Then, the heavenly being bowed his head at the Buddha's feet and sat to one side.

时，彼天子即自化形，作此麁身，而立于地。于是，天子前礼佛足，退坐一面。

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the Heavenly Being with Hands, "Heavenly Being with Hands, when you were a human being here before, did you remember and not forget the sutras and teachings you received?"

尔时，世尊告手天子：「汝手天子，本于此间为人身时，所受经法，今故忆念不悉忘耶？」

The Heavenly Being with Hands said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I remember and do not forget what I received before. When I was a human being before, I did not fully understand the teachings I heard. Now I also remember them, just as the World-Honored One has explained well. The World-Honored One said, 'If one is in a place of peace and happiness, one can remember the teachings, not in a place of suffering.' This is true. As the World-Honored One was surrounded by various kinds of fourfold assemblies in Jambudvīpa and taught the Dharma, those fourfold assemblies all practiced what the Buddha taught. It is the same for me. In the heaven of no heat, I teach the Dharma to a great assembly of gods and humans, and those gods all accept and practice it."

手天子白佛言：「世尊！本所受持，今悉不忘。本人间时，有所闻法，不尽得者，今亦忆念，如世尊善说。世尊说言：『若人安乐处，能忆持法，非为苦处。』此说真实。如世尊在阎浮提，种种杂类，四众围遶，而为说法，彼诸四众闻佛所说，皆悉奉行。我亦如是，于无热天上，为诸天人大会说法，彼诸天众悉受修学。」

The Buddha said to the Heavenly Being with Hands, "When you were a human being here, because of being insatiable in how many teachings, were you able to be reborn in the heaven of no heat?"

佛告手天子：「汝于此人间时，于几法无厌足故，而得生彼无热天中？」

The Heavenly Being with Hands said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Because of being insatiable in three teachings, when my body perished and my life ended, I was reborn in the heaven of no heat. What are the three teachings? Because of being insatiable in seeing the Buddha, when my body perished and my life ended, I was reborn in the heaven of no heat; because of being insatiable in the Buddha's teachings, I was reborn in the heaven of no heat; because of being insatiable in making offerings to the Sangha, when my body perished and my life ended, I was reborn in the heaven of no heat." At that time, the Heavenly Being with Hands spoke a verse,

手天子白佛：「世尊！我于三法无厌足故，身坏命终，生无热天。何等三法？我于见佛无厌故，身坏命终生无热天；我于佛法无厌足故，生无热天；供养众僧无厌足故，身坏命终，生无热天。」时，手天子即说偈言：

Being insatiable in seeing the Buddha, and in hearing the Dharma, and
in making offerings to the Sangha, never being satisfied, upholding the teachings
of the sages, taming the defilements of stinginess and attachment, because of
being insatiable in these three teachings, I was reborn in the heaven
of no heat.

「见佛无厌足，　　闻法亦无厌，供养于众僧，　　亦未曾知足，受持贤圣法，　　调伏悭著垢，三法不知足，　　故生无热天。」

At that time, the Heavenly Being with Hands, having heard the Buddha's teaching, was delighted and followed the teaching. He then disappeared.

时，手天子闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，即没不现。
