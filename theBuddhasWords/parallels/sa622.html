SĀ 622

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 622（六二 庵罗女二）

Parallel to: SN 47.2

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling among the people of Vṛji, and arrived at the mango grove in Vaiśālī.

一时，佛在跋祇人间游行，到鞞舍离国庵罗园中住。

At that time, the woman Amrapālī heard that the World-Honored One was traveling among the people of Vṛji and had arrived at the mango grove in Vaiśālī. She adorned herself, took a carriage, left the city of Vaiśālī, and went to where the World-Honored One was to pay respects and make offerings. She arrived at the gate of the mango grove, got off the carriage, and walked forward. From afar, she saw the World-Honored One surrounded by a great assembly, expounding the Dharma.

尔时，庵罗女闻世尊跋祇人间游行至庵罗园中住，即自庄严乘车，出鞞舍离城，诣世尊所，恭敬供养。诣庵罗园门，下车步进，遥见世尊与诸大众围遶说法。

The World-Honored One saw Amrapālī coming from afar and said to the bhikṣus, "You bhikṣus should diligently restrain your minds and abide in right mindfulness and right knowledge. Now Amrapālī is coming, so I admonish you. What is it for a bhikṣu to diligently restrain his mind and abide? If a bhikṣu has already arisen evil and unwholesome dharmas, he should cut them off, generate desire, and diligently restrain his mind with skillful means. If evil and unwholesome dharmas have not yet arisen, he should prevent them from arising. If wholesome dharmas have not yet arisen, he should cause them to arise. If wholesome dharmas have already arisen, he should cause them to abide without forgetting, cultivate them, and increase them. He should generate desire, and diligently restrain his mind with skillful means. This is called a bhikṣu diligently restraining his mind and abiding.

世尊遥见庵罗女来，语诸比丘：「汝等比丘勤摄心住，正念正智。今庵罗女来，是故诫汝。云何为比丘勤摄心住？若比丘已生恶不善法当断，生欲、方便，精进摄心。未生恶不善法不令起，未生善法令生，已生善法令住不忘，修习增满，生欲、方便，精勤摄心，是名比丘勤摄心住。

What is it for a bhikṣu to have right knowledge? If a bhikṣu's going, coming, deportment, and behavior always follow right knowledge, looking back, looking around, bending, stretching, bowing, looking up, holding robes and alms bowl, walking, standing, sitting, lying down, sleeping, waking, speaking, and being silent all abide in right knowledge. This is right knowledge.

「云何名比丘正智？若比丘去来威仪常随正智，回顾视瞻，屈伸俯仰，执持衣钵，行住坐卧，眠觉语默，皆随正智住，是正智。

What is right mindfulness? If a bhikṣu abides in mindfulness of the body observing the body internally, diligently applying himself with right knowledge and right mindfulness, taming worldly greed and sorrow. Likewise, mindfulness of feelings, mind, and dharmas observing dharmas internally, diligently applying himself with right knowledge and right mindfulness, taming worldly greed and sorrow. This is called right mindfulness for a bhikṣu. Therefore, you should diligently restrain your minds, with right knowledge and right mindfulness. Now Amrapālī is coming, so I admonish you.

「云何正念？若比丘内身身观念住，精勤方便，正智正念，调伏世间贪忧。如是受、心、法法观念住，精勤方便，正智正念，调伏世间贪忧，是名比丘正念。是故汝等勤摄其心，正智正念，今庵罗女来，是故诫汝。」

At that time, Amrapālī went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed her head at his feet, and stood to one side.

时，庵罗女诣世尊所，稽首礼足，却住一面。

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke various teachings for Amrapālī, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting her. Having shown, taught, illuminated, and delighted her, he remained silent.

尔时，世尊为庵罗女种种说法，示、教、照、喜；示、教、照、喜已，默然而住。

At that time, Amrapālī adjusted her clothes, bowed to the Buddha, joined her palms, and said to the Buddha, "I only wish that the World-Honored One and the great assembly would accept my invitation for a midday meal tomorrow."

尔时，庵罗女整衣服，为佛作礼，合掌白佛：「唯愿世尊与诸大众明日受我请中食。」

At that time, the World-Honored One silently accepted the invitation. Amrapālī, knowing that the World-Honored One had silently accepted the invitation, bowed at his feet and returned to her own home. She prepared various foods, arranged the seats, and in the morning sent a messenger to inform the Buddha, "The time has come."

尔时，世尊默然受请。庵罗女知世尊默然受请已，稽首礼足，还归自家，设种种食，布置床座，晨朝遣使白佛：「时到。」

At that time, the World-Honored One went with the great assembly to Amrapālī's house and took his seat.

尔时，世尊与诸大众诣庵罗女舍，就座而坐。

At that time, Amrapālī personally served various foods and drinks. After the meal, they washed their hands and rinsed their mouths, and put away the bowls. Then Amrapālī brought a small bench and sat in front of the Buddha to listen to the Dharma.

时，庵罗女手自供养种种饮食。食讫，澡漱、洗钵竟。时，庵罗女持一小床坐于佛前，听佛说法。

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse of rejoicing for Amrapālī:

尔时，世尊为庵罗女说随喜偈：

The giver is loved by people, followed by many, their fame
increases day by day, heard far and wide, always harmonious and
elegant in the midst of the assembly, free from stinginess and
fearless. Therefore, give with wisdom, cutting off stinginess forever without remainder
Born above in the Heaven of the Thirty-three, enjoying happiness for
a long night, practicing virtue throughout their life, entertaining in Nandana
Park, with a hundred kinds of heavenly pleasures, the five desires
delighting their minds. Having heard the Dharma taught by the Buddha
in this human realm, they become disciples of the Well-Gone One
delighting in being reborn through transformation.

「施者人爱念，　　多众所随从，名称日增高，　　远近皆悉闻，处众常和雅，　　离悭无所畏。是故智慧施，　　断悭永无余。上生忉利天，　　长夜受快乐，尽寿常修德，　　娱乐难陀园，百种诸天乐，　　五欲悦其心。彼于此人间，　　闻佛所说法，为善逝弟子，　　乐彼受化生。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke various teachings for Amrapālī, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting her. Having shown, taught, illuminated, and delighted her, he rose from his seat and departed.

尔时，世尊为庵罗女种种说法，示、教、照、喜；示、教、照、喜已，从座起而去。
