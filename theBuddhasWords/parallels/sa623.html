SĀ 623

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 623（六二三） 世间

Parallel to: SN 47.20

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying in the Deer Park at Isipatana near Varanasi.

一时，佛住波罗奈仙人住处鹿野苑中。

At that time the World-Honored One said to the bhikṣus, "In the world, what is considered beautiful? What is considered beautiful in the world that can cause many people to gather and watch?"

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「世间言美色，世间美色者，能令多人集聚观看者不？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

诸比丘白佛：「如是，世尊！」

The Buddha said to the bhikṣus, "If the beautiful things in the world, the beautiful things in the world, can also perform various songs, dances, and music, and cause even more people to gather and watch?"

佛告比丘：「若世间美色，世间美色者，又能种种歌舞伎乐，复极令多众聚集看不？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

比丘白佛：「如是，世尊！」

The Buddha said to the bhikṣus, "If there are beautiful things in the world, beautiful things in the world, performing various songs, dances, and music in one place, and a large crowd gathers in one place, if there is a man who is not foolish or deluded, who desires happiness and turns away from suffering, who is greedy for life and afraid of death, and someone tells him, 'Man, you should hold a bowl full of oil and pass by the beautiful things in the world and the large crowd. If you spill even a drop of oil, you will be beheaded.' What do you think, bhikṣus? Can that man holding the bowl of oil not think about the bowl of oil, not think about the killer, and watch those entertainers and the large crowd?"

佛告比丘：「若有世间美色，世间美色者，在于一处，作种种歌舞伎乐戏笑，复有大众云集一处，若有士夫不愚不痴，乐乐背苦，贪生畏死，有人语言：『士夫，汝当持满油钵，于世间美色者所及大众中过，使一能杀人者，拔刀随汝，若失一渧油者，辄当斩汝命。』云何？比丘！彼持油钵士夫能不念油钵，不念杀人者，观彼伎女及大众不？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "No, World-Honored One! Why is that? World-Honored One! That man sees a sword-wielding killer behind him and always thinks, 'If I spill a drop of oil, that sword-wielding killer will cut off my head.' He focuses his mind on the bowl of oil and slowly passes by the beautiful things in the world and the large crowd, not daring to glance around."

比丘白佛：「不也，世尊！所以者何？世尊！彼士夫自见其后有拔刀者，常作是念：『我若落油一渧，彼拔刀者当截我头。』唯一其心，系念油钵，于世间美色及大众中徐步而过，不敢顾眄。」

"Thus, bhikṣus! If there are śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who properly control their bodies, are self-reliant, unify their minds, do not pay attention to sounds and colors, and well restrain all mental phenomena, abiding in the mindfulness of the body, they are my disciples who follow my teachings. How do bhikṣus properly control their bodies, are self-reliant, unify their minds, do not pay attention to sounds and colors, restrain all mental phenomena, and abide in the mindfulness of the body? Thus, bhikṣus! Observing the body with the body, diligently applying skillful means, with right knowledge and right mindfulness, taming worldly greed and sorrow, the same is true for the mindfulness of feelings, mind, and phenomena. This is called bhikṣus properly controlling their bodies, being self-reliant, unifying their minds, not paying attention to sounds and colors, well restraining mental phenomena, and abiding in the four mindfulnesses."

「如是，比丘！若有沙门、婆罗门正身自重，一其心念，不顾声色，善摄一切心法，住身念处者，则是我弟子，随我教者。云何为比丘正身自重，一其心念，不顾声色，摄持一切心法，住身念处。如是，比丘！身身观念，精勤方便，正智正念，调伏世间贪忧，受、心、法法观念住亦复如是。是名比丘正身自重，一其心念，不顾声色，善摄心法，住四念处。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

Concentrate your mind and uphold the oil bowl
protect your own mind, and never reach the
other shore. It is very difficult to surpass
the subtle and wonderful teachings spoken by the
Buddhas. The sword of the teachings should be
upheld with a concentrated mind. It is not
the matter of the carelessness of ordinary people
that can enter such non-carelessness.

「专心正念，　　护持油钵，自心随护，　　未曾至方，甚难得过，　　胜妙微细，诸佛所说，　　言教利剑。当一其心，　　专精护持，非彼凡人，　　放逸之事，能入如是，　　不放逸教。」

After the Buddha spoke this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully upheld it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
