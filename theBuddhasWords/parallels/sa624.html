SĀ 624

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 624（六二四） 郁低迦

Parallel to: SN 47.16

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Uttara came to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha’s feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, “Excellent, World-Honored One! Please teach the Dharma for me. Having heard the Dharma, I will dwell alone in a quiet place, diligently contemplating and not being negligent. Contemplating: ‘The reason good sons shave their heads and beards, with right faith leave home, and go forth to practice the path……’ as extensively explained above, up to ‘not accepting future existence.’

尔时，尊者欝低迦来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面，白佛言：「善哉！世尊！为我说法，我闻法已，当独一静处，专精思惟，不放逸住，思惟：『所以善男子剃除须发，正信非家，出家学道……。』」如上广说，乃至「不受后有。」

The Buddha told Uttara, “It is so, it is so, as you have said. But what I have taught does not please my mind, and their work is also not accomplished. Although they follow behind me, they do not gain any benefit, but rather give rise to obstacles.”

佛告欝低迦：「如是，如是，如汝所说。但于我所说法，不悦我心，彼所事业亦不成就，虽随我后，而不得利，反生障阂。」

Uttara said to the Buddha, “World-Honored One, what you have taught, I can make the World-Honored One's mind joyful, and accomplish my own work, without giving rise to obstacles. I only pray that the World-Honored One will teach the Dharma for me. I will dwell alone in a quiet place, diligently contemplating and not being negligent……' as extensively explained above, up to 'not accepting future existence.' Thus he made the request a second and third time.

欝低迦白佛：「世尊所说，我则能令世尊心悦，自业成就，不生障阂。唯愿世尊为我说法，我当独一静处，专精思惟，不放逸住……」如上广说，乃至「不受后有。」如是第二、第三请。

At that time, the World-Honored One told Uttara, "You should first purify your initial practice, and then cultivate the holy life."

尔时，世尊告欝低迦：「汝当先净其初业，然后修习梵行。」

Uttara said to the Buddha, "How should I now purify my initial practice and cultivate the holy life?"

欝低迦白佛：「我今云何净其初业，修习梵行？」

The Buddha told Uttara, "You should first purify your precepts, straighten your views, and perfect the three karmas. Then cultivate the four foundations of mindfulness. What are the four? Mindfulness of the body internally, with body contemplation, diligent effort, right knowledge, right mindfulness, and taming worldly greed and sorrow. Thus, mindfulness of the body externally, mindfulness of the body internally and externally, mindfulness of feelings, mind, and dharmas with dharma contemplation..." and so on, explaining in detail.

佛告欝低迦：「汝当先净其戒，直其见，具足三业，然后修四念处。何等为四？内身身观念住，专精方便，正智正念，调伏世间贪忧。如是外身、内外身身观念住，受、心、法法观念住……」亦如是广说。

At that time, Uttara, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully rejoiced and rose from his seat to depart.

时，欝低迦闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，从座起而去。

At that time, Uttara, having received the Buddha's instruction, stayed alone in a quiet place, diligently contemplating without negligence, thinking: "The reason good sons shave their heads, wear the monastic robe, with right faith leave home and renounce the world, and even not accept future existence..."

时，欝低迦闻佛教授已，独一静处，专精思惟，不放逸住，思惟：「所以善男子剃除须发，著袈裟衣，正信非家，出家学道，乃至不受后有。」

As Uttara asked, so did other bhikshus ask, as explained above.

如欝低迦所问，如是异比丘所问亦如上说。
