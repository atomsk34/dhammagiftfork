SĀ 639

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 639（六三九） 布萨

Parallel to: SN 47.14

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying by the banks of the Vaddha River in the Mango Grove of the Umbrella Tree in the country of Mathura, not long after the parinirvana of Venerable Sariputra and Maudgalyayana. At that time, on the fifteenth day of the lunar month, the World-Honored One took his seat in front of the assembly.

一时，佛住摩偷罗国跋陀罗河侧伞盖庵罗树林中，尊者舍利弗、目揵连涅槃未久。尔时，世尊月十五日布萨时，于大众前敷座而坐。

At that time, the World-Honored One observed the assembly and said to the bhikshus, "I see the assembly as empty, because of the parinirvana of Sariputra and Maudgalyayana. Among my disciples, only these two were able to teach the Dharma well, instruct and admonish, and speak with eloquence. There are two kinds of wealth, worldly wealth and the wealth of the Dharma. Worldly wealth is sought from worldly people, and the wealth of the Dharma is sought from Sariputra and Maudgalyayana. The Tathāgata has already given up both worldly wealth and the wealth of the Dharma.

尔时，世尊观察众会已，告诸比丘：「我观大众，见已虚空，以舍利弗、大目揵连般涅槃故。我声闻唯此二人善能说法，教诫、教授，辩说满足。有二种财，钱财及法财，钱财者从世人求，法财者从舍利弗、大目揵连求，如来已离施财及法财。

"Do not grieve and be distressed because of the parinirvana of Sariputra and Maudgalyayana. It is like a large tree with luxuriant roots, trunk, branches, leaves, flowers, and fruits. When a large branch breaks off first, it is like a treasure mountain, with a large rock collapsing first. In the same way, in the great assembly of the Tathāgata, the two great disciples Sariputra and Maudgalyayana have passed into parinirvana first. Therefore, bhikshus, do not grieve and be distressed. How can there be anything that is born, arisen, made, conditioned, and subject to decay that does not perish? To wish for it not to decay is impossible. I have already said that all that is lovable and desirable will eventually be separated. I will not be here for long. Therefore, you should know that you should rely on yourselves, rely on the Dharma, and not rely on anything else. This means to abide in mindfulness of the body, diligently applying right wisdom and right mindfulness to subdue worldly greed and sorrow. In the same way, to abide in mindfulness of the body, feelings, mind, and dharmas, diligently applying right wisdom and right mindfulness to subdue worldly greed and sorrow, this is called relying on oneself, relying on the Dharma, and not relying on anything else."

「汝等莫以舍利弗、目揵连涅槃故愁忧苦恼。譬如大树，根、茎、枝、叶、华、果茂盛，大枝先折，亦如宝山，大岩先崩。如是，如来大众之中，舍利弗、目揵连二大声闻先般涅槃。是故，比丘！汝等勿生愁忧苦恼，何有生法、起法、作法、为法、坏败之法而不磨灭？欲令不坏，无有是处，我先已说，一切可爱之物皆归离散，我今不久亦当过去。是故汝等当知，自洲以自依、法洲以法依、不异洲不异依，谓内身身观念住，精勤方便，正智正念，调伏世间贪忧。如是外身、内外身，受、心、法法观念住，精勤方便，正智正念，调伏世间贪忧，是名自洲以自依，法洲以法依，不异洲不异依。」

Having spoken this sutra, the Buddha said to the bhikshus, "Good, good! The bhikshus should uphold and recite this sutra."

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
