SĀ 669

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 669（六六九） 摄

Parallel to: AN 4.32 / EĀ² 2.6

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus...as above. The difference is: "All dharmas that are taken by the assembly are all the four means of embracing. Some take giving, some take kind speech, some take beneficial action, and some take equality. In the past, the assembly of the past took what was taken, which were also the four means of embracing. The assembly of the future will take what is taken, which will also be the four means of embracing. Some will take giving, some will take kind speech, some will take beneficial action, and some will take equality."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘……如上说。差别者：「若所有法，是众之所取，一切皆是四摄事。或有一取施者，或一取爱语者，或一取行利者，或一取同利者。过去世时，过去世众，以有所取者，亦是四摄事。未来世众，当有所取者，亦是四摄事。或一取施者，或一取爱语，或一取行利者，或一取同利。」

At that time the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

Giving and kind speech, or beneficial action, and equality in all practices
arise, each according to their suitability. Embracing the world with this, like
a cart is transported by the axle. Without the four means of
embracing in the world, the mother's kindness is forgotten by the child
and there is also no father or other honored ones who are
humbly served. Because there are the four means of embracing, the Dharma
of following and conforming, therefore there are great beings whose virtues cover the world.

「布施及爱语，　　或有行利者，同利诸行生，　　各随其所应。以此摄世间，　　犹车因釭运。世无四摄事，　　母恩子养忘，亦无父等尊，　　谦下之奉事。以有四摄事，　　随顺之法故，是故有大士，　　德被于世间。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully upheld it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
