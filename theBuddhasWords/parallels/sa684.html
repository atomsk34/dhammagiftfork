SĀ 684

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 684（六八四） 十力

Parallel to: MN 12 (~) / AN 6.64 (~) / AN 10.21 / SĀ 701 / EĀ 46.4

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "If a bhikṣu generates disgust, detachment, cessation, non-arising, and liberation towards form, he is called an arhat, a samyak-saṃbuddha. The same is said for feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness. If a bhikṣu generates disgust, detachment, non-arising, and liberation towards form, he is called an arhat who is liberated through wisdom. The same is said for feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness. Bhikṣus, what are the various differences between the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, and the arhat who is liberated through wisdom?"

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「若比丘于色生厌、离欲、灭尽、不起、解脱，是名阿罗诃三藐三佛陀。受、想、行、识亦如是说。若复比丘于色生厌、离欲、不起、解脱者，是名阿罗汉慧解脱。受、想、行、识亦如是说。诸比丘！如来应等正觉、阿罗汉慧解脱有何种种别异。」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "The World-Honored One is the root of the Dharma, the eye of the Dharma, the reliance of the Dharma. We only wish that you would explain it. Having heard it, the bhikṣus will accept and uphold it."

诸比丘白佛：「世尊是法根、法眼、法依，唯愿为说，诸比丘闻已，当受奉行。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "Listen carefully and consider it well, I will explain it to you. The Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, first, without hearing the Dharma, is able to awaken to it by himself, realize it in the present life, and attain the three bodhi-minds. In the future, he is able to expound the true Dharma and awaken the śrāvakas, namely the four foundations of mindfulness, four right efforts, four bases of supernormal power, five faculties, five powers, seven factors of awakening, and eightfold noble path. This is called the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One. The Dharma that has not been attained can be attained, the holy life that has not been established can be established, he is able to know the path well and teach the path well, and he is the leader of the multitude. Then the śrāvakas accomplish the Dharma and the path according to the Dharma and the path, delighting in following the great teacher's instructions and teachings, and are skilled in the true Dharma. This is called the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, the arhat who is liberated through wisdom, the various differences.

佛告比丘：「谛听，善思，当为汝说。如来、应、等正觉者，先未闻法，能自觉知，现法自] cf.【丽】知，得三菩提，于未来世能说正法，觉诸声闻，所谓四念处、四正断、四如意足、五根、五力、七觉分、八圣道分，是名如来、应、等正觉。所未得法能得，未制梵行能制，能善知道、善说道，为众将导，然后声闻成就随法随道，乐奉大师教诫、教授，善于正法，是名如来应等正觉、阿罗汉慧解脱种种别异。

Furthermore, the five powers of learning and the ten powers of the Tathāgata. What are the powers of learning? They are the power of faith, the power of diligence, the power of mindfulness, the power of concentration, and the power of wisdom. What are the ten powers of the Tathāgata? They are the Tathāgata's true knowledge of what is possible and what is not possible. This is called the first power of the Tathāgata. If one accomplishes this power, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, attains the supreme knowledge of the Tathāgata, turns the wheel of the Dharma, and roars the lion's roar in the great assembly.

「复次，五学力、如来十力。何等为学力？谓信力、精进力、念力、定力、慧力。何等为如来十力？谓如来处非处如实知，是名如来初力。若成就此力者，如来、应、等正觉得先佛最胜处智，转于梵轮，于大众中能师子吼而吼。

Furthermore, the Tathāgata's true knowledge of past, future, and present karmic dharmas, their causes, and their results. This is called the second power of the Tathāgata. The Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, having accomplished this power, attains the supreme place of the former Buddhas, is able to turn the wheel of the Dharma, and roars the lion's roar in the great assembly.

「复次，如来于过去、未来、现在业法，受因事报如实知，是名第二如来力。如来、应、等正觉成就此力，得先佛最胜处，能转梵轮，于大众中作师子吼而吼。

Furthermore, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One's true knowledge of the defilements, purity, and places of purity in the concentrations, liberations, samādhis, and samāpattis. This is called the third power of the Tathāgata. If one accomplishes this power, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, attains the supreme knowledge of the former Buddhas, is able to turn the wheel of the Dharma, and roars the lion's roar in the great assembly.

「复次，如来、应、等正觉禅解脱，三昧正受，染恶清净，处净如实知，是名如来第三力。若此力成就，如来、应、等正觉得先佛最胜处智，能转梵轮，于大众中师子吼而吼。

Furthermore, the Tathāgata's true knowledge of the various differences in the faculties of sentient beings. This is called the fourth power of the Tathāgata. If one accomplishes this power, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, attains the supreme knowledge of the former Buddhas, is able to turn the wheel of the Dharma, and roars the lion's roar in the great assembly.

「复次，如来知众生种种诸根差别如实知，是名如来第四力。若成就此力，如来、应、等正觉得先佛最胜处智，能转梵轮，于大众中师子吼而吼。

Furthermore, the Tathāgata's true knowledge of the various understandings of sentient beings. This is called the fifth power of the Tathāgata. If one accomplishes this power, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, attains the supreme knowledge of the former Buddhas, is able to turn the wheel of the Dharma, and roars the lion's roar in the great assembly.

「复次，如来悉知众生种种意解如实知，是名第五如来力。若此力成就，如来、应、等正觉得先佛最胜处智，能转梵轮，于大众中师子吼而吼。

Furthermore, the Tathāgata's true knowledge of the various realms of sentient beings in the world. This is called the sixth power of the Tathāgata. If one accomplishes this power, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, attains the supreme knowledge of the former Buddhas, is able to turn the wheel of the Dharma, and roars the lion's roar in the great assembly.

「复次，如来悉知世间众生种种诸界如实知，是名第六如来力。若于此力成就，如来、应、等正觉得先佛最胜处智，能转梵轮，于大众中师子吼而吼。

Furthermore, the Tathāgata's true knowledge of all paths to be reached. This is called the seventh power of the Tathāgata. If one accomplishes this power, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, attains the supreme knowledge of the former Buddhas, is able to turn the wheel of the Dharma, and roars the lion's roar in the great assembly.

「复次，如来于一切至处道如实知，是名第七如来力。若此力成就，如来、应、等正觉得先佛最胜处智，能转梵轮，于大众中师子吼而吼。

Furthermore, the Tathāgata's true knowledge of past lives, from one birth to a hundred thousand births, from one eon to a hundred thousand eons. I was born in such a family, with such a surname, such a name, such food, such feelings of suffering and happiness, such a long life, such a long stay, such a lifespan. I died in that place and was born in this place, born in that place and died in this place, such actions, such causes, such directions. The past experiences are all truly known. This is called the eighth power of the Tathāgata. If one accomplishes this power, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, attains the supreme knowledge of the former Buddhas, is able to turn the wheel of the Dharma, and roars the lion's roar in the great assembly.

「复次，如来于过去宿命种种事忆念：『从一生至百千生，从一劫至百千劫。我尔时于彼生如是族、如是姓、如是名、如是食、如是苦乐觉、如是长寿、如是久住、如是寿分齐。我于彼处死、此处生，彼处生、此处死，如是行、如是因、如是方。』宿命所更悉如实知，是名第八如来力。若此力成就，如来、应、等正觉得先佛最胜处智，能转梵轮，于大众中师子吼而吼。

Furthermore, the Tathāgata's true knowledge of the passing away and rebirth of beings, seeing with the pure divine eye that surpasses the human eye, the good and bad, inferior and superior forms, those going to the lower realms and those going to the good realms, according to their karmic conditions. 'These beings have accomplished evil physical, verbal, and mental actions, have slandered the sages, and received the results of wrong views. By these causes and conditions, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they fall into the lower realms and are born in hell.' 'These beings have accomplished good physical, verbal, and mental actions, have not slandered the sages, and have received the results of right views. By those causes and conditions, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are born in the good realms, in the heavens.' All this is truly known. This is called the ninth power of the Tathāgata. If one accomplishes this power, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, attains the supreme knowledge of the former Buddhas, is able to turn the wheel of the Dharma, and roars the lion's roar in the great assembly.

「复次，如来以天眼净过于人眼，见众生死时、生时，妙色、恶色、下色、上色，向于恶趣、向于善趣、随业法受悉如实知：『此众生身恶业成就，口、意恶业成就，谤毁贤圣，受邪见业；以是因缘，身坏命终，堕恶趣，生地狱中。此众生身善行，口、意善行，不谤贤圣，正见业法受；彼因彼缘，身坏命终，生善趣天上。』悉如实知，是名第九如来力。若此力成就，如来、应、等正觉得先佛最胜处智，能转梵轮，于大众中师子吼而吼。

Furthermore, the Tathāgata's true knowledge of the destruction of the taints, with the mind liberated from taints, liberated by wisdom, and in this very life, he himself knows and realizes: 'My birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, there will be no more of this state.' This is called the tenth power of the Tathāgata. If one accomplishes this power, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, attains the supreme knowledge of the former Buddhas, is able to turn the wheel of the Dharma, and roars the lion's roar in the great assembly.

「复次，如来诸漏已尽，无漏心解脱、慧解脱，现法自知身作证：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』是名第十如来力。若此力成就，如来、应、等正觉得先佛最胜处智，能转梵轮，于大众中师子吼而吼。

"These ten powers are only accomplished by the Tathāgata. This is called the various distinctions between the Tathāgata and the śrāvakas."

「如此十力，唯如来成就，是名如来与声闻种种差别。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha had said and joyfully practiced it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
