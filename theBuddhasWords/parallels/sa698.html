SĀ 698

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 698（六九八） 九力

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "There are nine powers. What are the nine? They are the power of faith, the power of diligence, the power of shame, the power of remorse, the power of mindfulness, the power of concentration, the power of wisdom, the power of counting, and the power of cultivation. What is the power of faith? It is to generate right faith in the Tathāgata, to penetrate deeply and be firm..." as explained above. "What is the power of diligence? It is the four right efforts..." as explained above. "What is the power of shame?..." as explained above. "What is the power of remorse?..." as explained above. "What is the power of mindfulness? It is to abide in contemplation of the body within the body..." as explained above. "What is the power of concentration? It is the four dhyānas. What is the power of wisdom? It is the four noble truths. What is the power of counting? It means that the noble disciples, in a quiet room or under a tree, practice as follows: 'Those who engage in evil deeds of body and speech will receive evil retribution in this life and the next world...'..." as explained above in detail. "What is the power of cultivation? It means to cultivate the four foundations of mindfulness..." as explained above.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「有九力。何等为九？谓信力、精进力、惭力、愧力、念力、定力、慧力、数力、修力。何等为信力？于如来所起正信心，深入坚固……」如上说。「何等为精进力？谓四正断……」如上说。「何等为惭力？……」如上说。「何等为愧力？……」如上说。「何等为念力？谓内身身观住……」如上说。「何等为定力？谓四禅。何等为慧力？谓四圣谛。何等为数力？谓圣弟子若于闲房、树下作如是学：『身、口恶行者于现法后世当受恶报……』」如上广说。「何等为修力？谓修四念处……」如前说。

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished what he had to say. The bhikṣus, having heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
