SĀ 715

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 715（七一五） 食

Parallel to: SN 46.2 / SN 46.51

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "There are five hindrances and seven factors of awakening. There is food and there is no food. I will now explain. Listen carefully and consider it well, I will explain it to you. It is like the body relying on food to stand, not not eating. In the same way, the five hindrances rely on food to stand, not not eating. What is the food for the hindrance of sensual desire? It is contact and the incorrect contemplation of it. It causes the arising of sensual desire that has not yet arisen, and it causes the increase of sensual desire that has already arisen. This is called the food for the hindrance of sensual desire.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「有五盖、七觉分，有食、无食，我今当说。谛听，善思，当为汝说。譬如身依食而立，非不食。如是五盖依于食而立，非不食。贪欲盖以何为食？谓触相，于彼不正思惟，未起贪欲令起，已起贪欲能令增广，是名欲爱盖之食。

What is the food for the hindrance of ill will? It is the aspect of obstruction, and the incorrect contemplation of it. It causes the arising of the hindrance of ill will that has not yet arisen, and it causes the increase of the hindrance of ill will that has already arisen. This is called the food for the hindrance of ill will.

「何等为瞋恚盖食？谓障碍相，于彼不正思惟，未起瞋恚盖令起，已起瞋恚盖能令增广，是名瞋恚盖食。

What is the food for the hindrance of sloth and torpor? There are five dharmas. What are the five? Weakness, lack of joy, yawning, overeating, and laziness. The incorrect contemplation of them causes the arising of the hindrance of sloth and torpor that has not yet arisen, and it causes the increase of the hindrance of sloth and torpor that has already arisen. This is called the food for the hindrance of sloth and torpor.

「何等为睡眠盖食？有五法。何等为五？微弱、不乐、欠呿、多食、懈怠，于彼不正思惟，未起睡眠盖令起，已起睡眠盖能令增广，是名睡眠盖食。

What is the food for the hindrance of agitation and remorse? There are four dharmas. What are the four? The perception of relatives, the perception of crowds, the perception of deities, and the perception of past amusements. One gives rise to perception by oneself or is caused by others to give rise to perception. The incorrect contemplation of them causes the arising of the hindrance of agitation and remorse that has not yet arisen, and it causes the increase of the hindrance of agitation and remorse that has already arisen. This is called the food for the hindrance of agitation and remorse.

「何等为掉悔盖食？有四法。何等为四？谓亲属觉、人众觉、天觉、本所经娱乐觉。自忆念、他人令忆念而生觉，于彼起不正思惟，未起掉悔令起，已起掉悔令其增广，是名掉悔盖食。

What is the food for the hindrance of doubt? There are three times. What are the three? The past time, the future time, and the present time. Doubt about the past time, doubt about the future time, and doubt about the present time. The incorrect contemplation of them causes the arising of the hindrance of doubt that has not yet arisen, and it causes the increase of the hindrance of doubt that has already arisen. This is called the food for the hindrance of doubt.

「何等为疑盖食？有三世。何等为三？谓过去世、未来世、现在世。于过去世犹豫、未来世犹豫、现在世犹豫，于彼起不正思惟，未起疑盖令起，已起疑盖能令增广，是名疑盖食。

It is like the body, which relies on food for growth and nourishment, not without food. In the same way, the seven factors of awakening rely on food for existence and nourishment, not without food.

「譬如身依于食而得长养，非不食。如是七觉分依食而住，依食长养，非不食。

What is the food for the factor of awakening of mindfulness? It is the non-contemplation of the four foundations of mindfulness. The factor of awakening of mindfulness that has not yet arisen does not arise, and the factor of awakening of mindfulness that has already arisen is made to regress. This is called the food for the factor of awakening of mindfulness.

「何等为念觉分不食？谓四念处不思惟，未起念觉分不起，已起念觉分令退，是名念觉分不食。

What is the food for the factor of awakening of investigating phenomena? It is the investigation of wholesome phenomena and the investigation of unwholesome phenomena. The non-contemplation of those causes the factor of awakening of investigating phenomena that has not yet arisen not to arise, and the factor of awakening of investigating phenomena that has already arisen to regress. This is called the food for the factor of awakening of investigating phenomena.

「何等为择法觉分不食？谓于善法撰择，于不善法撰择，于彼不思惟，未起择法觉分令不起，已起择法觉分令退，是名择法觉分不食。

What is the food for the factor of awakening of energy? It is the four right efforts. The non-contemplation of those causes the factor of awakening of energy that has not yet arisen not to arise, and the factor of awakening of energy that has already arisen to regress. This is called the food for the factor of awakening of energy.

「何等为精进觉分不食？谓四正断，于彼不思惟，未起精进觉分令不起，已起精进觉分令退，是名精进觉分不食。

What is the food for the factor of awakening of joy? There is joy, and there are phenomena that are the basis for joy. The non-contemplation of those causes the factor of awakening of joy that has not yet arisen not to arise, and the factor of awakening of joy that has already arisen to regress. This is called the food for the factor of awakening of joy.

「何等为喜觉分不食？有喜，有喜处法，于彼不思惟，未起喜觉分不起，已起喜觉分令退，是名喜觉分不食。

What is the food for the factor of awakening of tranquility? There is physical tranquility and mental tranquility. The non-contemplation of those causes the factor of awakening of tranquility that has not yet arisen not to arise, and the factor of awakening of tranquility that has already arisen to regress. This is called the food for the factor of awakening of tranquility.

「何等为猗觉分不食？有身猗息及心猗息，于彼不思惟，未生猗觉分不起，已生猗觉分令退，是名猗觉分不食。

What is the food for the factor of awakening of concentration? There are the four dhyānas. The non-contemplation of those causes the factor of awakening of concentration that has not yet arisen not to arise, and the factor of awakening of concentration that has already arisen to regress. This is called the food for the factor of awakening of concentration.

「何等为定觉分不食？有四禅，于彼不思惟，未起定觉分不起，已起定觉分令退，是名定觉分不食。

What is the food for the factor of awakening of equanimity? There are the three realms: the realm of cessation, the realm of dispassion, and the realm of extinction. The non-contemplation of those causes the factor of awakening of equanimity that has not yet arisen not to arise, and the factor of awakening of equanimity that has already arisen to regress. This is called the food for the factor of awakening of equanimity.

「何等为舍觉分不食？有三界，谓断界、无欲界、灭界，于彼不思惟，未起舍觉分不起，已起舍觉分令退，是名舍觉分不食。

What is the food for the hindrance of sensual desire? It is the contemplation of impurity. The contemplation of that causes the hindrance of sensual desire that has not yet arisen not to arise, and the hindrance of sensual desire that has already arisen to be eliminated. This is called the food for the hindrance of sensual desire.

「何等为贪欲盖不食？谓不净观，于彼思惟，未起贪欲盖不起，已起贪欲盖令断，是名贪欲盖不食。

What is the food for the hindrance of ill will? It is the contemplation of loving-kindness. The contemplation of that causes the hindrance of ill will that has not yet arisen not to arise, and the hindrance of ill will that has already arisen to be eliminated. This is called the food for the hindrance of ill will.

「何等为瞋恚盖不食？彼慈心思惟，未生瞋恚盖不起，已生瞋恚盖令灭，是名瞋恚盖不食。

What is the food for the hindrance of sloth and torpor? It is the contemplation of illumination. The contemplation of that causes the hindrance of sloth and torpor that has not yet arisen not to arise, and the hindrance of sloth and torpor that has already arisen to be eliminated. This is called the food for the hindrance of sloth and torpor.

「何等为睡眠盖不食？彼明照思惟，未生睡眠盖不起，已生睡眠盖令灭，是名睡眠盖不食。

What is the food for the hindrance of restlessness and remorse? It is the contemplation of tranquility. The contemplation of that causes the hindrance of restlessness and remorse that has not yet arisen not to arise, and the hindrance of restlessness and remorse that has already arisen to be eliminated. This is called the food for the hindrance of restlessness and remorse.

「何等为掉悔盖不食？彼寂止思惟，未生掉悔盖不起，已生掉悔盖令灭，是名掉悔盖不食。

What is the food for the hindrance of doubt? It is the contemplation of dependent origination. The contemplation of that causes the hindrance of doubt that has not yet arisen not to arise, and the hindrance of doubt that has already arisen to be eliminated. This is called the food for the hindrance of doubt. It is like the body that abides and stands by relying on food. In the same way, the seven factors of awakening abide and stand by relying on food.

「何等为疑盖不食？彼缘起法思惟，未生疑盖不起，已生疑盖令灭，是名疑盖不食。譬如身依食而住、依食而立；如是七觉分依食而住、依食而立。

What is the food for the factor of awakening of mindfulness? It is the contemplation of the four foundations of mindfulness. The contemplation of that causes the factor of awakening of mindfulness that has not yet arisen to arise, and the factor of awakening of mindfulness that has already arisen to increase and expand. This is called the food for the factor of awakening of mindfulness.

「何等为念觉分食？谓四念处思惟已，未生念觉分令起，已生念觉分转生令增广，是名念觉分食。

What is the food for the factor of awakening of investigation of phenomena? It is the investigation of good phenomena and the investigation of bad phenomena. The contemplation of that causes the factor of awakening of investigation of phenomena that has not yet arisen to arise, and the factor of awakening of investigation of phenomena that has already arisen to increase and expand. This is called the food for the factor of awakening of investigation of phenomena.

「何等为择法觉分食？有择善法，有择不善法，彼思惟已，未生择法觉分令起，已生择法觉分重生令增广，是名择法觉分食。

What is the food for the factor of awakening of energy? It is the contemplation of the four right efforts. The contemplation of that causes the factor of awakening of energy that has not yet arisen to arise, and the factor of awakening of energy that has already arisen to increase and expand. This is called the food for the factor of awakening of energy.

「何等为精进觉分食？彼四正断思惟，未生精进觉分令起，已生精进觉分重生令增广，是名精进觉分食。

What is the food for the factor of awakening of joy? It is joy and the basis of joy. The contemplation of that causes the factor of awakening of joy that has not yet arisen to arise, and the factor of awakening of joy that has already arisen to increase and expand. This is called the food for the factor of awakening of joy.

「何等为喜觉分食？有喜，有喜处，彼思惟，未生喜觉分令起，已生喜觉分重生令增广，是名喜觉分食。

What is the food for the factor of awakening of tranquility? It is the tranquility of body and mind. The contemplation of that causes the factor of awakening of tranquility that has not yet arisen to arise, and the factor of awakening of tranquility that has already arisen to increase and expand. This is called the food for the factor of awakening of tranquility.

「何等为猗觉分食，有身猗息、心猗息思惟，未生猗觉分令起，已生猗觉分重生令增广，是名猗觉分食。

What is the food for the factor of awakening of concentration? It is the four dhyānas. The contemplation of that causes the factor of awakening of concentration that has not yet arisen to arise, and the factor of awakening of concentration that has already arisen to increase and expand. This is called the food for the factor of awakening of concentration.

「何等为定觉分食？谓有四禅思惟，未生定觉分令生起，已生定觉分重生令增广，是名定觉分食。

What is the food for the factor of awakening of equanimity? There are three realms. What are the three? They are the realm of cessation, the realm of desirelessness, and the realm of extinction. The contemplation of that causes the factor of awakening of equanimity that has not yet arisen to arise, and the factor of awakening of equanimity that has already arisen to increase and expand. This is called the food for the factor of awakening of equanimity.

「何等为舍觉分食？有三界。何等三？谓断界、无欲界、灭界。彼思惟，未生舍觉分令起，已生舍觉分重生令增广，是名舍觉分食。」

After the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus who heard what the Buddha taught rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
