SĀ 722

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 722（七二二） 转轮王

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At one time, the World-Honored One addressed the monks, saying, "When a wheel-turning sage-king appears in the world, seven treasures appear in the world. How does the golden wheel treasure appear when a wheel-turning sage-king appears in the world? At that time, when a king of the kṣatriya caste who has been consecrated ascends to the top of a pavilion on the fifteenth day of the month, bathed and purified, observing the precepts, surrounded by his ministers, the golden wheel treasure appears from the east. The wheel has a thousand spokes, a complete hub and rim, with the marks of a wheel complete, made of heavenly true gold. It is said in ancient records that when a king of the kṣatriya caste who has been consecrated observes the precepts on the fifteenth day of the month, bathed and purified, the wheel treasure appears. 'Welcome, great king! This is your kingdom. This country is peaceful and the people are prosperous. Please stay here to teach and transform the people of the country. I will follow you.' The sage-king replies, 'Leaders of the villages! You should now only govern the people well. If there are those who do not comply, come and inform me. I will transform according to the Dharma, not unrighteously. Also, let the people of the country transform unrighteousness well. If it is like this, then you follow my transformation.' Then the sage-king crosses the eastern sea, following the path of the ancient sage-kings, to the southern sea. Having reached the southern sea, he crosses the southern sea to the western sea. Following the path of the ancient sage-kings, he crosses the western sea to the northern sea. The kings of the small countries in the south, west, and north welcome and invite him, also as extensively explained in the east. Then the golden wheel treasure follows the sage-king. Having crossed the northern sea, he returns to the palace of the king, stands in the sky above the palace, and this is called the appearance of the golden wheel treasure in the world when a wheel-turning sage-king appears in the world.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「转轮圣王出于世时，有七宝现于世间。云何转轮圣王出于世时，金轮宝现有？时，刹利灌顶圣王，月十五日，沐浴清净，受持斋戒，于楼阁上，大臣围遶。有金轮宝从东方出，轮有千辐，齐毂圆辋，轮相具足，天真金宝。古昔传闻，刹利灌顶大王月十五日布萨时，沐浴清净，受持福善。善来大王，此是王国，此国安隐，人民丰乐，愿于中止，教化国人，我则随从。圣王答言：『诸聚落主！汝今但当善化国人，有不顺者，当来白我，当如法化，莫作非法，亦令国人善化非法，若如是者，则从我化。』于是圣王从东海度，乘古圣王道，至于南海；至于南海，度于南海，至西海；乘于古昔圣王之道，度于西海，至于北海。南、西、北方诸小国王奉迎启请，亦如东方广说。于是金轮宝，圣王随从，度于北海，还至王宫正治殿上，住虚空中，是为转轮圣王出兴于世，金轮宝现于世间。

How does the white elephant treasure appear when a wheel-turning sage-king appears in the world? If a king of the kṣatriya caste who has been consecrated sees a pure-colored elephant, its color fresh and good, with seven limbs touching the ground, he is delighted in his mind. He says, 'This treasure elephant has come to me.' He tells the skilled elephant trainer to quickly train this treasure elephant. After training, bring it here.' The elephant trainer receives the order. In less than a day, the elephant is tamed. All the marks of taming are complete, just like other elephants that have been trained for years. Now this elephant treasure is tamed in one day, also like this. After training, it is brought to the king, who says to the great king, 'This elephant is already tamed. The king himself knows the time.' At that time, the sage-king observes that the marks of taming of this elephant are complete. He then rides the treasure elephant. In the morning, he travels around the four seas. At noon, he returns to the king's palace. This is called the appearance of the elephant treasure in the world when a wheel-turning sage-king appears in the world.

「云何为转轮圣王出兴于世，白象宝现于世间？若刹利灌顶大王纯色之象，其色鲜好，七支拄地，圣王见已，心则欣悦，今此宝象，来应于我，告善调象师，令速调此宝象，调已送来。象师受命，不盈一日，象即调伏，一切调伏相悉皆具足，犹如余象经年调者，今此象宝一日调伏亦复如是。调已送诣王所，上白大王：『此象已调，唯王自知时。』尔时，圣王观察此象调相已备，即乘宝象，于晨旦时周行四海，至日中时还归王宫，是名转轮圣王出兴于世，如此象宝现于世间。

How does the horse treasure appear when a wheel-turning sage-king appears in the world? The horse treasure of a wheel-turning sage-king is pure blue in color, with a glossy tail. When the sage-king sees the horse, he is delighted in his mind. He says, 'This divine horse has come to me.' He gives it to the horse trainer, telling him to quickly train it. After training, bring it here.' The horse trainer receives the order. In less than a day, the horse is tamed. It is just like other horses that have been trained for years. The horse treasure is also tamed like this. Knowing that the horse is tamed, he brings it back to the king and says, 'Great king! This horse is already tamed.' At that time, the sage-king observes that the marks of taming of the treasure horse are complete. He then rides the treasure horse in the morning. He travels around the four seas. At noon, he returns to the king's palace. This is called the appearance of the horse treasure in the world when a wheel-turning sage-king appears in the world.

「何等为转轮圣王出兴于世，马宝现于世间？转轮圣王所有马宝纯一青色，乌头泽尾，圣王见马，心生欣悦，今此神马来应我故，付调马师，令速调之，调已送来。马师奉教，不盈一日，其马即调，犹如余马经年调者，马宝调伏亦复如是。知马调已，还送奉王，白言：『大王！此马已调。』尔时，圣王观察宝马调相已备，于晨旦时乘此宝马周行四海，至日中时还归王宫，是名转轮圣王出兴于世，马宝现于世间。

How does the jewel treasure appear when a wheel-turning sage-king appears in the world? The jewel treasure of a wheel-turning sage-king is eight-faceted in shape, with a lustrous light that shines without any flaws. It always serves as a lamp in the king's palace. The wheel-turning sage-king examines the jewel. On a rainy night, he takes the four divisions of troops into the garden. Holding the jewel in front, the light shines brightly for one yojana. This is called the appearance of the jewel treasure in the world when a wheel-turning sage-king appears in the world.

「何等为转轮圣王出兴于世，摩尼珠宝现于世间？若转轮圣王所有宝珠，其形八楞，光泽明照，无诸类隙，于王宫内，常为灯明。转轮圣王察试宝珠，阴雨之夜，将四种兵入于园林，持珠前导，光明照耀，面一由旬，是为转轮圣王出兴于世，摩尼宝珠现于世间。

How does the jewel woman treasure appear when a wheel-turning sage-king appears in the world? The jewel woman treasure of a wheel-turning sage-king is neither black nor white, neither tall nor short, neither coarse nor fine, neither fat nor thin. Her limbs are upright and proper. When cold, her body is warm. When hot, her body is cool. Her body is soft, like a kālinga garment. The pores of her body emit the fragrance of sandalwood. The breath from her mouth and nose has the fragrance of blue lotus flowers. She sleeps later and wakes earlier. She looks at the king's facial expression and serves him appropriately. She speaks softly and lovingly. She focuses her mind and thoughts, giving rise to the king's way of thinking. Her mind is without transgression, let alone her body and speech. This is the jewel woman treasure of a wheel-turning sage-king.

「何等为转轮圣王出兴于世，贤玉女宝现于世间？转轮圣王所有玉女，不黑不白、不长不短、不麁不细、不肥不瘦，支体端正，寒时体暖、热时体凉，身体柔软如迦陵伽衣，身诸毛孔，出栴檀香，口鼻出息，作优钵罗香。后卧先起，瞻王意色，随宜奉事，软言爱语，端心正念，发王道意，心无违越，况复身、口，是为转轮圣王宝女。

How does the jewel minister treasure appear when a wheel-turning sage-king appears in the world? The jewel minister treasure of a wheel-turning sage-king is originally born with the heavenly eye due to practicing giving. He can see hidden treasures, whether they have owners or not, whether they are in water or on land, whether they are far or near. When the wheel-turning sage-king needs precious treasures, he immediately orders them. Whatever the king needs, he immediately offers them. Sometimes the sage-king tests that great minister to see his abilities. Riding a boat on the ocean, he tells the great minister, 'I need precious treasures.' The minister says to the king, 'Please wait by the shore for a moment, and I will offer them.' The king tells the minister, 'I do not need treasures from the shore now. Just give them all to me.' Then the great minister takes out four golden jars full of gold and precious treasures from the water and offers them to the sage-king. The king takes what he needs and uses it. When he has taken enough, the rest returns to the water. When a sage-king appears in the world, such a great minister appears in the world.

「云何为转轮圣王主藏臣宝现于世间？谓转轮圣王主藏大臣本行施故，生得天眼，能见伏藏，有主无主、若水若陆、若远若近，悉能见之，转轮圣王须珍宝，即便告勅，随王所须，辄以奉上。于是圣王有时试彼大臣，观其所能，乘船游海，告彼大臣：『我须宝物。』臣白王言：『小住岸边，当以奉上。』王告彼臣：『我今不须岸边之宝，且尽与我。』于是大臣即于水中出四金瓮，金宝满中，以奉圣王，王所须即取用之，若取足已，余则还归水中。圣王出世，则有如此主藏之臣现于世间。

How does the jewel general treasure appear when a wheel-turning sage-king appears in the world? The jewel general treasure of a wheel-turning sage-king is wise and eloquent. It is like the best planner in the world. Whatever the sage-king needs, he does it. Whether it is appropriate to go, stay, go out, or enter, the sage-king's four kinds of troops travel and rest along the way without being tired. He knows all the appropriate things for the sage-king to do, the merits of the present and future lives, and reports them to the sage-king. When a wheel-turning sage-king appears in the world, there is such a jewel general treasure.

「云何圣王出兴于世，有主兵之臣现于世间？谓有主兵臣聪明智辩。譬如世间善思量成就者，圣王所宜，彼则悉从，宜去、宜住、宜出、宜入。圣王四种兵行，道里顿止，不令疲勌，悉知圣王宜所应作，现法后世功德之事，以白圣王。转轮圣王出兴于世，有如是主兵之臣。

"Thus, when a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One appears in the world, the seven factors of enlightenment appear in the world. What are the seven? They are the factors of enlightenment of mindfulness, discrimination of phenomena, effort, joy, tranquility, concentration, and equanimity."

「如是如来、应、等正觉出兴于世，有七觉分现于世间。何等为七？谓念觉分、择法觉分、精进觉分、喜觉分、猗觉分、定觉分、舍觉分。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully practiced it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
