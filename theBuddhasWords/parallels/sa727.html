SĀ 727

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 727（七二七） 拘夷那竭

Parallel to: SN 46.16 / EĀ 39.6 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling among the people in the country of the wrestlers, staying between the city of Kuśinagara and the Hirannyavati River, at the side of a village. He told Venerable Ānanda, "Fold the Buddha's upper robe into four layers and spread it out. I have a backache and want to lie down and rest for a while."

一时，佛在力士聚落人间游行，于拘夷那竭城希连河中间住，于聚落侧告尊者阿难：「令四重襞叠敷世尊欝多罗僧，我今背疾，欲小卧息。」

Venerable Ānanda immediately followed the instruction, folded the upper robe into four layers and spread it out, then said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! The upper robe has been folded into four layers and spread out. The World-Honored One knows the time."

尊者阿难即受教勅，四重襞叠敷欝多罗僧已，白佛言：「世尊！已四重襞叠敷欝多罗僧，唯世尊知时。」

At that time, the World-Honored One folded his saṃghāṭī robe thickly, used it as a pillow, lay down on his right side, placed one foot on the other, focused his mind on the appearance of dawn, with right mindfulness and right knowledge, and thought of getting up. He told Venerable Ānanda, "You should explain the seven factors of awakening."

尔时，世尊厚襞僧伽梨枕头，右脇而卧，足足相累，系念明相，正念正智，作起觉想，告尊者阿难：「汝说七觉分。」

At that time, Venerable Ānanda said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! The factor of awakening of mindfulness, the World-Honored One himself has realized supreme perfect enlightenment and teaches it based on detachment, based on dispassion, based on cessation, and directed towards relinquishment. The factors of awakening of investigation of phenomena, energy, joy, tranquility, concentration, and equanimity, the World-Honored One himself has realized supreme perfect enlightenment and teaches them based on detachment, based on dispassion, based on cessation, and directed towards relinquishment."

时，尊者阿难即白佛言：「世尊！所谓念觉分，世尊自觉成等正觉，说依远离、依无欲、依灭、向于舍，择法、精进、喜、猗、定、舍觉分，世尊自觉成等正觉，说依远离、依无欲、依灭、向于舍。」

The Buddha told Ānanda, "You should explain energy."

佛告阿难：「汝说精进耶？」

Ānanda said to the Buddha, "I explain energy. World-Honored One! I explain energy. Well-Gone One!"

阿难白佛：「我说精进。世尊！说精进。善逝！」

The Buddha told Ānanda, "Only by cultivating and extensively cultivating energy can one attain supreme, perfect enlightenment." Having spoken these words, he sat upright with a focused mind.

佛告阿难：「唯精进，修习多修习，得阿耨多罗三藐三菩提。」说是语已，正坐端身系念。

At that time, a certain bhikṣu spoke a verse, saying:

时，有异比丘即说偈言：

Delighting in hearing the wonderful Dharma, I endured and quickly told others
The bhikṣu then spoke the Dharma, turning the seven factors of awakening
Excellent, Venerable Ānanda, clearly understanding and skillfully explaining, there are superior pure
dharmas, explained in detail and subtly. Mindfulness, discernment of dharmas, diligence, joy
tranquility, concentration, and equanimity - these are the seven factors of awakening
explained in detail and subtly. Having heard the explanation of the seven
factors of awakening, deeply understanding the taste of true awakening, even though
my body was afflicted with great suffering, I endured and sat upright
to listen. Observing the Dharma King, who always expounded for people, I
still delighted in hearing what was said, let alone those who had
not heard it before. The foremost great wisdom, revered by the Ten
Powers, they too should quickly come to listen to the explanation of
the true Dharma. Those who are learned and have penetrated the sūtras
and abhidharma, who are skilled in the Dharma and Vinaya, should listen
let alone others. Having heard the explanation of the true Dharma, listening
with a focused and wise mind, one attains the joy of detachment
from the Dharma explained by the Buddha. With joy, the body rests
in tranquility, and the mind is naturally joyful as well. With mental
joy, one attains right concentration, and with right observation, one engages in
practice. Those who are disgusted with the three realms, with minds liberated
from desire, disgusted with all realms of existence, not accumulating in the
human or heavenly realms, without remainder, like a lamp going out, attain
ultimate parinirvāṇa. The many benefits of hearing the Dharma are explained by
the supreme one. Therefore, one should concentrate and listen to what the
great teacher says.

「乐闻美妙法，　　忍疾告人说，比丘即说法，　　转于七觉分。善哉尊阿难，　　明解巧便说，有胜白净法，　　离垢微妙说。念、择法、精进、　　喜、猗、定、舍觉，此则七觉分，　　微妙之善说。闻说七觉分，　　深达正觉味，身婴大苦患，　　忍疾端坐听，观为正法王，　　常为人演说，犹乐闻所说，　　况余未闻者，第一大智慧，　　十力所礼者，彼亦应疾疾，　　来听说正法，诸多闻通达，　　契经阿毘昙，善通法律者，　　应听况余者，闻说如实法，　　专心黠慧听，于佛所说法，　　得离欲欢喜，欢喜身猗息，　　心自乐亦然，心乐得正受，　　正观有事行，厌恶三趣者，　　离欲心解脱，厌恶诸有趣，　　不集于人天，无余犹灯灭，　　究竟般涅槃，闻法多福利，　　最胜之所说，是故当专思，　　听大师所说。」

After the bhikṣu spoke this verse, he rose from his seat and left.

异比丘说此偈已，从座起而去。
