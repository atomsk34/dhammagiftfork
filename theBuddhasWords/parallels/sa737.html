SĀ 737

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 737（七三七） 七道品

Parallel to: SN 46.5 (~) / SĀ 733 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "What is called the factors of awakening? What are the factors of awakening?"

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「所谓觉分。何等为觉分？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "The World-Honored One is the root of the Dharma, the eye of the Dharma, and the reliance of the Dharma. We only wish that you would explain it. Having heard it, the bhikṣus will accept and uphold it."

诸比丘白佛：「世尊是法根、法眼、法依，唯愿为说。诸比丘闻已，当受奉行。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus and bhikṣuṇīs, "The seven factors of awakening refer to the seven factors of the path. Bhikṣus! These seven factors of awakening arise gradually, and having arisen gradually, they are cultivated to perfection."

佛告诸比丘、比丘尼：「七觉分者，谓七道品法。诸比丘！此七觉分渐次起，渐次起已，修习满足。」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "How do the seven factors of awakening arise gradually, and having arisen gradually, are they cultivated to perfection?"

诸比丘白佛：「云何七觉分渐次起，渐次起已，修习满足？

"If a bhikṣu abides in mindfulness of the body observing the body, having abided in mindfulness of the body observing the body, with focused mind, he should at that time cultivate the factor of awakening of mindfulness. Having cultivated the factor of awakening of mindfulness, he should cultivate it to perfection. That is to say, having cultivated the factor of awakening of mindfulness, he should at that time cultivate the factor of awakening of discrimination of phenomena. Having cultivated the factor of awakening of discrimination of phenomena, he should cultivate it to perfection. The same is said for the factors of awakening of energy, joy, tranquility, concentration, and equanimity. As with the internal body, so with the external body, the internal and external body, feeling, mind, and phenomena. Abiding in mindfulness observing phenomena, with focused mind, he should at that time cultivate the factor of awakening of mindfulness. Having cultivated the factor of awakening of mindfulness, he should cultivate it to perfection. Up to the factor of awakening of equanimity is also said to be the same. This is called the gradual arising of the seven factors of awakening of a bhikṣu, and having arisen gradually, they are cultivated to perfection."

「若比丘身身观念住，彼身身观念住已，专心系念不忘，当于尔时方便修念觉分；方便修念觉分已，修习满足，谓修念觉分已，于法选择，当于尔时修择法觉分方便，修择法觉分方便已，修习满足。如是精进、喜、猗、定、舍觉分亦如是说。如内身，如是外身、内外身，受、心、法法观念住，专心系念不忘，当于尔时方便修念觉分，方便修念觉分已，修习满足。乃至舍觉分亦如是说，是名比丘七觉分渐次起，渐次起已，修习满足。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha had said and joyfully practiced it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
