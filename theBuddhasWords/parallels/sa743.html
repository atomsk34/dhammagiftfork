SĀ 743

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 743（七四三） 慈

Parallel to: SN 46.54

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying in the village of Huangchen, in the land of the Shakyas. In the morning, many monks put on their robes, took their bowls, and entered the village of Huangchen to beg for alms.

一时，佛住释氏黄枕邑。时，众多比丘晨朝著衣持钵，入黄枕邑乞食。

At that time, many monks thought, "It is too early today, and it is not yet time to beg for alms. Let us go to the hermitage of the non-Buddhist ascetics." Then, many monks entered the hermitage of the non-Buddhist ascetics and exchanged greetings and pleasantries with the non-Buddhist ascetics. They sat to one side.

时，众多比丘作是念：「今日太早，乞食时未至，我等可过外道精舍。」尔时，众多比丘即入外道精舍，与诸外道出家共相问讯慰劳已，于一面坐。

The non-Buddhist ascetics said, "Does the Shakyamuni monk teach his disciples in this way: 'Abandon the five hindrances, which afflict the mind, weaken wisdom, obstruct the path, and do not lead to nirvana. Fully concentrate the mind on the four foundations of mindfulness. With a mind filled with loving-kindness, free from enmity, jealousy, and anger, vast, immeasurable, well-cultivated, and fully developed, extend it to the four directions, the intermediate directions, above, below, and all around. With a mind filled with loving-kindness, free from enmity, jealousy, and anger, vast, immeasurable, well-cultivated, and fully developed, extend it to all worlds. In the same way, cultivate compassion, joy, and equanimity.' We also teach our disciples in this way. What is the difference between us and that Shakyamuni monk? We are both able to teach the Dharma.

诸外道出家言：「沙门瞿昙为诸弟子说如是法不：『断五盖恼心，慧力羸、为障碍分、不趣涅槃，尽摄其心，住四念处。心与慈俱，无怨无嫉，亦无瞋恚，广大无量，善修充满；四方、四维、上、下一切世间，心与慈俱，无怨无嫉，亦无瞋恚，广大无量，善修习充满，如是修习。』悲、喜、舍心俱亦如是说。我等亦复为诸弟子作如是说，我等与彼沙门瞿昙有何等异？所谓俱能说法。」

At that time, many monks heard what the non-Buddhist ascetics said. They were displeased but did not rebuke them. They rose from their seats and left. They entered the city of Huangchen to beg for alms. After returning to the monastery, they put away their robes and bowls, washed their feet, went to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at his feet, and sat to one side. They reported in detail to the World-Honored One what the non-Buddhist ascetics had said.

时，众多比丘闻诸外道出家所说，心不喜悦，默然不呵，从座起去。入黄枕邑，乞食已，还精舍，举衣钵，洗足已，诣佛所，稽首礼足，退坐一面。以彼外道出家所说广白世尊。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the monks, "You should ask those non-Buddhist ascetics, 'What is the superiority of cultivating a mind filled with loving-kindness? What is the superiority of cultivating compassion, joy, and equanimity?' When asked this, those non-Buddhist ascetics will be startled and scattered. They may speak of external matters, or they may be angry, arrogant, denigrating, disobedient, and unable to bear it. They may be silent, wilting, with lowered heads, losing their eloquence, and dwelling in thought. Why is this? I do not see any gods, demons, Brahmas, monks, Brahmins, gods, or humans in the assembly who, upon hearing my teachings, would joyfully follow them, except for the Tathāgata and his disciples. Monks! Cultivating a mind filled with loving-kindness is the most supreme in purity; cultivating compassion is the most supreme in the sphere of emptiness; cultivating joy is the most supreme in the sphere of consciousness; cultivating equanimity is the most supreme in the sphere of nothingness.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「如彼外道出家所说，汝等应问：『修习慈心，为何所胜？修习悲、喜、舍心，为何所胜？』如是问时，彼诸外道出家，心则骇散，或说外异事，或瞋慢、毁呰、违背、不忍，或默然萎熟，低头失辩，思惟而住。所以者何？我不见诸天、魔、梵、沙门、婆罗门、天、人众中，闻我所说随顺乐者，唯除如来及声闻众者。比丘！心与慈俱多修习，于净最胜；悲心修习多修习，空入处最胜；喜心修习多修习，识入处最胜；舍心修习多修习，无所有入处最胜。」

After the Buddha had spoken this sutra, the monks who heard what the Buddha had said rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
