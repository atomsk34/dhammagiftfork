SĀ 758

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 758（七五八） 三法

Parallel to: AN 3.62

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "There is fear of mother and child, and there is fear without mother and child. The foolish and ignorant common people speak of it, but they do not know the fear without mother and child, and the fear with mother and child.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「无母子畏，有母子畏。愚痴无闻凡夫所说，而不能知无母子畏，有母子畏。

Bhikṣus! There are three kinds of fear without mother and child, spoken of by the foolish and ignorant common people. What are the three? Bhikṣus! Sometimes there is a violent rebellion, causing harm to the country. The waves of the rebellion scatter everywhere, and children lose their mothers, and mothers lose their children. This is called the first kind of fear without mother and child, spoken of by the foolish and ignorant common people.

「诸比丘！有三种无母子畏，愚痴无闻凡夫所说。何等为三？诸比丘！有时兵凶乱起，残害国土，随流波迸，子失其母，母失其子，是名第一无母子畏，愚痴无闻凡夫所说。

Furthermore, bhikṣus! Sometimes a great fire suddenly breaks out, burning down cities, towns, and villages. The people flee in all directions, and mothers and children lose each other. This is called the second kind of fear without mother and child, spoken of by the foolish and ignorant common people.

「复次，比丘！有时大火卒起，焚烧城邑聚落，人民驰走，母子相失，是名第二无母子畏，愚痴无闻凡夫所说。

Furthermore, bhikṣus! Sometimes there is heavy rain in the mountains, and the floodwaters flow out, sweeping away the villages. The people flee in all directions, and mothers and children lose each other. This is called the third kind of fear without mother and child, spoken of by the foolish and ignorant common people.

「复次，比丘！有时山中大雨，洪水流出，漂没聚落，人民驰走，母子相失，是名第三无母子畏，愚痴无闻凡夫所说。

However, these fears are fears with mother and child, but the foolish and ignorant common people call them fears without mother and child. Sometimes there is a violent uprising, causing harm to the country, and the people flee in all directions, losing each other. Sometimes they may see each other again. This is called the first kind of fear with mother and child, but the foolish and ignorant common people call it a fear without mother and child.

「然此等畏，是有母子畏，愚痴无闻凡夫说名无母子畏。彼有时兵凶乱起，残害国土，随流波迸，母子相失，或时于彼母子相见，是名第一有母子畏，愚痴无闻凡夫说名无母子畏。

Furthermore, a great fire suddenly breaks out, burning the cities, towns, and villages. The people flee in all directions, losing each other, but sometimes they may see each other again. This is called the second kind of fear with mother and child, but the foolish and ignorant common people call it a fear without mother and child.

「复次，大火卒起，焚烧城邑聚落，人民驰走，母子相失，或复相见，是名第二有母子畏，愚痴无闻凡夫说名无母子畏。

Furthermore, there is heavy rain in the mountains, causing floods that sweep away the villages. The people flee in all directions, losing each other, but sometimes they may find each other again. This is called the third kind of fear with mother and child, but the foolish and ignorant common people call it a fear without mother and child.

「复次，山中大雨，洪水流出，漂没聚落，此人驰走，母子相失，或寻相见，是名第三有母子畏，愚痴无闻凡夫说名无母子畏。

Bhikkhus! There are three kinds of fear without mother and child, which I have personally realized and attained to the supreme enlightenment and declared. What are the three? If a son is old, there is no mother who can say to him: 'Son! Do not be old. I will take your place.' When the mother is old, there is also no son who can say: 'Mother! Do not be old. I will take your place.' This is called the first kind of fear without mother and child, which I have personally realized and attained to the supreme enlightenment and declared.

「比丘！有三种无母子畏，是我自觉成三菩提之所记说。何等为三？若比丘！子若老时，无母能语：『子！汝莫老，我当代汝。』其母老时，亦无子语：『母！令莫老，我代之老。』是名第一无母子畏，我自觉成三菩提之所记说。

Furthermore, bhikkhus! Sometimes when a son is sick, the mother cannot say to him: 'Son! Do not be sick. I will take your place.' When the mother is sick, the son also cannot say: 'Mother! Do not be sick. I will take your place.' This is called the second kind of fear without mother and child, which I have personally realized and attained to the supreme enlightenment and declared.

「复次，比丘！有时子病，母不能语：『子！令莫病，我当代汝。』母病之时，子亦不能语：『母！莫病，我当代母。』是名第二无母子畏，我自觉成三菩提之所记说。

Furthermore, when a son dies, there is no mother who can say: 'Son! Do not die. I will take your place.' When the mother dies, there is no son who can say: 'Mother! Do not die. I will take your place.' This is called the third kind of fear without mother and child, which I have personally realized and attained to the supreme enlightenment and declared.

「复次，子若死时，无母能语：『子！令莫死，我今代汝。』母若死时，无子能语：『母！令莫死，我当代母。』是名第三无母子畏，我自觉成三菩提之所记说。」

The bhikkhus asked the Buddha: "Is there a path and a way to cultivate and extensively cultivate, to eliminate the first three kinds of fear with mother and child, and to eliminate the last three kinds of fear without mother and child?"

诸比丘白佛：「有道有迹，修习多修习，断前三种有母子畏，断后三种无母子畏不？」

The Buddha told the bhikkhus: "There is a path and a way to eliminate those three fears. What is the path? What is the way, to cultivate and extensively cultivate, to eliminate the first three kinds of fear with mother and child, and to eliminate the last three kinds of fear without mother and child? It is the eightfold noble path, namely right view, right intention, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right concentration."

佛告比丘：「有道有迹，断彼三畏。何等为道。何等为迹，修习多修习，断前三种有母子畏，断后三种无母子畏？谓八圣道分，正见、正志、正语、正业、正命、正方便、正念、正定。」

After the Buddha spoke this sutra, the bhikkhus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully practiced it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
