SĀ 788

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 788（七八八） 向邪

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Those who go astray go against the Dharma, do not delight in the Dharma. Those who go in the right direction delight in the Dharma, do not go against the Dharma.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「向邪者违于法，不乐于法，向正者乐于法，不违于法。

What is meant by those who go astray going against the Dharma and not delighting in the Dharma? If a person with wrong views acts with the body and speaks with the mouth according to their views, if they think, desire, wish, do, or follow, they will all obtain unloved, unmindful, and undesirable results. Why is this? Because of wrong views. Wrong views give rise to wrong intentions, wrong speech, wrong actions, wrong livelihood, wrong effort, wrong mindfulness, and wrong concentration. It is like a bitter fruit planted in the ground, watered in a timely manner, and obtaining the flavors of earth, water, fire, and wind, all of which are bitter. Why is this? Because the seed is bitter. In the same way, a person with wrong views acts with the body and speaks with the mouth according to their views, and if they think, desire, wish, do, or follow, they will all obtain unloved, unmindful, and undesirable results. Why is this? Because of wrong views. Wrong views can give rise to wrong intentions, up to wrong concentration. This is called those who go astray going against the Dharma and not delighting in the Dharma.

「何等为向邪者违于法，不乐于法？若邪见人身业如所见，口业如所见，若思、若欲、若愿、若为、彼皆随顺，一切得不爱果，不念、不可意果。所以者何？恶见？谓邪见。邪见者，起邪志、邪语、邪业、邪命、邪方便、邪念、邪定。譬如苦果，种著地中，随时溉灌，彼得地味、水味、火味、风味，一切悉苦。所以者何？以种苦故。如是邪见人，身业如所见，口业如所见，若思、若欲、若愿、若为，悉皆随顺，彼一切得不爱、不念、不可意果。所以者何？恶见者？谓邪见。邪见者，能起邪志，乃至邪定，是名向邪者违于法，不乐于法。

What is meant by those who go in the right direction delighting in the Dharma and not going against the Dharma? If a person with right views acts with the body and speaks with the mouth according to their views, if they think, desire, wish, do, or follow, they will all obtain lovable, mindful, and agreeable results. Why is this? Because of right views. Right views can give rise to right intentions, up to right concentration. It is like planting sugarcane, rice, wheat, or grapes in the ground, watering them in a timely manner, and obtaining the flavors of earth, water, fire, and wind, all of which are sweet and delicious. Why is this? Because the seeds are sweet. In the same way, a person with right views acts with the body and speaks with the mouth according to their views, and if they think, desire, wish, do, or follow, they will all obtain lovable, mindful, and agreeable results. Why is this? Because of right views. Right views can give rise to right intentions, up to right concentration. This is called those who go in the right direction delighting in the Dharma and not going against the Dharma.

「何等为向正者乐于法，不违于法？若正见人身业如所见，口业如所见，若思、若欲、若为，悉皆随顺，彼一切得可爱、可念、可意果。所以者何？善见谓正见，正见者能起正志，乃至正定。譬如甘蔗、稻、麦、蒲桃种著地中，随时溉灌，彼得地味、水味、火味、风味，彼一切味悉甜美。所以者何？以种子甜故。如是正见人，身业如所见，口业如所见，若思、若欲、若愿、若为，悉皆随顺，彼一切得可爱、可念、可意果。所以者何？善见者？谓正见。正见者，能起正志，乃至正定，是名向正者乐于法，不违于法。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully practiced it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。

The same can be said for the mundane and supramundane. As in the above three sūtras, it is also said in verse:

世间、出世间亦如是说，如上三经，亦皆说偈言：

The inferior Dharma should not be approached, and indulgence should not
be practiced. One should not cultivate wrong views, which increase the
world. Even if there is a world, those with superior right
views, though reborn hundreds of thousands of times, will never fall
into evil destinies.

「鄙法不应近，　　放逸不应行，不应习邪见，　　增长于世间，假使有世间，　　正见增上者，虽复百千生，　　终不堕恶趣。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha had said and joyfully upheld it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
