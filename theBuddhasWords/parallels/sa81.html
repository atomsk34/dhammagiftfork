SĀ 81

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 81（八一） 富兰那

Parallel to: SN 22.60

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying at the Monkey Pond in Vaiśālī, in a two-storied lecture hall.

一时，佛住毘耶离猕猴池侧重阁讲堂。

At that time, there was a Licchavi named Mahānāma who would go to visit the Buddha every day. He thought, "If I go to see the World-Honored One too early, the World-Honored One and the bhikṣus I know will all be in meditation. I should go to the place of the Ājīvika ascetic Sattapaṇṇi." He then went to the place where Pūraṇa Kāśyapa was staying.

尔时，有离车名摩诃男，日日游行，往诣佛所。时，彼离车作是念：「若我早诣世尊所者，世尊及我知识比丘皆悉禅思，我今当诣七庵罗树阿耆毘外道所。」即往诣彼富兰那迦叶住处。

At that time, Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, the leader of the non-Buddhist groups, was surrounded by five hundred non-Buddhists, loudly laughing and discussing worldly matters. When Pūraṇa Kāśyapa saw Mahānāma the Licchavi coming from afar, he told his followers to be quiet, saying, "Be silent! This Mahānāma the Licchavi is a disciple of the śramaṇa Gautama. He is a lay disciple of the śramaṇa Gautama, the foremost in Vaiśālī, who always enjoys tranquility and praises tranquility. Wherever he goes, it is a tranquil assembly. Therefore, you should all be tranquil."

时，富兰那迦叶——外道众主，与五百外道前后围遶，高声嬉戏，论说俗事。时，富兰那迦叶遥见离车摩诃男来，告其眷属，令寂静住：「汝等默然！是离车摩诃男是沙门瞿昙弟子，此是沙门瞿昙白衣弟子，毘耶离中最为上首，常乐静寂，赞叹寂静，彼所之诣寂静之众，是故汝等应当寂静。」

Then Mahānāma went to the assembly of Pūraṇa Kāśyapa and exchanged greetings with him. After the greetings, he sat to one side. Mahānāma said to Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, "I heard that you teach your disciples that sentient beings become defiled without cause or condition, and that sentient beings become pure without cause or condition. Is this true? Or is this just slander from outsiders? Is this something that worldly people discuss? Is it the Dharma or not the Dharma? Do worldly people discuss, debate, and criticize it?"

时，摩诃男诣彼众富兰那所，与富兰那共相问讯，相慰劳已，却坐一面。时，摩诃男语富兰那言：「我闻富兰那为诸弟子说法：『无因、无缘众生有垢，无因、无缘众生清净。』世有此论，汝为审有此，为是外人相毁之言？世人所撰，为是法、为非法，颇有世人共论、难问、嫌责以不？」

Pūraṇa Kāśyapa said, "This discussion is true, not false. I establish this discussion according to the Dharma. What I say is all in accordance with the Dharma. No worldly person has come to debate, question, or criticize it. Why is that? Mahānāma! I see and say like this: 'Sentient beings become defiled without cause or condition, and sentient beings become pure without cause or condition.'"

富兰那迦叶言：「实有此论，非世妄传。我立此论，是如法论，我说此法，皆是顺法，无有世人来共难问而呵责者。所以者何？摩诃男！我如是见、如是说：『无因、无缘众生有垢，无因、无缘众生清净。』」

Then Mahānāma, hearing Pūraṇa Kāśyapa's words, was displeased. After rebuking him, he rose from his seat and left, going to the World-Honored One. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and told the Buddha in detail about the discussion with Pūraṇa Kāśyapa.

时，摩诃男闻富兰那所说，心不喜乐，呵骂已，从坐起去，向世尊所，头面礼足，却坐一面，以向与富兰那所论事，向佛广说。

The Buddha told the Licchavi Mahānāma, "Pūraṇa Kāśyapa's words are not worth recording. Pūraṇa Kāśyapa is foolish, not discerning, not skillful, and speaks without cause, saying, 'Sentient beings become defiled without cause or condition, and sentient beings become pure without cause or condition.' Why is that? Sentient beings become defiled with cause and condition, and sentient beings become pure with cause and condition.

佛告离车摩诃男：「彼富兰那为出意语，不足记也。如是富兰那愚痴，不辨、不善、非因而作是说：『无因、无缘众生有垢，无因、无缘众生清净。』所以者何？有因、有缘众生有垢，有因、有缘众生清净。

Mahānāma, what causes and conditions make sentient beings defiled? What causes and conditions make sentient beings pure? Mahānāma, if form were entirely suffering, not pleasure, not conducive to pleasure, not nourished by pleasure, and separate from pleasure, sentient beings would not become attached to it. Mahānāma, because form is not entirely suffering, it is pleasure, conducive to pleasure, nourished by pleasure, and not separate from pleasure. Therefore sentient beings become attached to form; because of attachment, there is bondage; because of bondage, there is affliction. Mahānāma, if feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness were entirely suffering, not pleasure, not conducive to pleasure, not nourished by pleasure, and separate from pleasure, sentient beings would not become attached to them. Mahānāma, because consciousness is not entirely suffering, it is pleasure, conducive to pleasure, nourished by pleasure, and not separate from pleasure. Therefore sentient beings become attached to consciousness; because of attachment, there is bondage; because of bondage, there is affliction. Mahānāma, this is called the causes and conditions for sentient beings to be defiled.

「摩诃男！何因、何缘众生有垢，何因、何缘众生清净？摩诃男！若色一向是苦，非乐、非随乐、非乐长养、离乐者，众生不应因此而生乐著。摩诃男！以色非一向是苦，是乐、随乐、乐所长养、不离乐，是故众生于色染著；染著故系，系故有恼。摩诃男！若受、想、行、识一向是苦，非乐、非随乐、非乐长养、离乐者，众生不应因此而生乐著。摩诃男！以识非一向是苦，是乐，随乐、乐所长养、不离乐，是故众生于识染著；染著故系，系故生恼。摩诃男！是名有因、有缘众生有垢。

Mahānāma, what causes and conditions make sentient beings pure? Mahānāma, if form were entirely pleasure, not suffering, not conducive to suffering, not nourished by suffering, and separate from suffering, sentient beings would not become disenchanted with form. Mahānāma, because form is not entirely pleasure, it is suffering, conducive to suffering, nourished by suffering, and not separate from suffering. Therefore sentient beings become disenchanted with form; because of disenchantment, there is no delight; because of no delight, there is liberation. Mahānāma, if feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness were entirely pleasure, not suffering, not conducive to suffering, not nourished by suffering, and separate from suffering, sentient beings would not become disenchanted with consciousness. Mahānāma, because feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness are not entirely pleasure, they are suffering, conducive to suffering, nourished by suffering, and not separate from suffering. Therefore sentient beings become disenchanted with consciousness; because of disenchantment, there is no delight; because of no delight, there is liberation. Mahānāma, this is called the causes and conditions for sentient beings to be pure.

「摩诃男！何因、何缘众生清净？摩诃男！若色一向是乐，非苦、非随苦、非忧苦长养、离苦者，众生不应因色而生厌离。摩诃男！以色非一向乐，是苦、随苦、忧苦长养、不离苦，是故众生厌离于色；厌故不乐，不乐故解脱。摩诃男！若受、想、行、识一向是乐，非苦、非随苦、非忧苦长养、离苦者，众生不应因识而生厌离。摩诃男！以受、想、行、识非一向乐，是苦、随苦、忧苦长养、不离苦，是故众生厌离于识；厌故不乐，不乐故解脱。摩诃男！是名有因、有缘众生清净。」

At that time, Mahānāma, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully rejoiced, paid homage to the Buddha, and departed.

时，摩诃男闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，礼佛而退。

knowing the Dharma and the heavy burden, going to, observing
desire and greed, birth, and brief explanation, Dharma seal, Pūraṇa

知法及重担　　往诣、观、欲贪生及与略说　　法印、富兰那
