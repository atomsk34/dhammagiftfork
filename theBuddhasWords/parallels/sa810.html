SĀ 810

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 810（八一〇） 金刚

Parallel to: SN 54.13

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Saralī Forest by the banks of the Vajra River.

一时，佛住金刚跋求摩河侧萨罗梨林中。

At that time, Venerable Ānanda was alone in a quiet place, contemplating in meditation, and had this thought: "Is there one Dharma that, when cultivated and practiced much, leads to the fulfillment of four dharmas; when the four dharmas are fulfilled, the seven dharmas are fulfilled; when the seven dharmas are fulfilled, the two dharmas are fulfilled?"

尔时，尊者阿难独一静处，思惟禅思，作如是念：「颇有一法，修习多修习，令四法满足；四法满足已，七法满足；七法满足已，二法满足？」

Then Venerable Ānanda, having awakened from meditation, went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha: "World-Honored One! I was alone in a quiet place, contemplating in meditation, and had this thought: 'Is there one Dharma that, when cultivated and practiced much, leads to the fulfillment of four dharmas, and so on, up to the fulfillment of two dharmas? I now ask the World-Honored One, is there one Dharma that, when cultivated and practiced much, can lead to the fulfillment of four dharmas, and so on, up to the fulfillment of two dharmas?'"

时，尊者阿难从禅觉已，往诣佛所，稽首礼足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！我独一静处，思惟禅思，作是念：『颇有一法，多修习已，令四法满足，乃至二法满足？我今问世尊，宁有一法，多修习已，能令，乃至二法满足耶？』」

The Buddha told Ānanda: "There is one Dharma that, when cultivated and practiced much, can lead to the fulfillment of two dharmas. What is the one Dharma? It is mindfulness of inhalation and exhalation. When cultivated and practiced much, it can lead to the fulfillment of the four foundations of mindfulness; when the four foundations of mindfulness are fulfilled, the seven factors of awakening are fulfilled; when the seven factors of awakening are fulfilled, clear comprehension and liberation are fulfilled. How does cultivating mindfulness of inhalation and exhalation lead to the fulfillment of the four foundations of mindfulness? This bhikṣu relies on a village, and so on, and practices mindfulness of inhalation and exhalation as if exhaling. Ānanda! In this way, the noble disciple, when practicing mindfulness of inhalation, practices mindfulness of inhalation; when practicing mindfulness of exhalation, practices mindfulness of exhalation; whether long or short, he is aware of all bodily activities, and when practicing mindfulness of inhalation, practices mindfulness of inhalation; when practicing mindfulness of exhalation, practices mindfulness of exhalation; when the bodily activities cease and he practices mindfulness of inhalation, he practices mindfulness of inhalation with the cessation of bodily activities; when the bodily activities cease and he practices mindfulness of exhalation, he practices mindfulness of exhalation with the cessation of bodily activities. At that time, the noble disciple abides in mindfulness of the body observing the body, and for those who are different from the body, he also contemplates the body in the same way.

佛告阿难：「有一法，多修习已，乃至能令二法满足。何等为一法？谓安那般那念。多修习已，能令四念处满足；四念处满足已，七觉分满足；七觉分满足已，明、解脱满足。云何修安那般那念，四念处满足？是比丘依止聚落，乃至如灭出息念学。阿难！如是圣弟子入息念时如入息念学，出息念时如出息念学；若长若短，一切身行觉知，入息念时如入息念学，出息念时如出息念学；身行休息入息念时，如身行休息入息念学；身行休息出息念时，如身行休息出息念学。圣弟子尔时身身观念住，异于身者，彼亦如是随身比思惟。

If at some time the noble disciple is aware of joy, pleasure, mental activity, and the cessation of mental activity, when practicing mindfulness of inhalation, he practices mindfulness of inhalation with the cessation of mental activity; when practicing mindfulness of exhalation, he practices mindfulness of exhalation with the cessation of mental activity. At that time, the noble disciple abides in mindfulness of feelings observing feelings, and for those who have different feelings, he also contemplates feelings in the same way.

「若有时圣弟子喜觉知，乐觉知，心行觉知，心行息觉知，入息念时如心行息入息念学，心行息出息念时如心行息出息念学。是圣弟子尔时受受观念住，若复异受者，彼亦受随身比思惟。有时圣弟子心觉知，心悦、心定、心解脱觉知，入息念时如入息念学，心解脱出息念时如心解脱出息念学，是圣弟子尔时心心观念住，若有异心者，彼亦随心比思惟。

"If at some time the noble disciple contemplates impermanence, cessation, dispassion, and cessation, practicing mindfulness of inhalation and exhalation as if contemplating impermanence, cessation, dispassion, and cessation, at that time the noble disciple abides in mindfulness of the Dharma observing the Dharma, and for those who are different from the Dharma, he also contemplates the Dharma in the same way. This is called cultivating mindfulness of inhalation and exhalation, fulfilling the four foundations of mindfulness."

「若圣弟子有时观无常、断、无欲、灭，如无常、断、无欲、灭观住学，是圣弟子尔时法法观念住异于法者，亦随法比思惟，是名修安那般那念，满足四念处。」

Ānanda addressed the Buddha, saying: "Thus cultivating mindfulness of inhalation and exhalation fulfills the four foundations of mindfulness. How does cultivating the four foundations of mindfulness fulfill the seven factors of awakening?"

阿难白佛：「如是修习安那般那念，令四念处满足。云何修四念处，令七觉分满足？」

The Buddha told Ānanda: "If a bhikṣu abides in mindfulness of the body observing the body, having abided in mindfulness, he abides in mindfulness without forgetting. At that time, he cultivates the factor of awakening of mindfulness by skillful means. Having cultivated the factor of awakening of mindfulness, the factor of awakening of mindfulness is fulfilled. Having fulfilled the factor of awakening of mindfulness, he selects and contemplates the Dharma. At that time, he cultivates the factor of awakening of discrimination of phenomena by skillful means. Having cultivated the factor of awakening of discrimination of phenomena, the factor of awakening of discrimination of phenomena is fulfilled. Having selected, distinguished, and contemplated the Dharma, he attains the skillful means of diligence. At that time, he cultivates the factor of awakening of diligence by skillful means. Having cultivated the factor of awakening of diligence, the factor of awakening of diligence is fulfilled. Having skillfully exerted diligence, his mind is joyful. At that time, he cultivates the factor of awakening of joy by skillful means. Having cultivated the factor of awakening of joy, the factor of awakening of joy is fulfilled. Having been joyful, his body and mind are tranquil. At that time, he cultivates the factor of awakening of tranquility by skillful means. Having cultivated the factor of awakening of tranquility, the factor of awakening of tranquility is fulfilled. Having been tranquil, his body and mind are blissful. He attains samādhi. At that time, he cultivates the factor of awakening of concentration. Having cultivated the factor of awakening of concentration, the factor of awakening of concentration is fulfilled. Having fulfilled the factor of awakening of concentration, greed and sorrow are extinguished, and he attains equanimity. At that time, he cultivates the factor of awakening of equanimity by skillful means. Having cultivated the factor of awakening of equanimity, the factor of awakening of equanimity is fulfilled. The same is said for the foundations of mindfulness of feeling, mind, and phenomena. This is called cultivating the four foundations of mindfulness, fulfilling the seven factors of awakening."

佛告阿难：「若比丘身身观念住，念住已，系念住不忘。尔时方便修念觉分，修念觉分已，念觉分满足；念觉满足已，于法选择思量。尔时方便修择法觉分，修择法觉分已，择法觉分满足，于法选择分别思量已，得精勤方便。尔时方便修习精进觉分，修精进觉分已，精进觉分满足，方便精进已，则心欢喜。尔时方便修喜觉分，修喜觉分已，喜觉分满足，欢喜已，身心猗息。尔时方便修猗觉分，修猗觉分已，猗觉分满足，身心乐已，得三昧。尔时修定觉分，修定觉分已，定觉分满足，定觉分满足已，贪忧则灭，得平等舍。尔时方便修舍觉分，修舍觉分已，舍觉分满足，受、心、法法念处亦如是说，是名修四念处，满足七觉分。」

Ānanda addressed the Buddha, saying: "This is called cultivating the four foundations of mindfulness, fulfilling the seven factors of awakening. How does cultivating the seven factors of awakening fulfill clear comprehension and liberation?"

阿难白佛：「是名修四念处，满足七觉分。云何修七觉分，满足明、解脱？」

The Buddha told Ānanda: "If a bhikṣu cultivates the factor of awakening of mindfulness, he relies on seclusion, relies on non-attachment, relies on cessation, and inclines towards letting go. Having cultivated the factor of awakening of mindfulness, he fulfills clear comprehension and liberation. Up to cultivating the factor of awakening of equanimity, he relies on seclusion, relies on non-attachment, relies on cessation, and inclines towards letting go. Having cultivated the factor of awakening of equanimity in this way, clear comprehension and liberation are fulfilled. Ānanda! This is called the mutual similarity and mutual nourishment of dharmas. In this way, these thirteen dharmas are one dharma as the dominant condition, one dharma as the gateway, gradually increasing and progressing, and being cultivated to fulfillment."

佛告阿难：「若比丘修念觉分，依远离、依无欲、依灭、向于舍；修念觉分已，满足明、解脱。乃至修舍觉分，依远离、依无欲、依灭、向于舍，如是修舍觉分已，明、解脱满足。阿难！是名法法相类、法法相润。如是十三法，一法为增上，一法为门，次第增进，修习满足。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the World-Honored One, the Venerable Ānanda, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and upheld them.

佛说此经已，尊者阿难闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
