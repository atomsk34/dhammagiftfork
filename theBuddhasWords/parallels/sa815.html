SĀ 815

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 815（八一五） 布萨

Parallel to: MN 118 / SN 54.13–16 (~) / SĀ 803 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying at Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī during the summer retreat. At that time, many senior bhikṣus were staying in the trees and caves near the World-Honored One. Then, some young bhikṣus went to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园夏安居。尔时，众多上座声闻于世尊左右树下、窟中安居。时，有众多年少比丘诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面。

The Buddha spoke various teachings to the young bhikṣus, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting them. Having shown, taught, illuminated, and delighted them, he remained silent.

佛为诸年少比丘种种说法，示、教、照、喜；示、教、照、喜已，默然住。

The young bhikṣus, having heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced and followed his teachings. They rose from their seats, bowed, and departed.

诸年少比丘闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，从座起，作礼而去。

The young bhikṣus went to the elder bhikṣus, paid homage at their feet, and sat to one side.

诸年少比丘往诣上座比丘所，礼诸上座足已，于一面坐。

At that time, the elder bhikṣus thought, "We should accept these young bhikṣus, either one accepting one, or one accepting two or three or many." Having thought this, they immediately accepted them, either one accepting one, or accepting two or three or many. Some elders accepted up to sixty.

时，诸上座比丘作是念：「我等当摄受此诸年少比丘，或一人受一人，或一人受二、三、多人。」作是念已，即便摄受，或一人受一人，或受二、三、多人，或有上座乃至受六十人。

At that time, the World-Honored One, on the fifteenth day of the fortnight, sat down in front of the assembly and arranged his seat.

尔时，世尊十五日布萨时，于大众前敷座而坐。

At that time, the World-Honored One, having observed the bhikṣus, said to the bhikṣus, "Excellent! Excellent! I am pleased with the bhikṣus practicing the right things. Therefore, bhikṣus! You should diligently practice."

尔时，世尊观察诸比丘已，告比丘：「善哉！善哉！我今喜诸比丘行诸正事。是故，比丘！当勤精进。」

In this Śrāvastī country, during the Māgha month, the bhikṣus in various places heard that the World-Honored One was staying in Śrāvastī during the Māgha month. After completing the robe, they took their robes and alms bowls and traveled through the human realm of Śrāvastī. Gradually arriving in Śrāvastī, they put away their robes and alms bowls, washed their feet, went to where the World-Honored One was, bowed their heads at his feet, and then sat to one side.

于此舍卫国，满迦低月，诸处人间比丘闻世尊于舍卫国安居，满迦低月满已，作衣竟，持衣钵，于舍卫国人间游行。渐至舍卫国，举衣钵，洗足已，诣世尊所，稽首礼足已，退坐一面。

At that time, the World-Honored One, for the bhikṣus in the human realm, spoke various teachings, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting them, then remained silent.

尔时，世尊为人间比丘种种说法，示、教、照、喜已，默然住。

At that time, the bhikṣus in the human realm, having heard the Buddha's teachings, rejoiced and followed them. They rose from their seats, bowed, and departed. They went to where the senior bhikṣus were, bowed their heads at their feet, and then sat to one side.

尔时人间比丘闻佛说法，欢喜随喜，从座起，作礼而去。往诣上座比丘所，稽首礼足，退坐一面。

At that time, the senior bhikṣus thought, "We should accept these bhikṣus from the human realm. One person can accept one person, or two, three, up to many people." They then accepted them, with one person accepting one person, or two, three, up to some accepting sixty people. Those senior bhikṣus accepted the bhikṣus from the human realm, instructing and teaching them, well knowing the sequence of before and after.

时，诸上座作是念：「我等当受此人间比丘，或一人受一人，或二、三，乃至多人。」即便受之，或一人受一人，或二、三，乃至有受六十人者。彼上座比丘受诸人间比丘，教诫、教授，善知先后次第。

At that time, the World Honored One, on the fifteenth day of the month during the confession ceremony, sat on a prepared seat in front of the great assembly, observed the assembly of bhikṣus, and addressed the bhikṣus, saying, "Excellent! Excellent! Bhikṣus! I rejoice in the correct practices of you all, and delight in the correct practices of you all. Bhikṣus! The assemblies of bhikṣus of past buddhas also practiced correctly as they do now. The assemblies of bhikṣus of future buddhas will also be like this, practicing correctly as they do now. Why is this? Because in this assembly, the elder bhikṣus have attained the first, second, third, and fourth dhyānas, and dwell in the perfection of loving-kindness, compassion, joy, and equanimity, the sphere of infinite space, the sphere of infinite consciousness, the sphere of nothingness, and the sphere of neither perception nor non-perception. Some bhikṣus have exhausted the three fetters, attained the state of srota-āpanna, will not fall into the evil destinies, are definitely headed for supreme perfect enlightenment, will be reborn seven times among gods and humans, and will finally put an end to suffering. Some bhikṣus have exhausted the three fetters, and their greed, hatred, and delusion are weakened, attaining the state of sakṛd-āgāmin. Some bhikṣus have exhausted the five lower fetters, attained the state of anāgāmin, and will be born in parinirvāṇa, never again to be reborn in this world. Some bhikṣus have attained the realm of immeasurable supernormal powers, the divine ear, knowledge of others' minds, knowledge of past lives, knowledge of death and rebirth, and knowledge of the exhaustion of taints. Some bhikṣus cultivate the contemplation of impurity to eliminate desire, cultivate loving-kindness to eliminate hatred, cultivate the perception of impermanence to eliminate the conceit of self, and cultivate the mindfulness of inhalation and exhalation to eliminate conceptualization. How do bhikṣus cultivate the mindfulness of inhalation and exhalation to eliminate conceptualization? These bhikṣus rely on a village, and so on, and observe the cessation of exhalation as they learn to observe the cessation of exhalation. This is called cultivating the mindfulness of inhalation and exhalation to eliminate conceptualization."

尔时，世尊月十五日布萨时，于大众前敷座而坐，观察诸比丘众，告诸比丘：「善哉！善哉！诸比丘！我欣汝等所行正事，乐汝等所行正事。诸比丘！过去诸佛亦有比丘众所行正事如今此众，未来诸佛所有诸众亦当如是所行正事如今此众。所以者何？今此众中诸长老比丘，有得初禅、第二禅、第三禅、第四禅，慈、悲、喜、舍，空入处、识入处、无所有入处、非想非非想处具足住。有比丘三结尽，得须陀洹，不堕恶趣法，决定正向三菩提，七有天人往生，究竟苦边。有比丘三结尽，贪、恚、痴薄，得斯陀含。有比丘五下分结尽，得阿那含，生般涅槃，不复还生此世。有比丘得无量神通境界，天耳、他心智、宿命智、生死智、漏尽智。有比丘修不净观，断贪欲，修慈心，断瞋恚，修无常想，断我慢，修安那般那念，断觉想。云何？比丘！修安那般那念断觉想？是比丘依止聚落，乃至观灭出息如观灭出息学，是名修安那般那念断觉想。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha said to the bhikṣus, "Excellent! Excellent! Bhikṣus! I rejoice in the correct practices of you all, and delight in the correct practices of you all. Bhikṣus! The assemblies of bhikṣus of past buddhas also practiced correctly as they do now. The assemblies of bhikṣus of future

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
