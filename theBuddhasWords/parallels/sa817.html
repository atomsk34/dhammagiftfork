SĀ 817

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 817（八一七） 学

Parallel to: AN 3.89 / SĀ 832

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "There are also three kinds of learning. What are the three? They are the superior learning of morality, the superior learning of concentration, and the superior learning of wisdom. What is the superior learning of morality? If a bhikṣu abides in the precepts, the rules of deportment, and the code of conduct, and is fully endowed with them, seeing even the slightest fault he gives rise to fear, and upholds the precepts of learning. What is the superior learning of concentration? If a bhikṣu is free from desire and evil and unwholesome dharmas, and fully abides in the fourth dhyāna. What is the superior learning of wisdom? If a bhikṣu truly knows this noble truth of suffering, the noble truth of the origin of suffering, the noble truth of the cessation of suffering, and the noble truth of the path to the cessation of suffering, this is called the superior learning of wisdom."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「亦复有三学。何等为三？谓增上戒学、增上意学、增上慧学。何等为增上戒学？若比丘住于戒波罗提木叉律仪，威仪行处具足，见微细罪则生怖畏，受持学戒。何等为增上意学？若比丘离欲、恶不善法，乃至第四禅具足住。何等为增上慧学？是比丘此苦圣谛如实知，集、灭、道圣谛如实知，是名增上慧学。」

At that time the World-Honored One spoke a verse, as above.

尔时，世尊即说偈，如上所说。

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished what he had to say. The bhikṣus, having heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
