SĀ 821

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 821（八二一） 涅槃

Parallel to: AN 3.86

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Beyond the two hundred and fifty precepts, I will explain the Prātimokṣa Sūtra in sequence every half month. If those good sons wish to study according to their own intentions, I will teach them the three trainings. If they train in these three trainings, they will embrace all the precepts of training. What are the three? They are the superior training in morality, the superior training in concentration, and the superior training in wisdom.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「过二百五十戒，随次半月来说波罗提木叉修多罗，若彼善男子自随意所欲而学者，我为说三学。若学此三学，则摄受一切学戒。何等为三？谓增上戒学、增上意学、增上慧学。

What is the superior training in morality? It is when a bhikṣu places great emphasis on morality, with morality as the superior factor, but does not place great emphasis on concentration, with concentration not as the superior factor, and does not place great emphasis on wisdom, with wisdom not as the superior factor; he upholds the subtle precepts in each aspect, and upholds the precepts of training. Knowing and seeing thus, he cuts off the three fetters, namely, the view of the body, the grasping of precepts, and doubt; he weakens greed, hatred, and delusion, and attains the path of the one-seed. He who has not yet fully awakened to that stage is called a once-returner, he who has not yet fully awakened to that stage is called a family-to-family, he who has not yet fully awakened to that stage is called seven-existence, he who has not yet fully awakened to that stage is called one who follows the Dharma, he who has not yet fully awakened to that stage is called one who follows faith. This is called the superior training in morality.

「何等为增上戒学？是比丘重于戒，戒增上，不重于定，定不增上，不重于慧，慧不增上；于彼彼分细微戒，乃至受持学戒。如是知、如是见，断三结，谓身见、戒取、疑，贪、恚、痴薄，成一种子道。彼地未等觉者，名斯陀含，彼地未等觉者，名家家，彼地未等觉者，名七有，彼地未等觉者，名随法行，彼地未等觉者，名随信行，是名增上戒学。

What is the superior training in concentration? It is when a bhikṣu places great emphasis on morality, with morality as the superior factor, and places great emphasis on concentration, with concentration as the superior factor, but does not place great emphasis on wisdom, with wisdom not as the superior factor; he upholds the subtle precepts in each aspect, and upholds the precepts of training. Knowing and seeing thus, he cuts off the five lower fetters, namely, the view of the body, the grasping of precepts, doubt, sensual desire, and hatred. Having cut off these five lower fetters, he attains the middle parinirvāṇa. He who has not yet fully awakened to that stage attains the parinirvāṇa of birth, he who has not yet fully awakened to that stage attains the parinirvāṇa without practice, he who has not yet fully awakened to that stage attains the parinirvāṇa with practice, he who has not yet fully awakened to that stage attains the parinirvāṇa of the upper stream. This is called the superior training in concentration.

「何等为增上意学？是名比丘重于戒，戒增上，重于定，定增上，不重于慧，慧不增上；于彼彼分细微戒学，乃至受持学戒。如是知、如是见，断五下分结，谓身见、戒取、疑、贪欲、瞋恚。断此五下分，能得中般涅槃。彼地未等觉者，得生般涅槃，彼地未等觉者，得无行般涅槃，彼地未等觉者，得有行般涅槃，彼地未等觉者，得上流般涅槃，是名增上意学。

"What is the superior training in wisdom? It is when a bhikṣu places great emphasis on morality, with morality as the superior factor, places great emphasis on concentration, with concentration as the superior factor, and places great emphasis on wisdom, with wisdom as the superior factor. Knowing and seeing thus, he is liberated from the mind's defilements of desire, existence, and ignorance. With liberation, he knows and sees: 'My birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, there is no more of this state.' This is called the superior training in wisdom."

「何等为增上慧学？是比丘重于戒，戒增上，重于定，定增上，重于慧，慧增上。如是知、如是见，欲有漏心解脱、有有漏心解脱、无明有漏心解脱，解脱知见：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』是名增上慧学。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully practiced it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
