SĀ 827

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 827（八二七） 耕磨

Parallel to: AN 3.83

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the World-Honored One addressed the bhikṣus, saying: "It is like a farmer who has three kinds of fields, which he cultivates well according to the seasons. What are the three? It means that the farmer plows and cultivates according to the seasons, irrigates according to the seasons, and sows seeds according to the seasons. After the farmer has plowed and cultivated, irrigated, and sown seeds according to the seasons, he does not think like this: 'I want to make them grow, bear fruit, and ripen today, or tomorrow or the day after.' Bhikṣus! However, that farmer, after plowing the fields, irrigating, and sowing seeds, does not think like this: 'I want them to grow, bear fruit, and ripen today, or tomorrow or the day after.' But those seeds, once they have entered the ground, will naturally grow, bear fruit, and ripen according to the seasons. In the same way, bhikṣus, if one cultivates well according to the seasons in these three trainings - namely, the training in good precepts, the training in good mind, and the training in good wisdom - one does not think like this: 'I want to attain the non-arising of the contaminants, and the mind well liberated today, or tomorrow or the day after.' One does not think like this: 'By the power of the supernatural, I want to make it so that today, or tomorrow or the day after, the contaminants do not arise, and the mind is well liberated.' But having already cultivated the training in superior precepts, the training in superior mind, and the training in superior wisdom according to the seasons, one will naturally attain the non-arising of the contaminants, and the mind well liberated at that time.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「譬如田夫有三种作田，随时善作。何等为三？谓彼田夫随时耕磨，随时溉灌，随时下种。彼田夫随时耕磨、溉灌、下种已，不作是念：『欲令今日生长，今日果实，今日成熟，若明日、后日也。』诸比丘！然彼长者耕田、溉灌、下种已，不作是念：『今日生长、果实、成熟，若明日、若复后日。』而彼种子已入地中，则自随时生长，果实成熟。如是，比丘于此三学随时善学？谓善戒学、善意学、善慧学已，不作是念：『欲令我今日得不起诸漏，心善解脱，若明日、若后日。』不作是念：『自然神力能令今日，若明日、后日，不起诸漏，心善解脱。』彼已随时增上戒学、增上意学、增上慧学已，随彼时节，自得不起诸漏，心善解脱。

It is like a hen that lays ten to twelve eggs. She keeps them warm and protects them according to the seasons. She does not think like this: 'I want to peck at them with my mouth or scrape them with my beak today, tomorrow, or the day after, so that my chicks can be born safely.' However, that hen incubates her chicks well, protects them according to the seasons, and the chicks are naturally born safely. In the same way, bhikṣus, if one cultivates well the three trainings - namely, the training in good precepts, the training in good mind, and the training in good wisdom - one will naturally attain the non-arising of the contaminants, and the mind well liberated at that time.

「譬如，比丘！伏鸡生卵，若十乃至十二，随时消息，冷暖爱护。彼伏鸡不作是念：『我今日，若明日、后日，当以口啄，若以瓜刮，令其儿安隐得生。』然其伏鸡善伏其子，爱护随时，其子自然安隐得生。如是，比丘善学三学，随其时节，自得不起诸漏，心善解脱。」

After the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus who heard what the Buddha taught rejoiced and received it respectfully.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
