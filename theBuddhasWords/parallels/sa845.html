SĀ 845

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 845（八四五） 恐怖

Parallel to: SN 55.29 / AN 9.28

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "If a bhikṣu rests from the five fears and enemies, and is certain about three things without doubt, and truly knows and sees the noble and correct path, then that noble disciple can declare for himself: 'The evil destinies of hell, animal realm, and hungry ghosts are exhausted. I have attained the stage of stream-entry, not falling into the ways of evil destinies. I am definitely progressing towards supreme perfect enlightenment, taking birth seven times among gods and humans, ultimately reaching the end of suffering.'

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「若比丘于五恐怖怨对休息，三事决定，不生疑惑，如实知见贤圣正道，彼圣弟子能自记说：『地狱、畜生、饿鬼恶趣已尽，得须陀洹，不堕恶趣法，决定正向三菩提，七有天人往生，究竟苦边。』

What are the five fears and enemies that are rested from? If there is fear and enmity arising from the causes and conditions of killing, then for one who abstains from killing, the fear and enmity arising from the causes and conditions of the sin of killing is rested from. If there is fear and enmity arising from the causes and conditions of stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and drinking alcohol, then for one who abstains from stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, and drinking alcohol, the fear and enmity arising from the causes and conditions of the sin is rested from. These are called the resting from the five fears arising from the causes and conditions of sin and enmity.

「何等为五恐怖怨对休息？若杀生因缘罪怨对恐怖生，若离杀生者，彼杀生罪怨对因缘生恐怖休息。若偷盗、邪婬、妄语、饮酒罪怨对因缘生恐怖；彼若离偷盗、邪婬、妄语、饮酒罪怨对者，因缘恐怖休息，是名罪怨对因缘生五恐怖休息。

What are the three things that are certain without doubt? They are being certain about the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Saṃgha, and being free from doubt. These are called the three things that are certain without doubt.

「何等为三事决定，不生疑惑？谓于佛决定离于疑惑，于法、僧决定离疑惑，是名三法决定离疑惑。

What is called the knowledge and vision of the noble path as it really is? It is the knowledge of this noble truth of suffering as it really is, the knowledge of this noble truth of the origin of suffering, the knowledge of this noble truth of the cessation of suffering, and the knowledge of this noble truth of the path leading to the cessation of suffering. This is called the knowledge and vision of the noble path as it really is.

「何等名为圣道如实知见？谓此苦圣谛如实知，此苦集圣谛、此苦灭圣谛、此苦灭道迹圣谛如实知，是名圣道如实知见。

"If one rests from the five fears, the five terrors, the five enemies, and the five opponents, is certain about the three things, and knows and sees the noble path as it really is, then that noble disciple can declare for themselves: 'My hell is exhausted, my animal realm, hungry ghost realm, and evil destinies are exhausted. I have attained the stream, not falling into the unwholesome destinies, definitely progressing to supreme awakening, taking birth seven times among gods and humans, and ultimately reaching the end of suffering.'"

「若于此五恐怖罪怨对休息、于三法决定离疑惑、于圣意如实知见，是圣弟子能自记说：『我地狱尽，畜生、饿鬼恶趣尽，得须陀洹，不堕恶趣法，决定正趣三菩提，七有天人往生，究竟苦边。』」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully practiced it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
