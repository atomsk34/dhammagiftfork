SĀ 848

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 848（八四八） 天道

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "There are four kinds of heavenly paths of the gods. What are the four? They are: a noble disciple recollects the matters of the Tathāgata, thus: the Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, Perfect in Knowledge and Conduct, Well-Gone, Knower of the World, Unsurpassed, Tamer of Men, Teacher of Gods and Men, Buddha, World-Honored One. Regarding these matters of the Tathāgata, they give rise to a mind of rejoicing. Having rejoiced, their mind is joyful. Having a joyful mind, their body becomes tranquil. Having a tranquil body, they experience bliss. Having experienced bliss, they attain samādhi. Having attained samādhi, the noble disciple studies thus: 'What is the heavenly path of the gods?' They further think: 'I have heard that non-anger is the supreme heavenly path of the gods.' They think: 'From today onwards, whether in fear or peace in the world, I shall not give rise to anger. I shall only personally experience the pure and perfect heavenly path of the gods.' This is called the first heavenly path of the gods, which purifies impure beings and further purifies those who are already pure.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「有四种诸天天道。何等为四？谓圣弟子念如来事，如是：如来、应、等正觉、明行足、善逝、世间解、无上士、调御丈夫、天人师、佛世尊。于此如来事生随喜心，随喜已，心欢悦，心欢悦已，身猗息，身猗息已，觉受乐，觉受乐已，三昧定，三昧定已，圣弟子作如是学：『何等为诸天天道？』复作是念：『我闻无恚为上诸天天道。』作是念：『我从今日，于世间若怖若安，不起瞋恚，我但当自受纯一满净诸天天道。』是名第一诸天天道，未净众生令净，已净者重令净。

Furthermore, bhikṣus! A noble disciple recollects the matters of the Dharma, namely, the Tathāgata's teaching of the true Dharma and Vinaya, which is free from all burning in this very life, not dependent on time, and leads to the realization of nirvāṇa, which is to be personally realized through direct knowledge. Having understood these matters of the Dharma, the mind gives rise to rejoicing. Having rejoiced, the body becomes tranquil. Having a tranquil body, one experiences bliss. Having experienced bliss, one attains samādhi. Having attained samādhi, the noble disciple studies thus: 'What is the heavenly path of the gods?' They further think: 'I have heard that non-anger is the supreme heavenly path of the gods. From today onwards, whether in fear or peace in the world, I shall not give rise to anger. I shall uphold the pure and perfect heavenly path of the gods.' This is called the second heavenly path of the gods.

「复次，比丘！圣弟子念于法事，谓如来说正法、律，现法离诸炽然，不待时节，通达涅槃，即身观察，缘自觉知。如是知法事已，心生随喜；随喜已，身猗息，身猗息已，觉受乐，觉受乐已，三昧定，三昧定已，圣弟子作如是学：『何等为诸天天道？』复作是念：『我闻无恚为上诸天天道，我从今日，于此世间若怖若安，不起瞋恚，我当受持纯一满净诸天天道。』是名第二诸天天道。

Furthermore, bhikṣus! If one gives rise to right mindfulness regarding the matters of the Saṅgha, namely, the Saṅgha of the World-Honored One's disciples who are upright, equal, worthy of offerings, respect, and veneration, the unsurpassed field of merit. Having thus rightly recollected the matters of the Saṅgha, the mind gives rise to rejoicing. Having rejoiced, one attains joy. Having joy, the body becomes tranquil. Having a tranquil body, one experiences bliss. Having experienced bliss, one attains samādhi. Having attained samādhi, the noble disciple studies thus: 'What is the heavenly path of the gods?' They further think: 'I have heard that non-anger is the supreme heavenly path of the gods. From today onwards, whether in fear or peace in the world, I shall not give rise to anger. I shall only personally experience the pure and perfect heavenly path of the gods.' This is called the third heavenly path of the gods.

「复次，比丘！若于僧事起于正念，谓世尊弟子僧正直等向，所应恭敬、尊重、供养，无上福田。彼如是于诸僧事正忆念已，心生随喜；心随喜已，得欢悦，欢悦已，身猗息，身猗息已，觉受乐，觉受乐已，三昧定，三昧定已，彼圣弟子作如是学：『何等诸天天道？』复作是念：『我闻诸天无恚为上诸天天道，我从今日，于诸世间若怖若安，不起瞋恚，我但当受持纯一满净诸天天道。』是名第三诸天天道。

Furthermore, bhikṣus! A noble disciple recollects the matters of his own precepts, saying: 'I have precepts that are unbroken, unstained, unadulterated, praised by the wise, and not despised by the wise.' Having thus rightly recollected these matters of precepts, the mind gives rise to rejoicing. Having rejoiced, one attains joy. Having joy, the body becomes tranquil. Having a tranquil body, one experiences bliss. Having experienced bliss, one attains samādhi. Having attained samādhi, the noble disciple thinks: 'What is the heavenly path of the gods?' They further think: 'I have heard that non-anger is the supreme heavenly path of the gods. From today onwards, whether in fear or peace in the world, I shall not give rise to anger. I shall personally experience the pure and perfect heavenly path of the gods.' This is called the fourth heavenly path of the gods.

「复次，比丘！谓圣弟子自念所有戒事，随忆念言：『我于此不缺戒、不污戒、不杂戒、明智所叹戒、智者不厌戒。』于如是等戒事正忆念已，心生随喜；随喜已，欢悦，欢悦已；身猗息，身猗息已，觉受乐，觉受乐已，三昧定，三昧定已，圣弟子作是念：『何等为诸天天道？』复作是念：『我闻诸天无恚为上，我从今日，于诸世间若怖若安，不起瞋恚，我当受持纯一满净诸天天道。』是名第四诸天天道。未净众生令净，已净者重令净。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully practiced it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
