SĀ 854

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 854（八五四） 那黎迦

Parallel to: SN 55.10

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Naḷaka Monastery in the Naḷaka village. At that time many people in the Naḷaka village had died.

一时，佛住那梨迦聚落繁耆迦精舍。尔时，那梨迦聚落多人命终。

At that time, many bhikṣus put on their robes, took their alms bowls, and entered the Naḷaka village to beg for food. They heard that the upāsaka Kaiśika of the Naḷaka village had passed away, as well as Nīkaṭa, Kharagāla, Kādambarī, Jālu, Upālu, Licchavi, Āli-Licchavi, Bhadrika, Subhadrika, Yaśa, Yaśodhara, Yaśa-Uttara, and all had passed away. Having heard this, they returned to the monastery, put away their robes and bowls, washed their feet, went to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! We, many bhikṣus, entered the Naḷaka village in the morning to beg for food. We heard that the upāsaka Kaiśika and others had passed away. World-Honored One! Where will they be reborn after passing away? "

时，有众多比丘著衣持钵，入那梨迦聚落乞食，闻那梨迦聚落罽迦舍优婆塞命终，尼迦咤、佉楞迦罗、迦多梨沙婆、阇露、优婆阇露、梨色咤、阿梨色咤、跋陀罗、须跋陀罗、耶舍耶输陀、耶舍欝多罗悉皆命终。闻已，还精舍，举衣钵，洗足已，诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！我等众多比丘晨朝入那梨迦聚落乞食，闻罽迦舍优婆塞等命终。世尊！彼等命终，当生何处？」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "That Kaiśika and others have already severed the five lower fetters and attained the state of anāgāmin. They will attain parinirvāṇa in the heavens and will not be reborn in this world again."

佛告诸比丘：「彼罽迦舍等已断五下分结，得阿那含，于天上般涅槃，不复还生此世。」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! There are also more than two hundred and fifty upāsakas who have passed away. There are also five hundred upāsakas who have passed away in this Naḷaka village. They have all exhausted the five lower fetters and attained the state of anāgāmin. They will attain parinirvāṇa in the heavens and will not be reborn in this world again. There are also more than two hundred and fifty upāsakas who have passed away. They have all exhausted the three fetters, and their greed, anger, and delusion are weakened. They have attained the state of sakṛd-āgāmin. They will be reborn once more and ultimately end suffering. There are also five hundred upāsakas in this Naḷaka village who have passed away. They have exhausted the three fetters and attained the state of srota-āpanna. They will not fall into the evil destinies. They are definitely heading towards supreme perfect enlightenment. They will be reborn seven times among the gods and humans and ultimately end suffering."

诸比丘白佛：「世尊！复有过二百五十优婆塞命终，复有五百优婆塞于此那梨迦聚落命终，皆五下分结尽，得阿那含，于彼天上般涅槃，不复还生此世？复有过二百五十优婆塞命终，皆三结尽，贪、恚、痴薄，得斯陀含，当受一生，究竟苦边？此那梨迦聚落复有五百优婆塞于此那梨迦聚落命终，三结尽，得须陀洹，不堕恶趣法，决定正向三菩提，七有天人往生，究竟苦边？」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "You ask about those who have passed away, but it is in vain. It is not something the Tathāgata enjoys answering. All who are born will die. What is so strange about that? Whether the Tathāgata appears in the world or not, the nature of the Dharma abides. The Tathāgata himself knows and realizes perfect enlightenment, manifests and explains, and distinguishes and reveals. That is to say, because of this, that exists. Because of the arising of this, that arises. Conditioned by ignorance, there are volitional formations, and so on, up to conditioned by birth, there are old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, suffering, and distress. Thus the mass of suffering arises. When ignorance ceases, volitional formations cease, and so on, up to when birth ceases, old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, suffering, and distress cease. Thus the mass of suffering ceases. Now I will explain the Dharma Mirror Sutra for you. Listen carefully and think well, and I will explain it for you. What is the Dharma Mirror Sutra? It is that the noble disciple has unshakable faith in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Saṃgha, and has accomplished the noble precepts."

佛告诸比丘：「汝等随彼命终、彼命终而问者，徒劳耳！非是如来所乐答者。夫生者有死，何足为奇？如来出世及不出世，法性常住。彼如来自知成等正觉，显现演说，分别开示。所谓是事有故是事有，是事起故是事起，缘无明有行，乃至缘生有老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦。如是苦阴集；无明灭则行灭，乃至生灭则老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦灭，如是苦阴灭。今当为汝说法镜经，谛听，善思，当为汝说。何等为法镜经？谓圣弟子于佛不坏净，于法、僧不坏净，圣戒成就。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus were delighted and practiced accordingly.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
