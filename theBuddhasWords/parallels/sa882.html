SĀ 882

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 882（八八二） 不放逸根本

Parallel to: SN 49.13–22

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Just as various grasses and trees all depend on the earth to grow, so do various wholesome dharmas all depend on non-distraction as their basis..." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「譬如百草药木，皆依于地而得生长；如是种种善法，皆依不放逸为本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

Just as black agarwood is the best of all fragrances, so is non-distraction the best of all wholesome dharmas.

「譬如黑沈水香是众香之上，如是种种善法，不放逸最为其上。

Just as solid fragrances, red sandalwood is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction. Thus, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如坚固之香，赤栴檀为第一；如是一切善法，一切皆不放逸为根本。如是，乃至涅槃。

Just as all water and land flowers, the blue lotus flower is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如水陆诸华，优钵罗华为第一；如是一切善法，皆不放逸为根本，乃至涅槃。

Just as land-grown flowers, the jasmine flower is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如陆地生华，摩利沙华为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本，乃至涅槃。

"Just as, bhikṣus! among all animal tracks, the elephant track is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如，比丘！一切畜生迹中，象迹为上；如是一切诸善法，不放逸最为根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among all animals, the lion is the best, called the lord of animals, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如一切畜生，师子为第一；所谓畜生主，如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among all houses and buildings, the roof is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如一切屋舍堂阁，以栋为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本。

"Just as among all jambū fruits, only those that can be called jambū fruits are the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如一切阎浮果，唯得阎浮名者，果最为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本。

"Just as among all śāla trees, the śāla tree is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「如是一切俱毘陀罗树，萨婆耶旨罗俱毘陀罗树为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among all mountains, Mount Sumeru is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如诸山，以须弥山王为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among all gold, Jambūnada gold is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如一切金，以阎浮提金为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among all garments, Kāśika fine cotton is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如一切衣中，伽尸细［叠＊毛］为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among all colors, white is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如一切色中，以白色为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among all birds, the garuḍa is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如众鸟，以金翅鸟为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among all kings, the wheel-turning sage-king is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如诸王，转轮圣王为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among all heavenly kings, the four great heavenly kings are the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如一切天王，四大天王为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among all the thirty-three heavens, Śakra is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如一切三十三天，以帝释为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among the Yāma Heaven, the Yāma Heaven King is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如焰摩天中，以宿焰摩天王为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among the Tuṣita Heaven, the Tuṣita Heaven King is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如兜率陀天，以兜率陀天王为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among the Nirmāṇarati Heaven, the Nirmāṇarati Heaven King is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如化乐天，以善化乐天王为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among the Paranirmitavaśavartin Heaven, the Paranirmitavaśavartin Heaven Prince is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如他化自在天，以善他化自在天子为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among the Brahmakāyika Heaven, the Mahābrahmā is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如梵天，大梵王为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as all the rivers of Jambudvīpa flow into the great ocean, and the great ocean is the best, because it can contain them, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如阎浮提一切众流皆顺趣大海，其大海者最为第一；以容受故，如是一切善法皆顺不放逸……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as all raindrops return to the great ocean, so are all wholesome dharmas all based on non-distraction, up to nirvāṇa...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如一切雨渧皆归大海，如是一切善法皆顺趣不放逸海……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as all rivers, the Anavatapta River is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas, non-distraction is the best...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如一切萨罗，阿耨大萨罗为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为第一……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as all rivers in Jambudvīpa, the four great rivers are the best, namely the Ganges, Yamunā, Sarabhū, and Sītā, so are all wholesome dharmas, non-distraction is the best...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如阎浮提一切河，四大河为第一；谓恒河、新头、搏叉、司陀，如是一切善法，不放逸为第一……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as the light of all stars, the moon is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas, non-distraction is the best...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如众星光明，月为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为第一……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among all beings with large bodies, Rāhu the asura is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas, non-distraction is their root...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如诸大身众生，罗睺罗阿修罗最为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among those who enjoy the five desires, King Dhṛtarāṣṭra is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas, non-distraction is their root...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如诸受五欲者，顶生王为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among the supernatural powers of the desire realm, Māra Pāpīyas is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas, non-distraction is their root...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如欲界诸神力，天魔波旬为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among all beings, those without feet, with two feet, with four feet, with many feet, with form, formless, with perception, without perception, neither with perception nor without perception, the Tathāgata is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas, non-distraction is their root...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如一切众生，无足、两足、四足、多足，色、无色，想、无想，非想、非无想，如来为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among all dharmas, conditioned and unconditioned, detachment is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas, non-distraction is their root...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如所有诸法，有为、无为，离贪欲为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among all groups of dharmas, the Tathāgata's group is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas, non-distraction is their root...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如一切诸法众，如来众为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

"Just as among all the realms of practice, the holy realm of Brahmā is the best, so are all wholesome dharmas, non-distraction is their root...." as above, up to nirvāṇa.

「譬如一切所有诸界苦行，梵行圣界为第一；如是一切善法，不放逸为其根本……」如上说，乃至涅槃。

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha had said and joyfully upheld it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
