SĀ 885

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 885（八八五） 无学三明

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "There are three kinds of supernormal knowledge for one who has no more learning to do. What are the three? They are the supernormal knowledge of recollecting past lives, the supernormal knowledge of knowing the death and rebirth of beings, and the supernormal knowledge of the destruction of the contaminants for one who has no more learning to do.

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「有无学三明。何等为三？谓无学宿命智证通、无学生死智证通、无学漏尽智证通。

What is the supernormal knowledge of recollecting past lives for one who has no more learning to do? It means that the noble disciple knows various past lives, from one to a hundred thousand million lives, even to the formation and destruction of eons. He knows, 'I and beings in the past had such names, such births, such natures, such food, such experiences of suffering and happiness, such long lives, such long abodes, such limited experiences. I and beings died in this place and were born in another place, died in another place and were born in this place. We had such practices, such causes, such beliefs.' He knows all these things about past lives.

「云何无学宿命智证通？谓圣弟子知种种宿命事，从一生至百千万亿生，乃至劫数成坏，我及众生宿命所更如是名、如是生、如是性、如是食、如是受苦乐、如是长寿、如是久住、如是受分齐；我及众生于此处死、余处生，于余处死、此处生，有如是行、如是因、如是信，受种种宿命事，皆悉了知，是名宿命智证明。

What is the supernormal knowledge of knowing the death and rebirth of beings? It means that the noble disciple, with his pure divine eye surpassing that of humans, sees beings dying and being born, with good forms, bad forms, superior forms, and inferior forms. He knows, 'These beings, having committed evil deeds with their bodies, speech, and minds, having slandered the noble ones, and having held wrong views, will, at the breaking up of the body at death, be reborn in the lower realms, in the hells.' He also knows, 'These beings, having committed good deeds with their bodies, speech, and minds, having not slandered the noble ones, and having held right views, will, at the breaking up of the body at death, be reborn in the good realms, among gods and humans.' This is called the supernormal knowledge of knowing the death and rebirth of beings.

「云何生死智证明？谓圣弟子天眼净过于人眼，见诸众生死时、生时，善色、恶色，上色、下色，向于恶趣，随业受生如实知；如此众生身恶行成就、口恶行成就、意恶行成就，谤圣人，邪见受邪法因缘故，身坏命终，生恶趣泥犁中；此众生身善行、口善行，意善行，不谤毁圣人，正见成就，身坏命终，生于善趣天人中，是名生死智证明。

What is the supernormal knowledge of the exhaustion of contaminants? It means that the noble disciple truly knows this suffering, the origin of this suffering, the cessation of this suffering, and the path leading to the cessation of this suffering. Knowing and seeing thus, he is liberated from the contaminant of sensual desire, liberated from the contaminant of existence, liberated from the contaminant of ignorance. With liberation, there is the knowledge: 'Liberated.' He knows: 'My birth is exhausted, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, there will be no more of this state.' This is called the supernormal knowledge of the exhaustion of contaminants.

「云何漏尽智证明？谓圣弟子此苦如实知，此苦集、此苦灭、此苦灭道迹如实知；彼如是知、如是见，欲有漏心解脱、有有漏心解脱、无明漏心解脱，解脱知见：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』是名漏尽智证明。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊即说偈言：

Observing and knowing past lives, seeing birth in the heavens and evil
destinies, the exhaustion of birth and death and contaminants, this is the
illumination of the Sage. Knowing the mind is liberated, from all craving
and attachment, thoroughly penetrating the three realms, therefore it is called the three illuminations.

「观察知宿命，　　见天恶趣生，生死诸漏尽，　　是则牟尼明。知心得解脱，　　一切诸贪爱，三处悉通达，　　故说为三明。」

After the Buddha had spoken this sutra, the bhikṣus who heard what the Buddha had said rejoiced and upheld it.

佛说是经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
