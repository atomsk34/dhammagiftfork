SĀ 89

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 89（八九） 优波迦

Parallel to: AN 4.39 / SĀ² 2.89

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, there was a young brahmin named Upatiṣya who came to where the Buddha was. He exchanged greetings and pleasantries with the World-Honored One, then sat to one side and said to the Buddha, "Gautama! Brahmins always praise the great assembly of the evil and arrogant. Does the śramaṇa Gautama also praise the great assembly of the evil and arrogant?"

时，有年少婆罗门名优波迦，来诣佛所，与世尊面相问讯慰劳已，退坐一面，白佛言：「瞿昙！诸婆罗门常称叹邪盛大会，沙门瞿昙亦复称叹邪盛大会不？」

The Buddha told Upatiṣya, "I do not praise it in one way. There are some great assemblies of the evil and arrogant that are praiseworthy, and there are some that are not praiseworthy."

佛告优波迦：「我不一向称叹，或有邪盛大会可称叹，或有邪盛大会不可称叹。」

Upatiṣya said to the Buddha, "What kind of great assembly of the evil and arrogant is praiseworthy? What kind of great assembly of the evil and arrogant is not praiseworthy?"

优波迦白佛：「何等邪盛大会可称叹？何等邪盛大会不可称叹？」

The Buddha told Upatiṣya, "If a great assembly of the evil and arrogant ties up many young bulls, water bulls, and water cows, and kills all the sheep, goats, and other small creatures, oppressing and tormenting them, and forces their servants and workers to beat and frighten them, causing them to weep and cry, unhappy and miserable, making them work in great suffering, I do not praise such a great assembly of the evil and arrogant, because it creates great difficulties. But if a great assembly does not tie up the cattle, and does not make the sentient beings suffer and work, I praise such a great assembly of the evil and arrogant, because it does not create great difficulties." At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

佛告优波迦：「若邪盛大会系群少特牛、水特、水牸，及诸羊犊、小小众生悉皆伤杀，逼迫苦切，仆使作人，鞭笞恐怛，悲泣号呼，不喜不乐，众苦作役。如是等邪盛大会，我不称叹，以造大难故。若复大会不系缚群牛，乃至不令众生辛苦作役者。如是邪盛大会，我所称叹，以不造大难故。尔时，世尊即说偈言：

Horse sacrifices and other great assemblies create many difficulties
Such evil and arrogant assemblies are not praised by
the great sages. Binding all sentient beings and killing
tiny insects, this is not a proper assembly, not
in accord with the great sages. If one does
not harm sentient beings and create difficulties, such assemblies
are called proper, praised by the great sages. Generosity
and making offerings are in accord with the Dharma
with pure minds, the virtuous are the good field
of blessings. Such great assemblies are assemblies of arhats
assemblies that attain great fruits, rejoiced by the devas
Self-respectfully inviting, giving with one's own hands, both self
and others are pure, such giving attains great fruits
The wise give like this, with faith and liberation
without fault, enjoying the world, the wise are reborn there.

「马祀等大会，　　造诸大难事，如是等邪盛，　　大仙不称叹。系缚诸众生，　　杀害微细虫，是非为正会，　　大仙不随顺。若不害众生，　　造作众难者，是等名正会，　　大仙随称叹。惠施修供养，　　为应法邪盛，施者清净心，　　梵行良福田。如是大会者，　　是则罗汉会，是会得大果，　　诸天皆欢喜。自行恭敬请，　　自手而施与，彼我悉清净，　　是施得大果。慧者如是施，　　信心应解脱，无罪乐世间，　　智者往生彼。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha said to Upatiṣya, "The brāhmaṇa Upatiṣya, having heard the Buddha's teaching, was delighted and followed the Buddha's teachings. He bowed and left.

佛说此经已，优波迦婆罗门闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
