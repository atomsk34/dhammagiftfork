SĀ 905

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 905（九〇五） 外道

Parallel to: SN 16.12 / SĀ² 2.120

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha. At that time, Venerable Mahākāśyapa and Venerable Śāriputra were staying on Vulture Peak Mountain.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。尔时，尊者摩诃迦叶、尊者舍利弗、住耆阇崛山中。

At that time, many non-Buddhist renunciants went to Venerable Śāriputra. After exchanging greetings and pleasantries with Venerable Śāriputra, they sat to one side and said to Venerable Śāriputra, "What do you think, Śāriputra? Does the Tathāgata have rebirth after death?"

时，有众多外道出家诣尊者舍利弗，与尊者面相问讯慰劳已，退坐一面，语尊者舍利弗言：「云何？舍利弗！如来有后生死耶？」

Śāriputra said, "The World-Honored One said that this is indeterminate."

舍利弗言：「诸外道！世尊说言，此是无记。」

He further asked, "What do you think, Śāriputra? Is the Tathāgata without rebirth after death?"

又问：「云何？舍利弗！如来无后生死耶？」

Śāriputra replied, "The World-Honored One said that this is indeterminate."

舍利弗答言：「诸外道！世尊说言，此是无记。」

He further asked, "What do you think, Śāriputra? Is the Tathāgata with rebirth after death or without rebirth after death?"

又问：「舍利弗！如来有后生死、无后生死耶？」

Śāriputra replied, "The World-Honored One said that this is indeterminate."

舍利弗答言：「世尊说言，此是无记。」

He further asked Śāriputra, "What do you think, Śāriputra? Is the Tathāgata neither with rebirth after death nor without rebirth after death?"

又问舍利弗：「如来非有后生死、非无后生死耶？」

Śāriputra replied, "The World-Honored One said that this is indeterminate."

舍利弗答言：「诸外道！世尊说言，此是无记。」

The non-Buddhist renunciants further asked Venerable Śāriputra, "Why do you answer all questions about the Tathāgata's rebirth after death, non-rebirth after death, both rebirth and non-rebirth after death, neither rebirth nor non-rebirth after death, by saying, 'The World-Honored One said this is indeterminate'? How can you be like a fool, ignorant, unskillful, and unable to distinguish, like an infant without self-nature?" Having said this, they rose from their seats and left.

诸外道出家又问尊者舍利弗：「云何所问如来有后生死、无后生死、有后无后、非有后非无后，一切答言：『世尊说，此是无记。』云何为上座，如愚如痴，不善不辩，如婴儿无自性智？」作此语已，从坐起去。

At that time, Venerable Mahākāśyapa and Venerable Śāriputra were not far apart, each sitting under a tree, meditating during the day. Venerable Śāriputra knew that the non-Buddhist renunciants had left. He went to where Venerable Mahākāśyapa was, exchanged greetings and pleasantries, and then sat to one side. He told Venerable Mahākāśyapa in detail about the discussion he had just had with the non-Buddhist renunciants: "Venerable Mahākāśyapa, for what reason did the World-Honored One not give a definitive answer about rebirth after death, non-rebirth after death, both rebirth and non-rebirth after death, neither rebirth nor non-rebirth after death?"

尔时，尊者摩诃迦叶、尊者舍利弗相去不远，各坐树下，昼日禅思。尊者舍利弗知诸外道出家去已，诣尊者摩诃迦叶所，共相问讯慰劳已，退坐一面。以向与诸外道出家所论说事，具白尊者摩诃迦叶：「尊者摩诃迦叶！何因何缘世尊不记说，后有生死、后无生死、后有后无、非有非无生死耶？」

Venerable Mahākāśyapa said to Śāriputra, "If one says that the Tathāgata has rebirth after death, that is form. If one says that the Tathāgata has no rebirth after death, that is form. If one says that the Tathāgata has both rebirth and non-rebirth after death, that is form. If one says that the Tathāgata is neither with nor without rebirth after death, that is form. The Tathāgata has exhausted form and is well liberated in mind. To say that he has rebirth after death is not so. To say that he has no rebirth after death, both rebirth and non-rebirth after death, or neither rebirth nor non-rebirth after death is also not so. The Tathāgata has exhausted form and is well liberated in mind. He is very profound, vast, immeasurable, countless, and peacefully extinguished in nirvāṇa.

尊者摩诃迦叶语舍利弗言：「若说如来后有生死者，是则为色；若说如来无后生死，是则为色；若说如来有后生死、无后生死，是则为色；若说如来非有后、非无后生死，是则为色。如来者，色已尽，心善解脱。言有后生死者，此则不然；无后生死、有后无后、非有后非无后生死，此亦不然。如来者，色已尽，心善解脱，甚深广大，无量无数，寂灭涅槃。

Śāriputra, if one says that the Tathāgata has rebirth after death, that is grasping, perception, volition, consciousness, movement, thought, deception, existence, and craving, up to neither existence nor non-existence after death is also said in the same way. The Tathāgata has exhausted craving and is well liberated in mind. Therefore, to say that he has rebirth after death is not so. To say that he has no rebirth after death, both rebirth and non-rebirth after death, or neither rebirth nor non-rebirth after death is also not so. The Tathāgata has exhausted craving and is well liberated in mind. He is very profound, vast, immeasurable, countless, and peacefully extinguished in nirvāṇa.

「舍利弗！若说如来有后生死者，是则为受、为想、为行、为识、为动、为虑、为虚诳、为有为、为爱，乃至非有非无后有亦如是说。如来者，爱已尽，心善解脱，是故说后有者不然，后无、后有无、后非有非无者不然，如来者，爱已尽，心善解脱，甚深广大，无量无数，寂灭涅槃。舍利弗！如是因、如是缘，故有问世尊：『如来若有、若无、若有无、若非有非无后生死？』不可记说。」

At that time, the two noble ones had finished their discussion and each returned to their respective places.

时，二正士共论议已，各还本处。
