SĀ 906

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 906（九〇六） 法减灭

Parallel to: SN 16.13 / SĀ² 2.121

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the Venerable Mahākāśyapa was staying in the Deer Park Lecture Hall in the Eastern Garden of Śrāvastī. In the afternoon, he emerged from meditation and went to where the Buddha was. He bowed his head at the Buddha’s feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, “World-Honored One, for what reason did the World-Honored One previously establish few precepts for the śrāvakas, and many śrāvakas were delighted to practice and study them? Now, having established many precepts for the śrāvakas, few śrāvakas are delighted to practice and study them?”

尔时，尊者摩诃迦叶住舍卫国东园鹿子母讲堂，晡时从禅觉，往诣佛所，稽首礼足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！何因何缘，世尊先为诸声闻少制戒时，多有比丘心乐习学；今多为声闻制戒，而诸比丘少乐习学？」

The Buddha said, “So it is, Kāśyapa! The defilements of lifespan, afflictions, eons, sentient beings, and views—when the good dharmas of sentient beings decline, the Great Teacher establishes many precepts for the śrāvakas, and few are delighted to practice and study them. Kāśyapa! It is like when an eon is about to be destroyed, the true treasures have not yet disappeared, but various counterfeit treasures appear in the world; once the counterfeit treasures appear, the true treasures disappear. So it is, Kāśyapa! When the Tathāgata’s true Dharma is about to disappear, the counterfeit Dharma appears; once the counterfeit Dharma appears in the world, the true Dharma disappears. It is like a great ocean where a ship carrying many precious treasures sinks all at once; the Tathāgata’s true Dharma does not disappear gradually like this. The Tathāgata’s true Dharma is not destroyed by the earth element, not destroyed by the water, fire, or wind elements, and so on. Even when evil sentient beings appear in the world, delighting in doing evil, wishing to do evil, and accomplishing evil, speaking of what is not the Dharma as the Dharma, speaking of what is the Dharma as not the Dharma, speaking of what is not the Vinaya as the Vinaya, speaking of what is the Vinaya as not the Vinaya, with the burning of counterfeit Dharma, phrases, and flavors, the Tathāgata’s true Dharma disappears.

佛言：「如是，迦叶！命浊、烦恼浊、劫浊、众生浊、见浊，众生善法退减故，大师为诸声闻多制禁戒，少乐习学，迦叶。譬如劫欲坏时，真宝未灭，有诸相似伪宝出于世间；伪宝出已，真宝则没。如是，迦叶！如来正法欲灭之时，有相似像法生；相似像法出世间已，正法则灭。譬如大海中，船载多珍宝，则顿沉没；如来正法则不如是渐渐消灭。如来正法不为地界所坏，不为水、火、风界所坏，乃至恶众生出世，乐行诸恶、欲行诸恶、成就诸恶，非法言法、法言非法、非律言律、律言非律，以相似法，句味炽然，如来正法于此则没。

Kāśyapa! There are five causes and conditions that can cause the Tathāgata’s true Dharma to sink. What are the five? If bhikṣus do not respect, honor, or make offerings to the Great Teacher, and then rely on him to stay, if they do not respect, honor, or make offerings to the Dharma, the teachings, the practices, or the various holy life praised by the Great Teacher, and rely on them to stay, Kāśyapa! These are the five causes and conditions that cause the Tathāgata’s true Dharma to sink.

「迦叶！有五因缘能令如来正法沉没。何等为五？若比丘于大师所，不敬不重，不下意供养；于大师所，不敬不重，不下意供养已，然复依猗而住。若法、若学、若随顺教、若诸梵行，大师所称叹者，不敬不重，不下意供养，而依止住。是名，迦叶！五因缘故，如来正法于此沉没。

Kāśyapa! There are five causes and conditions that cause the Tathāgata’s Dharma and Vinaya not to sink, be forgotten, or decline. What are the five? If bhikṣus respect, honor, and make offerings to the Great Teacher, and rely on him to stay, if they respect, honor, and make offerings to the Dharma, the teachings, the practices, or the various holy life praised by the Great Teacher, and rely on them to stay, Kāśyapa! These are the five causes and conditions that cause the Tathāgata’s Dharma and Vinaya not to sink, be forgotten, or decline. Therefore, Kāśyapa! You should train like this: 'You should respect, honor, and make offerings to the Great Teacher, and rely on him to stay; if the Dharma, the teachings, the practices, or the various holy life praised by the Great Teacher, you should respect, honor, and make offerings to them, and rely on them to stay.'

「迦叶！有五因缘令如来法、律不没、不忘、不退。何等为五？若比丘于大师所，恭敬尊重，下意供养，依止而住，若法、若学、若随顺教、若诸梵行，大师所称叹者，恭敬尊重，下意供养，依止而住。迦叶！是名五因缘如来法、律不没、不忘、不退。是故，迦叶！当如是学：『于大师所，当修恭敬尊重，下意供养，依止而住；若法、若学、若随顺教、若诸梵行，大师所赞叹者，恭敬尊重，下意供养，依止而住。』」

After the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, Venerable Mahākāśyapa rejoiced and followed suit, making obeisance and departing.

佛说是经已，尊者摩诃迦叶欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
