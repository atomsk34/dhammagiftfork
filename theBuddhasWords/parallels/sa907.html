SĀ 907

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 907（九〇七） 动摇

Parallel to: SN 42.2 / SĀ² 2.122

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, the village headman of Charavara came to where the Buddha was, greeted him with a bow, and asked about his well-being. After exchanging greetings, he sat to one side and said to the Buddha, "Gautama! I have heard the elders and sages of the past say that if a performer entertains a large assembly with singing, dancing, and jokes, and performs various tricks to make the assembly happy and joyful, then due to this karmic cause, when their body perishes and their life ends, they will be reborn in the Heaven of Joy. What is said about this in the teachings of Gautama?"

时，有遮罗周罗那罗聚落主来诣佛所，面前问讯慰劳，问讯慰劳已，退坐一面，白佛言：「瞿昙！我闻古昔歌舞戏笑耆年宿士作如是说：『若伎儿于大众中歌舞戏笑，作种种伎，令彼大众欢乐喜笑，以是业缘，身坏命终，生欢喜天。』于此，瞿昙法中所说云何？」

The Buddha told the village headman, "Stop! Do not ask about this matter." He did this a second and a third time, but the village headman still insisted.

佛告聚落主：「且止！莫问此义。」如是再三，犹请不已。

The Buddha said to the village headman, "I will ask you a question. Please answer as you see fit. In the past, the beings in this village were not free from desire, bound by the bonds of desire, not free from anger, bound by the bonds of anger, and not free from delusion, bound by the bonds of delusion. Those performers sat in the assembly and entertained the people with various singing, dancing, and jokes, making them happy and joyful. Village headman! When those people were happy and joyful, did they not increase the bonds of greed, anger, and delusion?"

佛告聚落主：「我今问汝，随汝意答。古昔此聚落众生不离贪欲、贪欲缚所缚，不离瞋恚、瞋恚缚所缚，不离愚痴、愚痴缚所缚。彼诸伎儿于大众坐中，种种歌舞伎乐嬉戏，令彼众人欢乐喜笑。聚落主！当其彼人欢乐喜笑者，岂不增长贪、恚、痴缚耶？」

The village headman said to the Buddha, "Yes, Gautama!"

聚落主白佛言：「如是，瞿昙！」

"Village headman! It is like a person who is bound with a rope. Someone who has long harbored ill will towards this person, wishing to harm them and cause them unhappiness, repeatedly sprinkles water on the rope that binds them. Would this person not become even more tightly bound?"

「聚落主！譬如有人以绳反缚，有人长夜以恶心欲令此人非义饶益，不安不乐，数数以水浇所缚绳，此人被缚岂不转增急耶？」

The village headman said, "Yes, Gautama!"

聚落主言：「如是，瞿昙！」

The Buddha said, "Village headman! The beings of the past were also like this. Not free from the bonds of greed, anger, and delusion, they took pleasure in play, joy, and laughter, which only increased their bonds."

佛言：「聚落主！古昔众生亦复如是，不离贪欲、瞋恚、痴缚，缘彼嬉戏欢乐喜笑，更增其缚。」

The village headman said, "Indeed, Gautama! Those entertainers bring joy and laughter to beings, which only increases their bonds of greed, anger, and delusion. For this reason, it is impossible for them to be reborn in a good realm after their bodies perish and their lives end!"

聚落主言：「实尔，瞿昙！彼诸伎儿令其众生欢乐喜笑，转增贪欲、瞋恚、痴缚。以是因缘，身坏命终，生善趣者，无有是处！」

The Buddha told the village headman, "If you say that the entertainers of the past brought joy and laughter to the masses, and that through this karmic cause they were reborn in the Heaven of Joy, then that is a wrong view! Those with wrong views will be reborn in one of two realms: the realm of hell or the realm of animals."

佛告聚落主：「若言古昔伎儿能令大众欢乐喜笑，以是业缘，生欢喜天者，是则邪见！若邪见者，应生二趣，若地狱趣、若畜生趣。」

When these words were spoken, the village headman of Charavana wept with tears streaming down his face!

说是语时，遮罗周罗那罗聚落主悲泣流泪！

At that time, the World-Honored One told the village headman, "That is why I asked you three times before and you did not answer. I said, 'Village headman, stop! Do not ask about this matter.'"

尔时，世尊告聚落主：「是故我先三问不答，言聚落主：『且止！莫问此义。』」

The village headman said to the Buddha, "Gautama! I do not weep because of what you say. I think to myself, 'How have I been deceived by those foolish, ignorant, and unskillful performers, who say that performing various music in the great assembly can lead to the Heaven of Joy? I now definitely think, "How can performers' singing and dancing lead to the Heaven of Joy?" Gautama! From today onwards, I abandon those performers' evil and unwholesome activities, and take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha of monks."

聚落主白佛言：「瞿昙！我不以瞿昙说故而悲泣也。我自念，昔来云何为彼愚痴不辨不善诸伎儿辈所见欺诳，言大众中作诸伎乐，乃至生欢喜天。我今定思：『云何伎儿歌舞嬉戏生欢喜天？』瞿昙！我从今日，舍彼伎儿恶不善业，归佛、归法、归比丘僧。」

The Buddha said, "Excellent! Village headman! This is the true essential."

佛言：「善哉！聚落主！此真实要。」

At that time, the village headman of Charu-na-ra heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced, and with palms joined, paid homage at the Buddha's feet, delighted and left.

尔时，遮罗周罗那罗聚落主闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，顶礼佛足，欢喜而去。
