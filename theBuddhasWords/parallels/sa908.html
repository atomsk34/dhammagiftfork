SĀ 908

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 908（九〇八） 争斗

Parallel to: SN 42.3 / SĀ² 2.123

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, the village headman Zhantouhu came to where the Buddha was, paid respects and inquired about his well-being. After inquiring about his well-being, he sat to one side and said to the Buddha, "Gautama! I have heard the elders of the past say that if one in battle wears heavy armor, wields sharp weapons, leads the vanguard, and is able to skillfully subdue the enemy, one will be reborn in the heaven of the subjugation of enemies through arrows. What is the meaning of this in the Dharma of Gautama?"

尔时，战鬪活聚落主来诣佛所，恭敬问讯，问讯已，退坐一面，白佛言：「瞿昙！我闻古昔战鬪活耆年宿士作是言：『若战鬪活，身被重铠，手执利器，将士先锋，堪能方便摧伏怨敌，缘此业报，生箭降伏天。』于瞿昙法中，其义云何？」

The Buddha told Zhantouhu, "Stop! Do not ask about this meaning."

佛告战鬪活：「且止！莫问此义。」

He asked this three times, and the Buddha stopped him three times. He still persisted in asking.

如是再三问，亦再三止之，犹问不已。

The Buddha said to the village head, "I will ask you a question. Answer as you wish. Village head, what do you think? If Zhantouhu, wearing armor, is a vanguard warrior, capable of skillfully subduing enemies, would this person not first give rise to a mind of harm, wanting to capture, bind, shackle, chop, stab, and kill them?"

佛告聚落主：「我今问汝，随汝意答。聚落主！于意云何？若战鬪活，身被甲胄，为战士先锋，堪能方便摧伏怨敌，此人岂不先起伤害之心，欲摄缚枷鏁，斫刺杀害于彼耶？」

The village head said to the Buddha, "Yes, World-Honored One!"

聚落主白佛：「如是，世尊！」

The Buddha said to the village head, "For Zhantouhu, there are three kinds of evil, whether by body, speech, or mind. Due to these three kinds of evil causes and conditions, it is impossible for them to, upon the disintegration of the body and the end of life, be reborn in the good realms of the Arrow Subduing Gods."

佛告聚落主：「为战鬪活，有三种恶邪，若身若口若意；以此三种恶邪因缘，身坏命终，得生善趣箭降伏天者，无有是处。」

The Buddha said to the village head, "If the elders of the Zhantouhu of the past had such views and spoke like this, saying that if the Zhantouhu wear armor, hold sharp weapons, and bravely advance to defeat the enemy, they will be reborn in the Arrow Subduing Gods due to these causes and conditions, then this is a wrong view. Those with wrong views will be reborn in two places, either in the hells or as animals."

佛告聚落主：「若古昔战鬪活耆年宿士，作如是见、作如是说，若诸战鬪活，身被甲胄，手执利器，命敌先登，堪能方便摧伏怨敌，以是因缘，生箭降伏天者，是则邪见。邪见之人，应生二处，若地狱趣、若畜生趣。」

When this was said, the village head wept.

说是语时，彼聚落主悲泣流泪！

The Buddha said to the village head, "For this reason, I told you to stop and not speak to you twice before."

佛告聚落主：「以是义故，我先再三语汝：『且止！不为汝说。』」

The village head said to the Buddha, "I do not weep because of what Gautama says. I think of the foolish, ignorant, and long-deceived elders of the past who said, 'If one lives by fighting, wearing armor, wielding sharp weapons, and taking the lead in battle, one will be reborn in the Heaven of Arrow-Subjugation.' Therefore, I weep. I now definitely think that those who live by fighting and evil deeds will not be reborn in the Heaven of Arrow-Subjugation when their bodies perish and their lives end. Gautama! From today, I abandon all evil deeds and take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha of monks."

聚落主白佛言：「我不以瞿昙语故悲泣，我念古昔诸鬪战活耆年宿士愚痴，不善不辨，长夜，欺诳作如是言：『若战鬪活，身被甲胄，手执利器，命敌先登，乃至得生箭降伏天。』是故悲泣。我今定思：『诸战鬪活，恶业因缘，身坏命终，生箭降伏天者，无有是处。』瞿昙！我从今日，舍诸恶业，归佛、归法、归比丘僧。」

The Buddha said to the village head, "This is the true essential."

佛告聚落主：「此真实要。」

At that time, the village head of the fighting and living heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced, and followed his joy. He then rose from his seat, paid homage, and departed.

时，战鬪活聚落主闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，即从坐起，作礼而去。
