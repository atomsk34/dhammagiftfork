SĀ 909

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 909（九〇九） 调马

Parallel to: SN 42.5 / SĀ² 2.124

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, a village headman named Horse Tamer came to where the Buddha was, paid respects, asked about his well-being, and sat to one side.

时，有调马聚落主来诣佛所，恭敬问讯，退坐一面。

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the village headman Horse Tamer, "What are the methods for taming horses?"

尔时，世尊告调马聚落主：「调伏马者，有几种法？」

The village headman replied, "Gautama! There are three methods. What are the three? They are: first, gentleness, second, harshness, and third, both gentleness and harshness."

聚落主答言：「瞿昙！有三种法。何等为三？谓一者柔软，二者刚强，三者柔软刚强。」

The Buddha said to the village headman, "If the horse is still not tamed with the three methods, what should be done?"

佛告聚落主：「若以三种法，马犹不调，当如之何？」

The village headman said, "Should it be killed?"

聚落主言：「便当杀之？」

The village headman said to the Buddha, "Gautama! How many methods should be used to tame the supreme and well-tamed man?"

聚落主白佛言：「瞿昙！无上调御丈夫者，当以几种法调御丈夫？」

The Buddha told the village headman, "I also tame men with three methods. What are the three? First, gentleness, second, harshness, and third, both gentleness and harshness."

佛告聚落主：「我亦以三法调御丈夫。何等为三？一者柔软，二者刚强，三者柔软刚强。」

The village headman said to the Buddha, "Gautama! If the three methods of taming men are still not effective, what should be done?"

聚落主白佛：「瞿昙！若三种调御丈夫，犹不调者，当如之何？」

The Buddha said, "Village headman! If the three methods of taming are still not effective, then kill them. Why? So that my teachings are not humiliated."

佛言：「聚落主！三事调伏犹不调者，便当杀之。所以者何？莫令我法有所屈辱。」

The horse trainer village headman said to the Buddha, "In the Dharma of Gautama, killing is impure. In the Dharma of Gautama, one should not kill. But now you say that those who are not tamed should also be killed?"

调马聚落主白佛言：「瞿昙法中，杀生者不净，瞿昙法中不应杀，而今说言：『不调伏者，亦当杀之。』？」

The Buddha told the village headman, "As you say, in the Tathāgata's Dharma, killing is impure. The Tathāgata should not have killing. Village headman! But I tame men with three kinds of methods. If they are not tamed, I no longer speak to them, no longer teach them, no longer instruct them. Village headman! If the Tathāgata no longer speaks to men, no longer teaches them, no longer instructs them, isn't that killing?"

佛告聚落主：「如汝所言：『如来法中，杀生者不净，如来不应有杀。』聚落主！然我以三种法调御丈夫，彼不调者，不复与语，不复教授，不复教诫。聚落主！若如来调御丈夫，不复与语，不复教授，不复教诫，岂非杀耶？」

The horse trainer village headman said to the Buddha, "Gautama! If the tamed men no longer speak, no longer teach, no longer instruct, it truly is killing. Therefore, from today, I abandon all evil deeds and take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha of monks."

调马聚落主白佛言：「瞿昙！若调御丈夫不复与语，不复教授，不复教诫，真为杀也。是故我从今日，舍诸恶业，归佛、归法、归比丘僧。」

The Buddha told the village headman, "This is the true essential."

佛告聚落主：「此真实要。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the horse trainer village headman, having heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced and followed his joy. He then rose from his seat, paid homage, and departed.

佛说此经已，调马聚落主闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，即从坐起，作礼而去。
