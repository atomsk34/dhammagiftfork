SĀ 91

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 91（九一） 郁阇迦

Parallel to: AN 8.54 (~) / AN 8.55 / SĀ² 2.91

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, there was a young brahmin named Uccaṅga who came to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha’s feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, “World-Honored One! How many practices should a layperson at home follow in order to attain peace and happiness in this life?”

时，有年少婆罗门名欝阇迦，来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！俗人在家当行几法，得现法安及现法乐？」

The Buddha told the brahmin, “There are four practices that a layperson at home can follow to attain peace and happiness in this life. What are the four? They are: being skillful, being guarded, having good friends, and having a correct livelihood.

佛告婆罗门：「有四法，俗人在家得现法安、现法乐。何等为四？谓方便具足、守护具足、善知识具足、正命具足。

What is meant by being skillful? It means that a good man engages in various crafts and occupations to support himself, such as farming, commerce, government work, or writing, accounting, and drawing. Diligent practice in those crafts and occupations is called being skillful.

「何等为方便具足？谓善男子种种工巧业处以自营生，谓种田、商贾，或以王事，或以书疏算画。于彼彼工巧业处精勤修行，是名方便具足。

What is meant by being guarded? It means that a good man obtains money and grain through skillful means, earned through his own labor and obtained lawfully. He is able to protect them well, preventing them from being stolen by kings, thieves, water, or fire, or from being lost through negligence. He does not let those who are not loved take them, nor does he let them be destroyed by various calamities. This is called being well guarded by a good man.

「何等为守护具足？谓善男子所有钱谷，方便所得，自手执作，如法而得，能极守护，不令王、贼、水、火劫夺漂没令失，不善守护者亡失，不爱念者辄取，及诸灾患所坏，是名善男子善守护。

What is meant by having good friends? It means that a good man does not fall into the stream, is not negligent, is not deceitful, and is not treacherous. Such friends are able to comfort well, preventing the arising of sorrow and suffering that has not yet arisen, and causing the awakening of sorrow and suffering that has already arisen. They cause joy and happiness that has not yet arisen to quickly arise, and protect joy and happiness that has already arisen from being lost. This is called having good friends by a good man.

「何等为善知识具足？若有善男子不落度、不放逸、不虚妄、不凶险，如是知识能善安慰，未生忧苦能令不生，已生忧苦能令开觉，未生喜乐能令速生，已生喜乐护令不失，是名善男子善知识具足。

What is meant by having a correct livelihood? It means that a good man weighs and measures his money and goods, and manages them well, not allowing more to come in than go out, or more to go out than come in. Like a person holding a scale, he adds to the light side and subtracts from the heavy side, and stops when it is balanced. In this way, a good man weighs and measures his wealth, spending and earning equally, not allowing more to come in than go out, or more to go out than come in. If a good man has no money and wealth but spends it lavishly, this is called living like a rare udumbara flower, without seeds, foolishly greedy, not caring about the future. Or if a good man has abundant wealth but cannot use it, others will say he is a foolish person, like a starving dog. Therefore, a good man should be able to weigh and measure his money and goods, spending and earning equally. This is called having a correct livelihood. In this way, brahmin, one who accomplishes these four dharmas will be at peace and happy in this life.

「云何为正命具足？谓善男子所有钱财出内称量，周圆掌护，不令多入少出也、多出少入也。如执秤者，少则增之，多则减之，知平而舍。如是，善男子称量财物，等入等出，莫令入多出少、出多入少，若善男子无有钱财而广散用，以此生活，人皆名为优昙钵果，无有种子，愚痴贪欲，不顾其后。或有善男子财物丰多，不能食用，傍人皆言是愚痴人如饿死狗。是故，善男子所有钱财能自称量，等入等出，是名正命具足。如是，婆罗门！四法成就，现法安、现法乐。」

The brahmin said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! How many dharmas can a householder have to bring peace and happiness in future lives?"

婆罗门白佛言：「世尊！在家之人有几法，能令后世安、后世乐？」

The Buddha told the brahmin, "A householder can have four dharmas to bring peace and happiness in future lives. What are the four? They are having faith, precepts, generosity, and wisdom.

佛告婆罗门：「在家之人有四法，能令后世安、后世乐。何等为四？谓信具足、戒具足、施具足、慧具足。

What is having faith? It means that a good man has a mind of faith and respect towards the Tathāgata, establishes the foundation of faith, which cannot be destroyed by any gods, demons, Brahmas, or other worldly people.

「何等为信具足？谓善男子于如来所，得信敬心，建立信本，非诸天、魔、梵及余世人同法所坏，是名善男子信具足。

What is having precepts? It means that a good man does not kill, steal, engage in sexual misconduct, lie, or drink alcohol.

「何等戒具足？谓善男子不杀生、不偷盗、不邪婬、不妄语、不饮酒，是名戒具足。

What is having generosity? It means that a good man, while living at home, is free from the mind of stinginess and defilement, practices giving with liberation, always gives with his own hands, delights in practicing giving, and practices giving with an equal mind.

「云何施具足？谓善男子离悭垢心，在于居家，行解脱施，常自手与，乐修行舍，等心行施，是名善男子施具足。

What is having wisdom? It means that a good man truly knows the noble truth of suffering, the noble truth of the origin of suffering, the noble truth of the cessation of suffering, and the noble truth of the path. This is called a good man having wisdom. If a good man practices these four things at home, he will attain peace and happiness in the next life.

「云何为慧具足？谓善男子苦圣谛如实知，习、灭、道圣谛如实知，是名善男子慧具足。若善男子在家行此四法者，能得后世安、后世乐。」

At that time, the World-Honored One again spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊复说偈言：

Establishing various practices with skillful means, accumulating and being able to protect, knowing
and associating with good men, living righteously for oneself. Pure faith and precepts
are complete, giving away and removing the defilement of stinginess, purifying the swift
path, attaining peace and happiness in the next world. If dwelling in a
household, accomplishing these eight dharmas, carefully and respectfully speaking what is said, equally
and rightly awakened to what is known. Attaining peace in this present life
joyfully dwelling in happiness in this present life, joyfully dwelling in happiness in
the next world.

「方便建诸业，　　积集能守护，知识善男子，　　正命以自活。净信戒具足，　　惠施离悭垢，净除于速道，　　得后世安乐。若处于居家，　　成就于八法，审谛尊所说，　　等正觉所知。现法得安隐，　　现法喜乐住，后世喜乐住。」

Having spoken this sūtra, Ujaka heard what the Buddha had said, rejoiced, bowed, and departed.

佛说此经已，欝阇迦闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
