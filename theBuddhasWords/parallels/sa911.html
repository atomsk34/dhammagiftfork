SĀ 911

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 911（九一一） 顶发

Parallel to: SN 42.10 / SĀ² 2.126

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, the village headman Maṇiśikhin came to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. He said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! The other day, the king gathered the ministers and discussed, 'How do the śramaṇa disciples of the Śākya monks accept and keep gold, silver, and precious things for themselves? Is it pure or impure?' Some said, 'The śramaṇa disciples of the Śākya should accept and keep gold, silver, and precious things for themselves.' Others said, 'They should not accept and keep gold, silver, and precious things for themselves.' World-Honored One! Those who say that the śramaṇa disciples of the Śākya should accept and keep gold, silver, and precious things for themselves, do they hear it from the Buddha or do they speak it themselves? Those who speak like this, do they follow the Dharma or not? Do they speak truthfully or falsely? Will those who speak like this not fall into a place of blame?"

时，有摩尼珠髻聚落主来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！先日国王集诸大臣，共论议言：『云何沙门释子比丘自为受畜金银宝物，为净耶？为不净耶？』其中有言：『沙门释子应受畜金银宝物。』又复有言：『不应自为受畜金银宝物。』世尊！彼言沙门释子应自为受畜金银宝物者，为从佛闻？为自出意说？作是语者，为随顺法？为不随顺？为真实说？为虚妄说？如是说者，得不堕于呵责处耶？」

The Buddha told the village headman, "This is false speech, not true speech, not Dharma speech, not following speech, and falls into a place of blame. Why is that? Because the śramaṇa disciples of the Śākya accept and keep gold, silver, and precious things for themselves, it is impure. If they accept and keep gold, silver, and precious things for themselves, it is not the Dharma of śramaṇas, not the Dharma of the Śākya clan."

佛告聚落主：「此则妄说，非真实说、非是法说、非随顺说，堕呵责处。所以者何？沙门释子自为受畜金银宝物者，不清净故；若自为己受畜金银宝物者，非沙门法、非释种子法。」

The village headman said to the Buddha, "How wonderful! World-Honored One! The śramaṇa disciples of the Śākya accepting and keeping gold, silver, and precious things for themselves is not the Dharma of śramaṇas, not the Dharma of the Śākya clan. This is true speech! World-Honored One! Those who speak like this increase the supreme and wonderful. I also say like this: 'The śramaṇa disciples of the Śākya should not accept and keep gold, silver, and precious things for themselves.'"

聚落主白佛言：「奇哉！世尊！沙门释子受畜金银宝物者，非沙门法、非释种子法，此真实说！世尊！作是说者，增长胜妙，我亦作是说：『沙门释子不应自为受畜金银宝物。』」

The Buddha told the village headman, "If the śramaṇa disciples of the Śākya accept and keep gold, silver, and precious things for themselves and it is pure, then the five desires and merits should all be pure!"

佛告聚落主：「若沙门释子自为受畜金银珍宝清净者，五欲功德悉应清净！」

The village headman with a topknot of jewels, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully bowed and departed.

摩尼珠髻聚落主闻佛所说，欢喜作礼而去。

At that time, the World-Honored One, knowing that the village headman with a topknot of jewels had left, told Venerable Ānanda, "Call all the bhikṣus who are staying in the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove to gather in the dining hall."

尔时，世尊知摩尼珠髻聚落主去已，告尊者阿难：「若诸比丘依止迦兰陀竹园住者，悉呼令集于食堂。」

At that time, Venerable Ānanda accepted the Buddha's instruction and thoroughly announced, "Call all the bhikṣus who are staying in the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove to gather in the dining hall." When the bhikṣus had gathered, he went to inform the World-Honored One, "The bhikṣus have already gathered in the dining hall. May the World-Honored One know the time!"

时，尊者阿难即受佛教，周遍宣令依止迦兰陀竹园比丘集于食堂。比丘集已，往白世尊：「诸比丘已集食堂，惟世尊知时！」

At that time, the World-Honored One went to the dining hall, took his seat before the assembly, and said to the bhikṣus, "Today, the village headman with a topknot of jewels came to see me and said, 'Recently, the king gathered the ministers and discussed this: "Do the śramaṇas, sons of Śākya, themselves accept gold, silver, and precious things? Is it pure or not?" Some said it is pure, some said it is not pure. Now I ask the World-Honored One, who said it is pure, did they hear it from the Buddha or did they speak falsely on their own?" ...' and so on, as above, in detail. The village headman with a topknot of jewels, having heard my explanation, rejoiced and followed in rejoicing. He bowed and left.

尔时，世尊往诣食堂，大众前坐，坐已，告诸比丘：「今日有摩尼珠髻聚落主来谅我所，作如是言：『先日国王集诸大臣，作如是论议：「沙门释子自为受畜金银宝物，为清净不？」其中有言清净者，有言不清净者。今问世尊，言清净者，为从佛闻？为自妄说？』……」如上广说。「彼摩尼珠髻聚落主闻我所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。

"Bhikṣus! The king and ministers gathered to discuss. That village headman with a topknot of jewels roared like a lion in the assembly, saying, 'The śramaṇas, sons of Śākya, should not themselves accept gold, silver, and precious things.' Bhikṣus! From today onwards, you should ask for wood when you need wood, ask for grass when you need grass, ask for a cart when you need a cart, ask for a worker when you need a worker. Do not accept gold, silver, and various precious things for yourselves!"

「诸比丘！国王、大臣共集论议，彼摩尼珠髻聚落主于大众前师子吼说：『沙门释种子不应自为受畜金银宝物。』诸比丘！汝等从今日，须木索木、须草索草、须车索车、须作人索作人，慎勿为己受取金银种种宝物！」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished. The bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
