SĀ 912

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 912（九一二） 王发

Parallel to: SN 42.12 / SĀ² 2.127

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Thus have I heard:

一时，佛住瞻婆国揭伽池侧。时，有王顶聚落主来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the headman of the village of Rājagṛha: "Now, sentient beings rely on two extremes. What are the two? One is to delight in and cling to the inferior, rustic, ordinary, and foolish pleasures of the five desires. The other is to practice self-mortification, which is incorrect and not beneficial. Headman, there are three kinds of pleasure in enjoying desires, which are inferior, rustic, ordinary, and foolish; and there are three kinds of self-mortification, which are incorrect and not beneficial.

尔时，世尊告王顶聚落主：「今者众生依于二边。何等为二？一者乐著卑下、田舍、常人凡夫五欲。二者自苦方便，不正、非义饶益。聚落主！有三种乐受欲乐，卑下、田舍、常人凡夫；有三种自苦方便，不正、非义饶益。

Headman, what are the three kinds of inferior, rustic, ordinary, and foolish pleasures in enjoying desires? There are those who enjoy desires in an improper and indiscriminate way, not for their own peace and happiness, not to provide for their parents, support their brothers, wives, children, servants, relatives, friends, and acquaintances, nor to make timely offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas in the hope of attaining a superior state of peace and happiness and a future birth in heaven. This is called the first kind of worldly enjoyment of desires.

「聚落主！何等为三种卑下、田舍、常人凡夫乐受欲乐？有受欲者，非法滥取，不以安乐自供，不供养父母、给足兄弟、妻子、奴婢、眷属、朋友、知识，亦不随时供养沙门、婆罗门、仰求胜处安乐果报、未来生天，是名世间第一受欲。

Furthermore, headman, those who enjoy desires take wealth through proper and improper means, enjoy themselves, provide for their parents, support their brothers, wives, children, servants, relatives, friends, and acquaintances, but do not make timely offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas in the hope of attaining a superior state of peace and happiness and a future birth in heaven. This is called the second kind of enjoyment of desires.

「复次，聚落主！受欲乐者，以法、非法滥取财物，以乐自供，供养父母，给足、兄弟、妻子、奴婢、眷属、朋友、知识，而不随时供养沙门、婆罗门、仰求胜处安乐果报、未来生天，是名第二受欲乐者。

Furthermore, headman, those who enjoy desires seek wealth through proper means, not through improper means, enjoy themselves, provide for their parents, support their brothers, wives, children, servants, relatives, and acquaintances, and make timely offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas in the hope of attaining a superior state of peace and happiness and a future birth in heaven. This is called the third kind of enjoyment of desires.

「复次，聚落主！有受欲乐者，以法求财，不以滥取，以乐自供，供养父母，给足兄弟、妻子、奴婢、眷属、知识，随时供养沙门、婆罗门，仰求胜处安乐果报，未来生天，是名第三受欲乐者。

Headman, I do not say that all enjoyment of desires is equal. I say that those who enjoy desires are inferior, I say that those who enjoy desires are middling, I say that those who enjoy desires are superior.

「聚落主！我不一向说受欲平等，我说受欲者其人卑下，我说受欲者是其中人，我说受欲者是其胜人。

What is meant by inferior enjoyment of desires? It means seeking wealth through improper means, up to not seeking a superior state of peace and happiness and a future birth in heaven. This is what I call inferior enjoyment of desires.

「何等为卑下受欲者？谓非法滥取，乃至不仰求胜处安乐果报、未来生天，是名我说卑下者受欲。

What is meant by middling enjoyment of desires? It means seeking wealth through both proper and improper means, up to not seeking a future birth in heaven. This is what I call the second kind of middling enjoyment of desires.

「何等为中人受欲？谓受欲者以法、非法而求财物，乃至不求未来生天，是名我说第二中人受欲。

What is meant by superior enjoyment of desires? It means seeking wealth through proper means, up to seeking a future birth in heaven. This is what I call the third kind of superior enjoyment of desires.

「何等为我说胜人受欲？谓彼以法求财，乃至未来生天，是名我说第三胜人受欲。

What are the three kinds of self-mortification that are unwholesome, incorrect, and not conducive to benefit? There is one who mortifies himself and lives in a state of emaciation. Initially he violates the precepts and defiles the precepts. He practices various austerities and diligently abides in a dwelling. He is unable to attain the state of freedom from burning, the superhuman state, the sublime knowledge and vision, and the peaceful dwelling in this very life. Householder, this is called the first kind of self-mortification that leads to emaciation.

「何等为三种自苦方便？是苦非法、不正、非义饶益？有一自苦枯槁活，初始犯戒、污戒，彼修种种苦行，精勤方便住处住，彼不能现法得离炽然、过人法、胜妙知见安乐住。聚落主！是名第一自苦方便枯槁活。

Furthermore, there is self-mortification that leads to emaciation, but one does not initially violate the precepts or defile the precepts. One practices various austerities, but one does not attain the state of freedom from burning, the superhuman state, the sublime knowledge and vision, and the peaceful dwelling in this very life through this. This is called the second kind of self-mortification that leads to emaciation.

「复次，自苦方便枯槁活，始不犯戒、污戒，而修种种苦行，亦不由此现法得离炽然、过人法、胜妙知见安乐住，是名第二自苦方便枯槁活。

Furthermore, there is self-mortification that leads to emaciation, but one does not initially violate the precepts or defile the precepts. One practices various austerities, but one is also unable to attain the state of freedom from burning, the superhuman state, the sublime knowledge and vision, and the peaceful dwelling in this very life through this. This is called the third kind of self-mortification that leads to emaciation.

「复次，自苦方便枯槁活，不初始犯戒、污戒，然修种种苦行方便，亦不能现法离炽然、得过人法、胜妙知见安乐住，是名第三自苦方便枯槁活。

Householder, I do not say that all self-mortification that leads to emaciation is the same. I say that some self-mortification that leads to emaciation is for inferior people, some is for middling people, and some is for superior people.

「聚落主！我不说一切自苦方便枯槁活悉等，我说有自苦方便是卑劣人，有说自苦方便是中人，有说自苦方便是胜人。

What is self-mortification that leads to emaciation for inferior people? If one's self-mortification that leads to emaciation initially violates the precepts and defiles the precepts, and one does not attain the sublime knowledge and vision and peaceful dwelling, I say that this is self-mortification that leads to emaciation for inferior people.

「何等自苦方便卑劣人？若彼自苦方便，初始犯戒、污戒，乃至不得胜妙知见安乐住，是名我说自苦方便卑劣人。

What is self-mortification that leads to emaciation for middling people? If one's self-mortification that leads to emaciation does not initially violate the precepts and defile the precepts, and one does not attain the sublime knowledge and vision and peaceful dwelling, I say that this is self-mortification that leads to emaciation for middling people.

「何等为自苦方便中人？若彼自苦方便，不初始犯戒、污戒，乃至不得胜妙知见安乐住，是名我说自苦方便中间人。

What is self-mortification that leads to emaciation for superior people? If one's self-mortification that leads to emaciation is emaciated and alive, does not initially violate the precepts and defile the precepts, and does not attain the sublime knowledge and vision and peaceful dwelling, I say that this is self-mortification that leads to emaciation for superior people.

「何等为自苦方便胜人？若彼自苦方便枯槁活，不初始犯戒、污戒，乃至不得胜妙知见安乐住，是名我说自苦方便胜人。

Householder, these are the three kinds of self-mortification that lead to emaciation. They are suffering, not the Dharma, not right, and not beneficial.

「聚落主！是名三种自苦方便，是苦非法、不正、非义饶益。

"Householder, there is a path and a way that does not lead to the three kinds of sensual pleasures and their corresponding methods, which are inferior, rustic, and ordinary, and that does not lead to the three kinds of self-mortification that are suffering, not the Dharma, not right, and not beneficial. Householder, what is the path and what is the way that does not lead to the three kinds of sensual pleasures and the three kinds of self-mortification? Householder, because of the hindrance of desire and greed, one may wish to harm oneself, harm others, or harm both oneself and others. In this life and the next, one will attain the retribution of suffering, with mental afflictions and suffering. Because of the hindrance of anger and delusion, one may wish to harm oneself, harm others, or harm both oneself and others. In this life and the next, one will attain the retribution of suffering, with mental afflictions and suffering. If one is free from the hindrance of desire and does not wish to harm oneself, harm others, or harm both oneself and others, one will not attain the retribution of suffering in this life and the next. One's mind and mental factors will always experience joy and happiness. In this way, if one is free from the hindrances of anger and delusion, one will not wish to harm oneself, harm others, or harm both oneself and others. One will not attain the retribution of suffering in this life and the next. One's mind and mental factors will always experience peace and happiness. In this life, one will be far from burning and not wait for the right time. One will be close to nirvana, and one will personally realize it in this very life. Householder, in this way, one is far from burning in this very life, does not wait for the right time, is close to nirvana, and personally realizes it in this very life. This is the eightfold noble path - right view, and so on, up to right concentration."

「聚落主！有道有迹，不向三种受欲随顺方便，卑下、田舍、常人凡夫，不向三种自苦方便，是苦非法、不正、非义饶益。聚落主！何等为道，何等为迹，不向三种受欲、三种自苦方便？聚落主！为欲贪障阂故，或欲自害，或欲害他，或欲俱害，现法后世得斯罪报，心法忧苦。瞋恚、痴所障，或欲自害，或欲害他，或欲俱害，现法后世得斯罪报，心法忧苦。若离贪障，不欲方便自害、害他、自他俱害，不现法后世受斯罪报；彼心、心法常受喜乐，如是离瞋恚、愚痴障阂，不欲自害，不欲害他、自他俱害，不现法后世受斯罪报；彼心、心法常受安乐，于现法中，远离炽然，不待时节，亲近涅槃，即此身现缘自觉知。聚落主！如此现法永离炽然，不待时节，亲近涅槃，即此现身缘自觉知者，为八圣道——正见乃至正定。」

When the World-Honored One spoke this Dharma, the village headman Wangding was free from defilements and attained the pure Dharma eye. At that time, the village headman Wangding saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, understood the Dharma, deeply penetrated the Dharma, crossed over doubts, and was not dependent on others. He attained fearlessness in the true Dharma and Vinaya. He immediately rose from his seat, adjusted his robes, joined his palms, and said to the Buddha, "I am now liberated. World-Honored One! I take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha of bhikṣus. From now until the end of my life, I will be an upāsaka."

当其世尊说是法时，王顶聚落主远尘离垢，得法眼净。时，王顶聚落主见法、得法、知法、深入于法，度疑不由于他，于正法、律得无所畏。即从坐起，整衣服，合掌白佛：「我今已度。世尊！归佛、归法、归比丘僧，从今尽寿为优婆塞。」

At that time, having heard the Buddha's teaching, he rejoiced and followed it. He bowed and departed.

时，闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
