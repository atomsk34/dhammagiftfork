SĀ 916

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 916（九一六） 说何论

Parallel to: SN 42.8 / SĀ² 2.131

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Good Clothing Mango Grove in the village of Nala.

一时，佛住那罗聚落好衣庵罗园中。

At that time, the headman of the Kṣatriya clan, a disciple of the Nirgranthas, came to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side.

时，有刀师氏聚落主，尼揵弟子，来诣佛所，稽首礼足，退坐一面。

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the headman, "What do you want to discuss? What did Nirgrantha Jñātiputra say?"

尔时，世尊告聚落主：「欲何所论？尼揵若提子为何所说？」

The headman said, "That Nirgrantha Jñātiputra said, 'Those who kill all fall into the hells, because of their many actions they are led there; likewise, those who steal, engage in sexual misconduct, and speak falsehoods all fall into the hells, because of their many actions they are led there.'"

聚落主言：「彼尼揵若提子说：『杀生者，一切皆堕泥犁中，以多行故，则将至彼；如是盗、邪婬、妄语皆堕泥犁中，以多行故，则将至彼。』」

The Buddha told the headman, "If it is as Nirgrantha Jñātiputra said, those who kill all fall into the hells, because of their many actions they are led there, then there would be no sentient beings falling into the hells. Headman, what do you think? Which sentient beings have the intention to kill at all times? At what time do they have the intention not to kill? Up to what time do they have the intention to speak falsehoods, and at what time do they have the intention not to speak falsehoods?"

佛告聚落主：「若如尼犍若提子说杀生者堕泥犁中，以多行故，而往生彼者，则无有众生堕泥犁中。聚落主！于意云何？何等众生于一切时有心杀生？复于何时有心不杀生，乃至何时有心妄语，何时有心不妄语？」

The headman said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! People have the intention to kill and speak falsehoods for a short time during the day and night, but for a long time they do not have the intention to kill or speak falsehoods."

聚落主白佛言：「世尊！人于昼夜，少时有心杀生，乃至少时有心妄语，而多时不有心杀生，乃至妄语。」

The Buddha told the headman, "If it is so, then would there not be no one falling into the hells? As Nirgrantha said: 'Those who kill all fall into the hells, because of their many actions they are led there. Up to those who speak falsehoods are also like this.' Headman! That great teacher appeared in the world, awakened, contemplated, entered the state of contemplation, and abided. On the ground of ordinary beings, he spoke for himself, contemplated as he pleased, and taught his disciples in this way: 'Those who kill all fall into the hells, because of their many actions they are led there. Up to those who speak falsehoods are also like this.' His disciples, if they believe his words, say: 'Our great teacher knows what he knows, sees what he sees, and can teach his disciples like this: "Those who kill all fall into the hells, because of their many actions they are led there." We have had the intention to kill, steal, engage in sexual misconduct, and speak falsehoods, and we will fall into the hells.' They attain such views, and do not abandon those views, do not dislike those actions, do not awaken to those regrets. In future lives, they do not abandon killing, up to not abandoning speaking falsehoods. Their liberation of mind is not fulfilled, and their liberation of wisdom is also not fulfilled. Because their liberation of mind is not fulfilled and their liberation of wisdom is not fulfilled, they slander the noble ones with wrong views. Because of the causes and conditions of wrong views, when their bodies perish and their lives end, they are born in the evil destinies, in the hells. Thus, headman! There are causes and conditions for afflictions in sentient beings, and there are causes and conditions for afflictions in sentient beings' actions.

佛告聚落主：「若如是者，岂非无有人堕于泥犁中耶？如尼揵所说：『有人杀生者，一切堕泥犁中，多习行者将往生彼，乃至妄语亦复如是。』聚落主！彼大师出兴于世，觉想筹量，入觉想地住。于凡夫地自辩所说，随意筹量，为诸弟子作如是说法，言杀生者，一切皆堕泥犁中，多习行将往生彼，乃至妄语亦复如是。彼诸弟子若信其说，言：『我大师知其所知，见其所见，能为弟子作如是说：「若杀生者，一切皆堕泥犁中，多习行故，将往生彼。」我本有心杀生、偷盗、邪婬、妄语，当堕泥犁中。』得如是见，乃至不舍此见，不厌彼业，不觉彼悔，于未来世，不舍杀生，乃至不舍妄语，彼意解脱不满足，慧解脱亦不满足。意解脱不满足、慧解脱不满足故，则为谤圣邪见；邪见因缘故，身坏命终，生恶趣泥犁中。如是，聚落主！有因、有缘众生烦恼，有因、有缘众生业烦恼。

Headman! The Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, the One with Perfect Knowledge and Conduct, the Well-Gone One, the Knower of the World, the Unsurpassed One, the Tamer of Men, the Teacher of Gods and Men, the Buddha, the World-Honored One appears in the world, always reproaching sentient beings for killing, praising not killing; reproaching stealing, sexual misconduct, and false speech, praising not stealing, not sexual misconduct, and not false speech. He always uses this Dharma to teach the Shravakas, causing them to remember, enjoy, believe, and respect, saying: 'Our great teacher knows what he knows, sees what he sees, reproaches killing, praises not killing, up to reproaches false speech, praises not false speech. From the past until now, because of our ignorance and lack of wisdom, we have had the intention to kill. Because of this, we now repent.' Although he cannot make them not commit those actions, still because of this repentance, in future lives they will be able to avoid killing, up to avoiding stealing, sexual misconduct, and false speech. They will also be able to fully attain liberation of mind and liberation of wisdom. Having fully attained liberation of mind and liberation of wisdom, they will not slander the sages, and will accomplish right views. Because of the causes and conditions of right views, they will be born in the good destinies, in the heavens. Thus, headman! There are causes and conditions for the purification of sentient beings' afflictions and actions.

「聚落主！如来、应、等正觉、明行足、善逝、世间解、无上士、调御丈夫、天人师、佛世尊出兴于世，常为众生呵责杀生，赞叹不杀；呵责偷盗、邪婬、妄语，赞叹不盗、不婬、不妄语。常以此法，化诸声闻，令念、乐、信、重，言：『我大师知其所知，见其所见，呵责杀生，赞叹不杀，乃至呵责妄语，赞叹不妄语，我从昔来，以愚痴无慧，有心杀生，我缘是故，今自悔责。』虽不能令彼业不为，且因此悔责故，于未来世，得离杀生，乃至得离盗、婬、妄语，亦得满足正意解脱，满足慧解脱，意解脱、慧解脱满足已，得不谤贤圣，正见成就；正见因缘故，得生善趣天上。如是，聚落主！有因、有缘众生业烦恼清净。

"Headman! Those Shravakas of great learning study thus: 'Observing the arising of mind, whether it is killing or not killing, throughout the day and night.' If there is a mind to kill, they should repent and not be like that. If there is no mind to kill, they have no enmity or hatred, and give rise to joy. Having given rise to joy, joy arises. Having given rise to joy, the mind rests in tranquility. Having the mind rest in tranquility, one experiences bliss. Having experienced bliss, the mind becomes concentrated. Having the mind become concentrated, the Shravakas of the noble ones, with minds full of loving-kindness, without enmity or jealousy, without anger or resentment, vast and immeasurable, fully abide in the right concentration of one direction; two directions, three directions, up to four directions, the four intermediate directions, above and below, the entire world, with minds full of loving-kindness, without enmity or jealousy, without anger or resentment, vast and immeasurable, well cultivated, filling all directions, fully abiding in the right concentration."

「聚落主！彼多闻圣弟子作如是学：『随时昼夜观察所起少有心杀生、多有心不杀生。』若于有心杀生，当自悔责，不是不类。若不有心杀生，无怨无憎，心生随喜；随喜已，欢喜生；欢喜已，心猗息；心猗息已，心受乐；受乐已，则心定。心定已，圣弟子心与慈俱，无怨无嫉，无有瞋恚，广大无量，满于一方，正受住；二方、三方，乃至四方、四维、上下、一切世间，心与慈俱，无怨无嫉，无有瞋恚，广大无量，善修习，充满诸方，具足正受住。」

At that time, the World-Honored One took a small amount of soil with his fingernails and said to the headman of the Kshudra clan, "What do you think, headman? Is the soil on my fingernails more, or is the earth more?"

尔时，世尊以爪甲抄少土，语刀师氏聚落主言：「云何？聚落主！我爪甲土多？大地为多？」

The headman said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! The soil on the fingernails is very little, while the earth is immeasurable and countless."

聚落主白佛言：「世尊！爪甲土少少耳，大地土无量无数。」

The Buddha told the headman, "Just as the soil on the fingernails is very little, and the earth is immeasurable in number, likewise, when the mind is accompanied by loving-kindness, cultivated and practiced much, all limited actions are like the soil on the fingernails, unable to take away or cause to abide. Likewise, stealing is countered with a compassionate mind, sexual misconduct is countered with a joyful mind, false speech is countered with a mind of equanimity, and it cannot be compared."

佛告聚落主：「如甲上之土甚少，大地之土其数无量。如是心与慈俱，修习多修习，诸有量业者，如甲上土，不能将去，不能令住。如是偷盗对以悲心，邪婬对以喜心，妄语对以舍心，不得为比。」

When this was said, the headman of the knife-maker clan was free from defilements and attained the pure Dharma eye. The headman saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, realized the Dharma, knew the Dharma, penetrated deeply into the Dharma, was free from doubts, did not rely on others, and was not led by others. He attained fearlessness in the true Dharma and Vinaya. He rose from his seat, arranged his robes, touched his right knee to the ground, joined his palms, and said to the Buddha, "I am liberated, World-Honored One! I have crossed over, World-Honored One! I now take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha of monks, for as long as I live as a lay devotee. World-Honored One! It is like a person seeking light, taking a horse's tail as a wick for a lamp, trying to blow it into flame, but never getting any light, only exhausting themselves in vain, and the lamp never being lit. I was also like this, seeking wisdom, but associating with the foolish Nirgrantha Jains, practicing with the foolish, uniting with the foolish, and serving the foolish, only exhausting myself in vain, not attaining wisdom. Therefore, I now take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha again. From now on, I will have little faith, respect, love, and mindfulness for those foolish, unskillful, and unwise Nirgrantha Jains, and will now distance myself from them. Therefore, I now take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha for the third time, and will be a lay devotee for as long as I live, purifying my mind."

说是语时，刀师氏聚落主远尘离垢，得法眼净，聚落主见法、得法、觉法、知法、深入于法，离诸狐疑不由于他、不随于他，于正法、律得无所畏。从坐起，整衣服，右膝著地，合掌白佛：「我已度。世尊！已越。世尊！我今归佛、归法、归比丘僧，尽其寿命为优婆塞。世尊！譬如士夫欲求灯明，取其马尾，以为灯炷，欲吹令然，终不得明，徒自疲劳，灯竟不然。我亦如是，欲求明智，于诸愚痴尼揵子所，愚痴习近，愚痴和合，愚痴奉事，徒自劳苦，不得明智，是故我今重归依佛、归法、归僧。从今以去，于彼尼揵愚痴不善不辩者所，少信、少敬、少爱、少念，于今远离。是故，我今第三归佛、归法、归僧，乃至尽寿，为优婆塞，自净其心。」

At that time, the headman of the knife-maker clan, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and followed it with joy, paid homage, and departed.

时，刀师氏聚落主闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
