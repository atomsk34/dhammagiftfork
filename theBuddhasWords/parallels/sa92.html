SĀ 92

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 92（九二） 憍慢

Parallel to: SN 7.15 / SĀ² 2.258

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling through the country of Kosala and arrived at Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在拘萨罗人间游行，至舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, there was a conceited brāhman living in Śrāvastī. His parents' families were both pure, without blemish. He was able to speak, and his seven generations of ancestors were all pure. He was a brāhman teacher, proficient in debate, and well-versed in all the treatises and records. He understood the superiority and inferiority of the teachings, and clearly explained the various phrases and sentences. He was handsome and had a high sense of self, whether it was due to his family, appearance, intelligence, or wealth. He did not respect his parents, elders, or teachers. He heard that the śramaṇa Gautama was traveling through the country of Kosala and had arrived at Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī. Having heard this, he thought, "I shall go to where the śramaṇa Gautama is. If he says something, I shall debate with him; if he says nothing, I shall return in silence."

尔时，舍卫国有憍慢婆罗门止住，父母种姓俱净，无瑕点能说者，七世相承悉皆清净；为婆罗门师，言论通达，诸论记典悉了万名，解法优劣，分明诸，句句记说，容貌端正。或生志高、族姓志高、容色志高、聪明志高、财富志高，不敬父母、诸尊、师长。闻沙门瞿昙在拘萨罗国人间游行，至舍卫国祇树给孤独园。闻已，作是念：「我当往彼沙门瞿昙所，若有所说，我当共论；无所说者，默然而还。」

At that time, the conceited brāhman rode in a white horse carriage, with young brāhmans leading the way in front and following behind. He held a golden-handled umbrella and a golden bottle in his hand. He went to see the World-Honored One. When he reached the gate of the garden, he got down from the carriage and walked.

时，憍慢婆罗门乘白马车，诸年少婆罗门前后导从，持金柄伞盖，手执金瓶，往见世尊。至于园门，下车步进。

At that time, the World-Honored One was teaching the Dharma surrounded by a great assembly, but he did not pay attention to the conceited brāhman.

尔时，世尊与诸大众围遶说法，不时顾念憍慢婆罗门。

At that time, the conceited brāhman thought, "The śramaṇa Gautama does not pay attention to me. I should go back now."

时，憍慢婆罗门作是念：「沙门瞿昙不顾念我，且当还去。」

At that time, the World-Honored One, knowing the thoughts of the conceited brāhman, spoke a verse:

尔时，世尊知憍慢婆罗门心念，而说偈言：

The conceited one has come here, and his arrogance has increased even more. I came
here for the sake of the Dharma, and I should increase the Dharma even more.

「憍慢既来此，　　不善更增慢，向以义故来，　　应转增其义。」

At that time, the conceited brāhman thought, "The Śramaṇa Gautama already knows my mind." He wanted to pay homage.

时，憍慢婆罗门作是念：「沙门瞿昙已知我心。」欲修敬礼。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the conceited brāhman, "Stop! Stop! No need to pay homage. Purity of mind is sufficient."

尔时，世尊告憍慢婆罗门：「止！止！不须作礼，心净已足。」

At that time, the great assembly all loudly exclaimed, "How wonderful! The World-Honored One, the Venerable One of great power! This conceited brāhman, relying on the arrogance of birth, the arrogance of family, the arrogance of appearance, the arrogance of intelligence, and the arrogance of wealth, does not respect parents, elders, and teachers. Now he is humble before the Śramaṇa Gautama, wishing to pay homage by touching his feet."

时，诸大众咸各高声唱言：「奇哉！世尊！大德大力。今此憍慢婆罗门恃生憍慢、族姓憍慢、容色憍慢、聪明憍慢、财富憍慢，不敬父母、诸尊、师长，今于沙门瞿昙所谦卑下下，欲接足礼。」

At that time, the conceited brāhman, in front of the great assembly, called for silence and spoke in verse:

时，憍慢婆罗门于大众前唱令静默，而说偈言：

How does one not give rise to arrogance? How does one give rise
to respect? How does one skillfully console? How does one skillfully make offerings?

「云何不起慢？　　云何起恭敬？云何善慰谕？　　云何善供养？」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke in verse:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

Parents, elder brothers, preceptors, teachers, and those worthy of respect
- one should not give rise to arrogance towards them
One should be well-respectful, humble, and ask for instruction, wholeheartedly
serve them, and make various offerings. Those with minds free
from greed, anger, and delusion, arhats who have exhausted the
taints, well-liberated by right knowledge, and subdued arrogant minds -
towards these sages and saints, one should join palms and
bow in reverence.

「父母及长兄，　　和尚诸师长，及诸尊重者，　　所不应生慢。应当善恭敬，　　谦下而问讯，尽心而奉事，　　兼设诸供养。离贪恚痴心，　　漏尽阿罗汉，正智善解脱，　　伏诸憍慢心。于此贤圣等，　　合掌稽首礼。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke the Dharma for the arrogant brahmins in various ways, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting them. As the World-Honored Buddha spoke the Dharma in stages, he spoke of the merits of generosity, upholding precepts, and being reborn in heaven, the flavors and afflictions of desire, purity, liberation, and detachment, and the factors of purity. Speaking thus extensively, like a white and pure garment without any black impurities, it quickly accepts the dye. The arrogant brahmins then realized the Four Noble Truths - suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path - on their seats, and attained the uninterrupted stages.

尔时，世尊为憍慢婆罗门种种说法，示、教、照、喜。如佛世尊次第说法，说布施、持戒、生天功德，爱欲味患烦恼，清净、出要、远离，诸清净分……如是广说，如白净衣无诸黑恶，速受染色。憍慢婆罗门即] cf.【丽】于座上解四圣谛——苦、习、灭、道，得无间等。

At that time, the arrogant brahmins saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, understood the Dharma, and entered the Dharma. They transcended all doubts, not relying on others for liberation. In the true Dharma, they attained fearlessness. They then rose from their seats, adjusted their robes, bowed to the Buddha, joined their palms, and said to the Buddha, "May I now be allowed to go forth and receive full ordination in the true Dharma?"

时，憍慢婆罗门见法、得法、知法、入法，度诸疑惑，不由他度，于正法中得无所畏。即从座起，整衣服，为佛作礼，合掌白佛：「我今可得于正法中出家、受具足不？」

The Buddha told the arrogant brahmins, "You may now go forth and receive full ordination in the true Dharma."

佛告憍慢婆罗门：「汝今可得于正法中出家、受具足。」

They then went forth and practiced meditation alone, thinking, "The reason good sons shave their heads, wear the monastic robes, leave home with right faith, and go forth to practice the path..." They attained arahantship with well-liberated minds.

彼即出家，独正思惟：「所以善男子剃除须发，著袈裟衣，正信非家，出家学道……。」得阿罗汉，心善解脱。
