SĀ 928

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 928（九二八） 得果

Parallel to: SN 55.49 (~) / SĀ² 2.153

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying in the NiKuLa grove in the country of Kapilavastu.

一时，佛住迦毘罗卫国尼拘律园中。

At that time, the Licchavi Mahanama came to where the Buddha was, together with five hundred upasakas. He bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! What is meant by upasaka?"

尔时，释氏摩诃男与五百优婆塞来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！云何名优婆塞？」

The Buddha told Mahanama, "An upasaka is one who abides in purity at home, and for as long as he lives, takes refuge in the Three Jewels. He is an upasaka who acknowledges me!"

佛告摩诃男：「优婆塞者，在家净住，乃至尽寿，归依三宝，为优婆塞，证知我！」

Mahanama said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! What is meant by an upasaka who is a stream-enterer?"

摩诃男白佛言：「世尊！云何名优婆塞须陀洹？」

The Buddha told Mahanama, "An upasaka who is a stream-enterer is one who has already severed and knows the three fetters, namely, the view of a self, adherence to rules and rituals, and doubt. Mahanama! This is called an upasaka who is a stream-enterer."

佛告摩诃男：「优婆塞须陀洹者，三结已断已知，谓身见、戒取、疑。摩诃男！是名优婆塞须陀洹。」

Mahanama said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! What is meant by an upasaka who is a once-returner?"

摩诃男白佛言：「世尊！云何名优婆塞斯陀含？」

The Buddha told Mahanama, "An upasaka who is a once-returner is one who has already severed and knows the three fetters, namely, greed, anger, and delusion, and whose greed, anger, and delusion are weakened. Mahanama! This is called an upasaka who is a once-returner."

佛告摩诃男：「谓优婆塞三结已断已知，贪、恚、痴薄。摩诃男！是名优婆塞斯陀含。」

Mahanama said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! What is meant by an upasaka who is a non-returner?"

摩诃男白佛言：「世尊！云何名优婆塞阿那含？」

The Buddha told Mahanama, "An upasaka who is a non-returner is one who has already severed and knows the five lower fetters, namely, the view of a self, adherence to rules and rituals, doubt, greed, and anger. Mahanama! This is called an upasaka who is a non-returner."

佛告摩诃男：「优婆塞阿那含者，五下分结已断已知，谓身见、戒取、疑、贪欲、瞋恚。摩诃男！是名优婆塞阿那含。」

At that time, Mahanama looked at the five hundred upasakas and said, "How wonderful! The upasakas who live at home are able to attain such profound and wonderful merits."

时，摩诃男释氏顾视五百优婆塞，而作是言：「奇哉！诸优婆塞，在家清白，乃得如是深妙功德。」

At that time, the upasaka Mahanama, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and followed the teaching. He rose from his seat, bowed, and departed.

时，摩诃男优婆塞闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，从坐起，作礼而去。
