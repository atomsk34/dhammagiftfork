SĀ 929

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 929（九二九） 一切行

Parallel to: AN 8.25 / SĀ² 2.154

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying in the NiKuLa grove in the country of Kapilavastu.

一时，佛住迦毘罗卫国尼拘律园中。

At that time, Śākya Mahānāma came to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! What is meant by 'upāsaka'?"

尔时，释氏摩诃男来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！云何名优婆塞？」

The Buddha told Mahānāma, "An upāsaka is one who is pure in the household life, and who, for the rest of his life, takes refuge in the Three Jewels. He is an upāsaka who acknowledges me!"

佛告摩诃男：「优婆塞者，在家清白，乃至尽寿，归依三宝，为优婆塞，证知我！」

Mahānāma said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! What is meant by 'fulfilling all the duties of an upāsaka'?"

摩诃男白佛：「世尊！云何为满足一切优婆塞事？」

The Buddha told Mahānāma, "If an upāsaka has faith but not precepts, then he is not complete. He should diligently practice to be complete in pure precepts. If he is complete in faith and precepts but does not give, then he is not complete. Because he is not complete, he should diligently practice to cultivate giving to be complete. If he is complete in faith, precepts, and giving, but cannot go to see śramaṇas and listen to the true Dharma in a timely manner, then he is not complete. Because he is not complete, he should diligently practice to go to the stūpa temple to see the śramaṇas and listen to the true Dharma with a single-minded mind. If he is not able to contemplate the profound meaning of the Dharma, then he is not complete. Because he is not complete, he should diligently practice to have faith, precepts, giving, and listening. Having listened, he should be able to uphold it. If he is not able to observe the profound meaning of the Dharma, then he is not complete. Because he is not complete, he should diligently practice to have faith, precepts, giving, listening, upholding, and observing the profound and wonderful meaning. If he is not able to follow the Dharma and practice in accordance with the Dharma, then he is not complete. Because he is not complete, he should diligently practice to have faith, precepts, giving, listening, upholding, observing the profound meaning, and following the Dharma and practicing in accordance with the Dharma. Mahānāma! This is called being complete in all aspects of the upāsaka's duties."

佛告摩诃男：「若优婆塞有信无戒，是则不具，当勤方便，具足净戒。具足信、戒而不施者，是则不具；以不具故，精勤方便，修习布施，令其具足满。信、戒、施满，不能随时往诣沙门，听受正法，是则不具；以不具故，精勤方便，随时往诣塔寺，见诸沙门，不一心听受正法，是不具足。信、戒、施、闻修习满足，闻已不持，是不具足；以不具足故，精勤方便，随时往诣沙门，专心听法，闻则能持。不能观察诸法深义，是不具足；不具足故，精勤方便，信、戒、施、闻，闻则能持，持已，观察甚深妙义，而不随顺知法次法向，是则不具，以不具故，精勤方便，信、戒、施、闻，受持观察，了达深义，随顺行法次法向。摩诃男！是名满足一切种优婆塞事。」

Mahānāma said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! What is meant by 'an upāsaka who can comfort himself but not others'?"

摩诃男白佛：「世尊！云何名优婆塞能自安慰，不安慰他？」

The Buddha told Mahānāma, "If an upāsaka can establish precepts for himself but not others; can uphold pure precepts himself but not others; can give alms himself but not establish others in giving; can go to the stūpa temple to see the śramaṇas himself but not encourage others to go to the stūpa temple to see the śramaṇas; can listen to the Dharma himself but not encourage others to enjoy listening to the true Dharma; can uphold the Dharma he has heard himself but not cause others to uphold the true Dharma; can observe the profound and wonderful meaning himself but not encourage others to observe the profound meaning; can know the profound Dharma himself and practice in accordance with the Dharma and the stages of the Dharma, but not encourage others to practice in accordance with the Dharma and the stages of the Dharma. Mahānāma! If one accomplishes these eight dharmas, it is called an upāsaka who can comfort himself but not others."

佛告摩诃男：「若优婆塞能自立戒，不能令他立于正戒；自持净戒，不能令他持戒具足；自行布施，不能以施建立于他；自诣塔寺见诸沙门，不能劝他令诣塔寺往见沙门；自专听法，不能劝人乐听正法；闻法自持，不能令他受持正法；自能观察甚深妙义，不能劝人令观深义；自知深法能随顺行法次法向，不能劝人令随顺行法次法向。摩诃男！如是八法成就者，是名优婆塞能自安慰，不安慰他。」

Mahānāma said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! How many dharmas does an upāsaka accomplish to comfort himself and others?"

摩诃男白佛：「世尊！优婆塞成就几法自安安他？」

The Buddha told Mahānāma, "If an upāsaka accomplishes sixteen dharmas, it is called an upāsaka who can comfort himself and others. What are the sixteen? Mahānāma! If an upāsaka is endowed with right faith and establishes others; upholds pure precepts himself and also establishes others in pure precepts; gives alms himself and teaches others to give; goes to the stūpa temple to see the śramaṇas himself and also teaches others to go to the stūpa temple to see the śramaṇas; concentrates on listening to the Dharma himself and also teaches others to listen; upholds the Dharma he has received himself and also teaches others to uphold it; observes the meaning himself and also teaches others to observe it; knows the profound meaning himself and practices in accordance with the Dharma and the stages of the Dharma, and also teaches others to understand the profound meaning and practice in accordance with the Dharma and the stages of the Dharma. Mahānāma! If one accomplishes these sixteen dharmas, it is called an upāsaka who can comfort himself and also comfort others."

佛告摩诃男：「若优婆塞成就十六法者，是名优婆塞自安安他。何等为十六？摩诃男！若优婆塞具足正信，建立他人；自持净戒，亦以净戒建立他人；自行布施，教人行施；自诣塔寺见诸沙门，亦教人往见诸沙门；自专听法，亦教人听；自受持法，教人受持；自观察义，教人观察；自知深义，随顺修行法次法向，亦复教人解了深义，随顺修行法次法向。摩诃男！如是十六法成就者，是名优婆塞能自安慰，亦安慰他人。

"Mahānāma! If an upāsaka accomplishes these sixteen dharmas, all the great assemblies will go to him, namely the assembly of brāhmaṇas, the assembly of kṣatriyas, the assembly of elders, and the assembly of śramaṇas. He will be radiant with dignity among the assemblies. It is like the sun, which shines brightly in the beginning, middle, and end. In the same way, an upāsaka who accomplishes the sixteen dharmas will be radiant with dignity in the beginning, middle, and end. In this way, Mahānāma! If an upāsaka accomplishes the sixteen dharmas, he is difficult to find in the world."

「摩诃男！若优婆塞成就如是十六法者，彼诸大众悉诣其所，谓婆罗门众、刹利众、长者众、沙门众，于诸众中威德显曜。譬如日轮，初、中及后，光明显照。如是，优婆塞十六法成就者，初、中及后，威德显照。如是，摩诃男！若优婆塞十六法成就者，世间难得。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished. Mahānāma of the Śākya clan, having heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced and was delighted. He immediately rose from his seat, bowed, and departed.

佛说此经已，释氏摩诃男闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，即从坐起，作礼而去。
