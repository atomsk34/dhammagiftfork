SĀ 93

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 93（九三） 火

Parallel to: AN 7.47 / SĀ² 2.259

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling through the country of Kosala and arrived at Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī.

一时，佛在拘萨罗人间游行，至舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, a long-bodied brahmin held a great and evil assembly like this - he had seven hundred special bulls lined up and tied to posts, special cows, buffaloes, goats, calves, and various small insects were all bound, and he prepared various foods and widely practiced giving. Various non-Buddhist practitioners from various countries all gathered at the place of the evil assembly.

时，有长身婆罗门，作如是邪盛大会——以七百特牛行列系柱，特、牸、水牛及诸羊犊、种种小虫悉皆系缚，办诸饮食、广行布施，种种外道从诸国国皆悉来集邪盛会所。

At that time, the long-bodied brahmin heard that the Śramaṇa Gautama was traveling through the country of Kosala and had arrived at Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī. He thought: "I am now holding a great and evil assembly. The reason I have seven hundred special bulls lined up and tied to posts, and even the smallest insects are all bound, is for the sake of the great and evil assembly. Various non-Buddhist practitioners from various countries have all come to the assembly. I should now go to where the Śramaṇa Gautama is and ask about the Dharma of the great and evil assembly, so that there are no deficiencies in the divisions of the great and evil assembly that I am holding." Having thought this, he rode in a white horse carriage, with young brahmins leading the way in front and following behind, holding a golden-handled umbrella and a golden water bottle. He left the city of Śrāvastī and went to where the World-Honored One was, paying respect and serving him. When he arrived at the gate of the monastery, he got down from the carriage and walked forward. When he reached the Buddha's presence, they greeted each other and exchanged greetings. He then sat to one side and said to the Buddha: "Gautama! I now wish to hold a great and evil assembly. I have seven hundred special bulls lined up and tied to posts, and even the smallest insects are all bound. It is for the sake of the great and evil assembly that various non-Buddhist practitioners from various countries have all come to the place of the great and evil assembly. I have also heard that Gautama is traveling through the country of Kosala and has arrived at Jetavana Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī. I am now coming to ask Gautama about the Dharma of the great and evil assembly and the divisions of the various things, so that there are no deficiencies in the divisions of the great and evil assembly that I am holding.

时，长身婆罗门闻沙门瞿昙从拘萨罗人间游行，至舍卫国祇树给孤独园。作是念：「我今办邪盛大会，所以七百特牛行列系柱，乃至小小诸虫皆悉系缚。为邪盛大会故，种种异道从诸国国来至会所。我今当往沙门瞿昙所问邪盛法，莫令我作邪盛大会，分数中有所短少。」作是念已，乘白马车，诸年少婆罗门前后导从，持金柄伞盖，执金澡瓶，出舍卫城，诣世尊所，恭敬承事。至精舍门，下车步进，至于佛前，面相问讯慰劳已，退坐一面，白佛言：「瞿昙！我今欲作邪盛大会，以七百特牛行列系柱，乃至小小诸虫皆悉系缚。为邪盛大会故，种种异道从诸国国皆悉来至邪盛会所。又闻瞿昙从拘萨罗人间游行，至舍卫国祇树给孤独园。我今故来请问瞿昙邪盛大会法诸物分数，莫令我所作邪盛大会诸分数之中有所短少。」

The Buddha told the brahmin: "There may be a master of a great and evil assembly who practices giving and making merit, but gives rise to sin, and is cut by three swords, obtaining an unwholesome fruit of retribution. What are the three? They are the sword of the body, the sword of speech, and the sword of the mind.

佛告婆罗门：「或有一邪盛大会主行施作福而生于罪，为三刀剑之所刻削，得不善果报。何等三？谓身刀剑、口刀剑、意刀剑。

What is meant by the sword of the mind giving rise to suffering and retribution? For example, a master of a great assembly thinks like this: 'I am holding a great and evil assembly. I will kill so many young and strong bulls, so many water bulls and water cows, so many sheep and calves, and various insects.' This is called the sword of the mind giving rise to suffering and retribution. Although such a donor may think of making various kinds of offerings and various kinds of worship, he actually gives rise to sin!

「何等为意刀剑生诸苦报？如一会主造作大会，作是思惟：『我作邪盛大会，当杀尔所少壮特牛，尔所水特、水牸，尔所羊犊及种种诸虫。』是名意刀剑生诸苦报。如是施主虽念作种种布施、种种供养，实生于罪！

What is meant by the sword of speech giving rise to suffering and retribution? For example, a master of a great assembly gives such instructions: 'I am now holding a great and evil assembly. You should kill so many young and strong bulls, up to killing so many tiny insects.' This is called the sword of speech giving rise to suffering and retribution. Although the master of the great assembly makes such offerings and worship, he actually gives rise to sin!

「云何为口刀剑生诸苦报？有一会主造作大会，作如是教：『我今作邪盛大会，汝等当杀尔所少壮特牛，乃至杀害尔所微细虫。』是名口刀剑生诸苦报，大会主虽作是布施、供养，实生于罪！

What is meant by the sword of the body giving rise to suffering and retribution? For example, a master of a great assembly personally kills so many bulls, up to killing various tiny insects. This is called the sword of the body giving rise to suffering and retribution. Although the master of the great assembly thinks of making various kinds of offerings and various kinds of worship, he actually gives rise to sin!

「云何为身刀剑生诸苦报？谓有一大会主造作大会，自手伤杀尔所特牛，乃至杀害种种细虫，是名身刀剑生诸苦报。彼大会主虽作是念种种布施、种种供养，实生于罪！

However, Brahmins should diligently make offerings to the three fires, respectfully worship and serve them at all times, and provide them with happiness. What are the three? First, the root; second, the household; third, the field of merit.

「然婆罗门当勤供养三火，随时恭敬，礼拜奉事，施其安乐。何等为三？一者根本，二者居家，三者福田。

What is the fire of the root? How does one respectfully worship and serve it, and provide it with happiness? It means that a good man obtains wealth through skillful means, and uses his hands and feet to work hard to obtain it according to the Dharma. He makes offerings to his parents to bring them happiness. This is called the fire of the root. Why is it called the root? Because a good man is born from them, namely his parents, it is called the root. Because a good man respects his roots, he respectfully worships and serves them at all times, and provides them with happiness.

「何者为根本火？随时恭敬，奉事供养，施其安乐？谓善男子方便得财，手足勤苦，如法所得，供养父母，令得安乐，是名根本火。何故名为根本？若善男子从彼而生，所谓父母，故名根本。善男子以崇本故，随时恭敬，奉事供养，施以安乐。

What is the fire of the household? How does a good man raise and provide happiness for it at all times? It means that a good man obtains wealth through skillful means, and uses his hands and feet to work hard to obtain it according to the Dharma. He provides for his wife, children, relatives, servants, and hired workers, giving them what they need at all times, and respectfully providing them with happiness. This is called the fire of the household. Why is it called the household? Because a good man lives in the household, sharing joy and sorrow, and doing everything together, it is called the household. Therefore, a good man provides for them at all times, and gives them happiness.

「何等为居家火？善男子随时育养，施以安乐？谓善男子方便得财，手足勤苦，如法所得，供给妻子、宗亲、眷属、仆使、佣客，随时给与，恭敬施安，是名家火。何故名家？其善男子处于居家，乐则同乐，苦则同苦，在所为作皆相顺从，故名为家。是故善男子随时供给，施与安乐。

"What is the fire of the field? How does a good man respectfully worship and serve it, and provide it with happiness at all times? It means that a good man obtains wealth through skillful means, and uses his hands and feet to work hard to obtain it according to the Dharma. He serves and makes offerings to śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas who are well-disciplined in greed, anger, and delusion. Such śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas establish the field of merit, and those who revere and increase it will enjoy happiness and its rewards in the future, being reborn in heaven. This is called the fire of the field. Why is it called the field? Because it is a field of merit for the world, it is called the field. Therefore, a good man respectfully worships and serves it at all times, and provides it with happiness." At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse, saying:

「何等名田火？善男子随时恭敬，尊重供养，施其安乐？谓善男子方便得财，手足勤劳，如法所得，奉事供养诸沙门、婆罗门，善能调伏贪、恚、痴者，如是等沙门、婆罗门，建立福田，崇向增进，乐分乐报，未来生天，是名田火。何故名田？为世福田，谓为应供，是故名田。是善男子随时恭敬，奉事供养，施其安乐。」尔时，世尊复说偈言：

The root, the household, the field of merit, and the fire of offerings
these fires increase offerings, providing abundant and peaceful happiness. The world of happiness
without sin, the wise go there, as if wealth and Dharma meet again
making offerings to those who should be supported. Because of making offerings to
those who should be supported, one is born in heaven and gains a good reputation.

「根本及居家，　　应供福田火，是火增供养，　　充足安隐乐。无罪乐世间，　　慧者往生彼，如法财复会，　　供养所应养。供养应养故，　　生天得名称。

But, brahmin, now the good man should extinguish the three fires that he previously offered to. What are the three? They are the fires of greed, anger, and delusion. Why is this? If the fire of greed is not extinguished, it harms oneself, harms others, harms both self and others, brings offenses in this life, brings offenses in the next life, brings offenses in this life and the next life, and gives rise to mental suffering and distress. The fires of anger and delusion are also like this. Brahmin, if a good man serves the fire of firewood, he suffers at all times, burns at all times, and extinguishes the fire at all times, suffering because of the causes and conditions.

「然，婆罗门！今善男子先所供养三火应断令灭。何等为三？谓贪欲火、瞋恚火、愚痴火。所以者何？若贪火不断不灭者，自害害他，自他俱害，现法得罪，后世得罪，现法后世得罪，缘彼而生心法忧苦，恚火、痴火亦复如是。婆罗门！若善男子事积薪火，随时辛苦，随时然，随时灭火因缘受苦。」

At that time, the brahmin of long body remained silent.

尔时，长身婆罗门默然而住。

At that time, there was a brahmin's son named Uttara sitting in the assembly.

时，有婆罗门子名欝多罗，于会中坐。

The brahmin of long body was silent for a while, then he said to Uttara, "Can you go to the assembly of the heretics and release the oxen tied to the pillars and all the beings bound there, and tell them, 'The brahmin of long body says to you: "You are free to roam the mountains, forests, and wilderness, eating the grass that is not cut, drinking the pure flowing water, enjoying the pleasures of the four directions."'"

长身婆罗门须臾默然，思惟已，告欝多罗：「汝能往至邪盛会所，放彼系柱特牛及诸众生受系缚者，悉皆放不？而告之言：『长身婆罗门语汝：「随意自在，山泽旷野，食不断草，饮净流水，四方风中受诸快乐。」』」

Uttara said, "As the master commands!" He went to the assembly of the heretics and released the beings, telling them, "The brahmin of long body says to you: 'You are free to roam the mountains, forests, and wilderness, drinking water, eating grass, and enjoying the four winds as you please.'"

欝多罗白言：「随大师教！」即往彼邪盛会所放诸众生，而告之言：「长身婆罗门语汝：『随其所乐，山泽旷野，饮水食草，四风自适。』」

At that time, the World-Honored One knew Uttara. Having known, he spoke various teachings to the brahmin of long body, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting him. As in the Vinaya, the World-Honored One first spoke the precepts, then spoke of generosity and the merits of rebirth in the heavens, the flavors and afflictions of desire, the necessity for renunciation, purity, and the purification of afflictions, revealing and manifesting them. Just as fresh and clean white cloth is easy to take dye, so it was also for the brahmin of long body. He saw the Four Noble Truths right there on his seat and attained the uninterrupted stages.

尔时，世尊知欝多罗。知已，为长身婆罗门种种说法，示、教、照、喜。如律，世尊说法先后，说戒、说施及生天功德，爱欲味患，出要、清净、烦恼清净，开示现显。譬如鲜净白［叠＊毛］易受染色，长身婆罗门亦复如是。即于座上见四真谛，得无间等。

At that time, the brahmin of long body saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, knew the Dharma, entered the Dharma, transcended all doubts, and was not led by others. He attained fearlessness in the true Dharma. He then rose from his seat, adjusted his robes, bared his right shoulder, joined his palms, and said to the Buddha, "I am liberated, World-Honored One! From this day forth, until the end of my life, I take refuge in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha of monks. I am a lay devotee. Please bear witness to me. I only wish that the World-Honored One and the great assembly would accept my offering of food." At that time, the World-Honored One silently consented.

时，长身婆罗门见法、得法、知法、入法，度诸疑惑，不由他度，于正法中得无所畏。即从座起，整衣服，偏袒右肩，合掌白佛：「已度，世尊！我从今日尽其寿命，归佛、归法、归比丘僧，为优婆塞，证知我。唯愿世尊与诸大众受我饭食。尔时，世尊默然而许。」

At that time, the brahmin of long body, knowing that the Buddha had accepted the invitation, bowed to the Buddha, circumambulated him three times to the right, and departed. The brahmin of long body returned to the place of the heretics, arranged the pure and excellent offerings, set up the seats, and sent a messenger to invite the Buddha, saying, "The time has come. May the Sage know the time.

时，长身婆罗门知佛受请已，为佛作礼，右绕三匝而去。长身婆罗门还邪盛处，所诸供办净美好者，布置床座，遣使请佛，白言：「时到，惟圣知时。」

At that time, the World-Honored One put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and was surrounded by the great assembly. He went to the place where the brahmin of long body was holding the assembly and took his seat in front of the great assembly.

尔时，世尊著衣持钵，大众围绕，往到长身婆罗门会所，大众前坐。

At that time, the brahmin of long body, knowing that the World-Honored One had finished his meal, personally served various foods and drinks. After the meal, he washed his hands and rinsed his mouth, and put away the bowl. He then arranged a low seat separately and sat at the front of the great assembly to listen to the Dharma.

时，长身婆罗门知世尊坐定已，手自供养种种饮食。食已，澡漱洗钵毕，别敷卑床，于大众前端坐听法。

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke various teachings to the brahmin of long body, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting him. Having arisen from his seat, he departed.

尔时，世尊为长身婆罗门说种种法，示、教、照、喜已，从座起而去。
