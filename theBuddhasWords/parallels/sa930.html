SĀ 930

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 930（九三〇） 自轻

Parallel to: SN 55.22 / SĀ² 2.155

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying in the NiKuLa grove in the country of Kapilavastu.

一时，佛住迦毘罗卫国尼拘律园中。

At that time, Śākya Mahānāma came to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. He said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! This country of Kapilavastu is peaceful, prosperous, and has a large population. Whenever I go out, I am accompanied by many followers, and there are always wild elephants, mad people, and mad vehicles with us. I am afraid that I will be born and die with these mad ones, forgetting to contemplate the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha. I think to myself, when my life ends, where will I be reborn?"

尔时，释氏摩诃男来诣佛所，稽首礼足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！此迦毘罗卫国安隐丰乐，人民炽盛，我每出入时，众多羽从，狂象、狂人、狂乘常与是俱。我自恐与此诸狂俱生俱死，忘于念佛、念法、念比丘僧。我自思惟，命终之时，当生何处？」

The Buddha told Mahānāma, "Do not be afraid, do not be terrified. After your life ends, you will not be reborn in evil realms. You will also have no evil. It is like a large tree that grows downward, flows downward, and drains downward. If you cut off its roots, where will it fall?"

佛告摩诃男：「莫恐，莫怖，命终之后，不生恶趣，终亦无恶。譬如大树，顺下、顺注、顺输，若截根本，当堕何处？」

Mahānāma said to the Buddha, "It will follow where it grows downward, flows downward, and drains downward."

摩诃男白佛：「随彼顺下、顺注、顺输。」

The Buddha told Mahānāma, "It is the same for you. When your life ends, you will not be reborn in evil realms. You will also have no evil. Why is that? You have long cultivated mindfulness of the Buddha, mindfulness of the Dharma, and mindfulness of the Sangha. When your life ends, this body, whether burned by fire or discarded in a charnel ground, will be blown by the wind and exposed to the sun, eventually becoming dust. But your mind and consciousness, long and far, perfumed by right faith, perfumed by precepts, giving, hearing, and wisdom, will ascend and go to a place of peace and happiness, and you will be reborn in the heavens in the future."

佛告摩诃男：「汝亦如是，若命终时，不生恶趣，终亦无恶。所以者何？汝已长夜修习念佛、念法、念僧，若命终时，此身若火烧，若弃冢间，风飘日曝，久成尘末，而心意识久远长夜正信所熏，戒、施、闻、慧所熏，神识上升，向安乐处，未来生天。」

At that time, Mahānāma heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced, made obeisance, and left.

时，摩诃男闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，作礼而去。
