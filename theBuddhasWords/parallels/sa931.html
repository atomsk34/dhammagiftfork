SĀ 931

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 931（九三一） 住处

Parallel to: AN 6.10 / AN 6.25 / SĀ² 2.156

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying in the NiKuLa grove in the country of Kapilavastu.

一时，佛住迦毘罗卫国尼拘律园中。

At that time, Śākya Mahānāma came to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. He said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! If a bhikṣu is on the path of learning, seeking what has not yet been attained, ascending to the path of the noble ones, and peacefully entering nirvāṇa, World-Honored One! How should he cultivate and dwell in much cultivation, so that in this Dharma and Vinaya he can exhaust all taints, attain liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom without taints, and in this very life know and realize for himself: 'My birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and I know that I will not be subject to any future lives'?"

尔时，释氏摩诃男来诣佛所，稽首礼足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！若比丘在于学地，求所未得，上升进道，安隐涅槃。世尊！彼当云何修习，多修习住，于此法、律得诸漏尽，无漏心解脱、慧解脱，现法自知作证：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』？」

The Buddha told Mahānāma, "If a bhikṣu is on the path of learning, seeking what has not yet been attained, ascending to the path of the noble ones, and peacefully entering nirvāṇa, then at that time he should cultivate the six recollections, and so forth, until he attains nirvāṇa. It is like a hungry person whose body is emaciated. If he obtains delicious food, his body will become plump and lustrous. In the same way, a bhikṣu who abides on the path of learning, seeking what has not yet been attained, ascending to the path of the noble ones, and peacefully entering nirvāṇa, cultivates the six recollections, and so forth, until he quickly attains peaceful nirvāṇa.

佛告摩诃男：「若比丘在于学地，求所未得，上升进道，安隐涅槃，彼于尔时，当修六念，乃至进得涅槃。譬如饥人，身体羸瘦，得美味食，身体肥泽。如是，比丘住在学地，求所未得，上升进道，安隐涅槃，修六随念，乃至疾得安隐涅槃。

What are the six recollections? They are: the noble disciple recollects the Tathāgata: 'The Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One, Perfect in Knowledge and Conduct, Sugata, Knower of the World, Unsurpassed Trainer of those who can be trained, Teacher of gods and men, Buddha, Bhagavān.' When the noble disciple recollects in this way, he does not give rise to the fetters of greed, anger, or delusion, but his mind becomes upright. He attains the meaning of the Tathāgata, attains the true Dharma of the Tathāgata, and gives rise to a mind of joy towards the true Dharma of the Tathāgata and towards the Tathāgata. Having given rise to a mind of joy, he becomes delighted; having become delighted, his body becomes tranquil; having become tranquil, he experiences bliss; having experienced bliss, his mind becomes concentrated; having a concentrated mind, that noble disciple, among the fierce and dangerous beings, has no hindrances, enters the stream of the Dharma, and proceeds to nirvāṇa.

「何等六念？谓圣弟子念如来事：『如来、应、等正觉、明行足、善逝、世间解、无上士、调御丈夫、天人师、佛世尊。』圣弟子如是念时，不起贪欲缠，不起瞋恚、愚痴心，其心正直。得如来义，得如来正法，于如来正法、于如来所得随喜心；随喜心已，欢悦；欢悦已，身猗息；身猗息已，觉受乐；觉受乐已，其心定；心定已，彼圣弟子于凶崄众生中，无诸罣阂，入法流水，乃至涅槃。

Furthermore, the noble disciple recollects the Dharma: 'The World-Honored One's Dharma and Vinaya can presently liberate from the burning of birth and death, not waiting for a time, and can be realized in the present life, relying on personal realization.' When the noble disciple recollects the Dharma in this way, he does not give rise to greed, anger, or delusion, and so forth, up to being perfumed by recollecting the Dharma, progressing to nirvāṇa.

「复次，圣弟子念于法事，世尊法、律，现法能离生死炽然，不待时节，通达现法，缘自觉知。圣弟子如是念法者，不起贪欲、瞋恚、愚痴，乃至念法所熏，升进涅槃。

Furthermore, the noble disciple recollects the Saṅgha: 'The World-Honored One's disciples are well-inclined, rightly-inclined, straight-inclined, sincerely-inclined, practicing in accordance with the Dharma, heading towards the stream-enterer, attaining the stream-enterer, heading towards the once-returner, attaining the once-returner, heading towards the non-returner, attaining the non-returner, heading towards the arhat, attaining the arhat. This is the noble Saṅgha of four pairs, eight individuals. This is called the noble Saṅgha of the World-Honored One's disciples, who are fully endowed with pure precepts, fully endowed with concentration, fully endowed with wisdom, fully endowed with liberation, fully endowed with the knowledge and vision of liberation, worthy of offerings, worthy of hospitality, worthy of gifts, worthy of salutations, the unsurpassed field of merit in the world. When the noble disciple recollects the Saṅgha in this way, he does not give rise to greed, anger, or delusion, and so forth, up to being perfumed by recollecting the Saṅgha, progressing to nirvāṇa.

「复次，圣弟子念于僧事，世尊弟子善向、正向、直向、诚向，行随顺法，有向须陀洹、得须陀洹，向斯陀含、得斯陀含，向阿那含、得阿那含，向阿罗汉、得阿罗汉，此是四双八辈贤圣，是名世尊弟子僧，净戒具足、三昧具足、智慧具足、解脱具足、解脱知见具足，所应奉迎，承事供养，为良福田。圣弟子如是念僧事时，不起贪欲、瞋恚、愚痴，乃至念僧所熏，升进涅槃。

Furthermore, the noble disciple recollects his own pure precepts: 'My precepts are unbroken, unflawed, unstained, unadulterated, not taken from others, well-guarded, praised by the wise, not despised by the wise.' When the noble disciple recollects his precepts in this way, he does not give rise to greed, anger, or delusion, and so forth, up to being perfumed by recollecting the precepts, progressing to nirvāṇa.

「复次，圣弟子自念净戒，不坏戒、不缺戒、不污戒、不杂戒、不他取戒、善护戒、明者称誉戒、智者不厌戒。圣弟子如是念戒时，不起贪欲、瞋恚、愚痴，乃至念戒所熏，升进涅槃。

Furthermore, the noble disciple recollects his own giving: 'I have obtained a good benefit, for among those beings who are defiled by stinginess, I have attained a place free from the defilement of stinginess. I practice giving with a mind of non-householder, giving with my own hands, delighting in the practice of giving, and giving equally. When the noble disciple recollects his giving in this way, he does not give rise to greed, anger, or delusion, and so forth, up to being perfumed by recollecting giving, progressing to nirvāṇa.

「复次，圣弟子自念施事，我得善利，于悭垢众生中而得离悭垢处，于非家行解脱施，常自手施，乐行舍法，具足等施。圣弟子如是念施时，不起贪欲、瞋恚、愚痴，乃至念施所熏，升进涅槃。

Furthermore, the noble disciple recollects the affairs of the gods: 'There are the Four Great Heavenly Kings, the Thirty-three Gods, the Yāma Gods, the Tuṣita Gods, the Nirmāṇarati Gods, and the Paranirmitavaśavartin Gods. If there are those with right faith, upon the end of their lives here, they are reborn among those gods. I too should practice this right faith; they attain pure precepts, giving, learning, renunciation, and wisdom, and upon the end of their lives here, they are reborn among those gods. I too should now practice these precepts, giving, learning, and wisdom.' When the noble disciple recollects the affairs of the gods in this way, he does not give rise to greed, anger, or delusion, his mind is upright, and he contemplates those gods. That noble disciple, with such an upright mind, attains the profound benefits of the Dharma, attains the profound benefits of the meaning, and attains the benefit of those gods rejoicing in his following. Having rejoiced, he gives rise to joy; having given rise to joy, his body becomes tranquil; having become tranquil, he experiences the bliss of awareness; having experienced the bliss of awareness, he attains mental concentration; having attained mental concentration, that noble disciple, among the masses of fierce beings, is without obstruction, and enters the stream of the Dharma. Being perfumed by recollecting the gods, he progresses to nirvāṇa.

「复次，圣弟子念诸天事，有四大天王、三十三天、焰摩天、兜率陀天、化乐天、他化自在天。若有正信心者，于此命终，生彼诸天，我亦当行此正信；彼得净戒、施、闻、舍、慧，于此命终，生彼诸天，我今亦当行此戒、施、闻、慧。圣弟子如是念天事者，不起贪欲、瞋恚、愚痴，其心正直，缘彼诸天。彼圣弟子如是直心者，得深法利、得深义利、得彼诸天饶益随喜；随喜已，生欣悦；欣悦已，身猗息；身猗息已，觉受乐；觉受乐已，得心定；心定已，彼圣弟子处凶崄众生中，无诸罣阂，入法水流。念天所熏故，升进涅槃。

"Mahānāma, if a monk dwells on the stage of training, wishing to ascend to the bliss of nirvāṇa, and cultivates this much, quickly attaining nirvāṇa, he will quickly exhaust the contaminants in the true Dharma and Vinaya, attain the liberation of the mind and liberation by wisdom without contaminants, and in this very life, know and realize for himself: 'My birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and I will not experience another existence after this one.'"

「摩诃男！若比丘住于学地，欲求上升安乐涅槃。如是多修习，疾得涅槃者，于正法、律速尽诸漏，无漏心解脱、慧解脱，现法自知作证：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』」

At that time, Mahānāma of the Śākya clan, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and followed it. He rose from his seat, bowed, and departed.

时，释氏摩诃男闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，从坐起，作礼而去。
