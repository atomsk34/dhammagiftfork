SĀ 935

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 935（九三五） 含罗

Parallel to: SN 55.23 / SĀ² 2.159

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying in the NiKuLa grove in the country of Kapilavastu.

一时，佛住迦毘罗卫国尼拘律园中。

At that time, a Śākya named Śāṭha said to Śākya Mahānāma, "The World-Honored One has said that a stream-enterer accomplishes how many kinds of dharmas?"

尔时，释氏名曰沙陀，语释氏摩诃男：「世尊说须陀洹成就几种法？」

Mahānāma replied, "The World-Honored One has said that a stream-enterer accomplishes four dharmas. What are the four? They are: unshakable faith in the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṃgha, and the accomplishment of the holy precepts. These are said to be the four dharmas accomplished by a stream-enterer."

摩诃男答言：「世尊说须陀洹成就四法。何等为四？谓于佛不坏净，法、僧不坏净，圣戒成就，是名四法成就须陀洹。」

The Śākya Śāṭha said to the Śākya Mahānāma, "Do not speak like that! Do not say that the World-Honored One has said that the four dharmas accomplish a stream-enterer. Rather, the three dharmas accomplish a stream-enterer. What are the three? They are: unshakable faith in the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṃgha. These three dharmas accomplish a stream-enterer." Thus he spoke a third time. The Śākya Mahānāma was unable to make Śāṭha accept the four dharmas, and the Śākya Śāṭha was unable to make Mahānāma accept the three dharmas. They went together to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side.

释氏沙陀语释氏摩诃男：「莫作是说！莫作是言世尊说四法成就须陀洹。然彼三法成就须陀洹。何等为三？谓于佛不坏净、于法不坏净、于僧不坏净。如是三法成就须陀洹。」如是第三说。释氏摩诃男不能令沙陀受四法，释氏沙陀不能令摩诃男受三法，共诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面。

The Śākya Mahānāma said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! The Śākya Śāṭha came to me and asked, 'The World-Honored One has said how many dharmas accomplish a stream-enterer?' I then replied, 'The World-Honored One has said that the four dharmas accomplish a stream-enterer. What are the four? They are: unshakable faith in the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṃgha, and the accomplishment of the holy precepts. These four dharmas accomplish a stream-enterer.' The Śākya Śāṭha said, 'Śākya Mahānāma! Do not speak like that! The World-Honored One has said that the four dharmas accomplish a stream-enterer, but only the three dharmas accomplish a stream-enterer. What are the three? They are: unshakable faith in the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṃgha. The World-Honored One has said that these three dharmas accomplish a stream-enterer.' Thus he spoke a second and a third time. I was also unable to make him accept the four dharmas, and the Śākya Śāṭha was also unable to make me accept the three dharmas. Therefore, we have both come to where the World-Honored One is. Now I ask the World-Honored One, how many dharmas does a stream-enterer accomplish?"

释氏摩诃男白佛言：「世尊！释氏沙陀来诣我所，问我言：『世尊说几法成就须陀洹？』我即答言：『世尊说四法成就须陀洹。何等为四？谓于佛不坏净，于法、僧不坏净，圣戒成就。如是四法成就须陀洹。』释氏沙陀作是言：『释氏摩诃男！莫作是语，世尊说四法成就须陀洹，但三法成就须陀洹。何等为三？谓于佛不坏净、于法不坏净、于僧不坏净，世尊说如是三法成就须陀洹。』如是再三说，我亦不能令彼释氏沙陀受四法，释氏沙陀亦不能令我受三法，是故俱来诣世尊所，今问世尊，须陀洹成就几法？」

At that time, the Śākya Śāṭha rose from his seat, bowed to the Buddha, joined his palms, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! If there arise such and similar dharmas, I would rather follow the World-Honored One than the community of monks. If there arise such and similar dharmas, I would rather follow the World-Honored One than the community of nuns, the community of male lay followers, the community of female lay followers, gods, Māra, Brahmā, śramaṇas, brāhmaṇas, gods, humans, and so forth."

时，沙陀释氏从坐起，为佛作礼，合掌白佛：「世尊！若有如是像类法起，一者世尊，一者比丘僧，我宁随世尊，不随比丘僧。或有如是像类法起，一者世尊，一者比丘尼僧、优婆塞、优婆夷、若天、若魔、若梵、若沙门、婆罗门、诸天、世人，我宁随世尊！不随余众。」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to the Śākya Mahānāma, "Mahānāma! How would you respond to the Śākya Śāṭha's argument?"

尔时，世尊告释氏摩诃男：「如摩诃男！释氏沙陀作如是论，汝当云何？」

Mahānāma said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! Regarding the Śākya Śāṭha's argument, I know how to respond. I would only say it is good and true."

摩诃男白佛：「世尊！彼沙陀释氏作如是论，我知复可说，我唯言善，唯言真实。」

The Buddha said to Mahānāma, "Therefore, you should know that the four dharmas are accomplished for the srota-āpanna. The unshakable faith in the Buddha, the unshakable faith in the Dharma and Saṃgha, and the accomplishment of the holy precepts. Thus, one should uphold them."

佛告摩诃男：「是故当知四法成就须陀洹，于佛不坏净，于法、僧不坏净，圣戒成就。如是受持。」

At that time, Mahānāma of the Śākya clan, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and followed it. He rose from his seat, bowed, and departed.

时，释氏摩诃男闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，从坐起，作礼而去。
