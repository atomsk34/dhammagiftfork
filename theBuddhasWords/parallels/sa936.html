SĀ 936

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 936（九三六） 麤手

Parallel to: SN 55.24 / SĀ² 2.160

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying in the NiKuLa grove in the country of Kapilavastu.

一时，佛住迦毘罗卫国尼拘律园中。

At that time, the Śākya clan of Kapilavastu gathered in the worship hall and discussed, asking Mahānāman, "How is it that the Buddha predicted that the Śākya Baśu, who had passed away, would attain the stream-enterer stage, not fall into the unwholesome destinies, be definitely headed for supreme perfect enlightenment, be reborn seven times among gods and humans, and ultimately reach the end of suffering? Yet Baśu committed offenses and drank alcohol, and the Buddha still predicted that he would attain the stream-enterer stage and ultimately reach the end of suffering. You, Mahānāman, should go ask the Buddha. We will uphold the Buddha's teachings as he explains them to you!"

时，有迦毘罗卫释氏集供养堂，作如是论，问：「摩诃男！云何最后记说彼百手释氏命终，世尊记彼得须陀洹，不堕恶趣法，决定正向三菩提，七有天人往生，究竟苦边。然彼百手释氏犯戒饮酒，而复世尊记彼得须陀洹，乃至究竟苦边。汝摩诃男！当往问佛，如佛所说，我等奉持！」

Then Mahānāman went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! We Śākyas of Kapilavastu gathered in the worship hall and discussed, asking Mahānāman, 'How is it that the Buddha predicted that the Śākya Baśu, who had passed away, would attain the stream-enterer stage, and so on, ultimately reaching the end of suffering? You should now go ask the Buddha again. We will uphold the Buddha's teachings as he explains them to you!' I now ask the Buddha. Please explain it for me."

尔时，摩诃男往诣佛所，稽首礼足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！我等迦毘罗卫诸释氏集供养堂，作如是论，摩诃男：『云何最后记说，是中百手释氏命终，世尊记说得须陀洹，乃至究竟苦边。汝今当往重问世尊，如世尊所说，我等奉持！』我今问佛，唯愿解说。」

The Buddha told Mahānāman, "When the noble disciples say 'the Blessed One, the Blessed One,' they speak of the Blessed One with their mouths, but their minds are rightly mindful and see straight, all entering the Blessed One. When they say 'the true Dharma, the true Dharma,' they speak of the true Dharma with their mouths, but their minds are rightly mindful and see straight, all entering the true Dharma. When they say 'the good Sangha, the good Sangha,' they speak of the good Sangha with their mouths, but their minds are rightly mindful and see straight, all entering the good Sangha. Thus, Mahānāman, the noble disciples have single-minded pure faith in the Buddha, the Dharma, and the Sangha. They have the wisdom of Dharma benefits, the wisdom of liberation, and the wisdom of determination. They fully realize the eight liberations with their own wisdom and see that the contaminants are exhausted. Thus, the noble disciples do not go to the hells, the animal realm, or the realm of hungry ghosts. They do not fall into the evil destinies. They are called arhats who are liberated in both ways.

佛告摩诃男：「『善逝大师、善逝大师』者，圣弟子所说，口说善逝，而心正念直见，悉入善逝。『正法律、正法律』者，圣弟子所说，口说正法，发心正念直见，悉入正法。『善向僧、善向僧』者，圣弟子所说，口说善向，发心正念直见，悉入善向。如是，摩诃男！圣弟子于佛一向净信，于法、僧一向净信，于法利智、出智、决定智，八解脱具足身作证，以智慧见，有漏断知。如是圣弟子不趣地狱、畜生、饿鬼，不堕恶趣，说阿罗汉俱解脱。

Furthermore, Mahānāman, the noble disciple has single-minded pure faith in the Buddha, and so on, up to the wisdom of determination, but does not fully realize the eight liberations with their own wisdom. However, they know and see that the contaminants are exhausted. This is called a noble disciple who does not fall into the evil destinies, up to one liberated by wisdom. Furthermore, Mahānāman, the noble disciple has single-minded pure faith in the Buddha, and so on, up to the wisdom of determination, and fully realizes the eight liberations with their own wisdom, but does not see the exhaustion of the contaminants. This is called a noble disciple who does not fall into the evil destinies, up to one liberated by the body.

「复次，摩诃男！圣弟子一向于佛清净信，乃至决定智慧，不得八解脱身作证具足住，然彼知见有漏断，是名圣弟子不堕恶趣，乃至慧解脱。复次，摩诃男！圣弟子一向于佛清净信，乃至决定智慧，八解脱身] cf.【丽】作证具足住，而不见有漏断，是名圣弟子不堕恶趣，乃至身证。

Furthermore, Mahānāman, if the noble disciple has single-minded pure faith in the Buddha, and so on, up to the wisdom of determination, but does not fully realize the eight liberations with their own wisdom, but truly knows and sees the true Dharma and Vinaya, this is called a noble disciple who does not fall into the evil destinies, up to one who has the vision of the Dharma. Furthermore, Mahānāman, the noble disciple has single-minded pure faith in the Buddha, and so on, up to the wisdom of determination, and truly knows and sees the true Dharma and Vinaya, but does not attain the vision of the Dharma. This is called a noble disciple who does not fall into the evil destinies, up to one liberated by faith.

「复次，摩诃男！若圣弟子一向于佛清净信，乃至决定智慧，不得八解脱身作证具足住，然于正法、律如实知见，是名圣弟子不堕恶趣，乃至见到。复次，摩诃男！圣弟子一向于佛清净信，乃至决定智慧，于正法、律如实知见，不得见到，是名圣弟子不堕恶趣，乃至信解脱。

Furthermore, Mahānāman, the noble disciple has pure faith in the Buddha's words, pure faith in the Dharma, and pure faith in the Sangha. They have superior wisdom in the five dharmas, and are able to endure them with discernment, namely faith, diligence, mindfulness, concentration, and wisdom. This is called a noble disciple who does not fall into the evil destinies, up to one who practices in accordance with the Dharma.

「复次，摩诃男！圣弟子信于佛言说清净，信法、信僧言说清净，于五法增上智慧，审谛堪忍，谓信、精进、念、定、慧，是名圣弟子不堕恶趣，乃至随法行。

Furthermore, Mahānāman, the noble disciple has pure faith in the Buddha's words, pure faith in the Dharma, and pure faith in the Sangha. They have lesser wisdom in the five dharmas, and are able to endure them with discernment, namely faith, diligence, mindfulness, concentration, and wisdom. This is called a noble disciple who does not fall into the evil destinies, up to one who practices in accordance with faith.

「复次，摩诃男！圣弟子信于佛言说清净，信法、信僧言说清净，乃至五法少慧，审谛堪忍，谓信、精进、念、定、慧，是名圣弟子不堕恶趣，乃至随信行。

Mahānāman, it is impossible for this firm tree to understand the meaning of what I have said. If it could understand, I would have declared it. How much less could the Śākya named Bahussuta not be declared to have attained the stream-entry? Mahānāman, when the Śākya named Bahussuta was about to die, he upheld the pure precepts and gave up drinking alcohol. Then he died. I declare that he has attained the stream-entry, and will ultimately reach the end of suffering.

「摩诃男！此坚固树，于我所说能知义者，无有是处！若能知者，我则记说，况复百手释氏而不记说得须陀洹？摩诃男！百手释氏临命终时，受持净戒，舍离饮酒，然后命终，我记说彼得须陀洹，乃至究竟苦边。」

Having heard the Buddha's teaching, Mahānāman Śākya was delighted and followed the Buddha's teaching. He rose from his seat, bowed, and departed.

摩诃男释氏闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，从坐起，作礼而去。
