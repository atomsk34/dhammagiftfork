SĀ 938

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 938（九三八） 泪

Parallel to: SN 15.3 / SĀ² 2.331 / EĀ 51.1

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Sentient beings have been transmigrating in the cycle of birth and death since beginningless time. They have been turning around for a long time without knowing the original source of suffering."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「众生无始生死以来，长夜轮转，不知苦之本际。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "What do you think? Is the flowing water of the Ganges River and the four great oceans more, or is the amount of tears you have shed while transmigrating in the cycle of birth and death for a long time more?"

佛告诸比丘：「于意云何？恒河流水，乃至四大海，其水为多？为汝等长夜轮转生死，流泪为多？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "According to my understanding of the meaning of what the World-Honored One has said, the tears we have shed while transmigrating in the cycle of birth and death for a long time are much more than the water in the Ganges River and the four great oceans."

诸比丘白佛：「如我解世尊所说义，我等长夜轮转生死，流泪甚多，过于恒水及四大海。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "Excellent! Excellent! The tears you have shed while transmigrating in the cycle of birth and death for a long time are much more than the water in the Ganges River and the four great oceans. Why is this? For a long time, you have lost your parents, brothers, sisters, relatives, and friends, and lost your wealth, and the tears shed for them are immeasurable. For a long time, you have been abandoned in charnel grounds, with pus and blood flowing out, and you have been in the hells, animal realms, and hungry ghost realms. Bhikṣus! From beginningless time, you have transmigrated in the cycle of birth and death for a long time, and the blood and tears from your bodies are immeasurable."

佛告比丘：「善哉！善哉！汝等长夜轮转生死，流泪甚多，非彼恒水及四大海。所以者何？汝等长夜丧失父母、兄弟、姊妹、宗亲、知识，丧失钱财，为之流泪，甚多无量。汝等长夜弃于塜间，脓血流出，及生地狱、畜生、饿鬼。诸比丘！汝等从无始生死，长夜轮转，其身血泪甚多无量。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "Is form permanent or impermanent?"

佛告诸比丘：「色为常耶？为无常耶？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "Impermanent, World-Honored One!"

比丘白佛：「无常。世尊！」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "If it is impermanent, is it suffering?"

佛告比丘：「若无常者，是苦耶？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "It is suffering, World-Honored One!"

比丘白佛：「是苦。世尊！」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "If it is impermanent and suffering, it is a changing and transforming phenomenon. Could the learned noble disciples see self, other, or characteristics in it?"

佛告比丘：「若无常、苦者，是变易法，多闻圣弟子宁于其中见我、异我、相在不？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "No, World-Honored One!" The same applies to feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness.

比丘白佛：「不也，世尊！」受、想、行、识亦复如是。

"Bhikṣus! The noble disciples know and see thus, and are liberated from form, feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness. They are liberated from birth, old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress."

「诸比丘！圣弟子如是知、如是见，乃至于色解脱，于受、想、行、识解脱，解脱生、老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha finished what he had to say. The bhikṣus, having heard the Buddha's words, rejoiced and received them respectfully.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
