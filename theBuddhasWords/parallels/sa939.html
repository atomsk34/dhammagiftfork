SĀ 939

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 939（九三九） 母乳

Parallel to: SN 15.4 / SĀ² 2.332

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time the World-Honored One told the bhikṣus, "Sentient beings, from beginningless time in the cycle of birth and death, covered by ignorance, bound by craving around their necks, wandering for a long time, do not know the original source of suffering."

尔时，世尊告诸比丘：「众生于无始生死，无明所盖，爱系其颈，长夜轮转，不知苦之本际。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "What do you think? Is the flowing water of the Ganges and the four great oceans more, or is the milk you have drunk while wandering in the cycle of birth and death for a long time more?"

佛告诸比丘：「于意云何？恒河流水及四大海，其水为多，汝等长夜轮转生死，饮其母乳为多耶？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "According to my understanding of the meaning of what the World-Honored One has said, the milk we have drunk while wandering in the cycle of birth and death for a long time is more than the water in the Ganges and the four great oceans."

比丘白佛：「如我解世尊所说义，我等长夜轮转生死，饮其母乳，多于恒河及四大海水。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "Excellent! Excellent! The milk you have drunk while wandering in the cycle of birth and death for a long time is indeed more than the water in the Ganges and the four great oceans. Why is this? For a long time, you have been reborn as elephants, drinking the milk of your mothers, countless times. You have also been reborn as camels, horses, cows, donkeys, and other animals, drinking the milk of your mothers, countless times. For a long time, you have been abandoned in charnel grounds, with pus and blood flowing out, also countless times. You have fallen into the hells, animal realms, and hungry ghost realms, with marrow and blood flowing out, also like this. Bhikṣus! Since beginningless time, you have been wandering in the cycle of birth and death, not knowing the original limit of suffering. What do you think, bhikṣus? Is form permanent or impermanent?"

佛告比丘：「善哉！善哉！汝等长夜轮转生死，饮其母乳，多于恒河及四大海水。所以者何？汝等长夜或生象中，饮其母乳，无量无数。或生驼、马、牛、驴诸禽兽类饮其母乳，其数无量。汝等长夜弃于塜间，脓血流出，亦复无量。或堕地狱、畜生、饿鬼，髓血流出，亦复如是。比丘！汝等无始生死轮转已来，不知苦之本际。云何？比丘！色为常耶？为无常耶？」

The bhikṣus said to the Buddha, "Impermanent, World-Honored One!"

比丘白佛：「非常。世尊！」

The Buddha said, 'Up to the noble disciples observing the five aggregates as not-self and not mine, they attain non-grasping in the world. Not grasping, they are unattached. What is to be done is done, and they know for themselves that they will not experience future existence.'

「乃至圣弟子于五受阴观察非我、非我所，于诸世间得无所取；不取已，无所著，所作已作，自知不受后有。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha said and joyfully practiced it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
