SĀ 957

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 957（九五七） 身命

Parallel to: SN 44.9 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, a renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan came to where the Buddha was, joined his palms in greeting, and asked, "Gautama! I wish to ask something. Is there time to answer me?"

时，有婆蹉种出家来诣佛所，合掌问讯，问讯已，退坐一面，白佛言：「瞿昙！欲有所问，宁有闲暇见答以不？」

The Buddha told the renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan, "Ask as you wish, and I will explain for you."

佛告婆蹉种出家：「随汝所问，当为汝说。」

The renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan said to the Buddha, "How is it, Gautama! Is life the same as the body?"

婆蹉种出家白佛言：「云何？瞿昙！命即身耶？」

The Buddha told the renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan, "If life is the same as the body, this is indeterminate."

佛告婆蹉种出家：「命即身者，此是无记。」

"How is it, Gautama! Is life different from the body?"

「云何？瞿昙！为命异身异耶？」

The Buddha told the renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan, "If life is different from the body, this is also indeterminate."

佛告婆蹉种出家：「命异身异者，此亦无记。」

The renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan said to the Buddha, "How is it, Gautama! Is life the same as the body? You say it is indeterminate. Is life different from the body? You say it is indeterminate. What is so wonderful about the śramaṇa Gautama? When his disciples die, he immediately says, 'So-and-so is reborn there, so-and-so is reborn there.' Those disciples, when their lives end and they abandon their bodies, immediately take rebirth in other places with a mind-born body. At that time, is it not that life is different from the body?"

婆蹉种出家白佛：「云何？瞿昙！命即身耶？答言：『无记』。命异身异？答言：『无记』。沙门瞿昙有何等奇？弟子命终，即记说言：『某生彼处，某生彼处。』彼诸弟子于此命终舍身，即乘意生身生于余处。当于尔时，非为命异身异也？」

The Buddha told the Ba-ts'a clan, "This is said with remainder, not without remainder."

佛告婆蹉：「此说有余，不说无余。」

The Ba-ts'a clan said to the Buddha, "Gautama! How is it said with remainder, not without remainder?"

婆蹉白佛：「瞿昙！云何说有余，不说无余？」

The Buddha told the Ba-ts'a clan, "It is like fire, which can burn with remainder, not without remainder."

佛告婆蹉：「譬如火，有余得然，非无余。」

The Ba-ts'a clan said to the Buddha, "I see fire burning without remainder."

婆蹉白佛：「我见火无余亦然。」

The Buddha asked the Ba-ts'a clan, "How do you see fire burning without remainder?"

佛告婆蹉：「云何见火无余亦然？」

The Ba-ts'a clan said to the Buddha, "It is like a great mass of burning fire. If a strong wind comes and blows, the fire flies into the air. Is it not without any remaining fire?"

婆蹉白佛：「譬如大聚炽火，疾风来吹，火飞空中，岂非无余火耶？」

The Buddha told the Ba-ts'a clan, "The fire blown and carried by the wind still has some remaining. It is not without any remaining."

佛告婆蹉：「风吹飞火，即是有余，非无余也。」

The Ba-ts'a clan said to the Buddha, "Gautama! The fire flying in the air, how can you say it has some remaining?"

婆蹉白佛：「瞿昙！空中飞火。云何名有余？」

The Buddha told the Ba-ts'a clan, "The fire flying in the air relies on the wind to stay and burn. Because it relies on the wind, I say it has some remaining."

佛告婆蹉：「空中飞火依风故住，依风故然；以依风故，故说有余。」

The Ba-ts'a clan said to the Buddha, "Beings here, at the end of their lives, take a mind-made body and are reborn elsewhere. How can you say they have some remaining?"

婆蹉白佛：「众生于此命终，乘意生身往生余处。云何有余？」

The Buddha told the Ba-ts'a clan, "Beings here, at the end of their lives, take a mind-made body and are reborn elsewhere. At that time, because of attachment, they take and abide. Therefore, I say they have some remaining."

佛告婆蹉：「众生于此处命终，乘意生身生于余处，当于尔时，因爱故取，因爱而住，故说有余。」

The Ba-ts'a clan said to the Buddha, "Beings are attached to what remains and cling to what remains. Only the World-Honored One has attained the remainderless and realized perfect enlightenment. Śramaṇa Gautama! There are many things in the world. I take my leave and return."

婆蹉白佛：「众生以爱乐有余，染著有余；唯有世尊得彼无余，成等正觉。沙门瞿昙！世间多缘，请辞还去。」

The Buddha told the Ba-ts'a clan, "It is time for you to know."

佛告婆蹉：「宜知是时！」

The Ba-ts'a clan, having heard the Buddha's teaching while leaving home, rejoiced and followed the joy. He rose from his seat and departed.

婆蹉出家闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，从坐起而去。
