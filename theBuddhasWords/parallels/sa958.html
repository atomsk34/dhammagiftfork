SĀ 958

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 958（九五八） 目连

Parallel to: SN 44.7 / SĀ² 2.191

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Kalandakanivāpa in the Bamboo Grove near Rājagṛha. At that time Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana was also staying there.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。尔时，尊者大目揵连亦于彼住。

At that time a monk of the Bahe clan went to Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana and exchanged greetings with him. After the greetings and polite conversation, he sat to one side and said to Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, “I have a question. Would you have the leisure to answer it?”

时，有婆蹉种出家诣尊者大目揵连所，与尊者目揵连面相问讯慰劳，慰劳已，退坐一面，语尊者大目揵连：「欲有所问，宁有闲暇见答以不？」

Maudgalyāyana replied, “Bahe, ask as you wish. I will answer what I know.”

目连答言：「婆蹉！随意所问，知者当答。」

Then the monk of the Bahe clan asked Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, “For what reason and condition do other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas answer when asked about the Tathāgata’s existence after death, non-existence after death, both existence and non-existence after death, or neither existence nor non-existence after death? But when asked by śramaṇa Gautama about the Tathāgata’s existence after death, non-existence after death, both existence and non-existence after death, or neither existence nor non-existence after death, he does not give a definite answer?”

时，婆蹉种出家问尊者目揵连：「何因、何缘余沙门、婆罗门有人来问云何如来有后死、无后死、有无后死、非有非无后死，皆悉随答。而沙门瞿昙有来问言如来有后死、无后死、有无后死、非有非无后死，而不记说？」

Mahāmaudgalyāyana said, “Bahe, other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas do not truly know form, the origin of form, the cessation of form, the taste of form, the suffering of form, and the exit from form. Because they do not truly know, they grasp at the Tathāgata's existence after death, non-existence after death, both existence and non-existence after death, and neither existence nor non-existence after death. They do not truly know feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness, the origin of consciousness, the cessation of consciousness, the taste of consciousness, the suffering of consciousness, and the exit from consciousness. Because they do not truly know, they grasp at the Tathāgata's existence after death, non-existence after death, both existence and non-existence after death, and neither existence nor non-existence after death.

目揵连言：「婆蹉！余沙门、婆罗门于色、色集、色灭、色味、色患、色出不如实知；不如实知故，于如来有后死则取著，如来无后死、有后死、有无后死、非有后死非无后死则生取著。受、想、行、识、识集、识灭、识味、识患、识出不如实知；不如实知故，于如来有后死生取著，无后死、有无后死、非有非无后死生取著。

The Tathāgata truly knows form, the origin of form, the cessation of form, the taste of form, the suffering of form, and the exit from form. Because he truly knows, he is not attached to the Tathāgata's existence after death, non-existence after death, both existence and non-existence after death, or neither existence nor non-existence after death. He truly knows feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness, the origin of consciousness, the cessation of consciousness, the taste of consciousness, the suffering of consciousness, and the exit from consciousness. Because he truly knows, he is not attached to the Tathāgata's existence after death, non-existence after death, both existence and non-existence after death, or neither existence nor non-existence after death. It is profound, vast, immeasurable, and countless, all completely extinguished.

「如来者，于色如实知，色集、色灭、色味、色患、色出如实知；如实知故，于如来有后死则不著，无后死、有无后死、非有非无后死则不著。受、想、行、识如实知，识集、识灭、识味、识患、识出如实知；如实知故，于如来有后死则不然，无后死、有无后死、非有非无后死则不然。甚深广大，无量无数，皆悉寂灭。

"Bahe, for such reasons and conditions, other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, if they come and ask about the Tathāgata's existence after death, non-existence after death, both existence and non-existence after death, or neither existence nor non-existence after death, will give a definitive answer. For such reasons and conditions, if the Tathāgata is asked about the Tathāgata's existence after death, non-existence after death, both existence and non-existence after death, or neither existence nor non-existence after death, he will not give a definitive answer."

「婆蹉！如是因、如是缘，余沙门、婆罗门若有来问如来有后死、无后死、有无后死、非有非无后死，则为记说。如是因、如是缘，如来若有来问如来有后死、无后死、有无后死、非有非无后死，不为记说。」

At that time, the renunciant of the Bahe clan, having heard what Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana said, joyfully assented and rose from his seat to depart.

时，婆蹉种出家闻尊者大目揵连所说，欢喜随喜，从坐起而去。
