SĀ 959

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 959（九五九） 迦旃延

Parallel to: SN 44.8 (~) / SN 44.11 (~) / SĀ² 2.192 (~) / SĀ² 2.193 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, a renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan came to where the Buddha was, joined his palms in greeting, and asked, "Gautama, for what reason and condition do other śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas, if asked..." and so on, as above.

时，有婆蹉种出家来诣佛所，合掌问讯，问讯已，退坐一面，白佛言：「瞿昙！何因、何缘余沙门、婆罗门若有来问……」如上广说。

At that time, the renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan exclaimed, "How wonderful, Gautama! The meaning of the disciple and the master are the same, the phrases are the same, the flavors are the same, up to the same ultimate meaning. Gautama, I now go to Mahāmaudgalyāyana and ask him about this meaning, these phrases, and these flavors. He also answers me with this meaning, these phrases, and these flavors, just as Gautama has said. Therefore, Gautama, it is truly wonderful that the meaning of the master and disciple are the same, the phrases are the same, the flavors are the same, and the ultimate meaning is the same."

尔时，婆蹉种出家叹言：「奇哉！瞿昙！弟子、大师义同义、句同句、味同味，乃至同第一义。瞿昙！我今诣摩诃目揵连，以如是义、如是句、如是味而问于彼，彼亦以如是义、如是句、如是味而答我，如今瞿昙所说。是故，瞿昙！真为奇特，大师、弟子义同义、句同句、味同味，同第一义。」

At that time, the renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan had some causes and conditions to go to the village of Nālaka. After finishing his affairs, he went to Venerable Sañjayin Kātyāyana, exchanged greetings, and sat to one side. He asked Sañjayin Kātyāyana, "For what reason and condition does the śramaṇa Gautama, if asked about the Tathāgata's existence after death, non-existence after death, both existence and non-existence after death, or neither existence nor non-existence after death, not give a definitive answer?"

尔时，婆蹉种出家有诸因缘至那梨聚落。营事讫已，诣尊者诜陀迦旃延所，共相问讯，问讯已，退坐一面，问诜陀迦旃延：「何因、何缘沙门瞿昙若有来问如来有后死、无后死、有无后死、非有非无后死，不为记说？」

Sañjayin Kātyāyana said to the renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan, "I will ask you now, please answer as you wish. What do you think? If there are causes and conditions, if one practices giving, whether it be form, formless, with perception, without perception, neither with nor without perception, if those causes, those conditions, those practices cease without remainder, and after they have ceased forever, does the Tathāgata give a definitive answer about existence after death, non-existence after death, both existence and non-existence after death, or neither existence nor non-existence after death?"

诜陀迦旃延语婆蹉种出家：「我今问汝，随意答我。于汝意云何？若因、若缘，若行身施，若色、若无色，若想、若非想、若非想非非想，若彼因、彼缘、彼行无余行灭。永灭已，如来于彼有所记说，言：『有后死、无后死、有无后死、非有非无后死』耶？」

The renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan said to Sañjayin Kātyāyana, "If there are causes and conditions, if one establishes various practices, whether it be form, formless, with perception, without perception, neither with nor without perception, if those causes, those conditions, those practices cease without remainder, how does Gautama give a definitive answer about the Tathāgata's existence after death, non-existence after death, both existence and non-existence after death, or neither existence nor non-existence after death?"

婆蹉种出家语诜陀迦旃延：「若因、若缘，若种施设诸行，若色、若非色，若想、若非想、若非想非非想，彼因、彼缘、彼行无余灭。云何瞿昙于彼记说如来有后死、无后死、有无后死、非有非无后死？」

Sañjayin Kātyāyana said to the renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan, "Therefore, the Tathāgata, for this cause and this condition, does not give a definitive answer when asked about the Tathāgata's existence after death, non-existence after death, both existence and non-existence after death, or neither existence nor non-existence after death."

诜陀迦旃延语婆蹉种出家：「是故如来以是因、以是缘故，有问如来有后死、无后死、有无后死、非有非无后死，不为记说。」

The renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan asked Sañjayin Kātyāyana, "How long have you been a disciple of the śramaṇa Gautama?"

婆蹉种出家问诜陀迦旃延：「汝于沙门瞿昙弟子，为日久如？」

Sañjayin Kātyāyana replied, "It has been a little over three years since I went forth to practice the holy life in the true Dharma and Vinaya."

诜陀迦旃延答言：「少过三年，于正法、律出家修梵行。」

The renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan said, "Sañjayin Kātyāyana! You have quickly obtained such good benefits. Having gone forth for such a short time, you have attained such bodily and verbal discipline, and also such wisdom and eloquence."

婆蹉种出家言：「诜陀迦旃延！快得善利，少时出家而得如是身律仪、口律仪，又得如是智慧辩才。」

At that time, the renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan, having heard Sañjayin Kātyāyana's words, joyfully assented and rose from his seat to depart.

时，婆蹉种出家闻诜陀迦旃延所说，欢喜随喜，从坐起去。
