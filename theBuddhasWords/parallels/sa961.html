SĀ 961

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 961（九六一） 无我

Parallel to: SN 44.10 / SĀ² 2.195

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, a renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan came to where the Buddha was, joined his palms in greeting, and asked, "What is it, Gautama? Is there a self?"

时，有婆蹉种出家来诣佛所，合掌问讯，问讯已，退坐一面，白佛言：「云何？瞿昙！为有我耶？」

At that time, the World-Honored One remained silent and did not answer.

尔时，世尊默然不答。

Thus, it was asked three times. At that time, the World-Honored One also did not answer three times.

如是再三。尔时，世尊亦再三不答。

At that time, the renunciant of the Baizhi clan thought, "I have already asked the śramaṇa Gautama three times, but he has not answered. I should just return."

尔时，婆蹉种出家作是念：「我已三问沙门瞿昙，而不见答，但当还去。」

At that time, Venerable Ānanda was standing behind the Buddha, holding a fan and fanning the Buddha. Ānanda then said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! The renunciant of the Baizhi clan asked three times, but why did the World-Honored One not answer? Will this not increase the evil and wrong views of the renunciant of the Baizhi clan, saying that the śramaṇa cannot answer his questions?"

时，尊者阿难住于佛后，执扇扇佛。尔时，阿难白佛言：「世尊！彼婆蹉种出家三问，世尊何故不答？岂不增彼婆蹉种出家恶邪见，言沙门不能答其所问？」

The Buddha told Ānanda, "If I were to say there is a self, it would increase their previous wrong views. If I were to say there is no self, would their previous delusion not increase even more delusion, saying that what was previously a self is now completely extinguished? If there was a self previously, that would be the view of eternalism, and now being extinguished would be the view of annihilationism. The Tathāgata is free from these two extremes and teaches the Dharma in the middle way, namely, 'Because this exists, that exists; because this arises, that arises,' that is, conditioned by ignorance, there are karmic formations, up to birth, old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress."

佛告阿难：「我若答言有我，则增彼先来邪见；若答言无我，彼先痴惑岂不更增痴惑？言先有我从今断灭。若先来有我则是常见，于今断灭则是断见。如来离于二边，处中说法，所谓是事有故是事有，是事起故是事生，谓缘无明行，乃至生、老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦灭。」

Having spoken this sūtra, Venerable Ānanda, having heard what the Buddha said, rejoiced and upheld it.

佛说此经已，尊者阿难闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
