SĀ 962

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 962（九六二） 见

Parallel to: MN 72 / SĀ² 2.196

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, the renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan came to where the Buddha was, exchanged greetings with the World-Honored One, and after the greetings, sat to one side and said to the Buddha, "Gautama, how do you Gautama hold such a view and make such a statement: 'The world is permanent, this is true, the rest is false'?"

尔时，婆蹉种出家来诣佛所，与世尊面相问讯，问讯已，退坐一面，白佛言：「瞿昙！云何瞿昙作如是见、如是说：『世间常，此是真实，余则虚妄。』耶？」

The Buddha told the renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan, "I do not hold such a view and make such a statement: 'The world is permanent, this is true, the rest is false'."

佛告婆蹉种出家：「我不作如是见、如是说：『世间常，是则真实，余则虚妄。』」

"How do you Gautama hold such a view and make such a statement: 'The world is impermanent, both permanent and impermanent, neither permanent nor impermanent; has an end, has no end, both has an end and has no end, neither has an end nor has no end; life is the body, life is different from the body, the Tathāgata exists after death, does not exist after death, both exists and does not exist after death, neither exists nor does not exist after death'?"

「云何瞿昙作如是见、如是说：『世间无常、常无常、非常非无常，有边、无边、边无边、非边非无边，命即是身、命异身异，如来有后死、无后死、有无后死、非有非无后死。』？」

The Buddha told the renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan, "I do not hold such a view and make such a statement, up to neither existence nor non-existence after death."

佛告婆蹉种出家：「我不作如是见、如是说，乃至非有非无后死。」

At that time, the renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan said to the Buddha, "Gautama! Regarding this view, what faults do you see, that you do not speak of any of these views?"

尔时，婆蹉种出家白佛言：「瞿昙！于此见，见何等过患，而于此诸见，一切不说？」

The Buddha told the renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan, "If one holds the view that the world is permanent, this is true and the rest is false, this is an inverted view, this is an observing view, this is a wavering view, this is a defiled view, this is a binding view, this is suffering, this is obstruction, this is affliction, this is heat, bound by the fetters of views, ignorant and unlearned ordinary people will give rise to birth, old age, sickness, death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress in the future. Ba-ts'a clan renunciant! If one holds the view that the world is impermanent, both permanent and impermanent, neither permanent nor impermanent, has an end, has no end, both has an end and has no end, neither has an end nor has no end, this is life, this is the body, life is different from the body, the Tathāgata exists after death, does not exist after death, both exists and does not exist after death, neither exists nor does not exist after death. This is an inverted view, up to the arising of sorrow, lamentation, pain, and distress."

佛告婆蹉种出家：「若作是见，世间常，此则真实，余则虚妄者，此是倒见、此是观察见、此是动摇见、此是垢污见、此是结见，是苦、是阂、是恼、是热，见结所系，愚痴无闻凡夫于未来世，生、老、病、死、忧、悲、恼、苦生。婆蹉种出家！若作是见，世间无常、常无常、非常非无常，有边、无边、边无边、非有边非无边，是命是身、命异身异，如来有后死、无后死、有无后死、非有非无后死。此是倒见，乃至忧、悲、恼、苦生。」

The renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan said to the Buddha, "Gautama! What view do you hold?"

婆蹉种出家白佛：「瞿昙！何所见？」

The Buddha told the renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan, "The Tathāgata's view is complete. Ba-ts'a clan renunciant! The Tathāgata's view is to see this noble truth of suffering, this noble truth of the origin of suffering, this noble truth of the cessation of suffering, and this noble truth of the path leading to the cessation of suffering. Having such knowledge and such views, one abandons all views, all feelings, all births, all I and mine, and the afflictions of pride and attachment. One cuts them off, pacifies them, cools them, and realizes the truth. Such is liberation. Bhikṣu! Birth is not so, nor is non-birth so."

佛告婆蹉种出家：「如来所见已毕。婆蹉种出家！然如来见，谓见此苦圣谛、此苦集圣谛、此苦灭圣谛、此苦灭道迹圣谛；作如是知、如是见已，于一切见、一切受、一切生，一切我、我所见、我慢系著使，断灭、寂静、清凉、真实，如是等解脱。比丘！生者不然，不生亦不然。」

The Ba-ts'a clan said to the Buddha, "Gautama! Why do you say that birth is not so?"

婆蹉白佛：「瞿昙！何故说言生者不然？」

The Buddha told the Ba-ts'a clan, "I will ask you, answer as you wish. Ba-ts'a! Suppose someone lights a fire in front of you. Do you see the fire burning? Then the fire is extinguished right in front of you. Do you see the fire extinguished?"

佛告婆蹉：「我今问汝，随意答我。婆蹉！犹如有人于汝前然火，汝见火然不？即于汝前火灭，汝见火灭不？」

The Ba-ts'a clan said to the Buddha, "Yes, Gautama!"

婆蹉白佛：「如是，瞿昙！」

The Buddha told the Ba-ts'a clan, "If someone asks you, 'Where did the fire go? Did it go to the east, west, south, or north?' If asked this, how would you answer?"

佛告婆蹉：「若有人问汝：『向者火然，今在何处？为东方去耶？西方、南方、北方去耶？』如是问者，汝云何说？」

The Ba-ts'a clan said to the Buddha, "Gautama! If someone were to ask me this, I would answer like this: 'If someone were to light a fire in front of me, it would burn because of the firewood and grass. If more firewood is not added, the fire will be extinguished forever and will not arise again. To say that it goes to the east, south, west, or north is not right.'"

婆蹉白佛：「瞿昙！若有来作如是问者，我当作如是答：『若有于我前然火，薪草因缘故然，若不增薪，火则永灭，不复更起，东方、南方、西方、北方去者，是则不然。』」

The Buddha told the Ba-ts'a clan, "I also say that form is already cut off and known, and feeling, perception, formation, and consciousness are already cut off and known. Cutting off its root is like cutting off the top of a palm tree, with no further growth. It will never arise again in the future. To say that it goes to the east, south, west, or north is not right. It is very profound, vast, immeasurable, and countless, and is forever extinguished."

佛告婆蹉：「我亦如是说，色已断已知，受、想、行、识已断已知，断其根本，如截多罗树头，无复生分，于未来世永不复起。若至东方，南、西、北方，是则不然，甚深广大，无量无数永灭。」

The Ba-ts'a clan said to the Buddha, "I will give an analogy."

婆蹉白佛：「我当说譬。」

The Buddha told the Ba-ts'a clan, "It is the right time to know."

佛告婆蹉：「为知是时。」

The Ba-ts'a clan said to the Buddha, "Gautama! It is like a good and clean piece of land near a city or village, where a dense forest of strong and solid trees grows. There is a large strong and solid tree that has been there for many thousands of years. As time goes by, the branches and leaves fall off, the bark becomes rotten, and only the trunk stands alone. In the same way, Gautama! The Dharma and Vinaya of the Tathāgata is devoid of branches, leaves, and twigs, and only the empty trunk stands alone and strong."

婆蹉白佛：「瞿昙！譬如近城邑聚落，有好净地，生坚固林，有一大坚固树，其生已来经数千岁，日夜既久，枝叶零落，皮肤枯朽，唯干独立。如是，瞿昙！如来法、律离诸枝条柯] cf.【丽】叶，唯空干坚固独立。」

At that time, the Ba-ts'a clan who had gone forth heard what the Buddha said, and with joy and delight, he rose from his seat and departed.

尔时，婆蹉出家闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，从] cf.【丽】坐起去。
