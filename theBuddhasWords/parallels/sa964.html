SĀ 964

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 964（九六四） 出家

Parallel to: MN 73 / SĀ² 2.198

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, a renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan came to where the Buddha was, exchanged greetings with the World-Honored One, and then sat to one side. He said to the Buddha, "Gautama! I wish to ask something. Is there time to explain it for me?" At that time, the World-Honored One remained silent.

时，有婆蹉种出家来诣佛所，与世尊面相慰劳已，退坐一面，白佛言：「瞿昙！欲有所问，宁有闲暇为解说不？」尔时，世尊默然而住。

The renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan asked a second and third time, and the Buddha also remained silent a second and third time.

婆蹉种出家第二、第三问，佛亦第二、第三默然而住。

At that time, the renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan said to the Buddha, "I follow the teachings of Gautama. Why do you remain silent when I ask something?"

时，婆蹉种出家白佛言：「我与瞿昙共相随顺，今有所问，何故默然？」

At that time, the World-Honored One thought, "This renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan has been sincere and straightforward for a long time, without deceit or pretense. When he asks questions, it is because he does not know, not to intentionally disturb. I shall now accept his questions with the Abhidharma and Vinaya." Having thought this, he said to the renunciant of the Ba-ts'a clan, "Ask whatever you wish, and I shall explain it for you."

尔时，世尊作是念：「此婆蹉种出家长夜质直，不谄不伪，时有所问，皆以不知故，非故恼乱，我今当以阿毘昙律纳受于彼。」作是念已，告婆蹉种出家：「随汝所问，当为解说。」

Ba-ts'a said to the Buddha, "What do you think, Gautama? Are there good dharmas?"

婆蹉白佛：「云何？瞿昙！有善法耶？」

The Buddha replied, "There are."

佛答言：「有。」

Ba-ts'a said to the Buddha, "Please explain the good and bad dharmas to me, so that I may understand."

婆蹉白佛：「当为我说善、不善法，令我得解。」

The Buddha said to Ba-ts'a, "I will now briefly explain the good and bad dharmas for you. Listen carefully and consider well. Ba-ts'a! Desire is an unwholesome dharma, subduing desire is a wholesome dharma; anger, hatred, and delusion are unwholesome dharmas, subduing anger, hatred, and delusion are wholesome dharmas. Taking life is an unwholesome dharma, refraining from taking life is a wholesome dharma; stealing, sexual misconduct, false speech, divisive speech, harsh speech, idle chatter, greed, hatred, and wrong views are unwholesome dharmas, not stealing, up to right views are wholesome dharmas. This, Ba-ts'a! I have now explained the three kinds of wholesome dharmas and the three kinds of unwholesome dharmas. Thus, the noble disciple truly knows the three kinds of wholesome dharmas and the three kinds of unwholesome dharmas. One who truly knows the ten kinds of unwholesome dharmas and the ten kinds of wholesome dharmas will completely extinguish desire, anger, and delusion. One who completely extinguishes all defiled dharmas will attain liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom. One will know and realize in this very life: 'My birth is ended, the holy life has been established, what was to be done has been done, and I will not experience another existence after this one.'"

佛告婆蹉：「我今当为汝略说善、不善法。谛听，善思。婆蹉！贪欲者是不善法，调伏贪欲是则善法；瞋恚、愚痴是不善法，调伏恚、痴是则善法。杀生者是不善法，离杀生者是则善法；偷盗、邪婬、妄语、两舌、恶口、绮语、贪、恚、邪见是不善法，不盗，乃至正见是则善法，是为，婆蹉！我今已说三种善法、三种不善法。如是，圣弟子于三种善法、三种不善法如实知，十种不善法、十种善法如实知者，则于贪欲无余灭尽，瞋恚、愚痴无余灭尽者，则于一切有漏灭尽，无漏心解脱、慧解脱，现法自知作证：『我生已尽，梵行已立，所作已作，自知不受后有。』」

Ba-ts'a said to the Buddha, "Is there a bhikkhu who, in this Dharma and Vinaya, has exhausted the contaminants, attained liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom, and will not experience another existence after this one?"

婆蹉白佛：「颇有一比丘于此法、律得尽有漏，无漏心解脱，乃至不受后有耶？」

The Buddha said to Ba-ts'a, "Not only one, two, or three, but up to five hundred. There are many bhikkhus who, in this Dharma and Vinaya, have exhausted the contaminants, attained liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom, and will not experience another existence after this one."

佛告婆蹉：「不但若一，若二、若三，乃至五百，有众多比丘于此法、律尽诸有漏，乃至不受后有。」

Ba-ts'a said to the Buddha, "Let's set aside the bhikkhus. Is there a bhikkhuni who, in this Dharma and Vinaya, has exhausted the contaminants, attained liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom, and will not experience another existence after this one?"

婆蹉白佛：「且置比丘，有一比丘尼于此法、律尽诸有漏，乃至不受后有不？」

The Buddha said to Ba-ts'a, "Not only one, two, or three bhikkhunis, but up to five hundred. There are many bhikkhunis who, in this Dharma and Vinaya, have exhausted the contaminants, attained liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom, and will not experience another existence after this one."

佛告婆蹉：「不但一、二、三比丘尼，乃至五百，有众多比丘尼于此法、律尽诸有漏，乃至不受后有。」

Ba-ts'a said to the Buddha, "Let's set aside the bhikkhunis. Is there a male lay follower who practices the brahma-life and attains liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom, having crossed over doubt in this Dharma and Vinaya?"

婆蹉白佛：「置比丘尼，有一优婆塞修诸梵行，于此法、律度狐疑不？」

The Buddha said to Ba-ts'a, "Not only one, two, or three, but up to five hundred male lay followers. There are many male lay followers who practice the brahma-life and, in this Dharma and Vinaya, sever the five lower fetters, attain the state of a non-returner, and will not be reborn in this world again."

佛告婆蹉：「不但一、二、三，乃至五百优婆塞，乃有众多优婆塞修诸梵行，于此法、律断五下分结，得成阿那含，不复还生此。」

Ba-ts'a said to the Buddha, "Let's set aside the male lay followers. Is there a female lay follower who practices the brahma-life and attains liberation of mind and liberation through wisdom, having crossed over doubt in this Dharma and Vinaya?"

婆蹉白佛：「复置优婆塞，颇有一优婆夷于此法、律修持梵行，于此法、律度狐疑不？」

The Buddha said to Ba-ts'a, "Not only one, two, or three female lay followers, but up to five hundred. There are many female lay followers who, in this Dharma and Vinaya, sever the five lower fetters, are born in that realm, attain the state of a non-returner, and will not be reborn in this world again."

佛告婆蹉：「不但一、二、三优婆夷，乃至五百，乃有众多优婆夷于此法、律断五下分结，于彼化生，得阿那含，不复还生此。」

Ba-ts'a said to the Buddha, "Let's set aside the monks, nuns, male lay followers, and female lay followers who practice the brahma-life. Is there a male lay follower who enjoys the five desires, but who has crossed over doubt in this Dharma and Vinaya?"

婆蹉白佛：「置比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆夷修梵行者，颇有优婆塞受五欲，而于此法、律度狐疑不？」

The Buddha said to Ba-ts'a, "Not only one, two, or three, but up to five hundred. There are many male lay followers who live at home with wives and children, adorned with fragrant flowers, and who keep male and female servants. In this Dharma and Vinaya, they sever the three fetters, and their greed, anger, and delusion are weakened. They attain the state of a once-returner, coming and going once, and ultimately reaching the end of suffering."

佛告婆蹉：「不但一、二、三，乃至五百，乃有众多优婆塞居家妻子，香华严饰，畜养奴婢，于此法、律断三结，贪、恚、痴薄，得斯陀含，一往一来，究竟苦边。」

Ba-ts'a said to the Buddha, "Let's set aside the male lay followers. Is there a female lay follower who enjoys the five desires, but who has crossed over doubt in this Dharma and Vinaya?"

婆蹉白佛：「复置优婆塞，颇有一优婆夷受习五欲，于此法、律得度狐疑不？」

The Buddha said to Ba-ts'a, "Not only one, two, or three, but up to five hundred. There are many female lay followers who live at home, raising sons and daughters, enjoying the five desires, adorned with fragrant flowers. In this Dharma and Vinaya, they exhaust the three fetters, attain the state of a stream-enterer, do not fall into the unwholesome destinies, are definitely headed for supreme perfect enlightenment, are reborn seven times among gods and humans, and ultimately reach the end of suffering."

佛告婆蹉：「不但一、二、三，乃至五百，乃有众多优婆夷在于居家，畜养男女，服习五欲，华香严饰，于此法、律三结尽，得须陀洹，不堕恶趣法，决定正向三菩提，七有天人往生，究竟苦边。」

Ba-ts'a said to the Buddha, "Gautama! If the Śramaṇa Gautama attained perfect enlightenment, and if the monks, nuns, male lay followers, female lay followers who practiced the brahma-conduct, and the male lay followers and female lay followers who enjoyed the five desires, did not attain such merits, then it would not be complete; but because the Śramaṇa Gautama attained perfect enlightenment, and the monks, nuns, male lay followers, female lay followers who practiced the brahma-conduct, and the male lay followers and female lay followers who enjoyed the five desires, attained those merits, it is complete. Gautama! Now I will give an analogy.

婆蹉白佛言：「瞿昙！若沙门瞿昙成等正觉，若比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆夷修梵行者，及优婆塞、优婆夷服习五欲，不得如是功德者，则不满足；以沙门瞿昙成等正觉，比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆夷修诸梵行，及优婆塞、优婆夷服习五欲，而成就尔所功德故，则为满足。瞿昙！今当说譬。」

The Buddha said to Ba-ts'a, "Speak as you wish."

佛告婆蹉：「随意所说。」

Ba-ts'a said to the Buddha, "Just as when it rains heavily, the water flows downward, so too is the Dharma and Vinaya of Gautama. Monks, nuns, male lay followers, female lay followers, whether male or female, all follow the flow and head towards nirvāṇa, flowing towards nirvāṇa. How wonderful! The Dharma and Vinaya of the Buddha, Dharma, and Saṃgha are equal. If a renunciant from another path comes to Gautama and seeks to go forth and receive full ordination in the true Dharma and Vinaya, how long does it take before he is allowed to go forth?"

婆蹉白佛：「如天大雨，水流随下，瞿昙法、律亦复如是。比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆夷，若男、若女，悉皆随流，向于涅槃，浚输涅槃。甚奇！佛、法、僧平等法、律。为余异道出家来诣瞿昙所，于正法、律求出家、受具足者，几时便听出家？」

The Buddha said to Ba-ts'a, "If a renunciant from another path comes to the true Dharma and Vinaya and seeks to go forth and receive full ordination, he should stay with his preceptor for up to four months, wearing the robe. This is just a rough limit for people."

佛告婆蹉：「若余异道出家欲来于正法、律求出家、受具足者，乃至四月于和尚所受衣而住，然此是为人粗作齐限耳。」

Ba-ts'a said to the Buddha, "If renunciants from other paths come to the true Dharma and Vinaya and seek to go forth and receive full ordination, and if they are allowed to go forth after staying with their preceptors for up to four months, then I am able to stay with my preceptor for four months. If I am allowed to go forth and receive full ordination in the true Dharma and Vinaya, I will go forth and receive full ordination in the Dharma of Gautama and practice the holy life."

婆蹉白佛：「若诸异道出家来于正法、律欲求出家、受具足，听于和尚所受依，若满四月听出家者，我今堪能于四月在和尚所受依。若于正法、律而得出家、受具足，我当于瞿昙法中出家、受具足，修持梵行。」

The Buddha said to Ba-ts'a, "Didn't I previously make a rough limit for people?"

佛告婆蹉：「我先不说粗为人作分齐耶？」

Ba-ts'a said to the Buddha, "Yes, Gautama!"

婆蹉白佛：「如是，瞿昙！」

At that time the World Honored One told the bhikṣus, "You should allow Ba-ts'a to go forth and receive full ordination in the true Dharma and Vinaya."

尔时世尊告诸比丘：「汝等当度彼婆蹉出家于正法、律出家、受具足。」

Ba-ts'a was able to go forth and receive full ordination in the true Dharma and Vinaya, becoming a bhikṣu. Within half a month, he knew, recognized, saw, attained, realized, and realized all that should be known, recognized, seen, attained, realized, and realized in the Tathāgata's true Dharma.

婆蹉种出家即得于正法、律出家、受具足，成比丘分，乃至半月，学所应知、应识、应见、应得、应觉、应证，悉知、悉识、悉见、悉得、悉觉、悉证如来正法。

Venerable Ba-ts'a thought, "I have now realized all that should be known, recognized, seen, attained, realized, and realized. I will now go to see the World Honored One."

尊者婆蹉作是念：「我今已觉所应知、应识、应见、应得、应觉、应证，彼一切悉知、悉识、悉见、悉得、悉觉、悉证，今当往见世尊。」

At that time, Ba-ts'a went to where the World Honored One was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World Honored One! I have realized all that should be known, recognized, seen, attained, realized, and realized in the World Honored One's true Dharma. I only wish that the World Honored One would teach me the Dharma. Having heard the Dharma, I will dwell alone in a quiet place, diligently contemplating and abiding without negligence, contemplating: 'The reason good sons shave their heads and beards, put on the monastic robe, leave home with right faith, practice the path, and know for themselves that they will not be reborn again.'"

是时，婆蹉诣世尊所，稽首礼足，于一面住，白佛言：「世尊！我于学所应知、应识、应见、应得、应觉、应证，悉知、悉识、悉见、悉得、悉觉、悉证世尊正法，唯愿世尊为我说法，我闻法已，当独一静处，专精思惟，不放逸住，思惟：『所以善男子剃除须发，著袈裟衣，正信出家学道，乃至自知不受后有。』」

The Buddha told Ba-ts'a, "There are two dharmas that, when cultivated and practiced much, are called tranquility and insight. Cultivating and practicing these two dharmas much, one can know the realms and fruits, realize the realms, know the various realms, and realize the various realms. Thus, a bhikṣu who seeks to be free from desire and evil and unwholesome dharmas, and dwells in the fourth dhyāna in full, with loving-kindness, compassion, joy, and equanimity, in the sphere of the base of infinite space, the base of infinite consciousness, the base of nothingness, and the base of neither-perception-nor-non-perception, can cause me to exhaust the three fetters and attain the state of a srota-āpanna; with the exhaustion of the three fetters, and the thinning of greed, hatred, and delusion, one attains the state of a sakṛd-āgāmin; with the exhaustion of the five lower fetters, one attains the state of an anāgāmin; with various supernatural powers, the divine eye, divine ear, knowledge of others' minds, knowledge of past lives, knowledge of birth and death, and knowledge of the exhaustion of taints, one attains all of them. Therefore, bhikṣus! You should cultivate the two dharmas, cultivate them much, and cultivate them repeatedly. By cultivating the two dharmas, one can know the various realms, and even exhaust the taints."

佛告婆蹉：「有二法，修习多修习，所谓止、观。此二法修习多修习，得知界、果，觉了于界，知种种界，觉种种界。如是，比丘！欲求离欲，恶不善法，乃至第四禅具足住，慈、悲、喜、舍，空入处、识入处、无所有入处、非想非非想入处，令我三结尽，得须陀洹；三结尽，贪、恚、痴薄，得斯陀含；五下分结尽，得阿那含；种种神通境界，天眼、天耳、他心智、宿命智、生死智、漏尽智皆悉得。是故，比丘！当修二法，修习多修习，修二法故，知种种界，乃至漏尽。」

At that time, Venerable Ba-ts'a, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully bowed and departed. At that time, Ba-ts'a dwelled alone in a quiet place, diligently contemplating and abiding without negligence, and knew for himself that he would not be reborn again.

尔时，尊者婆蹉闻佛所说，欢喜作礼而去。尔时，婆蹉独一静处，专精思惟，不放逸住，乃至自知不受后有。

At that time, many bhikṣus were adorned and ready to go to the World-Honored One to pay respects and make offerings.

时，有众多比丘庄严方便，欲诣世尊恭敬供养。

At that time, Ba-ts'a asked the many bhikṣus, "Are you adorned and ready to go to the World-Honored One to pay respects and make offerings?"

尔时，婆蹉问众多比丘：「汝等庄严方便，欲诣世尊恭敬供养耶？」

The bhikṣus replied, "Yes."

诸比丘答言：「尔。」

At that time, Ba-ts'a said to the bhikṣus, "Venerable ones! Please convey my words to pay respects to the World-Honored One, inquiring about his well-being, ease, and freedom from illness and distress, and whether he is dwelling in peace and happiness. Say: 'The bhikṣu Ba-ts'a says to the World-Honored One: "I have already made offerings to the World-Honored One, fully served and delighted him, not without delight; I have done all that the Master and disciples should do, made offerings to the Master, and delighted him, not without delight."'

尔时，婆蹉语诸比丘：「尊者！持我语，敬礼世尊，问讯起居轻利，少病少恼，安乐住不？言：『婆蹉比丘白世尊言：「我已供养世尊，具足奉事，令欢悦，非不欢悦；大师弟子所作皆悉已作，供养大师，令欢悦，非不欢悦。」』」

At that time, many bhikṣus went to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at his feet, and sat to one side. They said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! The Venerable Ba-ts'a bows his head in respect at the feet of the World-Honored One..." up to "...delighted, not without delight."

时，众多比丘往诣佛所，稽首礼足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！尊者婆蹉稽首敬礼世尊足……」乃至「欢悦，非不欢悦。」

The Buddha told the bhikṣus, "The devas have already told me, and now you also speak. The Tathāgata has attained the supreme knowledge and vision, and also like the bhikṣu Ba-ts'a, has such virtuous power."

佛告诸比丘：「诸天先已语我，汝今复说。如来成就第一知见，亦如婆蹉比丘，有如是德力。」

At that time, the World-Honored One gave the first prediction to that bhikṣu Ba-ts'a.

尔时，世尊为彼婆蹉比丘说第一记。

After the Buddha had spoken this sūtra, the bhikṣus heard what the Buddha had said and joyfully practiced it.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
