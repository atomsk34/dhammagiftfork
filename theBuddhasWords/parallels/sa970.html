SĀ 970

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 970（九七〇） 舍罗步

Parallel to: AN 3.64 / SĀ² 2.204

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, there was an ascetic named Śāradvatīputra in Rājagṛha, who was staying by the side of the Sumāgadhā Pond. He proclaimed to his own assembly, "I know all the Dharma of the Śramaṇa Śākya. I have already known their Dharma and Vinaya, but I have abandoned them all."

时，王舍城有外道出家名舍罗步，住须摩竭陀池侧，于自众中作如是唱言：「沙门释子法我悉知，我先已知彼法、律而悉弃舍。」

At that time, many bhikṣus put on their robes in the morning, took their alms bowls, and entered Rājagṛha to beg for food. They heard that there was an ascetic named Śāradvatīputra who was staying by the side of the Sumāgadhā Pond in Rājagṛha. He proclaimed to his own assembly, "I have already known all the Dharma and Vinaya of the Śramaṇa Śākya. I have already known their Dharma and Vinaya, and then abandoned them." Having heard this, after finishing their alms round, they returned to the monastery, put away their robes and bowls, washed their feet, went to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at his feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! We put on our robes in the morning, took our alms bowls, and entered Rājagṛha to beg for food. We heard that there was an ascetic named Śāradvatīputra who was staying by the side of the Sumāgadhā Pond in Rājagṛha. He proclaimed to his own assembly, 'I have already known all the Dharma and Vinaya of the Śramaṇa Śākya. I have already known their Dharma and Vinaya, and then abandoned them.' Excellent! World-Honored One! Please go to the side of the Sumāgadhā Pond out of compassion for him!"

时，有众多比丘晨朝著衣持钵，入王舍城乞食。闻有外道名舍罗步，住王舍城须摩竭陀池侧，于自众中作如是唱：「沙门释子所有法、律我悉已知，先已知彼法、律，然后弃舍。」闻是语已，乞食毕，还精舍，举衣钵，洗足已，往诣佛所，稽首礼足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！我等晨朝著衣持钵，入王舍城乞食，闻有外道出家名舍罗步，住王舍城须摩竭陀池侧，于自众中作是唱言：『沙门释子法我已悉知，知彼法、律已，然后弃舍。』善哉！世尊！可自往彼须摩竭陀池侧，怜愍彼故！」

At that time, the World-Honored One silently consented. In the afternoon, he arose from meditation and went to the side of the Sumāgadhā Pond where the ascetic Śāradvatīputra was.

尔时，世尊默然而许，于日晡时从禅觉，往到须摩竭陀池侧外道舍罗步所。

At that time, the ascetic Śāradvatīputra saw from afar that the World-Honored One was coming. He immediately prepared a seat and invited the Buddha to sit.

时，舍罗步外道出家遥见世尊来，即敷床坐，请佛令坐。

The Buddha then took his seat and said to Śāradvatīputra, "Is it true that you said, 'I have already understood all the teachings and discipline of the Śramaṇa disciples. Having understood their teachings and discipline, I then abandoned them'?" At that time, Śāradvatīputra remained silent and did not answer.

佛即就坐，告舍罗步言：「汝实作是语：『沙门释子所有法、律，我悉已知。知彼法、律已，然后弃舍。』耶？」时，舍罗步默而不答。

The Buddha said to Śāradvatīputra, "You should now speak. Why are you silent? I will rejoice in what you have fully understood, and I will make you fully understand what you have not yet understood." At that time, Śāradvatīputra still remained silent. He was silent like this a second and a third time.

佛告舍罗步：「汝今应说，何故默然？汝所知满足者，我则随喜；不满足者，我当令汝满足。」时，舍罗步犹故默然。如是第二、第三说，彼再三默然住。

At that time, Śāradvatīputra had a disciple who practiced the same way. He said to Śāradvatīputra, "Master, you should go to the Śramaṇa Gautama to speak about what you know and see. Now the Śramaṇa Gautama has come here himself. Why don't you speak? The Śramaṇa Gautama also told you, 'If you are satisfied, I will rejoice. If you are not satisfied, I will make you satisfied.' Why are you silent and not speaking?" When that disciple of Śāradvatīputra urged him, he also remained silent.

时，舍罗步有一梵行弟子白舍罗步言：「师应往诣沙门瞿昙说所知见，今沙门瞿昙自来诣此，何故不说？沙门瞿昙又告师言：『若满足者，我则随喜；不满足者，当令满足。』何故默然而不记说？」彼舍罗步梵行弟子劝时，亦复默然。

At that time, the World-Honored One said to Śāradvatīputra, "If someone says, 'The Śramaṇa Gautama is not a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One,' if I were to admonish and question him well, and when I admonish and question him well, he would then speak about various external matters; or he would be angry, arrogant, and obstinate, and would not be able to manifest himself due to opposition and intolerance; or he would remain silent, ashamed and bowing his head, secretly reflecting on himself, just like Śāradvatīputra now. If someone says, 'The Śramaṇa Gautama does not have the right Dharma and Vinaya,' if I were to admonish and question him well, he would also remain silent like you today. If someone says, 'The Śramaṇa Gautama's disciples do not go in the right direction,' if I were to admonish and question him well, he would also remain silent like you today." At that time, the World-Honored One, having roared the lion's roar by the side of the Sumāgadhā Pond, rose from his seat and departed.

尔时，世尊告舍罗步：「若复有言：『沙门瞿昙非如来、应、等正觉。』我若善谏善问，善谏善问时，彼则辽落说诸外事；或忿恚慢覆，对阂不忍，无由能现；或默然抱愧低头，密自思省，如今舍罗步。若复作如是言非沙门瞿昙无正法、律者，我若善谏善问，彼亦如汝今日默然而住。若复有言非沙门瞿昙声闻善向者，我若善谏善问，彼亦乃至如汝今日默然而住。」尔时，世尊于须摩竭陀池侧师子吼已，从坐起而去。

At that time, Śāradvatīputra's disciple said to Śāradvatīputra, "It is like a cow whose two horns have been cut off. It enters an empty cow pen and kneels down, roaring loudly. The teacher is also like this, roaring like a lion among the disciples of the Śramaṇa Gautama. It is like a woman who wants to make a man's voice, but when she speaks, she makes a woman's voice. The teacher is also like this, roaring like a lion among the disciples of the Śramaṇa Gautama who are not disciples. It is like a jackal who wants to make a fox's voice, but when it speaks, it still makes a jackal's voice. The teacher is also like this, wanting to roar like a lion among the disciples of the Śramaṇa Gautama who are not disciples.

尔时，舍罗步梵行弟子语舍罗步言：「譬如有牛，截其两角，入空牛栏中，跪地大吼；师亦如是，于无沙门瞿昙弟子众中作师子吼。譬如女人欲作丈夫声，发声即作女声；师亦如是，于非沙门瞿昙弟子众中作师子吼。譬如野干欲作狐声，发声还作野干声；师亦如是，于非沙门瞿昙弟子众中欲作师子吼。」

At that time, Śāradvatīputra's disciple, having reproached and reviled Śāradvatīputra in front of him, rose from his seat and left.

时，舍罗步梵行弟子于舍罗步面前呵责毁呰已，从坐起去。
