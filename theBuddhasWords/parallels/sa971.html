SĀ 971

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 971（九七一） 上坐

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at the Kalandaka Bamboo Grove in Rājagṛha.

一时，佛住王舍城迦兰陀竹园。

At that time, there was a non-Buddhist ascetic named Upasampada living by the Sumāgadhā Pond in Rājagṛha. He said to his followers, "I will recite a verse. If you can respond, I will practice the holy life with you."

尔时，王舍城须摩竭陀池侧有外道出家名上坐，住彼池侧，于自众中作如是语：「我说一偈，若能报者，我当于彼修行梵行。」

At that time, many bhikṣus put on their robes in the morning, took their alms bowls, and entered Rājagṛha to beg for food. They heard that there was a non-Buddhist ascetic named Upasampada living by the Sumāgadhā Pond. He said to his followers, "I will recite a verse. If you can respond, I will practice the holy life with you." After begging for food, they returned to the monastery, put away their robes and bowls, washed their feet, went to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at the Buddha's feet, sat to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! This morning, we entered the city with many bhikṣus to beg for food. We heard that there was a non-Buddhist ascetic named Upasampada living by the Sumāgadhā Pond. He said to his followers, 'I will recite a verse. If you can respond, I will practice the holy life with you.' We only wish that the World-Honored One would go there out of compassion!"

时，有众多比丘晨朝著衣持钵，入王舍城乞食，闻有外道出家名曰上座，住须摩竭陀池侧，于自众中作如是说：「我说一偈，有能报者，我当于彼所修行梵行。」乞食毕，还精舍，举衣钵，洗足已，诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面，白佛言：「世尊！我今晨朝与众多比丘入城乞食，闻有外道出家名曰上坐，住须摩竭陀池侧，于自众中作如是说：『我说一偈，有能报者，我当于彼修行梵行。』唯愿世尊应自往彼，哀愍故！」

At that time, the World-Honored One silently accepted. In the afternoon, he arose from meditation and went to the Sumāgadhā Pond.

尔时，世尊默然而许，即日晡时从禅觉，往至须摩竭陀池侧。

At that time, the non-Buddhist ascetic Upasampada saw the World-Honored One from afar. He immediately prepared a seat and invited the Buddha to sit. After the Buddha sat down, he said to the non-Buddhist ascetic Upasampada, "Is it true that you said, 'I will recite a verse. If you can respond, I will practice the holy life with you'? You may now recite the verse. I can respond to it."

时，上坐外道出家遥见世尊，即敷床座，请佛令坐。世尊坐已，告上坐外道出家言：「汝实作是语：『我说一偈，若能报者，我当于彼修行梵行』耶？汝今便可说偈，我能报答。」

At that time, the non-Buddhist ascetic Upasampada piled up rope beds to make a high seat and ascended it. He then spoke a verse, saying:

时，彼外道即累绳床以为高座，自升其上。即说偈言：

Monks live by the Dharma, not terrifying sentient beings. With
peaceful minds, they practice detachment, upholding precepts and following tranquility.

「比丘以法活，　　不恐怖众生，意寂行舍离，　　持戒顺息止。」

At that time, the World-Honored One knew the thoughts of the senior non-Buddhist ascetic. He then spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，世尊知彼上坐外道心。即说偈言：

If you can follow your own words, I
will regard you as a good person. Observing
what you say, your words and actions do
not match. Be peaceful, self-disciplined, and do not
terrify sentient beings. Practice with a peaceful mind
detached, upholding pure precepts. Follow self-discipline and tranquility
with body, speech, and mind free from evil
Well-restrained in dwelling, not allowing negligence. This is
called following self-discipline and tranquility.

「汝于所说偈，　　能自随转者，我当于汝所，　　作善士夫观，观汝今所说，　　言行不相应，寂止自调伏，　　莫恐怖众生，行意寂远离，　　受持净戒者，顺调伏寂止，　　身口心离恶，善摄于住处，　　不令放逸者，是则名随顺，　　调伏及寂止。」

At that time, the senior non-Buddhist ascetic thought, "The Śramaṇa Gautama already knows my mind." He then descended from his seat, joined his palms, and said to the Buddha, "Can I now go forth and receive full ordination in the true Dharma and Vinaya, and become a bhikṣu?"

尔时，上坐外道出家作是念：「沙门瞿昙已知我心。」即从床而下，合掌白佛言：「今我可得于正法、律出家、受具足，成比丘法不？」

The Buddha told the senior non-Buddhist ascetic, "Now you can go forth and receive full ordination in the true Dharma and Vinaya, and become a bhikṣu."

佛告上坐外道出家：「今汝可得于正法、律出家、受具足，成比丘分。」

Thus, the senior non-Buddhist ascetic went forth and became a bhikṣu. Having become a bhikṣu, he thought, "The reason good sons shave their heads and beards, put on the saffron robes, leave the household life out of right faith, and go forth to practice the path..." up to "his mind is well liberated and he attains arahantship."

如是上坐外道出家得出家，作比丘已，思惟：「所以善男子剃除须发，著袈裟衣，正信非家，出家学道……。」乃至心善解脱，得阿罗汉。
