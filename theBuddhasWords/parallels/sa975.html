SĀ 975

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 975（九七五） 补缕低迦

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, the non-Buddhist ascetic Pūraṇa Kāśyapa came to where the Buddha was, exchanged greetings with the World-Honored One, and sat to one side. He said to the Buddha, "Gautama! The other day, many non-Buddhist ascetics and brāhmaṇas gathered in the Unprecedented Lecture Hall and discussed this: 'The wisdom of the śramaṇa Gautama is like an empty house. He cannot establish debates in the great assembly, saying what should be and what should not be, what is appropriate and what is inappropriate. It is like a blind cow that only grazes along the edges and does not enter the central field. The śramaṇa Gautama is also like this, without what should be and what should not be, without what is appropriate and what is inappropriate.'"

尔时，补缕低迦外道出家来诣佛所，与世尊面相问讯慰劳已，退坐一面，白佛言：「瞿昙！先日众多种种异道出家沙门、婆罗门集于未曾有讲堂，作如是论议：『沙门瞿昙智慧犹如空舍，不能于大众中建立论议，此应此不应，此合此不合。譬如盲牛，偏行边畔，不入中田；沙门瞿昙亦复如是，无应不应，无合不合。』」

The Buddha told Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, "These non-Buddhist ascetics discuss what should be and what should not be, what is appropriate and what is inappropriate. In the holy Dharma and Vinaya, it is like children's play. It is like a man in his eighties or nineties, with white hair and missing teeth, playing children's games, molding mud into elephants, horses, and various forms. People all say, 'This old child!' In this way, Kāśyapa, various discussions, saying what should be and what should not be, what is appropriate and what is inappropriate, in the holy Dharma and Vinaya, are like children's play. However, among them, there is no bhikṣu who is skillful and appropriate."

佛告补缕低迦：「此诸外道论议，说应不应，合不合，于圣法、律，如小儿戏。譬如士夫，年八、九十，发白齿落，作小儿戏，团治泥土，作象作马，种种形类，众人皆言：『此老小儿。』如是。火种！种种诸论，谓应不应，合不合，于圣法、律，如小儿戏，然于彼中，无有比丘方便所应。」

Pūraṇa Kāśyapa said to the Buddha, "Gautama! Where is there a bhikṣu who is skillful and appropriate?"

补缕低迦白佛：「瞿昙！于何处有比丘方便所应？」

The Buddha told the non-Buddhist ascetics, "To purify the impure is called a bhikṣu's skillful and appropriate means. To tame the untamed is called a bhikṣu's skillful and appropriate means. To make the undetermined attain right acceptance is called a bhikṣu's skillful and appropriate means. To liberate the unliberated is called a bhikṣu's skillful and appropriate means. To cause the unceased to cease, the unknowing to know, the unpracticed to practice, and the unattained to attain, is called a bhikṣu's skillful and appropriate means.

佛告外道：「不清净者令其清净，是名比丘方便所应。不调令调，是名比丘方便所应。诸不定者令得正受，是名比丘方便所应。不解脱者令得解脱，是名比丘方便所应。不断令断、不知令知、不修令修、不得令得，是名比丘方便所应。

How does one purify the impure? It means to purify impure precepts. How does one tame the untamed? It means to tame the untamed eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind faculties. This is called taming the untamed. How does one make the undetermined attain right acceptance? It means to make the mind attain right acceptance. How does one liberate the unliberated? It means to liberate the mind from desire, anger, and delusion. How does one cause the unceased to cease? It means to cause the cessation of ignorance, existence, and craving. How does one cause the unknowing to know? It means to cause the knowledge of name and form. How does one cause the unpracticed to practice? It means to cause the practice of tranquility and insight. How does one cause the unattained to attain? It means to cause the attainment of parinirvāṇa. This is called a bhikṣu's skillful and appropriate means.

「云何不净令净？谓戒不净者，令其清净。云何不调伏令其调伏？谓眼根。耳、鼻、舌、身、意根不调伏，令其调伏，是名不调伏者令其调伏。云何不定令其正受，谓心不正定，令得正受。云何不解脱者令得解脱？谓心不解脱贪欲、恚、痴，令得解脱。云何不断令断？谓无明、有、爱，不断令断。云何不知令知？谓其名色，不知令知。云何不修令修？谓止、观，不修令修。云何不得令得？谓般涅槃，不得令得，是名比丘方便所应。」

Pūrṇa said to the Buddha, "Gautama! This is what a bhikṣu should skillfully and appropriately do, and what a steadfast bhikṣu should skillfully and appropriately do, namely, to exhaust all taints.

补缕低迦白佛言：「瞿昙！是义比丘方便所应，是坚固比丘方便所应，所谓尽诸有漏。」

At that time, the non-Buddhist Pūrṇa, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully approved and rose from his seat to depart.

时，补缕低迦外道出家闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，从坐起而去。
