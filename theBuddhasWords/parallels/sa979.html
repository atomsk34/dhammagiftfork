SĀ 979

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 979（九七九） 须跋陀罗

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying in the Kuśinagara country, in the grove of the twin śāla trees where the strong men were born.

一时，佛住俱夷那竭国力士生处坚固双树林中。

At that time, when the World-Honored One's time of parinirvāṇa had come, he said to Venerable Ānanda, "Ānanda, prepare a rope bed for the World-Honored One between the twin trees, with the head pointing north. The Tathāgata will attain parinirvāṇa without remainder in the middle of the night."

尔时，世尊涅槃时至，告尊者阿难：「汝为世尊于双树间敷绳床，北首，如来今日中夜于无余涅槃而般涅槃。」

At that time, Venerable Ānanda, having received the instruction, prepared a rope bed between the twin trees, with the head pointing north. He then went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, stood to one side, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! I have already prepared a rope bed between the twin trees, with the head pointing north."

尔时，尊者阿难奉教，于双树间敷绳床，北首，讫，来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退住一面，白佛言：「世尊！已于双树间敷绳床，北首。」

At that time, the World-Honored One went to between the twin trees, lay down on the rope bed with his head pointing north, right side down, feet on top of each other, with mindfulness and clear perception, right mindfulness and right knowledge.

尔时，世尊诣双树间，于绳床上北首右脇而卧，足足相累，系念明想，正念正智。

At that time, there was an ascetic named Subhadra in the country of Kuśinagara. He was 120 years old, old and feeble, and was respected and offered by the people of Kuśinagara as if he were an arhat. When Subhadra heard that the World-Honored One would enter parinirvāṇa without remainder in the middle of the night, he had doubts and hoped to resolve them. He thought, "The Śramaṇa Gautama has the power to awaken me. I should now go to the Śramaṇa Gautama and ask him about my doubts." He then left Kuśinagara and went to where the World-Honored One was.

时，俱夷那竭国有须跋陀罗外道出家，百二十岁，年耆根熟，为俱夷那竭国人恭敬供养，如阿罗汉。彼须跋陀罗出家闻世尊今日中夜当于无余涅槃而般涅槃，然我有所疑，希望而住，沙门瞿昙有力，能开觉我，我今当诣沙门瞿昙，问其所疑。即出俱夷那竭，诣世尊所。

At that time, Venerable Ānanda was walking outside the garden gate. Subhadra said to Ānanda, "I heard that the Śramaṇa Gautama will enter parinirvāṇa without remainder in the middle of the night. I have doubts and hope to resolve them. The Śramaṇa Gautama has the power to awaken me. If Ānanda is not too busy, please go and tell Gautama on my behalf. If he has a moment, he can answer my questions."

尔时，尊者阿难于园门外经行。时，须跋陀罗语阿难言：「我闻沙门瞿昙今日中夜于无余涅槃而般涅槃，我有所疑，希望而住，沙门瞿昙有力，能开觉我。若阿难不惮劳者，为我往白瞿昙，少有闲暇，答我所问？」

Ānanda replied, "Do not trouble the World-Honored One! The World-Honored One is tired."

阿难答言：「莫逼世尊！世尊疲极。」

Thus Subhadra asked Venerable Ānanda three times, but Ānanda still refused three times.

如是须跋陀罗再三请尊者阿难，尊者阿难亦再三不许。

Subhadra said, "I have heard the ancient elders say that a Tathāgata, Arhat, Perfectly Enlightened One appears in the world only after a long time, like the udumbara flower. Now the Tathāgata will enter parinirvāṇa in the realm of no remainder in the middle of the night. I have doubts about the Dharma and abide in faith. The Śramaṇa Gautama has the power to awaken me. If Ānanda is not weary, please inform the Śramaṇa Gautama for me."

须跋陀罗言：「我闻古昔出家耆年大师所说，久久乃有如来、应、等正觉出于世间，如优昙钵花。而今如来中夜当于无余涅槃界而般涅槃，我今于法疑，信心而住，沙门瞿昙有力，能开觉我，若阿难不惮劳者，为我白沙门瞿昙。」

Ānanda replied, "Subhadra! Do not trouble the World-Honored One! The World-Honored One is exhausted today."

阿难复答言：「须跋陀罗！莫逼世尊！世尊今日疲极。」

At that time, the World-Honored One heard Ānanda speaking with Subhadra with his divine ear. He told Venerable Ānanda, "Do not stop the non-Buddhist Subhadra from going forth! Let him come in and ask his doubts. Why is that? This is the last time to debate with non-Buddhists who have gone forth. This is the last time to attain the state of a well-come bhikṣu, namely Subhadra."

尔时，世尊以天耳闻阿难与须跋陀罗共语来往，而告尊者阿难：「莫遮外道出家须跋陀罗！令入问其所疑。所以者何？此是最后与外道出家论议，此是最后得证声闻善来比丘，所谓须跋陀罗。」

At that time, Subhadra, with the World-Honored One opening his roots of goodness, joyfully increased and went to where the World-Honored One was. He exchanged greetings and pleasantries with the World-Honored One, then sat to one side and said to the Buddha, "Gautama! The worldly views are that the six teachers, such as Pūraṇa Kāśyapa, each have their own views: 'This is a śramaṇa, this is a śramaṇa.' How is it, Gautama? Are there really such views?"

尔时，须跋陀罗，世尊为开善根，欢喜增上，诣世尊所，与世尊面相问讯慰劳已，退坐一面，白佛言：「瞿昙！凡世间入处，谓富兰那迦叶等六师，各作如是宗：『此是沙门，此是沙门。』云何？瞿昙！为实各各有是宗不？」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse for him, saying:

尔时，世尊即为说偈言：

At the age of twenty-nine, I left home to cultivate the good
path. Since attaining enlightenment, more than fifty years have passed. I am
endowed with samādhi, wisdom, and practice, and always cultivate pure precepts. Apart
from this small part of the path, there are no śramaṇas outside of this.

「始年二十九，　　出家修善道，成道至于今，　　经五十余年，三昧明行具，　　常修于净戒，离斯少道分，　　此外无沙门。」

The Buddha told Subhadra, "In the true Dharma and Vinaya, those who do not attain the eightfold right path cannot attain the first śramaṇa, nor the second, third, or fourth śramaṇa. Subhadra! In this Dharma and Vinaya, those who attain the eightfold right path attain the first śramaṇa, the second, third, and fourth śramaṇa. Apart from this, there are no śramaṇas among non-Buddhists. They are merely teachers of heterodox paths, empty śramaṇas and brāhmaṇas. Therefore, I now roar like a lion in the assembly."

佛告须跋陀罗：「于正法、律不得八正道者，亦不得初沙门，亦不得第二、第三、第四沙门。须跋陀罗！于此法、律得八正道者，得初沙门，得第二、第三、第四沙门，除此已，于外道无沙门，斯则异道之师，空沙门、婆罗门耳。是故，我今于众中作师子吼。」

When this teaching was spoken, the non-Buddhist Subhadra, having left home, was far from defilements and free from obscurations, and attained the purity of the Dharma eye. At that time, Subhadra saw the Dharma, attained the Dharma, knew the Dharma, entered the Dharma, transcended all doubts, did not rely on others' faith, and was not liberated by others. In the true Dharma and Vinaya, he attained fearlessness. He rose from his seat, adjusted his robes, touched his right knee to the ground, and said to Venerable Ānanda, "You have obtained great benefits. You have obtained the Great Master. You are a disciple of the Great Master. You are sprinkled with the Great Master's rain, which is poured on your head. If I can now leave home and receive full ordination in the true Dharma and Vinaya, and attain the status of a bhikṣu, I will also obtain these great benefits!"

说是法时，须跋陀罗外道出家远尘离垢，得法眼净。尔时，须跋陀罗见法、得法、知法、入法，度诸狐疑，不由他信，不由他度，于正法、律得无所畏。从坐起，整衣服，右膝著地，白尊者阿难：「汝得善利，汝得大师，为大师弟子，为大师雨，雨灌其顶。我今若得于正法、律出家、受具足，得比丘分者，亦当得斯善利！」

At that time, Venerable Ānanda said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! This non-Buddhist Subhadra, who has left home, now seeks to leave home and receive full ordination in the true Dharma and Vinaya, and attain the status of a bhikṣu."

时，尊者阿难白佛言：「世尊！是须跋陀罗外道出家今求于正法、律出家、受具足，得比丘分。」

At that time, the World-Honored One said to Subhadra, "This bhikṣu has come to practice the holy life."

尔时，世尊告须跋陀罗：「此比丘来修行梵行。」

Venerable Subhadra then left home and received full ordination, becoming a bhikṣu. He contemplated in this way, and his mind was well liberated, attaining arahantship.

彼尊者须跋陀罗即于尔时出家，即是受具足，成比丘分。如是思惟，乃至心善解脱，得阿罗汉。

At that time, Venerable Subhadra attained arahantship and, knowing the bliss of liberation, thought, "I cannot bear to see the Buddha enter parinirvāṇa. I shall enter parinirvāṇa first." Venerable Subhadra then entered parinirvāṇa first, and then the World-Honored One entered parinirvāṇa.

时，尊者须跋陀罗得阿罗汉，解脱乐觉知已，作是念：「我不忍见佛般涅槃，我当先般涅槃。」时，尊者须跋陀罗先般涅槃已，然后世尊般涅槃。
