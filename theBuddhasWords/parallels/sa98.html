SĀ 98

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 98（九八） 耕田

Parallel to: SN 7.11 / Snp 1.4 (~) / SĀ² 2.264 / SĀ³ 3.1

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time, the Buddha was traveling among the people of Kosala and arrived at a village called Naraka, where he stayed in a forest called Naraka.

一时，佛在拘萨罗人间游行，至一那罗聚落，住一那罗林中。

At that time, the World-Honored One put on his robe, took his alms bowl, and entered a village called Dhara to beg for alms. He thought, "It's still very early today. Let me first pass by the place where the brahmin Kheduttha is preparing food."

尔时，世尊著衣持钵，入一陀罗聚落乞食，而作是念：「今日大早，今且可过耕田婆罗豆婆遮婆罗门作饮食处。」

At that time, the brahmin Kheduttha was plowing the fields with five hundred plows to prepare food. When he saw the World-Honored One from afar, he said, "Gautama! I am now plowing the fields and sowing seeds to prepare food. The śramaṇa Gautama should also plow the fields and sow seeds to prepare food."

尔时，耕田婆罗豆婆遮婆罗门五百具犁耕田，为作饮食。时，耕田婆罗豆婆遮婆罗门遥见世尊，白言：「瞿昙！我今耕田下种，以供饮食，沙门瞿昙亦应耕田下种，以供饮食。」

The Buddha told the brahmin, "I also plow the fields and sow seeds to prepare food."

佛告婆罗门：「我亦耕田下种，以供饮食。」

The brahmin said to the Buddha, "I have never seen the śramaṇa Gautama with a plow, yoke, harness, rope, plowshare, or whip. Yet now Gautama says, 'I also plow the fields and sow seeds to prepare food.'"

婆罗门白佛：「我都不见沙门瞿昙若犁、若轭、若鞅、若縻、若镵、若鞭，而今瞿昙说言：『我亦耕田下种，以供饮食。』」

At that time, the brahmin plowman Varṣākāra spoke a verse, saying:

尔时，耕田婆罗豆婆遮婆罗门即说偈言：

You yourself say you plow the fields, but I do not see you plowing. Please
tell me about plowing the fields, so that I may know the method of plowing.

「自说耕田者，　　而不见其耕，为我说耕田，　　令我知耕法。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse in reply,

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

Faith is the seed, suffering is the timely rain
Wisdom is the yoke, and conscience is the axle
Right mindfulness guards oneself, the good charioteer. Concealing body
and speech, knowing the place of food, truth is
the true vehicle, pleasure in dwelling is laziness, diligence
is neglect, peaceful and swift progress. Going straight without
turning back, reaching the place of no sorrow. Thus
the one who plows the fields attains the fruit
of immortality; thus the one who plows the fields
does not return to experience existence.

「信心为种子，　　苦行为时雨，智慧为时轭，　　惭愧心为辕，正念自守护，　　是则善御者。包藏身口业，　　知食处内藏，真实为真乘，　　乐住为懈息，精进为废荒，　　安隐而速进。直往不转还，　　得到无忧处。如是耕田者，　　逮得甘露果；如是耕田者，　　不还受诸有。」

At that time, the plowman Brāhmaṇa Pāyāsi said to the Buddha, "Good plowman! Gautama! Excellent plowman! Gautama!" Then the plowman Brāhmaṇa Pāyāsi, hearing the World-Honored One's verse, his faith increased even more. He offered a full bowl of fragrant and delicious food to the World-Honored One. The World-Honored One did not accept it, because it was obtained through reciting verses. He then spoke a verse,

时，耕田婆罗豆婆遮婆罗门白佛言：「善耕田！瞿昙！极善耕田！瞿昙！」于是耕田婆罗豆婆遮婆罗门闻世尊说偈，心转增信，以满钵香美饮食以奉世尊。世尊不受，以因说偈得故。即说偈言：

I do not eat food obtained through reciting verses.

「不因说法故，　　受彼食而食。」

Thus it is extensively explained, as was extensively explained before for the Brāhmaṇa Agni and Pāyāsi.

如是广说，如前为火与婆罗门广说。

At that time, the plowman Brāhmaṇa Pāyāsi said to the Buddha, "Gautama! Where should I place this food now?"

时，耕田婆罗豆婆遮婆罗门白佛言：「瞿昙！今以此食安著何处？」

The Buddha told the Brāhmaṇa, "I do not see any gods, Māras, Brahmās, Śramaṇas, Brāhmaṇas, devas, humans, or any other beings in the world who are able to eat this food and be at ease. Brāhmaṇa! You should take this food and place it in water with no living creatures, or on land with little living grass."

佛告婆罗门：「我不见诸天、魔、梵、沙门、婆罗门、天神、世人堪食此食而得安身。婆罗门！汝持此食著无虫水中，及少生草地。」

At that time, the Brāhmaṇa took this food and placed it in water with no living creatures. The water immediately smoked, boiled, and hissed, like a hot ball thrown into cold water, hissing. In the same way, when that food was placed in water with no living creatures, it smoked, boiled, and hissed.

时，婆罗门即持此食著无虫水中，水即烟起涌沸，啾啾作声。如热丸投于冷水，啾啾作声。如是彼食投著无虫水中，烟起涌沸，啾啾作声。

At that time, the Brāhmaṇa thought, "The Śramaṇa Gautama is truly extraordinary! He is greatly virtuous and powerful, causing the food and drink to perform such supernatural feats.

时，婆罗门作是念：「沙门瞿昙实为奇特！大德大力，乃令饮食神变如是。」

At that time, the Brāhmaṇa, seeing the auspicious signs of the food, his faith increased even more. He said to the Buddha, "Gautama! Can I now go forth and receive full ordination in the true Dharma?"

时，彼婆罗门见食瑞应，信心转增，白佛言：「瞿昙！我今可得于正法中出家、受具足不？」

The Buddha told the Brāhmaṇa, "You can now go forth and receive full ordination in the true Dharma, and attain the status of a bhikṣu." Having gone forth, he contemplated in solitude, "The reason why a noble son shaves his beard and hair, puts on the dyed robe, with right faith leaves home to practice the path..." and attained arahantship, with his mind well liberated.

佛告婆罗门：「汝今可得于正法中出家、受具足，得比丘分。」彼即出家已，独静思惟：「所以族姓子剃除须发，著袈裟衣，正信非家，出家学道……。」乃至得阿罗汉，心善解脱。
