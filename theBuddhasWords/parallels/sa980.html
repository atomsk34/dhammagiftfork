SĀ 980

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 980（九八〇） 毘舍利贾客

Parallel to: AN 8.34 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was traveling among the people of the Vṛji country and arrived at the city of Vaiśālī, where he stayed at the pavilion by the Monkey Pond. At that time, there were many merchants in the city of Vaiśālī who were preparing to travel to the country of Takṣaśilā. Having heard that the World-Honored One was traveling among the people of the Vṛji country and had arrived at the city of Vaiśālī, where he was staying at the pavilion by the Monkey Pond, they came to where the Buddha was, bowed their heads at his feet, and sat to one side. The Buddha taught the Dharma to the merchants in various ways, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting them. Having shown, taught, illuminated, and delighted them, he remained silent.

一时，佛在跋耆人间游行，至毘舍离国，住猕猴池侧重阁讲堂。时，毘舍离国有众多贾客，欲向怛刹尸罗国方便庄严。是众多贾客闻世尊于跋耆人间游行，至毘舍离国，住猕猴池侧重阁讲堂。闻已，来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面。佛为诸贾客种种说法，示、教、照、喜；示、教、照、喜已，默然住。

At that time, the merchants rose from their seats, adjusted their robes, bowed to the Buddha, joined their palms, and said to the Buddha, "World-Honored One! We merchants are preparing to travel to the country of Takṣaśilā. We only wish that the World-Honored One and the great assembly would accept our offerings tomorrow."

时，诸贾客从坐起，整衣服，为佛作礼，合掌白佛言：「世尊！我等诸贾客方便庄严，欲至怛刹尸罗国，唯愿世尊与诸大众明旦受我供养。」

At that time, the World-Honored One silently accepted.

尔时，世尊默然而许。

At that time, the merchants, knowing that the World-Honored One had accepted the invitation, rose from their seats, bowed at the Buddha's feet, and returned to their own homes. They prepared various pure and delicious foods, laid out seats, and placed clean water. In the morning, they sent a messenger to inform the Buddha, saying, "The time has come."

时，诸贾客知世尊受请已，从坐起，礼佛足，各还自家，办种种净美饮食，敷床座，安置净水。晨朝遣使，白佛：「时到。」

At that time, the World-Honored One, together with the great assembly, put on his robes and took his alms bowl. He went to where the merchants were and took his seat.

尔时，世尊与诸大众著衣持钵。诣诸贾客所，就座而坐。

At that time, the merchants personally served pure and delicious food. After the meal and washing the bowls, they took a low seat and sat in front of the great assembly to listen to the Buddha's teaching.

时，诸贾客以净美饮食自手供养。食毕洗钵讫，取卑小床，于大众前坐，听佛说法。

At that time, the World-Honored One told the merchants, "When you are traveling in the wilderness, there will be many terrifying things that make your hair stand on end. At that time, you should think of the Tathāgata, the Tathāgata, the Arhat, the Perfectly Enlightened One, up to the Buddha, the World-Honored One. If you think like this, your fear will be removed. Also, think of the Dharma, the Buddha's true Dharma and Vinaya, which can be realized in this life, not waiting for a time, and can be realized through personal experience. Also, think of the Saṃgha, the disciples of the World-Honored One who are well-directed and rightly directed, up to the field of merit in the world. If you think like this, your fear will be removed.

尔时，世尊告诸贾客：「汝等当行于旷野中，有诸恐怖，心惊毛竖。尔时，当念如来事，谓如来、应、等正觉，乃至佛世尊。如是念者，恐怖则除。又，念法事，佛正法、律，现法能离炽然，不待时节，通达亲近，缘自觉知。又，念僧事，世尊弟子善向、正向，乃至世间福田。如是念者，恐怖即除。

In the past, the gods and asuras fought together. At that time, Śakra, Lord of the Gods, told the gods, 'When you fight with the asuras, if you are afraid, you should think of my banner called the Banner of Subjugation. When you think of that banner, your fear will be removed. If you do not think of my banner, you should think of the banner of the god Vaiśravaṇa; if you do not think of the banner of the god Vaiśravaṇa, you should think of the banner of the god Dhṛtarāṣṭra. When you think of those banners, your fear will be removed.' Thus, merchants! When you are in the wilderness and are afraid, you should think of the Tathāgata, the Dharma, and the Saṃgha.

「过去世时，天、阿须轮共鬪。时，天帝释告诸天众：『汝等与阿须轮共鬪战之时，生恐怖者，当念我幢，名摧伏幢。念彼幢时，恐怖得除。若不念我幢者，当念伊舍那天子幢；若不念伊舍那天子幢者，当念婆留那天子幢。念彼幢时，恐怖即除。』如是，诸商人！汝等于旷野中，有恐怖者，当念如来事、法事、僧事。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse of offering and rejoicing for the merchants of Vaiśālī:

尔时，世尊为诸毘舍离贾客说供养随喜偈：

Offering to the bhikṣu saṃgha, with food and drink according to the
season, single-mindedly contemplating and reflecting, with right knowledge and practicing equanimity. Pure
objects are good fields of merit, which you all possess in full
Relying on these merits and benefits, you will attain peace and happiness
for a long time. If you give rise to the aspiration to
seek various benefits, they will all be accomplished. Two-legged and four-legged beings
will be at peace, and the roads for coming and going will
be peaceful. Peaceful at night and peaceful during the day, free from
all evil, like a fertile and good field, with pure and excellent
seeds. Irrigating them with timely moisture, the harvest is immeasurable. Pure precepts
are good fields of merit, with fine and delicious food as seeds
Practicing correctly in accordance with them, the ultimate wonderful fruits will be
accomplished. Therefore, those who practice giving, wishing to attain all virtues, should
follow the practice of wisdom, and various fruits will naturally be accomplished
To the one who is accomplished in wisdom and practice, with a
correct mind, pay utmost respect. Cultivating various roots of goodness, one will
ultimately attain great benefits and blessings. Truly knowing the world, one will
attain the complete fulfillment of right views. Fully endowed with the right
path, one will progress fully, far removed from all defilements, attaining the
path of nirvāṇa, ultimately reaching the end of suffering. This is called
being fully endowed with all virtues.

「供养比丘僧，　　饮食随时服，专念谛思惟，　　正知而行舍。净物良福田，　　汝等悉具足，缘斯功德利，　　长夜获安乐。发心有所求，　　众利悉皆应，两足四足安，　　道路往来安。夜安昼亦安，　　一切离诸恶，如沃壤良田，　　精纯好种子。溉灌以时泽，　　收实不可量，净戒良福田，　　精肴饍种子。正行以将顺，　　终期妙果成，是故行施者，　　欲求备众德。当随智慧行，　　众果自然备，于明行足尊，　　正心尽恭敬。种殖众善本，　　终获大福利，如实知世间，　　得具备正见。具足见正道，　　具足而升进，远离一切垢，　　逮得涅槃道，究竟于苦边，　　是名备众德。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke various teachings to the merchants of Vaiśālī, showing, teaching, illuminating, and delighting them. Having arisen from his seat, he departed.

尔时，世尊为诸毘舍离贾客说种种法，示、教、照、喜已，从坐起去。
