SĀ 990

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 990（九九〇） 鹿住

Parallel to: AN 6.44 (~) / AN 10.75 / SĀ 991 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, Venerable Ānanda, having put on his robe and taken his alms bowl in the morning, went to Śrāvastī to beg for alms, and arrived at the house of the laywoman Deer-dweller.

尔时，尊者阿难晨朝著衣持钵，诣舍卫城，次第乞食，至鹿住优婆夷舍。

The laywoman Deer-dweller saw Venerable Ānanda from afar, quickly prepared a seat, and said, "Venerable Ānanda, please sit down."

鹿住优婆夷遥见尊者阿难，疾敷床座，白言：「尊者阿难令坐。」

At that time, the laywoman Deer-dweller bowed her head at Ānanda's feet, stepped back to one side, and said to Venerable Ānanda, "How can it be said that the World-Honored One knows the Dharma? My father, Pūraṇa, practiced pure conduct, free from desires, not attached to fragrances and flowers, far from worldly vulgarity; my uncle, Rṣabha, did not practice pure conduct, but he was content. Both of them have passed away, and now the World-Honored One has declared that the two of them will be reborn in the same realm, have the same rebirth, and in the next life attain the state of once-returner, being born in Tuṣita Heaven, coming to this world once more, and ultimately ending suffering. How is it, Ānanda? Is it the same for those who practice pure conduct and those who do not, to be reborn in the same realm, have the same rebirth, and have the same next life?"

时，鹿住优婆夷稽首礼阿难足，退住一面，白尊者阿难：「云何言世尊知法？我父富兰那先修梵行，离欲清净，不著香花，远诸凡鄙；叔父梨师达多不修梵行，然其知足。二俱命终，而今世尊俱记二人同生一趣，同一受生，同于后世得斯陀含，生兜率天，一来世间，究竟苦边。云何？阿难！修梵行、不修梵行，同生一趣、同一受生、同其后世？」

Ānanda replied, "Sister! Wait a moment. You cannot know the differences in the faculties of beings in the world. The Tathāgata knows the superiority and inferiority of the faculties of beings in the world." Having said this, he rose from his seat and left.

阿难答言：「姊妹！汝今且停。汝不能知众生世间根之差别，如来悉知众生世间根之优劣。」如是说已，从坐起去。

At that time, Venerable Ānanda returned to the monastery, took up his robe and bowl, washed his feet, went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. He told the World-Honored One in detail what the laywoman Deer-dweller had said.

时，尊者阿难还精舍，举衣钵，洗足已，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面。以鹿住优婆夷所说广白世尊。

The Buddha told Ānanda, "How can that laywoman Deer-dweller know the superiority and inferiority of the faculties of beings in the world? Ānanda, the Tathāgata knows the superiority and inferiority of the faculties of beings in the world. Ānanda, there may be one who violates the precepts, but they do not truly know the liberation of the mind and the liberation through wisdom. The violation of the precepts they have committed will not be completely extinguished, completely submerged, and completely desireless. There may be one who violates the precepts, but they truly know the liberation of the mind and the liberation through wisdom. The violation of the precepts they have committed will be completely extinguished, completely submerged, and completely desireless. Those who measure say, 'This also has such a teaching, and that also has that teaching. They should be born together in the same realm, have the same rebirth, and have the same future life.' Those who measure in this way will not benefit for a long time and will suffer.

佛告阿难：「彼鹿住优婆夷云何能知众生世间根之优劣？阿难！如来悉知众生世间根之优劣。阿难！或有一犯戒，彼于心解脱、慧解脱不如实知，彼所起犯戒无余灭、无余没、无余欲尽。或有一犯戒，于心解脱、慧解脱如实知，彼所起犯戒无余灭、无余没、无余欲尽。于彼筹量者言：『此亦有如是法，彼亦有是法，此则应俱同生一趣、同一受生、同一后世。』彼如是筹量者，得长夜非义饶益苦。

Ānanda, those who violate the precepts do not truly know the liberation of the mind and the liberation through wisdom. The violation of the precepts they have committed will not be completely extinguished, completely submerged, and completely desireless. Know that such a person is retreating, not advancing. I say that such a person is in the category of retreat. Ānanda, there are those who violate the precepts, but they truly know the liberation of the mind and the liberation through wisdom. The violation of the precepts they have committed will be completely extinguished, completely submerged, and completely desireless. Know that such a person is advancing, not retreating. I say that such a person is in the category of advancement. Apart from the Tathāgata, who can fully know these two with their intervals? Therefore, Ānanda, do not measure and take people. Measuring people leads to illness. Measuring people brings about one's own suffering. Only the Tathāgata can know people. The same is true for the two who violate the precepts and the two who uphold the precepts. They do not truly know the liberation of the mind and the liberation through wisdom. The upholding of the precepts they have committed will not be completely extinguished. If they are agitated, they do not truly know the liberation of the mind and the liberation through wisdom. The agitation they have will not be completely extinguished. If they are angry and resentful, they do not truly know the liberation of the mind and the liberation through wisdom. The anger and resentment they have will not be completely extinguished. If they are afflicted by craving, they truly know the liberation of the mind and the liberation through wisdom. The affliction by craving they have will be completely extinguished." The defiled and pure are explained as above, up to "the Tathāgata can know people.

「阿难！彼犯戒者，于心解脱、慧解脱不如实知，彼所起犯戒无余灭、无余没、无余欲尽。当知此人是退，非胜进，我说彼人为退分。阿难！有犯戒，彼于心解脱、慧解脱如实知，彼于所起犯戒无余灭、无余没、无余欲尽。当知是人胜进不退，我说彼人为胜进分。自非如来，此二有间，谁能悉知？是故，阿难！莫筹量人人而取，人善筹量人人而病。人筹量人人，自招其患。唯有如来能知人耳，如二犯戒，二持戒亦如是。彼于心解脱、慧解脱不如实知，彼所起持戒无余灭。若掉动者，彼于心解脱、慧解脱不如实知，彼所起掉无余灭。彼若瞋恨者，彼于心解脱、慧解脱不如实知，彼所起瞋恨无余灭。若苦贪者，彼于心解脱、慧解脱如实知，彼所起苦贪无余灭……」秽污清净如上说，乃至「如来能知人人。

"Ānanda, the laywoman Deer-dweller is foolish and has little wisdom, yet she gives rise to doubt in her mind when the Tathāgata speaks the Dharma in one way. How could it be, Ānanda, that what the Tathāgata says is twofold?"

「阿难！鹿住优婆夷愚痴少智，而于如来一向说法心生狐疑。云何？阿难！如来所说，岂有二耶？」

Ānanda said to the Buddha, “No, World-Honored One!”

阿难白佛：「不也，世尊！」

The Buddha told Ānanda, "Excellent! Excellent! It is impossible for the Tathāgata to speak the Dharma in two ways. Ānanda, if Pūraṇa holds precepts and Rṣidatta also holds the same precepts, Pūraṇa cannot know where they will be reborn. Where will Rṣidatta be reborn? How will he be reborn? What will his future be like? If Rṣidatta has the wisdom he has accomplished, Pūraṇa also has this wisdom, then Rṣidatta also cannot know where Pūraṇa will be reborn. How will he be reborn? What will his future be like? Ānanda, Pūraṇa is superior in precepts, and Rṣidatta is superior in wisdom. When they both die, I say that the two will be reborn in the same realm, have the same rebirth, and their future will also be the same. They are both once-returners, born in Tuṣita Heaven, and will ultimately end suffering in one more lifetime here. The two have an interval, and apart from the Tathāgata, who can know this? Therefore, Ānanda, do not measure people. Those who measure people will only diminish themselves. Only the Tathāgata can know people."

佛告阿难：「善哉！善哉！如来说法若有二者，无有是处。阿难！若富兰那持戒，梨师达多亦同持戒者，所生之趣，富兰那所不能知。梨师达多为生何趣？云何受生？云何后世？若梨师达多所成就智，富兰那亦成就此智者，梨师达多亦不能知彼富兰那当生何趣？云何受生？后世云何？阿难！彼富兰那持戒胜，梨师达多智慧胜；彼俱命终，我说二人同生一趣，同一受生，后世亦同是斯陀含，生兜率天，一来生此究竟苦边。彼二有间，自非如来，谁能得知？是故，阿难！莫量人人，量人人者，自生损减。唯有如来能知人耳。」

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha said to Venerable Ānanda, "Excellent! Excellent!" Ānanda, having heard the Buddha's teaching, rejoiced and upheld it.

佛说此经已，尊者阿难闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
