SĀ 991

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 991（九九一） 鹿住

Parallel to: AN 6.44 / AN 10.75 (~) / SĀ 990 (~)

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

Once, the Buddha was staying in the city of Śākya, in the summer retreat, while other monks were staying in the Jeta Grove Anāthapiṇḍada-ārāma in Śrāvastī. In the morning, the monks put on their robes, took their bowls, and entered Śrāvastī to beg for food. They gradually came to the house of the laywoman Luzhu. The laywoman Luzhu saw the monks coming from afar, quickly prepared a seat, and invited the monks to sit down, as described in the previous sūtra of Ānanda.

一时，佛住释氏弥城留利邑夏安居，有余比丘于舍卫国祇树给孤独园夏安居。时，彼比丘于晨朝著衣持钵，入舍卫城乞食，次第至鹿住优婆夷舍，鹿住优婆夷遥见比丘来，疾敷床座，请比丘令坐，如上阿难修多罗说。

At that time, the monk said to the laywoman Luzhu, "Sister, wait! How do you know the superiority and inferiority of sentient beings' faculties? Sister, only the Tathāgata can know the superiority and inferiority of sentient beings' faculties." Having said this, he got up from his seat and left.

时，彼比丘语鹿住优婆夷：「姊妹且停！汝那得知众生根之优劣。姊妹！唯有如来能知众生根之优劣。」如是说已，从坐起去。

After the monk had completed the three-month summer retreat and made his robes, he took his robes and bowl and went to the city of Śākya in the country of Liuli. Having arrived, he put away his robes and bowl, washed his feet, went to where the Buddha was, bowed at the Buddha's feet, and sat to one side. He told the Buddha in detail about the discussion he had with the laywoman Luzhu.

时，彼比丘三月夏安居讫，作衣竟，持衣钵，往诣弥城留利释氏邑。到已，举衣钵，洗足已，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面。以共鹿住优婆夷所论说事向佛广说。

The Buddha told the monk, "How could the laywoman Luzhu know the superiority and inferiority of the faculties of sentient beings in the world? Only the Tathāgata can know the superiority and inferiority of the faculties of sentient beings in the world. If one is not free from anger, hatred, pride, and sometimes gives rise to greed, does not listen to the Dharma, does not study much learning, is not subdued in views, and cannot sometimes give rise to the Dharma of the mind of liberation, then, monk, if there is another who is not free from anger and pride, sometimes gives rise to greed, but hears the Dharma, cultivates much learning, is subdued in good views, and can sometimes give rise to the Dharma of the mind of liberation, if one contemplates that this one has this Dharma and that one has that Dharma, then they have the same destination, the same rebirth, and the same future life; contemplating in this way, one will only gain non-benefit and suffering. Monk, if that person is not free from anger and pride, sometimes gives rise to greed, does not listen to the Dharma, does not practice much learning, is not subdued in views, and does not sometimes attain the Dharma of the mind of liberation, I say that this person is base and inferior. Monk, if that person is not free from anger and pride, sometimes gives rise to greed, but hears the Dharma, delights in much learning, is subdued in views, and can sometimes attain the Dharma of the mind of liberation, I say that this person is the best and most wonderful. There is a difference between these two, and who but the Tathāgata can know it? Therefore, monk, do not measure people, for even the Tathāgata can know their superiority and inferiority.

佛告比丘：「鹿住优婆夷云何能知世间众生诸根优劣？唯有如来能知世间众生诸根优劣耳。不离瞋恨、憍慢，时起贪法，不听受法，不学多闻，于法不调伏见，不能时时起解脱心法。比丘！若复有一不离瞋慢，时起贪法，然彼闻法，修学多闻，于善调伏见，时时能起解脱心法。若思量彼，此有是法，彼有是法，此则同一趣、同一受生、同一后世；如是思量者，长得非义不饶益苦。比丘！若复彼人不离瞋慢，时时起贪法，亦不听法，不习多闻，不调伏见，亦不时时得解脱心法，我说此人卑鄙下贱。比丘！若复彼人不离瞋慢，时时起贪法，然彼闻法，乐多闻，调伏诸见，时时能得解脱心法，我说是人第一胜妙。彼二有间，自非如来，谁能别知？是故，比丘！莫量人人，乃至如来能知优劣。

"Monk, furthermore, there is one who is not free from anger and pride, sometimes gives rise to verbal evil conduct..." The rest is as above. "Monk, furthermore, there is one who is virtuous, peaceful, and agreeable to live with, delights in clear wisdom, and delights in living with those who cultivate the holy life, but does not delight in hearing the Dharma, and does not sometimes attain the Dharma of mind liberation. One should know that such a person abides in the ground of virtue and cannot progress further. The ground of virtue refers to the realms of humans and gods. Furthermore, there is one who is virtuous by nature, peaceful and agreeable to live with, delights in the holy life, and takes them as companions, delights in hearing the true Dharma, learns much, is well subdued in views, and sometimes attains the Dharma of mind liberation. One should know that such a person can progress further on the ground of virtue, and one should know that such a person is capable in the stream of the true Dharma. There is a difference between these two, and who but the Tathāgata can know it? Therefore, monk, do not measure people, for measuring people only brings harm to oneself. Only the Tathāgata can know people. Monk, the laywoman Luzhu is foolish and has little wisdom..." The sūtra explains in detail.

「比丘！复次，有一不离瞋慢，时时起口恶行……」余如上说。「比丘！复次，有一贤善，安乐同止，欣乐明智，修梵行者乐与同止，而彼不乐闻法，乃至不时时得心法解脱，当知彼人住贤善地，不能转进。贤善地者，谓人、天趣。复次，有一其性贤善，同止安乐，欣乐梵行，以为伴侣，乐闻正法，学习多闻，善调伏见，时时能得解脱心法，当知彼人于贤善地能转胜进，当知此人于正法流有所堪能。此二有间，自非如来，谁能别知？是故，比丘！莫量人人，量人人者，自招其患。唯有如来能知人耳。比丘！鹿住优婆夷愚痴少智……」如上修多罗广说。

Having spoken this sūtra, the Buddha said to the monks, "Good, good! The monks listened to the Buddha's teachings with joy and practiced accordingly.

佛说此经已，诸比丘闻佛所说，欢喜奉行。
