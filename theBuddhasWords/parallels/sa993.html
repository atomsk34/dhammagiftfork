SĀ 993

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 993（九九三） 赞大声闻

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time, there were elder bhikṣus who followed the Buddha, relying on him for support. They were Venerable Ājñātakauṇḍinya, Venerable Mahākāśyapa, Venerable Śāriputra, Venerable Mahāmaudgalyāyana, Venerable Aniruddha, Venerable Bhadrika, Venerable Revata, Venerable Nanda, Venerable Upananda, Venerable Rāhula, Venerable Anuruddha, Venerable Kimbila, Venerable Bhagu, Venerable Kapphiṇa, Venerable Kālodāyin, Venerable Mahākapphiṇa, Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila, Venerable Mahācunda, Venerable Mahākaśyapa, Venerable Mahākapphiṇa, Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila, Venerable Mahācunda, Venerable Mahākaśyapa, Venerable Mahākapphiṇa, Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila, Venerable Mahācunda, Venerable Mahākaśyapa, Venerable Mahākapphiṇa, Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila, Venerable Mahācunda, Venerable Mahākaśyapa, Venerable Mahākapphiṇa, Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila, Venerable Mahācunda, Venerable Mahākaśyapa, Venerable Mahākapphiṇa, Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila, Venerable Mahācunda, Venerable Mahākaśyapa, Venerable Mahākapphiṇa, Venerable Mahākauṣṭhila, Venerable Mahācunda, Venerable Mahākaśyapa, Venerable Mahākapphiṇa, Venerable Mahā

时，有诸上座比丘随佛左右，依止而住。所谓尊者阿若憍陈如、尊者摩诃迦叶、尊者舍利弗、尊者摩诃目揵连、尊者阿那律陀、尊者二十亿耳、尊者陀罗骠摩罗子、尊者婆那迦婆娑、尊者耶舍舍罗迦毘诃利、尊者富留那、尊者分陀檀尼迦。如此及余上座比丘随佛左右，依止而住。

At that time, Venerable Vāgīśa was staying at the Deer Park Lecture Hall in the Eastern Garden in Śrāvastī. Venerable Vāgīśa thought, "Today the World-Honored One is at Anāthapiṇḍada's Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī, with the elder bhikṣus following him, relying on him for support. I should now go to where the World-Honored One is and each speak a verse in praise of the elder bhikṣus."

时，尊者婆耆舍住舍卫国东园鹿子母讲堂。时，尊者婆耆舍作是念：「今日世尊在舍卫国祇树给孤独园，诸上座比丘随佛左右，依止而住，我今当往至世尊所，各各说偈叹诸上座比丘。」

Having had this thought, he went to where the Buddha was, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and withdrew to one side, speaking a verse, saying:

作是念已，往诣佛所，稽首佛足，退住一面，而说偈言：

The elder bhikṣus have cut off all desires, Transcended all
worldly accumulations. Deep in wisdom, few in words, Courageous and
diligent in skillful means, Their moral virtue is pure and
evident. I now bow my head in reverence. Having subdued
all māras and enemies, Far removed from the masses, Not
bound by the five desires, Always practicing in solitude, Pure
and few in desires, I now bow my head in
reverence. The Śākyan clan, excelling in meditation, Not negligent, The
mind delights in samādhi, Pure, free from defilements, Eloquent in
explaining the profound meaning. Therefore I bow my head in
reverence. The supernormal powers and wisdom attained Surpass all supernormal
powers. Among the assembly of the six supernormal powers, He
is self-mastery, fearless, The supreme of all supernormal powers. Therefore
I bow my head in reverence. In the great trichiliocosm
The births and deaths of the five paths, Up to
the Brahma world, The superior and inferior thoughts of humans
and gods, The pure divine eye sees all. Therefore I
bow my head in reverence. With diligent skillful means, Cutting
off the accumulation of all desires, Breaking the net of
birth and death, The mind always delights in the true
Dharma, Free from all hopes and desires, Transcending to the
other shore, Pure, without defilements. Therefore I bow my head
in reverence. Forever free from all fears, Without reliance, free
from possessions, Content, transcending doubts, Subduing all māra enemies, The
body mindfulness contemplation is pure. Therefore I bow my head
in reverence. There are no worldly afflictions, The thicket of
thorns is gone. The bonds and fetters are forever removed
The causes and conditions of the three realms are cut
off. Purifying and extinguishing all defilements, Ultimately manifesting clearly, Leaving
the forest and the non-forest. Therefore I bow my head
in reverence. Without a dwelling place to rely on, The
illusions, delusions, and anger are extinguished. Subduing all desires and
joys, Emerging from all views, Pure, without flaws. Therefore I
bow my head in reverence. The mind is self-mastery, firm
and unmoving. The great virtue of wisdom and power Is
difficult to subdue, yet he can subdue. Cutting off the
bonds of ignorance. Therefore I bow my head in reverence
The great being is free from darkness, The tranquil and
extinguished Muni. The true Dharma is free from defilements, The
light shines forth on its own, Illuminating all worlds. Therefore
he is called the Buddha. The earth deities, the devas
in space, The sons of the thirty-three heavens, Their light
is all outshone. Therefore he is called the Buddha. Transcending
the limits of birth and death, Surpassing the masses, Gentle
and well-tamed, The perfect enlightenment of the foremost enlightenment. Cutting
off all bonds and fetters,

「上上座比丘，　　已断诸贪欲，超过诸世间，　　一切之积聚，深智少言说，　　勇猛勤方便，道德净明显，　　我今稽首礼。已伏诸魔怨，　　远离于群聚，不为五欲缚，　　常习于空闲，清虚而寡欲，　　我今稽首礼。遮罗延胜族，　　禅思不放逸，内心乐正受，　　清净离尘秽，辩慧显深义，　　是故稽首礼。所得神通慧，　　超诸神通力，六神通众中，　　自在无所畏，神通最胜故，　　是故稽首礼。于大千世界，　　五道诸趣生，乃至于梵世，　　人天优劣想，净天眼悉见，　　是故稽首礼。精勤方便力，　　断除诸爱集，坏裂生死网，　　心常乐正法，离诸悕望想，　　超度于彼岸，清净无尘秽，　　是故稽首礼。永离诸恐畏，　　无依离财物，知足度疑惑，　　伏诸魔怨敌，身念观清净，　　是故稽首礼。无有诸世间，　　烦恼棘刺林，结缚使永除，　　三有因缘断，精练灭诸垢，　　究竟明显现，于林离林去，　　是故稽首礼。无舍宅所依，　　幻伪痴恚灭，调伏诸爱喜，　　出一切见处，清净无瑕秽，　　是故稽首礼。其心自在转，　　坚固不倾动，智慧大德力，　　难伏魔能伏，断除无明结，　　是故稽首礼。大人离暗冥，　　寂灭牟尼尊。正法离垢过，　　光明自显照，照一切世界，　　是故名为佛。地神虚空天，　　三十三天子，光明悉映障，　　是故名为佛。度生死有边，　　超逾越群众，柔弱善调伏，　　正觉第一觉。断一切结缚，　　伏一切异道，降一切魔怨，　　得无上正觉。离尘灭诸垢，　　是故稽首礼。」

When the Venerable Vāgīśa praised in verse, the bhikṣus who heard his words were all greatly delighted!

尊者婆耆舍偈赞叹时，诸比丘闻其所说，皆大欢喜！
