SĀ 999

Saṃyuktāgama杂阿含经

SA 999（九九九） 生欢喜

Parallel to: SN 2.23 / SĀ² 2.136

Thus have I heard:

如是我闻：

At one time the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in the Jeta Grove near Śrāvastī.

一时，佛住舍卫国祇树给孤独园。

At that time there was a god named Śikhī, of exceptional beauty, who came to where the Buddha was in the latter part of the night, bowed his head at the Buddha’s feet, and sat to one side. The light from his body illuminated the Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park.

时，有天子名悉鞞梨，容色绝妙，于后夜时来诣佛所，稽首佛足，退坐一面。身诸光明遍照祇树给孤独园。

At that time the god spoke a verse:

时，彼天子而说偈言：

All gods and worldly people delight in food. Is there
any world where blessings and happiness follow one by themselves?

「诸天及世人，　　于食悉欣乐，颇有诸世间，　　福乐自随逐。」

At that time, the World-Honored One spoke a verse in reply:

尔时，世尊说偈答言：

Pure faith and generous giving, in this world and the next
wherever one goes, the rewards of blessings always follow like a
shadow. Therefore, one should abandon stinginess and practice pure giving. Having
given, the mind is joyful, and one receives in this world
and the next.

「净信心惠施，　　此世及后世，随其所至处，　　福报常影随。是故当舍悭，　　行无垢惠施，施已心欢喜，　　此世他世受。」

At that time, the Śiviri-heavenly son addressed the Buddha, saying: "How wonderful! World-Honored One! You have well spoken this meaning:

时，彼悉鞞梨天子白佛言：「奇哉！世尊！善说斯义：

Pure faith and generous giving, in this world and the next
wherever one goes, the rewards of blessings always follow like a
shadow. Therefore, one should abandon stinginess and practice pure giving. Having
given, the mind is joyful, and one receives in this world
and the next.

「净信心惠施，　　此世及他世，随其所至处，　　福报常影随。是故当舍悭，　　行无垢惠施，施已心欢喜，　　此世他世受。」

The Śiviri-heavenly son addressed the Buddha, saying: "World-Honored One! I myself know that in a past life, I was once a king named Śiviri. I made offerings at the four city gates for the sake of merit, and in the city center, at the four intersections, I also made offerings and created merit.

悉鞞梨天子白佛言：「世尊！我自知过去世时曾为国王，名悉鞞梨，于四城门普施为福，于其城内有四交道，亦于其中布施作福。

At that time, the chief queen came to me and said: 'Great King, you are making great merit, but I am powerless to cultivate merit.' I then told her: 'The offerings and merit made outside the eastern gate of the city all belong to you.' The princes then came to me and said: 'The Great King is making much merit, and the queen is also doing the same, but we are powerless to make merit. We now wish to rely on the Great King to make a little merit.' I then replied: 'The offerings and merit made outside the southern gate of the city all belong to you.' The ministers then came to me and said: 'Today the Great King is making much merit, and the queen and princes are also doing the same, but we are powerless to make merit. We wish to rely on the Great King to do a little.' I then told them: 'The offerings and merit made outside the western gate of the city all belong to you.' The generals then came to me and said: 'Today the Great King is making much merit, and the queen, princes, ministers, and generals are all doing the same, but we are powerless to cultivate merit. We wish to rely on the Great King to do a little.' I then replied: 'The offerings and merit made outside the northern gate of the city all belong to you.' The common people of the country then came to me and said: 'Today the Great King is making much merit, and the queen, princes, ministers, generals, and commoners are all doing the same, but we are powerless to cultivate merit. We wish to rely on the Great King to do a little.' I then replied: 'The offerings and merit made at the four intersections in the city all belong to you.' At that time, the king, queen, princes, ministers, generals, and commoners all made offerings and cultivated merit. The offerings and merit I had previously made were then cut off.

「时，有第一夫人来语我言：『大王大作福德，而我无力修诸福业。』我时告言：『城东门外布施作福悉皆属汝。』时，诸王子复来白我：『大王多作功德，夫人亦同，而我无力作诸福业，我今愿得依于大王少作功德。』我时答言：『城南门外所作施福悉皆属汝。』时，有大臣复来白我：『今日大王多作功德，夫人、王子悉皆共之，而我无力作诸福业，愿依大王少有所作。』我时告言：『城西门外所作施福悉皆属汝。』时，诸将士复来白我：『今日大王多作功德，夫人、太子及诸大臣悉皆共之，唯我无力能修福业，愿依大王得有所作。』我时答言：『城北门外所作施福悉皆属汝。』国中庶民复来白我：『今日大王多作功德，夫人、王子、大臣、诸将悉皆共之，唯我无力不能修福，愿依大王少有所作。』我时答言：『于其城内四交道头所作施福悉属汝等。』尔时，国王夫人、王子、大臣、将士、庶民悉皆惠施，作诸功德，我先所作惠施功德于兹则断。

At that time, the people I had sent to make merit returned to me, bowed to me, and said: 'Great King, you should know that the places where merit is cultivated, the queen, princes, ministers, generals, and commoners each occupy their places, making offerings and cultivating merit. The offerings and merit you made are now cut off.' I then replied: 'Good men! The annual tribute of goods from the border countries that should enter me, divide it in half to enter the treasury, and divide the other half to make offerings and cultivate merit there.' Upon hearing my instructions, they went to the border countries, collected the goods, sent half to the treasury, and left the other half there to make offerings and cultivate merit.

「时，我所使诸作福者，还至我所，为我作礼而白我言：『大王当知，诸修福处，夫人、王子、大臣、将士及诸庶民各据其处，行施作福，大王所施于兹则断。』我时答言：『善男子！诸方边国岁输财物应入我者，分半入库，分其半分，即于彼处惠施作福。』彼闻教旨，往诣边国，集诸财物，半送于库，半留于彼惠施作福。

"I have long given such offerings and cultivated merit, and have long obtained lovable, memorable, and agreeable karmic rewards, always enjoying happiness without end. All these meritorious deeds, karmic fruits, and karmic rewards are included in the great accumulation of merit. It is like the five great rivers flowing into one stream, namely the Ganges, Yāpunā, Sarayū, Irrawaddy, and Mahī. These five rivers flowing into one stream cannot be measured in hundreds of thousands of myriads of dou and hu. The great river water becomes a great accumulation of water. I am also like this. The meritorious deeds, karmic fruits, and karmic rewards cannot be measured, and are all included in the great accumulation of merit."

「我先长夜如是惠施作福，长夜常得可爱、可念、可意福报，常受快乐，无有穷极。以斯福业及福果福报，悉皆入于大功德聚数。譬如五大河合为一流，所谓恒河、耶蒲那、萨罗由、伊罗跋提、摩醯。如是五河合为一流，无有人能量其河水百千万亿斗斛之数。彼大河水得为大水聚数，我亦如是，所作功德果、功德报不可称量，悉得入于大功德聚数。」

At that time, the Śivika Heaven's son, having heard the Buddha's teaching, joyfully rejoiced, bowed his head at the Buddha's feet, and then disappeared.

尔时，悉鞞梨天子闻佛所说，欢喜随喜，稽首佛足，即没不现。
