T 210.18

Dharmapada (T 210) 法句经

T 210.18 刀杖品法句经第十八十有四章

Parallel to: SN 1.18 / AN 5.174 (~) / Dhp 129–145 / Ud 2.3 / Snp 3.11 (~) / Iti (~) / T 210.31 / T 212.6 / T 212.29 / T 212.31 / T 212.34 / T 213.5 / T 213.28 / T 213.30 / T 213.33

The chapter on weapons teaches the practice of kindness and compassion, without resorting to weapons or harming sentient beings.

刀杖品者，教习慈仁，无行刀杖贼害众生。

All fear death, and none are not afraid of the pain of the
staff. Consider yourself as an example, do not kill, do not use the staff.

一切皆惧死，　　莫不畏杖痛，恕己可为譬，　　勿杀勿行杖。

Those who can always bring peace to all beings, without inflicting any harm, will not
encounter harm in this life, and will be long at ease in the next life.

能常安群生，　　不加诸楚毒，现世不逢害，　　后世长安隐。

One should not speak harshly, for words will be feared as retribution. Evil leads to misfortune, and weapons return to the body;

不当麤言，　　言当畏报，恶往祸来，刀杖归躯；

Speaking with goodness is like striking a bell or chime. The
body is not subject to discussion, and transcending the world becomes easy.

出言以善，　　如叩钟磬，身无论议，　　度世则易。

Those who falsely accuse the innocent with a cane or staff will suffer ten times the calamity, with swift retribution.

欧杖良善、妄谗无罪，其殃十倍，　　灾迅无赦：

In this life, they will suffer intense pain; their bodies will be maimed and broken; they will naturally suffer from illness and mental confusion;

生受酷痛；　　形体毁折；自然恼病；失意恍惚；

They will be falsely accused by others; they may face difficulties with the county
officials; their wealth and property will be exhausted; their relatives and friends will be separated;

人所诬咎；　　或县官厄；财产耗尽；　　亲戚离别；

Their homes and possessions will be burned by fire; when they die, they will enter hell. These are the ten consequences.

舍宅所有，灾火焚烧；死入地狱。　　如是为十。

Even if they shave their heads, wear long robes of grass, bathe, and sit on rocks, how can they eliminate their delusions and attachments?

虽倮剪发，　　长服草衣，沐浴踞石，奈痴结何？

They do not cut down, kill, or burn, nor do they seek
victory. People love them and wherever they go, there is no resentment.

不伐杀烧，　　亦不求胜，人爱天下，　　所适无怨。

In the world, there are those who can know shame and modesty. This is called guiding and
advancing, like urging a good horse, like urging a fine horse, advancing on the path can go far.

世党有人，能知惭愧，是名诱进，　　如策良马、如策善马，　　进道能远。

People who have faith, precepts, concentration, and diligence, who receive the path and attain wisdom, will then extinguish all suffering.

人有信戒，定意精进，受道慧成，　　便灭众苦。

Self-adornment through cultivating the Dharma, extinguishing harm and receiving pure conduct, not striking
sentient beings with a staff - this is the path of a śramaṇa.

自严以修法，　　灭损受净行，杖不加群生，　　是沙门道人。

Harmless to the world, one will never be harmed throughout
one's life. Always compassionate towards all, who can be an enemy?

无害于天下，　　终身不遇害，常慈于一切，　　孰能与为怨？
