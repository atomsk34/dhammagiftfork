T 210.4

Dharmapada (T 210) 法句经

T 210.4 笃信品法句经第四十有八章

Parallel to: SN 10.12 / Dhp 167–178 / Dhp 290–305 / Snp 1.10 / T 212.11 / T 213.10

The chapter on firm faith is the root of establishing the Way. The fruit is in the right view of causes, and the practice does not look back.

笃信品者，立道之根，果于因正见，行不回顾。

Faith, shame, precepts, and the wealth of the mind - these are praised by the refined and noble. This
path is spoken of by the wise and intelligent, and thus one ascends to the heavens and the world.

信惭戒意财，　　是法雅士誉，斯道明智说，　　如是升天世。

The foolish do not cultivate heavenly conduct, nor do they praise giving
Those who assist the good through faith and giving will thus attain peace.

愚不修天行，　　亦不誉布施，信施助善者，　　从是到彼安。

Those who have faith are true and long-lived, dwelling in peace by contemplating the Dharma
Those who are close attain the supreme mind, and the wise live a life of virtue.

信者真人长，　　念法所住安；近者意得上，　　智寿寿中贤。

Faith can attain the path, the Dharma leads to liberation. From hearing, one gains wisdom, and wherever one goes, there is illumination.

信能得道，　　法致灭度，从闻得智，所到有明；

Faith can cross the abyss, taking on the role of
a ferryman. Diligence eliminates suffering, wisdom reaches the other shore.

信能度渊，　　摄为船师，精进除苦，　　慧到彼岸。

A person with faith and practice is praised by the sages. Those who delight in non-action have all their bonds untied.

士有信行，为圣所誉，乐无为者，　　一切缚解。

Faith and precepts, with wisdom and intention able to practice, the strong man crosses over anger, and from this is freed from the abyss.

信之与戒，　　慧意能行，健夫度恚，从是脱渊。

Faith causes precepts to be sincere, and also receives wisdom
Wherever one is able to practice, one sees nourishment everywhere.

信使戒诚，　　亦受智慧，在在能行、　　处处见养。

Compared to worldly wealth, wisdom and faith are illuminating. This is the supreme treasure, the family property is extraordinary.

比方世利，慧信为明，是财上宝，　　家产非常。

Those who wish to see the truth and enjoy listening to the Dharma, are able to abandon the defilements of stinginess. This is called faith.

欲见诸真，　　乐听讲法，能舍悭垢，此之为信。

Faith can cross the river, its merit is hard
to steal. It can stop theft, the wild mendicants enjoy.

信能度河，　　其福难夺，能禁止盗，　　野沙门乐。

Without faith, one does not practice. One likes to peel away the right words, like clumsily scooping water, digging a well and stirring up mud.

无信不习，好剥正言，如拙取水，　　掘泉扬泥。

The wise practice wisdom, enjoying looking up at the clear stream, like skillfully scooping water, thinking to keep it undisturbed.

贤夫习智，　　乐仰清流，如善取水，思令不扰。

Faith does not taint others, only the wise can associate with people
If it is good, then learn; if not good, then stay away.

信不染他，　　唯贤与人，可好则学、　　非好则远。

Faith is my vehicle, no one knows what it carries. Like a well-trained elephant, self-discipline is supreme.

信为我舆，莫知斯载，如大象调，　　自调最胜。

Faith, morality, and the wealth of shame and conscience, the wealth of learning, generosity, and wisdom are the seven kinds of wealth.

信财、戒财，　　惭、愧亦财，闻财、施财、慧为七财。

From faith, uphold morality, always contemplate the Dharma, with
wisdom and beneficial conduct, serve and respect without forgetting.

从信守戒，　　常净观法，慧而利行，　　奉敬不忘。

If one is born with this wealth, regardless of gender, one will never be poor. The wise recognize the truth.

生有此财，不问男女，终以不贫，　　贤者识真。
