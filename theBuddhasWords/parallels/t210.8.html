T 210.8

Dharmapada (T 210) 法句经

T 210.8 言语品法句经第八十有二章

Parallel to: SN 6.9 / SN 6.10 / SN 8.5 / AN 10.89 / Dhp 360–382 / Snp 3.3 / Snp 3.10 / Thag 21 / T 210.34 / T 212.9 / T 213.8

The chapter on language is about how to use reason when speaking, expressing oneself, and discussing.

言语品者，所以戒口，发说、谈论当用道理。

Harsh words and insults, arrogance and contempt for others, giving rise to these actions, only increases hatred.

恶言骂詈，　　憍陵蔑人，兴起是行，疾怨滋生；

Humble words and agreeable speech, respecting others, abandoning
attachment and enduring evil, hatred and resentment naturally cease.

逊言顺辞，　　尊敬于人，弃结忍恶，　　疾怨自灭。

The axe of a person's life is in their mouth, and it is through their evil words that they cut themselves.

夫士之生，斧在口中，所以斩身，　　由其恶言。

Strife for little gain is like concealing lost wealth, leading to disputes and causing the mind to turn towards evil.

诤为少利，　　如掩失财，从彼致诤，令意向恶。

Both praise and blame are evil, and those who delight in
stirring up disputes with their mouths will all be without peace.

誉恶、恶所誉，　　是二俱为恶，好以口侩鬪，　　是后皆无安。

Without the Way, one falls into evil paths, increasing the suffering of hell. Dwelling
far from fools, cultivating patience, and contemplating the truth, there will be no transgression.

无道堕恶道，　　自增地狱苦，远愚修忍意，　　念谛则无犯。

From goodness, one attains liberation; from evil, one does not attain liberation. Those
who are well liberated are wise, and this is liberation from evil afflictions.

从善得解脱，　　为恶不得解，善解者为贤，　　是为脱恶恼。

Liberation is to embrace one's own harmful intentions, not being rash in speech
and speaking meaningfully and according to the Dharma. Such speech is gentle and sweet.

解自抱损意，　　不躁言得中，义说如法说，　　是言柔软甘。

Therefore, in speech, one must ensure one's own well-being
and not harm others. This is called skillful speech.

是以言语者，　　必使己无患，亦不尅众人，　　是为能善言。

Speech should be agreeable to the mind, and bring joy. It should
not lead to evil intentions. When spoken, it should be acceptable to all.

言使投意可，　　亦令得欢喜，不使至恶意，　　出言众悉可。

Speaking with sincerity and sweetness, according to the Dharma and without fault, being
truthful, meaningful, and in accordance with the Dharma, this is called approaching the path.

至诚甘露说，　　如法而无过，谛如义、如法，　　是为近道立。

Speaking in accordance with the Buddha's words brings good fortune and liberation. It
is able to create an endless expanse. This is called the supreme speech.

说如佛言者，　　是吉得灭度，为能作浩际，　　是谓言中上。
