T 213.10

Faji yaosong jing / Udānavarga (T 213) 法集要颂经

T 213.10 法集要颂经正信品第十

Parallel to: SN 10.12 / Dhp 167–178 / Dhp 235–255 / Dhp 290–305 / Snp 1.10 / T 210.4 / T 212.11

Faith, shame, morality, and generosity—the wise praise these qualities. This path
is taught by the wise, leading to rebirth in the heavenly realms.

信惭戒布施，　　上士誉此法，斯道明智说，　　得生于天界。

The foolish do not cultivate heavenly conduct, nor do they praise generosity. Those
who are upright and rejoice in giving will attain happiness in future lives.

愚不修天行，　　亦不赞布施，正直随喜施，　　彼得后世乐。

Those who have faith are true people, long-lived, and dwell in the contemplation of the Dharma
Those who approach should attain the supreme, the wise, the long-lived, the middling, and the virtuous.

信者真人长，　　念法所安住，近者应得上，　　智寿中中贤。

What is the highest human karma? What conduct leads to happiness? What is essential
and what is essential to be renounced? What is the highest longevity and middling longevity?

人业何者上？　　何行致欢乐？何要出要者？　　何寿寿中上？

Those who have faith are true people, long-lived, and dwell in the contemplation of the Dharma
Those who are real attain the supreme mind, the wise, the long-lived, and the middling and virtuous.

信者真人长，　　念法所安住，实者意得上，　　智寿寿中贤。

Through the wealth of faith, one attains the path and self-realization, the extinction of
the Dharma. Through good hearing, one attains wisdom and is liberated from all bonds.

信财乃得道，　　自致法灭度，善闻从慧得，　　得脱一切缚。

Faith and precepts are the Dharma, and the wise mind is able
to practice. The strong man overcomes anger and is liberated from the abyss.

信之与戒法，　　慧意则能行，健夫度恚怒，　　从是得脱渊。

Faith causes precepts to be accomplished, and also attains longevity and wisdom. Wherever
one is, one is able to practice, and wherever one goes, one sees offerings.

信使戒成就，　　亦获寿及慧，在在则能行，　　处处见供养。

Giving is associated with fighting and gathering, this karma is not practiced by the
wise. When giving, it is not the time for fighting. Why hesitate to give quickly?

施共与鬪集，　　此业智不处，施时非鬪时，　　速施何疑虑？

This is the worldly benefit, wisdom and faith are the mother of wisdom
This wealth is the treasure of the world, family wealth is not permanent.

此方出世利，　　慧信为智母，是财出世宝，　　家产则非常。

Those who wish to see the truth, delight in hearing the Dharma teachings, and
are able to abandon the mind of stinginess and defilement, this is the supreme faith.

欲见诸真者，　　乐听闻法教，能舍悭垢心，　　此乃为上信。

Faith can cross the river of existence, its merit is difficult to encroach
upon, it can prevent theft and robbery, the joy of the solitary monk.

信能渡有河，　　其福难侵夺，能禁止窃盗，　　闲静沙门乐。

The monk always comes to the place of the wise, who sees with joy
and the others who have deep faith, hearing it, they give rise to joy.

沙门恒来至，　　智者所见乐，及余笃信者，　　闻则生欢喜。

If a person is full of distress, coveting others' clothing
and food, that person will not attain samādhi day or night.

若人怀懊恼，　　贪他人衣食，彼人昼夜寐，　　不获三摩地。

If a person can cut off greed, like cutting down a
palm tree, that person will then attain samādhi day and night.

若人能断贪，　　如截多罗树，彼人则昼夜，　　及获三摩地。

Without faith, one does not practice, and likes to peel away correct speech
like digging to take clear spring water, digging up the mud of spring water.

无信不修行，　　好剥正言说，如掘取清泉，　　掘泉扬其泥。

The wise practice faith and conduct, delighting in looking up to the pure
flow, like skillfully taking spring water, thinking of the coolness without stirring up turbidity.

智者习信行，　　乐仰清净流，如善取泉水，　　思冷不扰浊。

Faith and wisdom are not stained by others, only wisdom and the virtuous. If it
is not good, then distance oneself from it; if it is good, then approach and learn.

信智不染他，　　惟智与贤仁，非好则远之，　　可好则近学。

Whether one delights in faith or not, one should contemplate in silence. Stay
far away from those without faith; for the virtuous, one should practice faith.

乐信与不乐，　　寂默自应思，远离无信者，　　信仁应行之。

Impermanence, desire, and greed, Carelessness, and delight. Virtuous conduct, speech, And action, faith as the tenth.

无常及欲、贪　　放逸与爱乐戒善、行、语言　　业、信为第十
