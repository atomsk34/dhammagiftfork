T 213.30

Faji yaosong jing / Udānavarga (T 213) 法集要颂经

T 213.30 法集要颂经乐品第三十

Parallel to: SN 3.14 / SN 4.18 / SN 10.8 / AN 3.35 / Dhp 76–89 / Dhp 129–145 / Dhp 167–178 / Dhp 179–196 / Dhp 197–208 / Dhp 290–305 / Dhp 320–333 / Ud 2.1 / Ud 2.2 / Ud 2.3 / Ud 2.4 / Ud 2.5 / Ud 2.9 / Ud 2.10 / Ud 8.10 / Snp 1.1 / T 210.18 / T 210.22 / T 210.31 / T 210.38 / T 212.31

If you are patient, your enemies will be defeated. If you are arrogant, you will be despised
If you rest your mind, you will be happy. There is no victory or defeat in the mind.

忍胜则怨贼，　　自负则自鄙，息意则快乐，　　无胜无负心。

If you disturb others, seeking peace and happiness for
yourself, you will only create enemies and never attain liberation.

若人扰乱彼，　　自求安乐世，遂成其怨憎，　　终不得解脱。

If you enjoy sensual pleasures and use weapons to harm living beings
seeking peace for yourself, you will not find happiness in future lives.

善乐于爱欲，　　以杖加群生，于中自求安，　　后世不得乐。

If you wish for happiness, do not use weapons to harm living
beings. Seeking happiness for yourself, you will also find happiness in future lives.

人欲得欢乐，　　杖不加群生，于中自求乐，　　后世亦得乐。

Enjoy the Dharma and delight in practicing it. Be careful not to engage in evil
Those who can practice the Dharma well will find happiness in this and future lives.

乐法乐学行，　　慎莫行恶法，能善行法者，　　今世后世乐。

Those who protect the Dharma and practice it will cultivate the Dharma and receive good rewards
This accords with the teachings of the Dharma. Practicing the Dharma does not lead to evil.

护法行法者，　　修法获善报，此应法律教，　　行法不趣恶。

Those who protect the Dharma and practice it are like a canopy covering their form
This accords with the teachings of the Dharma. Practicing the Dharma does not lead to evil.

护法行法者，　　如盖覆其形，此应法律教，　　行法不趣恶。

Evil conduct leads to hell, and rebirth in the evil realms. Unlawful
conduct leads to self-destruction, like grasping a poisonous snake with one's hand.

恶行入地狱，　　所生堕恶道，非法自陷溺，　　如手把蚖蛇。

Neither lawful nor unlawful conduct has the same retribution. Unlawful conduct
leads to hell, while the true Dharma leads to rebirth in heaven.

不以法非法，　　二事俱同报，非法入地狱，　　正法生于天。

Giving and fighting are the same in this respect: the wise do not
praise this merit. When giving, one is also fighting. These two things are equal.

施与战同处，　　此德智不誉，施时亦战时，　　此事二俱等。

When a person encounters hundreds of thousands of changes, they are all equal in eliminating the enemy
of arrogance. At that time, giving with a pure mind is the most excellent among the strong.

人遭百千变，　　等除憍慢怨，时施清净心，　　健夫最为胜。

Forbearance is superior to the many, and precepts are superior to the many lazy
Those who give with faith and wisdom will receive good rewards in their next life.

忍少得胜多，　　戒胜懈怠多，有信慧施者，　　后身受善报。

The reward of giving with joy is that all one's wishes are fully
accomplished. One quickly attains the first extinction and gradually enters the state of non-action.

快乐施福报，　　所愿皆全成，速得第一灭，　　渐入无为际。

If they seek skillful means, the wise and holy give wisdom. Having exhausted
the original source of suffering, one should know that they will receive great rewards.

若彼求方便，　　贤圣智慧施，尽其苦原本，　　当知获大报。

Loving the Dharma, peaceful and secure, the mind and intention are pure and
clean. The Dharma spoken by the wise and holy is enjoyed by the wise.

爱法善安隐，　　心意洁清净，贤圣所说法，　　智者所娱乐。

If one's mind delights in meditation, it also delights in non-arising. It also
delights in the four foundations of mindfulness, as well as the seven factors
of awakening, and the four bases of supernatural power, and the eightfold noble path.

若人心乐禅，　　亦复乐不起，亦乐四意止，　　并及七觉意，及彼四神足，　　贤圣八品道。

Delighting in solid food, delighting in wearing the Dharma robes, delighting
in walking meditation, delighting in dwelling in the mountains and forests.

善乐于抟食，　　善乐摄法服，善乐于经行，　　乐处于山薮。

Having attained the abode of peace and bliss, present in the Dharma
without fabrication, having transcended all fears, surpassing the world's defilements and attachments.

已逮安乐处，　　现法而无为，已越诸恐惧，　　超世诸染著。

Delighting in mindfulness and retention, well observing all dharmas, excellent
indeed, harmless in the world, nurturing the various kinds of beings.

善乐于念持，　　善观于诸法，善哉世无害，　　养育众生类。

In the world, free from desire and attachment, transcending the mind's defilements and clinging
able to extinguish one's arrogance and pride - this is called the foremost bliss.

世无欲爱乐，　　越诸染著意，能灭己憍慢，　　此名第一乐。

The bliss of the aged upholding precepts, the bliss of having faith and
accomplishment, the bliss of discerning the meaning and purport, not creating various evil karma.

耆年持戒乐，　　有信成就乐，分别义趣乐，　　不造众恶业。

In the world, the bliss of having parents, the bliss of harmony in
the assembly, the bliss of being a śramaṇa, the bliss of tranquility and concentration.

世有父母乐，　　众集和亦乐，世有沙门乐，　　静志乐亦然。

The bliss of the appearance of the buddhas, the bliss of being able to receive
the Dharma teachings, the bliss of the harmony of the saṃgha, harmony always brings peace.

诸佛出兴乐，　　说法堪受乐，众僧和合乐，　　和则常有安。

The bliss of upholding the precepts completely, the bliss of extensive learning and knowledge
the bliss of seeing the true person, the bliss of the practice of liberation.

持戒完具乐，　　多闻广知乐，覩见真人乐，　　解脱行迹乐。

The bliss of the pure and cool water of virtue, the joy of gathering the wealth of
the Dharma, the joy of attaining wisdom and insight, the joy of destroying arrogance and having no wrong.

德水清凉乐，　　法财自集快，得智明慧快，　　灭慢无邪快。

The bliss of seeing the virtuous, the joy of being together with them
the joy of not associating with fools, the joy of being firmly established forever.

得覩诸贤乐，　　同会亦复乐，不与愚从事，　　毕固永已乐。

Not associating with fools for countless days, living with fools
is difficult, like being with enemies and those one dislikes
Living with the wise is easy, like being with close relatives.

不与愚从事，　　经历无数日，与愚同居难，　　如与怨憎会，与智同处易，　　如共亲亲会。

It is very difficult to encounter a wise person, who is never born in vain
If one is born in a place where they are, that family will surely be blessed.

人智甚难遇，　　终不虚托生，设当托生处，　　彼家必蒙庆。

Everyone attains peace and tranquility, the brahmin attains nirvana
not being tainted by desire, completely liberated from all places.

一切得安隐，　　梵志取灭度，不为欲所染，　　尽脱于诸处。

Completely severing inauspicious bonds, subduing inner afflictions, forever ceasing
to sleep, the mind and consciousness become clear and penetrating.

尽断不祥结，　　降伏内烦恼，永息得睡眠，　　心识得清彻。

Be careful not to be attached to pleasure, one should follow the practice
of protection, one should contemplate letting go of the world, and observe joyful things.

慎莫著于乐，　　当就护来行，当念舍于世，　　观于快乐事。

The pleasures of the world and the pleasures of the heavens
this is called the utmost of love, not even one-sixteenth is attained.

如世欲欢乐，　　及彼天上乐，此名为爱极，　　十六未获一。

If one wishes to discard the heavy burden, and not create heavy karma again, the heavy
burden is the suffering of the world, being able to discard it is the fastest happiness.

欲舍于重担，　　更不造重业，重担世之苦，　　能舍最快乐。

Completely cutting off all desires and lust, and extinguishing all activities, and
extinguishing the root of the five aggregates, no longer accepting the three realms.

尽断诸爱欲，　　及灭一切行，并灭五蕴本，　　更不受三有。

The accumulation of meaning brings happiness, friends and food bring happiness, that
extinction is peaceful happiness, spreading to others, suffering takes happiness as its basis.

义聚则有乐，　　朋友食福乐，彼灭寂然乐，　　展转普及人，苦以乐为本。

Like a blazing fire pit, blazing and burning, gradually going out, not knowing where it goes.

犹彼焰火炉，赫焰而炽然，渐渐而还灭，不知所凑处。

Those who see like this are free from the mire of desire, and even when they leave, there is no place to go, attaining the happiness of the immovable.

如是等见人，免于爱欲泥，去亦无处所，以获无动乐。

In the middle, there is no anger, there is change and impermanence, removing sorrow and no worries, peacefully observing the existence of the world.

中间无有恚，有变易不停，除忧无有愁，寂然观世有。

There is happiness and no affliction, hearing much of the true Dharma, even if one sees harm, people are greedy for form.

有乐无有恼，正法而多闻，设见有所损，人人贪于色。

Without afflictions, the world is well-lived, the great Dharma knows the source of afflictions, people should understand the flaws of afflictions, people's minds are bound and attached, also bound to the basis of form.

无结世善寿，大法知结源，人当明结瑕，人人心缚著，亦缚于色本。

All suffering from humiliation, all happiness from one's own doing
victory and defeat naturally arise, yet there is nothing gained.

一切受辱苦，　　一切任己乐，胜负自然兴，　　竟不有所获。

Those who wish for happiness and longevity can endure the light retribution, the
patient one is patient with others, the impatient one dwells in all existences.

诸欲得乐寿，　　能忍彼轻报，忍者忍于人，　　不忍处诸有。

Those who wish for happiness and longevity are unconfused about afflictions
the confused one is confused about others, I am without confusion.

诸欲得乐寿，　　于惑而无惑，惑者惑于人，　　我斯无有惑。

Those who wish for happiness and longevity, having ended themselves without afflictions, should
eat the food of mindfulness, like the gods of the Radiant Sound Heaven
who always take mindfulness as food, with no burning of the mind and body.

诸欲得乐寿，　　终己无结者，当食于念食，　　如彼光音天，恒以念为食，　　意身无所烧。

Sentient beings see suffering and happiness, the holy Dharma is indestructible, although
encountering the traces of happiness, without traces, how can there be contact?

众生见苦乐，　　圣法无损坏，虽值触乐迹，　　无迹焉有触。

Like a bhikṣu in concentration, not attached to any defilements, sentient
beings undergo suffering and happiness, but are unable to perceive it.

如苾刍在定，　　不著一切垢，众生遭苦乐，　　而不能觉知。

Tathāgata, much learning, oneself, extensive, good friend, nirvāṇa, contemplation, karmic hindrances, correspondence, happiness, tenth.

如来与多闻　　己身、广、善友圆寂、观、罪障　　相应、乐第十
