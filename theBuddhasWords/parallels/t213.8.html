T 213.8

Faji yaosong jing / Udānavarga (T 213) 法集要颂经

T 213.8 法集要颂经语言品第八

Parallel to: SN 6.9 / SN 6.10 / SN 8.5 / AN 10.89 / Dhp 157–166 / Dhp 306–319 / Dhp 360–382 / Ud 4.8 / Snp 3.3 / Snp 3.10 / Iti / Thag 21 / T 210.8 / T 210.30 / T 210.34 / T 210.38 / T 212.9

Lying leads to hell, saying you didn't do it when you did
you'll suffer the consequences of both, and your actions will drag you away.

妄语入地狱，　　作之言不作，二罪后俱受，　　是行自牵去。

Those who are always violent and evil have
an axe in their mouths that cuts themselves.

恒怀暴恶人，　　斧在口中出，所以自伤身，　　由其出恶言。

The Dharma teacher delights people with his words, speaking countless
truths, causing me to become pregnant, not ashamed of this ritual.

说法自悦人，　　口出无量义，使我怀妊身，　　不惭此仪式。

To praise evil and be praised in return, both are evil. Those
who like to fight with their mouths will have no peace later.

誉恶恶还誉，　　是二俱为恶，好以口快鬪，　　彼后皆无安。

Striving for a small profit is like losing a treasure
From that comes fighting, with the mind heading towards evil paths.

争为微少利，　　如掩失财宝，从彼致鬪诤，　　合意向恶道。

Hundreds of thousands of niraya hells, thirty-six and five hells, those
who slander the sages, with mouth and mind making evil vows.

百千尼罗浮，　　三十六五狱，诽谤贤圣者，　　口意发恶愿。

Without the path, falling into evil paths, increasing the suffering of hell, avoiding fools
and cultivating the mind of patience, contemplating the truth then there is no transgression.

无道堕恶道，　　自增地狱苦，远愚修忍意，　　念谛则无犯。

If relying on the inner treasure store, relying on the sages to
live, fools fall into evil paths, still wishing to do wrong views.

若倚内宝藏，　　依贤圣活命，愚者堕恶道，　　犹愿邪见作。

Having missed this excellent opportunity, making further vows and seeking, will
never see the truth, let alone wish to see the ultimate.

以失今良会，　　更立誓愿求，终不见圣谛，　　况欲见究竟？

Bamboo and reeds produce fruit and dry up, still harming their
own bodies. If speaking, one should speak well, not teach evil dharmas.

竹芦生实干，　　还害其自躯，若吐言当善，　　不演恶法教。

From good one attains liberation, from evil one does not attain liberation
The wise are liberated from good, this is liberation from evil destinies.

从善得解脱，　　为恶不得解，善解者为贤，　　是为脱恶趣。

The wise and holy do not understand it like that, like the foolish who
attain liberation. The bhikṣu should restrain his mind, not speaking rashly to attain loyalty.

圣贤解不然，　　如彼愚得解，苾刍挹损意，　　不躁言得忠。

Speaking in accordance with meaning and the Dharma, speaking with gentle words, speaking well
the teachings of the wise and holy, speaking the Dharma and the Dharma twice
speaking in accordance with mindfulness three times, speaking in accordance with truth four times.

义说如法说，　　所语言柔软，善说贤圣教，　　法说如法二，念说如念三，　　谛说如谛四。

Therefore, when speaking, one must ensure that the mind is free from
affliction, and also not harm sentient beings. This is called speaking well.

是以言语者，　　必使心无患，亦不尅有情，　　是为能善言。

Let the words be agreeable to the mind, and also bring joy
not leading to evil intentions. When speaking, let it be acceptable to all.

言使投意可，　　亦令得欢喜，不使至恶意，　　出言众悉可。

Speaking with sincerity and sweetness, teaching the Dharma without compare, speaking the truth and
meaning according to the Dharma - this is establishing the basis of the path.

至诚甘露说，　　说法无有上，谛说义如法，　　是为立道本。

Speaking as the Buddha says is auspicious and attains liberation, able to
cut off the edge of suffering - this is called the best speech.

如说佛言者，　　是吉得灭度，为能断苦际，　　是谓言中上。
